GOVERNMENT DECISION 2. In the financial year 2018-19, for tap water supply schemes, an amount of Rs. A grant of Rs. 6.89025 crore (Rupees Six Crores Ninety-nine Lakhs Two Thousand Five Hundred only) is being sanctioned and released to Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur on Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) for coverage under General Component.
Conditions 1) The Director, Water and Sanitation Support Agency, is to spend this grant for completion of the physically complete but financially incomplete village/ hamlet schemes taken up under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme. The Director, Water and Sanitation Support Agency, while spending the grant, should spend on priority for completion of schemes in fluoride/ arsenic affected villages/ hamlets, schemes in villages under Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana, sanitation free villages.
(2) The works of the schemes of the villages/ wards covered under the above mentioned schemes as per the priority in the fixed annual action plan of this department for the implementation of water supply schemes under the general component of this subsidy and the Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
