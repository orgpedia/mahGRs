(itself) The matter of extending the continuation of 12 temporary posts on the establishment was under consideration of the Government. In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 10 temporary posts for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell at the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) establishment and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalswarajya Project have been created under the Jalswarajya Phase-II programme for the duration of the programme and are temporary posts at the department's establishment.
As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes related to providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 12 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 31st August, 2023 and the Clarification dated 6th September, 2023 of the Finance Department, read with the above, the Government is approving the continuation of 12 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024.
Expenditure on 10 temporary posts for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Title-3451 Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467) should be met from the sanctioned provision for the financial year 2023-2024. The expenditure on salary and allowances for the 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarajya Project should be made for the duration of the programme from the account number 31868434781 of State Bank of India, Belapur, Belapur Branch, Navi Mumbai.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the provisions of the Government Decision dated 31st August, 2023 and the Sanction Letter dated 6th September, 2023 of the Finance Department.
