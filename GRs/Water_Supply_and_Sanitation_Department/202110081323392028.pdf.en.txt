The proposal-
Defined Contribution Pension Scheme has been implemented under the Government Resolution dated 31.10.2005 for the State Government employees who joined Government service on or after 1.11.2005 and are presently covered under the National Pension Scheme (NPS). In the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran also, the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS) to the employees of the Authority appointed on or after 1.11.2005 has been implemented by the Authority vide Circular No. Under this scheme, 10% contribution of the concerned employee from the salary of the employee and 10 10% contribution from the authority is credited to the DCPS account of the concerned employee by the authority.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MAJIPRA-2016/ PR. No. 50/ PAPU-23 2. Subject to the Government Decision (3) dated 23rd March, 2017, the State Government has taken over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. The employer's (State Government) share to Government employees under the National Pension Scheme has been reduced from 10% to 14% as per Government Order dated 19.8.2019. On the same lines, the government was considering to increase the employer's share to 14% instead of 10% under the defined contribution pension scheme for the employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority.
Government's decision
The employer's share to be paid to the employees appointed in the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran on or after 1.11.2005 and who are covered under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS) is being made as" Employer's Contribution" to the extent of 14% of the amount of" Subscriber's Basic Pay plus Dearness Allowance" subject to the following conditions.
(i) The Authority shall take action in accordance with the rules of Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority by entering into a separate agreement with the National Pension Scheme Trust as notified vide letter dated 26.7.2021.
The above increase in employer's contribution will be payable from 1st April, 2019.
3. Monthly Contribution amounting to 10% of the amount of" Basic Pay plus Dearness Allowance" of the employee as" Employee's Contribution" shall be deducted from the salary of the employee concerned as before.
The expenditure in this case should be spent under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, (Mandatory) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" and it should be met from the approved budgetary grants.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the concurrence received from the Department of Finance, Informal Reference No. 287/ Expenditure-3 dated 17.8.2021 and Informal Reference No. 105/21/ Service-4 dated 7.9.2021.
