The Proposal -
The Union Ministry of Jal Shakti has issued guidelines dated 24th December, 2019 for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored "Jal Jeevan Mission Scheme" in the state. As per the guidelines of the Central Government, guidelines for implementation of Jal Jeevan FRM Scheme in the State have been issued vide Government Decision dated 04.09.2020. As per the guidelines of the Central Government, payments under the Jal Jeevan Fem Yojana should be made only after the completed works of rural tap water supply schemes are inspected through the Tertiary Screening System (TPL).
