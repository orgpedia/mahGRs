The Proposal:
All the employees/ officers of the Engineering Board working on the premises of the Government establishment have been transferred to the Maharashtra Life Authority by the Government after the formation of the Maharashtra Water Supply and Drainage Board in 1979. The grant is provided by the Government under the head 2215 1638 to the Authority for payment of pension and other retirement benefits in respect of the service obligations of the said employee. A budget provision of Rs. 3,54,18, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Fifty Four Lakh Eighteen Thousand only) has been made for this purpose for the financial year 2017-18. This provision has been made available to the Maharashtra Life Authority.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2017-18, the demand number Y-2, 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to other government institutions (01) Assistance to other government institutions (01) (03) Pension payable to government employees transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran
