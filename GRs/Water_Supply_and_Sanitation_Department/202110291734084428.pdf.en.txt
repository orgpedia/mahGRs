The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22, a total of Rs. 29.1214 crore has been disbursed under the Government Decision No. 2 to 5 and 7 to 11 under the head of Demand No. Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub-Divisional Establishment for Implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843) to meet the salary expenditure of Class 1 and Class 2 officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad.

Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, a grant of Rs. 6,80,00, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores Eighty Lakh only) for the salary of 1st Class and 1st Class Officers of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" is being sanctioned to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the concerned Zilla Parishad as shown in the" Statement A" accompanying this Government Decision.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. The funds disbursed to the Kolhapur Zilla Parishad are subject to the condition that the expenditure incurred in the year 2020-21 is reconciled with the expenditure in the Accountant General's Office. The Kolhapur Zilla Parishad should not draw funds from the treasury without the completion of the pending reconciliation work and the Kolhapur District Treasury should not make payments without a certificate to that effect.
3. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business, Financial Regulations, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing and spending the disbursed funds from the Treasury.
