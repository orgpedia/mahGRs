Funding needs to be made available. It was under consideration of the government to disburse funds to the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Aurangabad from the funds remaining with the department.
Government's decision:
To meet the expenditure on the measures taken/ to be taken to solve the drinking water shortage in the Konkan and Aurangabad divisions as per the norms laid down by the Government for solving the drinking water shortage in rural/ urban areas, an amount of Rs. 256.42 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Six Lakhs and Forty Two Thousand only) is being sanctioned and released to the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Aurangabad through the Budget Distribution System subject to the terms and conditions laid down in the prevailing Standing Government Resolution as well as the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions
1) The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Aurangabad should ensure that the funds are disbursed to the respective District Collectors. The Collector, Jalna should allocate this fund on priority for the well acquisition measures.
The funds will be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas for the period 2017-18 and for the payment of pending dues during this period.
3) Ensure that the funds are not kept in a personal ledger account or bank account and are disbursed directly to the contractors through electronic clearing services.
The District Collectors who have not received the utilization certificates of the funds released in the financial year 2016-17 and 2017-18 for the purpose of drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas, should disburse the funds to the respective District Collectors only after verifying that they have received the utilization certificates of the entire expenditure (100%) of the funds distributed in the year 2016-17 and at least 75% of the funds distributed in the financial year 2017-18.
5) Government Circular, Department of Finance. The districts which have fulfilled the conditions as on 18.04.2017 and 30.04.2017. The funds should be distributed to the districts.
