Preamble: As per the review of the posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) under the Government Decision dated 04.09.2002, read with the said Read No.1 of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, 25 new posts have been created for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department as per the Government Decision dated 22.03.2012. The 25 temporary posts included in the figure of Water Supply and Sanitation Department have been approved to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 as per the Government decision dated 16.09.2020 in Read No.3, 23.02.2021 in Read No.5, 30.08.2021 in Read No.7, 02.03.2022 in Read No.9, 30.08.2022 in Read No.11 and 28.02.2023 in Read No.13.
The Government Decision dated 31st August, 2023 and the Corrigendum dated 06th September, 2023 of the Finance Department at the above mentioned Reading No. 14 and 15 have empowered all the Administrative Departments to extend the period from 01st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 25 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Implementation of schemes for providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities through the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation as per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government Page 2 of 5

The department needs more manpower. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 25 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 31st August, 2023 and the Clarification dated 06th September, 2023 of the Finance Department read above, the Government is approving the continuation of 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) from 01st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 as indicated in Form-A attached to this Government Decision.
The expenditure on this should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2023-2024 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 31st August, 2023 and the Sanction Letter dated 06th September, 2023 of the Finance Department.
