A total of 139 such temporary posts were approved to be continued from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 as per the decision of the Government in Reference No.3.
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Division in Reference No. 1 and 2, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024. In view of the reasons stated by the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune for extension of temporary posts and in view of the provision in the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference to the Finance Department, the Government has decided to extend the period of extension of temporary posts under reference No. 1 in the Ground Water Survey and Development System. The extension of 379 temporary posts fixed under Section 6 is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the urgency of the work in the Ground Water Survey and Development System mentioned in the Preamble, this Government has approved the continuation of 379 temporary posts in the establishment of Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
379 temporary posts as indicated in" Annexure-A" on the establishment of Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune are being approved for continuation subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The said period is being extended subject to the assumption that the said temporary posts have not lapsed.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
(3) As from the date on which the officer/ employee holding the post of Superintendant is absorbed in the other posts, the Superintendents post shall stand automatically abolished.
The expenditure for the aforesaid temporary posts shall be met from the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts referred to in Annexure-B.
