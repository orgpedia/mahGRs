Government Order, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, No: APNA-0221/ PR No. 103/ PAPU-15 dated 06th February, 2023 The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year.
The provisional final seniority of the sub-agency (Mechanics), Group-A cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System dated 01/01/2019 to 01/01/2021 has been published vide Circular No. 2 dated 01/01/2021. Subsequently, promotions to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) in Ground Water Survey and Development System have been regularized/ adjusted under the Government Order at Reference No. 3. The General Administration Department has included the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical), Group-A from the year 2021 to 2022 as per the instructions given under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference. Provisional seniority chart showing the status as on 01/01/2022 and 01/01/2023 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
