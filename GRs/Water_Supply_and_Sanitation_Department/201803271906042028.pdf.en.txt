For the year 2018, a revised budget provision of Rs. 9,20,95, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crore Twenty Lakh Ninety Five Thousand only) has been made for 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaried).
Out of the said provision, Rs. 2,50,50, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) has been disbursed to the concerned Zilla Parishads as per the Government Decision No. 3, and Rs. 47,50,000/- (Rupees Forty Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) has been disbursed as per the Government Decision No. 4, read with the Government Decision No. Considering the suggestions given by the Government Circular No. 1 and No. 2 of the Finance Department, information was sought from all the District Councils under the letter No. 5 of the Finance Department. Considering the expenditure incurred so far and the demand of the Zila Parishads who have submitted detailed complete information, the Government is considering to disburse a total amount of Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh only) under the head of Accounts 2215 1881 for the non-salary expenditure of the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zila Parishads.
Government's decision:
Considering the suggestions given by the Finance Department vide circulars dated 18th April, 2017 and 30th June, 2017, information was sought from all the Zila Parishads vide letter dated 30th December, 2017. Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions (District Level) (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions Under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads- (Local Area) (01) (05) Regular Establishment (2215 1881) Under the heading" 31, Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs only) for Assistant Grants (Non-Salary) for the following three Zilla Parishads for the purpose of meeting the non-salary expenditure of Rural Water Supply Department.
