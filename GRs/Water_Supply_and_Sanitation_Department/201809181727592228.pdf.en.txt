The Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency should inform the Government of the allocation from this free subsidy.
The powers to examine the accounts of expenditure made by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for these schemes from this grant are being given to the Accountant General, Maharashtra, Mumbai/ Nagpur or the officer of this department who has been given special powers. If the concerned documents, accountant general or officer of the special authorized department demand, the water and sanitation assistance agency should provide them immediately.
As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure-II to the Bombay Finance Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority should be submitted to the Accountant General by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for inspection along with relevant documents as per the demand of the Accountant General.
The Water and Sanitation Support Agency shall issue the utilization certificates of the grants received by the Water and Sanitation Support Agency from the Government or the grants received in the subsequent period. The Director, Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency shall certify and sign the programme-wise amounts of grants and expenditure sanctioned and released as indicated in the Utilization Certificate. The certificate of utility shall be signed by the Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
The utilization certificate of this subsidy should be submitted to the government in the prescribed format and copies of the utilization certificate should also be sent to the Accountant General.
The expenditure of this grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 001 on the prescribed date.
Under this Government decision, the funds released to the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai are being disbursed to the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai on BEAMS system.
