No. 55/2020, dated 05/10/2020 Preamble: The Central Government has decided to restructure the Centrally Sponsored National Rural Drinking Water Programme by subsuming it with Jal Jeevan Mission from 2009-10. Accordingly, the State Cabinet in its meeting held on 08/07/2020 has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Jal Jeevan Mission in the State as per the decision of the Government in No. 1.
The State Water and Sanitation Mission has been entrusted with the responsibility of planning, implementation and monitoring of Jal Jeevan Mission and sanitation related programmes in the State.
Since 2019, the Union Government has restructured the National Rural Drinking Water Programme and launched Jal Jeevan Mission. However, as there is no provision for" Bharat Nirman Cell" under Jal Jeevan Mission, the Divisional Bharat Nirman Cell has been closed down vide Government Order dated 30/09/2020.
The Mission Director, State Water and Sanitation Mission has brought to the notice of the Government that due to the closure of Bharat Nirman Cell, there is a problem in the control of rural water supply program, technical scrutiny of water supply schemes and there is a hindrance in the collection of information by coordinating information at the district level.

A request has been made to the government under reference No. 2 to nominate officers in the office of the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, to control the Mission at the department level.
Government order:
Considering the request made by Mission Director, State Water and Sanitation Mission, to achieve the objective of providing pure, adequate and sustainable water supply to rural households at all times and at convenient places under Jal Jeevan Mission, the Sub Divisional Engineer (Architecture) in the office of Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Authority at Divisional level is being appointed as" Nodal Officer" for the works indicated before his name to control the water supply schemes and related matters being implemented by Zilla Parishad and Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
Deputy Divisional Engineer (Architecture)- Nodal officer to carry out all kinds of works at the divisional level under Jal Jeevan Mission by the Zilla Parishads of the division and Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
The concerned officers should take care of the responsibility of Jal Jeevan Mission by taking care of their own work. No additional pay or allowance shall be admissible to these officers for the responsibilities assigned to them as Nodal Officers.
