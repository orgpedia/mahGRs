Preamble For the Financial Year 2018-2019, under the sub-heading 02 Wages under the head Main Accounts 4402 1148, an amount of Rs. 3.30 lakh and 052 machinery and equipment under sub-heading Rs. 53.84 lakh has been sanctioned for Ground Water Survey and Development System.
Mr. Mohammed Sadiq Shaikh Ameer and other daily wage employees of the office under the Regional Deputy Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Amravati Division, Amravati, have filed a suit in various courts against the payment of wages/ dues to the concerned. The court has given an order. The payment of Rs. 10,70,257/- has been submitted by the Deputy Director, Amaravati to the Directorate for providing grants. However, during the current financial year, the Directorate has been unable to pay the arrears and monthly wages in respect of daily wage employees due to the sanction of meagre subsidy in wages in the Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub-Sub- The Directorate has submitted a proposal for redistribution of Rs. 14.00 lakhs. In view of this, the matter of providing necessary funds by redistribution under the sub-heading 02 wages under the said head of accounts was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Among the available provisions in the budget of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation for the financial year 2018-2019, Demand No. Y-6, Head Account 4402, Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 4402 1148. 14.00 lakh is being approved by the Government to be reallocated for meeting the incremental expenditure under this head.
The concerned authorities should take note of the revised provisions after redistribution and take further action regarding the distribution of the said fund.
