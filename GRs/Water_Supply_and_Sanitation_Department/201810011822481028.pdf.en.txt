The Proposal:
For meeting the non-salary expenditure on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishad vide Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zila Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions (District Level) (01) Assistance to Zila Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions Establishment Grants to Zila Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (Local Area) (01) (05) Regular Establishment (2215 1881), a budget provision of Rs. 10,23,28, 000/- has been made for the financial year 2018-2019 under the head 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
The matter of disbursement of funds under the above head of accounts in the current financial year to the District Councils fulfilling the conditions prescribed in the Government Decision dated 2.4.2018 of the Finance Department is under consideration of the Government.
