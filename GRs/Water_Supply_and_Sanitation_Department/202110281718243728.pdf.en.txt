No.46/ Papu 10, Dy. 01.03.2021 Government Circular issued by the Central Government. The Guidelines issued on Jal Jeevan Mission on 25.12.2019 and the Reference No. 1 d. Jal Jeevan Mission is being implemented in the State as per the Government decision dated 04.09.2021.
After providing water supply through functional tap connections as per Jal Jeevan Mission standards to 100% households in the village under Jal Jeevan Mission, such village shall be declared as" Har Ghar Jal" and a certificate issued in this regard. It is listed in 5.4. Reference No. 2 d. Instructions have been issued to all the Regional Offices vide letter dated 01.03.2021. The detailed procedure is as follows:
After providing water supply through individual tap connections to each household in the village as per Jal Jeevan Mission standards, the concerned Deputy Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad should provide" Har Ghar Jal" certificate/ completion report to the Gram Panchayat/ Village Water and Sanitation Committee of the concerned village as per Annexure-1 along with this circular.
After receiving the completion certificate from the Deputy Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, the concerned Gram Panchayat should take a decision to declare the village" Har Ghar Jal" as per Annexure-2 with this circular.
Thereafter, the Gram Panchayat/ Village Water and Sanitation Committee shall take action in the Gram Sabha of the village to declare the village" Har Ghar Jal" as follows:

a) During the Gram Sabha, the concerned Sarpanch/ President, Village Water and Sanitation Committee addressed the Gram Sabha- Is water supply available to all the families in the village as well as to the schools/ Anganwadi centres/ Ashramshalas etc. through separate tap connections? Is there any family in the village which is not deprived of water supply through a separate tap connection? " It should be asked.
(b) The members of the Gram Sabha should raise their hands in response.
A good quality video of the event, usually 2-3 minutes, needs to be recorded and uploaded to the JJM-IMIS portal-Village details.
After this, the Sarpanch, Gram Panchayat/ President, Village Water and Sanitation Committee should give a certificate as per Annexure-3 with this circular. Such certificates should be uploaded immediately on the JJM-IMIS portal by the respective Zilla Parishads.
The Deputy Chief Executive Officer, Water and Sanitation, Zila Parishad will be ultimately responsible for uploading the information on the JJM-IMIS portal and mobile app after the concerned village is declared" Har Ghar Jal."
