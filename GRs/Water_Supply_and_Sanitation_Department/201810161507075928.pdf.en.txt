Government Decision: In view of the facts mentioned in the foregoing, the Hon. Old Dhamangaon (Ta. Dhamangaon Railway, G. Amravati) Rs. 1377.14 lacs (Rs. The revised administrative sanction under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) is being granted subject to the following conditions.
The scheme will be implemented by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
2) The terms and conditions of the original administrative approval of the scheme in the government decision and the order of technical approval should be strictly followed.
3) Once the scheme is completed, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran that the scheme has been actually completed.
As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should jointly conduct an inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The CAG report in 2013 showed that 21% of the water supply schemes in the state were closed due to non-payment of electricity dues due to lack of maintenance. Therefore, the water tariff should be increased from time to time as per the requirement to keep the scheme financially viable.
5) The accounts should be closed by transferring the scheme to the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad after testing the scheme.
6) The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will not get any assistance/ grant from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation of the scheme, its maintenance, repairs (including special repairs), depreciation in the scheme or for the renovation of machinery.
7) Action should be taken according to the prevailing policy on open defecation.
It will be the responsibility of the Maharashtra Life Authority to ensure that the scheme is not rescheduled.
