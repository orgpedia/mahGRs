conditional condition
The fund should be spent only for the implementation of regional tap water supply schemes approved to be revived under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme.

V. The material progress for the purpose for which the funds have been disbursed should be submitted along with the monthly expenditure report every month. The accounts of the allocation of funds should be kept up to date at the level of the office that collects and disburses the grants in accordance with the procedures set out in the records. Similarly, the expenditure incurred every three months should be verified with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed certificate should be submitted to the government immediately. Also, the controlling officer should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds under any circumstances.
(vi) The District Council shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure made out of this grant. In this regard, vide No. The Accountant General, Maharashtra-1/ 2, Mumbai/ Nagpur, as well as the officers authorized in this department, will have the right to see the books and accounts of the Zilla Parishad for audit, so that the government can ensure that the guidelines set by the government under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Program are being fulfilled by the Zilla Parishad. Also, the District Council will provide full support and facilities to the Accountant General so that he can carry out his work properly.
The expenditure of the grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the report of the reconciliation should be submitted by the Zilla Parishad to the Office Papu-19/ Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai on the prescribed dates. Similarly, the utility certificate should be submitted to the Accountant General's Office, Mumbai/ Nagpur and Papu-19 Office.
