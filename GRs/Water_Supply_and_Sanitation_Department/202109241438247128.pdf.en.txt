Jal Jeevan Mission is being implemented in the State since 2019. The central government. The operational guidelines of Jal Jeevan Mission have been issued on 25.12.2019.
Based on this, guidelines have been issued for implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission in the state as per the decision of the concerned government.
Jal Jeevan Mission is intended to be a mass movement for successful implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission. For efficient operation and long-term impact of schemes under Jal Jeevan Mission, the Gram Panchayat/ Village Water& Sanitation Committee at village level must take the responsibility of planning, management and implementation of the scheme at the initiative. The Jal Jeevan Mission will help in creating a mass movement if a sense of ownership is created among the people regarding the resources generated through the schemes under the Jal Jeevan Mission. Therefore, the matter of providing information about the Jal Jeevan Mission in the villages is under the consideration of the government.
The government circular has been issued subject to strict adherence to social distancing and guidelines issued by the government from time to time regarding Covid-19. Regarding holding of Online/ Offline Gram Sabha on 2 nd October, 2021, the Department of Rural Development has issued Reference No. Advice is given in 2. In this Gram Sabha, the topic of Jal Jeevan Mission should be included and awareness should be created about Jal Jeevan Mission. In this Gram Sabha, the matters mentioned in Annexure-A will be discussed.
