The period from 1st March, 2020 to 28th February, 2021 under the Government Order dated September, 2020 and from 1st March, 2021 to 31st August, 2021 under the Government Order dated 26th February, 2021 read with Rule No. 7 The Government decision dated 30 th August, 2021 has been approved for continuation from 1 st September, 2021 to 28 th February, 2022.
As per the Government Resolution dated 22.02.2022 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said Review but not included in the rolls.
Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 12 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 10 temporary posts for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell at the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) establishment and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalswarajya Project have been created under the Jalswarajya Phase-II programme for the duration of the programme and are temporary posts at the department's establishment.
As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes related to providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 12 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 22.02.2022 of the Finance Department read with the above, the Government is approving the continuation of 12 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as shown in Form A attached with this Government Decision.
