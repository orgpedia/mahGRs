Preamble In the financial year 2017-18, in respect of Central share of subsidy received from the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP), a demand No. Y-02, Accounts Head 2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 789, Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, 01, Scheme under Scheduled Caste Appropriation, (01) (01) National Rural Drinking Water Programme (SC Sub-Plan) (Central Share), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) (2215 9844).
Government Decision: In the financial year 2017-18, for the above mentioned components for tap water supply schemes, an amount of Rs. 20,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Crores only) is being sanctioned and released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran through Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) for coverage and water quality component on the following terms.
Conditions: (1) The Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is to spend this grant in the financial year 2017-18 only under the Scheduled Caste Appropriation as per the instructions of the Central Government for coverage and water quality component in respect of tap water supply schemes in rural habitations having majority of/ dominated by SC population.
(ii) The respective implementing agencies shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure incurred from these grants.
The Accountant General, Maharashtra- 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur as well as the officer authorised in this section shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts of the concerned enforcement agencies for audit so as to enable the Government to ensure that the above conditions/ conditions are being complied with by the concerned enforcement agencies. The concerned enforcement agencies shall provide full support and facilitation to the Accountant General to enable him to discharge his functions in a proper manner.
