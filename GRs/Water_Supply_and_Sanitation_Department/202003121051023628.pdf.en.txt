The 2 temporary posts have been approved to be continued from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 as per the decision of the Government dated 11.10.2019 in the said Chapter No.3.
As per the Government Resolution dated 12.02.2020 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020, which are included in their rolls and have been created after the approval of the said Review but have not been included in the rolls. Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 10 temporary posts in the Improvement Assistance and Project Management Cell of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalaswarajya Project. The government is taking the following steps:
The government has decided to provide clean and adequate drinking water to the people of the state. Jalswarajya Phase-II is being implemented with World Bank funding through the Improvement Support and Management Cell under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. For this work to be carried out properly, 10 temporary posts are required for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarojya Project.
Phase 2 of the project is expected to be completed by March 2020. The government is committed to provide clean and adequate drinking water to the people of the state. Preparation of Annual Action Plans in Urban/ Rural areas of all Revenue Divisions of the State, Shortage Relief Programmes, Rural/ Urban Water Supply, World Bank funded projects, Sant Gadgebaba Gramin/ Urban Sanitation Mission, Swachh Bharat Mission (GRAM), Solid Waste Management, Jal Jeevan Mission, various programmes of Ground Water Survey Development System are being implemented by this Department and many works are yet to be completed.
The 60th meeting of the Empowered Committee for implementation of Externally Aided Water Supply and Sanitation Programme was held under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department. As per point 5 of the minutes of the meeting, the programme under Jal Swarajya Phase-II was launched in June, 2020
