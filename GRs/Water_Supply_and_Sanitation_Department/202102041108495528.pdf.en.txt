National Rural Drinking Water Programme Hingani (Ta. The budget of the tap water supply scheme at Selu, G. Wardha has been accorded the basic administrative approval by the Chairman and Chairman, Water Management and Sanitation Committee, Zila Parishad, Wardha, vide order No. 1.
As per Reference No. 2, the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad, Wardha has accorded technical approval to the original budget. As per Reference No. 3, the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad, Wardha has given revised technical approval to the budget of the said scheme. Accordingly, the Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad, Wardha had submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference Letter No. 4 for administrative approval of the revised plan and budget of the present tap water supply scheme. Accordingly, in the original plan of the present scheme, the diameter of the supply well was increased as per the suggestion of the Assistant Geologist.

Government Decision No. Rugrape-2020/ PR No. 88/ Papu 13, the Government was considering to give administrative approval to the revised budget and plan of the scheme as the other components of the scheme were being increased as per the requirement in order to provide 100% tap connections in the villages and the pipeline of the scheme was broken in the highway work being carried out by the Public Works Department.
Government's decision:
M. Hingani (Ta. Administrative approval is being accorded to the revised cost estimate of Rs. 155.45 lakh (Dhobal) for the tap water supply scheme at Selu, G. Wardha subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad concerned shall be responsible to take over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad, Wardha that the scheme has been actually completed.
As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
The necessary planning regarding 100% functional tap connections should be done by the Gram Panchayat.
