(ii) The works of the scheme should be done by inviting e-tender in the prescribed manner. Bids with higher prices will not be accepted. It should be ensured that the work of the scheme is completed within the administrative approved cost. If the cost of the scheme increases, the state government will not be responsible for it and such schemes will be eligible for cancellation at any stage. Disciplinary action will also be taken against the concerned at the government level.
No tender should be invited for the scheme without obtaining necessary land acquisition and all other permissions (Water Reservation Permit of Water Resources Department, Forest/ Railway/ Road Crossing etc.). If the tender is invited without fulfilling these conditions, the administrative approval given under this government decision will be considered canceled.
(iv) The works of the scheme should be completed in a time bound and planned manner in a phased manner at the approved cost.
V. Other works should not be started until the origin of the plan is developed. Other works in the scheme should be carried out only after making sure that there is enough water available in the source.
(vi) In order to meet the expenditure for operation and maintenance of the scheme, the concerned Gram Panchayat shall fix the rate of water tap for domestic, non-domestic and institutional tap holders as mentioned in the proposal for implementation of the scheme and increase it from time to time.
