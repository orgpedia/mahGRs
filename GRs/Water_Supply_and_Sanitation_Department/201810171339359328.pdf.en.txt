Preamble: The Planning Department has issued instructions to close down the schemes related to Water Supply and Sanitation Department which have not made any provision/ expenditure for the last 3 years. Creation of a separate unit for the externally funded, German funded KfW project in the Project Planning and Control Room of this Department and creation of designation for the said unit vide Reference No. The decision has been taken by the government. As per the instructions of the Planning Department, the matter of closing the" District Water Management Cell" scheme was under the consideration of the government.
The German-funded KfW project" Apna Pani" was launched on a pilot basis in three districts of Pune, Aurangabad and Ahmednagar in Maharashtra. The project has been in the works since 2011. The District Water Management Cell (K.F.W.) (1111080010) created for this scheme was being provided under the account head 22151961.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: JASWAPR-1217/ PR. NO. 326/ PAPU-11, DATED:- 17.10.2018
No expenditure has been incurred on District Water Management Cell (22151961) scheme in all the three years 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18. Accordingly, as per the instructions of the Planning Department, the closure of the District Water Management Cell (22151961) scheme is being approved and the Government is giving approval to reduce the above scheme from the District Water Management Cell (K.F.W.) (1111080010) scheme system.
