The Proposal:
In pursuance of the letter dated 18/11/2020 of the Central Government at Reference No. 2, (Annexure A- Letter from the Central Government at Reference No. 2), to implement the pilot projects on cooperative basis (on Banas Kata, Gujarat basis) in the State and the Gobardhan projects at the State level during the period from 2021-2022 to 2022-25, accordingly, the State level technical machinery required for these projects will be empanelled for the approval of the proposals, approval of the budget proposals of the pilot projects at the State level. A committee headed by the Chief Secretary, Maharashtra State, Mantralaya, Mumbai, and comprising officials from various departments of the state with representation in the matter of Gobardhan, was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
In view of the above directions of the Central Government, the implementation of pilot projects on cooperative basis (Banas Kata, Gujarat basis) in the State and Gobardhan projects at the State level during 2021-22 to 2022-25 will be taken up.

Committee's mandate:
(i) To identify and finalize the State level pilot Gobardhan projects and grant final approval to the projects for successful implementation of Gobardhan programme.
(ii) State level facilitation mechanism for pilot Gobardhan project.
(iii) Ensure planning of funds for the project by aligning the funds of Convergence like MNRE, 15th Finance Commission, Cooperative Dairy Development Societies/ Gaushalas, etc.
4) To direct the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, as the implementing agency of the State Level Pilot Gobardhan Project, for effective implementation of the project.
