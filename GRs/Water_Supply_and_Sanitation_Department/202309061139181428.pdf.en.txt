Provisional promotion to the post (Group-A) is being made subject to the following conditions: The promotion is being made subject to the condition that no court case or departmental inquiry is proposed or pending against the promoted officer. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental enquiry/ court case is registered on the date of this order, the said promotion order shall stand automatically cancelled.
The provisions of the Revenue Department Allotment Amendment Rules, 2021 are applicable for the appointment of these officers to the Maharashtra Government Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts by direct service and promotion and the promoted officers must complete a minimum of three years of service after promotion in the allotted revenue department.
Action will be taken in accordance with the Government Decision, General Administration Department, dated 12.09.2016 in respect of the officers who refuse promotion in the said promotion order.
The concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad should relieve their officers immediately (within a maximum of seven days) to join the promoted post and submit a report to the government. The concerned promoted officer should join the new place of posting after being relieved from the present post with minimum period of joining without going on leave. The concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency should appoint the promoted officers without delay and submit a report to the government.
