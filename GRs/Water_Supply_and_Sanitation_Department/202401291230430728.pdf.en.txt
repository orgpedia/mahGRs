Government Circular: -
The Department of General Administration has issued a comprehensive policy as per the notification in this regard for preparation and publication of annual reports of officers / employees of the government service. The category "Senior Geologist" in the Ground Water Surveillance and Development System is Group-A. A temporary checkpoint has been created indicating the status of the 01.01.2024 checkpoint and this checkpoint is attached to this circular as Annexure-A.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Surveillance and Development System, Ground Water Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to consider this circular as included in the accompanying list and submit it.
It should be brought to the notice of all officers working in the department, working in the subordinate office of the system, retired from service and on deputation from the establishment of the system or otherwise changed cadre. If there are any genuine errors or omissions in the report, the officer concerned should submit his / her report through the Directorate.
