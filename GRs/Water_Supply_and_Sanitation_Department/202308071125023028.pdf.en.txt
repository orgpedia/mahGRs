Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year as per the notification at No. 1 under reference. The provisional seniority of the category" Assistant Geophysicist" Group-B in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01.01.2022 to 01.01.2023 has been notified vide Reference No.3. Considering the objections received in this regard, a provisional final seniority chart showing the status of Assistant Geophysicist, Group-B cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01.01.2022 to 01.01.2023 has been prepared. This seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this circular.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to withdraw this circular from the service of the subordinate office of the Agency, which is included in the accompanying seniority notice and is functioning in his department.
