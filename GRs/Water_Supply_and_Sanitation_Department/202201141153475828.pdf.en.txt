The Proposal:
Government to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of officers and employees of Maharashtra Life Authority and ETP charges to be deposited with Maharashtra Life Authority.
Cabinet takes Policy decision to deposit Technical Charges with Government dated 16.03.2017

Government Resolution No: Budget-1421/ P.No.70/ Papu-23
A Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017 has been issued in this regard. A total amount of Rs. 105.2806 crore has been released to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2021-22 under the head 22158041 as grant in aid towards payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 37,21,94, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Crores Twenty One Lakh Four Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority as grant under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22150041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salaries and allowances of its officers/ employees under this Government decision subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Decision shall be used for the salary/ allowances of the employees of the regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the year 2021-22. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
Technical charges and ETP charges due to the Authority at the end of January, 2022 for the year 2021-22 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
(a) The Department of Finance. As per the directions given by the Government Circular dated 22.11.2021, the Authority should take an undertaking from the concerned employee regarding the overpayment amount.
