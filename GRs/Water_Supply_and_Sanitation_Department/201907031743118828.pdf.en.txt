1.The transferred officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their respective offices and come to join. The head of the office and the establishment officer concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an officer without fixing a mandatory waiting period.
(2) The officers being posted under the said order shall be required to join immediately at the place of their posting. If any officer fails to appear at the place of posting or tries to directly or indirectly pressurize the officer to change the transfer order, it should be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officer. Such officers will be liable for disciplinary action as the act violates Rule 3 (3) and Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
These orders will come into force with immediate effect. The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency is being informed to immediately submit to the government the information whether the transferred officers have been relieved of their duties and how they have joined the transferred office.
