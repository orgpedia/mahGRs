The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 04/02/2005 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development machinery under this Department. The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune has requested the Government to extend the temporary posts of 273 posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency from the posts remaining after the review of the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency to this department for the implementation of the assigned schemes, machinery, etc. on the subject of water supply and keeping in view the urgency of the work.
A total of 273 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department in head" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 182 in head" 2702 Small Irrigation Managers" were approved to be continued from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020 as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2.

As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 1, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020. Considering the reasons mentioned by the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune for extending the temporary posts and the provision in the Government Resolution No. 1 under the reference of the Finance Department, the matter of extension of 273 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble and the urgency of the work in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, this Government has decided to continue 273 temporary posts in the establishment of Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020.
Approval is being given for continuation of 273 temporary posts indicated in" Annexure-A" on the establishment of Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The said extension is being granted subject to the assumption that other temporary posts, excluding the executive posts, are not vacant for more than six months.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
(3) As from the date on which the officer/ employee holding the post of Superintendant is absorbed in the other posts, the Superintendents post shall stand automatically abolished.
The expenditure for the aforesaid temporary posts shall be met from the provision made under the head of accounts referred to in Annexure-B for the year 2020-21.
