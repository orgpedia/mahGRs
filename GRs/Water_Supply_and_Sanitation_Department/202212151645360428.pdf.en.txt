The Proposal:
The funds made available by the Finance Department on Budget Control System for the financial year 2022-23 under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for Implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" for meeting the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishads have been disbursed to the Zila Parishads under the Government Decision No.
Now the matter of disbursing the next salary fund to all the Zila Parishads as well as the travel fund to the respective Zila Parishads fulfilling the prescribed terms and conditions is under the consideration of the Government, taking into account the total salary expenditure of the Zila Parishads.

Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (22151843)," a total grant of Rs. 4,41,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Forty-one Lakhs only) to the officers of Class-I and Class-II on the establishment of Zila Parishad Rural Water Supply Department, 1, Rs. 4,36,00, 000/- for salary and 11, Rs. 5,00,000/- for domestic travel expenses, as shown in the Statement A accompanying this Government Decision.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. Also, the expenditure incurred is in addition to the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's Office.
Take breaks from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.

The terms and conditions mentioned in this circular are being fulfilled.
