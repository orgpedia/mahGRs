The State Government has decided to allocate Rs. Subsidy coverage of Rs. 44.00 crore (Rupees Forty Four Crores only) is being sanctioned and released by the Budgetary System for this component (BEAMS) subject to the following conditions.
The grant is to be spent by the implementing agencies on priority basis for completion of works of the schemes in the villages/ wards covered under the annual action plan fixed under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme as well as for the progress of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes in which Government participation is mandatory.
2) Please be advised that this fund is required to be spent in the financial year 2019-20 only.
(3) The implementing agency shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure made out of this grant.
The Accountant General, Maharashtra- 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur, as well as the officers authorized by this Department, shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts of the implementing agencies for audit, so that the Government may ensure that the guidelines laid down by the Central Government under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme are being fulfilled by the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Also, the Accountant General will be provided with full support and facilities to enable him to carry out his work properly.
(5) The Zila Parishad shall reconcile the expenditure of the said grant with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's Office and the report of the reconciliation shall be submitted to the Office of the Commissioner, Papu-10/ Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, on the prescribed date.
