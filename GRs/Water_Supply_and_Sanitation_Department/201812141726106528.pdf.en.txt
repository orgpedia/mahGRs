In his letter dated 13.12.2018, the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for a temporary water supply scheme worth Rs. 90,25,000/- for the period of 2018-19 under the scarcity measures scheme for Mouje Dhakephal, Dilapur, Dhupkheda, Bedgaon, Taherpur and Nandalgaon, Taluka Paithan, District Aurangabad.
To grant administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures outside the prescribed financial and physical parameters in urban and rural areas to mitigate drinking water scarcity.

The Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department in its meeting held on 14.12.2018 discussed the proposal in detail and approved the proposal in the meeting. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Considering the facts mentioned in the preamble of the Government decision, the proposal of temporary water supply scheme of Mauja Dhakephal and 5 villages, Taluka Paithan, District Aurangabad at a cost of Rs. 90,25,000/- (Rupees Ninety Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) is being given administrative approval under scarcity subject to the following conditions: 1. The Collector, Aurangabad shall first declare the drinking water shortage in the areas of temporary water supply scheme of Mauje Dhakephal, Dilapur, Dhupkheda, Bedgaon, Taherpur and Nandalgaon, Taluka Paithan, District Aurangabad.
2) The temporary water supply scheme mentioned in the proposal should be implemented by the Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner. Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (10 days).
The tender process must be completed by December 30, 2018. Approval of the scheme will be cancelled if the work order is not issued by this date.
It is necessary to complete the work of the scheme by February 15, 2019 and start water supply from the scheme. However, if the water supply is not resumed by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
After the works mentioned in the proposal of this scheme are completed by the Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done by the Zilla Parishad.
8) The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. Subsequently, 30% of the funds should be disbursed considering the progress of the scheme at the level of Divisional Commissioner. The remaining 20% will be paid after the project is completed.
