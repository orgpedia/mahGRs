Conditions

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. GRAPAUP-2020/ P.No.10/ PAPU18 2. The original administrative approval accorded to the scheme as well as the terms and conditions mentioned while granting the revised technical approval should be strictly adhered to.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation to the effect that the scheme has been actually completed by the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Nanded. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
7. Action to be taken as per the prevailing policy on trespass.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
The enhanced cost of Rs. 6.18 lakh (Rupees Six Lakh Eighteen Thousand only) in the revised cost of the scheme should be met from the sanctioned grant under the head Demand No.Y-02,2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 102 Rural Water Supply Programme, National Rural Drinking Water Mission (Jal Jeevan Mission) (State Share) (22152071) and the final expenditure thereunder.
