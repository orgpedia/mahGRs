The Proposal:
The government has taken a policy decision to take responsibility for the salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority. Approved by the Cabinet
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Majipra-1118/ PR No.114/ Papu-23
Accordingly, a Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017 has been issued.
To pay the salaries and allowances of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority.
A budgetary provision of Rs. 185,61,00, 000/- has been made for the financial year 2018-19 under the head 22150041 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and 100% of the funds have been made available on the budgetary system by the Finance Department vide Circular No. A grant of Rs. 125,21,29, 000/- towards salary/ allowances for the period April, 2018 to January, 2019 has so far been disbursed to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as per the Government decision vide Circular Nos.3, 4, 5 and 7. Considering the remaining period of the financial year, the matter of disbursement of necessary funds to the Authority is under consideration of the Government.
Further, the Dearness Allowance of Government employees has been increased to 142% with effect from January, 2018 and the arrears have been made admissible as per the decision of the Government in (8). Accordingly, the matter of providing necessary funds to pay the arrears of dearness allowance of the employees of Maharashtra Life Authority is under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The amount of Rs. 26,85,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Eighty Five Lakhs only) out of the amount budgeted for the year 2018-19 under the head Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in respect of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (2215A041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the month of February and March, 2019 to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and arrears of Dearness Allowance (DA) of 3% from January, 2018
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for payment of salaries/ allowances to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran on regular establishment for the year 2018-19 as well as for payment of arrears of Dearness Allowance with effect from January, 2018 with an increase of 3%.

The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority in January and February, 2019 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Patil, Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. C. B. Nikam, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department as Controlling Officer.
