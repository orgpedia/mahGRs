Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year.
The provisional final seniority of the Deputy Director (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development Agency dated 01/01/2018 has been published vide Government Circular No.4 hereunder. The General Administration Department, as per the instructions issued under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, has included the names of the officers appointed by nomination in the year 2018 to the post of Deputy Director (Group-A) and the officers appointed by promotion and prepared a provisional additional seniority list showing the status of the Deputy Director (Group-A) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2019, 01/01/2020 and 01/01/2021. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the department's establishment. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority information, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. Facts in the statement of the officer The Directorate should examine the service book and other available records of the officer and submit it to the government along with the opinion of the Director before July 15, 2021, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority notice by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the name of the officer in the seniority list shall be considered for determining seniority in the cadre of Deputy Director only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
