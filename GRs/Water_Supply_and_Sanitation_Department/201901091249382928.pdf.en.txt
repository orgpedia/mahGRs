The Proposal:
The Government shall be responsible for the salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers and employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority and the ETP charges to be deposited with the Maharashtra Life Authority.
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to deposit the Technical Fee with the Government and accordingly the Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017 has been issued. A budget provision of Rs. 185,61,00, 000/- has been made for the financial year 2018-19 under the head 22151041 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. 70% of these funds are budgeted by the Department of Finance under Circular (2)

Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 12,52,22, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Fifty Two Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply, 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (2215A041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the month of January, 2019, out of the amount budgeted in 2018-19, subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for payment of salaries/ allowances to the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2018-19. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
(b) Since the Government has not accepted any dues in respect of salary/ allowances as on 23.03.2017, the Fund should not be utilised for any dues in respect of salary/ allowances as on that date.
The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority in December, 2018 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Patil, Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. C. B. Nikam, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department as Controlling Officer.
