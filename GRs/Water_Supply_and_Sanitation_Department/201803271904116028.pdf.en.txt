The matter of disbursement of funds to Divisional Commissioner, Amravati from the funds remaining with the Department out of the funds disbursed by the (Relief and Rehabilitation) Department vide its Government Resolution dated 16.10.2017 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 1056.16 lakh (Rs.10 crore, fifty six lakh, sixteen thousand only) is being sanctioned and released to Divisional Commissioner, Amaravati through Budget Distribution System subject to the terms and conditions as laid down in the prevailing Standing Government Resolution and the following conditions, to meet the expenditure incurred/ to be incurred on the measures to be taken for drinking water scarcity in Amaravati Division as per the norms laid down by the Government for drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas.
Terms and Conditions
1) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati shall ensure that the said funds are disbursed to the respective District Collectors in accordance with the scheme.
The funds shall be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas for the scarcity period 2016-17 and 2017-18 and for the payment of pending dues during this period. Care should be taken to ensure that the funds are not used for other short-term measures.
3) Both the measures mentioned in the government decision are being implemented by the Maharashtra Life Authority, the funds for those measures should not be distributed to the District Council, the concerned District Collector should immediately distribute the funds to the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, who is implementing the scheme.
4) Further, while seeking funds for the measures to be taken for mitigation of scarcity, the subject-wise demand as to what are the proposed measures to be taken for mitigation of scarcity for what period, how much funds are required for it, as well as the funds disbursed in the current financial year, the expenditure incurred, the balance funds, etc. Besides, it should be noted that the funds will be approved in the future.
