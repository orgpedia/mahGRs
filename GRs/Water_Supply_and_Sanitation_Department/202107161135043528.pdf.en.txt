The Proposal:
A total amount of Rs. 65.2081 crore has been disbursed towards expenditure of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) for the financial year 2021-22 under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (2215 1881) to meet the expenditure of Salary Allowances on the establishments of Rural Water Supply Department in Zilla Parishads. Further, a total amount of Rs. 35.00 crore has been released to the concerned Zila Parishads as per the Government decision vide Note No.5 of the Notification No.31, 2020, towards expenditure on Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salary).

Considering this, the government is considering to disburse the next salary fund to the concerned Zila Parishads as well as the next non-salary fund to the Zila Parishads fulfilling the prescribed terms and conditions.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01), Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, 36 Grants under the head (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881), Rs. 11,45,00, 000/-for Auxiliary Grants (Salary) and Rs. 15,00,000/-for Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salary) totalling Rs. 11,60,00, 000/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Sixty Lakhs only) were sanctioned by the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad concerned.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. The said funds are being released subject to the deposit of the unutilized funds of the year 2020-21 under the aforesaid heads to the Government and the funds should not be drawn from the Treasury without the unutilized funds being deposited to the Government. The non-salaried fund should be used by the respective Zila Parishads as per requirement for meeting travel expenses, office expenses, lease, motor vehicles, electricity payments, petrol/ oil/ lubricants etc.
3. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business, Financial Regulations, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing and spending the disbursed funds from the Treasury. as well as finance

(5) The Government shall ensure that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed under the said Government Decision is submitted to the Government and care is taken to make further requisition for the required funds. The expenditure from the grant of accounts sanctioned by this Government under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Compulsory) (22151881) shall be spent and the same shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2021-22.

Further, these Government decisions are being issued within the scope of the powers given to the departments and the funds available on the BIMS system under the Government Circular, Finance Division No. 2021/ PR No. 48/ Finance-3, dated April 6, 2021 and dated June 24, 2021. The terms and conditions mentioned in the circular of the Finance Department are being fulfilled.
