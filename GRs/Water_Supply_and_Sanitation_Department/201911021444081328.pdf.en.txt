Reference No. for measures taken/ to be taken during the period of scarcity 2018-19 The Department has received a proposal for seeking funds under letter No. 4 to 11. For the financial year 2019-2020, the Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation) was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the funds demanded by the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nagpur and Maharashtra Life Authority from the funds disbursed to this department by the Government Decision No. 12.
Government's decision:
As per the norms laid down by the Government for drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas, the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nagpur and Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran are being sanctioned and released funds to the tune of Rs. 38098.82 lakhs (Rupees Three Hundred Eighty Crores, Eighty Nine Lakhs, Eighty Two Thousand Only) under the head of accounts 22450093 for the measures taken/ to be taken for drinking water scarcity in their respective areas, subject to the terms and conditions laid down in the extant Standing Government Resolution and the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions
1) The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur should distribute the funds directly to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad for taking measures under the scarcity in rural areas.
2) Urban measures which are being implemented through Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils.
For this, the Divisional Commissioner should distribute it to the concerned District Collector. The Collector should disburse the funds to the concerned Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Principal, Municipal Council.

3) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran should disburse the funds for the measures to be implemented by the Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran under the drinking water shortage to the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran.
The funds should be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas for the scarcity period 2018-19 and for the payment of pending dues during this period. Care should be taken to ensure that the funds are not used for other short-term measures.
5) The Divisional Commissioner should allocate funds on priority to the measures like well acquisition, tanker water supply and even if the proposal submitted by the Divisional Commissioner does not ask for the measures approved from the government level, these measures should also be allocated on priority to the scale of work as per the order of the concerned administrative approval.
The funds are being provided for the mitigation measures taken during the period of scarcity (October, 2019 to September, 2019) during 2018-19. From this fund, the payments made before March, 2019 should be paid in full. The rest of the funds (periodically to be spent on further activities). Allocate funds accordingly.
7) It should be ensured that the funds are not kept in the personal ledger account or bank account and are distributed directly to the contractors through electronic clearing services.
8) While disbursing the funds, it should be ensured that the funds have not been disbursed for the related measures earlier.
9) The funds for urban and rural measures being implemented by the Maharashtra Life Authority should not be distributed to the Zilla Parishad. The funds should be immediately disbursed by the concerned District Collector to the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, who is implementing the scheme. Also, for the measures being implemented through the Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, the District Collector should distribute the funds to the concerned Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Principal, Municipal Council.
10) Further, while seeking funds for the measures to be taken for the relief of scarcity, a case-wise demand as to the period for which the proposed measures to be taken for the relief of scarcity, the amount of funds required for it, as well as information about the funds disbursed in the current financial year, the expenditure incurred, the balance funds, etc., and the utilization certificate therefor should be submitted. Besides, it should be noted that the funds will be approved in the future.
