The Proposal:
Consolidated guidelines have been passed by the General Administration Department under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference for recommending eligible officers/ employees for promotion in Government service. The General Administration Department has also instructed to form a Divisional Promotion Committee as per the provisions of this government decision.
In order to provide an opportunity to the Branch Engineer/ Junior Engineer working under the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad to be promoted to the post of Sub-Divisional Officer/ Engineer and Deputy Engineer (Graduate) in the Maharashtra Life Authority, 136 posts have been fixed under the Government Decision dated 31/07/2012. In order to be promoted to these posts, the Divisional Promotion Committee should be formed under the decision of the Government at Reference No. 2.

However, as the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1 in the context of the General Administration Department required the formation of a Divisional Promotion Committee under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, concerned department for the promotion to the posts of Group-A for non-ministerial cadres, the Government was considering passing a revised order cancelling the Government Decision No. 2 in the context of preparing the selection list for the promotion of Branch Engineer/ Junior Engineer working under the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad to the post of Sub-Divisional Officer/ Engineer and Deputy Engineer (Graduate) in the Maharashtra Life Authority.
Government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the following Divisional Promotion Committee is being constituted to prepare the selection list for the promotion of Branch Engineer/ Junior Engineer working under the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad to the post of Sub-Divisional Officer/ Engineer and Deputy Engineer (Graduate) in the Maharashtra Life Authority.
