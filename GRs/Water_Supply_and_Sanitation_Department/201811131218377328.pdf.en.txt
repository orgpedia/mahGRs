Introduction -
Water Supply and Sanitation Department (WSSD), Maharashtra is in the process of developing Information Communication and Tech. (ICT) based Integrated Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) system that encompasses the entire spectrum of activities in WATSAN sector, Maharashtra covering all sector schemes such as NRDWP, SBM - Gramin etc. and all sector institutions including the WSSO, GSDA, DWSM cells, ZPs, BRCs and GPs. DLI#1 of World Bank is linked with Integrated M&E system. While a project implementation committee (PIC) has been set up under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) wherein NIC, DIT and Finance Secretary are members, it is felt that the department needs an internal technical and administrative person to help to implement such large scale projects. There is need to have technical advisor from Government to assist the department from time to time on use of various technologies on a regular basis.
Resolution :
Taking into consideration the above background Shri. Moiz Hussain Hussain Ali Scientist-G & Deputy Director General, who is at present working with NIC is appointed as a Technical Advisor to Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) for the period of 6 months from 01.11.2018 upto 30.04.2019 extendable further after review of usefulness of this arrangement.

Scope of work of Technical Advisor:
1) Interaction with Consultant and system integrators of projects.
2) To assist Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) in reviewing and advising in the progress of various ongoing e-governance projects.
3) To advise Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) from time to time and would also participate in PIC as special invitee to take the advantage of his experience in these projects and seek future direction to expedite the implementation.
Conditions and Remuneration :
Technical Advisor will be invited in scheduled review meetings to oversee the progress of software work being done by the Consultant and system integrator and will be given Honorarium of Rs. 15000 and out of pocket expenses (Rs. 10000) per month to take care of transport needs etc.
