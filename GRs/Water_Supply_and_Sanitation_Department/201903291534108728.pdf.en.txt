The Proposal:
The provident fund of all the employees/ officers working with Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran since 1982-83 is deposited with the Government under Account Head-8336 Civil Deposit as a contribution/ statutory deposit from the Authority. The interest on the total amount deposited with the government at the end of March is paid at the interest rate of the provident fund in the prevailing manner under the head of accounts 2049 1268.
