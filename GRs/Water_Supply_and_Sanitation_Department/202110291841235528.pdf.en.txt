The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. Payment of Salaries and Allowances of Officers/ Employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran
The total grant-in-aid to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2021-22 under the head 2215A041 is Rs. 59.7477 crore.

It is distributed according to the governing decisions of 3, 4, 6, 7 and 8. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 45,53,29, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Five Crore Thirty Three Lakhs Thirty Nine Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22150041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for payment of salary and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority subject to the following conditions:
a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Decision will be used for the salary/ allowances of the employees of the regular establishment of the Maharashtra Life Authority for the year 2021-22.
be used. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
Technical charges and ETP charges due to the Authority at the end of October, 2021 for the year 2021-22 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Rajendra Madke has been appointed as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. Ram Gangje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been appointed as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
