The Proposal:
As per the Government decisions dated 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015, a total of 4022 permanent/ temporary posts have been sanctioned in Class-I to Class-IV cadres of Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions in the District Councils. As per the Government decision dated 18.3.2019, a total of 2726 temporary posts in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Division were extended from 1.3.2019 to 30.9.2019. The government is considering extending the temporary posts in rural water supply departments for the next period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes.

Government's decision:
As per the figure approved vide Government Decision, dated 4.3.2011 and dated 4.8.2015, a total of 2726 temporary posts in various cadres as shown in the attached' Statement A" working in Rural Water Supply Departments and Sub-Divisions under Zila Parishads are being allowed to continue from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 subject to the assumption that the posts are not vacant for a period of more than six months and no irregular appointments have been made to those posts.
The Department of Finance has issued instructions to abolish 25% of the sanctioned posts of Group-D cadre as per Para 1 (5) of the Government Resolution dated 14.1.2016. Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 22.3.2018, the existing 81 vacancies in Group-D cadre in the Rural Water Supply Department of the Zilla Parishad have been abolished and the temporary posts have not been extended under this Government decision.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of a total of 278 temporary posts of Gazetted Officers of Class-I and Class-II in these posts should be covered under the head of Accounts 2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 01 Water Supply, 001 Operation and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843) from the provision approved in the financial year 2019-2020.
