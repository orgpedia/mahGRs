Preamble: Divisional Commissioner, Amravati has approved the implementation of the Mouje Dhotra Nandai, T.T. under the scarcity mitigation scheme for the period 2017-18. A proposal of Rs. 45,22,390/- has been submitted to the Government for temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme in Deulgaon, District Buldana. This proposal was considered for approval in the meeting held on 03.04.2018 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department for granting administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures in urban and rural areas beyond the prescribed financial and physical parameters for mitigation of drinking water scarcity.
This proposal was discussed in the meeting of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department on 03.04.2018.

The proposal was approved. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
In view of the facts mentioned in the Preamble of the Government Resolution, administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal of Temporary Supplementary Tap Water Supply Scheme at Mauje Dhotra Nandai, Taluka Deulgaon, District Buldana at a cost of Rs. 45,22,390/- (Rupees Forty Five Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Three Hundred and Ninety only) subject to the following conditions: 1. The Collector, Buldana shall first declare that there is scarcity of drinking water in the village Mauje Dhotra Nandai, Taluka Deulgaon.
2) The supplementary tap water supply scheme mentioned in the proposal should be implemented by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Buldana.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner.
Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by April 18, 2018.
If the tender process is not completed by April 18, 2018, the approval of the scheme will be considered canceled.
The water supply from the scheme should be started by completing the works of the scheme by May 15, 2018. However, if the water supply from the scheme does not start by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
8) After the work mentioned in the proposal of the scheme is completed by the Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done by the concerned Gram Panchayat.
9) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. The remaining funds will be released after the completion of the project.
10) If the floating pump and pipes used for the said solution are not used after the shortage period, then the said pump and pipes must be removed and kept in the custody of the Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad, Buldana. So that it is possible to use them in the same place or other places in the next shortage period as per the requirement.
