Introduction: An ambitious innovative concept of planting 50 crore trees to reduce the intensity of the problem of global warming, climate and weather change has been approved at the government level. As per the order of the Revenue and Forest Department, the tree plantation program has been taken up in the state of Maharashtra for three years from 2017 to 2019, with a target of planting 50 crore trees in these three years. Each department is assigned an annual goal. As per the above decision of the Department of Revenue and Forest, the funds for the implementation of the scheme" Tree Plantation and Maintenance" in each department shall be made available to the department every year at the rate of 0.5 percent of the total budgetary allocation from the" Programme Expenditure." The project was proposed to be implemented under the Water Supply Department. Accordingly, a new head of accounts for the provision in the budget for 2019-2020 was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 25.7.2018 in the context of Revenue and Forest Department, administrative approval is being given to implement the scheme of" Tree Plantation and Maintenance" in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department and to open new head of accounts as follows to provide funds within the limit of 0.5% of" Programme Expenditure" available to the Department every year.
