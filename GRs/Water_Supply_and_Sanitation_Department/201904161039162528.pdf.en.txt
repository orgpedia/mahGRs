The Proposal:
The Interim Budget for 2019-20 has been presented to the Legislative Assembly and the Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 to meet the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 for the period up to the passing of the Appropriation Bill. The Bill has been converted into an Act and is published as Maharashtra Act No. 2 in the Government of Maharashtra, Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 8th March, 2019. Out of the budgetary provision for the year 2019-20, funds for the four months April, 2019 to July, 2019 have been released by the Department of Finance through a circular.
Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (2215 1843)" for the financial year 2019-20 under the head' Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for Implementation of Water Supply Programme (01)" for meeting the salary and non-salary expenses of Class 1 and Class 2 officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zilla Parishad.

A total budget provision of Rs. 1,23,38, 000/- (Rupees Forty Four Crores Forty Lakhs Seventy Thousand only) has been made for Rs. 44, 40,70,000/-.
Now, the Finance Department, vide the aforesaid circular, has made available on the Budgetary System, out of the said provision, an amount of Rs. 12,27,15, 000/-for the year 2019-20. Considering the funds released to the respective Zila Parishads during 2018-19, the Government is considering disbursement of salary funds for four months from April 2019 to July 2019.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2019-20, a total grant of Rs. 11,50,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) as shown in the' Statement A' accompanying this Government decision to meet the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Mandatory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
