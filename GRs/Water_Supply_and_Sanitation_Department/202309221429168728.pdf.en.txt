The Proposal:
In order to ensure smooth implementation of rural water supply schemes in ZPs, a total of 4147 posts in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions of ZPs have been earmarked as per Government Decision dated 28.12.2022 and out of these 2496 posts have been earmarked as permanent posts and remaining 1651 as temporary posts as per Government Decision dated 20.09.2023. The Department of Finance, vide Government Resolution dated 31.08.2023 and Corrigendum dated 6.09.2023, has decided to fill up all the temporary posts in all the Administrative Divisions as per their respective pattern.

Accordingly, the government is considering to extend the temporary posts in rural water supply departments for the next period to ensure the proper implementation of rural water supply schemes in the district councils.
Government's decision:
As per the figure approved vide Government Resolution, dated 28.12.2022, a total of 1651 temporary posts in various cadres as shown in the attached" Return A" in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Divisions under Zila Parishads are being approved to be continued from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
