Preamble: Officers have been appointed to the post of" Junior Geologist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System for a period of two years as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 2 and 4. These appointments have been made by the first appointment in the government service on the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of officers/ employees in Government service and since the officers posted to the post of Junior Geologist Ground Water Survey and Development System have satisfactorily completed their probationary period of 2 years and the recommendation has been made by the Directorate under Reference No. 3 and 5.
