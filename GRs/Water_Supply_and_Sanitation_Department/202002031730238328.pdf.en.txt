Preamble: As per the Government decision on Reference No. 1, the rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by the Government departments has been prescribed. Paragraph 3.2 of the said rule book specifies the types of procurement for different amounts and the procurement committee is to be constituted under each administrative department/ office to enable all the administrative departments of the ministry to complete the procurement process as mentioned in paragraph 2.9.3. Therefore, the government is considering the formation of a new procurement committee at the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) level by cancelling the government decision at reference number 02.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: GOVERNMENT DECISION As mentioned above, at the level of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), a Procurement Committee is being constituted as follows to undertake the procurement process as per the procedure prescribed in the rule book prescribed under the relevant Government Decision.
