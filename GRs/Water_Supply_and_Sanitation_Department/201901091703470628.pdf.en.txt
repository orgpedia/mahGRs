Government's decision
Phulsingnagar (Vasanthnagar), Ta. Umri, G. Nanded, Rs.
The revised budget of Rs. 24,56,910/- (Rupees Twenty Four Lakhs Fifty Nine Thousand Nine Hundred and Ten only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
1) The scheme will be implemented through the District Council.
2) The terms and conditions of the original administrative approval of the scheme in the government decision and the order of technical approval should be strictly followed.
As per the CAG report of 2013, 21 per cent of the water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills due to lack of maintenance. Therefore, the water tariff should be increased from time to time as per the requirement to keep the scheme financially viable.
4) The accounts should be closed by transferring the scheme to the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad after testing the scheme.
5) The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad concerned will not get any assistance/ grant from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation of the schemes, their maintenance, repairs and maintenance, special repairs, depreciation in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
6) Action should be taken according to the prevailing policy on open defecation.
7) It will be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the scheme is not revised.
The remaining work of the project should be completed within 6 months.
2. The revised cost of the scheme is Rs. 2,83,338/- (Rupees Two Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Thirty Eight only) from the State share/ District Planning Board/ Minimum Needs Programme under National Rural Drinking Water Programme.
3. Revised administrative approval for the scheme is to be accorded by the Finance Department. 30/2013 Regulations, Part-I of the Government Order, Sub-section-3, No. 6 (6), Paragraph No. 27 (a) as per the authority conferred on the department.
