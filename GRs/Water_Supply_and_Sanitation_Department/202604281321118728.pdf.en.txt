The Proposal: -
A policy decision has been taken to provide subsidy to the Zila Parishad by the Government for the salary of eligible employees working on Converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishments on Rural Water Supply Schemes under the Zila Parishad. The Cabinet has taken a meeting dated 17th November, 2022 and accordingly the Government Decision dated 30th January, 2023 has been issued. Further disbursement of funds to these Zila Parishads for regular salaries of the working staff is under consideration of the Government taking into account the expenditure incurred by the Zila Parishads so far.
The Government's Decisions:
Subsidy amounting to a total of Rs. 1,41,85, 000 / - (Rupees One Crore Forty One Lakh Eighty Five Thousand Only) is being disbursed to the following Zila Parishads for the regular salary of the employees of the converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishment of the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zila Parishad for the financial year "04, Retirement Expenditure under Account 22151914."
