The Proposal:
Out of total 2841 temporary posts on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishads, 1914 working temporary posts have been extended till 28.02.2021 as per the Government decision dated 29.10.2020. In this regard, the Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune vide letter dated 18.9.2020 has extended the tenure of 122 working temporary posts out of their total 143 temporary posts till 28.02.2021.

The request has been made by Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune vide letter dated 10.11.2020. Further, as per the communication dated 18.9.2020 from Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune, one post of Air Compressor Driver was shown as vacant by mistake. Therefore, as per the Government decision dated 29th October, 2020, the said post has not been extended. In the absence of extension of these vacant temporary posts, the salaries of the officers/ employees of the said Zila Parishad are pending from the month of September/ October.
Therefore, the matter of extending the tenure of these temporary posts on the establishment of the Rural Water Supply Department in the Pune Zilla Parishad is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Out of total 143 temporary posts on establishment of Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune, 122 working temporary posts have been extended till 28.02.2021. Now, as per the decision of the Government, one post of Deputy Engineer Mechanic, one post of Air Compressor Driver and two temporary posts of Senior Assistant on the establishment of Pune, Zilla Parishad, Rural Water Supply Department are being approved to continue from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of the temporary posts of Class-I and Class-II Gazetted Officers in these posts should be covered under the head of Accounts 2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 01 Water Supply, 001 Operation and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub-Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843) from the provision approved in the financial year 2020-2021.
Expenditure on salary and allowances of temporary posts of non-gazetted officers/ employees of Class-III and Class-IV should be shared under the head Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads (Local Area) (01) (05) Regular Establishment (2215 1881) under section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961, from the provision approved in the financial year 2020-2021.
The said Government Resolution, Finance Department, Government Resolution No. 2016/ Q. No.8/ 16/ A. The PUC is being issued as per the powers conferred on the administrative departments vide order dated February 12, 2020 and dated August 31, 2020.
