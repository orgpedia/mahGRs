At present, there is a lack of manpower in Vaso for the implementation of the water supply program. As there are fewer posts in the Superintending Engineer cadre, the chances of getting the post of Superintending Engineer on deputation are less. Therefore, the government was considering the matter of temporarily downgrading the post of Superintending Engineer in the Water and Sanitation Support Institution to the post of Executive Engineer. The revised profile of Vaso is under process and the post will be included separately thereafter.
