The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. An amount of Rs. 19,00,00, 000/- has been sanctioned as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the financial year 2020-21 under the head 22158041 towards payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. Accordingly, in the year 2020-21, the matter of disbursement of salary subsidy to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the above head of accounts for three months from April, 2020 to June, 2020 was under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 19,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty-nine Crores only) sanctioned in the year 2020-21 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salary and allowances of officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No. Y2,2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22150041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" is being disbursed to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under this Government decision subject to the following conditions.
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for payment of salaries/ allowances to the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2020-21. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
(b) The technical charges and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of April, 2020 for the year 2020-21 should be deposited with the Government immediately. Also, the fee due at the end of June, 2020 should be deposited with the government before July 5, 2020.
Abhay Mahajan, Joint Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been posted as Controller of Water Supply and Sanitation.
The grants sanctioned under this Government decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government from time to time. Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure. Accordingly, action should be taken for payment of salary as per the procedure mentioned therein for the period mentioned in the Government Circular No.Sanikya-2020/ PR No. 62/ Koshasra-5, dated 1st April, 2020 of the Finance Department.
