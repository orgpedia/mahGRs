The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. A total grant of Rs. 28.50 crore has been released to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2021-22 under the head 22150041 as grant in aid for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 18,88,29, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Thirty Nine Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant-in-aid to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salaries and allowances of its officers/ employees, subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Decision shall be used for the salary/ allowances of the employees of the regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the year 2021-22. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of July, 2021 for the year 2021-22 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Patil has been posted as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. R. M. Gengaje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been posted as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
4) Before drawing the funds from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
