The Proposal:
The reference No. 5 here. As per the Government Decision dated 27th March, 2018, the Member Secretary, Majipra (for schemes implemented through Majipra) and the Chief Engineer, Majipra, Regional Department (for schemes implemented through Zila Parishad) have been empowered to grant permission to take up works of sub-sections/ Phase-I and II together in the case of water supply schemes under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme, where there are permanent and sustainable surface water sources like rivers, dams, ponds etc.
The Chief Minister sanctioned Rs. Water supply schemes worth more than Rs. 5.00 crore are being implemented by Majipra. As the scope of these schemes is large, in order to complete all the works within the stipulated time, where sustainable surface sources like rivers/ dams/ ponds etc. are available and permanent water is available, if the physical progress of the work of Phase 1 and 2 of the scheme is at least 60 percent, then the sub-section/ Phase-3 of the scheme will be covered.

The matter of conferring the power to grant permission to take up the works to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Life Authority was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the facts mentioned in the Preamble, in order to ensure the implementation of the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme in a time-bound and effective manner, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is implementing water supply schemes in the areas of rivers, dams, ponds, etc.
The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being empowered to grant permission to take up works of sub-segment/ Phase-III of the scheme, if at least 60% of the physical progress of the works of Phase-I and II of the schemes is completed, provided that sustainable surface sources are available and permanent water is available.
