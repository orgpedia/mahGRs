The Proposal:
The diversity of rainfall, topography and adverse geo-stratigraphic conditions in the state of Maharashtra have naturally limited the availability of groundwater resources. As a result, the ground water level is decreasing day by day due to various reasons. Considering this situation, the Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Act, 2009 (Maharashtra Act No. 26 of 2013) has been promulgated to control the unlimited exploitation of ground water in the state, to develop and manage ground water in an integrated manner and to conserve public drinking water sources.
The draft rules, namely, the Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018, for carrying out the purposes of the Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Act, 2009, have been notified vide Notification No. 2, as under: The draft rules are open for public comments and if any person has any objection or suggestion regarding the draft rules, it should be sent to the Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, 7th Floor, Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital Building Complex, Near Crawford Market, Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mumbai 400 001 in writing or by e-mail. The draft rules of Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018 have been widely publicized and various seminars have been organized by the Water Supply and Sanitation Department and the offices under the control of the department. Through this, the government is receiving a large number of objections and suggestions regarding the draft rules. Considering these objections and suggestions, it was under the consideration of the government to form a committee to analyze these objections and suggestions in detail and accordingly determine whether the relevant rules in the draft rules need to be changed, and whether the objections and suggestions received by the government are appropriate to accept.
Government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble and the need to take action within the prescribed time frame to consider the large number of objections and suggestions received by the Government, the following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Shivajinagar, Pune to analyze the objections and suggestions received by the Government under the draft rules of Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018 and to give feedback to the Government on each of the objections and suggestions.
