The Proposal:
Junior Geologist (Group-B) in Ground Water Survey and Development System, recommended by Maharashtra Public Service Commission. 9300- 34800+ Grade Pay Rs. 4500/- plus dearness and other allowances as per rules, was appointed under the order at Reference No. 3 for a probation period of two years from the date on which the concerned candidate assumes charge. Among the total 33 candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for the post of Junior Geologist, the name of Mrs. Shubhangi Sahebrao Gurve was also included at No. 11.
Among the candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mrs. Shubhangi Sahebrao Gurve, a candidate from No. 11, has been recommended for the post of Junior Geologist.

The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) had reserved the post for women.
There is a provision for verification of certificates of women candidates recommended for the post reserved for women by the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune before their appointment. Accordingly, in the case of women candidates, provision has been made for grant of temporary appointment subject to verification of the said certificate if their certificates are not verified within one month as per the Government Decision, 2 (a) (8) of the Department of Women and Child Development, No. 82/2001/ MSEA-2000/ PR No. 415/ KA-2, dated 25th May, 2001. However, orders of posting of 31 candidates other than Ms. Gurve's name were issued vide order at Reference No. 3. Subsequently, vide letter dated 05/05/2015 from the Deputy Commissioner, Women Development, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune, Ms. Shubhangi Gurve was appointed as Junior Geologist under Government Order No. 2 under reference for a probation period of two years, after receiving the Validity Certificate for her appointment to the post reserved for women belonging to non-residential category.
Smt. Shubhangi Sahebrao Gurve, in her representation at Reference No.1, has requested the Government to provide the date 12/09/2014 as the reference date for seniority of the candidates and to revise the seniority list as per merit.
Government's decision:
In view of the aforesaid request made by Smt. Shubhangi Sahebrao Gurve, Junior Geologist, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency and the delay in appointing her to the post of Junior Geologist due to administrative reasons, it is hereby approved by this Government to appoint Smt. Shubhangi Sahebrao Gurve as Junior Geologist, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency vide Rule No. 5 (1)& (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 with the date 12/09/2014 as the date of appointment. However, this date will be valid only for seniority.
Smt. Shubhangi Sahebrao Gurve, Junior Geologist, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency has been appointed as Junior Geologist in accordance with Rule 5 (1)& (2) of Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 with the date 12/09/2014 as the date of appointment.
