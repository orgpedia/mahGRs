The Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (DWS& S) was contemplating to procure 25 computers, 6 printers, 21 multifunction printers, 13 scanners for making it a fully e-office.
Government's decision:
The government has taken a decision. As on 05.03.2021, the Project Implementation Committee has been reconstituted to establish internal computerization and e-governance in the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. The committee was constituted by the General Administration Department (Directorate of Information and Technology). The Government may monitor/ scrutinize/ approve e-governance project proposals of the Department above Rs.10 lakh and upto Rs.5 crore as mentioned in para 12.7 of the decision dated 23.09.2011. Accordingly, the Project Implementation Committee in its meeting held on 30.05.2023 has approved the procurement of 25 Computers, 6 Printers, 21 Multifunction Printers, 13 Scanners for Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
