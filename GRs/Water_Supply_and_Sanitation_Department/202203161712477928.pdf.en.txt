Preamble: The post of" Assistant Geologist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency has been appointed for a period of two years under the Government Resolution No. 2. These appointments have been made by the first appointment in the government service on the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of the officers/ employees of the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Deputy Director Ground Water Survey and Development System have satisfactorily completed their probationary period of 2 years and the same has been recommended by the Directorate under Reference No. 3, the Government was considering the matter of giving them a substitute appointment as regular" Assistant Geologist" by ending the probationary period of these officers.
