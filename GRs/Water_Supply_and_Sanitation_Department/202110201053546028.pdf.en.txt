Preamble: Expenditure on measures taken to alleviate water scarcity in rural areas of the State is met from the grants made available by this Department under the head (2215 A 195).
The Department has received the proposal for seeking funds from Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Navi Mumbai and Pune, Amravati and Nagpur divisions for the period October, 2020 to June, 2021 for the measures to be taken/ to be taken during the shortage period of 2020-21. During the financial year 2021-22, the funds budgeted under the head of Accounts (2215A 195) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Plan) were released to the Divisional Commissioner.

Terms and Conditions
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune, Amravati and Nagpur shall disburse the funds directly to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad for taking measures under scarcity in rural areas.
The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran shall disburse the funds to the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran for the measures to be taken by the Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran under the drinking water scarcity for the period 2020-21 (from October, 2020 to June, 2021) for the relief measures taken for drinking water scarcity in rural areas and for the payment of pending dues during this period. This money cannot be used for other projects.

The District Collector/ Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad/ Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall ensure that the various schemes/ water supply related works taken up in the previous year have been completed with the provision made for the relief of natural disasters.
12) The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ CEO, Zila Parishad should ensure that water is a vital/ essential item, there is no malpractice under it, the same scheme is not repeated every year and unnecessary expenditure is not incurred on it.
The Divisional Commissioner shall submit to the Government, every month, along with the monthly expenditure report, information on the physical progress of the district for the purpose for which the funds have been disbursed. The accounts of the allocation of funds should be kept up to date at the level of the office of the recipient and disburser of the grant in accordance with the procedures laid down in the records. Similarly, the information about the actual expenditure to be incurred every month should be submitted to the government by the 5th of the next month. The expenditure incurred every three months should be verified with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the certificate with quarterly verification should be submitted to this department immediately.
Also, the District Collector/ Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad as well as Member Secretary, Maharashtra Life Authority should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds under any circumstances. If any amount is not spent from the subsidy disbursed, the amount should be returned to the government within the stipulated time. Ask for the necessary funds. Don't ask for more money.
