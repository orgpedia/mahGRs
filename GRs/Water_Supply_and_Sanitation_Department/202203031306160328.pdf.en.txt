Preamble: Expenditure on measures taken to alleviate water scarcity in rural areas of the State is met from the grants made available by this Department under the head (2215 A 195).
The Department has received the proposal of Konkan Division seeking funds for the period October, 2020 to June, 2021 for the measures taken/ to be taken during the shortage period of 2020-21. In the financial year 2021, 22, the funds budgeted under the target of Account Head (2215A 195) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Plan) were under the consideration of the Government to disburse the funds for the measures demanded and actually completed by the Divisional Commissioner Konkan.
Government's decision:
The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan has been asked to take measures to address the drinking water shortage in his department as per the norms laid down by the government to address the drinking water shortage in rural areas.

Terms and Conditions
(1) The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan shall disburse the funds under the scarcity measures in rural areas directly to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad.
The funds should be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural areas for the period 2020-21 (i.e. October, 2020 to June, 2021) as well as for the payment of pending dues during this period. This money cannot be used for other projects.
3) The Divisional Commissioner should allocate funds on priority to the measures like well acquisition, tanker water supply and even if the proposal submitted by the Divisional Commissioner does not ask for the measures approved by the government, these measures should also be allocated on priority as per the order of the concerned administrative approval.
4) It should be ensured that the funds are disbursed directly to the contractors through Electronic Clearing Services/ RTGS, without keeping the personal ledger account or bank account.
5) While disbursing the funds, it should be ensured that the funds have not been disbursed to the concerned measures earlier.
