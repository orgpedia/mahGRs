The Proposal:
The funds have been released to the concerned Zilla Parishads under the head" Demand No.Y-2, 2215- Water Supply and Sanitation, 196- Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions, (01)- Gramin Tap Water Supply Scheme, (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees of Regular Establishments (Mandatory) (22151914), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" to meet the salary expenditure of the employees of Converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad for the financial year 2021-22. Considering the expenditure of these Zila Parishads so far, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the respective Zila Parishads for the salary and allowances of the employees for the financial year 2021-22. The following decision has been taken:

Government's decision:
A total amount of Rs. 64,00,000/- (Rupees Sixty Four Lakh only) is being disbursed to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the ZP for the financial year 2021-22 for the salary and allowances of 38 working employees mentioned in the accompanying statement on Converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishment in the Department of Rural Water Supply in Ratnagiri and Sangli ZP.
