Conditions
(1) All the remaining works should be completed within 3 months at the revised cost mentioned above.
(2) The terms and conditions mentioned in the original and revised administrative approval as well as in the revised and revised technical approval granted to the scheme shall be strictly complied with.
(3) On completion of the scheme, it shall be handed over to the concerned District Council. The concerned Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Board, New Panvel, Raigad that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned executive engineer, zilla parishad, executive engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, contractor and concerned gram panchayat officials should jointly conduct an inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
(4) The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned shall not receive any grant from the Government for the maintenance or renovation of machinery or depreciation of the scheme to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance and repair of the scheme.
(5) After the completion of the scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government body (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad) to transfer the scheme after testing the scheme, and the accounts of the scheme should be closed.
