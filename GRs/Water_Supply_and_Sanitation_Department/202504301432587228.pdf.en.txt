Conditions
1) The CEO / Chief Engineer will be responsible for completing all the remaining works within the prescribed time frame at the above mentioned revised cost.

3) On completion of the scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat / Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad that the scheme has actually been completed. Immediately after the actual completion of the scheme, a joint inspection of the scheme should be conducted by the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials and any defects found in such inspection should be corrected before handing over the scheme.
4) The Gram Panchayat / Zilla Parishad concerned shall not receive any assistance from the Government for meeting the recurring expenditure incurred every year for implementation, maintenance, repairs (including special modifications), reduction in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
5) On completion of the scheme, it will be mandatory for the local body (concerned Gram Panchayat / Zila Parishad) to implement the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
6) According to a CAG report in 2013, 21 per cent of water supply schemes in the state have been shut down due to poor maintenance / non-payment of electricity bills due to lack of these maintenance repairs. 7) Action should be taken in accordance with the prevailing policy on extortion.
8) It shall be the responsibility of the Zilla Parishad that the present scheme shall not be revised under any circumstances.
