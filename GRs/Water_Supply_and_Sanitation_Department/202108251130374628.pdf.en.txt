The Proposal:
A budget provision of Rs. 50.8148 crore has been made for the financial year 2021-22 under the head Demand Number Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for Implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" to meet the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishad.
Out of this provision, Rs. 14.8214 crore has been released to the Zila Parishads as grant in aid for payment of salaries as per the Government decision vide Notes 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 8 in the Budget. Now the district's

The government is considering the issue of disbursement of the next salary grant to the Zilla Parishads after taking into account the salary expenditure of the Parishads for the year 2021-22.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, a total grant of Rs. 1,60,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Lakh only) is being sanctioned to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the concerned Zilla Parishad as shown in the' Statement A' accompanying this Government decision to meet the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Mandatory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (22151843)."
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
4) Before drawing the funds from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed. Further, the Department of Finance should consider the suggestions given under the Government Decision dated 24.6.2021 in the context and cut down the expenditure.
