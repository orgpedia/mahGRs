Conditions
The Chief Executive Officer / Chief Engineer shall be responsible for completing all the remaining works within the prescribed time frame at the above mentioned revised cost.
2. The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the revised technical approval as well as the original administrative approval agreed upon for the scheme should be strictly adhered to.
3. On completion of the scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat / Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad that the scheme has actually been completed. Immediately after the actual completion of the scheme, a joint inspection of the scheme should be conducted by the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials and any defects found in such inspection should be corrected before handing over the scheme.
4. The Gram Panchayat / Zila Parishad concerned shall not receive any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred annually for implementation, maintenance, repairs (including special modifications), depreciation in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
On completion of the scheme, it will be mandatory for the local body (concerned Gram Panchayat / Zila Parishad) to implement the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
6. According to a 2013 (57) report, 21 per cent of water supply schemes in the state have been shut down due to poor maintenance / non-payment of electricity 001115. Hence, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat / Zilla Parishad to increase the water tariff as per requirement.
7. Action should be taken in accordance with the prevailing policy on corruption.
8. The District Council shall be responsible for ensuring that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
