Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year as per the Government Circular No. 1 and Notification No. 2. """ Senior Chemicals"" category in Ground Water Survey and Development System w.e.f. 01.01.2018." Provisional Common Seniority of 01.01.2021 has been issued vide Government Circular No. 3.
