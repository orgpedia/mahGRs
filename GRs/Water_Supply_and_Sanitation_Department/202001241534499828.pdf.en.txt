Preamble: The Government shall repay the loan taken by the Life Insurance Corporation after June 1968 for water supply and drainage schemes in the C category municipalities having a population of more than 20,000 as per the decision of the Government in the ratio of 50:50 for the first five years and thereafter for the remaining period. The amount of loan and interest paid by the government to the Life Insurance Corporation is the loan given by the government to the municipalities. But after the formation of Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran (formerly Maharashtra Water Supply and Sewerage Board) in 1977, the responsibility of the loan of the Life Insurance Corporation of Maharashtra

The grant is given to the Maharashtra Life Authority to pay the installments of the loan as it has been placed on the Life Authority. Under reference As per the procedure laid down under the Government Order dated 7th March, 1977, the instalments due to the Life Insurance Corporation on 1st June and 1st December every year are paid by the Maharashtra Life Authority. The grant sanctioned to the Maharashtra Life Authority is considered as a loan. Under reference The Maharashtra Life Authority will recover the loan from the municipality in the next 20 years on the same terms and conditions as the municipalities had borrowed from the Life Insurance Corporation as per the government decision of June 8, 1968.
However, the loan was not repaid to the Life Insurance Corporation of India as the loan from the municipalities (Life Insurance Corporation of India and loans taken by the Municipalities from the Government) was not repaid in full and the amount to be repaid to the Life Insurance Corporation of India was adjusted against penal interest in the first place. However, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse the provision available to the Life Insurance Corporation of India to reduce the liability of the payment regime and in the alternative to make the Life Insurance Corporation of India available to pay the dues.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Subject to the figures mentioned in the Preamble under this Government Decision, an amount of Rs. 2,28,95, 425/- towards principal and Rs. 64,00,136/- towards interest as on 01.06.2019 and Rs. 2,92,95, 980/- towards principal and Rs. 49,34,593/- towards interest as on 01.12.2019 is required to be made available to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for repayment of loan to Life Insurance Corporation of India during the financial year 2019-20.
2. Out of the above amount available provision of Rs. 2,03,64, 600/- (Rupees Two Crores Three Lakhs Sixty Four Thousand Six Hundred only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under this Government decision.

The following matters shall be dealt with in respect of the grants released under this decision.
(a) The Maharashtra Life Authority shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure of the above sanctioned amount.
(b) The Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall spend the above sanctioned amount for the specific purpose for which it has been sanctioned.
(a) In order to enable the Government to ensure that the conditions contained in clauses (a) and (b) above are fulfilled by the Maharashtra Life Authority, the Accountant General 1/ 2, Mumbai/ Nagpur shall have the power to audit the books of accounts and to examine the accounts and the Authority shall extend all assistance and facilities to the Accountant General to enable him to discharge his functions properly.
(d) The Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall make available all documents relating to these expenses for inspection by the Accountant General, if so requested by him in accordance with Rule No. 2 of the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959 Annexure- 22.
