The Proposal:
In pursuance of the directions of the Hon'ble Supreme Court in Writ Petition (Civil) No. 82/2011 dated 31st October, 2013, instructions have been issued to constitute a Civil Services Board under the Government Order at Reference No.
Further, vide Notification at Reference No. (2), the competent authority for transfer of officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran has been specified. Therefore, two separate government orders have been issued in this regard.
Considering the above facts, the matter of passing corrigendum to the Government Resolution at Reference No. (1) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's stand:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being excluded from the Government Resolution at Reference No. 1. The Civil Services Board (1), which was set up for the Maharashtra Life Authority, is being abolished.
Since the changes mentioned in the above paragraph are applicable only to the Maharashtra Life Authority, the other provisions in the reference No. (1) shall continue to apply.
The officers/ staff of Group A, Group B and Group C in Maharashtra Life Authority should be posted/ transferred with the approval of the competent authority as specified in the notification dated 4th June, 2014 of Maharashtra Life Authority.
