Government Resolution, Revenue and Forest Department, No: SCY-2018/ PR No.247/ M-7, dated 29.11.2018 Preamble- Contingency measures to be taken to overcome drinking water scarcity include supply of water through tankers/ bullock carts. As per the decision of the Government No. 1, it has been provided that the District Collector shall constitute a committee to conduct a joint inspection of the villages where it is necessary to supply drinking water by tankers/ bullock carts. The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee.
Government's decision
The Election Commission of India has announced the Lok Sabha Elections 2019 from 10th March, 2019. The Model Code of Conduct has come into force during this period. The Committee is being reconstituted to conduct joint inspection of villages where drinking water supply by tankers/ bullock carts is found necessary during the period of Model Code of Conduct.
