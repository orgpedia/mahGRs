The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Mahotsav Rural Dalit Vasti Water Supply and Sanitation Scheme will provide private tap connections and individual toilet facilities to families belonging to Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in rural areas of the state. 1 This has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 18th November, 2011. Under this scheme, the government provides grants to the concerned district councils for the construction of private tap connections and individual toilets to families belonging to scheduled castes and neo-Buddhist communities in rural areas of the state, as well as for additional works in accordance with the water supply, storage, mechanical system in those areas.
