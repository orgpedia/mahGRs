Preamble: The Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-sections-I to V regarding amendment of the financial powers conferred by the Department of Finance. As per the Government Order dated 17.04.2015, the Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department has been given various financial powers as Head of the Department. Under consideration was the reallocation of financial and administrative powers in respect of non-policy matters out of the financial powers conferred on the Heads of Departments as per the said Government decision. The following decisions are being taken:
Government Decision: In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 17.04.2015 and subject to the conditions contained therein, the financial powers conferred on the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Papu-I) of the Department are being conferred by re-assigning certain financial powers conferred on the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department as Head of the Department.
