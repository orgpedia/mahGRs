The Proposal:
Government to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of officers and employees of Maharashtra Life Authority and ETP charges to be deposited with Maharashtra Life Authority.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1421/ P.No.70/ Papu-23
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to deposit the Technical Fee with the Government and accordingly the Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017 has been issued. A total grant of Rs. 142.50 crore has been released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2021-22 under the head 22158041 as grant in aid for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Now the matter of disbursement of funds made available by the Finance Department under the above head of accounts is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 38,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Crores only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for payment of salaries and allowances to its officers/ employees.
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for the purpose of payment of salary/ allowances and accrued leave of the officers/ employees on regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
Technical charges and ETP charges due to the Authority at the end of March, 2022 for the year 2021-22 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
(c) The Authority shall ensure that the leave encashment benefits of any retired employee of a regular establishment are not kept pending for a long period. Also as per the instructions given in the letter at (5) under reference, 2 out of 4 pages on regular establishment retired
