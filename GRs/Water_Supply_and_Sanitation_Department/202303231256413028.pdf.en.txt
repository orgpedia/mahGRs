Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year. """ Auxiliary Chemicals"" Group-B in Ground Water Survey and Development System with effect from 01.01.2022." A provisional seniority chart showing the position as on 01.01.2023 has been prepared and is annexed as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice who are retired from service working in their department, subordinate office of the Agency and who are on deputation from the establishment of the Agency or have changed cadre by other means. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority notice, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the Directorate. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
