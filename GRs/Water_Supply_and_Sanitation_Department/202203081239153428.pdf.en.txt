Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparing and publishing seniority list of government officials/ employees every year. This policy has since been amended by reference No. 4 and No. 5. Provisional Common Seniority for Senior Diving Engineer (Group A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2019 to 01/01/2021 has been notified vide Circular No.3 hereunder. No objections have been received with regard to temporary seniority. A provisional final seniority chart showing the status of Senior Digging Engineer (Group A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2019, 01/01/2020 and 01/01/2021 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this letter.
