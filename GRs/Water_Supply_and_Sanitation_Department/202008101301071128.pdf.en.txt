The following instructions should be strictly followed for the immediate and effective implementation of the transfer order:
The above mentioned Assistant Geologist is being relieved unilaterally from the office mentioned in Rakkana No. 3 from the date of this order i.e. 10/08/2019 (M. No. The order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned office.
The transferred officers should immediately join the office mentioned in Rakana No. 4 and submit the report to the government through the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System without delay. If it is found that the transferred officers have not joined the place of transfer, disciplinary action will be taken against them under the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. The transferred officers should be reinstated immediately. The head of the office and the establishment officer concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an officer without fixing a mandatory waiting period.
(3) The officers being posted under the said order shall be required to join immediately at the place of their posting. If any officer fails to appear at the place of posting or tries to directly or indirectly pressurize the officer to change the transfer order, it should be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officer. Also see Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Page 2 of 4.

Such officers shall be liable to disciplinary action for violating Rule 3 (3) and Rule 23 of the Rules, 1979.
Since these transfers are required to be effected effectively, the salary of all the transferred officers as on 10/08/2020 shall be drawn by the office in which they are presently working. The salary from 11/08/2020 should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer office under any circumstances. After the concerned officer arrives at the transfer office, the post-transfer office should deduct the salary and submit a report to the government. Instructions are being issued to all Heads of Departments and Heads of Offices in the Ground Water Survey and Development System that they will be held personally liable if the drawing and disbursement officer of the office in which they were working before the transfer of salary from 11/08/2020 is paid.
These orders will come into force with immediate effect. The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency is being informed to submit the information to the government immediately as to whether the concerned officers have joined the office of the transferred officer or how.
