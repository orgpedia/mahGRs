As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure-II to the Bombay Finance Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority should be submitted to the Accountant General by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for inspection along with relevant documents as per the demand of the Accountant General.
The Water and Sanitation Support Agency shall issue the utilization certificates of the grants received by the Water and Sanitation Support Agency from the Government or the grants received in the subsequent period. The Director, Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency shall certify and sign the programme-wise amounts of grants and expenditure sanctioned and released as indicated in the Utilization Certificate. The certificate of utility shall be signed by the Joint Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
The utilization certificate of this subsidy should be submitted to the government in the prescribed format and copies of the utilization certificate should also be sent to the Accountant General.
The expenditure of this grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 001 on the prescribed date.
Under this Government decision, the funds released to the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai are being disbursed to the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai on BEAMS system. Sakore, Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai and Shri M. B. Hazari, Joint Secretary, Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, Ministry of Water Resources, Mumbai as Controlling Officers. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai shall draw the amount disbursed under this order from the office of the Sub-Treasury Office, Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai and deposit the amount in the accounts of Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai through NEFT system.
Expenditure arising out of the said grants, vide Demand No.22215192, Assistance to Municipalities/ Municipal Councils (02) State Plan- (02) (02) Subsidiary Grants for Water Supply and Sewerage Schemes of Municipalities/ Municipal Councils, 2215 1647, 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment), shall be included under the head of expenditure and the same shall be met out of the approved provision for the financial year 2018-19.
