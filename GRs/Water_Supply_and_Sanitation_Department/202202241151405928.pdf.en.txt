A total amount of Rs. 194.7581 crore has been allocated for 36 Auxiliary Grants (Pay) Expenditure for the financial year 2021-22 under the head Establishment (Compulsory) (2215 1881). Now the matter of disbursing the next salary fund after considering the total salary expenditure and demand of the Zila Parishads is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, Grants-in-Aid (Salary) under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Compulsory) (22151881)"
The grant amounting to Rs. 16,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixteen Crores Thirty Lakhs only) is sanctioned and disbursed to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad as indicated in the" Statement A" accompanying this Government Resolution.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. The Government has released unutilized funds for the year 2020-21 under the above head.
The said funds are being disbursed subject to deposit and the unutilized funds should not be drawn from the treasury without depositing the same with the government.
3. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. This money is spent on other things.
Financial irregularities will be considered and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business, Financial Regulations, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing and spending the disbursed funds from the Treasury. as well as the Department of Finance
Be sure to follow the instructions given from time to time.
(5) The Government shall ensure that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed under the said Government Decision is submitted to the Government and care is taken to make further requisition for the required funds. The District Councils concerned noted that the funds would not be disbursed until further demand letters were received.
