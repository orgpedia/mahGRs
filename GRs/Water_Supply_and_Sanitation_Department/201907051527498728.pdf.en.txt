INTRODUCTION: Under the above Government decision, instructions have been issued regarding appointment of Swachchagrahis, objectives of appointment, duties and responsibilities, incentive amount, eligibility, selection and other ancillary matters to meet the remaining objectives of sustainable use of sanitation facilities and cleanliness in Gram Panchayats. The decision was taken in the year 2018-19. The matter of extension of the date of appointment of Swachhagrahi under the said Government decision till 31st December, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision- Under the Government Decision here, instructions have been issued regarding the appointment of Swachchagrahis, the purpose of appointment, duties and responsibilities, incentive amount, eligibility, selection and other ancillary matters to meet the remaining objectives of sustainable use of sanitation facilities and cleanliness in Gram Panchayats. The decision will be implemented from 2018-19.
