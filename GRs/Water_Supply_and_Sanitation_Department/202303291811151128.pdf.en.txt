Terms and Conditions
The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Amravati should directly distribute the funds to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad for taking measures under the shortage in rural areas.
The funds should be released for the measures taken to address the drinking water shortage in rural areas for the 2021-2022 shortage period as well as for the payment of pending dues during this period. This money cannot be used for other projects.
3) The Divisional Commissioner should allocate funds on priority to measures such as acquisition of wells, supply of water by tankers and acquisition of new bore wells.
4) It should be ensured that the funds are disbursed directly to the contractors through Electronic Clearing Services/ RTGS, without keeping the personal ledger account or bank account.
