Government Decision No.Jjimi-0120/ PR No. 65/ Papu-07 dated 04th November, 2020 3) Letter No. RAVESWAS/ VASO 08/ No. 13/567 D. 17/04/2023 Preamble The Centrally Sponsored Jal Jeevan Mission has been launched by reorienting the National Rural Drinking Water Programme from the year 2019. The Central Government has launched the Jal Jeevan Mission. Based on the guidelines issued on 25.12.2019, for implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission in the State, under reference No. (1) The guidelines have been issued in accordance with the decisions of the Government here. Jal Jeevan Mission is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme with 50% Central Share and 50% State Share.
The reference No. Under Jal Jeevan Mission, 39 posts have been sanctioned for State Water Sanitation Mission at State level and 136 posts for District Water and Sanitation Mission at district level and the salary/ allowances of the officers/ employees on deputation have been approved to be 100% State share. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the Government to declare Drawing and Disbursing Officers as well as Controlling Officers at the State level and at the District level to draw the necessary funds for the salaries/ allowances of the officers and employees on deputation under the Jal Jeevan Mission.
Government's decision
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the following Drawing and Disbursing Officers and Controlling Officers are being declared at the State and District level from the financial year 2023-24 as per the provisions of Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for drawing the necessary funds for the salary/ allowances of the officers and employees on deputation working under the Centrally Sponsored Programme of Jal Jeevan Mission.
