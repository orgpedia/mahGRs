The Proposal:
Budget provision of Rs. 244.00 crore has been made for the financial year 2020-21 under the head 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to meet the non-salary expenditure on establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishads vide Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zila Parishads (01) Assistance to Zila Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zila Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zila Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881). Out of the provision, a total of Rs. 61.00 crore has so far been disbursed to the Zila Parishads towards wage subsidy as per the Government decision vide Notes 3, 4 and 5 of the Vachha.
The Department of Finance vide circular dated 28.7.2020 has issued directions for release of balance amount of second instalment of salary for the month of March, 2020, after the salary for the month of July, 2020 is passed by the Treasury. Accordingly, considering the total salary expenditure of the respective Zila Parishads on the BIMS system for the year 2020-21, it is under consideration of the Government to disburse subsidy to the Zila Parishads for further salary expenditure.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, a total grant of Rs. 24,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Forty Lakh only) as shown in the statement A accompanying this Government decision for the Auxiliary Grants (Salaries) under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, 36 (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)" is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure.
(3) All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the statement A accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
4. Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said decision is submitted to the government. The concerned district shall not disburse the funds until further requisition is received.

Councils should be noted. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
Expenditure from the grants sanctioned by this Government under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Compulsory) (22151881) 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries), shall be spent and the same shall be credited to the accounts of the Government.
The grant will be disbursed during 2020-21.
This Government decision is issued under the authority given to the departments under the Government Circular No.Finance-2020/ PR No. 64/ Earth-3 of the Finance Department, dated April 16, 2020.
The terms and conditions set forth herein are being complied with.
