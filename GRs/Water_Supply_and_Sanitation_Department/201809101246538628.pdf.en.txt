The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the department's establishment. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted to the Government before 10th October, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers so that it is possible to make necessary changes in the seniority list by verifying it.
Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining seniority in the cadre of Planning Officer only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
