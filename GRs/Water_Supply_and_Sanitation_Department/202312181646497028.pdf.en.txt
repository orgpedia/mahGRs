The Proposal:
The Government has, vide Government Resolution dated 23.3.2017, taken over the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and has sanctioned a sum of Rs.2215A052 for the financial year 2023-24 as grant to meet the expenditure on pension of retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. A budgetary provision of Rs 263.4744 crore has been made. In order to pay the pension benefits to the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2023-24, the Authority has to pay a total of Rs. 131,73,72, 000/- has been disbursed. At present, the matter of disbursing further funds under this head is under the consideration of the government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: A grant of Rs. 110,58,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crore and Fifty Eight Lakh only) under the head" Subsidiary Grants (Compulsory) (2215 A052) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried)" to the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for payment of pension benefits for the financial year 2023-24, is being released to the Authority subject to the following conditions:-
(i) Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall ensure that the amount of the subsidy disbursed under this Government decision is spent for the same purpose as it is sanctioned for the pension benefits of retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2023-24. These funds should not be used for retirement benefits.
(ii) Expenditure on other items of the Fund shall be deemed to be financial irregularities and the officers/ employees concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action for such action.
(iii) Separate accounts should be maintained of the amount of free subsidy.
(iv) The Authority shall inform the Government from time to time about the allocations made under the free grants.
v) The grants being disbursed under the target of" 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment)" are" conditional" in nature as per the Government decision dated 12.01.2023 of the Department of Finance. The authority should submit the utilization certificate of the free subsidy to the government. The Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall certify the amount of grant-in-aid sanctioned and released as well as the amount of expenditure shown in the Utilization Certificate.
The utility certificates should be signed by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Only such utility certificates will be acceptable to the government.
vi) The expenditure of the released subsidy should be reconciled with the expenditure reported to the Accountant General's Office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department on the prescribed date.

(vii) The Authority shall ensure that the retirement benefits of any retired employee do not remain pending for a long period. Old cases should be settled on priority while paying retirement benefits to retired employees. A chronological list of retired officers/ employees should be prepared and action taken to pay pensionary benefits on a priority basis. The certificate that such action is being taken should be submitted to the government when requesting further funds.
(viii) A detailed list of employees whose retirement gratuity and gratuity are pending for more than one year should be submitted to the Government while seeking further funds.
ix) Technical charges and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of November 2023 and December 2023 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Rajendra Madke has been appointed as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Ram Gangje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been appointed as the Controlling Officer.
The pension subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should pension grants be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
The expenditure in the said case shall be included in the financial statement for the year 2023-24 by including the expenditure under the head" Demand No.Y-2, 22215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to other Non-Governmental Organizations, (01) Assistance to Government Employees transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for sanction of pensionary benefits (Compulsory) (22158052) 31, Assistance Grants (Non-Payment)."
This Government Resolution is being issued as per the concurrence received under the Finance Department, Informal Reference No.411/ Expenditure-3, dated 03.11.2023.
