The Proposal:
Under the General Administration Department. Instructions have been given to create more number of posts and transfer their services to more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category under the Government Order dated 21.12.2019 but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or have submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes. Accordingly, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for creation of 6 more posts for transfer of 6 employees to the post of Service Superintendents under the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of 6 additional posts for employees in the office under the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency.
Government Decision: Subject to the Government Decision under the General Administration Department, in the office under the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, the services of 06 employees mentioned in the table below who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category, but have not submitted the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes. Approval is being given to create 06 supernumerary posts on the establishment of the Directorate for a period of 11 months with effect from 21/12/2019 as a temporary measure for transfer to the post of Supernumerary or till the date on which he would have retired, whichever is earlier.
