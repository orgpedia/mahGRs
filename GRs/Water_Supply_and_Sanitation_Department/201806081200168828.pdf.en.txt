Government Decision: Rs. The revised budget of Rs. 2,32,06, 200/- (Rupees Two Crores Thirty Two Lakhs Six Thousand Two Hundred only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat and Zilla Parishad will be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad Aurangabad that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials and contractors should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
4. On completion of the scheme, it shall be mandatory for the concerned Gram Panchayat to implement the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
7. Action to be taken as per the prevailing policy on trespass.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
2. The revised cost of the scheme is Rs. 30,09,503/- (Rupees Thirty Lakhs Nine Thousand Five Hundred and Three only) from the following State share/ District Planning Board/ Minimum Needs Programme.
