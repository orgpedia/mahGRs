INTRODUCTION: As per the decision of the Government, 22 State Level Core Resource Institutions and 5 State Level Core Principal Resource Institutions were selected. Under the government circular at serial number 2, 83 district-level main resource institutions were selected. The KRCs have been selected for a period of 3 years, which was extended by 1 year as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3. The Central Government vide letter dated 02/08/2019 has directed to disburse the incentive subsidy to the beneficiary families by taking steps so that all the families in the rural areas of the State have individual toilets by 31/12/2019.
As per the directions of the Government of India, it was under the consideration of the Government to extend the time limit up to 31/12/2019 for the completion of the targets under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin).
