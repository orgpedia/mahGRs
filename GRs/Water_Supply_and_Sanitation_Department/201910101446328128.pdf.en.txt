Preamble As per the Government decision dated 22.03.2012, 25 temporary posts created for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department at No.1 in the said Chapter have been approved to be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 as per the Government decision dated 28.02.2019 in No.3 in the said Chapter.
Under the Government Resolution dated 11th September, 2019 of the Finance Department No.44 of the said chapter, all the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts included in their rolls and created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 25 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). The government is taking the following steps:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.109/ Papu-01
Government Decisions In line with the provision of clean and pure drinking water and sanitation facilities through the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the department needs more manpower to effectively implement the schemes and to meet the increased workload arising out of it.
Therefore, 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) need to be continued.
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 11th September, 2019 of the Finance Department, No. 4, read with the Government Resolution, 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), as indicated in Form A attached to this Government Resolution, are being allowed to continue from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020.
Expenditure on the said 25 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2019-20 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 11th September, 2019 of the Finance Department.
