Government's decision:
As per the norms laid down by the Government for mitigation of drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas, a sum of Rs. 32033.00 lakhs (in letters three hundred and twenty crore thirty three lakhs only) is being sanctioned and released to the Divisional Commissioners, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur under the head of accounts 22450093 for taking up/ implementing measures for mitigation of drinking water scarcity in their respective divisions through budgetary allocation system subject to the following conditions and terms and conditions as provided in the extant Standing Government Resolution.
Terms and Conditions
The Divisional Commissioner, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur shall disburse the funds directly to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad for taking measures under the scarcity in rural areas.
2) Urban measures which are being implemented through Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils.
For this, the Divisional Commissioner should distribute it to the concerned District Collector. The Collector should disburse the funds to the concerned Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Principal, Municipal Council.
3) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Authority shall disburse the funds for the measures to be implemented by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the drinking water shortage to the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
The funds should be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas for the scarcity period 2018-19 and for the payment of pending dues during this period. Care should be taken to ensure that the funds are not used for other short-term measures.
5) The Divisional Commissioner should allocate funds on priority to the measures like well acquisition, tanker water supply and even if the proposal submitted by the Divisional Commissioner does not ask for the measures approved by the government, these measures should also be allocated on priority as per the order of the concerned administrative approval. Temporary tap water supply scheme and special repair of the tap scheme.
