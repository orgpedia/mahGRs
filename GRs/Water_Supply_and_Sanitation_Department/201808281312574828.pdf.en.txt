GOVERNMENT DECISIONS, WATER SUPPLY AND SANITAL DEPARTMENT, NO.: APNA-0218/ PR No. 163/ Papu15, dated 31st May, 2018 GOVERNMENT ORDER:
As per Section 4 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the transfer of officers in the cadre of" Branch Engineer" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System on administrative grounds was effected vide Government Order dated 31/05/2018. However, since there is no vacancy in the place of transfer allotted to Mr. S. K. Sukte, Branch Engineer at Ahmednagar, a request was made to the competent authority to transfer him elsewhere.
As per the approval given by the competent authority, Shri.
The transfer order of SK Sukte, Branch Engineer is being revised and he is being given a revised posting as indicated in column number 4 in front of his name.
