The expenditure on water scarcity measures in rural and urban areas of the State is met from the grants available from the Revenue and Forest Departments under the head" Assistance for Natural Disaster Mitigation" (2245). Reference No. for measures taken/ to be taken during the period of scarcity 2018-19 The Department has received the proposal for seeking funds under letter No. 4 to 10. For the financial year 2019-20, the Government was considering the matter of disbursement of funds demanded by the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad from the funds disbursed by the Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation) to this department through its Government Resolution No. 11.
Government's decision:
As per the norms laid down by the Government for the alleviation of drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas, the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik and Aurangabad is being sanctioned and released funds to the tune of Rs. 3184.05 lakhs (Rupees Thirty One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Five Thousand only) under the head of accounts 2245 2185 for the measures taken/ to be taken for the alleviation of drinking water scarcity in their respective areas through the Budget Distribution System subject to the terms and conditions laid down in the extant Standing Government Resolution and the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions
1) The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik and Aurangabad, should distribute the funds directly to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad for taking measures under the shortage in rural areas.
2) Urban measures which are being implemented through Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils.
