Preamble The decision to absorb the Attendant Assistants working as Attendant Assistants under the Employment Guarantee Scheme into the Government service is under reference No. Decisions 1 and 2 were made.
Accordingly, Mr. Suresh Chaukade, Public Works Department No. 1, Yavatmal as Assistant to Deputy Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Amravati Division, Amravati. As per the order dated 29.09.2003, the post of Junior Scribe was appointed in the office of Deputy Commissioner (s), Sub Division, Zila Parishad Washim by adjustment. Then according to the age. He retired on 30.06.2013 from the Office of Senior Geologist, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Yavatmal in the rank of Senior Scribe. However, the qualifying service must be at least 10 years of service to get the benefit of pension. Therefore, it was under consideration of the Government to add 3 months and 6 days to the qualifying service period of Mr. Choukade as a special case.
Government Decision: In order to make the benefit of pension and related benefits admissible to Shri Suresh Mahadev Choukade, Retired Senior Scribe, Rule 54 (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 has been relaxed to 3 months 6 days as a special case during his qualifying service period.
