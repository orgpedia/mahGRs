Government order:
The following officers who are eligible for promotion to the cadre of" Deputy Engineer (Mechanical), Group-A" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System are being approved in the Provisional Selection List for the year 2016-17 for regular promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) from the date indicated against their names in the 4 vacancies in the open category by regulating the ad-hoc/ temporary promotion in the cadre of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical).
The date of regular promotion to the post of" Deputy Engineer (Mechanical), Group-A" has been fixed in accordance with the qualifications prescribed for the promotion, taking into account the seniority of the said officers in the cadre of Branch Engineer. This date of regular promotion will be subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
