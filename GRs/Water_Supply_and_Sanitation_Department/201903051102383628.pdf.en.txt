2. The Department of Finance, vide Government Circular No. 9 of 2018-19, has made available 100% of the budgetary allocation for wage and 90% of the non-wage budgetary allocation on the system. As on date, a total of Rs. 120,36,00, 000/-for 36 Auxiliary Grants (Salary) and Rs. 5,57,00, 000/-for 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salary) have been disbursed to the Zila Parishads as per the Government Decision No. In view of the expenditure on the treasury channel of the Zila Parishads as on 11.2.2019, the expenditure is in excess of the funds disbursed by all the Zila Parishads except Ahmednagar Zila Parishad. Therefore, it is under the consideration of the Government to disburse additional salary subsidy to all the Zila Parishads except Ahmednagar under the above headings taking into account the average salary expenditure for two months and the estimated expenditure of the Seventh Pay Commission. Further, the matter of disbursement of non-salary fund under the above head of accounts to the Zila Parishads fulfilling the conditions prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 2.4.2018 of the Finance Department is under consideration of the Government.

(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the statement A accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiary through ECS. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
