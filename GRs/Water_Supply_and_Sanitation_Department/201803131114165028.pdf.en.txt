As per the Government Resolution dated 05.03.2018 of the Department of Finance, No.4 of the said Chapter, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all the existing temporary posts included in their rolls and created after the approval of the earlier Review but not included in the rolls from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018. Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 12 temporary posts for Improvement Assistance and Project Management Cell on the establishment of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 3 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalaswarajya Project.
The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
The government is committed to provide clean and safe drinking water to the people of the state. Jalswarajya Phase-II is being implemented with World Bank funding through the Improvement Support and Management Cell under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. For this work to be carried out properly, 12 temporary posts are required for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell at the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 3 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jal Swarojya Project.
Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 05.03.2018 of the Finance Department, read with the above, the Government have approved the continuation of 12 temporary posts for Improvement Support and Project Management Cell in the establishment of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) as shown in Form A attached to this Government Resolution and 3 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jalswarajya Project as shown in Form B from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018.
Expenditure on 12 temporary posts for Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on establishment of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2018-2019 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head 3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467). The expenditure on salary and allowances of 3 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarajya Project will be borne by the State Bank of India for the Jal Swarajya Programme.
