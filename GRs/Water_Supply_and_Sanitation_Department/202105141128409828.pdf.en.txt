The proposal-
In the financial year 2021-2022, to meet the cost of installation of Ground Water Survey and Development System, under the head" Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation in Demand No.Y-06,4402, (00) (101) Soil Survey and Testing, (01) (01) Small Irrigation Schemes under One Ground Water Survey and Development Agency (Mandatory)," a sum of Rs. 27,72,55, 000/- and Rs. 3,81,000/- has been provided. Out of which, Rs. 2,77,25, 500/- and Rs. 19050/- respectively have been released as Grants-in-aid. Under Section 1, the budget has been distributed to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System on Forecasting, Distribution and Sun Control System. Under the objectives of (01) Salary and (02) Wages, the matter of providing subsidy to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune to meet the expenditure for the next period was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS In the financial year 2021-2022, to meet the expenditure on wages and wages of Ground Water Survey and Development System, under the head 4402 1148, an amount of Rs. 1,38,62, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Eight Lakh Sixty Two Thousand Only) and 19,050/- (Rupees Nineteen Thousand Fifty Only) respectively to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune.
