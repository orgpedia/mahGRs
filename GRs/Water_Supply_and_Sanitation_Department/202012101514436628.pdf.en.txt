The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. A total grant of Rs. 111.55 crore has been sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21 under the head 22150041 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Now the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1420/ PR No.91/ PAPU-23
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 21,44,89, 000/- (Rupees Twenty One Crores Forty Four Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant-in-aid to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salaries and allowances of its officers/ employees, subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for payment of salaries/ allowances to the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2020-21. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of November, 2020 for the year 2020-21 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Patil has been posted as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. R. M. Gengaje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been posted as the Controlling Officer.
The grants sanctioned under this Government decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government from time to time. Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure.
4. The salary subsidy provided by the government will be mandatorily credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
