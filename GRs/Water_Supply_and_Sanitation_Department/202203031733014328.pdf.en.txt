Government order
In terms of this Department, vide order dated 11.08.2021, Shri Ashok P.
Awhad has been appointed as Chief Engineer, Majipra, Nashik on deputation to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for a period of one year. However, Mr. Awhad has been on medical leave since 13.12.2021 and is still on leave. The Department is implementing an ambitious project, Jal Jeevan Mission, which is targeted to be completed by March 2024. However, a review meeting was held on the project.
The Authority, under reference (1) mentioned that Mr. Awhad is not attending and reviewing the works at the regional division level and requested to relieve Mr. Awhad from the services of the Authority.

Government Order No: Majipra-1122/ PR No.45/ Papu-23
Considering the reasons stated by the Authority and the retirement of Mr. Awhad in March, 2022, his services are of no use to the Authority. Paragraph 5 (d) (5) of the Government Resolution dated 17.12.2016 in the context of General Administration Department provides for immediate termination of the period of deputation by the competent authority citing specific reasons such as irregularity, irregularity, dereliction of duty, etc. Accordingly, from the date of this order, the services of Mr. Awhad in the Authority (Mr. No.) are being terminated and relieved to attend their parent department.
