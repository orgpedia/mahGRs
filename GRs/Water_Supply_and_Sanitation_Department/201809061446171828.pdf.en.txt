Government's decision The special Jafrabad G. Jalna Rs. The revised budget of Rs. 1,89,97, 600/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Nine Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Six Hundred only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
The terms and conditions mentioned in the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad, Jalna that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned executive engineer, officials of the Zilla Parishad and the concerned Gram Panchayat and the contractor should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (subject to special amendments), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
As the works of the said scheme have been completed, the accounts of the scheme should be closed by handing over the scheme to the local body (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad) after examining the scheme.
5. Action should be taken as per the prevailing policy on dowry.
The contractor concerned will be obliged to carry out the remaining works of the revised plan according to the technical changes.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
