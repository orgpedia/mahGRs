Introduction: In order to make all the government services available to the general public at affordable prices through information technology, transparent and fast. The e-governance policy of the state has been announced under the Government Decision No. 1. Accordingly, laptops and other materials have been made available to the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the Commissioner of Interior, the Ground Water Survey and Development System, the State of Maharashtra and their subordinate offices. Installation of MS-Office and antivirus on the laptop provided was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: With a view to computerize the office of the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System in line with the overall e-governance and to strengthen the Ground Water Survey and Development System.
