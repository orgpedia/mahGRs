1) Government Circular No: LIC-1083/ CR- 235/59, Dy. 16.03.1984 2) Government Resolution No. 1088/1/78/ Government Guarantee of Finance Department, Govt. 05.11.1999 3) Government Resolution No. 20.09.2006 Preamble The Life Insurance Corporation of India provides loans to the Zilla Parishads for rural tap water supply schemes in the State. The government has provided guarantee for this. As per the Government Resolution No.LIC-1087/ CR- 544/59 of the Rural Development Department, the amount of interest and principal of the loan outstanding is Rs. 100/- back to Rs. 2/- at this rate, the district councils have to pay the guarantee fee to the government twice a year for the half-yearly period. As there is delay in payment of guarantee fee by Zila Parishads, the payment of guarantee fee on the lines of repayment of loan and interest thereon for rural water supply schemes by Life Insurance Corporation of India is also made through Maharashtra Jeevan Authority as per the provisions of Central Mechanism of Payment of Guarantee Fee, Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, Govt. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse a grant of Rs. 1,21,68, 610/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty One Lakh Sixty Six Thousand Six Hundred and Ten only) to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for payment of guarantee fee for the first and second half of the financial year 2018-19, which was admissible on the Contingent Liability as on 31st March and 31st September, 2018 and payable on 1st April and 1st October, 2018, by book adjustment through the Offices of Accounts.
