The Proposal:
A total of 4022 permanent/ temporary posts of Class-I to Class-IV cadre in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions of Zila Parishads have been sanctioned vide Government Decisions No. 1 and 2 dated 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015 respectively.
Out of these posts, a total of 1918 temporary posts have been extended till 28.2.2021 as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 4& 5. Temporary posts in rural water supply departments need to be extended for a further period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes.

Vacant posts will be filled. The posts which are lying vacant and whose additional charge has been given to other officers/ employees shall be deemed to have lapsed if they are not filled up on regular basis within six months from the date of occurrence of the vacancy. Further, as per the provisions of Section 3.3, the posts of Head of Department/ Regional Head of Department/ Head of Office shall not lapse even if they remain vacant for a period of more than six months. The posts are of Executive Engineer (Construction), Deputy Engineer (Construction/ Mechanical) in Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Division. Therefore, it is necessary to extend the tenure of all temporary posts (working/ vacant) of this cadre. Considering this fact, the government is considering to extend the temporary posts in the rural water supply departments for the next period
Government's decision:
As per the figure approved vide Government Decision, dated 4.3.2011 and dated 4.8.2015, a total of 2002 temporary posts in various cadres as shown in the attached" Statement A" in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Divisions under Zila Parishads are being allowed to continue from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 subject to the assumption that these posts are not vacant for a period of more than six months and no irregular appointments have been made to those posts.
The Department of Finance has issued instructions to abolish 25% of the sanctioned posts of Group-D cadre as per Para 1 (5) of the Government Resolution dated 14.1.2016.
Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 22.3.2018, the existing 81 vacancies in Group-D cadre in the Rural Water Supply Department of the Zilla Parishad have been abolished and the temporary posts have not been extended under this Government decision.
