Preamble: The post of" Assistant Geologist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency has been appointed for a period of two years under the Government Resolution No. 2. These appointments have been made by the first appointment in the government service on the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of the probation period of the officers/ employees of the Government service and since the officers posted to the post of Assistant Geologist, Ground Water Survey and Development System have satisfactorily completed their probation period of 2 years and the same has been recommended by the Directorate under Reference No. 3, the matter of their expiry of the probation period and their transfer as regular" Assistant Geologist" was under consideration of the Government.
