As per the Government decision dated 30 th August, 2021, the extension has been approved from 1 st September, 2020 to 28 th February, 2022.
As per the Government Resolution dated 22.02.2022 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said Review but not included in the rolls.
Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 24 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes related to providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 22.02.2022 of the Finance Department read above, the Government is granting approval for the continuation of 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as shown in Form A attached with this Government Decision.
The expenditure on this should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2022-2023 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451- Secretariat Financial Services 090- Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 22 nd February, 2022 of the Finance Department.
