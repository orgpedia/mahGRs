Government's decision:
As per the norms laid down by the Government for addressing the drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas, the expenditure on the measures taken/ to be taken to address the drinking water scarcity in Konkan Pune, Nashik and Aurangabad under their respective departments has been estimated at Rs. 3305.444 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Three Crores Five Lakh Forty Four Thousand Four Hundred only) is being sanctioned and released to the Divisional Commissioners, Konkan, Pune, Nashik and Aurangabad through the Budget Distribution System subject to the terms and conditions laid down in the prevailing Standing Government Resolution and the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions
1) The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik and Aurangabad, should distribute the funds directly to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad for taking measures under the shortage in rural areas.
