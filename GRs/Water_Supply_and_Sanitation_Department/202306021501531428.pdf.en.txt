Preamble: Under the Government Order dated 01.12.2016 issued in the context of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, a revised rule book on procedures for office procurement to be carried out by the Government Departments has been issued. Para 2.9.3 of the said rule book provides for constitution of the Ministerial Administrative Department (itself) Procurement Committee. As well as Rule No. 2.10 provides for the appointment of one or more Procurement Officers. Considering this, the government was considering the formation of a new procurement committee for the purchase of e-office system and computer materials under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the provisions of the revised Rules of Procedure for procurement of office by Government Departments in the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, in paragraphs No. 2.9.3 and 2.10 of the Rules of Procedure, the following Procurement Committees are being constituted for the purchase of e-office related computers etc. for the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. Similarly, procurement officers are being designated to the department/ office supplier as well as to the cell officer, computer branch to be done on behalf of the department/ office.
