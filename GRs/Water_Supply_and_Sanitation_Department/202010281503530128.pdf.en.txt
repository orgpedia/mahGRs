Government Resolution No: Budget-1420/ P.S.No.90/ Papu-23
A budgetary provision of Rs. 244.00 crore has been made for grants-in-aid (Salaries). Out of this provision, a total of Rs. 109.80 crore has been disbursed to the Zila Parishads for payment of salaries as per the Government decision vide Notes 3, 4, 5, 7 and 8 in the Budget. Now, the matter of disbursement of further wage subsidy to the Zila Parishads is under consideration of the Government considering the total salary expenditure of the Zila Parishads on BIMS system for the year 2020-21.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, a total grant of Rs. 36,60,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Sixty Lacs only) as shown in the statement A accompanying this Government decision, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01), Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)" is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure.
(3) All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the statement A accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
4. Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said decision is submitted to the government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
