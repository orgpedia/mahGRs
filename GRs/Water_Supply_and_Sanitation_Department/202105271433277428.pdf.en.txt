The Proposal:
A budget provision of Rs. 50.8148 crore has been made for the financial year 2021-22 under the head Demand Number Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for Implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" to meet the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishad.
Out of the said provision, necessary grants for payment of salaries to the Zila Parishads have been disbursed as per the decision of the Government under (2) and (3). However, the payment of medical leave dues
The Sangli Zilla Parishad has pointed out that the subsidy is decreasing.

It also said that there is not enough money for April salaries. Therefore, the matter of disbursing additional grants to the two Zilla Parishads of Sangli and Pune is under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)," Rs. 13,00,000/- (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs only) and Rs. 8,00,000/- (Rupees Eight Lakhs only). 21,00,000/ (Rupees Twenty One Lakh only) is sanctioned and disbursed to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the Zilla Parishad.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts disbursed under this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
