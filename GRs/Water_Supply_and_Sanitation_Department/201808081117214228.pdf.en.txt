Government order:
The services of Smt.Bhagyashree Chandrakant Maggiwar, Deputy Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, are being provided for the Jalaswarajya Phase-II project on deputation in the capacity of Deputy Director, Special Control Room, Jalaswarajya Phase-II for a period of three years or till the year in which the project is terminated (whichever is earlier) subject to the following conditions:
Conditions: (1) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence from the date on which Mrs. Maggiwar enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner. After three years, the period of deputation shall expire on the date on which they join their parent department after handing over the charge of the post of deputation.
(2) The Government/ competent authority reserves the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
3) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority and
4) The Deputy Director shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that she intends to return to the parent department.
The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System and the Project Director, Jalaswarajya Phase-II in consultation with each other, after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Residential Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Bhagyashree Chandrakant Maggiwar will be handled by the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
