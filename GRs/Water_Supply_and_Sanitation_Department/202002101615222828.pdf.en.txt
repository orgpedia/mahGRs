Revised administrative approval is being accorded to the revised cost estimate of Rs. 60.98 lakh (Dhobal) for the tap water supply scheme at Koregaon, G. Satara subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining works should be completed before 31st March, 2020 at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The Gram Panchayat concerned shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Satara that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
5. On completion of the scheme, it will be mandatory for the local body to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
7. Action to be taken as per the prevailing policy on trespass.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
