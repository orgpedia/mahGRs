Introduction As the per capita cost of 47 tap water supply scheme in Nandurbar district is more than the prescribed norm, the proposal for approval of this tap water supply scheme has been submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar under reference number 10 for the approval of the Government.
The proposal for the said tap water supply scheme has been accorded technical approval by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar. Therefore, the matter of administrative approval of the budget of 47 tap water supply scheme in Nandurbar district under the Jal Jeevan Mission program was under consideration of the government.
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble of the Government Resolution, administrative approval is being accorded to 47 schemes in Nandurbar district at the cost and design as mentioned in Column-7 of the table below for the 55 litres per capita per day (lpcd) tap water supply scheme at budgeted cost subject to fulfilment of the following terms and conditions under Jal Jeevan Mission Programme.
