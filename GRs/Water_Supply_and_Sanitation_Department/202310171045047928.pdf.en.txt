The Proposal:
Maharashtra Life Authority has been established under the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1976. Further, the applicability of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 to the employees of the Authority has been approved by the then Urban Development Department vide letter dated 21.01.1984. the State Government on or after 1.11.2005

The Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS)/ National Pension Scheme (NPS) has been implemented for the employees engaged in the service. Similarly, the scheme has been made applicable to the employees appointed on or after 1.11.2005 in the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran by the Authority vide circular dated 14.05.2010. The benefits of Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, Maharashtra Civil Services (Depreciation of Pension) Rules, 1984 and General Provident Fund are not admissible to the employees who are covered under Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS)/ National Pension Scheme.
Now, the Department of Finance has made family pension and death gratuity admissible to the family of an employee in the service of the State Government appointed on or after 1.11.2005 under the Government decision dated 31.03.2023 under reference to the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS)/ National Pension Scheme (NPS), and sickness pension and retirement gratuity admissible to the employee retiring from Government service, if the employee dies during the service period. Further, the Finance Department has informed that the decision will be applicable to the employees of the aided institutions with appropriate modifications and the concerned Ministries/ Departments have been instructed to take action at their level to issue separate orders in this regard.
The Government has accepted the liability of retirement salary of the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran vide Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017. Therefore, the matter of applying the benefits sanctioned by the Department of Finance under the Government Decision dated 31.03.2023 and the Corrigendum dated 20.04.2023 to the employees of the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS)/ National Pension Scheme (NPS) was under the consideration of the Government to the concerned officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
The employees who have been appointed on or after 1.11.2005 in the Maharashtra Life Authority and who are in the defined contribution pension scheme (DCPS)/ National Pension Scheme (NPS) are being allowed to apply the provisions of the Government decision dated 31.03.2023 in the context of the Finance Department as follows.

c) Retirement allowance is also being applied to the employees retiring from the service of the Authority.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 shall continue to apply for the purpose of retirement gratuity and death gratuity to be provided in accordance with the above decision. "
The ex-gratia subsidy scheme implemented by the Finance Department under the Government decision dated 29.09.2018 shall not be applicable to the employees of the Authority.
The families of the employees who died between 1.11.2005 and the date of this Government decision will have to submit the option in Sample-3 along with this Government decision to the Head of the Office to get the death gratuity, family pension arrears and regular family pension. Accordingly, the benefit of family pension will be admissible to the family pensioner on the condition that the amount of ex-gratia grant received by him and the amount of government contribution under the defined pension scheme/ National Pension System is adjusted with the interest/ benefits. After this amount is adjusted, the family pension will be admissible to the family pensioner on a regular basis.
In the event of death or sickness of the employee in service of the Authority, the employee currently working under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension System shall submit to the Head of the Substitute Office in Form-2 regarding the family pension or sickness pension admissible as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 or the accumulated amount deposited in his Permanent Pension Account Number (PRAN) under the National Pension System to the family or his/ her family respectively as per the orders issued by the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority from time to time.
