Introduction: The basic objective of the rural sanitation programme is to improve the health status of the rural population.
Sant Gadge Baba Gram Swachhta Abhiyan, Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Swachh Gram competition is being run since 2000-01 to make the villagers aware of the importance of the programme through active and continuous participation in the campaign/ programme being run by the government to raise the health and living standards of the people in the rural areas by adopting hygiene habits.
The Government of India has been implementing the ambitious programme of Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) since 2nd October, 2014. As per the objectives of the programme, the entire rural Maharashtra has become" Open Defecation Free" in April 2018 as per the baseline survey of 2012, by bringing about a paradigm shift in the practice of open defecation by using individual toilets, which have been constructed on a large scale, for the people in the rural areas of the State. It is necessary to make sustainable use of individual toilets, to keep the entire premises of villages, schools, anganwadis, government/ semi-government buildings, offices, co-operative societies, public markets, ST stands, pilgrimage places, tourist places, etc. completely clean, by making arrangements for adequate number of toilets and toilets. In this regard, the government has taken up various programmes at the gram panchayat, block, gram panchayat and zilla parishad levels. In this way, the Gram Panchayats who have done a great job in the area related to cleanliness and rural development will be honoured by Sant Gadge Baba Gram.

4. Rules of Sant Gadge Baba Gram Swachhta Abhiyan: (i) The inspection committees at each level shall record in the record books of the Gram Panchayats as to when and for how long they inspected the Gram Panchayats in the area assigned to them.
(ii) The evaluation shall be done in an impartial manner, with strict scrutiny by each member of the Screening Committee. The Scrutiny Committee at each level shall prepare the Score Sheet after scrutiny in the jurisdiction assigned to them and all the members shall sign the Score Sheet.
(iii) The Inspection Committee shall assess the record of work done by each Gram Panchayat up to the period of inspection.
