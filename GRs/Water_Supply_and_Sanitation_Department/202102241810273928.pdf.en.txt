Preamble As per Government Decision dated 29.01.2018 and dated 10.01.2020, the Action Plan for 2015-16 to 2023-24 of Global Fund assisted National Hydrological Project-Ground Water Programme has been accorded administrative approval and revised formal approval. Under this project, the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency has submitted a proposal to grant administrative approval for the purchase of 354 DWLR (Digital Water Level Recorder) with Telemetry to be installed on the existing monitoring well. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the purchase of 354 DWLR with Telemetry to be installed on the existing inspection well was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Administrative approval is being accorded for procurement of 354 DWLR with Telemetry for National Hydrology Project supported by Global Bank subject to the following conditions:
1) The cost of the project is Rs. 470.80 lakh should be ensured.
(2) The expenditure for the above purpose shall be met from the grants received from the Central Government.
