In view of the large number of vacancies in the cadre of Chief Engineer (Architecture) in the Maharashtra Life Authority and the unavailability of qualified officers in the Authority for promotion to this post, the Maharashtra Life Authority has requested to fill these posts by deputation through an officer from the Water Resources Department. Accordingly, the proposal for deputation of officers of the Water Resources Department to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran has been submitted to this Department under reference (3).
The following officers of the Water Resources Department are being appointed on deputation for a period of one year in the Maharashtra Life Authority under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
