Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. The final seniority as on 01/01/2011 of the Chief Geophysicist (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System has been published vide Government Circular No. 2 hereunder. The General Administration Department has included the names of the officers who were included in the selection list for the post of Chief Geophysicist (Group-A) from 2011 to 2017 as per the instructions given under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference. 01/01/2012, Dy. 01/01/2013, Dy. 01/01/2014, Dy. 01/01/2015, Dy. 01/01/2016, Dy. Provisional common seniority chart showing the status as on 0/01/2017 and 01/01/2018 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to the post of Chief Geophysicist, Group-A, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, are also given in Annexure-B.
