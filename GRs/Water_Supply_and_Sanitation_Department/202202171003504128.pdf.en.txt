Introduction Jaigarh (TG Ratnagiri) Regional Water Supply Scheme. 32,02,67, 500/- under National Rural Drinking Water Programme vide Government Resolution No. Grapapu 1318/ No.221/ Papu-06, Dy. The original administrative approval has been granted as on 07.01.2019. The Chief Engineer, Majipra, Thane gave the budget of this scheme. The Technical Approval has been granted on 07.02.2019. The tender committee has received the tender for the work under this scheme. As per the decision taken in the meeting dated 17.11.2021, the Member Secretary has issued Reference No. 4 was requested. However, in case of technical changes in the working of the scheme or change in the cost of the scheme, the proposal for revised administrative approval of the scheme may be submitted. 5 was reported. Accordingly, since the mechanical works of the scheme have not yet been tendered, the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Thane, has given a revised budget with technical changes in the present scheme by making the budget of these works as per the 2021-22 schedule and increasing the original budget price by making the budget of Kaljondi Dam and related works as per the 2021-22 schedule. The revised technical approval has been granted on 27.01.2022. Accordingly, the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Mumbai has proposed to give revised administrative approval to the revised plan and budget along with technical changes in the proposed tap water supply scheme.
