Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 04.09.2002 in the said Chapter No.1 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, 24 temporary posts included in the figure of the Department as per the review of posts in the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) have been approved to be continued from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 as per the Government decision dated 05.10.2018 in the said Chapter No.2.
Under the Government Resolution dated 21st February, 2019 of the Department of Finance, No. 3 of the said Chapter, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts included in their rolls and created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls from 1st March, 2019 to 30th September, 2019. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 24 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). The government is taking the following steps:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.110/ Papu-01
Government Decision: As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes in line with providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities.
Therefore, it is necessary to continue 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 21.02.2019 of the Finance Department, read with the above, the Government is granting approval for the continuation of 24 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) as shown in Form A attached to this Government Resolution from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
Expenditure on 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467), shall be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2019-2020.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative departments as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 21st February, 2019 of the Finance Department.
