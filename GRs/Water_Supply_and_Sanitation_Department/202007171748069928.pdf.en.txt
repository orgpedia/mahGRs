The Proposal:
As the Government has taken the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority as per the Government decision dated 23.3.2017 and as the Government is likely to take a long time to pay the pension expenses of the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority directly through the Treasury from the regular Principal Accounts Title 2071, the new Account Title 22158052, which was created temporarily to provide subsidy to the Authority for the retirement of the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, has been extended for the year 2020-21 by the Government decision dated 9th July, 2020 with the consent of the Finance Department and the Accountant General's Office, Mumbai and Nagpur.

The Government was considering to disburse the grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the financial year 2020-21 under the head 22158052 for the purpose of payment of retirement benefits to retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2020-21, a grant of Rs. 57,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Seven Crores Forty Lakh only) is being released to the Authority under the head" Demand No.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to Other Non-Government Organizations (01) Assistance to Other Non-Government Organizations (01) (01) Assistance to Government Employees Transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (2215 A052) 31, Assistance Grants (Compulsory) for Grant of Pension Benefits (Non-Salary)" subject to the following conditions:
(a) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall ensure that the amount of the grant is spent for the same purposes as the grant is sanctioned for the pension benefits of retired officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
(b) The funds shall not be used for repayment of any previous dues. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
e) Separate accounts of the amount of free subsidy should be maintained.
(e) The Authority shall inform the Government from time to time about the allocations made under the free grants.
