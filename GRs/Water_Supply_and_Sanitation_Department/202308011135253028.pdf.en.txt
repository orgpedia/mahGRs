Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees of the government service every year as per the notification at reference No. 1. The provisional Seniority Report for the category of" Senior Geologist" Group-A in the Ground Water Survey and Development System has been published under Reference No. 3 for the period ending 01.01.2023. After considering the objections received in this regard, a provisional final seniority chart showing the status of Group A Senior Geologists in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01.01.2023 has been prepared. This seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this circular.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to withdraw this circular from the service of the subordinate office of the Agency, which is included in the accompanying seniority notice and is functioning in his department.
