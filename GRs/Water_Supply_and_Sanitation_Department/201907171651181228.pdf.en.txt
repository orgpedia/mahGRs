Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) was launched on 2nd October, 2014 with the objective of achieving sustainable sanitation in rural areas. The Government of India has decided to make the entire country open defecation free by 2nd October, 2019. As per the directions of the Central Government and the list of households in the baseline survey, rural Maharashtra has been declared as Open Defecation Free on 18th April, 2018.
The first phase of verification of all open defecation free Gram Panchayats has been completed as per the directions issued vide Government Resolution No. 2 dated 27th October, 2015.
In a Gram Panchayat that is open defecation free, the sanitation facilities created must be used by every member of the family, and the sustainability of sanitation facilities must be maintained.
In this regard, the Central Government vide letter dated 06.12.2018, vide No. 1 of the

(b) Re-verification Committee: After the preliminary verification committee completes the verification, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad shall constitute a re-verification committee for each taluk under the chairmanship of the Deputy Chief Executive Officer (Water and Sanitation). The scope of this committee will be limited to district level verification. The committee will consist of 1) Taluk Coordinator of the concerned Taluk in the District Water and Sanitation Mission Cell 2. Head of the Department of the concerned Taluk 3. Officers of the rank of Extension Officer 4. Block Resource Centre (BRC)/ Cluster Resource Centre (CRC) of the concerned Taluk. The members of this committee shall not be entitled to any remuneration for this examination. This committee should be provided with vehicle facility from the district level. If the vehicle is not available with the district cell, the vehicle should be taken on a rental basis for the duration of the verification period (maximum period of 1 month). The expenditure should be met from the funds sanctioned at the district level under the Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) under the head Information, Education and Communication.
