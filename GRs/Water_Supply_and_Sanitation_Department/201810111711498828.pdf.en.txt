Introduction: Mr. Mohammad Sadiq Sheikh Amir, a daily wage worker, Senior Geologist's Office, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Yavatmal, spoke about regularization in government service. Complaint ULP No. 46/2011 was filed in the Industrial Court, Yavatmal. The request of the applicant under the order dated 29th November, 2016 of the Hon'ble Industrial Court has been partially accepted and it is decided to send a proposal to the Government to retain the applicant in Government service and not to remove the applicant till the Government takes a decision on the said proposal. The Industrial Court has passed an order. Accordingly, the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, vide reference No. The proposal has been submitted under letter No. 2 of the Industrial Court. Considering the order dated 29.11.2016, it was under the consideration of the Government to take a decision on the proposal to regularize the services of Mr. Mohammad Sadiq Sheikh Amir on daily wages.
Government Decision: Shri. In the case of Complaint ULP No. 46/2011 filed in the Industrial Court, Yavatmal. The court ruled. In accordance with the order dated 29.11.2016, the Government is pleased to regularize their services on daily wages in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under this Department from the date indicated in column No. 3 of the following prospectus to the vacant post in the open category of" Guard" in Class-IV cadre subject to the terms and conditions thereunder.
