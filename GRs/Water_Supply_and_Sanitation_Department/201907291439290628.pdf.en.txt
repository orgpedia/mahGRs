Preamble: The Central Government has planned to implement Jal Shakti Abhiyan as a time bound programme for water conservation and efficient irrigation. Under this campaign, water conservation and irrigation will be implemented in about 1500 drought-affected taluks of India. The programme will be implemented in two phases under the Grama Swaraj Abhiyan. From July 1, 2019. It will be held on September 15, 2019.
And then the second phase. from October 2, 2019. It will be held on November 30, 2019. The objective of the Mission is to provide sustainable water supply by creating efficient irrigation system and water conservation works through mass movement by preparing a plan in coordination with all the concerned departments in an integrated manner at the state level. A Jal Shakti Abhiyan Coordination Cell was being set up in the Water and Sanitation Support Agency at the state level for coordination of matters, collection of information on it, processing it, reviewing the work at the district level and preparing its report.

Functions and Responsibilities of the Class
(i) To regulate and co-ordinate the functioning of the State Level Committees to be constituted for co-ordination between the Central Government and the State Governments.
(ii) To follow up with the District Collectors, Chief Executive Officers and Executive Engineers, Zilla Parishads of the selected districts of the State for finalizing the water conservation plans related to the Mission.
(iii) Planning of television conferences to review the Jal Shakti Mission at the State level.
(iv) To plan and regulate the related meetings.
V. To follow up with the districts for filling up the various formats provided by the Central Government.
vi. State Nodal Officer to collect information from the districts for fixing targets for the activities prescribed in the selected districts under the Mission.
vii. Progress of Jal Shakti Abhiyan of the State and its components on the Central Government Portal (Source and Distribution of Funds, District Water Conservation Plan, Planning of use of media for this campaign, District level and Taluka level officials under the campaign)
