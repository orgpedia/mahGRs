The Proposal:
A total of 4022 permanent/ temporary posts of Class-I to Class-IV cadre in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions of Zila Parishads have been sanctioned vide Government Decisions No. 1 and 2 dated 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015 respectively.
Out of these posts, a total of 2002 temporary posts have been extended till 31.08.2021 as per the Government decision at Reference No.6. Temporary posts in rural water supply departments need to be extended for a further period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes. The temporary posts included in the figure of the department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but which have not been included in the figure. The Department of Finance is empowered to extend the period from 1.09.2021 to 28.02.2022

Government's decision
As per Government Decision, dated 15.04.2021, a total of 2002 temporary posts in various cadres in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Divisions under Zila Parishads have been extended. The 2002 temporary posts in the said Government decision are being allowed to continue from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 subject to the condition that the posts are not vacant for more than six months and no irregular appointments have been made to those posts.
The Finance Department has issued instructions to abolish 25% of the sanctioned posts of Group-D cadre as per Para 1 (5) of the Government Resolution dated 14.1.2016.
Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 22.3.2018 in the context of this Department, the existing 81 vacant posts of Group-D cadre in the Rural Water Supply Department of the Zilla Parishad have been abolished and the temporary posts have not been extended under this Government decision.

The expenditure on salary and allowances of a total of 1740 temporary posts of Class-III and Class-IV non-gazetted officers/ employees under the head' Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads (Local Area) (01) (05) Regular Establishment (2215 1881) under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961' shall be met from the provision approved in the financial year 2021-2022.
