Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year. """ Joint Director Group-A"" in Ground Water Survey and Development System with effect from 01.01.2016.""" A provisional seniority chart showing the position as on 01.01.2023 has been prepared and is annexed as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice and working in his department, working in the subordinate office of the Agency, retired from service and on deputation from the establishment of the Agency or who have changed cadre by other means. If there are any actual errors or errors in the seniority information, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate.
