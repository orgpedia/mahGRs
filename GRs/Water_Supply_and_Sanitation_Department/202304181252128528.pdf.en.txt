(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds under the said Government Decision as well as the certificates of utilization of the funds disbursed earlier along with the certificate of submission to the Accountant General. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. Also, the expenditure incurred should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's office from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
