GOVERNMENT DECISION, FINANCE DEPARTMENT NO.: FINANCE-2018/ PR No. 69/ FINANCE-3 dated 2nd April, 2018
The Proposal:
For meeting the non-salary expenditure on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishad vide Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zila Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions (District Level) (01) Assistance to Zila Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions Establishment Grants to Zila Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (Local Area) (01) (05) Regular Establishment (2215 1881), a budget provision of Rs. 10,23,28, 000/- has been made for the financial year 2018-2019 under the head 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
The matter of disbursement of funds under the above head of accounts in the current financial year to the District Councils fulfilling the conditions prescribed in the Government Decision dated 2.4.2018 of the Finance Department is under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1418/ PR No. 116/ Papu-23
Government's decision:
In order to meet the non-salary expenditure on establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in ZP, a grant of Rs. 18,00,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakh only) is being released to Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, ZP, Beed for the financial year 2018-19 under the head" Establishment Grants to ZP/ Panchayat Institutions under Section 183 of the Maharashtra ZP and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961- (Local Area) 01 (05) Regular Establishment (2215 1881)." The funds will be utilised by the concerned Zila Parishad for meeting the travel expenses, office expenses, lease, petrol/ oil/ lubricants of the employees of Rural Water Supply Department, as per requirement.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. The concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts disbursed. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the purpose for which they were allocated.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
These Government decisions are being issued within the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Government Circular, Finance Division No. 2018/ PR No. 69/ Finance-3, dated April 2, 2018. The terms and conditions mentioned in this circular are being fulfilled.
