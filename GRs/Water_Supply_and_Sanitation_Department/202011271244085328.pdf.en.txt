Preamble The Union Government has launched a new Jal Jeevan Mission by restructuring the National Rural Drinking Water Programme. The Central Government has launched the Jal Jeevan Mission. As per the guidelines issued on 25.12.2019, detailed guidelines for implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission in the State vide Reference No. 1 has been issued by the Government. Under the Jal Jeevan Mission, drinking water is supplied to the beneficiaries of rural scheduled castes in the special component scheme for scheduled castes. In the financial year 2020-2021, the budget outlay for the Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes (State share) is Rs. 50.00 crore out of which Rs. 7.5 crore (15%) has been made available by the Department of Finance (under head 2215 A097) to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance on Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS). This fund is provided by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance to this department. 2 is distributed by the decision of the government.
