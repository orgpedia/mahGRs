GOVERNMENT DECISION The revised administrative sanction for the Mouje Pardi Takmore Water Supply Scheme (T.G. Washim) at a revised cost of Rs. 3,38,05, 000/- (Dhoble) (Rupees Three Crores Thirty-eight Lakhs Five Thousand only) is being accorded subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 6 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Department, Washim that the scheme has been actually completed.
As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned executive engineer, zilla parishad, executive engineer, Maharashtra life faculty, contractor and the concerned gram panchayat officer should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to the CAG report of 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state are in the process of non-payment of power dues (Poor maintenance/ nonpayment) due to lack of maintenance.
