Now the matter of distributing the next salary fund to the district councils is under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
For the financial year 2023-24, a total amount of Rs. 7,19,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crore and Nineteen Lakh only) is being sanctioned to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the concerned Zilla Parishad as shown in the' Statement A' accompanying this Government decision to meet the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration, (01) Establishment, (01) (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Mandatory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)."
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. Also, the expenditure incurred should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's office from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed.
4) Before drawing and spending the funds from the treasury, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
Expenditure from the fund sanctioned through this Government Resolution should be spent under the head Demand No.Y2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub-Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (Mandatory) (22151843) 01, Salary" and it should be met from the provision sanctioned for the financial year 2023-24.
