Preamble To implement Jal Jeevan Mission in the State Reference No. 1 d. The guidelines have been issued under the Government decision dated 04.09.2020. The guidelines of Jal Jeevan Mission envisage separate allocation of 2% of the funds received from the Central Government under Jal Jeevan Mission programme under Water Quality Control and Surveying (WQMS) and 5% under Support Activities. The Central and State funding pattern is 60:40 for the aided component and the Water Quality Control and Surveying component. Under the Jal Jeevan Mission, funds equivalent to the funds provided by the Central Government should be distributed under the State Account.
