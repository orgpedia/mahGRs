The Proposal:
The provident fund of all the employees/ officers working with Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran since 1982-83 is deposited with the Government under Account Head-8336 Civil Deposit as a contribution/ statutory deposit from the Authority. The interest is paid to the Authority under head of accounts 20491268 in the prevailing manner at the rate of interest of Provident Fund on the total amount deposited with the Government at the end of March.

2. Deposit of provident fund of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran to the Government. As on 31.3.2020, Rs. 309,99,18, 659/- has been reported by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran vide letter dated 9.10.2020 and 15.12.2020. On this amount, at the prevailing rate of interest on provident fund, for the nine months from April, 2020 to December, 2020, the total amount of Rs. 16,50,70, 669/- is admissible as interest.
3) Out of the above amount of interest, Rs. 2.50 crore has been released to the Authority vide Government Resolution dated 11.11.2020 and now the balance amount of Rs. An amount of Rs. 14.0070 crore towards interest is yet to be paid to the Authority. However, out of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2020-21, only Rs. An amount of Rs. 7.50 crore is available on the budgetary system and a proposal to adjust this amount through book adjustment is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Demand No.Y-1, 2049 Interest Payments (01) (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268) to meet the cost of interest on provident fund of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for nine months from April, 2020 to December, 2020 in the financial year 2020-21. 7,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Lakhs only) is being released under this Government decision.
The expenditure from the provision sanctioned under this Government decision shall be adjusted by book adjustment and the expenditure shall be met from the sanctioned grant for the financial year 2020-21 by showing the expenditure under the head Demand No.Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268).
