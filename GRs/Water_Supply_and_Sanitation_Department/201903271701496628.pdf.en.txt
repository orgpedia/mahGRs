Preamble As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under this Department.
Under the Government decision at Reference No. 2, all the administrative departments shall be entitled to the temporary posts included in their contours and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but which have not been included in the contours. The extension was granted till 28.02.2019. Accordingly, a total of 273 temporary posts, namely 91 in the head" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 182 in the head" 2702 Small Irrigation Managements" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department have been filled up as per the decision of the Government in the reference No. 3. 01.10.2018 to D. The period was extended till 28.02.2019.
As per the decision of the government at reference number 4, now these temporary posts will be given. 01.03.2019 to D. The concerned administrative departments have been empowered to grant extension till 30.09.2019.
Accordingly, vide letter No. 5 under reference, the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, has sanctioned 91 posts in the head" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 182 posts in the head" 2702 Minor Irrigation Managers" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, taking the total number of temporary posts to 273. To be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: APNA 0218/ PR No. 273/ PAPU 15, dated 28th March, 2019
An extension has been requested. Considering these matters, the matter of extending the tenure of these temporary posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Considering the urgency of the work in the Ground Water Survey and Development System and considering the extension of the temporary posts from 01/10/2018 to 22/02/2019, the Government is approving the continuation of 91 posts in the head of Accounts" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 182 posts in the head of Accounts" 2702 Small Finance Managers" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, totalling 273 temporary posts (attached statement-a nisar) from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019.
The expenditure incurred on these posts should be met from the sanctioned provisions of the financial year under the respective head of accounts.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Ministries/ Departments under Government Decision No. 5 of the Department of Finance.
