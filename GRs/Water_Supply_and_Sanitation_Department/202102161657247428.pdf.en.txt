Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to appoint a supplier to provide a new duplex digital copier machine for the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself), Go to Hospital Complex Building, Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mantralaya, Mumbai-01 on lease basis (for a period of one year from the date of installation of the machine) for a period of one year (including supply of spare parts, papers, toner ink and services of private machinists). Accordingly, following the prevailing procedure, the sealed tariff sheets were sought from the website of the Government of Maharashtra vide Notice dated 01.02.2021. Accordingly, a total of 05 tariff sheets received by the Department were considered in the meeting held on 09.02.2021 in the chamber of Joint Secretary (Papu-02/ Registration Branch). The meeting decided to appoint a supplier who has submitted the lowest bidder from among the qualified bidders in the technical examination, to provide a new duplex digital copier machine for this department on lease basis for 1 year (including supply of spare parts, papers, toner ink and services of private machinists).
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In a meeting held under the chairmanship of Joint Secretary, Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (Papu-02/ Registration Branch), the eligible suppliers were selected from the eligible suppliers. Copy Classic (M/ s. Copy Classic), A 5/1, Opposite Shivshahi Library, Near Chaudhary House, Shivaji Nagar, Vakola, Santacruz (East), Mumbai- 400 054.
Accordingly, a new Duplex Digital Copier Machine in the premises of Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself), GoT Hospital Complex Building, Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 001 will be provided on lease basis for a period of one year (including supply of spare parts, papers, toner ink and services of private machinists) from 17.02.2021 to 16.02.2022. The rates of Copy Classic (M/ s Copy Classic), A 5/1, Opposite Shivshahi Library, Near Chaudhary House, Shivaji Nagar, Vakola, Santacruz (East), Mumbai- 400 054 have been accepted by the Department on the terms and conditions mentioned below.

1) In the tariff, the new copier machine with all the facilities of Canon's brand Canon IR 2525/2535 (New Brand) of Canon Company will be rented out to provide the service with the service of the machine operator and 75 GSM paper. 0.33/- (Rupees Thirty Three only) (including toner, drum, spare parts and preservatives etc.) has been approved by the Department. The machine should be equipped with heavy duty type of machine with an average speed of more than 80 per minute for printing on 75 GSM A-3 and A-4 size paper. Any increase in the rate during the lease period
However, there will be no increase in the rates as mentioned in the tariff agreement.
2) The copier machine will remain in the possession of this Department for the period from 17.02.2021 to 16.02.2022.
3) The details of the model and serial number of the said machine, along with the opinion of the supervisory officer/ staff of each machine, should be submitted to the Registration Branch, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Go to Hospital Complex Building, Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 001.
4) The shadows that are drawn on the machine must be legible. If not, such copies will be subtracted from the total number of copies. Also, it will be mandatory to submit the payment by deducting the number of waste copies as 2% of the total number of printed copies.
5) The remaining amount will be paid by deducting the necessary deductions as per government rules from the amount of payment submitted by the supplier every month.
6) If the machine operator is absent on any office working day or is unable to provide the service agreed by this agreement due to technical malfunctions in the machine or any other reasons, then a fine of Rs. A fine of Rs 500 will be imposed on him.
7) It will be mandatory to keep a separate register to keep an account of the copies taken out every day and to take a reading on it in front of the employees of the registration branch. Also, it is mandatory to obtain the signature of the officer/ employee of the registration branch or the personal assistant of the joint secretary/ deputy secretary (registration branch). The service report of the machine must be submitted to the registration branch.
8) It shall be binding on the supplier to comply with all the terms and conditions contained in the Notice dated 01.02.2021 issued by this Department. The Government shall have the right to terminate the contract with the supplier unilaterally at any stage if all the terms and conditions contained in the notice are not complied with satisfactorily.
