Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. The final seniority of the category of" Senior Geophysicist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been notified vide Government Circular No. 2. As per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Senior Geophysicist for the year 2015, 2016 and 2017 have been included in the provisional common seniority list of the officers of the cadre of Senior Geophysicist in the Ground Water Survey and Development System showing their status as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure A to this circular.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is hereby informed that this circular should be brought to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice and working in your department, working in the subordinate office of the agency, retired from service and on deputation from the establishment of the agency or who have changed cadre by any other means. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government along with the opinion of the Directorate by September 24, 2018 after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it is possible to make necessary changes in the seniority information by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining the seniority of Senior Geophysicist Cadre only as per Government Notification, General Administration Department, No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
