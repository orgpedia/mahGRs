Mr. Prashant Anand Khardiwar is being appointed as Assistant Administrative Officer, Group-B, Ground Water Survey and Development System, on a temporary basis from the date of his physical appearance, subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The pay scale of the candidate is Rs. 9300-34800+ Grade Pay 4400/- (S- 15:41800-132300 as per 7th Pay Commission).
(2) In addition to the above pay scale, Dearness Allowance, House Rent Allowance, Local Supplementary Allowance will be admissible at such rates as may be determined by the Government from time to time.
(3) The candidate should be registered with the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution No. 2715/ No. 302/ Work-8D. The prescribed probation policy as on 22/06/2021 shall continue to apply.
(4) No increment will be admissible to the candidate during the probation period. In case the candidate is not under probation after satisfactory completion of probation period, the pay hike and collateral dues as admissible will be paid.
(5) If the candidate fails to maintain the expected standard of work during the probation period or if the conduct is found to be improper, not up to the expected standard or unsatisfactory, his service shall be terminated without any prior notice or he shall be eligible for reduction in service.
Knowledge of Computer Handling/ Usage has been prescribed as a requisite qualification for appointment in Government Service vide Government Order, General Administration Department No. Training-2000/ S. No. 61/2001/39, dated 07/08/2001. Accordingly, it is mandatory for the candidate to provide the required certificate under the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. Training-2000/ PR No. 61/2001/39, dated 19/03/2003 within two years from the date of appointment order. If he does not provide the certificate within the prescribed time, then his service in the post of Assistant Administrative Officer will be terminated without any prior notice on the expiry of the said period.
7) The appointment of the said candidate is being made subject to the rules prescribed for direct service entry appointment for the post of Assistant Administrative Officer.
8) The candidate must have passed the prescribed departmental examination. Also, if they have not already passed the departmental examination or have not been exempted from passing the examination, they will have to pass the departmental examination, Marathi and Hindi language examination as per the prescribed rules.
The salary of the candidate should be fixed as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981.
