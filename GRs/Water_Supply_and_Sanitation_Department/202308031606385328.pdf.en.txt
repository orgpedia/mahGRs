Introduction: Read here. The policy on the use of vehicles in government and other offices has been set under the Government Decision No. 1. Under this government decision, the administrative department has been directed to review the vehicles and vehicle categories taking into account the responsibilities of the officials who have been handed over the vehicles. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune, under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, had submitted a proposal to the Government under the letter No. 2 to approve the review of vehicles after reviewing the vehicles with all the offices under the Directorate. Read No. As per the procedure laid down under Section 1, the proposal was submitted for approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee and as per the approval given by the committee, the matter of allowing the vehicle for groundwater survey and development system was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Considering the duties and responsibilities of the posts on the establishment of the Ground Water Survey and Development System, the vehicle review is being approved with the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee to make the vehicle permissible for the posts for which the vehicle facility is required as follows:
