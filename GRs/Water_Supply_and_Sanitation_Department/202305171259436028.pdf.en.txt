2) Government Circular, General Administration Department, No: SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ Work 12, dated July 13, 2021.
Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year. A provisional seniority chart showing the status of" Junior Geologist" Group-B in the Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01.01.2016 to 01.01.2023 has been prepared and attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice who are retired from service working in their department, subordinate office of the Agency and who are on deputation from the establishment of the Agency or have changed cadre by other means. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate.
