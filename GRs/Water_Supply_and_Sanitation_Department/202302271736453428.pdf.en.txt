Conditions
1. All remaining works should be completed within the stipulated time at the revised cost as per Annexure-A.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad concerned shall be responsible to take over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad, Buldana that the scheme has been actually completed. The concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad concerned will not receive any assistance from the Government for meeting recurring expenditure incurred annually for implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to complete the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21 per cent of the water supply schemes in the state were closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
7. Action to be taken as per the prevailing policy on trespass.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval to the scheme should be strictly followed.
10. It should be ensured at the Zilla Parishad level that the tender process has been carried out properly.
