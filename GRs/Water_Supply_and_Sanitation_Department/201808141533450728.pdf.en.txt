For the year 2018-19, a total amount of Rs. 32,54,45, 000 (Rupees Thirty Two Crore Fifty Four Lakh Forty Five Thousand Only) has been provided in the Budget for repayment of principal and interest on loans raised by Mumbai Metropolitan Development Authority (MMRDA) for Vasai Virar Sub-Regional Water Supply Scheme, as per the Government Decision dated 10.06.2002 and issued from time to time by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Last year, the Finance Department did not provide the amount due in December 2017, as it needed funds for the farmer loan waiver, but it was provided in the 2018-19 fiscal. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 16,82,71, 701/- has been spent so far under this head. The Department now has an outlay of Rs. 15,71,73, 299 crore for the year 2018-19.
As per the proposal received from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and considered by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, a total amount of Rs. 15,70,34, 545/- (Rupees Fifteen Crore Seventy Lakh Thirty Four Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Five only) towards pre-payment of principal and interest as on 31.08.2018 was under consideration of the Government for disbursement to the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority during the current year i.e. 2018-19.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the period from 04.03.2010 to 13.03.2014, the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) is releasing to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, the amount of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 15,70,34, 545/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores Seventy Lakhs Thirty Four Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Five only) towards principal amount of Rs. 14,89,15, 061/- and interest amount of Rs. 81,19,484/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores Seventy Lakhs Thirty Four Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Five only) to be provided to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) as on 31.08.2018, towards pre-payment of loan taken by MRA from Vasai Virar Sub-Regional Water Supply Scheme.
(a) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure of the above sanctioned amount.
(b) The amount so sanctioned shall be deposited in a separate specified account opened for the purpose. This money should not be used for any other purpose. This should be informed to the government.
e) Utilization certificates of these sanctioned grants are to be issued by the Authority. The amount of expenditure shown in it should be certified and signed by the Director (Finance) of the Authority and the certificates should be counter-signed to the Member Secretary of the Authority. Copies of the Utilization Certificate should be sent to the Accountant General, Maharashtra, Mumbai/ Nagpur and the Subordinate and Accounts Officer.
(e) The Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall take action for reconciliation of the expenditure made from this sanctioned grant and report the same to this Department (Office No. to send (20). Also, the information about the withdrawal of money from the treasury should be sent to this department with a standard number.
The payment, drawn up with the signature of Mr. S. Dange, Director (Finance) and signed by Mr. Y. T. Padvi, Deputy Secretary, shall be submitted to the Accounts and Accounts Officer, Mumbai. For this, Director (Finance) (Mr. Su.Se Dange), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Also, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department (Mr. Y.T. Padvi) is being declared as the Controlling Officer. As the sanctioned amount of the said payment is for the payment of principal and interest on the loan raised by the government, the amount should be immediately credited to the accounts of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, Mumbai through ECS system.
