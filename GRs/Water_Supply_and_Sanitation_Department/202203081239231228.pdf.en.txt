The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees of government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. Subsequently, the said Government has issued instructions under the contextual notification/ circulars No. 4 and 5 superseding the said decision. Provisional Common Seniority Report showing the status of Planning Officer (Group-B) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been published vide Government Circular No.2 herein and Provisional Common Seniority Report dated 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 vide Government Circular No.3 herein. In accordance with the instructions issued by the General Administration Department, the officers included in the selection list for the post of Planning Officer (Group-B) from the year 2011 to 2017 will be included in the cadre of Planning Officer (Group-B) in the Ground Water Survey and Development System with effect from 01/01/2012
