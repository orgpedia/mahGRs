the prelude
The Central Government has kept 50% of the funds allocated to Gram Panchayats under the Fifteenth Finance Commission in a tied form and has instructed that it should be used mainly for drinking water and sanitation schemes. Under the 15th Finance Commission, the funds to be distributed to the Gram Panchayats are distributed by the Rural Development Department. The Rural Development Department has taken a decision to disburse the 15th Finance Commission funds. Released on June 29, 2020.
While disbursing the tied funds from this fund, the government was considering issuing clear guidelines through the Water Supply and Sanitation Department to be used for rural water supply scheme programs under the Jal Jeevan Mission.
Government circular:
At present, the tied funds allocated to the Gram Panchayats under the Fifteenth Finance Commission should be used on priority for water supply works. Accordingly, the following instructions should be followed to ensure that the tied share of the funds allocated to the Gram Panchayats under the Fifteenth Finance Commission should be used only for providing 100% Functional Household Tap Connections (FHTCs) by the respective Gram Panchayats till further orders.
a) The villages which have been included under Jal Jeevan Mission, Reference No. The 5. 30.6.2020 The Government has decided to complete the Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) works at the Gram Panchayat level for the selected Executive Engineers for corrective re-connection. The administrative approval can be given to the Gram Panchayat within the limits of the administrative powers of the Gram Panchayat regarding the use of the Finance Commission funds given by the Rural Development Department. The Deputy Engineer, Water Supply and Sanitation Sub-Division, District Council will be competent to give technical approval to such works.
