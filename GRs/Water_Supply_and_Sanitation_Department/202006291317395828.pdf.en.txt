The Proposal:
Satish D. Umrikar was appointed as the chairman of the committee. The appointment was made by the first appointment in the government service to the post of" Joint Director" as recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of the officers/ employees of the Government service and since the officers posted to the post of Joint Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System have satisfactorily completed their probationary period of 2 years and the same has been recommended by the Directorate in Reference No. 3, the Government was considering to appoint Mr. Satish Umrikar as a substitute for regular" Joint Director" by terminating his probationary period.
