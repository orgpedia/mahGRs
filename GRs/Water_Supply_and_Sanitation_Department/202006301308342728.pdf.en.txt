Introduction: On the lines of the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan of the Central Government, the State Government has launched the Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan (UMA) scheme to involve students of engineering colleges, technical and other educational institutions in the State to find solutions using technology on various social and development-related problems in the State.
