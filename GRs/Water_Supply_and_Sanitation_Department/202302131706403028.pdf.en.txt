The Proposal:
All government services should be made available to the general public through information technology at affordable, transparent and fast rates. The e-governance policy of the state has been announced under the Government Decision No. 1. Accordingly, the purchase of computers and other materials for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the Commissioner of Interior, the Ground Water Survey and Development System, the State of Maharashtra and its subordinate offices was under the consideration of the government.
