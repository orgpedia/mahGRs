The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. A budget provision of Rs. 185,61,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Five Crores Sixty Six Lakh only) has been made for the financial year 2018-19 under the head 22158041 as grant to the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. The Department of Finance, vide the above circular, has made available 70% of the said provision on Budgetary System for the year 2018-19. Accordingly, in the year 2018-19, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran has

Under the head of accounts, the government was considering to disburse funds for three months from April 2018 to June 2018.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 34,26,60, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Four Crores Twenty Six Lakh Sixty Thousand only) out of the amount budgeted in the year 2018-19 under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (2215A041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under this Government decision subject to the following conditions.
(a) The Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran should first deposit the amount of Rs. 19,47,57, 312/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Forty Seven Lakhs Fifty Seven Thousand Three Hundred Twelve only), which is left unspent out of the total fund disbursed for the year 2017-18 towards salary/ allowances, to the Government.
(b) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for the payment of salary/ allowances to the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2018-19. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
Technical charges and ETP charges collected by the Authority at the end of June 2018 for the year 2018-19 should be deposited with the Government.
Sudhakar Dange has been appointed as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. M. B. Sawant, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been appointed as the Controlling Officer.
