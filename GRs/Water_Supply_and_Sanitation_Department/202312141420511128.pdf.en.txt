The Proposal:
Action is being taken by the department as per the instructions given by the government regarding the use of e-office system. In this proceeding, a proposal was received vide letter dated 29.05.2023 to streamline the functioning of the office of the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune under the department and to make the administration system dynamic and efficient, as well as to upgrade the necessary infrastructure of the decision base system being created for groundwater management in the state to the data center. Accordingly, the Project Implementation Committee in its meeting dated 30.05.2023 has approved the procurement of Computing and other ancillary materials in the Infrastructure Data Centre for the office of the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune from the budgetary provision for the financial year 2023-24. The matter of granting administrative approval for the expenditure to be incurred for this work was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Action is being taken by the department as per the instructions given by the government regarding the use of e-office system. The proceedings will involve streamlining the functioning of the office of the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune under the department and computer and other ancillary materials as shown in the table below to make the administration system dynamic and efficient, as well as to update the necessary infrastructure of the decision base system being created for groundwater management of the state in the data centre.
