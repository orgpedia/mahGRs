The Proposal:
Through the rural sanitation program, the villagers should be actively and consistently involved in the campaign/ program implemented by the government to raise the health, living standards of the rural people and to show the importance of the program, the Sant Gadge Baba Gram Swachhta Abhiyan, Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Swachh Gram Competition is being implemented from 2000-01. The criteria for the competition are determined every year to examine the Gram Panchayats participating in this campaign, and government decisions are issued at the government level. Accordingly, guidelines have been issued for the year 2019-20 under the contextual Government decisions.
As restrictions and restrictions have been imposed in the state of Maharashtra since March 2020 to prevent the spread of Corona virus, Sant Gadge Baba Gram Swachhta Abhiyan has not been implemented in the year 2020-21 and 2021-22. Therefore, as per the norms laid down under the Government Decision dated 23/07/2019, it was under the consideration of the Government to conduct a combined competition for a period of two years from 2020-21 to 2021-22.
Government's decision
Accordingly, considering the criteria set under the Government Decision dated 23/07/2019, a combined competition should be held for the year 2020-21 to 2021-22.
The composition, procedure, examination, criteria and amount of prizes of the committee under Sant Gadge Baba Gram Swachhta Abhiyan will be as mentioned in the Government Decision dated 23/07/2019.
