The Proposal: -
A policy decision has been taken to provide subsidy to the Zila Parishad by the Government for the salary of eligible employees working on Converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishments on Rural Water Supply Schemes under the Zila Parishad. The Cabinet has taken a meeting dated 17th November, 2022 and accordingly the Government Decision dated 30th January, 2023 has been issued. Considering the expenditure incurred by the Zila Parishads in the financial year 2025-26, further disbursement of funds to these Zila Parishads for regular salaries of the employees employed is under consideration of the Government.
