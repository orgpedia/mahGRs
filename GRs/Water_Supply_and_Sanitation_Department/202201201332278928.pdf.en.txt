The guidelines of Jal Jeevan Mission envisage separate allocation of 2% of the funds received from the Central Government under Jal Jeevan Mission programme under Water Quality Control and Surveying (WQMS) and 5% under Support Activities. The Central and State funding pattern for water quality control and survey and assisted component is 60:40.
The Central Government has released Rs. 1666.6368 crore for Water Quality Control and Surveying and Aided Subjects. 116.7 crore. Accordingly, as per the Central and State Finance Figures (60:40) under the said head, the Finance Department (Head of Accounts 2215 9971) has released Rs. 77.8 crore has been made available on the available budgetary allocation system. This fund is required to be made available to the State Nodal Account of the State Water and Sanitation Mission.
Government's decision
