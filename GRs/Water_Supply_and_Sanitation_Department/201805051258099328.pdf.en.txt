Government Resolution No: MUPAYO-2018/ PR No.78/ PAPU-19 was under consideration of the Government to provide powers to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad to accept tenders for water supply schemes and to relax the condition of permission for road/ rail/ forest crossings required for issuing commissioning orders.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad is being empowered to accept tenders for water supply schemes being implemented by the Zilla Parishad under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme at rates up to 5% above the assessed value as per the current schedule. Tenders for such schemes need not be submitted to the government for prior approval. However, after the acceptance of the tender, the details of such schemes should be informed to the government without delay. Also, before accepting the tender for an additional rate of up to 5%, the work should be negotiated with the concerned contractor at the budgeted rate.
The condition of this permission is being relaxed for issuing work orders for the water supply schemes under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme for which permission is required for road/ railway/ forest crossing. Therefore, there is no question of issuing work orders for water supply schemes for which permission for road/ forest/ railway crossing is still unavailable, but the tender process has been completed. However, these permissions (road/ rail/ forest crossing) should be taken on time while carrying out the work of the scheme.
