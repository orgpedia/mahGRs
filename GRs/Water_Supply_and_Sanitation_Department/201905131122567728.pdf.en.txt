Preamble: The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government for administrative sanction of works under drinking water scarcity for Parbhani City of Parbhani Municipal Corporation for the scarcity period 2018-19. This proposal was considered for approval in the meeting held on 09.05.2019 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department for giving administrative approval to the urgent water supply schemes/ measures beyond the prescribed financial and physical parameters for mitigation of drinking water scarcity in urban and rural areas.
As the proposed shortage measures are required to be taken by the Parbhani Municipal Corporation, the matter of administrative approval of the proposed work of Rs. 22.75 lakh for desilting, deepening and related works is under the consideration of the Government.

Government's decision
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the proposal of Parbhani Municipal Corporation to take measures under drinking water scarcity for Parbhani city for the scarcity period of 2018-19 at a cost of Rs. 22.75 lakh (Rupees Twenty Two Lakh Seventy Five Thousand only) as mentioned in the Preamble is being accorded administrative approval under this Order subject to the following conditions:
1) The District Collector, Parbhani should first declare that there is a shortage of drinking water in Parbhani city.
The government will provide a subsidy of Rs. 20.47 lakh i.e. 90% of the total cost of Rs. 22.75 lakh. The remaining amount i.e. 10% of the scheme is to be paid by the Parbhani Municipal Corporation as public contribution amounting to Rs. 2.28 lakh.
(3) Only after the Parbhani Municipal Corporation has spent 10 per cent of the population under the scheme in the first place, the remaining funds should be distributed to the Parbhani Municipal Corporation through the Divisional Commissioner.
4) This scheme should be implemented through Parbhani Municipal Corporation.
The tender process must be completed by May 29, 2019.
Approval of the project will be cancelled if the project is not completed by that date.
It is necessary to start water supply from the scheme by completing the works of the scheme by June 15, 2019.
7) The work of the scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner and it should be ensured that the work of the scheme is completed within the approved price.
8) The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad shall make available to the District Collector, Parbhani, funds equivalent to 50% of the grant-in-aid payable at the beginning of the scheme for the said scheme.
The remaining 30% of the funds should be disbursed after considering the progress of the scheme at the level of Divisional Commissioner. The remaining 20% will be paid after the project is completed.
9) Since the work of the scheme must be completed on time, a progress report should be sent to the government every Monday of the week from the tender process of the scheme to the start of water supply from the scheme.
