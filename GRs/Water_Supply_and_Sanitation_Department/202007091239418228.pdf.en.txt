The Proposal:
To meet the salary and non-salary expenses of Class 1 and Class 2 officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishad, a budget provision of Rs. 47.4905 crore has been made for the financial year 2020-21 under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for Implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843). Out of this provision, a total amount of Rs. 10.2490 crore has been released to the Zila Parishads towards their salary expenditure as per the Government decision in Table No. 3 and 4.
However, as the salary fund is insufficient, the matter of disbursement of subsidy to the Zila Parishads for the next salary expenditure is under consideration of the Government, taking into account the total salary expenditure on the treasury of the respective Zila Parishads for the year 2020-21.
