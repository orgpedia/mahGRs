The Proposal:
The provident fund of all the employees/ officers working with Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran since 1982-83 is deposited with the Government under Account Head-8336 Civil Deposit as a contribution/ statutory deposit from the Authority.
The interest on the total amount deposited with the government at the end of March is paid at the interest rate of the provident fund in the prevailing manner under the head of accounts 2049 1268.
The Government has a total of Rs. 287,22,89, 699/-. On this amount
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Budget-1419/ PR No.108/ Papu-23
A proposal is under consideration of the government to adjust the interest amount by book adjustment at the prevailing interest rate of provident fund.
Government's decision:
For the six months from April, 2019 to September, 2019 in the financial year 2019-20, the demand number Y-1, 2049, interest payments (01), interest on the provident fund of the Maharashtra Life Authority employees (mandatory) 45, interest (2049 1268) under the heading of Rs. 11,41,73, 516/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Forty One Lakh Seventy Three Thousand Five Hundred Sixteen only) is being released under this Government decision.
The expenditure from the provision sanctioned under this Government decision shall be adjusted by way of book adjustment and the expenditure shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20 by showing the expenditure under the head Demand No.Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268) for payment of interest on provident fund of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
The funds sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be deposited by book adjustment under the head" 8336-Civil Deposits, 800 Other Deposits, (00) (08) Maharashtra Water Supply and Sewerage Board Employees Provident Fund (8336 5067)."
This Government decision is in accordance with the Government Circular No.Finance-2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3 of the Department of Finance, dated 1st April, 2019 and Government Circular No.Finance-2019/ PR. No.92/ Earth-3, dated 8th July, 2019 is being issued in the powers conferred to the Administrative Departments.
This fund is the interest amount on the provident fund and since the book adjustment of this fund is being done, the conditions mentioned in paragraphs 7 to 18 of this government circular as well as other conditions and conditions mentioned in the Annexure are being fulfilled.
