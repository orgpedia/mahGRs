The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. The said Bill, having been assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra, has been published in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV dated 27th March, 2018 as Maharashtra Act No. 22 of 2018. Further, the Budgetary allocation of mandatory expenditure required for the first nine months of the year 2018-2019 (ending December, 2018) has been made by the Department of Finance through a circular on Budgetary System with a ceiling of 70% of the total annual allocation.

Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Samitis Under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, a budget provision of Rs. 8,76,56, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Seventy Six Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand only) has been made for the financial year 2018-2019 under the head Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads- (Local Area) (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees on Regular Establishment (2215 1914).
Now, the Finance Department, vide the above circular, has made available 70% of the said provision for the year 2018-2019 on the budgetary system. Accordingly, in the year 2017-2018, considering the actual expenditure incurred by the respective District Councils under the above head, the matter of disbursement of funds for the three months from April, 2018 to June, 2018 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2018-19, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions (District Level) (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (Local Area) (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees on Regular Establishment (2215 1914)" 36, for the Assistant Grants (Salary), the total grant as shown in column 3 of statement A accompanying this Government decision is Rs. 1,80,50, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Eighty Lakh Fifty Thousand only) to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officers of the respective Zilla Parishads.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in column No. 3 of the return A accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
