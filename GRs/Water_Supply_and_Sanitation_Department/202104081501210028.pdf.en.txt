Read 1) Government Resolution No: WQM-2019/ No.49/ Papu-12, Dy. February 12, 2021 Preamble As per the decision dated February 12, 2021, the Government has decided to revise the charges for testing of private samples of water in the laboratory under the Ground Water Survey and Development (GWSD) system and also the policy for preliminary testing by FTK of private samples received in the laboratory. Accordingly, it has been provided that the procurement and distribution of field test kits should be done by the Ground Water Survey and Development System. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the purchase of Field Test Kits (FTK) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Administrative approval is being granted for procurement of Field Test Kits (FTK) required for private sampling of water in the laboratory under Ground Water Survey and Development System subject to the following conditions.
(1) It may be ensured that the expenditure for the above purpose is within the fund limit of Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh only) for the financial year 2021-22.
2) The expenditure for the above purpose will be met from 2% of the funds of Water Quality Control and Surveying Programme under Jal Jeevan Mission.
