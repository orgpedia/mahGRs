Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No: SRV-2011/ Q.No. 284/12, dated 21st October, 2011.
Government circular:
The General Administration Department, as per the instructions issued under the relevant Government decision, has prepared a provisional common seniority list showing the status of" Junior Geologist (Group-B)" cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. This seniority is being attached as Annexure-A to this circular.
The services of 45 junior geologists who were appointed to the post of Junior Geologist by the Government of this Department vide Government Decision No. Apna-2309/ PR No. 314/ Papu-15 dated May 04, 2012 for a period of two years, but did not pass the departmental examination at the prescribed opportunity, were terminated. Against this order. As per the decision of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, the services of 16 junior geologists, who passed out in 2008 on three occasions, have been reinstated with continuity and consequential benefits. The services of 21 Junior Geologists who have not passed the Departmental Examination have been restored on the condition that they will be provided a special opportunity to pass the Departmental Examination. The Special Departmental Examination for these 21 officers was conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on 04th and 05th July, 2013 and the result of this examination was declared on 05th December, 2013. Therefore, the original seniority of these officers cannot be maintained in this case. Therefore, the seniority of these officers in the cadre of" Junior Geologist" has been fixed from the date of passing the examination.
In this case, the Law and Justice Department has issued an informal reference number U.O.R. to Water Supply and Sanitation Department/ Papu-15No. 84/246/ Civil/ Confl./ 2016/ A Br. , Dt. As of March 22, 2017.

According to the feedback given, the seniority of the candidates who have not passed the departmental examination within the prescribed time has been taken into account from the day they passed the examination.
The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune is hereby informed that this circular should be brought to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in the subordinate office of the Agency, retired from service and are on deputation from the establishment of the Agency or have changed cadre in any other way. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The Directorate shall examine the service books and other available records of the officers and submit the same along with the opinion of the Director to the Government before 20th September, 2018. That is, it will be possible to make the necessary changes in the seniority list by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list will be considered for determining seniority in the cadre of Junior Geologist only as per Government Notification General Administration Department No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21/06/1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
