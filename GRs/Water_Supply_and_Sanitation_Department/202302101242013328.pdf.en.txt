The Proposal:
Expenditure on measures taken to alleviate water scarcity in urban areas of the State is met from the grants available from this Department under the head (2215A 201). The department has received the proposal of Konkan and Aurangabad division seeking funds for the measures actually completed during the 2016-17 and 2018-19 shortage period. During the financial year 2022-23, the funds budgeted under the target of Account Head (2215A 201) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Plan) were under consideration of the Government to disburse the funds for the measures demanded and actually completed by the Divisional Commissioners Konkan and Aurangabad.

Government's decision:
As per the norms laid down by the Government to address the drinking water shortage in urban areas, the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Aurangabad, has been allocated a sum of Rs. 126.30 lakh (Rs.1 crore 26 lakh 30 thousand only) under the expenditure on the measures actually completed for addressing the drinking water shortage in their respective divisions (Head of Accounts 2215A201).
The budget is being approved and released by the delivery system subject to the terms and conditions made as well as the following conditions.
