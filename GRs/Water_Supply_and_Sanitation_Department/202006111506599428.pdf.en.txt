The Proposal:
In order to meet the salary expenditure of daily wage workers on regular establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishad, a budget provision of Rs. 11.3076 crore has been made for the financial year 2020-21 under the head' 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zila Parishads (01), Assistance to Zila Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zila Parishads (01) (07), Daily Wage Workers on Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151914)" under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961.
The Department of Finance vide circular dated 16th April, 2020 has made available subsidy amounting to Rs. 2.8269 crore under target of 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on BIMS system.

3. The said funds are being released subject to the deposit of unutilized funds of 2019-20 under the aforesaid heads to the Government and the aforesaid funds shall not be drawn from the Treasury unless the unutilized funds are deposited with the Government.
The grants sanctioned under this Government decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts disbursed. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiary through ECS.
Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
(5) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed without receiving further information and demand letter.
