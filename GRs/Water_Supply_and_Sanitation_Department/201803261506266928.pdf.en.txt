The Proposal:
In the financial year 2017-18, in respect of Central share of grant received from the Government of India under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) under the Scheduled Tribes Area Sub-Plan (ST Sub-Plan), a Demand No. T-5, Accounts Head 2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 01 Water Supply, 796, Tribal Area Sub Plan (01) Schemes under Tribal Area Utilization (01) (09), National Rural Drinking Water Programme (Central Share, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried)) (2215 9853) under the head State Schemes (01) (09), Rs. 19,21,63, 000/- under the aforesaid Government Resolution No. Under Section 4, the Tribal Development Department has handed over the budget to this department through the distribution system. The remaining amount of Rs. A proposal for sanction of Rs. 8,22,78, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Seventy Eight Thousand only) to the implementing agencies for rural water supply schemes was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2017-18, the accounts for tap water supply schemes were 22159853 with a balance of Rs. 8,22,78, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Seventy Eight Thousand only) is being sanctioned and released to Zilla Parishad, Palghar through the Budgetary System for these units (BEAMS) in terms of coverage and quality on the following terms.
Conditions:
The subsidy should be spent on priority basis by the concerned Nodal Agencies for completion of works in the physically completed villages/ hamlets covered under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDP) as per the priorities mentioned in the Annual Action Plan for coverage and quality of tribal use and in the Centrally Sponsored Schemes in which Government participation is mandatory. Also, this subsidy should be spent on priority for completion of schemes in physically complete but financially incomplete villages/ wards/ Fluoride/ Arsenic, Sansad Adarsh Gram, Open Defecation Free villages/ wards.
