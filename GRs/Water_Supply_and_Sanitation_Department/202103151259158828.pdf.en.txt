GOVERNMENT Circular: In a letter dated 9th June, 2015 from Joint Secretary, Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin), Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation, New Delhi, the definition of Open Defecation Free Gram Panchayat has been clarified. Accordingly, guidelines have been issued for self-declaration and verification of open defecation free Gram Panchayats under Government Decision dated 27.10.2015 at Reference No.4 and for second phase verification of open defecation free Gram Panchayats under Reference No.5 dated 18.07.2019.
Now, the Joint Secretary, Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin), Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti, New Delhi vide letter dated 30th June, 2020 has detailed the procedure for declaration and verification of villages as ODF Plus under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-II.
Accordingly, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Gramin) Phase- Instructions are being issued to declare and verify the inner villages as Open Defecation Free Plus (ODF Plus).
As per the Government decision dated 27th October, 2015, all the Gram Panchayats in the rural areas (which did not exist as villages or habitations in the rural areas of the concerned district earlier) which were not declared and verified as open defecation free earlier, should be declared and verified as open defecation free.

As there is no main resource agency at the state and district level at present, for this verification, the CEO, Zila Parishad should verify the village by forming a committee or through inter-Taluk verification and accordingly the records of the villages should be done as per the directions of the Government of India in the SBM-MIS Government Resolution dated 27th October, 2015.
After the first phase of verification as per Government Decision, dated 27th October, 2015, to ensure the continuity of sanitation in the Gram Panchayats which have been declared Open Defecation Free as per the instructions in the Government Decision, dated 18th July, 2019, at Reference No. 5, the second phase of verification of Gram Panchayats should be done and the records of Gram Panchayats should be made on the SBM-MIS of the Central Government.
In order to provide ODF Plus status to the district by completing individual as well as public level sanitation works as per this Government Circular, the district should declare all the villages and the entire district as ODF Plus by 2024-25 in their area in a phased manner by meeting the following criteria, after planning year-wise.
Definition of ODF Plus:" ODF Plus means construction/ restoration of toilets, community toilet complexes, solid and waste water management and other ancillary works in villages rated as open defecation free for rural areas. The criteria for ODF Plus are as follows: (i) Open Defecation Free (ODF) Sustainability (ODFS) Toilet facilities in village schools, Anganwadis and Gram Panchayat offices.

At least 5 messages of Information, Education and Communication (IEC) on sanitation-free sustainability, proper use of toilets, solid waste and wastewater management, hygiene habits and sustainable sanitation etc. should be displayed in the village.
(ii) Solid Waste Management All public places (primary schools, anganwadi centres, gram panchayat offices) and at least 80% of households should have effective solid waste management.
Under Solid Waste Management, there should be an adequate system of classifying and collecting individual and public level compost peat, plastic waste for animal and farm waste etc.

(iii) Wastewater Management All public places (primary schools, anganwadi centres, gram panchayat offices) and at least 80% of households should have effective sewerage management.
Under the sewerage management by taking up individual and public level soak pits, drains, management of kitchen and bathing waste/ rain water/ septic tank discharge, black water etc.
(iv) Visible Sanitation of the Village: Visible sanitation of the village should be ensured by ensuring minimum generation of waste and minimum stagnant water in all public places and 80% of the households in the village and no plastic waste dumps.
Geo-tagging of individual and public level Solid Waste and Sewerage Management, completed public toilets, school and Anganwadi sanitation/ toilets etc., shall be done through the mobile application of SBM-Phase|| provided by the Central Government.
After self-declaration by the Gram Panchayat as Open Defecation Free Plus (ODF Plus), the mobile user should upload the accompanying" Form-B" on the SBM Phase II Mobile App.
(c) Verification process for villages declared ODF Plus: The declaration of a village declared ODF Plus shall be done on the mobile application of SBM-Phase|| followed by a thorough verification of the same by a tri-state agency designated at the state level within 90 days of the declaration of the village as ODF Plus. However, if at the time of verification, a third party does not exist, the entire verification of the village should be done by the committee mentioned at" D" below. The verification committee should complete the verification of a village in a maximum of 2 days.
If the above committee finds any error during the verification of the villages, the committee should give such feedback. These errors should be rectified by the concerned village within 1 month. After the error is corrected by the concerned village, if the village has fulfilled the criteria of ODF Plus by verifying the errors, again through the committee mentioned in" D" below, then the information about these villages being ODF Plus, the Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti should inform the Deputy CEO (Water and Sanitation) and the Deputy CEO (Water and Sanitation) from the district level should register the information of such villages from PM 05.

(i) The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad shall constitute a committee of officers and employees of the District/ Taluka/ Village level or non-official members. A quorum of the Committee shall consist of four members. The members of the committee will include district, taluk and village level officers, employees, sarpanches, gram panchayat members, swachhagrahis, anganwadi workers, health workers, women self-help groups, non-government members and other members notified by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad. The powers to induct members shall remain with the Chief Executive Officer, District Council.
(ii) To facilitate ODF Plus verification of the village, the verification committee of the village to be verified by the CEO, ZP, shall consist of the members mentioned above (i) except the officers at the district, taluka level, other members should be from the adjoining Gram Panchayat area. It should be ensured that no government/ non-government member belonging to the Gram Panchayat within the limits of the village to be verified shall be included in the verification committee.
(ii) No remuneration of any kind shall be admissible to the Verification Committee.
(iv) The members of the said Committee/ Organization should have detailed knowledge of ODF Plus verification. The training should be imparted at the district and taluk levels. The cost of training should be funded within the limits under the IEC component.
v) The committee/ institution after verification shall submit the report of verification in signature to the concerned Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti. The concerned Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti should submit the report at the district level.
vi) The responsibility of controlling this process should be fixed at the District Water and Sanitation Mission Cell at the district level.
(vi) While verifying ODF Plus of each village, it should be verified as per the accompanying Form A and the accompanying Forms D to I.
