Administrative approval is being granted under shortage subject to the following conditions: -
1) All the special repair works mentioned in the proposal are performed by the Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life.
The authority should be run through Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar.
2) The works of the scheme should be done by inviting e-tendering in the prescribed manner. High-Priced Bids
should not be accepted.
3) The tender of the scheme is being allowed for a short period (07 days).
4) It is necessary to complete the tender process and issue an immediate commissioning order.
5) After the completion of the tender process, the works of the scheme will be completed within one month from the date of completion of the scheme.
The water supply needs to start.
6) Ensure that the works of the scheme are completed immediately within the sanctioned / approved cost.
If the cost of the scheme increases, the government will not be responsible for it.
7) Subsidies are available from the government in case of any increase in the cost of the scheme for any reason.
It will not be done.
8) Submission subject to transfer of the plan to the Zilla Parishad on completion of the said works.
