The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparing and publishing seniority list of government officials/ employees every year. The Common Final Seniority of Senior Digging Engineer (Group-A) Category in Ground Water Survey and Development System dated 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been published vide Circular No. 2 dated 01/01/2015. As per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the names of the officers included in the selection list of 2015, 2016 and 2017 in the Senior Excavation Engineer (Group-A) cadre have been included in the provisional common seniority list showing the status of Senior Excavation Engineer (Group-A) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this letter.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to the post of Senior Excavation Engineer, Group-A, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, will also be considered.

If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the statements of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government before 23rd September, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority list after verifying them. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority indicated against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining the seniority of the Senior Excavation Engineer cadre only as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21/06/1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
