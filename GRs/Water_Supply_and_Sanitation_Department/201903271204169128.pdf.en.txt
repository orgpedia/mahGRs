The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
The government is committed to provide clean and safe drinking water to the people of the state. Jalswarajya Phase-II is being implemented with World Bank funding through the Improvement Support and Management Cell under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. For this work to be carried out properly, 10 temporary posts are required for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarojya Project.
Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 21.02.2019 of the Department of Finance, No. 4 read above, 10 temporary posts for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell at the establishment of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) shown in Form A attached to this Government Resolution and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarajya Project shown in Form B
The posts are allowed to be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
Expenditure on 10 temporary posts for Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on establishment of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head 3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467), should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2019-2020. The expenditure on salary and allowances for the 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jalswarajya Project should be made for the duration of the programme from the account number 31868434781 of State Bank of India, Belapur, Belapur Branch, Navi Mumbai.
