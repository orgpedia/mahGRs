Preamble: To provide powers to accept tenders for additional rates for temporary water supply schemes and special repairs to tap water schemes taken under drinking water scarcity, to give some other suggestions at the field level to deal with drinking water scarcity, etc.

It was under the consideration of the government. This proposal. The approval was given at the meeting of the Cabinet Sub-Committee held on 05.03.2019 under the Chairmanship of the Minister for Relief and Rehabilitation.
Accordingly, a government order was issued.
Government Circular: Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the following decisions are being taken regarding the measures to be taken for water supply under scarcity.
(1) It is hereby approved to authorize the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad to accept tenders for temporary water supply schemes and special repair of tap water supply schemes taken up under drinking water scarcity, at a rate up to 10% above the assessed price as per the current tariff schedule. These rights will remain in force till 30th June, 2019.
(2) All temporary water supply schemes and special repairs of tap water supply schemes shall be completed before 31st March, 2019.
(3) In the taluk where drinking water is being supplied through tankers on a large scale, proposals for temporary water supply schemes should be submitted based on the source from which these tankers are filled.
(4) A team of officers from the Water Supply and Sanitation Department from the ministerial level will review the tanker affected districts.
(5) Reference No. The circular dated December 27, 2018 had prescribed the modalities for disbursement of funds to Municipal Corporations, Municipalities and Municipal Councils.
However, now these instructions given through this circular are being partially changed and the funds provided to the municipal corporations, municipalities and municipal councils should be provided by the divisional commissioner through the collector.
