The contours of the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation have been fixed vide Government Decision No. 9,10,13 and 14 dated 04.02.2005, 17.04.2013, 26.05.2016 and 08.09.2020 respectively. Geologist and Deputy Engineer in the Ground Water Survey and Development System (GDS). The contours of the required posts in the District Councils in the cadre were fixed by the Government under Reference Government Decisions No. 11 and 12 dated 04.03.2011 and 04.08.2015 respectively. The revised figure of the required posts in the District Council has been fixed by the Government Resolution No. 15 dated December 28, 2022. The Finance Department has issued instructions for a fresh review of all government and semi-government posts. Accordingly, a review of the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System was carried out. The report was submitted to a high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The matter of fixing the revised pattern of posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as approved by this Committee was under consideration of the Government. The following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION 02 dated 13.12.2022 MAY. As per the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary, the revised figure of 1183 posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System is being approved under this government decision. The post wise details of these posts are as per Return No. 1 along with the details of district wise and office wise posts in Returns 2 and 3.

Consolidation of Posts: As approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, the designation of certain posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency will be as follows.
The designation of the cadre will be Geographic Information System Assistant by combining the two cadres of Geographic Information System Assistant and Surveyor. The posts of working surveyors will remain in the dead cadre of surveyors. After amalgamation of the said cadre, the functions and duties of the cadre of Geographic Information System Assistant, Geographic Information System Senior Assistant, Geographic Information System Officer, Service Access Rules and related service matters will be determined separately.
The designation of the cadre will be Assistant Applicant by combining two equivalent cadres namely Mechanics and Assistant Applicants. As the designation of Assistant Applicant is being kept by combining two equivalent cadres- Mechanic and Assistant Applicant, the working staff of Mechanic Cadre (excluding 5 posts) will be adjusted in the Assistant Applicant Cadre. The lowest 5 employees working as per the service level in the mechanical cadre will remain in the dead cadre. As the posts of Combined Assistant Applicant Cadre fall vacant, the above 5 posts of deceased cadre of Mechanics will be adjusted to the post of Regular Assistant Applicant. The functions and duties of the combined cadre of Assistant Applicants (Mechanics) will be revised separately. The functions and duties of the Mechanic and Assistant Applicant Cadre during the intervening period will now be applicable to the employees of the Combined Assistant Applicant Cadre.
