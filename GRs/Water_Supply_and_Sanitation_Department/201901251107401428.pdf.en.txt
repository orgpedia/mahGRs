The ad-hoc promotion of the above officers to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical), (Group-A) is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental enquiry is pending or proposed against them and subject to the following conditions:
(a) The said ad-hoc promotion shall be for the first 11 months or till the appointment of a regular Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) on the selection list or till the availability of a candidate from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, whichever is earlier.
(b) The above promotions are subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(e) This ad-hoc promotion will not entitle the officers concerned to regular promotion i.e. seniority.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The promoted officers should be relieved from work by the concerned office immediately and report to this department. Also, the promoted officers should join the office immediately. Any request for change in the post will not be entertained.
