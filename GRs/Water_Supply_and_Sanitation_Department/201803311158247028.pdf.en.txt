Reference No. for measures taken/ to be taken during the period of scarcity 2016-17 and 2017-18 The Department has received the proposal for seeking funds under letter No. 5 to 7. Also, the department must provide 50% of the funds for the measures approved by the government. For the financial year 2017-18, the Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation) was under the consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the Divisional Commissioners, Konkan, Nashik, Aurangabad and Amravati from the funds released by the Government vide its decision dated 31.03.2018 and the balance lying with the Department.
Government's decision:
To meet the expenditure on the measures taken/ to be taken to solve the drinking water shortage in the Konkan, Nashik, Aurangabad and Amravati divisions as per the criteria laid down by the Government for the solution of drinking water shortage in rural/ urban areas, Rs. 1878.24 lakhs under sub-account head no. 22452185 and Rs.
