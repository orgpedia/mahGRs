The Proposal:
Government decision The Maharashtra Sujal and Nirmal Abhiyan is being implemented from 22nd October, 2008 with the objective of providing adequate, quality services, affordable service charges and various technical, financial and managerial improvements in respect of urban water supply, sewerage, toilet construction and solid waste management.
The main objective of the Maharashtra Sujal Nirmal Abhiyan is to ensure universal access to water supply and sanitation facilities to all the citizens of the state.

The Water and Sanitation Assistance Society should inform the government about the allocation from the subsidy in this distribution.
The powers to examine the accounts of expenditure made by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for these schemes from this grant are being given to the Accountant General, Maharashtra, Mumbai/ Nagpur or the officer of this department who has been given special powers. The Water and Sanitation Assistance Society should submit the relevant documents to the Accountant General if requested by the Accountant General or the officer of the department given special powers.
As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure-II to the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority should be submitted to the Accountant General by the Water and Sanitation Support Society for inspection along with relevant documents as per the demand of the Accountant General.
The Water and Sanitation Assistance Society should sign the utilization certificates of the grants received from the Government or the grants that will be received in the next period by certifying the programme-wise amounts of disbursed grants and expenditure as indicated in the utilization certificate. The certificate of utility shall be signed by the Joint Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
The utilization certificate of this subsidy should be submitted to the government in the prescribed format and copies of the utilization certificate should also be sent to the Accountant General.
The expenditure of this grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 001, on the prescribed date.
