The 150th birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi, falls on October 2, 2019.
The Government of India pays homage to Mahatma Gandhi on his 150th birth anniversary. Accordingly, during the period from 02.10.2018 to 02.10.2020, various programmes under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Gramin) were being implemented and the matter was under consideration of the Government for further progress in respect of some of the important programmes.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS Under the Centrally Sponsored Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin), directions/ instructions have been issued to the District Councils under the relevant Government Orders/ Letters regarding the action to be taken for sustainable rural sanitation. Accordingly, the following programmes under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Gramin) on the occasion of 150th Birth Anniversary of the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi, should be implemented within the time frame given from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
