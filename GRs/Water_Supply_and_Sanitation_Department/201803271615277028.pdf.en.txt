As per the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated 17th January, 2018, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed on 18th January, 2018 with Israel Government Company (Mekorot) for conducting survey and other administrative work for preparation of pre-feasibility report of Water Grid Scheme to overcome the persistent water shortage in Marathwada region. As per the provisions of the MoU, the Comprehensive Agreement is required to be signed within a month, the Comprehensive Agreement has been signed on 21st February, 2018.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to constitute a high-powered committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra, to decide the scope of work in this contract, to make changes in it and to review the work to be done and to give the Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department the authority to coordinate the entire work from the government level.
Government Decision To determine the scope of work, to make changes in it and to review the work to be done as per the comprehensive agreement for the water supply scheme through the Marathwada Water Grid System. The Cabinet also approved the constitution of a high-powered committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Maharashtra, and the Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department to coordinate the entire work at the government level.
