In order to reduce the time expenditure for the immediate completion of the measures taken to alleviate the drinking water shortage, the government was considering changing the authority to accept tenders for water supply schemes to be implemented during this shortage period. This proposal. The approval was given at the meeting of the Cabinet Sub-Committee held on 18.12.2018 under the Chairmanship of the Minister for Relief and Rehabilitation.
