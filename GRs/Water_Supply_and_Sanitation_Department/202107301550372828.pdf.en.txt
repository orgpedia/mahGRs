Preamble: The Collector, Jalgaon has decided to extend the benefits under the Shortage Relief Scheme for the period 2020-21. A proposal for a well repair scheme at Bodwad, District Jalgaon at a cost of Rs. 5,88,843/- (Rupees Five Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Forty Three only) has been submitted to the Government. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval. The proposal for the repair of a well of Tap Water Supply Scheme at Bodwad, G. Jalgaon at a cost of Rs. 5,88,843/- (Rupees Five Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Forty Three only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions: 1. The repair of the well mentioned in the proposal should be carried out by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon.
2) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner. Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
3) Tendering of the scheme is being allowed.
