Government Decision: Rs. The revised budget of Rs. 3,18,51, 108/ (Rupees Three Crores Eighteen Lakhs Fifty One Thousand One Hundred and Eight only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
3. It shall be mandatory for the concerned Gram Panchayat to hand over the scheme after the completion of the scheme.
After completion of the scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat and Zilla Parishad will be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of notification from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad Aurangabad that the scheme has been completed in reality. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials and contractors should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
(5) The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned shall not receive any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (subject to special amendments), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state were closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
7) Action should be taken according to the prevailing policy on dowry.
8) It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
