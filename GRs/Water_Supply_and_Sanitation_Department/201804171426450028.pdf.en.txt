Preamble: The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati had submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for the special amendment of the Regional Tap Water Supply Scheme for the period of 2017-18 under the Water Scarcity Programme at a cost of Rs. 77,59,700/- (Rupees Seventy Seven Lakh, Sixty Nine Thousand, Seven Hundred Only) in the Taluka Chikhli, District Buldana. The proposal was considered by the Secretary Level Standing Committee in its meeting held on 11th April, 2018.
On the proposal received from the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati, regarding the special repair measures of the Mouza Shelgaon Atol 5 Village Regional Water Pipeline Scheme, Shri. Out of the work proposed in the Standing Committee meeting held on 11th April, 2018

The Standing Committee approved a total cost of Rs. 30,41,900/-, which includes Desilting of Jack Well and Pump House over Jack Well, Repairs of Water Treatment Plants, Repairs of Raw/ Pure Water Rising Main and Pumping Machinery. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the facts stated in the Preamble, the Secretary Level Standing Committee in its meeting held on 11th April, 2018 has approved the proposal of Rs. 30,41,900/- (Rupees Thirty Lakhs, Forty One Thousand, Nine Hundred only) covering the works proposed under the Special Amendment of Mouza Shelgaon Atol 5 Villages Regional Tap Water Scheme.
Accordingly, this proposal is being given administrative approval under the following conditions: 1) The District Collector, Buldhana shall first declare that there is a shortage of drinking water in the area of the concerned Regional Water Supply Scheme in Chikhli taluka.
2) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner.
Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by April 27, 2018. If the project is not completed by that date, the permit will be cancelled.
The project has to be completed within stipulated time frame.
6) After the works mentioned in the proposal of the scheme are completed by the Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done by the concerned Gram Panchayat.
7) The Zilla Parishad should immediately recover the water from the villages under the regional water supply scheme of Mauje Shelgaon Atol.
8) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. The remaining funds will be released after the completion of the project.
9) Since the work of the scheme must be completed in time, every week from the tender process of the scheme to the start of water supply from the scheme
The report will be submitted to the government by Monday.
