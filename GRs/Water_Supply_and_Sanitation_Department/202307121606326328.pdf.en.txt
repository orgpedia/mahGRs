Government's decision:
For the financial year 2023-24, under the headings 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01), Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Samitis, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881), 36, for the Subsidiary Grants (Salaries), as shown in the statement A accompanying this Government Resolution, an amount of Rs. 24,93,00, 000/-and 31, for the Subsidiary Grants (Non-Pay), Rs. 88,00,000/-total Rs. 25,81,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores One Lakhs)
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. The said funds are being disbursed subject to the deposit of the unspent funds of 2022-23 to the Government under the aforesaid heads and the funds shall not be drawn from the Treasury unless the unspent funds are deposited to the Government. The non-salaried fund should be utilised by the respective Zila Parishads as per requirement for meeting travel expenses, office expenses, leasing, motor vehicles, electricity payments, petrol/ oil/ lubricants etc.
3. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business, Financial Regulations, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing and spending the disbursed funds from the Treasury.
The grants being disbursed under the target of" 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" are unconditional as per the Government decision dated 12.01.2023 of the Department of Finance. However, the subsidy provided by the government will be mandatorily credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.

The terms and conditions mentioned in the circular of the Finance Department are being fulfilled.
