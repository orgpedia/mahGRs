Government's decision:
For the financial year 2019-20, a total grant of Rs. 12,29,45, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Thirty-nine Lakhs Forty-five Thousand only) has been provided to the officers of Class I and Class II on the establishment of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)."
This Government decision is being approved by the respective District Councils as indicated in the accompanying' Statement A'.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiary through ECS. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
