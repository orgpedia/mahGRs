The Proposal:
In order to make all the services of the government available to the general public at an affordable price through information technology, transparent and fast. The e-governance policy of the state has been announced under the Government Decision No. 1. Water Supply and Sanitation Department,

Procurement of computers and other materials for the office of the Under Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, State of Maharashtra and its subordinate offices was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Computer content printers in the office of the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System and its subordinate offices have been asked to be computerized to ensure complete e-governance and strengthening of the Ground Water Survey and Development System. The Project Implementation Committee constituted as per the Government Resolution dated 08.02.2023 for the purchase of computers and other materials for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the Commissioner of Interior, the Ground Water Survey and Development System, the State of Maharashtra and its subordinate offices has approved the purchase of computer materials as per the following details.
