Resolution
Shri. Moiz Hussain Hussain Ali has been appointed as a Technical Advisor to Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) for the period of 5 months from 01.11.2019
upto 31.03.2020 vide Government Resolution at reference no.2.
Shri. Moiz Hussain Hussain Ali is reappointed as a Technical Advisor to Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) for further period of 6 months from 01.04.2020 to 30.09.2020 extendable further after review of usefulness of this arrangement.
Scope of work of Technical Advisor: 1) Interaction with Consultant and system integrators of projects.
2) To assist Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) in reviewing and advising in the progress of various ongoing e-governance projects.
3) To advise Additional Chief Secretary (WSS) from time to time and
would also participate in PIC as special invitee to take the advantage of his experience in these projects and seek future
