Government Circular, Finance Division No: Finance-2018/ PR No. 69/ Finance-3 dated 2nd April, 2018
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the state legislature. The Bill has been converted into an Act and published as Maharashtra Act No. 22 of 2018 in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 27th March, 2018. A circular issued by the Finance Department has informed the Water Supply and Sanitation Department that as per the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2018, 70 per cent of the total budgetary provisions under the scheme and non-plan provisions for the financial year 2018-19 have been made available on the budget system for the first nine months of 2018-19 (end-December 2018).
7610- Loan to Government Employees etc., for meeting the establishment and other expenses of Ground Water Survey and Development System for the financial year 2018-19. 4,44,51, 000/- has been provided. As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, the matter of making available the grants disbursed under the said head to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune is under consideration.

As per the instructions of the Department of Finance, for the year 2018-19, to meet the expenditure of the first nine months (April to December) of the financial year 2018-19 on the budget distribution system, the subsidy available on the budget distribution system under the head" Loans to 7610 Government employees etc." is being sanctioned and released to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune through this Government Decision.
