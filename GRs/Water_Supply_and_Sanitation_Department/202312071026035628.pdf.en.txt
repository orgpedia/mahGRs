Conditions
(1) All remaining works should be completed within the stipulated time frame at the revised administrative sanction cost.
(2) The original administrative approval accorded to the scheme as well as the revised technical approval shall be subject to strict compliance with the terms and conditions specified.
3) Once the project is completed, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad concerned shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad, Chandrapur that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
(4) The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned shall not receive any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (subject to special amendments), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
7) Action should be taken according to the prevailing policy on dowry.
8) It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
9) The terms and conditions mentioned in the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval to the scheme should be strictly adhered to.
