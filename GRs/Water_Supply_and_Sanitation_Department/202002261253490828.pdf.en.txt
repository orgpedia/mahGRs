Preamble As per the Government decision dated 22.03.2012, 25 temporary posts created for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department at No.1 in the said Chapter have been approved to be continued from 1.10.2019 to 20.02.2020 as per the Government decision dated 10.10.2019 in No.3 in the said Chapter.
As per the Government Resolution dated 12th February, 2020 of the Department of Finance, No.4 of the said Chapter, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts, which are included in their rolls and which have been created after the approval of the previous Review but have not been included in the rolls, from 01st March, 2020 to 31st August, 2020. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 25 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). The government is taking the following steps:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.109/ Papu-01
Government Decisions In line with the provision of clean and pure drinking water and sanitation facilities through the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the department needs more manpower to effectively implement the schemes and to meet the increased workload arising out of it.
Therefore, 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) need to be continued.
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 12th February, 2019 of the Finance Department, dated 4th February, 2019, the Government is approving the continuation of 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), as shown in Form A attached with this Government Resolution, from 01st March, 2020 to 31st August, 2020.
Expenditure on the said 25 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2019-20 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative departments as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 12th February, 2020 of the Finance Department.
