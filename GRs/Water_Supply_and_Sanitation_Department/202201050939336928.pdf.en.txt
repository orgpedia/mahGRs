Preamble As per the decision of the Government dated 26th November, 2020, instructions have been issued to implement the Centrally Sponsored Atal Bhujal Yojana in the State. In this decision, No. The Cabinet has approved a single point approach for effective implementation of DLI 3 and 4 in FY' 9, which prescribes provision of year-wise funds to the concerned departments for five years (2020-21 to 2024-25) through separate head of accounts by the State Governments, if the need arises. Accordingly, the matter of approval for opening a new head of account for the said purpose was under consideration.
Government's decision
In case it is not possible to provide funds for the schemes implemented by the concerned department to meet the target to be achieved by the unitary approach of Atal Bhujal Yojana, it is being approved to open the head of account for the financial provision to be made from the state government fund as follows.
