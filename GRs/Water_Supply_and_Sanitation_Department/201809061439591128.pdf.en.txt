Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. Provisional Common Seniority of Chief Dredging Engineer (Group A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been notified vide Circular No. 2 dated 01/01/2015. The General Administration Department, as per the instructions issued under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, has included the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Chief Excavation Engineer (Group-A) for the years 2015, 2016 and 2017 and prepared a provisional common seniority list showing the status of the Chief Excavation Engineer (Group A) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. The submitted seniority has been attached as Annexure-A with this circular.
Also, the names of officers in the selection list for promotion to the post of Chief Engraving Engineer, Group-A, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, are also attached.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in your department, retired from the department and are on deputation from the establishment of the department. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government before October 06, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it is possible to make necessary changes in the seniority notice after verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be considered for determining seniority in the cadre of Chief Excavator only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
