Introduction: To provide private tap connections and individual toilet facilities to families belonging to Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in rural areas of the state, the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Rural Dalit Vasti Water Supply and Sanitation Scheme was implemented. 1 This has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 18th November, 2011. Under this scheme, the government provides grants to the respective district councils for the construction of private tap connections and individual toilets to families belonging to Scheduled Castes and Newly-educated communities in rural areas of the state, as well as for additional works in accordance with the water supply distribution, storage, mechanical system in the area.
