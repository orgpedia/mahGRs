As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and the concerned Gram Panchayat officer should jointly conduct an inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
