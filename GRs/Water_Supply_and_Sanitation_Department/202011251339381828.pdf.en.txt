The Proposal:
With a view to improve the rural water supply sector in the State and to provide clean and adequate drinking water to the rural population, the Government of India has decided to implement the following schemes: The Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme is being implemented in the State as per the Government Decision dated 1st May, 2016.
Under this programme, it has been decided to disburse the maintenance repair fund to be disbursed to the Regional Rural Tap Water Supply Schemes as incentive subsidy. This is in accordance with the Government Decision dated 26.11.2018. For the financial year 2020-21, Rs. 150.00 crore has been provided. 30.00 crore has been provided. It was under consideration of the government to release and distribute the funds to the regional offices through the State Water and Sanitation Mission, Cidco Bhavan, Belapur and Navi Mumbai.
