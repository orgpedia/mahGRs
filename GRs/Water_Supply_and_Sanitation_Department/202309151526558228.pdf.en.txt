Preamble: As per the review of the posts in the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) under the Government Decision dated 4th September, 2002, the 24 temporary posts included in the figure of the Department have been approved to be continued from 1st September, 2020 to 31st August, 2023 under the Government Decision dated 16th September, 2020, No. 4th February, 2021, No. 6th August, 2021, No. 8th March, 2022 and No. 10th August, 2022.
The Government Decision dated 31st August, 2023 and the Corrigendum dated 6th September, 2023 of the Finance Department at the above mentioned Reading No. 13 and 14 have empowered all the Administrative Departments to extend the period from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 24 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Water Supply and Sanitation Department implements schemes in accordance with the provision of safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities.

Government Resolution No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.109/ Papu-01
The department needs more manpower. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 24 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 31st August, 2023 and the Corrigendum dated 6th September, 2023 of the Finance Department, read with the above, the Government is approving the continuation of 24 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024.
The expenditure on this should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2023-2024 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Water Supply and Sanitation Department (3451 0467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 31st August, 2023 and the Sanction Letter dated 06th September, 2023 of the Finance Department.
