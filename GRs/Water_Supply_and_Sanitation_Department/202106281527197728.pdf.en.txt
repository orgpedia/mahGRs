Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. Provisional Common Seniority of the Chief Excavation Engineer (Group-A) cadre of Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been published vide Circular No. 2 dated 01/01/2015. Also, the provisional common seniority for the period from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 has been notified vide Government Circular No. 3. The General Administration Department has prepared a provisional common seniority list showing the status of the Chief Excavation Engineer (Group-A) category in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2019, 01/01/2020 and 01/01/2021 by including the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Chief Excavation Engineer (Group-A) for the years 2018, 2019 and 2020 as per the instructions given under the Government Decision at Reference No.1. The submitted seniority has been attached as Annexure-A with this circular.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in your department, retired from the department and are on deputation from the establishment of the department. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the statement of the officers should be submitted to the government before July 28, 2021, along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service book and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority notice after verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be considered for determining seniority in the cadre of Chief Excavator only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV 1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
