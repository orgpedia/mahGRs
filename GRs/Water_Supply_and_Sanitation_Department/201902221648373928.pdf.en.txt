Government Decision: Mauje Atpadi and other 53 villages (Dist. Revised administrative approval is being accorded to the revised cost estimate of Rs. 10384.78 lakh (Dhobal) for the tap water supply scheme at Atpadi, G. Sangli subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Life Authority shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Sangli that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and the concerned Gram Panchayat officer should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Life Authority will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenses incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation in the scheme or for the renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Life Authority) to hand over the scheme.
The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to the CAG report of 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state are in the process of non-payment of power dues (Poor maintenance/ nonpayment) due to lack of maintenance.
