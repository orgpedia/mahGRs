Introduction The Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad, Parbhani has submitted a proposal for the approval of the government under reference number 09 as the per capita cost of Mauje Jalalpur (T.G. Parbhani) tap water supply scheme is higher than the prescribed norm. The proposal for the said tap water supply scheme has been accorded technical approval by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani vide order dated 26.11.2021.
The approval has been given in the meeting of the Technical Committee dated 09.10.2021 under the chairmanship of Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Board, Nanded. Hence, Rs. 86.35 lakh was under the consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval under Jal Jeevan Mission Programme.
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble to the Government Resolution, the per capita consumption of 55 litres of water at Mauje Jalalpur (T.G. Parbhani) has been increased from Rs. 7184/- per annum. 86.35 lakhs (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand only)
