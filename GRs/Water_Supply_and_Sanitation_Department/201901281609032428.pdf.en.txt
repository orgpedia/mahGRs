Preamble: Under National Rural Drinking Water Programme, Chincholi Malhi and 15 villages of Taluka. Proposal for administrative approval of the budget and plan for the enhanced per capita cost of the Regional Water Supply Scheme, Kej G. Beed vide Letter No. 1 has been submitted to the government for approval. The scheme was launched by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Aurangabad. The Technical Approval has been granted on 27.12.2018.
Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the plan and budget of the present scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Chincholi Malhi and 15 villages of the region. The Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget and Plan at a nominal cost of Rs. 31,84,08, 100/- (Rupees Thirty One Crore Four Lakh Eight Thousand Hundred Only) subject to the following conditions.
The scheme will be implemented by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Beed. The contractor will be responsible for the maintenance and repair of the plan for at least 1 year after the implementation of the plan.
2) The work of the scheme should be done by inviting e-tendering in the specified manner.

9) If there is an increase in the cost of the scheme for any reason, the government will not provide subsidy.
10) Action should be taken regarding the release of open defecation as per the existing provisions of the government.
11) The conditions for the technical approval of the scheme should be fulfilled. Also, it will be mandatory to conduct a thorough technical examination of the scheme as per the prevailing policy of the government.
12) After the implementation of the scheme is completed, the concerned implementing agency will be required to submit a certificate of completion of the scheme to the competent authority.
13) If any irregularity, misappropriation and malpractices of any nature are found in the implementation of the scheme, the concerned officers/ employees responsible for the irregularity will be liable for disciplinary action.
The Water and Sanitation Support Organization (WASO) will be responsible for obtaining utilization certificates and proof of completion of the scheme from the concerned implementing agencies and submitting them to the government as the scheme progresses.
15) Ensure that the scheme is being implemented in accordance with the existing policy.
16) If the Gram Panchayat has electricity dues, work orders should not be issued until it is paid in full.
17) It will be the responsibility of the concerned Executive Engineer to check whether the accounts of the previous scheme have been completed in financial terms or how. Account needs to be completed.
It should be ensured at the field level that the scheme is included in the Annual Action Plan for 2018-19.
19) The Chief Engineer shall obtain and confirm the report on the source/ water sustainability of the scheme before giving the work order.
