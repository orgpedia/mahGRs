The amount of grant-in-aid sanctioned and released should be spent for the specified purpose only till 31st March, 2020.
The Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency should inform the government about the allocation from this free subsidy.
The powers to examine the accounts of expenditure made by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for these schemes from this grant are being given to the Accountant General, Maharashtra, Mumbai/ Nagpur or the officer of this department who has been given special powers. If the concerned documents, accountant general or the officer of the special authorized department demand, the water and sanitation assistance agency should provide them immediately.
As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure-II to the Bombay Finance Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority shall be submitted to the Accountant General by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for inspection along with relevant documents as per the demand of the Accountant General.
The Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency shall issue the utilization certificates of the grants received by it from the Government or the grants which it will receive in the future. The Director, Water and Sanitation Support Agency shall certify and sign the programme-wise amounts of grant and expenditure sanctioned and released as indicated in the Utilization Certificate. The certificate of utility shall be signed by the Joint Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
The utilization certificate of this subsidy should be submitted to the government in the prescribed format and copies of the utilization certificate should also be sent to the Accountant General.
The expenditure of this grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 001 on the prescribed date.
Under this Government decision, the funds released to the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai are being disbursed to the Director, Water and Sanitation Support Society, Belapur, Navi Mumbai on BEAMS system.
