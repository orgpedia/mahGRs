Preamble- In the financial year 2023-2024, to meet the cost of installation of Ground Water Survey and Development System, under the head" Capital Expenditure on Demand No.Y-06, 4402 Soil and Water Conservation, (00) (101) Soil Survey and Testing, (01) (01) Minor Irrigation Schemes under One Ground Water Survey and Development Agency (4402 1148) (Mandatory)," a total amount of Rs. 32,28,14, 000/- has been provided. Out of which the grants received from the Finance Department have been distributed to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune.
Under this head, the matter of providing grants to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune, to meet the expenditure for the next period under the targets under which the grants have now been received from the Finance Department, was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS During the financial year 2023-2024, a total grant of Rs. 3,27,17, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Seventeen Thousand only) is being released to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune as shown in column 5 of the following statement to meet the expenditure on establishment of Ground Water Survey and Development System and other ancillary matters.
