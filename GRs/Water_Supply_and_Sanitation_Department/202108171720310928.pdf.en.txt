The Ground Water Survey and Development (GWS& D) System has been functioning since 16th July, 1971 as a separate Directorate. Since then, the administrative head of the system has been the Director, an officer of the Indian Administrative Service cadre. The government was considering changing the designation of the head of the department from" director" to" commissioner."
Government's decision
The designation of the" Director," who heads the Directorate of Ground Water Survey and Development System under the control of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, is being changed as follows.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. |                                                      |                                                          | Current designation                          |   |   | Revised designation |   |   | shera |
| 1    | Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency | Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency | Cadre Posts in Indian Administrative Service |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(3) The designation shall be deemed to have been amended at the place where the present designation is shown as above. The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Authority should take necessary action in this regard.
The said Government Decision is based on the General Administration Department's Informal Reference No. 114/ Ka-10, Dy. Issued pursuant to the concurrence received on 30.06.2021 and vide Finance Department's Non-official Reference No. 212/2021/ Service-9, dated 6.08.2021.
