GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the proposal of Rs. 97,67,630/- (Rupees Ninety Seven Lakhs Ninety Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Thirty only), which includes the works proposed under the special amendment of the scheme of Mauja Kothli 7 Villages Regional Tap Water Supply Scheme, Taluka Motala, District Buldana, is being given administrative approval under scarcity subject to the following conditions: 1. The Collector, Buldhana shall first declare that there is a shortage of drinking water in the area of the regional water supply scheme of Mauja Kothli 7 Villages Regional Tap Water Supply Scheme, Taluka Motala, District Buldana.
2) All special repair works mentioned in the proposal should be carried out by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Buldana.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner. Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by April 27, 2018. If the project is not completed by that date, the permit will be cancelled.
The project should be completed within the stipulated time and water supply should be resumed.
7) After the work mentioned in the proposal of this scheme is completed through the Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done through the concerned Gram Panchayat.
8) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. The remaining funds will be released after the completion of the project.
