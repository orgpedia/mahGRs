Preamble The Zonal Examination for Gazetted Officers of Group-A and Group-B Cadre (Technical/ Intellectual) in Ground Water Survey and Development System is conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission as per the provisions of the Zonal Examination Rules, 1992. As per the provisions of the Departmental Examination Rules, 1992, it is necessary to pass the departmental examination within a probation period of 2 years. However, in the year 2008, 2013 and 2017, after the departmental examination was conducted, a total of 20 officers of the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency could not pass the departmental examination within the prescribed probation period. In view of the fact that these 20 unsuccessful candidates did not get enough opportunities in the prescribed examination period, and the duration of the opportunity of this departmental examination and the candidate is a Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) awardee and considering the long period of government service, the government was considering to give a special opportunity to the 20 officers (technical/ non-technical) who failed the departmental examination on two occasions in the Group-A and Group-B cadre of the Ground Water Survey and Development System.
Departmental Examination for Gazetted Officers of Group-A and Group-B Cadre (Technical/ Intellectual) in Ground Water Survey and Development System In accordance with the provisions of the Departmental Examination Rules, 1992, those who could not pass the departmental examination within the probation period of 2 years are eligible to apply.
