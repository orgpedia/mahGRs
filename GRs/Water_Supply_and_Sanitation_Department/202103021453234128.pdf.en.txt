Government Resolution No: Budget-1420/ P.No.92/ Papu-23
The total amount of Rs. 33.2390 crore for the financial year 2020-21 under the head" Departmental/ Sub Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" has been disbursed as per the Government Decision No.3, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10 and 11. Also, a total amount of Rs. 12.3380 lakh has been disbursed to the concerned District Councils under the Government Decision No. 12 of 2020 for domestic travel expenses.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)," the Government has decided to pay the salary and domestic travel expenses of Class 1 and Class 2 officers on the establishment of District Council Rural Water Supply Department.
A total amount of Rs. 7,20,90, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Twenty Lakhs Ninety Thousand only) as shown in the' Statement A' is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure. In this regard, the expenditure should be borne by the concerned office after taking into account the instructions issued by the Finance Department from time to time under the Government Decision dated May 4, 2020 and thereafter.

3.The fund should be utilised for payment of salary and travel expenses of the officials on the establishment of the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
4. Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said decision is submitted to the government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
Expenditure from the grant sanctioned through this Government Resolution should be spent under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (1) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) (22151843) for implementation of Water Supply Programme and it should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21.
This Government Decision is being issued taking into consideration the powers vested in the Departments under the Government Circular No.Finance-2020/ No. 64/ Earth-3 of the Finance Department dated 16th April, 2020 as well as the funds made available by the Finance Department on the BIMS system.
The terms and conditions mentioned in the circular of the Finance Department are being fulfilled.
