The Proposal:
The Government has taken the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the Government Decision dated 23.3.2017 and since the Government is likely to take a long time to pay the pension expenses of the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority through the Treasury from the regular Principal Accounts Title 2071, the new Account Title created temporarily for the purpose of providing subsidy to the Authority for the financial year 2018-19 on the retirement salary of the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority has been extended for the year 2019-20 under the Government Decision dated 10.1.2019 with the consent of the Principal Accountant General's Office and the Finance Department.

An amount of Rs. 84,47,67, 000/- has been sanctioned as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the financial year 2019-20 under the head 22151052 towards payment of retirement benefits to retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. Accordingly, in the year 2019-20, the matter of disbursement of pension grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the above head of accounts for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2019-20, a grant of Rs. 75,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Seventy Five Crores only) under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to Other Non-Government Organizations (01) Assistance to Other Non-Government Organizations (01) (01) Assistance to Government Employees Transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (3115 A052) 31, Assistance Grants (Non-Salary)" is being released to the Authority subject to the following conditions.
(a) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall ensure that the amount of the grant is spent for the same purposes as the grant is sanctioned for the pension benefits of retired officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
(b) This fund should not be used for any outstanding dues as on 23.3.2017. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
e) Separate accounts of the amount of free subsidy should be maintained.
(e) The Authority shall inform the Government from time to time about the allocations made under the free grants.
(a) The accounts of the Authority as per Rule No. 3 of Annexure 2 to the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959 shall be submitted by the Authority for inspection along with relevant documents.

f) The Authority shall submit the utilization certificate of the free subsidy to the Government. The Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall certify the amount of grant-in-aid and expenditure as approved and released as indicated in the Utilization Certificate. The utility certificates should be signed by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Only such utility certificates will be acceptable to the government.
The expenditure of the released subsidy should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's Office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department on the prescribed date.
Patil has been posted as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. R. M. Gengaje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been posted as the Controlling Officer.
The pension subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should pension grants be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
The expenditure in the said case should be included in the financial statement for the year 2019-20 under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to other Non-Governmental Organizations, (01) Assistance to Government Employees transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Grants (Compulsory) (2215 A052) 31, Assistance Grants (Non-Payment)."
These Government decisions are being issued within the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Circular, Finance Division No.Finance-2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3, dated 1st April, 2019. The terms and conditions mentioned in this circular are being fulfilled.
