The Proposal:
46 daily wage workers working on rural water supply schemes in Nanded Zilla Parishad have been given benefits as per the Kalelkar order. The Bombay High Court, Aurangabad Bench, in Writ Petition No. 2617/2008. As the impugned order dated 8.05.2018 was not implemented by the Court, the contempt petition No. 750/2018 was filed. In respect of salary and arrears of salary of Group-D employees in the said court case, pursuant to the decision of the Government, Rs. 2,04,63, 379/- has been disbursed to Nanded Zilla Parishad as a special case. Now, the government was considering to disburse funds to the Nanded Zilla Parishad for the salary and allowances of these employees for the financial year 2020-21. The following decision has been taken:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: ZIPGRA-1218/ PR No.335/ PAPU-23
Government's decision:
The Bombay High Court, Aurangabad Bench, in Writ Petition No. 2617/2008. As per the order dated 8.05.2018 passed by the Court, out of the total 46 employees of Nanded Zilla Parishad on rural water supply schemes in the said court case, 26 employees mentioned in the accompanying statement were paid Rs. 1,05,92, 450/- (Rupees One Crore Five Lakhs Ninety Two Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty only) is being distributed to Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nanded.
2. The Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nanded is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for drawing this fund from the treasury.
The fund has been sanctioned for the salaries and allowances of 26 employees mentioned in the accompanying statement for the financial year 2020-21 and the use of this fund for other purposes will be considered as financial irregularity for which the concerned responsible officers will be liable for disciplinary action.
4. Utilization certificate of the said amount should be submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nanded.
The expenditure sanctioned through this Government Resolution shall be spent under the head" Demand No.Y-2, 2215- Water Supply and Sanitation, 196- Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions, (01)- Gramin Tap Water Supply Scheme, (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees of Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151914), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" and shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21.
The Government Decision is issued within the scope of the powers conferred on the Departments under the Government Circular No.Finance 2020/ PR No. 64/ Finance-3 of the Finance Department dated 16th April, 2020 and within the limits of funds made available by the Finance Department on the BIMS system.
The terms and conditions mentioned in this circular of the Finance Department are being fulfilled.
