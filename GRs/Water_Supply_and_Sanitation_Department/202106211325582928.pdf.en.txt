The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparing and publishing seniority list of government officials/ employees every year. The Common Final Seniority of Senior Digging Engineer (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been notified vide Government Circular No.2 and Provisional Common Seniority from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 vide Government Circular No.3. As per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the names of the officers included in the selection list of 2015, 2016 and 2017 in the Senior Excavation Engineer (Group-A) cadre have been included in the provisional final common seniority list showing the status of Senior Excavation Engineer (Group A) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this letter.
