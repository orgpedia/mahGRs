Preamble: Government Reference No. (1) Under the decision of the Government here, guidelines have been issued for the implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission in the state and 39 posts have been approved for the implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission. The posts of" Project Director- Jal Jeevan Mission" and" Project Director Swachh Bharat Mission Gramin" have been indicated in Annexure-I at No. 2 and No. 3 respectively.
Now the reference No. (2) According to this letter, The Mission Director, Jal Jeevan Mission, State Water and Sanitation Mission, Belapur, Navi Mumbai has informed the Government that the name of the sanctioned post at the district level as well as at the State level to look after the work of Jal Jeevan Mission is Project Director, which is creating ambiguity in the correspondence. The post of Project Director in State Water and Sanitation Mission (SWSM) offices in other States has the designation of Additional Mission Director. Hence, the names of the posts of Project Director- Jal Jeevan Mission and Project Director- Swachh Bharat Mission- Gramin at the State level are respectively' Additional Mission."

Director- Jal Jeevan Mission and Additional Mission Director- Swachh Bharat Mission- Gramin. Mission Director, Jal Jeevan Mission, State Water and Sanitation Mission, Belapur, Navi Mumbai has requested the government. In view of the above request, the matter of change in the designation of the posts mentioned in Annexure-I at No. 2 and 3 in the Government Resolution dated 04.09.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this Government Decision dated 04.09.2020, the designations of the State level posts of Project Director- Jal Jeevan Mission and Project Director- Swachh Bharat Mission- Gramin mentioned in Annexure-I at Annexure-II and Annexure-III are being changed to" Additional Mission Director- Jal Jeevan Mission" and" Additional Mission Director- Swachh Bharat Mission Gramin" respectively.
This change is only a change in designation and there is no change in the pay scale, tenure and responsibilities of these posts.
