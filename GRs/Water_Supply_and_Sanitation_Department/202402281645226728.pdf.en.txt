For immediate and efficient implementation of the transfer order, the following instructions shall be strictly followed: -
The above mentioned Senior Geologist is being unilaterally relieved from the office mentioned in Rakkana No. 3 with effect from the date 29/02/2024 (M.No.). The order is a cease and desist order and does not require a fresh order from the office concerned.
2. The transferred officers shall immediately report to the Government through the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Mechanism at the office mentioned in ROCANA No. 4 without delay. The transferred officers joined the place of transfer.

If found not, disciplinary action will be taken against them under the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. The transferred officers should be engaged without delay. The head of the office and the establishment officer concerned shall be held liable in the event of non-payment of salary and allowances without a mandatory waiting period due to non-acquisition of an officer.
The officers being posted under such order are required to join immediately at the place of their posting. In case of non-appearance at the place of posting or any officer directly or indirectly attempting to pressurise for modification of transfer order, the same shall be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officer. Such officers will also be liable for disciplinary action as the act violates Rule 3 (3) and Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
Since these transfers need to be implemented effectively, the salary of all transferred officers up to the date 29/02/2024 should be drawn by the office where they are currently working. Under no circumstances should the salary from the date 01/03/2024 be withdrawn from the pre-transfer office. The pay should be drawn by the post transfer office after the officer concerned appears at the transfer office and the report should be submitted to the government. Salary from date 01/03/2024 in pre-transfer office
If paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer, they will be held personally liable,
SAT instructions are being issued to all Head of Departments and Head of Offices in the Ground Water Survey and Development System.
Since the above changes are being made at the request of the concerned, they will not be allowed any travel allowance or incidental allowances along with the period of assumption of office to join their place of transfer.
These orders will come into force with immediate effect. The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Mechanism is being informed to immediately submit to the Government the information as to whether or not the transferred concerned officers have joined the transferred office.
