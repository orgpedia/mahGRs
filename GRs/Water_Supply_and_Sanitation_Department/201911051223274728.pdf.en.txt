Government's stand:
The condition number 06 in the above reference government decision was stated as follows.
" The funds are being provided for the mitigation measures taken during the period of scarcity (October, 2019 to September, 2019) during 2018-19. From this fund, the payments made before March, 2019 should be paid in full. The rest of the funds (periodically to be spent on further activities). Allocate funds accordingly. "
Instead, read condition number 6 as follows:
The funds are being provided for the mitigation measures taken during the period of shortage (October 2018 to September 2019) during 2018-19. From this fund, the payments made before March, 2019 should be paid in full. The rest of the funds (periodically to be spent on further activities). The available funds should be allocated accordingly.
