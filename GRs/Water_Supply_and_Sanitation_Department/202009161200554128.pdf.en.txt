Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 10 temporary posts in the Improvement Assistance and Project Management Cell of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalaswarajya Project. The government is taking the following steps:
The government has decided to provide clean and adequate drinking water to the people of the state. Jalswarajya Phase-II is being implemented with World Bank funding through the Improvement Support and Management Cell under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. For this work to be carried out properly, 10 temporary posts are required for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarojya Project.
02 Now, the World Bank and the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, vide letter dated 9th March, 2020, have extended the programme under Jal Swarajya Phase-II till 30th September, 2020. However, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 12.02.2020 and 31.08.2020 respectively of the Finance Department at No.2 and No.5 of the above mentioned readings, 10 temporary posts for Reform Support and Project Management Cell of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) and 2 temporary posts for Reform Support and Project Management Cell of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) created under the World Bank funded Jalswarajya Phase-II Project have not been created under the Jalswarajya Phase-II Programme only for the duration of the Programme and are temporary posts for the establishment of the Department.
