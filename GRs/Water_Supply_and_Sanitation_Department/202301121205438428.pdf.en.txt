The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Life Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Life Authority with the Government.
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Budget-1422/ PR No.81/ PAPU-23
The decision has been made. A total amount of Rs. 131.9803 crore has been released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2022-23 under the head 2215A041 as grant in aid towards payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 29,54,39, 000/- (Rupees Twenty-nine Crores Forty-four Lakhs Forty-nine Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority as grant under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in respect of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" under this Government Order subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be used for the approved and admissible salary/ allowances for the year 2022-23 of the employees on regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
b) Technical charges and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of December, 2022 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
(a) The Department of Finance. As per the directions given by the Government Circular dated 22.11.2021, the Authority should take an undertaking from the concerned employee regarding the overpayment amount.
Rajendra Madke, Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, as Drawing and Disbursing Officer to receive the amount disbursed under this Government Order.
