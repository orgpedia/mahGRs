Preamble: The Government Circular dated 14th February, 2018 specifies that temporary water supply schemes and special repairs of tap water schemes taken under drinking water scarcity should not be approved at the field level after 31st March, 2018. However, due to this year's General Election Code of Conduct, these measures could not be approved before March 31, 2019, so the government was considering extending the deadline to March 31, 2019. This proposal. The approval was given at the meeting of the Cabinet Sub-Committee held on 02.05.2019 under the Chairmanship of the Minister for Relief and Rehabilitation. Accordingly, a government order was issued.

Government Circular: Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the following decisions are being taken regarding the measures to be taken for water supply under scarcity.
As per the instructions in the Government Circular, dated 14.02.2018, all the measures to be taken under the shortage pending before 31st March, may be approved by the level of Divisional Commissioner and Collector by 15th May, 2019. Also, approval is being given to complete the measures that were approved in the pre-Code period but are pending.
