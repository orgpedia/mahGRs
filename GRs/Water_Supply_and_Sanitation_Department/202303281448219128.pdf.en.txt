Material progress for the purpose for which the funds have been disbursed should be submitted along with the monthly expenditure report every month. The accounts of the allocation of funds should be kept up to date at the level of the office that collects and disburses the grants in accordance with the procedures set out in the records. Similarly, the expenditure incurred every three months should be verified with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed certificate should be submitted to this government immediately. Also, the controlling officer should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds under any circumstances.
The Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure incurred from this grant. In this regard, vide No. The Accountant General, Maharashtra- 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and the authorized officers of this department will have the right to see the books and accounts of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for audit so that the government can ensure that the guidelines set by the government under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme are being fulfilled by the Zilla Parishad. Also, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran will provide full support and facilities to the Accountant General to carry out his work properly.
The expenditure of the grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the reconciliation report should be submitted to the Office of the Commissioner, PUP-19/ PUP-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai on the prescribed dates. Similarly, the utility certificate should be submitted to the Accountant General's Office, Mumbai/ Nagpur and Papu-19 Office.
