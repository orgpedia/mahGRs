Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparing and publishing seniority list of government officials/ employees every year. Accordingly, the provisional seniority of Assistant Geologist (Group-B) cadre of Ground Water Survey and Development System w.e.f. 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 is being considered as No. 2 has been issued by the Government here. As per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Assistant Geologist (Group-B) for the years 2015, 2016 and 2017 have been included in the provisional common seniority list showing the status of Assistant Geologist (Group-B) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018. This seniority is being attached as Annexure-A to this circular.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to the post of Assistant Geologist, Group-B, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, are also included in Annexure-B. After their regular promotion orders are issued, they will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the department's establishment. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government before 20th September, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers so that it is possible to make necessary changes in the seniority notice after verifying the same.
Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list will be considered for determining seniority in the Assistant Geologist cadre only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
