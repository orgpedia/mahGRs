The Proposal:
Shri H.A. Joshi has been appointed as Assistant Geologist in Ground Water Survey and Development System for a period of two years as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2. The appointment is the first in the government service to the post of" Assistant Geologist" as recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The revised guidelines for termination of probationary period of officers/ employees in Government service have been passed under the Government decision at Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department and since Shri H.A.
