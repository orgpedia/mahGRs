Preamble: As per the review of the posts in the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) under the Government Decision dated 4th September, 2002 in the said Read No.1 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, 24 temporary posts included in the figure of the Department have been approved to be continued from 1st September, 2020 to 31st August, 2021 under the Government Decision dated 16th September, 2020 in the said Read No.2 and Read No.4 of the Department dated 23rd February, 2021.
As per the Government Resolution dated 10th August, 2021 of the Finance Department, all the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls from 1st September, 2021 to 28th February, 2022. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 24 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Aastha 2016/ PR No.110/ Papu-01
Government Decision: As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes related to providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 10th August, 2021 of the Finance Department read above, the Government is approving the continuation of 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) from 1st September, 2021 to 28th February, 2022 as shown in Form A attached with this Government Decision.
The expenditure on this should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2021-2022 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Account Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (34510467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 10th August, 2021 of the Department of Finance.
