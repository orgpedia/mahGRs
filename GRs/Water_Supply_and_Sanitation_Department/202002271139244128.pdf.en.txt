In order to provide an opportunity to the Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer working under the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad to be promoted to the post of Deputy Engineer (Architecture) in the Maharashtra Life Authority, the contours of the Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad have been fixed as per the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 and the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1976 by the Government Decision dated 4th March, 2011. According to this figure, 206 posts of deputy executive cadre have been created in the district council.
As per the order dated 31.07.2012, 136 posts have been earmarked for the promotion of Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer of Zila Parishad to the post of Deputy Engineer (Construction).
Accordingly, the posts of Assistant Executive Engineer/ Assistant Engineer Grade-I in the Zila Parishad will be filled by direct service, the posts of Deputy Engineer (Graduate) by promotion and the posts of Sub Divisional Officer/ Deputy Engineer (Diploma holder) by promotion in the ratio of 34: 33: 33 respectively.
02. Taking into consideration the facts stated in the above paragraph, the following Branch Engineers (Group-B, Gazetted) in Zila Parishad have been selected from the selection list for the year 2018-19, with effect from the date of this order, as" Deputy Engineer (Architecture)" Group-A, Maharashtra Life Authority, in the pay scale of Rs. 15600-39100+ Grade Pay Rs. 5400/- (as per 6th Pay Commission). The promotions are being made on ad-hoc basis subject to the final outcome in the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court and they are being allotted Revenue Divisions as indicated in column No.6. Also their postings are being done in the office shown in column number 7 in front of their names:

The promotion of the above officers to the post of Deputy Engineer (Architecture), Group-A is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry is pending or proposed against them.
04. The High Court, Bombay in its judgment dated 04/08/2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 has declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed in the Supreme Court. This petition is still pending. In this regard, the Department of Law and Justice has given the opinion that there is no legal problem in giving promotion to open category employees. Accordingly, at present, the vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota are only temporary. It has to be done as per the Supreme Court's verdict. The General Administration Department vide letter dated 29/12/2017 has directed that every administrative department shall ensure that before giving promotion to the Backward Class officers/ employees in the Service Excellence List, they do not come up in the Service Excellence List by availing the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 25/05/2004.
Accordingly, he has been promoted to the post of Deputy Engineer (Architecture) in Maharashtra Life Authority. Subject to the final outcome of Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court, the vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota are being made purely on temporary basis.
The above promoted Deputy Engineer (Architecture) should submit the option of fixation of his salary in the post of promotion to the concerned Head of Office within one month from the date of promotion. In case such an option is not given, their pay will be determined by considering the date of promotion as per the provisions of Rule No. 11 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The promoted officers should be relieved from work by the concerned office immediately and report to this department. Also, the promoted officers should join the office immediately. Any request for change in the post will not be entertained.
