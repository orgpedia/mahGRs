The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 22.03.2012, 25 temporary posts created for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department in the said Ward No. 1 have been approved to be continued from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018.
As per the Government Resolution dated 05.03.2018 of the Finance Department, all the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts, which are included in their rolls and have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls, from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 25 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). The government is taking the following steps:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.109/ Papu-01
Government's decision:
In order to provide clean and pure drinking water and sanitation facilities through the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the Department requires more manpower to efficiently carry out the increased workload arising out of the implementation of the schemes. Therefore, 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) need to be continued.
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 05.03.2018 of the Finance Department, dated 03.03.2018, the Government have approved the continuation of 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) as shown in Form A attached to this Government Resolution from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018.
Expenditure on the said 25 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2018-19 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090 Secretariat (1) Water Supply and Sanitation Department (34510467).
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative departments as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 05.03.2018 of the Finance Department.
