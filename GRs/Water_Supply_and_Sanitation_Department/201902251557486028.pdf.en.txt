Introduction: Swajal, a community based single village solar powered small tap water supply scheme, is being implemented by the Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Government of India in Aspirational Districts of Maharashtra and all other States. As per the guidelines of the Swajal Yojana, the matter of nomination of state level and district level implementing agencies for the Swajal Yojana was under consideration.
The government of India has selected the aspirational districts of Osmanabad, Gadchiroli, Nandurbar and Washim for the Swajal Yojana.
2. As per the guidelines of Swajal Yojana, the scheme is being implemented by the Water Supply and Sanitation Department and since Swajal Yojana is based on solar energy, the implementation of Small Tap Water Supply Scheme on solar energy is through the system of Ground Water Survey and Development.
