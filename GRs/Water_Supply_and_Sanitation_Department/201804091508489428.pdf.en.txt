The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati has submitted a proposal worth Rs. 47,27,800/- to the Government for the temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme at Mauja Aarambhi, Taluka Digras, District Yavatmal under the scarcity measures for the period 2017-18. This proposal was considered for approval in the meeting held on 03.04.2018 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department for granting administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures in urban and rural areas beyond the prescribed financial and physical parameters for mitigation of drinking water scarcity.
This proposal was discussed in the meeting of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department on 03.04.2018.

The proposal was approved. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Considering the facts mentioned in the preamble of the Government decision, the proposal for the Purak Tap Water Supply Scheme in Mauza Aarambhi, Taluka Digras, District Yavatmal at a cost of Rs. 47,27,800/- (Rupees Forty Seven Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Eight Hundred Only) is being given administrative approval under scarcity subject to the following conditions: 1. The Collector, Yavatmal shall first declare that there is a shortage of drinking water in the villages of Mauza Aarambhi, Taluka Digras.
2) The temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme mentioned in the proposal should be implemented by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner.
Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by April 18, 2018.
If the tender process is not completed by April 18, 2018, the approval of the scheme will be considered canceled.
The water supply from the scheme should be started by completing the works of the scheme by May 15, 2018. However, if the water supply from the scheme does not start by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
8) After the works mentioned in the proposal of the scheme are completed through the Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done through the concerned Gram Panchayat.
9) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. The remaining funds will be released after the completion of the project.
10) If the pumps and pipes used for this solution will not be used after the shortage period, then the executive engineer, rural water supply department, zilla parishad, Yavatmal will remove the pumps and pipes.
