The Cabinet decided that if the water reserves under the Water Resources Department are to be used as sources for rural water supply schemes, then water reservation approval from the department will not be required. was held on 16.5.2017.
In accordance with this decision, the Department The Government decision has been issued on 16.9.2017. Sadar The provisions of the Government Order dated 16.9.2017 are applicable only to the schemes of the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme which are implemented by the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
Therefore, prior approval of water reservation should not be required for projects taken up by this department under Jal Swarojya-2, National Rural Drinking Water Programme through Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran/ Zilla Parishads and also for tap water supply schemes implemented by Zilla Parishads under Chief Minister Rural Drinking Water Programme. The meeting was held on 19.07.2018. The matter of issuing necessary instructions in accordance with the above decision of the Cabinet was under consideration of the Government.
Considering the facts mentioned in the preamble of the government decision, it has been decided that if the water reserves under the Water Resources Department are to be used as a source for the National Rural Drinking Water Programme, Jal Swaraj and Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme implemented under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, then prior approval of the Water Reservation Department will not be required.
This Government decision is in pursuance of the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting dated 19.07.2018 and also in accordance with the recommendations of the Department of Finance. Reference No. It is being issued with the approval received under 195/2018.
