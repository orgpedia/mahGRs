2) Government Circular, Finance Department, No: Finance-2023/ No.40/ Finance-3, No. Government Resolution, Water Supply and Sanitation Department No. 1423/ No. 97/ Papu 23, 2023 Preamble of 14.06.2023:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. A total grant of Rs. 45.6357 crore has been released to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2023-24 under the head 22150041 as grant in aid for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.

An amount of Rs. 50,79,72, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Crores Eighty Nine Lakhs Seventy Two Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head' Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salaries and Allowances (Compulsory/ Datta) (22158041), 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)' under this Government Order subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for the payment of approved and admissible salary/ allowances to the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2023-24. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be considered as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
(b) Technical charges and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of August 2023 and September 2023 shall be deposited with the Government immediately.
Rajendra Madke has been appointed as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. Ram Gangje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been appointed as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
4) Before drawing the funds from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
5. Since the grant-in-aid is for salary, it is" unconditional" in nature as per the Government Decision dated 12.01.2023 of the Department of Finance.
