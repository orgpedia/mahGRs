The Proposal:
The Government has taken the responsibility of the salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority under the Government Decision No. 1 and the monthly income received from the ETP and Technical Service Fee is deposited with the Government as per the provisions of the Government Decision. As per the provisions of the same government decision, it has been decided to create a separate head of account at the department level to meet the expenses on regular salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority and make provision for funds as required.
A new accounting standard has been prepared in consultation with the Principal Accountant General's Office and the Finance Department under the Government Resolution No. 2 in the context of making financial provision for providing subsidy to the Authority for giving the benefit of salary and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority.

A proposal for Supplementary Demand was submitted to the Finance Department in the Winter Session of 2019 of the Legislature for providing funds to the Authority for payment of benefits of salary and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017. Out of the requested funds, Rs. The Finance Department has agreed to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 200.00 crore to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran by sanctioning Supplementary Demands. An amount of Rs. 65.00 crore has been made available for payment of benefits of salary and allowances. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 65.00 crore was under consideration of the Government for disbursement of grants-in-aid to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017.
Government's decision:
For the benefit of salary and allowances for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017 of the working officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, the Authority has to pay Rs.
65,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Crores only) is sanctioned and disbursed under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 22215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 01- Water Supply, 001- Operation and Administration, 01- Establishment, (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (22158041) (Compulsory/ Dattamat), 36-Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) (Dattamat)" subject to the following conditions:
Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran should ensure that the amount of grant is spent only for the benefit of salary and allowances of the working officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017.
(2) The expenditure of this fund on other matters shall be deemed to be financial irregularities and the officer/ employee concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action for such action.
3) Separate accounts will be maintained for the amount of subsidy disbursed.
The amount of grant-in-aid should be spent for the specified purpose for which it has been sanctioned and released, by 31st March, 2020.

5) The Maharashtra Life Authority should inform the government from time to time about the allocation from the distributed subsidy.
As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure 2 to the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority shall be submitted to the Maharashtra Life Authority for inspection along with relevant documents.
7) The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority has to give the utilization certificates of the grants received from the government or the grants that will be received in the next period. The Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall certify and sign the amount of grant sanctioned and disbursed as well as the amount of expenditure shown in the Utilization Certificate. The utility certificates should be signed by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Only such utility certificates will be acceptable to the government.
8) The Government should submit the utilization certificate of this subsidy.
9) The cost of the subsidy disbursed should be reconciled with the cost reported to the Accountant General's Office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accountant General, Papu-20 on the prescribed date.
The Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being declared as Controlling Officer for receiving the grants disbursed under this Government decision.
It will be mandatory for the government to transfer the salary and allowances to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the amount of grant of salary and allowances be disbursed in lump sum or offline mode.
The expenditure in this case shall be met from the financial provisions of the year 2019-20 by meeting the expenditure under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 22215-Water Supply and Sanitation, 01-Water Supply, 001-Operation and Administration, 01-Establishment, (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (2215A041) (Mandatory/ Dattamat), 36-Supportive Grants (Salary) (Dattamat)."
