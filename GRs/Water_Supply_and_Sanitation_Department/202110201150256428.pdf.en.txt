(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. The said funds are being released subject to the deposit of the unutilized funds of the year 2020-21 under the aforesaid heads to the Government and the funds should not be drawn from the Treasury without the unutilized funds being deposited to the Government. The non-salaried fund should be utilised by the respective Zila Parishads for meeting travel expenses, office expenses, leasing, motor vehicles, electricity payments, petrol/ oil/ lubricants, as per requirement.
3. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
