Preamble: With a view to provide pure and adequate drinking water to the rural population of the State. The Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme is being implemented in the state under the government decision here. For the implementation of new water supply schemes sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme (Account Head No.22159942) for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 230.13 crore has been made and the Finance Department has made available funds equivalent to 40% of the budgetary provision on the Budget Distribution System. Accordingly, the government was considering sanctioning and releasing grants to 11 district councils of Ahmednagar, Akola, Dhule, Kolhapur, Jalna, Satara, Ratnagiri, Wardha, Chandrapur, Buldhana and Sindhudurg for the implementation of tap water supply schemes approved under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme.

Terms and Conditions 1) The Zilla Parishad should spend this grant only for the implementation of new tap water supply schemes approved under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme.
The funds are to be spent in the financial year 2021-22. It should be ensured that it does not lapse or is not withdrawn from the treasury and deposited in the bank account.
3) The implementation agency will be responsible for conducting a third-party technical examination of the works of the tap water supply schemes as per the guidelines given under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme and to fulfill the observations made.
(4) No expenditure shall be incurred on any scheme in excess of the administratively approved cost.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MUPPEO-2021/ PR. No.30/ PAPU-195) Funds disbursed to the District Councils should not be kept in the Personal Provident Fund (PLA) or such other account.
6) When the funds are sought under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Program, the funds will not be sanctioned in the future without submitting information about the funds distributed in the current financial year, the expenses incurred, the balance funds, etc.
7) The material progress for the purpose for which the funds have been disbursed should be submitted along with the monthly expenditure report every month. The accounts of the allocation of funds should be kept up to date at the level of the office that collects and disburses the grants in accordance with the procedures set out in the records. Similarly, the expenditure incurred every three months should be verified with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed certificate should be submitted to this department immediately. Also, the controlling officer should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds under any circumstances.
(8) The District Councils shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure made out of this grant. The Accountant General, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and the officers authorized in this Department shall have the right to consult the books and accounts of the concerned implementing agencies for audit to ensure that the guidelines laid down by the Central Government under the Rural Drinking Water Programme and the terms and conditions of this Government Decision dated 7th May, 2016 are fulfilled by the Zilla Parishad. Also, the concerned enforcement agencies will provide full support and facilitation to the Accountants General to enable them to perform their functions properly.
9) The expenditure of the grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the reconciliation report should be submitted to the Office of the concerned implementing agencies Papu-19/ Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai on the prescribed dates. Similarly, the utility certificate should be submitted to the Accountant General's Office, Mumbai/ Nagpur and Papu-19 Office.
10) Department of Finance No. 4 The provisions of the government circular here should be strictly followed. Also, the necessary certificates should be submitted to this department.
