The Proposal:
Payment of salaries and allowances of officers/ employees of Maharashtra Life Authority.
In view of this, a sum of Rs. 144,92,00, 000/- has been released to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2019-20 under the head 22151041 as grant in aid. Further, for payment of arrears of salary and allowances from 23.3.2017 to 30.11.2017, the Authority has been granted Rs. 65,00,00, 000/- has been disbursed as per the Government Resolution No.6 in the Budget. Now, the matter of disbursement of funds for salaries and allowances up to the month of February to March under the above head of accounts is under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
As a grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salary and allowances of officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, a grant of Rs. 35,26,24, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Crores Six Hundred and Twenty Four Lakhs only) under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" is being released for the salary and allowances up to March, subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Decision shall be utilised for payment of salary for the year 2019-20 to the employees of the regular establishment of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. This fund should not be used for any previous arrears of salary. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of March, 2020 for the year 2019-20 should be deposited with the Government immediately. Fees due at the end of December 2019 should be paid before January 5 and those due at the end of January 2020 should be paid before February 5.
Patil as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. Abhay Mahajan, Bh. The Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department has been declared as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
