As per the provisions of the Maharashtra General Provident Fund Rule 30, in order to provide benefits under the Provident Fund Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme to the heirs of employees who died while working in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the department in the financial year 2023-2024," Demand No. Y-3,2355, Social Security and Welfare 60, Other Social Security and Welfare Program 104, Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme Government Provident Fund (01) Provisions in respect of Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme (01) (01) Provisions in respect of Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme (Mandatory) 50, Other Expenditure (2235 2421) Under the head" Rs. 9,00,000/- has been provided. Out of this, a grant of Rs. 90,000/- was under consideration to be made available to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune on Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS System) to meet the expenditure for the next period under this head.
Government Decision As per the provisions of Maharashtra General Provident Fund Rule 30 for payment of benefit under Provident Fund Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme to the heirs of employees who died while working in Ground Water Survey and Development System during the financial year 2023-2024, an amount of Rs. 90,000/- (Rupees Ninety Thousand only) is being released to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune by this Government.
