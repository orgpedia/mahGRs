The Proposal:
The State Government is responsible for the salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority. 1 Adopted by the Government here, the monthly income received from the Maharashtra Life Authority for ETP and technical service fee is deposited with the Government every month. As per the provisions of this Government Resolution, it was decided to create a separate head of account at the department level to meet the expenses on regular salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority and to make provision for funds as required therein, accordingly. 2215 A041 has been created for payment of salaries, allowances and pensions of officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
Since the amount due to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran towards ETP charges and technical service charges for the period from 23rd March, 2017 to 30th November, 2017 has not been deposited by the Authority with the Government, the payment of salary, allowances and pension for the period remains to be reimbursed by the Authority. The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority has deposited the amount of ETP charges and technical service charges for this period with the government.

Rajaram Narayan Vithalkar and others. Writ Petition No. 1022/2017 was filed in the High Court, Bench Nagpur. In the petition, the petitioners have sought immediate payment of pending arrears of the employees of the Authority, immediate and effective implementation of the benefits given to the government employees in the future, as well as payment of arrears of dearness allowance and stipend to the employees of the Authority. the High Court. In this petition, As the proceedings were not carried out as per the decision of the Court dated 19/09/2018, Mr. Vithalkar and others have filed a writ petition in the Hon'ble Court. The High Court, Nagpur Bench, in contempt petition No. 68/2019 has been filed.
In view of the above facts stated in the Preamble, the Government was considering payment of dearness allowance to the officers/ employees and retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 30,80,00, 000 (Rupees Thirty Crores Eighty Lakh only) as grant in respect of difference in Dearness Allowance for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017 to the officers/ employees and retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being released to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head" Demand No. Y-02,2215-Water Supply& Sanitation, 01-Water Supply, 001-Operation& Administration, (01) (04)-Subsidiary Grants in respect of Salaries& Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (22158041) (Mandatory-Datmat), 36-Support Grant (Salary) (Datmat)" subject to the following conditions:
Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran should ensure that the amount of grant is spent for the same works as the difference of dearness allowance being sanctioned for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017 for the officers/ employees and retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran.
(2) The expenditure of this Fund on other matters shall be deemed to be financial irregularities and the officers/ employees concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action for such action.
3) The amount of the said subsidy should be kept in separate accounts and a copy of the same should be sent to the government.

4) The grant amount should be spent immediately for the specific purpose for which it has been sanctioned.
