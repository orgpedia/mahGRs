Preamble: As per the review of the posts in the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) under the Government Decision dated 4th September, 2002 in the said Read No. 1 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, the 24 temporary posts included in the figure of the Department have been approved to be continued from 1st September, 2020 to 28th February, 2023 under the Government Decision dated 16th September, 2020 in the said Read No. 2, 23rd February, 2021 in the said Read No. 4, 30th August, 2021 in the said Read No. 6, 02nd March, 2022 in the said Read No. 8 and 30th August, 2022 in the said Read No. 10.
As per the Government Resolution dated 08.02.2023 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said Review but not included in the rolls.
Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 24 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes in line with providing clean and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities.
Therefore, it is necessary to continue 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 18.08.2022 of the Finance Department read above, the Government is approving the continuation of 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 as shown in Form A attached with this Government Decision.
The expenditure on this should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2022-2023 and 2023-2024 under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Water Supply and Sanitation Department (3451 0467).
