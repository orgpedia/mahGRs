Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year as per the Government Circular No. 1 and Government Notification No. 2. Provisional Common Seniority for the cadre of" Senior Geophysicist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 has been notified vide Government Circular No. 3. The Government Circular No. 4. Seniority from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 has been provisionally finalized with effect from 09.08.2021. As per the instructions given by the General Administration Department in the Government Circular No. 1 and Government Notification No. 2, the names of the officers included in the selection list for the year 2018, 2019 and 2020 for the post of Senior Geophysicist have been included in the Senior Geophysicist in the Ground Water Survey and Development System.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is hereby informed that this circular should be brought to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice and working in your department, working in the subordinate office of the agency, retired from service and on deputation from the establishment of the agency or who have changed cadre by any other means. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government by September 9, 2021, along with the feedback of the Directorate after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority information by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
