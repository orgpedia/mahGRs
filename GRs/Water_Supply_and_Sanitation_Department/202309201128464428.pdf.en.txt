Preamble: Under the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 issued in the context of Industries, Power and Labour Department, a revised rule book on procedures for office procurement to be carried out by Government Departments. Paragraph No. 2.9.2 of the said rule book provides for constitution of a State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee. As well as Rule No. 2.10 provides for determining one or more Procurement Officers. Keeping this in view, the Government was contemplating to constitute a" State Level Administrative Departmental Procurement Committee" for the procurement process/ empanelment to be carried out at the State level under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gram) Phase-II under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 2.9.2 and 2.10 of the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by Government departments in the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the Swachh Bharat Mission (Gr. For the procurement process/ empanelment to be carried out at the state level, a" State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee" is being constituted as follows. Similarly, Room Officers (PAPU-08), Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, Ministry are being designated as Procurement Officers for entering into contracts on behalf of the Department with the department suppliers.
