Funds should not be withdrawn from the treasury. The non-salaried fund should be utilised by the respective Zila Parishads for meeting travel expenses, office expenses, lease, motor vehicles, electricity payments, petrol/ oil/ lubricants etc.
4. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure of the fund on other items will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
The concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business of the State Government, the Financial Rules, the Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions before drawing and spending the disbursed funds from the Treasury.
The subsidy being disbursed under the target of" 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" is unconditional as per the Government decision dated 12.01.2023 of the Department of Finance. However, the subsidy provided by the government will be mandatorily credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS.
Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
7.31 The grants being disbursed under the target of Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) are" conditional" in nature and care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
