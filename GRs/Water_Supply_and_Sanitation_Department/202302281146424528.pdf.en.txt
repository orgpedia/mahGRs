Preamble: As per the review of the posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) under the Government decision dated 04.09.2002, 25 new posts have been created for the Water Supply and Sanitation Department as per the Government decision dated 22.03.2012. The 25 temporary posts included in the figure of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation have been approved to be continued from 1st September, 2020 to 28th February, 2023 as per the Government decision dated 16th September, 2020 at Read No. 3, 23rd February, 2021 at Read No. 5, 30th August, 2021 at Read No. 7, 02nd March, 2022 at Read No. 9 and 30th August, 2022 at Read No. 11.
As per the Government Resolution dated 08.02.2023 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of the existing temporary posts included in their rolls as well as the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said Review but not included in the rolls from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 25 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes in line with providing clean and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 25 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 08.02.2023 of the Department of Finance, read with No. 12 above, the Form-A attached to this Government Resolution shall be displayed.
