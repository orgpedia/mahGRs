The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. In order to make financial provision for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, a new head of account 2215A041 has been created vide Government Resolution dated 6.11.2017.
During the year 2017-18, a total of Rs. The authority had spent Rs 145.45 crore. However, due to the time taken to make actual provision under the new head of accounts, the Authority could not disburse the full amount of funds for the purpose of payment of salary in 2017-18. during the financial year

The government had earmarked only Rs. 39.42 crore. The remaining Rs. 106.03 crore out of Rs. 65.00 crore has been released as per the Government decision dated 24.01.2020. The remaining Rs. 41.03 crore under head 2215A041. 41.03 crore has been approved through Supplementary Demands in the Winter Session of December, 2022. The Finance Ministry has approved the release of 50 per cent of the funds. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of the said fund to the Authority for reimbursement of salary and allowances expenses of the employees of the Authority for the year 2017-18 is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 20,51,50, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as grant in aid to reimburse the expenditure of salary and allowances for the period 2017-18 to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head" Demand No.Y.22215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Auxiliary Grants in Aid of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36 Auxiliary Grants in Aid (Salary)" subject to the following conditions:
(a) The Maharashtra Jeevan Pariyojana Authority has spent its depreciation fund for the period 2017-18 on the salary and allowances of its officers/ employees. Hence, the funds received from the government should be re-deposited by the authorities in their depreciation fund.
(b) The certificate of deposit of the said amount in the Deposit Fund Account shall be submitted by the Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran to the Government.
(c) The Fund shall not be used for payment of salary and other allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
