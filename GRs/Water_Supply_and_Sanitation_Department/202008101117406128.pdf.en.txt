The Proposal:
In order to provide adequate and pure drinking water to the rural population considering the long-term needs and health of the rural areas of the State, vide reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 07.05.2016, it has been decided to implement" Mukhya Mantri Rural Drinking Water Programme (MRDWP)" in the State. As per the provisions of this Government decision, a time period of 24 months (from planning to commissioning) has been fixed for completion of works of water supply schemes. With a view to reduce the delay in the implementation of the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme, the government was considering to provide powers to the Chief Executive Officer and the Zilla Parishad to extend the works of the sanctioned water supply schemes as per the need.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad is being empowered to extend the time limit for the works of water supply schemes sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme, if it is necessary for technical/ unavoidable reasons.
