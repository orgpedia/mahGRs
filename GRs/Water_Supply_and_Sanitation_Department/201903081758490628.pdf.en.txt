Preamble- To overcome the persistent water shortage in the Marathwada region, the Government of Israel has decided to conduct a survey and other administrative work to prepare a pre-feasibility report of the Water Grid Scheme. The MoU with Mekorot was signed on 18th January, 2018. As per the provisions of the MoU, a comprehensive agreement is required to be signed within a month, the date of signing of the MoU is February 21, 2018.
Accordingly, the company is in the process of preparing a comprehensive plan and has submitted a total of 4 reports in this regard.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to allow the construction of a grid for drinking water and the planning, implementation, operation and maintenance of this work through the Maharashtra Life Authority.
To overcome the persistent water shortage in the Marathwada region, the government has approved the construction of a grid to bring unclean water to all the villages in the form of a loop system for drinking water at a rate of 40 LPCD and the planning, implementation, operation and maintenance of this work through the Maharashtra Life Authority.
