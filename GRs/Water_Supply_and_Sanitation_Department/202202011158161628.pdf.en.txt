Introduction: Under the Jal Swarojya-2 programme, Reference No. Under the Government decision here, the Under Secretary, Finance, Water Supply and Sanitation Department was declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and the Project Manager, Improvement Assistance and Project Management Cell as the" Controlling Officer." At present, the post of Project Manager under Jal Swarojya-2 programme is vacant. Accordingly, the matter of declaring a new" Controlling Officer" was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the implementation of the programme under Jalswarajya-2 programme, the Under Secretary, (Papu 11), Improvement Support and Project Management Cell is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
Under this programme for Improvement Support and Project Management Cell Reference No. The powers conferred on the Controller of Finance to sign the cheques under the Statement I accompanying the Government Resolution are being delegated to the Under Secretary (PAPU-11), Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
