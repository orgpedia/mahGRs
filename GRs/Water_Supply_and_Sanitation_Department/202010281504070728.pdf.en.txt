The Proposal:
As per the Government decisions dated 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015, a total of 4022 permanent/ temporary posts have been sanctioned in Class-I to Class-IV cadres of Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions in the District Councils. As per the Government Resolution, dated 20.3.2020, a total of 2726 temporary posts in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Divisions were extended till 31.8.2020. The government is considering extending the temporary posts in rural water supply departments for the next period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes.

Government's decision:
Out of total 2841 temporary posts on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishads, 2726 temporary posts have been extended till 31.08.2020. However, out of total 2841 temporary posts on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in the District Councils, 1914 temporary posts are working. The posts lying vacant for more than six months as per the provisions of Para 3 of the Department of Finance, Government Resolution No. ASANK-1001/ Q.No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms, dated 10th September, 2001, lapse. The posts which are lying vacant and whose additional charge has been given to other officers/ employees shall be deemed to have lapsed if they are not filled up on regular basis within six months from the date of occurrence of the vacancy. A separate decision is being taken in this regard after getting more information about the vacant posts. In view of the above, the existing 1914 temporary posts on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zila Parishads are being allowed to continue from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 subject to the assumption that the posts are not vacant for more than six months and no irregular appointments have been made to those posts.
The Department of Finance has issued instructions to abolish 25% of the sanctioned posts of Group-D cadre as per Para 1 (5) of the Government Resolution dated 14.1.2016.
Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 22.3.2018, the existing 81 vacancies in Group-D cadre in the Rural Water Supply Department of the Zilla Parishad have been abolished and the temporary posts have not been extended under this Government decision.
The expenditure on the salary and allowances of the total 155 temporary posts of Class-I and Class-II Gazetted Officers in the said posts should be covered under the head of Accounts 2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, 01 Water Supply, 001 Operation and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub-Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843) from the provision approved in the financial year 2020-2021.
