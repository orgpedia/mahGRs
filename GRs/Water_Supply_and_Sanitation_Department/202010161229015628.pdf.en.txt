The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 04/02/2005 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development machinery under this Department. The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune has requested the Government to extend the temporary posts of 273 posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency from the posts remaining after the review of the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency to this department for the implementation of the assigned schemes, machinery, etc. on the subject of water supply and keeping in view the urgency of the work.
A total of 273 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department in the head" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 182 in the head" 2702 Minor Irrigation Managements" were approved to be continued from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020 as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2.

As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 1, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021. Considering the reasons mentioned by the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune for extending the temporary posts and the provision in the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of the Finance Department, the matter of extending the temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government for the extension of 273 temporary posts in the agency. However, 155 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency have been lying vacant for more than six months. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution, Finance Department, No.Assank-1001/ Q.No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms, dated 10/09/2001, the posts lying vacant for more than six months expire. The posts which have become vacant and whose additional charge has been given to other officers/ employees are deemed to have lapsed if they are not filled up on regular basis within six months from the date of occurrence of the vacancy.
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble and the urgency of the work in the Ground Water Survey and Development Mechanism and in view of the provision in the Government Decision dated 10/09/2001 of the Finance Department mentioned in the above paragraph, the Government have decided to continue the remaining 118 temporary posts from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021, after deducting the posts lying vacant for more than six months from the temporary posts proposed by the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Mechanism, Pune.
Approval is being given for continuation of 118 temporary posts indicated in" Annexure A" on the establishment of Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The said extension is being granted subject to the assumption that other temporary posts, excluding the executive posts, are not vacant for more than six months.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
