Preamble: The Hydrology Project is being implemented by the Central Government with the financial assistance of the World Bank and under this project, various development works have been taken up in the state through the Ground Water Survey and Development Mechanism in respect of groundwater. The project has been expanded to the national level and is now being implemented as the National Hydrology Project. The funds received under the National Hydrology Project are 100% grant in aid and the Central Government has provided component wise funds to the tune of Rs. 36.00 crore for the State of Maharashtra.
Under the National Hydrology Project, works like Hydrological Information System, Decision Support System for Flood Control, Conservation of Water Resources, Repairing or Rejuvenation of Over Depth Drilling Holes or Coupon Links, Taking Materials for Video Conferencing, Providing Training to System Officials for Hydrology Project, Awareness on Ground Water.

2) The specifications of these materials should be ensured to be as per requirement and up to date.
3) The technical specifications of the items to be purchased should be in accordance with the specifications agreed by the experts. 4) It should be ensured that the items to be purchased are purchased only with proper and correct specifications by conducting a pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection before and after supply.
5) Storage and distribution of the items to be purchased after supply, supply chain management and repair and maintenance of the items will be done with the supplier manufacturers in the required agreement and management in a proper manner to ensure that these items do not remain unused and are used in a timely manner.

7) It should be ensured that there is no duplication of the purchase of these materials.
8) The guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) should be strictly followed while carrying out the entire procurement process of these items.
9) Necessary maintenance repair agreement should be made with the supplier for maintenance repair of the purchased material.
