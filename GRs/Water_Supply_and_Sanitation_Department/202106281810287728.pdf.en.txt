Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year.
Provisional seniority of Deputy Director (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development Agency dated 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017, 01/01/2018 has been notified vide Government Circular No.4& 5 respectively. Considering the objections received in this regard, a provisional final seniority list showing the status of Deputy Director (Group A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
