Guidelines for management of solid waste and wastewater in rural areas of the State under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-II have been issued vide Government Circular No. 2 dated 01.02.2018.
The government was contemplating issuing guidelines in this regard.
Considering the short time available (as on 31/03/2021) for actual implementation of Annual Action Plan 2020-21, the following supplementary guidelines are being issued:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Swabhumi 2020/ PR No. 116/ Papu 16
(a) The process of preparation of Solid Waste and Waste Water Management Plan shall be carried out in accordance with the guidelines attached. (Statement) b) After providing technical approval and administrative approval to the plan received from the village in the prescribed form (Form-A in the relevant Government No. 2), the action to be taken for the village level solid waste and sewage sub-station within the permissible limit under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-I should be taken as per the guidelines in the relevant Government No. 2. Also, while taking up the works involved in the Kritisangam, the Kritisangam-wise work should be taken up as per the prevailing procedures of various systems/ departments and guidelines issued from time to time.
e) In the Government Resolution dated 07/10/2017 of the Department of Rural Development, at Reference No. 4, a provision has been mentioned regarding grant of administrative approval for implementation of various development schemes in the District Councils. However, in the said Government decision, as there is no mention of the technical approval to be given to the development schemes, the Deputy Engineer, Rural Water Supply Sub-Division, Zilla Parishad and Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad will have the power to grant technical approval for the works related to solid waste and sewage management up to Rs. 5.0 lakh and above Rs. 5.0 lakh respectively.
Further, in view of the provisions contained in Part-II of the Table annexed to the Governance Decision dated 07/10/2017 of the Department of Rural Development, the powers to grant administrative sanction up to Rs. 5.0 lakh for Solid Waste and Sewerage Management works under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-I, to Block Development Officers, Panchayat Samitis and for works costing more than Rs. 5.0 lakh, to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad shall continue to be vested in e) The Government Decision dated 23/02/2018 of the Department of Rural Development, No. 3, as per the procedure laid down in the Governance Decision, Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-II, for Solid Waste and Sewerage Management works as" Implementing Mechanism"
e) For the district level action plan such as Menstrual and Plastic Management (Taluka level), Gobardhan and Sewage Management, action should be taken as per the instructions in the Government Circular No. 2.
(f) to take action in respect of the action of the 15th Finance Commission in accordance with the provisions of the Government Resolution of the Rural Development Department at Reference No. 5.
