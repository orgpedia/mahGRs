The proposal-
In line with the revised pay structure as per the recommendations of the Seventh Central Pay Commission, the Government have sanctioned revised pension to the retired State Government employees in accordance with the Seventh Central Pay Commission with effect from 01/01/2016 as per the Government decision at Reference No.4.
As the ETP charges received by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department are frequently decreasing, the serious question of how to meet the expenses on salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers and employees of the Authority has arisen before the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. Therefore, the decision of the Government of Maharashtra No. 2 in the context

The State Government has taken over the responsibility of regular salary and allowances of the officers/ employees of the Life Authority as well as the pension of the retired officers/ employees.
Retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran are being paid pension as per the pay scale of the 6th Pay Commission as per the decision of the Government in the context (1). Retired State Government employees have been sanctioned revised pension as per 7th Pay Commission with effect from 01.01.2016. However, since the Government had taken over the salary/ allowances of the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with effect from 23rd March, 2017, the matter of sanction of revised pension to the retired employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission was under consideration of the Government since April, 2017.
Government's decision:
The revised pension is being sanctioned to the retired employees of Maharashtra Life Authority with effect from 1st April, 2017 as per the powers conferred under Section 66 (2) (b) of the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1979. The revised pension of these employees under reference No. 4 and 5 shall be considered in the manner set out in the Governing Decision.
Pension to be paid to the concerned retired officers/ employees as per this revised pay scale from 1st of next month (from 1st October, 2021).

The Authority shall separately demand from the Government the amount of allowable arrears as per actual consideration after taking into account the revised pension of all retired employees in respect of allowable arrears from April 1, 2017 to September 30, 2021.
