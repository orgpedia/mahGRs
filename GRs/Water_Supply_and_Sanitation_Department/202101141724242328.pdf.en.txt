The Proposal:
The Government has, vide Government Resolution dated 23.3.2017, taken over the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and sanctioned a sum of Rs.2215A052/- to the Authority for the financial year 2020-21 as subsidy to meet the expenditure on pension of retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.

125,44,78, 500/- has been disbursed as per the decision of the Government of India vide serial numbers 5, 6 and 7 in the Budget. Now the matter of disbursement of further funds made available by the Finance Department under the above head of accounts is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2020-21, a grant of Rs. 62,72,39, 250/- (Rupees Sixty Two Crore Seventy Two Lakhs Seventy Nine Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) is being released to the Authority under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to Other Non-Government Organizations (01) Assistance to Other Non-Government Organizations (01) (01) Assistance to Government Employees Transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (Subsidy Compulsory) (2215 A052) 31, Assistance to Non-Salaried)" subject to the following conditions:
(a) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall ensure that the amount of the grant is spent for the same purposes as the grant is sanctioned for the pension benefits of retired officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
(b) This fund should not be used for any outstanding dues as on 30.03.2017. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
e) Separate accounts of the amount of free subsidy should be maintained.
(e) The Authority shall inform the Government from time to time about the allocations made under the free grants.
