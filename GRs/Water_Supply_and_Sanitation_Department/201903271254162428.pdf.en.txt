Preamble: As per the provisions of Section 8 of the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1976, the Government has the power to approve the necessary posts for the Maharashtra Life Authority. The configuration of the posts of the Regional Offices in the Authority has been fixed by the Government Decision dated 22nd June, 2001. The Finance Department has issued instructions to abolish 25% of the sanctioned posts in Group-D cadre under Paragraph 1 (5) of the Government Resolution dated 14th January, 2016. Accordingly, the matter of abolition of 25 per cent of the sanctioned number of posts in Group-D cadre on the regular establishment of Maharashtra Life Authority was under the consideration of the Government. The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
A total of 777 posts are sanctioned in Group-D cadre on regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, including 2 Binders, 2 Ferraprinters, 3 Naiks, 21 Daktaris, 110 Chowkidars and 639 peons. A total of 194 posts are to be filled.
