Preamble: The Maharashtra Life Authority is empowered to make regulations with the approval of the Government regarding the recruitment of officers/ employees of the Authority as per the provisions of Section 66 of the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1976. However, the Authority has not framed separate Service Entry Rules for all the posts in the Authority and the Service Entry Rules framed by the Department of Water Resources for the Engineering Cadre in that Department have been made applicable to the Authority under the Government Order under reference (1). The Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution (2) to draft the Service Entry Rules in pursuance of the preparation of separate Service Entry Rules of the Authority for the Engineering Cadre in the Maharashtra Life Authority. The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee. The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
The Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 13.04.2018 to draft the Service Entry Rules in pursuance of the preparation of separate Service Entry Rules of the Authority for the Engineers of Maharashtra Life Authority is being reconstituted as follows.
Gengje, Deputy Secretary- Committee Chairperson Smt. Dipali Deshpande-Savedkar, Chief Administrative Officer (Majipra)- Committee Member Mr. Ganesh Pawar, Under Secretary- Committee Member Secretary
