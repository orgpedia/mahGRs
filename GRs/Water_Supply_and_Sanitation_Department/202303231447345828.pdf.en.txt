The Proposal:
The provident fund of all the employees/ officers working with Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran since 1982-83 is deposited with the Government under Account Head-8336 Civil Deposit as a contribution/ statutory deposit from the Authority.
The interest is paid to the Authority under head of accounts 20491268 in the prevailing manner at the rate of interest of Provident Fund on the total amount deposited with the Government at the end of March.
2. Deposits of Employees Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran with Government as on 31.3.2022 amounting to Rs. 344, 45, 53, 944/- deposited by the Accountant General

The office has intimated vide letter dated 15.2.2023 and accordingly the request for disbursement of interest amount has been made vide letter dated 17.2.2023 by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Accordingly, a proposal is under consideration of the Government to disburse the interest amount on the deposits to the Authority through book adjustment at the prevailing rate of interest of Provident Fund.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2022-23, an amount of Rs. 24,45,63, 330/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Forty Five Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand Thirty Three only) is being disbursed to the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head Demand No. Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (20491268) to meet the interest expenditure on the provident fund of the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
The expenditure from the provision sanctioned under this Government decision is to be adjusted by way of book adjustment and this expenditure is to be met from the sanctioned grant for the financial year 2022-23 by showing the expenditure towards payment of interest on the provident fund of the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head" Demand No. Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268)."
The funds sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be deposited by book adjustment under the head" 8336-Civil Deposits, 800 Other Deposits, (00) (08) Maharashtra Water Supply and Sewerage Board Employees Provident Fund."
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the consent received from the Finance Department, Informal Reference No. 96/ 2023/ Expenditure-3, dated 02.03.2023 and the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Government Circular, Finance Department, No. The terms and conditions mentioned in this circular are being fulfilled.
