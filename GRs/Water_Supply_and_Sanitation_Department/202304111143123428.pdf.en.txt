The Proposal:
In order to ensure smooth implementation of rural water supply schemes in ZPs, a total of 4147 posts in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions of ZPs have been identified as revised figure vide Government Decision dated 28.12.2022 and 2765 of these posts have been identified as permanent posts and remaining 1382 as temporary posts vide Government Decision dated 10.04.2023. The Department of Finance vide Government Resolution dated 21.6.2006, has empowered all the Administrative Departments to extend the tenure of all temporary posts included in their profile from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. Accordingly, the government is considering to extend the temporary posts in rural water supply departments for the next period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes in the district councils.
