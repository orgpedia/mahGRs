The proposal-
In line with the revised pay structure as per the recommendations of the Seventh Central Pay Commission, the State Government employees have been sanctioned revised pay structure in accordance with the Seventh Central Pay Commission with effect from 01/01/2016 as per the Government Notification at Reference No.3.
As the ETP charges received by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department are frequently decreasing, the serious question of how to meet the expenses on salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers and employees of the Authority has arisen before the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. Therefore, pursuant to the Government's decision at Reference No. 2

The state government has taken the responsibility of regular salary and allowances of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority as well as the pension of the retired officers/ employees.
The employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pariyojana are being paid salaries and allowances as per the pay scale of the Sixth Pay Commission as per the decision of the Government under clause (1). The revised pay structure has been approved for the state government employees as per the 7th Pay Commission with effect from January 1, 2016. However, since the Government has taken over the responsibility of salary/ allowances of the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran with effect from 23rd March, 2017, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the revised pay structure for the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran with effect from April, 2017 in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission.
Government's decision
As per the powers conferred under Section 66 (2) (b) of the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1976, the revised pay structure as indicated in the Annexure A is being sanctioned to the officers/ employees of the Authority with effect from 1st April, 2017. The salary of the concerned officers/ employees should be paid as per this revised pay scale from 1st of next month (from 1st October, 2021).
