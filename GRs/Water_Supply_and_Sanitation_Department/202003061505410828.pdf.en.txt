Government's decision
Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran, Mumbai office has been allocated Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores only) is being sanctioned and released by the Budget Estimates System (BEAMS) subject to the following conditions.
1) The funds should be spent in proportion to the population under tribal application.
The grant is to be spent by the concerned implementing agencies during the financial year 2019-20 in respect of tap water supply scheme in the areas dominated by Scheduled Tribes in rural areas under the Scheduled Tribes Sub Plan as per the instructions of the Central Government and the State Government from time to time.
(3) The respective implementing agencies shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure incurred from these grants.
The Accountant General, Maharashtra- 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur, as well as the officer authorized in this section, shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts of the concerned implementing agencies for audit, so as to ensure that the terms and conditions of this Government decision and the guidelines laid down by the Central Government under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme (Jal Jeevan Mission) are being fulfilled by the concerned implementing agencies. Also, the concerned enforcement agencies will provide full support and facilitation to the Accountants General to enable them to perform their functions properly.
