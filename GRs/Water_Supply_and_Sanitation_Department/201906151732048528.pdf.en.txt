The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. An amount of Rs. 59,74,98, 000/- has been sanctioned as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the financial year 2019-20 under the head 22158041 towards payment of salary and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority. Accordingly, in the year 2019-20, the matter of disbursement of salary subsidy to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the above head of accounts for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1419/ PR No. 55/ PAPU-23
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 51,77,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty One Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs only) out of the Account Grant sanctioned in the year 2019-20 to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (2215A041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under this Government decision subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be used for the payment of salary/ allowances to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2019-20. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority during 2019-20 at the end of May, 2019 should be deposited with the Government immediately. Also, the fee due at the end of June 2019 should be deposited with the government before July 5, 2019.
Patil has been posted as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. R. M. Gengaje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been posted as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
