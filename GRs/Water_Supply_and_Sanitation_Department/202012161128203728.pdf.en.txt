Circular to the Government, Finance Department, No. 2020/ No.62/ Exchequer-5, D. Circular dated 1st April, 2020 2. Government Circular, Finance Department, No. April 16, 2020
The Proposal:
Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)" for meeting the travel expenses of Class 1 and Class 2 officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in Zilla Parishad, for the financial year 2020-21 under the heading:
Budget provision of Rs. 1,23,38, 000/- has been made for domestic travel expenses.
Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure. Accordingly, as per the Government Circular dated 16th April, 2020 of the Department of Finance, expenditure under other objectives i.e. 11 domestic travel expenses should not be incurred by the departments without the approval of the Department of Finance. Accordingly, in its submission to the Department of Finance, the Department of Finance has made available only 10% of the total provision under target 11, domestic travel expenditure. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of travel allowance is under consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1420/ P.No.92/ Papu-23
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, a total amount of Rs. 12,33,800/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer to meet the travel expenses of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Mandatory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)."
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. Considering the overall economic situation of the State in view of the health and economic disaster caused by COVID-19, it is necessary to curtail expenditure and defer some expenditure.
3. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
4. Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said decision is submitted to the government.
The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
Expenditure from the grant sanctioned through this Government Resolution should be spent under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Director and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Compulsory) (22151843)" 11, Domestic Travel Expenditure for implementation of Water Supply Programme and it should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21.
