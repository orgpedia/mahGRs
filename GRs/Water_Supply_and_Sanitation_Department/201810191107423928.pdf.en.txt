For the financial year 2018-19, the Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation), vide its Government Resolution dated 17.10.2018, was considering the issue of release of funds to the Divisional Commissioners, Pune, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur.
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 8335.00 lakhs (Rupees Eighty Three Crores Thirty Five Lakhs only) is being sanctioned and released to the Divisional Commissioners, Pune, Amravati, Aurangabad and Nagpur under the head of accounts 22450093 to meet the expenditure on the measures taken/ to be taken for drinking water scarcity in Pune, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur divisions as per the norms laid down by the Government in the Standing Government Decisions in force in rural/ urban areas and subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions
Divisional Commissioners of Pune, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur should ensure that the funds are distributed to the respective Collectors.
The funds will be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas for the period 2017-18 as well as for the previous scarcity period and for the payment of pending dues during this period. While disbursing the funds, the Collector should take a decision from his level itself to allocate funds on priority for well acquisition, small contractors and drinking water tankers, taking into account the individual demands. The same amount of funds should be distributed district-wise.
