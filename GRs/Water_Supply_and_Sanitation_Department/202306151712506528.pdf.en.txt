Preamble- In the financial year 2023-2024, to meet the cost of installation of Ground Water Survey and Development System, under the head" Capital Expenditure on Demand No.Y-06, 4402 Soil and Water Conservation, (00) (101) Soil Survey and Testing, (01) (01) Minor Irrigation Schemes under One Ground Water Survey and Development Agency (4402 1148) (Mandatory)," a total amount of Rs. 32, 28, 14, 000/- has been provided. Of these, the grants received from the Finance Department have already been disbursed to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune.
Under this head, the matter of providing grants to the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune, to meet the expenditure for the next period under the targets under which the grants have now been received from the Finance Department, was under consideration.
Government Decision In the financial year 2023-2024, a total grant of Rs. 1,50,09, 700/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh Nine Thousand Seven Hundred Only) as shown in Column V of the following statement to meet the expenditure on installation of Ground Water Survey and Development System and other ancillary matters.
