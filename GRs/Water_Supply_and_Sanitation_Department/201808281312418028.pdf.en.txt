GOVERNMENT DECISIONS, WATER SUPPLY AND SANITAL DEPARTMENT, NO.: APNA-0218/ PR No. 163/ Papu15, dated 31st May, 2018 GOVERNMENT ORDER:
As per Section 4 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the transfer of officers in the cadre of" Junior Geologist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency on administrative grounds was effected vide Government Order dated 31/05/2018. However, since the transfers of some of these officers were not in accordance with the policy of counselling passed by the General Administration Department dated 09/04/2018, the competent authority was requested to reconsider the transfers of the concerned.
As per the approval given by the competent authority, the transfer orders of the following Junior Geologists in the Ground Water Survey and Development System are being revised and they are being given revised postings as indicated in column number 4 before their names.
