The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. Payment of Salaries and Allowances of Officers/ Employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Majipra-1118/ PR No.114/ Papu-23
A budget provision of Rs. 185,61,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Five Crores Sixty Six Lakh only) has been made for the financial year 2018-19 under the head 22151041 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. The Department of Finance, vide the above circular, has made available 70% of the said provision on Budgetary System for the year 2018-19. Out of the total provision for expenditure on payroll at the end of September, 2018, Rs. 68,91,60, 000/- has been disbursed to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra vide Grants-in-Aid No. 3 and 4 respectively. Under the above head of accounts, funds are required to be disbursed to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the expenditure of salary/ allowances for the next period from October, 2018. Further, as per the decision of the Government in (5) above, Dearness Allowance has been made admissible to the officers/ employees of the Authority at par with other Government employees and the arrears of Dearness Allowance from the date 1.01.2018 onwards will be met from the sanctioned and available grants for the year 2018-19 by incurring expenditure under the head 22158041. The DA of the employees of the Authority has been taken into consideration as 113% while disbursing funds from the Government to the Authority and from 1.01.2018 onwards, the DA rate has been taken into account as 139% and the Authority is required to disburse funds to pay the outstanding amount to the employees. Accordingly, the matter of disbursing the necessary funds to the Maharashtra Life Authority is under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
To meet the expenditure of salary/ allowances for the period from October, 2018 to December, 2018 of officers/ employees on regular establishment in Maharashtra Life Authority, Rs. 37,78,59, 000/- for the period from 1st January, 2018 to 31st August, 2018. 5,98,88, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Forty Seven Thousand Only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as grant under the head" Demand No.Y.22215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran against Salaries and Allowances (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22150041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)." The rate of Dearness Allowance in respect of expenditure on pay/ allowances for the period October, 2018 to December, 2018 has been worked out as 139 and the amount is being disbursed subject to the following conditions:
