(1) The above-mentioned sub-sections. He is being relieved unilaterally from the office mentioned in Rokana No. 3 with effect from the date of this order i.e. 23rd March, 2022 (M. No. The order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned office.

The transferred officers should immediately join the office mentioned in Rakana No. 4 and submit the report to the government through the Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System without delay. If it is found that the transferred officers have not joined the place of transfer, disciplinary action will be taken against them under the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. The transferred officers should be reinstated immediately. The head of the office and the establishment officer concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an officer without fixing a mandatory waiting period.
(3) The officers being posted under the said order shall be required to join immediately at the place of their posting. If any officer fails to appear at the place of posting or tries to directly or indirectly pressurize the transfer order, it should be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officer. Such officers will be liable for disciplinary action as the act violates Rule 3 (3) and Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
Since these transfers are required to be effected effectively, the salary of all the transferred officers as on 23/03/2022 shall be drawn by the office in which they are presently working. The salary from the date 24/03/2022 should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer office under any circumstances. After the concerned officer arrives at the transfer office, the post-transfer office should deduct the salary and submit a report to the government. Instructions are being issued to all Heads of Departments and Heads of Offices in the Ground Water Survey and Development System that they will be held personally responsible if the drawing and disbursement officer of the office in which they were working before the transfer of salary from 24/03/2022 is paid.
Since the above transfers are being made on the request of the concerned, they will not be allowed to join the place of their transfer during the period of assumption of office and any kind of travelling allowance or incidental allowances.
These orders will come into force with immediate effect. The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency is being informed to immediately submit to the government the information whether the transferred officers have joined the transferred office or how.
