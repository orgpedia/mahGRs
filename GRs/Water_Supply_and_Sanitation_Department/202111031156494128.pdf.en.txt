The Proposal:
The Government has taken the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran vide Government Resolution dated 23.3.2017. In view of the fact that it may take a long time for the government to pay the pension expenses of retired employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority through the treasury from the regular Principal Accounts Title 2071, the new account title 2215A052, which was created temporarily to provide subsidy to the Authority for the retirement salary of retired employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, was extended till 2020-21 by the government decision dated 09.07.2020. The government is planning to extend the scheme for 2021-22.

Government's decision:
