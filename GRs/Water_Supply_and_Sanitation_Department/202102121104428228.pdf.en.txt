2. Deposit of provident fund of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran to the Government. A proposal to disburse an amount of Rs. 7.50 crore to the Authority through book adjustment is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2020-21, to meet the cost of interest on the provident fund of the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, under the head Demand No." Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on the Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268), the Authority has to pay Rs. 7,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Lakhs only) is being disbursed under this Government decision.
The expenditure from the provision sanctioned under this Government decision shall be adjusted by way of book adjustment and the expenditure shall be met from the sanctioned grant for the financial year 2020-21 by showing the expenditure towards payment of interest on the provident fund of the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the head Demand No. Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268).
