Read 1. Meeting 2018/ No.78/ PAPU-19D dated 19th April 2018) No. Meeting 2019/ S. No.49/ Papu-07D July 16, 2019 Preamble- Under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme, Jal Swarajya Phase-II and Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme in the State, the Government was considering to set guidelines for the use of solar pumps in new schemes wherever possible to reduce the future expenditure on payment of dues and alternative maintenance of schemes under the programme and to increase the use of sustainable energy sources.
Government Decision: The implementation of solar energy projects for the projects in progress under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme and the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme in the state, the expenditure for which should be included in the budget of the schemes under which the schemes have been approved. While the implementation of the above schemes is done through the Zilla Parishad or Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will work as the implementation mechanism for all solar power projects. The tender process for these solar power projects should be carried out jointly by the central office and Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
