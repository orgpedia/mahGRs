Preamble: The Government Circular dated 14th February, 2018 specifies that temporary water supply schemes and special repairs of tap water schemes taken under drinking water scarcity should not be approved at the field level after 31st March, 2018. However, as these measures could not be approved before 31st March, 2019 due to the Model Code of Conduct for this year's General Election to the Lok Sabha, the Government Circular No.8 of May, 2019 was extended till 15th May, 2019. However, considering the administrative approval, tender process and other aspects, as well as this year's acute water shortage, the government was considering extending the deadline. Government Circular: Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the following decisions are being taken regarding the measures to be taken for water supply under scarcity.
As per the instructions in the Government Circular, dated 08.05.2019, all the measures to be taken under the scarcity can be approved by the level of Divisional Commissioner and Collector by May 15, 2019. However, it was informed that now all the measures to be taken under the scarcity can be approved by the level of Divisional Commissioner and Collector by June 15, 2019, for which the water supply can be started after completion of the measures before June 30, 2019.
