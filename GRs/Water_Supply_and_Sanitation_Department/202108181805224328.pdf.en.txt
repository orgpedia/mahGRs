The Proposal:
A grant of Rs. 144.00 lakh has been released to the concerned Zilla Parishads under the head" Demand No.Y-2, 2215-Water Supply and Sanitation, 196 Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, (01)-Gramin Tap Water Supply Scheme, (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees of Regular Establishments (Mandatory) (22151914), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" to meet the salary expenditure of the employees of Converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishments of Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad for the financial year 2021-22. The government was considering to disburse funds to the Ratnagiri Zilla Parishad for the salary and allowances of the employees working on the converted regular temporary (CRT) establishment in the rural water supply department of the Ratnagiri Zilla Parishad for the financial year 2021-22. The following decision has been taken:

Government Resolution No: Budget-1421/ No.66/ Papu-23
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 22,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakh only) is being disbursed to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, ZP, Ratnagiri for the salary and allowances of 31 employees mentioned in the accompanying statement on Converted Regular Temporary (CRT) Establishment in the Rural Water Supply Department of Ratnagiri ZP for the financial year 2021-22.
The Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw this fund from the treasury.
The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS.
Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
3. The fund has been sanctioned for the salary and allowances of 31 employees mentioned in the accompanying statement for the financial year 2021-22 and the use of this fund for other purposes will be considered as financial irregularity for which the concerned responsible officers will be liable for disciplinary action. If an employee in the accompanying list is not currently employed, the matter should be brought to the notice of the government immediately by the concerned district council.
4) Before drawing the funds from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
Before demanding further funds, the concerned District Council should submit the utilization certificate of the said amount with the signature of the Chief Executive Officer.
6. The expenditure sanctioned by this Government under the head Demand No.Y-2, 2215- Water Supply and Sanitation, 196- Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions, (01)- Rural Tap Water Supply Scheme, (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees of Regular Establishments (Mandatory) (22151914), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
