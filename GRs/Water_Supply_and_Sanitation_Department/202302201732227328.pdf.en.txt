A total amount of Rs. 601.75 lakhs has been disbursed under the Grants (Non-salaried) Scheme as per the Government decision vide Note No. 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8 and 9 of the earlier Notes.
The revised contours of the Rural Water Supply Department under the Zilla Parishad have been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 28.12.2022. In this framework, separate sub-divisions have been sanctioned for each taluk and the number of sub-divisions under the Water Supply Department has been increased from 173 to 355. The matter of disbursing funds for the expenditure of these new subdivisions is under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, a grant of Rs. 2,00,56, 400/- (Rupees Two Crores only) under the head 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable) for the financial year 2022-23 under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)" is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad as shown in the" Statement A" accompanying this Government Resolution.
The non-salary funds to be disbursed under this Government Decision shall be utilised by the respective District Councils for the expenditure of the new Sub-Divisions as per the requirement. This money should not be used for any other purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The grants sanctioned under this Government decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
4. Care should be taken to submit to the Government the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as to deposit the unutilized funds disbursed earlier. Also, the expenditure incurred should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's office from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed. Payment of subsidy by the Government through ECS to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
