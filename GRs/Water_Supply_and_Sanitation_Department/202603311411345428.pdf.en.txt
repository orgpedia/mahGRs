Introduction: Sant Gadgebaba Gram Sanitation Mission is being implemented in rural areas of the state with a view to increase the spontaneous public participation of rural people in rural sanitation programmes. Under this campaign, awards are given to eligible Gram Panchayats at Zilla Parishad group level, Zilla Parishad level, Block level and State level under the Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Swachh Gram competition. Awards are also given to eligible Gram Panchayats under Special Awards.
