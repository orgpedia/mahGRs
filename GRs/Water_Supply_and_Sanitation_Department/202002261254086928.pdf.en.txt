Preamble As per the review of the posts in the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) under the Government decision dated 04.09.2002, 24 temporary posts included in the figure of the Department have been approved to be continued from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 as per the Government decision dated 11.10.2019 in the said Chapter No.3 of the Department.
As per the Government Resolution dated 12th February, 2020 of the Department of Finance, No.4 of the said Chapter, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts, which are included in their rolls and which have been created after the approval of the previous Review but have not been included in the rolls, from 01st March, 2020 to 31st August, 2020. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of 24 temporary posts in the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself). The government is taking the following steps:

Government Resolution No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.110/ Papu-01
Government Decisions As per the policy of the Central Government and the State Government, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation needs more manpower to implement the schemes in line with providing safe and adequate drinking water and sanitation facilities. Therefore, it is necessary to continue 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself).
Therefore, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 12th February, 2020 of the Finance Department, read with the above, the Government is approving the continuation of 24 temporary posts in the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) as shown in Form A attached with this Government Decision from 01st March, 2020 to 31st August, 2020.
Expenditure on 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head-3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467), shall be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2019-2020.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative departments as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 12th February, 2020 of the Finance Department.
