The policy decision to deposit ETP charges and technical charges with the Government was taken by the Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 and accordingly, the Government has issued a Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017. A total grant of Rs. 182.0392 crore has been released to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2022-23 under the head 2215A041 as grant in aid for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 35,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Crores only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salaries and allowances of its officers/ employees under this Government decision subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be used for the approved and admissible salary/ allowances for the year 2022-23 of the employees on regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
(b) The technical charges and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of February, 2023 shall be deposited with the Government immediately.
(a) The Department of Finance. As per the directions given by the Government Circular dated 22.11.2021, the Authority should take an undertaking from the concerned employee regarding the overpayment amount.
