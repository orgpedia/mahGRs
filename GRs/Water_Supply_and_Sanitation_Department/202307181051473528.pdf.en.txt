Department of General Administration Letter No. Parivartan-2023/ PR No. 16/18, dated 10th July, 2023 Government Circular The Central Government has launched an online portal for training of government officers/ employees in the name of" Karmayogi Bharat" and this project is being implemented in the state for capacity building of officers/ employees of various government departments/ offices in the state through Mission Karmayogi. Many useful trainings are available free of cost on this training portal. Mission Karmayogi has been launched in the State on 12th May, 2023 and the following training on Mission Karmayogi Portal is being made mandatory for all Auxiliary Cell Officers/ Clerks-Typists and Personal Assistants in the Department as well as in the field offices under the Department to undergo online training for a period of two months from 1st August 2023 to 30th September 2023.
Link- http:// igotkarmayogi.gov.in/ 1. Digital Literacy
2. Noting and Drafting
3. Code of Conduct for Government Employees
4. Effective Communication
5. Introduction to Government E Marketplace
6. Stay Safe in Cyber Space
The training is mandatory and the certificate of completion of the training and the report of the completion of the work by the employees of the department should be submitted to the Establishment Branch and the employees of the subordinate field offices of the department to the concerned Office Papu 15 and Office Papu 23.
