Government's decision Vishnupuri, T.G. The revised budget of Rs. 3,22,94, 100/- (Rupees Three Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Forty Four Thousand Nine Hundred only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
(1) The revised cost estimates being approved for the scheme shall be subject to technical approval from the competent authority.
The terms and conditions mentioned in the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation to the effect that the scheme has been actually completed by the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Nanded. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned executive engineer, officials of the Zilla Parishad and the concerned Gram Panchayat and the contractor should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
4. to meet recurring expenditure incurred annually for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs) of the scheme, related to depreciation in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad will not get any help from the government.
Since the works of the scheme have been completed, after examining the scheme, the scheme should be transferred to the local body (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad) and the accounts of the scheme should be closed.
6. Action to be taken as per the prevailing policy on dowry.
7. It shall be incumbent upon the contractor concerned to carry out the remaining works of the revised scheme as per the technical changes.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
2. The revised cost of the scheme is Rs. 13,79,037/- (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand Thirty Seven only) should be made available from the following State share/ District Planning Board/ Minimum Needs Programme.
