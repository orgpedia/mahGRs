The Proposal:
Caste validity of candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribes who have been appointed but have not been able to produce the caste validity certificate of Scheduled Tribes or Other Backward Classes.
Creation of more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have submitted their certificates.
General Administration Department under consideration for transfer to the post of Service Superintendents
As per the Government Decision dated 21.12.2019. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Life Authority has proposed to create 98 supernumerary posts in the office under the Maharashtra Life Authority to transfer the services of 98 employees to the rank of superior.
The reference has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 27.11.2020. Accordingly
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Majipra-1120/ PR No. 96/ Papu-23
A total of 98 supernumerary posts for employees in the subordinate offices of the Maharashtra Life Authority were under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 98 supernumerary posts are being approved to be created on the establishment of Maharashtra Life Authority for the transfer of a total of 98 employees who were appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category in the office under the Maharashtra Life Authority under the Government decision under the General Administration Department, but could not produce the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribe or the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Class, to the supernumerary post temporarily for 11 months from the date of service 21.12.2019 or till the date on which they would have retired if they had remained in service, whichever is earlier. The details of the posts of these 98 officers/ employees are mentioned in the accompanying prospectus.
Further necessary action in respect of appointment of the said officer/ staff to the post of Superintendents shall be taken by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
3. The expenditure on the salary of the said Superintendents shall be defrayed under the heads of accounts relating to the salary of the office concerned and shall be met from the grants sanctioned during the relevant financial year.
