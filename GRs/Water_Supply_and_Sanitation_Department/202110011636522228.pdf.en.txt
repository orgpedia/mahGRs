No.133/ Temporary Posts/ Extension/ 440/2021, dated 21/09/2021 Preamble: As per the Government Resolution dated 04/02/2005 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development machinery under the Department. The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune has requested the Government to extend the temporary posts of 273 posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency from the posts remaining after the review of the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency to this department for the implementation of water supply schemes, machinery, etc. and keeping in view the urgency of the work.
A total of 136 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department in the head" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 89 in the head" 2702 Small Irrigation Managers" were approved to be continued from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 under the Government decision No. 2.
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Division at Reference No. 1, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022. Considering this, the government is considering to extend the term of 149 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Keeping in view the urgency of work in the Ground Water Survey and Development System mentioned in the Preamble, this Government has decided to continue 149 temporary posts in the establishment of Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
149 temporary posts as indicated in" Annexure-A" on the establishment of Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune are being approved to be continued subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The said period is being extended subject to the assumption that the said temporary posts have not lapsed.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
(3) As from the date on which the officer/ employee holding the post of Superintendant is absorbed in the other posts, the Superintendents post shall stand automatically abolished.
The expenditure for the aforesaid temporary posts shall be met from the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts referred to in Annexure-B.
