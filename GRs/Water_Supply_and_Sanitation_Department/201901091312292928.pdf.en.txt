The Proposal:
The amount specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 for the year 2018-2019 has been approved by the Legislature and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra.
The provident fund of all the employees/ officers working with the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran since 1982-83 is deposited with the Government under the head of accounts-8336 civil deposits as the amount of subscription/ statutory deposit received from the Authority.

The interest on the total amount deposited with the government at the end of March is paid at the interest rate of the provident fund in the prevailing manner under the head of accounts 2049 1268.
The Government has a total of Rs. 3 crore in its account at the end of March, 2018 towards the provident fund deposits of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. 267,06,59, 699/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Sixty Six Crores Six Lakhs Sixty Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Ninety Nine only). A proposal is under consideration of the government to adjust the interest amount by book adjustment at the prevailing rate of interest on the provident fund.
Government's decision:
Demand No.Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) (01) Interest on Provident Fund (2049 1268) of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2018-19 for nine months from April, 2018 to December, 2018. 14,15,26, 000/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Fifteen Lakhs Twenty Six Thousand only) is being released under this Government decision.
The expenditure arising out of the provision sanctioned under this Government decision shall be adjusted by way of book adjustment and the expenditure shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2018-19 by showing the expenditure under the head Demand No.Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (2049 1268) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (45, Interest).
The funds sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be deposited by book adjustment under the head" 8336-Civil Deposits, 800 Other Deposits, (00) (08) Maharashtra Water Supply and Sewerage Board Employees Provident Fund (8336 5067)."
This Government decision is being issued under the authority given to the departments under the Government Circular No. Finance-2018/ PR No. 69/ Finance-3, dated April 2, 2018 of the Finance Department. The fund is an interest-bearing provident fund and will be book-adjusted
