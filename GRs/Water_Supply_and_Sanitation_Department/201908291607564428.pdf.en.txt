The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Life Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Life Authority with the Government. A budget provision of Rs. 239,43,69, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Nineteen Crores Thirty Three Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand only) has been made for the financial year 2019-20 under the head 22151041 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. The Department of Finance vide circular dated 8.7.2019 has made available 60% of the said provision on Budgetary System for the year 2019-20. A grant of Rs. 51,77,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty One Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs only) for the four months April, 2019 to July, 2019 has been released to the Authority as per the Government Resolution No.4 of the Budget.

Now, the matter of disbursement of wage subsidy for the five months from August, 2019 to December, 2019 under the above head of accounts is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
A grant of Rs. 62,35,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Two Crores Thirty Five Lakhs only) is being released as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the month of August, 2019 to December, 2019 for the following five months under the head Demand No. Y2,2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22158041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be used for the payment of salary/ allowances to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2019-20. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
The technical fees and ETP charges deposited with the Authority during 2019-20 at the end of July, 2019 should be deposited with the Government immediately. Also, the fee due at the end of August, 2019 should be paid to the government before September 5, 2019, the fee due at the end of September, 2019, before October 5, 2019, the fee due at the end of October, 2019, before November 5, 2019, and the fee due at the end of November, 2019, before December 5, 2019.
2. In order to receive the amount disbursed under this Government decision, the Director (Finance) of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, Mr. Y. B. Patil, has been appointed as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mrs. Nidhi Chaudhary, Bh. The Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department has been declared as the Controlling Officer.
