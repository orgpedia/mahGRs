A total of 273 temporary posts have been given. It is requested to extend the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. Considering these matters, the matter of extending the tenure of these temporary posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Considering the urgency of the work in the Ground Water Survey and Development System and the extension of the temporary posts from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018, the Government is approving the continuation of 91 posts in the head of Accounts" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 182 posts in the head of Accounts" 2702 Small Enterprises" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, totalling 273 temporary posts (as per attached statement-A) from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019.
The expenditure incurred on these posts should be met from the sanctioned provisions of the financial year under the respective head of accounts.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Ministries/ Departments under the Government Decision No. 4 of the Department of Finance.
