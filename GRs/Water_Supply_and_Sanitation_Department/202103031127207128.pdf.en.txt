Government's decision:
Pension of retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, like other government employees, will be paid by the government under the account head 20710103 for the year 2021-22.
The procedure to be adopted from the Government Decision dated 17.10.2019 has been prescribed in the context. The decision has been put on hold till further orders.
Department of Finance Informal Reference No. 218/20/ Treasury No.5 dated 6.10.2020 and Directorate, Accounts and Treasuries Informal Reference No. This Government decision is being issued based on the feedback received under 4/32-A, dated 7.1.2021.
