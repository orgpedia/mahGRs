The Proposal:
The draft rules, titled Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018, have been published for public comments through a contextual notification to carry out the provisions of the Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Act, 2009. Objections to be sent to any person regarding these draft rules should be sent in writing to the Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department by 31/08/2018 or by e-mail to psec. wssd@maharashtra. gov. in by 31/08/2018. The objections/ suggestions received till 31/08/2018 will be considered by the Government.
All the office-bearers have been instructed to widely publicize the rules to get public feedback on the draft rules, namely, Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018, framed under the Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Act, 2009. Apart from this, seminars on draft rules are also being organized in all important offices of the state. This is expected to spread the ground water rules all over the state and receive public objections.
Publicity/ Workshops are being organized by the Department/ various offices of the Government to publicize the draft rules of Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018.

The Government was considering extension of time for consideration of the draft rules by the Government for receiving more quality objections and suggestions on the draft rules.
Government circular:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the draft rules namely Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018 published vide Notification under reference are hereby extended till 30/09/2018 for consideration by the Government.
Any person desirous of filing objections or suggestions in respect of the draft rules, Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018 before the above mentioned date, may send the same in writing to the Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, 7th Floor, Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital Building Complex, Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mumbai before 30/09/2018 or by e-mail to psec. wssd@maharashtra. gov. in.
The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is hereby informed that due action may be taken immediately to inform all Government offices/ general public about the extension of time given till 30/09/2018 for filing objections on the draft rules namely Maharashtra Ground Water (Development and Management) Rules, 2018.
