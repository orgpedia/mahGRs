The proposal-
The" Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021" has been approved by the Legislature and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is published in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, D. It was released on March 15, 2021.
The Budget provision for the financial year 2021-2022 has been prescribed by the Circular regarding the disbursement of funds through Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS) in the range of 5-10% of the total annual provision for the month of April.
During the financial year 2021-2022, to meet the cost of installation of Ground Water Survey and Development System, under the head" Demand No.Y-04, 2702 Minor Irrigation, (02) Ground Water, (005) Exploration, (01) Research and Development Establishment of Assets, (01) (01) Research and Development Establishment of Ground Water Assets (2702 3918) (Mandatory)," an amount of Rs. 52,47,65, 000/- has been provided. As per the circular of the Finance Department, under the objectives for which the grant has been released under the said head, it was under consideration to make available the budgetary grant to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune to meet the expenditure for the month of April in the current financial year.
