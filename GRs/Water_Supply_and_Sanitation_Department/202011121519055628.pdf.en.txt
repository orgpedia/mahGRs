Preamble: The Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme is being implemented in the State from 2016-17 to 2019-20 as per the decision of the Government dated 7th May, 2016 at No.1 under the purview of the Department with the objective of providing pure and adequate drinking water to the rural population of the State.
Since the period ended on 31st March, 2020, the reference No. The Government has decided to extend the existing scheme for two years (upto 2021-22) to complete the works on the progress/ incomplete water supply schemes sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme. Now, under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme, the government was considering extending the maintenance of regional water supply schemes for two years (up to 2021-22).
Government's decision:
In view of the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the Maintenance of Regional Water Supply Schemes under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme is amended (Head of Accounts No. 22159962) for a period of two years (upto 2021-22).
