The expenditure on water scarcity measures in rural and urban areas of the State is met from the grants available under the head" Assistance for Natural Disaster Mitigation" (2245). For the measures taken/ to be taken during the period of scarcity during 2017-18 and earlier, Reference No. The Department has received a proposal for seeking funds under letter No. 5-9. For the financial year 2018-19, the Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation) has sanctioned Rs. 15000.00 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Crores only) has been transferred to this Department. The funds were to be distributed to the divisional commissioners of Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad and Nagpur.
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 13333.60 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Thirty Three Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) is being sanctioned and released to the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad and Nagpur under the head of Accounts 22450093 for meeting the expenditure on the measures taken/ to be taken for the drinking water scarcity in Pune and Aurangabad divisions as per the norms prescribed by the Government for the purpose of mitigation of drinking water scarcity in rural/ urban areas subject to the following conditions.
The Divisional Commissioners of Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad and Nagpur shall ensure that the funds under the scarcity measures in rural areas are distributed among the respective Chief Executive Officers, Zilla Parishads and the funds under the scarcity measures in urban areas are distributed among the respective Commissioners, Municipal Corporations/ Principalities and Municipal Councils.
