Government Decision: In view of the facts mentioned in the foregoing, the Hon. Anand (Dy. return, G.
Jalna) by approving the technical changes in the work of the tap water supply scheme, Rs. 42.03 lakh (Rs. Forty Two Lakhs Three Thousand only) is being provided revised administrative approval under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme subject to the following conditions.
1) The scheme will be implemented through the District Council.
2) The terms and conditions of the original administrative approval order and the technical approval order of the scheme should be strictly followed.
3) Guidelines issued from time to time under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme should be strictly followed.
The CAG report in 2013 showed that 21% of the water supply schemes in the state were closed due to non-payment of electricity dues due to lack of maintenance. Therefore, the water tariff should be increased from time to time as per the requirement to keep the scheme financially viable.
5) After testing the scheme, the accounts should be closed by transferring the scheme to the concerned Gram Panchayat.
6) The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad will not get any assistance/ grant from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation of the scheme, its maintenance, repairs (subject to special amendments), depreciation in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
7) The district council will be responsible for ensuring that the scheme is not rescheduled.
The remaining works of the project should be completed within the stipulated time.
2. The revised cost of the scheme is Rs. 5.48 lakhs (Rupees Five Lakhs Forty Eight Thousand only).
