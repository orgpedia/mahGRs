INTRODUCTION: Under the Government decision, instructions have been issued regarding the appointment of Swachchagrahis, the objectives, duties and responsibilities of the appointment, the amount of incentive, eligibility, selection and other ancillary matters to meet the remaining objectives of sustainable use of sanitation facilities and cleanliness in Gram Panchayats. The decision was taken in the year 2018-19. The date of appointment of Swachchagrahi has been extended till 31st December, 2019 as per the decision of the Government vide Read No. 2.
The matter of further extension of appointment of these Swachhagrahis till 31st March, 2020 was under consideration of the Government.
