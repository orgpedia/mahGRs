As per the Government Resolution dated 7th September, 2018 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts, which are included in their rolls and which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls, from 1st October, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.
Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 10 temporary posts in the Improvement Assistance and Project Management Cell of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalaswarajya Project. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
The government is committed to provide clean and safe drinking water to the people of the state. Jalswarajya Phase-II is being implemented with World Bank funding through the Improvement Support and Management Cell under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. For this work to be carried out properly, 10 temporary posts are required for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarojya Project.
Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 7th September, 2018 of the Finance Department, No.4 of the above said Readings, 10 temporary posts for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell at the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) as shown in Form-A attached to this Government Resolution and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarajya Project shown in Form-B.
The posts are being allowed to continue from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
Expenditure on 10 temporary posts for Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself), under the head Demand No.Y-5, Accounts Head 3451-Secretariat Financial Services-090-Secretariat (1) Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (3451 0467), shall be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2018-2019. The expenditure on salary and allowances for 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarajya Project will be borne by State Bank of India for Jal Swarajya Programme.
