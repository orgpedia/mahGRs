5) 100% household tap connections and meters should be included in the scheme. 6) Even if the village refuses to take water despite the Gram Sabha resolution/ guarantee letter, at least 50% of the water table should be recovered from him. In this regard, the villagers should be given an idea before the tender process and the Gram Sabha should take a resolution in this regard.

7) The existing schemes should not be operated in the villages covered under the scheme so that the scheme is not used seasonally.

13) If the cost of the scheme increases for any reason, no additional subsidy will be provided by the government.
