Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 and Government Notification No. 2. As per the provisions of this Government Resolution, vide Government Circular No. 3, the provisional common seniority of the officers/ employees of the Group-B cadre of the Ground Water Survey and Development System was published from 1st January, 2009 to 1st January, 2013.
Also, the final seniority of the officers/ employees of the Branch Engineer, Group-B cadre from January 1, 2009 to January 1, 2015 was published in the Government Circular No. 4 under reference. The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is hereby informed that this circular should be brought to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice and working in your department, working in the subordinate office of the agency, retired from service and on deputation from the establishment of the agency or who have changed their cadre in any other way.
