(1) Rs. 15 lakh for works up to Rs. 15.00 lakh will include works of separate water supply schemes for villages, household tap connections (FHTC) as well as corrective re-connections.
(ii) Under Jal Jeevan Mission, only the schemes included in the Annual Action Plan approved by the State Level Plan Sanctioning Committee will be implemented through Jal Jeevan Mission.
(iii) District Water and Sanitation Mission Level Committee headed by the District Collector. It will provide administrative approval to schemes up to a limit of Rs. 5.00 crore. Orders for administrative approval should be issued at the level of Joint Chairman, District Water and Sanitation Mission and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad.
(iv) Water supply schemes under Jal Jeevan Mission should be sent for administrative approval only after technical examination and approval of the competent authority.
A copy of the Detailed Project Report (DPR), budget and technical sanction order of the schemes should be submitted along with the proposal for administrative approval.
(v) Rs. Proposals for schemes costing more than Rs. 5.00 crore will be sent from the Regional Offices to the State Water and Sanitation Missions.
