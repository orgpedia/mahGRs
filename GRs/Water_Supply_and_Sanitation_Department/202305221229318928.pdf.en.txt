Preamble: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 17.11.2022 has taken a policy decision to provide grant-in-aid to the Zilla Parishad for payment of wages to eligible employees working on regular temporary (CRT) establishments under the Conversion of Rural Water Supply Schemes under Zilla Parishad and accordingly the Government has issued a decision on 30.01.2023. Instructions have been issued under the Government Decision dated 30th January, 2023 to the concerned District Councils to demand the necessary funds from the Government from the 1st of the next month after taking into account the exact monthly salary of the existing employees. Accordingly, the Government is sanctioning grant-in-aid to the Zila Parishad for the month of February, 2023 for the financial year 2022-23 for the salary of the employees working under the Zila Parishad.
