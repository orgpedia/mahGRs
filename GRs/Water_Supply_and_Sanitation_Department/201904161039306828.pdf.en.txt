The Proposal:
The Interim Budget for 2019-20 has been presented to the Legislative Assembly and the Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 to meet the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 for the period up to the passing of the Appropriation Bill. The Bill has been enacted and published as Maharashtra Act No. 2 of the Government of Maharashtra, Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 8th March, 2019. Out of the budgetary provision for the year 2019-20, funds for the four months April, 2019 to July, 2019 have been released by the Department of Finance through a circular.

A total budget provision of Rs. 11,39,26, 000/- has been made for the financial year 2019-20 under the head Employees (Compulsory) (22151914) for 36, Rs. 11,30,76, 000/- for Subsidiary Grants (Salary) and Rs. 8,50,000/- for 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
Now, the Finance Department vide the aforesaid circular has made available on the budgetary system a wage subsidy of Rs. 3,21,41, 000/- out of the said provision for the year 2019-20. Considering the grants disbursed to the respective Zila Parishads during 2018-19, the matter of disbursement of wage subsidy for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
To meet the salary and non-salary expenses of the daily wage employees working on the regular establishment of the Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad during the year 2019-20.

The said funds are being disbursed subject to the deposit of the unutilized funds of 2018-19 to the Government under the aforesaid heads and the said funds shall not be drawn from the Treasury unless the unutilized funds are deposited to the Government.
The grants sanctioned under this Government decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts disbursed. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiary through ECS.
Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
4. Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said decision is submitted to the government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
Expenditure from the grants sanctioned by this Government under the head Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (01) (07) Daily Wage Employees of Regular Establishments (Mandatory) (22151914) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20.
