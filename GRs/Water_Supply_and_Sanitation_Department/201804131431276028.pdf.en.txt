The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. The said Bill, having been assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra, has been published in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV dated 27th March, 2018 as Maharashtra Act No. 22 of 2018. Further, the Budgetary allocation of mandatory expenditure required for the first nine months of the year 2018-2019 (ending December, 2018) has been made by the Department of Finance through a circular on Budgetary System with a ceiling of 70% of the total annual allocation.

Government's decision:
For the financial year 2018-2019, a total grant of Rs. 35,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Crore Thirty Lakh only) as shown in column 3 of the Statement A accompanying this Government Resolution under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions (District Level) (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961- (Local Area) (05) Regular Establishment (22151881)" has been sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in column 3 of the return A accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
