Introduction: Mauje Shittur Taraf Malkapur (Ta. Shahuwadi, Dist. Kolhapur), Gram Panchayat, Shittur Tarf Malkapur (Dist. Shahuwadi, G. Kolhapur) has provided the basic administrative approval under Gram Sabha Resolution. As per reference number 2, the Superintending Engineer, M.G.P.N. and Gr. The Board of Governors, Sangli has accorded original technical approval to the Budget. However, the cost of the project has increased due to the high cost of the project. Post facto approval has been granted under Reference No. 3 of the tender for the work of this scheme. The Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has given revised technical approval to the budget of the scheme presented under reference number 4. Accordingly, the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, had submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference Letter No. 5 to give revised administrative approval to the design and budget of the present tap water supply scheme.

Accordingly, the matter of granting revised administrative approval to the plan and budget of the present scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Mauje Shittur Taraf Malkapur (Ta. Shahuwadi, G. Kolhapur) The revised administrative sanction of Rs. 176.47 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Seventy Six Lakh Forty Seven Thousand only) is being granted subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (subject to special amendments) of the scheme, depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
