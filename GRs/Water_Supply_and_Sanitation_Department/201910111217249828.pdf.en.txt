The existing 10 temporary posts and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarajya Project under the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2014 at No.2 in the said Vacha have been approved to be continued from 01st March, 2019 to 30th September, 2019 as per the Government Decision dated 26th March, 2019 at No.4 in the said Vacha.
As per the Government Resolution dated 11th September, 2019 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts included in their rolls and created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls from 01st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020. Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 10 temporary posts in the Improvement Assistance and Project Management Cell of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank-funded Jalaswarajya Project. The government is taking the following steps:
The government has decided to provide clean and adequate drinking water to the people of the state. Jalswarajya Phase-II is being implemented with World Bank funding through the Improvement Support and Management Cell under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation. For this work to be carried out properly, 10 temporary posts are required for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jal Swarojya Project.
Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 11th September, 2019 of the Department of Finance, read with the above, the Government is approving the continuation of 10 temporary posts for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell on the establishment of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (itself) shown in Form A attached to this Government Resolution and 2 temporary posts created under the World Bank funded Jalaswarajya Project shown in Form B from 01st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020.
