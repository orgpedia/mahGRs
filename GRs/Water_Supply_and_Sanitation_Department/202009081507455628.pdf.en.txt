The Proposal:
Outline of posts in Ground Water Survey and Development System under Water Supply and Sanitation Department. With the approval of the Cabinet and the High-Level Committee, the government decision at reference number 3 has been fixed. The Finance Department has issued instructions to abolish 25 per cent of the sanctioned posts in Group D cadre as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2. Accordingly, the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter No. 1 under reference to abolish 62 posts i.e. 25% of the sanctioned posts in Group-D cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System. As per the suggestions given by the Finance Department under the Government Resolution No. 2 and the proposal submitted by the Directorate, the matter of abolition of 25 percent of the sanctioned number of posts in Group D cadre on the regular establishment of Ground Water Survey and Development System was under the consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: Apna-0220/ No.24/ Papu-15
Government's decision:
In view of the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Shivajinagar, Pune has proposed that 25% of the sanctioned posts (62 posts) in Group-D cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development Agency are being abolished as indicated in the Statement A and B of the Government decision.
If more Group-D employees are working in the system than the remaining posts due to the abolition of 62 posts in Group-D cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, they should be accommodated in the vacancies in other offices within the Directorate. Also, the concerned appointing authority should ensure that the posts abolished in Group-D are not filled further.
3. The said Government Decision is being issued by the Department of Finance after taking into consideration the suggestions given under the Government Decision at Reference No. 2.
