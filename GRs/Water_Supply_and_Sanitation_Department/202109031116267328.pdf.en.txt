The Proposal:
Government to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of officers and employees of Maharashtra Life Authority and ETP charges to be deposited with Maharashtra Life Authority.
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to deposit the Technical Fee with the Government and accordingly the Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017 has been issued. 22150041 as grant to the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.

A total of Rs. 47.3829 crore has been disbursed under the head Accounts for the financial year 2021-22 as per the decisions of the Government vide Grants-in-Aid 4, 5 and 8. Further disbursement of the grant is under the consideration of the Government.
Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran has made a total demand of Rs. 12.3648 crore for accrued leave encashment under letter dated 25.8.2021. Till last year, pensioners were being paid leave accrued from the grant of pension by the Depositories. Now, based on the circular dated 16.1.2008 of the Department of Finance, the Authority proposes to pay the accrued leave encashment allowance from the subsidy and has sought an amount of Rs. 12.3648 crore. The matter of disbursement of salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 12,36,48, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Sixty Six Lakhs Forty Eight Thousand only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority as grant under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salary and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (2215A041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for encashment of earned leave of retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, subject to the following conditions.
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for encashment of accrued leave of retired officers/ employees on regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The technical fees and ETP charges collected by the Authority at the end of August, 2021 for the year 2021-22 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
