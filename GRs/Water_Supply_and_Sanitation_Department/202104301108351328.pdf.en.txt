The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been approved by the Legislature and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is published as Maharashtra Act No. 5 of 2021 in the Government of Maharashtra, Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, dated 15th March, 2021. The Department of Finance has released 10% of the total annual budgetary provision as a temporary measure for mandatory expenditure for the financial year 2021-22 through Budget Control System.
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Life Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Life Authority with the Government and accordingly a Government Resolution dated 23.03.2017 has been issued. 22150041 as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1421/ P.No.70/ Papu-23
A grant of Rs. 19.00 crore for the financial year 2021-22 has been made available by the Department of Finance on the Budgetary System vide Government Circular dated 6.4.2021. The matter of disbursement of salary subsidy is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 19,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty-nine Crores only) is being released to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the head" Demand No.Y.2215, Water Supply and Sanitation, 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) Establishment (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants in lieu of Salaries and Allowances to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, (Mandatory/ Dattamat) (22150041), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)" as grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the salaries and allowances of the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority subject to the following conditions.
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Decision shall be used for the salary/ allowances of the employees of the regular establishment of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the year 2021-22. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The technical fees and ETP charges collected by the Authority at the end of April, 2021 for the year 2021-22 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Patil has been posted as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. R. M. Gengaje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been posted as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
