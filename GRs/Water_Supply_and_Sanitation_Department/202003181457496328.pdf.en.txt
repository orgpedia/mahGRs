The Proposal:
The provident fund of all the employees/ officers working with Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran since 1982-83 is deposited with the Government under the head of accounts-8336 civil deposits in the form of the amount received from the Authority as a contribution/ statutory deposit.

The interest on the total amount deposited with the government at the end of March is paid at the interest rate of the provident fund in the prevailing manner under the head of accounts 2049 1268.
The Government has a total of Rs. 287,22,89, 699/-. A proposal is under consideration of the government to adjust the interest amount by book adjustment at the prevailing rate of interest on the provident fund.
Government's decision:
For the six months from October, 2019 to March, 2020 of the financial year 2019-20, the demand number Y-1, 2049, interest payments (01), interest on the provident fund of the Maharashtra Life Authority employees (mandatory) 45, interest (2049 1268) for meeting the interest expenses on the provident fund of the employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority, the authority has to pay Rs. 11,34,55, 444/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Thirty Four Lakh Fifty Five Thousand Four Hundred Forty Four only) is being released under this Government decision.
The expenditure from the provision sanctioned under this Government decision shall be adjusted by way of book adjustment and the expenditure shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20 by showing the expenditure under the head Demand No.Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Employees (Mandatory) 45, Interest (2049 1268) for payment of interest on provident fund of employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
The funds sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be deposited by book adjustment under the head" 8336-Civil Deposits, 800 Other Deposits, (00) (08) Maharashtra Water Supply and Sewerage Board Employees Provident Fund (8336 5067)."
This Government decision is based on the Government Circular No.Finance-2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3 of the Finance Department, dated 1st April, 2019 and Government Circular No.Finance-2019/ PR.
