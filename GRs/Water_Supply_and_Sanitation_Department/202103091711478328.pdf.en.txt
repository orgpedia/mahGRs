The Proposal:
The Government has, vide Government Resolution dated 23.3.2017, taken over the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and sanctioned grant-in-aid to meet the expenditure on pension of retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.

Government Resolution No: Budget 1420/ PR No.94/ Papu-23
Therefore, a sum of Rs. 188,17,17, 750/- has been released to the Authority for the financial year 2020-21 under the head 2215A052 under the Government Resolution No. Now the matter of disbursement of further funds made available by the Finance Department under the above head of accounts is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the financial year 2020-21, a grant of Rs. 62,72,39,250/- (Rupees Sixty Two Crores Seventy Two Lakhs Seventy Nine Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) is being released to the Authority under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 199, Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations (01) Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations (01) (01) Assistance to Government Employees transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Authority (3115 A052) 31, Assistance Grants (Compulsory) for sanction of pension benefits."
(i) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall ensure that the grant disbursed under this Government decision is sanctioned for the pension benefits of retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and the amount from this grant is spent for the same purpose.
(ii) This fund should not be used for any outstanding dues as on 30.03.2017. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the officers/ employees concerned will be liable for disciplinary action for such action.
(iii) Separate accounts should be maintained of the amount of free subsidy.
(iv) The Authority shall inform the Government from time to time about the allocations made under the free grants.
