In his letter dated 10.04.2018, the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati had submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for the special repair of the tap scheme in Mehkar City, District Buldana at a cost of Rs. 244.32 lakhs under the Water Scarcity Programme for the scarcity period of 2017-18. The proposal was considered by the Secretary Level Standing Committee in its meeting held on 11th April, 2018.
The tap water supply scheme of Mehkar town dates back to 1999 and is frequently damaged due to the failure of the pump and reduced discharge from the pump. Since the CI pipe is worn out and needs special repair, the shortage under the scheme is 1) Raw Water Pumping Machinery and Transformer Sub Station at Head Work Site. 2) Replacement of 340 mm dia, AC pipe with 350 mm, D.I.K., K-7 Pipes 2100 m3) Pure Water Pumping Machine

Government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati, has decided to implement the water scarcity tap scheme in Mehkar City, District Buldana with special remedial measures, involving an outlay of Rs. The administrative approval is being granted to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 1,95,49, 850/- (Rupees One Crore, Ninety Five Lakhs, Ninety Five Thousand, Eight Hundred and Fifty only) subject to the following conditions.
1) The District Collector, Buldana should first declare that there is a shortage of drinking water in Mehkar town.
(2) The plan shall be implemented by the City Council.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner. Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by April 27, 2018. If the tender process is not completed by this date, the approval of the scheme will be considered canceled.
6) The project should be completed within the stipulated time.
The government will provide a subsidy of Rs 1,75,94, 865/- i.e. 90% of the total cost of Rs 1,95,49, 850/- of the scheme. The remaining amount i.e. 10% of the scheme (being Mehkar' B' Municipal Council) is to be paid by the Municipal Council, Mehkar as a contribution of Rs. 19,54,985/-.
The work will begin after the project's funds are allocated.
