The Proposal:
As per the letter dated 18/11/2020 of the Central Government at Reference No. 2 (Annexure A),
In line with the effective implementation of the GobarDhan program, for the implementation of pilot projects and GobarDhan projects of 2020-21 in the state, Mo. The Chief Secretary, Maharashtra has directed to constitute a committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Maharashtra, consisting of officials from various departments of the state with representation in the matter of Gobardhan. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a state-level committee for the implementation of Gobardhan projects.
Government's decision:
In view of the above directions of the Central Government, in pursuance of the pilot projects and effective implementation of Gobardhan projects for the year 2020-21 in the State, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to implement the above schemes. The Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra as follows:
