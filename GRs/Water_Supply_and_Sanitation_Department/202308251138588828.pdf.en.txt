the prelude
As Sunil Mahajan, then Assistant Administrative Officer (Group-B) Ground Water Survey and Development System, had applied for the post of Chief Administrative Officer (Group-A) Maharashtra Medical Education and Research Service through prescribed route, as per the request of Shri Sunil Mahajan, he has been relieved from his duties under the provisions of paragraph 2 (b) (1) of the General Administration Department 02.12.1997 vide Government Order dated 1.06.2023 to join as Chief Administrative Officer (Group-A) Government Medical College, Satara in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical Education and Research Service. Subject to the provisions of Rule 22 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981, Shri Sunil Mahajan has been relieved of his duties by retaining his original designation as Assistant Administrative Officer (Group-B) in the Ground Water Survey and Development System.
Sunil Hilal Mahajan vide his application dated 11.07.2023 has requested to be reinstated to the post of Assistant Administrative Officer (Group-B) in the Ground Water Survey Development System, which was his original lien due to his family problems.

The matter of re-engagement in the post of Ground Water Survey Development Agency (Group-B) was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
Shri Sunil Hilal Mahajan, Chief Administrative Officer, Government Medical Education and Research Service (Group-A) is being appointed as Assistant Administrative Officer (Group-B) in the Ground Water Survey Development System in his parent cadre subject to the conditions of his original appointment/ promotion order. Sunil Hilal Mahajan has been posted as Assistant Administrative Officer, Regional Deputy Director Office, Ground Water Survey Development Agency, Nashik. Mr. Mahajan should be appointed to the post immediately.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune shall ensure that the officers shall join the place of posting as per this order immediately and submit a report to the Government.
