The proposal-
During the financial year 2021-2022, a total amount of Rs. 31,61,40, 000/- has been provided under the head" Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, (00) (101) Soil Survey and Testing, (01) (01) Minor Irrigation Schemes (Compulsory) under One Ground Water Survey and Development Agency" to meet the cost of installation of Ground Water Survey and Development System. Out of this, a grant of Rs. 6,97,55, 550/-is sanctioned as per the provisions of the reference No. The budget estimates under 1, 2 and 3 have been distributed to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System on Distribution and Solar System. The matter of providing grant to the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune to meet the expenditure for the next period under the head of (01) Salary and (02) Wages was under consideration.
