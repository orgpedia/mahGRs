The Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.03.2017 has taken a policy decision to take over the responsibility of salary, allowances and pension of the officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and to deposit the ETP charges and technical fees collected from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority with the Government. The Government is considering to disburse grant to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the financial year 2023-24 under the head 2215 A041 as grant in respect of payment of salary and allowances to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.

An amount of Rs. 45,63,57, 000/- (Rupees Forty Five Crores Thirty Six Lakhs Fifty Seven Thousand only) is being released under this Government Order subject to the following conditions:
(a) The funds to be disbursed under this Government Resolution shall be utilised for the payment of approved and admissible salary/ allowances to the employees of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the year 2023-24. This fund should not be used for any past dues of salary/ allowances. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
b) Technical charges and ETP charges deposited with the Authority at the end of June, 2023 should be deposited with the Government immediately.
Rajendra Madke has been appointed as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Mr. Ram Gangje, Deputy Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been appointed as the Controlling Officer.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
