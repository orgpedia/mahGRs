Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparing and publishing seniority list of government officials/ employees every year. The final seniority as on 01/01/2001 of the cadre of" Assistant Geophysicist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System has been notified vide Government Circular No. 2. The General Administration Department, as per the instructions issued under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference, has included the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Assistant Geophysicist for the years 2001 to 2020 and prepared the consolidated common provisional seniority of the officers of the cadre of Senior Geophysicist in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2006 and 01/01/2011 and the provisional common seniority as on 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2021. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is hereby informed that this circular should be brought to the notice of all the officers included in the accompanying seniority notice and working in your department, working in the subordinate office of the agency, retired from service and on deputation from the establishment of the agency or who have changed cadre by any other means. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government by 28th July 2021 along with the feedback of the Directorate after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority list by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining seniority in the cadre of Assistant Geophysicist only as per Government Notification, General Administration Department, No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
