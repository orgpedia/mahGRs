In the financial year 2017-18, the interest on the Employees' Provident Fund of the Maharashtra Life Authority was Rs. 9,92,28, 103/- (Rupees Nine Crores Ninety Two Lakhs Eighty Eight Thousand One Hundred and Three only) has been released under the Government decision dated 06.09.2020. Government's decision:
For the financial year 2017-18, to meet the cost of interest on the provident fund of the employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority, Demand No. Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) (01) Interest on the Provident Fund of the Maharashtra Life Authority Employees (2049 1268) Under the head 45, Interest, Rs. 8,45,71, 897/- (Rupees Eight Crores Forty Five Lakhs Seventy One Thousand Eight Hundred and Ninety Seven only) of interest is being disbursed under this Government decision.
The expenditure from the provision sanctioned under this Government decision shall be adjusted by book adjustment and the expenditure shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2017-18 by showing the expenditure under the head Demand No.Y-1, 2049, Interest Payments (01) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority Employees (2049 1268) Interest on Provident Fund of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority Employees (45, Interest).
