The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparing and publishing seniority list of government officials/ employees every year. The Common Final Seniority of Senior Digging Engineer (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been notified vide Government Circular No.2 and Provisional Common Seniority from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 vide Government Circular No.3. As per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the names of the officers included in the selection list of 2018, 2019 and 2020 in the Senior Excavation Engineer (Group-A) cadre have been included in the provisional common seniority list showing the status of Senior Excavation Engineer (Group-A) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2019, 01/01/2020 and 01/01/2021. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this letter.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to the post of Senior Excavation Engineer, Group-A, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, will also be considered.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the concerned officers who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in the agency, retired from the agency, on deputation from the establishment of the agency or have changed the cadre in any other way.
If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted to the government before July 21, 2021, along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service book and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority list by verifying them. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority indicated against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining the seniority of the Senior Excavation Engineer cadre only as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21/06/1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
