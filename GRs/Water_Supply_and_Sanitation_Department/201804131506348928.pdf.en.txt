GOVERNMENT DECISIONS, WATER SUPPLY AND SANITAL DEPARTMENT, NO. EST-1097/ PR No. 370/ PAPU-23, dated 8.02.1999
Preamble: The Maharashtra Life Authority is empowered to make regulations with the approval of the Government regarding the recruitment of officers/ employees of the Authority as per the provisions of Section 66 of the Maharashtra Life Authority Act, 1976. However, the Authority has not framed separate Service Entry Rules for all the posts in the Authority and the Service Entry Rules framed by the Water Resources Department for the Engineering Cadre in that Department have been made applicable to the Authority under the decision of the Government in the context.
The government was considering setting up a committee to draft the service entry rules for the engineering cadre in the Maharashtra Life Authority in line with the authority's separate service entry rules. The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
Separate Service Entry Rules for Engineers in Maharashtra Life Authority
The following committee is being constituted to draft the Service Access Rules.
