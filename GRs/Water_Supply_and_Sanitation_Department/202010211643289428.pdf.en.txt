The Government has decided to restructure the Centrally Sponsored National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) by subsuming it with Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) from 2009-10. Accordingly, the State Cabinet in its meeting held on 08/07/2020 has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Jal Jeevan Mission in the State as per the decision of the Government in No. 1.
The State Water and Sanitation Mission has been entrusted with the responsibility of planning, implementation and monitoring of Jal Jeevan Mission and sanitation related programmes in the State.
Since 2019, the Union Government has restructured the National Rural Drinking Water Programme and launched Jal Jeevan Mission. However, as there is no provision for" Bharat Nirman Cell" under Jal Jeevan Mission, the Divisional Bharat Nirman Cell has been closed down vide Government Order dated 30/09/2020.
Mission Director, State Water and Sanitation Mission has brought to the notice of the Government that due to the closure of Bharat Nirman Cell, there is a problem in the control of rural water supply program, technical scrutiny of water supply schemes and there is a hindrance in the collection of information by coordinating information at the district level.

The request for nomination of officers to the office has been made to the Government vide letter No. 2.
Government order:
Considering the request made by Mission Director, State Water and Sanitation Mission, to achieve the objective of providing pure, adequate and sustainable water supply to rural households at all times and convenient places under Jal Jeevan Mission, Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being appointed as" Controlling Officer" at Divisional level to control the water supply schemes and related matters being implemented by Zilla Parishad and Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under Jal Jeevan Mission.
Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran to review, monitor and act as a link between Zilla Parishads in the division and Jal Jeevan Mission of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and State Government and field offices.
The concerned officers should take care of the responsibility of Jal Jeevan Mission by taking care of their own work. No additional pay or allowance shall be admissible to these officers for the duties assigned to them as Controlling Officers.
