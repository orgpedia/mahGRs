GOVERNMENT NO.: RAGRAPE-2019/ PR No.99/ PAPU-10
Decision of the Government of India regarding selection of Banking Partner of the Department for National Rural Drinking Water Programme/ Jal Jeevan Mission for Public Finance Management System of the Central Government. Meeting of the Committee appointed under the Government Decision dated 7.10.2019. It was held on 9.10.2019. As per the presentations made by the concerned banks during the meeting, the Committee has selected ICICI Bank as the Banking Partner for opening of State and District level accounts under National Rural Drinking Water Programme/ Jal Jeevan Mission in the State.
As per the decision of the Committee, approval is being accorded for selection of ICICI Bank as Banking Partner for National Rural Drinking Water Programme/ Jal Jeevan Mission for PFMS. ICICI Bank will be responsible for the implementation of PFMS and all related aspects under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme/ Jal Jeevan Mission. Banks will have to strictly follow the guidelines of the Public Financial Management System (PFMS) of the Central Government and the Security Advisory of the Central Government in this regard. All terms and conditions will be updated from time to time.
