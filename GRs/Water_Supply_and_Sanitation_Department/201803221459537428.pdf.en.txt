Preamble: With a view to increase the spontaneous participation of the rural people in the rural sanitation program, the Sant Gadgebaba Gram Swachhta Abhiyan is being implemented in the rural areas of the state. Revised orders have been issued under Sections 1 and 2 of the Act. Under this campaign, awards and special awards are given to eligible Gram Panchayats at Panchayat Samiti level, Zila Parishad level, Divisional level and State level. Under this campaign, eligible Panchayat Samitis and eligible Zila Parishads are also given awards.
