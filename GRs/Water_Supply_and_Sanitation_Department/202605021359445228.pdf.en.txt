Solid Waste Management Rules, 2026.
The Proposal: -
The Solid Waste Management Rules, 2026 have been issued by the Department of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India vide Notification dated 27th January, 2026 and come into force with effect from 01st April, 2026. The main objectives of these rules are scientific management of waste in urban and rural areas, waste classification at source, recycling, and minimization of missile-borne waste. The rules entrust various responsibilities to the state government's Water Supply and Sanitation Department regarding solid waste management in rural areas, including formulating and ensuring implementation of a state-level solid waste management policy, planning waste collection and transportation process disposal, and emphasizing the principles of Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Recovery. The responsibilities assigned to the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation by this rule include the formation of a Steering Committee to promote a circular economy.
The Government's Decisions:
For effective implementation of Solid Waste Management Rules, 2026, Government is approving setting up of Steering Committee as under for effective, transparent and sustainable solid waste management system in rural areas as directed in this rule.

The mandate of the committee is as follows: To establish an effective, transparent and sustainable solid waste management system in rural areas and to promote a circular economy.
To take action in line with the implementation in rural areas of the instructions given by the State Level Committee under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary for effective implementation of Solid Waste Management Rules, 2026.
To encourage and guide the participation of Serb stakeholders in promoting a circular economy.
The report is based on a per-capita review of the work being done in rural local bodies with regard to various aspects relating to setting up of effective, transparent and sustainable solid waste management systems in rural areas and circular economy. Submission to the Chief Secretary.
