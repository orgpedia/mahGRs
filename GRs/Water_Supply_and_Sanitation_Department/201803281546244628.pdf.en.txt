Conditions
After testing the scheme, the scheme should be transferred to the concerned Gram Panchayat and its accounts should be closed.
As per the CAG report of 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills due to lack of maintenance, therefore, the water tariff should be increased from time to time to make the scheme financially viable.
It shall be the responsibility of the District Council to ensure that the present scheme is not revised under any circumstances.
4. The terms and conditions mentioned in the original administrative approval as well as in the revised approval of the scheme should be strictly followed.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
