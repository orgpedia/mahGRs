Preamble: With a view to improve the rural water supply sector in the State and to provide clean and potable drinking water to the rural population, vide Reference No. 1 here. The decision to implement the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme has been taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated May 7, 2016. Under this programme, 83 defunct regional water supply schemes are proposed to be revived. If a program is canceled for any reason, you have the right to receive other programs. The Committee is headed by the Minister (Water Supply and Sanitation).
According to The Committee is headed by the Minister for Water Supply and Sanitation. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on December 18, 2018, the Government was considering the inclusion of 5 new schemes in the regional schemes to be revived under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme.
Government's decision:
Considering the facts stated in the Preamble, Shri. The Committee is headed by the Minister for Water Supply and Sanitation. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on December 18, 2018, the following 5 schemes are being included in the regional water supply schemes to be revived under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme.
