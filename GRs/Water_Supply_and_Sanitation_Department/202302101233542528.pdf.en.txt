(1) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Samitis, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, 36 for the Financial Year 2022-23 under the head' (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Compulsory) (2215 1881)', a total amount of Rs. 218.02 crore has been released towards expenditure on Auxiliary Grants (Salaries) and Rs. 5.20 crore towards expenditure on Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salary). Now, considering the total salary expenditure and demand of the Zila Parishads, the matter of disbursing salary funds to the concerned Zila Parishads as well as non-salary funds to the Zila Parishads fulfilling the prescribed terms and conditions is under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01), Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)," 36, Rs. 4,17,00, 000/-for Auxiliary Grants (Salary) and Rs. 81,75,200/-for Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 4,98,75, 200/- (Rupees Four Crore Eighty Nine Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Two Hundred Only)
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. The non-salaried fund should be utilised by the respective Zila Parishads for meeting the expenses like travel expenses, office expenses, lease tax, motor vehicles, electricity bills, petrol/ oil/ lubricants, machinery and equipment etc.
(3) Care should be taken to submit to the Government the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as to deposit the unutilized funds disbursed earlier. Also, the expenditure incurred is in addition to the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's Office.
Take breaks from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed. Payment of subsidy by the Government through ECS to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
