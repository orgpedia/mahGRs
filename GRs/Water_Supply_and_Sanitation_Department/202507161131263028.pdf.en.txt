Conditions
The Chief Executive Officer / Chief Engineer will be responsible for completing all the remaining works within the prescribed time frame at the above mentioned revised cost.

The concerned Gram Panchayat / Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of the intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zila Parishad that the scheme has actually been completed. Immediately after the actual completion of the scheme, a joint inspection of the scheme should be conducted by the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials and any defects found in such inspection should be corrected before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat / Zilla Parishad concerned shall not receive any assistance from the Government for meeting the recurring expenditure incurred annually for implementation, maintenance, repairs (including special modifications), depreciation in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
