The said funds are being released subject to the deposit of the unutilized funds of 2017-18 under the aforesaid heads to the Government and the said funds shall not be drawn from the Treasury unless the unutilized funds are deposited with the Government.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts disbursed. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiary through ECS.
Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy be disbursed in a lump sum and offline manner.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received.
