Government Decision: Mauje Sukalwadi (Walhe) (Ta. Purandar (Pune) Rs. The revised budget of Rs. 52.43 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Two Lakhs Forty Three Thousand only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation from the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune that the scheme has been actually completed. As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Life Authority will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenses incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation in the scheme or for the renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
