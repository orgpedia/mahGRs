Government order:
Ms. Deepali Deshpande-Savdekar, Additional CEO, Maharashtra Life Authority under Water Supply and Sanitation Department, has been directed by the Department of Rural Development vide letter No.33, as per reference, to provide her services on deputation to the post of Chief Administrative Officer. Necessary action regarding their deputation has been taken by the Village Development Department as per the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1& 2. Accordingly, Smt. Deepali Deshpande-Savdekar, Additional CEO is being appointed as" Chief Administrative Officer" Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran on deputation for a period of three years initially subject to the following conditions:

Government Order No: Majipra-1120/ PR No. 95/ Papu-23
(1) Mrs. Dipali Deshpande-Savdekar's deputation shall commence from the date she assumes charge of her office and shall end on the date on which she resumes her official charge.
(2) The Government/ competent authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
3) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority.
Smt. Dipali Deshpande-Savdekar will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that she intends to go to the parent department.
5) There is an impression that the Rural Development Department has complied with the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 and 2 in the context of the appointment of Mrs. Dipali Deshpande-Savdekar. However, if at any stage it is found that there is a violation of the provisions of the said Government Order in the case of Ms. Deshpande-Savdekar, the Water Supply and Sanitation Department shall have the power to cancel the deputation of Ms. Deshpande Savdekar and send her back to the parent department.
As Smt. Dipali Deshpande-Savdekar is being deputed, the terms and conditions of her deputation are to be decided by the Maharashtra Life Authority and the Rural Development Department, taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Voluntary Service, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Dipali Deshpande-Savdekar will be handled by the Rural Development Department.
