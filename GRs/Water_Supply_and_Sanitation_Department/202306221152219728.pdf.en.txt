Government's decision:
For the financial year 2023-24, a total grant of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores only) is being sanctioned to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the concerned Zilla Parishad as shown in the Statement A accompanying this Government Decision under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01), Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)."
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. Also, the expenditure incurred is in addition to the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's Office.
Take breaks from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
4) Before drawing the funds from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
5. Since the grant-in-aid is for salary, it is" unconditional" in nature as per the Government decision dated 15.01.2023 of the Department of Finance.
