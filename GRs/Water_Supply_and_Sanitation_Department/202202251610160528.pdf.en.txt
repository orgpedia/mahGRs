Government's decision:

A grant of Rs. 1,37,50, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) is being sanctioned to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the concerned Zilla Parishad as indicated in the" Statement A" accompanying this Government Resolution.
2. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts indicated in this Government Resolution. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. Expenditure on other items of this fund will be treated as financial irregularity and the concerned officers/ employees will be liable for disciplinary action for such actions.
The concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business of the State Government, Financial Rules, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions before drawing and spending the disbursed funds from the Treasury.
Also in the finance department. Care should be taken to ensure that there is a cut in expenditure as per the instructions in the circular dated 24.6.2021.

4. Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government.
