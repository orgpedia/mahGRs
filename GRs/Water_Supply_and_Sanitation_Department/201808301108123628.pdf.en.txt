Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. The provisional seniority of the Deputy Director (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development Agency dated 01/01/2016 has been published vide Government Circular No. 2 hereunder. The General Administration Department, in accordance with the instructions given under the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, has included the names of the officers included in the selection list of 2016 and 2017 for the post of Deputy Director (Group-A) in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, and prepared a provisional common seniority chart showing the status of the Deputy Director (Group-A) cadre as on 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to the post of Deputy Director, Group-A, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, are also included in Annexure-B. After their regular promotion orders are issued, they will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the department's establishment. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the statements of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government before 30th September, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority information after verifying it.
Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be considered for determining seniority in the cadre of Deputy Director only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
