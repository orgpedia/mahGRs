The Proposal:
Action is being taken by the department as per the instructions given by the government regarding the use of e-office system. In this operation, the computerization of the archive has been done by scanning the' A' and' B' classes as well as the proceedings. Similarly, class C and other records are proposed to be scanned. The roadmap is designed to store the scanned archive in a computer, index it, upload the archive to a server or cloud, and use the stored documents scanned from the server or cloud through a scanning application. Accordingly, the Project Implementation Committee in its meeting dated 30.05.2023 has approved the procurement of Data Storage Servers under the Department from the budgetary provision for FY 2023-24. Administrative approval for the expenditure was under consideration of the government.
