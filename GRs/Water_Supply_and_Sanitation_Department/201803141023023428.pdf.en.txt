As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure-II to the Bombay Finance Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority should be submitted to the Accountant General by the Water and Sanitation Assistance Society for inspection along with relevant documents as per the demand of the Accountant General.
The Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency shall issue the utilization certificates of the grants received by it from the Government or the grants which it will receive in the future. The Director, Water and Sanitation Assistance Agency shall certify and sign the programme-wise amounts of grants and expenditure sanctioned and released as indicated in the Utilization Certificate. The utilization certificate of this subsidy should be submitted to the government in the prescribed format and copies of the utilization certificate should also be sent to the Accountant General.
The expenditure of this grant should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accounts Officer, Papu-20, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 001 on the prescribed date.
