The Proposal:
As per the letter dated 18/11/2020 of the Central Government (Addendum A), dated 18/11/2020, under reference No. 2, for effective implementation of Gobardhan programme, the State has been directed to constitute a District Level Committee comprising of officers of various departments of the district having representation in respect of Gobardhan under the chairmanship of the concerned Collector for implementation of pilot projects and Gobardhan projects in the district for the year 2020-21. Accordingly, a district-level committee comprising officials from various departments concerned under the chairmanship of the Collector was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the above directions of the Central Government, a District Level Committee under the Chairmanship of the District Collector is being constituted for effective implementation of pilot projects and Gobardhan projects in the districts of the State for the year 2020-21 as follows:

The District Collector and the Chairman of the Committee shall have the right to include other members in the said Committee as per requirement.
Committee's mandate:
1) Technical mechanism at district level for successful implementation of Gobardhan programme
Adoption of procedures for empanelment of technical agencies and approval of empanelled technical bodies.
2) Gobardhan project at the district level for successful implementation of Gobardhan programme
Designing, finalizing and approving projects.
Committee Duration:
The Committee will continue to function till 31/03/2021. The government has the power to extend the deadline if needed.
