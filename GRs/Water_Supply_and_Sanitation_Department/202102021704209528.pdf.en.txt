Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees of the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1 in the above context. The final seniority as on 01/01/2011 of the Administrative Officer (Group-A) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System has been published vide Circular No. 2 dated 01/01/2011. The Provisional Common Seniority as on 01/01/2018 has been created vide Government Circular No.3 in the above context. The General Administration Department has included the names of the officers who were included in the selection list for the post of Administration Officer (Group-A) from 2019 to 2021 as per the instructions given under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference. 01/01/2019, Dy. Provisional common seniority chart showing the status as on 01/01/2020 and 01/01/2021 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to Group-A posts, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, are also included in Annexure-B. After their regular promotion orders are issued, they will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and who have retired from the department working in their department and are on deputation from the establishment of the department. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate.
The facts in the statement of the officers should be submitted to the government before March 3, 2021, along with the opinion of the Director, after examining the service book and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority notice by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be considered for determining seniority in the cadre of Administrative Officers only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
