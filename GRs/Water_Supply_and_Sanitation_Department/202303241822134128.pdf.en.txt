Preamble: Expenditure on measures taken to alleviate water scarcity in rural areas of the State is met from the grants made available by this Department under the head (2215 A 195).
The Department has received a proposal from the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Mumbai for this period seeking funds for the measures actually completed during the shortage period of 2021-22. During the financial year 2022-23, the funds budgeted under the target of Account Head (2215A 195) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Plan) were under consideration of the Government to disburse funds for the measures demanded and actually completed by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Mumbai.
Government's decision:
As per the norms laid down by the Government for addressing the drinking water shortage in rural areas, the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran, Mumbai, has to bear the cost of the actual completed measures for addressing the drinking water shortage in his department (Head of Accounts 2215A 195).

Terms and Conditions
(1) The Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Authority shall disburse the funds for the measures to be taken by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the drinking water shortage to the concerned Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
The funds will be released for the implementation of the measures taken to mitigate the drinking water scarcity in rural areas for the period 2021-22 and for the payment of pending dues during this period. This money cannot be used for other projects.
The funds will be disbursed directly to the contractors through Electronic Clearing Services/ RTGS without maintaining a personal ledger account or bank account.
