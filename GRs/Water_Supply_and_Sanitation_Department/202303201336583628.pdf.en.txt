Preamble The General Administration Department has issued instructions under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 to create supernumerary posts and transfer their services to supernumerary posts for the officers/ employees who are appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or have not submitted the caste validity certificate of the Backward Classes. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran had submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 27.11.2020 for creation of additional posts in the office of the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for transfer of 98 employees to the post of Service Superintendents. Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 28.12.2020, 98 supernumerary posts have been created for the employees in the subordinate offices of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. 57 The name of Mr. J.R. Rahate is included herein and his name is to be omitted.
