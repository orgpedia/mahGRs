Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees of government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. The final seniority as on 01/01/2011 of Senior Chemical (Group-A) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System has been published vide Circular No. 2, dated 01/01/2011. The General Administration Department has included the names of the officers who were selected for the post of Senior Chemical (Group A) from the year 2011 to 2017 as per the instructions given under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference. 01/01/2012, Dy. 01/01/2013, Dy. 01/01/2014, Dy. 01/01/2015, Dy. 01/01/2016, Dy. Provisional common seniority chart showing the status as on 0/01/2017 and 01/01/2018 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.
The names of officers in the select list of promotion to the post of Senior Chemical, Group-A, whose regular promotion orders have not been issued, have also been included in Annexure-B. After their regular promotion orders are issued, they will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the department's establishment. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted by the Directorate to the Government before 29th September, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers so that it is possible to make necessary changes in the seniority notice after verifying it.
Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining the seniority in the senior chemical cadre only as per Government Notification S.P.V. No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21st June, 1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
