Preamble: The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal worth Rs. 49.42 lakhs to the Government for administrative approval of the temporary supplementary tap scheme in Mauje Rajur, Taluka Bhokardan, District Jalna under the scarcity measures for the period 2017-18. This proposal was considered for approval in the meeting held on 03.05.2018 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department for granting administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures in urban and rural areas beyond the prescribed financial and physical parameters to mitigate drinking water scarcity.
This proposal was discussed in the meeting of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department on 03.05.2018.

After the discussion, the proposal included in the budget was approved. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Considering the facts mentioned in the preamble of the Government decision, the proposal of temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme in Mauje Rajur, Taluka Bhokardan, District Jalna at a cost of Rs. 49.42 lakhs (Rupees Nineteen Fifty Five Lakhs, Forty Two Thousand only) is being given administrative approval under scarcity subject to the following conditions: 1. First of all, it should be declared that there is a shortage of drinking water in these villages.
2) All the works mentioned in this proposal should be implemented by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Jalna.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner. Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by May 20, 2018. If the tender is not completed by this date, the approval of the scheme will be considered as cancelled.
The water supply from the scheme should be started by completing the works of the scheme by June 6, 2018. However, if the water supply from the scheme does not start by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled
7) After the works mentioned in the proposal of the scheme are completed through the Zilla Parishad, Jalna, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done through the concerned Gram Panchayat.
8) The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. The remaining funds will be released after the completion of the project.
9) Since the work of the scheme must be completed in time, the progress report should be sent to the government every Monday of the week from the tender process of the scheme to the start of water supply from the scheme.
