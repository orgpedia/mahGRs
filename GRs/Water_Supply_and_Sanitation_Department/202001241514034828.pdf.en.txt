The Proposal:
The Government has taken the responsibility of the salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers and employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority under the Government Resolution No. 1. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution, the monthly income received from the ETP and technical service fee is deposited with the Government. As per the provisions of the same government decision, it has been decided to create a separate head of account at the department level to meet the expenses on regular salaries, allowances and pensions of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority and make provision for funds as required.
In order to make financial provision for providing subsidy to the Authority for giving retirement benefits to the retired officers/ employees of the Maharashtra Life Authority, a new account head has been created in the form of provisional account under the Government Resolution No. 2.

A proposal for Supplementary Demand was submitted to the Finance Department in the Winter Session of 2019 of the Legislature for providing funds to the Authority for payment of retirement salary benefits to retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017. Out of the requested funds, Rs. The Finance Department has agreed to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 200.00 crore to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran by sanctioning Supplementary Demands. An amount of Rs. Rs. 135.00 crore has been provided for payment of pensionary benefits. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 135.00 crore towards retirement benefits of retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017, the amount paid to the Authority towards retirement benefits of retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is Rs.
135,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Thirty Five Crores only) is being sanctioned and disbursed under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 22115-Water Supply and Sanitation, 199-Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations, 01-Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations, (01) (04) Government Grants for sanction of pension benefits to Government employees transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (22150052) (Compulsory/ Datta), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Pay)" subject to the following conditions:
Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran should ensure that the amount of grant is spent only for the purpose of retirement benefits of retired officers/ employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the period from 23/03/2017 to 30/11/2017.
(2) The expenditure of this fund on other matters shall be deemed to be financial irregularities and the officer/ employee concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action for such action.
3) Separate accounts will be maintained for the amount of subsidy disbursed.
The amount of grant-in-aid should be spent for the specified purpose for which it has been sanctioned and released, by 31st March, 2020.

5) The Maharashtra Life Authority should inform the government from time to time about the allocation from the distributed subsidy.
As per Rule No. 3 of Annexure 2 to the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, the accounts of the Authority shall be submitted to the Maharashtra Life Authority for inspection along with relevant documents.
7) The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority has to give the utilization certificates of the grants received from the government or the grants that will be received in the next period. The Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall certify and sign the amount of grant sanctioned and disbursed as well as the amount of expenditure shown in the Utilization Certificate. The utility certificates should be signed by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran. Only such utility certificates will be acceptable to the government.
8) The Government should submit the utilization certificate of this subsidy.
9) The cost of the subsidy disbursed should be reconciled with the cost reported to the Accountant General's Office and the prescribed report should be submitted to the Accountant General, Papu-20 on the prescribed date.
The Director (Finance), Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being declared as Controlling Officer for receiving the grants disbursed under this Government decision.
The pension subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiary. Under no circumstances should pension grants be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
The expenditure in this case shall be met from the financial provisions of the year 2019-20 by deducting expenditure under the head" Demand No. Y-2, 2215-Water Supply and Sanitation, 199 Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations, 01-Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations, (01) (04) Government Grant for sanction of pension benefits to Government employees transferred to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (2215A052) (Compulsory/ Datta), 31-Assistant Grant (Non-Salary)."
