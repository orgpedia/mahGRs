The Proposal:
The officers have been appointed as Junior Geologists in the Ground Water Survey and Development System for a period of two years as per the Government decisions at Reference No. 2, 3 and 4. These appointments have been made by the first appointment in the government service in the post of" Junior Geologist" as recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of the officers/ employees of the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Junior Geologist, Ground Water Survey and Development System have satisfactorily completed their probationary period of 2 years and the same has been recommended by the Directorate under Reference No. 3 and 4, the officers will be placed under probation.
