Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961, 36 for the Financial Year 2022-23 under the head' (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Compulsory) (2215 1881)', a total amount of Rs. 222.19 crore has been released towards expenditure on Auxiliary Grants (Salaries) and Rs. 802.32 lakh towards expenditure on Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salary). Now, the matter of disbursing salary and non-salary funds to the concerned Zila Parishads is under the consideration of the government, taking into account the total salary expenditure and demand of the Zila Parishads.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, a total grant of Rs. 8, 15,00,000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Fifteen Lakhs only) under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salary) Rs. 8,00,00, 000/- and 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) Rs. 15,00,000/- was shown in the Statement A accompanying this Government Resolution.
2. District Council Jalna has released Rs. The non-salary grant of Rs. 15.00 lakh has not been drawn from the Treasury despite repeated intimations/ letters. Therefore, the said grant allocated to Jalna Zilla Parishad has been withdrawn on the BEAMS system and the said grant has been reallocated. The grant sanctioned under this Government decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. The non-salaried fund should be utilised by the respective Zila Parishads for meeting the expenses like travel expenses, office expenses, lease tax, motor vehicles, electricity bills, petrol/ oil/ lubricants, machinery and equipment etc.
(3) Care should be taken to submit to the Government the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as to deposit the unutilized funds disbursed earlier. Also, the expenditure incurred should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's office from time to time. Further funds will be released if the work is pending.

Expenditure from the grant of accounts sanctioned by this Government vide Demand No.Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961, (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)" shall be spent under the head of accounts and shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2022-23.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the consent received for the expenditure of non-salary funds under the Finance Department Informal Reference No. 250/ Expenditure-3, dated 22.08.2022, as well as the powers given to the administrative departments regarding the salary fund and the limit of funds made available by the Finance Department on the BIMS system.
