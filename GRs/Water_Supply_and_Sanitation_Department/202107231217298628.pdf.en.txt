Government's decision:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, for the purpose of implementing measures to provide water from the Krishna River through the Tembhu Upasana Irrigation Scheme for drinking and livestock purposes to the Mayani Lake and surrounding villages and 16 villages in the eastern part of Man Taluk, vide reference No.
1 The Government has sanctioned Rs. 905.21 lakh out of which Rs.
665.99 lakh. 3 has been issued to the Divisional Commissioner, Pune as per the decision of the Government. The remaining amount of Rs. 239.22 lakhs is being sanctioned and released to the Divisional Commissioner, Pune through the Budget Distribution System subject to the terms and conditions laid down in the extant Government Resolution as well as the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions
(1) The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall disburse the said fund to the concerned Collector.
2) The funds will be used to pay the outstanding bills. Ensure that the funds are not used for other short-term measures.
3) The funds of the scheme, which are being implemented by the Water Resources Department, should be immediately distributed by the concerned district collector to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Sangli Irrigation Board, Sangli, who is implementing the scheme.
The funds are to be spent in the financial year 2021-22. It should be ensured that it does not lapse or is not withdrawn from the treasury and deposited in the bank account.
5) The utilization certificate should be submitted to the government for full utilization of the funds being distributed.
