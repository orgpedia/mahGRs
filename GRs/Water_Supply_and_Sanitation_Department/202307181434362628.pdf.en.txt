Government circular
Central Government to impart training to Government officers/ employees under the name of' Karmayogi Bharat'
An online portal has been launched. Many useful trainings are available free of cost on this training portal. All the officers/ employees of the state are required to undergo various types of useful training using this portal. Accordingly, it is being made mandatory for all officers and employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and Ground Water Survey and Development System to undergo various trainings on the" Karmayogi Bharat" portal.
The process of conducting various trainings on the portal should be carried out regularly in the next period. The modalities for conducting training on" Karmayogi Bharat" Portal are set out in Annexure A attached.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development Mechanism and Member Secretary, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran shall instruct all concerned to conduct online training of all officers/ staff under them at the place where they are working and submit a report of completion.
The Director, Maharashtra Environment and Engineering Training Institute, Nashik should resolve the difficulties faced by all concerned in conducting the said training. the Maharashtra Life Authority and Ground Water
