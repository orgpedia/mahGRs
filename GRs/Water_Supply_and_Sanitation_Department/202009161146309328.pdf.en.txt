Preamble: Administrative approval has been accorded for the temporary water supply scheme of Mauje Dhakephal and 5 villages, Taluka Paithan, District Aurangabad at a cost of Rs. 90,25,000/- under the scarcity measures for the period 2018-19. However, during the actual work, excavation has been done in the raw rock and hard rock. The Jayakwadi reservoir project was planned for this purpose. However, this chariot has been extinguished by the Irrigation Department and the cost has increased as the water (back water) in the reservoir has gone back by about 1 km in the summer. The pumping machine starter has been changed. Hence, the original plan of Rs. 9.71 lakh. The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad

Reference No.- 03 Rs. The revised budget of Rs. 99,96,000/- has been submitted to the Government for administrative approval. The revised budget has been given technical approval by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad. Accordingly, the matter of giving administrative approval to the revised budget of Mauje Dhakephal and 5 villages, Taluka Paithan, District Aurangabad was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Considering the facts mentioned in the foregoing, the proposal of temporary water supply scheme of Mauje Dhakephal and 5 villages, Taluka Paithan, District Aurangabad at a cost of Rs. 99,96,000/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs Nine Thousand Sixteen Thousand only) is being given revised administrative approval under scarcity subject to the following conditions:
1) The terms and conditions of the original administrative approval and technical approval order of the scheme should be strictly followed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state have been shut down due to non-payment of electricity dues due to lack of maintenance. Therefore
The water tariff should be increased from time to time as per requirement to keep the scheme financially viable.
2. Expenditure on Demand No. C-6, 2245 Assistance for relief of natural calamities, 01-Deprivation, 102 Drinking water supply, 91- Expenditure as per the norms of State Disaster Response Fund, (91) (01) Emergency supply of drinking water (22450093) (Dattamat) (Non-salaried)-31, Subsidiary Grants to be deducted from the provision under this head and finally spent thereunder.
3. Revised Administrative Approval to the said Scheme is vide Government Resolution No. 30/2013/ Regulations/ Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015 in Part-I, Sub-section-3, A.No.6 (six), Paragraph No. 27 (a) as per the authority conferred on the administrative department.
