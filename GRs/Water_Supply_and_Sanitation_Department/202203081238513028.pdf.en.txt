Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year as per the Government Circular No. 1 and Notification No. 2. The final seniority of the category" Assistant Geophysicist" in the Ground Water Survey and Development System dated 01/01/2001 has been published vide Government Circular No. 3. The General Administration Department, in accordance with the instructions given by the Government Circular No. 1 under reference No. 4, issued a Circular of Temporary Common Seniority indicating the status of the officers of the cadre of Assistant Geophysicist in the Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2002 to 01/01/2021 by including the names of the officers included in the selection list of the year 2001 to 2020 for the post of Assistant Geophysicist, and publishing the objection in this regard.
