Circular to the Government, Finance Department, No: Finance-2023/ No.40/ Finance-3, No. Government Resolution, Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, No. 1423/ No. 95/ Papu-23, D. 18th April, 2023
The Proposal:
Demand No.Y-2 for payment of salary expenses of Class 1 and Class 2 cadre officers on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad Under the head of the article Y-2" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub-Divisional Establishment for implementation of Water Supply Programme (2215 1843)"
For the financial year 2023-24, a total amount of Rs. 55.8963 crore has been budgeted for 01st Pay Commission. Among them, the Department of Finance has a budget control system.
The Rs. A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore has been disbursed to the Zilla Parishads as per the Government Resolution No. 2 in the Vachha. At present, the matter of disbursing the next salary grant to the Zila Parishads is under the consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: Budget-1423/ No. 95/ Papu-23
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2023-24, a total grant of Rs. 5,93,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore and Ninety Three Lakh only) as shown in the' Statement A' accompanying this Government decision to meet the salary expenditure of Class I and Class II officers on the establishment of Zilla Parishad Rural Water Supply Department under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 01, Water Supply 001, Operation and Administration (01) (01) Divisional/ Sub Divisional Establishment (Mandatory) for implementation of Water Supply Programme (22151843)" is being sanctioned to the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the concerned Zilla Parishad.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the' Return A" accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose.
(3) Care should be taken to demand further necessary funds only after ensuring that the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed earlier as well as under the said Government decision is submitted to the Government. The concerned District Councils should note that the funds will not be disbursed until the next demand letter is received. Also, the expenditure incurred should be reconciled with the expenditure recorded by the Accountant General's office from time to time. No further funds will be released if the works are delayed. The subsidy will be credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
4) Before drawing the funds from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
