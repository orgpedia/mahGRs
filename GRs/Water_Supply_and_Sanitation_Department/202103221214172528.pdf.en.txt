As per the decision of the Government dated 04/02/2005 of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development machinery under the Department. The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune has requested the Government to extend the temporary posts of 273 posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency from the remaining posts after the review of the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, keeping in view the urgency of the work and the implementation of water supply schemes, machinery, etc.
A total of 118 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department in the head" 4402 Capital Expenditure" and 78 in the head" 2702 Small Irrigation Managements" were approved to be continued from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021. As the Deputy Director is the Head of the Regional Department and the Senior Geologist is the Head of the Office, the Finance Department, as per the provisions of Government Decision 10.09.2001, does not lapse even if a vacancy remains for more than six months. So, for the post of deputy director and senior geologist under the head of accounting 4402, the capital cost is 1.

The following 04 temporary posts under Irrigation Department and head of Accounts' 4402 Capital Expenditure' are also required to be extended.
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 1, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021. Considering the reasons mentioned by the Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Pune for extending the temporary posts and the provision in the Government Decision No. 1 in the context of the Finance Department, the matter of extending 136 temporary posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble and the urgency of the work in the Ground Water Survey and Development System, this Government has decided to continue 136 temporary posts in the establishment of Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
Approval is being accorded for continuation of 136 temporary posts as indicated in Annexure-A on the establishment of Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency, Pune subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The said period is being extended subject to the assumption that the said temporary posts have not lapsed.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
(3) As from the date on which the officer/ employee holding the post of Superintendant is absorbed in the other posts, the Superintendents post shall stand automatically abolished.
The expenditure for the aforesaid temporary posts shall be met from the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts referred to in Annexure-B.
The present Government Resolution, Government Resolution No. 2016/ No. 08/16/ A.P.C.
being issued pursuant to the authority conferred on the Administrative Department vide dated 02/03/2021.
