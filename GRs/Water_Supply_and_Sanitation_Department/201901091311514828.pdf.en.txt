For the year 2018-19, the Finance Department has made available 70% of the said provision on budgetary system and out of this provision, necessary grants have been released to the concerned District Councils in 2018-19 so far, as per the decision of the Government of 2nd to 7th Context.
The matter of disbursement of funds under the above head of accounts to the concerned District Councils is under consideration of the Government, taking into account the expenditure incurred by the District Councils so far and the terms and conditions of the circular under reference (1).
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2018-2019, to meet the salary and non-salary expenses of Class-III and Class-IV officers and staff on the establishment of Rural Water Supply Department in the Zilla Parishad, a sum of Rs. 12,63,00, 000/- and Rs. 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) under the head 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions (District Level) (01) Assistance to Zilla Parishad/ Panchayat Institutions Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads (Local Area) (01) (05) Regular Establishment (22151881) under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 has been sanctioned. Subject to the release of unspent funds to the Government for the year 2017-18, funds are being disbursed under the target of 31. Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment). Funds under this target should not be drawn from the treasury without depositing the previous unpaid funds to the government. 31. Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried): The funds under this scheme shall be utilized by the respective District Councils for meeting the travel expenses, office expenses, lease rent, motor vehicle rent, petrol/ oil/ lubricants, as may be required.
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. All the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the amounts shown in the statement A accompanying this Government decision. Also, ensure that the funds are used for the intended purpose. ECS in the bank account of the salary subsidy beneficiaries provided by the government
