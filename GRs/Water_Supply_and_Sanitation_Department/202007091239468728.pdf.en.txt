The Proposal:
The Government has taken the responsibility of payment of salary, allowances and pension to the employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority under the Government Decision dated 23.3.2017 and since it is likely to take a long time for the payment of pension to the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority through the Treasury from the regular Principal Accounts Title 2071, the new Account Title 22158052, which was created temporarily to provide subsidy to the Retirement Salary Authority of the retired employees of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, was extended till 2019-20 under the Government Decision dated 10.01.2019. The government is considering extending the deadline for 2020-21.

Government's decision:
2215A052, which has been provisionally approved for making financial provision for grant of ex-gratia to the Authority for retirement of employees retired from Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, is being extended for the next year 2020-21.
The Government has decided to issue a circular to the Finance Department vide Non-official Reference No. 156/19/ Expenditure-3, dated 20.06.2019, vide letter No. III/ U.O.R.-28/ MJP/ Pension/ 87, dated 17.06.2020 as per the consensus arrived at.
