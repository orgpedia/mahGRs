The Proposal:
As many as 54 villages in Javali taluka (Satara district) face drinking water shortage during summer, there is a demand for construction of Bondarwadi dam for permanent drinking water to these villages. In pursuance of this demand, meetings have been held from time to time. The meeting discussed in detail the construction of the dam at Bondarwadi. 54 villages in Javali taluk need to be supplied water as per 55 LPCD throughout the year. The government was considering the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Satara, to prepare a feasibility report of the project by examining the ground water level of the village, the water available, the land acquisition required for the construction of the dam, the problems faced at the local level, the drinking water requirement of 54 villages, how the water will be supplied to 54 villages from the dam, etc.
Government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Collector, Satara to examine various aspects and prepare a feasibility report regarding the construction of Bondarwadi Dam for permanent drinking water for 54 villages in Javali Taluka (G. Satara).

The committee shall prepare a comprehensive report on the construction of Bondarwadi Dam and supply of water to 54 villages from Bondarwadi Dam after taking into account the decisions taken in the previous meeting held since 2013. The report is divided into two parts. Below are the two parts.
(1) A detailed report on the construction of a dam for water supply.
(2) Detailed report of water supply schemes for 54 villages in Javali taluka.
The draft project report should be prepared by including both the above points. The report will be submitted in two months" time.
