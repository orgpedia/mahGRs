Government circular:
A Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Director, Ground Water Survey and Development Agency to implement measures like storage tanks on Catch the Rain basis to store and use drinking water in villages/ wards/ habitations facing drinking water problem. As per the report of the Committee, the State has been provided with drinking water during the period of 3 to 4 months of summer and certain other periods due to water stress. Meenatai Thackeray Rural Water Storage Scheme" Context No. The decision is being implemented across the state.
Under the Late Meenatai Thackeray Rural Water Storage Scheme, the following measures are being taken: 1. Metallic Storage Tanks.
2. Ferrocement or RCC cement storage tanks.
