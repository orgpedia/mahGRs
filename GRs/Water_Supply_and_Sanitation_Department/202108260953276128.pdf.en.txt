GOVERNMENT DECISION, WATER SUPPLY AND SANITAL DEPARTMENT NO. JJAMI-2019/ PR. NO. 138/ PAPU10 (07), Dy. 04.09.2020 Preamble Jal Jeevan Mission is being implemented in the State since 2019. The central government. The operational guidelines of Jal Jeevan Mission have been issued on 25.12.2019.
Based on this, guidelines have been issued for implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission in the state as per the decision of the concerned government.
Jal Jeevan Mission is intended to be a mass movement for successful implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission. For efficient operation and long-term security of schemes under Jal Jeevan Mission, local bodies and Gram Panchayats/ Village Water& Sanitation Committees (VWSCs) are required to take the responsibility of planning, management and implementation of the scheme. The Jal Jeevan Mission will help in creating a mass movement if a sense of ownership is created among the people regarding the resources generated through the schemes under the Jal Jeevan Mission. The role of Gram Panchayats in the implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission should be understood by the Central Government. Guidelines for Gram Panchayats have been issued on 29.09.2020. As per para 4.24 of the said guidelines, under the Information, Education and Communication component of Jal Jeevan Mission, there is a provision for display of information related to the scheme on the facade/ strategic location at the implementation site. A proposal had been sent to the Government in this regard.
