The Central Government has launched an online portal for training government officers/ employees under the name of" Karmayogi Bharat" and is implementing this project in the state to increase the capacity of officers/ employees in various government departments/ offices through the project Mission Karmayogi.
Many useful trainings are available free of cost on this training portal. The Mission Karmayogi has been launched in the State on 12th May, 2023.
The following training on Mission Karmayogi Portal is being made mandatory for all the officers/ staff of Class 2 and Class 3 cadre working on the establishment of Water Supply and Sanitation Department (itself) and Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Ground Water Survey and Development System and State Water and Sanitation Mission Belapur under this department to undergo online training for a period of two months from 1st August 2023 to 30th September 2023.
Link: http:// www. igotkarmayogi. gov. in/
1. Digital Literacy 2. Noting and Drafting 3. Code of Conduct for Government Employees 4. Effective Communication 5. Introduction to Government E Marketplace 6. Stay Safe in Cyber Space
The training is mandatory and the certificate of completion of the training and the report of the work should be submitted to the Establishment Branch, the report of the Maharashtra Life Authority to Papu-23, the report of the Bhujal Survey and Development System to Papu-15 and the report of the State Water and Sanitation Mission to Papu-7.
