2. Reference No. By the decision of the government in the 3rd, the public charge for rural water supply schemes has been completely abolished. Therefore, the funds allocated by the government for the implementation of this scheme are subject to Article No. 3 shall be as set forth in the governing decision.
The scheme is granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
The scheme should be implemented by the District Council, Wardha. After completion of the scheme, the District Council shall ensure that it has been successfully completed and after completion of the scheme and testing, the maintenance of the scheme shall be carried out by the District Council/ Shikhar/ Joint Committee as mentioned in the Government Resolution No. 4.
2. To meet the expenditure for operation and maintenance of the scheme, the concerned Gram Panchayat shall provide at least Rs. 1200/- per family per year and it should be increased with time. The Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad, Wardha should himself ensure that the villagers are ready to fill the water table. The government will not provide any subsidy for the maintenance and operation of the project.
3. Strengthening of the resources of the scheme. 100% household tap connections should be included in the scheme.
(4) If the expenditure of the scheme increases for any reason, no government subsidy will be provided for the increased expenditure.
5. Action should be taken as per the prevailing policy on making the village encroachment free.
The work of the plan should be done in a time-bound and planned manner.
