Preamble: The post of" Assistant Administrative Officer" in the Survey and Development Agency has been appointed for a probation period of two years as per the decision of the Government in the context No. 2 Ground Water. These appointments have been made by the first appointment in the government service on the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of the officers/ employees of the Government service and since the officers posted to the post of Assistant Administrative Officer Ground Water Survey and Development System have satisfactorily completed their probationary period of 2 years and the same has been recommended by the Directorate under Reference No. 3, the Government was considering the matter of giving them a substitute appointment as a regular" Assistant Administrative Officer" by terminating the probationary period of these officers.
Governance Decision: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the following" Assistant Administrative Officers" in the Ground Water Survey and Development machinery, as indicated in the table below, have satisfactorily completed the requisite formalities to complete their probationary period, as mentioned in column No. 4
