The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2020 has been approved by the Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is published as Maharashtra Act No. 15 in the Government of Maharashtra, Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, dated 18th March, 2020. However, the Department of Finance has released the Budget Estimates for the first three months (April, 2020 to June, 2020) of the Budget Estimates for the Financial Year 2020-21 through the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS) in the range of 15-25% of the total annual outlay.

Considering the total expenditure on account of salaries of the respective Zila Parishads in the year 2019-20, the Government is considering to disburse the said grant to the Zila Parishads for a period of three months from April, 2020 to June, 2020.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, a total amount of Rs. 24,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Forty Lacs only) is being sanctioned to the concerned Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad as shown in the Statement A accompanying this Government Decision for the Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head" 2215, Water Supply and Sanitation 196, Assistance to Zilla Parishads (01), Assistance to Zilla Parishads/ Panchayat Institutions, Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (01) (05) Regular Establishment (Mandatory) (22151881)."
(2) The grants sanctioned under this Government Resolution shall be subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time. Accordingly, action should be taken for payment of salaries as per the procedure mentioned in the Government Circular No. 2020/ No. 62/ Koshasra-5, dated 1st April, 2020 of the Finance Department for the period mentioned therein.
