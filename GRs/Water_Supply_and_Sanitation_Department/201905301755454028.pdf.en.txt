02. Ma. The High Court, Bombay in its judgment dated 04/08/2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 has declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed in the Supreme Court. The petition is still pending. the Open Category Officers/

The Department of Law and Justice has said that there is no legal problem in giving promotions to the employees. Also, Chief Geophysicist in Ground Water Survey and Development System is a stand-alone post and reservation is not applicable in recruitment and promotion for stand-alone post as per General Administration Department, Government Circular dated 02/11/1996. According to Subject to the adjudication of the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court and subject to the following conditions, Shri N.V. Sharma, Senior Geophysicist is being promoted to the cadre of Chief Geophysicist in the Ground Water Survey and Development System on ad-hoc basis on purely temporary basis:
(a) The said promotion shall be an ad-hoc promotion of temporary nature as an administrative exigency.
(b) The said promotion is being given only on ad-hoc/ ad-hoc basis considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Senior Geophysicist and this seniority list has not been challenged.
The selection of eligible Senior Geophysicists for the year 2018-19 is ad-hoc in nature and promotion to the post of Chief Geophysicist is ad-hoc in nature. As a result, the senior geophysicist in the selection list will not get any rights of regularity and seniority in the post of Chief Geophysicist.
(e) The ad-hoc promotion of Shri N.V. Sharma, Senior Geophysicist to the post of Chief Geophysicist is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated against him till the date of this order. The administration should ensure this.
If Mr. N.V. Sharma, Senior Geophysicist does not accept the ad-hoc promotion to the post of Chief Geophysicist, his name will be removed from the selection list for the year 2018-19 and his eligibility will be checked for ad-hoc promotion to the post of Chief Geophysicist in the third year selection list without considering his name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The promoted officers should be relieved from work by the concerned office immediately and report to this department. Also, the promoted officers should join the office immediately. Any request for change in the post will not be entertained.
