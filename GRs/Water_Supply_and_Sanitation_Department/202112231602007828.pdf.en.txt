Preamble: With a view to provide pure and adequate drinking water to the rural population of the State. The Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme is being implemented in the state under the government decision here. For the implementation of new water supply schemes sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme (Account Head No. 2215 9942) for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 230.13 crore has been made and the Finance Department has made available funds equivalent to 50% of the budgetary allocation on the Budget Distribution System. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning and releasing grants to Aurangabad and Raigad Zilla Parishad for the implementation of tap water supply schemes sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, for the implementation of the new tap water supply schemes sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme (Head of Accounts No. under 2215 9942) to the Aurangabad Zilla Parishad as mentioned in the following statement:

Terms and Conditions 1) The Zilla Parishad should spend this grant only for the implementation of new tap water supply schemes approved under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme.
The funds are to be spent in the financial year 2021-22. It should be ensured that it does not lapse or is not withdrawn from the treasury and deposited in the bank account.
3) The implementation agency will be responsible for conducting a third-party technical examination of the works of the tap water supply schemes as per the guidelines given under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme and to fulfill the observations made.
(4) No expenditure shall be incurred on any scheme in excess of the administratively approved cost.
5) The funds disbursed to the Zila Parishads should not be kept in the Personal Provident Fund (PLA) or any other such account.
6) When the funds are sought under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Program, the funds will not be sanctioned in the future without submitting information about the funds distributed in the current financial year, the expenses incurred, the balance funds, etc.
7) The material progress for the purpose for which the funds have been disbursed should be submitted along with the monthly expenditure report every month. Accounts of allocation of funds, collection and disbursement of grants as per procedure prescribed in the records
