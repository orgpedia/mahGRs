Preamble: The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal worth Rs. 1182.41 lakh for the temporary supplementary water supply scheme for Khuldabad town and 20 other villages for the scarcity period of 2018-19. The proposal is for grant of administrative approval to the Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department for temporary water supply schemes/ measures in urban and rural areas beyond the prescribed financial and physical parameters to address the drinking water shortage.

The Standing Committee constituted under his Chairmanship was considered for approval in its meeting held on 14.12.2018.
The proposal was discussed in detail in the standing committee meeting and the plan was approved and the matter of administrative approval of the plan was under consideration of the government.
Taking into account the facts mentioned in the above-mentioned preamble, the government decided to demolish Khulatabad town and 20 other villages. The proposal for Provisional Supplementary Water Supply Scheme at Khulatabad, District Aurangabad at a cost of Rs. 1182.41 lakhs (Rupees Eleven Crores, Eighty Two Lakhs, Forty One Thousand only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions: 1. The Collector, Aurangabad shall first declare that there is scarcity of drinking water in Khulatabad town and 20 other villages covered under the Provisional Supplementary Water Supply Scheme.
2) The work of temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme under the scheme for Khuldabad city and 20 other villages mentioned in the proposal should be done by the Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Aurangabad.
3) If Khuldabad Municipality is a' C' class municipality, it is required to pay 5% of the population to its account.
4) The cost of the project is Rs. 529.82 lakhs so their 5% amount i.e. With a population of 26.49 lakh, the financial outlay of the scheme will be as follows:

5) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner. Bids with higher prices will not be accepted.
6) Tender is allowed to be drawn for a short period (10 days).
The tender process must be completed by January 12, 2019. Approval of the scheme will be cancelled if the work order is not issued by this date.

The water supply from the scheme must be started by completing the work of the scheme before March 12, 2019. However, if the water supply is not resumed by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
9) After the completion of the tender process, the project must be completed within the stipulated time as per the terms and conditions of the contract. No extension will be granted under any circumstances.
10) It should be ensured that the work of the scheme is completed within the administrative approved cost.
The government will not provide more funds. If the cost of the scheme increases, it will be taken seriously by the government and disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned.
11) If any irregularity, misappropriation or malpractice is found in the implementation of the scheme, the concerned officers/ employees responsible for it will be subject to disciplinary action.
After the completion of the scheme, the responsibility of running the scheme will remain with the Khuldabad Municipal Council.
The operation order of the scheme should not be issued until a resolution is received that the Khuldabad Municipal Council is ready to take the responsibility of running the scheme.
13) The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad, should provide funds equal to 50% of the government share at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for this scheme. The remaining 30% of the funds should be disbursed by the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad after seeing the progress of the scheme. The remaining 20% will be given after the scheme is implemented.
Since the work of the scheme must be completed on time, a progress report should be sent to the government every Monday of the week from the tender process of the scheme to the start of water supply from the scheme.
