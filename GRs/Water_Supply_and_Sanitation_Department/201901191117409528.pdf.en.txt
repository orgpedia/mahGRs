Read: 1) Government letter, Revenue and Forest Department No. SCY 2018/ PR. No.251/ M-7, dated 08.01.2019 (minutes of the meeting)
Preamble- At the Tehsil level, based on the information received through the GPS system in the Panchayat Samiti office, information about the rates of tankers will be collected and based on this, special training will be conducted for the concerned employees who will make the payment and the related expenses will be paid from the fund received under the scarcity. The Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister for Relief and Rehabilitation in its meeting held on 03.01.2019 considered and agreed for approval.
The government was contemplating issuing a notification in this regard.
Government's decision
The concerned staff dealing with the issue of drinking water shortage in the Panchayat Samiti office of the Zilla Parishad and preparing the payment of drinking water and tankers are being approved to conduct one-day training at the" Mitra" (Maharashtra Environmental Engineering and Research Institute), an institute functioning in Nashik under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
2. It is being approved as a special case that the expenditure for this training will be paid from the fund available under drinking water scarcity.
