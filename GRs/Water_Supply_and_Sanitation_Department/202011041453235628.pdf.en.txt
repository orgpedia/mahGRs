Introduction: Under the Jal Swarojya-2 programme, Reference No. 1 The Controller of Finance has been declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and" Controlling Officer" under the Government Order.
All the posts on deputation created for the period of Jalswarajya-2 programme are mentioned in Reference No. 2 in the order. It was released on 30.9.2020. This includes the position of" Controller of Finance." As a result, the government was considering declaring a new" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" as well as a" Controller Officer" to handle all aspects of expenditure under the Jal Swarajya-2 programme.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the implementation of the programme under Jal Swarojya-2 programme, Project Manager, Improvement Support and Project Management Cell is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Under Secretary (Finance), Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
Also, under this program for the Improvement Support and Project Management Cell, Reference No. The powers conferred on the Controller of Finance to sign the cheques under the Statement I accompanying the Government Resolution hereunder are being delegated to the Under Secretary (Finance), Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, Ministry of Water Resources, Mumbai.
