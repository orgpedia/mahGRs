Government Circular: The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for the preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1. The Common Final Seniority of" Auxiliary Chemicals" category in Ground Water Survey and Development System for the period from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 has been published vide Circular No. 2. The General Administration Department, in accordance with the instructions given under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference, has included the names of the officers included in the selection list for the post of Assistant Rasayani for the years 2015, 2016 and 2017 and prepared a provisional common seniority chart showing the status of the" Assistant Rasayani" cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01/01/2016, 01/01/2017 and 01/01/2018. This seniority is being attached to this circular as Annexure A.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority list and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the establishment of the department. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the officer should submit his statement through the directorate.
The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted to the Government before 24th September, 2018 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers so that it is possible to make necessary changes in the seniority list after verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list will be considered for determining seniority in the Auxiliary Chemical Cadre only as per the Government Notification General Administration Department, No. SRV-1076/ XII, dated 21/06/1982. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
