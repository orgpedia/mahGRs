Preamble: Reference to the budget of the tap water supply scheme in Mauje Markhel (Deglur, Dist. Nanded), Gram Panchayat No. 1, Mauje Markhel (Dist. Deglur, G. Nanded) has provided the basic administrative approval under the Gram Sabha Resolution. As per Reference No. 2, the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has accorded the original technical approval to the budget. However, the cost of the project has increased due to the high cost of the project. Post facto approval has been granted under Reference No. 3 of the tender for the work of this scheme. The Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has technically approved the budget of the scheme presented under reference number 4. Accordingly, the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Zilla Parishad, Nanded, has proposed to give revised administrative approval to the draft and budget of the tap water supply scheme.

It was submitted to the government. Accordingly, the matter of granting revised administrative approval to the plan and budget of the present scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Rs. The revised administrative sanction for the revised cost of Rs. 294.4 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Four Hundred and Ninety Five Lakhs Forty Thousand only) is being granted subject to the following conditions.
All the remaining work should be completed in 3 months at the revised price mentioned above.
The terms and conditions mentioned while granting the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
After completion of this scheme, it should be handed over to the concerned Gram Panchayat. The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad shall be responsible for taking over the scheme within one month from the date of receipt of intimation to the effect that the scheme has been actually completed by the concerned Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Nanded.
As soon as the scheme is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad and concerned Gram Panchayat officials should conduct a joint inspection of the scheme and if any defects are found in such inspection, they should be rectified before handing over the scheme.
The Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad concerned will not get any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (including special repairs), depreciation or renovation of machinery.
After the completion of this scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad) to hand over the scheme. The accounts of the scheme should also be closed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. Therefore, in order to make the scheme financially viable, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad to increase the water tariff as per the need.
