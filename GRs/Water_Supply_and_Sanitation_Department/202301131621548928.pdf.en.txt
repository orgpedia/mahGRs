Reference: 1) General Administration Department- CER. Letter No., 2022.A. 2001/30 A.D. 30.12.2022 and 09.01.2023 Preamble- 73rd Anniversary of Republic Day to be held at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai. A meeting was held under the chairmanship of Joint Secretary (Rajshrikshan-30) on 10th January, 2023 at Mantralaya to discuss the concept, jingle, model and final guidance to the concerned departments. As per the discussion in the meeting, it has been agreed to present the tableau through the Water Supply and Sanitation Department. Accordingly, the Department has decided to present a tableau based on the theme of Jal Jeevan Mission. The approval was given by the Minister of Water Supply and Sanitation. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the presentation of tableaux was under consideration.
The decision was taken at the 73rd Republic Day celebrations at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai.
It will be completed on January 26, 2023. For this, the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation is giving approval to present a tableau based on the Jal Jeevan Mission program. Accordingly, it is necessary to prepare tableaux, jingles, models according to the concept of the above program on Republic Day as well as the guidelines given by the General Administration Department. For this, the industry will have to prepare a model of the tableau, jingle from the interested organization.
