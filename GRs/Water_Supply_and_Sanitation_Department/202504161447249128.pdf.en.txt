Government Circular: -
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy as per the notification No. 1 in this regard for preparation and publication of seniority certificates of officers / employees of the government service every year. The provisional seniority of "Branch Engineer" Group-B in Ground Water Survey and Development System up to the date 01.01.2024 has been published under reference No. 3. Taking into consideration the objections received in this regard, a provisional final seniority chart has been prepared showing the rank of Branch Engineer, Group-B in Ground Water Survey and Development System as on 01.01.2024 and 01.01.2025. This seniority is appended to this circular as Annexure-A.
The Commissioner, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Ground Water Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all officers involved in the corresponding seniority list and working in his department, retired from service in the subordinate office of the system and on deputation from the establishment of the system or otherwise changed cadre.
