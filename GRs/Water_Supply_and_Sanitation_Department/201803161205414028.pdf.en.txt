The Proposal:
A total of 4022 permanent/ temporary posts of Class-I to Class-IV cadre in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Division in Zilla Parishads have been sanctioned under Government Decisions dated 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015 respectively. Accordingly, as per the Government Decision No. 9 dated 3.2.2018, a total of 2787 temporary posts in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Divisions were approved to be continued from 1.1.2018 to 28.2.2018. Temporary posts in rural water supply departments need to be extended for a further period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes. Therefore, it was under consideration of the Government to extend the period of continuation of sanctioned temporary posts in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions in Zila Parishads from 1.1.2018 to 28.2.2018.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the figures approved under Government Decisions, dated 4.3.2011 and dated 4.8.2015, a total of 2787 temporary posts in various cadres as shown in the attached' Statement A" working in Rural Water Supply Departments and Sub-Divisions under Zila Parishads are being allowed to continue from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018 subject to the assumption that the posts are not vacant for a period of more than six months and no irregular appointments have been made to those posts.
