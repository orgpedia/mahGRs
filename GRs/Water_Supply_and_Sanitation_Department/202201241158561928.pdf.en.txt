Preamble: The Central Government has allocated 2% of the total outlay of Central Rural Water Supply Scheme (CRWS) as contingency for natural calamities for the financial year 2012-13.
An additional amount of Rs. 15.00 crore has been sanctioned to the State of Maharashtra as 100% Government share vide order dated 07.08.2012. This additional fund will be utilized for the repair/ revival of defunct/ defunct rural water supply schemes in the deficit distribution and drought affected districts of the State. 1500 lakh has been made available to the Regional Offices under the above Government decision.
Out of the Rs. 149.59 lakh has been disbursed to the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur vide Government Resolution, dated 27.03.2014. Out of this Rs. Rs. 125.14 crore has been spent.
The remaining amount of Rs. 24.45 lakh has been deposited in government accounts through challans. The money has to be returned to the central government. The government had provided funds for the purpose.

Government Decision: Out of the funds released by the Central Government for the financial year 2012-13 for natural calamities, the amount deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State Government is Rs. 24,45,058/- is being made available for reimbursement to the Central Government.
