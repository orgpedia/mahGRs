The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati, has submitted a proposal to the Government for the year 2017-18 under the Shortage Mitigation Scheme of Barsitakali Nagar Panchayat at a cost of Rs. 30.64 lakhs which includes 1) Temporary Supplementary Faucet Scheme at Indira Awas Nagar, 2) Temporary Supplementary Faucet Scheme at Naik Nagar, Phule Nagar, 3) Deepening and Temporary Supplementary Faucet Scheme at Indira Nagar and 4) Water Supply Works through Hand Pump Repair in Nagar Panchayat area. This proposal was considered in the meeting held on 23.04.2018 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department to give administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures outside the prescribed financial and physical parameters in urban and rural areas to overcome the drinking water shortage.

The Standing Committee in its meeting held on 23.04.2018 discussed the proposal in detail and after considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, approved the proposal for 1) Temporary Supplementary Sewer Scheme at Indira Awas Nagar, 2) Temporary Supplementary Sewer Scheme at Naik Nagar, Phule Nagar and 3) Deepening of existing Public Well and Temporary Supplementary Sewerage Scheme at Indira Nagar. It was decided in the meeting that the repair work of hand pumps in the Nagar Panchayat area should be done from the self-fund of the Nagar Panchayat. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble of the Government Resolution, administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for the construction of 1) Temporary Supplementary Sewer Scheme at Indira Awas Nagar, 2) Temporary Supplementary Sewer Scheme at Naik Nagar, Phule Nagar and 3) Deepening of existing Public Well and Temporary Supplementary Sewer Scheme at Indira Nagar at a cost of Rs. 22.46 lakh (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Forty Six Thousand only) subject to the following conditions: 1) The Collector, Akola shall first declare that there is scarcity of drinking water in Barshitakali town.
2) The supplementary tap water supply schemes mentioned in the proposal should be implemented by the Nagar Panchayat Barshitakali.
3) The work of these schemes should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner.
Ensure that the projects are completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the schemes is being allowed for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by May 11, 2018.
If the tender process is not completed by May 11, 2018, the approval of the schemes will be considered as cancelled.
The water supply from the scheme should be started by completing the works of the schemes by May 25, 2018. However, if the water supply from the scheme does not start by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
After the works mentioned in the proposal of these schemes are completed by the Nagar Panchayat, Barshitakali, the maintenance of the schemes and the management of the schemes will be required to be done by the concerned Nagar Panchayat.
