The Proposal:
A total of 4022 permanent/ temporary posts of Class-I to Class-IV cadre in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions of Zila Parishads have been sanctioned vide Government Decisions No. 1 and 2 dated 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015 respectively.
Out of these posts, a total of 2002 temporary posts have been extended till 28.2.2022 as per the Government decision at Reference No.4. Temporary posts in rural water supply departments need to be extended for a further period to ensure proper implementation of rural water supply schemes. Therefore, the matter of extension of sanctioned temporary posts in Rural Water Supply Department/ Sub-Divisions in Zila Parishads is under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
As per the figure approved vide Government Resolution, dated 4.3.2011, 4.3.2011 and 4.8.2015, a total of 1898 temporary posts in various cadres as shown in the attached' Statement A" in Rural Water Supply Department and Sub-Divisions under Zila Parishads are being allowed to continue from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 subject to the assumption that the posts are not vacant for a period of more than six months and no irregular appointments have been made to those posts.
The Department of Finance has issued instructions to abolish 25% of the sanctioned posts of Group-D cadre as per Para 1 (5) of the Government Resolution dated 14.1.2016.
Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 22.3.2018, the existing 81 vacancies in Group-D cadre in the Rural Water Supply Department of the Zilla Parishad have been abolished and the temporary posts have not been extended under this Government decision.
3.The concerned officers should take care not to make new appointments without the approval of the government in the vacant post for which the term has not been extended.
