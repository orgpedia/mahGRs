Introduction: As per Section 40 of the Disaster Management Act, 2005, every department is supposed to prepare a disaster management plan. As per the instructions of the Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation) Department, the Water Supply and Sanitation Department with the help of UNICEF representatives was under the consideration of the government to form a working group to prepare the disaster management plan of the department.
Government Decision: Under the Disaster Management Act, 2005, a working group is being constituted to prepare a disaster management plan of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
The task force to prepare a disaster management plan is as follows:
