Government Decision: Moje Pangri Gawthanwadi Water Supply Scheme (Ta. Sangameshwar, G. Ratnagiri) The revised budget of Rs. 37,99,697/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Ninety Seven only) is being accorded revised administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions
1) All the remaining works should be completed in 6 months at the revised price mentioned above.
2) The terms and conditions mentioned in the original administrative approval as well as the revised technical approval granted to the scheme should be strictly followed.
(3) The Gram Panchayat concerned shall not receive any assistance from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation, maintenance, repair (subject to special amendments), depreciation or renovation of machinery in the scheme.
4) After the completion of the scheme, it will be mandatory for the local self-government institution (concerned district council) to hand over the scheme.
As per the CAG report of 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state have been closed due to non-payment of electricity bills. In order to make this scheme financially viable, the responsibility of increasing the water tariff as per the need will be of the concerned Gram Panchayat.
6) Action should be taken according to the prevailing policy on eviction.
7) Reference No. 5 The third technical examination should be conducted as per the directions of the government decision here.
8) The present scheme shall not be revised under any circumstances and the responsibility of closing the accounts of the scheme shall be of the Executive Engineer (GRAPU) Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri.
2. The revised cost of the scheme is Rs. 4,73,400/- (Rupees Four Lakh Seventy Three Thousand Four Hundred only) under National Rural Drinking Water Programme from the State share/ District Planning Board/ Minimum Needs Programme.
