The proposal-
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The reference No. 4 As per the decision of the Government, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for the disabled. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the matter of ensuring the disabled category for the posts in the Ground Water Survey and Development System was under the consideration of the Government.
