Conditions
The scheme should be implemented by the Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad. The contractor will be responsible for the maintenance and repair of the plan for at least 1 year after the implementation of the plan.
2. The work of the scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in a proper manner.
No work order shall be issued for the scheme unless the necessary land acquisition and all other permissions for the scheme have been obtained. Without fulfilling this condition, work orders should not be issued. If the executive order is issued without fulfilling this condition, the administrative approval given under this government decision will be considered null and void.
4. The works of the scheme should be completed in a phased manner in a time bound and planned manner at the approved cost.
5. No other work should be started until the origin of the plan is developed. Other works in the scheme should be carried out only after ensuring that there is enough water in the spring.
6. Correct the details of expenditure shown in the Detailed Project Report for operation and maintenance of the scheme and keep it to a minimum. Also, to meet the expenditure and to make the scheme financially self-sustaining, the concerned Gram Panchayat should fix the minimum water tap rate for the household tap holder and increase it from time to time.
The Government will not provide any assistance/ grant to the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad for the maintenance of the scheme or for the renovation of the machinery to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the operation, inspection and maintenance of the scheme.
