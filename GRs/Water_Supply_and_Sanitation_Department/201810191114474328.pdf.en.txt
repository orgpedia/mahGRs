Government Decision: In view of the facts mentioned in the foregoing, the Hon. Savana (Ta. Chikhli, G. Buldana) Rs. 339.36 Lakhs (Rs. The revised administrative sanction under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) is being granted subject to the following conditions.
1) The scheme will be implemented by the District Council.
2) The terms and conditions of the original administrative approval of the scheme in the government decision and the order of technical approval should be strictly followed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state have been shut down due to non-payment of electricity dues due to lack of maintenance. Therefore, the water tariff should be increased from time to time as per the requirement to keep the scheme financially viable.
4) The accounts should be closed by transferring the scheme to the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zilla Parishad after testing the scheme.
5) The concerned Gram Panchayat/ Zila Parishad will not get any assistance/ grant from the Government to meet the recurring expenditure incurred every year for the implementation of the scheme, its maintenance, repairs (subject to special amendments), depreciation in the scheme or renovation of machinery.
6) Action should be taken according to the prevailing policy on open defecation.
7) It will be the responsibility of the Maharashtra Life Authority to ensure that the scheme is not rescheduled.
The remaining work of the scheme should be completed in 6 months.
2. The revised cost of the scheme is Rs. 44.03 lakh (Rupees Forty Four Lakh Three Thousand only) under National Rural Drinking Water Programme from the State share/ District Planning Board/ Minimum Needs Programme.
