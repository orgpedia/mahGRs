Approval has been accorded for approval of measures like/ Special Repair of Fencing Wells etc., as per prescribed financial and physical norms upto 30.04.2020. These works are to be completed by 31.05.2020. However, the matter of relaxing the period for completing the shortage relief measures in line with the coronavirus infection was under the consideration of the government.
The following decisions are being taken regarding the drinking water shortage work in line with the corona virus infection.
Provisional water supply schemes and tube well/ bore well special repairs etc., as per prescribed norms during the current scarcity period 2019-20 are being sanctioned upto 30.06.2020.
However, the remedial works should be completed at the earliest so that these works can be utilized to the maximum during the scarcity period.
