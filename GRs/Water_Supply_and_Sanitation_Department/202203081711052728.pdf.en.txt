Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees of government service every year under the Government Resolution No. 1.
Subsequently, the said Government has issued instructions under the contextual notification/ circulars No. 4 and 5 superseding the said decision. The Provisional Common Seniority Report showing the status of Planning Officer (Group-B) cadre in Ground Water Survey and Development System from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2015 and from 01/01/2016 to 01/01/2018 has been published vide Government Circular No. 2 and 3 respectively. Also, the Government Circular No. 6. Seniority from 01/01/2012 to 01/01/2018 has been provisionally finalised for March, 2022.
As per the instructions of the General Administration Department, the officers of the Planning Officer (Group-B) cadre in the Ground Water Survey and Development System will be included in the selection list for the post of Planning Officer (Group-B) from 2018 to 2022. 01/01/2019, Dy. 01/01/2020, Dy. 01/01/2021 and D. A provisional seniority chart showing the status as on 01/01/2022 has been prepared. The said seniority has been attached as Annexure-A to this Circular.

The Director, Ground Water Survey and Development System, Bhujal Bhawan, Shivajinagar, Pune is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority notice and are working in their department, retired from the department and on deputation from the department's establishment. If there are any genuine mistakes or errors in the seniority list, the concerned officer should submit his statement through the directorate. The facts in the representation of the officers should be submitted to the Government before March, 2022 along with the opinion of the Director after examining the service books and other available records of the officers, so that it will be possible to make necessary changes in the seniority list by verifying it. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list will be considered only for determining the seniority of the cadre of Planning Officer. This date will not be taken into account for any other financial benefit.
