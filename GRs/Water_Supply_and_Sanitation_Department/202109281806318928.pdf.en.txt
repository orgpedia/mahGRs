Maharashtra Environmental Engineering Training and Research Institute (METRA) is an institute under the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, under the Maharashtra Jeevan Adhikaran at Nashik. For the sustainable running of the institute, METRA is being declared as a state level training institute of the Water Supply and Sanitation Department of the State Government.
The institute will function as per the training policy of the state of Maharashtra. The training activities of the institute will be related to the core technical activities of the department as well as the subordinate institutions of the department. An annual training plan will be prepared for this technical training program. Also, the rates of these trainings will be as per the training rates of the State Training Policy.
