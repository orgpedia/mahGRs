The funds will be used for providing individual tap connections (FHTC) to every rural household under Jal Jeevan Mission, as well as for supporting activities and water quality control surveys. After disbursement of funds, the fund should be managed monthly/ quarterly as per the financial plan in line with the physical target.
The funds should be utilised on priority for aligning Jal Jeevan Mission with the National Rural Drinking Water Programme through corrective re-alignment of ongoing/ completed schemes. Priority should be given to quality-affected villages, SC/ ST dominated villages/ hamlets, villages in Sansad Adarsh Grams, villages in aspirational districts.
The above fund comprises of a 25% Flexi Fund.
