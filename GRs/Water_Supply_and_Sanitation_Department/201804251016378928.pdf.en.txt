Preamble- Divisional Commissioner, Amravati announces release of funds under Shortage Relief Scheme for the period 2017-18. , Ta. A proposal of Rs. 14,85,500/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Eighty Five Thousand Five Hundred only) for a temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme at Khamgaon, District Buldana has been submitted to the Government. This proposal was considered in the meeting held on 23.04.2018 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department to give administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures outside the prescribed financial and physical parameters in urban and rural areas to overcome the drinking water shortage.
The proposal was discussed in the standing committee meeting. After discussion, it was decided to reduce the amount of GST included in the budget to a certain extent and a total of Rs. 14.42 lakh

In view of the facts mentioned in the Government Resolution, administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal of temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme at a cost of Rs. 14.42 lakh (Rs.14 lakh, forty two thousand only) in Mauje Sutala Bu, Taluka Khamgaon, District Buldana subject to the following conditions: 1. The Collector, Buldana shall first declare that Mauje Sutala, Taluka Khamgaon village is facing drinking water shortage.
2) The supplementary tap water supply scheme mentioned in the proposal should be implemented by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Buldana.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner.
Ensure that the work is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (7 days).
The tender process must be completed by May 11, 2018.
If the tender process is not completed by May 11, 2018, the approval of the scheme will be considered canceled.
The water supply from the scheme should be started by completing the works of the scheme by May 25, 2018. However, if the water supply from the scheme does not start by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
8) After the work mentioned in the proposal of the scheme is completed by the Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done by the concerned Gram Panchayat.
9) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. The remaining funds will be released after the completion of the project.
