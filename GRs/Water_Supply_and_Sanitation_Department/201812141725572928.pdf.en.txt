Preamble- Divisional Commissioner, Nashik conducts Mouje Paridhi under Emergency Water Supply Scheme for the period of scarcity 2018-19. Jamner, District- A proposal of Rs. 44.03 lakh for temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme in Jalgaon has been submitted to the Government. This proposal was considered for approval in the meeting held on 14.12.2018 of the Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department for granting administrative approval to temporary water supply schemes/ measures in urban and rural areas beyond the prescribed financial and physical parameters to overcome the drinking water shortage.
The Standing Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, in its meeting held on 14.12.2018, discussed the proposal in detail.

The proposal was approved. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Government Resolution The Divisional Commissioner, Nashik has given administrative approval to the temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme in Mauje Paladhi, Taluka Jamner, District Jalgaon. 3 submitted to the government. In this proposal, the Standing Committee has approved the proposal of Rs. 44.03 lakh (Rupees Forty Four Lakh Three Thousand only) for the temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme at Namud Mauje Paladhi, Taluka Jamner, District Jalgaon in the meeting dated 14.12.2018. Accordingly, under this order, administrative approval is being given to the proposal of temporary supplementary tap water supply scheme for Mauje Paladhi, Taluka Jamner, District Jalgaon subject to the following conditions: 1) The Collector, Jalgaon shall first declare that there is a shortage of drinking water in the villages of Mauje Paladhi Taluka Jamner.
2) The supplementary water supply scheme mentioned in the proposal should be implemented by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon.
3) The work of this scheme should be done by inviting e-tenders in the prescribed manner.
4) Ensure that the project is completed within the approved cost.
The tender of the scheme is being allowed to be taken out for a short period (07 days).
The tender process must be completed by December 30, 2018.
If the tender process is not completed by December 30, 2018, the approval of the scheme will be considered canceled.
It is necessary to start water supply from the scheme by completing the works of the scheme by February 15, 2019. However, if the water supply from the scheme does not start by this date, the approval of the scheme will be cancelled.
9) After the work mentioned in the proposal of the scheme is completed by the Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, the maintenance of the scheme and the management of the scheme will be required to be done by the concerned Gram Panchayat.
10) The Divisional Commissioner, Nashik should provide 50% of the funds at the beginning of the scheme after the tender process for the said scheme. After that 30% of the funds should be distributed at the level of the Divisional Commissioner taking into account the progress of the scheme. The remaining 20% will be paid after the project is completed.
