GOVERNOR'S DECISION: In view of the facts mentioned in the foregoing, the Government of the State of Mauje Tirthapuri, (dt. Dhansavangi, G.
Jalna) by approving the technical changes in the work of the tap water supply scheme, Rs. The revised administrative approval is being granted under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme subject to the following conditions:
1) The scheme will be implemented through the District Council.
The responsibility of implementing the scheme, following the prevailing government decisions/ rules, CVC principles, financial limits, prescribed tender procedures, correcting the directions and errors given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in connection with the project and work will be the responsibility of the Zilla Parishad, Jalna.
(3) The revised administrative approval means that the technical aspects of the report, the tender, the zoning decision or the irregularity will not be considered as approval, the case will be examined by the relevant body of the government of the then prevailing rule.
4) The terms and conditions of the original administrative sanction order and the technical sanction order of the scheme should be strictly followed.
5) Guidelines issued from time to time under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Programme should be strictly followed.
According to a CAG report in 2013, 21% of the water supply schemes in the state have been shut down due to non-payment of electricity dues due to lack of maintenance. Therefore, the water tariff should be increased from time to time as per the requirement to keep the scheme financially viable.
7) After testing the scheme, the accounts should be closed by transferring the scheme to the concerned Gram Panchayat.
