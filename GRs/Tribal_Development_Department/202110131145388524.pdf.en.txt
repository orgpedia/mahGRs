The fund is being distributed on the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) and the fund should be spent on a total of 7 works as mentioned in the attached statement within the limit mentioned in Raka 5.
The disbursement of the said funds is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The Controlling Authority concerned shall ensure that the expenditure is spent only on the works included in Annexure-D of the Budget Publication and within the administrative sanction/ revised administrative limit.
2) The concerned Controlling Officer should ensure that the said funds are spent only on the 7 works mentioned in the accompanying statement.
3) The Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nashik, shall visit the said structures from time to time to inspect the quality and quality of work and to ensure that the funds disbursed are spent properly.
4) The funds are being disbursed as per the demand received through the Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal Board), Nashik/ Nagpur. Expenditures must be completed by March 31, 2022.
(5) The Controlling Officer shall compile a report of the expenditure incurred from the said fund and submit it to the Government in the prescribed form.
(6) The Controlling Officer shall be responsible for compliance of the terms and conditions prescribed in the Government Circular issued from time to time by the Finance Department.
