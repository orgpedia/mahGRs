GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In terms of the circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued by the Department of Finance, the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21 have been worked out keeping in view the pandemic situation arising out of Corona Virus in the State. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has provided funds from time to time on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Tribal Component Program under the Government Decisions here, Demand No. Under the following schemes being implemented by the Department of School Education under the head 2202-General Education in T-5, 36 Supplementary Grants (Salaries) subject to the limit of 60% of the provisions in the annual budget have been disbursed to the Department of School Education.
Now, in the month of October, funds have been made available for disbursement by the Department of Finance on Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 70% of the total budgetary allocation. Considering this, the Government is approving to disburse the remaining 10% of the funds for the following schemes under the head title 2202-General Education in Demand No. T-5 under the Tribal Component Programme to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department on the budgetary fund distribution system.
