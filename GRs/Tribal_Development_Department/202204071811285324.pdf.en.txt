Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik vide letter No. Vikesh 2015/ PR No. 51/ K. 8 (5) dt. 23.11.2021 and D. 1.12.2021 Introduction Various welfare schemes are implemented for the development of tribal community through Tribal Development Department. With a view to providing irrigation facilities to the tribal farmers and providing them the benefit of sustainable agricultural income and thereby improving their standard of living, in order to increase the agricultural income of the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes who have received forest pattas under the Forest Rights Act, 2006, and to provide water to the farm through a well/ borewell, installation of solar pump in the field of the forest pattas holders to increase their income, Bore well/ dug well with solar pump (5 hp) for irrigation of land given under FRA 2006 under the guidelines of the scheme No. 1 The above decisions were issued by the government. However, it is requested under Reference 2 that the implementation of the scheme should be at the state level and the well subsidy to the beneficiary should be revised as per the prevailing government decision of the Planning Department and the Solar Pump and Panel subsidy of the Power Department. In pursuance of the said request, the matter of issuing revised guidelines by superseding the guidelines issued under the Government decision in reference 1 in the present case was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Construction of Bore Well/ Dug Well with Solar Pump (5 HP) for irrigation of land given under FRA 2006 under Special Central Assistance to the beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes who have received forest pattas under Forest Rights Act, with a view to augmenting their income by installing solar pumps in the fields of forest patta holders. 1800.00 lakh for the implementation of the approved scheme. The revised guidelines are being approved as per Annexure No. 1 annexed to the Guidelines issued under Annexure 1 read with Government Decision dated 26.08.2021.
