To examine the requirement of furniture in government ashramshalas, to establish an ashramshala rejuvenation team to inspect and take measures for physical/ basic facilities and to take action for the improvement of ashramshalas.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Tribal Development Department
Govt. Clearance Sheet No. Shaasha-2018/ PR No.471/ K.A.13
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date is November 22, 2018.
Read- Government Circular No. Shaasha-2018/ No. 471/ K. 13D November 6, 2018.
The government has issued instructions to check the requirement of furniture in government ashramshalas, to set up ashramshala transformation team to inspect the physical/ basic facilities and take measures to improve the ashramshala. The government was considering amending Bill No. 17.
Accordingly, clause (17) of the said circular reads," The Ashramshalas assigned to the concerned officers/ employees shall be closed from 19.11.2018 to 31.02.2018. to be visited during 05.12.2018. During this visit, the furniture requirement of the ashramshala, every physical facility should be physically inspected and a plan should be made for the repairs and improvements to be made. The parameters/ subjects for the inspection to be carried out by the team in the ashramshala will be made available to the team separately. Instead of the above, in the case of Ashramshalas designated by the concerned officers/ employees vide Bill No. 17 dated 26.11.2018, the following orders have been issued: Visits should be made by 10.12.2018. During this visit, the furniture requirement of the ashramshala, every physical facility should be physically inspected and a plan should be made for the repairs and improvements to be made. The parameters/ subjects for inspection to be carried out in the ashramshala shall be made available to the team separately.
Also, wherever in this circular, there is a mention of" Government Ashramshala," it should be read as" Government Ashramshala and Hostel." (A list of accommodations is attached.)
