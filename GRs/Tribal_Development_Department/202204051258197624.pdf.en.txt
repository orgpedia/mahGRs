The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Department of Finance has made available 10% funds for the month of April on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system and 7% funds for the remaining items. Accordingly, 10% of the budgetary provisions under the scheme of programme expenditure of Tribal Development Department under item R. 1.-V.Ten and 36.-Assistant Grants (Salaries) and other official matters under item No. 3- Overtime allowance, 6- telephone, electricity and water charges, 10 contractual services, 11 travel expenses, 13 office expenses, 24- petrol, oil and fuel.
The government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. T-5 for the schemes mentioned in Rakaana No. 2 in the accompanying return as under Rakaana No. 5 in case of Batch No. 1- the salary.
The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 7% of the budgetary provisions for other office matters to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
