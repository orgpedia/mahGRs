Introduction: 1st Department of Finance. As per the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for the schemes under mandatory expenditure are to be made available for distribution on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system.
