Government's decision:
Under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of the remaining 10% of the funds as per Pillar-5 for the schemes in the accompanying prospectus in Demand Number T-9 to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the State is likely to remain very critical for some time to come due to the lockdown, the Controller General of Expenditure should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. This fund should be used for regular payment of salaries. It should not be used to pay any pending liabilities at present.
