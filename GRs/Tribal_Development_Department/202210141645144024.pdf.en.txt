Government's decision:
The Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune has been entrusted with the following works of maintenance, repair, renovation, furniture and other ancillary works: Administrative approval is being accorded to the plan and budget for an amount of Rs. 4,69,40, 165/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy-Nine Lakhs Forty-One Thousand One Hundred and Fifty Five only).
1) Construction of a third floor in an area where there are only two floors.
as well as internal repairs and renovations.
2) Construction of office furniture, partitions and virtual roofs in the building.
3) Electrification.
2. Administrative approval is being granted to the project subject to the following conditions:
Conditions
1) The detailed plans and concept drawings of the work should be prepared and detailed budgets should be prepared and technical approval of the competent authority should be obtained.
2) While working on the provision made in a rough form, the work should be undertaken by preparing a detailed budget. Also, make a budget for other things according to the plan of the place.
3) Ensure that the financial limit of the work is within the limit of the amount of administrative approval.
4) While proposing the construction of the third floor, it has been ensured on the spot that the existing original building has the capacity to bear the additional load and the building will be safe for use after the completion of the work.
5) It is assured that the building will remain in use for a considerable period after the expenditure is incurred and the works are completed.
6) Before undertaking such works, permission for the concerned works should be obtained from the Municipal Corporation/ Electricity Department etc. establishments and these should be included in the detailed budget.
