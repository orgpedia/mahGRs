The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the Finance Department issued a circular. Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) as on 12.04.2023 for the first nine months of the financial year 2023-24
The funds will be disbursed through Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System in the ratio of 20:20:30 per quarter within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation (till the end of December, 2023).
Accordingly, the Government was considering to disburse 10% of the funds available for disbursement in the budgetary allocation for the financial year 2023-24 under the scheme of Subsidiary Grants to Agricultural Universities being implemented by the Department of Agriculture under the Tribal Component Programme.
