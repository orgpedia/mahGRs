The Proposal:
A total of 288 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune under the Tribal Development Department and in the office of the eight Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committee were extended from March 1, 2018 to September 30, 2018, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1. On the expiry of the said period, in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution referred to in Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, the Government was considering the extension of a total of 288 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune and in the office of the eight Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committee.
Government's decision:
A total of 288 temporary posts mentioned in the table below in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune and in the office of the eight Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committees are being extended from October 1, 2018 to February 28, 2019.
