The proposal-
Proposals were submitted to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India from the State Government for funding under the Tribal Development Programme for the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, New Delhi convened a meeting of the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC). It was held on 5th May 2020 through video conferencing. Read the meeting here. According to the report, the central government has spent a total of Rs. 1510.00 lakh has been approved. Accordingly, the Central Government has released Rs. 753.67 lakh has been disbursed to Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Now, under the second and third tranches for the Tribal Development Programme, Reference No. 3, 4 and 5 respectively. 28.72 lakh, Rs. 497.84 lakh and Rs. 131.43 crore. 657.99 lakh was under the consideration of the Government for disbursement to the implementing agencies.
the Government Resolution
The Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India has sanctioned a total amount of Rs. 1510.00 Lakhs (Rs. 15 crore only) The total amount received under the second and third installments for the further works of this fund is Rs. 657.99 lakh in the fund table at pillar no. Government approval is hereby being granted to distribute the quantities indicated in Section 6.
