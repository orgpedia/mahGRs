Preamble For the Financial Year 2021-22 in the context of Finance Department No. Guidelines have been issued for taking financial measures by setting spending priorities under Government Circulars 1 and 2. Accordingly, as provided from time to time on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Finance Department, 30% of the salary under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation, 15% for other office matters and Rs.
31 under A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Amrut Aahar Yojana subject to a ceiling of 25% in respect of No. 3 Funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the decision of the Government.
Now, the Finance Department has made available 37% of the budgetary allocation under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme on the Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) in respect of wages and 30% for all other items. Accordingly, out of the provisions available for distribution within this limit, the matter of distribution of the remaining provision was under consideration of the Government considering the provisions distributed earlier.
Government's decision
Under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme, as mentioned in the accompanying prospectus under Demand No. T-9, T-10 and T-11, the Government is approving the distribution and expenditure of the remaining provisions mentioned in Pillar-7 to the concerned Controlling Officer and Collector, taking into account the provisions previously distributed, within the limit of 37% for salary and 30% for all other matters out of the budgetary provisions.
