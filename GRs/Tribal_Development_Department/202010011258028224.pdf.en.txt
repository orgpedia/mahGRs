The Proposal:
The scheme is implemented by the Government Ashramshala Group (Account Head 2225 D734) under the Tribal Component Programme. Under this scheme, the teachers/ employees of class-3 and class-4 in government ashramshalas are appointed on daily/ hourly/ contractual basis. However, the Department of Finance vide Government Circular dated 16th April, 2020 had directed to make provision for the same under the target of" 10 Contractual Services." Accordingly, the target of providing 10-contracted services through symbolic supplementary demand has been achieved in the Monsoon Session of the Legislature in September, 2020. The government was planning to allocate funds for the purpose.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under the Tribal Component Programme, during the financial year 2020-21, Government Ashramshala Group in Demand No. T-5 (Account Head 2225 D734) 10- Contractual Service of these schemes, an amount of Rs. 700.00 lakhs to the Controlling Officer and Commissioner, Tribal Development, State of Maharashtra.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious in the next period due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner.
