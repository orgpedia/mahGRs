Government's decision:
Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Project Officer, Nashik Division, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Dhule under Shirpur Education Society, Shirpur, Aided Ashramshala Shirpur, Dist. It is functioning at Shirpur, Dhule. gartad, Ta. Approval is being given to shift to Shirpur, District Dhule subject to the following conditions.
(a) There should be sufficient space for ashramshalas, hostels and other physical facilities at Gartad.
(b) Within one year of shifting the Ashram, the institution is required to start the construction of Ashram building, hostel building at the said place.
(c) The area should have adequate health facilities, electricity and drinking water.
d) All the students who are currently admitted should be accommodated in the Ashramshala.
e) The institution must provide the necessary physical facilities for the students at the said place as per the norms in the Ashramshala Code.
