Introduction: Under this scheme, students belonging to Scheduled Tribes are provided with a subsistence allowance and tuition fee, examination fee at the rate of monthly payment as per the guidelines issued by the Central Government from time to time. About 1,40,000 Scheduled Tribe students are benefitted annually under this scheme. The scheme is being implemented online through the state government's' MahaDBT" portal. Accordingly, Finance Department No. 2 Under the Government Circular issued herein, the Government was considering the release of funds under the scheme of Post Matric Scholarship (State Share) of Government of India under the sub-heading" Welfare of Backward Classes" in the programme expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme, in the revised estimate limit for the financial year 2020-21 under the account head 2225 D 941.
