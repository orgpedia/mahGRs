Preamble: Grants under Special Central Assistance from the Central Government, under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and under the scheme of Tribal Development Programme are received by the State Government every year. Guidelines have been issued on 02.03.2021 for implementation of Creation of PVTG Cell for Targeted Intervention under Tribal Development Programme to ensure implementation of programmes and schemes launched for tribals, setting up of separate cells with trained manpower for regular review, evaluation and monitoring, research for new schemes. The guidelines also mention about the appointment of project coordinators for Integrated Tribal Development Project, Dahanu, Jawhar, Shahapur, Pen, Nashik, Ghodegaon, Kinwat, Pandharkawada, Pusad, Dharani, Nagpur, Deori, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Chimur, Gadchiroli, Aheri and Bhamragad. However, since there is no presence of Adivasi population in the jurisdiction of the office of the Integrated Tribal Development Project, Deori, the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune, has decided to exclude the project office and include the district of Wardha in the jurisdiction of the office of the Integrated Tribal Development Project, Wardha. 2 has been sent to the government. Accordingly, the government is considering to include the office of Integrated Tribal Development Project, Wardha and exclude the office of Integrated Tribal Development Project, Deori in this scheme.
