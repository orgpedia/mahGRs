The Proposal:
1 1 under the Finance Department. As per the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory expenditure are to be made available for distribution on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system. Accordingly, at present, the Finance Department is making available the budgetary provisions of the schemes within the specified limit for disbursement on the monthly Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. As made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Finance Department, the demand under the head Backward Classes Welfare under Tribal Component Programme No. Funds to the extent of 76.54% of the budgetary provisions under the objectives of Subsidiary Grants (Programme Expenditure) (2225F129), 36-Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) in T-5 (01) (73) for running Ashramshalas and Post-Mulgodiya Ashramshalas have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Officer and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available funds to the extent of 94% of the budgeted provision for wages under the said scheme for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, considering the funds released earlier, the matter of disbursement of the remaining available funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
