the Proposal. The decision to introduce the Central Government's Pre-Secondary Scholarship Scheme in place of the Golden Jubilee Tribal Pre-Secondary Scholarship Scheme for Scheduled Tribe students studying in Class 9 and Class 10 in the state has been taken as per the decision of the Government in the context.
The government was considering opening a new separate title account for the scheme.
Government Decision: - To replace the Golden Jubilee Tribal Pre-Secondary Scholarship Scheme with the Central Government Pre-Secondary Scholarship Scheme for Scheduled Tribe students studying in Class 9th and Class 10th in the State The Government is approving the opening of a new chapter for the implementation of this scheme as follows.
