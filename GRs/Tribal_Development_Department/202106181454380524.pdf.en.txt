The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking financial measures by setting priorities for expenditure under the government circular here. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) by the Finance Department, as made available on the delivery system from time to time, have been prepared in respect of the schemes covered under the mandatory and programme expenditure of the Tribal Development Department vide No. 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) for the purpose of reference No. As per the decision of the Government, funds have been released to the concerned Controlling Authorities within the limit of 25% of the budgetary provisions at the end of the month of June, 2021.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds@ 5% of the budgetary provisions for 01- Salaries, 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on the Budget Estimates Distribution System (BEAMS) during the month of July, 2021. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the" District Annual Tribal Appropriation" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. For the purpose of 01-salary for the districts mentioned in Racket No. 2 of the accompanying return under T-9, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 5% of the increased funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Racket No. 5 of the budgetary provisions.
2) Rs. A sum of Rs. 122.3715 lakh has been disbursed on Budget Fund Distribution System. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
