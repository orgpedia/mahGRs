Proposal- In the context of the Central Government. The revised instructions for managing the fund disbursement and utilization of Centrally Sponsored Schemes have been issued vide Memorandum dated 23rd March, 2021 and accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued the revised instructions to the Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of India. The guidelines have been issued by the Government Circular dated 1st July 2021. The Department of Tribal Development has already been referred to. The Government decision dated 27.06.2017 prescribes the modalities for implementation of Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Sponsored Schemes, however, the same needs to be modified accordingly as per the revised instructions issued by the Central Government.
The Government was considering the appointment of Nodal Officer, State Nodal Agency (SNA) for Centrally Sponsored Schemes with Central Share and State Share under the schemes being implemented by the Tribal Development Department to adopt the revised methodology as per the instructions of the Central Government.

The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Post Matric Scholarship) is being declared as the Nodal Officer for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Government of India Post Matric Scholarship (Programme)" (Post Matric Scholarship) being implemented by the Tribal Development Department. The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik is being declared as the State Nodal Agency (SNA) for the implementation of the scheme.
A separate savings account should be opened in a nationalized bank for the scheme of Post Matric Scholarship for implementation of the scheme as per the revised procedure through PFMS system at the level of State Nodal Agency (SNA). It should also be mapped to the PFMS system.
3) Centrally Sponsored Scheme. Funds will be received as per the revised methodology from 01.07.2021. Accordingly, the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has issued a circular regarding the management of funds distribution and utilization of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme vide Reference No. 2 Necessary action should be taken immediately in accordance with the instructions given in the circular.
