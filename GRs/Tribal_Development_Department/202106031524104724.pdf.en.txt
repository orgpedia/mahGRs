The Proposal:
275 (1) of the Central Government and the construction of 15 cm central kitchen and 4 central kitchen for the students of government ashramshalas, hostels and Eklavya Residential School in the state has been approved with the funds of the special central agency. Accordingly, revised guidelines have been laid down for these kitchens under the relevant government decisions. The construction of these kitchens is proposed to be done through the construction cell of the Tribal Development Department with government funds. A Technical Advisory Committee was set up to provide technical assistance.
Government's decision:
275 (1) of the Central Government and the construction of 15 cm central kitchen and 4 central kitchen for the students of government ashramshalas, hostels and Eklavya Residential School in the state has been approved with the funds of the special central agency. Accordingly, revised guidelines have been laid down for these kitchens under the relevant government decisions.
Accordingly, the construction of the above mentioned 15 cm central kitchen and 4 central kitchens will be done by the Construction Cell of the Tribal Development Department.
2) A Technical Advisory Committee is being constituted to prepare the plans for the construction of these structures and to provide technical guidance for the construction works accordingly.
