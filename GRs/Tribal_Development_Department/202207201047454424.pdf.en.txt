The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month.
In this regard, under the programme expenditure of the Tribal Development Department, the establishment matter No.
21% of the budgetary provisions have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities for expenditure on 02- wages, 06- telephone, electricity and water charges, 10- contractual services, 13- office expenses and 14- lease and taxes for the month of June, 2022.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available additional 7% funds for the month of July, 2022 for disbursement on Budget Fund Distribution System. The matter of disbursement of this increased fund to the concerned controlling authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 in the accompanying statement under Demand T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
