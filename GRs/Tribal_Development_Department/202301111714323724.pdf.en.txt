The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure are to be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Finance Department is making available the budgetary provisions of the schemes within the specified limits for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month. Accordingly, the funds so far available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system for the schemes under the Programme Expenditure in Tribal Component Programme have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system in the range of 90% for salaries, 60% for office matters and 42-55% for other matters. Accordingly, the matter of disbursing the remaining incremental funds for the schemes under the programme expenditure to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 to the accompanying statement under T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
