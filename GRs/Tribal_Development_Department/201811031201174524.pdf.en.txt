Introduction: Government hostels for tribal students are constructed in tribal sub-plan and outside the sub-plan area. In Maharashtra, 502 government ashramshalas and 495 government hostels have been sanctioned under the Tribal Development Department, out of which 491 hostels are functioning. A new type plan for the construction of government ashramshalas and hostels has been approved by the department in September 2017. Accordingly, proposals for construction of buildings are being received at the regional level for administrative approval. After receiving the proposal, the technical scrutiny of the proposals by the Chief Engineer, Construction Management Cell, Tribal Development, the process of giving administrative approval to it was being done by the concerned office. Now, the government was considering to set a priority for the construction of the proposal of the tribal ashram and hostels and to give administrative approval to these proposals and to form a committee at the department level to review the pending proposals.
Government Decision: As per the new type plan for the construction of the government tribal ashram and hostel building, a committee is being formed at the department level to give administrative approval to the proposals by determining the priority and reviewing the pending proposals of the hostel.
The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:

Meetings of the Committee shall be held on the first and third Fridays of each month (as the case may be). The committee will review the proposals approved earlier on the day when the construction proposals are not available for the approval of the committee.
The committee will review the proposals received from the government for administrative approval, proposals pending at the department and government level for administrative approval, proposals pending due to lack of technical approval, progress of hostel construction, proposals pending due to lack of land, etc.
3. Proposals which have completed technical examination by the Construction Management Cell, Tribal Development should be submitted for the consideration of the Committee.
The proposal for Ashramshala and Hostels should be submitted to the Government through the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik. After examining its administrative aspects, the complete proposal should be sent to the construction room for technical examination. Thereafter, the proposal shall be placed before the said Committee for its approval and orders for administrative approval of the proposals approved by the said Committee shall be issued by Office- 5.
