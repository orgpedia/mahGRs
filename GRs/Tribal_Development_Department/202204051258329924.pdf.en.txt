The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, vide Government Circular No.1 of the Department of Finance, 10% of the budgetary allocation for the purpose of salary and 7% of the budgetary allocation for other purposes has been made available for disbursement through the Department of Finance on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS). Accordingly, 36 schemes are being implemented by the School Education Department under the Tribal Component Programme.
As per the proposal No. 2 regarding the release of funds for the purpose of Subsidiary Grants (Salaries), the matter was under consideration of the Government to be distributed to the School Education Department.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, under the year 2022-23, Demand No. 2202 in the T-5
Under the head General Education, the total amount of 10% of the annual budgetary allocation for salaries as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department is Rs. 1042.29 lakh to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
