The proposal of Sports Equipment's at Ashram Schools was presented by the department mainly for the purpose of providing various sports materials to the students of Adivasi Ashram Schools for sports and to create a special interest in sports among them.
Government Ashramshalas are run by the Tribal Development Department for the education of tribal students. The aim of the department is to provide quality education to the students studying in these government ashramshalas as well as to provide various facilities to the students to develop their talents in entertainment, personality development, sports. Apart from studies, the department has proposed to the central government a scheme called Sports Equipment's at Ashram Schools to explore and promote other talents in students, to develop a special interest in sports among them, and to develop the personality of students.
