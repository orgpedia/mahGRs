Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000.00 (Rupees Two Hundred Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand Only) has been made available for disbursement to 116 villages of 78 Gram Sabhas in Sakri Panchayat Samiti area under Dhule Zilla Parishad and 71 villages of 52 Gram Sabhas in Shirpur Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts. 485.1643/- Rs. 13,80,64, 165.35/- (Rs. 13 crore eighty lakh sixty four thousand one hundred and fifty five paise only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the attached Annexure Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to this department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Dhule.
