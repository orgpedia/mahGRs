In this budget, the government was considering to disburse funds for the physically completed/ progress works of roads and bridges in the tribal areas within the limits provided by the Finance Department on the budgetary fund distribution system.
Government's decision
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. For a total of 507 physically completed/ in progress works connected with roads and bridges in the tribal areas under the head District and Other Roads (State Level Plan) (5054 517), 53-Big Construction in T-6, the concerned Controlling Officer and Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, will have to pay Rs. 9998.30 lakh (Rupees Ninety Nine Crore Ninety Eight Lakh Thirty Thousand only) is being approved for disbursement on Budget Fund Distribution System.

The said funds have been disbursed on the budgetary allocation system and the disbursement of the funds is being approved subject to the following conditions: 1. The Controlling Authority shall ensure that the works included in the Annexure D of the Budget Estimates Part III for the year 2023-24 are spent on the works up to the year 2023-24 in the accompanying statement and within the limits of administrative approval.
2) The funds should be spent in accordance with the work as mentioned in the accompanying list, which should not be changed in any way. If the said provisions are required to be amended for some unavoidable reasons, the proposal should be submitted to the Government with detailed reasons.

As the fund is being disbursed to the 100% physically completed and in-progress works as demanded by the concerned Controlling Officer, the fund should be allocated for the 507 works.
4) A detailed proposal to exclude physically and financially completed road works from Schedule D of Budget Estimates Part-III for 2023-24 should be submitted to the Government within one month. Also, these works should be excluded from the BEAMS system and submitted to the government.
The concerned Controlling Officer shall ensure that the said provision is spent only on the works in the tribal sub-plan area as determined under the Government Decision, Tribal Development Department No. TSP- 1086/8710/ PR No. 31/ K. 5 dated 9.3.1990.
6) Also, while spending the said provision, the Government Decision, Tribal Development Department, No. TSP 2003/ Q. No. 101/ Ka-6, dated 26th July, 2004 and TSP-2005/ Q. Care should be taken to strictly comply with the conditions in No. 59/ Q-6, dated November 10, 2005.
The utilization certificate of the disbursed funds should be submitted to the government by March 31, 2024.
