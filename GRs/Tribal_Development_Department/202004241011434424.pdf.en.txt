Government's decision
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2020 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2020) for the financial year 2020-21 has been approved by the Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is in addition to the provisions of the Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, Dt. 18.03.2020 has been published.
Accordingly, vide Circular No.1 of the Department of Finance dated 16.04.2020, considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona Virus, it has been approved to disburse funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary provisions of essential and programme expenditure for the first three months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020). Also, at present, 10 per cent of the total budgetary allocation has been made available on the Budget Fund Distribution System. Accordingly, under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. The following schemes are being implemented by the Department of School Education under the main heading 2202-General Education in T-5. 2 In pursuance of the proposal herein, the Government is approving the allocation of 10% of the annual budgetary allocation to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System as made available for distribution by the Finance Department.
