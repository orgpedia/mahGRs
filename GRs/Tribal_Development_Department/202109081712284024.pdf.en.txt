The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking fiscal measures by setting spending priorities as per the decisions of the government. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System from time to time, funds to the extent of 50% of the budgetary provision for the salary component of the schemes covered under the mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department, subject to the provisions of the Reference No. 2 has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the decision of the Government.
Now, at the end of September, the Department of Finance has decided to implement the mandatory expenditure plans as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System under Chapter No.
01- The matter of disbursement of increased allocation for salaries within the limit of 10 percent of the budgeted allocation was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the budgetary allocation to the concerned Controlling Authority as per Rakaan No. 5 for the schemes in Rakaan No. 2 of the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the terms and conditions of the state government.
(3) The Government shall take such decision within the limits and subject to the reference made by the Finance Department on the budgetary allocation system. The powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Decision dated 24.06.2021 are being issued.
