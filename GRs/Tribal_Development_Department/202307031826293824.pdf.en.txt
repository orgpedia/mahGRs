The Proposal:
The Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006, Rules, 2008 and Amendments Rules, 2012 are being strictly implemented to implement the Vanamitra Scheme for time-bound settlement of forest rights claims and appeals. 2 The instructions have been issued by the government circular here. Under this, the forest area in the individual forest rights claims approved by the district level forest rights committee is being processed by the Land Records Department, for which the necessary funds are made available to the concerned collector as a" nodal agency" through the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. In pursuance of the demand made by the concerned District Collector for the contractual manpower and office expenses appointed for the implementation of the Forest Rights Act in the State, the Commissioner, Tribal and Research and Training Institute, Pune has sanctioned Rs. 558.28 lakh is proposed to be provided to the Finance Department vide reference No. 4 were presented.
On this proposal, the Finance Department has approved the release of funds for this scheme within the limit of 20 percent of the budgeted allocation. The matter of disbursement of funds in accordance with the above approval of the Finance Department was under consideration of the Government.
