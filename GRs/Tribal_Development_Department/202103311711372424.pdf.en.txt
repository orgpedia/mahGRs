The preamble-
The proposal for the financial year 2020-21 was submitted to the Central Government for getting funds under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India. In pursuance of these proposals, the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, New Delhi held a meeting of the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC). It was held on 5th May 2020 through video conferencing. Reference No. 1 As per the aforesaid history, the proposals approved in 2019-20 but due in 2020-21 and the new proposals for 2020-21 have been approved by the Central Government.
Central Government Read No. 2 and No. 3 Under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India, for the implementation of the schemes sanctioned in the year 2019-20 but which are due for the year 2020-21, Rs. 4573.16 lakh has been disbursed. Accordingly, for the year 2020-21, Rs. 2171.35 lakh to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
