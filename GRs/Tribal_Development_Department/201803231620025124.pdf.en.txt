Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement to 129 villages of 92 Gram Sabhas in the area of Kinwat Panchayat Samiti under Nanded Zilla Parishad and 49 villages of 32 Gram Sabhas in the area of Mahur Panchayat Samiti in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts. 485.1643/- Rs. 3,21,31, 944.14/ (Rs. Three crore twenty one lakh thirty one thousand nine hundred and forty four paise only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to this department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nanded.
