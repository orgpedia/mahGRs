The Proposal:
The students of Government Ashramshalas and Hostels should study the DBT policy for personal use items and stationery, books etc. as well as for the students of hostels at the district, division and municipal levels. 1 The formation of the study group has been approved by the government decision here. Reference No. The study group was given a one-month extension as per the decision of the government.
Considering the fact that the work of the study group has now been completed and it will take another 15 days to complete the report and submit it to the government, the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune, has issued his Reference No. 4 Let the Study Group submit its report in pursuance of the proposal made hereunder. The matter of extension up to 31.10.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The students of Government Ashramshalas and Hostels should study the DBT policy for providing food to the students in the hostels at the district, division and municipal levels for personal use items and stationery, books etc.
