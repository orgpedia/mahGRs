The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System is being made available for distribution of funds in a limited manner through the Finance Department through Government Circular No. 1 in the context of Finance Department. Under the schemes being implemented by the Department of School Education under the Tribal Component Programme as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Department through the Finance Department, 50% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) have been released to the Department of Tribal Affairs vide Notification No. 2 has been distributed to the School Education Department as per the government decision here.
Now, a total of 60% of the budgetary allocation has been made for the 36 Auxiliary Grants (Salaries) target for disbursement through the Finance Division. Accordingly, an increase of 10% is proposed to be made in the fund allocation. 3 The matter of disbursing funds to the School Education Department in accordance with the proposal here was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, under the year 2022-23, Demand No. 2202 General Education in T-5, taking into account the devolved provisions within the limit of 50% of the provisions in the annual budget for salaries as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department under the head of General Education, Rs. 1042.29500 Lakhs (Rs.
