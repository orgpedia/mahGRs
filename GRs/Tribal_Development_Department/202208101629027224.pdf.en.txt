Preamble: In the context of Finance Department No. 1 here. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system.
Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month.
Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) as made available for distribution on the distribution system under the Programme Expenditure of the Tribal Development Department under the Scheme of Scheduled Tribes Certification Scrutiny Committee (2225 4122) has been made available under the Scheme vide Chapter No. 10- Contractual services; 13- Office expenses for which 35% of the budgetary provisions are allocated to the fund under reference No. 2 The Tribal Research and Training Institute has been allotted to the concerned Controlling Officer and Commissioner as per the Government decisions.
At present, 8 Scheduled Tribe Certificate Scrutiny Committees are functioning in the state and the process of starting the work of 7 new committees is underway. It is imperative that the functioning of the Scheduled Tribes Certificate Validity Check Committee under the Department continues on a regular basis and these Committees have been specifically assigned additional functions/ objectives at the beginning of the academic year. The Finance Department has agreed to disburse funds to the extent of 49% of the budgetary allocation under the scheme for the purpose of providing 10 contractual services and 13 office expenses. The matter of disbursing the remaining funds within this limit was under the consideration of the government.
