PROGRAMME Under the Special Central Assistance Scheme, PILOT Project of Developing Solar Ashram SCHOOL is being implemented at a cost of Rs. 500.00 lakh has been approved as per the Government Resolution vide Letter No. 1.Y below.
2. Instructions have been given to implement the scheme at the level of concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, as per the decision of the concerned Government. But since the scheme is state-wide, in order to have a uniform procedure in the entire state while implementing the scheme, the present plan should be implemented at the level of Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik. Proposal 2 has been submitted. The proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the revised guidelines for the implementation of the PILOT Project of DEVELOPING SOLAR ASHRAM SCHOOL at the level of Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
The government has decided to provide a total of Rs. 500.00 lakh for implementation of" PILOT Project of DEVELOPING SOLAR ASHRAM SCHOOL" and the revised guidelines for implementation at the level of Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik are being approved as per Annexure No. 1 annexed herewith.
2. The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik shall take immediate action to implement the scheme within the limits of the provisions distributed as per the guidelines of the said scheme and shall take care to submit its utilization certificate along with financial and physical report to the Government.
