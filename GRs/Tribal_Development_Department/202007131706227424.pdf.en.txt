Governing Decision 25.06.2020:
Finance Department No. In a circular dated 16th April, 2020, it has been approved to disburse funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation for the first 3 months of the financial year 2020-21 for the schemes of essential and programme expenditure, taking into account the pandemic situation in the State.
Commissioner, Tribal Development No. As per the letter, the government had received a proposal to provide funds for the financial year 2020-21 under the Nucleus Budget Scheme to take necessary measures in view of the situation arising out of Corona virus and Cyclone Nisarga in the state. In pursuance of the above proposal, the Government is approving the release of 20% of the budgeted amount of Rs. 50.00 crore to the Controller General and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik under Demand Number T-5 under Tribal Component Programme (01) (34)-Tribal Sub Plan Area (2225 1704), 50 Other Expenditure during the financial year 2020-21.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to remain critical for some time to come due to the lockdown, the Controller should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. This fund should be used to take necessary measures as per local needs to overcome the situation arising due to Corona virus and natural cyclone in the state.
