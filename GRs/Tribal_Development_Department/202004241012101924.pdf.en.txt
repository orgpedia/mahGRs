Government's decision
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2020 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2020) for the financial year 2020-21 has been approved by the Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is in addition to the provisions of the Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, Dt. 18.03.2020 has been published.
Accordingly, Finance Department No. In a circular dated 16th April, 2020, it has been approved to disburse funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation of essential and programme expenditure for the first three months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020), considering the pandemic situation in the State. Accordingly, under the Tribal Component Programme, 10% of the total budgetary allocation for the specific objectives mentioned in the Government Circular in the context of Finance Department for the schemes under the sub-development head" Welfare of Backward Classes" in the programme expenditure demand number T-5 has been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. At present, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement on Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 20% of the total budgetary allocation.
In view of this, the Government is approving the release and utilization of the remaining 10% of the available funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities for disbursement of the objectives of 01-Salary and 36-Support Grants (Salary) for the schemes in Demand No. T-5 mentioned in the accompanying statement under the Programme Expenditure.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious for the next 3 to 4 months due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds in this distribution. This fund should be used for regular payment of salaries. It should not be used to pay any pending liabilities at present.
