Government's decision:
275 (1) of the Central Government and special central funds have been approved for the construction of 15 cm central kitchen and 4 central kitchen for the students of government ashramshalas, hostels and Eklavya Residential School in the state. Accordingly, reference No. 2 The revised guidelines are set out in the decision here. Reference No. The administrative approval has been granted here under the Government Memorandum dated 04.03.2022 and the construction is being done by the Construction Cell of the Tribal Development Department with the funds of the State Government. The work is in progress and in the final stages.
There are 497 Government Ashramshalas functioning under the Tribal Development Department and about 2,00,000 students are studying in them. The government is providing free education, boarding, lodging, uniforms and food to the students. At present, the central kitchens under 5 projects namely Integrated Tribal Development Project, Dahanu, Nashik, Jawhar, Nandurbar and Ghodegaon are providing breakfast, two meals and snacks to about 78000 students of government ashramshalas in their area. Except the government ashramshalas attached to the said central kitchens, meals are being prepared in other government ashramshalas with the help of cooks, maids, for which necessary food grains, pulses and other groceries, spices etc. are being procured and supplied through e-tendering. The students of the above Government Tribal Ashramshala will also be provided breakfast, lunch and dinner through the central kitchen as mentioned above.
