Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the scheme (PESA 5% Direct Funding Scheme to Gram Panchayats) to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications every year to Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement to 73 villages of 31 Gram Sabhas in Jivti Panchayat Samiti area, 59 villages of 33 Gram Sabhas in Rajura Panchayat Samiti area, 63 villages of 30 Gram Sabhas in Korpana Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts. 485.1643/- Rs. 2,69,98, 421.04/- (Rs. Two crore seventy nine lakh ninety eight thousand four hundred and twenty one four paise only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise.
The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to this department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Chandrapur.
