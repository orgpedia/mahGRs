The Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer shall ensure compliance of the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Powers Rulebook and the rules and regulations in respect of the Finance Department while spending the said provisions. (3) The report of expenditure incurred by the Controlling Authority out of the provision made available, information on the material objective achieved in terms of headings/ sub-headings, utilization of funds, etc.
The information should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the sub-plan area.
