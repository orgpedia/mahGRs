The Proposal:
The Central Government's decision on the Seventh Central Pay Commission
The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary to recommend implementation of revised pay scales for Government employees and other eligible employees. The government has decided to accept the recommendations of the committee. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2018 regarding the implementation of the revised pay structure for full-time teaching and non-teaching staff of government schools along with other state government employees with effect from January 1, 2016, have been notified vide Reference No. 1 has been notified. Also Reference No. As per Circular No. 2, a detailed procedure has been prescribed for determination of initial salary and payment of arrears. Accordingly, the employees have been directed to pay the allowable arrears for the period from 1st January, 2016 to 31st December, 2018 in 5 equal installments over a period of 5 years from the financial year 2019-20.
However, in view of the problems arising out of the COVID-19 pandemic in the State and its adverse impact on the State's revenue collection, it has been decided by the Government vide Decision dated 23.06.2020 of the Department of Finance to defer the payment of instalment due as on 1st July, 2020 by one year to the State Government employees and other eligible employees and all categories of pensioners. In view of this, it is decided to defer the payment of second installment due to the teaching and non-teaching staff of private aided schools in the state on July 1, 2020.
