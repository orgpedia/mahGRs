Government Resolution dated July 13, 2022
Preamble: The Central Government has decided to introduce Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY) to provide integrated economic and social development based on the concept of model village to the villages having Scheduled Tribe population by changing the format of the Special Central Assistance Scheme. The scheme aims to address the gaps in various sectors such as education, health care, infrastructure, livelihood, etc., in line with other flagship schemes of the Central and State Governments. Directive guidelines have been issued by the Central Government under Read No. 1 letters for the implementation of the scheme.
Now, the matter of taking a new accounting title for the use of the funds of this scheme is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
For the implementation of the 100% centrally sponsored scheme of Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Yojana, the following separate heads of accounts are being approved by the Government.
