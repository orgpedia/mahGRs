Government Decision Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Lavagad Scheme for the year 2019-20 for the Scheduled Tribe category Demand No. T-5, 2401, Crop Protection, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, Scheme under State Schemes, (01 (36) Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Lavgarh Scheme, Account Head (2401A898) Rs. However, since the period of planting the fruit tree branches/ saplings in the financial year starts from June, July, in the context of the Department of Agriculture No. 2 In accordance with the proposal made hereunder, under the head of Accounts, Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Lavgarh Scheme (2401A898), an amount of Rs. 490.00 lakhs (Rupees Four Crores, Ninety Lakhs only) to Secretary (Agriculture), Department of Agriculture& Farmers Welfare, Ministry of Agriculture& Farmers Welfare, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department, is being sanctioned on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government Policy on Expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area as well as in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the sub-plan area.
