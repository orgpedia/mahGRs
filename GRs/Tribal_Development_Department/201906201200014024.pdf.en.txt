Introduction: The Government of India and the Government of Maharashtra are celebrating the 125th birth anniversary of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar in a big way. The scheme aims to create 1.25 lakh SC/ ST entrepreneurs and 1.25 lakh women entrepreneurs in the next 3 years.
The central government has made a financial provision for this and the amount has been transferred to SIDBI. SIDBI has created a security cover of this amount and SIDBI will guarantee the loan that will be given to the beneficiaries. Under the scheme, the beneficiary will have to contribute 25 per cent of the total project cost as his/ her contribution. It was decided to provide margin money by implementing the above scheme in the state. taken in the Cabinet.
Accordingly, the government has decided to provide margin money to the members of the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist community. Issued on March 8, 2018, the government was considering providing 15 per cent margin money to the eligible beneficiaries of the scheme.

Government Resolution No: AVIMA-2019/ No.18/ Q.3
Government Decision: In the absence of the ability to pay the margin money of the new entrepreneur beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Tribe category, who are eligible to take the concessions of the Scheduled Tribes of the State of Maharashtra under the Stand Up India scheme of the Central Government, by providing a maximum of 15 percent margin money to these new entrepreneur beneficiaries out of 25 percent of the total project cost.
Front-end subsidy is being approved.
Under the scheme, the remaining 15 per cent of the Front End Subsidy will be provided by the State Government after the entrepreneur from the Scheduled Tribe category who is eligible to avail the concessions in the State of Maharashtra pays 10 per cent of the self-interest and the bank approves 75 per cent of the loan to the applicant under the Stand Up India Scheme.
1.1 The benefit of this scheme will be given to the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribe concession in the state who are eligible under the Stand Up India Scheme.
1.2 The concerned beneficiaries are required to submit the demand letter for grant in favour of the Managing Director, Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation, Nashik in the return prescribed at Annexure' A'. Along with the application form, an Aadhaar card and a passport size photograph should also be enclosed.
1.3 The loan sanction letter of the bank must be submitted to the Managing Director, Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation, Nashik.
1.4 The said amount should be disbursed by the Managing Director, Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation, Nashik to the loan account of the beneficiaries through NEFT/ RTGS within 15 days.
Recently, the District Treasury Office of Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation, Nashik has opened a self-financing accounts (PLA) account. 8629 and Scheme No. This is 84430081. The Managing Director, Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation Limited, Nashik will act as the Administrator of the SWAYAPANJI Account.
The funds remaining in the self-financing accounts (PLA) in the existing District Treasury Office, Nashik with the Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation should be disbursed for this scheme.
4. The Government hereby approves to deposit the subsidy amount received by Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation from time to time in the existing Self Provident Account (PLA) and withdraw the amount of such subsidy.
