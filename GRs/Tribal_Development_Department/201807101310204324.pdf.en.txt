The Government of Maharashtra
the Tribal Development Department
Government Sanction Letter No: Project Committee-2016/ S. 121/ KA-3 (Part-3)
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 10th of July 2018
Read: Government Decision No. Project Committee-2016/ P.S. 121/ K-3, Dy. 26th March, 2018.
Government's stand:
As per the decision of the Government, Chairman and Non-official members were appointed to the Project Level (Planning) Committees to review the schemes/ programmes in the tribal sub-plan areas in the tribal development projects in the tribal districts of the State.
The Government was contemplating to make changes in the appointments of Chairperson and Non-official members made to the Project Level (Planning) Committees to review the schemes/ programmes in the tribal sub-plan areas in the tribal development projects in the tribal districts of the State.
It was also contemplated to appoint new chairpersons and non-official members to the Project Level (Planning) Committees in Mumbai and Solapur under the Tribal Development Project Area.
Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions, namely, change of Chairpersons and Non-Official Members on certain Project Level (Planning Review) Committees appointed to review the schemes/ programmes in the Tribal Project Area and fresh appointment of Chairpersons and Non-Official Members on Project Level (Planning) Committees in Mumbai and Solapur under the Tribal Development Project Area.
