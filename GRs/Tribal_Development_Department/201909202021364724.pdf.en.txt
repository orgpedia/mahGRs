Guidelines for implementation of various schemes approved by the Central Government for Special Central Assistance 2013-14 have been approved under Government Resolution No. 1. In these guidelines, the condition number 8 (6) and 9 (6) of both the schemes, namely, imparting training to the youth of SC No.-5 through Prabodhan Multi-Purpose Institute, Nashik and imparting training to the youth of ST No. 10 through Office Automation Course through Infosoft Computer, Nashik, is being relaxed as a one-time special case to pay the remaining 20% amount to the institution after the institution allots the certificate/ university certificate of NCVT/ similar institution of the government to all the trainees respectively. This will not be a precedent.
