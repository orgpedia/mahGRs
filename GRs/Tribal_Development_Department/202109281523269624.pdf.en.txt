the prelude
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking financial measures by setting spending priorities as per the decisions of the government. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) by the Department of Finance, as made available from time to time on the Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Government decisions here, funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities to the extent of 50% of the funds for the salary under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available 60% of the budgetary allocation under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme (DAAP) on the Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) by the end of September 2021. Accordingly, out of the provisions available for distribution in this limit, the matter of distribution of the remaining 10% provision was under consideration of the Government, considering the provisions distributed earlier.
Government's decision
Considering the provision made earlier out of the budgetary provisions for the subject of wages for the district in the accompanying statement under District Annual Tribal Component Programme under Demand No. T-9, the Government is approving the distribution and expenditure of 10% of the budgetary provisions mentioned in Pillar-5 to the concerned Controlling Officer and Collector.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
