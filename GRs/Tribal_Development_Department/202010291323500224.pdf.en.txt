Preamble: The Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 has come into force in the State with effect from 31.12.2007 and the rules framed thereunder have come into force with effect from 01.01.2008. Rule 9 of the Act provides for constitution of a State Level Controlling Committee by the State Government for proper implementation of the Act in a time bound manner. According to reference no. 2 As per the decision of the government here, a state-level senior management committee was formed at the state level. In this committee, as per the provision of rule 9 (g), the nomination of three Scheduled Tribes members of the State Tribal Advisory Council is to be done by the Chairman of the Tribal Advisory Council. A new cabinet has been formed. According to reference no. 3 The Maharashtra State Tribal Advisory Council has been reconstituted as per the decision of the Government here. Therefore, the state government was considering reconstituting the said committee by including three Scheduled Tribes members of the Maharashtra State Tribal Advisory Council in the state-level control committee.
Government's decision:
As per Rule 9 of the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 and Rules, 2008 Amendments Rules, 2012, the Government is reconstituting the State Level Senior Management Committee as follows:
