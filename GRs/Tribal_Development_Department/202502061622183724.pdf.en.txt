The Proposal: -
Hostels for ST students are being run in the state by the Tribal Development Department. In these hostels, 3% of the seats are reserved for blind and disabled students as per the Government Decision dated 3rd August, 2004. The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (49 of 2016) has been passed by the Central Government vide Notification dated 28th December, 2016 for Persons with Disabilities. As per the provisions of the said Acts, a provision has been made to reserve 5% seats for students with disabilities in government schemes as well as in educational institutions. In view of this provision, the Scheduled Tribes in the State
The government was considering reserving 5 per cent of the seats in hostels run by the Tribal Development Department for students with disabilities.
The Government's Decisions:
Taking into consideration the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Government is approving the reservation of 5% seats for students with disabilities in hostels run by the Tribal Development Department in the State for ST students.
