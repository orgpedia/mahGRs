The proposal of Recreation Rooms for Government Hostels was introduced mainly to provide facilities for recreation, personality development, sports, besides studies to the students of Government Hostels.
Hostels are run by the Tribal Development Department for the purpose of providing accommodation for the education of tribal students. The aim of the department is to provide quality education to the students residing in these government hostels along with various facilities to promote the talents of the students in entertainment, personality development, sports. The scheme of Recreation Rooms for Government Hostels was proposed to the Central Government with a view to explore and develop other qualities in students besides studies, thereby developing the personality of the students, providing facilities for the students in the hostels through the department.
