The disbursement of the said funds is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The Controlling Authority concerned shall ensure that the expenditure on the above mentioned works is within the limit of administrative/ revised administrative sanction amount.
2) The concerned Controlling Authority shall ensure that the said funds are spent on the above mentioned works only.
3) The Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nagpur shall visit the said structures from time to time to inspect the quality and quality of work and to ensure that the funds disbursed are spent properly.
The funds disbursed must be spent by the end of March 31, 2020.
(5) The Controlling Officer shall compile a report of the expenditure incurred out of the said fund and submit it to the Government in the prescribed form.
The Controlling Officer will be responsible for complying with the terms and conditions prescribed in the Government Circular issued from time to time by the Finance Department.
