The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Development Department, the roads and bridges under the Tribal Utilization Scheme are taken up by the respective District Councils under the head 3054 2722 of the scheme.
During the Budget Session 2018-19, a total of 672 works involving a total cost of Rs. 11885.48 lakh for roads and bridges under headings 3054 2722 were included in the Budget Statements (New Subjects). A report was sought from the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, as to whether these works are in the tribal sub-plan area or not. According to the report, a total of 62 roads and bridges have been constructed outside the tribal sub-plan area. According to the government, a total of 69 road and bridge works, including 5 works proposed to be cancelled by the Zilla Parishad, Nashik, and 2 works in Nashik district, were under consideration.
