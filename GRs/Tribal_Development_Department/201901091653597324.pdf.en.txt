Regarding disbursement of 5% of funds of tribal utilization for the year 2018-2019 to Gram Panchayats in Chikhaldara, Dharani Panchayat Samitis under Amravati Zilla Parishad in Scheduled Areas.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Tribal Development Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. PESA-2018/ PR No. 380/2/ K. 17
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Road
Ministry, Extension Building, Mumbai- 400032
Date: January 9th, 2019
Read:- 1. Tribal Development Department, S.L.No.Pesa-2015/ S.No.19/ K.A-17, Dy. 21.04.2015.
Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only) has been made available for disbursement to 151 villages of 52 Gram Sabhas in the area of Chikhaldara Panchayat Samiti under Amravati Zilla Parishad and 150 villages of 62 Gram Sabhas in the area of Dharani Panchayat Samiti in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts. 424.2108/- Rs. 9,59,16, 604.17/- (Rs. Nine crores, sixty-nine lakhs, sixteen thousand, six hundred and four, seventeen paise only)
This amount is being made available directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to the department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati.
