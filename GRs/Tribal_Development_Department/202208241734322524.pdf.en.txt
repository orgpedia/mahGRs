The Proposal:
Finance Department No. 1 here. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Finance Department is presently making available the budgetary provisions of the schemes within the limits of the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) per month for distribution on the distribution system. Accordingly, for some of the schemes under Programme Expenditure in Tribal Component Programme, so far, funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the purpose of 01-Salary and 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) subject to 55% ceiling.
Now, by the end of the month of August, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement funds within the limit of 60% of the budgetary provisions for disbursement of funds towards" Salaries" on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse the remaining funds available to the concerned Controlling Authorities within the limit of 5% for the purpose of 01-Salary and 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) of the schemes under the Programme Expenditure.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 in the accompanying statement under Demand T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
