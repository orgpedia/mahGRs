The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month. Accordingly, for some of the schemes under Programme Expenditure in Tribal Component Programme, so far, funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the approval of 32% limit for the purpose of 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary).
Now at the end of the month of June, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement the enhanced funds within the limit of 50% of the budgetary provisions for disbursement of funds towards" Salaries" on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the extent of 18% to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the purpose of 01-Salary and 36-Support Grants (Salary) of the schemes under the Programme Expenditure.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 in the accompanying statement under Demand T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
