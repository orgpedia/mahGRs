Introduction: A separate construction management cell under the Tribal Development Department for the construction and maintenance of government ashramshalas and hostels. It was established by the Government Decision dated 14.07.2016. It is seen that the functioning of the departments and sub-departments is adversely affected due to the large number of vacancies of deputy engineers in the departments and sub-divisions under the construction management cell working under the Tribal Development Department. Also, since the power to fill these posts is under the Public Works Department and the Public Works Department also does not have enough manpower, these posts are vacant for a long time. General Administration Department As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 17.12.2016, the following Committee under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has recommended for selection of retired officers for the post of Deputy Director. Accordingly, the government was considering the appointment of retired deputy engineers on contract basis for a period of one year for the implementation of works under the construction management cells.
