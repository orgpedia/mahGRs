Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the scheme (PESA 5% Direct Funding Scheme to Gram Panchayats) to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications every year to Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000.00 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) is available for disbursement, so that 177 villages of 88 Gram Sabhas in Dahanu Panchayat Samiti area under Palghar Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas of the State, 61 villages of 41 Gram Sabhas in Talasari Panchayat Samiti area, 171 villages of 108 Gram Sabhas in Palghar Panchayat Samiti area, 76 villages of 64 Gram Sabhas in Vasai Panchayat area, 56 villages of 27 Gram Sabhas in Mokhada Panchayat area, 108 villages of 50 Gram Sabhas in Jawhar Panchayat area, 168 villages of 84 Gram Sabhas in Wada Panchayat area, 93 villages of 42 Gram Sabhas in Vikramgad Panchayat area can be transferred to their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts at Dahanu, Talasari, Palghar, Vasai, etc. 485.1643/ Rs. 47,52,48, 963.38/- (Rs. forty-seven crore fifty-two lakh forty-eight thousand nine hundred and thirty-eight paisa only) is mentioned in the Annexure attached to the fund, gram sabha wise
