Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only) is available for disbursement, so that 73 villages of 31 Gram Sabhas in the area of Jivti Panchayat Samiti under Chandrapur Zilla Parishad, 59 villages of 33 Gram Sabhas in the area of Rajura Panchayat Samiti, 63 villages of 30 Gram Sabhas in area of Korpana Panchayat Samiti have been provided Rs. 424.2108/- Rs. 2,36,06, 481.87/- (Rs. Two crore, thirty six lakh, six thousand, four hundred and eighty one, eighty seven rupees only) is being made available directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the attached Annexure Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. as well as the use of available funds
The certificate should be submitted to the department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Chandrapur.
