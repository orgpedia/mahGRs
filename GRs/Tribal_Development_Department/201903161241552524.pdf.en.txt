Government's decision:
Mahurkund and Nagalwadi, Minor Projects, T.T., under Water Resources Department under Tribal Component Programme, 2018-19 Rs. 166.35 lakh and Rs. Administrative approval has been granted for works amounting to Rs. 348.58 lakh under the Government Resolution No.2 in the context.
Under the Tribal Component Programme, in the financial year 2018-19, Demand No. T-5,2702-Small Irrigation, 01-Surface Water, 796-Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01) (04)- Voluntary Grants (Extension& Improvement), 33-Finance Assistance. A sum of Rs. 150.00 lakh has been budgeted in the revised estimates.
Under the Water Resources Department No. 4 As per the proposal mentioned herein, under the Tribal Component Programme, Mahurkund and Nagalwadi, Small Projects, Tt. Under the Tribal Component Programme for the special repair and restoration works of the projects of Saoner, G. Nagpur, Demand No. T-5,2702- Minor Irrigation, 01- Surface Water, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01) (04)- Voluntary Grants (Extension& Improvement), 33 Grants-in-aid amounting to Rs. 100.00 lakhs and Rs. 50.00 lakhs out of total Rs. 150.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) to the Principal Secretary, Water Resources Department, Ministry of Water Resources, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government Policy on Expenditure. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, utilization certificate of funds, material objectives achieved as per head/ sub-heading, etc.
The information should be sent to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, Tribal Development Department and Water Resources Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area as well as on the schemes outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can monitor the expenditure on schemes in tribal areas and outside the area of application.
