Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the scheme (PESA 5% Direct Funding Scheme to Gram Panchayats) to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications every year to Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement to 151 villages of 52 Gram Sabhas in the Amravati Zilla Parishad and 150 villages of 62 Gram Sabhas in the Dharani Panchayat Samiti area of the State. 485.1643/- Rs. 10,96,98, 551.41/- (Rs. Ten crore nine lakh ninety eight thousand five hundred one hundred and forty one paisa only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to this department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati.
