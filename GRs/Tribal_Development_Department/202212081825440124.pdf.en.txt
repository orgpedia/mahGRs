2) As mentioned above, Rs. 9300.00 lakhs (Rupees Ninety Three Crores only) is being disbursed on Budget Distribution System to Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik as Controlling Officer. They should immediately disburse the budgetary allocation to the Director, State Management Cell- Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Rural Housing Office and Deputy Director, State Management Cell (Rural Housing) as the implementing officer of the scheme.
(3) In the exercise of the said provision, the financial rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rules Book, the Financial Powers Rules Book, as well as in the reference to the Finance Department, shall apply. The responsibility of strictly following the directions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022 will be the responsibility of the concerned administrative office.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. Accordingly, the report of the expenditure incurred, information about the material goal achieved account-wise/ sub-account-wise, fund utilization certificate, etc. should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month.
5. It shall be the responsibility of the concerned office to ensure that only the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Tribes are benefited from the provisions made available under the Tribal Component Programme.
