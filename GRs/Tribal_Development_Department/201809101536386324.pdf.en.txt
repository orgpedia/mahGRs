The works mentioned above should be carried out only after ensuring that the works have not been carried out from any other fund and the process of adjustment/ closure of the said Ashramshalas is not started. The concerned project officer will be responsible for ensuring that no work is left undone.
The budget includes furniture (5%), electrification (2%), water supply and sanitation (2%) and Goods and Services Tax (18%).
The tendering process for these works should be done in accordance with the prevailing procedures/ rules. Also, the financial and physical report of the utility certificate related to the construction should be submitted to the government in the prescribed form.
After the completion of the work, the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project and the concerned Public Works (Tribal) Department will be responsible for uploading the video/ photographs on the website of the department and the Central Government and geo-tagging the work.
After the completion of the said works, a board should be put up at the work place regarding the completion of the said works through the central scheme and the work should be evaluated through the third party system.
