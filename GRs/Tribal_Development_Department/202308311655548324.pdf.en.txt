Introduction: The state government gets funds from the central government for the implementation of various central schemes. The 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) on administrative cost to State/ UT for implementation of the scheme has been launched from 2023-24. The guidelines for the scheme have been issued by the central government. The government is now considering approving a new accounting title for the implementation of the scheme.
Government's decision
The Government is approving separate accounting for 100% centrally sponsored scheme of administrative cost to State/ UT for implementation of the scheme as follows.
