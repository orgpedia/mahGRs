Under the National Food Security Mission (NFSA), from 2018-19, two schemes namely, Mission 1 (Oilseeds) and Mission 3 (Tree Oilseeds) have been included. 5 As per the proposal here, the Agriculture Department has demanded central share for this scheme. Under the headings Account Head (2401 A557), 33-Finance Assistance (for Sugarcane& Oilseeds) for the Central share of the scheme, Rs. 49.188 lakh (Rupees Nineteen Fifty Lakhs, Eighteen Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being sanctioned to the Secretary (Agriculture), Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development, Ministry of Agriculture, Dairy Development, Mumbai, as Head of Administrative Department, for disbursement and expenditure on Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). The required State share for the same should be provided from the District Annual Tribal Appropriation.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government Policy on Expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
The expenditure incurred by the Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal component programme area shall be recorded separately.
