Preamble: The Government provides maintenance grant, building rent grant and contingency grant to the aided Ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department. Accordingly, under the Tribal Component Programme, during the year 2023-24, grant-in-aid (Programme Expenditure) to NGOs for running Ashramshalas and Post-Mulgodiya Ashramshalas (Head of Accounts 2225F129), 31-assistant grant (non-salaried) of Rs. Rs 350.00 crore has been allocated for this.
In the academic year 2023-24, to provide necessary facilities to the students of aided ashramshalas, Rs. A proposal seeking Rs 87.50 crore has been submitted to the Finance Department. The proposal was under the consideration of the government to disburse funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
