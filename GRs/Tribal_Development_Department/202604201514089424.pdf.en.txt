The Proposal: -
The Department of Finance (DoF) has issued a notification regarding the disbursement of funds for the financial year 2026-27. The instructions have been circulated through the government circular of 09.04.2026. According to the instructions in the circular, the budgeted funds for this financial year 2026-27 under the Tribal Component Programme are to be disbursed under Budget Estimates, Allocations and Control Woes TEX (BEAMS) for the first nine months (end-December, 2026) with a ceiling of 60%. At present, the Department of Finance provides for budgetary projections, allocations, and regulatory WTS (BEAMS) disbursements within a limit of 60 per cent of the budgeted allocations for mandatory expenditure schemes under the Tribal Component Programme.
Accordingly, for the schemes of compulsory expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme, the Government Decision No. 2 in the Reference No. 01 - Salary and 02 - Remaining available provisions in the limit of 60%, excluding the provisions distributed for wage matters, and for the schemes in the T-03 (Other Social Security and Welfare Programme-104, Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme - Government Provident Fund - (00) (01) - Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme for subscribers of the Provident Fund), the 100% fund shall be provided to the Controlling Officer concerned.
The issue of distribution was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Under the "Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. For the schemes mentioned in the accompanying returns under T-2, T-3, T-4 and T-08, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per ROCAN No.7.
The funds have been disbursed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The funds should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Care should be taken by the concerned to comply with the working rules, financial rules, provisions and conditions of the State Government in the Budget Manual while spending these funds.
