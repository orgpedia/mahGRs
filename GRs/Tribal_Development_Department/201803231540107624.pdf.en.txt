Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the scheme (PESA 5% Direct Funding Scheme to Gram Panchayats) to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications every year to Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement to 166 villages of 151 Gram Sabhas in Akole Panchayat Samiti area under Ahmednagar Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas of the State. 485.1643/- Rs. 4,38,80, 196.83/- (Rs. Four crore thirty eight lakh eighty eight thousand one hundred and nine thirty three paisa only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to the department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar.
