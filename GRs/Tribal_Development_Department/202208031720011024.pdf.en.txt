Introduction: Under the Tribal Component Programme, as per the State Training Policy, every year, foundation training for newly inducted officers/ employees in all cadres of the Tribal Development Department, post-promotion training for promoted officers/ employees, revision training at various stages of service and orientation programmes are organized regularly by the Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. Sadar
