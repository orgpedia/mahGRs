Government Resolution under Tribal Component Programme Demand No. T-5, 2217, Urban Development, 80-General, 796 Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) (05) Financial Assistance to Tribals in Urban Areas in Urban Local Bodies under Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Maha Abhiyan (2217A065) In the financial year 2018-19, Rs. 1100.00 lakh has been budgeted.
Accordingly, the Urban Development Department, No. 2 As proposed hereinabove, out of the provision for the financial year 2018-19 under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Maha Abhiyan (01) (05), financial assistance to the tribals in the urban areas of the Urban Local Bodies under the head Financial Assistance to the Tribals in Urban Local Bodies (2217 A 065) has been provided for water supply projects of Igatpuri and Jawhar Municipal Councils at a cost of Rs. 323.80 lakh and Rs. 167.00 Lakhs out of which Rs. 490.80 lakh (Rs. Four Crore Ninety Lakh Eighty Thousand only) is being sanctioned to the Principal Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Urban Development Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area as well as in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the sub-plan area.
