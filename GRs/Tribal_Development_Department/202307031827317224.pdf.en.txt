the prelude
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been approved by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Finance Department vide No. As per the Government circular here, 30% of the budgetary allocation for 01-Wage and 20% for other items under District Annual Tribal Component Programme on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System has been made available for distribution on Budget Distribution System. These funds have been distributed to the concerned regulatory authorities.
Under the' Bharat Ratna Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Amrit Aahar Yojana' being implemented under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme, tribal women and children are being provided with full calorie diet to prevent malnutrition and infant mortality in tribal areas. Considering the importance and sensitivity of the scheme and the funds available for disbursement by the Finance Department, it is proposed to provide 100% budgetary allocation for the year 2023-24 for the scheme. The proposal was submitted to the Finance Department. In pursuance of this proposal, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement 50% of the budgetary allocation for the scheme. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of the remaining 30% of the funds available for disbursement to the concerned Controlling Authority was under the consideration of the Government considering the funds released earlier within the fund disbursement limit provided by the Finance Department.
