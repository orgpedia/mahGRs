The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 under reference, the Finance Department has made available funds on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System within the limits specified in the Budget Estimates, Budget Estimates and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System under reference No. 1 of the Tribal Development Department. 2 As per the Government decision, 25% of the budgetary allocation has been provided for the salary component and 20% for the office bound component for the schemes covered under the programme expenditure. However, the said funds have not been released within the limit of 20% of the budgetary allocation made by the Finance Department for the purpose of 24-Petrol, Oil and Lubricants under Scheduled Tribes Certification Scrutiny Committee (Programme Expenditure) (22254122). The matter was under consideration of the Government for release of funds.
Government's decision:
