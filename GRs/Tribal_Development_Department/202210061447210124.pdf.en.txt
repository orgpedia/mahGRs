The Proposal:
Department of Tribal Development As per the decision of the Government dated 18.10.1989, training of tribal youth for pre-recruitment in police and armed forces has been started in Igatpuri (Nashik), Jawhar (Palghar), Nandurbar, Rajura (Chandrapur), Ambegaon (Pune), Kinwat (Nanded), Desaiganj (Gadchiroli), Dharani (Amravati) and Dhubri. As per the Government decision dated 28.08.2002, 9 training centres have been operationalized at Akole (Ahmednagar). Three training sessions (4 months each) are conducted in a year at these training centres. The cost required for these training centres is met from the budgetary provision under the Tribal Component Programme (T-5) under Demand Number (01) (01) Establishment of Special Education and Guidance Centre (SECC) in the head of the scheme 2230 0755, 50-Other expenditure.
During the financial year 2022-23, under reference No. 2 As per the letter, the Government has received a proposal seeking funds for the necessary expenses of some of these training centres and for the pending expenses of the previous year. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the funds to the Controlling Authority within the limit available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System through the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, in Demand No. T-05, the following (01) (01)-Special Education and Guidance Centres have been established under the scheme for the year 2022-23 for pre-recruitment training of Police and Armed Forces. An amount of Rs. 120.83 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Twenty Lakh Eighty Three Thousand only) is being sanctioned for disbursement and expenditure to the Controller and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
