The Proposal:
Considering the situation of the Corona virus epidemic in the state, the Finance Department has given Rs. Circular dated 16.04.2020 has laid down the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided from time to time on the Budget Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Government decisions herein, provision of 75% of the programme expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) for the schemes under the sub-heading" Welfare of Backward Classes" has been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. Subsidised Ashramshalas under this sub-heading have been provided with grant-in-aid to NGOs for running Ashramshalas and Post-Mool Udyog Ashramshalas within the limit of 80% of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2020-21 under the target of 36-Subsidised Grants (Salaries) under Scheme 2225F129. As the funds for salaries under the scheme were insufficient, the government was considering to disburse the remaining 10 per cent of the 90 per cent limit of the budgetary provision with the approval of the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, the remaining 10% of the 90% limit of the budgetary provision under the objective of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under 2225F129 of the Scheme for Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) to run Ashramshalas and Post-Mool Industry Ashramshalas for the Subsidized Ashramshalas in Demand No. T-5 under the head of Sub-Development of" Welfare of Backward Classes" in the Programme Expenditure is Rs. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 7800.00 lakhs (Rupees Eighty Eight Crores only) to the Controlling Officer and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to remain critical for some time due to the lockdown, the Controller while spending the funds in this distribution, the Controller will plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
