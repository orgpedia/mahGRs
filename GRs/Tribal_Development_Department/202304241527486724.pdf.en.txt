Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Vikas Kendra is a scheme implemented by the Central Government. The main objective of the scheme is to collect minor forest produce from the members of the self-help groups in the Scheduled Areas and other areas of Maharashtra mentioned in the Fifth Schedule to the Constitution of India, to sell the collected minor forest produce by adding value to it. For the effective and speedy implementation of the Van Dhan Yojana, the matter of appointing the Tribal Development Commissionerate, Nashik as the State Agent Institute (SIP) in place of the Shabari Finance and Development Corporation Limited, Nashik, and to monitor the implementation of the scheme properly, the" State Level Steering Committee" at the government level has been constituted. The government was formed by the decision of 5. Reference No. As per Section 06, the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has suggested to the National Commission for Scheduled Tribes to reform the State Level Steering Committee and has requested the Government to reform the same. Accordingly, the matter of reforming the state-level steering committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision: The State Steering Committee constituted to monitor the Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Vikas Kendra Scheme under the Government decision dated 20 th May, 2022 is being amended as follows.
