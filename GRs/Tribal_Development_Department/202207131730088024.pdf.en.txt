The proposal-
Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India provides for special Central assistance and funds under the scheme of tribal development. During the year 2019-20, under the Tribal Development Scheme, the State Government has been allocated Rs. 1510.00 lacs and for 2 programmes of additional proposals Rs. 1000.00 lakh for 18 programmes. 2510.00 lakh has been sanctioned and received from the Central Government. As per the letter in Reference 4, Commissioner, AV has sanctioned the unspent funds of the programmes named in Reference 1 to 3 to the Housing for PVTG approved in the additional proposals of the Tribal Development Scheme in the same year amounting to Rs. 750.00 lakh has been requested to be made available for the programme. On the request of the Commissioner, Rs. 600.00 lakh for the Housing for PVTG programme has been approved by the Government vide Reference No.5. Reference 6 and 7 have proposed targets for primitive households. On the request of the Commissioner, A.V. 600.00 lakh under the Housing for PVTG programme was under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Adim Janjatiya Vikas Karyakram, Housing for PVTG in the year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 750.00 lakh for the implementation of the approved programme. 600.00 lakh out of the provision of Rs. 8.8 lakh for the construction of 451 household toilets for tribal families on the lines of Shabari Ghar Kul scheme (Rs. 1.32 lakh and Rs. 1.42 lakh) is being approved for construction.
