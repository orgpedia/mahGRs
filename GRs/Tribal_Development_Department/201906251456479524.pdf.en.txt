Introduction: Refer No. As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Tribal Development Corporation Limited, Nashik has been given the post of President and Vice President. Minister of State (Rural Development) Minister of State (Tribal Development) or persons who have done notable work in the field of social/ economic development and cultural field of tribals have been approved to be elected as Chairman and Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra State Cooperative Tribal Development Corporation. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of selecting a person who has done remarkable work in the social/ economic development and cultural field of the tribals for the post of vice-president of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Tribal Development Corporation Limited, Nashik. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: On the Tribal Development Corporation in the post of" Vice President,"" Shri. Uttamrao Raghoji Ingale is being appointed through this government decision.
The term of the Vice-President- until further orders of the Government.
The honorarium and other facilities payable as per the Government Decision No. Shau 10.10/ P.S. 96/10/ S.A. dated 13th February, 2012 of the Finance Department shall be provided to the Vice-Chairman of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Tribal Development Corporation Limited, Nashik.
