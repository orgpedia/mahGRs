As per the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik on 30.06.2022 in pursuance of the decision taken on 11.07.20212, the Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nagpur and Project Officer, AAV, Gadchiroli have informed that there is no objection to the closure of the aided Ashramshala, Sukala due to lack of number of students in classes 1 to 7 for the year 2015-16 to 2019-20. In view of the feedback given by the Director, Indira Educational Institutions, Armori, G. Gadchiroli, the aided (Indira) Primary Ashramshala, Sukala, has been established.
The closure of Primary Ashramshala, Armori, G. Gadchiroli (Classes 1 to 7) from the academic year 2023-24 is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
(9) (Indira) Primary Ashramshala, Sukala, Tt., funded by Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nagpur. The students of classes 1 to 7 in the primary ashramshala of Armori, G. Gadchiroli should be immediately adjusted in the nearest government or aided ashramshala.
(2) Aided (Indira) Primary Ashramshala, Sukala, Ta. The staff who will be surplus due to closure of Ashramshala (Class 1 to Class 7) at Armori, G. Gadchiroli should be immediately adjusted in other aided Ashramshalas run by the Institute.
