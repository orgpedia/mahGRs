2.A total of Rs. 150.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Crores only) is being disbursed to Principal Secretary (Power), Industries, Power and Labour Department as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System. In this case, the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer has to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook and the rules and regulations in the context of the Finance Department while spending the said provisions. The directions given in the Government Decisions dated 24.06.2021 and 14.10.2021 should be strictly followed.
The report of expenditure incurred from the provisions made available, material objective-wise information achieved account head-wise/ sub-account head-wise, utilization certificate of funds, etc. should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month and the funds should be disbursed.
A proposal for further disbursement of funds should be submitted along with the utilization certificate.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can control the expenditure on schemes in tribal areas and outside the sub-plan area.
5 Care should be taken to ensure that only tribal beneficiaries are benefited from the provisions made available under the Tribal Component Programme.
