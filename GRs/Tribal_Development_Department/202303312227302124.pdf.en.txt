The Proposal:
Proposal for release of funds under Tribal Component Programme 2022-23 under various heads of accounts by way of redeployment as per the demand was sent to Finance Department vide Reference No. 2 was presented. The proposal was under the consideration of the government for disbursement of funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, under Demand T-5, the Government is approving the disbursement and spending of funds as per Rakana No. 5 to the concerned Controlling Authorities as approved by the Finance Department from the budgeted funds for the year 2022-23 under the schemes mentioned in Rakana No. 2 of the Batana Statement.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
3. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer may refer to Pillar No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 should be circulated immediately to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers under them. Further, while spending the funds distributed under the said Government Decision, the Controlling Authorities have to follow the Financial Rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, Financial Powers Rule Book and also in the context of Finance Department vide No. Follow the instructions given in the Government Circular 1 strictly.
If funds are required for schemes/ matters other than those for which funds have been disbursed under the said Government Decision, the Controlling Officer shall submit a proposal to the Government with detailed and self-explanatory reasons.
