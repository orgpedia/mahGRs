The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been approved by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system through the Finance Department has been implemented under the District Annual Tribal Plan vide Chapter No. 20% of the fund is provided for disbursement of 1st salary, 06th telephone, electricity and water bills, 10th contractual services, 14th lease and taxes and 34th scholarship/ education etc.
Subordinate No. Under the Government decision here, 10% funds have been allocated for 01-wage and 5% for 02-wage and 10 contractual services. Now, in accordance with the provisions made available by the Finance Department, the government was considering to disburse the remaining funds under the program expenditure plans of the Tribal Development Department to the concerned controlling officer.
Government's decision:
Under the" District Annual Tribal Appropriation" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. T-9, T-10 and T-11 for the districts mentioned in Rakaana No. 2 of the accompanying return below, in case No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 20% of the budgetary provisions to the concerned Controlling Authorities in respect of 1st salary, 06th telephone, electricity bill and water charges, 10th contractual services, 14th lease and taxes and 34th scholarships/ scholarships etc. and 10% of the budgetary provisions for other matters.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending this fund, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the conditions.
