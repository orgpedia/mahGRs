The Proposal:
The percentage of candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribes in the State appearing in competitive examinations is low.
Therefore, in order to bring these candidates into the mainstream of development, the training programs for various competitive examinations are being implemented by the Tribal Development Department in accordance with the government decision at No. 2 in the context of passing the competitive examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) and getting a chance to become an administrative officer. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune, has decided to provide funds for the implementation of this programme in the financial year 2021-22. 3 proposal has been submitted. In this regard, vide No. 4 The issue of disbursement of funds to the Controlling Authority within the limit approved by the Finance Department on the basis of the figure here was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, in the financial year 2021-22, under the head of Demand No. T-5, 2225-Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, it is approved to provide training to the students of Scheduled Tribes in the competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission to enable them to get opportunities in the Indian and State Administrative Service.
