Read reference here. 1 Department of Social Welfare and Sports, Government Decision, d. 24.02.1982, in case of natural calamity or accidental death of students in Government Tribal Ashramshalas, Rs. 5,000/- was sanctioned as grant-in-aid. But since this subsidy is low, read the reference number mentioned here. 2 Tribal Development Department, Government Decisions, Dy. As on 23.03.1992, the grant has been increased to Rs. Rs. 15,000. As on 25.08.2011, the ex-gratia amount has been increased to Rs. 75,000/- respectively. At present, the ex-gratia amount of Rs. Rs 75,000. It has been about 7 years since the revision of the ex-gratia amount.
Therefore, the amount of this gift is very small in the current situation. The people's representatives have demanded that the amount be increased from time to time. as well as the Legislature
During the session, the members have demanded an increase in the amount as it is very low. Hence, the government was contemplating to increase the amount.
Government's decision:
The government has now decided that the students of the Scheduled Tribes will be educated in the government tribal ashramshala, aided ashramshala and in the ashramshala of the nominated school.
