Government Resolution, Tribal Development Department, No. 2013/ No.682/ KA-13 dated 7th May, 2015.
Preamble: In order to provide quality education to the students of Scheduled Tribes, the Central Government has decided to establish residential schools for tribal students at the State level with the funds to be distributed under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India. Accordingly, 14 Eklavya Residential Schools have been finally started in the state today. The schools will start classes from class 6 to 12 in a phased manner as per the curriculum of the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.
For schools. As per the Government decision dated 7/5/2015, the pattern and pay scale/ honorarium of teaching/ non-teaching staff has been fixed. In view of the resolution passed by the Executive Board of Maharashtra Tribal Public School Society, Nashik in its meeting dated 18th September, 2017, the matter of revision of pay scale of teachers/ non-teaching staff of Eklavya Residential School on the lines of Navodaya Vidyalaya was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: 1. To maintain the quality and standard of education in Eklavya Residential School on the lines of Navodaya Vidyalaya, the Principal, Teachers, Male and Female Superintendents of the said school are being sanctioned the following pay scales on the lines of Navodaya Vidyalaya. The revised pay scale will be effective from January 1, 2018. The dearness allowance will be paid as per rules. The remaining funds will be provided by the state government.

The principal, teachers, male and female superintendents of Eklavya Residential School will have a probationary period for the first two years. If the quality of their service during this period is found satisfactory, a decision will be taken by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Tribal Public School Society, Nashik, regarding the extension of their probationary period.
(3) House rent allowance and transport allowance shall not be payable to the staff provided accommodation in the school premises, taking into account the residential nature of the Eklavya Residential School. Employees who do not have accommodation will be paid house rent and transport allowance as per rules. Also, no other allowances will be admissible to the employees of Eklavya Residential School.
Since the staff working in Eklavya Residential School is working under Maharashtra Tribal Public School Society, Nashik, no other financial benefits including group insurance scheme, pension after retirement, death-cum-service gratuity will be admissible to any employee of Eklavya Residential School.
(5) The posts of peons or class IV servants in every Eklavya Residential School shall be filled up by outsourcing from the level of Principal subject to the condition that the right to permanent employment in the institution cannot be denied.
6) Safety and cleanliness works in Eklavya Residential Schools will be done through external agencies at the level of principals by inviting tenders in the prescribed manner.
The expenditure of Eklavya Residential Schools will be met from the funds received from the Central Government under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India. The expenditure to be provided at the State level shall be met from the provision approved under the head Demand No.T-5, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 02, Welfare of Scheduled Tribes, 796 Tribal Area Sub-Plan, Central Assistance under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India, (01) (44) Ashramshala Group (Central Scheme) (2225 2587).
