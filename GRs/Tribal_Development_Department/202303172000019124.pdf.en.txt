The Central Sector Scheme of National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes and Cardiovascular Diseases (NCDC), which is being implemented with a total outlay of Rs. 234.74 lakh to the Public Health Department under reference No. 2 here has been distributed by the government decision. However, pending the matching State share, the State share will be raised by way of Supplementary Demand in the ensuing Budget Session of Maharashtra Legislature in March, 2023. A sum of Rs. 156.49 lakh has been sanctioned. Regarding the disbursement of this fund, No. Under Section 2, the matter of disbursement of funds was under consideration of the Government in accordance with the proposal received from the Public Health Department.
In pursuance of the above proposal, the Government have decided to allocate funds within the limit available for disbursement from the budgetary provisions of 2022-23, subject to the approval of the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA). 2 The matter of distribution of funds in accordance with the approval received at the site was under the consideration of the Government.
