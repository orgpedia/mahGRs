Introduction- Since the livelihood of the tribal brothers is rain-fed agriculture, there is no guarantee of assured income through farming every year. The guidelines have been approved under the government decision in reference 1 to provide units of 10 goats and 1 goat to women self-help groups as the goat farming associated with agriculture can guarantee them a fixed income and this business is mainly associated with women. However, since the Government has decided to revise the procurement price of goats and goats in terms of the Government decision, the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has requested to adjust the procurement price of goats and goats approved in Reference 1. 3 is written here. In line with this demand, the government was considering approving the revised guidelines of the scheme" Supply of Goat Units to Women SHGs (10 Female+ 1 Male)" by superseding the government decision at reference 1.
Government's decision
The Government decision issued on 1st January, 2021 regarding the guidelines of the scheme" Supply of Goat Units to Women SHGs (10 Female+ 1 Male)" worth Rs. 500.00 lakhs approved under the programme to increase the income of women SHGs through goat rearing, strengthen the SHGs, reduce their migration is being superseded with Annexure 1. Further, the revised guidelines for implementation of the scheme are being approved as per Annexure No. 1 annexed to this decision.
2. The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik shall take immediate action to implement the scheme within the limits of the provisions distributed as per the said guidelines and shall take care to submit its utilization certificate along with financial and physical report to the Government.
