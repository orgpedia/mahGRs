The proposal-
The grants are provided annually by the Central Government under the Special Central Assistance to the States under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and under the Scheme of Adim Janjatiya Vikas Karyakram. Proposals for grants under this program. It is submitted to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India with the approval of the Executive Committee headed by the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India, a proposal for" Implementation of DBT Scheme for Ashram School" for the year 2016-17 was submitted to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs.
2 Lakhs of students are studying in the ashramshalas of the Tribal Development Department.
It is the responsibility of the Tribal Development Department to provide basic and necessary facilities like providing abundant food, good shelter, along with quality education to the students of the ashramshala.
For this, various schemes are being implemented by the Tribal Development Department. A proposal was submitted to implement the" Implementation of DBT Scheme for Ashram School" by the department to update the data of students in all ashramshalas in accordance with the schemes which are given through Direct Benefit Transfer, to update the software to control the DBT scheme, to provide training to the concerned staff in all ashram schools and project offices, to purchase separate computers and printers for DBT at the level of all project offices, Additional Commissioner and Commissioner Tribal Development and to work on DBT through it throughout the year.
