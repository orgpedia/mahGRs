Government's decision
The circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued by the Department of Finance specifies the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21 keeping in view the pandemic situation in the State. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has provided funds from time to time on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Government decisions here, 70% of the funds have already been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities under the scheme of 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) for the schemes in Demand No. T-2 and T-4 of the programme cost under the Tribal Component Programme.
Now, in the month of December, the Department of Finance has released 75% of the total budgetary allocation for the objectives of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on the Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS). 11 Domestic travel expenses have been provided for disbursement of funds in this regard with a ceiling of 20%.
Accordingly, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities within the limit of 20% for the remaining 5% for 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) and 11 Travel Expenses, after deducting the funds disbursed earlier.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Considering the pandemic situation in the state due to Corona virus, the financial condition of the state is likely to be serious for some time to come, so the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. This fund should be used for regular payment of salaries. It should not be used to pay any pending liabilities at present.
