The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking fiscal measures by setting spending priorities as per the decisions of the government. Now, in the month of August, the Department of Finance, as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System, has issued a notification under the Scheme of Programme Expenditure, in Chapter No.
