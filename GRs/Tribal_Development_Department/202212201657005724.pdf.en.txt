The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for the schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure are being made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month. Accordingly, under the Programme Expenditure in Tribal Component Programme, Government Ashramshalas, for the purpose of 10-contracted services under the scheme, funds to the extent of 56% have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
As the funds for the remuneration of the employees of Class-3 and Class-4 posts in the government ashramshalas are insufficient, the matter of disbursing the necessary funds to the concerned controlling officer as approved by the Finance Department for the purpose of 10-contract service was under the consideration of the government.
