Preamble: The Tribal Development Department has established Government Tribal Hostels across the State to provide secondary, higher secondary and college education to the children of Scheduled Tribes at the Taluk, District and Divisional levels. In respect of admission of students in these hostels, in the aforesaid context, No. 1 here. The rules have been implemented under the Government Decision of 3.8.2004.
Also, in reference No. 2 and 3 here. As per the Government Decisions dated 21.9.2005 and 19.7.2006, provisions have been made for admission of students in these hostels under special category. The government was considering amending the existing provisions of admission process for students in government tribal hostels as per rules and special cases.
Accordingly, in respect of admission of students in Government Tribal Hostels, as a special case, vide No. 2 to 4, No. 21.9.2005, dt. 19.7.2006 and D. All Government decisions of 20.08.2019 are being superseded.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: (1) Subject to the provisions of this Government Order, No. 1 here. The proviso to paragraph (a) of the Government Order dated 3.8.2004 regarding reservation of seats for OBC students in the hostels of the Tribal Department is hereby repealed. Henceforth, all the seats in all the government tribal hostels in the state will be filled only from tribal students.
(2) In every Government Hostel for tribal boys and girls, 5% of the seats falling vacant every year shall be filled from the Government level as a" special case" from among the students belonging to Scheduled Tribes.

(3) Students participating in the provision of admission in the Government Tribal Hostel as a special case are being made mandatory to apply on the website www. swayammahaonline. gov. in.
(4) As a special case, hostel admission shall be granted only for the duration of the course.
(5) The limit of income of the family for admission of special cases shall be fixed by the Government. The hostel will remain as prescribed for admission rules under the Government decision dated 3.8.2004.
(6) The list of students to be admitted to the Government Tribal Hostel as a special case shall be given to the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, by approving it at the government level. It will be delivered on September 30th.
(7) D. After September 30, under a special case, the site was deemed" undesirable," and the concerned project officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, gave the site to them. The payment should be made in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decree of 3.8.2004.
(8) The applications of the students who are not able to enter the hostel due to lack of vacant seats should be considered by the Project Officer for the SWAYAM scheme.
