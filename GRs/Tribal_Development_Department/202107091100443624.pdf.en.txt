The Proposal:

2.A total of Rs. 569.24 lakh (Rupees Five Crore Seventy One Lakh Twenty Four Thousand only) is being disbursed to Secretary, Agriculture Department as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System. In this case, the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer has to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook and the rules and regulations in the context of the Finance Department while spending the said provisions. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 24.06.2021.
3. Report of expenditure incurred by the Controller on account of the provision made, information on the material progress achieved in terms of headings/ sub-headings, utilization of funds.
The information should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately, so that the Government may be informed of the expenditure incurred by the tribal sub-plan area.
Expenditure on schemes in the area and outside the sub-plan area can be controlled.
Under the provisions made available under the Tribal Component Programme, schemes for creation of infrastructure in the tribal sub-plan areas as well as for individual benefit should be taken up and in the tribal sub-plan outlying areas only schemes for individual benefit should be taken up.
