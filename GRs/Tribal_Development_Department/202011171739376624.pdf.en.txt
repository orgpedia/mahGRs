The circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued by the Department of Finance specifies the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21 keeping in view the pandemic situation in the State. Also, the No. Under Section 2, the Department of Finance has made planning recommendations for expenditure within the limit of 33% of the schemes under the programme. The matter of disbursement of funds in accordance with the proposal submitted under Section 4 was under consideration of the Government.
