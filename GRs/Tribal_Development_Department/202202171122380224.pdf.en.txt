The Proposal:
The Central share received for the first installment for the year 2021-22 for commercial crops under the Krishi Unnati Yojana National Security Mission being implemented by the Department of Agriculture and Padum under the Tribal Component Programme is Rs. 9.86 lakh (Rs. 6.23 and Rs. 3.63 lakh) and State share of Rs. 6.57 lakh (Rs. 4.15 lakhs and Sugarcane- 2.42 lakhs). 16.43 lakh has been sanctioned by the Department of Agriculture and Padum for disbursement and expenditure out of the budgetary provisions of 2021-22. The proposal under section 3 was under consideration of the Government for approval.
Government's decision
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. The Central share received for the first instalment for the year 2021-22 for commercial crops (Cotton and Sugarcane) under the T-5 Heading 2401-Krishi Unnati Yojana-National Security Mission is Rs. 9.86 lakh (Rs. 6.23 and Rs. 3.63 lakh) and State share of Rs. 6.57 lakh (Rs. 4.15 lakhs and Sugarcane- 2.42 lakhs). 16.43 lakh is being approved for disbursement and expenditure from the budgetary provisions for 2021-22 as follows:

2) As mentioned above, Rs. 16.43 lakh (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand only) is being disbursed to Secretary, Department of Agriculture as Head of Administration on Budget Distribution System. The funds have been disbursed on the Budget Fund Distribution System. In this case, the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer has to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook and the rules and regulations in the context of the Finance Department while spending the said provisions. The directions given in the Government Resolution dated 14.10.2021 should be strictly followed.
The report of expenditure incurred from the provisions made available, material objective-wise information achieved in the head of accounts/ sub-head of accounts, utilization certificate, etc. should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month and a proposal should be submitted for further disbursement of funds along with the utilization certificate.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure is recorded in the tribal sub-plan area.
5. Tribal Sub Plan schemes for creation of infrastructure in the tribal sub-plan area as well as individual benefit schemes should be taken up from the provision made under the Tribal Component Programme.
6. This Government Decision Read No. 1 is being issued under the authority conferred on the Department.
