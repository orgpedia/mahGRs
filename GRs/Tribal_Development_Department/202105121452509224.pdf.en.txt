The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Finance Department, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) through the Finance Department as made available on the Distribution System, the funds within the limit of 15% for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) of the mandatory expenditure schemes of the Tribal Development Department have been released to the concerned Controlling Officer vide No. 2 has been distributed here by government decision.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds amounting to 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the release and expenditure of 5% of the budgetary allocation to the concerned Controlling Authority as per Rakaan No.5 for the salary for the schemes mentioned in Rakaan No.2 of the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules of the state government, financial rules, provisions in the budget rule book and all the terms and conditions.
(3) The Government shall take such decision within the limits and subject to the reference made by the Finance Department on the budgetary allocation system. The powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Circular dated 06.04.2021 are being issued.
