Introduction The percentage of Scheduled Tribe students in the state passing the administrative service examinations is very low compared to other communities. As the general economic condition of the tribal community is weak, the educated students of this community are not able to qualify for the Administrative Services Competitive Examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission due to lack of necessary training for administrative services. Accordingly, the Government vide its decision dated 15.07.2014 has approved the provision of 8 months pre-competition training for Administrative Services Competitive Examinations conducted by Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission to 25 ST students in each of the 8 Universities of the State and 10 ST students at Yashada, Pune. The Government Corrigendum dated 25.03.2019 has increased the duration of the training from 8 months to 11 months. Considering the demand of the Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune to increase the cost of the training program to implement it more effectively, the government was considering to approve the revised cost of the training program.
