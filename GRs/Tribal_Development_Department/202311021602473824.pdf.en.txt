Preamble: Under the Tribal Component Programme, Subsidiary Grants to General Secondary Schools, Subsidiary Grants to Non-Government Junior Colleges and Assistance to a Batch of Tribal Students in Sainik Schools being implemented by the School Education Department. Under Section 2, funds have been allocated within the limit of 60% of the total budgetary provisions.
Thereafter, the Department of School Education has decided to disburse funds to the extent of 40 percent for payment of salaries to the employees of non-government secondary schools in the tribal areas. The proposal was submitted to the Finance Department. The proposal was under the consideration of the Government to disburse funds from the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24 in accordance with the approval given by the Finance Department to disburse funds within the limit of 40% of the total 100% of the budgetary provisions.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2022-23, Demand No. Under the head of T-5, 2202 General Education, with the approval of the Department of Finance, the following grants-in-aid to General Secondary Schools, Subsidiary Grants to Non-Government Junior Colleges and assistance to the batch of tribal students in Sainik Schools are being approved to be disbursed from the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24, within a total limit of 40%:
