The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Finance Department, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) as made available on the distribution system by the Finance Department, the total amount of 15% of the total amount for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation under the Tribal Component Programme has been released to the concerned Controlling Officer vide No. 2 It is distributed according to the decisions of the government.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds amounting to 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Under the" District Annual Tribal Appropriation" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. For the purpose of 01-salary for the districts mentioned in the accompanying returns under T-9, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the increased funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Budgetary Provision No. 5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending this fund, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the conditions.
(3) The Government shall take such decision within the limits and subject to the reference made by the Finance Department on the budgetary allocation system. The powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Circular dated 06.04.2021 are being issued.
