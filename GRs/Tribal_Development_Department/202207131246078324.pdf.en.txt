Read 1. Central Government Ministry of Tribal Affairs vide letter No. 17011/01/2020/ SCA, dated 17 th March, 2022.
Preamble: Grants under Special Central Assistance are provided annually by the Central Government to the State Governments under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and under the scheme of Tribal Development Programme. The Central Government has decided to launch the Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Vikas Yojana (PMAAGY) to provide integrated social and economic development of villages with Scheduled Tribe population based on the concept of model village by replacing the existing Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme. The scheme aims to bridge the gap between the Central and State Governments' flagship schemes in various sectors such as education, health care, communication, infrastructure, livelihood, etc. Guidelines have been issued by the Central Government for the implementation of this scheme. Based on these guidelines, the government was considering issuing detailed guidelines for the implementation of the scheme in the establishments of the state.
Guidelines for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Vikas Yojana (PMAAGY) with a view to ensure integrated social and economic development of villages having significant population of Scheduled Tribes based on the concept of model village are being approved as per Annexure attached to this Government decision.
