(2) Head Accounts- 0235 Social Security and Welfare, Deputy Head Accounts- Head- 60 Other Social Security and Welfare Programmes, Sub-head- 801 Interest or other deposits on unspent balance from the grantee, Group Head- (02) Other deposits Sub-group Head- (02) (02) Other deposits in Single Nodal Account, (Tribal Development Department) (0235027201)
