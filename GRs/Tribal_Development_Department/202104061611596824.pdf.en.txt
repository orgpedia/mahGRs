Preamble: Under the Tribal Development Department, Roads and Bridges under Tribal Utilization, Road works under Public Works Department are taken up under heading 5054 of the scheme.
In the Budget Session of March 2020, the works of roads and bridges under head 5054 517 were included in the Budget Statement (New Subject). A report was sought from the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, as to whether these works are in the tribal sub-plan area or not. According to the report, the road works that are outside the tribal sub-plan area need to be cancelled. Also, the works which could not be completed within the approved price, the works which have been left out in the budget book for 2020-21, the works which have not yet been tendered/ the works which have not yet started/ the works completed through other schemes were under the consideration of the government.
