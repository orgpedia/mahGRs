The Proposal:
The percentage of candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribes in the State appearing in competitive examinations is low. Therefore, in order to bring these candidates into the mainstream of development, the training programs for various competitive examinations are being implemented by the Tribal Development Department in accordance with the decision of the Government in Context No. 2 to get a chance to become an administrative officer by passing the competitive examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC). Accordingly, the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune has decided to provide funds for the implementation of this programme in the financial year 2022-23. 3 proposal has been submitted. In this regard, vide No. 4 It was under consideration of the Government to disburse the funds to the Controlling Authority in accordance with the approval received on the land here.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the financial year 2022-23, it is being approved to disburse funds under this scheme to provide training to the students of 2225 Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities under the head Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities to enable them to get opportunities for Indian and State Administrative Services in the competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
