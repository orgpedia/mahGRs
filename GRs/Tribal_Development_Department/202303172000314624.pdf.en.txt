The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the year 2022-23, under the head Demand Number T-6, Rs. 6000.00 lakh in the Budget and revised estimate of Rs. 5520.00 lacs have been sanctioned. Accordingly, as per the approval of the Finance Department, the State has been provided with 70% of the budgetary allocation to meet the expenditure up to March, 2023. The matter of disbursement of funds made available on the Budget Estimate Distribution System under this head was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Government Ashramshala Construction (OTSP) (Head of Accounts 4225 1118) under Chapter No. 53-Big Construction Under this, out of the budgetary provision for 2022-23, Rs. 1380.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirteen Crores Eighty Lakhs only) to the Controlling Officer and Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nashik/ Nagpur.
