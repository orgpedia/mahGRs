Government's decision
In view of the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona virus, the Finance Department vide Circular No.1 dated 16th April, 2020 has approved the release of funds at the department level for the first 3 months of the financial year 2020-21 (end of June, 2020) for the specified objectives of the schemes of mandatory and programme expenditure in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided on the Budget Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. Under the Tribal Component Programme, the Government has decided to make available to the concerned Controller General of Funds (DCFs) the entire amount available for the purpose of 1st salary and 36th Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 35%, while 10% of the available funds for the purpose of 2nd wage, 6th telephone, electricity and water charges, 10th contract services, 13th office expenses, 14th lease and taxes, 24th petrol, oil and lubricants, 19th food expenses, 45th interest.
At present, the Finance Department has provided 20% of the budgetary allocation for the following purposes: 02- wages, 06- telephone, electricity and water charges, 10- contract services, 13- office expenses, 14- lease and taxes, 24- petrol, oil and lubricants. Accordingly, the Government is approving the release of 10% of the funds available for distribution as per Pillar-5 to the Controller Officers of Pillar-6 for the schemes mentioned in Pillar No. 2 of the accompanying prospectus in Demand No. T-5 under the sub-development head" Welfare of Backward Classes" in the programme expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious in the next period due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner.
3. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer may refer to Pillar No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 should be circulated immediately to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers under them. Also, the heads of departments and controlling officers while spending the funds distributed under this government decision, the financial rules and finance in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook.
