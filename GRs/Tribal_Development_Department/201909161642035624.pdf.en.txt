The Proposal:
Reference No. 1 decision of the government. Chandrapur, G.S. Approval has been given to start 4 new hostels for boys and girls of Scheduled Tribes at Savli, G. Chandrapur with a capacity of 75 each.
The proposal to create 20 new posts for these 4 new hostels was approved by the High Level Committee. This was approved in the meeting held on 08.07.2019. Accordingly, the matter of creating new posts for the management of these 4 new hostels was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Chandrapur, G.S. The Government is approving creation of 8 new posts for the management of 4 new hostels for boys and girls belonging to Scheduled Tribes at Savli, G. Chandrapur with a capacity of 75 each.
