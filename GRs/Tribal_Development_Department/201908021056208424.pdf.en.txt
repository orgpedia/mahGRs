The Tribal Development Department had appealed to the Project Officer, AAV Project Office, Government, Non-Government/ Social/ Private Organizations to submit innovative proposals for the grant received under this scheme. The proposal is mainly to prepare a batch of Super-50 by taking the 10th pass students of tribal ashramshalas at the state level and giving them residential training on the 11th and 12th CBSE syllabus as well as medical and engineering entrance examinations to get them admitted to reputed institutions in the country.
