The Proposal:
The percentage of Scheduled Tribe candidates in the state appearing in competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is low. Therefore, in order to bring these candidates in the mainstream of development, to get a chance to become an administrative officer by passing the Civil Services Competitive Examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), such candidates should be given financial assistance as an incentive for preparing for the Civil Services Main Examination and Interview of the Union Public Service Commission. 3 has been approved by the government. Accordingly, the proposal for seeking funds for the financial year 2020-21 under the Tribal Component Programme is under reference No. 4 has been received by the Government. The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2020-21, under the Tribal Component Programme, the following funds are being sanctioned with the approval of the Finance Department to provide training to the students of Scheduled Tribes under the head of Demand No. T-5, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities in the competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission to enable them to get opportunities in the Indian and State Administrative Service.
