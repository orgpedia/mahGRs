Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the financial year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement to the total tribal population of 64,425 in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti Bank accounts in 21 villages of 14 Gram Sabhas in Chopra Panchayat Samiti area, 14 villages of 5 Gram Sabhas in Yawal Panchayat Samiti area, 23 villages of 13 Gram Sabhas in Raver Panchayat Samiti area under Jalgaon Zila Parishad in the Scheduled Areas of the State. 485.1643/- Rs. 3,12,56, 707.80/- (Rs. Three crore twelve lakh fifty five thousand seven hundred and eighty paisa only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to this department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon.
