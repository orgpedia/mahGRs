The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking fiscal measures by setting spending priorities as per the decisions of the government. Accordingly, as provided by the Finance Department on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System from time to time, funds to the extent of 90% of the budgetary provision for wage matters and 75% for other purposes covered under the mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department, subject to the provisions of Section No. 3 has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the decision of the Government.
Now in reference No. 2 As per the Government Circular, the Department of Finance has made provisions for mandatory expenditure schemes on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System in the range of 80-95% for specific purposes except in case of item 32- Contribution and item 26- Advertisement and Publicity. Accordingly, considering the provision made earlier for specific objectives under the mandatory expenditure schemes, the matter of disbursement of the remaining funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Rakaan No. 6 for the schemes in Rakaan No. 2 of the accompanying returns under T-1, T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the provisions of the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Rules, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions.
