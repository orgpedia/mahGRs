The Proposal:
Under this scheme, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides grants-in-aid to the District Councils for educational fees, examination fees, maintenance and scholarships to the students of Scheduled Tribes admitted in the aided hostels under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Accordingly, during the year 2021-22, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has been allocated Rs. 100.00 lakh has been disbursed. Now the matter of distribution of funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department on the land at No. 3 was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for payment of educational fee, examination fee, maintenance and scholarship to the students of Scheduled Tribes admitted in the aided hostels under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Demand No. Subsidiary Grants to the Zilla Parishads under the head 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities in T-5 as approved in the revised estimates under the Scheme are being sanctioned to the Principal Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department to disburse the remaining funds on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS).
