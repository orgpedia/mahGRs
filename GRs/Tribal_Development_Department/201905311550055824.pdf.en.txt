Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the scheme (PESA 5% Direct Funding Scheme to Gram Panchayats) to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications every year to Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the financial year 2018-2019, Rs. 80,36,57,700/- (Rupees Eighty Crores Thirty Six Lakhs Fifty Seven Thousand Seven Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 80,36,57, 700/- (Rupees Eighty Six Crores Eighty Six Lakhs Sixty Seven Thousand Seven Hundred Only) has been made available for disbursement to 101 villages of 55 Gram Sabhas in Murbad Panchayat Samiti area under Thane Zilla Parishad, 72 villages of 39 Gram Sabhas in Bhiwandi Panchayat Samiti area, 230 villages of 110 Gram Sabhas in Shahapur Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts. 181.8046/- Rs. 3,26,45, 201.70/- (Rs. Three crore twenty six lakh forty five thousand two hundred and one seventy paise only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds provided and the report of the case-wise expenditure should be submitted to this department through the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane.
