Introduction: Aided ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department are provided with maintenance grant, building rent grant as well as contingency grant by the government. Accordingly, under the Tribal Components Programme, the Government had received a proposal under Reference No. 2 for requisition of funds for providing necessary facilities to students in aided ashramshalas during the year 2026-2027 and payment of ex-gratia to the respective institutions. Accordingly, the proposal for disbursement of funds under reference No. 3 was submitted to the Finance Department. The proposal was under the consideration of the government to disburse the funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
