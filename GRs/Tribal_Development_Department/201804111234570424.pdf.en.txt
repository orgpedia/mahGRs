The proposal-
The Central Government has approved a total of 28 proposals amounting to Rs. 12810.38 lakh under the Special Central Assistance Scheme for the year 2017-18 vide its letter dated 30/05/2017.
Under this scheme, the central government has set up 150 scholarship cells. A sum of Rs. 22.88 crore has been sanctioned. This fund reference No. 2 has been allocated to the Commissioner Tribal Development, Nashik as per the decision of the Government here. The Youth Dreamers Foundation (YDF), Delhi is working to provide scholarships for higher education and career choice in the field of education. The main objectives of the project are to establish a scholarship centre to provide scholarships to students of Ashramshalas for pursuing higher education, guidance for career guidance, guidance for centralized admission process required for professional education, filling up of applications for centralized admission and reducing the leakage rate in higher education. YDF aims to ensure that at least 80% of students in classes 10 and 12 get admission to higher education. Setting up of 150 scholarship cells was proposed by the Youth Dreamers Foundation (YDF), Delhi.
In order to ensure that the students of 10th and 12th standard in the Integrated Tribal Project Office, Nashik and Kalwan Sub-Administration Ashramshalas are not deprived of higher education due to financial difficulties and to provide access to higher education to all, the scheme" Setting up 150 scholarship cell" is being approved jointly by the Youth Dreamers Foundation (YDF) and the Tribal Development Department as per the guidelines of the scheme with available funds of Rs. 22.88 lakh.
