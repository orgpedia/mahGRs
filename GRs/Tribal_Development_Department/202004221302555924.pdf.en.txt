Government's decision
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2020 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2020) for the financial year 2020-21 has been approved by the Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is in addition to the provisions of the Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, Dt. 18.03.2020 has been published.
Accordingly, vide Circular No.1 of the Department of Finance, dated 16.04.2020, considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona virus, it has been approved to disburse funds at the department level within the limit of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation of essential and programme expenditure for the first three months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020). Also, at present, funds are made available for disbursement on the Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) within the limit of 10% of the total budgetary allocation. Accordingly, for the schemes mentioned in column no. 2 of the statement of demand no. T-1, T-2 and T-4 under the Tribal Component Programme, the Government is approving to disburse and spend 10% of the total budgetary allocation as per column 5 to the Controlling Authority in column 6 for the specific objectives mentioned in the Government Circular under the reference of the Finance Department.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious for the next 3 to 4 months due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds in this distribution.
3. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer may refer to Pillar No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 shall be distributed to the drawing and disbursing officers under their command. Also, while spending the funds distributed under this government decision, the head of the department and the controlling officers will have to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook, as well as in the context of the Finance Department. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
Under the said Government decision, funds are being released to the Administrative Department as authorized for certain specified purposes prescribed by the Government Circular in the context of the Finance Department. Apart from this, all other expenses have to be spent with the permission of the Finance Department.
