The Proposal:
The Finance Department vide its circular dated 16.04.2020 has released the funds for the financial year 2020-21 considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona Virus
The distribution system is set. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided from time to time on the Budget Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 For the schemes under the sub-heading Programme Expenditure-Backward Classes Welfare under the Tribal Component Programme as per the Government Decisions

Now in the month of November, 01-salary on the budgetary fund distribution system through the Finance Department,
For the purpose of Subsidiary Grants (Salaries), funds to the extent of 70% have been made available for disbursement. Accordingly, the government was considering to allocate the remaining 10 per cent funds for this purpose.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the programme cost to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Pillar 5 for the schemes in the accompanying prospectus in Demand No. T-5 under the sub-development head Welfare of Backward Classes in the programme cost.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious in the next period due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. This fund should be used for regular payment of salaries. It should not be used to pay any pending liabilities at present.
