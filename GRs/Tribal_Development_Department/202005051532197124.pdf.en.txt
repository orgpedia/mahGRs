Government's decision
The Finance Department vide Circular dated 16.04.2020 has approved the release of funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation of essential and programme expenditure for the first 3 months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020), considering the pandemic situation in the State.
Accordingly, No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 21.04.2020, for the schemes under the sub-development head" Welfare of Backward Classes" in Demand No. T-5 of the programme expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme, 10% of the total budgetary allocation for the specific objectives mentioned in the Government Circular under the reference of the Finance Department has been allocated. As per the Government decision dated 8.05.2020, 10% of the funds for 01-Salary and 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. At present, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement on Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 25% of the total budgetary allocation. In view of this, the Government is approving the release and expenditure of remaining 5% of the available funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities for disbursement of the objectives of 01-Salary and 36-Support Grants (Salary) for the schemes in Demand No. T-5 mentioned in the accompanying statement under the Programme Expenditure.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious for some time to come due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds. This fund should be used only for regular salary payments and not for payment of arrears.
3. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer may refer to Pillar No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 should be circulated immediately to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers under them. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
