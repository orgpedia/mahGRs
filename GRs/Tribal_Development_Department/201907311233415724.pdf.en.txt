Read: 1) Tribal Development Department Government Decision No. AVI 2017/ PR No.105/ KA.05 dated 01.08.2017.
Preamble: As per the decision of the State Government, 9th August is celebrated as" World Tribal Day" in the State. World Tribal Day 2019 will be held in Nagpur. Meritorious students, sportspersons, artistes and sportspersons will be felicitated on the occasion. The awards were distributed through an online system from the jurisdiction of 29 project offices in the state. Applications were invited till 30.07.2019. Applications have been received for each category. The government was considering the formation of a department-level committee to select eligible applicants for the awards after scrutinizing the applications received.
Government Decision: After scrutinizing the applications received for the award to be given on the occasion of World Tribal Day on 9th August, 2019, the following committee is being constituted at the block level to finalize the eligible applicants for the awards.
