The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Maharashtra State, Nashik shall ensure that the funds within the limits of the funds disbursed are immediately withdrawn and distributed to the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development/ Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project/ Scheme Implementing Mechanism.
Reference No. 4 of the Central Government for the year 2018-19 The administrative approval for the above schemes A.No. 1 and 2 has been issued at the Government level. The guidelines for the above schemes vide No. 4,5,6, 14 and 19 will be issued at the level of Government/ Commissionerate/ Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. For the rest of the schemes, the Additional Commissioner/ Project Officer gave the government decision. As per the powers conferred under 22.12.2017, guidelines for the implementation of the scheme should be issued and the modalities should be worked out for the preparation of the blueprint, identification of beneficiaries, assessment and disbursement of arrears etc. The concerned Additional Commissioner and Project Officer will be responsible for issuing administrative approval/ guidelines for these schemes and start the implementation of the schemes immediately.
The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik shall be responsible for obtaining monthly progress report of all the schemes approved by the Central Government from the concerned Additional Commissioner/ Project Officer and submit it to the Government within 10th of every month and to make available the financial and physical progress report of the scheme and utilization certificate regarding the expenditure incurred before further disbursement of funds to the concerned in respect of the schemes in operation.
Geo-tagged photographs and list of beneficiaries of the schemes to be implemented in the jurisdiction of the concerned Project Officer should be uploaded on the website www. stcmis. gov. in/ smis. The project officer concerned should ensure that the necessary documents regarding the implementation of the project are kept by the concerned project officer and it is preserved properly.
7. The grants sanctioned by this Government decision shall be spent for the purposes for which they have been sanctioned and the concerned shall take care to submit the utilization certificate along with the financial and physical report to the Government.
