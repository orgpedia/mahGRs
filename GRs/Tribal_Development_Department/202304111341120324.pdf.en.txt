Subject to the terms, conditions and conditions, the approval was granted vide Government Resolution dated 16.05.2012.
Shri Sainath Educational Institutions, Pratappur, Taloda. Taloda, Ta. Nandurbar run Aided Ashramshala, Jambhipani, Ta. Akkaluwa, G. Nandurbar School was shifted to Mauje Ranjani. The proposal received under reference No.6 regarding relocation to Taloda, G. Nandurbar was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Shri Sainath Educational Institutions, Pratappur, Taloda. Taloda, Ta. Nandurbar run Aided Ashramshala, Jambhipani, Ta. Akkaluwa, G. Nandurbar School was shifted to Mauje Ranjani. The government has decided to approve the relocation of Taloda, G. Nandurbar on the following terms and conditions.
All the students who were in the previous location of the school should be accommodated in the new location.
The institution should accommodate the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the old school in the new place.
There should be a separate building for school and hostel in the new place. Adequate facilities like toilets, drinking water and toilets should be provided.
4. Facilities and materials to be provided to the students.
The institution shall transfer the students of the said school to the new place at its own cost.
6. The condition of minimum marks of the transferred school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
It should be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval in case of a school that is transferred.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the acts, rules, orders, etc. prescribed by the Government from time to time in respect of ashramshalas, teachers/ non-teaching staff, salary/ post-pay grant, students, etc.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if fresh complaints are received with the Government or the Regional Officer of the Tribal Development Department regarding the transfer or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the transfer have been violated.
2. The Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik shall examine all the aspects and take necessary action regarding the transfer of the school by including the conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions, if necessary, and inform the government about it.
