The Katkari, Madia Gond and Kolam tribes of the state have been notified as Particularly Vulnerable Tribes by the central government. These people live in remote and mountainous areas. For this purpose, Rs.1 crore has been allocated for the development of primitive tribes for the year 2019-20. 2000.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget.
Of this, 60 per cent of the amount is Rs. 1200.00 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System. the Government Resolution
Under the State Scheme for Development of Primitive Tribes (STD), during the year 2019-20, Rs. 1200.00 lakh is being sanctioned for construction of 845 houses for tribal families on the lines of Shabari Gharkul Scheme.
The scheme for construction of houses should be implemented through the State Management Cell-Rural Housing established under the Department of Rural Development. The list of eligible beneficiaries will be made available to the State Management Cell-Rural Housing by the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project. The district-wise target and distribution of funds for households under the scheme will be as mentioned in the table below. In addition, the beneficiaries of the tribal families eligible for housing in the state should also be given priority benefits from other schemes of the department.
