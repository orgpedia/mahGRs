The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, at present, the Finance Department is making available the budgetary provisions of the schemes within the specified limit for disbursement on the monthly Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) of the schemes under the head Backward Classes Welfare under Tribal Component Programme as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Finance Department have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available 5% of the budgetary provision for the said purpose for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of the said funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 5% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01- Salary, 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the schemes mentioned in Table No. 2 of the accompanying prospectus under T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules of the state government, financial rules, provisions in the budget rule book and all the terms and conditions.
