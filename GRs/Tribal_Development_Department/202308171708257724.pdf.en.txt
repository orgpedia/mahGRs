The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been approved by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has made available funds under the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) for disbursement of funds within the limit of 48% of the budgetary allocation for the schemes under compulsory and programme expenditure such as salaries, scholarships and scholarships. In this regard, the programme expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme is included in the schemes of the Tribal Development Department in Chapter No. Considering the funds released earlier for the purpose of 1-Salary and 34-Scholarships and Scholarships, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the available funds to the concerned Controlling Officer within the limit available for disbursement.
Government Decision: Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. T-5 or below.
The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authority as per Rakaanah No. 5 for the schemes mentioned in Rakaanah No. 2 of the return.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the provisions and conditions laid down in the working rules, financial rules, budget rule book of the state government.
3. The said Government Decision is being issued by the Finance Department within the limits of the funds made available on the Budget Fund Distribution System within the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
