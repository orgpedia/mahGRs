Government's decision
Under the Tribal Component Programme, fruit trees and vegetables should be planted in the neighbourhood of tribal families. T-5, 2401, Crop Protection, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, Schemes under State Schemes, (1 (32) Planting of Fruits and Vegetables in the Backyard of Tribal Households, Head of Accounts (2401A806), 33, Financial Assistance. A sum of Rs. 24.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget Estimates 2019-20. Accordingly, the Agriculture Department No. 3 In pursuance of the proposal herein, to cultivate fruit trees and vegetables in the backyard of tribal families, under the head Accounts Title (2401A806), 33, Financial Assistance, with 60% limit of Rs. 14.40 lakh (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Forty Thousand only) is being sanctioned to the Additional Chief Secretary (Agriculture), Department of Agriculture and Padum, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budget Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure.
Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
