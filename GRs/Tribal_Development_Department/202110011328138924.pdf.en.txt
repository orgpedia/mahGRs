The Central Government provides grants to the State Governments every year under the scheme of Special Central Assistance, Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and the Primitive Tribes Development Programme. The proposals for grants received under this scheme are examined in detail by the State Government and selected proposals are submitted to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India. The Tribal Development Department proposed the scheme" Cage Fishery" to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs with the objective of providing training, guidance and business materials to the tribal families of the state, providing them with means of income and contributing to their livelihood by financing the tribal families to do fish business.
Ministry of Tribal Affairs in the meeting of Project Appraisal Committee held on 04/09/2014 has approved the implementation of" Cage Fishery" scheme with an outlay of Rs. 1000.00 lakh under Special Central Assistance Scheme for the year 2014-15. Accordingly, the funds provided by the Central Government vide Reference No. 2 The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has been transferred as per the decision of the Government here.
