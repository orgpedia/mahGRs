The Model Solar Village Project for PvTG habitations/ das/ village/ remote village under Special Central Assistance under the Government Order under the Corrigendum is Rs. 1500.00 lakh has been approved for implementation of the scheme. The scheme has been approved for the year 2015-16 under Special Central Assistance. However, the year of sanction of the scheme has been revised to 2016-17. Also, in the scope of the scheme implementation, the project office Aheri has been mentioned twice and the project office Gadchiroli has been mentioned. Accordingly, the government decided to amend the law.
(2) Now, accordingly, in the Government Order in context and in the Annexure thereto.
Where the year of sanction of the scheme is mentioned as" 2016-17," the year of sanction of the scheme shall be read as" 2015-16."
Point number 4 in Annexure 1 with the Governing Decision mentions Project Office Aheri twice in the scope of the scheme. The project office is mentioned only once and the project office Gadchiroli is included.
