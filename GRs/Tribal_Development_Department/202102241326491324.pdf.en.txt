Government's decision
The circular dated 16.04.2020 of the Finance Department has prescribed the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21, considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona virus. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided from time to time on the Budget Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 As per the Government decisions, the provision of 90% limit for the purpose of 01-Wage for the schemes under the sub-heading of Backward Classes Welfare under the Tribal Component Programme has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. Now, in the month of March, funds have been made available for disbursement in the budgetary allocation system by the Finance Department for the target of 01-salary with a ceiling of 95%.
Accordingly, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of the remaining funds to the concerned Controlling Authority as per column-5 of the accompanying statement, within the limit provided by the Finance Department for the schemes in Demand Number T-5 of the program cost under the Tribal Component Program.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be serious for some time to come due to the outbreak of corona virus, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. Only regular salary should be paid from the fund allocated for salary.
No pending dues should be paid at present.
