It has been approved to form a study group under the decision of the government under the context of studying the DBT policy for the students of government ashramshalas and hostels for personal use items and stationery, books, etc. as well as for the students of hostels at the district division and municipal corporation level.
The government's decision
Salary and Allowances:
Members other than those retired from Government service and non-official members are entitled to an attendance allowance of Rs. 5000/- and all the members will be paid at the rate of 2 Tier AC tickets of the Railways depending on the journey done physically.
All arrangements regarding the study group meetings should be made by the regional offices.
In place of this,
Salary and Allowances:
Attendance allowance to the members retired from Government service, other members and non-official members for the period of the Committee is Rs. 5000/- and all the members will be paid at the rate of 2 Tier AC tickets of the Railways depending on the journey done physically.
All arrangements regarding the study group meetings should be made by the regional offices.
