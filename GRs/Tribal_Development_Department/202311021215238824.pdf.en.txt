Introduction: The state government gets funds from the central government for the implementation of various central schemes. The 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Controlling Administrative Cost to State/ UT for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes has been launched from 2023-24 and guidelines have been issued under reference 1.
Reference 2: The Central Government has issued guidelines for managing the distribution and allocation of funds for Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the Finance Department has issued guidelines vide Circular No. 3. Accordingly, it is now under the consideration of the Government to declare a Nodal Officer, State Nodal Agency (SNA), and Implementing Agency for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Administrative Cost to State/ UT for implementation of the scheme.
Government's decision
The Government is approving the declaration of Nodal Officer, Single Nodal Agency (SNA), and Implementing Agency for the implementation of the 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Administrative Cost to State/ UT for implementation of the scheme.
