Introduction: Government hostels are run by the department to facilitate the students of Scheduled Tribes to pursue higher education. A total of 495 government hostels have been sanctioned under the Tribal Development Department, out of which 491 hostels are currently functional. Around 56,000 students are studying in these hostels every year. Salaries and allowances, travel allowance, provident fund advances, loan payments and all other dues of the employees of the said Government Hostels
The Housekeepers have been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers from time to time under the decisions of the Government of the day herein referred to above for making such payments. However, in some hostels, if the post of housekeeper is vacant for some reason, in such places, additional charge is assigned as in-charge housekeeper. However, since the in-charge Home Guard was not declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer, in such a situation, there was a problem in sending the salary related payments of the employees and payments for other matters to the Treasury Branch, so the matter of declaring the in-charge Home Guard as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
