Under the Tribal Component Programme, special assistance is provided to the beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes under the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme being implemented by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Accordingly, in the financial year 2022-23, as per the Government Circular No.1 of the Department of Finance, the available provisions within the limit of 14% of the budgetary provisions under these schemes have been disbursed to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Department of Finance.
The Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) of the Department of Finance has fixed the ceiling of fund allocation for these schemes at 21 per cent. 3 As per the proposal here, the matter of 7% of the funds available for distribution to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. The following funds are being sanctioned for the schemes of T-5, TOP 2235, Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme in Social Security and Welfare and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme in the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 (Rs. million).
