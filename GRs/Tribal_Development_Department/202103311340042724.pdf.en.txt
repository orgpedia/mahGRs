The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Legislative Assembly has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 for the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, as a temporary measure for the April month, provided for the following specific objectives on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS):

Accordingly, the School Education Department has been allocated 10% of the funds for salary matters under the headings 2202-All General Education under the Government Resolution No. 2. Now the Finance Department has made available 5 percent increase in funds for salary on the Budget Fund Distribution System (BEAMS) and the matter was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2021-22, Demand No. The Government is approving the release of funds to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department within the limit of 5% of the budgetary allocations for the following schemes in respect of salary matters under the head of T-5, 2202 General Education.
