the order
In pursuance of the proposal received from the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Tribal Development, Nashik under the aforesaid Reference No. (2) along with the application made by Smt.Rekha Vikram Sanap in the aforesaid Reference No. (3), the Head of the Administrative Department is pleased to direct the Commissioner, Department of Finance, in terms of the powers conferred on him by the Government Resolution No.Anneo 2017/ PR No. 29/ Service-4, dated 29th September, 2018, in the Reference No. Vikram Sheshrao Sanap (Mayat Chowkidar), Government Tribal Girls Hostel (New), Deori, Ta. Deori is sanctioning ex-gratia of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only) to the nominee/ legal heir of G. Gondia, Smt. Rekha Vikram Sanap.
This" ex-gratia" is being sanctioned on account of death of a Government servant on 03.09.2019 before completion of 10 years of Government service. The order will remain valid for a period of one year from the date of order.
The expenditure should be made under the head" Demand No. G-6,2071- Pension and other retirement benefits 01 Civil, 103, Compassionate Allowances, (00) (06)- Defined Contributions/ Grants-in-Aid under National Pension Scheme (20710722)."
