Preamble: Detailed orders have been issued under the Government Decision dated 10.09.2011 of the Finance Department to review the restrictions and contours regarding creation of posts, recruitment and revival of posts and establishment and management of cells under the Additional Cadre Schemes.
In pursuance of the Government decision dated 10.09.2001 of the Finance Department, a total of 134 posts of Tribal Development (Self) Department were demarcated under the Government decision of Reference No. 2 of the Department. Thereafter, on the establishment of Tribal Development (Self) Department vide Reference No. 3 and Reference No. A total of 18 new posts have been created under the decision of the 4th government.
In the 9th meeting of the High Level Committee held on 18.11.2021 under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Chief Secretary, taking into consideration the contours of the department as decided as on 07.03.2006 and the newly created posts, a total of 148 posts have been approved including 133 regular posts and 15 external posts to be taken up in the establishment of Tribal Development (Self) Department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, the revised pattern of 148 posts in the Tribal Development Department (itself) as indicated in Form A below is being approved.
