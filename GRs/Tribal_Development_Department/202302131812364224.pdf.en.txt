Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 31/03/2022 under section 3054 2722, Rs. 2410 road and bridge improvement works were sanctioned at a cost of Rs. 499.99 crore. However, the Government had received complaints that some of the sanctioned works had been accorded administrative sanction by the respective District Councils even when they were outside the tribal areas and some of the works were not permissible/ in violation of the norms. Therefore, the said works were suspended vide Government letter dated 25/04/2022. In this regard, the Government vide its decision dated 31/03/2022 has sanctioned Rs. All road and bridge improvement works worth Rs. 499.99 crore are being cancelled.
