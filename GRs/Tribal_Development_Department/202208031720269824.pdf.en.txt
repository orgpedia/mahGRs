The Proposal:
Finance Department No. 1 here. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month.
Accordingly, as per the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) available for distribution on the distribution system, the establishment under the Programme Expenditure of Tribal Development Department, Subject No. 02- Wages; 6- Telephone, Electricity and Water charges; 10- Contractual services; 13- Office expenses 28% of the budgeted provisions and 14- Lease and taxes 21% of the budgeted provisions. 2 has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Government decisions here.
Now, in the month of August, the Finance Department has made available additional funds for distribution on the Budget Fund Distribution System for the above matters and the matter of disbursement of the increased funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under the consideration of the Government.
Government decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 in the accompanying statement under Demand T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
