The Government has decided to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal welfare to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA 5% Direct Benefit Scheme to Gram Panchayats).
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000.00 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand Only) has been made available for disbursement to 101 villages of 55 Gram Sabhas in Murbad Panchayat Samiti area under Thane Zilla Parishad, 72 villages of 39 Gram Sabhas in Bhiwandi Panchayat Samiti area, 230 villages of 110 Gram Sabhas in Shahapur Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts. 485.1643/- Rs. 8,71,17, 065.84/- (Rs. Eight crore seventy one lakh seventeen thousand fifty four eighty five paise only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. It is also provided
The utilization certificate of the fund should be submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane to this department.
