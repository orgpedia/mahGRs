Government circular
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-paragraph (1) of paragraph 5 of the Fifth Schedule to the Constitution of India, the Governor is pleased to appoint a Governor to fill up the posts of Direct Service in 17 cadres in the Scheduled Areas of the State. Notification dated 29.08.2019 has been issued. Guidelines for implementation of this notification have been issued under the Government Decision No. 2 and No. 3 of the General Administration Department and the Government Preamble.
The Scheduled Tribe candidates who have applied for the above mentioned direct service posts should provide the Resident Certificate of the Scheduled Area (PESA) to the candidates within the stipulated time by the concerned Project Office, Integrated Tribal Development Project Office.
