Introduction: The Jury and Board of Assessors were appointed by the Government Resolution No. 2 and Government Corrigendum No. 3 at Vacha to appoint an architect to prepare the concept and construction plan for the Gondwana Tribal Cultural Museum and Training Sub-Centre at Nagpur. The Tribal Development Department has an independent Construction Management Cell, which has been created in 2016 to manage the construction of Ashramshalas, hostels, offices etc. under the Tribal Development Department. Therefore, the matter of appointing a revised jury and board of assessors for the Gondwana Tribal Cultural Museum and Training Sub-Centre was under the consideration of the government.
Decision of the Government: As per the rules of the Council of Architecture, New Delhi, a Jury and Board of Assessors are being appointed to scrutinize the ideas received from the architect for the preparation of the concept and construction plan of the Gondwana Tribal Cultural Museum and Training Sub-Centre.
