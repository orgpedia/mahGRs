The Proposal:
By providing sports facilities to the students of Scheduled Tribes to develop their talent, the students of Scheduled Tribes can excel at the state and national level in many sports. Considering this, the matter of formulating a sports policy for the development of scheduled students through the Tribal Development Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A committee is being constituted by the Tribal Development Department to formulate a sports policy for providing sports facilities to the students of Scheduled Tribes.
