The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the Government Circular dated 16.03.2023, the fund allocation limit has been fixed in the Revised Estimates for the year 2022-23 for the schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure. Budget Estimates, Allocations and Controls (BEAMS) have also been made available for disbursement on the delivery system for the remaining entire fund disbursement within the limit of 100% of the revised estimates for specific objectives of the schemes. In this regard, the matter of disbursement of funds available for the remaining disbursement to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under consideration of the Government considering the funds previously disbursed under the Government Decisions at Reference No.2 for the mandatory expenditure schemes of the Tribal Development Department.
