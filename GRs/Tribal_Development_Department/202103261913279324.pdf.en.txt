The Proposal:
Under this scheme, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides grants-in-aid to the District Councils for educational fees, examination fees, maintenance and scholarships to the students of Scheduled Tribes admitted in the aided hostels under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds as requested by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance under Reference No. 2 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for payment of educational fee, examination fee, maintenance and scholarship to the students of Scheduled Tribes admitted in the aided hostels under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Demand No. 2225 of T-5- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities to the District Councils under the head Subsidiary Grants for Education Fee, Examination Fee, Protection and Scholarship etc.
