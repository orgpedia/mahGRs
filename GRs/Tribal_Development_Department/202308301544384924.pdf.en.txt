The Proposal:
As many as 4,638 ST students are enrolled in 110 aided hostels under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Under the Tribal Component Programme, grants to the Zila Parishads for educational fees, examination fees, maintenance and scholarships are provided to the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department for the remuneration of the employees of such hostels under this scheme. In this regard, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has decided to pay the pending honorarium of the employees of the aided hostels under reference No. The proposal was submitted to the Finance Department. The proposal was under the consideration of the government for disbursement of funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme Demand No. In accordance with the approval of the Finance Department to pay the pending honorarium of the employees of the aided hostels under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department under the head of T-5, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, grants to the Zilla Parishads for educational fees, examination fees, maintenance and scholarships have been sanctioned for the scheme with a ceiling of 40% of Rs. 150.00 lakh is being approved for disbursement and expenditure from the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24 as follows:
