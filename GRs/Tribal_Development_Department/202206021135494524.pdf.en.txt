The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. In pursuance of this, funds are being made available for distribution in a limited manner on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system through the Finance Department through Government Circular No. 1 in the context of Finance Department. Under the schemes being implemented by the Department of School Education under the Tribal Component Programme as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system by the Department of Finance, 25% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) has been disbursed to the Department of School Education as per the Government decisions at Reference No. 2.
Now that a total of 32% of the budgetary provision has been made available for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for disbursement through the Finance Department, the matter of disbursement of funds to the School Education Department in accordance with the proposal at No. 3 was under the consideration of the Government for disbursement of the remaining available additional 7% funds.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, under the year 2022-23, Demand No. 2202 General Education in T-5, taking into account the devolved provision within the limit of 25% of the annual budgetary provision for salaries as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department under the head of General Education, the remaining 7% of the total amount of Rs. 729.6065 lakh to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
