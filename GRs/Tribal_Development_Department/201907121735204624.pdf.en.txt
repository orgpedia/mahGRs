Under the Tribal Component Programme, the School Education Department implements the schemes of grant-in-aid to general secondary schools, grant-in-aid to non-government junior colleges and assistance to the batch of tribal students in Sainik Schools. Accordingly, out of the total annual budgetary provision for these schemes under the Tribal Component Programme for Tribal Students 2019-20, funds for the four months April, 2019 to July, 2019 have been released to the States under reference No. 2 has been handed over to the School Education Department.
