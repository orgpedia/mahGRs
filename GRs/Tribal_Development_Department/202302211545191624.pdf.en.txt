The preamble-
The Government of India has launched a new 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme, Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY-G) from 2021-22 to provide integrated economic and social development of villages with Scheduled Tribes population on the model village concept.
For this, the Central Government has issued guidelines under Reference 1. In pursuance of these guidelines, the State Government has issued guidelines under the Government Resolution at Reference 2. For the year 2021-22 and 2022-23, Rs. 11071.44 Lakhs and Rs. 2414.06 as against Rs. 13481.44 lakh has been released to the State Government. Now, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse this fund from the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23.
the Government Resolution
The Central Government has released Rs. 13481.44 Lakhs (Rs. One hundred and forty four crore units target forty four thousand rupees only) in the financial year 2022-23
This Government resolution approves the allocation to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik from the budgetary provision.
