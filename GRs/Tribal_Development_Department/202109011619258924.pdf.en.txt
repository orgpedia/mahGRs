the prelude
To provide education in English medium residential schools under the Tribal Component Programme. Under this scheme, funds will be released from the provisions in the current year's budget to pay the pending fees for the previous year 2020-21 and the fees due for the current academic year 2021-22. The proposal was submitted to the Finance Department. In pursuance of this proposal, under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. Under T-5, the head of accounts, head 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities (02) (29) Teaching of Scheduled Tribe students in English medium reputed residential schools, head of accounts (2225 C 999), Chapter No. Under Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaried) Budget 2021-22, Rs. 370.00 crore as per the approval of the Finance Department out of the provision of Rs. A sum of Rs. 141.21 crore was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
