Government's decision
The Annual Budget for the financial year 2019-20 has been approved in the Monsoon Session, 2019 and the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2019 (including the appropriation of accounts) has been passed by the Legislature and the Bill has become an Act. As per the Act, No. Under Section 3, funds have been sanctioned for the first nine months in proportion to the budgetary provisions in the Budget Estimates for 2019-20 with a ceiling of 60%. Accordingly, under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. The Government is approving the expenditure on Budget Fund Distribution System for the nine months April, 2019 to December, 2019 out of the total budgetary provision on Plan/ Controller Officer wise total mandatory expenditure mentioned in Column No. 2 of the Statement along with mandatory expenditure from T-1 to T-4 and T-8.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Accordingly, all the stakeholders should ensure that the expenditure for the nine months from April, 2019 to December, 2019 is as per the cash flow available on the computer distribution system.
