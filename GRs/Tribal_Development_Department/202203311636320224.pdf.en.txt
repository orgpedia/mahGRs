The disbursement of the said funds is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions:
In the accompanying prospectus, the works mentioned district-wise are mentioned as No. As per section 4, the funds are being distributed work-wise and the concerned Controlling Officer should take care of spending the funds on these works.
Before issuing the work order, it should be ensured that the work is in the tribal sub-plan area/ additional tribal sub-plan area/ mada area/ minimada area. No work orders should be issued for any work outside such areas.
(iii) It shall be ensured that the works in the accompanying statement have not been carried out earlier in any other scheme or have not been sanctioned in any other scheme in the recent past.
(iv) Before giving work orders in the accompanying prospectus, it shall be ensured that the circulars and guidelines issued in this regard by the Government from time to time have been complied with.
V. In the present case, the Tribal Development Department, Government Resolution No. TSP 2003/ p. No.101/ Ka-6, D. July 26, 2004. TSP 2005/ Q.No.59/ Ka-6, D. dated 10th November, 2005 and No. Annexure- 2019/ S. No.81/ K-14, D. Care should be taken to strictly comply with the terms of August 9, 2019.
VI. Since the distribution of funds is work-wise, the funds should be spent on the same works. Also, it should be ensured that the sanctioned administrative cost remains within the limit.
The responsibility of fulfilling the above conditions will be of the concerned Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, District Council.
