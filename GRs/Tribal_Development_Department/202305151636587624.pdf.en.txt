Preamble: Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Swayam Yojana has been launched to provide food, accommodation and other educational materials to the students of Scheduled Tribes in the total number of 20,000 who have taken admission in the post-Class XII courses from the academic year 2016-17 but have not been admitted to the government hostels.
Read No. 1 of the Governing Decision (a) In the other criteria point no. The maximum age of the students to take advantage of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Swayam Yojana is more than 28 years
There is no provision. There have been demands from various quarters to revise the age limit to 30. In view of this demand, the matter of raising the maximum age of students to 30 years to take advantage of the Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Swayam Yojana was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 1. The maximum age of the students for availing the benefit of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Swayam Yojana should not be more than 30 years. This change has been approved by the government.
Further, the rest of the terms and conditions of the Government Decision read 1, 2 and 3 shall remain the same as before.
The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik will be responsible for the improvement of the existing computer system in accordance with the above amendments.
