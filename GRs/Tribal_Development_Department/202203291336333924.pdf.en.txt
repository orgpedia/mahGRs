The Proposal:
Agriculture Department in the State. The Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme is being implemented since 2018. Under this scheme, the subsidy payable to the beneficiary farmers is provided for three years subject to the provisions of the scheme. Accordingly, the proposal of the Department of Agriculture regarding the release of funds from the allocation under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2021-22 for the payment of pending liabilities of Scheduled Tribe farmers for the year 2018-19 and 2019-20 was under consideration of the Government as per the approval of the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for Scheduled Tribes, Demand No. T-5, MAIN ACCOUNT 2401- Under the Crop Promotion Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme, the following funds are being sanctioned and spent from the budgetary provision for the year 2021-22 with the approval of the Finance Department to pay the pending liabilities of the Scheduled Tribe farmers for the year 2018-19 and 2019-20.
