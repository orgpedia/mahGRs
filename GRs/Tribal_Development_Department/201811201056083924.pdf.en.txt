Corrigendum
Read No. 2 The paragraphs in the Government Preamble here are as follows: As per the Government decision dated 10/10/2013, the benefit of the scheme can be given if the slope is less than 8% and the slope is more than 8%, therefore, the scheme should be implemented only in the lands received under the Forest Rights Act rather than the slope of the land.
Instead, read the following paragraph.
Government Resolution No. 2013/ No. 114/ Water-7 of the Department of Water Conservation 10/10/2013 and D. As per the Government decision dated 15/10/2013, the benefit of the scheme can be given if the slope is less than 8 percent and the benefit of the Padakai scheme if the slope is more than 8 percent.
