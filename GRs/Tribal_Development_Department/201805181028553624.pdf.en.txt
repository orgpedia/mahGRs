Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. 2435-Other Agricultural Programmes, 60-Others, 796-Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) (01) Loan Waiver to Farmers under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017 (2435 0106) in the year 2018-19. A budgetary provision of Rs. 940.00 crore has been made.
Department of Co-operation, Textiles and Commerce, No. Under the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana-2017, as proposed by the Nasti here, the budgetary provision under the Tribal Component Programme to waive off loans of farmers belonging to Scheduled Tribes in the state has been increased from Rs. In pursuance of the demand for providing funds to the tune of Rs. 150.00 crore vide Demand No. 2435 Other Agricultural Programmes in T-5, 60-Others, 796-Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) (01) Loan Waiver to Scheduled Tribe Farmers under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017 (Head of Accounts- 2435 0106) In the first phase of 2018-19, Rs. 150.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crores only) is being approved by this Government to be disbursed to the Additional Chief Secretary, Cooperation, Textiles Department (Cooperation) as the Head of the Administrative Department of the Department implementing the scheme on the Budget Distribution System.
In this case, the head of the department/ controlling officer should take care that the funds are used only for the loan waiver of the farmers of the Scheduled Tribes. Also, the Controlling Officer concerned should send the report of the expenditure incurred from the provision made, the material objectives achieved, the utilization certificate, etc. to the Tribal Development Department, Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Mumbai; Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Cooperation, Marketing and Textile Department by the 10th of every month.
