The Proposal:
Agriculture Improvement Scheme under Tribal Component Programme Agriculture under National Security Mission
The Department of Agriculture and Padum has decided to allocate funds from the provisions of 2019-20 and 2020-21 for increasing the production of oilseeds in the paddy field (TRFA-OilSeed) for the food crops being implemented by the Padum Department. The matter of approving the proposal submitted under section 5 was under the consideration of the Government.
