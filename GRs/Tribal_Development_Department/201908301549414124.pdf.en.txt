The Government of Maharashtra has created a separate Tribal Development Department in the year 1983 for the development of tribal applications. At present, 29 projects are being implemented in the district. The reference No. Under the Government decision of 03rd July, approval has been accorded for setting up of a new Integrated Tribal Development Project Office at Wardha. Accordingly, a total of 36 posts are required for the Project Office, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Wardha Office, out of which 31 posts have been made available by transferring from the Integrated Tribal Development Office in Bhandara, Nagpur, Mumbai and Solapur. Also, 1 post of Chowkidar will be filled through external sources. The government was considering the creation of 4 new posts of Project Officer, Assistant Accounts Officer, Office Superintendent and Research Assistant.
Government's decision
The Integrated Tribal Development Project, Wardha office under the Tribal Development Department has been started and the Government is approving the creation of 4 new posts as shown in the table below.
