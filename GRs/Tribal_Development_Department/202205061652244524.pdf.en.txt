The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, vide Government Circular No.1 of the Department of Finance, funds amounting to 25% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of salary on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) have been made available for disbursement through the Finance Division. Accordingly, 10% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the schemes being implemented by the School Education Department under the Tribal Component Programme has been disbursed to the School Education Department under the Government decision at Reference No. 2. Now, the matter of disbursement of funds to the School Education Department was under consideration of the Government in accordance with the proposal at No. 3 regarding the release of the remaining 15% of the funds available for disbursement.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, under the year 2022-23, Demand No. Taking into account the devolved provision within the limit of 10% of the annual budgetary provision for salaries as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department under the head General Education 2202 in T-5, the remaining 15% of the total amount of Rs. 1563.4475 lakh to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
