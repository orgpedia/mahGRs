The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been approved by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has decided to provide 34% of the total budgetary allocation under the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system for mandatory and programme expenditure on wages for schemes, 30% for 10-contractual services, 17-computer expenditure and 50-other expenditure.
20% of the funds will be allocated for distribution. Accordingly, under the mandatory expenditure plans of the Tribal Development Department, Chapter No. It was under consideration of the Government to disburse 10% of the budgetary provisions to the concerned Controlling Authorities for 1- Salary, 10- 20% for Contractual Services, 17- Computer Expenditure and 50- Other Expenditure.
Government Decision: Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. T-5 for the schemes mentioned in Rakaana No. 2 of the accompanying return under item No. 5. The Government is approving to disburse and spend 10% of the budgetary provisions to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the matters of 1- Salary, 10- 20% for Contractual Services, 17- Computer Expenditure and 50- Other Expenditure.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the provisions and conditions laid down in the working rules, financial rules, budget rule book of the state government.
