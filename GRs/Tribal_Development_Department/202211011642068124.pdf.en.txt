Read:- Tribal Development Department Government Decision No. Scholarship-2021/ PR No. 90/ K-12 dated 17.01.2022
GOVERNMENT REFERENCE: The revised guidelines for disbursement of Post Matric Scholarship to Scheduled Tribes students have been issued vide GOVERNMENT DECISION dated 17.01.2022.
Issue No. 5.11 of the said Government Decision states that the scheme will not be applicable to the students whose admission to the professional courses has been done through the Institutional Level Quota and Management Level Quota as well as Spot Round or Mock Up Round. The mention of" Spot Round or Mock Up Round" is being omitted. The benefit of scholarship will be admissible to the students selected through" Spot Round or Mop Up Round."
