2. Action should be taken on the basis of government decisions, circulars, instructions etc. issued by the government from time to time in accordance with the Goods and Services Act.
Works should be started only after ensuring that the above mentioned works have not been done from any other fund and the process of adjustment/ closure of the said Ashramshalas is not started. The concerned project officer will be responsible for ensuring that no work is left undone.
The budget includes furniture (5%), electrification (2%), water supply and sanitation (2%) and Goods and Services Tax (18%). Care should be taken to keep the financial limit of this work within the limit of the amount of administrative approval.
The tendering process for these works should be done in accordance with the prevailing procedures/ rules. Also, the financial and physical report of the utility certificate related to the construction should be submitted to the government in the prescribed form.
After the completion of the work, the responsibility of uploading the video/ photographs on the website of the department and the central government and geo-tagging of the work will be of the concerned project officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project and the concerned Public Works (Tribal Department). After the completion of the said works, a board should be put up at the work place regarding the completion of the said works through the central scheme and the work should be evaluated through the third party system.
7. The scheme should be implemented through the respective Public Works (Tribal) Departments who prepare the budgets.
