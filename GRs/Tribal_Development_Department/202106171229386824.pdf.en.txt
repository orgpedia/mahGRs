Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Government of India, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, has decided to provide reservation of posts in the State Government. As per instructions issued vide notification dated 04.01.2021, the Tribal Development Department, Govt. 16.02.2021 and dt. As on 23.02.2021, 92 posts of Direct Service in Group A, B, C and D of the Department have been ensured for Divyangjan. As mentioned in the above Government decision, all the posts in the promotion chain of the above mentioned 92 posts are to be ensured for the respective disabled categories. Further, if the duties and responsibilities of a post are in accordance with the above 92 posts ensured for the disabled categories, it is deemed that the post is ensured for the respective category of disabled, even if the designation of the said post is different. Thus, it was under the consideration of the government to publish the list of all the posts in Group-A, B, C and D cadres in the promotion chain to be ensured for the disabled in the department.
