Preamble: Under the Special Central Assistance Scheme for 2017-18, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 262.00 lakh for the scheme" Livelihood Generation through Bamboo based Enterprises" as per reference No. 1 is given above. This fund reference No. 2& 3 has been allotted to Commissioner Tribal Development, Nashik as per the decision of the Government. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed between the Tribal Development Department and Konkan Bamboo and Cane Development Centre (KONBAC) to implement the scheme in the jurisdiction of Integrated Tribal Development Project Office, Jawhar and Dahanu in Palghar district. Entrepreneurship life skills training in making furniture of international standards from bamboo, a natural resource available in the region, will be imparted by KONBAC, Sindhudurg. Under this scheme, the youth of the state will get employment for 12 months and will be able to run their livelihood.
KONBAC is registered under Section 25 of the Companies Act, 2004. This organization works to uplift the lifestyle of the people in backward, poor rural areas. The institute has a long experience of working in bamboo processing, bamboo cultivation, manufacturing of various items from bamboo and providing training in this regard. In the last 8 years, the institute has worked on various projects related to bamboo processing, training, capacity building, etc. with various departments of the Central Government. It has also worked on projects such as Bamboo Furniture Manufacturing Unit, Bamboo Craft Manufacturing Unit, Bamboo Costruction Technology Demonstration Unit, Bamboo Construction Components, Fabrication and Erectionunit Unit. Therefore, it is proposed to approve the guidelines for the implementation of the scheme" Livelihood Generation through Bamboo Based Enterprises" through this organization, which is known and has a long experience of working in this field.
The government has decided to make international quality furniture from bamboo as part of its programme to provide entrepreneurship, life skills training and employment to the youth.
