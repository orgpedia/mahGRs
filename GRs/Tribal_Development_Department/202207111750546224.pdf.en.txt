The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the sub-plan area.
