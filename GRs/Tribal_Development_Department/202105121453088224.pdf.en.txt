The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Department of School Education has been allocated funds to the extent of 15% for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the schemes being implemented by the Department of School Education under the Tribal Component Programme as made available on the Budget Estimate, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Department of Finance. 2 has been distributed here by government decision.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds amounting to 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2021-22, Demand No. 2202 General Education in T-5, the total out of the limit of 10% of the annual budgetary provision for salaries as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department under the head of General Education is Rs. 625.00 lakh to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
