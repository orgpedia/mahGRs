The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. Guidelines have been issued for taking fiscal measures by setting spending priorities under Government Decisions 1 and 2. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) from time to time, funds within the limit of 75% of the budgetary provision for the salary component of the schemes covered under the mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department, subject to the approval of the Board. 2 has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Government decisions here.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement the provisions within the limit of 85% of the budgetary provisions for the mandatory expenditure schemes on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of the remaining funds to the concerned Controlling Authority was under consideration of the Government considering the provision made earlier for the matter of salaries under the Compulsory Expenditure Scheme.
Government's decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Rakaana No. 5 for the schemes in Rakaana No. 2 in the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the terms and conditions of the state government.
