3.The concerned senior officers are informed that the above officers who have been transferred should be relieved immediately to join the place of transfer, and the information about when the said officers were relieved to join the place of transfer and the date on which they joined the post should be informed to the government immediately.
4. No leave shall be granted to the concerned officers without prior approval of the Government. Also, under Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave application of the said officer shall be accepted by the parent office. If such applications are received at the originating office, they should be delivered at the registered post to the original address of the concerned officer. If the original office sends this application to the government, it will not be taken into consideration.
5. It may be noted that in case of non-appearance at the place of posting immediately as per the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of political pressure, disciplinary action will be initiated against the concerned officers considering the matter as misconduct as the act violates Rule 23 of the M.N.S. (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
These orders will come into effect immediately.
