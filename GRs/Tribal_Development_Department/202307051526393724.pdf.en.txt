The Proposal:
The Central Government has issued guidelines under Reference 1 to launch the scheme of Adi Adarsh Gram Vikas for integrated economic and social development of villages with ST population based on the concept of model village. In order to have clarity in the state level institutions for implementation of the scheme, the State Government has issued Guidelines based on the suggestions of the Central Government under the Government Resolution Reference 2. The scheme will be implemented from 2021-22 to 2025-26. For this, the Central Government has selected 3605 villages and every year one-fifth of the villages will be given financial assistance of about Rs. 20.38 lakh will be provided to each village.
