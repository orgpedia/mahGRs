The Proposal -
In line with the implementation of the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme in urban areas, read the Tribal Development Department No. According to 1. The guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision dated 11th January, 2024. Also Read No. 2 d. A total of 5175 targets have been set for the three districts of Nagpur, Nandurbar, and Kolhapur as per the government decision dated 20.02.2024. Considering the additional demand received from the people's representatives and the field office, the government was considering setting up an additional target for urban areas under the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme.
Government Decision - Considering the budgetary allocations available for the Hahari area under the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme in the financial year 2023-24 and the period at the end of the financial year, the Government is approving setting of district-wise incremental targets / targets for the financial year 2023-24 as per the Annexure attached to this Government Decision.
