The Proposal:
""" In order to ensure time-bound disposal of forest rights claims and appeals by strictly implementing the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006, Rules, 2008 and Amendment Rules, 2012, the Government has decided to implement the"""" Vanamitra Yojana"""" under the provisions of the said Act.""" 2 The instructions have been issued by the government circular here. Under this, the forest area in individual forest rights claims approved by the district level forest rights committee is being processed by the Land Records Department, for which the necessary funds are made available to the concerned collector as a" nodal agency" through the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. In this regard, the Commissioner, Tribal and Research and Training Institute, Pune, has issued instructions to the concerned District Collectors to provide funds for the enumeration of forest lands in forest rights claims by the Land Records Offices. Proposal 4 has been submitted. The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
