The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Development Department, the government provides maintenance grant, building rent grant and contingency grant to the aided ashramshalas. In the current academic year, classes from 5th to 12th were started for a limited period due to the outbreak of Corona virus. A decision has been taken regarding the grant-in-aid to the Ashramshalas funded under No. 4 and No. 5 this year. The government was planning to allocate funds for the purpose.
Government's decision:
Under the Demand No. T-5 of the Tribal Development Department under the head of 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities (01) (73), under the scheme of Aided Ashramshalas run by NGOs (2225F129), in Chapter No. 31 Under Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) Rs. The Government is approving the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 9593.47 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Nine Crore Ninety Three Lakh Forty Seven Thousand only) to the Controlling Officer and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
The funds are being distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. The said fund shall be utilized for the purpose of payment of grant-in-aid to the aided Ashramshalas for the academic year 2020-21, as determined under Reference Nos. 4 and 5.
