The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 under the mandatory expenditure schemes are being made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month.
Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) as made available for distribution on the distribution system under the mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department under the schemes viz. 01- 70% of the budgetary provision for wages; 49% of the budgetary provisions for office matters viz., 02-Wages, 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water charges, 10-Contractual services, 13-Office expenses and 14-Lease and Taxes; 45 100% for interest and 35% for other items. 2 has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Government decisions here.
Now, the Finance Department has introduced item No. 01- 5% of the total budgeted fund for salaries and 7% of the total budgeted fund for other items has been made available for distribution on the Budget Fund Distribution System. The matter of disbursement of these funds to the concerned controlling authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per Racket No. 5 for the schemes mentioned in Racket No. 2 of the accompanying statement under the demand for schemes T-2, T-3, T-4 and T-8 under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department.
