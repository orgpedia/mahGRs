In pursuance of the proposal received from the Commissioner, Tribal Development Commissionerate, Nashik under the aforesaid Reference No. (2) and in pursuance of the application made by Shri Sunil Chander Adhav in the aforesaid Reference No. (3), the Head of the Administrative Department has directed him to send a copy of the proposal to the Government in the Finance Department vide Government Resolution No. 29/ Service-4, dated 29th September, 2018. Suman Sadashiv Bhosale (Myat Safaigar) is sanctioning ex-gratia of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only) to his nominee/ legal heir Mr. Sunil Chander Adhav.
This" ex-gratia" is being sanctioned on account of death of a Government servant on 24.07.2013 before completion of 10 years of Government service. The order will remain valid for a period of one year from the date of order.
The expenditure should be made under the head" Demand No. G-6,2071- Pension and other retirement benefits 01 Civil, 103, Compassionate Allowances, (00) (06)- Defined Contributions/ Grants-in-Aid under National Pension Scheme (20710722)."
