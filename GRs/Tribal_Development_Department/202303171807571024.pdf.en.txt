The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the year 2022-23, under the head Demand Number T-6, Rs. 3000.00 lakhs in the Budget and revised estimate of Rs. 2210.00 lakhs have been sanctioned. Accordingly, as per the approval of the Finance Department, the State has been provided with 70% of the budgetary allocation to meet the expenditure up to March, 2023. The matter of disbursement of funds made available on the Budget Estimate Distribution System under this head was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. Under Construction of Hostels in T-6 (TSP) (4225 1083), 53-Major Structures, out of the budgetary provision for the year 2022-23, Rs. 709.62 lakh (Rupees Seven Crore Nine Lakh Sixty Two Thousand only) is being sanctioned to the Controller and Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nashik/ Nagpur as follows:

The fund is being distributed on the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) and the fund should be spent on a total of 9 works as mentioned in the attached statement within the limit mentioned in Rakaana 4.
The disbursement of the said funds is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The Controlling Authority concerned shall ensure that the expenditure is spent only on the works included in Annexure-D of the Budget Publication and within the administrative sanction/ revised administrative limit.
2) The concerned Controlling Officer shall ensure that the said funds are spent only on the 9 works mentioned in the accompanying statement.
The Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nashik/ Nagpur shall visit the said structures from time to time to inspect the quality and quality of work and to ensure that the funds disbursed are spent properly.
4) The Executive Engineer, Public Works (Tribal Division, Thane/ Nashik and Executive Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Department, Amravati and Chandrapur are disbursing the funds as per the demand submitted by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board and should ensure that the funds are spent immediately.
(5) The Controlling Officer shall compile a report of the expenditure incurred from the said fund and submit it to the Government in the prescribed form.
(6) The Controlling Officer shall be responsible for compliance of the terms and conditions prescribed in the Government Circular issued from time to time by the Finance Department.
