circular
The District Collector and the Member Secretary of the District Planning Committee are instructed to prepare a draft plan of the districts by reporting the district-wise budget under the District Annual Plan every year through the Tribal Development Department for the Tribal Component Program on the lines of the District Plan (General). The District Collector prepares the draft plans and submits them to the District Planning Committee for approval. The district planning committees decide on the amount of funds to be allocated for the district-level schemes under this plan.
New appointments of Guardian Ministers and Chairmen of District Planning Committees for all districts are expected to be made in the near future. Against this backdrop, the administrative approvals granted to the works under various schemes under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme 2022-23 under the authority of the State Government as per the provisions of Section 12 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Structure and Works) Act, 1998, are being kept in abeyance from April 1, 2022 till date. Also, after the appointment of the new Guardian Minister, the list of the administrative approved works should be submitted to the Guardian Minister for review and a decision should be taken with the consent of the newly appointed Guardian Minister.
