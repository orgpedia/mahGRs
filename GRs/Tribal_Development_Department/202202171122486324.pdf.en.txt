The Proposal:
Till 2017-18, the state was receiving funds from the Centre only under the general category for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Project Tiger, which is being implemented by the Revenue and Forest Departments. However, during the year 2019-20 and 2020-21, the State has received funds for the said scheme, including from General, SC and ST categories. Accordingly, in the July, 2021 session, a total amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 3104.77 lakh has been budgeted through Supplementary Demands. In the context of the Revenue and Forest Department regarding the release of funds from this budgetary provision, No. 3 As the Finance Department had agreed to the proposal here, the matter of distribution of funds was under the consideration of the Government.
