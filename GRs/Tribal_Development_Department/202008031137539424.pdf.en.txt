Government's decision
The circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued by the Department of Finance specifies the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21 keeping in view the pandemic situation in the State. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has provided funds from time to time on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Tribal Component Program under the Government Decisions here, Demand No. Under the headings 2202-General Education in T-5, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the schemes being implemented by the following School Education Departments have been disbursed to the School Education Department within the limit of 35% of the provisions in the annual budget.
Now, in the month of August, funds have been made available for disbursement by the Department of Finance on Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01 salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 45% of the total budgetary allocation. Considering this, the Government is approving to disburse the remaining 10% of the 45% limit available for the following schemes under the head of demand No. 2202-General Education in T-5 under Tribal Component Programme to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System.
