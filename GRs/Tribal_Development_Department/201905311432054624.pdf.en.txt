Government's decision:
The scheme of providing 5% of the total budget of tribal development to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme for Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the financial year 2018-2019, Rs. 80,36,57, 700/- (Rupees Eighty Crores, Thirty Six Lakhs, Fifty Seven Thousand, Seven Hundred Only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 80,36,57, 700/- (Rupees Eighty Crores, Thirty Six Lakhs, Fifty Seven Thousand, Seven Hundred Only) to 166 villages of 151 Gram Sabhas in Akole Panchayat Samiti area under Ahmednagar Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas of the State.
A total of 90,444 tribal population in the gram panchayat area of Pesa area in Akole panchayat samiti in the bank account of the Samiti, Rs. 181.8046/- Rs. 1,64,43, 137.32/
(Rupees One Crore, Sixty Four Lakhs, Forty Three Thousand, One Hundred and Thirty Two Paisa only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. as well as the use of available funds
The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar should submit the report of the certificate and case-wise expenditure to the department.
