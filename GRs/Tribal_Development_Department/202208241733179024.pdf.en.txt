The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system.
Accordingly, the Department of Finance is presently making available the provisions within the limits of budgetary provisions of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month. Accordingly, for some of the schemes under Programme Expenditure in Tribal Component Programme, so far, funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities subject to 50% ceiling for the purpose of 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary).
Now, in the month of August end, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of funds in the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System in respect of" Salaries" subject to an increase in the limit of 55% of the budgetary provisions. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the extent of 5% to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the purpose of 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) of the schemes under the Programme Expenditure.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 in the accompanying statement under Demand T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
3. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer may refer to Pillar No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 should be circulated immediately to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers under them. Further, while spending the funds distributed under the said Government Decision, the Controlling Authorities shall ensure compliance of the Financial Rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Powers Rulebook as well as the rules issued by the Department of Finance. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022.
