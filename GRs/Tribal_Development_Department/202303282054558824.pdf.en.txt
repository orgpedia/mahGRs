The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the year 2022-23, under the head Demand Number T-6, Rs. 6000.00 lakh in the Budget and revised estimate of Rs. 5520.00 lacs have been sanctioned. As per the approval of the Department of Finance, the Government was considering to disburse the funds made available on the Budget Estimate Distribution System within the limits of the Revised Budget Estimates to meet the expenditure up to March, 2023.
Government's decision:
Government Ashramshala Construction (OTSP) (Head of Accounts 4225 1118) under Chapter No. Under Major Constructions, out of the revised Budget Estimates 2022-23, an amount of Rs. 309.55 lakh (Rupees Three Crore Nine Lakh Fifty Five Thousand only) to the Controlling Officer and Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nashik/ Nagpur.
