Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to fix the posts in government service for the disabled. The central government has given. The list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disability notified under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of this decision, the matter of ensuring the disabled category from Group-A to Group-D in the field offices under and subordinate to the Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation, Nashik and Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation, Nashik was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published vide notification dated 04.01.2021. As mentioned in the notification, the Central Government has ensured that the posts of Group A to Group D, which are in the state government establishments, are in the list published by the Central Government.
