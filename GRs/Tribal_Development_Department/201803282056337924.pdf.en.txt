For minor irrigation projects of 101 to 250 hectares of irrigation capacity in Murbad, Thane, the Principal Secretary, Soil and Water Conservation Department as the head of the department is being given approval to distribute on budget distribution system.

Also, instructions should be given to send the report of expenditure from the provision made available, information about the material goal achieved account-wise/ sub-account-wise, fund utilization certificate, instructions to be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development, Nashik and Soil and Water Conservation Departments by the 10th of every month.
4. Provisions made under the Tribal Component Programme should be spent under the Tribal Sub-Plan area and it should be ensured that the beneficiaries of the scheme are the Scheduled Tribes on priority.
