The Central Government provides grants under the Special Central Assistance to the States under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and under the scheme of Adim Janjatiya Vikas Karyakram every year. Proposals for grants under this program. It is submitted to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India with the approval of the Executive Committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The proposals for the year 2018-19 included identification of best Gram Panchayats from all the PESA Gram Panchayats of 19 AAV Project Offices in the State, creating awareness about the utilization of PESA funds, creating a sense of competition among PESA Gram Panchayats for their development, encouraging all the people to participate in the development of the village at Gram Panchayat level in line with the scheme.
There are a total of 2874 PESA villages under 19 AAV Project Offices in the State. With a view to ensure all-round development of the villages in this Pesa area, the Tribal Development Department allocates 5% of the total budget of the department every year and this fund is directly transferred to the Pesa Gram Panchayats in proportion to their population. The funds will be utilised for various development works. The scheme of" Model Gram Panchayat (Convergence of development schemes and PESA)" was proposed to the Central Government so that the gram panchayats who have made good use of this fund can be honored as an incentive to create a sense of competition among them and develop the village. Ministry of Tribal Affairs. The Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) in its meeting held on 23.05.2018 has approved the implementation of" Model Gram Panchayat (Convergence of Development Schemes and PESA)" Scheme for the year 2018-19 at a total cost of Rs. 608.85 lakh. 01 approved vide letter No. The first and second instalments of the funds have been disbursed through 2 letters and the funds have been disbursed to the Commissioner Tribal Development, Nashik as per the decision of the concerned Government.
