In this case, the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer has to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook and the rules and regulations in the context of the Finance Department while spending the said provisions. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
The report of expenditure incurred from the provisions made available, material objective-wise information achieved account head-wise/ sub-account head-wise, utilization certificate of funds, etc. should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month and the funds should be disbursed.
