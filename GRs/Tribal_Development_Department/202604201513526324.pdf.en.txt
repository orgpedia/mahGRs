The Proposal: -
The Department of Finance (DoF) has issued a notification regarding the disbursement of funds for the financial year 2026-27. The instructions have been circulated through the government circular of 09.04.2026. According to the instructions in this circular, the budgeted funds for the financial year 2026-27 under the Tribal Component Programme are to be disbursed under Budget Estimates, Allocations and Control WoT (BEAMS) for the first nine months (end-December, 2026) with a ceiling of 60%. At present, the Department of Finance provides for a cap of 60 per cent of the budgetary allocations for schemes in the mandatory form of programme expenditure under the Tribal Component Programme through the Budget Estimates, Allocations and Control System (BIS). / 5) have been made available for distribution.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to disburse the remaining available provisions to the concerned Controlling Officer, subject to a ceiling of 60 per cent, in respect of Subject No. 01 - Salary, 02 - Wages, 10 - Contractual Services and 36 - Auxiliary Grants (Salaries), as per Government Decision No. 2 in the Reference for Programme Expenditure Schemes under the Tribal Components Programme.
The Government's Decisions:
Under the "Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. For the schemes mentioned in the accompanying returns under T-04 and T-05, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds as per ROCAN No. 6 to the respective Controlling Authorities.
The funds have been disbursed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The funds should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Care should be taken by the concerned to comply with the working rules, financial rules, provisions and conditions of the State Government in the Budget Manual while spending these funds.
