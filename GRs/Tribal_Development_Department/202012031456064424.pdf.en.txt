The Proposal:
The Central share for the year 2020-21 for the Scheduled Tribes component of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Krishi Unnati Yojana- Assistance for Extension Reforms to Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) being implemented by the Department of Agriculture under the Tribal Component Programme has been increased from Rs. 77.91 lakh and State share of Rs. 51.94 lakh has been released by the Agriculture Department. The matter of disbursement of funds in accordance with the proposal submitted under Section 4 was under consideration of the Government.
