The Proposal:
The beneficiaries of the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme for the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes in the districts falling under the Tribal Areas under the Tribal Utilization Scheme. The matter of reconstituting the committee under the chairmanship of the Guardian Minister of the concerned districts by April 25, 2023 was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
For the selection of the beneficiaries of Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme in the rural areas, read the Government Decision No. 6, Tribal Development Department, Govt. Approval is being given to reconstitute the committee constituted by 25th April, 2023 as follows:
Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development Department- Chairman
Deputy Chief Executive Officer (Panchayat), Zilla Parishad Member
Project Director, District Rural Development Agency, Zilla Parishad Member 4. Executive Engineer, Construction, Zilla Parishad Member
Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project- Member Secretary
The committee chairman should conduct the selection process of beneficiaries for the Shabari tribal housing scheme by organizing meetings of the committee in the respective districts of his jurisdiction. This committee will be responsible for all rural housing schemes implemented by the Tribal Development Department. The mandate of this Committee is to implement the Government Decision No. 5. It will be held on March 15, 2016.
