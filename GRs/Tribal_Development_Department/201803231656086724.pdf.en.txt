Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000.00 (Rupees Two Hundred Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand Only) has been made available for disbursement to 63 villages of 36 Gram Sabhas in Ambegaon Panchayat Samiti area under Pune Zilla Parishad and 65 villages of 47 Gram Sabhas in Junnar Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts for a total tribal population of 79,196 in Gram Panchayats of Pesa area in Ambegaon, Junnar Panchayat Samiti. 485.1643/- Rs. 3,84,23, 069.17/ (Rs. Three crore eighty four lakh twenty three thousand seventy seven paisa only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to this department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune.
