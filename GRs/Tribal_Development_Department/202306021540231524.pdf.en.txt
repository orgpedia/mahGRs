The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, financial assistance is provided to eligible beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes for construction of pucca houses under Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme. A goal is set for each year and funds are allocated accordingly. Out of the budgetary provision of the scheme, Rs. 23099.80 lakh is proposed to be released from the budgetary provisions for the year 2023-24. The request was made to the Finance Department. The proposal was under the consideration of the government for disbursement of funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
Government Decision: Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. Under the head Account Head, T-5, 2225-Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, for the year 2023-24, out of the provisions of Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme, Rs. 23099.80 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Thirty Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) is being approved for disbursement and expenditure: (Rs. million).
