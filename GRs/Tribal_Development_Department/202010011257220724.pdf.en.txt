Government's decision
The circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued by the Department of Finance specifies the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21 keeping in view the pandemic situation in the State. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has provided funds from time to time on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Tribal Component Program under the Government Decisions here, Demand No. Under the following schemes being implemented by the Department of School Education under the head 2202-General Education in T-5, 36 Supplementary Grants (Salaries) subject to a limit of 50% of the annual budgetary allocation have been disbursed to the Department of School Education.
Now, in the month of October, funds have been made available for disbursement through Department of Finance on Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) subject to a ceiling of 60% of the total budgetary provisions. Considering this, the Government is approving to disburse the remaining 10% of the funds for the following schemes under the head title 2202-General Education in Demand No. T-5 under the Tribal Component Programme to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department on the budgetary fund distribution system.
