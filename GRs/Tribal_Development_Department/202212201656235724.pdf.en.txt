The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Finance Department is presently making available the provisions within the specified limits of budgetary provisions of the schemes for disbursement on the monthly Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities within 75% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) of the schemes under the head Backward Classes Welfare under the Tribal Component Programme and 94% for the salaries of the aided Ashramshalas as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Finance Department.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available 10% of the budgeted allocation for the said objective for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of these funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities, except the aided Ashramshalas, was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01- Salary, 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the schemes mentioned in Table No. 2 of the accompanying prospectus under T-5.
