Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble, out of a total of 497 Government Ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department, the Commissioner, Tribal Development, State of Maharashtra, Nashik has issued reference No. As proposed under Section 3, 250 Ashramshalas in Annexure A are being declared as" Adarsh Ashramshalas." In order to develop the existing ashramshalas as model ashramshalas, the following important aspects/ components are being incorporated in each model ashramshala.
(a) Physical Facilities: (i) Every model ashramshala should have a well-equipped school building. It should have ICT lab, science lab, library, music and art room and adequate toilets.
(ii) There should be a separate and well-equipped hostel building for residential boys and girls.
(iii) Staff quarters for teaching and non-teaching staff. (iv) Each Adarsh Ashramshala should have well-equipped dining hall and multi-purpose hall.
(v) There should be a separate toilet with adequate facilities for students with disabilities.
(vi) Every model ashram should have clean and adequate drinking water, well-maintained classrooms, attractive buildings, playgrounds and adequate sports equipment.
(vii) Each Adarsh Ashramshala premises should have a separate Seek Room with all amenities.
It should also include physical facilities such as solar inverters, adequate space for washing and drying clothes, protective wall and adequate CCTV cameras.
(vii) The up-gradation should include facilities like digital classrooms, virtual classrooms, tab labs, computer labs, etc.
(b) Educational Facilities: (i) Adarsh Ashramshalas should consciously develop 21st century skills such as creative thinking, critical thinking, scientific temperament, upholding constitutional values, collaboration, communication skills, among others.
