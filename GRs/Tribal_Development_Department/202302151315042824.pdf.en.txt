As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 08.02.2023, for the schemes under Compulsory and Programme Expenditure, the time limit for disbursement of funds has been prescribed for the period up to the finalization of Revised Estimates for the year 2022-23. Also, the funds as per the disbursement limit are made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) disbursement system. In this regard, under the Annual Tribal Component Programme of the District No. 2 Considering the funds disbursed under the Government decisions here, the matter of disbursing the funds available for the remaining disbursement to the concerned Controlling Authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme, the Government is approving the disbursement and spending of the increased funds as per Pillar-5 of the budgetary provisions for the districts mentioned in the accompanying prospectus under Demand No. T-9, T-10 and T-11 to the concerned Controlling Officer and Collector.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
