The Proposal -
In line with the implementation of the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme in urban areas, the Tribal Development Department has issued a circular. Guidelines issued by Government Decision dated 11th January, 2024
have been made. Demand from field offices to provide targets for urban areas under the scheme
was received by the government. Accordingly, targets have been set for urban areas under the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme.
The matter was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
This Government decision is linked to the budgetary provision available for urban areas under the Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme during the financial year and the period at the end of the financial year.
Government to fix district-wise targets / targets for the financial year 2023-24 as per Annexure-I.
