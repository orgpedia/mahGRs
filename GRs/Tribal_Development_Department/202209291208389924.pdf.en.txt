The Controlling Officer concerned shall ensure that the works included in Annexure D of Budget Estimates Part-III for 2022-23 are spent only on the works up to 2022-23 in the accompanying statement and within the limits of administrative approval.
2) The funds should be spent in accordance with the work as mentioned in the accompanying list, which should not be changed in any way. If the said provisions are required to be amended for some unavoidable reasons, the proposal should be submitted to the Government with detailed reasons.
Since the fund is being disbursed for 100% physically completed and in-progress works as demanded by the concerned Controlling Officer, the fund should be allocated for the 261 works.
4) A detailed proposal for deletion of physically and financially completed road works from Schedule D of Budget Estimate Part-III for 2022-23 should be submitted to the Government within one month. Also, these works should be excluded from the BEAMS system and submitted to the government.
The concerned Controlling Officer shall ensure that the said provision is spent only on the works in the tribal sub-plan area as determined under the Government Decision, Tribal Development Department No. TSP- 1086/8710/ PR No. 31/ K. 5 dated 9.3.1990.
6) Also, while spending the said provision, the Government Decision, Tribal Development Department, No. TSP 2003/ Q. No. 101/ Ka-6, dated 26th July, 2004 and TSP-2005/ Q. Care should be taken to strictly comply with the conditions contained in No. 59/ Q-6, dated November 10, 2005.
The utilization certificate of the disbursed funds should be submitted to the government by January, 2023.
