The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, a scholarship scheme is implemented for students belonging to Scheduled Tribes studying in medical and similar colleges. For the year 2021-22, Rs. 3000.00 lakhs has been disbursed to Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik. Under this scheme, for pending applications from 2019-20 to 2021-22, an additional amount of Rs. 24.91 crore through reallocation. 4 The matter of disbursement of increased funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department on the issue was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the pending applications for the year 2019-20 to 2021-22 under the scholarship scheme for the students belonging to Scheduled Tribes studying in medical and similar colleges under the Tribal Component Programme, the following incremental funds are being sanctioned with the approval of the Finance Department to be disbursed and spent by the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik as the Controlling Officer of the scheme.
