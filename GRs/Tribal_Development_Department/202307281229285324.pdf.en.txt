The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the year 2023-24, under the head Demand Number T-6, Rs. 4225 crore has been allocated for the welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities. 10000.00 lakh is provided in the budget. Now the Finance Department has provided funds within the limit of 20 percent of the budgetary allocation. The matter of disbursement of funds made available on the Budget Estimate Distribution System under this head was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:

The disbursement of the said funds is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The Controlling Authority concerned shall ensure that the expenditure is spent only on the works included in Annexure-D of the Budget Publication and within the administrative sanction/ revised administrative limit.
(2) The Controlling Officer concerned shall ensure that the said funds are spent only on the 29 works mentioned in the accompanying statement.
The Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Board, Nashik/ Nagpur shall visit the said structures from time to time to inspect the quality and quality of work and to ensure that the funds disbursed are spent properly.
4) The Executive Engineer, Public Works (Tribal Department), Nashik/ Nagpur, shall ensure that the funds are disbursed as per the demand submitted by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) and that the funds are spent immediately.
(5) The Controlling Officer shall compile a report of the expenditure incurred from the said fund and submit it to the Government in the prescribed form.
(6) The Controlling Officer shall be responsible for compliance of the terms and conditions prescribed in the Government Circular issued from time to time by the Finance Department.
4. The decision of the Finance Department. being issued as per the provisions of Government Circular dated 12.04.2023.
