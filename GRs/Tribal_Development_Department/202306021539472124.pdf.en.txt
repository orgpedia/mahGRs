While spending the funds disbursed under the said Government Decision, the Controlling Officer shall ensure compliance of the financial rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook as well as the rules prescribed by the Finance Department. The instructions given in the Government Circular dated 12.04.2023 should be strictly followed.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. Report on expenditure incurred, information on material progress achieved in terms of headings/ sub-headings, utilization of funds, etc.
The information should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month.
5. It shall be the responsibility of the concerned department to ensure that only the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Tribes are benefited from the provisions made available under the Tribal Component Programme. This will mainly cover schemes for individual benefit, including schemes for creating infrastructure in tribal sub-plans. However, tribal sub-schemes should be taken up only for individual benefit schemes in the external sector.
