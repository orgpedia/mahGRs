The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. In pursuance of this, funds are being made available for distribution in a limited manner on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system through the Finance Department through Government Circular No. 1 in the context of Finance Department. As provided on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Finance Department, 60% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the schemes being implemented by the School Education Department under the Tribal Component Programme has been disbursed to the School Education Department as per the Government decisions at Reference No. 2.
Now that a total of 5% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) has been made available for distribution through the Finance Department, the matter of disbursement of funds to the School Education Department in accordance with the proposal at No. 3 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, under the year 2022-23, Demand No. 2202 General Education in T-5, as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department under the head General Education, the total amount of Rs. 521.14750 lakh to the Secretary, Department of School Education, Ministry of Education, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
