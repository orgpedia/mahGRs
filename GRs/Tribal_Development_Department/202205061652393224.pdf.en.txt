Under the Tribal Component Programme, special assistance is provided to the beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes under the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme being implemented by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, vide No. Proposal has been received under Section 2.
During the financial year 2022-23, as per the Government Circular No. 1 under reference of the Department of Finance, provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted provision are being made available for monthly disbursement by the Department of Finance on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system for all purposes except 01 Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salary). Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to disburse the funds available for distribution to the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.
Government Decision Demand for Scheduled Tribes under Tribal Component Programme No. Approval is being given to disburse and spend the funds available for disbursement in the limit of 14% of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 for the schemes in T-5 head heading 2235, Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme in Social Security and Welfare and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme (Rs. million).

2) As mentioned above, Rs. 5180.00 lakhs (Rupees Fifty One Crore Eighty Lakh only) to the Principal Secretary, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department.
(3) In the exercise of the said provision, the financial rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rules Book, the Financial Powers Rules Book, as well as in the reference to the Finance Department, shall apply. The responsibility of strictly following the directions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022 will be of the concerned administrative department. The Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, tribal sub-plan outer area shall be recorded separately. Accordingly, the report of the expenditure incurred, information about the material goal achieved account-wise/ sub-account-wise, fund utilization certificate, etc. should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month.
4. It shall be the responsibility of the concerned department to ensure that only the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Tribes are benefited from the provisions made available under the Tribal Component Programme.
