Introduction: A 16-member review committee headed by Mr. Vivek Pandit, former Member of Legislative Assembly, has been constituted under the Government Resolution No. 1 to recommend to the Government how all the schemes being implemented in the tribal areas in the State have affected the lives of the tribal brothers so far and what measures can be effectively implemented to bring about a radical change in their lives.
the first meeting of the committee. It was held on 21.02.2019 at Mantralaya, Mumbai. The meeting decided to include 1) Principal Secretary, Agriculture Department, 2) Commissioner, Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, Maharashtra State, Nagpur and 3) Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Maharashtra State, Nagpur as members in the review committee to ensure effective implementation of government schemes for providing local employment to tribal people. Jagdish Patil, retired Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, has experience in dealing with the problems of the Pardhi community and Dr. K. R. Sonpure has experience in dealing with the health of the tribal community in the remote areas of Vidarbha. Their experience can be used by the state level review committee. Hence, the government had decided to appoint him as a special invitee to the committee.
The Review Committee constituted under the Government Resolution No. 1 under the Chairmanship of Mr. Vivek Pandit, former Member of Legislative Assembly, now includes 1) Principal Secretary, Agriculture Department, Ministry, Mumbai, 2) Commissioner, Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, Maharashtra State, Nagpur and 3) Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Maharashtra State, Nagpur as members. Jagdish Patil, retired, Divisional Commissioner and Dr. K. R. Sonpure, Member Secretary, Chief Minister's Medical Assistance Cell are being appointed as special invitees.
