INTRODUCTION: To reduce the prevalence of malnutrition among women in the Scheduled Areas due to lack of calories and protein in their diet, serious problems such as malnutrition, maternal mortality, infant mortality and low birth weight, read the table below. On 18th November, 2015, the Government has decided to provide one-time square feeding to pregnant women and lactating mothers under the Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Amrit Aahar Yojana. It was launched in the state from December 1, 2015. The average cost of one square meal per beneficiary per day under this scheme is Rs. 25/- has been fixed. Read about this in Figure 2. As per the Government decision dated 22nd June, 2018, the amount per beneficiary per day has been increased to Rs. It was 35/-.

The meeting of the State Level Central Control Committee constituted as per the decision of the Government dated 18th November, 2015. It was held on 02.02.2023. In the meeting, the demand to increase the rate of the Amrit Aahar scheme by the Aahar committees at the local level was discussed.
Accordingly, the government was considering to increase the rate of one time square meal per day to pregnant women and lactating mothers in Phase-1 of Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Amrit Aahar Yojana.
