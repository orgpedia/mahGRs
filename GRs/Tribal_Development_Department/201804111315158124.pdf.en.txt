GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme, for the financial year 2018-19, 70% of the budgetary allocation for Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Amrut Aahar Yojana is being released to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, as per the District-wise provisions indicated in column No. 5 of the accompanying statement subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. While spending the funds distributed by the said Government Decision, the Controlling Authorities shall exercise their powers under the financial powers provided under the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Powers Rulebook and in the context of the Finance Department. The guidelines issued vide Government Circular No. 2.04.2018 are to be strictly followed.
2. All stakeholders should be careful to spend as per the cash flow available on the computer distribution system (BEAMS).
3. The Controlling Authorities may refer to the Statement in column No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 shall be immediately distributed to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers under them.
