the prelude
Secretary, Ulhas Parisar Pratishthan operating Dr. Krishnarao Torskar Junior College has been requested to close the class of Science stream due to lack of students. The letter is 2. The institute has decided to close the science stream for class 11 and class 12.
The Court of Appeal No. 4873/2022 was filed by the Secretary, Ullas Campus Foundation against the Project Officer, AAVP, Shahapur and others. On this petition. The court has passed an order to hear the matter on September 22, 2022, and decide the matter within six months.
Accordingly, the government was considering the closure of the science wing of the Ulhas Campus Foundation-run Dr. Krishnarao Torsakar Junior College, Karav (Vangani).
Government's decision
In view of the directions issued by the High Court, Bombay dated 22.09.2022 and the request made by the Secretary, Ulhas Parisar Pratishthan, Dr.
Krishna Rao Torsakar Junior College, Karav (Vangani) has been allowed to close its science classes. The teaching and non-teaching staff working in the Science Department of the said Junior College shall be transferred as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 11.06.2019.
