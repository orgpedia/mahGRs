Read:- Tribal Development Department Government Resolution No. 2012/ No. 38 (Part-I)/ K. 17, D. March 28, 2013
Government Circular: To provide houses to the Scheduled Tribe beneficiaries whose houses are made of mud or the families of the Scheduled Tribes who are homeless in the state. The" Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme" is being implemented in the State since 2013 as per the Government decision dated 28/03/2013. In this government decision, it has been mentioned about the documents to be submitted by the applicant for the Shabari tribal housing scheme. The prescribed form of application is not mentioned while applying for this scheme. Therefore, different forms of applications and different forms of documents are being demanded from the applicants by the regional offices.
2. Therefore, instead of the documents mentioned in the Government decision here, the sample of the application in the attached Annexure for the Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme and the documents mentioned therein are prescribed and the application and documents should be taken from the applicant accordingly.
Applicants are not required to submit any other documents. Applicants must submit a completed application form on a plain paper. A printed application is not required. These applications and documents will be applicable for all the schemes of the Tribal Development Department.
