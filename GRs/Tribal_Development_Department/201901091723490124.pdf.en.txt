Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only) has been made available for disbursement to 116 villages of 78 Gram Sabhas in Sakri Panchayat Samiti area under Dhule Zilla Parishad and 71 villages of 52 Gram Sabhas in Shirpur Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts.
424.2108/- Rs. 12,07,18, 512.03/- (Rs. Twelve crore, seven lakh, eighteen thousand, five hundred and twelve, three paisa only) is being made available directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the attached Annexure Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds made available should be submitted to the department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Dhule.
