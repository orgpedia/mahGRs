Government's decision:
The demand for the Centrally Sponsored National Rural Livelihood Mission under the Tribal Component Programme for the financial year 2020-21 is No. T-5,2501- Special Village Development Programme, 01- Integrated Village Development Programme, 796- Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 01- Scheme under Tribal Area Appropriation, (01) (06)- Financial Assistance for Special Skill Development Project under Maharashtra State Rural Subsidies Mission (Central Share) (2501 2953), 33- Financial Assistance. 5040.00 lakhs under State Account (01) (05)-Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission State Share (2501 2935), 33-Finance Assistance. 3360.00 lakhs is budgeted.
2. Refer No. In accordance with the proposal of the Department of Rural Development, it is being approved to disburse 30% of the budgetary allocation for the above Central and State accounts for the year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Central Share (2501 2953) under the head Rs. 1512.00 lakhs (Rupees Fifteen Crores Twelve Lakhs only) and State Accounts (2501 2935) under this head Rs. 1008.00 lakhs (Rupees Ten Crore Eight Lakh only) is being disbursed to Additional Chief Secretary, Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS).
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department in the case should ensure that the fund is spent in the same year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, the utilization certificate of the fund, the material objectives achieved in the headings/ sub-headings etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal component programme area shall be recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
