No.45/ Q. 19, D. 19/08/2017 Preamble- Under the Scheme of Special Central Assistance, Government of India has sanctioned Rs. 300.00 lakh for the year 2017-18 for setting up Libraries in Ashram Schools. This fund reference No. 3 has been allocated to the Commissioner Tribal Development, Nashik as per the decision of the Government here. The scheme will be implemented under a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the Tribal Development Department and Eklavya Foundation. Eklavya Foundation has earlier successfully worked with the Government of Madhya Pradesh in developing the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) 2005 and Syllabus and Textbooks based on the NCF with the Social Science Teaching Programme and Primary Education Programme (PRASHIKA) in Government School National Council of Education Research and Training (NCERT). The Eklavya Foundation has also implemented the above programmes in the states of Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Rajasthan and Kerala and is collaborating with the State Councils of Educational Research and Training (SCERTs), Government of Andhra Pradesh in the preparation of curriculum and textbooks. In addition to this, the Eklavya Institute itself has organized workshops with a view to empowering teachers and enhancing the teaching process by making changes.
A small standardized course has also been started in this regard. In this way, for the training of teachers and for the knowledge of science to students in their mother tongue, many books have been translated in tribal languages like Gondi, Korku, Malvi, Munderi, apart from being translated in popular languages like Hindi, Marathi, it is proposed to approve the guidelines for the implementation of the scheme Setting up Libraries in Ashram Schools by this organization, which is known and has a long experience of work in this field.
Setting up Libraries in Ashram Schools, a programme to provide science knowledge to teachers and students of Ashramshalas, will be implemented jointly by the Eklavya Foundation and the Tribal Development Department. 300.00 is being approved as per Annexure No. 1 annexed to this decision
2. The implementation of the scheme within the limits of the allocated provisions as per the guidelines of the scheme should be expedited and its utilization certificate should be submitted to the government along with the financial and physical report.
