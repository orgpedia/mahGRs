Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) is available for disbursement to 57 villages of 24 Gram Sabhas in the area of Maregaon Panchayat Samiti under Yavatmal Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas of the State, 38 villages of 26 Gram Sabhas in the area of Ralegaon Panchayat Samiti, 108 villages of 53 Gram Sabhas in area of Kelapur Panchayat Samiti, 35 villages of 27 Gram Sabhas in area of Ghatanji Panchayat Samiti, 74 villages of 30 Gram Sabhas in area of Jharijamani Panchayat Samiti, 12 villages of 8 Gram Sabhas in area of Arni Panchayat Samiti in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti Bank Account. 485.1643/- Rs. 5,31,28, 398.05/- (Rs. Five crore thirty-one lakh thirty-eight thousand three hundred and eighty-nine paise only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal should submit the utilization certificate of the funds made available to this department.
