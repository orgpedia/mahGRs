Government Resolution No. 2023/ P.No. 93/ K.O.01
Women employees working in the Tribal Development Department (itself), Mantralaya are hereby informed that they should submit any complaint regarding sexual harassment to the Chairperson or Member Secretary of the Internal Committee.
The functions of the Women's Complaints Committee shall be as follows:
The Central Government has notified the Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 on 23rd April, 2013 and Rules made thereunder on 09th December, 2013. To take necessary measures to prevent sexual harassment of women and to provide a safe working environment.
2. Redress of complaints received at the level of the department, scrutinize the complaints within a stipulated time and submit proposals for further action to the appropriate authority in accordance with the complaints.
To create awareness to prevent incidents like sexual harassment and to ensure the creation of a safe environment by holding regular meetings of the Committee.
At least four members of the committee must be present to make the decision.
(5) To prepare an annual report in accordance with the provisions of this Act.
3.The process of putting up the name board of the above committee in front of the department should be done immediately through the registration branch.
