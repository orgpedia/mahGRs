Government's decision:
The demand for the Centrally Sponsored National Rural Livelihood Mission under the Tribal Component Programme for the financial year 2019-20 is No. T-5,2501- Special Village Development Programme, 01- Integrated Village Development Programme, 796- Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 01- Scheme under Tribal Area Appropriation, (01) (06)- Financial Assistance for Special Skill Development Project under Maharashtra State Rural Subsidies Mission (Central Share) (2501 2953), 33- Financial Assistance. 3089.00 lakhs under State Account (01) (05)-Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission State Share (2501 2935), 33-Finance Assistance. 1060.00 lakhs is budgeted.
2. Refer No. 3 from the Central Government of Rs. 4604.25 lakh has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State, as proposed by the Department of Rural Development, for the year 2019-20, the budget under the head of the above Central Account (2501 2953) is Rs. 3089.00 lacs out of which Rs. 3089.00 lacs (Rupees Thirty Crores Ninety Nine Lacs only) and Rs. 1060.00 lakhs out of the budgeted fund of Rs. 1060.00 lakh (Rupees Ten Crore Sixty Lakh only) is being sanctioned to the Additional Chief Secretary, Rural Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS).
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department in the case should ensure that the fund is spent in the same year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, the utilization certificate of the fund, the material objectives achieved in the headings/ sub-headings etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai by the 10th of every month.
