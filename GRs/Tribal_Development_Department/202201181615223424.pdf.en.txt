(a) The Corporation has issued the 7th Pay Commission by the Finance Department/ Government e.g. d. January 30th, 2019. The terms and conditions of the Government Decision/ Circular issued on 20th February, 2019 and from time to time shall be adopted as guidelines and the revised pay scale shall be implemented accordingly.
(b) The benefit to the officers/ employees of the Corporation should not be more than the pay scale applied to the equivalent posts in the Government while giving the benefit of the revised pay scale. The revised pay scales should be applicable within that limit.
(e) The benefit of the said revised pay scale shall be admissible to the number of posts presently filled in the Corporation. As per the scheme, prior approval of the Government will be required while allowing the said benefit to the new posts/ cadre out of 1298 posts.
(e) If any change is to be made in the position on the establishment of the Corporation or if any position is to be upgraded/ downgraded, the action regarding filling up of vacant/ falling vacant posts/ creation of new posts/ cadre in the Corporation shall be subject to the approval of the Board of Directors in the first instance and thereafter to the approval of the Government.
(a) The. The Corporation will be fully empowered to take a decision on payment of arrears for the period from 1st July, 2021 till the implementation of the revised pay scales, either in a lump sum or in a phased manner. It will be the responsibility of the corporation to ensure that the corporation does not incur losses in the future due to this and the implementation of the revised pay scale.
(2) If any question arises regarding the implementation of any provision of this order, it shall be resolved by the Tribal Department of the Government of Maharashtra in consultation with the Finance Department and the decision of the Department thereon shall be final.
(3) The expenditure on account of payment to the employees under the revised pay scale shall be met from the funds received by the Corporation from the Government on account of managerial grant. No additional fee is to be paid for it.
4. These orders are issued with the concurrence of the Finance Department and their informal reference No.85/ CE. Issued under 03.01.2021.
