Government's decision
In the Circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued in Reference No.1 of the Department of Finance, considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona Virus, it was approved to disburse funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation of essential and programme expenditure for the first 3 months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020).
Accordingly, as provided on the budgetary fund distribution system by the Finance Department, under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. Under the headings 2202-General Education in T-5, 36 Supplementary Grants (Salaries) under the schemes being implemented by the following School Education Departments have been disbursed to the School Education Department within the limit of 25% of the allocation in the annual budget.
At present, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement under the Budgetary Allocation Mechanism (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01- Salary and 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 35% of the total budgetary allocation. Considering this, the Government is approving to disburse the remaining 10% of the 35% limit available for the following schemes under the head of demand No. 2202-General Education in T-5 under Tribal Component Programme to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System.
