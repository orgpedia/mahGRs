The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Department of Finance has provided Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) on the distribution system as provided under the Tribal Component Programme under the School Education Department for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) to the School Education Department within the limit of 50%. 2 has been distributed here by government decision.
At the end of September, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds amounting to 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01- Salary, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on the Budget Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds for the scheme of Assistance to Tribal Students in Sainik Schools (2202H973) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2021-22, Demand No. As provided by the Finance Department for disbursement under the head 2202 General Education in T-5, assistance to the batch of tribal students in Sainik School (2202H973), 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) for a total of Rs. 150.00 lakh (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
The funds have been disbursed on the budgetary distribution system as above and in case the head of the department/ controlling officer spends the said provision, the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook as well as in the context of the Finance Department. Strictly follow the directions given in the Government Resolution dated 24.06.2021.
