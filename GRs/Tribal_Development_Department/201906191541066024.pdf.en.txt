It has been decided to implement and control through the department. Accordingly, the matter of transferring the scheme to the Rural Development Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble, as decided in the 50th meeting of the Maharashtra State Tribal Advisory Council, it is being approved to transfer the scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal utilization every year to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the state (5% direct funding scheme to PESA Gram Panchayats) to the Rural Development Department. Also, the computer system pesafundmanagement. maharashtra. gov. in developed by MahaOnline for the control of this scheme is also being transferred to the Rural Development Department. However, the User Name and Password of the above computer system should be made available by the Rural Development Department to this Department for controlling the scheme.
