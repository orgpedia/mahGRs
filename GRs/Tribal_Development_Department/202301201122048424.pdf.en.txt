Proposal In the context of Finance Department 1. As per the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for the schemes under mandatory expenditure are to be made available for distribution on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system. Accordingly, at present, the Finance Department is making available the budgetary provisions of the schemes within the specified limit for disbursement on the monthly Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system.
