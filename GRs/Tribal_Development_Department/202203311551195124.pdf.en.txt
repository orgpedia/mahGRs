The Central Government provides grants to the State Governments every year under the scheme of Special Central Assistance, Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and the Primitive Tribal Development Programme. The proposal was submitted to the Central Government for the subsidy to be received under the scheme during 2016-17. These proposals included the project titled" Integrated Agriculture Development Plan for PVTG Beneficiaries from FRA." The objective of the project is to reduce the migration rate and improve the quality of life of forest-holder tribal farmers by improving the economic status of their families through integrated development of their lands.
Ministry of Tribal Affairs for 2016-17. In the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) meeting held on 08/02/2017, the Integrated Agriculture Development Plan for PVTG Beneficiaries from FRA under Tribal Development Programme for the year 2016-17 was approved with an outlay of Rs. 800.00 lakh has been sanctioned for the project. The Commissioner for Tribal Development has submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference 3 for the establishment of ShadeNets for 51 forest lease holder tribal farmers of the State on 0.20 hectare area per farmer in the land of tribal farmers who have received land through Forest Rights Act with the aim of raising the income of the farmers by adopting protected farming practices and producing high quality and exportable products.
As per the proposal, 51 forest lease holders from the primitive tribes of the state are proposed to be provided financial assistance for setting up shed net on 0.20 hectare area per farmer.
For each beneficiary, Rs. 16,32,000/- is proposed to be spent. For this, 9 5% subsidy will be given by the government and 5% share is expected to be given by the beneficiaries. Accordingly, each beneficiary will get Rs. 15,50,400/- and Government subsidy of Rs. 81,600/- is proposed to be paid to the beneficiaries. A total of 51 beneficiaries were given Rs. 7,90,70, 400/- and Rs. Beneficiary share of Rs. 41,61,600/-
