The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the year 2021-22, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) as per the decision of the Government at No. 1 under the purview of the Department of Finance, has been implemented on the distribution system within the limits as made available from time to time and on the separate budget with the approval of the Department of Finance. Under T-5 Scheduled Tribe Certificate Examination (Programme Expenditure) (22254122), funds to the extent of 75% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of 01 salary, 10 contractual services and 13 office expenses have been disbursed to the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune.
At present, 8 Scheduled Tribe Certificate Scrutiny Committees are functioning in the state and the process of starting the work of 7 new committees is underway. It is imperative that the functioning of the Scheduled Tribes Certificate Validity Check Committee under the Department continues on a regular basis and these Committees have been specifically assigned additional functions/ objectives at the beginning of the academic year. Accordingly, in view of the increased demand for funds under the objectives of 01-Salary, 10- Contractual Service and 13- Office Expenditure under the scheme, the Finance Department has agreed to release funds within the limit of 90% of the budgetary provision. The issue of disbursing the remaining funds within this limit was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. Under the scheme titled 2225- Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committee (2225 4122), the Government is approving to disburse and spend the remaining funds within the limit of 90% of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2021-22 to the Controller and Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune for the following specific items as agreed by the Finance Department.
