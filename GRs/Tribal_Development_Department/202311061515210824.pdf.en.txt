Introduction: The Central Government has declared 3 tribes in the state as primitive tribes. These primitive tribes are extremely backward tribes of tribal society and their inhabitants are scattered in remote areas. It is necessary to bring them into the mainstream of development by providing them basic facilities. For this purpose, various development works recommended by the Project Officer for carrying out developmental works under the scheme of Tribal Development Programme, vide No. The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the Government Decision No. 2, 3 and 4. Accordingly, out of the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24, the Finance Department has made available on the Budget Fund Distribution System, an amount of Rs. 250.00 lakh under the purview of Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Bhamragad and Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Pen, vide reference No. works mentioned in 2, 3 and 4. The Minister (Tribal) has approved. Therefore, the matter of disbursement of funds for the said administrative sanctioned works was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. 2225 under T-V (Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities), (01) (49) (2225 C 885). 31. The total amount of funds released under Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) from the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24 by the Finance Department is Rs. 249.90534 lakh (Rupees Two Crores Nineteen Five Lakh Ninety Five Thousand Five Hundred Thirty Four only) to the concerned Controller and Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project.
