Preamble: Reference No. As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, a total of 8937 temporary posts in the subordinate offices of the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik under the Tribal Development Department were extended from October 1, 2018 to February 28, 2019. Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, the Government is approving the extension of the existing 8937 temporary posts in the office of Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
