Government's decision:
Finance Department No. In a circular dated 16th April, 2020, it has been approved to disburse funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation for the first 3 months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020) for the schemes of essential and programme expenditure, taking into account the pandemic situation arising out of Corona virus in the State.
Under Tribal Component Programme Demand No.T-5 (02) (29)- Teaching of ST students in English medium reputed residential schools (2225 C 999), 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) under budgetary allocation of Rs. 360.00 crore has been budgeted and as per the proposal of Commissioner, Tribal Development, vide reference No. 2 As per the decisions of the Government, a total amount of Rs. 8462.756 lakh has been disbursed to the Commissionerate.
Now the No. As per the proposal received under Section 3, the Government is approving the release of funds to the extent of Rs. 537.244 lakh (Rupees Five Crore Thirty-Seven Lakhs Twenty-four Thousand Four Hundred only) to the Controller General and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, out of the available provision within the limit of 25%, subject to the approval of the Finance Department.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. The funds will be utilised only for clearing the pending fee payments for the academic year 2019-20 generated on the online system of the designated residential schools under the department.
(3) The Head of the Department and the Controlling Authorities, while spending the funds distributed under the said Government Decision, shall be bound by the Financial Rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, Financial Powers Rule Book.
