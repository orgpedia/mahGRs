Preamble: Under the Tribal Component Programme, in the Budget Estimates 2023-24, an amount of Rs. 10000.00 lakh is the budgeted amount.
In this budget, the government was considering to disburse funds for the physically completed/ in progress works of road repair/ improvement in the tribal areas of Nashik district within the limits provided by the Finance Department on the budget fund distribution system.
Government Resolution Demand No.T-5 for the financial year 2023-24 Under the head Accounts-Title 3054-Roads and Bridges, (04)- District and Other Roads, 796-Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01)-Plan under Tribal Area, (01) (06) (State Level Plan) (Programme Expenditure), (Head of Accounts- 30542722), 27-Small Construction, Rs. 10000.00 lakh is provided in the budget. Out of this, a total of Rs. 1000.00 lakh is being sanctioned to the Controlling Officer and CEO, Zilla Parishad, Nashik for disbursement and expenditure subject to the following conditions:

The disbursement of funds is subject to the following conditions:
(1) The concerned Controlling Authority shall ensure that the funds from the said disbursed fund are spent only on the 27 works in the list of works which are materially complete and have pending liabilities on progress and within the limits of administrative approval.
2) The funds should be spent in accordance with the work as mentioned in the accompanying list, which should not be changed in any way. Also, the concerned controlling officer should ensure that these works are not approved or exempted from any other scheme.
The concerned Controlling Officer shall ensure that the said provision is spent only on the works in the tribal sub-plan area as determined under the Government Decision, Tribal Development Department No. TSP- 1086/8710/ PR No. 31/ K. 5, dated 9.3.1990.
Before the tender process for these works, the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project should certify that these works fall in the Tribal Sub Plan Area (TSP)/ Additional Tribal Sub Plan Area (ATSP) MADA/ Mini MADA/ PESA area. If these works are not completed in the tribal sub-plan area/ additional tribal sub-plan area/ Mada/ Mini Mada/ Pesa area, the works should not be started.
5) It should be ensured that the work has not been done earlier in other schemes or is not approved in other schemes at present. If these works are approved in other schemes, the government should be informed immediately to cancel such works.
6) Before giving the work order, it should be ensured that the circulars and guidelines issued by the government in this regard from time to time have been complied with.
