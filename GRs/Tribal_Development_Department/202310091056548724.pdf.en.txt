The Proposal:
The" Bhagwan Birsa Munda Jodraste Yojana" was approved in the Cabinet Meeting dated 18/08/2023. The Government Resolution dated 13/09/2023 has been issued here. As mentioned in the said decision, the government was considering the formation of a committee at the department level to implement the scheme.
Government's decision:
Under the Bhagwan Birsa Munda Connectivity Scheme, a Committee is being constituted to examine the proposals received from the Regional Offices regarding the unclassified and unclassified road works to be taken up from the 8th to the 12th year and to approve the relevant master plan and the annual plan thereunder as under:
