(1) The institution shall be bound to provide facilities to the students as prescribed under the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the Ashramshala Code, 2019.
(2) The voluntary organization shall follow the terms and conditions and rules prescribed by the Government while appointing the teaching and non-teaching staff. The grant payable to the institution will not be admissible if the prescribed qualified employee is not appointed. The recognition of the ashramshala will be cancelled in the case.
It should be noted that if untrained teacher candidates are appointed, their salary will not be the responsibility of the government and the entire responsibility will be of the institution.
(3) The institution shall provide classrooms and accommodation in the ashramshala in a separate building. The building will have toilets, bathrooms and other facilities. Adequate water supply should be ensured. All other necessary infrastructure is in place.
(4) The institution shall construct its own separate building for the Ashramshala (including boys and girls hostel) within the stipulated time as per the undertaking. Building rent subsidy will not be admissible to the institution.
(5) The grant shall be admissible to the institution in accordance with the form and rules prescribed by the Government from time to time.
(6) The institution shall not be allowed to inter-operate with any other institution.
(7) It shall be obligatory on the institution to comply with the Ashramshala Code and the order regarding the aided Ashramshala of the Tribal Development Department issued from time to time.
(8) It shall be obligatory on the institution to accommodate the employees of the closed ashramshala who have not been adjusted so far.
(9) There are restrictions on recruitment under the Government Decision dated 04.05.2019 of the Finance Department. Only after the relaxation of these restrictions, the Institute shall fill up the post in the prescribed manner in the presence of the Project Officer/ Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nagpur.
