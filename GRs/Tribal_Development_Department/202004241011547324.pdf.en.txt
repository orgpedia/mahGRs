Government's decision
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2020 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2020) for the financial year 2020-21 has been approved by the Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is in addition to the provisions of the Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, Dt. 18.03.2020 has been published.
Accordingly, Finance Department No. In a circular dated 16th April, 2020, it has been approved to disburse funds at the department level within the limit of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation of essential and programme expenditure for the first three months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020), considering the pandemic situation in the State. Accordingly, No. 2 As per the decision of the Government, 10% of the total budgetary allocation has been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the specific objectives mentioned in the Government Circular in the context of the Finance Department for the mandatory expenditure schemes in Demand No. T-1, T-2 and T-4 under the Tribal Component Programme. At present, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement on Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) in respect of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) subject to a ceiling of 20% of the total budgetary allocation. In view of this, the Government is approving the release and expenditure of remaining 10% of the available funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities for disbursement of the target of 01-salary for the mandatory expenditure schemes in Demand No. T-2 and T-4 mentioned in the accompanying statement.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious for the next 3 to 4 months due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds in this distribution. This fund should be used for regular payment of salaries. It should not be used to pay any pending liabilities at present.
