the prelude
Pardhi is an extremely backward tribe found in the state. There are many problems related to the health and nutrition of this tribe. The tribe is found in Yavatmal district along with other parts of the state. The people of Yavatmal district are facing a lot of health problems. If a health guard is appointed in this area, primary health care will be provided. Also, many epidemics can be controlled through awareness in the Parsi community. Deendayal Bahu Purvashikshan Mandal, Yavatmal is an organization working in Yavatmal district for the various problems of backward people in rural areas.
Agriculture, health care, and education are also important. The organization works to provide education and scholarships to students for the families of suicide victims. It has also done special work in water conservation, organic farming, self-help groups etc. Therefore, in view of the experience of this organization in this area, the government was considering to approve the guidelines for the implementation of the scheme" Providing Preliminary Health Services to Paradhi community in Yavatmal District through Community Health Workers" jointly by the Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Pandharkawada/ Pusad and Deendayal Bahu Udeshiya Prasarak Mandal, Yavatmal.
Government's decision
In order to provide health facilities through health guards in Pardhi Padas of Yavatmal district and to create awareness among them about health, the implementation guidelines for the scheme" Providing Preliminary Health Services to Pardhi community in Yavatmal District through Community Health Workers" jointly implemented by the Tribal Development Department and Deendayal Bahu Udeshiya Prasarak Mandal, Yavatmal at a total cost of Rs. 51.98 lakh are being approved as per Annexure No.1 attached through this decision.
2. The Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Pandharkawada/ Pusad shall take immediate action for implementation of the scheme within the limits of the allocated provisions as per the guidelines of the said scheme and shall take care to submit financial and physical reports to the Government.
3. Expenditure on Demand No. Various schemes for the development of T-09, Pardhi community should be done from the funds available at the district level under the head- TT 240039 District Level Scheme).
