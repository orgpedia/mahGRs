The Department of Tribal Development has prepared Education Ranking Framework, School Development Plans, and Resource Mobilization Frameworks for Ashram Shalas with the objective of creating a score structure for measuring the quality of education in Ashram Shalas, determining criteria for determining school score structure, promoting excellent Ashram Shalas, and formulating programs as per need. 250.00 lakh has been proposed to the Central Government.
Ashramshalas are run by the Tribal Development Department for the education of tribal students. The aim of the department is to provide quality education to tribal students. A proposal was submitted to the Union Government for creating an Education Ranking Framework, School Development Plans, and Resource Mobilization Frameworks for ashram shramas to create a score structure for measuring the quality of education in ashram schools, to set criteria for school score structure, to create a personal development plan for the school, to build the capacity of the value-adjuster who inspects the school, to give incentive awards to ashramshalas who do excellent work, to design area-wise programmes for ashramshalas that are lagging behind in score, and to increase the quality of education there. The Central Government has approved the implementation of this scheme under Special Central Assistance for the year 2018-19 vide letter No. 1 to the tune of Rs. Rs. 250.00 crore. Reference 2.
