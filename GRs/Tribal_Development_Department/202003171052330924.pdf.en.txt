Government's decision:
The demand for the Centrally Sponsored National Rural Livelihood Mission under the Tribal Component Programme for the financial year 2019-20 is No. T-5, 2501 Special Village Development Programme, 01 Integrated Village Development Programme, 796 Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 01 Scheme under Tribal Area Appropriation, (01) (10) Financial Assistance to Scheduled Tribes for Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana (State Share) (2501A057), 33 Financial Assistance. A budget provision of Rs. 563.01 lakh has been made.
2. Refer No. 3 Under the letter of the Central Government here, the Central Account for the year 2016-17 and 2017-18 is Rs. 633.01 lakh has been deposited with Maharashtra State Livelihood Mission (MSLM). 563.01 lakh out of the budgeted fund of Rs. 422.01 lakhs (Rupees Four Crore Twenty Two Lakhs One Thousand only) to the Additional Chief Secretary, Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department in the case should ensure that the fund is spent in the same year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, the utilization certificate of the fund, the material objectives achieved in the headings/ sub-headings etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal component programme area shall be recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
