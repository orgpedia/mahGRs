4. It may be noted that in case of non-appearance at the place of posting immediately as per the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of political pressure, disciplinary action will be initiated against the concerned officers considering the matter as misconduct as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the M.N.S. (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
