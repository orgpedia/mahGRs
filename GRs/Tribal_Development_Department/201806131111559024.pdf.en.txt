Government's decision:
For the financial year 2018-19, the demand for the Centrally Sponsored National Rural Livelihood Mission under the Tribal Component Programme was Rs. T-5,2501 Special Village Development Programme, 01 Integrated Village Development Programme, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, 01 Tribal Area Scheme under Utilization, (01) (07) Financial Assistance (Central Share) for Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission (2501 2962), 33 Financial Assistance A sum of Rs. 5040.00 lakhs under the head of Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission (2501 2793), 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Payment). 2200.00 lakhs is budgeted.
The reference No. 2 from the Central Government of Rs. 1071.20 lakh has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State, as proposed by the Department of Rural Development, for the year 2018-19, the budget outlay under the head of the above Central Account (2501 2962) is Rs. 5040.00 lacs out of which Rs. 1071.20 lakhs (Rupees Ten Crores One Hundred and Seventy-one Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) and Rs. 2200.00 lakh out of the budgeted fund of Rs. 714.13 lakh (Rupees Seven Crores Fourteen Lakhs Thirteen Thousand only) is being sanctioned to Secretary, Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS).
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department in the case should ensure that the fund is spent in the same year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, the utilization certificate of the fund, the material objectives achieved in the headings/ sub-headings etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai by the 10th of every month.
The expenditure incurred by the Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department on the schemes in the Tribal Sub Plan Area, in the Additional Tribal Sub Plan Area and outside the Tribal Component Programme Area shall be recorded separately.
