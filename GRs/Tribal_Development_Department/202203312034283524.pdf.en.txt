Read- 1.Samak Named Government Corrigendum dated 31st January 2022.
As per the Namud Sasan Corrigendum, under the State Level Tribal Development Programme, a total of 3,390 homesteads have been approved for the year 2021-22 to solve the housing problem of the Katkari, Madia Gond and Kolam primitive tribes of the state. Now, as per the revised budget provision under this scheme, Rs. 3000.00 lakhs has been made available. From this fund, a total of 1842 additional houses were targeted for the year 2020-21 for the beneficiaries of the primitive tribes in addition to the above mentioned.
Government Decision- In addition to the district-wise household target given in the Reference Government Corrigendum for the year 2021-22 under the State Scheme of Primitive Tribal Development Programme, the Government is giving more district-wise household target as mentioned in the following table.
Accordingly, the cost of construction of houses is being approved.
