The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, pursuant to the Government Circular No. 1 under reference, has decided to increase the limit of funds made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Department of Finance, in the context of the Department of Tribal Development, No. 2 Under the Government decision, 25% of the budgetary allocation has been provided for salary and 10% for office bound matters under the mandatory schemes.
Now, the Finance Department has decided to introduce Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS) in Chapter No. 2 wages, 6 telephone, electricity and water charges, 13 office expenses, 14 rent and taxes at 5%, 10 contract services at 15% and 24 petrol, oil and lubricants at 10%. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of the increased funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Racket No. 5 for the matter of obligation for the schemes mentioned in Racket No. 2 in the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the terms and conditions of the state government.
(3) The Government shall take such decision within the limits and subject to the reference made by the Finance Department on the budgetary allocation system. The powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Circular dated 06.04.2021 are being issued.
