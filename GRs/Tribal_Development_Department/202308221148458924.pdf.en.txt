For supply of vegetables, mutton, chicken, eggs, bananas to the students of Government Ashramshalas, a project-wise e-tendering process should be implemented for all Ashramshalas under the respective Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project Office through a committee headed by the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development.
3. The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development to implement the tender process. :
1) The concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development Chairman 2) Joint Director of Industries (Divisional Office)/ Representative Member 3) Assistant Commissioner (Finance), Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development Member 4) Assistant Project Officer (Education) Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development: Member 5) Two Project Officers under the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development Member 6) Assistant Commissioner, Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development Member Secretary
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(a) The project cost shall be determined on the basis of the geographical location of the project.
Project-wise tenders should be floated for supply of vegetables, mutton, chicken, eggs, bananas to the students of government ashramshalas through this committee.
However, considering the geographical distance and small number of schools in the government ashramshalas under the Integrated Tribal Development Project, Solapur, Ghodegaon, Aurangabad, Kalamnuri, Akola, Bhandara, Chimur, Nagpur and Wardha, the ashramshala-wise tender process should be implemented.
e) Under each project, while executing the tender process for the said Ashramshala-wise
The Project Officer shall ensure that the rates of Ashramshala, which has the highest rate of vegetables, mutton, chicken, eggs, bananas to be supplied to the Ashramshalas in the area, shall not be more than 5% of the average rates of other Ashramshalas under the project. In case of ashramshalas where the rates are more than 5% of the average rates, a proposal should be submitted to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, stating the justifiable reasons for the increase in rates.
