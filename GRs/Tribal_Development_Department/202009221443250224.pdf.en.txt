Government's decision
The circular dated 16.04.2020 of the Finance Department has prescribed the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21, considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona virus. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided from time to time on the Budget Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. Under the Tribal Component Programme, 50% of the budgetary allocation for the objectives of 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) for the schemes under mandatory expenditure and 20% of the allocation for the objectives of office expenditure have been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. Now, in the month of October, funds have been made available through the Finance Department on the Budget Fund Distribution System for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Support Grants (Salary) within the limit of 60% and for the purpose of 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water Charges, 13-Office Expenses, 14-Lease and Taxes within the limit of 30%.
Accordingly, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the funds as per Pillar-5 to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the schemes in the accompanying prospectus in the mandatory expenditure demand number T-2 and T-4 under the Tribal Component Programme.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious in the next period due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner.
3. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer may refer to Pillar No. The provisions mentioned in section 5 shall be distributed to the drawing and disbursing officers under their command. Also, while spending the funds distributed under this government decision, the head of the department and the controlling officers will have to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook, as well as in the context of the Finance Department. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
4. The decision of the Government shall be subject to the approval of the Finance Department. It is being issued under the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
