The Proposal:
School Education Dept. As per the decision of the Government dated 24.12.2007, additional batches for tribal students have been started in Sainik Schools in the State. A grant is provided to cover the tuition fees and hostel expenses of these tribal students enrolled in Sainik School.
Under the Tribal Component Programme, in the financial year 2023-24, Demand No. Support to the contingent of tribal students in Military Schools in T-5 (01) (07) (Programme Expenditure) (Head of Accounts 2202H973), Chapter No. 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) Below Rs. 533.73 lakh to the Finance Department. 3 was requested. The proposal was under the consideration of the government to disburse funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
2225 under Demand No.T-5 of the Tribal Development Department- General Education (01) (07)- Assistance to the batch of tribal students in Sainik Schools (Programme Expenditure) (2202H973), Chapter No. As per the approval of the Finance Department under Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary), out of the budgetary provisions of 2023-24, Rs. 533.73 lakh (Rupees Five Crore Thirty Three Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) to the Controller and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
