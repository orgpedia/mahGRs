Government's decision
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2020 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2020) for the financial year 2020-21 has been approved by the Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is in addition to the provisions of the Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, Dt. 18.03.2020 has been published.
Accordingly, vide Circular No.1 of the Department of Finance dated 16.04.2020, considering the pandemic situation in the State due to Corona Virus, it has been approved to disburse funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary provisions of essential and programme expenditure for the first three months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020).
Read as follows. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. 20% of the total budgetary allocation for the following schemes under the head 2202 General Education in T-5 has been released to the Department of School Education. At present, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement under the Budgetary Allocation Mechanism (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01- Salary and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) within the limit of 25% of the total budgetary provisions.
In view of this, the Government is approving to disburse the following 5% of the available funds for these schemes on the budgetary fund distribution system to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department.
