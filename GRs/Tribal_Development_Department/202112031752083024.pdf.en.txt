Preamble: Scheme for imparting education to the students of Scheduled Tribes in the English medium reputed residential schools in the city. It is being implemented in the State as per the Government decision dated 28.08.2009. Further, in order to facilitate the academic progress of the ST students admitted under the scheme along with other students studying in the nominated schools selected under the scheme, it has been decided to grant admission in the initial phase of Class 1 and Class 2 from the academic year 2017-18. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 29.5.2017. To streamline the implementation of the scheme

Due to the lockdown in the state due to the outbreak of COVID-19, the entire educational institutions were closed for some time during 2020-21. Accordingly, approval has been accorded vide letter dated 26.03.2021 at Reference No.5 for payment of dues to the nominated residential schools. Accordingly, it is understood that the old designated residential schools have been made permanent in the academic year 2020-21.
Due to the outbreak of Covid-19, the state was again put under lockdown in 2021. All educational institutions in the state were closed for the day. As per the Government Circular No.6 dated 07.07.2021 issued by the Department of School Education and Sports, it has been approved to start classes from class 8 to class 12 from 15.07.2021 in COVID free areas of the state. Further, vide Government Circular No. 7 dated 10.08.2021 issued under the School Education and Sports Department, it has been approved to start classes from 5th to 7th standard in rural areas and from 8th to 12th standard in urban areas of the State from 17.08.2021. Similarly, the Government Circular No. 8 dated 24.09.2021 under the School Education and Sports Department has approved the safe start of classes from class 5 to class 12 in rural areas and class 8 to class 12 in urban areas in the state from 04.10.2021.
In view of the above, the matter of retaining the old schools selected earlier under this scheme for the current academic year 2021 under the nominated scheme was under the consideration of the government.
The state government had imposed a lockdown due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
All educational institutions in the state were closed for the day. A meeting was held on 14.07.2021 regarding selection of schools for the academic year 2021-22 under the Nominated Residential Schools Scheme. In the meeting held on 14.07.2021, it has been directed to review the academic progress of the students admitted in the old schools and take a decision on giving more students after ascertaining the academic standards of the concerned school.
