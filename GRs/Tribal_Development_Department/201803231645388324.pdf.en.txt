Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the scheme (PESA 5% Direct Funding Scheme to Gram Panchayats) to provide 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications every year to Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) are available for disbursement. 151 villages in 86 Gram Sabhas in Kalwan Panchayat Samiti area under Nashik ZP, 213 villages in 73 Gram Sabhas in Peth Panchayat Samiti area, 138 villages in 103 Gram Sabhas in Dindori Panchayat Samiti area, 67 villages in 63 Gram Sabhas in Igatpuri Panchayat area, 47 villages in 32 Gram Sabhas in Nashik Panchayat area, 124 villages in 84 Gram Sabhas in Tribhankeshwar Panchayat area, 190 villages in 61 Gram Sabhas in Surgana Panchayat area, 75 villages in 50 Gram Sabhas in Baglan Panchayat area, 41 villages in 22 Gram Sabhas in Deola Panchayat area have bank accounts of their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti. 485.1643/- Rs. 44,63,95, 274.18/- (Rs. forty four crore sixty three lakh ninety five thousand two hundred and four seventy eight paise only) as mentioned in the Annexure attached to the fund, Gram Sabha wise
