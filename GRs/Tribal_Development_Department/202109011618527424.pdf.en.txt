the prelude
For the financial year 2021-22. Guidelines have been issued for taking financial measures by setting spending priorities under Government Circulars 1 and 2. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) by the Department of Finance, as made available from time to time on the Distribution System, vide Reference No. Under the decision of the Government here, funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Officer to the extent of 37% for salary and 30% for other matters under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation.
Now, the Finance Department has made available 50% of the budgetary allocation under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme on Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS) in respect of 01- Salary, 02- Wages and 10- Contractual Services and 40% of the budgetary allocation on 06- Telephone, Electricity and Water Charges, 13- Office Expenses, 14- Rent, Bandage and Tax. Accordingly, out of the provisions available for distribution within this limit, the matter of distribution of the remaining provision was under consideration of the Government considering the provisions distributed earlier.
Government's decision
Considering the provisions previously allocated out of the budgetary provisions in the accompanying statement under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme under Demand No. T-9, T-10, the Government is approving the remaining provisions mentioned in Pillar-5 to be distributed and spent by the concerned Controlling Officer and the Collector.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
