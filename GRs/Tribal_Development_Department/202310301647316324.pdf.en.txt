In order to increase the pass rate of the Administrative Services Examination of the students of the Scheduled Tribes of the state, the Administrative Services Competitive Examination Training Program conducted by the Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission is implemented as per the decision of the government in the context.
Read No. As per the letter, the proposal for disbursement of funds under the scheme for the financial year 2023-24 has been received by the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. Under the Tribal Sub-Plan Scheme for the year 2023-24, Rs. 5000.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 1000.00 lakhs is available for disbursement.
