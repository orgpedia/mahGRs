The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSC), Drought Area Development Programme and Climate Change Sustainable Agriculture Control Model and Outreach Programme, being implemented by the Department of Agriculture under Tribal Component Programme, has been extended to the STs for the year 2020-21 with the first instalment for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 respectively.
The central share of the financial year is Rs. 109.851 lakh and State share of Rs. 73.234 lakh has been released by the Department of Agriculture. The matter of disbursement of funds in accordance with the proposal submitted under Section 4 was under consideration of the Government.
