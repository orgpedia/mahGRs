The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 8.02.2023, for the schemes under Compulsory and Programme Expenditure, the limit of fund disbursement has been prescribed for the period up to the time of finalization of Revised Estimates for the year 2022-23. Also, the funds as per the disbursement limit are made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) disbursement system. In this regard, the Government was considering the issue of disbursement of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the distribution of the remaining funds for the mandatory expenditure schemes of the Tribal Development Department in the context of the Government decisions at No.2, the revised estimates received from the field offices and the proposed revised estimates from the Government level.
Government's decision:
The demand for schemes under" Compulsory Expenditure" of Tribal Development Department No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Racket No. 5 for the schemes mentioned in Racket No. 2 of the accompanying returns T-2, T-4 and below.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
