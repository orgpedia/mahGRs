Preamble: Under the Tribal Development Department, Roads and Bridges under Tribal Utilization, Road works under Public Works Department are taken up under heading 5054 of the scheme.
For the financial year 2017-18, the Chief Minister has approved the distribution of funds available at the department level under Account Head 5054 and Account Head 3054 (State Level Schemes). Comments were posted on June 19, 2017. Accordingly, the disbursement of funds under the head of accounts 5054 5117 has been taken up.
Accordingly, information was sought from the concerned Superintendent Engineer and Public Works Board. The department will have pending liabilities and ma. Considering the directions of the Chief Minister and the information received from the concerned Project Officers and Integrated Tribal Development Project, the government was considering the matter of cancelling the works of a total of 588 roads and excluding these works from the budget estimates of the department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Total 14776.00 Lakhs (Rs. One hundred and forty seven crore seventy six lakh only) of total roads 588 (five hundred and eighty eight)
