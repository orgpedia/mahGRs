The Division Level Forest Rights Committee shall fix the date and time for hearing the claimant's application and communicate the same in writing to the claimant and the concerned District Level Committee.
The notice shall be served at least 15 days in advance at a convenient public place in the village of the claimant.
4. The appeal of the claimant may be admitted or rejected by the Division Level Committee. Accordingly, the appellant and the District Forest Rights Committee should be informed.
After the department level committee approves the appeal application of the claimant, it should be reported to the concerned collector for further appropriate action on the claim and for necessary corrections and updates in the government records.
(3) The non-official members of the aforesaid Committee shall be given the decision of the Government of the Finance Department. Paragraph No. 03.03.2010 Travel allowance and daily allowance will be admissible under Section 12.
In order to maintain uniformity in the functioning of all the Division Level Forest Rights Committees, the sample of the claimant's appeal to the Committee and the sample of the committee's appeal to be accepted or rejected, the records to be maintained by the Committee, the register and the report to be submitted to the Forest Rights Nodal Agency shall be made available to the Division Level Forest Rights Committees separately by the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune.
