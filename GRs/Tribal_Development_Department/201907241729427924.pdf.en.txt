With a view to digitally empower the students residing in the Government Ashram Schools/ Hostels under the Tribal Development Department and to bring them into the digital mainstream for their economic and social empowerment, the Department of Tribal Development has proposed to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India, a scheme titled" Anant Computing- Digital Inclusion of Tribal Youth by teaching in Vernacular Coding Language including NLP." This proposal has been approved by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs in the Project Appraisal (Committee PAC) meeting held on 12.02.2019 vide Letter of Reference No. 1.An amount of Rs. 200.00 lakh and Rs. 200.00 lakh respectively for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21. The funds have been distributed to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, as per the decision of the concerned government.
GrassRoot Software Innovation Technologies Private Limited is an information technology company founded in 2012. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed between Ananta Computing and Tribal Development Department. Ananta Computing was the first company in India to develop an operating system for tablets in the local language. Infinite Computing mainly works on digital inclusion and makes local language applications. This has made it possible to deliver high-quality applications in local languages to more people. Ananta Computing has worked with NABARD, RUSA (Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan), Mumbai APMC, Indian Railway Station Development Corporation Limited on digital inclusion. Anant Computing's innovative work was selected in the India Innovation Growth Program organized by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India in 2014. He was also selected for the Start-up India programme.
