the prelude
As per the decision of the Government, a target of 1168 has been fixed for total 08 districts for the year 2018-19 under the Adim Janjatiya Gharkul Yojana. Accordingly, the target of 117 households has been set for Nagpur district. However, in Nagpur district, there are difficulties in achieving the target of the scheme due to non-availability of eligible beneficiaries to provide house site under the scheme. Therefore, a request has been made under the reference letter to transfer this target to other districts.
Accordingly, in order to provide houses to the eligible beneficiaries of Wardha district, where there is a demand for tribal houses, the Government was considering to transfer the target of 117 houses given to Nagpur district in the year 2018-19 under the Adim Janajati Ghar Kul Yojana to Wardha district.
the Government Purifier
As per the decision of the Government, the target of 117 houses to be provided to Nagpur district under the Adim Janjati Ghar Kul Yojana for the year 2018-19 (total fund of Rs. 154.44 lakh) for Wardha district. Accordingly, the revised target for Wardha district is 143 and the sanctioned fund is Rs. 188.76 lakh. While achieving the target given to Wardha district, Rs. Rs 1.32 lakh. However, the Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Wardha will have the power to modify the limit of total funds received at the local level keeping in view the criteria of plain and hilly areas.
