Women employees working at Marya, Nashik are hereby informed that if they have any complaint regarding sexual harassment, it should be submitted to the Chairperson or Member Secretary of the Internal Committee or the Internal Complaints Committee.
The functions of the Women's Complaints Committee shall be as follows:
The Central Government has notified the Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 dated 23rd April, 2013 and Rules made thereunder on 09th December, 2013. To take necessary measures to prevent sexual harassment of women and to provide a safe working environment.
(ii) to examine the complaints received at the level of the department within a stipulated time and submit proposals for further action to the appropriate authority in accordance with the complaints.
To create awareness to prevent incidents like sexual harassment and to ensure the creation of a safe environment by holding regular meetings of the Committee.
At least four members of the committee must be present to make the decision.
(5) To prepare an annual report in accordance with the provisions of this Act.
