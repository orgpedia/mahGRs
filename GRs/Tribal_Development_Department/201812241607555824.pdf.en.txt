Circular: The Government has decided to pay the salaries and allowances of the teachers and non-teaching staff of the aided ashramshalas run by the NGOs under the Tribal Development Department through the" Ashramshalar" salary system. Further, instructions have been issued under Circular No. 2 in this regard to take punitive and administrative action against the concerned Project Officer as well as the Headmaster of the concerned Ashramshala if the regular salary of all the teachers/ non-teaching staff is not paid through the Ashramshala system on the 1st of every month. Similarly, the responsible officer of the headmaster of the concerned school to send the complete and accurate salary payments of the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the aided ashramshalas to the concerned Project Officer by the 15th of every month, the Project Officer as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the payment of salary on the 1st of every month and the concerned Additional Commissioner as the Supervisor/ Controlling Officer for this entire work. Responsibility has been assigned by the circular of 3. Further, if the complete and accurate salary payments of the teaching and non-teaching staff are not prepared and sent to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer by the 15th day, the school principal and the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer will be held responsible if the salaries of the concerned teachers/ non-teaching staff are not paid on the 1st day after receiving the payments within the prescribed period.
