Introduction With a view to promote in-depth study of the process of social and economic changes in tribal areas and tribal communities, the scheme of Research Scholarships for 2 candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribes has been introduced by Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune in the aforesaid Reference No. Decisions 1 and 2 are being implemented. Reference No. The rate of research scholarship under the Government decision of 1 is Rs. 2800/- per annum and Rs. 10,000/- has been fixed. The rate has not been revised since 2011.
For the candidates belonging to the Scheduled Castes in the state of Maharashtra, BARTI, Pune, for the candidates belonging to the Maratha and Kunbi castes, Sarathi, Pune, and for the candidates belonging to the Other Backward Castes, Nomadic and Denotified Tribes, Mahajyoti, Nagpur, will be awarded Ph.D.
Financial assistance is provided for the course. Therefore, there was a demand from various tribal representatives and organizations in the state to start a scheme to provide scholarship for PhD courses for Scheduled Tribe candidates as well. Therefore, in order to increase the target of the scheme and improve the amount of scholarship by including other faculties in the ongoing research scholarship, the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune, has issued the above Reference No.
The matter of issuing revised guidelines of the scheme in accordance with the proposal received under letter No. 3 was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to award Ph.D. to 100 Scheduled Tribe candidates in Maharashtra every year.
