A total of 20,870 posts in the field offices under the Tribal Development Department have been fixed as per the Government decision mentioned in A. No. 2 to 18 above.
As per the Government decision in the aforesaid Act 1 of the Finance Department, it has been notified to fix the revised figure after reviewing the sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to review the posts in the field offices under the Tribal Development Department and fix the revised figure.
As per the decision taken by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries constituted by the Government to review the posts in the Regional Offices under the Tribal Development Department and to examine in depth the proposals for creation of new posts, revival of posts and review of posts etc. as per requirement, a total of 14,359 regular posts for the Regional Offices under the Tribal Development Department and a total of 6088 services to be taken up by external agencies are being approved under this Government decision.
