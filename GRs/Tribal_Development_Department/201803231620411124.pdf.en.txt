Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No.
The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. In the year 2017-2018, Rs. 214,30,87, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) has been made available for disbursement.
2) For this scheme, Rs. 214,30,87, 000.00 (Rupees Two Hundred Fourteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand only) is available for disbursement to the beneficiaries of the scheme who are residing in 67 Gram Sabhas comprising 98 villages in Taloda Panchayat Samiti area under Nandurbar Zilla Parishad, 192 villages in 77 Gram Sabhas in Akkalkuwa Panchayat Samiti area, 160 villages in 50 Gram Sabhas in Akrani Panchayat Samiti area, 170 villages in 114 Gram Sabhas in Navapur Panchayat Samiti area, 107 villages in 98 Gram Sabhas in Nandurbar Panchayat Samiti area, 142 villages in 112 Gram Sabhas in Shahada Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts in Taloda, Akkalkuwa, Akrani, Navapur, Nandurbar, Shahada Gram Panchayats in Pesa area of the State. 485.1643/- Rs. 49,75,18, 973.49/- (Rs. Nineteen fifty five crore seventy five lakh eighteen thousand nine hundred and seventy nine rupees only) is being provided directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the Annexure attached Gram Sabha wise.
This fund will be provided by the concerned Gram Sabha to the tribals of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada under it.
