Introduction: The government and aided ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department were closed due to the outbreak of the Corona (COVID-19) virus. Reference No. As per the decision of the Government dated 26/07/2021, the schools of Government Ashramshalas, Aided Ashramshalas and Eklavya Residential Schools from 8th to 12th standard have been approved to start from 2nd August, 2021. Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi is organizing Swachhta Pakhwada from 1st to 15th August, 2021 across the country. The directions are given here under letter dated 14/07/2021. Accordingly, the state government was considering issuing detailed guidelines on the implementation of cleanliness fortnight in government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential School, government hostels and tribal research and training institute under the tribal development department.

Government Circular No: SHAASH 2021/ PR No.62/ K.A.13-A
Government Circular: Under the Tribal Development Department of the State, the principals/ housekeepers/ concerned officials should take the following action to implement the cleanliness fortnight from August 1 to August 15, 2021 in government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential School, government hostels and tribal research and training institutes.
1.To make students, teachers, non-teaching staff and officers/ employees of government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential Schools and government hostels aware of Swachhta Pakhwada and create awareness in this regard.
2) To sensitize the students, teachers, non-teaching staff and officers/ employees of government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential Schools and government hostels.
3.To inspect the cleanliness in government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential School, government hostels and tribal research and training institute through a tri-committee and take necessary action accordingly.
The guidelines/ instructions issued by the Central and State Governments from time to time in relation to COVID-19 should be strictly followed.
5) Prepare a plan for cleaning and disinfecting your school and institution and take action accordingly.
6) Students, teachers, non-teaching staff and officers/ employees of government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential Schools and government hostels should be given a pledge on cleanliness. This pledge is available on Swachh Bharat Portal.
Students, teachers, non-teaching staff and officers/ employees of government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential Schools and government hostels should conduct a cleanliness drive in the school premises/ neighbourhood/ village area.
Students, teachers, non-teaching staff and officers/ employees of government and aided ashramshalas, Eklavya Model Residential Schools and government hostels should carry out tree plantation drive in the school/ office as well as in the vicinity of the house/ village. Each student should plant one sapling.
9.Take necessary steps for the management of waste and waste water.
Students, teachers, non-teaching staff and officers/ employees should conduct a plastic-free environment campaign in the school/ office as well as in the vicinity of home/ village.
To create awareness among students about cleanliness, painting/ essay/ slogan/ rangoli/ elocution competitions should be organized.
12. Webinar on sanitation to be organized at Integrated Tribal Development Project level.
