The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme being implemented by the Department of School Education under the main head General Education, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the schemes of 1) Subsidiary Grants to General Secondary Schools (2202 1901), 2) Subsidiary Grants to Non-Government Junior Colleges (2202 1948) and 3) Financial Assistance to Batch of Tribal Students in Military Schools (2202 H 973) have been sanctioned for the year 2021-22 with a total outlay of Rs. 6250.00 lakhs under reference No. 2 has been distributed to the School Education Department as per the government decisions here. Incremental provision for wages under these schemes has been budgeted through Supplementary Demands for Grants in the Winter Session of December 2021.
Accordingly, the School Education Department, No. 4 As per the approval given by the Finance Department on the issue, the government was considering to disburse the budgeted funds to the School Education Department through supplementary demand.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2021-22, Demand No. 2202 General Education in T-5, as made available by the Department of Finance for disbursement on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS), the total amount of Rs. 6037.33 lakh (Rupees Sixty Crores Thirty Seven Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand only) to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Ministry of Education, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
