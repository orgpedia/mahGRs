Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand, Three Hundred only) is available for disbursement in 218 villages of 61 Gram Sabhas in Dhanora Panchayat Samiti area under Gadchiroli Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas of the State.
119 villages in 44 Gram Sabhas in Kurkheda Panchayat Samiti area, 120 villages in 29 Gram Sabhas in Korchi Panchayat Samiti area, 49 villages in 20 Gram Sabhas in Gadchiroli Panchayat Samiti area, 49 villages in 16 Gram Sabhas in Armori Panchayat Samiti area, 74 villages in 38 Gram Sabhas in Chamorshi Panchayat Samiti area, 8 villages in 6 Gram Sabhas in Desaiganj Panchayat Samiti area, 113 villages in 39 Gram Sabhas in Sironcha Panchayat area, 150 villages in 39 Gram Sabhas in Aheri Panchayat area, 37 villages in 11 Gram Sabhas in Moolacherra Panchayat area, 100 villages in 19 Gram Sabhas in Bhamragad Panchayat area, 180 villages in 31 Gram Sabhas in Aitapalli Panchayat area have been provided with financial assistance in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts.
