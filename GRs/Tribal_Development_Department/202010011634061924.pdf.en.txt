The Proposal:
A total of 288 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune under the Tribal Development Department and in the office of the Scheduled Tribe Certification Scrutiny Committee under it, have been notified under reference No. The period was extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. On expiry of the said period, the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune vide Reference No. As proposed under Reference No.2 of the Finance Department. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Section 3, the Government was considering the extension of a total of 288 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune and in the office of the 08 Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committee under him.
Government's decision:
A total of 288 temporary posts mentioned in the next table in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune and in the office of the 8 Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committee under him are being extended from September 1, 2020 to February 28, 2021 under this Government decision.
