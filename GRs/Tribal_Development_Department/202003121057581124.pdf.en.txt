The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in the context, a scheme has been launched to provide the benefit of reimbursement of vocational training fee to the Scheduled Tribes students who have taken admission in the seats available under the PPP (Public Private Partnership) scheme in Government Industrial Training Institutes as well as in Private Industrial Training Institutes through centralized online mode from the admission session 2019-20. The government was considering providing separate head of accounts for the provision and expenditure of funds for the scheme.
Government's decision:
Expenditure under the Scheme for reimbursement of Vocational Training Fee introduced for the seats available under PPP (Public Private Partnership) Scheme in Government Industrial Training Institutes and for the ST students admitted through Central Online System in Private Industrial Training Institutes shall be made in the order available under the head of Accounts from the financial year 2020-21.
