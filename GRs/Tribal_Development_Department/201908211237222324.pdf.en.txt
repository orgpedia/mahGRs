Preamble: The Tribal Development Department has established Government Tribal Hostels across the State to provide secondary, higher secondary and college education to the children of Scheduled Tribes at the Taluk, District and Divisional levels. In respect of admission of students in these hostels, in the aforesaid context, No. 1 here. The rules have been implemented under the Government Decision of 3.8.2004. Also, in reference No. 2 and 3 here. As per the Government Decisions dated 21.9.2005 and 19.7.2006, provisions have been made for admission of students in these hostels under special category. The matter of amending the existing procedure/ provision for admission of students in these hostels as a special case was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the said Government decision, in the context of the above, regarding admission of students in Government Tribal Hostels as a special case, vide No. 1 here. By repealing the provisions contained in paragraph (a) (2) of paragraph 1 of the Government Decision of 3.8.2004, as well as in the aforesaid Reference No. 2 and 3 here. The Government is implementing the following guidelines by superseding the Government Decisions dated 21.9.2005 and 19.7.2006.
(1) In every Government Hostel for tribal boys and girls, 5% of the total number of seats available in that year shall be filled up from amongst the students belonging to Backward Classes (Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Castes, Nomadic Tribes and Other Backward Classes) from the Government level as a" special case."

(2) It is being made mandatory for the students participating in the provision of admission in the Government Tribal Hostel as a special case to apply on the website www. swayammahaonline. gov. in.
(3) As a special case, hostel admission shall be granted only for the duration of the course.
(4) The limit of income of the family for admission of special cases shall be fixed by the Government. The hostel will remain as prescribed for admission rules under the Government decision dated 3.8.2004.
(5) The list of students to be admitted to the Government Tribal Hostel as a special case shall be given to the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, after approval from the Government. It will be delivered on September 30th.
(6). After September 30, the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, considered the site as" Unreserved" under the Special Subject. The payment should be made in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decree of 3.8.2004.
(7) Applications of students who are unable to enter the hostel due to lack of vacant seats should be considered by the Project Officer for the SWAYAM scheme.
