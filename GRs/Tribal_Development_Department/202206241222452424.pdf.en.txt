The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System is being made available for distribution of funds in a limited manner through the Finance Department through Government Circular No. 1 in the context of Finance Department. Under the schemes being implemented by the Department of School Education under the Tribal Component Programme as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system by the Department of Finance, 32% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) has been disbursed to the Department of School Education as per the Government decisions at Reference No. 2.
Now that a total of 50% of the budgetary provision has been made available for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for disbursement through the Finance Department, the matter of disbursement of funds to the School Education Department in accordance with the proposal at No. 3 was under the consideration of the Government for disbursement of the remaining available additional 18% funds.
