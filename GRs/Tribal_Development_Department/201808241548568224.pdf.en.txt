The Government of India has sanctioned Rs. 300.00 lakh for the year 2017-18 under the scheme Setting up Libraries in Ashram Schools under the Special Central Assistance Scheme. This fund reference No. 3 has been allocated to the Commissioner Tribal Development, Nashik as per the decision of the Government here. The scheme is being implemented by the Tribal Development Department and Eklavya Foundation. The implementation of the scheme No. 4 The guidelines have been approved under the government decision here.
However, since the method of distribution of funds and the stages of year-wise distribution have not been mentioned in this government decision, the method of distribution of funds is being determined as follows for the implementation of this scheme.
Mode of disbursement of funds
------------------------------------------------------
| A. No   | Year       | Funds disbursed (Rs. Lakhs) |
| First   | 138.81030/ |
| the 2nd | 91.89130/  |
| the 3rd | 69.29840/  |
| Total   | 300.00/-   |
------------------------------------------------------
The funds will be transferred to Eklavya Foundation, Bhopal through Commissioner, Tribal Development Department in a year-wise manner as mentioned above.
The first year's fund should be paid to the institution at the beginning of the implementation of the scheme.
The second and third year funds will be disbursed to the institute only after satisfactory report of the Control and Evaluation Committee of the scheme.
