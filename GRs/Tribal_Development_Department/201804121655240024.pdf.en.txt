Preamble: The National Commission for Scheduled Tribes (NCST) has been constituted under Article 338A of the Constitution of India, as per the notification in this regard. The National Commission for Scheduled Tribes (NCST) reviews the programmes and schemes being implemented by the States for the development and protection of Scheduled Tribes. For this, the members of the commission visit the state either together or separately. During the visit, he interacts with the representatives of various levels of State Government as well as political/ industry associations/ local bodies/ NGOs working for the upliftment of tribals. Against this backdrop, the government was considering declaring the chairman, vice-chairman and members of the National Commission for Scheduled Tribes as" state guests."
The Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members of the National Commission for Scheduled Tribes (NCST) are being declared as" State Guests" for the entire period of the Commission's stay in Maharashtra.
Government Circular, General Administration Department, No. SGR-1002/ 688/ CR- 92/31, d. As per the guidelines issued on 01.01.2004, the entire expenditure incurred by the Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Members and Secretaries of the National Commission for Scheduled Tribes for the entire period of the Commission for arranging the tour to Maharashtra will be shared under the Tribal Development Department as per the provisions available under the head Demand No.T-5, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, Welfare of 02 Scheduled Tribes, 796 Tribal Area Measures, (01) Schemes under State Schemes, (01) (01) Commissionerate of Tribal Development (2225 1393), 13 Office Expenditure. Payments in this regard should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the Political Affairs Cell of the General Administration Department.
