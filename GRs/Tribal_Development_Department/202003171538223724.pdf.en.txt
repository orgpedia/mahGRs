Government's decision:
The Ministry of Law and Justice has enacted the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. It was released on December 28, 2016. Section 37 (a) of the Act provides as follows:
The appropriate Government and the local authorities shall, by notification, make schemes in favour of persons with benchmark disabilities, to provide,
(a) five percent, reservation in allotment of agriculture land and housing in all relevant schemes and development programmes, with appropriate priority to women with benchmark disabilities.
2. For providing houses to the persons belonging to Scheduled Tribes in the State, read No. 1 above. Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme has been launched as per the Government decision dated 28th March, 2013.
Therefore, in view of the above provision of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Government is approving 5% reservation for persons with disabilities in Shabari Adivasi Housing Scheme. Priority should be given to women.
