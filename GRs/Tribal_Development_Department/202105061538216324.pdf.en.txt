In reference to the above No. As per the Government decision dated 01.01.2020, a Project Level (Planning Review) Committee has been constituted to review the schemes/ programmes in the tribal sub-plan areas. Thereafter, No. The composition and functioning of the Project Level (Planning and Evaluation) Committee, as well as other ancillary changes, were made under the Government Decision No. 2. At present, the composition of the Project Level (Planning and Evaluation) Committee was under consideration for some modifications.
Government decision
The composition of the Project Level (Planning and Evaluation) Committee mentioned in the Government Resolution No. 2 in the context is being amended as follows.
The terms of reference of the Committee, the minutes of the meeting and the decisions taken at the meeting shall continue to be in force.
Committees shall be composed of:
(i) Government nominated Tribal person as Chairperson of the Project Area (ii) Additional Commissioner, Vice-Chairperson of Tribal Development (iii) Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha) in the Project Area (iv) Member of Legislative Assembly (Vidhan Sabha)/ Special Invitee Council (v) Chairman of Social Welfare Committee of Zila Parishad and Chairman of Women and Child Welfare Committee (vi) Member to be nominated by the Government in the Project Area (vi) 6 Non-Government Members (Out of these 6 Non-Government Members, at least 1 Female Member from Scheduled Tribes and 5 Male Members from Scheduled Tribes)
