Introduction: There is a law, rule, law to protect the rights of children, value education and to prevent sexual abuse, which is useful for the student in the government ashram school under the tribal development department. But students and teachers are not aware of it. For this, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations was requested to prepare a media plan for providing pictorial books in the form of a coffee table book in government ashramshalas/ government hostels/ aided schools to create awareness about the rights of children in ashramshalas, value education and awareness about the POCSO Act to prevent sexual abuse. Accordingly, Rs. 200.00 lakh has been prepared by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations and the plan has been approved by the Committee constituted under the decision of the Government in the context of the General Administration Department. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
The Media Plan of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for providing pictorial books in the form of coffee table books in Government Ashramshalas/ Government Hostels/ Aided Schools for awareness on the rights of children, value education and POCSO Act to prevent sexual abuse, as well as Good Touch-Bad Touch for the students of Government Ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department was presented in the meeting held on 10/02/2022. The following Media Plans are being approved with a ceiling of Rs. 200.00 lakhs.
