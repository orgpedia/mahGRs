The Proposal:
The Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006, Rules, 2008 and Amendments Rules, 2012 are being strictly enforced to ensure time bound disposal of forest rights claims and appeals. 2 The government's
Notifications have been issued. Under this, the forest area in individual forest rights claims approved by the district level forest rights committee is being processed by the Land Records Department, for which the necessary funds are made available to the concerned collector as a" nodal agency" through the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. In pursuance of the demand made by the concerned District Collector for the contractual manpower and office expenses appointed for the implementation of the Forest Rights Act in the State, the Commissioner, Tribal and Research and Training Institute, Pune has sanctioned Rs. 247.20 lakh is proposed to be provided to the Finance Department vide Reference No. 5 were presented. The proposal was under the consideration of the government for disbursement of funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
