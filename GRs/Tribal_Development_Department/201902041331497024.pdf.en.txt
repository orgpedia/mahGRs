Government Decision Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden For the year 2018-19 for the Scheduled Tribe category for the Lavagadh Scheme Demand No. T-5, 2401, Crop Protection, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, Scheme under State Schemes, (01 (36) Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Lavgarh Scheme, Account Head (2401 9898) Rs. 450.00 lakh has been budgeted in the Legislative Assembly session of November, 2018. Accordingly, the Agriculture Department No. 4 In pursuance of the proposal herein, under the head of Accounts, Bhausaheb Phundkar Phalbagh Lavgarh Scheme (2401A898), an amount of Rs. 360.00 lakhs (Rupees Three Crores, Sixty Lakh only) to Secretary (Agriculture), Department of Agriculture& Farmers Welfare, Ministry of Agriculture& Farmers Welfare, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area as well as in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the sub-plan area.
(4) From the provision made under the Tribal Component Programme, schemes for creation of infrastructure in the tribal sub-plan area as well as individual benefit schemes should be taken up and only individual benefit schemes should be taken up in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area.
