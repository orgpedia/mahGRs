The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking fiscal measures by setting spending priorities as per the decisions of the government. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided from time to time on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System, has allocated 37% of the budgetary provision for the salary component of the schemes covered under the programme expenditure of the Tribal Development Department and 10-25% for other office purposes subject to the reference No. 2 Funds have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Government decisions.
Now, by the end of the month of August, the Department of Finance has made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System, under the Scheme of Programme Expenditure, in Chapter No. The Government was considering to disburse 13 per cent of the budgetary provision for 01 salary and 36 per cent of the grant-in-aid (salary), 35 per cent for 02 wages, 10 per cent for 11 domestic travel expenses, 03 per cent for 03 overtime allowance, 06 per cent for telephone, electricity and water charges, 13 per cent for office expenses, 14 per cent for lease and taxes, 19 per cent for food expenses, 21 per cent for supplies and materials and 50 per cent for other expenses and 25 per cent for 10 contract services and 17 per cent for computer expenses.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Budget provisions in the Annexure No. 5 for the matters covered under the schemes mentioned in Annexure No. 2 to the accompanying statement under T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
