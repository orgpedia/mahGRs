In order to provide English medium education to the ST students of Tribal Development Department, private English medium reputed residential schools have been identified. Under this scheme, students belonging to Scheduled Tribes are admitted in these residential schools. The schools selected under this scheme for classes 9th to 12th, will be affiliated by the respective institution as per the School Education Department Reference No. 4 Approval is being given to start following the instructions of the government decision here and according to the decision of the local level.
