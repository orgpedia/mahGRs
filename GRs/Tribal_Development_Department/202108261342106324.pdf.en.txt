The Central Government provides grants under the Special Central Assistance (SCA) Scheme, under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and the Tribal Development Programme to the State Governments every year. The selected proposals will be submitted to the central government after detailed scrutiny.
Various welfare schemes are implemented for the development of the tribal community through the Tribal Development Department. The department aims to provide irrigation facilities to the tribal farmers to provide them the benefit of sustainable agricultural income generation and thereby improve their living standards.
In order to increase the agricultural income of the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes who have received forest pattas under the Forest Rights Act 2006, and to install solar pumps through a well/ borewell for water supply to agriculture, the scheme Bore well/ dug well with solar pump (5 hp) for irrigation of land given under FRA 2006 was submitted to the Central Government. This scheme was launched by the Central Government under Special Central Assistance for the year 2015-16. 1 has been sanctioned and the funds have been disbursed to the State Government. The funds received have been disbursed to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik by the Government of the area under Section 2
Now, Bore well/ Dug well with solar pump (5 hp) for irrigation of land given under FRA 2006 for the year 2015-16 under Special Central Assistance is Rs. A proposal to approve the guidelines for implementation of the scheme at a cost of Rs. 1800.00 lakhs is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Bore well/ dug well with solar pump (5 hp) for irrigation of land given under FRA 2006" with a view to increase the income of the farmers by constructing bore well/ dug well and installing solar pump to supply water to the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes who have received forest pattas under the Forest Rights Act. The guidelines for implementation of the approved scheme worth Rs. 1800.00 lakh are being approved as per Annexure No.1 annexed to this decision.
