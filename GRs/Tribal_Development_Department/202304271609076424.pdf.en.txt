The Proposal:
The Central share received under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Dryland Area Development (RAD) under National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) being implemented by the Department of Agriculture under Tribal Component Programme for the year 2022-23 is Rs. 47.00 lakhs and State share of Rs. 31.34 lakhs for the financial year 2023-24. The proposal was submitted to the Finance Department. In pursuance of the approval given by the Finance Department to the above proposal, the matter of disbursement of funds from the budgetary provisions for the year 2023-24 was under consideration of the Government:
Government's decision:
