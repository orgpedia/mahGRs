Government's decision
As mentioned in the Preamble, the Government is approving the appointment of Deputy Commissioner, Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development as" Member Secretary" on the District Level Committee constituted under the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 and Rules, 2008 at the headquarters of the Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development.
To dispose of the pending forest rights claims and appeals by strictly implementing the provisions of the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 and Rules, 2008 and Rules, 2012. Under Section 7, a special" Vanamitra Abhiyan" is being implemented. The concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development will be responsible if any work of the District Level Committee remains pending during this campaign.
