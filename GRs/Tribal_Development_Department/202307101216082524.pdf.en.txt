Introduction: In the above context, No. The letter of 2. Pal, dated 21.08.2004. A children's hostel has been sanctioned at Raver, Jalgaon.
The reference No. As per letter No. 3, the Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Yawal has brought the following facts to the notice of the Government. The sanctioned strength of the hostel is 75 and the intake of students has been between 20 and 30 during the period 2013-14 to 2022-23. The building is on lease.
Also, the building does not have all the necessary facilities. The number of students enrolled is very low. Pal is located in the most inaccessible part of the Satpuda forest. There is a lot of forest around. Students are at risk from wild animals. As Pal is located in a very remote area, the students are deprived of water supply, network and other facilities due to the power supply being disrupted. Only the Arts stream is available for graduation at PALA. There are 576 ST students in Faizpur.
Faizpur has a municipal high school, junior college, undergraduate, postgraduate degrees, various diploma courses as well as professional courses.
Considering the above facts, the Government Children's Hostel Pal, Ta. Raver Hostel has been converted into Government Tribal Boys Hostel, Faizpur. The matter of relocating Yawal to Jalgaon was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The sanctioned intake capacity of the Government Hostel for Tribal Children, Pal, is 75. The government is giving permission to relocate Yawal, G. Jalgaon.
