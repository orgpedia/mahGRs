Introduction: Department of Education. As per the decision of the Government dated 24.12.2007, additional batches for tribal students have been started in Sainik Schools in the State. The grant is provided to cover the educational expenses and hostel maintenance of these tribal students admitted in Sainik School.
Under the Tribal Component Programme, in the financial year 2023-24, Demand No. Support to the contingent of tribal students in Military Schools in T-5 (01) (07) (Programme Expenditure) (Head of Accounts 2202H973), Chapter No. Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) Below Rs. 800.00 lakhs to the Finance Department. 2 was requested. The proposal was under the consideration of the government to disburse funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
