In pursuance of the proposal received from the Commissioner, Tribal Development Commissionerate, Nashik under the aforesaid Reference No. (2) along with the application made by Smt.Vaishali Sushilkumar Pendbhaje in the aforesaid Reference No. (3), the Head of the Administrative Department has directed her to take necessary action in accordance with the powers conferred on her by the Finance Department vide Government Resolution No.Anneo-2017/ PR.No. Smt. Vaishali Sushilkumar Pendbhaje, the nominee/ legal heir of Sushilkumar Vilas Pendbhaje (Mayat Chowkidar) is being sanctioned ex-gratia of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only).

The expenditure should be made under the head" Demand No. G-6,2071- Pension and other retirement benefits 01 Civil, 103, Compassionate Allowances, (00) (06)- Defined Contributions/ Grants-in-Aid under National Pension Scheme (20710722)."
