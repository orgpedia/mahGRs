3. The ad-hoc promotions given to the above officers are subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) Vacancies in the open category in the promotion and direct service quota will be filled only on a temporary basis. A Special Leave Petition No. In respect of Filling under 28306/2017, S.P.V./ 16-B has issued a D.O. The approval is being accorded in accordance with the instructions issued vide letter dated 29.12.2017.
(2) Since the said promotion is ad-hoc in nature, the officers in the selection list and in the promotion list will not be entitled to any regularisation and promotion as a result of this ad-hoc promotion.
(3) The ad-hoc promotion to the post in the direct service quota shall be for a period of 11 months or until the candidates who have gone on deputation return or the candidates who have been granted commission are available, whichever is earlier. In case of receipt of candidates approved by the Commission in the interim period, the officers who have been promoted ad hoc will be demoted. The promotion is temporary in nature and the post will not be held by the concerned officers on a permanent basis.
4) This promotion is ad-hoc in nature and is subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
5) The ad-hoc promotions are being given without affecting the seniority of officers who are senior but whose cases are kept open for consideration.
Promoted officers appointed by ad hoc promotion are required to appear within seven days from the date of receipt of their orders of appointment. Similarly, if they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time, the government may consider them not to be appointed, according to the decision of the General Administration Department No. SRV-2015/ Q. No. 303/ Q. The appointments will be cancelled by their selection as per the provisions of 12/09/2016.
(5) The Head of the Office/ Head of the Department shall immediately relieve the officers appointed by ad-hoc promotion to join the new post by making local alternative arrangements and shall instruct the concerned to immediately appear at the place of new appointment. After taking charge, the concerned officers should submit their report to the government through the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik and the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune.
6. Promoted officers appointed by ad hoc promotion should join their new place of posting immediately. They should not go on leave illegally. their attention in this regard.
