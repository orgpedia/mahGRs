The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been approved by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has made available 10% of the budgetary allocation for the purpose of 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS). Accordingly, under the schemes being implemented by the School Education Department, the Government was considering to disburse the funds available to the School Education Department within the limit of 10% for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries).
Government Decision: Under the Tribal Component Programme for the year 2023-24, Demand No. 2202 General Education in T-5, the total out of the 10% limit of the provisions in the annual budget for salary as made available for disbursement by the Finance Department under the head of General Education is Rs. 1042.295 lakhs (Rupees Ten Crores Forty Two Lakhs Thirty Nineteen Thousand Five Hundred only) to the Principal Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department. million).
