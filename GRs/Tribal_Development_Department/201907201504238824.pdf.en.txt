Government Decision- Under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme" Read for the Financial Year 2019-20 No. 3 Under the Government decision here, accounting grant (33% provision) was disbursed to meet the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019. Now, in the Monsoon Session, 2019, the Legislature has approved the" Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2019" (including the amounts contributed). Under Section 4, the Finance Department has approved the allocation and expenditure of funds for 2019-20 within the limit of 60% of the total budgetary allocation. Accordingly, the remaining allocation is being disbursed and expenditure is being approved within the financial limit of 60%.
2. It is stated in the case that the funds budgeted under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation for the Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Amrut Aahar Yojana, in addition to the provision of this contribution, shall be transferred to the concerned No. 2 is listed here. It was distributed to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Scheme as per the Government decision dated 11.04.2019. Now, the Finance Department has stopped the facility of making available the funds of the District Annual Tribal Component Program from the government level to other controlling officers besides the Collector on the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) and has made this facility available on the Collector Account. Accordingly, the remaining funds for Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Amrit Aahar Scheme are being distributed to the respective District Collectors within the financial limit of 60%.
