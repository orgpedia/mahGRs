The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the state legislature. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, as made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System by the Department of Finance, funds to the extent of 15% for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) of the schemes under the head Backward Classes Welfare under the Tribal Component Programme have been released to the concerned Controlling Authorities vide No. 2 It is distributed according to the decisions of the government.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds amounting to 10% of the budgetary provisions for the purpose of 01-Salary, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the enhanced budgetary allocation for the purpose of 01- Salary, 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the schemes mentioned in Table No. 2 of the accompanying prospectus under T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending this fund, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the conditions.
