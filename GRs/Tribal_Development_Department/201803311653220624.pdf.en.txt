Proposal in respect of Finance Department No. 1 here. The GoM dated 02/04/2018 has approved the release of funds on Budget Estimates, Distribution and Monitoring System (BEAMS) for the first nine months of 2017-19 (ending December 2018) subject to a ceiling of 70% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, under Tribal Appropriation Demand No. The Government was contemplating to disburse 70% of the budgetary provision for the year 2018-19 to the Principal Secretary, School Education Department as Head of the Scheme Implementation Department under the head T-5, 2202-General Education, 02-Secondary Education, 796-Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01) Scheme under Tribal Area Appropriation, (01) Scheme under State Schemes, (01) Subsidiary Grants to General Secondary Schools (2202, 1901), (01) (04) Subsidiary Grants to Non-Government Junior Colleges (2202, 1948) and (01) (07) Assistance to Tribal Students in Military Schools (2202H973) 36-Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
Government's decision
As the Department of School Education is implementing the schemes of Auxiliary Grants to General Secondary Schools (Apex 22021901), Auxiliary Grants to Non-Government Junior Colleges (Apex 22021948) and Assistance to Tribal Batch of Students in Sainik Schools (Apex 2202H973) 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries), the Secretary, Department of School Education is being granted approval to disburse funds to the Department of School Education for the purpose of payment of salaries/ emoluments for the first nine months of the year 2018-19 within the limit of 70% of the budgetary provision as under:
