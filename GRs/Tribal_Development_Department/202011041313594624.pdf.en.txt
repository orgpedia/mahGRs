The Proposal:
Considering the situation arising out of the Corona virus in the state, in the financial year 2020-21 under the Tribal Development Department, in order to provide education to the students of the schools under the Tribal Development Department at home, the Government Ashramshala, Eklavya Residential Ashramshala, Aided Ashramshala and Named Schools have been asked to implement the alternative education initiative called Unlock Learning. The decision has been approved by the government. In this regard, the government was considering to disburse funds to the Commissioner, Tribal Development.
Government's decision:
The following funds are being sanctioned for Unlock Learning activities in Government Ashramshalas, Eklavya Residential Ashramshalas, Aided Ashramshalas and Nominated Schools under the Tribal Development Department.
