The Proposal:
Under the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 and Rules, 2008 and Amended Rules, 2012, the District Collector of the concerned district has been empowered to extend the term of office of a total of 69 managers in the state for the implementation of collective forest rights. In the 4. have been provided under the Government Decision dated 10.12.2020. However, it was under the consideration of the Government to provide extension of powers to the concerned office heads to the posts of Nodal Officer against a total of 69 managers, total 5 managers in the Forest Rights Law Office and 1 each in the offices of Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, Amravati, Thane and Nagpur.
Government's decision:
Under the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 and Rules, 2008 and Amended Rules, 2012, the concerned office heads are being empowered to extend the tenure of 5 posts of Nodal Officer for implementation of Community Forest Rights, 1 each in the Office of Forest Rights Law Office and the Office of the Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, Amravati, Thane and Nagpur.
