The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, in accordance with the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Department of Finance has made available 10% funds for the month of April on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system and 7% funds for the remaining items. Accordingly, under the mandatory expenditure plans of the Tribal Development Department, Chapter No. 10% of the budgetary provisions for salaries and other office matters. Under the consideration of the government, 7% of the budgetary provisions for 3- overtime allowance, 6- telephone, electricity and water charges, 10- contractual services, 11- travel expenses, 12- foreign travel, 13- office expenses, were to be disbursed to the concerned controlling officer.
Government's decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the budgetary provisions for the salary component and 7% for other office components as per Racket No. 5 for the schemes mentioned in Racket No. 2 in the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4 to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned persons should refer to the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book and the Finance Department's reference No. 1 Care should be taken to comply with the provisions and conditions set forth in the government circular here.
