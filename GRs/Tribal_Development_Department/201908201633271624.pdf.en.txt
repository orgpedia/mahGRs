The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Development Department, residential government ashramshalas are run by the government and residential aided ashramshalas are run by NGOs. The criteria for the posts of non-teaching staff for these government ashramshalas and aided ashramshalas run by NGOs are as under. The ashramshala code here has been fixed in 2019. Accordingly, the Government is issuing the following decision regarding the criteria for determining the eligibility criteria for non-teaching posts in Government Ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department and Aided Ashramshalas run by NGOs.
Government Decision: All the previous government decisions regarding fixing the figure for non-teaching staff in government ashramshalas and all aided ashramshalas run by NGOs under the Tribal Development Department in the state are being revised and the revised figure is being applied as follows:

(3) If any of the Ashramshalas is found to be surplus in terms of the above criteria, they shall be accommodated in other Ashramshalas of the same institution. If there are no vacancies in other ashramshalas of the same institution, they will be adjusted in other institutions in the respective Additional Commissioner's area. If there are no vacancies left for adjustment, the posts of non-teaching staff will continue to work in the same institution till the retirement of the said staff from that post and the additional posts will automatically lapse after the retirement of the said staff. No institution will be given approval for recruitment unless all the additional staff in the ashramshalas are adjusted. Before the recruitment, the Additional Commissioner/ Project Officer should get it certified by the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik.
For the posts of Assistant Superintendents/ Superintendents in the above mentioned No. 4 and No. 5, the pay scale is in the Grade Pay of Rs. The pay scale in the 7th Pay Commission Pay Matrix as per 1900 will be S-6 19900-63200. A Ph.D. or equivalent degree is required. The scholarship amount can be paid on the basis of the number of residential students in the academic session 2019-20 as well as the number of students in the last two years. If the prescribed number of students is regular for more than three consecutive years, their services can be regularized.
Additional new posts to be created as per the criteria in this Government decision will be sanctioned after the approval. The pay scale of Multi-Purpose Employees will be decided separately. The posts will be filled on a remuneration basis for the first three years. The posts will be given permanent recognition in the 4th year after taking into account the number of residential students for 3 consecutive years.
For the purpose of accreditation, the headmasters of all ashramshalas should fill the information on the online portal by the end of September 30 every year. The number of students at the end of September 30 each year should be taken into account for the assessment. Proposals should be sent to the government according to the number of students at the end of September 30. Approval from the government will be required. No additional posts will be filled up without the approval of the Authority.
