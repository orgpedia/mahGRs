The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, financial assistance is provided to eligible beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes for construction of pucca houses under Shabari Tribal Ghar Kul Yojana. A goal is set for each year and funding is allocated. In this regard, vide No. As proposed herein, the Government was considering to disburse the pending funds at the end of the year 2019-20 from the budgetary provision for the year 2020-21.
Government Decision: Under the Tribal Component Programme, in the financial year 2020-21, under the main head of Demand Number T-5, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, Shabari Tribal Ghar Kool Yojana, the funds pending at the end of the year 2019-20 are being approved to be disbursed and spent as follows with the approval of the Finance Department with 50% limit from the budgetary provision for the year 2020-21.
