The Proposal: -
As per the Government decision dated 11/01/2024 above, guidelines have been issued regarding implementation of Shabari Adivasi Gharakul Yojana in urban areas. Regarding the eligibility of beneficiaries in rural areas under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) in the state, the Housing Department has issued a notification at Reference No. The Government Decision dated 2nd 20/10/2018 provides that it is mandatory for the applicant to have land in his / her own name to avail the benefit of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (under Section 31), otherwise the Ajandar is not eligible to avail the benefit of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. For successful implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, beneficiaries in rural areas who do not own land should be eligible under the BLC component on the basis of property tax receipts for the last 3 years. Accordingly, the issue of notification regarding eligibility of beneficiaries in rural areas under Shabari Adivasi Ghar Kul Yojana Urban (for urban areas) was under consideration of the Government.
The Government's Decisions:
Reference No. The guidelines issued for implementation of Shabari Adivasi Ghar Kul Yojana in urban areas vide Government Decision dated 1 11/01/2024 herein lay down the criteria regarding eligibility of beneficiaries. Under the Housing Department, No. In line with the Government Decision dated 2nd 20/10/2018, this Government Decision is approving the implementation of Shabari Adivasi Ghar Kul Yojana in urban (Urban) areas to qualify beneficiaries in rural areas who do not own land on the basis of property tax receipts for the last 2 years.
