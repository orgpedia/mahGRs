GOVERNMENT DECISION- Special Collective Incentive Scheme for Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar (SC/ ST) Entrepreneurs under Tribal Appropriation Demand No. T-5, 2852, Industries, 80 General, 796-Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) (02) Special Group Incentive Scheme for ST Entrepreneurs (2852 A013) for 2018-19. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been allocated for the project. Accordingly, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour No. 2 In accordance with the proposal herein, for the above scheme (2852A013), under this head, Rs. 7.00 crore (Rs.7 crore only) to the Controller General of Industries, Additional Chief Secretary (Industries), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Government of Maharashtra on Budget Distribution System.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure. Also, instructions should be given to send the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Industries, Energy and Labour Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area as well as in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the sub-plan area.
