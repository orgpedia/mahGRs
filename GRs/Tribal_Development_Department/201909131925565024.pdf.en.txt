The Proposal:
The hostels sanctioned by the Tribal Development Department are mostly in the municipal and municipal corporation areas. in the Municipal/ Municipal area
The hostel building is constructed on the available government land. Provision to purchase private land where government land is not available for construction of hostel building. This was decided by the Government Decision of 31/08/2009. It takes about 4 years for the land to be directly in the name of the department and for the actual construction to be completed. During this period, the payment of the hostel rent is started. So, the funds are required to purchase private land, pay rent and construct the actual building and for this a period of 4 years is required. Also, in metropolitan areas like Thane, Pune, Nagpur, etc., private land is not available for government purposes at government rates or market prices because of the shortage of land and the business of urban housing is more effective. Therefore, the Government has approved the policy to purchase a ready-made private building for the Government Hostel for Tribal Boys and Girls under the Tribal Development Department as per the Government Decision No. 4 in Wacha of the Tribal Development Department, if the ready-made building for the hostel is available with the necessary requirements.
