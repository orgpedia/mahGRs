Preamble: The Department of Tribal Development had invited proposals from Non-Government/ Social/ Private Organizations for grant under Special Central Assistance for the year 2018-19, under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and under Adim Janjatiya Vikas Karyakram on email. Detailed presentations and reviews of the proposals received. On 22.03.2018, three panels were constituted for scrutiny of proposals at Sahyadri Guest House. A detailed presentation of the proposal has been made by NORID in front of the panel. Through this scheme, the project proposes to provide protein-rich food in the form of tasty fish at affordable prices in the tribal areas and at the same time to make the tribal people self-reliant in getting employment in this fisheries sector. The fish production project has been designed in collaboration with the government, which creates the water source, and the tribal people who consume it.

A variety of activities are included in the program. The scheme" NORID and Fishcop Fed- Fishery Cage, mono, poly culture (Nasik)@ 3 reservoirs" was proposed to the Government of India under the programme to design the project with a view to maximize fish production from the selected water bodies thereby providing protein-rich food and various employment opportunities for the tribal areas. Ministry of Tribal Affairs. The Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) in its meeting held on 23.05.2018 has approved the implementation of the scheme" NORID and Fishcop Fed Fishery cage, mono, poly culture (Nasik)@ 3 reservoirs" for the year 2018-19 with a total outlay of Rs. 118.20 Lakhs has been sanctioned under Reference 01 Letter and disbursed under Reference 02 Letter as 1st installment and 2nd installment of funds.
Reference No. 3 has been issued to Commissioner Tribal Development, Nashik.
NORID Nagpur is a partner organization of the National Federation of Fisheries Co-operatives (FISHCOPFED). NORID has been working in collaboration with FISHCOPFED to implement various development programmes in tribal and rural areas across the country in the areas of animal husbandry, forestry, agro-business, etc. It undertakes various activities such as identifying water sources, forest areas available for rural, tribal and fisher community in the area, developing skills for employment generation, providing technical and practical training for self-employment to tribal rural people in the area and making them self-reliant. NORID has provided training in self-employment and fish farming to tribal villagers in Thane rural forest area. NORID, a reputed organization with a long experience in this field, has received Rs. The matter of approving the guidelines of this scheme with a fund of Rs. 118.2 lakh is under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision In order to make available protein rich food in the form of tasty fish at affordable prices in the tribal areas and also to make the tribal people self-reliant for employment in this fisheries sector, the implementation guidelines for the scheme" NORID and Fishcop Fed- Fishery cage, mono, poly culture (Nasik)@ 3 reservoirs" jointly implemented by NORID and Tribal Development Department at a total cost of Rs. 118.20 lakh are being approved as per Annexure No.1 annexed through this decision.
