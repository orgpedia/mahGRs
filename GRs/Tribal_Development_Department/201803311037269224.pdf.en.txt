The proposal: Additional Chief Secretary to Chief Minister As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 27.11.2017, the Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur, under the administrative control of the Ministry of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, has been sanctioned for the upgradation of Radiotherapy Department and provision of state-of-the-art facilities for the treatment of cancer. T-6, 4210 Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health, 01-Civil Health Services, 796-Tribal Area Sub Plan, (00) (01)-Providing Modern Facilities for Cancer Treatment at Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur (4210 2607), 52-Machinery and Equipment. 400.00 lakh has been budgeted in the Winter Session 2017 through Supplementary Demands. Further, for the financial year 2017-18, Rs. 320.00 lakhs has been approved. The funds were to be distributed to the Medical Education and Drugs Department.
Government Decision: As proposed under the Tribal Component Programme under No. 3 of the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department, Demand No. T-6, 4210 Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health, 01-Civil Health Services, 796-Tribal Area Sub Plan, (00) (01)-Providing Modern Facilities for Cancer Treatment at Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur (4210 2607), 52-Machinery and Equipment. Government approval is being accorded to disburse a sum of Rs. 320.00 lakh (Rupees Three Crore Twenty Lakh only) to the Controller of the Scheme, Secretary, Medical Education and Drugs Department on Budget Distribution System.
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department should ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure.
