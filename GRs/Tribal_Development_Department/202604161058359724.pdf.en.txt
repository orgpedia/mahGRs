The Proposal: -
The Department of Finance (DoF) has issued a notification regarding the disbursement of funds for the financial year 2026-27. The instructions have been circulated through the government circular of 09.04.2026. According to the instructions in this circular, the budgeted funds for the financial year 2026-27 under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme for the first nine months (end-December, 2026) are to be allocated through the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System within a limit of 8%. / 8) to be distributed.
