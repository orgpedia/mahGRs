Preamble: The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY-G) for integrated, economic and social development of villages with Scheduled Tribe population based on the concept of model village. The state government has issued guidelines for the implementation of the scheme. Now, as per the reference 2 letter, the central government in the scheme guidelines has made a provision for setting up of" Village Level Convergence Committee" at the village level. Accordingly, the inclusion of village level convergence committee in the context government decision was under consideration of the state government.
Government's decision
The Government have approved the inclusion of the following points in the sub-topic of the implementation of the scheme and the activities at the level of implementation and related activities in the implementation of the scheme, in point number 4 of the Government Decision issued in reference 1 for the implementation of the 100 centrally sponsored scheme of Pradhan Mantri Adi Adarsh Gram Vikas.
Village Level Convergence Committee- At the village level to carry out the functions related to the scheme
A committee will be formed as follows:
1.Sarpanch- The President
All tribal members of the Gram Panchayat.
3. Anganwadi sevak/ sevika, ASHA sevika, ANM.
4. Mechanism relating to Panchayat Raj and Public Works.
5. Those working under NREGA scheme.

Even if the composition of the committee is as above, if the chairman of the committee is of the opinion that inclusion of members in the committee, exclusion of members is necessary for the implementation of the scheme, the chairman can take a decision as per the requirement. However, the transfer of a member of the tribal community will be done only with the inclusion of a member of the tribal community.
The committee will meet within three months or as required for smooth implementation of the scheme.
The committee will play a vital role in the survey of needs, collection of information, preparation and implementation of village development plans, assessment and control, etc.
