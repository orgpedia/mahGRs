Preamble to the proposal dated 20/11/2023: With a view to encourage/ recognize the work of individuals and social organizations doing remarkable work for the overall development of tribals in the State of Maharashtra, the State Government has been honouring their work by conferring the State Awards namely" Adivasi Sevak Puraskar and Adivasi Seva Sanstha Puraskar" to individuals and organizations respectively. The award for the year 2022-23 has not been given due to the Corona situation and some unavoidable reasons.
Read here. The meeting of the State Level Selection Committee for the Tribal Servants/ Institutions Award constituted as per the Government Decision dated 4. Done on 07/11/2023. As recommended by the Committee, the proposal was received by the Government from the Member Secretary of the State Level Selection Committee and the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik as per Article No. 6. In pursuance of the proposal, the Government was contemplating to confer the combined Adivasi Sevak/ Adivasi Seva Sanstha Awards for four years i.e. 2019-20, 2020-2021, 2021-22 and 2022-23.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to confer the combined" Adivasi Sevak and Adivasi Seva Sanstha" awards for four years- 2019-20, 2020-2021, 2021-22 and 2022-23. A total of 60 individuals have been selected for" Adivasi Sevak Puraskar" for four years from 2019-20 to 2022-23 and 8 institutions for" Adivasi Seva Sanstha Puraskar" for 2021-22 and 2022-23. By this decision of the Government, the names of the persons who have been conferred the award as tribal sevaks are being declared in the attached return' A' and the names of the tribal service institutions as shown in the return' B'.
The Adivasi Sevak Awardees and the Adivasi Seva Sanstha Awardees (1 or 2 representatives of the Sanstha) should be given permissible benefits as per the provisions of the Government Decision in Articles 2 and 3 at Vacha.
The names of the persons selected for the award of" Adivasi Sevak" and" Adivasi Seva Sanstha" shall be published in the Maharashtra Government Gazette (General Part).
