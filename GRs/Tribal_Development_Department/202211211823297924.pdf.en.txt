Preamble: Under the" Shabari Adivasi Gharkul Yojana,"" Pardhi Vikas Karyakram" and" Adim Janjati Vikas Karyakram" for the people belonging to Scheduled Tribes in rural and urban areas of Maharashtra, the government was considering to approve the logo of the houses to be built under the above scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The logo for the houses constructed under Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme, Pardhi Development Programme and Adim Janjati Development Programme has been finalized and the logo is as per Annexure-A attached. It will be mandatory to affix this symbol on the houses constructed under Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme, Pardhi Development Programme and Adim Janjati Development Programme.
2. It is being approved to bear the cost of installation of the logo from the administrative fund received by the State Management Cell-Rural Housing.
