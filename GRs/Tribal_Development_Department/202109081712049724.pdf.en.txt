2.A total of Rs. 1203.33 lakh (Rupees Twelve Crore Three Lakh Thirty Three Thousand only) is being disbursed to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System. In this case, the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer has to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook and the rules and regulations in the context of the Finance Department while spending the said provisions. The directions given in the Government Resolution dated 24.06.2021 should be strictly followed.
3. Report on expenditure incurred on account of provision made, information on material achievement of target in terms of head of accounts/ sub-head of accounts, utilization of funds, etc.
The information should be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month.
4. The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the sub-plan area.
5. From the provision made under the Tribal Component Programme, schemes for creation of infrastructure in the tribal sub-plan area as well as individual benefit schemes should be taken up and only individual benefit schemes should be taken up in the tribal sub-plan outlying areas.
