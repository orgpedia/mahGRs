The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month. Accordingly, under the programme expenditure in Tribal Component Programme, so far 65% of the funds for 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) targets, 42% of the funds for Office targets and 28% of the funds for other targets for some schemes have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system within the limit of 49% of the budgetary provisions for office matters and 35% for other matters. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse the remaining 7% of the increased funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities for office and other purposes of the schemes under the programme expenditure.
Government's decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Budget provisions under the schemes mentioned in the Annexure No. 2 to the accompanying statement under T-5.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
