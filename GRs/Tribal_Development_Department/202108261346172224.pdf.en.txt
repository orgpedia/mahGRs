The Integrated Agriculture Development Programme and allied (animal husbandry, fisheries, dairy development, etc.) are proposed to reduce the migration of tribal farmers who have received land pattas under the Forest Rights Act, 2006, by increasing their income through agricultural ancillary activities. The activities for IFR and CFR are being carried out under FRA 2006. The Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) in its meeting held on 08/02/2017 has approved and released funds under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India for the year 2016-17. This fund reference No. 1 has been distributed to the implementing agency.
