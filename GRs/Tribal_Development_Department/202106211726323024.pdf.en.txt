The Proposal:
It has been approved under the Government Decision No. 2 dated 31.03.2016 to reimburse the tuition fees and examination fees of the students belonging to Scheduled Tribes admitted in Government, Non-Government, Aided and Unaided Colleges/ Technological Universities and Government Universities on non-aided basis for the selected professional courses from the year 2015-16 as per the rates fixed by the Tuition Fee Committee, Authority or Council.
However, in pursuance of the Government Resolution dated 31.3.2016 issued by the Tribal Development Department regarding reimbursement of academic fees payable to students admitted to medical courses," however, the students admitted at the institution level on the seats remaining vacant after the completion of the said admission process shall not be benefitted from the scheme even if such admissions are approved by the Admission Control Committee. The scheme will also be applicable to students who have taken admission through the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) for diploma, undergraduate and postgraduate courses related to technical education. However, there is no mention of admission through the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) in connection with admission to the Health Sciences course.
