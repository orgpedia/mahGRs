Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Vikas Kendra is a scheme implemented by the Central Government. The main objective of this scheme is to collect minor forest produce from the members of the self-help groups in the Scheduled Areas and other areas of Maharashtra mentioned in the Fifth Schedule to the Constitution of India, to sell the collected minor forest produce by adding value to it. The Tribal Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (TRIFED), Government of India has issued Reference No. As per the guidelines received under Section 1, the State Government has issued separate guidelines vide Reference No. 5 decisions have been made. Government Decision: The main objective of this scheme is to use the traditional knowledge, skills and modern information technology of the tribals on the minor forest produce produced in the forest, to process the minor forest produce and sell it by adding value to it, thereby improving the living standards of the tribals.
Notification No. 19/17/2019 of the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Govt. of India. The scheme Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Vikas Kendra has been implemented by the Central Government under 26/02/2019. Accordingly, approval is being given to implement the Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Vikas Yojana in the State for the sale of Minor Forest Produce (MFP) and other items of value addition with the help of Self Help Groups (SHGs) in the State, which have at least 70% members of the Scheduled Tribes.
Sabari Finance and Development Corporation Limited will be the State Agent Institute (SIP) for the implementation of the scheme. Nashik has been approved.
The Tribal Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (TRIFED), New Delhi has set a target of setting up 220 Van Dhan Vikas Kendras in Maharashtra with the help of self-help groups of Scheduled Tribes in the scheduled areas to sell value addition on minor forest produce and other items available in their area.
Tribal Development Department, Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Mumbai will be the nodal department for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Vikas Kendra (PMVK) Group of Schemes. Sabari Finance and Development Corporation Ltd. , Nashik will act as State Implementing Agency.
The guidelines on how to set up a Van Dhan Vikas Kendra Group are available on the website https:// trifed. tribal. gov. in/ pmvdy/ guideline of Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation of India Limited, New Delhi.

Self-help groups (SHGs) should consist of a maximum of 20 beneficiaries to form 1 Van Dhan Bachat Group (SHG) in which at least 80% of the beneficiaries should be from Scheduled Tribes.
