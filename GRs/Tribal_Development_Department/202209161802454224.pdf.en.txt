Preamble: Scheme for imparting education to the students of Scheduled Tribes in the English medium reputed residential schools in the city. It is being implemented in the State as per the Government decision dated 28.08.2009. Further, in order to facilitate the academic progress of the ST students admitted under the scheme along with other students studying in the nominated schools selected under the scheme, it has been decided to grant admission in the initial phase of Class 1 and Class 2 from the academic year 2017-18. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 29.5.2017. In order to streamline the implementation of this scheme, the schools should be selected on the basis of the score obtained by determining the educational and residential facilities available in the school and its quality and to determine the fees to be paid to the schools. 18.05.2018 and Reference No.4 herein. The revised policy has been decided by the Government Corrigendum dated 09.06.2018.

Government's decision:
A meeting of the Steering Committee was held on 07.09.2022 regarding selection of schools for the academic year 2022-23 under the Nominated Residential Schools Scheme.
As per the discussion in the meeting, the schools which were earlier selected under the scheme under the jurisdiction of the Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Thane, are being scored according to the prevailing criteria and a decision is being taken to retain these nominated schools for the year 2022-23.
