Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves the" Vocational Training Fee Reimbursement" Scheme to provide the benefit of reimbursement of Vocational Training Fee to the Scheduled Tribes students who have taken admission in Government Industrial Training Institutes (Public Private Partnership) under PPP Scheme as well as in Private Industrial Training Institutes through Central Online Mode from the admission session 2019-20.
The Business Training Fee Reimbursement Scheme will be as follows: 1. The seats available for admission to Government and PPP courses in Industrial Training Institutes and for students admitted to courses in Private Industrial Training Institutes will be paid by the Department of Higher and Technical Education. The training fee has been fixed as per the Government decision dated 14.05.2015. Accordingly, reimbursement of 100% of the training fee for students belonging to Scheduled Tribes admitted to courses in Private Industrial Training Institutes and seats available for admission under PPP scheme through Central Online System will be admissible to the students belonging to Scheduled Tribes.
The amount of reimbursement of business training fee of the students will be deposited in the bank account of the Institute Management Committee and Private Industrial Training Institute set up under the PPP scheme in the Government Industrial Training Institute through the MahaDBT portal.
