Preamble: Vocational training centres attached to some Government Ashramshalas are being run under the Tribal Development Department. The proposal of the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik was received by the Government to provide funds for the remuneration of the employees working in some of these training centers in the year 2022-23 and 2023-24. As per the proposal, the matter of disbursement of funds to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik for payment of honorarium for the period of service of 8 employees of Rupapeth under Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Chandrapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. Establishment of Special Education and Guidance Centre under T-5, Head Head Head 2230, Labour and Employment (01) (01) Under this scheme, the following funds are being sanctioned for payment of honorarium for the period 2022-23 and 2023-24 to the 8 employees of Vocational Training Centres at Rupapeth attached to the Government Ashram under the Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Chandrapur.
