Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No.
1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only) are available for distribution. 151 villages in 86 Gram Sabhas in Kalwan Panchayat Samiti area under Nashik ZP, 213 villages in 73 Gram Sabhas in Peth Panchayat Samiti area, 138 villages in 103 Gram Sabhas in Dindori Panchayat Samiti area, 66 villages in 63 Gram Sabhas in Igatpuri Panchayat area, 47 villages in 32 Gram Sabhas in Nashik Panchayat area, 124 villages in 84 Gram Sabhas in Trimbakeshwar Panchayat area, 190 villages in 61 Gram Sabhas in Surgana Panchayat area, 75 villages in 50 Gram Sabhas in Baglan Panchayat area, 41 villages in 22 Gram Sabhas in Devla Panchayat area. 424.2108/ Rs. 39,10,34, 109.61/- (Rs. Forty-nine crore, ten lakh, thirty-four thousand, one hundred and nine sixty-one paise only) is being made available directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the attached Annexure Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it.
