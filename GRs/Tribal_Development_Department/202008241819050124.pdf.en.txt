For the financial year 2020-21, for the Centrally Sponsored" Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin)" programme, the demand under Tribal Appropriation No. T-5, 2215 Water Supply and Sanitation, Tribal Area Sub-Plan, Non-Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (02) Sewerage and Sanitation, 796, Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) (03) Construction of Individual Sanitary House (Central) (Programme) under Swachh Bharat Mission, (2215 9862) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-MoS), under the head Rs. 16132.00 lakh has been budgeted.
2. Central Government Read No. Under the above scheme, Rs. 2797.53 lakh has been provided. The Water Supply and Sanitation Department, No. 4 Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Programme (22159862), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment), as proposed on the Annexure, for the financial year 2020-21 under this head, an amount of Rs. 2797.53 lakh (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores Ninety Seven Lakhs Fifty Three Thousand only) to the Additional Chief Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department as Head of the Administrative Department.
The concerned Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made available, fund utilization certificate, physical targets achieved account-wise/ sub-account-wise, etc. information should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and Water Supply and Sanitation Department by the 10th of every month.
