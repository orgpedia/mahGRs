Preamble: Proposal to revise the works to be taken up under the scheme, the implementation mechanism/ procedures and other criteria by approving the works to be suggested for the development of tribal sub-plan areas, additional tribal sub-plan areas, mada, mini mada and tribal sub-plan outlying areas (including rural and urban areas) of the State. on 2/11/2022 Ma. After discussing the proposal, the Cabinet decided to form a Cabinet Sub-Committee to consider the proposal of the Department to improve the works, implementation mechanism/ procedures and other criteria to be taken under the scheme by approving the works to be taken up under the scheme from the government level. " Accordingly, the matter of forming a cabinet sub-committee was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under the scheme of' Thakkar Bappa Tribal Habitat Improvement," the proposal of the Tribal Development Department to improve the works, implementation mechanism/ procedures and other criteria to be taken under the scheme by approving the works to be done at the government level. The Council of Ministers. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 2/11/2022, the Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted as follows:
