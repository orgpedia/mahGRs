Introduction: The Katkari, Madia Gond and Kolam tribes of the state have been notified as Particularly Vulnerable Tribes by the Central Government. The Tribal Development Scheme is implemented under the State Plan for the development of these tribes. Primitive Housing Scheme is being implemented to solve the problem of accommodation of these tribes as it has been found that they are migrating mainly due to the problem of accommodation. The scheme is being implemented through State Management Cell-Rural Housing since 2016-17. The target for housing was 7006 during 2016-17 to 2019-20. To achieve the target of Rs. 8817.66 lakh was required. However, Rs. 7399.32 lakh has been disbursed to the State Management Cell and Rs. A sum of Rs. 1418.34 lakh is pending for disbursement. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse this fund within the limit of funds available from the budgetary provision for 2020-21.
