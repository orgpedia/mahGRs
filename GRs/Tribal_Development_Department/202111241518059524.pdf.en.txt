The Proposal:
Students from class 1 to 12 are eligible to apply. Also, the students of Scheduled Tribes are provided assistance for higher education in government hostels and under the Swayam scheme. A total of 497 government ashramshalas are functioning under the Tribal Development Department for the educational development of tribal students in the state. The contribution of these ashramshalas in the overall development of the tribals is remarkable. To meet the changing challenges of modern times, these schools need to be radically transformed and the physical, intellectual and mental development of the students through education. Accordingly, the government was considering the development of 121' Adarsh Ashramshalas" out of the said government ashramshalas.
Government Decision: In the first phase of the total 497 government ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department, the schools in the adjoining Schedule A are being declared as" Model Ashramshalas." In order to develop the existing schools as Model Ashramshalas, the following important aspects/ components are being incorporated in each Model Ashramshala.
(a) Physical Facilities: (1) Every model ashramshala should have a well-equipped school building. It should have ICT lab, science lab, library, music and art room and adequate toilets.
(ii) There should be a separate and well-equipped hostel building for residential boys and girls.
(iii) Staff quarters for teaching and non-teaching staff. (iv) Each Adarsh Ashramshala should have well-equipped dining hall and multi-purpose hall.
(v) There should be a separate toilet with adequate facilities for students with disabilities.
(vi) Every model ashram should have clean and adequate drinking water, well-maintained classrooms, attractive buildings, playgrounds and adequate sports equipment.
(vii) Each Adarsh Ashramshala premises should have a separate Seek Room with all amenities.
It should also include physical facilities such as solar inverters, adequate space for washing and drying clothes, protective walls and adequate CCTV cameras.

(viii) The up-gradation should include facilities such as digital classrooms, virtual classrooms, lat labs, computer labs, etc.
(b) Educational Facilities: (i) Adarsh Ashramshalas should consciously inculcate 21st century skills such as creative thinking, critical thinking, scientific temperament, upholding constitutional values, collaboration, communication skills, etc.
(ii) To create a conducive learning environment in every Adarsh Ashramshala for the students to learn.
(ii) In the library of the Ashram, supplementary reading books and reference books of various subjects as well as encyclopaedias should be made available.
(iv) Educational programmes should be conducted in a structured manner such as group study along with self-study.
(v) Try to harmonize both the mother tongue and English of the students.
(vi) Activity based learning should be imparted to enhance the knowledge of the students.
(vii) Students should be given constructive enjoyment education so that they can learn as much as possible in the shortest possible time.
(vii) To develop leadership qualities in students to face various challenges in the present competitive era, various activities like study, speech, writing, acting, singing and other school activities should be conducted.
(ix) Teachers should go beyond bookish knowledge and give priority to providing facilities like tabs, virtual classrooms, e-learning etc.
There shall be a steering committee under the chairmanship of the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, to determine the curriculum and procedures in connection with the provision of physical and educational facilities as above. The composition of the Steering Committee shall be as follows:
