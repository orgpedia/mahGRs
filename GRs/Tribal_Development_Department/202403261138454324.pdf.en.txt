Pradhan Mantri Janjatiya Adivasi Nyaya Maha Abhiyan (PM JANMAN) has been implemented on 15th November, 2023. For this, the Central Government has issued guidelines of the scheme on 2nd January 2024.
The scheme aims at the holistic development of the tribal community by covering 11 priority areas essential for raising the standard of living through the respective 9 Ministries. Multi-purpose centres and Van Dhan Vikas Kendras are among the 11 priority areas announced by the central government and are being implemented directly by the Tribal Development Department. The government was considering approving a new accounting title for the implementation of the scheme.
