Preamble: The Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006, Rules, 2008 and Amendments Rules, 2012 are being strictly implemented to implement the Vanamitra Scheme for time-bound settlement of forest rights claims and appeals. 2 The instructions have been issued by the government circular here. Under this, the forest area in the individual forest rights claims approved by the district level forest rights committee is being processed by the Land Records Department, for which the necessary funds are made available to the concerned collector as a" nodal agency" through the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune. In this regard, the Commissioner, Tribal and Research and Training Institute, Pune, has issued instructions to provide funds to the concerned District Collectors for the purpose of enumeration of forest lands in forest rights claims by the Land Records Offices. Proposal 4 has been submitted. The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
