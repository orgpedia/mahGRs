Government's decision
The Finance Department vide Circular dated 16.04.2020 has approved the release of funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budgetary allocation of essential and programme expenditure for the first 3 months of the financial year 2020-21 (ending June, 2020), considering the pandemic situation in the State.
Accordingly, No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 21.04.2020, 10% of the total budgetary allocation for the specific objectives mentioned in the Government Circular under the Finance Department for the mandatory expenditure schemes in Demand No. T-1, T-2 and T-4 under the Tribal Component Programme will be provided to the beneficiaries. As per the Government decision dated 8.05.2020, 10% of the funds for 01-Salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) of Compulsory Expenditure Schemes have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. At present, the Department of Finance has made available funds for disbursement of 01-Salary and 36-Support Grants (Salaries) subject to a ceiling of 25% of the total budgetary allocation. In view of this, the Government is approving the release and expenditure of the remaining 5% of the available funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities for the distribution of 01-Wage Objectives for the mandatory expenditure schemes in Demand No. T-2 and T-4 mentioned in the accompanying statement.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious for some time to come due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan the precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds. This fund should be used only for regular salary payments and not for payment of arrears.
