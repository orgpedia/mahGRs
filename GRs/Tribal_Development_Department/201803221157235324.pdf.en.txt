The Proposal:
Under the District Tribal Component Programme, funds were transferred to Akola District from the funds of Thakkar Bappa Habitat Improvement Scheme under Amravati District for the year 2017-18 for rehabilitation of people displaced by Melghat Tiger Reserve in Amravati District. With this, the Amravati district has increased its allocation for various development works under the Rural Social Transformation Mission (VSTM) programme. A sum of Rs. 3.00 crore had been sanctioned for the project. As per the demand, Rs. 3.00 crore was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the District Annual Tribal Component Programme, the Government has approved the release of Rs. 3.00 crore for Amravati district, which was sanctioned in the Winter Session, 2017. In the case of Account Head (31) (31) 2225 E356-Sub-Aid to District Amaravati, the amount of Rs. 3.00 crore is being made available to District Collector, District Amaravati as Controlling Officer on Budget Distribution System.
