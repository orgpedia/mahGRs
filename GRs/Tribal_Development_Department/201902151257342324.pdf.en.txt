Preamble- To improve the physical and basic facilities in government ashramshalas, follow-up is being done through various means. A special drive is being carried out for this. But these facilities need to be made available on a regular basis.
In some of these ashramshalas, these facilities did not seem to be permanently available. Accordingly, the officers/ employees of various agencies under the Tribal Development Department visited the government ashramshalas and hostels and inquired about the facilities there, cleanliness, food, problems of students, teachers and non-teaching staff etc. in the ashramshalas, reviewed the basic facilities in the ashramshalas, discussed with the school management to solve the problems in the ashramshalas and to prepare a plan to take measures and control its implementation. Ashramshala transformation team was formed under circulars 1 and 2. The teams He had visited the government ashram and hostel from November 26, 2018 to December 5, 2018. During this visit, the government was considering to direct the teams to visit the ashramshalas and hostels again to follow the instructions given by these teams.
Government Circular: 1) The Tribal Development Department (Ministry itself), Tribal Commissionerate/ Tribal Development Corporation/ Shabari Tribal Finance and Development Corporation, Additional Commissioner, Tribal Construction Cell and Integrated Tribal Development Project Officer/ Staff of the Ashramshala Transformation Team to review the various facilities in the Government Ashramshala, prepare a plan for its repair and basic growth and control its implementation.
