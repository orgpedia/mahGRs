The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 8.02.2023, for the schemes under Compulsory and Programme Expenditure, the limit of fund disbursement has been prescribed for the period up to the time of finalization of Revised Estimates for the year 2022-23. The increased funds have also been made available for disbursement on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. In this regard, the Government was considering the issue of disbursement of funds available for the remaining disbursement to the concerned Controlling Authorities, taking into account the funds disbursed under the Government decisions at the reference No. 2 for the schemes of mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department, the revised estimates received from the field offices and the proposed revised estimates from the Government level.
Government's decision:
The demand for schemes under" Compulsory Expenditure" of Tribal Development Department No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Rakaanah No. 5 for the schemes mentioned in Rakaanah No. 2 in the accompanying returns under T-2, T-4 and T-8.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
