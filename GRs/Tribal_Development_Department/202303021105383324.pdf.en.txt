Introduction: Under the Government decision of serial number 1, Bhumika, it was decided to provide land for tilling under the Swabhiman Yojana to empower the tribals living below the poverty line and to provide sources of income to landless families living below the poverty line. 50 per cent of the cost of the land was to be borne by the government as interest free loan and 50 per cent as subsidy.
The scheme was implemented from 2004 to 2016.
The government was considering providing district-wise head of accounts for the collection of loans disbursed from the government, as notified by the Accountant General's Office.
Government's decision
This Government decision approves creation of 36 new district wise separate head accounts as mentioned in the attached statement' A' for collection of interest free loans disbursed by the Government under Swabhiman Yojana and for empowerment of tribals living below the poverty line.
The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry vide reference No. 3/ Finance-19, being issued pursuant to the approval received as on 01.02.2023.
