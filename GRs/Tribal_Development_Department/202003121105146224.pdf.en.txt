Government Corrigendum: Reference No. 01 here. Terms and Conditions for eligibility of benefit under Tuition Fee Compensation Scheme in Government Resolution dated 31.03.2016 under the heading' Conditions No. " The concerned educational institutions implementing professional courses eligible under the scheme will be required to obtain assessment and accreditation from the competent apex body within the next 3 years i. e. by the academic year 2018-19. Thereafter, from the academic year 2019-20 onwards, if the institutions do not receive assessment and rating from the respective apex institutions, such institutions will be excluded from the scheme till they receive assessment and rating and from that year onwards, the scheme will be applicable only for the students who are admitted for the first year in the institution receiving rating.
In lieu of this
" The concerned educational institutions (NAAC, NBA, etc.) implementing eligible courses under the scheme, after being eligible for assessment/ re-assessment, shall be eligible to apply for obtaining such assessment and quality rating from the competent apex body/ body/ board by the end of the academic year 2019-20," it said.
Apart from this, all the terms and conditions of the scheme will remain the same.
