the prelude
The circular dated 16.04.2020 of the Finance Department has prescribed the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21, considering the situation arising out of Corona Virus in the State. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, as provided from time to time on the Budget Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 3 Under the Tribal Component Programme under the Government decisions herein, 60% of the budgetary allocation for the purpose of District Annual Tribal Appropriation is provided in the Budget Estimates. 30% of the budgetary provision for the purpose of telephone, electricity and water charges, 13% of office expenses and 14% of rent and taxes and the provision for item No. 20% of the budgetary allocation for 02-Mazuri, 10-Contractual Services, 24 Petrol, Oil and Lubricants and 10% of the allocation for other purposes has been distributed to the concerned Controlling Authorities. Out of this, funds were released only for" Bharat Ratna Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Amrit Aahar Yojana" under the target of 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) with a ceiling of 25%. Now, as per the approval given by the Finance Department to make available funds for distribution within the limit of 25 percent of the total budgetary allocation under the District Annual Tribal Component Program, the remaining funds within this limit were under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation Scheme, the Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of the remaining funds to the concerned Controlling Officer as per column-6 of the accompanying statement, after deducting the funds released earlier within the limit of 25% of the budgetary allocation for the sub-schemes/ objectives in Demand No. T-9, T-10 and T-11.
50% of the funds to be provided under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation Fund will be used for COVID-19 response and strengthening of district level health infrastructure of the Public Health Department and Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department, as required.
