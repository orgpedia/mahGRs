The Proposal:
The Department of Agriculture and Padum has decided to allocate funds from the budgetary provisions for the year 2022-23 for the SWAYAM project being implemented by the Department of Agriculture and Padum in 16 tribal districts of the state for providing eggs in the diet of children in Anganwadis in rural Parsa and Scheduled Areas/ Tribal Sub Plan areas and for creating self-employment. 2 has been proposed. In pursuance of this proposal, the matter of disbursement of funds within the limit available for disbursement was under consideration of the Government.
