The Central Government has provided special central assistance to Organizing FPOs of 15000 farmers for processing, distribution, branding and retailing during 2018-19 and 2019-20 and a total amount of Rs. 8760.00 lakh has been sanctioned.
At present, tribal farmers in the state sell their agricultural produce to traders or process industries individually. For this, tribal farmers can be benefited by getting better price/ price if they produce, process and sell these farm products with the help of collective development centers on cooperative basis.
4. Under consideration of the Government, it was proposed to approve the guidelines of the scheme' Organizing FPOs of 15000 farmers for processing, distribution, branding and retailing" to help the tribal farmers in processing and packaging etc. and to sell their collected farm products at a better price by forming a farmers' cooperative society/ association of agricultural producers/ collective development center by mobilizing the tribal farmers through Shabari Tribal Development Corporation, Nashik.
Government's decision:
The Central Government has provided special central assistance and grant under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India for the financial year 2018-19 and 2019-20 for the implementation of the scheme Organizing FPOs of 15000 farmers for processing, distribution, branding and retailing. 8760.00 lakh has been sanctioned.
