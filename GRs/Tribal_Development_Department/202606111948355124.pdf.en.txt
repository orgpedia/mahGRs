The Proposal: -
The government started the "Ashramshala Group" scheme from the year 1972-73 with the aim of spreading education among the tribal community in the hilly and remote areas of the state of Maharashtra. Under this scheme, Ashramshala is an important focal point for the educational development of the tribal area. A total of 500 government ashramshalas are functioning under the tribal division. More than 2 lakh students are studying in these government ashramshalas. Government ashramshalas are located in rural and remote areas. In order to bring tribal students from these rural and remote areas into the mainstream of the society, they have been promoted to 17 primary ashramshalas under the department with the objective of providing them access to secondary and higher secondary education. 8th to 10th standard) and 48 Secondary Ashramshalas were upgraded to Higher Secondary (e.g. 11th and 12th) and creation of necessary posts for the same was under consideration of the Government.
