4. Promoted officers appointed by way of temporary promotion are required to join immediately from the date of issue of their appointment orders. (5) The Head of the Office/ Head of the Department shall immediately relieve the officers appointed by temporary promotion to join the new post by making local alternative arrangements and shall instruct the concerned to immediately appear at the place of new appointment.
