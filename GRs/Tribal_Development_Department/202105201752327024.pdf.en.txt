Read:- Tribal Development Department, Government Resolution No. STC-2119/ PR No. 34/ Ka. The date is September 13, 2019.
The proposal: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 3rd September, 2019 had approved the constitution of seven new ST Certificate Scrutiny Committees at Palghar, Nashik 2, Dhule, Kinwat, Nanded, Yavatmal, Gadchiroli and Chandrapur in addition to the existing 8 ST Certificate Scrutiny Committees.
Accordingly, the orders have been issued under the Government Order dated 13th September, 2019. Accordingly, out of the seven new committees to be formed, the change in the headquarters/ name of Dhule, Chandrapur and Gondia committees was under the consideration of the government.
Circular: The Government has decided to change the names of the Scheduled Tribes Certificate Examination Committees to be set up in Dhule, Chandrapur and Gondia to Nandurbar-2, Gadchiroli-2 and Nagpur-2 respectively and to have their headquarters at Nandurbar, Gadchiroli and Nagpur respectively.
