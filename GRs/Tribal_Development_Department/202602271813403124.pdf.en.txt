Introduction: - Other Backward Classes through Bahujan Welfare Department. A meeting was held under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister on 04.11.2025 to discuss various issues of the Banjara community and the minutes of the meeting have been issued by the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department under a letter dated 11.12.2025.
As per the decision taken in the meeting, a report has been submitted to the Hon'ble Chief Minister to study and examine the demand for inclusion of the community in the Scheduled Tribes along with the sub-category of the Banjara category. The government was considering setting up a committee headed by a retired high court judge.
Government Decision: - To study and submit a report with a view to examine the demand for inclusion of this community in the Scheduled Tribes along with the sub-category of Banjara category. A 6-member committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of a retired High Court Judge as under:
