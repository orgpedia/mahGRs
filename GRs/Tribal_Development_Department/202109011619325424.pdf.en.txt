The Proposal:
Finance in connection with the handling of banking transactions of funds disbursed under the schemes of the Department
Instructions are given from time to time by the department. Also, instructions have been issued by the Finance Department to adopt some improved procedures related to the new PFMS system. Accordingly, all the sectoral agencies under the Tribal Development Department shall regulate the compliance of the said instructions, as well as all the banking transactions under the Department.
The government was contemplating setting up of a State-level monitoring committee.
The government's decision:
In order to maintain uniformity in all banking transactions under various zonal offices of the Tribal Development Department, the Government is approving the constitution of a State Level Controlling Committee as follows. The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:

The Committee will consider the methodology being used by the Tribal Development Department and the Subordinate Regional Offices for disbursement of funds under various schemes and make recommendations in accordance with the instructions issued by the Finance Department from time to time for the effective and expeditious implementation of the schemes of the Department, for the availability of computer technology based systems/ services by the banks and for the uniformity in handling of banking transactions at all the zonal levels of the Department.
