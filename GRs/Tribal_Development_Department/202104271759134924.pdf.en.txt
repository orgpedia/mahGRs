In order to provide their benefits directly to the needy tribals by implementing the contextual No. The central budget/ nucleus budget scheme is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the government here.
At present, the corona virus is spreading rapidly in the country. Considering the limited sources of income in the tribal areas, the expenditure incurred for the Remdesivir injection required for the patient belonging to the Scheduled Tribes admitted to a private hospital due to Corona disease should be made available from the Nucleus Budget Scheme under the Tribal Component Programme. The decision has been approved by the government. In this regard, the government was considering to allocate the necessary funds for the expenses incurred for Remdesivir injection.
Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, during the financial year 2021-22, the Government has approved the release of Rs. 3.00 crore (Rupees Three Crores only) to the Controller General and Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik from the budgetary provision under Demand No. T-5 (01) (34)-Tribal Sub Plan Area (2225 1704), 50-Other Expenditure.
2. Subject to this Fund No. As per the Government decision dated 20th April, 2021, the amount is being disbursed to meet the expenses incurred for the required Remdesivir injection to a Scheduled Tribe patient admitted in a private hospital for Corona treatment.
