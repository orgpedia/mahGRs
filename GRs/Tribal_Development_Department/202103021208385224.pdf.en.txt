The creation of PVTG Cell for Targeted Intervention has been proposed by the Tribal Development Department with the main objective of providing trained manpower through the scheme to carry out the programmes launched for the primitive tribes, regular review of the schemes, ensuring the implementation of the schemes and programmes as planned, resolving the bottlenecks in their implementation, monitoring and evaluation, research on new schemes and other such works.
