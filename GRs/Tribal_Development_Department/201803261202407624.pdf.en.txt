The Proposal:
A total of 288 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune under the Tribal Development Department and in the office of the 8 Scheduled Tribes Certification Scrutiny Committee were extended from January 1, 2018 to February 28, 2018 as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3. On the expiry of this period, the government was considering to extend the tenure of 288 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune and 8 Scheduled Tribe Certificate Scrutiny Committee offices by exercising the authority received by the administrative department under reference number 1 and 2 of the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
A total of 288 temporary posts mentioned in the table below in the office of the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune and in the office of the 8 Scheduled Tribes Certificate Scrutiny Committee are being extended from March 1, 2018 to September 30, 2018 under this Government decision.
