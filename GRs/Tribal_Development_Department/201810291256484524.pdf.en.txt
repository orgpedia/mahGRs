Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, for the financial year 2018-19, Demand No. T-5, 2501, Special Programme for Rural Development, 01, Integrated Rural Development Programme, 796, Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) (06) Financial Assistance (Programme) for Special Skill Development Project under Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission, (Central Share), (2501 2953), 33-Financial Assistance. 2268.00 lakhs and for matching State share (01) (05) Financial assistance on Special Project for Skill Development under Aajeevika (Maharashtra State Rural Livelihoods Mission) (State Share) (Programme), (2501 2935), under the head 33-Finance Assistance Rs. 1075.00 lakhs has been budgeted.
Rural Development Department No. 3 In pursuance of the proposal made hereunder, under the National Rural Livelihood Mission of the Central Government, the Deendayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (Central Share), (2501 2953), under the head 33-Finance Assistance, an amount of Rs. 1118.67 lakhs and for the matching State share (2501 2935), under the head 33-Finance Assistance, Rs. 745.78 lakh is being sanctioned to Secretary, Rural Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Rural Development Department by the 10th of every month.
