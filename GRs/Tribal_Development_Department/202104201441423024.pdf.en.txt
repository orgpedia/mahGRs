Introduction: The pass percentage of Scheduled Tribe students in the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) examination in the state of Maharashtra is very low compared to other social groups. Students live in remote areas. Their financial condition is poor. In such a situation, they are educated. But due to lack of proper training, they are not able to qualify in the examinations conducted for the Indian Administrative Service. Also, various tribal representatives/ organizations in the state are demanding that arrangements should be made for training to provide pre-examination training to tribal students for the examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission. In this regard, the government is considering providing pre-examination training to the meritorious candidates of the Scheduled Tribes for the Civil Services Examination of the Union Public Service Commission in a reputed private training institute in Delhi or Maharashtra on the lines of BARTI, Pune.
Government Decision: The percentage of candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribes in the State appearing in competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is low. The number of candidates belonging to the Scheduled Tribes is also less. Therefore, to bring them into the mainstream of development, the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has decided to provide training to these candidates through reputed private professional institutions for the entire preparation (pre and main examination, interview) of the Civil Services Examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) to enable them to become administrative officers after passing the Civil Services Competitive Examination.

Selection Process: The candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribes who are domiciled in the State of Maharashtra and who wish to undergo training in the said Training Institute and fulfill all the general qualifications mentioned above will be invited to apply. For this, advertisement will be given in important dailies of the widest reach and highest consumption through the Tribal Research and Training Institute. If the number of candidates is more than 100, the question paper is prepared on the lines of the Civil Services Examination of the Union Public Service Commission.
The merit list and waiting list will be published at the tribal research and training institute level after conducting the entrance test. The first 100 trainees will be selected under this scheme. The trainees can take advantage of this scheme only once.
If more than 100 candidates are received to take advantage of this scheme, then the applications of the candidates under this scheme will be invited by conducting an e-tender process from various institutions conducting online/ offline entrance exams by advertising in state-level dailies through Tribal Research and Training or it will be planned by Tribal Research and Training Institute.
4) All the candidates are eligible for:
1) Candidate must be a graduate in any discipline with the above mentioned qualifications, education, age.
All other requirements and qualifications must be met.
Candidate should be a resident/ domicile of Maharashtra State for at least 5 years.
The candidate's caste must be from the list of Scheduled Tribes of Maharashtra State.
4. At the time of admission to the training, the candidate must have a valid certificate of Scheduled Tribe.
5.The candidates who are in government service will not be able to take advantage of this training scheme.
Incentive financial assistance for preparation of the Union Public Service Commission Mains Examination and Interview in the same year as per the Government Decision No. 75/ 09, dated 13th November, 2020 cannot be taken advantage of this scheme.
Preference will be given to candidates belonging to the Scheduled Tribes (PVTG).
Seats will be reserved for girls and the disabled as per rules. However, in case of non-availability of girls and differently-abled eligible candidates in the reserved seats, the seat will be given to the eligible candidate in the order of merit from the Scheduled Tribes.

5) Facilities and Conditions provided to the trainees:
1.Every trainee undergoing training at a reputed training institute in Delhi will be paid a stipend of Rs. Rs. 12,000.
2.Each trainee will be paid a stipend of Rs. 8,000/- will be provided.
Each trainee will be given a one-time grant of Rs.14,000/-for purchase of books.
4. For commencing training at Delhi centre and for return journey after completion of training, Rs. 2000 will be transferred to the bank accounts of the trainees as travel allowance and travel expenses.
Attendance of the trainees is mandatory at least 75% for this scholarship. The amount of scholarship will be transferred through DBT to the bank accounts of the trainees only after receiving the attendance sheet of each month from the concerned training institute. If any trainee fails to attend the training or if the attendance is less than 75 per cent, the trainee will be disqualified if he/ she puts pressure on the Tribal Research and Training Institute for scholarship.
If the candidate wants to leave the training for any reason at any stage, then the candidate will have to return the cost incurred by the institute for training during the training to the Tribal Research and Training Institute.
7.If it is found that the student has taken advantage of the scheme by submitting false documents
Legal action will be taken against them by recovering the entire training cost.
If the candidate does not avail the accommodation provided by the Institute, he/ she is required to inform/ provide the address of the accommodation and the warranty letter to this office as well as to the training institute.
Candidates are responsible for the safety and security of all their results.
75% attendance is required for training in the said training program but if the student is absent from training due to serious illness, accident, attending other competitive exams (due to natural calamity/ family problem/ medical reasons), then the final decision regarding payment of scholarship to the students in exceptional circumstances will be taken by the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune.
The selected candidates will be required to join the training institute in Delhi or Maharashtra immediately after selection and submit the guarantee letter to the office of Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune within 7 days. The candidates who do not join within 15 days from the date of appointment, will be de-selected and the candidates in the waiting list will be selected on merit and sent to Delhi for training.
