The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, financial assistance is provided to eligible beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes for construction of pucca houses under Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme. A goal is set for each year and funding is allocated. Under this scheme, to meet the target of households for the year 2023-24, Rs. 96900.20 Lakhs in the Budget Estimates 2023-24 to the Finance Department vide reference No. 3 was requested. The proposal was under the consideration of the government for disbursement of funds as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
Government Resolution under Tribal Component Programme Demand No. The budgetary provisions for the year 2023-24 for the Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme under the head Account Head, T-5, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities are as follows: 24900.20 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Nineteen Crores Twenty Thousand only) is being approved for disbursement and expenditure: (Rs. million).
