The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, as per the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Department of Finance is making available a certain limit of funds on the Budget Estimate, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system for distribution. Accordingly, the funds received vide reference No. 2 Under the mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department as per the decision of the Government here, for the schemes No. 1- 55% of the budgetary allocation for the salary component has been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
Now, the Finance Department has introduced item No. Incremental funds to the extent of 5% of the budgeted funds for wage items have been made available for disbursement on the Budget Fund Distribution System. The matter of disbursement of these funds to the concerned controlling authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Demand No. 1 for wages under" Compulsory Expenditure" Scheme of Tribal Development Department. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure to the concerned Controlling Authorities within the limit of 5% of the budgetary provisions as per Racket No. 5 for the schemes mentioned in Racket No. 2 in the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned persons should refer to the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book and the Finance Department's reference No. 1 Care should be taken to comply with the provisions and conditions laid down by the Government Circular here.
