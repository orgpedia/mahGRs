The Proposal:
The Finance Department was requested to disburse funds to the students of government ashramshalas under the Tribal Development Department for the provision of facilities and other things. Accordingly, the Finance Department has approved the first expenditure within the available 10 percent limit. The government was planning to allocate funds for the purpose.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the Tribal Component Programme, Government Ashramshala Samooh under the main head of Demand Number T-5, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, in the financial year 2020-21, the following funds are being approved with the approval of the Finance Department for school supplies and other items to the students under this scheme.
