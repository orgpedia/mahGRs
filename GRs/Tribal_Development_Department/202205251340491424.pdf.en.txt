The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022. Accordingly, as per the Government Circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Department of Finance is making available a certain limit of funds on the Budget Estimate, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system for distribution. Accordingly, the funds received vide reference No. 2 Under the mandatory expenditure of the Tribal Development Department as per the decision of the Government here, for the schemes No. 25% of the total budgeted fund for salary, 50% for interest and 14% for all other items have been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
Now, the Finance Department has decided to provide funds under the mandatory expenditure for utilization up to the end of the month of June, 2022 under the schemes as per Chapter No. 45- Excess fund in the limit of 7% of the total budgeted fund for other than interest is made available for distribution on the budgeted fund distribution system. The matter of disbursement of these funds to the concerned controlling authorities was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Demand No. For appropriation under" Compulsory Expenditure" of Tribal Development Department upto the end of June, 2022. The Government is approving the disbursement and spending of the increased funds within the limit of 7% of the budgetary provisions as per Racket No.5 for the schemes mentioned in Racket No.2 to T-4 and T-8 of the accompanying returns to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned persons should refer to the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book and the Finance Department's reference No. 1 Care should be taken to comply with the provisions and conditions laid down by the Government Circular here.
