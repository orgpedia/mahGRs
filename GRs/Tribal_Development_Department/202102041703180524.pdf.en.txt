GOVERNMENT DECISIONS A circular dated 16th April, 2020 issued by the Department of Finance specifies the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2020-21, taking into account the pandemic situation arising out of Corona virus in the State. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has provided funds from time to time on the Budgetary Fund Distribution System, vide Reference No. 2 Under the Tribal Component Program under the Government Decisions here, Demand No. Under the head of T-5, 2202-General Education, the Department of School Education is implementing the following grants-in-aid to non-government junior colleges (22021948) 36 grants-in-aid (salary).
Now in the month of February, funds have been made available for disbursement by the Department of Finance on the Budgetary Allocation System (BEAMS) for the purpose of 01 salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) subject to a ceiling of 90% of the total budgetary provisions. Taking this into consideration, under the Tribal Component Programme, the remaining amount of 90% of the ceiling under the head of demand number T-5, 2202-General Education, to non-government junior colleges, is Rs. 41.50 lakh to the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be serious in the next period due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. This amount should be used for regular payment of salary. It should not be used to pay any pending liabilities at present.
3. The head of the department/ controlling officer should immediately distribute the distributed provisions to the drawing and disbursing officers under him. Also, while spending the funds distributed under this government decision, the head of the department and the controlling officers will have to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook, as well as in the context of the Finance Department. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
