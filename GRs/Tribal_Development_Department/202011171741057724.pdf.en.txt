As per the Government decision dated 16.12.2009 of the Tribal Development Department, the responsibility of approving the District Annual Tribal Sub Plan has been entrusted to the concerned District Planning Committee. Accordingly, the responsibility of preparing the district plan will be of the Collector as the Member Secretary of the District Planning Committee and the concerned Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project will be under his control. Further, in pursuance of the Government decision dated 27.01.2017 of the Department of Finance, it has been decided to consolidate the under Plan and non-Plan expenditure from the financial year 2017-18 onwards and the budgetary expenditure has been further categorized as" Compulsory Expenditure,"" Expenditure on Programmes" and" Revenue" and" Other (Capital and Credit) Expenditure." As per the decision of the Government, from the year 2017-18, the Tribal Sub-Plan is being called Tribal Component Schemes. Accordingly, the following guidelines are being issued for preparation of District Annual Tribal Sub Plan under Tribal Component Programme for the financial year 2021-22.
The Tribal Component Programme is a part of the Annual Plan of the State and formulation of the Annual Tribal Component Programme is complementary to the Annual State Programme. The draft plan of the District Annual Tribal Component Programme for the year 2021-22 should be prepared by the concerned Project Officer in consultation with the District Collector and should be submitted for approval of the District Tribal Sub-Plan Committee/ District Planning Committee and should be submitted for the State level meeting.
For this, care should be taken to take action within the period mentioned in Annexure-' E'.

10) In respect of various schemes, the Project Officers should visit their project areas and hold discussions with the field level implementing officers and accordingly identify the exact requirement in terms of tribal beneficiaries in their area and determine the exact schemes that need to be taken up.
