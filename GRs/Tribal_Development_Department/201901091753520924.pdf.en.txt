Regarding the distribution of 5% of the funds of tribal utilization for the year 2018-2019 to the Gram Panchayats of Maregaon, Ralegaon, Kelapur, Ghatanji, Jharijamani, Arni Panchayat Samitis under Yavatmal Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas.

the Tribal Development Department

Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Road
Ministry, Extension Building, Mumbai-400032.
Date: January 9th, 2019
Read:- 1. Tribal Development Department, S.No.Pesa-2015/ PR. No.19/ K-17, Dy. 21.04.2015.
Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only) is available for disbursement, so that 57 villages of 24 Gram Sabhas in the area of Maregaon Panchayat Samiti under Yavatmal Zilla Parishad, 38 villages of 26 Gram Sabhas in area of Ralegaon Panchayat Samiti, 108 villages of 53 Gram Sabhas in area of Kelapur Panchayat Samiti, 35 villages of 27 Gram Sabhas in area of Ghatanji Panchayat Samiti, 74 villages of 30 Gram Sabhas in area of Jharijamani Panchayat Samiti, 12 villages of 8 Gram Sabhas in area of Arni Panchayat Samiti have received the amount in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samitis bank accounts in the area of Maregaon, Ralegaon, Kelapur, Ghatanji, Jharijamani, Arni.
424.2108/- Rs. 4,64,53, 626.42/- (Rs. Four crore, sixty four lakh, fifty three thousand, six hundred and twenty six, forty two paise only) is being made available directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the attached Annexure Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal should submit the utilization certificate of the funds made available to the department.
