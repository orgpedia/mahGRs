Note:
1) One of cumin rice/ lemon rice/ onion rice/ pulao should be given at least twice a day.
2.Pappad, pickle, curd and salad should be given along with lunch every day. Also, you should have a bowl of oatmeal and curd with dinner.
3. Seasonal fruits like cucumber, papaya, banana, guava, apple, orange, melon, etc should be given in the amount of 75 to 100 grams per student.
4. Give 100 grams of groundnut laddu, popcorn, dry gram, farsan in the proportion of 100 grams per student.
Each student will be given 1 egg and 2 bananas.
On the 2nd and 4th Sunday of every month, chicken and goat mutton should be given to non-vegetarian students in the amount of at least 100 grams to a maximum of 150 grams.
On the 2nd and 4th Sunday of every month, vegetarian students should be given at least 100 grams of sweet dishes like shira, kheer, gulabjam.
8.Packed in peanut oil, sesame oil or sunflower oil for cooking meals and snacks
Use is mandatory. No other oil should be used.
