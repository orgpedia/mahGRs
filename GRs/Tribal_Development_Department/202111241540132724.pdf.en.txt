The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, financial assistance is provided to eligible beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes for construction of pucca houses under Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme. Under the scheme, the target is fixed every year and funds are made available accordingly. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, with the approval of the Finance Department for the pending liabilities for the year 2019-20, Rs. 8750.00 lakhs has been disbursed. The reference No. 3 In pursuance of the proposal herein, the Scheme has been extended to meet the pending liabilities for the period 2019-20 and the housing requirement for the year 2021-22 from the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2021-22 to the extent of Rs. 1 here. As per the decision of the Finance Department dated 14.10.2021, the remaining 25% of the available limit of Rs. 8750.00 lakhs was under consideration of the Government.
