The funds will be used only for reimbursement of tuition fees of 100 eligible students who have been admitted for vocational training since 2019-20. In the case of the Head of Department/ Controlling Officer, while spending this provision, the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook will be included in it.
