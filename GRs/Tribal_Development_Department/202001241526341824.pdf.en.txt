The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 1876.110 lakhs (Rupees Eighteen Crores Seventy Six Lakhs Eleven Thousand only) to the Principal Secretary, Rural Development Department as Head of the Administrative Department.
The funds have been disbursed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of Administrative Department concerned shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government Policy on Expenditure. Also, the concerned field officer should send the report of expenditure from the provision made available, fund utilization certificate, material targets achieved account-wise/ sub-account-wise, etc. to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and Rural Development Department by the 10th of every month.
The expenditure incurred by the Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department on schemes in the Tribal Sub Plan Area, in the Additional Tribal Sub Plan Area and outside the Tribal Component Programme Area shall be recorded separately.
