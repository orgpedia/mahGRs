The Proposal:
Jijamata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aided Ashramshala run by Mehekar G. Buldhana, Chinchala, Ta. Mehekar G. To start classes of 11th and 12th Arts and Science from the academic year 2009-10 in Buldhana, Reference No. 1 here. The approval was granted in accordance with the Government Decision dated 21.08.2009. Classes 11 and 12 of Arts and Science stream are being held in the ashram.
2. Now Jijamata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Mehekar G. Buldhana has decided to close the classes of 11th and 12th Arts and Science from the academic year 2021-22 as there are 4 high schools in the vicinity of their aided ashramshala and the tribal students are now inclined towards studying in the city and are facing difficulties in collecting sufficient marks for the 11th and 12th classes in their ashramshala. The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, vide reference No. 2 here. As per the letter dated 27.08.2021, a proposal has been submitted to the government regarding the closure of Arts and Science classes of Class 11 and 12 of Higher Secondary in the thematic ashramshala.
Government Decision: Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik vide Reference No. 2 here. Pursuant to the proposal dated 27.08.2021, Jijamata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, a subsidized Ashramshala run by Mehekar G. Buldhana, Chinchala, Tt. Mehekar G. Class 11th and 12th Arts and Science of Higher Secondary School in Buldhana

The permanent closure of the branch classes from the academic year 2021-22 is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
The Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project, Akola shall make the adjustment of the students of class 11th and 12th in the Sadar Ashramshala on priority to the nearest government or aided Ashramshala which is convenient for the students.
Commissioner/ Additional Commissioner/ Project Officer shall make adjustment of teaching and non-teaching staff of higher secondary in the said Ashramshala on priority to the vacancies in other aided Ashramshala. The process should be initiated immediately.
3. Jijamata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aided Ashramshala run by Mehekar G. Buldhana, Chinchala, Ta. Mehekar G. Buldhana will no longer offer classes in the Arts and Science stream of Class 11 and 12 of Higher Secondary.
