Regarding the distribution of 5% of the funds of tribal utilization for the year 2018-2019 to the Gram Panchayats of Murbad, Bhiwandi, Shahapur Panchayat Samitis under Thane Zilla Parishad in the Scheduled Areas.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Tribal Development Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. PESAA-2018/ PR No. 380/12/ K. 17
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Road
Ministry, Extension Building, Mumbai- 400032
Date: January 9th, 2019
Read:- 1. Tribal Development Department, S.L.No.Pesa-2015/ S.No.19/ K.A-17, Dy. 21.04.2015.
Government Decision: The scheme of providing 5% of the fixed cost of tribal applications to the Gram Panchayats in the scheduled areas of the State every year (PESA Direct Funding Scheme to the Gram Panchayats) has been notified vide Reference No. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only).
2) For this scheme, Rs. 187,52,01, 300/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs, One Thousand and Three Hundred only) is available for disbursement, so that 101 villages of 55 Gram Sabhas in Murbad Panchayat Samiti area under Thane Zilla Parishad, 72 villages of 39 Gram Sabhas in Bhiwandi Panchayat Samiti area, 230 villages of 110 Gram Sabhas in Shahapur Panchayat Samiti area in their Gram Sabha Kosh Samiti bank accounts, for a total tribal population of 1,79,562 in Murbad, Bhiwandi, Shahapur Panchayat Samiti area, Rs. 424.2108/- Rs. 7,61,72, 137.30/- (Rs. Seven crore, sixty one lakh, seventy two thousand, one hundred thirty seven, thirty paise only) is being made available directly through the RTGS system as mentioned in the attached Annexure Gram Sabha wise. The funds shall be spent by the respective Gram Sabha in proportion to the tribal population of the village/ hamlet/ wadi/ pada falling under it. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds provided should be submitted to the department by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane.
