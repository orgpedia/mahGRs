The Tribal Development Department had appealed to the Project Officer, AAV Project Office, Non-Government/ Social/ Private Organizations to send proposals for the grant received under this scheme. After detailed scrutiny of the proposals received, the selected proposals were submitted to the Executive Committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The proposal includes a progress campaign. The proposal is mainly to implement development programmes in respect of Nagli/ Ragi, a major crop grown on light land in the tribal areas of Palghar, Nashik and Thane districts, to increase income as well as nutritional security of the people of the area.
