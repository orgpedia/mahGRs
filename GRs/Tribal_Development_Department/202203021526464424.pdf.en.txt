Corrigendum to the Government Order dated January 31, 2022.
2. Letter from Director, State Management Cell-Rural Housing No. Gr/ Accounts (RIO) Pr/ 2020/2239 dated 23.02.2022
As per the Namud Sasan Corrigendum, under the State Level Programme for the Development of Aboriginal Tribes, to solve the problem of housing for the primitive tribes of Katkari, Madia Gond and Kolam in the state, the construction of houses for the year 2021-22 has been approved and the total number of houses has been revised to 3390. For this, funds have been transferred to the State Management Cell-Rural Housing through the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik. A request has been made to the Government under Reference 2 to authorize the Director, State Management Cell-Deputy Director of Rural Housing Office as Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw this fund on the Public Distribution System. A decision in this regard was pending with the government.
Purifier-
Accordingly, paragraph 2 of the Governance Corrigendum referred to herein is being amended as follows.
Rs. 4499.70 Lakhs (Rs. Forty four crore ninety nine lakh seventy thousand only) is being disbursed to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik as the Controlling Officer on the Budget Distribution System. He should immediately disburse the budgetary allocation on the BEAMS system to the Director, State Management Cell- Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the Rural Housing Office and the Deputy Director, State Management Cell- Rural Housing as the implementing officer of the scheme.
