The Proposal:
A committee has been formed under No. 2 of Vacha under the chairmanship of Mr. Vivek Pandit, former Member of Legislative Assembly, to review the various schemes being implemented in the tribal areas of the state. Also, read this. As per the Government decision dated 28th May, 2019, Shri Vivek Pandit has been accorded the status of Minister of State and the corresponding service facilities have been made permissible.
It has now been decided to retain the appointment of Mr. Vivek Pandit as the Chairman of the said Committee and give him the status of Minister instead of Minister of State. Accordingly, the matter of issuing a revised government decision by overruling the government decision at Read No. 3 here was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A committee has been formed under No. 2 of Vacha under the chairmanship of Mr. Vivek Pandit, former Member of Legislative Assembly, to review the various schemes being implemented in the tribal areas of the state. Also, read this. As per the Government decision dated 28th May, 2019, Shri Vivek Pandit has been accorded the status of Minister of State and the corresponding service facilities have been made permissible. Now, by authorizing this government decision, Mr. Vivek Pandit ji is being given the status of Minister from the date of this government decision and he will be entitled to the following services as per Read Here No. 1:
