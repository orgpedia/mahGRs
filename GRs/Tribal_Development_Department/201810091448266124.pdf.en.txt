Government's decision:
Under the Tribal Component Programme for Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, Demand No. In T-5, 2210- Medical Services and Public Health- Schemes under Five Year Plans, 03- Rural Health Services- Tuberculosis, 796- Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 01- Scheme under Tribal Area Appropriation- Scheme under State Schemes (01) (06) Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (2210 G289) 31- Subsidiary Grants ((Non-Payment)), under the head Rs. Rs. 1500.00 lakhs has been allocated for this.
Public Health Department No. 2 Account head for Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (2210 G289) 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried), as proposed on the Annexure, under this head subject to a ceiling of 70% of Rs. 1050.00 lakh (Rupees Ten Crore Fifty Lakh only) to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department as Head of the Administrative Department.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government Policy on Expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, the concerned field officer should be careful to send to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; as well as the Tribal Development Department and Public Health Department by the 10th of every month.
The Head of the Department and the Controlling Officer shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal component programme area is recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
