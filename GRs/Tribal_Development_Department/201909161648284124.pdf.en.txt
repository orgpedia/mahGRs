Introduction: Among the scheduled tribes of the state, the Katkari, Madiyagond, Kolam tribes are the most backward. Special efforts are needed for their educational and economic development. Various schemes are being implemented for the tribal people with the funds of the Central Government. In the meeting of the Tribal Advisory Council held on 11th February, 2019, the Tribal Advisory Council has recommended to start separate Ashramshalas for the primitive tribes. The aided Ashramshalas, which have been closed permanently, are proposed to be handed over to the institutions working for the primitive tribes without opening the Ashramshalas additionally
2. All India Backward Classes Social Enlightenment Society, Kalyan, Thane. A self-financed school for girls of Katkari community in Shahapur, Thane district, running from 1st to 8th standard since 1999, is functioning regularly till date. For the last 35 years, the organization has been implementing many social projects in the tribal women and child development sector with the help of the Government of Maharashtra and the Central Government. The institute has been awarded the Dalit Mitra and Shahu Phule Ambedkar Award by the government for its outstanding work in the social sector.
The institution has sufficient educational experience to run the school and has land and other facilities.
