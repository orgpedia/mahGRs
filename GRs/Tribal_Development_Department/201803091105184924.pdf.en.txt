The Government have accorded approval to disburse the amount from the budgeted fund for the year 2017-18 to the Controlling Officer of the scheme, Principal Secretary (Rural Development), Rural Development and Water Conservation Department on budgetary outlay basis.
The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department in the case should ensure that the fund is spent in the same year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure. Also, instructions should be given to send the report of expenditure from the provision made available, information about the material goal achieved account-wise/ sub-account-wise, fund utilization certificate, instructions to be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development and Rural Development and Water Conservation Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal component programme area shall be recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
5. Allocations made under Tribal Component Programme should be spent only for tribal beneficiaries and under tribal sub-plan areas. Also, the allocation made outside the tribal sub-plan area should be spent only for the tribal beneficiaries of that area.
