The committee submitted its report No. (07). Submitted to the Government along with the recommendations of the Committee vide letter dated 27.12.2021. Among the committee's key recommendations are the following: :
The committee has recommended the following 3 points to make the benefits of the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme of the state government acceptable to the students admitted to professional courses in the deemed universities of the state.
(i) Simple Average: Simple average is the sum of the fees prescribed for each private, professional institution of the course concerned divided by the total number of private professional institutions of the course concerned.
(ii) Mean Value of Maximum and Minimum Fee The mean value of the maximum and minimum fees charged for the course is the sum of the two.
(iii) Weighted Average: Weighted Average is the number of students who have enrolled in a college divided by the total number of students enrolled.
2. Discounting Factor: The Committee has recommended a Discounting Factor to reduce the pressure on the students to give preference to the preferred university.
