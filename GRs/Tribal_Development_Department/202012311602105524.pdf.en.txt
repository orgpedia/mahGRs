Government's decision:
The proposal submitted by the Additional Commissioner regarding the purchase of bedding materials (1) Gadi coir mattress bunk bed, (2) Gadi coir mattress single bed and (3) pillow) for government ashramshalas and hostels under the jurisdiction of the Additional Commissioner, Thane, Nashik, Amravati and Nagpur, from the Maharashtra State Machinery Corporation. The proposal submitted by the Additional Commissioner regarding the purchase of (1) blankets, (2) bedsheets and (3) cushion covers from the Maharashtra State Machinery Corporation under Section 3 of the Act is referred to in Reference No. Administrative and financial approval has been granted under Section 4. Reference No. Under Section 5, it has been informed about the suspension of the procurement process of the said bedding materials. In this decision, the Government vide reference No. 3 and Reference No. The administrative and financial sanction orders have been cancelled.
Financial sanction has been granted for supply of bending mattress mattresses to the students of Ashramshalas and hostels under the Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nagpur, Amravati and Thane for the academic year 2017-18 and Additional Commissioner, Nashik for the academic year 2018-19. Hence, reference No. 1 The bedding materials (1) Coir mattresses (2) pillows, (3) blankets, (4) bedsheets and (5) pillow covers may be used as per requirement, taking into account the duration of the items mentioned in Annexure-A accompanying the Government decision. As per Para 5.14 of the Procurement Policy laid down under Section 2 of the Act, the tender process for procurement from Micro and Small Scale Industries manufacturers should be conducted at the level of Commissioner, Tribal Development, State of Maharashtra, Nashik.
