Preamble: The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has been empowered to disburse funds for Swayam Yojana, Government Hostel Scheme and Named School Scheme as per Government Decision 1 to 3. However, in view of the technical difficulties in this scheme, the students are facing the wrath of the students and their organizations if they do not receive their DBT funds on time. Also, in the scheme of providing education to Scheduled Tribe students in residential schools, the marking of schools, allocation of students to schools and visits to schools are being done from the level of the Upper Commissioner, in such a situation, only the power to allocate grants is at the level of the Commissioner. Considering all these aspects, the matter of appointing the Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development as the controlling officer of the above three schemes was under the consideration of the government.
In view of the above, the following decisions have been taken:
Government Decision: Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development is being appointed as Controlling Officer for Government Hostel Scheme, Swayam Scheme and Nominated Scheme. The process of disbursement of funds for the above three schemes should be done at the level of the Additional Commissioner.
The office of the Additional Commissioner will be fully responsible for receiving the funds from the government through DBT and paying them to the beneficiaries within the stipulated time.
