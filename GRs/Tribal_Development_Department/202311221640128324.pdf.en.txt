Mantralaya, Mumbai 400032
Date: 22/11/2022
1. Synchronized Government Resolution dated 26.7.2022 and dated 26.12.2022 2. Letter from Commissioner, Tribal Development No. 17.09.2023
Introduction- Since the livelihood of the tribal brothers is rain-fed agriculture, there is no guarantee of assured income through farming every year. By combining goat farming with agriculture, they can get a guaranteed income and since this business is mainly associated with women, women self-help groups will be given units of 10 goats and 1 goat. 1 The guidelines have been approved under the government decision here. Read No. Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik has requested to amend the said guidelines vide letter No. 2. In this regard, the matter of revising the guidelines of the program was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Guidelines for Supply of Goat Units to Women SHGs (10 Female+ 1 Male) Scheme worth Rs. 500.00 lakhs approved under the programme to strengthen the SHGs by increasing the income of women SHGs through goat rearing and reducing their migration. 1 Approval is being granted to amend the points 2, 3, 8 and 9 of Annexure 1 to the Government Decisions issued hereunder as indicated in Annexure 1 annexed to this Decision. No changes have been made to the items other than the above and the other sub-items in items 2 and 8 above.
2. The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik shall take immediate action to implement the scheme within the limits of the provisions distributed as per the said guidelines and shall take care to submit its utilization certificate along with financial and physical report to the Government.
