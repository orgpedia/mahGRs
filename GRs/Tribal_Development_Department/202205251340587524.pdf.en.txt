The Proposal:
1 in the Finance Department. As per the instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 4.04.2022, funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary provisions required for the first nine months of the year 2022-23 for schemes under mandatory and programme expenditure will be made available for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, the Department of Finance is currently making available the provisions within the limit of 7% of the budgeted allocation of the schemes for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system every month. Accordingly, under the Programme Expenditure in Tribal Component Programme, so far, funds have been released to the concerned Controlling Authorities for certain schemes within the limit of 25% for the purpose of 01 salary and 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries), 14% for other office purposes and for certain schemes as separately approved.
Now, for the month of May-end, for the month of June, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement the enhanced funds within the limit of 7% of the budgetary provisions for disbursement on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) delivery system. Accordingly, at present, 32% of the funds are available for disbursement for the 1st Pay and 36th Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) targets and 21% for the remaining targets. As the Corona related restrictions have been relaxed, the government was considering to disburse the remaining 7% of the funds available for the 01-salary and 36-support grants (salary) of the schemes and other office purposes under the program expenditure and 21% of the funds available for the distribution of some schemes to the concerned controlling authorities.
Government decision:
Under the programme expenditure of the Tribal Development Department' Demand No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of funds to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per the Budget provisions under the Schemes mentioned in Annexure No. 2 to the accompanying returns under T-4 and T-5.
