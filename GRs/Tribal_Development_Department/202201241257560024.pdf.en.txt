The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking fiscal measures by prioritising expenditure as per government decisions. Accordingly, as provided by the Department of Finance on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) Distribution System from time to time, the budgetary allocation to the extent of 90% of the wages of the Scheduled Tribes Certification Scrutiny Committees and Aided Ashramshalas covered under the programme expenditure of the Department of Tribal Development has been disbursed to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
Now, the Finance Department has made available funds for disbursement of wages under these schemes within the limit of 100% of the budgetary provision. Accordingly, considering the funds released earlier, the matter of disbursement of the remaining incremental provision to the concerned Controlling Authority was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision:
Under the" Programme Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. In T-5, in TITLE 2225-SC, following schemes for the welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, the remaining 10% of the fund within the limit of 100% is being sanctioned and spent as per the approval of the Finance Department for payment of salaries.
