Department of Tribal Development, Government of India vide Decision No. 2019/ No. 63/ K. 19, d. 29/06/2019 and D. 23/07/2019 Preamble Grants under Special Central Assistance are provided by the Central Government to the State Governments every year under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India and under the scheme of Tribal Development Programme. The Tribal Development Department had appealed to the Project Officer, AAV Project Office, Government, Non-Government/ Social/ Private Organizations to submit innovative proposals for the grant received under this scheme. A detailed review of the proposals received. The report was submitted to the committee headed by the Chief Secretary.
In this proposal, the proposal of women power was received. The proposal is mainly to improve the nutritional status of the students by providing nutritious, tasty and delicious food to the students of tribal ashram schools by setting up a central kitchen in the jurisdiction of AAV Project Office Kalwan, Dhule and Shahapur.
2. With a view to improve the nutritional status of the students in the residential ashramshalas and hostels and to provide them with high quality nutritious food, the Tribal Development Department proposed to the Central Government to implement this scheme with the approval of the Executive Committee headed by the Chief Secretary and the Government. In the Project Appraisal (Committee PAC) meeting held on 12.02.2019 by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, the Scheme" Stri Shakti Establish and Run Central Kitchen" was approved for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 under Article 275 (1) of the Constitution of India for implementation by the Stri Shakti Sansthan with an outlay of Rs. A total amount of Rs. 2648.23 lakh is proposed to be provided in the proposals of Rs. 1324.41 lakh and Rs. 1323.82 lakh respectively. 1 has been approved and the said fund has been disbursed to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, as per the decision of the Government in question.
At present, 502 government ashram schools, 25 Eklavya residential ashram schools, English medium schools and 491 hostels for students are functioning in Maharashtra. The overall development of these students is the responsibility of the Tribal Development Department. The department is implementing special schemes to ensure their health, nutrition and education properly. The establishment of the central kitchen will improve the nutritional status of the students of the ashramshalas and hostels belonging to the Tribal Development Department and provide them with high-quality nutritious food. Under the scheme, it is proposed to set up 4 central kitchens at Project Office, Kalwan, two at Project Office, Dhule and one at Shahapur.

Sthree Shakti is a registered organization working for women empowerment since 1993.
It is an ISO certified organization working towards economic empowerment of women by participating in the Stree Shakti initiative. Through this organization, Shramik Annapurna Yojana in the state of Gujarat, and more than 4500 schools in India are providing food related services. A part of this has been used to run a restaurant at the Delhi Secretariat since 2009 and a restaurant at the Delhi Police Headquarters since January 2015. The company has 17 years of experience in this field. The central kitchen is being run by the Tribal Development Department at Nandurbar, Jawhar and construction is underway at Ghodegaon. A proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the guidelines for the implementation of the scheme" Stree Shakti- Establish and Run Central Kitchen" through an organization approved internally by the organization with such a reputation and experience of working with the department and having a long experience in this field.
The government has decided to implement the scheme" Stri Shakti-Establish and run central kitchen" jointly with the Tribal Development Department to improve the nutritional status of the students in the residential ashram and hostel of the Tribal Development Department. 2648.23 lakh is being sanctioned as per Annexure-I annexed to this decision.
2. The Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik shall take immediate action to implement the scheme within the limits of the provisions distributed as per the guidelines of the said scheme and shall take care to submit its utilization certificate along with financial and physical report to the Government.
