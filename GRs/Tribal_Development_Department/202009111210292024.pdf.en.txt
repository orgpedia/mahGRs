The Controlling Officer/ Head of the Department shall ensure that the funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government Policy on Expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, instructions should be given by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area as well as in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the sub-plan area.
