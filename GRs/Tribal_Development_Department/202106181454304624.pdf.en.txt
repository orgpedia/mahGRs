Preamble: For the Financial Year 2021-22, in the context of Finance Department, No. 1 Guidelines have been issued for taking financial measures by setting priorities for expenditure under the government circular here. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) by the Finance Department, as made available on the delivery system from time to time, have been prepared in respect of the schemes covered under the mandatory and programme expenditure of the Tribal Development Department vide No. 01-Salary, 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) for the purpose of reference No. As per the decision of the Government, funds have been released to the concerned Controlling Authorities within the limit of 25% of the budgetary provisions at the end of the month of June, 2021.
Now, the Department of Finance has made available for disbursement of enhanced funds@ 5% of the budgetary provisions for 01- Salaries, 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on the Budget Estimates Distribution System (BEAMS) during the month of July, 2021. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the" Compulsory Expenditure" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. The Government is approving the release and expenditure of 5% of the increased budgetary allocation to the concerned Controlling Authorities as per Rakaan No. 5 for payment of salaries for the schemes mentioned in Rakaan No. 2 of the accompanying returns under T-2 and T-4.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending these funds, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules of the state government, financial rules, provisions in the budget rule book and all the terms and conditions.
