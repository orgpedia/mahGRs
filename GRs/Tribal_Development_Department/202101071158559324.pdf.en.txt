Grants are provided. Details of the proposals received under the scheme
The proposals will be sent to the central government for approval. Accordingly, a proposal has been submitted to the Central Government for the creation of special schools for the students of primitive tribes.
It was proposed to the Central Government to implement the scheme in the Ashram Schools which are currently running and have the largest number of tribal students.
2. Establishing model schools for tribal students. The Government proposed to the Central Government to implement this scheme with the objective of establishing schools on the lines of Eklavya Residential School for the students of the primitive tribes so as to enhance the educational quality of the students by providing quality education to the students of the primitive tribes through ashramshalas with state-of-the-art facilities and thereby bringing the students of the primitive tribes into the mainstream of education along with other children. In the meeting of the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) of the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, it was approved to implement the scheme" Creating dedicated schools for 3 PVTGs with special facilities on the basis of EMRS." The funds received for this purpose have been distributed to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik as per the relevant Government decisions.
