The proposal-
Regarding the provision of funds to the Gram Panchayats in the Scheduled Areas of the State at 5% of the annual fixed cost of the Tribal Component Programme (5% Direct Funding Scheme for Pesa Gram Panchayats), Reference No. 01 Approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 21.04.2015. For the year 2018-19, the Account Head for the Scheme (2501 2971) is Rs. 26788.59 lakh has been provided in the Budget. 70% of the budget of Rs. 18752.013 lakh is available for disbursement on Budget Distribution System. The matter of distribution of this fund on the budget distribution system was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
5% direct funding to Gram Panchayats in the Scheduled Areas (PESA) of the State Under the Scheme Head of Accounts (2501 2971) for the year 2018-19, at present, 70% of the budgeted funds amounting to Rs. 18752.013 lakh (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Seven Crores Fifty Two Lakhs One Thousand Three Hundred only) is being made available to the Controller General and Joint Secretary (Office 17), Tribal Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai on Budget Distribution System.
