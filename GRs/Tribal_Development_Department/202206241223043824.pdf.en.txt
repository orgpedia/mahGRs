The Proposal:
Under the Tribal Component Programme, financial assistance is provided to eligible beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes for construction of pucca houses under Shabari Tribal Housing Scheme. Under this scheme, every year the target of the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes is fixed and funds are provided accordingly. In order to meet the target of houses allotted for the year 2016-17 to 2021-22, under reference No. 2 In pursuance of the proposal herein referred No. 3 The issue of disbursement of funds to the extent available through the Finance Department on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) distribution system as per the approval on the note here was under consideration of the Government.
