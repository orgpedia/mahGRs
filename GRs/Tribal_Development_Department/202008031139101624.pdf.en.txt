Government's decision:
In order to overcome the situation arising out of the Corona virus, it is necessary to provide assistance to the beneficiaries under the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme under the Tribal Component Program. 2 As per the decision of the Government, funds have been allocated for these schemes in the range of 60-65% of the total budgetary allocation on the budgetary allocation system. Now, under the Tribal Component Programme, Demand No. 2235-Social Security and Welfare in T-5, under the heading of Main Articles, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Read No. 3 Remaining provision within the limit of budgetary provision as proposed herein is being approved to disburse and spend the funds for the following schemes for the financial year 2020-21 on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS) to the Secretary, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department.

The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be critical in the next few months due to the lockdown, the controlling officer should plan and spend the funds in the prescribed manner. It should be ensured that this fund is spent in this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy.
Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, the concerned field officer should be careful to send to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development; Tribal Development Department and Social Justice and Special Assistance Department by the 10th of every month.
The Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal component programme area is recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on the schemes in the tribal area and outside the sub-plan area. It should be ensured that only the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Tribes are given the benefit of the provision made available under the Tribal Component Programme.
4. The head of the department/ controlling officer should immediately distribute the distributed provisions to the drawing and disbursing officers under him. Also, while spending the funds distributed under this government decision, the head of the department and the controlling officers will have to follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook, as well as in the context of the Finance Department. Strictly follow the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020.
5. The decision of the Government shall be subject to the approval of the Finance Department. The powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020 and the Department of Finance. Issued pursuant to the Consent received vide Note dated 17.08.2020.
