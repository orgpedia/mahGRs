The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 has been approved by the state legislature. Accordingly, the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BEAMS) through the Finance Department have been made available on the delivery system under the Tribal Component Programme under the District Annual Tribal Appropriation in Chapter No. It was under consideration of the Government to disburse 10% of the budgetary provisions to the concerned Controlling Authorities for 1- Salaries, 5% of the budgetary provisions for 2- Wages and 5% of the budgetary provisions for 10- Contractual Services.
Government's decision:
Under the" District Annual Tribal Appropriation" of the Tribal Development Department, Demand No. T-9 and T-10 for the districts mentioned in Rakaana No. 2 of the accompanying return below, in case No. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of 10% of the budgetary provisions for 1-Wage, 5% of the budgetary provisions for 2-Wage and 5% of the budgetary provisions for 10-Contractual Services to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. The amount should be disbursed to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer. While spending this fund, the concerned should ensure compliance with the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book and all the conditions.
3. The said Government Decision is being issued by the Finance Department within the limits of the funds made available on the Budget Fund Distribution System within the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
