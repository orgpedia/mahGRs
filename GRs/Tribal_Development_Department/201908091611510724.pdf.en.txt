the prelude
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 5 of the Constitution of India,
The President of India, based on the 1971 Census, vide Reference No. It has been declared a Scheduled Area of the State of Maharashtra by a notification dated 2nd December, 1985. Whereas on the basis of the 1981 census, the Tribal Development Department has issued Reference No. 2. Under Tribal Sub-Plan Area and Additional Tribal Sub-Plan Area are declared. The area of villages and towns in the notified core tribal sub-plan areas (including scheduled areas 1985) and additional tribal sub-plan areas in the State of Maharashtra has changed between 1981 and 2019.
As per the proviso to clause 1 of section 4 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Act, 1966 (Maharashtra Act No. 41 of 1966), a Revenue Officer not below the rank of Tehsildar is empowered to notify any area outside the village limits of a village having each wadi and separate dwelling. In exercise of the said powers, the said Revenue Officer has converted the revenue villages from the Additional Tribal Sub Plan areas into new separate revenue villages. However, the government has noticed that such converted villages are not getting the benefit of schemes in the tribal sub-plan area. Therefore, the matter of providing the benefits of the tribal sub-plan area to the villages converted into the new independent revenue villages was under the consideration of the government.
As per the proviso to clause 1 of section 4 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Act, 1966 (Maharashtra Act No. 41 of 1966), a Revenue Officer not below the rank of Tehsildar shall, in reference No. The villages which have been converted into new independent revenue villages from the revenue villages in the Additional Tribal Sub Plan area declared under Section 2 from the year 1981 to 2019 should be notified on priority.
As per the proviso to clause 1 of section 4 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Act, 1966 (Maharashtra Act No. 41 of 1966), the benefits of the schemes in the tribal sub-plan area shall be admissible to the villages newly converted from revenue villages to separate revenue villages in the additional tribal sub-plan area declared by a revenue officer not below the rank of a tehsildar.
