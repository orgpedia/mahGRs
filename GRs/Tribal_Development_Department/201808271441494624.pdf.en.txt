(2 Letters) Preamble: Proposals were submitted to the Central Government under the Scheme of Assistance to Tribal Research Institutes for the financial year 2018-19. In pursuance of these proposals, a meeting of the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) was held at Ministry of Tribal Affairs, New Delhi. It was held on 23/05/2018. According to the report, the central government has allocated Rs. 485.01 lakh has been approved. Out of this, the Central Government has released Rs. 388.00 lakhs has been disbursed as first installment amounting to 80%. The Rs. 388.00 lakh to the Commissioner, Tribal Research and Training Institute, Pune.
Government Decision: The Government of India has decided to allocate a total of Rs. 388.00 Lakhs (Rs. Three crore eighty eight lakh rupees only) Funds for the following works at Pillar No. Government approval is being provided to spend within the limits of the funds indicated in Section 4.
