As per the decision of the Government dated 7th December, 2018, 100 workshops have been approved to be organized in various colleges under the auspices of the Department of Cultural Affairs and the Department of Marathi Language on the occasion of the birth centenary of Deshpande, G.D. Madgulkar and Sudhir Phadke. In the first phase, workshops have been approved to be held in 36 colleges in Schedule A with the decision of the government. Out of the 36 colleges approved, the college at Ahmednagar has expressed its inability to organize the workshop and is being approved to organize the same in the new 15 colleges of Schedule A attached with it from 7th to 29th January, 2019. The cost per workshop is as follows:
