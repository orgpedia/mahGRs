11) Ensure that the said provision is not used for non-plan work (maintenance / repair etc.).
12) It shall be mandatory to obtain a no-objection certificate from the said Devasthan / Institution in respect of the other works proposed for development of the pilgrimage sites and to be carried out at the site of the Devasthan / Institute.
