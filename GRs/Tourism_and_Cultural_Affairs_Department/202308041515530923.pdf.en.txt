Introduction:" Shivrajyabhishek Mahotsav" was celebrated on the occasion of 350th coronation year of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It will begin on June 2, 2023. Various programmes are being held across the State on the occasion. In the same background, the government was considering to give administrative and financial approval to the" Special Post Ticket" and the expenses for printing, according to which the subjects describing the persons/ places/ events/ areas etc. related to the life of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj would be decided.
Government Decision: On the occasion of the 350th coronation anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, administrative and financial approval is being given for a special postage stamp of Rs. 12,00,000/-and Rs. 15,000/-for printing, amounting to a total of Rs. 12,15,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh Fifteen Thousand only).
2. Cost Account Heading Demand No. ZD-2, Main Articles 2205 Art and Culture, (102) Art and Culture, (13) Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, (13) (01) Programmes under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, (2205 3733) 50 Other expenditure (s) to be met from the financial provisions available under this head.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
4. The said expenditure is covered by the Government Decision No. VIPR-2013/ EXCHANGE, dated the 17th April, 2015, Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-section-3, S. No. 4, Para No. 27 (2) (a) is being sanctioned as per the powers conferred on the administrative department.
