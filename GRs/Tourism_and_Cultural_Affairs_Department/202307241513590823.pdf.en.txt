The Proposal:
Many social reformers, writers, poets, political leaders and freedom fighters have contributed a lot to India's independence and the betterment of the nation. The contribution of revolutionary freedom fighter Veer Savarkar is important in this. Also, Veer Savarkar's fierce patriotism and sacrifice for the country continues to inspire the youth even today. On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Swatantryaveer Savarkar on 28th May, various programs/ activities will be organized at the state and district level under the above mentioned 1st with a view to honouring his work and propagating his ideas.
Administrative approval was given. Accordingly, the matter of financial approval of the cost of the program completed in Panvel, Raigad district was under the consideration of the government.
