Introduction: The aforementioned No. 1 The revised guidelines for the Scheme of Grant-in-Aid to Drama Producing Societies have been prepared under the Government Decision dated 19th January, 2016. Accordingly, the No. 2 The play" Sangeet Sant Tukaram" has been rated" B" as per the letter of Directorate of Cultural Affairs dated 19th May, 2018. According to the rules, the plays eligible for the grant are required to present 75 experiments according to the terms and conditions of the theatrical production grant scheme within 1 year from the date of the letter. However, the actors who played the role in the play" Sangeet Sant Tukaram" could not experiment with the play" Sangeet Sant Tukaram" due to election duty. Therefore, in view of the responsibility of the artist for the election duty, the matter of giving extension to this institution for the play" Sangeet Sant Tukaram" was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
""""""" The play"""""""" Sangeet Sant Tukaram""""""""" by Om Natyagandha, Mumbai is being given extension of 3 months from 19th May, 2019 as a special case for submission of 75 experiments for grant under the scheme of""""""""" Grant-in-Aid to Theatre Producing Societies""."""""""
This case should not be taken as a precedent.
