Preamble: Manual of Schemes to be implemented by the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy for the overall upliftment and development of Sindhi literature. 1 has been decided by the government here. Under the scheme of Folk Literature and Cultural Programmes, the Government was considering to approve the expenses for organizing a literary workshop based on the life of Smritishesh Shri Sundar Butani in Nagpur through the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, an expenditure of Rs. 1,72,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Seventy Two Thousand Only) is being approved for organizing a literary workshop based on the life of Smritishesh Shri Sundar Butani to be held in Nagpur.
