The Proposal:
The rules of the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi for the promotion of Sindhi language have been issued under the Government Decision No. 1 above. As per the rules, the award scheme is one of the most important schemes of the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi and under this scheme, literary persons who have done valuable work and made substantial contributions in the field of Sindhi literature are awarded annually. The Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi has decided to accept the names of awardees for the three years 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the names of the awardees of Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi for the financial years 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23, as mentioned in the return' A',' B' and' C' respectively, are being approved under this Government Decision.
