The Proposal:
The" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" i. e." Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" has started from 12 th March, 2021 across the country. The events under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav will continue till August, 2023.
Accordingly, the aforesaid No. 1 As per the decision of the Government here, the" Swarajya Mahotsav" is being organized in the State from 9 th August, 2022 to 17 th August, 2022. The programme is being organised at the village, taluk and district levels. With a view to encourage more people to take up this initiative in rural areas, the Government was considering the distribution of funds at the Taluk level and Panchayat Samiti level in the State for organizing programmes under' Swarajya Mahotsav."
Government's decision:
Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" initiative, from 9th August, 2022 to 17th August, 2022, under the" Swarajya Mahotsav," various programs will be organized up to the level of Taluk and Village level on a large scale and with a view to get maximum participation of the public in these activities in the rural areas, 358 Talukas and 351 Panchayat Samitis of the State will be provided Rs. 5,00,000/- each for organizing programs.
