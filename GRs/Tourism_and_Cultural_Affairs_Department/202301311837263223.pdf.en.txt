In this committee, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being appointed as the Member Secretary of the committee for organizing the meetings of the committee. However, since the examination of the plays must be done by experts, the director, cultural affairs, who is an administrative officer, will not have the right to examine the play.
(3) The Committee shall continue to function for a period of three years from the date of such decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee, whichever is earlier.
The members of the Drama Screening Committee will be paid an honorarium of Rs.500/-per play and their travel expenses will be sanctioned as per the rates of the second air-conditioned sleeper train of the Railways. Arrangements for their stay and food will be made by the Directorate of Cultural Affairs.
If the members of the Drama Screening Committee are associated with the writing/ direction/ acting/ technical/ music or other aspects of the play applied for the grant, then the said member will not be able to examine the relevant play.
A minimum of 11 out of 23 members must be present to judge the play.
7. Meeting allowance to non-official members of the Committee shall be admissible in accordance with the prevailing Government decision of the Finance Department.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
