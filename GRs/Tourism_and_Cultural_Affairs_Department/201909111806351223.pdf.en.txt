Introduction: Reference No. 1 of the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs. The Board of Editors of Maharashtra Zila Darshak was reconstituted by the Government in its decision dated 19.11.2019. The term of office of the Editorial Board shall be for a period of three years from the date of such decision or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier. The government was considering a proposal to appoint members for Nashik and Nandurbar districts in the editorial board.
Government Decision: Girish Takle for Nashik District Gazetteer and Prof. The government has approved the appointment of Dr. Sarjerao Bhamre as an approved member.
Travel Allowance, Daily Allowance admissible to non-official members of the Board of Editors, Finance Division No. 1010/ PR No. 2/ Service-5, dated 3.3.2010 and Meeting Allowance, Government Resolution, Finance Division, No. Meeting-2012/ S.No.1/ Service-5 dated 24.5.2012. However, these non-government members will not be allowed to travel by air and first class in railway air-conditioning. The changes made by the Government from time to time in the travel allowance, daily allowance and meeting allowance will be applicable to the non-official members of this Editorial Board.
