Preamble: The Government grant in aid to the International Film Festival of India is sanctioned by the Government in accordance with the decision of the Government in the context of the Government. The 3 institutions which are funded are Pune International Film Festival and Asian International Film Festival, Mumbai and in addition, Ajantaverul Mahotsav Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar by Marathwada Art, Culture and Film Foundation, which fulfills the criteria laid down on the principle of" first come first serve," will receive a maximum of Rs. A grant of Rs. 5.00 lakh was sanctioned.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, a grant-in-aid of Rs. 5.00 lakh in the form of non-recurring grant is being sanctioned by the State Government to the Marathwada Art, Culture and Film Foundation and the Ajanta-Verul Mahotsav Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar for International Film Festival, subject to the following conditions:
(a) The grant will be based on the availability of funds.
b) The organizing organization should be registered with the Charity Commissioner's Office at least 3 years ago. A written proposal must be submitted along with the application.
The grant will be admissible to the organizations organizing International Film Festival of Short Films, Documentaries, Animation Films and Full Length Films and the Festival of Marathi Films only. The objective of the organizing body should be the dissemination, promotion, conservation and dissemination of the art and culture through the medium of film.

e) The organization will be required to explain the exact objective of the film project along with the proposal. The festival is being organized with the objective of promoting the interest of the general public by bringing to them films of different themes, innovative themes, films of international/ national/ state level or award winning quality.
a) Organizing agency should work on the principle of no profit or loss.
(f) The institution shall submit to the Government a report on its activities for at least three years.
The organization will be required to submit evidence such as printed advertisements, written materials, newspaper clippings, photographs, etc. regarding the festival.
The Institute shall be required to submit to the Government a report on the accounts of the Chartered Accountants for the last three years.
(a) The Institute shall appoint at least one representative of the Government on the Awards/ Felicitation Committee to be presented at the Film Festival.
(i) Advertisement, invitation card etc. of the film festival must prominently mention" Cultural Affairs Department in collaboration with Government of Maharashtra." The cooperation of the Government of Maharashtra will be clearly understood by everyone associated with the festival as well as the media, in such a way that the organizers should highlight it before and during the festival.
(a) The Institute shall be solely responsible for ensuring that the films selected for the Film Festival are of high quality and taste. There should be a special section for the festival of quality Marathi films in the film festival.
Proposals for the film festival will be required to be submitted through the Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai. The Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai shall appoint a Scrutiny Committee at their level to scrutinize the proposal received and submit the proposal to the Government along with their feedback and recommendations.
The comments of the Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai will be required as to whether the organisation seeking funds for the film festival is fulfilling all the above mentioned aspects.
(a) The decision of the Government on the funding of the Film Festival shall be final. It will be mandatory to submit the Utilization Certificate, Profit and Loss Account and Balance Sheet at least 3 months after the conclusion of the film festival.
(a) The said fund shall not be utilised for construction of permanent structures.
a) The expenses of the office of the institution shall not be deducted from the said expenses.
(a) The Planning Committee for the said Festival shall consist of the Managing Director on behalf of the Government or a representative authorised by him.
