The government has decided to organize various programs throughout the year as it is the 350th year of the coronation of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. In order to chalk out a follow-up programme, a meeting was held on 15th July, 2023 under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Minister (S.K.) to organize a workshop on Modi Script in Maharashtra for the promotion and dissemination of Modi Script through the office of the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai and Rs. An amount of Rs. 10.00 lakh has been provided for. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Archives, Mumbai, vide the aforesaid letter, has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 10.00 lakh has been submitted to the Government for approval.
Government Decision: Under the umbrella scheme of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," on the occasion of" 350th Shivarajyabhishek Ceremony," Directorate of Archives has decided to organize workshops on Modi Script in 14 Vidyapiths (One Day) in Maharashtra for the promotion and propagation of Modi Script and for the one-day workshop, the estimated cost as shown in the table below is Rs. 72,000/ (Rupees Seventy Two Thousand only) The expected cost of 14 (one day) workshops is Rs. 10,08,000/ (Rupees Ten Lakhs Eight Thousand only) is being given administrative sanction under this Government decision.
