Introduction: The aforementioned No. 1 By the decision of the Government here, Bharat Ratna Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Promotion Scheme has been launched. There are four different activities under this program.
One of them is Bharat Ratna. Bhimsen Joshi Youth Scholarship Scheme is an initiative. Under this initiative, scholarships are sanctioned for 2 years at the rate of Rs.5,000/-per month to meritorious and needy students pursuing studies in classical music. Sub-Division No. As per the decision of the Government, seven students have been selected under this initiative for the period from December 2017 to November 2019. They were identified as No. Under Phase 2, the benefit of thematic scholarship has been provided for the first phase. Now, the matter of sanctioning scholarships to these eligible beneficiary students for the period December, 2018 to November, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Bhimsen Joshi Yuva Scholarship initiative, a total of 7 students who have qualified in the year 2018-19 have been sanctioned scholarship for a period of two years from December 2017 to November 2019. The expenditure of Rs. 4,20,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh Twenty Thousand only) is being sanctioned for the second phase of the scholarship for the month of December, 2018 to November, 2019 at the rate of Rs.5,000/- per month as per the following details:
