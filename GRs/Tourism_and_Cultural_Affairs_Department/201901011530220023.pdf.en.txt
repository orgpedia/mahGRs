The Proposal:
Under the above decision of the government, the revised rules of the scheme" Grant for the use of theatre to the theatre production institutions" have been prepared. Every year, grants are sanctioned to encourage institutions to produce new plays and to experiment with new plays to bring them in front of the audience. As per the Government decision dated 16th July, 2018, a grant of Rs. 2,50,000/-for 10 experiments was sanctioned for the play" Welcome Zindagi" by Trikut, Thane for the year 2018-2019. When this organization presented the evidence of 25 experiments of the play' Welcome Zindagi', the matter of giving them experiment grant for these 25 experiments was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, the following drama production houses are being sanctioned a grant of Rs. 6,25,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh Twenty Five Thousand only) for the experimentation of new drama. Below are the details about it.
