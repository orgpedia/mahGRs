Preamble: On the occasion of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," various programmes are being organized. The programme is a cultural programme to celebrate the glory of the diamond, golden and centenary year of the great personalities associated with art and art and to promote and disseminate the knowledge of young artists. Against this backdrop, on the occasion of the birthday of Sangeet Surya Keshavrao Bhosale, a special cultural program on" Sangeet Sanyukt Manapman," a play on him, an exhibition of photographs, famous verses from the plays of Sangeet Surya Keshavrao Bhosale and a Natya Pravesh has been organized in Kolhapur from 8th, 9th and 10th August, 2023.
Administrative and financial sanction for the programme and its expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
On the occasion of the birthday of Sangeet Suryavanshi Keshavrao Bhosale, a special cultural program involving the performance of the play" Sangeet Samyukta Manapman," exhibition of photographs, famous verses from his plays and theatrical performances, and the cost of organizing the event is Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only).
