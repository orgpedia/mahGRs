The Proposal:
Professional/ amateur theatre, children's theatre camps and competitions are organised by the government every year to enrich the theatre scene in Maharashtra. Like the normal children the handicapped children also take part in the child drama competition in a significant way. Considering this, the above mentioned No. Under Section 2 of the Act, the Government has been conducting separate amateur children's theatre competitions for the differently abled at one centre every year since 2019. Considering the disability of children with disabilities, the age of a child with disabilities for school admission is generally 2 to 5 years more than the general children. Therefore, the matter of determining the eligibility of children with disabilities to participate in this competition was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra government organises the annual Marathi Divyang Children's Theatre Competition. It is being approved that" Divyang students" studying up to Class X will be eligible to participate in this competition.
