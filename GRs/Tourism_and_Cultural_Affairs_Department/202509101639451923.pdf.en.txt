The Proposal: -
Preservation and repair works of various State Protected Monuments have been taken up at the above mentioned No. Administrative approval / revised administrative approval has been granted under Government Decisions 1 to 4. Accordingly, funds have been made available for these works from time to time. Works on these monuments are in progress and a total payment of ₹5,99,46, 964 / -is requested for financial sanction.
The Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums has issued a notification in Reference No. The letter is from 5 to 7.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to grant financial sanction for payment of maintenance and repair works of these monuments.
The Government's Decisions:
Under this Government Decision, financial sanction is being given to the following works of various State Protected Monuments for a total amount of ₹5,99,46, 964 / - (Rupees Five Crore Ninety Nine Lakh Forty Six Thousand Ninety Four Hundred and Sixty Four).
