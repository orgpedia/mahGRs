Regarding the use of the logo on government programs and government correspondence on the occasion of" 350th Shivrajyabhishek."
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs
Government Resolution No. Narrow 8221/ P.No.459/ S.C.I.4
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: July 24, 2023
Preamble: On the occasion of" 350th Shivarajyabhishekam," Shivakal Mangal symbols and a special motto highlighting the valour and valour of Maharajas have been dedicated. The purpose behind this is to rekindle the awareness of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's thoughts in the Marathi mind and to spread information about Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj through this medium wherever there is a Marathi person in the world. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to use the said logo in the publicity/ publicity of the programmes organized throughout the year by the State Government as well as in the official correspondence.
Government Decision: On the occasion of the" 350th Shivarajyabhishek," the special logo ensured in the Appendix-" A" highlighting the auspicious symbols of Shiv times and the valor of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj should be used carefully in the publicity/ publicity of all government programs of the government and in government correspondence. Also, the logo should be displayed on the facade of all government offices. The matter should be brought to the notice of all the Ministries/ Departments concerned and appropriate instructions should be circulated to the field offices concerned for taking appropriate action.
