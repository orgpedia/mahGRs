GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the above Reference No. 1 dated 26/11/2018 of the Collector, Chandrapur, A proposal has been submitted to provide funds for the construction of a hall and beautification of the premises at Sri Rajarajeshwara Devasthanam in Pobhurna.
An amount of Rs. 175.00 crore has been budgeted in 2018-19 under Regional Tourism Development Scheme for providing infrastructure facilities at tourist places. As per the instructions of the Finance Department, out of the total available fund of Rs. 17500.00 lakhs, 70% i.e. Rs. 12250.00 lakhs is available for disbursement, out of which Rs. 918.12 lakhs has been disbursed. So, (Rs. 12250.00 lakhs minus Rs. 918.12 lakhs) Rs. 11331.88 lakhs is available for disbursement.
11331.88 lakhs in Chandrapur District. Administrative approval is being accorded for the development works of Rs. 222.24 lakhs for construction of auditorium and beautification of premises at Sri Rajarajeshwara Devasthan at Pombhurna, subject to technical approval and the Government hereby approves disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 222.24 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Two Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand only) to the District Collector, Chandrapur for the year 2018-19 from the grants available under Regional Tourism Development Scheme.

11) It will be mandatory to obtain a no-objection certificate from the concerned temple/ institution in respect of the works proposed to be done on the site of the temple/ institution for the development of the pilgrimage sites. Also, it will be mandatory for the District Collector to enter into an agreement with the concerned Devasthan/ institution for the maintenance/ repair to be carried out by the concerned institution after the completion of such work. It should be noted that the government will no longer provide any funds for such maintenance repairs and such a condition should be included in the contract. The agreement should also include a condition that all facilities and tourist sites created by this work will be open to all citizens.
12) The physical progress of the works to be done from the expenditure from this fund and the monthly statements of the expenditure from the fund should be submitted to the government and the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation without fail by the 10th of every month. Also, after completion of the work, the certificate of completion and the utility certificate regarding the funds should be submitted to the government and the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation without fail.
13) After the completion of this work, the responsibility for the maintenance of this work and other ancillary works will be of the operational system and related organizations mentioned in this order.
