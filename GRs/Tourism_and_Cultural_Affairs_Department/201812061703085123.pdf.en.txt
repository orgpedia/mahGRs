3) Memorandum No. 17/2018/ Language-3 of Marathi Language Department No. 17/ 2018/ No. 17/2018/ Language-3, D. It is the birth centenary year of renowned poet, lyricist, dialogue writer G. D. Madgulkar and veteran music composer Sudhir Phadke. To honour the work of these great personalities, the year 2018-19 is being celebrated as the birth centenary year of Ma. This was announced by the Finance Minister in his Budget Speech for 2018-19. Accordingly, in honour of these great personalities, various programmes to be organized to mark their birth centenary year have been scheduled to be held on the above mentioned dates i.e. 1st September, 2018 and 1st December, 2018. The Committee was constituted by the Government on 26th September, 2018. Further, an Executive Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 25th October, 2018 for organizing the events. The programme will be jointly organised by the Department of Cultural Affairs and the Department of Marathi Language. Approval of the programmes recommended by the committee was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The following programmes are being approved for the year 2018-19 to commemorate the birth centenary of Shri G.D. Madgulkar, noted poet, script, dialogue writer and lyricist and Shri Sudhir Phadke, veteran music composer.
The number and format of the programmes will be changed as per the requirement.
