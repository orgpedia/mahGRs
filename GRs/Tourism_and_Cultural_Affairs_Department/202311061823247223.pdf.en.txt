Preamble: On the occasion of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Department has started the practice of organizing various innovative activities. The aim is to provide young artists with a wide range of knowledge and exposure through innovative programmes/ activities. On the same lines, administrative and financial approval for the expenses of organizing the theatre festival on multilingual epics from different states of the country was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, administrative and financial approval is being given for organizing and spending an amount of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakh only) on the Natya Mahotsav jointly organized by Sanskar Bharati and Directorate of Cultural Affairs at Nagpur from 14th September, 2023 to 17th September, 2023.
