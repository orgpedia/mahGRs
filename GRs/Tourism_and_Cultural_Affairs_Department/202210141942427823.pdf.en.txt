K. 4, dated 30th September, 2022 Preamble: Due to the deviation of rainfall in the last few years, sometimes there is drought and sometimes there is excessive rainfall. As a result, problems such as floods and droughts are frequent. This has adversely affected agricultural productivity. Increasing urbanization and industrialization have increased the pressure on available water. Increasing problems like pollution are reducing the usefulness of available groundwater. Siltation in rivers/ reservoirs has reduced their carrying capacity/ storage capacity. In view of all these aspects, a State Level Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary under the aforesaid relevant Government Decision to study the river and its problems and resolve them. On the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, the River Dialogue Yatra under the campaign" Chala Janjatiya Nadila" has been started from Sevagram Wardha on 2 nd October 2022.
Objectives of the Mission: 1. Organizing River Dialogue Campaign.
2. Designing measures to make rivers accessible to the public 3. Conducting comprehensive study of rivers with the cooperation of citizens and publicizing and disseminating the same 4. Drafting for making Amrit Vahini.
5. To prepare a roadmap for promotion and propagation of river health and human health.
6. Planning for promotion and dissemination of river bank and stream biodiversity in each district.
To collect information on river basin maps, river flood lines, watershed maps, soil erosion, habitat records, flood and drought records for the last five years.
8. Raising the ground water level by stopping rain water at the right place and creating awareness.
9. To study the three major causes and effects of encroachment, exploitation and pollution.
10.Planning and implementation of River Dialogue Yatra.

Under this campaign, the government was considering the formation of a district-wise committee under the chairmanship of the Collector to provide information about the selected rivers flowing through each district, its promotion and assistance in related matters.
The government has decided to set up the following committees in each district of the state to support the' Chala Janjatiya Nadila' campaign under the' Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav'.

Tenure of the Committee: One year from the date of issue of the Government Resolution or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
Committee's mandate: The District Collectors of the respective districts will be empowered to plan the Yatra at the district level according to the local conditions in order to provide all the necessary support from all the departments of the government in a timely manner so that the River Yatra" Chala Janjatiya Nadila" becomes a movement of content, impact and public participation.
The concerned department should provide the river traveller with the necessary supplementary information as per the accompanying annexure for the preparation of the yatra.
Action should be taken by the Collector's office to declare a coordinating officer from the regional office mentioned in the table for coordination during the yatra.
Facilities to be provided by the Collector to the coordinators of the" Chala Januya Nadila" campaign in the committee and the members of the team working with them:• Arrangements should be made so that one of the coordinators of the respective department will be present during the visit to the government rest house, gram sabha, women's meeting, schools, colleges, government offices, industries, etc.
Provide all necessary support to make the trip a success.
The Collectorate should arrange a canter and a passenger vehicle for the coordinator of the" Chala Jaanua Nadila" campaign (picture boards, information boards, LEDs, etc.) in the committee for publicity and publicity during the river yatra.
The Collector should appoint responsible officers under him so that the committee chairman and nodal officer should have regular contact with all the departments.
The information to be made available by the Collector to the coordinators of the' Chala Januya Nadi' campaign is attached in Annexure-II.

Amrut Nadi Yatra period: During this yatra, at least 75 rivers across the state will be covered between October 2, 2022 and January 26, 2023. (The number of rivers can be more than 75).
In every district, this campaign should be launched on the occasion of the birth anniversary of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam on October 15, 2022 by organizing a committee meeting at the district headquarters.
Action Plan of the Mission: The first phase of the Yatra/ Exercise should be completed between 15 th October 2022 to 30 th November 2022. The second phase of the yatra will be from December 1, 2022 to December 31, 2022.
Based on this study, a program of public education and enlightenment should be planned.
The finalization of the report will be done between January 1, 2023 and January 20, 2023. The Collector should take note of this and issue instructions to the officials concerned.
The Collector should submit a report to the Government Coordination Department for this initiative by January 22, 2022.
7. Possible form of Amrit Nadi Yatra: The Nadi Yatra will be in three stages Preparation:- The coordinators of the" Chala Jaanua Nadi" campaign at the district level should get the necessary information from the local administration through the Collector within the prescribed time.
The local administration/ people's representatives/ institutions etc. of the place where the yatra is going to enter will be informed by the concerned regional government agencies within the stipulated time. The committee should try to ensure maximum participation of local students/ women/ retired officers/ employees in this initiative.
Visit to rural/ urban areas:- Generally visit will be from sunrise to sunset (don't mind planning for two days if required).
The matter will be discussed, in the second session, Shivar Pheri/ field visit, industry visit, etc.

In order to know the problems of the river, the members of the river yatra should form the following types of groups, discuss with the concerned officials where necessary and submit a report to the members of the district committee about the problems and measures to be taken. The coordinator members of the' Let's go to the river' campaign will scrutinize and submit a report to the Collector.
River Study Group River Problem Analysis Group River Diagnosis Group River Treatment Group Expert Group from Schools, Colleges, Technical Colleges etc.
River Pathshala: At the local level, activities should be carried out through school/ college students and other people's participation through the coordinators of the" Let's go to the river" campaign to reach out to different sections about the problems of the river and possible solutions to them.
09. Funds: Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" initiative, funds have been directed to be made available as a special case under the letter of Planning Department vide letter No. DAP-1022/ PR No. 93/ K. 1481 dated 11th May, 2022 to the extent of maximum 10% of the sanctioned allocation for innovative schemes under District Annual Plans. Accordingly, the expenditure on administrative and other ancillary matters for this campaign should be made from the funds of the District Planning Development Committee.
