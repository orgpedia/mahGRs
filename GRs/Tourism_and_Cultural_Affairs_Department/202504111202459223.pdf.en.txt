Read: - 1) Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Government Decisions, No. Narrow 8224 / P.S. No.262 / S.C. 4, dated 04.10.2024
The Proposal: -
The Maharashtra State Gorbanjara Sahitya Akademi is a non-governmental organisation under the aforesaid Government Decision.
Members were appointed. The government was considering cancelling the appointments of non-official members on the committee.
The Government's Decisions:
Non-governmental on Maharashtra State Gorbanjara Sahitya Akademi in the aforesaid Government Decision
The appointments of the members have been cancelled.
