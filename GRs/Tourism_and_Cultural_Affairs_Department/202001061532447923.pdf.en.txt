Preamble" Aarambh Pratishthan" Dombivali organizes" Talsangram" state level drumming competition every year. In the year 2019-20 also, the event will be held on 26th and 27th January, 2020 and the matter of sanctioning expenditure for the event as a" special case" was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION As a special case, a sum of Rs. 2,50,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being sanctioned for the State Level Dhol Taasha Competition" Taalsangram" to be organized by Aarambh Pratishthan, Dombivli from 26th to 27th January, 2020.
