Government decision: With the approval of the Cabinet of Ministers, it has been decided to create a Directorate of Tourism in the State for effective planning and implementation of the Tourism Policy of the Government as per the Government decision dated 12.4.2016. Further, as per the Government Decisions dated 14.7.2016 and 1.11.2017, 55 new posts have been approved to be created on the establishment of Directorate of Tourism.
The Director, Directorate of Tourism, Maharashtra State Office, Mumbai is being declared as" Head of Office and Head of Department" and the Joint Director, Directorate of Tourism, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer." Further, in the main article, heading 3452, Demand No. The Chief Accounts Officer, Directorate of Tourism, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the works related to Directorate of Tourism for ZD-4. The order will be limited to financial matters.
