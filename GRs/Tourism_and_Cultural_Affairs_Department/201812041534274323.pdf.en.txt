Introduction: Bharat Ratna Pt. The scheme of conferring Bhimsen Joshi Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for Classical Music has been announced by the Government vide its decision dated 30th January, 2013. The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh and a citation.
2018-19. Bhimsen Joshi Award for lifetime achievement in classical music It was held on 29th October, 2018 under the chairmanship of Minister, Cultural Affairs. As per the recommendation of the dignitaries in the meeting, Bharat Ratna Pt. The Government of India has decided to confer the Bhimsen Joshi Award for lifetime achievement in classical music for the year 2018-2019.
Government Decision: For the year 2018-2019, Bharat Ratna Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Lifetime Achievement Award Keshavrao Ginde is being announced.
