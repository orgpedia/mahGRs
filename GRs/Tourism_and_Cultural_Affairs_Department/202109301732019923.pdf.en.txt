Introduction: The aforementioned No. As per the letters received, from 12 th March, 2021 onwards, various programmes have been organized in the State under the Central Government scheme of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" to commemorate 75 years of Independence.
As of August 11, 2021. The Chief Secretary chaired a meeting with the Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries of various Ministries regarding the organization of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" i. e." Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav." In the meeting, it was suggested that this festival should be organized by organizing 75 programs across the state in 75 weeks till August 15, 2022, as suggested by the Central Government on the occasion of the 75th year of independence. The government was considering the use of the logo on various media in government correspondence and other places in accordance with the government's intention to spread and publicize the program using maximum media.
Government Decision: Under the scheme of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" i. e." Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the logo as specified in Annexure' A" should be used in all official communications of the Government as well as in other places/ various media.
