The Proposal:
The Agro Tourism Policy of the State has been approved as per the decision of the Government in the context of achieving tourism development by creating a well-planned system to provide supplementary business to the farmers of the State and to enable the urban tourists to enjoy tourism by staying in peaceful, natural places. In order to promote agro-tourism in the state, there will be a state-level' Maharashtra Agro-Tourism and Rural Tourism Development Committee' for publicity, publicity and to solve the problems. As per this provision, a state-level' Maharashtra Agriculture Tourism and Rural Tourism Development Committee' was to be set up.
Government's decision:
The state government has decided to set up a state-level' Maharashtra Agro Tourism and Rural Tourism Development Committee' to promote agro-tourism in the state.

Functions of the Committee:
(i) Review of the Agriculture Tourism Policy from time to time.
2) Suggest suitable changes in the agro-tourism policy.
3) Coordination with various departments of the Central and State Governments related to agri-tourism policy.
Research and development in agriculture tourism.
5. Suggest various options of financing to empower Agro Tourism Centres.
6) Creation of model agro-tourism centres through agricultural universities and agricultural colleges.
7) To prepare a skill development plan for agritourism centres.
8) To coordinate and promote agro-tourism among the organizations working at the divisional and state levels.
9) To make efforts to implement successful initiatives related to agro-tourism in Maharashtra at the national/ international level.
10) To attract foreign tourists by giving wide publicity to the agro-tourism centres of the State at national/ international level.
To take the agriculture tourism of Maharashtra to national/ international level by participating in exhibitions, conferences related to agriculture tourism at national/ international level.
