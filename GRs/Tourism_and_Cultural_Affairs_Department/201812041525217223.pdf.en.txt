Introduction: The scheme of conferring the award in the name of Natavarya Prabhakar Panshikar to a veteran theatre artist for his outstanding performance in theatre has been started as per the decision of the Government dated 2nd March, 2006. The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh and a citation.
The selection committee for the Natavarya Prabhakar Panashekar Rangabhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2018-19 held a meeting at Shri. It was held on 29th October, 2018 under the chairmanship of Minister, Cultural Affairs. As per the recommendation of the dignitaries in the meeting, the government was considering to announce the Natavarya Prabhakar Panashikar Rangabhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2018-2019.
Government's decision:
Shri Jayant Savarkar has been conferred with the Natvarya Prabhakar Panshikar Rangbhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Award for the year 2018-2019.
