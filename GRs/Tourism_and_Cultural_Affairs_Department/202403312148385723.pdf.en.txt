The Proposal: -
Under the "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," a public convenience centre will be set up at the site of monuments under the Archaeological Survey of India. Therefore, the Department of Archaeology, Pune has decided to set up a Public Facilitation Centre in the protected area of 12 monuments under Reference No. As per the Government Decision of 01, Rs. 12,70,48, 114 / - has been granted administrative approval. The Department of Archaeology, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar has decided to set up a Public Facilitation Centre in the protected area of 13 monuments under Reference No. As per the Government Decision of 02, a sum of Rs. 11,97,89, 081 / - has been granted administrative approval. Also, the Department of Archaeology, Nanded has proposed to set up a Public Facilitation Centre in the protected area of 10 monuments under Reference No. As per the Government Decision of 03, Rs. 8,10,21, 601 / - has been granted administrative approval. For this, the concerned Assistant Director, Department of Archaeology has signed an MoU with Sulabh International Sodal Service Organization. Construction of Public Facilitation Centres at monuments under the Archaeological Survey of India, Pune, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and Nanded with 15% implementation fee.
