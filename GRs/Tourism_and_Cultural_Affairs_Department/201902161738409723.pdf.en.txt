The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi was established on 9th March 1982 by the Government of Maharashtra for the promotion and encouragement of Hindi literature. To implement various schemes for the betterment of Hindi language through this academy and in connection with the implementation of that scheme, the department's aforesaid No. The guidelines have been prepared in accordance with the decisions of the government under reference 1 and 2. The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi has announced the names of awardees for the year 2017-2018.
