4) Care should be taken to ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned subsidy under any circumstances. 7) Every scheme in respect of which funds are being made available shall require the approval of the competent administrator and the financial authority in accordance with the return of the financial authority.
8) Every such plan shall be subject to such procedures as may be required by financial regulations with respect to construction or purchase or service supply contracts.
