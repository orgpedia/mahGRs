The Asian Film Festival is organised by The Asian Film Foundation, Mumbai. This institution has given. 14.12.2018 to dt. A proposal for funding for the 17th 3rd Asian Film Festival to be held during 20.12.2018 has been submitted. the above decision of the Government. Policy for funding of International Film Festival Organizations has been announced on 18.10.2012. Pursuant to this policy, as per the proposal submitted to The Asian Film Foundation, Mumbai, under the Reference Letter for the 17th Third Eye Asian Film Festival, the Government has sanctioned Rs. 10.00 lakh (Rupees Ten Lakh only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
Conditions:
1.The Cultural Affairs Department of the Government of Maharashtra should be given maximum publicity in the advertisement to be made for this festival. (Placards/ banners, display of Maharashtra Government logo in advertisements etc.) 2. Local public representatives are required to be invited for the festival.
The jury of the festival must include representatives of the government.
4. The grant sanctioned shall be non-recurring and subject to the terms and conditions of the regular grant of the Government.
5. Utilization certificate in respect of this grant shall be required to be submitted within one month.
(6) The grant shall be utilised only for the purpose for which it is sanctioned and immediately. If not, the subsidy amount will have to be returned to the government at the prevailing bank interest rate.
7. The cost of the festival should be certified by a chartered accountant and the details of the deposit should be submitted to the government.
The Government shall have full powers to modify, amend or interpret all the above criteria/ conditions and the orders passed by the Government in this regard shall be final and binding on the concerned. In case of dispute between the two parties, the decision of the Principal Secretary (Cultural Affairs) shall be final and binding on both the parties. However, no party is allowed to approach the court if arbitration is appointed.
The Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being authorized to enter into an agreement with the above terms and conditions.
