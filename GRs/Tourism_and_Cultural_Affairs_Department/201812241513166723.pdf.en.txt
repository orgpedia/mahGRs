Preamble: The Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India is organizing an orientation programme" Know India Programme" (KIP) to create awareness about Indian cultural heritage and its contemporary aspects among the youth of Indian origin in the age group of 18 to 30 years abroad. The 53rd edition of Know India Programme is being organized by the Government of India from 20th January 2019 to 13th February 2019. About 40 youth from India and abroad are expected to participate in the programme. The Know India Programme will be held in Maharashtra from 24th to 28th January, 2019. During this period, the teams will visit places like industrial establishments in Maharashtra, historical tourist places in Maharashtra and Jalyukt Shivar. A proposal in this regard has been sent to the central government. The government had approved the cost of the programme.
Government's decision:
