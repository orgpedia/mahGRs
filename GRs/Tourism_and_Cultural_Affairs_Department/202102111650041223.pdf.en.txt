In pursuance of the development works related to the Construction Department, Archaeology Department and Forest Department
The proposal was approved with the following recommendations: Be sure to follow these instructions carefully.
1) Public Works Department: As the roads proposed by the Public Works Department are in the possession of the District Council, the said roads should be transferred to the Public Works Department and the road improvement works should be done. The period of liability for defect in the construction of roads should be at least five years as per the norms of the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana.
2) Archaeology Department: While working in this department, work should be done in order to preserve the ancient forts. Since the current budget presented by the Department of Antiquities has been made in a smooth manner, the works should be taken up by preparing a detailed budget and the amount of the detailed budget is Rs. The limit is Rs 7.5 crore.
Forest Department: Rs. The works amounting to Rs. 4.99 crore were approved in principle by the Committee. The forest department while doing the work of the park should consider the trees of less water consuming species only without proposing lawns in order to plan the work on the lines of forest park. Apart from this, it should be planned that there will be a sustainable forest garden by developing small dams and rainwater harvesting in the area of the fort.
4) The work in the Shivneri Fort Conservation and Area Development should be planned on the lines of the Revenue Model so that the project becomes self-sustainable.
3. The provision in paragraph 13 of the Government Resolution dated 4/05/2020 of the Finance Department in Reference No. 2 for the said development works is a special case subject to the approval of the Finance Department.
Therefore, the technical approval, administrative approval, and relaxation are being given to these development works.
Tenders, commissioning orders and other ancillary activities are being approved.
