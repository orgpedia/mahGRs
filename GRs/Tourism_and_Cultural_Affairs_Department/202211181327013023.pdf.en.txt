Introduction: The Academy had invited an open competitive e-tender for leasing out of its ground floor restaurant at P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai for a period of 5 years. A total of eight companies submitted bids in the e-tendering process. Out of these, 1 institution was found ineligible in the technical examination. The commercial bids of 7 institutions were opened. The highest (H1) monthly base price among these 7 institutions is Rs. 7,02,000/- and an annual increment of 1% (for 5 years, a total of Rs. 4,29,70, 866/-) to Preeti Caterers for renting out the hotel for 5 years. The proposal has been sent to the government for approval. The proposal is under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The ground floor restaurant at the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai, has the highest (H1) monthly base price of Rs. 7,02,000/- (Rs. seven lakh two thousand only) and those giving 1% annual increment (for 5 years total Rs.
4,29,70, 866/- (Rs. Four crore thirty-nine lakh seventy-eight thousand sixty-six only) is hereby approved to be leased out to Preeti Caterers for a period of five years.
Project Director, P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai should sign the necessary agreement with Preeti Caterers and send a copy of it to this department.
Project Director, P.L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai to obtain necessary bank guarantee from Preeti Caterers as per the provisions of the agreement.
