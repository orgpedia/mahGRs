Read:- Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums Letter No. Monuments/ 2023/2724, Dy. 13.10.2023
The proposal: It has been decided to take up various LokYogi Namo 11 point programme under MODIFY@73 on the occasion of 73rd birthday of the Prime Minister. A total of 11 missions of different departments have been selected under this 11-point programme. The Department of Cultural Affairs has been appointed as the nodal department for the Namo Shrine Improvement Campaign. Under this engineering, 73 holy and historical religious places are to be selected for improvement. The 73 locations include:
1. Renovation of historical and ancient temples 2. Providing basic amenities 3. Digital Darshan 4. beautification of premises 5. Sanitation
Under this campaign, 73 sites need to be identified immediately. The Directorate of Archaeology and Museums has selected 73 historical sites and ancient temples under the NaMo 11-point programme and informed the government. The list includes historical temples, barawas, ponds. Under the Namo 11-point programme, the government was considering to approve the work of these 73 historical sites and temples.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the Namo 11 point programme, approval is being given to take up works of 73 historical sites, temples as indicated in the accompanying Form-A.
Directions regarding implementation of NAMO-11 are as follows: The Directorate of Archaeology and Museums should immediately prepare a budget/ plan for taking up the works.
2) At 73 places, information about these structures should be displayed on the notice board with QR code. Also, digital and physical maps should be prepared.

A committee of at least 5 devotees should be formed for the proper maintenance and supervision of each selected temple.
(4) The Committee shall make arrangements to visit the temple once in every fifteen days.
5) Prepare a schedule of activities. It is planned to be completed by 31.08.2024.
6) A web portal should be created for the implementation of this program. Every seven days, a report of the work done, a report in the form of video and photographs should be uploaded on this web portal.
7) Appropriate action should be taken to help the students of the College of Architects in this work.
The conservation and preservation of these 73 historical sites and temples will be undertaken by the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums. The expenditure on these works should be met from the funds sanctioned under the Regular Plan, District Annual Plan, Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav.
Remaining works shown under NaMo 11 Point Programme viz. Providing basic facilities, digital darshan, beautification of the premises, cleanliness etc. should be taken up by the Tourism Department as per the requirement.
In this regard, the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums in coordination with the Department of Tourism should provide information about the works to be undertaken at 73 historical sites and temple sites. Also, the tourism department should take up these works in consultation with the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums.
