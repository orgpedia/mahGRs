Minutes of the meeting of the Maharashtra Bhushan Award Committee dated 31st January, 2023
Introduction: The aforementioned No. Maharashtra Bhushan Award is given to the eminent person who has done the highest work in any field in the state of Maharashtra.
The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 10.00 lakh (Rupees Ten Lakh), a citation, a memento and a shawl. The aforementioned No. 2 for the Maharashtra Bhushan Award. The decision to increase the award amount was taken at the meeting of the committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister on 31 January 2023. Accordingly, the matter of approving the increase in the award amount was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the amount of Maharashtra Bhushan Award has been increased from Rs. 10.00 lakh to Rs. 25.00 lakh.
The decision will be implemented from the end of 2021.
