Introduction: - A temporary STATA "RRNA" exhibition will be held at Laxmi Vilas Palace, Kolhapur Museum, the birthplace of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj. The exhibition will feature an international-standard display of the then fragile, precious scriptures from the museum as well as the late Girish Jadhav's collection. The exhibition system needs to include all international aspects including CCTV system, air-conditioning system, protection of TRAM artifacts, crowd management of visitors to the exhibition, clock room, etc. to create a well-equipped science gallery.
