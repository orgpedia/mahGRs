The Proposal:
The Government have accorded administrative approval for the Light Illumination of 13 important and famous historical buildings/ structures in the city of Mumbai at a cost of Rs. 28,71,67, 352/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Crores One Hundred and Seventy Seven Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand Fifty Two only) vide Government Order dated 13.09.2021 in Reference No. 4,98,42, 612/-. The High Court was considering revoking the administrative approval of this work and giving revised administrative approval to this work.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Directorate of Tourism to the Government vide letter dated 10.05.2022 in Reference No. 2, cancelling the administrative approval of the work of" High Court" at serial No. 1 in the Government Decision at Reference No. 1, the Government has decided to cancel the administrative approval of the Government of Tamil Nadu. Revised Administrative Sanction for Light Illumination of Bombay High Court Building for a sum of Rs. 8,94,52, 473/- (Rupees Eight Crores Ninety Four Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand Four Hundred and Seventy Three only) as mentioned in the table below.
