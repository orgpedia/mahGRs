Preamble: On the occasion of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," various programmes are being organized. This programme is being organized to honour the art and art related personalities on the basis of their diamond, golden, centenary year and to promote and disseminate the art and art work to the young artists. Against this backdrop, the government was considering giving administrative and financial approval to the cultural program and the expenses of the event to celebrate the birth anniversary of Ram Ganesh Gadkari and make his work known.
Government's decision:
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Ram Ganesh Gadkari, Shri. The administrative and financial sanction for the cultural programmes to be held at Vasai and the expenditure involved in organizing the same is being provided under this Government Resolution for an amount of Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh only) as mentioned in the table below.
