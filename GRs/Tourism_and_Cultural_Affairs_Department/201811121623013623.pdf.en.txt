The Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016 has been approved vide Government Resolution, Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, No.-TDC 2015/11/ PR No. 1021/ Tourism, dated 4.5.2016. Eligibility factors have been provided in the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016 as per para- 5.5. The classification and criteria of mega projects are given in para 6 of Economic Incentive No. 6.1.1. Accordingly, the fixed capital investment in Indian Rupees for the" C" category area classification of the mega project is Rs. 25.00 crore, direct employment generation is 75 and the completion period is 48 months.
As per Para 6 of the Tourism Policy 2016, the Ministry of Tourism has decided to grant more incentives/ concessions and relax the terms and conditions for recognition of tourism projects as Mega Project Unit and Ultra Mega Project. A high-powered committee headed by the Chief Secretary has been constituted.
The meeting of the High Powered Committee was held on 21.8.2018 to decide on the Mega Project Unit for Alibaug Resorts and Hotels LLP. In the meeting, the high-powered committee, Alibaug Resorts and Hotels LLP Tourism Project has been approved as a Mega Project Unit under the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016. In accordance with this. The proposal to grant the status of Mega Project Unit to Alibaug Resorts and Hotels LLP Tourism Project as per the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016 was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Alibaug Resorts and Hotels LLP The five-star hotel tourism project will be set up on 24 acres of land in Alibaug, Raigad district. The proposed capital investment in the project is approximately Rs. 500.00 crore and will generate employment for 450 persons. Considering this, the project is fulfilling the criteria of a mega project as per the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016. It is a part of the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016. Considering the decisions of the High Powered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting dated 21.8.2018, the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA), chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, has decided to constitute a High Powered Committee to review the situation in the country. The Government has decided to grant the status of Mega Project Unit to the five star hotel tourism project as per the Maharashtra Tourism Project 2016 subject to the following conditions: a) The project is declared as Mega Project with 10% enhancement in IPS and a two year extension in the eligibility period as prescribed in the policy.
b) Undertaking would need to approach UD and competent planning authority separately for resolution of issues pertaining to FSI and height restrictions.
In case of violation of the norms or terms/ conditions/ rules laid down under the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016, the approval given to the above project as a Mega Project Unit as per the Maharashtra Tourism Policy 2016 will be automatically cancelled.
The approval granted as a Mega Project Unit as per the Maharashtra Tourism Policy, 2016 shall not be a substitute for the approval of permits/ no-objection certificates/ permissions, etc. required under any other Act, Rules, etc.
