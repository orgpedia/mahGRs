Terms and Conditions: -
1) The procedure prescribed under the financial rules and various regulations should be followed while spending from the funds disbursed.
2) The entire funds provided will be recovered along with interest if any future violation of the Terms and Bars of the Regional Tourism Development Plan, Government Decision, dated 4th November, 2010, is noticed.
3) Care should be taken to ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned subsidy under any circumstances. Expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant shall be the responsibility of the District Collector concerned and no additional grant shall be made available against the excess cost.
4) The expenditure from the sanctioned provision should be incurred by 31st March, 2025 and the sanctioned works should be completed immediately.
5) Plaques should be put up at the workplace stating that the work was done under the Regional Tourism Development Scheme.
6) Every scheme in respect of which funds are being made available shall require the approval of the competent administrator and the financial authority in accordance with the return of the financial authority.
7) It will be mandatory to follow the rules of the Public Works Department in cases of proposed works of roads.
8) Ensure that the said provision is not used for non-plan work (maintenance / repair etc.).
9) It shall be mandatory to obtain a No Objection Certificate from the said Devasthan / Institution in respect of such works and other works which are to be carried out at the site of the Devasthan / Institute. Also, on completion of such work, it will be mandatory for the Collector to enter into an agreement with the concerned Devasthan / Institution regarding the maintenance / repair to be carried out by the concerned institution.
