Introduction: Every year, the Ministry of Defence, Government of India organises a tableau parade on Republic Day. Each state is involved in this event. The state of Maharashtra presents various themes related to the tableau to be displayed at the New Delhi Tableau Parade every year. This year, a painting exhibition on the theme "Inspiration of Indian Democracy: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj" will be held across the state. During the curtain raiser of the Maharashtra State Film Festival in New Delhi on Republic Day, Shri. The President, Shri. The Vice President, Shri.
