INTERNATIONAL BUDDHIST CONCLAVE 2018 is being organized by the Government of India from 22nd to 25th August, 2018. Maharashtra has been included in this. Therefore, the Conclave will be held on 23.8.2018 at Aurangabad.
In this context, the government was considering to approve the expenses on the Actual Expenditure Basis for the organization of the festival and the expenses on the accommodation, food, reception, vehicle, security, medical facilities and other necessary ancillary arrangements for the delegates coming to this conclave.
Government Decision: To promote the development and promotion of tourism destinations in the State of Maharashtra at national and international level and to celebrate various tourism festivals, an outlay of Rs. 78.00 crore has been budgeted for the Department of Tourism for the financial year 2018-2019 under the head" Publicity." finance
As per the Circular dated 2.4.2018 of the Department, 70% of the budget outlay i.e. Rs. 54.60 crore is available for disbursement at the end of the month of December, 2018 as per the Monthly Fund Distribution System.
The following expenditure is being approved from the said amount of Rs. 54.60 crore subject to a ceiling of Rs. 2.00 crore (Rs.2.00 crore only) for the purpose of organizing the 6th International Buddhist Conclave 2018 at Aurangabad from 22nd to 25th August, 2018:

The Government hereby approves payment of Rs. 2.00 crore only (Rupees Two Crores only) to the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: (1) The Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation shall bear the cost of the said tour by following the prescribed procedure. It should be done with the approval of the Minister (Tourism) and the Chairman, Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation. The funds not earmarked for specific purposes will be used with the approval of the government.
2) The Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation should spend the above mentioned amount from the fund sanctioned for publicity.
(3) Competent administrative and financial approval is required to be obtained in respect of each scheme in respect of which funds are being made available, subject to the reversion of financial powers.
4) The procedure prescribed under the Financial Rules and various Regulations should be followed while spending from the funds disbursed.
5) Under no circumstances will the expenditure be more than the sanctioned grant, the entire responsibility for this will be of the Managing Director, Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation and the excess expenditure will not be reimbursed by the government.
(6) Every such scheme shall be governed by the procedures required under the financial rules relating to construction or procurement or contract for supply of services. In this regard, the instructions of the Government Circular, Finance Department, No. 1002/ PR No. 15/ Treasurer No. 5, dated 6.2.2002 shall be strictly followed.
