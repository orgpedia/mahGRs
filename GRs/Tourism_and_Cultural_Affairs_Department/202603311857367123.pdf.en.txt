The Proposal: -
The office of the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums, Mumbai is located in the Fort George Fort, a protected monument. The office has a special collection room, which holds coins of various denominations received from the fund. For example, for installing a fire-resistant storage cabinet to keep these historic coins safe and sound, an amount of Rs. 73,91,520 -- This amount will be credited to the specified number. Administrative approval is granted under section 1. Regarding the provision of funds for this work, the Directorate has issued a notification in the form of the aforesaid No. The proposal has been submitted under letter 2 here. Accordingly, approval was accorded for disbursement of funds for the purpose.
The issue of payment was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Under this Government Decision, the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums, Mumbai is approving the release of funds @ ₹73,91,520 - (Rupees Seventy Three Lakh Ninety One Thousand Five Hundred and Twenty Only) (inclusive of all taxes) for the installation of 18 Fire Resisting Storage Cabinets to preserve and maintain the historical coins of various denominations housed in the Special Collection Room (Long Room) at Fort George Fort.
The Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums is the Controlling Officer for the same.
So being declared and he or the officer authorised by him is being declared as the Drawing and Distributing Officer.
