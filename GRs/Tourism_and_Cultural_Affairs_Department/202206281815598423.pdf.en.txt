Introduction: All Sindhi speakers are scattered all over India and they are also minorities. Sindhi is not the official language of any state. Therefore, the Sindhi Sahitya Academy has been established in the state for the promotion and encouragement of Sindhi literature.
The aforementioned No. The appointments of non-official members to the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy appointed under section 3 of the said Act shall be made in accordance with the aforesaid No. 4 have been cancelled by the government. The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy is being restructured and the following appointments are being made.
(a) Member of Parliament 1. Minister of Cultural Affairs 2. Ratan Chawla, Nashik Member 6. Dr. S. K. Punshi, Amravati Member 7. Ang Gauri Chhabria, Mumbai Member

The term of office of the Chairperson and Members of the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government Resolution or until the Committee is reconstituted, whichever is earlier. The President, Vice-President, Secretary, Department of Cultural Affairs and Joint Director and Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy will continue to function of the Akademi even if the appointment of non-official members is terminated. The Academy will continue to exist.
The Academy Office will be responsible for organizing various programs of the Academy. The officers of the Academy will be responsible for organizing the program and spending the funds as per the government rules and prescribed procedures under the guidance and cooperation of the Executive President and members of the Academy.
Travel and Meeting Allowance:- The non-official members of the Committee are entitled to the financial assistance of Rs.
