The Proposal:
Under the Regional Tourism Development Scheme for providing infrastructure facilities at tourist places, in the year 2018-19, under the decision of the Government in Reference No.1, Rs. The administrative sanction for the works amounting to Rs. 1298.76 lakhs was stayed till further orders by the Government vide its decision at Reference No. 2. According to the report submitted by the Directorate of Tourism through the letter at reference number 3 and 4, the government was considering to start the work by lifting the suspension of some works and cancel the administrative approval of some works.
Government's decision:
In respect of the works which have been suspended in the year 2018-19 under Regional Tourism Development Scheme by giving administrative approval under the Government decision at Reference No. 1, till further orders under the Government decision at Reference No. 2, the Government hereby approves to take further action on the commencement of the works by lifting the suspension of the works in Table' A' below and revoking the administrative approval of the works in Table' B' as per the report submitted by the Directorate of Tourism under the letter at Reference No. 3 and 4.
