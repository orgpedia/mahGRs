Preamble: Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," various activities/ programmes are being organized by the Department on a large scale. The purpose of these programs is to make the guests of not only the state but also of other states know the culture of Maharashtra. In the same background, for the members of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Administrative Affairs, the Department of Cultural Affairs has issued a notification for the promotion and dissemination of folk art of the state. On 5th July, 2023, a cultural program and soft music of Maharashtra was presented (during dinner) at Hotel Trident. Administrative and financial sanction for the programme and its expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative and financial approval is being given under this Government decision for organizing a cultural program" Maharashtra's Folklore" and soft music at Hotel Trident on 5th July, 2023 at a cost of Rs. 12,50,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh Fifty Thousand only).
