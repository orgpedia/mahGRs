Read:- Letter from the Directorate of Cultural Affairs No. 2947, dated May 18, 2023.
Preamble: Various programmes/ activities are being undertaken across the country to mark Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav. The objective of this programme is to make the history of great personalities, revolutionaries and various revolutionary organizations contributing to the Indian freedom struggle known to the general public, especially the younger generation. On the same lines, the government was considering to give administrative and financial approval to the expenses of the video presentation of the program" Krantiveer's Amrit Gatha" through the audio-visual medium of the history of the Indian freedom struggle and revolutionaries.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, an amount of Rs. 6.00 lakh (Rupees Six Lakh only) has been sanctioned towards administrative and financial sanction for the video presentation of" Krantiveeranchi Amritgatha," an audio-visual presentation on the history of the revolutionaries of the Indian freedom struggle.
The said expenditure should be met from the financial provision approved under the head ZD-2, 2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Promotion, Various Festivals and Other Programmes (2205 1507)-50 for the year 2023-24.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
4. The said expenditure is covered by the Government Decision No. VIPR-2013/ EXCHANGE, dated the 17th April, 2015, Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-section-3, S. No. 4, Para No. 27 (2) (a) is being sanctioned as per the powers conferred on the administrative department.
