The Proposal: -
Under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, Ma. The Core Committee headed by the Minister (S.K.) According to the reference letter at the meeting on 06.02.2024, a sum of Rs. A fund of 25.00 lakh has been approved.
There are a total of 387 state protected monuments and 13 museums in the state under the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums. These state protected monuments are preserved and conserved under the Maharashtra Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1960. Works are carried out under the Directorate through the Divisional Offices of the Assistant Director, Ratnagiri, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Nanded, Nagpur as well as the Divisional Custodian's Offices for Museums. The conservation of state protected monuments has been undertaken on a large scale. Also, 73 historical sites / temples have been taken up under the Namo-11 programme. A proposal has been sent for UNESCO World Nomination for Maratha Military Architecture, Sea Forts, and Crafts. Of these, the Government of India has sent a proposal for a UNESCO World Nomination of the Maratha Military Establishment to UNESCO. Similarly, Mahavarsa, Vaibhav Nurturing Scheme are also being implemented by the Directorate. It has become extremely necessary to create a web portal for the regulation of various activities / works undertaken by the Directorate. The government was considering allowing the creation of a web portal to monitor and control all work under the Directorate of Subsidiary Services by making the process online.
