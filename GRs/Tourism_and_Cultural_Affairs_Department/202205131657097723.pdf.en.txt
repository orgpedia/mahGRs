Introduction: The aforementioned No. As per the Government decision dated 22nd January, 2021, the Theatre Screening Committee (Professional, Musical and Experimental) has been reconstituted under the Grant-in-Aid Scheme for New Theatre Productions to Theatre Producing Organizations. The government was contemplating to include some new members in the committee as non-official members.
Government Decision: Under the Scheme of Grant-in-Aid for New Theatre Productions to Theatre Producing Organizations, the Theatre Screening Committee (Professional, Musical and Experimental) has been reconstituted vide Government Decision dated 22nd January, 2021. The following new members are being approved as non-official members of the committee.
