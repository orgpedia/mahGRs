Introduction: The aforementioned No. The decision of the government here has been approved to implement the cultural policy of the state of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the aforesaid No. The committee was formed to review the implementation of the cultural policy of the state of Maharashtra under section 2 and 3. The policy also proposes to revisit the policy every five years, taking into account the changing nature of cultural aspects and suggestions received from experts in the field and all common citizens. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of revising the implementation of the policy and setting a new date to suggest changes in the policy as per the need.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to review the implementation of the Maharashtra State Cultural Policy 2010 and to suggest changes to the Government as per the need, the aforesaid No. This Government has decided to abolish the Committee constituted under the Government Decisions No. 2 and No. 3 and to constitute the following Committee.

The mandate of the Committee is to review the implementation of the Cultural Policy, the nature of cultural matters change with time. Therefore, to suggest suitable changes in the cultural policy according to the changing situation, to suggest additions and to brainstorm and submit the draft policy to the government according to the suggestions received in the context of the policy from experts in various fields and the general public.
Meetings of the Committee: Meetings of the Committee shall be held once in three months.
Duration of the Committee:- 1 year from the date of issue of the decision by this Government or until the Government issues an order, whichever is earlier.
