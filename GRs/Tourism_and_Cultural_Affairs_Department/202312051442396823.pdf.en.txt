GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MAPUVI 2023/ P.No.204/ S.K.2
Decision of the Government: 6691 sq.m. land of Directorate of Archives at Bandra for Mumbai Metro Line-3, Colaba Bandra- SIPZ Project has been allotted to Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for temporary transfer of the same for a period of 3 years subject to the following terms and conditions. The deadline has been extended to December 31, 2023.
6691 sq.m. of land belonging to the Directorate of Archives can be allotted for the project till 31st December 2023.
If the free space is given to the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRCL) for use for about 2 years, the responsibility of freeing up the space required by the department at the time when the actual construction of the building of the Directorate of Archives begins in these two years will be with the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRCL) and the concerned contractor and other related departments.
In case of any change in the plan prepared by considering the plot of the Directorate of Archives, the other permission given for construction will be retained accordingly or if not possible, the responsibility of giving immediate permission will be with the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRCL) and other concerned departments.
During the actual operation of the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited (MMRCL), the contractor concerned shall submit the details/ report related to the work to the Director, Directorate of Archives, Mumbai from time to time.
After December 31, 2023, the possession of the plot will remain with the Directorate of Archives.
