Introduction: The Theatre Experimentation Board has been constituted under Section 33 (w) (wa) (iii) of the Police Act, 1951 to pre-examine the codes of experiments to be performed on stage. After the preliminary examination of the codes of experiments to be performed in the theater, the theater experiment inspection board gives the certificate of competence to perform the experiments in the place of public entertainment. For pre-examination of the received codes, the above-mentioned reference of eminent persons with experience/ expertise in the field of literature, art, etc. Decisions 1 and 2. 16.12.2020 and D. The appointment was made on 13.10.2021. This appointment was made in reference no. 3 d. The Government decision dated 08.09.2022 has been cancelled. At present, government and other drama competitions are going on all over Maharashtra. Also, since the competition is going to start soon, the rules of the various institutions/ educational institutions/ playwrights, professional plays etc. participating in the competition have been received/ are being received by the Board for examination. Therefore, the matter of appointing non-official members to examine the codes received by the Board until the fresh reorganization of the Theatre Experiment Board was under the consideration of the Government.
The government is now appointing the next members to this board until the Theatre Experiment Inspection Board is reconstituted again to give eligibility certificates after pre-examination of the assistants received due to the government decision to abolish the currently functioning Theatre Board.
