The Proposal: -
A special cabinet meeting was held at Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar on 16.09.2021. The decision included the preservation of historical monuments in the Marathwada region, particularly the development of an ancient architectural site at Charthana, District Parbhani. As per the decision taken in the Hon'ble Cabinet meeting, the conservation and repair work of the State Protected Monument of Ganesha Temple, Charthana, District Parbhani has been taken up at a cost of Rs.
