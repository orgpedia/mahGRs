Introduction: Various experiments were being done on the stage of musical theater at that time. The play" Sangeet Samyukta Manapman," which was in vogue at that time, was presented by Sangeet Surya Keshavrao Bhosale and Balgandharva together.
On the occasion of the completion of 101 years of the' Sangeet Sanyukt Manapman' theatre experiment and to commemorate the memory of Keshavrao Bhosale and Balgandharva, a cultural program was organized on 7th July, 2023 at Ravindra Natya Mandir, Prabhadevi, Mumbai. The said programme was accorded administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 1 above. However, financial sanction for the programme and its expenditure was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, financial approval is being given for an amount of Rs. 20.00 lakh (Rupees Twenty Lakh only) for the cultural program organized on the occasion of the completion of 101 years of" Sangeet Samyukta Manapman" at Ravindra Natya Mandir, Prabhadevi, Mumbai on 7th July, 2023.
The expenditure is to be met from the financial provision approved for the year 2023-24 under the head of Accounting Demand No.ZD-2, 2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Conservation, Various Festivals and Other Programmes (Programmes) (2205 1507)-50.
