Introduction: On the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, various cultural programmes and activities are being organized by the department. In the same background, modern technological approaches are required to bring dynamism in administrative work.
Therefore, considering the number of young generation participating in the state drama competition, it was necessary to change the entry system of the drama competition. On the same lines, the Government was considering administrative approval for the expenditure incurred on inviting entries for the Maharashtra State Drama Competition, 2023-24 through online mode.
Government Decision: All the Maharashtra State Drama Competitions to be organized by the Directorate of Cultural Affairs under the Department of Cultural Affairs, the cost of creating a portal for inviting entries through online mode is Rs. 6,32,244/- (Rupees Six Lakh Thirty Two Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Four only).
