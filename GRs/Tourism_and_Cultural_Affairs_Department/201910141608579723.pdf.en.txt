INTRODUCTION: The incomplete work of the experimental theatre on the 5th and 6th floor and the multipurpose hall on the 7th floor of the Ravindra Natya Mandir at the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy will be completed on a private-public partnership basis. The tender process has been approved under the Government Decision dated 18th July, 2019. A committee of experts at the level of Project Director, P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai, was being considered to implement and control the process.
