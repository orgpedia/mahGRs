Introduction: Every year, the Ministry of Defence, Government of India organises the Republic Day Parade in New Delhi. The Maharashtra government had joined the Quit India Movement for the Republic Day parade. The matter of approving the expenditure for the construction of the tableau and organising the parade was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The expenditure incurred on the construction and running of the tableau for the Republic Day Celebrations on 26th January, 2019 at New Delhi is Rs. 67,43,000/- (Rupees Sixty Seven Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand only) which is being sanctioned as per the following details.
