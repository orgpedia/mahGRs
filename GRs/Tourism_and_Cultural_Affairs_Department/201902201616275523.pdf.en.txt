The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi was established on 9th March 1982 by the Government of Maharashtra for the promotion and encouragement of Hindi literature. To implement various schemes for the betterment of Hindi language through this academy and in connection with the implementation of that scheme, the department's aforesaid No. Guidelines 1 and 2 have been prepared. The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi has decided to bear the expenses of the award ceremony for the year 2017-2018.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the following expenditure is being approved for the award ceremony of Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy for the year 2017-2018.
