The Proposal:
The rules of the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi for the promotion of Hindi language have been issued in accordance with the government decision at No. 1 above. The award scheme is one of the most important schemes of the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi and under this scheme, literary persons who have done valuable work and contributed substantially in the field of Hindi literature are awarded annually. The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi has announced the names of awardees for the three years 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the names of the awardees mentioned in the returns A, B and C respectively attached with the financial year 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 of Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy are being approved under this Government Decision.
