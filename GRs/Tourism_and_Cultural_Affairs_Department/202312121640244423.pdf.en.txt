Preamble: The minutes of the meeting of the Core Committee constituted under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav held on 5 th December, 2022 are given in the Annexure No. Issued by letter 1. According to the said order, approval has been given to provide funds to the Cultural Affairs Department for the" Shivarajyabhishek- Various Programmes."
The aforementioned No. As per the administrative sanction granted under Sections 2 and 3, the Government was contemplating to grant financial sanction to the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai for the expenditure incurred for publishing the book" Selected Rare Letters of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj" and conducting one-day workshops in the Universities of the State for the promotion and propagation of" Modi Script."
Government Decision: Under an umbrella scheme for Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, financial approval is being given for spending a total of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Lakhs Eight Thousand only) for the 350th Coronation Ceremony for publishing a book titled" Selected Rare Letters of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj" in the form of a coffee table book and Rs. 10,08,000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Lakh Eight Thousand only) for conducting one day workshop in 14 Universities of Maharashtra for" Promotion and Dissemination of Modi Script."
