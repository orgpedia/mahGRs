The proposal:
Directorate of Archaeology and Museums has submitted a proposal for administrative and financial approval of the tender for selection of expert architect agency for preparation of proposal for UNESCO oral and intangible heritage of Ganeshotsav in Maharashtra through e-tendering. The matter of giving administrative and financial approval to the proposal of this minimum tender was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the said Government Decision, the selection of expert architect bodies for preparing the proposal for" Ganeshotsav in Maharashtra" UNESCO Oral and Intangible Heritage will be done through e-tendering. Abha Lamba Associates, Mumbai Administrative and Financial Sanction is being accorded to the following Minimum Tenders for an amount of Rs. 9,90,000/- (Rupees Nine Lakh Ninety Thousand only) (inclusive of all taxes).
