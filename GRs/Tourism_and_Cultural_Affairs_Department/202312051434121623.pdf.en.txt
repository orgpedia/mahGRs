The Proposal:
During the Navratri festival, the administrative approval has been given for the cultural program" Navdurga: Jagar Stree Shakti" and its organizing expenses at a total of 10 places including 6 revenue department headquarters as well as three and a half Shakti Peeths in Kolhapur, Tuljapur, Mahur and Vani.
