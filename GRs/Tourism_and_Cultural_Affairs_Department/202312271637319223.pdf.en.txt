The action taken by the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation with the approval of the High Level Committee for the development of resorts and open spaces of the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation in Mahabaleshwar, Matheran, Harihareshwar, Mithbav (resort+ land), Tadoba, Fardapur, Ganpatipule in terms of tourism through public-private participation is being approved post facto.
The following modalities are being approved for the development of all other open spaces and other properties (resorts and open lands) of Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation (MTDC) received in the tender process on the basis of tourism through public-private partnership: 2.1. The report will be submitted to a high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary.
2.2 The High Level Committee shall have the power to choose the appropriate option from the options suggested by the Project Consultant for development of these properties through public-private partnership.
2.3. The High Level Committee shall have the power to determine the period of concession payable to the developer of the said properties through public-private partnership within the time limit fixed by the Department of Revenue.
2.4. The final approval of the tender holder as qualified and recommended by the High Level Committee as a tourist destination should be done with the approval of the Government.
While implementing the above decisions, the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation shall ensure that the provisions of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 and the Maharashtra Land Revenue (Disposal of Government Land) Rules, 1971, as well as the orders/ circulars issued by the Revenue Department from time to time, are duly complied with and there is no loss of revenue to the Government, while determining the lease rate/ tenure, land use and other ancillary policy matters.
