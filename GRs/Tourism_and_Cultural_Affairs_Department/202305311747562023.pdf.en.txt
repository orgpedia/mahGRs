Preamble: Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Department is organizing various programmes under the regular heading, so that the younger generation gets to know the new and old art forms and also gets a platform to showcase their art forms.
In Maharashtra, along with social awareness, entertainment is also done through various social songs and lectures based on Phule-Shahu-Ambedkari ideologies. With a view to preserve the social heritage and to make art accessible to the folk artists, the Government was considering to give administrative and financial approval for organizing Phule-Shahu-Ambedkari Jalsha in the financial year 2023-2024 and for organizing a symposium on the occasion of the birth centenary of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj and the expenditure incurred on it.
Government Decision: The total expenditure of Rs. 37,90,000/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Lakhs Ninety Thousand only) on the following programmes of organizing Phule-Shahu Ambedkar Jalsha and Seminar on the occasion of the birth centenary of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj in the financial year 2023-2024 is being given administrative and financial approval under this Government Decision as per the following details.
