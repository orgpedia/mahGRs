Preventing sexual harassment of women in the workplace. Writ Petition No. 666-670/1992 was filed in the Supreme Court. In it.
The Supreme Court has stayed the implementation of the Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. It was released on December 9, 2013. The Department of Women and Child Development has issued instructions to form a committee to investigate complaints of sexual harassment under the provisions of the Act and recommend action accordingly. 2 decisions have been made by the government. Reference No. 4 is here. The Women Grievance Redressal Committee was constituted vide order dated 13.12.2016. The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
a) To take cognizance of complaints of sexual harassment of women officers/ employees of the department.
(b) to make recommendations to the Principal Secretary from time to time to inquire into the complaint and take disciplinary action against the concerned, if any.
(c) submit an annual report to the Secretary of State.
