The proposal m. The birth anniversary of former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi is observed as" Sadbhavna Diwas" or Goodwill Day on August 20 in the country.
On this occasion, the Directorate organized programmes at 6 locations in Mumbai from 20th August to 5th September, 2019. The matter of post-facto approval of the expenses incurred for organizing these cultural programs was under the consideration of the government.
