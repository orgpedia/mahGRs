The Proposal:
For the promotion and operation of tourist places in the district and for the growth of tourists, the government in collaboration with the district administration at the district level has issued Decision No. As per the provisions of TDC 2010/1/ PR No. 22/ Tourism, dated 04.02.2010, a sum of Rs. 5.00 lakh is made available to the concerned administration for organizing Tourism and Cultural Festivals. However, considering the price hike/ inflation in the remuneration of the artists and all other components since 2010 till date, as well as the nature of the festival and the total cost of the event, it was contemplated to increase the funds available for tourism and cultural festivals organized in collaboration with the district administration at the district level from Rs. 5.00 lakh to Rs. 10.00 lakh and to implement these festivals through the Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of Government Resolution No. 1 dated 04.02.2010, the Government hereby grants approval for the conduct of Tourism and Cultural Festivals at the District level in association with the Directorate of Tourism and the District Administration and to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 10.00 lakh each against the expenditure of Rs. 5.00 lakh on the said Festivals subject to the following terms and conditions. :
The tourism and cultural festivals to be organized every year should be included in the annual publicity action plan (Media Plan) to be approved with the consent of the Planning and Finance Department for that financial year.

2) Organizations receiving sponsorship funds for the tourism and cultural festival to be organized in collaboration with the district administration at the district level should be given the necessary publicity through the festival.
3) The festival should be organized through local level service-oriented organizations, private organizations, as well as program coordinators appointed by the government.
4) The concerned Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism shall act as the implementing agency for the implementation of the festival.
5) Only one district-level tourism and cultural festival should be held in a district in a year.
6) Tourism and cultural festivals should be held at important tourist places in the district.
Tourism and cultural festivals should include local artists, local cultural troupes and self-help groups selling local products.
8) Local resorts, hotels, accommodation and breakfast centres, agro-tourism centres, home dining centres, tour operators, travel agents, guides should be involved in the festival as per requirement.
9) The festival should be well filmed and used for publicity.
All other terms and conditions, except the financial limit in the Government Decision, dated 04.02.2010 in Reference No. 1, shall also be applicable for the organization of district-level tourism and cultural festivals.
