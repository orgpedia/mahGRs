Preamble: The Government has received requests from various associations/ institutions in the sector to allow the opening of cinema halls which have been closed in the wake of COVID-19. With the Covid-19 outbreak hampering the country's economic progress, the government has eased restrictions on the opening of various industries in a phased manner to boost the industry. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines for the opening of cinemas in the state in a controlled manner was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions in this regard:
Government Decision: In the wake of COVID-19, cinema halls in the state will be allowed to open subject to the guidelines in Annexure-A attached with this government decision. The government is giving approval to start in a controlled manner from November 5, 2020.
Considering the noble intention of the government behind granting this permission, it is necessary for all concerned to operate the cinema halls in a manner that does not violate any of the restrictions of the Central Government and the State Government regarding COVID-19. Action will be taken against those found violating these guidelines.
The instructions contained in the order issued from time to time in accordance with the lockdown announced by the Government to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus will continue to apply in this case.
