In the wake of COVID-19 pandemic, the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has decided to shut down the theatres under its control from December, 2020. A special concession in the fare was given for Marathi plays and orchestras from April 1, 2023. Even though the theatres have reopened now, various theatre organizations have been demanding a concession in the rent of Ravindra Natya Mandir theatre on the lines of the concession given by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation to revive the theatre sector in the post-Covid period. Considering the above situation, the matter of giving concession in the rent charged for Marathi plays in Ravindra Natya Mandir and Prabhadevi Natyagruha was under the consideration of the government.
