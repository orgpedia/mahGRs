GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 30-2015/ PR No. 76/ CE. 2 dated 26th June, 2015
Introduction: Many institutions, organizations, colleges, universities, libraries, museums and private individuals in the state have documents of national importance. The National Archives of India, Government of India, New Delhi is funding the preservation of these important and rare documents/ books. A State-level Screening Committee has been constituted to examine the proposal.
The government had decided to cancel the appointments of all the non-official members of the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this Government decision, the following non-official members appointed to the State Level Scrutiny Committee of the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai are being terminated. :
--------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Name                    | designation         |
| 1.   | Dr. Manjari Kamat       | non-official member |
| 2.   | Prof. Dr. Bhawna Patole | non-official member |
| 3.   | Mr. A.K. Kharade        | non-official member |
--------------------------------------------------------
The appointments of all non-official members/ approved members of the State Level Scrutiny Committee of the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai shall stand cancelled with effect from the date of issue of this Government Order.
