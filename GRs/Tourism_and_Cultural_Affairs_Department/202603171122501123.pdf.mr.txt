The following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit
Account (VPDA)
1. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of
the relevant G.R. have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
3. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
4. The VPDA 15 applicable only for the State Schemes and not for Central Sector or
Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
5. The Receipt to the said VPDA through Nil Bill procedure, shall be only for the
bills drawn under :
e MTR-31B for the object heads 27-Minor Works and 53-Major Works ;
e MTR-45B for the object heads of expendure 33-subsides and 50-Other
Charges ;
e MTR-44 101 the object heads of expendure 31-Grant-in-Aid (Non Salary)
and 35-Grant-in-Aid for creating of Capital Assets.
e MTR-29 101 the object heads of expendure 52-Machinery and Equipment
shall be applicable from 1 April 2025
6. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate in the prescribed
format after incurring expenditure from the VPDA to the offices viz O/o AG
(A&E)-I, Mumbai, O/o PAG (A&E)-II, Nagpur or O/o PAO, Mumbai as per the
respective jurisdiction for the Bills drawn under MTR-44 for object heads of
expenditure 31-Grant-in-Aid (Non Salary) and 35-Grant-in-Aid for creating of
Capital Assets.
For Bills drawn under MTR-31B, MTR-45B, MTR-29 the Utilisation Certificate
shall be submitted to the concerned administrative departments.
