The Proposal: -
The Marathi language has been accorded the status of a classical language by the Government of India. This is a historical and
The glorious moment is a matter of great pride for all Marathi speakers. On this occasion, approval will be given to organize a cultural program based on Marathi language through singing, playing, dancing in "Hyderabad."
The request has been made by the Directorate under their reference letter. Accordingly, the event was organized.
Administrative and financial approval of the expenditure incurred was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
On Marathi language at Hyderabad on the occasion of Marathi language getting the status of a classical language
This Government has allocated a sum of Rs. 3,50,000 - (Rupees Three Lakh Fifty Thousand only) for organizing the cultural program based on singing, playing, dancing.
Administrative and financial approval is being granted under the resolution.
