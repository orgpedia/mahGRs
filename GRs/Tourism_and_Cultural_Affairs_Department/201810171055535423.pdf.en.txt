(2) The Committee is hereby approved to engage the services of the experts/ consultants in the above statement A and B for the first one year. Considering the nature of the project and the experience and seniority of the said consultant/ expert persons in the field, the Government have decided to take up the project in accordance with the Government Resolution, Public Works Department No. The contract should be signed with them for one year within the limit of daily honorarium rate as on 24.01.2017. The remuneration to be paid to these experts will be excluding service tax/ casual expenditure as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision of the Public Works Department.
(3) The District Collector, Raigad and the Member Secretary, Raigad Fort and Area Development Tourism Planning Authority shall make available the staff required for the project planning, evaluation and control room such as Special Officer/ Personal Assistant/ peon from the staff of the offices concerned with the project without directly hiring them on contractual basis or in case of non-availability of adequate staff in such manner, the services of such staff shall be made available from the staff hired on contract basis by the said offices.
(4) The Collector, Raigad shall make available 2 DV vehicles for the Chairman and members of the Authority for tours to be undertaken in connection with the activities of the Authority. In case of non-availability of DV vehicles, the Collector should make arrangements at his level by taking the vehicles on lease on a case-to-case basis.
(5) The expenditure for the Project Planning, Evaluation and Control Room under the Raigad Fort and Area Development Tourism Planning Authority approved under the Government Decision No. 1 above is Rs. 2886.13 lakh from the provision of contingency expenditure and within that limit. Also, the project cost of Rs. 60608.97 lakhs, the contingency expenditure should be provided as per actual expenditure.
