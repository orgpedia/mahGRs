Introduction: - Various events have been started to be organized throughout the year from 6th June, 2023, the date fixed for the 350th coronation ceremony of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. On this premise, various events / activities are being organized throughout the year by the State Government. Against the same backdrop, the government was considering approving biographical information about Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj as well as the expenditure incurred on the proclamation of Shiva in the Ministry of Dignity building.
Government Decision: - An expenditure of Rs. 22,20,000 - (Rupees twenty-two lakh twenty thousand only) on the celebration of the 350th Shivrajyabhisheka Mahotsav in the Mantralaya building is being approved under this Government Decision.
