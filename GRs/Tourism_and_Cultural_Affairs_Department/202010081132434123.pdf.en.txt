The Proposal:
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Govt. Under 16/11/2016, Sri Kshetra Nira Nrusinghpur, Dist. 260.86-crore development plan and the formation of an executive committee under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Pune, for the implementation of the said development plan. It was under the consideration of the government to include the construction of a bus stand at Nrusinghpur in the development plan.
Government's decision:
Reference No. 1. This Government has approved the inclusion of the work of" Construction of Bus Stand at Nrusinghpur" in the development plan of Shri Kshetra Nira Nrusinghpur, G. Pune Shrine Board approved vide Government Resolution dated 16/11/2016.
2. The approval is for" Sri Kshetra Nr. Nr. Singhpur Development Plan of Rs. 260.86 crore, subject to the condition that the cost of maintenance of the bus stand shall be borne by the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC).
Reference No. 1 for this approval. The terms and conditions of the Governing Decision dated 16/11/2016 shall continue to apply as before.
