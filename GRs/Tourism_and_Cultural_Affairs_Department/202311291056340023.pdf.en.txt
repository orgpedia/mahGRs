The proposal:
The proposal for grant of administrative and financial approval to the tender for selection of Expert Architect Institute for preparation of proposal for UNESCO Oral and Intangible Heritage of Pandharpur Wari in Maharashtra through e-tendering has been submitted by the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums for approval under Reference Letter. The proposal for the minimum tender was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the said Government Decision, the selection of Expert Architects for preparing the proposal for" Pandharpur Wari in Maharashtra" UNESCO Oral and Intangible Heritage will be done through e-tendering. Abha Lamba Associates, Mumbai Administrative and Financial Sanction is being accorded to the following Minimum Tenders for an amount of Rs. 9,90,000/- (Rupees Nine Lakh Ninety Thousand only) (inclusive of all taxes).
