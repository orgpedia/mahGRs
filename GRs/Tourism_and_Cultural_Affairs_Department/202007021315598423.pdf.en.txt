Introduction: The aforementioned No. Under the scheme of conferring lifetime achievement award on a veteran theatre artist for his outstanding performance in theatre was approved.
The aforementioned No. Under Section 2, the award was recognized as the Natvarya Prabhakar Panshikar Rangabhoomi Lifetime Achievement Award.
While selecting the awardee for this award, the government was considering to give this award posthumously to honor the outstanding performance of the veteran theater artist in the field of theater art for a long time.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to confer the Natavarya Prabhakar Panashekar Rangabhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Award posthumously to honour the veteran theatre personality for his long and distinguished career in the field of theatre.
