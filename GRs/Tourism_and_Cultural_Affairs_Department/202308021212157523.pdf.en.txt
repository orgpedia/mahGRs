To give administrative approval to the expenditure incurred for publishing" Selected Rare Letters of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj" in the form of a coffee table book on the occasion of" 350th Shivrajyabhishek Ceremony" under the umbrella scheme" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav."
The Government of Maharashtra

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,

Date: August 2, 2023.
Read: Director, Directorate of Archives, vide No. Letter dated 20.07.2023
The government has decided to organize various programs throughout the year to mark the 350th year of the coronation of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The date for the programme is 15 July 2023. In the meeting held under the chairmanship of the Minister (Cultural Affairs), it was decided to publish the" Rare Letters of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj" in book form through the office of the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai and for this, Rs. An amount of Rs. 25.00 lakh has been provided for. Accordingly, the proposal has been submitted to the Government for approval by the Director, Directorate of Archives vide the aforesaid letter.
Government Decision: Under the umbrella scheme of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," on the occasion of the" 350th Shivrajyabhishek Ceremony," the documents of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj should be treasured. Administrative sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only).
The Director, Directorate of Records, Mumbai is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer and he or an officer authorised by him is authorised to sign the payment in this behalf.
0.3 The expenditure for the same shall be met from the available provision for the financial year 2023-24 under the head Demand No." ZD-2, PRADHAAN ACCOUNT, TITLE 2205 ART AND CULTURE (13) AZADIYA KA AMRIT MAHOTSAV (13) (01) AZADIYA KA AMRIT MAHOTSAV PROGRAMME (PROGRAMME) (2205 3733), 50- OTHER EXPENSES."
