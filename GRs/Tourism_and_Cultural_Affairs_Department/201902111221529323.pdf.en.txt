Read: Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi's letter No. Marahinsa/ 40/2019, dated January 11, 2019
Introduction: Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi runs various activities for the promotion and propagation of Hindi through various institutions in Maharashtra. Under this, various seminars, seminars, symposiums, etc. programs are organized for the development of Hindi language and literature. Accordingly, a one-day National Seminar was organized on 21st January, 2019 at Ghatkopar with the objective of promoting language and literature in collaboration with Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy.
The government was contemplating to bear the cost.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: Under this Government Order, the expenditure of Rs.77,100/- (Rupees Seventy Seven Thousand and Hundred only) on the one day seminar organized by Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy at Ghatkopar on 21st January, 2019 is being approved post facto.
