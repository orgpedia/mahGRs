The Proposal:
The State Cultural Policy 2010 has been announced to mark the golden jubilee year of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the aforesaid Reference No. Under Section 1, the" State Cultural Fund" has been established. Regarding the matters for which this fund should be utilized as mentioned in this Government Decision, the above-mentioned Reference No. Rule 2 has been established.
Accordingly, the matter of providing financial assistance to the institutions recommended by the Scrutiny and Control Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to provide financial assistance to the following institutions from the State Cultural Fund.
Approval is being given.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A.  | Name of the institution                    | Direct Funding |
| 1.    | We Crop Pune                               | Rs. 3.00 Lakhs |
| 2.    | Vanvasi Kalyan Ashram, Nashik, Maharashtra | Rs. 2.00 Lakhs |
| Total | Rs. 5.00 Lakhs                             |
