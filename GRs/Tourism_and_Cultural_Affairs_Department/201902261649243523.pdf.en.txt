Introduction: The folk art of Shahiri is an integral part of the glorious culture and tradition of Maharashtra. In order to preserve and promote this folk art, the Government was considering to approve the expenditure for the Shaheri Mahotsav to be organized by the State Government from 26th to 28th February, 2019.
Government Decision: The estimated expenditure of Rs. 20,50,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh Fifty Thousand only) for organizing Shahiri Mahotsav-2019 at Aurangabad District is being approved as per the following details.
