As per 11.02.2016, instructions have been issued to review the sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them and fix a revised figure. Also Reference No. 3, Department of Finance, Govt. Guidelines have been issued to fix the revised figure as on 29.06.2017. In the meeting held on 06.03.2023, the proposal for the revised pattern of 11 posts of the Regional Office of Theatre Experimentation Supervisory Board, Mumbai was placed for consideration. The minutes of the meeting No. 4 As per the communication received by the Department, the revised pattern of 7 regular posts+ 4 manpower services to be taken up by external agencies in the Regional Office of Theatre Experimentation Monitoring Board, Mumbai has been approved. Accordingly, the matter of approving the revised figure of posts in the Regional Office of the Theatre Experiment Review Board, Mumbai was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As indicated in the accompanying prospectus A and B under the said Government Decision, the revised pattern of 7 regular posts in the Regional Office of Theatre Experimentation Supervisory Board, Mumbai+ 4 manpower services to be taken up by external agencies is being approved. The posts shown in A.No. 5 and 6 of' Return-A' are being declared as dead cadre. The existing number of employees in the deceased cadre of A.No. 5 and 6 will be automatically cancelled after the post falls vacant due to retirement or other reasons. Thereafter, the services indicated in the' Statement-B' should be taken up by external agencies as per requirement. The details of the posts currently working in the cadre declared as' dead cadre' are shown in the' Return-C'.
