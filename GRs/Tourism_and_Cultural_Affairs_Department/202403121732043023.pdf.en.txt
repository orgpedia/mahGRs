Introduction: - About the development of Marathwada. A special meeting of the state cabinet was held at Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Done on 16.09.2023. The decision included the preservation of historic Vastu in the Marathwada region and specifically the development of ancient Vastu Sthalas in Charthana.
As per the decision taken in the cabinet meeting, the memorial at Math (Vihar), Charthana, District Parbhani near Gokuleshwar Temple will be preserved and repaired at a cost of Rs. 88,45,092 - May of this amount.
The detailed budget prepared by the Transition Design Studio, Mumbai is presented by the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums.
Accordingly, a proposal to grant administrative approval to the budget for the conservation and repair work of this state protected monument was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - The State Protected Monument at Math (Vihar), Charthana, G. Parbhani near Gokuleshwar Temple will be renovated at a cost of Rs. 88,45,092 - Administrative approval is being granted under this Government Decision (including Goods and Services Tax).
