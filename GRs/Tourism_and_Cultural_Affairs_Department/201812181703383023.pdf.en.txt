Various programs to be organized jointly by the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs and the Department of Marathi Language on the birth centenary of Deshpande, G.D. Madgulkar and Sudhir Phadke have been approved under the government decision dated December 6, 2018. The programmes included 10 cultural programmes and the government was considering giving approval to the institutions presenting the programmes and the expenditure on them. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The following cultural programmes to be organized to mark the birth centenary year 2018-19 of Deshpande, noted poet, script, dialogue writer and lyricist G.D. Madgulkar and veteran music composer and singer Sudhir Phadke are being approved to be presented by the following institutions from December 2018 to March 31, 2019.
