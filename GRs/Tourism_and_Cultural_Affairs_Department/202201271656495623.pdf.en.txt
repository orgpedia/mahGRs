Preamble: The Film Screening Committee has passed the aforesaid Government Resolution for sanctioning financial assistance under the scheme of" Grant of Financial Assistance for Production of Quality Marathi Films" to be implemented by the Government. It was registered on 29.11.2018. the above decision of the Government. It has been cancelled with effect from 18.01.2020. the above decision of the Government. The Film Screening Committee was constituted on 23.09.2021. At present, the government was considering the appointment of some new members in this committee.
Government Decision: To sanction financial assistance for the production of quality Marathi films. The Government is now appointing the following new members to the Committee which was reconstituted vide Government Resolution dated 23.09.2021:

The Film Screening Committee constituted to examine the films and recommend the films eligible for financial assistance as per the scheme of" Grant of Financial Assistance for Production of Quality Marathi Films" being implemented by the Government will function under the Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari Corporation, Goregaon, Mumbai. The term of office of the Committee shall be for a period of three years from the date of such decision or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
33% attendance is required for screening of films. On the recommendations of the committee members, further action will be taken by the Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagar Nigam, Goregaon, Mumbai, as per the provisions of the scheme.
The non-official members of this committee will be entitled to travel allowance, meeting allowance and honorarium as per the rules of the Finance Department. The cost for this should be borne by Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagar Nigam, Goregaon, Mumbai from the service charge received by them under the scheme.
