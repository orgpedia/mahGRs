GOVERNMENT RECOGNITION: The District level works sanctioned under Regional Tourism Development Scheme for providing infrastructure facilities at tourist places during the financial year 2011-12 and 2013-14 to 2018-19 have been approved by the Government vide its decision dated 11/09/2019 for the year 2019-20 to disburse an amount of Rs. 6836.02 lakh to the District Collectors as mentioned in Pillar No.5 of the District for the year 2019-20. The amount disbursed for the following activities mentioned in this Government Resolution should be read as mentioned in clause 5 below. Accordingly, this Government decision approves the release of Rs. 1357.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirteen Crores Fifty Seven Lakhs only) to the concerned District Collectors for the said works, as mentioned in Pillar No.5 hereby.
