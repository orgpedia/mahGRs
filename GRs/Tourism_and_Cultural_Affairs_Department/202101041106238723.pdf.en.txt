Introduction: In order to give industrial status to the hospitality sector, hoteliers registered with the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, have to pay Rs. As per the decision taken by the Government, from 01.04.2021, electricity tariff, power tariff, water tariff, property tax, development tax, enhanced floor area index and non-agriculture tax will be levied at industrial rates.
It has also been decided to complete the process of determining the state criteria by appointing an expert committee within two months to levy tax/ duty at industrial rates after meeting the prescribed criteria for obtaining the Basic Minimum Standards from hoteliers who are not registered with the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India. An expert committee was set up to look into the matter.
Government's decision:
In order to provide industrial status to the hospitality sector, it is hereby approved to constitute the following committee to determine the criteria for obtaining the Basic Minimum Standards for hoteliers who are not registered with the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India.
