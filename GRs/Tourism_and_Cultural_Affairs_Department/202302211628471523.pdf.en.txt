Administrative and financial sanction for expenditure incurred for exhibition on Partition Agony Day on 14 th August, 2022.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs
Government Resolution No. 2022/ P. No. 125/ S.K. 2
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 21, 2023.
Read:- 1) Govt. CER-2022/ Q.No. 124/30, D. 12-08-2022 2) Director, Directorate of Archives No. Pura/ Sancha/ Granthal/ Vividi/ 2023/ B179, dated 19th January, 2023.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: On the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, on the occasion of Partition Agony Day on August 14, 2022, the pain of millions of victims of partition of the country and the suffering of the displaced people should be presented before the public.
The exhibition was held at the Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai. The total expenditure incurred on the exhibition organized by the Directorate of Archives on the occasion of Partition Agony Day on 14 th August, 2022 is Rs. An amount of Rs.1,35,448/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Five Thousand Four Hundred and Forty Eight only) is being provided as administrative and financial sanction as per this Government decision.
The expenditure will be met from the provision made during the financial year 2022-23 under the head" Demand No. ZD-02, Principal Head 2205-Arts and Culture, (00) 104 PRODUCT OFFICES (2205 1795), 13 OFFICE EXPENSES."
The Director, Directorate of Records, Mumbai is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer and he or an officer authorised by him is authorised to sign the payment in this behalf.
The utilization certificate of the expenditure incurred for the said programme should be submitted by the Director, Directorate of Archives, Mumbai to the Government within one month.
30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015 is being issued in accordance with the authority vested in the Administrative Department under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division III No. 4, Paragraph 27 (2) (a) and the concurrence given by the Finance Department for the distribution.
