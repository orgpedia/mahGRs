Introduction: Sonegaon Lake is an ancient and historic lake in Nagpur city, located in the south-western part of the city. As per the directions of the government, the Nagpur Municipal Corporation had submitted a proposal for the Sonegaon Lake beautification project for approval. Development of Basic Amenities in the Municipal Area Under the scheme of 2018-19, the Government has decided to implement the Sonegaon Lake Beautification Project under the head of accounts (22170913). 28/02/2019 by granting administrative approval for Rs. A sum of Rs. 17.32 crore has been released. The major works under the project include construction of the remaining retaining wall in the southern and northern parts of the lake, park, shops, amphitheatre, footpath and recharge shaft in the northern part. The protection wall on the western side has been completed to prevent the lake from being re-laid.
The original project report of the Sonegaon Lake beautification project included construction of the remaining retaining wall on the north and south sides of the lake and development work on the north side. Under Sonegaon Lake Beautification Project Phase 2, peripheral development works in the southern, western and eastern parts of the lake have been proposed. With this project, Sonegaon Lake will be developed as a tourist destination in Nagpur city.
The proposal for administrative approval and sanction of funds for the Phase-II of Sonegaon Lake beautification project was under consideration of the Government.
