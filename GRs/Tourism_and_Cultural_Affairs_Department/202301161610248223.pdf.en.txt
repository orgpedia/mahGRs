The term of office of the Chairperson and Members of the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee, whichever is earlier. Even if the appointment of non-official members is terminated, the President, Secretary, Department of Cultural Affairs and Joint Director and Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi will continue to function. The Academy will continue to exist.
The Academy Office will be responsible for organizing various programs of the Academy. The officers of the Academy will be responsible for organizing the program and its expenses as per the government rules and prescribed procedures, under the guidance and cooperation of the Executive President and members of the Academy.
