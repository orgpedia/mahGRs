Government's decision:
With the approval of the Hon'ble Cabinet of Ministers, it has been decided to create a Directorate of Tourism in the State for effective formulation and implementation of the Tourism Policy of the Government vide Government Resolution dated 12.4.2016. Further, as per the Government Decisions dated 14.7.2016 and 1.11.2017, 55 new posts have been approved to be created on the establishment of Directorate of Tourism.
Under this order, the Deputy Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Nashik, the Deputy Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Aurangabad, the Deputy Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Amravati, the Deputy Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Nagpur, the Deputy Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Konkan Bhawan, CBD Belapur and the Assistant Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Pune, have been directed to submit a report to the Head of Regional Office and Head of Accounts (34522972), Demand No. Being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for ZD-4.
