The Proposal:
Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Department is organizing various programmes on a regular basis. Various cultural programmes have been organised across the state to mark the occasion. So that the younger generation gets to know the works of new-age art/ culture/ great personalities and gets a platform to present their art and the importance of that relevance can be elaborated through cultural programs. Against this background, the government was considering giving administrative and financial approval to the programme to be held on the occasion of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar's Mahaparinirvan Din in Nashik and the expenditure on it.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded under this Government Decision for the programme to be held at Nashik on the occasion of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar's Mahaparinirvan Din on December, 2023 and the expenditure to be incurred thereon is Rs. 7,00,000/- (Rupees Seven Lakhs only).
Cost Accounting Demand No. 2. ZD-2, 2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Conservation, Various Festivals and other Programmes (2205 1507)- 50 Other expenditure to be met from the financial provision sanctioned for the year 2023-24 under this head.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs will be responsible for submitting the details of the programme.
