The Proposal: -
Commercial plays are subsidized by the Department of Cultural Affairs.
On the same lines, a committee has been formed to implement a subsidy scheme for professional children's plays as well.
The matter was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Subsidy Scheme for Professional Children's Plays on the lines of Commercial Plays
Under this Government decision, it is being approved to constitute the following Committees for implementation.
