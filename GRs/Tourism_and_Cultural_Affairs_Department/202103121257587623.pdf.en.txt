Preamble: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Right of Persons Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Accordingly, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has instructed to ensure the posts in the state government service for the disabled. dated 02.02.2021 vide No. 5 decisions have been made by the government. As per the said Government decision, the following action is required to be taken: (a) The Government decision regarding the posts in Group-A, Group-B, Group-C and Group-D, which are in the State Government establishment (including the posts in the subordinate offices), as ensured by the Central Government for the disabled, shall be issued by the concerned administrative department at their level.
(b) The posts in the State Government service shall be reserved for persons with disabilities, even if the designation of the posts in the list published by the Central Government is different in terms of duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scales. Accordingly, the concerned administrative department should include these posts in the government decision.
As per the above instructions, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to ensure the posts of Divyang in the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai and Subordinate Divisional Offices, Department of Darshanika, Mumbai and P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai.
