Preamble: The post of Short Writer working on contract basis in the office of the Board of Superintendence of Theatre Experiments is mentioned above. As per the Government Decision dated 15th December, 2011, Rs. 10,000/- will be awarded. Considering the nature of the work of the board and inflation, the matter of increasing the remuneration of the post of short writer was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the Government Decision, the honorarium of the post of Short Writer working on contract basis in the office of the Theatre Experiment Board has been increased by Rs.10,000/- per month from the date of this Government Decision. The government has sanctioned Rs. 20,000.
2. Expenditure on Demand No. ZD-2, 2205 Art and Culture (00) 001, Operation and Administration (02) (01) Office of the Inspector of State Theatre Experiments (2205 1383), 13 Office expenses to be met out of the provision sanctioned in that year under this head.
3. This Government decision is based on the informal reference of the Finance Department No. 180/2019/ Expenditure-15, D. being issued pursuant to the Consent received as on 09.07.2019.
