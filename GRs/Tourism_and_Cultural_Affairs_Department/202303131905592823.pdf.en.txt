Introduction: The Sindhi Sahitya Academy has been established in the state for the promotion and encouragement of Sindhi language and literature. The aforementioned No. The appointments of non-official members to the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy appointed under Section 3 have been cancelled. The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy is being restructured as follows.

The period of appointment of the President and members of the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee or until the Government issues an order cancelling the appointments of non-official members, whichever is earlier. Even if the appointment of non-official members is terminated, the chairman, principal secretary, department of cultural affairs and joint director and member secretary of Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy will continue to function. The Academy will continue to exist.
The Academy Office will be responsible for organizing various programs of the Academy. The officers of the Academy will be responsible for organizing the program and spending the funds as per the government rules and prescribed procedures under the guidance and cooperation of the Executive President and members of the Academy.
