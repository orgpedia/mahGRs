The coronation ceremony of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of Hindavi Swarajya, was held on Jyeshtha Shuddha Trayodashi Saka 1595 i.e. 6 June 1674.
Accordingly, the 350th year of Shivrajyabhisheka has started from Jyeshtha Shuddha Trayodashi Shakya 1945 i.e. June 2, 2023. A variety of activities are planned throughout the year. Against this backdrop, the biography of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj will be translated into Braille for the visually impaired. The government had constituted a committee for the purpose.
Government's decision:
The biography of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj will be translated into Braille for the visually impaired. Approval is being given to constitute a" Central Control Committee" as follows for planning this initiative.

Functions of the Committee: 1. Selection of books for printing literature in Braille on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
2. Determination of the time and manner of action.
3. Determination of expenditure for this purpose.
4. Selecting the institution for distribution of printed books.
Tenure of the Committee: Automatic expiry of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's biography after it is converted into Braille for the visually impaired. No new order will be issued. However, the maximum period of this committee will be up to the anniversary of the 350th Shivrajyabhishek ceremony.
