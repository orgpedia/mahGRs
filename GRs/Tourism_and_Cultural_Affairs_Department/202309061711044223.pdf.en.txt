Introduction: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's tigress is an invaluable treasure of history and the sentiments of the people of the state are associated with it. The tiger is scheduled to be brought back to the country from the UK. The transfer of these tigers personally needs to be done responsibly and carefully. For this, Chief Minister (CM), Chief Secretary (Home), Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums, and a delegation from the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums visited the Victoria and Albert Museum and other museums in London. The visit will be from 29.09.2023 to 04.10.2023. Hence, the Rs. Administrative sanction for expenditure amounting to Rs. 50,14,550/- was under consideration.
Government Decision: Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for expenditure of Rs. 50,14,550/- (Rupees Fifty Lakh Fourteen Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty only).
Expenditure for the same shall be met from the available funds for the financial year 2023-24 under head Demand No.ZD 2. Art and Culture, Operation, (13) Under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav (13) (01) Programme (s) under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav (2205 3733) 50 Other expenditure.
