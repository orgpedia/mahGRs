Introduction: The Government of India has conferred the civilian award of Padma Bhushan on eminent musician Late Naushad Ali for his outstanding achievements in the field of music.
The matter of post-facto approval of the expenses incurred for its planning was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Renowned Musician Late. On the occasion of the birth centenary of Naushad Ali, a musical programme titled" Swar Rajani" featuring popular songs of Avit Godi composed by him was held at Borivali (West), Mumbai on Friday, August 2, 2019. The expenditure of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only) incurred for its implementation is being given ex-post facto approval by this Government.
