Introduction: The government was considering to celebrate the Mahadurg festival every year in the state to introduce the historical work of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and to create attraction among domestic and foreign tourists about the fort fortresses of the state that were consecrated by his footsteps.
Government Decision: Shivneri Fort, Tt. Junnar, Pune to promote Fort Tourism in the State. The Mahadurga Utsav is being approved to be held from February 18 to 22, 2023. For this, a committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Pune and the members of the committee will be as follows:
1. Mr. Praveen Darekar, Shri. Member of the Legislative Council- Member

Shri Jayendra More, Theatre Artist, Satara- Member
Deputy Director, Divisional Directorate of Tourism, Pune- Member Secretary
The District Collector, Pune will also act as the nodal officer for the celebration.
The Directorate of Tourism has approved the appointment of 3 independent agencies for the purpose of organizing the event through a process of Expression of Interest (EOI) for 1) Ground Event, 2) Tent Accommodation, 3) Reservation Arrangement.
