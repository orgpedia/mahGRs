Introduction: P. L. Deshpande founded the Maharashtra Kala Academy. It was established on July 2, 1991.
As per the decision taken by the Improvement Committee of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation in its meeting held on 21.06.1960, for the construction of Tagore Memorial Theatre (now named Ravindra Natya Mandir, Mumbai) located at P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation has issued reference No. According to a letter dated 1st September, Sane Guruji, Nardula Tank, Sayani Road, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, 9028 S.O. The land was transferred free of cost subject to the following conditions:
The managing committee of the Tagore Memorial Theatre will have representatives of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
Free auditorium will be provided for the cultural program of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
In case of excess income, 50% of the income should be given to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
Condition No. As per clause 1, the proposal for constitution of a Management Committee to guide the management matters of Ravindra Natya Mandir vide Reference No. Project Director, P. L. Deshpande, Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai has submitted to the Government under Section 2. As per the proposal received from Project Director, P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, P. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, the government was considering the formation of a" management committee" for the Ravindra Natya Mandir in Mumbai.
The Government has decided to constitute a" Management Committee" for the Ravindra Natya Mandir at the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai to guide the management of the Ravindra Natya Mandir as follows:
