The Proposal:
The Government has issued a notification under the Companies Act, 1956 to promote tourism in the State. Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation has been established on 21/1/1975. The Government has made available Government lands at several places in the State to the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation for implementing and developing tourism projects in the State as well as for meeting the objectives of the Tourism Policy.
To promote private participation in tourism by leasing out open lands and developed properties held by Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation.
And to develop the tourist places of the state according to global standards. The matter of taking a policy decision by making some amendments in the government decision of 17.02.1995 was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The policy of privatization of government lands/ properties under the control of Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation for tourism development is being approved in principle.
