The Proposal:
As per the provisions of Section 8 (1) (2) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, every department and the head of the office under it is required to publish a citizen's charter.
According to the The Citizen's Charter of the Department has been published on 4.10.2012. However, the said Charter needed to be amended
Government Decision: Now the revised citizens' charter of the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs (itself) is being published by making suitable amendments in the previously published citizens' charter.
