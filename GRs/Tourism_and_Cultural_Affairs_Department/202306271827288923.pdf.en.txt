Pandharpur is a historical and cultural heritage of about 800 years and is one of the few religious and spiritual sites in the world. A large number of devotees from across the State and outside the State are expected to participate in the celebrations.
Every year on the occasion of Ashadhi Wari, two different Dindis are organized in the name of Shri Sant Tukoba Maharaj and Shri Sant Dnyaneshwar Maharaj from Dehu and Alandi. These two main dindis travel on two routes every year and lakhs of devotees travel on these palkhi routes every year.
At the same time, the palanquins of many saints are on their way to Pandharpur. The government has welcomed this. The administrative approval of the program and its expenses was under the consideration of the government as it would be held on June 28, 2023 at Mauje Isbavi in the presence of the Minister, Head of Cultural Affairs.
Government's decision:
The administrative approval is being given under this Government decision for the expenditure of Rs. 12,65,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh Sixty Five Thousand only) for organizing the program to welcome Saint Palkhi on the occasion of Devshayani Ashadhi Ekadashi at Mauje Isabavi on 28th June, 2023.
The said expenditure should be met from the financial provision approved under the head Z- 2, 2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Promotion, Various Festivals and Other Programmes (22051507)-50 for the year 2023-24.
