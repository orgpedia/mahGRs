Constitution of various committees for organizing the festival: For successful and effective implementation of the festival in the state, the following committees will be formed: 1. State Level Committee. The committee will be headed by the Chief Minister and include senior members of the cabinet, the Legislative Council Chairman and the Speaker of the Legislative Assembly, along with dignitaries from various fields. The committee will decide on the policy.
Core Committee:- As the Minister of Coordinating Department. The committee will be headed by the Minister of Cultural Affairs with the inclusion of Ministers in charge of various core departments.
The Implementation Committee: The committee will be headed by the Chief Secretary with the participation of the secretaries of the core departments. The committee will comprise secretaries of urban development, revenue, information technology, rural development, general administration, tourism, cultural affairs, home, finance, planning, school education, higher and technical education, agriculture and industries. Under the Umbrella Scheme, the Committee is being empowered to approve and sanction funds for schemes/ activities submitted by various departments, except the Department of Cultural Affairs.
District Level Committee: The committee will be headed by the District Collector. The District Collector of the concerned district will have the authority to constitute this committee. The committee will comprise the Chief Executive Officer of the Zilla Parishad, Social Welfare Officer, Education Officer, Agriculture Officer, officers of the departments of Industry/ Police/ Planning/ Tourism etc. and dignitaries from the industry/ social sector/ literature/ agriculture/ arts/ field. The committee will plan and monitor the implementation of the programme at the district level. Report of the district-level events
