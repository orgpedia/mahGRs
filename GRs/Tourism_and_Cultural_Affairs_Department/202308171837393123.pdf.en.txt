Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: August 17, 2023
Reference:- 1) Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs vide Government Resolution No. GUSAA-2013/ RULES/ P.No.105/ Sec.4, D. June 18, 2013.
2) Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Government Resolution No. 7122/ No. 473/ S.C.C. 13th March, 2023.
The Proposal:
The rules of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Akademi for the promotion of Gujarati language have been issued in accordance with the government decision at the above mentioned No. 1. As per the rules, the award scheme is one of the most important schemes of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy and under this scheme, literary persons who have done valuable work and made substantial contributions in the field of Gujarati literature are awarded annually. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the names of awardees selected by the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy for the three years 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the names of the awardees mentioned in the returns A, B and C respectively attached with the financial year 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy are being approved under this Government Decision.
