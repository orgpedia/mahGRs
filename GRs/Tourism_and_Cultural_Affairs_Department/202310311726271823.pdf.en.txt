The Proposal:
There is a huge scope to develop water tourism in the dam areas and other water bodies of the state, thereby boosting the local economy and creating employment opportunities for the locals. A new tourist destination will be developed by establishing high-quality water tourism in the area of Gosikhurd reservoir in Gosikhurd Dharan district Bhandara. A proposal to develop world-class water tourism at Gosikhurd reservoir in Bhandara was also under consideration of the government to generate employment for the local youth and contribute to the local economy.
