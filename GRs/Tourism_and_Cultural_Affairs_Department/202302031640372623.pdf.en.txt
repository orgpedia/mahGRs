Introduction: Vishalgad in Kolhapur district is an important fort of Hindavi Swarajya founded by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Vishalgad Fort, Shahuwadi, G. Kolhapur is a state protected monument. There have been many encroachments on this historic fort. A meeting was held under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Kolhapur, regarding the removal of encroachments on Vishalgad. It was completed on 07.12.2022. The Collector, Kolhapur has directed the Assistant Director, Archaeology Department, Pune, to take legal action regarding illegal encroachments, construction and other activities on the fort, filing of complaints, filing of claims in the competent court, filing of caveats, etc. 2 is authorized. As the Assistant Director, Archaeology Department, Pune, did not have any necessary mechanism to remove the encroachments, he had contacted the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department to remove the encroachments. Accordingly, the detailed budget prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Kolhapur was approved by the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums vide Reference No. 03 has been submitted to the Government for approval.
