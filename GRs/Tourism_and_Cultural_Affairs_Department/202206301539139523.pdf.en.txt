GOVERNMENT Circular: Guidelines of the Central Government regarding implementation of" Har Ghar Jhanda" initiative under" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" during the period from 11 th August, 2022 to 17 th August, 2022 as per the instructions of the Central Government vide No. The Government Circulars of 2 have been circulated together. Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India No. Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the initiative of" Har Ghar Tiranga" is being implemented under the Quasi Government Letter No. 1.
In view of the change in the name of this initiative by the Central Government, the" Har Ghar Jhanda" initiative under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" should now be implemented in the state under the name of" Har Ghar Tiranga." Also, wherever there is a mention of" Har Ghar Jhanda" initiative, it should be called" Har Ghar Tiranga" initiative.
