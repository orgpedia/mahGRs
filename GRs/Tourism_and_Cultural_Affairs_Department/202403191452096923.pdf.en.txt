Read: - 1. Letter from the Directorate of Cultural Affairs No. 4087, dated 20th July, 2023.
Preamble: - The death anniversary of Sant Chokhoba was celebrated on 18th May, 2023 at Shree Kshetra Pandharpur in association with the Department of Cultural Affairs and Warkari Sahitya Parishad, Maharashtra, an organisation of the Warkari sect which gives the slogan of equality. Administrative and financial approval of the actual cost of the event was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Under this decision, administrative and financial sanction is being given for the total expenditure of Rs. 15,00,000 - (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) for the cultural program and its organization on the occasion of the death anniversary of Sant Chokhoba on 18th May, 2023 at Shree Kshetra Pandharpur in association with the Department of Cultural Affairs and Warkari Sahitya Parishad, Maharashtra.
02. Expenditure to be met from the financial provision approved for this year for the head of the account titled Demand No. ZD-2,2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Drama and Music Festival, (02) (02) State Art Culture Preservation, Conservation of Various Festivals and Other Events (2205 1507) -50.
03. For this, Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as Controller Officer and Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as Drawing and Distribution Officer.
04. The expenditure is as per Government Decision No. v. 2013 / Exchange, dated 17th April, 2015, Financial Rights Manual 1978, Part-I, Sub-section-3, A.No.4, Paragraph No. 27 (2) (a) is being granted in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Division.
