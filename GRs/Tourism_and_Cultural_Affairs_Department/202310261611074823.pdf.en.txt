Preamble: The designation of specialist architects under the Directorate for the Works of State Protected Monuments and Museums existed earlier. Reference No. The Government hereby cancelled the decision and directed to adopt the prevailing system of Envelope-I (Technical) and Envelope-II (Financial) by inviting public competitive tenders for the selection of architects. However, it is taking a long time to call for tenders and appoint an architect. The Directorate had invited interested proposals from the expert architects having adequate experience in the conservation and restoration of the State Protected Monuments under the Directorate to ensure that the original architectural design as per the Archaeological Indication is maintained and the construction and development of the Museum as per the Museology is carried out in a quality and efficient manner. In this, the Directorate has proposed to approve the nomination of a total of 42 architects who have qualified after scrutiny. 3 submitted to the government.
Government Decision: According to the proposal of the Directorate, the names of a total of 42 architects are being approved by this Government Decision as per the accompanying Statement-A so that the works of conservation and repair of State Protected Monuments should be carried out in a time-bound manner and the original architectural design should be maintained as per the archaeological indications, and the construction and development of the museum as per the museology should be done in a quality and standard manner.
