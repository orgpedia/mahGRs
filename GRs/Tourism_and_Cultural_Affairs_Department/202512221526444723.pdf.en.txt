The Proposal:
The ST Palace Temple in Nagpur is an internationally renowned Buddhist landmark and is considered an important religious and tourist attraction in the Nagpur area. The temple, built in 1999, is known for its unique Japanese architecture, serene surroundings, and is a world-class centre of Buddha worship. Situated along Umred Road in Nagpur district, the temple is also famous as the "Peace Palace." The huge white marble statue of Lord Buddha erected here attracts tourists from all over the country and abroad, Buddhist devotees as well as researchers.
2. The site has gained special recognition as a centre of Buddhist religious and cultural tourism globally and is visited annually by devotees from Japan and other foreign countries. The place is of great religious as well as tourist importance due to the beautiful gardens, meditation halls and peaceful environment of the temple complex. Nagpur being the geographical centre of the country has made it convenient for tourists from within the country and abroad to reach here. The Dragon Palace temple has thus become a centre for religious tourism as well as international cultural and intellectual exchange.
