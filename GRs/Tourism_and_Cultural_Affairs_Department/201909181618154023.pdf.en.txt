The Proposal:
Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshakar's contribution in the field of heritage conservation and museum movement-building is invaluable. He was an expert not only in museum management but also in archaeology, heritage conservation, art, architecture and iconography. He is also known as the Bhishma Pitamah of Indian Museology. Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshakar was instrumental in the heritage conservation movement that started in the city of Mumbai and was instrumental in bringing in laws that protect heritage sites.
He was awarded the Padma Shri in the year 2003. In his memory and as a tribute to his unparalleled achievements in this field, the Government of Maharashtra was contemplating to institute a Lifetime Achievement Award in his name.
Also, Padma Shri Madhukar Dhavalikar was instrumental in the study of Chalcolithic culture, in the evaluation of excavation of historical archaeological sites. At the same time, he made an in-depth study of mud sculptures, Satavahana period and Rashtrakuta period caves. Many of his research papers are published.
He has been honoured with a Gold Medal by the Archaeological Survey of India, an Honorary Membership by the Royal Asiatic Society and a Padma Shri by the Government of India for his work in this field.
The Maharashtra government was considering to start a scholarship scheme in their name and give them due recognition.
Government's decision:
In order to honour the work of Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshakar and Padma Shri Madhukar Dhavalikar, the Government has decided to announce Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshakar Lifetime Achievement Award and Padma Shri Madhukar Dhavalikar Scholarship Scheme. The total award/ scholarship amount will be Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only).
The award/ scholarship will be given to a person who has made outstanding contributions in the field of museum science, archaeology, heritage conservation, Indian iconography and ancient architecture.
In the first year, a senior researcher with outstanding achievement in this field will be given Rs. 5.00 lakh will be awarded, while 2 meritorious students pursuing advanced courses/ research like doctorate in the following year will be awarded Rs. Rs 2.50 lakh. The scheme will be implemented in the order of one year award and the next year scholarship. The amount of the award may be used for scholarships in the year in which the person eligible for the award is not available.

Selection of Awards and Scholarships:
1) The award is given to the best museum, archaeological, heritage conservation and ancient architecture in India.
Senior research scholars who have completed 45 years of age with outstanding performance in the subject will be eligible.
(2) Students who are working and enrolled for research in the above mentioned subjects in a university in India will be eligible for this scholarship.
3) Applications for this purpose will be invited by the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums by public notice.
Priority will be given to researchers from Maharashtra for this purpose.
5) The following committees will be constituted for the scrutiny of applications received for awards and scholarships and a decision will be taken. The award will be presented annually on May 31, the birth anniversary of Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshakar.
6) The award carries a cash prize, a citation and a plaque.
