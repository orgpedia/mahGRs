The Shri Ramaswamy Temple Complex on Giri Peak in Ramtek town, which is consecrated with the feet of Lord Shri Ram in Nagpur district, is a pilgrimage site for devotees and tourists. The monuments (temples and gates) in the Sri Ramaswami Temple Complex area have been declared as state protected monuments in 1996 in view of the historical importance of the site. Varah Darwaza, Shirpur Darwaza, Bhairav Darwaza, and Gokul Darwaza are designed to reach the Kewal Ram Mandir, Lakshman Mandir, and Ramkund, Vakataka period temples in the temple complex area respectively. However, since a large number of illegal constructions have taken place in the area, encroachments have been removed.
