Preamble: - Special meeting of the State Cabinet on Marathwada Development held at Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Done on 16.09.2023. The decision included the preservation of historic Vastu in the Marathwada region and specifically the development of ancient Vastu Sthalas in Charthana.
As per the decision taken in the cabinet meeting, the conservation and repair work of the memorial at Jenstambh, Charthana, District Parbhani was taken up at a cost of Rs. 2,56,68, 566 / - in May. The detailed budget prepared by the Transition Design Studio, Mumbai is presented by the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums. Accordingly, a proposal to grant administrative approval to the budget for the conservation and repair work of this state protected monument was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - Preservation of State Protected Monument at Jenstambh, Charthana, District Parbhani Rs. 2,56,68, 566 / - (Rupees Two Crore Rupees Two Hundred and Sixty Five Thousand Sixty Six Only) (inclusive of Goods and Services Tax) is being granted administrative sanction under this Act.

The Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the same and he or the officer authorised by him is being authorised to sign the payment in this regard. It will also be the responsibility of the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums and the concerned Conservation Architect to ensure that the work is completed as per the archaeological criteria / standards.
