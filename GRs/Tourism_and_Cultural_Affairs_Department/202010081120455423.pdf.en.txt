Introduction: The aforementioned No. Under the Maharashtra Bhushan Award Scheme, the Government of Maharashtra has instituted the Maharashtra Bhushan Award to recognize the highest achievement in any field in the State of Maharashtra. The Maharashtra Bhushan Award is the state's highest civilian honour. This award is given to those who have served in any field with an unparalleled spirit and have done remarkable work for the upliftment of human life through it.
