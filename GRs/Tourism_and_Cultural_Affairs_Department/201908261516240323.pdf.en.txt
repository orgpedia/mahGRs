Read:- 1) Government Decision, Tourism and Cultural Affairs Department No. GUSAA 2013/ RULE/ PR No.105/ Sec.4, dated 18th June, 2013) Government Resolution, Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs No. GUSA 7118/ Q.No.21/ S.K.4, dated 02.11.2018) of Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy, Mumbai vide letter No. Maragusa/ 1529/2019 dated 19th August, 2019 Preamble: The Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy, Mumbai has been established on 30th August, 1996 for the overall promotion and encouragement of Gujarati literature. In connection with the implementation of various schemes for the promotion of Gujarati language through this academy, the above mentioned No. 1 The rules have been prepared as per the decision of the government. Under this regulation, literary awards are given on various subjects. The aforementioned No. 2 In this Regulation, the Prakirna (Review) Prize for Literature has been included in the Prakirna (Review) Prize for Literature. The government was considering to name the award as' Gulabdas Broker Puraskar (Prakirna)'.
Government's decision:
The aforementioned No. 2 The Government hereby approve the renaming of the 9th Prakirn (Review) Award as Gulabdas Broker Award (Prakirn), which has been included in the Vangmay Award in the Scheme Manual of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy.
