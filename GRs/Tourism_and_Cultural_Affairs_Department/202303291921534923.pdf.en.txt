Preamble: As per the minutes circulated vide the aforesaid letter dated 5 th December, 2022 of the Core Committee meeting constituted under" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the matter of providing funds for activities/ programmes of various departments/ offices was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. 60,18,80, 244/- (Rupees Sixty Crores Eighteen Lakhs Eighty Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Four only) is being sanctioned as per the following details under the Umbrella Scheme for" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" to the Departments/ Offices which have received administrative approval for their activities/ programmes in the core committee's history circulated under the aforesaid letter, for organizing the following programmes in association with the Directorate of Cultural Affairs and Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai.
1) Details of the proposals from the Directorate of Cultural Affairs under the Department of Cultural Affairs are as follows:
