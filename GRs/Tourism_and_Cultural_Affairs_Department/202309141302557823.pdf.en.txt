Preamble: Various programmes and activities are being undertaken by the Government to mark the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Mukti Sangram Din. Against this backdrop, the government was considering setting up a committee to decide on the issue of special stamps and envelopes detailing the persons/ places/ events/ areas etc. associated with the Marathwada Liberation War and to give administrative and financial approval to the expenses incurred for printing the stamps.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative and financial approval is being given under this Government Decision for the formation of a committee to determine the issues related to the printing of postage stamps and envelopes on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Liberation War Day and the expenditure of Rs. 15,07,525/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Seven Thousand Five Hundred and Twenty Five only) for the printing of special postage stamps and envelopes.
02. A committee is being constituted to determine the issues related to" Marathwada Mukti Sangram Din Amrit Mahotsav" by identifying the persons/ places/ events/ areas etc.
