Government's decision:
As per the decision taken by the Summit Committee chaired by the Hon'ble Chief Minister in its meeting dated 15/3/2017, the Government under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs has approved the plan of Rs. 60608.97 lakh for the development of Raigad Fort and surrounding tourism under the Government Resolution No. 3.
Also, under the Government decision dated 20/12/2017 at Reference No. 4, an Authority has been constituted for the development of Raigad Fort and Area Tourism Development Plan. A sum of Rs. 6000.00 lakh has been provided so far for the development works under this scheme.
During the Monsoon Session of June, 2019, a supplementary demand of Rs. 2000.00 lakh has been approved for the works in the Raigad Fort and Area Tourism Development Plan. A Circular dated 9/7/2019 of the Department of Finance at Reference No.7 approving the Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been issued.

Terms and Conditions:
(1) The procedure prescribed under the Financial Rules and various Regulations shall be followed while making expenditure out of the funds disbursed.
2) It should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned grant under any circumstances. If the expenditure is more than the sanctioned grant, the responsibility will be of the Collector, Raigad and no additional grant will be provided for the excess expenditure.
The expenditure to be incurred from the sanctioned allocation should be made by March 31, 2020 and the sanctioned works should be completed immediately.
4) Information boards of the Tourism Department should be installed at the workplaces of the Tourism Department.
