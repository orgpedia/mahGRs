Preamble: Various programmes and activities are being undertaken by the Government to mark the Amrit Mahotsav of the Marathwada Liberation War. The two-act play" Hyderabad Azadi Ka Rann Sangram," based on the Marathwada Liberation War, will be staged in 76 talukas of eight districts of Marathwada. The government was considering giving administrative and financial approval to the expenses incurred for the performance of the play.
The two-act play titled" Hyderabad Azadi Ka Rann Sangram" on the theme of Marathwada Liberation War will be staged in 76 talukas of eight districts of Marathwada. For this purpose, administrative and financial approval is being given for the total expenditure of Rs. 1,90,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakh only) for the performance of the play in 76 Taluks at the rate of Rs. 2.50 lakh per Taluk.
