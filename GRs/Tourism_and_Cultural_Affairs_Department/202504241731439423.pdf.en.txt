the Government Circular
All officers / staff of the Department of Tourism, Directorate of Tourism and Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation are hereby informed that the headquarters should be left only with the written permission of their superiors on government holidays. Also, all officers / staff of the same branch or office shall not leave the headquarters at the same time on any government holiday.
(2) The officers / employees who do not comply with the instructions given by this circular shall be dealt with in accordance with the instructions given by the Government circular herein.
