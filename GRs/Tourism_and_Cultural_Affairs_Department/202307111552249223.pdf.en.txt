Preamble: The National Implementation Committee under the Chairmanship of Union Home Minister in its meeting held on 19.5.2015 decided to set up a memorial in memory of freedom fighter Tatya Tope to commemorate his 200th birth anniversary. The proposal has been sent to the Maharashtra government for approval. In response to the proposal submitted by the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs to the Central Government, the Central Government, under the Centenary and Anniversaries Celebration Scheme, vide letter dated 23.2.2016, directed that 25% of the cost of the project and the cost of the site be borne by the State Government. The Centre will bear 75% of the cost, while the state government will bear 15% and municipal corporations 10%.
