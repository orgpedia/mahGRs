Government's decision:
Project Director, P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Shri. The e-tendering process was carried out with the approval of the government to appoint the institute on a lease basis to manage the rental of eleven residential rooms on the sixth floor of the Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy for 10 years. The e-tendering process for the Hospitality Services has been initiated at the highest bidder rate (H-1B) of Rs. 2,76,000/- (excluding GST) has been received. Accordingly, a proposal has been made to give approval for the management of renting out eleven residential rooms on the sixth floor of the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy to Aarambh Hospitality Services, an organization that offers the highest rates. Deshpande, the Maharashtra Kala Academy had submitted a reference letter to the government.
Accordingly, the work of arranging the rent of eleven residential rooms on the sixth floor of the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Art Academy has been started. The Government are pleased to approve the payment of rent at the rate of Rs. 2,76,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Seventy Six Thousand only) (excluding Goods and Services Tax) for ten years and subject to the terms and conditions of the tender issued by the original tender and certificate.
3. Project Director, P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy shall enter into necessary agreement with the said institution as per the terms and conditions of the tender and submit a copy thereof to the Government.
