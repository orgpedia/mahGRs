Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in the aforesaid No. 1, a scheme has been approved to confer the Jnanoba-Tukaram Award annually to an author who has written excellent literature/ prose on Sant literature at the all-India level. The Thematic Selection Committee was reconstituted under the Government Decision at No. 3 above, while the appointments of non-official members to the Committee have been cancelled under the Government Decision at No. 5 above. The government was considering reconstituting the selection committee for the award.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government decision, it is being approved to reconstitute the Jnanoba-Tukaram Award Selection Committee as follows.
