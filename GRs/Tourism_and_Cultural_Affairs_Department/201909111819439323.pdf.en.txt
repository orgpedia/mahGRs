Introduction: The aforementioned No. Under the decision of the government here, rules have been fixed to sanction grants to the theater production houses for the production of new plays. Accordingly, the quality of the play is determined by examining the drama examining committee formed. Under this test, grant is sanctioned to the graded plays for conducting experiments.
It was under consideration of the Government to sanction grants to the plays which were graded by the Drama Screening Committee according to the number of experiments set out in the Thematic Manual. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
