Introduction: Many social reformers, writers, poets, political leaders and freedom fighters have contributed a lot to India's independence and the betterment of the nation. The contribution of revolutionary freedom fighter Veer Savarkar is important in this. Also, Veer Savarkar's fierce patriotism and sacrifice for the country continues to inspire the youth even today. On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Swatantryaveer Savarkar on 28th May, administrative approval was given to organize various programs/ activities at the state level and district level under the above mentioned 1, with a view to honouring his work and propagating his ideas. Accordingly, the matter of financial sanction of expenditure for the special cultural programme held at Veer Savarkar Auditorium, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
