Introduction: Refer No. 1 of this section. Under the Government decision of 30th October, 2013, a scheme for providing financial assistance for the production of quality Marathi films has been implemented. For effective and smooth implementation of this scheme, Reference No. 2 here. Some amendments have been made under the Government Corrigendum dated 28th August, 2014. Now the reference No. 3 here. As per the letter dated February 2, 2017, the Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon and the members of the Marathi Film Screening Committee have suggested some amendments in the subsidy scheme. Accordingly, as per the discussions held at the government level from time to time in this regard, Reference No. The matter of making necessary changes in the government decision here was under consideration.
After considering all the suggestions received regarding the amendment in the existing scheme of sanctioning financial assistance to promote the production of quality Marathi films, the Government has decided to make the following amendments:
