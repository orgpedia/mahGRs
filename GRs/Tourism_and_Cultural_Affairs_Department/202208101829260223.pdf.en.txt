Government Circular: To commemorate 75 years of India's Independence, the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" is being celebrated across the country. The activities are expected to be conducted at various levels till 15th August, 2023. The programme is being run across various districts of the state. Under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, the above mentioned No. It has been decided to organize" Swarajya Mahotsav" in the State from 9th August to 17th August, 2022. Under the" Swarajya Mahotsav," the format of programmes/ activities to be organized at the state level/ district level/ taluka level and village level has been decided.
2. The" Group Singing of National Anthem" initiative in the planning of State level events as mentioned in Annexure-A under" Swarajya Mahotsav" is now scheduled to be held on 17 th August, 2022 at 11.00 AM.
3. General instructions are being issued to all the Ministries/ Departments and field offices under them for the successful implementation of the" Sangam National Anthem" initiative under" Swarajya Mahotsav."
It should be ensured that the group national anthem will be sung all over the state at the same time on August 17 at 11:00 am.
The participation of students and teachers from private, government and all other types of schools/ colleges/ educational institutions/ universities in the singing of this national anthem will be mandatory.
All concerned should note that the group national anthem will begin at exactly 11:00 am and the national anthem is expected to be sung between 11:00 am and 11:01 am.

4. The group knows the National Anthem at the time of singing- All concerned should take special care to create awareness among the citizens at all levels to ensure that the National Anthem is not insulted inadvertently.
All concerned agencies should appeal to all the citizens of the state to sing the national anthem on August 17 at 11.00 am.
All stakeholders should ensure that officials/ employees of private establishments, commercial establishments, institutions, government and semi-government offices, offices related to the central government and the state government should also take active part in this initiative.
All students should be given detailed information about this initiative in advance in schools/ colleges/ educational institutions/ universities. All government and private offices, schools, colleges, universities, anganwadis, anganwadis and anganwadis will remain closed.
8. The Department of School Education and the Department of Higher and Technical Education shall direct all concerned to ensure that the students should be present together in the open hall, classroom, or hall for singing the national anthem, and teachers, non-teaching staff, office bearers of educational institutions should also be involved in this activity.
The Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department and the Urban Development Department should direct all concerned to ensure active participation of all administrative officers and officials at the village level, ward level (at the level of Municipality/ Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat/ Municipal Corporation).
10. The District Collector shall regulate the district for this initiative and all the government, semi-government, cooperative and private establishments, business establishments and institutions in the district shall be informed about this initiative from the level of the Collector and make a public appeal for singing the national anthem. The tahsildars at the taluk level should also take up this initiative.
11.The mass media like print media, social media, private channels, radio, FM radio, community radio etc. should be used for awareness and publicity of the programme.
All concerned should ensure that all administrative departments contribute to the awareness, dissemination and promotion of this initiative to make the group singing of the national anthem a success through the local self-government institutions, the revenue system, all universities, schools and colleges, the police system, and all the mechanisms related to the day-to-day affairs of ordinary citizens.
