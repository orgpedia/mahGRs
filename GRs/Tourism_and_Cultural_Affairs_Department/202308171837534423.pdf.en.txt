Preamble: In order to promote and disseminate the folk art and other cultural aspects of Maharashtra worldwide through cultural programs organized in connection with the G-20 Summit held in Pune, the Department through the Directorate of Cultural Affairs was under the consideration of the Government to give administrative and financial approval to the program showcasing the culture of Maharashtra and its expenses.
