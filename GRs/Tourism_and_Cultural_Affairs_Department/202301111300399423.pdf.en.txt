The term of office of the Advisory Committee shall be for a period of three years from the date of such decision of the Government or until the reconstitution of the Committee, whichever is earlier.
The Advisory Committee will make recommendations for the selection of artists, planning of festivals, various activities, workshops, camps to be held by the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy.
Meeting Allowance to Non-Official Members of the Committee as per Government Decision, Finance Division, No. 2012/ No. 1/ Service-5, dated 24th May, 2012 and Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance as per Government Decision, Finance Division, No. 1010/ PR. No. 2/ Service-5 as per Para 12 dated 3rd March, 2010 or as amended by the Government.
