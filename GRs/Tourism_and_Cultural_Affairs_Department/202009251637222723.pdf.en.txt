Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of the event: March 11, 2020
The government is making efforts to boost tourism in the state. Mumbai is the financial capital of the country. To bring Mumbai on the world tourism map, the government is planning to develop tourism facilities at various places in Mumbai and develop these tourist places in coordination with the Mumbai Municipal Corporation, Archaeological Department, Director General of Police and Collector, Mumbai Suburban. Under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. Minister of Tourism. The meeting was held on February 13, 2020. As per the decision taken in the meeting, the decision was taken to create facilities for tourists in the places which are tourist-friendly in Mumbai city and Mumbai suburban district. The following Committees are being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Tourism):

(a) The functions of the Committee:
1) To supervise and control the implementation of the works sanctioned under the funds provided by the Government for the development of tourism in Brihanmumbai as well as the works sanctioned from the funds of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and the District Planning Committee.
(ii) To suggest measures for the development of tourism in Mumbai.
3) To resolve the obstacles in the development of tourism and to follow up the works.
4) Review the progress of tourism development works from time to time.
5) Decision to make Brihanmumbai famous and popular at the national and international level to increase tourism.
(c) The Board shall meet at least once a month.
