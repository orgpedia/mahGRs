Order: Mr. Ramdas Khedkar Resident Deputy Collector, Nashik is being appointed as Deputy Director (Tourism), Regional Office, Directorate of Tourism, Nashik on deputation subject to the following terms and conditions: a) Deputy Director (Tourism) Regional Office, Nashik in the pay scale of Rs. 67700 to Rs. 208700) and Shri Ramdas Khedkar till the date of assumption of charge of the said post or until further orders, subject to demotion.
(b) The service of the Deputy Collector shall commence on the date on which Mr. Ramdas Khedkar, Resident Deputy Collector, Nashik, joins the office of the Deputy Collector and shall end on the date on which he resumes charge of his original office.
(c) If the Government considers it necessary in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority i.e. Revenue and Forest Department shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
d) If their services are not required by a non-employee employer, the government i.e. the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs will have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the Department shall give a notice of three months to the Revenue and Forest Department; and d) Mr. Ramdas Khedkar shall be repatriated to the parent Department after giving at least three months notice to the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs that he intends to return to the parent Department.
e) Shri Ramdas Khedkar, Maharashtra Civil Service (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) The provisions of Rules 39 and 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981 and the provisions of Annexure III thereunder shall continue to apply. Further, the provisions of General Administration Department, S.R.V.-2011/ S.No. 137/12 dated 17/12/2016 shall also apply.
f) The service matters of Shri Ramdas Khedkar will be handled by his parent department.
