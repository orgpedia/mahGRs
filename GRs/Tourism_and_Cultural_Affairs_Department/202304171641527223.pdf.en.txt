Government's decision:
The post of Chief Administrative Officer of Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai is vacant. A proposal has been received from the Revenue and Forest Department to appoint Mrs. Geeta Nishikant Deshpande, District Superintendent, Land Records (attached), Deputy Director, Land Records, Konkan Region, Mumbai Group-A, as the Chief Administrative Officer of Maharashtra Film, Theatre, Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai. Accordingly, the post of Chief Administrative Officer, Maharashtra Film, Theatre, Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai, Group A, has been reduced to S23, Rs. 67,700-2, 08,700, in the pay scale of S20, Rs. 56,000-1, 77,500, and the post of Chief Administrative Officer in this Corporation, Mrs. Geeta Nishikant Deshpande, District Superintendent (attached), Deputy Director, Land Records, Konkan Region, Mumbai, Group A, is being appointed on deputation.
The said deputation shall continue for a period of three years from the date on which he enters upon the office of the deputation or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier. The terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 17th December, 2016 and dated 16th February, 2018 in the context of General Administration Department shall continue to be applicable to Smt.
