The Proposal:
On the occasion of" 350th Shivarajyabhishek Ceremony," various programs/ activities are being organized at Forts Raigad and Pachad. 1 The District Collector, Raigad has been authorised as per the decision of the Government here. The government has also directed the Collector, Raigad, to demand the expenses related to the cultural programs to be organized by the Collector through the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to give administrative approval to the proposed expenditure submitted to the Government under the above mentioned 2 and 3 on the matters related to the cultural program to be held on the occasion of the" 350th Shivrajyabhishek Ceremony 2023."
Government's decision:
The administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution for the cultural programme to be held on the occasion of" 350th Shivarajyabhishek Ceremony" at Mauje Fort, Raigad and Pachad from 01.06.2023 to 07.06.2023 at an estimated cost of Rs. 4,77,52, 000/- (Rupees Four Crore Seventy Seven Lakh Fifty Two Thousand only) as mentioned in the table below.
The details of the cost of the program under the 350th Shivrajyabhishek Ceremony are as follows:
