Introduction: Swatantryaveer Savarkar has made a significant contribution in the freedom struggle of India and in the formation of numerous revolutionaries who laid down their lives for the country.
At the same time, while the Savarkar brothers were in jail, their fellow sisters Bai, Mai and Tai of the Savarkar family kept the torch of national work burning for the liberation of Mother India. The presentation of the play" Triveni," based on the sacrifice made by the trio, their dedication and Swatantryaveer Savarkar's role in the formation of Swarajya, was jointly organized by the Directorate of Cultural Affairs and the Institute of Resonance Arts, Multi-Purpose and Sensitization, Nagpur on June 26, 2023 at Nagpur.
Administrative approval was given to the theater experiment under the government decision of the above mentioned 1. However, now the matter of giving financial approval to the expenses incurred for organizing the theatrical experiment" Triveni" was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the aegis of Directorate of Cultural Affairs and Resonance Arts and Culture, Multi-Purpose Institute and Samvedna Sanstha, Nagpur, based on the sacrifices made by Bai, Mai and Tai of the Savarkar family, their dedication and the role of Swatantryaveer Savarkar in the formation of Swarajya, an amount of Rs.
