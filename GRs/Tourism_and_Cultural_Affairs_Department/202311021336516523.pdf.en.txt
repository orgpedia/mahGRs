The Proposal:
Shri Gajbadevi Temple at Mithbav Vaitambaldega in Deogarh Taluka is situated on the banks of the vast sea. Many tourists and pilgrims visit this place. Many times tourists face inconvenience due to lack of facilities. It was proposed to create facilities for the development of this tourist spot.
Government's decision
