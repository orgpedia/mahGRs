Preamble: In order to contain the spread of COVID-19 virus, the Government has relaxed the restrictions imposed on various activities/ works under the Government Orders issued from time to time. The Government has received requests from various organizations/ institutions in the sector to allow the opening of closed halls/ other events in open spaces which have been closed in the wake of COVID-19. In view of this, the State Government vide No. As per the order, permission was given to start cinema halls and theaters, on the same lines, the government was considering to approve the guidelines for the start of closed halls and other cultural programs in open spaces. Following this, the government decided to take action.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the wake of COVID-19, the Government is permitting the opening of closed halls/ open spaces and other cultural programmes in the State in a regulated manner with effect from 5th November, 2020 subject to the guidelines contained in Annexure-A annexed to this Government Decision.
2. Keeping in view the noble intention of the Government behind granting this permission, it is necessary for all concerned to conduct the events in closed halls/ open spaces in a manner that does not violate any of the restrictions imposed by the Central Government and State Governments in the context of COVID-19. Action will be taken against those found violating these guidelines.
