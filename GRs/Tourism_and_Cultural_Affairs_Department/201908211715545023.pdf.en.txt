Government Decision: The State Cultural Awards are conferred on eminent personalities for their outstanding work in the field of culture. The awards are given in 12 categories- theatre, drama, dance, vocal, kirtan, kaladaan, instrumental music, shahiri, sub-classical music, film, folk art and tribal Girijan art. The award of Rs. 1.00 lakh and a citation. A committee has been formed to select the awardees. As per the recommendations of the Committee, the State Cultural Awards, 2019 are being announced to the following dignitaries.
