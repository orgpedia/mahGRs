Preamble: - The state protected monument, the Vadeshwar Mahadev Temple, is located in Ambhai, a village about 20 km from Golgaon on the Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar to Anjitha Caves road. The temple is visited by a large number of devotees. It is a masterpiece of medieval architecture and the temple has suffered extensive damage over time. Much of the temple is built in jambha stones, with a small barav located next to the temple. The top of the temple has been extensively carved and the mandapa portion of the temple has been extensively damaged. The temple has been desecrated by the later painting of the interior and exterior of the temple by the local people. A special meeting of the Maharashtra State Cabinet on the development work of Marathwada was held at Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar on 16.09.2023. The decision taken in this meeting includes the preservation of historical architecture in the Marathwada region. Chichhod District Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar State Protected Monument is being preserved and repaired.
