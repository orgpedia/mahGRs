The Proposal:
The aforementioned No. 1 By the decision of the Government here, Bharat Ratna Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Promotion Scheme has been approved. The prize money of the award is Rs. 5.00 lakh, a citation and a memento. The awardees were selected by a committee headed by Bharat Ratna Pt. The Government was considering to announce the name of the awardee for the year 2020 for the Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Lifetime Achievement Award.
Government's decision:
Bharat Ratna 2020 Bhimsen Joshi Lifetime Achievement Award in Classical Music is being conferred on Smt. N. Rajam. The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh, a citation and a memento.
