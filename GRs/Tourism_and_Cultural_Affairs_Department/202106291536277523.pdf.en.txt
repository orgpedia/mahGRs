Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure a post in the government service for the disabled. Accordingly, as per the Central Government notification dated 04.01.2021, a notification has been issued to ensure the posts of various cadres in the government service for the disabled. According to the notification, all the administrative departments have been instructed to ensure the posts for the disabled in the state government establishments as per the government decision of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance dated February 2, 2021. Accordingly, a circular has been issued on March 2, 2021, to ensure the posts on the establishment of the Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai and Maharashtra State Hindi, Sindhi, Gujarati Sahitya Academy, Mumbai under this department. However, the post of driver in the Directorate of Cultural Affairs was not excluded from the disability category.
The matter was under consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
The Expert Committee constituted for the purpose of determining the posts of Divyangjan in the Government Service in its meeting held on 19th and 20th May, 2021 has decided to exclude the post of' Driver' in Group-C from the list of Divyangjan.
2. As per the above decision taken by the Expert Committee constituted for the purpose of determining the post of Divyang in the Government Service, the post of" Driver" in Group-C in the Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being excluded from the determination of the post of Divyang under this Government decision.
