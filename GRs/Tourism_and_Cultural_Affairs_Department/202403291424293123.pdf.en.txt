Accordingly, the No. 3 Under the Government Decision, the Department of Higher and Technical Education has given administrative approval for a fund of Rs. 10.00 crores to set up Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Adhyasan Kendra at Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi to provide information about the thoughts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, as well as courses on military strategy and forts and fortifications of the Marathi Empire. Government Decision: - Under the umbrella scheme for "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Government of India is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 10.00 crore (Rupees Ten Crore only) for the establishment of "Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Adhyasan Kendra" at Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi.
