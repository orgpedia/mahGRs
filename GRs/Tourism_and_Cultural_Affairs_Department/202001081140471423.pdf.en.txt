Preamble: The 70th Anniversary Celebrations of the Indian Republic Day will be held on 26th January, 2020 at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai. A cultural programme will be held as part of the celebrations. The matter of approving the concept picture of this tableau and the organizing cost of the tableau parade was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The tableau to be taken out by the State Government on the theme" 350 years of Sarakhel Kanhoji Angre" will be taken out by the Department of Cultural Affairs during the 70th Anniversary Celebrations of the Republic Day of India to be held in Mumbai on 26th January, 2020. The expenditure of Rs. 47,15,000/- (Rupees Forty Seven Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) towards the concept and planning of this tableau parade is being approved as per the following details.
