Government Decision: The Department of Government Decision. Policy for funding of International Film Festival Organizations has been announced on 18.10.2012. In line with this policy, the Kalamaharshi Baburao Painter Film Society, Kolhapur, has issued a circular. For the Fifth Kolhapur International Film Festival held from 14.12.2017 to 21.12.2017, Rs. The financial assistance of Rs. 4.00 lakh (Rupees Four Lakh only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
The financial assistance sanctioned will be admissible as per the Bombay Financial Rules.
(2) The financial assistance sanctioned shall be non-recurring and subject to the terms and conditions of the regular financial assistance from the Government.
3. Funds should be utilised only for the purpose for which they are sanctioned and immediately. Failing which, the amount of financial assistance will have to be returned to the government along with the prevailing interest rate of the bank.
It will be mandatory to submit the Utilization Certificate, Profit and Loss Account and Balance Sheet at least 3 months after the conclusion of the film festival.
At least one representative of the Government shall be nominated by the Institute on the Awards/ Felicitation Committee to be presented at the Film Festival.
6. The film festival should be highlighted as" in association with Department of Cultural Affairs, Government of Maharashtra" through advertisement, invitation card, etc. The cooperation of the Government of Maharashtra will be clearly understood by everyone associated with the festival as well as the media, in such a way that the organizers should highlight it before and during the festival.
7. Governing body to give decision. It will be mandatory to fulfill all the terms and conditions of the policy announced under 18.10.2012.
The Government shall have full powers to modify, amend or interpret all the above rules and the orders issued by the Government in this regard shall be final and binding on the parties concerned and in case of dispute between the two parties signing the agreement in this regard, the decision of the Principal Secretary (Tourism and Cultural Affairs) shall be final and binding on the parties. However, no party is allowed to approach the court if arbitration is appointed.
