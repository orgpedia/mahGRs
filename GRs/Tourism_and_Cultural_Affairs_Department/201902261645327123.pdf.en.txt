Dr. Kisan Maharaj Sakhare was selected for the Jnanoba Tukaram Award for the year 2017-18. The award ceremony was held on 31st August, 2018 at Annabhau Sathe Natyagruha, Pune and the matter of sanctioning the expenditure incurred for the event was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, the expenditure of Rs. 8,00,658/- (Rupees Eight Lakhs Six Hundred and Fifty Eight only) incurred on the Gyanoba Tukaram Award Ceremony for the year 2017-18 is being given ex-post facto approval as per the following details.
