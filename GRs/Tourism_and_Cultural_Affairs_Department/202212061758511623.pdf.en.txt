Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to constitute a committee for planning cultural programmes to be organized under the Cultural Department on the occasion of Marathwada Mukti Sangram Amrit Mahotsav which started from 17 th September, 2022.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to form a committee as follows to plan the cultural programs to be organized under the Department of Cultural Affairs on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Mukti Sangram.

It will be the task of the committee to recommend to the government through the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, about the planning and outline of the programs to be organized under the Department of Cultural Affairs on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Mukti Sangram. The Committee will function till 30th September, 2023 or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier.
Travel and Meeting Allowance: The non-official members of the committee will be entitled to travel allowance and meeting allowance as per the prevailing government decision of the Finance Department.
For this purpose, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as Controlling Officer and Accountant Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
