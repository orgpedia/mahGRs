The Proposal:
The aforementioned No. Under the decision of the government here, a scheme has been started to honor the senior theater artist with an award in the name of Natvarya Prabhakar Panashikar for outstanding performance in the theater. The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh, a citation and a memento. Accordingly, the government was considering to announce the Natavarya Prabhakar Panashekar Rangabhoomi Lifetime Achievement Award for the year 2019-20.
Government Decision: Natavarya Prabhakar Panashikar Rangabhoomi Lifetime Achievement Award for the year 2019-20. Ratnakar Matkari is being declared dead.
