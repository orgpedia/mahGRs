Government's decision:
In order to promote tourism in Nanded district, the Directorate of Tourism, District Administration, Nanded and Gurudwara Administration, Nanded have agreed to organize Veer Bal Diwas and Nanded Mahotsav at Nanded subject to the following terms and conditions.
2) Terms and conditions
All the expenses of the programs proposed by the district administration should be done through the District Annual Plan, District Council, Municipal Corporation and CSR.
Travel arrangements for the tourists/ devotees coming to other tourist places in the district should be made at the local level through MSRTC.
Religious programmes will be held in the Gurdwara premises while cultural programmes will be held in public places outside the Gurdwara premises.
The publicity campaign of the event will be carried out by the Directorate of Tourism and other field level programs will be carried out by the District Administration, Nanded.
For this festival, a committee is being formed under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Nanded and the members of the committee will be as follows:
1 Collector, Nanded Chairman 2 District Superintendent of Police, Nanded Member 3 Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation, Nanded Member CEO, Zilla Parishad, Nanded Member Deputy Director, Divisional Directorate of Tourism, Aurangabad. The Member Secretary
The District Collector, Nanded will also act as the Nodal Officer for organizing the festival.
