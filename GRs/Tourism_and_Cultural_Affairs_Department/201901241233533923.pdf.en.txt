INTRODUCTION: As per the communication dated 3.11.2018 issued in the context of Cultural Affairs Sub-Division under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Ministry of External Affairs has decided to bear the cost of transportation for the persons coming for the orientation programme" Know India Programme (KIP)" to create awareness about Indian cultural heritage and contemporary aspects among the youth of Indian origin in foreign countries in Mahabaleshwar from 24th to 28th January, 2019. The Tourism Sub-Division has requested to do as per the directions given by the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for this arrangement and approving the expenditure was under consideration.
Government Decision: In order to promote the development of tourism destinations in the State of Maharashtra at national and international level and to celebrate various tourism festivals, an amount of Rs. 78.00 crore has been budgeted for the Department of Tourism for the financial year 2018-2019 under the head" Publicity (Main AccountsShirsha Sanketank-34521931)." As per the Government Circular dated 2.4.2018 of the Department of Finance, 70% of the budget outlay i.e. Rs. 54.60 crore is available for disbursement at the end of the month of December, 2018 as per the Monthly Fund Distribution System.
