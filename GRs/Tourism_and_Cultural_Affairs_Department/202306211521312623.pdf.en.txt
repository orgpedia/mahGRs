In the meeting held on 06.03.2023, the proposal for the revised pattern of 39 posts in the Regional Office of the Department of Philosophy, Mumbai was placed for consideration. The minutes of the meeting No. 4 As per the letter received from the Department, the revised figure of' 33 regular posts+ 6 manpower services to be taken by external agencies' in the Regional Office of the Department of Philosophy, Mumbai has been approved. Accordingly, the matter of approving the revised pattern of posts in the zonal office of the Department of Philosophy, Mumbai was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As indicated in the accompanying prospectus A and B under this Government Decision, the revised pattern of 33 regular posts+ 6 manpower services to be taken up by external agencies in the Regional Office of the Department of Visitors, Mumbai is being approved. The posts shown in the' Return-A' at No. 11 to 13 are being declared as dead cadre. The post will lapse after the post falls vacant due to retirement or other reasons. After that, the services should be taken over by external agencies as per the requirement. Under this condition, it is being approved to take the services of 6 posts as shown in' Return-B' through external agencies.
