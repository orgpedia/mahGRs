The matter of administrative approval of the closing cultural program and its expenditure on the occasion of the year was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: An amount of Rs. 18,65,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand only) is being administratively sanctioned for the celebration of the Birth Centenary of Padma Shri Shaheer Krishnarao Sable.
The said expenditure should be met from the financial provision approved under the head Z- 2, 2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Promotion, Various Festivals and Other Programmes (22051507)-50 for the year 2023-24.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
