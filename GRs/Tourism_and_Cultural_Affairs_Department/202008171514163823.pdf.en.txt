To set up a State Level Task Force for revival of tourism sector in the wake of COVID-19.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs
Government Decision No: TDS 2020/5/ P.No.268/ Tourism,
Madame Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032,
Date: 17th of August 2020
Introduction: During the lockdown period imposed across the country due to COVID-19, the tourism sector of the state has suffered a lot and the tourism sector of the state is facing various challenges and problems. There is a need for concerted efforts by the government and all stakeholders in the tourism sector to address this crisis and meet the challenges.
The Government was contemplating to constitute a State Level Task Force to effectively deal with various challenges/ issues faced by the tourism sector in the State during the lockdown period imposed across the State in the wake of COVID-19 and to discuss the action plan in this regard.
Government Decision: To effectively deal with the various challenges/ problems faced by the tourism sector in the state during the lockdown period imposed across the state in the wake of COVID-19 and to discuss the action plan in this regard. A State Level Task Force is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Tourism) as follows:

The functions of the Task Force shall be as follows:
The task force will discuss the action plan for the revival of the tourism sector in the state.
The task force will suggest Covid-19 SOP protocols, investment and marketing activities.
It will help in sustaining Public Private Partnership (PPP) in tourism development.
