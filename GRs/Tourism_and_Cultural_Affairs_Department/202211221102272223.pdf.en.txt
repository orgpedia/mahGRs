Preamble: The State Agro Tourism Policy has been approved as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 to achieve tourism development by creating a well-planned system to provide supplementary business to the farmers of the State and to enable the urban tourists to enjoy tourism in a peaceful, natural place.
The state government has decided to set up a state-level Maharashtra Agro Tourism and Rural Tourism Development Committee to promote agro-tourism in the state.
Appointments of experts in the field of agri-tourism appointed under the Government decision at reference no. 2 on the' State Level Maharashtra Agri-Tourism and Rural Tourism Development Committee' have been cancelled under the Government decision at reference no. 3.
The government was considering appointing experts in the field of agri-tourism on this committee.
Government Decision: The government has taken a decision to promote and publicize agro-tourism in the state. Approval is being given to appoint the following experts in the field of Agro Tourism at the State Level Maharashtra Agro Tourism and Rural Tourism Development Committee constituted under 28.09.2020:
