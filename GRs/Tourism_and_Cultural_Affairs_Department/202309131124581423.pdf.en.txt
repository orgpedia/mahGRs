The coronation ceremony of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of Hindavi Swarajya, was held on Jyeshtha Shuddha Trayodashi Saka 1595, i.e. 6 June 1674. Accordingly, the 350th year of Shivrajyabhisheka is starting from Jyeshtha Shuddha Trayodashi Shakya 1945 i.e. June 2, 2023. The State Government has been organizing various programmes/ activities throughout the year on this occasion.
It was under the consideration of the government to give administrative and financial approval to the expenditure on the meetings held from time to time for the planning of the programs to be organized on the occasion of the" 350th Shivrajyabhishek Mahotsav" and the advertisements published in the newspaper by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for the publicity of the program.
Government Decision: The amount of expenditure on the meetings held from time to time at Raj Bhavan, Mumbai on the occasion of 350th Shivarajyabhishek Mahotsav and on the advertisements published in the newspaper by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations is Rs. 15,93,653/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Ninety Three Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty Three only).
