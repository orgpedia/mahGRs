Introduction: As per the decision of the Government in the aforesaid Act No. 1, the Gana Samrajini Lata Mangeshkar Award is given to an artist who has done outstanding work in the field of singing and music for a long time. The prize money of the award is Rs. 5.00 lakh, a citation and a memento.
The government was considering the names of awardees for the year 2020 as selected by the committee constituted to recommend the awardees for this award.
Government's decision:
Usha Mangeshkar will be conferred with the prestigious Lata Mangeshkar Award for the year 2020. The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh, a citation and a memento.
