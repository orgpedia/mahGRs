The Proposal: -
The Maharashtra State Telugu Sahitya Akademi is a non-governmental organisation under the aegis of the Government of Maharashtra.
Members were appointed. The government was considering cancelling the appointments of non-official members on the committee.
The Government's Decisions:
Non-official on Maharashtra State Telugu Sahitya Academy in the aforesaid Government Decision
The appointments of the members have been cancelled.
