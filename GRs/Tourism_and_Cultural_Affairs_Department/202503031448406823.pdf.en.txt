The Proposal: -
On the occasion of "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," various innovative events are being organized along with regular events. The events / activities are held with the motto of Diamond, Amrit, Shatabdi of such person / work of art to honour those who have made rich contributions to the field of art and literature and to make the new generation aware of that work / person / significance and to add to the knowledge of the new generation through that information. Against the same backdrop, administrative sanction was granted under the above-mentioned Government Decision for organizing and incurring expenditure on the cultural programme on the occasion of the birth centenary of the great personality / artist. The government was now considering granting financial approval for the event.
Government Decision: - A sum of Rs. 90,00,000 - (Rupees Ninety Lakhs only) is being provided as financial sanction under this Government Decision for the 20 Celebrations of the Birth Centenary, Silver Jubilee, Golden Jubilee Celebrations of the following personalities / artists with a total outlay of Rs.4.5 lakhs per event: -
