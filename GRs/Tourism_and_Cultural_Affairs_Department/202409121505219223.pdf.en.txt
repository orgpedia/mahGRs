Gagangarh Fort, Ta. Gaganbawda, Dist. Kolhapur
It has been declared a state protected monument.
Proposal for a preliminary notification to be made
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs
Government Decision No: RASANSMA-2023 / P.No.310 / SC-3
Madam Cama Marg, Martyrdom Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: 12th September, 2024
The Government's Decisions:
Gagangarh Fort, Ta. As the due process for declaring Gaganbawda, District Kolhapur as a State Protected Monument has been completed, the Manager, Government Central Press, Charni Road, Mumbai is hereby directed by this Government Decision to publish the preliminary notification accompanying this decision in the Government of Maharashtra General Gazette Part-IV in Marathi and English and to forward 30 copies of the notification to the Additional Chief Secretary, Department of Cultural Affairs, Ministry of Mumbai and the Director, Directorate of Archaeological Museums, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums shall arrange for a copy of such preliminary notification to be placed at a prominent place near the monument. The objections and suggestions should be sought and acted upon within two months from the date of publication of the aforesaid preliminary notification and the said preliminary notification should be published on the website of the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums as well as in various local newspapers.
The Government decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
