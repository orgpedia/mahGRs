Introduction:- The Swadesh Darshan Scheme was launched in 2015 to develop the domestic tourism sector and under this scheme 100 percent subsidy is received from the Central Government on the project cost.
The objectives of the scheme are:
1) To raise the local economic level through tourism.
2) To provide employment and self-employment to the local people.
To develop skills of local people through tourism.
Increase private sector investment in the tourism sector.
Preserving local culture and natural resources.
Under the Swadesh Darshan 2.0 scheme, a State Steering Committee will be set up at the state level as per the guidelines prescribed by the Central Government. defined in 7.2.1.
