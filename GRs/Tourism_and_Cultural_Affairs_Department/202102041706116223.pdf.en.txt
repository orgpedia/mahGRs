Conditions: 1. In the advertisement for this festival, it will be necessary to give maximum publicity to the Cultural Affairs Department of the Government of Maharashtra.

4. The grant sanctioned shall be non-recurring and subject to the terms and conditions of the regular grant of the Government.
5. Utilization certificate in respect of this grant shall be required to be submitted within one month.
(6) The grant shall be utilised only for the purpose for which it is sanctioned and immediately.
If not, the subsidy amount will have to be returned to the government at the prevailing bank interest rate.
7. The cost of the festival should be certified by a chartered accountant and the details of the deposit should be submitted to the government.
The Government shall have full powers to modify, amend or interpret all the above criteria/ conditions and the orders passed by the Government in this regard shall be final and binding on the concerned. In case of dispute between the two parties, the decision of the Principal Secretary (Cultural Affairs) shall be final and binding on both the parties. However, no party is allowed to approach the court if arbitration is appointed. The Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being authorized to enter into an agreement with the above terms and conditions.
Before disbursement of the said grant, the Director, Cultural Affairs shall first ensure the fulfillment of the aforesaid 6th to 7th grants for the 18th Pune International Film Festival and thereafter disburse the grants for the 19th Pune International Film Festival.
The decision was taken by the organising committee. It will be mandatory to fulfill all the terms and conditions of the policy announced under 18.10.2012.
The Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai is declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for drawing and disbursing the relevant payments.
The expenditure for the same shall be met from the available provision for the financial year 2020-2021 under the head" Demand No.ZD-2, 2205 Art& Culture (00) 102, Art& Culture Operations (03) (06) International Film Festival (2205 1581), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
