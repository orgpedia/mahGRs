Pandharpur is a historical and cultural heritage of about 800 years and is one of the few religious and spiritual sites in the world. A large number of devotees from across the State and outside the State are expected to participate in the celebrations.
Every year on the occasion of Ashadhi Wari, two different Dindis are organized in the name of Shri Sant Tukoba Maharaj and Shri Sant Dnyaneshwar Maharaj from Dehu and Alandi. These two main dindis travel on two routes every year and lakhs of devotees travel on these palkhi routes every year. The programme was held at No. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. Now the matter of financial approval of the actual cost of the program was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The total expenditure incurred on the occasion of Devshayani Ashadhi Ekadashi to welcome Saint Palkhi on 28th June, 2023 at Mauje Isabavi is Rs. An amount of Rs. 3,61,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) is being provided as financial sanction under this Government Resolution.
The expenditure is to be met from the financial provision approved under the head ZD-2,2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Promotion, Various Festivals and Other Programmes (22051507)-50 for the year 2023-24.
