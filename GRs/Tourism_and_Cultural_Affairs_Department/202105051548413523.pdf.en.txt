The government is taking preventive measures to control the spread of Covid-19 in the state. It was the responsibility of the government to create awareness in the society about the precautions to be taken to prevent the infection and the vaccination to be done accordingly.
Government Decision: To create awareness among the artists in the field of experimental art in every district about the precautions to be taken to prevent the spread of COVID-19 in the state and the vaccination to be done accordingly, Rs. 5,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores only) is being approved through this Government Resolution.
2. Awareness programmes will be conducted by folk artists of various folk art forms, performing artists of experimental art.
3. Subjects to be chosen for awareness: In the wake of Corona, artists should create awareness through their art on topics such as marriage ceremonies in the presence of a minimum number of people, religious matters in the presence of a minimum number of people, festivals, customs, events, festivals, use of masks, hand washing, care of the elderly and young children, government's Corona guidelines, vaccination, etc. as well as on topics suggested by the Collector or the officials authorized by him according to the local situation and need.

Selection of artists: The selection of artists for this awareness program will be done by the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai with the help of Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. Priority will be given to local artists. The district-wise lists of selected artists will be made available to the respective district collectors through the Directorate of Cultural Affairs. The awareness programmes will be conducted by a committee under the chairmanship of the District Collector.
Participation of the Collector's Office: Corona awareness programs will be controlled by a local committee headed by the concerned Collector. The committee will monitor the programme. The committee will comprise of the respective district information officers of the Information and Public Relations Department. The District Information Officer is expected to act as a coordinator in this campaign. The District Collectors are being empowered to hold programmes of the artists from the list of artists received from the Directorate of Cultural Affairs in the district as and when required.
Work orders and payment of artist honorarium: Work orders shall be issued to the artists by the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs or by the officers authorized by him. After the completion of the program, the director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, will deposit the honorarium in the account of the artists as per the recommendation of the District Control Committee.
7. Certifying Authority:
In rural areas, after the performance of the program at the Gram Panchayat level in group or solo form, the headmaster/ gram sevak/ talathi of the Zilla Parishad School in the village will be required to give a certificate to the artist about the completion of the program.
The application form must be accompanied by a certificate.
In urban areas, after the performance of the program in group or solo form, the officer authorized by the Ward Officer in the concerned Municipal Corporation area/ officer authorized by the Chief Officer in the Municipal Area/ Talathi will be required to give a certificate to the artist about the completion of the program. The said certificate has to be attached with the demand letter for the same honorarium 8. Artiste honorarium:
