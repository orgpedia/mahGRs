Maharashtra: Maharashtra government has decided to confer Lata Mangeshkar Award, the highest honour in the field of performing arts, to artistes who have excelled in the field of singing/ music for a long time. The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh and a citation. The committee has been constituted as per the decision of the Government dated 12th September, 2018 to select the artists under this award.
As per the selection committee's recommendation for the prestigious Lata Mangeshkar Award for the year 2018, the award will be presented to renowned music composer Vijay Patil aka Ram Laxman.
