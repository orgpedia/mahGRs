Preamble: - The minutes of the meeting of the Core Committee constituted under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav held on 5th December, 2022, vide Notification No. Issued under letters 1 and 2. According to the document, a total of ₹43.36 crore has been approved for "Symposiums, Photo Exhibitions, Documentary Production, 3D Scanning and Printing of Antiquities, Documentation" and ₹90.00 crore for conservation and conservation projects of other protected monuments on the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav.
Accordingly, the No. 3 Government decisions here have given administrative approval for the production of documentaries on state protected monuments and government museums.
Funding under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav was under consideration of the government in accordance with this administrative approval.
Government Decision: - Under an umbrella scheme for "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums is being permitted to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 14,22,13, 500 / - (Rupees Fourteen Crore Twenty Two Lakh Thirteen Thousand Five Hundred Only) (inclusive of all taxes) for making documentaries on State Protected Monuments and Government Museums.
The expenditure shall be met from the financial provision approved for the year under the head Account Title Demand No. ZD-2,2205, Art & Culture, 102, Art & Culture, (13) Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, (13) (01) Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav Under Programme (2205 3733) -50 Other Expenditure (Programme).
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the controlling officer for the distribution of funds. Also, they are being empowered to declare the officer in the office of the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums as the Controller. The officer authorised by the concerned Controller is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
