Read:- Letter from the Directorate of Cultural Affairs No. 3381, dated June 9, 2023.
Introduction: Swatantryaveer Savarkar has made a significant contribution in the freedom struggle of India and in the formation of numerous revolutionaries who laid down their lives for the country. At the same time, while the Savarkar brothers were in jail, their fellow sisters Bai, Mai and Tai of the Savarkar family kept the torch of national work burning for the liberation of Mother India. The presentation of the play" Triveni," based on the sacrifice made by these three, their dedication and the role of Swatantryaveer Savarkar in the formation of Swarajya, will be held in Nagpur on June 26, 2023, under the auspices of the Directorate of Cultural Affairs and the Institute of Resonance Arts, Multipurpose and Sensitivity. The matter of giving administrative approval to the expenses incurred for organizing the play" Triveni" was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the auspices of the Directorate of Cultural Affairs and the Resonance Arts, Multipurpose and Sensitivity Institute, Nagpur, an amount of Rs. 2,20,500/- (Rupees Two Lakh Twenty Thousand Five Hundred only) is being given administrative approval under this Government Decision to organize the play" Triveni" based on the sacrifices made by Bai, Mai and Tai of the Savarkar family, their dedication and Swatantryaveer Savarkar's role in the formation of Swaraj.
