Introduction: The pilot nomination of the heritage site is sent to UNESCO by the Archaeological Survey of India, New Delhi for declaration of Aurangabad as a World Heritage City.
To prepare the proposal for this nomination and to prepare the necessary documents for the nomination process, the organization of the conservation architect with experience in this work should be selected through open tender. The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision of 1.
Accordingly, the tender of Tejaswini Aphale Associates, Aurangabad, one of the technically qualified conservation architects, was awarded at a minimum (Rs. 32,39,100/-) as aforesaid No. The Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums has requested for approval of the tender vide letter No. 2. The matter was under consideration of the government. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
May Tejaswini Afle Associates, Aurangabad, who has the experience of this work, has donated Rs. Minimum tender of Rs. 32,39,100/- (Rupees Thirty Two Lakhs Forty Nine Thousand Hundred only) is being approved.
These works are of a special kind and need to be done according to archaeological indications. The proposal for the Minimum Tender submitted by the Director Archaeology for this work is being approved as per the powers vested in the administrative departments under Para No. 220 of the Maharashtra Public Works Rules Book No. 6 in Part I Sub-Division 5 of the Maharashtra Financial Powers Rules Book 1978.
