Government Decisions: Many institutions, organizations, colleges, universities, libraries, museums and private individuals in the state have nationally important documents. The National Archives of India, Government of India, New Delhi is funding the preservation of these important and rare documents/ books.
A State Level Screening Committee (SLSC) has been constituted to examine the proposals. 1 here. The decision was taken on 26.06.2015. Since the term of the committee had expired, the matter of reconstitution of the committee was under the consideration of the government.
Now the government is approving the reconstitution of the state level scrutiny committee. The composition of the committee will be as follows:

The term of appointment of members to the State Level Scrutiny Committee shall be for a period of 3 years or until further orders of the Government whichever is earlier. The jurisdiction of the Commission shall be the State of Maharashtra. The Committee shall hold at least one meeting in a financial year or more as may be required.
The functions of the State Level Committee shall be as follows: (a) To receive applications/ proposals from various non-governmental organizations, institutions and individuals under the purview of the Committee for the Grant-in Aid Scheme of National Archives of India, Government of India, New Delhi.
To examine the general aspects of the application/ proposal to ascertain the following:
1) Does the application/ proposal meet the criteria of the financing scheme or how? (ii) To examine the present status of the applying organization/ institution/ individual and their ability to make commensurate financial contribution to the scheme from their own funds.
(iii) To examine the significance and feasibility of the proposal/ project.
