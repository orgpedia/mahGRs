Procedure for constitution of the Committee:- The names of the Chairman and Vice-Chairman and other non-official members of the committee, except the Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban districts, shall be recommended by the Hon'ble Governor of the district. The guardian minister will send the order to the additional chief executive officer of the concerned district, the zila parishad, and the additional chief executive officer of the concerned zila parishad will immediately issue orders for the formation of the committee. Also, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai, for Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban districts, proposed to appoint eminent persons in the field of literature/ art as Chairman, Vice-Chairman and other non-official members. The Minister will submit to the Secretary (Urban Development) and after his approval, the order for the formation of district level committees for Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban will be issued by the Directorate immediately.
Duration of the Committee: The term of the Committee shall be for a period of three years. The Committee shall be reconstituted every three years. However, till the reconstitution of the committee, the existing committee will continue to function and the process of selecting litterateurs/ artists for the award at the district level will continue through it. So that there is no delay in the process of selection of litterateurs/ artists for the honorarium by the committee and they are not deprived of the honorarium.
