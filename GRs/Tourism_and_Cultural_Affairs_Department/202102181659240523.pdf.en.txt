The Proposal:
The authority to select the' B' category tourist destinations in the district and submit the proposal to the Government has been delegated to the Committee headed by the Collector in the Government Resolution in Reference No. 1, under the Government Resolution in Reference No. 2. Also, the criteria and procedure for declaring' B' category tourist destinations in the state have been fixed in the government decision in reference number 3.
2. Shrivardhan in Raigad district is about 20 km south of Murud Janjira Fort and about 4.80 km north of Shri Kshetra Harihareshwar, also known as' Dakshin Kashi'. It is located on the Arabian Sea coast. A large number of tourists visit Shrivardhan because of the ancient temples located in and around the place, religious places in Sri Kshetra Harihareshwar, Dive Agar and other nearby areas and the vast sea shore.
3." Shrivardhan" is a tourist spot situated on the Arabian Sea coast since ancient times. The city has a coastline of about 4 km. The climate of Shrivardhan is always cold due to the presence of the Red Hills in the north, the sea on one side and the high mountains on the other side and the adjoining Kalinje creek in the east.
4. District Collector, Raigad. As per the letter dated 14/12/2020, it is recommended to accord' B' category tourist status to the tourist place" Shrivardhan, G. Raigad." Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of giving' B' category status to Shrivardhan and G. Raigad tourist destinations.

Government Decision No. TDC- 2020/12/ P. No. 837/ Tourism,
Government Decision: The tourist spot" Shrivardhan, G. Raigad" is visited by approximately more than 5 lakh tourists every year. Considering the state level importance of this tourist place, the number of tourists visiting this tourist place, historical and geographical importance etc., the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Government vide Decision No. TDS- 2016/11/ P.S. 862/ Tourism, DTHS, Govt. The Government hereby approves declaration of' Shrivardhan, G. Raigad' as a' B' category tourist destination as per the criteria and procedure laid down for declaration of' B' category tourist destinations in the State under 25/4/2017.
