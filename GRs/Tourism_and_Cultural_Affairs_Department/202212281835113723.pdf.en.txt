2) Department of Finance, Government Circular No. 01.07.2021
Preamble: The Memorandum of Reference (1) of the Central Government directs to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds.
Accordingly, revised guidelines on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes have been issued by the Department of Finance vide Circular No. 2 to the Government. The Department of Finance, vide the said circular, has directed to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of the schemes under the 12th Finance Commission as a nodal officer for coordination of the schemes under the said 12th Finance Commission. As per the directions of the Finance Department, the matter of appointing the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and as the Nodal Officer was under consideration of the Government to bring more effectiveness in the process of allocation of expenditure.
Government Decision: As per the instructions in the circular at Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums is being appointed as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums as the Nodal Officer for the implementation of the schemes under the 12th Finance Commission being implemented by the Department of Archaeology under the Department of Cultural Affairs.
