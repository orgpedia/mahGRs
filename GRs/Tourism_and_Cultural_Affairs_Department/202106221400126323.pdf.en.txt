Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government service for persons with disabilities. Accordingly, the Central Govt. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, a notification has been issued for ensuring the posts of various cadres in the government service for the disabled. According to the notification, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions were issued to all the administrative departments regarding ensuring the posts in the State Government establishments for the disabled. However, the proposal to exempt the posts of driver cadre in Group-C of the corporation while determining the posts for the disabled was submitted for the approval of the expert committee. The meeting held on 19th and 20th May, 2021 has approved the removal of this post. Accordingly, a proposal is under the consideration of the government to exclude the post of driver from the Group-C cadre on the establishment of the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs.
Government's decision:
Issued by the Central Government. As per the list published in the Notification dated 04.01.2021 for Group-A to Group-D Cadre ensured for Divyang, the post of Driver in Group-C cadre at the establishment of Directorate of Archaeology and Museums under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs is being excluded from the list for Divyang.
