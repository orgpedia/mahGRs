Introduction: Khadigamat is an ancient folk drama of Maharashtra. Khadi Gamat Mahotsav is organised by the Government with the objective of preserving, preserving and promoting the endangered folk arts in the modern era. The matter of approving the cost of organizing the festival was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The expenditure of Rs. 4,69,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh Seventy-Nine Thousand only) for the Kharigamat Mahotsav 2018-19 to be held at Kanhan, G.Nagpur in the month of February, 2019 is being approved as shown in the following statement.
