Introduction: Rajguru, Shri. Khed, the birthplace of martyr Shivaram Hari Rajguru and Thorla Wada in Pune district have been declared as a state protected monument in the year 2000.
In the eastern part of this building, known as Rajguruwada, the great revolutionary martyr Shivaram Hari Rajguru was buried. He was born on August 24, 1908. The remaining private part of the original Wada and the area in front of the Wada need to be acquired, a Wada-like building should be constructed in the front of the old Wada and it should be constructed in which administrative office, souvenir department, library, library and museum, audio-visual presentation center, restaurant, washroom, dressing room and store room, amphitheater and stage etc. should be constructed. The project was executed by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department (North), Pune. 86.35 crore. The budget was prepared for 2015-2016. The budget is likely to see an increase in the new tariff list as well as the increase in the Goods and Services Tax. The government was contemplating to form a committee to study the plan and make recommendations to the government for the development of the memorial complex.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Government is approving the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Pune to study and submit a report regarding the development plan of the Hutatma Shivaram Hari Rajguru Memorial Complex as follows:
