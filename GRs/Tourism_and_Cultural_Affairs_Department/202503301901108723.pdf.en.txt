The agreement should also include a condition that all facilities and tourist spots created by the work will be open to all citizens.
10) The Directorate of Tourism shall submit to the Government by the 10th of every month, without fail, the physical progress of the works to be done out of the expenditure from the Fund and the monthly statements of the expenditure from the Fund.

14) The utilization certificate regarding the fund should be submitted to the government through the Directorate of Tourism without fail.
