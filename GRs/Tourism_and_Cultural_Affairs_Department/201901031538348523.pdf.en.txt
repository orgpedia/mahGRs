Introduction: Professional/ amateur theatre, children's theatre camps and competitions are organised by the government every year to enrich the theatre scene in Maharashtra. Children's theatre competitions have been held in Maharashtra since 2002. A large number of companies participate in this competition.
In these children's drama competitions, like the general children, the children with disabilities also participate in a significant way. It is not appropriate to hold a combined children's play competition for disabled children and ordinary children. The Minister (Cultural Affairs) announced a separate Children's Theatre Competition for Divyang children at the 98th Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Natya Sammelan held at Mulund, Mumbai on 14th June, 2018. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant permission to organize an amateur children's theatre competition for the disabled at one centre every year.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to organize a separate amateur Marathi Divyang Child Theatre Competition for Divyang children every year at one centre from the year 2018-19.
