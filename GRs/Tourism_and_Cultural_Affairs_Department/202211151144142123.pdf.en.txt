Government's decision:
A large number of tourists go for trekking/ tourism in the various Fort Fort places of the state. A total of 18 portable toilets for tourists/ trekkers at the foot of the forts/ forts as well as at the trekking sites mentioned in the table below will be constructed under the Regional Tourism Development Scheme 2022-23 at a cost of Rs. 2,04,36, 546/- (Rupees Two Crores Four Lakh Thirty Six Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Six only) is hereby approved for disbursement to the Director, Directorate of Tourism, Mumbai.

Tenders for setting up of portable toilets should be invited only after the availability of space and water required for setting up of such portable toilets and after obtaining an undertaking from the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Department/ System for maintenance, repair and operation for the initial period of one year.
Tenders should be invited to design, build, operate, maintain and transfer a separate model for the permanent maintenance and operation of these portable toilets within a period of one year after their installation. This process should be completed within one year of installation of portable toilets so that these toilets remain in use in an orderly manner after a period of one year.
4. Care should be taken to provide drinking water facility in the place of these portable toilets.
