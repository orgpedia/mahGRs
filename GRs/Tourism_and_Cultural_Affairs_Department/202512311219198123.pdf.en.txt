The Proposal:
Moje Angapur Vandana T.G. Satara is a spiritual, historical, religious and freedom struggle village with a history and a very important place on the social and royal table. Punished by the feet of saints, this Angapur Vandana village has been associated with the stay of Sant Ramdas Swami, Sant Gadgebaba, Sant Tuka Wipro, Sant Tukaram Maharaj, Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil (Karmaveer Anna), who took the Ganga of education to the villages in the modern era. The village has a beautiful ghat on the banks of the Krishna River opposite the ancient Shivnath Temple B. Also, Samarth Ramdas Swami has found idols of Prabhu Shri Ram b Anglai Devi in Kaladoha in Krishna river and installed them at Sri Kshetra Chafal and Sajjangarh. The Vitthal Temple of Brahmapuri, popularly known as Prati Pandharpur, across the Krishna River, is visited by a large number of devotees / tourists throughout the year.
The authority to select and submit proposals to the Government for Category B tourist destinations in the district has been delegated to the Committee headed by the Collector in the Government Decision at Reference No. 2, Government Decision at Reference No. 1. Accordingly, the criteria and procedures for declaring "Category B" tourist destinations in the state have been laid down by the Government Decision at Reference No. 3.
Also, District Planning Committee, Satara. ISH Mojay Angapur Vandana of District Planning Committee held on 09.07.2025, Ta. The place Satara has been given the status of "Class C" Tirtha Sthal under reference No. 5 is mentioned in the annotation here.
3. Accordingly, considering the religious, cultural and tourist significance of the place as well as the large presence of devotees and tourists, it was under the consideration of the Government to accord "B" category tourist status to the village of Moje Angapur Vandana, T.G. Satara.
