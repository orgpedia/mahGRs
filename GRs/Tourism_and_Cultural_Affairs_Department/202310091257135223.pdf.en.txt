The Proposal:
The" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" is being celebrated in the state and the country with great fervor on the occasion of the 75th year of Indian independence. On the occasion of the culmination of the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Central Government has announced the campaign" Meri Mati, Mera Desh" i. e." My Land, My Country." According to this slogan, the aim is to create awareness, love and literacy about our soil in every state.
