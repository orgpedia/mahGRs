Introduction: The Government is organizing the State Drama Competition for the last 60 years with the objective of providing a platform to the budding artists, to tap their latent talents, to develop personality through those talents, to promote and spread the art of drama from all levels, to create a cultural environment. Competitions in Marathi, Hindi, Sanskrit, Sanskrit, Balnatya and Divyang Balnatya are organised at the state level every year by the Cultural Affairs Department. The State Amateur Marathi Sangeet Natak Competition is a state level competition. In order to commemorate the centenary year of music composer Keshavrao Bhosale, the competition has been named after him. 3 was requested.
Keshavrao Bhosale was a legendary Marathi actor and singer. From 1890 onwards, he performed in many of his most famous plays such as Sangeet Sarada, Sangeet Saubhadra, Sangeet Mrichakatik and Sangeet Manapman. To honour him as an outstanding actor and singer in the field of music drama, the state government was considering to rename the state amateur Marathi music drama competition as' Sangeet Suryavanshi Keshavrao Bhosale Sangeet Natya Karyakram."
The Maharashtra government has decided to rename the Maharashtra State Marathi Amateur Sangeet Natak Competition (MSMAT) as' Sangeet Surya Keshavrao Bhosale Sangeet Natak Competition'.
