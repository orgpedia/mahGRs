Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure a post in the government service for the disabled. Accordingly, as per the Central Government notification dated 04.01.2021, a notification has been issued to ensure the posts of various cadres in the government service for the disabled. According to the notification, all the administrative departments have been instructed to ensure the posts for the disabled in the state government establishments as per the government decision of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance dated February 2, 2021. Accordingly, it is under the consideration of the Government to ensure the posts for the disabled in the establishment of the Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai and the Maharashtra State Hindi/ Sindhi/ Gujarati Sahitya Academy, Mumbai under this department.
Government Decision: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government service for persons with disabilities. As per the aforesaid Central Government Notification dated 04.01.2021, a notification has been issued regarding the fixation of posts of cadres for Divyangjan in Government Services. According to the notification, the posts in the Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai and the Maharashtra State Hindi/ Sindhi/ Gujarati Sahitya Academy, Mumbai, are being ensured for the disabled persons as indicated in the prospectus A and B attached with the establishment.
