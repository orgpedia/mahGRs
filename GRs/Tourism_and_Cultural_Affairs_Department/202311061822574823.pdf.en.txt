Introduction: Many national figures, revolutionaries and freedom fighters have contributed a lot to India's independence. Many movements, satyagrahas, and Andalas were carried out for the freedom of the country. This includes the historic' Quit India' movement. On 9th August 1942, the Quit India Movement was launched to overthrow the British rule. The 9th of August is celebrated as the August Revolution Day. On the occasion of this historic day, to perpetuate the flame of patriotism and to salute the known and unknown martyrs who sacrificed their lives for Indian independence, a cultural program based on the Quit India Movement was organized.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: On the occasion of Kranti Diwas on 9th August, 2023, an amount of Rs. 42.00 lakh is being provided as administrative and financial sanction for organizing a cultural programme on the theme of Quit India Movement at Raj Bhavan, Mumbai on 18th August, 2023.
