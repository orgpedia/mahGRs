The Proposal:
Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshkar Lifetime Achievement Award and Padma Shri Madhukar Dhavalikar Scholarship Scheme have been announced as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The award and scholarship scheme will be given to individuals who have made outstanding contributions in the field of museum science, archaeology, heritage conservation, Indian iconography and ancient architecture. Senior Research Fellow Rs. 5.00 lakh and 2 meritorious students pursuing advanced courses/ research like doctorate in the following year will be awarded Rs. Rs 2.50 lakh. Thus, the scheme is intended to be implemented in the order of award one year and scholarship the next year. Awards and Scholarships Committee. It is headed by the Minister of Cultural Affairs. The committee also has 3 non-official members who have done notable work in the field of museums, archaeology, heritage conservation, architecture and iconography. But in the last two years, due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the non-official chair of this committee could not be nominated. Therefore, it was now under the consideration of the Government to nominate 3 non-official members who have done notable work in the field of museums, archaeology, heritage conservation, architecture and iconography.
Government's decision:
The following non-official members are being nominated to the Padma Shri Sadashiv Gorakshkar Lifetime Achievement Award and Padma Shri Madhukar Dhavalikar Scholarship Scheme Scrutiny Committee.
Dr. Arvind Jamkhedkar, (Former President, Indian Council of Historical Research (ICHR), New Delhi and Former Pro-Vice-Chancellor, Deccan College Deemed to be University, Pune.
Dr. Pramod Joglekar, Former Professor, Deccan College, Pune.
