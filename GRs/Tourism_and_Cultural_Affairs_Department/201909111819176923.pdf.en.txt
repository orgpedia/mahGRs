Introduction: Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi through the Maharashtra Government Institutions is implementing various activities for the promotion of Hindi. Under this, various seminars, symposiums, symposiums etc. are organized for the development of Hindi language and literature. Accordingly, in collaboration with the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy, various programs will be organized at different places with the aim of promoting language and literature. The matter was under consideration of the government for approval of the expenditure to be incurred.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, an expenditure of Rs. 4,90,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh Ninety Thousand only) is being approved for organizing programmes for the promotion of language and literature in collaboration with Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy as detailed below.
