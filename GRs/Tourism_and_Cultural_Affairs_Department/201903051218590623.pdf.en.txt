In order to promote the development and promotion of tourist destinations in the State of Maharashtra at national and international level and to celebrate various tourism festivals, an amount of Rs. 78.00 crore has been budgeted under the head" PRASIDHI (3452 1931)" in the financial year 2018-19. As per the Government Circular No.7 of the Finance Department dated 25.1.2019, 70% of the budgeted outlay of Rs. 54.60 crore is available for disbursement. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 3827.42 lakh is remaining for disbursement.
As per the existing procedure, funds are released to the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation by approving the annual publicity plan regarding the expenditure of the fund under" Publicity" and the payments to the concerned organizers/ institutions are made after the approval of the government on the expenditure on publicity matters. The Department of Tourism and/ or Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation are required to disburse the remaining funds for participation in exhibitions, festivals and ancillary events and sponsorship of such events in the coming period, as payments for events involving tourism publicity and expenditure approved by the Government remain pending. Therefore, it is hereby approved to disburse the amount of Rs. 3827.42 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Eight Crores Thirty Seven Lakhs Forty Two Thousand only) available for disbursement under PRASIDHI (3452 1931) to Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation.
4. Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation (MTDC) has implemented the scheme as per the provisions laid down in the publicity plan. The funds should be spent with the approval of the Minister of Tourism and Chairman, Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation.
