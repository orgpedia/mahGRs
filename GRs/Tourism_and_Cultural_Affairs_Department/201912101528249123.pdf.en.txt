Introduction: Department of School Education and Sports, No. 2 As per the decision of the Government dated 24th November, 2017, a new procedure has been decided to award enhanced marks in the Secondary School Certificate (Class X) examination to the students who have excelled in the field of classical arts, painting and participating in the folk arts.
A meeting of the Scrutiny Committee was held on 13th November, 2019 to select institutions for awarding enhanced marks in the Secondary School Certificate (Class 10) Examination to be held in March, 2020. In the meeting, the screening committee recommended the names of the institutions to be eligible for the academic year 2019-20. As per the recommendation of the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, the aforesaid No. As per the proposal submitted under Section 3, the matter of granting recognition to institutions in the field of classical art, singing, playing and dance as well as institutions in the field of folk art was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under this decision, the following institutions in the field of classical music, dance and instrumental music and folk art are being selected for giving certificate to students in the certificate examination (Class 10) in secondary schools to be held from March, 2020.
