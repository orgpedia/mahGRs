The Proposal:
With a view to promote tourism development in the State and to expedite the development of tourism in the State, a Tourism Development Area Committee has been constituted vide Notification No. TPV 4392/6065/ UD 11 (RDP), dated 19/9/1995 of the Urban Development Department to recommend and submit for consideration of the Government a proposal for classification of land in" No Development Area" into" Tourism Development Area." The composition of the committee is as follows:
