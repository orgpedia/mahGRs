Government decision: As per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting dated 04.07.2021, in order to give industrial status to the hospitality sector, it is being approved to levy electricity tariff and electricity charges at industrial rates for hoteliers as follows:
1) The hoteliers registered with the Ministry of Tourism, Govt. of India. From April 1, 2021, electricity rates and electricity tariffs should be at industrial rates.
2) The power tariff and electricity charges should be implemented at industrial rates after the order of the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission to the hotels registered with the state government.
3) The Maharashtra Electricity Distribution Company should file a petition before the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission for a mid-term review of the charging of electricity rates at industrial rates.
