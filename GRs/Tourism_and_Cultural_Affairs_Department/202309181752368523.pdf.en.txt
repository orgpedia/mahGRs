Introduction: Mumbai is the financial capital of the country and a major commercial center. The city of Mumbai has a special attraction among the people of the country and abroad for Bollywood, nightlife, shopping, beach tourism, cultural and historical heritage etc. Every day thousands of domestic and foreign tourists visit Mumbai.
The Mumbai Festival will be organized in various parts of Mumbai city and suburbs for a total period of 9 days from 3rd Saturday to 4th Sunday of January every year on the lines of Dubai and New York Festival to promote tourism in the state and put Mumbai on the world tourism map.
The festival will be held from January 20, 2024 to January 28, 2024. The government was considering the formation of the Mumbai Festival Foundation to continue the public participation in the organization of the Mumbai Festival.
Government Decision: In order to ensure the continuity of public participation in the organization of the Mumbai Festival, it is being approved to form the Mumbai Festival Foundation by registering it under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013.
