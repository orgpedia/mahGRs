Preamble: Various programmes/ activities are being undertaken across the country to mark Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav. In these programs, cultural programs are organized by the department to ensure that the great personalities and revolutionaries who worked for the country in the pre-independence and post-independence era are known to the society and the new generation, their work is honored and their information is disseminated.
On the same occasion, the former Prime Minister paid tributes to the martyrs. On the occasion of Atalji's death anniversary, a cultural program titled" Tirangayan" was organized at Vasantrao Deshpande Auditorium, Nagpur on 29th August, 2023 to pay tribute to Atalji. Administrative and financial sanction for the cultural programme and its expenses was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Salute to the brave martyrs on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Independence and pay homage to the former Prime Minister of the country, Ma.
