Introduction: The Rangabhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Awards are given annually by the State Government to promote the field of theatre. Vinayak Thorat and Jayant Savarkar have been awarded the Annasaheb Kirloskar Sangeet Rangbhoomi Lifetime Achievement Award. The award ceremony was held on 13th December 2018 at Mumbai Marathi Sahitya Sangh Mandir, Girgaum, Mumbai. The government had approved the cost of the project.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, an amount of Rs. 10,44,200/- (Rupees Ten Lakh Forty Four Thousand Two Hundred Only) is being sanctioned for the organization of Prabhakar Panshikar Rangbhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar and Sangeetacharya Balwant Pandurang and Annasaheb Kirloskar Maharashtra State Sangeet Rangbhoomi Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar.
