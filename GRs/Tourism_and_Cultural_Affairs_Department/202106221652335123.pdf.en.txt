Decision of the Central Government: The notification for ensuring the post of cadre for Divyangjan in Government service has been issued on 04/01/2021. In pursuance of this notification, the Government have decided to provide reservation of posts for the disabled in the category of Group-A to Group-D in the establishments of the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai, Department of Philosophy, Mumbai and P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Reference No. 5 has been removed. Further, as per the said notification, the proposal for reservation of posts in other cadres to be ensured for the disabled, which is not notified by the Central Government, was required to be submitted for the approval of the Expert Committee constituted for reservation of posts for the disabled in the Government service. Accordingly, the proposal for the post of" Driver" in Group-C on the establishment of P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs was placed in the meeting of the Expert Committee dated 19/5/2021.
In the meeting, the expert committee has decided to exclude the post of' driver' in Group-C from the disability category.
