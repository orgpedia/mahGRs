Preamble: Under the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat scheme, an MoU has been signed between the States of Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh for strengthening cultural ties. Accordingly, the Government was considering sanctioning grants as a" special case" for meeting some of the expenditure incurred on cultural programmes organised by the Mission in Maharashtra, Vile Parle (East), Mumbai.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, a grant of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only) is being sanctioned to Abhiyaan, Vile Parle (East) Mumbai as a special case for organizing cultural programs in Maharashtra under the scheme" Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat."
