Dr. Gurumukh Jagwani has been appointed as the working president of Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi as per the decision of the government in the aforesaid Roll No. 1. Gurmukh Jagwani, working president, Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy, has been given the status of" Minister of State." Jagwani's appointment as working president, Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi will continue till the reconstitution of the committee.
