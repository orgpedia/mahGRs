As per the decision of the Government dated 6th December, 2018, a total of 100 workshops on writing, poetry and music to be organized jointly by the Department of Cultural Affairs and the Department of Marathi Language on the occasion of the birth centenary of Deshpande, G.D. Madgulkar and Sudhir Phadke have been approved. During the year 2018-19, 55 workshops have been organized. Now the matter of approving the detailed expenditure for organizing the remaining 45 workshops was under the consideration of the government.
The Government has decided to organize 45 more writing, poetry and music workshops in the financial year 2019-20 to mark the birth centenary of Deshpande, G.D. Madgulkar and Sudhir Phadke. Accordingly, Rs. 1.00 lakh is being approved as per the details mentioned below.
