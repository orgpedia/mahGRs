Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to approve the expenditure incurred on the cultural programme" Panchrangi Pathhebapurao" to be organized by the Mumbai University Folk Art Academy as a special case.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government decision, a one-time grant of Rs. 6,00,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh only) is being sanctioned as a special case to the Mumbai University Folk Art Academy for conducting the cultural programme" Pancharangi Paththebapurav" at three different places in Maharashtra at a cost of Rs. 2,00,000/- per programme. The grant is all inclusive and no separate grant will be sanctioned for other expenses like theatre rent, sound/ lighting, advertisement, artist accommodation expenses etc.
