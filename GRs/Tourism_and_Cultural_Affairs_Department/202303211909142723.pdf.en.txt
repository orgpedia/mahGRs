Preamble:" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" is being celebrated in the State to mark 75 years of Indian Independence. Various committees have been formed under the government decision of Reference No. 2 for organizing the festival. In it. A core committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Minister of Cultural Affairs. The core committee will decide the plan of action for the programme recommended by the policy committee under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav and recommend to the stakeholders about the planning of funds. According to reference no. As per the letter dated 5.12.2022, the Core Committee has sanctioned Rs.1 crore for the construction of infrastructure facilities at Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai. The project has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs 100 crore.
Further work to be done from this fund has been approved by the Board of Directors of Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Goregaon, Mumbai. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to these works was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Administrative approval is being accorded for the following infrastructure works at Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai at a cost of Rs. 1,01,93, 00,000/- (Rupees One Hundred and One Crore Ninety Three Lakhs only) subject to the following terms and conditions:
