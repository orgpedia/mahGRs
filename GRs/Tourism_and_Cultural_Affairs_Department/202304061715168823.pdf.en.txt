The Proposal:
The aforementioned No. Maharashtra Bhushan Award is given to the eminent person who has done the highest work in any field in the state of Maharashtra. Maharashtra Bhushan Award for the year 2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Bhushan Award for the year 2022 Dattatraya Narayan alias Appasaheb Dharmadhikari is being announced under this government decision.
The award carries a cash prize of Rs. 25.00 lakh (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs), a citation, a medal and a shawl.
