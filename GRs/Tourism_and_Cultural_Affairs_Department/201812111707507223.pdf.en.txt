The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Amateur Marathi Drama Competition is organised under the Maharashtra State Drama Festival. The new codes presented in these competitions are judged by eminent personalities, elders and experts in the field of theatre. For this examination, the examiners are required to pay a fee of Rs.100/- as per the Government Order dated 23rd July 1993. 51/- is paid.
Every year, a total of 57 codes are submitted to the examiners from 19 centers in 3 ways each.
Since the remuneration paid to the examiners was very low, the matter of increasing the remuneration was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 23rd July, 1993, passed under this Government Decision, a sum of Rs. 51/- to Rs. An increase of Rs.300/- per Code is being approved under this Government decision.
These orders will come into effect from the 57th Maharashtra State Theatre Festival.
The Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
