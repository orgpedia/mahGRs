The competition will be held in Marathi, Hindi, Sanskrit, Music, Vocational, Children's and Divyang Children's Dramas. This activity is a regular feature of the department and the preliminary rounds of Marathi, children's theatre and professional theatre competitions are held.

Administrative and financial approval for the event was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The prize distribution ceremony of the 61st Amateur Marathi Drama Competition and the preliminary round of the 19th Children's Drama Competition was held simultaneously on Monday, August 7, 2023, at five venues in Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad and Nagpur.
