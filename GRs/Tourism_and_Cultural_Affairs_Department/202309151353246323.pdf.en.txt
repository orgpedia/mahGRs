Introduction:" Shivrajyabhishek Mahotsav" was celebrated on the occasion of 350th coronation year of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It will begin on June 2, 2023. Various programmes are being held across the State on the occasion. In the same background, the Government was considering to give administrative and financial approval to the expenses incurred for carrying the Jalakalash Fort from 1108 holy places to Raigad through Rath Yatra from May 25 to May 28, 2023 under the auspices of Shri Shivarajyabhishek Dinotsav Seva Samiti, Durgaraj Raigad, Shri Shivaji Raigad Smarak Mandal and Directorate of Cultural Affairs.
Government Decision: Administrative and financial approval is being given for the Jalakalash Rath Yatra from Mumbai to Raigad from May 25 to May 28, 2023, jointly organized by Shri Shivarajyabhishek Dinotsav Seva Samiti, Durgaraj Raigad and Shri Shivaji Raigad Smarak Mandal and Directorate of Cultural Affairs at a total cost of Rs. 25.00 lakh (Rupees Twenty Five Lakh only).
2. Cost Account Heading Demand No. ZD-2, Main article 2205 Art and culture, (102) Art and culture, (13) Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, (13) (01) Programmes under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, (22053733) 50 Other expenditure (s) to be met from the financial provisions available under this article.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
4. The said expenditure is covered by the Government Decision No. VIPR-2013/ EXCHANGE, dated the 17th April, 2015, Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-section-3, S. No. 4, Para No. 27 (2) (a) is being sanctioned as per the powers conferred on the administrative department.
