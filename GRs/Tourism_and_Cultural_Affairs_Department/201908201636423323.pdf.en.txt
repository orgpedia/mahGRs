According to the rules, the award scheme is a scheme under which literary persons who have made significant contributions to Sindhi literature are awarded annually to encourage writers to produce outstanding Sindhi literature. The Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi has announced the names of awardees for the year 2018-19.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the awardees and the amount of the award for the year 2018-19 of Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy are being recognized as follows.
