Krishna Rao Ganpatrao Sable, who took the glorious tradition of Maharashtra to the country through the" Lokdhara" program in the united Maharashtra movement after the freedom struggle, was the" Brand Ambassador" of Maharashtra's folk art. The song" Jai Jai Maharashtra Majha" reached every corner of Maharashtra with his mountainous voice. He actively participated in the freedom movement of 1942, Goa and Hyderabad liberation movements, Samyukta Maharashtra movement, promotion of prohibition, nurturing folk artists, revival of folk songs and many other constructive activities. He was the first recipient of the Padma Shri. Apart from the post of President of Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Shahiri Parishad, Sable is the only Shaheer to have held the post of President of Marathi Natya Sammelan. The government considers it its duty to keep the memory of such a great artist alive. This is the centenary year of Shaheer Sable.
The government was contemplating to form a committee for various programmes to be held throughout the year in his memory.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The following Committees are being constituted to plan various programmes to commemorate the birth centenary year of" Shaheer Krishnarao Ganpatrao Sable" which began on 3 rd September, 2022.

Functions of the Committee:- Planning and submitting the programs to the Government through the Directorate of Cultural Affairs by September, 2023.
Meetings of the Committee: Meetings of the Committee shall be held once in three months.
Term of the Committee: The term of the Committee shall automatically come to an end on the expiry of the year of its birth centenary. No separate order will be issued for this.
Travel and Meeting Allowance: The non-official members of the committee will be entitled to travel allowance and meeting allowance as per the prevailing government decision of the Finance Department.
For this purpose, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as Controlling Officer and Accountant Drawing and Disbursement.
