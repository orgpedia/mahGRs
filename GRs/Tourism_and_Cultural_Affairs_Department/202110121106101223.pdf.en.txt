The Proposal:
The Government has received requests from various associations/ institutions in the sector to allow the opening of cinema halls which have been closed in the wake of COVID-19. With the Covid-19 outbreak hampering the country's economic progress, the government has eased restrictions on the opening of various industries in a phased manner to boost the industry. In the meanwhile. In the meeting of the Task Force held on 25.09.2021, Ma. The Chief Minister has announced that theatres and cinema halls will be allowed to open in the state by following health rules. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines for the opening of cinemas in the state in a controlled manner was under the consideration of the government.
The government has taken the following decisions in this regard:
Government Decision: In the wake of COVID-19, cinema halls in the state will be allowed to open subject to the guidelines in Annexure-A attached with this government decision. The government is giving approval to start in a controlled manner from October 22, 2021.
Considering the noble intention of the government behind giving this permission, it is necessary for all concerned to operate the cinema halls in a manner that they do not violate any of the restrictions of the Central Government and the State Government in the context of COVID-19. Action will be taken against those found violating these guidelines.
The mandatory instructions issued by the Government from time to time to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus will continue to apply in this case.
