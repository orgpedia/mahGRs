Introduction: Marathi theatre has a tradition of more than 150 years. By restoring and reviving the works of those quality plays (old and new) and preserving them through digitalization through the known techniques, these old plays will be able to be enjoyed by the theater lovers, the knowledge of the best art will be available to the future generations. It will also help in strengthening and enhancing the cultural heritage and movement. To implement this scheme, a sub-committee of experts will be constituted to select plays for preservation and conservation of old quality rare plays. Two papers have been proposed.
Accordingly, the matter of forming a sub-committee of experts for the selection of plays for the preservation and conservation of old quality rare plays was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to constitute the following sub-committee of experts for selection of plays for preservation and conservation of old quality rare plays.

The term of office of the Sub-Committee shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee, whichever is earlier.
The members of the sub-committee have to meet and select 5 to 10 good old quality plays. Necessary action should be taken to save these plays.
