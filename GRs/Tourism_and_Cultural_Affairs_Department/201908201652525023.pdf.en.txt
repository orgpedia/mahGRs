The Maharashtra State Hindi Award Scheme is a scheme under which literary persons who have made significant contributions in the field of Hindi literature are awarded annually to encourage writers for producing excellent Hindi literature. Accordingly, the matter of recognition of the awardees and the amount of the respective awards for the year 2018-19 by the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi Awardees for the financial year 2018-19 and their respective awards amounting to Rs. 10,91,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh Ninety One Thousand only) are being recognized as per the following details.
