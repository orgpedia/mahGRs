The Proposal:
On the occasion of" 350th Shivarajyabhishek Ceremony," various programs/ activities are being organized at Forts Raigad and Pachad. 1 The District Collector, Raigad has been authorised as per the decision of the Government here. The government has also directed the Collector, Raigad, to demand the expenses related to the cultural programs to be organized by the Collector through the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs.
