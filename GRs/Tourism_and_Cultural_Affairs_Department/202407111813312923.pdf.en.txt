Preamble: - The 46th session of the World Heritage Centre Committee. It will be held in New Delhi from 21 to 31 July 2024. Replicas of 12 forts in the World Heritage Maratha Military Forts nomination will be placed in this convention. The proposal of Marathi military architecture in Maharashtra has been included in the tentative list of World Heritage Nominations by UNESCO by ATA. A final decision in this regard will be taken at the 46th session of the UNESCO World Heritage Committee scheduled from 12 July to 31 July 2024 in New Delhi.
The convention is expected to display a scale model (replica) of 12 forts and a tableau. Accordingly, for this purpose, the Director carried out the process of appointing an Architect's Institute to conceptualize and design the exhibition's curtain raiser (9181191) by following the prescribed procedure.
