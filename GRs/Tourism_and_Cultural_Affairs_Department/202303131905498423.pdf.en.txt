The period of appointment of the President and members of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee or until the Government issues an order cancelling the appointments of non-official members, whichever is earlier.
Even if the appointment of non-official members is terminated, the chairman, principal secretary, department of cultural affairs and joint director and member secretary of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy will continue to function. The Academy will continue to exist.
The Academy Office will be responsible for organizing various programs of the Academy. The officers of the Academy will be responsible for organizing the program and spending the funds as per the government rules and prescribed procedures under the guidance and cooperation of the Executive President and members of the Academy.
Travel and Meeting Allowance:- The non-official members of the Committee are entitled to the allowance of Rs.
Travel 1010/ PR No. 2/ Service-5, dated 3rd March, 2010 as per Government Decision, Travel Allowance& Finance Department, Government Decision No. The sitting allowance will be admissible as on 24th May, 2012.
