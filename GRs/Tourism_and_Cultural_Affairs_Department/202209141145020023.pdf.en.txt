Preamble: In accordance with the decisions of the Government referred to above. The State Government has decided to give awards to the best Ganeshotsav Mandals in the Ganeshotsav season starting from 31 st August, 2022 and has decided the selection procedure for the same. The government was considering appointing member secretaries to the state-level committees formed to select three winners from the Ganeshotsav Mandals selected by the district-level committees in all the districts.
Government's stand:
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Govt. It has been decided to constitute a State Level Committee under the PULDE 2022/ PR No. 133/ S.C. 2, dated 26th August, 2022, serial number 8 in para 1). 8), read as follows under serial number (2)
" State Liaison Officer, National Service Scheme is being appointed as Member Secretary of the State Level Selection Committee. "
