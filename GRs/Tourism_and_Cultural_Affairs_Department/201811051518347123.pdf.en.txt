Read:- 1) Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department No. HSC-1707/ (222/07)/ Umashi-1, dated 25th March, 2010. Narrow 2016/ Q.No.202/ SD 2, dated 7th January, 2017
Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 7th January, 2017 of the Department of School Education and Sports, a new procedure has been decided to award enhanced marks in the Secondary School Certificate (Class 10) examination to the students who have excelled in the field of classical arts, painting and participating in the folk art.
A meeting of the Scrutiny Committee was held on October 1, 2018 to select the institutions for awarding increased marks in the 2019 Secondary School Certificate (Class 10) examination. While recommending the names of institutions to be eligible for the year 2018-19, the Scrutiny Committee recommended that the institutions which have been eligible for three consecutive academic years 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 need not be re-registered for the next three years. As per the proposal submitted by the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs, the matter of granting recognition to institutions in the field of classical art, singing, playing and dance as well as institutions in the field of folk art was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, the following institutions are being selected for awarding certificates in the field of classical music, dance and instrumental music and folk art to give relaxation of enhanced marks to the students in the Certificate Examination (Class 10) in Secondary Schools to be held from March, 2019.
