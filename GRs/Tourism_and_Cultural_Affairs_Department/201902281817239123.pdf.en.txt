Introduction: The aforementioned No. As per the decision of the government here, the award-winning Ekankika Festival has been started in the state. Accordingly, the proposal to organize this festival and drama training workshops along with it in the current financial year was under the consideration of the government.
The state government has decided to organise the two-day award-winning festival at six places in the state- Alibag, Ichalkaranji, Yevla, Latur, Washim and Bhandara.

4. With a view to attract the youth towards theatre during the conduct of award winning solo festivals, Government is approving to conduct two days theatre training workshops at all the above mentioned six centres. Preference will be given to college students and the training will be provided free of cost.
The total expenditure of Rs. 5,42,300/- (Rupees Five Lakhs Forty Two Thousand Three Hundred only) for the above sanctioned Ekankika Mahotsav is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
