The geographical location of the tourist place" Murud-Janjira" in Raigad district is attractive. As one enters the city the palace of Nababi Nazakati is visible, then the unfathomable Arabian Sea and the main witness of history standing firmly in this sea' Padmadurg' and the charming Murud beach are seen. After that, the deity of the village, Sri Koteswari, was seen at the city's gate. Also in the central part of the city are the Sree Bhogeswara Temple as well as the Sree Lakshmi Narayana and other ancient temples. Similarly, in Murud town, there is the historic Shri Datta Mandir, built around 1750 on a flat ground on top of a scenic hill. Also on the same hill is the Eidgah, a beautiful structure that offers panoramic views of the city. The village is located close to the city. The Janjira Fort is also visible from there.
3. Murud-Janjira is a historical water-fortress and is visited by foreign tourists from France, Germany, England, Paris, Australia and other countries during the months of October to May. About 5 to 6 lakh tourists visit this waterfall and Murud beach every year. Every tourist who comes to Murud, visits the city of Murud, visiting the Kashid Beach, Nandgaon Ganapati Temple, Murud Beach, Janjira Fort and Siddhinbab Palace in Murud Taluka.

As per letter dated 14/12/2020, it is recommended to grant' B' category tourist destination status to the tourist place" Murud-Janjira, G. Raigad." Accordingly, the government was considering the issue of granting' B' category status to the tourist destinations of Murud-Janjira and G. Raigad.
Government Decision: Approximately 5 to 6 lakh tourists visit this tourist spot" Murud-Janjira, G. Raigarh" every year. Considering the state level importance of this tourist place, the number of tourists visiting this tourist place, historical and geographical importance etc., the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, Government vide Decision No. TDS- 2016/11/ P.S. 862/ Tourism, DTHS, Govt. The Government hereby approves declaration of" Murud-Janjira, G. Raigad" as' B' category tourist destination as per the criteria and procedure laid down for declaration of' B' category tourist destinations in the State under 25/4/2017.
