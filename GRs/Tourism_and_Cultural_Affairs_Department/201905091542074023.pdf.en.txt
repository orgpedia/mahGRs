Introduction: P. L. Deshpande, Project Director for procurement of Digital Cinema Projectors (DCPs) for Mini Theatres of Maharashtra Kala Academy. The revised amount presented by L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai is Rs. 51,67,500/- (Rupees Fifty-one Lakhs Sixty-six Thousand Five Hundred only) for the financial year 2018-19. It was approved by the government on February 5, 2019. However, the project director, P. L. Deshpande of the Maharashtra Kala Academy, denied the allegations. As informed vide letter dated 24.04.2019, no payment has been made to Digital Cinema Projector (DCP) supplier as the supply and commissioning of Digital Cinema Projector (DCP) was not done before March, 2019. Now that the Digital Cinema Projector (DCP) has been supplied and commissioned, the matter of approving the expenditure from the available provision for the financial year 2019-20 to pay the dues to the supplier was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As the Digital Cinema Projector (DCP) has been supplied and commissioned as proposed by the Project Director, P.L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, the work has been completed at a cost of Rs. Under this Government decision, expenditure is being approved within the limit of Rs. 51,67,500/- (Rupees Fifty-one Lakhs Sixty-six Thousand Five Hundred only).
