Preamble: Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Department is organizing various programmes on a regular basis. Various cultural programmes have been organised across the state to mark the occasion. So that the younger generation gets to know the new-age art/ culture and gets a platform to present their art and the importance of that relevance can be elaborated through cultural programs. Against this background, the matter of administrative and financial approval for the' Padva Pahat' program to be held in Nashik and its expenses was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative and financial approval is being accorded under this Government Decision for the event" Padva Pahat" to be held at Nashik on 14th November, 2023 and the cost of organizing the same is Rs. 7,00,000/- (Rupees Seven Lakh only).
Cost Accounting Demand No. 2. ZD-2, 2205, Art and Culture, 102, Art and Culture Operations, (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival, (02) State Art, Culture Preservation, Conservation, Various Festivals and other Programmes (2205 1507)- 50 Other expenditure to be met from the financial provision sanctioned for the year 2023-24 under this head.
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs will be responsible for submitting the details of the programme.
