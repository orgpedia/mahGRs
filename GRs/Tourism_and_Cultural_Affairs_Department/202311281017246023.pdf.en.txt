Preamble: Share Contribution (Programme) to Kolhapur Chitranagari Corporation (42020785) 54 Investment Under this head Rs. 7.00 crore has been budgeted and the Finance Department has allocated Rs. 1.40 crore (Rupees One Crore Forty Lakh only) has been made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS). The matter of administrative approval for the distribution of share capital to Kolhapur City Corporation in the financial year 2023-24 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2023-24, as a share contribution to the redevelopment project of Kolhapur City, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1.40 crore (Rupees One Crore Forty Lakh only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The funds provided by the Government for the capital work should be calculated in the share of the Government in the capital of Kolhapur Chitranagar Corporation.
(2) The funds shall be used for the purposes for which they are provided to the Corporation. Funds should not be used for any other purpose.
(3) The utilization certificate in the prescribed format certified by the Chartered Accountants of the funds made available by the Government should be submitted within the prescribed time.
The details of the expenditure incurred by the Corporation as well as the progress and material achievement of the above works should be communicated to the Board of Directors from time to time and also to the Department every three months.
The approval of the Board of Directors should be obtained before spending the said funds.
2. Cost of No. The capital expenditure on education, sports, art and culture (ZD-5,4202) (00) (02) should be shared with Kolhapur Chitranagar Corporation under the head' Share Capital Contribution (Programme) (42020785) 54 Investments" from the available allocation for the financial year 2023-24.
