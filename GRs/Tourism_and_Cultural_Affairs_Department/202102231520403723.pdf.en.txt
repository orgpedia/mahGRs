Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government service for persons with disabilities. For this, the Central Govt. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of this Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure the posts for the disabled category in the posts of Group A to Group D cadre in the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums office under the Department of Cultural Affairs.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, has issued a notification to ensure reservation of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for PwDs as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the state government establishment, as well as the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, will be ensured for the disabled in the state government service, even if their designations are different.
As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021.

This Government has decided to ensure that the posts in the category of Group A to Group D in the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums under the Department of Cultural Affairs in the list published for the category of Group A to Group D ensured for the disabled in the notification dated 04.01.2021 are as indicated in the returns A and B for the disabled.
The decision was taken by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. Notification dated 04.01.2021 and issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. being issued as per the instructions issued vide Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
