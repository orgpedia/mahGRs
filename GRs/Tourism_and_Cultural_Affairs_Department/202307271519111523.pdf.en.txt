On the occasion, Shri. As per the directions of the Chief Minister, a cultural program showcasing the culture of Maharashtra was organized by the Cultural Department. The programme included folk dance, skit, mime and songs of Maharashtra. Administrative and financial approval for the cultural programme and its expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government decision: A cultural programme showcasing the culture of Maharashtra, presented at Raj Bhavan, Mumbai on 6th July, 2023 on the occasion of the civic reception of the President of India and the cost of organizing the same is Rs. 24,70,000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Lakh Seventy Thousand only).
