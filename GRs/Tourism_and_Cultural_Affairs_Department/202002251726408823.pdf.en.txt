Introduction: The aforementioned No. The State Cultural Awards are conferred every year to the distinguished personalities who have done remarkable work in 12 different cultural fields under the Government Decisions 1 and 2.
The awards include those in the field of theatre (Marathi) and the proposal to name the award after Natasamrat Dr Shriram Lagoo was under consideration of the government.
The State Cultural Awards are conferred on 12 personalities from different cultural fields. This year's award in the field of theatre (Marathi) will be given in the name of' Natasamrat Dr. Shriram Lagoo'.
