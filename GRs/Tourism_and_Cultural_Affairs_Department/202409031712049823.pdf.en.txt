The Government's Decisions:
bhon, T. Sangrampur, Dist. Buldhana, declared as a State Protected Monument
As the due process has been completed, the Manager, Government Central Press, Charni Road, Mumbai is being directed by this Government Decision to publish the accompanying preliminary notification of this decision in the Government of Maharashtra General Gazette Part-IV in Marathi and English and to forward 30 copies of the notification to the Additional Chief Secretary, Department of Cultural Affairs, Mantralaya, Mumbai as well as the Director, Directorate of Archaeological Museums, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
A copy of the said preliminary notification by the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums.
Arrangements should be made to place it in a prominent place near the memorial. On the publication of the aforesaid preliminary notification,
Objections and suggestions should be sought and acted upon within two months from the date and the said preliminary notification should be published on the website of the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums as well as in various local newspapers.
The Government decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
