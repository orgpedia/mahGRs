Preamble: On the occasion of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," various programmes are being organized. Under this program, various programs related to independence are organized by the Cultural Affairs Department with the aim of making the younger generation aware of the events of the freedom movement and to inspire the spirit of nationalism. A set of 40 artists will showcase the journey from the Vedic times to the present day based on about 65 episodes with excellent music. It is under the consideration of the government to give administrative and financial approval to the cultural programs and event expenses organized in this background.
Government's decision:
On the occasion of Quit India Memorial Day, a cultural program and a morning walk will be organized in Mumbai on 9th August, 2023 and the amount of Rs. 7,50,000/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being given administrative and financial approval under this Government decision.
