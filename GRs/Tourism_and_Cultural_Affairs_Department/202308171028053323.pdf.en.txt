According to the government's decision, In the meeting held on 5.12.2022, the core committee constituted under the chairmanship of Minister of Cultural Affairs has approved the implementation of various programs and activities on the occasion of Marathwada Mukti Sangram Amrut Mahotsav along with various programs and funds for the same.

The minutes of the meeting held on 15th July, 2023 have been received under the Government Order No. 3 and under the said order, the work of making a documentary based on the Marathwada Liberation Struggle has been directed to be done by the Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai.
Accordingly, the Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai had advertised in the newspapers for the selection of the institution to produce the documentary. The Committee constituted to scrutinize the proposals received by the Corporation pursuant to the advertisement, has prepared a Statement of Concept, Code, Historical Experience and Expenditure Statement. The letter has been submitted. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval for the production of this documentary and the expenses incurred for it was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In accordance with the proposal of Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai, a documentary film on Marathwada Liberation War will be produced. The Pune-based production house will invest Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1,52,33, 505/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Two Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Five Hundred and Five only).
The Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai will be the coordinating agency for the production of the documentary and the funds will be distributed to Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation, Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari, Goregaon, Mumbai.
The expenditure on this work should be met from the grant sanctioned in the financial year 2023-24 under the head" Demand Cr ZD-2, 2205 Art and Culture, 102 Art and Culture Operation, (13) Swantantantra's Amrit Mahotsav (13) (01) Program (s) under Swantantra's Amrit Mahotsav (2205 3733), 50, Other expenditure."
