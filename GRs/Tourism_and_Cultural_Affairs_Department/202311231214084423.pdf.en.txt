The Proposal:
Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," the Department is organizing various programmes on a regular basis. Various cultural programmes have been organised across the state to mark the occasion. So that the younger generation gets to know the works of new-age art/ culture/ great personalities and gets a platform to present their art and the importance of that relevance can be elaborated through cultural programs. Accordingly, the matter of giving administrative and financial approval to the program" Diwali Pahat" organized by the Delhi Maratha Pratishthan and its expenses was under the consideration of the government
Government Decision: Administrative and financial approval is being accorded under this Government Decision for organizing and spending an amount of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only) on Diwali Pahat in Delhi on the occasion of Diwali.
