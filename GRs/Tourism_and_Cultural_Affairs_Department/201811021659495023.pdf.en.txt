The Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy, Mumbai was established on 30th August, 1996 for the overall promotion and encouragement of Gujarati literature. In connection with the implementation of various schemes for the promotion of Gujarati language through this academy, the reference No. 1 The rules have been prepared as per the decision of the government. Under this regulation, 8 literary awards are given in different subjects. The government was considering including the Prakirna (review) award in this literary award.
Government Decision: The 9th Prakirna (Review) Award is being included in the Vangmay Award in the Scheme of Schemes of the Maharashtra State Gujarati Sahitya Academy as per the decision of the Government.
Eligibility: Original literary works published between 1st January and 31st December of every year will be considered under this.
2. Under this award, the literary genre of" Mittarna (Review)" will be considered.
While selecting the books of Gujarati writers for this award, the concerned writer should have lived in Maharashtra for at least 15 years.
