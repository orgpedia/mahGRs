Preamble: - The issue of granting the status of Category-A tourist spot to Shri Ganeda Temple Hill in Nagpur city was under consideration of the Government. The idol at Shri Ganesh Mandir hill is 200 years old and is visited by about 1 million local and 150 to 200 foreign pilgrims every year.
Jhasan Decision: - Government Decision, Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, No: TDS - 2015/12 Process. The criteria and procedures for declaring a Category A tourist destination in the state have been laid down under TDS- 2015/12 Q. No. 1065 / Tourism, D 8.3.2016 and Zashan Purification Sheet, Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, No. Accordingly, Shri Ganesh Temple Hill in Nagpur city is being declared as a Category 'A' tourist destination.
