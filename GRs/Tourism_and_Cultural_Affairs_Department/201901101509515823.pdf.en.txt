The Proposal:
Various programmes to be organized to mark the birth centenary of Deshpande, G.D. Madgulkar and Sudhir Phadke are mentioned in the above reference No. Under the Government Decisions dated 6th December, 2018 and 14th December, 2018, the expenditure of 10 cultural programs and the institutions presenting these programs have been approved respectively. The government was considering approving the cost of advertisement-design in the program invitation card, design bank drop, board, banner, hoardings design newspaper for these programs. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
In order to ensure consistency in the publicity of 10 cultural programs to be organized on the occasion of the birth centenary year 2018-19 of Deshpande, noted poet, script, dialogue writer and lyricist G.D. Madgulkar and veteran musician and singer Sudhir Phadke, the government is approving the expenditure on the following items:
