Letter No. 6721 of Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Mumbai, dated 30th November, 2022.
Preamble: Under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav and the Diamond Jubilee of the formation of the State of Maharashtra, administrative approval has been given to organize a theatre festival of five plays and one single act in the state and outside the state under the 75th year of Indian independence. Out of these, the matter of giving honorarium to the 14 experiments of the play Sangeet Sant Tukaram was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav and Diamond Jubilee," the Government has approved to give an amount of Rs. 21,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh only) to 14 experiments of the play Sangeet Sant Tukaram in the state and outside the state at the rate of Rs. 1,50,000/- per play.
The said expenditure should be met from the financial provision sanctioned for the year 2023-24 under the head of Accounting Head Demand No.ZD-2, 2205, Art and Culture, Schemes under Five Year Plan, Schemes under State Plan (02) (02) State Dance, Drama, Tamasha and Music Festival (2250 1507).
For this, the Director, Directorate of Cultural Affairs is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Accountant, Directorate of Cultural Affairs as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
4. The said expenditure is covered by the Government Decision No. v. 2013/ Exchange, dated April 04, 2022, Financial Powers Rules, Book 1078, Part-I, Sub-section-3, No. 4 Paragraph No. 27 (2) (a) is being sanctioned as per the powers conferred on the administrative department.
