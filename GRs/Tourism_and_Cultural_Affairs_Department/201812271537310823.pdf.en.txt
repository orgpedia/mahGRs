The Proposal:
In order to encourage the folk arts to survive, to make the next generation aware of these folk arts and to preserve all the traditional folk arts of Maharashtra as a whole, a scheme has been started under the decision of the Department dated 1st January, 2008 to provide grants to the art troupes for capital expenditure for costumes, instruments, costumes, materials, tent materials, electricity, sound materials and vehicles etc. as well as for experimentation of the art troupes.
During the financial year 2018-2019, the Government was considering sanctioning grants to 80 art troupes for capital expenditure to the art troupes of folk artists.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. 33,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs only) is being sanctioned as per the statement given below for capital expenditure to a total of 80 Art Courses under the subsidy package to the Folk Artist Teams during the financial year 2018-2019.
