Introduction: By the decision of the Deputy Government, Bharat Ratna Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Promotion Scheme has been launched. Bharat Ratna 2018 Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Lifetime Achievement Award Keshavrao Ginde has been announced. The award ceremony was held on 10th January, 2019 at Yashwantrao Chavan Natyagruha, Kothrud, Pune and was held from 10th to 12th January, 2019 in the august presence of Bharat Ratna Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Classical Music Festival was organized. Bharat Ratna Pt.
