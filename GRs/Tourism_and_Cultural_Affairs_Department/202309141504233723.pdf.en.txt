Preamble: Various committees have been constituted as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 for effective and successful implementation of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav. According to the government's decision, The Core Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Minister, Cultural Affairs in its meeting held on 5 th December, 2022 has approved the implementation of various programs and activities on the occasion of Marathwada Mukti Sangram Amrit Mahotsav along with various programs and providing funds for the same. 2 letter has been published.
Further, in the meeting held on 24.07.2023 under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary Cultural Affairs, it was decided to release the gazetteers of" Marathwada Mukti Sangram:" Vande Mataram Movement" among the programmes to be taken up on the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Mukti Sangram. Accordingly, vide reference No. 4, the Executive Editor and Secretary, Department of Darshanika, have sanctioned a sum of Rs. A proposal has been submitted for administrative approval for an expenditure of Rs. 30.00 lakhs.
Government's decision:
Marathwada was liberated from the Nizam rule on 17 September 1948. Every year 17 September is celebrated as" Marathwada Mukti Sangram Diwas" in all the districts of Marathwada division. The freedom struggle of Marathwada is completing 75 years this year. On the occasion of this Amrit Mahotsav, the publication and printing of the English language gazetteer titled' Marathwada Mukti Sangram: Vande Mataram Movement" and the e-book (pen drive) of this book will cost Rs. Administrative sanction is being accorded by this Government for expenditure amounting to Rs. 30,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Lakh only).
