The Proposal: -
Pandharpur Ki Wari is a historical and cultural reserve that dates back nearly 800 years.
It is a cultural heritage that is shared by only a few religious and spiritual regions of the world.
It is an important event. A large number of Warkari-devotees from the state and outside the state participated in the festival.
They participate without any expectations.
Every year on the occasion of Ashadhi Wari, Shri Sant Tukoba Maharaj and Shri Sant from Dehu and Alandi
Two different Dindis are held in the name of Dnyaneshwar Maharaj. There are two main
The Dindy is traversed by two routes every year and lakhs of devotees visit these palanquins every year.
They travel with the wind. Accordingly, tableaux exhibition and cultural programmes on both these Palkhi routes were held.
The expenditure incurred for organizing the event was borne by the Directorate under their aforesaid letter of reference.
Administrative approval and financial approval are requested. Administrative approval and financial support.
Approval was under consideration of the government.
