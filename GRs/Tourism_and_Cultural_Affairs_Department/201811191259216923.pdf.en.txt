Introduction: Reference No. 1 of the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs. The Board of Editors of Maharashtra Zila Darshak was reconstituted by a Government Resolution dated 05.09.2015. The term of office of the Editorial Board shall be for a period of three years from the date of such decision or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier. A proposal to reconstitute the Maharashtra Zilla Darshana Editor's Board was under the consideration of the government for administrative reasons.
Government Decision: Reference No. 1 here. The decision is to cancel the appointments of all non-official members, expert members and accepted members appointed under the Government decision dated 05.09.2015. Further, it is hereby approved to reconstitute and appoint the following President and Members of the Maharashtra Zila Darshnika Editorial Board for a period of 3 years from the date of this order or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier:
Manjiri Bhalerao Non-Official Member (11) Dr. Ambrish Khare Non-Official Member (12) Executive Editor and Secretary, Darshika Division Member Secretary
