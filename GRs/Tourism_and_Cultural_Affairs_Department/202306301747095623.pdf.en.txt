Government circular
Preventing sexual harassment of women in the workplace. Writ Petition No. 666-670/1992 was filed in the Supreme Court. In it. The Supreme Court has stayed the implementation of the Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. It was released on December 9, 2013. The Department of Women and Child Development has issued instructions to every employer to constitute an Internal Complaints Committee to inquire into complaints of sexual harassment as per the provisions of the Act and recommend action accordingly. 2 decisions have been made by the government.
Accordingly, Reference No. 4 here. The Grievance Committee was constituted under the order dated 13.12.2016 and vide Reference No. The committee was reconstituted under Sections 5 and 6. Government Circular No: Aastha-2016/ PR No.125/ Establishment
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
a) To take cognizance of complaints of sexual harassment of women officers/ employees of the department.
(b) to make recommendations to the Principal Secretary from time to time to investigate the complaint and take disciplinary action against the concerned, if found to be true.
(c) To submit to the Principal Secretary an annual report of the work done.
The non-official member shall be paid for each working day of the Internal Complaints Committee. The allowance/ travel expenses will be admissible as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 19.06.2014.
