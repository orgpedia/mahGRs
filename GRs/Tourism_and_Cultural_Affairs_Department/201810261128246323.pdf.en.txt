Hindi Sahitya Akademi has been established in the State for the promotion and encouragement of Hindi literature as Hindi is the national language. The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi was re-constituted by a Government Resolution dated 15th September, 2015. Since the term of the committee had expired, the matter of restructuring the academy was under the consideration of the government.
The Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi has been dissolved by the state government.

The term of office of the Chairperson and Members of the Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee, whichever is earlier. Even if the appointment of non-official members is terminated, the chairman, principal secretary, department of cultural affairs and joint director and member secretary of Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy will continue to function. The Academy will continue to exist.
The Academy Office will be responsible for organizing various programs of the Academy. The officers of the Academy will be responsible for organizing the program and spending the funds as per the government rules and prescribed procedures under the guidance and cooperation of the Executive President and members of the Academy.
Travel and Meeting Allowance:- Non-official members of the Committee are entitled to the financial assistance of Rs. Travel 1098/ PR No. 74/98/ Service-5, dated 4th December, 1995 vide Government Order, Travel Allowance& Finance Department, Government Order No. The sitting allowance will be admissible as on 24th May, 2012. The Joint Director, Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Akademi will be the authority to sign on the payment of travel allowance.
