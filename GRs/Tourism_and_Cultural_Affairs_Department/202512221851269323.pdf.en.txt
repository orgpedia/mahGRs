The Government's Decisions:
Pargad Fort, Ta. Regarding the declaration of Chandgad, District Kolhapur as a State Protected Monument.
As the process of commissioning is complete, call the Manager, Government Central Press, Charni Road, Mumbai.
The government is directing that the final notification of this decision be in Marathi and English.
The Government of Maharashtra should publish in the General Gazette Part-IV and 30 copies of the said final notification.
The same shall be forwarded to the Chief Secretary, Department of Cultural Affairs, Ministry of Culture, Mumbai and to the Director, Directorate of Archaeological Museums, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
02. Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums has issued a copy of the said final notification near the memorial.
Arrangements should be made to put it in prominent places.
The Government decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
