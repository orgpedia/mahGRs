Government Decision: India is completing 75 years of its independence. The Government of India has informed all the States about the celebration of" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" from 12 th March, 2021 to 15 th August, 2023 on this glorious occasion.
Accordingly, instructions have already been issued from time to time through Government Decisions/ Government Circulars/ Government Letters regarding the celebration of" Amrit Mahotsav of Indian Independence" during this period in the State.
