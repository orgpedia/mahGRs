Read:- Letter from the Executive Editor and Secretary, Department of Philosophy, No. 655, p. 3.7.2019 and 861, dated 31.7.2019.
Introduction: The State Gazetteer is based on the special theme of the state and is published by the Department of Philosophy. The Marathi music industry is celebrating 175 years of its existence. The glorious musical and theatrical tradition of Maharashtra should be preserved and promoted. With this objective, the Government intends to publish a separate State Gazetteer (Darsanika) on the subject" 175 years of History of Marathi Music Theatre" in the State Gazetteer Series by the State Darsanika Department on the occasion of the Centenary Amrit Mahotsav of Marathi Music Theatre, in three volumes, so as to revive the memories of the glorious festival of music theatre and to inspire the present generation. For guidance and advice. The government was considering the formation of a core committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Cultural Affairs).
Government Decision: As the Marathi musical theatre is completing 175 years, it is being approved under this order to publish an independent state gazetteer (Darsanika) on this subject in three volumes on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav after the centenary. These volumes will be published annually over the next three years.
This work. The Government has approved the constitution of the following main committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Cultural Affairs). The term of the Committee shall be for a period of three years or until further orders, whichever is earlier. The duties of the members of the said Main Committee shall be as mentioned in Annexure-A annexed.
