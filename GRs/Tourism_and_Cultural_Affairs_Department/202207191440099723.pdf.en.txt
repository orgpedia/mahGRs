State Cultural Awards are given by the state government to the artists who have done significant work in the field of culture. The Department honours the artists who have made remarkable achievements in the field of Kirtan/ Social Enlightenment, Tamasha, Folk Art etc. In order to give due recognition to the artists who have made valuable contributions in these various arts, the government was considering naming the public welfare schemes implemented by the department after the names of the great personalities.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the" Cultural Award" and" Senior Literary and Artiste's Honorarium" scheme mentioned in the following table, which is being provided by the Department of Cultural Affairs, is being approved.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Prize matter                                                 | The name of the award                                              |
| 1.   | Tamasha Folk Art Award under State Cultural Awards           | Pavlabai Tabaji Bhalerao Hivargaonkar                              |
| 2.   | Kirtan/ Samaj Prabodhan Puraskar under State Cultural Awards | H.B.P. Shivraj alias Ramdas Maharaj Jadhav                         |
| 3.   | Literary and artistic awards                                 | 'Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj' Senior Literary and Artistic Award Scheme |
