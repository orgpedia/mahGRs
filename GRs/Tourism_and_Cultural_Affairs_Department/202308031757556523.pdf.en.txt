Introduction: The work of Chhatrapati Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj is multifaceted. He had taken many revolutionary decisions and implemented many programmes/ schemes/ initiatives in different fields. Due to his misfortune and ingenuity, many positive developments have taken place in the social, political, economic and cultural spheres of Maharashtra.
Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj was revered as a king with a rich legacy of social work, his work for the upliftment of the Untouchables, his vision of progressive Maharashtra and his rule accordingly, his patronage of sports like wrestling and his encouragement to music and traditional folk arts.
Chhatrapati Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj was born on June 26, 1874 in Kolhapur. This year is the centenary year of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj and 150 years of his birth anniversary is being celebrated. On this occasion, on the eve of the birth anniversary of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj i. e. 25th June, 2023, special cultural programs were organized at Keshavrao Bhosale Natyagruha, Kolhapur. Administrative and financial approval for the programme and its expenditure was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: On the eve of the birth anniversary of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj i.e. on 25th June, 2023, an amount of Rs. 12,10,500/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Ten Thousand Fifty Five only) is being sanctioned for the special cultural program and event at Keshavrao Bhosale Natyagruha, Kolhapur.
