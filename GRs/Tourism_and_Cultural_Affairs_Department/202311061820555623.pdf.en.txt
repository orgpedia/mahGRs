Introduction: On the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, various innovative cultural programmes and activities are being organized by the Department. The activities include cultural programmes by the freedom fighters, various artists and craftsmen whose art or craft has completed its diamond, golden and centenary years.
Also, various programs are being organized to make the younger generation aware of the real heroes of the freedom movement.
On the same lines, it is proposed to organize a revolutionary play Dharti Aaba Bhagwan Birsa Munda. The matter of giving administrative and financial approval to the theater experiment and its organizing expenses was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, administrative and financial approval is being given for the cost of Rs. 2,50,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Fifty Thousand only) for organizing the revolutionary drama Dharti Aaba Bhagwan Birsa Munda.
