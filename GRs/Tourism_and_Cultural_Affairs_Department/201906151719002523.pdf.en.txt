Introduction: All Sindhi speakers are scattered all over India and they are also minorities. Sindhi is not the official language of any state. Therefore, the Sindhi Sahitya Academy has been established in the state for the promotion and encouragement of Sindhi literature.
The aforementioned No. The matter of reconstitution of the committee was under the consideration of the government as the period of appointment of non-official members on the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy appointed under section 3 was over.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy is being restructured and the following appointments are being made.
(a) Members of the Government

The term of office of the Chairperson and Members of the Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy shall be for a period of three years from the date of this Government decision or until the reconstitution of the Committee, whichever is earlier. Even if the appointment of non-official members is terminated, the chairman, principal secretary, department of cultural affairs and joint director and member secretary of Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy will continue to function. The Academy will continue to exist.

Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy will work for the overall development of Sindhi language. Similarly, the state language will implement various schemes as per the constitution of the Academy for exchange of ideas through two translations and other literary endeavours, Marathi and Sindhi.
