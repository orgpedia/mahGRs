Decision of the Central Government: A notification for ensuring the posts of cadres for the disabled in the government services has been issued on 04.01.2021. In pursuance of the said notification, the Government decision regarding reservation of posts for Divyangjan in Group-D Cadre in the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs (itself), Reference No. 5 has been removed. Further, as per the said notification, the proposal for reservation of posts in other cadres to be ensured for the disabled, which is not notified by the Central Government, was required to be submitted for the approval of the Expert Committee constituted for reservation of posts for the disabled in the Government service. Accordingly, the proposal for the post of' Driver' in Group-C on the establishment of the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs (itself) was approved by the Expert Committee. The meeting was held on 19.5.2021. In the meeting, the expert committee has decided to exclude the post of' driver' in' Group-C' from the disability category.
