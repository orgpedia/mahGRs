3. terms and conditions:
(1) The procedure prescribed under the Financial Rules and various Regulations shall be followed while making expenditure out of the funds disbursed.
2) It should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned grant under any circumstances. If the expenditure is more than the sanctioned grant, the Directorate of Tourism will be responsible for it and no additional grant will be provided for the excess expenditure.
Expenditure to be incurred from the sanctioned allocation should be made by March 31, 2022 and the sanctioned works should be completed immediately.
The expenditure mentioned above should be met from the budgetary provision for the year 2021-22 under the head Demand No.ZD-4,3452-Tourism, Infrastructure for Tourism, 101 Tourist Centres (02) Schemes under Five Year Plans-Schemes under State Schemes, (02) (18) Grants for Basic Amenities for Various Places for Tourism Development (3452 1958) 31, Subsidiary Grants. The Chief Accounts Officer, Directorate of Tourism, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Joint Director, Directorate of Tourism, Maharashtra State, Mumbai as the Controlling Officer for drawing and disbursing the amount of Subsidiary Grants sanctioned. The amount of grant sanctioned as per this decision should be disbursed to the concerned by submitting the payments to the Admiralty and Accounts Officer, Mumbai and the compliance report on the proceedings should be submitted to the government within one month.
