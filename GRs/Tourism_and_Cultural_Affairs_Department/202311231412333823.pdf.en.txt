GOVERNMENT OF SOCIAL JUSTICE, CULTURAL WORKS AND SPECIAL ASSISTANCE DIVISION, GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 23rd March, 2006.
The Proposal:
The Regional Office of the P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, is located at No. Under the government decision here, a figure of 16 posts was approved. The post of Project Director has been included in this government decision. This post is the head of the academy. As the head of the academy, the project director is responsible for controlling the entire academy's operations, implementing the academy's arts and cultural plans, and controlling grants as the" controlling authority." As the designation of the head of the regional office of the Directorate of Archaeology and Museums, Directorate of Archives and Directorate of Cultural Affairs under the administrative control of this department with similar duties and responsibilities is" Director," the matter of the designation of the project director, P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy Regional Office as" Director" was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have approved the designation of the post of Project Director in the Regional Office of P. L. Deshpande Maharashtra Kala Academy, Mumbai as Director subject to the following terms and conditions:
1.The change in designation will not result in any change in the existing promotion chain.
(2) The change of designation shall not alter the duties and responsibilities of the post as determined earlier.
(3) No request for change in the existing pay scale of the post due to change in designation shall be entertained in future.
