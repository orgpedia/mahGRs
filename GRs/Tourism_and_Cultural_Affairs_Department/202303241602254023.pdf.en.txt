Preamble: Share Contribution (Programme) to Kolhapur Chitranagar Corporation (42020785) 54 Investment Under this head Rs. 7.00 crore has been budgeted and out of this provision, the Department of Finance has allocated Rs. A sum of Rs. 4.90 crore (Rupees Four Crores Ninety Lakh only) has been provided on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS). It was under consideration of the Government to approve the distribution of equity to Kolhapur City Corporation for the financial year 2022-23.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2022-23, as a share contribution to the redevelopment project of Kolhapur City Corporation, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 4.90 crore (Rupees Four Crore Ninety Lakh only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The funds provided by the Government for the capital work should be calculated in the share of the Government in the capital of Kolhapur Chitranagar Corporation.
(2) The funds shall be used for the purposes for which they are provided. Funds should not be used for any other purpose.
(3) The utilization certificate in the prescribed format certified by the Chartered Accountants of the funds made available by the Government should be submitted within the prescribed time.
The details of the expenditure incurred by the Corporation as well as the progress and material achievement of the above works should be communicated to the Board of Directors from time to time and also to the Department every three months.
The approval of the Board of Directors should be obtained before spending the said funds.
