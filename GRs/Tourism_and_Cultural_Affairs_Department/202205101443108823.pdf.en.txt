Introduction: To promote tourism in Konkan, by representing local people's representatives. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Minister for Tourism. The appointment of Mr. Pramod Jathar (Ex-MLA) as Vice-Chairman of the said Committee has been cancelled vide Government Resolution No. 2. The matter of appointing the vacant post of' Vice President' of the Konkan Tourism Development Committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Mr. Sandesh Parkar, District Sindhudurg is being appointed as the' Vice President' of the" Konkan Tourism Development Committee" constituted under reference dated 9/8/2019.
