Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad may increase the number of members of the committee as per requirement.
Duration of the Committee:
The term of the Committee shall be until the end of the Programme, after which the Committee shall automatically cease to exist.

Committee's mandate:
1. Marathwada Mukti Sangram on the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav. 28.08.2023 and Dy. on 31.08.2023. Planning, implementation and monitoring of the programmes as per the programme outline to be decided in the two meetings to be held under the chairmanship of the Minister of Cultural Affairs.
2. Determination of space with ancillary permissions for various events.
3. Providing all facilities in line with various programmes.
4. Planning and implementation of public order, security arrangements, parking, communication, publicity etc.
To take all measures for the successful planning of these programmes.
