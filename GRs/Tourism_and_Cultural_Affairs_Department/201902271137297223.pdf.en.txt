Under the Tourism Sub-Division, guidance and advice regarding the implementation of e-governance projects and other matters related to tourism will be provided. The Project Implementation Committee (PIC) has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Tourism) vide Government Resolution dated 29.12.2018. The committee The meeting was held on 7.1.2019. In the meeting, M/ s. Consulting firms Ernst and Young and MKPMG made presentations on the topic. On 8.1.2019, a presentation was made on the subject by the company M.P.W.C. After finding the presentations made by one of the three companies, M.K.P.M.G., a consultancy firm, satisfactory, the Committee decided to appoint M.K.P.M.G. to guide and advise on the implementation of e-governance projects related to tourism and other matters under the Tourism Sub-Division. KPMG was chosen.
2. As per the decision of the Project Implementation Committee, the following representatives of the consultancy firm M.K.P.M.G. will be appointed to guide and advise on the implementation of the e-governance project and other matters related to tourism under the Tourism Sub-Division. The appointments are being made by this Government for a period of 1 year initially with effect from 10.1.2019.
1) Principal Consultant -1
2) Managing Consultant -2
3) Senior Consultant-2
(for both Tourism and MTDC Department)
(3) After the expiry of the period of one year of such appointment, it may be extended for one more year as per the rules of the Government.
