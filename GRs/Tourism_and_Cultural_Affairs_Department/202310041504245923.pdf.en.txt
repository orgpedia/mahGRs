Introduction: The government organizes children's theatre competitions to create a sense of theatre movement among the children of the state and to provide a platform to the artists among them. Accordingly, the 60th Maharashtra State Amateur Marathi, Hindi, Sangeet and Sanskrit State Drama Competition as well as the 18th Maharashtra State Children's Theatre Competition and the 3rd Divyang Child Theatre Competition Final Round State Level Prize Distribution Ceremony and Festival of the first plays in this competition were held from 10th to 14th July, 2023 at Ravindra Natya Mandir, Prabhadevi, Mumbai. Administrative and financial sanction for the programme and its expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, administrative and financial approval is being given for the 60th Maharashtra State Amateur Marathi, Hindi, Music and Sanskrit State Drama Competition held at Ravindra Natya Mandir, Prabhadevi, Mumbai from 10th to 14th July, 2023, as well as for the 18th Maharashtra State Child Drama Competition and the 3rd Divyang Child Drama Competition.
