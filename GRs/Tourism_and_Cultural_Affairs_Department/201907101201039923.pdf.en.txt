On the occasion of Ashadhi Ekadashi, lakhs of devotees from all corners of the state come to Pandharpur, Solapur to visit Vithal. Considering the importance of this day in the saint tradition, the state government organizes cultural programs. The matter of approving the cost of organizing this cultural program was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, approval is being accorded for organizing a cultural programme on the occasion of Ashadhi Ekadashi at Pandharpur, Dist. Solapur for an amount of Rs. 15,00,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) as per the following details.
