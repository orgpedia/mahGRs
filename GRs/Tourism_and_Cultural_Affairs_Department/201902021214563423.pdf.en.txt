This decision was taken by the great musician. As a special gesture, a grant of Rs. 17,00,000/- (Rupees Seventeen Lakhs only) is being sanctioned for organizing the event" Swar Snehal" on the occasion of birth centenary of Snehal Bhatkar alias Vasudev Bhatkar.
