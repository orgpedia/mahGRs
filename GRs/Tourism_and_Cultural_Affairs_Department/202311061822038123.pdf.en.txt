Preamble: Various programmes and activities are being undertaken by the State on the occasion of 350th Coronation Year of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav." In the same background, the government was considering to give administrative and financial approval to the organizing cost of the cultural program" Azadi 75" based on revolutionaries, freedom fighters who made a valuable contribution to the country's freedom struggle and to showcase the culture of Maharashtra on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Independence.
Government Decision: On the occasion of 350th Coronation Year of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," an amount of Rs. 12,10,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Ten Thousand only) is being provided as administrative and financial sanction for organizing the cultural program" Azadi 75" on 16th August, 2023 at Borivali, Mumbai.
