Preamble: The 350th anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is being celebrated with various cultural programmes/ activities across the state. On this occasion, the Government was considering to give administrative and financial approval to the cultural program" Shivavandana" to be held at Durbar Hall, Raj Bhavan on June 6, 2023 and the expenses incurred on it.
Government Decision:" On the occasion of the 350th" Shivarajyabhishek Ceremony" of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the cultural program" Shivvandana" to be held on June 6, 2023 at Darbar Hall, Raj Bhavan and the amount of Rs. 33,15,000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) is being given administrative and financial approval under this Government Decision.
