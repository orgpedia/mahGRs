Introduction: The aforementioned No. As per the decisions of the government, every year the State Cultural Awards are awarded to the distinguished personalities who have done remarkable work in 12 different cultural fields. The awards include in the field of dance and the proposal to give this award in the name of" Acharya Parvati Kumar" was under the consideration of the government.
The State Cultural Awards are conferred on 12 personalities from different cultural fields. Among these, the award in the field of' Nritya' will be given in the name of' Acharya Parvati Kumar' from this year.
