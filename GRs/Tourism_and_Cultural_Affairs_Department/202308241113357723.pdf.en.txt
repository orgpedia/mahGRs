the Government Order
As the post of Joint Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai is lying vacant since 01.6.2023 due to superannuation with effect from 01.06.2023, Shri Sanjay Krishnaji Patil, Deputy Commissioner of State Tax, Office of the Commissioner of State Tax, Mumbai is being posted on deputation to the post of Joint Managing Director, Maharashtra Film, Theatre and Cultural Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai, as per the provisions of the rules in force under reference 1& 2.
The terms and conditions of the said deputation are set out in the Annexure as per Annexure-II under Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-Acceptance Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981 and in view of the instructions issued from time to time by the Finance Department and the General Administration Department.
After Sanjay Krishnaji Patil takes over as Joint Managing Director, the additional charge of the post held by Mrs. Geeta Deshpande, Chief Administrative Officer, will automatically cease to exist.
