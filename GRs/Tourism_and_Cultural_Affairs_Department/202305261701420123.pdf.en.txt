Vinayak Damodar Savarkar aka Swatantryaveer Savarkar's contribution to the nation is immense. On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Swatantryaveer Savarkar, various programs/ activities have been organized to honor his work and to promote his ideas.
The Government was considering to celebrate the birthday of Vinayak Damodar Savarkar, alias Swatantryaveer Savarkar, on 28th May as" Swatantryaveer Gaurav Diwas."
The Government has decided to celebrate the birthday of Shri Vinayak Damodar Savarkar alias Swatantryaveer Savarkar on 28th May as" Swatantryaveer Gaurav Divas" to honour his contribution to the cause of freedom.
The following types of various programs/ activities should be successfully organized by the various departments on the occasion of the birthday of Swatantryaveer Savarkar by the heads of the ministerial departments, head of the department offices in the state.
1." Veer Yatra": Organizing guided tours to historical places and monuments associated with Veer Savarkar and to make the youth aware about the life and contribution of Veer Savarkar in the freedom struggle.
Seminars: Organizing seminars in colleges and universities with historians, experts and scholars to discuss the life and work of Veer Savarkar.
4. To organize open seminars of students in various educational institutions on the themes of patriotism, valor and Veer Savarkar's vision for Bharatbhoomi.

All Ministerial Departments, Divisional Commissioners, all Collectors and Chief Executive Officers should take the cooperation of the concerned in the field of their department to successfully organize these programs/ activities from their available budgetary provision.
