Read: Letter from Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad No. JA No. 2023/ S.P./ M.M.D./ SANKIRNA/ KAVI-, dated 7th September, 2023
Preamble: Various programmes and activities are being undertaken by the Government to mark the culmination of the Amrit Mahotsav year of the Marathwada Liberation War. On this occasion, the matter of approving the sample of oath to be taken in various government programs to be organized in Marathwada region was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The following sample of oaths to be taken in various government programs to be organized in Marathwada region on the occasion of the concluding ceremony of the Amrit Mahotsav year of Marathwada Liberation War are being approved under this government decision.
A sample of the oath:
We will take full pride in the freedom struggle of Marathwada, which gave us the citizenship of independent India and connected us with the glorious Maharashtra.
We will be grateful to the fact that this freedom struggle has been proved by the invaluable sacrifices of many freedom fighters and the active support of the people.
As citizens of sovereign India, we will always remain committed to the prosperity of the land of Marathwada while adhering to the Maharashtra Dharma.
