Preamble: Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" initiative, various innovative programmes/ activities are being organized. With the aim of honouring the people who have made great contributions in the field of art and literature, and to make the new generation aware of those works/ persons/ important virtues and to increase the knowledge of the new generation through that information, festivals are organized on the occasion of diamond, nectar, centenary of such persons/ works. On the same lines, the government was considering giving financial approval to the initiative of recreating old plays and preserving those plays by filming them.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, an amount of Rs. 28,80,000/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) is being approved for the activities to be undertaken for re-creation of old plays and preservation of those plays.
