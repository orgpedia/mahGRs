Preamble: Reference No. The Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Academy awards literary prizes to those who have made significant contributions to the field of Sindhi literature.
Accordingly, the matter of approving the expenditure for the award ceremony for the year 2018-19 was under consideration.
Government Decision: The Maharashtra State Sindhi Sahitya Akademi has approved the expenditure of Rs. 8,71,500/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Seventy-One Thousand Five Hundred only) for the award ceremony for the year 2018-19 as per the following details.
