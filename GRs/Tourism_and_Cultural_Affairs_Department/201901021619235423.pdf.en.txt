Read:- 1) District Planning Committee, Collector's Office, Pune's letter No. Ni-7/ Visas/ Khed/ 2018-19/3820, Dy. 17.09.2018 2) Letter dated 03.12.2018 from the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums, Mumbai vide No.
Government Decision: The State Protected Forts of Sangramdurg Fort, Chakan, Taluka Khed, District Pune have been damaged to some extent over time and the architectural remains inside the fort have been buried under the rubble. It is necessary to remove the debris from the fort area and expose the architecture. Member of Legislative Assembly, Khed-Alandi has approved excavation at Sangramdurg Fort from his local development fund. Mahesh Landge, Shri. Bhosari, Member of Legislative Assembly, and Firangoji Narsala Pratishthan, the fort's caretaker, have been following up on the restoration of the fort. For excavation in the premises of the monument, the Sangramdurg Fort at the aforesaid No. The Assistant District Planning Officer, District Planning Committee, Pune has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 5.00 lakh under the MLA Local Area Development Programme 2018-19. In pursuance of this approval, the Assistant Director, Pune has prepared a detailed budget of Rs. 5.00 lakh for the year 2018-19 as per the State Schedule.
Request for administrative and financial approval of the said Budget No. 2 under the Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums to the Government. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Under this Government decision, an amount of Rs. 5.00 lakh (Rupees Five Lakhs only) has been allocated for the excavation of the Sangramdurg Fort, Chakan, Taluk Khed, District Pune. Administrative and financial approval is being given subject to the approval given by the Assistant District Planning Officer.
The Director, Directorate of Archaeology and Museums is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer and he or an officer authorised by him is authorised to sign the payment in this regard.
