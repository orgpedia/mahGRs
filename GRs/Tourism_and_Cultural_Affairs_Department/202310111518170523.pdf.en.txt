Introduction: In order to preserve and promote our culture, to maintain social commitment during the festival celebrations and to celebrate eco-friendly festivals, the State Government has decided to give awards to the best public Ganeshotsav Mandals in the State during the Sarvajanik Ganeshotsav held from 19.9.2023 to 28.08.2023. The government has taken a decision. For this, a total of 44 best Ganeshotsav Mandals, 3 each from Mumbai, Mumbai (Suburban), Pune and Thane districts and 1 each from other districts have been selected. The names of the first, second and third place winners of the district level Ganeshotsav Mandals out of 44 best Ganeshotsav Mandals received by the District Level Committee have been recommended by the District Level Committee constituted under the Government Decision No. The State Level Committee constituted under the Government Resolution No. 1. The report submitted to the government through the letter here has been submitted.
The best Ganeshotsav Mandals in the state will be awarded with a prize of Rs. 5.00 lakh, the first place will get Rs. 2.50 lakh and the third place will get Rs. 1.00 lakh. The remaining 41 Ganeshotsav Mandals will be awarded with prizes and certificates of Rs 25,000/- each by the State Government. Accordingly, the government was considering to announce the names of the public Ganeshotsav Mandals in the Public Ganeshotsav Competition 2023.
