Clarification on guidelines for shooting of films, TV serials/ OTTs in the wake of COVID-19.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: COCHIN-2020/ P.S. NO. 37/ P.S. 1
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry (Extension), Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 23rd of June 2020
See also: Government Decision dated 30 May 2020
Government's decision
The shooting of films, TV serials/ OTTs, which have been stopped due to COVID-19 outbreak, will be resumed in a controlled manner. The guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision dated May 30, 2020.
Some issues were raised regarding the difficulties faced by the concerned institutions/ organizations in implementing the guidelines in the said Government decision. Clarification orders are being issued in this regard. These points and their explanations are given in the accompanying Table.
3. The agencies concerned shall take action in accordance with the said clarificatory instructions.
