Guidelines for reopening of theatres under" Mission Begin Again" in the wake of COVID-19.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs
Government Resolution No.: Narikari 8220/ P. No. 211/ S.C. 4
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry (Extension), Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is November 5, 2020
Read: 1) Central Government, Ministry of Home Affairs, Order No. 40-3/2020-DM-I (A), Dy. 30.09.2020 2) Order, Revenue& Forest Department, Disaster Management, Relief& Rehabilitation, No.DMU-2020/ PR No.92/ DSM-1, dated 3rd November, 2020
Preamble: In order to contain the spread of COVID-19 virus, the Government has imposed restrictions on various activities/ works under the Government Orders issued from time to time. Also, certain restrictions have been relaxed. The Government has received requests from various associations/ institutions in the sector for permission to start theatres which have been closed in the wake of COVID-19. In view of the above, the State Govt. 2 Theatres have been allowed to start as per the order here. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to set guidelines for the opening of theatres in a controlled manner. Hence, the government took this decision.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the wake of COVID-19, the Government has approved the opening of theatres in the State in a controlled manner with effect from 5th November, 2020, subject to the guidelines contained in Annexure-A and (Annexure-I) annexed to this Government Decision.
2. Keeping in view the noble intention of the Government behind granting this permission, it is necessary for all concerned to operate the theatres in a manner that they do not violate any of the restrictions of the Central Government and the State Government regarding COVID-19. Action will be taken against those found violating these guidelines.
