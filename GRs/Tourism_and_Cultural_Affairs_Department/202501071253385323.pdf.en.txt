The following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA) 1. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant G.R. have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
3. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
4. The VPDA is applicable only for the State Schemes and not for Central Sector or Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
5. The Receipt to the said VPDA shall be only such grants drawn under MTR 44 for the object heads 31 Grants-in-Aid (Non salary) and 35 Grants-in-Aid for creation of capital assets. No amount under Capital heads and Loan heads of expenditure shall be transferred to the VPDA. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate after incurring expenditure from the VPDA.
6. The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the respective Grant
7. The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund after completion of one year from the date of drawal of the amount of funds/date of credit of the amount to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order, whichever 15 earlier.
8. The balances under the VPDA shall be reconciled monthly by the Administrator with the District Treasury Officer.
9. The balances shall be reconciled with the A.G. office by the Treasury Officer who shall submit Balance Certificate to A.G. office for confirmation of their balances in the books of A.G at the end of the Financial Year.
