Decision: The government. Policy for funding of International Film Festival Organizations has been announced on 18.10.2012. In pursuance of this policy, Metro Foundation of India and Kala Vaibhava Sanstha, Nashik have contributed Rs. 5.00 lakh within the limit of 80% of the subsidy of Rs. 4.00 lakh (Rupees Four Lakhs) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
The financial assistance sanctioned will be admissible as per the Bombay Financial Rules.
(2) The grant sanctioned shall be non-recurring and subject to the terms and conditions of the regular grant-in-aid of the Government.
3. Funds should be utilised only for the purpose for which they are sanctioned and immediately.
If not, the amount of financial assistance will have to be returned to the government along with the prevailing interest rate of the bank.
It will be mandatory to submit the Utilization Certificate, Profit and Loss Account and Balance Sheet at least 3 months after the conclusion of the film festival.
At least one representative of the Government shall be nominated by the Institute on the Awards/ Felicitation Committee to be presented at the Film Festival.
6. The film festival should be highlighted as" in association with Department of Cultural Affairs, Government of Maharashtra" through advertisement, invitation card, etc. The cooperation of the Government of Maharashtra will be clearly understood by all associated with the festival as well as the media, in such a way that the organizers should highlight it before and during the festival.
7. Governing body to give decision. It will be mandatory to fulfill all the terms and conditions of the policy announced under 18.10.2012.
