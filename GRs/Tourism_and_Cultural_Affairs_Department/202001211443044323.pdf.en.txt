Regarding the cancellation of the appointments of non-government members on the main committee for publishing a gazetteer on Marathi musical theatre in the state gazetteer series.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs,
Government Resolution No: Darshani-2020/ P.S.No.15/ P.S.O.2
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: January 21, 2020
Read: Government Resolution No.Darshni-2019/ P.S.No.64/ 2 dated 19th September, 2019
Preamble: As the Marathi musical theatre is completing 175 years, the main committee has been constituted to publish a separate state gazetteer on the subject in three volumes on the occasion of the centenary Amrit Mahotsav. The government had decided to cancel the appointments of all the non-official members of the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this Government Decision, the appointments of the following non-official members to the Main Committee constituted for the purpose of publishing the Gazetteer on the subject of Marathi Sangeet Rangbhoomi in the State Gazetteer Series are being cancelled:
-----------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Name                       | designation         |
| 1.   | Dr. Ravindra Ghangurde     | non-official member |
| 2.   | Dr. Mukund Ramamurthe      | non-official member |
| 3.   | Mr. Shaunak Abhishek       | non-official member |
| 4.   | Mr. Gyanesh Pendharkar     | non-official member |
| 5.   | Mr. Sanjay Gosavi          | non-official member |
| 6.   | Mrs. Medha Gogate-Joglekar | non-official member |
-----------------------------------------------------------
The appointments of all non-official members on the main committee constituted for publishing the gazetteer on Marathi Sangeet Rangbhoomi in the State Gazetteer Series shall stand cancelled with effect from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
