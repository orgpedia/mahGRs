Ms. Supriya Karmarkar, Deputy Inspector General and Deputy Controller of Stamps, Aurangabad is being appointed as Assistant Director (Tourism), Regional Office, Directorate of Tourism, Pune on deputation subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) Ms. Supriya Karmarkar is being appointed as Assistant Director (Tourism), Regional Office, Pune till the date of her joining or until further orders, subject to upgrade.
(b) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence on the date on which Smt. Supriya Karmarkar, Deputy Inspector General and Deputy Controller of Stamps, Aurangabad, joins the office of Deputy Commissioner and shall end on the date on which she resumes charge of her original office.
(c) If the Government considers it necessary in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority i.e. Revenue and Forest Department shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
d) If their services are not required by a non-employee employer, the government i.e. the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs will have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the Department shall give a notice of three months to the Revenue and Forest Department; and d) Ms. Supriya Karmarkar shall be repatriated to the parent Department after giving at least three months notice to the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs that she intends to return to the parent Department.
(e) Mrs. Supriya Karmarkar shall be governed by the provisions of Rules 39 and 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Accession, Non-Acceptance of Services) Rules, 1981 and the provisions of Annexure III thereunder. Further, the provisions of General Administration Department, S.R.V.-2011/ S.No. 137/12 dated 17/12/2016 shall also apply.
The service matters of Ms. Supriya Karmarkar will be handled by her parent department.
