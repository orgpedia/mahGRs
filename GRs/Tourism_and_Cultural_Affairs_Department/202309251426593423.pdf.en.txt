Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date of release: September 25, 2023
Reference: 1. Department of Tourism, Government Decision No. TDC- 2014/2/ Q. No.93/ Tourism, D. 14/07/2016 2. Department of Finance, Ministry of Finance, Government Order No. Sanleco-2023/ No. 90/ Treasurer, P.O. 11/08/2023.
Preamble: Shri Vithal Sude, Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Government of Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service. Shri Vithal Sudhe, Deputy Director being in the pay scale of S-23 (67,700- 2,08,700), Chief Accounts Officer, Directorate of Tourism was proposed to be promoted to the pay scale of S-16 (44,900- 1,42,400), Deputy Director, Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service, S-23 (67,700- 2,08,700).
Government Decision: The post of Chief Accounts Officer, Directorate of Tourism, S-16 (44,900 1,42,400) is being temporarily upgraded to the rank of Deputy Director, Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service, S-23 (67,700- 2,08,700).
The enhanced post will continue to be in existence till the incumbent, Mr. Sudhe, Deputy Director, Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service, is appointed to the post, after which the post will automatically be converted into the original post, that is, the post of Chief Accounts Officer and will be downgraded.
The order was issued by the Finance Department in its informal reference No. 3817, d. are being issued as per the agreement dated 14/09/2023.
