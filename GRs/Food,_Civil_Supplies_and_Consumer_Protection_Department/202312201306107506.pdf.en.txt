Whereas, Mr. Nilesh Vithalrao Rathod, Inspecting Officer (Supplies), Tehsil Office Washim, District Washim, has been booked under Sections 7 and 12 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 (Amendment 2018) at Police Station, Washim City, District Washim, for offence No. 0896/2022, dated 25.11.2022 in exercise of the powers conferred under Rule 4 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, vide Reference No. 5, Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division. He has been suspended from Government service vide order dated 12.12.2022.
Accordingly, in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 4 (5) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, and in supersession of the said order, Shri Nilesh Vithalrao Rathod is being reinstated in the Government service.
Further, under this order, Mr. Nilesh Vithalrao Rathod, Inspection Officer (Supply) is being posted to the vacant post of Inspection Officer (Supply), Tehsil Office, Kannada, Taluka-Kannada, District-Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar after reinstatement.
4. District Supply Officer, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar to implement this order and engage Mr. Nilesh Vithalrao Rathod, Inspection Officer (Supply).
