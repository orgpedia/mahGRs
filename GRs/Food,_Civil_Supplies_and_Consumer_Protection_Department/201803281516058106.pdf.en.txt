Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. Varasgaon-Kolad Administrative approval has been given after examining the proposal of repairing the godown in Roha G. Raigad. This administrative approval has been given to Varasgaon-Kolad. The matter of providing funds for the repair work of the godown in Roha G. Raigad was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision The approval is being given to provide funds for the repair work of the government godown at Roha G. Raigad as indicated in the following statement subject to the conditions in the order granting administrative approval.

2.The repair work of the government godowns provided with funds as above should be started and completed immediately. After the warehouse repair is started, first the complete details of the repair as well as the progress of the repair should be reviewed every month by the District Supply Officer, Raigad.
3. The District Supply Officer, Raigad should take care to submit the utility certificate to the government as soon as the repair work of the government godown is completed.
4. The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Supply Officer, Raigad concerned shall jointly prepare a site inspection report to verify that the repair work of the said godown has been completed and the work is as per the approved budget and it should be preserved in the records.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that there is no cost overrun in repair of godowns due to delay in repair or other reasons. It may be noted that these funds cannot be transferred/ retained as a deposit contribution.
6. The cost of repair of the godown during the financial year 2017-18 should be spent under the head Demand No. M-2, Head Account Head 2408 Food Storage and Warehousing, (03) Procurement Distribution and Price Control (03) (02) Mufassal, (Non-Plan) (Dattamat) (2408 0108) 27 Small Constructions.
Similarly, if the funds provided for the repair of the warehouse are not likely to be spent for some reason, then a report should be submitted to the government with the reasons. If the funds disbursed under the said order are not spent before 30th March, 2018, the action to withdraw these funds should be taken by the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary.
District Supply Officer, Raigad should take care that no more funds are released for the repair work of the concerned godowns than the cost as per the administrative approval order. The entire responsibility of this will be of the District Supply Officer, Raigad.
9. In the administrative sanction order, the brief nature of the work is mentioned and in fact, the repair work of the godown should be completed according to the budget of the repair work submitted to the government. That is, the repair work of the warehouse should be completed according to the budget presented to the government.
The order is being issued in accordance with the financial powers conferred on the administrative department as per Schedule 10 of Part I Sub-Division V of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 and Para 134 of the Maharashtra Public Works Rules, 1978.
