The government's decision:
The following are the 733 temporary supply posts under various schemes/ works at Divisional/ District/ Taluka level under the administrative control of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department (14 tables containing details of these 733 temporary posts are attached herewith). The deadline was extended to February 28, 2018. Now the posts are not vacant for more than 6 months subject to the condition that the date is from 1st March, 2018. The government has decided to continue the scheme till September 30, 2018. :
