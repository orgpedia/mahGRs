The Proposal:
The high-level committee has unanimously approved the review of posts in the establishment of various department heads under this department. Accordingly, the revised figure has been declared under the Government Decision dated 26/6/2003 in Reference No. 1 regarding declaration of revised figure.
Reference No. 2. 2 As per the decision of the Government, 210 temporary posts out of the total sanctioned 940 posts at Mumbai and district level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 1/10/2018 to 28/2/2019. After the expiry of the said period, the following decision has been taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 210 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A annexed to this Government Decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation, are being extended from 1/3/2019 to 30/9/2019 (seven months).
The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head Demand No., M-3, Main Account Head 3475, Other General Financial Services (106), Regulation of Weights and Measures (00) (01) Regulation of Weights and Measures, (34750093) and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20.
