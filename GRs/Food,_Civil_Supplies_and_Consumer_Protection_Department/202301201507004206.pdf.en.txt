3 The funds provided for the construction of the warehouse concerned should be utilised for the construction of the same warehouse. Disciplinary action will be proposed against the concerned officers/ employees responsible if it is found that the said funds have been utilised for construction of other godowns.
Disciplinary action will be proposed against the concerned officers/ employees responsible for the delay in the construction of the warehouse or if it is found that the increased expenditure on the construction of the warehouse is due to other reasons.
As soon as the funds sanctioned for the respective warehouses are spent directly, the warehouse-wise Utilization Certificate of the appropriation of funds is mandatory to be submitted to the government by the concerned District Supply Officer/ Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation, Pune.
While the work of construction of the said godown is in progress, the concerned Superintending Engineer, Public Works Department/ Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation, Pune should visit at least three times to ensure the quality of work and submit such report to the Collector in writing from time to time.
7. The Collector shall personally visit the godown before taking over the godown after completion of the construction of the godown and ensure that the work done is of good quality. If there are any lapses, it should be rectified by the Public Works Department/ Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation, Pune and its site inspection report should be preserved in the records.
The funds referred to in Sections 1 to 38 shall be transferred to the District Supply Officer concerned and the funds referred to in Sections 39 to 54 shall be transferred to the District Supply Officer concerned. As per the provision dated 26.08.2022, the transfer to a separate account opened for this scheme by Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation, Pune should be done by the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary, Mumbai.
As soon as this order is received, the provisions of the above statement No. 1 to 38 should be immediately distributed by the District Collector to the concerned Executive Engineer of the Public Works Department on the Budget Distribution System (BDS). Cash should not be withdrawn.
The funds made available under this Order shall be spent by the end of February 28, 2023. The funds made available under this order cannot be transferred as deposit contribution.
The concerned District Supply Officer/ Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation, Pune shall ensure that no more funds are disbursed beyond the revised estimated cost after scrutiny as per the Administrative Sanction Order for the construction of the concerned warehouse.
