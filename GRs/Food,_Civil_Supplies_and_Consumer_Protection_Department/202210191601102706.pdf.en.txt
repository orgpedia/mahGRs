Preamble: Reviewing the posts of all cadres in the subordinate offices of the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department. With the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary, Reference No. 1 The revised pattern of posts under this Department has been fixed as per the Government decision dated 18/04/2022. Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 04/07/2022 of this Department, the cadre wise number of posts at Divisional/ District/ Taluk level in the Sub Divisional level Supply Offices has been fixed.
As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 21st June, 2006 of the Finance Department, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to convert 80% of the total posts into permanent posts as per the revised figure of posts in all cadres in the Subordinate Supply Offices of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
A total of 119 sanctioned posts in all cadres at the level of Deputy Commissioner (Supplies) office under Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department. There are a total of 4175 sanctioned posts in all cadres at the district level and taluka level. There are a total of 45 posts in all cadres in the office of Food Distribution Officer Grade-2. The Government have decided to regularise 80% of the sanctioned posts of 4339 posts in all the cadres of the said offices subject to the conditions contained in the Government Resolution dated 21st June, 2006 of the Finance Department as per the revised figure. The permanent/ temporary classification of the posts will be as indicated in the attached prospectus.
