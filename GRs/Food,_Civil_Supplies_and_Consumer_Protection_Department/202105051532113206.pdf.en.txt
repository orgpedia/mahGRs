Introduction: Refer No. Under the Government Order here, Mr. Shalikram Baliram Bharadi, District Supply Officer (Suspended), was dismissed from Government service on the ground that the allegations against him were serious in nature and his conduct had tarnished the image of the Government. The order dated 27.11.2020 was suspended.
Reference No. 2. Under Section 2, Mr. Bharadi has submitted an application to the government to cancel the suspension and join the job. In response to this request, The Minister (Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection) has issued orders to issue strict warning to Mr. Bharadi and make him join duty. Therefore, the matter of revoking Mr. Bharadi's suspension and reinstating him in government service was under the consideration of the Government.
the Government Order
Whereas, in exercise of the power conferred by Rule 4 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, the Government have appointed Shri Bharadi as a Member of the Commission vide Reference No. 1 has been suspended from government service.
Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred under Rule 4 (5) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, this order is hereby issued ending the suspension of Mr. Bharadi, District Supply Officer and issuing strict warning to him.
3. Mr. Shalikram Baliram Bharadi, District Supply Officer is being re-appointed as District Supply Officer, Chandrapur.
