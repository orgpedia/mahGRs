Introduction: Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection The revised Service Entry Rules for the posts of Deputy Commissioner (Supply), District Supply Officer and Food Distribution Officer, Group-A, Assistant District Supply Officer, Technical Officer and Inspection Officer (Supply), Group-B (Gazetted) under the Department have been published through a notification dated 27.3.2023.
As the post of Inspection Officer (Supplies) is a state level post, as per the revised entry into service rules, the post of Inspection Officer (Supplies) is a state level post. Therefore, the Inspection Officer (Supplies), Group-B Gazetted, in the Supplies Department under the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department, has been appointed as the Supervisory Officer. The matter of publication of Provisional Seniority List for finalizing the Provisional Seniority List showing the status as on 28.03.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT Circular: In view of the matter mentioned in the Preamble, now the posts of Inspection Officer (Supplies), Group-B Gazetted, in the Supplies Department under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection will be filled up. The provisional seniority list showing the position as on 28.03.2023 is being published in Annexure-A.

4. All Deputy Commissioners (Supply) are requested to publicize the said provisional seniority list properly and bring it to the notice of all concerned employees of the department. Also, all the concerned employees should make a copy of this seniority list available on the government website.
If the concerned employees have objections regarding this seniority list, the concerned employees will have to submit their objections.
The objections should be submitted to the Government through the Head of the Department (along with their comments by the Head of the Department) within 15 days from the date of this circular along with proper evidence/ certificate in support of the objection.
Objections not received within the above mentioned deadline will not be considered and the seniority list will be finalized.
As per Rule 3 (2) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the date of actual joining of the cadre is required to be mentioned in column 8 of Annexure-A before finalizing the said seniority list. Accordingly, all the concerned Deputy Commissioners (Supply) should submit the above information to the Government within 10 days from the date of this circular.
