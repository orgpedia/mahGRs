Read:- Government Decision dated 27/11/2020
Preamble: As per the decision of the Government. Pimpri. It has been approved to permanently transfer the warehouse at Osmanabad to the Election Department for storage of EVMs and VVPATs subject to payment of cost of Rs. 1,51,05, 000/- to the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection. However, since the construction of the godown has been overburdened, the matter of approval for payment of the excess cost is under consideration.
Government Corrigendum: 2. Pimpri. The Department has incurred an expenditure of Rs. 1,86,87, 469/-for construction of godown at Osmanabad. Therefore, in paragraph 2 of the Government Order read above, instead of the above-mentioned amount of Rs. 1,51,05, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty-one Lakh Five Thousand), it should be read as Rs. 1,86,57, 469/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty-six Lakhs Fifty-seven Thousand Four Hundred and Seventy-one).
