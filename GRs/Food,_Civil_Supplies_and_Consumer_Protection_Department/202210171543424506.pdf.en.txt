As per the Government Order, the Assistant Rationing Officer, Group-C, under the Controller Rationing and Director Civil Supplies, Mumbai, in the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, has been promoted to the post of Rationing Officer/ Chief Inspection Officer, Group-B (Gazetted). The place of posting has been notified as" 39 F Dombivali" by the promotion of Mr. Deepak Shrikrishna Rao Dolus (Divyang) from 18. Instead, it should be read as" 39 F Up Dombivali."
