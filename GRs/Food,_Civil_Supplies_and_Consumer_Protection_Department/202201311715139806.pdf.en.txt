No new personnel has been appointed in the cadre of Assistant Controller Valid Metrology during the calendar year 2019 and 2020. In fact, d. The seniority list of the newly appointed employees between January 1, 2021 and December 31, 2021 is being published directly on January 1, 2022.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been adopted while preparing this seniority list. The said common seniority has been regularly worked out to determine the inter se seniority of employees working in the cadre of Assistant Controller, Valid Metrology.
The concerned authorities should submit suggestions and objections, if any, to the government through the Controller, Valid Metrology, within 15 days from the date of publication of this provisional seniority list. In case of non-receipt of objections/ errors within the stipulated period, the final seniority list will be published, assuming the details mentioned in the seniority list are correct.
5. The Controller, Valid Metrology shall bring to the notice of all employees of the said cadre the temporary common seniority in the attached qualification of the Assistant Controller. Further, after receiving representations from the concerned employees regarding objections/ errors, the Controller of Information regarding the issues therein shall examine the same as per the records in the valid Metrology Office and submit the representations along with the feedback to the Government within the next 15 days.
