Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. The proposal for the repair of these state government warehouses has been given administrative approval after due scrutiny by the Public Works Department. dated 19.07.2017 and dt. Under the Government decision dated 12.03.2018, funds were provided for the repair work of the godown. The District Collector distributed Rs. 64,68,000/- has been dedicated to Budget Distribution System. District Supply Officer, Yavatmal Considering the expenditure as per the administrative approval earlier, the remaining required funds have been sought for the repair work of the godown at Kelapur. This is white.
The matter of disbursing funds for the repair work of the godown in Kelapur G. Yavatmal was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
White The approval is being given to provide funds for the repair work of the government warehouse in Kelapur G. Yavatmal as indicated in the following statement, subject to the conditions in the order granting administrative approval.

2.The repair work of the government godowns provided with funds as above should be started and completed immediately. After the repair of the godown is started, the district supply officer, Yavatmal, should first review the complete details of the repair and related repairs every month and submit the progress of the repair to the government.
3. The District Supply Officer, Yavatmal should take care to submit the utility certificate to the government as soon as the repair work of the government warehouse is completed.
4. The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Supply Officer, Yavatmal concerned shall jointly prepare the site inspection report after completion of the repair work of the said godown and ensure that the work is done as per the approved budget and it is preserved in the records.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that there is no cost overrun in repair of godowns due to delay in repair or other reasons. It may be noted that these funds cannot be transferred/ retained as a deposit contribution.
6. The cost of repair of the godown during the financial year 2017-18 should be spent under the head Demand No. M-2, Head Account Head 2408 Food Storage and Warehousing, (03) Procurement, Distribution and Price Control (03) (02) Mufassal, (Non-Plan) (Dattamat) (24080108) 27 Small Constructions.
7. Similarly, if for some reason the funds provided for the repair work of the concerned godown are not likely to be spent, then a report should be submitted to the government with the reasons. If the funds disbursed under the said order are not spent before 30th March, 2018, the action to withdraw these funds should be taken by the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary.
District Supply Officer, Yavatmal should take care that no more funds are released for the repair work of the concerned godowns than the cost as per the administrative approval order. The District Supply Officer, Yavatmal will be responsible for this.
9. In the administrative sanction order, the brief nature of the work is mentioned and in fact, the repair work of the godown should be completed according to the budget of the repair work submitted to the government.
That is, the repair work of the warehouse should be completed according to the budget presented to the government.
