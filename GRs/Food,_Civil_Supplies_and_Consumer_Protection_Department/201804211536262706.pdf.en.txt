the prelude
Under the public distribution system, essential commodities are distributed to ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. Goregaon, Raigad District, Govt. 1, T. The budget for the repair of the godown at Mangaon has been received by the government from the District Supply Officer, Raigad. Goregaon, Govt. 1, T. The matter of administrative approval to the proposal for the repair of Mangaon, G. Raigad godowns was under consideration of the government
Government's decision
Goregaon in Raigad district, Government Warehouse No. 1, T. Administrative approval is being granted for the Mangaon repair works and budget subject to the following conditions.
1) Work should not be started without the approval of the warehouse map, concept and site plan by the competent authorities.
2) First of all, the budgets should be certified by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department.
3) Care should be taken that the repair of the warehouse does not incur additional costs due to delay in repair or other reasons.
4) The District Supply Officer Raigad should first ensure the need for the repair work in the budget and only then take up the work.
