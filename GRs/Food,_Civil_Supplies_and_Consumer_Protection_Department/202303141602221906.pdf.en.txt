The Proposal:
According to the Finance Department. The Government Circular dated 01.07.2021 has issued revised instructions on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. As per the revised procedure, for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme, the concerned administrative department is required to appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the bank authorised for the scheme. According to the Department. As per the circular dated 01.07.2022, Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary, Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection has been appointed as SNA for National Food Security Scheme to be implemented under National Food Security Act, 2013. A single Nodal Bank Account has also been opened in ICICI Bank and 4048 MH- Revision of norms for central assistance released to States/ UTs on meeting expenditure on intra state movement of foodgrains and FPS dealers margin under NFSA-4048 [MH505] for transfer of funds under National Food Security Scheme on PFMS system.
Government's decision:
MH-Revision of norms for Central Assistance under National Food Security Act, 2013 is being notified to States/ UTs for meeting expenditure on intra state movement handling of foodgrains and FPS dealers margin under NFSA-4048 [MH505] as Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Office of the Accountant, Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary, Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, Mumbai, (Drawing and Disbursing Code 7101005361).
