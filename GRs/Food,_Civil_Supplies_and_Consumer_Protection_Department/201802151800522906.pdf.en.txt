Preamble: The Government of India, Ministry of Consumer Affairs& Public Distribution, Department of Food& Public Distribution has issued instructions through a letter regarding the implementation of the special cleanliness drive from fair price shops from 16th February, 2018 to 28th February, 2018.
Accordingly, a proposal to implement a special cleanliness drive was under the consideration of the government.
Government Circular: The circular instructs to implement special cleanliness drive in fair price shops from 16th February, 2018 to 28th February, 2018.
2. Swachhta Pakhwada- Under the special cleanliness drive, fair price shops are required to take action as follows: I. Cleanliness drive should be carried out in the shops of fair price shopkeepers and retail kerosene license holders and in the vicinity of the shops.
Boards with various messages, slogans and other instructions related to cleanliness should be prominently displayed on the facades of all fair price shops and retail kerosene outlets.
Steps should be taken to prevent the spread of diseases and pests. If chemicals/ substances are to be used in this, due care should be taken that they are not harmful to human life.
(iv) Food samples should be kept in a clean and transparent package in a conspicuous place.
