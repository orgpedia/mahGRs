Circular
At present, the central government is taking necessary measures to ensure smooth supply of LPG and other petroleum products in view of the war-torn situation in Middle-East Asia. Accordingly, necessary instructions have been issued by the Central Government to the Oilseeds Producers and Marketing Companies under the respective orders.
Further measures are being directed to be taken immediately to regularize the LPG chain in the state, to effectively manage the potential shortage situation as well as to avoid unnecessary panic among the citizens.
To ensure smooth supply of LPG, to monitor the supply chain and to coordinate in the event of possible shortages, special committees should be set up in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region as well as at the district level as follows.

11. WhatsApp groups should be created at the Gram Panchayat and Taluka level to create awareness among the citizens by VAT on the representatives of PRIs / Social Organizations / Organizations.
12. Vehicles transporting and distributing LPG as well as gas agencies and distribution staff should be provided with adequate police protection.
13. Strict action should be taken as per the provisions of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 against the person committing irregularities like illegal use of domestic gas, hoarding and black marketing.
14. Since domestic LPG gas cylinders are a necessity of life, the above instructions should be strictly followed.
15. This circular shall remain in force until the present situation is restored.
