The government's decision:
The 842 temporary posts of warehouse staff appointed by the respective District Collectors as per the powers conferred to the District Collectors, Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Ahmednagar, Jalgaon, Solapur, Beed, Osmanabad, Latur, Akola, Buldhana, Nagpur, Bhandara, Gondia, Chandrapur and Gadchiroli for appointment of warehouse staff vide Government Decision No. EST- 1071/14706/ M, dated 29/06/71, dated 07/08/71, dated 25/08/71 and dated 14/12/71 have been filled up with effect from 01/03/2021 under the Government Decision No. The deadline has been extended to 31/08/2021.
Since the requirement of the posts of the said 842 warehouse employees is required to be continued, the Government hereby approve the continuation of the said posts subject to the condition that the total number of 842 temporary posts indicated in the accompanying return A are not vacant for a period of more than six months, from 1st March, 2022 to 31st August, 2022 or till the new pattern of the Department is approved, whichever is earlier. No new post should be created without the prior approval of the government. Also, the Collector should take further action only after confirming the posts of extension.
