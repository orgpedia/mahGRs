The order:
Khairnar, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Raver Division, Jalgaon, was arrested by the Anti-Corruption Bureau, Jalgaon on September 27, 2022, at the office of Deputy Superintendent of Police, Anti-Corruption Bureau, Jalgaon, under the Prevention of Corruption Act.
And, as Mr. Khairnar Inspector is under investigation for a criminal offence against him and was remanded to police/ judicial custody for a period of more than 48 hours with effect from 27.09.2022.
Khairnar, Inspector, Validation, Raver Department, Dist.
Jalgaon is deemed to have been placed under suspension under the provisions of sub-rule (2) of rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979 with effect from the date of his detention in police/ judicial custody i.e. 27.09.2022 and shall remain suspended till further orders.
It is further ordered that during the period this order shall be in force, Shri. Headquarters of Khairnar Inspector, Joint Controller, Valid Metrology, Nashik will be at Divisional Office, Nashik. Khairnar, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Raver Division, G. Jalgaon will not be able to accept private jobs. They cannot engage in business or trade. If, while under suspension, Mr. S.R. Khairnar, Inspector, takes up a private job or engages himself in trade or industry, he shall be deemed to have committed misconduct and shall be ineligible for the subsistence allowance.
Khairnar, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Raver Division, Jalgaon, shall be paid subsistence allowance and other supplementary allowances in accordance with the provisions of Rule 68 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Payment during the period of assumption of office, post-retirement service, suspension, dismissal from service) Rules, 1981, so long as the order of suspension is in force.
