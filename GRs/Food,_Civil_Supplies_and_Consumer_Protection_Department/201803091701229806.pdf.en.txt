Preamble: The Government have decided to allow the sale of milk and milk products of Mahananda Dairy from the official fair price shops in the Mumbai-Thane rationing zone under the control of the Controller of Rationing and Director of Civil Supplies, Mumbai. Permission has been granted as on 19.01.2018.
On the same lines, to increase the income of fair price shops in all other districts of the state, the government was considering a proposal to allow milk and milk products of Mahananda Dairy to be put for sale in all fair price shops in the state.
Government Decision: Allow milk and milk products of Mahananda Dairy to be put up for sale from the official fair price shop in the Mumbai-Thane rationing zone under the control of the Controller of Rationing and Director of Civil Supplies, Mumbai. The permission has been granted as per the Government decision dated 19.01.2018.
Now, in order to increase the income of all the authorized fair price shops in the state including the Mumbai-Thane rationing area, the milk and dairy products of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited, Mumbai (Mahananda) are being allowed to be sold in all the authorized fair price shops in the state.
Milk and milk products will be distributed to fair price shops through authorized distributors of Mahananda Dairy.
