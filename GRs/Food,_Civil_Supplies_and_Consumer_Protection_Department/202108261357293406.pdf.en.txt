As per the notification dated 8 th April, 2021, for effective implementation of Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016, instructions have been issued for constitution of State Level Certification Committees in all States and UTs and functions of the Committee have been assigned. Central government. As per the notification dated April 8, 2021, the government was contemplating to set up a state-level certification committee.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the setting up of State Level Certification Committee at the State level to regulate the quality and standardization of products by effectively implementing the Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016 in the State.
The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:
Principal Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour and Employment, Mumbai Member, Development Commissioner, Directorate of Industries, Mumbai Member, Maharashtra Chambers of Commerce and Industry, Mumbai Member, Federation of Association of Small Scale Industries (FASSI), Mumbai Member

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
To identify areas for creating new Indian Standards.
To determine the products, processes and systems to comply with Indian standards.
To suggest measures for effective implementation of the quality control orders issued by the Government of India.
(iv) Suggest measures to create awareness among consumers about the standards.
v. To take necessary measures to create a quality eco-system in the State.
(vi) to make other recommendations on the Bureau of Indian Standards.
The representative of the NGO will be appointed by the Chairman of the State Level Certification Committee and will have a tenure of three years. However, they can be re-designated.
Meetings of the Committee shall be held at least twice in a year and as many times as the Chairman deems fit.
The Deputy Director (General), Bureau of Indian Standards, Western Region, Mumbai shall provide necessary support to the Member Secretary in conducting the meetings of the aforesaid Committee and review the decisions taken in the meeting. Bureau of Indian Standards, Western Region, Mumbai will be the nodal office for this purpose.
8. The orders/ instructions issued pursuant to this order shall come into force with immediate effect from the date of issue of this Government Order.
