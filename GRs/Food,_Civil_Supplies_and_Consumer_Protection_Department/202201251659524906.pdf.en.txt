The memo:
The office of the Food Distribution Officer under the District Collector, Pune, is functioning at the Government Grain Warehouse, Fursungi. Santosh Jaihind Bhalerao (Mathadi Worker) died on 18/07/2020 due to Corona Virus infection while disembarking and disembarking his vehicle at Government Grain Warehouse, Phursungi, Pune for supply of essential commodities under Corona Virus (COVID-19)" Preventive Measures" as per the orders of Food Distribution Officer Office, Pune in the wake of COVID-19 pandemic.
2. The District Collector, Pune has declared the deceased as a Mathadi worker. Bhalerao's heirs are entitled to receive a sum of Rs.1000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty only) as per Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 2020/ Q. No.4/ Expenditure-9, dated 29/05/2020. A proposal for sanction of ex-gratia assistance to the tune of Rs. 50,00,000 (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) has been submitted.
In accordance with the order dated 24/03/2020 issued by the Office of Food Distribution Officer, Pune. Bhalerao was deputed for loading and unloading of foodgrains in a vehicle at Government Grain Warehouse, Phursungi, Pune for supply of essential commodities under the Corona Virus (COVID-19) preventive measures.
4. District Collector, Pune and Assistant Food Distribution Officer Office, Pune. It is certified that Shri Santosh Bhalerao was regularly attending duty for 14 days i.e. upto noon of 13/07/2020 before the date of his death and was discharging his duties towards supply of essential commodities under" Preventive Measures" to contain Corona Virus (COVID-19) outbreak at Government Grain Warehouse Fursungi, Pune.
