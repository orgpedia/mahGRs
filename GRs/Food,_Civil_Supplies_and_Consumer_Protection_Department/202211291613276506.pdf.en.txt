The State Food Commission has been constituted under Section 16 of the National Food Security Act, 2013. The Central Government will provide funds to the State Food Commission for setting up Non-Building Assets.
As per the revised methodology for Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes, the concerned administrative department shall appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for that scheme. In addition, the Finance Department has also issued Reference No. 1 regarding the appointment of an officer of at least Deputy Secretary rank as Nodal Officer for coordination of the scheme. 2 Government circulars have been issued. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the implementation and coordination of the said Centrally Sponsored Scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Finance Department Reference No. As per the instructions given in the Government Circular, the Government is approving the appointment of Single Nodal Agency and Nodal Officer for the implementation and coordination of Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented by the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection as follows.
