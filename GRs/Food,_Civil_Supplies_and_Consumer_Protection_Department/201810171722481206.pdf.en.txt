GOVERNMENT DECISIONS Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection No. 2017/ No. 149/ No. 22, D. September 12, 2017.
Preamble: Under the Targeted Public Distribution System in the State of Maharashtra, the gram and urad obtained from the procurement of gram and urad by the Central Government should be made available to the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority families at subsidized rates. In that regard. As per the demand made by the State Government vide letter dated 6.9.2018, the Central Government has provided Chana and Urad to the State of Maharashtra. The e-tendering process will be carried out for lifting of these gram and urad from the designated godowns of NAFED and transporting these gram and urad to the government godowns as prescribed by the state government through NeML NCDEX Group Company. The e-tendering process has already been started. The policy has been framed in accordance with the Government Decision dated 12.9.2017.
A decision is being taken to distribute 2 kg pulses per month to the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority families in the state as follows.
Decision of the Government: 1) It is hereby approved to distribute a total of 2 kg of pulses (1 kg of gram and 1 kg of urad dal or one of both pulses subject to a ceiling of 2 kg) per beneficiary per month at the rate ofâ‚¹ 35/-per kg to the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority families in the targeted Public Distribution System.
