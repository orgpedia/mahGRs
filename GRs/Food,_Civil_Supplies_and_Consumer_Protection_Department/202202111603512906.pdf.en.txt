Proposal: Decision to be made. 27.12.2021 to implement the Shiv Bhojan Scheme in the State more effectively and to have effective control over the functioning Shiv Bhojan Kendras. Instructions for installation of CCTV systems have been issued till 31.01.2022. The government was considering extending the deadline. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: 1) CCTVs to be installed at Shiv Bhojan centers. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2022. The officials at the field level have taken action to install CCTV systems in all Shiv Bhojan centers under their control. It will be held on February 28, 2022.
2). The Shiv Bhojan centers which will not have CCTV system by February 28, 2022. to be closed from 01.03.2022.
Necessary action should be taken immediately in accordance with the above instructions.
