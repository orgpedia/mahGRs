Government Circular: In order to meet the targets given under the National Food Security Act, 2013 in the state, eligible beneficiaries have been selected and included in the Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority family beneficiaries. 15.7.2019 to D. Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Antyodaya Abhiyan has been decided to be implemented during 14.8.2019. Under the said mission, the target has been set to provide 100 percent ration distribution to all eligible ration card holders under the National Food Security Scheme, 100 percent food grain distribution and gas connection to every family.
In pursuance of the above objective, instructions are being issued to all Deputy Commissioners (Supply), all District Supply Officers, all Food Distribution Officers as under: Appropriate action should be taken to provide gas connection as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 24th July, 2019.
Action should be initiated immediately as communicated to the Collector under the Government letter dated July 10, 2019.
Under the National Food Security Scheme for your district/ area. The targets set under the Government Decision of 16.11.2018 should be met without delay.
→ In order to accomplish this goal. The eligible beneficiaries should be selected as per the criteria prescribed under the Government Decision dated 6.7.2019.
→ The government should be informed of the additional amount required or the amount to be dedicated while completing this amount.
