Introduction: As per the above mentioned Government Decision No. 1, a total of 18000 Shiv Bhojan Thalis have been made available to the poor and needy people of the state at subsidized rates. In this, the limit of Shiv Bhojan scheme has been doubled as per the government decision dated February 18, 2020. Further, as per the circular dated 26.03.2020, the scheme has been extended to Taluk level from 01.04.2020 and the allocation to each district has been increased by five times. As per the circular dated March 28, 2020, the scope of the scheme has been increased five times from March 28, 2020 to the taluka level.
