The order:
The mother, Ma. Special Petition No. Prasad P. Kulkarni, Inspector, Legal Metrology under Prevention of Corruption Act. 05/2007 and sentenced to two years rigorous imprisonment and a fine of Rs.
Whereas, on conviction of Shri Prasad Kulkarni in the aforesaid criminal offence, the Controller, Valid Metrology vide order dated 19/12/2011 has dismissed him from service under Rule 13 (1) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
Prasad Kulkarni, Shri. Special Court, Aurangabad
Criminal Appeal No. 561/2011 was filed in the High Court, Bombay. And in this appeal. The High Court vide its order dated 21/10/2022 has directed the Hon. The Special Court has set aside the order of the Special Court and acquitted Mr. Kulkarni of all the charges.
Accordingly, the order dated 19/12/2011 of the Controller, Valid Metrology under Rule 25 (A) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979 is hereby set aside and Shri Prasad Kulkarni is being reinstated in Government service.
Shri Kulkarni is being posted as Inspector, Valid Metrology, Ahmednagar-I Division.
