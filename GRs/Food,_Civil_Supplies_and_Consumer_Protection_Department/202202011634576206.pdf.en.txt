Regarding the setting up of a portal for online procurement of paddy and coarse grains from the marketing season 2021-22.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Resolution No. Parikrama-2621/ P.No. 233/ No. P. 29.
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 1, 2022.
Read:- Government Decision No: Parichay- 2621/ P.No. 233/ No. Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection dated 12th September, 2021.
Preamble: As per the decision of the Government, in case of a significant increase in the prices of cheese items like Tur, Chickpea, Sugar etc. in the open market, it has already been approved to use the platform of NeML NCDEX Group Company for the distribution of these cheese items at fair prices under the targetted Public Distribution System and the process of paddy procurement under the Base Price Purchase Scheme using the platform of NeML NCDEX Group Company. Similarly, under the government decision, it has been approved to conduct the e-auction process in all the districts through the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department through the NeML NCDEX Group Company.
The matter of giving approval to use other portals as an alternative arrangement with the portal of the said company was under consideration of the government.
Based on the above, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: In case of substantial increase in the prices of cheese items like Tur, Chickpea, Sugar etc. in the open market, it is hereby approved to allocate these items at reasonable prices under the targeted Public Distribution System and to process the procurement of paddy under the Base Price Purchase Scheme using the platform of NeML NCDEX Group Company and BSE E-AGRICULTURAL MARKETS LIMITED (BEAM).
At the government level, the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection is approving the e-auction process in all the districts using the platform of NeML NCDEX Group Company and BSE E AGRICULTURAL MARKETS LIMITED (BEAM).
