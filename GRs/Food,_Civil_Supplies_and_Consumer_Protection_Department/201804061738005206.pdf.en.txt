In order to increase the income of all the authorized fair price shops in the state, the milk and milk products of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited, Mumbai (Mahananda) have been allowed to be sold in all the authorized fair price shops in the state. Similarly, the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries Development has requested to allow the distribution of milk and dairy products produced under the Government Milk Scheme in the state of Maharashtra as well as the Aarey brand milk and dairy products from the ration distribution centers in the state. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration to allow the sale of milk and milk products produced under the Government Milk Scheme in the state of Maharashtra as well as Aarey brand milk and milk products from all authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state.
Maharashtra: To increase the income of fair price shops in the state, milk and milk products produced under the government milk scheme in the state of Maharashtra, as well as Aarey brand milk and milk products, are being allowed to be put up for sale in all authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state.
Milk and milk products of these schemes will be distributed to fair price shops in the state through the authorized distributors of the government milk scheme and Aarey brand in the state of Maharashtra.

As per the scheme of the Government Milk Scheme and Aarey Band in the state of Maharashtra, for the commission received on the sale of milk and dairy products, the fair price shop owners will have to interact with the distributors of the respective schemes. This transaction will be between the government milk shops in the state of Maharashtra as well as the Aarey brand and the concerned fair price shop. The government will not interfere in it.
