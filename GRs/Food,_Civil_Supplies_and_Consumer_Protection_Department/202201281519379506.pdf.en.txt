Patil, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Shirpur Division, G. Dhule was arrested by the Anti-Corruption Bureau, Worli, Mumbai on December 8, 2021 at the Anti-Corruption Bureau, Worli, Mumbai under the Prevention of Corruption Act.
And whereas, Mr. S. K. Patil, Inspector, a criminal offence case against him is under investigation and he was remanded to police/ judicial custody for a period of more than 48 hours with effect from 8th December, 2021.
Patil, Inspector, Validiatrics, Shirpur Division, G. Dhule, is deemed to have been suspended with effect from the date of his detention in police/ judicial custody, i.e., 8th December, 2021, under the provisions of sub-rule (2) of rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, and shall remain suspended till further orders.
It is further ordered that during the period of operation of this order, the office of Mr. S. K. Patil, Inspector, Headquarters, Controller, Valid Metrology, shall remain at Headquarters, Mumbai. Mr. Patil, Inspector, Valid Metrology cannot leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Controller, Valid Metrology.
Patil, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Shirpur Division, G. Dhule will not be able to accept private jobs. They cannot engage in business or trade. If, while under suspension, Mr. S. K. Patil, Inspector, takes up a private job or engages himself in trade or industry, he shall be deemed to have committed misconduct and shall be ineligible to receive subsistence allowance.
