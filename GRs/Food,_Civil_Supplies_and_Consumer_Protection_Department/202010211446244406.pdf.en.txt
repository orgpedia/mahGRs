Circular:
The process of Unlock 5 has started across the country. The state government. Restaurants, hotels have been allowed to open with 50% seating capacity from 05.10.2020. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines regarding Shiv Bhojan centers was under the consideration of the government. The following guidelines have been issued in this regard:
1) The seating arrangement in the Shiv Bhojan center should be changed to ensure a distance of at least one meter between two tables.
2) Social distancing of 3 feet should be strictly followed at the Shiv Bhojan center.
3) The staff preparing and distributing Shiv Bhojan should wash their hands frequently with soap.
4) It will be mandatory for the employees of the Shiv Bhojan center to wear a mask.
5) The entire Shiv Bhojan center should be disinfected every day.
6) Ensure that the air in the Shiv Bhojan center is cool.
7) Disinfect all utensils, including plates, bowls, spoons, cups, etc. used in the Shiv Bhojan center.
8) If possible, separate entry and exit routes should be maintained at Shiv Bhojan centres.
9) Provide hand sanitizers and hand sanitizers.
10) Every customer should be checked with a thermal gun. If the body temperature is found to be more than 100 degrees Fahrenheit, then the customer should be provided with the Shiv Bhojan thali in the take-away form by following all the rules of disinfection.
