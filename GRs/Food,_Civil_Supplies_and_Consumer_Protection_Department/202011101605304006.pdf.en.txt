GOVERNMENT ORDER: During the year 2019-20, Mr. M.A. Dongre, Short Writer (Upper Grade), Group B Pay Scale-S-15 (Rs. 41800-132300) to Personal Assistant, Group B, Gazetted- Pay Scale S-16 (Rs. 44900-142400) is being promoted to the post of Controller, Valid Metrology, Headquarters, Mumbai Office as Personal Assistant, Group B subject to conditions prescribed by this order.
Mr. A. Dongre is being promoted to the post of Personal Assistant, Group B in the open category vacancy only on temporary basis subject to the following conditions:
(1) The said promotion shall be a temporary promotion as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Personal Assistant, Group B, and this seniority list has also not been challenged.
3) The promotion is temporary in nature and the post will not be held by the concerned officers permanently.
The selection of eligible Short Writers (Senior Grade) for the year 2019-20 is provisional and promotion to the post of Personal Assistant is provisional. As a result, the selected officer will not be entitled to any regular post and seniority in the service.
5) The promotion is temporary in nature and will be regularized only according to the posts available in the promoted quota.
(6) If the concerned Short Writer (Senior Grade) does not accept the temporary promotion to the post of Personal Assistant, the General Administration Department, Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ Q.No.303/ Ka. 12, d. As on 12.09.2016, their names will be removed from the list for the year 2019-20 and their eligibility will be checked for temporary promotion to the post of Personal Assistant in the third year selection list without considering their names in the list for the next two years.
(7) The temporary promotion of the said officer to the post of Personal Assistant is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated against the said officer till the date of this order.
(8) This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
