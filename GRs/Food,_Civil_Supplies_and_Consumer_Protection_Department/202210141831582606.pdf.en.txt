The Proposal:
The above 1, vide Government Resolution dated 26/6/2003, has approved the revised pattern of posts in the establishment of various Heads of Departments under this Department. These include posts under Controller, Valid Metrology.
Under the above 3 Government Decisions, the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection has decided the revised figure in the Government Decision dated 18th April, 2022, after reviewing the posts in the valid metrology system. Out of 944 posts, 661 posts have been approved and the remaining 283 posts have been declared dead in the category of Drivers/ Drivers (Heavy)/ Crane Drivers/ Sepoys/ Naiks/ Hamals. These positions are suggested to be taken up by external agencies as per requirement.
As per the Government decision dated 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022, 210 temporary posts out of total 940 sanctioned posts at Mumbai and District level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended.
The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.

Government's decision:
A total of 183 temporary posts as shown in the prospectus A attached to this Government decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation, are presently being extended from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 (six months).
