Introduction: As per the above mentioned government decisions, Shiv Bhojan Thali has been made available to the poor and needy people of the state at a discounted rate and the quantity of thali has been increased from time to time with the approval of the cabinet.
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra. During the financial year 2021-22, the Finance Department has allocated Rs. 150.00 crore has been sanctioned, out of which Rs. 14 against an unofficial reference of Rs. 25.00 crore has been made available for disbursement. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grants received from the Finance Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: To provide Shiv Bhojan Thali to the poor and needy people at a discounted price, the offices of the District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller (Ration) have been asked to provide the same.
