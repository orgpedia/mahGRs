Introduction: The process of" Break the Chain" has started across the state. Accordingly, Reference No. 11 mentioned here. As per the Government decision dated 15 th April, 2021, the Government has approved to make Shiv Bhojan Thali available free of cost for one month beyond 15 th April, 2021.
Strict restrictions were imposed in the state under the process of" Break the Chain." In principle, the period of providing Shiv Bhojan Thali free of cost to the beneficiaries has been extended to avoid the poor and needy people from suffering due to lack of food. The government has extended the deadline by another month. In view of the Covid-19 situation in the state, the proposal to extend this period for the next one month was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government decision: from June 15, 2021. Shiv Bhojan Thali should be made available to the beneficiaries free of cost during the period from 14th July, 2021.
