The Proposal:
Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. The administrative approval given by the Collector, Palghar, to the warehouse repair works in Palghar district under the relevant Government letter has been accepted as a special case at the Government level. The matter of disbursing funds for the warehouse repair works for which the administrative approval was approved was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Subject to the conditions set out in the relevant GO regarding administrative approval, it is being approved to provide funds for warehouse repair work in Palghar district as indicated in the following statement.
