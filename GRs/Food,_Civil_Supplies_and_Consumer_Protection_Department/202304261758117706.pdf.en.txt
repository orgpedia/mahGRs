The proposal:
Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Government Decision. In the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by the Government Departments attached as on 01.12.2016, vide case No. 2.9.2 of the Act provides for constitution of State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committees.
In accordance with the said directive, the previous government decided to purchase the necessary equipment from the government grain warehouse in the state through this department. The Departmental Warehousing Purchase Committee was constituted by the Office of NAPU-16A vide Government Resolution 07.10.2021 dated 17.04.2017. The government was considering the formation of a newly reformed departmental store purchase committee in line with the transfer of subjects that took place in the intervening period.
The government has decided to form a divisional store purchase committee to purchase twine for handling foodgrains, thread for sewing bags and tarpaulin and other necessary equipment for foodgrain security in government grain warehouses in the state. Accordingly, the Purchase Committee is being constituted as follows.
