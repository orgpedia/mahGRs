Preamble: Strengthening of Central Consumer Forum for construction of building and other non-construction activities for State Consumer Redressal Commissions and District Consumer Redressal Commissions under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
For this, it is necessary to send a financial proposal to the central government with the approval of the Empowered Committee formed under the scheme. Therefore, as per the guidelines of the scheme, the matter of forming a power supply committee under the scheme of strengthening the consumer forum was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
Under the Central Government's scheme of Strengthening of Consumer Fora, the Empowerment Committee is being constituted as follows:
Secretary, Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection-Chairman (2) Joint Secretary, Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection-Member (3) Secretary (Construction), Public Works Department, Ministry Member (4) Manager (Administration), State Consumer Redressal Commission, Mumbai-Member
The functions of the Empowered Committee shall be as follows: (1) To approve the financial proposals to be submitted to the Central Government.
(2) To ensure that the proposals submitted to the Committee are submitted in consultation with the State Commission concerned.
(3) To ensure that the posts of Chairperson and Members are created and functioning in the District Commission for which financial assistance is proposed.
(4) To approve the proposal for utilization of funds utilised by the Central Government for construction of buildings and for purchase of other construction materials.
