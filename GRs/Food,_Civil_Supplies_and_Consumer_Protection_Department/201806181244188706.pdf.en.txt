3. Construction of Government godowns funded as above should be completed immediately. Once the construction of the godowns starts, the district supply officer should first review the complete details of the construction and the related construction every month and submit the progress of the construction to the government.
4. The District Supply Officer should take care to submit the utilization certificate to the government as soon as the construction of the government warehouse is completed. As soon as the funds for the warehouse are actually spent, the utilization certificate of the total disbursed funds for the relevant work should be submitted to the government immediately.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that the cost of construction of godowns does not increase due to delay in construction or other reasons.
While the construction of the godown is in progress, the concerned Superintending Engineer, Public Works Department should visit at least three times to ensure the quality of work and submit such reports to the Collector in writing from time to time.

7. The Collector shall personally visit the godown before taking over the godown after completion of the construction of the godown and ensure that the work done is of good quality. As soon as the said order is received, the said provisions should be immediately distributed by the District Collector to the concerned Executive Engineer of the Public Works Department on the budget distribution system. Cash should not be withdrawn.
9. Funds made available under this Order. Expenditures as of October 31, 2018. Funds made available under this order cannot be transferred as deposit contribution.
