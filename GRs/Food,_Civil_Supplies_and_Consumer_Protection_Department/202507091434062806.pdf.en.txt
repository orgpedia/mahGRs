The Proposal: -
Department of Finance Government Decision No. Signed 2023 / Q. 190/2023 Treasuries 5, D. As on 14.02.2024, the Drawing and Disbursing Officers and other non-executive authorities have been issued Model No.
It has been approved to implement the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA) system and methodology for utilization of funds drawn under the VF (Object Head) from the Consolidated Fund of the State for 27-small constructions, 31-subsidized grants (non-salaried), 33-financed, 35-subsidized for creation of capital asset, 50-other expenses, 53-large constructions and other purposes as may be declared by the Finance Department from time to time.
The Government was considering the issue of declaring the Financial Adviser under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the Office of the Deputy Secretary as a "Virtual Personal Deposit Accounting Administrator" (VPDA) as per the provisions of this Government Decision 11. 1 to enable the Administrative Departments of the concerned Ministries to declare Regional Drawing and Disbursing Officers and other non-executive authorities as Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrators with the concurrence of the Department of Finance for utilization of the Supplementary Grants Fund to be drawn from the Consolidated Fund of the State.
Government Decision: - The Accounts Officer and Drawing and Disbursing Officer No. 7101005361 of the Office of the Subordinate Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary of this Department is being declared as the "Virtual Personal Deposit Accounting Administrator" (VPDA) for utilization of the funds of Subsidiary Grants to be drawn from the Consolidated Fund of the State under 27-Small Constructions, 31-Subsidized Grants (Non-Pay), 33-Financial, 35-Grants for Construction of Capital Assets, 50-Other Expenditure, 52, Machinery, 53-Small Constructions and such other purposes as may be declared by the Finance Department from time to time.
The Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrator shall be required to act in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decisions in Reference as well as the Government Decisions issued in this behalf from time to time.
This recognition was given by the Accountant General (Lt. and a) is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions as specified by the Office.
1) The said VPDA shall be the subject to audit.
2) The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
3) No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of fund.
