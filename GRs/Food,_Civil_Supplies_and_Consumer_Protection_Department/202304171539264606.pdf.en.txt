GOVERNMENT DECISION All the vacancies in the promotion quota as per the provisions of the General Administration Department, dated 07.05.2021, will be filled up as per the status of 25.05.2004, purely on temporary basis. The decision is being taken based on a High Court order.
Provisional promotion of Inspector, Valid Metrology to the post of Assistant Controller is being made subject to the following conditions:
(1) The promotion shall be of temporary nature as an administrative exigency.
2) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned officer in this post.
3) The promotions to the post of Assistant Controller of Qualified Inspector, Valid Metrology for the year 2022-23 are provisional. As a result, the selected officers will not be entitled to any regular post and seniority in service.
4) These promotions are temporary in nature and will be regularized only as per the posts available in the promoted quota.
5) If the concerned Inspector, Valid Metrology does not accept the temporary promotion to the post of Assistant Controller, Valid Metrology, the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. SRV-2015/ Page 2 of 3
