Government Decision: Govt. As per the Government decision dated 31.01.2014, instructions have been issued for constitution of a Civil Services Board to recommend to the competent authority regarding posting and transfer of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) officers in the Government Service. Accordingly, a Civil Service Board has been constituted under the above Government Order for making recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers working in the Validation System and Consumer Protection System under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
The Department of Legal Metrology and Department of Consumer Protection under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection were functioning on the same establishment before November, 2013. Now that the posts have been adjusted according to the choice of officers/ staff in both the systems, the above 3 have been appointed to recommend to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) officers working in the Valid Metrology System and Consumer Protection System.
