The concerned Inspector shall immediately execute the same as the order of discharge without waiting for any other order at the place of transfer and submit his report to the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and to the Government immediately.
The above Inspector shall assume charge of the new post forthwith, he shall not be unauthorisedly absent otherwise his absence shall be treated as unauthorised absence and shall be made without pay. Also, the possibility of their services being terminated cannot be ruled out. Relatives are being informed.
The period of appointment, travel allowance and other incidental allowances will be admissible to the concerned officers for joining the new post.
5. The Controller, Validation, is informed that the date of demitting office and assuming charge by the concerned Inspector shall be intimated to the Government immediately. If the concerned officer does not report to the place of transfer within the prescribed period, the government should be informed immediately. This will enable swift disciplinary action against the concerned. As per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the transfer applications of the transferred officers should not be accepted/ approved.
