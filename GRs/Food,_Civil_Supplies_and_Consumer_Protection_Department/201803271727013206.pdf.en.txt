Background:- NCDEX Spot Exchange Ltd. through e-auction. A proposal to amend the prescribed terms and conditions for the purchase of sugar through them was under the consideration of the government.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
NCDEX Spot Exchange Ltd. The terms and conditions for the purchase of sugar through these are prescribed by the Government Order dated 19th June, 2014. Paragraphs 2.8 and 8.12 of the said Government Resolution are being amended as follows and paragraph 4.4 is being inserted afresh.
Revised Paragraph 2.8- For the last 2 years, the annual turnover has been Rs. It should not be less than Rs 4 crore.
New Paragraph 4.4- The process of registration of NCDEX Spot Exchange Ltd., which is in accordance with the terms and conditions prevailing at the moment, to have government control over the registration of suppliers. NCDEX Spot Exchange Ltd., the Registering Proposal of the said Suppliers for grant of final approval to the said Suppliers.
It will be sent to the government for final approval.
Paragraph 8.12 shall be amended to read as follows:
Amended Paragraph 8.12 The bids of all bidders participating in the e-auction shall be valid for a maximum period of 15 (fifteen) working days from the date of auction.
