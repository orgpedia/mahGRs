Preamble: The Governing Decision on the revised configuration of the subordinate offices of the Department. 18.4.2022. As per this decision, the officers/ employees working in the office of the Director, Supply Commissioner, Mumbai are proposed to be adjusted in the office of the supply system.
(2) The decision of the Department. As on 26.6.2003, a total of 14 posts were sanctioned in the cadre of Senior Clerk in the office of the Commissioner of Supplies. Of these, 5 were filled and 9 were vacant.
As approved in the meeting of the Promotion Committee held on 20.10.2020, As on 26.6.2003, 7 qualified Clerk-Typists have been promoted to the post of Senior Clerk.
At present, there are a total of 11 vacancies in the post. As per the revised pattern, 5 posts of Senior Clerk in the office of the Commissioner of Supplies have been sanctioned. So, 6 senior clerks are being added. Out of them, 5 senior clerical employees are willing to work in the district supply branch (District Supply Officer Office) in the lower cadre post by protecting the pay scale.
Accordingly, the matter of adjustment of these additional employees to the lower cadre posts in the District Supply Branch was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is pleased to approve the following adjustment in the cadre of Clerk-Typist by preserving the pay scale S-8 (25500-81100) of the existing cadre of 5 additional staff appointed in the cadre of Senior Clerk in the office of the Commissioner of Supplies.
