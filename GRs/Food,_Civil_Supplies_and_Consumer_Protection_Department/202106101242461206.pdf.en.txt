The Proposal:
The National Food Security Act, 2013 has come into force in the State of Maharashtra with effect from 1st February, 2014. Section 16 (1) of the said Act provides for the constitution of a State Food Commission for the purpose of control and review of the implementation of the Act. Accordingly, the State Food Commission has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri Arun Deshpande vide Government Order dated 11.04.2017.
Section 16 (4) of the National Food Security Act, 2013 provides for the term of office of the Chairperson and Members of the State Food Commission as follows:
The Chairman and every other member shall hold office for such period not exceeding five years from the date on which he enters upon his office and shall be eligible for reappointment, however, no person shall hold office as Chairman or other member after he has attained the age of sixty-five years. " Shri Arun Deshpande, Chairman, State Food Commission is 65 years old. It will be completed on 6.6.2021.
