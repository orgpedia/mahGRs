The Proposal:
The high-level committee has unanimously approved the review of posts in the establishment of various department heads under this department. Accordingly, the revised figure has been approved under the Government Decision dated 26/6/2003 at 1 above to declare the revised figure.
In pursuance of the above Government decision, 210 temporary posts out of the total sanctioned 940 posts at Mumbai and District level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 1/3/2019 to 30/9/2019. The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
A total of 210 temporary posts as shown in the prospectus A attached with this Government decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation, are being extended from 1/10/2019 to 29/2/2020 (five months).
The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head Demand No., M-3, Main Account Head 3475, Other General Financial Services (106), Regulation of Weights and Measures (00) (01) Regulation of Weights and Measures, (34750093) and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20.
This Government Resolution, Government Resolution No. Post No.8/ 2016 The PUC is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide order dated 11/9/2019.
