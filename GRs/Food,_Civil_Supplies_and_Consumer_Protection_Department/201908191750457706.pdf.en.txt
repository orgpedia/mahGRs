Introduction: In order to meet the target set for the State of Maharashtra under the National Food Security Act, 2013, eligible beneficiaries have been selected and included in the Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority family beneficiaries. 15.7.2019 to D. Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Antyodaya Abhiyan during 14.8.2019. This has been fixed by the circular dated 24.7.2019. Under the said mission, the target has been set to provide 100 percent ration distribution to eligible and needy families under the National Food Security Scheme, 100 percent food grain distribution and gas connection to every family.
In pursuance of the above objectives, all concerned have been given the reference. The instructions have been issued vide Government Circular dated 24.7.2019. The campaign has been receiving a large number of responses and the government was considering extending the implementation of the campaign to meet the target. Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:
Government Circular: 1. Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Antyodaya Abhiyaan is hereby recognized by the Govt. It is being extended till 25.8.2019. After this, the government will review the target and take further decisions.
2) Implementation of the program. All other instructions issued vide Government Circular dated 24.7.2019 shall remain in force.
