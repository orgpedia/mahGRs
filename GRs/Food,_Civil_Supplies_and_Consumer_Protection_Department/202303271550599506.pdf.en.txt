The Proposal:
The above reference No. 2 As per the Government decision dated 18 th April, 2022, the revised figure of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department has been fixed after reviewing the posts in valid metrology system. A total of 661 posts have been approved for valid metrology system and the remaining 283 posts have been declared dead in the category of driver/ driver (heavy)/ crane driver/ peon/ naik/ hamal.
In pursuance of the above Government decision, 183 temporary posts out of the existing posts at Mumbai and District level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023. The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 180 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A attached to this Government Decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation, are presently being extended from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 (six months).
