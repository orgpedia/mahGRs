As per the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986, State Consumer Redressal Commission at the State level and District Consumer Redressal Forum at the District level have been set up.
Also, in pursuance of the amendments made by the Central Government in the Consumer Protection Act, 2002, the State Commission has been constituted with Parikrama Benches (Circuit Benches) at Nagpur and Aurangabad respectively. With the increase in the functioning of the State Commission, the District Forum, a total of 497 temporary posts of officers/ staff have been created for the offices of the State Commission and the Nagpur and Aurangabad Parikrama Benches of the Commission as well as the District Forum from time to time.
