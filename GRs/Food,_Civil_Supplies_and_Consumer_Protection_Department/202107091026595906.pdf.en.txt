The Proposal:
As per the above-mentioned government decisions, Shiv Bhojan Thali has been made available to the poor and needy people of the state at a discounted price and the number of thali has been increased from time to time with the approval of the cabinet.
As per the request made by the District Supply Officer, the matter of reallocation of funds made available by the Finance Department earlier was under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
In order to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali at subsidized rates to the poor and needy persons, the offices of District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller (Rationing) have been allocated Rs. A grant of Rs. 65.00 crore was disbursed. The re-allocation of this grant is now being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. Subject to all the terms and conditions of the Government Order and Circulars referred to above, the amount mentioned in Annexure-A is Rs. 65,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Crore only) is being reallocated.
(2) The grant shall be used for the purpose for which it is granted. This amount cannot be used for any other purpose.
(3) The accounts of the expenditure to be made out of the aforesaid grants shall be kept in the books of accounts in force and the consolidated utilization certificate of the payments made in that month after the payment is made from the treasury by your office in this regard shall be submitted to the Accountant General's Office and the Government by the 15th of the following month after the end of the month.
4. The Collector (District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer) at the district level is being authorised to draw the amount as mentioned in Letter-A from the Treasury and the Controller Rationing and Director Civil Supplies is being authorised to draw the amount from the office of the Subordinate and Accounts Officer, Mumbai for the Mumbai/ Thane Rationing Area.
The above grant shall be provided to the institution concerned every 15 days through online RTGS mode after considering the information of Shiv Bhojan App as directed by the Government earlier.
