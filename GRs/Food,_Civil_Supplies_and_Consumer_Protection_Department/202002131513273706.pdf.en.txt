The Proposal:
For the storage of foodgrains under PDS, 2 gunny bags at Kandhar, 1 gunny bag and 1 kurla (each on 1800th May) in Nanded district. The proposal for the construction of the godowns has been received by the Government from the District Supply Officer, Nanded and the Public Works Department has agreed to give administrative approval for the budget and construction of these godowns.
Therefore, the matter of administrative approval for the construction of government godowns under the project taken up under the Rural Infrastructure Development Fund of NABARD was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of godowns at 2, Barul 1 and Kurula 1 at Kandhar in Nanded district subject to the following conditions.
The detailed budget for the year 2017-18 has been prepared by the Assistant (Class 1), Public Works Sub-Division, Kandhar and certified by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Nanded, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nanded, Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and District Supply Officer, Nanded on behalf of the Consumer Department.
