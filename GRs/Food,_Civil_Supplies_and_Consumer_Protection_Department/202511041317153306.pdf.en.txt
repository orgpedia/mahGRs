Proposal
The implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through SNA-SPARSH for effective fund distribution of Centrally Sponsored Schemes has been communicated by the Central Government vide letter No.1 of the context. As per the Government Decision No. 2, the National Food Security Scheme of this Department (€58-4048 - Assistance to State Agencies for Intra State Movement Handling of Food Grains and FPS Dealers Margin under NFSA) & (SLS-MH 5075-MH - Revision of norms for Central Assistance Released to States / UTs for meeting expenditure on Intra-State Movement Handling of Food Grains and FPS Dealers Margin under NFSA) has been incorporated for implementation through SNA-SPARSH. Accordingly, the National Food Security Scheme is being implemented through the SNA-SPARSH system since February 02, 2005.
Under the National Food Security Scheme, the funds received from the Central Government in the year 2025-26 under Reference No. 2 and 4 are general, scheduled castes and scheduled tribes category-wise. For the National Food Security Scheme, the government is considering taking 3 separate heads of accounts and balance sheets of expenditure - Central share, State share and additional State share for Scheduled Tribes. Accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: - A new head of account is being opened for the incidental expenditure for the Scheduled Tribes category under the National Food Security Scheme as follows.
