Implementation of National Food Security Act, 2013 in Maharashtra. It has been started from 01/02/2014. The State Food Commission has been constituted under Section 16 of the National Food Security Act, 2013. The matter of declaring an office head for the office of the State Food Commission was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
The post of Secretary (Group-A) has been created for the office of the State Food Commission vide Government Resolution No. Asthapra-1017/ PR No. 179/ NAPU-15 dated 27th June, 2017. Accordingly, the Secretary is being declared as the head of the office of the State Food Commission.
