Government Order: As per the Government notification dated 26th April, 2018, Shri Mahesh Dhawale and Smt. Preeti Baithule have been appointed as Members, State Food Commission. Also, the. Shri Mahesh Dhawale has been given the additional charge of the post till his regular appointment as Chairman, State Food Commission vide Government Order dated 29.10.2021.
As per the provisions of Section 16 (4) of the National Food Security Act, 2013, Shri Mahesh Dhawale and Smt. The term of office of Shri Mahesh Dhawale and Smt. Preeti Baithule as Members, State Food Commission is hereby extended up to 25th April, 2023. Extend the deadline. The tenure of appointment is for a period of six months from April 26, 2023, or regular appointment to the said post, whichever is earlier. During the period, Shri Mahesh Dhawale will continue to hold the additional charge of the post of Chairman, State Food Commission.
The order will come into effect immediately.
