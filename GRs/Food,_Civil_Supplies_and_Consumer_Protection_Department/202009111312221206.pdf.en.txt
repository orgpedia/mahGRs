Preamble: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 26/6/2003, the revised pattern of posts on establishment of various Heads of Departments under this Department has been approved. These include posts under Controller, Valid Metrology.
In pursuance of the above decision, 210 temporary posts out of the total sanctioned 940 posts at Mumbai and District level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 29/02/2020 to 31/08/2020.
The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
The government's decision:
A total of 210 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A attached with this Government decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation Sciences are being extended from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021 (six months).
The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head" Demand No., M-3, Main Account Head 3475, Other General Financial Services (106), Regulation of Weights and Measures (00) (01) Regulation of Weights and Measures, (34750093)" and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21.

Issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide order dated 31/08/2020.
