Government's decision:
District Consumer Grievance Redressal Forum has been set up in the State to redress the grievances of consumers under the provisions of Consumer Protection Act, 1986. In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of section 10 and sub-section (2) of section 30 of the said Act, the following candidates are being appointed to the vacant post of Chairperson of the District Consumer Redressal Forum mentioned against their names:

Terms and Conditions:
(1) The term of office of the President shall be for a period of one year from the date on which he enters upon his office. If the Chairman of the State Commission is of the opinion that the performance of the candidate is not satisfactory, the period of probation will be extended.
2) The appointment of the concerned candidates is being made subject to medical examination and police verification report. However, the appointment of candidates whose police verification/ medical examination reports are adverse/ contain any adverse remarks will be cancelled.
(3) The Chairperson of the District Forum shall be appointed for a term of five years or till he attains the age of 65 years, whichever is earlier.
(4) They shall not be re-appointed or re-appointed.
(5) The remuneration of the members of the District Consumer Redressal Forum shall be admissible in accordance with the provisions of clause (1) of rule 3 of the Maharashtra Consumer Protection Rules, 2000, as amended from time to time.
(6) All other terms and conditions of service of the Chairperson of the District Consumer Forum shall be governed by the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 and Rules, 2000 as amended by the Government from time to time.
(7) The President of the District Forum shall be entitled to receive the daily and travel allowance admissible to first class officers of the State Government while on tour for the purpose of the District Forum.
However, they will not be allowed to travel by air or first class train.
The Chairperson of the concerned District Forum may be removed from the post as per the provision of Rule 3 (4) of the Maharashtra Consumer Protection Rules, 2000.
(9) If the Government considers it necessary, the services of the President of the concerned District Forum shall be transferred from one District Forum to another in public interest or for administrative reasons, or the Government shall utilise their services by linking the two District Forums as per the recommendation of the State Commission, taking into account the workload or pending cases.
If the concerned does not take charge within 15 days from the date of issue of the order of appointment, his/ her appointment will be automatically cancelled.
11) These appointments are being made after reviewing the work business.
