Circular:
In the context of the Department. Under the National Food Security Act, 2013, as per the decision taken by the Government on 03.10.2023, the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana and Priority Family Ration Card holders in the targeted public distribution system of the State and above poverty line (APL) Keshari Kisan Ration Card holders in all the districts of Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and Amravati Division and 14 farmer suicide-affected districts of Wardha Division in Nagpur Division will be provided with a ration kit" Anandacho Ration" consisting of 6 ration items namely Rava, Chana Dal, Maida and Poha in the quantity of 1 kg sugar, 1 litre edible oil and half kg each for the upcoming Diwali festival in 2023. through October 25, 2023. It has been decided to disburse Rs.100/- per person through e-PoS system at concessional rates till 30th November, 2023.
Accordingly, the following instructions are being issued regarding distribution of ration kits to eligible ration card holders:
Under the Public Distribution System of the State, the ration kits containing 1 kg sugar, 1 litre edible oil and 6 ration kits each in the quantity of half kg semolina, chana dal, maida and poha should be distributed at the rate of₹ 100/- per set through e-PoS system to the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana, priority families as well as APL (Keshari) farmer ration cards of 14 farmer suicide-affected districts of Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and Amravati division and Wardha division of Nagpur division.

These include Just Kitchen Pvt Ltd and Indo Allied Protein Foods Pvt Ltd. The ration kits should be delivered to the respective taluk godowns within the stipulated time.
3. It is necessary to keep a record of the ration coming to the warehouse and the ration being sent to the fair price shop in online mode. Accordingly, care should be taken to take proper records on the AePDS portal.
4. The District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller concerned shall be responsible for ensuring proper unloading of ration received from the suppliers in the Taluk godowns. The department will bear the expenses for the transportation of the foodgrains. Accordingly, these payments should be submitted to the Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary.
5. Ration kits should be accepted by the supplier only after ensuring that the expiry date of the ration included in the ration kit is at least 4 months for sugar, chana dal, poha, edible oil and at least 3 months for maida, rawa.
6. Ration kits should be accepted only after obtaining NABL accredited laboratory certificate from the supplier that the Ration kit meets FSSAI standards. It should be ensured that poor quality ration/ ration kits are not accepted under any circumstances.
7. On receipt of ration kits from the supplier in the Taluka warehouse, the District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller shall randomly collect samples of ration kits in front of the supplier's representative by appointing an officer not below the rank of Tehsildar or himself. The samples will be sent to NABL laboratory for testing. If the sample is found to be not meeting the prescribed standards, the supplier should be informed immediately and action should be taken to replace the concerned ration kit.
The ration kits are to be distributed at the rate of₹ 100/- per set. The fair price shop owners should deduct the margin and deposit the remaining amount with the District Supply Officer// Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller within 7 days after the distribution of ration kits. Margin will be paid to fair price shop owners for selling these ration kits as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 4th September, 2017. Also taking into account the period of delivery of the ration kit, the. 25.10.2023 to D. Fair price shop owners should be informed about the distribution of full ration kits within 30.11.2023.
