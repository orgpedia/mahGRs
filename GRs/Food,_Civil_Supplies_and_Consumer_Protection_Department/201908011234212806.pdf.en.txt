Government Circular: The final seniority list showing the status as on 01/01/2018 of the District Supply Officers/ Food Grain Distribution Officers Group A in the supply cadre in the District and Divisional Offices under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection has been published vide Circular dated 10.12.2018.
Now, the officers of the cadre of District Supply Officer/ Food Grain Distribution Officer Group-A in the supply cadre in the district and divisional offices under the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department will be transferred. The Provisional Seniority List showing the position as on 01/01/2019 is being published in the Annexure.
(3) In case of objections of the concerned officers regarding the said seniority list, the concerned officers shall submit the same to the Government through the Head of the Department (along with their comments by the Head of the Department) along with appropriate evidence/ certificate in support of their objections within 15 days from the date of this circular. Objections/ Suggestions received after the above deadline will not be considered.
4. All the Divisional Commissioners are requested to obtain a copy of the said provisional seniority list from the website. Inform all the concerned officers of your department to make a copy of the seniority list available on the website.
