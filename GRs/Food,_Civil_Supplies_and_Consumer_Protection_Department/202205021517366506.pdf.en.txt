Subject: Review of Assistant District Supply Officer/ Assistant Food Distribution Officer (Group-B) cadre of 50-55 years.
Reference-1) Government Resolution, General Administration Department No. LPL-2017/ P. No.21/ K. 10.06.2019 2) Government Order Food, Civil Supplies, Consumer Protection Department No.Ashtar No.-2619/ PR No. 319/ No. P. 15, p. January 7th, 2020
As per the provisions of Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the procedure has been prescribed under the Government Resolution No. 1 to exercise the eligibility of Government officers/ employees to continue their Government service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying Government service.
2. Reference No. Under the order, the Departmental Review Committee has been constituted to review the Group-A and Group-B cadre officers under the administrative control of the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Affairs Department.
Accordingly, the proposal for review of services of 02 officers of the cadre of Assistant District Supply Officer/ Assistant Food Distribution Officer (Gazetted, Group-B) in the supply system was placed before the Divisional Review Committee. The eligibility criteria for continuation of their Government service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying Government service was examined. In accordance with the recommendation of the Divisional Review Committee meeting, the Government is approving the continuation of the services of the following officers in the cadre of Assistant District Supply Officer/ Assistant Food Distribution Officer (Gazetted, Group-B) in the supply chain, beyond the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
