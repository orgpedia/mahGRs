This scheme is being implemented in Gadchiroli district through the District Collector, Gadchiroli.
Accordingly, the District Supply Officer, Controller and Assistant District Supply Officer have been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers to meet the expenditure on the scheme from the funds available under the said head of accounts. They should not spend more than the sanctioned grant under the said head of accounts and should submit the report and utility certificate after reconciling the expenditure to the Financial Advisor and Deputy Secretary, Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department.
