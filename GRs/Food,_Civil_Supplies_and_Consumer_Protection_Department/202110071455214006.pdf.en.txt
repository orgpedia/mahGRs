Introduction: Department of Energy and Labor. Paragraph 2.9.2 of the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2016 directs that every administrative department shall constitute a Divisional Warehousing Procurement Committee for procurement of goods under the division.
Accordingly, in order to stop the prevailing practice of stitching the sacks of food grains in the government godowns in the state with the help of standardized twine and instead to seal the sacks by sewing machine using color marked thread, the above mentioned Reference No. 2 here. The Departmental Warehousing Purchase Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 17.04.2017. The government was considering to set up a new departmental store purchase committee to purchase essential commodities in government granaries by reforming the working of the committee. According to reference no. 02 here. The following revised orders are being issued by superseding the Government Order dated 17.04.2017.
The government has decided to form a divisional store purchase committee to purchase twine for handling food grains, yarn for sewing bags and tarpaulin and other necessary tools for food security in government grain warehouses in the state. Accordingly, the following committees have been formed:
