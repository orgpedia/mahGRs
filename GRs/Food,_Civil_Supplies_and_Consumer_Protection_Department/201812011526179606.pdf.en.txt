Various programs are organized by the NGO to create awareness among the consumers in the state. With a view to propagate and spread consumer movement among the people, the Government provides grants to NGOs for putting up posters, organizing seminars/ workshops, street plays, Bharudas, folk dances, and conducting padayatras, cycle rallies etc.
In order to provide financial assistance to eligible institutions, the Government has constituted a Consumer Welfare Fund and the rules have been published in the Gazette dated 20.11.1992. Under sub-rule 8 of the Fund Rules, there is a provision to constitute a" Fund Management Committee" to manage the Fund. Accordingly, the term of office of two representatives of Voluntary Consumer Organizations appointed vide Government Resolution dated 12th April, 2016 at Reference No. 2 has expired. Therefore, the proposal to appoint two new non-official members to the Fund Management Committee instead of those two non-official members was under the consideration of the government.
As per the provisions of Rule 8 of the Maharashtra Consumer Protection and Guidance Fund Rules, 1992, the following 2 non-official members are being appointed to the Fund Management Committee.

As the provisions of the said Rules are applicable only for rail/ road travel, the non-official members appointed on the Committee shall not be allowed to travel by air as well as by rail air-conditioned first class.
