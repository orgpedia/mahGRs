(2) The re-appointment of the said Chairperson shall take effect on the date on which he submits to the State Commission the prescribed form of affidavit and undertaking and takes charge of his office. The re-appointment of the said Chairman shall be subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The re-appointment of the concerned candidate will be subject to medical examination and police verification report, whose police verification/ medical examination report will be adverse/ if any adverse remarks are mentioned in it, the appointment will be cancelled.
(2) The Chairperson of the District Forum shall be re-appointed for a period of five years or till he attains the age of 65 years, whichever is earlier.
(3) The Chairperson of the District Consumer Redressal Forum to be appointed by nomination from the Advocate Cadre shall be appointed by the Government vide Circular No. GRASSANK- 2209/348/ PR No. 4375/ GRASS.3, Dt. Salary/ honorarium as fixed as on 03.11.2010 will be admissible.
(4) The Chairman of the District Forum shall be entitled to receive the daily and travel allowance admissible to first class officers of the State Government while on tour for the purpose of the District Forum. However, they will not be allowed to travel by air or first class train.
(5) All other terms and conditions of service of the Chairperson of the District Consumer Forum shall be governed by the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 and Rules, 2000 as amended by the Government from time to time.
(6) In the public interest or for administrative reasons, the services of the Chairman of the District Consumer Forum may be transferred from one District Forum to another, or his services may be utilised by linking two District Forums as may be decided by the State Commission, considering the workload or pending cases.
7) If the concerned does not take charge within 15 days from the date of issue of the order of re-appointment, his appointment will be automatically cancelled.
