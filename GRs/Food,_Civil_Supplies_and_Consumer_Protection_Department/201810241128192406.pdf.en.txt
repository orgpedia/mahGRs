Read: Letter from Managing Director, Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune No: Masavim/ Vikaso/ Rastabhav/ 208/18, Dy. 17.9.2018
Preamble: The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation Limited, Pune has requested to allow the distribution of the produce produced under the brand" MahaFarm" of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune for sale in all authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to allow the products to be put up for sale under the brand" MahaFarm" from all the authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state.
Government's decision:
In order to increase the income of fair price shopkeepers in the state, products produced under the brand" MahaFarm" of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune are being allowed to be put up for sale in all authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state.
The products produced under the brand" MahaFarm" of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune will be distributed to fair price shops in the state through the company's distributors.
According to the scheme of the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, for the commission received on the sale of" MahaFarm" products, the concerned fair price shop owners will have to contact the distributors of the respective schemes and the office of the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation. The transaction will be between Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation Limited, Pune and the concerned fair price shop. There will be no interference or involvement of the government.
