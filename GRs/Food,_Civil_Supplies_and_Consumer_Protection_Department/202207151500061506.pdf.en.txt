Introduction: The context. In pursuance of the decision dated 08.04.2022 of the Government to use Dunedge Pallets for storage of foodgrains in Government granaries in Raigad and Nashik districts under Public Distribution System on pilot basis, a total of 21750 units for 27 granaries in Raigad district and 15600 units for 17 granaries in Nashik district were approved to be procured through competitive online bidding process on Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal of the Central Government through BOQ mode. Necessary technicalities were mentioned in the Request for Proposal (RFP).
Government Decision: In the meeting held on 30.05.2022, approval was accorded for procurement of dunnage pellets on pilot basis for storage of foodgrains in Government food grain godowns under Public Distribution System from Deluxe Recycling (India) Pvt. Ltd., a tenderer who is technically qualified and L-1 bidder for procurement from GeM portal and at a cost of Rs. 6,19,87, 500/- per piece of Rs.2850/- for 3 years warranty period.
Reference letter no. Under 3rd and 4th, the District Supply Officers, Raigad and Nashik have informed that they have accepted the dunnage pallets purchased from the GeM portal. Also, warehouse wise invoices for supply of Dungeons Pallets to warehouses in Raigad and Nashik districts by Deluxe Recycling India Pvt Ltd have been received from GeM portal.
