In order to increase the income of all the authorized fair price shop owners in the state, the milk and milk products of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited, Mumbai (Mahananda) have been allowed to be sold in all the authorized fair price shops in the state as per the government decision dated January 19, 2018 and March 9, 2018 and the distribution of milk and milk products produced under the government milk scheme in the state of Maharashtra and Aarey brand milk and milk products has been allowed to be sold from the ration shops in the state. Further, Noga products have been allowed to be put up for sale in all authorized fair price shops/ ration shops in the State vide Government Order dated 27.04.2018.
On the same lines, Kolhapur District Khadi Gramodyog Sangh, Kolhapur has requested to allow all the items produced by Kolhapur District Khadi Gramodyog Sangh, Kolhapur to be put up for sale in all the authorized fair price/ ration shops in the district. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to allow all the products produced by the Kolhapur District Khadi Gramodyog Sangh, Kolhapur, from all the authorized fair price/ ration shops in the district to be put up for sale in all the authorized fair price/ ration shops in the district.
Government Decision: In order to increase the income of fair price shop owners in the state, at present, eco-friendly mosquito repellent mixtures, eco-friendly sanitary napkins and other items available from Khadi and Village Industries produced by District Khadi Village Industries Union, Kolhapur are being allowed to be put up for sale in all authorized fair price shops/ ration shops in Kolhapur district.
2.The distribution will be done through the authorized distributors of Kolhapur District Khadi Gramodyog Sangh, Kolhapur to fair price shops in Kolhapur district.
