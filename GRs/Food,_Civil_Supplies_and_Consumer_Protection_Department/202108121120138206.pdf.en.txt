The Proposal:
The Director, Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation Limited, Nagpur has requested to distribute the produce produced under the brand of National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (NAFED) to all the authorized fair price shops/ ration shops in the state.
Government's decision:
In order to increase the income of fair price shop owners in the state, tea products produced under the brand of National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (NAFED) are being allowed to be put up for sale in all authorized fair price shops/ ration shops in the state.
The brand of tea of National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (NAFED) will be distributed through its distributors to fair price/ ration shops in the state. Further, for the commission received on the sale of tea produced by them, the fair price/ ration shop owners will have to interact with the distributors of the scheme and the office of National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (NAFED). The transaction will be between the National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Limited (NAFED) and the fair price/ ration shop owners. The government will not interfere in it.
