Government's decision
2. Neral Government Grain Warehouse in Raigad District, Ta. Administrative approval is being granted for the repair work and budget of the Karjat Government Warehouse subject to the following conditions.
1) The detailed budget based on the schedule for the year 2017-18 will be submitted to the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Sub-Division No. 1 Karjat and witnessed by Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Panvel and District Supply Officer, Raigad-Alibag on behalf of Consumer Department.
(2) Care should be taken that the repair of the warehouse does not incur additional costs due to delay in repair or other reasons.
3) The District Supply Officer Raigad should first ensure the need for the repair work in the budget and only then take up the work.
4) Work should not be started without the approval of the warehouse map, concept and site plan by the competent authorities.

4. Repair works should be taken up as soon as funds are available for repair of Government godowns which have received administrative approval as above. After receiving the funds, the repair work of government godowns should be started immediately and completed. After the repair of the godown is started, the district supply officer, Raigad, should first review the complete details of the repair and related repairs every month and submit the progress of the repair to the government.
5. Repair of the said godowns should be done by the Public Works Department. Necessary funds will be provided to the Public Works Department for this.
6. The District Supply Officer should take care to submit the utility certificate to the government as soon as the repair work of the government godown is completed.
7. In the administrative sanction order, the brief nature of the work is mentioned and in fact, the repair work of the godown should be completed as per the budget of the repair work submitted to the government. That is, the repair work of the warehouse should be completed according to the budget presented to the government.
8. District Supply Officers shall personally scrutinize the requirement of warehouse repair work as mentioned in the said order to avoid unnecessary re-tuning of warehouse repair work.
The government should be informed about the repetition or discrepancy in the warehouse repair work. This should be continued till the next government order.
