The Proposal:
Shri A.P. Bhangale, Chairman, State Consumer Grievances Redressal Commission. Age Limit is ending on 18/09/2020. As per the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, the process is underway for regular appointment to the post of Chairman, State Consumer Redressal Commission. As the possibility of judicial and administrative work being disturbed by keeping the post of Chairman, State Commission vacant during the period of the appointment cannot be ruled out, the matter of appointment of the senior most member (in the order of appointment), who was working till the regular appointment to the post of Chairman, State Commission, as interim Chairman, State Consumer Grievances Redressal Commission, Mumbai, was under consideration of the Government.
Government order:
Shri D.R. Shirasao, Judicial Member, State Consumer Grievances Redressal Commission, Mumbai is being appointed as Pro-tem Chairman, State Consumer Grievances Redressal Commission, Mumbai. The acting chairman and other members will not hear and give a decision on the disputed matter.
They can unilaterally dispose of the cases and give further dates for hearing.
However, in ex-parte cases, the opinion of such pro-tem Speaker and member shall necessarily be unanimous.
(2) The appointment of the pro-tem Chairman shall automatically stand terminated on the appointment of the regular Chairman, State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, Mumbai.
