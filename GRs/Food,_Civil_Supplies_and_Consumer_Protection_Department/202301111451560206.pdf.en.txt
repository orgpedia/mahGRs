The Consumer Protection (Direct Selling) Rules, 2021.
the prelude
Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution. The Consumer Protection (Direct Selling) Rules, 2021 have been notified vide Notification dated 28th December, 2021. Rule 11 provides for the State Government to establish a mechanism for monitoring or supervising the activities of direct selling companies and direct sellers to ensure that they comply with these rules.
Government's decision
As per the decision of the Government, a committee is being constituted to monitor/ supervise the direct selling companies and direct sellers in Maharashtra as follows:

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
To ensure that the direct selling companies and direct sellers comply with the provisions of the Consumer Protection (Direct Selling) Rules, 2021.
Supervise/ supervise the operations of the direct selling company and the direct seller.
The Committee shall meet after every three months or with the consent of the President.
