Reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 18.04.2022, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the subordinate offices of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection. According to the reference no. 2 here. The cadre wise number of posts have been sanctioned at State/ Divisional/ District/ Taluk level for the Divisional Supply Office in the Regional Supply System under the Department as on 04.07.2022. Among them, the post of Assistant Food Distribution Officer (Establishment) was given the charge of the post of Drawing and Distribution Officer. However, as per the revised pattern, only one post of Assistant Food Distribution Officer has been sanctioned to this office. However, the post needs to be declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
Reference No. 3 As requested by the Food Distribution Office, Pune, the matter of declaring Assistant Food Distribution Officer, Pune as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration at the Government level.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the Office of Food Distribution Officer, Pune," Assistant Food Distribution Officer" is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
