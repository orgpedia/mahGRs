Government circular
The Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals has issued instructions after holding frequent meetings that government/ semi-government ration shops, warehouses are also required to obtain license/ registration as per Section 31 of the Food Safety and Standards Act 2006.
Under this section, all fair price shops in the state are required to register under Section 31 of the Food Safety and Standards Act 2006, failing which action will be taken under this Act. Therefore, all fair price shop owners in the state should register with the Food and Drug Administration under Section 31 of the Food Safety and Standards Act 2006. All District Supply Officers/ Food Distribution Officers and Controller, Rationing, should organize online registration camps on www. fssai. gov. in and ensure that all shopkeepers are registered.
