The Proposal:
Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Affairs Department As per 16/11/2013, the cadre of Legal Metrology Department and Consumer Protection System have been separated. As per clause (6) of this Government Order, there shall be vacancies in the concerned departments/ agencies as the case may be. The officers/ employees should be included in this category. The candidate must be available to fill the said position in the system in which he is working at the time of such adjustment. Mrs. Jyoti Patil, Manager and Mr. Vilas Pawar, Manager have requested for adjustment in the post of Assistant Controller in the valid Metrology System from the Consumer Protection System.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the above Government Decision, The following officers working in the post of Manager in the Consumer Protection System are being adjusted in the post of Assistant Controller, Valid Metrology with the approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of 16/11/2013.
