Introduction: Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to the ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. The matter of providing funds for the repair works of the godown, which was approved by the administration, was under the consideration of the government.

2.The repair work of the government godowns provided with funds as above should be completed immediately.
3. Utilization Certificate on completion of the repair work of the said Government Warehouse
The district supply officer should take care of submitting it to the government.
After the repair work of the godown is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Supply Officer should jointly make a site inspection report to ensure that the work is done according to the approved budget and it should be preserved in the records.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that there is no cost overrun in repair of godowns due to delay in repair or other reasons. The said funds cannot be transferred/ retained as a deposit contribution.
