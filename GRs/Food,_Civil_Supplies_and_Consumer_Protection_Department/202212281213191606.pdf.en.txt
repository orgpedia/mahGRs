The order:
A case has been registered against Mr. Zarekar, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Pachora Division, G. Jalgaon on 22.11.2022 under section 7 of Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 at Police Station Pahur.
A criminal case is also pending against him.
Zarekar, Inspector, Validation, Pachora Division, G. Jalgaon under the provisions of sub-rule (1) (a) of rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, is hereby placed under suspension with effect from the date of this order and shall remain suspended till further orders.
It is further ordered that during the period of operation of this order, Mr. V. S. Zarekar, Headquarter of the Inspector, Joint Controller, Valid Metrology, Nashik Divisional Office, Nashik shall remain in place. Mr. Zarekar, Inspector, Valid Metrology cannot leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Controller, Valid Metrology.
Zarekar, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Pachora Division, G. Jalgaon will not be able to accept private jobs. They cannot engage in business or trade.
Zaker, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Pachora Division, Jalgaon, shall be paid subsistence allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the provisions of Rule 68 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Provisions during the period of assumption of office, non-commissioned service, suspension, dismissal from service) Rules, 1981, till the time of suspension orders. Private employment by Mr. V.S.O. Zarekar Inspector while under suspension
If accepted or trapped in trade or industry, they will be deemed to have committed misconduct and will be ineligible for subsistence allowance.
