(2) The appointment of the said Chairperson shall take effect on the date on which he submits to the State Commission, in the prescribed form, his affidavits and affidavits and on his assuming charge of his office. The Government hereby approve the appointment of the said Chairperson subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The appointment of the concerned will be subject to the production of a health certificate signed by the District Surgeon or the District Medical Officer.
(2) The State Consumer Redressal Commission, Mumbai shall take action as per rules regarding the verification of antecedents of the concerned.
(3) If the medical report and the antecedent verification report are found to be adverse, the appointment of the candidates will be cancelled.
(4) The Chairperson of the District Forum shall hold office for a period of five years from the date of his appointment or till he attains the age of 65 years, whichever is earlier.
(5) The Chairperson of the District Forum shall be entitled to receive the daily and travel allowance admissible to first class officers of the State Government while on tour for the purpose of the District Forum. However, they will not be allowed to travel by air or first class train.
The other terms and conditions of service of the Chairperson of the District Consumer Forum shall be governed by the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 and the Consumer Protection (Appointment, Remuneration, Allowances and Conditions of Service of Chairperson and Members of State Commission and District Forum) Rules, 2019.
(7) In public interest or for administrative reasons, the services of the Chairman of the District Consumer Forum shall be liable to be transferred from one District Forum to another District Forum or his services may be utilised by linking two District Forums as may be decided by the State Commission, considering the workload or pending cases.
8) If the concerned does not take charge within 15 days from the date of issue of the order of appointment, his appointment will be automatically cancelled.
