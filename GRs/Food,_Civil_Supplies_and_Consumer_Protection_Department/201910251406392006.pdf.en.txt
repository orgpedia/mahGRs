Government Order:
Shri S.S. Kakade, Deputy Controller of Validation System, Headquarters, Mumbai, is being transferred to the post of Deputy Controller, Validation System, Mumbai Metropolitan Region, Mumbai, on his request, with the approval of the competent authority for transfer as per the provisions of Section 4 (3), 4 (4) (2) and 5 of the Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The concerned Deputy Controller should immediately join the place of transfer without waiting for any other order, considering the same as a discharge order, and submit his report to the Controller, Legal Metrology, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Government immediately.
3. The above Deputy Controller shall take charge of the new post forthwith and shall not be unauthorisedly absent otherwise his absence shall be treated as unauthorised absence and shall be made without pay. Also, the possibility of their service being interrupted cannot be ruled out.
Relatives are being informed.
(4) The period of appointment, travel allowance and other incidental allowances shall not be admissible to the officers concerned for joining the post of new posting.
5. The Controller, Validation is informed that the date of assumption of charge by the concerned officer should be intimated to the Government immediately. If the concerned officer does not join the place of transfer within the prescribed period, the government should be informed immediately. This will enable swift disciplinary action against the concerned. As per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services Leave) Rules, 1981, applications for leave of transferred officers should not be accepted/ approved.
