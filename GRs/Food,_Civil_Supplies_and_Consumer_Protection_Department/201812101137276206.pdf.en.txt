On allowing existing SKO dealers to apply for new petrol pumps.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Circular No: PETROL-2018/ PR No.418/ NO. 27, Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
The date: December 10, 2018.
Circular: Due to the increase in the number of gas connection ration cards in the state, the demand for kerosene in the state is decreasing. Therefore, various kerosene licensee organizations make demands to the state government regarding rehabilitation of kerosene licensees and payment of honorarium to licensees. Keeping this in view, the Government has approved the distribution of free-sale kerosene and small cylinders of 5 kg to kerosene licensees and fair price shop owners vide Government Order dated 7th September, 2017. The government is also following up with oil companies from time to time to provide alternative employment to kerosene licensees. Accordingly, it has been decided to give an opportunity to the existing SKO dealers in the state to apply for a new petrol pump.
An advertisement has been published on the website www.petrolpumpdealerchayan.in on 25.11.2018 for sanctioning new petrol pumps in urban and rural areas in the state through the three centrally sponsored oil companies. The last date to apply online is 24.12.2018. (The detailed brochure regarding the mode of application, eligibility of the applicant, application fee, terms and conditions, selection process, etc. is attached herewith. The website is also available in Hindi and English. In this case, individuals and organizations as well as existing SKO dealers of the state can apply online for getting a new petrol pump. Addresses and telephone numbers of the regional offices of the three oil companies have also been listed. (Annexure-B) Interested wholesale kerosene licensees may contact the Regional Offices of Oil Companies for further details.
