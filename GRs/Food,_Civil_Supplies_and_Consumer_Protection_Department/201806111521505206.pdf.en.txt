Under the Minimum Support Price (MSP) procurement scheme, the amount of transportation cost of paddy/ CMR rice is available to the State Government through Food Corporation of India. Under the above scheme, the rates for transportation of paddy procured in the season 2012-13 to 2015-16 have been decided by the Committee headed by the concerned Collector to pay the difference between the transportation rates fixed by inviting tenders for transportation of paddy/ rice at the local level and the transportation rates announced by the Food Corporation of India vide Government Decision No. The difference is Rs. A sum of Rs. 26.64 crore has already been sanctioned by the Finance Department.
It was under consideration of the government to pay this amount to the transporters. In this regard, the following decisions are being taken: Government Decisions: Rates for transportation of paddy/ CMR rice procured during the season 2012-13 to 2015-16 to compensate the difference between the rates fixed by the Committee headed by the concerned Collector by inviting tenders for transportation of paddy/ rice at the local level and the rates announced by the Food Corporation of India. The expenditure of Rs. 26.68 crore is to be met from the personal accounts of the Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary. For this, the expenditure should be spent from the available provision in the budget for 2018-19, and if necessary, the proposal of supplementary demand should be submitted to the Finance Department by the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary.
The said Government Resolution is being issued under the Informal Reference 257/2017 of the Department of Finance with the concurrence obtained by digital signature.
