(3) After the said order is issued, the transferred officer/ employee shall ensure that he joins the place of transfer within the period of his joining as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, the period of absence will be considered as non-working day. Therefore, the above Inspector/ Assistant Controller should take charge of the new post immediately. The concerned are being made aware that they should not be absent unofficially otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorised absence and will be made unpaid and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out.
The above Inspector/ Assistant Controller, who is transferred on request, will not be entitled to the period of appointment, travelling allowance and other incidental allowances for joining the post of the new post.
5. The Controller, Validation, is informed that the date of demitting office and assumption of charge by the concerned Inspector shall be intimated to the Government forthwith. If the concerned officer does not join the place of transfer within the prescribed time, it should be informed to the government immediately, it will be possible to take disciplinary action against the concerned soon. As per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the transfer applications of the transferred officers should not be accepted or approved.
