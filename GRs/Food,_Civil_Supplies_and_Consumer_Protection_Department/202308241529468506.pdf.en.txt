(1) Government Resolution No. Adhapu-2015/ PR No. 100/ NAPU-22 dated 24th July, 2015 (2) Government Resolution No.Ravadu-1017/ Pr. 70/ NAPU-31, dated 4th September, 2017 3) Govt. No.45/ NAPU-22 dated 22nd August, 2023
Circular: In the context of the Department. As per the Government decision dated 22.08.2023, under the National Food Security Act, 2013, it has been decided to distribute a ration kit" Ration of Happiness" containing 1 kg each of Rava, Chana Dal, Sugar and 1 litre edible oil to the Antyodaya Anna Yojana and Priority Family Ration Card holders in the targeted Public Distribution System in the State as well as to the Above Poverty Line (APL) Kesari Kisan Ration Card holders in all the districts of Aurangabad and Amravati divisions and 14 farmer suicide-affected districts of Wardha division in Nagpur division at a subsidized rate of₹ 100/- per day through e-POS system from 1st September to 30th September during Gauri-Ganpati festival.
Accordingly, the following instructions are being issued regarding distribution of ration kits to eligible ration card holders:
Under the Public Distribution System in the state, 1 ration kit consisting of 4 ration kits of 1 kg semolina, 1 kg gram, 1 kg sugar and 1 litre edible oil should be distributed through the e-PoS system at the rate of₹ 100/- per kit to the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana, priority families as well as 14 farmer suicide-affected APL (Keshari) farmer ration card holders of all the districts of Aurangabad and Amravati division and Wardha division of Nagpur division.

2. District-wise details of number of ration kits to be supplied are attached. Within this limit, the Central Warehouse, Mumbai should deliver the ration kits to the respective Taluka Warehouses.
3. It is necessary to keep a record of the ration coming to the warehouse and the ration being sent to the fair price shop in online mode. Accordingly, care should be taken to take proper records on the AePDS portal.
4. The District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller concerned shall be responsible for ensuring proper unloading of ration received from the suppliers in the Taluk godowns. The department will bear the expenses for the transportation of the foodgrains. Accordingly, these payments should be submitted to the Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary.
The ration kits should be accepted by the supplier only after ensuring that the expiry date of the ration kits is at least 4 months.
6. Ration kits should be accepted only after obtaining NABL accredited laboratory certificate from the supplier that the Ration kit meets FSSAI standards. It should be ensured that poor quality ration/ ration kits are not accepted under any circumstances.
7. On receipt of ration kits from the supplier in the Taluka warehouse, the District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller shall randomly collect samples of ration kits in front of the supplier's representative by appointing an officer not below the rank of Tehsildar or himself. The samples will be sent to NABL laboratory for testing. If the sample is found to be not meeting the prescribed standards, the supplier should be informed immediately and action should be taken to replace the concerned ration kit.
The ration kits are to be distributed at the rate of₹ 100/- per set. The fair price shop owners should deduct the margin and deposit the remaining amount with the District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller within 7 days after the distribution of ration kits. Margin will be paid to fair price shop owners for selling these ration kits as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 4th September, 2017. Further, considering the period of ration kits distribution, fair price shop owners should be informed at their level about the distribution of the entire ration kits within a period of one month.

9. In order to ensure the benefit of the" Anandacho Ration" scheme, the concerned District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller should take action to spread awareness about this scheme.
The District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer/ Deputy Controller shall make available to the Government from time to time the details of ration kits received and distributed for their district and submit the utility certificate to the Government.
11. Department's letter to the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary regarding the agreement with Kendriya Bhandar, Mumbai for the purchase of the said ration kits. Mid-2023/ p. No. 45/ Napu- 22D. 23.08.2023 has been notified. Accordingly, the financial advisor and the deputy secretary should take action.
Kendriya Bhandar, Mumbai shall submit to the Government daily information on the following issues on the e-mail napu22.mhpds@mah.gov.in and to the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary:
a) Ration kits delivered to the places specified by the government (including names of places).
(b) Ration kits remaining with the tenderer/ Kendriya Bhandar, Mumbai.
All concerned should take action in accordance with the above procedure. All concerned should note that in case of any lapse in this process, the concerned will be held responsible.
