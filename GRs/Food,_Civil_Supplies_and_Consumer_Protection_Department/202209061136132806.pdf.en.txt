Reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 18.04.2022, the revised pattern has been approved after reviewing the posts in the subordinate offices of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection. According to the reference no. 2 here. The cadre wise number of posts has been sanctioned at State/ Divisional/ District/ Taluk level for the Divisional Supply Office in the Regional Supply System under the Department as on 04.07.2022. As per the revised figure, the Accounts Officer in the District Supply Office has been declared as dead cadre, the services of the Accounts Officer have been transferred to the parent department and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer is required to be declared for signing the payments for submission of salary dues and office expenses dues and other day to day financial transactions in the District Supply Office to the Treasury.
Reference No. 3 As requested by the District Supply Officer, Palghar, the matter of declaring the Assistant Accounts Officer as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration at the Government level.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the office of the District Supply Officer, Palghar," Assistant Accounts Officer is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
