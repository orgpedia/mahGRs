Introduction: In the context of the above. The State Food Commission has been constituted under the provisions of Section 16 (1) of the National Food Security Act, 2013 vide Government Order dated 11th April, 2018. As per the Government decision dated 2nd May, 2017, members belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes have been appointed in the State Food Commission.
As per the request made by Mrs. Gauri Chandran and Mrs. Kalpana Dastane to the Govt. The matter of appointment of new members in place of the members of the State Food Commission, as decided by the Government on 11.4.2017, was under consideration. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government's decision:
As per the request made by Mrs. Gauri Chandran and Mrs. Kalpana Dastane to the Govt. The appointments to the posts of Member, State Food Commission made vide Government Order dated 11.4.2017 are hereby cancelled and replaced by the following members:

As per the provisions of Section 16 (2) of the National Food Security Act, 2013, the State Food Commission shall consist of at least two women members and at least one member each from the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes categories.
