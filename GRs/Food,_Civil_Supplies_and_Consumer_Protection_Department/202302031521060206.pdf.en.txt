Instructions were given to distribute rice fortified with three micro-nutrients- B12 and Folic Acid. Also Reference No. Gadchiroli district has been selected for pilot implementation of the project. However, for the period 2020-21 and 2021-22, the said scheme will be implemented in the entire Gadchiroli district with Central and State funding in the ratio of 75:25. The decision has been taken in accordance with Government Order No. 4.
