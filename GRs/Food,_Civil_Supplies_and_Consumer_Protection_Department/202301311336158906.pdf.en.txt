Introduction: As per the above mentioned government decision, it has been approved to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali at a discounted rate to the poor and needy people of the state. For the year 2022-23, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 220.00 crore has been approved. Earlier, the Finance Department had released Rs. 107.80 crore has been disbursed to Subordinate Offices. Further, for the month of November, 2022 and December, 2022, the Finance Department has released Rs. A sum of Rs. 24.20 crore has been earmarked for the purpose. However, the matter of distributing the amount to the offices of the District Supply Officer, Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller (Ration) was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali at subsidized rates to the poor and needy persons, the Department of Finance has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 24,20,00, 000/- has been released. The disbursement to the districts is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
1. Subject to all the terms and conditions of the Government Order and Circular mentioned above, the amount mentioned in Annexure-A is Rs. 24,20,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crore Twenty Lakh only) is being sanctioned to the concerned District Supply Officer, Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller Rationing.
