Regarding the distribution of" Anandachya Ration" to eligible ration card holders under the Public Distribution System in the state on the occasion of upcoming festivals like Gudi Padwa, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jayanti, etc.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Decision No: Adhapu-2023/ P.No. 22
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 22, 2023.
The Proposal:
Under the Public Distribution System (PDS) in the State, 1 kit (Anandachari Ration) containing 1 kg each of Rava, Chana Dal, Sugar and 1 litre of Palm Oil was distributed at the rate of₹ 100/- per quintal to the Antyodaya Anna Yojana, Priority Family Ration Card holders and Above Poverty Line (APL) Keshari Kisan Ration Card holders in all the districts of Aurangabad and Amravati Division and 14 farmer suicide affected districts of Wardha Division in Nagpur Division on the occasion of Diwali 2022. On the same lines, the above ration card holders are requested to distribute" Anandachya Ration" consisting of 1 kg each of Rava, Gram, Sugar and 1 litre of Palm Oil on the occasion of upcoming festivals like Gudi Padwa, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jayanti, etc. at a discounted rate of₹ 100/- per day through e-PoS system for a period of 1 month from Gudi Padwa, Marathi New Year. The Cabinet has approved the following decisions:
Decision of the Government: 1. Under the Public Distribution System of the State, the Antyodaya Anna Yojana, priority families and all the farmers of Aurangabad and Amravati divisions and Wardha of Nagpur division are being approved to distribute" Anandachya Ration" containing rice, gram, sugar and palm oil in the quantity of 1 kg each on the occasion of upcoming festivals like Gudi Padwa, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jayanti, etc. to the above poverty line (APL) Keshari Kisan Ration Card holders of 14 suicide-affected districts at a discounted rate of₹ 100/- per unit through e-PoS system for a period of 1 month from Gudi Padwa Marathi New Year.
Approval is being given to implement the tender process by extending the time period for submission of tenders through online portal mahatenders. gov. in to 15 days instead of 21 days from the date of publication of tender notice for procurement of essential ration items for the above" Ration of Anand."
