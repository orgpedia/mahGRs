The Proposal:
The above 1, vide Government Resolution dated 26/6/2003, has approved the revised pattern of posts in the establishment of various Heads of Departments under this Department.
These include posts under Controller, Valid Metrology.
As per the Government decision dated 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021, out of total 940 sanctioned posts, 210 temporary posts at Mumbai and district level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended.
The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
A total of 210 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A attached with this Government decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation Sciences are being extended from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 (six months).
The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head" Demand No., M-3, Main Account Head 3475, Other General Financial Services (106), Regulation of Weights and Measures (00) (01) Regulation of Weights and Measures, (34750093)" and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2021-2022.
