Government's decision:
The Supreme Court in C.A. 831/ 2023 has held that 3.3.2023 and M.A. 397/ 2023. In pursuance of the directions issued on 24.3.2023, the recruitment process for a total of 112 vacancies of Chairperson and Members of the Consumer Redressal Commission has been carried out through written examination and interview.
As per the recommendations of the selection committee. 15.9.2023 has been published on the website of the Department. On the recommendation of the Selection Committee, the candidates concerned are being posted/ appointed in the State Consumer Redressal Commission and District Consumer Redressal Commission mentioned against their names as per the accompanying prospectus (Annexure A).
The candidates are being appointed for a period of four years from the date of joining of the candidate in the concerned post at the place mentioned in the return (Annexure A) or 65 years of age of the candidate concerned, whichever is earlier.
The candidates should join the concerned office within 15 days from the date of this Government decision. The candidates whose appointment has been indicated in the probable vacancy should join the post within 15 days from the date of the probable vacancy. If the candidate fails to join during this period, the appointment of the candidate will be automatically cancelled.
During the period of appointment, the candidate can resign from the post under Rule 7 of the Consumer Protection (Qualification of appointment, method of recruitment, procedure of appointment, term of office, resignation and removal of the president and members of the State Commission and District Commission) Rules, 2020.
