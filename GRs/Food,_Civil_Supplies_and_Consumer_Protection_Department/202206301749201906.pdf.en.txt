# Page 1
Provisional Seniority List showing the status of Hapur Trained Qualified and Interested Officers dated 1/1/2019, 1/1/2020, 1/1/2021 and 1/1/2022 for promotion to the post of Technical Officer.
MOM
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Circular No: Seniority-2122/ No.19/ No.15,
Ministry Extension, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: 30th of June 2022.
Director, Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, Mumbai and Superintending and Chief Inspection Officer in the office of the Commissioner of Supplies, Divisional Warehouse Inspector in the Divisional Office and Supply Inspection Officer in the District Office, Deputy Tahsildar under the District Collector, who have successfully completed training in Hapur, are eligible for promotion to the post of Technical Officer. Accordingly, the desirability of the eligible employees for promotion to the post of Technical Officer, who were working in the said posts on 1/1/2019, 1/1/2020, 1/1/2021 and 1/1/2022, was sought through the respective offices. Accordingly, the Provisional Seniority List showing the position of Technical Officer as on 1/1/2019, 1/1/2020, 1/1/2021 and 1/1/2022 is being published in the accompanying Returns-1, Returns-2, Returns-3 and Returns-4 respectively.
All the Divisional Commissioners, Director Civil Supplies, Mumbai, Office of the Commissioner of Supplies, Mumbai and Directorate Civil Supplies (Go/ Wa), Mumbai are hereby informed to publicize the said seniority list properly and bring it to the notice of all the concerned cadre employees. If the officers/ employees have issues/ objections on this seniority list, the concerned should send it through their department heads. Offices should submit their issues/ objections, if any, along with their feedback to the government within 15 days from the date of this circular.
It may be noted that objections not received by the concerned within the stipulated time will not be considered and the said provisional seniority list will be finalised.
