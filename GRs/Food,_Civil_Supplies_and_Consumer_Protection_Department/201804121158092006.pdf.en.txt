Government circular: Fair price shops are managed through the RCMS (Ration Card Management System) system in the state. Therefore, the government has accepted the FPS database in RCMS as a standard database. Therefore, the number of shops in RCMS is being considered final by the government.
The government has fixed April 30, 2018 as the date for finalising the matter.
The total number of Fair Price Shops (FPS) in your area during this period and the details should match the number of Fair Price Shops in the RCMS. Also, a certificate should be issued that the number of fair price shops in RCMS has been finalized on May 2, 2018.
The government will consider the information of fair price shops in RCMS from May 2, 2018.
Henceforth, no new fair price shops shall be permitted to open without creating a new fair price shop and its details in the RCMS system.
