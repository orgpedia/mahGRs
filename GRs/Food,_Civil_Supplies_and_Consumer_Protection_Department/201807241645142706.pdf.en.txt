(2) The concerned Inspector shall immediately join the place of transfer without waiting for any other order, considering the same as a discharge order, and submit his report to the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and to the Government immediately.
The above Inspector shall assume charge of the new post forthwith, he shall not be unauthorisedly absent otherwise his absence shall be treated as unauthorised absence and shall be made without pay. Also, the possibility of their services being terminated cannot be ruled out.
Relatives are being informed.
The period of appointment, travel allowance and other incidental allowances will be admissible to the concerned officers for joining the post of new posting.

5. The Controller, Validation, is informed that the date of demitting office and assumption of charge by the officer concerned should be intimated to the Government immediately. If the concerned officer does not join the place of transfer within the prescribed period, the government should be informed immediately.
This will enable swift disciplinary action against the concerned. As per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, leave applications of transferred officers should not be accepted/ sanctioned.
