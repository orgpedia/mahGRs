Terms and Conditions:
(1) The term of office of the President shall be for a period of one year from the date on which he enters upon his office. If the Chairperson of the State Commission is of the opinion that the performance of the candidate is not satisfactory, the period of probation will be extended.
(2) The appointment of the concerned candidates will be subject to medical examination and police verification report, whose police verification/ medical examination report will be adverse/ if any adverse remarks are mentioned in it, their appointment will be cancelled.
(3) The Chairperson of the District Forum shall be appointed for a term of five years or till he attains the age of 65 years, whichever is earlier.
(4) They shall not be re-appointed or re-appointed.
(5) The remuneration of the members of the District Consumer Redressal Forum shall be admissible in accordance with the provisions of clause (1) of rule 3 of the Maharashtra Consumer Protection Rules, 2000, as amended from time to time.
(6) All other terms and conditions of service of the Chairperson of the District Consumer Forum shall be governed by the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 and Rules, 2000 as amended by the Government from time to time.
(7) The President of the District Forum shall be entitled to receive the daily and travel allowance admissible to first class officers of the State Government while on tour for the purpose of the District Forum.
However, they will not be allowed to travel by air or first class train.
The Chairperson of the concerned District Forum may be removed from the post as per the provision of Rule 3 (4) of the Maharashtra Consumer Protection Rules, 2000.
(9) In public interest or for administrative reasons, the services of the Chairman of the District Consumer Forum shall be transferred from one District Forum to another District Forum or his services shall be utilised by linking two District Forums as may be decided by the State Commission considering the workload or pending cases.
10) If the concerned does not take charge within 15 days from the date of issue of the order of appointment, his appointment will be automatically cancelled.
11) These appointments are being made after reviewing the work business.
