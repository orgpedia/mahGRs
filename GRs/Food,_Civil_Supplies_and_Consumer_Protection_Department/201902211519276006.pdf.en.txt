2.The repair work of the government godowns provided with funds as above should be started and completed immediately. After the warehouse repair is started, the district supply officer should first review the complete details of the repair as well as the related repairs every month and submit the progress of the repair to the government.
3. The District Supply Officer should take care to submit the utility certificate to the government as soon as the repair work of the government godown is completed.
After the repair work of the godown is completed, the concerned Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Supply Officer should jointly make a site inspection report to ensure that the work is done according to the approved budget and it should be preserved in the records.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that there is no cost overrun in repairing the godown due to delay or other reasons. It may be noted that these funds cannot be transferred/ retained as a deposit contribution.
6. The cost of repair of the godown during the financial year 2018-19 shall be spent under the head Demand No.M-2, Head Account Head 2408 Food Storage and Warehousing, (03) Procurement, Distribution and Price Control (03) (02) Mufassal, (Non-Plan) (Dattamat) (2408 0108) 27 Small Constructions.
7. Similarly, if for some reason the funds provided for the repair work of the concerned godown are not likely to be spent, then a report should be submitted to the government with the reasons. The funds have been distributed as per the order. If there is no expenditure before March 30, 2019, the withdrawal of funds should be done through the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary.
8. The District Supply Officer concerned shall ensure that no more funds are released for the repair work of the concerned godowns than the cost as per the Administrative Sanction Order.
The district administration will be responsible for this.
9. In the administrative sanction order, the brief nature of the work is mentioned and in fact, the repair work of the godown should be completed according to the budget of the repair work submitted to the government. That is, the repair work of the warehouse should be completed according to the budget presented to the government.
