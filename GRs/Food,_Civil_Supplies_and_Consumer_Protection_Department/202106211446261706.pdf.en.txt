The Proposal:
Farmers of all the districts of Aurangabad and Amravati division and 14 districts of Wardha having APL (Keshari) ration cards are given the benefit of 5 kg food grains per person per month (rice at the rate of₹ 3/ kg and wheat at the rate of₹ 2/ kg). At present, about 49 lakh beneficiaries are being provided subsidized foodgrains under this scheme. 20,034 required for these beneficiaries. tons (12,689 m.). tonnes of wheat and 7,345 ma. Tonnes of rice are procured by the central government at economic cost.
The state government is bearing a huge financial burden due to the increasing prices of foodgrains procured by the central government every year. The government was considering setting up a committee to examine whether the state government's funds would be saved if the foodgrains were made available at a lower rate than the economic cost by implementing the tender process. Accordingly, a committee is being constituted to conduct the tender process for the procurement of food grains required for the farmers holding APL (Keshari) ration cards in all the districts of Aurangabad and Amravati divisions and Wardha.
