the prelude
The Consumer Protection Act, 1986 has been amended and the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 has been amended. It has come into effect from 20.07.2020. Section 8 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 provides for the establishment of District Consumer Protection Councils at the district level to promote and protect the rights of consumers. According to the above No. 1 here. The Maharashtra Consumer Protection (State and District Consumer Protection Council) Rules, 2022 have been framed vide notification dated 18.02.2022. The Council shall consist of elected members of the Government and the non-Government. Accordingly, the matter of appointment of government/ non-government members to the Consumer Protection Councils was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 8 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (35 of 2019) and Rule 10 of the Maharashtra Consumer Protection Rules, 2022, the Government of Maharashtra hereby constitutes District Consumer Protection Councils for all the districts of the State.
The total number of members of the District Consumer Protection Council is 42, out of which the government members will be as follows:
1 Collector President 2 Upper Collector Vice President 3 Deputy Controller, Validation Officer Member 4 Superintendent of Police Member 5 Deputy CEO, Zilla Parishad Member 6 Assistant Commissioner, Food and Drug Administration Member 7 Executive Engineer, Telecom Department Member 8 Executive Engineer (Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company) Member 9 District Agriculture Superintendent Member

10 Health Officers, Zila Parishad Members, 11 Assistant Regional Transport Officers, 12 District Information Officers, 13 District Supply Officers, Member Secretaries
The term of office of the District Consumer Protection Council shall be three years from the date of issue of this Government Order.
The procedure of the District Consumer Protection Council, the appointment of non-official members on it, the criteria for selection, etc. are covered in the Maharashtra Consumer Protection Rules, 2022 and the Government Decision. 29.08.2022.
Out of the total number of members on the District Consumer Protection Council, excluding the above 13 government members, all the non-government members of the remaining various categories should be appointed by the concerned Collector and Controller of Ration in accordance with the procedure prescribed in the Maharashtra Consumer Protection (State and District Consumer Protection Council) Rules, 2022 and subject to the Police Character Verification Report.
