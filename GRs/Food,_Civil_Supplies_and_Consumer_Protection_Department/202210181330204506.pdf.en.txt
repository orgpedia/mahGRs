Preamble: After reviewing the posts in the office under the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department, Reference No. 1 The revised pattern of posts has been approved as per the decision of the government. As per this figure, a total of 1128 regular posts and 263 posts through outsourcing have been sanctioned for the ration distribution system on the establishment of Controller, Rationing and Director Civil Supplies under the Government decision dated 25 th August, 2022. As per the previous figure, a total of 99 temporary posts, 50 in the part-time sweeper cadre and 49 in the part-time mehter cadre, have been notified in the Reference No. 02 As per the Government decision dated 01/03/2022 dated 31.08.2022 dated 31.08.2022 dated 31.08.2022 dated 15.06.2022, the office of the Controller, Rationing and Director Civil Supplies, Mumbai has declared the part time sweeper and part time mehter cadre of the office as dead cadre as per the revised figure Government decision dated 25/08/2022.
Reference No. Under the decision of the Finance Department here, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts from September 1, 2022, to February 28, 2023, which are included in their figure and which were created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the figure. At present, there are 90 posts in this cadre in the office of the Controller Rationing, Mumbai.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the requirement of total 90 posts for the office of the Controller, Rationing and Director, Civil Supplies, Mumbai, 44 in the part-time sweeper cadre and 46 in the part-time mehter cadre, the Government hereby approves the continuation of these posts from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 subject to the condition that these posts are not vacant for more than 6 months.
