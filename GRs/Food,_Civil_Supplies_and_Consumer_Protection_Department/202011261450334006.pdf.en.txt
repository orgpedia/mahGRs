Introduction: The Shiv Bhojan scheme was launched to provide food at affordable prices to the poor and needy people of the state. It was launched on January 26, 2020. Saksham Khanawalas, NGOs, women self-help groups, eateries, restaurants or messes are selected through a committee at the zonal level to implement the scheme.
The government was contemplating to amend the Act. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
The committees at the municipal, district and taluka level should recommend to the government through the concerned collector and the committee in the Mumbai-Thane rationing area through the controller of ration and the director of civil supplies.
The final selection of the Saksham Khanavas, NGOs, women self-help groups, eateries, restaurants or messes will be done at the government level from the proposals recommended by the committee.
