Preamble: After reviewing the posts in the office under the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department, Reference No. 1 The revised pattern of posts has been approved as per the decision of the government. As mentioned in the Statement 8 accompanying this Government decision, 9 posts have been sanctioned for the State Food Commission, Mumbai office and these posts are temporary posts. As a result, these posts have been shown as temporary posts in the service system.
Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. 2 and Reference No. 3 The Government hereby authorize the Administrative Departments to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts, which are included in their rolls and which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls, from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024. Accordingly, the matter of extending the sanctioned posts in the State Food, Commission, Mumbai office was under consideration at the government level.
Government's decision:
Considering the requirement of the following posts in the State Employment Commission, Mumbai Office, the Government is pleased to grant approval for the continuation of the said posts from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 subject to the condition that the posts are not vacant for more than 6 months.
