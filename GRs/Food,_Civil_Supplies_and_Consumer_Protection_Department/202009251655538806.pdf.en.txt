As per the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986, State Consumer Redressal Commission at the State level and District Consumer Redressal Forum at the District level have been set up.
Also, in pursuance of the amendments made by the Central Government in the Consumer Protection Act, 2002, the State Commission has been constituted with Parikrama Benches (Circuit Benches) at Nagpur and Aurangabad respectively.
With the increase in the functioning of the State Commission, the District Forum, a total of 497 temporary posts of officers/ staff have been created for the State Commission and the offices of the Nagpur and Aurangabad Parikrama Benches of the Commission and the District Forum from time to time.

In exercise of the powers conferred on the Department under the Government Order No. 1, 497 temporary posts of the State Consumer Redressal Commission and its subordinate offices have been created. 01.03.2020 to dt. Reference No. 31.08.2020 The deadline was extended by the government.
In the meantime, the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 has lapsed and the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 has lapsed. It has come into effect from 20.07.2020. As per the provisions of the said Act, 2019, it is necessary to make rules regarding the salary and allowances payable, other terms and conditions of service of the officers/ employees of the State and District Commissions and action is proposed accordingly. However, in the present case, the aforesaid Finance Department Reference No. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Department under Government Order No.3, a total of 497 temporary posts on the establishment of State Consumer Redressal Commissions and their subordinate offices are hereby approved for extension for the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 as indicated in Annexure A annexed to this Government Order, subject to the following conditions:
