Date: 20th of July 2019.
Introduction: Implementation of National Food Security Act, 2013 in the State of Maharashtra. It has been started from 01/02/2014. As per the provisions of the Act, 75% (76.72% for Maharashtra) of the country's rural and 50% (45.34% for Maharashtra) of the urban population is entitled to subsidized foodgrains. The State Food Commission has been constituted on 5th July, 2016 under Section 16 of the National Food Security Act, 2013. The matter of declaring a Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the office of the State Food Commission was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION:" Accounts Officer is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the office of State Food Commission.
