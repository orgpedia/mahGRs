Preamble: As per the Government Resolution dated 31st January, 2014 of the General Administration Department, instructions have been issued to constitute a Civil Service Board to recommend to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) officers in the Government Service. Accordingly, a Reference No. 20 has been issued for making recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers working in the Validation System and Consumer Protection System under the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department. The Civil Service Board has been constituted under Section 2.
Before November 2013, two departments, namely, the Legal Metrology Department and the Consumer Protection Department under the Food and Civil Supplies Department were functioning from the same establishment. The Committee of the Civil Service Board consisting of the Manager to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of officers in the valid metrology system under the Government decision dated 11th May, 2015.
State Consumer Redressal Commission has been appointed as a member. Now the posts have been adjusted according to the choice of officers/ employees in both the systems.
Accordingly, the matter of reconstitution of the Civil Services Board constituted to recommend to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers working in the Validation System and Consumer Protection System was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Reference No. Formed for making recommendations to the competent authority regarding posting/ transfer of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers working in the valid Metrology System under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection. The Civil Service Board at paragraph 2 (a) of the Government Resolution here is being reconstituted as follows. :
