Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. The budgets for the repair of government godowns in Parbhani district have been received by the government through a reference letter. The Public Works Department has agreed to scrutinise the budget and give administrative approval to it. Accordingly, the Government No. 3 The matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal for the repair of this warehouse was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The Government Store No. Administrative approval is being granted to the amendment works and budget of 3 subject to the following conditions.

4. Repair works should be taken up as soon as funds are available for repair of Government godowns which have received administrative approval as above. After receiving the funds, the repair work of government godowns should be started immediately and completed. After the repair of the godown is started, the district supply officer, Parbhani, should first review the complete details of the repair and related repairs every month and submit the progress of the repair to the government.
5. Repair of the said godowns should be done by the Public Works Department.
Necessary funds will be provided to the Public Works Department for this.
6. The District Supply Officer should take care to submit the utility certificate to the government as soon as the repair work of the government godown is completed.
7. In the administrative sanction order, the brief nature of the work is mentioned and in fact, the repair work of the godown should be completed as per the budget of the repair work submitted to the government. That is, the repair work of the warehouse should be completed according to the budget presented to the government.
8. District Supply Officers shall personally scrutinize the requirement of warehouse repair work as mentioned in the said order to avoid unnecessary re-tuning of warehouse repair work.
