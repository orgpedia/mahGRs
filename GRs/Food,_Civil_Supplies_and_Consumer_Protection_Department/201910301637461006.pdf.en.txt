(1) Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016
The State Consumer Redressal Commission, Mumbai is functioning at the State level under the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 under the aegis of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection with its Benches at Aurangabad and Nagpur. Also, a total of 40' District Consumer Grievance Redressal Forums' are functioning at the district level.
As per the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory to appoint a' Disability Grievance Redressal Officer' in every government establishment. Accordingly, the' Manager (Administration)" for the office of State Consumer Redressal Commission, Mumbai, the' Manager (Legal)" for Aurangabad and Nagpur Bench Offices and the' Manager (Group-B) Gazetted" for all District Consumer Redressal Forum Offices are being declared as' Disabled Grievance Redressal Officer."
