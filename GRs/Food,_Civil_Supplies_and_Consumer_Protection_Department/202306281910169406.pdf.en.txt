Since the officers mentioned in the above table are transferred periodically, they are allowed to join the place of transfer subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981.
4. It may be noted that if the transferred officers mentioned in the above table attempt to cancel the transfer by directly or indirectly exerting pressure for the same, the action will be considered as misconduct as it is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
No leave shall be granted to the officer so posted without prior approval and no leave applications of the officer so posted shall be entertained by the parent office in accordance with Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned officer by sending the registration fee to the original address.
6. It is also a serious matter that the transferred officers mentioned in the above table have not complied with the orders passed with the approval of the competent authority under the Transfer Act.
The officer who does not comply with this will be liable for suspension under Section- 2.1 (2) (v) of Section 2 of the Departmental Inquiry Rules, 1991.
