The Proposal:
To provide subsidized food to the poor and needy people of the state. Under the Government decision dated 01.01.2020," Shiv Bhojan Scheme" was launched. It was launched on January 26, 2020.
The Shiv Bhojan scheme is an ambitious scheme of the government and has been running smoothly for the last three years. The demand of this department on BEAMS system No. Since M02 mentions 24080772 subsidy for 10 Rupees Thali Scheme-33 subsidies under the head Scheme and Heads, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to make appropriate changes in the BEAMS system to reflect the name of the scheme as" Shiv Bhojan Scheme."
Government's decision:
1. Financial provision for the scheme to be implemented to provide Shiv Bhojan at subsidized rates to the poor and needy people of the state. M2 Main article 2408 Food, Storage and Warehousing 01 Food, 101 Procurement and Supply (03) Procurement, Distribution and Price Control (03) (23) Rs.
2. By this Government decision, it is being approved to rename the scheme as" Shiv Bhojan Yojana" instead of" Financial Assistance for Rs. Accordingly, the title of the scheme is being changed as follows:
2408 Food, Storage and Warehousing 01 Food, 101 Procurement and Supply (03) Procurement, Distribution and Price Control (03) (23) Shiv Bhojan Scheme (Programme) (24080772) 33 Financial Assistance.
