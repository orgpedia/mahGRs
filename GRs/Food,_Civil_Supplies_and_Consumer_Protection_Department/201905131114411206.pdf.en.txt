Introduction: Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to the ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. Reference Letter No. Under Section 7, the administrative approval given by the District Collector, Palghar to the warehouse repair works in Palghar district has been accepted as a special case at the government level. The repair work of the godown has been completed and the Executive Engineer, Jawahar G. Palghar has requested to provide funds for this work. 8 has been made. The District Supply Officer, Raigad vide letter No. As per Section 9, funds have been sought for warehouse repair works with pending payments.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to disburse funds for the repair works of godowns in Palghar and Raigad districts where the works were completed and pending payments were made.
Government's decision:
Repair of godowns in Palghar district where works have been completed and pending payments in Raigad district subject to the conditions mentioned in the Government Decision/ Letter regarding administrative approval.
