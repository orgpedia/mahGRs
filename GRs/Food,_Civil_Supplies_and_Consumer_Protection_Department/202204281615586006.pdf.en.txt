The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, in its original application No. 88/2008.
The promotions granted in the cadre of Assistant Controller, Valid Metrology on 20.10.2008 for the period from 1984 to 17/4/1996 have been ordered to be reviewed on the basis of 50:50 ratio of direct service and promotion. Though the orders of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal pertain to the period 1984-1996, it is pertinent to review all the promotions given so far in this cadre as it affects all the subsequent selection lists. Accordingly, all the promotions given in the valid metric system from 1984 to 2008 have been reviewed and the revised selection list has been approved by the General Administration Department as well as the Government.
On the basis of the above reviewed selections, Mr. Kejkar is being promoted in the year 2001-2002 and is eligible for the following promotions: 06/04/2002 is the date of regular promotion to the post of Assistant Controller.
As per the Government decision dated 14th January, 2021, the designation of the post of Inspector Selection Category has been made as Assistant Controller and the designation of the post of Assistant Controller has been made as Deputy Controller.
Accordingly, the present designation of Shri P.B. Kejkar is Deputy Controller, Valid Metrology.
