Preamble: In order to prevent pilferage/ misappropriation of foodgrains by detecting ineligible beneficiaries under the National Food Security Act, 2013 and to distribute foodgrains to the beneficiaries in a transparent manner, as per the guidelines of the Central Government, complete computerization of the Public Distribution System in the State is being done. 1 here. 18.05.2016 has been decided.
Under the scheme, the state government is implementing the Integrated Management of Public Distribution System (IMPS).
As per the revised methodology for Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes, the concerned administrative department shall appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for that scheme. In addition, the Finance Department has also issued Reference No. 1 regarding the appointment of an officer of at least Deputy Secretary rank as Nodal Officer for coordination of the scheme. 3 Government circulars have been issued. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the implementation and coordination of the said Centrally Sponsored Scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Finance Department Reference No. As per the instructions given in the Government Circular, the Government is approving the appointment of Single Nodal Agency and Nodal Officer for implementation and coordination of Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented by the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection as follows.
