After certifying the bags of foodgrains in the government grain godowns where electricity is available in the state, bag sewing machines are used to sew them and twine knots are used in the government grain godowns where electricity is not available.
The central government has made a big change in the procurement process under the National Procurement Policy.
Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is a portal developed by Government of India for procurement of goods and services by Government Departments/ Organizations. The modalities of this portal have been approved by the State Government for procurement of goods and services. The decision has been approved by the government. Accordingly, if there are two or more suppliers of goods and services available for purchase on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal, then the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal will be used for purchase of such goods and services. The targeted departments/ offices mentioned in para 2.4 of 01/12/2016 have been made mandatory. Accordingly, reference to Procurement Procedure No. 1 here. Necessary changes have been made in the government decision dated December 1, 2016.
According to the Department No. 4 For 500 government godowns where electricity is available, 1100 sewing machines, their spare parts, hanging hooks and thread for sewing machines have been procured through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal and supplied to the field offices.

In the government grain godowns where electricity was available, adequate sewing machines were not provided. Also, the government was considering providing bag sewing machines, a set of its spare parts, with hanging hooks in the government granaries where new electricity has been made available, as per the demand made by some districts of the state.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The procurement of 445 Bag Closer Machines (with one-year warranty), its spare parts and hanging hooks, which can be easily handled for stitching after validating the foodgrain bags in the government grain godowns in the state where electricity is available/ procured, is being approved through a competitive online tender process through BOQ mode on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM), a central government portal.
A bag sewing machine on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal with a hanging hook and a one-year guarantee is priced at Rs. Rs 13,450. Accordingly, 445 bags of sewing machines with hanging hooks and one-year guarantee will be purchased at an estimated total cost of Rs. 59,85,250/- (Rupees Ninety-nine Lakhs Eighty-five Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved by the Government through this Order.
After the purchase of the above 445 sacks of sewing machines, the cost of maintenance repair for the next one year after the guarantee period of 1 year is estimated to be Rs. 66,75,000/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakh Seventy Five Thousand only) is being approved by the Government through this Order.

(8) Responsibilities of the District Supply Office:
8.1 Distribution of sewing machines received for the district While distributing the sewing machines to the government godowns where electricity is available, the District Supply Officer shall consider the storage capacity of the godown building and fix the minimum to maximum limit of 500 units per godown building. The distribution of sewing machines like 2 bag sewing machines for a ton capacity should be done taking into account the bag sewing machines distributed earlier. (e.g. 100/250/350/ 500 MAY) For a warehouse building with a tonnage of 2 sewing machines and a warehouse capacity of more than 500 mA. 2 sewing machines each for warehouse capacity in tonnes times i.e. 2500 m.l. 10 sewing machines per ton should be distributed in this way.
8.2 Regular use of sewing machines requires constant lubrication.
Similarly, after heating the sewing machine, it is necessary to stop using it and start working with another sewing machine. The government has pointed out that by ignoring this, sewing machines become hot and turn off. Therefore, the supply office should bring this matter to the notice of the concerned users.
