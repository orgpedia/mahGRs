(10) Disputes and Appeals relating to the provisions of the Online Contract:
The Supplier may appeal to the Government within 30 days from the date of the award of the award in respect of the terms and conditions of the online contract as well as penalties, recovery, etc. The decision taken by the government will be final and binding on both the parties. The Supplier may request the provision of the Contract within a maximum period of 6 months after the conclusion of the Online Contract.
