The order:
A case has been registered against Yende, Inspector, Legal Metrology, Ambad Division, G. Jalna under Section 7 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 on 23rd November, 2022 at Police Station Ashti vide case No. 263/ 2022. A criminal case is also pending against him.
Yende, Inspector, Validation, Ambad Division, Dist.
Jalna is being suspended under the provisions of sub-rule (1) (a) of rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, with effect from the date of this order and shall remain suspended till further orders.
It is further ordered that during the period of operation of this order, the headquarters of Mr. P. V. Yende, Inspector, Joint Controller, Valid Metrology, Aurangabad Divisional Office, Aurangabad shall remain in place. Mr. Yende, Inspector, Valid Metrology cannot leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Controller, Valid Metrology. Yende, inspector, legal metrology, tambourine department, G. Jalna will not be able to accept private jobs. They cannot engage in business or trade.
Yende, Inspector, Valid Metrology, Ambad Division, G. Jalna, as long as there are orders of suspension, should be paid subsistence allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the provisions of Rule 68 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service, Provisions during Suspension, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. If, while under suspension, Mr. P. V. Yende, Inspector, takes up a private job or engages himself in trade or industry, he shall be deemed to have committed misconduct and shall be ineligible to receive subsistence allowance.
