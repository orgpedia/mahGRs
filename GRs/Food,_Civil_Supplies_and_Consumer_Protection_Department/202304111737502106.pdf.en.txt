Introduction: Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection The revised Service Entry Rules have been issued for the posts of Deputy Commissioner (Supplies), District Supplies Officer and Food Distribution Officer, Group-A, Assistant District Supplies Officer, Technical Officer and Inspection Officer (Supplies), Group-B (Gazetted) under the Department vide notification dated 27.3.2023.
The text of the notification. 7 (a) states that the appointment to the post of Inspection Officer (Supplies), Group-B (Gazetted) shall be made by promotion of suitable person on the basis of combined merit from amongst persons holding the posts of Supply Inspector, Grain Purchase Inspector, Warehousing Manager (Grade-II) and Top Clerk (Supplies) and having not less than three years of regular and continuous service in those posts. The post of Inspection Officer (Supply) is a state level post
As per the revised service entry rules, the post of Inspection Officer (Supply) is a state-level post.
Therefore, the staff of Supply Inspector (Group-C) cadre in the Supply Branch of the Divisional Office under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection will not be allowed to work. It was under consideration of the Government to release the Provisional Seniority List to finalize the State level consolidated seniority list showing the status as on 28.03.2023.
Government Circular: In view of the matter mentioned in the introduction, the Supply Inspector of the Supply Cadre in the Divisional Office under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, (Group-C) of the staff of the Server Group. The State level consolidated provisional figures showing the position as on 28.03.2023 are being published in Annexure-A along with the Seniority.

All Deputy Commissioners (Supplies) are requested to publicize the said provisional seniority list properly and bring it to the notice of all the concerned employees of the department. Objections not received within the aforesaid deadline will not be considered and this seniority list will be finalized.
