Reference No. As per the Government Order dated 24th May, 2005, Shri K.V. Patil was temporarily promoted to the post of Assistant Controller from the post of Inspector of Selection.
The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, in its original application No. 88/2008. Orders have been issued on 20.10.2008 to review the promotions given in the cadre of Assistant Controller, Valid Metrology during the period from 1984 to 17/4/1996 by assuming 50% of the direct service and promotions. Though the orders of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal pertain to the period 1984-1996, it is pertinent to review all the promotions given so far in this cadre as it affects all the subsequent selection lists. Accordingly, all the promotions given in the valid metric system from 1984 to 2008 have been reviewed and the revised selection list has been approved by the General Administration Department as well as the Government.
On the basis of the above reviewed selections, Mr. K.V. Patil is being promoted during the 2001-2002 selection year and he is being given a salary of Rs. 01/07/2001 is the last date of promotion to the post of Assistant Controller.
As per the Government decision dated 14th January, 2021, the designation of the post of Inspector Selection Category has been made as Assistant Controller and the designation of the post of Assistant Controller has been made as Deputy Controller. Accordingly, Shri.
Patil's current designation is Deputy Controller, Valid Metrology.
