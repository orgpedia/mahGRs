Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 18th April, 2022, the revised pattern of posts in the office of the Controller, Valid Metrology, Mumbai under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection has been fixed in which a total of 13 posts of drivers have been declared as dead cadre. Also, these posts are approved to be filled by external agencies. Accordingly, the Controller, Valid Metrology has requested the Government to approve the services of two drivers on contract basis at their establishment. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the services of two drivers on contract basis for the office of the Controller, Valid Metrology, Mumbai.
Government Decision: The Government approved the establishment of Controller, Validation Metrology by the agency M/ s Krishna Motors Providing Manpower Service, Jogeshwari, Mumbai on contract basis for the period from 25/5/2022 to 24/5/2023 (for 11 months).
