2. Expenditure on" Demand No. Capital expenditure on M-4, Head Accounts Heading 4408, 02 Food Storage and Warehousing 01/ Food 101 Procurement and Supply, 02 Food Storage and Warehousing 101, Rural Warehousing Programme, (01) Construction of New Warehouses (01) Construction of New Warehousing (under MUFSAL) (Scheme) (4408 0246), 53 Large Constructions" under the head of accounts and the provisions made during the financial year 2018-19.
3. Construction of Government godowns funded as above should be completed immediately. Once the construction of the godowns starts, the district supply officer should first review the complete details of the construction and the related construction every month and submit the progress of the construction to the government.
4. The District Supply Officer should take care to submit the utilization certificate to the government as soon as the construction of the government warehouse is completed. As soon as the funds for the warehouse are actually spent, the utilization certificate of the total disbursed funds for the relevant work should be submitted to the government immediately.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that the cost of construction of godowns does not increase due to delay in construction or other reasons.
While the construction work of these godowns is in progress, the concerned Superintending Engineer, Public Works Department should visit at least three times to ensure the quality of work and submit such reports to the Collector in writing from time to time.
7. The Collector shall personally visit the godown before taking over the godown after completion of the construction of the godown and ensure that the work done is of good quality. If there are any lapses, it should be rectified by the Public Works Department and its site inspection report should be preserved in the records.
