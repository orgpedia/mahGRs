Government Resolution No:- Sugar-1117/ PR No.91/ NAPU-19 dated 25th July, 2017 2) Government Resolution No: Sugar-1117/ PR. No.65/ NAPU-19, dated 14th June, 2017.
Preamble: A proposal for distribution of sugar at the rate of 1 kg per family for the month of October, 2018 to the beneficiary families of priority households in the State of Maharashtra was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The Government of Maharashtra has decided to allocate sugar at the rate of 1 kg per family for the month of October, 2018 to the beneficiary families of priority households in the State of Maharashtra.
In pursuance of the above proposal, the Department of Finance has approved the allocation of 1 kg of sugar per family for October, 2018 to the beneficiary families of priority households at an outlay of Rs. 41.75 crore.
2) As mentioned above, the Finance Department has approved the expenditure of Rs. 41.75 crore for Mumbai Division under the head Account Heading Rs. 6.45 crore and Rs. 32.55 crore for Mufassal Division under the head Account Heading Rs.44080077 and Rs.44080095 respectively. The detailed cost is to be spent under the headings M-04, 01, Food 101, Procurement and Supply, (02) Procurement and Distribution and Price Control, (02) (01), Mumbai City- Cost of Procurement (Mandatory) (44080077) 21, Supply and Material and (02) (02) Mufassal (Dattamat) (Mandatory) 44080095, 21, Supply and Material, respectively.
Similarly, after distributing sugar to the beneficiary families (after selling sugar at Rs.20 per kg), the funds available from the Mumbai division should be deposited under the head 44080166 and the funds available from the sale from the mofussil division should be deposited under the head 44080175.
4) This Order is being issued in accordance with the concurrence of the Finance Department received under Informal Reference No. 260/ Expenditure-10, dated 27/09/2018 of the Finance Department.
