(2) Specifications and procurement procedures:
2.1 Once-used serviceable' B-Twil jute gunny bags' weighing not less than 500 gms but having storage capacity of 50 kg (Dimensions94L* 57W) should be procured through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal.
For the said Once-used serviceable-B-Twill jute gunny bags, lab testing is required to be done by an organization registered/ certified by NABL. Accordingly, all bidders are required to submit their samples of single-use gunny bags to the government for lab testing.
2.2 The Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal shall publish an e-tender inviting bids from the suppliers of the above-mentioned Once-used serviceable-B-Twill jute gunny bags whose average turnover for the preceding 3 financial years 2020-21,2021- 22,2022-23 is at least Rs 90 crore. On receipt of e-bid from the bidders, the participating eligible bidders shall submit to the tenderer, if they are authorized suppliers, a letter of authorization from the manufacturer to appoint an authorized distributor, a valid copy of ISO certificate of the manufacturer, if the manufacturer is a tenderer, along with a certified audit report showing the financial turnover for the last 3 years.
