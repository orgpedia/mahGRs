Introduction: As per the above mentioned government decision, it has been approved to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali at a discounted rate to the poor and needy people of the state. For the financial year 2023-24, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 220.00 crore has been approved. As per the Finance Department, Rs. 44.00 crore has been made available for disbursement. However, the matter of distributing the amount to the offices of the District Supply Officer, Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller (Ration) is under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali at subsidized rates to the poor and needy persons, the Department of Finance has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 44,00,00, 000/- has been released. The disbursement to the districts is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
1. Subject to all the terms and conditions of the Government Order and Circular mentioned above, the amount mentioned in Annexure-A is Rs. 44,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Four Crores only) is being sanctioned to the concerned District Supply Officer, Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller Rationing.
(2) The grant shall be used for the purpose for which it is granted. This amount cannot be used for any other purpose.
(3) The accounts of the expenditure to be made out of the aforesaid grants shall be kept in the books of accounts in force and the utilization certificate of the payments made in that month after the payment is made from the treasury through your office in this regard shall be submitted to the Accountant General's Office and the Government by the 15th of the following month after the end of the month as per the standard and date.
4. The Collector (District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer) at the district level is being authorised to draw the amount as mentioned in Letter-A from the Treasury and the Controller, Rationing and Director, Civil Supplies, Sub Divisional Office, Thane, to draw the subsidy from the Office of the Subordinate& Accounts Officer, Mumbai for Mumbai/ Thane Rationing Area.
