Government Decision: In order to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali to the poor and needy persons at subsidized rates, the Department of Finance has sanctioned Rs. 195.00 crore has been released, out of which Rs. 195,00,00, 000/- has been disbursed, the remaining amount of Rs. 45 crore by the Department of Finance in Informal Reference No. 71/- (Rs. 17.02.2022 and are being approved for distribution to the districts subject to the following terms and conditions.
1. Subject to all the terms and conditions of the Government Order and Circular mentioned above, the amount mentioned in Annexure-A is Rs. 45,00,000, 000/- (Rupees Forty Five Crores only) is being disbursed.
(2) The grant shall be used for the purpose for which it is granted. This amount cannot be used for any other purpose.
(3) The accounts of the expenditure to be made out of the aforesaid grants shall be kept in the books of accounts in force and the utilization certificate of the payments made in that month after the payment is made from the treasury through your office in this regard shall be submitted to the Accountant General's Office and the Government by the 15th of the following month after the end of the month as per the standard and date.
4. The Collector (District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer) at the district level is being authorised to draw the amount as mentioned in Letter-A from the Treasury and the Controller Rationing and Director Civil Supplies is being authorised to draw the amount from the office of the Subordinate and Accounts Officer, Mumbai for the Mumbai/ Thane Rationing Area.
5. The above grant shall be provided to the concerned institution every 15 days through online RTGS mode after considering the information of Shiv Bhojan App as directed by the Government earlier. (e.g. payment for the period 26/01/2020 to 09/02/2020 on 10/02/2020 and for the period 10/02/2020 to 24/02/2020 on 25/02/2020. Since the payment of the institutions has to be done online, the bank account details of the institutions should be obtained and appropriate action should be taken.
