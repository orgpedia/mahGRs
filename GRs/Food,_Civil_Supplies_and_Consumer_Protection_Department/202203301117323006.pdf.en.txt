Introduction: As per the above-mentioned government decisions, Shiv Bhojan Thali has been made available to the poor and needy people of the state at a discounted price. During the financial year 2021-22, the Finance Department has allocated Rs. 150.00 crore and grant in aid of Rs. 90.00 crore out of the total sanctioned demand of Rs. 240.00 crore has been disbursed to the offices of District Supply Officers/ Food Distribution Officers and Deputy Controllers (Rationing).
The A Circle Office, Parel and E Circle Office, Wadala in the Mumbai region have sought additional funds to provide the pending Shiv Bhojan scheme payments. Some of the District Offices have allocated Rs. 3,62,30, 407/- has been returned to the budgeting system. Out of which, District Supply Office, Hingoli has received Rs. 95,00,000/- has been disbursed. A Circle Office, Parel, Mumbai Area has been allotted Rs. 29,00,000/- and to E Circle Office, Wadala Rs. 32,00,000/- out of total Rs. The matter of disbursement of Rs. 61,00,000/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In order to provide Shiv Bhojan Thali at concessional rates to the poor and needy persons, the offices of the District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer and Deputy Controller (Rationing) have been sanctioned a grant of Rs. 150 crore and Rs. 90 crore. 240 crore has been released by the Finance Department. 240,00,00, 000/- has already been disbursed, unutilized funds have been returned from some districts out of the disbursed grants. The disbursement of Rs. 61,00,000/- is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. Subject to all the terms and conditions of the Government Order and Circular mentioned above, the amount mentioned in Annexure-A is Rs. 61,00,000/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakh only) is being distributed.
(2) The grant shall be used for the purpose for which it is granted. This amount cannot be used for any other purpose.
(3) The accounts of the expenditure to be made out of the aforesaid grants shall be kept in the books of accounts in force and the utilization certificate of the payments made in that month after the payment is made from the treasury through your office in this regard shall be submitted to the Accountant General's Office and the Government by the 15th of the following month after the end of the month as per the standard and date.
4. Collector (District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer) at the district level to draw the amount as mentioned in the letter-A from the treasury.
