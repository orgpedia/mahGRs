Introduction: Shiv Bhojan Thali was distributed to the beneficiaries so that the poor and needy people do not have to suffer due to lack of food in the wake of strict restrictions imposed in the state under the process of" Break the Chain." It was initially approved for one month to be made available for free from April 15, 2021. This period has been extended from time to time. has expired on 14.07.2021. The proposal to extend the period was under the consideration of the government in view of the Covid 19 situation in the state.
The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government decision: from July 15, 2021. Shiv Bhojan Thali should be made available to the beneficiaries free of cost during the period from 14th September, 2021.
