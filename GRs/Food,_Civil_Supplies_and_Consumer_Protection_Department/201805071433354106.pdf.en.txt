In order to increase the income of all the authorized fair price shops in the state, the milk and milk products of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited, Mumbai (Mahananda) have been allowed to be sold in all the authorized fair price shops in the state. Further, as per the Government of Maharashtra's decision dated April 5, 2018, the distribution of milk and milk products produced under the Government Milk Scheme in the State of Maharashtra and Aarey brand milk and milk products has been allowed to be sold from the ration distribution centers in the State.
On the same lines, Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai has requested to allow the distribution of Noga produce for sale in all authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to allow the sale of Noga products from all the authorized fair price/ ration shops in the state.
Government Decision: In order to increase the income of fair price shop owners in the state, Noga products are being allowed to be put up for sale in all authorized fair price shops/ ration shops in the state.
The products of Noga Company will be distributed to fair price shops in the state through the company's distributors.
3. The fair price shop owners will have to contact the distributors of the schemes and the divisional office of the Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation for the commission received on the sale of the products of the Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation. The transaction will be between the Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai and the concerned fair price shop. The government will not interfere in it. Along with this, a list of distributors and divisional offices of Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation has been attached.
