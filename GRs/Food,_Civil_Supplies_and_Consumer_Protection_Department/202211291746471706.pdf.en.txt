Government's decision:
For the implementation of all the Centrally Sponsored Schemes to be implemented for updating the profile of Centrally Sponsored Schemes on the Scheme Information System (MP-SIMS), the Department is required to appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) by Government decision and open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for the scheme and appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as Nodal Officer for coordination of the scheme. Therefore, the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the following schemes are being appointed as per the decision of the said Government as mentioned in their front page:
