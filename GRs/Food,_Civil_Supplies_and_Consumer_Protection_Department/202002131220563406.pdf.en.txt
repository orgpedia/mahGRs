Preamble: The Price Monitoring Committee (PMC) has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 28.12.2015 to formulate an action plan for controlling the prices of urea/ tur, edible oil, edible oil seeds and other pulses in the event of an increase in average prices by more than 15% of the prices specified under the Essential Commodities Act, 1955.
The committee is headed by the Additional Chief Secretary (Agriculture and Farmers Welfare) and has Secretary (Finance) and Secretary, Agriculture Produce and Market Committee as members. Besides, the committee comprises of 1 Grahak Panchayat member and 2 members of NGOs (Grahak) (in rural areas of the state). Secretary (A.N.P.& Gr. Secretary) is the Member Secretary of the Committee. In pursuance of the above decision of the Government dated 3rd November, 2016, three non-official members have been appointed to the Price Control Committee.
As per the note dated 03.01.2020 of the Chief Secretary, instructions have been issued for the reconstitution of new Boards and Committees by cancelling the appointments of the members appointed on the Government/ Non-Government Boards and Committees established by various Acts/ Acts/ Rules under each Administrative Division. Accordingly, the matter of cancellation of the membership of non-official members on the price control committee was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The following Non-official Members who were appointed to the Committee on Price Control under the Government Decision dated 3rd November, 2016 are being disqualified from the membership of the Committee with effect from the date of this Order.
