Biodiesel for blending with high speed diesel for transportation purposes-2019. The notification 2. Released on April 30, 2019.
Accordingly, the All India Biodiesel Association, Mumbai has requested the government to allow the sale of biodiesel in the state by formulating an all-inclusive policy regarding the sale of biodiesel in the state on the lines of the central government notification. Also, the government has received complaints about the illegal opening of biodiesel outlets in many places in the state as there is no policy of the government regarding the sale of biodiesel. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to set a policy on the sale of biodiesel in the state to prevent the illegal sale of biodiesel in the state and to make biodiesel available in the state by making it easier for biodiesel manufacturers, sellers and suppliers to do business.
Accordingly, in exercise of all the powers conferred and empowered in this behalf under the provisions of clause 6 (a) 1 of the Motor Spirit and High Speed Diesel (Regulation of Supply, Distribution and Prevention of Malpractices) Order, 2005, the State Government is formulating a policy for the production, storage, supply and sale of bio-diesel for blending with high speed diesel for transportation purposes.

(2) Prohibition of manufacture, storage, supply and sale of biodiesel without registration.
No person shall manufacture, store, supply or sell Biodiesel (B-100) unless it is registered with the competent authority.
(3) An application for registration
Application for registration for manufacture, storage, supply and sale of Biodiesel (B-100) shall be made in the format specified in Annexure-I. The application can be made only after obtaining the certificate of registration/ permission/ no-objection mentioned in Annexure-2 for manufacturers and Annexure-3 for stockists, suppliers and sellers.
The application for registration as manufacturer, stockist, supplier or dealer of biodiesel shall be submitted to the Controller Rationing and Director, Civil Supplies, Mumbai in Mumbai-Thane rationing zone as the competent authority and to the concerned District Supply Officer/ Food Distribution Officer in other districts.
(4) The registration or refusal to register shall be made by the competent authority on receipt of a duly completed application for registration in the prescribed format and on payment of a fee of Rs. 1000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty only) and after making such enquiry as may be deemed necessary, complete proposal 2 to the Government along with the certificates of registration/ permission/ no-objection mentioned in Annexure-II or III, as the case may be, within a period of 1 to 2 weeks. The competent authority shall, with the prior approval of the Government, register or refuse registration as the case may be. (Form of Registration- Annexure- 4) Further, the competent authority or the Government, as the case may be, may refuse to register the applicant after giving him a reasonable opportunity of being represented and for reasons to be stated in writing.

This registration will be for the manufacture, storage, supply and sale of Biodiesel (B-100) only. No biodiesel mixed mixture can be sold to a registered person.
(5) The registration for manufacture, storage and sale of Biodiesel (B-100) shall be valid for a period of two years from the date of registration.
(6) Renewal of Registration The registrant desirous of renewing the registration shall, not less than 45 days before the expiry of the period of validity of the registration, pay a fee of Rs. Application for renewal in the prescribed format along with an annual renewal fee of Rs.500/- will have to be submitted to the competent authority.
The Competent Authority shall, on receipt of a duly completed application for renovation in the prescribed format and after payment of the renovation fee, submit a complete proposal 2 to the Government within 1 to 2 weeks after making such inquiry as it may deem necessary. The competent authority may, with the previous approval of the Government, renew or cancel the registration as the case may be.
Further, the competent authority or the Government, as the case may be, may refuse to grant renewal to the applicant after giving him a reasonable opportunity of being represented and for reasons to be stated in writing.
(7) Cancellation or suspension of registration The competent authority may, with the previous approval of the Government, suspend or cancel the registration if the registrant or agent or his servant or any person acting on his behalf does not comply with any directions or instructions issued by the Government or the competent authority.
Provided that a registration may be suspended or cancelled only after the registrant has been given a reasonable opportunity to present his case against the proposed action of cancellation or suspension.

(a) Keeping records and records
The registered biodiesel manufacturers, stockists, suppliers and dealers shall maintain a record of the production, storage, supply and sale of biodiesel along with the details of biodiesel supply and the test report of NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories) accredited laboratory. Also the biodiesel dealers will keep the details of at least 3 previous supplies received from the supplier. This list will be updated periodically. Also, the competent authority of the Central or State Government authorised for the purpose shall make available the records for inspection and examination, if so requested.
Registered biodiesel manufacturers, stockists, suppliers, dealers will maintain a register of each sale of biodiesel and the same will be updated on a daily basis. These records will be kept at the physical place of sale and also at the office.
Also, the quantity, price, date and time of the biodiesel sold at the time of each sale of biodiesel, including vehicle number, customer name and contact number.
A bill will be generated. A copy of this payment will be kept with the customer and a copy as a record.
