A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 and subject to the following conditions, the concerned Field Assistants, Group-C are being promoted to the vacant post of Inspector, Group-B Cadre on purely temporary basis.

2) There is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of this category of promotion, field assistant. Also, considering the fact that the seniority list of this cadre has not even been challenged, it is being given only in the form of temporary/ ad-hoc promotions.
(3) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers promoted as a result of this temporary promotion shall not be entitled to any regularisation.
