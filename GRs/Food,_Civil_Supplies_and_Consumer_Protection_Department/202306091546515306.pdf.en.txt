Provisional promotion of the said Inspector to the post of Assistant Controller is being made subject to the following conditions:

2) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned officer in this post.
3) The promotions to the post of Assistant Controller of Qualified Inspector, Valid Metrology for the year 2022-23 are provisional. As a result, the selected officers will not be entitled to any regular post and seniority in service.
4) These promotions are temporary in nature and will be regularized only as per the posts available in the promoted quota.
