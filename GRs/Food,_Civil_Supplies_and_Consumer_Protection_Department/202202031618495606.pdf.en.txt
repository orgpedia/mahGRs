The Controller, Rationing and Director, Civil Supplies, Mumbai is working under the control of the D Circle at S.K.No. 26-D, Andheri. He passed away on 15.04.2021.
2. The Controller, Rationing and Director, Civil Supplies, Mumbai, has appointed the said deceased Rationing Officer as its Commissioner.
