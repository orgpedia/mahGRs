As on 14.02.2024, the Drawing and Disbursing Officers and other non-executive authorities have been issued Model No. It has been approved to implement the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA) system and methodology for utilization of funds drawn under the VF (Object Head) from the Consolidated Fund of the State for 27-small constructions, 31-subsidized grants (non-salaried), 33-financed, 35-subsidized for creation of capital asset, 50-other expenses, 53-large constructions and other purposes as may be declared by the Finance Department from time to time. As per the provision in this Government Decision 11. 1, the Government was contemplating to declare as "Virtual Personal Deposit Accounts Administrator" (VPDA) the Deposit Accounts Officer in the Subordinate Regional Office of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, as the Regional Deposit and Disbursement Officers and other Non-Governmental Implementing Authorities would be competent to declare as Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrators with the concurrence of the Department of Finance for utilization of the Supplementary Grants Fund to be drawn from the Consolidated Fund of the State.
Government Decision: - The Drawing and Disbursing Officer mentioned in Annexure A, B, C, FS attached to the Sub-Regional Office of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection is being declared as the "Virtual Deposit Personal Accounting Administrator" (VPDA) for utilization of the Supplementary Grants Fund to be drawn from the Consolidated Fund of the State under 27-Small Constructions, 31-Supplementary Grants (Non-Salary), 33-Finance, 35-Grant for appointment of Capital Grants, 50-Other Expenditure, 52, Machinery, 53-Small Constructions and such other purposes as may be declared by the Department of Finance from time to time and is being permitted to open a Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA) at the Subordinate Accounts Office, Mumbai or the concerned District Treasury Office, as the case may be. The Drawing and Disbursement Officer, Accountant General, (Lt. b. a) To obtain an authorization from the office to act directly as a Personal Deposit Accounting Administrator, the Accountant General, (Lt. and a) submit the proposal to the office and the Accountant General, (Lt. b. a) Such authority shall obtain the letter forthwith in due coordination with the office. The Virtual Deposit Accounting Administrator Index will be issued by the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai after the authorization letter is commissioned by the Accountant General, (Lt & A), with the respective Drawing and Disbursing Officers and their office heads being personally responsible for the priority completion of the proceedings.
