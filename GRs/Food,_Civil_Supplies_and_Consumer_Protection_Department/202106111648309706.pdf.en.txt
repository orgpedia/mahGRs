The Proposal:
The request for creation of two notional posts of Drivers on the establishment of Controller, Valid Metrology, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and sanction for taking over the services of these posts by external agencies has been made by the Controller, Valid Metrology, Mumbai vide letter dated 5 th February, 2021. Accordingly, the Government was considering the creation of notional posts of two drivers for the Controller, Valid Metrology, Mumbai and the Government's approval to make the services of these posts available on contract basis through external agencies. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the creation of two notional posts of Drivers on the establishment of Controller, Valid Metrology, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and making available the services of these posts on contract basis through external agencies for a period upto the finalization of the revised pattern of the said office or one year whichever is earlier. Drivers who wish to work after retiring from government service and are physically capable should also be considered while taking a driver from an external agency.
