Action to be taken against the erring officials/ employees responsible for irregularities and lapses in the distribution of ration cards.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Corrigendum No.Shivap-2019/ P.No.38/ NAPU-28
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date is November 9, 2020
Read: Govt. VIPA-2013/ Q.No.8/ NAPU-28 dated 26.08.2013
Government's stand:
Instructions have been issued vide Government Circular dated 26.08.2013 to take action against the erring officials/ employees responsible for irregularities and lapses in the distribution of ration cards under the Targeted Public Distribution System in the State. The proposal to amend the suspension order was under consideration as it was inconsistent with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979 and the Departmental Inquiry Rules, 1991. Accordingly, the Government vide circular No. 6 is being improved.
