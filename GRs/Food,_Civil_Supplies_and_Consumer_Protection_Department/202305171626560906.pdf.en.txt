The Proposal:
Government Decision, Finance Department. Relaxation of restriction on recruitment as on 31.10.2022 has been approved to fill up 100% of the vacancies in the direct service quota in the departments where the revised pattern has been finalised. Accordingly, with the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, the Government of the Department took the decision. As on 18.4.2022, the revised pattern has been fixed after reviewing the posts in all the offices under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
General Administration Department As per the Government decision dated 21.11.2022, direct recruitment to the posts in the direct service quota in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) will be done by conducting competitive examinations. I& O BPS has been approved to be taken online through this company.
Accordingly, the process of filling up the vacant posts of Supply Inspectors in the Supply Branch and High Level Clerks under the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary through the IBPS Company and the appointment of the Coordinating Officer to carry out the recruitment process was under the consideration of the Government.
