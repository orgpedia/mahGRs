Due to the outbreak of the Corona virus, the workers, migrants, homeless and out-of-town students, etc. are suffering from lack of food. The cost of a Shiv Bhojan thali is Rs 5. For a period of three months from 30.03.2020, Rs. 5/- has been fixed. This period is No. 7 here. Circular dated 01.10.2020 is hereby extended till further orders.
The price of Shiv Bhojan thali. upto 31.03.2021 Rs. 5/- to be made by the Cabinet. It has been approved in the meeting dated 29.10.2020. According to the As on 31.03.2021, the cost of Shiv Bhojan Thali is Rs. 5/- will be enough. Additional grants will be made available in the near future.
The concerned Deputy Controller of Ration, District Supply Officer, Food Distribution Officer, Tehsildar should immediately bring the matter to the notice of the eatery operators and give wide publicity to the matter.
