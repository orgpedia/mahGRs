Introduction: In order to ensure that the subsidy given under the targeted public distribution system reaches the beneficiaries, the State Government has decided to implement the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) project notified by the Central Government initially in 2 shops in the Mumbai-Thane rationing area. This has been done in accordance with the Government Decision dated 21.8.2018. Also the. Guidelines for implementation of the scheme have been issued vide circular dated 29.9.2018.
In order to give an expanded look to the above project, it was under the consideration of the government to implement the project in 4 new shops including the 2 shops currently in operation of DBT in the Mumbai-Thane rationing area. The following decisions are being taken:
Government's decision:
Under the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT-Cash) project, the pilot project will be implemented in a total of 6 shops including two fair price shops 1/ A/ 106/ Azad Maidan and 11/ A/ 61/ Mahalaxmi in Circle A of the Mumbai-Thane rationing area. The Proof of Concept (PoC) will be implemented from October 2019 to April 2020. After the assessment of this PoC, it will be considered to extend the period for the next six months.
2. DBT (Cash) of Fair Price Shops covered under the project as per the survey conducted by J-PAL during the month of October, 2019.
