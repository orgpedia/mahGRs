Government's decision:
As per the above mentioned Government decision, temporary posts have been created in various cadres in the Controller, Validation System and these posts have been extended from time to time.
In pursuance of the above order dated 16/3/2018, a total of 210 temporary posts at Mumbai and district level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 1/3/2018 to 30/9/2018.
In pursuance of the above order dated 7/9/2018 dated 1/10/2018 to 28/2/2019 (five months), a total of 210 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A annexed to this Government decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation and Statistics are being sanctioned by the Government subject to the conditions mentioned below.
(1) None of the proposed 210 posts shall remain vacant for a period of more than six months.
2) Salary expenditure on these posts should be met from the approved grant for the financial year 2018-2019.
3) The posts proposed are in the revised pattern and hence there will be no change in the revised pattern or cadre.
(4) The assessment shall be carried out annually.
The High Level Committee has unanimously approved the review of the posts in the establishment of various heads of departments under this department. Accordingly, the Government Decision dated 26/6/2003 at serial number 1 has been issued to declare the revised figure.
