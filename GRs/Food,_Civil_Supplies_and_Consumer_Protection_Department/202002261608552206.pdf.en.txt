Preamble: Shri Rajendra Narayansa Barad, Civil Judge, Senior Level as Manager (Law) on deputation at Parikrama Bench, Nagpur Establishment of State Consumer Redressal Commission vide Reference No. The appointment was made as per the Government Order No. 1. The High Court, Bombay has recommended the appointment of Mr. Bhimrao Tulsiram Yengade, 8th Joint Judicial Judge Senior Level and Additional Chief Magistrate, Nagpur on deputation to his vacant post as he has been posted as District Judge-3 and Assistant Sessions Judge. As per the recommendation of the High Court, the State Commission has requested for the posting of Mr. Bhimrao Tulsiram Yengade to the vacant post of Manager (Law) vide letter No. 2 above.
Therefore, the matter of posting of Mr. Bhimrao Tulsiram Yengade on deputation to the vacant post of Manager (Law) at Parikrama Bench, Nagpur of State Consumer Redressal Commission was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Parikrama Bench of the State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, Nagpur has recommended the appointment of an officer from the Judicial Service to the vacant post of Manager (Law) on the establishment. the High Court of Bombay. the State Commission vide letter dated 22.11.2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the State Consumer Redressal Commission to the Government, the following deputation is being posted on the vacant post of Manager (Law) at the Parikrama Bench, Nagpur Establishment of the State Consumer Redressal Commission.
