Preamble- The Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection vide the aforesaid Reference No. 1 As per the Government decision mentioned herein, it has been decided to implement the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Distribution of Fortified Rice through Public Distribution System" in Gadchiroli district of the State on pilot basis in 2020-21 and 2021-22 with financial assistance (75:25) from the Centre and the State.
As per the revised methodology on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) shall be appointed by the concerned administrative department for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and a separate single bank account shall be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme. In addition, the Finance Department has also issued Reference No. 1 regarding the appointment of an officer of at least Deputy Secretary rank as Nodal Officer for coordination of the scheme. 3 Government circulars have been issued. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the implementation and coordination of the said Centrally Sponsored Scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Reference No. 3 As per the instructions given in the Government Circular here, the Government is approving the appointment of Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer as follows for the implementation and coordination of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme" Fortification of Rice and its Distribution under Public Distribution System" being implemented on a pilot basis in Gadchiroli district by the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
