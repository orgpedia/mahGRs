Government circular:
The Government has been receiving a number of complaints from the people of the State that the fair price/ ration shops are not distributing these consumer items on time, regularly and on a daily basis, despite the fact that the various consumer goods distributed under the Public Distribution System are required to be distributed among the people within the prescribed time. In order to prevent this, the government has issued necessary instructions to fair price shops from time to time.
The circular provides the following information in the public interest for the people of the state to get the consumer goods within the prescribed time frame/ limit and the necessary instructions to be given to the fair price/ ration shops.
The instructions given by the government regarding fair price/ ration shops for the welfare of the people of the state.
Government Circular, A.N.P. and Gr. No.-Savivya- 1002/384/ Q. No.900/ NAPU 28, Dy. 21.02.2002.
To ensure smooth distribution of consumer goods to the people of the state through fair price/ ration shops, fair price/ ration shops should be kept open for 4 hours in the morning and 4 hours in the afternoon. The exact timings in this regard should be decided by the Upper District Collector/ Controller Rationing, Mumbai, as per the local situation.
