The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Circular No: Asthapra-1319/ No. 267/ No. 15,
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is November 26, 2019
circular
Provisional seniority list showing the status as on 01/01/2019 of Assistant District Supply Officers/ Assistant Foodgrain Distribution Officers/ Technical Officers in the Subordinate Supply Offices of the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection is being published vide circular.
The concerned Divisional Commissioner is requested to publicize these seniority lists properly and bring them to the notice of all the concerned officers. In case of objections of the concerned officers regarding the said seniority lists, the concerned officers shall submit the same to the Government through the Heads of Departments (with their comments by the Heads of Departments) along with appropriate evidence/ certificate in support of their objections within 15 days from the date of this circular.
Objections not received within the above prescribed time will not be considered and this seniority list will be finalized.
