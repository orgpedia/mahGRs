Government's decision:
The District Consumer Redressal Commission, Aurangabad and State Consumer Redressal Commission, Maharashtra, Mumbai Regional Bench, Aurangabad have jointly constructed a building for the purpose at an estimated cost of Rs. 8,43,42, 661/-. Administrative approval has been granted. By the end of March 2022, Rs. 7,99,90, 930/- has been provided so far. Hence, the administrative sanction of Rs. 43,51,731/- was undistributed.
The State Consumer Redressal Commission, Mumbai has sanctioned the balance amount of Rs. 43,51,731/- is sought to be disbursed. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 43,51,731/- is being disbursed during the financial year 2022-23.
The expenditure is to be met from the grants sanctioned during the year 2022-2023 under the head Demand No.M.5, Head of Accounts, 5475, Capital Expenditure on Other General Financial Services, 102, Civil Supplies, (00) (01) Construction of Building for Consumer Forum (54750239), Programme 53, Major Constructions.
4. Since the funds have been made available for this work so far, the completion report and utilization certificate of the work should be submitted to the government.
