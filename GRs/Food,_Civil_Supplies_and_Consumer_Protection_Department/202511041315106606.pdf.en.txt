Proposal
The Central Government has, vide letter No. 9 of the Reference, provided for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through SNA-SPARSH for effective fund distribution of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. As per the Government Decision No. 2, the National Food Security Scheme of the Department ((55-4048 - Assistance to State Agencies for Intra State Movement Handling of Food Grains and FPS Dealers Margin under NFSA) & (SLS-MH 505-MH - Revision of norms for Central Assistance Released to States / UTs for meeting expenditure on Intra-State Movement Handling of Food Grains and FPS Dealers Margin under NFSA) is the underlying HO for implementation through SNA-SPARSH. Accordingly, the National Food Security Scheme is being implemented through the SNA-SPARSH system since February 2025.
Under the National Food Security Scheme, the funds received from the Central Government in the year 2025-26, as referred to in A.No. 2 & 4, are general, scheduled castes and scheduled tribes category-wise.
For the National Food Security Scheme, the government is considering taking up 2 separate heads of accounts for expenditure and separate heads of accounts for collection - Central share, State share and additional State share for SCs.
Accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: - A new head of account is being opened for the incidental expenditure for SC category under the National Food Security Scheme as follows.
