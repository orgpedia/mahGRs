Regarding constitution of" Task Force" for finalization of pending accounts of Paddy/ Millets/ Wheat from 2005-06 to 2016-17.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection
Government Resolution No: Parichay- 2618/ No. 164/ No. 29
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry (Extension), Mumbai-400032.
Date: July 24, 2018
Preamble Under the Minimum Support Price (MSP) procurement scheme, a large amount of funds are required for procurement and further management of paddy/ coarse cereals and reimbursement of expenditure incurred. Since 2005-06 to 2016-17, the accounts of paddy/ coarse grains/ wheat from both the agent agencies are pending and a large amount of funds of the State is lying with the Central Government. Since these accounts are pending, it is necessary to complete the pending accounts at the earliest and submit them to the Central Government to receive the funds before the next season begins. Therefore, the matter of setting up a" Task Force" to finalize the pending accounts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Since the accounts of Paddy/ Millets/ Wheat from 2005-06 to 2016-17 are pending with both the Agent Agencies, it is necessary to complete and submit the pending accounts to the Central Government at the earliest before the commencement of the next season (2018-19) to get the funds stuck with the Central Government. The Government hereby approves the constitution of a" Task Force" to finalize these pending accounts.
Manoj Sharma- Manager (Services), Tribal Development Corporation, Nashik- Member 4) Mr. V.U. Yadav- Assistant Director, Financial Advisor and Deputy Secretary Office- Member Secretary In addition to this, Financial Advisor and Deputy Secretary Office and both the agents should provide the necessary manpower to complete the process immediately.
