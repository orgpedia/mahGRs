Government Circular: Under the National Food Security Act, 2013, the implementation of the food security scheme is under the control of the Central Government. The letter dated 31.05.2022 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of fund allocation.
2. Distribution of funds for Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, as per the instructions issued vide circular dated 01.07.2021, the following Single Nodal Agency (SNA) is being approved for appointment of Nodal Officer for coordination of Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented by Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection and for implementation and utilization of Public Finance Management System (PFMS) for disbursement of funds under National Food Security Act, 2013:
