Introduction: The above reference No. 2 The revised figure of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department vide Government Resolution dated 18.04.2022 has fixed the prescribed figure after reviewing the posts in the valid metrology system. A total of 661 posts have been sanctioned for valid metrology system and the remaining 283 posts have been declared dead in the category of driver/ driver (heavy)/ crane driver/ peon/ naik/ hamal.
In pursuance of the above Government decision, 183 temporary posts out of the existing posts at Mumbai and District level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023.
The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 175 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A annexed to this Government Decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation, are presently being extended from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024 (six months). The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head" Demand No., M-3, Main Account Head 3475, Other General Financial Services (106), Regulation of Weights and Measures (00) (01) Regulation of Weights and Measures, (34750093)" and shall be met from the approved grants for the financial year 2023-2024.
