The Proposal:
The above 1, vide Government Resolution dated 26/6/2003, has approved the revised pattern of posts in the establishment of various Heads of Departments under this Department.
These include posts under Controller, Valid Metrology.
In pursuance of the above decision, 210 temporary posts out of the total sanctioned 940 posts at Mumbai and District level establishments under the Controller, Validation, Maharashtra State, Mumbai were extended for the period from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021.
The proposal to extend the deadline was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision
A total of 210 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A annexed to this Government decision on the establishment of Controller, Validation Sciences are being extended from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 (six months).
The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head" Demand No., M-3, Main Account Head 3475, Other General Financial Services (106), Regulation of Weights and Measures (00) (01) Regulation of Weights and Measures, (34750093)" and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2021-22.
