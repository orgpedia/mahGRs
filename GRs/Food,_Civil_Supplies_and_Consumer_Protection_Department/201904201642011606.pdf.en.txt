Government Circular- Final Seniority List for the category of Selector Inspector in the Validation System as on 1st January, 2011 vide No. 2 By order of the Office of the Controller, Validation, the details have been published in accordance with the procedures.
The above No. 2. 1 The Government has decided to have a comprehensive policy regarding the seniority of the officers/ employees in the government service. As per the said Government decision, after the publication of the final seniority of a cadre, while publishing the provisional seniority list for the next year, it is necessary to publish the provisional additional seniority by including only the officers and employees newly appointed in the said cadre in the prescribed manner. However, no fresh appointment has been made in the cadre of Inspector of Selection in the Validation System during the period from 1st January, 2011 to 31st December, 2018. Accordingly, without publishing the provisional seniority list for the cadre of Observer Category as on 1st January, 2019, the final seniority list is being published directly and is annexed herewith as Annexure-A to this Circular.
3. The said seniority list has been prepared to determine the respective seniority of the officers who are working in the cadre of Inspector of Selection on regular basis. The seniority list has been prepared in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Qualification) Rules, 1982.
4. The Controller, Validation should inform the officers of the cadre of Selection Inspector to obtain a copy of the said seniority list from the website.
