The Proposal:
The criteria and procedure for the distribution of triple ration cards to citizens in the state have been fixed in accordance with the government decisions in 1, 2 and 3 above. Under the targeted public distribution system in the state, the government has given the above No. Permissible ration cards are issued to eligible families under the criteria set by the government in 1, 2 and 3. Guidelines regarding care and caution to be taken while issuing new ration cards. 4 decisions have been made. Orphans are not able to meet all the conditions in the existing norms for ration cards. Therefore, a proposal to fix criteria for distribution of ration cards to orphans was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: As per the National Food Security Act, 2013, after receiving the application from the orphans for a new ration card, a new permissible ration card should be issued according to the following criteria and the benefits that are permissible on the ration card should be given.
1) Orphans up to the age of 28 years should be given benefits under the Priority Family Scheme by distributing Temporary Yellow (BPL) ration cards. Orphans above the age of 28 years should be given ration cards and benefits as per their income.
