Introduction: The National Food Security Act, 2013 has come into force in the State of Maharashtra with effect from 1st February, 2014. Section 16 (1) of the said Act provides for the constitution of a State Food Commission for the purpose of control and review of the implementation of the Act.
Accordingly, the State Food Commission has been constituted vide Government Order dated 11th April, 2017.
Under reference As per the provisions of Section 16 (2) of the National Food Security Act, 2013 vide Government Order dated 2.5.2017, Shri. Supriya Sunil Khairnar has been appointed as Member, State Food Commission in the category of Scheduled Castes and Shri Homraj Ananda Koli has been appointed as Member, State Food Commission in the category of Scheduled Tribes.
Section 16 (4) of the National Food Security Act, 2013 provides for the following:
The Chairman and every other member shall hold office for such period not exceeding five years from the date on which he enters upon his office and shall be eligible for reappointment, however, no person shall hold office as Chairman or other member after he has attained the age of sixty-five years. "
