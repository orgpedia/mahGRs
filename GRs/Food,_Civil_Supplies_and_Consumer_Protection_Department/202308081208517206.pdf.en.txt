The Proposal:
As per the revised guidelines of the Government of India dated 19.08.2021 for expeditiously sending the market prices collected by the Market Price Monitoring Cells under the 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Strengthening of Price Monitoring Cell" and for strengthening/ upgrading the quality of the Control Room, the Central Government has made provision for appointment of Data Entry Operator on contract basis for each of the Market Price Collection Centres in the State and for purchase of smart phones and SIM cards/ data cards. As per this provision, subject-wise provision is being made available under this scheme as per the decision of the next government.
Government's decision:
Central Government Reference No. 2 Under the scheme" Strengthening of Price Control Rooms," as per Annexure A for the period 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24, a total amount of Rs. 87,74,000/- (Rupees Eighty Seven Lakhs only) has been collected for nineteen Market Price Collection Centres at Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune, Nashik, Dharani-Amravati, Mangaon-Raigad, Latur, Akluj-Solapur, Rahuri-Ahmednagar, Khultabad-Aurangabad, Chandrapur, Karad-Satara, Arni-Yavatmal, Kankavali-Sindhudurg, Jalgaon, Islampur-Sangli, Partur-Jalna, Akola and Gondia in the State. Approval is being given to distribute the funds to the concerned through the" Single Nodal Agency (SNA)" Financial Advisor and Deputy Secretary, Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department. The said expenditure shall be made out of the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts below.

As per the guidelines dated 19.08.2021, the Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary, Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, under the Controller, Rationing and Director, Civil Supplies, Mumbai, and District Supply Officers and Food Distribution Officers, is mandated to submit the Utilization Certificate GFR 2017 (GFR 2017) in the prescribed format and Financial Progress Certificate, Annexure-III in the prescribed format to the Central Government and the State Government within the stipulated time, based on the details of expenditure out of the funds disbursed to the respective Market Price Collection Centres.
3. Office of the Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary, Controller Rationing and Director Civil Supplies, Mumbai and the concerned District Supply Officer and Food Distribution Officer. As per the Guidelines dated 19th August, 21, para. 3.2 The terms and conditions are strictly adhered to.
Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary, Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection have been directed to disburse the amount of honorarium and SIM card/ data card of the data entry operator approved by the Central Government by March 2024. This amount should be paid in full to the concerned centers by July-2023 and should be disbursed to the account of the concerned District Supply Officer or Food Distribution Officer on a monthly basis by March-2024. Also, the expenditure should be coordinated with the Accountant General's office, follow the prescribed procedure and submit a report to the central government and the state government.
The said Order is contained in the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division-1, Sequence No. 6, Rule No. This is being done in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative department under the provision of section 40 (1).
