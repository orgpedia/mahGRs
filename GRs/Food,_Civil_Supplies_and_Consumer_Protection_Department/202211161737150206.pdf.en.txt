The memo:
The procedure has been prescribed under the Government Decision No. 1 under the General Administration Department regarding the review of the eligibility-ineligibility test of the Inspector of Valid Metrology System, (Group-B), Gazetted Officer beyond the age of 50% 55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service.
The Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the order of Reference No. 2 to review the Group-A and Group-B cadre officers of the Controller, Valid Metrology System under the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
Pursuant to the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee in its meeting held on 21.10.2022, a total of 86 officers in the cadre of Inspector, Valid Metrology, as mentioned in the attached form, are being approved to continue their services beyond the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
The service book of the officers mentioned in the attached form should be taken on record that they have been approved to continue their service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completing 30 years of qualifying service.
