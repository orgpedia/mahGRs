As per the provisions of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986, State Consumer Redressal Commission at the State level and District Consumer Redressal Forum at the District level have been set up. Also, in pursuance of the amendments made by the Central Government in the Consumer Protection Act, 2002, the State Commission has been constituted with Parikrama Benches (Circuit Benches) at Nagpur and Aurangabad respectively. With the increase in the functioning of the State Commission, the District Forum, a total of 497 temporary posts of officers/ staff have been created for the State Commission and the offices of the Nagpur and Aurangabad Parikrama Benches of the Commission and the District Forum from time to time.
Reference No. 2 of the above Finance Department. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Department under the Government Order No. 1, 497 temporary posts of the State Consumer Redressal Commission and its subordinate offices have been created. Reference No. 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 The deadline was extended by the government.
Reference No. 3 of the above Finance Department. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Department under Government Order No.3, a total of 497 temporary posts on the establishment of State Consumer Redressal Commissions and their subordinate offices are hereby approved for extension for the period from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 as indicated in Annexure A annexed to this Government Order, subject to the following conditions:
(1) None of the 497 posts proposed to be created shall be vacant for a period of more than six months.
2) The cost of salary for these posts should be met from the approved grant for the financial year 2020-2021.
