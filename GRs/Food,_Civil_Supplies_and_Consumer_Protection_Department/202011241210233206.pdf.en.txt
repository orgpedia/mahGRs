The Proposal:
In the wake of Corona virus, the proposal to distribute sugar at the rate of 1 kg per family for the month of November, 2020 to the beneficiaries of priority families of the state as well as APL (Keshari) farmers in 14 districts on the occasion of Diwali festival and the expenses incurred for it has been approved. This has been approved in the Cabinet Meeting dated 05.11.2020. Accordingly, the following orders have been issued:
Government Decision: The decision approves distribution of sugar at the rate of 1 kg per family per month for the month of November, 2020 to the beneficiaries of priority households and APL (Keshari) farmers in 14 districts of the State.
