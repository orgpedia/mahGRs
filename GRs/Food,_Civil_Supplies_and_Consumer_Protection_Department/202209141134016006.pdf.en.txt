The Deputy Controller Rationing, Head Office, Mumbai vide Reference No. 2 The aforesaid letter is requested to consider the period of suspension of Mr. Pandurang Varadkar, Retd. The Metropolitan Magistrate, 17th Court, Borivali, Mumbai vide his order dated 20/7/2015 has acquitted Mr. Varadkar of the said offence. Also Ma.
