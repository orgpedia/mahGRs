Government Circular: The process of" Break the Chain" has started across the state. As stated above. As per the Government decision dated 15 th April, 2021, the Government has approved to make Shiv Bhojan Thali available free of cost for one month beyond 15 th April, 2021. Strict restrictions have been imposed in the state under the process of" Break the Chain." Accordingly, the time was given to provide Shiv Bhojan thali free of cost to the beneficiaries so that the poor and needy people do not have to suffer for lack of food. The proposal was under consideration of the government to extend it for a further period of one month with effect from May 15, 2021. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government decision: The Shiv Bhojan Thali has been made available to the beneficiaries free of cost for a period of one month from May 15, 2021. During this period, the number of Shiv Bhojan thalis in the entire state has been increased to 2.00 lakh per day. The deadline May 15, 2021
