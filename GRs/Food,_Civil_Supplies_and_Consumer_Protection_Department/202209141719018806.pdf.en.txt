GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: DAKSHAS-1007/ PR No. 548/ NAPU 21, dated 23rd January, 2008.
In order to ensure public participation in monitoring the PDS, the Government has issued orders under the Government Decision dated 23/01/2008 for the formation of Vigilance Committees at the village, taluk, municipality, municipal and district levels in the State to monitor the distribution of essential commodities distributed through the PDS. There is a provision for appointment of non-official members in these Vigilance Committees and the tenure of non-official members is three years from the date of appointment or until the appointment of new non-official members in their place, whichever is earlier.
A proposal was under consideration of the government to reconsider the appointments of all non-official members appointed to all undertakings/ corporations/ boards/ committees/ authorities under the administrative department. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government has decided to cancel the appointments of non-official members on the vigilance committees formed at the village, taluka, municipality, municipal corporation and district levels in the state from the date of this order.
