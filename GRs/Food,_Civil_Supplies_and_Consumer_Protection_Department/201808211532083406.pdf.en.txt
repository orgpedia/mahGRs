the prelude
Construction of new godown at Waki G. Pune for storage of foodgrains under Public Distribution System. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 13.01.2013. The District Supply Officer, Pune, had informed that the works required to make the godown convenient for use were not included in the original budget approved by the administration, which included internal roads, toilets, security guard room, water tank, porter's rest house, godown office. Accordingly, administrative approval has been given to the budget of internal roads, toilets, security guard room, water tank, porter's rest house, godown office in Waki G. Pune. The administrative approval was given. The matter of disbursing funds for the construction of the premises of the government warehouse in Khed was under the consideration of the government.
