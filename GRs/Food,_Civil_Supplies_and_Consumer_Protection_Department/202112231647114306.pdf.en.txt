(a) The officer/ employee. The officers/ employees who have been elevated to the top of the service merit list by availing the benefit of reservation in promotion as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004. If they joined the Government service on or before 25.05.2004, they will be eligible to apply. Those who have joined Government service after 25.05.2004 will be eligible for further promotion as per the original service merit of their entry into service.
(d) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the result of this temporary promotion will not entitle the officers of the select list to any regularity and superannuation.
