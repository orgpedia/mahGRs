No.1480/ NAPU-16B, dated 02.01.2018 10) of District Supply Officer, Raigad vide letter No. supply/ supply. 3/ Mangaon Warehouse Repair Fund/ 489392/2017, Dy. 18.12.2017 Preamble Under the Public Distribution System, essential commodities are distributed to the ration card holders in the state. The food grains received from the Food Corporation of India are stored in the godowns of the state government. The proposal for the repair of these state government warehouses has been given administrative approval after due scrutiny by the Public Works Department. Funds were provided for the repair works of the godowns as per the decision of the Government dated 11.02.2015. However, no funds were allocated for the project in 2014-15. Therefore, the District Supply Officer Raigad has demanded the remaining necessary funds considering the expenses as per the administrative approval before this. The matter of disbursing funds for the repair works of the godowns and providing funds for the repair works of the godowns which were given administrative approval in the year 2017-18 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Subject to the conditions set out in the order granting administrative approval for the repair of government warehouses, it is being approved to provide funds as indicated in the following statement.

2.The repair works of the government godowns provided with funds as above should be started and completed immediately. After the warehouse repair is started, the district supply officer should first review the complete details of the repair and the related repair every month and submit the progress of the repair to the government.
3. The District Supply Officer should take care to submit the utility certificate to the government as soon as the repair work of the government godown is completed.
4. The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Supply Officer concerned shall jointly prepare a site inspection report after completion of the repair work of the said godown to ensure that the work is done as per the approved budget and it should be preserved in the records.
5. Care should be taken to ensure that there is no increase in the cost of repairing the warehouse due to delay in repair or other reasons. Note that the said funds cannot be transferred/ kept as a deposit
6. The cost of repair of the godown during the financial year 2017-18 should be spent under the head Demand No. M-2, Head Account Head 2408 Food Storage and Warehousing, (03) Procurement Distribution and Price Control (03) (02) Mufassal, (Non-Plan) (Dattamat) (2408 0108) 27 Small Constructions.
Similarly, if the funds provided for the repair of the warehouse are not likely to be spent for some reason, then a report should be submitted to the government with the reasons.
