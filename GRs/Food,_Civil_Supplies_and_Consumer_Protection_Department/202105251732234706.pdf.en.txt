The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the Central Government's Consumer Protection (Qualification for appointment, method of recruitment, procedure of appointment, term of office, resignation and removal of the President and Members of the State Commission and District Commission) Rules, 2020 under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, the Government has decided to recommend to the State Government for appointment of Chairman and Members of the State Consumer Redressal Commission and District Consumer Redressal Commission. A three-member selection committee has been constituted with effect from November 6, 2020. As per the rules, the state government has invited applications from eligible and interested candidates for filling up 33 posts of chairman and members of the state commission and district commission. Further necessary action in accordance with the applications received is required to be taken immediately by the selection committee. However, due to the recent surge in Covid-19 cases in the state. The selection committee members nominated by the Chief Secretary, Principal Secretary, Medical Education are busy in the prioritization of that post. Hence, they are unable to give the required time for the selection committee's work. Therefore, instead of the Principal Secretary, Medical Education, the departments which do not have COVID-19 related work, such departments
Selection Committee of Secretaries. Request the Chief Secretary to re-nominate a representative for this. the Chief Secretary was appointed. Accordingly, the proposal to change the member of the selection committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Government Decision dated 06.04.2020 to recommend to the State Government for appointment of Chairman and Members of the State Consumer Redressal Commission and District Consumer Redressal Commission.
