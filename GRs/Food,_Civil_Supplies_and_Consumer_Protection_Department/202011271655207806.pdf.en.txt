Introduction: As it is not possible to hold women's gram sabhas due to the outbreak of the Covid-19 epidemic in the state, the government decision of this department, D. The condition of approval of Mahila Gram Sabha as per para 4 (ii) (e) of the guidelines issued under 6.7.2017 regarding approval of Fair Price Shops/ Ration Distribution Shops and Kerosene Licenses cannot be fulfilled. Proposals in this regard are being sent to the state government. Accordingly, the proposal to remove the condition in the government decision was under consideration.
The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decisions: Government Decisions, Food and Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department. The detailed programme and procedure for approval of Fair Price Shops has been laid down as on 6.7.2017.
Paragraph 4 (i) (e) thereof includes the condition of approval of the Gram Sabha. In the wake of the current Covid 19 pandemic, the Rural Development Department As per the circular dated 12.5.2020, holding of Gram Sabha has been temporarily suspended. At present, gram sabhas are not being held for the above purpose.
Hence, the government took this decision. The condition of sending the proposal to the Gram Sabha for consideration or recommendation while approving fair price shops for the year 6.7.2017 is being relaxed till further orders.
