The reference No. The process of unlocking has been started in the state by making five levels of the state under 6. Accordingly, the following instructions are being issued regarding Shiv Bhojan centers.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Level 1         | Level 2             | The Level 3         | Level 4 and Level 5 |
| Format          | As always           | 50% of the capacity | 50% of the capacity | Parcel facility only |
| Time            | 11.00 am to 4.00 pm | 11.00 am to 4.00 pm | 11.00 am to 4.00 pm | 11.00 am to 4.00 pm  |
| Parcel facility | No                  | No                  | No                  | Yes                  |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In addition
1) Wherever the parcel facility is operational, Shiv Bhojan should be made available to the customers through the take away facility from the Shiv Bhojan center by following the rules of disinfection at the Shiv Bhojan center.
2) It should be ensured that no beneficiary goes back without Shiv Bhojan during this time.
3) It should be ensured that the Shiv Bhojan center is not closed without any valid reason.
4) The entire Shiv Bhojan center should be disinfected daily.
5) It will be mandatory for the staff of the Shiv Bhojan center as well as the customer who comes to collect the parcel to use the mask.

6) Social distancing norms should be strictly followed at Shiv Bhojan centres.
7) Employees preparing and distributing Shiv Bhojan should wash their hands with soap frequently.
8) The driver of the Shiv Bhojan center should take care that the photos of the beneficiaries with false names are not put on the Shiv Bhojan app.
9) All Shiv Bhojan centers should be strictly inspected at least once this month by the supply system.
All the deputy controllers of ration, district supply officers, food distribution officers and tehsildars should immediately bring this matter to the notice of the Shiv Bhojan center operators and give it wide publicity.
