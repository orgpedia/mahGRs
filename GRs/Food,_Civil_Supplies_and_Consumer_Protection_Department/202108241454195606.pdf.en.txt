Government Decision: Reference No. 2 As per the instructions issued by the Central Government under the NAMUDA letter, to address the problems of malnutrition and anaemia in the State, the distribution of fortified rice under the Integrated Child Development Scheme (ICDS) and Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDM) in collaboration with the Central Department of Women and Child Development and School Education respectively, as well as distribution of fortified rice under the Public Distribution System (PDS), is being done in the State to control the implementation of these schemes, and to maintain proper coordination between the Central and State Governments and the concerned administrative departments to solve the problems arising during the implementation. The Government has approved the constitution of a Steering Committee of Secretaries of the concerned departments under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary as follows:

The mandate of the Committee is to: (i) Review the action taken for distribution of fortified rice under ICDS/ MDM/ PDS schemes, department-wise, every three months.
2) To conduct a comparative study of the figures of malnutrition and anaemia in the state in collaboration with the health department.
3) To take necessary action for effective implementation of the scheme.
