GOVERNMENT ORDER: Shri Arun Deshpande, Chairman, State Food Commission is 65 years old. Since the said period ended on 6.6.2021, his tenure in the said post has come to an end as per the provision of Section 16 (4) of the National Food Security Act, 2013. In this regard, The decision has been taken in accordance with the government decision of June 10, 2021. He will hold the additional charge of the post of Chairman, State Food Commission, which is lying vacant due to his retirement, till his regular appointment. Mahesh Dhawale, Member, State Food Commission. The order will come into effect immediately.
