1) The Executive Engineer concerned should start the tender process of the works only after verifying that the above mentioned rehabilitated villages have been rehabilitated due to the Urdhva Penganga Project and the works under the proposal have not been done earlier from other schemes.
(2) The concerned executing agency shall be responsible for recovery of minor mineral ownership charges for the activities covered in the proposal.
3) Department of Revenue and Forestry Government Decision No. RPA 2008/ S.No.550/ R-1D 01/08/2009 and Government Circular No.RPA-0111/ Q.No.18/ R-1, Dt. The concerned Executive Engineer will be responsible for ensuring that the physical standards prescribed for providing civic amenities in the rehabilitated/ extended village under 28/3/2011 are met.
4) The concerned Executive Engineer shall ensure that the expenditure on the proposed civic amenities works does not exceed the approved budget/ prescribed limit.
5) The work-wise amount indicated in the budget for the proposed civic amenities works should be as per the technical and administrative approval. The concerned Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer shall ensure that the conditions of technical and administrative approval are complied with in accordance with the prescribed procedures.
The concerned Project Officer shall immediately transfer the civic amenities proposed under Section (18) of the Maharashtra Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons Act, 1999 to the Hingoli Zilla Parishad after completion of all the formalities and obtaining certificate from the Collector, Hingoli, accordingly.
7) The concerned Divisional Commissioner, Collector/ Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad as well as the concerned agencies should ensure that the facilities provided under the rehabilitation scheme are not provided again from other schemes.
8) The project management shall ensure that the quality of the proposed civic amenities works is maintained.
(9) To ensure compliance of policies, terms/ conditions etc. prescribed by the Government from time to time in respect of providing civic amenities to the rehabilitated villages affected by the project prior to 1976.
10) It should be ensured that the Executive Engineer, (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli is the appropriate office for the execution of works under the said budget.
The bidding process and the work order:
(a) The Executive Engineer, (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli shall immediately commence the tendering process of the proposed civic amenity works by fulfilling the conditions in the Administrative Sanction Order submitted.
(b) No prior approval or approval shall be required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for the tendering process of the proposed civic amenities works.
(c) The Government will provide funds from the Rehabilitation Department for the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages under the proposal.
