The Proposal:
The Cheque Facility on BEAMS system has been discontinued from 2022-23. Considering the various urgent works in the forest department, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, had requested the government to resume these services for smooth and timely execution of financial planning related to this work and for the implementation of district annual plans by the scheme/ district planning committee under the mandatory and program at the field level. Following that. The government was considering to restore the existing cheque facility on BEAMS system before April 1, 2022.
Government order:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide Reference No. 2 here. Proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 20.09.2022
Accordingly, with the approval of the Department of Finance, it is being approved to provide CMP/ Cheque facility for the following 19 heads of accounts:
