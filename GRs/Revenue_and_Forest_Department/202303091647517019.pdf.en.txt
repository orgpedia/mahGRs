The Proposal:
The Joint District Registrar Class-I, Parbhani has sanctioned Rs. 2,15,15, 368/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifteen Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty Six only) has been requested by the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter under reference.
The said budget has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Parbhani and technical approval has been granted by the Superintending Engineer, NANDED, who is the highest technical authority at the field level and the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has agreed.
The budget is based on the 2020-2021 budget.
Government's decision:
The Joint District Registrar Class-I, Parbhani has sanctioned Rs. 2,15,15, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifteen Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only) as per Annexure A, subject to the following conditions, the Government is granting administrative approval.
(i) It is mandatory to get the maps and plans of this work approved by the Government Architect before undertaking this work. It must also be signed by the consumer.
(ii) The ownership of the land should be ascertained before starting the work.
(ii) Government Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, D.C., on the rates of State Tariff Ex-Schedule Matters while giving technical approval. Action to be taken as on 11.04.2017.
(vi) Use of e-tendering system for matters relating to procurement in the proposal or for the approval of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour. The Department of Consumer Affairs may consider separately at their level as to whether or not it is possible to adopt the GeM portal mechanism developed by the Central Government for procurement of goods and services under the Government decision dated 24.08.2017
