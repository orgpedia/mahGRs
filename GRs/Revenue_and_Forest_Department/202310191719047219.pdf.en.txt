(6) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Periods of Appointment, Non-Appointment and Removal) Rules, 1981, failing which the period of absence shall be deemed to be" dies non."
7. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the Government for any reason. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
8. It may be noted that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
