Introduction: Context. As per the decision of the government here 1 and 2, the rates of ex-gratia assistance from the State Disaster Response Fund have been fixed for the persons affected in the event of natural disasters in the state. A proposal to provide assistance from the State Disaster Response Fund to the next of kin of persons who died in various natural calamities in the State during 2021-22, for the damage caused by house collapse, purchase of new livestock in place of dead animals, etc. Letters 3 and 4 have been received by the Government. Accordingly, the proposal to provide assistance from the State Disaster Response Fund was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Divisional Commissioners, Amravati and Nagpur are being sanctioned funds under the head of accounts mentioned in Form-A attached to this Government Decision to provide assistance as per the standard of State Disaster Response Fund to the next of kin of the persons who died in natural calamities in Amravati and Nagpur divisions in the year 2021-22, for the loss of houses and purchase of livestock instead of dead animals.
Demand No. 2. C-6, 2245 Assistance for Natural Calamities, 02 Floods, Cyclones, etc. (91) Expenditure as per the State Disaster Response Fund Standard (SDRF) is being released to Divisional Commissioners, Amravati and Nagpur, out of the grants-in-aid made available to them during the current financial year 2021-22, as per Annexure-A mentioned in the Annexure-A under this heading, through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS).
