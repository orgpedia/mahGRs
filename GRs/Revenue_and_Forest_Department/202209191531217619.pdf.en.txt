The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government regarding the continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests cadre transferred to the Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 vide letter no.
Accordingly, the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests cadre transferred to the Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per Government Resolution No.
Now, the powers to extend the tenure of the existing temporary posts included in the pattern of Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the pattern have been conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the Government Decision of Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, the Government have accorded approval for continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the cadre of Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Samhyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2022-2023 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
