The committee will prepare a plan for the conservation, conservation and development of the Lonar lake in consultation with experts and non-governmental organizations in various fields and control the implementation of the plan.
The non-official members nominated by the Chairman/ Co-Chairman shall be experts in the field concerned and shall be appointed by name. The members should attend the meeting without sending their representatives.
(3) The Chairman of the said Committee may, for special reasons, constitute a sub-committee as may be required. The sub-committee will be chaired by one of the members of the main committee and will include experts in the relevant field. The Sub-Committee shall submit a report to the Main Committee for necessary action and decision.
Attendance of the Chairman or the Co-Chairman and at least 1 non-official member and at least 1 advocate shall be mandatory for the meeting of the Committee to complete its quorum.
At present, the work of this committee should be done by the officers and staff made available to the Shegaon Development Authority. After the completion of the work of the Shegaon Development Authority, the services of these officers and employees will be transferred to the Lonar Lake Development Committee.
The works within the framework fixed by the Lonar Lake Development Committee should be carried out from the funds received from various departments as well as under convergence.
