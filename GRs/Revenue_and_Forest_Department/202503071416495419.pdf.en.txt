The Proposal: -
A large number of complaints / representations are being received from local public representatives and common citizens at the government level as well as at the field level regarding the mining and transportation of illegal minor minerals in the state. Such a complaint needs to be investigated in order to address and discern it. It is necessary to put in place a definite time frame for processing such complaints / representations received. Accordingly, a circular from the government is required to instruct the field machinery to fix a time frame for taking action in accordance with the complaint / statement received regarding illegal minor mining and transportation.
Accordingly, the issue of issuing instructions is under the consideration of the government.
Government Circular: -
The following action is being taken as per the complaints / representations received from general public and local public regarding illegal minor mining and transportation.
(i) Complaints / representations received by the field officer / office from general public and local public representatives with regard to illegal minor mining and transportation of minerals and such complaints / representations forwarded to the field agencies at the government level, shall be probed by the field agencies within 7 days from the receipt of the complaints / report issues.
(ii) If the investigation of the complaint finds illegal minor mineral mining and transportation, penal action shall be taken against the concerned within the next 15 days by the competent authority under sections 47 (7) and 47 (8) of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966, as well as the provisions of the Minor Mineral Exploration (Rules and Regulations) Rules, 2013.
(iii) Information regarding the action taken in pursuance of such complaint / statement should be communicated in writing to the complainant / statement holder concerned.
