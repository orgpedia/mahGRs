Maharashtra Forest Industrial Development Corporation (MFIDC) is proposed to be set up in the state of Maharashtra. The MFIDC will be set up with the objective of promoting forest produce based industries such as wood furniture and other products, bamboo furniture and other products, NTFP products, empowering the human resources associated with them and increasing the income of forest produce producers and entrepreneurs. Therefore, in order to make high-quality products (furniture, forest medicinal products, etc.) available to the general public at affordable prices, the government was considering the formation of an advisory committee to set up the Maharashtra Forest Industrial Development Corporation (MFIDC) on the lines of the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC).
Forest cover: About 20% of the total geographical area of the state of Maharashtra (61,907 sq km) is covered by forest. Vidarbha has a large area under forest cover. The forest produce is used for livelihood on a large scale by the forest department. There is a need for an advisory committee to set up the Maharashtra Forest Industrial Development Corporation (MFIDC) on the lines of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) in the context of bamboo or 33 types of crops in the forest, plants with medicinal properties.
A planning framework needs to be prepared by conducting a detailed study in both Nagpur for furniture clusters and Chandrapur for herbal and processing industries on a pilot basis.
It needs to be studied in terms of the capital required, the manpower required, the skills and credit provided through central government schemes.
