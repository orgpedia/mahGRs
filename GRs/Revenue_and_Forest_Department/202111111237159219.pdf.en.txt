The proposal: As the remuneration and allowances payable to the Chairman of Vasantrao Naik Krishi Swavalamban Mission are to be paid by the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati from the provision of his establishment expenses, the expenditure is made under the head 2053 0449. Under the headings 10- Contractual Service, the salary of the contractual officers/ employees in the office of the Mission in the Contractual Service is paid and 13 office expenses are covered under this head for vehicle and other office expenses.
Since the remuneration and allowances payable to the Chairman of the Mission are to be paid by the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati from the provision of his establishment expenses, a supplementary demand proposal has to be submitted to meet the expenses of the Mission under the said head of accounts. So why The Department of Finance was requested to open a new head of account under head account 2235 for the provision of funds for the salaries and allowances of the officers/ employees of the Vasantrao Naik Krishi Swavalamban Mission. The Finance Department has approved the opening of new head of accounts under mandatory expenditure. Similarly, the proposal for opening of new head of accounts has also been approved by the Principal Accountant General (Accounts& Entitlements)-II, Maharashtra, Nagpur vide his letter dated 22.09.2021. Following that, K. The matter of opening a new head of account under the head of head of account 2235 for the office of Vasantrao Naik Krishi Swavalamban Mission, Amravati was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the opening of a new Accounting Head for the office of K.Vasantrao Naik Krishi Swavalamban Mission, Amaravati under the Head Accounting Head 2235 as follows.
