The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests cadre transferred to the Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2023 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision in Reference No.6 and 7.
Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the cadre transferred to the Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 9 of the Finance Department to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
