(6) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions for Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. It may be noted that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
