2. As aforesaid, the availability of funds to the concerned executing agency through Divisional Commissioner, Amravati is being approved subject to the following conditions.
The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned administrative machinery through BEAMS, the budget disbursement system.
2) The concerned executing agency should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the government, Divisional Commissioner Amravati and Collector Amravati.
(3) The funds sanctioned and received under this Order shall be spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and the concerned executing agency shall ensure that no expenditure is incurred in excess of the sanctioned grant while spending the said funds.
4) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
(5) The concerned executing agency shall ensure that no funds received under the head of accounts mentioned in the said order shall remain outstanding or the funds received under the said order shall be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Personal Principal Account) of the concerned agency.
(6) The Controlling Officer concerned shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure arising out of the above mentioned accounts and submit the same to the Accountant General in accordance with the prevailing accounting system of the Government. Accordingly, the work of reconciling the expenditure under this head, appropriation accounts, etc. should be done by the concerned as per the prevailing practice.
7) The expenditure statements/ reports in this regard should be submitted to the Government without fail. The expenditure reconciliation statements related to the above funds should be submitted to the government without fail with the Reconciliation Slip of the Accountant General's Office.
8) The concerned executing agency should immediately submit the Utilization Certificate of the funds disbursed under this order to the Accountant General's Office, the concerned District Collector/ Divisional Commissioner/ Government.
9) Under the head of accounts in this regard, the allocation of expenditure in accordance with the funds approved in the current financial year 2022-2023 should be submitted to the government by the concerned within the prescribed period.
The above instructions should be strictly implemented by all concerned in accordance with financial discipline.
