Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Extended Building 4th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: June 8, 2023.
Standing Orders: The Governor of Maharashtra, under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Government Rules of Business made under Article 166 of the Constitution of India, has issued instructions for the smooth functioning of the State of Maharashtra in Part II, General Instructions No. 4 in the month.
The Minister (Revenue) has directed that in the original Act, the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013, the State Government has made additional provisions for the State of Maharashtra under the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act (Maharashtra Amendment), 2018, to acquire private lands for various projects in the State. In the event of such acquisition, proposals are received from time to time by the Government for exemption from Chapter II (assessment of social impact) and III (provisions relating to food security) under Section 10A of the said said Act. Such cases need to be dealt with quickly.
Accordingly, the Government have decided to approve the proposals for exemption from Chapter II (assessment of social impact) and III (provisions relating to food security) under Section 10A of the said said Act, after examining the quality of proposals received for approval of the Government and publishing the notification in the Government Gazette. The powers of the Minister (Revenue) are being delegated to the Principal Secretary (Forests, Land Acquisition) through this order.
(2) These standing orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
