Order:-
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra
State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion
of 0.532 ha. Forest land under section-2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 for laying
underground gravity 1000 mm dia. cement pipeline to solve drinking water problem in
drought prone villages in Jath Tahsil, Dist. Sangali, water source from Jath canal of
Krishna Koyana Lift Irrigation Project in the State of Maharashtra
2. In exercise of powers under general approval granted by MoEF&CC, Govt. of India
vide Handbook of FCA Guidelines-2019 (Chapter-4, Para. 4.3), 'In-Principle' approval
was accorded by Government of Maharashtra vide under reference no. 2.
3. Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra
State, Nagpur, vide his letters under reference No.3 has requested that, the said in-principle
approval should be cancelled, as per request made by User Agency
4. Considering the above facts, the in-principle approval is being cancelled.
