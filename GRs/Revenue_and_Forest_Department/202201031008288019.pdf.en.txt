the prelude
Under the Koyna project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mahabaleshwar taluka of Satara district have been taken up under reference No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted as per the orders herein. Request for funds amounting to Rs. 27.63 lakhs for payment of Civil Amenities works for the completed/ in progress works of the Administrative Sanctioned Executive Engineer, DHOM Canals Division No. 2, presented by Satara.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to provide a total amount of Rs. 27,63,000/-for the payment of civic amenities works as per the progress of work for the work in progress.
Government's decision
Under the Koyna Project, the Government is approving a total amount of Rs. 27,63,000/- for the civic amenities works in progress in the rehabilitated villages of Kalamgaon (A) and Kalamgaon (B) in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara district in the current financial year 2021-2022 subject to the following conditions.
