Preamble: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 08/02/2023 of the Finance Department, 15 temporary posts outside the sanctioned pattern for cleaning work in the Administrative Building of Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan were extended for the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 under the Government Decision dated 13/03/2023 in the context of Revenue and Forest Department.
Under the Finance Department, Government Decision, dated 31/08/2023 and Government Sanction Letter, dated 06/09/2023, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the figure. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to extend 15 temporary posts outside the sanctioned figure for cleaning work in the Konkan Bhavan Administrative Building for the period from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024.
