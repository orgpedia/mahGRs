Preamble: The Departmental Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the decision of the Government at No. 6 under reference to scrutinize the proposals received from the District Collector for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund for taking various measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State. The General Manager, BEST has sent a proposal to the Government seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund vide letter No. 7.
A meeting of the State Executive Committee was held on 23.08.2021 to take a decision on the said proposal. Accordingly, as per the approval given by the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 23.08.2021, the matter of disbursement of funds for taking various measures in the context of COVID-19 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The State Executive Committee, after considering the recommendations received from the General Manager, BEST, and the State Disaster Response Fund, has decided to meet the expenditure to be incurred as per the standards and aspects of the State Disaster Response Fund during the previous year and the current financial year to prevent the outbreak of COVID-19 virus. As per the approval given in the meeting dated 23.08.2021, from the State Disaster Response Fund, Rs. 29000.00 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Ninety Crores only) is being provided as mentioned in the table below.

The funds being disbursed through this Government Resolution will be utilized for the following matters as approved in the meeting of the Divisional Scrutiny Committee and as per reference No. 5 Expenditure should be made on such matters as may be permissible from the State Disaster Response Fund as per the decision of the Government herein.
1. Measures for quatantine sample collection and screening : (a) Provision for temporary accommodation, food, clothing, medical care etc. for people affected and sheltered in quarantine camps (other than home quarantine) or for cluster containment operations.
(b) Cost of consumables for sample collection.
(c) Support for checking screening and contact tracing.
2. Procurement of essential equipment's/labs for response to COVID-19: (a) Cost of setting up additional testing laboratories within the Government and the cost of consumable and testing kit.
(b) Cost of personal protection equipment for healthcare, municipal, police and fire authorities (c) Cost of Thermal Scanners, Ventilators, Air Purifiers, Oxygen generation and storage plant in hospitals, strengthening ambulance services for transport of patients, setting up containment zones, Covid-19 hospital, Covid-19 care centers and consumables in the Government hospitals.
2. As per the decision taken by the State Executive Committee, the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, has to meet the expenditure on the above mentioned items through the Collector, Mumbai City (for BEST activities), a total of Rs. 29000.00 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Ninety Crores only) is being approved.
The funds sanctioned as above should be disbursed by the office M-11 to the concerned Divisional Commissioner, Konkan on the BEAMS system.
The instructions given under the Government Decision No. 3 and No. 5 should be followed while spending the above funds. The funds should be utilised for the purpose for which they were sanctioned. The money should not be deposited in any bank account. Expenditure should be made within the limits of the grant sanctioned under this order.
