Preamble: Reference No. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) provides training to the candidates of Group-A cadre who are recruited directly by the State Service Examination every year under the combined examination conducted by Yashoda, Pune. Based on the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2021, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission is required to provide training to 11 probationary sub-collectors of Group' A' cadre who have been appointed in direct service through the State Service Examination-2021 recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Accordingly, the Government was considering creation of more number of posts to pay the salary of 11 probationary candidates in Group-A cadre.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the creation of a maximum number of posts for 11 Sub Divisional Officers of" Group-A" cadre recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the State Service (Main) Examination-2021 and appointed for the programme under training by the General Administration Department for a period of two years from 03/10/2023 to 03/10/2025.
2. The expenditure on salary for the period from 03/10/2023 to 03/10/2025 for the said number of posts will be borne by the candidates under Scrutiny for the district wise training.
