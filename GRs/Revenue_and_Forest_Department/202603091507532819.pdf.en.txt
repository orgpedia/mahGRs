This 'In-principle' approval is subject to the following conditions-
1) The legal status of the land shall remain unchanged.
2) Deputy Conservator of Forest, Amravati shall ensure the legal status of Survey /
Gut No. 126, 127 &128 at Village Mund Nishankrao and Survey / Gut No. 86/1
& 84, at Village Badnera Kasba, Prior to commencement of work.
3) User Agency shall defray the cost of plantation (including ten years
maintenance) of 100 trees shall be levied from the User Agency towards
Compensatory Afforestation. As far as possible, a mixture of local indigenous
species along with 10% RET species of Amravati District shall be planted and
monoculture of any species may be avoided. Details of land proposed for
Plantation (Record of Rights & KML File), shall be submitted before Stage-
II/Final approval.
4) Net Present Value:-
a) The Forest Department shall charge the Net Present Value (NPV) for the 0.1281 ha. Forest land to be diverted under this proposal from the User
Agency as per the orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India dated 30/10/2002, 01/08/2003, 28/03/2008, 24/04/2008 and 09/05/2008 in IA
No.566 in WP (C) No.202/1995 and as per the guidelines issued by the Ministry via letters No.5-1/1998-FC (72.11), dated 18/09/2003 as well as
letter No.5-2/2006-FC, dated 03/10/2006 and 5-3/2007-FC, dated
05/02/2009 & File No.5-3/2011-FC(Vol-D, Dated 06/01/2022 in this
regard.
b) Additional amount of the NPV of the diverted forest land, if any, becoming due after finalization of the same by the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India on
receipt of the report from the Expert Committee, shall be charged from the User Agency. The User Agency shall furnish an undertaking to this effect.
>) The Gas Pipeline will be laid within maximum size of the trench of 2 meters
depth & 0.8 meter width.
6) If bank guarantee is not submitted with concern Road Authority (NHAI/
MSRDC/PWD/ZP), the agency shall submit a bank guarantee at the rate
determined by the concerned authorities and an undertaking to that effect shall
also be submitted. If the agency fails to reclaim the land to the satisfaction of
concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests, the cost for reclamation will be
covered from the bank guarantee.
7) If the pipeline is going to be laid in the formation width of the road, for
reclamation of side drain and for tarred portion, then additional charges in form
of bank guarantee shall be given at the rate determined by the concerned
authorities. An undertaking shall be obtained from the User Agency that it has
agreed to make good any loss to Forest/ Environment while laying the pipeline
in the forest area and during maintenance of the same in future.
8) The User Agency shall make good of any loss to Forest / Environment. An
undertaking shall be obtained from the User Agency that it has agreed to make

good any loss to Forest/ Environment while laying Gas Pipeline in the forest
area and during maintenance of the same in future.
9) All the funds received from the user agency under the project shall be transferred / deposited to CAMPA Import only through ~ e-portal
(http://parivesh.nic.in/ )
10) For further maintenance of the project, the agency shall obtain permission from
the concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests as and when required.
11) The forest land shall not be used for any purpose other than specified in the
proposal.
12) No felling of trees shall be done during the implementation of project.
13) No new forest area beyond the Right of Way of existing road should be broken
for laying of Gas Pipeline.
14) The User Agency shall pay the cost of Net Present Value as per latest Guidelines
of MoEF&CC.
15) Underground Gas Pipeline can be laid only outside the confines of the Protected
Areas.
16) No labour camp shall be established on the forest land.
17) After completion of the project, User Agency will reclaim the area under Right
of Way suitably.
18) The layout plan of the proposal shall not be changed without prior approval of
the State Government.
19) This sanction is subject to the final clearance by the Competent Authorities for
the project and concerned PWD / NHAT authorities.
20) The User Agency shall compensate the private holders of forest land if any, in
accordance with the prevalent laws, rules & guidelines.
21) The User Agency shall obtain Environmental Clearance as per the provisions of
the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, if applicable.
22) All other conditions as may be mandatory under relevant Acts, Rules and
Guidelines shall be complied by the User Agency.
23) All the conditions stipulated by the Forest Department shall be binding on User
Agency.
24) In case, the proposed area falls in the RoW of the road passing through National
Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries, prior permission of the State Board for Wildlife
should be obtained.
25) Final Approval shall be granted by the State Government after compliance of
conditions in this order.
26) Any breach of the conditions by the User Agency will be treated as violation of
the provisions of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
