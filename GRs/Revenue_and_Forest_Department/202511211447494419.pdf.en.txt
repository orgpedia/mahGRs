The Proposal: -
The Committee headed by Divisional Commissioner, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar was under the consideration of the Government to make such amendments in the Government Decision dated 08.04.2025 as per the recommendations made in the Government Decision on Sand / Sand Export Policy-2025, dated 08.04.2025 in line with the composition of the Taluk Level Sand Control Committee and District Level Sand Control Committee.
Government Decisions: Government Decisions, Revenue and Forest Department, No: Gokhani - 10/0125 Q. No. 05 / B-1, D. The composition of the following Committees, namely, "B" and "C" in Part-II of this Act is being amended as follows: - 1) Part-II B) The Taluk Level Sand Control Committee is being constituted with the Principal / Deputy Commissioner / Joint Commissioner Urban Area as its members.
2) Part-II (c) The District Level Sand Control Committee is being constituted with Additional Collector or Resident Deputy Collector as its member.
