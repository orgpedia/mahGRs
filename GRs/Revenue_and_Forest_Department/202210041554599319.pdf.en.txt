Preamble: The Central Government has given instructions to complete the basic requirements for creating digital information related to farmers. Accordingly, it is intended to develop a technology-based system for three things: (i) linking of farmers' data base with the records, (ii) Geo referencing of village maps, and (iii) GIS based real time crop monitoring. For this, Aadhaar seeding and other information needs to be included in the existing database of the Land Records Department. This digital database related to farmers will be used by the departments of Revenue, Agriculture, Cooperation, Revenue (Relief and Rehabilitation), etc. This will improve the quality of life of the farmers. The State Government had constituted a State Level Monitoring and Monitoring Committee for the purpose. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Since the Central Government has given instructions to complete the basic aspects of creating digital information related to farmers, it is meant to develop a technology-based system for three things: (i) attaching farmers' data base to the record, (ii) geo referencing of village maps, (iii) GIS based real time crop inspection. For this, Aadhaar seeding and other information needs to be included in the existing database of the Revenue/ Land Records Department. A Steering Committee is being constituted at the State level to take policy decisions and review their implementation.
