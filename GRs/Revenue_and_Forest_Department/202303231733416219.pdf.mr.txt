Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 3.71 ha. Forest land for development work of Mata Jagdamba Devi (Mohata
Devi) Temple premises at Village Mohate, Tal. Pathardi, Dist- Ahmednagar to Government of Maharashtra,
AND whereas, the Government of Maharashtra forwarded the proposal to Regional
Office, MoEF&CC, Govt. of India, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.2;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letters under
reference No.3, accorded ‘In-principle' approval to divert the 3.71 ha. Forest Land for the above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer and Government of Maharashtra vide letters under reference No.4 have submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded 'Final

approval' to divert the 3.71 ha. Forest land for diversion of 3.71 ha. Forest land for
development work of Mata Jagdamba Devi (Mohata Devi) Temple premises at Village
Mohate, Tal. Pathardi, Dist- Ahmednagar under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 3.71 ha.
Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under
reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Chief Executive Officer, Shri. Jagdambadevi Sarvajanik Trust, Mohta for
development work of Mata Jagdamba Devi (Mohata Devi) Temple premises.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 31.01.2019, 31.12.2020 and dated 26.09.2022 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC,
Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle' and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Conservator of Forests (T), Nashik shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency before transferring the land to User Agency. He shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 31.01.2019, 31.12.2020 and dated 26.09.2022, Rules & Guidelines made
/ issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T), Nashik shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
