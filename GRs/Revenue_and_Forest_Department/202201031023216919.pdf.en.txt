The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer Dhom Canal Department No. 2, Satara should pay the pending dues of civic amenities works immediately after receiving the funds sanctioned under this order, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the government, divisional commissioner, Pune and the concerned collector immediately.

(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
The Superintending Engineer and the Executive Engineer of the concerned system shall ensure that no funds under the head of the head of account" Demand No. C-10, Head Account No. 4701, 01, Major Irrigation- Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727)" (Dattamat) mentioned in the said order shall remain unutilized or the funds received under the said order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned system.
The Superintending Engineer and the Executive Engineer of the concerned machinery shall ensure that no short payment of one year is pending under the head of the said order under the head" Demand No.C-10, Head Accounts 4701, 01, Major Irrigation Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727)" (Dattamat).
The Controller General shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure to be incurred by the concerned Controller General under the head' Demand No. C-10, Main Accounts, Heading 4701, 01, Major Irrigation Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727)' (Dattamat) and submit them to the Accountant General as per the prevailing accounting system of the Government. Accordingly, the work of reconciliation of expenditure under this head, appropriation accounts, etc. should be done by the concerned as per the prevailing practice.
10) The expenditure statements/ reports in this regard should be submitted to the Government without fail. The expenditure reconciliation statements related to the above funds should be submitted to the government without fail with the Reconciliation Slip of the Accountant General's Office.
11) The concerned Superintending Engineer and the concerned Executive Engineer should immediately submit the utilization certificate of the fund distributed under this order to the Accountant General's Office, the concerned Collector/ Divisional Commissioner/ Government.
