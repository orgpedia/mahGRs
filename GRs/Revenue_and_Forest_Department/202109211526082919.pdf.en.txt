The Proposal:
Government Resolution No. 2, d. As on 13/09/2019, a total of 19034 regular+ 429 supernumerary posts and 8329 Forest Labourers (Numerical) in various cadres for the Forest Department have been approved with a revised figure of 27792 posts.
A committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur to reorganize the sanctioned posts as per the revised format under the Forest Department.
The meeting of the said Committee was held on 03/12/2019 Considering the recommendations of the said Committee, the matter of restructuring the sanctioned posts newswise and branch wise as per the revised pattern was under the consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Government Resolution No. 2, d. As on 13/09/2019, the total sanctioned strength of Forest Department is 19034 regular+ 429 supernumerary posts and 8329 Forest Labourers (Numerical) in various cadres.
(a) The office-wise details of the total 585 posts abolished in various cadres in the prevailing figure shall be as per Annexure-A.
(b) Relocation of 48 posts in 12 different cadres in the existing pattern has been proposed and the details will be as per Annexure-B.
(e) The details of sanctioned posts in various cadres as per the revised pattern shall be as per Annexure-A annexed to this Government Resolution.
