Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has
submitted a proposal to the State Government, which was received from M/s Bharti
Airtal Limited, Pune for diversion of 0.1014 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanrakshan
Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for laying underground Optical Fiber Cable
along the road in Right of Way of NH-193 in from Dudha to Dalsawangi in
Buldhana Tehsil of District Buldhana in the State of Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its
order under reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle’ approval with certain conditions
to above proposal for diversion of 0.1014 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests &
Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.3 has
submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under
general approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of
# Page 2
Guidelines for effective and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra 1s
pleased to grant Final Approval for Diversion of 0.1014 ha. Forest Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.1014 ha. Forest
Land consisting of Reserved Forests specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No.l and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be
diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. M/s Bharti Airtal Limited, Pune for diversion of
0.1014 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980
for laying underground Optical Fiber Cable along the road in Right of Way of NH-
193 in from Dudha to Dalsawangi in Buldhana Tehsil of District Buldhana in the
State of Maharashtra.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of
following conditions by the User Agency-
1. DCF, Buldhana shall ensure compliance of the condition no.14 in ‘in-
principle’ order dated 04.09.2024, before handover actual possession to the
User Agency
2. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned
in the Order dated 04.09.2024.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed
activities and also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall
facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In
case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the
permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-
forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will
reconfirm that Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and
Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
5. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above
mentioned '‘1n-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The
Chief Conservator of Forests (Territorial), Amravati shall ensure the strict
compliance of the conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the
report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and
Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
6. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of
Maharashtra’s order dated 04.09.2024, in this order and Rules and Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980
Shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.

7. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned
Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report
through the State Government to Deputy Director General of Forests
(Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the
guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
