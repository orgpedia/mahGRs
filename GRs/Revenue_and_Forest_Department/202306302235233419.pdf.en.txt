Appointment by appointment
Sunanda Parwe, Tehsildar, has been appointed as Tehsildar and Chief Administrative Officer, Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Aurangabad.

the Revenue and Forest Department
Government Order No.Promotion-3022/ PR No.206/ E-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,

Date of release: June 30, 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) of the Maharashtra Transfer Act, 2005 and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Smt. Sunanda Parwe, Tahsildar is being appointed as Tahsildar and Chief Administrative Officer, Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Aurangabad on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Mrs. Sunanda Parwe, Tehsildar is being relieved unilaterally as per this order. The above officers should be posted immediately.
(3) The said order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector.
The services of Mrs. Sunanda Parve, Tehsildar are being handed over to the Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai in accordance with the deputation from the office of the Tahsildar and Chief Administrative Officer, Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Aurangabad.
5. Mrs. Sunanda Parwe, Tehsildar to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
