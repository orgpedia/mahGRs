the prelude
Under the Bhatghar and Veer project, the budget of civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Gunand, Paravale Hinge, Nhavi, Bhambwade, and Sarola in Bhor taluka of Pune district was approved by the Divisional Commissioner, Pune, under reference No. Letters 9 and 11 were submitted to the government.
Part I of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, sub-section (3), serial number 4, paragraph no. 27 (2) (a), as per the powers vested in the Administrative Department in giving administrative approval to the scheme or proposal, as well as the decision of the Government vide Finance Division No. V.P.R- 10.08/ P.S. 70/2008/ Regulations, D. As per the powers conferred under 15.5.2009, the Government has decided to grant administrative approval to the budgets of the civic amenities works under the scheme of providing civic amenities to the villages rehabilitated due to the project before 1976. SAMKIND 2011/ Q.No.80 (Part-II)/ R-8, D. 25/07/2013 has been issued.
Thereafter, the Department of Finance, in exercise of the powers conferred under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-sections-I to V, vide reference No. Amendments have been made under Government Decisions 4 and 7. In accordance with the said amendments and in accordance with the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, in Part I, sub-section (3), serial number 4, paragraph No. 27 (2) (a), as per the power vested in the Administrative Department to grant administrative approval to a scheme or proposal, the matter of granting administrative approval to permissible civic amenity works out of the civic amenity works mentioned in the proposal received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
