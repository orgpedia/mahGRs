Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
Diversion of 0.3990 ha Protected Forest Land for construction of access/approach road
including underground water pipeline, underground electricity cable, drainage line, road side
strip plantation etc. for warehousing project at village Kasane, Tal. Bhiwandi, Dist. Thane, in
the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has submitted
proposal to Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0.3990 ha. The Forest Land for the
# Page 2
Government Resolution No.: FLD- 1221/C.R.148/F-10
above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject
to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under   reference No.4 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the State Government;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letters under   reference No.5 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment,
Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no.6 Accorded 'Final
approval 'to diversification of 0.3990 ha. Protected Forest Land for Construction of
access/approach road including underground water pipeline, underground electricity cable,
drainage line, road side strip plantation etc. for warehousing project at village Kasane, Tal.
Bhiwandi, Dist. Thane, in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Forest (Conservation)
Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.7 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order 11 the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.3990
ha. consisting of Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. M/s. Sai Sadguru Realty, LLp, H.No.287, Bhoirgaon,
Village-Vahuli, Opp. Sai Dhara Main Gate, Post-Padgha, Bhiwandi for construction of
access/approach road including underground water pipeline, underground electricity cable,
drainage line, road side strip plantation etc. for warehousing project at village Kasane, Tal.
Bhiwandi, Dist. Thane.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letter
dated 20.05.2022 and dated 05.09.2023 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Integrated Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur,
referred above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall ensure the strict
compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this
regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the

the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
