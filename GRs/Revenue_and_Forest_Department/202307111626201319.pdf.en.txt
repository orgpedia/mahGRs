GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: REVERSE- 2023/ PR No.41/ R-8-1) The tender process of the works should be started by the concerned Executive Engineer only after verifying that the above mentioned rehabilitated village has been rehabilitated due to the Ujani project and the works under the proposal have not been done earlier from other schemes and the works will not be released.
(2) The concerned executing agency shall be responsible for recovery of minor mineral ownership charges for the activities covered in the proposal.
3) Department of Revenue and Forestry Government Decision No. RPA 2008/ S.No.550/ R-1D 01/08/2009 and Government Circular No.RPA-0111/ Q.No.18/ R-1, Dt. The concerned Executive Engineer will be responsible for ensuring that the physical standards prescribed for providing civic amenities in the rehabilitated/ extended village under 28/3/2011 are met.
4) The concerned Executive Engineer shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the proposed civic amenities shall not exceed the approved budget/ ceiling.
5) The amount of work-wise amount indicated in the budget for the proposed civic amenities work should be as per the technical and administrative approval. The concerned Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer shall ensure that the conditions of technical and administrative approval are complied with in accordance with the prescribed procedures.
The Project Officer concerned shall immediately transfer the civic amenities proposed under Section (18) of the Maharashtra Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons Act, 1999 to the Ahmednagar Zilla Parishad after completion of all the formalities and obtaining certificate from the District Collector, Ahmednagar.
7) The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector/ Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad and the concerned agencies should ensure that the facilities provided under the rehabilitation scheme are not restored from other schemes.
8) The project management shall ensure that the quality of the proposed civic amenities works is maintained.
(9) Ensure compliance with the policies, terms and conditions etc. prescribed by the Government in respect of providing civic amenities to the rehabilitated villages affected by the project prior to 1976.
10) To implement the works under this budget, the Executive Engineer, Ujani Canal Division No. 8 Solapur should be ensured to be a proper office.
Tender Process and Commissioning Order: (a) The Executive Engineer, Ujani Canal Division No. 8 Solapur should immediately start the tender process for the proposed civic amenity work by fulfilling the conditions in the present Administrative Sanction Order.
(b) The tender process for the proposed civic amenities work shall not require prior approval and approval of the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation.
(c) The Government will provide funds from the Rehabilitation Department for the civic amenities work in the rehabilitated villages under the proposal.
