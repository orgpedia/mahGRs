Under the Ujani project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Chandaj and Shevare in Madha taluka of Solapur district have been taken up under reference No. The administrative approval has been granted under Government Decisions 4 and 5. Now, for payment of the civil works completed and in progress, vide reference No. 8 The proposal for the demand of funds has been submitted to the Government by the Assistant Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for the civic amenities works completed and in progress under the proposal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision The Government has approved the disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 15,36,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Thirty Six Thousand only) for the completed and in-progress civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Chandj and Shevare in Madha Taluka of Solapur district in the current financial year 2023-2024 under the Ujani Project subject to the following conditions.

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works to the concerned Executive Engineer without any delay on receipt from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Ujani Canal Division No. As soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received by Solapur, the pending dues of civic amenities should be paid immediately, and accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune as well as Collector, Solapur and Collector, Ahmednagar.

6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
