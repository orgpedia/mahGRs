आहे.:-
1. Funds received for land acquisition shall be credited to this PLA and shall be withdrawn to compensate the land owners in case of acquisition of land.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account and to keep Account operative.
3. The Administrator shall be responsible for reconciliation of the Account with Treasury/Sub Treasury.
4. Ona Monthly basis, the balances in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled with the Treasury Office and yearly reconciliation with O/o The Principal Accountant General (A&E) by the the Treasury Office for all types of Personal Deposits 5. At the end of each financial year, the balance in the PLA may be forwarded by the Treasury office to O/o The Principal Accountant General (A&E) for confirmation.
6. The Finance Department GR dated 05/09/2023 regarding operation of the PD/PLA should be followed.
