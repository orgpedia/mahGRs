Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion of 0.9286
ha. of forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for
construction of Rope Suspension Bridge (Skywalk) connecting Hurricane Point and
Goreghat point at Chikhaldara Hill Station Municipal Council, Taluka-Chikhaldara, Dist.
Amaravati in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra vide letter under reference No.2 has
submitted the proposal to the Regional Office, MoEFCC, Government of India, Nagpur
under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
# Page 2
Government Order No.: FLD-2021/C.R.39/F-10
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.3, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 0.9286 ha. Forest Land for
the above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Regional Office, MoEFCC, Government of India,
Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no.5 accorded ‘Final
approval’ to divert the of 0.9286 ha of Reserve forest land in favour of CIDCO, Amaravati
for construction of Rope Suspension Bridge (Skywalk) connecting Hurricane Point and
Goreghat point at Chikhaldara Hill Station Municipal Council, Taluka-Chikhaldara, Dist.
Amaravati in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.9286
ha. consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. CIDCO, Amaravati for construction of Rope
Suspension Bridge (Skywalk) connecting Hurricane Point and Goreghat point at
Chikhaldara Hill Station Municipal Council, Taluka-Chikhaldara, Dist. Amaravati in the
State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 19.01.2022 and dated 31.10.2022 of Integrated Regional Office, MOEF&CC,
Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of
following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, East Melghat should ensure that, the fulfilment of
Condition No.2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 and other conditions mentioned in the order, dated
31.10.2022, before hand over actual possession of forest land to User Agency.
2. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 6 ‘in-
principle’ and ‘final approval’ letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur,
referred above. The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Field
Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati shall ensure the strict compliance of
the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra
State, Nagpur from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered

access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 19.01.2022 and dated 31.10.2022, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
