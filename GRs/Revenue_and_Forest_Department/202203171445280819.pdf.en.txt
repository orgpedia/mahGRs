Preamble: In the petition No. 305/1995 regarding encroachment in Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, Hon.
As per the order of the High Court, 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest created under the scheme" Creation of posts for Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali" has been approved by the Government for continuation in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Government decision of Reference No. 3 and 4.
2. Now, vide letter Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has filed a petition vide No. 305/1995 regarding encroachment of Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali. As per the orders of the High Court, a proposal has been submitted to the Government for approval to continue the 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in the cadre created under the scheme' Creation of posts for Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali" from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
The Finance Department has been empowered to extend the term of the existing temporary posts from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 including the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern. 6 have been provided to the administrative departments under the Government Decision.
