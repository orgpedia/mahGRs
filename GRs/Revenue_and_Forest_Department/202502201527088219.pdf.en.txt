| Government Decision, General Administration Department dated 19.03.2016                                                             |
| The Collector has been declared as the Head of the Department. The Collector is the Revenue                                         |
| The position is in the department's profile. In addition to the functions of the revenue department, other departments              |
| The role of coordination and control should be played by the District Collector.                                                    |
| Revenue at village, taluka, sub-division and district levels and various other departments.                                         |
| The District Collector is responsible for the implementation, coordination and control of the schemes.                              |
| Hence, the District Collector is also called for meetings of various departments of the state government.                           |
| Attendance is required in person or by VC. For most days of the week                                                                |
| The District Collector is invited for meetings from different departments.                                                          |
| Due to the busy schedule, the workload on them is increasing day by day.                                                            |
| There is a delay in the implementation of the works as well as the 100-day programme of the Hon'ble Chief Minister.                 |
| It is observed that there is coherence / planning in the work of the Collector.                                                     |
| to enable them to bring about and render due justice to their obligations.                                                          |
| The following instructions are being issued.                                                                                        |
| 1) The Chief Minister, the Deputy Chief Minister and the Chief Secretary, except for the other departments of the State Government. |
| If you want to hold a meeting with the collector, only on Wednesdays and Thursdays.                                                 |
| These two days should be conducted by the VC as well, preferably, on a non-revenue basis.                                           |
| Revenue for the concerned department before holding a meeting regarding the essential issue.                                        |
| Take prior written permission from the department.                                                                                  |
| (2) The District Collector shall be called for meeting by other departments, except at the District Headquarters.                   |
| At places e.g. Ministries, Divisional Commissioner's Offices, Revenue Department for meetings                                       |
| Avoid calling in person without permission.                                                                                         |
