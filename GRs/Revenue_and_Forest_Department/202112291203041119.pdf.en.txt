Preamble: The issue of providing breakfast and lunch to the officers and staff of the Ministry Control Room (State Emergency Operations Center) from the Ministry Restaurant was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Ministry Control Room is the State Emergency Operations Centre. The centre is open 24 hours a day. Since emergency services are a service related to the life of the people, it is necessary to provide snacks/ meals etc. to the officers and staff on duty in this center from the Mantralaya/ Square Restaurant. In case of emergency and as the said officers and staff cannot leave the Ministry Control Room at regular times, the above arrangements need to be made. Therefore, the State Disaster Management Authority has approved to bear the cost of this system.
The expenditure is borne by the Revenue and Forest Department. C-6, Head of Accounts No. 2245. Assistance for relief of natural calamities. Scheme under 80 General Five Year Plans.
