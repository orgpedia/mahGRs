Establishment of State Level Monitoring Committee for quality check of the Folk Biodiversity Registers prepared in the State of Maharashtra.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department

Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: April 5, 2021
The Proposal:
The National Green Tribunal, New Delhi vide its Original Application No. 347/2016, dated 18.03.2020, has directed that the quality of the Lok Biodiversity Register being prepared shall be of good standard and a State Level Monitoring Committee shall be constituted to evaluate the same. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a state-level control committee to check the quality of the folk biodiversity registers prepared in the state.
Government Decision: To check the quality of the folk biodiversity registers prepared in the State, a State Level Monitoring Committee is being constituted with subject experts as follows:

The functions of the State Level Steering Committee constituted as above shall be as follows:
1. The People's Biodiversity Registers prepared by adopting appropriate procedures.
Testing the sample to check the quality.
Interacting with various stakeholders involved in the preparation of the People's Biodiversity Register.
Quarterly Report on Biodiversity Survey of Maharashtra
To be submitted to the Board, Nagpur.
