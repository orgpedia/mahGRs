Introduction: In the case of sudden natural disasters in the state, it takes a lot of time to distribute relief to the victims after receiving proposals through panchnamas. Therefore, in order to enable immediate disbursement of relief to the citizens affected by natural calamities and their heirs, the Government was considering to grant approval to the Collector to draw the amount from the treasury on minus (-) authorization letter under head 2245 0155 (Assistance to the families of persons killed and injured due to natural calamities, etc.).
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As immediate relief is required to be provided to the citizens affected by natural calamities, deaths and injuries as well as to their next of kin, in order to meet the expenditure for the financial year 2022-23, approval is being given for withdrawal of amount from the treasury on demand number C-6, 2245, under the head Assistance for Relief of Natural Calamities 02, Floods, Cyclones, etc. 101, Anugraha Sahayata (91) Expenditure as per State Disaster Response Fund Standards (91) (01) Cash Allowance, Assistance to the families of deceased persons and Assistance to the injured (Compulsory) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) (2245 0155) on minus (-) authority letter under the head.
These Government decisions are being issued as per the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. 156/2022/ Expenditure-9, dated 13.5.2022 of the Department of Finance.
