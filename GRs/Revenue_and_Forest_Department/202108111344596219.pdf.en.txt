Preamble: A proposal was submitted to the Cabinet to provide assistance to the affected people who suffered losses due to the floods in various districts of the state caused by heavy rains in July, 2021.
As per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting held on 3.8.20210, the matter of issuing Government Order in the matter was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to provide the following assistance to the affected people for the damage caused by the floods in various districts of the State due to heavy rains in July, 2021.

The other terms and conditions prescribed in the relevant Government Decision shall continue to apply while providing the amount of assistance.
(2) The amount of assistance provided in the State Disaster Response Fund in excess of the prescribed norms or rates shall be spent from the State Government Fund as shown in the above statement under the head of account.
(3) The norms and rates of assistance of the State Disaster Response Fund as prescribed in the Government Resolution No. 1, as the case may be, shall continue to apply for damages other than the above relief measures and norms.
4. Beneficiaries to be determined after conducting the panchnama. After that, the amount will be credited to the bank account of the beneficiary. The money should not be deposited in any bank account. After the completion of the process of distribution of assistance to the beneficiaries, the list of beneficiaries and details of assistance should be published on the website of the districts. Also, the district collectors should take action to fill the list of beneficiaries and details of assistance in the system on the central government's website ndmis. mha. gov. in.
