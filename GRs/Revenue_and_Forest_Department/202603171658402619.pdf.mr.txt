Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the
State Government, which was received from M/s Bharti Airtel Ltd., Pune for diversion of 0.112
ha forest land for Laying an Underground Optical Fiber Cable along existing Right of Way of
Risod to Hingoli and Lonar Road in Washim District in the State of Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order under
reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above proposal for
re-diversion of 0.112 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general
approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final Approval for re-
diversion of 0.112 ha. Forest Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.112 ha. Forest Land consisting
of Reserved Forest land specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under
reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be re-diverted to the User Agency,
1.e. M/s Bharti Airtel Ltd., Pune for Laying an Underground Optical Fiber Cable along existing
# Page 2
Right of Way of Risod to Hingoli and Lonar Road in Washim District in the State of
Maharashtra.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests (1), Washim shall ensure compliance of the condition no.
2,4,5 & 14 in ‘in-principle' order dated 31.07.2025, before actual handing over the forest
land to the user agency.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
Order dated 31.07.2025.
For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also to
carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the
area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the
non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that
Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection)
Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above mentioned
'1n-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. Chief Conservator of
Forests (T), Yavatmal shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User
Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra’s order dated 31.07.2025, in this order and Rules and Guidelines made / issued under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount to violation of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be _ taken
accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State
Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry
of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980.
