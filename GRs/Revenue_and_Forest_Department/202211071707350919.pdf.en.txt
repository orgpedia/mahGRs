Government's decision:
The Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Department of Land Records has issued the provisional additional seniority for the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2015. It was published in 2.
In this, the Commissioner of Jamabandi and the Director of Land Records, Pune, were informed about the distribution of this seniority to the concerned officers and to receive and send to the government any objections/ applications of the concerned officers in this regard.
However, since the names of the officers who appeared under CPTP-1 in direct service have not been included in the seniority list for the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2014 and the names of the officers who appeared under CPTP-1 in direct service for the period from 01.01.2015 to 31.012.2015 have been included twice in the seniority list for the year 2014, it has been decided to issue fresh notification for revised provisional additional seniority of the officers who appeared in the prescribed route of promotion and direct service during the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2015 and for the period from 01.01.2016 to 31.12.2021 for the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Group-B. 3 submitted to the Government.

This notice is being published subject to the decision of the Supreme Court, High Court, Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal or any other court in the matter of petitions filed or pending, and if any change is required to be made in this notice due to the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission or otherwise.
The Commissioner and Director of Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune, shall make available the provisional additional seniority of the said period from 2014 to 2021 to the concerned officers and shall instruct them to display the said seniority on the notice board in their office and in the office of the Deputy Superintendent of Land Records under the Deputy Director of Land Records. Further, the officers promoted as Deputy Superintendents of Land Records, Group-B Cadre from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2021 should verify whether their names have been included in the provisional additional seniority list or not and submit their objections/ applications, if any, to the Commissioner of Settlement within 15 days from the date of publication of the provisional additional seniority list. Applications received after the deadline will not be considered.
While publishing the seniority list, it may be appropriate to mention the following points: (a) After the publication of the said provisional additional seniority list, the aggrieved officers shall submit their objections/ applications to the Government through the Commissioner of Settlement, Pune within 15 days from the date of publication of the provisional additional seniority list. Also, the objections/ applications received thereafter will not be considered.
(b) The said provisional additional seniority shall be immediately distributed by the Commissioner of Settlement, Pune to the concerned Deputy Superintendent of Land Records and shall also be published on the notice board of the office.
(a) If there are any factual errors in this provisional seniority list, such as the name of an officer is missing or there are errors in the initials, date of birth or date of appointment, the Commissioner of Conscription, Pune shall look into them and bring them to the Government's direction. Also, the objections/ applications received within the stipulated time should be thoroughly scrutinized and their feedback should be submitted along with the evidence, i.e., appropriate corrections will be made while publishing the final seniority.
