Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) of the Maharashtra Transfer Act, 2005 and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Mr. Abhijit Khole, Tehsildar is being appointed as Assistant Commissioner, Municipal Corporation, Thane on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Mr. Abhijit Khole, Assistant Commissioner, Municipal Corporation, Thane is being relieved unilaterally as per this order. The above officers should be posted immediately.
(3) The said order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector.
4. The services of Mr. Abhijit Khole, Tehsildar are being handed over to Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai in accordance with the deputation to the post of Assistant Commissioner, Municipal Corporation, Thane.
Abhijit Khole, Tehsildar should appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
