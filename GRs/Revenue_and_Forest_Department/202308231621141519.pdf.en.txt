Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. Guidelines have been issued for the distribution of financial funds for the year 2023-24 in accordance with the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2023.
The proposal for the disbursement of funds under the scheme Establishment (Programme) of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority (2406 A087) for the year 2023-24 has been submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for the approval of the Government under the letter of reference No. 2 and the funds have been disbursed under the Government decision of reference No. 3. The government has taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
In view of the circular of Finance Department vide Reference No. 1 and in view of the demand for funds made under the letter of Reference No. 2 to the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur i.e. 40.15 lakh is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
The expenditure on the scheme is as under:- Head of Accounts 2406, Forestry and Wildlife, 02, Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (19) Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority (Programme) (2406 A087)

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 05.23/ S.No.175/ F-1
The budgetary allocations for the year 2023-24 under the head of accounts should be made from the grants available through the system.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur shall disburse the funds only after verifying that the funds are covered under the scheme of Establishment (Programme) of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority (2406 A087).
(iii) The funds shall be spent only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical sanction has been accorded to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority, as required.
The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the terms of reference of the proposal.
V. Various Guidelines issued by the Finance Department and Circular No. No.40/ Earth-3, dated 12.04.2023 to be strictly followed.
This Government Decision is being issued pursuant to Finance Department, Government Circular No:-Finance 2023/ P.No.40/ Finance 3 dated 12.04.2023.
