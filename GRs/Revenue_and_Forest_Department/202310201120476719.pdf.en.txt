Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and in Annexure 2 of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Shri Nilesh Jadhav, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Registrar and Assistant Professor, Divisional Administrative Training Institute, Nashik on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2. Mr. Nilesh Jadhav, Deputy Collector should immediately report to the post of Registrar and Assistant Professor, Divisional Administrative Training Institute, Nashik.
