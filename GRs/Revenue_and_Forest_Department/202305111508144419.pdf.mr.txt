Order: Whereas, the Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State,
Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion of 17.56 ha. Forest land in favour of M/s. Jayaswal NECO Ltd for extraction of Iron Ore from
Survey No. 553 and 568 at Manegaon, Tal. Amgaon, Dist. Gondia to the Government of Maharashtra; AND Whereas, the Government of Maharashtra, vide letter under reference No.2
has submitted above said proposal to Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Govt. of India, Bhopal;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Government of
India after due consideration, vide letters under reference No.3, accorded ‘In-principle' approval to divert the 17.56 ha. Forest Land for the above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of

Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide letter under reference no. 5 accorded 'Final approval' to divert the 17.56 ha. Forest Land for extraction of Iron Ore at Manegaon, Tal. Amgaon, Dist. Gondia in favour of M/s. Jayaswal NECO Ltd under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
AND whereas, the Collector, Gondia has issued certificate under Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 for 17.04 ha. forest land in Survey No.553 of Village Manegaon vide letter under reference No.7
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that out of proposed 17.56 ha. forest land, only 17.04 ha. forest land in Survey No.553 at Village Manegaon, Tal. Amgaon, Dist. Gondia be diverted to the User Agency, i.e.
M/s. Jayaswal NECO Ltd, M.I.D.C, Hingna Road, Nagpur for extraction of Iron Ore.
Decision for remaining 0.52 ha. land at Survey No.568 at Village Manegaon, Tal.
Amgaon, Dist. Gondia will be taken separately.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 27.03.2009, 15.04.2009 and dated 16.08.2022 of Government of India.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. Proposed land at Survey No.568, Village Manegaon, Tal. Amgaon, Dist. Gondia shall not be handed over to the User Agency, till further directions of the State Government.
2. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in principle' and 'final approval' letters of Government of India, referred above.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Nagpur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
3. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of India’s letters dated 27.03.2009, 15.04.2009 and dated 16.08.2022, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
4. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Nagpur shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
