Maharashtra is a disaster prone state and lightning strikes in the state cause huge losses to human beings and resources. For this, it has been decided to install 1 such lightning arrester system in every Gram Panchayat area on a pilot basis in 3 districts of the state. For this, the government was considering to allocate funds to the Public Works Department (Electricity). The Public Works Department (Power) has sanctioned Rs.2,687 crore for the installation of a total of 2687 power arrester systems in Gram Panchayats of three districts of Pune, Gadchiroli and Chandrapur. Budget of Rs.58,03,17,539/- (Rupees Fifty Eight Crores Three Lakhs Seventeen Thousand Five Hundred and Forty-nine only) has been presented. There were proposals for 2169 gram panchayat buildings that would qualify. It has been approved by the government.
Reference No. The government has decided to pay Rs. 2 crore, reference no. 2 of the Government Rs. 23 crore has been allocated for the purpose. Also Reference No. 3 of the Government Rs. 12 crore has been disbursed to Public Works Department (Power).

Government's decision:
A total of 2169 Gram Panchayats in the three districts of Pune, Gadchiroli and Chandrapur have been eligible for installation of lightning arrester system.
