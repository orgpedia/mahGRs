The office of the Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat was responsible for the administration and management of the protected areas of Melghat Tiger Reserve and Katepurna Wildlife Sanctuary and the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), East Nagpur was its controlling officer. However, in the year 2018, certain additional protected areas such as Dnyanganga Wildlife Sanctuary, Tipeshwar Wildlife Sanctuary, have been handed over to the Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve for administration and management.
As per AFO-2018/ PR No. 04/ F-7, dated 28/12/2018, the rank of Chief Conservator of Forests has been upgraded to the rank of Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests. Their controlling officer is the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife).
As per the Government Order No. AFO-2021/ PR No. 04/ F-7, dated 28/04/2021, the post of Area Director Melghat has been downgraded to the rank of Chief Conservator of Forests. Therefore, as in the year 2018, Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) East Nagpur was functioning as the Controlling Officer of this post, now the matter was under consideration of the Government to declare Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) East, Nagpur as the Controlling Officer of the post of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati.
