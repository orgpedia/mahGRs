The Proposal:
The heavy rainfall in July 2021 has led to flood situation in 7 districts of the state. Due to this flood situation, it is necessary to shift the citizens to safer places and start relief camps for them and provide them food, water, medical facilities. The government was also considering to distribute funds in advance to the concerned districts through the Divisional Commissioners for removing garbage from public places, disposal of dead bodies, drainage of water from flood-affected areas to prevent the spread of diseases after the flood situation is contained and the flood waters recede.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In the wake of the flood situation caused by the heavy rains in July, 2021 in 7 districts of the State, the Government has approved the release of a total amount of Rs. 650.00 lakh (Rupees Six Crore and Fifty Lakh only) as mentioned in the table below from the State Disaster Response Fund to the concerned districts through the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Pune in advance for the purpose of shifting the citizens to safer places and opening relief camps for them and providing them with food, water, medical facilities, clothing, etc. facilities, as well as removing garbage from public places, disposal of dead bodies, drainage of water from the flood affected areas to prevent the spread of diseases after the flood situation is contained and the flood waters recede.
