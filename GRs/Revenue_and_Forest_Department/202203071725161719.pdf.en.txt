The Land Records Department has received complaints regarding the tampering of original records of the Land Records Department by fabricating the maps of the boundary permanent enumeration under the office of Borivali G. Mumbai Suburban and Deputy Superintendent, Land Records, Mumbai Suburban. The Leader of Opposition, Maharashtra Legislative Council and other Members have passed a resolution under Rule 101 of the Maharashtra Legislative Council (Winter Session 2021) through a calling attention motion No. There were 340 participants. During the discussion on this topic, Shri. The Minister (Revenue) had announced an inquiry into the entire matter by the Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune. Accordingly, a committee has been formed under the chairmanship of Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune. The Member Secretary and Deputy Director, Land Records, Konkan Region, Mumbai, referred to the case. The committee has been asked to give an extension of two months. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Municipal Geo-statutory Officer, Goregaon Sub-Division Mauje Erangel, Ta. The Inquiry Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Pune under the decision of the Government in this regard to investigate the entire matter of tampering with the original records of the Land Records Department by fabricating the maps of the boundary permanent enumeration under the office of Borivali G. Mumbai Suburban and Deputy Superintendent, Land Records, Mumbai Suburban is being extended by 2 months.
The committee should investigate the matter thoroughly and submit a self-explanatory report to the government within 2 months.
