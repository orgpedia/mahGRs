Preamble- The final award on payment of compensation by the Land Acquisition Authorities while acquiring land for the rehabilitation of earthquake victims in Osmanabad district was passed. As the compensation of the land was not admissible to the concerned land holders, they decided to seek enhanced compensation as per Section 18 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894. A case was filed against the government. The civil court has decided to give increased compensation to the landowners. The Department of Law and Justice, Aurangabad has given its opinion on not appealing against the said decisions. Total amount to be provided under letters 3 to 7 is Rs. 7,43,87, 598/-
