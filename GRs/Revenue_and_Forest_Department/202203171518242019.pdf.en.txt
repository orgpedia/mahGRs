The Proposal:
The Government has accorded approval for continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Government Decision of Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 6 of the Department of Finance to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department of Reference No. 6, the Government is approving the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 in the year 2022-2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of protected areas under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 22.02.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
