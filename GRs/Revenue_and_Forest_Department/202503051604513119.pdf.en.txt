Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the State Government
for diversion of 0.20 ha. of forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 for installation of 'C' BAND Doppler Weather Radar in Gut. No. 132,
Compartment No. 39, at Village Mhaismal, Taluka- Khultabad, Dist. Chatrapati
Sambhaji Nagar in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No. 2 has
submitted proposal to Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur;

AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0. 20 ha. The Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
The said approval is subject to fulfilment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 5 Accorded 'Final
Approval 'to divert the 0.2 ha of forest land in preference of M / s. the Regional Meteorological
Centre, RC Church Colaba, Mumbai, for installation of 'C' Band Doppler Weather
Radar in Gut. No. 132, Compartment No. 39, at Village Mhaismal, Taluka- Khultabad,
Dist. Chatrapati Sambhaji Nagar in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.6 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.20
ha. consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.8. M/s. Regional Meteorological Center, RC
Church Colaba, Mumbai for installation of 'C' BAND Doppler Weather Radar in Gut.
No. 132, Compartment No. 39, at Village Mhaismal, Taluka- Khultabad, Dist.
Chatrapati Sambhaji Nagar in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 24.11.2023 and dated 10.12.2024 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Chatrapati Sambhaji Nagar shall ensure
compliance of the condition no. 1.2 & 1.13 in Final order dated 10.12.2024,
before handover actual possession to the User Agency
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the '1in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (1), Chatrapati Sambhaji Nagar shall ensure the
strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report
in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
