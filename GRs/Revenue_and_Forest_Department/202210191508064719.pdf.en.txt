The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur shall disburse the funds only after verifying that the funds are covered under the scheme Strengthening of Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board (Programme) (2406 8696).
(iv) The funds shall be spent only after ascertaining that the proposals under the scheme have been approved by the competent authority as required.

VI. Care should be taken to keep the required accounts in order/ accurate in relation to the said expenditure.
VII.
