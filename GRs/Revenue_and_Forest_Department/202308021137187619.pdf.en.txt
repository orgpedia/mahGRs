Director, Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun As per intimation vide letter dated 30/06/2023, 03 (three) probationary officers of the course 2021-2023 of the Indian Forest Service, Maharashtra Cadre of the batch of 2021 are undergoing training at Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun. The officers under the said training will be relieved from the ongoing professional training at Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun to join the respective State Cadres for 4 months On the Job Training (OJT) training in the second week of August, 2023. Accordingly, the Government was considering creation of 03 (three) supernumerary posts of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the Junior Time Pay Scale (Pay Scale Level 10 in the Pay Matrix) as per the practice for conducting" On the Job Training (OJT)" training for the probationary officers of the Indian Forest Service of 2021 batch.
Decision of the Government: 03 (three) probationary officers of the Maharashtra Cadre of the Indian Forest Service of the batch of 2021 will be relieved from the Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun in the second week of August, 2023. Director, Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun As informed vide letter dated 30/06/2023, the said probationary officers are required to undergo 16 weeks (dated 07.08.2023 to 24.11.2023) On the Job Training (OJT) in the Forest Circle of the State on joining the State.
The Government is approving the conduct of 16 weeks" On the Job Training (OJT)" training to the said probationary BWSE officers as informed by the Principal Chief of Forest (Head of Forest Force), Nagpur vide letter dated 20/07/2023.
