The distribution of assistance shall be made in accordance with the decisions taken by the Government at that time and in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Government Decision issued in that decision, the accounts, records/ registers of the distribution of this assistance shall be kept up to date at the level of the office collecting and distributing the grant in accordance with the prescribed procedures. In order to have complete control over the expenditure, the Controlling Officers (Divisional Commissioners) should disburse the grants handed over to them to the districts which need the funds, without fully disbursing them to the District Collectors. The grants given to the District Collectors should not be considered for their additional grant demand unless they have spent a substantial amount directly. Also, the grants disbursed by the Divisional Commissioners should be reallocated before seeking additional grants from the government. Also, while demanding more provision, the concerned district collector should get such a certificate that the entire provision demanded can be spent. Explanatory reasons for the additional subsidy should also be mentioned. Similarly, the department should send the monthly expenditure statement of the previous month by the 5th of every month without fail.
