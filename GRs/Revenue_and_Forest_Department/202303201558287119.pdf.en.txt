The Proposal:
The presence of leopards in Walwa and Shirala taluks of Sangli district has increased. The Minister (Forests), Sir has made a statement that a committee of Hon'ble Members of the Legislative Assembly will be constituted to suggest measures on human-leopard conflict in the State. Accordingly, the government is considering the formation of a committee to suggest measures to reduce the number of leopards, their increased presence in urban settlements and the suffering caused to the citizens.
Government Decision: The following committee is being constituted to suggest measures to deal with the increased number of leopards in the state, their increased presence in urban settlements and the suffering caused to the citizens as well as human-leopard conflict.
