Preamble: Reference No. The State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) has been set up jointly by the Central Government and the State Government to provide assistance to the affected persons in the event of natural disasters in the State. Prescribed natural calamities have been included for the permissibility of assistance. A proposal to provide assistance from the State Disaster Response Fund to the next of kin of the deceased and the injured persons in the fire at SVS Aqua Company, Pune is under reference No. The government has received 3 letters. Accordingly, the proposal to provide financial assistance from the State Disaster Response Fund was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: SVS Aqua Company, Pune. The Divisional Commissioner, Pune has been directed to provide financial assistance as per the norms of State Disaster Response Fund to the next of kin of the deceased and the injured persons in the fire incident of 07.06.2021. 68,25,400/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Four Hundred only) is being approved by this Government.
The amount payable to the heirs of the deceased and the injured persons is being made available to the Collector, Pune through the Divisional Commissioner, Pune. The Cell Officer M-11 should disburse the funds on the budgeted distribution system.
3.
