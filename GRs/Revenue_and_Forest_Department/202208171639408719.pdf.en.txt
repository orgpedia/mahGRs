Government circular:
Refer to 1. By a Government Decision dated May 10, 2021, both Tehsildars and Naib Tehsildars have been declared as State Level Cadres. Supplementary instructions have been issued in pursuance of the Government decision at Reference No. 2.
In accordance with the provisions of this Government Order, the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 notified by the General Administration Department, the service excellence at the Revenue Division level is considered for all the purposes for which the service excellence is required for the period of appointment to the post of Deputy Tahsildar and thereafter for the period of service in this post. Further, there is a provision to compile the State Level Service Excellence List of the cadre of Naib Tehsildar for the limited purpose of promotion from the post of Naib Tehsildar to Tehsildar cadre at the State Level as per the procedure prescribed by the Government Decision dated 13.04.2022 of the Department of Revenue.
In view of this provision, all the revenue departmental commissioners should collect the seniority of the seniority submitted to the government and appoint a deputy tehsildar. The Interim Service Merit List of 01.01.2015 has been published vide Circular No. 3. In this circular, the Divisional Commissioner has included the date of birth of the Deputy Tahsildar in this interim service merit list, the date of retirement, whether the officer concerned is working or how, in which cadre if the officer concerned has been promoted and when, the marks of the officers of the direct service in the commission, the years of the direct service examination/ promotion selection list, all the Deputy Tahsildars who are working or how. 2 The Government have examined and brought to the notice of all concerned the said Interim Service Excellence List as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 13.04.2022 and have received the representations of the concerned on this Interim Service Excellence List and have submitted their self-explanatory comments on the same to the Government.
The feedback received from all the Divisional Commissioners on the objections/ suggestions on the Interim Service Merit List will be scrutinized at the government level, after examining the feedback given by the Divisional Commissioners and making necessary changes and taking into account the records available with the government, for the limited purpose of promotion to the post of Tehsildar, Naib Tehsildar (Gazetted) Group-B, dated 01.01.2015

The final list of services. Pursuant to the adjudication on various pending original applications before the Benches of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Form A is being published.
In this list, the seniority of the candidates who have been recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission by nomination and who have joined within the prescribed time limit has been determined according to their score. Since the seniority of the Deputy Tahsildars finalized by all the Divisional Commissioners at their level has been compiled at the state level on the basis of the information made available to the government, the Divisional Commissioner will be responsible if any questions arise regarding the seniority list in this regard.
