GOVERNMENT MEMORY: Under the Finance Division, Reference No. 1, Government Decision, dated 29th May, 2020, it has been decided to provide insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance to the employees who die due to COVID-19 virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
Late Dilip Bhalchandra Dhere, Driver, an employee working at the establishment of Tehsil Office, Tuljapur under the District Collector, Osmanabad has died on 05/06/2021 due to Corona Virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
3. The Collector, Osmanabad has sanctioned an ex-gratia of Rs.20,000/-to the next of kin of Late Dilip Bhalchandra Dhere, Driver, as per Government Resolution, Finance Division No.Parivartan-2020/ PR No.4/ Expenditure-9, dated 29/05/2020. A proposal for sanction of ex-gratia assistance to the tune of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) has been submitted vide letter No. 2 as under.
4. Late Dilip Bhalchandra Dhere, Driver of the vehicle was on duty of" prevention" in view of the universal epidemic of" COVID-19" and he was on duty for a period of 14 days prior to the date of his death, as certified by the Collector, Osmanabad.
5. The death of late Dilip Bhalchandra Dhere, driver is related to" COVID-19" virus, the report of the concerned Qutawal Hospital, Tuljapur stated.
