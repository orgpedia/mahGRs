GOVERNMENT DECISION: Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide reference No. Under the Maharashtra Subordinate Trade Regulation Scheme, 461 temporary posts created under the scheme have been requested to be approved for continuation in the financial year 2023-24.
Earlier, the Revenue and Forest Department had issued reference No. 46 temporary posts created under the Maharashtra Subordinate Trade Regulation Scheme Post-Graduation Scheme under Government Decision No. 3 and 4 have been extended for the year 2022-23 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Finance Department Reference No. 5 The Government vide Order dated 08.02.2023 has empowered all the Administrative Departments to extend the period of continuance of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier Review but not included in the rolls from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Accordingly, 461 temporary posts created under the Maharashtra Subordinate Trade Regulation Scheme (MTSRPS) have been filled. 01.03.2023 to Dy. Continuation up to 31.08.2023 is being accorded with the approval of the Government as per the prospectus attached. This extension of reference No. These posts are being given subject to the condition that they are included in the figure fixed under Government Decisions 1 and 2.
The expenditure on establishment of these posts should be met from the grants available in the financial year 2023-24 under the head 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 105 Forest Products, (01) Counselling by Government Agency, (01) Counselling by Government Agency (Tendu) (2406 0452), (01) Salary and should be spent for the above posts within the limits of the budgetary provisions to be approved. During this period, after reviewing the sanctioned posts in their establishment, the revised figure needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. No extension of time will be granted to finalise the revised schedule.
