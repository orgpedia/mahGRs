Government order:
Subject to the provisions of Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and the conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Shri Dayalsinh Thakur, Deputy Collector, Aurangabad Division is being retained and appointed as Deputy Collector and Deputy General Manager (Navnagar), Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Thane on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of three years.
2. The services of Shri Dayalsinh Thakur, Deputy Collector are being transferred to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Works Department, Ministry, Mumbai in pursuance of his deputation as Deputy Collector and Deputy General Manager (Navnagar), Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Thane.
3. Mr. Dayalsinh Thakur, Deputy Collector to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai immediately.
