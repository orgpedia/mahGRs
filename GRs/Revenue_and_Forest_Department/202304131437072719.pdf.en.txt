Letter No. Resettlement/ Works-I/ Extension Proposal/ February, 2023, dated 10/02/2023 Preamble: As per the above decision, under the Government Resolution No. 1, a review of the posts at the zonal level of the Resettlement Department has been decided and the figure of 32 posts in the Konkan Division has been fixed as per this Government Resolution.
A total of 32 sanctioned posts for rehabilitation work under Konkan Division as per the diagram, 6 temporary+ 2 temporary posts in Palghar district through external mechanism, 38+2 (through external mechanism) were extended from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 as per the decision of the Government dated 11/10/2022 in Reference No.5. Now, under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in Reference No.6 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend all existing temporary posts in their establishments and offices under their administrative control from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023. Therefore, the matter of extension of the said 38+2 (through external mechanisms) temporary posts from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby approve the extension of the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 for the posts to be filled up through 38+ 2 external mechanism mentioned in the accompanying statement for the rehabilitation of project oustees in the office of the Commissioner, Konkan Division, Navi Mumbai and District Rehabilitation Office, Thane/ Raigad/ Ratnagiri/ Sindhudurg/ Palghar, subject to the following conditions.

GOVERNMENT NOTE: EST-2023/ S.No.23/ 2-3, 1. All these posts shall be deemed to be temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department.
(2) None of the posts in the return shall remain vacant or under additional charge for a period of more than six months.
3. Out of the expenditure on these 38+2 (externally) temporary posts for the financial year 2023-2024, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 5 posts in the office of the Commissioner, Konkan Division, Navi Mumbai should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5 Main Account Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare 202 (02)- Scheme for commencement of production- (03) Rehabilitation of project affected persons- (03) (01)- Commissioner Konkan (2235 1871) (Non-Plan)" and the expenditure under the same head should be incurred from the sanctioned grants for the financial year 2023-2024. Further, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 33+2 (externally) posts on rehabilitation establishments in the Collectorates should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5- 2235 Plan under Social Security and Welfare Five Year Plans Scheme-202 (04) Plan to Start Production under State Schemes- (04) (01) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons- (04) (01)-Commissioner Konkan (22351933) (Under Schemes)" and it should be shared under the same head from the sanctioned grants for the year 2023-2024. The expenditure under these schemes should be finally transferred to the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation.
This Government decision is being issued as per the powers conferred to the Administrative Departments under Government Decision No. Designation 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 08/02/2023 of the Finance Department.
