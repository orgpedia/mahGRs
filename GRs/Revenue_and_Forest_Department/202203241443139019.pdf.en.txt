(1) The funds received under this order by the Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall be handed over to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara as the concerned Controlling Officer.

The budget should be distributed immediately through BEAMS.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for the civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation to pay the dues of the civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Dhom Canal Department No. 2, Satara should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and the concerned Collector.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
The Executive Engineer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are spent by the end of March 2022. Also, the sanctioned amount cannot be kept in any bank account, self-financing account or government account without expenditure.
8) The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned system shall ensure that no funds under the head of the main account head" Demand No.C-10, Main Account Head 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01, Major Irrigation Commercial, 01, Director and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat)" mentioned in the said order, remain outstanding or the funds received under the said order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Personal Principal Account) of the concerned system.
The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned machinery shall ensure that no short payment of one year is pending under the head' Demand No. C-10, Main Account, Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01, Major Irrigation Commercial, 01, Director and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects (Programme), 53 Major Constructions (4701-H727) (Dattamat)'.
