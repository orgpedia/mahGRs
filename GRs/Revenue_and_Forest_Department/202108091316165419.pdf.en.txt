The above officers are being promoted on ad-hoc/ temporary basis subject to the following conditions:
(i) M. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015. Against the decision given by the State Government on 04/08/2017, Ma. The Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No.
The General Administration Department/ 16B has issued a notification regarding the filling up under 28306/2017. As per the instructions given under the Government Decision dated 07/05/2021, only on a temporary basis. The decision is being taken based on a High Court order.
(ii) The promotion of the above officers shall be subject to the final seniority of Assistant Conservator of Forests, Group-A (Junior Grade) Cadre and consequent changes therein and the promotion shall not entitle the above officers to seniority from the date of such promotion.
(iii) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the said temporary promotion will not entitle the officers in the select list to any regularisation or promotion.
(iv) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 2 (d) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the said officers to be appointed by promotion shall be required to join within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of issue of the order of promotion.

(vi) The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officers without any prior notice or notice.
(vii) The said promotion shall be admissible to the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai/ Nagpur/ Aurangabad as well as to the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India. High Court/ High Court. If any claim/ petition is filed regarding seniority from the cadre of Assistant Conservator of Forests or Divisional Forest Officer in the Supreme Court etc., the same shall be subject to the final disposal of the pending petition.
The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting. If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, then they are not interested in the promotion, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, D. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled. Also, the officers who refuse promotion should be stopped immediately if they have been given the benefit of 12/24 years of the post of Assistant Conservator of Forest under the Assured Progress Scheme.
Please note that they will be given this benefit after direct promotion to the cadre of Assistant Conservator of Forests.
(5) The option of fixation of pay should be given immediately after the promoted officer takes charge of the post of Assistant Conservator of Forests. If such an option is not given, their salary shall be determined in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, Rule No. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The Controlling Authorities shall relieve the officer who has been given the said appointment/ posting immediately to appear in place of the new appointment. Also, the concerned Controlling Authorities should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail website ashok. lakkas@nic. in about the date on which the concerned officers were relieved from their duties to appear in place of the new appointees.
