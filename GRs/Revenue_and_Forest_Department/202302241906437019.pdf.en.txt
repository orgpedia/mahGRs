The Proposal:

Beed, Rs. 2,53,45, 428/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Three Lakhs Forty Five Thousand Four Hundred and Twenty Eight only), the request for grant of administrative approval was made by the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Pune vide Reference No. 1 is written here.
The said budget has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Aurangabad for the work and the technical approval has been granted by the competent authority (Superintending Engineer, Osmanabad) to grant technical sanction at the field level and the Chief Engineer (Architecture) and (Electrical), Regional Department of Public Works, Aurangabad, who are the highest technical authorities at the field level, have agreed.
The budget is based on the 2019-2020 budget.
Government's decision:
The Deputy Inspector General of Registration and Deputy Controller of Stamps, Aurangabad has sanctioned Rs. 2,53,45, 428/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Three Lakhs Forty Five Thousand Four Hundred and Twenty Eight only) as per Annexure A, subject to the following conditions.
(i) Before undertaking the said work, it is mandatory to get the maps and plans of this work approved by the Government Architect. It must also be signed by the consumer.
(ii) The ownership of the land should be ascertained before starting the work.
(ii) Government Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, D.C., on the rates of State Tariff Ex-Schedule Matters while giving technical approval. Action to be taken as on 11.04.2017.
(vi) Use of e-tendering system for matters relating to procurement in the proposal or for the approval of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour. Centre for Procurement of Goods and Services under Government Decision dated 24.08.2017
