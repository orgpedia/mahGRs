23rd September, 2011 Preamble: In order to make the administration of the State more dynamic and people-oriented, the e-Governance Policy of the State has been amended vide Preamble No. Number 4 is determined by the government decision here.
A comprehensive work procedure has been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 29.10.2001 for computerization of work in Government departments/ offices. The government has also decided to form a project implementation committee in every administrative division to implement the computerization project under the administrative department. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a Project Implementation Committee under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Forest) to implement the e-governance project under the Forest Sub-Division of the Revenue and Forest Department.
Government Decision: The General Administration Department has issued the above Reference No. Considering the guidelines/ directions given under the Government Decision mentioned herein 1-4, a Project Implementation Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Forests) is being constituted to implement the e-Governance Project under Forest Sub-Division of Revenue& Forest Department as follows:

If there is no unanimity in the committee regarding the approval of the e-governance project, the project will be decided by the government of the Department of Information Technology. The recommendations are to be submitted to the High Powered Committee constituted on 5th October, 2001.
After the approval of the Project Implementation Committee, the procurement/ contract in respect of e-governance related matters such as provision of computerization, availability of hardware/ software, development/ maintenance of website, purchase of other IT related equipment and development of computer system, repair and improvement should be done in the manner prescribed by the Government and in accordance with the policy to be decided by the Government as per the decision/ circular issued from time to time.
The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary of the Office concerned shall be the Member Secretary of the said Committee for the purpose of considering the matter before the Committee, the Office concerned shall conduct the meeting of the Project Implementation Committee, prepare the minutes of the meeting and take further action accordingly.
Committees should meet as often as they deem necessary.
