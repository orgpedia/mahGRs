Government's decision:
Development of Nature Tourism Sites Under this scheme, 6 temporary posts working at Shri Chauranginath Nature Tourism Site have been allowed to continue during the financial year 2020-21 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021. The deadline has been extended by the Government Decisions 2 and 3. The Cell Officer (Cell-3), Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide Reference No. Under Section 5, it has been requested to approve the continuation of the proposed 6 temporary posts in the financial year 2021-22.
Finance Department Reference No. 4 Under the Government Decision dated 15.02.2021, all the administrative departments have to fill up the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as those created after the approval of the review earlier but which have not been included in the rolls. All such existing temporary posts have been granted extension of tenure from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
Accordingly, under the Scheme of Development of Nature Tourism Sites, Government approval is being accorded for continuation of 6 temporary posts at Shri Chowringhee, Nature Tourism Sites from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 as per the attached prospectus. Reference No. 1 These posts are being offered subject to the condition that they are included in the pay band fixed by the Government.

The cost of salary and allowances for these 6 posts should be met from the grants available in the financial year 2021-22 under the head Head- 2406, Forestry and Wildlife, 800 (02) Other expenses, under Five Year Plans, Scheme (02) (06) Ecotourism (State) (2406 2295) 01-Salary under State Schemes and the expenditure for the above posts should be within the limits of the budgetary provisions to be approved.
