Preamble: The Government have approved the continuation of 69 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023 as per the Government Decision of Reference No. 3 and 4.
Now, vide letter Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for Government approval for continuation of 69 posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali during the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Department of Finance has been empowered to extend the term of temporary posts included in the format of the Department and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. 6 has been provided to the administrative departments under the Government Decision.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 02.23/ PS No.57/ F-1
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department, Reference No.6, the Government is approving the continuation of 69 temporary posts in the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali (as mentioned in the annexure attached) for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 69 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, under the head" 2406 Afforestation and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Afforestation, 800 Other Expenditure, (01) Other Expenditure, (01) (02) Forest Upavanes (24060659)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08.02.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
