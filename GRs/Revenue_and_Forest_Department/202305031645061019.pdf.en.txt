Preamble: The 31 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims in Nashik Division were approved for extension from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 vide Government Resolution dated 14/10/2022 issued at Reference No.5.
As per the Government Order dated 8/02/2023 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up all the existing temporary posts in their establishment and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control. 01/03/2023 to Dy. The authority has been given the power to extend the deadline till 31/08/2023. Department of Finance, Nashik. A total of 31 temporary posts sanctioned as per the figure fixed by the Government Decision dated 28/01/2010. 01/03/2023 to Dy. The proposal for extension of the period for the current year up to 31/08/2023 has been submitted by the Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation), Nashik Division to the Government vide Reference No. 7. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these 31 temporary posts.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have approved the extension of 31 temporary posts mentioned in the attached statement for the rehabilitation of project oustees in the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik Division, Nashik and District Rehabilitation Office, Nashik/ Dhule/ Jalgaon/ Ahmednagar from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 subject to the following conditions.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: EST-2023/ PR No. 46/ R-3 1. All these posts should be considered as temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to the holders of these posts as per rules.
(2) None of the posts in the return shall remain vacant or under additional charge for a period of more than six months.
3. Out of the expenditure for the financial year 2023-2024 on these 31 posts, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 6 posts in the office of the Commissioner, Nashik Division, Nashik should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5 Main Account Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare (03) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, Commissioner Nashik (03) (03) (2235 1891) (Mandatory) (Dattamat)" and the expenditure under the same head should be spent from the grant sanctioned for the year 2023-2024. Further, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 25 posts on rehabilitation establishments in the Collectorates should be put under the head" Demand No.C5, Main Accounts Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, (04) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, Commissioner Nashik (04) (03) (2235 1951) (Mandatory) (Dattamat)" and it should be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2023-2024 under the same head. The expenditure under these schemes should be finally transferred to the Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Finance Department Government Decision, No:- Designation 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08/02/2023.
