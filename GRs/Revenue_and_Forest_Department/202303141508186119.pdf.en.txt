Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 of the Finance Department, 1075 temporary posts in the figure of Sub Divisional Officer, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer in the establishment and under their command of the office of Divisional Commissioner, Pune and Collector, Pune, Satara, Sangli, Solapur and Kolhapur have been created in the context of Revenue and Forest Department for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023. The extension was granted by the Government Decision dated 03/10/2022.
2. Under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the rolls with effect from 01/03/2023. The administrative departments have been empowered to extend the deadline till 31/08/2023. Accordingly, the extension of 1075 temporary posts outside the figure on the establishment of the Divisional Commissioner and the zonal offices under him in the Pune Division was under the consideration of the Government for the above period.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1075 posts of Deputy Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers in the establishment of the offices of the Divisional Commissioner, Pune and the District Collector, Pune, Satara, Sangli, Solapur and Kolhapur and in the field offices under them are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the conditions of the Government Decision dated 08/02/2023 under the Finance Department.
2. Expenditure on such temporary posts subject to reference No. 03 The amount of the relevant statement accompanying the Government decision hereunder shall be deducted from the budgetary grants sanctioned for the year 2023-24 under the head of accounts shown in Annexure- No. 05.
