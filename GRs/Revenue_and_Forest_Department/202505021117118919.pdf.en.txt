The Proposal: -
On 22nd December, 1967, the then Hon'ble Chief Minister of Kerala, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu visited the Koyna area to rehabilitate the persons / families affected by the earthquake in the remote area. The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. In it, Ma. The Chief Minister, Maharashtra State is the ex-officio President. The Chief Secretary is the ex-officio Vice-Chairman / Trustee and the Joint Secretary, Revenue & Forest Department / R-5 is also the ex-officio Secretary / Trustee. As per the provisions of Rule B of the aforesaid Committee Regulations, out of the total 9 Trustees of the Trust, 3 are ex-officio Trustees and the remaining 6 are elected by the members of the Legislative Assembly from the Koyna earthquake affected taluka. Also, 2 Hon'ble Members of the Legislative Assembly are inducted as invited members. The government was considering reconstituting the Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee as a new cabinet was formed in the state due to the general elections to the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly in 2024.
The Government's Decisions:
With the formation of a new cabinet in the state, the Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee's board of trustees needs to be reconstituted. Accordingly, the Board of Trustees is being reconstituted as per the provisions of Rule 6 of the Rules and Regulations of the Koyna Earthquake WaT Funding Committee as follows. : -
