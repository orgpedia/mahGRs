Government's decision
A total amount of Rs. 87,07,000/- (Rupees Eighty Seven Lakhs Seven Thousand Only) has been provided under the Jayakwadi Project for the construction of new Khampimpri to Prabhudgaon road asphalting works in the rehabilitated village of Mauje Khampimpri in Shevgaon Taluka of Ahmednagar district.
