Preamble: In pursuance of the above notification, the Government has declared 96.01 sq.km. area of Butibori and North Umred Forest Range in Nagpur Forest Division of Nagpur District as Munia Conservation Reserve. The Government was considering the formation of a Muniya Conservation Reserve Management Committee to manage the area and to advise the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) and the Chief Wildlife Warden.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of Section 36B (1) and (2) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972, a Management Committee is being constituted for the management of Muniya Conservation Reserve as under:

The functions of the Management Committee shall be as follows:
(1) The jurisdiction of the Committee shall be limited to the Munia Conservation Reserve.
The Committee shall forward proposals and suggestions to the Chief Wildlife Warden for protection and management of the Conservation Reserve and shall provide necessary support.
The Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the provisions of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and other forest laws and Acts in the conservation reserve.
The tenure of the Committee shall be for a period of three years from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
