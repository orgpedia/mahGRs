ORDER:-
As per the guidelines issued by Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pension vide notification dated 30.12.2021 for the promotion of IFS officers, it is necessary to file Immovable Property Return (IPR) for the previous year for being considered for appointment in the next level of pay in the pay matrix. According to minutes of Screening Committee held on 27.12.2022 for Promotion to Principal Chief Conservator of Forests grade, Smt. Imtienla Ao, IFS (1992) was found fit for Promotion to Principal Chief Conservator of Forests grade.
However, at that time she had not submitted her Immovable Property Return (IPR) online for the previous year as mandated by Government of India. Hence, she was not given Proforma Promotion to the grade of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests.
2. In the Screening Committee held on 17.12.2024 for Promotion to Principal Chief Conservator of Forests grade, it was decided that the benefits of pro-forma promotion be given to Smt. Imtienla Ao, IFS (1992) immediately after she submits her IPR online. Smt.
Imtienla Ao, IFS (1992) has submitted her Immovable Property Return online as on 01.01.2025 on 10.01.2025.
3. Accordingly, she is now being promoted to the grade of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Level 16 in the Pay Matrix) from the date mentioned before her name, as per Rule 8 (5) and (6) of IFS (Pay) Rules, 2016 and guidelines issued by Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India vide letter dated 18.05.2023.
