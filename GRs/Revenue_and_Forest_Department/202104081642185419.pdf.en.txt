Introduction: Due to the COVID-19 pandemic situation in the country, the The lockdown has been imposed since March 25, 2020. This has adversely affected the construction industry. As a result, the supply of materials to the construction sector has been affected to a great extent. Also, due to the return of a large number of migrant workers, the work in the construction sector remained incomplete and as a result, a large number of construction projects have been adversely affected. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken in respect of licences granted for minor mineral extraction and transportation:
The Union Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs vide Reference No. 1 In view of the unforeseen and emergency situation arising out of the notification, all the concerned authorities are directed to consider extending the statutory period of approval of permits, permissions, no-objection certificates, construction start certificates, occupancy certificates, fee to be deposited with the Authority, etc. by 6 months automatically. As per the directions of the Central Government, the State Urban Development Department vide Reference No. According to the circular, the time limit for various permits, no-objection certificates, invoices, etc. required for construction permits has been extended by 9 months.

The Central Government Issue No. 1 The time limit for grant of minor mineral exploration permits to builders issued by the Collectorate or its subordinate offices based on the guidelines herein is also required to be extended for a period of 6 months.
Accordingly, if the excavation and transportation has not been completed for the license expiring after March 25, 2020, the deadline is being extended for a further period of 6 months.
Considering the impossibility due to unforeseen and emergency situations due to the above reasons, the Collector should take action to extend the deadline for the next 3 months, after taking into account the appropriate reasons if the construction business cannot complete the excavation and transportation as per the given license.
