GOVERNMENT MEMORY: Under the Finance Division, Reference No. 1, Government Decision, dated 29th May, 2020, it has been decided to provide insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance to the employees who die due to COVID-19 virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
2. Late Pandurang Sakharam Korde, Sepoy, an employee working at the establishment of Tehsil Office, Mehkar under Collector, Buldana has died on 22/05/2021 due to Corona Virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duty.
3. The Collector, Buldana has sanctioned an ex-gratia amount of Rs.20,000/-to the next of kin of late Pandurang Sakharam Korde, Sepoy, as per Government Resolution, Finance Division No.Sanikya-2020/ PR No.4/ Expenditure-9, dated 29/05/2020. A proposal for sanction of ex-gratia assistance to the tune of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) has been submitted vide letter No. 2 as under.
4. Collector Buldana has certified that late Pandurang Sakharam Korde, Sepoy was performing the duty of" prevention" in view of the universal epidemic of" COVID-19" and was present on duty during the period of 14 days preceding the date of his death.
