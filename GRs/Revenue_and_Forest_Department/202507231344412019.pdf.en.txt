(2) A separate booklet on Department-wise District Affiliation Training and Independent Workforce Training Programme under various departments has been released to the Deputy Collectors under the aforesaid examination. Accordingly, the District Collector concerned has issued a circular to the Sub-Divisional Magistrate under the scrutiny. The training program should be prepared immediately from the 24.07.2025 and a copy of it should be made available to the Sub-Collector under examination.
(2) The Sub-Divisional Collector under scrutiny shall submit the same to the Resident Deputy Collector and Liaison / Coordinating Officer, Deputy Commissioner (Revenue) of the concerned district by e-mail on a daily basis without fail.
(3) The nodal officer of the department and the liaison officer of the training institutes shall exercise direct control over the trainee. Also, evaluate the work done on the trainee every month, take a monthly report on the basis of experience, and evaluate the report. The report should be certified every month and sent to the General Administration Department.
(4) The District Collector of the concerned district shall submit a report to the Additional Commissioner (Revenue) regarding the day-to-day attendance / absence / conduct etc. of the Sub-Collector under examination.
(5) If it is found that the trainee has misbehaved or not taken the training seriously enough during the training period, such incidents shall be immediately brought to the notice of the concerned Collector by contacting the Coordinating Officer / Nodal Officer of the Department and the Liaison Officer of the training institutions as well as the Additional Commissioner (Revenue). The report in this regard should be submitted by the Coordinating Officer, Additional Commissioner (Revenue) to the Revenue and Forest Department and General Administration Department of the Government.
(6) Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department, No.EST-2025 / P.No.40 / E-8, D. A large number of posts have been created to pay the salary of 35 probationary candidates in the Sub-Collector cadre in the Combined Probationary Training Programme-10 (CPTP-10) under the 23.04.2025. The salary and allowances of the Sub-Divisional Magistrate under probation in the allotted division will be paid through the concerned Divisional Commissioner.
(7) The trainees are required to pass the examination to be conducted under the training programme within the prescribed time frame during the department-wise district affiliation training period. Also, the instructions issued by the General Administration Department from time to time should be strictly followed for successful conduct of the training programme.
(8) Government Decisions for the evaluation of Sub-District Collectors under examination in the stages of Divisional Training, General Administration Department No. TRN-1114 / Q. 84/18 -B, D. The responsibility of taking action as directed in the 22.04.2015 shall lie with the Coordinating Officer, Additional Commissioner (Revenue).
