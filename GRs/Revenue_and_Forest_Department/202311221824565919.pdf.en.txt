Preamble: Reference No. As per the decision of the Government here, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) provides training to the candidates of Group-B cadre who are recruited directly by the State Service Examination every year under the combined examination conducted by Vanamati, Nagpur. Based on the final result of the State Service Examination-2021, the State Service Examination-2021 recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
A total of 13 probationary Deputy Superintendents, Land Records, Group-B candidates of Group-B Cadre who are directly recruited are required to undergo training under (CPTP-9). Therefore, the matter of creating more number of posts to pay the salary of the said 13 probationary candidates in the' Group-B' cadre of Vanamati, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the creation of a maximum number of posts for a period of two years from 03.10.2023 to 03/10/2025 for 13 Sub-Inspectors of Group-B, Deputy Superintendents of Land Records, Group-B, recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the State Service Examination-2021 and appointed by the General Administration Department for the programme under training.
2. The expenditure on salary for the period from 03.10.2023 to 03.10.2025 on the said supernumerary posts will be borne by the candidates under the purview of" Demand No. C-1, 2029
Land Revenue 103 Land Records 103 (01) (02) District Land Records (2029 0422) Chapter No. 01 Salary to be shared from the approved budgetary grants under this head.
