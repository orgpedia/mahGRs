GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 30.21/ S.No.69/ F-1
Government's decision:
As per the permission granted by the Planning Department and Finance Department under the scheme Rehabilitation of Villages in National Parks and Sanctuaries (2406-2241), the Government has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 10.00 lakh per family for 298 families and Rs. 3262.50 lakh for 48 land holders subject to the following conditions.
Proposed Expenditure:- Account head 2406 Afforestation and Wildlife, 02 Ecological Afforestation and Wildlife, 110 Conservation of Wildlife (00) (11) Determination of Rights and Privileges Rehabilitation of Villages in National Parks and Sanctuaries (State) (2406-2241).
The funds allocated shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
3. Care should be taken to keep the required accounts in order/ accurate in relation to the said expenditure.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, considering the urgency of the proposal submitted for the approval of the Government, should allocate funds within the limit of funds available on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS).
