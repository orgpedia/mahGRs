Department of Revenue and Forest, Government Resolution No: WLP- 09.21/ W.S.No.246/ F-1, Dy. As of October 28, 2021:
An amount of Rs. 6000.00 lakh has been sanctioned and budgeted for the year 2021-22 under the State Level Scheme for Establishment of Wildlife Handicaps (2406-2232). However, as per the guidelines issued by the Department of Finance in respect of disbursement of funds in 2021-22 under Government Resolution of Reference No.4, an amount of Rs. 1100.00 lakh has been reserved by the Department. Therefore, the government decision of Reference No. 6 is being cancelled and the next decision is being taken.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the proposal submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur under the letter of reference No. 5 and the funds available under the scheme of Establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406-2232), the Government have decided, subject to the following conditions, to establish a Wildlife Treatment Centre at Bavdhan of Pune Forest Division as per the attached form, at a total cost of Rs. 1100.00 lakhs is being disbursed.

Under the scheme, the establishment of (00) (10) Wildlife Disability Centre (2406 2232) should be done from the funds received from the Budget Distribution System.
(ii) The funds shall be spent only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical sanction has been accorded to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority, as required.
(iii) Ensure that no violation of the provisions of the Indian Forest Act, 1927, the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the guidelines of the National Zoological Park is committed and necessary permission is obtained.
(iv) It shall be ensured that the subsidy disbursed will be spent only on the works mentioned in the proposal which have received administrative approval of the competent authority.
(v) The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time and in accordance with the terms of the proposal.
The concerned Controller/ Enforcement Officer shall be responsible for following the instructions and procedures laid down by the Government for the proposed construction. It will also be their responsibility to avoid any financial irregularities.
VII. Procurement, if any, shall be made by following the procedure prescribed by the Government.
