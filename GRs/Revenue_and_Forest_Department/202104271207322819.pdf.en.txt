Read: 1) Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department No. LND 1092/ PR No.84/ J-1, dated 30.6.1992 2) Govt. Land 1097/2214/ PR No.93/ J-1, dated 8.7.1999 Preamble:
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code-1966 and the Maharashtra Land Revenue (Disposal of Government Land) Rules-1971, the rates to be charged in respect of land given in possession or on lease after taking possession of government land in advance, shall be revised by the government in accordance with the Government Decision dated 8.9.1999. By an order dated 8.07.1999, the following provisions have been made:
The rate of interest to be charged on the final price of government land given in advance and the amount due between the provisional price and the final price and the rate of annual ground rent of government land given on ground rent shall be equal to the' Prime Lending Rate' of the State Bank of India. This is done annually. The rate should be at par with the State Bank of India's Prime Lending Rate as on January 1 and should be constant for the entire year.
In pursuance of the said provision, the P.L.R. of the State Bank of India as on 01.01.2017 was
The Prime Lending Rate is announced and communicated to the regional agencies. But now, according to the revised guidelines of the Reserve Bank of India, the P.L.R. has been replaced by M.L.R. The term C.L.R. (Marginal Cost of Funds based Lending Rate) is being used. Accordingly, the State Bank of India has fixed the MCLR for the full year as on 01.01.2021 at 7%. Therefore, the government was considering to announce the rate of interest to be charged on the final price of the government land given in advance and the amount due between the temporary price and the final price and the rate of annual land rent of the government land rented by the land rent for the whole year 2021 and to inform it to the field offices.
Government circular:
The rate of interest to be charged on the amount of possession of such lands after giving advance possession of the government land, from the date of giving advance possession of the land to the date of fixing and paying the price of the final possession and the rate to be charged for land rent in respect of government lands leased out shall be 7% for the whole of the year 2021.
