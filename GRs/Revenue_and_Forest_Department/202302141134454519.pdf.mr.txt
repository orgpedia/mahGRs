GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA,
Revenue and Forest Department,
Order No. FDC-2022/CR-152/F-5
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032.
Dated: 13th February, 2023
Reference - 1) Government DO letter No.AEO-1122/5/2022/10, dtd.29/09/2022.
2) Managing Director, FDCM Ltd., Nagpur letter No.CSL/Director
/132 Nagpur, Dated 19/10/2022
Order
As per the powers conferred under Article 89 (1), (2), and (4) of the Articles of
Association of the Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited (“FDCM”), it
is hereby decided to nominate Managing Director, Maharashtra Tourism Development
Corporation Limited, Mumbai as Director in his / her ex-officio capacity on the Board of
Directors of Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited.
2. The appointment shall take effect from the date of allotment of Director Identification
Number or from the date of this order, whichever is later.
3. For the purpose of appointment the ex-officio having the additional charge will not
be reckoned/considered.
4. The appointment of Smt. Jayashree Bhoj, IAS, Nominee Director is being withdrawn
from the date of this order.
5. In order No.FDC-2022/CR-117/F-5 dated 13.10.2022 read Managing Director,
MTDC, Mumbai as Director instead of Mrs. Jayashree Bhoj, Nominee Director in the given
table with Sr. No. 5.
6. Managing Director/Company Secretary, FDCM Ltd. Nagpur to take necessary action
in this regards.
