GOVERNMENT ORDER: - The Chief Executive Officer and Special Duty Officer, Dharavi Redevelopment Project, vide the said letter, has informed the Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue) about the urgent need to fill up 5 posts of Deputy Tehsildars under Revenue and Forest Department in Dharavi Redevelopment Project Office. Accordingly, as 5 posts of Deputy Tehsildars of Dharavi Redevelopment Project Slum Rehabilitation Authority Bandra (East), Mumbai Suburban are required to be filled by immediate deputation, in view of the administrative exigencies, subject to the conditions and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, superannuation and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981, 1) Shri Rupesh Kisan Palve, Resident Deputy Tahsildar, Tehsil Office Borivali, Mumbai Suburban and 2) Shri. Lata Patil (Koli), Naib Tehsildar (Revenue), Tehsil Office Borivali, District Mumbai Suburban are being appointed as "Naib Tehsildar, Dharavi Redevelopment Project, Slum Rehabilitation Authority Bandra (East), District Mumbai Suburban" on deputation with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years with effect from the date of this order.
2. Mr. Palve and Mr. Patil (Koli), Naib Tehsildar is being unilaterally relieved from the government level to join the post of appointment given by the deputation. Accordingly, they should immediately join the posts of deputation and submit such report to the Government through CEO & Officer on Special Duty, Dharavi Redevelopment Project Bandra (East), District Mumbai Suburban.
This Government decision will be made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
