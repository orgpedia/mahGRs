This 'In-principle' approval is issued subject to the following conditions:-
1) The legal status of the land shall remain unchanged.
2) The user agency shall submit the certificate of the Collector, Gadchiroli under
the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional forest Dwellers (Recognition of
Forest Rights) Act, 2006 along with the Stage-1 Compliance Report.
3) As per Central Government (MoEF&CC) guidelines a single proposal must be
submitted for the entire project as per administrative approval and technical
order, Therefore, Deputy Conservator of Forests will certify that the proposal is
a standalone project.
4) The User Agency as well as DCF shall ensure that the present diversion shall not
form a part of any future proposal & will remain as an individual proposal only.
>) The demarcation of the proposed forest area shall be carried out at suitable
places as per the direction of the DCF concerned at the cost of User Agency.
6) (a) User Agency shall provide double degraded land for compensatory
afforestation. Details of land proposed for Compensatory afforestation (Record
of Rights & KML File), shall be submitted along with the Stage-1 Compliance
Report.
(b) The User Agency shall transfer the cost of raising and maintaining the
compensatory afforestation, at the current wage rate, to the State Forest
Department. The CA scheme may include afforestation of indigenous species
with appropriate provision for anticipated cost increase for works scheduled for
subsequent years;
7) Net Present Value:-
a) The Forest Department shall charge the Net Present Value (NPV) for the 2.00 ha. Forest land to be diverted under this proposal from the User
Agency as per the orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India dated 30/10/2002, 01/08/2003, 28/03/2008, 24/04/2008 and 09/05/2008 in IA
No.566 in WP (C) No.202/1995 and as per the guidelines issued by the Ministry vide letters No.5-1/1998-FC (Pt.If), dated 18/09/2003 as well as letter No.5-2/2006-FC, dated 03/10/2006 and 5-3/2007-FC, dated
05/02/2009 & File No.5-3/2011-FC(Vol-I, Dated 06/01/2022 in this
regard.
b) Additional amount of the NPV of the diverted forest land, if any, becoming due after finalization of the same by the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India on
# Page 3
receipt of the report from the Expert Committee, shall be charged from the User Agency. The User Agency shall furnish an undertaking to this effect.
8) All the funds received from the User Agency under the project shall be
transferred / deposited to CAMPA _ fund only 'through _ e-portal
(https://parivehs.nic.in);
9) No labour camp shall be established on the forest land and the User Agency
shall provide firewood preferably alternate fuels to the labourers and the staff
working at the site so as to avoid any damage and pressure on the nearby forest
areas;
10) The User Agency shall defray the cost of removal of 403 trees in the proposed
area. However, minimum number of trees will be felled. The trees so felled by
the Forest Department accoutred for separately in terms of exploration sale /
disposal and the cost of falling shall be recovered from the user agency.
11) The User Agency shall ensure that because of this project, no damage 1s caused
to the wildlife available in the area;
12)The user agency shall explore the possibility of translocation of maximum
number of trees identified to be felled and shall ensure that any tree felling shall
be done only when it is unavoidable and that too under strict supervision of the
State Fores Department;
13) The user agency in consultation with the concerned Deputy Conservator of
forest shall create and maintain alternate habitat/home for the avifauna, whose
nesting trees are to be cleared in this project. Bird's nests artificially made out of
eco-friendly material shall be used in the area, including forest area and human
settlements, adjoining the forest area being diverted for the project;
14) The total forest area utilized for this project shall not exceed 2 ha. and the forest
area diverted shall not be used for any purpose other than those shown in the
diversion proposal. The User Agency shall furnish an undertaking to this effect;
15) The cost of felling of trees shall be deposited by the User Agency with the State
Forest Department;
16) User Agency shall obtain Environment Clearance as per the provisions of the
Environmental (Protection) Act, 1986, 1f applicable;
17) Sufficient firewood, preferably the alternate fuel, shall be provided by the User
Agency to the labourers after purchasing the same from the State Forest
Department or the Forest development Corporation or any other legal source of
alternate fuel;
18) The User Agency shall ensure compliance to all acts, rules, regulations and
guidelines of the Ministry for the time being in force, as applicable to the subject
project;
19) The layout plan of the proposal shall not be changed without prior approval of
State Government (Forest Department).
# Page 4
20) The period of diversion under this approval shall be co-terminus with the period
of lease to be granted in favour of the user agency or the project life, whichever
is less.
21) The forest land shall not be used for any purpose other than that specified in the
project proposal.
22)The forest land proposed to be diverted shall under no circumstances be
transferred to any other agencies, department or person without prior approval of
the State and Central Government.
23) This approval shall be valid only for the diversion of forest land outside the
confines of the Protected Areas.
24) This sanction is subject to the final clearance by the competent authorities for
the project.
25) All other conditions as may be mandatory under relevant Acts, Rules and
Guidelines shall be complied by the User Agency.
26) Violation of any of these conditions will amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan
Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken as per the
provision of Adhiniyam and rules / guidelines issued there under;
27) Any other condition that the Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate
Change may stipulate from time to time in the interest of conservation,
protection and development of forests & wildlife.
28) Any breach of the conditions by the User Agency will be treated as violation of
the provisions of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
29) The compliance report shall be uploaded on e- portal (https://parivesh.nic.in/).
