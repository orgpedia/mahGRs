And whereas, the Pindari Minor Irrigation Scheme project is of state benefit and will benefit the general public, i.e., it is a public interest project and is site specific as per the guidelines of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 of the Government of India. (2) The Government of India have recommended diversion of 9.051 hectares of protected forest area under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980; and, therefore, the Government of India, after considering the proposal, have accorded in principle approval to the proposal submitted by the State Government under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 vide letter No. 3 above. - 5 The final approval has been given to the diversion under the letter hereunder; and since, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, vide his letter No. 6 above, has informed that the project machinery has fulfilled the conditions of the final approval letter dated 30/08/2019 of the Central Government; therefore, the Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the Project Authority i.e., the Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Department, Kalwa, Thane, be directed to take necessary action under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, for the construction of Pendhri Minor Irrigation Scheme at Mauje-Eklehre, Tt. A total of 9.051 hectares of forest land, as mentioned in the project proposal attached with the letter of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forest, Thane, Murbad and the Union Territory Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, in the schedule given below, which includes protected forest land, should be diverted for this proposal. This diversion should be completed subject to fulfilment of the terms and conditions in the letters of the Government of India dated 21/09/2017 and 30/08/2019 and also after fulfilment of the following conditions:
The Project Mechanism shall comply with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India as mentioned above.
