The Proposal:
For the construction of new building of the office of the Deputy Registrar of Registration, Amravati, District Yavatmal, Rs. Government's decision:
The Deputy Registrar of Registration, Amravati Subordinate to the Deputy Inspector General of Registration, Maregaon, District- Yavatmal, has sanctioned a sum of Rs. The Government is granting administrative approval to the invoice for an amount of Rs. 3,60,40, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Sixty Lakh Forty Thousand only) subject to the following conditions.
(i) It is mandatory to get the maps and plans of this work approved by the Government Architect before undertaking this work. It must also be signed by the consumer.
(ii) The ownership of the land should be ascertained before starting the work.
