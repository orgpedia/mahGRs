Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 22/02/2022 of the Finance Department, 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad as well as District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed, Sub Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers in the establishment and their subordinate offices were extended for the period from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022 under the Government decision dated 29/03/2022 of the Revenue and Forest Department.
2. Under the Government Order dated 18/08/2022 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of review earlier but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed were under the consideration of the Government for extension of the above period.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed in the establishment and in the area offices of Sub Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers under them are being extended for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 subject to the conditions of the Government Decision dated 18/08/2022 in the context of Finance Department.
2. Expenditure on such temporary posts subject to reference No. 03 In the relevant statement accompanying the Government decision herein, Raka-No. The amount under the head of accounts shown in section 05 should be shared from the budgetary grants sanctioned for 2022-23.
The Finance Department has directed all the departments to take note that the revised figure in the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 in the context of the Finance Department needs to be approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries in the meantime and no further extension of time will be granted to finalize the revised figure.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Departments under Finance Department, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18/08/2022.
