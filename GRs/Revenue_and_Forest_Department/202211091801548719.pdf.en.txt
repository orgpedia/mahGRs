The Proposal:
The National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) has decided to implement 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Strengthening of District Disaster Management Authorities (DDMA) in 115 most disaster-prone districts of the country, including 4 most vulnerable districts of Jalgaon, Nandurbar, Gadchiroli and Nanded. The National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) has prepared the modalities and signed an MoU with the central government in this regard. A disaster management consultant was appointed to implement it. The tasks assigned to them are fixed in the contract. Considering the monthly remuneration, the matter of paying it is under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A Disaster Management Consultant has been appointed to implement the scheme with a monthly remuneration of Rs. 70,000/- (Rupees Seventy Thousand only) will help the District Disaster Management Authority to implement the following measures to reduce the disaster risk of the concerned district.
Implementing the guidelines for disaster mitigation given at the United Nations World Conference in Sendai.
To assist in the preparation of District Disaster Management Plan keeping in view the Disaster Management Act, 2005 and the guidelines of the Sendai Council.
To conduct training programmes on colour training, capacity building and training at Taluka and Gram Panchayat level to create awareness about the disaster.
iv) Coordinate with the District Administration and Taluk and Gram Panchayats for disaster preparedness, disaster response and mitigation measures.
To create awareness among the people about disaster risk management at the district, taluka and gram panchayat levels.
