Order:
In exercise of powers under general approval granted by MoEF&CC, Govt. of India
vide Handbook of FCA Guidelines-2019, Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant 'In
principle' approval to the diversion of 0.278 ha. Forest Land under section-2 of the Forest
(Conservation) Act, 1980 for laying of underground Optical Fiber Cable passing within Right
of Way of existing SH-124 & SH-143 road at Shamgaon, Antawadi & Vagheri, Tal. Karad,
Dist. Satara.

2. This 'In-principle' approval is issued subject to the following conditions
1) The legal status of the land shall remain unchanged.
2) The cost of Net Present Value shall be exempted.
3) The Optical Fiber Cable Line will be laid within maximum size of the trench of 2.00
meters depth & 1.0 meter width with cable line under the PVC pipeline.
4) The land under project will be reclaimed by the User Agency for which the agency shall submit a bank guarantee at the rate of Rs.50/- per running meter and an undertaking to
that effect shall also be submitted. If the agency fails to reclaim the land to the
satisfaction of concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests, the cost for reclamation will be covered from the bank guarantee.
5) If the cable is going to be laid in the formation width of the road, then additional charges
in form of bank guarantee shall be given at the rate of Rs.35/- per running meter.
Similarly for reclamation of side drain and for tarred portion, the guarantee will be at
the rate of Rs.300/- per meter and Rs.750/- per meter respectively. An undertaking shall
be obtained from the User Agency that it has agreed to make good any loss to Forest/
Environment while laying the cable in the forest area and during maintenance of the same in future.
6) For further maintenance of the project, the agency shall obtain permission from the concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests as and when required.
7) The forest land shall not be used for any purpose other than specified in the proposal.
8) No felling of trees shall be done during the implementation of project.
9) No new forest area beyond the Right of Way of existing road should be broken for laying Optical Fiber Cable.
10) The User Agency shall defray the cost of Compensatory Afforestation over 1.0 ha.
degraded Forest land at Gut No. 592, Compt. No. 786 at Village Nandal, Tal. Phaltan, Dist. Satara.
11) Underground Optical Fiber Cable can be laid only outside the confines of the Protected Areas.
12) No labour camp shall be established on the forest land.
13) After completion of the project, User Agency will reclaim the area under Right of Way suitably.
14) The layout plan of the proposal shall not be changed without prior approval of the State Government.
15) This sanction is subject to the final clearance by the Department of Information &
Technology for the project and concerned PWD authorities for the project.
16) The User Agency shall compensate the private owners of forest land if any, in
accordance with the prevalent laws, rules & guidelines.

17) The User Agency shall obtain Environmental Clearance as per the provisions of the
Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, if applicable.
18) All other conditions as may be mandatory under relevant Acts, Rules and Guidelines
shall be complied by the User Agency.
19) All the conditions stipulated by the Forest Department shall be binding on User Agency.
20) The User Agency shall make good of any loss to Forest / Environment.

Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries, prior permission of the State Board for Wildlife should
