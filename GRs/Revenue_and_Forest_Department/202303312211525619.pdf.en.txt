The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. The funds are being given to them.
The above expenditure should be incurred as per the prescribed procedure and as per the monthly fund flow available on the budget distribution system. There should be no financial irregularities.
The funds should be spent only after verifying that the technical and administrative approval has been granted by the competent authority as required for the works proposed under the said schemes.
The Controlling Authorities are advised to ensure that no additional expenditure is incurred under any circumstances over and above the total disbursed/ to be disbursed.
5. While spending this provision, the terms and conditions laid down by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed.
6. Before drawing the funds available on the budget distribution system from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms/ conditions should be followed.
7. Limit expenditure to the extent of grants sanctioned and disbursed in the current financial year 2022-23.
(8) It shall be ensured that the amount withdrawn after the withdrawal of the said grant from the concerned treasury is recorded in the records of the Accountant General's Office under the same head and submit a certificate to the Government in this regard.
The Institute shall forthwith submit to the Government and the Accountant General the certificate of appropriation of the grants made by it for the previous year. Similarly, the detailed payment and utilization certificate of the subsidy being distributed now should also be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and the government immediately.
The Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur shall have the power to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure made out of the above grant and the said institution shall provide complete facilities to him for the same.
11. It should be ensured that the expenditure meets the conditions of the circulars dated 04.04.2022, 08.02.2023 and 16.03.2023 of the Finance Department.
The approval shall be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the Annexure to Government Decision No.MSC-2011/ P.No. 178/ F-5, dated 30.9.2011.
13. It shall be ensured that the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the provisions of the existing laws and rules are not violated while carrying out such activities.
14. While implementing the works, it should be ensured that the works are integrated and e-tendering system is adopted as per rules.
The above expenditure should be met from the grants available during the financial year 2022-23 under the head Demand No. C-07 Principal Head-2406, Forestry and Wildlife, Five Year Plans, Scheme (02) (06) Ecotourism (State) (2406-2295) under State Schemes.
