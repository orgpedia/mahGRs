Proposal: - Ma. The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" is functioning under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, State of Maharashtra and the sanctioned funds for sanction of new works for the year 2024-25 have been sanctioned as per the percentage of Taluk wise allocation. Accordingly, 35 per cent of the funds have been earmarked for Taluka Patan and the issue of administrative sanction for development works in Patan Taluka was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - With a total of 16 development works in Patan taluka for the purpose of devoting funds to the Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee only, as per the rules of the Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee, for the development of villages located in the remote areas of 8 taluks (Patan, Karad (North), Karad (South), Javali, Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Sangameshwar Vab Chiplun) falling in the Koyna earthquake prone area (Koyna), without spending any other works, the funds received by the committee from the State Government from the budget of Maharashtra and the office of the Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad Construction Department (South), Satara and Executive Engineer, Public Works (West) Department, Satara, a total of 16 works have been given technical approval. The amount of technical approval for 16 works is Rs. 2,35,00, 000 / - (Rupees Two Crore and Thirty Five Lakhs only). Accordingly, administrative sanction is being accorded to the development works in Patan Taluka from the sanctioned grant as mentioned in Annexure-A attached thereto. The funds will be distributed to the District Collector, Satara. 2 The Executive Committee of the Committee in its meeting dated 29.07.2013 has decided as follows for the implementation of the work under the Committee.
