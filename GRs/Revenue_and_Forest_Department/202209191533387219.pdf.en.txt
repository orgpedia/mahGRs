The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval regarding continuation of 10 temporary posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 under Reference No.
2. Accordingly, 10 temporary posts made available by way of category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" have been granted Government approval for continuation in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 under the Government decision of Reference No.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 have been conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No.

Government's decision:
Subject to the Government decision of Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, Government is granting approval for continuation of 10 temporary posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" in the year 2022-2023 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Therefore, the expenditure on establishment of 10 temporary posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (19) Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority (2406 A087)" should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
