Appointment on the post of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force) Maharashtra State, Nagpur
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASTRA
Revenue and Forest Department
Government Order No: AFO-2022/C.R.05/F-7
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date: 28th February, 2022
ORDER
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to promote Shri. Y. L. P. Rao, Indian Forest
Service (1986) to the Apex Scale (Level 17 in Pay Matrix) and appoint him as the Principal
Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur consequent
to the retirement of Shri. G. Saiprakash, I.F.S. on 31st January, 2022 (A.N.).
Shri. Y. L. P. Rao, IFS shall accordingly assume the charge on 28th February, 2022
(A.N.).
