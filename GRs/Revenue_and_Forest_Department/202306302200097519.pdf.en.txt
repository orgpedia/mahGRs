(5) During the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assignment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Period of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981, the concerned officer shall hold office at the place of posting. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers transferred by the parent office should be accepted. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
7. It may be noted that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
