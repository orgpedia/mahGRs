The officers concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action under the provisions of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, if they are found not to have joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of assumption of office in accordance with the said provision under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Retirement, Non-Service and Provisions of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
5. The concerned officer shall take care to join the place of posting within the prescribed period as per the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, non-acceptance of service and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981 or else the period of absence from the place of posting shall be deemed to be "non-working day" (& & 5 / 70 / /).
6. Leave of the officer concerned shall not be granted for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers transferred by the original office shall be entertained. If such applications are received, they should be returned by post to the concerned authorities at the original address.
7. In case of non-acceptance of appointment immediately under the said order or attempt to cancel the transfer by any kind of coercion, the concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action as it is misconduct as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
