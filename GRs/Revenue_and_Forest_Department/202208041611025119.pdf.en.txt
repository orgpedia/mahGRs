Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. As per Circular No. 1, instructions have been issued to allocate the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure required for the first nine months of 2022-23 (ending December, 2022) to the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEMS) for FY 2022-23, after recording the monthly fund statements, budget estimates, allocation and control system (BEMS) of budgetary provision by the administrative departments.
Under this scheme, the tribal account holders are given an advance by the government till the time the trees are auctioned after cutting them in their private ownership. According to the law, the payment must be made within the stipulated time. A total of Rs. 296.75 lakh is required for Yavatmal, Chandrapur, Amravati, Nagpur and Gadchiroli Forest Ranges and the funds are to be disbursed through BDS. 2 letter to the government.
The matter is under consideration of the government to disburse the funds.
