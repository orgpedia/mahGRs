Establishment of a Study Committee regarding Ghodbunder to Gaimukh elevated road and Gaimukh to Thane road development project.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
Government Resolution No: WLP-0122/ P.No.22/ F-1
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: February 17, 2022
Preamble: 17th Meeting of the State Board of Wildlife. The meeting was held on 12.10.2021. 8.8451 for widening of existing road from Ghodbunder to Gaimukh elevated road and Gaimukh to Thane under 7.12. The Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation (MSRDC) had submitted a proposal for the same. As per the decision taken by the State Wildlife Board in this case, the government was considering to form a study committee to examine the possibility of developing the road in such a way that the road could be upgraded with a suitable structure and 6 to 8 lanes and heavy vehicles could also pass through it.
Government's decision:
8.8451 for widening of existing road from Ghodbunder to Gaimukh elevated road and from Gaimukh to Thane. A Study Committee is being set up to study the proposal of diversion of forest land by involving representatives of Wildlife Institute of India, Dehradun, Wildlife Conservation Trust, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation as under:

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1. To examine the possibility of developing the road in such a way that the road can be upgraded and made 6 to 8 lanes with a suitable design by realigning the road and heavy vehicles can also pass through it and the existing road will be completely closed to traffic and wildlife will be free to move.
