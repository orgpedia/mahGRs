Introduction In the first (Budget) session of the year 2022 of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly on the irregularities in the land acquisition of Dhule-Solapur National Highway, Mr. Shirish Kumar Naik (Navapur), Member of Legislative Assembly, raised an unstarred question no. There were 40746. In pursuance of this question, the Government Memorandum No. 2022/55/ No. 28/ A-4, D. 28.04.2022 and Govt. W.S. 2022/55/ Q.No.28/ A-4, Dy. The Government had appointed Divisional Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation) Aurangabad Division as the Inquiry Officer vide order dated 06.05.2022. According to the investigating officer. The preliminary enquiry report was submitted to the Government vide letter dated 24.08.2022 and further orders were under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: A detailed and thorough inquiry/ investigation has been conducted by the Divisional Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation) Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad regarding the" irregularities in the land acquisition of Dhule Solapur National Highway." The Government has accepted the preliminary enquiry report submitted to the Government vide letter dated 24.08.2022. Based on the objections mentioned in the preliminary inquiry report, the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad, should take action by fixing responsibility on the concerned as per the rules and submit a compliance report to the government.
