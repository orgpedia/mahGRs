Preamble: 150 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme were approved to be continued in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per Government decision in Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for Government approval for continuation of 150 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
3. The powers to extend the term of office of temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 have been delegated to the concerned Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No.6 of the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department, Government is approving the continuation of 150 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
