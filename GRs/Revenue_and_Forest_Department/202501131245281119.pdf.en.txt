Preface:-
The First National Project Steering Committee of the Indo-German Bilateral Cooperation Project "Restore, Conservation and Project Forest and Tree Cover for NDC implementation in India (RECAP4NDC)" was held on 16.01.2024 .In the meeting, it was decided that two committees i.e. The State Level Steering Committee and State Level Executive Committee will be constituted under the project for smooth
implementation and Coordination. Accordingly, Inspector General of Forests, Green India Mission, Ministry of Environment, Forest and climate change, Government of India, vide letter at Reference No.1 has requested to Constitute State Level Project Steering Committee and State Level Executive Committee, under the Indo-German Bilateral Cooperation project "Restore, Conserve and Project Forestry and Tree Cover for NDC Implementation in India (RECAP4NDC).
Additionally, it is mentioned in above letter that the State Government may appoint members to both proposed committees in accordance with their State- Specific administrative arrangements within the Forests Department and in accordance with the necessary coordination with project line departments.
Accordingly, the Chief Executive Officer (Maha CAMPA), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter at reference No.2 and 3 has submitted a proposal for the government approval. This issue of the constitution of the Committees was under consideration of the Government.

Term of Reference for the Committee:
The State Level Project Steering Committee, RECAP4NDC Project will mainly be responsible for undertaking state-level policy decisions, coordination amongst relevant departments and organisations, guidelines issuance for effective

implementation of the project. The main functions of the committee will be
following:
1. Undertaking State-level policy decisions, including project implementation, monitoring and evaluation.
2. Reviewing of the work being carried out by various technical organisations under the Project.
3. Leveraging funds through various government schemes and projects working on forestry and allied sectors.
4. Timely reviewing of project implementation, annual plan of operations (APOs) and funds mobilisation through public and private sectors.
5. Ensuring sustainability of the project by linking the project interventions in-line with the on-going interventions taken by relevant departments under schemes/programs.
6. Ensuring coordinated efforts at the level of district and block for systematic rationalization and utilization of available resources.
Committee meeting:
The meeting of State Level Project Steering Committee, RECAP4NDC Project may be organized at least annually or as per the requirement of the project.
