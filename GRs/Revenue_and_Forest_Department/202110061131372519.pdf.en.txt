Preamble: It was decided in the meeting of the Cabinet Sub-Committee held on 12th May, 2021 that the proposals for seeking funds at the rate of State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) for the loss of agricultural crops due to unseasonal rain and hailstorm during March, April and May, 2021 should be sought from all the Divisional Commissioners and assistance should be provided to them accordingly. Accordingly, Rs. A proposal seeking Rs 12226.30 lakh was received from the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nagpur. Accordingly, a proposal of Rs. 12226.30 lakh was submitted to the Cabinet Sub-Committee for approval in a circular manner, which was approved by the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
2. It has been approved to provide financial assistance for the matters mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 13/05/2015 in serial number 5" Agricultural matters." Accordingly, the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan,

A sum of Rs. 12226.30 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Two Crores Twenty Six Lakhs Thirty Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government for Pune, Nasik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur.
Government's decision:
As per the approval of the Government, funds are being sanctioned to the Divisional Commissioners, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur for providing assistance to the farmers/ crops affected due to hailstorm and unseasonal rains in the districts of Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur during March, April and May, 2021.
As per the prospectus attached with this Government decision, the funds are being made available to the concerned districts through the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nagpur. The Cell Officer (M-11) shall disburse the said funds on the Budget Distribution System.
3. Expenditure for the above purpose at the prevailing rate of Rs. 12226.30 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Two Crores Twenty Six Lakhs Thirty Thousand only) is to be met from the grant sanctioned under the head Principal Account Head" 2245- Financial Assistance for Relief of Natural Calamities, 02 Floods, etc., 101 Anugrah Assistance, (91) Expenditure as per State Disaster Response Fund norms, (91) (05) Assistance to farmers on account of crop loss, 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) (2245 2452)."
The terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 13/05/2015 should be fulfilled while disbursing the said funds. Also, the following precautions should be taken while distributing the aid.
(i) As per extant rules, assistance for loss of agricultural/ perennial crops is admissible to those who have suffered loss of 33% or more.
ii) As per the prevailing practice, the assistance should be distributed to the Collector as per the report submitted by all the Collectors as per the panchnama completed with the joint signature of the Krishi Sahayaks, Talathis and Gram Sevaks.
iii) The amount of assistance should be directly credited to the bank savings account of the affected person.
(iv) No assistance shall be provided to any affected person in cash or kind.
(v) No bank shall make any recovery from the amount of crop loss assistance while crediting the amount directly to the bank account of the account holder. For this, the cooperation department should issue appropriate orders.

5. The Divisional Commissioner shall ensure that the funds are utilised for the purpose for which they have been allocated. The accounts, records and records of the disbursement of funds should be kept up to date at the level of the office where the grants are drawn and disbursed as per the prescribed procedures. Utilization certificates should be submitted to the department immediately after reconciling the expenditure incurred with the expenditure recorded in the Treasury Office and the Accountant General's Office every three months. In any case, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned limit. If any amount is not spent from the subsidy disbursed, it should be returned to the government within the prescribed time.
This order is being issued under the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 361/ Expenditure- 9/2021 dated 14.09.2021.
