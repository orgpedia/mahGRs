The Proposal:
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 4 (1) (bb) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972, the Government of Maharashtra in exercise of the powers conferred by the said Act, hereby make provision for the establishment of a Wildlife Sanctuary in the State of Maharashtra in 26 districts in accordance with the provisions of the Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, 2016, vide Government of Maharashtra Order, dated 26.02.2019, for 5 districts in accordance with the provisions of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972, in force in respect of 26 districts in the State. 3 Honorary Wildlife Wardens have been appointed as per the decision of the government here.
Government's decision:
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 4 (1) (bb) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972, the following Honorary Wildlife Wardens are being appointed for a period of three years from the date of the decision of the Government.
