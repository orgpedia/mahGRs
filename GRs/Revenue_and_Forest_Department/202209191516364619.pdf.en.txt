Preamble: The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference No. 3& 5 for continuation of 42 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme" Pench Tiger Reserve" during the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Accordingly, 42 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme" Pench Tiger Reserve" have been sanctioned for the year 2022-2023 to be continued from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the Finance Department has been empowered to extend the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the pattern. 6 have been provided to the administrative departments under the Government Decision.

The government's decision:
