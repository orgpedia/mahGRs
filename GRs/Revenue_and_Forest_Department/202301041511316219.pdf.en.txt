Introduction: In the above reference No. 1 for providing furniture to the newly constructed Collectorate building in Wardha. 918.39 lakh was submitted to the Government for administrative approval.
The Budget has been prepared by Assistant Engineer Grade-I, S.B. Sub-Division, Wardha and submitted to the Executive Engineer, S.B. Wardha Police has investigated. Chief Engineer, S. B. Mandal, Chandrapur and Chief Engineer, S. The Board has signed.
The provisions of this Budget are based on the State Rates for 2021-22.
Government Decision: Rs.
The Government is granting administrative approval to the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 918.39 lakhs (Rupees Nine Crores Eighteen Lakhs Forty Nine Thousand only) subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions 1) The ownership of the land should be verified before giving administrative approval to the work.
2) If the work falls under the jurisdiction of the Archaeology Department, the prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be obtained.
3) While giving technical approval, the State Government has issued Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, D. Action should be taken as per 11/04/2017.
4) The Department of Industries, Energy and Labour has adopted the e-tendering system for the procurement related matters in the proposal. The suggestions issued under the Government Decision dated 24/08/2017 should be taken into consideration.
5) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T.K., D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
6) The electrification and other ancillary works should be properly planned and completed during the construction period itself.
7) Action should be taken in accordance with the instructions given by the government from time to time regarding the various facilities to be made available for the disabled in the building.
