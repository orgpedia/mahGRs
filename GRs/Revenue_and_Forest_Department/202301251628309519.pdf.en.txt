Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion of 0.6804 ha. 'Private Forest' land [Restored before 25/10/1980
u/s 22A of Maharashtra Private Forest (Acquisition) Act, 1975] in favour of Shri.
Tushar Chintamani Mengale on behalf of Shri. Prithviraj Sankalchand Sanghavi for
construction of Resort, Residence, Raod, Water Tank, etc at Village Kune Nama, Tal.
Maval, Dist. Pune;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has
submitted proposal to Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Govt. of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
In reference to No. 3, accord' In-Principle' approval to divert the 0.6804 ha. private

Forest land for the above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded 'Final approval' to divert the 0.6804 ha. 'Private Forest' land [Restored before 25/10/1980 u/s 22A of Maharashtra Private Forest (Acquisition) Act, 1975] for construction of Resort, Residence, Raod, Water Tank, etc at Village Kune Nama, Tal.
Maval, Dist. Pune;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in this case;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.6804 ha. consisting of Private Forest Land [Restored before 25/10/1980 u/s 22A of Maharashtra Private Forest (Acquisition) Act, 1975] more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Shri. Tushar Chintamani Mengale on behalf of Shri. Prithviraj Sankalchand Sanghavi for construction of Resort, Residence, Raod, Water Tank, etc at Village Kune Nama, Tal. Maval, Dist. Pune.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 31.05.2022 and dated 10.10.2022 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the' In Principle' and' Final Approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF& CC, Nagpur, referred above. The Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 31.05.2022 and dated 10.10.2022, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
