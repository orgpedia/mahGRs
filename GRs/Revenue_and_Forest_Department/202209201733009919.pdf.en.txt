Preamble Under the Ujani Project, the work of" Internal Dredging Roads" in the rehabilitated village of Dahegaon in Karmala Taluka of Solapur district has been taken up under Project No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted as per the government decision here. The said administrative approval has been granted and payment for the civil works in progress has been made vide Reference No. The proposal for the demand of funds has been submitted to the government by the Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for civic amenities works in progress under the proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government Under the Ujani Project, the Government is approving the disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 7,50,000/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Fifty Thousand only) for the construction of" Internal Paving Roads" in the rehabilitated village of Dahegaon in Karmala Taluka of Solapur district in the current financial year 2022-2023 subject to the following conditions:

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works to the concerned Executive Engineer without any delay on receipt from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Ujani Canal Division No. As soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received by Solapur, the pending dues of civic amenities works should be paid immediately, and accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and Collector, Solapur.
