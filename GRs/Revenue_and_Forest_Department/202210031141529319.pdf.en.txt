The Proposal:
The State Executive Committee meeting had approved the tender process for implementing the Incidence Response System (IRS) system as the lowest rate (L-1) tender was of M/ s Prutech.
To prepare the Request for Proposal (RFP) and Bill of Quantity (BOQ) of the tenders to be received for the implementation of the IRS system in the state. Wipro has been authorised.
Prutek examined the payment submitted by the company.
M/ s Prutech has issued Reference No. 1 for implementation of Incidence Response System (IRS) system at State Emergency Operation Centre (SEOC) and 34 District Emergency Operation Centre (DCOC). The Government has decided to provide Rs. 11,25,76, 574/- (Rupees Eleven Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Seventy Six Thousand Five Hundred Seventy Four only) has been paid. The matter of payment of the amount recommended by the company Wipro was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
According to the stage of work mentioned in the tender agreement, the IRS (Incident Response System) system has been implemented in the state. Prutek has submitted the payment.
The payment, submitted by Wipro, includes the Rs. 18,76,27,623/ (Rupees Eighteen Crores Seventy Six Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Twenty Three only) out of which 60% is Rs. 11,25,76, 574/- has been paid. The remaining amount of Rs. 7,50,51, 049/- and Rs. 2,91,64, 251/- aggregating to Rs. 10,42,15, 300/- (Rupees Ten Crores Forty Two Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Three Hundred only). The company has been given government approval to pay the amount.
