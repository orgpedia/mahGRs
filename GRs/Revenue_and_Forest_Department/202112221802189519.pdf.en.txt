A meeting of the Departmental Promotion Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Forests) was held to prepare the list of candidates for promotion from the cadre of Personal Assistant, Group-A (Junior Grade) (Pay Scale S- 16-44900-142400) to the cadre of Senior Personal Assistant, Group-A (Senior Grade) (Pay Scale S. 23-67700-208700) in the Forest Department for the year 2020-21. Thereafter, with the approval of the Civil Services Board as well as the competent authority, the following officers are being posted as Senior Personal Assistants, Group-A (Senior Grade) Cadre, on purely temporary promotion:
