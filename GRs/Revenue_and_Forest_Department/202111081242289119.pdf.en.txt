Read:- Revenue and Forest Department, Government Resolution, No.- FDM-2016/ No. 330/ F-2D. 1.1.2021 Preamble: The Department of Revenue and Forestry Government Decision No. As on 01.01.2021, the Finance Department, vide Government Resolution dated 17.4.2015, in exercise of the powers conferred under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part I, Sub-Division-1, Sequence No. 1, 2 and 3, has declared the Head of Department, Regional Head of Department, Office Head for the Forest Department. It was under the consideration of the government to amend the names of some of these designations in Marathi and to make suitable amendments in them as their designations were to be mentioned in English.
Corrigendum: In exercise of the powers conferred under Rule No. 2 of the Fiscal Powers Rules, 1978 Part I, Sub-Division 1, Sequence No. 1, 2 and 3 of the Government Decision, dated 17.4.2015, the Department of Revenue and Forest Department for Forest Department, Government Decision, dated 1.1.2021, hereby notifies the following:
