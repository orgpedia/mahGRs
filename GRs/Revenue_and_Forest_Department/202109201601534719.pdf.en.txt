Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No.7, the Government have accorded approval for continuation of 69 temporary posts in the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali (as mentioned in the Annexure) during the year 2020-2021 from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The expenditure on the establishment of 69 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2021-2022 under the head 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 800 (01) Other Expenditure, (01) (02) Forest Upavane (2406 0659).
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10.08.2021 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
