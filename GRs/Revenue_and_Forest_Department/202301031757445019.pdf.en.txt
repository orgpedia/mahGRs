The Proposal:
" Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray Maharashtra Samruddhi Mahamarg" is an ambitious project of the state government. The Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited (MSRDC) is the implementing agency for the project. The Department of Revenue and Forest, Government of Maharashtra vide its order dated 14.11.2018, has notified the Nagpur-Mumbai Shigrachar Expressway Project as a Vital Public Project under the fourth proviso to sub-rule (I) of rule 46 of the Maharashtra Minor Mineral Exploration (Development and Regulation) Rules, 2013, exempting the exploration of minor minerals required for the project from the imposition of proprietary charges under the said rules. Accordingly, the Revenue and Forest Department vide Government Circular dated 27.05.2019 has prescribed the procedure for grant of permission for minor mineral mining for Nagpur-Mumbai Shigar Sanchar Expressway (Maharashtra Samruddhi Highway).
However, due to non-compliance of this procedure by the concerned contractors, the Regional Revenue Officers have taken punitive action against the concerned contractor under section 48 (7) and 48 (8) of the Maharashtra Revenue Land Code, 1966. punishable in some cases.

The appeals filed by the concerned contractors before various authorities against the action taken orders are still pending while in some cases, the revenue machinery has initiated recovery proceedings as per the penal orders. Also, there are different punishments. have been submitted in court.
Considering the importance of Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray Maharashtra Samruddhi Mahamarg from the point of view of the State, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 17.11.2022 has approved the proposal submitted by the Public Works Department to cancel all the cases pending before the Regional Revenue System and all the cases pending before the Regional Revenue System in respect of minor minerals used in the construction of this Highway. Accordingly, the matter of cancellation of all the cases pending before the Regional Revenue Administration and orders of penal action issued by the Regional Revenue Administration regarding minor minerals used for the construction of Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray Maharashtra Samruddhi Highway was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the approval given in the meeting of the State Cabinet dated 17.11.2022, the Government is taking the following decision:" All the penal action orders issued by the Regional Revenue Administration regarding minor minerals used for the construction of Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray Maharashtra Samruddhi Highway and the cases pending before the Regional Revenue Administration regarding the penal action should be cancelled."
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the consensus reached in the Informal Reference No. 211/2022, dated 19.09.2022 of the Planning Department, Informal Reference No. 4/ B- 2/2022 dated 26.09.2022 of the Revenue and Forest Department and Informal Reference No. 246/2022 Expenditure-11, dated 03.10.2022 of the Finance Department.
