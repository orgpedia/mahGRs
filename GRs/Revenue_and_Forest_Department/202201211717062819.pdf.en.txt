Regarding the formation of a study group to bring uniformity in the service organization of the Talathi community.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. SRIKH- 2021/ PR No.268/ E-10
First Floor, Main Building, Ministry, Madame Cama Street,
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: January 17, 2022
The proposal: A meeting was held on 22/10/2021 at Mumbai under the chairmanship of A.M.S. (Revenue) to discuss the representation dated 20/10/2021 submitted by Maharashtra Rajya Talathi Patwari Mandaladhikari Samanvay Mahasangha to (Revenue). As per the discussion and directions given in the meeting, the appointing authority of Talathi cadre is the Sub-Divisional Officer and their primary service merit list is kept at the sub-division level. However, at the time of promotion to the post of Circle Officer, this list is compiled at the district level and a revised service excellence list is kept. In view of the difficulties arising in the service excellence list, it was decided to constitute a" Study Group" under the chairmanship of the Deputy Commissioner, Konkan Division, Konkan to report and recommend to the government on what should be the procedure to bring uniformity in service excellence. Accordingly, the matter of setting up such a study group was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The following study group is being formed to submit a report to the government on the problems arising in the preparation of the service of Talathi and Mandal officers in the state and what should be the procedure to bring uniformity in the service.
Mahesh Suryavanshi (Talathi), Member, G. Kolhapur
The study group should submit its report and recommendations to the government within 3 months.
