A Special Leave Petition No. Vacancies in the open category in the quota of promotion under 28306/2017. 25.05.2004 regarding filling up of vacancies in purely temporary basis as per the existing service conditions. In accordance with the instructions given under the Government Decision dated 07.05.2021. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 07.05.2021. The said employees are being given temporary promotion subject to final decision thereon as challenged in the High Court, Mumbai.
(2) In respect of promotion to Group-A and Group-B posts of differently abled employees, S.P.V./ 16-A
The approval is being given as per the instructions issued under the Government Decision dated 20.04.2023.
(3) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers in the select list shall not be entitled to regularisation as a result of this temporary promotion.
All the officers in the selection list have fulfilled the criteria of promotion as prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 01.08.2019 of the General Administration Department. Under these conditions, the list is approved.
(5) In respect of promotion of officers who are eligible as per the merit of service in the said selection list, but have been denied promotion, the Department of General Administration, Govt. The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should take care to take action as per the Government decision dated 12.09.2016.

If the Commission approves candidates in time or if the candidates on deputation return, the officer who has been promoted ad hoc will be demoted. This promotion is temporary in nature and the officer concerned will not have the right of permanent retention in this post.
7) The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati, should take care to get the approval of the General Administration Department and the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the regular selection list by adjusting the ad-hoc promotions given to the vacancies in the direct service quota, according to the availability of posts in the regular promotion quota.
8) The benefit of the salary of the promoted cadre will not be admissible unless the above officers who are being promoted to the cadre of Naib Tehsildar have joined directly after the posting.
9) If the above officers do not join the post of the post given in the Naib Tehsildar cadre immediately after the promotion, action will be taken to cancel the promotion of the concerned officer.
(10) No leave of absence shall be granted to the officers concerned from the date of receipt of the order of posting in respect of the said temporary promotion without the prior approval of the Government.
11) In case of any other objection in the future regarding judicial or promotion, the entire responsibility will be of the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati.
4. With immediate effect, the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati, is directed to relieve the remaining officers, except those connected with the elections to the Gram Panchayats, from their duties immediately to attend to the postings and to attend to the postings immediately and to relieve the officers who have been appointed as the election decision officers for the Gram Panchayat elections to join the post of promotion after the expiry of the code of conduct for the Gram Panchayat elections. A report should be submitted to the government.
The above officers are required to join the post of the post immediately by handing over the charge of their post to other officers in consultation with the Divisional Commissioner/ Head of the concerned office.
