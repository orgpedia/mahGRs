Preamble: The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2023 under Reference No.
Accordingly, the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for protection of Protected Areas in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 have been conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 03.22/ S.No.69/ F-1
Government's decision:
The Government have approved the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas in 2022-2023 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of protected areas under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Protection, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Protection (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
