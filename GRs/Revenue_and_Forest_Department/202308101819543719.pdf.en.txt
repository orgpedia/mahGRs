The Proposal:
Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune has been registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 25th June, 2018.
Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune has set up 6 divisional offices (Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad and Pune) for the effective and timely implementation of various welfare schemes for the economic, social and educational development of Marathas, Kunbi-Marathas and Maratha-Kunbi.
Government order:
