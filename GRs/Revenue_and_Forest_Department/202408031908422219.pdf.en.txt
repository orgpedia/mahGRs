The officers concerned shall be liable for disciplinary action under the provisions of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, if they are found not to have joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of assumption of office in accordance with the said provision under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Retirement, Non-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
The officer concerned shall take care to join the place of posting within the prescribed period as per the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, non-serving service and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981 or else, if he does not join the post of this post, his absence shall be on "non-working days * (7 & 5)." Those concerned should note that it will be calculated as 70 / /.
6. Leave of the officer concerned shall not be granted for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers transferred by the original office shall be entertained. If such applications are received, they shall be returned to the concerned authorities by post, registered at the original address. 7. The concerned shall note that in case of failure to join the post of posting immediately or attempt to cancel the transfer by any kind of coercion under the said order, disciplinary action shall be taken against them on the ground that the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, treating it as misconduct.
