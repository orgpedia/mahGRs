Preamble: The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference No. 3 and 5 for continuation of 150 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Accordingly, 150 temporary posts created under the Biodiversity Conservation Programme were approved to be continued in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 have been conferred on the concerned administrative departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department, 150 temporary posts created under the Biodiversity Conservation Programme are being approved for continuation in the year 2022-2023 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
