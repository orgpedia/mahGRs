ORDER: Whereas, as per the authority received vide letter dated 10/05/2000 from the State Government, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter dated 10/05/2000, received from the Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Bandra (West), Mumbai, under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, a proposal for construction of third bridge over Thane creek on Sion-Panvel highway at Mauje-Vashi, Tt. Thane and Mauje-Turbhe. A proposal for diversion of 1.4074 hectares of forest area in Mumbai Suburban was submitted to the Central Government with a recommendation under the aforesaid letter No. 1;
# Page 2
GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: FLD-2021/ PR No.130/ F-10
And whereas, the Government of India, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station House Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, has accorded in principle approval to the said proposal submitted under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 vide letter A. No. 2 above. Final approval for diversion has been accorded vide letter No. 4 hereunder; and whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station House Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide their letter No. 5 above, has informed that the project machinery has fulfilled the conditions stipulated in the Final Approval Letter dated 30/10/2020 of the Central Government; and whereas, the State Government has accorded approval to the diversion of forest land to the Government of Maharashtra for the purpose of conservation of forest land. The High Court, Bombay vide its order dated 06.09.2018 has permitted the commencement of work in the area of the project; therefore, the Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the Project Authority i.e., the Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Bandra (West), Mumbai, be directed to divert a total of 1.4074 hectares of forest land, including reserve and pebble forest area, mentioned in the following schedule, for the construction of the third bridge over Thane creek on Sion-Panvel Highway under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, as detailed in the Project Proposal attached along with the letter from the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Forest Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur. This diversion should be completed subject to fulfillment of the terms and conditions in the letters dated 14/05/2019 and 30/10/2020 of the Government of India and also after fulfillment of the following conditions.
The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Forest Cell, Mumbai shall ensure that the project machinery complies with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India mentioned above. The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Kandhalvan Cell, Mumbai shall submit a report from time to time to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Officers, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur.
Non-compliance with the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and the rules and guidelines made thereunder and any of the conditions contained in this Order shall be a violation of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and legal action shall be taken accordingly.
