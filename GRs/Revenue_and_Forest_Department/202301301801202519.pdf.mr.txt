Order:
Whereas, the Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State,
Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion of 2.33
ha. 'Private Forest' land [Restored before 25/10/1980 u/s 22A of Maharashtra Private
Forest (Acquisition) Act, 1975] in favour of M/s. Karnala Holiday Inn Pvt. Ltd for
construction of Resort and raising plantation at Village Kalhe, Tal. Panvel, Dist.
Raigad;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has
submitted proposal to Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Govt. of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under reference No.3,
accorded 'In-Principle' approval to divert the 2.33 ha. Forest land for the above project
under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur and Govt. of Maharashtra vide letters under

AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in this case;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 2.33 ha. consisting of Private Forest Land [Restored before 25/10/1980 u/s 22A of Maharashtra Private Forest (Acquisition) Act, 1975] more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Shri. Vipin Bajaj, Director, Karnala Holiday Inn Pvt. Ltd for construction of construction of Resort and raising plantation at Village Kalhe, Tal. Panvel, Dist. Raigad.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 09.03.2018 and dated 05.05.2021 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC,
Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the â€˜In Principle' and 'Final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above.
