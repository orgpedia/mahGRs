Government Decision 33 posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Nagpur Division have been created as per the Government Decision No. The extension was granted from 01.3.2022 to 31.8.2022. This extension has come to an end on 31.8.2022. Accordingly, the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur vide reference No. 2 Posts of 33 temporary posts. Proposal for extension from 01.9.2022 to 28.2.2022 is under consideration.
As per the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, the field level posts under the Relief and Rehabilitation Sub-Division have been reviewed and a total of 33 posts of Nagpur Division on Rehabilitation Establishment have been delineated. Finance Department Reference No. As per Order No. 3, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts from 01.9.2022 to 28.2.2023. As per the decision of the Government, the existing 33 temporary posts in Form-A, which were created after the approval of the previous review, are being extended from 01.9.2022 to 28.2.2023.
The above posts are temporary in nature and should be treated as additional posts in addition to the permanent posts of the concerned department. Along with these posts, the office bearers will also be entitled to dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the rules of the government. Further, the Government has approved the extension subject to the assumption that the above posts were not vacant for more than six months.
