Preamble: The Departmental Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the decision of the Government at No. 6 under reference to scrutinize the proposals received from the District Collector for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund for taking various measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State. The Divisional Scrutiny Committee has scrutinized the proposals received from the concerned District Collectors for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund and sent their recommendations to the government through a letter at reference number 7 to 12. The meeting of the State Executive Committee was held on 27.04.2021 to decide on the recommendations received from the Divisional Scrutiny Committee. Accordingly, as per the approval given by the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 27.04.2021, the matter of disbursement of funds for taking various measures in the context of COVID-19 was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In the current financial year, the demand for funds received from various districts for COVID-19 measures has been examined by the Divisional Scrutiny Committees headed by Divisional Commissioners and recommended from the State Disaster Response Fund for permissible items as per the State Disaster Response Fund norms. The first instalment of Rs. 176,29,05, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Seventy Six Crores Thirty-nine Lakhs Five Thousand only) is being approved for disbursement to the concerned districts as mentioned above.
The funds being disbursed through this Government Resolution will be utilized for the following matters as approved in the meeting of the Divisional Scrutiny Committee and as per reference No. It's here 5
Expenditure should be made on permissible items from the State Disaster Response Fund as per the decision of the Government.
1. Measures for quatantine sample collection and screening : (a) Provision for temporary accomodation, food, clothing, medical care etc. for people affected and sheltered in quarantine camps (other than home quarantine) or for cluster containment operations.
(b) Cost of consumables for sample collection.
(c) Support for checking screening and conact tracing.
2. Procurement of essential equipment's/labs for reponse to COVID-19: (a) Cost of setting up addtional testing laboratories within the Government and the cost of consumable and testing kit.
(b) Cost of personal protection equipment for healthcare, municipal, police and fire authorities (c) Cost of Thermal Scanners, Ventilators, Air Purifiers, Oxygen generation and storage plant in hospitals, strengthening ambulance services for transport of patients, setting up containment zones, Covid-19 hospital, Covid-19 care centers and consumables in the Government hospitals.
