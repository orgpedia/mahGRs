GOVERNMENT ORDER: Subject to the provisions of Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri Swapnil Tangde, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Deputy Collector, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years by retaining the allotted Amravati Division.
2. The services of Shri Swapnil Tangde, Deputy Collector, are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry of Urban Development, Mumbai in pursuance of his deputation as Deputy Collector, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai.
3.Shri Swapnil Tangde, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai.
