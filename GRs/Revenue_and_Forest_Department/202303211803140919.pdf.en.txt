Terms and Conditions
The Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned executing agency through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
As soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received by the concerned executing agency, the pending dues of civic amenities should be paid immediately, and the completion report should be submitted to the government, Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and Collector, Nagpur immediately.

(3) The funds sanctioned and received under this Order shall be spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and the concerned executing agency shall ensure that no expenditure exceeds the sanctioned grant while spending the said funds.
4. The Project Officer concerned shall ensure that no expenditure is incurred on works other than the civic amenities for which funds have been sanctioned without prior approval of the Government.
The concerned executing agency shall ensure that no funds received under the head Main Account mentioned in the said Order shall remain unutilized or the funds received under the said Order shall be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned agency.
(6) The Controlling Officer concerned shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure under the head' Main Accounts' mentioned in the said order and submit the same to the Accountant General in accordance with the prevailing accounting system of the Government. Accordingly, the work of reconciliation of expenditure under this head, appropriation accounts, etc. should be done by the concerned as per the prevailing practice.
7. The expenditure statements/ reports in this regard should be submitted to the Government without fail. The expenditure reconciliation statements related to the above funds should be submitted to the government along with the Reconciliation Slip of the Accountant General's Office without fail.
The concerned executing agency should submit the Utilization Certificate of the funds disbursed under this order to the Government immediately by the Accountant General's Office, the concerned Collector/ Divisional Commissioner.
9. The concerned shall submit to the Government within the prescribed period the appropriation account of expenditure in accordance with the funds sanctioned in the current financial year 2021-2022 under the head of accounts in this regard.
10. The above instructions should be strictly implemented by all concerned in accordance with fiscal discipline.
The M-11 office of the Revenue and Forest Department should take immediate action to make this fund available to the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur on the BEAMS system.
