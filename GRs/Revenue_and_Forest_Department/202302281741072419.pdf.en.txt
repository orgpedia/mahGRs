The main work of the land records department in the state is to establish the boundaries of the land according to the land records, to prepare new land records by counting the land holdings on the land, to calculate the land acquisition and to prepare the records according to the acquisition area. The boundaries of the land are determined and shown to the landholder so that the grievances of the people are resolved. For this purpose, the Geometer/ Conservation Geometer/ Controller goes to the actual counting place and makes the counting map and mounts the land records on it to show the same, to show the land marks etc. Conservation Geometers are concerned with the technical aspects of urban areas. Handling all these technical aspects requires a laptop for the working geometer/ conservation geometer/ surveyor. If the land surveyors/ conservancy land surveyors/ tenderers are provided with laptops, it will be convenient to release the counting cases immediately by showing the boundaries of the place and will help in resolving the grievances of the public.
