Voluntary use of the word" Vande Mataram" instead of" Hello" in salutations during telephone or mobile conversations for official purposes.

the Revenue and Forest Department

circular
All the officers and employees of the Forest Department should be public or public representatives for government work.
It is requested to use the word" Vande Mataram" instead of" Hello" while greeting them over telephone or mobile phone.
