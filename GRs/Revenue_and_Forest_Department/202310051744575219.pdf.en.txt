(2) This order comes into force with immediate effect and the above mentioned officers are being relieved unilaterally under this order from the post they are holding to join the post indicated by the transfer.
The date on which the concerned officer has appeared in place of the new appointment should be informed to the government immediately by e-mail/ post at desk9- rfd@mah. gov. in.
(4) If it is found that the concerned officer has not joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of his appointment as per the said provision prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Self-Election Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, he shall be liable to be proceeded against in accordance with the Government Order, No.
186/16/ K. 14, Dy. The concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken as per the provisions of 23.12.2016.
(5) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day.

6. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the Government for any reason. Further, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the said officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the original address of the concerned officer by post.
7. Since the aforesaid orders are being issued with the approval of the competent authority, the Divisional Commissioner shall appoint the Tehsildar or other officers under scrutiny at the place of posting to other vacant posts if they have been given posting by temporary arrangement from the level of Divisional Commissioner.
All concerned should note that if they do not join the post immediately as per the said transfer order or try to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them considering this misconduct as a violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
9. The said Government Order has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra and its Computer Code No. It is 202310051744575219. This government order is being issued by digitally signing.
