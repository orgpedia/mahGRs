Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 3.12 ha. Reserved Forest land for construction of Lower Tapi Project Village at
Pimpri, Kamalgaon, Vadgaon BK., & Jalod in favour of Executive Engineer, Hatnur Canal
Division in Jalgaon under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980
in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Regional Office (Central) Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 3. 12 ha. Reserved Forest Land for
the above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
# Page 2
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No. 4 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 5 Accorded 'Final
approval 'to divert the 3. 12 ha. Reserved Forest Land in Favourite of Executive Engineer, Hatnur
Canal Division in Jalgaon for construction of Lower Tapi Project Village at Pimpri, Kamalgaon,
Vadgaon BK., & Jalod under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.6 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 3.12 ha.
consisting of Reserved forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide
letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. in favour of Executive Engineer, Hatnur Canal Division in Jalgaon for
construction of Lower Tapi Project Village at Pimpri, Kamalgaon, Vadgaon BK., & Jalod, Tal-
Chopada, Dist-Jalgaon in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 03.05.2024 and dated 29.07.2024 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle' and
'Final approval 'letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above. The
Conservator of Forests (T), Dhule shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions
by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal
Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time
to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoOEF&CC letters dated 03.05.2024 and dated 29.07.2024, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest
(Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also
to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access
or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest
area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T), Dhule shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director General of
Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate

Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under
