Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Shri Amrit Natekar, Deputy Collector (Awaiting Appointment) is being appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
The services of Mr. Amrit Natekar, Deputy Collector, are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai, in accordance with the deputation to the post of Deputy Commissioner, Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.
3. Mr. Amrit Natekar, Deputy Collector to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai immediately.
