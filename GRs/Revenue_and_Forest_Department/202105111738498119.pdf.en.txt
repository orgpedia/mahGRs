(2) Orders for the appointment of other employees to the posts vacated by the promotion of the above officers shall be issued separately.
The above officers are being promoted on ad-hoc/ temporary basis subject to the following conditions:
a) Open category vacancies in the direct service quota will be filled only on a temporary basis. The Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. The General Administration Department/ 16B has issued a notification regarding the filling up under 28306/2017. Approval is being accorded as per instructions issued vide letter dated 29/12/2017.

(b) The ad-hoc promotion to the post falling vacant under the direct service quota shall be for a period of 11 months or until the candidates are available from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, whichever is earlier. In the meantime, if a Commission-approved candidate becomes available, the ad-hoc promoted officer will be demoted. The promotion is temporary in nature and the officer concerned will not hold the post permanently.
(c) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers in the selection list will not be entitled to any regularisation and promotion as a result of the temporary promotion.
d) Government Decisions, General Administration Department. 15/12/2017 and D. As per the provisions of 30/08/2018, in the case of promotion of officers who are suspended on the date of the meeting of the Divisional Promotion Committee or against whom disciplinary proceedings have been initiated in accordance with the disciplinary proceedings, or against whom a court case of criminal charges is pending, the Divisional Promotion Committee has examined their eligibility in accordance with the provisions of the Confidential Records and other Service Entry Rules and the evaluation and the copy of the Departmental Promotion Committee in respect of this officer has been sealed in a separate packet. Action will be taken as per the government's directions.
(e) The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officers without any prior notice or notice.
e) The said promotion shall be made by the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai/ Nagpur/ Aurangabad as well as by the Hon. High Court/ High Court. If any claim/ petition is filed in the Supreme Court, etc. regarding seniority in the Forest Ranger, Group-B cadre, the same shall be subject to the final disposal of the pending petition.
The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting. If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, then they are not interested in the promotion, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, D. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled. Also, the officers who refuse promotion, if they are given the benefit of 12/24 years of the post of Forest Ranger, Group-B under the Assured Progress Scheme, it will be stopped immediately and they will be given the benefit after direct promotion to Forest Ranger, Group-B cadre.
5. The promoted officer should immediately give the option of fixation of pay on taking charge of the post of Forest Ranger, Group-B. If such an option is not given, their salary shall be determined in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, Rule No. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
