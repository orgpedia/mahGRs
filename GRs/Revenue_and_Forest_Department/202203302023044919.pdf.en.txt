The Proposal:
The National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) teams are deployed in the flood-prone districts ahead of the monsoon. The National Disaster Response Force, Pune, has also been asked to be called in for rescue operations in the flood-affected areas. Accordingly, requisition for vehicles required for National Disaster Response Force is made by the District Collector, Pune. This year, vehicles were arranged from Pune to send the National Disaster Response Force teams to the planned districts. The matter of approving the vehicle expenses incurred for this was under the consideration of the government.
