2. Under the Government Order dated 18/08/2022 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, 1534 temporary posts in the figure of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and District Collector, Nagpur/ Wardha/ Bhandara/ Gondia/ Chandrapur and Gadchiroli and in the area offices of Sub Divisional Officer, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer under them and 154 (including newly increased posts) temporary posts outside the figure of various offices in the Revenue Department were under the consideration of the Government for extension of the above period.
Government Decision: On the establishment of the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur as well as the District Collector, Nagpur/ Wardha/ Bhandara/ Gondia/ Chandrapur and Gadchiroli, and the Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer under him, subject to the figures in the zonal office, No. 1534 temporary posts in the statement attached with the Government decision dated 15/04/2017 dated 03.04.2022 and 154 (including newly increased posts) temporary posts outside the diagram on establishment of various offices in the Revenue Department are being extended for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 subject to the conditions contained in the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 in the context of Department of Finance.
2. Expenditure on such temporary posts subject to reference No. 03 In the relevant statement accompanying the Government decision herein, Raka-No. The budgetary grants for the year 2022-2023 under the head of accounts indicated in 05 should be apportioned.
The Finance Department has directed all the departments to take note that the revised figure in the interim period as mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 18/08/2022 in the context of the Finance Department needs to be approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries and no further extension of time will be granted for finalizing the revised figure.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Finance Department, No. Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18/08/2022.
