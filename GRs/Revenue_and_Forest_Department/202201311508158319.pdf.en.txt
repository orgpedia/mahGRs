(a) People-oriented components:
(ix) Current status of recovery of gratuity in respect of sale/ change of use of private lands acquired for industrial purposes by companies under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 Part VII, as permitted by the Government:
Under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 Part VII, private lands have been acquired for industrial purposes for companies. Section 44A of the Act provides as follows:
" No company for which any land has been acquired under this Part shall be entitled to sell, mortgage, donate, lease or otherwise transfer the said land or any part thereof, without the previous sanction of the appropriate Government. "
Also, the Government has issued a Government Resolution No. 01/2017/ No. 11/ A-2, D. The policy on sale/ change of use of acquired land for industrial purposes has been framed under Part-VII of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894.
