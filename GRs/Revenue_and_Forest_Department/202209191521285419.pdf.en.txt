The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue the 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Accordingly, the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern, with effect from 01.09.2022.

The powers to extend the period up to 28.02.2023 have been delegated to the Administrative Departments under the Government Resolution of Reference No. 6 of the Department of Finance.
Government's decision:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department, the Government is approving the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati, under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development and Reconstruction, (01) Administration and Conservation, (01) Administration and Conservation (2406 0096)" should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
