Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve The proposal for continuation of 224 temporary posts created under this scheme for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024 was submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for the approval of the Government vide Reference No.
The powers to extend the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review and the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review were granted to the concerned administrative departments from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. As per the Government Resolution No.9 of the Finance Department, 224 temporary posts created under the scheme of establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve were approved to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2023-2024.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format have been provided to the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 11 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department of Reference No.11, 224 temporary posts created under the scheme for establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve are being approved for continuation from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-2024.
