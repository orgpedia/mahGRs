The Proposal:
An amount of Rs. 6000.00 lakh has been sanctioned and budgeted for the year 2021-22 under the State Level Scheme for Establishment of Wildlife Handicaps (2406-2232).
As per the decision of the Government of Reference No. 1, approval has been given to set up a treatment centre for wild animals in an area of 22 acres at Mauje Bavdhan in Pune forest circle. Subject to the administrative approval given to the budgets for the work of setting up the treatment centre for wildlife, a total amount of Rs. 2248.56 lakh has been approved for e-tendering. As per the Government decision in Reference No. 2 for e-tendering activities approved in the year 2020-21, earlier Rs. 300.00 lakhs has been disbursed.
Now, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter of reference No.5, has approved for the year 2021-22, for the activities in the e-tender for setting up of a treatment centre for wild animals at Mauje Bavdhan in Pune forest circle, the remaining amount of Rs. 1948.56 lakh has been submitted to the Government for approval. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the proposal submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur under the letter of reference No. 5 and the funds available under the scheme of Establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406-2232), the Government have decided, subject to the following conditions, to establish a Wildlife Treatment Centre at Bavdhan of Pune Forest Division as per the attached form, at a total cost of Rs. 2400.00 lakhs is being disbursed.
