Government Decision: A total of 42 contractual posts of 36 District Disaster Management Officers and 06 Divisional Disaster Management Coordinators have been given to implement the Maharashtra State Disaster Management Programme as a permanent scheme of the Government and for the implementation of this programme. The Government has approved extension of moratorium for a period of 11 months with effect from 01.01.2022.
State Disaster Management Authority In the meeting dated 09.07.2021, the monthly honorarium of District Disaster Management Authority and Divisional Disaster Management Coordinator was increased to Rs. 45,000/- respectively.
The expenditure for this work shall be met from the provision made under the head Demand No.C.6,2245, Assistance for Prevention of Natural Calamities, 80 General (01) (01) Establishment of office of Maharashtra State Disaster Management Authority and Equipment with Furniture and Materials (2245 1761) 10 Contractual Services.
