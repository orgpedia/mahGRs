The Proposal:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification to ensure reservation of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021.
As mentioned in the notification, in respect of the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service, even if the designations are different, the posts in the State Government Service will be ensured for the disabled and the proposal for the designations for the disabled in respect of the posts existing in addition to the above mentioned posts in the establishment of the State Government should be sent by the concerned departments to the expert committee constituted by the Government vide circular dated 12.11.2020 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 02.02.2021. Accordingly, as recommended by the Expert Committee referred to in Reference No.7 in its meeting dated 22.02.2021, the Department of Revenue and Forest Department, Department of Records and Stamps has decided to appoint a new Director in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D in the Department of Revenue and Forest Department. 8 Posts for Divyang have been ensured by the government here.
