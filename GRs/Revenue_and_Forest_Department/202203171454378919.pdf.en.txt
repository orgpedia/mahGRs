The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 142 temporary posts created under the post-Plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 and 7.
Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
3. The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Governing Decision of Reference No.9 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of Administrative Department and all existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier review but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department, Government of India has approved the continuation of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
2. The expenditure on establishment of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation under the head" 2406 Afforestation and Wildlife, 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available during the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 22.02.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
