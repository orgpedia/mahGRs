Introduction: In order to help the farmers in the next season in case of loss due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods and cyclones, the farmers are given assistance in the form of input subsidy at a prescribed rate from the State Disaster Response Fund.
Also, assistance is provided at prescribed rates for other approved items of the State Disaster Response Fund.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Department of Revenue and Forest No.CLS-2022/ PR No. 349/ M-3, Dy. As on 27th March, 2023, the revised rates and norms of State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) for providing assistance for damages caused by various natural calamities in the State will be released. It will come into effect from November 1, 2022.
The untimely rains in the state during March, April and May 2023 have caused major losses to the farmers. If the crop loss is more than 33 per cent, the farmers are given financial assistance at the prescribed rate of 2 per cent per hectare.
