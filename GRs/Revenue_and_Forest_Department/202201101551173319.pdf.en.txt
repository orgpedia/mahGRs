Proposal Under Koyna Project, Mauje Pali No. 2 T.G. In respect of the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated village of Satara No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted as per the government decision here. Among the works given administrative approval were the construction of a community temple.
To pay the civic amenities work in progress, the reference No. 6 Proposal for Demand of Funds vide letter from Executive Engineer, Dhom Canal Division No. 2, Satara has submitted to the government.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 10,53,000/-for the civic amenities works in progress under the proposed" Construction of Samaj Mandir."
Government's decision
Under the Koyna Project, Mauje Pali No. The Government is approving a total amount of Rs. 10,53,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand only) for the construction of" Samaj Mandir" at Pragatipath in the rehabilitated village of 2 T.G. Satara for the current financial year 2021-22 subject to the following conditions:

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works to the concerned Executive Engineer without any delay as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Dhom Canal Department No. 2, Satara should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and the concerned Collector.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
