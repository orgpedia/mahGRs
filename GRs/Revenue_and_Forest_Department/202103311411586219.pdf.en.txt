The 12 posts of additional collectors, deputy collectors and tehsildars in the revenue cadre, whose services were terminated as the court did not grant a stay and the caste validity certificate was invalid, were created on the establishment of the respective divisional commissioners. Since the period of 11 months of the officers/ employees appointed to the superannuation post has expired, the matter of extension of the superannuation and above appointments to such services was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Resolution: Under Section 3, 12 superannuation posts of Additional Collector, Sub-Collector and Tehsildar officers in the revenue cadre, whose services were terminated due to invalidation of caste validity certificate, have been created on the establishment of the concerned Divisional Commissioner. Of these, 4 officers- 2 from the sub-collector cadre and 2 from the tehsildar cadre- have retired.
