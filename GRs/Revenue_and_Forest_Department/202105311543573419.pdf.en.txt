Order: Whereas, as per the authority received vide letter dated 10/05/2000 from the State Government, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, has issued orders under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, received from the Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Department, Kalwa-Thane, for the construction of the Tambdi Minor Irrigation Scheme at Mauje-Tambdi, Th. The proposal was submitted along with the recommendation under letter No. 1 hereunder; and the Government of India, vide letter No. 2 above, have accorded in principle approval to the proposal submitted by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Forest Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. This approval is granted subject to the project mechanism meeting the conditions as laid down in the Government of India letter dated 01/03/2019;
