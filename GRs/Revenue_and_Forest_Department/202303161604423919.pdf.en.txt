For the year 2022-23, under the scheme Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (Programme) (2406 0775), the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur and the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur have sanctioned Rs. 6616.731 lakh has been submitted to the Government for approval. The government has taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
In view of the reference No. 1 circular of the Department of Finance, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur and the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, in view of the demand for funds made under the letters of reference No. 2, 4, 6 and 7 under the head Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (2406-0775), the Budget Fund has been allocated Rs. The release of funds amounting to Rs. 1853.87 lakhs is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions as per attached statement: i. The amount to be spent on this scheme in the year 2022-23 should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System, target-wise, under the head Accounts Heading 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 110- Conservation of Wildlife (00) (02) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (2406 0775).

ii.The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds after verifying that the activities mentioned in the letter of reference No. 2, 4, 6 and 7 are included in the said scheme.
(iii) The funds should be spent on the proposed works under the scheme only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical approval has been granted by the competent authority as required.
The funds disbursed should be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
(v) The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds in accordance with the prevailing Government decisions issued from time to time in respect of procurement of materials and other purchases by the Finance Department.
vi. While implementing the works, it should be ensured that the works are integrated and e-tendering system is adopted as per rules.
vii. The various Guidelines issued by the Department of Finance and the Department of Finance, Government Circular No. No.43/ Earth-3, dated 04.04.2022 to be strictly followed.
This Government Decision is being issued pursuant to Finance Department, Government Circular No: Finance-2022/ P. No.43/ Finance-3 dated 04.04.2022.
