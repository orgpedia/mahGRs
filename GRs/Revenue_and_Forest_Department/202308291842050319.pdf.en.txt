Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri Vinay Gosavi, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Deputy Collector and Competent Authority, Dharavi Redevelopment Project Slum Rehabilitation Authority, Mumbai on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
The services of Shri Vinay Gosavi, Deputy Collector, are being transferred to the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Mumbai, in pursuance of his deputation as Deputy Collector and Competent Authority, Dharavi Redevelopment Project Slum Rehabilitation Authority, Mumbai.
3. Mr. Vinay Gosavi, Deputy Collector to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Ministry, Mumbai immediately.
