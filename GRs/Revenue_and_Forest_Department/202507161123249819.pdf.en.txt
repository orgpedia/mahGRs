The Proposal: -
The Revenue Department is the principal department in the district administration and people from all strata of the society come into contact with this department for day-to-day activities. Therefore, it has been directed to set up an all-equipped "War Room" at Mantralaya, Mumbai to provide wide publicity to the schemes of the Revenue Department and its Sub-Divisions and to keep the website of the Revenue Department up-to-date by effectively using various media and social media to disseminate information about the public welfare schemes, various innovative initiatives and campaigns, campaigns and various public utility programmes of the Government implemented by the Revenue Department, Commissioner and Director, Land Records Department and Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Pune. The Revenue Minister has given in the review meetings of the Revenue Department dated 25.06.2025.
As per the above directions of the Hon'ble Minister, the Government was considering setting up of an all-equipped "War Room" at Mantralaya, Mumbai to give wide publicity to the schemes, activities, campaigns, campaigns and programmes being implemented by the Revenue Department and to keep the Department's website up to date.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government hereby approves the expenditure of Rs. 2,50,00, 000 / - (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakhs) towards establishment of a well-equipped "War Room" at the Ministry of Revenue, Mumbai for the purpose of dissemination of information about public welfare schemes, various innovative initiatives, campaigns and programmes of the Government and various public utility programmes to the general public through effective use of various media and social media and keeping the website of the Department of Revenue up to date.

The objectives of the establishment of the war room, structure B components, tools and facilities are as follows to effectively disseminate information about the functioning of all the three units of the Revenue Department to the public through social media and other means of communication in a consolidated manner through this war room.
Installation of ait room:
1. To give consolidated and coordinated publicity to the public welfare schemes implemented by the Revenue Department, the Depository Commissioner and the Director, Land Records Department and the Registration and Stamp Department.
2. To create a positive image of revenue and its sub-segments on social media.
3. Make the Department of Revenue's website up-to-date and easily accessible with information on the Department's policies.
4. Effective communication, feedback collection and analysis of various campaigns and activities.
5. Creation of technically competent, analytical and responsive mechanisms at the survey levels.
