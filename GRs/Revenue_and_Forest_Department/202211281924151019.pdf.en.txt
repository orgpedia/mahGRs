4. The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of posting by the promotion. If they do not appear at the place of posting within the prescribed time after being relieved as such, they will not be interested in the promotion, according to the Government Decision, General Administration Department, Dy. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled.
It may also be noted that the officers who refuse promotion should be given 12/24 years of benefit of the post of Administrative Officer under the Assured Progress Scheme, which should be stopped immediately and they will be given this benefit after direct promotion to the Administrative Officer cadre.
(5) The promoted officer shall, on assuming charge of the post of Administrative Officer, Group-A, immediately give the option of fixation of pay. If such an option is not given, their salary shall be determined in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, Rule No. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
