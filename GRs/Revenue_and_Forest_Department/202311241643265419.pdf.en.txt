Preamble: Rs. 1.00 lakh. Since it is not possible to estimate the number of farmer suicides every year, the Collector is authorized to disburse a nominal amount under the head of accounts and deduct the expenditure incurred for this purpose during the year from the treasury on a letter of authorization. The expenditure incurred on these items is adjusted by way of Supplementary Demands in the Budget Session. Provisions made in the financial year 2023-24 under head 2235 3195, Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department, No. SCY-2023/ S.No.17/ M-7 dated 26.06.2023 and 12.07.2023 have been circulated to all the Divisional Commissioners. However, the matter was under consideration of the government to approve the expenditure on this matter minus the authority letter from the treasury.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: To provide immediate relief to the heirs of farmers who committed suicide due to the three reasons of failure, indebtedness and debt repayment, Demand No. C-5, Head of Accounts 2235, Social Security and Welfare 02, Social Welfare-104, Welfare of the Elderly, Disabled and Destitute (00) (03) Financial Assistance to the heirs of farmers who committed suicide (Compulsory) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (Dattamat) (2235 3195) The Government is approving to continue the facility of passing on the payments to the Treasury to the undersigned for meeting the expenditure for this purpose for the financial year 2023-24. Accordingly, the Collector is being authorized to draw funds from the treasury on a minus authority letter for this purpose.
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall entrust the responsibility of keeping an account of the expenditure incurred under Demand No. C-5, Main Account Head 22353195, in the financial year 2023-24, to the responsible officer in his office and the responsible officer shall report the total actual expenditure incurred under this head up to December, 2023 and the actual expenditure incurred during the last three years from January to March for the period of 3 months.
