Introduction: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has been entrusted with the task of preparing, distributing and implementing comprehensive advertising campaigns to effectively disseminate information about people-oriented schemes, development projects, etc. implemented by various departments of the State Government to the public.
The promotion listed under 16/03/2020 is carried out by the organization. General Administration Department 12/04/2023 As per the decision of the Government, the list of category-wise Advertising Agencies" A,"" B" and" C" for carrying out publicity and advertisement related works for the schemes of various departments of the Government has been extended till the list of new Advertising Agencies is finalised. In order to effectively disseminate the information about the people-oriented schemes, development projects etc. implemented by the Revenue and Forest Departments to the public, it is necessary to appoint an agency through tender process for the work of preparing, distributing and implementing comprehensive advertising campaigns by the listed advertising agency. The Government Circular dated 18/05/2023 of the Revenue and Forest Department has constituted a State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee. The meeting was chaired by the Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue, Stamp Duty and Registration). Dated 31/05/2023. It was held at 12.00. In the meeting, it was agreed that a sealed tender should be invited from the agency notified by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for the said work. For this, it is under the consideration of the government to implement the tender process for the related work and give administrative approval to the estimated cost.
