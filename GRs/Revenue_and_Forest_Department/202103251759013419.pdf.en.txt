Preamble: Pench Tiger Reserve Government has accorded approval for the continuation of 42 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme during the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 42 temporary posts created under Pench Tiger Reserve Scheme from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 in the year 2021-2022.
The Finance Department has been empowered to extend the term of the existing temporary posts from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 including the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the pattern. 4 have been provided to the administrative departments under the Government Decision.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the decision of the Finance Department, Government of India vide Reference No. 4, 42 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan scheme Pench Tiger Reserve are being continued during the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
