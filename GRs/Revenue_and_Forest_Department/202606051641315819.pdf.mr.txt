Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to
the State Government, which was received from M/s. Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited,
Gadchiroli for diversion 1.5256 ha. Forest Land under section 2 (11) of the Van (Sanrakshan
Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for laying of underground Optical Fibre Cable along
the road in RoW of Purada to Korchi, Marartola to Yerkadi, Tekameta to Kotgul, Shirpur to
Arotondi, Usegaon to Shivraypur Chek, Chicholi to Belgaon, Khamtala to Malewada in
Armori, Wadsa, Kurkheda, Korchi, Dhanora Tehsil of District Gadchiroli in the State of
Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order
under reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above
proposal for diversion of 1.5256 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;

NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general
approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final Approval for re-
diversion of 1.5256 ha. Forest Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 1.5256 ha. Forest Land
consisting of Reserved & Protected Forest land specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be
diverted to the User Agency, i.e. M/s. Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited, Gadchiroli for laying
of underground Optical Fibre Cable along the road in RoW of Purada to Korchi, Marartola to
Yerkadi, Tekameta to Kotgul, Shirpur to Arotondi, Usegaon to Shivrajpur Chek, Chicholi to
Belgaon, Khamtala to Malewada in Armori, Wadsa, Kurkheda, Korchi, Dhanora Tehsil of
District Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests (1), Wadsa shall ensure compliance of the condition
no. 4, 5 & 19 in ‘in-principle' order dated 15.09.2023, before actual handing over the
forest land to the user agency.
2. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
Order dated 15.09.2023.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits
the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that
Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment
(Protection) Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
5. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above
mentioned ‘in-principle’ and in this order of Government of Maharashtra Chief
Conservator of Forests (T), Gadchiroli shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra
State, Nagpur from time to time.
6. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra’s
order dated 15.09.2023, in this order and Rules and Guidelines made / issued under
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount to violation of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
