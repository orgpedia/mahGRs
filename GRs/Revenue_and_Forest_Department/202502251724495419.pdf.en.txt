The Proposal -
Read about the implementation of the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPSA) system and procedures for the Drawing and Disbursing Officers and other non-governmental operational authorities for faster disbursement and control of subsidy. Paragraph 11. 1 of the said Government Decision provides that the Administrative Departments of the concerned Ministries shall be competent to declare as Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrator (VPDAA), with the concurrence of the Department of Finance, the Regional Drawing and Disbursing Officers and other Non-Governmental Implementing Authorities, for utilization of the funds drawn from the Consolidated Fund of the State under the 27 - Small Constructions, 31 - Subsidiary Subsidies (Non-Payment), 33 - Financing, 35 - Subsidies for Regulation of Capital Assets, 50 - Other Expenditures, 53 - Large Constructions, and such other Subsidiary Grants as may be declared by the Department of Finance from time to time.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to declare the Drawing and Disbursing Officer mentioned in "Return A" in various offices under the Revenue and Forest Department as Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrator (VPDAA). Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.

8- The balances shall be reconciled with A.G. Office by the District Treasury Officer who shall submit Balance Certificate to A.G. Office for confirmation of their balances in the books of A.G. Office at the end of the Financial Year.
9-Monthly Plus Minus Memo related to this' Virtual Personal Deposit Account 'shall be sent to the AG Office.
10 - The validity of Virtual Personal Deposit Account will be till 31/03/2025 .The unspent balances under the 'Virtual Personal Deposit Account' shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund on or before 31 March 2025. Further extension of authority for Virtual Personal Deposit Account can only be allowed with the concurrence of the Principal Accountant General (A & E).

All rules including Rule 495 of Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 shall be applicable to the 'Virtual Personal Deposit Account'.
