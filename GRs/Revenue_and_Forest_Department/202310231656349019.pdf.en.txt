The circular dated 12.04.2023 issued by the Department of Finance vide Reference No. 1 dated 12.04.2023, has issued instructions regarding the distribution of Budgetary Funds on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEMS) for the first nine months of 2023-24 (ending December, 2023), after recording the monthly fund statements, budget estimates, allocation and control system (BEMS) of budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24 by the administrative departments.
Under the Government decision dated 28.07.2023, all the works related to afforestation and soil and water conservation in the forest area will be carried out under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme and the schemes implemented under the Forest Department.
The payment has been approved from the available provisions under the scheme. As per the provisions of the said Government decision, all the works to be carried out under the scheme of" Solid Forestry Programme at Community Level" are also meant to be carried out under the Convergence. However, the works under the Mahatma Gandhi National Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) will be taken up under convergence with the works being taken up under various schemes under the Forest Department.
In order to remove the administrative and technical difficulties faced by the field officers of the forest department, discussions are being held at the senior level to issue instructions regarding the implementation of the convergence plan at the level of the forest department and the planning department (ROHO) by holding meetings from time to time and by correspondence to the central government. In such cases, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Nagpur and the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Social Forestry), Pune, should disburse the funds after verifying the same. The funds should be disbursed only after verifying the technical/ administrative approval granted. It also sought funds from the government.

Before disbursement of the said funds, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall ensure that the funds allocated for the year 2023-24 for the said scheme are not diverted to any other place other than the budgetary distribution system.
The provisions of the Government Circular at Reference No. 1 of the Finance Department and the provisions mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, Fiscal Rights Rule Book and the instructions given from time to time should be followed while spending this fund.
Forest News/ Department wise details of the allocation made and details of the programme implemented/ expenditure incurred should be submitted to the Government in the prescribed format every month. Also, the control of the scheme should be done by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur.
