The Proposal:
Since there are two separate project implementation committees for computerization of the Revenue Department, one for the Revenue and Land Records Office and one for the Office of Registration and Stamp Duty, the government was now considering the formation of a single project implementation committee for the entire Revenue Department.
Government's decision:
The two existing Project Implementation Committees for computerization of Revenue and Land Records and Registration and Stamp Duty offices in the Revenue Department are being merged to form the following Project Implementation Committees.
