Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Project Tiger, 100% subsidy was received from the Central Government for non-recurring expenditure and 50% for recurring expenditure for development and conservation of Tiger Reserves.
As per the letter dated 18.12.2015 of the Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, New Delhi, the share pattern of 60:40 under 100% central share was notified for non-recurring expenditure activities under Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Project Tiger. As per the revised share pattern, 60% Central share and corresponding 40% State share is expected to be released for non-recurring expenditure activities under Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Project Tiger.
As per the revised fund share pattern, the State Government will have to bear 40% of the burden, so it is also the responsibility of the State Government to bear 40% of the burden of salary of the officers/ staff working in the Special Tiger Protection Force.
In view of the time taken by the Central Government to approve the Annual Enforcement Report (APO) under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Project Tiger, the Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) has decided to increase the salary of its officers/ staff for the period from March, 2022 to August, 2022 from Rs. 1441.98 lakh is proposed to be shared from the State Share 40% (2406-A102) of 01-Pay Objective Head in the head Accounts submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forest (Wildlife), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur to the Government vide letter No. 3. The decision is taken accordingly.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Pursuant to the proposal submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur to the Government vide Reference No. 2, the salary of the officers/ staff working in the Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for the period from March, 2022 to August, 2022 has been increased from Rs. 1441.98 lakh is being sanctioned under the said Government decision to be spent from the State share 40% (2406-A102) of the head 01-Pay Target subject to the following conditions.
