Government order:
Under reference The revised pattern of posts in the Forest Department has been approved as per the Government Decision dated 13/09/2019. Also, the sanctioned posts as per this diagram should be reorganized forest-wise and branch-wise. This has been done in accordance with the Government Decision dated 21/09/2021. Therefore, some posts in the forest department have been abolished. The government was also considering posting of officers of the cadre of Divisional Forest Officer Group-A (senior category) and Assistant Conservator of Forests Group-A (junior category) in these posts.
Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of the Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Works Act, 2005, Section 4 (4) and 4 (5), the following transfers are being effected in respect of Divisional Forest Officer Group-A (Senior Grade) and Assistant Conservator of Forests Group-A (Junior Grade) cadre officers, who are working in the demoted post with the approval of the competent authority. :
