Terms and Conditions: 1) The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur shall be the Controlling Officer for the said Fund. The funds are being given to them.
2) The above expenditure should be incurred as per the prescribed procedure and as per the monthly fund flow available on the budget distribution system. There should be no financial irregularities.
(3) The funds shall be spent only after it is ascertained that the technical and administrative approval has been granted by the competent authority as required for the works proposed under the said schemes.
4) The Controlling Authorities are advised to ensure that no additional expenditure is incurred under any circumstances over and above the total disbursed/ to be disbursed.
5) While spending this provision, the terms and conditions laid down by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed.
6) All the provisions of the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions should be followed before drawing and spending the funds available on the budget distribution system from the treasury.
7) Limit expenditure to the extent of grants sanctioned and disbursed in the current financial year 2022-23.
