The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government regarding the continuation of 137 temporary posts created under the scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
Accordingly, Government has accorded sanction for continuation of 137 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation during the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 under Government Resolution No.4.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the pattern of Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern have been provided to the Administrative Department under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department in Reference No. 6, the Government is approving the continuation of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation for the year 2022-2023 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
2. Therefore, the expenditure on establishment of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available during the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
