Government's decision:
As per the Government Resolution, Revenue and Forest Department, No. Parivartan-1004/ PR No. 88/ L-1, dated 28.03.2006, the figure of the posts in the Collector, Mumbai City Geology and Land Records Department has been fixed. Whereas Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department No. Provisional- 2021/ P. No. 202/ E-7, D. As on 12.04.2022, 42 temporary posts in the Mumbai City Geo-Guidance and Land Records Branch at the establishment of Collector, Mumbai City have been extended from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
Now, in pursuance of the Government Decision, Finance Department No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022, after reviewing the sanctioned posts in all the administrative departments and offices under them, the power has been conferred on all the administrative departments to extend the tenure of the temporary posts included in the diagram and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the diagram from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Extension of the period from 1.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 subject to the condition that it has not been curtailed.
