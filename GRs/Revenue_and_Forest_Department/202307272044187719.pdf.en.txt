(8) If it is found that the officer concerned has not joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of his appointment as per the said provisions laid down under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment and Removal of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, he shall be liable to be proceeded against in accordance with the provisions of Government Order No. Narrow 2016/ S.No. 186/16/ Office 14, Sec.
All concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken as per the provisions of 23.12.2016.
(9) After the general transfer orders are issued, the transferred officers shall ensure that they join the place of transfer within the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services and Provisions for Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. All concerned should note that in case of non-appointment of a replacement during this period, the period of his absence shall be treated as" dies non."
10. The concerned officer shall not be granted leave without prior permission of the Government for any reason. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address. If the leave applications of the transferred officers are sent to the parent office, these applications will not be taken into consideration.
All concerned should note that if they do not join the post immediately as per the said transfer orders or try to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, they will be considered as misconduct and disciplinary action will be taken against them as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
