Preamble: It was stated that hailstorm and unseasonal rain caused damage to the agricultural crops in the state from January to February, 2021. Therefore, the government was directed to send a proposal to the government for assistance to the farmers. The proposals have been received from all the Divisional Commissioners vide letter No. 2 to 6. In view of the loss to the farmers due to this natural calamity, as per the decision of the Cabinet Sub-Committee held on 12.05.2021, the matter of disbursement of funds to provide relief to the affected farmers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government has approved the release of a total amount of Rs. 4783.52 lakh (Rs. For Nashik Division, funds are currently being released for losses in the month of February, 2021.

The following precautions shall be taken while allocating assistance for crop and perennial crop loss: (i) As per the extant rules, assistance for crop/ perennial crop loss shall be admissible to those who have suffered loss of 33% or more.
As per the prevailing system, the Collector should distribute the relief to the affected farmers according to the panchnama completed with the joint signature of the agricultural assistant, talathi and gram sevak.
iii) The amount of assistance should be directly credited to the bank accounts of the affected farmers.
(iv) No assistance in cash or kind shall be provided to any affected farmer.
(v) No bank shall make any recovery from the amount of assistance while crediting the amount directly to the bank account of the account holder. For this, the cooperation department should issue appropriate orders.
The Government have approved the release of total amount of Rs. 4783.52 lakhs (Rs. Forty Seven Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand only) as mentioned in the Statement A annexed to this Government Resolution as above, under the head Demand No. C-6, Principal Accounts Head 2245- Assistance for Relief of Natural Calamities, 02, Floods, Cyclones, etc., 101, Anugraha Sahay (91) Expenditure as per State Disaster Response Fund norms, (91) (05) Assistance to farmers for Crop Loss due to Natural Calamities, 31 Subsidiary Grants (2245 2452).
The office-bearers should disburse the funds through the Divisional Commissioner on the BEAMS system.
4. While spending the above funds, the instructions and norms prescribed in the relevant government decision should be strictly followed. The funds should be utilised for the purpose for which they were sanctioned. The amount will be transferred to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries. These funds should not be unnecessarily drawn and deposited in the bank accounts of the Drawing and Disbursing Officers. Expenditure should be made within the limits of the grant sanctioned under this order. After the completion of the process of distribution of assistance to the beneficiaries, the list of beneficiaries and details of assistance should be published on the website of the districts.
5. The accounts of expenditure from the Fund shall be maintained at the level of the Office drawing up the Fund and the Treasury Offices and the Accountant General of the expenditure made.
Quarterly coordination with the office should be done. The Divisional Commissioner will be responsible for receiving the utilization certificates of the fund immediately after the above mentioned funds made available from the State Disaster Response Fund are spent and submit them to the government together.
