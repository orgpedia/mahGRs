The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 142 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 under the Government decision of Reference No.6 and 7.
Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue the 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 9 of the Finance Department to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern, from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department, the Government is approving the continuation of 142 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2023-2024.
The expenditure on the establishment of 138 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No.8/ 16/ A. P.C., dated 08.02.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
