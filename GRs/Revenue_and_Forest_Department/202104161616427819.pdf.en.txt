Preamble: An Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution No. 4 under reference to scrutinize the proposals received from the Divisional Commissioners for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund for taking various measures to contain the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State. The Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee has scrutinized the proposals received from the Konkan divisions for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund and sent their recommendations to the government through letter No. 5. The meeting of the State Executive Committee was held on 15.04.2021 to decide on the recommendations received from the Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee. Accordingly, as per the approval given by the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 15.04.2021, the matter of disbursement of funds to Aurangabad Division and Amravati Division for taking various measures regarding COVID-19 was under consideration of the Government.
