The Proposal:
The Central Government has issued a Government Order under Section 51 of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013. The Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority has been established at Nagpur under 20.12.2016 and a total of 13 posts have been created for it. Considering the nature of the work of the Authority (like judicial work), the matter of extension of these posts was under the consideration of the Government as there was an urgent need for the posts.
Government's decision
A total of 13 temporary posts in the office of Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Nagpur. As per the Government decision dated 03.04.2023. This has been extended up to 31.8.2023. Now, the finance department, the government has taken the decision. 31.8.2023 and Corrigendum No. Subject to the conditions provided under 06.09.2023 and as per the authority provided to the Administrative Department, the temporary posts mentioned in the return in the office of Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Nagpur will be filled by the Deputy Commissioner. The date is being extended from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
