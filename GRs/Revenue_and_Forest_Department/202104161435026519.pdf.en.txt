As per the provision of 15.6.2017 (Revised), the wife of Mr. Sheikh Abdul Majeed Abdul Razzaq, Assistant Conservator of Forests (Rohio and Wildlife) Gondia, was declared mentally ill by the Government vide letter No. As on 27/05/2019, approval has been given to Shri Shaikh to change the cadre from Nagpur Revenue to Aurangabad Revenue.
As per the Government Order dated 17/07/2019, Shri Shaikh has been posted as Assistant Conservator of Forests (Junkas& Campa) in Aurangabad Revenue Division and Nanded Forest Division. The Prime Minister and The Chief Secretary issued the government order after considering the orders regarding the filling of vacancies in the Naxal-affected and tribal departments. As on 21/08/2019, Shri Sheikh Abdul Majeed Abdul Razzaq, Assistant Conservator of Forests (Rohio& Wildlife), Gondia has been transferred and posted as Assistant Conservator of Forests (Junkas& Campa), Kinwat, Nanded Forest Division. It has been postponed to 31/05/2020. The moratorium has expired on 31/5/2020. Accordingly, Shri Sheikh Abdul Majeed Abdul Razzaq, Assistant Conservator of Forests has been relieved from the charge of Gondia Forest Division on 19/06/2020.
Since Sheikh Abdul Majeed Abdul Razzaq's wife is suffering from a mental illness, in view of her treatment, Sheikh's request for a revised posting to the district has been requested. The Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, Division Bench, Nagpur, passed the order on the original application No. 339/2020 vide order dated 20/07/2020 passed by Shri.
