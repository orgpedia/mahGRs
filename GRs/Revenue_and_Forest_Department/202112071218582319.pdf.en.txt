Inclusion of Principal Secretary (Forests) as a member of the Joint Project Implementation Committee constituted for Revenue, Land Records and Registration and Stamp Duty Offices in the Revenue Department.

the Revenue and Forest Department
Govt. Sanitary Card No: Sankiran-2021/ PR No.313/ L-1.
The first floor, the main building, the ministry,
Madame Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai-400032.
The date: December 7, 2021.
Reference:- Department of Revenue and Forest, Government Resolution No: 2021/ No.313/ L-1, Dy. 4.10.2021.
Preamble: As there are two separate Project Implementation Committees for computerization of the Department under the Revenue Department, one for the Revenue and Land Records Office and one for the Office of the Registrar Stamp Duty, a single Project Implementation Committee for the entire Revenue Department has been constituted as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The government was considering including the Principal Secretary (Forests) as a member of the committee. Accordingly, the following resolution was adopted:
Government Decision: The Principal Secretary (Forests) is being included as a member in the Joint Project Implementation Committee formed for the Revenue, Land Records and Registration and Stamp Duty Office in the Revenue Department for computerization of the department under the Revenue Department.
(2) The other provisions of the Government Resolution regarding the constitution of the Joint Project Implementation Committee shall remain in force.
