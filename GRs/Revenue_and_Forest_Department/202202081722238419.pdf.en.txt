The proposal: Kasarwadi, Mineral mining without the permission of the Central Government on the land notified as forest in 1953 in Hatkanangale, District Kolhapur, environmental damage, allotment of forest land to landless people. The National Green Tribunal vide its order dated 20/12/2021 has allowed the quarrying without permission
Action has been taken against the donors, the damage to the environment has been investigated by a joint committee and a restoration plan has been prepared and the amount should be recovered from the concerned persons. A joint committee had been formed by the government in this regard.
Verdict: The case is being investigated. As per the order dated 20/12/2021 passed by the National Green Tribunal, Special Branch, Pune, a Joint Committee is being constituted as follows to take action.

The functions of the said Joint Committee shall be as follows: 1. As per the order of the court, the following issues should be investigated.
a) Action should be proposed against the responsible officials who allowed the excavation without permission in the said forest land.

a) In addition to the above. The other issues in the order of the tribunal should be examined accordingly.
