3. The above mentioned employees are being promoted subject to the following conditions: (a) The approval is being given as per the instructions given under the Government Decision dated 20.04.2023, S.P.V./ 16-A, regarding the promotion of Divyang employees to the posts in Group-A and Group-B.
(b) The said promotion is only temporary in nature and the said temporary promotion will not entitle the officers concerned to regular promotion i.e. seniority.
(c) Since the said post of promotion is under the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the promoted officer shall be eligible for seniority and other service benefits in the said post only after regularization of the promotion (selection list) granted to the post with the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(e) The promotion shall be subject to the order of the Government regarding retention in service beyond the age of 50/55 years.
The above officers who have been promoted to the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Group-B from Group-C post will be eligible for the benefits of the salary of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Group-B (Pay Scale S-15- 41800-132300) only after joining the post of posting and taking charge.
(f) The Government reserves the right to cancel such promotions without any prior notice and intimation.
4. The Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records (M.R.), Pune shall take necessary further action for relieving the above mentioned officers from their present posts and presenting them in the promoted post.
Senior officers concerned are advised to inform the government immediately by e-mail/ post at doe6.rfd@maharashtra. gov. in about the date on which the concerned officer has reached the place of posting.
If it is found that the said officer has not joined the place of posting within the period prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in the Period of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, he shall be liable to be proceeded against in accordance with the provisions of Government Order No. It may be noted that disciplinary action will be taken in accordance with the provisions of S.O. 2016/ S.No. 186/16/ O/ o 14 dated 23.12.2016.
(7) After the said order is issued, the said officers shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions for Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. In case of non-appointment to the post during the prescribed period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
