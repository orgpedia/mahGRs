12. Immediately verify/ confirm the payments submitted by the institution and submit the report to the office of the Commissioner of Deposits.
The Commissioner of Settlement and the Director, Land Records, Pune shall have the power to decide such matters as may be necessary other than those mentioned above.
The Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra, Pune will be responsible for reviewing the progress of the implementation of this project and creating and maintaining a consolidated dashboard accordingly.
The Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra, Pune will be responsible for formulating a time-bound programme for the completion of this digitization project and providing it to all the districts and for its effective control.
The Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Pune shall ensure that the funds available for the implementation of the said project are spent within the stipulated time frame.
