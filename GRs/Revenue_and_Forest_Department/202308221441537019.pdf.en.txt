Preamble: The Revenue Department has decided to celebrate 1st August, 2023 as" Revenue Day" throughout the State and to observe" Revenue Week" from 1st to 7th August this year throughout the State to felicitate the officers/ employees who have completed the works on time and as per schedule and to keep the activities of the Revenue Department in public domain. The decision was taken by the government.
As per the" Revenue Week" from 1st August, at least 4 to 5 days in advance, a press release should be prepared and distributed highlighting the outline of these programs, the nature of the programs, the schedule, how these schemes are going to benefit the citizens/ public. Also, the government was considering approving the cost of advertisement given in newspapers and television channels for the promotion and publicity of the program through leading newspapers, news channels and social media in the district.
Government Decision: In line with the" Revenue Week" from 1st August, the Government is approving a total expenditure of Rs. 1,38,73, 321/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty-eight Lakhs Seventy-three Thousand Three Hundred and Twenty-one only) for the promotion and publicity of the programme by leading newspapers and news channels in the district by preparing a press release highlighting the outline of the programmes, the nature of the programmes, the schedule of the programmes and how these schemes are going to benefit the citizens/ public.
2. Expenditure on account of Revenue and Forest Department No. (00) (01) 26 under 2052 0212- Advertisement and Publicity to be covered by the grants to be made available during the year 2023-24 under this head.
