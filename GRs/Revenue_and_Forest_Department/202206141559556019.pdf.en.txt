Reference to write and preserve confidential reports of Government officers/ employees
The consolidated general instructions have been issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Decision No. 1.
In pursuance of the provisions of Section 51 of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition and Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013, a Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority has been constituted for all the Revenue Departments for the purpose of expeditiously resolving disputes relating to land acquisition. Forest and Revenue Department As per the notification dated 14.3.2022. A presiding officer has been appointed. The presiding officer. The Court has commenced its business on 04.5.2022. A total of 13 posts have been sanctioned in the office of Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Aurangabad.
The Registrar, Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Aurangabad vide their Reference No. 3 It is requested to pass an order regarding who should report and review the confidential reports of the officers and employees working in the above-mentioned posts in the Authority. A proposal was under the consideration of the government to lay down procedures for reporting and reviewing confidential reports of officers and employees of the Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Aurangabad.
