GOVERNMENT ORDER NO. REPLACEMENT-2023/ PR No. 102/ E-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date of release: June 30, 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) of the Maharashtra Transfer Act, 2005 and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Mr. Umesh Bhaskar Patil, Tahsildar and Competent Authority-9, Jhopra, Mumbai is being appointed on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Mr. Umesh Bhaskar Patil, Tehsildar is being relieved unilaterally as per this order. The above officers should be posted immediately.
(3) The said order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector.
The services of Mr. Umesh Bhaskar Patil, Tehsildar are being handed over to the Housing Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai in accordance with the deputation from the office of the Tahsildar and Competent Authority-9, Jhopupra, Mumbai.
5. Mr. Umesh Bhaskar Patil, Tehsildar to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
