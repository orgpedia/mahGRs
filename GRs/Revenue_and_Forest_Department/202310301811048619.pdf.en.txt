The Proposal:
To create awareness about forest conservation among the citizens of the state and to create tree love among the youth, Van Mahotsav program is celebrated by planting trees at various places from 15th June to 30th September. During the Vanamahotsava season, saplings are supplied to farmers, NGOs and social organizations from government nurseries at a discounted rate within the limits set by the government. Funds are distributed under the head" Vanamahotsava" 2406 8551 for nurseries for the production, maintenance and nurturing of the required saplings.

The fund is being handed over to the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur as the Controlling Officer. Care should be taken to implement the work to be taken up under this scheme as per the action plan, as per the prescribed procedure, as per the prescribed terms and conditions and within the stipulated time. Detailed guidelines in this regard should be given to the field officers and the implementation should be reviewed from time to time. Controlling authorities should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds. Further, the funds being disbursed in the above table should be spent only for the works done under the scheme during the month of April to September, 2023. Instructions should be given to the concerned not to spend on other works.
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Nagpur will submit the utilization certificate regarding the funds disbursed under the scheme in the year 2023-24 to the Government within 7 days. Administrative and technical approval of the concerned competent authority for the relevant works while spending the said funds.
The funds should be disbursed as soon as they are disbursed. The entire responsibility of this will be of the office of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur.
Before disbursement of the said funds, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, shall ensure that the funds allocated for the year 2023-24 for the said scheme are not diverted to other places other than the budgetary distribution system.
While spending this fund, the provisions of the Government Circular at Reference No. 1 of the Finance Department and the provisions mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, Fiscal Rights Rule Book and the instructions given from time to time should be followed.
The information and details of the provision distributed district-wise and the details of the programme implemented/ expenditure incurred should be submitted to the government in the prescribed form every month. Also, the control of the scheme should be done by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur.
The expenditure under the scheme should be met from the fund in the approved budget for the year 2023-24 under the head of accounts mentioned below.
