The Proposal:
As per the pattern of the posts at the zonal level of the Rehabilitation Department, a figure of 140 posts in the Pune Division was fixed. However, under the Government Decision dated 21.04.2010 and Government Decision dated 28.01.2010, 25 posts were reduced to 115 posts and thereafter Government Decision No. In pursuance of Composite Regulation 2013/487/ PR No.27/ A-3 dated 03.07.2013, 103 temporary posts were sanctioned after 2013-14 till the previous year by reducing 12 posts. As per the Government decision dated 11.04.2022, 103 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune Division and for the acquisition of land in the beneficiary areas were granted extension till 31.08.2022.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all the existing temporary posts from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision dated 18.08.2022 of the Finance Department. The decision also states that all administrative departments should expedite the process of reviewing the sanctioned posts under the control of administrative departments in the meantime and getting the approval of the high-level secretary committee to the revised figure.
Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 103 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune Division and for the acquisition of land in the beneficiary areas till 28.02.2023.
Government's decision:
In the accompanying statement, under 9 non-plan and 94 district level schemes of the Commissioner's Office for the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune division,
