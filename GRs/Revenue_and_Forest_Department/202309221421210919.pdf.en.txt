GOVERNMENT ORDER: 1 decision of the government. The State Government has taken a policy decision to provide compensation to the next of kin of the officers/ staff who died/ suffered permanent disability in forest fires, wildlife attacks, attacks by forest smugglers/ poachers and while rescuing wild animals under the Forest Rights Act, 2022 (12.10.2022).
As per the provisions of this Government decision, in case of death of Group-A to Group-D officers/ employees due to the above mentioned cause, their legal heirs will be given Rs. 25.00 lakhs as compensation.
