The Proposal:
As per the revised estimates under the head Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee Jan Van Vikas Yojana (2406 8711) for the financial year 2021-22, a total of Rs. 6800.00 lakh has been budgeted. Finance Department Reference No. Guidelines have been issued for disbursement of funds in 2021-22 under Government Decisions 1, 2, 3, 4 and 9.
As per the letter of reference No. 5, 6, 7 and 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted the proposal under the scheme Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Jan-Van Vikas Yojana (2406 8711) for the approval of the Government and further decision is being taken by the Government.

Government's decision:
In view of the decision of the Government in Reference No. 9 of the Finance Department, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter No. 5& 6 and the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter No. 7& 8 of the Reference No. 1176.38 lakhs is being approved for disbursement on the following terms and conditions (Rs. million).

The amount to be spent on this scheme shall be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System in 2021-22 under the head No. 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02, Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Protection of Wildlife, (00) (18) Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Jan-Van Vikas Yojana (2406-8711).
The funds should be spent only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority as required.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds at his level keeping in view the need and urgency of the matter in the attached letter.
The funds disbursed should be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
