GOVERNMENT DECISION: 79 temporary posts under the post-plan scheme of" Forest Conservation General Protection" have been filled up from 1.3.2021 to 31.8.2021 vide Reference No. The deadline has been extended as per the government's decision. Now the reference No. 3 Under the Government Decision dated 10.8.2021 of the Finance Department here, the powers to extend the temporary posts from 1.9.2021 to 22.2.2022 have been provided to the administrative departments.
As per the authority provided by the Department of Finance and as per the proposal received from the Office of the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Nagpur, a total of 79 temporary posts in Statement A accompanying this Government decision under the scheme of Forest Conservation General Protection are hereby approved to be continued from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022.
The expenditure for the said 79 temporary posts shall be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2021-22 under the head Principal Head 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Post-Plan 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development Reconstruction, (03) Protection of Forest from Fire, (03) Protection of Forest from Fire (2406 0131), 01 Salary.
The said Government Decision is being issued in the authority conferred by the Department of Finance, Government Decision No. 2016/ Procedure 8/16/ APC dated 10.8.2021.
