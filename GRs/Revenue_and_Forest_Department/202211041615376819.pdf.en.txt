Preamble: The Sub-Committee of the Finance Department has approved the filling up of 1020 vacancies in the direct service quota in the Group-C posts of Group 4 cadre in the Land Records Department. Accordingly, the recruitment process has been started by publishing an advertisement in various newspapers on 09.12.2021. The cost of recruitment is Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) has been sanctioned. A request has been received from Jamabandi Commissioner and Director Land Records Maharashtra State, Pune to provide 90% of the said supplementary grant. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of subsidy was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Out of the grant sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demand for the cost of direct service recruitment process in Group-C posts Group-4 cadre in Land Records Department, Rs. 1,50,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) has been made available by the Department of Finance on Budget Fund Distribution System. Accordingly, the Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune has been directed to pay a sum of Rs. Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for release of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,50,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS) subject to the following conditions:
(1) The Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune shall expedite the process of collecting the said funds and making payment to the concerned.
