Introduction: The Revenue Department is the backbone of the state administration and the Revenue Department, Registration and Stamps Department and Land Records Department handle the affairs related to immovable properties. The scope of quasi-judicial and administrative work carried out under the Revenue, Land Records and Registration Department is very wide. For this, Government decisions and Government circulars have been amended from time to time regarding the activities to be carried out under the legal provisions on various subjects.
The action needs to be taken on the basis of government decisions/ government circulars issued in different periods on the same subject. For this purpose, a proposal has been sent to the Government regarding the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune for the consolidation and streamlining of Government decisions. 2 has been obtained. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: An Integrated Project Management Unit (UPMU) has been set up for operationalization, feasibility, state-wide implementation and coordination of computerization activities in all the departments of Revenue Department (itself), Registration and Stamps Department, Land Records Department, Land Acquisition Branch and Relief and Rehabilitation Department.
The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner& Director, Land Records, Pune to consolidate and streamline the governance decisions under the Unified Project Management Unit (UPMU).
