Appointment of Director and nomination of Chairman on the Board of Directors of Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Ltd.
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA
Revenue and Forest Department
Order No. FDC-2022/C.R.-116/F-5
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032.
Dated: - 30th November, 2022.
Order
In exercise of the powers conferred under Article 89 (1), and article 104 of the Articles of Association of the Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited ("FDCM"), the Governor of Maharashtra is pleased to appoint and nominate Hon'ble Minister (Forests) as the Director and Chairman respectively on the Board of Director of FDCM in his ex-officio capacity.
The aforesaid appointment as Director and Chairman shall be subject to securing the Director Identification Number (DIN) from the competent Authority as stipulated in the Companies Act, 2013.
Managing Director/Company Secretary, FDCM to take necessary action in this regards, whenever necessary.
