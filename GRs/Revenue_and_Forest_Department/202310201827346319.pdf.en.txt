The date on which the concerned officer has appeared in place of the new appointment should be informed to the government immediately by e2.revenue@maharashtra. gov. in by e-mail/ post.
If it is found that the concerned officer has not joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of his posting as per the said provisions prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, disciplinary action will be taken against him as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
(4) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions for Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
