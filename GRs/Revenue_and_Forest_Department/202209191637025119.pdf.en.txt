Government Decision: The decision was taken on August 24, 2015. The appointment of Mr. Kishore Tiwari (Non-Government) as the Chairman of Vasantrao Naik Krishi Swavlamban Mission is being cancelled by this government decision. Until further orders, Divisional Commissioner, Amravati, Sh. Vasantrao Naik has been given the additional charge of the chairman of the Agriculture Self-Reliance Mission.
Divisional Commissioner, Amravati is a government officer and since salary and allowances are payable to him as per rules, honorarium, allowances etc. will not be applicable to him separately.
