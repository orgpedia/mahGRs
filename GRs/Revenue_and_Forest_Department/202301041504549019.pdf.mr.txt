Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a
proposal for diversion of 1.2 ha. ‘Private Forest’ land (Restored before 25/10/1980) in favour of M/s Sanjay Madhav Thorve & others for construction of Residential Houses, Roads, Electric Line, etc at Village Kunenama Tal. Maval, Dist. Pune;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has
submitted proposal to Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Govt. of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
under reference No.3, accorded 'In-Principle' approval to divert the 1.20 ha. Private
Forest land (Restored before 25/10/1980) for the above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;

AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded 'Final approval' to divert the 1.2 ha. 'Private Forest land (Restored before 25/10/1980) in favour of M/s Sanjay Madhav Thorve & others for construction of Residential Houses, Roads, Electric Line, etc at Village Kunenama Tal. Maval, Dist. Pune under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in this case;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 1.20 ha consisting of Private Forest Land (Restored before 25/10/1980) more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. M/s Sanjay Madhav Thorve & others for construction of Residential Houses, Roads, Electric Line, etc at Village Kunenama Tal. Maval, Dist. Pune.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 31.05.2022 and dated 10.10.2022 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC,
Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the In Principle' and 'Final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above. The Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 31.05.2022 and dated 10.10.2022, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
