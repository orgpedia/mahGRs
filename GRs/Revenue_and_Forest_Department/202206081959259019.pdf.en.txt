The Proposal:
The flood situation in Mahad city has been attributed to the accumulation of silt in the Savitri river. The report of the committee appointed by the Irrigation Department states that the purpose of the softening of the river is to remove silt from the river and thereby prevent flood situations. The mechanical department of the Water Resources Department has provided the machinery to remove the silt and has been requested under the reference letter to provide funds to those plants for fuel expenditure.
