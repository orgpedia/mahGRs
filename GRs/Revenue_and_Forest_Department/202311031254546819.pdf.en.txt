Read: Revenue and Forest Department, Government Resolution No: LVSU-2023/ PR No.114/ F-9, dated 26.07.2023
Preamble: In the second monsoon session of 2023, in the context of collective forest rights in the forest department Gadchiroli, Alapalli and Vadsa and harvesting of bamboo through gram sabhas in the compartments under PESA, an interesting suggestion was raised under Rule 105 of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. During the discussion on this important suggestion, a committee will be appointed on the problems related to bamboo harvesting. The Minister gave instructions.
Accordingly, the Committee was appointed as per the Government decision dated 26.07.2023. The government was considering to give an extension of 3 months to the committee as some important issues were yet to be discussed to take a decision regarding collective forest rights and bamboo harvesting under PESA in Gadchiroli, Alapalli and Vadsa forest divisions.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Read more It is hereby approved to extend the term of the Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 26.07.2023 upto 3 months from the date of issue of the said Government Decision.
