The scope of the Research Advisory Committee shall be as follows: -
1. Prioritizing research work and research projects under Agroforestry projects.
Designing a research work plan.
2. Designing research topics and making relevant financial plans for planning.
3. Approval of research projects to be undertaken by the Forest Department out of project funds.
4. Coordinate research projects within the institute / department.
5. Formulating the subject matter of the forest research project to be given to external agencies, their scope of work.
The cost involved and the selection of the appropriate institutions.
6. Wildlife management, selecting and implementing projects to reduce wildlife and human conflict.
7. Coordination with National Standards Bodies and exchange of research information.
