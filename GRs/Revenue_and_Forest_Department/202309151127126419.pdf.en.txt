The Proposal:
The temporary posts created for the office of the Collector and Project Director, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar and the field office for the rehabilitation and other ancillary works of the Sardar Sarovar Project will be given. The deadline has been extended to August 31, 2023.
The Collector and Project Director, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar have requested for further extension of the 33 temporary posts as the rehabilitation work of the project victims is still going on. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these posts.
Government Decision: 33 posts mentioned in the annexure created for the rehabilitation of Sardar Sarovar Project victims in the District Collector Office (SSP) at Nandurbar and the Regional Office have been sanctioned. The period of extension is from September 01, 2023 to February 28, 2024.
These 33 posts should be considered as a temporary increase in the permanent cadre of the concerned department.
Dearness Allowance and other allowances will be admissible to the holders of these posts as per rules.
The expenditure for all the above temporary posts shall be met from the grants sanctioned in the financial year 2023-2024 under Demand No." MAIN ACCOUNT TITLE C-5,2235- SOCIAL SECURITY AND WELFARE, 200 OTHER MEASURES OF SUPPORT- (03) (07) Revenue& Forest Department Upper Collector, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar (2235-3132) (Non-Plan Datta Mat)" Thereafter, the said expenditure shall be adjusted in consultation with the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar, by book adjustment under the head of Water Resources Department, by the end of the year 2023-2024 under the head" Demand No. C- 11-4801- Capital Expenditure on Power Projects 01 Hydro Power Generation- Sardar Sarovar Project- Under Schemes."
