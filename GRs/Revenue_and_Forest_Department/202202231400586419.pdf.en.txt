4. The concerned officers should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government.
Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave application of the transferred officer. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
If the concerned officer does not appear in the post of transfer immediately as per the said transfer order or tries to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against him considering it as a misconduct as the action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
