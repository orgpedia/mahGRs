Preamble: The Government have accorded approval for the continuation of 69 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 in the year 2021-2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue 69 posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
The Department of Finance has been empowered to extend the term of temporary posts included in the format of the department as well as all existing temporary posts created after approval of the review earlier but not included in the format from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022. 6 has been provided to the administrative departments under the Government Decision.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department, Reference No.6, the Government is approving the continuation of 69 temporary posts in the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali (as mentioned in the annexure) for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
The expenditure on establishment of 69 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 800 Other Expenditure, (01) Other Expenditure, (01) (02) Forest Upavanes (24060659)" shall be met from the provision available during the financial year 2022-2023.
