Appointment of Nominee Director on the Board of Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Ltd.
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA
Revenue and Forest Department
Order No. FDC-2021/CR-100/F-5
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032.
Dated - 29th September, 2021
Reference - 1) Government DO letter No.AEO-1121/10/2021/10, dtd.24/08/2021.
2) Managing Director, FDCM Ltd., Nagpur letter No.CSL/Govt.
Correspondence /65 Nagpur, Dated 06/09/2021
Order
As per the powers conferred under Article 89 (1), (2), and (4) of the Articles of Association
of the Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited (“FDCM”), it is hereby decided to
nominate Smt. Jayshree Bhoj (IAS), Managing Director, Maharashtra Tourism Development
Corporation Limited, Mumbai as Nominee Director on the Board of Directors of Forest
Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited.
2. Her appointment shall take effect from the date of allotment of Director Identification
Number or from the date of this order, whichever is later.
3. The appointment of Shri Ashutosh Salil (IAS), Nominee Director is being withdrawn from
the date of this order.
4. Managing Director/Company Secretary, FDCM Ltd. Nagpur to take necessary action in this
regards.
