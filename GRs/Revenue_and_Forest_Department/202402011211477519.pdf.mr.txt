अटी व शार्ती:-
1) Funds received for land acquisition shall be credited to this PLA and shall be withdrawn to compensate the land owners in case of acquisition of land.
2) All provisions of Accounting Rules for treasuries 1992 and all provisions of PLA in view of MTR 1968 Vol.I shall apply to this PLA.
3) The administrator should be ensure proper maintenance of Account and to keep the Account operative.
4) The Administrator shall be responsible for reconciliation with Treasury / Sub Treasury.
5) A fresh concurrence shall be sought for any change in the purpose of deposit of funds into the PLA and withdrawals of the funds therefrom.
6) This PLA shall be subjected to Audit as per rule.
