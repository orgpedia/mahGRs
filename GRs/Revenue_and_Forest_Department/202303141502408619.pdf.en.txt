Under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the rolls with effect from 01/03/2023.
