Introduction: Since 2020-21, due to the Covid situation, funds under the post-Plan scheme have not been received as per requirement due to a large reduction in funds. Hence, the rent of the offices on lease basis from the year 2020-21 is pending. As a result, the government has to bear the burden of interest and penalties.
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur In the letter dated 24.02.2022, the amount for payment of monthly rent, electricity bill and water bill for the month of July, 2020 to January, 2022 of BSNL Sanchar Laxmi Building is Rs. 3,58,60, 481/- is due and 2% late payment on the said rent and 15% increment on expiry of 3 years as per the terms of the agreement is Rs. 60,27,607/- aggregating to Rs. 4,18,88, 088/- for the year 2021-22 under the head of Accounts Administration and Protection (2406 0096) has been submitted to the Government stating that it is necessary for the sub-heading of 14-Lease and Tax and for this purpose, a proposal has been submitted to the Government to reallocate the funds available under sub-heading 11- Domestic Travel Expenses under the sub-heading of 14-Lease and Tax.
Accordingly, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, was under the consideration of the Government to reallocate the funds available under the sub-heading of Title Administration and Protection (2406 0096) sub-heading 11-Inland Travel Expenditure for the year 2021-22 under the sub-heading 14-Lease and Tax.
