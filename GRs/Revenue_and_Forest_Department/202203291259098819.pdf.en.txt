Preamble: The preliminary works for the proposed Zoo at Gorewada for the financial year 2021-22 under the head (4406-3858) as per revised estimates, a total of Rs. 5625.00 lakh has been budgeted. Finance Department Reference No. Guidelines have been issued for disbursement of funds in 2021-22 under Government Decisions 1, 2, 3, 5 and 6.
Further decision is being taken by the Government in accordance with the proposals submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for Government approval under the scheme of preliminary works (4406-3858) of the proposed zoo at Gorewada.
Government's decision:
In view of the decision of the Government in Reference No. 6 of the Department of Finance, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, in view of the demand for funds made under Reference No. 4, the preliminary works of the proposed zoo at Gorewada (4406-3858) under the heading of Accounts, for various purposes as shown in the table below, Rs. 939.91 lakhs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions as per attached statement:

The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur shall disburse the funds for the preliminary works of the proposed zoo at Gorewada in Nagpur district only after ensuring that the requisite viability gap funding from the Government has not been released earlier.
(ii) Proposed Expenditure: The capital expenditure on account head 4406, Forestry and Wildlife 02, Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 111 Zoo, (00) (01) Preliminary works of the proposed Zoo at Gorewada (State) 4406 3858 should be spent from the grants available through the Budget Distribution System during 2019-2020 under the head of account head 4406.
The funds shall be released by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted to the proposed works under the schemes by the competent authority as required.
The funds disbursed should be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
