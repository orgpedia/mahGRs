Introduction: The Naradve Project (G. Sindhudurg) for getting alternate land or financial compensation from the project owners. In this case, The High Court asked the petitioners to allocate alternative show land or monetary compensation within five months. The command is given on 10.11.2022. Sadar D. Due to non-implementation of the order dated 10.11.2025 within the stipulated time period, the petitioners have challenged the order. Contempt Petition No. 327/2023 and 7 other attached contempt petitions have been filed. The contempt petitions. The hearing was held on 20.03.2025 and was delivered to the petitioners / project participants related to the Naradve project. Due to non-allocation of land or monetary compensation as per the order dated 10.11.2022. The High Court has expressed deep displeasure.
In pursuance of the order of the High Court, Shri. The meeting was chaired by the Chief Secretary and attended by all the concerned officers. The meeting was held on 01.04.2025. The Water Resources Department has agreed to provide funds to the District Collector, Sindhudurg as the administrative mechanism for the Nardave project to comply with the said order of the High Court. However, as the office of the District Rehabilitation Officer, Sindhudurg, does not have a Personal Property Account (PLA) to receive these funds, the Deputy Collector (Rehabilitation), Sindhudurg, has submitted a proposal to the government to open a Personal Property Account (PLA) under the reference letter. The proposal was under consideration of the government for approval.
The government's decision:
The Deputy Collector (Rehabilitation) as per rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968,
To open a Private Register Account in the name of the Collectorate, Sindhudurg, subject to the following conditions:
Approval is being given.
