Government Decision: A revised budget of Rs. 230.25 lakh has been made for the financial year 2020-21 under the scheme" Maharashtra Scheduled Tribes Sale of Trees to Consumers (Regulation) Act, 1969 Provisions to Tribal Account Holders" (2406 0686). Out of which a total of Rs. 150.00 lakhs (02 wages Rs. 10.50 lakh, 50 other expenses- Rs. 139.50 lakhs) has been disbursed and the balance is Rs. 80.25 lakhs (02 wages- Rs. 10.50 lakh, 50 other expenses- Rs. 69.75 lakhs) to the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur subject to the following conditions:
1) The funds should be spent for the purpose for which the expenditure has been sanctioned 2) It should be ensured that there is no financial irregularity.
The fiscal limits prescribed by the Department of Finance under the Government Decision dated 11/7/2001 shall continue to apply.
The approval is for the period 2020-21.
2. The said expenditure should be shown under the head Demand No. C-7,2406 Forestry and Wildlife 800 Other Expenditure, (01) Other Expenditure, (01) (05)" Provisions to Tribal Account holders under the Maharashtra Sale of Trees to Scheduled Tribes Occupants (Regulations) Act, 1969" (2406 0686) and should be met from the budgetary grants sanctioned in the year 2020-21. While spending the funds, the instructions given by the Finance Department in the monthly fund statement and other matters should be followed.
This Government decision is in line with the circular dated 15.03.2021 of the Finance Department and the Government Decision No. VYAPR-2013/ PR. 30/2013/ Regulations/ Part-II of the Finance Department, dated 17.04.2015 Part-I Sub-Division-III, the power conferred on the Department under paragraph 27 (2) as well as the Informal Reference No. 162/ Expenditure-10, being issued pursuant to the approval received vide dated 26.3.2021.
