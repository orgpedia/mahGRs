Preamble- The payment of salaries and allowances of the employees of the Regional Office of the Forest Department of the Government will be passed from the treasury like the employees of other departments from February 2006. The decision has been taken as per the Government decision dated 04.01.2006. Accordingly, as per Rule 13 and Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the list of Drawing and Disbursing Officers in the department is sent to the Accountant General's Office. Accordingly, the lists of Drawing and Disbursing Officers of the Department have been published as per the Government decisions issued from time to time.
Accordingly, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur, vide his Reference No. 2 A proposal has been submitted to the Government to declare Forest Range Officer (Wildlife) Parva as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for discharging the salary and allowances of his subordinate officers/ staff and for other field activities. Accordingly, the matter of declaring the said designation as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur vide his Reference No. 2 In pursuance of the proposal submitted vide letter dated 01.02.2020, the post of Forest Range Officer (Wildlife), Parwa, has been transferred to the Department of Finance. the areas under their jurisdiction as per the Government decision dated 04.01.2006
