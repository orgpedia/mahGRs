Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a
proposal to the State Government, which was received from Sub-Divisional Engineer,
NOFN, OFC, BSNL, Pune for 0.0853 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanarakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for laying of BSNL Optical Fibre Cable for 4G
saturation project passing through forest area at village Harpud, Varoti BK, Tal. Velhe,
Dist. Pune in the State of Maharashtra.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its |
| order under reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle'’ approval with certain conditions to |
| above proposal for diversion of 0.0853 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van |
| (Sanarakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980; |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.3 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of
Guidelines for effective and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van
# Page 2
(Sanarakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 [Chapter-4, Para-4.2], Government
of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final Approval for diversion of 0.0853 ha. Forest
land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.0853 ha. Forest Land
consisting of Reserved Forest land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below,
be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. Sub-Divisional Engineer, NOFN, OFC, BSNL,
Pune for laying of BSNL Optical Fibre Cable for 4G saturation project passing through
forest area at village Harpud, Varoti BK, Tal. Velhe, Dist. Pune in the State of
Maharashtra. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of
following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Pune should ensure that, the fulfilment of
Condition No. 2, 4 & 5 and other conditions mentioned in the order, dated
15.09.2023, before actual handing over the forest land to the user agency.
2. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in
the Order dated 15.09.2023.
3. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will
reconfirm that compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and
Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
4. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above
mentioned ‘in-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The
Chief Conservator of Forests (Territorial), Pune shall ensure the strict
compliance of the conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in
this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
5. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of
Maharashtra’s order dated 15.09.2023, in this order and Rules and Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanarakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980,
shall amount to violation of Van (Sanarakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
6. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief
Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the
State Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional
Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van
(Sanarakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
