As provided to the concerned administrative departments under the Government decision of Reference No. 9 of the Finance Department, 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest transferred to the Conservator of Forest and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur was approved for continuation in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
