The Proposal:
State Wildlife Board In the 16th meeting held on 04.12.2020, it has been decided to constitute a sub-committee of the Board to examine the possibility of declaring some areas of conservation reserves in the State as sanctuaries. According to reference no. Under the decision of the 1st Government, 3 sub-committees of the Board have been constituted. At the same time, the government was considering the formation of a sub-committee as per the decision of the board to examine the possibility of declaring the Mahendri Conservation Reserve as a sanctuary.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to constitute a Sub-Committee comprising of non-official members of the State Board of Wildlife and officers of the area to examine the possibility of declaring the Mahendrai Conservation Reserve as a sanctuary as follows:
