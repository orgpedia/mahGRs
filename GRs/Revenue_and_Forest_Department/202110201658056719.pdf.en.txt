The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. Care should be taken to implement the work to be taken under this scheme as per the action plan, prescribed procedures and within the stipulated time. Detailed guidelines in this regard should be given to the field officers and the implementation should be reviewed from time to time. The funds will be spent on approved projects.
Funds should be disbursed only after ensuring that technical and administrative approval is given to the proposed works under the scheme. Controlling authorities should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds.
Before disbursing the funds, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development) shall ensure that the funds allocated for the scheme for the year 2021-22 are not diverted to any other place other than the budgetary distribution system. The disbursement of funds should be made subject to these conditions.
