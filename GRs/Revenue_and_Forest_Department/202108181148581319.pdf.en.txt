Government Decision: In accordance with Rule 3 and the accompanying Sample 4 of the All India Services (Performance Appraisal Report) Rules, 2007, Officers of Indian Forest Service who are 40 years of age or above as on 31/3/2020 are required to undergo Medical Examination for the year 2020-21. Orders have been issued under the Government Decision dated 16/04/2021. Medical examination. The instructions to be followed till 30/06/2021 have been issued as per the relevant Government decisions.
In view of the increasing prevalence of Novel Corona Virus (COVID-19), Reference No. In a letter dated 02.02.2020, the Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India, has issued the timeline for medical examination of All India Services Officers for the year 2020-21. It is proposed to be extended up to 31/12/2021.
Accordingly, for the year 2020-21, the time limit for conducting medical examinations for Indian Forest Service officers of Maharashtra cadre who are 40 years of age or above has been extended. is being extended till 31/12/2021.
