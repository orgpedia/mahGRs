Government circular:
While considering the stamp duty required while executing/ registering the land deed/ deed/ deed provided by the Government in possession/ deed/ lease, the stamp duty to be levied on the said deed/ deed without assessing the income as per the instructions given by the above referential Government Decision/ Circular shall be levied as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Stamp Act/ Rules determining the actual retail market value of income and the Government Decision/ Circular/ Order issued accordingly.
