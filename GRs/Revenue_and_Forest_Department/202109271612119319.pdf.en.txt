Preamble: Under the above Reference No. 1, a review of the posts at the zonal level of the Rehabilitation Department has been fixed and as per the decision of the Government, the figure of 32 posts in the Konkan Division has been fixed.
A total of 32 sanctioned as per the diagram for rehabilitation work under Konkan Division+ 6 temporary+ 2 temporary posts in Palghar district through external mechanism 38+2 (through external mechanism) such temporary posts were extended from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 as per the Government decision dated 4/03/2021. Now, under the Government Order dated 4/08/2021 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend all the existing temporary posts on their establishment and establishment of offices under their administrative control from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022. Therefore, the matter of extension of the said 38+2 (through external mechanisms) temporary posts from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the continuation of the posts to be filled through the 38+ 2 external mechanism created for the rehabilitation of project oustees in the office of the Commissioner, Konkan Division, Navi Mumbai and District Rehabilitation Office, Thane/ Raigad/ Ratnagiri/ Sindhudurg/ Palghar from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 subject to the following conditions.
All these posts should be considered as a temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to the holders of these posts as per rules.
