Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.! has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 63.3666 ha. Forest land under section-2 of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for construction and laying of 220 KV LILO Transmission
line from existing SPCL-IPCL near tower 64 to GAIL Usar Substation required for upcoming
500 KTPA PDH-PP Plant in Raigad District in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has submitted
proposal to Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 63.3666 ha. The Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;

AND whereas, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (HOFF), Maharashtra State,
Nagapur vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions to
the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 5 Accorded 'Final Approval'
to divert the 63.3666 ha. of Forest land in favour GAIL (India) Ltd., Raigad for construction
and laying of 220 KV LILO Transmission line from existing SPCL-IPCL near tower 64 to
GAIL Usar Substation required for upcoming 500 KTPA PDH-PP Plant in Raigad District in
the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order in the matter;
AND whereas, the Collector, Raigad has issued certificate under Scheduled Tribes and
Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 for 63.3581 ha.
forest land, vide letter under reference No.7.
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that out of proposed
63.3666 ha. forest land only 63.3581 ha. forest land more specifically described in the
proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. ] and as per details given in the Schedule
given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. GAIL (India) Ltd., Raigad, for construction
and laying of 220 KV LILO Transmission line from existing SPCL-IPCL near tower 64 to
GAIL Usar Substation required for upcoming 500 KTPA PDH-PP Plant in Raigad District in
the State of Maharashtra. Decision for remaining 0.0085 ha. forest land will be taken
separately, after compliance of Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers
(Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 15.02.2024 and dated 06.05.2024 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land 15 also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. Rest of the 0.0085 ha. proposed Land shall not be handed over to the User Agency, till
further direction of the State Government.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoOEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry 11 the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the
free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
