Circular: The Government Notification, General Administration Department dated 21.06.1982 prescribes guidelines for the publication of seniority of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. Accordingly, the seniority of each calendar year of each category is to be prepared and published considering 1st January as the base date of that year. General Administration Department Reference No. 1 The Government Decision dated 21.10.2011 mentioned herein has given detailed instructions for determining the policy on seniority. As per these instructions, the matter of publishing the combined final seniority of the officers of the cadre of Additional Collector in the Revenue Service was under consideration.
