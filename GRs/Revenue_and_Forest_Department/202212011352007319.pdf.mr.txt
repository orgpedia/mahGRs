Order
In exercise of the powers conferred under Article 24 & 35 of the Articles of
Association of the Company to the Governor of Maharashtra, it is hereby decided to allow
the transfer of one equity share of Rs.100/- each to following person in their ex-officio
capacity.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Sr. No. | Ex-officio | Designation on the Board |
| 1 | Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur | Director |
| 2 | Managing Director, Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited. | Managing Director FDCM Ltd. |
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. The transfer shall take effect from the date of allotment of Director Identification
Number, or from the date of taking over the charge, whichever is later.
3. Managing Director/Company Secretary, FDCM Ltd. to take necessary action in this
regard.
4. Any two Directors and Company Secretary, FDCM Ltd. be and are hereby
authorized to sign and issue the share certificate, whenever required.
