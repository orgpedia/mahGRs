Preamble- The final award was passed on the compensation of the lands acquired by the Land Acquisition Officers while acquiring the lands for the rehabilitation of the earthquake victims in Osmanabad district. As the compensation for the land was not admissible to the land holder, the Government decided to increase the compensation under Section 18 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894. A case was filed against the government. The civil court has decided to give increased compensation to the landlord.
The Law and Justice Department, Aurangabad, has given its opinion on not appealing against the decision. Therefore, the Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation), Divisional Commissioner Office, Aurangabad had requested to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 21,67,756/-. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of Rs. 21,67,756/- is under consideration of Government.
Government Decision: Land acquired for the rehabilitation of earthquake victims in Osmanabad district. As per the decision of the Civil Court, the Government is pleased to disburse the amount of Rs. 21,67,756/- as demanded by the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad for payment of enhanced remuneration in the case mentioned in the following table as per the provisions of Section 18 and Section 28A of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894.
