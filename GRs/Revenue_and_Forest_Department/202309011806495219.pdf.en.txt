Government order:
The reference No. Swapnil Tangde was appointed as Deputy Collector, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai on deputation. Pursuant to the orders passed by the competent authority vide reference No. As per the letter, the Housing Department has been informed about the return of the services of Mrs. Vaishali Lambhate to the parent department. Accordingly, Reference No. 3 Subject to the provisions of Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of transfers and delays in discharge of official duties) Act, 2005 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, post-retirement service and suspension, dismissal, removal from service) Rules, 1981, the Amravati Division allotted to Shri Swapnil Tangade, Deputy Collector, shall be retained by the competent authority-4, Slum Rehabilitation Authority, Mumbai and the services of Smt. Vaishali Lambhate shall be returned to the parent department and the vacancy shall be filled up by a deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of three years.
The services of Mr. Swapnil Tangde, Deputy Collector are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Mumbai, in pursuance of his deputation to the post of Competent Authority-4, Slum Rehabilitation Authority, Mumbai.
