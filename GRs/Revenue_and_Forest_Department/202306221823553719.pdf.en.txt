Introduction: In order to help the farmers in the next season in case of loss due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods and cyclones, the farmers are given assistance in the form of input subsidy at a prescribed rate from the State Disaster Response Fund. Also, assistance is provided at prescribed rates for other approved items of the State Disaster Response Fund. Heavy rain is a disaster declared by the state government and if there is more than 65 mm of rain in 24 hours in the automatic weather station provided in the revenue circle, the damage to the agricultural farmers is done in all the villages of the mandal. If the crop loss is more than 33 per cent, the farmers are given subsidy at the rate prescribed for the area where the loss has occurred.
The Chief Minister said that assistance in the form of financial assistance to the affected farmers, who suffered crop loss in various districts due to heavy rains and floods in July, 2022, and assistance for other losses would be provided. Pursuant to the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 10.08.2022, the Government have decided to implement the Government Resolution, Revenue and Forest Department No.CLS-2022/ PR No.253/ M-3, dated 22.08.2022, for the loss of agricultural crops in various districts of the State due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods, etc., during the period from June to October, 2022.
