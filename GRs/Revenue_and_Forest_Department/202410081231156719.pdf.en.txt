Proposal: - Ma. The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" is functioning under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, State of Maharashtra and the sanctioned funds for sanction of new works for the year 2024-25 have been sanctioned as per the percentage of Taluk wise approval. Accordingly, the funds have been sanctioned as per the Taluk-wise percentage. Accordingly, 7 per cent of the funds have been earmarked for Taluka Javali and the issue of administrative sanction for development works in Taluka Javali was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - For the development of villages located in the remote areas of 8 taluks (Patan, Karad (North), Karad (South), Javali, Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Sangameshwar and Chiplun) falling under the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee without any expenditure from the budget of the State of Maharashtra and the funds received by the committee from the State Government, a total of 12 works have been given technical approval by the office of Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad Construction Department, South (, Satara) for disbursement of funds to the District Collector, Satara for the purpose of expenditure for the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee only as per the rules of the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee. The amount of technical approval for 12 works is Rs. 3,65,00, 000 / - (Rupees Three Crore Sixty Five Lakhs only). Accordingly, from the sanctioned grant, the development works in Javali Taluka are being accorded administrative sanction as mentioned in Annexure-A attached thereto.
The Executive Committee of the Committee in its meetings dated 29.07.2013 has decided as follows to carry out the functions under the Committee.
