Introduction: - Natural sand is of great ecological importance and is important for the natural baby water system. Therefore, in order to reduce the stress on river bed sand, the Sand / Sand Export Policy-2025 provides for promoting the use of eco-friendly sand that can be produced from overburden from mining of other major minerals like coal or iron ore as by-products as well as from overburden from mines adopted by the Central / State Government.
The government was considering revising the existing base rate so that more entrepreneurs could come forward to produce eco-friendly sand that could be produced from overburden.
