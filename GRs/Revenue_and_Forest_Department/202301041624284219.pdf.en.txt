Introduction: The subject of land acquisition is under the jurisdiction of the Revenue and Forest Department. However, the land acquisition department is part of the revenue department. under the Minister of Revenue. It is under the direct administrative control of the Secretary (Forests). However, the correspondence from various agencies related to land acquisition is done in the form of the Ministry of Revenue Department. Hence, there is a delay in receiving such letters. This matter It has to be brought to the notice of the High Court, Legislature, Accountant General, Ministerial Administrative Divisions, Regional Offices of the Revenue Department, various authorities (for whom land acquisition is done), etc. The matter was under consideration of the government to bring this fact to the notice of all through a circular, following which the government is giving the following information.
Government Circular: 1) All correspondence regarding land acquisition shall be addressed to the Principal Secretary (Forests), Office No. 103 (No. 022 2202 3363), 1st Floor, Main Building, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
The Land Acquisition Branch has four functions (A-1, A-2, A-3, A-4). Land Acquisition Cell No. 451 (Office of the Joint Secretary), 452 and 453, 4th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
All correspondence (administrative, court, legislative) regarding land acquisition should be done taking into account the above mentioned facts.
