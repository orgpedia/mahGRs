4. The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of posting by the promotion. If they do not appear at the place of posting within the prescribed time after being relieved as such, they will not be interested in the promotion, according to the Government Decision, General Administration Department, Dy. 30/04/1991 and D. It may also be noted that the officers who are denied promotion, if they have been given the benefit of 12/24 years of self-help group-A post under the Assured Progress Scheme, it should be stopped immediately and they will be given this benefit after direct promotion to self-help group-A cadre.
5. The promoted officer should immediately give the option of fixation of pay on taking charge of the post of Personal Assistant, Group-A. If such an option is not given, their salary shall be determined in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, Rule No. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The officer who has been given a posting by this promotion should be relieved immediately to appear in the post of promotion by the controlling officers. Also, the concerned Controlling Authorities should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail website ananda. shendge@nic. in about the date on which the concerned officers were relieved of their duties to appear for the post of promotion and the date on which they appeared at the place of posting by promotion.
