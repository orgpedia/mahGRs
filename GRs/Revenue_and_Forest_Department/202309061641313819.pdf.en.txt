As per the provisions of the said Act under 25.07.2007, in respect of grant of Government land at concessional rates to the blind and disabled persons and to the institutions dealing with the blind and disabled persons, and in respect of grant of Government land to the institution for construction of flats/ houses for residential purposes, each such institution has decided to include 3% of its total number of members from the category of blind and disabled.
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has come into force. In the policy dated 25.07.2007, while sanctioning government land to the institution for the purpose of construction of residential/ housing, it was under the consideration of the government that each such institution should provide reservation for the disabled category to the extent of 5% of the total number of its members.
