The Proposal:
Establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) for the financial year 2023-24 with a total outlay of Rs. 8800.00 lakh has been approved and budgeted.
Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. Guidelines have been issued for the distribution of financial funds for the year 2023-24 in accordance with the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2023.
Reference No. 5 Under the decision of the Deputy Conservator of Forests, Western Range, Nashik Forest Division, Rs. Administrative approval has been granted for a budget of Rs. 800.00 lakhs. Further, as per Reference No. 6& 7, for the year 2023-24, Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning& Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has sanctioned Rs. 1236.36 lakh has been submitted to the Government for approval. The government has taken the decision in this regard.
Government Decision: In view of the Government Decision of Reference No. 5 and the demand for funds made by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter No. 6 and 7 of Reference No. 1 of Finance Department, under the heading Establishment of Wildlife Handicap Centre (2406 2232), a sum of Rs. 1061.51 Lakhs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions as per attached statement: i. The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System in the year 2023-24 under the scheme (00) (10) (2406 2232) under the State Plan under the head No. 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife under the Five Year Plan.

The funds should be spent only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority as required.
iii. Procurement should be done through GeM portal by following the required procedure.
(iv) Please ensure that no funds are spent from other schemes other than the scheme for establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) on the work items in the proposal submitted for Government approval.
(vi) Ensure that no violation of the provisions of the Indian Forest Act, 1927, the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the guidelines of the National Zoological Park is committed and necessary permission is obtained.
(vi) It shall be ensured that the subsidy disbursed will be spent only on the works mentioned in the proposal which have received administrative approval of the competent authority.
The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the provisions of the proposal.
viii. The concerned Controller/ Implementing Officer shall follow the instructions and procedures prescribed by the Government for the proposed construction. It will also be their responsibility to avoid any financial irregularities.
ix. Procurement, if it involves procurement, should be done by following the procedure prescribed by the Government.
X. Expenditure in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the Department from time to time in pursuance of the said scheme and the guidelines laid down by the Finance Department from time to time shall be in accordance with the terms of reference of the proposal.
xi. The various guidelines issued by the Finance Department and the Finance Department, Government Circular No. No.40/ Earth-3, dated 12.04.2023 to be strictly followed.
This Government Decision is being issued pursuant to Finance Department, Government Circular No:-Finance 2023/ P.No.40/ Finance-3, dated 12.04.2023.
