Appointment to the post of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force) Maharashtra State
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASTRA
Revenue and Forest Department
Government Order No: AFO-2023/C.R.76/F-7
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date: 31st August, 2023
ORDER
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to promote Shri Shailesh G. Tembhurnikar,
Indian Forest Service (1987) to the Apex Scale (Level 17 in Pay Matrix) and appoint him as
the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State
consequent to the retirement of the present incumbent Dr. Y. L. P. Rao, I.F.S. on 31st August,
2023 (A.N.).
Shri Shailesh G. Tembhurnikar, IFS shall accordingly assume the charge on 31st
August, 2023 (A.N.).
This Government Order is available at the Government of Maharashtra's website
