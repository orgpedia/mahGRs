Preamble: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 22/02/2022 of the Finance Department, 15 temporary posts outside the sanctioned pattern for cleaning work in the Administrative Building of Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan were extended for the period from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022 under the Government Decision dated 29/03/2022 in the context of Revenue and Forest Department.
Under the Government Resolution dated 18/08/2022 in the context of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to extend 15 temporary posts outside the sanctioned figure for cleaning work in the Konkan Bhavan Administrative Building for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: EST-2022/ PR No. 57/ E-8
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, has issued orders for the cleaning of the Konkan Bhavan Administrative Building. 02 The 15 temporary posts in the statement attached with the Government decision herein are being extended for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 subject to the conditions contained in the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 in the context of Department of Finance.
2. Expenditure on such temporary posts subject to reference No. 02 The amount of the relevant statement accompanying the Government decision hereunder shall be apportioned from the budgetary grants approved for the year 2022-2023 under the head of accounts shown in No. 06.
The Finance Department has directed all the administrative departments to take note that the revised figure in the Government Decision dated 18/08/2022 in the context of the Finance Department needs to be approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries in the meantime and no further extension of time will be granted to finalize the revised figure.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Finance Department, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18/08/2022.
