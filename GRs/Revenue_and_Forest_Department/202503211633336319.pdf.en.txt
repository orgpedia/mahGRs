Government Decision: The temporary posts created for the office of Collector and Project Director, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar and the field office for rehabilitation and other ancillary works of Sardar Sarovar Project were extended till 28th February, 2025. The District Collector and Project Director, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar has requested for further extension of these 33 temporary posts as the rehabilitation work of the project victims is still going on. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these posts.
The 33 posts mentioned in the accompanying annexure created for the District Collectorate (SPR) and the Regional Office at Nandurbar for the rehabilitation of Sardar Sarovar Project victims are being extended for the period from 01.03.2025 to 31.08.2025.
3. These 33 posts shall be deemed to be temporary additions to the permanent cadre of the department concerned. Dearness Allowance and other allowances will continue to be admissible to these office bearers as per rules.
4. Expenditure for all temporary posts above B shall be first met from the grant sanctioned under Demand No. "Heading C-5, 2235 - Social Security and Welfare, Loo Assistance Other Measures - (03) (07) Revenue & Forest Department Upper Collector, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar (2235-3132) (Non-Plan Dattamat)" in the additional year 2024-2025. Thereafter, the expenditure should be adjusted in consultation with the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar by adjusting the books under the head "Demand No. C - 119-4801 - Capital Outlay on Power Project 01 - Hydropower Generation - Sardar Saatho Project - Plans" of the Water Resources Department at the end of the year 2024-2025.
5. The said Government Decision Government Decision No. The posts are being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments vide Notification No. 2016 / Q 08/2016, APC, dated 03/03/2025.
