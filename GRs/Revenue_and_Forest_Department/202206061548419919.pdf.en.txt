Introduction: Non-agriculture tax is a basic tax on land and is an important source of revenue for the state.
As per the provisions of section 45, 42 and section 109 to 114 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Act, 1966, provision has been made for levy of non-agriculture charges in respect of land used for non-agriculture. The Maharashtra Land Revenue (Adaptation of Land Use and Non-Agriculture Assessment) Rules, 1969, provide for this.
In accordance with the above provisions, the procedure for determining the rates for non-agricultural use in urban as well as rural areas, determining the period of guarantee for such rates, fixing the rates while fixing the non-agricultural rates have been mentioned in the aforesaid Articles and Rules.
As per the aforesaid Act and Rules, for issuing directions regarding lifting of the moratorium on non-agriculture recovery and recovery of non-agriculture on the basis of revised non-agriculture rate, vide Notification No. The government issued a circular dated February 5, 2018. According to the circular, instructions have been given to the field officers regarding how to charge the amount of non-agricultural inputs by fixing the non-agricultural rate during the guarantee period from 2001 to 2006 to 2011/ 2011 to 2016/ 2016 to 2021.
Accordingly, the non-agriculture tax was being collected by issuing notices to the respective income tax holders at the field level. However, the collection of this non-agricultural tax burden is a huge burden on the common man, as per the attention-grabbing discussion in the Budget Session 2022 of the Vidhan Sabha.
Accordingly, vide No. 2 vide letter dated 07/04/2022, all the District Collectors have been informed that the Government Circular dated 5th February, 2018 specifying

Government Resolution No: NAP-2022/ P.S. No.50/ J-1A
As per the directions, the levy of non-agriculture tax at the field level is hereby temporarily suspended till further orders. Also, the enforcement action should be stopped.
In this background, the matter of appointing a study committee to review the rates and rate-wise levy of non-agriculture tax and make suitable recommendations to the government was under the consideration of the government.
-------------------------
| Government's decision |
|                       |
-------------------------
Based on the revised non-agriculture rate in the state, the following study committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue) to conduct an exhaustive study and make appropriate recommendations to the government to remove the moratorium on the recovery of non-agriculture levy and to streamline the ancillary implementation to be done in this regard.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(a) To reconsider the guidelines on non-agriculture tax mentioned in the Government Circular, dated 5th February, 2018.
(b) To consider re-fixing of revised non-agriculture rates.
c) To make recommendations on ancillary matters.
