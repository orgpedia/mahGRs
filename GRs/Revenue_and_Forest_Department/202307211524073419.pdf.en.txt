Introduction: The state government receives revenue from various sources every year. For this, the target of revenue collection is set by the Finance Department to all the concerned administrative departments. Accordingly, the revenue collected from the field offices under the control of the Revenue Department every year is credited under the head' 0029-Land Revenue" and' 0853-Deposits from Minor Mineral Exploration Rules." Considering the revenue collection target given by the Finance Department, it was under the consideration of the Government to fix the combined revenue collection target for all the Divisional Commissioners and Jamabandi Commissioners and Director, Land Records, Pune under the said head of accounts for the financial year 2023-2024.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2023-2024, the total amount of revenue collected under the headings" 0029-Land Revenue" and" 0853-Recovery from Minor Mineral Exploration Rules" is Rs. The cumulative target of Rs. 7295.10 crore is being worked out at the State level. 2. District wise allocation of the above targets shall be made by the Divisional Commissioner taking into account the recovery potential of the district. Regular monthly review of revenue collection should be done as per the target fixed and the report of revenue collection for the completed month should be submitted to the government by the 5th of the following month.
The target of revenue collection fixed by the Government as above should be achieved before 31st March, 2024 by following the prescribed procedure. For this, instructions to strictly follow the following instructions should be given to all the controlling officers by all the Divisional Commissioners and Jamabandi Commissioners and Director, Land Records, Pune from their level.
(a) The records maintained in the department should match the records maintained in the Treasury and the Audit Office. For this, the deposits should be regularly matched with the treasury office and the certificate of matching the deposits with the treasury office should be submitted to the government through the divisional commissioner's office on the email desktrev. mu- mh@gov. in every month without fail.
(b) The amount of revenue to be credited to the Government should be as per the scheme notations titled" 0029- Land Revenue" and" 0853- Deposits from Minor Mineral Exploration Rules" for accurate figures of revenue collection. It should be ensured that the revenue figures to be assessed by the Government as per the scheme indicators other than the scheme indicators are not shown under the said head of accounts.
(c) The amount of revenue collected from the field office should be deposited with the Government through GRAS system only. Care should be taken that the amount is not deposited by the government in any other way, otherwise the concerned officers/ employees will be held responsible.
