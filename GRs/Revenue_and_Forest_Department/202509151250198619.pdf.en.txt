The Proposal: -
General Administration Department Reference No. As per the Government Decision at 02, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission conducts Combined Probationary Training through SAAC, Nagpur for the candidates belonging to Group-B cadre who are directly recruited by the State Service Examination every year. Based on the final result of the State Service Examination-2023, training is required to be imparted to 10 Probationary Deputy Superintendents, Land Records, Group-B candidates (CPTP-11) of Group-B cadre directly appointed by the State Service Examination-2023 recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Therefore, the issue of creation of additional posts of Deputy Superintendent, Land Records, to pay the salary of 10 probationary candidates of Group-B cadre was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the creation of a maximum number of posts for the post of Deputy Superintendent, Land Records, Group-B under the "Group-B" cadre, recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the State Service Examination-2023 and appointed for the programme under training by the General Administration Department, for a period of two years from the date 01.09.2025 to the date 01.09.2027.
