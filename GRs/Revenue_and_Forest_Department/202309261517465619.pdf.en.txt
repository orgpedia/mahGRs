The 69 temporary posts created on the establishment were approved to be continued in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 as per the Government decision of Reference No.7.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format have been provided to the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 8 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Subject to the Government decision of Reference No.8 of the Finance Department, Government is granting approval for continuation of 96 temporary posts in the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Director, Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-24.
The expenditure on establishment of 69 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forest and Director Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-24 under the head 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 800 (01) Other Expenditure, (01) (02) Forest Upavane (2406 0659).
