(10) After the said transfer order is issued, the transferred officer shall ensure that he joins the place of transfer within the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. All concerned should note that in case of non-appointment of a replacement during this period, the period of his absence shall be treated as" dies non."
(11) No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned without the prior approval of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address. If the leave applications of the transferred officers are sent to the parent office, these applications will not be taken into consideration.
All concerned should note that if they do not join the post immediately as per the said transfer orders or try to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them considering it as a misconduct as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
