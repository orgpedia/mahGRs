Read:- Project Implementation Committee Meeting dated 23/9/2021
Preamble: The" e-Pick Probe" programme is being implemented across the State from 15 th August, 2021 to provide information to the farmers about the sowing of crops through a mobile app. A Project Implementation Cell has been set up under the control of Commissioner of Jamabandi and Director, Land Records, Pune for implementation of the project. The Commissioner of Jamabandi and Director, Land Records, Pune have been entrusted with the responsibility of making this programme a success across the state. Accordingly, the Commissioner of Jamabandi and Director, Land Records, Pune are taking action to implement the said programme. The expenditure for the promotion, publicity and ancillary expenses of the scheme is Rs. It was under consideration of the Government to provide powers to the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Pune to make changes in the matter of expenditure subject to a limit of Rs. 5 crore. Accordingly, the following resolution was adopted:
Government Decision: Out of the amount demanded by the Project Implementation Committee for publicity and publicity and other incidental expenses under the e-Crop Inspection Project, Rs. 5 crore has been approved to be spent by the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Pune from the Personal Property Account (PLA) account. The Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Pune are being empowered to make changes in the matter of expenditure subject to a ceiling of Rs.5 crore.
