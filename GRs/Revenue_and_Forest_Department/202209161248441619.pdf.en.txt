Cancellation of appointment of Chairperson and non-officio members of Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Revenue and Forestry,
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. WLP- 07.22/ W.No.198/ F-1
Ministry, Mumbai 400032.
The date is September 16, 2022.
Read:
Department of Revenue and Forest, Government Resolution No:- WLP- 08.15/ W.S. No.36/ F-1, dated 22.03.2021.
Government's decision:
The appointments of Chairperson and non-official members nominated by the Government vide reference No. 1 to the Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board stand cancelled with effect from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
(2) The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board, Nagpur shall take action to bring the above Government decision to the notice of the nominated Chairman and non-official members of the Biodiversity Board.
