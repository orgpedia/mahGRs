Section 50 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966
The Proposal:
For the removal of encroachments on the government land in the government colony, Bandra (East), in Mumbai Suburban District, eviction of unauthorized persons and freeing the plot in a planned manner, etc. and for the development project of the Bandra Government Colony, the property officer is a post of the rank of Deputy Collector. EST 2019/ Q. No.55/ Administration-1, dated 12th March, 2020. As the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 is related to the Revenue and Forest Department, the authority of the competent authority in the post of Deputy Collector and Estate Officer to remove the encroachments and remove the encroachments in Bandra Government Colony, Bandra (East) has been given by the Revenue and Forest Department on 09/02/2023. The directions were given at a war room meeting held with the Chief Minister.
Accordingly, the Public Works Department has requested the Revenue and Forest Department to take necessary action, following which the government has taken the following decision.
Government's decision:
For removal of encroachments and removal of encroachments in the Government Estate at Bandra (East) in Mumbai Suburban District, the Public Works Department has created the post of" Deputy Collector and Estate Officer" vide Government Resolution dated 12/03/2020.
The said officers are being delegated powers under Section 50 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966.
