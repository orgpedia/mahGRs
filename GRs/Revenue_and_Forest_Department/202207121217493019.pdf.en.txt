Government decision: The High Court of Bombay, vide Public Interest Litigation No. 87/2006 dated 17/09/2018, a Review Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue) has been constituted under Government Resolution dated 15/12/2018 to review the functioning of the Control Committee under the Chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Konkan, constituted for the protection and conservation of the mangrove forests in the coastal districts of Mumbai, Mumbai Suburban, Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg in the State.
The committee will review the action taken/ work done by the Controlling Committee headed by Divisional Commissioner Konkan from now on in quarterly to half yearly period.
The other provisions of the Government Decision dated 15/12/2018 shall remain in force.
