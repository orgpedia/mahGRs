Introduction: Section 51 of the Right to Fair Compensation and Tenancy in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 provides for the establishment of a Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority by the Central Government. Accordingly, the authority has been formed for the revenue department of Amravati and Nagpur in Nagpur.
A total of 13 posts are vacant.
