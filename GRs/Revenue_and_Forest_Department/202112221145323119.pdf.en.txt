Introduction: All Talathis and Mandal Officers in the state need a laptop, printer and digital signature (DSC) to operate the e-manipulation system online. Earlier, as directed by the government, talathis and mandal officers of many districts were directed to distribute laptops and printers by making funds available from the special innovative scheme of the District Planning Committee (DPC).
In some districts, where laptops have become defunct and outdated, it is necessary to provide new laptops to the Talathi and Mandal officers for the smooth functioning of the e-modification system online. However, in these districts, funds cannot be spent from the District Planning Committee for the same purpose.
In this regard, the Talathi and Mandal Officers Association always insists on providing laptops and printers to all Talathi and Mandal Officers by the government and for this, funds are not available from the government level. 1 was approved.
Accordingly, the office of the Jamabandi Commissioner and Director of Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune, approved the expenditure of funds from the self-provisioning account to the District Collector at the district level for the purchase of laptops for all Talathi/ Mandal officers in the state. However, the specifications of different laptops are given from different places while buying this laptop. Order No. 2018/ P.S. 209/ L-1 passed by the Govt. The specifications have been fixed under Annexure-B dated 31/3/2021. However, laptops of these specifications are not available on the GeM Portal as per the instructions given by the IT Cell of the Central and State Governments and Make in India from time to time. Alternatively, it is being changed/ repaired and laptops are being purchased and problems like price rise have been reported from many districts. After considering all the above factors and considering the following policy regarding the generic specifications of laptops, all-in-one printers and scanners for Talathis and Mandal Officers to be purchased from GEM Portal free of cost.
