The Proposal:
The Government has accorded sanction for continuation of 12 posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" during the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 under Government Resolution No.
2. Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, has submitted a proposal for Government approval regarding continuation of 12 temporary posts made available by way of gradation/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" in the year 2021, 2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 03.21/ S.No.58/ F-1
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 7 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Government of Finance Department vide Reference No.7, Government has accorded approval for continuation of 12 temporary posts made available in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 by way of category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority."
The expenditure incurred on establishment of 12 temporary posts made available by way of category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" under the head 2406, Afforestation and Wildlife, 02, Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (19) Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Faculty (2406 A087) should be met from the provision made available in the financial year 2021-2022.
