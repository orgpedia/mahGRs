Introduction: In the land record system, the major work of land enumeration is handled by the staff of the cadre of land surveyors. Since the nature of the work is technical, Mr. Pawan Kumar Rewaram Kewate and other 98 land surveyors have demanded the sanction of technical pay scale to the cadre of land surveyors. Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai Bench at Nagpur in the original application No. 436/2017 was filed. On this, Ma. The court gave. The following directions have been issued to the Government on 14.01.2020:
" In the view of this matter and the caution given by the Hon'ble Apex Court, we are of the firm view that it is necessary in the interest of justice to direct the Government to constitute a Committee to examine the matter keeping in view the pay scales admissible to the post of Surveyor in Land Record and Survey Department, Forest Department and PCMC, Pune in comparison with their qualifications and exact nature of the duties and take decision within reasonable time. In the result we pass the following order
The matter of forming the necessary committee in accordance with the above order of the Hon'ble Tribunal was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision: The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai Bench, Nagpur has passed an order in respect of the demand for sanction of Technical Pay Scale to the cadre of Land Surveyors. In pursuance of the directions dated 14.01.2020, the Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner of Jamabandi and Director, Land Records, Pune as follows:

The committee will have the following functions:
1. m. Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai Bench, Nagpur Considering the main content of the order dated 14/01/2020, the Committee should objectively examine the demand of the Land Surveyors and make a clear recommendation to the Government whether or not the Technical Pay Scale should be sanctioned for the cadre of" Land Surveyors" with proper documentation and rationale.
2.To examine the demand objectively, taking into account the shortcomings of the previous efforts to approve the technical pay scale for the cadre of Land Surveyors.
3. Examine the feasibility of granting technical status and pay scale to the cadre of land surveyors in the Land Records Department, Forest Department and Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation by considering the qualification and exact duties of the post of surveyor, pay scale, opportunity for promotion, additional burden on the government and modern technology used in the future.
(4) The Chairman shall have the power to appoint such expert as may be necessary to assist the Committee.
