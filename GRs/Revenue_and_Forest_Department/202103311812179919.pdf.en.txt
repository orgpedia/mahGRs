Preamble: Under the Digital India Land Records Modernization Programme in the State, the Office of the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune has developed a list of projects like Computerization No. 7/12, 8A, E-Modification, E-Record, etc. The e-modification order in this ignorance is implemented by the Talati and Mandal officers to connect the laptops of the respective Talati and Mandal officers of the state to the cloud server. Reference No. 4 is here.
As per the Government decision dated 02/09/2020, for the purchase of 1081 laptops for five districts of Nagpur, Bhandara, Akola, Ahmednagar and Kolhapur, an amount of Rs. 4,86,45, 000/- has been sanctioned. A total amount of Rs 45000/- each has been sanctioned for the purchase of a total of 1367 laptops for 1154 Talathis and 213 Mandal Officers in 12 districts of the state. A proposal to approve the transfer of expenditure amounting to Rs. 6,15,15, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores Fifteen Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only) from the balance in the self-employment account to the concerned Collector is being considered in Reference No. 5 submitted by the Commissioner and Director, Land Records, State of Maharashtra, Pune. The government has issued orders towards this.
Orders: As per Appendix A attached to link the laptops of the respective Talathis and Mandal Officers of the state with the cloud server for the implementation of the e-modification order under e-Mahabhoomi, 1154 Talathis and 213 Mandal Officers of 12 districts have been given Rs. 45000/- to a total of Rs. 6,15,15, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores Fifteen Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only) from the balance in the personal provident accounts of the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, State of Maharashtra, Pune.

The Commissioner of Settlement and the Director of Land Records, State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be bound to comply with the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Resolution No. 2 and 3 and to keep an account of the deposits and expenditure relating to their provident accounts in accordance with the said expenditure items.
It shall be the responsibility of the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune to send the financial and physical report regarding the said fund to the Government along with the Utilization Certificate. The government will also have the right to examine the deposit and expenditure of this fund. The Collector should maintain separate accounts for the utilisation of the fund and the expenditure should be reconciled with the Accountant General from time to time.
Be careful about giving. The Commissioner of Deposits, Pune, should send the financial and physical report to the government along with the utilization certificate.
