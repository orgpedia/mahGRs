The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government under Reference No. 3& 5," Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in the post-plan scheme for the continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in Nagzira Sanctuary in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
2. Accordingly, the Government have accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary in the post Plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 have been conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No.

Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 6, the Government have accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the post-plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in the year 2022-2023 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 1 temporary post created on the establishment of the Conservator of Forest and Director, Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023 under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)."
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
