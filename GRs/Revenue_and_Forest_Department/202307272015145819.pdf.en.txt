Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Government order:
Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri Vikas Patil, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Deputy Collector, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai on deputation for a period of 3 years with the approval of the competent authority in the interest of public service and administrative convenience.
2. The services of Mr. Vikas Patil, Deputy Collector are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai, in accordance with the deputation to the post of Deputy Collector, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai.
3. Mr. Vikas Patil, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
