Preamble:-
The Senior Geologist, (Project-in-Charge), Economic Geology, Geological Survey
of India, CR Nagpur has submitted a proposal for to carry out drilling for geological investigation for critical mineral commodities like copper and associated minerals of 01 Nos. test (scout) boreholes near north west of Azimabad, Bhandara District to the State
Forest Department. APCCF & Nodal Officer has submitted this proposal to Government of
Maharashtra vide letter under referred above.
Order:-
In exercise of powers under general approval granted by MoOEF&CC, Govt. of India vide Handbook of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Rules, 2023, Guidelines-2023
(Chapter-6, Para. 6.1), Government of Maharashtra is pleased to permission to carry out drilling of 01 Nos. test (Scout) boreholes near north west Azimabad Bhandara.

3. This permission is subject to the following conditions-
1) Deputy Conservation of Forest shall ensure that the area involved in the project does not form part of any Protected area such as National Park, Wildlife Sanctuary, Nature Reserve, Tiger Reserve, Tiger Corridor, prior to the commencement of work.
2) The legal status of the forest land will remain unchanged.
3) Construction of temporary dwelling/hutments etc., for labourers will not be allowed in the forest areas.
4) Adequate precautions shall be taken to safeguard and adjoining area from explosives and it shall not be stored in the forest area.
5) The User Agency will not dump the overburden on the forest land.

6) The User Agency should clearly understand that this permission is being granted only for bore holes over 0.03 ha. for geological investigation.
7) The User Agency shall be allowed exploratory drilling of a maximum of | bore
holes of 4” diameter over 0.03 ha. forest land.
8) The said permission shall remain admissible up to the prescribed period specified
under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Rules, 2023.
9) Adequate precautions shall be taken to safeguard the adjoining areas from
explosives and it shall not be stored in the forest area.
10) The UA should clearly understand that this permission is being granted only for
carry of drilling of 1 borehole over 0.03 ha.
11) The permission for drilling in the area do not confer any right to the user agency to
claim forest land diversion in future for ancillary activities related to the project
under section 241) of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. All
such cases will be dealt on merits.
12) User agency shall seek permission of DCF/DFO of concern Forest Division before
entering into the forest area.
13) User agency shall not sublease the drilling work.
14) A bore-hole shall be dug with prior permission of the DCF/DFO.
15) Proper plugging of boreholes shall be made after drilling activities are complete.
16) User agency shall report the progress of drilling work to the DCF/DFO, and also
furnish detailed report thereof on completion of the project.
17) No tree felling shall be undertaken for drilling activities. Project activities shall be
restricted to clearing of bushes and lopping of tree branches it any for the purpose of
site preparation.
18) Adequate measures shall be taken by the user agency to ensure that drilling
activities do not harm the wildlife in the area. Any damage done in this context,
shall be compensated by the user agency as per assessment by the local DCF/DFO.
19) The user agency shall be responsible for any loss to the flora and fauna in the
surrounding. Any damage done in this regard shall be compensated by the user
agency from the project cost as per assessment of the local DCF/DFO.
20) DCF, Bhandara may impose any other condition for protection and conservation of
the flora and fauna in the forest area.
21) In case, rights over forest land proposed to be used for drilling purpose, has already
been settled in favour of eligible claimants as per provisions of the Forest Rights
Act, 2006, the claimants shall either be compensated appropriately or location of
borehole be suitably re-located.
22) No fire will be kindled inside and in the vicinity of the forest area.
23) User Agency shall provide a list of authorized officials/ workers engaged in drilling
to the forest department before commencing the work and also provide the Identity
Cards/ Documents to each such official/ worker, to be produced when demanded by
the forest department.
24) The samples collected during the drilling shall be used purely for investigation
purposes and shall in no case be used for trade or commerce purpose;
25) DCF, Bhandara shall deploy adequate staff to supervise the activities to be
undertaken by the user agency to prevent any damage to the flora (especially the
