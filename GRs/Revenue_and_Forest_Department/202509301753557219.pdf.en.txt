GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASTRA
Revenue and Forest Department
Government Order No: AFO-2025/C.R.101/F-7
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date : 30" September, 2025
ORDER
Government of Maharashtra 15 pleased to promote Shri. M. Srinivasa Rao, Indian
Forest Service (1992), Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) to the Apex Scale
(Level 17 in IFS Pay Matrix) and appoint him to the post of Principal Chief Conservator of
Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State consequent to the retirement of Smt.
Shomita Biswas, IFS on 30" September, 2025 (AN).
Shri. M. Srinivasa Rao, IFS shall accordingly assume the charge on 30" September,
2025 (AN).
