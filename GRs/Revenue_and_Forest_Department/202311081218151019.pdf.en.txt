(4) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be treated as non-working day.
5.The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the said officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the original address of the concerned officer by post.
(6) As the aforesaid orders are being issued with the approval of the competent authority, the Divisional Commissioner shall give posting to such officers in other vacant posts if the Tahsildar or other officers under the purview of the place of posting have been given posting by temporary arrangement from the level of Divisional Commissioner.
7. It may be noted by all concerned that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said order or any attempt to cancel the post by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them considering the act as a violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
8. The said Government Order has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra and its Computer Code No. It is 202311081218151019. This government order is being issued by digitally signing.
