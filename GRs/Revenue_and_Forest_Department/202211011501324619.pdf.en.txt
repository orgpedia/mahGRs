Preamble- The payment of salary and allowances of the employees of the Forest Department of the Government has been passed from the treasury like the employees of other departments since February 2006. The decision has been taken as per the Government decision dated 04.01.2006.
Accordingly, as per Rule 13 and Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the list of Drawing and Disbursing Officers in the department is sent to the Accountant General's Office. Accordingly, the lists of Drawing and Disbursing Officers of the Department have been published as per the Government decisions issued from time to time.
Accordingly, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur, vide his Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to declare the post of Deputy Conservator of Forest (Wildlife) Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer by retaining the earlier Drawing and Disbursing Officer code number instead of Conservator of Forest (Wildlife), Pune. Accordingly, the matter of declaring the said post as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" was under consideration of the Government.
The decision was taken by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur, in his Reference No. 1 and the Finance Department. In accordance with the Government Order dated 04.01.2006, the post of Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for discharging the salary and allowances of field staff under his charge, keeping the old Drawing and Disbursing Numerical Number (2201-001108) intact:
