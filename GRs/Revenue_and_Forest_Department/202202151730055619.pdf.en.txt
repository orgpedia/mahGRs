Preamble: Creation of Online Charge by Banks with the representatives of State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC). The meeting was held on 12.10.2021 under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue). As per the decision taken in the meeting, the Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune has submitted a proposal for the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune to develop a facility for registering online loads.
Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune, to develop a facility to register the online load.
The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following Committee of the Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune is being constituted to develop the facility for registering online loads.
