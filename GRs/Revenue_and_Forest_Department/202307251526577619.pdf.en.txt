Now, for payment of the civil works completed and in progress, vide reference No. The proposal for the demand of funds has been submitted to the government by the Assistant Superintending Engineer and Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for the civic amenities works completed and in progress under the proposal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision The Government is approving the disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 1,24,80, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Four Lakh Eighty Thousand only) for the completed and in progress civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Pandharpur, Mohol and Madha taluks of Solapur district under the Ujani Project in the current financial year 2023-2024 subject to the following conditions.

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works to the concerned Executive Engineer without any delay on receipt from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Ujani Canal Division No. As soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received by Solapur, the pending dues of civic amenities works should be paid immediately, and accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and Collector, Solapur.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
No funds shall be left under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order, namely, Demand No.C-10, Head Account Head 4701, 01, Major Irrigation- Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53, Major Construction (4701-H-727) (Dattamat) or the funds received under the said order shall be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned agency.
