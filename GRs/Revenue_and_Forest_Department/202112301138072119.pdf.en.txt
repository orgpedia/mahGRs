Preamble: In pursuance of the above notification, the Government has declared an area of 56.92 sq.km.in the Amboli-Dodamarg Forest Range of Sawantwadi Forest Division in Sindhudurg district of Kolhapur Forest Range as Amboli-Dodamarg Conservation Reserve. The Government was contemplating to constitute a Management Committee for the management of the Amboli-Doda Marg Conservation Reserve to advise the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) and Chief Wildlife Warden, Maharashtra State, Nagpur.
The Government have decided to constitute a Management Committee for the management of the Amboli-Dodamarg Conservation Reserve as per the provisions of Section 36-B (1) and (2) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972.

The functions of the Management Committee shall be as follows:
The jurisdiction of the Committee shall be limited to the Amboli-Doda Marg Conservation Reserve.
The Committee shall forward proposals and suggestions to the Chief Wildlife Warden for protection and management of the Conservation Reserve and shall provide necessary support.
The Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the provisions of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and other forest laws and Acts in the conservation reserve.
The tenure of the Committee shall be for a period of three years from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
