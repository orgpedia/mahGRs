The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 7 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern.
