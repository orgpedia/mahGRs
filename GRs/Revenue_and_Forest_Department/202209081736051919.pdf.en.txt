Introduction: In order to help the farmers in the next season in case of loss due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods and cyclones, the farmers are given assistance in the form of input subsidy at a prescribed rate from the State Disaster Response Fund. Also, assistance is provided for other approved items of the State Disaster Response Fund at prescribed rates. The Chief Minister said that assistance in the form of financial assistance to the affected farmers, who suffered crop loss in various districts due to heavy rains and floods in July, 2022, and assistance for other losses would be provided. Pursuant to the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting dated 10.08.2022, the Government have decided to transfer the Government Resolution Revenue& Forest Department No.CLS-2022/ PR No. 253/ M-3, D. The Government has approved to provide assistance to the affected farmers in the form of fixed subsidy at enhanced rates for the loss of agricultural crops in various districts of the State due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods during the period from June to October, 2022 as under:-
