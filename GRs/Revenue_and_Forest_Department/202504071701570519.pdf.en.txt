The Proposal: -
Crores of cases related to land disputes in Maharashtra and in the country are pending in various courts for years. Among them are the disputes of ownership, disputes over farm plots, disputes over possession of land, road cancellations, cancellations on farm land counts, cancellations due to wrong records of rights, cancellations due to encroachment on agriculture, agrarian sisterhood disputes, distribution disputes between brothers and sisters, errors in government schemes or disputes over non-application of proposals, etc. Farm land disputes are complex and complex in nature and have been going on for years due to the lack of adequate mechanisms in the courts and administration. Since farmland is a sensitive subject for everyone, disputes over it have led to feelings of dissatisfaction and estrangement in family relationships. These disputes have cost generations, and the present generation is also wasting its time and money, and there seems to be no significant progress in ending such disputes.
In order to resolve the disputes between the farmers regarding the ownership of the agricultural land and to promote harmony and mutual understanding in the society, the Government has decided to levy a stamp duty of nominal Rs. 1000 / - and registration fee of Rs.
