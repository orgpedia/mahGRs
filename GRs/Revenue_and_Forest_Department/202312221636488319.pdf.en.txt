The Government revenue of 3865 sq.m. is being transferred to Mumbai Metro-3 Project for development of the property to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority at a nominal rate as may be decided by the Government from time to time on lease basis for 30 years subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1. The use of the said Government Income shall not be allowed for any purpose other than the proposed purpose.

The planned utilization of the sanctioned land by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) will be required to commence within the next three years.
The said land shall not be mortgaged or transferred or sold in any manner without the prior permission of the Government/ Collector.
The land was allotted to the Urban Development Department No. MRD-3311/ PR No. 149/ NV-7, Dy. As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 3rd March, 2014, the transfer to Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited through the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall be approved.
It shall be the responsibility of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) to ensure that the use of the said government land for the proposed purpose is permissible from the planning point of view before construction is carried out by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation or the concerned competent authority.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) will be responsible for obtaining prior approval of the MCZMA before starting the actual use of the land.
