2. The said order comes into immediate effect and the aforesaid officer issued the order in column no. Immediately join the position of the post mentioned in section 4. The concerned officer should immediately inform the Government by email / post at the date of his / her appearance at the place of posting given to him / her.
The officer concerned shall take care of joining the place of posting within the prescribed period as per the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, Non-Service and Provisions during periods of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981. Those concerned should note that if they do not otherwise join the position of the incumbent, their period of absence will count as a "non-working day" (/ 7129 non).
4. Leave shall not be granted to the officer concerned for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers transferred by the original office shall be entertained. such an application.
If received, they must be returned to the relevant authorities by post, registered at the original address.
5. The concerned shall note that in case of non-acceptance of appointment immediately under the said order or attempt to cancel the transfer under any kind of pressure, disciplinary action shall be taken against them on the ground that the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, treating it as misconduct.
