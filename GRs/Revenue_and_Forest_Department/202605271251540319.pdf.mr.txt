Order
WHEREAS under Article 89(2) of the Articles of Association of the Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited, all the Directors (including Chairman, and Managing Director) of the said Company shall retire from office at its every Annual General Meeting.
AND WHEREAS the 52™ Annual General Meeting in respect of the financial year ended 31.3.2025 is due to be held in due course and all the existing Directors of the said Company, appointed vide the Government order under reference are to retire at the aforesaid meeting.
NOW THEREFORE the Governor of Maharashtra, in exercise of the powers conferred on him under Article 89(1), 89(4) and Article 104 of the Articles of Association of the company, 1s pleased to make the following appointments on the Board of Director of the Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited, which shall take effect immediately after the existing Directors in the Board of Directors retire from their office at the 527" Annual General Meeting of the Company under Article 89(2) of the Articles of Association:-
