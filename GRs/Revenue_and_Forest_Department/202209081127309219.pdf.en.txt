(2) As funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.

(4) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
5) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
(6) The Executive Engineer of the concerned system shall ensure that no funds under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order" Demand No.C-10, Head Account Heading 4701, 01, Major Irrigation- Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53, Major Constructions (4701-H-727)" (Dattamat) shall remain unutilized or the funds received under the said order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Personal Principal Account) of the concerned system.
