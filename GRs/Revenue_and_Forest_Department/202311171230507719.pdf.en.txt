For the public purpose/ facility of construction of" Central Building 2," as per the provisions of Section 40 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 and Rules 5, 6, 8 and 50 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue (Disposal of Government Land) Rules, 1971, the Government is pleased to grant the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune, the right of possession in a non-occupancy manner and at a revenue free price, subject to the following terms and conditions and on such terms and conditions as the Collector, Pune may deem necessary.

Terms and Conditions: 1) The Subject Area Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune shall hold the office as a Class II tenant.
(2) The land or any part thereof or any interest therein shall not be transferred by way of sale, gift, exchange, mortgage, lease, private-public partnership or any other mode of transfer except with the prior permission of the Revenue and Forest Department of the Government.
(3) No pecuniary transaction shall be entered into in such a manner as to create any right of any other person/ institution/ company etc. on the said land or any part thereof and the said land shall not be subdivided without prior approval of the Revenue and Forest Department of the Government.
The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune shall use the said Government land only for the approved purpose (construction of Central Building 2). If the land or any part of it is to be used temporarily or permanently for any other purpose, prior approval of the Revenue and Forest Department of the Government will be required.
It will be mandatory to start using the land for the approved purpose within 3 years of receiving possession of the land.
(6) Development on the subject land shall be subject to the approval of the planning authority concerned and in accordance with the approved development control rules. Apart from this, approval/ prior permission/ no-objection from other concerned departments/ government agencies will also be mandatory.
7) It will be mandatory to get the official count and demarcation of the land done by the concerned Deputy Superintendent of Land Records of the Department of Acquisition and then give physical possession of the land.
8) It should be ensured that the planned purpose at the said place does not cause any inconvenience to the account holders concerned and their legitimate transactions are not affected.
9) Other regular terms and conditions regarding the government land approval will continue to apply.
10) The Collector, Pune shall have the power to prescribe such other terms and conditions as may be deemed necessary in accordance with the situation.
11) In case of violation of any of the above conditions, the Government shall have the right to deposit the said land to the Government in case of violation of the conditions.
