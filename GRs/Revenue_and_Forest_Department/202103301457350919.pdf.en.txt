Preamble: An Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution No. 4 under reference to scrutinize the proposals received from the Divisional Commissioners for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund for taking various measures to contain the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State. The Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee has scrutinized the proposals received from Nagpur Division for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund and sent their recommendations to the Government under letter No. 5. The meeting of the State Executive Committee was held on 16.02.2021 to decide on the recommendations received from the Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee. Accordingly, as per the approval given by the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 16.02.2021, the matter of disbursement of funds to the Nagpur Division for taking various measures in the context of COVID-19 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Based on the recommendations received from the Inter-Departmental Scrutiny Committee for taking various measures in Nagpur and Chandrapur districts to contain the spread of COVID-19 virus, the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 16.02.2021 has decided to release an amount of Rs. 75,44,69, 000/- (Rupees Seventy Five Crores Forty Four Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand only) from the State Disaster Response Fund to the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur.
