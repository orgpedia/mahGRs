The Proposal:
In the Post Plan Scheme" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park," the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per Government decision in Reference No.
2. Now, as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for Government approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the post-plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in Nagzira Sanctuary for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 6 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 6, the Government has approved the continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in the post-plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 1 temporary post created on the establishment of the Conservator of Forest and Director, Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024 under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)."
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08.02.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
