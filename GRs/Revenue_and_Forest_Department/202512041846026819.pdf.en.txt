The Proposal:
In order to increase the accountability of the government to the citizens and increase their trust by bringing more transparency in the functioning of the revenue department, it is essential to conduct an in-depth examination of complaints of serious nature received against the officers and employees of the department.
A proper inquiry into the complaints will help in increasing the credibility of the administration. So that's
A vigilance team needs to be set up at the divisional commissioner level for immediate and impartial redressal of complaints.
Preliminary enquiry into complaints of serious nature received in respect of minor mineral / land / stamp / land count and allied activities through vigilance team, physical surveillance as required, etc.
The process of verification of documents and submission of reports will be more dynamic and effective.
This will help curb irregularities by increasing discipline in the revenue department. Accordingly, the government was considering setting up vigilance teams at the divisional commissioner level to inquire into complaints received from the government regarding officers / staff in the revenue department. Accordingly, the government has now decided to:
The government's decision:
In respect of officers / staff of Revenue Departments in respect of complaints received in respect of Revenue Operations (Subordinate Minerals / Land / Stamps / Land Counting and allied operations).
The government has decided to set up vigilance teams at the divisional commissioner level to look into specific complaints that will be directed by the state government. The composition of the vigilance team will be as follows. The vigilance team is headed by the Additional Commissioner (Revenue), Divisional Commissioner's Office.
