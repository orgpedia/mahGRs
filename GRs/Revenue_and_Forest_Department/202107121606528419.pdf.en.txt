Since the order has been issued to take action by 31/12/2019, it is directed to take action to transfer the officers/ employees to this non-statutory post by creating more number of posts of such officers/ employees. Accordingly, the matter of creating more number of posts on the establishment of the Collector, Jalgaon in the Revenue Divisional Cadre was under the consideration of the Government. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: If the certificate of Scheduled Tribe is invalid, if the caste validity certificate of Special Backward Class or any other backward class is submitted after being invalid, if the claim of Scheduled Tribe is abandoned, if the proposal is not submitted to the caste verification committee, against the decision of the caste verification committee. However, a petition was filed in the court. The maximum number of posts of 03 employees of Group' C' cadre of revenue cadre who have not been stayed by the court and whose service has been terminated due to invalidation of caste validity certificate are being created at the establishment of Collector, Jalgaon. Details of these posts are mentioned in the notification.
(2) The number of employees to be transferred to the post of superintendents, including the number of officers in service, created for the post in which they are presently working, shall continue to be in existence for a period of 11 months, or till the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier, if they had continued in service.
