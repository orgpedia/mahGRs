Preamble: The Government of India has received instructions to spend the funds received under the Centrally Sponsored Schemes during 2021-22 through PFMS system. Accordingly, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) has been declared for the concerned Centrally Sponsored Scheme. Also, for these SNAs, accounts have been opened in the bank as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the concerned project agencies have been registered on the PFMS system.
The letter dated Reference No. 1 has directed that the funds released by the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the year 2021-22 under the Central share shall be returned to the Central Government if the State Government has not deposited the same in the account of the concerned SNA at the end of 31.3.2022. However, vide Reference No. 2, the time limit for crediting the funds received under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme to the account of SNA has been extended till 20.7.2022. After that, the money will be returned to the central government.
Accordingly, vide Reference No. 3, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted the information regarding Central share and corresponding State share received from the Central Government and deposited with the State Government in the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Modern Forestry and Control Management (Integrated Forest Conservation Scheme).
Government Resolution: As per the proposal submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for Government approval under Reference No. 3, the following funds are being approved to be deposited in the account of Single Nodal Agency (SNA) under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Modern Forestry and Control Management (Integrated Forest Conservation Scheme).
