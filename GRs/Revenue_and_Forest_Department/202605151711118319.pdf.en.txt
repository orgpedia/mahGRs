Proposal: - Part-IV of the Sand / Sand Export Policy 2025 provides that the bidders shall be obliged to provide free of cost a maximum of 20 of the sand mined by them to the eligible bidders for the release of sand from the river / creek basin under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana as well as for the housing of the beneficiaries belonging to the Economically Backward Classes.
As per the above mentioned provision, at present, a maximum of 10 per cent of the excavated sand is being made available free of cost under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana as well as for housing of beneficiaries belonging to economically backward classes. However, in some districts, including Nagpur, with a large number of household beneficiaries, the provision of 10 of excavated sand as per the said provision in the Government Decision dated 08.04.2025 as VTA for all such beneficiaries will enable maximum availability of sand under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana as well as for housing of beneficiaries belonging to the Economically Backward Classes by including a provision for making 10 of the total sand available in the vast sand dunes available to the household beneficiaries.
The government was considering to include a provision for providing 10 per cent of the total sand available in the sand pit to the household beneficiaries instead of the maximum provision of 10 per cent of the excavated sand in the government decision dated 08.04.2025.
The government's decision:
In the light of the discussion in the Preamble, the No. Government Decision, Revenue & Forest Department, No: Gokhani - 10/0125 Q. No. 05 / B-1, D. The provision mentioned herein in Part-IV 'Exit by Auction' in serial number (e) 'Process of Auction' (xxi) is being repealed and is now being replaced with the following amended provisions:
Part-IV
Exit by auction
(a) The auction process
(xxi) The bidders shall be obliged to make available free of cost 90 of the total sand stock available in the sandy ghats by the eligible bidders for the release of sand from the river / backwaters to the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana as well as to the households of the beneficiaries belonging to the Economically Backward Classes.
No amount shall be admissible to the bidder by the Government in respect of the same.
