The Proposal:
To provide training in wildlife management and production forestry to foresters and senior officers working in Maharashtra. For the appointment of nominated members of the Governing Body of the" Forestry and Wildlife Training Institute, Chandrapur (Maharashtra State)" established under the Chandrapur Forest Academy on the lines of Kundal, District Sangli, Reference No. Approval has been given under Annexure-B of the Government Resolution No. 1. However, since it is difficult to establish quorum in the 21 member Governing Body, the reference No. As per letter No. 2 and No. 3, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the reconstitution of the Regulatory Board.
In pursuance of the said proposal, the matter of approval of the proposal for the reconstitution of the office of the Forestry and Wildlife Training Institute, Chandrapur Regulatory Board was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government have accorded approval to the proposal for restructuring of the Board of Governors of Forestry and Wildlife Training Institute, Chandrapur (Maharashtra State) under Chandrapur Forest Academy as under:
