1) The Executive Engineer concerned should start the tender process of the works only after verifying that the above mentioned rehabilitated villages have been rehabilitated due to the Bhatghar Dam and the works under the proposal have not been done earlier from other schemes.
(2) The concerned executing agency shall be responsible for recovery of minor mineral ownership charges for the activities covered in the proposal.
3) Department of Revenue and Forestry Government Decision No. RPA 2008/ Q.No. 550/2-1 D. The concerned Executive Engineer shall be responsible for ensuring that the physical standards prescribed for providing civic amenities in the rehabilitated/ extended village under the Government Circular No. RPA-011/ P. No. 18/ R-1, dated 28/3/2011 are met.
4) The concerned Executive Engineer shall ensure that the expenditure on the proposed civic amenities works does not exceed the approved budget/ prescribed limit.
5) The work-wise amount indicated in the budget for the proposed civic amenities works should be as per the technical and administrative approval. The concerned Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer shall ensure that the conditions of technical and administrative approval are complied with in accordance with the prescribed procedures.
The Project Officer concerned shall immediately transfer the civic amenities proposed under Section (18) of the Maharashtra Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons Act, 1999 to the concerned Zilla Parishad after completion of all aspects and obtaining a certificate from the Collector, Pune accordingly.
7) The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector/ Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad as well as the concerned agencies should ensure that the facilities provided under the rehabilitation scheme are not restored from other schemes.
8) The project management shall ensure that the quality of the proposed civic amenities works is maintained.
(9) To ensure compliance of policies, terms/ conditions etc. prescribed by the Government from time to time in respect of providing civic amenities to the rehabilitated villages affected by the project prior to 1976.
10) It should be ensured that the Executive Engineer, Public Works (South Zone), Pune, G.Pune is the appropriate office to execute the works under the said budget.
(a) The Executive Engineer, Public Works (South Division), Pune, G. Pune shall immediately commence the tender process for the proposed civic amenity works after fulfilling the conditions in the administrative sanction orders submitted.
(b) No prior approval or approval shall be required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for the tendering process of the proposed civic amenities works.
(c) As funds will be made available by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for the civic amenity works in the rehabilitated villages under the proposal, there is no need to obtain tender documents from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for issuing work order after the acceptance of tender.
(d) The Executive Engineer, Public Works (South Division), Pune, G. Pune shall start the civil works immediately after the completion of the tender process for the proposed civil works by issuing the work order for the works tendered.
