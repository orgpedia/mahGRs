Under the Koyna project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Gogve, Mauje Devli (Talemala), Mauje Kalamgaon (Boudhavasti), Mauje Kalamgaon (Hirvavasti) and Mauje Rameghar in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara district have been taken up under reference No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted as per the orders herein. The amount for payment of civic amenities works completed/ in progress out of the administrative sanction of Rs. 62.51 lakh has been proposed by the Executive Engineer, Dhom Canal Division No. 2, presented by Satara.
Accordingly, for payment of civic amenities works as per the progress of works completed/ in progress under the proposal, a total amount of Rs. 62,51,000/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.

4) Executive Engineer Dhom Canal Department No. 2, Satara should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and the concerned Collector.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned system shall ensure that no funds under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order," Demand No.C-10, Main Account Head 4701- Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01- Major Irrigation- Commercial, 001- Operation and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects (Programme), 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat)" shall remain outstanding or the funds received under the said order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned system.
The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned agency shall ensure that no short payment of one year is pending under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order, namely," Demand No.C-10, Main Accounts Head 4701- Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01- Major Irrigation- Commercial, 001- Operation and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects (Programme), 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat)."
