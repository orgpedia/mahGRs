The Proposal:
Strengthening of State Biodiversity Boards (2406 8696) for the financial year 2021-22. A budget of Rs. 2103.72 lakh has been allocated for this purpose. Finance Department Reference No. Guidelines have been issued for disbursement of funds in 2021-22 under Government Decisions 1, 2, 3 and 4.
