The Proposal:
In the Post Plan Scheme" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park," the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary in the year 2020 and 2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
2. Now, as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for Government approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the post-Plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022.
Extension of the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for temporary posts included in the pattern of the Department and all existing temporary posts created after approval of review earlier but not included in the pattern.

The powers to grant have been delegated to the administrative departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 7 of the Department of Finance.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 7, the Government have accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Post-Plan Scheme for" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park."
The expenditure on establishment of 1 temporary post created on the establishment of the Conservator of Forest and Director, Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve should be covered by the provision available in the financial year 2021-2022 under the head of 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766).
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10.08.2021 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
