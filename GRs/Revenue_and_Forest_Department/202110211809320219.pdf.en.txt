Preamble: To provide assistance to the affected farmers who have suffered crop loss due to excessive rainfall from June to October, 2021 as well as due to flood situation in various districts of the State. The issue was discussed in the Cabinet meeting held on 13.10.2021. A decision in this regard was taken at the state cabinet meeting.
Government Decision: The Government is approving to provide the following assistance to the affected farmers who have suffered crop losses due to heavy rains from June to October, 2021 as well as the flood situation in various districts of the State.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | baba                             | State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF)                       | Increased rate of assistance                              |
| 1    | for crop loss                    | Rs.6800/- per hectare, subject to a limit of 2 hectares   | Rs.10,000/- per hectare, subject to a limit of 2 hectares |
| 2    | for the loss of crops            | Rs.13,500/- per hectare, subject to a limit of 2 hectares | Rs.15,000/- per hectare, subject to a limit of 2 hectares |
| 3.   | Help for loss of perennial crops | Rs.18000/- per hectare, subject to a limit of 2 hectares  | Rs.25,000/- per hectare, subject to a limit of 2 hectares |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The other terms and conditions prescribed in the relevant Government Decision shall continue to apply while providing the amount of assistance.
