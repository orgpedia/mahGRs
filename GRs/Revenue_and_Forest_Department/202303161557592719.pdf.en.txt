The Proposal:
Establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) for the financial year 2022-23 with a total outlay of Rs. 8700.00 lakh has been approved and budgeted.
Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. Guidelines have been issued for disbursement of financial funds for the year 2022-23 in accordance with the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2022 vide Circular No.
3. Under the scheme of Establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for the year 2022-23 under the letter of reference No. 5385.16 lakh has been submitted to the Government for approval. The government has taken the decision in this regard.
Government Decision: In view of the demand for funds made by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, in terms of Reference No. 2 and Letter No. 3 of the Department of Finance, the establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) under the heading of Budget Fund Distribution System within the limit of Rs. 1342.70 lakhs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions as per attached statement:
The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System in the year 2022-23 under the scheme (00) (10) (2406 2232) of the State Plan under the Account No. 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife under the Five Year Plan.
The funds should be spent only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority as required.
(iii) Please ensure that no funds are spent from other schemes other than the scheme for establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) on the work items in the proposal submitted for Government approval.
