The proposal:
In the year 2022-23, an amount of Rs. 8700.00 lakh has been sanctioned and budgeted for the establishment of Wildlife Handicap Centre (2406 2232) under State Level Scheme.
As per the decision of the Government of Reference No. 1, approval has been given to set up a treatment centre for wild animals in an area of 22 acres at Mauje Bavdhan in Pune forest circle. Subject to the administrative approval given to the budgets for the work of setting up the treatment centre for wildlife, a total amount of Rs. 2248.56 lakh has been approved for e-tendering. For the e-tendering activities sanctioned during 2020-21, as per the Government decision in Reference No. 2 and 3, an amount of Rs.
1400.00 lakhs has been disbursed.
Now, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter of reference No. 5, has approved for the year 2022-23, for the activities in the e-tender for setting up a treatment centre for wild animals at Mauje Bavdhan in Pune forest circle, the remaining amount of Rs.
A proposal of Rs 848.56 lakh has been submitted to the government for approval. A decision in this regard will be taken later.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 05.22/ S.No.130/ F-1
The government's decision:
In accordance with the proposal submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur under the letter of reference No. 5 and the funds available under the scheme of establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232), the Government has decided to provide a total of Rs. 848.56 lakh is being disbursed.
The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System in the year 2022-23 under the scheme (00) (10) (2406 2232) of the State Plan under the Account No. 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife under the Five Year Plan.
The funds should be spent only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority as required.
(iii) Please ensure that no funds are spent from other schemes other than the scheme for establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) on the work items in the proposal submitted for Government approval.
(iv) It shall be ensured that no violation of the provisions of the Indian Forest Act, 1927, the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the guidelines of the National Zoological Park is committed and necessary permission is obtained.
V. It should be ensured that the subsidy disbursed will be spent only on the works mentioned in the proposal which have received administrative approval from the competent authority.
(vi) The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the provisions of the proposal.
vii. The Controller/ Implementing Officer concerned shall follow the instructions and procedures prescribed by the Government for the proposed construction.
It will also be their responsibility to avoid any financial irregularities.
viii. Procurement, if any, should be done in accordance with the procedure prescribed by the Government.
ix. Expenditure shall be incurred in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the Department from time to time in pursuance of the said scheme and in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the Finance Department from time to time in accordance with the terms of reference of the proposal.
