Government order:
Rajendra Ganesh Bonde, the then Divisional Forest Officer, Social Forestry, Amravati, in the cadre of Divisional Forest Officer, Group-A (Senior Grade), vide Reference No. 2 was suspended under the Government Order dated 19/10/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 4 (5) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, hereby order, Shri Rajendra Ganesh Bonde, Divisional Forest Officer, has been appointed as the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Nagpur Subject to the decision dated 25/02/2021, being reinstated in Government service, the post is being posted in the non-executive vacancy of Director, Forest Training Institute, Jalna under Aurangabad Revenue Division after the reinstatement.
The proceedings of the departmental inquiry against Shri Rajendra Ganesh Bonde, Divisional Forest Officer will continue even after his reinstatement. They should also join the place of posting within 15 days from the date of this order. During this period, the suspension of Shri Rajendra Ganesh Bonde, Divisional Forest Officer will be effective from the date he joins the duty.
