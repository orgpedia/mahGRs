The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests cadre transferred to Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, vide letter No.8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the cadre transferred to the Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 9 of the Finance Department to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department, the Government has approved the continuation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in the cadre of Conservator of Forest and Area Director, Samhyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 in the year 2022-2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest transferred to Conservator of Forest and Area Director, Samhyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 001 Operations and Administration, (01) Operations and Administration, (06) Principal Chief Conservator of Forest, Social Forestry (2406 8506)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
