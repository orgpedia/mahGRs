The government's decision:

Applications and complaints etc. are in progress. For this, a request has been made by the Chairman, Housing Resolution Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar for extension of these posts. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these posts.
2. The 9 posts mentioned in the attached annexure in the Sewerage Management Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar Office are being extended for the period from 01/03/2025 to 31/08/2025.
3. Expenditure for the said temporary posts at the establishment of Garhwal Resolution Authority, Nandurbar to be first met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2024-2024 under Demand Order C - 5-2235 - Social Security Welfare, 01 Rehabilitation, 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes, (03) (08) Ga-Hye Resolution Authority, Nandurbar Office (2235 3141) Non-Plan in respect of Sardar Sarovar Project victims. Thereafter, the expenditure is estimated at the end of the year 2024-2025 under the head "Demand No. I-11-4801 - Capital expenditure on power project-01 - Hydropower Generation (20) - Sardar Sarovar Project - Under Schemes "should be adjusted by adjusting the books below in consultation with the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar.
