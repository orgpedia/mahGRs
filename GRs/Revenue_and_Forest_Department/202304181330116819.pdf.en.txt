Considering the above, the matter of taking effective measures as per the Government Decision/ Circular of the General Administration Department was under the consideration of the Government for expeditious disposal of the pending departmental enquiry cases in the Forest Department.
Government Circular: Considering the long-pending departmental inquiry cases in the forest department, it is being informed to take the following measures.
(a) to file a complaint:
(1) The competent authority shall take care to issue a charge sheet to the suspended government officer/ employee within 90 days from the date of suspension.
(2) If the Government officer/ employee has not been suspended, the competent authority shall take care to issue a charge sheet within 15 days after the completion of the preliminary inquiry or after receiving the reply in accordance with the show-cause notice, if the reply is not satisfactory.
(b) reinstatement of the suspended officer:
(1) In cases of suspended government servants in which a departmental inquiry has been initiated within a period of 3 months and a charge sheet has been issued, the decision on whether to continue the suspension after reviewing the suspension within 3 months from the date of suspension shall be taken at the level of the competent authority with a clear order (including the reason). In this regard, the provisions issued from time to time by the General Administration Department should be considered.
