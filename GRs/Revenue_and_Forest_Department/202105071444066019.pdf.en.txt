The Minister (Forest) is the ex-officio Vice-Chairman. It is also required to appoint three members of the State Legislature as per Article 6 (1) (a), three members from the non-governmental organization concerned with wildlife protection as per Article 6 (1) (d) and 10 non-governmental members concerned with environmental protection as per Article 6 (1) (e) (two of whom are representatives of Scheduled Tribes).
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Board for Wildlife has been reconstituted in accordance with Section 6 of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972. Rohidas Namdeo Dagale is being appointed as a non-official member (representative of Scheduled Tribes) in the Maharashtra State Board for Wildlife as per the provisions of Section (1) (e) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 for appointment of 10 non-official members (out of which two members are representatives of Scheduled Tribes) concerned with environmental protection.
The term of office of members other than ex-officio members of the Maharashtra State Board for Wildlife shall be for a period of three years from the date of issue of this Government Order.
Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance of Non-Official Members, as per the amended provisions of Annexure 42 (O) Section 1 of Bombay Civil Services Rules, Clause-2 and Government Resolution, Finance Division No. Travel-1098/ PR No. 74/98-Service-5, dated 4.12.1999, Government Corrigendum, Finance Division No. No.74/ 98/ Service-5 dated 7.12.1999 and TRA- 1470/131/ XIII dated 27.1.1971. Non-government members will not be allowed to travel by air or air-conditioned class-1 class of the railways. Private vehicles are also not allowed.
