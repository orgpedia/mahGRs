Constitution of a committee to identify sites other than Aarey for rehabilitation of eligible encroachers in Sanjay Gandhi National Park.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
Government Resolution No: WLP-0921/ P.No.248/ F-1
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: December 22, 2021
The proposal: In a meeting held on 30 th September, 2021 under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister regarding rehabilitation of tribal forest dwellers and encroachers in the Sanjay Gandhi National Park area, the difficulties being faced in the area of Aarey for rehabilitation of eligible encroachers were discussed. Accordingly, to determine the place other than Aarey to rehabilitate the eligible encroachers. A committee was formed under the chairmanship of the Minister of Tourism, Environment and Governance and the Guardian Minister, Mumbai Suburban. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: In order to maintain the forest area of Sanjay Gandhi National Park and to avoid the difficulties arising in the future regarding rehabilitation, it is necessary to rehabilitate the forest area at the earliest.
