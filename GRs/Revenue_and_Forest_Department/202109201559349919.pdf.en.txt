The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 14 temporary posts created on the establishment of Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 under the Government Decision of Reference No. 2 and 3 in the year 2020-2021.
Now, as per Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 14 temporary posts created on the establishment of Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati for the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022.

The powers to extend the tenure of all such existing temporary posts from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 have been delegated to the administrative departments under the Government Resolution of Reference No.7 of the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department, the Government has approved the continuation of 14 temporary posts created on the establishment of Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati in the year 2021-2022 from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The expenditure on the establishment of 14 temporary posts created on the establishment of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati, should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2021-2022 under the head of 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development and Reconstruction, (01) Administration and Protection, (01) Administration and Protection, 01 Salary (2406 0096).
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10.08.2021 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
