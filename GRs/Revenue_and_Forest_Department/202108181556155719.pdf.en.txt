Appointment of Nominee Director on the Board of Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Ltd.
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA,
Revenue and Forest Department,
Order No. FDC-2021/CR-80/F-5
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032.
Dated: 18 August, 2021
Reference - 1) Government DO letter No.AEO-1121/10/2021/10, dtd.03/06/2021.
2) Managing Director, FDCM Ltd., Nagpur letter No.CSL/Govt.
Correspondence /20 Nagpur, Dated 10/06/2021
Order
As per the powers conferred under Article 89 (1), (2), and (4) of the Articles of Association
of the Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited (" FDCM"), it is hereby decided
nominate Shri. B. Venugopal Reddy, Principal Secretary (Forests), Maharashtra State as Nominee
Director on the Board of Directors of Forest Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited.
2. His appointment shall take effect from the date of allotment of Director Identification
Number or from the date of this order, whichever is later.
3. The appointment of Shri Milind Jayant Mhaiskar, Nominee Director is being withdrawn
from the date of this order.
4. Managing Director/Company Secretary, FDCM Ltd. Nagpur to take necessary action in this
regards.
