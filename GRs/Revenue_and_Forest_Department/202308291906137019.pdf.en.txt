Appointment by appointment
Vijaya Bankar, Deputy Collector (Revenue), Bhandara has been posted as General Manager, Nagpur Improvement Trust, Nagpur.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
GOVERNMENT ORDER, NO. TRF-0623/ PR No.152 (PART-1)/ E-2
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: August 29, 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Government order:
Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Smt. Vijaya Banakar, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as General Manager, Nagpur Improvement Trust, Nagpur on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
Vijaya Bankar, Deputy Collector, is being transferred to Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry of Urban Development, Mumbai, in accordance with the deputation of General Manager, Nagpur Improvement Project, Nagpur.
Mrs. Vijaya Banakar, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
