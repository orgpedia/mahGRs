This extension is expiring on 31.8.2021. Accordingly, the Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation), Divisional Commissioner Office, Nagpur issued Reference No. 4 Posts of 33 temporary posts. Proposal for extension from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022 has been submitted.
As per the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, the field level posts under the Relief and Rehabilitation Sub-Division have been reviewed and a total of 33 posts of Nagpur Division on Rehabilitation Establishment have been outlined in the relevant Government Decision No. determined by 1. Finance Department Reference No. As per Order No. 4, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022. Accordingly, 33 temporary posts mentioned in the accompanying Form-A contained in the Annexure are being approved for extension from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022.
The above posts are temporary in nature and should be treated as additional posts in addition to the permanent posts of the concerned department. Also, the office bearers will be entitled to dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the rules of the government. Further, the Government has approved the extension subject to the assumption that the above posts were not vacant for more than six months.
