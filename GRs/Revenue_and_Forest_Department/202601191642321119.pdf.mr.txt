Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 0.4704 ha. Forest Land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980 for construction of 33 KV x 11 KV, 1 MV double circuit electric overhead line for co-
generated solar power plant at Village Thoseghar, Tal.Satara, Dist. Satara.;
# Page 2
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Regional Office (Central) Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.3, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 0.4704 ha. forest Land for the
above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.5 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 6 accorded ‘Final approval’
to divert the 0.4704 ha of forest land in favour of M/s. Shital Abhijit Pote for construction of
33 KV x 11 KV, 1 MV double circuit electric overhead line for co-generated solar power plant at Village Thoseghar, Tal.Satara, Dist. Satara;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.7 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.4704
ha. consisting of Reserved forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be
diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. M/s. Shital Abhijit Pote for construction of 33 KV x 11 KV,
1 MV double circuit electric overhead line for co-generated solar power plant at Village
Thoseghar, Tal.Satara, Dist. Satara
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 26.08.2022 and dated 06.11.2023 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Satara should ensure that fulfillment of condition
No.2 & 13 mentioned in the letter dated 06.11.2023 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC,
Nagpur before actual handing over of the forest land to the user agency.
2. User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’ and
‘final approval’ letters of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above. The
Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report 1n this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
# Page 3
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits
the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MOEF&CC
letters dated 26.08.2022 and dated 06.11.2023, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall
amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and
action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of
Forests (T), Kolhapur shall report through the State Government to the Deputy
Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment,
Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down
in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
