The proposal-
As per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2016, 55 bonded labourers have been issued release certificates in the year 2019 in Pune district. As immediate financial assistance of Rs.20,000/- each is payable to the 55 released bonded labourers as per the provisions of Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2016, the Collector, Pune, has issued orders vide No. 1 The letter has requested the government to provide a total of Rs. 11.00 lakh. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
A total of 55 bonded labourers have been issued release certificates in Pune district as per the provisions of Central Bonded Labour Rehabilitation Scheme, 2016. As per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2016, a total amount of Rs. 11,00,000/-is being sanctioned by the Government for providing immediate financial assistance of Rs.20,000/-each to the 55 released bonded labourers.
