Setting up of a Special Investigation Team to probe the killing of T-1 tigress in Ralegaon area of Pandharkawada Forest Division.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
Government Resolution No: WLP-0818/ P.No.290/ F-1
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: June 11, 2021
Preamble: In the case of killing of tigress T-1 in Ralegaon area of Pandharkawada forest division in Yavatmal district, as per the opinion of the Department of Law and Justice, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai, and the Hon. The High Court, Bombay, Bench at Nagpur in Criminal Public Interest Litigation No. 1/2019 dated 09.02.2021. The government had constituted a special investigation team (SIT) to probe the case.
Government Decision: To fix responsibility against the culprits in the killing of T-1 tigress in Ralegaon area of Pandharkawada forest division in Yavatmal district, a committee is being formed by involving experts from various departments such as Home Department, Law and Justice Department, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries Department.

The members of the Special Inquiry Committee will be provided with the inquiry report of the committee set up by the National Tiger Conservation Authority and the report of the government-constituted committee through the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur.
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1. m. The High Court, Nagpur has disposed of Criminal Public Interest Litigation No. 1/2019 to be inquired into by the Committee in accordance with the points contained in the order dated 09.02.2021.
