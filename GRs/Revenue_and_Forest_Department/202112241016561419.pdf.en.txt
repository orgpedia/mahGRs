To provide funds for the remuneration of 42 posts of District Disaster Management Officers created for the Maharashtra State Disaster Management Programme.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation)
Government Decision No:-AYP-2021/ P.S.94/ AYP-1
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 24th of December 2021
A total of 42 posts of 36 District Disaster Management Officers and 6 Divisional Disaster Management Coordinators created and working on contract basis for the implementation of Maharashtra State Disaster Management Program and this program have been extended till December 2021. A monthly stipend is being paid. Out of these posts, the post of Divisional Disaster Management Coordinator, Pune is vacant, so excluding the honorarium of that post, the other 41 posts will be paid Rs. 45,000/- each as ex-gratia for 1 month (December) to the Divisional Commissioner as per the accompanying letter. The Government is approving to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 18,45,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs, Forty Five Thousand only) on BDS. This fund should be distributed on BDS by the Divisional Commissioner to the concerned district.
