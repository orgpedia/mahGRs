GOVERNMENT Circular: In this regard, directions have been issued to all the concerned Regional Revenue Officers/ Authorities regarding the compensation and other due benefits to be given to the forest rights holders and forest belts while acquiring their lands under the Right to Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 for various projects in the State.
Individual forest rights holders under the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional and Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 are defined as" land holders" under Section 3 (c) of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013. Thus, they are entitled to all the benefits admissible as" landholders."
(2) Whereas, under the Land Acquisition Act, the land holder is entitled to the compensation amount and other rehabilitation and resettlement benefits in respect of the land acquired by him. And, therefore, the government has clarified that individual forest rights holders will be recognised as land holders. Accordingly, the compensation amount and other rehabilitation and resettlement benefits provided under the Land Acquisition Act should be paid while acquiring the lands held by the individual forest rights holders under the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional and Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006.
(2) This order shall be effective immediately.
