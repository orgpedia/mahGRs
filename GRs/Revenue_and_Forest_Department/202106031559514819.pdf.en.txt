GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SRIKH- 2021/ P.S. NO. 83/ F-9
In order to utilize the above grant, it shall be obligatory on the concerned to comply with the following terms and conditions specified under the heading (2406-A096).
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. The funds are being given to them.
The above expenditure should be incurred as per the prescribed procedure and as per the monthly fund flow available on the budget distribution system. There should be no financial irregularities.
The Controlling Authorities are advised to ensure that no additional expenditure is incurred under any circumstances over and above the total disbursement.
4. While spending this provision, the terms and conditions laid down by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed.
5. Before drawing the funds available on the budget distribution system from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms and conditions should be followed.
The expenditure should be limited to the limit of subsidy sanctioned and disbursed in the current financial year 2021-22.
7. It shall be ensured that the amount withdrawn after withdrawal of the said grant from the concerned treasury is recorded in the records of the Accountant General's office under the same head and submit a certificate to the Government.
8. The Institute shall forthwith submit to the Government and the Accountant General the certificate of appropriation of the grants made by it for the previous year. Similarly, the detailed payment and utilization certificate of the subsidy being distributed now should also be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and the government immediately.
The Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur shall have the power to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure made out of the above grant and the said institution shall provide complete facilities to him for the same.
10. It should be ensured that the expenditure meets the conditions of the circular dated 06.04.2021 of the Finance Department.
The approval shall be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the Annexure to Government Decision No.MSC-2011/ P.No. 178/ F-5, dated 30.9.2011.
The above expenditure is to be met from the subsidy to be made available during the financial year 2021-22 under the head Demand No.C-7, Main Accounts Title 2406, Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110-Wildlife Protection (00) (20) Establishment of Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board (01) (General) (2406-A096).
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the circular dated 06.04.2021 of the Finance Department and the authority conferred on the Department under the Government Decision No. V.P./ 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Exchange, Part-2, dated 17.4.2015 of the Finance Department.
