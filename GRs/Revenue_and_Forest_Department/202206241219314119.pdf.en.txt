Introduction: The Revenue Department is the backbone of the state administration and the Revenue Department, Registration and Stamps Department and Land Records Department handle the affairs related to immovable properties. Various computerization initiatives are underway in all the three departments to provide more people-oriented services to the citizens. In addition, it is proposed to develop various systems of computerization under the Land Acquisition Branch as well as the Relief and Rehabilitation Department. Therefore, an Integrated Project Implementation Cell has been set up under the decision of the government in the context of operationalization, feasibility, state-wide implementation and coordination of computerization activities in all these departments. Necessary manpower has been prescribed in the context government decision to make this cell operational. The Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune has recommended changes in the manpower mentioned in the relevant government decision. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Preamble: An Integrated Project Implementation Cell has been set up for operationalization, feasibility, State-wide implementation and coordination of computerization activities in the Department.
