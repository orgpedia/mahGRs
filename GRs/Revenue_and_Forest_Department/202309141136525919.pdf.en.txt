Date: 14th of September 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Government order:
Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Mr. Mahesh Sagar, Special Land Acquisition Officer, Aurangabad is being appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years by retaining the Aurangabad Division allotted to him.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Shri Mahesh Sagar, Deputy Collector is being relieved unilaterally from the post he is holding to join the post immediately.
The services of Mr. Mahesh Sagar, Deputy Collector, in pursuance of his deputation as Deputy Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation, are being handed over to the Principal Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
4. Mr. Mahesh Sagar, Deputy Collector to appear before the Principal Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai immediately.
