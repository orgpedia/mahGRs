If the concerned officer does not appear at the place of appointment as per the said transfer order or tries to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against him considering it as misconduct as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
