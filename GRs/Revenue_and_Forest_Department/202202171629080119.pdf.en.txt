As per the notification on Reference No. 1 above, the Government has declared 67.82 sq.km. area of Warud Forest Range in Amravati Forest Division of Amravati District as Mahendri Conservation Reserve. The Government was considering the formation of a Management Committee for the management of the area and to advise the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) and the Chief Wildlife Warden.
As per the provisions of Section 36-B (1) and (2) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972, a Management Committee is being constituted for the management of the Mahendrai Conservation Reserve as follows:

The functions of the Management Committee shall be as follows:
(1) The jurisdiction of the Committee shall be limited to the Mahendrai Conservation Reserve.
The Committee shall forward proposals and suggestions to the Chief Wildlife Warden for protection and management of the Conservation Reserve and shall provide necessary support.
The Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the provisions of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and other forest laws and Acts in the conservation reserve.
The tenure of the Committee shall be for a period of three years from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
