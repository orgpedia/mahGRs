The approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1) The legal status of forest area will not change.
2) In the present case, since the forest has been broken, the project management will have to pay the required amount to the forest department as per the prevailing rate system.
The optical fiber cable has a maximum depth of 2.00 m and a width of 1.00 m.
It should be put in a trench. Cover the cable with PVC.
4) The land used for the project will be restored by the project management. Also, the project machinery will have to submit a bank guarantee at the rate of Rs 50/- per meter for the land to be used. If the land used for the project is in the opinion of the concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests
If the antecedents are not satisfactory, the cost will be recovered by encashing the guarantee of the bank.
5) If the optical fibre cable has to be laid through the existing road width, then an additional guarantee letter of the bank is submitted to the project machinery at the rate of Rs 35/- per meter.
have to do. The damage to the road side excavation and asphalting will have to be repaired and for this, a bank guarantee will have to be submitted to the project management at the rate of Rs 300/- per meter and Rs 750/- per meter respectively. The project agency will have to submit an undertaking that any damage caused to the forest area or the environment will be compensated while laying and maintaining the optical fibre cable in the future.
6) It shall be mandatory for the Project Management to obtain permission from the concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests as per requirement for future maintenance work of the project.
(7) Forest land shall not be used for any purpose other than the purpose for which it is sought in the project proposal.
8) Trees will not be cut for the implementation of the project.
9) No other forest area, which is not permitted along the existing road, can be used while laying the optical fibre cable to the project system.
10) The details of the amount paid for alternative afforestation as per the prevailing rate system by the project machinery and other amounts paid as per the new rules during the intervening period will be provided by the Chief Conservator of Forest (PRF). ), Kolhapur will present.
