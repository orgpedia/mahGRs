The Proposal:
On 17 th May, 2021, Cyclone Tauktae hit the coastal and some other districts of the state. Orders have been issued under the relevant Government decisions to provide assistance to the persons affected by natural calamities for various losses from the State Disaster Response Fund/ National Disaster Response Fund at the rates prescribed by the Central Government.
The Cabinet decided to provide assistance to the affected persons of cyclone Tauktae as per the rate of assistance prescribed under the decision of the government in this regard. The meeting was held on 27.05.2021. As per the decision taken in the meeting, the matter of issuing orders regarding providing assistance to the affected people of' Tauktae' cyclone was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The government is approving to provide assistance to the affected citizens at the following rates for the damage caused by the cyclone Tauktae in various districts of the state.
