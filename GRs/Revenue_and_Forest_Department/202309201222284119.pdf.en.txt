Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 08/02/2023 of the Finance Department, a total of 143 temporary posts outside the figure of 84 posts for non-agricultural work in Konkan Division and 59 posts for Additional Tahsildar Agricultural Land Tribunal were extended for the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 under the Government decision dated 13/03/2023 in the context of Revenue and Forest Department.
The Department of Finance, Government Decision, dated 31/08/2023 and the Government Preamble, dated 06/09/2023 have empowered all the Administrative Departments to extend the period from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, a total of 143 temporary posts outside the figure of 84 posts for non-agricultural work and 59 posts of Additional Tehsildar Agricultural Land Tribunal were under the consideration of the government for the period from September 1, 2023 to February 29, 2024.

Government's decision:
A total of 143 temporary posts outside the figure of 84 posts for non-agricultural work in the Konkan division and 59 posts for Additional Tahsildar Agricultural Land Tribunal have been included in the reference No. The extension of temporary posts in the statement attached with the Government decision herein is being extended for the period from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 subject to the conditions contained in the Government Decision dated 31/08/2023 of the Finance Department and Government Clarification dated 06/09/2023.
2. Expenditure on these temporary posts" Demand No. C-1, 2053-District Administration, (093) District Establishment, (01) General Establishment, (01) (01), Commissioner Konkan, (20530028) 01, to be shared from the budgetary grants sanctioned for the year 2023-2024 under the head' Salaries."
The Finance Department has directed all the administrative departments to take note that the revised figure in the Government Resolution dated 31/08/2023 in the context of the Finance Department needs to be approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries in the meantime and no further extension of time will be granted to finalize the revised figure.
