A proposal was submitted to the Cabinet to provide assistance to the affected people affected by the flood situation in various districts of the state due to heavy rains in July 2021. In pursuance of the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 03.08.2021, the Government Resolution No. 3 has been issued.
Government's decision:
The citizens affected for the damage caused by the floods in various districts of the state due to the heavy rains in July, 2021, are advised to refer to Reference No. 3 Government decisions have helped. The cabinet sub-committee decided to relax the criteria for assistance in respect of shopkeepers and tapri-holders in this government decision as follows. It has been approved in the meeting dated 27.10.2021.
Accordingly, the government is approving to provide assistance to shopkeepers and tapri holders as per the revised criteria as follows.
