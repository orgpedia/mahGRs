Preamble- The payment of salary and allowances of the employees of the Forest Department of the Government from February-2006 will be passed from the treasury like the employees of other departments. The decision has been taken as per the Government decision dated 04.01.2006.
Accordingly, as per Rule 13 and Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the list of Drawing and Disbursing Officers in the department is sent to the Accountant General's Office. Accordingly, the lists of Drawing and Disbursing Officers of the Department have been published as per the Government decisions issued from time to time.

Accordingly, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur, vide his Reference No. 2 vide letter dated 01.02.2020 and the Conservator of Forests& Range Director, Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve, Chandrapur vide their Reference No. 3 A proposal has been submitted to the Government to declare the post of Assistant Conservator of Forest, STPF, Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve, Chandrapur as Forest Range Officer (Special Tiger Protection Force) Chandrapur with the same Drawing and Disbursing Officer Number (4801006114) and to declare Forest Range Officer (Special Tiger Protection Force) Mul and Chimur as Drawing and Disbursing Officers by issuing new Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Accordingly, the matter of declaring the said designation as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur vide his Reference No. 2 vide letter dated 01.02.2020 and the Conservator of Forests& Range Director, Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve, Chandrapur vide their Reference No. 3 In pursuance of the proposal made vide letter dated 01.04.2017, the Assistant Conservator of Forest, Tiger Protection Force (STPF), Tadoba, Andhra Tiger Reserve, Chandrapur has been replaced by the Forest Range Officer (Special Tiger Protection Force), Chandrapur by retaining the old Drawing and Disbursing Number (4801006114) and providing new Drawing and Disbursing Number to the Forest Range Officer (Special Tiger Protection Force), Mul and Chimur. As per the Government Order dated 04.01.2006, the Drawing and Disbursing Officers are being declared to disburse the salary and allowances of the field staff under their command:
