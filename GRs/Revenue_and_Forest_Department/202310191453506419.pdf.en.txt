Introduction: Due to the increasing industrialization, urbanization and rapid expansion of the residential area of the taluka, the current tehsil office of Ahmednagar Taluka is under a lot of work pressure. Therefore, in view of administrative convenience, a proposal was under consideration of the government to set up a separate Additional Tehsil Office at Ahmednagar in Ahmednagar district and create necessary posts for it.
Government's decision:
The approval is being given for the establishment of an Additional Tehsil Office at Ahmednagar in Ahmednagar district and the sanction of posts and other ancillary matters as follows.
In order to strengthen the Ahmednagar Tehsil Office in Ahmednagar District, the Government is approving the following 2 posts for the new office of the Additional Tehsildar, Ahmednagar on regular pay scale, and these posts will remain a part of the existing cadre.
