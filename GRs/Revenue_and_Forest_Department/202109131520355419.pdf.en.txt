Government Decision: Strengthening of Work Planning Mechanism 77 temporary posts under this Post-Plan Scheme have been sanctioned from 1.3.2021 to 31.8.2021 in Reference No. The deadline has been extended by the government. Now the reference No. 3 Under the Government Decision dated 10.8.2021 of the Finance Department here, the powers to extend the temporary posts from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022 have been provided to the administrative departments.
As per the proposal received from the Office of the Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Nagpur, as per the authority provided by the Finance Department, a total of 77 temporary posts in Statement A accompanying this Government Decision under the Post-Plan Scheme are hereby approved to be continued from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022.
The expenditure for the said 77 temporary posts shall be met from the approved allocation for the financial year 2021-22 under the head of Principal Head- 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development Reconstruction, (04) (01) Improvement and Preparation of Work Plan (2406 0149), 01 Salary.
The said Government Decision is being issued by the Department of Finance, within the powers conferred by Government Decision No. 2016/ 8/16/ APC dated 10.8.2021.
