the prelude
Under the Dhom and Kanher Project, a total of 4 urban amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Vagheshwar (Masur) in Karad Taluka of Satara district and Mauje Kondavali (Paratwadi) and Mauje Chandwadi (Tadvale) in Koregaon Taluka have been taken up under the project. 4 hereunder and under reference No. Administrative approval has been granted under the Government Order No. 6 and No. 8. The funds have been distributed under the government orders of 5, 7 and 9. For payment of civil works completed out of the administrative sanction works, the reference No. 11 Proposal for Demand of Funds through a letter addressed to the Executive Engineer, Dhom Canal Division No. 2, Satara has submitted to the government.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to provide a sum of Rs. 21,51,000/-together for the civic amenities works completed under the proposal.
Government's decision
Under the Dhom and Kanher Project, the Government is approving the disbursement of a total of Rs. 21,51,000/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Fifty One Thousand only) for the 4 completed civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Vagheshwar (Masur) in Karad Taluka of Satara district and Mauje Kondavali (Paratwadi) and Mauje Chandavadi (Tadvale) in Koregaon Taluka, subject to the following conditions in the current financial year 2021-22.
