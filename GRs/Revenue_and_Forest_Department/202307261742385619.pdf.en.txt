GOVERNMENT DECISION: Reorganization of Maharashtra State Board for Wildlife in terms of Section 6 of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 vide the aforesaid Reference No. Decisions 1 and 2 were made. Appointment of the members of the said Board vide reference no. 3 has been cancelled by the government. Therefore, the proposal to reconstitute the Maharashtra State Wildlife Board was under the consideration of the government. The Board is now being reconstituted as follows:

The term of office of the members other than the ex-officio members of the Board shall be for a period of three years from the date of issue of the Government Order.
Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance of Non-Government Members, as per the amended provisions of Annexure 42 (O) Section 1 to the Bombay Civil Services Rules, Vol. No.74/ 98/ Service-5 dated 7.12.1999 and TRA- 1470/131/ XIII dated 27.1.1971. Non-government members will not be allowed to travel by air or air-conditioned class-1 class of the railways. Private vehicles are also not allowed.
Expenditure on account of travelling allowance and daily allowance of non-official members of the Committee shall be covered under the head Demand No. C-16 Account Head 2406, Forestry and Wildlife 02, Environment Forestry and Wildlife 110 (i) Conservation of Nature and Protection of Wild Animals (2406 0766) and shall be met from the sanctioned grant. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer to sign the bills of travelling allowance of non-official members.
