The proposal:
As per the pattern of the posts at the zonal level of the Rehabilitation Department, a figure of 140 posts in the Pune Division was fixed. However, under the Government Decision dated 21.04.2010 and Government Decision dated 28.01.2010, 25 posts were reduced to 115 posts and thereafter Government Decision No. In pursuance of Composite Regulation 2013/487/ PR No.27/ A-3 dated 03.07.2013, 103 temporary posts were sanctioned after 2013-14 till the previous year by reducing 12 posts. As per the Government decision dated 18.03.2021, 103 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune Division and for the acquisition of land in the beneficiary areas were granted extension till 31.08.2021.
The administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Government Order dated 10.08.2021 of the Finance Department. The decision also states that all administrative departments should expedite the process of reviewing the sanctioned posts under the control of administrative departments in the meantime and getting the approval of the high-level secretary committee to the revised figure.
Accordingly, the Government was considering extension of 103 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune Division and for the acquisition of land in the beneficiary areas till 28.02.2022.

The government's decision:
For the rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune Division, 103 temporary posts shown in the accompanying prospectus under 9 Non-Plan and 94 District Level Schemes in the Commissioner's Office are being extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 subject to the following conditions.
All these posts should be considered as a temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to the holders of these posts as per rules.
(2) None of the posts in the return shall remain vacant or under additional charge for a period of more than six months.
Out of the total 103 temporary posts as above, expenditure on 9 posts at Commissioner level under head Demand No.C-5" 2235 Social Security and Welfare (01) Rehabilitation, 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes, (03) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, (03) (02) Commissioner Pune, (Compulsory), (22351882) 01 Salary" and expenditure on 94 posts at District level under head Demand C-5" 2235 Social Security and Welfare (01) Rehabilitation, 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes, (04) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons (04) (02) Commissioner, Pune (Compulsory) (22351942) 01- Salary" should be included in the account for the year 2021-2022
The details of the posts being extended are indicated in the attached prospectus.
This Government Decision is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments vide Government Decision No:- No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 10.08.2021 of the Finance Department.
