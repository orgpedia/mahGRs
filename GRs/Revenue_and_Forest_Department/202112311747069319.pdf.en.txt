The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
(1) The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan shall disburse the funds received by him under this order to the Collector, Thane immediately through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
2) The Collector, Thane shall draw the said fund and disburse the said fund by cheque to the Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Division, Thane.
3) The Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Division, Thane, should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division and the concerned Collector.
(4) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
5) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
(6) No funds shall be left out under the head of the head of the said Order namely Demand No.C-10, Head of Account 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation, 01, Major Irrigation-Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H727) (Dattamat).
