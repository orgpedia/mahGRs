The government's decision:
09 temporary posts for the office of Garhwal Resolution Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar were extended till 31st August, 2025. The Grievance Redressal Authority is still dealing with appeals, review applications, complaints, etc. For this, a request has been made by the Chairman, Garhwal Resolution Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar for extension of these posts. Accordingly, the issue of extension of these posts was under consideration of the Government.

2. The 9 posts mentioned in the attached annexure in the Sewerage Management Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar Office are being extended for the period from 01/09/2025 to 28/02/2026.
3. Expenditure for the said temporary posts at the establishment of Garhwal Resolution Authority, Nandurbar to be first met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2025-2026 under C- 5-2235 - Social Security Welfare, 01 Rehabilitation, 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes, (03) (08) Ga-Hye Resolution Authority, Nandurbar Office (2235 3141) Non-Plan in respect of Sardar Sarovar Project victims. Thereafter, the expenditure is estimated at the end of the year 2025-2026 under the head "Demand No. I - 11-4801 - Capital expenditure on seed project - 019 - Hydropower Generation (10) - Sardar Sarovar Project - Under Schemes "should be adjusted by adjusting the books below in consultation with the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar.
4. The said Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision No. Designation 2016 / No. 8/16 APC, dated 26/08/2025 of the Department of Finance.
