Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1! has submitted a proposal to
the State Government, which was received from Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai for diversion
9.04 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for
Medicinal tree Plantation and establishment of Cultivation facility Project at Village Donvat,
Tal. Khalapur, Dist. Raigad in the State of Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra vide letter under reference No.2 has
submitted the proposal to the Integrated Regional Office, MoEFCC, Government of India,
Nagpur under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Government of India,
Nagpur, vide letter under reference no. 3, has conveyed that the proposal for diversion of 9.04
ha. Forest land for Medicinal tree Plantation and establishment of Cultivation facility Project
at Village Donvat, Tal. Khalapur, Dist. Raigad was forwarded to the MOEF&CC, New Delhi
for approval after the recommendation of the REC and the proposal has not been accepted by
MoEF&CC, New Delhi citing the proposal as "non-site specific" and justifications for
locating it on forest land or alternatives explored/reasons of not accepting alternative lands are
not satisfactory.
AND whereas, the MoEF&CC, New Delhi vide letter under reference no.4 has
conveyed to resubmit the proposal in light of the new facts brought in by the user agency to the Ministry.
AND whereas, the State Government vide letters under reference No.5 has
resubmitted this proposal to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur after due consideration, vide letter under reference
No.6, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 9.04 ha. Forest Land for the above project
under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The said approval
is subject to fulfilment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.7 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the State Government;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letters under reference No.8 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 9 accorded ‘Final
approval’ to divert the of 9.04 ha. of forest land in favour of Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai
for Establishment of Medicinal Tree Plantation and establishment of cultivation facility
project at Village- Donvat, Taluka- Khalapur, District- Raigad in the State of Maharashtra
under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.10 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;

AND whereas, the Collector Raigad has issued revised certificate under Scheduled
Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 for
8.9440 ha. forest land mention in Schedule given below;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that out of proposed
9.0400 ha. forest land only 8.9440 ha (0.0960 ha. area excluded for the road) area consisting
of Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter
under reference No. 1, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai
for Establishment of Medicinal Tree Plantation and establishment of cultivation facility
project at Village- Donvat, Taluka- Khalapur, District- Raigad in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 21.07.2023 and dated 22.09.2023 of Integrated Regional Office, MoEF&CC,
Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. As per the revised FRA certificate of the Collector, Raigad, the extent of the area
excluded for the road (0.0960 ha.) should be demarcated by the Deputy Conservator of
Forests, Alibag with the help of the concerned Land Records Officer and the project
authority. The map of the demarcated area should be submitted to the Government of
Maharashtra in due course. User Agency should obtain a final certificate from the
Collector, Raigad that no any claims of forest rights holders on the proposed area other
than the 0.0960 ha. are pending and submit the same to the State Government in due
course. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Alibag will hand over actual possession of land
to the User Agency after fulfilment of above conditions.
2. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’ and
‘final approval’ letters of Integrated Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred
above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of
the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State,
Nagpur from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also
to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in
the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or
create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area
for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Integrated Regional Office,
MoEF&CC letters dated 21.07.2023 and dated 22.09.2023, in this order and Rules &
Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980,
shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and
action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of
Forests (T), Thane shall report through the State Government to the Integrated Regional
Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
