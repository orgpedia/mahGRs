Government's decision:
The preliminary works of the proposed zoo at Gorewada under the head (4406-3858) under the decision of the Government of India, subject to certain conditions, will be completed at a cost of Rs. 939.91 lakh has been approved. The following conditions have been removed.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds for the preliminary works of the proposed zoo at Gorewada in Nagpur district only after ensuring that the requisite viability gap funding from the Government has not been released earlier.
