Government's decision:
94 temporary posts on land acquisition establishment in Aurangabad Division were sanctioned from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023. As there is a requirement of 94 temporary posts on the land acquisition establishment in Aurangabad division, these temporary posts will be given to the land owners. 01.03.2023 to Dy.
For extension up to 31.08.2023, the office of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad vide Reference No. A letter of 2 is requested.
Department of Finance Reference No. As per the order dated 01.03.2023, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. As per the decision of the Government, the existing 94 temporary posts mentioned in Annexure A, which have been created after the approval of the previous review, but have not been included in the Annexure, are being approved for extension from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
(i) The employees in the posts which have been decided as surplus have been absorbed in the vacant posts under the department or transferred to the Additional Cadre Cell in the Finance Department.
2) None of the above 94 temporary posts are vacant for a period of more than 6 months and the said posts are included in the review of temporary posts.
3) The cost of salary for these temporary posts should be met from the sanctioned grant.
4) All these posts should be considered as temporary in the permanent cadre of that department.
5) These posts will be eligible for dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the orders issued by the government from time to time.
