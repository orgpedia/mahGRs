The Proposal:
The 43 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims in Aurangabad Division were approved for extension from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 under Government Decision Revenue and Forest Department No. EST-2021/ PR No. 27/ R-3, dated 04/03/2021.
Department of Finance As per the Government Decision dated 10/08/2021, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up all the existing temporary posts in their establishment and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control. 01/09/2021 to Th. The extension has been granted till 28/02/2022. Department of Finance A total of 43 temporary posts sanctioned in Aurangabad Division as per the figure fixed by the Government Decision dated 28/01/2010 have been sanctioned. 01/09/2021 to Th. The matter of extension for the current year up to 28/02/2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The 43 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project oustees in the office of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Rehabilitation Office at Aurangabad/ Beed/ Jalna/ Parbhani/ Nanded/ Osmanabad and Latur are being allowed to continue from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 subject to the following conditions.
(1) All posts shall be deemed to be temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to the holders of these posts as per rules.

Out of the expenditure for the financial year 2021-2022 on these 43 posts, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 7 posts in the office of the Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5 Main Accounts Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare (03) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, Commissioner Aurangabad (03) (06) (2235 1924) (Mandatory) (Dattamat)" and the expenditure under the same head should be spent from the grant sanctioned for the year 2021-2022. Further, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 36 posts on rehabilitation establishments in the Collectorates should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5, Main Accounts Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, (04) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, Commissioner Aurangabad (04) (06) (2235 1989) (Mandatory) (Dattamat)" and it should be shared under the same head from the sanctioned grant for the year 2021-2022. The expenditure under these schemes should be finally transferred to the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation.
