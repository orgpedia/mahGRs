Introduction: In the case of sudden natural disasters in the state, it takes a lot of time to distribute relief to the victims after receiving proposals through panchnamas. Therefore, to enable the distribution of assistance to the families of the deceased and the injured in case of human loss in natural disasters, the government was considering to approve the collection of money from the treasury at the rate of the State Disaster Response Fund under the head 2245 0155 and in case of loss of livestock under the head 2245 0315.
Government Decision: Since it is necessary to disburse immediate relief to the families of the deceased in case of loss of human life and distribution of relief to the injured, loss of livestock, etc. in natural disasters, it is being approved to draw the amount from the treasury on minus (-) authorization letter under the following head of accounts to meet the expenses of the financial year 2023-24. This provision shall apply to all items of expenditure under this head.
(a) Relief to the victims of natural calamities (as per the norms of State Disaster Response Fund). C-6, Principal Account Head-2245- Financial Assistance for Relief of Natural Calamities, 02 Floods, Cyclones, etc. (91) Expenditure as per the State Disaster Response Fund Standards, (91) (01) Cash Allowance, Assistance to the family of deceased and assistance to the injured, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) (22450155)
b) Loss of Livestock in Natural Calamities (as per the norms of State Disaster Response Fund). C-6, Principal Account Head- 2245- Financial assistance for relief of natural calamities, 02 floods, cyclones, etc. 117 Assistance to farmers for purchase of livestock, (91) Expenditure as per State Disaster Response Fund norms, (91) (01) Assistance to small and marginal farmers for purchase of livestock in lieu of dead animals, 31 Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried) (2245 0315)
The assistance to be given to the victims in case of loss of human life/ loss of livestock etc. in natural calamities should be given to the authority as per the standard of State Disaster Response Fund as per the Government Decision dated 27.3.2023 of the Department of Revenue and Forest, on account head 2245 0155 and account head 2245 0315.
