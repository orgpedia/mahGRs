GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 32 temporary posts in the Land Records Cell have been extended till 28.2.2022 as per the Government Decision of Reference No. 2. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide Reference No. Since the extension of these posts has expired under Section 4, the extension of these posts has been requested in the year 2022-23.
As per the Government Decision dated 22.02.2022 of the Department of Finance, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under them till 31.08.2022. Accordingly, 32 temporary posts as indicated in the attached prospectus are being extended for the period from 1.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 subject to the following terms and conditions:-
a) The 32 temporary posts are not vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The said 32 temporary posts are in the Land Records Office and the same are being filled up in the Forest Department under Review of Posts vide Reference No. 1 It must be included in the designations approved by the governing decision.
The expenditure on this post shall be met from the grants to be sanctioned in the financial year 2022-23 under the headings 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation and Development, 101 (Two) Survey of Forest, Jamabandi and Simankan (2406 0102).
The said Government Decision is being issued by the Finance Department as per the authority conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Decision No. Designation-2016/ P. No. 8/16/ APC dated 22.02.2022.
