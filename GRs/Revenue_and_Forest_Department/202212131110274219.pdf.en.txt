Damage caused by the disaster
Committee to be set up to use modern technology to conduct survey
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Revenue and Forest (Relief and Rehabilitation)
Government Resolution No:- APR-2022/ P.S. No.314/ APR-1
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 13th of December 2022
Reference:- State Executive Committee The minutes of the meeting dated 25.11.2022.
Government Decision: As it has been observed from time to time that while responding to various disasters occurring in the State, the prevailing methodology leads to errors in estimating the exact damage and delays in providing immediate relief to the victims, it has been decided to use modern tools to make the process more objective. Therefore, the instructions have been given to form a committee to expedite the survey and panchnama of the damage after the disaster and to examine the usefulness and feasibility of the proposals received to survey the damage to crops and other properties in the disaster using advanced technology.
