GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: FLD-2316/ PR No. 101/ F-10
The Government of India has recommended to the Government of India to divert 0.3095 hectares (Radhanagari Sanctuary Area) of protected forest area under Section-2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980; and accordingly, the Government of India, after considering the proposal, has accorded in-principle approval to the proposal submitted by the State Government under Section-2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 vide letter A. No.3 above. Final approval has been given for the diversion vide letter No. 5 dated 11/02/2021 of the Central Government; and accordingly, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station House Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide their letter No. 6 dated 11/02/2021 of the Central Government has sanctioned the project.
The Government of Maharashtra hereby directs the Project Authority i.e. Water Engineer, City Water Supply and Sewerage Department, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur to take steps to lay underground drinking water pipeline as well as 33/11 KV underground water supply pipeline under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
For laying the transmission line, a total of 0.3095 hectares of forest land, as mentioned in the project proposal attached with the letter of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forest and Central Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur in Kolhapur district and mentioned in the schedule below, which includes protected forest land, should be diverted for this proposal. This diversion should be completed subject to fulfillment of the terms and conditions in the letters dated 12/05/2016 and 11/02/2021 of the Government of India and also after fulfillment of the following conditions.
The Project Mechanism shall comply with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India mentioned above. The Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Sahyadri Tiger Reserve, Kolhapur, assures that the project machinery will strictly comply with all the conditions.
