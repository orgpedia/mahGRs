As per the Government Decision dated 22.02.2022 of the Department of Finance, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under them till 31.08.2022. Accordingly, 33 temporary posts as indicated in the above prospectus are being extended for the period from 1.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The said 33 temporary posts shall not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) These 33 temporary posts are in the Land Records Office and will be filled up after the revision of the posts in the Forest Department vide reference No. 2 It must be included in the designations approved by the governing decision.
(3) In the present case, when the posts created for mangrove management, protection and conservation are filled up through external sources, the expenditure on honorarium on the said establishment shall be met from the grants available under the head 2406-Forestation and Wildlife (2406 7223), 101 (11) Forest Conservation and Development, (11) (32) 13 Central Assistance for Management of Forests as per the recommendations of the Central Finance Commission (CFC). However, at the time when these posts are to be filled up by regular officers/ employees, their salary and allowances shall be paid from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2021-22 under the head of the head Accounts- 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan) 101 Forest Conservation and Development (01) (01) Administration and Protection (2406 0096).
The said Government Decision is being issued by the Finance Department as per the authority conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Decision No. Designation-2016/ P. No. 8/16/ APC dated 22.02.2022.
