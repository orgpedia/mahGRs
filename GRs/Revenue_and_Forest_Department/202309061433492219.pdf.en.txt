The concerned officers should be careful to join the place of posting during the period. In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
6. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
If they do not join the post immediately as per the said order or try to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
