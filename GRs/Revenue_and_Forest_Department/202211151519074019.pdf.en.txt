Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion of 0.1731 ha. Forest land in favour of Shri Gopal Malpani,
Chandrapur for construction of Petrol Pump at Village- Sarangpur in Chandrapur District;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letters under reference No.2 has
submitted proposal to Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Govt. of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
under reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0.1731 ha. Forest
Land for the above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.4 has
submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded
# Page 2
Government Order No.: FLD-2020/C.R.200/F-10
'Final approval' to divert the 0.1731 ha. Forest land in favour of Shri Gopal Malpani,
Chandrapur for construction of Petrol Pump at Village - Sarangpur in Chandrapur District, under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.1731 ha consisting of Reserve Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letters under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Shri Gopal Malpani, Chandrapur for
construction of Petrol Pump at Village - Sarangpur, in Chandrapur District.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 04.05.2022 and dated 24.08.2022 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the
principle' and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Chandrapur shall ensure
the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 04.05.2022 and dated 24.08.2022, in this order and
Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall
amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator
of Forests (T), Chandrapur shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
