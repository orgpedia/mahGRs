The proposal-
The rates and criteria of assistance to be given to the disaster affected persons in natural disasters were fixed for the period from 2015 to 2020 under the Government Resolution No. 3. The central government has included natural disasters such as cyclone, earthquake, fire, flood, tsunami, hailstorm, landslide, snow avalanche, locust swarm, drought, cloudburst and severe cold, pest attack. The rates have also been introduced by the state government for calamities such as untimely rain, excessive rain (including loss to farmers), accidental fire, sea surge, lightning. The process of revising the rates and criteria of this assistance is underway at the central government level. However, the rates and criteria of assistance applied under the Government Decision at Reference No. 3 are currently in place. Apart from this, the rates and criteria of increased assistance in the event of natural disasters from time to time are implemented by the state government.

A proposal was submitted to the cabinet to form a permanent cabinet sub-committee to decide on such proposals so that immediate relief can be provided to the disaster victims affected by natural disaster events. In pursuance of the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting dated 29.5.2018, the Permanent Cabinet Sub-Committee has been constituted vide Government Resolution No. 4 dated 13th June, 2018. The Cabinet Sub-Committee has been vested with full powers under the Government Resolution dated 5th December, 2018 at Reference No.4 to take immediate measures/ control/ decision for the drought period including the drought period of 2018, in case of any drought in future. Cabinet meeting held on 6th March, 2020. On receipt of directions regarding constitution of various Committees under the Chairmanship of the Minister in accordance with seniority, the said Cabinet Sub-Committee was reconstituted vide Government Resolution dated 24.06.2020. Now that a new government has been formed in the state, the matter of reconstituting the said cabinet sub-committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
The Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted under the Government Resolution, Revenue and Forest Department, No: Scarcity-2014/ PR No. 233/ M-7, dated 13th June, 2018 to take decisions on providing immediate relief to the disaster victims affected by natural calamities has been reconstituted and the Government approves the constitution of a permanent Cabinet Sub-Committee as amended as follows.
