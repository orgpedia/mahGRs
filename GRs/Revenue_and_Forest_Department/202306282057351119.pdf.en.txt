Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave application of the officer transferred by the parent office should be accepted. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned officer at the original address by post. If the leave application of the transferred officer is sent to the parent office, the application will not be taken into consideration.
