Government Decision: The Government has decided to fill up a total of 64 posts in the Gosikhurd Special Cell created on the rehabilitation establishment under the Special Cell set up for early allocation, survey and implementation of the special economic package approved to the project victims under the Gosikhurd National Project. as on 15.9.2021. The period was extended from 01.9.2021 to 28.2.2022.
2. Under the special financial package announced for Gosikhurd project, the work of subsidy allocation and rehabilitation is going on in Nagpur and Bhandara districts and the work of package allocation and rehabilitation is still pending. In order to complete the remaining work, a total of 37 posts mentioned in the next head of Gosikhurd Special Cell will be filled from 01.3.2022. Extension up to 31.8.2022 (6 months).
