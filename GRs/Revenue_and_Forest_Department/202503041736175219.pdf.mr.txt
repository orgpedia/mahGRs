This 'In-principle' approval is issued subject to the following conditions:-
1) The legal status of the land shall remain unchanged.
2) User Agency shall obtain Wildlife Clearance, Prior to commencement of work.
3) The User Agency will submit the Records of Rights of proposed forest land,
Maps, Tree Cutting Estimate and Technical Approval of the proposed diversion,
along with Stage-I Compliance.
4) The demarcation of the proposed forest area shall be carried out at suitable
places as per the direction of the DCF concerned at the cost of User Agency.
5) Compensatory Afforestation:-
a) Compensatory afforestation shall be taken up by the Forest Department over
4.00 ha. degraded forest land at Compt. No.574, Survey No. 38/1 in Village-
Tadgaon, Beat- Tadgaon, Round-Tadgaon in Gadchiroli District at the cost of
the User Agency;
b) The cost of compensatory afforestation at the prevailing wage rates as per
compensatory afforestation scheme and the cost of survey, demarcation and erection of permanent pillars if required on the CA land shall be deposited in
advance with the Forest Department by the project authority;
6) Net Present Value:-
a) The Forest Department shall charge the Net Present Value (NPV) for the 2.00 ha. Forest land to be diverted under this proposal from the User Agency as per the orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India dated 30/10/2002, 01/08/2003, 28/03/2008, 24/04/2008 and 09/05/2008 in IA
No.566 in WP (C) No.202/1995 and as per the guidelines issued by the Ministry vide letters No.5-1/1998-FC (71.11), dated 18/09/2003 as well as
letter No.5-2/2006-FC, dated 03/10/2006 and 5-3/2007-FC, dated
05/02/2009 & File No.5-3/2011-FC(Vol-D, Dated 06/01/2022 in this
regard.
b) Additional amount of the NPV of the diverted forest land, if any, becoming due after finalization of the same by the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India on receipt of the report from the Expert Committee, shall be charged from the User Agency. The User Agency shall furnish an undertaking to this effect.
7) All the funds received from the User Agency under the project shall be transferred/ deposited to CAMPA _ fund only through - e-portal
(https://parivehs.nic.in);
8) The complete compliance of the FRA, 2006 shall be ensured by way of prescribed certificate from the concerned District Collector.
9) No labour camp shall be established on the forest land and the User Agency shall provide firewood preferably alternate fuels to the labourers and the staff
working at the site so as to avoid any damage and pressure on the nearby forest
areas;

10) No additional or new path will be constructed inside the forest area for the
transportation of construction materials for execution of the project.
11) The User Agency shall ensure that because of this project, no damage 15 caused
to the wildlife available in the area;
12) The user agency shall ensure that any tree felling shall be done only when it is unavoidable and that too under strict supervision of the State Fores Department;
13) The user agency in consultation with the concerned Deputy Conservator of forest shall create and maintain alternate habitat/home for the avifauna, whose
nesting trees are to be cleared in this project. Bird's nests artificially made out of
eco-friendly material shall be used in the area, including forest area and human settlements, adjoining the forest area being diverted for the project; if required.
14) The total forest area utilized for this project shall not exceed 2 ha. and the forest
area diverted shall not be used for any purpose other than those shown in the diversion proposal. The User Agency shall furnish an undertaking to this effect;
15) The cost of felling of trees shall be deposited by the User Agency with the State
Forest Department;
16) User Agency shall obtain Environment Clearance as per the provisions of the Environmental (Protection) Act, 1986, 1f applicable;
17) Sufficient firewood, preferably the alternate fuel, shall be provided by the User
Agency to the labourers after purchasing the same from the State Forest
Department or the Forest development Corporation or any other legal source of alternate fuel;
18) The User Agency shall ensure compliance to all acts, rules, regulations and
guidelines of the Ministry for the time being ॥ force, as applicable to the subject
project;
19) The layout plan of the proposal shall not be changed without prior approval of
State Government (Forest Department).
20) The period of diversion under this approval shall be co-terminus with the period
of lease to be granted in favour of the user agency or the project life, whichever is less.
21) The forest land shall not be used for any purpose other than that specified in the
project proposal.
22) The forest land proposed to be diverted shall under no circumstances be
transferred to any other agencies, department or person without prior approval of the State and Central Government.
23) This sanction is subject to the final clearance by the competent authorities for
the project.
