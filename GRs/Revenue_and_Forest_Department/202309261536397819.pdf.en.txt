GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 02.22/ PS 63/ F-1
As per the powers conferred to the concerned administrative departments under the Government Resolution No. 6 of the Finance Department, 150 temporary posts created under the Biodiversity Conservation Programme were approved to be continued in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format have been provided to the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 8 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government's decision:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 8 of the Finance Department, Government is approving the continuation of 150 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme for the year 2023-2024 from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The expenditure on establishment of 150 temporary posts created under the Biodiversity Conservation Programme under the head" 2406 Afforestation and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
