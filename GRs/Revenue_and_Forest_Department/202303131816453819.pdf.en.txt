The Proposal:
The Government has accorded approval for continuation of 111 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme of Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Tiger Reserve Melghat during the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for continuation of 104 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Tiger Reserve Melghat from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 6 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 6, the Government is approving the continuation of 104 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme of Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Tiger Reserve Melghat from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 104 temporary posts created under the scheme titled" Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Tiger Reserve Melghat," shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024 under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)."
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08.02.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
