The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22, a total amount of Rs. 103800.00 lakh has been budgeted. Finance Department Reference No. Guidelines have been issued for disbursement of funds in 2021-22 under Government Decisions 1, 3, 5 and 7.
As per Reference No. 8, 9 and 10, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has sanctioned Rs. 36389.37 lakh has been submitted to the Government for approval and further decision is being taken by the Government.

Government's decision:
In view of the decision of the Government in Reference No.7 of the Department of Finance, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, in view of the demand for funds made under Reference No. 8, 9 and 10, under the heading of Community Solid Forestry Programme (4406 0492), Rs. 18736.84 lakhs is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions: (Rs. million).

The amount to be spent on this scheme should be met from the funds received through the budget distribution system in the year 2021-22 under the head heading 4406 Afforestation and Wildlife, Capital Expenditure on Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development and Regeneration, (00) (06) 02, Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Protection of Wildlife, (00) (06) Solid Afforestation Programme (Programme) (4406 0492) at the community level.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. Care should be taken to implement the work to be taken under this scheme as per the action plan, prescribed procedures and within the stipulated time.
Detailed guidelines in this regard should be given to the field officers and the implementation should be reviewed from time to time. The funds will be spent on approved projects. Funds should be disbursed only after ensuring that technical and administrative approval is given to the proposed works under the scheme. Controlling authorities should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned funds.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development) will submit the utilization certificate regarding the funds disbursed under the scheme in the year 2021-22 to the government within 7 days.
Before disbursing the funds, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development) shall ensure that the funds allocated for the scheme for the year 2021-22 are not diverted to any other place other than the budgetary distribution system. The disbursement of funds should be made subject to these conditions.
