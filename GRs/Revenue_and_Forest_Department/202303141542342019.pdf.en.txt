Preamble: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 18/08/2022 of the Finance Department, 15 temporary posts outside the sanctioned pattern for cleaning work in the Administrative Building of Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan were extended for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 under the Government Decision dated 03/10/2022 in the context of Revenue and Forest Department.
2. Under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to extend 15 temporary posts outside the sanctioned figure for cleaning work in the Konkan Bhavan Administrative Building for the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, has issued orders for the cleaning of the Konkan Bhavan Administrative Building. 02 The 15 temporary posts in the prospectus attached with the Government decision herein are being extended for the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 subject to the conditions contained in the Government decision dated 08/02/2023 in the context of Department of Finance.
