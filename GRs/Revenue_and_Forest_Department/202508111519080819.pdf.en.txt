It is being announced.
1- The said "Virtual Personal Deposit Account" shall be subject to audit.
2- The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said "Virtual Personal Deposit Account."
No amount shall be transferred to the 'Virtual Personal Deposit Account' to avoid lapses of funds.
4- "Virtual Personal Deposit Account" is applicable only for the State Schemes and not for Central Sector and Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
5- The Receipt to the said "Virtual Personal Deposit Account" shall be only such grants drawn under MTR 44 for the object heads mentioned in the Finance Department GR dated 14.02.2024. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate after incoming expenditure from the 'Virtual Personal Deposit Account'.
6 - The expenditure from the "Virtual Personal Deposit Account" shall be as per the sanction order of the respective grant.
7- The balances under the "Virtual Personal Deposit Account" shall be reconciled monthly by the Administrator with the District Treasury Officer.
8- The balances shall be reconciled with A.G. Office by the District Treasury Officer who shall submit Balance Certificate to A.G. Office for confirmation of their balances in the books of A.G. Office at the end of the Financial Year.
9- Monthly Plus Minus Memo related to this "Virtual Personal Deposit Account shall be sent to the A.G. Office.
10 - The validity of Virtual Personal Deposit Account will be till 31/03/2026 .The unspent balances under the 'Virtual Personal Deposit Account' shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund on or before 31 March 2026. Further extension of authority for Virtual Personal Deposit Account can only be allowed with the concurrence of the Principal Accountant General (A & E).
11- All the conditions / instructions in the Financial Department Narrow / 2022 / Q. 110/2023 Treasuries - 5 dated 14-02-2024 shall be followed.
All rules including Rule 495 of Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 shall be applicable to the 'Virtual Personal Deposit Account'.
