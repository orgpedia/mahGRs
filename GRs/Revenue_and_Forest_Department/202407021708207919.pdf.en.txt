Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to Deputy Director General
of Forests (Central), Government of India, Ministry of Environment & Forests, Regional
Office (WCZ), Nagpur for diversion of 0.479 ha. Mangrove (Reserve Forest) and Mangrove
(mudflat) land in favour of Maharashtra Maritime Board for construction of Kharwadeshri
Approach Jetty and Sloping Berthing Jetty and Approach road facilitated to RO-RO Jetty in
Village Jalsar & Kharmendnri, Tal. & District Palghar in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.2, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0.479 ha. The Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the Government of India;

AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no.4 Accorded 'Final approval' to
divert the of .0479 ha of Mangrove (Reserve forest) and Mangrove (Mudflat) land in favour
of Maharashtra Maritime Board for construction of Kharwadeshri Approach Jetty and Sloping
Berthing Jetty and Approach road facilitated to RO-RO Jetty in Village Jalsar & Kharmendri,
Tal. & District Palghar in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam

AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.5 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.479 ha.
consisting of Mangrove (Reserve Forest) and Mangrove (Mudflat) Land more specifically
described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Maharashtra Maritime
Board for construction of Kharwadeshri Approach Jetty and Sloping Berthing Jetty and
Approach road facilitated to RO-RO Jetty in Village Jalsar & Kharmendri, Tal. & District
Palghar in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 22.10.2020 and dated 06.01.2024 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. If the number of Trees / Mangrove trees to be felled increases during implementation
of the project, user agency shall obtain prior permission from Central Government and
Hon'ble High Court accordingly.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 22.10.2020 and dated 06.01.2024, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall
amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and
action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director
General of Forests (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
