The Land Records Department has received complaints regarding the tampering of original records of the Land Records Department by fabricating the maps of the boundary permanent enumeration under the office of Borivali G. Mumbai Suburban and Deputy Superintendent, Land Records, Mumbai Suburban. The Leader of Opposition, Maharashtra Legislative Council and other Members have given notice under Rule 101 of the Maharashtra Legislative Council (Winter) Session 2021. There were 340 participants. During the discussion on this topic, Shri. The Minister (Revenue) had announced an inquiry into the entire matter by the Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune. Accordingly, a committee headed by the Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune, was constituted to investigate the matter. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Municipal Geo-statutory Officer, Goregaon Sub-Division Mauje Erangel, Ta. The following committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of the Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, Pune to investigate the entire matter of tampering with the original records of the Land Records Department by preparing maps of the boundary permanent enumeration under the office of Borivali G. Mumbai Suburban and Deputy Superintendent, Land Records, Mumbai Suburban.

The mandate of the said Committee: 1) Scrutiny of 884 boundary count maps under the Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Mumbai Suburban, present in the notice, how many of them have been tampered with. Submit the report.
2) To investigate and submit a report on the cases of unauthorized changes in the original records in the records under the Town Geology Goregaon.
(3) To investigate the construction of buildings with or without permission by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation or other authorities on the basis of fake records/ maps of both the above places.
4) To fix the responsibility of the officers and employees responsible for the irregularities in the above 1, 2, and 3 and to report the action to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation/ other authorities as necessary.
5) To recommend action to be taken against the construction of the building on the basis of fake and false records.
(6) To take steps to prevent recurrence of such incidents in future.
The committee should investigate the matter thoroughly and submit a self-explanatory report to the government within a month.
