As per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021, a total of 6 sedentary workers have been issued release certificates, 5 in Ahmednagar district and 1 in Nashik district.
As immediate financial assistance of Rs.30,000/- each is payable to the above 6 released bonded labourers under the provisions of Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021, the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik has issued orders vide No. Letters 1 to 3 have asked the government to provide funds. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
As per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021, a total of 6 bonded labourers, 5 in Ahmednagar district and 1 in Nashik district, have been issued release certificates.
The Government is approving disbursement of an amount of Rs. 1,80,000/- each to the 6 freed bonded labourers for immediate financial assistance of Rs.30,000/- each under the provisions of Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021.
