Preamble- The Government has approved the policy for undertaking afforestation by entering into a tripartite agreement on participation of private and non-governmental organizations for reforestation of degraded forest areas vide Government Resolution dated 23/09/2011.
M. Dynamix Vacation Resort Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Forest Department for afforestation of 10.662 hectares of degraded forest area. The proposal was placed before the high-powered committee by the additional principal chief conservator of forest and central officer, Nagpur, and the member secretary, high-powered committee. The proposal has been approved by the high-powered committee.
The Government Order Notwithstanding the approval of the High Powered Committee, the Government is now issuing an order that in accordance with the proposal submitted by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, Nagpur, the Government has issued a notification to the effect. As per the proposal submitted vide letter dated 17.05.2022, the following degraded forest areas are proposed to be covered. Dynamix Vacation Resort Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai is an industrial enterprise. The government has approved the project to plant trees on a total area of 10.662 hectares in a tripartite agreement between Hiranandani Foundation, Mumbai, a non-governmental organization, and the Deputy Conservator of Forests, Alibag Forest Department.
