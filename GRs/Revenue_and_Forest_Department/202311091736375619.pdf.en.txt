As per reference number 1, rehabilitation of villages affected by heavy rains in 1983 has been approved. Accordingly, the proposal to grant administrative approval to the civil facility works of construction of internal CC roads and drains in the flood rehabilitated villages submitted by the Collector under Nanded Reference 8 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The total cost of the following civic amenity works in the flood rehabilitated villages of Naigaon, Umri and Loha in Nanded district is Rs. Administrative Sanction for the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 4570.00 lakhs (Rupees Forty Five Crores Seventy Lakhs only) is being accorded subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The responsibility of recovering the minor mineral ownership rights proposed to be included in the civil works will be of the concerned operational machinery.
2) Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department No. GTN 1083/217/ R-12, Dt. 10-02-1983, Government Decision No. RCPA 2008/ Q.No.550/ R-1, D. 1-08-2009 and Government Circular No. RPA 111/ Q.No.18/ R-1, Dy. To provide civic amenities in the rehabilitated/ extended villages under 28-03-2011

The concerned Executive Engineer will be responsible for carrying out the work according to the prescribed physical standards.
(3) The Superintending Engineer and the Executive Engineer concerned shall take proper care that the expenditure incurred on the proposed civic amenity works shall not exceed the limit prescribed in the approved budgets.
(4) The concerned Superintending Engineer and the concerned Executive Engineer of the concerned executing agency shall ensure that the works indicated in the budget for the proposed civic amenities works shall be carried out in accordance with the prescribed procedures as per the conditions of technical and administrative approval.
5) The concerned Executive Engineer shall, after completion of the proposed civic amenities in all respects and obtaining certificate from the District Collector, Nanded, accordingly, transfer the same to the concerned Zilla Parishad and inform the Government Rehabilitation Department through the District Collector, Nanded/ Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad in this regard.
(6) As per the rules in force in the Maharashtra Public Works Department, the technical approval of the Superintending Engineer/ Executive Engineer of the concerned executing agency should be provided to the budgets of the said civic amenities works.
7) The civic amenities provided under the rehabilitation scheme will not be restored from any other scheme. The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector/ Chief Executive Officer, Executive Engineer (Construction), Public Works Department as well as the concerned agencies should ensure that these works are not included in other schemes and that no expenditure has been incurred on these works.
2. Tender Process and Commissioning Order: The Executive Engineer (Construction), Public Works Department, Deglur, Nanded and Bhokar G. Nanded shall initiate the tendering process of the proposed civic amenity works immediately by fulfilling the conditions in the Administrative Sanction Order presented. Also, after the tender process is completed, the work of civic amenities should be started immediately by giving the work order for the tender received.
3. Executive Engineer, Public Works, Deglur, Nanded and Bhokar G.
Nanded said. Report on the progress of civil works/ expenditure etc., initiated as mentioned in clause (2) and the expenditure as planned for completion of these works during the financial year 2023-24 as per the progress of the work, be submitted to the Government under the head" Demand No.C-6, Head of Accounts Head 2245- Assistance for Prevention of Natural Calamities, 02- Floods, Cyclones, etc., 101 Anugrah Assistance (93) Others (93) (05) Expenditure on Roads, Water Supply, Schools, Quarries, Electricity Supply, Civil Facilities (Compulsory) (Dattamat) (22450988), 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) for rehabilitation of persons affected by floods." Accordingly, for the financial year 2023-24, the necessary funds from the sanctioned and available allocation will be made available to the concerned implementing agency through the Divisional Commissioner, Sabhajinagar (Aurangabad) on the budgetary allocation system. Pending Utilization Certificates should be submitted on priority basis in respect of funds disbursed for the said facility works.
