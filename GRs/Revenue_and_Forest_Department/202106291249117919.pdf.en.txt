The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for Divyangjan as per notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published on the Central Government website. As per the Central Government notification (on the lines of Central Government), instructions regarding ensuring posts for Divyangjan in State Government establishments are issued by the Department of Government Decisions, Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. 02.02.2021. In view of these suggestions, the matter of ensuring posts for the disabled for the posts of Group A to Group-D cadre on the establishment of the Land Records Department under the Revenue and Forest Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. Notification dated 04.01.2021 and Government Decision, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. Considering the suggestions given under 02.02.2021, a proposal for ensuring posts for Divyangjan in the category of Group-A to Group-D in the Land Records Department under the Revenue and Forest Department was submitted to the Expert Committee. On the recommendation of the Expert Committee, the posts for the disabled are being ensured as indicated in the accompanying letter.
(2) The said Government Decision, under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. is being issued vide Notification dated 23.06.2021.
