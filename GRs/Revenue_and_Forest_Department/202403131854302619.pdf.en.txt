4. The officers concerned shall take care to join the place of posting within the prescribed period as per the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, non-acceptance of service and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981 or else their absence from the place of posting shall be deemed to be "non-working day" (& 8570).
