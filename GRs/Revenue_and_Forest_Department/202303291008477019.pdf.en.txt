Terms and Conditions
The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati should immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned administrative machinery through BEAMS, the budget disbursement system.
2) The concerned executing agency should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Amaravati and Collector, Amaravati.
(3) The funds sanctioned and received under this order shall be spent for the same purpose for which the sanctioned civic amenities have been constructed. Under no circumstances will the expenditure be more than the sanctioned grant while spending this fund and the work will be completed within the stipulated time. Also, the concerned executive body should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
(4) The concerned executing agency shall ensure that no funds received under the head Main Account mentioned in the said Order shall remain unutilized or the funds received under the said Order shall be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned agency.
(5) The Controlling Officer concerned shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure under the head of main accounts mentioned in the said order and submit the same to the Accountant General as per the prevailing accounting system of the Government. Accordingly, the work of reconciliation of expenditure under this head, appropriation accounts, etc. should be done by the concerned as per the prevailing practice.
6) The expenditure statements/ reports in this regard should be submitted to the government without fail. The expenditure reconciliation statements should be submitted to the government without fail with the Reconciliation Slip of the Accountant General's Office.
7) The concerned executing agency should immediately submit the Utilization Certificate of the funds disbursed under this order to the Accountant General's Office, the concerned Collector/ Divisional Commissioner/ Government.
