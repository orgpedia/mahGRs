The above mentioned officer is being promoted on ad-hoc/ temporary basis subject to the following conditions:
a) Vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota will be filled only on a temporary basis. The Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. The General Administration Department/ 16-B has issued a notification regarding the filling up under 28306/2017. Approval is being accorded as per instructions issued vide letter dated 29/12/2017.
(b) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, as a result of this temporary promotion, the officer in the selection list shall not be entitled to any regularisation or extension of service.
(c) The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officer without any prior notice or notice.
(d) The said promotion shall be made by the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai/ Nagpur/ Aurangabad as well as by the Hon. High Court/ High Court. If any claim/ petition regarding seniority is filed from the cadre of Auxiliary Forestry Group-B or Forestry Group-A in the Supreme Court etc., the same shall be subject to the final disposal of the pending petition.
The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting. If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, then they are not interested in the promotion, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, D. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled. Also, if the promotion is denied by the said officer, the 12/24 years benefit of Forest Statistical (Group-A) post under Assured Progress Scheme should be stopped immediately.
5. Promoted officers should be given the option of fixation of pay immediately on taking charge of the post of Forest Officer (Group-A). If such an option is not given, their salary shall be determined in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, Rule No. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
