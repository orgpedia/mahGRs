Before disbursement of the said funds, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, shall ensure that the funds allocated for the year 2021-22 for the said scheme are not diverted to other places other than the budgetary distribution system.
