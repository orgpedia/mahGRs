Introduction: In the case of sudden natural disasters in the state, it takes a lot of time to distribute relief to the victims after receiving proposals through panchnamas. Therefore, in case of loss of property (clothes, household utensils/ articles) in natural calamities, the amount is drawn from the treasury of minus (-) authority under head 2245 0155 so as to enable immediate relief from the State Disaster Response Fund to the affected/ displaced families. Similarly, to enable immediate disbursement of relief from the State exchequer at the prescribed rate, the Government was considering to give approval to the Collector to draw the amount from the treasury on minus (-) authorization letter under head of accounts 2245 2194.
Government's decision:
As immediate relief is required to be disbursed to the affected/ displaced families in case of loss of property (clothing, household utensils/ articles) in natural calamities, it is being approved to draw the amount from the treasury on minus (-) authorization letter under the following head of accounts to meet the expenditure for the financial year 2022-23.
a) Damage to property (clothes, household utensils/ articles). C-6, Principal Account Head-2245- Financial Assistance for Relief of Natural Calamities, 02 Floods, etc., 101, Ex-gratia assistance, (92) Expenditure over and above the State Disaster Response Fund Standard, (92) (01) Cash Allowance Assistance to the family of the deceased and assistance to the injured, 31 Subsidiary Grants (2245 2194) 2. Reference No. 2 is mentioned here. Under the Government decision dated 22.8.2022, the families affected by natural calamities during the current season from June to October, 2022 and migrated will be given financial assistance of Rs. 5,000/- for loss of clothes and Rs. 5,000/- for damage to household utensils/ articles. Of this, Rs. 2,500/- and Rs. Rs 2,500. The amount of Rs.5,000/- is admissible as per the State Disaster Response Fund norms and the expenditure is made from 2245 0155. The remaining Rs. 2,500/- and Rs. Rs 2,500/-. 5,000/- per family should be deducted from the State Disaster Response Fund standard in addition to the accounting head 2245 2194.
