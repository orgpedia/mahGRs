The powers to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 were delegated to the concerned administrative departments under the Government Resolution of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department. As per the Government Resolution of Reference No. 7 of the Post-Plan Scheme" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park," 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest at Nagzira Sanctuary was approved for continuation from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2023-2024.
Now, under the decision of the Finance Department, Reference No. 8, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in the pattern of the administrative department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern, from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 8, the Government has approved the continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in the post-plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" in the year 2023-2024 from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
2. Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park. The expenditure on establishment of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary in the post-plan scheme shall be covered under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" with the provision to be made available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 31.08.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
