(a) Department of Finance, Government Resolution, No. VVV-1097/ No. 26/97/ Budget-19, D. Review of the standard revision plans/ programmes of the staff while extending the temporary posts as per 20.12.1997.
(b) The posts which have been sanctioned for a specified period shall be cancelled after the expiry of the said period.
(e) In case of vacancies arising out of retirement or any other reason, such posts shall automatically lapse.
