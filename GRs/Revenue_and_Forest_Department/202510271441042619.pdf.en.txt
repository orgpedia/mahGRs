Government Decision, Revenue A Forest Department, No: Gokhani - 10/0125 Q. No. 05 / B-1, D. The provision mentioned herein in Part-X of the 08.04.2025 - "Control of sand coming from other States" (11) is being repealed and now replaced with the following amended provisions: - Part-X "Control of sand coming from other States": - (1) Only persons / entities who have been granted ATMS / Sand Depot / Storage-VICRO license by the concerned State Administration shall be licensed. For this purpose, sand block / depot holders and stock-sale licensees from other states should apply through the Mahakunya system to the District Collector in the district where the vehicles of the sand bank are going to enter first to sell the sand in the state of Maharashtra. While submitting the online application, it will be mandatory for the applicant to produce a copy of PAN card, Aadhaar HIS, Balughat / Depot / Stock-Vico License Order of that state and the quantity (in tonnes) certified by the concerned Collector for transporting sand to other states every year.
The Collector should examine the application and submit the complete proposal with his / her self-explanatory feedback and complete documentation for the approval of the Government. Once the license is approved by the government, the collector should take the necessary action.
This Government Corrigendum is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
