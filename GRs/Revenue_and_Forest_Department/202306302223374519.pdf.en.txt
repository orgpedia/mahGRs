Date of release: June 30, 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Transfer Act, 2005 and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Mr. Sunil Sherkhane, Tahsildar is being appointed on deputation to the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation for a period of 3 years with the approval of the competent authority in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience.
2. The said order comes into force with immediate effect and Shri Sunil Sherkhane, Tehsildar is being relieved unilaterally from his present posting in accordance with the said order.
(3) The said order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector.
The services of Mr. Sunil Sherkhane, Tehsildar are being handed over to the Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai in accordance with the deputation in the office of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation.
5. Mr. Sunil Sherkhane, Tehsildar to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
