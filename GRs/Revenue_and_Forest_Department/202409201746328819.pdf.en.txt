Government Circular: - As per the policy in force before 1976 as well as the Maharashtra Project Restricted Authority.
Rural plots allotted to eligible project victims as per Rehabilitation Act 1976, 1986 and 1999.
The calculation of occupancy value / possession amount is being clarified as follows.
Allotment to eligible project victims in rehabilitated villages Village plot occupancy / possession amount
The occupancy price should be calculated as follows: -
(1) The possession / possession of a rural plot of land acquired for resettlement by the Government.
The occupancy price should be calculated as follows:
Compensation for acquisition of acquired land + demarcation, improvement, levelling, counting and
The amount of the plot depends on the amount spent on the marking, etc.
However, the amount of the possession shall be calculated in accordance with the Tiger Calculator of the plot concerned.
If the amount is more than that, the possession amount may be calculated according to the Quick Calculator.
(2) The occupancy price of a rural plot of land originally owned by the Government shall be the amount calculated as per the Tiger Calculator of the plot.
