It should be ensured that the subsidy will be spent only on the works mentioned in the proposal which have received technical and administrative approval of the competent authority.
The funds disbursed under the said Government Decision shall be spent within the limits specified in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines laid down by the Finance Department from time to time and in accordance with the provisions of the proposal.
