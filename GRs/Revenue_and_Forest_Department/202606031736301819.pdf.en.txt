Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 28.297 ha. forest land for laying of underground 50 mm to 1600 mm diameter.
P.C.C.F./B.W.S.E./H.D.P.E. type pipeline for agricultural irrigation of command falling under Urmodi Maan irrigation project to drought prone 21 villages, pipeline passing through forest land at Village- Mhaswad, Waki, Valai, Dhamani, Pimpari, Lodhawade, Gondawale
Kh. & Gondawale Bk. Tal. Man, Dist. Satara in the State of Maharashtra.

AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Regional Office (Central) Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 28.297 ha. The forest land for the
above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra & Government of India;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letters under reference No.5 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 6 Accorded 'Final Approval'
to divert the 28.297 ha of forest land in favour of the Executive Engineer, Urmodi Dam
Division, Satara for for laying of underground 50 mm to 1600 mm _ diameter.
P.C.C.F./B.W.S.E./H.D.P.E. type pipeline for agricultural irrigation of command falling
under Urmodi Maan irrigation project to drought prone 21 villages, pipeline passing through forest land at Village- Mhaswad, Waki, Valai, Dhamani, Pimpari, Lodhawade, Gondawale
Kh. & Gondawale Bk. Tal. Man, Dist. Satara in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.7 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 28.297
ha. consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.8. The Executive Engineer, Urmodi Dam Division, Satara for
laying of underground 50 mm to 1600 mm diameter. P.C.C.F./B.W.S.E./H.D.P.E. type
pipeline for agricultural irrigation of command falling under Urmodi Maan irrigation project
to drought prone 21 villages, pipeline passing through forest land at Village- Mhaswad, Waki, Valai, Dhamani, Pimpari, Lodhawade, Gondawale Kh. & Gondawale Bk. Tal. Man,
Dist. Satara in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 10.11.2023 and dated 05.04.2025 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land 1s also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Satara should ensure that fulfillment of condition
No. 11 & vii mentioned in the letter dated 05.04.2025 of Regional Office,
MoEF&CC, Nagpur before giving possession of the forest land to the user agency.
# Page 3
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle' and 'final approval' letters of the Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
3. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MOEF&CC letters dated 10.11.2023 and dated 05.04.2025, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
4. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Kolhapur shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
