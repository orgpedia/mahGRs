Introduction: It has been observed that the flood situation in Mahad city is caused by the accumulation of silt in the Savitri river. The report of the committee appointed by the Irrigation Department states that the purpose of the softening of the river is to remove silt from the river and thereby prevent flood situations. The mechanical department of the Water Resources Department has provided the machinery to remove the silt and has been requested under the reference letter to provide funds to those plants for fuel expenditure.
The government was considering providing funds for the purpose.
Government Decision: The State Executive Committee has approved the supply of fuel to the silt removal plants as the removal of silt from the Savitri River is a matter of disaster mitigation. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rs.
