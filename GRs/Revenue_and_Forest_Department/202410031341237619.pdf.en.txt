Subsequently, in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 4 (5) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, as recommended by the Suspension Review Committee, the suspension of Mr. Vijay Borude, Tehsildar was terminated and he was transferred to the concerned No. 3 has been restored to government service by order herein. Government Orders: -
