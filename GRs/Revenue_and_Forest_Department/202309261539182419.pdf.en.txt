The Proposal:
The Government had accorded approval for the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati during the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
2. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur had submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government under Reference No.5 letter for the year 2023-2024 to continue the 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The powers to extend the term of office of temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 shall be conferred on the concerned administrative departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department.

Accordingly, 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati were approved to be continued in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format have been provided to the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 8 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department, the Government has approved the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-2024.
The expenditure on establishment of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati, under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development and Reconstruction, (01) Administration and Conservation, (01) (01) Administration and Conservation (2406 0096)" should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No.
8/16/ APC, dated 31.08.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
