Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to
the State Government, which was received from M/s. Telesonic Network Limited, Mumbai
for diversion of 0.561 ha. Forest land for laying of underground Optical Fiber Cable Line
passing within Right of Way of existing road at Village Waghare, Tal. Parola, Village-
Manyarkheda & Ruikheda, Tal. Muktainagar, Dist. Jalgaon.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order under
reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above proposal for
diversion of 0.561 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general approval given vide MOEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Fimal Approval for
diversion of 0.561 ha. Forest Land.
# Page 2
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.561 ha. Forest Land consisting
of Reserved Forests specifically described 11 the proposal submitted vide letter under reference
No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. M/s.
Telesonic Network Limited, Mumbai for laying of underground Optical Fiber Cable Line
passing within Right of Way of existing road.
This approval for diversion of forest land 1s also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Jalgaon shall ensure compliance of the condition no.3
& 16 in 'in-principle' order dated 27.03.2023, before actual handling over the forest
land to the user agency.
. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Order dated 27.03.2023.
. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry
in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access
or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest
area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection)
Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above mentioned 'In-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief Conservator
of Forests (T), Dhule shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User
Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to
time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra's order dated 27.03.2023, This order and rules and guidelines made / issued under
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount to violation of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief
Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State
Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry
of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per
procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980.
