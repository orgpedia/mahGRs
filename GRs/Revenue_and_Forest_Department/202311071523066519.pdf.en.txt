The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Nashik shall immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer and Administrator, Benefit Area Development Authority, Jalgaon as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer and Administrator, Benefit Area Development Authority, Jalgaon shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works to the concerned Executive Engineer without any delay as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) The Executive Engineer, Girna Irrigation Department, Jalgaon should immediately pay the pending dues of the civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Nashik and Collector, Jalgaon.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
