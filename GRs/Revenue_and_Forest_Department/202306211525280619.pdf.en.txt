The Proposal:
A committee has been formed under the relevant government decision of the honourable Member of Legislative Assembly to suggest measures on human-leopard conflict in the state. In view of the increasing presence of leopards in Nashik district and incidents of human-leopard conflict, the government was considering the inclusion of a respected member from Nashik district in the committee.
Government's decision:
Shri Diliprao Shankarrao Banakar, Member of Legislative Assembly, is being included as a member in the committee constituted to suggest measures on the increased number of leopards in the state, their increased presence in urban settlements and the suffering caused by them to the citizens and to find solutions to the human-leopard conflict.
