Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to
the State Government, which was received from M/s. Bharti Airtel Ltd, Pune for re-
diversion of 0.6404 ha. forest land for laying of underground OFC duct & cable within
RoW of existing Phaltan to Koregaon Road at Village Tathavade in Phaltan Taluka and
Village Bhadale in Koregaon Taluka District Satara in State of Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order
under reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above
proposal for diversion of 0.6404 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
# Page 2
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under
general approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines
for effective and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final
Approval for Diversion of 0.6404 ha. Forest Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.6404 ha. Forest Land
consisting of Reserved Forest land specifically described in the proposal submitted vide
letter under reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the
User Agency, 1.6. M/s. Bharti Airtel Ltd, Pune for re-diversion of 0.6404 ha. forest land for
laying of underground OFC duct & cable within RoW of existing Phaltan to Koregaon
Road at Village Tathavade in Phaltan Taluka and Village Bhadale in Koregaon Taluka
District Satara in State of Maharashtra.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
DCF, Satara shall ensure compliance of the condition no. 2, 4, 5 & 14 in '1n-
principle 'order dated 21.07.2025, before actual holding over the forest land to the
user agency.
2. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
Order dated 21.07.2025.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits
the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm
that Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment
(Protection) Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
5. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above
mentioned "in-principle" and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief
Conservator of Forests (Territorial), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict compliance of
the conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to
# Page 3
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra
State, Nagpur from time to time.
6. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of
Maharashtra's order dated 21.07.2025, in this order and rules and guidelines made
/ issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount
to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action
would be taken accordingly.
7. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief
Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the
State Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office,
Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur
as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
