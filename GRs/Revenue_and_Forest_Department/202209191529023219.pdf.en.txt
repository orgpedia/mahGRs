The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue the 224 temporary posts created under the scheme of establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in 2022-2023.
Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve The Government has approved the continuation of 224 temporary posts created under this scheme in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format, from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 have been conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No.
