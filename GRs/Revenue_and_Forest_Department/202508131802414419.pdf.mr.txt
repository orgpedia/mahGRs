Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, has submitted a proposal for diversion of 3.07 ha.
of Forest Land for residential buildings for staff of Animal Husbandry Division,
Bhamburda, Distt. Pune to State Government and State Government vide under letter
reference No.1 has sent the proposal for approval of Regional Chief Conservator of
Forests (General), Government of India, Ministry of Environment of Forests, Regional
Office, Western Region, Bhopal;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
under reference No.2, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 3.07 ha. Forest
land for the above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the
User Agency;
AND whereas, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Maharashtra State,
Nagpur, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.3 has submitted a
# Page 2
revised proposal for diversion of 0.99 ha. of Forest Land for residential buildings for
staff of Animal Husbandry Division, Bhamburda, Distt. Pune;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.4 has sent
the revised proposal for approval of Regional Chief Conservator of Forests (General),
Government of India, Ministry of Environment of Forests, Regional Office, Western
Region, Bhopal;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
under reference No.5, accorded ‘Final approval’ to divert the 0.99 ha. land for the
above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
The said approval is subject to fulfilment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of
0.99 ha. consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No.3 & 4 be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e.
Principal State level Training center Animal Husbandry Department, Gokhalenagar,
Pune for residential buildings for staff, in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in
the letter dated 25.08.1993 and dated 01.12.1998 of Regional Office, Western Region,
Bhopal. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of
following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Pune should ensure that fulfillment of condition mentioned in the letter dated 01.12.1998 of Regional Office, Western Region, Bhopal, before actual handing over of the forest land to the User Agency.
2. User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’
and “final approval’ letters of Regional Office, Western Region, Bhopal, referred
above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall ensure the strict
compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in
this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities
and also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency
prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this
Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, Western Region, Bhopal letter dated 25.08.1993 and dated 01.12.1998, ॥ this order and
Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
# Page 3
Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief
Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall report through the State Government to
the Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of
Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per
procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
