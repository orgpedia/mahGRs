The Proposal:
The Government has accorded approval for continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 under the Government Decision of Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 6 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department, the Government has approved the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2023-2024.
The expenditure on establishment of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08.02.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
