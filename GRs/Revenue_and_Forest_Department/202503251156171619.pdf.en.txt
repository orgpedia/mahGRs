Proposal: - Ma. The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" is functioning under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, State of Maharashtra and the sanctioned funds for sanction of new works for the year 2024-25 have been sanctioned as per the percentage of Taluk wise allocation. Accordingly, the funds have been sanctioned as per the Taluk-wise percentage. Accordingly, 15 per cent of the funds have been earmarked for the Taluka Chiplun as per which administrative sanction and release of funds for development works in Chiplun Taluka was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - By making a provision from the budget of the State of Maharashtra for the "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" and the funds received by the Committee from the State Government without any other expenditure, for the development of the villages situated in the remote areas of 8 taluks (Patan, Karad (North), Karad (South), Javali, Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Sangameshwar and Chiplun) falling in the Koyna earthquake prone area, as per the rules of the "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" for the purpose of spending only for the purpose of the Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee, the total number of development works in Chiplun Taluk to be disbursed to the Collector, Ratnagiri is 86.
