To keep a reserve fund for immediate financial assistance to the victims of human loss caused by wildlife attacks.

the Revenue and Forest Department

The Ministry, Mumbai, 400 032
Date: 23rd of June, 2023.
The Proposal:
The entire allocation budgeted by the State Government for the year 2023-24 for immediate payment of compensation in case of attacks by wild animals is Rs. 7000.00 lakhs has been disbursed on the Budget Distribution System. The Government was considering the provision of a permanent reserve fund at the regional level to pay immediate financial compensation for human losses in wildlife attacks.
Government circular
Provision should be made for immediate payment of monetary compensation for human loss caused by wildlife attack as follows:
1) A permanent reserve fund should be set up at the regional level to provide immediate financial compensation for human losses caused by wildlife attacks.
2) The reserve fund shall be provided to the office of the Chief Conservator of Forests/ Conservator of Forests as per the forest report, taking into account the number of incidents of human loss in the forest report in the previous period, with a minimum amount of Rs. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur will be responsible for fixing the ceiling between Rs. 25.00 lakh and Rs. 100.00 lakh.
(3) The Chief Conservator of Forests/ Conservator of Forests shall immediately pay the financial compensation admissible to the next of kin of the victims from the said reserve fund as per the rules in force in cases of loss of human life due to wild animal attack and request the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for funds to restore the amount spent from the reserve fund. The funds must be kept in reserve until the end of February. The funds can then be used to pay compensation in other cases.
(4) The Chief Conservator of Forests/ Conservator of Forests concerned shall be responsible for non-payment of compensation from the said reserve fund except in cases of human loss.
