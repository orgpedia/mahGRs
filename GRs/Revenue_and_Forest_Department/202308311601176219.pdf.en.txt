The Proposal:
For the removal of encroachments on the government land in the government colony, Bandra (East), in Mumbai Suburban District, eviction of unauthorized persons and freeing the plot in a planned manner, etc. and for the development project of the Bandra Government Colony, the property officer is a post of the rank of Deputy Collector. EST 2019/ PR No. 55/ Administration-1, dated March 12, 2020. Accordingly, the Public Works Department has delegated the powers vested under Section 50 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 to the Deputy Collector and Estate Officer to remove/ evict encroachments on the revenue of Government Estate at Bandra (East) as requested by the Revenue Department vide Government Decision No. 2623/ PR No. 60/ J-1, dated 27/03/2023. However, since the powers vested under Section 53 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 are not available, the Public Works Department has requested the Revenue Department to provide the powers vested under Section 53 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 to the Deputy Collector and Estate Officer.
