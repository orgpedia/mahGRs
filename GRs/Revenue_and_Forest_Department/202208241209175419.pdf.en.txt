As per the directions given by the Minister (Forest), Maharashtra State, and the demand from the people's representatives regarding the increase in financial assistance, the government has decided to increase the amount of financial assistance in the case of human loss and livestock loss caused by wildlife.
Government's decision:
In case of human loss and death of livestock due to attack by tiger, leopard, bear, bison, wild boar, wolf, hyena, jackal, crocodile, elephant and rhinoceros (Dhol), financial assistance should be given as follows.
In case of death, permanent disability or injury to the person, the amount of financial assistance should be given as follows.
