The above 33 posts are temporary in nature and shall be treated as additional posts in addition to the permanent posts of the concerned department. Along with these posts, the office bearers will also be entitled to dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the rules of the government. Further, the Government has approved the extension subject to the assumption that the above posts were not vacant for more than six months.
Out of the above posts, the expenditure of the sanctioned staff for rehabilitation in the Commissioner's Office is as follows: Demand No. C-5 Main Account Head* 2235-Social Security and Welfare (01) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons (03) Rehabilitation (202) (03) (04) (Post-Plan- 22351906) and expenditure of the District Level Staff is as under: Demand No. C05, Main Accounts Heading 2235-Social Security and Welfare (01) Scheme under State Schemes Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons (04) Rehabilitation (202) (04) (04) (Under Schemes- 22351962) The expenditure to be incurred during the year 202-2022 under the head heading should be met from the approved provisions of 2001-2022.
