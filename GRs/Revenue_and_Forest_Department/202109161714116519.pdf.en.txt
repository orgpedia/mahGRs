The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to revise the pay scale of the State Government employees as per the Seventh Pay Commission, Finance Division No. Vepur-2019/ PR No. 1/ Service-9D. 30/01/2019 has been approved with effect from 01.01.2016. A total of 1345 officers/ employees mentioned at Rakana No.-5 in Annexure-I in Maharashtra Forest Development Corporation Limited will be given revised pay scale as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission subject to the terms and conditions of the Government decision dated 30/01/2019 as above and the following conditions: It is being approved by the Government with effect from 01.07.2021.
