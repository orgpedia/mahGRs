Government order:
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority for transfer, vide Government Order dated 21.02.2023, Shri Anand Reddy Yellu, Bhavse, Deputy Conservator of Forests (Regional), Yavatmal was transferred and posted on administrative grounds to the vacant post of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Forest Conservation), Nagpur.
Dhananjay Vaibhase, Bhavse were posted as Deputy Conservator of Forests (Regional), Yavatmal in place of Anand Reddy Yellu, Bhavse.
2. Mr. Anand Reddy Yellu, Bhavse protested against the Government's transfer order. The original application No. 136/2023 was filed in the Central Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai. In this case, The Government vide letter dated 27.02.2023, has decided to relieve Dhananjay Vaibhase, Bhavse of the additional charge of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Planning), Yavatmal and give the same to Mr. Anand Reddy Yellu, Bhavse as additional charge till further orders. Accordingly, Shri Reddy has taken over the additional charge of the said post on 01.03.2023.
3. Mr. Anand Reddy Yellu, Bhavse. In the original application No. 136/2023 filed in the Central Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, Shri. The Court vide its final order dated 07.07.2023 has quashed the posting of Shri Reddy and Shri Bhavse as Deputy Conservator of Forests (Forest Conservation), Nagpur and ordered their continuance in the post of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Planning), Yavatmal till the completion of two years period as per the interim order dated 24.02.2023. Reddy, Shri. In accordance with the order of the Central Administrative Tribunal. Writ Petition No. 4386/2023 was filed in the Bombay High Court, Nagpur Bench. In this case The High Court on 19.07.2023 has ordered that the salary and emoluments of the Deputy Conservator of Forests (Planning), Yavatmal, who has served in the post of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Planning), be paid to Shri Reddy and Shri Bhavse till the date they hold the post.
