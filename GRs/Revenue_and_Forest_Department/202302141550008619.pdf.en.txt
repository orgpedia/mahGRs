Proposed Expenditure:- The expenditure to be incurred from the grants to be made available through the Budget Distribution System in 2022-23 under the objectives of Headings- 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Conservation of Wildlife (00) (11) Determination of Rights and Privileges- Rehabilitation of Villages in National Parks and Sanctuaries (State) (2406-2241).
