Proposal: - Ma. The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" is functioning under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, State of Maharashtra and the sanctioned funds for sanction of new works for the year 2024-25 have been sanctioned as per the percentage of Taluk wise approval. Accordingly, 35 per cent of the funds have been earmarked for Taluka Patan and the issue of administrative sanction for development works in Patan Taluka was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - For the development of villages located in the remote areas of 8 taluks (Patan, Karad (North), Karad (South), Javali, Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Sangameshwar and Chiplun) falling under the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee without spending the funds received by the committee from the state government from the budget of Maharashtra and without spending on any other work, as per the rules of the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee, the total number of development works in Patan taluka for the purpose of disbursement of funds to the Collector, Satara for the purpose of spending only for the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee is 423. A total of 43 works have been given technical approval by the office of the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department (West), Satara, Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad Construction Department (South), Satara and Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply, Zilla Parishad, Satara. The amount of technical sanction for 43 works is Rs. 6,85,00, 000 / - (Rupees Six crore with five lakhs only). Accordingly, from the sanctioned grant, the development works in Patan Taluka are being accorded administrative sanction as specified in Annexure-A attached thereto. The funds will be distributed to the District Collector, Satara.
The Executive Committee of the Committee in its meeting dated 29.07.2013 has decided as follows for the implementation of the internal work of the Committee. "It was decided to authorise the District Collectors to implement these various works by adopting the B method of government rules. The administrative machinery appointed by the District Collector should also carry out the work by following the government rules.
