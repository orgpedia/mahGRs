Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the State Government for
diversion of 12.05 ha. Forest land under Forest (Conservation), 1980 for Construction of
Shribhuvan Minor Irrigation Tank, Irrigation cum drinking water supply project Tank at
Shribhuvan Tal. Surgana Dist. Nashik in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 12.05 ha. The Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
# Page 2
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.5 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 6 Accorded 'Final
approval 'to divert the 12.05 ha. of Forest Land in Favour of Minor Irrigation Division, Nashik
for construction of Shribhuvan Minor Irrigation Tank, [Irrigation cum drinking water supply
project Tank at Shribhuvan Tal. Surgana Dist. Nashik in the State of Maharashtra under
section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No. 7 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 12.05 ha
consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide
letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted
to the User Agency, 1.e. Minor Irrigation Division, Nashik for construction of Shribhuvan
Minor Irrigation Tank, Irrigation cum drinking water supply project Tank at Shribhuvan Tal.
Surgana Dist. Nashik in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 13.09.2022 and dated 30.05.2023 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, East Nashik shall ensure that the FRA certificate must be obtained for the area to be diverted, before the actual handing over of the forest
land to the User Agency.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Nashik shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the
free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MOEF&CC,
Nagpur, letters dated 13.09.2022 and dated 30.05.2023, in this order and Rules &
# Page 3
Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980, shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of
Forests (T), Nashik shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director
General of Forest (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in
guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
