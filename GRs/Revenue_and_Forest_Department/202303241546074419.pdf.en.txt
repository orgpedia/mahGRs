Government circular:
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 lay down the overall policy/ rules for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the government service every year. According to reference no. 2 vide Circular dated 17.3.2022 of the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune, the Inspector General of Registration and Joint District Registrar of Stamps Division have appointed two equivalent cadre officers, namely, Sub-Divisional Registrar Class-II and Deputy Superintendent of Stamps. The Common Service Seniority List dated 1/1/2022 was published after taking into account the objections/ errors/ factual errors etc. received from the officers.
Now, the final service merit list dated 1/1/2022 of the officers of the two equivalent cadres of Inspector General of Registration and Joint District Registrar Class-II/ Cum-Second Registrar Class-II and Deputy Superintendent of Stamps in the Department of Stamps has been published vide Reference No. 1 is being published as per Annexure-A annexed to the Rules contained in the Notification. As Shri R. M. Gaikwad has been approved for the cadre of Joint District Registrar Class-II, the Service Excellence List dated 1.1.2008 to 1.1.2022 is being amended by adding the name of Shri R. M. Gaikwad in the Service Excellence List as shown in Annexure-B.
