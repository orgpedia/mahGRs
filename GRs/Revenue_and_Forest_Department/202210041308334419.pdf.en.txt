Government's decision:
The 51 posts created for the rehabilitation of the project victims affected by various irrigation and other projects in the Amaravati revenue division have been given under the relevant Government Decision No. 2. 01.3.2022 to D. The extension of temporary posts was granted till 31.8.2022.
As per the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, the regional level posts under the Relief and Rehabilitation Sub-Division have been reviewed and the figure of a total of 52 posts in the Amravati Division on the rehabilitation establishment has been fixed in accordance with the relevant Government Decision No. 1.
Accordingly, the Government is granting approval for continuation of 51 posts from 01.9.2022 to 28.2.2023 (6 months) as indicated in the accompanying Forms A and B.
The above posts are temporary in nature and should be treated as additional posts in addition to the permanent posts of the concerned department. Along with these posts, the office bearers will also be entitled to dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the rules of the government. Also, the government has approved the extension of tenure subject to the assumption that the above posts were not vacant for more than six months.
