The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 142 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation during the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 under the Government decision of Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 142 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation for the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 10 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern.

Government Decision: As per the decision of the Finance Department, Government of India has approved the continuation of 142 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation for the year 2021-2022 from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The expenditure on the establishment of 142 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2020-2021 under the head 2406-Forestation and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02-Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110-Wildlife Conservation (00) (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766).
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No.
8/16/ APC, dated 10.08.2021 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
