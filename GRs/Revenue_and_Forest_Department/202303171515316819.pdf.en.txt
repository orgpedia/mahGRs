GOVERNMENT MEMORY: Under the Finance Division, Reference No. 1, Government Decision, dated 29th May, 2020, it has been decided to provide insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance to the employees who die due to COVID-19 virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
Late Sudhir Pancham Raut, Sepoy, an employee working at Collectorate, Chandrapur has died on 11/04/2021 due to Corona Virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duty.
3. Collector, Chandrapur has sanctioned an ex-gratia amount of Rs.20 lakh to the family of Late Sudhir Pancham Raut, Sepoy, as per Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 2020/ PR No.4/ Expenditure-9, dated 29/05/2020. A proposal for sanction of ex-gratia assistance to the tune of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) has been submitted vide letter No. 2 as under.
4. The Collector, Chandrapur has certified that late Sudhir Pancham Raut, Sepoy was performing the duty of" prevention" in view of the universal epidemic of" COVID-19" and that he was present on duty during the period of 14 days preceding the date of his death.
