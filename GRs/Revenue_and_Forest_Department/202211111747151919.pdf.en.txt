The bidding process and the work order:
(a) The Executive Engineer, (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli shall immediately commence the tender process of the proposed civic amenity work by fulfilling the conditions in the administrative approval order submitted.
(b) The tender process for the proposed civic amenities work shall not require prior approval and approval of the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation.
(c) As the Government's Rehabilitation Department will provide funds for the construction of civic amenities in the rehabilitated villages under the proposal, there is no need to obtain tender documents from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for issuing work orders after the acceptance of the tender.
(d) The Executive Engineer, (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli shall start the civil facility works immediately after the completion of the tender process for the proposed civil facility work by issuing the work order for the tender received.
