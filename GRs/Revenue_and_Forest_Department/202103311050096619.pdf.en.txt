In view of the demand, for the financial year 2020-21, under the revised estimate limit of Rs.31-Rs.52 crore under Subsidiary Grants (non-salary) for Chandrapur Forest Academy under the head" Chandrapur Academy of Forest Administration, Development and Management, Chandrapur (2415-1077)," Rs. 70.00 lakhs is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. The funds are being given to them.
The above expenditure should be incurred as per the prescribed procedure and as per the monthly fund flow available on the budget distribution system. There should be no financial irregularities.
The Controlling Authorities are advised to ensure that no additional expenditure is incurred under any circumstances over and above the total disbursed/ to be disbursed.
4. While spending this provision, the terms and conditions laid down by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed.

(6) It shall be ensured that the amount withdrawn after the withdrawal of the said grant from the concerned treasury is recorded in the records of the Accountant General's Office under the same head and submit a certificate to the Government in this regard.
7. The Institute shall forthwith submit to the Government and the Accountant General the certificate of appropriation of the grants made available to it for the previous year. Similarly, the detailed payment and utilization certificate of the subsidy being distributed now should also be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and the government immediately.

9. Follow the prescribed procedure before making actual expenditure and withdraw as per requirement.
