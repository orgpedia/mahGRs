A total of 415 posts will be filled under the Entertainment Fee Branch. 01.09.2021 to dt. The matter of extension for the period up to 28.02.2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
A total of 415 temporary posts, including 36 at divisional level and 379 at district level, in a total of 9 cadres in the entertainment fee branch of all divisional commissioner offices and all district collector offices in the state have been filled. 01.09.2021 to dt. The extension for the period up to 28.02.2022 is being granted subject to the following conditions: 1. A total of 415 posts at Divisional and District level have already been sanctioned by the Revenue Department. If not included in the figure fixed by the Government Decision of 20th March, 2006, the proposal for review of such posts shall be submitted to this Department (to Office of Commissioner E-8) by the concerned Divisional Commissioner's Office for obtaining approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries within the period of extension. After the department's designations are approved, the adjustment of those positions will be handled by Office E-8.
The Deputy Commissioner (Entertainment Fee) under the control of all Divisional Commissioners shall, in accordance with the rules made thereunder, make regular inspection of the records relating to the collection of entertainment fee due from the Collectorates in their respective divisions and submit the reports of the inspection for each calendar month to the Government before the 15th day of the following calendar month.
3. All Divisional Commissioners/ Collectors shall ensure that the services of officers/ staff in temporary posts sanctioned for the entertainment fee mechanism in the concerned department/ district are not utilised for any other purpose without prior approval of the Government. Also, if the government finds that these posts have been used in other branches without the prior permission of the government, such posts will be abolished.
The office bearers of all the above temporary posts are entitled to all other allowances including travelling allowance, local supplementary allowance as per the prevailing Government rules.
5. A total of 415 number of temporary posts in 9 cadres as shown in the accompanying' Return A" and' Return B" shall be deemed to be temporary in their respective cadres.
