Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to
the State Government, which was received from M/s Telesonic Network Limited, Nagapur
for 1.1903 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980
for laying of underground Optical Fiber Cable Line passing within Row of existing NH-
930 (Ajaypur to Chandrapur km 251/820 to km 271/420) MDR-21 Chiroli to Ajaypur (km
34/30 to km 19/00) and NH-930 Mul to Janada (km 230/850 to 239/250) in the State of
Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order
under reference No. 2 & 3 accorded "1n-principle" approval with certain conditions to above
proposal for diversion of 1.1903 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines
# Page 2
Government Order No.: FLD-2022/C.R.25/F-10
for effective and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 [Chapter-4, Para-4.2], Government of Maharashtra is
pleased to grant Final Approval for Diversion of 1.1903 ha. Forest Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 1.1903 ha. Forest Land
consisting of Reserved Forest, Protected Forest & Unclass Forest Land more specifically
described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No.1 and mentioned in the
Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. M/s Telesonic Network
Limited, Nagpur for laying of underground Optical Fiber Cable Line passing within Row
of existing NH-930 (Ajaypur to Chandrapur km 251/820 to km 271/420) MDR-21 Chiroli
to Ajaypur (km 34/30 to km 19/00) and NH-930 Mul to Janada (km 230/850 to 239/250) in
the State of Maharashtra.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Divisional Forest Officer (T) Chandrapur shall ensure that the proposal is approved by the ESZ Monitoring Committee before handed over actual possession of forest
land to the User Agency.
2. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
Order dated 14.03.2022 & 06.04.2022.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment
(Protection) Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
5. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above
mentioned 'in-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief
Conservator of Forests (Territorial), Chandrapur shall ensure the strict compliance
of the conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra
State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra's order dated 14.03.2022 & 06.04.2022, in this order and rules and
Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to
violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
7. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional
Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
