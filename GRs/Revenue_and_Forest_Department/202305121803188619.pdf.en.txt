Preamble: Reference No. 1 of the Government Order. As on 18.12.2019, ad-hoc promotions in Forest Range Officer Group B cadre have been regularized for the years 2012-13 and 2013-14. The Government was now considering regularization of ad-hoc promotions for the selection years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17 by considering the following category-wise vacancies in the promotion quota:
------------------------------------------------
| A.A.  | the year of selection | Vacant posts |
| 1     | 2014-15               | 53           |
| 2     | 2015-16               | 93           |
| 3     | 2016-17               | 136          |
| Total | 282                   |
------------------------------------------------
The Government Order is regularizing the ad-hoc promotions given in the cadre of Forest Range Officer Group-B during the period 2011-12 to 2016-17, taking into account the category-wise vacancies available in the selection list from 2014-15 to 2016-17, subject to the following conditions as per the accompanying returns A, B and C, as on the date indicated against their names:
(a) The regular schedule. A Special Leave Petition No. The approval is being granted subject to the final decision of 28306/2017.
(b) There is no presumption that any senior officer was inadvertently left out for promotion while preparing the selection list.
(e) The officers in the selection list should not be promoted without obtaining caste validity certificate.
(e) There is a presumption that the employees in the select list who have been given ad-hoc promotions subject to departmental inquiry have been acquitted in the departmental inquiry.
3. The said Government Order is issued by the General Administration Department vide No. 14/ No. 12, D. being issued as per the approval given on 27.2.2023.
