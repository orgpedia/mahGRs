Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
Diversion of 0.9337 ha Reserved Forest and Protected Forest in favour of National Highway
Authority of India, PIU, Pune for four laning of Alandi- Pandharpur-Mohal section of Palkhi
Marg (Km 117/00 to Km 166/400) at Village- Nimbhore, Kalaj, Taluka- Phaltan, District- Satara
in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.2, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0.9337 ha. The forest land for the

AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,

conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted

AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &

PIU, Pune under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to
issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.9337 ha.
consisting of Reserve and Protected forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. National Highway Authority of India, PIU, Pune for four
laning of Alandi- Pandharpur-Mohal section of Palkhi Marg (Km 117/00 to Km 166/400) at
Village- Nimbhore, Kalaj, Taluka- Phaltan, District- Satara in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letter
dated 16.08.2022 and dated 15.05.2023 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur. This approval
for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User
Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Satara should ensure that fulfillment of condition No. 1 &
111 mentioned in the letter dated 15.05.2023 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur
before handover possession of the forest land to the user agency.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle' and
'Final approval 'letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above. The
Conservator of Forests (T), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions
by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal
Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to
time.
3. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 16.08.2022 and dated 15.05.2023, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980., shall
