Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 0.7830 ha. Forest Land for laying close Pipe Line distribution system from 600
Meter to 1140 meter on Sorna Feeder Canal of Sorna Medium Project at Village Bawanthadi
South Block No.12, Taluka Tumsar, Dist. Bhandara in the State of Maharashtra under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam 1980;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has submitted
proposal to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of
Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.3, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 0.7830 ha. Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;

Government Order No.: FLD-3524/C.R.128/F-10
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded ‘Final approval’
to divert the of 0.7830 ha. of Forest land in favour of Executive Engineer, Bhandara Irrigation
Division, Bhandara for laying close Pipe Line distribution system from 600 Meter to 1140
meter on Sorna Feeder Canal of Sorna Medium Project at Village Bawanthadi South Block
No.12, Taluka Tumsar, Dist. Bhandara in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.7830
ha. consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be
diverted to the User Agency, 1.8. Executive Engineer, Bhandara Irrigation Division, Bhandara
for laying close Pipe Line distribution system from 600 Meter to 1140 meter on Sorna Feeder
Canal of Sorna Medium Project at Village Bawanthadi South Block No.12, Taluka-Tumsar,
Dist. Bhandara in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 23.08.2024 and dated 05.02.2025 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Bhandara shall ensure compliance of the condition no.
1.2 & 1.8 mentioned in final order dated 05.02.2025, before actual handing over of the forest land to the User Agency.
2. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Bhandara shall ensure that the FRA certificate must be
obtained for the area to be diverted, before the actual handing over of the forest land to the User Agency.
3. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’
and ‘final approval’ letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Nagpur shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC
letters dated 23.08.2024 and dated 05.02.2025, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall
