the prelude
Under the Veer project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Bhambwade and Mauje Gunand in Bhor taluka of Pune district have been taken up under reference No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted as per the government decision here. For payment of civic amenities works on the progress route for which the said administrative approval has been granted, vide reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government by the Executive Engineer, Public Works (South) Division 2, Pune.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for civic amenities works in progress under the proposal was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has approved the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 18,57,715/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifteen only) for the construction of' Crematorium Shed" on the Pragatipatha in Mauje Bhambwade and Mauje Gunand rehabilitated villages of Bhor taluka of Pune district during the current financial year 2022-2023 subject to the following conditions:

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received by him under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Pune as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Pune shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
The Executive Engineer, Public Works (South) Department, Pune shall immediately clear the pending dues of the civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received.
