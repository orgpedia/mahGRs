Cabinet to set up a sub-committee to study landslides and landslides caused by heavy rains in the state and determine the danger zone
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
(Disaster management, relief and rehabilitation)
Government Resolution No.2021/ No.209/ No.1
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The 21st of October 2021
Introduction:' Landslides' and' Landslides' are among the major disasters occurring in the state of Maharashtra. The government was considering setting up a Cabinet sub-committee to study the possibility of such a disaster and to identify possible disaster zones with the help of an expert committee and various authorities.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the formation of a Cabinet Sub-Committee as follows to determine the area of potential danger as a preventive measure in the case of' landslide' and' landslide' in the state of Maharashtra.
1 Ma. Minister of Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation. Minister of Public Works (excluding Public Undertakings) 3. Minister of Rural Development 4. Minister of Water Supply and Sanitation 5. The Minister, Environment Member Principal Secretary (Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation) will be the Secretary of the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
