Order: Whereas, as per the authority received under the letter dated 10/05/2000 of the State Government, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, has taken over the construction of Vandeli Storage Lake under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 from the Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Department, Kalwa-Thane. The proposal was submitted along with the recommendation under letter No. 1 hereunder; and the Government of India, vide letter No. 2 above, have accorded in principle approval to the proposal submitted by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Forest Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. This approval is granted subject to the project mechanism meeting the conditions as laid down in the Government of India letter dated 30/10/2020;
