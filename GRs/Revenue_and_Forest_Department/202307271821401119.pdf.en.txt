GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Transfer Act, 2005 and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Tenure of Office, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Mr. Sachin Mhaske, Tehsildar, Pune is being appointed as Tehsildar Administration, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Pune on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Shri Sachin Mhaske, Tehsildar is being relieved unilaterally as per this order. The above officers should be posted immediately.
(3) The said order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector.
The services of Mr. Sachin Mhaske, Tehsildar are being handed over to the Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai in accordance with the deputation from the office of Tehsildar Administration, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Pune.
5. Mr. Sachin Mhaske, Tehsildar to appear before Principal Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
