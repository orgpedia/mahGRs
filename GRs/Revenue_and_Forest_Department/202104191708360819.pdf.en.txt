The memo:
As per the provisions of Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to review the eligibility of Government officers/ employees to continue in Government service beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after completion of 30 years of qualifying service, based on the prescribed criteria, to ensure that only competent and efficient officers/ employees continue in Government service in public interest and to retire officers/ employees with inefficiency and doubtful integrity from Government service prematurely.
(2) In respect of Group A and Group B officers of the Department, a decision has been taken to review the eligibility for continuing in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service. The procedures have been laid down jointly under Circulars 1 and 2.
3. Reference No. Under the orders issued here, a Divisional Review Committee has been formed under the Forest Department. The Government is giving approval for continuation of Rajendra G. Agase, Forest Statistician, (Group-A) before the age of 50 years or after 30 years of qualifying service.
