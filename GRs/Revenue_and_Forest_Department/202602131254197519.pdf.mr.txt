Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 0.7160 ha. Protected Forest land under section-2 of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Sanvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for construction of approach road including water pipeline,
Electrical line, drainage line along the road in private land Gut No.164 at Village Mapgaon,
Tal. Alibag, Dist. Raigad, in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Regional Office (Central) Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur for diversion of 0.2684925 ha. Forest land as
per approach road policy dated. 09.01.2024;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.3, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 0.2684925 ha. forest Land for
# Page 2
the above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded ‘Final approval’
to divert the 0.2684925 ha. of forest land in favour of Authorized Signatory, Mr. Sanjeev
Kanvar for Vandhana Sanjeev Kanwar & others, at Village Mapgaon, Tal.Alibag, Dist.
Raigad for construction of approach road including water pipeline, Electrical line, drainage
line along the road in private land Gut No.164 at Village Mapgaon, Tal. Alibag, Dist. Raigad
in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of
0.2684925 ha. consisting of Reserved forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. Authorized Signatory, Mr. Sanjeev Kanvar for
Vandhana Sanjeev Kanwar & others, at Village Mapgaon, Tal.Alibag, Dist. Raigad for
construction of approach road including water pipeline, Electrical line, drainage line along the
road in private land Gut No.164 at Village Mapgaon, Tal. Alibag, Dist. Raigad in the State of
Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 08.04.2024 and dated 24.09.2024 of Regional Office, MOEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Alibag should ensure that fulfillment of condition
No. 1.2 & 1.7 mentioned in the letter dated 24.09.2024 of Regional Office,
MoEF&CC, Nagpur before the actual handing over of the forest land to the user
agency.
2. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Alibag shall ensure that the FRA certificate is for the
land of old survey no. 80 to be diverted or obtain a revised FRA certificate before the actual handing over of the forest land to the User Agency.
3. User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’ and
‘final approval’ letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above. Chief
Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions
by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal
Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time
to time.
4. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statutory duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
# Page 3
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
5. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoOEF&CC letters dated 08.04.2024 and dated 24.09.2024, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall
amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and
action would be taken accordingly.
6. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director
General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
