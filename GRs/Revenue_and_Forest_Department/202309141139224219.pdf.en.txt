Appointment by appointment
Shama Dhok Pawar, Resident Deputy Collector, Solapur has been appointed as Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Pune.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
Government Order No.TRF-0623/ P.No.152 (Part-1)/ E-2
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: 14th of September 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Government order:
Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Recruitment Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Smt. Shama Dhok-Pawar, Resident Deputy Collector, Solapur is being appointed as Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Pune on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Mrs. Shama Dhok-Pawar, Deputy Collector is being relieved unilaterally from the post she is holding to join the post she has been transferred and posted with immediate effect.
The services of Smt. Shama Dhok-Pawar, Deputy Collector are being handed over to the Principal Secretary, Tourism, Ministry of Tourism, Mumbai in pursuance of her deputation as Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Pune.
Mrs. Shama Dhok-Pawar, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Principal Secretary, Tourism Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
