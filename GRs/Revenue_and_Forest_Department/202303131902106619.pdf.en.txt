The Proposal:
Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve The Government has approved the continuation of 224 temporary posts created under this scheme in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision in Reference No.6 and 7.
Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue the 224 temporary posts created under the scheme of formation of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-2024.
Extension of term of office of temporary posts included in the pattern of Administrative Department and all existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier review but not included in the pattern from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
