The Proposal:
9 teams of National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) have been deployed in the state. from 1st July 2022. 15 th September, 2022 and 2 columns of State Disaster Response Force (SDRF). from June 15, 2022. The concerned authorities were informed about the appointment in different districts for the period up to September 15, 2022. The matter of providing funds to the concerned districts for their food and travel expenses during the deployment of these units was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
It was decided to deploy State Disaster Response Force and National Disaster Response Force units in the respective districts to deal with possible floods in the state. Their food and accommodation costs Rs. 15,00,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) is being made available to the concerned districts.
