Preamble- The final award on payment of compensation by the Land Acquisition Officers while acquiring land for the rehabilitation of earthquake victims in Latur district was passed. As the compensation of the land was not admissible to the concerned land holders, they decided to seek enhanced compensation as per Section 18 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894. A case was filed against the government. The civil court has decided to give increased compensation to the landowners. The Department of Law and Justice, Aurangabad has given its opinion on not appealing against the said decisions. 3 A request was made to provide funds through a letter here. Based on the request, the government had sanctioned the funds.
Government Decision- Land acquired for the rehabilitation of earthquake victims in Latur district. As per the decision of the Civil Court, the Government is pleased to disburse the amount of Rs. 12,74,300/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Seventy Four Thousand Three Hundred only) as demanded by the Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation), Aurangabad for payment of enhanced compensation in the matter mentioned in the following table as per the provisions of Section 18 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894.
