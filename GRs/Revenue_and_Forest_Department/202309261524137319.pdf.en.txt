GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 02.23/ S.No.59/ F-1
As per the decision of the Government vide Reference No.7, 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation were approved to be continued during the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the format have been provided to the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 8 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government's decision:
Subject to the decision of the Government in Reference No.8 of the Finance Department, the Government is approving the continuation of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-24.
Expenditure on establishment of 137 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Wildlife Protection and Nature Conservation should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-24 under the head 2406-Forestation and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02-Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110-Wildlife Conservation (00) (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766).
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No.
8/16/ APC, dated 31.08.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
