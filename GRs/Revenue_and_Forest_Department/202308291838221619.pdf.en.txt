Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
GOVERNMENT ORDER: Subject to the provisions of Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Tenure of Office, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Shri Bhagwat Doiphode, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as the Chief Executive Officer, Smart City, Nashik on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years by retaining the allotted Amravati Division.
The services of Mr. Bhagwat Doifode, Deputy Collector are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai, in accordance with the deputation of the post of CEO, Smart City, Nashik.
3. Shri Bhagwat Doifode, Deputy Collector to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai immediately.
