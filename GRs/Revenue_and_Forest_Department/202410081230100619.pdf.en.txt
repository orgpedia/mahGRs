Proposal: - Ma. The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" is functioning under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, State of Maharashtra and the sanctioned funds for sanction of new works for the year 2024-25 have been sanctioned as per the percentage of Taluk wise allocation. Accordingly, the allocation of funds has been approved as per the Taluk-wise percentage. Accordingly, 2 per cent of the funds have been earmarked for Taluka Satara as per which administrative sanction for development works in Satara Taluka was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - For the development of the villages located in the remote areas of 8 taluks (Patan, Karad (North), Karad (South), Jawalo, Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Sangameshwar and Chiplun) falling under the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee without spending the funds received by the committee from the State Government from the budget of Maharashtra and without spending on any other work, as per the rules of the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee, the total number of development works in Satara Taluk for the purpose of disbursement of funds to the Collector, Satara only for the purpose of Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee is 6 and a total of 6 works have been given technical approval by the Office of the Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad Construction Department, North (Satara). The amount of technical approval for 6 works is Rs. 1,20,00, 000 / - (Rupees one crore twenty lakhs only). Accordingly, from the sanctioned grant, the development works in Satara Taluka are being accorded administrative sanction as specified in Annexure-A attached thereto.
The Executive Committee of the Committee in its meetings dated 29.07.2013 has decided as follows to carry out the functions under the Committee.
