As per the Government decision dated 8.02.2023 of the Department of Finance, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under them till 31.08.2023. Accordingly, 18 temporary posts as mentioned in the said Return-A are being extended for the period from 1.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The said 18 temporary posts shall not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The said 18 temporary posts are in the Land Records Office and the same are being filled up in the Forest Department under Review of Posts vide Reference No. 1 It must be included in the designations approved by the governing decision.
The expenditure on this post shall be met out of the grant to be sanctioned in the financial year 2023-24 under the head of Principal Head 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation Development and Regeneration (01) (02) Survey of Forests, Settlement and Simconification (2406 0102).
4. The said Government Decision is being issued by the Finance Department in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Decision No. Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 8.02.2023.
