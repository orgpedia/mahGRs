In Amravati. Since the intensity of tremors felt in the underground since 12/08/2018 is being felt in the nearby villages of Jhilpi, Gaulandoh, Khaparkheda, temporary shelters have been created for the safety of the citizens. Accordingly, the District Collector has sanctioned Rs. 12,006,175/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Six Thousand One Hundred and Seventy Five only) has been submitted to the Government for sanction of funds. As proposed by the District Collector, Amravati, Mauje Sadrabadi, Th. " Administrative approval" is being given by this Government to the cost of the Temporary Remedial Scheme carried out at the site of mild earthquake in Dharani, G. Amaravati. Also, Rs. 12,006,175/- (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Six Thousand One Hundred and Seventy Five only) is being sanctioned through this Government Resolution.
2. Post-facto approval is being accorded to authorize the District Collector, Amravati for the said work.
3.
