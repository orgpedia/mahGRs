As per the order passed by the Registrar General and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune, vide reference No. 3 By order dated 20.07.2016, it has been decided to reinstate Mr. Deshpande in Government service subject to departmental inquiry and he has been posted as Joint Registrar, Aurangabad No. 4 positions have been filled.
4. Whereas the Joint Secondary Registrar Class-II, who remained without posting from the date of 20.7.2016 till he joined the post i.e. from 10.9.2015 to 22.7.2016, has submitted an application to the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune for regularization of his suspension period as duty period.
5. Whereas, after completion of the departmental inquiry of Mr. Deshpande, the Government has appointed Mr. As on 1.11.2021, Shri.
Deshpande has been sentenced to 2 years of salary increase to be stopped by permanently affecting the salary increase in the future. Therefore, the suspension of Mr. Deshpande is justified.
Therefore, in view of the above, it is decided to regularize the period of suspension of Shri P.S. Deshpande, Additional Registrar Class-II by invoking the provisions of Rule 72 (a) and (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assumption of Office, Post-Graduate Service and Payment of Salaries during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
(1) In exercise of the provisions of Rule 72 (a) and (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assignment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri P.S. Deshpande, Dy. 10.09.2015 to dt. 22.07.2016 shall be deemed to be deemed to be a period of suspension for all other purposes, except for the purposes of retirement benefits.
(2) Mr. P.S. Deshpande shall be paid 75% of the salary and allowances which would have been admissible to him if he had not been suspended during the period from 10.9.2015 to 22.7.2016.
(3) The Inspector General of Registration and the Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take action to adjust the amount of subsistence allowance paid to Mr. Deshpande earlier from this amount and pay him the amount which would be net due to him.
The said order is in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assumption of Office, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, vide Rule No. In accordance with the provisions of section 72 (3) of the Act, the Department of Finance, Government Resolution No.Praja-2015/ No.P.S.25/ Service-6, D. are being issued as per delegated powers as on 25.4.2017.
