The Proposal: -
A total of 42 posts of Divisional Disaster Management Coordinator-6 posts and District Disaster Management Officer-36 posts have been created on contract basis for implementation of Maharashtra State Disaster Management Programme. These 42 posts have been filled up as per the decision of the Government in this regard. October 02, 2024 to D. The deadline has been extended for a period of 19 months till August 31, 2025.
For the month of November, 2024, Rs. 18,90,000 - (Rupees eighteen lakhs and ninety thousand only) was under consideration of the Government to distribute the remuneration to the Divisional Commissioners on the Budgetary Distribution System (BDS).
The Government's Decisions:
A total of 42 posts of 6 Divisional Disaster Management Coordinators and 36 District Disaster Management Officers have been created and functioning on contract basis for implementation of disaster management programme in the state of Maharashtra at a cost of Rs. 45,000 / - for the month of November 2024 aggregating to Rs. 18,90,000 - (Rupees eighteen lakh ninety thousand only) is being approved for disbursement to the districts through Divisional Commissioner on Budgetary Distribution System (BDS).
