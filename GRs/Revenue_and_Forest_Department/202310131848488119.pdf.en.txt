GOVERNMENT ORDER: Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II of Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Tenure of Office, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Mr. Abhijit Ghorpade, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Director, State Environmental Action Cell on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
Abhijit Ghorpade, Deputy Collector, is being transferred to Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Department, Ministry of Environment, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai, in addition to his deputation as Director, State Environmental Action Cell.
3.Shri Abhijit Ghorpade, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Principal Secretary, Environment and Atmospheric Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
