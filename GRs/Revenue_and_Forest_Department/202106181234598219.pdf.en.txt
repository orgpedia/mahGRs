ORDER: Whereas, as per the authority received from the State Government vide letter dated 10/05/2000, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter dated 10/05/2000, received from the Executive Engineer, Engineering Department, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Bandra (East), Mumbai, under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, a proposal for construction of flyover at Kalanagar Junction at Mauje-Parigharakhadi, Tt. Andheri, G. Mumbai Suburban at 0.0484

The proposal was submitted along with the recommendation under letter No. 1 hereunder; and the Government of India, vide letter No. 2 above, have accorded in principle approval to the proposal submitted by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Forest Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. Final approval for diversion has been accorded vide letter No. 4 hereunder; and whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station House Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide their letter No. 5 above, has informed that the project machinery has fulfilled the conditions as per the final approval letter dated 08/04/2020 of the Central Government; and The High Court, Bombay vide its order dated 06.09.2016 has permitted the commencement of work in the area of the project; therefore, the Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the Project Authority i.e., the Executive Engineer, Engineering Department, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Bandra (East), Mumbai, be directed to divert a total of 0.0484 hectares of forest land, which includes the area of the Saffron Forest, for the construction of the flyover at Kalanagar Junction under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, as detailed in the Project Proposal attached with the letter of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Forest Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur and as mentioned in the Schedule below. This diversion should be completed subject to fulfilment of the terms and conditions in the letters of the Government of India dated 31/01/2019 and 08/04/2020 and also after fulfilment of the following conditions.
The Project Mechanism shall comply with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India mentioned above. The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Kandhalvan Cell, Mumbai shall ensure that the project machinery complies with all the conditions.
