Order: Whereas, as per the authority received from the State Government vide letter dated 10/05/2000, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Forest Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter dated 10/05/2000, received from the Divisional Forest Officer, Mumbai Condolence Forest Reserve, Mumbai, under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, a proposal for the development of Condolence Forest Conservation Centre and Condolence Forest Park at Mauje Gorai, Tt. The proposal for diversion of 0.2260 hectares of reserve forest area in the suburbs of Mumbai was submitted to the Central Government along with the recommendation vide letter No. 1 above; and the Government of India, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and the Central Government Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, vide letter No. 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, submitted to the Central Government, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra

The proposal has been approved in principle vide letter No. 2 above. 4 The final approval for the diversion has been accorded vide letter dated 18/05/2021 of the Central Government vide letter No. 5 dated 01.04.2021 by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station House Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide his letter No.5 above, informing that the project machinery has fulfilled the conditions of the final approval letter dated 18/05/2021 of the Central Government; therefore, the Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the Project Authority i.e.the Divisional Forest Officer, Mumbai Kandhalvan Conservation Cell, Mumbai, to develop the Kandhalvan Conservation Centre and Kandhalvan Park under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 in the project proposal attached with the letter of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Household, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, as detailed in the project proposal and the following: This diversion should be completed subject to fulfillment of the terms and conditions in the letters dated 09/07/2019 and 18/05/2021 of the Government of India as well as after fulfillment of the following conditions.
The Project Mechanism shall comply with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India mentioned above. The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Forest Cell, Mumbai shall ensure that the project machinery complies with all the conditions. The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Kandhalvan Cell, Mumbai shall submit a report from time to time to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Officers, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur.
Non-compliance with the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and the rules and guidelines made thereunder and any of the conditions contained in this Order shall be a violation of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and legal action shall be taken accordingly.
