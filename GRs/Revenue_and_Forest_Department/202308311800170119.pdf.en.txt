The transferred officers should ensure that they join the place of transfer within the stipulated time as per rules. In case of non-appointment of a replacement during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
6. No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned without prior permission of the Government. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers transferred by the parent office should be accepted. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post. If the leave application of the transferred officers is sent to the parent office, then this application will not be taken into consideration.
If he does not join the post immediately as per the said transfer orders or tries to cancel the transfer under any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against him considering it as a misconduct as the action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. The officials concerned should take note of this.
