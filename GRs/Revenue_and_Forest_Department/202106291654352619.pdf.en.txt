The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to be appointed to posts in the government service. The central government has given. For Persons with Disabilities as per notification dated 04.01.2021
The list has been published on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification List of Post Identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government Decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In accordance with the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure posts in Government service for Divyangs for the posts in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D in the establishments under the Revenue Department and to ensure these posts for the newly Divyang category by reviewing the list of seats in Government service ensured for Divyangs at fixed intervals not exceeding 3 years.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification to ensure that posts for Divyangjan under the State Government are filled up in accordance with Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. As mentioned in it, the Central Government has ensured the posts for the disabled from Group-A to Group-D
