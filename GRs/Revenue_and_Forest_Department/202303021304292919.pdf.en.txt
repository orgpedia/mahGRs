For capacity building and preparedness, 10% of the funds will come from the State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF) as recommended by the 15th Finance Commission. The guidelines in this regard vide No. 1 issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India.
The funds received for capacity building and preparedness as per the guidelines have been made available for projects to be implemented at the national and state level keeping in view the disaster risk, strengthening of pre-disaster information systems, preparedness and response mechanisms, training, awareness generation and strengthening of disaster response forces and systems.
For the utilization of the above funds received by the State Disaster Management Authority, at the State level, under the State Disaster Management Authority, under reference No. The committee was formed as per the decision of the government.
The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee.
Government Decision: As per the recommendation of the 15th Finance Commission for capacity building and preparedness, Government is approving the constitution of the following committee within Maharashtra State Disaster Management Authority to evaluate and scrutinize the proposals/ works to be taken up under the 10% funds to be received from the State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF).
