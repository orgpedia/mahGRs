Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a
proposal for diversion of 0.39 ha. Mangrove Forest land for construction of approach
road with spur line to Mogharpada Car Depot for Mumbai Metro-4 & 4A (Wadala- Mulund-Thane-Kasarvadali-Gaimukh) through Mangrove Forest Gut No.30 of Village
Mogharpada, Tal. Thane, Dist. Thane in the State of Maharashtra;
# Page 2
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent
the proposal for approval of Regional Office (Central) Ministry of Environment, Forests
& Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
Under reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the .39 ha. Un-notified
Mangrove Forest for the above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfilment of certain
conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 5 Accorded 'Final
Approval 'to divert the .39 ha forest land in favour of Executive Engineer, Metro PIU,
MMRDA, Mumbai for construction of approach road with spur line to Mogharpada Car Depot for Mumbai Metro-4 & 4A (Wadala-Mulund-Thane-Kasarvadali-Gaimukh)
through Mangrove Forest Gut No.30 of Village Mogharpada, Tal. Thane, Dist. Thane in
the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of
0.39 ha. consisting Un-notified Mangrove Forest Land more specifically described in
the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e Executive Engineer, Metro
PIU, MMRDA, Mumbai for construction of approach road with spur line to Mogharpada Car Depot for Mumbai Metro-4 & 4A (Wadala-Mulund-Thane-
Kasarvadali-Gaimukh) through Mangrove Forest Gut No.30 of Village Mogharpada,
Tal. Thane, Dist. Thane in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in
the letter dated 01.10.2024 and dated 11.02.2025 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC,
Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of
following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Divisional Forests Officer, Mangrove Division, North Kokan should ensure that
fulfillment of condition No. 1.2 mentioned in the letter dated 11.02.2025 of
Regional Office, MoOEF&CC, Nagpur before actual handing over the forest land
to the user agency.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred
above. Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Mangrove Cell,
Mumbai shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency
# Page 3
and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time
to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities
and also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency
prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this
Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office,
MoEF&CC letters dated 01.10.2024 and dated 11.02.2025, in this order and
Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Mangrove Cell, Mumbai shall report
through the State Government to the Deputy Director General of Forests
(Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
