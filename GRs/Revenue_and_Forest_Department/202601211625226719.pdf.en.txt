Order :
Whereas, Government of India, Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate
Change, Regional Office, Bhopal, vide its letters referred at sr. no.l above, has given
formal approval to the proposal for diversion of 30.115 ha. of Reserved forest land for
installation of Wind Power Project of M/s. Shri. Maruti Wind Park Developers in Satara
District in Village Kukudwad, Agaswadi, Kanharwadi and Hiwarwadi, Tal. Khatav,
District Satara on the area mentioned in the schedule appended to the Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests (HOFF), Maharashtra State, Nagpur letter no.Desk-17/NC/II/ID-
No./(16)/2573/10-11, dated.22.11.2010;
AND Whereas, Shri. Maruti Wind Park Developers, Satara has completed
erection of windmills project at Village Kukudwad, Agaswadi, Kanharwadi and
Hiwarwadi, Tal. Khatav, District Satara;

has approved the policy to transfer the lease of diverted forest land in favour of
Inverstors / Power Producers;

Climate Change, Regional Office, Bhopal vide its letter referred at sr. No. 3 above has

Any deviation from the conditions mentioned in the above order and this order
Shall amount to the violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 & shall be dealt with accordingly.
The Chief Conservator of Forests (Territorial), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict
compliance of the conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in
this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office,
MoEF&CC letters dated dated 29.03.2025, 25.07.2025, 30.08.2011 and
26.08.2014 in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be
taken accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of
Forests (T), Kolhapur shall report through the State Government to the Deputy
Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of
Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur.
