Terms and Conditions:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. The funds are being given to them.
The above expenditure should be incurred as per the prescribed procedure and as per the monthly fund flow available on the budget distribution system. There should be no financial irregularities.
The funds should be spent only after verifying that the technical and administrative approval has been granted by the competent authority as required for the works proposed under the said schemes.
The Controlling Authorities are advised to ensure that no additional expenditure is incurred under any circumstances over and above the total disbursed/ to be disbursed.
5. While spending this provision, the terms and conditions laid down by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed.
6. Before drawing the funds available on the budget distribution system from the treasury and while spending, the working rules, financial rules, provisions of the budget rule book of the state government and all the terms/ conditions should be followed.
7. Limit expenditure to the extent of grants sanctioned and disbursed in the current financial year 2022-23.

Similarly, the detailed payment and utilization certificate of the subsidy being distributed now should also be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and the government immediately.
