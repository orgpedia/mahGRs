Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the
State Government, which was received from The Executive Engineer, Public Work Division,
Kolhapur for diversion of 0.738 ha. Forest land under section-2 of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for construction of Road from Majarewadi to Ghosarwad MDR-
92 at Village-Ghosarwad, Tal. Shirol, Dist. Kolhapur;
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order under
The reference no. 2 Accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to the above proposal for
diversion of 0.738 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 [Chapter-4, Para-4.3], Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final
Approval for Diversion of 0.738 ha. Forest Land.

Government Order No.: FLD-2023/C.R.177/F-10
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.738 ha. Forest Land consisting
of Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under
reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e.
The Executive Engineer, Public Work Division, Kolhapur for diversion of 0.738 ha. Forest land
under section-2 of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for construction of
Road from Majarewadi to Ghosarwad MDR-92 at Village-Ghosarwad, Tal. Shirol, Dist.
Kolhapur.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests, Kolhapur should ensure that, the fulfilment of all
conditions mentioned in the order, dated 25.07.2023, before giving actual possession of
forest land to User Agency.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Order
dated 25.07.2023.
. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection)
Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above mentioned
'1n-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief Conservator of
Forests (Territorial), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the
User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra's
order dated 25.07.2023, in this order and Rules and Guidelines made / issued under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief
Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State
Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry
of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per
procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980.
