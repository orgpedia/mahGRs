The Proposal:
For the financial year 2021-22, under the head Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee Jan Van Vikas Yojana (2406-8711), a total of Rs. 10000.00 lakh has been budgeted. Finance Department Reference No. Guidelines have been issued for disbursement of funds in 2021-22 under Government Decisions 1, 2 and 3.
