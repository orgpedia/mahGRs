Preamble: The Departmental Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the decision of the Government at No. 6 under reference to scrutinize the proposals received from the District Collector for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund for taking various measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State. The Divisional Scrutiny Committee has examined the proposals received from the concerned District Collectors for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund and sent their recommendations to the Government through a letter at reference number 7 to 8. The meeting of the State Executive Committee was held on 06.01.2020 to decide on the recommendations received from the Departmental Scrutiny Committee. Accordingly, as per the approval given by the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 06.01.2022, the matter of disbursement of funds for taking various measures in the context of COVID-19 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Considering the recommendations received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune and Nagpur and the State Disaster Response Fund, the State Executive Committee has decided to meet the expenditure to be incurred as per the standards and aspects of the State Disaster Response Fund in the current financial year to prevent the outbreak of COVID-19 virus. As per the approval given in the meeting dated 06.01.2022, from the State Disaster Response Fund, Rs. 3858.07 lakh (Rupees Thirty Eight Crores Thirty Eight Lakhs Seven Thousand only) is being provided.

The following terms and conditions should be specifically followed while spending the fund: 1) The concerned Collectorate shall ensure that the items are not proposed from other schemes to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus so that there is no duplication.
(2) Wherever necessary, administrative, technical approvals and tender procedures should be followed.
(3) It shall be ensured that all the criteria for inviting, accepting and procuring tenders, where necessary, shall be in accordance with the procedures and policies of the Government in force at that time.
4) While procuring RT-PCR kits at market price, the prices fixed by Hofkyn Biopharma Limited for RT-PCR kits and VTM kits should be purchased at the minimum price.
