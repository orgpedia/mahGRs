The decision was taken by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur. 5, dated 29.03.2023 and addressed to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Aakashta Forestry), Maharashtra State, Nagpur. 6 vide letter dated 08.08.2023 to the Deputy Conservator of Forests (Regional), Nanded Forest Division, Nanded, for permission to open a Personal Property Account (PLA). Accordingly, the Government is approving the opening of a new Personal Accounts (PLA) Account in the name of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Regional), Nanded Forest Division, Nanded.
The Principal Accountant General (Accounts and Licensure), Maharashtra-II, Nagpur has granted permission to open a new Personal Principal Accounts (PLA) account in the name of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Regional), Nanded Forest Division, Nanded subject to the following terms and conditions: The sale of Tendu will be done by the Forest Department through tender in the name of the Personal Ledger Account of the Deputy Conservator of Forests (Regional), Nanded, Forest Department, District Nanded and the amount of royalty received from the sale should be deposited in the said Personal Ledger Account.
2. Also, the amount of royalty received from the sale of tendu leaves should be spent for distribution of incentive bonus as wages to the labourers.
The concerned Administrator/ Office will be responsible for managing the financial transactions on the self-managed account from time to time. Also, the concerned administrator/ office should ensure that the account is not closed due to non-financial transactions in the future.
