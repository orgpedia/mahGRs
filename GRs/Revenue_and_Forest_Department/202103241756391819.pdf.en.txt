Government's decision:
Reference No. 21 of the Government of India has been issued for continuation of 15 temporary posts in the (Non-Plan) scheme under Kundal Development, Administration and Management Prabodhini (Forest) during the financial year 2020-21 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021. The deadline has been extended by the Government Decisions 2 and 3. The Cell Officer (Cell-3), Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide Reference No. Under Section 5, it has been requested to approve the continuation of 15 temporary posts proposed in the financial year 2021-22.
Finance Department Reference No. 4 Under the Government Decision dated 15.02.2021, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period of continuance from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 of the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the Review earlier but have not been included in the rolls.
Accordingly, 15 temporary posts in the (Non-Plan) scheme under Kundal Development, Administration and Management Prabodhini (Forest) are being approved by the Government as per the prospectus attached with it for continuation from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. This extension of reference No. 1 These posts are being offered subject to the condition that they are included in the pay band fixed by the Government.
