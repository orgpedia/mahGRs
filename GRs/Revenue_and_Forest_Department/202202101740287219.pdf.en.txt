The Proposal:
For the year 2021-22, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Project Tiger, the Government of India has sanctioned a total amount of Rs. 1426.25 lakhs as first instalment of Central share of Rs. 592.59 lakh and corresponding State share of Rs. 477.09 lakh. 1069.68 lakh has been sanctioned.
Government's decision:
Therefore, the Government have decided to implement the activities approved by the Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, New Delhi vide letter No. 4 under the said decision. 699 515 lakh is being provided for expenditure under the Statement of Fund Annexure subject to the following conditions:
