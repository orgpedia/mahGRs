Proposed Expenditure:- The expenditure to be incurred from the grants to be made available through the Budget Distribution System in the year 2022-23 under the head of Accounts Head 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 101 (11) State Plan under the Five Year Plan for Forest Conservation, Development and Regeneration (11) (36) Strengthening of State Biodiversity Boards (2406-8696).
(ii) The funds are to be spent only on the basis of the proposals submitted for the approval of the Government and how they are to be verified.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur shall disburse the funds only after verifying that the funds are covered under the scheme Strengthening of Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board (Programme) (2406 8696).
(iv) The funds should be spent only after ascertaining that the proposed works under the scheme have been approved by the competent authority as required.
(v) The funds allocated shall be spent within the limits prescribed for the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines laid down by the Finance Department from time to time and in accordance with the terms of reference.
vi. Care should be taken to maintain proper/ accurate accounts in respect of the said expenditure.
vii. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds in accordance with the prevailing Government Decisions issued from time to time in respect of procurement of materials and other purchases by the Finance Department.
viii. The various guidelines issued by the Finance Department and the Finance Department, Govt. No.43/ Earth-3, dated 04.04.2022 to be strictly followed.
