(4) If it is found that the officer concerned has not joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of his appointment as per the said provisions prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment and Removal of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, he shall be liable to be proceeded against in accordance with the Government Order, No. Narrow 2016/ S.No. 186/ 16/ Office 14, Dy. In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
