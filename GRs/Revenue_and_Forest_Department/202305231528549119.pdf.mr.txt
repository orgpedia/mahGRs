Order:
In exercise of powers under general approval given by MoEF&CC, Govt. of India
vide Handbook of FCA Guidelines-2019, Chapter 4 (para-4.2), Government of Maharashtra
is pleased to grant 'In-Principle' approval to the diversion of 2.2933 ha. Forest Land under
section-2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 for laying of underground Optical Fiber
Cable along the road in RoW of MDR-28 on Somanpalli Mal to Rompalli Route in Sironcha
Tehsil of District Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra.

2. This 'In-principle' approval is issued subject to the following conditions:
1) The legal status of the land shall remain unchanged.
2) User Agency shall submit NOC of FDCM.
3) User Agency shall submit the Record of Rights of proposed land village- Somanpalli
S, (New Comp. No. AB020, AA310 & AA311), village- Kopela -1, (New Comp. No.
AA298, AA296 & AA295), village- Bodela -1, (New Comp. No. AA282, AA233 &
AA232), village- Mangigudam-2B-(S), (New Comp. No. SA161), village
Mangigudam-2B-(S), (New Comp. No. SA161), village-Mangigudam -(S)A, (New
Comp. No. SA147 & SA146), village-Mangigudam -(W)A, (New Comp. No. SA124
& SA125), village- Sirkonda- 1(E), (New Comp. No. SA103 & SA101), village
Sirkonda- (W) 1 A, (New Comp. No. SA083), village- Gagnur -1, (New Comp. No.
SA063) & village- Rompalli (N), (New Comp. No. SA60), along with Stage-I
compliance.

4) The concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests shall ensure legal status of proposed Forest land, before granting working permission.
5) User Agency shall defray the cost of plantation (including ten years maintenance) of 100 trees shall be levied from the User Agency towards Compensatory Afforestation.
As far as possible, a mixture of local indigenous species along with 10% RET species of Gadchiroli District shall be planted and monoculture of any species may be avoided. Details of land proposed for Plantation (Record of Rights & KML File), shall be submitted before Stage-II/Final approval.
6) The Optical Fiber Cable Line will be laid within maximum size of the trench of 2.00 meters depth & 0.6 meter width with cable line under the OFC pipeline.
7) If bank guarantee is not submitted with concern Road Authority (NHAI/ MSRDC/PWD/ZP), the agency shall submit a bank guarantee at the rate of Rs.50/ per running meter and an undertaking to that effect shall also be submitted. If the agency fails to reclaim the land to the satisfaction of concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests, the cost for reclamation will be covered from the bank guarantee.
8) If the cable is going to be laid in the formation width of the road, then additional charges in form of bank guarantee shall be given at the rate of Rs.35/- per running meter. Similarly for reclamation of side drain and for tarred portion, the guarantee will be at the rate of Rs.300/- per meter and Rs.750/- per meter respectively. An undertaking shall be obtained from the User Agency that it has agreed to make good any loss to Forest/ Environment while laying the cable in the forest area and during maintenance of the same in future.
9) For further maintenance of the project, the agency shall obtain permission from the concerned Deputy Conservator of Forests as and when required.
10) The forest land shall not be used for any purpose other than specified in the proposal.
11) No felling of trees shall be done during the implementation of project.
12) No new forest area beyond the Right of Way of existing road should be broken for laying Optical Fiber Cable.
13) The User Agency shall pay the cost of additional Net Present Value, if required in future.
14) No labour camp shall be established on the forest land.
15) Underground Optical Fiber Cable can be laid only outside the confines of the Protected Areas.
16) The User Agency after implementation of the project shall provide internet broadband connection of 100 Mbps speed to Zinganoor & Asaralli Range offices.
17) The Proposed work shall be carried out only during day time.
18) After completion of the project, User Agency will reclaim the area under RoW suitably.

19) The layout plan of the proposal shall not be changed without prior approval of the State Government.
20) This sanction is subject to the final clearance by the Department of Information &

22) The User Agency shall obtain Environmental Clearance as per the provisions of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, if applicable.
23) All other conditions as may be mandatory under relevant Acts, Rules and Guidelines
shall be complied by the User Agency.
24) All the conditions stipulated by the Forest Department shall be binding on User
Agency.
25) The User Agency shall make good of any loss to Forest / Environment.
26) All necessary approvals required for the project as per the prevailing laws of the land would be obtained by User Agency.
27) Any breach of the conditions by the User Agency will be treated as violation of the
provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
