3. The Divisional Commissioner concerned shall immediately inform the Government as to the date on which the officer concerned appeared in place of the new appointment.
4. If the officer concerned is found not to have joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the term of office, in accordance with the said provision prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, voluntary service and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981, the Government decision against him, No. Narik-2016 / Q. No. 186/16 k. 14, D. Those concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken in accordance with the provision in the 23.12.2016.
The officer concerned shall take care of joining the place of posting within the time prescribed under the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, Non-Service and Provisions for suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981. Those concerned should note that disciplinary action will be initiated against them if they do not otherwise join the position of this post.
6. Leave of the officer concerned shall not be granted for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government, and as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the original office shall not accept any leave applications of such officers. If such applications are received, they shall be returned by post to the original address of the said officer.
7. Since the aforesaid order is being issued with the approval of the competent authority, the Divisional Commissioner shall post such officers in other vacant posts, if at the place of posting, the Tehsildar or other officers under examination have been provisionally posted at the level of Divisional Commissioner. The officers appointed by this order, after joining the office of their respective office bearers, shall be deemed to have been appointed as such. The services of the officers temporarily transferred as per the letter of government of 15.03.2024 should be restored to the original position.
8. All concerned shall note that in the event of non-acceptance of the office of immediate posting under the said transfer order or attempt to cancel the transfer under any kind of pressure, disciplinary action shall be taken against them on the ground of misconduct as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
