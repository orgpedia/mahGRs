(a) The funds disbursed under the said Government Decision shall be spent through PFMS system. For this purpose, the funds available under the 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) Scheme should be utilized.
(b) the amount disbursed under the said Government decision shall not be spent on the detailed works approved by the Central Government under the reference letter and unless such works have the administrative and technical approval of the competent authority.
(e) The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Central Government, subject to the rules in force and in accordance with the guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time and in accordance with the modalities proposed by the Central Government.
e) The Controller/ Enforcement Officer concerned will be responsible for following the instructions and procedures laid down by the Government for the proposed major construction.
e) The various Guidelines issued by the Department of Finance, Department of Finance, Government Circular Finance 2022/ PR No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 04.04.2022 and Department of Finance, Government Circular Finance-2022/ PR-2022. No.43/ Meaning 3, dated 16.03.2023 to be strictly followed.
