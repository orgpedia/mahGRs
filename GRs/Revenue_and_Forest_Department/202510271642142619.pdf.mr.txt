Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the
State Government, which was received from M/s Lloyds Metal & Energy Ltd., Konsari for 7.7754
ha. Forest Land under section 2 (11) of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980
for laying of underground slurry pipeline along theroad within RoW of existing road from Hedri to
Konsari in Etapalli, Chamorshi & Mulchera Tehsil of District Gadchiroli in the State of
Maharashtra;
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order under
reference No. 2 accorded ‘in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above proposal for
diversion of 7.7754 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra. wherein it 1s mentioned that, originally proposed
total 7.7754 ha area includes 3.0678 ha. forest area of the Bhamragarh Forest Division and 4.7076
ha. forest area of the Allapalli Forest Division. Only 0.2553 ha. forest area out of 3.0678 ha. of the
# Page 2
Bhamragarh Forest Division in Com.No.SE197 & SE198 is overlapping with the already diverted
area of Surjagarh Iron Ore Mines. Therefore, the user agency submitted that, an already diverted
overlap area is not required for the laying of the slurry pipeline. Hence, requested to exclude
already diverted 0.2553 ha (Com.No.SE197 & SE198) forest area from permission and The total
area required for laying the said Slurry Pipeline is 8.8226 ha, of which 7.4747 ha is forest area and
1.3479 ha is non-forest area.
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general
approval given vide MOEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective and
transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980 Handbook of Guideline, 2023 [Chapter-4, Para-4.2], Government of Maharashtra is pleased
to grant Final Approval for Diversion of 7.4747 ha. Forest Land only, mentioned in Schedule
below.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 7.4747 ha. Forest Land consisting of
Reserved Forest & Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the
User Agency, 1.e. M/s Lloyds Metal & Energy Ltd., Konsari for 7.4747 ha. Forest land under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for laying of underground slurry pipeline along
the road within RoW of existing road from Hedri to Konsari in Etapalli, Chamorshi & Mulchera
Tehsil of District Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra. This approval for diversion of forest land
is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Concerned DCF shall ensure, the area proposed for diversion is a forest area, before actual handing over of the forest land to the User Agency.
2. Concerned DCF shall ensure the rights of forest right holders are not being affected, before
actual handing over of the forest land to the User Agency.
3. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Order
dated 13.12.2023.
4. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also to
carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the
area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create
hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the
non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
5. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that
compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection) Act,
1986 are not needed for the project.
6. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above mentioned 'in-
principle’ and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief Conservator of
Forests (Territorial), Gadchiroli shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the
User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
# Page 3
7. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra’s order dated 13.12.2023, in this order and Rules and Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
8. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
