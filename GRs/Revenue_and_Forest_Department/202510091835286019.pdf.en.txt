Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to
the State Government, which was received from M/s Bharti Airtal Limited, Pune for diversion
of 0.0890 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for
laying underground Optical Fiber Cable of Within RoW of the Existing Road of Akola-Patur-
Charangaon Road in Akola District in the State of Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order
under reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above
proposal for diversion of 0.0890 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letters under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general
approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final Approval for
Diversion of 0.0890 ha. Forest Land.
# Page 2
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.0890 ha. Forest Land
consisting of Reserved Forests specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter
under reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User
Agency, 1.8. M/s Bharti Airtal Limited, Pune for Re-Diversion of 0.0890 ha. of Forest land
under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for laying underground Optical
Fiber Cable of Within RoW of the Existing Road of Akola-Patur-Charangaon Road in Akola
District in the State of Maharashtra
This approval for diversion of forest land 1s also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
DCF, Akola shall ensure compliance of the condition no.4 & 15 in 'in-principle' order.
dated 26.07.2024, before handover actual possession to the User Agency.
DCF, Akola shall ensure the legal status of proposed land for diversion is Forest,
before handover actual possession to the User Agency.
. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Order dated 26.07.2024.
For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the
free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that
Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection)
Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above
mentioned 'in-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief
Conservator of Forests (Territorial), Amravati shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra's order dated 26.07.2024, in this order and rules and guidelines made / issued under
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount to violation of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief
Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State
Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office,
Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as
