The Proposal:
For more effective efforts in conserving the mangrove forest and to bring a positive impact on the lives of the coastal people, the Government has taken the following decisions: Under the patronage of the Chief Minister and Shri. The Mangrove and Marine Biodiversity Conservation Foundation of Maharashtra (MFOM) has been set up under the chairmanship of the Minister (Forests).
The Mangrove and Marine Biodiversity Conservation Foundation of Maharashtra (MFOM) has constituted an Executive Committee for the purpose of administratively implementing the decisions taken by the Board of Governors and the Foundation for administrative functioning, management, supervision and control of the Mangrove and Marine Biodiversity Conservation Foundation of Maharashtra (MFOM).
There is a provision to nominate" one" member of the State Legislature as a" member" of the said Board of Governors. Now, the matter of changing the number of nominated legislators in the Board of Governors was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Two members of the state legislature can be nominated as" members" in the Board of Governors of Mangrove and Marine Biodiversity Conservation Foundation of Maharashtra (MFOM).
The other terms and conditions of the Government Decision No. S- 30/2015/ P. No. 219/ F-3, dated 23.09.2015 shall remain unchanged.
