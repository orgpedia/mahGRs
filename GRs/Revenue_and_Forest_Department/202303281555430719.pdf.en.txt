The proposal: In the 16th meeting of the State Board of Wildlife held on 04.12.2020 under the Chairmanship of Chief Minister and Chairman, State Board of Wildlife, the proposal to set up Transit Treatment Centres in each district of Maharashtra State has been approved.
Jamun Circle under Yavatmal Forest Range, Jamun Destination Cell No. 513 The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has requested for grant of administrative approval for the construction of Transit Treatment Centre at Yavatmal (Bhosa) vide letter No. 3.
Government Resolution: The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted to the Government for approval Rs. 937.71 lakh is being accorded administrative sanction by the Government subject to the following conditions (excluding financial sanction) as per the technical concurrence given by the PWD. :
Before starting the work, the ownership of the land should be verified.
(ii) The cost of construction of the said work shall be met from the budget available from the provision sanctioned under the scheme.
(iii) While dealing with the provision made in a staggered manner, the work should be taken up only after the approval of the detailed budgetary rates.
(iv) Ensure that there is no violation of the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the existing laws/ rules.
