Preamble: The 158 temporary posts created under the Biodiversity Conservation Programme were approved to be continued from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 under the Government decision of Reference No. 5 and 6 in 2020 and 2021.
Now, vide Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval for continuation of 158 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme during the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The powers to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in the figure of Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure have been provided to the concerned administrative departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 10 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 03.20/ S.No.54/ F-1
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the Government decision in Reference No. 10 of the Department of Finance, Government is approving the continuation of 158 temporary posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme during the year 2021-2022 from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
Therefore, the expenditure on the establishment of 158 temporary posts created under the Biodiversity Conservation Program should be covered under the headings 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766) from the provision available in the financial year 2021-2022.
