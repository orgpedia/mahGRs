GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the reference No. 1 circular of the Finance Department, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, in view of the demand for funds under reference No. 2 letter, the Budget Fund under the head Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (2406-0775) has been allocated Rs. 1058.029 lacs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions as per attached statement:
The amount to be spent on this scheme in the year 2022-23 should be met from the funds received through the target-wise budget distribution system under the head of accounts head 2406- Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Conservation of Wildlife (00) (02) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (24060775).
ii.The concerned Controlling Authorities shall ensure that the activities mentioned in the letter of reference No. 2 are included in the said scheme.
(iii) The funds should be spent on the proposed works under the scheme only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical approval has been granted by the competent authority as required.
The funds disbursed should be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
