ORDER: Whereas, as per the authority received from the State Government vide letter dated 10/05/2000, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, had submitted a proposal to the Central Government vide letter No.1 above, received from the Executive Engineer (Turbhe), Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai, under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, for diversion of 0.035 hectares of landfill area in Mauje-Sonkhar, Taluka and District Thane for laying of sewerage line from Juinagar to Sanpada Sewage Treatment Plant;

GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: FLD-1219/ PR No. 171/ F-10
And whereas, the Government of India, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station House Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, has accorded in principle approval to the said proposal submitted under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 vide letter A. No. 2 above. Final approval for diversion has been accorded vide letter No. 4 hereof; and whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Station Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide their letter No. 5 above has informed that the project machinery has fulfilled the conditions of the final approval letter dated 12/05/2021 of the Central Government; and The High Court, Bombay vide its order dated 06.09.2016 has permitted the commencement of work in the area of the project; therefore, the Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the Project Authority i.e., the Executive Engineer (Turbhe), Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai, be directed to divert a total of 0.035 hectares of forest land for laying the sewerage line from Juinagar to Sanpada Sewage Treatment Plant under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, as detailed in the Project Proposal attached with the letter of the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forest and Central Forest Officer, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur and as mentioned in the Schedule below, which includes the area of the Onion Forest. This diversion should be completed subject to fulfillment of the terms and conditions in the letters of the Government of India dated 29/05/2019 and 12/05/2021 and also after fulfillment of the following conditions.
The Project Mechanism shall comply with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India mentioned above. The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Forest Cell, Mumbai shall ensure that the project machinery complies with all the conditions.
The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Kandhalvan Cell, Mumbai shall submit a report from time to time to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Officers, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur.
Non-compliance with the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and the rules and guidelines made thereunder and any of the conditions contained in this Order shall be a violation of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and legal action shall be taken accordingly.
