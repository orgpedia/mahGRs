Preamble: - The Department of General Administration, under the Government Decision in Reference, has directed the respective Administrative Departments to declare the post-wise appointment authority designation under separate orders as to who will be the appointing authority to take the final decision on the matters of direct service, promotion and appointment / posting of Group-B (non-gazetted) Group-C and Group-D posts.
Accordingly, the issue of declaring an appointing authority for Group-B (non-gazetted) Group-C and Group-D employees of the Land Records Department was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the declaration of the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) Group-C and Group-D of the Land Records Department as "Appointment Authority" as mentioned in No. 4 of the following prospectus to take a final decision on the matters of appointments / postings to be made by direct service, promotion and deputation.
