Government circular
In order to ensure the overall upliftment of the third gender community in the state and to bring them into the development stream of the society, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has constituted a" Divisional Third Gender Rights Protection and Welfare Board" under the chairmanship of all divisional commissioners. The board will have one member who will be a zonal level officer nominated by the Additional Chief Secretary, Revenue Department, Government of Maharashtra. In this background, the" Deputy Commissioner (General Administration)" is being appointed as a' Member nominated by the Additional Chief Secretary" at every Revenue Divisional level on the" Divisional Protection and Welfare Board" constituted at the Divisional level under the Revenue Department of the State under the Chairmanship of the Divisional Commissioner.
(2) The Deputy Commissioner (General Administration), Divisional Commissioner's Office as a member of the said Board shall attend the meetings of the" Divisional Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board" constituted under the decision of the Government in question from time to time and take necessary action for the overall upliftment and development of the transgenders.
