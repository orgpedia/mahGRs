2) The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter No. Room-9/ Bhavse/ No.08/ Transfer-2022/1148/22-23 dated 20.09.2022.
Preamble: A meeting of the Executive Committee for implementation of Maharashtra Genome Fund Project was held on 05.09.2022 under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Forests). In the meeting, it was decided to set up a" Special Cell" for the implementation of the Maharashtra Genome Fund Project and appoint 1 Deputy Conservator of Forests/ Divisional Forest Officer to handle the administrative work of the Cell. Guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision dated 25.05.2022 regarding the implementation of Maharashtra Gene Bank Project.
Accordingly, there is a provision to evaluate the project after completion of 5 years of implementation of the project and accordingly seek the approval of the government if changes are required in the project. In this background, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter dated 20.09.2022, has proposed to transfer the post of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Land Records) Eastern Region, Nagpur to the office of Maharashtra State Biodiversity Board, Nagpur in view of the urgent need for a senior officer of the rank of Deputy Conservator of Forests in the office of Maharashtra Biodiversity Board, Nagpur.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: The post of Deputy Conservator of Forests (Land Records) Eastern Region, Nagpur is lying vacant since 29/04/2021.
In pursuance of the above proposal of the Forest Force Chief, the Government has decided to extend the effective implementation of the Maharashtra Genome Fund Project for the next 5 years and thereafter, subject to the reference date of 25.05.2022.
