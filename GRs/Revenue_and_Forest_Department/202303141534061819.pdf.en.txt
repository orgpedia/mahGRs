Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 of the Finance Department, 1534 temporary posts in the diagrams of Sub Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers in the field offices of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and District Collector, Nagpur/ Wardha/ Bhandara/ Gondia/ Chandrapur and Gadchiroli were extended for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023.
2. Under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls. Accordingly, the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and the District Collectors, Nagpur, Wardha, Bhandara, Gondia, Chandrapur and Gadchiroli, along with the Deputy Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers in their respective jurisdictions, have been directed to ensure that no person is deprived of his/ her basic facilities.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: EST-2022/ PR No. 56/ E-8
The Government was considering the extension of 1534 temporary posts in the office set up as well as 154 (including the newly increased posts) temporary posts outside the set up of various offices in the Revenue Department.
Government Decision: On the establishment of the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur as well as the District Collector, Nagpur/ Wardha/ Bhandara/ Gondia/ Chandrapur and Gadchiroli, and the Sub Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers under them, subject to the figures in the zonal office. 1534 temporary posts in the statement attached with the Government decision dated 15/04/2017 herein and 154 (including newly increased posts) temporary posts outside the Figure on Establishment of various offices in the Revenue Department are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the conditions contained in the Government decision dated 08/02/2023 in the context of Department of Finance.
2. Expenditure on such temporary posts subject to reference No. 03 In the relevant statement accompanying the Government decision herein, Raka-No. The budgetary grants for the year 2023-2024 under the head of accounts indicated in 05 should be apportioned.
The Finance Department has directed all the departments to take note that the revised figure in the interim period as mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 08/02/2023 in the context of the Finance Department needs to be approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries and no further extension of time will be granted to finalize the revised figure.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Finance Department, No. Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08/02/2023.
