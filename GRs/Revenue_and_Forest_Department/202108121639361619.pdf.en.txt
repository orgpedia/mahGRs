Preamble: The Departmental Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the decision of the Government at No. 6 under reference to scrutinize the proposals received from the District Collector for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund for taking various measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State. The Divisional Scrutiny Committee has examined the proposals received from the respective Collectors for seeking funds from the State Disaster Response Fund and sent their recommendations to the Government under letter No. 7. The meeting of the State Executive Committee was held on 15.06.2021 to decide on the recommendations received from the Departmental Scrutiny Committee. Accordingly, as per the approval given by the State Executive Committee in its meeting held on 15.06.2021, the matter of disbursement of funds for taking various measures in the context of COVID-19 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The State Executive Committee, after considering the recommendations received from the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur, and the State Disaster Response Fund, has decided to meet the expenditure to be incurred as per the norms and aspects of the State Disaster Response Fund during the current financial year to prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus. As per the approval given in the meeting dated 15.06.2021, from the State Disaster Response Fund, a sum of Rs. 395.75 lakh (Rupees Three Crore Ninety Five Lakh Seventy Five Thousand only) is being provided.

The funds being disbursed through this Government Resolution will be utilized for the following matters as approved in the meeting of the Divisional Scrutiny Committee and as per reference No. 5 Expenditure should be made on such matters as may be permissible from the State Disaster Response Fund as per the decision of the Government herein.
1. Measures for quarantine sample collection and screening : (a) Provision for temporary accommodation, food, clothing, medical care etc. for people affected and sheltered in quarantine camps (other than home quarantine) or for cluster containment operations.
(b) Cost of consumables for sample collection.
(c) Support for checking screening and contact tracing.
2. Procurement of essential equipment's/labs for response to COVID-19: (a) Cost of setting up additional testing laboratories within the Government and the cost of consumable and testing kit.
(b) Cost of personal protection equipment for healthcare, municipal, police and fire authorities (c) Cost of Thermal Scanners, Ventilators, Air Purifiers, Oxygen generation and storage plant in hospitals, strengthening ambulance services for transport of patients, setting up containment zones, Covid-19 hospital, Covid-19 care centers and consumables in the Government hospitals.
