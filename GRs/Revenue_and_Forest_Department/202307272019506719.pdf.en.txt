Appointment by appointment
Anjali Dhanorkar, Deputy Collector (Awaiting Appointment) has been appointed as Joint Director (Administration), Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Research, Pune on deputation.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Revenue and Forest Department
Government Order No.TRF-0423/ PR No.98/ E-2
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: July 27, 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Government order:
Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed under Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Smt. Anjali Dhanorkar, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Joint Director (Administration), Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Research, Pune on deputation for a period of 3 years with the approval of the competent authority in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience.
Anjali Dhanorkar, Deputy Collector, is being transferred to Additional Chief Secretary, Agriculture Department, Ministry of Agriculture, Mumbai, in addition to her deputation as Joint Director (Administration), Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Research, Pune.
Anjali Dhanorkar, Deputy Collector to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Agriculture Department, Ministry, Mumbai immediately.
