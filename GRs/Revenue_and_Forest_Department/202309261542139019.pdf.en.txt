The Proposal:
The Government had accorded approval for continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for protection of Protected Areas during the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
The proposal to continue the temporary posts of 100 Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024 was submitted by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur under the letter of reference No. 5 for the approval of the Government.
The powers to extend the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review and the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review, from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 were provided to the concerned administrative departments under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department.

The administrative departments have been delegated powers to extend all existing temporary posts created after approval of the review but not included in the figure, from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department of Reference No. 8, the Government is approving the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 in the year 2023-2024.
The expenditure on establishment of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 31.08.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
