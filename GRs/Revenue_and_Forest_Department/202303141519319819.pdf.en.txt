Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 of the Department of Finance, a total of 1315 temporary posts in the figure of Divisional Commissioner Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai, office of the District Collector, Mumbai City, Mumbai Suburban, Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg and in the field offices of Sub Divisional Officer, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer under him were extended for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 under the Government decision dated 03/10/2022 in the context of the Department of Revenue and Forest.
2. Under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls. Accordingly, the government was considering to extend the term of 1315 temporary posts outside the figure on the establishment of the Divisional Commissioner and the field offices under him in the Konkan region.
Decision of the Government: Establishment of the office of the Divisional Commissioner Konkan Division, Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai and the establishment of the District Collector, Mumbai City, Mumbai Suburban, Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg and the Sub Divisional Officers, Tahsildars and Mandal Officers under them. 03 A total of 1315 provisional returns out of the 20 returns attached with the Government decision dated 13/04/2017. from March 1, 2023. The extension is being granted subject to the terms of the Government Resolution dated 08/02/2023 in the context of Department of Finance for the period 31st August, 2023.
