The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Executive Engineer (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) As funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
3) The Executive Engineer (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, should immediately clear the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad and Collector, Hingoli.
(4) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
