the prelude
The Government has decided to celebrate 1st August every year as" Revenue Day." 1 has been issued. Also, regarding the celebration of" Revenue Week" in the State from" Revenue Day" dated 1st August through Revenue Department, Reference No. Under Section 4, the instructions have been issued.
Various measures are taken by the Revenue Department to effectively implement the services provided to the citizens of the state and provide the benefits of the services/ schemes to the citizens. In order to make these schemes more people-oriented, to increase the participation of more and more citizens in these schemes and to implement the schemes at a faster pace, the government has decided to form a committee in connection with the preparation of" Vision Document" and" Annual Directory" for divisional/ district/ taluk level offices under the Revenue Department. The Minister (Revenue) has given instructions. Accordingly, the government had constituted the committee.
Government's decision
With a view to effectively implement the services provided by the Revenue Department to the citizens and to provide benefits of the services/ schemes to the citizens and to increase the participation of the citizens and to ensure the speedy implementation of the schemes, a committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Pune for the preparation of" Vision Document" and" Annual Directory" for the Divisional/ District/ Taluk level offices under the Revenue Department.
