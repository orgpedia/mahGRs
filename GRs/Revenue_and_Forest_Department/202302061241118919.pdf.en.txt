Kundalika River Revdanda Creek, Reti Group No. With regard to the recovery of the amount deposited by the Government on account of sand/ sand mining license from Mahadevkhar to Gofan, Sub-Group A-2 through Dhubi/ Hatpatti.
Shri Ganesh Tukaram Baji
Government Memorandum: Letter from the Additional Collector, Raigad No. Khasha/ Gaukh/ Return/ Bazi/ 42998/2022 D. Pursuant to letter dated 26/09/2022, it is informed that Kundalika River is flowing through Revdanda creek, Reti Group No. Ganesh Tukaram Baji for the license to excavate sand/ sand from Mahadevkhar to Gofan, Sub Group A-2 through 600 brass size sinks/ hand-plates, the amount of ownership is Rs. 9,24,600/- respectively. The government has been deposited on 30/12/2017. Despite depositing this amount, the Collector, Raigad Office has not approved the sand mining license of this group to Ganesh Tukaram Baji.
2. Subba, Additional Collector, Raigad. Report dated 26/09/2022 and the Sand/ Sand Export Policy to facilitate shipping through the Coastal Regulation Zone. 21/05/2015 In view of the provision in C4 (E), Kundalika River is being dammed at Revdanda creek, Reti Group No. The Government has decided to refund the amount of Rs. 9,24,600/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand Six Hundred only) to the applicant Mr. Ganesh Tukaram Baji for the grant of license for sand/ sand mining from Mahadevkhar to Gofan, Sub Group A-2 through 600 brass size sinks/ hand-plates.
Therefore, the Government hereby approve the payment of the amount of Rs. 9,24,600/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand Six Hundred only) to the applicant, Mr. Ganesh Tukaram Baji, on the condition that it is confirmed that he has actually deposited the amount of sand/ sand mining license through submersion/ hand tapping with the Government and has not paid the amount earlier.
