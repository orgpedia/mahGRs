Government Decision: - Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur Read No. Under Sections 4 and 7, Forest Workers Cooperative Societies in the state of Maharashtra have been requested to grant approval for continuation of 150 temporary posts required for Kup work in the additional year 2025-26. Read No. 2 The scheme covers 150 posts out of a total of 26,573 posts in the Bann department as per the revised structure reorganization.
Earlier, read the Finance Department No. 2 In accordance with the Government decision mentioned herein, the Revenue and Forest Department read No. The Government of Maharashtra has decided to extend the 150 temporary posts required for Kup work by Forest Workers Cooperative Societies in the state of Maharashtra from the date of 01.03.2025 to 31.08.2025 in the year 2025-26.
Read Finance Department No. The Government Decision dated 6 26.08.2025 hereby empowers all the Administrative Departments to extend the continuation of the temporary posts included in their profile as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the profile from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026.
Accordingly, 150 temporary posts required for kupa work have been provided through Forest Workers Cooperative Societies in the state of Maharashtra. Continuation from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026 is being granted government approval as per the attached statement. Read this extension No. These positions are being offered subject to the condition that they are included in the figure determined by Government Decisions 1 and 2.
