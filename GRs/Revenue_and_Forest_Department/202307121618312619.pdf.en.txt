Preamble: Establishment of Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board for the year 2023-24 under the head (2406-A096) with an outlay of Rs. 500.00 lakhs has been approved and budgeted. Reference No. 3 of the Government Rs. 63.701 lakh has been disbursed.
Finance Department Reference No. The Circular No. 1 provides for the allocation of mandatory and programme budgetary funds required for the first nine months (ending December, 2023) of the financial year 2023-24 to the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEMS) within a limit of 70% of the total annual provision.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide reference No. 2 for Rs. Current Affairs Proposal of Rs. 500.00 lakhs has been received and requested for grant. Accordingly, the government was considering the issue of providing subsidy for this work.
Government Resolution: Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur i.e. Refer No. Proposal submitted as per section 2 and Reference No. In view of the circular issued herein, in view of the demand under the proposal, the Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board (2406-A096) has been constituted to meet the expenditure on salary and other aspects of the Managing Director, Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board, Nagpur, from the scheme of Rs. 55.347 lakhs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions:

The funds should be spent only after verifying that the technical and administrative approval has been granted by the competent authority as required for the works proposed under the said schemes.
