Preamble: The Government have accorded approval for continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Range Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati during the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 6 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department, the Government is approving the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 in the year 2023-2024.
The expenditure on establishment of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati, under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development and Reconstruction, (01) Administration and Conservation, (01) (01) Administration and Conservation (2406 0096)" should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2023-2024.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No.16/ Q. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 08.02.2023 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
