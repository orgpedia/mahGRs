Accordingly, 461 temporary posts created under the Maharashtra Subordinate Trade Regulation Scheme (MSTRSS) have been sanctioned. Continuation from 01.09.2024 to 28.02.2025 is being granted government approval as per the attached statement. Read the extension here No. These positions are being offered subject to the condition that they are included in the figure determined by the Government Decision 1B2.
The expenditure on establishment of these posts shall be met from the grants available in the financial year 2024-25 under the head 2406 Forestry & Wildlife (Post Plan), 01 Forestry, 105 Forest Produce, (01) Counselling by Government Agency, (01) Counselling by Government Agency (Tendu) (2406 0452), (01) Salary and shall be spent for the above posts within the limits of budgetary provisions as may be agreed. Thereafter, the time period for fixing the revised figure will not be extended.
