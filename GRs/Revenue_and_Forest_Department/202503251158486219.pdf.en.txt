The Proposal: -
The "Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee" is functioning under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Chief Minister, State of Maharashtra and the sanctioned funds for sanction of new works for the year 2024-25 have been sanctioned as per the percentage of Taluk wise allocation. Accordingly, the funds have been sanctioned as per the Taluk-wise percentage. Accordingly, 7 per cent of the funds have been earmarked for Taluka Javali as per reference No. The issue of funding was under the consideration of the government as per the administrative sanction given under Section 6.
Government Decision: - For the development of the villages located in the remote areas of 8 taluks (Patan, Karad (North), Karad (South), Javali, Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Sangameshwar and Chiplun) falling in the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee without spending the funds received by the committee from the state government from the budget of Maharashtra and without spending on any other work, as per the rules of the Koyna Seismic Rehabilitation Fund Committee, for the purpose of spending only for the Koyna Earthquake Rehabilitation Fund Committee, the number of development works in Javali taluka is 12 in total and a total of 12 works have been given technical approval by the office of the Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad Construction (South), Satara.
