Government Decision: 166 temporary posts on the land acquisition establishment of the Divisional Commissioner, Pune As per the Government decision dated 11.10.2022, the date was extended up to 28.02.2023. Now the 166 temporary posts in the accompanying table will be filled on the following terms and conditions: The Government approves extension of tenure from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 1) The said posts are not lying vacant or lapsed for more than 6 months.
2) The review of temporary positions includes these positions.
All these posts should be considered as temporary additions to the permanent cadre of the concerned department.
3.The holders of the said posts shall be entitled to dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the orders of the Government issued from time to time.
4. Expenditure on these teams/ posts vide Demand No. The expenditure shall be apportioned from the sanctioned grants for the financial year 2023-2024 by showing the correct head of accounts as C-1, 2029-Land Revenue, 001-Operation and Administration (02) Land Acquisition Establishment (Under Schemes) (02) (03) Commissioner, Pune- 01 Salary (2029 0215) and (01) Land Acquisition Establishment (Non-Plan) (01) (03) Commissioner, Pune- 01 Salary (2029 0082) and (01) Land Acquisition Establishment (Non-Plan) (01) (07) Director, Town Planning, Pune- 01 Salary (20290153) and the actual expenditure shall be transferred to the concerned Editorial Department (other than Revenue and Forest Departments) by the end of the year.
