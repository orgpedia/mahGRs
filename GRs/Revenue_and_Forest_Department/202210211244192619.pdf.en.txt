No.57/ M-11, dated 21st September 2022 Preamble: In case of sudden natural calamities in the State, the time taken for the receipt of proposals and disbursement of relief to the affected is long. Therefore, in order to make it possible to distribute immediate relief to the victims in case of loss of livestock in natural calamities, the government was considering to approve the Collector to draw the amount from the treasury on minus (-) authorization letter under the head of accounts 2245 0315 as per the rates of the State Disaster Response Fund and under the head of accounts 2245 2363 to make it possible to distribute immediate relief at the prescribed rates from the State Fund.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As immediate relief is required to be disbursed to the affected in case of loss of livestock in natural calamities, approval is being accorded to draw the amount from the treasury on minus (-) authorization letter under the following head of accounts to meet the expenditure for the financial year 2022-23.
a) Loss of Livestock in Natural Calamities (as per the norms of State Disaster Response Fund). C-6, Principal Account Head-2245-Finance assistance for mitigation of natural calamities, 02 floods, cyclones, etc. 117 Assistance to farmers for purchase of livestock, (91) Expenditure as per State Disaster Response Fund norms, (91) (01) Assistance to small and marginal landholding farmers for purchase of livestock in lieu of dead animals, (31) Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried) (2245 0315) b) Loss of livestock in natural calamities (other than as per State Disaster Response Fund norms) Demand No. Financial assistance for mitigation of natural calamities, 02 floods, cyclones, etc. 117 Assistance to farmers for purchase of livestock, (92) Expenditure over and above the State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF), (92) (01) Assistance to small and marginal farmers for purchase of livestock in lieu of dead animals, 31 Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried) (2245 2363)
2. Reference No. 2 here. The rates prescribed under the Government Order, Revenue and Forest Department, dated 11.08.2021 have been made admissible for losses other than agricultural crop during the current season from June to October, 2022 as per Government Order dated 22.8.2022. In case of loss, the assistance to be given to the affected persons should be given on the authority as per the standard of State Disaster Response Fund as per the Government decision dated 11.08.2021 of the Department of Revenue and Revenue, in addition to the standard of State Disaster Response Fund, in the account head 22450315 and in the account head minus 2245 2363.
3. While disbursing funds on minus authority, the concerned officer shall disburse the funds after verifying that the Panchnama of the said matters has been done and the loss caused to the cattle is due to natural calamity (after examining the post-mortem report of the cattle). Also, the report of the concerned Livestock Development Officer should be examined and funds should be disbursed where necessary. The report should be submitted to the government in the form of a demand for funds.
These Government decisions are being issued as per the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. 316/2022/ Expenditure-9 of the Department of Finance, dated 28.9.2022.
