The Proposal:
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has been directed to adopt revised procedures under the Memorandum dated 23.3.2021 in this regard. Accordingly, Finance Department vide Reference No. 2 The Government Circular here has given revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Regulation Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, a single nodal agency (SNA) is required to be appointed for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and a separate single nodal bank account is required to be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme.
It has also been directed to appoint at least Deputy Secretary level officers as Nodal Officers for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, as per the recommendations of the Fifteenth Finance Commission, the Government was considering the appointment of a single nodal agency and nodal officer for the funds received in the State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF).
Government's decision:
Finance Department Reference No. As per the instructions given in the circular, the Government has decided to appoint a single nodal agency and a nodal officer for coordination as mentioned in the table below for the implementation of the schemes for the funds received under the State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF) as per the recommendations of the Fifteenth Finance Commission.
