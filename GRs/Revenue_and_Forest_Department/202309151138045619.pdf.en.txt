GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: 3118/ PS No. 03/2-5
Preamble: 09 temporary posts for the office of the Grievance Redressal Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar The deadline has been extended to August 31, 2023. Appeals, review applications and complaints etc. are still pending in the Grievance Redressal Authority. For this, the Chairman, Grievance Redressal Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar has requested for extension of these posts. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these posts.
Government's decision:
9 posts mentioned in the accompanying annexure in the office of Grievance Redressal Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar. 01/09/2023 to D. The deadline has been extended to 28/02/2024.
Expenditure on the above temporary posts in the establishment of the Garbage Disposal Authority, Nandurbar should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2023-2024 under Demand No. C- 5-2235-Social Security and Welfare, 01 Rehabilitation, 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes, (03) (08) Village Relief Authority in respect of Sardar Sarovar Project victims, Nandurbar Office (2235 3141). Thereafter, this expenditure will be met by the end of the year 2023-2024 as per the head book of the Water Resources Department titled" Demand No. The capital expenditure on I-11-4801- Power Project- 01- Hydel Power Generation (10)- Sardar Sarovar Project- under the Scheme" should be adjusted as per the advice of the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar, through book adjustments under this section.
