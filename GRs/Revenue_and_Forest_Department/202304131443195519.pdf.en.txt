Preamble: 43 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims in Aurangabad Division were granted extension from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 vide Government Resolution Revenue& Forest Department No.EST-2021/ PR No. 28/ R-3, dated 30/09/2022.
Finance Department No. 6 In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 08/02/2023, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up all the existing temporary posts in their establishment and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control. 01/03/2023 to Dy. The authority has been given the power to extend the deadline till 31/08/2023. Accordingly, a total of 43 temporary posts sanctioned in Aurangabad Division will be filled up with effect from 01/03/2023. The matter of extension for the current year up to 31/08/2023 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby approve the extension of 43 temporary posts mentioned in the accompanying prospectus for the rehabilitation of project oustees in the office of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Rehabilitation Office at Aurangabad/ Beed/ Jalna/ Parbhani/ Nanded/ Osmanabad and Latur from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2023 subject to the following conditions.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: EST-2023/ PR No.45/ R-3 1. All the posts shall be deemed to be temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to the holders of these posts as per rules.
(2) None of the posts in the return shall remain vacant or under additional charge for a period of more than six months.
3. Out of the expenditure for the financial year 2023-2024 on these 43 posts, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 7 posts in the office of the Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5 Main Account Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare (03) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, Commissioner Aurangabad (03) (06) (2235 1924) (Mandatory) (Dattamat)" and the expenditure under the same head should be incurred from the grants sanctioned for the year 2023-2024. Further, the expenditure on salary and other allowances of 36 posts on rehabilitation establishment in the Collectorates should be put under the head" Demand No. C-5, Main Accounts Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, (04) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons, Commissioner Aurangabad (04) (06) (2235 1989) (Mandatory) (Dattamat)" and it should be covered under the same head from the sanctioned grants for the year 2023-2024. The expenditure under these schemes should be finally transferred to the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation.
This Government Resolution, Department of Finance Government Resolution No. 2016/ P. No. 8/ 16/ A. P.C., dated 08/02/2023 is being issued as per the powers conferred to the Administrative Departments.
