I. The amount to be spent on this scheme in the year 2021-22 should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System under the Head of Accounts Heading 2406- Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Conservation of Wildlife (00) (02) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (2406 0775).
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, shall ensure that the activities mentioned in letter No. 3 are included in the scheme and disburse the funds keeping in view the urgency of the activities mentioned in the proposal.
(iii) The funds shall be spent only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical sanction has been accorded to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority, as required.
The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the terms of reference of the proposal.
(v) The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds in accordance with the prevailing Government decisions issued from time to time in respect of procurement of materials and other purchases by the Finance Department.
VI. The various guidelines issued by the Finance Department and the Finance Department, Government Circular No. No.48/ Finance-3, dated 06.04.2021 and Department of Finance, Government Circular No: Finance-2021/ P.O. No.48/ Earth-3, dated 24.06.2021 to be strictly followed.
