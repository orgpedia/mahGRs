The Proposal:
The Government has accorded approval for continuation of 158 posts created under Biodiversity Conservation Programme during the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 under the Government Decision of Reference No.5 and 6.
Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue 158 temporary posts created under the Sabbas Biodiversity Conservation Programme for the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 7 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Division of Reference No. 7, the Government is approving the continuation of 158 temporary posts created under the Sabbas Biodiversity Conservation Programme in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
The expenditure on the establishment of 158 temporary posts created under the Sabbath Biodiversity Conservation Programme should be covered by the provision available in the financial year 2021-2022 under the headings 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766).
