Introduction: The extended area of the Kagal Municipal Council has not been mapped due to lack of town geology.
As the city geodesy has not been done, the extent and area of the plots in the extended area has not been determined, so the city geodesy of the extended area of the Kagal Municipal Council is necessary. If the city geo-estimation is done, the records of the extent of land, area and ownership of the land in the extended area will be determined. This will facilitate the preparation of development plans within the area of the extended area and grant of development permissions to the constructions in that area. Accordingly, the matter of preparing the map of the plot and the ownership document by giving the Town Geo-Evaluation and Town Geo-Evaluation Number of the extended area of the Kagal Municipal Council was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: The Government is approving the preparation of the map and ownership document of the plot by giving the Town Geo Assessment and Town Geo Assessment Number of the extended area of the Kagal Municipal Council subject to the following terms and conditions.
(i) The proceedings for commencement of town land measurement work in accordance with sections 79 and 126 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 shall be taken up only after ensuring that the provisions of section 122 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 are fulfilled.
e City Geo-assessment of the extended area of the Kagal Municipal Council is to be done, for which the details of the expenditure required are to be taken from the Deputy Director, Land Records, Pune Region, Pune and the amount is to be borne by the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration and Kagal.

The Chief Officer, Kagal Municipal Council will be responsible for the process of making available by the Municipal Council and providing the necessary documents to the Land Records Department.
(ii) It shall be necessary for the Kagal Municipal Council to compute the extent of the area and the expenditure incurred for preparing the map and title deed of the plot by giving the Town Geo-assessment Number.
(iv) The Deputy Director, Land Records, Pune Region, Pune shall be responsible for obtaining from the Municipal Council an undertaking that the Kagal Municipal Council is ready to bear the additional expenditure if the said document is not completed within the prescribed time or for any reason.
(v) The Deputy Director, Land Records, Pune Region, Pune shall be responsible for providing the services of the Land Surveyor, the Nimtandar, the Shirastedar/ Headquarter Assistant from the Deputy Superintendent Land Records Office in the district or the district under the Deputy Director, Land Records, Pune Region, Pune, for the purpose of enumeration of the extended area within the limits of the Kagal Municipal Council and the staff required to prepare the map of the plot and the ownership document by giving the Town Geo Assessment Number. Also, it should be ensured that the functioning of the office where the services of the employees have been made available is not affected.
(vi) The Commissioner of Settlement and the Director of Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune shall be responsible for the strict compliance of the rules/ government decisions/ etc. issued by the Government from time to time in respect of the calculation of Town Geo-assessment and ancillary work to be carried out by a private agency.
(vii) The enquiry should be completed by making available the services of the officers available in the Land Records, Pune Region, Pune Division to carry out the enquiry.
(viii) Proceedings for town geo-assessment and survey of income of the said villages should be taken up using modern technology (drones, ETS machines, etc.) wherever feasible.
(ix) The work of geo-estimation and survey of the said town should be completed within one year.
The Deputy Director, Land Records, Pune Region, Pune will be responsible for submitting the progress report of the town geo-estimation and survey of the extended area under the Kagal Municipal Council to the Government every month.
