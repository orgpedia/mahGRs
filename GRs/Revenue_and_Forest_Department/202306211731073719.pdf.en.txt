Preamble: The Central Government is implementing a scheme to provide 4G services in a total of 2386 villages in the state which are deprived of internet facilities. The programme is being implemented in accordance with the Prime Minister's wishes. On the 27th July 2022. The Prime Minister chaired the meeting. Published on July 27, 2022. In pursuance of the decision taken at the Cabinet meeting, the Chief General Manager, BSNL, Shri. As per the letter dated 16 th August, 2022 and the Government decision dated 29/11/2022, it has been decided to provide 200 sq.m. of land free of cost for the purpose of" Ground Based Tower and Hoisting of Equipment" in 2386 specified villages in remote villages of Maharashtra. Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL), a Central Government organization, has been sanctioned for GROUND BASED TOWER AND HOISTING EQUIPMENT in the villages where it has been requested to provide telecommunication and internet facilities in remote villages. A proposal to provide concession in counting fee in respect of new proposals for allotment of land was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL), a Central Government organization, has been asked to provide telecommunication and internet facilities in remote villages for the purpose of GROUND BASED TOWER AND HOISTING EQUIPMENT. Ruling on 29/11/2022
In the 2386 specified villages approved under the decision, 200 sq.m. area will be counted for the purpose of tower construction and further 200 sq.m. area will be allocated for this purpose. I.
As per the extant policy for new proposals for allotment of land, expeditious enumeration is being approved by levying a simple enumeration fee fixed for" companies, other institutions, non-farmers, and in case of land acquisition (enumeration of area within the geo-measurement area)."
