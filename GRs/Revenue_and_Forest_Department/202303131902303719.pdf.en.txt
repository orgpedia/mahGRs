The Proposal:
The Government has accorded approval for continuation of 12 posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 under the Government decision of Reference No.
2. Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue the 10 temporary posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" for the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 9 of the Finance Department to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 02.23/ PS No.69/ F-1
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No.9, the Government is granting approval for continuation of 10 temporary posts made available by category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" in the year 2023-2024 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Therefore, the expenditure on establishment of 10 temporary posts made available by way of category/ adjustment under the scheme" Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority" under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (19) Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority (2406 A087)" should be met from the provision made available in the financial year 2023-2024.
