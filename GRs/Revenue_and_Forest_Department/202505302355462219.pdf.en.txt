2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect. The above-mentioned officers are being unilaterally relieved from their positions at the government level under this order, with immediate effect, to join the post indicated for transfer. The officers concerned should immediately join the place of posting given by the transfer and the compliance report should be submitted by the concerned to the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik.
3. The officers who have been given 1 year extension under this order in the present working position shall be eligible for transfer at the next general transfer.
The order is a GAAS search and the concerned Divisional Commissioner / Collector is not required to issue a fresh order. The date on which the officer concerned appeared at the place of transfer / posting shall be intimated to the Government immediately by e-mail / post at deske9-rfd@mah.goy.in.
It may be noted that disciplinary action shall be taken against the officers concerned if they are found not to have joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of appointment in accordance with the said provision prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, voluntary service and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981.
The officer concerned shall take care of joining the place of posting within the time prescribed under the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, Non-Service and Provisions for suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981. Those concerned should also note that their absence will be treated as a non-deceasing day if they do not otherwise join the position of this post.
7. Leave shall not be granted for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government if the officer concerned fails to join the place of posting as transferred within the prescribed period. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of such officers. If such applications are received, they shall be returned by post to the original address of the said officer.
Since the aforesaid order is being issued with the approval of the competent authority, the SHAT officers shall be posted by the Divisional Commissioner in other vacant posts if the Tehsildar or other officers under probation at the place of posting are temporarily posted at the level of Divisional Commissioner.
9. In the event of non-joining of the place of posting transferred under the said order or attempt to cancel the transfer by any kind of coercion, the said action shall be governed by the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
