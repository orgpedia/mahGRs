the prelude
Under the Bhathghar project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Sonarwadi-Kondgaon, Dhangarvasti-Pangari and Dere in Bhor taluka of Pune district have been taken up under reference No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted under the government decisions here. For payment of civic amenities works on the progress route for which the said administrative approval has been granted, vide reference No. 5 The proposal for the demand of funds has been submitted to the Government by the Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for civic amenities works in progress under the proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the Bhatghar Project, the Government is approving a total amount of Rs. 48,54,926/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lakh Forty Four Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty Six only) for the civic amenities works on the progress path in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Sonarwadi-Kondagaon, Dhangarvasti-Pangari and Dere in Bhor taluka of Pune district for the current financial year 2022-2023 subject to the following conditions.

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
(4) The Executive Engineer, Rural Water Supply Department, Zilla Parishad, Pune shall immediately clear the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received.
