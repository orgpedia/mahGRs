Preamble: 20% of the funds for disaster mitigation are to be received from National Disaster Response Fund and State Disaster Response Fund as per the recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission. Guidelines in this regard have been issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide No. 1 has been issued.
As per the guidelines, the funds received for disaster mitigation have been provided for mitigation measures for projects to be implemented keeping in view the disaster risk, strengthening of disaster early warning system, preparedness and response mechanisms, training, awareness generation and strengthening of disaster response force and systems.
For the utilization of the above funds received by the State Disaster Management Authority, at the State level, under the State Disaster Management Authority, under reference No. 2 A committee was constituted to evaluate the proposals received for disaster mitigation under the government decision here. The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee.
Government Decision: As per the recommendation of the 15th Finance Commission, the Government of Maharashtra is approving the constitution of the following committees within the Maharashtra State Disaster Management Authority to evaluate the proposals received for disaster mitigation under the 20% funds received from the National Disaster Response Fund and State Disaster Response Fund from the Central Government and to recommend to the State Executive Committee in accordance with the proposal.
