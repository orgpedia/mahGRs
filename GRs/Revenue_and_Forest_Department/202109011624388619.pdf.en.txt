GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government, under the authority of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 and rules made thereunder, is hereby approving the implementation of a special drive for providing free of cost copies of G.No. 7/12 Updated Record of Rights received by computerized digital signature from the Mandawali developed under the Digital Land Records Modernization Programme, e-Mahabhoomi, to every account holder through the respective Talathas in the village.
The campaign will be launched on October 2, 2021 (Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti).
The Commissioner of Settlement and Director, Land Records, State of Maharashtra, Pune will be responsible for providing necessary technical assistance to all concerned.
