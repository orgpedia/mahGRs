(3) The meeting No. APR 2022/ No. 67/22/ APR-2, Dy. 23.09.2022 Preamble: The heavy rainfall in Satara district in July 2021 has caused extensive landslides in Patan taluks causing loss of life and property. The government was considering the permanent rehabilitation of 7 landslide-affected villages of Ambeghar Khala, Ambeghar Varchhe, Dhokawale, Mirgaon, Humbarli, Shidrukwadi and Jitkarwadi (Jintti).
Government Decision: Between July 21 and July 24, 2021, heavy rains have caused massive landslides/ landslides in the talukas of Patan, Javali, Mahabaleshwar, Wai and Satara, killing 34 people and causing financial losses. To avoid such incidents in these taluks due to possible heavy rains, a survey has been conducted by the Geological Survey of India (GSI) department and they have recommended the rehabilitation of some villages. Accordingly, it is proposed to permanently rehabilitate 7 villages namely Ambeghar Lower, Ambeghar Upper, Dhokawale, Mirgaon, Humbarli, Shidrukwadi, Jitkarwadi (Jinti) in Patan taluka with the resolution of District Disaster Management Authority.
