Order:
Whereas, the Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State,
Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for diversion of 84.41 ha. Forest land in favour of Karnataka EMTA Coal Mines Ltd. for Baranj Open Cast Coal Mine to Government of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Government of Maharashtra has submitted above proposal to MoEF, Govt. of India vide letter under reference No.2,
AND whereas, the MoEF&CC, Govt. of India after due consideration, vide letter
under reference No.3, accorded 'In-Principle' approval to divert the 84.41 ha. Forest Land in favour of Karnataka EMTA Coal Mines Ltd for the above project under section
2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the MoEF&CC, Govt. of India, vide letter under reference No.5,
conveyed its approval (on the basis of recommendation of Ministry of Coal) to transfer the Forest Clearance from Original user i.e. M/s. Karnataka EMTA Coal Mines Ltd in
whose favour In-principle approval was accorded, to new User Agency i.e.

the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions in 'In-Principle' approval letter to the MoEF&CC, Government of India;
AND whereas, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 6 accorded â€˜Final approval' to to divert the 84.41 ha. Forest Land in favour of M/s. Karnataka Power Corporation Ltd under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.7 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the Forest Area of 84.41 ha. consisting of Protected Forest / Zudpi Jungle Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. M/s.Karnataka Power Corporation Ltd for Baranj Open Cast Coal Mine in Chandrapur District.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dt.09.12.2011, dt.13.05.2015 and dated 22.22.2021 of MoEF&CC, New Delhi. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. The User Agency must comply with the mitigation measures suggested by the committee under chairmanship of APCCF &WL, East-Nagpur.
2. The User Agency shall compensate the private owners of forest land if any,
in accordance with the prevalent laws, rules & guidelines.
3. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the letters dt.09.12.2011, dt.13.05.2015 and dt.22.22.2021 of MoEF&CC, New Delhi, referred above. The Conservator of Forests (T), Chandrapur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in MoEF&CC letters dt.09.12.2011, dt.13.05.2015 and dt.22.22.2021, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T), Chandrapur shall report through the State Government to the Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
