Preamble Under the Purna Project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Aundha Nagnath Taluka of Hingoli district affected by Yeldari and Siddheshwar Dam have been taken up under reference No. 4 Administrative approval has been granted as per the government decision here. Also, the No. 5 Funds have already been disbursed as per the order here. The progress/ completed civic amenities works have been approved by the office of the Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation), Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad vide Reference No. A proposal seeking funds was submitted to the government. To correct the errors in the proposal, Reference No. 9 was issued by the government. Accordingly, the Deputy Commissioner (Rehabilitation), Office of the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad, vide Reference No. 8 A proposal has been submitted seeking funds by submitting a report to correct the errors in the letter.
Accordingly, the government was considering providing funds to the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Aundha Nagnath taluka of Hingoli district affected by the Yeldari and Siddheshwar dams under the Purna project.

The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Executive Engineer (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Executive Engineer (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, shall immediately clear the pending dues of the civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad and the concerned District Collector.
(3) The expenditure shall be made on the same civic amenities works as provided under this Order. Also, the Executive Engineer should ensure that no expenditure is incurred in excess of the sanctioned grant while spending this fund.
4) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
5) The main document mentioned in the order is Demand No. The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned system shall ensure that no funds under the head of C-10, 4701, Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01-Major Irrigation-Commercial, 001-Operation and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat) shall remain unutilized or the funds received under this order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned system.
6) The main document mentioned in the order is Demand No. The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned agency shall ensure that no short payment of one year's time is pending under the head of C-10, 4701, Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01-Major Irrigation Commercial, 001-Operation and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects (Programme), 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat).
(7) The main demand No. The Controlling Officer concerned shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure under the head of C-10, 4701, Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01-Major Irrigation Commercial, 001 Operation and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat) and submit them to the Accountant General as per the prevailing accounting system of the Government. Accordingly, the work of reconciliation of expenditure under this head, appropriation accounts, etc. should be done by the concerned as per the prevailing practice.
