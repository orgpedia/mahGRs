Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the
State Government, which was received from Vodafone Idea Telecom Infrastructure Ltd, Pune for
re-diversion of 0.4690 ha forest land for laying of Optical Fiber Cable along the RoW of
Gadhinglaj-Ajra-Halkarni (RHS Side) in Sindhudurg District in the state of Maharashtra.
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order under
reference No. 2 accorded 'in-principle' approval with certain conditions to above proposal for
re-diversion of 0.4690 ha. Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final Approval for re-
diversion of 0.4690 ha. Forest Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.4690 ha. Forest Land consisting
of Undecided PF & Reserved Forest land specifically described in the proposal submitted vide
letter under reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be re-diverted to the
User Agency, 1.8. M/s. Vodafone Idea Telecom Infrastructure Ltd, Pune for laying of Optical
Fiber Cable along the RoW of Gadhinglaj-Ajra-Halkarni (RHS Side) in Sindhudurg District in
the state of Maharashtra.

This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forests (T), Sawantwadi shall ensure compliance of the condition
no. 2,5,6 & 15 in 'in-principle' order dated 11.09.2025, before actual handling over the
forest land to the user agency.
2. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
Order dated 11.09.2025.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also to
carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry 11 the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the
non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that
Compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection)
Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
5. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above mentioned
'in-principle 'and in this order of the Government of Maharashtra. the Chief Conservator of
Forests (T), Kolhapur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User
Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra's order dated 11.09.2025, in this order and rules and guidelines made / issued under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 shall amount to violation of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be _ taken
accordingly.
7. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State
Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry
of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980.
