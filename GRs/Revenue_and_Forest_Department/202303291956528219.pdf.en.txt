The Proposal:
The Government has decided to provide six months basic training to the newly appointed Talathi employees before giving the first appointment to the post of Talathi under the State Training Policy. (2) Here. The decision was taken on 24/03/2014. Accordingly, a total of 129 trainees have been trained at Marathwada Revenue Training Prabodhini, Aurangabad in five batches from January 2020 to November 2023. Accordingly, the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad has sanctioned Rs. 2,11,48, 800/- has been requested to be made available to the Training Centre.
Similarly, the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati has released training fee of Rs. 3,09,60, 000/- has been requested.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to fund the training fee provided under the State Training Policy for the financial year 2022-2023 (2052 5205) from the balance fund sanctioned under this head.
