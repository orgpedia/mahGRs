The Government of India has enacted the Disaster Management Act, 2005 to deal with various disaster related matters such as prevention, shelter, preparedness, response, relief and rehabilitation.
Among other things, the state. The Maharashtra State Disaster Management Authority has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister.
