Government circular:
The said Reference No.1 was issued under the Chairmanship of the Divisional Commissioner for deciding the appeals of forest rights claims in the scheduled areas rejected by the District Level Forest Rights Committee under the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006 and Rules 2008 and Amended Rules, 2012. 1 The Divisional Forest Rights Committee has been formed as per the decision of the government here. The composition of the Committee is as per the Government decision and it is headed by the Divisional Commissioner for the Revenue Division and includes as its members a representative of the Chief Conservator of Forests not below the rank of Chief Conservator of Forests or Deputy Chief Conservator of Forests of the Revenue Division. Since the structure of the Forest Department is independent of the revenue departments of the government, the Chief Conservator of Forests is being nominated as follows in accordance with the work of the Divisional Forest Rights Committee.
