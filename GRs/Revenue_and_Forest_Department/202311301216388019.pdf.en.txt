Government Memorandum: In the auction held on 17/01/2014 for the year 2013-14 in Nandurbar district, Mauje Sasde, Tt. Sachin Ramchandra Thorat, G. Sangli were approved for the highest bid of Rs. 1,70,02, 222/-.
The said sand block was sanctioned to the auctioneers as per the agreement entered into by the auctioneers and after confirming the amount of auction by the auctioneers vide order dated 12/02/2014 of the Collector, Nandurbar. In accordance with this order, the possession of the said sand was given to the auctioneer. Issued on 22/02/2014.
Due to the resentment of the villagers, the auctioneers gave a statement that they are closing the sand pier from 31/05/2014. Additional District Magistrate Nandurbar has issued orders in this regard. As per the letter dated 01/06/2004, Tahsildar Shahada was given the said sand from the ghat. It was informed that from 31/05/2014 onwards, it will be ensured that sand is not transported through quarrying. He also directed that the sand pit should be closed immediately and a complete inspection of the sand pit should be done. Accordingly, Tehsildar Shahada submitted a report to Collector Nandurbar regarding the excavation of 2200 brass sand by the auctioneers at the end of 30/05/2014.
