The Proposal -
The post of Deputy Collector (Encroachment) has been given additional charge in respect of land matters on the establishment of the Collectorate, Chandrapur. The regular charge and the additional charge of the post of Deputy Collector (Encroachment) are affecting the land affairs. 2 is reported in the letter here. Revenue and Forest Department Reference No. 3 As per Annexure-III of the Government Decision hereunder, the designations in the office of Divisional Commissioner and Collector have been changed. Also, except Chandrapur
The designation Deputy Collector (Encroachment) does not exist. Hence, the designation of Deputy Collector (Encroachment) at the Collectorate Chandrapur should be changed to Deputy Collector (Land).
The matter was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decision-
In the present case, the government is changing the designation of "Sub-District Officer (Encroachment)" to "Sub-District Officer (Land)" at the Collectorate Chandrapur. This should be reported to the establishment of the Revenue and Forest Department.
