(2) The number of employees to be transferred to the post of Superintendents, including the number of officers in service, created for the post in which they are presently working, shall continue to hold the post temporarily for a period of 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier, had they continued in service.
The superannuation of the officers and employees who have been retired before the decision of the relevant Government shall continue for a temporary period of 11 months or till the date on which they retire, whichever is earlier, by creating a superannuation for the post in which they were working before their retirement.
A total number of 16 superannuated posts viz., 1 in Group-A cadre, 4 in Group-B cadre, 8 in Group-C cadre, 2 in Group-D cadre and Mrs. N.H. Shariff Group-C (terminated staff), who have been terminated from service, shall be created on the establishment of the Inspector General and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune and their subordinate establishments from the date mentioned in the return 1 and 2 and the services of the concerned staff shall be transferred to that number of post.
The monthly salary and allowances that the concerned employees were receiving before being transferred to a higher post, should be paid to them until further orders.
The expenditure on the salary of the above number of posts should be covered under the head of salary accounts of the department in which the concerned employee is presently working and should be met from the sanctioned grant of that financial year.
