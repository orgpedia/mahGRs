Preamble: In the petition No. 305/1995 regarding encroachment in Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, Hon.
As per the order of the High Court, 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest created under the scheme" Creation of posts for Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali" has been approved by the Government for continuation in the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 as per the Government decision in Reference No. 2 and 3.
2. Now, vide letter Reference No. 5 to the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, in Petition No. 305/1995 regarding encroachment in Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali, the Hon'ble Chief Conservator of Forests, Government of Maharashtra, has directed that no encroachment shall be allowed in the Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali. As per the orders of the High Court, a proposal has been submitted for the approval of the Government to continue the 1 temporary post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in the cadre created under the scheme' Creation of posts for Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Borivali' from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 in the year 2021-2022.
The Department of Finance has been empowered to extend the term of temporary posts included in the format of the department as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of review earlier but not included in the format from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022. The administrative departments have been provided under the Government Decision No. 7.
