Preamble: 84.41 of Chandrapur District for Baranj Open Cast Mine. The proposal for diversion of forest area under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 has been submitted many years ago. Project area taking into account the changes in wildlife policies in the recent past
Studies on whether or not wildlife areas or wildlife/ tiger corridors are included
The state government has decided to form a committee to look into the matter.
Government's decision:
The Committee is being constituted to study whether the project area of Chandrapur district for Baranj Open Cast Mine is included in the wildlife area or wildlife/ tiger corridor and accordingly the requirement of wildlife clearance/ mitigation plan etc. The composition of the committee will be as follows:
Composition of the Committee: 1) Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife) East, Nagpur Chairman 2) Representative Member of National Tiger Conservation Authority 3) Representative of Eco Pro Society, Chandrapur, Non-official Member Member of State Board of Wildlife
The Committee shall study in detail whether the project area is included in wildlife area or wildlife/ tiger corridor and submit a report to the Government as to whether wildlife clearance/ mitigation plans are required in the case. in the committee.
Other officers may be added as associate members as required.
