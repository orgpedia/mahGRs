GOVERNMENT MEMORY: Under the Finance Division, Reference No. 1, Government Decision, dated 29th May, 2020, it has been decided to provide insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance to the employees who die due to COVID-19 virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
Late Atish Nandkumar Bansode, Revenue Assistant, an employee working at the establishment of Sub-Collector Land Acquisition (Manjra Project), Osmanabad under the District Collector, Osmanabad has died on 10/04/2021 due to Corona Virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
3. The Collector, Osmanabad has sanctioned an ex-gratia of Rs.10,000/-to the family of Late Atish Nandkumar Bansode, Revenue Assistant, as per Government Resolution, Finance Division No.Parivartan-2020/ PR No.4/ Expenditure-9 dated 29/05/2020. A proposal for sanction of ex-gratia assistance to the tune of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) has been submitted vide letter No. 2 as under.
4. The Collector, Osmanabad has certified that Late Atish Nandkumar Bansode, Revenue Assistant was performing the duty of" prevention" in view of the universal epidemic of" COVID-19" and he was present on duty during the period of 14 days preceding the date of his death.
