19/12/2022 Preamble As per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021, 19 sedentary workers have been given emancipation certificates in Ahmednagar district. As per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labourers Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021, the said 19 freed bonded labourers are to be given an immediate financial assistance of Rs. Letters 1 and 2 have asked the government to provide funds. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
A total of 19 bonded labourers have been issued release certificates in Ahmednagar district as per the provisions of the Central Bonded Labour Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021. The Government is approving the release of an amount of Rs. 5,70,000/- each to the 19 freed bonded labourers for immediate financial assistance of Rs.30,000/- each under the provisions of Central Bonded Labour Rehabilitation Scheme, 2021.
