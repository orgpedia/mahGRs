Preamble: Reference No. Under the decision of the Government of India, the Gramin Jamabandi Project (SVAMITVA Scheme) is being implemented in the State. Commissioner of Jamabandi and Director of Land Records, Pune, has issued an order regarding the approval of the government to open a deposit account for depositing the fee/ amount collected on the basis of the charter prepared by geo-assessment of income in the village under the Grama-Tana Jamabandi Project (Swamitva Yojana) being implemented in the state. 2 has been proposed. Accordingly, under the Gaonthan Jamabandi project, the government was considering opening a Depository Account (DEPOSITORY ACCOUNT) in Bank of Baroda with Virtual Account (VAN) to deposit the fee/ amount collected on the basis of the charter prepared by geo-assessment of income in the village.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government: Under the Grama-Tana Jamabandi Project being implemented in the state, the fee/ amount collected from the charter generated by geo-assessment of income in the village is being approved to open a Depository Account (DEPOSITORY ACCOUNT) in Bank of Baroda with Virtual Account (VAN) subject to the following conditions.
(i) It shall be incumbent upon the Commissioner of Deposits and Director, Land Records, Pune to conduct monthly/ quarterly reconciliation and audit of the transactions in the said bank accounts. Also, the responsibility of submitting the report to the government from time to time will be of the Jamabandi Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Pune.
