Accordingly, the Divisional Commissioner, Divisions Nagpur, Aurangabad, Amravati, Pune, Nashik and Konkan have requested for extension of temporary posts of Talathi cadre. The matter of approving these posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Out of the total 12636 sanctioned posts of Talathi cadre in the entire state, 8574 posts are permanent and the remaining 4062 posts are temporary. All Divisional Commissioner's Offices certify that none of these posts is vacant for a period of more than six months.
