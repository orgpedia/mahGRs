The Proposal:
09 temporary posts for the office of Garhwal Resolution Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar were extended till 31st August, 2024. The Grievance Redressal Authority is still dealing with appeals, review petitions, complaints, etc. For this, a request has been made by the Chairman, Garhwal Resolution Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar for extension of these posts. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these posts.

Government Decision No: 3118 / Q. 03 / R-5
The government's decision:
The 9 posts mentioned in the Annexure to the Grievance Redressal Authority, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar Office are being extended for the period from 1st September, 2024 to 29th February, 2025.
3. Expenditure for the said temporary posts at the establishment of Garhwal Resolution Authority, Nandurbar to be first met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2024-2025 under Demand Order C - 5-2235 - Social Security Welfare, 01 Rehabilitation, 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes, (03) (08) Ga-Hye Resolution Authority, Nandurbar Office (2235 3141) Non-Plan in respect of Sardar Sarovar Project victims. Thereafter, the expenditure is estimated at the end of the year 2024-2025 under the head "Demand No. I-191-4801 - Capital expenditure on power project-01 - Hydropower Generation (10) - Sardar Sarovar Project - Under Schemes "should be adjusted by adjusting the books below in consultation with the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar.
4. The said Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision No. Designation-2016, / Q. 8/16 APC, dated 06/09/2024 of the Department of Finance.
