Bamboo Research and Training Centre, Chandrapur has come under the administrative control of Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Research, Education and Training) and Designated Director, Chandrapur Forest Administration, Development and Management Academy, Chandrapur. Now the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Personnel), Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide Reference No. According to the letter of 3, the government had received a proposal to change the governing body and executive committee. In pursuance of this proposal, the Government was considering the matter of changing the office bearers of the above functioning Regulatory Board and the office bearers of the Executive Committee.
The Bamboo Research and Training Centre, Chandrapur has been brought under the administrative control of Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Research, Education and Training) and ex-officio Director, Chandrapur Forest Administration, Development and Management Academy, Chandrapur. Accordingly, the Executive Committee and Regulatory Board functioning under the Bamboo Research and Training Center, Chandrapur are being reformed as follows.
