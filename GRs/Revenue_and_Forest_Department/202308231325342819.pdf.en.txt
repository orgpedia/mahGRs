The Proposal:
The Supreme Court in Writ Petition (Civil) No. In pursuance of the direction given in Rule 82/2011, it has been decided to constitute a Division wise Civil Services Board to make recommendations on the postings and transfers of officers/ employees belonging to Group-A, Group-B and Group-C in the Government Services.
The Government has decided to constitute a Civil Service Board under the Government Order No. 3 and 4 in the context of the instructions given under the above Government Decision to make recommendations to the competent authority for the posting of Tahsildar/ Deputy Collector/ Deputy Collector (Selection Grade)/ Additional Collector/ Additional Collector (Selection Grade) cadre officers in Group-A of the Revenue Service under the Revenue Department as well as for periodic/ untimely and premature transfers and transfers outside the department to be made as per the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005.
3. The Government was considering changes in the structure of the Civil Services Board constituted under Reference No. 3, 4 and 5 for making recommendations to the competent authority regarding posting, periodic/ untimely and premature transfers, out of department transfers and appointment by deputation of officers in the cadre of Tahsildar/ Deputy Collector/ Deputy Collector (Selection Grade)/ Additional Collector/ Additional Collector (Selection Grade) under the Revenue Department on administrative grounds.
In view of this, in reference to section 3, No. 4 and No. 5 The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Decision No. 2023/ Procedure 194/ E-1
Government's decision:
The Civil Services Board is being reconstituted to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting, periodic/ untimely and premature transfers, out-of-department transfers and appointment by deputation of officers in the cadre of Tahsildar/ Deputy Collector/ Deputy Collector (Selection Grade)/ Additional Collector/ Additional Collector (Selection Grade) under the Revenue Department as follows:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary (Revenue)                    | Chairman             |
| 2. | the Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Forests)                                     | the members          |
| 3. | Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Establishment), Tribal Development Department | the members          |
| 4. | Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (E-1, E-2& E-3), Revenue& Forest Department    | The Member Secretary |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The proposal for the posting of officers in the list, as well as proposals for transfers and deputation, after the competent authority finalizes the departmental cadre allocation for the appointment of officers to the post of Tehsildar/ Deputy Collector/ Deputy Collector (Selection Grade)/ Additional Collector (Selection Grade)/ Additional Collector (Selection Grade) by the concerned office by promotion/ direct service, shall be submitted to the Civil Service Board in accordance with the provisions of the Notification dated 28.4.2015 and the Maharashtra Regulation of Government Employees Act, 2005. On receipt of the recommendation from the Civil Services Board, the proposal shall be submitted to the competent authority for approval as per the instructions in the relevant Government Resolution.
This order comes into force with immediate effect and must be strictly enforced.
