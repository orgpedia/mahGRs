The following instructions should be strictly implemented by all concerned in accordance with financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Bhima Canal Board, Solapur shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities works without any delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for the civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation to pay the dues of the civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Ujani Canal Division No. As soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received by Solapur, the pending dues of civic amenities should be paid immediately and the completion report should be submitted to the government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and the concerned Collector immediately.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer of the concerned machinery shall ensure that no funds under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order," Demand No.C-10, Main Account Head 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01, Major Irrigation- Commercial, 01, Director and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects, 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat)" shall remain outstanding or the funds received under the said order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Private Principal Account) of the concerned machinery.
The Superintending Engineer and the Executive Engineer of the concerned machinery shall ensure that no short payment of one year is pending under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order under the head Demand No.C-10, Main Accounts Head 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 01, Major Irrigation- Commercial, 01, Director and Administration, (00) (03) Other Projects (Programme), 53 Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat).
