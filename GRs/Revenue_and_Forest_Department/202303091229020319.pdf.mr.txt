Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to Integrated
Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India, Nagpur for diversion of 0.432 ha. Reserved forest land in favour of Deputy Commissioner, Property and Management, Pune Municipal Corporation, Pune for
construction of New Ground Service Reservoir (GSR) of 6ml capacity in Reserved
Forest Gut No. 97 of Village Bhamburda, Tal. Haveli, Dist. Pune in the State of Maharashtra,
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
under reference No.2, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0.432 ha. Reserved
Forest Land for the above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;

AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 4 accorded 'Final approval' to divert the of 0.432 ha. Reserved forest land in favour of Deputy Commissioner, Property and Management, Pune Municipal Corporation, Pune for construction of New Ground Service Reservoir (GSR) of 6ml capacity in Reserved Forest Gut No. 97 of Village Bhamburda, Tal. Haveli, Dist. Pune in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.5 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.432 ha consisting of Reserved Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Deputy Commissioner, Property and Management, Pune Municipal Corporation, Pune for construction of New Ground Service Reservoir (GSR) of 6ml capacity in Reserved Forest Gut No. 97 of Village Bhamburda, Tal. Haveli, Dist. Pune in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letters dated 30.11.2021 and dated 10.10.2022 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the â€˜in principle' and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above. The Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 30.11.2021 and dated 10.10.2022, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T), Pune shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer (Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
