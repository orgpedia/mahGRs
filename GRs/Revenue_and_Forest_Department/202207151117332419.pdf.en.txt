Preamble: It was stated that hailstorm and unseasonal rain during the month of December, 2019-January, 2020 caused damage to the agricultural crops in the state. Therefore, the government has directed to send the details of the assistance required to provide assistance to the affected farmers through the Commissioner of Agriculture, according to the criteria and rates of the State Disaster Response Fund. 3.1.2020. As per this suggestion, the Agriculture Commissioner has proposed to provide necessary assistance to the affected farmers for the loss of agricultural crops after the joint panchnama, which is more than 33 percent, according to the criteria and rates of the State Disaster Response Fund. 15.09.2020 and D. 17.07.2020 was received. The Cabinet sub-committee was formed to provide assistance to the farmers affected by the natural calamity. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 16.12.2020, the affected farmers have been provided with reference no. The assistance has been made permissible under Government Decision No. 2.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The farmers affected by hailstorm and untimely rains in the State during the period from February to May, 2020 and the damage caused to the standing crops and perennial crops, are directed to submit a report to the Government in this regard. A total of Rs. 2. 24776.52 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Forty Seven Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand only) has been disbursed through all the Divisional Commissioners.
