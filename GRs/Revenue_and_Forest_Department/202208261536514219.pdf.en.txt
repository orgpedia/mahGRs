Divisional Commissioners, District Collectors and CEOs of Zila Parishads are being invited for the conference from time to time or are being informed to attend it through video conference
As a result, it has been observed that it is affecting the pre-scheduled visits/ meetings of such officers as well as implementation of various schemes. Besides, it has also been observed that the day-to-day functioning at the level of district level field visits and disaster management offices is also affected. Ministerial level meetings and video conferences
And to ensure that the day-to-day functioning at the field level is streamlined and the implementation of welfare schemes is not affected, the following instructions are being issued:
" At the ministry level. The Prime Minister, Mr. Deputy Chief Minister and Shri. Except for the Chief Secretary, all such video conferences should be held only on two days- Monday and Thursday. "
All the Ministries/ Departments are hereby informed to bring these directions to the notice of all the Heads of Departments/ Offices under their administrative control.
