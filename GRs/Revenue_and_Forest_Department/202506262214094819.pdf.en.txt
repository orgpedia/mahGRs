2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect. The above-mentioned officers are being unilaterally relieved of their duties at the government level under this order from the post they are holding, with immediate effect, to join the post indicated for transfer. The officers concerned should join immediately at the place of posting given by the transfer and the compliance report should be submitted by the concerned to the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan.
The said order is an order of discharge and there is no need for fresh order to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner / Collector. The date on which the officer concerned appeared at the place of transfer / posting shall be intimated to the Government immediately by e-mail / post at deske9-rfd@mah.gov.in.
4. It may be noted that disciplinary action shall be taken against the officers concerned if they are found not to have joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the term of office in accordance with the said provision prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, voluntary service and period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981.

The officer concerned shall take care of joining the place of posting within the time prescribed under the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, Non-Service and Provisions for suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981. Those concerned should also note that in the event of non-acceptance of office, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-deceasing day.
6. Leave shall not be granted for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government if the officer concerned fails to join the place of posting as transferred within the prescribed period. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of such officers. If such applications are received, they shall be returned by post to the original address of the said officer.
7. Since the aforesaid order is being issued with the approval of the competent authority, the Divisional Commissioner shall post such officers in other vacant posts, if at the place of posting, the Tehsildar or other officers under examination have been provisionally posted at the level of Divisional Commissioner.
8. All concerned shall note that in case of non-joining of the post transferred under the said order or any attempt to cancel the transfer under any kind of pressure, disciplinary action shall be taken against them as if the act violated Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and amounted to misconduct.
9. To make the said Government Decision available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
