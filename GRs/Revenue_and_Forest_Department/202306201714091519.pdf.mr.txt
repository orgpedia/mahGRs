Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 21.495 ha of Reserved and Private Forest land for construction of Yeve Minor
Irrigation Project at Village-Yeve, Tal. Ambernath, District-Thane;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.2 has submitted
proposal to Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 21.495 ha. Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;

AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 5 accorded ‘Final
approval' to divert the of 21.495 ha of Reserved and Private Forest land in favour of Small
Scale Irrigation (W.C.) Division Kalwa, Thane for construction of Minor Irrigation Project at
Village-Yeve, Tal. Ambernath, District-Thane under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act,
1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 21.495 ha
consisting of Reserved Forest and Private Forest Land more specifically described in the
proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule
given below, be diverted to the User Agency, i.e. Executive Engineer, Small Scale Irrigation
(W.C.) Division Kalwa, Thane for construction of Minor Irrigation Project at Village-Yeve, Tal. Ambernath, District-Thane.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 22.08.2019 and dated 16.01.2023 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of: lowing conditions by the User Agency
1. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle'
and final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC
letters dated 22.08.2019 and dated 16.01.2023, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of
Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests
(T), Thane shall report through the State Government to the Regional Officer
(Central), Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India,
Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act,
1980.
