The funds should be spent on the proposed works under the scheme only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted by the competent authority as required.
III. Please be careful that the funds from other schemes other than the scheme for establishment of Wildlife Disabilities Hospital (2406 2232) are not spent on the proposals submitted for the approval of the Government.
(iv) It shall be ensured that no violation of the provisions of the Indian Forest Act, 1927, the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the guidelines of the National Zoological Park is committed and necessary permission is obtained.
V. It should be ensured that the subsidy disbursed will be spent only on the works mentioned in the proposal which have received administrative approval from the competent authority.
The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, as well as in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
The concerned Controller/ Enforcement Officer shall be responsible for following the instructions and procedures laid down by the Government for the proposed construction. It will also be their responsibility to avoid any financial irregularities.
VIII. Procurement, if it involves procurement, shall be made by following the procedure prescribed by the Government.
IX. Expenditure to be made in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the Department from time to time in pursuance of the said scheme and the guidelines laid down by the Finance Department from time to time in accordance with the terms of reference.
X. The various guidelines issued by the Finance Department and the Finance Department, Government Circular No. No.43/ Earth-3, dated 04.04.2022 to be strictly followed.
