GOVERNMENT MEMORY: Under the Finance Division, Reference No. 1, Government Decision, dated 29th May, 2020, it has been decided to provide insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance to the employees who die due to COVID-19 virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
Late Ishwar Ramchandra Budhe, Top Clerk, an employee working at the establishment of Tehsil Office, Nagpur (Rural) under the Collectorate, Nagpur has died on 28/04/2021 due to Corona Virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
The Collector, Nagpur has sanctioned an ex-gratia amount of Rs.20,000/-to the family of late Ishwar Ramchandra Budhe, Top Clerk, as per Government Resolution, Finance Division No.Sanikya-2020/ PR No.4/ Expenditure-9, dated 29/05/2020. A proposal for sanction of ex-gratia assistance to the tune of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) has been submitted vide letter No. 2 as under.
4. Collector Nagpur has certified that Late Ishwar Ramchandra Budhe, Top Clerk was performing the duty of" Prevention" in line with the universal epidemic of" COVID-19" and was present on duty during the 14 days preceding the date of his death.
5. The death of late Ishwar Ramchandra Budhe, a top clerk, is related to the" COVID-19" virus, the report of the concerned Shalinitai Meghe Hospital and Research Centre Wanadongri, Nagpur stated.
