The Proposal:
The 43 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project victims in Aurangabad Division were approved for extension from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2023 under Government Decision Revenue& Forest Department No.EST-2021/ PR No. 28/ R-3, dated 11/04/2022.
Department of Finance By the Government Decision of 18/08/2022, all the administrative departments have been directed to fill all the existing temporary posts in their establishment and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control. 01/09/2022 to D. The extension has been granted till 28/02/2023. Department of Finance A total of 43 temporary posts sanctioned in Aurangabad Division as per the figure fixed by the Government Decision dated 28/01/2010 have been sanctioned. 01/09/2022 to D. The matter of extension of the current year up to 28/02/2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The 43 temporary posts created for the rehabilitation of project oustees in the office of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Rehabilitation Office at Aurangabad/ Beed/ Jalna/ Parbhani/ Nanded/ Osmanabad and Latur are being allowed to continue from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 subject to the following conditions.
(1) All posts shall be deemed to be temporary increase in the cadre of the concerned department. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to the holders of these posts as per rules.
(2) None of the posts in the return shall remain vacant or under additional charge for a period of more than six months.
