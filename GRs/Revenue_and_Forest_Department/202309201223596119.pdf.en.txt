Under 06/09/2023, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of the existing temporary posts from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of review earlier but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed were under the consideration of the government to extend the above period.
