Preamble: Establishment of Bamboo Research and Training Centre at Chichpalli (T.G. Chandrapur) for the year 2023-24 (4415-0192). 2500.00 lakh is sanctioned and budgeted.
Finance Department Reference No. The Circular No. 1 has been issued regarding the distribution of budgetary allocations for the first nine months of 2023-24 (ending December, 2023) on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEMS), subject to mandatory and programmatic budgetary allocations required for the first nine months of 2023-24 (ending December, 2023), after the monthly fund statements, budget estimates, allocation and control system (BEMS) of the budgetary allocations for the financial year 2023-24 have been recorded by the administrative departments.
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur, in pursuance of the budgetary provisions under the said scheme, vide reference No. 2 to meet the expenditure of Rs. 26.82 lakh has been received and requested for grant. Accordingly, the government was considering to provide subsidy for this work.
Government Resolution: Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur i.e. Refer No. Proposal submitted as per section 2 and Reference No. In view of the circular dated 01.04.2021, considering the demand under the proposal, the Director, Bamboo Research and Training Centre, Chichpalli, has been sanctioned an amount of Rs.20,000/-for the year 2023-24 from the scheme of Establishment of Bamboo Research and Training Centre at Chichpalli (Tg Chandrapur) (4415-0192). A sum of Rs. 9.84 lakh has been sanctioned.
Below are the terms and conditions:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur will be the Controlling Officer for the Fund. The funds are being given to them.
The above expenditure should be incurred as per the prescribed procedure and as per the monthly fund flow available on the budget distribution system. There should be no financial irregularities.
