The government's decision:
1. Provisional posts created for the office of Collector and Project Director, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar and Regional Office for rehabilitation and other ancillary works of Sardar Sarovar Project. The deadline was extended until August 31, 2024. The District Collector and Project Director, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar has requested for further extension of these 33 temporary posts as the rehabilitation work of the project victims is still going on. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of these posts.
The 33 posts mentioned in the accompanying annexure created for the District Collectorate (SPR) and the Regional Office at Nandurbar for the rehabilitation of Sardar Sarovar Project victims are being extended for the period from 1st September, 2024 to 29th February, 2025.
3. These 33 posts shall be deemed to be temporary additions to the permanent cadre of the department concerned. Dearness Allowance and other allowances will continue to be admissible to these office bearers as per rules.
4. The expenditure for all the above temporary posts shall be first met from the grant sanctioned under Demand No. "Main Account Heading C-5, 2235 - Social Security and Welfare, 200 Other Measures of Assistance - (03) (07) Revenue & Forest Department Upper Collector, Sardar Sarovar Project, Nandurbar (2235-3132) (Non-Plan Datta Mat)" during the financial year 2024-2025. Thereafter, the expenditure will be borne by the Department of Water Resources towards the end of the year under the head "Demand No. C-11-4801 - Capital Expenditure on Seed Project 01 - Hydropower Generation - Sardar Saatho Project - Schemes."
The books under this should be adjusted by adjustment in consultation with the Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar.
The said Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision No. Designation 2016 / Q. No. 08/2016, APC, dated 06/09/2024 of the Department of Finance.
