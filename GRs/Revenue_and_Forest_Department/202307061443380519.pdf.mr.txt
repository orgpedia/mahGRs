ORDER:
As per the provisions therein Maharashtra Government Servants Regulation of
Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Official Duties Act, 2005, with the
approval of Competent Authority, the officers in Indian Forest Service were promoted
vide Government order dated 28.06.2023 and 30.06.2023 referred above. Now, the
following officer in Indian Forest Service is being promoted to the grade of Principal
Chief Conservator of Forests (in Pay Matrix Level 16) from the date mentioned before
his name, as per Rule 8 (5) and (6) of IFS (Pay) Rules, 2016.
