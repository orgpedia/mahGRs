The importance of minor minerals in the development of the state is unparalleled. Mineral minerals are mainly used for large, medium and small projects, road construction, etc. The raising of infrastructure depends on the availability of minor minerals. The Government has taken measures from time to time to curb illegal mining and transportation of minor minerals. However, some incidents of illegal mining and transportation of minor minerals are coming to the notice of the government. The government was considering setting up a vigilance team in the coastal areas of Maharashtra to effectively curb illegal mining and transportation of minor minerals.
Government Decision: A Vigilance and Monitoring Team under the control of the Collector, comprising the following officers, is being constituted to control illegal mining and transportation of minor minerals in the coastal areas of Maharashtra and to conduct surprise inspections. (i) Additional Collector/ Deputy Collector/ Tehsildar Head of the team (ii) Representative of CIDCO, Navi Mumbai (where necessary) Member (iii) Representative of Maharashtra Maritime Board, Mumbai Member (iv) Representative of Commissioner of Police/ Superintendent of Police Member (v) Representative of Forest Department Member (vi) Representative of Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat Member (viii) Representative of Regional Transport Officer Member (viii) Representative of District Superintendent, Land Records Member (viii) District Mining Officer Member Secretary
