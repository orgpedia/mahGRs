The above mentioned officer is being promoted on ad-hoc/ temporary basis subject to the following conditions:
a) Vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota will be filled only on a temporary basis. The Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. The General Administration Department/ 16-B has issued a notification regarding the filling up under 28306/2017. Approval is being accorded as per instructions issued vide letter dated 29/12/2017.
(b) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, as a result of this temporary promotion, the officer in the selection list shall not be entitled to any regularisation or extension of service.
(c) The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officer without any prior notice or notice.
(d) The said promotion shall be made by the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai/ Nagpur/ Aurangabad as well as by the Hon. High Court/ High Court. If any claim/ petition is filed in the Supreme Court etc. regarding seniority from the cadre of Forest Statistics Group-A or Main Forest Statistics Group-A, the same shall be subject to the final disposal of the pending petition.
The promoted officer is informed that he/ she is required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting. If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, then they are not interested in the promotion, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, D. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled. Also, if the promotion is denied by the said officer, the 12/24 years benefit of the post of Chief Forester (Group-A) under the Assured Progress Scheme should be stopped immediately.
(5) The option of fixation of pay should be given immediately after the promoted officer takes charge of the post of Chief Forest Officer (Group-A). If such an option is not given, their salary shall be determined in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, Rule No. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The Controlling Authorities shall relieve the officer who has been given the said appointment/ posting immediately to appear in place of the new appointment. Also on page 2 of 3
