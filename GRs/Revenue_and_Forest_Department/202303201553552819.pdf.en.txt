Government's decision:

Approval is being granted to disburse such funds subject to the following terms and conditions: i. The expenditure on these schemes shall be met from the grants available through the Budget Distribution System in the year 2022-23 under the head of Accounts Head 2406, Forestry and Wildlife, 02, Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (19) Establishment of Maharashtra State Zoo Authority (Programme) (2406 A087).
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur shall disburse the said funds only after verifying that they are covered under the scheme of Establishment (Programme) (2406 A087) of the Maharashtra State Zoo Authority.
(iii) The funds should be spent only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical sanction has been accorded to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority as required.
The funds disbursed should be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the objectives set out in the proposal.
V. Various Guidelines issued by the Department of Finance and Department of Finance, Government Circular No. No.43/ Earth-3, dated 04.04.2022 to be strictly followed.
