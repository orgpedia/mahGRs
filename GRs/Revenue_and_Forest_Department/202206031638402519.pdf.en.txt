Introduction: The sale of Revenue Stamps is carried out through various offices of the Department of Posts throughout the State. A commission is paid to the post office for the sale of revenue stamps. on the sale of such revenue stamps. 01.10.2003 to D. The proposal to pay the arrears of 30.06.2009 to the post office was under the consideration of the Government.
The decision was taken by the government to sell revenue stamps to Pune, Konkan, Aurangabad, Nashik, Amravati and Nagpur divisions of the postal department. 01.10.2003 to D. The amount of arrears due as on 30.06.2009 is Rs. 44,51,741/- (Rs. Forty four lakh fifty one thousand seven hundred forty one only) is being approved by the government. Pune, Konkan, Aurangabad, Nashik, Amravati and Nagpur divisions have been given the following relief.
