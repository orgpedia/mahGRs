The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue 104 temporary posts created under the post-plan scheme of Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Tiger Reserve Melghat from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023 under Reference No. 3 and 5.
Accordingly, the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 104 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme of Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Project Tiger Melghat in the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern, with effect from 01.09.2022.

The powers to extend the period up to 28.02.2023 have been delegated to the Administrative Departments under the Government Resolution of Reference No. 6 of the Department of Finance.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Finance Department, Government of India vide Reference No. 6, has accorded approval for continuation of 104 temporary posts created under the Post-Plan Scheme for Conservation of Nature and Wildlife, Project Tiger Melghat from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 104 temporary posts created under the scheme titled" Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation, Project Tiger Melghat" under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)" shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
