Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Abhijit Wande-Patil, Deputy Divisional Officer, Kalyan, G.Thane is being appointed as Deputy Collector and Competent Authority, Zopupra-I, Bandra (City), Mumbai on deputation for a period of 3 years with the approval of the Competent Authority in the interest of public service and administrative convenience.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Mr. Abhijit Wande-Patil, Deputy Collector is being relieved unilaterally under this order from the post he is holding to join the post immediately.
The services of Mr. Abhijit Wande-Patil, Deputy Collector, are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Ministry, Mumbai, in pursuance of his deputation to the post of Deputy Collector and Competent Authority, Zopupra-1, Bandra (City), Mumbai.
4.Shri Abhijit Wande-Patil, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Ministry, Mumbai.
