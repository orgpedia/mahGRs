The Proposal:
As per the recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission, the funds received in the State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) will henceforth be received under the State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF), out of which 80% of the funds have been approved to be spent under the existing State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) and the remaining 20% under the State Disaster Mitigation Fund (SDMF). Detailed instructions regarding the structure, functioning, utilization and accounting of the State Response Mitigation Fund have been given by the Ministry of Finance, Government of India vide letter dated 31.5.2021. In accordance with the guidelines of the Central Government, State Disaster Mitigation Fund (SDMF) has been set up vide notification dated 9.9.2021. The Central Government will provide 75 per cent of the funds and the State Government will provide the remaining 25 per cent. Under the State Disaster Mitigation Fund, the matter of opening the head of accounts for budgeting/ spending of funds/ collection of contributions and deductions and provision of interest etc. was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving opening of new head of accounts under the State Disaster Mitigation Fund for budgeting/ spending of funds/ collection of contributions and deductions and provision of interest, etc.
