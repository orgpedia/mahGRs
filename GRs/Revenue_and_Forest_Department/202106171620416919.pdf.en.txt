Preamble: As informed by the Director, Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun vide letter dated 02/02/2021, dated 15/05/2021 and dated 09/06/2021, the probationary officers of the batch of 2019, belonging to the Maharashtra cadre of the Indian Forest Service, of the course 2019-2021 are undergoing professional training at Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun. Some of the training programmes of the officers under this ambit such as Outstation Filed Activity and Off Campus Modules have not been conducted due to disruptions in training due to the spread of COVID-19 across the country.
The required training of these trainees is being conducted in the online mode in May-June 2021 at the Academy and they will be sent to join the state on June 30, 2021. The probationary officers have been requested to undergo 12 weeks of" Additional on the Job Training (OJT)" in the respective states.
Accordingly, the Government was considering creation of 07 (seven) supernumerary posts of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the Junior Time Scale (Pay Grade Level 10 in the Pay Matrix) for payment of salary and allowances during the period of" Additional on the Job Training (OJT)" for the probationary officers of the 2019 batch of the Indian Forest Service.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: 07 probationers of the Indian Forest Service, Maharashtra Cadre of the 2019 batch will be relieved from Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun on 30/06/2021. As informed by the Director, Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy, Dehradun vide letter dated 02/02/2021, the above said probationary officers are required to undergo a period of 12 weeks (Duration: 01 week) on joining in the State. from 1st July 2021. September 30, 2021)" Additional on the Job Training (OJT)" training is to be imparted in the forest circles of the State.
Accordingly, for conducting the 12 weeks" Additional on the Job Training (OJT)" training in the State for the probationary officers of the Maharashtra batch of the Indian Forest Service, the following 07 (seven) supernumerary posts of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the Forest Range, Maharashtra, in the Junior Time Pay Scale (Pay Scale Level 10 in the Pay Matrix) are being created.
