Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 18/08/2022 of the Finance Department, 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed in the establishment and their subordinate offices of Sub Divisional Officer, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer were extended for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 under the Government decision dated 03/10/2022 of the Revenue and Forest Department.
2. Under the Government Order dated 08/02/2023 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the rolls. Accordingly, 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed were under the consideration of the Government for extension of the above period.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1786 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad as well as District Collector, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Hingoli, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad and Beed in the establishment and in the area offices of Sub Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers under them are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the conditions of the Government Decision dated 08/02/2023 in the context of the Finance Department.
