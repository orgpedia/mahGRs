The Proposal:
Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve The Government has approved the continuation of 224 temporary posts created under this scheme in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.6 and 7.
2. Now, as per Reference No. 8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval for continuation of 224 temporary posts created under the Scheme for Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Sabbath Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 for the year 2022-2023.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 9 of the Finance Department to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of Administrative Departments and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern.

Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 9, 224 temporary posts created under the Scheme for establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Sabbath Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve are being continued during 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve. Expenditure on establishment of 224 temporary posts created under this scheme under the head" 2406 Afforestation and Wildlife, 02, Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Protection, (00) (04) Tiger Project (60% Central Share) (2406 1243) and 2406 Afforestation and Wildlife, 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Protection, (00) (21) Tiger Project (40% State Share) (24069102)" will be borne by the Government of India during the financial year 2022-2023.
