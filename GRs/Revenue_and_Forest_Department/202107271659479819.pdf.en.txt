Preamble: In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has directed to adopt revised procedures vide order dated 23rd March, 2021. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate single bank account in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as the nodal officer for coordination of these centrally sponsored schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a single nodal agency and nodal officer for centrally sponsored schemes under the National Cyclone Risk Mitigation Project was under consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: Reference No. As per the instructions issued vide Circular No.2, a Single Nodal Agency and Nodal Officer are being appointed for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes of National Cyclone Risk Mitigation Project as under:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. | the name of the department                                                        | Single Nodal Agency     | Nodal Officer       |
| 1  | Department of Revenue and Forest (Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation) | National Cyclone Threat |  Mitigation Project | Director |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Director (I/ C) is being appointed as Nodal Officer for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Cyclone Risk Mitigation Project.
Funds for Centrally Sponsored Schemes will be received by the Central Government from 1st July 2021 as per the revised methodology. Therefore, the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) appointed as aforesaid, has decided to implement the Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the Department of Finance vide Reference No. 2 All necessary action should be taken immediately in accordance with the instructions given in the circular.
