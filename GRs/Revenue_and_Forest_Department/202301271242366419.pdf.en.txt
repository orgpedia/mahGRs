Introduction: The state of Maharashtra is prone to disasters and faces various calamities from time to time. In response to these disasters, various training programmes are conducted at the district level by disaster response teams as well as local response mechanisms. The District Disaster Management Authority has proposed a demand for this as there is a need for a temporary shelter center at that time. As per the demand, the technical committee headed by the Director, Maharashtra Fire Service certified the utility of two different sizes of 4X4 meter and 10X5 meter temporary shelters to the districts. The state committee has approved it. A request was placed on the GeM portal for the availability of these shelters. In accordance with that. They were given the work order as the rates charged by the company Life Line Security and Systems were the lowest (L1). The shelter centre will be provided by the company in phases. The work order mentions the payment of the first stage shelter provided by the company in order to pay the payment of the same stage after training the concerned there. Accordingly, the matter of payment of the first phase of payment submitted by the said company was under consideration of the Government.
