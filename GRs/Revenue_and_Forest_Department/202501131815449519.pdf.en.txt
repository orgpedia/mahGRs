Introduction: The State Disaster Management Department is implementing several disaster mitigation projects in the State for public access to information on disaster response measures, disaster response procedures, information on disaster management undertaken at the State level, information on advisories issued by other authorities or disaster management authorities. It was under consideration of the government to provide administrative approval for the development of a website for the State Disaster Management Authority (SDMA) and the expenditure incurred for the same, so that information on the progress of these works and the current status of these works could be made available on a single platform together and a dashboard required for monitoring the physical progress of these works.
Government Decision: - Works related to disaster mitigation projects and mitigation being carried out in the state by the Disaster Management Department and development of a website for the State Disaster Management Authority (SDMA) to make available to the public the progress and current status of these works, a total of Rs. 97,88,334 - Administrative approval is being granted for this cost and the Maha-IT Authority is being designated as the "Implementation Mechanism" for developing the website for the State Disaster Management Authority (SDMA).
