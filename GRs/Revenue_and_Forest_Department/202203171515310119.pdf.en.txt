The Proposal:
The Government has approved the continuation of 14 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Government decision of Reference No. 3 and 4.
Now, as per Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Provisional posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but not included in the pattern, with effect from 01.03.2022.

The powers to extend the period up to 31.08.2022 have been delegated to the Administrative Departments under the Government Resolution of Reference No.6 of the Department of Finance.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 6, the Government is approving the continuation of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Field Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 in the year 2022-2023.
The expenditure on establishment of 13 temporary posts created on the establishment of Chief Conservator of Forests and Area Director, Melghat Tiger Reserve, Amravati, under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation, Development and Reconstruction, (01) Administration and Conservation, (01) Administration and Conservation (2406 0096)" should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 22.02.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
