The orders of posting of Sandipan Sanap and Mr. Chandrabhan Khandait will be issued separately.
(3) The aforesaid order comes into force with immediate effect and the above mentioned officers are being relieved unilaterally under this order from the post they were holding to join the post indicated by the transfer. He has given his position. It is mandatory to join on June 30, 2023 (BC).
4. The date on which the concerned officer has appeared in place of the new appointment should be informed to the Government immediately by e-mail/ post at e2.revenue@maharashtra.
(5) If it is found that the officer concerned has not joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of appointment as per the said provisions prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment and Removal) Rules, 1981, the Government Decision No. Narrow 2016/ S.No. 186/ 16/ Office 14, Dy. The concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken as per the provisions of 23.12.2016.
(6) The officers concerned shall hold office during the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions for Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. 30.06.2023 (BC) to join. In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
7. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the Government for any reason.
Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address.
8. It may be noted that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
