The Proposal:
The Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, has issued orders to this effect. 2 A proposal has been submitted to the Government under the scheme" Training of Forest Personnel" (4415 0154).
For the scheme" Training of Forest Personnel" (4415 0154) for the year 2023-24, 13-Office expenditure of Rs. 14.56 lakh and 50 other expenses of Rs. 231.26 lakh. 245.82 lakh subsidy is available on Budget Distribution System.
3. However, for the scheme" Training of Forest Personnel" (4415 0154) on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS) through Finance Department, 13-office expenditure of Rs. 14.56 lakh and 50 other expenses of Rs. 231.26 lakh. 245.82 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System.
Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds under the head" Training to Forest Personnel (44150154)" by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 5. As proposed by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur and as provided by the Finance Department on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS), the scheme" Training of Forest Personnel" (4415 0154) entails an expenditure of Rs. 14.56 lakh and 50 other expenses of Rs. 231.26 lakh. 245.82 lakh has been approved for disbursement and expenditure under this Government decision
