The Proposal:
With regard to the administrative work and functioning of the Registration and Stamps Department, if an application/ complaint/ suggestion is received through the" Aapka Sarkar" portal or other means regarding the functioning of the offices of the Second Registrar of the Registration and Stamps Department, the Joint District Registrar Class-2, the Stamps Collector, the Joint District Registrar, the Deputy Inspector General of Registration and the Deputy Controller of Stamps and the Town Geo-Assessment Officer of the Land Records Department, the Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, the District Superintendent of Land Records, the Deputy Director of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the Deputy Director of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the Deputy Director of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the Deputy Director of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the Deputy Registrar of Land Records, the Deputy Registrar of Land Records, the District Registrar of Land Records, the Deputy Registrar of the District, the Deputy Registrar of the District Registrar of In the review meeting on" Ease of Doing Business" held under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra, Shri. The Chief Secretary has directed to appoint a three-tier Grievance Redressal Officer in all the districts of Maharashtra to facilitate the administrative work and functioning of the above-named offices.
In pursuance of this direction, the matter of appointing a Grievance Redressal Officer in the Registration and Stamps Department as well as in the Land Records Department was under consideration of the Government.
Government circular:
In all the districts of Maharashtra, the Deputy Registrar of Registration and Stamps Department, Joint District Registrar Class-2, District Collector of Stamps, Joint District Registrar, Deputy Inspector General of Registration and Deputy Controller of Stamps and Town Geo-Assessment Officer of Land Records Department, Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, District Superintendent of Land Records, Deputy Director of Land Records, Divisional Commissioner (Revenue) is being appointed as the third Grievance Redressal Officer in the office of the Deputy Director of Land Records.
If an application is received from the Divisional Commissioner (Revenue) as the third grievance redressal officer, the Divisional Commissioner shall investigate the complaint independently and impartially and report to the applicant/ complainant at his level.
The order will come into effect from the date of issue of this circular.
