The bidding process and the work order:
(a) The Executive Engineer, Public Works (South) Department, Pune shall immediately commence the tendering process of the proposed civic amenity work by fulfilling the conditions in the Administrative Sanction Order submitted.
(b) The tender process for the proposed civic amenities work shall not require prior approval and approval of the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation.
(c) As the Government Rehabilitation Department will provide funds for the construction of civic amenities in the rehabilitated villages under the proposal, there is no need to obtain tender documents from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for issuing work order after the acceptance of tender.
(d) The Executive Engineer, Public Works (South) Department, Pune shall start the civil works immediately after the completion of the tender process for the proposed civil works.
