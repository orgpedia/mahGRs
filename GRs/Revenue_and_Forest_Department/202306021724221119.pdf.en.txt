Preamble Under the Utar Painganga Project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Mauje Garolachi Wadi, Tikhadi, Sandus, Borja, Pimpaldari and Savargaon (T.G. Hingoli) have been taken up under the project No. The administrative approval has been granted under the Government Decisions No. 4 and 5. The Executive Engineer (Construction), Zilla Parishad, Hingoli has accorded administrative approval for payment of the said civic amenity works completed vide reference No. A proposal seeking funds has been submitted to the government through a letter here.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for payment of civil works completed under the proposal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision The Government has approved the disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 1,79,99, 664/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty-nine Lakhs Ninety-nine Thousand Sixty-four only) for the rehabilitation of the villages of Mauje Garolachi Wadi, Tikhadi, Sandus, Borja, Pimpaladari and Savargaon (T. G. Hingoli) under the Upar Penganga Project in the current financial year 2023-2024 subject to the following conditions.
