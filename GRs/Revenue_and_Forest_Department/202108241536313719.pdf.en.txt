Preamble: The Government has decided to provide six months basic training to the newly appointed Talathi employees before giving the first appointment to the post of Talathi under the State Training Policy. 2 here. The decision was taken on 24.03.2014. Accordingly, 13 Talathi (Group-C) cadre personnel have been trained at Marathwada Revenue Training Prabodhini, Aurangabad in four months from December 2019 to March 2020. The training centres are required to fund the training fee paid to the Talathi employees under the training programme.
The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad and the Director, Marathwada Revenue Prabodhini, Aurangabad respectively issued the order vide reference No. 4 and 5 Rs. 10,88,620/- (Rupees Ten Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Six Hundred and Twenty only).
Accordingly," the matter of sanctioning and disbursing funds under the scheme of training (2052 5205) under the State Training Policy was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is pleased to approve the release of funds to the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad for the training to be provided to the Talathi cadre employees of the Revenue Department under the State Training Policy as mentioned in the table below.
