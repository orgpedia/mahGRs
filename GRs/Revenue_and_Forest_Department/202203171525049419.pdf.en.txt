The Proposal:
Establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve The Government has approved the continuation of 224 temporary posts created under this scheme in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 29.02.2022 as per the Government Decision of Reference No.6 and 7.
Now, vide letter No.8, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval for continuation of 224 temporary posts created under the scheme for establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Sabbagaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve and Melghat Tiger Reserve from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Government Resolution No. 9 of the Finance Department to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
