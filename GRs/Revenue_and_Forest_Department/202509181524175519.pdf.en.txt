The Proposal: -
Government approval has been granted for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests cadre at Nagzira Sanctuary under the Post Plan Scheme "Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" from date 01.03.2025 to date 31.08.2025 under Government Decision No. 3 in the year 2025-26.
2. Now, under the letter of reference No. 5, the Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), State of Maharashtra, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for Government approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests cadre at Nagzira Sanctuary in the post-plan scheme "Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" from date 01.09.2025 to date 28.02.2026 in the year 2025-26.
Under the Government Decision of Reference No. 4 of the Department of Finance, the administrative divisions have been empowered to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in the structure of the administrative division as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the structure from the date 01.09.2025 to the date 28.02.2026.
