(Personal Ledger Account) उघडण्यास, महाराष्ट्र कोषागार नियम १९६८ (खंड-१) मधील नियम ४९५ च्या तरतुदी
अन्वये प्राप्त अधिकारात अटीच्या अधीन राहून शासनाची मान्यता देण्यात येत आहे :-
1. The cheques received with the cases pending before the Authority under Section 64, 75, 76, 77, 78 and 79 of The Right to fair compensation and transparency in Land Acquisition and, Rehabilitation & Resettlement Act, 2013 should be deposited in the bank on fix deposit. Also, the funds in the said PLA should spend for distributing the amount deposited in the fix deposit to the concerned after the announcement of the judgement in the case.
. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account and to keep Account operative.
. The Administrator shall be responsible for reconciliation of the Account with Treasury/Sub Treasury.
. On a Monthly basis, the balances in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled with the Treasury Office and yearly reconciliation with O/o The Principal Accountant General (A&E) by the Treasury Office for all types of Personal Deposits
. At the end of each financial year, the balance in the PLA may be forwarded by the Treasury office to O/o The Principal Accountant General (A&E) for confirmation.
. The Finance Department GR dated 05/09/2023 regarding operation of the PD/PLA should be followed.
. All provision of Accounting Rules for treasuries 1992 and all provisions of PLA in view of MTR 1968 Vol. I shall apply to this Pi.A.
8. This PLA shall be subject to Audit as per rule.
