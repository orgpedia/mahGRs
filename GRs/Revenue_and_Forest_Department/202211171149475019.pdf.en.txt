Preamble: To assess the financial loss and damage due to excessive rainfall and flood situation in the State in July 2022, from 01.08.2022. During 04.08.2022, the Central Team visited Badhad district in Nagpur Division. The team was accorded the status of state guest by the General Administration Department. The District Disaster Management Authority, Collectorate, Nagpur has spent a total of Rs. 3,00,629/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Six Hundred and Nineteen only) has been submitted to the Government. The matter was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To assess the financial loss and damage due to heavy rains and flood situation in the State in July 2022, with effect from 01.08.2022. During 04.08.2022, the Central Team visited Badhad district in Nagpur Division. During the inspection of the Central team members, Rs. 3,00,629/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Six Hundred and Nineteen only) is being sanctioned. Costs are detailed below.
