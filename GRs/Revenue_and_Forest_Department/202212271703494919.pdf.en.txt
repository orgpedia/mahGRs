Time-bound programme of selection: (a) The Head of Department and Head of Office under the Forest Department shall review the posts of Forest Guards under their command on a regular basis so as to enable the Regional Selection Committee to recommend the candidates within a fixed time frame.
(2) Care should be taken to send the requisition for the vacancies in the Forest Guard posts to be recruited directly to the concerned Regional Selection Committee immediately and the Regional Selection Committee should immediately proceed with the selection process.
(b) In proceeding as aforesaid, the appointing authority shall send a requisition to the Regional Selection Committee for estimating the number of posts of Forest Guards to be available for recruitment by nomination by the end of the month of August of the following year in the office under his command.
