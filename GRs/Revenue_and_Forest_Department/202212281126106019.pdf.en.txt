the prelude
Out of 9 Operators (Ministry Control Operators) in the State Emergency Operation Centre sanctioned by the State Disaster Management Authority, 5 posts have been filled as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The deadline has been extended to December 31, 2022. Out of them, only 4 operators are working now due to the resignation of Mr. Yogesh Morey. The recruitment drive is being conducted to fill 9 vacancies. However, in view of the time required, the proposal to give extension to the four operators for 3 months was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
The fresh selection process is being carried out as the period of extension granted to Mantralaya Control Room Operators working in State Emergency Operation Centres has expired. However, since the process will take some more time, the following Ministry Control Operators (total 4 posts) have been appointed with the approval of Principal Secretary, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation. from January 2, 2023. The government has decided to extend the deadline till March 31, 2023.
