Read:- Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department, No. CLS-2021/ Q. No. 254/ M-3, Dy. 26.11.2021
The proposal: The Supreme Court has dismissed the petition No. 541/2021 in Narrow Application No. 1805/2021 vide order dated 04.10.2021, to the next of kin of the persons who have died due to COVID-19, Rs. The Government Order dated 26.11.2021 has been issued regarding the grant of ex-gratia assistance of Rs.50,000/-. The Supreme Court It is ordered that there is no need to wait for 30 days for payment of ex-gratia assistance, if it is consistent with the order dated 04.10.2021. Accordingly, the matter of amending the Government Resolution dated 26.11.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Revenue and Forest Department In the Government Resolution dated 26.11.2021, vide para No. 9 has been amended as follows:
" After the receipt of applications under paragraphs 5, 6, 7 and 8 above, the District Disaster Management Authority shall give final approval on the computerized system for disbursement of ex-gratia grant. All approved cases will be available for information to the general public on the computer system. "
