The Proposal:
Considering the Goods and Services Tax Act which came into force in the state from July 1, 2017, a total of 415 temporary posts, 36 at the divisional level and 379 at the district level, have been created in all the districts of Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur divisions of the state for the purpose of collecting entertainment fee, betting tax and advertisement tax, preventing tax evasion and detecting and taking punitive action in cases of tax evasion. The decision was taken on August 18, 2022. 01.09.2022 to dt. The period was extended for 28.02.2023.
Now, under reference no. 11 of the Finance Department. As per the Government decision dated 08.02.2023, the temporary posts in the Administrative Department and offices under them will be filled up. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The power to extend the period up to 31.08.2023 has been delegated to the concerned Ministerial Administrative Department.

Accordingly, as per the provisions of Section 3 of the Maharashtra Amusement Fee Act, various means of entertainment shall be taxed. A total of 415 posts under the jurisdiction of the Entertainment Fee Branch at the divisional level and district level in all the cadres of all the entertainment branches in the state will be filled up till the collection of the amounts of entertainment fee levied till June 30, 2017 and the settlement of the large number of pending accounting paragraphs. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The matter of extension for the period up to 31.08.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
A total of 415 temporary posts, including 36 at divisional level and 379 at district level, in a total of 9 cadres in the entertainment fee branch of all divisional commissioner offices and all district collector offices in the state have been filled. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The period up to 31.08.2023 is being extended subject to the following conditions:
A total of 415 posts at the divisional and district level have already been given by the Revenue Department. If not included in the figure fixed by the Government Decision of 20th March, 2006, the proposal for review of such posts shall be submitted to this Department (to Office of Commissioner E-8) by the concerned Divisional Commissioner's Office for obtaining approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries within the period of extension. After the department's designations are approved, the adjustment of those positions will be handled by Office E-8.
The Deputy Commissioner (Entertainment Fee) under the control of all Divisional Commissioners shall, in accordance with the rules governing the regular inspection of the records relating to the collection of entertainment fees due from the Collectorates in their respective divisions, submit the reports of the inspections for each calendar month to the Government before the 15th day of the following calendar month in any case.
3. All Divisional Commissioners/ Collectors shall ensure that the services of officers/ staff in temporary posts sanctioned for the entertainment fee mechanism in the concerned department/ district are not utilised for any other purpose without prior approval of the Government. Also, if the government finds that these posts have been used in other branches without the prior permission of the government, such posts will be abolished.
The office bearers of all the above temporary posts are entitled to all other allowances including travelling allowance, local supplementary allowance as per the prevailing Government rules.
5. A total of 415 number of temporary posts in 9 cadres as shown in the accompanying' Return A" and' Return B" shall be deemed to be temporary in their respective cadres.
