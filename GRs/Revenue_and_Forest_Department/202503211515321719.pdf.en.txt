Letter from Divisional Commissioner, Pune No. Re / Upper / RR / 305/2025, d. 21.02.2025 Preamble -: As many as 103 temporary posts created for rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune division and acquisition of land in the benefit area were granted extension till date 28.02.2025 under the Government Decision dated 04.10.2024 of this division.
Under the Government Decision dated 03.03.2025 of the Department of Finance, the administrative divisions have been empowered to extend the tenure of all existing temporary posts from 01.03.2025 to 31.08.2025.
Accordingly, the government was considering extending till date the 103 temporary posts created for rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune division and acquisition of land in the benefit area.
Government Decision-: For rehabilitation of project victims affected by various irrigation projects in Pune Division, 103 temporary posts in the Commissioner's Office under 9 Non-Plan and 94 District Level Schemes as indicated in the attached return are being extended from 01.03.2025 to 31.08.2025 with effect from the date of the following conditions.
1. All these posts shall be deemed to be temporary increments in the cadre of the department concerned. Dearness Allowance and other allowances are payable to these office bearers as per rules.
2. None of the posts in the return shall remain vacant or continue with additional charge for a period not exceeding six months.
3. Out of the total 103 temporary posts as above, the expenditure at 9 posts at Commissioner level should be covered under the head Principal Account Head Demand No. C-5 "Social Security and Welfare (01) Rehabilitation 202 Other Rehabilitation Schemes 202 (three) Scheme for Setting up of Manufacturing units 202 (three) (two) (2) Rehabilitation of Project Affected Persons-020- (three) (two) (2) Commissioner Pune Salary (Post Plan) (2235188201).
