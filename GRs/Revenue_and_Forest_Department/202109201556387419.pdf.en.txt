The Proposal:
The Government has accorded approval for continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for protection of Protected Areas during the year 2020-2021 from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
Now, as per Reference No.5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 in the year 2021-2022.
3. The Administrative Departments have been empowered under the Governance Decision of Reference No. 7 of the Department of Finance to extend the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for the temporary posts included in the pattern of the Department and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the pattern.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the decision of the Finance Department, Government of India has approved the continuation of 100 temporary posts of Forest Guards created for the protection of Protected Areas from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 in 2021-2022.
The expenditure on establishment of 100 temporary posts of forest guards created for the protection of protected areas should be covered under the head 2406- Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Protection (00) (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wild Animal Protection (2406 0766), as available in the financial year 2021-2022.
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10.08.2021 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
