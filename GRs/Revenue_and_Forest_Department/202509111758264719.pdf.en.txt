The Cabinet According to the approval given on 29/11/2022, 200 sq.m. of free space will be made available in 2386 specified villages in remote villages of Maharashtra for the purpose of "GROUND BASED TOWER AND HOISTING OF EQUIPMENT," approval by the concerned Collector within 15 days, provision of infrastructure as well as power supply and connection through MSEDCL, as well as right of way for laying OFC cables. Subsequently, the General Manager, Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) cited technical difficulties in some of the 2386 villages sanctioned earlier as the reason for not being able to provide space for the tower's purpose. While the letter from the 24.02.2023 sought to allot land for the purpose of tower construction in the revised 2751 villages, the government decided in accordance with the said request. It was decided to provide 200 sq.m. free of cost for tower construction in 2751 villages as amended under 05.04.2023.
Now, the Chief General Manager, BSNL has given his approval. Pursuant to the request made under the letter dated 30th May, 2025 for providing 200 sq.km. of free space for the purpose of "GROUND BASED TOWER AND HOISTING OF EQUIPMENT" in 930 villages in the adjoining Annexure, the Government has decided to provide 200 sq.km. of land free of cost for the purpose. A proposal for revised list is being approved for the purpose mentioned in November 29, 2022. However, the government decided on 930 villages in the annexure attached by the respective district collector. Action should be taken as per the provision approved under 29th November, 2022.
The decision was taken by the Finance Department's A.No. 403/2025 Expenditure-9, D is being issued as per the consent received under 21.07.2025.
The decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra's website - www.maharashtra.gov.in.
