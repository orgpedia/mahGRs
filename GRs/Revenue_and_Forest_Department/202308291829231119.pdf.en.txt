Government order:
The reference No. Avinash Shinde has been appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation on deputation. However, at present, the No. Subject to the conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer and Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri Avinash Shinde, Deputy Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation is being appointed as Assistant Commissioner, Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2. Assistant Commissioner, Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.
The services of Avinash Shinde, Deputy Collector are being handed over to Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai.
3. Mr. Avinash Shinde, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
