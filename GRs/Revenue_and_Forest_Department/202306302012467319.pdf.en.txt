(2) The aforesaid order comes into force with immediate effect and the aforesaid officers are being relieved unilaterally from the posts they were holding to join immediately in the post to which they have been transferred. He has given his position. It is mandatory to join before 03 July 2023 (BC).
3. The date on which the concerned officer has appeared in place of the new appointment should be informed to the government immediately by e-mail/ post at e3.revenue@maharashtra. gov. in.
(4) If it is found that the officer concerned has not joined the place of transfer before the expiry of the period of his appointment as per the said provisions prescribed under Rule 15 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment and Removal of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, he shall be liable to be proceeded against in accordance with the provisions of Government Order No. Narrow 2016/ S.No. 186/ 16/ Office 14, Dy. The concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken as per the provisions of 23.12.2016.
(5) During the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assignment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Period of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981, the concerned officer shall hold office at the place of posting. Be sure to join before July 03, 2023 (BC). In case of non-appointment to the post, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day."
6. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
If they do not join the post immediately as per the said order or try to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
