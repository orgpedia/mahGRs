Introduction: The Revenue Department is the backbone of the state administration and the Revenue Department, Registration and Stamps Department and Land Records Department handle the affairs related to immovable properties. Various computerization initiatives are underway in all the three departments to provide more people-oriented services to the citizens. It is also proposed to develop various systems of computerization under the Land Acquisition Branch and Relief and Rehabilitation Department. Therefore, an Integrated Project Implementation Cell has been set up under the decision of the Government in the context of operationalization, feasibility, state-wide implementation and coordination of computerization activities in all these departments. This Cell has been made functional and the matter of sanctioning the expenditure for the year 2023-24 through this Cell from the Personal Accounts Department of the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State Pune was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The Government Decision dated 18/04/2022 has established the Unified Project Management Unit (UPMU) in line with the operationalization, feasibility, statewide implementation and coordination of computerization activities in the department. Point number 6 of this Government Resolution provides for the expenditure to be incurred by the Integrated Project Management Cell as follows:
Issue No. The expenditure in respect of item no. 2, 3 and 4 of section 6 shall be met from the self-financing accounts at the level of Registration and Stamps Department for the initial one year from the establishment of the Integrated Project Management Cell. After that, the Registration and Stamp Department will collect the amount of service fee for the services provided to the citizens through the Integrated Project Management Cell.

The Policy Committee may decide on meeting the expenditure of the Unified PMU from the funds collected by fixing a fixed amount/ proportion (percentage) in the self-registration accounts at the level of the Commissioner and the Director of Land Records, Pune. These deposits will be reviewed every two years to ensure that the Unified PMU becomes self-sustaining.
The Committee has approved the subject Funds for UPMU for FY 2023-24 which was presented in the meeting of Project Integrated Policy Committee held on 23.03.2023 under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue).
The High-Powered Committee has directed that the expenditure for the Integrated Project Management Cell for the year 2023-24 should be made from the personal account of the Inspector General and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune. 23.03.2023 has been agreed. Accordingly, as per the recommendation of the Chairman, State Level Integrated Project Implementation Committee and the Commissioner and Director, Land Records, Maharashtra State, Pune, the expenditure for the Integrated Project Management Cell for the year 2023-24 is being approved to be spent from the Personal Accounts Department of the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Maharashtra State, Pune.
This Government Decision is being issued with the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. 139/23/ Co. P. 5, dated 01.06.2023 of the Department of Finance.
