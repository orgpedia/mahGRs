The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Benefit Area Development Authority, Aurangabad as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Benefit Area Development Authority, Aurangabad shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) The Executive Engineer, Aurangabad Irrigation Department, Aurangabad, should immediately pay the pending dues of civic amenities works as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad and the Collector, Aurangabad.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
