The Proposal:
1. Proposals have been received from the Government for grant of administrative approval to various Revenue Offices, Residences and other ancillary works at the district level under the Head Accounts (4059 0799).
The proposals received have been given technical approval by the Chief Engineer.
The budget is based on the State Rate List for 2021-22.
4. The matter of granting administrative approval to the above proposals was under consideration of the Government considering the urgency of the work and availability of funds.
GOVERNOR'S DECISION: The Government is granting administrative approval to 35 proposals received by the Revenue Department as per Annexure-B subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions (a) The maps and plans of this work must be approved by the Government Architect before undertaking the said work. It must also be signed by the consumer.
(b) The ownership of the land should be ascertained before starting the work.
(a) When dealing with a provision made in a lax manner, the work should be taken up only after sanctioning detailed budgets.
(d) While doing the actual work, the Department of Environment, Government should take action according to the guidelines mentioned in Decision No. ENV-2013/ PR No. 177/ D. No. 1 dated January 10, 2014.
(e) the Department of Industries, Power and Labour by adopting e-tendering system for procurement related matters in the proposal. The instructions issued by the Government Decision of 24/08/2017 should be taken into account.
(f) to take action in accordance with the instructions given by the Government from time to time regarding the various facilities to be provided for the disabled in the building.
