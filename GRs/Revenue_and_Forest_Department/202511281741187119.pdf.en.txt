Memorandum: - The Government had issued a comprehensive sand policy dated 19.04.2023 regarding the mining, storage and sale of sand / sand through online system. The revised sand policy has been issued vide Government Decision dated 16.02.2024 amending this sand policy. A sum of Rs. 2,16,71, 607 / - was disbursed to the Collector, Bhandara vide memorandum dated 10.02.2025 for payment of sand depots at Mauje Gudegaon in Bhandara district. However, due to the complaint received regarding irregularities in the depot, the fund has been dedicated to the government by the Collector, Bhandara. The depot has now been regularised. Therefore, the District Mining Officer, Bhandara has requested to pay the GAD funds.
