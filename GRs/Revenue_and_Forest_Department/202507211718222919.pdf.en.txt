Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for
diversion of 87.351 ha. of forests for the Gondkhairi Underground Coal Mine project at
Village-Gondkhairi, Tah. Kalmeshwar, Dist. Nagpur in favour of M/s. Adani Power
Maharashtra Ltd., in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India, New Delhi;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 87.351 ha. The forest land for the
# Page 2
above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of
those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India, New Delhi, wide under reference no. 5 accorded 'final approval' to divert the 87.351
ha. of forests for the Gondkhairi Underground Coal Mine project at Village-Gondkhairi, Tah.
Kalmeshwar, Dist. Nagpur in favour of M/s. Adani Power Maharashtra Ltd., in the State of
Maharashtra under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State Government
to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 87.351
ha. consisting of Zudpi Jungle & Section 4 RF Land more specifically described in the
proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.8. M/s. Adani Power Maharashtra Ltd. Nagpur
for diversion of 87.351 ha. of forests for the Gondkhairi Underground Coal Mine project at
Village-Gondkhairi, Tah. Kalmeshwar, Dist. Nagpur in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letter dated 02.01.2024 and dated 12.09.2024 of the Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi. This approval for diversion
of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency-
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle' and
'Final approval 'letters of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi, referred above. The Conservator of
Forests (T), Nagpur shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User
Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief
Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to
time.
2. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi letters
dated 02.01.2024 and dated 12.09.2024, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made /
issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to
violation of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would
be taken accordingly.
3. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests
(T), Nagpur shall report through the State Government to the Ministry of
Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division),
New Delhi as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
