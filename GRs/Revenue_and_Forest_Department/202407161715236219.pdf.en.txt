Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to the
State Government, which was received from Executive Engineer, Multistoried Building Division,
Chembur for Diversion of 0.0399 ha. Forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 for construction Road for approaching Railway over bridge in lieu of level
crossing 6 at Railway Km 63/400 on Diva- Vasai Section Railway line at Kharbao, Tal.
Bhiwandi, Dist. Thane, in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, Government of Maharashtra after due consideration, vide its order under
The reference no. 2 Accorded "in-principle" approval with certain conditions to the above proposal for
diversion of 0.0399 ha. Reserved Forest land under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
# Page 2
Government Order No.: FLD-2023/CR.134/F-10
NOW, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred to State Government under general
approval given vide MoEF&CC, Government of India's Handbook of Guidelines for effective
and transparent implementation of the provisions of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 [Chapter-4, Para-4.3], Government of Maharashtra is pleased to grant Final
Approval for diversion of 0.0399 ha. Land.
Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra directs that 0.0399 ha. Forest Land consisting
of Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter under
reference No.1 and mentioned in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e.
Executive Engineer, Multistoried Building Division, Chembur for construction Road for
approaching Railway over bridge in lieu of level crossing 6 at Railway Km 63/400 on Diva-
Vasai Section Railway line at Kharbao, Tal. Bhiwandi, Dist. Thane, in the State of Maharashtra.
This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following
conditions by the User Agency-
1. The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Order
dated 30.05.2023.
2. The Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests will reconfirm that compliances under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 and Environment (Protection)
Act, 1986 are not needed for the project.
For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the dual prescribed activities and also to carry out other statuary duties, the user agency shall facilitate unheeded - _ _ access / entry in the area to the local forest officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this order for the diversification of forest area
for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the above mentioned '1n-principle' and in this order of Government of Maharashtra. The Chief Conservator of
Forests (Territorial), Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency. CCF will furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Government of Maharashtra's order dated 30.05.2023, This order and rules and guidelines made / issued under Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
6. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief Conservator of Forests / Deputy Conservator of Forests shall report through the State Government to Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Officer, Ministry
of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in the guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980.
