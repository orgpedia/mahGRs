The Proposal: -
It has been decided to implement the Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee Jan-Vikas Yojana Reference No. 1 in the state for the overall development of the villages by reducing human-wildlife conflict in the villages adjoining the forest by achieving sustainable development of its resources, increasing productivity, reducing the dependency of the villagers on the forest, creating agricultural linkages, providing alternative employment and thereby raising the level of protection and management of forest-wildlife through the participation of the villagers.
The Government Decision of Reference No. 3 has included the issue of individually extending the benefit of solar power fencing to vulnerable villages in line with human wildlife conflict.
A committee has been constituted for the purpose under the chairmanship of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur.
The Government's Decisions:
As per the proposal submitted by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Wildlife), Maharashtra State, Nagpur for Government approval vide letter No.4, Dr.
Government approval is being granted to provide individual benefits of solar power fencing under the Yama Prasad Mukherjee Jan-Van Vikas Yojana.
