Under the Government Order dated 18/08/2022 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of review earlier but not included in the rolls. It has been decided to fill up 1368 temporary posts in the form of Deputy Divisional Officers, Tehsildars and Mandal Officers in the establishment of Divisional Commissioner, Amravati and District Collector, Amravati/ Washim/ Yavatmal/ Buldana and Akola. A total of 1375 temporary posts for the office of Kalamb G. Yavatmal were under the consideration of the Government for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1368 temporary posts in the form of Divisional Commissioner, Amravati and District Collector, Amravati/ Washim, Yavatmal/ Buldana and Mandal Officer in the establishment of Akola and their subordinate offices, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer in the zonal offices and the newly constructed Additional Tehsil Office in Yavatmal district, Metikheda, etc. A total of 1375 temporary posts for the office of Kalamb G. Yavatmal are being extended for the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 subject to the conditions in the Government Resolution dated 18/08/2022 in the context of Finance Department.
2. Expenditure on such temporary posts subject to reference No. 03 In the relevant statement accompanying the Government decision herein, Raka-No. The budgetary grants for the year 2022-2023 under the head of accounts indicated in 05 should be apportioned.
The Finance Department has directed all the departments to take note that the revised figure in the Government Resolution dated 18/08/2022 in the context of the Finance Department needs to be approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries in the meantime and no extension of time will be granted for fixing the revised figure thereafter.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Departments under Finance Department, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18/08/2022.
