A total of 106 posts, including 3 posts of Deputy Tahsildar and two Head Clerks in the office of Divisional Commissioner, Nashik Division and 103 posts in 16 teams of Special Land Acquisition Officers in Nashik Division, were extended from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 as per the Government decision dated 11.10.2022. Now, a total of 93 existing temporary posts in the Land Acquisition Establishment of Divisional Commissioner, Nashik are being extended from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 on the following terms and conditions: (a) None of the temporary posts which have been extended are lying vacant for more than 6 months and have lapsed.
b) The temporary posts to be extended are included in the review of the proposed land acquisition posts in the Nashik division.
All the above posts will be considered as temporary additions in the permanent cadre of the concerned department.
All the above-mentioned office holders will be eligible for dearness allowance and other supplementary allowances as per the orders issued by the government from time to time.
