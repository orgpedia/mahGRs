The Proposal:
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for the approval of the Government for the continuation of 224 temporary posts created under the scheme for the establishment of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 in 2022-2023.
Accordingly, 224 temporary posts created under the Scheme for Formation of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve have been sanctioned for the year 2022-2023 for continuation from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per Government Resolution No.
3. Now, the powers to extend the term of the existing temporary posts included in the format of Administrative Divisions as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the format, have been conferred on the Administrative Divisions under the Government Decision of Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Government's decision:
Formation of Special Tiger Protection Force (STPF) for Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve and Pench Tiger Reserve as per the Government decision in Reference No.6 of the Department of Finance, Government is approving the continuation of 224 temporary posts created under the scheme from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 in the year 2022-2023.
