Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a
# Page 2
proposal for diversion of 19.227 ha. forest land under Section-2 of Van (Sanrakshan
Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 for above ground conveyor along with road for conveyor maintenance & emergency road and transmission line in Villages Hedri, Bande in Tahsil Etapalli, Distrist Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra in favour of
M/s Lloyds Metals & Energy Limited;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent
the proposal for approval of Regional Office (Central) Ministry of Environment, Forests
& Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
Under reference No.3, accorded 'In-principle' approval for the proposed forest area for
diversion 1.e. 9.847 ha. Reserved Forest and Protected Forest Land for the above project
under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The said
approval is subject to fulfilment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter
Under reference No.5, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the remaining 9. 38 ha.
Reserved Forest and Protected Forest Land for the above project under section 2 Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The said approval is subject to
fulfilment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 7 Accorded 'Final
Approval 'to divert the 19.227 ha forest land in preference of M / s Lloyds Metals & Energy
Limited, Gadchiroli for above ground conveyor along with road for conveyor maintenance & emergency road and transmission line in Villages Hedri, Bande in Tahsil Etapalli, Distrist Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.8 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of
19.227 ha. consisting Reserved Forest and Protected Forest Land more specifically
described in the proposal submitted vide letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted to the User Agency, 1.e., M/s Lloyds
Metals & Energy Limited, Gadchiroli for above ground conveyor along with road for conveyor maintenance & emergency road and transmission line in Villages Hedri, Bande in Tahsil Etapalli, Distrist Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra.
# Page 3
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in
the letter dated 09.10.2024, dated 13.10.2025 and dated 24.12.2025 of Regional Office,
MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to
compliance of following conditions by the User Agency-
1. The Chief Conservator of Forests (1), Gadchiroli, in consultation with PCCF
(Wildlife), will ensure that wildlife clearance is not required for the project,
before actual handing over of forest land to the User Agency.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in
the letter dated 09.10.2024, dated 13.10.2025 and dated 24.12.2025.
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the
Principle 'and' final approval 'letters of MoEF & CC, Government of India (FC)
Division), referred above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Gadchiroli shall
ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall
furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of
Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities
and also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency
prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this
Order for the diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC letters dated 09.10.2024, dated 13.10.2025 and dated 24.12.2025, in
this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van (Sanrakshan
Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator
of Forests (T), Gadchiroli shall report through the State Government to the
Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of
Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per
procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
