Preamble: Under the State Scheme of" Provisions to Tribal Account Holders under the Maharashtra Regulation of Sale of Trees to Occupants of Scheduled Tribes (Regulation) Act, 1969 (2406 0686)," for the year 2021-22, a total amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 200.00 crore has been earmarked for this purpose.
Under this scheme, tribal account holders are given advance till the time the trees in their private ownership are auctioned by the government. According to the law, the payment must be made within the stipulated time. Accordingly, out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 120.34 lakh is required for Gadchiroli, Chandrapur and Nagpur Forest Circles and the funds are to be disbursed through BDS. 1 and 2 have been requested by the Government.
As per the Government decision dated 24.06.2021 of the Department of Finance in the wake of COVID-19, for the first half of the financial year 2021-22 (up to September, 2021), 50% of the budgeted funds were available for the targets of mandatory and programme-specific 02-Majoori and 25% of the budgeted funds were available for the targets of 50-Other Expenditure.
Department of Finance, Government vide Circular dated 29.07.2021, in view of the revenue deficit situation in the State due to the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, all the Administrative Departments were advised to plan expenditure in such a manner that the fund allocation/ expenditure limit for the programme expenditure, including the mandatory expenditure, would remain at 60% till the end of this year.
