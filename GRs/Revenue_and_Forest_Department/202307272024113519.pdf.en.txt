Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Subject to the provisions of Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and the conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Official Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Mrs. Lalita Babar, Deputy Collector, is being appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2. In pursuance of the deputation as Deputy Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, the services of Mrs. Lalita Babar, Deputy Collector are being handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai.
3. Mrs. Lalita Babar, Deputy Collector to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai immediately.
