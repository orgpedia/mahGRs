The Collector, Gadchiroli, vide his letter dated 27/08/2021, has submitted a proposal to the Government for sanctioning the purchase of new vehicles in lieu of the vehicles earmarked for the Collector, Gadchiroli. The proposal had been sent to the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is granting approval to the Collector, Gadchiroli, Nagpur Revenue Division to purchase a new vehicle in lieu of his earlier old unregistered vehicle No. MH-33-C-1111 (Car) and the subsequent expenditure of Rs. 9.00 lakh (Rupees Nine Lakh only) subject to the following conditions.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision, Finance Division No.Vahan-2017/ P.S. 41/17/ Regulations, dated 28/07/2020, the Collector, Gadchiroli, should purchase a vehicle within the maximum limit of Rs. 9.00 lakh (including the cost of the vehicle+ Goods and Services Tax+ Temporary Registration Charges+ Value Added Tax+ Accessories etc.).
