The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan should immediately distribute the funds received by him under this order to the Collector, Thane through the BEAMS budget distribution system.
2) The Collector, Thane shall disburse the said fund by cheque to the Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Division, Thane, as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) The Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority Division, Thane should pay the pending dues of civic amenities works immediately after receiving the funds sanctioned under this order, and accordingly, the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Konkan and Collector, Thane.
(5) The Executive Engineer shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
6) The concerned project officer should ensure that the sanctioned funds are not spent on other works without prior approval of the government.
The Executive Engineer shall ensure that no funds under the head' Demand No. C-10, Head Accounts 4701, 01, Major Irrigation Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53, Major Construction (4701-H-727)" (Dattamat) shall remain unutilized or the funds received under the said order shall not be deposited in any Operational Bank Account/ PLA (Personal Principal Account) of the concerned agency.
The Executive Engineer shall ensure that there are no short payments pending for one year under the head of the head article mentioned in the said order, namely, Demand No.C-10, Head Article 4701, 01, Major Irrigation Commercial, 001, Operation and Administration, (00) (00) (03) Other Projects, 53, Major Constructions (4701-H-727) (Dattamat).
