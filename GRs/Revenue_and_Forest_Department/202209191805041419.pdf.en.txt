The Proposal:
A central team has been sent to inspect the areas affected by natural disasters. Visited the affected areas of Nagpur, Gondia, Chandrapur and Gadchiroli districts of Nagpur Division from 11th to 13th September 2020. Accommodation, food and travel arrangements were made for the participants. The matter of approving the expenditure on travel, accommodation and food of the central government's inspection team to inspect the affected areas due to natural disasters was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
