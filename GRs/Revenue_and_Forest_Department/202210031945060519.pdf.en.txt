Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 22/02/2022 of the Department of Finance, a total of 1315 temporary posts in the figure of Divisional Commissioner Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai and in the office of District Collector, Mumbai City, Mumbai Suburban, Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg and in the field offices of Sub Divisional Officer, Tehsildar and Mandal Officer under him were extended for the period from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022 under the Government decision dated 29/03/2022 in the context of Revenue and Forest Department.
2. Under the Government Order dated 18/08/2022 in respect of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the government was considering to extend the term of 1315 temporary posts outside the figure on the establishment of the Divisional Commissioner and the field offices under him in the Konkan region.
