Introduction: In order to help the farmers in the next season in case of loss due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods and cyclones, the farmers are given assistance in the form of input subsidy at a prescribed rate from the State Disaster Response Fund. Also, assistance is provided at prescribed rates for other approved items of the State Disaster Response Fund. The Chief Minister directed to provide financial assistance in the form of financial assistance to the affected farmers who suffered crop loss in various districts due to heavy rains and floods in July 2022 and to provide assistance for other losses. The matter was discussed in the Cabinet meeting held on 10.08.2022. A decision in this regard was taken at the state cabinet meeting.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved to provide financial assistance to the affected farmers in the form of fixed subsidy at enhanced rates for the loss of agricultural crops in various districts of the State due to natural calamities like heavy rains, floods during the period from June to October, 2022.
