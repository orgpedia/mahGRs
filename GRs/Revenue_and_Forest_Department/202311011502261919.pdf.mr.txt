Order:
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to Integrated Regional
Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur
for diversion of 0.985 ha. of Reserve Forest land in favour of Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) for construction of the Metro piers and Bhakti Park
Station for Mumbai Metro line 4 at Village-Parel/Sewri, District-Mumbai City;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.2, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 0.985 ha. Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letters under reference No.3 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;

AND whereas, the Integrated Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests &
Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur, vide under reference no. 4 accorded ‘Final
approval' to divert the of 0.985 ha. Reserve forest land in favour of Mumbai Metropolitan
Region Development Authority (MMRDA) for construction of the Metro piers and Bhakti Park Station for Mumbai Metro line 4 at Village-Parel/Sewri, Mumbai City District in the
State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.5 has requested State Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
AND whereas, Collector Mumbai City has issued certificate under Scheduled Tribes
and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Rights) Act, 2006 for land at City
Survey No. 6 (part), Salt Pan Division in Mumbai City District.
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 0.985 ha.
consisting of Reserved forest land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide
letter under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be diverted
to the User Agency, i.e. Senior Planner (Metro PIU), Mumbai Metropolitan Region
Development Authority (MMRDA), Mumbai, for construction of the Metro piers and Bhakti
Park Station for Mumbai Metro line 4 at Village- Sewri (Salt Pan), Mumbai City District in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 11.03.2020 and dated 09.02.2023 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency
1. The 0.1236 ha. area proposed for approach road during construction phase shall be handed over back to State Forest Department.
2. If the number of Mangrove trees to be felled increases during implementation of the
project, user agency shall obtain prior permission from Central Government and Hon'ble High Court accordingly.
3. Divisional Forest Officer, Mangrove Cell, Mumbai should ensure that, the fulfillment of Conditions in the order, dated 09.02.2023, before giving actual possession of forest land to User Agency.
4. The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle'
and final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur, referred above.
Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Mangrove Cell, Mumbai shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the
report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.

5. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Regional Office, MoEF&CC
letters dated 11.03.2020 and dated 09.02.2023, in this order and Rules & Guidelines
made / issued under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, shall amount to violation of
Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
6. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Mangrove Cell, Mumbai shall report through
the State Government to the Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Deputy
Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment,
Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down
in guidelines under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
