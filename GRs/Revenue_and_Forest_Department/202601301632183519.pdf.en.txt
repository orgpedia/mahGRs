Preamble: - The Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (30 of 2013) The Right to Fair Compensation in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement (Maharashtra) Rules, 2014, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 109 and other survey powers empowered thereunder, is hereby notified by the Government under section 192 of the said Act. 11.01.2024 Rule (18) Sub-rule (3) prescribes financial limits for granting approval for land acquisition award.
It has been provided that in case of land acquisition where the amount of compensation payable is more than one hundred crore rupees, the Collector shall submit the draft award to the State Government through the Divisional Commissioners of the respective Revenue Departments as a pre-approval and the Collector shall declare the award only after obtaining the pre-approval of the State Government.
02. Against this backdrop, the draft award proposal received at the government level is expeditiously scrutinized and decided on the merits. The formation of a high-level committee at the level of Additional Chief Secretary (Forest, Land Acquisition) was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
For the above-mentioned purposes, the Government has now issued a notification. A high-level committee headed by the Additional Chief Secretary (Forest, Land Acquisition) is being constituted to scrutinize the draft judgment as follows.
Structure of the Committee: -
1) Ma. Additional Chief Secretary (Forest, Land Acquisition) _ Chairman 2) Md. Additional Chief Secretary (Public Works) _ Member 3) Md. Additional Chief Secretary (Agriculture) _ Member 4) Md. Principal Secretary (Law & Justice Department) _ Member 5) Md. Additional Chief Secretary / Principal Secretary / Secretary (Head of Land Acquisition Board / Administrative Division) _ Member 6) Md. Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps _ Member 7) Ma. Joint Director, Town Planning and Evaluation Department - Member

The High-Level Committee constituted to scrutinize / authorize the draft land acquisition award shall have the following functions.
[. Act B Rules governing draft assessment award received at Government level]
To make a recommendation to the Government for approval by scrutiny as per the provisions of the Act.
I. As well as for the evaluation of matters other than land in the draft land acquisition award.
Competent officers or expert officers in the relevant field may be invited to the meetings as special invitees.
In certain cases, the head of the relevant acquisition agency in the land acquisition proposal may also be invited to the meetings as an invited member as required.
04. After the recommendation of the High Level Committee, the Government ARM shall be the concerned Collector.
They will take action to announce the land acquisition award.
