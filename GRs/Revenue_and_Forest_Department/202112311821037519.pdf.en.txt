Under the Koyna project, the civic amenities works in the rehabilitated villages of Kamargaon (S), Kamargaon (Gokul), Nahibe-Medheghar, Kisarule-Shivandeshwar and Kisarule-Dhanakal in Patan taluka of Satara district have been completed. Administrative approval has been granted under Government Decisions 4, 5 and 6 and no funds have been disbursed for these works earlier. Administrative Sanction has been accorded to the Executive Engineer, Kanher Canal Division No. 2, Karavadi (Karad), G. Satara under reference No. 8 is presented here. In accordance with the proposal submitted by the Project Mechanism, the matter of providing funds for the civic amenities works in progress as mentioned in the proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the Koyna Project, the Government is approving a total amount of Rs. 41,57,890/- for the civic amenities works in progress in the rehabilitated villages of Kamargaon (S), Kamargaon (Gokul), Nahibe-Medheghar, Kisarule-Shivandeshwar and Kisarule-Dhanakal in Patan Taluka of Satara district in the current financial year 2021-22 subject to the following conditions:

The following instructions/ conditions shall be strictly enforced by all concerned in accordance with the financial discipline.
The Divisional Commissioner, Pune shall immediately disburse the funds received under this order to the concerned Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, as the concerned Controlling Officer through BEAMS, the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Project Board, Satara, shall disburse the said funds for civic amenities and related works without delay to the concerned Executive Engineer as soon as it is received from the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
3) Since funds are provided by the Rehabilitation Department of the Government for civic amenities works, no permission/ approval is required from the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation for payment of dues for civic amenities works.
4) Executive Engineer, Kanher Canal Department No. 2, Karavadi (Karad), G. Satara, as soon as the funds sanctioned under this order are received, the pending payments of civic amenities works should be paid immediately, accordingly the completion report should be submitted to the Government, Divisional Commissioner, Pune and Collector, Satara immediately.
(5) The Executive Engineer concerned shall ensure that the funds sanctioned and received under this order are spent only on the sanctioned civic amenities works and that no expenditure in excess of the sanctioned grant is incurred while spending the said funds.
