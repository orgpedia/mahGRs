The Proposal:
The Central Government has issued a Government Resolution No.51 under Section 51 of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013. Under the Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, a total of 13 posts have been created in Nagpur.
A total of 13 temporary posts on the establishment of Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Nagpur have been notified vide Reference No. 2 in the order. 1.3.2021 to dt. The deadline has been extended to 31.8.2021. Extend the deadline. 31.8.2021 has been completed.
Under the Government decision dated 10.8.2021 in the context of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the matter of extension was under consideration of the Government as per the request made by the Registrar, Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Nagpur.
Government's decision:
The following 13 temporary posts in the office of the Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Authority, Nagpur are being extended for a period from 1.9.2021 to 28.2.2022 (6 months) subject to the conditions in the Government Resolution dated 10.8.2021 in the context of the Department of Finance:
