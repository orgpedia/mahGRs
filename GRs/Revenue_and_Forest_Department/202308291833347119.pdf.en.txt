Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Order of the Government Mr. Tukaram Hulavale, Deputy Collector (Land Acquisition No. Dhule is being appointed as Deputy CEO, Shri Saibaba Sansthan, Shirdi on deputation for a period of 3 years with the approval of the competent authority in the interest of public service and administrative convenience.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Mr. Tukaram Hulawale, Deputy Collector is being relieved unilaterally from the post he is holding to join the post immediately.
As per the deputation to the post of Saibaba Sansthan, Shirdi, the services of Mr. Tukaram Hulawale, Deputy Collector are being handed over to the Principal Secretary and Legal Advisor, Department of Law and Justice, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai.
Shri Tukaram Hulawale, Deputy Collector to appear before the Principal Secretary and Legal Advisor, Law and Justice Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai immediately.
