Government Orders: -
Department of Finance, Government of India Decision dated 2nd March, 2019, as per the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission, three benefits to Government employees / officers after 10, 20 and 30 years of regular service "Promised Progress Scheme under Revised Service" have been implemented with effect from the date 01.01.2016.
As per the scheme, the first benefit of the Promised Progress Scheme (Pay Scale - 9300-34800, Grade Pay 5000) is being sanctioned to the officers of Class-II / Class-II cadre of District Registrar (Group-B Pay Scale 9300-34800 Grade Pay 4400) of Registration and Stamp Department who have completed 10 consecutive years of regular service in the post.
