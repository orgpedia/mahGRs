Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Government order:
Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2021 of S.P.V. In accordance with the provisions of Rule 8 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Mr. Mahadev Kirwale, Resident Deputy Collector, Dharashiv, is the competent authority and Deputy Collector, National Stock Exchange Limited (NSEL). (S.E.L.) is appointed on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
2.This order comes into force with immediate effect and Shri Mahadev Kirwale, Deputy Collector is being relieved unilaterally from the post he was holding to join the post immediately.
The services of Shri Mahadev Kirwale, Deputy Collector are being transferred to the Additional Chief Secretary, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai, in pursuance of his deputation to the said post.
