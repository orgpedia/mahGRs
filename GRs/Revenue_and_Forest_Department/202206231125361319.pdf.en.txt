The Proposal:
A total of 42 posts of 6 Divisional Disaster Management Coordinators and 36 District Disaster Management Officers have been created for the Maharashtra State Disaster Management Program and the implementation of this program on contractual basis. These positions are paid for by the government.
These posts are appointed by the government. from January 1, 2022. The government was considering the extension of the deadline for 11 months from November 30, 2022, to April and May, 2022, for disbursement of their two months' honorarium on the BDS system.
