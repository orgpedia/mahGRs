The Proposal:
In the Post-Plan Scheme" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park," the Government has accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary in the year 2021-2022 from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022 as per the Government decision in Reference No.
2. Now, as per Reference No. 5, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has submitted a proposal for Government approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the post-plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park" for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023.
Provisional posts included in the pattern of the Administrative Department and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the pattern, from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.

The power to grant extension has been delegated to the administrative departments under the Government Resolution of Reference No. 6 of the Department of Finance.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 6, the Government have accorded approval for continuation of 1 post of Assistant Conservator of Forest in Nagzira Sanctuary from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 in the post-plan scheme of" Establishment of Maldhok Bird Sanctuary, Nagzira Sanctuary and Tadoba National Park."
The expenditure on establishment of 1 temporary post created on the establishment of the Conservator of Forest and Director, Navegaon-Nagzira Tiger Reserve should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-2023 under the head" 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 02 Ecological Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife Conservation, (00) (01) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Conservation (2406 0766)."
This Government Decision, Government Decision No.1004/ Q.No.12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04.10.2004 of the Department of Finance and Government Decision No. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 22.02.2022 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
