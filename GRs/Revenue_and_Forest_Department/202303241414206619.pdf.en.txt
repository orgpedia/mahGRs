GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 32 temporary posts in the Land Records Cell have been extended till 28.2.2023 as per the Government Decision of Reference No. 2.
In the Forest Department, Reference No. 26573 posts in the revised figure approved under the Government Decisions dated 13.09.2019 and 21.09.2021 include 30 temporary posts out of 32 temporary posts under the said scheme. While 2 posts of Forest Conservator (Land Records), Nagpur and Deputy Conservator of Forest, Land Records Western Region Office, Panvel at Thane have been abolished. Accordingly, the 30 temporary posts indicated in the following statement A are now proposed to be extended till 28.02.2024 by the Deputy Conservator of Forests (Budget Planning and Development), Nagpur vide Reference No. 4 have been requested.
As per the Government decision dated 8.02.2023 of the Department of Finance, the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under them till 31.08.2023. Accordingly, the 30 temporary posts as indicated in the attached prospectus are being extended for the period from 1.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The said 30 temporary posts shall not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The said 30 temporary posts are in the Land Records Office and are to be filled up under the review of the posts in the Forest Department vide Reference No. 1 It must be included in the designations approved by the governing decision.
The expenditure for the said post shall be met from the grants to be sanctioned in the financial year 2023-24 under the headings 2406 Forestry and Wildlife (Non-Plan), 01 Forestry, 101 Forest Conservation and Development, 101 (Two) Survey of Forest, Jamabandi and Simankan (2406 0102).
