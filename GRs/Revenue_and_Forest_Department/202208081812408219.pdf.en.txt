Preamble: Due to floods caused by heavy rainfall in the state during the month of June and July, 2022, there has been damage to agricultural crops and property in some districts of the state. It was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds for providing assistance at the rate of State Disaster Response Fund for the loss of property caused by floods caused by heavy rains during the period June and July, 2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to disburse assistance to the affected people for the loss of cattle, fully destroyed/ partially collapsed kutcha/ pucca houses, huts, sacks, relief for damages and other permissible matters in some districts of the State due to the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the State during the month of June and July, 2022, a total amount of Rs. 1200.46 lakh (Rupees Twelve Crore and Sixty-Six Thousand only) for the loss of property as shown in the Annexure-I attached to this Government Decision from the State Disaster Response Fund is being sanctioned by the Government through the Divisional Commissioners to the concerned districts.
(2) As per the annexure attached to this Government Resolution, the Account-wise funds should be distributed immediately by Office-M-11 to the Divisional Commissioner on the BEAMS system.
(3) The funds shall be used for the purpose for which they were allocated.
