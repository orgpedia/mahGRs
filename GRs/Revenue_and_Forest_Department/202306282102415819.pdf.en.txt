(3) No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned for any reason except with the previous sanction of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave application of the officer transferred by the parent office should be accepted. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned officer at the original address by post. If the leave application of the transferred officer is sent to the parent office, the application will not be taken into consideration.
If the concerned officer does not appear at the place of appointment immediately as per the said transfer order or tries to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against him considering it as misconduct as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
