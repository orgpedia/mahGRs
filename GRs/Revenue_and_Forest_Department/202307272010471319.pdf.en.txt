Mr. Avinash Shinde, Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Pune has been posted as Deputy Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation on deputation. ..

Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Retirement and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, Shri Avinash Shinde, Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Pune is being appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
Avinash Shinde, Deputy Collector, is being transferred to the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry of Urban Development, Mumbai, in accordance with the deputation to the post of Deputy Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation.
3. Mr. Avinash Shinde, Deputy Collector should immediately appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
