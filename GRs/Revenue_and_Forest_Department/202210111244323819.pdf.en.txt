GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: WLP- 05.22/ S.No. 146/ F-1
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the reference No. 1 circular of the Finance Department, the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, in view of the demand for funds made under the letter of reference No. 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7, under the head Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (2406 0775), Rs. 806.30 lakhs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions as per attached statement:
I. The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System in 2022-23 under the head of accounts head 2406- Forestry and Wildlife, 110- Conservation of Wildlife (00) (02) Nature Conservation and Wildlife Management (2406 0775).
The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall ensure that the works mentioned in the letter of reference No. 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7 are included in the scheme and distribute the funds at his level keeping in view the urgency of the works mentioned in the proposal.
(iii) The funds shall be spent only after ascertaining that the administrative and technical sanction has been accorded to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority, as required.
The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the terms of reference of the proposal.
(v) The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur shall disburse the funds in accordance with the prevailing Government decisions issued from time to time in respect of procurement of materials and other purchases by the Finance Department.
VI. The various guidelines issued by the Department of Finance and the Department of Finance, Government Circular No. No.43/ Earth-3, dated 04.04.2022 to be strictly followed.
This Government Decision is being issued pursuant to Finance Department, Government Circular No: Finance-2022/ P. No.43/ Finance-3 dated 04.04.2022.
