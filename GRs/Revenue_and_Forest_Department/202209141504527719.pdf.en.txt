Introduction: Considering the economic opportunities and employment generation potential of the tourism sector, it is necessary to develop nature tourism, increase the livelihood opportunities of local villagers dependent on forest and promote nature education, etc. in a balanced and coordinated manner. Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 08.12.2015.
The government will take a policy decision on this. The Governing Body has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Minister (Forests), State of Maharashtra. An executive committee under the chairmanship of Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur has also been constituted. The members of the Board of Directors and the Executive Committee shall be appointed by the Board of Directors. It provides that the Minister (Forests) shall be nominated by the Government of Maharashtra and the Chairman, Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board.
The Governing Council and the Executive Committee of the Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board have issued the Bye-laws Reference No. 2 Approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.01.2016. Accordingly, the No. As per the Government decision dated 31.03.2021, the appointment of 3 nominated members to the Governing Board and 4 nominated members to the Executive Committee of Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board was approved.

Reference No. 1 of the Office of the Chief Secretary In pursuance of the direction to submit a formal proposal to the competent authority within seven days to cancel the appointments of all non-official members appointed to all undertakings/ corporations/ boards/ committees/ authorities under the administrative department as per the note dated 15.07.2022, the matter of cancellation of the appointment of 3 nominated members to the regulatory board and 4 nominated members to the executive committee of Maharashtra Natural Tourism Development Board was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Reference No. The appointments of the members nominated under the decision of the 3rd Government are being cancelled.
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Nature Tourism Board, Nagpur shall take action to bring the above Government decisions to the notice of the nominated members on the concerned Regulatory Board as well as the Executive Committee.
