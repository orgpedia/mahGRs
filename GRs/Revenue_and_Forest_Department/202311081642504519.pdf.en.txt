Government Decision: In view of the reference No. 1 circular of the Finance Department, the Government Decision of Reference No. 3 and the demand for funds made by the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Budget, Planning and Development), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, under reference No. 4 letter, the establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) under the heading of Rs. 1318.73 lakhs is being approved for disbursement subject to the following terms and conditions: i. The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds received through the Budget Distribution System in the year 2023-24 under the scheme (00) (10) (2406 2232) under the State Plan under the head No. 2406 Forestry and Wildlife, 02 Environmental Forestry and Wildlife, 110 Wildlife under the Five Year Plan.
The funds should be spent only after verifying that the administrative and technical approval has been granted to the proposed works under the scheme by the competent authority as required.

(iii) Please ensure that no funds are spent from other schemes other than the scheme for establishment of Wildlife Handicap Home (2406 2232) on the work items in the proposal submitted for Government approval.
(iv) It shall be ensured that no violation of the provisions of the Indian Forest Act, 1927, the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972 and the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 as well as the guidelines of the National Zoological Park is committed and necessary permission is obtained.
V. It should be ensured that the subsidy disbursed will be spent only on the works mentioned in the proposal which have received administrative approval from the competent authority.
(vi) The funds disbursed shall be spent within the limits prescribed in the scheme as approved by the Government, subject to the prevailing rules, prevailing policy and guidelines prescribed by the Finance Department from time to time, and in accordance with the provisions of the proposal.
vii. The Controller/ Implementing Officer concerned shall follow the instructions and procedures prescribed by the Government for the proposed construction.
It will also be their responsibility to avoid any financial irregularities.
If the activities include purchase, the purchase should be made by following the procedure prescribed by the government.
ix. Expenditure shall be incurred in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the Department from time to time in pursuance of the said scheme and in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the Finance Department from time to time in accordance with the terms of reference of the proposal.
Various guidelines issued by the Finance Department and Circular No. Finance-2023/ Q. No.40/ Earth-3, dated 12.04.2023 to be strictly followed.
