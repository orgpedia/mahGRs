Terms and Conditions:
The amount of monthly salary and allowances payable to Mr. Balasaheb Laxmanrao Deshmukh during the period of the said agreement shall be reckoned as provided in the decision of the Government in question.
(2) A bond/ guarantee shall be taken from Mr. Balasaheb Laxmanrao Deshmukh that he will be responsible for the completion of the work specified in the contract during the contract period. Also, while making contractual appointments in the said bond/ guarantee, the Government shall ensure that the terms and conditions prescribed under the aforesaid Government decision and the terms and conditions prescribed by the Department are accepted. It should also be mentioned that no other right will be demanded for not being able to be taken in regular service.
The expenditure on monthly remuneration and allowances payable to Shri Balasaheb Laxmanrao Deshmukh (Retd.) shall be met from the grants available for office expenses/ contingency fund.
Balasaheb Lakshmanrao Deshmukh (Retd), on being appointed on contract basis, shall not be entitled to any benefits of regular service or induction/ absorption in any cadre of the Government.
The work assigned to Shri Balasaheb Laxmanrao Deshmukh (Retd) shall be required to be completed within the stipulated time. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force), Nagpur should review their activities from time to time and submit an assessment report to the government.
(6) The Government shall be the competent authority to terminate the services of the Contracting Party in special circumstances.
