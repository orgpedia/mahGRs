Government order:
The Department of Land Records has decided to promote 76 employees out of the total 92 employees approved in the meeting of the Divisional Promotion Committee to the vacant posts of Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Group B in the promotion quota from Group-C posts of Group 2. Provisional promotion has been granted vide Government Order dated 27.05.2022. The following 8 employees are being re-designated by way of promotion to the post indicated in Pillar-3 in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, with partial change in their postings:
