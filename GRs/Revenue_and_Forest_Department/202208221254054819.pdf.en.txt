And whereas, the Government of Maharashtra vide Reference Letter No. After considering the proposal under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, the Government of India recommended diversion of 139.473 ha of forest land for the project. The proposal has been approved in principle under Section 2 of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. This approval is granted subject to certain conditions that the project mechanism will meet;

Under Section 6, the State Government has been requested to issue an order for diversion of forest land in the present case; therefore, the Government of Maharashtra is now directing that the Project Authority i.e. Executive Engineer, Palghar Irrigation Project Construction Department, Suryanagar, Dist. Dahanu, G. Palghar for Kholsapara-I Minor Irrigation Scheme 139.473 hectares of forest land in Palghar district as mentioned in the Schedule below should be diverted for this project. This is the Government of India's. 25.02.2016 and D. Subject to fulfillment of the terms and conditions in the letter dated 13.12.2021 and to be completed after fulfillment of the following conditions: 1) The Project Mechanism shall comply with all the conditions prescribed in the letter of in-principle approval and final approval of the Government of India mentioned above. The Chief Conservator of Forest (Pr), Thane will ensure that the project machinery complies with all the conditions. The Chief Conservator of Forests (Pr), Thane shall submit a report in this regard to the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Central Officers, State of Maharashtra, Nagpur from time to time.
(2) Non-compliance with the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and the rules and guidelines made thereunder and any of the conditions contained in this Order shall be a violation of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and legal action shall be taken accordingly.
(3) In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the concerned Chief Conservator of Forests shall submit a report to the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India through the State Government regarding the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.
