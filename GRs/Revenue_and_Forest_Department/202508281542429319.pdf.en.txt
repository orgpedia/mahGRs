(1) A separate booklet on Department-wise District Affiliation Training and Independent Workforce Training Programme under various departments has been released to the Deputy Collectors under the aforesaid examination.
Accordingly, the District Collector concerned has issued a circular to the Sub-Divisional Magistrate under the examination. The training program should be prepared immediately from the 01.09.2025 and a copy of it should be made available to the Sub-Collector under examination.
(2) The Sub-Collector under examination shall submit the same to the Resident Deputy Collector and Liaison / Coordinating Officer, Additional Commissioner (Revenue) of the concerned district by e-mail on a daily basis without fail.
(3) The nodal officer of the department and the liaison officer of the training institutes shall exercise direct control over the trainees. Also, assess the trainee's performance each month and take a monthly report based on the experience and evaluate the report. The report should be certified every month and sent to the General Administration Department.
(4) The District Collector of the concerned district shall submit a report to the Additional Commissioner (Revenue) regarding the day-to-day attendance / absence / conduct etc. of the Sub-Collector under examination.
(5) If it is found that the trainee has misbehaved or not taken the training seriously enough during the training period, such incidents shall be immediately brought to the notice of the concerned Collector by contacting the Coordinating Officer / Nodal Officer of the Department and the Liaison Officer of the training institutions as well as the Additional Commissioner (Revenue). The report in this regard should be submitted by the Coordinating Officer, Additional Commissioner (Revenue) to the Revenue and Forest Department and General Administration Department of the Government.
(6) A large number of posts are being created on the establishment of the Divisional Commissioner of the division allocated for training of the Sub-Collectors under probation. The salary and allowances of the Sub-Collector under probation in the division allotted to the trainees will be paid by the Divisional Commissioner in this regard.
(7) The trainees are required to pass the examination conducted under the training programme within the prescribed time frame during the department-wise district affiliation training period. Also, the instructions issued by the General Administration Department from time to time should be strictly followed for successful conduct of the training programme.
(8) Government Decisions for the evaluation of Sub-Districts under examination in the stages of Zonal Training, General Administration Department No.TRN-0424 / Q.No.37 / Trainee-1, D. The Coordination Officer Additional Commissioner (Revenue) shall be responsible for taking action in accordance with the directions in the 01.08.2025.
(9) GOVERNMENT ORDER, REVENUE AND FOREIGN DEPARTMENT, NO. SANKARN-0415 / P.S. 122 / E-2, D. The Office of the Additional Deputy (Revenue) Divisional Commissioner has been appointed as the Coordinating Officer of the division allocated to the Sub-Collector under scrutiny as per 15.09.2015. Accordingly, the Coordinating Officer, Additional Commissioner (Revenue) should take appropriate precautions for successful conduct of the training programme.
(10) The District is being allotted to the Sub-District Officers under probation only for training purposes.
(11) Sub-District Collectors under probation will be assigned districts for training for successful conduct of training programmes. The 01.09.2025 should be registered and the compliance report should be sent to the government immediately.
