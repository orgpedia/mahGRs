The Proposal: -
Government lands are provided for various purposes as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 and the Maharashtra Land Revenue (Settlement of Government Land) Rules, 1971 and as per the policy prescribed by the Government from time to time.
Mash areal Wa WCU (Public 71] 0080) reports that many projects are getting delayed due to non-allocation of PAP flats to the affected citizens within the stipulated time. The High Court in Writ Petition No. 8973/2023 is mentioned in the order passed in this court case dated 03/11/2023. Accordingly, a Task Force was set up by the Government under the Government Decision dated 23/11/2023 of the Housing Department to formulate a definite policy at the government level. The task force has submitted recommendations to the government and they are under the consideration of the government's housing department.
The administrative lands are usually given to MHADA / MMRDA etc. authorities with a 100% title deed. However, the public interest infrastructure project (Public Vital) It would not be fair and justified to recover the 100% amount of government land for rehabilitation of affected people / construction of PAP flats. Hence, it was under the consideration of the Government to allot Government land in Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban District as well as Government / Semi-Government land given by the Government to various Government Departments and Government Agencies under their control at concessional rates to Government / Semi-Government agencies / Government Authorities in which the Government is wholly or partly a stakeholder, such as CAU, MHADA, Dharavi Rehabilitation Project, for rehabilitation of people affected by Public Interest Infrastructure Project (2.
