Order:-
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Nagpur vide letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal to Integrated Regional
Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India, Nagpur
for diversion of 4.50 ha. forest land under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980, in favour of Project Officer Integrated Tribal Development, Gadchiroli for Establishing
Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) at Dhanora, Taluka-Dhanora, District-Gadchiroli
in the State of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
Reference No.2, accorded 'In-principle' approval to divert the 4.5 ha. The Forest Land for the
above project under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The
said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.3 and Government of Maharashtra
# Page 2
Government Order No. FLD-2021/C.R.122/F-10
vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those conditions to the
Government of India;
AND whereas, the Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India, Nagpur, wide under reference no. 5 Accorded 'Final Approval'
to divert the of 4.50 ha. of Forest land in favour of The Project Officer, Integrated Tribal
Development, Gadchiroli for Establishing Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) at
Dhanora, Taluka-Dhanora, District-Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.6 has requested State
Government to issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 4.50 ha
consisting of Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted
vide letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be
diverted to the User Agency, 1.e. The Project Officer, Integrated Tribal Development Project,
Gadchiroli for Establishing Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) at Dhanora, Taluka-
Dhanora, District-Gadchiroli in the State of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the
letters dated 28.02.2022 and dated 14.09.2023 of Regional Office, MoEF&CC, Nagpur. This
approval for diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by
the User Agency-
The User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the 'in-principle'.
and 'final approval' letters of Regional Office, MoEF & CC, Nagpur, referred above.
The Conservator of Forests (T), Gadchiroli shall ensure the strict compliance of the
conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish the report in this regard to Additional
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur
from time to time.
2. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and
also to carry out other statuary duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered
access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the
free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the
diversion of forest area for the non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
