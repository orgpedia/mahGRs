The decision has been taken as per the Government decision dated 04.01.2006.
Accordingly, as per Rule 13 and Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the list of Drawing and Disbursing Officers in the department is sent to the Accountant General's Office. Accordingly, the lists of Drawing and Disbursing Officers of the Department have been published as per the Government decisions issued from time to time.
Accordingly, for the office of the Chief Conservator of Forests (Works Planning), East Nagpur, to disburse the salary and allowances of his subordinate officers/ employees and for other field activities, the Chief Conservator of Forests (Works Planning), East Nagpur has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The decision has been taken by the government. However, the forest and revenue department has taken a decision in this regard. As per the revised format issued vide Order No. 21.9.2021, the post of Chief Conservator of Forests has been sanctioned in the office of Chief Conservator of Forests (Planning), East Nagpur. However, the government has issued a notification. As on 28.8.2020, the post of Chief Conservator of Forests (Planning), East Nagpur has been kept in abeyance and the post of Conservator of Forests (Planning), East Nagpur has been temporarily filled up i.e. 11.8.2020 to dt. provided for the period 10.8.2022 Therefore, since the post of Chief Conservator of Forests (Planning), East Nagpur, was functioning as the post of Conservator of Forests (Planning), the government was considering declaring the post of Drawing and Disbursing Officer (DDO) as long as the Conservator of Forests (Planning), East Nagpur, was functioning and thereafter the post.
