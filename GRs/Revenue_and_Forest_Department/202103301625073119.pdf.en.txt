Preamble: In view of below average rainfall in the Kharif season 2018 in the districts of the State, the Government has declared drought in 151 talukas, 268 revenue circles in addition to these 151 talukas and 931 villages in addition to these areas in the State.
In the meeting of the Cabinet Sub-Committee held on 24/01/2019 regarding the measures to be taken to overcome the problem of fodder required for the animals in the drought-affected areas, it was decided to open cattle camps to provide fodder and water to the animals as per the requirement in the drought-hit areas of the State. In this regard, Refer No. Administrative approval has been given to open fodder camps as per the government decision.
Reference No. During the year 2019-20, as demanded by the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik, a total amount of Rs. 320, 70,98,033/- has been disbursed through BEAMS system for the period April to August, 2019 amounting to Rs. 244,60,33, 033/- under Account Head (2245 0075) and Rs. 76, 10,65,000/- under Account Head (2245 2149).
