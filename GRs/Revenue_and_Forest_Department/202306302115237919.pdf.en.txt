Sachin Bhalerao, Tehsildar, has been appointed as Tehsildar and Manager Land, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation on deputation. ..

the Revenue and Forest Department
GOVERNMENT ORDER NO. REPLACEMENT-2023/ PR No. 102/ E-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date of release: June 30, 2023
Reference:- (1) The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 (2 of 2005) General Administration Department Notification No. 14.07.2021
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) of the Maharashtra Transfer Act, 2005 and subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II to Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Tenure of Office, Post-retirement Service and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Mr. Sachin Bhalerao, Tahsildar, is being appointed as Tahsildar and Manager Land, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience with the approval of the competent authority for a period of 3 years.
The order comes into force with immediate effect and Mr. Sachin Bhalerao, Tehsildar is being relieved unilaterally as per this order. The above officers should be posted immediately.
(3) The said order is a discharge order and no fresh order is required to be issued by the concerned Divisional Commissioner/ Collector.
The services of Mr. Sachin Bhalerao, Tehsildar, are being handed over to Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai in accordance with the deputation to the post of Tahsildar and Manager Land, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation.
5. Mr. Sachin Bhalerao, Tehsildar to appear before the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
