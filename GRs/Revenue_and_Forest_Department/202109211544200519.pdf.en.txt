Preamble: Flood waters spread over urban and rural areas in various districts of the State due to heavy rainfall on and after 26/7/2019. In order to provide assistance to the families affected by the flood situation, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 30/07/2019 and 07/08/2019 decided to provide assistance at special rates to the citizens affected by the flood situation. Further, in pursuance of the Government decision dated 11/09/2019, it has been decided to provide assistance at special rates to those affected by heavy rains and floods in the month of September and October. Accordingly, funds have been distributed to all the Divisional Commissioners.
Reference No. Under Section 4, the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 545.68 lakh. Similarly, reference No. Under Section 5, the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati has sanctioned an amount of Rs. An additional amount of Rs. 7.50 crore has been sanctioned. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 545.68 lakhs (Rupees Five Crores Forty Five Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand only) to the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik and Rs. 7.50 lakhs (Rupees Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) to the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati was under consideration of the Government.
