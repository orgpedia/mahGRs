Introduction: The procedures prescribed under the relevant Government Decision No. 1 regarding the removal of sand / sands accumulated in the shoals due to natural or other reasons in order to make the arable land cultivable for the farmers. The permission granted by the Collector to remove sand from the land of the show was suspended by the Government vide Government Gazette dated 19th January, 2024 till further orders as public representatives have brought to the notice of the Government that there are irregularities and other violations in the disposal of sand from the land of the show. It was now under the consideration of the Government to review and issue a revised decision regarding removal of sand / sands accumulated in agricultural land by superseding the Government Decision dated 29th June, 2016.
The Government Decision dated 29.06.2016 in Reference No. 1 is being superseded by this Decision as it shows irregularities and other violations in the disposal of sand from the land. Also, the following decisions are being taken to make the farm land cultivable by removing the sand / sands accumulated in the private farm land due to natural or other reasons: -
1) The District Collector shall submit a self-explanatory proposal along with his recommendation to the Government with the feedback of the Taluk Agriculture Officer as well as the Ground Water Survey and Development System regarding the possible extent of the excavation on the water body, as to whether or how it is advisable to remove the sand / sand from the agricultural land applied by the excavator to remove the accumulated sand / sand from the floodplains to make it arable or how and to what extent it would be advisable to remove the sand.
2) All proposals approved by the Collector for sand removal from agricultural land should be submitted to the government as they will be reviewed at the government level. The moratorium granted under the GO dated 19th January, 2024 shall continue till the final decision on the proposal is taken by the Government.
