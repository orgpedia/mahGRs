The Executive Engineer concerned shall be responsible for carrying out the inspection and work as per the physical standards prescribed for providing civic amenities in the rehabilitated / extended village under 28-03-2011.
3) The Superintending Engineer and Executive Engineer concerned shall take appropriate care that the expenditure on the proposed civil works does not exceed the amount in the approved budgets and the prescribed limit.
4) The concerned Superintending Engineer and the concerned Executive Engineer of the concerned operational machinery shall ensure that the work-wise amount indicated in the budget for the proposed civil facilities works shall comply with the conditions of technical and administrative approval as per the prescribed procedure.
5) The Executive Engineer concerned shall transfer the proposed civic amenities to the concerned Zilla Parishad on completion in all respects and accordingly after obtaining a certificate from the Collector, Amravati and communicate the same to the Rehabilitation Department of the Government through the Collector, Amravati.
6) The budgets for these civic amenities works should have been granted technical approval by the Superintending Engineer / Executive Engineer of the concerned implementing agency as per the prevailing rules in the Maharashtra Public Works Department Work Manual.
7) The project agency must ensure that the proposed civic amenities works have not been taken up earlier by any other scheme or that the works are not covered by any other scheme and that no expenditure has been incurred on these works in the past.
8) The Superintending Engineer concerned and the Executive Engineer concerned shall take due care that the civil works under the proposal are maintained to an appropriate standard.
9) The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Amravati should complete the existing procedures to meet the conditions in the Administrative Sanction Orders presented and start the civil facilities works under the proposal immediately.
