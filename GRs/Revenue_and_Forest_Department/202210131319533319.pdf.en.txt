Introduction: In the tourism development work to be done under the Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board, Nagpur, private architect consultancy firms are appointed for the work of planning the project, preparing project reports, preparing budgets, as well as monitoring the project works after the project is approved. Instead of conducting separate tender process for each project for such appointments, the government has decided to appoint 15 Vastu Shastra Consultants on the panel of Architecture Consultant Firms on the Vastu Shastra Advisory Panel of Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board to the best companies providing such services. 1 was approved by the government. It has been mentioned in the government decision that the appointment of Vastu Shastra Advisor on this panel will be for 3 years. Accordingly, the term of the said Panel has expired on 20.12.2021.
Reference No. 2 issued by the Managing Director, Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board, Nagpur vide reference No. The panel of architecture consultants firm in the government decision of 1 has requested for an extension of the appointment of Vastu Shastra Advisor. Accordingly, reference No. The appointment of 15 Vastu Shastra consultants on the panel of Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board in the government decision of 1 was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: On the panel of Maharashtra Nature Tourism Development Board Reference No. Approval is being given to extend the appointment of 15 Vastu Shastra Consultants approved under the Government Decision of March 1, 2023.
