Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for re-
diversion of 4.90 ha. forest land under section 2 of the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980 for construction of Hydro Power Plant with an installed capacity of 20.0 MW in
Gut No.224, 225 of Village Kochale, Taluka Mokhade, Dist. Palghar by using water stored in
Existing Hindrudhaysamrat Balasaheb Thackeray Middle Vaitarna Dam in the State of
Maharashtra;
# Page 2
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the proposal
for approval of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC
Division), New Delhi;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under reference
No.3, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to divert the 4.90 ha. forest Land for the above project
under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The said approval is
subject to fulfillment of certain conditions by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide letter under reference No.5 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India
(FC Division), New Delhi, vide under reference no. 6 accorded ‘Final approval’ to divert the 4.90
ha of forest land in favour of Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, Mumbai for construction of
Hydro Power Plant in existing Hindrudhaysamrat Balasaheb Thackeray Middle Vaitarna Dam in
the State of Maharashtra under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam,
1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.7 has requested State Government to
issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 4.90 ha.
consisting Reserved forest Land more specifically described in the proposal submitted vide letter
under reference No. 1 and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be rediverted to the
User Agency, 1.e. Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, Mumbai for construction of Hydro
Power Plant in existing Hindrudhaysamrat Balasaheb Thackeray Middle Vaitarna Dam in the State
of Maharashtra.
The diversion shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the letter
dated 09.10.2024 and dated 07.04.2025 of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi. This approval for diversion of forest land 1s also
subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Jawhar should ensure that fulfillment of condition No.1.3
mentioned 11 the letter dated 07.04.2025 of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate
Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi before actual handing over of the
forest land to the user agency.
# Page 3
Deputy Conservator of Forests, Jawhar shall, ensure that the User Agency has settled the
rights of the project affected Eksali Plot Holders and FRA Right Holders and that no claims
are pending at present, before actual handing over of the forest land to the user agency.
Deputy Conservator of Forests, Jawhar shall submit documents regarding User Agency has
settled the rights of the project affected Eksali Plot Holders and FRA Right Holders, under
the supervision of the District Collector.
User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’ and ‘final
approval’ letters of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India (FC Division), New Delhi, referred above. The Chief Conservator of Forests (T),
Thane shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall
furnish the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi letters dated
09.10.2024 and dated 07.04.2025, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued under
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of Van
(Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken accordingly.
In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Chief Conservator of Forests (T), Thane shall report through the State Government to the Deputy Director General of Forests (Central), Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change,
Government of India, Nagpur as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980.
