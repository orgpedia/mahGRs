Accordingly, out of the total fund of Rs. 905.21 lakh, Rs. 665.99 lakh has been provided in the above Reference No. Approved by 03. Government Decision: Under the scheme of providing water from the Krishna River to the Tembhu Upasana Irrigation Scheme for drinking water and livestock to the Mayani Lake and the surrounding villages and 16 villages of the Man East area, the Government has decided to implement the scheme. The administrative approval was given in accordance with the government decision. Accordingly, out of Rs. 905.21 lakh, Rs. 665.99 lakh has been approved for providing funds to Water Supply and Sanitation Department as per Government Resolution dated 10/07/2019.
According to reference no. Under the above Government decision, the funds were disbursed to the Water Supply and Sanitation Department through the Budget Distribution System. Now, the balance amount of Rs. 239. 22 lakh is being made available through Budget Distribution System (BDS) under this Government decision.
In the current financial year 2021-22, Demand No. A sum of Rs. 239.22 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Forty Nine Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand only) is being released to the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation under the sub-heading C-6, 2245, Assistance for Relief of Natural Calamities, 01, Avarakshana, 102 Drinking Water Supply, (92) Expenditure over and above the standard of State Disaster Response Fund, (92) (01) Emergency Supply of Drinking Water (Compulsory), 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-Monority) (2245 2185) which is to be finally spent by them under the above head.
3. It has been observed that despite orders issued from time to time to ensure that the funds available on the budget distribution system are not unnecessarily drawn from the treasury and kept in the bank in the absence of allocation. Therefore, strict instructions are being issued again under this government decision that the funds should not be withdrawn and kept in the bank. If such a thing is found, it should be noted that it is considered a temporary violation and disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned.
4. The Water Supply and Sanitation Department shall ensure that the funds are utilised for the purpose for which they have been allocated and that the funds are not diverted for any other purpose. The disbursed funds will be disbursed directly to the contractors through Electronic Clearing Services without maintaining a personal ledger account or bank account.
However, the Water Supply and Sanitation Department should take action accordingly.
5. The Water Supply and Sanitation Department shall submit to this Department the utilization certificate for the full utilization of the funds disbursed as above.
