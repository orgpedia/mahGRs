The Proposal:
Considering the urgency of day-to-day functioning of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs and the functioning of the Legislature Session, a proposal to procure 1 scanner for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is under consideration.
Accordingly, the Department has been registered as a Buyer's Department on the Government e-Marketplace portal of the Central Government as per the guidelines issued under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour of the Government of India for carrying out the procurement process. Further, Purchase Officer and Consignee Officer, Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Verification Officer have been appointed for the department under the order at reference serial number 3. Thus, subject to the policy set by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, the process of procuring 1 scanner required for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is to be done. Administrative and financial approvals are being sought.
Government's decision:
In view of the urgency of day-to-day functioning of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs and the functioning of the Legislature Session, administrative and financial approval is hereby accorded for procurement of 1 Scanner for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs, through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the provisions of the Government Order in respect of Department of Industries, Power and Labour. The expenditure on this account will be met out of the sanctioned funds available for the financial year 2019-20 under the head of accounts mentioned below.
