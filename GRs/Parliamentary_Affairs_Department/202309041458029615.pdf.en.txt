Women employees working in this department are informed that if they have any complaint regarding sexual harassment, they should submit it to the chairperson or member secretary of the committee or the internal complaints committee.
The functions of the said Complaints Committee shall be as follows: (a) The Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, April 23, 2013 and under. The rules have been published by the Central Government on 9th December, 2013. To take necessary measures to prevent sexual harassment of women and to provide a safe working environment to women employees.
(b) to examine the complaints received from the women employees under the Department for redressal within a stipulated time and submit a proposal for further action to the appropriate authorities in accordance with the complaints.
c) To create awareness to prevent incidents like sexual harassment and to ensure creation of safe environment by holding periodic meetings of the Committee.
