The Proposal:
In view of the amendments made in the Maharashtra Members of Legislative Assembly (Removal of Disqualification) Act, 1956, it was necessary to make some facilities permissible to the Chief Whip/ Whip in the Maharashtra Legislature.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the above mentioned facts, the Government have decided that the Chief Whip/ Whip in the Legislature shall be provided the following facilities through the Legislature Secretariat.
1. Token Remuneration:- Chief Mentor- Rs. 25,000/- and Rs. 20,000/- per month. Accordingly, the remuneration of the Chief Whip and the Whip should be calculated for a period of 12 months and should be paid with effect from 11th December, 2017.
2. During the session of the Legislature at Bombay, the Chief Whip was given Rs. 25,000/- and Rs. A total allowance of Rs. 20,000/- will be allowed. The Legislature Secretariat should take action to provide vehicles for the session in Nagpur from the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur.
3. a personal assistant, a clerk-typist and a peon.
Separate office in the Legislature Secretariat.
Telephone facility in the office of the Legislature Secretariat.
6. The Legislative Assembly shall consist of one Chief Whip of the ruling party and one of the constituent parties. Thereafter, one member each of the parties having 10 per cent of the total members will be the" Whip." This means that in the current Assembly, one member from the Bharatiya Janata Party and one from the Shiv Sena will be the" chief whip." The Bharatiya Janata Party, the Congress and the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) will have one member each.
7. There shall be a" Chief Whip" of the ruling party and one of the constituent parties in the Legislative Council. Thereafter, one member each of the parties having 10 per cent of the total members will be the" Whip." At present, there is one Shiv Sena and one BJP member in the Legislative Council. The Congress and the NCP will have one member each.

8. There shall be four Chief Whips and five Whips in the Legislature. The Chief Whip will have the status of a Minister only for the Rajshishtachar. However, the salary, allowances and any other facilities admissible to the post of minister will not be admissible to them.
9. The" Protode" shall have the status of Minister of State only for the Rajshishtachar. However, the salary, allowances and any other facilities admissible to the post of Minister of State will not be admissible to them.
(2) The functions of the Chief Whip and the Whip are mainly concerned with the business of the House of the Legislature and are limited to the duration of the session and all the facilities to be provided to them shall be provided by the Legislature Secretariat. The expenditure on all these facilities will be met from the approved budgetary provision of the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat.
For this, the Maharashtra Legislature Chief Whip/ Whip Allowance Rules should be prepared by the Legislature Secretariat as per the rules.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence given by the Department of Finance vide its Informal Reference No. 76/2018/ Expenditure-4 dated 26th September, 2018.
