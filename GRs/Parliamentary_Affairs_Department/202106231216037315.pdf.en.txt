The Proposal:
Considering the number of computers in the department of parliamentary affairs which have outlived their life and the urgency of the department's legislature session, a proposal to purchase 11 computer sets for this department is under consideration. Accordingly, the Department has been registered as a Buyer's Department on the Government e-Marketplace portal of the Central Government as per the guidelines issued under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour of the Government of India for carrying out the procurement process. Further, Purchase Officer and Consignee Officer, Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Verification Officer have been appointed for the department under the order at reference serial number 3. Thus, subject to the policy set by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the procurement process of the above mentioned materials required for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is to be completed. Administrative and financial sanction is being sought from the government.
Government Decision: In view of the above mentioned in the Preamble, considering the number of computers in the department which have outlived their life and the urgency of the legislature session, administrative and financial approval is being given to spend the necessary funds from the sanctioned provision available for the financial year 2021-22 under the head of accounts mentioned below for the purchase of 11 computer sets for this department.
