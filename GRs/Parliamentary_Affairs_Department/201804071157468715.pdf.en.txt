Government's decision:
The Legislature has approved the amount specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018. The Governor has given his assent to the same and the same has been published as Maharashtra Act No. 22 of 2018 in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 27th March, 2018.
Accordingly, for the year 2018-19, now, within the limit of 70% of the total sanctioned budgetary provision, the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS) is being disbursed to the Controlling Authorities on the basis of the monthly fund statement furnished by them as shown in the attached statement A, B and C.
The facility to draw forward/ reverse the funds as per the Monthly Statement of Funds for the first nine months of 2018-19 for the period April 2018 to December 2018 is being provided on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System.
The Controlling Authorities shall take care of the expenditure as per the instructions in the circular dated 02.04.2018 issued in respect of the Finance Department.
