The ensuing Winter Session of the Maharashtra Legislature 2017 is scheduled to commence from Monday, the 11th December, 2017 at Vidhan Bhavan, Nagpur. Instructions for handling the proceedings of the Session on priority basis have been issued to all the Departments from time to time vide Circular No.3 in the said context and earlier also. Accordingly, the following recommendations have been made:
Starred Questions:- (1) As per the circular at serial number 5 and 6 under the reference of the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, the dates for sending the copies of the answers to the starred questions of the Legislative Assembly/ Legislative Council to the Legislature Secretariat have been notified. The Legislature Secretariat has developed a computer system called QIS and as per their circulars at serial numbers 5 and 6 under reference, instructions have been issued to send the copies of the answers to the starred questions online through the QIS system and to transfer the questions to other departments online through the same system. These instructions should be strictly followed. If the answers to the questions are not sent in full and within the prescribed time, the relevant answers are not included in the printed list of questions and there is a strong disapproval from the honourable members of the House. Accordingly, in the first session of 2010, instructions were issued to seek clarifications from the Secretaries of the concerned departments. All the Ministries/ Departments may, therefore, take this matter seriously and kindly take care to send the copies of the answers to the starred questions relating to them to the Legislature Secretariat within the stipulated time and send the same to this Department in the accompanying return-1 on the next day after the expiry of the relevant stipulated time period. Also, the answers to the starred questions should be sent within the stipulated time. Please ensure strict compliance of the instructions given in the Chief Secretary's note dated 17th July, 2000.
