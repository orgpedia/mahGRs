As per the said amendments, the Chairman and Deputy Chairman of the Maharashtra Legislative Council, the Speaker and Deputy Speaker of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly, the Hon. It has been provided that the personal assistants of Ministers and Ministers of State, Leader of Opposition in the Maharashtra Legislature and Members of the Maharashtra Legislature shall be paid such fixed salary as the Government may, from time to time, by order, determine. Subsequently, the government was considering increasing the salary of personal assistants. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the light of the above mentioned in the Preamble, the fixed pay of Rs. 25000/- per month, which is presently admissible to the Personal Assistants of the above dignitaries, is being increased to Rs. 30,000/- per month with effect from 01.04.2022.
The facility will be implemented by the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat and the expenditure on this facility will be met from the approved budgetary provision of the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence given by the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 503/ Expenditure-4, dated 10th October, 2022.
