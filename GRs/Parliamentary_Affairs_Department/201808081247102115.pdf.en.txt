Preamble: The innovative scheme of" Mukhyamantri Sukarmi Puraskar Yojana" is being implemented at the Ministry level for the first time for the financial year 2018-19 to motivate and motivate the officers and staff to perform their duties efficiently and to their full potential. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to constitute a committee to select eligible officers/ employees for the" Vibhag Ratna Puraskar" to be given at the administrative division level as per the provisions of the relevant Government decision.
Government's decision:
In respect of selection of eligible officers/ staff of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs for the Vibhag Ratna Award to be given at the level of Ministerial Administrative Department.
