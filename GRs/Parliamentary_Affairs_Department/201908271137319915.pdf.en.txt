Circular:
Committees have been constituted for village, mandal, municipal and district levels. The Department of Public Administration has already issued orders from time to time under the aforesaid circulars and under the said circulars regarding the appointment of the members of the Legislative Assembly and has directed that at least one member of the Legislative Council/ Legislative Assembly should be appointed from the Scheduled Tribes while constituting such committees. Further, by the said Circulars (2) to (5), the appointments of members to the Government Committees at the District, Divisional and State level shall be made by the respective State Governments. It has been directed to appoint members at the taluk level with the prior permission of the Chief Minister and with the approval of the concerned District Liaison Minister.
2. However, as per the aforesaid order, it has come to the notice of the Government that while constituting the various levels of Government Committees, the members of the Legislative Council are not being represented.
Accordingly, all the administrative departments are being directed to ensure that the government committees at the taluka level, in which the members of the assembly are the' chairman', are concerned.
