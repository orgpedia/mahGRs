Considering the urgency of the day-to-day functioning of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs and the functioning of the Legislative Assembly, a proposal to purchase 5 computers, 4 printers and 2 laptops for this department is under consideration. Accordingly, the Department has been registered as a Buyer's Department on the Government e-Marketplace portal of the Central Government as per the guidelines issued under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour of the Government of India for carrying out the procurement process. Further, Purchase Officer and Consignee Officer, Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Verification Officer have been appointed for the department under the order at reference serial number 3. Thus, subject to the policy set by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the procurement process of the above mentioned materials required for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is to be completed. Administrative and financial sanction is being sought from the government.
Government Decision: In view of the urgency of the day-to-day functioning of the Department and the functioning of the Legislative Assembly in the light of the above mentioned Preamble, the Government is granting approval for the purchase of 5 computers, 4 printers and 2 laptops for the Department under the headings mentioned below from the sanctioned funds available for the financial year 2018-2019 and to spend the necessary funds for the same.
