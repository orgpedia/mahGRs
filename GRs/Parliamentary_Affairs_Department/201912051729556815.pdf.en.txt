Circular a
The ensuing Winter Session of the Maharashtra Legislature, 2019 is scheduled to commence from Monday, the 16th December, 2019 at Vidhan Bhavan, Nagpur. Instructions for handling the proceedings of the session on priority basis have been issued to all the departments from time to time in the circular at serial number 2 in the said context, as well as earlier. Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:
Starred Questions: All the Ministries/ Departments shall ensure that the proceedings in respect of Starred Questions in the Legislative Assembly and Legislative Council are carried out within the prescribed time limit as per the instructions in the circular to be issued by the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat.
Statements to be made under Rules 93 and 97 of the Notice of Interest:
(1) Necessary action in respect of the representations received under the instructions issued by this Department from time to time in the past, the representations on the notice of adjournment motion given under rule 97 of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly Rules and the representations on the notices given by the members under rule 93 of the Maharashtra Legislative Council Rules, shall be completed by all the departments within the prescribed time.

Annual Report:- The annual report of the recognized business of the Government, boards, corporations, autonomous bodies, etc. should be submitted to the Legislature in accordance with the prescribed procedure and within the prescribed time. As the matter is being taken seriously in the meetings of the Ad-hoc Committee of the Legislative Assembly on" Laying of Documents on the Table of the House," all the concerned departments should take appropriate action on the laying of annual reports of their respective boards, corporations, undertakings, autonomous bodies etc. on the table of the House on priority and in case of delay, the reasons should be communicated to the Legislature Secretariat.
Government Bills:- It is very important that the Government Bills are discussed and passed in both the Houses of the Legislature. The Minister/ Minister of State is expected to give satisfactory answers. Therefore, a self-explanatory note containing complete information regarding the amendments included in the said Bills was submitted by the Secretary of the concerned Department to the Hon. The Minister/ Minister of State may kindly prepare the same immediately and send a copy to the Chief Minister's Secretariat, as well as to this Department for information.
