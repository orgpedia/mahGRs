Finance Department No. As per the decision of the government here, the departments have been empowered to retain 80 percent of the total posts in the department which have completed the review of the posts and the approval of the Chief Secretary's Committee on the figure determined after the review.

Therefore, as per the revised figure, the conversion of temporary posts in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs into permanent posts was under the consideration of the Government.
