Government circular
The 2nd (Monsoon) Session of the Maharashtra Legislature 2018 has been convened at Vidhan Bhavan, Nagpur from Wednesday, the 4th July, 2018. The Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs has issued instructions in this regard through a circular. During the proceedings of both the Houses of the Legislature, the issue of any department in respect of any matter can be raised at any time and the presence of the Secretaries of the Departments at Nagpur as well as in the gallery of the House of the Legislature, if necessary, for the urgent proceedings on the issues concerned is mandatory and on the day when the House during the Legislature Session has important questions related to their departments, attention-grabbing suggestions, half-an-hour discussion, discussion on supplementary demands and voting and other important business, they must be present in the Officers' Gallery of the House and the nearest senior officer in the other House. Also, information about the issues that will be discussed in the House. The Minister/ Minister of State shall ensure that the same is made available immediately.
All Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries are, therefore, requested to be present at Nagpur during the session. But if, for some unavoidable reason, he is compelled to go on a tour outside Nagpur, he should take prior permission from me and his ministers concerned and appoint a responsible officer to conduct the legislature business in his absence.
