GOVERNMENT DECISION, GENERAL ADMINISTRATION DEPARTMENT, NO. LPL-2017/ PR No.21/ K.K.15 dated 10th June, 2019
Government's decision:
As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, the General Administration Department has decided to prematurely retire the officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity from the government service after reviewing their service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. The policy has been framed in accordance with the Government Decision dated 10.06.2019. Accordingly, a Committee is being constituted to review the services of 1) Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Scale) [Grade Pay 7600 and above but less than 10000] Cadre Officers, 2) Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Scale) [Grade Pay 7600 and below] Cadre Officers and 3) Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre Officers and 4) Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D Officers in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs as under:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1 | the Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs | Chairman             |
| 2 | Joint/ Deputy Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs            | the members          |
| 3 | Shri A.N. Vasave, Joint Secretary, Department of Law& Justice           | the members          |
| 4 | Under Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs                    | The Member Secretary |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The committee will submit its recommendations to the appropriate authority for final decision.
