The forthcoming Second (Monsoon) Session of the Maharashtra Legislature 2018 is scheduled to commence from Wednesday, the 4th July, 2018 at Vidhan Bhavan, Nagpur. Instructions for handling the proceedings of the Session on priority basis have been issued to all the Departments from time to time vide Circular No.3 in the said context and earlier also. Accordingly, the following recommendations have been made.
Starred Questions:- (1) The Legislature Secretariat has developed a computer system called QIS and as per its circulars at serial numbers 5 and 6 under reference, it has issued instructions for sending the copies of the answers to the starred questions online through the QIS system and transferring the same to other departments online through the same system. These instructions should be strictly followed. If the answers to the questions are not sent in full and within the prescribed time, the relevant answers are not included in the printed list of questions and there is a strong disapproval from the honourable members of the House. Accordingly, in the first session of 2010, instructions were issued to seek clarifications from the Secretaries of the concerned departments. All the Ministries/ Departments may, therefore, take this matter seriously and kindly take care to send the copies of the answers to the starred questions relating to them to the Legislature Secretariat within the stipulated time and send the same to this Department in the accompanying return-1 on the next day after the expiry of the relevant stipulated time period.
(2) The facts of the notice questions sent for disclosure shall be duly forwarded to the Legislature Secretariat within three days in accordance with the directions in the Government Circular No. Parliamentary Affairs Division No. Mavis- 1005/110/ PR No. 29/ V, dated February 9, 2005.
Transfer of starred questions and other matters: When the subject matter of any starred question, calling attention notice, resolution or proposal is not related to the department to which it has been received, the department shall immediately forward the same to the department to which it belongs and at the level of the secretary of the department.

Government Bills:- Government Bills to be discussed and passed in both the Houses of the Legislature. This is a very important matter and the issues raised by the Hon'ble Members in the discussion on the Bills in this regard were discussed by the Hon'ble Minister. The Minister/ Minister of State is expected to give satisfactory answers. Therefore, a self-explanatory note containing complete information regarding the amendments included in the said Bills was submitted by the Secretary of the concerned Department to the Hon. The Minister/ Minister of State should prepare before the discussion in the House and send a copy to the Chief Minister's Secretariat, as well as to this department for information.
Issues of propriety, Special Mentions and Deductions and Unstarred Questions:- In the House, Hon. Issues of propriety, deduction suggestions and special mentions raised by the members are not answered by them within the stipulated time and objections are often raised by them. Therefore, the questions of propriety, deduction suggestions and special mentions are answered by Ma. Members should be sent on time. Also, the answers to the most urgent questions should be sent to the Legislature Secretariat within the prescribed time. Accordingly, since the meeting of the Ad-hoc Committee on" Laying of Papers on the Table of the House" of the Legislative Assembly has taken serious note, the proceedings regarding this matter should be done on priority and in a timely manner. Also, the information on the matter received in the current session should be sent to this department immediately after the announcement of the end of the session in the accompanying statement-2. Since the information regarding the deduction notice is being compiled by the Finance Department, the information regarding the deduction notice should be sent to the Budget-3 Branch of the Finance Department.
Note on issues/ assurances/ directions in the House.- The issue of any department may be raised in the proceedings of the Houses of the Legislature and the Legislative Council at any time and accordingly the Hon. Instructions have been given to officials concerned to take immediate steps. Therefore, in order to expedite action as per the said direction, all the departments are being instructed to ensure that the officers of their departments are present in the galleries of both the Houses of the Legislative Assembly and the Legislative Council daily. The directions given by the presiding officers, Mr. The Minister shall direct that the assurances given in the House shall be taken on record and the information thereon shall be communicated to the officers of the concerned offices of his department on the same day. So that the concerned can take further action without delay by providing a copy of the work report immediately.
All the officers may kindly ensure that as per the aforesaid instructions, all the Ministries/ Departments should give their utmost priority to the affairs of the Legislature and the replies to the issues raised by the Hon'ble Members through various instruments are coherent, complete and comprehensive and the same are forwarded to the Secretariat of the Legislature/ to the Hon'ble Members concerned within the stipulated time.
