The Proposal:
The matter of setting up an Information Centre for Members of Parliament is in the process of the Lok Sabha Secretariat. Members of Parliament can be informed about it.
Accordingly, the Lok Sabha Secretariat has informed the Government that a Nodal Officer should be appointed for the State of Maharashtra. In pursuance of the said proposal of the Lok Sabha Secretariat, the matter of appointing a Nodal Officer for the State of Maharashtra was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
According to the Lok Sabha Secretariat. The Director (Immigration), Disaster Management Division, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being appointed as the Nodal Officer for the State of Maharashtra to liaise with the State of Lok Sabha Secretariat in case of any unforeseen situation arising out of the Information Centre to be set up by Lok Sabha Secretariat for Members of Parliament.
The details of the Coordinating Officer are as follows: Coordinating Officer:- Director (DM), Disaster Management Division Address:- Disaster Management Division, Main Building, 1st Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400032 Phone No:- 022-22026868/022-22026712 Email Address:- director. dm@maharashtra.gov.in
