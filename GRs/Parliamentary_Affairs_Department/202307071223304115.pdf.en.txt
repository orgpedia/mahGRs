Circular a
The ensuing Second (Monsoon Session) of the Maharashtra Legislature 2023 is scheduled to commence from Monday, the 17th July, 2023 at Vidhan Bhavan, Mumbai. Instructions have been issued to all the departments through circulars at serial number 2 and 3 in the context of the session. Accordingly, the following recommendations have been made.
Starred Questions:- (1) The Legislature Secretariat has developed a computer system called QIS and has given instructions for sending the copies of the answers to the starred questions online through the QIS system and transferring the same to other departments online through the same system. These instructions should be strictly followed. If the answers to the questions are not submitted in full and within the prescribed time, the answers are not included in the printed list of questions and there is a strong disapproval from the honourable members of the House. The issue was also discussed in the parliamentary board meeting. Accordingly, in the first session of 2010, instructions were issued to seek clarifications from the Secretaries of the concerned departments. Therefore, all the Ministries/ Departments may kindly take this matter seriously and ensure that the copies of the answers to the starred questions relating to them are sent to the Legislature Secretariat within the stipulated time.
(2) The facts of the notice questions sent for disclosure should be sent to the Legislature Secretariat within three days in accordance with the directions in the Government Circular No.Mavis-1005/ 110/ PR.No. 29/ 5, dated February 9, 2005.
