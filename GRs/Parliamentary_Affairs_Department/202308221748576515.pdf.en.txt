The Proposal:
The computer literature of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs has been reviewed.
Considering the number of computers that have outlived their life and the urgency of the department's legislature session, a proposal to purchase 08 computer sets for this department is under consideration. Accordingly, the Department has been registered as a Buyer's Department on the Government e-Marketplace portal of the Central Government as per the guidelines issued under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour of the Government of India for carrying out the procurement process. Further, Purchase Officer and Consignee Officer, Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Verification Officer have been appointed for the department under the order at reference serial number 3. Thus, subject to the policy set by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the procurement process of the above mentioned materials required for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is to be completed. Administrative and financial sanction is being sought from the government.
Government Decision: In view of the above mentioned in the Preamble, the number of computers in this department which have outlived their life and the urgent consideration of the proceedings of the Legislative Assembly
For this department, administrative and financial approval is being accorded for spending the required funds from the sanctioned provision available for the financial year 2023-24 under the head Accounts mentioned below.
