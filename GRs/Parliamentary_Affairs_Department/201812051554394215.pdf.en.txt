The Proposal:
The department is responsible for coordination of legislature sessions between the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat and the Ministerial Administrative Department.
A proposal for procurement of 3 TV sets for this Department is under consideration for providing uninterrupted and clear viewing of the proceedings of the Legislature Session. Considering the requirement of television sets to the Department of Parliamentary Affairs as per the nature of the work of the Legislature Session, the General Administration Department has decided to procure a total of three television sets, one each in the chambers of the Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs in the Vidhan Bhavan and in the two chambers of the Department where live viewing of the Assembly and the Legislative Council is to be done, as a" special case." Approved on 19/8/2017. As per the guidelines issued by the Government of India under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, Government of India, under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2, the Department has been registered as a Buyer's Department on the Government e-Marketplace portal of the Central Government. Further, Purchase Officer and Consignee Officer, Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Verification Officer have been appointed for the department under the order at reference serial number 3. Thus, subject to the policy decided by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the procurement process of 3 television sets required for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is to be completed. Administrative and financial sanction is being sought from the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In view of the above mentioned in the Preamble, considering the nature of work of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs in connection with the Legislature Session, 3 TV sets for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs under the heading of Accounts mentioned below have been commissioned for the financial year 2018-2019.
