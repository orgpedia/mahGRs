Government circular:
The Second Report on Assurances from the First (Budget) Session of 1994 to the Second (Monsoon) Session of 2015 has been tabled in the Legislative Assembly on 16th March, 2022. The Assembly Assurances Committee in its report has made some general recommendations with feedback comments in line with the fulfillment of the assurances made by various departments of the Ministry. These are summarized in the accompanying" Form A."
In view of the directions of the Assembly Assurances Committee, all the Divisional Secretaries are directed to immediately hold a review meeting with the concerned Joint Secretaries/ Deputy Secretaries of the Departments regarding all the Assembly Assurances pending with their departments.
