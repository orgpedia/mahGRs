Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts notified for Persons with Disabilities under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading Policy/ Act/ Rules Notification List of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability Notified on 04.01.2021. As mentioned in the notification, the posts in the list published by the Central Government in respect of the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, will be reserved for the disabled in the State Government Service, even if the duties and responsibilities, nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are different from the posts in the State Government Service.
