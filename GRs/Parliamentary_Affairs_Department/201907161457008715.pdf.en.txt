Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2019 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2019) has been passed by the Legislative Assembly. The Governor has given his assent to the same and the same has been published as Maharashtra Act No. 08 of 2019 in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 04.07.2019.
The accounts for the year 2019-20 have already been allotted to the Controlling Authorities under the Government Resolution dated 03.04.2019. Considering this allocation, now the Budget Estimates, within the limit of 60% of the total sanctioned budgetary provision, are being distributed to the Controlling Authorities through the Distribution and Controller System (BEAMS) on the basis of their monthly fund statement as shown in the attached statement A, B and C.
The facility to draw forward/ reverse the funds as per the Monthly Statement of Funds for the first nine months of 2019-20 for the period April, 2019 to December, 2019 is being provided on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System.
The Controlling Authorities shall take care of the expenditure as per the instructions in the circular dated 08.07.2019 issued in respect of the Finance Department.
