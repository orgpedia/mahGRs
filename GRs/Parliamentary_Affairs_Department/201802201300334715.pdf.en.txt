Government circular
The first (Budget) Session of the Maharashtra Legislature for the year 2018 has been convened at Vidhan Bhavan, Mumbai from Monday, the 26th February, 2018. The Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs has issued instructions in this regard through a circular. During the proceedings of both the Houses of the Legislature, the issue of any department in relation to any matter can be raised at any time and for the urgent proceedings of the concerned issues, the presence of the Secretaries of the departments in Mumbai as well as in the gallery of the House of the Legislature is mandatory at that time and on the day when the House of the Legislature during the session has important questions related to their departments, attention-grabbing suggestions, half-an-hour discussion, discussion on supplementary demands and voting and other important business, they must be present in the Officers' Gallery of the House and the nearest senior officer in the other House. He also gave details of the issues to be discussed in the meeting. The Minister/ Minister of State shall ensure that the same is made available immediately.
All Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries are, therefore, required to be present in Mumbai during the session. But if, for some unavoidable reason, he is compelled to go on a tour outside Mumbai, he should first seek prior permission from me and his ministers concerned and appoint an officer responsible for the conduct of legislative business in his absence.
