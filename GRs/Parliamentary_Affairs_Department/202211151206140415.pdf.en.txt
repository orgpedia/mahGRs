The Proposal:
As per the provisions of Section 6 (6) of the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956, a fixed salary of Rs.15000/- per month for the convenience of driver has been made admissible from 01.01.2021, subject to the Government decision dated 11.01.2021, to enable the existing members of the Maharashtra Legislature to discharge the functions of their office more effectively. The Cabinet in its meeting held on 23.08.2022 has approved an increase in the remuneration of Drivers from Rs.15,000/-to Rs.20,000/-per month.
Accordingly, the government was considering raising the fixed salary of these drivers.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision
In view of the above, the existing fixed emoluments of Rs.15000/-per month to the drivers of the sitting members of the Legislative Council are being increased to Rs.20,000/-per month with effect from 01.04.2022.
The Maharashtra Legislature Members (Appointment of Drivers) Rules, 2021 will be applicable to the driver to be appointed by the members of the legislature.
The facility will be implemented by the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat and the expenditure on this facility will be met from the approved budgetary provision of the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence given by the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 503/ Expenditure-4, dated 10th October, 2022.
