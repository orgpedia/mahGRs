Considering the urgency of the day-to-day functioning of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs and the functioning of the Legislative Assembly, a proposal was under consideration to purchase 5 computers, 4 printers and 2 laptops for this department. Accordingly, the Department has been registered as a Buyer's Department on the Government e-Marketplace portal of the Central Government as per the guidelines issued under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour of the Government of India for carrying out the procurement process. Further, Purchase Officer and Consignee Officer, Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Verification Officer have been appointed for the department under the order at reference serial number 3. Administrative approval has been given for the purchase of computers, printers and laptops for this department as per the reference serial number 4. in such a way
Pursuant to the policy laid down by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, the procurement process of the above mentioned items required for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs has been completed and the matter of sanctioning the expenditure incurred and disbursement of the same is under consideration.
Government Decision: 5 Computer Sets and 3 Single Function Printers procured for Department of Parliamentary Affairs
The purchase was made through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal. The Department has adopted online bidding method for procurement and the lowest bidder is SUN SYSTEMS, Delhi 110075 for Computer Set (L-1) and lowest for Single Function Printers.
