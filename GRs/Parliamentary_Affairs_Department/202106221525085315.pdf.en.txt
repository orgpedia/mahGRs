Government circular:
The first report on the promises made in the second (Monsoon Session) of 2015 has been tabled in the Legislative Assembly on 10th March, 2021. The Assembly Assurances Committee in its report has made some general recommendations with feedback comments in line with the fulfillment of the assurances made by various departments of the Ministry. These are summarized in the accompanying" Form A."
In view of the directions of the Assembly Assurances Committee, all the Divisional Secretaries are directed to ensure that all the Assembly Assurances pending with their respective departments are duly complied with.

However, the Secretaries of all the Ministries/ Departments shall ensure strict and strict compliance of the recommendations made by the Assurances Committee in its first report and submit the action taken report to the Committee within one month.
