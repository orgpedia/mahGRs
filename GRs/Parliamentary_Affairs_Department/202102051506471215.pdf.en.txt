The Proposal:
At the beginning of the budget session of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly, Shri. The Governor addresses the honourable members of both the Houses of the Legislature. It is customary to provide copies of these speeches from the executive folders to honourable members of the legislature and other dignitaries. The folders are provided by this section. A proposal to purchase folders for these dignitaries is under consideration of this Department.
Under the Government Decision No. 3 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor of the Government, it has been made mandatory for the departments to purchase goods and services on the Government e-Marketplace portal.
However, these folders belong to the Legislative Council. The presiding officer, Ma. Members of Parliament and other dignitaries
It is important to pay attention to their quality. The facility to view the folders directly in the procurement process through the Government e-Marketplace is not available. Since the presented folders are to be given to the dignitaries, the final selection is made by physically inspecting the folders. As there is no facility to access the sample folder from the Government e-Marketplace portal, there is no opportunity to check its quality, shape and size physically. In this background, as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 1st December, 2016, under Roll No. 2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour (Para 3.2.3), on the basis of the rate sheet, a special case
