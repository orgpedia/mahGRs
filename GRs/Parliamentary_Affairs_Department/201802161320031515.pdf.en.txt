The ensuing Budget Session of the Maharashtra Legislature will commence from Monday, the 26th February, 2018 at Vidhan Bhavan, Mumbai. Instructions for handling the proceedings of the Session on priority basis have been issued to all the Departments from time to time vide Circular No.3 in the said context and earlier also. Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:
Starred Questions:- (1) The Legislature Secretariat has developed a computer system called QIS and as per its circulars at serial numbers 5 and 6 under reference, it has issued instructions for sending the copies of the answers to the starred questions online through the QIS system and transferring the same to other departments online through the same system. These instructions should be strictly followed. If the answers to the questions are not sent in full and within the prescribed time, the relevant answers are not included in the printed list of questions and there is a strong disapproval from the honourable members of the House. Further, in pursuance of the directions issued earlier in the first session of 2010 regarding seeking explanation from the Secretaries of the concerned Departments, all the Ministries/ Departments may kindly take this matter seriously and take care to send the copies of the answers to the starred questions relating to them to the Legislature Secretariat within the stipulated time and send the same to this Department in the accompanying Return-1 on the next day of the expiry of the relevant due date.
(2) If the answers to the starred questions of the Legislative Assembly and the Legislative Council are negative, then the facts of the questions sent for information and disclosure should be sent to the Legislature Secretariat within three days in accordance with the directions given in the Government Circular No. 1005/ 110/ PR No. 29/ 5, dated February 9, 2005.
(3) Also, care should be taken to comply with the procedure regarding online transfer of starred questions to other departments as mentioned in the circulars of the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat at serial numbers 5 and 6 under reference.
