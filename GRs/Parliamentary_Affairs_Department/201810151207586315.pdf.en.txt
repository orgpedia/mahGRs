Government's statement:
On the basis of the final result of the Clerk-Typist Examination-2014 held for appointment to the post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi-English) in Group C vide Government Memorandum dated 24th July, 2015 of the General Administration Department, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has recommended Shri.
Sahebrao Bhiku Chavan was appointed as Clerk-Typist (Marathi) in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs. Accordingly, vide order dated 26.08.2015, Shri Sahebrao Bhiku Chavan has been appointed as Clerk-Typist in this Department.
As stated in the letter dated 31st May, 2018 under the reference of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mr. Chavan has mentioned in the general instructions given to the candidates on the website of Maharashtra Public Service Commission Online Application System, the instructions No. Violation of instructions 1.3.1 and 1.3.3. The Commission has decided to permanently debar Mr. Chavan from all examinations and selections conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission with effect from the date of examination i.e. 27th July, 2014 and the recommendation of Mr. Chavan for the post of Clerk-Typist in the said Clerk-Typist Examination, 2014 has been cancelled by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide order dated 31st May, 2018.

The Appointment of Shri Sahebrao Bhiku Chavan as Clerk Typist in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs has been cancelled vide Government Memorandum dated 15th September, 2018 and the Department of Parliamentary Affairs has directed the Department of Parliamentary Affairs to take appropriate action for cancellation of appointment of Shri Chavan as Clerk Typist. The appointment of Shri Sahebrao Bhiku Chavan as Clerk Typist in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs vide Government Memorandum dated 26.08.2015 of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs dated 15.09.2018 is hereby cancelled.
