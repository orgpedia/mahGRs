The fourth session of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Winter Session 2020) will be held at Vidhan Bhavan, Mumbai on Monday. It has been in effect since December 14, 2020. The issue of any department in relation to any matter can be raised at any time in the proceedings of both the Houses of the Legislature. Therefore, the presence of the secretaries of the departments at the headquarters as well as in the gallery of the House of the Legislature, if necessary, is mandatory for immediate action on the relevant issues. During the legislature session, on the day when the House has business related to their departments, discussion and voting on supplementary demands and other important business, the secretary of the department must be present in the secretary gallery of the House and the nearest senior officer should be present in the other House. Also, information about the issues that will be discussed in the House. Care should be taken that the Minister/ Minister of State will be made available immediately.
In view of the above, all the Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries are required to be present at Mumbai during the period of the session. But if, for some unavoidable reason, he is compelled to go on a tour outside Mumbai, he should take prior permission from me and his concerned ministers and appoint an officer responsible for the conduct of legislative business in his absence.

The time for action by the government is prescribed by the rules of the Legislative Assembly and the Legislative Council. However, in this case, similarly, the House expresses disapproval of the Hon'ble Members for not acting on the assurances given by the Government within the stipulated time. Also, in this regard. Presiding officers, from time to time, in meetings or through letters, direct the government to take strict action against the concerned. In this background, Hon'ble Members should ensure that the matters raised by various Parliamentary arms are disposed of within the prescribed time frame and that no such matters remain pending.
In view of the facts mentioned in the aforesaid paragraph, all the Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries shall personally ensure that the replies to the matters raised by the Hon'ble Members through various instruments are complete and comprehensive and that the same are forwarded to the Legislature Secretariat/ respective Hon'ble Members within the prescribed time and the Assurance Reports are submitted within the prescribed time.
Please note that if any matter raised through various Parliamentary instruments and the time limit set out above remains pending beyond the said time limit, strict action will be taken against the Secretary of the department concerned.
Be sure to follow the instructions above.
