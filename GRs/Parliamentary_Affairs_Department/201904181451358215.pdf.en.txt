Preamble: The Departmental Promotion Committee was constituted to consider the issue of allowing the benefit of higher pay scale under the Assured Progress Scheme to the employees of Group-B Non-Gazetted, Group-C and Group-D in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs under the revised scheme of three benefits.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to make the benefit of higher pay scale permissible to the employees of Group-B Non-Gazetted, Group-C and Group-D cadres of the Department of Parliamentary Affairs under the Assured Progress Scheme under the revised scheme of three benefits, the Departmental Promotion Committee for the Department of Parliamentary Affairs is being constituted as follows:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs | Chairman             |
| 2. | Under Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs                   | the members          |
| 3. | Cell Officer (Representation of Backward Classes)                      | the members          |
| 4. | Officer of the Chamber (Clause V), Department of Parliamentary Affairs | The Member Secretary |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
