GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MAFUYO-2019/ PR No. 149/ HEALTH-6 dated 23rd September, 2019
Under the scheme (2210 G 289), 60% of the funds under this head i.e. Rs. 9,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crores only) have been disbursed to this department. A proposal to distribute this fund to the State Health Guarantee Society for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, a conditional amount of Rs. 9,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crore only) is being transferred to the Controller General of Health Services for immediate transfer to the account of the State Health Guarantee Society from the provision made in the Budget under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2019-20 to pay the insurance premium of the beneficiary family under the scheme and to meet other administrative expenses on the scheme. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
Demand No. T-05 2210- Medical Services and Public Health 03- Rural Health Services Odd-Even Medical 796- Tribal Area Sub-Plan (01)- Scheme under Tribal Area Sub-Plan (01) (06)- Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (State Scheme) (TSP) 2210 G 289 (Dattamat) 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment)
The Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer for the transfer of the said funds to the Society.
The State Health Guarantee Society shall strictly comply with all the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated 19.09.2019 of the Tribal Development Department while spending the funds available under the Tribal Sub-Plan.
All concerned should strictly follow the instructions given by the Government Circular dated 08.07.2019 of the Finance Department. It is also certified that the conditions mentioned in Annexure No. 9 to the said Circular have been fulfilled.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the consent given by the Tribal Development Department under the Government Decision dated 19.09.2019 and the authority received from the Government Circular dated 08.07.2019 of the Finance Department.
