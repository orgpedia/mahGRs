Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The Central share (60%) of the funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of the scheme is directly credited to the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on State account.
For the implementation of the scheme in the financial year 2022-23, the Central Government has decided to provide Rs. The State has received an amount of Rs. 6,04,00, 206/- (Rupees Six Crores Four Lakhs Two Hundred and Six only) under the ESCROW ACCOUNT.
40% of the State share of Rs. 4,02,66, 804/- (Rupees Four Crore Two Lakh Sixty Six Thousand Eight Hundred Four only) was under consideration at the Government level. 40% of the State share of Rs. 4,02,66, 804/- (Rupees Four Crores Two Lakhs Sixty Six Thousand Eight Hundred Four only) has been released by the Tribal Development Department to the Principal Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department. As per the Government decision dated 30.11.2022, Government approval has been accorded to disburse the budget funds on the distribution system.
A decision in this regard was pending with the government.
