The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal vide order dated 12.04.2019 has directed to decide the unauthorised absence of Dr. Bholane in accordance with the provisions of Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 and in particular Rule 47. The matter is under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Dr. Usha Narendra Bholane, Medical Officer, Class-III has been absent without leave of absence from the date of her superannuation on 16.04.1983 to 30.11.2004 (21 years, 7 months and 14 days) as per her prescribed age. Rule 16 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 prescribes the period of continuous leave. According to this, no government employee can be granted any kind of leave for a period of more than five consecutive years. As per this provision, the period of the above prolonged unauthorised absence of Dr. Bholane is being treated as a period of service. As per the provisions of Rule 47 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, Dr.
Bholane will not be entitled to the previous service.
