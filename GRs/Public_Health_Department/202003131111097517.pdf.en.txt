GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the National Leprosy Control Programme, 1054 posts have been created as per the decision of the Government of Reference No. 1, out of which 384 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 384 temporary posts, 177 temporary posts have been made permanent as 80% of the number of posts have been made permanent under the government decision of reference number 2 and 3. The remaining 207 temporary posts are included in the establishment review approved in accordance with the Government Decision of Reference No. 1. Referred No. As per the Government decision dated 04.04.2020, the said 223 temporary posts were extended till 29.02.2020.

The 223 vacancies are for the posts of Medical Officer in Dr. Bandorwala Leprosy Hospital, Kondhwa, Pune.
