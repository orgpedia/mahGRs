The proposal-
The decision to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana has been taken as per the Government decision dated 26th February, 2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. During the financial year 2021-22, under the head 2210 G 449 (General), Rs. A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore has been allocated for this. Out of this amount, Rs. 3 crore has been released. A sum of Rs. 55.57 crore has been disbursed to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of insurance premium. The State share of administrative expenses for Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is Rs. 1,91,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety One Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.
