Introduction: Women and Cottage Hospital, Neknur, G. Beed. A total of 8 budgets and plans amounting to Rs. 21.63 lakhs have been submitted to the Government for administrative approval by the Joint Director, Health Services, Mumbai. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: For a total of 8 repair works in Women and Cottage Hospital Neknur, G. Beed, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 21.63 lakhs (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Sixty Three Thousand only).
