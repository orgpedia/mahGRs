GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. Divisional Reference Hospital, Amravati. The Swastik Unemployed Self Employment Service Co-operative Society, Amravati has provided sanitation services and room attendant manpower services from May 2018 to January 2019 at a cost of Rs. Pending payments of Rs. 66,08,400/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Eight Thousand Four Hundred only) are being allowed subject to the following conditions: The concerned authorities shall ensure that the contractual services mentioned in the proposal are carried out in accordance with the procedure and general directions as prescribed under the extant rules.
(1) It shall be the responsibility of the officer concerned to ensure that the contractual service mentioned in the proposal is actually received by the hospital concerned.
(2) The officer concerned shall ensure that the payment for the service contracted in the said proposal has not been made earlier.
(3) In case of any irregularity in the matter of such payments, the entire responsibility shall be of the concerned officer.
