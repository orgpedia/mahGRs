The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500) has been given the power to transfer Medical Officers, Group-A within the district to a committee headed by the Collector. The government was considering revoking the order.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100-177500) Medical Officers, Group-A are hereby permanently terminated the powers conferred on the Committee headed by the Collector under the Government Decision No. 2. However, except for the matters mentioned in the Government Resolution at Read No. 2, other matters shall continue to apply.
