GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. PURE-2019/ PR No.102/ HEALTH-6, dated 03.02.2020
A proposal to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) to the State Health Guarantee Society was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, a conditional amount of Rs. 191,31,17, 257/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ninety-one Crores Thirty-one Lakhs Seventeen Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Seven only) is being transferred to the account of the State Health Guarantee Society for the financial year 2019-20, to meet the insurance premium of the beneficiary family for the period from 01.01.2020 to 31.03.2020 and to meet other administrative expenses on the scheme. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
