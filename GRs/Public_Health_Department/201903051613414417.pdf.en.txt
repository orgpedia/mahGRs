The Department has been empowered to liaise with the Central Government for implementation, follow the instructions of the Central Government and take necessary action for effective implementation of the scheme. To effectively implement the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana in the State, the Government was considering to empanel private hospitals empanelled under Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana for Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
Government's decision:
As per the government decision dated September 21, 2018, the treatment has been approved in government hospitals at present. There are 405 private hospitals empanelled under Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. In this empanelled private hospital, the beneficiaries identified on the basis of Social, Economic and Caste Census (SECC) data under Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana are being approved for 840 treatments (out of 971 treatments, excluding 131 treatments reserved for government hospitals) in the empanelled private hospital, in combination with Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. Also, a total of 1393 treatments are being approved for inclusion in National TMS (Portability) to provide benefits of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana to patients in other states. The Chief Executive Officer, State Health Guarantee Society, Worli, Mumbai shall take action for making suitable changes in the contract with the empanelled hospitals.
