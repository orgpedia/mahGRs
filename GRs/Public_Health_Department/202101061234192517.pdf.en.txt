The Proposal:
In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. However, in order to provide preventive measures and treatment and vaccination against COVID-19 in the country and the state, the Central Government under the National Health Mission (NHM) had proposed to provide 100% central share of the disbursed grant to the State Health Societies for the general category of the National Rural Health Programme (NRHP). The details of the Central Assistance disbursed so far in this case are as follows. :

Government's decision:
The Government of India has approved the release of 100% Central share from the General Category (GEN) Budgetary Allocation for 2020-21 for COVID-19 vaccination for National Rural Health Programme under National Health Mission (NHM). 3 has been approved. For the distribution of the Central Share, the Account Head 2210F157 down to the year 2020
21 in the current financial year. A provision of Rs. 166947.31 lakh has been made. Therefore, Rs. 11,94,14, 720.00 (Rupees Eleven Crores Ninety Four Lakhs Fourteen Thousand Seven Hundred and Twenty only) is hereby approved.
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. The amount is to be deposited in the treasury by the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, State Health Societies for spending on the prescribed matters.
They should be handed over. The Deputy Director (a. And p. ), Health Services, Mumbai are being declared as" Controlling Authorities."
The expenditure on this proposal shall be covered under the head of accounts mentioned below and shall be met from the available provision sanctioned for the year 2020-21.
