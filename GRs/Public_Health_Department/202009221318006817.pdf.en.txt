Committee's mandate
1.Where in the state illegal abortion has taken place. An inquiry into the incident will be conducted and a report will be submitted to the government.
2. Identify the errors that lead to such incidents.
3) Suggest measures to prevent recurrence of such incidents.
The committee will submit its report to the government within a period of one month from the date of occurrence of the incident. The committee will have to submit its report within the stipulated time frame.
