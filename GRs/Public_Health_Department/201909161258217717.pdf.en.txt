The Proposal:
For the financial year 2019-20, Rs.2210 crore has been provided under the head G251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) for implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
341.50/-crore and by way of Supplementary Demand for June, 2019, Rs. 200.00/- crore out of total Rs. 541.50/ crore has been budgeted. Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, the budgetary provision for the financial year 2019-20 under the head of accounts shown below for the period from 1st October, 2019 to 31st December, 2019 for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiary family and for meeting other administrative expenses on the scheme is Rs. 179,79,27, 252/- (Rs. One hundred and nineteen crore nineteen lakh seventy two thousand two hundred and fifty two only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of the State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
