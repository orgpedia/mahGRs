The Proposal:
In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. However, in order to take preventive measures and treatment of COVID-19 pandemic in the country and in the State, the Central Government under National Health Mission (NHM) had proposed to provide 100% central share of devolved grants to the State Health Societies for general category of National Rural Health Programme (NRHP).
