The Proposal:
A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps of the building repair work of the District Hospital, Nashik was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: 2. The budget of the building repair work of the District Hospital, Nashik is based on the rate list of 2018-19. It includes a provision of 12 per cent for Goods and Services Tax and 0.5 per cent for labour insurance. Administrative approval is being accorded to the budgets and plans for laying of drainage pipelines, colouring, de-silting, water proofing, replacement of window panes, installation of paver blocks, installation of tiles and other ancillary works amounting to Rs. 1,12,00, 000/- subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1) Technical approval of the competent authority of the Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) Before starting the construction, the map should be approved by the architect, S.B. Department Nashik.

(3) When tendering the above work, a single tender should be issued for all the mentioned works without separately tendering each work.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
5) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director of Health Services Board in the Department of Health.
7) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
9) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
10) Ensure that the above-mentioned tasks are completed within this amount.
Expenditure on building repair work at District Hospital, Nashik should be shared under Demand No.H8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital expenditure on medical services and public health services, 01 Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance- (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Clinics, (1) (A) (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Large Constructions (42100014), 53- Large Constructions.
The Director of Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the government to allocate the amount in the budget for the repair work of the building of the District Hospital, Nashik.
