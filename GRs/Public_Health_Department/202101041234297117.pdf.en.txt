4) The rates of the proposed procurement are estimated at the time of granting administrative approval. Therefore, the lowest prices available on the GeM Portal, other state rates, National Pharmacy Pricing Authority (NPPA) should be considered. Also, if the items in the Haffkine Institute are being purchased or have been purchased, the rates should be taken as the estimated rates taking into account the minimum rates received in the tender. It should be ensured that the price is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the supplier has supplied the item to other places.
5) Pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection of the items to be purchased should be done to ensure that only the items with proper and correct specifications are purchased.
6) Storage and distribution of the items to be purchased after supply, supply chain management and inventory management should be done in a proper manner to ensure that these items do not remain unused and are used in a timely manner.
