Preamble: The Government has decided to select the service provider by inviting open tender through the National Health Mission to provide the service of ambulances equipped with medical equipment with state-of-the-art communication system to provide immediate medical assistance to the disaster victims in the state. A Tender Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Health) vide order dated 28.10.2010. The tender committee has qualified the tender submitted by M/ s BVG India and awarded the contract for the operation of Emergency Response Service (EMS) of a total of 937 ambulances, 233 Advance Life Saving (ALS) and 704 Basic Life Supporting (BLS), across the state in the next 5 years.
