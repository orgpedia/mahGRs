In the meeting of the State Supervisory Board held on 1/3/2016, it was decided to reconstitute the State Inspection and Monitoring Committee to more effectively implement the Pre-Conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques (Prevention of Sex Selection) Act 2003 in the State. Accordingly, the government decided to reconstitute the committee.
Government Decision The Government is approving the re-constitution of the following Inspection and Monitoring Committees under the Chairmanship of the State Women's Commission for the inspection of Sonography Centres registered under the provisions of the Pre-Conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic System (Prohibition of Sex Selection) Act, 2003 and for the implementation of the said Act.

The committee will have the following functions:
Controlling the implementation of the abortion law.
To inspect the Sonography Centre/ Genetic Counselling Centre and seek the help of the appropriate authorities concerned.
To notify the appropriate authorities concerned to seal the machine in case of malfunctioning in the Sonography Centre/ Genetic Counselling Centre.
The Committee shall, if any shortcomings are found in the implementation of the above Act, recommend remedial measures to the Supervisory Board and the Government for its effective implementation.
