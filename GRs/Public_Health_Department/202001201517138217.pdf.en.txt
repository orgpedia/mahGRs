Preamble: The matter of administrative approval of the budget and plans for the construction of the hospital building at Rural Hospital, Saundad, G. Gondia is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. The budget for the construction of main building and residence of the Rural Hospital, Saundad, G. Gondia, is based on the 2015-16 tariff list. The budget for the project has been fixed at 4 per cent. Apart from this, provision has been made for internal and external electrification, water supply, sewerage, roads under the fence wall, CC drain, solar power system, furniture, parking system, rain water harvesting, fire fighting system, lift, outdoor toilet, public center and incidental expenses, insurance etc. The provisions have been corrected and according to the recapitulation sheet attached with the amount of Rs. 1898.40 lakh, the administrative approval is being given to the budget and plans under this Government decision subject to the following terms and conditions:
