The Proposal:
The National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) was launched in April 2005 with the objective of providing accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural poor, the marginalized and the needy. Factors affecting health (e.g. Food, environment, sanitation, safe water supply, women and child development etc. From the year 2013-14, the Government of India has decided to take up the above health related programmes in urban areas under the name of" National Urban Health Mission." The Central share and State share is 60:40 and the Central Government has disbursed the first installment for 2018-19 under the general component on 60% share.
