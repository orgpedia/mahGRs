Introduction: Refer No. In a court case of abortion beyond 20 weeks under Sections 1 and 2, Md. A permanent medical board has been set up in 35 districts (including Mumbai) of the state to advise the court.
Reference No. Under the Central Government notification, abortion up to 24 weeks of pregnancy has been legalized under the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (Amendment) Rules, 2021 under the Medical Termination of Pregnancy Act, 1971 and approval of the Medical Board constituted by the State Government/ UT is required for medical abortion beyond 24 weeks of pregnancy. Accordingly, the Permanent Medical Boards constituted in the State under Reference No. 1 and 2, will henceforth refer to the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (Amendment) Rules, 2021, as per sub-clause (2B) of Section 3 of the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (Amendment) Rules, 2021, for approval of termination of pregnancy beyond 24 weeks of gestation by the Medical Board. The matter was under consideration of the Government to examine and give its opinion (approval or disapproval) as to whether it would be appropriate for the pregnant woman to undergo medical abortion as per the power conferred in the notification dated 03.04.2018 and to fix the revised jurisdiction of the Permanent Medical Board.
Government Decision: The Permanent Medical Boards constituted in the State under Reference No. 1 and 2 shall henceforth examine the pregnant woman who requests the Medical Board for approval of medical abortion beyond 24 weeks of pregnancy in accordance with sub-clause (2B) of Section 3 of the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (Amendment) Rules, 2021 and the report of the said pregnant woman as per the power conferred on them.

2 (b) to give the opinion of the Medical Board in Form-D on the approval or rejection of the request for approval of the Medical Board for medical abortion beyond 24 weeks of pregnancy as per clause 2 2 (b) of section 3 of the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (Amendment) Rules, 2021 within 3 days of receipt of the request from the Medical Board.
Request for approval of medical board for medical abortion beyond 24 weeks of pregnancy as per sub-clause (2B) of section 3 of the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (Amendment) Rules, 2021
To ensure that the procedure of medical abortion, if approved by the Medical Board as made, is carried out in an approved medical abortion centre with proper counselling and all safety precautions within 5 days of receipt of such request.
