The Proposal:
As the contract with United India Insurance, a Public Sector Insurance Company, had expired on 31.03.2023, the said contract was extended till 30.06.2023 vide Government Resolution dated 21.06.2023 in the above Reference No. The government is considering a comprehensive reform in the Integrated Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Yojana and Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana. It will take some time to complete the administrative work. Therefore, the government was considering extending the contract.
Government's decision:
The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed with United India Insurance Company (UNI) for Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) with effect from 01.07.2023. The period is being extended up to 30.09.2023. However, if the process of implementing the revised scheme is completed during the extension period, the agreement will be terminated with 60 days' advance notice as per the provisions of the existing agreement.
