The Proposal:
The National Hepatitis Control Programme has been launched in the country from 28.07.2018 and the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, New Delhi has issued guidelines for setting up of Model Treatment Centres, Training Centres, State Laboratory and District Labs in the programme. Accordingly, the programme is being implemented in 35 districts of Maharashtra and 27 Model Treatment Centres and Training Centres have been set up in the state. Accordingly, the matter of forming a Steering Committee and Inter-Sectoral Coordination Action Committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The government is also approving the formation of a Steering Committee and Inter-Sectoral Coordination Action Committee for effective implementation of the National Hepatitis Control Programme in the state.
