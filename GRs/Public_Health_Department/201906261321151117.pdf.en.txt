Introduction: As the rural hospital was sanctioned in Bhatkuli G. Amravati, the matter of shifting the primary health centre there was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: 2. Bhatkuli G. Amravati Rural Hospital has been approved as a primary health centre. The shifting of Bhatkuli to G. Amravati is being approved subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the Primary Health Center can be operational in the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
