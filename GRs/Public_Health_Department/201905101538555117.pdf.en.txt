Various social organizations had expressed their willingness to enter into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the state government to provide health facilities to all the general public. Following that, Ma. The Chief Minister presided over the function. MoUs have been signed with various institutions on 11/2/2019. The government was contemplating to take action as per the agreement. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
The state government has taken a decision to provide health services to all. The Chief Minister presided over the function. The Government is approving the following activities as mentioned in the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed with various institutions on 11/2/2019.
