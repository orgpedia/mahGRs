The proposal-
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. In the financial year 2023-24, the original allocation under the head 2210 G 888 (State share 40%) for the scheme was Rs. The budget is Rs 30 crore. For the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), insurance premium for the quarter October, 2023 to December, 2023 has been provided at State share (40%). 9,44,80, 372/- (Rupees Nine Crore Forty Four Lakh Eighty Three Thousand Thirty Two Only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.

Government Decisions For Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, the State Government will bear the premium (40%) for the period from 01.10.2023 to 31.12.2023 for the insurance premium of Rs. 9,44,80, 372/- (Rupees Nine Crore Forty Four Lakh Eighty Three Thousand Thirty Two only) to the Controller of Health Services for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
It is being delivered. The amount should be deposited in the State Health Insurance Society.
The funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions mentioned in Government Resolution No. 27/2022/ Regulations, dated 12.01.2023.
1) The amount of this fund at the insurance rate prescribed by the Government should be provided to the insurance company/ hospital concerned against the insurance of the beneficiaries of the combined Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society to be credited to the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
