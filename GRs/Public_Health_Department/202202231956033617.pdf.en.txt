The Proposal:
National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) was launched by the Government of India in April 2005 with the objective of providing accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population. Factors affecting health (e.g. Food, environment, sanitation, safe water supply, women and child development etc. From the year 2013-14, the Government of India has decided to take up the above health related programmes in urban areas under the name of" National Urban Health Mission."
The Centre's share in the scheme is 60: 40 per cent. The Government was contemplating to provide the funds for the Socially Special Component (SCSP) for the National Urban Health Mission to the State Health Societies from the approved budgetary allocation of 2021-22. The details of grants disbursed so far in this case are as follows.

Government's decision:
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance and the Department of Finance have approved the release of 60% Central and 40% State share for the Socially Special Component (SCSP) for the National Urban Health Programme under the National Health Mission. The budgetary allocation for 2021-22 for devolution of Central and State share is Rs. 1800.00 Lakhs and State Share 2210G627 for Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 2371.73 lakh has been made. As per this provision, Rs. 876.00 lakhs and State share of Rs. 1170.81 lakh has been disbursed and Rs. 924.00 lakhs and Rs. 1200.92 lakh is left. Hence, 60% of the amount of Rs. 310.00 lakhs and 40% State share of Rs. An amount of Rs. 206.67 lakh is hereby approved for disbursement to State Health Societies.
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment to the treasury and hand over the amount to the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, State Health Society for spending on the prescribed matters. The Deputy Director (a. And p. ), Health Services, Mumbai are being declared as" Controlling Authorities."
The expenditure on this proposal should be spent under the head of accounts as mentioned below and it should be met from the available provision sanctioned for the year 2021-22.
