Introduction: The public health department of the state provides health facilities to the patients from general hospitals, sub-district hospitals, rural hospitals and state workers insurance hospitals. However, due to the lack of medical officers and senior officials in the hospital, it is difficult to provide proper health facilities to the patients on time. Even after trying for the post of Medical Officer and Specialist, there is not enough medical officer available or even if available, there is a high rate of not joining the service if the post is not found in the preferred place. As a result, a large number of posts in the health service remain vacant, which is adversely affecting the patient service in the state. The decision was taken by the state health department.
