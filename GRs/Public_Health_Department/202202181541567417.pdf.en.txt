Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
3) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work can increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal for this should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
6) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
7) The Department of the Environment, in the course of actual work, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
