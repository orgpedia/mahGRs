Preamble- As per the guidelines of the Central Government, the Government was considering the formation of a technical committee to examine the feasibility of trauma care units and recommend to the Government to formulate new policies for starting trauma care units. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision A Technical Committee is being constituted to examine the feasibility of Trauma Care Centres in the State and prepare a broad framework of Trauma Care Centres in the State based on the guidelines of the Central Government and start recommending to the Government.

The functions of the Technical Committee shall be as follows:
Review of all approved trauma care units.
2. To formulate a policy on trauma care units in the State as per the guidelines issued by the Central Government.
3. Analyze the staff and equipment required for the trauma care unit.
4. To study classification and grading of trauma care units and affiliation of other hospitals.
To suggest measures and define criteria for optimum utilization of available resources and infrastructure.
6. Drafting of policy for starting new trauma care units.
