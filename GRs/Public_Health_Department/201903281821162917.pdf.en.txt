Decision of the Government Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCP) for the financial year 2017-18, the State Government has sanctioned 40% of the total funds for the establishment of Tertiary Cancer Care Centre at Nagpur to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Regional Cancer Research Centre, Nagpur. Under this decision, the government is approving the disbursement of a grant of Rs. 84.20 lakh (Rupees Four Eighty Five Lakhs Twenty Thousand only).
The cost of the proposal No. R-12210-Medical Services and Public Health, 31-Supplementary Grants (Non-Payment) to be shared under the National Non-Communicable Diseases Control Programme with the National Programme for the Prevention and Control of Diabetic Cancer and Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (General) (2210F 391) approved for the financial year 2018-19.
The funds will be disbursed to Joint Director, Health Services (Budget and Administration), Mumbai as Controlling Officer for payment to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Regional Cancer Research Centre, Nagpur.
The Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for this grant. They should submit the payment of this amount to the treasury and hand over the amount to the concerned program officers for spending on the prescribed matters.
