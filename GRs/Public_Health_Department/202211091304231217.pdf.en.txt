At present, there is no provision left under the head 2210F999 of the State Accounts of Tribal Applications under the National Ayush Mission. The Department of Tribal Development was requested to make available in the Monsoon Session, through Supplementary Demands, the inadequate provision for State share under the head 2210F999 for the Tribal Sub-Plan of National Ayush Mission. However, the proposal for supplementary demand was rejected as the provision was not spent at that time under the head of State Accounts 2210F999. The Central Government vide Reference No. 2 It is hereby informed that the States have been advised to transfer the entire State share to the SNA account before the release of the first instalment of funds for 2022-23, which has been released till 31.03.2022. As per the instructions of the Central Government, under the National AYUSH Mission, the amount of Rs. 206.93 lakh is required to be provided. Hence, the amount available under the head 2210F863 of State Accounts for tribal applications under National Urban Health Mission is Rs. 902.28 lakh in the State Account Head 2210F999 for tribal applications under National AYUSH Mission. The Government was considering providing Rs. 206.93 lakh through reallocation.
Government's decision:
Under the National Urban Health Mission of the Central Government, out of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23, Rs. 902.28 lakh under State Account Head 2210F999 of Tribal Utilization under National AYUSH Mission. 206.93 lakh is being released to State AYUSH Societies through re-appropriation subject to the following conditions:
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. The amount shall be deposited with the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission Director, National AYUSH Mission, State AYUSH Societies for making the payment to the Treasury and for spending on the prescribed matters.
