The Proposal:
The General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 The Government have issued a Policy on e-Governance for complete computerization of Government activities vide Government Resolution dated 29.10.2001. In Section No. In Section 7, the concerned administrative departments have given guidelines regarding the formation of Project Implementation Committees. Also, reference No. 2 The composition and functions of the Project Implementation Committee have been laid down under the Government Decision dated 23.09.2011. Under this provision, the government was considering the formation of a new Project Implementation Committee for the Public Health Department itself.
Government's decision:
The General Administration Department vide reference No. Computerization of the Public Health Department itself and e-governance taking into account the guidelines/ directions given under the above Government Decisions 1 to 2.
