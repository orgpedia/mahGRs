The proposal has been submitted to the government by the National Health Mission, Mumbai. Accordingly,
The proposal is subject to the following administrative approvals: :
Conditions:
1) The requirement/ demand for availing the said ambulance service from an external source should be ascertained. After that, the tender process will begin.
2) It should be ensured that the specifications for availing the said ambulance service from an external source are as per the requirement and up to date. The technical specifications of the matter should be as per the specifications agreed by the concerned experts (Technical Committee/ Officials). Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not designed in such a way that it will benefit any particular company. The responsibility of sending the requisition as per the technical specifications of the items approved in the PIP will be of the concerned programme head.
The tender process should be carried out only on the items included in the proposal.
(3) The tender process should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the revised rulebook of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by government departments published under the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. Bhankhas-2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated December 1, 2016.
4) The rates for availing this ambulance service through external sources should be fixed after the tender process. It should be ensured that the price is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the supplier has supplied the item to other places.
