As per the provisions of Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the General Administration Department has been directed to review the services of officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service. The consolidated procedure has been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 10.06.2019. As per the provisions of the said Government Resolution, the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services under the Commissionerate of Health Services, the District Surgeon Cadre in Group-A, the District Health Officer Cadre, the Specialist Cadre and above, and the Officers in the General State Service, Group-A (Level S-20: 56100-177500 and above in the Pay Matrix), will be constituted a Divisional Review Committee to try their eligibility for continuation of their service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
The Government have decided to constitute the following" Divisional Review Committee" under the Commissionerate of Health Services, to review the services of the officers working in the cadre of District Surgeon, District Health Officer, Specialist Cadre and above in Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 and above in the Pay Matrix) and General State Service, Group-A (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500 and above in the Pay Matrix) in the age group of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, on the basis of the criteria prescribed for determining their eligibility to continue in service:
