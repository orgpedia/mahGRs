Government Decision: 2. As the additional post of Director, Health Services has been created, now two posts of Director, Health Services-1 and Director, Health Services-2 will be functional at the state level. The headquarters of Director, Health Services-1 will be at Mumbai and that of Director, Health Services-2 will be at Pune.
