Introduction: Various concessions are given by the government to the hospitals of the organization registered under the Mumbai Trust Act. Under the scheme, the hospitals will provide treatment to poor and economically weaker patients.
To check that the expected tests and treatment have been done according to the quality, quality and disease, the above reference no. An inspection committee was set up. Check this
An Assistant Director (Health Services) was appointed as Member Secretary in the committee.
Thereafter, Govt. In view of the convenience of the work of the Inspection Committee, the Assistant Director, Health Services in the jurisdiction of Mumbai and the District Surgeon/ Additional District Surgeon (Medical) in the remaining jurisdiction have been appointed as the Member Secretary of the Inspection Committee.
It was under the consideration of the government to include accounting officers and related experts in the investigation.
Government's decision:
Reference No. The inspection committee is being reconstituted as follows to inspect the charitable hospitals registered under the Mumbai Trust Act by formalizing the government decisions at 1 and 2.
