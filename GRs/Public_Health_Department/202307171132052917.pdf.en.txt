GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MAAP-2023/ PR No. 127/ HEALTH-6, dated 18th July, 2023
The duties and responsibilities of the Committee shall be as follows:
To provide information about the involvement of the concerned hospital in the illegal kidney transplantation of Mrs. Sarika Sutar at Ruby Hall Clinic Pune on 24.03.2022 and to investigate and report on the basis of this information whether any irregularities have taken place.
2) To conduct a thorough investigation of all allegations and submit an objective report.
3) To recommend measures to be taken to prevent recurrence of any irregularity detected during the aforesaid enquiry.
(4) The Committee may call any person to be questioned in connection with the inquiry. It will also recommend action against the concerned and suggest measures to be taken to prevent such incidents in future.
(5) The Inquiry Committee may scrutinize the documents required in the concerned hospital. For this, the concerned hospital and other offices should fully cooperate with the inquiry committee and provide all the documents demanded by the committee.
In exercise of the powers conferred on the Government under the provisions of section 11 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 (60 of 1952), the powers conferred on the Committee under section 4 of the said Act are being made applicable.
All the members of the Committee shall assist the Chairperson in the conduct of the inquiry.
The terms and conditions of the committee are as follows:
The Chairman of the Committee, Mr. Chandrakant Bhadang, retired Judge, Bombay High Court, is being fixed an honorarium of Rs.15 lakh for three months.
The said Inquiry Committee shall conduct its work at Mumbai Headquarters and shall be provided with necessary office space, telephone arrangements, transport arrangements for travel as required in connection with the inquiry (including travel from Mumbai to Pune), staff, English booklets, office and stationery, informative documents, accommodation at Mumbai Headquarters and at the place of visit to be given to the Committee in connection with the inquiry, etc.
(3) The daily allowance, travel allowance and other incidental allowances payable to the members of the Committee shall be admissible as per the rules in force at that time.
4. The Commissioner, Health Services Commissionerate shall make provision for the expenditure of the said Committee from the funds available with it.
