The Proposal:
The State Health Mission has been set up to implement the National Rural Health Mission programme in the State as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The main objective of the scheme is to provide quality health services to the people of the state. The Central Government vide letter dated 2nd March, 2017 has notified the continuation of all Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM) during the year 2016-17. Accordingly, from April 1, 2017, till the time the National Health Mission is implemented by the Central Government, the Public Health Department, Government of Maharashtra will implement the National Health Mission under page 2 of page 5.
