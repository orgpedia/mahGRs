Preamble: A proposal has been received by the Government for grant of administrative approval for construction of residential quarters for officers/ staff at Rural Hospital, Sonepat District Parbhani.
A rough estimate for construction of residential quarters for the officers/ staff of Rural Hospital, Sonepat District Parbhani has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, PWD, Parbhani based on the rate schedule for the year 2020-21 at an amount of Rs. 1417.00 lakh. However, the Superintending Engineer, IDW, after inspection and according to the Revised Recapulation Sheet, the work was done at a cost of Rs. 1285.33 lakh had been submitted to the Government for administrative approval. As proposed by the Superintending Engineer, IDW, the Government was considering administrative sanction for construction of residential quarters for officers/ staff at Rural Hospital, Sonepat, District Parbhani at a total estimated cost of Rs. 1285.33 lakh. The following decisions have been taken by the Government:
Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Rural Hospital, Sonepat District, Parbhani at the cost of Rs. 1285.33 lakh as shown in Annexure A at the residence of the officer/ staff subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1) Before starting the work, the sample map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
2) When working on a loosely held provision, the work should be undertaken by making a detailed budget.
3) In the course of actual work, the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T.C. 1, D.
Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
