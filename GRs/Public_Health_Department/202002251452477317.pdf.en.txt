The proposal to disburse this fund to the State Health Guarantee Society for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented by the State Health Guarantee Society, a conditional amount of Rs. 6,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores only) will be transferred to the account of the State Health Guarantee Society immediately from the provision made in the budget under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2019-20 to pay the insurance premium of the beneficiary family under the scheme and to meet other administrative expenses on the scheme.
It is being handed over to the Controller. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
