Government Resolution No. RCH-2010/ No. 28/ Health-7B, D. 4.10.2010
Preamble- Establishment of a State Level Task Force Committee to investigate all maternal deaths in the State. As per the Government Decision dated 4.10.2010. The government was considering the formation of a Maternal Mortality Monitoring and Response Committee as a sub-committee of this committee. A decision in this regard is being taken.
The government has decided to investigate maternal deaths in the state as per the guidelines of the Maternal Death Surveillance and Response of the Central Government. A State Level Task Force has been constituted under 4.10.2010. The Maternal Mortality Monitoring and Response Committee is being set up to take measures to reduce maternal mortality rate by recording and investigating maternal deaths in the state. The Committee will act as a sub-committee of the State Level Task Force Committee. The committee is expected to meet every three months. Accordingly, the committee will recommend to the government to formulate a strategy to control the maternal mortality rate as per the discussion in the meeting.
The non-official members of the committee will be entitled to travel allowance and daily allowance as per the government's Class 1 officers. The cost should be met from the provision made in the RCH PIP.
