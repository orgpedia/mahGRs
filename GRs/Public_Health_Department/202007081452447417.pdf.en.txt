Department of Finance, Government of India vide Decision No. Finance-2020/ P.S. 65/ EARTH-3 dated 4th May, 2020," Priority Section 11) Department of Public Health, Department of Medical Education and Drugs, Department of Food and Civil Supplies and Department of Relief and Rehabilitation have been identified as priority departments to deal with the situation arising out of Corona Virus. As stated in the said Government Resolution, the Government Resolution is being issued under the authority conferred on the Department.
