The Proposal:
Under the National Urban Health Mission, to provide health services to the needy people in urban areas and remote areas of the state, where people are deprived of health services, densely populated slums of the city, as well as the generally economically weaker areas lacking medical facilities, under the National Urban Health Mission, in the year 2018, a total of 10 mobile medical units have been made operational in Mumbai Municipal Corporation-5, Nagpur-1, Nanded-1, Aurangabad-1, Navi Mumbai/ Panvel-1, Kolhapur-1 through service providers. The mobile medical unit visits the selected places once a month and provides free medical services to the needy and poor people there. The mobile medical unit has a dispensary vehicle with a total of 5 staff members including female medical officer, staff nurse, pharmacist, lab technician, and a driver. This is the service provider's deadline. The matter of approval of tender process for selection of new service provider was under consideration of the Government as it would expire on 31.03.2022.
