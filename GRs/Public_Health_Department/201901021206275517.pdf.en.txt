As per the notification dated 30.06.2018, Hospital Based Authorization Committees have been constituted in 16 hospitals under the Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Act, 1994. Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Department of Public Health or their representatives and Director, Health Services or their representatives have been included as members of the committee. Reference No. 2 The representatives of the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Public Health Department and Director, Health Services have been nominated to attend the meetings of the Human Organ Authority Committee as per the decision of the Government. In this regard, the Director, Health Services, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai, under reference No. 3 The matter of issuing a revised government decision in accordance with the proposal received here was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government decision dated 7.12.2018 regarding nomination of Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Department of Public Health and Director, Health Services as representatives to attend the meetings of the Hospital Based Authorization Committee constituted under the Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Act is being superseded.
