The proposal-
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. During the financial year 2020-21, under the head 2210 G 888 (State share 40%), Rs. 10,50,00, 000/- and by way of Supplementary Demand in September, 2020, Rs. 22.25 crore. A sum of Rs. 32.75 crore has been earmarked for the project. Out of this amount, Rs. 4 crore has been released. A sum of Rs. 8.44 crore has been disbursed to the State Health Guarantee Society. Government's decision
Out of the State share (40%) for Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Rs. Under this Government decision, conditional release of funds amounting to Rs. 7,93,70, 616/- (Rupees Seven Crores Ninety Three Lakhs Seventy Thousand Six Hundred Sixteen only) is being approved.

During the financial year 2020-21, the expenditure under the heads of 2210-Medical Services and Public Health 01-Civil Health Services 789-Odysclerosis, Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, (01) Scheme under Scheduled Castes Component Programme, (01) (04)-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (State Share 40%) (Programme) 2210 G 888, 20, Other Administrative Expenditure, 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) shall be incurred within the limit of the sanctioned outlay.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government.
