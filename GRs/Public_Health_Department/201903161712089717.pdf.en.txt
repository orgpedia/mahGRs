The Proposal:
Under the Ayushman Bharat scheme of the Central Government, Arogya Vardhini Kendras are being set up in the state to provide comprehensive primary health care. In a phased manner, 1828 primary health centres and 10,668 sub-centres will be converted into Arogyavardhini centres. As per the directions of the government, BAMS degree holders have been appointed as community health officers at all Arogyavardhini Kendras and work has been started to strengthen the infrastructure of all health centres. The matter of obtaining administrative approval for the purchase of necessary furniture for the said Arogyavardhini Kendra was under consideration. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per the proposal of the Commissioner (Health Services) and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai, for the purchase of necessary furniture for Arogyavardhini Kendra.
