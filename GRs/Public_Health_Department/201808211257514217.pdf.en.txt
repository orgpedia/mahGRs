Introduction: According to the findings of the World Health Organization (WHO), measles leads to an increase in infant mortality in India. Rubella infection also causes congenital diseases. The Central Government has issued instructions to implement Measles Rubella vaccination campaign in the State to reduce the high infant mortality rate and control congenital disabilities due to Rubella infection. A State Operation Group under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Department of Public Health was being constituted at the Government level to plan and implement the campaign as per the Central guidelines. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision 1. In order to eradicate Measles and Rubella by 2020 from the country, it has been decided to constitute a State Working Group for successful implementation of Measles-Rubella vaccination campaign for children in the age group of 9 months to 15 years in Maharashtra, as in other States.
2. On the implementation of the central government's Measles Rubella campaign in the state of Maharashtra. A State Operation Group is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Public Health Department. Committee members will include:

The functions of the Committee shall be: a) To prepare, monitor and evaluate the implementation of the Mission and to obtain necessary technical support from the stakeholders for the same.
b) To take necessary action to vaccinate against measles rubella.
c) To make full use of the available resources for this campaign in close coordination with the Government and other stakeholders.
d) To obtain the approval of the Government for the activities involved in the campaign.
e) To take planned action for making full use of various publicity and media in collaboration with the Information and Public Relations Department to spread the message of the campaign in simple and easy words to the public.
