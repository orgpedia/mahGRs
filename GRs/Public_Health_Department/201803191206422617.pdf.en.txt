Introduction Sparsh Hospital has been established by Pride India with the objective of providing health services/ facilities to the people of Sastur, G. Osmanabad in the backdrop of the earthquake of 1993. The Government had agreed to provide financial assistance to the hospital under the Government Decision dated 26.06.2000 by entering into an agreement with the institution with the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government.
Subsequently, a new uniform contract has been approved under the Government Resolution No. 2 under the Sixth Pay Commission by increasing the grant to the institution, modifying certain conditions and incorporating certain conditions. Accordingly, a grant of Rs. 53.04 lakhs) or the organization has to pay the salary and allowances and medicines of the employees (Rs. 4 lakh) and Rs. 2 lakh) whichever amount is less, the grant has been approved to the institution. In the Annexure A of this Government Resolution, Clause No. 11 dated 01.01.2018 by the office of the Assistant Director, Deputy Director Health Services, Latur. As per the recommendation of the Deputy Director, Health Services Board, Latur, the Government has received a proposal from the Joint Director to sanction a grant of Rs. 53.04 lakh for the year 2016-17 for the hospital. In this regard, the matter of approving the proposal for grant was under the consideration of the government as the institution was fulfilling the MoU.
Government's decision
