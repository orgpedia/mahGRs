GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the National Tuberculosis Control Programme (NTCP), 1310 posts have been created as per the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, out of which 831 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 831 temporary posts, 80% of the 575 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the decision of the Government in Table 2 above. The remaining 264 temporary posts will be filled as per the Government decision of Reference No. 3. This has been extended up to 31.08.2022. In order to provide continuous health related services to the people of the state, the above 264 posts are being approved to be continued from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
