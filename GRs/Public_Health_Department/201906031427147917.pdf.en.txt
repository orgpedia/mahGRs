The Proposal:
Dr. Madhavi Gopalrao Gavande was appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services cadre for a probation period of two years.
The appointment was made as the first appointment in the government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in 2008.
As per the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the preamble, as indicated in the table given in this paragraph, Dr. Madhavi Gopalrao Gawande" Medical Officer Group-A" of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, having satisfied the requirements for the expiry of his probationary period, his probationary period shall be extended beyond the period indicated in the table before his name in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
