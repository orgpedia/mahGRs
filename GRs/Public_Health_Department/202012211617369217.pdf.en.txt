The administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1. Industries should be purchased through e-tendering as the annual expenditure exceeds Rs. 3.00 lakhs as on 1st December, 2016 as per the decision of the Government of Power and Labour Department.
(2) The requirement/ demand for the said foodgrains and minor commodities should be ascertained and the tender process for the purchase of such items should be carried out.
The approval of the Central Procurement Committee should be obtained before placing the purchase order.
The Department of Industry, Energy and Labor should implement the prescribed procurement procedure in accordance with the provisions of the revised rulebook for office procurement by government departments published under the Government Decision dated December 1, 2016.
The rates of the proposed purchases are estimated at the time of granting administrative approval. Therefore, it should be ensured that the price fixed during the procurement process is less than the market price and does not exceed the rate at which the supplier has supplied the item to other places.
It should be ensured that the items to be purchased are purchased only with proper and proper specifications after conducting sample testing before/ after supply.
7. Storage and distribution of the items to be procured after supply, supply chain management and inventory management should be done in a proper manner to ensure that these items do not remain unused and are used within the stipulated time.
8. It should also be ensured that there is no duplication in the purchase of such goods.
The guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) should be strictly followed while carrying out the entire procurement process.
3. The tender in the said proposal should be purchased on priority from the Federation. However, in case of non-supply from the Federation in time, the tender should be purchased at the rate of tender only for a period of 1 month.
The expenditure in the said proposal shall be covered under the head Demand No.R-1, 2210 Medical Services and Public Health (05) Psychiatric Hospitals, (05) (01) Psychiatric Hospitals (Compulsory), 19 Food Expenditure and shall be covered by the approved provision.
