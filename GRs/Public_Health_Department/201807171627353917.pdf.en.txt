Preamble: 336 beds were sanctioned to the Thane District General Hospital. Since then, five new wards have been opened from time to time and a total of 31 beds have been increased for the implementation of various health-related programs. The hospital has 367 beds. In view of the demand from the local people's representatives regarding the increase in the beds of this hospital and the opening of a super speciality hospital there, the Joint Director, Health Services Hospitals (State Level) had submitted a proposal to the government to increase the beds of Thane District General Hospital and start a Reference Service Hospital there. The proposal had been approved by the government.
A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: Approval is being given to increase 67 beds in the existing 367 beds of Thane District General Hospital and to start a 140-bed Referral Service Hospital (Super Specialty Hospital) in its place as follows.
