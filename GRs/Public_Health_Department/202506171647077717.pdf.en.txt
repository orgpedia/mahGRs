Ma. Sri. Dr. Deepak Ra. Sawant, former Minister (Public Health and Family Welfare)
At No. 2, D. The Task Force has been constituted by a Government Decision dated June 2025. The committee was chaired by Dr. Deepak Ra. Grant of ministerial rank and permissible service facilities to Sawant.
The matter was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
2. In view of the above facts, the problem of malnutrition in the tribal areas of the state has been eradicated.
To do the same, a Task Force (Task Force) headed by Dr.Deepak Ra., Hon'ble Minister of State for Health and Family Welfare was constituted to review and suggest measures to reduce the Infant Mortality Rate (IMR) and Maternal Mortality Rate (MMR) as well as control of communicable diseases.
Sawant has been given the status of a minister since the date of this government decision.
