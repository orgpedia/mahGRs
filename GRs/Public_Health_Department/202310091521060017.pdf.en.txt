The Proposal:
As the contract with United India Insurance, a Public Sector Insurance Company, had expired on 31.03.2023, the said contract was extended till 30.06.2023 vide Government Resolution dated 21.06.2023 in the above Reference No. Also Reference No. The Government has extended the deadline till 30.09.2023. Reference No. A Government Resolution dated 28.07.2023 has been issued to extend the combined scheme of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana. It will take some time for the State Health Guarantee Society to set up the necessary mechanism to implement the scheme on a fully guaranteed basis in accordance with the implementation of this government decision. Therefore, the government was considering extending the contract.
# Page 2
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. NO. 2023/ PR. NO. 23/ HEALTH-6 dated 10th October, 2023
Government's decision:
The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with United India Insurance Company, selected for Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, has been extended with effect from 01.10.2023. The period is being extended up to 31.12.2023. However, if the process of implementing the revised scheme is completed during the extension period, the agreement will be terminated with 60 days' advance notice as per the provisions of the existing agreement.
The terms and conditions of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) dated 27.03.2020 signed between United India Insurance Company and State Health Guarantee Society, Worli, Mumbai and the provisions mentioned under the Government Decision of Reference No. 3 above shall remain in force.
(3) The decision of the Government is in accordance with the decision of the Insurance Company Selection Committee headed by the Additional Chief Secretary, Finance Department. It is being issued with the approval of the Governing Council chaired by the Chief Minister.
