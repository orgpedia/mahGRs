Preamble: Accessible, Affordable, Efficient, Accountable and Reliable Health Care to Rural Population
The Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, it is being decided that the proposal for providing 60% Central share of grant under Tribal Appropriation (TSP) to the State Health Societies for National Tuberculosis Control Programme (RNTCP) and National Leprosy Eradication Programme (NLEP) under National Health Mission (NHM) is under consideration of the Government.
