As per Rule 56 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981, when a government servant is assigned additional charge of another post in addition to his own post, then additional pay/ special pay is sanctioned to him for such other post. The officers have been delegated the sanction of additional pay/ special pay under Roll No.14 of Annexure-I of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981. Accordingly, Department of Finance has issued instructions from time to time regarding the powers to grant additional charge and rates of additional pay/ special pay under various Government decisions.
Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A officers are given additional charge at the zonal level/ directorate level. So there is no ambiguity. Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale as per 6th Pay Commission is Rs. 15600-39100 Grade Pay Rs. 6600) should be given additional charge at the Directorate General of Health Services level. Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale as per 6th Pay Commission Rs. 15600-39100 Grade Pay Rs. 6,600) should be submitted to the Government for grant of additional charge.
As per Rule 56 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, government employees are assigned additional charge of a post other than their own. Such additional charge of the second vacant post should be assigned to the most senior, experienced and efficient officer/ employee of the same cadre in the same administrative department, as far as possible, keeping in view the administrative convenience and urgency. Where such officers/ staff are not available, the senior most officer/ staff of the lower cadre attached to the post concerned should be given additional charge.
The concerned shall ensure that the officer/ employee given additional charge is able to discharge the duties and responsibilities of the post assigned to him along with the duties and responsibilities of his original post.

The officer/ staff undergoing departmental enquiry should not be given additional charge on the ground that the officer/ staff being given additional charge is likely to influence his own departmental enquiry or obstruct the departmental enquiry.
In cases where the additional charge is to be continued for two years, prior approval of the General Administration Department and the Finance Department is required before the expiry of the two-year period. Proposals for post-facto approval will not be considered if the provision of additional workload continues for more than two years without prior approval.
Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale as per 6th Pay Commission Rs. 15600 39100 Grade Pay Rs. 6600) should be submitted to the Government from time to time with details of the officers who have been given additional charge from the Directorate General of Health Services level.
