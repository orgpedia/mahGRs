Considering the role and importance of outreach, the government has decided to provide remuneration to ASHA volunteers and group promoters from the state government's fund. :
1. It is being approved to increase the monthly remuneration of ASHA volunteers from Rs. 2000/- to a total of Rs. 1000/- per month.
The group promoters are being allowed to increase the monthly remuneration from Rs.3000/- to a total of Rs.1200/- per month to be paid from the State Government funds.
3. It is being approved to pay COVID allowance of Rs. 500/- per month to ASHA volunteers and ASHA group promoters from the State Government's fund till the outbreak of Corona pandemic.
In the event of the repeal of the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897 and the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in respect of COVID-19 before 01.07.2022, and the amount payable for the same shall be Rs. 500/- per month, an increase of Rs.500/- per month is being approved for ASHAs and Group Promoters with effect from 01.07.2022.
The proposed increase in the aforesaid Sections 1, 2 and 3 is being approved to be paid from the month of July, 2021.
6. Annual recurring expenditure estimated at Rs. 135.60 crore is being approved.
The scheme will be effective from 01.07.2021 for the financial year 2021-22 and accordingly, the enhanced remuneration to ASHA volunteers and group promoters is being approved with effect from 01.07.2021 under the head 2210H015.
