Accordingly, the following recommendations are made.
1) Nurses are responsible for ensuring that newborns are not stolen / smuggled into nursing homes.
The nursing home from where the newborn is suspected to have been stolen / trafficked.
The registration of nursing homes was done by the competent authority in the Maharashtra Nursing Homes Registration Act, 1949.
Cancellation should be done immediately as per the provision if such an incident has taken place in a government hospital.
The responsibility should be fixed on the concerned.
2) Implementation of the division with a view to rehabilitating the victims of trafficking.
Give priority to the benefits of various upcoming schemes and take care of their mental health.
3) Investigation of crimes against civilians who have been victims of trafficking.
Medical examination of such citizens will be done carefully and the medical examination will be done accordingly.
The report should be made readily available to the police force.
The circular is available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra, www.maharashtra.gov.in.
