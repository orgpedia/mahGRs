Dressing service:
1) Dr. Bandorwala Leprosy Hospital, Kondhwa (BU) Pune-48 is a 350-bed Government Hospital with 180 to 200 in-patients. Soak in hydrogen peroxide in a bleaching solution in the right amount for 30 ml. It is obligatory to keep.
2) After half an hour, mix the soap powder with the borax powder in the right amount and soak in hot water and wash the clothes. After washing clothes, you need to apply a solution to soften the clothes. After that, if the hygiene is not found in the inspection of the supervisor, you will have to wash again.
3) Wash your bed linen, towels, towels, and bedding daily. Bed linen should be washed daily.
Apart from this, hospital curtains, bedside screen cloths, etc. will have to be washed every fifteen days.
5) Red woolen blankets need to be washed twice a month.
6) Every day at 11.30 am, from every hospital, surgery room, maternity home, etc., the clothes should be collected in a laundry bag and its record should be taken and the signature of the concerned head should be taken. And get it signed by the president.
Collect all the blood stained clothes from the surgical department in a separate bag and dip it in 1 to 1.5 percent sodium hypochlorite for 20 minutes. Use gloves while disinfecting blood stained clothes.
8) Wash and dry your clothes in a separate container.
9) Use of soap and washing powder for washing clothes. Dry the washed clothes in the sun only ironing the curtains.
10) If there is no water supply to the hospital due to some technical problems, the contractor should provide water and wash the clothes. No separate fee has to be remitted for this.
