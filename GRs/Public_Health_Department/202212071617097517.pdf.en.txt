Formation of Divisional Promotion Committee for promotion to all Group A posts in the State Workers Insurance Scheme under the Public Health Department.

Read:- General Administration Department, Government Resolution No: SRV 2018/ PR No. 159/ Q. 01.08.2019.
Government Decision: Govt. As per the Government decision dated 01.08.2019, a Departmental Promotion Committee at the level of Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary of the concerned Ministry/ Administrative Department is required to be constituted for promotion to all the posts in the jurisdiction of Establishment Board in the non-Ministerial Group-A cadre.
Accordingly, the Departmental Promotion Committee is being constituted to recommend promotions to all Group-A posts (7th Pay Commission posts having pay scale of S-24 or below) under the State Labour Insurance Scheme as under: :
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary-1, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Mumbai. | the Chairman         |
| 2. | Commissioner (State Workers Insurance Scheme)                                                                                | the members          |
| 3. | Director (Administration), (State Workers Insurance Scheme)                                                                  | the members          |
| 4. | Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Services-1 (Department of Public Health, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Mumbai)   | the Member Secretary |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
There should be one member representing the backward class in the departmental promotion committee. In the absence of such a member, an officer to represent the Backward Classes shall be included as an invited member with the concurrence of the Chairman of the Committee. The Departmental Promotion Committee has been constituted by the General Administration Department. This is being done as per the guidelines laid down in the Government Resolution dated 01.08.2019.
