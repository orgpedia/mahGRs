The Proposal:
30 bedded Rural Hospital Nagbhid, G. Chandrapur has been upgraded to 100 bedded Sub District Hospital with an outlay of Rs. A proposal for administrative approval of budget and maps amounting to Rs. 3178.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget and plan for the construction of 100 bed sub-district hospital building by upgrading the 30-bed rural hospital Nagbhid, G. Chandrapur is based on the rate list of 2021-22. As per the recapitulation sheet attached, the budget had shown a provision of 10 per cent for the price hike. 3178.00 lakhs are being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department concerned.
