Introduction: The public health department of the state provides health facilities to the patients from general hospitals, sub-district hospitals, rural hospitals and state workers insurance hospitals. However, due to the lack of medical officers and senior officials in the hospital, it is difficult to provide proper health facilities to the patients on time. There is a large number of cases where medical officers are not available in adequate numbers despite trying for the post of medical officer and specialist or if they are available, they are not able to join the service if they are not posted at the preferred place. As a result, a large number of posts in the health service remain vacant, which is adversely affecting the patient service in the state. Following this, the government took decision. The Government has decided to enhance the age of superannuation from 58 years to 60 years for the officers of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Medical Officers in Group-A and Medical Officers in Group-A and senior posts under the State Workers Insurance Scheme with effect from 29.08.2018.
