At present, the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A ear, nose and throat specialist category has been given the status of a doctor. The Interim Seniority Notice dated 01.01.2019 is being issued as per the accompanying Return-A. The objections of the officers in accordance with this seniority list should be submitted to the government within 1 month. A copy of the same should be sent to the Director, Health Services, Mumbai. The Director, Health Services should send their clear feedback on all the objections to the government immediately. If no objection is received within 1 month, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that no officer has any objection to this interim seniority list.
