Introduction- The Government has decided to implement the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana in Maharashtra. 08.12.2017. Ministry of Women and Child Development As per the Memorandum dated 24.11.2017, the Central Government has sanctioned an amount of 10% of the total funds of the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana to the State of Maharashtra as a flexible fund to be spent on innovative aspects and for administrative expenses. The government is considering the creation of a new index (CRC) to spend the funds.
Government Decision Under this Government decision, it is being approved to create a new head of account for the Central and State accounts for the Flexi Fund and Administrative Expenditure Fund for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana as follows.
