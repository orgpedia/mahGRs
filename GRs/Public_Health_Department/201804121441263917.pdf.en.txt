The proposal- d. As per the Government decision dated 6.12.2014, the powers to transfer Group B officers/ staff in Directorate of Health Services have been delegated to the Director, Directorate of Health Services and the powers to transfer Group B officers/ staff in the State Employees Insurance Scheme have been delegated to the Commissioner, State Employees Insurance Scheme. However, in the Civil Services Board constituted for the above cadre vide Government Resolution dated 17.4.2017, the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Public Health Department or such other Secretary as may be authorized by him was declared as the Chairman. In order to ensure consistency between the Government Decisions dated 6.12.2014 and 17.4.2017, the Government was considering amending the composition of the Civil Services Board in the Government Decision dated 17.4.2017. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
Government decision The Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-B and General State Services, Group-B are being excluded from the purview of the Civil Services Board constituted under the Government decision dated 17.4.2017.
The Commissioner, Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai and the Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme shall issue separate orders for the formation of Civil Services Boards for both the cadres of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-B as well as General State Services, Group-B.
