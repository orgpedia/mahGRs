The Proposal: -
The Indira Gandhi General Hospital, Ichalkaranji, District Kolhapur, under the Public Health Department, has been upgraded from a 200-bed to a 300-bed hospital. The number of patients admitted to the hospital has increased significantly. As a result, approval was under consideration to set up a Nursing Training Centre (GNM) with an intake capacity of 40 students at Indira Gandhi General Hospital, Ichalkaranji, District Kolhapur as per the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) standards to effectively provide MIT services to patients. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken:
The Government's Decisions:
