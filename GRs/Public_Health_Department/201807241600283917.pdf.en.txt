10th Floor, Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital Building Complex, Near Crawford Market, Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 001.
Date: 25th of July 2018.
Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No.2715/ Q.No.302/ 8, dated 29th February, 2016.
Preamble: The following Medical Officers have been appointed to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years.
These appointments have been made as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the year 2009, with the first appointment in the Government Service to the post of" Medical Officer Group A."
As per the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble to the Government Order, since the Medical Officers Group-A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as shown in the table referred to in this paragraph, have satisfactorily completed the necessary requirements for the expiry of their period of probation, their period of probation shall be as shown in the table before their names in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
