Read: 1) Directorate of Health Services, vide letter No. SANASE/ Cell-3/ TE-6/ ShendurJanaghatReport/ 5725-26/22 dated 25.04.2022.
The Proposal:
30 bedded Rural Hospital, Shendurjanaghat, Rs. 422.73 lakh was under the consideration of the Government for administrative approval of the budget and maps.
Government's decision:
The budget for construction of 30 bedded rural hospital, Shendurjanaghat G. Amravati is based on the tariff schedule of 2020-21. The budget of this work includes provision for fuel gas pipeline, bio-digester, rain water harvesting, solar roof, furniture, electrification, water supply and sewerage, fire prevention, and lift etc.
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.

2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
3) When e-tendering the work, the work should be tendered together without breaking it into pieces.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
5) The price of the proposed work should not be increased unnecessarily according to the situation of the area and the needs of the health institutions.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
The residential building shall be of type 1, type 2, type 3 and type 4. The total area of the residential building is 756.56 sqm. 32448.00/- per sq.m., Type 2 1st Floor Rs. 24960.00 per sq.m. and Type 3 and Type 4 Ground Floor Rs. 37440.00 per sq.
10) It should be ensured that the area of the proposed building is in accordance with the permissible standards of the staff.
The entire responsibility of fulfilling the conditions mentioned in 1 to 10 above will be of the concerned Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Surgeon.
4. Expenditure on construction of 30 bedded Rural Hospital, Shendurjanaghat, Amravati, should be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital expenditure on medical services and public health, 02- Rural Health Services, 04- Public Health, 104- Community Health Centre, (00) (01) Large Constructions (4210 0391), 53- Large Constructions.
The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the government to allocate the amount in the budget for the construction of a 30-bed rural hospital, Shendurjanaghat G.
