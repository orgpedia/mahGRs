Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The central share (60%) of the funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of the scheme is directly credited to the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on state account.
In the financial year 2021-22, cadre-wise funds (for general, SC and ST categories) have been received from the Central Government for implementation of the scheme. In this, reference no. 1 to Rs. 3,46,11, 354/- (Rupees Three Crores Forty Seven Lakhs Eleven Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty Four only) has been received by the State and deposited with ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the State share of Rs. 2,30,74, 236/- (Rupees Two Crore Thirty Lakh Seventy Four Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Six only) was under consideration at the Government level. 40% of the State share of Rs. 2,30,74, 236/- (Rupees Two Crores Thirty Lakhs Seventy Four Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Six only) has been released by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department. As per the Government decision dated 24.03.2022, Government approval has been accorded to disburse the budgetary funds on the distribution system. A decision in this regard was pending with the government.

The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made in the prescribed form and the report of the progress of the scheme in the prescribed form should be sent to the Central and State Government from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. This will be implemented from 2021-2022. The concerned office should ensure that no expenditure is incurred beyond the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana and that only the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Castes are benefited from the provision made available under the Scheduled Castes Component Programme.
