The Proposal:
In pursuance of the above decision of the Government in series 1 to 3, it has been approved to set up various health institutions in rural areas under the Public Health Department. The construction of the hospital has been completed. Also, the posts required for new health institutions such as sub-centres, primary health centres and rural hospitals as per the decision of the government in serial numbers 4 and 5.
The configuration has been set. The government was considering the creation of posts at the location of these hospitals in order to provide patient services to the rural population. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: For a total of 13 new health institutions completed in the state, approval is being given to create regular posts and notional skilled/ notional unskilled manpower and to start the functioning of health institutions as follows.
