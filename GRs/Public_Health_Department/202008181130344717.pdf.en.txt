The administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions: Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department will be required.
2) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
3) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals for the same should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in the respective areas. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director/ Additional Director, Health Services, Family Welfare, Parental Care and School Health, Pune of the Health Department of the respective Health Services Board.
5) The work should be done according to the new model maps by approving the architectural plan according to the maps by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed Architect and District Surgeon/ District Health Officer will be responsible for obtaining the construction permit and occupancy certificate as per the rules of the concerned planning authority as per the requirement. The letter for seeking permission should be signed by the District Surgeon/ District Health Officer.
should be done.
6) When bidding for this work, a single tender should be issued for all the works mentioned in the Recapitulation Sheet together without bidding for each work separately.
7) The Department of the Environment, while carrying out the actual work, should take action according to the guidelines mentioned in the Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No. 177/ T.K. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
8) The expenditure should be met from the fund to be sanctioned by the 13th Finance Commission to reduce infant mortality.
The fulfillment of the conditions in the above sequence 1 to 8 and the purchase is as per the rules and requirements.
