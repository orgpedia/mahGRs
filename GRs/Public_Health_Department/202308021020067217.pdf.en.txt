The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2023-24 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. The budget has allocated Rs 70 crore for this. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for payment of insurance premium for the period April, 2023 to June, 2023 and for the quarter July, 2023 to September, 2023 to the insurer. The proposal to disburse conditional funds to the tune of Rs. 24,95,20, 458/- to the State Health Guarantee Society was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the premium for the beneficiary family under the scheme for the period April, 2023 to June, 2023 and for the period July, 2023 to September, 2023 will be Rs. 24,95,20, 458/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Eight only) to the State Health Guarantee Society.

2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.
The fund will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society, Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. 24,95,20, 458/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Eight only) to the State Health Guarantee Society.
