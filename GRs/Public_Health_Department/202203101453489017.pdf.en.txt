The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2021-22 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. 80 crore has been earmarked in the budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.01.2022 to 31.03.2022 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, an amount of Rs. 24,48,79, 452/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Forty Eight Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Two only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
