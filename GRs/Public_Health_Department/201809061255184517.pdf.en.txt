Introduction: With the approval of the rural hospital in Shevgaon G. Ahmednagar and the start of the municipal council in Mauje Shevgaon, the primary health center in that place is Mauje Ghotan. The matter of shifting Shevgaon to Ahmednagar was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government's decision:
2. Mauje Shevgaon Ta. With the approval of the rural hospital in Shevgaon G. Ahmednagar and the start of the municipal council in Mauje Shevgaon, the primary health center in that place is Mauje Ghotan. The shifting of Shevgaon to Ahmednagar is being approved subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the shifted place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
