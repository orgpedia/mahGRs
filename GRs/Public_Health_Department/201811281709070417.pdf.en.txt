the prelude
As the daily sanitation service in the Regional Psychiatric Hospital Thane, Pune and Ratnagiri is required till the appointment of a new contractor after the expiry of the period of sanitation service there as per the terms and conditions of the tender, it is being decided that the extension of time for the cleaning service by the previous contractor and the approval of the expenditure incurred on the sanitation service in accordance with the proposal received under the above letter from the Additional Director (Programmes implemented by the Health Institute) Health Services, Mumbai for payment of the sanitation service was under the consideration of the Government.
