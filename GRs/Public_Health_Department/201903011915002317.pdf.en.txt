Government's decision:
Out of 20166 temporary posts under National Malaria Eradication Programme, 16133 temporary posts have been made permanent as per Government decision dated 30/4/2012. The remaining 4033 temporary posts as well as the revised figure left out of the review of the posts will be filled by the Public Health Department Government Decision No. A total of 4164 posts comprising 131 posts at Sub-Centre level sanctioned under PO No.117/ Service-1, 2007/ PR No.117/ Service-1, dated 21/9/2012 were filled up from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 under the aforesaid Act. No. 3 was continued as per the decision of the government. The deadline expired on 28.02.2019. Since these posts are necessary for the continuous provision of health services to the people of the state, the continuation of these posts from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 is being approved under this government decision. The extension is being given on the condition that a proposal will be submitted to the government immediately to improve the figure of these posts.
