Read: 1) Director, Health Services, Mumbai's letter No. Sanase/ Maa/ Te-1/ Pramru Thana/ Cell Repair Proposal/ 5208-10/2021 dated 04.03.2021.
2) Letter from Joint Director (Non-Communicable Diseases) Health Services, Mumbai No. Sanase/ M.A./ Te1/ Pramru Thane/ Building Repair/ 8091-92/21, dated 17th November, 2021 Preamble: All the buildings of Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane are dilapidated and dilapidated. The Public Works Department has carried out a structural inspection of the damaged buildings at the Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane. As per the letter dated 04.03.2021 in the above context, the Structural Repair of the damaged buildings as per the Structural Inspection Report of the said building at Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane has been submitted. The estimates and plans have been prepared by the Assistant Engineer, Grade 1, Public Works Sub-Division No. 1, Thane, and certified by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Division 1, Thane, the Superintending Engineer, Thane and the Medical Superintendent, Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane. It is also certified by the Chief Engineer, Regional Department Konkan.
Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the work was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: Administrative approval is being given to the total amount of Rs. 14,34,75, 859/- for the repair of the men and women's room building of Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane as follows.
