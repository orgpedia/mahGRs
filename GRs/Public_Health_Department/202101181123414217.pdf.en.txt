The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State on 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State from the financial year 2017-2018.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Government's decision:

Under this decision, the government is approving to disburse a subsidy of Rs. 6,10,03, 454/- (Rupees Six Crore Ten Lakh Three Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Four only).
Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should take action to deposit the payment of the grant made available under this government decision in the treasury, obtain the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
It should be certified that the subsidy disbursed under this government decision will be credited directly to the beneficiary's account after being deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
