Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society has been formed for the State Workers Insurance Scheme by the Government Decision dated May 5, 2018 of the Public Health Department to ensure that the money coming from the State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi is deposited directly into the society and important decisions are taken at a fast pace and medical facilities are provided to the insurance beneficiaries more easily and effectively. The structure of the Governing Body of the said Society has been decided by the Governing Decision dated 11th January 2019 of the Public Health Department.
Chairman, State Employees Insurance Corporation, New Delhi. The Minister of State for Labour& Employment, Government of India has nominated three members each as representatives of employers and workers for the post of members of the Governing Body of Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society, informed Additional Commissioner (P& D), State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi vide their letter dated 3rd July 2020. Accordingly, the government was considering appointing three employers' representatives and three workers' representatives on the said society.
