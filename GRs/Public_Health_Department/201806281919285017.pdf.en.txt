The Proposal:
For the financial year 2018-19, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, an amount of Rs. 341,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred and Forty One Crores Fifty Lakhs only). A proposal to disburse funds to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) for payment to the insurance company for the beneficiaries of the scheme, as well as for salary and administrative expenses was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, an amount of Rs. 239,05,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Forty Nine Crores Five Lakhs only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
