GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the National Tuberculosis Control Programme (NTCP), 1310 posts have been created as per the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, out of which 831 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 831 temporary posts, 80% of the 575 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the decision of the Government in Table 2 above. The remaining 264 temporary posts will be filled up as per the Government Resolution No. 3. The period was extended till 28.02.2023. In order to provide continuous health related services to the people of the state, the above 264 posts are being approved to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
The expenditure of these posts should be spent under the head of accounts shown against the posts in the statement attached with the Government decision and should be met from the approved grants for the year 2023-24.
