Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
The respective program head is fully responsible for fulfilling the conditions mentioned herein 1 to 8 above.
He will also be the Commissioner of Health Services, Mumbai.
Expenditure on construction of main building of 100 bedded Women's Hospital, Wardha will be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services-Odditional Care, 110- Hospitals and Clinics, (01)- Buildings, (01) (04) Large Constructions (Loans from HUDCO) (Programme) (42103202), 53- Large Constructions. Also, the Director, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the government to allocate the amount required for the work in the budget.
