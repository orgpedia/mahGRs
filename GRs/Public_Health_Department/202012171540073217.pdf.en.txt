3) Follow the rules of procedure and other general instructions.

5) The above instructions should be strictly followed by all concerned, otherwise disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned by fixing responsibility on them.
6) The cost of the project should be reasonable.
2. The expenditure is to be borne by the Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India on account of Main Account Head" Demand No.R-1, Main Account Head" 2210-Medical Services and Public Health, 01-Civil Health Services and Subject Medicine for the financial year 2020-21.
