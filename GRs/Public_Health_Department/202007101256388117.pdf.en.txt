The Proposal:
Dr. Prabha Sharangdhar Sakhare has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services cadre for a period of two years. This appointment is the first in the Government service as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in 1998 to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A."
2. Under the decision of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probation period of government service officers/ employees and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of termination of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, as indicated in the table given in this paragraph, Dr. Prabha Sharangdhar Sakhare, Medical Officer Group-A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, has satisfactorily completed the necessary requirements to complete his probationary period. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
