As per the Recapitulation Sheet, administrative approval is being granted under this Government Decision subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1) Technical approval of the competent authority of the Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) Before starting the construction, the map should be approved by the architect, S.B. Department Nashik.
(3) A single tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned in the tender without separate tenders for each work.
1) As the cost of the proposed work can increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the regional officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said Budget is a rough model Budget, the detailed estimates of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas. While granting such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Directors of the respective Health Service Boards of the Health Department.
3) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
5) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
6) Ensure that the above tasks are completed within this amount.
7) The concerned executive engineer should ensure that there is no duplication of work while doing this work.
