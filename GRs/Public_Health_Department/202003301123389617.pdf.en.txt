A sum of Rs. 717.57 crore has been allocated for the project. Out of this amount, Rs. 371.10 crore has been disbursed to State Health Guarantee Societies. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) for the period from 01.04.2020 to 30.06.2020 for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.04.2020 to 30.06.2020 and for meeting other administrative expenses on the scheme.
Government's decision:
As the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, a conditional amount of Rs. 245,11,26, 045/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Forty Five Crores Eleven Lakhs Twenty Six Thousand and Forty Five only) is being transferred to the account of the State Health Guarantee Society for the financial year 2019-20, to meet the insurance premium of the beneficiary family for the period from 01.04.2020 to 30.06.2020 and to meet other administrative expenses on the scheme. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
