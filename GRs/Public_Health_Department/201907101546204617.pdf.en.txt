The Proposal:
A proposal has been submitted to give administrative approval to the budget for renovation, new toilets and fencing of Ward No. 1 of Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Nagpur. The detailed budget of these works was submitted to the Assistant Engineer (Class-I), Integrated Component Department No. 1 (Medical), prepared by Public Works Department, Nagpur and attested by Executive Engineer, Integrated Component Department (Medical), Public Works Department, Nagpur and Medical Superintendent, Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Nagpur. The matter of granting administrative approval to the said budget was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Administrative approval is being accorded to the renovation of Ward No. 1, Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Nagpur at a cost of Rs. 76,44,000/-lakh for construction of new toilets and fencing as under:
