Government Decision: To handle the office and financial affairs of the Upazila Hospital Kalamnuri, District Hingoli Hospital, the Government Decision of the Finance Department No. VYPR-2013/ P.S. Further, in the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Rule No. As per provision of section 156, Medical Superintendent, Class-I, is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
