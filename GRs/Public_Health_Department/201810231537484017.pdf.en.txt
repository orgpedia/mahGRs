Government's decision:
Out of 20166 temporary posts under National Malaria Eradication Programme, 16133 temporary posts have been made permanent as per Government decision dated 30/4/2012. The remaining 4033 temporary posts as well as the revised figure left out of the review of the posts will be filled by the Public Health Department Government Decision No. A total of 4164 posts including 131 posts at Sub-Centre level sanctioned under PO No.117/ Service-1, 2007/ PR No.117/ Service-1, dated 21/9/2012 were filled up from 1/03/2018 to 30/09/2018 under the aforesaid Act. No. 3 was continued as per the decision of the government. The deadline It has expired on 30/09/2018. Since these posts are necessary for the continuous provision of health services to the people of the state, the continuation of these posts from 1/10/2018 to 28/02/2019 is being approved under this Government decision.
