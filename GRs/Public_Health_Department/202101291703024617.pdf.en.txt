The Proposal:
Rural Hospital, Rs. The proposal for administrative approval of budget and maps amounting to Rs. 379.35 lakh was under consideration of the Government. The decision is as follows:
Government's decision:
2. The Budget for construction of Staff Quarters at Rural Hospital, Sakharkherda, G. Buldhana is based on the Rate List of 2017-18. As per the Recapitulation Sheet attached to the Budget Estimates for the works amounting to Rs. 379.25 lakhs, 5% for Internal Electrification, 6% for External Electrification and 5% for Water Supply and Sewerage are being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.

2) The detailed estimate of the work should be prepared and approved by the Chief Engineer. Further action will be taken after the submission of the e-tender.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
5) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
9) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on construction of staff quarters at Rural Hospital, Sugarkheda, G. Buldhana should be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health, 02- Rural Health Services, 04- Public Health, 104- Community Health Centre, (00) (01) Large Constructions (42100391), 53- Large Constructions.
4. The Director, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of staff quarters at Rural Hospital, Sakharkhera, G. Buldhana.
