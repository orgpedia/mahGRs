Preamble: Under the chairmanship of Mr. Sudhir Thakare, former Chairman, Maharashtra Public Service Commission, a review meeting was held to suggest measures to make the system more efficient by changing the structure of the Public Health Department. The Expert Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 17.07.2018. The Expert Committee will suggest measures to strengthen health services and suggest improvements in the Directorate Health Services profile, especially at the field level/ block level, to enhance the efficiency of the Health Department and provide quality health services to the people. All the members of the committee have been appointed for a period of 6 months and the committee has been asked to hold meetings from time to time and submit the report to the government within 3 months. As the work of the expert committee is in progress and it will take some more time to complete the work of the committee, the matter of extending the work of the committee was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision- To suggest measures to make the system more efficient by changing the structure of the health department. The proceedings of the Expert Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 17.07.2018. The period is being extended up to 30.04.2019. Further, the expenditure for the functioning of the Committee should be met from the provision available under the head Management Cost under the State Health System Resource Centre (SHSRC).
