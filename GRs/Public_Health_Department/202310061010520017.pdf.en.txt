GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. APPLICATION-2023/ PR. No. 33 (4)/ BUDGET 2. The above funds/ grants made available by the Finance Department on BEAMS system during the financial year 2023-2024 are being released subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. The funds cannot be spent for purposes other than the purpose for which the funds/ grants have been sanctioned. Also, you can't switch to other titles.
2) The progress report of the said expenditure should be submitted to the government by the 10th of every month by the controlling officers as per the monthly fund statement.
3) While paying the subsidy, the rules mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget rule book should be followed. Further, the Finance Department shall pay the subsidy after obtaining the approval of the competent authority in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, as provided under Government Decision No. V.P.-2013/ PR. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015.
(4) When providing grants to private NGOs, the grant should be paid with the approval of the competent authority in accordance with the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-1, Sub-Division I, serial number 27, rule 149.
When disbursing funds for which an administrative sanction order is required, the grant shall be made after the approval of the competent authority as provided under Rule 27 (2) of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-I, Sub-section-III, Schedule-IV, Rule 27.
6) Before sanctioning the grants-in-aid to various Government/ Administrative Institutions and Aided Institutions, the concerned Controlling Officer should submit the utilization certificates of the expenditure of the grants-in-aid disbursed earlier by them to the Department. No further grant shall be released until the utilization of the grant given earlier is completed. The Treasury authorities shall not pass on the payment if a copy of the previous month's bank account statement of the institution concerned is not attached with the payment submitted for drawing funds from the grant.
7) The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and in offline mode.
8) In the case of expenditure on payment of dues, the funds shall not be withdrawn unless it is satisfied that the material purchased has been physically received and its accounts maintained properly.
9) In addition to the above terms and conditions, the provisions of the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book and all terms/ conditions must be followed while making the expenditure.
3. The said Government decision is in accordance with Rule 17 and Rule 142 of the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book and Sub-section-I of the Financial Powers Rule Book, 1978, Rule No. 149 and the Finance Department.
