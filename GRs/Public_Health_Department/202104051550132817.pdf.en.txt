The Proposal:
To extend the coverage of the scheme to all citizens for a limited period so that COVID-19 patients do not have to bear the financial burden of getting treatment in an authorized private hospital and to provide health assurance and financial relief to all citizens during the COVID-19 pandemic.
Benefit of treatment under Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana should be available to the beneficiary patients as well as other patients and the treatment methods reserved for government hospitals should be available.

Due to the expiry, the matter of extension from February 1, 2021 to March 31, 2021 was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The number of Covid-19 cases in Maharashtra is increasing at an alarming rate.
In view of the benefits being provided to the citizens of the state under the Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, all citizens should get health assurance and financial relief during the current pandemic crisis
For this purpose, the scheme which came into force vide Government Resolution dated 23rd May, 2020 to provide benefits to all citizens in the empanelled hospitals under Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana has been extended to all the hospitals in the State.
The extension is for a period of two months from February, 2021 to March 31, 2021.
