Preamble: Under the National Blindness Control Programme, a revised pattern of posts has been fixed for Directorate of Health Services (Headquarters, Mumbai, Programme Head wise and Regional Offices) under the Public Health Department as per the decision of the Government in Article No. 1.
Under the National Blindness Control Programme, 3 temporary posts have been notified at No. 2 above. Decision of the Government dated 11 October 2021. 01.09.2021 to dt. It was approved to continue till 28.02.2022. In view of the nature, need and importance of the work of these 3 temporary posts, these posts have been given. The matter of extension from 01.03.2022 to 30.08.2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the National Blindness Control Programme, the following 3 temporary working posts have been created as they are continuously providing health related services to the people of the state. The continuation from 01.03.2022 to 30.08.2022 is hereby approved. The period is being extended subject to the condition that the revised figure is approved.
