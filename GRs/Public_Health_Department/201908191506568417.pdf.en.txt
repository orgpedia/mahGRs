GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- In pursuance of the Government Decision at Reference No. 1, one post in the cadre of Joint Director, Health Services in the Directorate of Health Services under the Department of Public Health has been upgraded to the rank of Director, Health Services. With the creation of the additional post of Director Health Services, two posts have been created at the state level- Director Health Services-1 and Director Health Services-2. The Director, Health Services (1) is headquartered at Mumbai and the Director, Health Services (2) is headquartered at Pune. Division of work of these posts Reference No. 2 decision has been made.
Reference No. Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has recommended Dr. (Mrs.) Sadhana Sharadchandra Tidke, (OBC) for the post of Director, Health Services (1) Mumbai in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-29: 131100-216600 in the Pay Matrix) from open category. Accordingly, Dr. (Mrs.) Sadhana Sharadchandra Tidke (Tayde) is being appointed as Director, Health Services (1) Mumbai subject to the following terms and conditions by nomination.
The period of probation shall be two years from the date of assumption of charge of the post of Director, Health Services, Group-A, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services. If during the probationary period they do not attain the expected status or the conduct is found to be improper, of the expected quality or unsatisfactory, their service may be terminated or their probationary period shall be extended for such period as may be necessary. If his work is not satisfactory during such extended period, his service will be terminated without any prior notice. Also, one increment will be admissible after completion of one year of probation period. Further increments will be admissible after satisfactory completion of the probation period.
(Mrs.) Sadhana Sharadchandra Tidke should have registered her name under the Maharashtra Medical Professionals Act, 1961 or the Maharashtra Medical Council Act, 1965 before this appointment or should have been registered before the appointment in the Medical Registry of India.
