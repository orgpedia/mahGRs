Preamble: The Government was considering granting administrative approval for operating additional 60 BLS type ambulances (Opex model/ on lease basis) under National Health Mission from the subsidy available under Supplementary PIP sanctioned during the financial year 2019-20 through tender process from external service provider.
Government Decision: Considering the terms and conditions mentioned in the Supplementary PIP (2019-20) of the Central Government, the proposed scheme is to allow 60 BLS type ambulances to be operated by an external agency on the lines of Opex Model as per AIS Norm. Under this scheme, all the ambulances will be owned by the service provider. These ambulances will be equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, medicines and trained doctors and staff. The government had earmarked Rs. Rs 1.25 lakh.
