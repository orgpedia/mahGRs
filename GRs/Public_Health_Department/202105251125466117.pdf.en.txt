The promotion to the posts in the order of promotion is being made only on temporary basis to the posts vacant as per the joining of service subject to the following conditions: 1. The Special Leave Petition in the Supreme Court is being granted subject to the final judgment dated 28306/2017.
(2) Since the said appointment is purely temporary in nature, the above promoted officers shall not be entitled to any regularisation, seniority in service and incidental matters.
(3) The said temporary promotion is being made subject to the acceptance that there is no scope for any departmental enquiry against these officers.
4) The temporary promotion is being given without affecting the seniority of the officers who are senior but whose cases have been kept open for consideration.
5) The said temporary promotion is being given subject to the decision to be reviewed after the age of 50/55 years of these officers.
These officers will have to accept the promotion within seven days from the date of receipt of this order, failing which their promotion will be cancelled without any prior notice. These officers should be promoted immediately. If they take any leave, it will be considered as unauthorised absence and there is a possibility of disruption in their service. The officers of this
