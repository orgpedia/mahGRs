Government Decision- The Government is approving to sanction an amount of Rs. 15,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores only) as grant-in-aid to Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital.
The expenditure should be met from the provision made on BDS for the year 2022-23 under the head Demand No.R-1, 2210-Medical Services and Public Health, 01-Civil Health Services-Odysmal Treatment, 110-Hospital and Dispensaries, (03) Subsidiary Grants to Mufassal Hospitals and Dispensaries, (03) (01)-Subsidiary Grants to Non-Teaching Hospitals and Dispensaries, 31 Subsidiary Grants, (Mandatory) (22100173).
3. Deputy Director, Health Services, Mumbai Circle, Thane is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of grant to Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital, Parel, Mumbai.
The Director of Health Services, Mumbai shall examine the terms and conditions applicable to Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital (e.g. providing free or low-cost services to poor patients).
Whenever the accounts of Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital are required by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai for inspection, the same shall be submitted by the institution along with the documents furnished.
The amount received as government grant is to be kept separate from other routine activities by Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital, Parel, Mumbai.
