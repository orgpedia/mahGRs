State-level committees:
To advise on the scope of existing zoonotic diseases in the State and to list the zoonotic diseases/ infections according to their priority and importance in different categories such as bacterial, viral, fungal, parasitic and to assist the researchers in solving the problems on priority.
2. To identify areas of coordination and modus operandi in various departments such as medical, veterinary and allied departments/ institutions.
3. Review the animal diseases prevalent in the State and accordingly, the concerned State level officials to take up specific measures for prevention and control of the disease.
