The Proposal:
The Transplantation of Human Organs Act, 1994 and the Transplantation of Human Organs (Amendment) Act, 2011 and the Transplantation of Human Organs Rules, 2014 provide for the constitution of a State Advisory Committee to provide necessary assistance and advice to the appropriate authority for effective implementation of the said Act in each State. Accordingly, the No. The two-year term of the State Advisory Committee constituted vide Government Resolution dated 17.12.2017 has expired on 16.12.2019. In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Assistant Director (MAP), Health Services, Mumbai under Reference 2 and vide Reference No. 3 The matter of reconstitution of the State Advisory Committee was under the consideration of the Government in accordance with the comments of the Law and Justice Department here.
Government's decision:
The State Advisory Committee is being reconstituted for a period of two years from the date of this Government decision under Section 13 (a) of the Transplantation of Human Organs Act, 1994 and the Transplantation of Human Organs (Amendment) Act, 2011 and for providing assistance and advice to the appropriate authority under the Transplantation of Human Organs Rules, 2014 as follows:
