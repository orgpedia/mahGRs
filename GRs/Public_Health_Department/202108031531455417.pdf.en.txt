The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2021-22 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
Rs 50 crore has been set aside for this in the budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.07.2021 to 30.09.2021 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the provision made in the budget under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2021-22 for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiary family under the scheme is Rs. 25,03,21, 217/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Three Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred and Seventeen only) to be transferred to the account of State Health Guarantee Society immediately by the Controller.
The officer is being questioned. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
