The Proposal:
Rural Hospital, Mulher G. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps of the work amounting to Rs. 31.71 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget for the construction of the main building and residence of Rural Hospital, Mulher G. Nashik under the backlog is based on the rate schedule for the year 2021-22. The said budget has been prepared by the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Sub-Division, Satna. The budget includes the construction of the hospital (5752.98 sq.m.) and the rate of construction is Rs. 23000/- per sq.m. The hospital includes construction of main building and 25 Government Type 2 (G+ 3) and Type 3 (G+ 2) quarters. Provision has been made for fuel gas pipeline, bio-digester, rain water harvesting, solar roof, protection wall, and gate, electrification, water supply and sewerage, fire prevention, internal roads, land development, drainage, parking and ventilation system, etc. Administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution for the projects with a budget of Rs. 22.02 crore subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) Technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the works.

2) Before starting the construction, the map should be approved by the architect, S.B. Department Nashik.
(3) A single tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned in the tender without separate tenders for each work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
(5) As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director of Health Services Board in the Department of Health.
6) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) Ensure that the above tasks are completed within this amount.
The expenditure on construction of main building and residence at Rural Hospital, Mulher G. Nashik under the backlog will be covered under Demand No. H-9, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital expenditure on medical services and public health, 02- Rural Health Services, 04- Public Health, 200- Other programmes schemes under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major constructions (4210 0141), 53- Major constructions.
The Director of Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of the main building and residence of the Rural Hospital, Mulher G. Nashik under the backlog.
