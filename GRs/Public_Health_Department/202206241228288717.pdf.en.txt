Preamble: Regarding shifting of sanctioned sub-centres under the Public Health Department of the State due to unavoidable reasons, the aforesaid Reference No. The decision was taken by the government.
Accordingly, the Commissioner Health Services, Mumbai has proposed to shift some of the sub-centres in Nashik district to other places as they have come under the jurisdiction of the Municipal Corporation.
Accordingly, the matter of relocating these sub-centres was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. As the following Sub-Centres of Nashik District fall within the jurisdiction of the Municipal Corporation, the following Sub-Centres are hereby approved to be transferred:

Terms and Conditions:
1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the sub-center can be operational in the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building and staff accommodation where the sub-station can function at the place of relocation will be required. So that in this place there should be separate arrangement for the examination of female patients, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for the sitting of staff.
(3) The services of the staff of the said Sub-Centre shall be made available to other Sub-Centres till the Sub-Centre is started on a temporary basis.
4) 24 hours water and light arrangements, sanitation, etc. facilities should be available.
5) The District Planning and Development Board is required to make provision for the construction of this sub-centre from its District Plan.
