Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambejogai has increased the intake capacity of undergraduate medical students from 50 to 100. The proposal of the Director, Directorate of Health Services, to grant permission for temporary use of the sub-district hospital, Kej, G. Beed with six rooms, a lecture hall, a sanitarium, under the administrative control of the Dean, Swami Ramanand Teerth, Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambejogai, to meet the standards of the Indian Council of Medical Sciences, was under consideration of the Government. The following decisions have been taken:
Government Decision: The District Surgeon, District Hospital, Beed has increased the intake capacity of undergraduate medical students of Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambejogai for the next 3 years with six rooms, a lecture hall, and a sanitarium at Sub District Hospital, Kej, G. Beed to meet the standards of the Indian Council of Medical Sciences.
