The Proposal:
The Central Government vide its letter dated 01.11.2021 has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission." The importance of primary health care, strengthening public health institutions to deliver comprehensive primary health care, including health education and prevention, strengthening institutions of public health governance, as well as critical care services, including comprehensive diagnostic and treatment capacity, is a prerequisite for implementing the above programs to address the challenges posed by current and future pandemics/ pandemics. For this, the Union Government has approved the Mission for the period 2021-22 to 2025-26 to ensure delivery of health services by rural and urban local bodies. The government has also allocated Rs. 2021-22 crore for the purpose.
