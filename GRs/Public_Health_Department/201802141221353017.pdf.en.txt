GOVERNMENT DECISIONS As per the decision of the Government vide No. 1 above, it has been approved to upgrade the Dental Department functioning under the Public Health Department in the State. Accordingly, a total of 772 posts of Dental Surgeon, Group-A, Class-2 (1 post) and Dental Assistant, Group-D (1 post) have been created for 386 sub-district hospitals of the department with 30 beds. In Rural Hospital, Hingna, G.Nagpur, 2 posts have been sanctioned and out of these 2 posts, 1 post of Dental Surgeon, Group-A has been filled.
2. Now, as per the decision of the Government, 1 post of Dental Surgeon, Group-A, Rural Hospital, Hingna, G.Nagpur, under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Nagpur Circle, Nagpur, will be filled up. Effective from 04.10.2017. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2018.
