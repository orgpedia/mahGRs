The Proposal:
Out of 20166 temporary posts under National Malaria Elimination Programme, 16133 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the Government decision dated 30.04.2012. Remaining 4033 temporary posts and revised figure left out from the review of posts A total of 4164 posts including 131 posts at Sub-Centre level sanctioned under Public Health Department Reference Government Decision dated 21.09.2012 were continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 under the Government Decision dated 27.04.2023. The deadline It has expired on 31.08.2023. Since these posts are necessary for the continuous provision of health services to the people of the state, a total of 4164 posts have been given. 01.09.2023 to dt. The matter was under consideration of the Government to continue till 29.02.2024.
