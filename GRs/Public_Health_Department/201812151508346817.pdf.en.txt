The Proposal:
The proposal for grant of administrative approval for construction of 30 bedded Ayush Hospital under National Ayush Mission Programme has been submitted by the Commissioner, Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai to the Government. The proposal has been approved by the Union Government under the State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) 2017-18. Hence, the proposal was pending with the government.
Government's decision:
As per the proposal received from the National Health Mission Office, the Central Government has approved the construction of 30 bedded Ayush Hospital in four districts of Pune, Nandurbar, Ahmednagar and Sindhudurg under the State Annual Action Plan for the year 2017-18 under the National Ayush Mission.
