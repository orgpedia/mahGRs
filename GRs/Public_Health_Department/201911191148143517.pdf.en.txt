Preamble: Under the chairmanship of Mr. Sudhir Thakare, former Chairman, Maharashtra Public Service Commission, a review meeting was held to suggest measures to make the system more efficient by changing the structure of the Public Health Department. The Expert Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 17.07.2018. The Expert Committee will suggest measures to strengthen health services and suggest improvements in the Directorate Health Services profile, especially at the field level/ block level, to enhance the efficiency of the Health Department and provide quality health services to the people. All the members of the committee have been appointed for a period of 6 months and the committee has been asked to hold meetings from time to time and submit the report to the government within 3 months. He also chaired the expert committee. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 05.08.2019. The period was extended till 30.04.2019. However, the expert committee has so far held meetings from time to time and deliberated on it. The work of the committee is in progress and it will take some more time to complete the work.
