GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SAMARK 2023/ PR No. 63/ HEALTH 3
Government Decision: To provide free food service to malnourished children, sick children and women during delivery and post-delivery patients and their 2 children/ relatives living in the Melghat area during the entire period of ANC/ LABOUT/ PNC to the mother and her other two children/ relatives if the pregnant mother comes for delivery or is admitted in the government hospital of the department. Administrative Sanction is being granted for an amount not exceeding Rs. 1,79,53, 547/- subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The concerned officer shall be responsible for strict compliance of the instructions/ government decisions/ circulars/ rules issued by the Finance Department from time to time while clearing the pending payments.
2) The food service should be provided by the supplier who is providing the food service earlier.
(3) The officer concerned shall be responsible for strictly complying with the terms and conditions of the work order and contract issued earlier.
The expenditure should be met from Demand Number R-1, 2210 Medical Services and Public Health 22100146, 22100226 and 2210 0413 (13- Office Expenditure) and Tribal Development Department Account Head 2225E 356 (19- Food Expenditure).
5) In addition to the above terms and conditions, the concerned officers will be responsible for complying with the provisions of the State Government's Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions while providing food services.
The said Government Decision No.VIPR-2013/ P.S. No. 30/2013 Regulations, Part-II D. The Annexure to the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 15th April, 2015, in Part-I, Sub-Division-I, No. 6, Rule No. 40 is being issued subject to the authority conferred on the administrative department and subject to the condition of fulfilling the conditions mentioned therein.
