The Proposal:
The Medical Officer has been appointed for a period of two years in the cadre of Medical and Health Services, Maharashtra. The appointment is the first in the Government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Independent Selection Board in 2016.
2. Under the decision of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probation period of government service officers/ employees and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of termination of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Medical Officers Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, as shown in the Table referred to in this paragraph, have satisfactorily fulfilled the requirements for the expiry of their period of probation, and their period of probation shall be as indicated in the Table before their names in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
