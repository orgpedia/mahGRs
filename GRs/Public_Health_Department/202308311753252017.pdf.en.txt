Preamble: In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Plexipule, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to make available 40% State share of grant to State Health Societies from the budgetary provision of 2023-24 for tribal deployment for National Rural Health Programme under National Health Mission. The details of grants disbursed so far in this case are as follows.
