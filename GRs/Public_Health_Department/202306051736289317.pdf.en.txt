The proposal for construction of Trauma Care Centre at Rural Hospital Vashi G. Osmanabad was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Construction of Trauma Care Centre at Rural Hospital Vashi G. Osmanabad, as shown in Annexure A, with an outlay of Rs. 1150.64 lakh is being provided subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Before starting the work, the sample map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
2) When working on a loosely held provision, the work should be undertaken by making a detailed budget.
3) In the course of actual work, the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T.C. 1, D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
4) The cost of construction of this work should be borne by the Public Health Department from the budget available with them.
5) Public Works Department Government Decision No. BDG-2017/ No. 60/ Ima-2, D. Action to be taken as on 07.07.2017.
6) The instructions regarding the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the place is in the possession of the department.
The proposed building is a rural hospital with an area of 1602.40 sq.m. I am.
