Proposal- Decision of the Government of the Department of Public Health. As per the revised policy dated 13.01.2010, for grant of grants by the Government to the Nauroji Wadia Maternity Hospital, Parel, Mumbai, the grant is admissible to the institution only for the purpose of payment of salary allowance and expenditure on medicines out of the total expenditure of the hospital, subject to a ceiling of 85% of the total expenditure (50% of which is to be borne by the State Government and the remaining 50% by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation). The Department of Finance vide Notification dated 15.02.2021 has sanctioned an advance of Rs. 17,11,23, 000/- from the Contingency Fund for providing Auxiliary Grants to Naurosoji Wadia Maternity Hospital for the year 2020-21 based on the cost of 2019-20.

For the year 2020-21, Rs. 17,11,22, 576/- has been received by the Government. 17,11,22, 576/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision- The Government is approving to sanction an amount of Rs. 17,11,22, 576/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores Eleven Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Five Hundred and Seventy Six only) as grant-in-aid to Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital. The expenditure in this regard should be met from the Contingency Fund Advance sanctioned under the Memorandum of Finance Department vide No.Akni-2021/ Q.No.1/ Earth-9, dated 15th February, 2021.
The expenditure shall be met from the provision made on BDS for the year 2020-21 under the head of demand number R-1, 2210-Medical Services and Public Health, 01-Civil Health Services-Odysmal Treatment, 110-Hospital and Dispensaries, (03) Subsidiary Grants to Mufassal Hospitals and Dispensaries, (03) (01)-Subsidiary Grants to Non-Teaching Hospitals and Dispensaries, 31 Subsidiary Grants, (Mandatory) (22100173) Contingency Fund Index (8000 7716).
3. Deputy Director, Health Services, Mumbai Circle, Thane is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of grant to Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital, Parel, Mumbai.
The Deputy Director, Health Services, Mumbai Circle, Thane, in the order/ letter regarding handing over the amount for the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to spend from the Contingency Fund, should clearly mention in the order/ letter that the contingency fund advance on the payments to be submitted while spending from the advance amount should be written in red ink as approved under the Government Memorandum Finance Division No: Akani-2021/ PR No.1/ Earth-9, dated February 15, 2021 and this instruction should be underlined in red ink.
The Director of Health Services, Mumbai shall examine the terms and conditions applicable to Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital (e.g. providing free or low-cost services to poor patients).
Whenever the accounts of Nowrosjee Wadia Maternity Hospital are required by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai for inspection, the same shall be submitted by the institution along with the documents furnished.
