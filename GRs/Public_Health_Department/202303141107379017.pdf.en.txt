GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PURAK-2022/ PR. NO. 287/ HEALTH-6 dated 14th March, 2023.
Such conditional funds are being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of the State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
The funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions mentioned in Government Resolution No. 27/2022/ Regulations, dated 12.01.2023.
1) The amount of this fund at the insurance rate prescribed by the Government shall be provided to the insurance company/ hospital concerned against the insurance of the beneficiaries of the combined Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society to be deposited in the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
The Finance Department vide Circular No. Finance-2022/ PR No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 4th April, 2022 in Annexure No. The No. 9. The State Health Guarantee Society has informed the Government that the conditions from 1 to 10 have been fulfilled and the utilization certificate has been submitted for the purpose for which the funds were sanctioned. It is also being certified that the funds disbursed under this Government decision are being spent by depositing them in the separate bank account (ESCROW Account) of the State Health Guarantee Society and all the relevant provisions/ terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 4.04.2022 of the Finance Department have been complied with.
During the financial year 2022-23, expenditure under the head of 2210- Medical Services and Public Health, 01- Civil Health Services- Ophthalmology, 110- Hospitals and Clinics, 07- Ayushman Bharat Programme, (07) (02)- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (Programme) (State Share 40%), 2210 G 449, 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) should be incurred within the limits of the sanctioned allocation.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the financial year 2022-23. Prime Minister
