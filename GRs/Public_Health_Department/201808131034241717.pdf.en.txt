Preamble- Keeping in view the number of accidents and loss of lives on the Mumbai-Pune Expressway, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed between SaveLIFE Foundation (SLF) and Public Health Department on 28th May, 2018. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to provide guidelines for action to be taken in this regard by the Save Life Foundation (SLF) and the Public Health Department. The following decisions have been taken in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION As per the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between SaveLIFE Foundation and Public Health Department on 28th May, 2018, the Government is approving the following measures to be taken to curb the accident rate on Mumbai-Pune Expressway.
1) The following action will be taken by Save Life Foundation and Public Health Department.
1.1. SLF shall undertake the required research and studies to recommend measures required to strengthen emergency medical care for victims of road crashes that take place on the expressway. Health Dept. and SLF agree to jointly monitor the progress of the project.
