Government's decision:
The Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Prohibition of Trade and Commerce, Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 2003 (COTPA Act, 2003) has been enacted to prohibit the use of tobacco. COTPA-2003 is applicable to all tobacco products and is applicable to the entire country. Section 7 of the Act provides for a specific health warning on the wrapper of all tobacco products. The National Tobacco Control Programme is being implemented in all the districts of the State since 2016-17 under the National Health Mission. Awareness is being spread through various media about the ill-effects of tobacco and tobacco consumption.
2. d. The Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Packaging and Labelling) Rules, notified vide GSR 182 (E) dated 15th March, 2008, have been amended from time to time to display specific health warnings on all tobacco product packs. It has been made mandatory to display 85% health warning warning on both sides of the packaging of tobacco and tobacco products. GSR 331 (E) dated 3rd April, 2018 has been amended in Aadhaar April, 2018.
