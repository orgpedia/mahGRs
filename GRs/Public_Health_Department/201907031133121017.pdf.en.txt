Preface: Mouje Virur Gadegaon Ta. Since the village is being rehabilitated due to the WCL unit (coal mine) in Korpana G. Chandrapur, the matter of shifting the primary health center there was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Resolution: 2. Mauje Virur Gadegaon Ta. As the village is being rehabilitated due to WCL's unit (coal mine) in Korpana G. Chandrapur, the primary health center at that place is Mauje Kavathala Ta. The shifting of Koraput to G. Chandrapur is being approved subject to the following conditions: 1) The concerned Gram Panchayat shall make available free of cost space so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
3) Till the time the primary health centre is temporarily operational, the services of the officers and staff of the said primary health centre should be made available to other primary health centres.
