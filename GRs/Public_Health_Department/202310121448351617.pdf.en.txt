Preamble: Under the National Blindness Control Programme, a revised pattern of posts has been fixed for Directorate of Health Services (Headquarters, Mumbai, Programme Head wise and Regional Offices) under the Public Health Department as per the decision of the Government in Article No. 1.
Under the National Blindness Control Programme, 210 temporary posts have been notified in Article No. 2 as per the Government Decision dated 26th April, 2023. 01.03.2023 to dt. It was approved to continue till 31.08.2023. Considering the nature, need and importance of the work of these 210 temporary posts, these posts have been given. The matter of extension from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the National Blindness Control Programme, the following 210 temporary working posts have been created to provide health related services to the people of the state. The continuation from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 is hereby approved by the Government.
