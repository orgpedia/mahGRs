The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State on 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State from the financial year 2017-2018.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component as Central Account (Recurring) for the Financial Year 2019-2020. 2 orders of Rs. 35,98,54, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Crores Ninety Eight Lakh Fifty Four Thousand only) has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 23,99,02, 667/- (Rupees Twenty Three Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Two Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Six only) was under the consideration of the Government for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana.

Government's decision:
For effective implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central Government has released Central share (Recurring) of Rs. 35,98,54, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Crores Ninety Eight Lakh Fifty Four Thousand only) has been made available to the State. The State Government will contribute 40% of the total amount of Rs. Under this decision, the Government have approved the disbursement of subsidy amounting to Rs. 23,99,02, 667/- (Rupees Twenty Three Crore Ninety Nine Lakh Two Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Six only).
Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment of the grant made available under this government decision to the treasury, obtain the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
It should be certified that the subsidy disbursed under this government decision will be credited directly to the beneficiary's account after being deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
