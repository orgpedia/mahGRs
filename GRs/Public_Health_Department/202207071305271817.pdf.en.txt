Any objections/ objections/ suggestions etc. regarding the information shown in the Common Seniority List.
The authorities concerned shall submit their written representations along with appropriate evidence (certified/ attested copies) to the Government within 15 days from the date of publication of this circular in the prescribed manner. If the Government does not receive the representation within this period, the final seniority will be published assuming that the information and seniority shown in the provisional seniority list is acceptable to the concerned authorities and they have no objection to it.
