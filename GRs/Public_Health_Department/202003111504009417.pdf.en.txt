The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State during 2017-18. The funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of this scheme are directly deposited in the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on state accounts.
In the financial year 2018-19, cadre wise funds (for general, SC and ST categories) have been received from the Central Government for implementation of the scheme. The first, second, third and fourth instalments of Rs. 14,68,20,432/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Four Hundred Thirty Two only) has been received by the State and deposited in the ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the state's budget
