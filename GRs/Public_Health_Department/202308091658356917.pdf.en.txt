The Proposal:
The 200-bed hospital has been constructed at a cost of Rs. Proposal for administrative approval of the budget and plans worth Rs. 637.18 lakh is under consideration of the Government
The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
The budget of the furniture works at the 200-bed Women's Hospital Amaravati is based on the tariff of the year 2020-21. The furniture work has been budgeted by the Deputy Engineer, (Architecture), Public Works Sub-Division-1, Amravati; Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Amravati as well as Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board Amravati; and certified by the District Surgeon, Amravati, Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department Amravati. Accordingly, the administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution for the projects and schemes amounting to Rs. 637.18 lakh subject to the following terms and conditions:
Government Decision Department of Industry, Power and Labour dated 24th August, 2017, the tender process should be done as per the procedures and guidelines of Government e-Marketplace (GeM) for procurement of goods and services.

(2) The District Surgeon shall be responsible for inspecting the quality and quantity of such furniture and thereafter approving the payment of such supplier.
3) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required for this work.
4) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
5) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(6) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal for the same should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
8) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
9) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
The concerned Executive Engineer and the District Surgeon shall be responsible for fulfilling the terms and conditions mentioned in the aforesaid No. 1 to 9.
Expenditure on furniture works at 200 bedded Women's Hospital, Amravati will be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210-Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01-Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance)-Plan Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110 Hospitals and Clinics, (1) (a) Plan Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major Constructions (42100014), 53-Major Constructions.
