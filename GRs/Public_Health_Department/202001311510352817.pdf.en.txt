Government's decision:
The process of completing the administrative aspects of the revised Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana by completing the tender process is in the final stage. In order to ensure that there is no interruption in the insurance cover of the beneficiaries in the intervening period, the new tender shall be valid for a period of three months (from 01.01.2020 to 01.02.2020) till the implementation of the revised Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana. The existing Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana are being extended till 31st March, 2020, whichever is earlier.
The Government is approving payment of premium at the rate of Rs.640/- (Rupees Six Hundred and Forty only) per family per year to the insurance company for all the beneficiary components of both the schemes during the above extension period.
