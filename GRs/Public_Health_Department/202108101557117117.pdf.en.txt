Government's decision
Department of Public Health Administrative approval is being given to replace the old CFL fittings in the chamber of the Additional Chief Secretary, to install LED lights in the Anti-Chamber Room. The Executive Engineer, City Electricity Sub-Division, S.B.Division, Mumbai shall work in accordance with the prescribed procedure. The Executive Engineer, Town and Country Planning, Rs. Budget of Rs.57,021/- has been presented.
The cost of No. R-2251 Secretariat Social Services, 090, Secretariat (01) (01) Public Health-13, Office Expenditure (2251 0418) will be covered from the approved provision for the year 2021.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Resolution No: 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Exchange, Part II, dated 17th April, 2015, in terms of Rule 6 Rule 7 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division II.
