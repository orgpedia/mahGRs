The Proposal:
In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to make available 100% Central share of grant-in-aid to the State Health Societies for General Category (COVID-19) for National Rural Health Programme under National Health Mission.
Government Decision: 100% Central share of Budgetary allocation for COVID-19 under National Health Mission (NHM) from General Category (General) for National Rural Health Programme (NRHP) for 2020-21
