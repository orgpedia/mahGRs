Preamble: The Sub-Centre of Mauje, Navnagapur, has been shifted to the Sub-Centre Municipal Council area of Ahmednagar. The government was considering shifting him to Ahmednagar. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. As the sub-centre of Mauje Nagapur T.G. has gone to the Municipal Council area of Ahmednagar, the sub-centre of that place has been shifted to Mauje Navnagapur T.G. The relocation to Ahmednagar is being approved subject to the following conditions: 1) The concerned Gram Panchayat shall make available free of cost space so that the sub-station can function at the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building and staff accommodation where the sub-station can function at the place of relocation will be required. So that in this place there should be separate arrangement for the examination of female patients, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for the sitting of staff.
