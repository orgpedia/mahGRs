(2) The transferred officers shall be relieved of their duties forthwith and shall report at the place of their posting immediately. The compliance report on the joining/ demitting office of the transferred officers should be immediately submitted to the Commissioner, State Labour Insurance Scheme, Mumbai by the officers who have appointed/ relieved them and to the Government by the Commissioner, State Labour Insurance Corporation, Mumbai.
3. No leave of any kind shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise the responsibility shall be fixed on the officer granting leave.
(4) The transferred officers shall, after demitting office, join the new post during the period of office admissible to them. Absence of officers who do not comply with this time limit and remain mutually absent without being present at the place of posting shall be treated as" unauthorised absence" and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Everyone is aware of that.
The Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai shall not depute any officer to the place of his posting within the prescribed period. Administrative action should be initiated against them in time so that the officers appear on the new posting on time.
