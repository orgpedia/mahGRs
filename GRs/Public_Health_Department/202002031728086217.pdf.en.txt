Preamble: For the financial year 2019-20, under the head 2210 G 262 (SCP) for implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, Rs. 220,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Crores only) has been provided in the Budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the State Health Guarantee Society under head 2210 G 262 (SCP) for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.01.2020 to 31.03.2020, as well as for salary and administrative expenses.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, an amount of Rs. 26, 15,52,473/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Fifteen Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand Four Hundred and Seventy Three only) of State Health Guarantee
