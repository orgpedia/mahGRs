Government Decision- The Central share received from the Central Government for administrative expenses for the financial year 2019-2020 for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is Rs. 6,25,21, 268/- (Rupees Six Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty Six only) and 40% of the State share of Rs. 4,16,80, 845/ (Rupees Four Crores Sixteen Lakhs Eighty Eight Thousand Eight Hundred and Forty Five only) aggregating to Rs. 10,42,02, 113/- (Rupees Ten Crores Forty Two Lakhs Two Thousand One Hundred Thirteen only) is being released under this Government decision.
