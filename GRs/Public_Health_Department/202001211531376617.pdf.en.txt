The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State on 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State from the financial year 2017-2018.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component for Q3 FY 2019-2020 4 orders of Rs. 29,11,21, 886/- (Rupees Thirty-nine crore eleven lakh twenty-one thousand eight hundred and eighty eight only) has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 19,40,81, 257/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Forty Lakhs Eighty One Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Seven only) was under consideration of the Government for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana.

Government's decision:
In order to effectively implement the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) in the current financial year 2019-20, the Government has released the third installment of Rs. 29,11,21, 886/- (Rupees Thirty-nine crore eleven lakh twenty-one thousand eight hundred and eighty-eight only) has been provided to the State. The State Government will contribute 40% of the total amount of Rs. 19,40,81, 257/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Forty Lakhs Eighty One Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Seven only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should take action to deposit the payment of the grant made available under this government decision in the treasury, obtain the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
Government Circular No. 2019/ P. No. 92/ Finance-3 of the Finance Department. The terms and conditions contained in Annexure 8.7.2019 are fulfilled. Also, it should be certified that the subsidy disbursed under this government decision will be credited directly to the beneficiary's account after being credited to the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
