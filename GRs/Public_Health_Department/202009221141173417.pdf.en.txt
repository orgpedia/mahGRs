Preface: Mouje Mangal, Ta. A new primary health centre at Jalkot in Latur district was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As a special case, the establishment of a new Primary Health Centre at Jalkot, District Latur is being approved under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The new premises shall be equipped with a building for the functioning of the Sub-Centre and accommodation for the staff. There will be separate arrangements for out-patient services, female patient check-up, delivery, staff seating, and parking if there is an ambulance.
(2) The building shall be equipped with water and electricity.
3) The place of relocation should have all the necessary facilities for this sub-station.
(4) The District Planning and Development Board shall make provision for the construction of the main building and residences of the Sub-Centre at the new place from its District Plan.
