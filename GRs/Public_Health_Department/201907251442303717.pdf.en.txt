Introduction: The Department of Public Health is engaged in establishing various health institutions in rural areas.
Accordingly, in accordance with the above Government Decision dated 09.05.2019, Mauje Phujchurpeth, Ta. Approval has been given to set up a health sub-centre at Gondia. Health
The construction of the building is 75 per cent complete. However, since the Zilla Parishad Gondia had provided a separate building for the sub-centre, the matter of creating a post to operate the sub-centre was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following is the approval for creation of 03 posts for the new Sub-Centre at Phulchurpeth, T.G. Gondia:
