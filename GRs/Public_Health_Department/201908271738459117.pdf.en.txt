The Proposal:
A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps for the construction of a blood bank at the Rural Hospital, Warud, G. Amaravathi, at a cost of Rs. 66.88 lakhs was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget for the construction of the blood bank at Rural Hospital, Varud G. Amaravathi is based on the 2017-18 tariff schedule. The work budget has been prepared by Assistant Engineer Class-I, Public Works Sub-Division, Varud and certified by Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Amaravati; Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Amaravati; and District Surgeon, District Hospital, Amaravati. According to the budget, Rs. 61.40 lakh is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
(2) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.

3) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
6) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
8) While working directly, the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on construction of Blood Bank at Rural Hospital, Varud G. Amaravathi should be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210 Capital Expenditure on Medical Care and Public Health, 02 Rural Health Care, 04 Public Health, 104 Community Health Centre, (00) (01) Large Constructions (42100391), 53 Large Constructions.
4. The Director, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of a blood bank at Rural Hospital, Warud G. Amravati.
