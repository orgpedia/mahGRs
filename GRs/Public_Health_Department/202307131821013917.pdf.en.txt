Preamble: The matter of providing administrative approval to the e-tendering process and expenditure for the financial years 2023-24, 2024-25 and 2025-26 for filling up of contractual skilled and unskilled posts on contract basis and expenditure for their salary for the newly started women hospital, Ambejogai, Beed in the health institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Division, Latur was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
