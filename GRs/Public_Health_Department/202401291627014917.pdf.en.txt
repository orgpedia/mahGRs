Introduction - Setting up of 20-bed Rama Care Centre at Rural Hospital, Erandol District Jalgaon
A proposal to do so was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decision-
To set up a 20-bed Rama Care Centre at Rural Hospital, Erandol District Jalgaon.
This Government decision is being approved as a "special case." Separately, for the construction and post-production of the 20-bed Rama Care Centre, the land will be acquired in the prescribed manner.
Action will be taken.
