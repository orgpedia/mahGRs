The Proposal:
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (R.P. Act) provides guidelines for screening, assessment and issuance of disability certificates.
1 here are issued by the government decision. As per paragraph (a) of the said Government decision, Medical Boards have been constituted for assessment and distribution of certificates of disability and as per paragraph (c), Complaints/ Appeals and Referral Boards have been constituted.
2. All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Nagpur issued reference no. 1 As per the decision of the government here, a request has been made to get the login ID and password of the UDID (Unique Disability ID) system.
Since the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Nagpur, is under the control of the Central Government and the institute has specialized, highly specialized, trained staff and has state-of-the-art machinery, the government was considering providing the user ID and password of the UDID (Unique Disability ID) system to the institution to issue the disability certificate.
