Preamble: Under the National Health Mission, a special residential training workshop (Continue Medical Education (CME)) training is conducted for medical officers with the objective to encourage doctors working in remote and tribal areas, to make them aware of the health facilities in urban areas and to update the knowledge and skills of doctors. A proposal was under consideration of the government to grant administrative approval to carry out the tender process to appoint an external mechanism for this.
Government Decision: To encourage the doctors working in these remote and tribal areas, the administration has decided to conduct a tender process to appoint an external mechanism to conduct special residential training workshops (Continue Medical Education (CME)) training.
