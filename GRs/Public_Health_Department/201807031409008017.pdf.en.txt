Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group A (Pay Band 15600 to 39100 Grade Pay 5400) has been constituted under Section 6 (b) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005. The powers vested in the Minister (Public Health and Family Welfare) under the provisions of section 6 shall be vested in the Principal Secretary (Health), Ministry of Public Health, Mumbai, until further orders. The powers conferred under the order dated 29.05.2018 are hereby revoked.
