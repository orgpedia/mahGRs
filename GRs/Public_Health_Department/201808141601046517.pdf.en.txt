Terms and Conditions 1. Department of Industries, Power and Labour The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out as per the provisions of the revised rule book for office procurement to be carried out by the government departments published under the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2016.
After the completion of the tender process, the selected contractor should be given the work order after the approval of the Central Purchase Committee.
3. While giving administrative approval, it should be ensured that the rate fixed under the process is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the concerned service provider has supplied these items to other places.
