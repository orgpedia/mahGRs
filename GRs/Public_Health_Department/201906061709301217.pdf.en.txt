The Head of the Department is empowered to sanction reimbursement, subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961, to Government employees and their families, in respect of treatment taken in private hospitals for 27 diseases arising out of accident and for 5 serious diseases, to the extent of Rs. 3,00,000/- in each case of medical expenditure incurred in a private hospital approved by the Government for reimbursement of the entire expenditure. Cases of reimbursement of medical expenses beyond this limit are submitted to the administrative department of the relevant ministry. However, in view of the pending cases of proposals for reimbursement of medical expenses, there have been demands from various ministerial administrative departments to re-affirm these powers. Therefore, in pursuance of the said demand, it was now under the consideration of the Government to partially modify the financial powers of the Head of the Department to reimburse medical expenses up to Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh only) subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 vide Government Order dated 16.03.2016 and subsequent Government Orders issued from time to time.

The Head of the Administrative Department of the concerned Ministry shall have full powers to approve or take appropriate decision in the cases of reimbursement of medical expenses above Rs. 3,00,000/- subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and Government Decisions issued from time to time thereafter.
The Government hereby directs that while sanctioning cases at the level of Heads of Ministerial Administrative Departments, Heads of Departments and Regional Heads of Departments as mentioned above, the competent authority shall be responsible for strictly examining and approving the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued thereafter from time to time.
