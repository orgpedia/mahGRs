The Directorate of Health Services (DGHS) has submitted the budget and plan for the construction of a 100-bed sub-district hospital and residential block building for officers and staff in Uran district of Raigad at a cost of Rs 60,51,66, 108/- (Rs. The budget and the technical aspects of the plan were examined and sent to the Public Works Department for approval. The Public Works Department has agreed to give administrative approval for an amount of Rs. 57,51,33000/- (Rupees Fifty Seven Crores Fifty One Lakh Thirty Three Thousand only) by amending certain provisions of the Thematic Works Budget.
The cost of the project is Rs. 5.00 crore. It was submitted to the Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary that the construction has been approved from the CSR fund. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the said work was under the consideration of the government.
