In 2010, the Central Government amended the State Workers Insurance Act, 1948, under Section 58 (5) of which a provision has been prescribed for the State to set up a State Level State Workers Insurance Corporation. In pursuance of this provision, an independent State Level Corporation was constituted vide Government Resolution dated 21.06.2016. However, in the meetings of the State Workers Insurance Corporation held on 06.12.2017 and 16.02.2018, guidelines have been issued regarding the formation of societies for the State Workers Insurance Scheme. If the society is formed, the amount coming from the State Workers Insurance Corporation will be directly deposited in the society and important

It will be expedited and medical facilities will be provided to insurance beneficiaries more easily and effectively. Therefore, the State Level State Workers Insurance Corporation has been abolished and replaced by Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society vide Government Order dated 05.05.2018. In this new area where the Rakavi scheme is implemented, primary and secondary medical care is provided to the insured and beneficiaries registered through the Maha-Rakavi Society. Under Primary Medical Care, Subordinate Service Clinics of Maha-Rakavi Society have been started and under Secondary Medical Care, Rakavi Hospitals have been started, through which medical services are being provided to registered insured and beneficiaries.
The construction of buildings of State Workers Insurance Scheme Service Dispensary, Bagadganj, Nagpur has been completed and administrative approval was given earlier under the Government decision dated 20.08.2018 to construct a protective wall at the site to prevent encroachment of the plot and to protect the area of construction. However, due to increase in budgetary rates and non-commencement of actual work as per the norms, the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, CEO, Maha-Rakavi Society, Mumbai vide letter dated 15.03.2022 with detailed budget was under consideration of the Government for grant of revised administrative approval.
