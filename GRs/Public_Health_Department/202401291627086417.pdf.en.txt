The Proposal -
Primary Health Centre Bhisi, Ta. A proposal to upgrade Chimur G. Chandrapur to a 30-bed rural hospital was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decision-
Primary Health Centre Bhisi, Ta. The upgradation of Chimur G. Chandrapur to a 30-bed rural hospital is being approved under this Government Decision as a "special case." Sadar
Separate action will be taken for the construction and posting of the upgraded 30-bed rural hospital by acquiring the site in the prescribed manner.
