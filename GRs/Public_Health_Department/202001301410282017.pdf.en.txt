The Proposal:
Instructions have been given to set up Establishment of WHO PQS Accredited Lab as per the guidelines given by the Central Government at the National Cold Chain Resource Centre under the Deputy Director, Health Services (Transport), Pune. There are instructions from the Central Government to set up and operationalize national level testing labs for quality testing and capacity building of cold chain equipment. Under this, it is necessary to purchase equipment for conducting all kinds of tests according to the protocol of global rating. Further, grants have been made available under the National Health Mission for the implementation of the House Keeping Service for the management of the building of the Cold Chain Center through an external agency. Accordingly, the matter of obtaining administrative approval for the tender process to be carried out for the housekeeping service for the purchase of test lab equipment, spare parts materials and the management of cold chain source centers was under the consideration of the government.
