Preamble: The Central Government has enacted the Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Prohibition and Regulation of Trade and Commerce, Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 2003 (COTPA) throughout the country vide Gazette dated 19th May 2003. The Act has come into force with effect from 1st May 2004.
Subsequently, the National Tobacco Control Programme has been implemented in 2007-08 under the 11th Five Year Plan. As per Reference No. 4 to the guidelines issued by the Ministry of Health& Family Welfare, Government of India in 2003, for effective implementation of the Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (COTPA) Act, 2003 and National Tobacco Control Programme in the State. A State Level Coordination Committee (SLCC) headed by the Chief Secretary and a District Level Coordination Committee (DLCC) headed by the District Collector have been constituted at the State level. Accordingly, a Taluk Level Coordination Committee (TLCC) headed by the Tahsildar was under consideration to ensure effective implementation of the programme at the Taluk and Village level.
