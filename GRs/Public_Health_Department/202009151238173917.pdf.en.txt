GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC HEALTH, No.SERV-30011/ Q.No. 483/2014/ HEALTH-7, dated 22.06.2016.
Preamble: In pursuance of the above decision of the Government dated 22.06.2020, it has been approved to start the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Ayush Mission in the State and to set up State Ayush Society for the same. In the State AYUSH Society. The Governing Body is headed by the Chief Secretary and the Executive Committee is headed by the Principal Secretary, Public Health Department. Also, a State Programme Management Unit (SPMU) has been set up at the state level for implementation of AYUSH Mission.
Director, AYUSH, Maharashtra State has been appointed as Member Secretary of the Executive Committee of the State Health Society. Also, the work of the State Project Control Room is handled by them. However, since the Director, AYUSH is the Head of the Department under the administrative control of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, a proposal to make slight changes in the administrative system was under consideration of the Government to bring uniformity in the implementation of the National AYUSH Mission and to implement the Mission effectively.

Government Resolution No. SAV-30011/ PR No. 483/2014/ Health-7
Government Decisions: The bulk of the work of the National Health Mission is carried out under the administrative control of the Department of Public Health through the Commissioner and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Commissionerate of Health Services. The post of Commissioner, Health Services is held by a senior officer of the Indian Administrative Service in the pay scale. The Commissioner, Health Services is also designated as Mission Director, National Health Mission. Therefore, in order to bring uniformity in the implementation of the National AYUSH Mission and to make the Mission effective, the Government has decided as under: 1. The Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai is being appointed as Member Secretary of the Executive Committee of the State AYUSH Society in place of Director, Directorate of AYUSH, Mumbai. The Government decision dated 22.06.2016 is hereby amended.
The State Project Monitoring Unit (SPMU) of the National Ayush Mission is being transferred from the Director, Directorate of Ayush, Mumbai to the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Commissioner, Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai and Director, Directorate of AYUSH, Mumbai shall take necessary administrative action in this regard immediately.
This Government Decision was issued by the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals in Informal Reference No.
6/ Age-2, being issued with the approval of 10.06.2020.
