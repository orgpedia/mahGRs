AYUSHMAN BHARAT-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana has been merged with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and the decision to implement both the schemes in the State has been taken as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. For the financial year 2022-23, under the head 2210 G 494 (State share 40%), the budget outlay for the scheme is Rs. 15.00 crore and by way of Supplementary Demand 9,26,76, 000/- aggregating to Rs. A sum of Rs. 24,26,76, 000/- has been sanctioned. For the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, the government was considering to release funds to transfer the amount received by the beneficiary family under the scheme for the quarter of January, 2023 to March, 2023 and to pay the insurance premium for the quarter of April, 2023 to June, 2023 in the Escrow Account with the state share.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PURAK-2022/ PR No.281/ HEALTH-6 dated 27th March, 2023
Government's decision
As the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through State Health Guarantee Societies, the beneficiary family under the scheme will get a sum assured of Rs. Under this Government decision, funds are being sanctioned for the purpose of providing 31 Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried) with a conditional amount of Rs. 9,26,76, 000/-.
The funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions mentioned in Government Resolution No. 27/2022/ Regulations, dated 12.01.2023.
(1) The funds shall be provided to the insurance company for the insurance of the beneficiaries of the combined Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and to the concerned hospital for payment of claims on the basis of guarantee.
2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society to be credited to the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
The Finance Department vide Circular No. Finance-2022/ PR No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 4th April, 2022 in Annexure No. The No. 9. The State Health Guarantee Society has informed the Government that the conditions from 1 to 10 have been fulfilled and the utilization certificate has been submitted for the purpose for which the funds were sanctioned. It is also being certified that the funds disbursed under this Government decision are being spent by depositing them in the separate bank account (ESCROW Account) of the State Health Guarantee Society and all the relevant provisions/ terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 4.04.2022 of the Finance Department have been complied with.
