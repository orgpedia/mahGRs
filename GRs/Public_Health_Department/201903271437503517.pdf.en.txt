The proposal-
Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. For the year 2018-2019, Rs. 60,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Crores only) has been received. The Chief Executive Officer, State Health Guarantee Society has informed that the funds have been directly deposited by the Central Government in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Chief Executive Officer, State Health Guarantee Society. 5 is reported here. For the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, as per the revised estimates in the budget for the financial year 2018-2019, 2210 G 449 (State share 40%), 31 Subsidiary Grants-in-aid amounting to Rs. 68.00 crore has been budgeted. Out of this, Rs. 40,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Crores only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.
Government Decision Central share of funds for Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana Rs. 60,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Crores only) has been deposited in Escrow Account. The Government has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 40,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Crores only).
