Kutir Hospital, Achalpur G. The proposal for administrative approval of the preliminary budget of Rs. 50.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. The budget for the building repair of Kutir Hospital, Achalpur, Amaravati is based on the 2017-18 tariff list. The budget has been prepared by the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Department, Achalpur, G. The budget includes 4 per cent for contingency expenditure and 12 per cent for GST. Administrative approval is being granted under this Government Resolution for the projects and schemes with an outlay of Rs. 50,00,000/- subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions 1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department will be required for the work.
2) The provision of furniture and electrical works should be shown in detail and the rates for the same should be submitted to the government by submitting the break-up.
