The Proposal:
It has been decided to merge Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana with Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and implement both the schemes in the State as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. For the financial year 2021-22, an amount of Rs.2210.861 crore has been allocated under the head of Account for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. A sum of Rs. 186.13 crore has been allocated for the project. For the beneficiaries of this scheme, the premium for the period from April 2022 to June 2022 will be Rs. 32,49,06, 661/-and for payment of claims on guarantee basis Rs. 7,21,95, 092/- aggregating to Rs. 39,71,01, 753/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores Seventy One Lakh One Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty Three only) to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, the provision budgeted under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2021-22, for the period April 2022 to June 2022, an amount of Rs. 32,49,06, 661/-and for payment of claims on guarantee basis Rs. 7,21,95, 092/- aggregating to Rs. 39,71,01, 753/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores Seventy One Lakh One Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty Three only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the State Health Insurance Society.
