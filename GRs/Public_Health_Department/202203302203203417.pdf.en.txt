The Proposal:
The Director (Finance& Accounts), National Health Mission, Mumbai has requested under Reference Letter No. (1) to disburse the pending amount of Rs. 0.13 lakh on Central Account and Rs. 372.56 lakh on State Account for Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) under National Ayush Mission. Further, the Commissioner Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai vide letter No. 2 to the Commissioner, NHM, Mumbai has informed that the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission, launched in September, 2014, has been designed to provide AYUSH health services and education to the general public, enhance the quality of Ayurvedic, Homoeopathic and Unani medicines and supply of quality medicinal plants in a sustained manner. All necessary funds should be allocated for this.
Accordingly, the Government was considering to disburse the pending amount of Rs. 0.13 Lakhs and Rs. 372.56 Lakhs respectively out of the sanctioned grant of Central share of 60% and State share of 2210F999 under the head" 2210F981" for the financial year 2017-18 and 2019-20.

The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Food and Supplies Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment to the treasury and hand over the amount to the Commissioner Health Services and Mission Director, National AYUSH Mission, State AYUSH Society for spending on the prescribed matters. As per the decision taken by the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India, the release of funds is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The funds sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20 have been transferred from the State exchequer to the State AYUSH Society. The revalued funds will be used under the schemes approved by the central government.
(2) The aforesaid guidelines shall be implemented in accordance with the new guidelines issued by the Ministry of Finance, Government of India vide its Office Memorandum No: 1 (13) PFMS/ FCD/ 2020 dated 23.03.2021.
(3) The guidelines of the Central Government should be strictly followed by those implementing the Centrally Sponsored Mission of National AYUSH Mission.
(4) A written undertaking will be submitted to the Central Government regarding the disbursement of the previous year's funds (Central and State share) as per the Scheme of Approval (SAAP) by the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India.
(5) For Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP) under National AYUSH Mission, pending grants of Rs. 0.13 lakh from 31 Subsidiary Grants under the Central Share (60%) head" 2210F981" and Rs. 300.00 lakh from 31 Subsidiary Grants under the State Share head" 2210F999" will be disbursed.
(6) The grants to be disbursed are conditional and further grants will be disbursed only after submission of Utilization Certificate (UC) of the above funds as per Government Circular No: 2013/ PR No. 85/2013/ Budget-3, dated: 25th April, 2013 of the Finance Department.
