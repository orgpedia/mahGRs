Preamble: With a view to ensure that the relatives of the patient do not have to rush for the blood required by the patient in the government/ private hospitals of the state, as well as to provide safe blood supply at the processing fee fixed by the government, the state government through the State Blood Transfusion Council has decided to implement the innovative scheme/ initiative" Jeevan Amrit Seva" (Blood on Call) in all the districts of the state including Mumbai. The decision has been taken 1. Through this scheme, toll free no. After calling 104, blood or blood components are supplied to hospitals and nursing homes within a distance of one hour or 40 km from the district general hospital and hospitals attached to medical colleges on a motorcycle through cold chain or through staff deputed by the institution designated in the respective district for transportation. However, due to the unavailability of blood banks in the tribal and remote areas of Dharni, Sub-District Hospital in Amravati district, Rural Hospital Churni, Sub-District Hospital Dahanu in Palghar district, Sub-District Hospital Jawhar and Blood Storage Center Dhadgaon in Nandurbar district, and Blood Storage Center Akkalkuwa, the government was considering to start Blood on Call-Jeevan Amrit Seva from the tribal and remote district blood storage centers as a special case.
