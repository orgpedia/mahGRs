The Proposal:
Dr Pramod Shriram Chaudhary, Medical Officer, has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. The appointment was made in the year 2009 as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
2. Under the decision of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probation period of government service officers/ employees and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of termination of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the government.
Government Order:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, Dr. Pramod Shriram Chaudhary, Medical Officer Group-A, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, as shown in the table given in this paragraph, may, subject to the necessary conditions, terminate his probationary period.
As satisfactorily completed, their period of probation shall be as indicated in the table in front of their name in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
