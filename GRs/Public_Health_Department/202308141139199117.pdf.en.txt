As there are a large number of vacancies in the cadre of Additional Director/ Joint Director/ Deputy Director, Health Services in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A, these posts are temporarily filled by transferring from the lower cadre officers on a graded basis. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4), Section 4 (5) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority, the posts of Additional Director/ Joint Director/ Deputy Director, Health Services in Group-A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services are being temporarily downgraded and posted as follows:

(2) Since the said officers are being transferred by demotion to the rank of Senior Cadre, they cannot be entitled to regular promotion to the Senior Cadre or claim promotion. Also, they will not be eligible for higher pay.
3. In order to strengthen the headquarters/ offices at Mumbai and Pune under the Commissionerate of Health Services, the said posting is being made on one person one post basis, taking into account the administrative urgency and experience. If officers of the senior cadre become available by promotion or directly, the officers concerned will be transferred to their parent cadre.

The transferred officer should be relieved immediately to report at the place of his new posting and the concerned officer should report to the Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai and the Government.

No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
7. All concerned are being made aware that the absence of the transferred officers, who after relieving them, join the new post during the permissible period of posting, will be construed as" unauthorised absence" and in turn there will be interruption in their service.

If any officer tries to exert direct or indirect pressure to modify the transfer order, such officer shall be liable to disciplinary action.
