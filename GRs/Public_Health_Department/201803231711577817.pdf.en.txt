Preamble: The Maharashtra Emergency Medical Services Project Scheme has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution, dated 28th December, 2016. The scheme has been operationalized w.e.f. 26.01.2014. In 2014-15 and 2015-16, the scheme was partially funded by the Central Government through the National Health Mission and the remaining was being disbursed through the State Plan. As per the decision of the Government in the aforesaid No. 2, from the year 2016-17, the scheme will be transferred from the National Health Mission to the Director, Health Services.
