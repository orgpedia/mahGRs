The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed as Medical Officers Group-A in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre for a period of two years. These appointments are the first in the Government service to the post of Medical Officer Group-A as recommended by an independent Board in 2011.
2. Under the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNORMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Medical Officers Group-A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as indicated in the Table referred to in this paragraph, have satisfactorily fulfilled the requirements for the expiry of their probationary period and their probationary period shall be as indicated in the Table before their names in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
