(2) The said officers shall not be entitled for regular promotion to the post of Director, Health Services/ Additional Director, Health Services, or claim preferential treatment, on being transferred by way of demotion to the post of Director, Health Services/ Additional Director, Health Services.
Also, they will not be eligible for the post of Director, Health Services/ Additional Director, Health Services.
3. Director, Health Services/ Additional Director, Health Services cadre officers, if available by promotion or directly, the concerned officers shall be transferred and posted in their parent cadre.
The transferred officer should be relieved immediately to report at the place of his new posting and the concerned officer should report to the Director, Health Services, Mumbai and the Government immediately at the place of his new posting.
