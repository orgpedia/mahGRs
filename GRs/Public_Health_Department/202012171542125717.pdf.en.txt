The Proposal:
The proposal of the Joint Director, Health Services (Purchase Cell), Mumbai regarding the purchase of new Xerox machines from the Government e-Market Place (GeM) portal for the office of the Deputy Director, Health Services (Transport), Pune was under consideration of the Government for administrative approval. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision:
For purchase of new Xerox machines from Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal for the office of Deputy Director, Health Services (Transport), Pune, Rs. 2,16,393/- (Rupees Two Lakh, Sixteen Thousand, Three Hundred and Ninety Three only) is being accorded administrative approval.
The expenditure on these works should be met from the grants sanctioned under Demand No.R-1, Head of Accounts 2210- Medical Services and Public Health, (01) (10) Establishment of Public Health Transport Association (Mandatory) (2210 1053), 13-Office Expenditure.
