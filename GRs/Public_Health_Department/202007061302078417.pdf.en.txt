The proposal-
Under the Epidemic Disease Control Programme, medicines are procured for the control of water borne diseases, diarrhoea, gastro, typhoid, water borne diseases such as leptospirosis, swine flu, etc. The office of the Joint Director, Health Services (Malaria, Elephant and Waterborne Diseases), Pune, is responsible for the control of the epidemic situation in the state. At present, along with these diseases, it is also necessary to procure medicines urgently for the disease caused by the virus COVID-19, and since this matter is also covered under the Epidemic Control Program, a proposal has been received under the Reference Letter for granting administrative approval for the purchase of medicines for the Epidemic Control Program. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the purchase of these medicines was under the consideration of the government.
Government Resolution regarding Joint Director of Health Services (H& J) No. In pursuance of the proposal submitted vide letter dated 01.01.2020, for Epidemic Disease Control Programmes (on treatment of epidemics and COVID-19 outbreak) as shown in the accompanying prospectus (Rs. 1,01,60, 000/- (Rs.
