Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of this scheme are directly deposited in the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme in the state account.
In the financial year 2018-19, cadre wise funds (for general, SC and ST categories) have been received from the Central Government for implementation of the scheme. This includes the first, second, third and first instalment for the Scheduled Tribes (ST) category for 2019-2020.
12,81,08, 024/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Eighty One Lakh Eight Thousand Twenty Four only) has been received by the State and deposited with ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the State share of Rs. 8,54,05, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Fifty Four Lakhs Five Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
