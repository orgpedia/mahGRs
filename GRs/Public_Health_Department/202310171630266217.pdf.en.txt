Preamble: In pursuance of the decision of the Government in the aforesaid No. 2, the temporary posts in the Health Institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Kolhapur Circle, Kolhapur and the Deputy Director, Health Services, Pune Circle were extended till 31.08.2023. Since the said extension has expired and the posts are no longer required, the matter of extension of the said posts from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: Temporary posts in health institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Kolhapur Circle, Kolhapur and Deputy Director, Health Services, Pune Circle were extended till 31.08.2023. The Joint Director, Health Services, (Preventive/ Sub Centre/ Dispensaries), Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government to extend the temporary posts in the Health Institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Kolhapur Circle and Deputy Director, Health Services, Pune Circle from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Under this Government decision, the temporary posts and temporary posts mentioned in the table in Annexure-A with this in the health institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Kolhapur Circle, Kolhapur and Deputy Director, Health Services, Pune Circle are being extended from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
