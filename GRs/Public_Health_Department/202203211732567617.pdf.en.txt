These orders are being issued in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4), 4 (5) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005.
Dr. Sunil Pote should immediately appear at the place of transfer and submit a report to the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai and the Government.
