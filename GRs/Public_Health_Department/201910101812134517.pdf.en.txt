The Proposal:
The budget for supply and erection of separate new Rohit Cable for CT Scan at General Hospital Udgir, District Latur has been prepared by Executive Engineer, PWD, Power Department, Osmanabad and certified by Deputy Engineer, PWD, Power Sub-Division, Latur and District Surgeon, Latur. The technical approval has been given by the Superintending Engineer (Electrical), Pune Regional Circle, Public Works Department, Pune. The budget for 2019-20 is based on this. The matter of providing administrative approval for the supply and installation of a separate new Rohit cable for CT scan at General Hospital Udgir, District Latur was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision:
For providing and installing a new Rohit Cable for CT Scan at General Hospital Udgir, District Latur Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 14,62,594/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand Five Hundred and Ninety Four only).
