The Proposal:
Some of the medical officers in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A (Pay Scale S-20) were unauthorisedly absent for a long time due to various reasons. As per the request made by them for deposition and the proposal submitted by the Director Health Services, Mumbai for deposition of the concerned medical officers subject to certain conditions, the concerned medical officers have been engaged under the Government decision mentioned in the context.
As per the decision of the Government in question, the Medical Officers, Group-A being unauthorised absentees, the period from the date of their absence till their actual joining has been deemed to be a break in service as per Rule 47 (1) of the M.N.S. (Pension) Rules, 1982 and they have been absorbed. Of them, nine medical officers had requested the government to regularise the period of unauthorised absence by waiving the service clause. The matter of cancelling the service of these medical officers was under the consideration of the government.
