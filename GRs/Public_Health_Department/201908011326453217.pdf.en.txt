The Proposal:
Dr. Kranti Sundarrao Raut, Medical Officer, has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. The appointment was made in the year 2009 as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
As per the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble to the Government Order, as indicated in the table mentioned in this paragraph, Dr. Kranti Sundarrao Raut, Medical Officer Group-A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, has satisfactorily completed the necessary requirements to complete his probationary period. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
