(2) The transferred officer shall be relieved of his duties forthwith to report at the place of his new posting and shall be posted at the place of posting assigned to him by the officer concerned.
The report should be submitted to the Director of Health Services, Mumbai and the government.
No leave shall be granted to the transferred officer, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
4. The transferred officer shall join the new post during the permissible period of his superannuation, failing which his absence shall be treated as" unauthorised absence" and shall be liable to be terminated.
(5) If the officer concerned does not appear at the place of his posting, the Commissionerate shall take appropriate action against him.
Administrative action should be initiated and the proposal should be submitted for the approval of the government as per the need. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
(6) Any attempt by the officer concerned to bring direct or indirect pressure to modify the order of transfer shall entitle the officer concerned to take disciplinary action.
