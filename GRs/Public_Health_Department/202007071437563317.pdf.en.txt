The Proposal:
As there is no RT PCR lab in Jalna district, swab samples are being sent to District Hospital, Aurangabad for testing of COVID-19. A lot of time and money is being spent on transportation and testing of swab samples for COVID-19 testing. Jalna has a large number of industrial estates and families from Beed and other Marathwada regions are coming to Jalna district in large numbers. In Jalna district, SRPF and Police Training Centre personnel are returning to the district after serving in different areas (especially hotspots and containment zones). A proposal for an estimated cost of Rs 1,07,06, 920/- has been received by the Government.
As an emergency matter, the government was considering to spend it from the Chief Minister's Relief Fund.
