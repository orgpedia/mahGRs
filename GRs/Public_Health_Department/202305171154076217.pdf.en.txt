2.The terms and conditions of the appointment shall be as follows:
The amount to be paid to Shri Shivanand Taksale after deducting his present pension (including Dearness Allowance and Partial Pension) from the total salary (including Basic Pay and Dearness Allowance) being received by him before his retirement should be fixed as monthly industrial allowance.
As long as Mr. Taksale is working in the post of CEO on contractual basis, he will be entitled to all service concessions and allowances admissible to him for the post of CEO, State Health Guarantee Society.
During the period of the said appointment, Mr. Shivanand Taksale was performing the duties of the Chief Executive Officer, State Health Guarantee Society.
All rules relating to conduct, discipline and appeal applicable to officers in the service shall continue to apply.
During the period of such appointment, the Memorandum of Association of the Society and its rules and regulations shall be applicable to Mr. Shivanand Taksale.
In addition to the above terms and conditions, such terms and conditions as may be determined by the Government shall be binding on Mr. Taksale.
