The Proposal:
It was decided on 26.02.2019 to implement the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana in the State by merging both the schemes with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
The government has taken the decision. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2022-23 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. A sum of Rs. 70.00 crore has been provided in the Budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the period from October, 2022 to December, 2022 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
Government Decision: As the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the premium for payment of insurance premium for the period from October 2022 to December 2022 for the beneficiary family under the scheme will be Rs. 25,03,21, 217/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Three Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred Seventeen only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
