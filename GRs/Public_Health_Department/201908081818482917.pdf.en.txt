The Proposal:
Dr. Pradeep Marotirao Thakkarwad, Medical Officer mentioned in the table below, has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. This appointment is in reference No. The first appointment in the Government service was made in the year 2002 as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the post of" Medical Officer Group-A." However, since Dr. Thakkarwad was pursuing post-graduate medical education at Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad at the time of his appointment, he could not join the place of posting within the stipulated time. After completing his graduation, he was awarded the No. 2 The posting has been given in the year 2004 as per the government decision mentioned here.
2. Under the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
