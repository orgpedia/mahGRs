A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs 858.54 lakh was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The budget for the construction of 100 bedded Sub District Hospital Main Building and Residence by upgrading 50 bedded Sub Hospital, Muktainagar, G. Jalgaon is based on the schedule of 2020-21. The budget has made provisions for fuel gas pipeline, bio-digester, rainwater harvesting, solar roof, furniture, electrification, water supply and sewerage, fire prevention, and lift, etc. 1355.03 lakh and accommodation Rs. 858.54 crore. 2213.57 lakhs are being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
3) When e-tendering the work, the work should be tendered together without breaking it into pieces.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
5) The price of the proposed work should not be increased unnecessarily according to the situation of the area and the needs of the health institutions.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) The total area of the main building ground floor and first floor is 2568.01 sq.m. 33033.00 per sq.m. and the first floor construction rate is Rs. 30976.00 per sq.m.
10) The residential building will be of type 1, type 2, type 3, etc. The total area of the residential building is 1863.47 sq.m. 31339.00/- per sq.m., Type 1 Type 2 Wheel Floor Rs. 28193.00 per sq.m. and the rate of construction of second floor of type 1 and type 2 is Rs. 2541.00 per sq. I also have a Type 3. 30613.00 per sq.m. and First Floor Rs. 28314.00 sq.
11) It should be ensured that the area of the proposed building is in accordance with the permissible standards of the staff.
The entire responsibility of fulfilling the conditions mentioned in 1 to 11 above will be of the concerned Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Surgeon.
