Introduction: Th. The matter of granting administrative approval to upgrade the Primary Health Centre at Ghansawangi, Jalna district to a 50-bed sub-district hospital was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: Administrative approval is being given to upgrade the Primary Health Centre at Ghansawangi, District Jalna to a 50-bed sub-district hospital. Separate action will be taken for the construction and creation of posts for the 50-bed sub-district hospital in this category.
