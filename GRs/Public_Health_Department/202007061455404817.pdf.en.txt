Government Decision: 1246 temporary posts in health institutions under the control of Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Division, Latur. 01.10.2019 to D. The period was extended till 29.02.2020. Thereafter, the Finance Department has issued the aforesaid No. 1. As per the Government Circular dated 31st March, 2020, due to administrative difficulties arising out of COVID-19, temporary posts of all departments have been removed. This has been extended up to 31.05.2020. As the said extension has expired, the Commissionerate of Health Services has submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of the said posts vide letter No. 3 above.
1246 temporary posts in the health institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Division, Latur, mentioned in the" Annexure-A" attached to this Government Decision. 01.06.2020 to dt. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2020.
The salary of these temporary posts should be spent in the next accounting year and this expenditure should be met from the approved grant for the year 2020-2021.
