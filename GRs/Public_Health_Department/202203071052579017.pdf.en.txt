Preamble: State Advisory under the Biomedical Waste Management Act, 2016. The committee has been set up as per 1. However, as per the guidelines issued under the Biomedical Waste Management Act, 2016, the treatment/ diagnosis/ isolation of patients during the period of COVID-19 infection was not taken care of.
Therefore, the National Green Tribunal, Principal Bench, New Delhi vide original Application No. 180/2021 against Shri Mukul Kumar, Uttar Pradesh and others. While the State Advisory Committee has already been constituted in pursuance of the said petition, it is proposed to constitute a Committee for creating necessary facilities and infrastructure at the State level and district level for the care and treatment of COVID-19 patients and for the proper disposal of waste generated therefrom. Accordingly, the state government had constituted district-level committees.
Government Decision: The Government hereby approve the constitution of the following Committees at the District and State level under the Biomedical Waste Management Act, 2016 as proposed by the National Green Tribunal, Principal Bench.
