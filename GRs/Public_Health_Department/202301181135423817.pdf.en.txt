Government's decision
As the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through State Health Guarantee Societies, the State Government's share (40%) in the direct balance fund limit for payment of insurance premium and claims on NTMS/ Guarantee basis for the quarter from 1st January, 2023 to 31st March, 2023 of the beneficiary family under the scheme will be Rs. Under this Government decision, conditional release of funds to the tune of Rs. 2,90,28, 698/- (Rupees Two Crore Ninety Lakhs Twenty Eight Thousand Six Hundred and Ninety Eight only) is being approved.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society for crediting it to the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
Department of Finance Decision No. Finance for 2022. No. 43/ Meaning-3, D. It is being certified that the terms and conditions as on April 04, 2022 are being met. It is also being certified that after the funds disbursed under this government decision are deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme, the funds will be paid to the insurance company on the insurance premium of the beneficiaries of the scheme and to the concerned hospital on the basis of the guarantee claim.
The expenditure for the financial year 2022-23 should be within the limits of the provision sanctioned under the head of the following accounts.
Demand No. T-5 2210- Medical Services and Public Health, 03- Rural Health Services, Odd-Even Medical Services, 796- Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 01- Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 07- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (Programme) (State Share 40%) 2210 G 494, 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried)
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the financial year 2022-23.
