Government Resolution No: APDE-2017/ PR No.97/ Health-4
A total of 104 temporary posts (26 posts each) for GNM Nursing School at Nashik, Nandurbar, Beed and Nanded have been sanctioned subject to the following conditions: 1.01.2018 to dt. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2018.
(1) None of the above posts has been vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.
(2) Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.D. The terms and conditions prescribed as on 08.01.2018 should be scrupulously followed and acted upon accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised design of the office finally approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts are not extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowance of the officers/ employees, the responsibility will be of the Director, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai.
The expenditure on these posts will be covered under the head Account Head 2210- Medical Services and Public Health in Demand No.R-1, Scheme under 06-Five Year Plan- Scheme under State Plan, (06) (01)- Non-Teaching Government Hospitals and Dispensaries in Mofussil Areas (2210 0226) and Head Account Head in Demand No.R-1, Head Account Head 2211- Family Welfare, 003- Training, (01)- Family Welfare Training- 100 Centrally Sponsored Scheme, (01) (02)- Training of Paramedical Staff including Auxiliary Nurse Midwives, Midwives and Health Campaigners (2211 0111) for the financial year 2017-18.
This Government Resolution was issued by the Department of Finance, No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.D. The powers conferred on the Head of Administrative Department as on 08.01.2018 are being issued.
