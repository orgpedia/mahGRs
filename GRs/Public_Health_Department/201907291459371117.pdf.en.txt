Introduction: As there is no space available in Sakri G. Dhule, the place has been changed to the primary health center there. The matter of sanctioning Sakri G Dhule was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: 2. As there is no space available in Sakri G. Dhule, the place has been changed to the primary health center there. Approval at Sakri G. Dhule is being granted subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be mandatory for the concerned Gram Panchayat to provide free space so that the Primary Health Centre can be operationalized at the relocated place.
2) 24 hours water and light arrangements, sanitation, etc. should be available.
3) The District Planning and Development Board is required to make provision for the construction of the said health sub-centre from its District Plan.
4) A separate proposal for the creation of posts should be submitted to the government after the completion of 75 percent of the construction of the primary health center.
