Preface: Primary Health Centre, Nandura. The primary health centre is located at Nandura village in Buldhana district. The matter of shifting Nandura to Buldhana was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: 2. Primary Health Centre, Nandura Ta. The primary health centre is located at Nandura village in Buldhana district. The relocation of Nandura to Buldhana District is being approved subject to the following conditions: 1) It will be mandatory for the concerned Gram Panchayat to provide free space so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
3) Till the time the primary health centre is temporarily operational, the services of the officers and staff of the said primary health centre should be made available to other primary health centres.
