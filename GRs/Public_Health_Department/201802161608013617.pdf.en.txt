The Proposal:
Dr. Suresh Shamrao Dhekale, Medical Officer mentioned in the table below, has been appointed to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a probation period of two years. This appointment is the first in the Government service as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the year 1998 for the post of" Medical Officer Group-A."
As per the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of the officers/ employees of the Government Service and the matter of termination of probationary period of Dr. Suresh Shamrao Dhekale was under consideration of the Government as the said officer who was given posting as Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A has completed his two years probationary period satisfactorily.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, as indicated in the table given in this paragraph, Dr. Suresh Shamrao Dhekale Medical Officer Group-A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre has satisfactorily completed the necessary requirements for the expiry of his probation period. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
