The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre for a probation period of two years.
These appointments are the first in the Government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Independent Selection Board constituted under the Department of Public Health in 2014.
2.
