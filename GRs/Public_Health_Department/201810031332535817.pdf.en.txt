Introduction- Chembur Service Clinic was abruptly closed in January 2013 after a court order went against the government. Therefore, alternative arrangements were made for the insured in the Chembur area at the service hospital in Sion. At the same time, there was an urgency to start a service clinic in Bhiwandi. Therefore, Chembur Service Clinic has been shifted to Bhiwandi in January 2013. The matter was pending for the government's approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISION By this Government, it is hereby given ex-post facto approval to shift the State Workers Insurance Scheme Service Dispensary, Chembur, Mumbai to Bhiwandi, Thane district.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer concerned shall take action to obtain authorization letter from the Accountant General's Office-1, Mumbai, by submitting a proposal for shifting of the office, for payment of salary dues/ other incidental expenses of the employees of the said Hospital.
After receiving the letter of authorization from the Accountant General's office, he should take necessary action in accordance with the circular of the Finance Department No. 1010/ PR No. 10/ Treasurer No. 5, dated 09.02.2010.
The employees who have been working in the Sewa Hospital in Bhiwandi since 2013 have been paid house rent allowance and city allowance at the rate of Mumbai. The difference in the amount should be recovered from the concerned.
