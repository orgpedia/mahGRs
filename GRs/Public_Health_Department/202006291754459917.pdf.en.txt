The proposal-
For the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central Government has allocated Rs. 6,25,21, 268/- (Rupees Six Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty Six only) has been provided to the State. Under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) from the budgetary provision of 2019-2020, an amount of Rs. 6,25,21, 268/- and 40% of State share of Rs. 4,16,80, 845/- aggregating to Rs. The Government Resolution for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 10,42,02, 113/- was issued on 31.03.2020. However, the funds could not be drawn as they were not directly available on the budget distribution system. Therefore, the government was considering to allocate this fund from the available budgetary provision for the year 2020-2021. The central government has also decided to spend the central share of funds in 2020-2021. The approval has been accorded vide letter dated 15.05.2020. The government is taking steps in that direction.

4. The payment towards the funds disbursed under the said Government decision shall be made to the State Health Society-PMMVY for spending on the prescribed matters by depositing the same in the treasury.
