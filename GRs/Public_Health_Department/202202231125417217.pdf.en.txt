All concerned are being made aware that officers who do not comply with this time limit and remain absent from the place of posting mutually, after relieving the transferred officer, should join the new post during the permissible period of posting, their absence will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and there will be interruption in their service.

The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
