As per the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and rules prescribed thereunder from time to time, the Namco Charitable Trust-run SGS Cancer Hospital, Nashik, has been permitted to reimburse medical expenses to the state government employees and their families for treatment and surgery for serious diseases like cancer and its aftermath. The following conditions must be met:
Under Section 41A of the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950, 10% of the patients are treated free of cost and 10% of the patients are treated at a discounted rate.
To provide trained medical officers and staff as per the norms prescribed under the Bombay Nursing Homes Act, 1949.
