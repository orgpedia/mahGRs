Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to extend the contractual contractual cleaning service of the sub-district/ rural/ women's hospital under the District Surgeon, Latur for the period November, 2019 to December, 2020. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Decision of the Government: The contractual contractual sanitation service in a total of 13 hospitals, namely 1 Women's Hospital, Latur, 2 Sub-District Hospitals, Udgir and Nilanga and 10 Rural Hospitals Ahmednagar, Chakur, Jalkot, Deoni, Babhalgaon, Renapur, Murud, Ausa, Kasarsirsi and Killari, under the District Surgeon, Latur, is being extended for the period of 2018-19 from November, 2019 to December, 2020 subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. The contractors/ agencies concerned should be duly contracted by measuring their office with the help of the Public Works Department and completing the tender process accordingly.

(2) The work to be carried out by the external agency should be done by the contractor/ company/ institution with the approval of the competent authority by inviting tender from the government.
3) The company/ organization to which the work was assigned. An agreement should be reached with them. The agreement should contain the necessary terms and conditions. The Government shall have no liability for the services of the officers/ employees acting on behalf of the company/ institution under this agreement.
4) No posts will be created to work through the external system.
5) The payment of work to be done by external agencies must be shown under the detailed heading of" Office Expenditure" instead of under the detailed heading of" Salary."
6) While doing the work through an external system, it is necessary to save at least 10 to 25 percent of the cost that would have been incurred if the work had been done by creating posts regularly.
7) The external agency should be selected in a very transparent manner by conducting the tender process in the manner prescribed by the government.
8) The details of the saving of 10 to 25 percent in the expenditure incurred by the external agencies in taking the services as mentioned above as compared to the expenditure on filling up the posts in the regular mode, Government Circular No. Designation-2013/ No. 112/13/ Financial Reforms-1, No. The form with 02.12.2013 is required to be submitted to the government.
9) The above instructions should be strictly followed by all concerned, otherwise disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned by fixing responsibility on them.
10) Sanitation services will be subsidized under the National Health Mission (NHM) and the approval of the district committee will be required.
11) In order to continue the sanitation services in the hospital under the Public Health Department in the financial year 2021-22, the Department of Public Health, Government Decision No. Administrative approval has been granted as on 02.03.2021. Action should be taken accordingly.
The Deputy Director and the District Surgeon shall ensure that the selection of the first service provider is done by following the due process and the Deputy Director/ District Surgeon shall be fully responsible for the same.
The District Surgeon will be fully responsible for ensuring that the payments for sanitation services (National Health Mission (NHM)/ 13-Office expenses/ 10 Contractual services etc. are not paid twice from the grants received under various heads of accounts.
14) The expenditure should be made from the approved budgetary provision.
15) The expenditure for this should be made by following the prescribed procedure.
16) Government Circular, Finance Department No. Designation-2013/ PR No. 11/13/ VISU-1, dated 02.02.2013 and D. 19.12.2013 as well as the amendments/ provisions made in this Order from time to time.
