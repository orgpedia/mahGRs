Accordingly, the proposal is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
1) The requirement of the material should be verified/ verified. Thereafter, the tender process will be initiated.
2) The specifications of the material should be verified as per requirement and as up-to-date.
The technical specifications of the items to be procured should be as per the specifications agreed by the concerned experts (Technical Committee/ Officials). Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not designed in such a way that it will benefit any particular company. Also, only the items included in the proposal should be purchased.
3) Government Decisions, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. The procurement process should be carried out in the prescribed manner in accordance with the provisions of the Revised Manual of Procedures for Office Purchases to be made by Government Departments issued under Section 82/ Part-I/ Industry-4, dated 1st December, 2016.
4) The rates of the proposed procurement are estimated at the time of granting administrative approval. Therefore, the price should be determined at the end of the purchase process. It should be ensured that the price is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the supplier has supplied the item to other places.
5) Pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection of the items to be purchased should be done to ensure that only the items with proper and correct specifications are purchased.
6) Storage and distribution of the items to be procured after supply, supply chain management and inventory management should be done in a proper manner to ensure that these items do not remain unused and are used in a timely manner.
Further, the total expenditure for the said procurement is Rs. 17,04,78, 446/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores Four Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Four Hundred and Forty Six only) to be met from the grants sanctioned under various heads of accounts- Supplies and Materials under regular State grants during the financial year 2021-22. The procurement should be completed within the stipulated period of administrative approval.
