The Proposal:
Some of the medical officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100-177500) were unauthorisedly absent for a long time due to various reasons. Also, some medical officers were absent for postgraduate courses without prior permission from the government. As the Public Health Service is an essential service and there is a shortage of medical officers under the department, in view of the ongoing COVID-19 virus epidemic, the government was considering to recruit and post the concerned medical officers in the government service immediately after their unauthorized absence, as per the request made by the unauthorized absent medical officers and in accordance with the proposals/ recommendations submitted to the government by the Commissioner, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai.
Government Order:
As mentioned in the Preamble, the non-official absentee Medical Officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100 177500) are being recruited subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Table below this paragraph and are being given such posting as indicated against their names in the Table below for joining the post of Medical Officer Group-A.
