Preamble: The Government has decided to select the service provider by inviting open tender through the National Health Mission to provide the service of ambulances equipped with medical equipment with state-of-the-art communication system to provide immediate medical assistance to the disaster victims in the state. A Tender Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Health) vide order dated 28.10.2010. The tender committee decided to qualify the tender submitted by M/ s BVG India and award the contract for the operation of Emergency Response Service (EMS) of a total of 937 ambulances, 233 Advance Life Support (ALS) and 704 Basic Life Support (BLS), across the state in the next 5 years. BVG India, the service provider, has been issued a Government Order dated 28th March, 2013. The scheme has been operationalized w.e.f. 26.01.2014. The scheme was funded by the Government of India through National Health Mission (NHM) in 2014-15 and 2015-16 to some extent.

The rest of the grants were being disbursed from the state scheme. As per the decision of the Government in the aforesaid Act No. 2, the scheme was transferred from National Health Mission to Director, Health Services with effect from 2016-17. The scheme is being implemented from 2016-17. As per the MoU signed with the service provider in this regard, the contract expired on 31.01.2019, however, as a matter of urgency, the service provider was given extension to pay the February 2019 dues at the rate of January 2019, as per the Government decision dated 31.03.2019, so that the ambulance service can continue without any interruption in patient care. Subsequently, as per the decision taken by the Committee headed by the A.M.S. (Finance) constituted under the Government Decision dated 3rd January, 2019, to continue the ambulance service without interruption, the Government Decision dated 8th May, 2019 decided to extend the period of service delivery from 1st March, 2019 to 30th June, 2019 as per the rates of 2018-19. A decision in this regard is being taken.
