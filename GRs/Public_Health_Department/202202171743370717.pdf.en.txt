As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government in the public interest to continue the services of officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, by reviewing their service in order to test their eligibility (eligible/ ineligible) to continue in the Government service and prematurely retire the officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 The procedures and general instructions regarding service review are prescribed under the Government Decision dated 10.06.2019. Accordingly, for review of services of Group-B officers in the Public Health Department, Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 24.05.2021, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Public Health).
Administrative Officers, Group-B Cadre Officers in Public Health Department beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, their service review was submitted in a cyclical manner for approval of the Committee. The officers who have been recommended by the Departmental Review Committee after evaluating the service of the said officer, have been made eligible to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. It was for the government to approve it.
