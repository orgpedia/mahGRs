GOVERNMENT DECISIONS GENERAL ADMINISTRATION DEPARTMENT, NO. LPL2017/ PR. NO. 21/ K.-15, Dated 10.06.2019.
Preamble: As per the provisions of Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the General Administration Department has issued a notification to review the services of officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service. The consolidated methodology has been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 10.06.2019. As per the provisions of the said Government decision, the Government was contemplating to constitute a" Departmental Review Committee" for review of services of Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre (Medical Officers, Group-B/ General State Service Group-B) under the Commissionerate of Health Services and Group-A (Gazetted) and Group-B Gazetted Cadre Officers under the State Labour Insurance Scheme beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service and a" Departmental Reference Committee" for consideration of the representations.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Departmental Review Committee is being constituted under the Commissionerate of Health Services, Public Health Department, to review the services of Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre (Medical Officers Group-B/ General State Service Group-B) and Group-A (Gazetted) and Group-B Gazetted Officers under the State Employees Insurance Scheme, based on the prescribed criteria to test their eligibility to continue their services beyond the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
