Government Order:
Medical Officer (Pay Level S- 20:56100 177500) in Group A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services has been posted as Medical Officer (Tuberculosis) in Specialist Cadre (Pay Level S-23: 67700 208700). As on 02.06.2020, open category vacancies have been filled only on temporary basis by way of promotion. In the said promotion order, Dr. Rajiv Sheshrao Tambhale, Medical Officer, General Hospital, Alibaug has declined the promotion to the post of Medical Officer (Tuberculosis Physician) in Specialist Cadre on family grounds.
Accordingly, with the approval of the competent authority, the promotion of Dr. Rajiv Sheshrao Tambhale, Medical Officer to the post of Specialist Cadre Medical Officer (Tuberculosis Physician) is being cancelled under this order.
(2) If the said Medical Officer has availed the benefit of seniority in pay scale under the Assured Progress Scheme on being denied promotion, the said benefit shall be withdrawn as per the orders issued by the Finance Department from time to time and a compliance report thereon shall be submitted to the Government.
(3) A medical officer who is denied promotion shall not receive the date of retirement of the officer in the selection list in which he was selected but did not accept the promotion and of the officers in the subsequent two lists.
4) The order will come into effect immediately.
(5) The Commissionerate of Health Services and the Office of the Commissionerate of Health Services-3 shall take immediate action to reinstate the above mentioned medical officer in his parent cadre.
