The Proposal:
The Governing Council Committee has been constituted as per the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference for implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana. The matter of cancellation of the appointment of non-official members appointed as per the government decision in reference No. 2 to this committee was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government under reference No. 2 for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, the appointment of Mr. Mangesh Chivate, a non-official member on the Governing Council Committee, is being cancelled under this Government decision.
