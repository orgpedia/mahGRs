Government Decisions For Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, State Government share (40%) for the period April, 2023 to June, 2023, Rs. 7,85,07, 335/- (Rupees Seven Crores Five Lakhs Seven Thousand Three Hundred and Thirty Five only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
The amount should be deposited in the State Health Insurance Society.
The funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions mentioned in Government Resolution No. 27/2022/ Regulations, dated 12.01.2023.
1) The fund at the rate prescribed by the Government shall be provided to the hospital by taking the amount of claims on the basis of insurance/ guarantee against the insurance of the beneficiaries of the combined Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society to be credited to the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
The Finance Department vide Circular No. Finance-2022/ PR No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 4th April, 2022 in Annexure No. The No. 9. The State Health Guarantee Society has informed the Government that the conditions from 1 to 10 have been fulfilled and the utilization certificate has been submitted for the purpose for which the funds were sanctioned. It is also being certified that the funds disbursed under this Government decision are being spent by depositing them in the separate bank account (ESCROW Account) of the State Health Guarantee Society and all the relevant provisions/ terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 4.04.2022 of the Finance Department have been complied with.
