Preamble: The State Health Mission has been set up to implement the National Rural Health Mission programme in the State as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The main objective of the scheme is to provide quality health services to the people of the state. The Central Government vide letter dated 2nd March, 2017 has notified the continuation of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM) during the year 2016-17. Accordingly, all the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM) implemented by Public Health Department, Government of Maharashtra have been extended till the implementation of National Health Mission (NHM) from 1st April, 2017. Total 04 temporary posts created at State level for implementation of National Health Mission Scheme have been extended from time to time as per the decision of the Government in context. The temporary posts are as under: No. The deadline was extended till August 31, 2022. Since there will be a need for these 4 posts, the matter of extension from September 1, 2022 was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In the context of Finance Department No. As per the Government Resolution No. 16, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts included in their rolls from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
