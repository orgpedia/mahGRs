Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The central share (60%) of the funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of the scheme is directly credited to the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on state account.
In the financial year 2020-21, cadre-wise funds (for general, SC and ST categories) have been received from the Central Government for implementation of the scheme. In this, reference no. 1, 2 and 3 respectively. 1,15,37, 118/-, Rs. 6,92,22, 708/- and Rs. 3,46,11, 354/- aggregating to Rs. 11,53,71, 180/- has been received by the State and deposited in ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the State share of Rs. 7,69,14, 120/- (Rupees Seven Crores Seventy-nine Lakhs Fourteen Thousand One Hundred and Twenty only) was under consideration at the Government level. 40% of the State's share of Rs. 7,69,14, 120/- (Rupees Seven Crores Seventy-nine Lakhs Fourteen Thousand One Hundred and Twenty only) has been released by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department.
