Attention of all the above mentioned medical officers is being drawn to the bond issued by them for admission to Post Graduate Degree/ Post Graduate Course. As per the government order, if they fail to join the post within the stipulated time, they will have to pay Rs. 50 lakh (fifty lakhs) or such amount as the Government may from time to time prescribe and deposit in the Government Treasury, the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall initiate the proceedings forthwith and the entire responsibility for the same shall be of the Head of the Office concerned as well as the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the District Treasury Officer.
In view of the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic in the State, the Medical Officers posted under this Order should join their revised place of posting immediately (within three days from the date of issue of the order). Disciplinary action will be proposed against the medical officer if he does not join within the stipulated time.
If some medical officers have been appointed at the district level in the last two months to control the COVID-19 epidemic and in this situation, the post is not vacant, then the officials appointed in accordance with the order will be required to take their services on priority and take their services there or elsewhere in the same district as per the urgency, taking into account the profile of the health institution and the educational qualifications of the medical officer.
5. Medical Officers mentioned in Form-B shall be appointed on the ground that they have not passed the post graduate diploma course. 6. The Medical Officers mentioned in Form-" B" are being given temporary posting under the said order as they have failed the Post Graduate Diploma course. Medical Officer
