The Proposal:
Separate arrangements have been made at the Central Hospital, Ulhasnagar-3, G. Thane and some parts of the nearby Red Cross Hospital to deal with the disaster of COVID-19. The supply of oxygen is continuously being disrupted at high pressure for suspected and affected patients of Covid-19, as well as for other essential surgeries and critical patients. In the above circumstances, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to allow the Central Hospital, Ulhasnagar-3, to bear the expenses incurred for refilling of oxygen cylinders from the funds available in the private provident fund. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
Government Decision: Central Hospital, Ulhasnagar-3 has been allocated Rs. 2,48,780/- is being allowed to be spent from the funds available in the self-financing account as follows:
