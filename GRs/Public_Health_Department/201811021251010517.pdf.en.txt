Preamble: To continue the food service in a total of 495 hospitals under the Public Health Department, Rs. Administrative sanction of Rs. 47.28 crore was under consideration.
A total of 495 hospitals, including rural hospitals, sub-district hospitals, general hospitals, women's hospitals, and district hospitals, under the Public Health Department will be provided with food services. The food service is Rs. 70/- per patient per day subject to the condition that e-tendering process is carried out and the cost is met from the funds available during the financial year 2018-19. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 47.28 crore.
The daily diet is as follows:
