The Proposal:
A total of 34 blood banks are functioning in the public health department in the state and about 1.5 lakh blood bags are collected every year through these blood banks and blood is supplied to the needy patients. In this regard, the Govt. As per the circular dated April 27, 2015, the service charge rates for blood bags have been fixed. Considering the convenience of the general public and the needs and convenience of the general patients due to the COVID-19 epidemic in the country, the government was considering providing blood bags and blood components in the blood bank free of charge to the patients admitted in the medical college and government hospitals of the Public Health Department and the Department of Medical Education and Medicines.
Government's decision:
For the year 2020-21, the blood bags and blood components in the blood bank will be made available free of cost to the patients admitted in the Medical College Salangan Government Hospital of the Public Health Department and the Department of Medical Education and Medicines. The expenditure on this should be met from the funds received for this purpose in the Project Implementation Plan (PIP) for 2020-21 under the National Health Mission. This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
