The Proposal:
As per the recommendation of the Gabha Committee headed by the Chief Secretary, to reduce the infant mortality rate in 16 tribal districts, the government was considering the purchase of necessary vehicles for the district health officers and district surgeons in 16 tribal districts. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
Government's decision:
In order to reduce the infant mortality rate in 16 tribal districts of the state, administrative approval is being given to the District Health Officers and District Surgeons to purchase the following vehicles from the funds made available by the Tribal Development Department for field visits:
