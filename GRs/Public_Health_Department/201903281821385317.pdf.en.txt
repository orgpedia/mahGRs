The grant has been made available for the financial year 2017-18 vide order dated 16th June, 2017. According to the Under the Government Order dated 1st February, 2018, a grant of Rs. 1597.51 lakh was sanctioned to the Institute. However, since a sum of Rs. 1519.36 lakh has been disbursed till the end of March 2018, the Government was considering the release of the balance subsidy on account of the Central Government. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
The Government have decided to disburse the balance grant of Rs. 78.15 lakh (Rupees Seventy Eight Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) under the Tertiary Care Cancer Centre (TCCC) General Scheme at Vivekananda Medical Foundation and Research Centre, Latur under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke for the financial year 2017-18.
