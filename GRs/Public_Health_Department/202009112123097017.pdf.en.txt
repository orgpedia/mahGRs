The Proposal:
In view of the increasing prevalence of COVID-19 virus in the State, the rates to be charged by the private hospitals for testing of COVID/ Non-COVID patients as well as by the private laboratories for RTPCR, Rapid Antigen, Rapid Antibody tests etc.
The Notifications dated 30.04.2020, 21.05.2020 and 31.08.2020 prescribe the rates for various treatments/ investigations. However, in addition to these tests, patients may need to undergo a CT scan. It has come to the notice of the Government that complaints are being received from patients/ public representatives regarding charging of exorbitant amount by private hospitals or Tapasani centres where CT scan testing facilities are available for these tests.
Therefore, the matter of forming a committee to fix the rates of HRCT tests was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
Since CT Scan is required for treatment of various diseases including COVID, the following committee is being constituted to fix the charges for HRCT test by private hospitals/ diagnostic centres having HRCT facility in the State.
