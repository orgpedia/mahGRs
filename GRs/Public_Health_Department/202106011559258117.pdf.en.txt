Date is June 22, 2021.
1) Letter from Joint Director, Health Services Hospital (State Level), Mumbai No. 04.05.2021
Preamble: It is under consideration of the Government to allow the annual maintenance contract from the balance in the private endowment account of the hospital for regular inspection of the earthing as per the standard of the medical department of the hospital functioning under the Public Health Department. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision:
There should be no incidents of fire due to technical failure in electrification in hospitals under the Public Health Department. For this, permission is being granted to enter into an annual maintenance contract with the Power Division of the Public Works Department for regular inspection of the Earthing as per the standards of the Education Department. The expenditure is also being approved from the balance in the hospital's personal provident fund account.
Terms and Conditions 1) The concerned officer will be responsible for strictly complying with the terms and conditions of the contract with the Public Works Department.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the officer concerned to strictly comply with the instructions/ government decisions/ circulars/ rules issued by the Finance Department from time to time while making expenditure from the said personal provident account.
3) Make sure that the payment is correct. This will be the responsibility of the district administration.
