Preamble: In order to increase the undergraduate and postgraduate intake capacity of the Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambajogai, and to meet the standards of the National Council of Medical Sciences, the Government was considering the provision of six rooms, a lecture hall, and a sanitary house to the Sub-District Hospital, Kej G. Beed Institute.
A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: In view of the increase in the undergraduate and postgraduate admission capacity in the Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambajogai, and in accordance with the standards of the National Council of Medical Sciences, administrative approval is being given to temporarily provide the sub-district hospital, six rooms of the Kej G. Beed Institute, a lecture hall, and a sanitary house to the Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambajogai for the next three years.
2. The Dean, Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambajogai has sanctioned Rs. The said six rooms, one lecture hall and sanitary house at Beed should be handed over to the District Surgeon, Beed after 3 years. The Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Circle, Latur has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Dean, Swami Ramanand Teerth Rural Government Medical College and Hospital, Ambajogai, in this regard. The said six rooms, one lecture hall and a toilet at Beed should be allowed to be used for 3 years.
