Government's decision:
Out of 20166 temporary posts under National Malaria Eradication Programme, 16133 temporary posts have been made permanent as per Government decision dated 30/4/2012. The remaining 4033 temporary posts as well as the revised figure left out of the review of the posts will be filled by the Public Health Department Government Decision No. A total of 4164 posts of Sub-Centre level 131 posts sanctioned under PO No.117/ Service-1, 2007/ PR No.117/ Service-1, dated 21/9/2012 were filled up from 1/01/2018 to 28/2/2018 under the aforesaid Act. No. 3 was continued as per the decision of the government. Since these posts are essential for the continuous delivery of health services to the people of the state, the continuation of these posts from 1/3/2018 to 30/9/2018 is being approved under this Government decision.
The cost of these posts should be spent under the head of accounts shown below and should be met from the approved grant for the year 2018-2019.
