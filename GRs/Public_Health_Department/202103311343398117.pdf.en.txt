The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State from the financial year 2017-2018 in partnership (60: 40) with the Central and State Governments.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component as Central Account (Recurring) for FY 2020-2021 2 orders of Rs. 54,90,31, 086/- (Rupees Fifty Four Crores Ninety Lakh Thirty One Thousand Eighty Eight only) has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 36,60,20, 724/- (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Sixty Two Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand Seven Hundred and Twenty Four only) is required to be disbursed for Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana. However, during the financial year 2020-21, an amount of Rs. 34,90,93, 879/- was under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
In order to effectively implement the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central Government has released Central share (Recurring) of Rs. 54,90,31, 086/- (Rupees Fifty Four Crore Ninety Lakh Thirty One Thousand Eighty Eight only) has been provided to the State. The State Government will contribute 40% of the total amount of Rs. 36,60,20, 724/- (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Sixty Two Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Seven Hundred and Twenty Four only). Of the Rs. 34,90,93, 879/- (Rupees Thirty Four Crores Ninety Lakhs Ninety Three Thousand Eight Hundred and Nineteen only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment of the grant made available under this government decision to the treasury, obtain the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
It should be certified that the subsidy disbursed under this government decision will be credited directly to the beneficiary's account after being deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
In the financial year 2020-2021," Demand No. R-1, 2211 Family Welfare, (00) 103 Maternal and Child Health, (02) Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Sahyog Karyakram, (02) (01) Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (State Share 40%) (Programme) (2211 1388), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) to be spent within the limit of the provision sanctioned under this head.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made in the prescribed form and the report of the progress of the scheme in the prescribed form should be sent to the Central and State Government from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the 2020-2021 fiscal year. The concerned office should ensure that no expenditure is incurred beyond the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana.
