The Proposal:
Government Circular No. Orders have been issued under Section 1 regarding the issue of permanency certificate to every temporary government officer/ employee in connection with the appointment to the first post. As per the provisions of this circular, the appointing authority has the power to issue the certificate of permanency in respect of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers.
The power to take decisions to bring dynamism in the administration as per the requirement is referred to in Circular No. Under Section 2, the power to issue a certificate of permanency has been delegated to the Secretary of the Administrative Department.
Considering the number of Medical Officers, Group-A (Pay Scale S- 20:56100-177500) cadre, the number of Medical Officers eligible for Permanent Certification every year is more.
Also, the power to grant the certificate of permanency is withdrawn as the proposals of the concerned medical officers take a long time to reach this administrative department through the prescribed route.
The matter is under consideration of the government. Accordingly, instructions are being issued to delegate the decision-making power on the subject of" grant of certificate of continuance in service."

Government circular:
The Medical Officers of Group-A (Pay Scale S- 20:56100-177500) cadre in Maharashtra Medical Health Service are issued Permanent Certificates as per the terms and conditions mentioned in Note No. 1 to the General Administration Department. As per the circular No. 2 issued in the General Administration Department, the power to issue the Permanent Benefit Certificate has been delegated to the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary of the Administrative Department. Accordingly, the powers of the Principal Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400001 to issue Permanent Benefit Certificate to Medical Officers Group-A (Pay Scale S- 20:56100-177500) under Public Health Department are being delegated to the Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai.
