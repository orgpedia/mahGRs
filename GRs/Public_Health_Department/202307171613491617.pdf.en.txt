However, in the Corona global epidemic, all the medical officers of the state provided preventive and curative services to the patients day and night. He worked day and night as a Covid warrior during the Corona period. Under-service Medical Officers desirous of admission to Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma Courses could not prepare for NEET PG/ Diploma Courses entrance exam due to Corona Pandemic situation in 2020 and 2021. Accordingly, the in-service Medical Officers of the Department have requested for relaxation in age limit for admission to NEET PG-2023 for PG/ Diploma courses. In a meeting held at the government level in this regard, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission decided to relax the upper age limit for the entrance examination of NEET PG-2022 and NEET PG-2023 in view of the Covid situation in the year 2020 and 2021.
