Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is a centrally sponsored scheme with 60% funding from the Central Government and 40% from the State Government. For the first quarter of the financial year 2018-2019 (1st April to 30th June, 2018), the first instalment of Rs. 29,11,21, 886/- (Rupees Thirty-nine crore eleven lakh twenty-one thousand eight hundred and eighty six only) has been received. The funds have been directly deposited by the Central Government in the Escrow Account opened in the name of Commissioner, Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai, said Additional Director, Health Services, Family Welfare, Pune, in reference no. 1 has been reported. In the Budget Estimates 2018-2019, Rs. A sum of Rs. 65.00 crore has been provided in the Budget. Accordingly, the State share under the general component for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is Rs. 19,40,81, 260/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Forty Lakhs Eighty One Thousand Two Hundred Sixty only) (40%) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.

The Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment to the treasury, receive the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
It is being certified that the terms and conditions contained in the Annexure to the Government Circular No. Budget-2018/ PR No. 69/ Finance-3, dated 2.4.2018 of the Finance Department are being fulfilled. It is also being certified that the funds disbursed under this government decision will be directly credited to the accounts of the beneficiary beneficiaries after being directly credited to the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
Public Health Department As per the provisions of the Government Memorandum No. Finance-2018/ PR. 96/ Budget, dated 10.4.2018, during the financial year 2018-2019, expenditure should be incurred within the limit of the provision sanctioned under the head" Demand No. R-1, 2211-Family Welfare (00) 103 Maternal and Child Health, (02) Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Sahyog Karyakram, (02) (01) Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (State Share 40%) (Programme) (22111388), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Monority)."
The said expenditure is conditional and the utilization of the expenditure made should be sent to the Central and State Governments in the prescribed form and report of the progress of the scheme from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the 2018-2019 fiscal year. The concerned should ensure that the expenditure on the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana does not exceed the grant received.
This Government decision is in accordance with the Government Circular No. 69/ 2018/ 69/ 3 of the Finance Department. being issued pursuant to the Consent received under 2.4.2018.
