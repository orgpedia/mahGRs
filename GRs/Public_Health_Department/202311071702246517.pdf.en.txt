Preamble: As per the decision of the Government dated 26.02.2019, both the schemes of Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) have been merged with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY) in the State. 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Helpers for the financial year 2023-24 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana
A total of Rs. 692 crore. For the beneficiaries of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, the premium for the quarter October, 2023 to December, 2023 is Rs. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 219,60,14, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Nineteen Crores Sixty Lakh Four Thousand only) to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable).
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, the premium for the beneficiary family under the scheme for the quarter October 1, 2023 to December 31, 2023 has been increased from the provision made in the budget for the financial year 2023-24 to Rs. 219,60,14, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Nineteen Crores Sixty Lakhs Fourteen Thousand only) to the State Health Guarantee Society.
