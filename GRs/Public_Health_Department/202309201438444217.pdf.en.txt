Preamble: The State Health Mission has been set up to implement the National Rural Health Mission programme in the State as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The main objective of the scheme is to provide quality health services to the people of the state. The Central Government vide letter dated 2nd March, 2017 has informed that all the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM), which were implemented during 2016-17, will be continued during 2017-18. Accordingly, all the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM) implemented by Public Health Department, Government of Maharashtra have been extended till the implementation of National Health Mission (NHM) from 1st April, 2017. A total of 04 temporary posts created at State level for implementation of National Health Mission (NHM) have been extended from time to time as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The temporary posts are as under: No. 16 decision of the government. The period has been extended up to 31.08.2023. Since the requirement of these 4 posts will continue, the matter of extension from 01.09.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
