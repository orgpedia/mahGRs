Government Decision: To repair the Doctors/ Nurses Residential Building and Staff Colony at Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Yerawada, Pune, to repair the dormitory 1,2,3, to paint and to construct a dining room for women at Female Section 1, the budget and plan of this work has been prepared by the Assistant Engineer (Class-2), RMH Department, Yerawada, Pune, and coordinated by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune, and the Superintendent, Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Yerawada, Pune.
The project is scheduled to be completed in 2019-2020. Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget and Plan outlay of Rs. 239.44 lakhs as under:
