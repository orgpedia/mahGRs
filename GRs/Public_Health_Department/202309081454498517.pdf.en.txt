Functions of the Board of Evaluation: 1. The Board of Evaluation shall be constituted by a person with significant disability or by any other person.
Or the organization will process the request application for getting the support as it needs higher support.
The assessment board shall invite the person with disability requiring higher support for assessment and if required, conduct medical examination.
The Board of Assessors shall make the assessment on the basis of points (A to U) in the following table:
