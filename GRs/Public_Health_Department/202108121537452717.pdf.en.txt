Presently, the Covid-19 pandemic is raging in the state. As a result, more patients are being admitted to the hospital. This puts pressure on the entire hospital system. In such a situation, it is very important to keep the supply of electricity in the hospital in order. Reference No. 18 above regarding the annual maintenance contract with the Electrical Department of the Public Works Department for regular inspection of the Earthing as per the standard of the Medical Department of the Hospital and to make this expenditure from the balance in the personal provident account. The decision was approved by the government.
However, due to the suggestion of the Finance Department to make some amendments in this government decision, the following decision is being taken in this regard.
Government Decision: In accordance with the Government Decision at Reference No. 2 above and Reference No. 3 (a) Expenditure on the Vidyut System from the balance in the private provident fund account as mentioned herein.

It's been approved. Accordingly, there should be no incidents of fire due to technical failure in electrification in the hospital under the Public Health Department. For this, permission is being granted to enter into an annual maintenance contract with the Power Division of the Public Works Department for regular inspection of the Earthing as per the standards of the Education Department. The expenditure is also being approved from the balance in the hospital's personal provident fund account.
Terms and Conditions 1) The Medical Superintendent of the concerned hospital will be responsible for strictly complying with the terms and conditions of the contract with the Public Works Department.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the officer concerned to strictly comply with the instructions/ government decisions/ circulars/ rules issued by the Finance Department from time to time while making expenditure from the said personal provident account.
3) The Department of Industry, Energy and Labor gives the government decision when spending from the private provident account.
Purchases approved under 01.12.2016 should be made as per the procedure laid down in the rule book.
4) Make sure that the payment is correct. The medical superintendent of the concerned hospitals will be responsible for this.
5) The District Surgeon/ Medical Superintendent shall verify the payment and make the payment before making the payment.
6) The cost of the proposal should be met from the personal provident fund account.
7) It shall be the responsibility of the Medical Superintendent/ District Surgeon of the concerned hospital to strictly comply with all the terms and conditions while paying these expenses.
8) Any irregularity in the payment of these expenses will be the responsibility of the District Surgeon.
9) Expenditure above the financial limit should be taken prior approval of the government.
10) If any irregularity is found in the above case, the entire responsibility will be of the concerned Medical Superintendent/ District Surgeon.
