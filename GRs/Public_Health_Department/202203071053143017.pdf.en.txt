The Proposal:
It has been decided to provide loan from financial institution HUDCO for construction of new and existing hospitals under the Public Health Department. Accordingly, it is the policy of the department to do this work from the loan available from HUDCO. 50-bed Sub-District Hospital, Mirzagaon. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs. 13.64 crore was under consideration of the Government.
The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
2. 50-bed Sub-District Hospital, Mirzagaon building at Ahmednagar. Construction of Rs. 13.64 crore has been received and the same is based on the Rate List for 2021-22. It is prepared by the Assistant Engineer, Grade-1, Public Works Sub-Division, Karjat. The Executive Engineer has been certified by Public Works Department, Ahmednagar, as well as the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Ahmednagar, Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nashik and the District Surgeon, Ahmednagar. Accordingly, the administrative approval of the Budget and Plans amounting to Rs. 13.64 crore is being accorded under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) While constructing the building of all government hospitals, the Chief Architect and the Chief Engineer (Architecture/ Vidyut) should check the budget and maps accordingly, to ensure that the planning of the hospital building is done in accordance with Part 4 Fire and Safety of the National Building Code vol-1 according to the fire safety norm.
