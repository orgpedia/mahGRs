(1) None of the above posts has been vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.
(2) Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.D. The terms and conditions prescribed as on 11.09.2019 should be scrupulously followed and action taken accordingly.
