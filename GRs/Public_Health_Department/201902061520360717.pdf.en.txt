A proposal has been submitted to approve the opening of a health sub-center in Mauje Soygaon T.G. Buldhana and a decision is being taken in this regard.
The government has decided to set up a health sub-centre at Soygaon in Buldhana district. Separate action will be taken regarding the construction and designation of the health sub-center on the said site after acquiring the site in the prescribed manner.
