The proposal-
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. In the financial year 2021-22, under the head 2210 G 888 (State share 40%), Rs. A sum of Rs. 31.00 crore has been allocated for the project. For the period from 01.04.2021 to 30.06.2021 and from 01.07.2021 to 30.09.2021, State share of Rs. 15,78,78, 508/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Seventy Eight Thousand Five Hundred Eight Only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.

Government's decision
For Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, State share (40%) is Rs. The Government have decided to sanction conditional release of funds to the tune of Rs. 15,78,78, 508/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Seventy Eight Thousand Five Hundred Eight only).
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer for the transfer of funds to the Society.
Government of the Department of Finance Decision No. 2021/ No. 48/ Finance-3, d. It is being certified that the terms and conditions as of June 24, 2021 are being met. During the financial year 2021-22, the expenditure under the head of 2210- Medical Services and Public Health 01- Civil Health Services- Differently Abled Care 789, Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, (01) Scheme under Scheduled Castes Component Programme, (01) (04)- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (State Share 40%) (Programme) 2210 G 888, 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) should be spent within the limit of the provision approved under this head.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the financial year 2021-22.
The concerned should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
The State Health Guarantee Society shall strictly comply with all the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated July 29, 2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance while spending the funds available under the Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan.
