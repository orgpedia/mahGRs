The Proposal:
As per Government Decision dated 17/06/2019, administrative approval has been accorded for procurement of machinery for Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Amaravati. Accordingly, the tender process was carried out by Haffkine Jeeva Drug Manufacturing Corporation Limited (Procurement Cell), which received zero response to the tender.
Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Health Services, vide Reference No. As the specifications prepared at the Directorate level are prepared by the experts of Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai, it is under the consideration of the Government to approve the procurement of the machinery required for the Cancer Department in the Health Institutions by the Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai through Haffkine Corporation on the order of supply of the machinery determined by the tender process.
Government's stand:
Government Decision dated 17/06/2019 in Term No. 11 should be read as follows:
" Since the specifications for the purchase of equipment required for the cancer department at the Divisional Reference Service Hospital, Amravati are the same as the machinery of the Tata Memorial Institute, the government is approving the purchase of the proposed machinery by Haffkine Biopharmaceuticals Corporation, Mumbai, on the supply order determined by the said institute (Tata) through the prescribed tender process," read the letter.
