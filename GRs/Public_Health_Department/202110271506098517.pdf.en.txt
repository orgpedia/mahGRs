2.After completing the post-graduate degree/ diploma course, Read No. The Medical Officers who have not joined the place of posting within the stipulated period after the revised posting under the Government Order 1, 2 and 3 are personally responsible for their absence and their absence is not due to administrative reasons but to their personal and private interest. Therefore, action should be taken to grant them extraordinary (unpaid) leave as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, without payment of any salary and allowances for the period of absence from the date of their discharge till they join the place of actual posting.
Also, if necessary, a detailed proposal of leave approval should be submitted to the government for approval.
(3) The medical officers who have been given revised postings under this order shall join immediately at the place of their revised postings. Disciplinary action will be proposed against the concerned medical officer as unauthorized absence if he does not join within the prescribed time.
4. All the terms and conditions mentioned in the order shall be applicable to the medical officers mentioned in the said table.
