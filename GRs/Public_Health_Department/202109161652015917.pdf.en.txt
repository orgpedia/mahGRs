The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State from the financial year 2017-2018 in partnership (60: 40) with the Central and State Governments.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component as Central Account (Recurring) for the Financial Year 2021-2022. 2 orders of Rs. 27,45,15, 543/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Crore Forty Five Lakh Fifteen Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Three only) has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 18,30,10, 362/- was under consideration of the Government.

Under this Government decision, a sum of Rs. 18,30,10, 362/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Thirty Lakhs Ten Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty Two only) is being sanctioned.
Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should take action to deposit the payment of the grant made available under this government decision in the treasury, obtain the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
It should be certified that the subsidy disbursed under this government decision will be credited directly to the beneficiary's account after being deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
