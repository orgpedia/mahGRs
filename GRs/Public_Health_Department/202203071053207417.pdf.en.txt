The Proposal:
It has been decided to provide loan from financial institution HUDCO for construction of new and existing hospitals under the Public Health Department. Accordingly, it is the policy of the department to do this work from the loan available from HUDCO. Accordingly, a 30-bed rural hospital, Pachora G. Jalgaon will be upgraded to a 50-bed sub-district hospital. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs. 28.55 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget for construction of 50 bedded Sub District Hospital Hospital Main Building by upgrading 30 bedded Rural Hospital, Pachora G. Jalgaon is based on the tariff list of 2020-21. Provision has been made for fuel gas pipeline, bio-digester, rain water harvesting, solar roof, protection wall and gate, internal roads, furniture, electrical, water supply and sewerage, fire prevention and lift etc.

According to the main building, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution to the Budget and Plans of Rs. 28.55 crore subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1) The technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on construction of main building of 50 bedded sub-district hospital by upgrading 30 bedded rural hospital, Pachora G. Jalgaon should be shared under Demand No. H-8, Head Accounts 4210 Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01-Civil Health Services, 110-Hospital& Dispensaries, (01)-Buildings, (01) (04) Large Constructions (HUDCO Loans) (Programme) (42103202), 53-Big Constructions.
