New Delhi: The coronavirus disease (Covid-19) has been wreaking havoc around the world. As the virus is spreading rapidly in the country and the state, it is necessary to take immediate measures to control the spread of the disease. Accordingly, various measures taken by the Public Health Department and the notifications and orders issued accordingly. It was placed before the Cabinet on April 7, 2020.
Further, considering the time taken to implement the tender process as per the State Procurement Policy for Emergency Procurement, the Government has approved the procurement at the district and state level keeping in view the emergency health situation, subject to the following terms and conditions.
Government's decision:
(i) The procurement policy of the State has been decided. However, in view of the present health emergency situation, there is a possibility of long delay in the tender process and fixing the price, etc., in the emergency situation, until the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897 and the National Disaster Management Act, 2005 are implemented, the committee has been constituted at the level of the Collector for district level procurement and this committee has been given the right to make purchases on the following conditions.
2. The procurement has been allowed to be made from the funds available at the district level under the National Health Mission, funds under the District Annual Plans, funds received from the National Disaster Relief Cell as well as grants received by the Department of Medical Education under the National Disaster Management Cell, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule and grants received from the Department of Medical Education.
3. The said purchase was made with the approval and recommendation of a committee chaired by the Collector, under the aegis of Haffkine Procurement Cell, Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, Central Government Undertaking H.L.
Life Care and other Government undertakings have been permitted to operate as per the norms of KAPL Life Care and other State Government Medical Services Corporations.
