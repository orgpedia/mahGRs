Preamble: In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to make available 40% State share of subsidy under the general category to the State Health Societies for the National Rural Health Programme under the National Health Mission.
Government's decision:
The Finance Department has approved the release of 40% State share for the National Rural Health Programme (NRHP) under the National Health Mission (NHM). To disburse the State share, out of the approved budgetary provision of 2023-24, State share account head 22105246 has been allocated Rs. 81200.00 lakh has been budgeted. Therefore, out of this provision, 40% of the State share for Infrastructure& Maintenance is Rs. An amount of Rs. 6801.23 lakh is hereby approved for disbursement to State Health Societies.
