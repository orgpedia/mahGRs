Preamble: As per the decision of the State Government dated 26.02.2019, it has been decided to merge the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. For the financial year 2020-21, an amount of Rs.2210.861 crore has been provided under the head of Accounts for implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. 100,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Crores only) has been provided in the Budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP) to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.07.2020 to 30.09.2020 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
Government Decision: As the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the budgetary provision under the head shown below for the financial year 2020-21 for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiary family under the scheme is Rs. 32,82,58, 653/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores Eighty Two Lakhs Fifty Eight Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty Five only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
