3. No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
4. All concerned are being made aware that the absence of the transferred officers, who do not comply with this time limit and remain absent from the place of posting, will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and their service will be interrupted in the alternative.
