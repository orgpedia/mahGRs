(1) The appointment of Physiologist in the Specialist Cadre in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A shall be subject to probation for a period of two years from the date of his assumption of the post.
If they do not achieve the desired standard of work during the probationary period or their conduct is found to be inappropriate, their service may be terminated or their probationary period shall be extended for a period of 1 year. If their work is not satisfactory during this period, their service will be terminated without any prior notice. Also, the increase in salary during the probation period will be admissible as per the rules prescribed by the government. They will not be eligible for any further increment till the completion of the probation period.
2) If the candidate wants to resign from the government service or leave the government job in any other way, he should give at least one month's prior notice to the government. They cannot retire from service without one month's salary.
During their service, they will be required to serve in such capacity in connection with the Defence of India or the Defence Services, as the case may be, for a period of four years (including training period) on such terms as may be prescribed by the Central Government. This liability shall be limited to the first ten years of service and shall not apply ordinarily after the age of 45 years.
4) They will have to undergo training as prescribed by the government.
(5) If they have not already passed or are not exempted from passing the examination as per the rules regarding Hindi and Marathi language examination, they will be required to pass the Marathi/ Hindi language examination of the said Board within the prescribed time.
6) General Administration Department, Government Decision No. 2000/ No. 61/2001/39, d. Knowledge of computer handling/ usage has been prescribed as an essential qualification for appointment in Government service under Section 7.8.2000. Accordingly, the General Administration Department, vide Government Resolution No. 2000/ No. 61/2001/39, d. Necessary certificate under 19.3.2003 shall be required to be made available to the Director, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai within the prescribed time from the date of appointment order.
7) Pay Scale of Rs. 15600-39100 Grade Pay Rs. 6600 and they will continue to be paid allowances as permissible under rules.
The salary of the candidates will be determined as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981.
