District Health Officer Dr. Balakrishna Somanal and others were present. As per the Government Order dated 09.08.2021, the post of Research Officer, State Family Welfare Office, Pune was transferred. However, as the post was not vacant, the Additional Director, Health Services (Family Welfare, Mother Child Care and School Health), Pune, issued a notification to this effect. As per letter dated 25.08.2021, a proposal was submitted for partial change in the posting of Dr. Kamble to the post of Assistant Director, Health Services (Family Welfare), Pune. Accordingly, Dr. Balkrishna Sopan Kamble, Research Officer, State Family Welfare Office, Pune is being transferred and posted as Assistant Director, Health Services (Family Welfare), Pune in partial reshuffle.
These orders are being issued with the approval of the competent authority.
These orders are being issued in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
