(4) The transferred officers shall be relieved of their duties forthwith to report at the place of their posting and the transferred officers shall report at the place of their posting immediately. The compliance report on the joining/ demitting office of the transferred officers should be submitted by the officer who has appointed/ relieved them immediately to the Director, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai and the Director, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall submit it to the Government.
No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise the responsibility shall be fixed on the competent authority to grant leave.
(6) The transferred officers shall, after demitting office, join the new post. All concerned are being made aware that the absence of an officer who does not comply with this time limit and remains mutually absent without being present at the place of posting will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and the possibility of interruption in his service cannot be ruled out.
The Director, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai shall initiate administrative action against the officers who do not report at the place of their posting immediately so that the officers may report at the new post as soon as possible.
