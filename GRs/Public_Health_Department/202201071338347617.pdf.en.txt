The Proposal:
Under the National Health Mission (NHM) approved action plan for 2021-22, under FMR code 1.3.2.6, Rs. 3708 crore has been approved. Accordingly, the Commissionerate of Health Services vide Reference No. The matter of granting administrative approval to carry out the tendering process for providing services by the service provider as proposed under Section 1 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The approved Action Plan for 2021-22 under National Health Mission envisages to implement the prescribed tender process for selection of service provider under FMR code 1.3.2.6 for 67 hospitals and 800 Primary Health Centres in the State for 3 years for providing Mechanized Cleaning Services from external system and an estimated cost of Rs. 3708.00 lakhs is being granted administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions. :
Terms and Conditions: 1) The requirement/ demand for the said Mechanized Cleaning Services should be ascertained. Thereafter, the tender process will be initiated.
2) It should be ensured that the specifications of the Mechanized Cleaning Services are as per the requirement and up to date. The technical specifications required

It should be as per the specifications agreed by the concerned experts (technical committee/ officials).
Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not designed in such a way that it will benefit any particular company. Also, while preparing this specification, the Central Government should also take into account the instructions mentioned in the relevant FMR Code while approving the PIP.
3) Cleaning services will be provided to 67 hospitals in a mechanized manner through service providers appointed at the level of Deputy Director. Also, the service provider for the primary health centre will be selected through a tender at the district level. These service providers will provide cleaning services in a mechanized manner for the selected primary health centres under the district. Also, the selection of primary health centres will be determined on the basis of the number of deliveries that take place.
(4) The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by government departments published under the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry 4, dated December 1, 2016.
Similarly, it should be ensured that the provision of the Finance Department, Government Decisions dated 02.02.2013, 02.12.2013 and 02.08.2016 and the Government Decisions and Guidelines issued by the Government from time to time are fulfilled in order to take these services from the external system.
While selecting the external service provider for this service, the minimum rates available on the GeM Portal, other state tariffs, National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA) should be considered. Also, if the services of these entities in Haffkine are being provided or have been, the rates should be considered as estimated rates taking into account the minimum rates received in the tender. It should be ensured that the price is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the supplier has supplied the item to other places.
6) Further action on the tender should be taken only after finalizing the existing tender documents of the Commissionerate through the Scrutiny Committee and the rules regarding the terms and conditions of service and the scope of work to be included in the tender document while selecting the external service provider for the Mechanized Cleaning Services.
The funds required for this project should be met from the grant sanctioned by the Central Government under the heading FMR Code 1.3.2.6 in the approved PIP for the year 2021-22 under the National Health Mission. Accordingly, the funds required for this purpose should be demanded from the Central Government in the PIP of the National Health Mission every year.
