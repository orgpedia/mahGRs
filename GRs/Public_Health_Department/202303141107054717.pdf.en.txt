Preamble: As per the decision of the State Government dated 26.02.2019, both the schemes of Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana have been merged with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. For the financial year 2022-23, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable), a total of Rs. 881,56,79, 000/- has been provided through Budgetary and Supplementary Demands. For the beneficiaries of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, the premium for the period April 2023 to June 2023 is Rs. A proposal for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 217,85,79, 000/- to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented by the State Health Guarantee Society, the insurance premium for the beneficiary family under the scheme for the period April 2023 to June 2023 will be paid in the budget for the financial year 2022-23
Out of the provision made under the head shown below Rs. 217,85,79, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Seventeen Crores Eighty Five Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
