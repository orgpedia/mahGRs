The proposal-
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. During the financial year 2021-22, under the head 2210 G 494 (State Share 40%), Rs. A sum of Rs. 15.00 crore has been allocated for this purpose. For the period from 01.07.2021 to 30.09.2021, the State share for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is Rs. 6,04,85, 651/- (Rupees Six Crore Four Lakh Eighty Five Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty One only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.
Government's decision
Out of the State share (40%) for Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Rs. The Government have decided to sanction conditional release of funds amounting to Rs. 6,04,85, 651/- (Rupees Six Crores Four Lakhs Eighty Five Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty One only).
