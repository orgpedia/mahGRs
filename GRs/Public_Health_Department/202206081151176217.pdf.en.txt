The Proposal:
The decision to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana has been taken as per the Government decision dated 26th February, 2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. For the financial year 2022-23, under the head 2210 G 449 (General), Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore has been allocated for this purpose.

Government's decision
The insurance premium for Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is Rs. The Government has approved the conditional release of funds to the tune of Rs. 53.22 crore (Rupees Fifty Three Crore Twenty Two Lakh only).
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society to be deposited in the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
Department of Finance, Government Circular No. Finance- 2022/ Q.No.43/ Finance- 3, Dy. It is being certified as meeting the terms and conditions as on April 04, 2022. Also, it is being certified that after the funds disbursed under this government decision are deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme, the funds will be paid to the insurance company as insurance premium of the beneficiaries of the scheme.
The funds should be spent within the limits of the provision approved in the financial year 2022-23 under the following head of accounts.
Demand No. R-1 2210- Medical Services and Public Health 01- Civil Health Services 110- Ophthalmology 07- Hospitals and Clinics 07- Ayushman Bharat Programme (07) (02)- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (Programme) (State Share 40%) 2210 G 449, 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried)
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the financial year 2022-23. The concerned should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
