Preamble- Regarding the suggestion regarding the shortage of medicine in the primary health center of Aurangabad district, Dr. On 16.07.2018, a discussion was held in the Legislative Council under Rule 93 of the Legislative Council. " In the absence of medical officers in the health centre, where the death of a patient due to lack of treatment is found due to the absence of medical officers on duty/ duty, the concerned medical officer will be immediately suspended and recommended for cancellation of registration with the MMC. In this regard, the government has issued a notification.
Circular- If it is found that the patient or the mother/ child died during childbirth due to lack of treatment due to the absence of a medical officer on government health center/ hospital duty without permission, the concerned medical officer will be suspended immediately and the Maharashtra Government will be responsible for the death of the mother/ child during delivery.
A recommendation will be made to the Medical Council (MMC) to cancel his registration. Also, disciplinary action will be taken against the medical officer who is absent from duty without prior permission or strong reason and his salary increase will be stopped.
