The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State from the financial year 2017-2018 in partnership (60: 40) with the Central and State Governments.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component as Central Account (Recurring) for the Financial Year 2021-2022. 2 orders of Rs. 25,59,12, 588/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crore Sixty Nine Lakh Twelve Thousand Five Hundred Eighty Eight only) has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 17,06,08, 392/- was under consideration of the Government.
