Preamble: The State Health Mission has been set up to implement the National Rural Health Mission programme in the State as per the guidelines of the Central Government. the people of the state
The main objective of the scheme is to provide quality health services. The Government of India vide letter dated 02.03.2017 has informed that all the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM), which were implemented during 2016-17, will be continued during 2017-18. Accordingly, all Centrally Sponsored Schemes under National Health Mission (NHM) implemented by Public Health Department, Government of Maharashtra have been extended till the time the National Health Mission (NHM) programme is implemented by the Central Government with effect from 01.04.2017. A total of 04 temporary posts created under the Government decisions of Reference Number (12) at the State level for implementation of National Health Mission Scheme were extended from 01.03.2021 to 28.02.2022. In this regard, the Joint Director, Health Services (Finance& Administration), Mumbai vide letter no. (13) has requested for extension of the said 04 temporary posts for the year 2022-2023 from 01.03.2022 to 28.02.2023 as they are still required. However, as per the Government Resolution No. 14 of the Department of Finance, the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend all existing temporary posts for a period of six months from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 only. Accordingly, a proposal to extend the tenure of a total of 04 temporary posts in the Commissionerates included in the figure of the Department was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under the Government Decision No. 14 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been given temporary posts included in their rolls from 01.03.2022 to 02.04.2022
