The Proposal: -
Various health schemes and programmes are being implemented by the government to reduce the maternal and child mortality rate in the state. However, medical, administrative, social, and systemic factors are found to be involved in maternal and infant mortality. It is, therefore, necessary to investigate each such incident in depth and find out the reasons behind it as well as take effective preventive measures to prevent such incidents in the future. Accordingly, a State Level Task Force under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Public Health
