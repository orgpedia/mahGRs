Introduction: Molecular Diagnostics Laboratory Sindhudurg has been established at Sindhudurg for the purpose of procurement of machinery, furniture, chemicals, glass equipment for the diagnosis of Monkey Fever, Dengue, Chikungunya, Japanese Encephalitis, Meningitis, Typhoid, Cholera, Diphtheria, Shigella Dysentery, Vial Hepatitis A and E and Leptospirosis. The approval has been granted pursuant to the Government Resolution dated 25th January, 2018.
In pursuance of the letter dated 26.02.2018 of the Deputy Director, the matter of sanctioning the funds sanctioned for the said procurement on short payment was under consideration as the said procurement was not made within the stipulated time. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: Equipment and Equipment for Sindhudurg Nuclear Laboratory Rs. A sum of Rs. 1.14 crore (Rupees One Crore Fourteen Lakh only) is hereby approved to be withdrawn on short payment subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) Government Circular No. 1017/ PROCESS- 38/17/ REGULATION D. As per notification dated 18.12.2017 (2210 0988), under the heading of this article, 3.09.2017 No further payment is pending.
2) All detailed payments made after 30.09.2017 on short payment shall be settled promptly.
3) The detailed payment of this short payment will be submitted within one month.
4) It is mandatory to withdraw the amount on short payment as the expenditure cannot be made through regular payment.
