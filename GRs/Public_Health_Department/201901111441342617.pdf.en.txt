the prelude
The Maharashtra State Labour Insurance Corporation has decided to form a Maharashtra State Labour Insurance Society. This was decided in the Cabinet meeting held on 24.04.2018. The government had constituted a committee to look into the matter.
Government's decision
The Maharashtra government has decided to form a committee to review the working of the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society. The composition of the committee is as follows:
(1) Ma. Minister of Public Health (2) Minister of Finance and Planning (3) Minister, Labour- Member (4) Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Labour Insurance Society Member Secretary.
(1) The Board of Directors shall meet once in every six months.
(2) To review and guide the decisions taken and the work done by the Board of Directors and the Executive Board.
(3) To give suggestions and guidance to the Board of Directors, if required.
