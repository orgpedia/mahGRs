The pending payment of honorarium to ASHA workers under Prerna Project-Farmer Counselling Programme is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) Before making the payment, the concerned District Surgeon shall verify and certify that the payment has not been made earlier and is being made only for payment of pending honorarium to ASHA workers under Prerna Project Farmers Counselling Programme.
2) Under the Prerna Pariyojana Farmers Counselling Programme, pending payments of ASHA workers for more than 1 year are being approved and in addition, the responsibility of all issues and statutory matters such as accuracy of payments, payments not paid earlier, etc. will be with the concerned District Surgeon.
The cost of the proposal shall be met from the approved provision under the head 10- Contractual Service (22100217).
