GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. PRASHAMA 2017/ PR No.15/ HEALTH 3
10th floor of the building,

Date is July 18, 2019.
Read: 1) Directorate of Health Services, vide letter No: SANASE/ Cell-3/ TE-6/ GRARUANSING30BATHABANDH/ 16 dated 21.12.2016.
Preamble: The Government was considering administrative approval for the construction of the hospital building at Rural Hospital, Ansingh G. Washim at an estimated cost of Rs. 1301.00 lakh.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. The budget for the construction of the hospital building at Rural Hospital, Ansingh G. Washim under the backlog is based on the 2015-16 tariff schedule. In the budget for this work, 4 percent of incidental expenses and service tax have been adjusted. If the work is to be done outside the budget, then a provision of 4 percent centage charges should be made in it. As per the Recapitulation Sheet attached with the said amendment, the administrative approval is being accorded to the Budget and Schemes amounting to Rs. 1332.52 lakhs subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
2) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.

3) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the needs of the health institutions, the necessary proposals for this should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
(4) As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department concerned. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director of Health Services Board in the Department of Health.
5) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
7) The Department of the Environment, in the course of actual work, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on construction of hospital building at Rural Hospital, Ansingh G. Washim under the backlog should be covered under Demand No. H-9, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health, 04- Public Health, 200- Other Programmes, (00) (01) Major Constructions (4210 0141), 53- Major Constructions.
4. The Director, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to make budgetary provision for the construction of the hospital building at Rural Hospital, Ansingh G. Washim, under the backlog.
These orders are issued under the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision, Finance Department, No. 2013/ No. 30/ Regulations, dated 17.04.2015 in Annexure, Part-I, Sub-Division-V, No.1, Paragraph No.134 and in the informal reference No.1 of Public Works Department. 642 c/ d.
