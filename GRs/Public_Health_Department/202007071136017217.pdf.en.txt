The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2020-21 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. 15,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores only) has been provided in the Budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.07.2020 to 30.09.2020 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
