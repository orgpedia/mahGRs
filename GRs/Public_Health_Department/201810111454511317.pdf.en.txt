The Central Government has enacted the Mental Health Act, 2017 and vide its notification dated 20th October, 2018 vide clause 46 (1) of the said Act, a State Mental Health Authority has been constituted in the State. The matter of nominating members to this authority was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
The following members are being nominated by the Government to the Mental Health Authority constituted under Section 46 (1) of the Mental Health Act, 2017.
