The Medical Officers posted under this Order shall join the place of their revised posting within 7 days. Disciplinary action will be proposed against the concerned medical officer if he does not join after the prescribed time.
3. The Medical Officers who have been given postings after passing the Post Graduate Diploma Course under this Order are required to serve the Government (i.e. in the health institutions under the Public Health Department only) for the prescribed period. In case of non-service of prescribed period by the Medical Officers mentioned in the said Form-" A," Form-" B"&" C," a sum of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) shall be liable to pay fine.
4. The Medical Officers who have failed in the post-graduate degree/ diploma courses proposed by the Commissionerate submitted through a letter in Vacha, have not been given revised postings under this order.
