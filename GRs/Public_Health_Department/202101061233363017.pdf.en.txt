Selection of Service Provider and Conditions:
It is proposed to select the service provider at the per patient rate through a tender process for a period of 3 years.
Assessing patients before starting peritoneal service, verifying the patient's home, providing counseling services to their relatives, visiting patients at home
Health care and monitoring.
To provide training to patients and their relatives and health care workers in the nearest health facility.
Providing timely transportation of dialysis fluid, peritoneal bags, medicines, etc. from DH to the patient's home.
Emergency visits to the patient's home.
Each patient should be provided with 90 peritoneal dialysis bags per month and each patient should have a buffer stock of 2 weeks.
