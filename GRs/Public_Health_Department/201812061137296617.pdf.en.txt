Introduction: As the rural hospital at Pombhurna G. Chandrapur has been sanctioned, the matter of shifting the primary health centre there to a nearby area was under the consideration of the government.
The following decisions have been taken in this regard:
Government's decision:
2.Thanks. The primary health centre is located at Chandrapur village. The relocation of Pombhurna to G. Chandrapur is being approved subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the Primary Health Centre can be operationalized at the relocated place.
2) After the rural hospital Pombhurna became operational, the primary health center in that place was Mauje Umri Potdar. should be shifted to Pomburana.
3) The temporary arrangement of the building in which the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
