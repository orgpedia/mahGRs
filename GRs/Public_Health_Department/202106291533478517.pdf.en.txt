Preface: Mouze Lohgaon, Ta. The government is planning to set up a primary health centre in Parbhani district.
Government Decision: The establishment of Primary Health Centre at Parbhani, District Parbhani is being approved under this Government decision subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The new premises shall have a building for the functioning of the Primary Health Centre and accommodation for staff. There will be separate arrangements for out-patient services, female patient check-up, delivery, staff seating, and parking if there is an ambulance.
(2) The building shall be equipped with water and electricity.
3) The new location should have all the facilities required for the said Primary Health Centre.
The District Planning and Development Board will be required to make provision for the construction of the main building and residence of the primary health centre at the new place from their district plan.
