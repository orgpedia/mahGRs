The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State from the financial year 2017-2018 in partnership (60: 40) with the Central and State Governments.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component as Central Account (Recurring) for FY 2020-2021 2 and 3 Rs. 82,35,46, 629/- (Rupees Eighty Two Crores Thirty Five Lakhs Forty Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Nineteen only) has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. The State share of Rs. 54,90,31, 086/- is required to be disbursed as 40% of the corresponding amount. Of these, Rs. 34,90,93, 879/- has already been disbursed. 19,99,37, 207/- (Rupees Nineteen only)

The matter of disbursement of funds to the concerned agencies was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In order to effectively implement the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central Government has released Central share (Recurring) of Rs. 82,35,46, 629/- (Rupees Eighty Two Crores Thirty Five Lakhs Forty Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Nineteen only) has been made available to the State. 40% of the State share of Rs. 54,90,31, 086/- out of which Rs. 34,90,93, 879/- has already been disbursed. 19,99,37, 207/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Ninety Nine Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Seven only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should take action to deposit the payment of the grant made available under this government decision in the treasury, obtain the required 40% of the funds in the state account and deposit it in the Escrow Account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
Joint Director (Finance and Administration) Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this purpose.
3. It is certified that the subsidy disbursed under this Government decision will be credited directly to the accounts of the beneficiary beneficiaries after it is credited to the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme.
