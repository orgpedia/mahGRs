Government's decision:
Under the National Tuberculosis Control Programme, 1310 posts have been created as per the decision of the Government under Reference No. 1, out of which 831 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 831 temporary posts, 80% of the 575 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the decision of the Government in Table 2 above. The remaining 264 temporary posts will be filled as per the Government decision of Reference No. 3. This has been extended up to 31.08.2020. In order to provide continuous health services, 264 posts are being approved to be continued from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
1) The expenditure of these posts should be spent under the head of accounts shown in front of the posts in the statement attached with the government decision and should be met from the approved grant for the year 2020-21.
(2) The decision of the Finance Department No. Post No. 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ A.P.C.D. As per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under 31.08.2020.
