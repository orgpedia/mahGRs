Term of Non-Official Members: Non-Official Members may resign voluntarily from the Council as long as the Council is in operation or for a period of three years, whichever is earlier. Such members will not be re-elected. The Chairman shall have the power to cancel or modify the election of non-official members.
The committee will have jurisdiction over the entire state of Maharashtra.
