At present, the rules and authority of the Government of Maharashtra as well as the rules of the State Workers Insurance Corporation, Delhi are not applicable to the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society as the society does not have separate financial rules and authority to handle the accounting affairs of the society. Chaired by A.V. In the review meeting of the Internal Accounts Branch of Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society held on 07/08/2023, it was directed to formulate separate financial rules and authority of Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society on the lines of the Operational Guidelines for Financial Management prepared by the Central Government for the National Health Mission. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to set up a committee to formulate separate financial rules and authority for the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society.
The Cabinet has approved the constitution of a committee to formulate separate financial rules and authority for the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society on the lines of the Operational Guidelines for Financial Management prepared for the National Health Mission by the Central Government.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: (1) To frame bye-laws in accordance with Rule 6 (b2) of the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society Rules& Regulations Annexure-D.
(2) To make financial rules and authority for the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society.
(3) To make rules for the establishment, purchase, medical care and accounting activities of the Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society.
(4) The said Committee shall meet at least once in a month or may be extended as may be necessary.
(5) If the retired medical officer attends the meeting, he will be entitled to honorarium and travel allowance from the fund of Maharashtra State Workers Insurance Society as per the rules of the government.
(6) The minutes of the meeting shall be submitted to the Executive Committee.
