Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to give administrative approval for sanction of payment by the District Surgeon, District Hospital Ahmednagar to the contractual security guards of rural hospitals and district hospitals for the period 2014-15 and 2015-16, as the payment was pending for more than one year due to non-availability of subsidy. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: District Surgeon, District Hospital Ahmednagar has decided to pay the pending dues of contractual security guards of rural hospitals and district hospitals for the period 2014-15 and 2015-16. Administrative sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 18,18,814/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Eighteen Thousand Eight Hundred and Fourteen only).
As per Regulation No. 30/ 2013/ Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015, the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-I, Sub-Division-III, No. 6, Rule No. 40, are being issued in the authority provided to the Administrative Department.
