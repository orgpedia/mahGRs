Preamble: In pursuance of the above decision of the Government dated 01.08.2019, administrative approval has been accorded to the proposal for expenditure on tendering of printing materials required for various programmes out of the grants sanctioned under various heads of accounts under PIP for the financial year 2019-20 under National Health Mission. This includes Rs. 17 crore available under the Revised National TB Control Programme (RNTCP) Procurement of Sleeves and Drug Boxes (FMR Code- 6.5.2). 162.671 lakh was sanctioned for the purchase of cardboard boxes for the delivery of anti-tuberculosis drugs to MDR/ XDR TB patients every month.
However, the new drug regimen will be implemented as per the guidelines issued by the Central TB Division, New Delhi on The Programmatic Management of Drug Resistant TB (PMDT). In this

The number of Type-B cardboard boxes will be reduced as the use of injection will be eliminated. Also, since the procurement process for FY 2018-19 and FY 2019-20 has not yet been completed, the boxes purchased in FY 2018-19 will be sufficient.
Thus, the remaining Rs. 162.671 lakh has been reallocated under FMR Code 6.1.1.18 a- Procurement of Equipment and the proposal for procurement of LED FM required for sputum sampling of TB patients is under administrative approval.
Government's decision:
There are 1520 DMCs in the state, out of which 188 have LED FM. In other microscopic centers sputum samples are examined by binocular microscope. It is stated that LED FM microscope enables more accurate detection in less time. As per the guidelines of the Central TB Division, New Delhi, a microscope centre is to be set up in every public health institution in the state. At present, a total of 334 LED FMs have been received in the state. However, as proposed by the program head, since the process of purchasing the card board given in the previous Government Decision No. 17 dated 01.08.2019 has not been completed, the administrative approval is being revoked and the tender process for the purchase of LED FM is being carried out as follows. :
