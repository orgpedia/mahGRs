The proposal was under consideration of the Government to amend the Medical Care Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued from time to time in the Medical Care Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued in this regard.
Government Decisions Review Committee is being constituted to review the Medical Care Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued from time to time in accordance with the present situation and to determine a comprehensive policy keeping in view the future.
Joint Director (Hospitals), State Level, Health Services, Mumbai Chairman 2) Deputy Secretary (R& B), SAV, Mantralaya, Mumbai Member 3) Deputy Secretary (Financial Reforms), Finance Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai Member 4) District Surgeon, Thane Member 5) Medical Superintendent, St. George's Hospital, Mumbai Member 6) Deputy Director (Hospitals), State Level, Health Services, Mumbai Member Secretary

The functions of the said Committee shall be as follows:
To draft revised rules in line with the existing health services by reviewing the Medical Care Rules, 1961.
(ii) To review all the Government decisions with regard to reimbursement of medical expenses and suggest amendments/ concrete policy in this regard.
3) To study the good policies of the Central Government (CGHS) as well as other states in order to determine the reasonableness of expenditure in respect of reimbursement of medical expenses.
4) To suggest measures to bring uniformity in the rate sheet as per CGHS without differentiating between Government recognized private hospitals and other private hospitals.
5) To suggest measures to avoid delay in reimbursement of medical expenses to government employees.
6) To formulate and suggest guidelines on the contingency of the disease and whether the disease is sudden/ severe.
7) To suggest the need for inclusion of new diseases in the list of types of diseases mentioned in the Government Decision dated 19th March, 2005 and the reasons for the same.
8) Suggest measures to implement cashless medical services, police and their family medical schemes or government employees on the lines of the insurance umbrella scheme of the Finance Department.
9) To seek necessary feedback from the government employees/ organizations in this regard and suggest amendments accordingly.
The final report of the Committee shall be submitted to the Government within three months.
