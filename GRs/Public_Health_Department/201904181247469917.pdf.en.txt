The Central Government has launched the One Stop Crises Centre scheme across the country to provide facilities under one roof for women in distress. The districts covered under the scheme are Thane, Raigad, Nashik, Pune, Satara, Ahmednagar, Aurangabad, Akola, Nanded, Amravati and Nagpur. As per the norms of the Central Government, under this scheme, space from the hospital premises in the said districts is expected to be made available for One Stop Crises Centre. The cost of construction of the centre will be borne by the central government. In this regard, the proposal of the Joint Director, Health Services, Mumbai, to make available the land at the District Hospital, Ahmednagar, for the Centrally Sponsored One Stop Crises Centre was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The decision was taken at the One Stop Crises Centre (OSC) at District Hospital in Ahmednagar district. I am giving my approval to the proposal to make available under this government decision to set up a One Stop Crises Centre.
