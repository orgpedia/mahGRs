The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government No. 1, an inspection committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Joint Charity Commissioner to examine the quality, quality and disease-specific tests and treatment provided to the poor and economically weak patients under the scheme for providing treatment to them by the hospitals of the institutions registered under the Mumbai Trust Act. The matter of cancellation of the appointment of non-official members appointed as per the Government Supplementary Letter in Reference No. 2 to the said Committee was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the Government letter at Reference No. 2, the appointment of non-official member Mr. Omprakash Shetye on the Scrutiny Committee is being cancelled under this Government decision.
