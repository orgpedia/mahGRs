The Proposal:
Rural Hospital, Aundha Nagnath. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs. 3.15 crore was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
2. The budget of the repair work at Rural Hospital, Aundha Nagnath G. Hingoli is based on the tariff schedule of 2020-21. The budget has made provisions for internal building repair, repair of windows, doors, floor, water supply, solar, paver block, electrification, etc. Further, provision of 4% Contingency Expenditure has been made and as per Recapitulation Sheet at Annexure A attached, the administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Pramadu 2022/ PR No.31/ Health 4
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the budget to be made available for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on the construction of the repair of the Rural Hospital, Aundha Nagnath G. Hingoli should be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health, 02- Rural Health Services, 04- Public Health, 104- Community Health Centre, (00) (01) Large Constructions (4210 0391), 53- Large Constructions.
4. The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate the amount required for the construction of the repair of Rural Hospital, Aundha Nagnath G. Hingoli in the budget.
These orders are issued under the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Government Decision, Finance Department, No. 2013/ Q.
