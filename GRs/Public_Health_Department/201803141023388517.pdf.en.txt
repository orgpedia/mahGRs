Government Circular- Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group A Ophthalmologists (Pay Scale Rs. 15600-39100 Grade Pay 6600) of Specialist Cadre Officers, whose Interim Seniority as on 1.1.2018 is being published in the accompanying Return-A.
The director of seniority, directorate of health services should bring this to the notice of all the concerned authorities. The objections of the officers in accordance with this seniority list should be submitted to the government within 1 month. A copy of the same should be sent to the Director, Health Services, Mumbai. The Director, Health Services should send their clear feedback on all the objections to the government immediately. If no objection is received within 1 month, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that no official has any objection to this interim seniority list.
