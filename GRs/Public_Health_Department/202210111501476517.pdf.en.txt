The proposal-
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. For the financial year 2022-23, under the head 2210 G 888 (State share 40%), Rs. 28.00 crore.
For implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) for the period from October, 2022 to December, 2022, State share of Rs. 9,99,41, 232/- (Rupees Nine Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Forty One Thousand Two Hundred Thirty Two only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.

Under this Government decision, conditional release of funds amounting to Rs. 9,99,41, 232/- (Rupees Nine Crore Ninety Nine Lakhs Forty One Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Two only) is being approved.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society for crediting it to the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.

Also, it is being certified that after the funds disbursed under this government decision are deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme, the funds will be paid to the insurance company as insurance premium of the beneficiaries of the scheme.
During the financial year 2022-23, expenditure under the head of 2210- Medical Services and Public Health 01- Civil Health Services- Differential Treatment 789, Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, Scheme under Scheduled Castes Component Programme, (01) (04)- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (State Share 40%) (Programme) 2210 G 888, 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) shall be incurred within the limit of the sanctioned outlay.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the financial year 2022-23.
The concerned should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
The State Health Guarantee Society shall strictly comply with all the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated September 22, 2022 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance while spending the funds made available under the Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan.
