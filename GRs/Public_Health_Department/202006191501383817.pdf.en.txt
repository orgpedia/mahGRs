Preamble: As per the decision of the State Government dated 26.02.2019, it has been decided to merge the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. For the financial year 2020-21, under the head of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable) amounting to Rs. 400,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Hundred Crores only) has been budgeted. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the State Health Guarantee Societies under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiaries of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana for the period from 01.07.2020 to 30.09.2020.
Government's decision:
