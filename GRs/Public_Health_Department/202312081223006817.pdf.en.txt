The Proposal:
The Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is a centrally sponsored scheme being implemented in the state with the participation of the Centre and the state from the financial year 2017-18. The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. As the Central Government has revised the form of the scheme in the year 2023-2024, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 for the financial year 2023-2024 for the benefit to be given to the second child. The Centre has sanctioned Rs. 1,40,19, 262/- (Rupees One Crore, Forty Lakhs, Nineteen Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty Two only) as 40% of the corresponding State share of Rs. 93,46,175/- (Rupees Ninety Three Lakhs Sixty Four Thousand One Hundred and Seventy Five only) (Account Head- 2211 1441) was under consideration of the Government for sanctioning the same to the concerned agencies from the budgetary provision of 2023-24.
Government's decision:
In order to effectively implement the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central Government has decided to extend the benefit of the scheme for the second child in the financial year 2023-2024.
