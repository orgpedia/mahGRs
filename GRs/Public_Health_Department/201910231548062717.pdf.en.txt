Government's decision:
Under the National Malaria Eradication Programme, the Department of Public Health has issued Government Resolution No. A total of 4164 posts, including 131 posts at Sub-Centre level, sanctioned under POS No.117/ Service-1, 2007, dated 06.08.2007, have been filled up from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 under the aforesaid Act. No. 2 was continued as per the decision of the government. The deadline has expired on 30.09.2019. Since these posts are essential for the continued provision of health services to the people of the State, the continuation of these posts from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 is being approved under this Government decision.
The expenditure of these posts shall be covered under the head of accounts shown below and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2019-2020.
