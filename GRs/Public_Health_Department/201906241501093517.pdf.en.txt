The functions of the aforesaid Permanent Medical Boards constituted in the State shall be as follows:
(1) All cases relating to abortion after 20 weeks shall be heard by the Hon'ble Supreme Court/ High Court for the advice of the Medical Board. The concerned pregnant mother will be examined by convening a meeting of the Standing Medical Board immediately within 72 hours in the context of the referral by the High Court/ District Court.
(2) The report on the condition of the pregnant mother and the medical termination of pregnancy examined by the Permanent Medical Board shall be submitted to the court concerned in a sealed cover within 48 hours.
(3) Before submitting the report to the Court, the following matters are required to be considered.
(a) Whether or not continuing the pregnancy would endanger the womans life? It can also cause serious physical and mental damage.
How or why?
