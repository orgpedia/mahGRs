Government's decision:
It is proposed to be developed as a District Public Health Lab for the purposes of epidemiological survey from the grant available in the approved PIP of 2018-19 under National Disease Survey Programme. Accordingly, the Joint Director of Health Services (Malaria, Elephant and Waterborne Diseases), Pune, has proposed to develop District Public Health Labs at District Hospital, Satara, Amravati, Parbhani and Thane.
