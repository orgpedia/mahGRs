Preamble- The Joint Director, Health Services, Hospitals (State Level), Mumbai has submitted a proposal vide letter dated 19.05.2017 for obtaining administrative approval for the purchase of 30 x 300 mA X-ray machine for the health institution in the State. Accordingly, approval for procurement of X-ray machines has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 11.07.2017. Accordingly, the tender process for the purchase of X-ray machines is in the final stage. The financial year 2017-18 has come to an end and the expenditure for the said purpose could not be incurred during the said financial year. Accordingly, the Joint Director, Health Services, Hospitals (State Level), Mumbai vide letter dated 20.10.2018 has proposed to grant fresh administrative approval to the said procurement as per the provisions of the procurement policy to meet the expenditure for the said purpose out of the grant available for the current financial year 2018-19.
Accordingly, the matter of granting revised administrative approval (extension) for the purchase of 30 300 mA X-ray machine for the health institution in the state was under the consideration of the government.
