Under this Government decision, a total of 78 temporary posts of GNM Nursing School (26 posts each) in Jalna, Raigad and Parbhani will be filled subject to the following conditions: 1.03.2018 to dt. The period is being extended up to 30.09.2018.
(1) None of the above posts has been vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.
(2) Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.D. The terms and conditions prescribed as on 05.03.2018 shall be scrupulously followed and action taken accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised design of the office finally approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts are not extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowance of the officers/ employees, the responsibility will be of the Director, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai.
