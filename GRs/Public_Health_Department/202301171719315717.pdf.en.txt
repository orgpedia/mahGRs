As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Official Duties Act, 2005, the post of District Surgeon, District Hospital, Solapur is being transferred on administrative grounds to Dr. Dhananjay Patil, the then District Surgeon, District Hospital, Osmanabad (presently without posting). Dr. Pradeep Dhele, District Surgeon, District Hospital, Solapur, will continue to hold the post of Deputy Director, Health Services, Pune Circle, Pune, till further orders.
2. Dr. Dhananjay Patil should be relieved immediately to appear at the new place of posting and the concerned officer should report to the Deputy Secretary (Services-2), S.N. Department, Ministry, Mumbai and the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai.
No leave should be granted to Dr. Dhananjay Patil, otherwise disciplinary action will be taken against the competent authority for granting leave.
4.After relieving Dr. Dhananjay Patil, he should join the new post during the permissible period. If he fails to appear at the place of posting within the stipulated time or remains absent, his absence will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and his service will be interrupted.
If Dr. Dhananjay Patil does not appear at the place of posting within the prescribed time, the Commissionerate should initiate administrative action against him immediately and submit the proposal for the approval of the government as per the need. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
