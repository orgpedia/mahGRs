The Proposal:
It has been decided to provide loan from financial institution HUDCO for construction of new and existing hospitals under the Public Health Department. Accordingly, it is the policy of the department to do this work from the funds available from HUDCO. Accordingly, a 30-bed rural hospital, Katol G. Nagpur will be upgraded to a 50-bed sub-district hospital. 13.18.A proposal for administrative approval of budget and maps amounting to Rs. 13.18 crore was under consideration.
Government's decision:
The budget for construction of 50 bedded Sub District Hospital Main Building by upgrading 30 bedded Rural Hospital, Katol, G.Nagpur is based on the tariff list of 2021-22. The budget has earmarked 5 per cent for internal and 6 per cent for external electrification. A provision of 4% Contingency Expenditure has also been made and as per the Recapitulatin Sheet attached, the administrative approval is being granted under this Government Decision subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions: 1) The technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) The planning of the hospital building should be done according to Part 4 Fire and Safety in the National Building Code vol-1, according to the fire safety norm while constructing the building.
10) No further e-tendering shall be carried out without verifying the map used for the said building.
The expenditure on this work will be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services- 110, Odd-Even Medical Services- (01), Hospitals and Dispensary Buildings, (01) (04) Large Constructions (Programmes) (Loans from HUDCO) (42103202), 53- Large Constructions.
