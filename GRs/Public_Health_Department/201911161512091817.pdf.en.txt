Preamble- Under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central and State Governments have made available Rs. 17.70 crore. A proposal for grant of administrative approval for purchase of QR Code Reader Machine for an amount of Rs. 32,55,500/- has been received under the Reference Letter. The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) has added a new feature to its Aadhaar card. This new digitally signed QR Code can only be read using the Unique Identification Authority of India Windows based custom client for desktop/ laptop. Also, the authenticity of the digital signature of the Unique Identification Authority of India is verified. The central government. As per the letter dated 1.3.2019, States/ UTs have been advised to procure QR Code Scanner as prescribed by the Unique Identification Authority of India. In order to make the benefits of Pradhan Mantri Matritva Yojana accessible to the concerned beneficiaries, it is necessary to purchase a quality certified QR Code Scanner to scan Aadhaar as per the directions of the Unique Identification Authority of India. Accordingly, the government was considering granting administrative approval for the purchase of a total of 383 QR Code Reader Machines. The following decisions are being taken:
Joint Director, Health Services (Procurement Cell), Mumbai. In pursuance of the proposal submitted vide letter dated 16.10.2019, under Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) programme, a QR Code Reader Machine (total 383 nos. 8,500/-) available for purchase in the financial year 2019-2020.
