Government Circular dated 21st December, 2018 Government Circular: The final seniority list of officers of the District Surgeon Cadre in Group A (Pay Scale S: 23 67000-208700) of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services has been published vide Government Circular dated 1st January, 2011 indicating their respective seniority. Now, the provisional seniority list dated 1st January, 2021 has been prepared and annexed as Annexure-A to this circular.
All the concerned officers should ensure that the information displayed against their names in the seniority list published as above (such as date of birth, educational qualification, category, date of appointment, date of promotion, whether the appointment is by promotion/ nomination) is correct or not.
If there are any objections/ objections/ suggestions, etc. regarding the information shown in the seniority list, the concerned officers should submit their written statement along with appropriate evidence (certified/ attested copies) to the government within 1 month from the date of publication of this circular in the prescribed manner. Action will be taken to publish the final seniority list after considering the representations received during this period.
If the necessary written representations as mentioned above are not received by the Government within the prescribed time limit, it shall be deemed that the information and seniority shown in the said interim seniority list is acceptable to the concerned authorities and they have no objection thereto and the final seniority shall be published as soon as practicable.
5. Copies of the said Seniority List are being sent to all the Heads of Offices, Heads of Departments and Heads of all other Health Institutions. They are requested to bring this seniority to the notice of all concerned officers working under their control. If there are any genuine errors and omissions in it, they should be verified in accordance with paragraph 3 above and submitted to the government.
