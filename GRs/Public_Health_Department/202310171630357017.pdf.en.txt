Preamble: In pursuance of the above Government decision, the temporary posts in the Health Institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Circle, Latur were extended till 31.08.2023. Since the said extension has expired and the posts are no longer required, the matter of extension of the said posts from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Provisional posts in health institutions under the control of Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Division, Latur. The period has been extended up to 31.08.2023. With the expiry of the said extension, the Commissionerate of Health Services issued the above Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to extend the tenure of these posts.
2. Provisional posts in health institutions under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Circle, Latur, as mentioned in Annexure-A annexed to this Government Decision. 01.09.2023 to dt. The period is being extended up to 29.02.2024.
The salary of these temporary posts should be spent in the next accounting year and this expenditure should be met from the approved grant for the year 2023-2024.
