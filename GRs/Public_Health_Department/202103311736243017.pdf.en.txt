For the financial year 2020-21, under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP) for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, Rs. 100,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Crores only) and by way of Supplementary Demand in December, 2020 to the tune of Rs. 41.11 Crores. A sum of Rs. 141.11 crore has been allocated for the project. Out of this amount, a total of Rs. 97.80 crore has been disbursed to State Health Guarantee Societies. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse the funds to the State Health Guarantee Society within the revised estimate limit under head 2210 G 861 (SCP) for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.04.2021 to 30.06.2021 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, it is necessary to pay the insurance premium of the beneficiary family under the scheme.
