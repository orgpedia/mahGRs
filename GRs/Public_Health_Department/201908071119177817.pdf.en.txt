The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State on 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State from the financial year 2017-2018.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component for 1st Quarter of Financial Year 2019-2020 2 orders of Rs. 32,78,26, 994/- has been provided by the Central Government to the State. The grant has been directly credited to the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 21,85,51, 336/- was under the consideration of the Government for the distribution of subsidy under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2019-2020, for the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the budget of the Department under the head 2211 1388 has allocated Rs. 65,83,52, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand only).
