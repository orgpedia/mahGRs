The Proposal:
The Central Government vide its letter dated 01.11.2021 has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission." The importance of primary health care, strengthening public health institutions to deliver comprehensive primary health care, including health education and prevention, strengthening institutions of public health governance, as well as critical care services, including comprehensive diagnostic and treatment capacity, is a prerequisite for implementing the above programs to address the challenges posed by current and future pandemics/ pandemics. Rural and urban local bodies to provide health services
To ensure this, the Central Government will provide financial assistance for the period from 2021-22 to 2025-26.
