Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to declare" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the office of Molecular Diagnostic Laboratory, Sindhudurg. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the authority conferred under Rule-2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No. 3 in Annexure to the Government Decision No. VYPR-2013/ PR. No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 of the Department of Finance, the post of" Chief Medical Officer" for the office of Molecular Diagnostic Laboratory, Sindhudurg is being declared as" Head of the Office." Further, as per the provisions of Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the post of" Administrative Officer, Class-II" is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
