Introduction- In order to enhance the quality of health services provided to all pregnant women and newborns and to provide dignified and attentive care, the Central Government has taken an important policy decision to start obstetrics services. The guidelines for obstetrics services in India were launched in 2018.
The team of State Obstetrics Teachers is selected by the Deputy Director, Nursing, Mumbai and sent to the National Institute of Training for training. As mentioned in the guidelines of the Central Government regarding obstetrics services, a State Midwifery Task Force and State Midwifery Action Group with the help of National Midwifery Task Force were under the consideration of the Government.
The State Midwifery Task Force and the State Midwifery Action Group are being constituted under this decision.
