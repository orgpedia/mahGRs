Preamble: It is under consideration of the Government to grant ex-post facto approval for payment of pending dues for the period April, 2019 to December, 2019 for the contractual security guard service provided at Rural Hospital, Sihora G. Bhandara. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: The contractual security guard service provided at Rural Hospital, Sihora, G. Bhandara was terminated in the month of May, 2019 for the period April, 2019 to December, 2019. Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation Limited. The Pune-based supplier has paid Rs. Ex-post facto approval is being accorded for payment of pending dues amounting to Rs. 4,17,859.
Terms and Conditions 1) The concerned officer will be responsible for strictly complying with the terms and conditions in the previous work order and contract.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the concerned officer to strictly comply with the instructions/ government decisions/ circulars/ rules issued by the Finance Department from time to time while clearing the pending payments.
