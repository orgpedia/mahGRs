The Proposal:
Leakage prevention plan at Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Nashik and other minor repairs worth Rs. The proposal for grant of administrative approval to the preliminary budget of Rs. 46,19,000/- was under consideration of the Government. The decision is as follows:
Government's decision:
The cost of making leakage prevention plan and other minor repair works at the Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Nashik is based on the budget for the year 2019-2020. Deputy Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik; Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik; Deputy Director Health, Nashik and District Surgeon, General Hospital, Nashik. The preliminary budget of Rs. 46,19,900/- is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) The detailed budget and plan of the said work should be approved by the concerned Chief Engineer, Public Works Department.
2) While preparing the detailed budget, the Goods and Services Tax (GST) should be levied at the rate applicable.

(3) The work shall require the technical approval of the Chief Engineer of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
4) The provision of furniture and electrical works should be shown in detail and the rates for the same should be submitted to the government by submitting the break-up.
5) Before starting the construction, the map should be approved by the architect, S.B. Department Nashik.
(6) A single tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned in the tender without separate tenders for each work.
7) As it is possible to increase the cost of the proposed work according to the situation of the area and the needs of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budget of these works will be technically approved by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department. While granting such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Directors of the respective Health Service Boards of the Health Department.
9) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
11) The Department of the Environment, in the course of direct work, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
12) Ensure that the above-mentioned tasks are completed within this amount.
Expenditure on leakage prevention plan and other minor repair works at Divisional Reference Service Hospital, Nashik should be shared under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance)- Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Dispensaries, (1) (a) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major Constructions (42100014), 53- Major Constructions.
