A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse the required funds to the State Health Guarantee Society out of the remaining undistributed funds under the head of Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, it is necessary to pay the insurance premium of the beneficiary family in the first and second phase under the scheme, as well as to cover other administrative expenses on the scheme, for the year 2018-19.
Out of the budgetary provision of Rs. 230,60,09, 304/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Thirty Crores Sixty Lakhs Nine Thousand Three Hundred and Four only) to be transferred immediately to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
It is being handed over to the Controller. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
