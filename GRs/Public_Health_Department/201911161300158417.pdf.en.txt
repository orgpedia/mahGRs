The Proposal:
Dr. Rahul Murlidhar Joshi, Medical Officer, has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. The appointment was made in the year 2003 as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
As per the decision of the Government under the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probationary period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two years probationary period satisfactorily, the matter of termination of probationary period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
