The Government was considering sanctioning the necessary expenditure for the purchase of 10. 32 seater non-AC buses from the grant provided by the Central Government for the strengthening of GNM Nursing Training Centre.
Government's decision:
And for the strengthening of GNM Nursing Training Centre, a total of Rs. 1,84,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakhs Only) is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
Vehicles will be provided on priority to the Nursing Training Centres which do not have vehicles.
The Deputy Director (Nursing), Health Services, Mumbai, shall ensure this and issue orders for the allotment of vehicles to the training institutes.
