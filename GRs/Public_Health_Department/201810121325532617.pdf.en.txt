Read: 1) Directorate of Health Services, vide letter No. SANASE/ Cell-3/ TE-6/ SaruvashiReforms/ 7101-02/16 dated 06.05.2016.
Preamble: A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps for the repair work of the District General Hospital, Washim at an amount of Rs. 42.11 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. The budget of the repair works at District General Hospital, Washim is based on the district table for the year 2016-17. The budget has proposed a 4 per cent contingency cost, 5 per cent cent centage charges and 12 per cent Goods and Services Tax. If the work is to be done outside the budget, then a provision of 4 percent centage charges should be made in it.
In view of this provision, the following works with budget and plan amounting to Rs.46,78,00/- are being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
2) All works should be e-tendered together without separate tenders for each work.
3) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director of Health Services Board in the Department of Health.
6) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on repair works at District General Hospital, Washim should be shared under Demand No.H8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01 Civil Health Service (Medical Assistance)- Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Clinics, (1) (a) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major Constructions (42100014), 53- Major Constructions.
The Director of Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the repair work at District General Hospital, Washim.
