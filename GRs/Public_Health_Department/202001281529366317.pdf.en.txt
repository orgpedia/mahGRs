The Proposal:
The increasing number of vacancies due to discontinuation of temporary appointments and non-availability of regular medical officers in the required number of vacancies in rural/ tribal/ remote areas is adversely impacting the public health system.
It is mandatory to fill up the vacant posts of medical officers in the health department in order to keep the health services running smoothly. The government has decided to appoint medical officers on contract basis in the vacant seats in the year 2015. Accordingly, 1188 Contract Medical Officers have been appointed. Also, the contract medical officer of the Public Health Department will be paid an honorarium. Their remuneration has been enhanced as per the Government decisions dated 13.06.2019 and 04.07.2019. Accordingly, the remuneration of the said Contract Medical Officers under head 2210 0146 for the following 10 Contractual Services was increased from Rs. 56,16,84, 000/-
