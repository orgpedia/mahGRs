The government order said that due to the large number of vacancies in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A Joint Director, Health Services and Deputy Director, Health Services, it is affecting the health services.
There are administrative problems. Therefore, the matter of filling up the posts in this cadre on a temporary basis by downgrading the rank was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the posts of Joint Director, Deputy Director, Health Services are being upgraded and the following officers are being transferred. :
