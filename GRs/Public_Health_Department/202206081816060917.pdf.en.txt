2. Security guards to be hired by outsourcing as indicated in the head above
Administrative approval is being granted for the service subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) By measuring the office with the help of the Public Works Department and the number of security guards accordingly
It should be fixed.
(2) When working through an external agency, the Government has issued Decision No. SGA-1506/ No. 91/ Work-5, D. 08.11.2006
It should be done as per the rules.
3) Preference should be given to hire security guards from MESCO/ Security Board approved by the government.
If the security guard service is not available from them, the e-tendering process for the hospital should be carried out in the prescribed manner.
The selection of the external system should be done in a very transparent manner.
4) The service provider should be identified at the state level or department level or district level.
5) The maximum number of security guards allowed for the hospital is limited to local requirements.
The number should be approved by the concerned district surgeon.
6) Payment of next month's salary of security guards without ensuring that it is credited to their bank accounts
The condition that will not be done should be included in the Tender document.
7) The commissioning orders should be issued only after adequate provision is made in the budget for service delivery.
