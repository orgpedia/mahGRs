The Proposal: -
A number of health-related programmes are implemented under the Public Health Department.
At present, there is an increase in many health-related programs as well as administrative under the department.
The scope of work has increased. Hence, the functioning of the department was smooth in the changed scenario.
Director (1), Health Services, Mumbai and Director (2), Health Services, Pune for more efficient functioning.
It was under the consideration of the government to make changes in the work to be done by them.
The government's decision:
The functions of the Director (1), Health Services, Mumbai and the Director (2), Health Services, Pune are as follows:
The determination is being approved by this government decision.
