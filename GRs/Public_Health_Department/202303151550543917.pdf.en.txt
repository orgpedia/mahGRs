6. d. 24.10.2021 and dt. Candidates who have appeared for the examination held on 31.10.2021 and are directly affected due to the cancellation of the recruitment process are being identified as eligible candidates.
The candidates who have appeared in the physical test will have to pay the examination fee for the present examination or refund the examination fee paid by them after checking the available database. Separate instructions will be issued on the website of the department as well as in the advertisement regarding the filling of the application form according to the computer (software) system provided by the company ON (Tata Consultancy Services Limited).
7. Format of Examination- To fill up the posts in the technical/ professional cadre associated with the department. As per the guidelines issued in the Government decision dated 04.05.2022, for the posts in the technical/ professional cadre, 80 per cent of the marks will be related to the technical/ educational qualification associated with the post and the remaining 20 per cent of the marks will be related to Marathi, English, General Knowledge and Mathematics.
