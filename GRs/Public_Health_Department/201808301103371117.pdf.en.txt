As per the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and rules prescribed from time to time, the MGM-run Medical College and Hospital, Medical Centre and Research Institute, Aurangabad, has been allowed to reimburse the medical expenses of the state government employees and their families for the treatment and surgery of heart surgery cases, heart bypass surgery, angioplasty surgery, cancer diagnosis and surgery and kidney transplantation. The following conditions must be met:
