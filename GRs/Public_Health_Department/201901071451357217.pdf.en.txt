The Proposal:
In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to provide 60% Central share for General Category (General) and 40% State share subsidy to State Health Societies for National Rural Health Programme under National Health Mission.
