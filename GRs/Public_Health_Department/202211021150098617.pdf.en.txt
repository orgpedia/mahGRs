The Proposal:
30 bedded Rural Hospital, Dhanora G. Gadchiroli to be upgraded to 50 bedded Sub District Hospital, Rs. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs 873.27 lakh was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The budget for construction of building for 30 bedded rural hospital, Dhanora G. Upazila Hospital, Gadchiroli with 50 beds is based on the schedule of 2020-21.
The budget of this work includes provision for fuel gas pipeline, bio-digester, rainwater harvesting, solar roof, furniture, electrification, water supply and sewerage, fire prevention, etc.
Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
