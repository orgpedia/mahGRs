Introduction: As there was no space available in Trimbakeshwar G. Nashik, the matter of changing the location and sanctioning the primary health center there was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: 2. Due to the unavailability of space in Trimbakeshwar G. Nashik, the location was changed to the Primary Health Center Mauje Dahlewadi (Vijayanagar). Approval at Trimbakeshwar G. Nashik is being granted subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be mandatory for the concerned Gram Panchayat to provide free space so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the relocated place.
2) 24 hours water and light arrangements, sanitation, etc. should be available.
3) The District Planning and Development Board is required to make provision for the construction of the said health sub-centre from its District Plan.
