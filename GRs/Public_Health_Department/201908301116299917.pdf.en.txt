The administrative approval for the construction of State Level Drug Stores and Training Centre etc. sanctioned for Aurangabad district under National Health Mission is being given subject to the following terms and conditions as per Recapitulation Sheet attached to the Budget and Plan amounting to Rs. 3011.92 lakhs.
Terms and Conditions:
1) Before starting the work, the sample map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
2) When working on a loosely held provision, the work should be undertaken by making a detailed budget.
3) The Department of the Environment in the actual work, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1, D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
4) The cost of construction should be met from the available budget.
5) Public Works Department's Government Decision No. BDG 2017/ No.
60/ Building- 2, D. Action to be taken as on 07/07/2017.
6) The tender notice of the work should be published after receiving the certificate of possession of the planned place.
The expenditure on this proposal should be met from the funds received under the National Health Mission.
