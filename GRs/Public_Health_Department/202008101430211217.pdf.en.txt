GOVERNMENT ORDER: The Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai vide letter dated 01.04.2020 has requested that Dr. (Mrs.) Jayashree Sanjeev Borade, Medical Officer, Group-A, Service Clinic, Amravati, be voluntarily retired with effect from 01.04.2020 (Mid-Day) as per Rule 66 of Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 and issue an order to this effect.
In the subject matter, Dr. (Mrs.) Jayashree Sanjeev Borade, has given three months advance notice of voluntary retirement dated 31.12.2019. He has applied for voluntary retirement due to his ill health and personal problems. They have submitted an application to the Commissionerate that since the period of three months has been completed as on 31.03.2020, the voluntary retirement has been granted, assuming that they are voluntarily retiring after noon on 31.03.2020.
Dr. (Mrs.) Jayashree Borade was first appointed as Medical Officer, Group-B (then Class-III) in the Regional Secondary Service Selection Board, Nagpur vide its recommendation letter dated 24.09.1992. Accordingly, she joined as Medical Administration Officer, R.K.V.O.Hospital, Nagpur In-Service Clinic, Pulgaon on 08.02.1993 (Mid-Day). He has been promoted to the post of Medical Officer, Group-A vide Government Order dated 14.05.2012. As on 31.03.2020 (M. No.), she was working as Medical Officer, Group-A, at Sewa Dawakhana, Amravati.

Borade has completed 20 years of qualifying service as per the provisions of Rule 66 (1) of the M.N.S. (Pension) Rules, 1982. As per the provisions of Rule 66 (2) of the (Pension) Rules, 1982, Dr. Borade is deemed to have voluntarily retired on the date of expiry of the notice of voluntary retirement. The notice of voluntary retirement given by Borade is being accepted.
In accordance with the provisions of Rule 66 (1) (2) (5) of the (Pension) Rules, 1982, Dr. (Mrs.) Jayashree Borade, Medical Officer, Group-A is being declared to have taken voluntary retirement with effect from 01.04.2020 (Mid-Day).
