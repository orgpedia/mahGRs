Introduction: As the rural hospital was sanctioned in Mouda G. Nagpur, the matter of shifting the primary health centre there was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: 2. The primary health centre of the village is located at Mouda G.Nagpur. The shifting of Mouda to Nagpur is being approved subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the shifted place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
