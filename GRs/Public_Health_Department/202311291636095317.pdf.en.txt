Preamble: In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services such as Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Plexipule, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to provide 40% State share of grant from the budgetary provision of 2023-24 to the State Health Societies under the general category for the National Rural Health Programme under the National Health Mission. The details of grants disbursed so far in this case are as follows.
