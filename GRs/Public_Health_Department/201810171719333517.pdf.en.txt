Preamble The budget for the construction of the gate and other works of the 200 bedded District Hospital at Chikalthana, District Aurangabad has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, World Bank Project, Aurangabad and based on the rate list of 2017-18, it is Rs. Rs 95,12,811. The matter of administrative approval of this expenditure was under consideration of the government.
The government has decided to set up a 200-bed district hospital in Chikalthana, Aurangabad district. Administrative approval is being given to meet the expenditure of Rs.95,12,811/- (Rupees Ninety Five Lakhs, Twelve Thousand Eight Hundred and Eleven only) from the grants available during 2018-19.
