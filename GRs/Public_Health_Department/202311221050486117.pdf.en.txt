The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2023-24 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. The budget has allocated Rs 70 crore for this. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for payment of premium to the insurer for the quarter from 1st October, 2023 to 31st December, 2023 for the beneficiaries of the scheme. The proposal to disburse conditional funds to the tune of Rs. 24,95,78, 599/- to the State Health Guarantee Society was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the premium for payment of insurance premium for the period from 1st October, 2023 to 31st December, 2023 of the beneficiary family under the scheme will be Rs. 24,95,78, 599/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Seventy Eight Thousand Five Hundred Ninety Nine only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.

The fund at the rate prescribed by the Government shall be provided to the insurance company for the insurance of the beneficiaries of the Integrated Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and to the concerned hospital for payment of claims on the basis of guarantee.
2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.

This fund will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society, Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, with an outlay of Rs. 24,95,78, 599/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Eighty Eight Thousand Five Hundred and Ninety Nine only) to the State Health Guarantee Society. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts and Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
