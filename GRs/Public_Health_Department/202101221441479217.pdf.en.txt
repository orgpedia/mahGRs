Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of this scheme are directly deposited in the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme in the state account.
In the financial year 2020-21, cadre-wise funds (for general, SC and ST categories) have been received from the Central Government for implementation of the scheme. For Scheduled Tribes (ST), it is Rs. 1,00,66, 701/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Six Thousand Seven Hundred and One only) has been received by the State and deposited with ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the State share of Rs. 67,11,134/- (Rupees Sixty Seven Lakhs Eleven Thousand One Hundred and Thirty Four only) was under consideration of the Government. 40% of the State's share of Rs. 67,11,134/- (Rupees Sixty Seven Lakhs Eleven Thousand One Hundred and Thirty Four only) has been released by the Tribal Development Department to the Principal Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department. Government approval has been accorded to disburse the budget funds on the disbursement mechanism as per the Government decision dated 22.01.2021. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
