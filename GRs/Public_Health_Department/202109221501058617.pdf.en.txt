Government's decision
The temporary post of District Health Officer (Group-A), Zilla Parishad, Sindhudurg will be filled up subject to the following conditions: 1.03.2021 to dt. Extension for the period from 28.02.2022:
(1) The said post is not vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the post has been sanctioned have been complied with.
(2) Government Resolution, Finance Division No: Designation-2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.D. 15.02.2021.& S. No.2016/ S. No. 8/16/ APC, Dy. The terms and conditions prescribed as on 10.08.2021 should be scrupulously followed and acted upon accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised design of the office finally approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the prescribed time, if the temporary post is not extended for that further period and if there is a question of salary and allowance of the concerned officer, then the responsibility will be of the Director, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai.
