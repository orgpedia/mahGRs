Introduction: According to the findings of the World Health Organization (WHO), measles leads to an increase in infant mortality in India. Rubella infection also causes congenital diseases. The Central Government has issued instructions to implement Measles Rubella vaccination campaign in the State to reduce the high infant mortality rate and control congenital disabilities caused by Rubella infection. To plan, coordinate and implement the campaign as per the central guidelines. A State Steering Committee headed by the Chief Secretary was being considered at the government level. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision 1. In order to eradicate Measles and Rubella by 2020, it has been decided to constitute a State Level Steering Committee for successful implementation of Measles Rubella vaccination campaign in children in the age group of 9 months to 15 years in Maharashtra, as in other States.
2. On the implementation of the central government's Measles Rubella campaign in the state of Maharashtra. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. Committee members will include:

Functions of the Committee:
a) To take decisions in coordination with various departments of the state for various activities of the campaign.
b) Various non-governmental organizations, global organizations, UNICEF and other professional organizations (e.g. Indian Association of Paediatrics, representatives of medical colleges, etc.) and non-governmental organizations of the people (e.g., Lions Club, Rotary Club etc.) to participate in the campaign.
c) Planning for manpower and other materials in collaboration with other departments.
d) To take measures to award the wards of the district/ taluka/ civic department who have done excellent work for the implementation of the campaign.
(e) Suggest measures to correct the deficiencies identified in the review.
