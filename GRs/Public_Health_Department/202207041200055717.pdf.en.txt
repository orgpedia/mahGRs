The Proposal:
In pursuance of the decision of the Government vide Reference No. (1), an amount of Rs. 5,27,25, 14,212/-was administratively approved for the purchase of drugs, medical equipment, machinery and related consumables and other essential items from the funds available under various heads of accounts under the National Health Mission (NHM) and from the grants available under various heads of State Grants-in-aid during the financial year 2020-21. However, since the rates mentioned subject-wise are estimated as per administrative approval, the procurement cell of Haffkine Biopharmaceuticals Limited, Parel, Mumbai Corporation had informed that the tender received 10% higher rates as per Reference Letter No. Further, the Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai was directed to take revised administrative approval as per the rates received in the tender from the user department in the approval committee meeting dated 22.04.2022 and take a decision by publishing the tender again as per the decision taken in the approval committee meeting.
As directed by Haffkine Biopharmaceuticals Limited, Parel, Mumbai Corporation, the Commissionerate of Health Services had informed the Government vide letter no. (3) under reference that the actual number of items which are 10% or more as per the rates received in the tender has been reduced. It had also requested the government for obtaining revised administrative approval as the amount as per the original administrative approval for the matter proposed to be purchased from the state grant was withdrawn from the treasury through short payments during the financial year and transferred to the bank account of the Haffkine procurement cell.
The Commissioner Health Services and Mission Director and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai have approved the revised administrative approval by reducing the actual number of items received in the tender to 10% or more as per the directions of Haffkine Biopharmaceuticals Limited, Parel, Mumbai Corporation vide note no. Accordingly, the Commissionerate of Health Services had submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining revised administrative approval under letter (5) in the context. Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, the procurement cell of Haffkine Jeeva Drug Corporation had informed the Commissionerate of Health Services that it has received 10% more rates than the original administrative sanction amount for the procurement of medicines, pharmaceuticals and related items from the subsidy available under various heads of National Health Mission and State Grants.
Accordingly, by reducing the actual number of items which are 10% or more of the administrative sanction, the Commissionerate of Health Services is granting fresh revised administrative sanction for 34 items from the grants available under various heads of accounts under the National Health Mission to an amount of Rs. 18,01,64, 184/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores One Lakh Sixty Four Thousand One Hundred Four only) as per attached statement-1. Further, a fresh revised administrative sanction for an amount of Rs. 2,04,68, 017/- (Rupees Two Crore Four Lakh Sixty-six Thousand Seventeen only) is being granted for a total of 11 items as per the attached Statement-II under various heads of accounts of State Grants.
The grants-in-aid under National Health Mission (NHM), as above, for a total amount of Rs. 20,06,32, 201/- (Rupees Twenty Crores Six Lakhs Thirty Two Thousand Two Hundred and One only), out of the total amount of Rs. 18,01,64, 184/- shown in the Statement-1 and Rs. 2,04,68, 017/- shown in the Statement-2 under various heads of State Grants, are being given revised administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions: (1) The requirement/ demand of the said medicines, medical equipment, machinery and related consumables and other essential materials are being considered. After that, the purchase process will begin.

(7) It shall be ensured that after the supply of the items to be purchased, such items do not remain unused and are used within the stipulated time.
(8) The amount paid for the year 2020-2021 for the proposed purchase of medicines, medicinal materials and related matters, as per the original administrative approval under the Government Decision dated 20.10.2020, has been withdrawn from the treasury by way of a lump sum payment and transferred to the bank account of the procurement cell of Haffkine Jeeva Drug Corporation.
