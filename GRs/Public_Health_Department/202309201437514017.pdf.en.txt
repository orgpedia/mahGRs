As per the list of materials and equipment in the NAM Framework, for the 30-bed Ayush Hospital, 28 equipment of Panchakarma, 6 equipment of Ksharasutra, 7 equipment of Uttarbasti, 10 equipment of Raktamokshan and 3 equipment of Agnikarm, 5 equipment of physical therapy, 9 equipment of laboratory have been given. Similarly, in other districts, 10 bedded/ 30 bedded/ 50 bedded AYUSH hospitals will be proposed as per the guidelines of National AYUSH Mission. These hospitals will also need equipment for Unani treatment. Approval is being given to constitute the following Technical Committee to decide the technical specifications of the said equipment:

(b) Preparation of technical specifications of the Ayurveda and Unani equipments, materials, equipment and tools to be procured and conduct sample inspection/ pre-supply inspection, if required.
