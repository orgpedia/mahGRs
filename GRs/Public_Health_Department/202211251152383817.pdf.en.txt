The proposal-
Considering the increasing prevalence of measles in the state, the government was considering the formation of a committee of experts in the state to control it and prevent the increase in the number of patients and take measures for related matters.
The government has decided to appoint a" Task Force" to take measures to control the outbreak of measles in the state, guide action and related matters. The government is approving the inclusion of the following experts in this task force.
