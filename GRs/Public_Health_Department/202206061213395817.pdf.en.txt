A proposal to disburse thematic grants under the National AYUSH Mission to Socially Specialized Units was submitted to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. On this proposal, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has informed that under the Government Resolution No. (3) under reference head" 2210 G583," the budget provision for the year 2022-23 is Rs. 200.00 lakh and the Finance Department has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 39.38 lakh (Rupees Forty-nine Lakhs Thirty-eight Thousand only). As the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has approved the release of unutilized funds for the year 2019-20 under the scheme of Rashtriya Ayush Mission on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS) from the provision in the Budget 2022-23 under the head (01) (04) Rashtriya Ayush Mission State Share (40%) (Programme), Account Head 2210 G583, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Paid), an amount of Rs. 39.38 lakh (Rupees Forty Nine Lakhs Thirty Eight Thousand Only), the matter of disbursement of the said pending grants was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In respect of National AYUSH Mission Scheme under the head" 2210 G583," out of the budgetary provision of Rs. 313.81 lakh for the year 2022-23, the Department of Finance has made a budgetary provision of Rs. 200.00 lakh. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 39.38 lakhs (Rupees Forty Nine Lakhs Thirty Eight Thousand only) has been sanctioned.
Accordingly, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has approved the release of unutilized funds for the year 2019-20 under the scheme of National AYUSH Mission as proposed by National Health Mission, Mumbai for the year 2022-23 under the head" 2210 G583, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Paid)." Accordingly, out of the budgetary provision (01) (04), Rs. 39.38 lakh (Rupees Forty Nine Lakhs Thirty Eight Thousand Only) is being released for the year 2022-23 under the head National AYUSH Mission State Share (40%) (Programme), Account Head" 2210 G583, 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-Payment)" and the details are as under:
