The Proposal:
Rural Hospital, Kolgaon. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs 14.58 crore was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
2. Budget for construction of main building at Rural Hospital, Kolgaon, G. Ahmednagar is based on the 2017-18 tariff list. The budget has earmarked 5 per cent for internal and 6 per cent for external electrification.
In addition, a provision of 5% contingency expenditure has been made and as per the Recapitulation Sheet attached, administrative approval is being granted under this Government Decision subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required for this work.

2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
9) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on construction of main building of Rural Hospital, Kolgaon, G. Ahmednagar should be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital expenditure on medical services and public health, 02- Rural Health Services, 04- Public Health, 104- Community Health Centre, (00) (01) Large Constructions (4210 0391), 53- Large Constructions.
4. The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to make budgetary provision for the construction of the main building of the Rural Hospital, Kolgaon G. Ahmednagar.
