Introduction: Standard diet for adult and pediatric patients admitted in government hospitals. It was established by the Government Decision of 12.08.1996. A period of 30 years has passed since this government decision and it needs to be amended according to the changing circumstances. The type of diet and its quantity have changed for patients with diabetes, patients with tuberculosis, hospital in-patients and psychiatric patients.
Also, the type and quantity of diet needs to be revised as per ICMR's 2010 standard.
Accordingly, the matter of improving the diet and diet of patients admitted to government hospitals under the Public Health Department of the state was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
