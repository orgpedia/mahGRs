Preamble: The Government of India has launched the centrally sponsored scheme of National AYUSH Mission in September, 2014. The scheme envisages provision of AYUSH health services and education to the general public, upgradation of quality of Ayurvedic, Homeopathic and Unani medicines and supply of quality medicinal plants on a continuous basis. As per the scheme guidelines, a part of the funds required for the respective units should be provided by the central government and part by the state government.
