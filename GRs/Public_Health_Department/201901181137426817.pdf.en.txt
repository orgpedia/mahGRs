(1) The Joint Director (Non-Communicable Diseases), Health Services, Mumbai vide letter No. 217-A/ District/ Nodal Officer/ 3958/18 D. 5/11/2018 Government Circular- Under the National Non-Communicable Disease Control Programme, the following health related programmes are being implemented in 34 districts of the State.
National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke. (NPCDCS) (2) National Programme for Old Age Care (NPHCE) (3) National Programme for Control of Blindness (NPCB) (4) National Programme for Mental Health (NMHP) (5) National Programme for Tobacco Control (NTCP) (6) National Programme for Oral Health (NOHP) (7) National Programme for Palliative Care (NPPC) (8) National Programme for Prevention and Control of Deafness (NPPCD) The programme is being implemented at different levels in each district of the State as the scope of the programme is expected to increase in the next period.
However, since there does not seem to be a separate mechanism at the district level, the direct involvement of the District Surgeon and the District Health Officer is essential for the effective implementation of these programmes.
In order to ensure proper implementation of the National Non-Communicable Disease Control Programme, the District Level Coordinating Officers, Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs) are being appointed as follows.
