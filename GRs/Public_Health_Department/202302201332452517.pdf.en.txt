(2) The other terms and conditions of the promotion shall be as prescribed in the relevant Government Order.
Further, the revised posting as given above is being provided subject to the conditions in paragraph 3.
3. The above officers are being given revised postings by way of promotions only temporarily or by changing the temporary cadre as an administrative convenience till the availability of officers in the post of District Surgeons Cadre through Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Therefore, after the availability of officers in the District Surgeon Cadre, the officers who have been given posting by changing the cadre will be given posting in their parent Specialist Cadre. Also, their services will be considered in the seniority specialist cadre.
(4) These orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
The concerned Head of Office/ Regional Head of Office shall take action for grant of leave payable/ admissible on administrative grounds (without medical certificate) to the above officers who could not join the place of posting after being relieved as per the original posting order.
