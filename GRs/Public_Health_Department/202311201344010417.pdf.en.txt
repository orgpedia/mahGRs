The Proposal:
Post Basic B.Sc Nursing (PB B.Sc Nursing) is a two-year additional qualification under the service to enhance the professional skills and knowledge of GNM Principals working under the Commissionerate of Health Services.
As per the guidelines of the Indian Nursing Council, the following qualifications have been prescribed for the Course Director for teaching ANM GNM and B.Sc Nursing courses respectively.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The Name of the Course | Eligibility as per Indian Nursing Council                                                                                                      |
| 1.   | ANM                    | B.Sc. Nursing/Diploma in Nursing Education and Administration/Diploma in Public Health Nursing with 2 years of clinical experience.            |
| 2.   | GNM                    | M.Sc. Nursing or B.Sc. Nursing (Basic / Post Basic) or Diploma in Nursing Education and Administration with 2 years of professional experience |
| 3.   | B.S. Nursing           | M.Sc. (Nursing) with 1 year experience                                                                                                         |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As per the guidelines of the Nursing Council of India, B.Sc. (Nursing) and P.B.B.Sc. (Nursing) are mentioned as equivalent educational qualification as mentioned in the above mentioned 2 and 3.
The Service Entry Rules for the posts of Superintendents of Nursing Cadre to Assistant Superintendents have been notified on 27.02.2021. Basic BSc is the educational qualification mentioned in this service admission rule. However, since Post Basic BSc (Nursing) is the equivalent educational qualification for undergraduate courses, the government was considering including Post Basic BSc (Nursing) as an equivalent degree in the service entry rules. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: PBBSc (Nursing) Degree shall be deemed to be an equivalent degree in all the places where Basic BSc (Nursing) Degree is mentioned in the notified Service Entry Rules for various posts in Nursing Cadre.
However, the course PBBSc (Nursing) as prescribed in the Service Admission Rules will not be admissible if the course is taught through distance learning mode.
