The Proposal:
A total of 876 temporary posts are working under the National Malaria Prevention Programme Urban Malaria Scheme. These posts have been created as per Article 8 of the Constitution. 01.03.2017 to D. It was approved to continue till 30.09.2017. These temporary posts were under consideration of the Government for extension from 01.10.2017 to 28.02.2021 as per the scope of the Government Decision No. 1 to 7 mentioned in the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
These 876 temporary posts are not included in the figure of the Public Health Department. These posts are for various Municipalities/ Municipal Corporations under the National Malaria Prevention Programme Urban Malaria Scheme.

After this, the action regarding the extension of time will have to be taken by the Urban Development Department.
