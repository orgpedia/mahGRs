The premium amount for the quarter from 1st October, 2023 to 31st December, 2023 is Rs. 7,20,00, 283/- (Rupees Seven Crores Twenty Lakhs Two Hundred and Thirty Three only)
Under this Government decision, funds are being sanctioned for the purpose of Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried).
The funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions mentioned in Government Resolution No. 27/2022/ Regulations, dated 12.01.2023.
The fund at the rate prescribed by the Government shall be provided to the insurance company for the insurance of the beneficiaries of the Integrated Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and to the concerned hospital for payment of claims on the basis of guarantee.
2) The amount shall be submitted to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai in the form of a utility certificate for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company.
The funds will be transferred to the State Health Guarantee Society to be credited to the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, Maharashtra. The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for submission of the payment of the grant to the Accounts Officer, Mumbai.
The Finance Department vide Circular No. Finance-2023/ PR No.40/ Finance-3, dated 12th April, 2023 vide Annexure No. The No. 9. The State Health Guarantee Society has informed the Government that it has fulfilled the terms and conditions from 1 to 10 and has submitted the utilization certificate of the funds released by the Government earlier for the purpose for which the funds were sanctioned. It is also being certified that the funds disbursed under this Government decision are being spent by depositing them in the separate bank account (ESCROW Account) of the State Health Guarantee Society and all the relevant provisions/ terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 12.04.2023 of the Finance Department are being complied with.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The amount will be spent in the financial year 2023-24.
