GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the National Leprosy Control Programme, 1054 posts have been created as per the decision of the Government of Reference No. 1, out of which 384 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 384 temporary posts, 177 temporary posts have been made permanent as 80% of the government decisions of Reference No. 2 and 3. The remaining 207 temporary posts are included in the establishment review approved by the Government Decision of Reference No. 1. Referred No. The said 207 temporary posts were extended up to 30/9/2018 as per the Government decision dated 04.04.2018.
In order to ensure continuous delivery of health services, 207 temporary posts in the attached list are being continued from 1/10/2018 to 28/2/2019. These 207 posts include posts in Dr. Bandorwala Leprosy Hospital, Kondhwa, Pune.
The expenditure of these posts should be spent under the head of accounts shown against the designations in the return attached with the Government decision and should be met from the sanctioned grant for the year 2018-19.
