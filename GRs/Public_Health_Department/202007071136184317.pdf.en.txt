the prelude
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. During the financial year 2020-21, under the head 2210 G 494 (State Share 40%), Rs. A budgetary fund of Rs. 20,00,00, 000/- is available. For the period from 01.07.2020 to 30.09.2020, the State share for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is Rs. 6,05,52, 229/- (Rupees Six Crores Five Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand Two Hundred and Nineteen only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.
