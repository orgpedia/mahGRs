(3) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the above promoted officers will not be entitled to any regularity, seniority in service and incidental matters.
4) The said temporary promotion is being granted subject to the acceptance that no departmental enquiry is warranted against these officers.
5) The temporary promotion is being given without affecting the seniority of the officers who are senior but whose cases have been kept open for consideration.
These officers will have to accept the promotion within seven days from the date of receipt of this order, failing which their promotion will be cancelled without any prior notice. These officers should be promoted immediately. If they take any leave, it will be considered as unauthorised absence and there is a possibility of disruption in their service. Disciplinary action will be taken against these officers if they put any pressure to get their appointment orders modified.
These officers should be relieved immediately by their present Controlling Officers and should be absorbed by the Controlling Officers at their new place of posting and the report of such relieved/ absorbed should be submitted to the Government through the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai.
This order is being issued with the consent of the General Administration Department.
