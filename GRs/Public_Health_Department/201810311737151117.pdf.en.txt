Upgradation of 30 bedded rural hospital at Biloli, Nanded district to 50 bedded sub-district hospital.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Public Health
Government Resolution No: Category- 2015/ PR No. 136/ Health- 4
Ministry, 10th Floor, Complex Building,
Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital in Mumbai.
The date is November 13, 2018.
For details: Letter from Joint Director, Health Services, Hospitals (State Level), Mumbai No: SANASE/ Cell-3/ Grade-162/ GRARU, Biloli/ 50 Beds/ Grading/ 6594-97/15, Dated 11.06.2015.
Preamble: A proposal to upgrade the 30-bed rural hospital at Biloli, G. Nanded was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
The government has decided to upgrade the 30-bed rural hospital in Biloli, Nanded district to a 50-bed sub-district hospital.
An estimate plan for the construction of the building on the land available for the hospital should be prepared and submitted for administrative approval. Once the construction is completed, the proposal for creation of more posts should be submitted to the government.
