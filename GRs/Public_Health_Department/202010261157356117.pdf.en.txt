The Proposal:
Under the above Reference No. 1, for starting the new department of Paediatric, Neurosurgery, Plastic Surgery etc. at Reference Service Hospital, Nashik, two additional floors will be constructed on the said building at a cost of Rs. 2049.31 lakh budget and plans were given administrative approval.
As the executive engineer of the Public Works Department conducted a structural audit and opined that it was risky to construct an additional two floors on the building, the Departmental Reference Service was considering the matter of granting new administrative approval to the budget and plan for the construction of an additional two floors in the open space in the vicinity of the hospital. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. Reference Service Hospital, Nasik, the construction budget of the Renal Transplant Surgery Unit is based on the 2019-20 tariff schedule. It includes provisions for Goods and Services Tax, labour insurance, etc. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget and Schemes amounting to Rs. 1608.51 lakhs subject to the following terms and conditions.
The administrative approval granted on June 13, 2019 is also being cancelled. :
