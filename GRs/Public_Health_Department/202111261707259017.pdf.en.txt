The Proposal:
Some of the medical officers in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100-177500) were on unauthorised absence for a long period without prior permission of the government on various grounds. As public health service is an essential service and there is a shortage of medical officers under the department, the government was considering to reinstate these unauthorized absentee medical officers in the government service after their unauthorized absence subject to specified terms and conditions.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A The Medical Officers mentioned in the table below in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A were unauthorisedly absent for a long time. The Medical Officers mentioned in the Table below are being recruited subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Table under this paragraph and are being given such posting as may be indicated against their names in the Table below for joining the post of Medical Officer Group-A.
