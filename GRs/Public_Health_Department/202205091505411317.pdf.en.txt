Preamble: The Government have decided to provide post-retirement training and superintendence training to the employees of the Commissionerate of Health Services, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Directorate of Ayurveda and Food and Drug Administration. SGE 1160/66780/ V, D. As per 13.10.1970, post-service training and supervisory training for promotion was prescribed for the employees of the scribe class. Changes in the training curriculum were necessitated by the changes in government functioning over time, computerization, use of various computer systems in governance/ administration practices, and changed financial regulations. Also, with the increase in online training and training facilities, it is necessary to prescribe a revised duration of this training. Accordingly, a committee was formed by the Director, Health Services Commissionerate, Pune-2 for the curriculum as well as allied matters like service entry and training etc. The committee has made a comprehensive study of government affairs and hospital affairs and has proposed revised training courses, course duration and rules. Similarly, the policy on common matters regarding departmental examinations of various administrative departments of the state government is being read by the General Administration Department. 01 has been fixed in accordance with the government decision here.
