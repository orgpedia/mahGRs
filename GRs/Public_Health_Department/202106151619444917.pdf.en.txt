The Proposal:
A special task force of paediatricians has been set up to develop and guide preventive and necessary treatment strategies in case the third wave of COVID-19 is caused by genetic mutations and mutations, which are likely to infect children in the third wave. The government was considering the inclusion of Dr. Sameer Dalwai, Pediatrician, Hinduja Hospital in this special task force.
Government's decision:
A special task force of paediatricians is being formed under the chairmanship of Dr Suhas Prabhu.
