Preamble: Under the National Tuberculosis Control Programme, 1304 posts have been created as per the decision of the Government under Reference No. 1, out of which 831 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 831 temporary posts, 80% of the 575 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the decision of the Government in Table 2 above. The remaining 264 temporary posts will be filled up as per the Government Resolution No. 3. The period has been extended up to 31.08.2023. In order to provide continuous health related services to the people of the state, the government was considering to continue the 264 posts from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Government Decision: 264 temporary posts under National Tuberculosis Control Programme Approval is being given for continuation from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
