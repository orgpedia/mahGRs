Preamble: The District General Hospital, Thane is functioning in a 100 year old British era building and as some of the buildings of the hospital are very dilapidated and unfit for use, the demolition of the old buildings has been approved under the Government decision dated 9th March, 2018. Thereafter, the opening of 574 bedded District Hospital and Referral Hospital (Super Specialty Hospital) at Thane District General Hospital has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 17.07.2018. Accordingly, the Joint Director (Hospitals State Level), Directorate of Health Services, had submitted the estimates for the construction of 574-bed District Hospital and Referral Service Hospital (including super speciality) and Nurses Training Centre and Hostel Building at Thane District General Hospital to the Government for administrative approval. The cost of the building is Rs. The proposal is worth more than Rs. 15.00 crore. As per the minutes of the meeting held on 10/6/2019, the said construction has been approved by the Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the work was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
The government has decided to construct a 574-bed district hospital, a referral hospital and a nurse training centre and a hostel at the District General Hospital, Thane. 31411.29 Lakhs
