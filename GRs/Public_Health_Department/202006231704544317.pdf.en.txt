Thane: The spread of the coronavirus disease (Covid-19) has put the Thane Municipal Corporation's health system under pressure. On the request of Municipal Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation, Thane, to take necessary preventive measures to control this contagious disease, Dr. Charudatta Shinde, Officer on Special Duty, Shri. The Minister (Urban Development Department, Public Works (Public Undertakings)) Establishment is being appointed on deputation to the post of" Medical Health Officer" of Thane Municipal Corporation on temporary basis.
