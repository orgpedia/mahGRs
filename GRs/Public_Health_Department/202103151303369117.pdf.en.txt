The Proposal:
In the State, the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana has been merged with the revised Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. The scheme has been implemented with effect from 01.04.2020 in association with United India Insurance Company.
In view of the situation of COVID-19 pandemic in the State, the scope of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana has been extended as per the Government decision dated 23rd May, 2020.
