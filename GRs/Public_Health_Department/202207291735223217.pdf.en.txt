Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The central share (60%) of the funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of the scheme is directly credited to the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on state account.
For the financial year 2021-22, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 to Rs. 3,22,65, 864/- (Rupees Three Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand
Eight hundred and sixty four only) funds have been received by the state and these funds have been deposited in the Escrow Association. 40% of the State share of Rs. 2,15,10, 576/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifteen Lakhs Ten Thousand Five Hundred and Seventy Six only) was under consideration at the Government level. 40% of the State's share of Rs. 2,15,10, 576/- from the budgetary fund of the year 2022-2023 as the Head of Administrative Department from the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.

Government's decision:
For effective implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the State Government will provide 40% of the pending funds of the financial year 2021-2022 to the Scheduled Castes (SC) category from the budgetary allocation of 2022-2023, amounting to Rs. 2,15,10, 576/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifteen Lakhs Ten Thousand Five Hundred and Seventy Six only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
