Dr. (Mrs.) Ratna Ravkhande (Tatka.), District Surgeon Cadre, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700) District Surgeon, District Hospital, Nashik) was under the consideration of the Government for posting as there was no post at present. Accordingly, Dr. (Mrs.) Ratna Ravkhande, Tatka. District Surgeon, District Hospital, Nashik (presently without post) is being transferred and posted as Assistant Director, Maharashtra State AIDS Control Society, Wadala, Mumbai.
These orders are being issued in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
Dr. (Mrs.) Ratna Ravkhande should immediately appear at the place of transfer and submit a report to the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai and the Government.
4. No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
5. The transferred officers should join the new post during the permissible period of posting, the officers who do not comply with this time limit and remain absent from the place of posting, the absence of such officers will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and in the alternative, their service will be interrupted.
6. The Commissionerate shall initiate administrative action against the officers who fail to appear at the place of their posting and submit the proposal for approval of the Government as per requirement. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
7. Any officer who directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order shall be liable for disciplinary action.
8.The order will come into effect immediately.
