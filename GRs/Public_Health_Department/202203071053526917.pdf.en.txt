The Proposal:
It has been decided to provide loan from financial institution HUDCO for construction of new and existing hospitals under the Public Health Department. Accordingly, it is the policy of the department to do this work from the loan available from the financial institution HUDCO.
Accordingly, a 30-bed rural hospital and a 100-bed sub-district hospital will be constructed by upgrading Jamkhed in Ahmednagar district. The proposal to give administrative approval to the preliminary budget and plans of the same amount of Rs. 26.84 crore was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget for construction of 100 bedded Sub District Hospital accommodation by upgrading 30 bedded Rural Hospital, Jamkhed, G. Ahmednagar is based on the Rate List 2021-22. The total area of 85 flats of Type I (S+ 5) (20 flats), Type II (S+ 6) (48 flats), Type II (S+ 4) (16 flats), Type IV (G) (1 flat) is 6410.38 sqm. 26000/- per sq.m. The work has been mapped by a private architect. The budget has been prepared by the Deputy Engineer, Public Works Department, Jamkhed, Ahmednagar. executive

Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
6) Since the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budget of these works is technically approved by the Public Works Department.
It will be done by the competent authorities of the region.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) E-tendering process should not be carried out without verifying that the permissible area of the said building is as per the norms.
10) The District Surgeon should take action on the construction only after verifying that the proposed accommodation is of suitable size.
