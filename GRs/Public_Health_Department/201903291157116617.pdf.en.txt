Preamble: The Central Government's share of funds (60%) received for the Flexible Fund and Administrative Expenditure for the financial year 2017-2018 under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is Rs. 1270.44 Lakhs and State Fund (40%) share of Rs. 847.00 Lakhs out of which Rs. 2117.44 lakh (Rupees Twenty One Crore Seventeen Lakh Forty Four Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision- For the financial year 2017-2018, the Central Government will provide flexible funds and administrative expenses for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana. 1270.44 (Rupees Twelve Crore Seventy Lakh Forty Four Thousand only) has been provided to the State. Accordingly, 40% of the State share of Rs. 708.00 (Rupees Seven Crores Eight Lakhs only) to the concerned agencies, thus the total Central and State share of Rs. 2117.44 (Rupees Twenty One Crore Seventeen Lakh Forty Four Thousand only) has been approved through Supplementary Demands in the Winter Session 2018. The government has approved the distribution of this fund.
The Chief Administrative Officer, Additional Director, Office of Family Welfare, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Additional Director, Health Services, Family Welfare, Mother Child Care and School Health, Pune as Controlling Officer for the above funds.
