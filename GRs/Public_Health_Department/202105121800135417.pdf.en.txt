Preamble: Vaccination of the State's population is being done as per the policy of the Central Government under the preventive measures against the growing outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic. Accordingly, the State Cabinet has decided to vaccinate around 5.71 crore beneficiaries of the age group 18-44 years for COVID-19 in the State from May 1, 2021. Meeting held on 28th April 2021. Around 12 crore vaccine doses are required to be administered per person at the rate of 2 doses per person. As per the guidelines of the central government, the remaining 50 per cent of the vaccine quota can be purchased by the state governments from the approved vaccine manufacturing companies. For this purpose, the Department of Public Health has been allocated budgetary allocation of Rs. 421,19,66, 000/- has been disbursed on 100% BDS system. From this grant. The decision was taken by the Serum Institute, Pune. As on 07.05.2021 Rs. 54,54,47, 700/- The proposal for grant of approval for procurement of Covishield vaccine was approved by the Director (2), Health Services, Pune in the presence of Dr. It has been submitted for Government approval vide letter dated 12.05.2021.
Considering the urgency of COVID-19 pandemic containment measures, the matter of granting administrative approval is under consideration at the government level.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the current outbreak of COVID-19 in the State, Serum Institute, Pune is granting administrative approval for procurement of vaccine as mentioned in the table below for COVID-19 vaccination of beneficiaries in the age group of 18-44 years.
