Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai vide letter No., SAS/ Cell-3/ No. Grade 170/ Pahur/ 50 Beds/ Grading/ 7902-7904/15 dated 04.07.2015.
Preamble- Local public representatives have demanded to upgrade the rural hospital in Pahur G. Jalgaon to a 50-bed sub-district hospital. Accordingly, a proposal for upgradation of Rural Hospital at Pahur G.Jalgaon to 50 bedded Sub District Hospital has been made.
It was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
The government has decided to upgrade the rural hospital at Pahur in Jalgaon district to a 50-bed sub-district hospital.
2. Separate action will be taken for the construction and creation of posts for the 50-bed sub-district hospital in the said category by acquiring land in the prescribed manner.
