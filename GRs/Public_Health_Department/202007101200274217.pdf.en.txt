Preamble: 19 temporary posts in the Badshah Khan Eye Hospital, Mandvi, Dist. Nanded, working under the National Blindness Control Programme, will be filled up as per the revised figure in the Public Health Department, Government Decision No. Post No.117/ Service-1, dated 6th August, 2007. Of them, 16 temporary posts were filled by the Department of Public Health, Government Decision No. Annika/ Apde 2012/ PR No.191/ Arogya-5, dated 1st June, 2012 has been made permanent. The remaining 3 temporary posts will be filled as per the Government Decision No.Annika-2018/ Apde/ PR No. 264/ Arogya-5 dated 30th March, 2019 of the Public Health Department with effect from 01.03.2019. It has been approved to continue till 30.09.2019. The deadline has expired on 30.09.2019. However, the concerned District Surgeon and Deputy Director, Health Services have sanctioned 3 temporary posts at Badshah Khan Eye Hospital, Mandvi with effect from 1.10.2019. The proposal for extension of the period up to 28.02.2020 has not been submitted to the Government. Accordingly, the Joint Director, Health Services (Non-Communicable Diseases), Mumbai, has sanctioned 3 temporary posts. 01/10/2019 to D. Request for extension up to 31/08/2020.
Government Decision: Under the National Blindness Control Programme, the Headmistress of Badshah Khan Eye Hospital, Mandvi, G.Nanded, 3 temporary posts of Class-3 (1 post), Cell Servant Class-4 (1 post), Safaigar Class-4 (1 post) are required to provide health related facilities to the people of the state.
