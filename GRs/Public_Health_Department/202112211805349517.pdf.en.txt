Terms and Conditions:
(1) Before inviting tenders for the proposed extension, the work shall require technical approval of the competent authority in the Public Works Department.
(2) The head of the health institution concerned shall ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for construction of the extension.
(3) If it is possible to increase the cost of the proposed incremental construction according to the situation of the field place and the requirement of the health institutions, the necessary proposals for the same should be made by the concerned field officers of the Public Works Department in the respective areas.
(4) If the said budget is a rough sample budget, the detailed budgets of the concerned constructions may be sent to the Public Works Department for technical approval.
It will be taken up by the competent authorities in the area. The said approval and provisions will be finalised in consultation with the Deputy Director/ Additional Director, Health Services, Family Welfare, Parental Care and School Health, Pune of the respective Health Service Boards of the Health Department.
(5) If the proposed construction is to be done according to the new model map, the architectural plan should be approved according to the map through the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. Also, the newly appointed Architect and District Surgeon/ District Health Officer will be responsible for obtaining the construction permit and occupancy certificate as per the rules of the concerned planning authority, as per the requirement. As the case may be, the communication for seeking the said permission should be signed by the District Surgeon/ District Health Officer.
(6) When tendering a proposed construction, all the works mentioned in the Recapitulation Sheet should be tendered together without tendering each work separately.
