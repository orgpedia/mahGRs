the prelude
As per the broad framework of health institutions, the establishment of District Surgeon, General Hospital, Aurangabad has been approved by the decision of the government at No. 1 above. For this 200-bed district hospital, 279 officers and staff have been created as per the government decision at the above number 2. A proposal was under consideration to declare" Administrative Officer, Group-B" as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" to handle the management and financial affairs of the district hospital.
Government's decision
The District Surgeon, General Hospital, District Aurangabad Hospital shall be governed by the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. As per the provision of section 156," Administrative Officer, Class-B" is to be declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
