the prelude
Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. For the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), during the financial year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 120,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crores only) has been budgeted. Out of this provision, the State share for the period from 01.01.2020 to 31.03.2020 for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is Rs. 53,99,31, 185/- (Rupees Fifty Three Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Thirty One Thousand One Hundred and Fifty Eight only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.

Government's decision

It is confirmed that the conditions and conditions in the accompanying application are met. Also, it is being certified that after the funds disbursed under this government decision are deposited in the Escrow Account opened for the implementation of the scheme, the funds will be paid to the insurance company as insurance premium of the beneficiaries of the scheme.
The Department of Public Health, as per the provisions of Memorandum No. 2019/ PR No. 77/ Budget, dated 20th July, 2019, has to spend in the financial year 2019-20 within the limit of the provision approved under the head 2210- Medical Services and Public Health 01- Civil Health Services- Dysphagia 110- Hospitals and Clinics 07- Ayushman Bharat Programme (07) (02)- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (Programme) (State Share 40%) 2210 G 449, 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Medical).
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made and the report of the progress of the scheme should be sent to the Central and State Government in the prescribed form from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used in the 2019-20 financial year.
The concerned should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
