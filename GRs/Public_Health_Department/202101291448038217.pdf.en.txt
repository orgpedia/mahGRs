The Proposal:
Dr. Kishore Kaluram Surwase, Medical Officer, as mentioned in the table below, has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. The appointment is the first in the Government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Independent Selection Board constituted under the Department of Public Health in the year 2016.
As per the decision of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of the probation period of the officers/ employees of the government service, and since the medical officer who has been given the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A, has completed his two-year probation period satisfactorily, the termination of the probation period of these officers was under the consideration of the government.
