For the financial year 2022-23, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, an amount of Rs. 118 crore has been allocated in the budget. For the beneficiaries of this scheme, the premium for the period from October, 2022 to December, 2022 will be Rs. 32,84,77, 065/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crore Eighty Four Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand Sixty Five only) to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, the provision budgeted under the head of Accounts shown below for the financial year 2022-23 has been increased from Rs. 32,84,77, 065/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crore Eighty Four Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand Sixty Five only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the State Health Insurance Society.
