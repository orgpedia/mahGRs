The Proposal:
The Central Government has decided to allocate funds to the States for strengthening the health system in the States under the 15th Finance Commission. The amount will be disbursed over the next financial year (2021-22 to 2025-26). in the Semi-Government Letter dated 08.10.2021 of the Finance Department
In the financial year 2021-22, Rs. 1331.00 crore will be received by the State Government. Committees have been formed at district and taluk levels. The process of forming district and state level committees is underway. However, it is likely to be delayed as the nominated members of the state-level committee will be appointed with the approval of the Rural Development and Urban Development Department. However, the action plans to be sent to the central government in the interim period are bound to be sent with the recommendation of the state-level committee. In such a situation, the government was considering the formation of a state-level committee of government members.
Government's decision:
Under the 15th Finance Commission, the central government is setting up a state-level committee to allocate funds to the states for strengthening the health system in the state. The composition of the committee is as follows:
