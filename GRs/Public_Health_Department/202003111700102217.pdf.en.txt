Read:- Letter No. of Collector and Disaster Management Authority, Hingoli. ZARUHIN/ COVID-19/ RT-PCR/ 11119-21/2020 dated 22.07.2020.
Preamble: Hingoli district is currently witnessing a high surge of COVID-19 cases and as per ICMR guidelines, citizens in Containment Zones/ Contact Tracing shall be allowed to visit I. COVID-19 test (RAT) for L.I./ All Symptoms TPCR is mandatory.
The swab samples are being sent to a laboratory in Nanded, but it is taking three to four days to get the test report.
This necessitates putting a large number of citizens in institutional quarantine, putting a huge strain on the system and incurring huge expenditure. At present, 150-200 tests are expected in Hingoli district every day. In this backdrop, the District Collector and Disaster Management Authority, Hingoli has submitted a proposal for setting up of RT-PCR laboratory at District Hospital, Hingoli. Approval of the proposal
It was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
Government Decision: Approval is being given to operationalize the RT-PCR laboratory for diagnosis of COVID-19 virus disease at District Hospital, Hingoli and to fill up the technical and non-technical posts required for the same. For this, the necessary equipment prescribed by ICMR should be purchased. The National Appropriations for COVID-19 Measures

Instead of procuring the equipment and consumables required for this laboratory, supply orders should be issued to the District Hospital, Hingoli, as per the supply orders of the Government Medical College, Chhatrapati Pramila Raje Hospital, Kolhapur.
