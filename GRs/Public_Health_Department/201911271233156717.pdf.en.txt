Preamble: Under the National Urban Health Mission (NUHM), the scheme has been implemented since 2018-19 with the objective of providing regular primary, therapeutic and referral services in mobile vehicles in slum areas and remote areas of the city through mobile medical units and providing free services of the health department to the people. During the year 2019-20, 10 Mobile Medical Units have been approved for Municipal areas in the Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government. Accordingly, the government was considering granting administrative approval to the tender process for appointing an external agency to operate mobile medical units in 10 municipal areas.
Government's decision:
The Annual Action Plan approved by the Government of India for the financial year 2019-20 under the National Urban Health Mission (NUHM) contains the FMR Code U. Under the grant approved under 2.1.1, the mobile medical unit service is to be operated by an external service provider at a total of 10 locations- Nashik, Solapur, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Bhiwandi-Nizampur, Vasai-Virar, Mira Bhayandar and Ulhasnagar.
