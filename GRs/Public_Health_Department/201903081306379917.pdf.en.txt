Preface- The Pleasure. Primary Health Centre in North Solapur. The matter of shifting to North Solapur district was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has taken a decision. Primary Health Centre in North Solapur. The transfer to North Solapur, Dist. Solapur is being approved subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the transferred place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
