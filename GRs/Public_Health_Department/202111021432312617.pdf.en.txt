As per the provisions of Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the General Administration Department has issued a notification to review the services of officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service. The consolidated methodology has been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 10.06.2019.
As per the provisions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to constitute a" Divisional Review Committee" to try the eligibility of Medical Officers, Group-A (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500) of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre under the Commissionerate of Health Services to continue their service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
Government Order: Eligibility for continuation of service of Medical Officers Group-A (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500) of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre under the Commissionerate of Health Services at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier
