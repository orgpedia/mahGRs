The Proposal: -
The government was considering declaring the office as "Head of Office" and "Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Sub-district Hospital, Lohgaon, District Pune.
The Government's Decisions:
To the authority provided under Rule-2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-section-I, Roll No. 3 of the Appendix to the Government Decision No. 2013 / Q. No. 30/2013 Regulations, Part-II, dated 17.04.2015
Accordingly, the post of "Medical Superintendent (Class-I)" for the office of the Sub-District Hospital, Lohgaon, Dist. Pune, is designated as "Head of Office" in Rule No. 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
Being declared as "Drawing and Disbursing Officer" as per the provision.
