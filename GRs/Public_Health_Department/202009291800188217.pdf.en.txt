Administrative approval is being granted for the service indicated in the above head of the security guards to be hired by the outsourcing agency subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) The number of security guards should be determined by measuring their office with the help of the Public Works Department and determining the district accordingly.
(2) When working through an external agency, the Government has issued Decision No. SGA-1506/ No. 91/ Work-5, D. to be complied with the provisions of 08.11.2006.
3) Preference should be given to take security guards from the government-approved security board or MESCO.
In case of non-availability of security guard services by them, e-tendering process for all hospitals should be conducted simultaneously and at the state level in a very transparent manner.
4) The service provider should be identified at the state level or department level or district level.
5) The maximum number of security guards allowed for the hospital should be approved by the concerned Deputy Director, Health Services, as per the local requirement.
6) The tender document should include the condition that the next month's payment will not be paid unless the salary of the security guards is confirmed to be credited to their bank account.
