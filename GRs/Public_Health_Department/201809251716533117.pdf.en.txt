The Proposal:
For the year 2018-19, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, the budget outlay under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) is Rs. 341.50 crore and the funds available through Supplementary Demand for July, 2018 is Rs. 769.02 crore. A budgetary provision of Rs. 1110.52 crore is available. Of this fund, No. The government has decided to provide Rs. 2. 239.05 crore has been disbursed to State Health Guarantee Societies. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse the required funds to the State Health Guarantee Society out of the remaining undistributed funds under head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the beneficiaries of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, as well as for payment of salary and administrative expenses.
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, an amount of Rs. A conditional amount of Rs. 384,51,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred and Eighty Four Crores Fifty One Lakhs only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
