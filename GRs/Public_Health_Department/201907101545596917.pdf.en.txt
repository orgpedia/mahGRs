The proposal-
The Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS) has submitted a proposal to upgrade the 50-bed sub-district hospital at Karmala in Solapur district to a 100-bed sub-district hospital. Accordingly, the proposal to upgrade the hospital was under the consideration of the government.
A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision
Approval is being given to upgrade the 50-bed sub-district hospital in Karmala district Solapur to a 100-bed sub-district hospital.
2. Separate action will be taken for the construction and creation of posts for the 100-bed sub-district hospital in this category by acquiring land in the prescribed manner.
