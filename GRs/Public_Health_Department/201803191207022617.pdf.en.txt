A contract should contain the terms and conditions of the contract. The Government shall have no liability for the services of the officers/ employees acting on behalf of the Company/ Institution in this Agreement.
4) No posts will be created to work through the external system.
5) The payment of work to be done by external agencies must be shown under the detailed heading of" Office Expenditure" instead of under the detailed heading of" Salary."
6) While doing the work through an external system, it is necessary to save at least 10 to 25 percent of the cost that would have been incurred if the work had been done by creating posts regularly.
7) The external agency should be selected in a very transparent manner by conducting the tender process in the manner prescribed by the government.
8) The details of the savings of 10 to 25 percent in the expenditure incurred in taking the services as per the above by the external agencies as compared to the expenditure on filling the posts in the regular mode, the Government has issued Circular No. No. 112/13/ Fiscal Reforms-1, 2013. The form with 02.12.2013 is required to be submitted to the government.
9) The above instructions should be strictly followed by all concerned, otherwise disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned by fixing responsibility on them.
10) The cost should be appropriately covered.
