The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(i) Provide advice on protocol and technical guidance on operations at Molecular Diagnostics Laboratory, Sindhudurg.
(ii) Guidance on laboratory diagnosis, confirmatory testing, treatment and research.
(iii) To train and orient the laboratory staff in the technical skills and know-how of the latest equipment and analytical methods.
(iv) To review the working of the laboratory and to provide guidance on improvements.
Upgradation of Molecular Diagnostics Laboratory, Sindhudurg as per requirement in future and to guide in operationalization of Molecular Diagnostics Laboratories to be set up in Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Nashik and Amravati in future.
