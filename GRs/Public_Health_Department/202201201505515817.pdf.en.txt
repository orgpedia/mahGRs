As there are a large number of vacancies in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A Deputy Director, Health Services, these posts are filled by temporarily transferring from the lower cadre officers. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2), Section 4 (5) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005, the following officers are being transferred by way of downgrading to the rank of Deputy Director, Health Services, Group-A, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, with the approval of the competent authority:

(2) The said officers shall not be entitled for regular promotion to the post of Deputy Director, Health Services, or claim promotion to the post of Deputy Director, Health Services, by way of transfer by way of promotion from the cadre of Deputy Director, Health Services. Also, they will not be eligible for the post of Deputy Director, Health Services.

(6) The transferred officers, after relieving them, shall join the new post during the period of permissible posting, the officers who do not comply with this time limit and do not attend the place of posting mutually.
