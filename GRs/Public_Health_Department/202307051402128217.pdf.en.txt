Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and their performance of official duties.
As per the provisions of Section 4 and Section 6 of the Prevention of Delay Act, 2005, the following transfers are being made to the Specialist Medical Officer (Ear, Nose, Throat) Cadre (Pay Scale S-23: 67700-208700) with the approval of the competent authority.

(2) The transferred officer shall be relieved forthwith to report at his new place of posting and the concerned officer shall report to the Director, Health Services, Mumbai and the Government immediately at the place of posting assigned to him.
No leave shall be granted to the transferred officer, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
4. Since he is being transferred as per the request made by Dr. Varsha Sudhakar Shrikhande, he should join the new post immediately after being relieved from his duties.
If the transferred officer does not appear at the place of his posting, the Commissionerate should initiate administrative action against him immediately and submit the proposal for the approval of the government as per the need. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
(6) If the transferred officer tries to exert direct or indirect pressure to modify the order of transfer, the officer concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action.
7.The order will come into effect immediately.
