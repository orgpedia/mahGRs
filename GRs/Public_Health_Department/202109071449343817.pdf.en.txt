The Proposal:
The proposal of the Commissionerate of Health Services to upgrade the District Hospital, Nanded from 100 beds to 300 beds was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
Government's decision:
The government has decided to upgrade the District Hospital, Nanded from 100 beds to 300 beds.
