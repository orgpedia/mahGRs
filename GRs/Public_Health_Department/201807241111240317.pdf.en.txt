The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre for a probation period of two years. These appointments are the first appointments in the Government Service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Independent Selection Board in 2011.
As per the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Medical Officers Group-A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as indicated in the Table referred to in this paragraph, have satisfactorily fulfilled the requirements for the expiry of their period of probation, and their period of probation shall be as indicated in the Table before their names in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated.
Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
