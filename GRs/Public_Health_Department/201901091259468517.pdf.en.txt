Board/ 4964/2018, dated 13.12.2018 Preamble: In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services Rules, the candidates joining the State Service are referred to the Medical Board for medical examination after their selection for appointment. For the medical examination of such candidates, the State Reference No. 15 Medical Councils have been established under the Government Decision dated 28.02.2013. As per the proposal received from the Department of Medical Education and Medicines, a Medical Board will be constituted at Government Medical College, Chandrapur for conducting medical examination for two districts of Chandrapur and Gadchiroli on the lines of the Medical Board functioning in Medical Colleges. 1 The matter of reconstitution of the Medical Boards constituted under the Government decision dated 28.02.2013 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services Rules, medical boards have been constituted in the state to conduct medical examination of candidates joining the state service after their selection for appointment. In addition, medical boards are being set up in hospitals affiliated to government medical colleges at Government Medical College, Chandrapur. The composition of this medical board will be as follows.
1) Professor, Medicine 2) Professor, Surgery 3) Professor, Ophthalmology

The District Surgeons are empowered to conduct medical examination of Group-C and Group-D cadre employees as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services Rules. Accordingly, such certificates are expected to be issued by them every day except on holidays. If required in certain cases, the said candidate should be referred to the concerned Medical Board. Meetings of the above medical boards should be held every Monday.
If there is a need to change the medical board, the Director, Health Services, will submit a separate proposal to the Directorate of Health Services, Government of Mumbai. The rules and guidelines laid down in Chapter 19 of the Maharashtra Medical Code Part-I (Maharashtra Civil Medical Code) shall govern the functioning of all medical boards and their jurisdiction shall be limited to the districts under their jurisdiction.
This Government decision is being issued with the concurrence of the Department of Medical Education and Drugs.
