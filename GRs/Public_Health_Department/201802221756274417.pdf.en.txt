The proposal- m. Minister of Health and Family Welfare. on 24.11.2017. Principal Secretary (Health& Family Welfare), Commissioner, State Labour Insurance Scheme, Medical Commissioner, State Labour Insurance Corporation, New Delhi. The Minister (Health& Family Welfare) has directed that the State Workers Insurance Hospital, Kandivali be transferred to the State Workers Insurance Corporation. In pursuance of this direction, the matter of transfer of State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospitals, Kandivali to State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision- This Government decision approves the transfer of State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Kandivali to State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi.
All the officers and staff working in the State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Kandivali should be covered by the Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai at other State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospitals and Service Clinics.
Necessary further action regarding the transfer of the hospital shall be taken by the Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai and the Regional Director, State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi. as well
