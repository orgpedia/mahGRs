Preamble: In order to achieve the objectives of the Mobile Medical Unit Programme in rural areas of the State, especially in remote and tribal prone areas, under the National Health Mission, from the grant available under the supplementary PIP approved in the financial year 2019-20, the Government was considering to give administrative approval for operating 10 Mobile Medical Units through tender process from an external service provider.
Government Decision: Under the National Health Mission (NHM), during the financial year 2019-20, the Central Government has sanctioned 10 Mobile Medical Units under FMR Code 2.1.1.1 for which Rs. A grant of Rs. 246.20 lakh has been sanctioned. These mobile units are proposed at 10 locations in Pune, Satara, Ratnagiri, Osmanabad, Sindhudurg, Palghar, Nagpur, Amravati, Solapur and Aurangabad. The proposed 10 mobile medical units are to be commissioned by an external service provider. The mobile medical unit service involves the service provider purchasing vehicles, making necessary changes in them, providing facilities and providing manpower. The estimated cost of the medical unit is Rs. The estimated cost of the project is Rs. 246.20 crore.
