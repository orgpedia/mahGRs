Preamble: A Task Force of Specialist Doctors was constituted in the State as per the decision of the Government dated 13.04.2020 to plan and control the surge of cases during the first wave of COVID-19. A special task force of specialist doctors was set up to diagnose, analyse and take remedial measures. The number of Covid-19 cases is increasing in the state. The task force needs to be reconstituted in view of the increasing number of patients. Therefore, the matter of reconstituting the task force was under the consideration of the government.

Action to be taken by the Task Force: Establishing a patient management protocol for critically ill COVID-19 patients.
Recommending the requirement of specialist doctors and health care support staff in COVID-19 critical care hospitals.
Recommending appropriate drug protocols to maintain uniformity in treatment of critically ill COVID-19 patients.
