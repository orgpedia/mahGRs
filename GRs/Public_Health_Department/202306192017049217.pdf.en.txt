The Proposal:
ASHA volunteers and group promoters are working in the state under the National Health Mission programme. As per the guidelines, norms and rates of the Central Government, the grant sanctioned under the Project Implementation Plan under the National Health Mission (NHM) provides an incentive to ASHA volunteers and group promoters for performing a total of 78 services assigned under the National Health Mission (NHM), as determined by the Central Government. However, since the role of both ASHA volunteers and group promoters is important in the implementation of the National Health Mission programme and other national health related programmes, they have been asked to submit their report by July 17, 2020, September 09, 2021 and September 20, 2021. As per the Government Decision dated 10th April, 2023, Rs. It has been decided to give an increased remuneration of Rs.5000/- and Rs.6200/-.
