(1) The Joint Director, (Leprosy& Tuberculosis) Health Services, Pune vide letter No. SANASE/ Leprosy/ Bhandar/ 21045-48, Dy. 15.11.2018 2) Letter from the Medical Superintendent, Uro Hospital Aundh Pune No. Form/ Accounts/ Contract/ Clothing/ 89/19, D. 30.1.2019 Preamble Mr. Uttamrao Kanhoji Aswale, Government Contractor, Pune was appointed to carry out the process of contractual service of washing of clothes for the period 2018-2019 for the in-patient wash of the hospital at Uro Hospital, Aundh, Pune. However, the payment from the treasury could not be made as the contractual service did not have administrative approval. Therefore, for the period from 01.02.2018 to 31.01.2019, the Joint Director (Leprosy& Tuberculosis) Health Services, Pune issued Reference No. In view of the remorse received under section 1, the matter of ex-post facto sanction of expenditure on the above service was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Decision of the Government The issue of washing clothes of in-patients in the hospital of Uro Hospital, Aundh, Pune is an urgent matter and for this purpose Mr. Uttamrao Kanhoji Aswale, Government Contractor, Pune, who has been appointed for the service of washing clothes on contract basis for the period 2018-2019, said that the Government has decided to provide the facility to the patients of the hospital. 01.02.2018. For washing services provided during 31.01.2019, Rs. 02,10,072/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Ten Thousand Seventy Seven only) and payment of the said pending dues is hereby accorded ex-post facto approval. The cost of the above-mentioned laundry service at Uro Hospital, Aundh, Pune should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2018-2019 from the following head of accounts.
