Preamble: Under the National Leprosy Eradication Programme, a committee will be formed at the state level to rehabilitate the leprosy patients with Grade 2 in the state, create job opportunities, facilitate them to get the benefits of government schemes. The Minister (Public Health and Social Welfare) had promised in the Budget Session, 2023. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a state-level committee to rehabilitate leprosy patients in the state, create job opportunities and facilitate them to get the benefits of government schemes.
Government Decision- Under the National Leprosy Eradication Program, it is being approved to form a state-level committee in the state to rehabilitate leprosy patients with Grade 2 in the state, to create job opportunities, to facilitate them to get the benefits of government schemes. -

The committee comprises of Dr. Uday Thakkar, Dr. Vivek Pai, Dr. Uday Thakkar, Dr. Shantivan, Navi Mumbai, Dr. Vivek Pai, Mumbai Leprosy Project, Mumbai, and representatives of Leprosy Problem Redressal Committee and PAL (Leprosy Affected Persons).
The main function of the committee will be to create job opportunities for the rehabilitation of leprosy patients in the state and to increase the scope of benefits of government schemes for leprosy patients.
Meetings: The Committee shall meet at least once a year.
Various schemes of the government for the rehabilitation of leprosy patients: Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Yojana, Indira Awas Yojana, Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Economic Development Corporation, bank loans, seed capital for disabled persons, financial assistance and self-employment, etc. However, since all the above schemes do not come under the purview of the Health Department, a comprehensive committee is being appointed as better coordination with all the concerned departments is required.
