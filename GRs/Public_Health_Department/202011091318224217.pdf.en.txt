Preamble: The Government of India has launched the centrally sponsored scheme of National AYUSH Mission in September, 2014. The scheme envisages provision of AYUSH health services and education to the general public, upgradation of quality of Ayurvedic, Homeopathic and Unani medicines and supply of quality medicinal plants on a continuous basis. The scheme is 60 per cent funded by the central government and 40 per cent by state governments.
For the Socially Special Component (SCSP) for National AYUSH Mission under Centrally Sponsored Scheme, Rs. 98.20 Lakhs and pending State Accounts under head 2210G583 amounting to Rs. 185.32 crore. The Government has decided to allocate Rs. 283.52 lakh in the Budget Estimates 2020-21.
