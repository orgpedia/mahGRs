The Proposal:
The proposal for administrative approval of the budget and maps for the provision of CCTV facilities in the Phase-II building of the Divisional Reference Service Hospital, Amravati G. Amravati at an amount of Rs. 24,87,868/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: 2. Provision of CCTV facilities in Phase-II building of Divisional Reference Hospital, Amravati G. Amravati is based on the budget of 2017-18. This budget has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works, Electricity Department, Amravati. The budget includes 4 per cent for contingency expenditure and 12 per cent for GST.
Administrative approval is being granted under this Government Resolution for the projects with a budget of Rs. 24,77,616/- subject to the following terms and conditions:- 1. Technical approval of the competent authority of the Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the works.

2) Before starting the construction, the map should be approved by the architect, S.B. Department Nashik.
(3) A single tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned in the tender without separate tenders for each work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director of Health Services Board in the Department of Health.
6) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) Ensure that the above tasks are completed within this amount.
Expenditure on the work of providing CCTV facilities in the Phase-II building at Divisional Reference Service Hospital, Amravati G. Amravati should be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance) (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Dispensaries, (1) (A) (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Large Constructions (42100014), 53- Large Constructions.
The Director of Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for providing CCTV facilities in the Phase-II building of the Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Amravati G.
