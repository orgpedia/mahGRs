Introduction: The Central Government. In pursuance of the revised instructions issued for the management of funds disbursement and utilization of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Memorandum dated 23rd March, 2021, the Department of Finance has issued the following instructions: The guidelines have been issued vide circular dated 1st July, 2021.
It was under the purview of the Government to declare Nodal Officers, State Nodal Agencies (SNAs) and Implementing Agencies (IAs) for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes related to Family Welfare being implemented by the Public Health Department for adopting the revised methodology as per the instructions prescribed by the Central Government.
Government's decision:
The Nodal Officers for the following Centrally Sponsored Schemes related to Family Welfare being implemented by the Public Health Department are as follows:
