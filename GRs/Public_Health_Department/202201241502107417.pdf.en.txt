Preamble: Construction of 30 bedded Rural Hospital, Srigonda, G. Ahmednagar with 50 bedded Sub-District Hospital Main Building and Residence at a cost of Rs. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs 10.60 crore was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The budget for the construction of 50 bedded Sub District Hospital Main Building and Residence by upgrading the 30 bedded Rural Hospital, Shrigonda G. Ahmednagar is based on the Rate List of 2019-20. In the budget of this work, provision has been made for the construction of rural hospital, ground floor, residential building (Type-1, and 2) (G+ 3) and residential building (Type-3 and 4) (G-3), fuel gas pipeline, bio-digester, rainwater harvesting, furniture, protection wall, and gate, electrification, water supply and sewerage, fire prevention and lift, etc.

Terms and Conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, (Architecture and Public Works Department) before inviting tenders for the work.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
3) When e-tendering the work, the work should be tendered together without fragmentation.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
5) The price of the proposed work should not be increased unnecessarily according to the situation of the area and the needs of the health institutions.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining the construction permit and occupancy certificate as per the relevant rules of the local rural urban development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and the District Surgeon will be fully responsible for fulfilling the conditions mentioned in the above 1 to 8.
Expenditure on construction of 50 bedded sub-district hospital main building and residence by upgrading 30 bedded rural hospital, Shrigonda G. Ahmednagar should be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts Head 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Urban Health Services (Medical Assistance) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Clinics, (1) (a) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major Constructions (42100014), 53- Major Constructions.
The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of a 50-bed sub-district hospital main building and residence by upgrading the 30-bed rural hospital, Shrigonda G. Ahmednagar.
