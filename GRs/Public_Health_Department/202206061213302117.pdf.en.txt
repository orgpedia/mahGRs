The Proposal:
The Joint Director (Technical), National Health Mission, Mumbai vide Letter No. (1) dated 27.12.2021 has informed that out of the total procurement order of 1397 Vials of Factor FEIBA (500/ U/ Vial) out of the total subsidy of Rs. 399.71 lakh of PIP sanctioned through tender No: E-3328 for the financial year 2020-21, the supply of Factor FEIBA has been made to 9 centres functioning in the State and at present, a total of 200 Vials of Factor FEIBA are available for treatment in all the centres put together and approximately 150 Vials are being used per month and the stock is available for about 1 month. Further, since the tendering process through Haffkine is likely to take approximately 4 months or more, a subsidy of Rs. 200.00 lakh is required for the purchase of 698 Vials for thematic purposes at a cost of Rs.28,613/-per Vial. Accordingly, under NHM Free Drug Services, FMR Code 6.2.21.1 has requested the Central Government to grant administrative approval for procurement of 698 vials at a cost of Rs. 200.00 lakh (Rupees Two Hundred Lakh only) out of the proposed grant of Rs. 235.00 crore.

Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, the Commissionerate has proposed to the Central Government a grant of Rs. 235.00 crore, out of which a grant of Rs. 200.00 lakh (Rupees Two Hundred Lakh only) for the purchase of 698 Vials of NHM Free Drug Services under PIP, under the Restatement Order under the head FMR Code 6.2.21.1 is being administrated subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) Factor FEIBA will be procured from the grant of Rs. 235.00 crore proposed to the Central Government for approval in FMR Code 6.2.21.1 under NHM Free Drug Services by the Commissionerate for the year 2022-23 under the Project Implementation Plan (PIP) under National Health Mission.
(2) Purchases made under NHM Free Drug Services. Haffkine Bio Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. The tender process should be carried out at the level of Parel and Mumbai. After verifying that the procurement specifications are as per requirement and up to date, the technical specifications of the items to be procured should be as per the specifications agreed upon by the concerned experts/ technical committee/ officials.
(3) The technical specification of the items to be procured shall be such as to ensure that the procurement process is not carried out in a manner which will benefit the particular company. Also, the responsibility of sending the requisition as per the technical specifications of the items approved in the ROP will be of the respective head of the function. As the case may be, only the items included in the proposal should be purchased.
(4) Government departments published under the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No: Bhankhas-2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated December 1, 2016.
