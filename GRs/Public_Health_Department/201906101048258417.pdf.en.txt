Administrative approval of the above budget is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The work shall require technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
(2) At the time of tendering of the work, one tender should be issued for all the works mentioned together without separately tendering each work.
3) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals for the same should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
(4) As the said budget is a rough model budget, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Directors of the respective Health Service Boards of the Health Department at the time of technical approval of the detailed budgets of these works.
5) In the course of the actual work, the Department of Environment issued Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T.C. 1, D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014. Before starting the work, you need to confirm the ownership of the premises.
The expenditure on these structures should be met from the grants sanctioned under the head" Demand No.H-8, Main Accounts Title-4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Care and Public Health, 02- Rural Health Care, 04- Public Health, 104- Community Health Centre, (00) (01)- Major Constructions (4210 0391), 53- Major Constructions."
