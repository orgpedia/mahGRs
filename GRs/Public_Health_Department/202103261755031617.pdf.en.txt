Administrative approval is being accorded to the above budget subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The detailed budget and plan of the said work should be approved by the concerned Chief Engineer Public Works Department.
(2) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authority of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
(3) A single tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned in the tender without separate tenders for each work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas. While granting such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Directors of the respective Health Service Boards of the Health Department.
