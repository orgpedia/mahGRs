The Proposal -
As per the agreement between the then government and the Indian Red Cross Society, Bombay in 1938, the rent of the land is payable by the government to the institution concerned. Accordingly, the Indian Red Cross Society, Maharashtra State Branch, Mumbai is sanctioned a grant of ₹10, 116.50 per annum for the rent of the premises. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to provide a grant-in-aid of Rs.10, 116.50 for the year 2025-26.
The Government's Decision-
Under this Government decision, a grant-in-aid of Rs.10 / - (Rupees Ten Thousand One Hundred and Sixteen Rupees Fifty Paisa Only) is being sanctioned to the Indian Red Cross Society, Maharashtra State Branch, Mumbai for the rent of the premises.
