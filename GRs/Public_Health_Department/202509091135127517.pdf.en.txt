The Proposal: -
The government has decided to implement the e-office system effectively in the administrative divisions of the ministry and the process of implementation has started accordingly.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating the procurement of new computers, printers and scanners for implementation of e-office system for the Department of Public Health (itself) through bidding process through GeM portal.
The Government's Decisions:
The 28 new computers, 10 printers and 25 scanners for the Department of Public Health (itself) have been awarded at an estimated cost of Rs. Administrative approval is being granted for procurement of such an amount as 45.84 lakh subject to the following conditions and as per the instructions issued by the Department of Information Technology.
