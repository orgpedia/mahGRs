The Proposal:
The Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is a centrally sponsored scheme being implemented in the state with the participation of the Centre and the state from the financial year 2017-18. The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. As the Central Government has revised the form of the scheme in the year 2023-2024, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 for the financial year 2023-2024 for the benefit to be given to the second child. The Centre has sanctioned Rs. 1,60,67, 020/- (Rupees One Crore, Sixty Lakhs Six Hundred and Twenty Five Thousand Only) as per the State share of 40% of the Fund (Account Head- 2211 1512) and Central share of Rs. 1,07,11, 347/- (Rupees One Crore, Seven Lakhs, Eleven Thousand Three Hundred and Forty-Seven only) (Account Head- 22111477) was under consideration of the Government for sanctioning the same to the concerned agencies from the budgetary provision of 2023-24.

Government's decision:
In order to effectively implement the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, the Central Government has sanctioned Central share (Recurring) of Rs. 1,60,67, 020/- and 40% of the State share of Rs. 1,07,11, 347/- (Rupees One Crore, Seven Lakhs, Eleven Thousand Three Hundred and Forty-Seven only) (Head of Accounts- 2211 1477) is hereby approved to be released to the concerned agencies from the budgetary provision of 2023-24.
The Chief Administrative Officer, State Family Welfare Office, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment regarding the fund disbursed under this Government decision to the Treasury and hand over the fund to the State Health Society-PMMVY for spending on the prescribed matters. For this, the Additional Director, Health Services, State Family Welfare Office, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
3. It shall be certified that the subsidy disbursed under this Government decision shall be credited directly to the accounts of the beneficiaries after it is credited to the SNA opened for implementation of the scheme.

Demand No. N-03, 2211-Family Welfare, 789 Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, (01) Scheme under Scheduled Castes Component Programme, (01) (02) Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (Central Share 60%) (Programme) (2211 1512), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) and Demand No. N-03, 2211-Family Welfare, 789 Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, (01) Scheme under Scheduled Castes Component Programme, (01) (01) Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (State Share 40%) (Programme) (2211 1477), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) to be spent within the limit of the provision sanctioned under this head.
The expenditure is conditional and the utilization certificate of the expenditure made in the prescribed form and the report of the progress of the scheme in the prescribed form should be sent to the Central and State Government from time to time as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The funds will be used for the financial year 2023-2024. The concerned office should ensure that no expenditure is incurred beyond the grant received for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana.
