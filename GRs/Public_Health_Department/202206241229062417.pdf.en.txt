Introduction: Department of Cancer, Cardiology, Kidney Dialysis is functioning at Divisional Referral Hospital, Nashik. As per the Government Decision dated 24.10.2018, approval has been accorded for opening of 30 bedded Paediatric Unit, 30 bedded Neurosurgery Unit, 30 bedded Plastic Surgery Unit and 15 bedded Infant Intensive Care Unit (NICU). Permission has been granted for construction of two floors on the building of Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Nashik. Therefore, it is necessary to provide an alternative site for the functioning blood bank at the Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Nashik. At present, the place where the office of the Deputy Director of Health Services was functioning in the premises of the District Hospital, Nashik, is lying unutilized.
As a result, the office of the Deputy Director, Nashik was shifted to the premises of the District Hospital, Nashik and the blood bank at the Divisional Referral Service Hospital, Nashik was shifted to the building in which the Deputy Director, Health Services, Nashik is currently functioning. The following decision has been taken:
