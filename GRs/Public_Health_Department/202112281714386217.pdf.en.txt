Read- 1) Letter from the Joint Director, Health Services (PHC/ Sub-Centre/ Dispensaries), Mumbai Unique.
Introduction- In the first session of 2005, Ma. The Public Health Minister had assured that a committee would be formed for the primary health centres in the then 15 tribal districts and the MLA would be the chairman of that committee. The matter of forming the committee in accordance with this promise was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The government has decided to set up primary health centres in 16 tribal-dominated districts of the state, including Ahmednagar, Amravati, Chandrapur, Dhule, Gadchiroli, Gondia, Jalgaon, Nagpur, Nanded, Nandurbar, Nashik, Palghar, Pune, Raigad, Thane and Yavatmal.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1. Review the functioning of Primary Health Centres in the Taluk.
2. To make recommendations for improving the efficiency of primary health centres in the taluk.
3. The Taluk Health Officer shall convene the meeting of the Business Review Committee, present the minutes of the meeting and perform other ancillary functions.
The Committee shall meet once in three months.
