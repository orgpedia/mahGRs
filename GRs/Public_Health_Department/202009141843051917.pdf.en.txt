In view of the large number of vacancies in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A Joint Director, Health Services and Deputy Director, Health, in the Commissionerate of Health Services, the Director, Health Services, Mumbai, has issued a notification regarding the filling up of these posts temporarily by downgrading them. The following officers are being transferred and posted in the cadre of Joint Director, Deputy Director, Health Services as proposed vide letter dated 15.5.2020, with the approval of the competent authority. :

The above officers shall not be entitled to regular promotion to the post of Joint Director, Deputy Director, Health Services or claim seniority in the service. Also, there will be no pay scale for the posts of Joint Director, Deputy Director, Health Services.
(3) The above-mentioned postings shall remain in force till the time of regular promotions or availability of candidates from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, whichever is earlier.
