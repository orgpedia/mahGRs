Government Decision: Under the National Iodine Deficiency Disorder Control Programme, 20 temporary posts of Principal, Health and Family Welfare Training Centre, Galgand Survey Team at Nagpur, Kolhapur, Aurangabad, Pune, Nashik and Amravati are included in the revised figure approved by the Public Health Department under the Government Decision named at A. No. 1 and 2 above. Since these posts are providing health facilities to the people of the state as per the requirement, considering the nature and importance of the work of the posts, a proposal will be submitted to the government immediately for the revised figure of these 20 temporary posts. 01.03.2020 to dt. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2020.
