Preamble: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the service of officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, in order to test their eligibility (eligible/ ineligible) to remain in service. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 The procedures and general instructions regarding service review are prescribed under the Government Decision dated 10.06.2019. Accordingly, the services of Group-A officers of the Public Health Department have been referred to Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 24.05.2021, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Public Health). The service review of Group-A cadre officers of Public Health Department beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, was submitted in a cyclical manner for approval of the Committee.
