Preamble: Administrative approval was accorded for procurement of Computing Equipment (Tablets) under RCH Programme under PIP 2017-18 under National Health Mission. However, due to some technical issues, the work could not be taken up. Therefore, as per the proposal of the Additional Director, Health Services, Family Welfare and Parental Care and School Health, Pune, the matter of obtaining administrative approval for the purchase of computer equipment (tablets) under the RCH program in the financial year 2018-19 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the letter dated 23rd October, 2018 issued by the State Family Welfare, Pune, for computerized registration of mothers and children under RCH (National Reproductive Child Health Programme) under National Health Mission, the estimated cost of Rs. 10,000/- to purchase a total of 81 Computing Tablets from the committed subsidy in the PIP approved in 2018-19. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for purchase of Laptops/ Tablets for an amount of Rs. 8.10 Lakhs subject to the following conditions.
