Terms and Conditions:
1) The technical approval of the Chief Engineer, (Vidyut) Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) The Public Works Department is required to approve the external tariff as per the Budget Circular dated 29.10.2018.
3) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the budget to be made available for construction.
4) All works should be tendered together without separate tenders.
5) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(6) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal for the same should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
