Committee's mandate
1. Illegal abortion has been reported at District Hospital, Nashik. An inquiry into the incident will be conducted and a report will be submitted to the government.
2. To find out the lapses in the government machinery responsible for such incidents.
3) Suggest measures to prevent recurrence of such incidents.
Term of the Committee:
The committee will submit its report to the government within a period of 1 month from the date of incident. The committee will have to submit its report within the stipulated time frame.
The members of the committee will be entitled to daily allowances, rented vehicles and accommodation for their work.
