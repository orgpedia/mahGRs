the prelude
Public Health Department, Govt. As on 02.03.2015, the policy for appointment of Maharashtra Medical Officers under Public Health Department on contract basis has been finalized.
At the primary health centres in rural areas, MBBS qualified candidates are not willing to take up appointments at lower salaries or go for post-graduation after taking appointments, leaving the posts of medical officers vacant. Even if there are no medical officers, the expenditure on nursing technicians and other staff, expenditure on building, expenditure on machinery, etc. continue. But people are not getting treatment. In order to attract the medical aspirants to accept appointment on contract basis as the remuneration sanctioned to the contractual medical officers is less, the Department has decided to provide Rs. As per the Government decision dated 02.03.2015, a proposal to increase the fixed remuneration of the Contract Medical Officers, Group-A was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
1) Contractual Medical Officers working under the Public Health Department. The remuneration was fixed as on 02.03.2015. Now the honorarium of the contractual medical officer is being increased as follows.
