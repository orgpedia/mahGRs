Preamble: As per the decision of the State Government dated 26.02.2019, it has been decided to merge the Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. For the financial year 2020-21, an amount of Rs.2210.861 crore has been provided under the head of Accounts for implementation of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. 100,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Crores only) has been provided in the Budget. Out of this amount, Rs. 4 crore has been released. A sum of Rs. 32.82 crore has been disbursed to the State Health Guarantee Society. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP) for payment of premium to the insurance company for the period from 01.10.2020 to 31.12.2020 for the beneficiaries of the scheme.
Government Decision: As the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the budgetary provision under the head shown below for the financial year 2020-21 for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiary family under the scheme is Rs. 32,84,77, 056/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores Eighty Four Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand only) to be transferred to the account of State Health Guarantee Society immediately.
