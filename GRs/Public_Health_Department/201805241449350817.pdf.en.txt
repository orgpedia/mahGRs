Preamble- State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Mulund Residential Building Type-1, Building No. The proposal for administrative approval of the special amendment of 04th Plan amounting to Rs. 325.27/-lakh was under consideration of the Government.
The decision was taken by the government through the State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Mulund Residential Building Type-1, Building No. The total budget of Rs. 325.27/-lakh for the special revision of 04 is based on the table of 2017-18. This budget is being given administrative approval under this government decision subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions 1) The expenditure of this budget shall be met from the available funds.
2) This budget should be attested by the competent authority of the consumer department.
(3) The work shall be subject to the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
