The Proposal:
A proposal for administrative approval of the budget and blueprint for the construction of a 30-bed and 50-bed grade building at Rural Hospital, Chikhli, was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
2. Rural Hospital, Chikhli G. The budget for the construction of the 30 bedded 50 bedded Grade Building at Sub District Hospital, Buldhana is based on the map prepared by the Deputy Chief Architect, Public Works Department, Nagpur. The budget includes construction of buildings, electrification, water supply and sewerage, internal roads, CC drains, parking arrangements, rainwater harvesting, fire prevention, green building concept, furniture, etc. The Goods and Services Tax (GST) has been kept at 12 per cent. The administrative approval is being granted under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions as under:

4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
5) Since the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budget of these works is technically approved by the Public Works Department.
It will be done by the competent authorities of the region.
6) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on the construction of the Grade Building of the 30 bedded 50 bedded Sub-District Hospital at Rural Hospital, Chikhli, G.Buldhana. Expenditure on the said work will be shared under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Clinics), (1) (a) (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Large Constructions (42100014), 53- Large Constructions.
4. The Director, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of a 30 bedded, 50 bedded Grade Building at Rural Hospital, Chikhli G.
