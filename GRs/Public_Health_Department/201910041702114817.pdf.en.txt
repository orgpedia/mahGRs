The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme is being implemented in the State on 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State from the financial year 2017-2018.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total cost of the project and the State Government will contribute 40%. Reference No. for General Component for Q2 of FY 2019-2020 2 orders of Rs. 29,11,21, 886/- has been released by the Central Government to the State. The funds have been directly deposited in the escrow account opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai. 40% of the State share of Rs. 19,40,81, 257/- was under the consideration of the Government for the distribution of funds for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana.
Government's decision:
For effective implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana in the financial year 2019-20, the Central Government has allocated Rs. 19, 40, 81, 257/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores)
