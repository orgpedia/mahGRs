The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana with Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. For the financial year 2022-23, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs 130,91,95, 000 has been allocated for this. For the beneficiaries of this scheme, the premium for the period from April 2023 to June 2023 will be Rs.
32,49,06, 482/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores One Hundred Fifty Five Lakhs Six Thousand Four Hundred Only) to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented by the State Health Guarantee Society, the provision budgeted under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2022-23, for the period April 2023 to June 2023, an amount of Rs. 32,49,06, 482/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores One Hundred Fifty Five Lakhs Six Thousand Four Hundred Only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the State Health Insurance Society.
