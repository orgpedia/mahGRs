Terms and Conditions: 1) Before starting the work, the sample map, layout map as well as the detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
2) Public Works Department Government Resolution No. BDG-2017/ No. 60/ Buildings-2, No. Action to be taken as on 07.07.2017.
3) The tender notice for the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
(4) The work shall require technical approval of the competent authority of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
(5) At the time of tendering of the said work, one tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned together without separate tenders for each work.
(6) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
7) As the said budget is a rough model budget, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Directors of the respective Health Service Boards of the Health Department while approving the detailed budget of these works in technical terms.
