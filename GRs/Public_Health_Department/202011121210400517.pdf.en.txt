GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PURCHASE-2020/ PR. No. 112/ Arogya-6, dated 12th November, 2020 A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse the funds to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) for payment to the Company.
Government Decision: As the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the budgetary provision under the head shown below for the financial year 2020-21 for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiary family under the scheme is Rs. 25,03,21, 306/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Three Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Three Hundred and Six only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
Demand No. T-05 2210- Medical Services and Public Health 03- Rural Health Services Odd-Even Medical 796- Tribal Area Sub-Plan (01)- Scheme under Tribal Area Sub-Plan (01) (06)- Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (State Scheme) (TSP) 2210 G 289 (Datta Mat) 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried)
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Director (Finance and Administration), Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer for the transfer of funds to the Society.
The State Health Guarantee Society shall strictly comply with all the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated 28th October, 2020 of the Tribal Development Department while spending the funds available under the Tribal Sub-Plan.
All concerned should strictly follow the instructions given by the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020 of the Finance Department. It is also certified that the conditions mentioned in the accompanying circular have been fulfilled.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the Government Decision dated 28th October, 2020 of the Tribal Development Department and the concurrence given by the Finance Department vide No. 359/ Expenditure-13, dated 14.10.2020.
