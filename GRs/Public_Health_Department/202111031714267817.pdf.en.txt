The Proposal:
A proposal was under consideration of the government to give administrative approval to the budget for the construction of fire fighting system and water tank in the Indira Gandhi General Hospital, Ichalkaranji and Sub-district Hospital, Gadhinglaj, and Sub-district Hospital, Kasba Bawda, in Kolhapur district.
Government's decision:
The budget includes fire fighting measures for Indira Gandhi General Hospital, Ichalkaranji and Sub District Hospital, Gadhinglaj in Kolhapur district and construction of fire fighting system and water tank for Sub District Hospital, Kasba Bawda.
The rates are based on the 2020-21 schedule. These budgets have been prepared by the Executive Engineer (Vidyut), Public Works Department, Kolhapur. The Superintending Engineer (Vidyut), Public Works Board, Pune has given technical approval to the said budgets.

Terms and Conditions: 1) The technical approval of the Chief Engineer (Vidyut), Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) As per the provisions of the Government Circular dated 29.10.2018 of the Public Works Department, it is mandatory to approve the extern rate in the budget.
3) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
4) The work should be tendered together without separate tenders.
5) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(6) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
8) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
9) While working directly, the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1, D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
10) The responsibility of fulfilling the condition from 1 to 9 above will be of the concerned District Surgeon and Executive Engineer (Vidyut).
