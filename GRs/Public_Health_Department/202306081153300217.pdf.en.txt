Government's decision:
The Budget for construction of accommodation at Sub District Hospital, Brahmapuri G. Chandrapur with 100 beds is based on the Rate List 2021-22. The budget includes provision for furniture, electrification, water supply and sewerage, electrification, fire prevention, parking, internal roads, protection walls and gates, etc. 1409.00 Lakhs for construction of Type-3 and Type-4 flats and Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget and Plans for an amount of Rs. 1454.00 lakhs.
This administrative approval is granted subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.

2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
3) When e-tendering the work, the work should be tendered together without breaking it into pieces.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
5) The price of the proposed work should not be increased unnecessarily according to the situation of the area and the needs of the health institutions.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T.C. 1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
9) After examining the maps of the hospital, instructions were given to revise the maps of the hospital taking into account the oxygen pipeline required for the hospital, the place for air conditioners and the planning of their duct, the facilities to be provided to the disabled, the pathways to be done in the hospital for those persons, etc. and the functionality of the same. The maps of the hospital should be revised accordingly in consultation with experts in hospital designing and doctors and staff associated with the hospital
10) Sub-District Hospital, Brahmapuri District, within the limit of administrative approval price by incorporating amendments in the budget as per the guidelines of IPHS 2022 issued by the Central Government.
A 100-bed residential building will be constructed in Chandrapur.
11) It should be ensured that the area of the proposed building is in accordance with the permissible standards of the staff.
12) The concerned Deputy Director of Health Services and District Surgeon should review the construction at the field level from time to time.
The entire responsibility of fulfilling the conditions mentioned in 1 to 12 above will be of the concerned Executive Engineer, Public Works Department and District Surgeon.
