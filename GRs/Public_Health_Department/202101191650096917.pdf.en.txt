A Tender Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Health) vide order dated 28.10.2010. The Tender Committee has qualified the tender submitted by Me BVG India

As per the Government Decision dated 28th March, 2013, the Operation Order was issued to MeBVG India to operate Emergency Response Services (EMS) of a total of 937 Ambulances, 233 Advance Life Support (ALS) and 704 Basic Life Support (BLS) across the State in the next 5 years. The scheme has been operationalized w.e.f. 26.01.2014. In 2014-15 and 2015-16, the scheme was partially funded by the Central Government through the National Health Mission and the remaining was being disbursed through the State Plan. As per the decision of the Government in the aforesaid Act No. 2, the scheme was transferred from National Health Mission to Director, Health Services with effect from 2016-17. The scheme is being implemented from 2016-17. As per the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed with the service provider in this regard, the contract expired on 31.01.2019, however, as a matter of urgency, the service provider was given extension to pay the February 2019 dues at the rate of January 2019, as per the Government decision dated 31.03.2019, so that the ambulance service can continue without any interruption in patient care. Thereafter, as per the decision taken by the Committee headed by A.M.S.
