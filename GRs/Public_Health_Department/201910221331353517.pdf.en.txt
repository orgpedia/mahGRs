Preamble: Under the Government Order dated 01.12.2016 issued in the context of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, a revised rule book on procedures for office procurement to be carried out by the Government Departments has been issued. Para 2.9.3 of the said rule book provides for constitution of the Ministerial Administrative Department (itself) Procurement Committee. Considering this, the government was considering the formation of a purchase committee for the purchase of items required for the public health department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of paragraph no. 2.9.3 of the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by the Government Departments in the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the following Procurement Committees are being constituted for the Public Health Department (itself).
