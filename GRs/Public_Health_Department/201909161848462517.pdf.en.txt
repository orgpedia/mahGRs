Introduction: The growing area/ buildings of the government hospital and the problems arising regarding the safety of the hospital and the issues/ incidents like the baby running away from the hospital. Also, in view of the attacks on medical officers, a proposal was under consideration of the government to hire security guards through external agencies for the year 2019-2020 in various hospitals in the health care system such as rural hospitals/ sub-district hospitals/ district hospitals/ referral service hospitals and to give administrative approval for the expenses incurred.
Government Decision: Considering the increasing area/ buildings of the Government Hospital and the problems arising regarding the security of the Hospital, administrative approval is being given to the expenditure incurred for the appointment of security guards by external agencies in various hospitals like Rural Hospitals/ Sub-District Hospitals/ District Hospitals/ Referral Service Hospitals in the health care system for the year 2019-2020 as follows:
