GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the National Tuberculosis Control Programme (NTCP), 1310 posts have been created as per the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, out of which 831 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 831 temporary posts, 80% of the 575 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the decision of the Government in Table 2 above. The remaining 264 temporary posts will be filled up as per the Government Resolution No. 3. The period was extended till 29.02.2020. In order to provide continuity of health services, 264 posts are being approved to be continued from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020.
