Introduction- Vadgavamaval (Kanhe) Ta. A proposal to upgrade the 30-bed rural hospital in Maval, Pune district to a 50-bed sub-district hospital and set up a 20-bed trauma care centre was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision- Under this decision, approval is being given to upgrade the 30-bed rural hospital in Maval, G. Pune to a 50-bed sub-district hospital and to set up a 20-bed trauma care center.
Steps will be taken separately to acquire land and construct posts for the 50-bed sub-district hospital and 20-bed trauma care centre.
