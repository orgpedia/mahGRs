GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the National Tuberculosis Control Programme (NTCP), 1310 posts have been created as per the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, out of which 831 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 831 temporary posts, 80% of the 575 temporary posts have been made permanent as per the decision of the Government in Table 2 above. The remaining 256 temporary posts will be filled as per the Government decision of Reference No. 3. The deadline has been extended to 28/02/2018. In order to provide continuous health care services, 256 posts in the attached list are being approved to be continued from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018.
