The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has issued a notification under the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015. As per the notification dated 04/01/2018, seven services have been notified. Similarly, in respect of notifying online services under the Public Service Acts, MahaOnline Limited has been declared as the system integrator and the rate per service has been fixed. Accordingly, Maha Online Ltd. has been formed to provide this service to the citizens. The proposal to give administrative approval to the works to be carried out by them was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
MahaOnline Limited has been granted administrative approval to develop, integrate, etc. the services provided under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 as follows:
