Introduction: The budget for the construction of 100 bedded Women and Newborn Hospital at Sangli has been prepared by the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Sub-Division, Sangli and certified by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Miraj, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Kolhapur Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and District Surgeon, Government Hospital, Sangli. This is based on the 2017-18 budget. Earlier, a high-powered committee had approved the construction of a 100-bed women and newborn hospital in Sangli. It was approved in the meeting held on March 4, 2021.
However, under the National Health Mission, a new women's hospital has been sanctioned at Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Government Hospital, Sangli, which already has the facility. Therefore, the department had submitted a proposal before the high-powered committee to shift the hospital to Miraj, G. Sangli, instead of Sangli. Accordingly, the budget and plan for the construction of a 100-bed Women and Newborn Hospital in Miraj, District Sangli, instead of Sangli. A high-level committee, headed by the Chief Secretary, It has been approved in the meeting dated 29.11.2021. The matter of granting administrative approval to the budget was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
