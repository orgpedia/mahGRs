The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed as Medical Officers Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. These appointments have been made by the independent selection board as the first appointment in the government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation.
2. Under the decision of the General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probation period of government service officers/ employees and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of termination of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the government.
Government Order:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the following Medical Officers Group-A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as shown in the table given in this paragraph, have satisfactorily completed the requisite requirements for the expiry of their period of probation, and their period of probation shall be as indicated in the column No. 1 against their names in the table. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
