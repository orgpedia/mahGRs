GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: COMMITTEE-2023/ PR No.343/ HEALTH-6 dated 02.11.2023
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: (i) To give directions, make recommendations, call for reports, adopt hospitals, take action to all State Level, Divisional and District Level Committees under the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Ayushman Bharat Yojana under this Committee.
2) To recommend, adopt, call for reports, give directions, take action to all other departments (National Health Mission, Labour, National Child Health Programme).
3) Fund availability (Ayushman Bharat Yojana, Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Social Responsibility Fund) 4) There will be the power to form a sub-committee of medical experts to promote patient care.
Dr Omprakash Sadashiv Shete will be the cell head of the committee. They will get honorarium and other facilities as per the prevailing policy of the government.
The office of the Committee shall be at the State Health Guarantee Society, Jeevanadayee Bhavan, Worli, Mumbai.
The provisions of the Government Resolution dated 28th July, 2023 shall continue to be in force.
