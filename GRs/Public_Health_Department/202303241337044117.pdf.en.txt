The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. For the financial year 2022-23, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, the basic provision under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) is Rs. 70 crore and Rs. 29,46,04, 061/- aggregating to Rs. A sum of Rs 99,46,04, 061/- has been provided in the Budget. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for payment of premium for the period April, 2023 to June, 2023 to the insurance company. The proposal to disburse conditional funds to the tune of Rs. 29,46,04, 000/- to the State Health Guarantee Society was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the premium for payment of insurance premium for the quarter April, 2023 to June, 2023 of the beneficiary family under the scheme will be Rs. A conditional amount of Rs. 29,46,04, 000/- (Rupees Thirty-nine Crores Forty-six Lakhs Four Thousand only) is being handed over to the Controlling Officer for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society.
The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
