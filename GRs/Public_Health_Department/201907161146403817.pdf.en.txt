Preamble: Under the National Health Mission, the matter of obtaining administrative approval for providing True Nat services from external agencies from the grant made available under the Revised National Tuberculosis Programme in the financial year 2019-20 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Joint Director, Health Services (Procurement Cell), Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for providing Tru Nat services from external agencies from the grant made available under Revised National Tuberculosis Programme during the financial year 2019-20 under National Health Mission. The proposal has been approved by the Commissioner (Health Services) and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai. Under the Revised Tuberculosis Control Programme, in 112 DMCs with a high number of TB patients in the state, the facility of definitive diagnosis of TB and Rifiampicin Resistant Test is available to the maximum number of patients by identifying external institutions for screening through TruNat.
