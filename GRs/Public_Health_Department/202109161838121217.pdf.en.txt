The Proposal:
Some of the medical officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100-177500) were on unauthorised absence for a long period of time without obtaining prior permission of the Government on various grounds. Public health is an essential service.
In view of the shortage of officers, the government was considering the matter of reinstating these unauthorized absentee medical officers in government service after their unauthorized absence subject to the prescribed terms and conditions.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A The Medical Officers mentioned in the table below in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Group-A were unauthorisedly absent for a long time. The Medical Officers mentioned in the Table below are being recruited subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Table under this paragraph and are being given such posting as may be indicated against their names in the Table below for joining the post of Medical Officer Group-A.
