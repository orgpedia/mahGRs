The Proposal:
The Director (Finance& Accounts), National Health Mission, Mumbai had requested for release of pending Central Account balance of Rs. 1625.87 lakh and State Account balance of Rs. 1225.91 lakh for general category under National AYUSH Mission vide Reference Letter No. Further, the Commissioner Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai vide letter No. 2 to the Commissioner, NHM, Mumbai has informed that the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission, launched in September, 2014, has been designed to provide AYUSH health services and education to the general public, enhance the quality of Ayurvedic, Homoeopathic and Unani medicines and supply of quality medicinal plants in a sustained manner. It is necessary to allocate the necessary funds.
Meanwhile, as per the approval given by the Finance Department, out of the total proposed amount, Rs. 406.47 lakh (25%) as Central share and Rs. 306.47 lakh (25%) as State share, a total of Rs. 712.94 lakh has been disbursed as subsidy. The total Central share is Rs. 1219.40 lakh and the State share is Rs. 919.44 lakh.

During the financial year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 1219.40 lakh under the head" 2210 G135" and Rs. 919.44 lakh under the head" 2210 G144" of the State Accounts has not been sanctioned. Accordingly, in the Budget Estimates 2021-22, an amount of Rs. 2725.82 lakh has been provided under the head" 2210 G135" and Rs. 1817.21 lakh under the head" 2210 G144," amounting to Rs. 1219.40 lakh and Rs. 919.44 lakh respectively.
Further, as mentioned in the" Statement-2," the Central share of Rs. 1219.40 lakh (60%) under the head" 2210 G132" and State share of Rs. 919.44 lakh (40%) under the head" 2210 G144" is being re-validated in the Budget Estimates 2021-22.
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Food and Supplies Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment to the treasury and hand over the amount to the Commissioner Health Services and Mission Director, National AYUSH Mission, State AYUSH Society for spending on the prescribed matters. The Joint Director (a. And p. ), Health Services, Mumbai are being declared as" Controlling Authorities."
