The Proposal:
A proposal for administrative approval of the budget and maps for the construction of the main building of the Rural Hospital, Ghugus G. Chandrapur, at a cost of Rs. 1117.10 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget for the construction of the main building of the Rural Hospital, Ghugus G. Chandrapur is based on the 2017-18 tariff list. The budget has earmarked 5 per cent for internal and 6 per cent for external electrification.
Also, 12 per cent GST has been imposed on it. Provisions have been made as per the Recapitulation Sheet attached and administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution for the construction of the Hospital at a cost of Rs. 1055.29 lakhs subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
(2) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.

3) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
4) As the cost of the proposed work may increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposals should be made by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
6) If the work is to be done according to the new model maps, the architectural plan should be approved by the private architect appointed by the Public Health Department. The newly appointed architect and district surgeon will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the rules of the local rural development agency. Also, the letter requesting this permission must be signed by the district surgeon.
The maps and architectural plans of these works do not require separate approval from the Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect of the Public Works Department.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Expenditure on construction of main building at Rural Hospital, Ghugus G. Chandrapur should be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health, 02- Rural Health Services, 04- Public Health, 104- Community Health Centre, (00) (01) Major Constructions (4210 0391), 53- Major Constructions.
4. The Director, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to make budgetary provision for the construction of the main building of the Rural Hospital, Ghugus G. Chandrapur.
These orders are being issued under the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Government Decision, Finance Division, No. 2013/ Q. No. 30/ Regulations, dated 17.04.2015 in Annexure, Part I, Sub-Division-V, serial No. 1, Paragraph No. 134.
