The Maharashtra Medical and Health Services has decided to transfer the services of District Surgeon Cadre Officers in Group A to the Health Department of the Municipal Corporation.

Government Order: As the spread of corona infection is increasing rapidly in the state, especially in the municipal corporation areas, it is necessary to take measures to control it. For this, the services of the following officers in the cadre of District Surgeon in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 in the Pay Matrix) are being temporarily transferred to the post of Medical Officer of Health at the Municipal Corporation establishment indicated against their name till further orders.
