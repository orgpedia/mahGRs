2. The said promotion is being given as per the provisions of the Service Entry Rules for the post of' District Health Officer'.
3. Ma. The High Court dismissed the petition No. 2797/2015. Against the decision given on 04.08.2017, the State Govt. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. Accordingly, the promotion. A Special Leave Petition No. The vacancy in the open category in the promotion quota subject to the Judgment No. 28306/2017 is being made purely on temporary basis as per the service.
The said temporary promotion is being made subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
5. The said promotion is only temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in the post. Also, due to this promotion, the concerned officer will not get any rights of regular promotion i.e. seniority.
The officers who have been promoted shall give the option of fixation of pay within one month from the date of assumption of charge of the post after promotion, failing which their fixation of pay shall be governed by the provisions of Rule No. 3 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
7. The concerned officers should be relieved immediately to join their postings after promotion.
The concerned officers should report to the Deputy Director, Health Services, Director, Health Services, Mumbai and the Government within one month at the place of their posting. Any political pressure to change the post will invite disciplinary action under the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
8. Promoted officers to join the new post within the stipulated time after their discharge. The officers who do not appear on promotion within the prescribed time will be deemed to be unwilling to be promoted and their promotion will be cancelled. They will also be taken advantage of the promised progress scheme. Also, in case of non-acceptance of promotion, the General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 3030/ K-12, D.
Action will be taken as per the provisions of 12.09.2016. In this case, the concerned Deputy Director, Health Services i.e. the concerned medical officers should be relieved immediately to join the post of promotion. Also, a compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time frame as to whether they appear on time at the place of posting given by the promotion.
