The Proposal:
To extend the coverage of the scheme to all citizens for a limited period so that COVID-19 patients do not have to bear the financial burden of getting treatment in an authorized private hospital and to provide health assurance and financial relief to all citizens during the COVID-19 pandemic.
Benefit of treatment under Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana should be available to the beneficiary patients as well as other patients and the treatment methods reserved for government hospitals should be available.
The decision has been taken with a view to ensure availability of necessary resources in the context of prevention of COVID-19 pandemic to the doctors, other staff and ancillary staff of the hospitals empanelled under the scheme, with effect from 23rd May, 2020. Government's decision:
In view of the current number of COVID-19 patients in the state of Maharashtra and the benefits being provided to the citizens of the state under the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, in order to provide health assurance and financial relief to all the citizens in the current pandemic crisis, the scheme implemented under the Government decision dated May 23, 2020, for the benefit of all the citizens in the hospitals adopted under the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, is being extended for a period of three months from October 1, 2021 to December 31, 2021 as follows.
1) 996 treatments for beneficiaries with white ration cards (excluding farmer families in 14 drought affected districts) and beneficiaries without ration cards. The benefit will be admissible on the basis of the identity cards mentioned on May 23, 2020.
2). Out of 67 specified treatments in Annexure-C of the Government Decision dated 23.05.2020, 8 treatments will be provided through private empanelled hospitals at CGHS rates.
3) 20 treatments will be applicable for Covid patients.
(2) The decision of the Government shall be taken with the concurrence of the Planning Department and not by the Finance Department. Reference No. 477/2021/ Expenditure 13 dated 20.10.2021 is being issued as per the agreed terms.
