As per Rule 10 (4)& 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, the officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity are to be reviewed and given their services at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.

The government has a policy of premature retirement. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 01 here. The Government Decision dated 10.06.2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding service review. Accordingly, for the review of services of officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A District Surgeons, District Health Officers, Specialists and above (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 and above in the Pay Matrix) and Officers of General State Services, Group A (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500 and above in the Pay Matrix) under the Commissionerate of Health Services, Reference No. 02 here. As per the Government decision dated 20.02.2020, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department. Accordingly, the Departmental Review Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department, has issued a prescribed proposal to review the services of the officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A District Surgeons, District Health Officers and Specialists and above (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 and above in the Pay Matrix) and General State Services, Group A Chief Administrative Officers (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500 in the Pay Matrix) in the age group of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Presented in the meeting dated 11.07.2022. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, which evaluated the service of the said officers, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the officers who have been found eligible to continue in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
The decision was taken by the departmental review committee. As per the recommendation of the Committee in its meeting held on 11.07.2022, the services of the following 35 (thirty five) officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A District Surgeons, District Health Officers, Specialists and above in the pay matrix S-23: 67700-208700 and above and Chief Administrative Officers of General State Services, Group A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100-177500 in the pay matrix) under the Commissionerate of Health Services are:
