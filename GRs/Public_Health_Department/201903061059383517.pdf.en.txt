Government Order:
The Government Order No. 3 read above is being superseded by this Order and the revised order is being issued as follows:
Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai has mentioned in his letter regarding the revised postings after Post Graduate Diploma Courses to the Medical Officers who have passed and failed in the CPS Course under the Directorate of Health Services in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale Rs. 15,600-39, 100+ Grade Pay Rs. As per the proposal submitted vide letter dated 15.01.2019, considering the educational qualification of the concerned medical officers, vacancies in health institutions and administrative exigencies, the following postings are being given in Form A to the medical officers who have passed in the first attempt, in Form B to the medical officers who have passed in the second attempt, in Form C to the medical officers who have failed in the second attempt, in Form D to the medical officers who have not appeared in the examination for the second attempt, in Form E to the medical officers who have not appeared for the examination and in Form F to the medical officers who have passed the post-graduation in private as well as in-service.

The Medical Officers posted under this Order shall join the place of their revised posting within 7 days. Disciplinary action will be proposed against the concerned medical officer if he does not join after the prescribed time.
The Medical Officers who have been given posting after passing the Post Graduate Diploma Course under this Order are bound to serve the Government (i.e. in the health institutions under the Public Health Department only) for the prescribed period. In case of non-service of prescribed period by the Medical Officers mentioned in Form-A and Form-B, Rs. A fine of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees fifty lakhs only) shall be payable.
5. The Medical Officers mentioned in Form-" C," Form-" D" and Form-" E" shall be awarded as they have failed the Post Graduate Diploma Course. As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 14.12.2011, three increments will not be admissible.
6. The Medical Officers mentioned in Form-" C" are being given temporary posting under this order as they have failed in the Post Graduate Diploma Course. After the medical officer is passed out, the directorate should submit a proposal for his revised posting to the government without any delay.
7. Read to the Medical Officer mentioned in Form-D No. 2 The temporary posting was given after failing to comply with the order. However, since they have failed in the second attempt also, their revised posting is being done as indicated in Form-D.
