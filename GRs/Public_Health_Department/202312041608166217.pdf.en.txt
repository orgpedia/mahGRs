Under Sambhajinagar, 02 temporary posts were created in the Dental Department of Rural Hospital, Phulambri, G. Sambhajinagar. As there was a need for these posts, the matter of extension of these posts from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 was under consideration of the Government.

The salary of these temporary posts should be spent in the next head of account and this expenditure should be met from the approved grant for the year 2023-24.
