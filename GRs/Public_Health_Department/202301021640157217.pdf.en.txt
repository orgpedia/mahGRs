Introduction: The National Board of Examinations (NBE), an autonomous body of the Government of India, conducts examinations for various specialist courses leading to the award of Diplomate of National Board (DNB) degree/ diploma. The NBE conducts a national level common entrance test for admission to this course and seats are filled through centralized counselling on the basis of merit.
The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India had informed about the introduction of DNB Diploma courses in district level district hospitals. Accordingly, the Department of Public Health had submitted an application to the NBE. After the evaluation, the government was considering allowing the DNB diploma courses to be started in the institutions which have been approved to implement the DNB diploma courses and also to determine the admission process.
New Delhi: The National Board of Examinations (NBE), New Delhi has approved the conduct of DNB Diploma courses from the academic year 2021-22 in the recognized health institutions (District Hospitals) under the Department of Public Health. 50% of the total seats available for this course will be reserved for Medical Officers in the service of this department. The Director, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the competent authority to conduct the admission process for the seats in these institutions, complete the admission process in the prescribed manner based on the merit list of the candidates and the merit list. A committee has also been formed to carry out the process.
