Preamble-" The Government is considering to provide funds to the Tertiary Care Cancer Centre at Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Regional Cancer Care Centre, Nagpur under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (State Share) of National Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke Control Programme (NCDCPC) as per the State Share equivalent to 40% of the funds released by the Government of India in 2016-17 for the SCSP component. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision For the Financial Year 2017-18, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke, the Government has approved the release of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 120.61 lakh (Rupees One Crore Twenty Lakh Sixty-one Thousand only) equivalent to 40% of the amount sanctioned by the Central Government under the Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan (SCSP) for the establishment of Tertiary Care Cancer Centre (TCCC) at Rashtrasant Tukadoji Regional Cancer Research Centre, Nagpur.
The cost of the proposal No. R-12210-Medical Services and Public Health, 31-Supplementary Grants (Non-salaried) to be covered under the National Non-Communicable Diseases Control Programme under the head National Control Programme for Prevention of Diabetes, Cancer and Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke, Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan (SCSP) (2210 F729) for the financial year 2017-18.
The funds will be disbursed to Joint Director, Health Services (Budget and Administration), Pune as Controlling Officer for payment to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Regional Cancer Hospital and Research Centre, Tukadoji Chowk, Manewada Road, Nagpur-440 027.
