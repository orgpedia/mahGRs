The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019, Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being merged with Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and both the schemes are being implemented together from 01.04.2020 in the State. United India Insurance, a public sector insurance company, will provide a benefit of Rs. Health insurance cover of up to Rs 1.5 lakh is also being provided. For each family, Rs. 797/- is being paid to the insurance company. The contract with the said insurance company has expired on 31.03.2023. At the moment, it is proposed to improve the combined scheme. This process will take some time. Therefore, the government was considering extending the contract.
Government's decision:
The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) dated 27.03.2020 with United India Insurance Company, selected for Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, has been extended with effect from 01.04.2023. The period is being extended till 30.06.2023. During the period of extension, the Government is approving payment of premium to the insurance company at the rate of Rs. 855/- (Rupees Eight Hundred and Fifty Five only) per family per year for the beneficiary units of the combined Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
