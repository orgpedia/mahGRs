Preamble- In the second session of the year 2015, Sarvashri Amar Singh Pandit and others spoke on the topic of" Patients suffering in Beed District Hospital." The notice issued by Member of Legislative Council No. More than 125. In pursuance of the discussion held in the Legislative Assembly on 21.7.2015, a Committee under the Chairmanship of the Principal Secretary will be constituted to investigate the entire affairs of Beed and take a decision in this regard. I assure the House that no one will be spared if they are guilty, no one will be able to interfere politically, an impartial inquiry will be conducted and a report will be presented in the House in the next session. The Minister (Public Health and Family Welfare) has assured the legislature.
The government was contemplating to appoint a three-member committee to investigate the affairs of the Beed district related to the Public Health Department and to fulfill this promise. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
A three-member committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission (Member) and Joint Director, Health Services (Hospitals-State Level) (Member Secretary).
The above committee will investigate the affairs of the Beed district related to the Public Health Department and submit a report to the government as soon as possible.
