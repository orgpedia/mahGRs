Government's decision:
The budget for the renovation of 100 bedded Women and Child Hospital at District Hospital, Dhule is based on the 2017-18 tariff schedule. The budget has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Dhule. The budget has a provision of 1 per cent for contingency expenses and 0.5 per cent for labour insurance, according to which a total of Rs. 363.81 lakh is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
