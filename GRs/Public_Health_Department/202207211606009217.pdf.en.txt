In order to provide emergency medical assistance to the victims of disaster in the state, the National Health Mission (NHM) has entered into an agreement with BBVG India, a service provider, to provide Emergency Response Service (EMS) to a total of 937 ambulances, 233 Advance Life Support (ALS) and 704 Basic Life Support (BLS), across the state for 5 years, with effect from 26th January, 2014. Thereafter, the scheme was transferred from National Health Mission to Director, Health Services with effect from 2016-17, as per the decision of the Government in No. 2 above.
