Lonikar, Medical Superintendent, Sub-District Hospital, Kalamnuri, G. Hingoli. The representatives of the local people and the officials concerned were present. The District Surgeon, District Hospital, Hingoli, had appointed a three-member inquiry committee to conduct a detailed inquiry into the matter. A three-member probe committee has been formed to investigate the matter.
Following this, the Directorate General of Health Services, Mumbai has issued a circular. :
(1) The District Surgeon, District Hospital, Hingoli had appointed a three-member inquiry committee on the basis of the complaint against Dr. Lonikar. The committee has given its opinion on the proposal to transfer Dr Lonikar after conducting a detailed inquiry.
(2) Dr. Lonikar was found absent during the visit of the Maharashtra Legislature Estimates Committee to Hingoli district.
(3) Complaints have been lodged against Dr. Lonikar by the medical officer of the National Child Health Mission and the staff of the sub-district hospital, Kalamnuri.
(4) The hunger strike was held as no action was taken on the complaint against Dr. Lonikar.
The hunger-striker has demanded the district surgeon, Hingoli, to propose Dr Lonikar's transfer and send him on leave.
The Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS) has recommended the transfer of Dr Lonikar on the basis of various complaints against him and the report of the three-member inquiry committee.
Considering the above, the Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS). Lonikar, Medical Superintendent, Sub District Hospital, Kalamnuri, G. Hingoli.
