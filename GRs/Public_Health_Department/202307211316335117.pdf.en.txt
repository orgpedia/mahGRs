Note: Letter No. Sanase/ Cell-3/ Te-7/ Upji, Mul 100/ (703)/ Nag Man/ 1713-15/2023, dated 24.02.2023 from the Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai.
The proposal-
A proposal to upgrade the sub-district hospital children, G. Chandrapur from 50 beds to 100 beds sub-district hospital was under consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
The government has decided to upgrade the sub-district hospital from 50 beds to 100 beds.
Separate action will be taken regarding the construction and creation of posts by acquiring land in the prescribed manner for the 100-bed sub-district hospital in this category.
