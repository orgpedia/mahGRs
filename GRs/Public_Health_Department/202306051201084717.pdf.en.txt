Government Order- Reference No. As per the notification mentioned in clause 1, the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai has been declared as the Head of Department for Group C and D cadre employees under the Commissionerate of Health Services
Now, as per this Government Order, the powers of sanction of study leave to the Group-C and D employees of the Health Services Commissionerate are being delegated to the Commissioner, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai, as the Head of the Department, if the concerned employees are eligible for study leave as per the prescribed rules.
(3) Notwithstanding the fact that in the case of grant of study leave, in the past, in-service training of the employees concerned was referred to as deputation, the period of training indicated in such deputation should be treated as study leave and in such case, the remaining study leave should be granted as permissible. Further, after the date of this Government Order, any proposal for sanction of new study leave should be disposed of at the level of Commissioner, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai as above.
