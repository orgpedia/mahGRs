Preface- Today's the day. The jurisdiction of the primary health centre in Kalyan G. Thane has been included in the Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation. The government was considering shifting the primary health centre in Kalyan to Thane. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has taken a decision. As the jurisdiction of the primary health centre at Kalyan G. Thane has been included in the Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation, the primary health centre at that place will be located at Mauje Narivali. The transfer to Murbad G.Thane is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the primary health center can be operational in the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
