Preface: The Living. As the rural hospital at Jivti G. Chandrapur was sanctioned, the matter of shifting the primary health centre there was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: 2. The primary health centre at Jivti G Chandrapur has been approved as a rural hospital. The shifting of Jivti to G. Chandrapur is being approved subject to the following conditions: Terms and Conditions: 1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the Primary Health Centre can function at the shifted place.
2) After the rural hospital, Jivati becomes operational, the primary health centre there should be shifted to Mauje Vani B.T. Jivati.
3) The temporary arrangement of the building in which the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. Insofar as either
