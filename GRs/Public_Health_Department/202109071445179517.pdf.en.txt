P.P./ J.No. 1994/21, Dy. 1.07.2021 5) of Director, Health Services, Mumbai vide letter No. 29.07.2021 Preamble: As per Rule 10 (4)& 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them from Government service prematurely. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 01 here. The Government Decision dated 10.06.2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding service review. Accordingly, for the review of services of officers of the cadre of District Surgeon, District Health Officer and Specialist in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 in the Pay Matrix) under the Commissionerate of Health Services, Reference No. 02 here. As per the Government decision dated 20.02.2020, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department.
The Departmental Review Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department, has recommended for review of the services of the officers of the cadre of District Surgeon, District Health Officer and Specialist in Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 in the Pay Matrix) of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services under the Commissionerate of Health Services at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Presented in the meeting dated 27.08.2021. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, which evaluated the service of the said officers, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the officers who have been found eligible to remain in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.

Government Decision: In pursuance of the recommendation made by the Committee in its meeting held on 27.08.2021, it is hereby approved to continue the services of 07 (Seven) officers in the cadre of District Surgeon, District Health Officer and Specialist in Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 in the Pay Matrix) of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services under the Commissionerate of Health Services in the form attached herewith at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
The service book of the officers mentioned in the attached form should be taken on record that they have been approved to continue the service after their 50/55 years of age or after completing 30 years of qualifying service.
