Government Decision: Under the National Health Mission, it is very important that the government hospitals and ambulances of various health institutions used in the health department are always functional for patient care. Ambulances are of immense importance in the current Covid-19 situation. Accordingly, the demand for the necessary tyres for the ambulance vehicle has been received from various vehicle owners across the state. The validity of the earlier tender for tyres in the year 2019-20. expired on 15.09.2020. Therefore, it is necessary to urgently supply the necessary tires for the ambulance vehicle. Accordingly, the office of the Deputy Director, Health Services, (Transport) Pune-1 has sanctioned Rs. 1,56,86, 055/- is expected to be spent under National Health Mission.
