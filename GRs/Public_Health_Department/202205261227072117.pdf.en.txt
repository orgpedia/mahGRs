Director (Administration), State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai No. 1/1259-60/ 22/ dated 18.1.2022 GOVERNMENT ORDER: Dr. Chhagan Gopinath Nirmal, Medical Officer Group A, State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Chikalthana, Aurangabad, working in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical Insurance Service, Medical Officer, Group-A (Pay Scale S 20:56100-177500) has been nominated by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide order dated 16.12.1998 of the Public Health Department. Nirmal has completed 20 years of qualifying service as per the provisions of Rule 66 (1) of the M.N.S. (Pension) Rules, 1982. He has requested for voluntary retirement with effect from 16.12.2021 by giving one month notice vide letter dated 16.11.2021. He has not withdrawn the said notice and has voluntarily retired with effect from 16.12.2021. Department of Finance The Appointment Authority has full powers to reduce the period of 3 months notice of voluntary retirement as per delegated powers in Rule 10 (5), 65 and 66 of the M.N.S. (Pension) Rules, 1981 in Annexure 1 to Government Decision No. Seniway 1001/130/ Service-4, dated 2.6.2003. Dr. Chhagan Gopinath Nirmal, Medical Officer Group-A is being declared as voluntarily retired with effect from 16.12.2021 (after mid-day) as per the provisions of Rule No.66 (1) (2) (4) of the (Pension) Rules, 1982.
