The Proposal:
Out of the grant received under the National Urban Health Mission (NUHM) in the Action Plan approved under National Health Mission (NHM) for the financial year 2019-20, the amount required for Vulnerable Mapping in 95 cities has been estimated at Rs. The matter of getting administrative approval for the printing cost proposal of Rs 34.58 lakh was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The National Health Mission (NHM) has received the approval for its action plan for the financial year 2019-20. ASHAs are expected to carry out Vulnerable Mapping at NW and Cuttack Mandal level. For this, the government has issued the FMR Code U. 10.2.2 Mapping of Slums and Vulnerable Population in Metro Cities/ Other Cities and Towns dia Rs. A sum of Rs. 60.00 lakh has been sanctioned. Vulnerable mapping will be done door-to-door through ASHA workers from this fund. The estimated cost of printing and courier is Rs. A proposal for administrative approval for the project costing Rs 34.58 lakh has been submitted to the government by the Commissioner (Health Services) and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai.
