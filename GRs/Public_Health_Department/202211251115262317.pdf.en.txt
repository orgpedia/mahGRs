The proposal-
Mr. J. G. Walvi, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India has been appointed as Secretary, Ministry of Home Affairs under the Indian Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Act No. 15/1872) as" Registrar of Marriage Registration" is being appointed till further orders.
2. Mr. J. G. Valavi, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai to work as" Registrar of Marriage Registration" Rs. 100/- per month is also allowed.
Mr. J. G. Valavi, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai, shall strictly comply with the provisions of Sections 54, 55 and 62 of the Indian Christian Marriage Act, 1872.
The marriage certificate certified and issued every 3 months by Mr. J. G. Valavi, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai, should be sent to the Health Department and the concerned Registrar of Marriages in the matter of marriage registration as well as to the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Pune.
5. Notification regarding appointment of Shri J.G. Valvi, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Registrar of Marriage Registration is being published in the Gazette of Government of Maharashtra, Part-IVA.
