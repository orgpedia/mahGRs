3. Drivers hired through outsourcing
Administrative approval is granted for the service shown subject to the following terms and conditions:
There are:
Terms and Conditions
1) The number of drivers should be determined according to the number of ambulances operating in the hospital.

2) When working through external agencies, the Government has issued Decision No. SGA-1506/ No. 91/ Work-5D. to be complied with the provisions of 08.11.2006.
3) E-tendering process for all the hospitals should be carried out simultaneously in the prescribed manner and the external system should be selected in a very transparent manner.
4) The service provider should be identified at the state level or department level or district level.
5) The maximum number of drivers allowed for the hospital should be approved by the concerned District Surgeon as per the local requirement.
6) The tender document should include the condition that the next month's payment will not be paid unless the drivers' salary is confirmed to be credited to their bank account.
7) Central Procurement Committee Meeting History Meeting No. 3 (2021-22). It has been decided to publish e-tenders on GeM Portal as per the terms and conditions of the fresh tenders to be floated through Purchase Cell, Commissionerate Health Services under 26.07.2021.
Accordingly, if the subject of" Contractual Driver Service" fits in this condition, then the service should be taken by publishing an e-tender on the GEM Portal.
8) Proposal for payment of pending dues/ extension of the ongoing driver service should be submitted to the government separately.
Expenditure on the said proposal may be made in the form of Demand No.R-1, 2210 Medical Services and Public Health, 110 Hospitals and Clinics, (01) Muffasal Hospitals and Clinics, (01) Non-Providing Government Hospitals in Muffasal Area (22100146) or Demand No.R-1, 2210 Medical Services and Public Health, (01) (08) Upgradation of Primary Health Centre to Rural Hospital (Mandatory) (22100413) or Demand No.R-1, 2210 Medical Services and Public Health, (01) (09) Upgradation of Rural/ Cottage Hospitals to District Hospital (Mandatory) (22104624) under Maharashtra Health Development Project.
