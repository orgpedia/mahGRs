The Proposal:
Khapar, T. A proposal to upgrade the Primary Health Centre at Nandurbar in Akkalkuwa district to a 30-bed rural hospital was under consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
Khapar, T. The upgradation of the Primary Health Centre at Nandurbar in Akkalkuwa District to a 30-bed rural hospital is being approved as a "special case" under this Government decision. Separate action will be taken for the acquisition of land for the hospital in the prescribed manner for construction and posting of the hospital in this category.
The decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra's www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
