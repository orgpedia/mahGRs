With ICMR approving various manufacturing companies, the availability has increased. Also, the cost of transportation has come down. Keeping all these factors in mind, it was necessary to reduce the rate of RTPCR testing: The committee formed as per the government decision mentioned in Section 1 had directed to revise the rates. The committee's report has been accepted by the government. Accordingly, the matter of fixing the revised rates of RTPCR tests was under the consideration of the government.
