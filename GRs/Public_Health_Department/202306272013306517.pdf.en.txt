Preamble: The Government of India has launched the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission in September, 2014 to provide AYUSH health services and education to the general public, enhance the quality of Ayurvedic, Homeopathic and Unani medicines and supply of quality medicinal plants in a sustainable manner.
All necessary funds should be allocated for this. As per the instructions of the Central Government, the grants received by the National AYUSH Mission are deposited with the State Government and the grants are distributed through the treasury. Central government. The Memorandum dated 23 rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for the Fund Distribution and Appropriation Management System of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. The Government was considering to provide Central share to State AYUSH Societies from the approved budgetary allocation of 2023-24 for Social Justice and Special Assistance Component (SCSP) for National AYUSH Mission.

Government's decision:
The Central Share Account for the financial year 2023-24 for disbursement of Central and State share for Social Justice and Special Assistance Components (SCSP) under National AYUSH Mission is Rs. 386.30 lakhs and State share under head 2210G583 is Rs. 257.53
The budget has been allocated for this. The Centre has allocated Rs. 275.427 lakh and Rs. 183.618 lakh is being sanctioned to State AYUSH Societies.
The Administrative Officer, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above grant. They should submit the payment to the treasury and hand over the amount to the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, State AYUSH Society for spending on the prescribed matters. The Deputy Director (a. And p. ), Health Services, Mumbai are being declared as" Controlling Authorities."
The expenditure on the proposal shall be covered under the head of accounts as mentioned below and shall be met from the available provision approved for the year 2023-24.
