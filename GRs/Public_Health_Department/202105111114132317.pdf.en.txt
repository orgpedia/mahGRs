Dr. Manikrao Sangle, District Surgeon, District Hospital, Dhule, in the cadre of District Surgeon, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700), is currently on leave on medical grounds. Accordingly, Dr. Rajesh Gemu Vasave, Medical Officer (Surgery), District Hospital, Nandurbar is being transferred and posted as District Surgeon, District Hospital, Dhule.
These orders are being issued in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
Dr. Rajesh Vasave should immediately appear at the place of transfer and submit a report to the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai and the Government.
4. Separate action will be taken regarding the posting of Dr. Manikrao Sangle.
No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
6. The transferred officers shall join the new post during the permissible period of posting, the officers who do not comply with this time limit and remain absent from the place of posting mutually, the absence of such officers shall be treated as" unauthorised absence" and in the alternative, there shall be interruption in their service.
7. The Commissionerate shall initiate administrative action against the officers who fail to report at the place of their posting and submit the proposal for the approval of the Government as per requirement. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
(8) Any officer who directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order shall be liable to disciplinary action.
9.The order will come into effect immediately.
