The Proposal:
Rural Hospital, Ghoti. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs 2.96 crore was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
2. The budget for the building repair work at Rural Hospital, Ghoti G. Nashik is based on the rate schedule for the year 2021-22. The budget has allocated 4 per cent for internal contingency expenditure, 5 per cent for centage charges and 0.5 per cent for labour insurance. As per the Recapitulation Sheet attached, the Administrative Sanction is being accorded under this Government Resolution for the projects with an outlay of Rs. 2.96 crore subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
