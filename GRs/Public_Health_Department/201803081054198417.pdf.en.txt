As per the agreement between the then government and the Indian Red Cross Society, Bombay in 1938, the rent of the land is payable by the government to the concerned organization. Accordingly, a grant of Rs.10, 116.50 per annum is sanctioned to the Indian Red Cross Society, Maharashtra State Branch, Mumbai for the purpose of rent of the premises. The proposal to provide grant-in-aid amounting to Rs.70, 815.50 crore to Indian Red Cross Society, Maharashtra State Branch, Mumbai for seven years from 2011-12 to 2017-18 was under consideration of the Government vide letter dated 09.10.2017 from the Director, Health Services, Mumbai.
Government Decision Under this Government Decision, a grant-in-aid of Rs.70,816/- (Rupees Seventy Thousand Eight Hundred Sixteen only) is being sanctioned to the Indian Red Cross Society, Maharashtra State Branch, Mumbai for the rent of the premises for a period of seven years from 2011-12 to 2017-18.
