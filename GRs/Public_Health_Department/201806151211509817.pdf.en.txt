Preamble- Director (Medical), State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai vide Reference No. As per letter No. 2, a proposal was submitted to the Government for administrative approval for the construction of Rs. 28,71,346/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Lakhs Seventy One Thousand Three Hundred and Forty Six only) to start an Intensive Care Unit at the State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Worli. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the renovation of the structure for the opening of the Intensive Care Unit at the State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Worli, was under consideration of the Government.
The Government decision is based on the table of the 2015-16 Budget Estimates for the total amount of Rs. 28,71,346/- for the architectural repairs to be carried out for the opening of the Intensive Care Unit at the State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Worli, Mumbai. After considering the proposal (considering GST, Contingency Rate and other factors), the total budget outlay of Rs. 34,99,781/- considered is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
The expenditure shall be met from the funds available with the Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai.
