December 31, 2018 3) Department of Public Health, Government Decision No: 2016/ Q. No. 111/ Health-3, D. March 6, 2019 Government Order: Director, Health Services is an additional post. The post was created on January 9, 2017. The decision was taken on March 6, 2019. Accordingly, the headquarters of the Director, Health Services (1) has been fixed at Mumbai and the headquarters of the Director, Health Services (2) at Pune. The additional charge of the post of Director, Health Services (2), Pune is being temporarily transferred to Dr. (Mrs.) Archana Vasant Patil, Additional Director (Grade-Awakening), Health Services, Family Welfare, Parental Care and School Health, Pune till further orders.
Dr. Archana Vasant Patil, Additional Director (Grade-A), Health Services, Family Welfare, Parental Care and School Health, Pune, shall hold the additional charge of the post of Director, Health Services (2), Pune, in addition to the duties and responsibilities of her original post.
Government order The additional charge of the post of Director, Health Services (1) which was assigned to the Commissioner, Health Services and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai vide order dated 31. 12.2018 shall continue to be held by him till further orders.
