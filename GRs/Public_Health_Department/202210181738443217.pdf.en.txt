the Government Order
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Government Duties Act, 2005, the post of District Surgeon, District Hospital, Osmanabad is being transferred to Dr. Rajabhau Galande, Medical Officer (Beggars), District Hospital, Osmanabad on administrative grounds. As Dhananjay Patil will be without any posting and the order of his posting will be issued as per the order, Dr. Dhananjay Patil will appear in the office of the Deputy Director, Health Services, Latur Circle, Latur and his headquarters will remain the same till further orders.
Dr. Galande should be relieved of his duties immediately to appear at the place of his new posting and shall immediately appear at the place of posting assigned to him by the officer concerned.
The report should be submitted to the Deputy Secretary (Services-2), SA Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai and the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai.
No leave shall be granted to Dr. Galande, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
(4) After relieving Dr. Galande, he shall join the new post during the period of admissible service. Dr. Galande is being made aware that if he fails to report to the place of posting within the stipulated time or remains absent, his absence will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and his service will be interrupted.
If Dr. Galande does not appear at the place of posting within the prescribed time, the Commissionerate should initiate administrative action against him in time and submit the proposal for the approval of the government as per the need. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
(6) Any attempt by the officer concerned to bring direct or indirect pressure to modify the transfer order shall be liable to disciplinary action.
