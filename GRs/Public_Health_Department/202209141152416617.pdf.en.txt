Introduction: HMIS Eshushrut is one of the various programmes being implemented under the National Health Mission programme. The programme is proposed to be implemented in all health institutions in six selected districts as per the instructions given under FMR 17.6 in the Action Plan approved by the Central Government. The programme aims to create a paperless hospital and a computer system for day-to-day functioning of the hospital. The hardware and other computer components required for this need to be available in the hospital. The procurement is proposed to be done through e-tendering process from the subsidy available through the procurement cell of the Commissionerate.
