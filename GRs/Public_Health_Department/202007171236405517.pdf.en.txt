The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre for a probation period of two years.
These appointments are the first in the Government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by the Independent Selection Board constituted under the Department of Public Health in 2014.
2. The revised guidelines regarding the termination of probationary period of officers/ employees in government service have been passed under the Government Decision of the General Administration Department. The matter of termination of probationary period of Medical Officers, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A was under consideration of the Government as the officers posted had completed their probationary period of two years satisfactorily.
