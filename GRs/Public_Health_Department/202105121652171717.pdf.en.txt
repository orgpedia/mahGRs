Government Order:
The Director, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai has issued a notification regarding the revised postings of Medical Officers who have passed Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma Course under the Commissionerate of Health Services in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale S-20: 56100-177500). The proposal has been submitted vide letter dated 29.12.2020. In accordance with the said proposal, considering the educational qualification of the concerned medical officers, vacancies in health institutions and administrative exigencies, the following revised postings are being given to the medical officers who have passed the post graduate degree course in Form-A, to the medical officers who have passed the post graduate diploma course in Form-B and to the medical officers who have passed the post graduate degree (Ayurveda) in Form-C.

3. The Medical Officers who have been given postings after passing the Post Graduate Diploma Course under this Order are required to serve the Government (i.e. in the health institutions under the Public Health Department only) for the prescribed period. In case of non-service of prescribed period by the Medical Officers mentioned in the said Form-A, Form-B and Form-C, Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) shall be liable to pay fine.
Under this order, the medical officers who have failed in the post-graduate degree/ diploma courses proposed by the Commissionerate at Vacha have not been given the revised postings. Therefore, the medical officers who have failed the examination and have not taken the examination should be given temporary posting within 7 days considering the requirement of medical officers for COVID-19 from the Commissionerate level. Also, the Commissionerate shall submit a proposal to the Government for the revised posting of the said Medical Officer after he/ she passes the Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma course without any further delay.
