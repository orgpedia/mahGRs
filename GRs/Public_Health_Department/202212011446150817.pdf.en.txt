the Government Order
Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005
As per the provision, with the approval of the competent authority, Dr. Abhijit Phadnis, Medical Superintendent, Sub District Hospital, Baramati is being transferred and posted to the vacant post of" Assistant Director, Health Services (Medical), Pune."
2. Dr. Phadnis shall be relieved forthwith to report at the place of his new posting and the officer concerned shall report to the Government through the Deputy Director, Health Services and the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai.
3. No leave shall be granted to Dr. Phadnis, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
(4) After the retirement of Dr. Phadnis, he should join the new post during the permissible period of his appointment. Dr. Phadnis is being made aware that if he does not appear at the place of posting within the stipulated time or remains absent, his absence will be treated as" unauthorised absence" and his service will be interrupted in the alternative.
If Dr. Phadnis does not appear at the place of posting within the prescribed time, the Commissionerate should initiate administrative action against him in time and make a proposal as necessary.
It has to be approved by the government. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
(6) Any attempt by the officer concerned to bring direct or indirect pressure to modify the transfer order shall be liable to disciplinary action.
7.The order will come into effect immediately.
