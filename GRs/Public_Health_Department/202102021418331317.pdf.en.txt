Introduction: The General Hospital in Bhandara. On 09.01.2021, a massive fire broke out in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), resulting in the death of 10 infants. An expert committee has been set up to probe the incident. However, in view of the public outcry from the local media and public representatives in this case, it is expedient to give the post of Additional District Surgeon in the hospital to another non-executive post. Accordingly, a proposal was submitted by the Commissionerate of Health Services regarding the posting of Dr. (Mrs.) Sunita Badhe, Additional District Surgeon, General Hospital Bhandara to a non-executive post. In pursuance of the said proposal, the matter of posting of Dr. (Mrs.) Sunita Badhe, Additional District Surgeon, General Hospital, Bhandara to a non-executive post was under consideration of the Government.
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority, the officers of the District Surgeon Cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Scale S-23: 67700-208700 in the Pay Matrix) are being transferred on administrative grounds as follows. :
