Preamble: 19 temporary posts in the Badshah Khan Eye Hospital, Mandvi, Dist. Nanded, working under the National Blindness Control Programme, will be filled up as per the revised figure in the Public Health Department, Government Decision No. Post No.117/ Service-1 dated 6th August, 2007 has been approved. Of them, 16 temporary posts were filled by the Department of Public Health, Government Decision No. Annika/ Apde 2012/ PR No.191/ Arogya-5, dated 1st June, 2012 has been made permanent. The remaining 3 temporary posts will be filled up from 01.03.2018 as per the decision of the Government in respect of Public Health Department. It has been approved to continue till 30.09.2018. The deadline The matter of extension of the said posts was under consideration of the Government as they were expiring on 30.09.2018. This period is being extended subject to the condition that the revised figure is approved.
Government Decision: Under the National Blindness Control Program, the headmistress of Badshah Khan Eye Hospital, Mandvi, G.Nanded, 3 temporary posts of Class-3 (1 post), Room Servitor Class-4 (1 post), Safaigar Class-4 (1 post) are required to provide health related facilities to the people of the state. 01/10/2018 to Dy. This Government Resolution is being approved to continue till 28/02/2019.
