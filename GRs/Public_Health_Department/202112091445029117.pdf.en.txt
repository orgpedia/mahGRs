The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2021-22 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. 80 crore has been earmarked in the budget. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company for the period from October, 2021 to December, 2021.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the provision made in the budget under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2021-22 for payment of insurance premium to the beneficiary family under the scheme is Rs. 25,03,21, 217/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Three Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred and Seventeen only) to be transferred to the account of State Health Guarantee Society immediately by the Controller.
