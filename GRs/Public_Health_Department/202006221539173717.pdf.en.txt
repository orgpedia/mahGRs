Government's decision:
Under the National Malaria Eradication Programme, the Department of Public Health has issued Government Resolution No. A total of 4164 posts comprising 131 posts at Sub-Centre level sanctioned under PO No.117/ Service-1, 2007/ PR No.117/ Service-1, dated 06.08.2007 were filled up from 01.10.2020 to 29.02.2020 under the aforesaid Act. No. 2 was continued as per the decision of the government. The deadline expired on 29.02.2020. Since these posts are essential for the continuous delivery of health services to the people of the State, the Government has decided to continue these posts from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020.
The cost of these posts should be spent under the head of accounts shown below and should be met from the sanctioned grant for the year 2020-2021.
