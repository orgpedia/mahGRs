Government's decision:
The third wave of corona infection is not going to end in the state soon. Therefore, in order to take measures to control it and to implement it, it has been decided to increase the retirement age of Medical Officers in Group-A (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500 in the Pay Matrix) and all officers in Senior Level (Pay Level S-23: 67700-208700 and above) in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A (Pay Level S-20: 56100 177500 in the Pay Matrix) under the Commissionerate of Health Services under the Public Health Department and all working officers in Senior Level (Pay Level S-20: 56100-177500 and above) in the State Labour Insurance Scheme. The government will take a decision after the cabinet's approval.
31.05.2021. This government has taken that decision. The Cabinet has given its approval.
Further, the Finance Department should make necessary amendments in Rule 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 in order to increase the retirement age of Medical Officers and Senior Medical Officers of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A and Maharashtra Medical and Insurance Services, Group-A.
