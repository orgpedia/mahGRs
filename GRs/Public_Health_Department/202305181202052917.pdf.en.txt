Since he is being transferred as per the request of Dr. Charushila Patil, he should join the new post immediately after being relieved from his duties, if the concerned officer does not comply with this time limit and remains absent from the place of posting mutually, the absence of the concerned officer will be considered as" unauthorized absence" and alternatively, his service will be interrupted.
