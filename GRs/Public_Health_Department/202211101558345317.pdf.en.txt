Officers in the cadre of Joint Director, Health Services/ Deputy Director, Health Services, if available by promotion or directly, the concerned officers will be transferred to their parent cadre.
4. The transferred officer shall be relieved forthwith to report at his new place of posting and shall report at the place of posting assigned to him by the officer concerned.
The report should be submitted to the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai and the Government.
No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
(6) The transferred officers, after relieving them, shall join the new post during the period of permissible posting, the officers who do not comply with this time limit and do not report at the place of posting.
All concerned are being made aware that mutual absence of such officers will be construed as" unauthorised absence" and in turn, there will be interruption in their service.
7. Commissionerate to take action against officers who fail to appear at their place of posting.
The administrative action should be initiated immediately and the proposal should be submitted for the approval of the government as per the need. or
The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
(8) Any officer who directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order shall be liable to disciplinary action.
9.The order will come into effect immediately.
