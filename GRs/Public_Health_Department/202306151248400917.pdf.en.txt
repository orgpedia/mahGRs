the prelude
Inter-Departmental Coordination Committee has been reconstituted vide Government Resolution dated 02/09/2000 regarding Registration of Births and Deaths and vital statistics in the State. The government was considering including the Chief Registrar, State of Births and Deaths, Maharashtra, as a member of the committee as well as the members of the concerned departments in the committee to run Aadhaar-linked birth registration activities in selected government health institutions in the state in a proper manner.
Government Decision- The following members are being newly included in the Inter-Departmental Coordination Committee on Birth and Death Registration and Essential Statistics.
Commissioner, Health Services and Mission Director, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai.
Director, Department of Information Technology, Ministry of Information Technology, Mumbai.
Director, Health Services, and Chief Registrar, Births and Deaths, Directorate of Health Services, Pune.
Joint/ Deputy Secretary, Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Mumbai.
Joint Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
