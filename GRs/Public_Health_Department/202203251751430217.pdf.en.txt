The Proposal:
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. 2210 G 289 (TSP) for the financial year 2021-22 for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. A sum of Rs. 109.71 crore has been allocated for the project. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds under the head 2210 G 289 (TSP) to the State Health Guarantee Society for payment of insurance premium to the insurance company and claims on the basis of the guarantee for the period April, 2022 to June, 2022.
Government Decision: As the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Health Society, the premium for payment of insurance premium for the period April 2022 to June 2022 of the beneficiary family under the scheme has been increased from Rs. 24,76,00, 334/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Sixty Seven Lakhs Three Hundred and Thirty Four only) and Rs. 2,17,10, 746/- (Rupees Two Crores Seventeen Lakhs Ten Thousand Seven Hundred and Forty Six only) aggregating to Rs. 26,93,11, 080/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Ninety Three Lakhs Eleven Thousand Eighty Five only) to the State
