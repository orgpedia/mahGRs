GOVERNMENT DECISIONS As per the decision of the Government vide No. 1 above, it has been approved to upgrade the Dental Department functioning under the Public Health Department in the State. Accordingly, a total of 772 posts of Dental Surgeon, Group-A, Class-2 (1 post) and Dental Assistant, Group-D (1 post) have been created for 386 rural hospitals of the department with 30 beds. In Rural Hospital, Arni G. Yavatmal, 2 posts have been sanctioned and out of these 2 posts, 1 post of Dental Surgeon has been filled.
2. Now, as per the decision of the Government, 1 temporary post of Dental Surgeon at Rural Hospital, Arni G. Yavatmal under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Akola Circle, Akola has been created. with effect from 05.12.2017. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2018.
