The Proposal:
In the approved action plan for the financial year 2019-20 in the National Health Mission, the estimated amount for the purchase of equipment required for the health institutions in the state from the grant received under the National Blindness Control Programme is Rs. The matter of obtaining administrative approval for the proposal of expenditure amounting to Rs. 600.00 lakhs was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Project Implementation Plan for 2019-20 has been approved by the Central Government under the National Health Mission. The approved project implementation plan under the National Programme for Control of Blindness and Visual Impairment (NPDCP) under the FMR Code 6.1.1.19.a Grant in Aid for Strengthening of Districts (GAD) has been prepared with an outlay of Rs. A sum of Rs. 617.00 lakh has been received. Out of this grant, an estimated amount of Rs. 600.00 lakhs has been submitted to the Government by the Joint Director, Health Services (Non-Communicable Diseases), Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai for administrative approval. The proposal has been approved by the Commissioner (Health Services) and Mission Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai. Accordingly, the NPCB has decided to procure the required machinery for implementation of the programme through tender process.
