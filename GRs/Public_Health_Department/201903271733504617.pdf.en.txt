Government's decision
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for GNM Nursing School at Kunwar Tilak Singh General Hospital, Gondia, Dr. 50% of the Central Government share sanctioned vide letter dated 30.12.2014 amounting to Rs. 3,37,87, 500/- (Rupees Three Crores, Thirty Seven Lakhs, Eighty Seven Thousand, Five Hundred only) is being approved.
(2) The funds shall be spent after the Central Government notifies that they have been revived.
3. The expenditure on the said proposal should be met from the provision approved for the year 2018-19 under the head" Main Account Head, Demand No.R-1, 2210-Medical Services and Public Health, 01-Civil Health Service-Odysmal Treatment, 110 Hospitals and Clinics, 06 Schemes under Five Year Plans, 06 (32)- Nursing Training Institutes in Mofussil Area Centrally Sponsored Schemes (Central Share 50%), 53-Big Constructions (2210 G 191)."
The expenditure of this grant shall be strictly in accordance with the rules and guidelines specified in the Budget Rule Book and the Financial Powers Rule Book, as prescribed under Government Decision, Finance Division No., Assank-1001/ Q. No. 29/ Financial Reforms, dated 10th September, 2001. Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned grant. The said fund should be transferred to the Deputy Director (Nursing), Health Services, Mumbai as the Controlling Officer for payment to GNM Nursing School, Kunwar Tilak Singh General Hospital, Gondia.
5. The said Government Decision, Informal Reference No. 350/-, Rs. 04.07.2018 and Non-official Reference No. 145/2019/ Expenditure-13, Dy. is being issued with the consent received as on 29.03.2019.
