In pursuance of the said order, the following instructions shall be strictly complied with: 1) Medical officers transferred from the Government level shall be relieved forthwith to join the institution transferred from the health institution in which they are working. In the present situation, as the COVID-19 virus is everywhere, the medical officers who have been transferred from the level of the concerned Regional Deputy Director, the competent authority to relieve the concerned District Surgeon, Medical Superintendent and District Health Officer to join the concerned health institution should be relieved immediately.
Supervisory officers should strictly follow the provisions mentioned in the said Government Order. In case of non-compliance, disciplinary action will be proposed against the concerned Deputy Director/ District Surgeon/ Medical Superintendent/ District Health Officer.
(2) The concerned Medical Officers who have been promoted to the post should take care to join the post of posting immediately as per the order. Otherwise, disciplinary action will be taken against them. Also, the period from the date of this order to the date of joining shall be deemed to be the period of his service as an unauthorised absence.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officers of the concerned institution shall not, under any circumstances, pay the salary of the Medical Officers of the month of July, 2020 and subsequent months of the revised postings elsewhere.
