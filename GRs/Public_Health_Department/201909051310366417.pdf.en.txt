The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed as Medical Officers Group-A in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre for a period of two years. These appointments are the first in the Government service to the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" as recommended by an independent Board in 2014.
2. General Administration Department Read No. 1 As per the Government decision mentioned herein, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probation period of officers/ employees in Government service and since the officers posted to the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have satisfactorily completed their two years probation period, the matter of termination of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Medical Officers Group-A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as indicated in the Table referred to in this paragraph, have satisfactorily fulfilled the requirements for the expiry of their period of probation, and their period of probation shall be as indicated in the Table before their names in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
