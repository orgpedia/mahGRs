The proposal-
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana with Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented in the State with Central and State Governments contributing 60% and State Governments contributing 40%. During the financial year 2021-22, under the head 2210 G 494 (State Share 40%), Rs. 15.00 crore through Budgetary and Supplementary Demands amounting to Rs. 9.06 crore. A sum of Rs. 24.06 crore has been sanctioned.
For the period from 01.10.2021 to 31.12.2021, the State share for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is Rs. 6,04,85, 651/- (Rupees Six Crore Four Lakh Eighty Five Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty One only) was under consideration of the Government for transfer to Escrow Account.
