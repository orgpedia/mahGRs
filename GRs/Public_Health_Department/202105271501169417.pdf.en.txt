Introduction: The sub-centre of Shrigonda, G. Ahmednagar, has gone to the Municipal Council area. The matter of shifting Shrigonda G. to Ahmednagar was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government's decision:
2. Mauje Ghaypatwadi Ta. The sub-centre of Shrigonda, G. Ahmednagar, has gone to the Municipal Council area. The transfer of Shrigonda G. to Ahmednagar is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the sub-center can be operational in the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building and staff accommodation where the sub-station can function at the place of relocation will be required. So that in this place there should be separate arrangement for the examination of female patients, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for the sitting of staff.
