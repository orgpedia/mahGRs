Government's decision:
The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed with United India Insurance Company (UNI) for Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana will be effective from 01.01.2024. The deadline has been extended to January 31, 2024. The terms and conditions of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) dated 27.03.2020 signed between United India Insurance Company and State Health Guarantee Society, Worli, Mumbai and the provisions mentioned under the Government Decision of Reference No. 3 above shall remain in force.
