The Proposal:
Mauje Ardhapur, Ta. Primary health centre, Nanded, Maharashtra. The matter of shifting the district of Ardapur to Nanded was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Mauje Ardhapur, Ta. Primary health centre, Nanded, Maharashtra. The transfer of Ardhapur, District Nanded is being approved under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The new premises shall have a building for the functioning of the Primary Health Centre and accommodation for staff. Wherever there is an outpatient service, separate arrangements for female patient check-up, arrangements for delivery, staff seating, parking if there is an ambulance will be mandatory.
(2) The building shall be equipped with water and electricity.
3) The new location should have all the facilities required for the said primary health centre.
The District Planning and Development Board will be required to make provision for the construction of the main building and residence of the primary health centre at the new place from their district plan.
