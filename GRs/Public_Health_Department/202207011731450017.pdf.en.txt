Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The central share (60%) of the funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of the scheme is directly credited to the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on state account.
The scheme will be implemented in the financial year 2021-22. 1, the State has received an amount of Rs. 2,81,53, 548/- (Rupees Two Crore Eighty One Lakh Three Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Eight only) and the same has been deposited with the ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the State share of Rs. 1,87,69, 032/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Seven Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand Thirty Two only) was under consideration at the Government level. 40% of the State share of Rs. 1,87,69, 032/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Seven Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand Thirty Two only) has been released by the Tribal Development Department to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department, Ministry, Mumbai as Head of the Administrative Department. As per the Government Decision dated 20.06.2022, Government approval has been accorded for disbursement of budgetary funds on the distribution system. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
