As per the decision taken in the meeting on 27.09.2016, the" Bharat Ratna Pandit Bhimsen Joshi Hospital (Temba Hospital)" of Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has been handed over to the Public Health Department of the State Government. It has been transferred vide Government Resolution dated 26.02.2018.

Further, the officers/ employees who are to be included in the said Government decision shall fulfil the educational qualification, experience etc. prescribed under the rules of induction to that post. Dr Granthali Atmaram Pawar has been appointed as the eye surgeon at Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation. The Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai, being appointed on 18.10.2016 and possessing the educational qualification and experience prescribed in the Service Admission Rules for the post of Specialist Cadre Ophthalmic Surgeon in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A, has been appointed by the Director, Health Services (1), Mumbai, in the capacity of Deputy Director. Dr. Granthali Atmaram Pawar of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation has been appointed as the Specialist Cadre Ophthalmic Surgeon (Pay Scale S-23: 67700-208700) in Group-A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services under Public Health Department.
Appointment will be made on the following terms and conditions:
(1) Dr. He shall be deemed to have joined the Government service with retrospective effect from 24.05.2018.
(2) Dr. Granthali Pawar to the post of Specialist Cadre Ophthalmic Surgeon under this Government Order.

(3) According to the rules regarding the Marathi and Hindi language examination, if Dr. Pawar has not already passed this examination or has not been exempted from passing it, then he will be required to pass the Marathi/ Hindi language examination of this board within 1 year.
(4) Dr. Pawar will be required to submit the computer qualification certificate within 01 year.
(5) Dr. Granthali Pawar has been appointed as Specialist Cadre Ophthalmologist in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A.
The Pay Scale for the post of Surgeon will be S23-: 67700-208700.
Their salary will be fixed as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981.
(6) From the date of induction of Dr. Granthali Pawar into Government service, the defined contribution
Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme" shall continue to be applicable.
(7) The benefit of service in the erstwhile Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation to Dr. Granthali Pawar shall not be admissible for the purpose of salary protection and Assured Progress Scheme.
(8) Group insurance scheme, business tax, income tax and other deductions should be made from the salary of Dr. Granthali Pawar as per the prevailing rules of the government.
