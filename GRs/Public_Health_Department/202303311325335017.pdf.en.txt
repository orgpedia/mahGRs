Preamble As per the Department of Public Health, dated 20th March, 2015, it has been decided to reimburse the medical expenses of the patients who have been treated for severe life-threatening conditions in the private hospitals affected by swine flu.
As per the proposal submitted by the Director, Directorate of Health Services, Pune to the Government, the Supplementary Demands for Funds amounting to Rs. 3,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Thirty Lakhs only) towards reimbursement of pending medical expenses since 2018-19 for the patients who have been treated in private hospitals for Swine Flu in a critical condition with vital system were presented in the Monsoon Session 2022 of the Legislature. Accordingly, a supplementary demand of Rs. 3,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Thirty Lakhs only) was approved. The Finance Department had made available funds on BDS system within the limit of 50% of the amount of the supplementary demand i.e. Rs. 1,65,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Five Lakh only). He gave it. The funds have been released as per the Government decision dated 31.10.2022.
Now that the Finance Department has approved the release of the remaining amount of Rs. 1,65,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Five Lakh only), the matter of disbursement was under consideration of the Government.

Government Decision 1. As per the Government Decision dated 20th March, 2015, the Government has approved reimbursement of medical expenses to the patients who have undergone treatment for Swine Flu in private hospitals in the state on critical life support system or similar life support. Under this Government decision, the remaining amount of Rs. 1,65,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Five Lakh only) is being provided to reimburse the medical expenses pending since 2018-19 to the patients suffering from Swine Flu, who have been treated in private hospitals with critical life support system.
