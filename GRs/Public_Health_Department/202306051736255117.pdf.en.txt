Terms and Conditions: 1) Technical approval of the Chief Engineer, (Vidyut) Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) The Public Works Department is required to approve the external tariff as per the Budget Circular dated 29.10.2018.
3) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
4) The work should be tendered together without separate tenders.
5) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(6) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works will be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in the respective areas.
8) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
9) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
The above expenditure should be met from the grants sanctioned under the heads of Accounts Heading 4210- Capital expenditure on medical services and public health services, 01- Civil Health Services (Medical Support)- 110- Hospitals and Clinics, (01) Schemes under A Five Year Plan- Buildings (00) (01) Large Constructions (4210 0014), 53- Large Constructions and 4210- Capital expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 02- Rural Health Services (Medical Support)- Hospitals and Clinics, (04) Public Health, 104- Community Health Centres, (01) Large Constructions (4210 0391), 53 Large Constructions.
These orders are being issued under the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Government Decision, Finance Division, No. 2013/ Q. No. 30/2013 Regulations, Part-II, Annexure with dated 17.04.2015, Part-I, Sub-Division-V, Sequel Number-1, Paragraph No. 134.
