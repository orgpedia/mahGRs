Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana, a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, has been implemented in the State during 2017-18. The central share (60%) of the funds received from the Central Government for the implementation of the scheme is directly credited to the ESCROW ACCOUNT opened in the name of the Commissioner, Director of Health Services and Mission, National Health Mission, Mumbai and 40% of the funds received are made available to the scheme on state account.
In the financial year 2021-22, cadre-wise funds (for general, SC and ST categories) have been received from the Central Government for implementation of the scheme. In this, for the Scheduled Tribe (ST) component, Reference No. 1 to Rs. 3,02,00, 103/- (Rupees Three Crores Two Lakhs One Hundred and Three only) has been received by the State and deposited with ESCROW ACCOUNT. 40% of the State share of Rs. 2,01,33, 402/- (Rupees Two Crores One Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Four Hundred Two only) was under consideration at the Government level. 40% of the State's share of Rs. 2,01,33, 402/- (Rupees Two Crores One Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Four Hundred Two only) has been released by the Tribal Development Department to the Additional Chief Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the Head of the Administrative Department. As per the Government decision dated 27.09.2021, Government approval has been accorded for disbursement of budget funds on the disbursement system. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
