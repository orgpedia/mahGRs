Introduction In the broad framework of the establishment of health institutions in the state, under the decision of the government at No. 1, Mauje Koregaon, Tt. Approval has been given to set up a sub-station at Koregaon, G. Satara, which is running in a rented building. As Koregaon town is a Nagar Panchayat, the matter of shifting the sub-centre to another place was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision Approval is being given to shift the sanctioned posts of Sub-Centre, Officers and staff of Koregaon, District Satara along with Mauje of the same Taluka to Shirdhon subject to the following conditions.
1) The temporary arrangement of the building in which the sub-center can function and the arrangement of the staff accommodation will be necessary so that the space will be mandatory for outpatient services, separate arrangements for gynecological examination, delivery, staff seating, parking if there is an ambulance.
