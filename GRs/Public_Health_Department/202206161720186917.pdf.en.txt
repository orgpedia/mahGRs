Date: July 7, 2022.
Government circular
The post of Administrative Officer in the cadre of General State Service, Group-B under the Commissionerate of Health Services. 01.01.2016 to D. Provisional Seniority List for the period 01.01.2022 has been prepared and attached with the return as under- 1 to 7.
The Commissioner, Health Services Commissionerate, Mumbai shall take action to bring the said Provisional Seniority List to the notice of all concerned and invite objections, if any, regarding the Provisional Seniority List within a period of fifteen days from the date of issue of this Government Circular. The objections received should be forwarded by the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai to the Government along with their comments.
If no objections are received within the above mentioned period, it will be deemed that no one has any objection to this provisional seniority list and this provisional seniority list will be finalized.
