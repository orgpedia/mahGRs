For the beneficiaries of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, the insurance premium for the period from 01.07.2022 to 30.09.2022 is Rs. A proposal to disburse a sum of Rs. 220.25 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Five Crores Twenty Five Lakhs only) to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 251 (General), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented by the State Health Guarantee Society, the provision made in the budget for the financial year 2022-23 for payment of insurance premium of the beneficiary family under the scheme is Rs. A conditional amount of Rs. 220.25 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Five Crores only) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
