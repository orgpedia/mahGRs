GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the National Leprosy Control Programme, 1054 posts have been created as per the decision of the Government of Reference No. 1, out of which 384 posts have been kept temporary. Out of these 384 temporary posts, 177 temporary posts have been made permanent as 80% of the government decisions of Reference No. 2 and 3. The remaining 207 temporary posts are included in the establishment review approved in accordance with the Government Decision of Reference No. 1. Reference No. 4 Under the decision of the Government here, 16 temporary posts have been created in the office of the Assistant Director Health Services, Palghar. Under National Leprosy Control Programme, a total of 223 temporary posts have been filled up from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per reference No. The deadline was extended by a government decision. The 223 vacancies are for the posts of doctors at Dr Bandorwala Leprosy Hospital, Kondhwa, Pune.
Under the National Leprosy Control Programme, 223 temporary working posts have been created as they are continuously providing health related services to the people of the state. The continuation from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 is hereby approved by the Government. The period is being extended subject to the condition that the revised figure is approved.
