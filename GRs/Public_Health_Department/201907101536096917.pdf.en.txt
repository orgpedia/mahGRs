Preamble: The proposal of the Director, Directorate General of Health Services regarding upgradation of the 30-bed rural hospital at Bhokardan in Jalna district to a 50-bed sub-district hospital was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard will be taken later.
The government has decided to upgrade the 30-bed rural hospital in Bhokardan of Jalna district to a 50-bed sub-district hospital.
