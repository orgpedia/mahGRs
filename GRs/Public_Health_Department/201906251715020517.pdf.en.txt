Introduction- In the context of the large-scale uterus removal surgery among women undergoing lice removal in a private hospital in Beed district, Dr. Starred Question No.1 in the Legislative Council on 18.6.2019 46,500 was discussed. During the discussion, The Minister assured to constitute a committee of women legislators and experts at the state level for hysterectomy. In accordance with this assurance, the government has taken the following decision to form the committee.
The government has decided to form a committee at the state level to examine the hysterectomy in private hospitals in Beed district.
