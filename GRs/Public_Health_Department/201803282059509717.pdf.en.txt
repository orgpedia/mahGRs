Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 18.10.2017, administrative approval has been accorded for providing funds to the tune of Rs. 10.00 crore under Special Scheme for purchase of Linear Accelerator for Rashtrasant Tukadoji Cancer Hospital, Nagpur. The Government is planning to provide Rs. 10.00 crore under this special scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: For the purchase of Linear Accelerator Machine at Rashtrasant Tukadoji Regional Cancer Care Centre, Nagpur, Rs. Under this Government decision, a grant of Rs. 10.00 crore is being approved to be disbursed to the institution.
Terms and Conditions: If more than Rs.10 crore is required for the purchase of Linear Accelerator Machine, the excess amount will be borne by the concerned institution itself and if the amount spent is less than Rs.10 crore, the remaining amount will be refunded to the Government.
The funds will be disbursed to Joint Director, Health Services (Budget& Administration), Mumbai as Controlling Officer for payment to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Regional Cancer Hospital& Research Centre, Tukadoji Chowk, Manewada Road, Nagpur-440 027.
The Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for this grant. The payment of this amount should be submitted to the treasury and the amount should be handed over to the concerned programme officer for spending on the matters mentioned.
In this case, the terms and conditions of the Memorandum of Understanding between the Central Government/ State Government and Rashtrasant Tukadoji Regional Cancer Hospital and Research Center should be strictly followed.
