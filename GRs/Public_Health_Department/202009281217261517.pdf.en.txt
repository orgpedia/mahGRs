The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed as Medical Officers Group-A in the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre for a period of two years. The appointments have been made on the recommendation of the Independent Selection Board constituted under the Department of Public Health in the year 2012 for the post of" Medical Officer Group-A" in the Government Service.
2. Under the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the Medical Officers Group-A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as indicated in the Table referred to in this paragraph, have satisfactorily fulfilled the requirements for the expiry of their period of probation, and their period of probation shall be as indicated in the Table before their names in column No. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their service on regular basis (MC) is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
