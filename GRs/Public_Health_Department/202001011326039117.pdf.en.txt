Preamble: In order to remove 100% physical backlog of health institutions in the State, it is necessary to exclude the hospitals/ places where space is not available in the district of the backlog from the list of backlog and instead include the health institutions where space is available in the same district in the list of institutions under backlog. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to delete 28 sub-centres, 1 primary health centre and 4 rural hospitals from the list of backlog and instead include 28 sub-centres, 1 primary health centre and 4 rural hospitals/ trauma care units in the list of backlog. The government has decided to delete two hospitals- Thane and Rural Hospital, Saundar G. Gondia- from the list of backward classes.
Government's decision:
In order to remove 100% physical backlog of health institutions in the State, the hospitals/ places where space is not available in the backward districts have been excluded from the list of backward and instead the health institutions where space is available in the same district have been included in the list of institutions under the list of backward.
