INTRODUCTION: As per the decision of the Government dated 30th April, 2020, a new scheme for providing free spectacles to school children for prevention of visual impairment has been approved.
A request was made to the Accountant General's office to obtain a new head of accounts to provide funds for the scheme. The Office of the Accountant General vide its letter dated 29.7.2020 has approved the new head of accounts. Therefore, the government was considering to introduce a new scheme.
Government's decision:
The scheme for providing free spectacles to the school children for the prevention of visual impairment has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 30th April, 2020 and the Accountant General's Office is hereby approving the following heads of accounts as agreed under the informal reference dated 21.07.2020.
