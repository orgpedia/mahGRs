Preamble: As per the reference letter of the Directorate of Health Services, the administrative approval for the budget for the additional electrical work of the 100-bed sub-district hospital and 20-bed trauma care center in Panvel district Raigad was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 2. The budget of 100 bed sub-district hospital and 20 bed Trauma Care Centre at Panvel District Raigad, additional electrical work is based on the 2017-18 tariff list and the budget and plans of Rs. 2,99,58, 749/- (Rupees Two Crore Nine Lakh Eighty Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Nineteen only) with 12% GST, 4% Contingency Cost and 8% for centage charges etc. are being given administrative approval under this Government Decision subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The work shall require the technical approval of the competent authorities of the Public Works Department before inviting tenders for the work.
2) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(3) As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department concerned. While giving such technical approval, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Deputy Director of Health Services Board in the Department of Health.
4) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T.C. 1D.
Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
5) Specification, number, estimated cost, other aspects of the equipment are not mentioned in the above budget. However, the details of the tender should be submitted to the government for approval before publishing it.
6) These works should be done through e-tendering.
