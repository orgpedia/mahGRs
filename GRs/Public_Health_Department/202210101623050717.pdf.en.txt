Preamble As per the Department of Public Health, dated 20th March, 2015, it has been decided to reimburse the medical expenses of the patients who have been treated for severe life-threatening conditions in the private hospitals affected by swine flu.
As per the proposal submitted by the Director, Directorate of Health Services, Pune to the Government, the Supplementary Demands for Funds amounting to Rs. 3,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Thirty Lakhs only) towards reimbursement of medical expenses pending since 2018-19 for the patients who have been treated in private hospitals for severe life-threatening conditions due to Swine Flu were presented in the Monsoon Session 2022 of the Legislature. Accordingly, a supplementary demand for an amount of Rs. 3,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Thirty Lakhs only) has been approved. At present, within the limit of 50% of the sanctioned supplementary demand, the Finance Department has made available funds to the tune of Rs. 1,65,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Five Lakh only) to the Department on BDS system. The matter of disbursement of this fund and closure of this reimbursement scheme was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision (1) The Government has decided to reimburse the medical expenses of the patients who have been treated for Swine Flu in private hospitals in the state under the Government Decision dated March 20, 2015.
