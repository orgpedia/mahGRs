Preamble During the financial year 2019-20, the budget outlay under the head of Accounts- 22100291 is Rs. 4,62,00, 000/- and the Finance Department has disbursed 60% of the BDS funds amounting to Rs. 1,75,19, 000/-. The Directorate of Health Services has requested the Regional Psychiatric Hospitals to get administrative approval for the purchase of funds amounting to Rs. 2,50,07, 822/-for the purchase of psychiatric drugs. Therefore, after approving the amount proposed for administrative approval by the Additional Director, Directorate General of Health Services, Mumbai, the Finance Department is considering granting administrative approval to tender the budgetary grant of Rs. 2,50,07, 822/- subject to the condition that in future the expenditure on the BDS system distributed by the end of the financial year 2019 should be within the limit of the subsidy available.

As requested by the Additional Director, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai, vide Government Resolution dated 7.8.2019, vide letter dated 10.5.2019, the sanctioned grant for Regional Psychiatric Hospitals under the head Psychiatric Drugs Account No.22100291 amounting to Rs. 4,62,00, 000/- and for purchase of Psychiatric Drugs, the tender process for procurement of drugs amounting to Rs. 2,50,07, 822/- is being approved. However, administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions and subject to the condition that the expenditure should be within the limit of subsidy available on the BDS system till the end of the financial year 2019.
The following conditions: 1) The need/ demand for the said psychiatric drugs should be ascertained.
After that, the purchase process will begin.
2) It should be ensured that the specifications of these psychiatric drugs are as per requirement and up to date. The technical specifications of the items to be procured should be as per the specifications agreed by the concerned experts (Technical Committee/ Officials). It should also be ensured that the technical specification is not tailored to benefit any particular company. After that, you need to buy them.
3) Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, No. 2014/ No. 82/ Part-. . The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the Revised Manual of Procedures for Office Procurement to be carried out by Government Departments issued vide Notification No. 4 dated 01.12.2016.
4) While giving administrative approval, the proposed procurement rates are estimated and it shall be the responsibility of the Head of the Programme to record the minimum price received by the Hofkeen Procurement Cell from the tender for the same drug as the estimated price, so as to ensure that the price fixed during the procurement process is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the concerned supplier has supplied the same elsewhere.
5) Pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection of the items to be purchased should be done to ensure that only the items with proper and correct specifications are purchased.
6) It should be ensured that after the supply of the items to be purchased, these items will not be left unused and they will be used within the stipulated time.
7) The expenditure should be met from the grant sanctioned in 2019-20 under the head No. 2210 Medical Services and Public Health 01- Civil Health Services- Dyspharmacy 110- Hospitals and Clinics 110-6- Psychiatric Hospitals under Five Year Plan (06) (01)- Supplies and Supplies (22100291).
