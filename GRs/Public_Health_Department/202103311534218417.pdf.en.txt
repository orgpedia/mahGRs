GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. CORONA-2020/ PR. NO. 123/ HEALTH 5 dated 02.06.2020.
The Proposal:
The Government has constituted a Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Executive Officer, State Health Guarantee Society, Mumbai under the Government Resolution dated 02.06.2020 to negotiate with private laboratories and fix the rates of testing so that the laboratories in the State can be used to the full capacity and maximum tests can be done.
The number of Covid cases in the state has been increasing since February 2021. Earlier, there were about 2,000 Covid patients per day. The number is now growing by about 40,000 a year. Earlier, the state was conducting 1 lakh Covid tests per day. At present, 1.75 lakh tests are being conducted per day. Currently, the state's positivity rate is around 26 per cent. Therefore, it is necessary to increase the number of tests on a large scale in 3 states. The government has also received a recommendation from the ICMR in this regard. In view of the increasing number of cases and cases, the Government is taking the following decisions to increase the scope of the committee.
Government's decision:
The committee will survey and evaluate the Covid tests being conducted in private and government laboratories in the state and take measures to increase testing where necessary.
