Date is August 24, 2018
Read- 1) Department of Public Health Government Decision No. Rakayo-2017/ No. 620/ Rakyawi-2, d. 23.02.2018.
2) Letter from the Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai No. Commissioner/ RAKAVIO/ KA No. 09/9860/2018, dated 11.06.2018.
3) The Deputy Medical Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi vide letter No. U 16/25/6/ ESIH Kandivali/ 2008/ Med-II, Dated. 20.06.2018.
Preamble: The decision to transfer the State Workers Insurance Hospital, Kandivali to the State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi was taken under Reference 1. However, the State Employees Insurance Corporation, New Delhi has agreed to shift the hospital to the newly constructed building and run it by the State Government. Accordingly, Reference No. The matter of revoking the government decision issued under section 1 was under consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: The government decision issued under Section 1 is being revoked.
Further, approval is being given to shift the State Workers Insurance Scheme Hospital, Kandivali to the newly constructed building at Akurli Road, Kandivali by the State Workers Insurance Corporation, New Delhi.
The Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai should ensure that the hospital becomes operational immediately. Necessary action should also be taken to ensure availability of beds, medicines and equipment in the hospital.
