3. The said promotions are being given as per the provisions of the Service Admission Rules for the post of' District Surgeon'.
4. Ad hoc promotion. A Special Leave Petition No.
Vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota subject to 28306/2017 are being filled up only on temporary basis.
5. The ad-hoc promotion is subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The ad-hoc promotion to the cadre of District Surgeon is temporary for a period of 11 months and the post shall not be held by the officer concerned on a permanent basis.
Also, due to this promotion, the concerned officer will not get any rights of regular promotion i.e. seniority.
The officers who have been promoted shall give the option of fixation of pay within one month from the date of assumption of charge of the post after promotion, failing which their fixation of pay shall be governed by the provisions of Rule No. 7 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
8. The concerned officers should be relieved immediately to join their post after promotion. The concerned officers should report to the Deputy Director, Health Services, Director, Health Services, Mumbai and the Government within one month at the place of their posting. Any political pressure to change the post will invite disciplinary action under the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
9. Promoted officers to join the new post within the stipulated time after their discharge. Officers who do not appear on promotion within the prescribed time will be deemed to be unwilling to be promoted and their promotion will be cancelled. They will also be taken advantage of the promised progress scheme. the Deputy Director in this case.
