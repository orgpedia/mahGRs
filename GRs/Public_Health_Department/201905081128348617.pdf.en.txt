Preamble: The Government has decided to select the service provider by inviting open tender through the National Health Mission to provide the service of ambulances equipped with medical equipment with state-of-the-art communication system to provide immediate medical assistance to the disaster victims in the state. A Tender Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Health) vide order dated 28.10.2010. The tender committee has decided to qualify the tender submitted by M/ s BVG India for the implementation of Emergency Response Service (EMS) of 937 ambulances across the state in the next 5 years. BVG India, a service provider, was issued a work order vide Government Resolution, dated 28th March, 2013. Since the scheme was launched on 01.02.2014, the service provider has been given the deadline. It ended on January 31, 2019.
