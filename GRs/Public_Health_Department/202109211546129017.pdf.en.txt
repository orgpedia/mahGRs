The Proposal:
The following Medical Officers have been appointed as Medical Officers Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. The appointments have been made on the recommendation of the Independent Selection Board in the year 2013 for the post of" Medical Officer Group-A," which is the first appointment in Government service.
2. Under the decision of the General Administration Department, the revised guidelines have been passed regarding the termination of probation period of government service officers/ employees and the officers who have been given posting in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A, have been satisfied with their two-year probation period.
Since it had been completed, the matter of ending the probation period of these officers was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNORMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, the following Medical Officers Group A of the Maharashtra Medical and Health Services Cadre, as indicated in the table given in this paragraph, are required to complete their period of probation.
