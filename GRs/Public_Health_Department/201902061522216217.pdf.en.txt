No. 124/ Budget, D. 6.12.2018 Preamble- Central Government funds received for promotion and publicity of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana for the financial year 2018-2019 (60% share) Rs. 102.50 lakhs and State Government funds (40% share) Rs. 68.33 crore. A sum of Rs. 170.83 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Seventy Lakh Thirty Three Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision For the financial year 2018-2019, Central Government funds received for promotion and publicity of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (60% share) is Rs. 102.50 lakhs and State Government funds (40% share) Rs. 68.33 crore. This Government resolution approves the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 170.83 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Seventy Lakh Eighty Three Thousand only) sanctioned through Supplementary Demands in the Winter Session of 2018.
The Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Joint Director, Finance and Administration, Health Services, Mumbai as Controlling Officer for the aforesaid fund.
