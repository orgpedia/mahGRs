Preamble: In order to provide accessible, affordable, efficient, accountable and reliable health services to the rural population, the Government of India has launched the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) from April, 2005 across the country. Under this scheme, health services like Reproductive and Child Health Programme, regular immunization, Mission Flexipool, strengthening of infrastructure and pulse polio vaccination etc. are provided. Accordingly, under the National Health Mission, Rs. 11359.55 lacs to the corresponding State Account 22105246 amounting to Rs. 7573.03 lakh was under the consideration of the Government for providing 40% State share of grant from general category to the State Health Societies.
