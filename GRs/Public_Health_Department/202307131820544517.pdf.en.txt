Introduction: Indira Gandhi Memorial Hospital of Ichalkaranji Municipal Council has been transferred to the Public Health Department of the Government. The Government has decided to absorb 42 officers/ staff of the Indira Gandhi Memorial Hospital of Ichalkaranji Municipal Council into the Public Health Department. Issued on 27/12/2021. Out of the above 42 officers/ staff, 40 officers/ staff have been awarded. from 01/05/2019 and remaining 2 employees. Approved to enter into Government service with retrospective effect from 02/05/2019.
The Government has issued a Government Resolution regarding implementation of" Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme" under the Government Resolution dated 10/08/2022, from the date of induction of the above mentioned 42 officers/ staff.
In this regard, the people's representatives had requested the government to implement the old pension scheme for these officers/ employees. The matter was under consideration of the government.
42 officers/ staff of Indira Gandhi Memorial Hospital of Ichalkaranji Municipal Council were awarded with the rank of Sh. The Government have decided to implement the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme with effect from the date of induction into Government service vide order dated 10/08/2022 by superseding the Government decision dated 10/08/2022.
