GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the decision of the Government, 147 temporary posts of 37 medical teams constituted for the health check-up of students in the tribal sub-plan area have been filled up with effect from 01.10.2018. The extension was extended till 28.02.2019.
Now, under this Government decision, 147 temporary posts in these 37 medical teams are being approved to be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
The expenditure on these posts should be met from the following heads of accounts and the expenditure should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2019-2020: Demand No.R-1," 2210- Medical Services and Public Health, 06- Public Health, 001- Operation and Administration, (01) (08)- Upgradation of Primary Health Centre to the level of Rural Hospital, (2210 0413)"
