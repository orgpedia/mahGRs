The Proposal -
The Maharashtra Regulation of Government Transfers and Prevention of Delays in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005
Section 8 (1) (2) of the Act provides for the publication of a citizens' charter by each office or department.
has been done. Accordingly, the Citizens' Charter of the Department of Public Health is published on the government's computer website.
It was done but due to changes in the said Charter over time, the revised and updated Citizens' Charter was published.
It has been decided to do so. Accordingly, the Department of Public Health prepared an updated citizens' charter.
Reference No. 1 of the Committee constituted under the Circular dated 12.1.2010 of the General Administration Department herein.
Subject to further approval, the present citizens' charter is being published.
The Government's Decision-
Revised and updated Citizens' Charter of the Department of Public Health attached subject to the following conditions:
Books are being published. The citizens' certificate is also issued by the Public Health Department.
