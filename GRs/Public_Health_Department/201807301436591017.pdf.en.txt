GOVERNMENT DECISIONS As per the decision of the Government vide No. 1 above, it has been approved to upgrade the Dental Department functioning under the Public Health Department in the State. Accordingly, a total of 772 posts of Dental Surgeon, Group-A, Class-2 (1 post) and Dental Assistant, Group-D (1 post) have been created for 386 rural hospitals of the department with 30 beds. In the Rural Hospital, Balapur, G. Akola, 2 posts have been sanctioned and out of these 2 posts, 1 post of Dental Surgeon has been filled.
Now, as per the decision of the Government, one temporary post of Dental Surgeon, Group-A, Class-II in the Rural Hospital, Balapur, District Akola, under the Deputy Director, Health Services, Akola Circle, Akola, will be filled up. w.e.f. 16.03.2018 The period is being extended up to 30.09.2018.
