Government circular:
The Election Commission of India has announced the General Assembly Elections in the State on 21.10.2019. Government/ semi-government/ officers and staff/ police/ central paramilitary forces personnel have been deployed for election duty. Arrangements should be made to provide free health check-up, testing, medicine and treatment services to these employees immediately if they get injured or fall sick during the election process. These health check-ups, tests, medicines and treatment should be made available at all government hospitals/ government medical colleges/ hospitals of all local bodies, if the required facilities are not available there, then it should be provided at private hospitals affiliated to Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. If the hospital does not have adequate treatment facilities, arrangements should be made to provide treatment facilities in the nearest private hospital with the consent of the district surgeon of the concerned district. The government will bear the cost of treatment in private hospitals. Reimbursement of medical expenses in respect of State Government and semi-government employees through the concerned office in the prescribed manner.
