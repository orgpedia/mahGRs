Preamble: Upgradation of 30 bedded Rural Hospital to 100 bedded Sub District Hospital at Ghulewadi, Ahmednagar at a cost of Rs. The proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs. 29.70 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The budget for construction of building for 100 bedded sub-district hospital Ghulewadi G Ahmednagar by upgrading 30 bedded rural hospital is based on the tariff list of 2021-22. The budget for the work includes construction (ground and third floor 6742.84 sq.m.) at a cost of Rs. Provisions have been made for V, AC etc., and as per the Recapitulation Sheet attached, administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution for the projects with an outlay of Rs. 29.70 crore subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required.

2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Published on January 10, 2014
Action should be taken as per the guidelines.
The expenditure on this work will be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Dispensaries, (1) (A) (Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Large Constructions (42100014), 53- Large Constructions.
The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of a building in the 100-bed sub-district hospital Ghulewadi, Ahmednagar, by upgrading the 30-bed rural hospital.
