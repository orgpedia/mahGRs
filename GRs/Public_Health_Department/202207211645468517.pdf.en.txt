Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Preamble to the Government Resolution No. BGT-2022/ No. 48/ Budget (BGO), dated 14.07.2022:
It has been decided to merge Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana with Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and implement both the schemes in the State as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. For the financial year 2022-23, for the implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, an amount of Rs. 118 crore has been allocated in the budget. For the beneficiaries of this scheme, the premium for the period from July 2022 to September 2022 will be Rs. 32,84,77, 065/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores Eighty Four Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Pasts) to the State Health Guarantee Society under the head 2210 G 861 (SCP), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
Government's decision:
Since the Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana is being implemented through the State Health Guarantee Society, the provision budgeted under the head of accounts shown below for the financial year 2022-23, for payment of premium for the period from July 2022 to September 2022, an amount of Rs. 32,84,77, 065/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores Eighty Four Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Pasts) is being handed over to the Controlling Authority for immediate transfer to the account of State Health Guarantee Society. The amount should be deposited in the name of State Health Guarantee Society.
