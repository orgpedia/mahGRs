The state government has decided to provide free beds to Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad, at Primary Health Centres Pimpalwadi, Dhakephal, Niljagaon, Sub-Centres Isarwadi, Gharkin and Krishnapur for educational purposes.

The proposal of the Director of Health Services to allow the Government Medical College, Aurangabad to use the beds of Primary Health Center Pimpalwadi, Dhakephal, Niljagaon Sub-Centre Isarwadi, Gharkin and Krishnapur for educational purposes was under consideration of the government. The decision is as follows:
Government's decision
Government Medical College, Aurangabad is being given permission to use the beds of Primary Health Center Pimpalwadi, Dhakephal, Niljagaon Sub-Centre Isarwadi, Gharkin and Krishnapur for educational purposes free of cost.
