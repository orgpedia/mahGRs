The decision is as follows.
Government's decision:
The Administrative Officer, Group-B District Surgeon, Palghar Office is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" at District General Hospital, Palghar as per the provision of Rule No. 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
