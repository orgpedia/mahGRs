Preamble: A Task Force of Specialist Doctors was constituted in the State as per the decision of the Government dated 13.04.2020 to plan and control the surge during the first wave of COVID-19. A special task force of specialist doctors was formed to diagnose, analyze and take measures against the Covid disease. The number of Covid-19 cases is increasing in the state.
The task force needs to be reconstituted in view of the increasing number of patients. Therefore, the matter of reconstituting the task force was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The following specialist doctors are being appointed as Chairman/ Members to suggest/ guide measures for diagnosis, analysis and management of COVID disease.

Steps to be taken by the Task Force:
Establishing patient management protocols for critically ill COVID-19 patients.
Recommending the requirement of specialist doctors and health care support staff in COVID-19 critical care hospitals.
Recommending appropriate drug protocols to maintain uniformity in treatment of critically ill COVID-19 patients.
Any other recommendation as may be made by the Chairman of the Task Force.
The member secretary should report the recommendations made by the task force to the government from time to time.
