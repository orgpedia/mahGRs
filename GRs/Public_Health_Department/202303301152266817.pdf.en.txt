The Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority Act, 2023 has been enacted under the Public Health Department to make the procurement and supply of medicines and related materials of the Public Health Department and other departments more dynamic and streamlined. 17.03.2023 has been published. Hence, the government should take necessary steps in this regard.
The Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority Act, 2023 has been enacted under the Public Health Department to make the procurement and supply of medicines and related materials for the health institutions of the Public Health Department and other departments more dynamic and streamlined. As notified on 17.03.2023, instructions are being issued to take the following actions before implementing the" Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority 2023."

The entire process of providing office space, furniture, stationery, computer, information system, etc. for the Maharashtra Medical Goods Procurement Authority should be done by the Commissioner, Health Services, Health Services, Commissionerate, Mumbai. For this, the necessary resources should be utilised. Also, a suitable place should be found for the construction of a new building for the authority. A plan for the construction of the building should be prepared and a proposal should be submitted to the government.
It should be ensured that a place can be arranged in Mumbai for the Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority on a temporary basis. If such arrangements are not made, further action should be taken to take a temporary rented place in a private place. For this, the Commissioner Health Services, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai should do the work of finding a place and the agreement regarding it.
The existing medicine stores at the level of eight deputy directors, circle in the state will be used for this purpose. Also, if more space is required, more space can be made available by upgrading the available warehouses. Commissioner, Health Services, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall take necessary action in this regard.
Necessary posts under the Public Health Department are to be made available to the Authority by way of appointment/ deputation. The officers/ employees who will be dealing with the procurement process should be selected on priority. Proposals for manpower requisition for posts in other departments should be submitted to the concerned department by the Commissioner, Health Services, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai after the authority comes into existence. Also, the concerned administrative department will be required to provide the services of officers and employees with the necessary knowledge in the required cadre to the said authority.
5. The head of account from which funds are drawn on short payment for the purchase of medicines and related materials for the health institutions of the Public Health Department and other departments is transferred to the purchase room at Haffkine Corporation, Mumbai. Separate heads of accounts for the purposes of sub-title (31)-Additional Grants for Supplies and Materials and 52-Machinery and Equipment under the head Account of such revenue expenditure shall be created. Further, separate heads of accounts under Capital Expenditure Account, namely, 21. Supplies and Supplies and 52. Subsidiary Grants for Machinery and Equipment (35). Grants for Formation of Capital Grants shall be created. Further, for the salaries and allowances of the officers/ employees, contractual employees and other establishment related matters of the Authority, the head of accounts should be made as Subsidiary Grants-36 (Salary) and Subsidiary Grants-31 (Non-Salary).

The Commissioner of Health Services, Mumbai shall examine all proposals for the creation of necessary accounting titles relating to this department and the Authority and submit them to the Government. After accounting of the salary and establishment matters of the officers/ employees of the Authority, the proposals for the demand for grants for the implementation of the Authority should be submitted to the Government as per the requirement with proper support.
The Commissioner, Health Services, Commissionerate of Health Services, Mumbai shall be solely responsible for taking necessary measures to operationalize the Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority.
