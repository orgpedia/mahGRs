The Proposal:
To extend the coverage of the scheme to all citizens for a limited period so that COVID-19 patients do not have to bear the financial burden of getting treatment in an authorized private hospital and to provide health assurance and financial relief to all citizens during the COVID-19 pandemic.
The benefit of treatment under Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana should be available to other patients along with the beneficiary patients and the treatment methods reserved for government hospitals should be available under the scheme.
The decision was taken on 23rd May, 2020 with a view to ensure availability of necessary resources in the context of prevention of COVID-19 pandemic to the doctors, other staff and paramedics of the empanelled hospitals. Since the scheme is expiring on 31st July, 2020, the Government was considering extension of the period from 1st August, 2020 to 31st October, 2020.
Government's decision:
The number of Covid-19 cases in Maharashtra is increasing at an alarming rate.
Considering the benefits being provided to the citizens of the state under the Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana

Ensure health assurance and financial relief to all citizens in the current pandemic crisis
For this purpose, the scheme which came into force vide Government Resolution dated 23rd May, 2020, to provide benefits to all citizens in the empanelled hospitals under Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, has been extended to all the hospitals in the State.
The extension is for a period of three months from August, 2020 to 31st October, 2020.
