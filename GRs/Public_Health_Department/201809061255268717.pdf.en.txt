Introduction: The primary health centre at Ashti has been approved as a rural hospital in Ashti. The matter of shifting Ashti to G. Ashti was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government's decision:
2.Have fun. Ashti G. Wardha has been sanctioned as a rural hospital and the primary health centre there is Mauje Antora. Relocation of Ashti G. Wardha is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) It will be necessary to provide free space to the concerned Gram Panchayat so that the primary health center can be operational in the relocated place.
2) The temporary arrangement of building where the primary health centre can function at the place of relocation and the arrangement of staff accommodation will be required. So that in this place there should be an outpatient service, separate arrangement for female patient examination, arrangement for delivery, arrangement for staff seating.
(3) The services of the officers and staff of the primary health centre shall be made available to other primary health centres till the primary health centre is made functional on a temporary basis.
