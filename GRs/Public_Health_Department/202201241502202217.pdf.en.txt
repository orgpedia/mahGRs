The Proposal:
30 Rural Hospital, 50 bedded Sub District Hospital by upgrading Naramner G. Jalgaon, Rs. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs. 7.32 crore was under the consideration of the government. The following decision has been taken:
Government's decision:
The budget for the construction of 50 bedded Sub District Hospital building by upgrading 30 Rural Hospital, Amalner, Jalgaon is based on the tariff list of 2019-20. The budget for the work has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Mangalore. He has also been certified by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Jalgaon; Chief Engineer Public Works Department, Nashik; Deputy Director Health Services, Nashik; and District Surgeon, Jalgaon. Accordingly, administrative approval is being accorded to the projects with a budget of Rs. 7.32 crore subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions: 1) The technical approval of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department will be required before inviting tenders for the work.
2) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
(3) The work shall be tendered together without separate tenders.
4) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
(5) As the cost of the proposed work is likely to increase according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the area officers of the Public Works Department.
As the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budgets of these works shall be technically approved by the competent authorities of the Public Works Department in their respective areas.
7) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
8) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Published on January 10, 2014
Action should be taken as per the guidelines.
Expenditure on construction of 50 bedded Sub District Hospital, building by upgrading 30 Rural Hospital, Naramalner G. Jalgaon will be covered under Demand No.H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Urban Health Services (Medical Assistance) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Clinics, (1) (a) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major Constructions (42100014), 53- Major Constructions.
The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the government to allocate funds in the budget for the construction of a 50-bed sub-district hospital by upgrading the 30 rural hospital, Naramner G. Jalgaon.
