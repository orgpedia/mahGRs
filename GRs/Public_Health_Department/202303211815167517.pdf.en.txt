Terms and Conditions:
(1) The requirement/ demand for the said medicines and drug materials should be ascertained. Thereafter, the tender process will be initiated.

Only the items included in the proposal should be purchased.
(3) The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by government departments published under the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labour Department, c.
(4) The prices of the proposed procurement are estimated at the time of administrative approval, so the GEM Portal, the prices of other states, the National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority NPPA. Minimum rates available with National Medical Devices Authority (NDMA) should be considered. Also, the minimum rates received in the tender, if any, in respect of drugs, pharmaceuticals and equipment, if any, purchased or to be procured at Haffkine Institute, shall be recorded as the estimated rates in respect of drugs, pharmaceuticals and equipment to be called for tender and shall be determined at the end of the procurement process. The price is lower than the market price and the rate at which the supplier has supplied the product to other places. Be sure not to exceed this limit.
(5) Pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection of the items to be purchased should be done to ensure that only the items with proper and correct specifications are purchased.
