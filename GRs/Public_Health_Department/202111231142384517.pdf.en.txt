Preamble: The Central Government has decided to disburse grants to the States under the 15th Finance Commission to strengthen the health system in the rural/ urban local bodies in the State.
The Government of India will disburse this grant during the financial year 2021-22 to 2021-22.
To make these grants available to local bodies, a new accounting head needs to be opened. Accordingly, a proposal was submitted to the Finance Department and Accountant General-1, Mumbai office for the creation of a total of 8 new headings of accounts, namely 6 new headings of revenue expenditure for budgeting the funds received from the Central Government and 2 new headings of revenue collection for auxiliary grants received from the Central Government. The Finance Ministry has approved the proposal. Further, the Accountant General-1, Mumbai Office vide their letter dated 03.12.2021 has approved the title of the accounts. Therefore, the government was considering to introduce a new scheme.
Government Decision: In order to strengthen the health system in the rural/ urban local bodies of the State, the Central Government is approving a total of 8 new heads of accounts for disbursement of grants to the States under the 15th Finance Commission, viz. Below is the text of the article. :
