The proposal-
Wardha district is also known as Gandhi district due to the residence of Mahatma Gandhi. As there are large number of gram panchayats in the district, the people's representatives have set up primary health centres in these villages.
There is a frequent demand for installation. Gandhi district on the occasion of Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav
Therefore, it is necessary to strengthen the health service by establishing a primary health center for the large gram panchayat of Wardha district.
The proposal was under the consideration of the government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision
New primary health centres to be set up in Wardha taluka and district
The decision is taken by the government.
