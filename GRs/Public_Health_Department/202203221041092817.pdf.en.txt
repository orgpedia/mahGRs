Introduction: The revised Government Decision is being issued by superseding the Government Decision at Reference No. 7 hereunder.
It has been decided to implement both the schemes in the State by merging Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana with Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana as per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019. For the financial year 2021-22, an amount of Rs.2210.861 crore has been allocated under the head of Account for implementation of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. An amount of Rs. 152.00 crore (Rupees one hundred and fifty two crore only) has been provided in the Budget. For the beneficiaries of this scheme, the premium for the period from January 2022 to March 2022 will be Rs. 32,13,36, 258/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores Thirteen Lakhs Thirty Six Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Eight only) and Rs. 8,59,29, 869/- (Rupees Eight Crores Sixty-nine Lakhs Thirty-nine Thousand Eight Hundred and Seventy-one only) aggregating to Rs. 40,72,66, 127/- (Rupees Forty Crores Seventy Two Lakhs Sixty Six Thousand One Hundred and Twenty Seven only) Fund 2210 G 861
