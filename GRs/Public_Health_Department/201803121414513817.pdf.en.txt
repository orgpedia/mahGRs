Departmental Promotion Committee to be constituted for selection to the posts in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A and General State Services, Group-A
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Public Health
Government Resolution No: Promotion- 1118/ PR No. 24/ Service- 2
Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital Complex Building
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400
The date: March 13, 2018
Preamble: Under the Public Health Department, the Government was considering the formation of a Departmental Promotion Committee to prepare a selection list for appointment to the posts in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A and General State Services, Group-A.
Government Decision: The Departmental Promotion Committee is being constituted to prepare the selection list for the promotion of posts in Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A and General State Services, Group-A in the Public Health Department.
(i) Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary Public Health Chairman Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai (ii) Commissioner, Health Services and Director, National Health Mission, Mumbai (iii) Director, Health Services, Mumbai Member (iv) Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, (v) Public Health Department, Ministry of Health, Mumbai (v) Backward Classes Representative (Public Health Department, Ministry, Member Mumbai Group-A Cadre Officers) (vi) Chief Administrative Officer, Directorate of Health Services, Mumbai Member Secretary
The Departmental Promotion Committee will work taking into consideration the provisions of the order issued by the General Administration Department regarding the promotion from time to time, as well as the service entry rules of the posts, the updated service excellence, the updated confidential report, etc.
