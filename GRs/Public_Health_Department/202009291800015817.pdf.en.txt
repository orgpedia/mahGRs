Please refer to the above letter.
The Public Health Department has agreed to transfer the three primary health centres of Zilla Parishad, Palghar, Kadil Sopara, Nirmal and Chandansar, and 12 sub-centres of Naigaon, Sandoar, Umela, Nirmal, Chandansar, Pelhar, Bolinj, Chikhaldongri, Gokhivre, Juchandra, Sativali and Waliv under the jurisdiction of Vasai-Virar Municipal Corporation to Vasai-Virar Municipal Corporation.
The ZP, Palghar and Vasai-Virar municipal corporations should mutually decide the terms and conditions.
All health facilities provided by the above primary health centres and sub-centres will be required to be provided by the Municipal Corporation after the transfer. The Palghar Zilla Parishad should take a decision as per the need and submit a proposal to the government through the Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai, regarding the opening of primary health centres/ sub-centres in lieu of these primary health centres/ sub-centres, if needed in Palghar district.
