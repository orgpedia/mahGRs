The Proposal:
Dr. Dattatraya Madhav Junnarkar, Medical Officer, as mentioned in the table below, has been appointed as Medical Officer Group-A in the cadre of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services for a period of two years. The appointment was made in the year 2002 as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
As per the decision of the Government in the context of General Administration Department, revised guidelines have been passed regarding the expiry of probation period of the officers/ employees in the Government service and since the officers posted in the post of Medical Officer, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A have completed their two-year probation period satisfactorily, the matter of expiry of probation period of these officers was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, as indicated in the table given in this paragraph, Dr. Kranti Sundarrao Raut, Medical Officer Group-A of Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, has satisfactorily fulfilled the requirements to complete his probation period. The date mentioned in (M.No.) 5 is being terminated. Also, the date of continuation of their services on regular basis is mentioned in column No. 7 is mentioned.
