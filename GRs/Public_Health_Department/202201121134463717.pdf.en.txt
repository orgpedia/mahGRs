Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, Group-A posts of Deputy Director, Health Services are vacant in large numbers, due to which these posts are filled by temporarily transferring from the lower cadre officers. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2), Section 4 (5) and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005, the following officers are being transferred and posted by downgrading the rank to the cadre of Deputy Director, Health Services, Group-A, Maharashtra Medical and Health Services, with the approval of the competent authority:
