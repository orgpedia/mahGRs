The above administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: 1) The requirement/ demand for the said material should be verified/ verified. After that, the tender process should be carried out for printing these items.
2) The specifications of the material should be verified as per requirement and as up-to-date. The technical specifications of the items to be printed should be as per the specifications agreed by the concerned experts (Technical Committee/ Officials).
Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not designed in such a way that it will benefit any particular company.
The responsibility of sending the requisition as per the technical specifications of the items approved in the PIP will be of the concerned programme head. Only the items included in the proposal should be purchased.
(3) The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by government departments published under the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated December 1, 2016.
4) The rates of the proposed items are estimated at the time of granting administrative approval. Hence, the price should be fixed after the tender process. The price is lower than the market price.
And it should be ensured that the supplier concerned does not exceed the rate at which the matter is supplied to other places.
5) Pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection of the items to be printed should be done to ensure proper and correct specification.
6) Storage and distribution of printing items after supply, supply chain management and inventory management should be done in a proper manner to ensure that these items do not remain unused and are used in a timely manner.
7) Expenditure on the above is to be met from the grants made available by the State Health Societies under the National Health Mission for the year 2018-19.
