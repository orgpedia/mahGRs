The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority has been operationalized vide Government Notification dated 10.05.2023 for procurement of drugs, equipment and allied items from the health machinery under the Public Health Department, Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department. Accordingly, the purchase must now be made through the said authority. Government's decision:
For the health institutions under the Public Health Department and the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department, the District Planning Committee is approving 100% local procurement of medicines from the funds received in the budget year 2023-24.
