In 1967, the government had purchased a plot of land from Nagpur Sudhar Pranyas to start a service clinic under the State Workers Insurance Scheme in Bagadganj, Nagpur. Commissioner, State Workers Insurance Scheme, Mumbai vide Reference No. Under the letter of 3rd July, a total amount of Rs. 21,46,000/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Forty Six Thousand only) was submitted to the Government for the construction of fence wall of Sewa Dawakhana at Bagadganj, Nagpur. Accordingly, considering the Goods and Services Tax and other ancillary matters, the Government was considering granting administrative approval to the Budget with a total outlay of Rs. 22,88,940/-.
GOVERNMENT DECISION- Under this Government decision, administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of fence wall of Sewa Dawakhana at Bagadganj, Nagpur at a total cost of Rs. 22,88,940/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty only) subject to the following conditions.
