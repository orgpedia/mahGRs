Terms and Conditions:
(1) The period from the date of absence to the date of actual joining of Dr. Davane Medical Officer, Group-A shall be deemed to be a period of service for all service purposes and he shall be engaged. The entire period of his absence shall be deemed to be a break in service for all service purposes and shall be so clearly mentioned in his joining order.
(2) Before admitting a doctor, a medical board shall conduct a medical examination of the medical practitioner and obtain a certificate from the medical board that he is physically/ mentally competent to perform the service.
(3) The character and conduct report of the concerned medical officer (police investigation report) should be taken. (A police report should be taken that no complaint of criminal nature or under the Prevention of Corruption Act is pending.) )
(4) No regular/ temporary service before the absence as mentioned in No. 1 shall be added to the service after joining after the absence and the service shall not be availed continuously. Also, since the period of absence is considered as a break in service, the period of absence of the concerned medical officer will not be considered for any service scheme. The matter will not be taken to court in the future.
