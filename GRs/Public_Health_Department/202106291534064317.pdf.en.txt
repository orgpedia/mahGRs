Introduction: The government was considering the establishment of a new sub-center at Mauje Babhali, Taluka Parbhani, District Parbhani.
Government's decision:
Under this decision, approval is being given to set up new sub-stations at Mauje Babhali, Taluka Parbhani and District Parbhani subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The new premises shall be equipped with a building for the functioning of the Sub-Centre and accommodation for the staff. There will be separate arrangements for outpatient services, female patient check-up, delivery, staff seating, parking if there is an ambulance.
(2) The building shall be equipped with water and electricity.
3) The new location should have all the necessary facilities for this substation.
(4) The District Planning and Development Board shall make provision for the construction of the main building and residences of the Sub-Centre at the new place from its District Plan.
