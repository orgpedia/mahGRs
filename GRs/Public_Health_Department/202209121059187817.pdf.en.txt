The Proposal:
General Hospital, Malegaon. A proposal to give administrative approval to the budget and maps amounting to Rs 473.62 lakh was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Budget for the repair, beautification and concretization work at General Hospital, Malegaon G. Nashik is based on the Rate List of 2021-22. In the budget for this work, 0.5 per cent has been allocated for 4 per cent labour insurance for internal contingency expenses.
As per the Recapitulation Sheet attached, the administrative approval is being accorded under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) Before inviting tenders for the work, the technical approval of the Chief Engineer, (Vidyut) Public Works Department will be required for the work.

2) Public Works Department vide Government Circular dated 29.10.2018
It is mandatory to approve the external rate in the budget.
3) Administrative approval is being given subject to the condition that the work is funded from the available budget for construction.
4) The work should be tendered together without separate tenders.
5) The head of the health institution concerned should ensure the availability, suitability and adequacy of space for work.
6) If it is possible to increase the cost of the proposed work according to the situation of the area and the requirements of the health institutions, the necessary proposal should be approved by the zonal officers of the Public Works Department.
7) Since the said budget is a rough model budget, the detailed budget of these works is technically approved by the Public Works Department.
It will be done by the competent authorities of the region.
8) The Public Works Department will be responsible for obtaining construction permits and occupancy certificates as per the relevant rules of the local rural development agency.
9) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV 2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
10) The responsibility of fulfilling the condition from 1 to 9 above will be of the concerned District Surgeon and Executive Engineer, (Vidyut).
3. Expenditure on repair, beautification and concretization work at General Hospital, Malegaon G. Nashik will be covered under Demand No. H-8, Head of Accounts 4210- Capital Expenditure on Medical Services and Public Health Services, 01- Civil Health Services (Medical Assistance)- Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, 110- Hospitals and Dispensaries, (1) (a) Scheme Buildings under Five Year Plans, (00) (01) Major Constructions (42100014), 53- Major Constructions.
4. The Commissioner, Health Services, Mumbai should immediately submit a proposal to the Government to allocate funds in the budget for the repair, beautification and concretization work of the General Hospital, Malegaon, Nashik.
