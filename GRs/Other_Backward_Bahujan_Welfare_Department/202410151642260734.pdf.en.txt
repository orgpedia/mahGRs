The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Gawli community. The Government was contemplating to set up a "Srikrishna Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary)" as a subsidiary under Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited) for the overall development of the Gawli community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme) under Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited) to the youth of the Gawli community belonging to category B nomadic tribes of the State.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government is approving the setting up of Additional Development Corporation for the Gawli community under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of the Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes category in the State. The corporation will be called "Shrikushna Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be run by the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation. The details of the structure, functions, and schemes of the Shri Krishna Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Composition of Corporation (State / District level)
The headquarters of the subsidiary Shri Krishna Economic Development Corporation will be at the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation headquarters in Mumbai. District offices will continue to function in all the districts of the state.
The Board of Directors of the Corporation shall consist of the Chairman, Vice-Chairman and three non-officio Members B and four Executive Members.
