Reference No. 1 is mentioned here. Paragraph No in the Government Decision of 08.10.2024. 1B Paragraph No. 2 (1) is being amended as follows.
Paragraph No. As efforts are required for the social, economic and educational development of Other Backward Castes-A, Nomadic Tribes-B, Nomadic Tribes-C and Nomadic Tribes-D including Gor Banjara Tribe, it is being approved to set up an autonomous institute "Vasantrao Naik Research and Training Institute (VANARTY)" in Mumbai under Rule 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, primarily for research and study of these tribes, to pay close attention to their social, economic and educational development and to carry out various programmes / activities for students, youth and other candidates belonging to these communities.
Paragraph No. 9. To study and understand the social, educational and developmental issues of other deprived and marginalized members / families, mainly belonging to Other Denotified Castes-A, Nomadic Tribes-B, Nomadic Tribes-C and Nomadic Tribes-D, including the Gor Banjara tribe, and to suggest various measures to the Government for these elements.
