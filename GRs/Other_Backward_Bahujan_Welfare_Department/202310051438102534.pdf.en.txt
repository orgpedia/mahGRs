Government Memorandum: Rs 12,000 crore will be provided for the new" Modi Awas" housing scheme in the next 3 years to build 10 lakh houses in three years from 2023-24 to 2025-26, out of which 3 lakh houses are proposed to be completed in the first year 2023-24 at a cost of Rs 3,600 crore. Accordingly, Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) has been made available by way of Supplementary Demands and the funds have been released as per" Demand No. ZG-3, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 03- Welfare of Backward Classes, 102- Economic Development, (01) Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes, (01) Welfare of Other Backward Classes beneficiaries under the head Modi Awas Ghar Kul Yojana (2225F782) (Programme), 31-Aid Subsidiary Grants are being distributed to the Director, State Management Cell-Rural Housing Cell, CIDCO Bhawan, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai.
This Government Memorandum has been issued vide S.No. 807/ Expenditure-14, D.O. is being issued as per the consent received vide 06.09.2023.
