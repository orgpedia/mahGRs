GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: AMRUT 2020/ PR No.10/ Institution (AMRUT)
The government has decided to appoint Managing Director, Amrit, in place of Director, Other Backward Bahujan Directorate, to the post of Member Secretary of the Board of Governors of Amrit Institute. This has been approved in the meeting of the Governing Council held on 19.04.2023.
