The Virtual Personal Deposit account shall be opened under MH 8443 Civil Deposits 106 Personal Deposit (00) (02). Virtual Personal Deposit account
The following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA).
1. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms, and conditions of the relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
3. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
4. VPDA is applicable only for State scheme and not for Central sector and centrally sponsored schemes.
5. The Receipt to the said VPDA through Nil Bill procedure, shall be only for the bills drawn under :-
e MTR-31B for the object heads 27-Minor Works and 53-Major Works.
e MTR-45B for the object heads of expenditure 33- subsides and 50- Other Charges.
e MTR-44 for the object heads of expenditure 31- Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary) and 35-Grants in Aid for creating of Capital Assets.
e MTR-29_ for the object head of expenditure 52-Machinery and Equipment Shall be applicable from 1 April 2025.
6. The administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate in the prescribed format after incurring expenditure from the VPDA to the offices viz. O/o PAG (A&E)-1, Mumbai, O/o PAG (A&E)-Il Nagpur or O/o PAO, Mumbai as per the respective jurisdiction for the Bills drawn under MTR-44 for object heads of expenditure 31-Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary),35-Grants-in-Aid for creation of Capital Assets.
For bills drawn under MTR-31B,MTR-45B,MTR,29 the Utilisation Certificate shall be submitted to the concerned administrative departments.
7. The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the respective Grant.
8. The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund after completion of one year (365 days) from the date of drawal of the funds/ date of credit of the amount to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order, whichever is earlier.
9. The Monthly Plus Minus Memo shall be forwarded to this’ office by the respective treasury officer.
