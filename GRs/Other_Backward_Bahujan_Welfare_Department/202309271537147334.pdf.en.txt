Preamble: For the financial year 2023-24, 48% of the budgetary allocation for Post-Matric Scholarship for the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes has been released by the Finance Department on the BEAMS system. The Finance Department has released Rs. 690.1684 crore, out of which Rs. 591.0578 crore has been disbursed. From the remaining funds, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune, was under the consideration of the government to disburse funds for payment of scholarships for the registered applications on the MahaDBT portal.
Government Decision In the financial year 2023-24, 48% of the funds sanctioned as per the budgetary provision have been made available on the BEAMS system for payment of pending scholarships/ freeships to students belonging to the categories of Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes under the Post-Matric Scholarship and Freeship Scheme. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 96,72,35, 000/- (Rupees Ninety Five Crores Seventy Two Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand only) to the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune.

The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune shall be the controlling authority for the said scheme. The Controlling Officer shall spend the expenditure from the prescribed purpose within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for submitting the statement of expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and utility certificate to the government without fail.
Under the scheme, the Controller will be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controller of Utilization Certificates of the current financial year as well as the previous year's expenditure. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularity takes place while spending the said fund as per the terms and conditions of the Financial Rules, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Finance Department from time to time.
The Controlling Officer shall ensure that the conditions contained in the Government Decision/ Circular issued by the Finance Department from time to time are fulfilled while spending the funds.
5. The decision of the Government shall be subject to the approval of the Finance Department. The issue is being issued as per the approval given vide circular dated 12.04.2023 and as agreed by the Department of Finance from time to time.
