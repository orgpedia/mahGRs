The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2022 (Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2022) has been passed by the Legislative Assembly. The Governor has given his assent to it and it shall come into force as the Maharashtra Act No. 30 of 2022 by the Government of Maharashtra, Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, D. It will be released on March 28, 2022.
Subject to the terms and conditions of the circular dated 4 th April, 2022 of the Department of Finance, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programmed expenditure required for the first nine months of 2022-23 (ending December, 2022) will be disbursed on BEAMS within the limit of 60% of the total annual provision.
Since the revenue that has decreased in the lockdown during the Corona 2019 pandemic period of the last two years and this revenue will be received in phases, the department also needs to distribute and spend the budgeted funds in a phased manner as per the need.
At present, 1st salary, 4th pension and 36 Supplementary Grants (Salaries) will be disbursed at the rate of 10% in April 2022, 8% in May 2022 and 10% in June 2022. All remaining targets will be funded at 7% per month on BIMSTEC.
