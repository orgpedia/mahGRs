The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the seamen community. The sewing community, which belongs to the Other Backward Class category in the state, is largely engaged in the sewing business.
It was under consideration of the Government to set up "Shri Sant Shiromani Namdeo Maharaj Samastha Shrimpi Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary)" as a Subsidiary under Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the overall development of the Shrimpi community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Other Backward Classes (OBCs) under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is functioning at present for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Shri Sant Shiromani Namdeo Maharaj Samastha Shrimpi Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The details of the composition, functioning, and schemes to be implemented of the Shri Sant Shiromani Namdeo Maharaj Samastha Srimpi Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Composition of the Corporation (State Level / District Level) The headquarters of the Sub-Company shall be located at Mumbai at the headquarters of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in all the districts of the state.
