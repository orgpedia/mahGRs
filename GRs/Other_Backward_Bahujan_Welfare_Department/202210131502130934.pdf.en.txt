Preamble: The Foreign Scholarship Scheme (FSS) was introduced in 2018-19 with reference No. 1 for 10 students belonging to VIJABHAJ, IMWAV and VIMAPRA categories to enable them to pursue higher education in reputed institutions abroad. The date mentioned herein has been brought into force by the Government Decision dated 11th October, 2018.
Under the scheme, foreign scholarships are sanctioned to eligible students who are admitted to a foreign educational institution with an average world ranking of QS and THE within 200 for post-graduate and PhD courses in the Visva-Bharati, EMV and VIMAPRA categories in Maharashtra. Accordingly, for the year 2022-23, under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme, the Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Director, Other Backward Bahujan Directorate, Pune, has recommended the above mentioned reference No. Proposal 4 has been submitted. Under this scheme, Reference No. 5 is mentioned here. As per the Government decision dated 11/10/2022, the number of students has been increased from 10 to 50. Also Reference No. Under Section 6, the minutes of the meeting of the selection committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department have been issued. As per the approval of the selection committee, the government was considering the matter of selecting 50 deserving students and sanctioning them foreign scholarships.

Government Decision: Under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme, Reference No. The State Level Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare) vide Government Resolution dated 11.10.2018, hereby approves the grant of scholarship to a total of 50 students admitted in a foreign educational institution having world ranking within 200 on an average in QS and THE for Masters Degree and Ph.D. Courses in the category of VJBJ, IMAV and VIMAPRA in Maharashtra for higher studies abroad, subject to the condition that the number of students in the merit/ high score as per the branch/ course and subject to the fulfillment of caste validity certificate and other documents/ certificates mentioned in the accompanying Form-A.
2. The Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare Department concerned is being authorized to grant admissible scholarship to the student selected for foreign scholarship. Students selected for foreign scholarships. The Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare Department concerned should take action to disburse the permissible scholarship to the concerned student by taking the guarantees prescribed under the Government decision of 11.10.2018 and examining the original documents of the students.
The students who have been approved foreign scholarship by the government, after completing their education, are required to serve the country and benefit from their knowledge. In this regard, it will be mandatory to write a letter of guarantee (BOND) from the concerned student before approving the scholarship.
The student selected for the foreign scholarship must complete the Master's degree or PhD course abroad within the prescribed period. Under no circumstances will the student be given an extension or increased cost. In exceptional circumstances, if there is a delay in admission in a foreign university, the matter should be dealt with in accordance with the provisions of the government decision.
The amount of the scholarship will be recovered in full from the student selected for the foreign scholarship if the student changes the place or course of the educational institution, university or course approved for higher education.
The Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare Department concerned shall, after obtaining authenticated information from the concerned university, pay the tuition fee to the concerned university and the amount of the maintenance allowance and other incidental expenses to the bank account of the students who have been sanctioned the scholarship out of the total cost of the course for which the scholarship is sanctioned.
