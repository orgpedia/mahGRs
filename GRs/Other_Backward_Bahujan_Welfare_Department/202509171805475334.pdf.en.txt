Government Orders: -
Out of a total of 31 candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for the posts of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Officer (Group-B) (Gazetted) (Advertisement No. 133/2023) cadre, 29 candidates have been provisionally appointed and posted as "Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Officer (Group-B) (Gazetted)" under Government Order No. 1. All the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Order at Reference No. 1 shall apply in respect of the temporary appointment of Mr. Vishal Sunil Choudhary to the cadre of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Officer (Group-B) in direct service.
3. Also, Mr. Vishal Sunil Choudhary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Officer, Group-B will not be required to attend the office of the Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Dhule.
