Government's decision:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme for the Deprived Castes and Nomadic Tribes, Government Resolution Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. Under section 2 to 4, the Collector and Chairman of the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme District Committee, Yavatmal, has finalized the names of the beneficiaries in respect of the households in Yavatmal district. 5 submitted to the government.
For the year 2023-24, 3074 individual household eligible beneficiaries (as per Annexure A) in Yavatmal district will be given Rs. 1.20 lakh as against Rs. 36,88,80, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) and 4% Administrative Fund (Rs. 4800/- (Rs. 1,47,55, 200/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Seven Lakh Fifty Five Thousand Two Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 38,36,35, 200/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Crores Thirty Six Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand Two Hundred only) is being approved by the Government. At present, Rs. A sum of Rs. 4.00 crore (Rupees Four Crores only) is being released and the remaining funds will be released as per availability.

3. Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, the said approval is being given to 3074 individual house site beneficiaries in Annexure A subject to the following conditions.
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure' A' attached to it from among the beneficiaries who have been determined eligible by the Yavatmal District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
3. Person having caste certificate. The same person will be eligible for the benefit of this scheme. It will not benefit anyone else.
Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a difference in the name of one of the beneficiaries, the name of the parent/ elder or the surname, then to remove that difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
The District Level Committee shall be required to take necessary action in the matter and approve the housing scheme of the individual beneficiary subject to the provisions of the relevant Government Decision. Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case.
4. The District Collector, Yavatmal and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Yavatmal will be responsible for ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case.
5. Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Yavatmal is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
