Introduction: The Academy of Maharashtra, Research, Upliftment and Training (AMRUT) will be set up for the development of students, youth, young women, etc. from the economically weaker sections of the open category in the state. 1 Approved by the Government Decision dated 22.8.2019. The Society is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the Public Trust Act, 1950 (Bombay Act No. 29) has been made and the Memorandum of Association and Rules made for this purpose have been decided by the Government. Approved on 28/06/2021. As approved by the Executive Committee of AMRUT (Board of Governors) in its meeting dated 27.2.2023, the Managing Director, AMRUT has been delegated administrative and financial powers for planning and implementation of innovative schemes.
The Managing Director, AMRIT, vide reference No. 3 vide letter dated 13.3.2023, referred to herein, has requested for allocation of budgetary provision in the financial year 2022-23 for implementation of schemes for development of students, youth, women etc. belonging to economically weaker sections of the open category.
Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds to AMRUT by giving administrative approval for the implementation of the proposed new scheme at the office expenditure and institution level was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: AMRUT 2022/ PR No. 01/ Institutions
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Research Advancement and Training Academy (AMRUT) has been asked to start functioning and spend on planning and implementation of schemes at the institution level in the financial year 2022-23. Out of the provision available under the head ZG 2225 Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities (01) (10), Maharashtra Research Advancement and Training Academy (AMRUT) (Programme), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) (2225F 532), Rs. 23.52 crore (Rupees Twenty Three Crore Fifty Two Lakh only) is being approved. Utilisation certificates will have to be submitted to the government from time to time after spending the funds.
2. Deputy Secretary (Institutions) as Controlling Officer and Cell Officer (Cash Branch) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer to disburse the said funds.
