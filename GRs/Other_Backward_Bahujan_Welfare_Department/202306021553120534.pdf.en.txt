The Proposal:
As per the guidelines of the Central Government in 1998, scholarships are sanctioned to students belonging to Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes. Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Government of India Scholarship, the Central Government has sanctioned a total of 605 courses as per the group of courses prescribed vide letter No. 1 has been approved. It has also been decided that the power to increase or change the syllabus, as the case may be, will remain with the government or a government-appointed committee. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune, headed by Reader No. 2 of the committee. As per the decision taken in the meeting dated 14.12.2021, Diploma in Mechatronics Engineering is a job oriented course and is recommended to be eligible for scholarship/ freeship. Therefore, the government was considering to include this course in the Maha DBT system.
Government Decision: A committee constituted under the chairmanship of Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune. As per the decision taken in the meeting dated 14.12.2021, the Government is approving inclusion of the course Diploma in Mechatronics Engineering on MahaDBT system and mapping of the said course with the respective scholarship/ freeship schemes.
