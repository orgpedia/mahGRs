Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, the approval is being given to 124 individual household beneficiaries in the contiguous Schedule-A subject to the following conditions:
The benefit of the said scheme will be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure-A, who have been included among the beneficiaries qualified by the Latur District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
(3) The benefit shall be admissible to the person having the caste certificate and shall not be admissible to any other person.
Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a discrepancy in the name of any of the beneficiaries, in the name or surname of the parent/ elder, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned to remove the discrepancy. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
8. The District Level Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the terms and conditions contained in the relevant Government Decision/ Corrigendum.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in the said case will be of the Collector and the Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Latur and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Latur.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Latur as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme, Latur and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Latur shall not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions. No additional funds will be provided for this purpose.
