The memo:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana, a total of 753 individual household beneficiaries have been given post-facto approval in Gondia, Ratnagiri, Satara and Nagpur districts respectively, as per the Government decision in reference number 1 to 4 in 2021-22. A total of Rs. 753. 939.744 lakh has been sanctioned. 230.00 lakhs has been disbursed. For Rs. 709.744 lakh is to be released as per the availability of funds and completion of works.

The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" for disbursement of the said funds and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare/ Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare of the concerned districts as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
