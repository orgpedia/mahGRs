The Proposal: -
Demand for setting up of separate corporation for social upliftment of Teli community
It is being done by public representatives, various organizations for the past many years. The Teli community, which is among the Other Backward Classes in the state, is mainly in the business of oil refining and marketing.
The government was considering setting up the Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (SSSDC) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Teli community by providing the benefit of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) to the youth of the Teli community under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. Accordingly, the Corporation (Subsidiary) was incorporated on the date 08.10.2024. The decision was taken at a cabinet meeting.
Pursuant to this decision, the Government of Maharashtra has approved the establishment of a subsidiary company "Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation" under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation vide Government Decision dated 08.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government Decision dated 08.10.2024 under reference is being superseded by this Government Decision.
b) Included in the Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is currently functioning for the social upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the state.

The government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Teli community. The corporation will be called "Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Adhuri Vikas Nigam (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)."
