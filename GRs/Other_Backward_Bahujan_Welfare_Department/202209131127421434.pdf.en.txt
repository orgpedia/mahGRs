3rd Floor, New Administrative Building, Mumbai- 400 032.

Read the government's decision: 24th March, 2022
The Proposal:
Under the scheme of construction of houses for the Dhangar community of the nomadic ST-C category in Gondia district, the proposal of a total of 15 individual beneficiaries approved by the committee chaired by the Collector, Gondia, has been approved under the relevant government decision. The matter of sanctioning the release of funds to the extent of Rs. 18,00,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the scheme of construction of houses for Dhangar community, the proposal of a total of 15 individual beneficiaries of Dhangar community in Gondia district has been approved under the relevant government decision. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 18,00,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakh only).
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of the said funds and he shall disburse the same through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the office of the Director, State Management Cell-Rural Housing Cell, CIDCO Bhawan, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai. The amount shall be credited directly to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of the beneficiary mentioned in Annexure' A' attached with the decision of the Government in a phased manner as per the stage of construction through Direct Beneficiary Transfer (DBT) through Public Financial Management System (PFMS).
The additional expenditure shall be met out of the provisions made under the head Demand No.ZG-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 03, Welfare of Backward Classes (04) (03) Scheme for Construction of Houses for Dhangar Community in Nomadic Tribes (C) Category (Programme) (2225F461) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) during the financial year 2022-23.
