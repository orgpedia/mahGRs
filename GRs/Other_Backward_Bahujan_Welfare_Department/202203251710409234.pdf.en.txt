3. Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department. On 09.03.2017, it was separated from the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. As the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department has not issued a circular, students belonging to the category of Vimukta Jati/ Nomadic Tribes/ Other Backward Classes who are residents of Maharashtra state and have taken admission in a foreign state outside the state were deprived of the scholarship.
Therefore, the students belonging to the category of Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes residing in the State of Maharashtra who have taken admission for private unaided and permanent aided professional courses in foreign countries outside the State, will be provided Post-Matric Scholarship, Tuition Fee, Examination Fee Reimbursement, Living Allowance etc. by the Government of India from the year 2017-18.
The circular allows offline payments to be made to Aadhaar-linked bank accounts of students.
Accordingly, the proposals of account-wise funds of the students belonging to the state of Maharashtra and studying abroad in the category of students belonging to the Visva-Bhaj, Bima-Prabha and Emav categories should be examined as per the existing government provisions and submitted to the government immediately.
