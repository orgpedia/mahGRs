The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune shall be the Controlling Officer for the said schemes. The Controlling Officer shall spend the expenditure from the prescribed purpose within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for submitting the details of expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and utility certificate to the government without fail.
Under the above scheme, the Controller will be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controller of Utilization Certificates of the current financial year as well as the previous year's expenditure. The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune shall be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularities take place by spending the said funds as per the terms and conditions of the Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Finance Department from time to time as per the Financial Rules, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook.
