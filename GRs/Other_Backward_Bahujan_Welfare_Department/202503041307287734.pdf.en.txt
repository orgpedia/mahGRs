The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the social development of the seamen community. The sewing community, which belongs to the Other Backward Class category in the state, is largely engaged in the sewing business.
The youth of the state belonging to the seamen community are covered under various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Education Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development
As a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, to strive for greater upliftment for the overall development of the seamen community by availing the benefits of Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Subvention Scheme), Shri. Accordingly, the Corporation (Subsidiary) was incorporated on the date 10.10.2024. The decision was taken by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The establishment of a subsidiary company, Sant Shiromani Namdeo Maharaj Samastha Shimpi Samaj Economic Development Corporation, "has been approved under the Government Decision dated 15.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.

2. Functions of Shri Sant Shiromani Namdeo Maharaj Samastha Shimpi Samaj Economic Development Corporation: -
1. To work for the welfare and development of the seamen community in the state.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment at low interest rates to the members of the sewing community.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the sewing community.
4. To provide such services as may be deemed necessary for the manufacture, assembly and supply of agricultural products, commodities, materials and inputs to the sewing community.
