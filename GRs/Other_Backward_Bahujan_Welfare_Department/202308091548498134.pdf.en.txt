Introduction:- For the last several years, there has been a demand from the people's representatives and various organizations for the formation of a separate corporation for the social and economic development of the Veerashaiva-Lingayat community.
The government was considering the formation of Jagadjyoti Mahatma Basaveshwara Economic Development Corporation (JMBC) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSBCDC) for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Veerashaiva Lingayat community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Education Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
The Government has approved the establishment of Jagadjyoti Mahatma Basaveshwara Economic Development Corporation for the social and economic development of the Veerashaiva Lingayat community in the State under the existing Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the social and economic upliftment of the Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called Jagadvayoti Mahatma Basaveshwara Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation). The Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation will manage the project. The details of the structure, functions and schemes of Jagadjyoti Mahatma Basaveshwara Economic Development Corporation (JMBEDC) are as follows:
