Government's decision:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme for the Deprived Castes and Nomadic Tribes category, Government Resolution Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. As per the provisions of section 2 to 4, the proposal of the individual household beneficiaries of Jalgaon district, which has been approved by the District Level Committee in a meeting held under the chairmanship of the Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Household Scheme District Level Committee, Jalgaon, is referred to Reference No. The letter was received by the government.
In pursuance of the said proposal, post-facto approval was accorded to 1049 individual house site beneficiaries (as per Annexure-A) of Jalgaon district for the year 2023-24 under Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana and an amount of Rs. 1.20 lakh as against Rs. 12,58,80, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Fifty Eight Lakh Eighty Thousand only) and 4% Administrative Fund (Rs. 4800/- (Rs. 50,35,200/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand Two Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 13,09,15, 200/- (Rupees Thirteen Crore Nine Lakh Fifteen Thousand Two Hundred only) is being approved by the Government. Out of this amount, Rs. 3,28,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Twenty Eight Lakhs only) is being released and the remaining amount will be released as per the availability of funds under the scheme.

3. Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme, the same is being granted to 1049 individual house site beneficiaries in the contiguous Schedule A subject to the following conditions.
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure-A, who have been included among the beneficiaries identified by the Jalgaon District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
(3) The benefit shall be admissible to the person having the caste certificate and shall not be admissible to any other person.
Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a difference in the name of one of the beneficiaries, the name of the parent/ elder or the surname, then to remove that difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
8. The District Level Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the terms and conditions contained in the relevant Government Decision/ Corrigendum.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in the said case will be of the Collector and the Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Jalgaon and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalgaon.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalgaon is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. Further, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalgaon is being authorized to draw the said funds from the treasury. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. Also, the report should be submitted to the government.
