As the space provided by the General Administration Department for the use of the office of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department (Khud) is not enough, room No. 5, 2nd Floor, 195 J. Tata Marg, Churchgate, Mumbai 2000 S.O. The government has decided to rent out the space. The administrative approval has been accorded as on 18.04.2022.
It is mandatory for all the officers/ employees of the Ministries/ Departments to register their daily office attendance on the biometric machine. In order to procure the said biometric machines to record the daily attendance of the officers/ employees of the department who have been shifted to the premises of Motimahal building taken on rent for the office use of this department, tariff sheets were sought from the willing suppliers.
