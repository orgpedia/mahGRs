With regard to the declaration of Drawing and Disbursing and Controlling Officer for disbursement of funds for running hostels for students belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes.
The Government of Maharashtra
Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department
Government Memorandum No. 2023/ PR No. 23/ Scheme-5
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: April 28, 2023.
Read: 1) Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Government Resolution No. 2020/ No. 01/ Scheme-5, Dy. 29.11.2022.
2) Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Government Decision No. Vs-2020/ S.No.01/ Scheme-5, Dy. 28.02.2023 3) Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Govt. Decision No. 2022/ No. 19/ Scheme-5, Dy. 23.02.2023
Preamble: Odas have been issued to meet the recurring and non-recurring expenditure for the establishment and operation of 72 Government Hostels at 36 district places with a capacity of 100 students, 1 for boys and 1 for girls at each district level for students pursuing post-matriculation vocational education belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes in the State.
Accordingly, the matter of declaring Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as Controlling Officer for spending the funds allocated for running these hostels was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's statement:
For students pursuing post-matric vocational education belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes in the state, 72 government hostels with a capacity of 100 students at each district, 1 for boys and 1 for girls, will be allowed to operate by the government itself by renting out the building for the hostel, instead of giving it to NGOs registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 and the Societies Registration Act, 1860. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.02.2023. A provision has been made in the Budget for 2023-24 to meet the recurring and non-recurring expenditure for starting these hostels.
(2) The Government shall take a decision to provide funds for renting out buildings for running the hostels and to provide necessary materials to the students in the hostels as per requirement. New Account Head has been opened on 23.02.2023. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the disbursement of funds made available in accordance with the said item in the title.
