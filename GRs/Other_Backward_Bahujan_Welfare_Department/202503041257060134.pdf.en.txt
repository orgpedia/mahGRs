The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Ladshakhi Bani / Vani community.
The government was contemplating to set up "sixteen Kulaswini Adhuri Vikas Nigam (Subsidiary)" as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSBDC) to provide the benefit of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the overall development of the community. Accordingly, the Corporation (Subsidiary) was incorporated on the date 10.10.2024. The decision was taken in the cabinet meeting.
Pursuant to the said decision in the respective Cabinet meetings, the Government has approved the establishment of a Subsidiary Company "sixteen Kulaswamini Economic Development Corporation" under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation vide the Government Decision dated 15.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government Decision dated 15.10.2024 under reference is being superseded by this Government Decision.
b) The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Ladshakhia Vani / Bani community belonging to Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is functioning at present for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation is named as "sixteen Kulaswamini Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)."
