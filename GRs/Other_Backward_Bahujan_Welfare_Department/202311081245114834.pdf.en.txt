Government Decision: Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme for the categories of Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes, Government Decision Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. As per the provisions of Sections 2 to 4, the proposal for finalization of names of individual household beneficiaries in Gondia district, which was accorded administrative approval in the meeting held on 29.03.2022 under the chairmanship of the Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Open Estate/ Household Scheme District Level Committee, Gondia, has been referred to Reference No. The letter was received by the government.
In pursuance of the said proposal, post-facto approval to 552 individual household beneficiaries (as per Annexure-A) of Gondia district for the year 2023-24 under Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme and Rs. 1.20 lakh as against Rs. 6,62,40, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores Sixty Two Lakhs Forty Thousand only) and 4% of Administrative Fund (Rs. 4800/- (Rs. 26,49,600/- (Rupees Twenty-six Lakhs Twenty-nine Thousand Six Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 6,88,89, 600/- (Rupees Six Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Six Hundred only) is being approved by the Government. Out of this amount, Rs. 2,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores only) is being released and the remaining amount will be paid as per the availability of funds under the scheme.

3. Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Estate Scheme, 552 individual house site beneficiaries in Annexure-A are being given such approval subject to the following conditions.
1. out of the beneficiaries qualified and finalized by the Gondia District Level Committee.
The benefit of this scheme will be available only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure-A.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
(3) The benefit shall be admissible to the person having the caste certificate and shall not be admissible to any other person.
Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a difference in the name of one of the beneficiaries, the name of the parent/ elder or the surname, then to remove that difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
8. The District Level Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the terms and conditions contained in the relevant Government Decision/ Corrigendum.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case will be of the District Collector and the Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Gondia and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Gondia.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Gondia is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
District Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme, Gondia and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Gondia shall not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions. No additional funds will be provided for this purpose.
