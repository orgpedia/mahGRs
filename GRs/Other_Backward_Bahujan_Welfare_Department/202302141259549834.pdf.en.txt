Preamble: The decision to implement the scheme for construction of houses for the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic Tribe-C category has been taken as per the decision of the Government in the context. Under this scheme, a total of 105 individual beneficiaries have been approved by the District Level Committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector and Chairman, Dhangar Samaj Gharkul Yojana District Level Committee, Jalna for the financial year 2021-22. 3 Submitted to the Government under the letter hereunder, the matter of approval of the Government to approve the proposal and to disburse the permissible funds for the purpose was under consideration.
Government Decision: Under the scheme of construction of houses for Dhangar community, a committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector and Chairman, Dhangar Samaj Gharkul Yojana District Level Committee, Jalna has given administrative approval and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara has issued reference No. 3 The proposal of total 105 individual beneficiaries of Dhangar community submitted to the Government vide letter here (List of beneficiaries is annexed as Annexure-A). ) The approval of the government is being given subject to the terms and conditions of the relevant government decision. Also, a total of Rs. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,26,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Six Lakh only).
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being declared as the' Controlling Officer' for the disbursement of the said funds and he shall disburse the said funds to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the office of the Director, State Management Cell-Rural Housing Cell, CIDCO Bhawan, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS). The amount shall be credited directly to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of the beneficiary mentioned in Annexure' A' attached with the said Government decision, in a phased manner as per the stage of construction through Direct Beneficiary Transfer (DBT) mode through Public Financial Management System (PFMS).
