Introduction: Many children from poor families belonging to the Other Backward Classes category have to help their parents in their traditional occupation. These children cannot go to school. Therefore, with a view to promote education among the children of such Other Backward Classes poor families and to reduce the rate of leakage in education and to provide financial assistance for education, the Government of India has started the Government of India Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme from the year 1998-99.
The scheme (Central share 50% and State share 50%) has been revised with effect from 2017-18, as per the Government decision at Reference No. Under this scheme, the Central Government has provided financial assistance of Rs. 921.00 lakhs in the financial year 2017-18 and Rs. 1217.92 lakh. 2138.92 lakh was received.
