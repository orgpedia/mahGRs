The details of the vacancies as per the roll will be filled by the concerned educational institution in a computer system called "Pavitra." Accordingly, the educational institution concerned shall submit a proposal for advertisement clearance to the Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare Department concerned before the recruitment. The schedule of action to be taken by the educational institutions concerned as well as the competent authorities will be decided from time to time. It will be necessary to check the point list according to the prevailing procedure.
2.3 A separate login (Username, Password) will be made available to each educational institution, the respective Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department as well as the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, M.R., Pune. The Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare Department shall ensure that the information in the advertisement is correct within seven working days of receipt of the login details and agree if the information is correct. If the information is incomplete or inaccurate, they will deny it with a reason.
2.4 If the Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare does not act within the prescribed time limit of seven office days, the status thereof shall be displayed on the login page of the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, M.R., Pune. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, M.R., Pune will inform the Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare concerned within three working days of receiving the information on the login.
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune, will act as the point of reference in the confidential report of the respective Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, if they do not decide on the advertisement permit proposal within the prescribed time frame.
2.5 After approval by the Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, the advertisement on the War portal will appear on Wad Sigel and Login of the organization as per the demand of the organization. Also, the representative of the organization will receive an SSMS (message) regarding the same. The ad will appear on the https://mahateacherrecruitment.org.in website.
2.6 Interested and unqualified candidates shall apply within the prescribed period on the holy system with their qualifying marks in the aptitude and IQ test from the date of publication of the advertisement. Candidates may be given preference according to merit for all the vacancies available for the vacancies in the private educational institutions of Vijabhabhaj Ashramshalas.
A list of candidates in the order of 1: 23 for interview will be made available to the managers through the holy portal for each vacant post of teacher in the privately funded Vijabhabhaj Ashramshala as per the Government Notification, Department of School Education and CRODA, dated 12/2/2025.

The concerned private management will interview the shortlisted candidates available for interview on their login and check their teaching skills based on which the candidate with the highest marks will be selected for the vacancy. There will be a total of 30 marks for interview and teaching skills. The respective educational institutions will declare the marksheet B results of the candidates on the sacred system through interviews and teaching fees. They will also communicate their results in person to the candidate who attended the interview.
The candidate ranked first according to the scorecard will be appointed by the respective educational institution within 7 working days. If the selected candidate does not appear within 7 working days, the institution will appoint the next candidate in order of merit.
The educational institutions concerned shall verify the original documents of the candidates concerned during the interview of the shortlisted candidates. Duration of Appointment of Education Servants: - The period of probation of the designated Education Servants in the Private Aided Primary / Secondary / Higher Secondary / Sugarcane Harvesting Children's Residential Ashramshalas and Vidyaniketan Vidya Bhaj Ashramshalas of the Visayas category shall ordinarily be three years as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 27.02.2003 and dated 13.10.2000 in respect of the Department of School Education & Training.
4. Medium of appointment: - Appointment to the post shall be made only for the medium for which the candidate has applied, in accordance with the educational and professional qualification required for the post of Shiksha Sevak.
5. Age Limit of Education Servant: - The minimum age limit for the post of Education Servant shall be 18 years and the maximum age limit shall be 38 years for candidates belonging to open category and 43 years for candidates belonging to backward class. Parallel reservation and age limit prescribed by the government will be applicable to the differently abled candidates. The age limit as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time in respect of age limit shall apply to Education Servants.
