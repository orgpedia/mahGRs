The Proposal: -
To implement special programmes for the development of "Goan" community belonging to Special Backward Classes on the lines of ongoing schemes for Scheduled Tribes by the Tribal Development Department. The decision has been taken in the cabinet meeting dated 29.04.2025.
Accordingly, approximately 6000 students belonging to the "Gowari" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes are provided education from Class I to Class XII in reputed residential schools of English medium every year through Reference No. 2 hereby approved by the Government Decision dated 14.05.2025.
Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune has issued a notification in this regard vide Notification No. 4 In the letter dated 06.03.2026 mentioned herein, a proposal has been submitted to the Government for approval of admission of students belonging to MAR community to Shri Balashree Multipurpose Education Institute, Nagpur-run Rai English School AVS Junior College, Gadge Nagar, Hingna Road, Nagpur by selecting the school under the said scheme with the prescribed WATS (Total Marks obtained by the school for the year 2026-2027 85.50). Accordingly, Sri Balashree Institute of Multi-Purpose Education, Nagpur-run Rai English School and Junior College, Gadge Nagar, Hingna Road, Nagpur was selected for admission of Goan community students to a reputed English medium residential school in the city.
