Preamble: - There has been a demand for the formation of a separate corporation for the social and economic development of the carpenter community for the past many years by the public representatives, various organizations.
The carpenter community and its various tribes are mainly economically and socially backward. The youth of the carpenter community residing in all the districts of the state are provided with various schemes (seed capital schemes, etc.) on the lines of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation and Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme) Benefits
The government was considering setting up the Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the community.
Government Decision-: In the above background, the Government has decided as follows: 1. The Government is approving the establishment of Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Sutar community on the lines of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation and Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the socio-economic upliftment of all the tribes belonging to the Sutar community in the State.
The headquarters of the corporation shall be at Mumbai and district offices shall be functioning in all the districts of the state.

4) The functions of the Carpenter Society Economic Development Corporation shall be as follows: - 1. To work for the welfare and development of the carpenter community in the State.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment at low interest rates to persons belonging to the carpenter community.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the carpenter community.
4. Provide services as may be necessary for the manufacture, assembly and supply of materials and materials necessary for the carpenter community.
5. To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of the carpenter community in the State and to prepare reports for the scheme.
