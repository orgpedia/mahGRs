The Proposal: -
There has been a demand for the formation of a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Teli community for the past many years from the people's representatives, various organizations. The Teli community, which is among the Other Backward Classes in the state, is mainly into the business of salt, lime and coal making. The government was considering setting up the Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (SSSDC) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Teli community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) to the youth of Teli community residing in all the districts of the state.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Teli community belonging to Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)."
The subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The details of the composition, functions, and schemes of the Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
