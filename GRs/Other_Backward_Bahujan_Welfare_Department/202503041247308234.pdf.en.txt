The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there was a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the carpenter community. The carpenter community and its various tribes are mainly economically and socially backward. The Government decision dated w. 2 had approved the formation of a separate corporation, "Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation," to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Sutar community by providing benefits of various schemes under Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation and Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to the youth of the Sutar community residing in all the districts of the state. Also, Reference No. The date of the 2
The name of the corporation was changed to "Sant Bhojling Kaka Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation" by the certificate of 01.10.2024.
The carpenter community falls under the Other Backward Class category. The Sant Kashiba Gurav Youth Economic Development Corporation, ATA Mahatma Basaveshwara Economic Development Corporation and Sant Senaji Maharaj Keshashilpi Corporation, respectively, are subsidiaries established under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the Gurav, Veerashaiva-Lingayat and Nucleic Social Components, which were earlier included in the Other Backward Class category. On the same lines, it will be easier to set up the Sant Bhojling Kaka Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation as a subsidiary of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. Accordingly, a subsidiary company "Sant Bhojlig Kaka Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation" was under consideration under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.

Functions of Sant Bhojling Kaka Sutar Samaj Economic Development Corporation: -
1. To work for the welfare and development of the carpenter community in the State.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment at low interest rates to persons belonging to the carpenter community.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the carpenter community.
