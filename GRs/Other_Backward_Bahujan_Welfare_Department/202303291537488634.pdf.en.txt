Introduction For the development of students, youth and other candidates belonging to economically weaker sections of the open category by implementing various activities, training, research, programs, schemes, etc. and through other means, Reference No. 1 is mentioned here. The Maharashtra Research, Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) has been set up under the Government Resolution dated 22.08.2019. It is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the Public Trust Act, 1950 (Bombay Act 29 of 1950). Thereafter, the headquarters of the organization was shifted to Pune. 3 mentioned here. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 12.8.2022. Similarly, for the creation of a total of 20 posts for AMRUT, 3 regular and 17 external services, reference No. 4 The above has been approved under the Government Decision dated 30.08.2022.
At present, an officer of the cadre of Additional Collector has been appointed as the Managing Director of AMRUT.
A resolution has been passed in the meeting of the Board of Governors of AMRUT dated 27th February, 2023 to declare the Managing Director (AMRUT) as" Head of the Department" under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division I, Roll No. 1, for determining and effectively implementing various schemes, training, research, programs etc. across Maharashtra.
Accordingly, the government was considering declaring the Managing Director of Maharashtra Research, Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) as" Head of Department" and giving him all administrative and financial powers as Head of Department.

As per the decision taken by the Board of Governors of AMRUT in its meeting dated 27.02.2023, the Managing Director of Maharashtra Research, Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) is being declared as" Head of Department" under Financial Powers Rule Book 1978, Part I, Sub-Division I, No. 1, Rule No. 2.
As per the provisions of the Financial Rights Manual, such rights shall be limited to the extent of financial powers.
