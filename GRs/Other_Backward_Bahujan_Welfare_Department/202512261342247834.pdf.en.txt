Introduction: - In the context of the above. The Government of India has approved the establishment of Vasantrao Naik Research and Training Institute (VANARTY), an autonomous institute under Rule 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, at Mumbai, to focus on the socio-economic and educational development of Vimukt Jati-A, Vimukt Jati-B, Vimukt Jati-C and Vimukt Jati-D, including the Gor Banjara tribe, and to carry out various programmes / activities for students, youth and other candidates belonging to these communities. Ministers (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department) have been provided.
As per the provision in the Government Decision dated 08.10.2024, the matter of appointment of "First Director and Member as representative of the Government to form a One Party" for registration of Vasantrao Naik Research and Training Institute (One Party) under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - The following office bearers / officers are being appointed by the Government as "First Directors" for registration of Vasantrao Naik Research and Training Institute (VANARTI) under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013.
