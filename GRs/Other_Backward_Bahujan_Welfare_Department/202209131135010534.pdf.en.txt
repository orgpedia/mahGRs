Read- (1) Government Decision, EMOW, SAVASHEIMAPRA, VIJAYABHAJ, VISAMAPRA Welfare Department, No. Gruneo-2019/ S.No. 02/ Vijabhaj-1, Dy. 6/9/2019 (2) Letter from Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara No. Sasakambh/ Dhasaghyo/ Personal Home/ Posthumous Approval/ 2021-22/503/ Bhandara, Dy. 14/2/2022 Preamble: The decision to implement the scheme for construction of houses for the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic ST-C category has been taken as per the decision of the Government in the context. Under this scheme, the proposal of a total of 4 individual beneficiaries approved by the District Level Committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Bhandara was approved by the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara in reference No. 2 As per the letter submitted to the government, the matter of approving the proposal and allocating necessary funds for it was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the scheme of construction of houses for the Dhangar community, the committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Bhandara, and approved by the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara, issued reference No. 2 A total of 04 individual beneficiary proposals submitted to the Government vide letter No.2 (List of beneficiaries is annexed as Annexure-A). The approval of the government is being given subject to the conditions in the relevant government decision. Also, funds to the tune of Rs. 4,80,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh Eighty Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of the said funds and he shall disburse the said funds through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the office of the Director, State Management Cell-Rural Housing Cell, CIDCO Bhawan, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai. Direct Beneficiary Transfer (DBT) through Public Financial Management System (PFMS) should be credited directly to the Aadhaar linked bank account of the beneficiary mentioned in Annexure' A' attached with the said Government decision in a phased manner as per the stage of construction.
