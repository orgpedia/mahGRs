(9) If the number of applications received for admission under the scheme is more than the number of schools selected in a project area and the number of students approved therein, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district shall announce the selection list by selecting the students through a draw of lots. (10) The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district along with the Institute Director/ Headmaster of the nominated schools selected under the scheme shall hold a meeting in the first and second semester of the academic year to review the academic progress of the admitted students and the facilities provided to them.
(11) After the selection of the school under the nominated scheme, the Assistant Commissioner of the concerned district, Social Welfare, should visit the nominated schools in person twice in the first semester and twice in the second semester and submit a report to the government through the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune, regarding the friendly atmosphere in the school, the facilities provided to the students, the medical treatment, educational quality, nutrition, etc.
