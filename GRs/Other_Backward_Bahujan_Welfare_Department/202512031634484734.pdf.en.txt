The Proposal: -
It was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of 3 new heads of accounts for implementation of a total of 3 schemes namely "(i) Quality Scholarship for students of Class 5th to 10th studying in Secondary School / Insurance category (ii) Savitribai Phule Scholarship for girls studying in Class 5th to 7th studying in Visa and Insurance category and (iii) Payment of Tuition Fee and Examination Fee for Visa and Insurance students studying in Class 1st to 10th" by the Other Backward Classes Bahujan Kalyan Department.
Government Decision: - In order to implement "(1) Quality Scholarships for students studying in Secondary School / Insurance Category E. 5th to E. 10th" (2) Savitribai Phule Scholarship for girls studying in Education and Insurance Category E. 5th to 7th and (3) Provision of Tuition Fee and Examination Fee for Students studying in Education and Insurance Category E. 1st to 10th "by the Other Backward Classes Bahujan Kalyan Department, the following 3 Schemes are being approved to be opened: -
