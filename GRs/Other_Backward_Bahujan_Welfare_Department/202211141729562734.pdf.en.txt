the prelude
The General Administration Department has been instructed to issue separate orders to the administrative department regarding the level to which the cases related to the appointment/ posting of government employees in their direct service, promotion or deputation should be submitted and at which level the final decision should be taken. Accordingly, the matter of declaring the appointing authority for the appointment/ posting of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees in the approved format for the field office of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department by direct service, promotion or deputation was under consideration.
Government's decision:
""" The"""" Appointment Authority"""" is being declared to take final decision in the matter of appointment/ posting of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) as well as Group-C and Group-D employees under the administrative department of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare by direct service, promotion or deputation as follows:"""
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Positions                          | the appointing authority                                                                                  |
| 1    | Group B, Group C and Group D posts | the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department |
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
