Introduction: Under the Scholarship Scheme for Foreign Higher Education to the children of Visva-Bhaj and Bima categories for the session 2023-24. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 11.10.2018, a Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Govt. In the meeting held on 21.08.2023, 50 eligible students have been selected as per the branch/ course wise marks. The final selection has been made as per the Government decision dated 30.08.2023.
A total of 34 students, out of the total 50 shortlisted under the Abroad Scholarship Scheme for the year 2023-24, 32 students and 2 students for the year 2022-23, will be given financial assistance of Rs. The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune has requested the Government to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 12,88,10, 200/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Ten Thousand Two Hundred only). Accordingly, the matter of allocating funds to these 34 students under the foreign scholarship was under the consideration of the government as per the terms and conditions of the government decision.
