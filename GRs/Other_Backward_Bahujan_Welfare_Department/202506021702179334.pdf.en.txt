The Proposal: -
Scheme-wise funds have been budgeted for payment of post-matric scholarships and tuition fees and examination fees to the students belonging to Vimukt Caste, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes during this financial year. Out of the above funds, the Government was contemplating to disburse the funds made available by the Finance Department on the BEAMS system to the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Pune.
Government Decision: - The approved budgetary provisions for payment of scholarships and tuition fees and examination fees to the students belonging to the Denotified Castes, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes in the financial years 2025-26. The Government is approving the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 600.00 crore (Rs.600 crore only) to the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Pune.

For the scholarship scheme being implemented by the Department, the Controller shall submit to the Government the utilization certificates of the expenditure for the previous two financial years and the Controller shall be responsible for the utilization of the funds for the current years subject to verification of the utilization certificates. While spending these funds, the Department of Finance has issued Government Decisions / Circulars and Fiscal Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Section 1 to 5, Dt. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularities occur by complying with the terms and conditions in the 17.04.2015.
4. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune shall be responsible for the release of funds for the purpose for which the funds have been disbursed. Also, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for disbursing the funds only after ensuring that the procedure laid down in the case is followed.
5. The said Government Decision is based on the Government Circular No. Finance-2025 / Q. 44 / Budget-3, Dept. Issuance is being made in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Circular dated 07.04.2025 and the consent given by the Finance Department to the disbursement of funds.
