The terms and conditions to be followed while giving admission to the nominated school are as follows: 1) The concerned institution/ school will be required to fulfill the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 4/9/2019.
2) The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Parbhani shall inspect the concerned school in accordance with the Nominated Residential School Scheme. Also, all students enrolled in the school should be enrolled by verifying their Aadhaar card. After that, the government will take a decision after verifying the number of beneficiary students present in person and the number of direct resident students. The detailed report as per the terms and conditions of 04/09/2019 should be submitted to the Government through the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune for disbursement of funds with the approval of Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare.
(3) The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare concerned shall ensure that the institution is ready to provide all the facilities mentioned in the proposal submitted to the Government through the concerned institution/ school regarding the student approval under the scheme and shall enter into an agreement with the institution.
4) The institution/ school is not expected to start a separate batch/ class for the students of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category, it will be mandatory to educate the students admitted under the scheme along with other students in the regular batch/ class running in the school. If it is found that a separate batch/ class has been started for the students, action will be taken to register a case against the said institution and the recognition of the school will be cancelled immediately.
5) It is mandatory to appoint a female superintendent and caretaker in the girls' hostel admitted in the institution. If the school fails to do so, its recognition will be cancelled.
6) Under this scheme, the Assistant Commissioner of Social Welfare of the concerned district should take care of admitting students of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category in the schools approved by the government and within the limit of the number of students approved for the school.
As per the original provision of the scheme, the benefit of the scheme should be given only to those children whose annual family income is within the maximum limit of Rs. 1.00 lakh.
