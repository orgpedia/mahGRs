About the launch of the "Nataraja Award" and "Vishwakarma Award" schemes.
Government of Maharashtra
Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare Department
Government Decision Number: Future-2023 / Q. 24 / Plan-3
Martyr Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400032.
Date: February 13, 2025
Read: - Statement by Bhatke Vimukt Vikas Parishad, Maharashtra Province, dated 15/10/2022
Introduction: - Traditional folk artists and traditional artisans have a lion's share in the cultural fabric of the state of Maharashtra. The movement for the formation of the state of Maharashtra was shaped by traditional folk artists and traditional artisans through their art. Hence, it was under the consideration of the government to honour traditional folk artists in the category of Vimukt Jati B Bhatke Tribe with the "Nataraja Award" and traditional artisans with the "Vishwakarma Award."
Government Decision: - Ma. As per the decision taken by the Cabinet in its meetings dated 10/10/2024, the Government is approving the scheme of conferring "Nataraja Award" on traditional folk artists and "Vishwakarma Award" on traditional artisans belonging to Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes of the State.
The rules regarding the selection, criteria, qualifications, rules, etc. of the award are being prescribed as mentioned in Annexure A, B, HAS.
