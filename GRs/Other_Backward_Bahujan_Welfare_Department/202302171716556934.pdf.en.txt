Read the above mentioned Government Circular, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance No. CBC 2012/ Q.No.182/ Vijabhaj-1, D. Paragraph 2 (iii) of 25th March, 2013 reads as under:
" (iii) The last date of receipt/ submission of application for a post or a particular crucial date fixed for that post shall be taken as the date to verify that the candidate concerned does not fall in the category of advanced and non-advanced person/ group. "
The said paragraph 2 (ii) is being amended and the said paragraph 2 (ii) is to be read as under:
" (ii) To verify that the candidate concerned does not fall in the category of advanced and non-advanced person/ group, the non-criminal certificate of the candidate for that financial year should be taken into account. "
