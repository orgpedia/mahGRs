Introduction: On the lines of BARTI, various activities, programs, etc. have been implemented for the students, youth and other candidates from the economically weaker sections of the open category and to develop them through other means. The Maharashtra Academy of Research, Upliftment and Training (AMRUT) has been established. A total of 20 posts, including 3 regular and 17 external services, approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries for AMRUT were under consideration of the government. Hence, the proposal.
It was submitted to the Cabinet.
Government Decision As per the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, a total of 20 posts of 3 regular+ 17 external system services for the Maharashtra Maharashtra Research, Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) have been created. The Cabinet has approved the creation of the posts mentioned in the table below to operationalize the Maharashtra Research, Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
