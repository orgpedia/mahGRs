Government Decision: Govt. As per the notification dated 09.03.2017, a new Department namely Other Backward Bahujan Welfare was created out of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The revised figure of 821 posts on the establishment of the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate for the field offices of the newly created department was approved by the High Level Committee of the Government in Reference No. 3 decisions have been approved. As it will take some time to prepare the service recruitment rules in accordance with the recruitment to the above approved cadre-wise sanctioned posts in the department's field office, and then to advertise for the recruitment through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and the selection committee in the department, the department is in the process of inviting applications for the posts in the equivalent pay scale during the regular recruitment period.
Guidelines have been issued regarding the appointment of state government employees on deputation under Government Decision, General Administration Department, Reference No. 1 and 2.
Subject to the above instructions, approval is being accorded for the appointment of Mr. Abhijit Vijayrao Ghode, Top Clerk (Group-C), Collectorate, Mumbai City on deputation to the vacant post of Inspector in the office of the Regional Deputy Director, Amravati under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department subject to the following conditions:

The said deputation of Mr. Abhijit Vijayrao Ghode, Top Clerk, shall be for a period of three years initially from the date on which he enters upon his office.
Thereafter, the period of the said deputation may be extended as per the concurrence of the Revenue and Forest Departments, as may be required.
(2) If the services of Mr. A.V. Ghode, the Head Clerk, are deemed necessary by the parent authority in the interest of public service, the Government shall have the power to return them to their parent office at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
(3) The office of the Deputy Commissioner shall be at liberty to return the services of Mr. A.V. Ghode, Head Clerk, to the parent department, if the office of the Deputy Commissioner does not require them.
4) Mr. A.V. Ghode, Top Clerk will be allowed to return to his parent department if he gives at least 3 months prior notice in writing to the competent authority/ Government that he intends to return to his parent department.
(5) The deputation allowance shall not be admissible to Mr. A.V. Ghode, Head Clerk during the period of the above deputation.
Informal Reference to Finance Department (No. 343/ Service-9, Dt. As per the feedback received as on 04.09.2023, the pay structure of one post of Inspector during the period of deputation of Mr. A.V. Ghode, Top Clerk to the field office of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department is being reduced from S13, (₹ 35400-₹ 112,400) to S12, (₹ 32000-₹ 101,600). On the expiry of the period of deputation of Mr. A. V. Ghode, Head Clerk, the pay structure of the said post will automatically be restored as S.13. The rules of service in respect of service matters such as leave, group insurance scheme, medical exemption, leave travel exemption, etc. admissible to Mr. A. V. Ghode, Top Clerk during the period of deputation shall be applicable to him in his parent department.
The terms/ conditions and related administrative matters regarding the deputation will be decided in consultation with the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department and the Revenue and Forest Departments.
3. Mr. A. V. Ghode, the top clerk, to be relieved of his duties immediately by the Collector, Mumbai City, to join the post of Deputy Commissioner as mentioned above. Mr. A. V. Ghode, the top clerk, on receipt of the above orders of retirement, should immediately join the office of the Deputy Commissioner.
