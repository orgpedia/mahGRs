Introduction: The Vadar community, which is included in the category of Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes in the state, has been economically and socially deprived. The youth of the Vadar community of the state, who live in most of the districts of the state, have been trained under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation to take advantage of various schemes (seed capital loan scheme, direct loan scheme, personal loan interest repayment scheme, group loan interest repayment scheme) implemented under the state government for the social and economic upliftment of the Vadar community. The issue of setting up Maruti Chavan-Vadar Economic Development Corporation is proposed at the government level. To strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Vadar community in the state, Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation has been incorporated as a subsidiary company. The government was considering setting up a subsidiary of Maruti Chavan-Vadar Economic Development Corporation.
Government Decision:- Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, which is currently working for the social and economic upliftment of the Vadar community among the Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes in the state. The government has approved the setting up of Maruti Chavan-Vadar Economic Development Corporation (MSCDC). The company,' Pailwan Ka. It will be called Maruti Chavan-Vadar Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation). The Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation will manage the activities of the subsidiary.
