Preamble: - In pursuance of the Government decisions under reference, a scheme "To provide education to the students of Dhangar community belonging to Nomadic Tribes-C category in reputed residential schools of English medium" is being implemented.
At present, this scheme is being called * Dhangar Named School Scheme. "
King Yashwantrao Holkar did a lot of work for educational advancement in his career. He promoted inclusive and multi-lingual education. It promoted knowledge dissemination by providing financial assistance to traditional educational institutions such as Gurukul. Military education incorporated discipline, ethics, and leadership qualities. It was under the consideration of the government to name the scheme as "Raja Yashwantrao Holkar English Medium Residential Education Scheme" keeping in mind the fact that it should be the MT of the work of Shuru, Kusala, statesman, Prajahit Daksha Raja, who did not restrict education to the upper classes but opened it to all and started education for girls in his palace, thus giving impetus to the students studying under this scheme.
The Government's Decisions:
The scheme is being approved by the government to be named "Raje Yashwantrao Holkar English Medium Residential Education Scheme" to "provide education to students of Dhangar community belonging to nomadic tribe-C category in reputed residential schools of English medium."
