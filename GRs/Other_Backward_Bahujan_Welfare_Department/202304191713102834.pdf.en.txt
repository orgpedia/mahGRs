The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 (Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2023), as specified in column 4 of the Schedule, has been approved by the Legislature. The Governor has given his assent to it and it shall come into force as the Maharashtra Act No. 17 of 2023 by the Government of Maharashtra, Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, D. It will be released on March 29, 2023.
Subject to the terms and conditions of the circular dated 12th April, 2023 of the Department of Finance, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programmed expenditure required for the first nine months of 2023-24 (ending December, 2023) will be disbursed on BEAMS within the limit of 70% of the total annual provision.
Funding will be disbursed at the rate of 20% for the first quarter April-June 2023, 20% for the quarter July-September 2023 and 30% for the quarter October-December 2023. However, the funds available on the Budget Distribution System are required to be released by the Department of Finance before they are drawn from the Treasury. The guidelines in paragraphs 4 to 18 of the Government Circular dated 12.04.2023 and the guidelines in the Annexure shall be strictly followed.
