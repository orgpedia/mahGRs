The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Teli community. The Teli community, which is included in the Other Backward Classes of the state, is mainly engaged in the business of oilseeds-oil production and sale, grocery business, oilseed production and farming as well as small-scale trade, bazaars, and weekly market sales of oil and groceries. The government was considering setting up the Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (SSSDC) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Teli community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) to the youth of Teli community residing in all the districts of the state under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the setting up of Additional Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of Teli community belonging to Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)."
The subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The details of the composition, functions, and schemes of the Sant Shri Santaji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
