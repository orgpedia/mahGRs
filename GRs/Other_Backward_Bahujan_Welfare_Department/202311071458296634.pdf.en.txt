Government's decision:
Under Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, for providing necessary infrastructure facilities to the Tanda/ Habitat of Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes in Pune District, vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No.1, Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for 11 works for an amount of Rs. 71.00 lakhs and the funds for the said works will be disbursed as per the availability and suitability of the works. However, as per the Government letter in Reference No. 2 and 3, the works taken up under Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme but not tendered and not started despite the work order have been stayed. Now, the 11 works which were given administrative sanction under the Government decision dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No. 1 have been removed and the entire amount of Rs. 71.00 lakhs (Rupees Seventy One Lakh only) is hereby approved by the Government.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds.
3. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, before disbursing the aforesaid funds, vide Reference No. 1 It should be ensured that the terms and conditions of the government decision here are fulfilled.
