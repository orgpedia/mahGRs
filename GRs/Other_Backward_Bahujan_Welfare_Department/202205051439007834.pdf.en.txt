Reference:- Government Resolution, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, No. Asthapra-2017/ No. 08/ Establishment-2, dated 14/01/2021.
Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 14/1/2021 in the context of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, the revised pattern of posts on establishment of Regional Offices of the Department has been approved. Annexure- 4 (E) to the said Government Resolution- Revised pattern of sanctioned posts for the office of the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission as given below" Note: The post of Member Secretary in Table No. 1 above is of the cadre of Deputy Secretary or equivalent pay scale.
The officer should be recruited on deputation." However, clause (d) of sub-section (2) of section 3 of the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission Act, 2005, in section 2, provides that" an officer not below the rank of Joint Director in the Department of Social Justice who is or was an officer of the State Government shall be nominated to the post of Member Secretary."
The process of obtaining the approval of the General Administration Department for the bill of the Service Entry Rules for the recruitment of the post according to the revised format of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department is in progress, after which the process of regular recruitment is proposed to be carried out. Therefore, it is necessary to amend the provision of clause (d) of sub-section (2) of section 3 of section 2 of the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission Act, 2005 to provide for the appointment of a Joint Director or equivalent officer in the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department to the post of" Member Secretary" instead of an officer in the rank of Joint Director in the Social Justice Department.
In view of the above, the matter of excluding the post of Member Secretary in Note No. 1 of the above Table by deputation of a Deputy Secretary or an officer of the cadre of equivalent pay was under consideration of the Government under Annexure- 4 (e) below" Revised pattern of sanctioned posts for the office of the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission."
