Preamble:- On the lines of Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation, under the decision of the Government in the context, the equivalent amount of Rs. 10.00 lakh to a maximum of Rs. 50.00 lakh group loan interest subvention scheme is implemented by both Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Limited) and Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited). In the government decision on the group loan interest reimbursement scheme being implemented by the Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation, there is no mention of the District Beneficiary Selection Committee. However, for the group loan interest reimbursement schemes being implemented by Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation and Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, a District Beneficiary Selection Committee headed by the Collector has been included under the group loan interest reimbursement scheme as per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra. Therefore, both the Corporations have been receiving demands from social organizations to relax the condition of District Beneficiary Selection Committee in the Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme being implemented by both the Corporations. The resolution regarding the relaxation of the condition of the District Beneficiary Selection Committee in the Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme was passed at the 95th Board Meeting of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation and the 122nd Board Meeting of the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation respectively. Board of Directors approved.
Accordingly, the matter of relaxing the condition of the District Beneficiary Selection Committee in the Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Under the relevant Government Decision, the equivalent amount on the lines of Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation will be Rs. 10.00 lakh to a maximum of Rs. 50.00 lakh Group Loan Interest Subvention Scheme has been approved to be implemented by both the Corporations viz. Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Limited) and Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited). In the present situation, condition number 11 in the relevant government decision of the group loan interest reimbursement scheme being implemented by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation and the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation.
