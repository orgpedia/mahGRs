The Proposal:
Setting up a separate corporation for the social and economic development of the Ramoshi community
The demand has been made by various organizations and people's representatives. The Ramoshi community, which is among the Vimukta Jati and nomadic tribes of the state, is mainly economically and socially backward. The youth of the Ramoshi community living in all the districts of the state should get the benefit of various schemes (seed capital scheme, direct loan scheme, personal loan interest repayment scheme, group loan interest repayment scheme) implemented under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Ramoshi community.
The government was considering the formation of Raje Umaji Naik Economic Development Corporation (subsidiary) as a subsidiary under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation.
The government has decided to set up Raje Umaji Naik Economic Development Corporation (subsidiary) for the social and economic development of the Ramoshi community in the state under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, which is currently working for the social and economic upliftment of the Ramoshi community among the Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes in the state. The corporation will be called" Raje Umaji Naik Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation)." The Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation will manage the activities of the subsidiary.
The details of the composition, functions and schemes of the Raje Umaji Naik Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary) are as follows:

Functions of Raje Umaji Naik Economic Development Corporation: 1. To work for the welfare and development of Ramoshi community in the State.
(ii) To provide and recover loans for self-employment to the members of the Ramoshi community at low interest rates.
To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the Ramoshi community.
To provide such services as may be deemed necessary for the manufacture, assembly and supply of agricultural products, articles, materials and materials to the Ramoshi community.
To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of the Ramoshi community in the State and to prepare reports for the schemes.
