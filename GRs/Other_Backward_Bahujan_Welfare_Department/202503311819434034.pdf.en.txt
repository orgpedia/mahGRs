The Proposal: -
The Vimukt Jati nomadic tribe category in the state of Maharashtra has many castes, tribes and still exists.
They are living a migratory life by travelling. There are Tands / hamlets of many groups such as Laman, Banjara, Vanjari, Dhangar, Pardhi, in which people of this caste / tribe have lived for many years.
However, such hamlets or hamlets do not have access to basic facilities and, although they tend to be modern, the majority of this category of society lives in poverty. Come to Tande, Wadi or Basti now.
They need to be provided with some essential infrastructure so that the community can lead a stable life in the same place where the community of the class lives. Accordingly, for the implementation of the Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, read here No. Detailed under the Government Decision of 1.
Notifications have been issued. Accordingly, the District Level Committee under the Chairmanship of District Collector and Chairman, Vasantrao Naik Tanda / District Level Committee of Habitat Improvement Scheme approved and recommended the same.
Proposals for works in the state have been received by the government. Administrative approval and disbursement of funds for the proposed works was under the consideration of the Government.
