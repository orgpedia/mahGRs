The Proposal:
A proposal to open a new head of account under head of account 1601 for the funds received from the Central Government for the scheme of Pre-Matric Scholarship for Other Backward Classes and DNT category students of the Central Government. Approval of the Accountant General's office has been taken and computer data has been taken for the scheme. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to open a new account head for the Central Government's Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Other Backward Classes and DNT category students under the head Account Head MH 1601. Details of the new title are as follows:
