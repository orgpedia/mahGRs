The Proposal:
As per the guidelines of the Central Government in 1998, scholarships are sanctioned to students belonging to Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes. Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Government of India Scholarships, the Central Government has sanctioned a total of 605 courses as per the group of courses prescribed vide letter No. 1 has been approved. It has also been decided that the government or the government-appointed committee will have the power to increase or change the curriculum as the case may be.
Reference No. 2. A Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune to determine the employment oriented/ similar courses for scholarship/ free ship under 02.11.2020.
Reference No. 3 has been sent to the minutes of the meeting of the Committee held on 25.07.2023 and the Director has recommended approval for 67 courses. Out of these 67 courses, 17 courses have already been mapped on MahaDBT system and the remaining 50 courses were under the consideration of the government for mapping on MahaDBT system.
50 courses recommended by the Director of Government Decisions, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune vide Reference No. 3 have been implemented under Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department
