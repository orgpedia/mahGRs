Accordingly, the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation was granted 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under 2225 E 848 for the financial year 2025-26 AT with an account head of Rs. 47,67,250 - The Government is approving the disbursement of the grant-in-aid to the extent of (Rupees Forty-seven Lakh Sixty-Six Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Only) subject to the following conditions: -
1) Restrictions set by the government for expenditure in funds under the schemes should be strictly enforced. Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2) To streamline operations with a view to limiting the expenditure on management.
3) Submit to the government a detailed report of the amounts raised on various matters and the expenditure incurred thereon.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5) The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery ratio.
6) The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7) While spending the aforesaid funds, the concerned authorities shall utilise the funds in compliance with general financial rules, scheme terms and conditions, CVC guidelines, CAG observations, etc.
8) Reference No. 1 Every condition of the circular mentioned herein shall be fulfilled by the Corporation.
9) As per the circular, the salary subsidy will be mandatory to be provided by ECS in the officer / employee's back account. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy amount be disbursed collectively in a web offline mode.
The Deputy Secretary (Budget), Department of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Mantralaya, Mumbai shall be the "Controlling Officer" in respect of the above grants. The Accounts Officer (Cash Branch), Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has been declared as the "Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for drawing this grant amount. They are being empowered to disburse these grants to Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai, on payment of Form 44 (a) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The Subsidiary Grant (Salary) is an unconditional Subsidiary Grant disbursed under this Government Decision to Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai.
