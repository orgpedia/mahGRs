Government's decision:
Under the Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, for providing essential infrastructure facilities to the Tanda/ Habitat of Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes of Solapur District, vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No.1, Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for 17 works amounting to Rs. 98.30 lakhs. 2.00 lakh has been provided. The remaining funds will be disbursed as per the availability of works. However, as per the Government letter in Reference No. 2 and 3, the works taken up under Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, but not tendered, work order not issued and work order not started, have been put on hold. Now, out of the 17 works which have been accorded administrative sanction vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No. 1, a total of 13 works in Nos. 1 to 13 have been removed from the moratorium and Rs. The Government hereby approve the release of funds amounting to Rs. 79,30,000/- (Rupees Ninety-nine Lakhs Thirty Thousand only).
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Solapur is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds.
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Solapur shall ensure that the terms and conditions of the Government Resolution dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No. 1 are fulfilled before providing funds for the works.
