supply order
In order to inculcate technology in the learning process of the students of Vijabhaj Ashramshalas of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department and to make them learn with joy/ enthusiasm by bringing a positive change in their psychology, administrative approval is being given to 977 Vijabhaj Ashramshalas to purchase 1 computer, projector, interactive whiteboard, audio, TV and other components, multimedia content, integrated digital teaching device based portable smart classrooms for each school. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Order dated 26/05/2023.
Reference No. 2 for selection of suppliers as per the prevailing procedure of the Government in connection with the purchase of this Integrated Digital Teaching Device based portable smart classroom as per the said administrative approval. 2 on the GeM portal. The tender GeM/ 2023/ B/ 3495322 was floated on 26/05/2023. Accordingly, after the technical and financial examination of the interested agencies received, M/ s Krish Intratrade Private Limited has paid Rs. 19,43,25, 300/- (Rupees Twenty Nine Crore Forty Three Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Three Hundred only) plus taxes, which is the lowest (L-1) among other suppliers.
The above Government Decision, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department Reference No. 1, d. 5. The terms and conditions laid down in relation to the aforesaid supply are as follows.
1.It will be mandatory to supply the above materials and provide training to the teachers of the ashramshala according to the terms and conditions of the tender as well as the specifications contained in the tender.
(2) The supply of Integrated Digital Teaching Device based Portable Smart Classroom as per the prescribed standard shall be examined by the recognized agency specified under Annexure-12 of the rule book for the procedures for office procurement to be carried out by the Government departments as specified under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, Government Decision, dated 01/12/2016. Accordingly, in this case, Veermata Jijabai Institute of Technology, Mumbai, has provided the relevant items, quality and quality of the matter, etc.
The inspection report confirming the technical aspects should be obtained and supplied to the ashramshalas.
Integrated Digital Teaching Device based portable smart classrooms should be provided at their own cost within 60 days from the date of receipt of supply order as per the list of ashramshalas attached with the supply order/ allotment list. If the suppliers do not supply the above materials within 60 days, penal action will be taken against the suppliers as per the terms and conditions of the bid.
The Supplier shall be required to deposit 2% of the amount of the supply order in the office of the Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400032 within 8 days from the date of receipt of the supply order, provided that the period of performance bank guarantee shall be 3 years.
Within 8 days of receiving the supply order from the supplier, Rs. It will be mandatory to sign an agreement with the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department on the stamp paper of 500/-.
6. No additional amount shall be payable in addition to the approved price as the rates of materials mentioned in the above table are inclusive of all taxes and expenses. Hence, the supplier will be liable to pay all taxes as determined by the government, including GST and GST. After supplying the goods mentioned in the order, while submitting the invoice of supply, it will be necessary to mention the price of the goods and the amount of GST and all taxes, if permissible, separately. Also, the cost of all costs per material (e.g. training, transportation, etc.)
7. The supplier shall take the delivery challan of the supply of the above materials from the headmaster of the concerned ashram with a dated signature. After this, according to the number of materials received, the invoice, delivery challan and the concerned supplier should submit it to the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400032. After receiving the invoice, the payment will be made through the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
8. The supplier shall supply the material as per the availability of the above material and the running invoice shall be paid by the department accordingly.
