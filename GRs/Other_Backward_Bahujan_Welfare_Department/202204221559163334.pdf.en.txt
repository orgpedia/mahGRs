Preamble- vide Circular dated 4th April, 2022 in the context of Department of Finance. Restrictions on the distribution and expenditure of funds imposed under the Government Decision dated 24.6.2021 have been removed. As per the circular, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure for the 9th quarter of 2022-23 will be allocated on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS) within the limit of 60% of the total annual allocation. In fact, since funds are available in the limit of 10% on the BEAMS system, the matter of disbursing these funds to the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
According to the Finance Department. In pursuance of the Government Circular dated 4.4.2022, 10% of the budgetary provision of the Finance Department for the Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for the financial year 2022-23 has been made available on the BEAMS system. Accordingly, Rs. Approval is hereby given to the said Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 2,80,15, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Eighty Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only) to the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, under the following heads: thousands).
