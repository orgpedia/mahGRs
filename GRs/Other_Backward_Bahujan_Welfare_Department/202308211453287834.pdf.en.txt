Government's decision:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme for the Deprived Castes and Nomadic Tribes category, Government Resolution Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. As per the provisions of Sections 2 to 4, the District Level Committee for Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme, Osmanabad, under the Chairmanship of the Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan. 16.09.2019, Dy. 29.10.2021 and D. The proposal for finalization of names of individual house site beneficiaries in Osmanabad district, which was accorded administrative approval in the meetings held on 01.12.2022, is at Reference No. The letter was received by the government.
For the year 2023-24, for 1057 individual household beneficiaries in Osmanabad district (Annexure-A), Rs. 1.20 lakh as against Rs. 12,68,40, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Sixty Six Lakh Forty Thousand only) and 4% administrative fund (Rs. 4800/- (Rs. 50,73,600/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs Seventy Three Thousand Six Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 13,19,13, 600/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Nineteen Lakhs Thirteen Thousand Six Hundred only) is being approved by the Government. Out of this amount, Rs. 2,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores only) is being released and the remaining amount will be paid as per the availability of funds under the scheme.

The approval is being given to 1057 individual house site beneficiaries in the contiguous Schedule-A under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme subject to the following conditions:
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure-A included among the beneficiaries qualified and finalised by the Osmanabad District Level Committee.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
The benefit of the scheme will be permissible to the person who has a caste certificate and will not be permissible to another person.
Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a difference in the name of one of the beneficiaries, the name of the parent/ elder or the surname, then to remove that difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
The District Level Committee shall be required to take necessary action in the matter and approve the housing scheme of the individual beneficiary subject to the provisions of the relevant Government Decision. Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case.
The District Collector, Osmanabad and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Osmanabad will be responsible for ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Osmanabad as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
