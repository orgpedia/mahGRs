Government's decision:
Under the Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, to provide essential infrastructure facilities to the Tanda/ Habitat of Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes of Gadchiroli District, vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No.1, Rs. Administrative works for 17 works amounting to Rs. 51.00 lakhs
Funds have been sanctioned for the work and will be released as per availability.
Accordingly, as per the Government Decision dated 05.07.2022 in Reference No.22, the full amount of Rs. 51.00 lakhs has been disbursed. However, as per the Government letter in Reference No. 3 and 4, the works taken up under the said scheme but not tendered, work order not issued and work order not started have been stayed. Now, the moratorium on 17 works in Gadchiroli district which were granted administrative approval under the Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2022 in Reference No. 1 is being lifted.
Before disbursing the funds made available under the Government decision dated 05.07.2022 in Reference No. 2 for the said works, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Gadchiroli shall ensure that the terms and conditions contained in Reference No. 1 dated 31.03.2022 are fulfilled.
