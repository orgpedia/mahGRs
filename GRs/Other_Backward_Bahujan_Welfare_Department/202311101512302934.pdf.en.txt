Introduction: Under the Scholarship Scheme for Foreign Higher Education to the children of Visva-Bhaj and Bima categories for the session 2023-24. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 11.10.2018, a Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Govt. In the meeting held on 21.08.2023, 50 eligible students have been selected as per the branch/ course wise marks. The final selection has been made as per the Government decision dated 30.08.2023.
A total of 08 students out of total 50 shortlisted under the Overseas Scholarship Scheme for the year 2023-24 and 01 in 2022-23 will be given financial assistance of Rs. The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune has requested the Government to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 3,32,64, 671/- (Rupees Three Crores Thirty Two Lakhs Sixty Four Thousand Six Hundred and Seventy One only). Accordingly, the matter of allocating funds under the foreign scholarship to these 8 students was under the consideration of the government as per the terms and conditions of the government decision.
