Regarding linking of Aadhaar details of students in Ashramshalas/ Residential Schools/ Vidyaniketan/ Ashramshalas for the children of De-notified Caste/ Nomadic Tribes in the State.

Ministry, Mumbai 400032.
Date is October 7, 2022.
Reference:- Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 2022/ No. 27/ Finance Force, D. 08.06.2022
Preamble: The scheme of Ashramshalas/ Residential Schools/ Vidyaniketan/ Ashramshalas for the children of the Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribes in the State is implemented by this Department. Through this scheme, students are provided education through residential arrangements. The number of students depends on the number of grants, scholarships and grants. Therefore, the matter of linking the personal information of the beneficiary students of the Ashramshala of this department with Aadhaar card was under the consideration of the government as per the instructions of the relevant government decision.
Government circular:
Aadhaar enrolment is mandatory for all students admitted to Ashramshalas/ Residential Schools/ Vidyaniketan/ Ashramshalas for the children of Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribes in the State and the personal details of such beneficiary students should be linked with Aadhaar card.
2. The process of linking the names of all the residential students with Aadhaar card has been completed as the maintenance grant given to the residential students admitted in the Ashramshala is given according to the number of students. From December 1, 2022, the government will disburse the maintenance grant/ fund to the students whose personal details are linked to Aadhaar card.
3. The concerned Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, shall issue the" Form-A" for linking of personal details of all residential/ non-residential students of Ashramshalas with Aadhaar card within 10 days from the date of issue of this circular, along with the plan up to November, 2022. As per the above plan, the concerned Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare will be responsible for reviewing the process of linking Aadhaar every fortnight and implementing it in a proper manner.
