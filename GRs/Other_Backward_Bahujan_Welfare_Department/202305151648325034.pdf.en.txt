Preamble: For the financial year 2023-24, 10% of the budgetary allocation for Post Matric Scholarship for the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes has been released by the Finance Department on the BEAMS system.
It was under consideration of the government to disburse the funds demanded by the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune, for payment of scholarships for the registered applications on the MahaDBT portal from the funds made available by the Finance Department.
Government Decision: For the financial year 2023-24, 10% of the funds sanctioned as per the budgetary provision have been made available on the BEAMS system for payment of pending scholarships/ freeships to students belonging to the categories of Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes under the Post-Matric Scholarship and Freeship Scheme. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 127,78,50, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Seven Crores Eighty Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) to the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune.

The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be the controlling officer for the scheme. The Controlling Officer shall spend the expenditure from the prescribed purpose within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for submitting the statement of expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and utility certificate to the government without fail.
Under the scheme, the Controller will be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controller of Utilization Certificates of the current financial year as well as the previous year's expenditure. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularity takes place while spending this fund as per the terms and conditions of the Financial Manual, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Finance Department from time to time.
The Controlling Officer shall ensure that the conditions contained in the Government Decision/ Circular issued by the Finance Department from time to time are fulfilled while spending the funds.
(5) The decision of the Government shall be subject to the approval of the Finance Department. The issue is being issued as per the approval given vide circular dated 04.04.2022 and as agreed by the Department of Finance from time to time.
