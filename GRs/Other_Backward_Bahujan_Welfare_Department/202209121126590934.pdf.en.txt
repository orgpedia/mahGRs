Government Circular No: Shivruti-2022/ PR No. 04/ Education-1
Government circular:
The Government of India, as per the guidelines issued from time to time by the Central Government, has been approving the payment of Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship to the students belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes residing in the State of Maharashtra for admission to Government approved professional courses in the State through offline mode in their Aadhaar linked bank accounts from the year 2017-18.
Proposals of students belonging to the state of Maharashtra and studying abroad in the category of students belonging to the Visva-Bhaj, Bima-Prabha and Emav categories should be examined in accordance with the existing government provisions and the proposals for the accounting fund should be submitted to the government immediately.
