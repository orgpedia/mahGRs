The Proposal: -
Demand for formation of separate corporation for socio-economic development of Bari community
For many years, it was done by public representatives, various organizations. The Bari community, which is included in the Other Backward Classes of the state, is mainly in the business of producing betel leaves, betel leaves, and mustard. The Bari community youth residing in all the districts of the state are covered under various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Education) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
The government was considering setting up the Sant Shri Ruplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (SSREDC) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for economic upliftment for the overall development of the Bari community with the benefit of Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Kaushalya Vikas Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme). In addition to that,
The Corporation (Subsidiary) was incorporated on 04.10.2024. The decision was taken in the Cabinet meetings. In pursuance of this decision in the last Cabinet meetings, the Government has approved the establishment of a subsidiary company "Sant Shri Ruplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation" under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation vide Government Decision dated 09.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government Decision dated 09.10.2024 under reference is being superseded by this Government Decision.

b) The Government is approving the establishment of Sant Shri Ruplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Bari community in the State belonging to Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Sant Shri Ruplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." 2. Functions of Sant Shri Rupala Maharaj Economic Development Corporation: -
1. To work for the welfare and development of the Bari community in the State.
2. To provide loans for self-employment at low interest rates to persons belonging to the Bari community and to do its AGC.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical a managerial tools to further improve the condition of the society at large.
