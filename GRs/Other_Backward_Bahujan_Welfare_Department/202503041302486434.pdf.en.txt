The Proposal: -
For the past several years, there has been a demand from the people's representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Gujjar (Gujar / Gurjar, Rewa Gujar / Rewe Gujar, Leuva Gujar / Leuve Gujar, Somvanshi Gujar, Dode Gujar, Badgujar) community. The Gujjar community in the state falls under the Other Backward Class category. The government was considering setting up the Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Gujjar Samaj Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for economic upliftment of the youth of the Gujjar community by providing them the benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation in all the districts of the state.
The Government's Decisions:
(a) The Government is approving the establishment of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Gujjar Samaj Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Gujjar community in the State which is included in the Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Gujjar Samaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be operated under the control of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
b) The details of the composition, functioning, and schemes to be implemented of the Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Gujjar Samaj Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Structure of the Corporation (State Level / District Level)
The headquarters of the subsidiary will be located in Mumbai at the headquarters of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in all the districts of the state.

2. Functions of Vallabhbhai Sardar Patel Gujjar Samaj Economic Development Corporation: -
To work for the welfare and development of the Gujjar community in the state.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment to persons belonging to the Gujjar community at low interest rates.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical a managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the Gujjar community.
4. To provide such services as may be deemed necessary for the manufacture, assembly and supply of agricultural products, commodities, materials and materials to the Gujjar community.
5. To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of the Gujjar community in the State.
6. Implementation of schemes approved by the Government on the lines of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
