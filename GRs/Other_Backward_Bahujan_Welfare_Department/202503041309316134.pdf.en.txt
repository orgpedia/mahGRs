The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the community. The Lohani community, which is included in the Other Backward Classes of the state, is mainly engaged in the business of salt, lime and coal making. In order to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the community, the youth belonging to the Lotwana community residing in all the districts of the state are covered under various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The formation of the Vishnupant Ramchandra Dadre (Lonavani) Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary) was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Corporation (Subsidiary) was incorporated on the date 04.10.2024. The decision was taken in the cabinet meeting.
Pursuant to this decision in the Hon'ble Cabinet meetings, the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation has been constituted under the "Self-Employment Scheme." The establishment of a subsidiary, Vishnupant Ramchandra Dadre (Lowni) Economic Development Corporation, "has been approved by the Government of India vide its decision dated 09.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.
