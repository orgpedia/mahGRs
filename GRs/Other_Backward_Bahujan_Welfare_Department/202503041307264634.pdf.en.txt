The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives and various organizations to form a separate corporation for the social and economic development of the Nathpanthi (Nathpanthi Dwari Gosavi, Nathjogi, Bharadi Jogi, Kingribal, Mariwale, Bahurupi, Gosavi, crematorium Jogi, Balsantoshi Kingribale, Gondholo, Dombari, Chitrakali) community. Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation limited to youth from Nathpanthi community. Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Caste and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation Limited to take advantage of various schemes implemented under it (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme) and strive for greater upliftment for their overall development. Internally, the government was considering setting up the "Param Pujya Ganganath Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary)" as a subsidiary.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government is approving the setting up of an Additional Development Corporation under the existing Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation for the social upliftment of the Nathpanthi community in the State. The corporation will be called "Param Pujya Ganganath Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be run under the control of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation.
b) The details of the composition, functions, schemes to be implemented of the HHGMC are as follows: -
Structure of the Corporation (State Level / District Level)
The headquarters of the subsidiary will be in Mumbai at the headquarters of Basantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in all the districts of the state.
