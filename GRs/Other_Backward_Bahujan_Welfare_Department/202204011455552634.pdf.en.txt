Introduction: In the Budget Speech for the year 2021-22, Ma. The Deputy Chief Minister handed over Rs. Rs 50.00 crore. Accordingly, in the Monsoon Session, Chapter No. 168 to Rs. 50.00 crore has been sanctioned to Shamrao Peje Konkan Other Backward Economic Development Corporation under share capital. Since the share contribution limit of the corporation has been exhausted, the government was considering to disburse the fund under the share contribution of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation till the limit is increased.
Government decision: As per the announcement made by the Deputy Chief Minister in the budget speech of 2021-22, the amount sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demand under Share Contribution (42252072) to Shamrao Peje Konkan Other Backward Classes Economic Development Corporation is Rs. 50.00 crore (Rupees Fifty Crores only) is being provisionally reallocated and made available to Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (42251931) under the transfer of Shamrao Peje to Konkan Other Backward Classes Economic Development Corporation by following necessary procedures and rules.
The fund is being temporarily disbursed to the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. Once the cabinet approves it, the funds should be immediately transferred to the Shamrao Peje Konkan Other Backward Classes Economic Development Corporation.
The Deputy Secretary (Finance), Department of Excise, Excise and Insurance Welfare, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer in respect of the above grants. The Cell Officer (Cash Branch), Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Vimukt Jati Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai have been announced to collect the amount. They are being empowered to disburse this grant to Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Limited), Mumbai through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
