The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSBCDC) is planning to launch a scheme to provide self-employment to women belonging to the Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in the state.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation will implement the" Mahila Swayamsevak Interest Refund Scheme" for women belonging to Other Backward Classes.
The scheme and procedure will be as follows:
A. The purpose of the program
The scheme will be applicable to women belonging to Other Backward Classes in the women self-help groups of the state. E. Method of operation
The Women Self-Employment Interest Refund Scheme will be implemented online.
The proposal received through CMRC will be examined in the prescribed manner and the proposal will be submitted to the headquarters of the OBC Corporation through the district office of the OBC Corporation for the process of issuance of Letter of Intent.
The LOI eligibility certificate in the relevant proposal will be issued by the headquarters of the OBC Corporation and its copy will be forwarded to the CMRC for further action.
The validity of the Letter of Intent issued by the OBC Corporation will be 1 year.
