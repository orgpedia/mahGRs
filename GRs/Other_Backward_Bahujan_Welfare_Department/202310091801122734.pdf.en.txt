The Proposal:
General Administration Department Reference No. The procedure for the implementation of the Government Advertisement Distribution Policy has been determined under the Government Decision No. 3. Accordingly, the Department under the Chairmanship of the Director General, Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, has decided to implement the scheme for advertising and publicity of various schemes of the Department through various media.
In the meeting of the Media Planning and Implementation Committee on 23/ 6/ 2023, the media plan of the department was discussed and the media plan to be implemented by the department in the first phase of the financial year 2023-24 was finalized and the necessary tenders and work orders are being issued by the Directorate of Information and Public Relations in accordance with the publication to be made through various media. Accordingly, the matter of giving administrative approval and financial approval to the media plan of the department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
With reference to No. 4 Government decisions here are being formalized. With the newly formed Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, it is necessary to publicize and publicize the various schemes implemented by the department. The Director General of Information and Public Relations
