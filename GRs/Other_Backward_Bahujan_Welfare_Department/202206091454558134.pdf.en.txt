Preamble: Special programme for the development of Dhangar community The scheme for construction of houses for the Dhangar community belonging to nomadic tribe-C category is implemented as per the decision of the Government in Read No. 2. As per the decision of the Namud Government in Reference No. 1, it has been decided to implement the scheme on the lines of Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme. Under this scheme, Reference No. According to the 3rd, the recommendation of 86 beneficiaries has been received from the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Buldana, the matter of approving it was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Special Programme for the Development of Dhangar Community
For the construction of houses for the community, with the approval of the District Committee through the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Buldana, Reference No. Proposals of 86 individual beneficiaries (mentioned in Annexure A attached) received under Section 02 are hereby approved by the Government. The required funds are Rs. 1,07,32, 800/- (Rs. One crore seven lakh thirty two thousand eight hundred only) is being administratively approved to be distributed subject to the following conditions.
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure' A' included among the beneficiaries identified and finalised by the District Level Committee of Buldana district.
2. Caste certificates of all the beneficiaries should be of nomadic tribe-C category.

Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case will be of the Collector, Buldana and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Buldana.
In this case, according to the government decision issued by the government from time to time regarding the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, it will be permissible to select eligible beneficiaries according to the criteria and priority within the limits of the fund and spend within the minimum limit as per the provisions mentioned. No additional funds will be provided under any circumstances.
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Buldana will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the implementation of the said scheme. The necessary provision for the said scheme will be distributed by the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Buldana to the concerned agencies. The concerned District Collector and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare should not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions.
The expenditure in respect of the said scheme shall be met from the provision under the head Demand ZG-3, Main Accounts Head 2225, (04) Welfare of Nomadic Tribes-C (03) (04) Scheme for Construction of Houses for Dhangar Community in Nomadic Tribes-C (Programme) (2225-F461) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) during the financial year 2022-23.
