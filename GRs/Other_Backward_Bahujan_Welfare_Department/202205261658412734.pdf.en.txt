The Proposal:
Under reference 27.7.2021 and dt. Under the Government decision dated 06.12.2021, 11 courses under the Agricultural University mentioned in the" Statement-A" accompanying the Government decision have been declared as Professional Courses. Accordingly, it has been approved to implement the scheme of reimbursement of scholarship/ tuition fee, examination fee, subsistence allowance for the students belonging to the Visva-Bharati, Imav and Bima categories under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department for these courses on Maha DBT and Seva system and to pay the said amount from the academic year 2021-22. The scheme was launched by the government in 2020-21.
Government's decision:
Agricultural Education Policy and Agriculture Admission Process. Deputy Chief Minister Ajit Pawar. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 31.1.2022, Reference No. Under the Government decision mentioned herein 1 and 2, the scheme of reimbursement of scholarship/ tuition fee, examination fee, subsistence allowance for the students of other backward Bahujan Welfare Department for 11 courses under the Agricultural University has been approved to be implemented from the year 2020-21 on the Maha DBT and service system.
Accordingly, approval is being given under this Government decision for payment of scholarship/ friship for these 11 courses from the academic year 2020-21.
