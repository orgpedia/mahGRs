Preamble: - The implementation of the scheme "To provide education to the students of Dhangar community belonging to nomadic tribe-C category in reputed residential schools of English medium" has been approved under the relevant Government Decision. Accordingly, the implementation of such a scheme should be streamlined. The government was considering developing an online portal for the scheme to make it easier for students to choose a designated school by comparing the quality of the schools selected under the scheme, the facilities at the School of Evaluation F, and to make the application process easier for students and the facilities provided to students more effective and transparent.
Accordingly, the Managing Director, Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation Pvt. 3 is listed here. According to the letter dated 3.4.2025, the proposal submitted pursuant to the Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) for the creation of the online system and operation of the said portal has been approved by the Project Implementation Committee. Approval has been given in the meetings held on 28.4.2025 and the following decisions are being taken in pursuance of the administrative approval of such a proposal.
Government Decision: - In line with the implementation of the scheme of "imparting education in reputed residential schools of English medium to students of Dhangar community belonging to Nomadic Tribes-C category" through the portal, the Government of Maharashtra is approving the development of a mobile app along with the expenditure towards creation of an integrated online system and Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) for operation of the portal by Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation Pvt.
