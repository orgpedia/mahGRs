Government Decision: Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme for the sections of the Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes, Government Decision Reference No. 1 and Government Preamble Reference No. As per the provisions of Sections 2 to 4, the proposal for finalization of names of individual household beneficiaries in Jalna district, which was accorded administrative approval in the meeting held on 19.04.2023 under the chairmanship of Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme District Level Committee, Jalna, is referred to as Reference No. The letter was received by the government.
For the year 2023-24, 2165 individual household eligible beneficiaries (as per Annexure A) in Jalna district will be provided Rs. 1.20 lakh as against Rs. 25,98,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Twenty Eight Nine Lakhs only) and 4% Administrative Fund (Rs. 4800/- (Rs. 1,03,92, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Three Lakh Ninety Two Thousand only) aggregating to Rs. 27,01,92, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores One Lakh Ninety Two Thousand Only) is being approved by the Government. Out of this amount, Rs. 4,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores only) is being released and the remaining amount will be paid as per the availability of funds under the scheme.
The said approval is being granted to 2165 individual house site beneficiaries in the contiguous Schedule A under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme subject to the following conditions:
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure' A' attached therewith out of the beneficiaries who have been qualified by the Jalna District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
The benefit of the scheme will be permissible to the person who has a caste certificate and will not be permissible to another person.

Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.

7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
The District Level Committee shall be required to take necessary action in the matter and approve the housing scheme of the individual beneficiary subject to the provisions of the relevant Government Decision. Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case.
4. The District Collector, Jalna and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna will be responsible for ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case.
The District Collector, Jalna and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna shall not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions. No additional funds will be provided for this purpose.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provision under the head Demand ZG-3, Main Accounts for the financial year 2023-24, Title 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, Welfare of 03 Backward Classes, (01) (03) Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme for Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribes (2225-E821) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
8. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for submitting the utilization certificate of the funds so disbursed to the Accountant General and the Government.
