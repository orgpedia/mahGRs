Preamble: As per the Government Circular dated 10.02.2022 in Reference No. 1, Scheme wise Drawing and Disbursing Officers and Controlling Officers have been declared for the year 2022-23 for the subsidy to be disbursed by the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department. Accordingly, for the schemes to be implemented at the field level, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare has been declared as" Controlling Officer" and the concerned Regional Commissioner, Social Welfare/ Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." As per the revised figure of sanctioned posts at Regional Level under Annexure-II in the Government Resolution dated 14.01.2021, 7 posts of Regional Deputy Directors and 36 posts of Assistant Directors at District Level under Annexure-III have been sanctioned. Accordingly, the matter of declaring Regional Deputy Director/ Assistant Director as Drawing and Disbursing Officer in place of Regional Deputy Commissioner/ Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare was under consideration.
Circular: As per the Government Circular dated 10.02.2022, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, will continue to be the Controlling Officer for disbursement of grants for the schemes being implemented by this Department. However, in respect of Pillar No. 4 i.e. Drawing and Disbursing Officers, which is attached to this Government Circular, the same is hereby declared to be so.
