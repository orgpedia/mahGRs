The Proposal:
The Administrative Department shall issue separate orders as to the level to which the cases related to appointment/ posting in the service of the State Government by direct service, promotion, appointment or deputation should be submitted as the appointing authority/ head of the administrative department and at what level final decisions should be taken regarding the appointment/ posting of the concerned employees. 1, Government Decisions are given under the General Administration Department. Accordingly, for appointment/ posting of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees in direct service, promotion or deputation in the approved format for the field office of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Reference No. 2. The Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary of the Administrative Department has been declared the appointing authority under the Government Decision, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department. However, in view of the need for the Government to amend the said Government Decision for the convenience of administrative work, the power of the Administrative Department of the said cadre of employees is being delegated as follows.
