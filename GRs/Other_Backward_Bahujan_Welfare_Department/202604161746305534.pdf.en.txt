The Proposal: -
To implement special programmes for the development of "Goan" community belonging to Special Backward Classes on the lines of ongoing schemes for Scheduled Tribes by the Tribal Development Department. The decision has been taken in the cabinet meeting dated 29.04.2025.
Accordingly, approximately 6000 students belonging to the "Gowari" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes are provided education from Class I to Class XII in reputed English medium residential schools in nearby cities every year through Reference No. 2 hereby approved by the Government Decision dated 14.05.2025.
Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Pune has issued a notification in this regard vide Notification No. 4 In a letter dated 14.11.2025 mentioned herein, a proposal has been submitted to the Government with prescribed forms (total score obtained by the school -85) to approve the admission of students belonging to the same Goan community by selecting Smt. Umabai Multipurpose Education Institute, Gondia Operated Promotional English School, Chulod Road, Gondia under the said scheme. Smt. Umabai Multipurpose Institute of Education, Gondia to provide admission to Goan community students in reputed English medium residential schools in the city.
