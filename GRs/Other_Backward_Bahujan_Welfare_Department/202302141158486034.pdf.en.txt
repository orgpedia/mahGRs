The Head of the Department (Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune) is empowered to sanction reimbursement, subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961, to the Government employees and their families, subject to a ceiling of Rs. 3,00,000/- in each case, of medical expenses incurred on treatment of 29 emergent diseases in private hospitals and reimbursement of the entire expenditure incurred on 5 critical diseases in private hospitals approved by the Government. Cases of reimbursement of medical expenses beyond this limit are submitted to the administrative department of the ministry.
Department of Public Health Reference No. 03 The Government has modified the financial powers in the matter of reimbursement of medical expenses under the decision. The government has decided to implement the change made by the Public Health Department in the cases of teachers/ non-teaching staff in the ashramshalas of this department as follows.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 17th January, 2023 in Reference No. 1 of the Public Health Department, the following changes are being made in the Government decision dated 27th November, 2019 in Reference No. 3 regarding the powers regarding the permissible ceiling and sanction of reimbursement of medical expenses of teachers/ non-teaching staff of Vijabhaj Ashramshala run by the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.

The Head of the Administrative Department of the Ministry shall have full powers to approve or take appropriate decision in the matter of reimbursement of medical expenses above Rs. 5,00,000/- subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and Government Decisions issued from time to time.
The Government hereby directs that while approving the cases of teachers and non-teaching staff of Vijabhaj Ashramshala at the level of Head of the Ministerial Administrative Department, Head of the Department and Regional Head of the Department as mentioned above, the competent authority shall be responsible for strictly examining and approving the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Maintenance) Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued from time to time.
4. This Government decision should also be applicable to pending cases, but the cases decided earlier should not be resumed.
This Government Decision is subject to the Government Decision No. 1, Department of Public Health, No. Vaikhap 2022/ PR No.120/ RAKAVI-2, dated 17th January, 2023 are being issued subject to the enhanced limit.
