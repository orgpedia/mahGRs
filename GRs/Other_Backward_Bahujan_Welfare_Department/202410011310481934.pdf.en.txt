The Proposal: -
There has been a demand for the formation of a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of Sonar Samaj for the past many years by the people's representatives, various organizations. The government was considering setting up the Sant Narhari Maharaj Adhuri Vikas Nigam (Subsidiary) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for the overall development of the Sonar Samaj by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to the youth belonging to Other Backward Classes in the state.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of Sonar Samaj belonging to Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Sant Narhari Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)."
The subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The details of the composition, functions, and schemes of the Sant Narhari Maharaj Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Structure of the Corporation (State Level / District Level)
The headquarters of the subsidiary will be located in Mumbai at the headquarters of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in all the districts of the state.
