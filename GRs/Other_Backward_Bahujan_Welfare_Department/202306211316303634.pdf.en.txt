Government's decision:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme for the Deprived Castes and Nomadic Tribes category, Government Resolution Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. As per the provisions of section 2 to 4, the proposal for finalization of names of individual house site beneficiaries in Bhandara district, which was accorded administrative approval in the meeting held on 23.03.2022 under the chairmanship of the District Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana District Level Committee, Bhandara, has been sent to the State Government vide Reference No. The letter has been received by the government.
For the year 2023-24, 417 individual household eligible beneficiaries in Bhandara district (as per Annexure A) will be given Rs. 1.20 lakh as against Rs. 5,00,40, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Forty Thousand only) and 4% of Administrative Fund (Rs. 4800/- (Rs. 20,01,600/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs One Thousand Six Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 5,20,41, 600/- (Rupees Five Crore Twenty Lakhs Forty One Thousand Six Hundred only) is hereby approved by the Government. At present, Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.00 crore (Rupees Two Crores only) is being released and the remaining funds will be released as per the adequacy of work and availability of funds.
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, the approval is being given to 417 individual house site beneficiaries in Annexure A subject to the following conditions:
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure' A' attached to it out of the beneficiaries who have been selected by the Bhandara District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
The benefit of the scheme will be permissible to the person who has a caste certificate and will not be permissible to another person.

Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a difference in the name of one of the beneficiaries, the name of the parent/ elder or the surname, then to remove that difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
The District Level Committee shall be required to take necessary action in the matter and approve the housing scheme of the individual beneficiary subject to the provisions of the relevant Government Decision. Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case will be of the Collector, Bhandara and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara.
District Collector, Bhandara and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara should not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions. No additional funds will be provided for this purpose.
6. Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
The expenditure in this regard should be met from the financial provision available under the head Demand ZG-3, Main Accounts for the financial year 2023-24, Title 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, Welfare of 03 Backward Classes, (01) (03) Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme for Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribes (2225-E821) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
8. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for submitting the utilization certificate of the funds so disbursed to the Accountant General and the Government.
