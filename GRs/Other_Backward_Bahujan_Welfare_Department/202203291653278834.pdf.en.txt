31 to Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 20800.00 thousand has been made.
The Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation will be provided Rs. The grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 20800.00 thousand (Rupees Two Crores Eight Lakhs only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented. Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery rate.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7. While spending the above funds, the concerned officers should make use of the funds by complying with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations etc.
8. Reference No. Each of the conditions in Circulars 2 and 3 mentioned herein shall be fulfilled by the Corporation.
