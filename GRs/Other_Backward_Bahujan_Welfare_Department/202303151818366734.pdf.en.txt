Preamble: The decision regarding imparting education to the students of Dhangar community in English medium reputed residential schools in the cities has been taken vide Reference No. 1 here. The decision was taken on 4/9/2019. As per the provisions of the said Government Resolution, the proposal for selection of the school, Dhruva English School, Jalna, operated by Maharashtra Multipurpose Social Society, Jalna, along with the inspection report and the scoring report (total score 80) has been submitted to the Government by the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, M.R., Pune, through a letter under reference. Accordingly, under the scheme of' Teaching students of Dhangar community in English medium residential schools in the cities', the Maharashtra Multipurpose Social Institute, Jalna operated Dhruva English School, Jalna, was under the consideration of the government to approve the admission of 100 students of Dhangar community in the school for the academic year 2023-24 subject to the terms and conditions of the relevant government decision.
Government Decision: Under the scheme of' Education in English medium reputed residential schools in the cities for the students of Dhangar community', subject to the terms and conditions in the Government Decision issued from time to time by the Government, from the academic year 2023-24, the institutions/ schools mentioned in the following table and in front of the name of the school, Rakana No. As per Rule 4, the government is approving the admission of students of the Dhangar community in the nomadic tribe-C category within the limit of the approved number of students.

The following terms and conditions should be followed while giving admission to a nominated school: 1. The concerned institution/ school will be required to fulfill the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 4/9/2019.
2. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna should inspect the school in accordance with the Nominated Residential School Scheme and all the students admitted in the school should be registered by verifying their Aadhaar card. After that, the government will take a decision after verifying the number of beneficiary students present in person and the number of direct resident students. The detailed report as per the terms and conditions of 04/09/2019 should be submitted to the Government through the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune for the disbursement of funds with the approval of the Regional Deputy Commissioner.
3. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare concerned shall ensure that the institution is ready to provide all the facilities mentioned in the proposal submitted to the Government through the concerned institution/ school regarding student approval under the scheme and shall enter into an agreement with the institution.
The institution/ school is not expected to start a separate batch/ class for the students of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category, it will be mandatory to teach the students admitted under the scheme along with other students in the regular batch/ class running in the school. If it is found that a separate batch/ class has been started for the students, action will be taken to register a case against the said institution and the recognition of the school will be cancelled immediately.
It is mandatory to appoint a female superintendent and caretaker in the girls' hostel admitted in the institution. If the school fails to do so, its recognition will be cancelled.
The Assistant Commissioner of Social Welfare of the concerned district should take care to admit students of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category only in the schools approved by the government under this scheme and within the limit of the number of students approved for the concerned school.
As per the original provision of the scheme, the benefit of this scheme should be given only to those children whose annual family income is within the maximum limit of Rs. 1.00 lakh.
8. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district shall complete the process of admission of the students immediately by giving priority to the nearest school as well as the selected schools of the concerned project while admitting the students.
If the number of applications received for admission under the scheme is more than the number of schools selected in a project area and the number of students approved therein, the Assistant Commissioner of Social Welfare of the concerned district should announce the selection list by selecting the students through a draw of lots.

After the selection of the school under the nominated scheme, the Assistant Commissioner of the concerned district, Social Welfare, should visit the nominated schools in person twice in the first semester and twice in the second semester and submit a report to the government through the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune, regarding the friendly atmosphere in the school, the facilities provided to the students, the medicine, the educational quality, nutrition, etc.

2. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare concerned shall enter into an agreement with the nominated institution where a student of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category is to be admitted. The agreement should include the provision of education, accommodation, security, health, food and other facilities to the students of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category from the class admitted under the nominated scheme to class 12 and not to hold separate classes. Further, all the nominated schools selected under the nominated scheme will be rated on the online system to be developed for the scheme before the start of the new academic year every year and in case the selected nominated schools are found to be deficient in the required prescribed educational and residential facilities or if the school is found" ineligible" as per the prescribed rating, the students belonging to Dhangar community belonging to nomadic tribe-C category who have been de-recognized under the nominated scheme of the concerned school will be accommodated elsewhere. This should also be included in the contract.
