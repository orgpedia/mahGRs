Government's decision:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Housing Scheme for the Deprived Castes and Nomadic Tribes category, Government Resolution Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. As per the provisions of section 2 to 4, the proposal of 613 individual household beneficiaries of Bhandara district, who were administratively approved by the District Level Committee in its meeting held on 10.06.2022 under the chairmanship of the Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement/ Household Scheme District Level Committee, Bhandara, was sent to the State Government vide Reference No. The letter has been received by the government.
In pursuance of the said proposal, post-facto approval was accorded to 613 individual household beneficiaries (as per Annexure-A) in Bhandara district for the year 2023-24 under the Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana and an amount of Rs. Rs. 1.30 lakh. 7,96,90, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Ninety Thousand only) and 4% of Administrative Fund (Rs. 5200/- (Rs. 31,87,600/- (Rupees Thirty One Lakh Eighty Seven Thousand Six Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 8,28,77, 600/- (Rupees Eight Crores Twenty Eight Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Six Hundred only) is being approved by the Government. Out of this amount, Rs. 2,07,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Seven Lakhs only) is being released and the remaining amount will be released as per the availability of funds under the scheme.

3. Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, the approval is being given to 613 individual house site beneficiaries in Annexure-A subject to the following conditions.
The benefit of the said scheme will be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure-A included among the beneficiaries who have been qualified by the Bhandara District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
(3) The benefit shall be admissible to the person having the caste certificate and shall not be admissible to any other person.
Beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a discrepancy in the name of any of the beneficiaries, in the name or surname of the parent/ elder, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned to remove the discrepancy. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
The District Level Committee shall ensure that there is no violation of the terms and conditions of the relevant Government decisions/ clarifications.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in the said case will be of the Collector and the Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Bhandara and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara.
The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara is being authorized to draw the said funds from the treasury. Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
District Collector and Chairman, Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Bhandara and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara should not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions. No additional funds will be provided for this purpose.
