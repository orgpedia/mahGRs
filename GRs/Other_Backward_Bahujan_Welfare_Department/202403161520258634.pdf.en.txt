4) The Weavers' Community Economic Development Corporation shall have the following functions: - 1. To work for the welfare and development of the Weavers' Community in the State.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment at low interest rates to persons belonging to the weaving community.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the weaving community.
4. To provide services that are conducive to the manufacture, assembly and supply of materials and materials necessary for the weaving community.
5. To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of the weavers community in the State and to prepare reports for the scheme.
