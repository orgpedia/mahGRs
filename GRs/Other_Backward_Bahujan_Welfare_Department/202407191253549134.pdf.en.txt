Under the Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Scheme, 1236 individual housing beneficiaries in the adjoining Schedule A of Pune district are being approved subject to the following conditions: - The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible only to the beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure A attached to the beneficiaries finalised by the District Level Committee, Pune after scrutiny.
2. All the beneficiaries must belong to the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category and such person shall be entitled to the benefit of the said scheme.
If there is a discrepancy in the name or surname of one of the beneficiaries, the parent / guardian will be required to produce proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned to remove the discrepancy. The benefit of such a scheme will not be admissible to such a person unless the name / surname discrepancy is removed.
4. In case of duplication of names of eligible beneficiaries, action should be taken as per the policy of the government excluding such names. If the names of the beneficiaries are doubled, the Assistant Director will be responsible for them.
5. As per the provision in the Government Decision, it shall be mandatory to pass on the benefit of such scheme to only one person in the family.
6. The Assistant Director shall be responsible for providing benefits to the beneficiaries by ensuring that the beneficiaries selected under the scheme do not avail the benefits of other housing schemes.
7. The District Collector / Assistant Director of the concerned district shall be responsible for ensuring that all documents meet the terms and conditions as provided in the Government Decision.
8. The Assistant Director, Pune shall not spend the funds without the proviso to the terms and conditions of the Government decision. Also, the report on the work should be submitted to the government from time to time.
To disburse the said funds, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Kalyan, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the "Controlling Officer" and shall disburse the said funds through Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Office of the Director, State Management Cell - Rural Housing Cell, CIDCO Bhawan, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai.
