Government's decision
The Government of India has constituted the National Commission for Backward Classes, New Delhi for effective implementation and control of schemes for the Backward Classes. A President, Vice-President and Secretary are appointed by the Board. In order to make the functioning of the Commission more effective, the Chairman, Members and Member Secretaries of the Commission have been directed to ensure compliance of the provisions of Government Resolution, Social Justice and Special Assistance Division No. CBC- 12/2012/ P.S. No.120/ Mavak, D. He was declared the state guest on August 16, 2012. Further, facilities have been provided to the Chairman, Members and Member Secretaries of National Commission for Backward Classes, New Delhi, as State Guests of State, whenever they visit the State of Maharashtra on an official tour, subject to the following conditions as per their status:
(a) The facilities of administration, transport, accommodation and food shall be available only during office visits.
(b) This facility will be available for a maximum period of three days.
