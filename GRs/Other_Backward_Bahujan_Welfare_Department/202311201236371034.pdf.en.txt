Read:- A delegation of Dhangar community led by Chief Minister Shri. Minutes of the meeting held on 21.9.2023
Introduction: Ma. The Chief Minister presided over the meeting. In the meeting held on 21.9.2023, the States of Madhya Pradesh, Bihar and Telangana have been directed to send a delegation/ study group of State officials including representatives of Dhangar community to the State to study the functioning of the above States in terms of the criteria based on which caste certificates and other benefits have been provided to the castes/ tribes included in the caste list in their respective States. In this background, the names of the government officials and non-government members to be included in the delegation/ study group to be formed in the wake of the demand for reservation by including the Dhangar community in the Scheduled Tribes.
As the recommendation was received by the Government, the matter of constituting the delegation/ study group was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have accorded approval for constitution of a Delegation/ Study Group to study the procedures adopted by the States of Madhya Pradesh, Bihar and Telangana for providing caste certificates and other benefits to the castes/ tribes included in the caste list in their respective States.

2. The" mandate" of the delegation/ study group shall be as follows:
The delegation, in pursuance of the demand for reservation/ benefits of Scheduled Tribe category to the Dhangar community in the State, studied the procedures adopted by the States of Madhya Pradesh, Bihar and Telangana for grant of Scheduled Tribe caste certificates and other benefits to certain castes/ tribes included in the caste-wise list in their respective States.
To submit a report to the government:
(1) The said delegation/ study group shall visit the concerned offices in the States of Madhya Pradesh, Bihar and Telangana to study the procedure adopted for extending the benefit of reservation from the Scheduled Tribes category to certain castes in that State.
