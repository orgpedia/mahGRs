Preamble: It has been decided to confer the Mahatma Basaveshwara Samajika Samatha-Siva Award on the occasion of the birth anniversary of Mahatma Basaveshwara on the occasion of Vaishakha Shuddha (Akshaya Tritiya) every year to a person who is an artist, litterateur, social organizer and social worker working in the field of artistic, social enlightenment and literature for the social, educational, economic and organizational development of the Veerashaiva-Lingayat community.
As per the decision of the Government referred herein No. 2, the rules regarding the award have been prepared and as per Annexure-A of the said rules, 1 person and 1 organization are given the Mahatma Basaveshwara Samajika Samatha-Siva Award every year. The issue of award distribution was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: To select a person who is an artist, litterateur, social evangelist, social organizer and social worker working in the field of artistic, social enlightenment and literary for the social, educational, economic and organizational development of the Veerashaiva-Lingayat community and an organization working for this purpose. on 07.06.2022. State Level Selection Committee headed by Minister, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare and Chairperson Award Selection Committee.
