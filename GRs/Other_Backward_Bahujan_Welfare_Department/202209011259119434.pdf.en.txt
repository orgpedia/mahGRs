Government circular:
In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the office of the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune. As per the circular dated 25th March 2022, the students belonging to the category of Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes residing in the State of Maharashtra who have taken admission for private unaided and permanently aided professional courses in foreign countries outside the State will be entitled to Post-Matric Scholarship, Tuition Fee, Examination Fee Reimbursement, Maintenance Allowance, etc. from the year 2017-18.
A circular was issued regarding the offline payment to be made to the Aadhaar-linked bank account of the students.
However, the Department of Social Justice is providing scholarships and not freeships to the students belonging to the Scheduled Castes category who are studying abroad and are domiciled in Maharashtra. As per the decision of the Government dated 8.11.2017, under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Scholarship Scheme, a scholarship scheme is being implemented to provide higher education in educational institutions of the country to 100 students belonging to the Scheduled Castes category in the state.
Considering the guidelines of the Central Government and the prevailing policy of the Department of Social Justice, it is under the consideration of the Government to formulate a policy for providing scholarships to students belonging to the category of Vimukta Jati/ Nomadic Tribes/ Other Backward Classes for higher education in educational institutions in the country.
