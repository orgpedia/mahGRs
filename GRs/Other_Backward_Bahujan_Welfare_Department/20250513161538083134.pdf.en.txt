The Proposal: -
Girls belonging to the Most Backward Classes (EWS), Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) and Other Backward Classes (OBC) who are enrolled in professional courses have been granted the benefit of Yoo percentage instead of 50 per cent of the tuition fee and examination fee under the GAAS Government decision.
The government was considering allowing the opening of new books of accounts for the "Post-Matric Scholarship and Payment of Tuition Fee and Examination Fee to Girls Admitted to Vocational Courses in Other Backward Classes" Scheme being implemented by the Department of OBC Welfare.
