Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme, approval is being given to 58 individual house site beneficiaries in Schedule A with the decision of the Government of Jalna District subject to the following conditions.
The benefit of the said scheme shall be admissible to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure' A' added with the beneficiaries identified by the Jalna District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
2) All the beneficiaries must be the holders of the individual caste certificate of the Visva-Bharati category.
3. Person having caste certificate. The same person will be eligible for the benefit of this scheme.
It will not benefit anyone else.
The beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
6) If there is a difference in the name of the person, the name of the parent/ father or the surname, then to remove the difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
8. District Level Committee Government Resolution Reference No. 1 dated 24/1/2018 and Govt. 2 to 4, dated 8/1/2019, dated 8/3/2019 and dated 11/9/2019 respectively, it will be necessary to proceed to approve the housing scheme of the individual beneficiary by taking necessary action in the said case. Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case. No additional funds will be provided under any circumstances.
The responsibility of ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case will be of the Collector, Jalna and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna.
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the implementation of the said scheme. The necessary provision for the said scheme will be distributed by the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna to the concerned agencies. The Collector, Jalna and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Jalna shall not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions.
The expenditure in this regard should be met from the provision under the head Demand ZG-3, Main Account Head 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, Welfare of 03 Backward Classes, (01) (03) Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme for Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribes (2225-E821) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) during the financial year 2022-23.
