Government Memorandum: -
Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur has been sanctioned a grant of Rs.2,225 crore for construction of a 12-storey training centre building at Nagpur with the objective of grant-in-aid for manufacture of Account Head 2225F 497, 35 Bhandabali Powder for the financial year 2011 2025-26. Under this MoU, the Government is approving the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,64,80, 000 / - (Rupees One Crore Brass Lakh Eighty Thousand only).
The Managing Director, Mahajyoti, Nagpur shall be the controlling officer for expenditure of the said fund for disbursement of such fund. The Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Kalyan (Regional Division) Nagpur is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw this amount.
3. Expenditure on "Demand No. ZG-32225 - Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and
