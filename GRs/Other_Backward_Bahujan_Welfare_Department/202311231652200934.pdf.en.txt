The Proposal:
According to the Government Resolution No. 1. January 14, 2021. A total of 821 posts have been approved after approval of the High Powered Committee of Secretaries as on March 22, 2022. General Administration Department Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2020, the Administrative Departments were contemplating to constitute Civil Service Boards for making recommendations to the competent authority regarding posting/ transfer of officers/ employees belonging to Group-A, Group-B (Gazetted/ Gazetted) and Group-C. The government has taken the following decisions in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following Civil Services Board is being constituted to recommend to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) officers in the field offices of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department
