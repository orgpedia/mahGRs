The Proposal: -
To implement the scheme of applying financial concession in fees for competitive examination to unemployed youth of Dhangar community belonging to Nomadic Tribes-C category, read the reference No. 01 has been approved by the Government here. In order to provide clarity in the implementation of the scheme, the Managing Director, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute, Nagpur has issued a reference letter No. Guidance has been sought under Section 2.
02. In view of this, the Government was contemplating to frame guidelines / procedures for effective implementation of the scheme for applying financial rebate in fees for competitive examination to unemployed youth of Dhangar community belonging to Nomadic Tribes-C category.
The Government's Decisions:
The following guidelines / procedures are being approved by the Government for effective implementation of the Scheme for Application of Financial Relaxation in Fee for Competitive Examination to unemployed youth of Dhangar community belonging to Nomadic Tribes-C category.
The following criteria are being prescribed for availing the scheme:
The basic qualifications are: -
9. The student should belong to the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic tribe-C category.
The student must be a resident of the state of Maharashtra.
3. The student should belong to the non-creamy layer group.
4. Submission of caste certificate of nomadic tribe-C category issued by the competent authority shall be mandatory.

It will be mandatory for the student to link his / her Aadhaar number to his / her nationalised bank account.
Educational Qualification: The candidate should have passed the graduation examination with at least 60% marks.
The student selected under this scheme will be eligible for the benefit for a maximum of 3 years.
After the first 02 years of availing the benefit, in order to avail the benefit in the third year, these students must have passed at least 091 prelims examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the last 2 years.
3) Documents required for availing the benefits of the scheme: - > Domicile Certificate > Caste Certificate issued by the competent authority > Degree Certificate B Scorecard > Valid Non-Crimilier Certificate > Application for Examination conducted by Maharashtra Public Service Commission B Fee payment receipt.
Selection Process: - > The benefit of this scheme will be admissible for all competitive examinations conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
A student should be given the benefit of this scheme a maximum of 2 times in a year.
To avail the benefits of the scheme, students should submit applications on the online website of the Mahajyoti Sanstha.
The Managing Director, Mahajyoti, Nagpur will be responsible for finalizing the list of eligible beneficiaries of the scheme at the state level after scrutinizing the applications received.
5. Distribution of Grants: - Proposals received under the said scheme should be given benefits within the limit of funds received in each year. Also, eligible students under the scheme will have to pay 50 per cent of the total fee admissible for the examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission as a refund.
For this, the Mahajyoti Sanstha should ask the government for the necessary funds for the application of eligible beneficiaries under the scheme.
The Managing Director, Mahajyoti, is responsible for taking action to disburse grants to eligible students under the scheme within the limits of the funds received in the respective financial year.
It will be in Nagpur.
