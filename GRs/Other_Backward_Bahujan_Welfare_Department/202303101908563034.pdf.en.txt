Read the above mentioned Government Preamble mentioned at No. 3 here. 17th February, 2023 is being superseded and the revised orders are as follows:
Government Circular, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance No. CBC-2012/ No. 182/ Vijabhaj-1, Dy. The following sentence in paragraph 2 (ii) of 25th March, 2013 is being omitted.
" And to verify that the said candidate does not fall in the category of advanced and advanced person/ group on the particular crucial date prescribed. "
Government Circular, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance No. CBC-2012/ No. 182/ Vijabhaj-1, Dy. Paragraph 2 (iii) of 25th March, 2013 reads as under:
" (ii) The last date of receipt/ submission of application for a post or a particular crucial date fixed for that post shall be taken as the date to verify that the candidate concerned does not fall in the category of advanced and non-advanced person/ group. "
The said paragraph 2 (ii) is being amended and the said paragraph 2 (ii) is to be read as under:
" (ii) To verify that the candidate concerned does not fall in the category of advanced and not advanced person/ group, the non-crimilier certificate of the candidate should be taken from the financial year in which the last date of submission/ acceptance of application for a post or a particular crucial date fixed for that post falls.
