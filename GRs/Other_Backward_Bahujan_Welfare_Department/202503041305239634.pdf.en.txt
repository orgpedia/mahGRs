The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Leuva Patidar community. The Leuva Patidar community in the state belongs to the Other Backward Class category. It was under consideration of the Government to set up the Leva Patidar Samaj Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Leva Patidar community by providing the youth of the Leva Patidar community residing in all the districts of the state with the benefit of various schemes implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MASBFC) (Seed Bhandabal Yojana, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme).
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government is approving the setting up of the Leuva Patidar Samaj Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of the Leuva Patidar community in the State, which is included in the Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is currently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Leva Patidar Samaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be operated under the control of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
b) Details of the composition, functions, schemes to be implemented of the Patidar Samajik Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Structure of the Corporation (State Level / District Level)
The headquarters of the subsidiary will be located in Mumbai at the headquarters of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in the survey districts of the state.
