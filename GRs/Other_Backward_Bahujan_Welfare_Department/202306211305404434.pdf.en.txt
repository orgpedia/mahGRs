Government's decision:
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme for the sections of the Deprived Castes and Nomadic Tribes, Government Decision Reference No. 1 and Government Sanction Letter Reference No. Under 2 to 4, the District Collector and Chairman of Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme District Committee, Nagpur, has decided to provide free housing to the people of Mauja Pipla (Bh. ), T. The final proposal for construction of 1 colony for 20 beneficiaries in Annexure A along with the Administrative Sanction Order dated 07.06.2016 for construction of 1 colony for 20 beneficiaries in Savner, G.Nagpur has been sent by the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Nagpur vide Reference No. 5 submitted to the government.
2. Mauja Pipla (Bh. ), T. For the construction of 1 colony of 20 beneficiaries in Savner, G.Nagpur and Rs. 88,62,000/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) and 4% of the administrative fund of Rs. 96,000/- (Rupees Ninety Five Thousand only). 89,58,000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty-eight Lakhs Fifty-eight Thousand only) is hereby approved by the Government.
3. Mauja Pipla under Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme (Bh. ), T. For 1 colony to be constructed at Savner, G. Nagpur, 20 beneficiaries of Annexure A are being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
The benefit of the said scheme will be admissible only to the eligible beneficiaries mentioned in Annexure' A', who have been added to the eligible beneficiaries identified by the Nagpur District Level Committee and finalized after scrutiny.
Under this scheme, 5 guntas of land should be provided to each beneficiary family. A house with an area of 269 square feet should be constructed on the said land and the rest of the land should be provided opportunities for self-employment through various government schemes.

3. All beneficiaries must be holders of an individual caste certificate in the Visva-Bharati category and such person will be eligible for the benefit of the said scheme.
Under this scheme, beneficiaries will be required to submit income proof for the year 2022-23.
All beneficiaries will be required to submit domicile certificate within one month from the date of order.
If there is a difference in the name of any of the beneficiaries, in the name or surname of the parent/ elder, then to remove that difference, it will be necessary to submit proof of the name, parent's name and surname of the person concerned. If the difference in name/ surname is not removed, then such person will not be allowed to take advantage of the scheme.
7.If the names of eligible beneficiaries are found to be double, then action should be taken according to the policy of the government by excluding those names.
(8) The District Level Committee shall take necessary action in the matter subject to the provisions of the relevant Government Decision. Be careful not to violate the terms and conditions of the government decision in any case.
4. The District Collector, Nagpur and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Nagpur will be responsible for ensuring that all the documents and conditions of the beneficiaries are fulfilled in this case.
5. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer should distribute the necessary provisions to the concerned system. The report should be submitted to the government on time.
The District Collector, Nagpur and the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Nagpur shall not spend the fund without fulfilling all the terms and conditions. No additional funds will be provided under any circumstances.
