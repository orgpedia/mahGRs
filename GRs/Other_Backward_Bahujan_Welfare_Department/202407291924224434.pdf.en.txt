Introduction: - Residential Ashramshalas are run by this department on a 100% subsidized basis for children belonging to the Visva-Bharj category. Some of these primary ashramshalas were upgraded to secondary ashramshalas, while some secondary schools were upgraded to residential higher secondary ashramshalas over time. At present, about 977 primary, secondary and higher secondary ashramshalas are functioning under the department. The Ashramshalas run by NGOs are doing a commendable job to ensure the economic, social and educational progress of the student. Free accommodation, food, health facilities, sports facilities, educational materials, toiletries, dining utensils, etc. are being provided by the Ashramshala to the boys and girls of the Visva-Bharj category with education from classes 9 to 12.
For this, the government provides non-salaried grants to the relevant agencies.
2. A healthy lifestyle can be provided by the government to the students / students of the Ashramshala through a modern open gymnasium (081 051) using the open space in the Ashramshala. This will keep the students fit and healthy by allowing them to exercise in a modern gymnasium created in an open and natural environment and will in turn enhance their academic quality. Considering this, Ma. The Minister has directed to provide open gymnasiums (0081 0911) to Vijyabhaj Ashramshalas.
