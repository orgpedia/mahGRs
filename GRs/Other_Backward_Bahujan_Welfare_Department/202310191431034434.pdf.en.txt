Government Decision: Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSEDCL) has been provided with 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head 2225 E 857 (Subsidiary Grants (Compulsory) for the financial year 2023-24 to meet the expenditure on management and administration. A budgetary provision of Rs. 10,67,36, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Thirty Six Thousand only) has been made. Out of this, 10% of the total budgetary provision for the salaries of officers/ employees working in the corporation and the salaries of the external service employees i.e. Rs. 1,06,73, 600/- (Rupees One Crore Six Lakh Seventy Three Thousand Six Hundred only) has been disbursed to Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation under the relevant Government Resolution No. 3.
In the present situation, the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation has to pay the remaining 46% of the wage subsidy within the limit of 56% of the provision available on the BIMS system for 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the Head of Accounts 2225 E 857 (Compulsory) for the financial year 2023-24 to meet the expenses on management and administration. A 36-Additional Grant (Salary) amounting to Rs. 4,90,98, 560/- (Rupees Four Crore Ninety Lakh Eight Thousand Nine Thousand Five Hundred Sixty only) is being approved for disbursement subject to the following conditions.
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented.
Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2.Formalization of operations to limit the cost of management.
3.The details of the amounts spent on various matters and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the Government.
4.Cut down on expenses.
5.The Chief Minister should make efforts to ensure that the rate of recovery of loans is effectively implemented.
6.Sadar Scheme funds are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7.While spending the aforesaid funds, the concerned officers shall make use of the funds in compliance with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations, etc.
8.S Reference No. 1 Every condition of the circular mentioned herein shall be complied with by the Corporation.
