The proposal-
Department of Finance As per the circular dated 12.04.2023, the budgetary allocation for the first nine months (ending December, 2023) of the financial year 2023-24, including the mandatory and programme expenditure, is to be disbursed through the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS) within a limit of 70% of the total annual allocation. In fact, since funds are available in the limit of 10% on the BEAMS system, it was under the consideration of the government to disburse these funds to the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune.
Government's decision
According to the Finance Department. In accordance with the Government Circular dated 12.04.2023, the budgetary allocation made by the Department of Finance for the Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for the financial year 2023-24 on the BEAMS system will be released within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation i.e. 20% for the first quarter of April to June, 2023, 20% for July to September, 2023 and 30% for October to December, 2023. However, since the Finance Department has provided 10% funds on the BEAMS system, the said 10% funds shall be available under the following headings: 5 to Rs. 5,10,15, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Ten Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) to the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune.

The Controlling Officer shall spend the expenditure from the prescribed purpose within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid. The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be responsible for submitting the details of expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and utility certificate to the government without fail.
