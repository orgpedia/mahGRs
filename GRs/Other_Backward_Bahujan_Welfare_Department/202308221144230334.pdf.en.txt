Introduction: In the state of Maharashtra, many castes and tribes of nomadic tribes are still living as nomadic migrants. There are many groups of tandas/ hamlets such as Laman, Banjara, Dhangar, Pardhi etc. in which people of these castes/ tribes have been living for many years but such tandas or hamlets do not have access to basic facilities and are inclined towards modernity, but the majority of the society is living in poverty. For this, it is necessary to provide facilities to this community in tandas, wadis or hamlets so that they can lead a stable life.
