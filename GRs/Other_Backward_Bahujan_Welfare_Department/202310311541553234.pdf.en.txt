The Proposal:
There are many castes and tribes in the state of Maharashtra who are still living as migrants. Although there are many groups of tandas/ hamlets such as Laman, Banjara, Vanjari, Dhangar, Pardhi and people of these castes/ tribes have been living in such tandas for many years, such tandas or hamlets do not have access to basic facilities and are inclined towards modernity, but the majority of this category of society is living in poverty. In order to ensure a stable life for the community, it is necessary to provide them with some necessary infrastructure in the place where the community of this category is currently living, be it in the tandas, wadis or hamlets.
