Mahajyoti is an autonomous organization established to implement various activities for the benefit of youth belonging to Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes and other deprived and marginalized sections. Under Mahajyoti, the government was considering setting up a residential competitive examination training complex for 200 girls for training in the National Defence Academy (NDA) and other competitive examinations.
