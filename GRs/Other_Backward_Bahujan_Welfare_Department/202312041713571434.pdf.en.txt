Government's decision:
Under Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, for providing essential infrastructure facilities to the Tanda/ Habitat of Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes of Bhandara District, vide Government Resolution dated 30.03.2022 in Reference No.1, Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for 17 works for an amount of Rs. 90.00 lakhs. 1.00 lakh has been provided. The remaining funds will be disbursed as per the availability of works. However, as per the Government letter in Reference No. 2 and 3, the works taken up under Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, but not tendered, work order not issued and work order not started, have been put on hold. Now, in reference No. 1, 17 works which were given administrative approval under the Government decision dated 30.03.2022 have been removed and the balance amount of Rs. 89,00,000/- (Rupees Ninety-nine Lakh only) is hereby approved by the Government.
2. Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of the said funds.
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Bhandara shall ensure that the terms and conditions of the Government Resolution dated 30.03.2022 in Reference No. 1 are fulfilled before providing funds for the works.
