The Proposal: -
For the past several years, there has been a demand from the people's representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the social development of the Ladshakhi Vani / Vani community.
The government was considering setting up "sixteen Kulaswini Economic Development Corporations (Subsidiaries)" as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSBDC) to provide the benefit of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the overall development of the community.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) belonging to Other Backward Classes (OBCs) under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) in the State. The corporation will be called the "Sixteen Kulaswamini Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The details of the structure, functions, and schemes to be implemented of the Sixteen Kulaswamini Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Composition of the Corporation (State Level / District Level) - The headquarters of the Sub-Company shall be at Mumbai at the place of the headquarters of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in the survey districts of the state.
The Board of Directors of the Corporation shall consist of three non-official members including the Chairman, Vice-Chairman and the following four executive members.
