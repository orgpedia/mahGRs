The Proposal -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the community. The Lohani community, which is included in the Other Backward Classes of the state, is mainly engaged in the business of salt, lime and coal making. In order to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the community, youth belonging to the Lowani community residing in all the districts of the state are getting the benefits of various schemes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme). The formation of the Vishnupant Ramchandra Dadre (Lonavani) Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary) was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic upliftment of the Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is currently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of the Other Backward Classes in the state. The corporation, "Sh. Vishnupant Ramchandra Dadre (Lonavani) Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation) "will be called. The operations of the Subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. Details of the structure, functioning, and implementation of the Late Vishnupant Ramchandra Dadre (Lonavani) Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
Structure of the Corporation (State Level / District Level)
The headquarters of the subsidiary will be located in Mumbai at the headquarters of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. District offices will continue to function in all the districts of the state.
