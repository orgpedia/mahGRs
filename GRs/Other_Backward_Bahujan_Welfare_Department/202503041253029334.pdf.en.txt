The Proposal: -
There has been a demand for the formation of a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of Sonar Samaj for the past many years by the people's representatives, various organizations. The youth belonging to Other Backward Classes community in the state are covered under various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Individual) implemented under Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
The government was considering setting up the Sant Narhari Maharaj Adhuri Vikas Nigam (Subsidiary) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for greater upliftment of the Sonar Samaj for its overall development with the benefits of the Debt Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Debt Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme). Accordingly, the Corporation (Subsidiary) was constituted on the date of M. The decision was taken at a cabinet meeting.
Pursuant to this decision, the Government of Maharashtra has approved the establishment of a Sub-Company "Sant Narhari Maharaj Economic Development Corporation" under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation vide Government Decision dated 01.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government Decision dated 01.10.2024 under reference is being superseded by this Government Decision.
b) The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the social development of Sonar community belonging to Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the social upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation was named "Sant Narhari Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)."
