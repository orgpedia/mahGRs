Preamble:- The Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation has been established under the decision of the relevant Government for the economic upliftment and development of the Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes. The board of directors of the corporation shall not be less than 3 or more than 15. Accordingly, the Board of Directors has been constituted as follows: 1. Ms. Minister of Social Affairs- President.
2. m. Minister of State, Social Welfare- Deputy Chairman.
Secretary, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune- Director.
Director, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Managing Director, Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation, Mumbai.
