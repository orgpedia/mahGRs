As of FY 2021-22, the Central and State share for pre-matric scholarship for OBC students was 50:50 and for DNT students, the Central and State share was 75:25 and for OBC students, the Central share was 100%.
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India has issued revised guidelines vide letter dated 05.05.2022 for the period from 22.02.2021 to 2025-26 under the Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for students belonging to Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes. Under the scheme, the distribution of benefits under the scheme for the students of DNT category has been done in the form of pre-matric and post-matric scholarships with the central share being 60% and the state share being 40% from the academic year 2022-23. With the change in the format of the scheme, a new title for the pre-matric and post-matric scholarship scheme has been announced. The approval of the Principal Accountant General, Mumbai has been obtained and accordingly, the plan data and computer data have been taken for the scheme. The matter was under consideration of the government.
For the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Pre-Matric and Post-Matric Scholarship for DNT category students, the Central share has been revised to 60% and State share to 40%. Approval is being given for opening of new head of accounts under ZG-3, head 2225. Details of the new title are as follows:
