2) Letter dated 17.03.2023 from the Collector and Chairman, Vasantrao Naik Tanda Vasti Sudhar Yojana, Nashik No. 1023.
Introduction: In the state of Maharashtra, many castes and tribes of nomadic tribes are still living as nomadic migrants. There are many groups of tandas/ hamlets such as Laman, Banjara, Dhangar, Pardhi etc. in which people of these castes/ tribes have been living for many years but such tandas or hamlets do not have access to basic facilities and are inclined towards modernity, but the majority of the society is living in poverty. For this, it is necessary to provide facilities to this community in tandas, wadis or hamlets so that they can lead a stable life. Accordingly, for the implementation of Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme at Wacha, No. Detailed instructions have been issued under the government decision of 1. Accordingly, the proposal of the works recommended by the District Level Committee headed by the Collector and Chairman Vasantrao Naik Tanda/ Vasti Sudhar Yojana District Level Committee, Nashik. The government has received 2. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval and disbursement of funds to the works in the said proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, administrative approval has been given to 9 works (as per Annexure-A) in Nashik district recommended by the District Level Committee and Rs. 85.00 lakhs (Rupees Eighty Five Lakhs only) is hereby approved by the Government.
This fund should be spent from the provision mentioned in the Government Decision, Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No.-Imaw-2003/ PR No. 205/ Mawk-3, dated June 7, 2003 and approved in the year 2023-24 as per the order/ circular issued by the Government from time to time in this regard.
