Date: July 25, 2023.
Read: 1) Government Circular, Finance Division No. 2023/ No. 40/ Finance-3, D. 12th April, 2023) Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation vide letter No. Emaviv/ 2023-24/ Accounts/ 481, Dt. As of June 30, 2023
Government Decision: Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSEDCL) has been provided with 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head 2225 E 857 (Subsidiary Grants (Compulsory) for the financial year 2023-24 to meet the expenditure on management and administration. A budgetary provision of Rs. 10,67,36, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Thirty Six Thousand only) has been made.
Out of the said provision, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) amounting to Rs. 1,06,73, 600/- (Rupees One Crore Six Lakh Seventy Three Thousand Six Hundred only) are being approved to be disbursed subject to the following conditions, subject to a ceiling of 10% i.e.
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented.
Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery rate.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7. While spending the above funds, the concerned officers should make use of the funds by complying with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations etc.
8. Reference No. 1 Every condition of the circular mentioned herein shall be complied with by the Corporation.

As per the circular, it will be mandatory to provide the salary grant through ECS in the bank account of the officer/ employee. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
The Deputy Secretary (Budget), Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer in respect of the above grants. Accountant (Cash Branch), Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai have been announced to collect the amount. The payment in the form of 44 (A) of M.C.N. 44 is being authorized to be disbursed to the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Limited), Mumbai. Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Limited), Mumbai Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) disbursed under the said Government Decision are unconditional Subsidiary Grants.
