The Maharashtra Academy of Research, Upliftment and Training (AMRUT) (AMRUT) has been set up under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the Public Trust Act, 1950 (Bombay Act 29 of 1950).
Amrit Institute A total of 13 schemes have been approved in the Board of Governors meeting held on 13/3/2023. Also, since some more schemes will be included in the coming time, the tender process will have to be implemented in the near future to implement all those schemes. Also, some items will have to be purchased for the regular office work of AMRUT. Department of Industry, Energy and Labour has been entrusted with the procurement of power for the office work of AMRUT and for the implementation of the scheme implemented by the institute.

Government's decision:
