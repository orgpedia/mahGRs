The Proposal:
There are many castes and tribes in the category of nomadic tribes in the state of Maharashtra, who are still living a nomadic life. Although there are many groups of tandas/ hamlets such as Laman, Banjara, Vanjari, Dhangar, Pardhi and people of these castes/ tribes have been living in such tandas for many years, such tandas or hamlets do not have access to basic facilities and are inclined towards modernity, but the majority of this category of society is living in poverty. In order to ensure a stable life for the community, it is necessary to provide them with the necessary infrastructure in the place where the community is currently living, be it in a tandas, wadis or hamlets. Accordingly, for the implementation of Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme at Wacha, No. Detailed instructions have been issued under the government decision of 1. Accordingly, the proposal of the works recommended by the District Level Committee headed by Collector and Chairman Vasantrao Naik Tanda/ Vasti Sudhar Yojana District Level Committee, Dharashiv, was approved. The government has received 2. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval and disbursement of funds to the works in the said proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme, administrative approval has been given to 19 works (as per Annexure-A) in Dharashiv district recommended by the District Level Committee and Rs. 1.00 crore (Rupees One Crore only) is hereby approved by the Government.
