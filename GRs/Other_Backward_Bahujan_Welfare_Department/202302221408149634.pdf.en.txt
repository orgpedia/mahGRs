Preamble: As per the notification dated 09.03.2017 of the General Administration Department, the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department has been constituted and it is implementing schemes separately for Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes.
With the new establishment of the department, it is necessary to publicize and publicize the various schemes implemented by the department. For this purpose, the Department of Social Justice has a separate title under the program for the advertisement and publicity of these schemes. However, after the department of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare became independent from the department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, there is no separate account title under the office for the advertisement and publicity of the schemes of this department. Accordingly, a new head of account 2225F693 has been opened for implementing various schemes under the department. The opening has been approved as per the informal reference dated 22.11.2022. Therefore, the government was considering to implement the schemes of this department through various media.
Government Decision With the newly formed Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, it is necessary to publicize and publicize the various schemes implemented by the department. The publication will be disseminated through various mediums such as (a) Audio/ Video Visuals, (b) Print Media, (c) Events and BTL activity and (d) Digital Media. While making such publications, mainly through the creation of audio/ video about the schemes and its dissemination through radio, television, etc. newspapers, advertisements, interviews, as well as public programs on the information about the schemes, seminars, seminars, exhibitions, lectures, mobile vans, putting up of hoardings, LED screens and tableaux, as well as the programs related to the publication are being approved.
For the advertisement and publicity of the schemes of the department, the registered organizations with specific experience and professional qualification in the field of advertisement on the panel of the Directorate of Information and Public Relations or the organizations on the Gem Portal of the Central Government should carry out the publicity campaign by selecting the organizations to advertise the department for various media by adopting the e-tendering process.
