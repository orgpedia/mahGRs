The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Gawli community. The Government was contemplating to set up "Shrikrishna Adhuri Vikas Nigam (Subsidiary)" as a subsidiary under Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited) for the overall development of the Gawli community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme) under Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited) to the youth of the Gawli community belonging to category B nomadic tribes of the State. Accordingly, the Corporation (Subsidiary) was incorporated on the date 10.10.2024. The decision was taken in the cabinet meeting.
Pursuant to this decision of the Hon'ble Cabinet meeting, the formation of a Sub-Company "Shri Krishna Economic Development Corporation" under Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation has been approved vide Government Decision dated 15.10.2024 in this regard.
The government was considering making some amendments in line with this government decision.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government Decision dated 15.10.2024 under reference is being superseded by this Government Decision.
b) The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the Gawli community under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati Bimukt Tribe Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of the Vimukt Jati and Vimukt Tribe communities in the State. The corporation will be called "Shri Krishna Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be run under the control of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation.

2. Functions of Shri Krishna Economic Development Corporation: -
1. To work for the welfare and development of the Gawli community in the state.
2. To provide loans for self-employment at low interest rates to the Gawli community and its AGC.
3. To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the Gauli community.
4. To provide such services as may be deemed necessary for the manufacture, assembly and supply of agricultural products, commodities, materials and inputs to the grass-roots community.
5. To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of the Gawli community in the State and to prepare reports for the scheme.
6. Implementation of schemes approved by the Government on the lines of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation.
