Government circular:
As per the Government Decision No. 2020/ PR No. 01/ Scheme-5, dated 29.11.2022, 72 Government Hostels with a capacity of 100 students at 1 for boys and 1 for girls at each district for students pursuing post-matriculation vocational education belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes in the State have been constructed by taking on rent buildings at 36 district locations, through Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 and the Societies Registration Act, 1860. G.O.) and Rs. An amount of Rs. 73.81 crore has been sanctioned. However, instead of allowing NGOs registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 and the Societies Registration Act, 1860 to run hostels, the government has rented out the hostel building to itself and allowed it to operate 72 hostels, two each for 36 districts, with a ceiling of 200 per district, for 100 boys and 100 girls belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.02.2023.
2. The matter of how to submit a proposal for starting district-wise hostels and to determine the guidelines and rules in view of the arrangement of hostels was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the guidelines and rules as per Annexure 1 to 10 are being prescribed and action should be taken accordingly for running the hostels.
