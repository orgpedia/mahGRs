The government is considering setting up "Agri Samaj Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary)" as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MASBDC) to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the youth through various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
It was.
The Government's Decisions:
a) The Government is approving the setting up of an Economic Development Corporation for the socio-economic development of Other Backward Classes (OBCs) under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes in the State. The corporation will be called "Agri Social Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be operated under the control of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
b) The details of the structure, functions, schemes to be implemented of the Agri-Society Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
