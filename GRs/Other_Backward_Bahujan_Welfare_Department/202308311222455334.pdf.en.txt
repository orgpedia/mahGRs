Preamble: In pursuance of the decision of the Government in this regard, the Department of Education, Education and Insurance, dated 4/9/2019, has decided to provide education to the students of Dhangar community in English medium residential schools in the cities. As per the provision mentioned in paragraph (4) (a) of the said Government Decision, the schools proposed under the nominated scheme are to be examined in accordance with the inspection form in Form A (1) and Form A (2) and in Annexure B in Annexure A with the Government Decision and the facilities and standards in the concerned school are to be determined on the basis of the criteria in Annexure A with the Government Decision and the fees are to be determined according to the marks obtained. It has also been decided to conduct the ranking of the schools nominated under this scheme every year before the start of the new academic year and to decide the fees to be paid for that academic year at the government level. The decision was taken on 4/9/2019. Accordingly, the government was considering to approve the annual fee per student to be determined on the basis of the criteria prescribed for the schools recognized under the said scheme, as well as to determine the procedure for determining the educational fee and the procedure for distributing the educational fee.
Government's decision:
As per the Government Resolution, Department of Education, Education& Welfare, dated 4/9/2019, it has been decided to provide education to the students of Dhangar community in English medium reputed residential schools in the cities. As per the provision mentioned in paragraph (4) (a) of the said Government decision, the schools proposed under the nominated scheme shall be notified in accordance with the Government decision in Form-A (1) and Form-A (2) in Annexure A and in the inspection form in Annexure B.

After conducting the inspection, the provision has been made to determine the fee according to the facilities and standards in the concerned school by scoring on the basis of the criteria in Annexure-C with the government decision. It has also been decided to conduct the ranking of the schools nominated under this scheme every year before the start of the new academic year and to decide the fees to be paid for that academic year at the government level. The decision was taken on 4/9/2019. Accordingly, it is being approved under this Government decision to sanction the annual fee per student to the schools recognized under the said scheme based on the criteria prescribed by the respective schools as determined on the basis of the marks obtained annually.
2. In order to maintain uniformity in the management of designated residential schools, the following procedures should be followed in the matter of fee determination and fee distribution:
(a) Procedure for determining the fee:
(9) The Assistant Commissioner of Social Welfare concerned should visit the schools approved under this scheme to provide education to the students of Dhangar community in the English medium residential schools in the cities and score them on the basis of the criteria in Annexure-C with the decision of the government according to the facilities and standards in the respective schools.
(2) Considering the number of students admitted to the school directly from among the students approved under the nominated scheme (by verifying the Aadhaar of the students) and the marks obtained by the school, the amount of educational fee payable to the concerned school should be determined and a proposal regarding the same should be submitted to the concerned Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare (Regional) Department by the end of the month of July 15.
(3) The Regional Deputy Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare (Regional) Department concerned shall examine the proposal and submit it to the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, M.R., Pune by the end of July.
(4) The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, M.R., Pune shall examine the said proposal and approve the same. be submitted to the government by August 20.
(b) The procedure for payment of fees:
(1) The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Maharashtra, Pune has given a proposal regarding the distribution of funds permissible to the concerned schools. be submitted to the government by August 20.
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, M.R., Pune shall disburse the fund to the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer-Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare.

3) Funds should be distributed to the nominated schools in two installments as follows:
50% of the total amount to be disbursed to the concerned schools in the first installment should be disbursed in the month of August after the student's admission is confirmed at the beginning of the academic year.
The remaining 50% of the amount in the second installment should be disbursed in the month of December after checking the actual attendance of the students admitted in the respective school.
