Read: - Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare Department, Government Decision Serial No., dated 04th March, 2025
The Proposal: -
The formation of a subsidiary "Param Pujya Ganganath Maharaj Economic Development Corporation" for the Nathpanthi community under the state's main company, Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, was approved by the relevant government decision. Accordingly, the Subsidiary was incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, dated 30/05/2025. However, the name of the subsidiary has received objections from various organisations. Also, the name of this subsidiary will be changed to Shri Shiv Gorakshanath Nathpanthi Samaj Adhuri Vikas Nigam. " Date of the Council of Ministers
The approval has been given in the meetings on 17/07/2025. Accordingly, a change in the name of the subsidiary was under the consideration of the government.
Government Corrigendum: -
The name of the corporation mentioned in the Government Decision, dated 04/03/2025 under reference is "Shri Shiv Gorakshanath Nathpanthi Samaj Economic Development Corporation."
