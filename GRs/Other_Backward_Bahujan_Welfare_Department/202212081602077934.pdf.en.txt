Government Decision: Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (MSCBCDFC) has been sanctioned a sum of Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 102194.00 thousand (Rupees Ten Crore Twenty One Lakh Ninety Four Thousand only) has been made.
The Department of Finance has approved allocation of funds to 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under Managerial Grant to the officers/ employees of the Corporation within the limit of 65% of the original budgetary provision. This amount has also been made available on the BIMS system through the Finance Department.
50% of the budgeted amount of Rs. 51097.00 (Rupees Five Crore Ten Lakh Ninety Seven Thousand only). The same has been distributed to the Corporation vide Government Resolution dated 08/09/2022.
Now the remaining 15% is capped at Rs. A 36-Additional Grant (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1,53,29, 100/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Five Lakhs Thirty Nineteen Thousand Hundred only) is being approved to be disbursed subject to the following conditions.
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented.
Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery rate.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7. While spending the above funds, the concerned officers should make use of the funds by complying with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations etc.
