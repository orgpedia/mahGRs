The Proposal: -
For the past many years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the socio-economic development of the Bari community. The Bari community, which is included in the Other Backward Classes of the state, is mainly in the business of producing betel leaves, betel leaves, and mustard. The government was considering setting up the Sant Shri Ruplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (SSREDC) as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Bari community by providing the benefit of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) to the youth of Bari community residing in all the districts of the state.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the setting up of Additional Development Corporation for the socio-economic upliftment of Other Backward Classes of Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is functioning at present. The corporation will be called "Sant Shri Rooplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be managed by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The details of the composition, functions, and schemes of the Sant Shri Rooplal Maharaj Economic Development Corporation are as follows: -
