The Proposal: -
To implement special programmes for the development of "Goan" community belonging to Special Backward Classes on the lines of ongoing schemes for Scheduled Tribes by the Tribal Development Department. The decision has been taken in the cabinet meeting dated 29.04.2025.
Accordingly, approximately 6000 students belonging to the "Gowari" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes are provided education from Class I to Class XII in reputed residential schools of English medium every year through Reference No. 2 hereby approved by the Government Decision dated 14.05.2025.
Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Pune has issued a notification in this regard vide Notification No. 4 In a letter dated 17.10.2025 mentioned herein, Radhaswamy has submitted a proposal to the Government along with prescribed forms (total score obtained by the school -82) to approve the admission of Goan community students to this school by selecting Adarsh Sanskar Vidyalaya and Minimum College, Pipla Nagpur run by various executive educational institutions under the said scheme. Accordingly, Radheswamy various executives for admission of Goan community students in reputed English medium residential school.
The school has been selected as Adarsh Sanskar Vidyalaya and Minimum College, Pipla Nagpur, run by the educational institution, and has been designated as a Special Backward Class Goan School for the academic year 2011 2026-27.
