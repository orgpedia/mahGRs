Preamble: - For the social and economic upliftment of the community of Kunbi and Other Backward Classes in Konkan Division, Reference No. Shamrao Paige Konkan Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation has been set up as a subsidiary under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation by a Government decision. Also, Reference No. The Board of Directors of these Corporations consists of 6 non-official members, including the Chairman, Vice-Chairman and 7 other Directors, as per the Government Corrigendum No. 2. At present, the government was considering appointing 3 non-governmental members to the board of directors of these corporations.
Government Decision: - The following non-official members are being appointed on the Board of Directors of Shamrao Paige Konkan Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (Subsidiary Company) under Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
1. Shri Prakash Pandurang Bhangrath, Palghar 2. Shri. Suresh Magar, Raigad 3. Shri. Sunil Gopal Nagle, Ratnagiri
