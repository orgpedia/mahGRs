February 23, 2023.
The Proposal: The Cabinet in its meeting dated 27.09.2022 has approved to start 72 Government Hostels with 100 students capacity at each district level with 1 for boys and 1 for girls in 36 districts with rented buildings for students pursuing post-matric vocational education belonging to Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes in the State. Accordingly, the Government vide Decision No. Std. 2020/ S.No. 01/ Scheme-5, D. Issued on 29.11.2022. Since the scheme was new, the matter of opening a new account was under consideration.
The government has decided to start 72 government hostels in 36 districts with a capacity of 100 students in each district for students pursuing post-matric vocational education in the Other Backward Classes, Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes categories. The opening of new sub-headings under ZG-3, Prime Heading 2225, is being approved.
