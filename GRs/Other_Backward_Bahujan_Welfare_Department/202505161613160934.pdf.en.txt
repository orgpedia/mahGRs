The Proposal: -
Under the Government Decision dated 15.10.2024 under the context of this Department, the implementation of special programmes for the development of the "Goan" community in the Special Backward Classes on the lines of ongoing schemes for the Scheduled Tribes by the Tribal Development Department has been approved subject to the concurrence of the Finance Department. The modalities for implementation of the scheme, the eligibility criteria for beneficiaries of the scheme and the conditions for providing 15% margin money to the eligible new entrepreneurial beneficiaries belonging to the "Goan" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes under this special programme, have been laid down. The decision has been taken in the cabinet meeting dated 29/04/2025. Accordingly, Baba was under the consideration of the government to implement the scheme.
The Government's Decisions:
The scheme, Stand Up India of the Central Government, provides for providing a maximum margin money of 15% out of 25% of the total project cost to these AIH beneficiaries through Front End Subsidy, as "Goans," a special backward class beneficiary eligible for ST benefits in the state of Maharashtra, do not have the capacity to pay the margin money.
Under the scheme, AIST, which is eligible to avail the concession from the "Goans" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes in the state of Maharashtra, will pay 10 per cent on its own and after B Bank approves a loan of 75 per cent to the applicant under the WS Up India scheme, the remaining 15 per cent will be provided by the state government as Front End Subsidy.
2.1 The benefit of this scheme will be extended to the eligible beneficiaries belonging to the "Goans" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes of the State who are eligible under the Stand Up India Scheme.
The concerned beneficiaries are required to submit an application to the Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare in the return prescribed in Annexure "A." The application must be accompanied by a Udyog Aadhar registration card, caste certificate, and a bank loan approval letter.
2.3 The said amount shall be disbursed by the Assistant Director, Other Backward Classes Bahujan Kalyan within 15 days in the beneficiary's loan account (NEFT / RTGS).

3. To decide on the selection of beneficiaries under the scheme, a committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of each Collector as follows: -
the Chairman of the Collectorate
Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Member
General Manager, District Industries Centre Member
Manager, District Leading Back Member
Regional Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare: Member
Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare: Member Secretary
The proposals received by the committee headed by the Collector should be submitted to the government for approval for taking a final decision on the beneficiary selection. The demand for funds should be made by the Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare to the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
4. The Deputy Secretary, Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare Department, is being declared as the Controlling Officer. After receiving the demand for funds from the Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, the required funds will be disbursed to the Assistant Directors, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare of the respective districts. The concerned Assistant Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer at the district level for the implementation of the scheme.
5. The ASM accounting process for the scheme will be carried out separately.
6. Committee to be constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary / Principal Secretary / Secretary, OBC Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai to review the scheme from time to time. The committee will consist of Additional Chief Secretary / Principal Secretary / Secretary (Industries), Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Ministry of Power, Mumbai, as well as representatives from SIDBD.
7. The said Government Decision is being issued as per the concurrence received under No. No. 05/2025, dated 19.04.2025 of the Department of Finance.
The decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra's www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
