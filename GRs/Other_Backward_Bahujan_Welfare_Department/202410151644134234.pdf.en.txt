The Proposal: -
For the past several years, there has been a demand from the public representatives, various organizations to form a separate corporation for the social development of the Lohara community. The Lohara community, which is classified as a nomadic tribe-B in the state, is mainly into the business of manufacturing iron goods.
The government was considering setting up the Brahmalin Acharya Divyananda Puriji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary) as a subsidiary under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Vimukt Tribe Development Corporation to strive for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Lohara community by providing the youth of the Lohara community with the benefits of various schemes (Bij Bhandal Yojana, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme) implemented under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the establishment of an Economic Development Corporation for the social development of the Lohara community belonging to Other Nomadic Tribes-B category under the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, which is presently functioning for the social upliftment of the Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes in the State. The corporation will be called "Brahmalin Acharya Divyananda Puriji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation (a subsidiary of Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation)." The subsidiary will be run by the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati B Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation. 2. Functions of Brahmalin Acharya Puriji Maharaj Economic Development Corporation:
To work for the welfare and development of the blacksmith community in the state.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment at low interest rates to persons belonging to the blacksmith community.
To provide credit, resources and technical and managerial tools for improving the economic condition of the Lohara community.
To provide such services as may be deemed necessary for the manufacture, assembly and supply of agricultural products, articles, materials and materials to the blacksmith community.
To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of the blacksmith community in the State and to prepare reports for the scheme.
To implement schemes approved by the Government on the lines of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
