The Proposal:
The demand for the formation of a separate corporation for the social and economic development of the Gurav community has been made by the people's representatives and various organizations for the last several years. The Gurus, who belong to the Other Backward Classes in the state, are mainly in the business of managing temples. The Government was contemplating to set up Sant Kashiba Gurav Yuva Arthik Vikas Nigam (Subsidiary) as a subsidiary company under Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for the overall development and economic upliftment of the Gurav community by providing benefits of various schemes (Seed Capital Scheme, Direct Loan Scheme, Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Educational Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, Skill Development Training Scheme, Women Self-Employment Interest Repayment Scheme) being implemented under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
The Government has approved the establishment of Sant Kashiba Gurav Yuva Arthik Vikas Nigam for the social and economic development of the Gurav community included in the Other Backward Classes under the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation, which is currently working for the social and economic upliftment of the Other Backward Classes in the state.
The corporation will be called" Sant Kashiba Gurav Yuva Arthik Vikas Nigam (a subsidiary of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation)." The Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation will manage the project. Details of the structure, functions and schemes of Sant Kashiba Gurav Youth Economic Development Corporation (Subsidiary) are as follows:

Functions of Sant Kashiba Gurav Youth Economic Development Corporation: 1. To work for the welfare and development of the Gurav community in the State.
2. To provide and recover loans for self-employment to the people of Gurav community at low interest rates.
To provide financial resources, tools and technical and managerial tools to improve the economic condition of the poor community.
4. To provide such services as may be deemed necessary for the manufacture, supply and maintenance of agricultural products, articles, materials and materials to the community.
To initiate and promote schemes for the welfare of Gurv community in the State and to prepare reports for the schemes.
To implement the schemes approved by the Government on the lines of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
