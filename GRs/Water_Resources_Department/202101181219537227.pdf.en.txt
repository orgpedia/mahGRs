The Government of Maharashtra

Date: January 18, 2021
Government circular
Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A Cadre Officers of Water Resources Department. The provisional seniority list as on 01.01.2021 is annexed herewith as" Annexure-A." The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
All the Heads of Departments of Water Resources Department are hereby informed to bring the matter to the notice of all the officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture). The officers belonging to the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), if there are any objections/ suggestions regarding the said seniority list, they will be given. It should be submitted to the government along with the feedback of the concerned department heads by 16.02.2021.
In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
