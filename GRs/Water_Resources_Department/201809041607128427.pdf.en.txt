Jalgaon is situated on the banks of the Sonad river. The Sonad River is a major tributary of the Waghur River. Due to the flood in the Sonad river, a large number of puddles are being formed on the banks of the river. This can pose a flood risk to mosques and some people living along the river. This work should not endanger the life and property of the mosque and some people due to the discharge of the flood of the Sonad River, so this work is demanded by the Gram Panchayat Shendurni, Ta. Jamner passed resolution No. 12, d. 1.5.2015 and Resolution No. 4/ 3, Dt. This was done on 27.11.2015.
