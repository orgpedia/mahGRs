The Proposal:
The government has taken a decision. The Canal Advisory Committee has been reconstituted with effect from 18th March, 2016. As per this decision, the permanent office bearers have been included in the Canal Advisory Committee as government and non-government members. However, at the regional level, there is a demand to invite people associated with water planning as special invitees to the meeting, according to the time and place of the area. In this regard, the names of special ministers are not required to be approved at the government level for frequent project-wise/ season-wise meetings at the regional level. However, the matter was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Necessary persons/ entities related to irrigation management and water planning may be invited to the Canal Advisory Committee meetings to be held project-wise/ season-wise.
The invitation was extended by the Member Secretary, Canal Advisory Committee. Special invitees to be given to individuals/ institutions/ entities with the approval of the President.
