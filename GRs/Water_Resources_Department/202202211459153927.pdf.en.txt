Introduction In the current COVID-19 pandemic situation, there are difficulties in conducting training camps. Accordingly, GoToWebinar and GoToTrainig, a software proposed by Water Resources e-Governance Board, Pune for conducting various trainings and meetings of the department through online platforms, were approved by the e-Governance Implementation Committee for a period of 1 year. Various officers under the Water Resources Department have taken advantage of this system for training and webinars. The online webinar enabled the lecturers to deliver lectures at their respective locations and the knowledge and experience gained by the department officials.
With the help of this system, recording of all the webinars has become easy and they have been uploaded on the department's website as well as YouTube channel after the lecture. With the help of this system, all the trainees can be tested online after training and their online training certificates are also generated in this system and automatically delivered to their registered e-mail address. This system is very user friendly.
At present, the 1-year license of the above systems has expired on 08/11/2021. However, the renewal of the license of these systems for 1 year is Rs. Administrative approval for the proposal worth Rs 6,00,591/- was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the decision to grant administrative approval has been taken as follows.
Government's decision
Considering the utility and scope of the system for the Water Resources Department, the license of these systems is required to be renewed for 1 year. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 6,00,591/- (Rupees Six Lakh Five Hundred and Ninety One only).
