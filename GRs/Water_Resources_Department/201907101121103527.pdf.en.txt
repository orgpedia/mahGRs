As per the provisions of the Government Resolution, dated 15.10.1992, the offices under the control of this Department have been allowed to operate. Considering all these aspects, the government was considering the creation of a" Land Acquisition Cell" under the Corporation to provide posts for land acquisition, rehabilitation, etc. works under the Water Resources Department. As a result, the following decisions are made:
The government's decision:
The five corporations are Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune, Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur and Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad.
1) 5 posts of Deputy Engineer cadre under the Corporation should be dedicated and an officer of the rank of Deputy Collector in the Revenue Department should be appointed on deputation to that post.
The Appointment No. The action should be taken as per the provisions of the government decision of the General Administration Department at 9 and 10.
The pay scale of the post will be equivalent to the post of Deputy Engineer as per S-20, 56100-177500/-. There will be no financial burden.
3) The Administrative Controller of this post shall be the Executive Director in the office of the Corporation.
For the Land Acquisition Cell, in addition to the post of Deputy Collector, the services of retired Tehsildar (1 post) and retired Top Clerk (2 posts) should be made available through contractual mode.
Retired Tehsildar and Senior Clerk No. 11 Action should be taken according to the provisions of the Government Decision of the General Administration Department here.
The services of Junior Clerk (1 post) and peon (1 post) for the Land Acquisition Cell should be made available by the Corporation from its available manpower.
6) The proposal will not entail any financial burden other than the posts to be appointed by contract.
7) The expenditure should be met from the approved grant permissible to the Corporation.
The duties and responsibilities of the post will be as follows: 1. Preparation and preservation of accounts of land acquired by the Corporation for various major, medium and minor projects.
2. Update the records of changes in the' D' sheet of all the acquired lands and prepare a detailed account in this regard and save it.
3. Keeping an account of the lands acquired earlier for various projects under the corporation and updating and preserving the accounts of the lands given on lease basis or to other government departments.
