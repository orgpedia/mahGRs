Circular:
The last date of seniority of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Electrical& Mechanical) Cadre of Water Resources Department is 01.01.2018. 2 has been issued by the Government here. Now, the reference numbers of the provisional seniority of this cadre as on 01.01.2019, 01.01.2020, 01.01.2021 and 01.01.2022 have been published. 1 These are being published under Annexure-A, B, C and D as per the instructions given here under the Government Circular.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been mainly adopted in the preparation of the said provisional seniority.
All the concerned Head of Offices are requested to bring this seniority to the notice of all Assistant Engineers Grade-II (Electrical& Mechanical) (including officers on leave/ deputation). Further, if any genuine errors/ omissions are found in this seniority list, they will be considered as finalization of the said temporary seniority of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Electrical and Mechanical) category, assuming that they are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular.
