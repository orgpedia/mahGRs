GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: APMU 2021/ PR No. 97/ A (Ministry)
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Department of Water Resources (DWR) has decided to revise the establishment pattern of the Ministry. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. It has been approved in the meeting dated 6.6.2003. Accordingly, the approval of revised figure of 566 posts has been ordered vide Reference No. 1 here. issued vide Government Resolution dated 16.07.2003. Subsequently, 19 posts out of 566 posts have been abolished under the Government decision in A.No. 4 and 7 under reference. The sanctioned strength of the department is 547. Out of 547 posts, 203 temporary posts have been posted at 29 No. As per the Government Decision dated 08.03.2022. 01.03.2022 to D. The period has been extended up to 31.08.2022. According to the government decision at reference number 30 of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts. Accordingly, 203 temporary posts in the Water Resources Department (itself) have been given. 01.09.2022 to dt. The Government is granting extension up to 28.02.2023.
