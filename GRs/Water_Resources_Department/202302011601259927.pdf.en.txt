Preamble: The scheme of Nilpana, Mauje Nilpana, under the Patgaon project. Bhudargad is located on the Vedganga river flowing from the east of the village of Kolhapur. On both banks of this dam, the land along the upstream and downstream river banks is of soft layer.
In the year 2019-2021, due to heavy rains and floods, the land on both banks of the dam was washed away, causing extensive damage to the standing crop and posing a threat to the dam. The people's representatives have demanded that the agricultural land should not be further eroded and the river banks and embankments on both banks should be protected from flood water.
According to the blue. A fresh budget has been prepared by conducting a survey at the proposed site in Bhudargad, G. Kolhapur. This is the blue.
The proposal for construction of flood protection wall along the Vedganga river in Bhudargad, Kolhapur district was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government Decision: Mauje Nilpan under Maharashtra Krishna Valley Irrigation Development Corporation, Pune, Ta. For the construction of flood protection wall on Vedganga river at Bhudargad, Dist. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Rate Based Budget of Water Resources Department for the year 2022-23 and Public Works Department for the year 2022-23 with an outlay of Rs. 4,47,44, 008.00/- (Rupees Four Crores Forty Seven Lakhs Forty Four Thousand Eight) subject to the following conditions.
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of Decision No. PUSB-2019/ (Q.S. 115/2019)/ SINVYA (M) dated 31/08/2019.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 21/9/1989 and dt. As per 03/05/2018, the alignment of flood protection wall should be fixed only after the flood line is drawn.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
(d) No technical approval of the work shall be ordered unless the budget of the flood protection work has been approved by the Central Conceptual Organisation.
