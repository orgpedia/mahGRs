GOVERNMENT ORDER: From the lower cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture) to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Group' A' in the pay band of Rs. 56100-177500/- S-20, the 16 engineers mentioned in the prospectus-A are being promoted provisionally from the date they join the place of detailed posting subject to the prescribed terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: (1) The said promotions are being given to the officers to be promoted without prejudice to the seniority of the officers who are senior in service and whose cases are kept open for consideration.
(ii) Promotion to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction) does not mean that the concerned Engineer is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior-most officers shall be demoted on last come first go basis without giving any notice.
(iii) The said promotion is being given subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary proceedings of any kind are going on or proposed to be taken against the engineers mentioned in the return A. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental inquiry is ongoing or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
(iv) Promotion to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture) of the concerned Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture). (v) The powers of the Government to grant detailed post-promotion posting to engineers promoted to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture) are being temporarily delegated to the Executive Director/ Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer concerned in the matter.
Accordingly, the Executive Director/ Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer shall issue detailed posting orders regarding the promotion of the Sub-Divisional Engineer (s) after holding a meeting of the Civil Services Board at his level and considering the following conditions as per the recommendation of the Committee.
The detailed posting of the promoted engineer in the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (STA) should be given to the vacant post in the present situation and should not be given to the post falling vacant due to retirement/ transfer or promotion in future.
The Executive Director/ Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer, while giving detailed posting to the Sub Divisional Engineer (s), should consider the posts given additional charge from a long period in his subordinate office on priority at the time of posting.
Also, to speed up the work under the irrigation backlog, the government has decided to fill up the posts in the offices under the Irrigation Department in Hingoli and Nanded districts. The Governor has given instructions. Accordingly, the action should be taken by the Chief Engineer (Water Resources)/ (Lakshavi), Water Resources Department, Aurangabad.
The Executive Director/ Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer shall take action to provide detailed postings by balancing the vacancies in all the districts within the revenue division allotted to the promoted engineers in their area of work.
If the engineers promoted to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction) do not appear in the promoted post within 1 month from the date of this order, the promotion given to them will be cancelled on the assumption that they are not interested in the promotion. Also, if the promoted engineer refuses the promotion given to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (STA), action will be taken against him as per the government decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.
(vi) These promotions are temporary and subject to the publication of the last seniority in their parent cadre. Therefore, the concerned will not get any rights of seniority in the cadre of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture).
(vii) Engineers who are denied promotion on the ground that the promotion is temporary in nature but gets regularised over a period of time, shall be informed in writing that the benefit of time-bound promotion/ Assured Progress Scheme under the service, if given, shall be withdrawn. If an Assistant Engineer Grade-2 (Architecture) refuses promotion to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture), the report should be submitted to the government immediately.
(ix) The Executive Engineer of the concerned department shall be responsible for relieving the promoted engineers immediately after receiving the detailed posting orders for the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction). If there is a delay in this regard, the concerned executive engineer will be held responsible. The officers who will not appear for the promotion within the stipulated time.
