The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
Several parts of the state are reeling under drought and heavy rains. to take permanent measures to address this situation, and to use available water judiciously.
To prepare the planning plan, to make accurate estimation of water by using modern technology (such as satellite mapping, aerial photography, drone survey, etc.) of all the areas under the control of the state and to prepare permanent solutions and planning plan on this.
The committee will work in collaboration with the World Economic Forum's 4th Industrial Revolution Centre, Mumbai (WEF-4th IRC), Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur, and Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERI), Nashik, to prepare a plan for proper water use and planning.
The committee has been allocated Rs. Financial and administrative powers up to Rs 10 crore are being provided. the state to pay for this
Under the Water Resources Department, the five Irrigation Development Corporations are being empowered to take action to make available an equal amount from the funds.
The Committee shall submit its report to the Government before 31st March, 2020.
