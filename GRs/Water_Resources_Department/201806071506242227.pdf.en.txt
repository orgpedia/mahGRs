The Kharbhoomi scheme of Mankule, Sonkotha-Hashivare Kharbhoomi Yojana Taluka, Alibaug, District Raigad is included in the master plan. The Union Forest Ministry has exempted the scheme under the Coastal Regulation Act, 1991 (CRZ). The total length of the project is 8570 meters. The project is located on the banks of the Dharamtar river. This scheme is necessary because the dam of this scheme is weak and the farmers are incurring losses due to poor opening.
The Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai, has requested the Gram Panchayat, beneficiaries and public representatives to take up the project immediately. The proposal for the combined renovation of two separate projects- Mankule and Sonkotha-Hashivare Kharbhoomi Yojana Taluka, Alibaug and District Raigad- received under Letter No. 2 was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: Mankule, Sonkotha-Hasivare Kharbhumi Scheme, Ta. The administrative approval is hereby accorded to the two combined schemes of Alibag and G. Raigad at an estimated cost of Rs. 967.89 lakhs (Rupees Nine Hundred and Sixty Six Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand only) based on the rates of the Department for the year 2011-12 and Public Works Department for the year 2014-15.
