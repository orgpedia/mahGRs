During the monsoon season of 2021, heavy rains in the Konkan region caused extensive flooding of the Vashishti River, Savitri River, and other rivers. Several districts in the state, including Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg, have been affected by floods. With the flow of rainwater from the Sahyadri mountains year after year, the soil, rocks, boulders, the level of the rivers has increased. Also, there are natural obstacles. The removal of silt islands in the riverbed, simplification of the riverbed, and installation of earthen dams on the river where necessary are urgently needed for disaster relief.
Along with the Chief Minister. 27.10.2021 and dt. Instructions have been issued on 15.12.2021 to take measures on the flood situation as well as to take up works under the District Disaster Management Authority. For this, the work should be done using machinery for the mechanical department of the Water Resources Department. Also, instructions have been received to purchase the necessary machinery (plants) in view of the scope of work. Considering the existing machinery under the Water Resources Department and the works across the state, it is necessary to purchase new machinery. Accordingly, Reference No. The proposal was submitted by the Chief Engineer (Mechanical) under Section 4. Industries, Power and Labour Department, vide Government Resolution No. Bhankhas-2014/ No. 82/ Part-III/ Industries-4 dated 6-01.12.2016, Issue No. As per 2.13, the matter of granting administrative approval for the purchase of plants was under consideration of the government.
