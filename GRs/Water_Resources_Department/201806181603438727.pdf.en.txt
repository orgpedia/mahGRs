The Proposal:
Nagalwadi is a small project built on a local drain near Nagalwadi village on Badegaon Nagalwadi road in Savner taluka and completed in 1971. The length of the dam is 506 metres. The total command area of the project is 1637 hectares, cultivable area is 1364 hectares and the irrigation area of the project is 554 hectares. The project will provide irrigation to the villages of Nagalwadi, Sarra, Bhondetal and Sironji. The project canal was not able to discharge water to its full capacity due to siltation and erosion of the sloping soil and construction work. The distribution system required a lot of repairs due to heavy rains in 2012-13, 2013-14 and 2014-15. The works of dam, canal and distribution system are proposed as per the demand of the people's representatives and the water utility organization, Nagalwadi.
The total area under irrigation is 554. There are 80 of them. Irrigation of irrigated area is done. That is 474. The area is deprived of irrigation. Now, according to this proposal, after the restoration work, the entire area will be submerged. Accordingly, Reference No. 5 In exercise of the powers conferred on the Department by the Government decision herein, the matter of granting administrative approval to the special amendment proposal for the said repair work was under consideration of the Government.
