Government Resolution No. 2018/ 12/ No. 120/2018/ JASNI DI. 26/09/2018.
Government Decision: In the State of Maharashtra Act No. The Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation has been established under the provisions of the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997. The Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 2001. 24/08/2000 has come into effect. As per Section 4 (1) (b) of the said Amendment Act, Shri Jagdish Bhagwan Dhodi, Yashoda Niwas, Dhodi Puja, Navapur Road, P.O. Boisar, T.G. Palghar has been appointed as Vice-Chairman of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane vide Government Order dated 26/09/2018. Accordingly, Shri Jagdish Bhagwan Dhodi, Vice Chairman, Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane is being given the status of Minister of State.
The Vice-Chairperson was appointed by the Government of the Department of Finance vide Decision No. S.A. 10.10/ P.S. No. 96/10/ S.A.O., D. The services provided under 13/03/2012 shall continue to be permitted.
