The Nagpur Municipal Corporation supplies water to the city through two sources, Pench Project and Kolar Kanhan Barrage. The Kolar-Kanhan Barrage is proposed to meet the present and future needs of Nagpur city.
The storage capacity of Kolar Kanhan Barrage is 67.88 Dalgami, out of which 65.71 Dalgami is proposed to be supplied to Kanhan Water Works at present, 44.934 Dalgami to meet the future demand from the barrage and 68.957 Dalgami from Rohana Water Supply Scheme (proposed to reduce the temporary sanctioned lift of 78 Dalgami from Pench Project immediately) to Pench Gorewada Pipeline. To ensure smooth and assured water supply to all these schemes, the work of Kolar-Kanhan Barrage is proposed.
The project is a water supply project and as per the Maharashtra State Water Policy 2019, drinking water is a priority.
Accordingly, the proposal for administrative approval of the remaining work budget for the survey, investigation and preparation of detailed project report of the present scheme was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following resolution was passed:
