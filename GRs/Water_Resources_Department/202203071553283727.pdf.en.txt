Circular:
Reference No. 1 As per the instructions issued under the Government decision herein, subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission of the provisional seniority of the cadre of Senior Geologist and Assistant Geologist of the Water Resources Department dated 01.01.2022, reference No. 3 Government circulars were issued here. If any objections are found in the said seniority list, they are advised to bring the same to the notice of the Government in writing within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular. However, no objections have been received regarding the aforesaid provisional seniority.
Accordingly, the final seniority of Senior Geologist and Assistant Geologist of the department as on 01.01.2022 is being published under Annexure-A and B respectively, as attached subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been mainly adopted in the preparation of the said final seniority.
