Government Corrigendum: Government has revised the fees of Shri. D.M Nagolkar, Advocate on Record for Government of Maharashtra to appear before the Krishna Water Dispute Tribunal vide Government Resolution dated 03/07/2018 under reference. The corrections/ modifications in the said order on page 2 are as below:
1. Modification/Correction No. 1:
"Attending the Tribunal in New Delhi charges per day or part thereof"
should be read as
"Attending the KWDT/ Supreme Court in New Delhi Charges per day or part thereof"
2. Modification/Correction No. 2:
Under the head "conference held for preparation of the
case or Consultation at New Delhi/Mumbai/Pune Charges per hour or part thereof for the revised fees
"Rs. 9000/- Maximum Upto Rs. 75,000/- per day"
should be read as
"Rs. 13,000/- Maximum upto Rs.1,00,000/-per day"
