Introduction: A dam was constructed on the Mula river near Rahuri village in Rahuri taluka in 1974 and the available water during the rainy season is being supplied to irrigation through the right and left canals. The original left canal was built in 1974 and major repairs have not been made since. While irrigating through canals and distributaries, there is a large amount of leakage from the construction, which increases the transportation cost and disrupts irrigation management.
At present, the canal is being irrigated by the beneficiaries and the benefit area is being irrigated as per the Irrigation Act, 2005 by establishing 28 Small Market Level Water Utilization Societies.
