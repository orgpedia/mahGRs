Preamble: It is proposed to prepare a handbook containing the Central Water Commission guidelines with clarity on quality standards to ensure that the entire process of construction, tendering, proposed works and subsequent maintenance under the tube distribution system is carried out smoothly, so that it is possible to take coherent decisions for successful implementation of the tube distribution system at the field level. A committee has been formed to prepare a guidebook in this regard.
Government's decision
The following committees are being constituted to prepare handbooks which will guide the work of survey, exploration, conception, tendering, quality control, construction and maintenance repairs etc. under the tube distribution system.

The committee will have the following functions:
1) Setting standards keeping in mind the concept, the methodology, the use of available software.
2) Setting standards regarding quality control (mainly pipe testing before& after its laying, pipe laying etc.). (3) Preparation of guidelines on maintenance and repair (O& M) of duct distribution system.
4) Preparation of guidelines for the preparation of the budget.
5) The committee shall inspect the site of the construction and make an inspection of the same.
The time period for submission of all inclusive handbook to the Committee shall be 3 months.
