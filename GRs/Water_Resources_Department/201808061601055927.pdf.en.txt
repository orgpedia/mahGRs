The proposal-
The government has adopted a policy to increase the production capacity of agricultural land by removing the silt stored in the old dams in the state and also to desilt the reservoirs of all the dams in the state. Marina-2018/ (Q. No. 137/18)/ Lakshevi (Aastha), Dy. Issued on 03.08.2018. In this Government decision, it is directed that the procedure of e-tendering for the removal of sand/ sand mixed with silt and silt, the terms and conditions of the contract should be fixed by the Irrigation Development Corporations with the approval of the Government. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to constitute a committee to determine the terms and conditions of the contract and prepare a draft e-tender in a standardized format. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Circular No. The Government Decision No. 1 directs that the procedure of e-tendering for the removal of silt and silt mixed sand/ sand from major irrigation projects, the terms and conditions of the contract should be determined by the Irrigation Development Corporation with the approval of the Government.
Accordingly, a committee is being constituted under the Water Resources Department of the State to prepare a draft e-tender in a standardized format with the terms and conditions of the contract for the removal of silt and sand mixed with silt from the large irrigation projects, as specified in the relevant government decision.
