The Proposal:
The creation of all posts in the subordinate zonal office under the Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Uplift Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara) has been fixed as per the revised figure in accordance with the decision of the Government at No. 4 under reference. Reference No. 2 The extension granted to the offices of Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Upazila Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara), on the field establishment, to continue 536 posts on the fixed temporary establishment and 08 posts on the converted temporary establishment from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 has expired on 31.08.2023.
Reference No. 3, under the decision of the Government of Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, 4 divisions and 19 sub-divisions under the Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Upsa Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara) have been given the posts of fixed temporary establishment and converted temporary establishment. 01.09.2023 to dt. The proposal to extend the period up to 29.02.2024 is under consideration of the Government.
