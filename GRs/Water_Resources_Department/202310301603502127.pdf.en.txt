Introduction: Ghataprabha Medium Project, Chandgad, Dist. Kolhapur is located on the Ghataprabha River. Under this project, the main earthen floor lobby will be 1088 m high and 48.30 m wide. The width of the head is 6.50 mm. That's so much. The project is 90 m.
The door is long. The filling up of the project was completed in 2009-10 and since June 2011, the dam has been receiving water at a project capacity of 43.75 LHT. Under the project, a total of 7 crore barrages have been constructed and 6163 hectares of crop area has been created in two taluks of Chandgad and Gadhinglaj.
The original work under Ghataprabha Medium Project has received Second Revised Administrative Sanction at a cost of Rs. 127.66 crore with an outlay of Rs. 94.55 crore. Under the Ghataprabha project, the main dam, land acquisition and rehabilitation, 7 K.P. embankments, etc. are completed and the end weir and spray wall work in Sandwa is pending.
Due to heavy rains in August 2019, the right side of the Ghataprabha project has been flooded with about 85 meters of water. wide and 100 meters. The existing guide wall has collapsed in the energy mitigation area due to landslides in the length of soil and large boulders hitting the guide wall on the right side of the drain.
