Introduction: Jaikwadi Project- Paithan Dam on Godavari river at Paithan in Aurangabad district was completed in 1976. The proposal for obtaining the approval of the Government for the revised balance sheet of Jayakwadi Project as per the 1985 reallocation has been received through a letter addressed to the Executive Director, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad. Accordingly, the Government vide letter No. 2 has been reported. As per the approved project report, 2618.59 Dalghami water has been planned. In the last 42 years of Jaikwadi Project, the actual water use of Paithan Dam has been very different from the original project water use provision and actual water use. From time to time, changes have been made in the water use plan with the approval of the government according to the water requirement. The main changes are as follows:
1. Reduction in the area of benefits of Paithan Right and Left Canals due to high level of embankments on Godavari River.
2. Directly less evaporation than the original project provision of evaporation.
3. Reduction in discharge of water into Majalgaon Dam.
4. New provision for domestic and industrial water use 5. New provision for government lift irrigation schemes from reservoirs.
6. Renewed Sanction of Community and Individual Irrigation Schemes The matter of finalizing the revised balance sheet as per the provision of the above direct government approved water scheme was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION- 1. Approval in person submitted by the Executive Director, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad (Godavari River Basin Agency) as per the changes mentioned above
