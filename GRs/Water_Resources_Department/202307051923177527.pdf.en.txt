2. Travel allowance shall not be admissible to the officers concerned for joining the post of the transferor in pursuance of the transfer request.
3. The date on which the concerned officer was relieved to appear in place of the new appointment and the date on which he appeared in place of the new appointment should be immediately communicated to the Government on the e-mail website doesttclass1.wrd@maharashtra. gov. in.
4. No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned without prior permission of the Government.
Further, as per Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave application of a transferred officer shall be entertained by the parent office. If the leave application of the transferred officer is sent to the parent office, then these applications will not be taken into consideration.
5. It may be noted that in case of failure to report to the place of posting immediately in accordance with the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
