The matter was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
The Chief Engineer, North Maharashtra Region, Water Resources Department, Nashik Subordinate Superintending Engineer and Administrator, Benefit Area Development Authority, Executive Engineer under Ahmednagar, Divisional Office of Kadwa Canal Division, Nashik along with 4 Sub Divisional Offices under it, Superintending Engineer under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule under Dhule for the work of Sulwade Jamfal Kanoli Upasana Irrigation Scheme along with the sanctioned posts under it. It is hereby approved to operate under the following conditions as per the design indicated in Section 2.
(1) The offices of the said Department and Sub-Divisional Offices shall be made functional in the premises of the Government Building. There should be no extra expenditure on construction.
(2) The services of the officers of the sanctioned Group-A and Group-B cadres of the divisional and sub-divisional offices to be transferred shall function at the headquarters of the divisional and sub-divisional offices to be made functional by the transfer.

3) Also, the services of the approved Group-C and Group-D cadre employees of the office to be operated in the relocated place should be made available through the local circle office.
4) The inclusion of the currently working employees of Group-C and Group-D cadre under the Department and Sub-Divisional Office to be transferred should be done immediately in the vacant place of work in the office of the concerned Circle Office.
5) The process of handing over the project works and all the office records (judicial, accounting objections, other cases) related to the division and sub-divisional offices to other division and sub-divisional offices under the board should be done by the respective region/ mandal office.
6) For offices to be made functional after relocation, only the material available in the existing working office should be used. Administrative and administrative costs should not be increased. This will not put any financial burden on the government.
After the shifting (change) of the existing Divisional and Sub-Divisional Offices as above, the office structure will be as follows.
