Preamble
Government of India vide Notification from Ministry of Water Resources
dated 12 March, 2018 has constituted the Mahanadi Water Disputes Tribunal at New
Delhi for adjudication of water disputes regarding the interstate river Mahanadi. For the
work of Mahanadi Water Disputes Tribunal, Shri D. M. Nargolkar, Advocate, Supreme
Court, New Delhi was appointed as Advocate on Record vide Government resolution
dated 04/05/2018. Presently, Shri D. M. Nargolakar is designated as Senior Advocate
in the meeting held on 27th March, 2019 by the full court of the Supreme Court of India.
The proposal of appointment of Advocate on Record for Government of Maharashtra to
appear before Mahanadi Water Disputes Tribunal of Shri Viraj Kadam was under
consideration of Government of Maharashtra.
2.0 Government Resolution :
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to appoint Shri Viraj Kadam,
