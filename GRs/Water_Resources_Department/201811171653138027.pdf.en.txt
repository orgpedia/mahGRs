The concerned Chief Engineer will be empowered to prepare the concept drawings of the project component works from the consultants in this list.
General Instructions for Structural Design Consultants- The Structural Design Consultant who has been selected and included in the list of Structural Design Consultants must act as follows.
1) The concept of various component works under the project in the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana should include details of general layout, structural details, bar bending schedule, material specification, project planning with construction sequence, special requirements and all drawings required for construction.
2) The process of preparing the list of Structural Design Consultants has been done keeping in mind the urgency of the work, so they should take action to provide the concept in a time-bound manner.
3) The design by the Structural Design Consultants should be in compliance with the prevailing and relevant IS Code/ CWC Manual/ CDO Code of Practice/ Guidelines etc.
Reference to specific IS/ CWC Manual/ Guidelines etc. used for conceptualization should be mentioned separately at each place. Accordingly, the details of the assumptions used for the concept and their references etc. should be mentioned in the design note.
4) Structural Design Consultant should provide adequate drawings showing the detailed details of the concept. So that it is easy for the contractor to do the actual work through the drawings. Consultants will be required to be present at the workplace and provide guidance as per the requirement of the job.
5) Consultation Fee: The consultation fee to be paid to the Structural Design Consultant should be decided on the basis of the number of concept notes and drawings. In this regard, the consultation fee should be close to what the Central Design Organisation, Nashik (CDO) charges. The contribution fee to be paid to the Proof Consultant should be a fraction of the fee of the Structural Design Consultant and should not be more than the fee charged by the Central Design Association. However, the aggregate payment charges shall not exceed 1% of the estimated cost of the project component.

In the first step, the data related to the concept should be collected by the structural design consultant. The validation of this base material should be done by the concerned chief engineer.
The design approach for the concept project component based on the validated data should be prepared by the structural design consultant and approved by the concerned chief engineer. The concept prepared and submitted by the structural concept consultant should be first approved by the concerned Chief Engineer. Based on such approved concept, the budget should be given technical approval by the Chief Engineer concerned. After that, the tender for the component work should be called on this basis.
