Government circular
The Department of Water Resources and the Ministry of Water Resources have been informed about the various difficulties in the acquisition of new irrigation projects as well as completed or under construction projects. The Minister receives various demands and representations from the local public representatives. In order to take appropriate decisions and issue such directions to the field offices, it is very important to have complete information about the facts along with the current status of the project.
Accordingly, all the Executive Directors, on receipt of such representations, have furnished the facts, present position and their clear feedback on the project in the memorandum to the Department and to the Managing Director. The Minister needs to be informed. According to the Department and the Necessary proposals are to be sent to the Minister for approval with recommendations and the following directions are being issued in this regard.
1) After receiving the demands/ representations from the local public representatives regarding the acquisition of new irrigation projects, as well as various problems of completed or under-construction projects, the current status of the project should be first assessed at the field level.
2) Thereafter, pre-feasibility check should be carried out by verifying the availability of water to the concerned requisitioned project.
(3) The preliminary proposal should be submitted to the concerned Executive Director by taking action as above and the concerned Executive Director should submit the current status and feasibility of the project in accordance with the representation of the people's representatives, with their clear opinion to the Principal Secretary (G) as well as the Secretary. The Minister should be informed.
