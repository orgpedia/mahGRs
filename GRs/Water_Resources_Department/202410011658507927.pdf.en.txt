The Proposal: -
Sose Shedshall, Ta. Shirol, District Kolhapur experiences a flood situation during the monsoon season. Floods in 2019 and 2021, as well as frequent floods, have caused water from the Krishna basin to leak out of the basin, affecting temples, homes, cemeteries, and urban settlements. Public representatives have demanded the construction of a flood protection wall along the river to prevent damage to the riverbanks, including jackwells, houses, temples, settlements and lanes, roads, shops, and cemeteries due to the frequent flood situation in Pabsalaya. Accordingly, the zonal office has conducted a physical inspection and submitted the budget to the government. The issue of administrative approval of the budget was under consideration of the government.
