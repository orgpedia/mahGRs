The Proposal:
The project is proposed to be taken up under the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation (VIDC) at Borgaon Mohana village in Chandurbazar taluka of Amravati district. The Bordi Nala Medium Project envisages construction of a feeder canal from the Megha River (a tributary of the Purna River) near Pala village to bring water to the Kondawardha Balance Dam and Borgaon Mohana Dam and to provide irrigation through the Borgaon Mohana Irrigation Scheme.
As many as 4126 hectares of irrigated area in 13 villages of Chandurbazar and Achalpur taluks of Amravati district will be brought under irrigation.
Drinking water and evaporation costs will be 2.588 dlh.

The First Amended Administrative Sanction is granted subject to the following conditions:
5.1.The said revised administrative approval means that the approval cannot be taken for irregularities at various stages of project implementation.
5.2) Proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the project should be continued. The decision to grant revised administrative sanction first will not hamper the proceedings of the departmental inquiry.
5.3. New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
5.4. Work on tenders in progress should be completed on priority. The priority of the remaining works should be decided and works/ tenders should be processed accordingly so that the water storage is maximized for irrigation.
5.5 No additional expenditure or disbursement of funds beyond the limit of the First Amendment Administrative Sanction granted for any purpose for the said project. In this regard, the existing government decisions, CWC guidelines, limits on financial powers, prescribed tender procedures should be strictly implemented.
5.6) The State Level Technical Advisory Committee's Scrutiny Report mentioned at Point No. 26 and 27 are required.
5.7) Post facto approval of the Maharashtra Water Resources Regulatory Authority should be taken as stated by the Secretary level Sub-Committee and prompt action should be taken for obtaining approval of the Central Water Commission.
5.8) The project work should be completed as per the plan.
6. The said Government Decision was taken by Planning Department vide Non-Official Reference No.20/ 1434 dated 07/02/2019, Finance Department vide Non-Official Reference No.55/ Expenditure 12 dated 26/02/2019 and D. Held on 27/02/2019. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting.
