Bhima river flows near Solapur village. There is not enough water in the river during the rainy season. Therefore, the villages on both sides of the river face a huge shortage of water for agriculture during this period. In order to overcome this problem, as per the demand to convert the existing Vadapur Co. P. Dam near Vadapur village into a barrage. In the meeting held on 16/9/2016 at Mantralaya, Mumbai for completion of irrigation projects in Solapur district, discussion was held regarding construction of barrages on Bhima and Seena rivers. Instructions have been given about the concept of converting P. Bandhara into a barrage and the presentation of the budget. The Central Concept Picture Organization, Nashik has been instructed to submit a detailed proposal to the government regarding the conversion of South Solapur district into a barrage.
The Vadapur Ko P. Dam is constantly over-flanked and does not store enough water. The embankment needs to be converted into a barrage as there is leakage from the gates and it becomes difficult to remove and put back the gates in flood situations. This is 989.
