Government's decision:
If the water storage under the Water Resources Department is to be used as a source for schemes under the Chief Minister's Rural Drinking Water Program, then water reservation approval from the Water Resources Department is not required. While implementing this decision, it is necessary to follow the instructions of the Government Decision dated 30.1.2018 of the Department of Water Resources. Similarly, the instructions of the Water Resources Department dated 30.1.2018 must be followed while implementing the tap water supply schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority/ Zilla Parishads under the Jal Swarajya-2 and National Rural Drinking Water Programme.
