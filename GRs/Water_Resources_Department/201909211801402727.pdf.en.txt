The Second Revised Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions:
2.1) If the expenditure exceeds the administrative sanction price without the approval of the Government, action should be taken to regularize the expenditure by fixing the liability in the first place.
2.2) Follow the prevailing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial limits, prescribed tender procedures while implementing the project.
2.3) Any financial or procedural irregularity in respect of the project in the preceding period shall not be deemed to have been regularised by this approval.
2.4.The said revised administrative approval does not mean that the technical aspects of the report, the field decisions on the tender or the irregularities have been regularized by this decision.
2.5 No additional expenditure beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for any purpose for the said scheme.
2.6 The proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the scheme will continue. The decision to grant the revised administrative sanction will not hamper the proceedings of the departmental inquiry.
