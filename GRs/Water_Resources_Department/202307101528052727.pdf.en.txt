Reference No. 1 Subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission of the temporary seniority of the cadre of Senior Geologist and Assistant Geologist of the Water Resources Department as per the instructions given under the Government decision herein, dated 01.01.2023, Reference No. 3 Government circulars were issued here. The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been mainly adopted in the preparation of the said interim seniority.
