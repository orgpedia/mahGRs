Introduction: The Central Government has increased the pay scale of All India Services Officers of the Central Government based on the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission. It was amended on January 1, 2016.
Based on the decision of the Central Government, the State Government employees/ officers are entitled to the benefits of the 7th Pay Commission, Government Notification, Finance Department No. Vepur 2019/ PR No.1/ SEWA-9 has been implemented vide notification dated 30th January, 2019. Accordingly, a proposal to implement the revised pay scale as per the Seventh Pay Commission for the Chairman and Members of the Maharashtra Water Resources Regulatory Authority was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In view of the revision of pay and allowances of All India Services Officers by the Central Government, the existing pay scale of Chairman and Members of Maharashtra Water Resources Regulatory Authority (MWRRA) is being revised as per the Seventh Pay Commission.
