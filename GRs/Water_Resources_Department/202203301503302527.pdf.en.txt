Introduction: The Minor Irrigation Project Kone is under the Godavari Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad.
The project is located in Trimbakeshwar taluka of Nashik district on the Kashyapi tributary of Godavari basin near Kone village, 37 km from Nashik. The total live storage available in the project is 2.107 LMT which is 19.60 LMT as per 50% storage capacity of the project. The project was completed in 1985-86. The cultivable area of the project is 215 hectares and the irrigated area is 172 hectares. The right canal is 4 km long and 4 km wide The length is the left. It has been more than 35 years since the completion of the project. Due to flooding of the river channels at the project site, the left and right canal embankments have been damaged and the embankments have been broken at many places. As a result, the left canal has been closed for the last 10 years and the right canal for the last 30 years, causing drinking water and irrigation problems at the project. The construction of the project's canals dates back to 1985 and is in a dilapidated condition as the structures are made of stone and have been in a dilapidated condition for over 35 years. Therefore, as soon as the irrigation area on the project is restored and the water use agency on the project becomes operational, the following repair works have been proposed. Which L.P. Project?
