Introduction: Under the Jambre Medium Project, Taluka Chandgad, District Kolhapur, a 23.23-litre capacity dam is being constructed on Tamraparni, a tributary of Ghataprabha river near Jambre village. The project is under the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation (MKCDDC). A total area of 3773 hectares in Kolhapur district will be benefited by the project.
The original administrative approval for the project was accorded for an amount of Rs. 17.30 crore based on the 1990-91 tariff list as per Reference No.1 of the project for Jambre Medium Project, Taluka Chandgad, District Kolhapur. Thereafter, as per Reference No. 2, the first revised Administrative Sanction was granted for the year 1996-97 based on the Tariff List for an amount of Rs. 66.03 crore. Thereafter, Second Revised Administrative Sanction was accorded for the period 2008-09 at a rate of Rs. 146.77 crore based on Reference No. 3.
The Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune has submitted the third revised administrative sanction proposal of Rs. 182.75 crore based on the 2013-14 schedule to the Government of Maharashtra under reference no. 4. The proposal had been approved by the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: With this decision of the Government, revised administrative approval is being accorded to the Third Revised Project Report of Jambre Medium Project at a cost of Rs. 182.75 crore based on the 2013-14 tariff list. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 176.83 crore is provided for expenditure on works and Rs. 5.92 crore for incidental expenses. Sub-heading wise provisions are being added to Goshwara.
The third revised administrative approval granted to the scheme has been recorded in the Medium Project Operations Register at serial number 7/2019.
The remaining work of the project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the limits of the revised administrative approval.
