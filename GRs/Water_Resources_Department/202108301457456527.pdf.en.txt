Preface: Achalpur, Ta. Achalpur, G. Amravati, to prepare estimates for flood protection work on the Sapan River by conducting an immediate survey of the work. The directions were issued by Minister of State (MoS) for Water Resources and Land Resources Om Prakash alias Bachubhau Kadu. Accordingly, the budgets have been prepared as per the regional situation. A proposal has been received by the Government for grant of administrative approval for works costing Rs. 4,97,46, 100/-. The Sapan river near Achalpur village was flooded in 2007, 2011 and 2018 due to heavy rainfall during the monsoon season. Due to the flood, the Gandhi bridge and the Sri Krishna bridge on the river were submerged. Around 80 to 90 houses in the village, including the primary Urdu Girls School, Kasadpura and Achalpur, were inundated. Also, the river has been slightly eroded by the flood. Therefore, there is a fear of the floodwater to inundate the area/ houses up to a distance of about 700 meters from the Sri Krishna Bridge. There is also a possibility of water entering the houses. To prevent this danger, the construction of a protective wall on the side of the river bed can prevent this danger. So, Achalpur. The matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal of flood protection work on Sapan river in Achalpur, G. Amravati was under consideration of the government.
