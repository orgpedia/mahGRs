The Proposal:
The Purna Nevpur Medium Project is built on the Purna River near the village of Nevpur in Kannad taluka and the construction of the project was completed in 1997. The total irrigated area of the project is ICA 1696 hectares and the right canal is 7 km long. Left and Right Canal 14.7 km. I'm long. The length of the earthen dam of Purna Nevpur Medium Project is 2470 meters, the maximum height of the dam is 16.47 meters and the length of the sewerage is 325 meters.
The mud dam wall of the Purna Newpur medium project is buried everywhere and the approach road is also in disrepair. As water evaporates from the tank, it is lost. Therefore, it is necessary to repair the mud wall of the dam, as well as repair the shandas and guide walls. Also, some work needs to be done to make the necessary system for measuring water at various places on the canal.
Against this backdrop, the government was considering a proposal to give administrative approval under a special amendment to the budget of the Poorna Nevpur medium project in order to carry out the main dam and Sandwa repair work and other works of the canal from the perspective of dam safety.
