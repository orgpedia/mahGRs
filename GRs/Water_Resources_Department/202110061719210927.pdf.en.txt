The Proposal:
Bhima (Ujani) Dam is a dam on the Bhima River. Madha G. Solapur was completed in 1980. Proposal for obtaining Government approval for revised balance sheet of Bhima (Ujani) Project as per its re-planning for the year 2021 has been sent to the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune vide Reference Letter No. 5 is obtained. Reference No. As per the project report approved under Section 4, 2388.09 Dalghami water has been planned. In the last 40 years of the Bhima (Ujani) dam, there has been a huge difference between the original project water use provision and actual water use. From time to time, changes have been made in the water use plan with the approval of the government according to the need for water. Below are the main changes:
1. Provision of water for irrigation from reservoirs and canals.
2. Reduction in the benefit area of upstream right and left canals due to construction of K.P. embankments on river Bhima, river Man and river Seena in the benefit area of the dam.
Increased provision for domestic water use.
The matter of finalizing the revised balance sheet as per the provisions of the received water plan was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
As per the above changes, the balance sheet of the actual sanctioned water use re-planning submitted by the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune is being approved as per" Annexure-A."
2. Ujani project and Bhima, Seena, Man rivers benefit areas to be notified.
