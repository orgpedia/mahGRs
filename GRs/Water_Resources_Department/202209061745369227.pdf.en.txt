Introduction Under the Government decision dated 2 nd March, 2021 of the Finance Department, it has been made mandatory to use the Integrated Finance Management System (IFMS), a self-promoting accounting system, in all the Irrigation Corporations of the Water Resources Department from 1 nd April, 2022.
Accordingly, in order to start the use of this system in the stipulated time, the Water Resources Department, with the consent of the Finance Department, has issued a notification. As per the Government decision dated 28/12/2021, 129 Heads of Departments under Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane Corporation were declared as Disbursing Officers.
