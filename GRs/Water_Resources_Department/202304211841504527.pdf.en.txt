The Proposal:
Reference No. All administrative departments, under the decision of the Government of the Department of Finance, shall be entitled to temporary posts included in their figures as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the figures. The authority has been given to extend the date from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023.
Government's decision:
Now, as proposed under Reference No. 3 and as indicated in Annexure-I and II of the Annexure, the date of appointment of 76 posts of Superintending Engineer, Executive Engineer under Buldana Irrigation Project Board, Buldana, Minor Irrigation Department, Buldana Divisional Office and 1 post of Convertible Temporary Establishment. The period from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023 is being extended subject to the following conditions: A detailed description is given in the attached Annexure.
(1) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be held by the said employee till the time he is employed.
2) The above posts will be in service during the period proposed by the Department.
(3) The temporary posts to be sanctioned shall be in the prescribed format and shall not be vacant/ lapsed for a period of more than six months.
4) The number of posts/ reduction in posts/ increase in posts/ retirement etc. in the proposal made available by the Office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Amravati.
5) The proposal for further extension of all these posts should be submitted in the prescribed form along with the certificate without any delay.
