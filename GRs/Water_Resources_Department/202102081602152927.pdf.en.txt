The committee's mandate is as follows:
To suggest necessary changes in the present system and submit it to the government for computerization to make the field work related to irrigation management easy, smooth and transparent.
2. Approval of System Requirement Gathering Templates (RGT), Software Requirement Specifications (SRS) in consultation with all stakeholders for developing the system.
3. Finalize the module wise required master data for the system.
4. Approval of documents created by the developer at the stage of testing and acceptance of user acceptance of the system (operational/ final acceptance).
To examine the need and feasibility of change in the scope of work in the approved RGT/ SRS or in case of new demand from the field offices, to guide and develop the system accordingly and to conduct a thorough inspection of the system developed.

Committee Meetings and Meetings:
The Committee shall be appointed by the President on the recommendation of the Committee.
Committee meetings shall be held at the direction of the President. Officials of the Water Resources Department and other departments and private individuals can be invited to attend the meetings of the committee. There will be no prerequisites for a committee meeting. The Chairman of the Committee shall ensure that the proceedings are carried out in accordance with the time limit fixed by the Committee.
The tenure of the said Committee shall be for a period of 6 months from the date of the order of constitution of the Committee till the date of implementation of the said system.
