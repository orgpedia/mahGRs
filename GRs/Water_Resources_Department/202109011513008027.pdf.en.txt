Preamble: A total of 55 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and Small Irrigation Cell, Thane under the Corporation have been sanctioned vide reference No. 1 The extension was granted from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 vide Government Resolution dated 08.03.2021.
Accordingly, a total of 55 posts, including 41 posts in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and 14 posts in the office of the Corporation's Internal Minor Irrigation Cell, Thane, have been approved for extension beyond 31.08.2021. A proposal has been submitted for 3. Finance Department Reference No. In pursuance of the Government decision dated 10.08.2021, the following decisions are being taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 55 posts, including 41 posts in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and 14 posts in the office of the Small Irrigation Cell, Thane, under the Corporation, are being sanctioned for extension from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure A.
a) The fixed temporary posts should not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The posts on fixed temporary establishment shall be within the limit of 45297 posts as per the revised figure approved under the Government Decision dated 06.02.2003.
The expenditure on establishment of the above offices shall be met from the grant sanctioned by setting aside the capital expenditure on Demand No. I-5 4701 Large and Medium Projects 80 General 190 Investments in Public Sector and Other Undertakings (04) Share of Capital Contribution (Non-Plan) (04) (03) Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation Share of Capital Contribution (Compulsory) (4701 I-189).
