Government's decision:
On the basis of the result of the Combined Competitive Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai for the recruitment of Assistant Engineer Grade-II in Group-B posts in Maharashtra Engineering (Architecture) Service Examination, 2017, out of 65 candidates recommended for appointment to the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture) vide letter No.374 (2)/ 2017/14-B dated 21.6.2019, 56 candidates who have qualified for appointment have been appointed as Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture) in the pay scale S-15 (Rs. 41800-132300) in the revised pay matrix as per the Seventh Pay Commission, in the Water Resources Department for a period of two years subject to verification as per the decision of the Government No.2. Accordingly, the candidates were directed to appear for training at Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik on 02/03/2020. Accordingly, out of 56 trainees under Reference No. 3 letter, 44 trainees have appeared in person at META, Nashik. Accordingly, Assistant Engineer Grade-2 has appeared for training at Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy on 02/03/2020. Their training is ending on 30th June 2020 and the trainees are being posted as per the Divisional Cadre allocation as under: The date on which the trainees have appeared for training at Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik should be taken as the date of their posting.
