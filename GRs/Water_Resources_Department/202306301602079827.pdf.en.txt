Transfer of Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer/ Assistant Engineer Grade-II.
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources
GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: REPLACED 2023/ S. No. 50/2023/ A (Sh. (A)
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date:- 30/06/2023
Government Order: As per the provisions of section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Government Duties Act, 2005, the Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer/ Assistant Engineer mentioned in the accompanying statement (in column-2) working under the Water Resources Department, Grade-2 cadre engineers are being transferred on request to the place indicated against their names (as per column-4).
The concerned Executive Director/ Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer of the office to which the engineers mentioned in the order have been transferred, shall immediately issue orders for the detailed posting of the concerned engineers as indicated in column-4 of the above order.
The concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer of the office in which the engineer mentioned in the order is working is informed that he should relieve the transferred engineers immediately to join the place of transfer only after the detailed posting orders are issued.
In case of discharge of work without receipt of detailed posting orders and in case of difficulty in execution of orders due to non-availability of vacancies, the engineers concerned have to grant compulsory waiting period. It should be noted that in such cases, the concerned controlling officer will be held responsible.
The concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer is informed that the orders issued at the government level should be implemented first and only then the orders of transfers within the region at the zonal level should be implemented. There should be no change in the government's decision. If there is no vacancy at the place of posting or if there is administrative difficulty in executing the transfer due to other reasons, in such a case, the government should be informed immediately and a report should be submitted.
Also, the date on which the concerned engineer was relieved and the date on which he appeared in place of the new appointment should be informed to the government immediately on the e-mail id doesttsectengr. wrd@maharashtra. gov. in.
The concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer is informed that they should take care to ensure that the Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer/ Assistant Engineer Category-2 shall not be employed in excess of the sanctioned posts under any circumstances in the place of the transfer while implementing the transfer of the concerned Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer/ Assistant Engineer Category-2 as above.
The confidential report, service book and final salary certificate of the junior engineer/ branch engineer/ assistant engineer category-2, who is being transferred as above, should be sent immediately to the new office where the transfer has been made.
7. Since the said transfer is a request for transfer, the transferable travel allowance and other incidental benefits shall not be admissible to the said engineers.
8. Further, the Department of Water Resources, Government Notification, Dt. 25.04.2016 to effect general transfers of Group-B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) cadre officers in Regional Offices under the respective Chief Engineer and two Chief Engineer offices in the same Corporation.

The concerned Executive Director/ Director General is the competent authority. Accordingly, they are informed that considering the transfers made from the government level of Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer/ Assistant Engineer Grade-II under them, care should be taken to strictly follow the provisions of Section 5 (2) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prohibition of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005 and other provisions of the Act.
9. The concerned engineers should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government.
Further, as per Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the transferred employees. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned employees at the original address. If the leave applications of the transferred employees are sent to the parent office, these applications will not be taken into consideration.
The concerned Executive Director/ Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer is informed that he has issued the Government Letter No. Narrow- 2017/ (173/17)/ A (Class-II), Dy. Action to be taken as per instructions issued under 12.07.2017.
