The Proposal:
The Namud Bridge is a 3925 m long bridge over Nanded Sub Branch Canal. is located at a distance. The canal No. 1 comes under the jurisdiction of Limbgaon. Irrigation Branch No. Irrigation sub-division, Limbgaon is functioning for 1 and 3 branches and this sub-division is connected with irrigation management since 1965 under Purna Irrigation Department, Vasmatnagar and the total area of irrigation of the sub-division is 14889 hectares.
The jurisdiction of this subdivision is spread over three districts (Hingoli, Parbhani and Nanded).
Wasmat Branch Canal 0 to 29.30 km.
Nanded sub-branch canal from 0 to 20.13 km
The irrigation program is effectively carried out by all the distribution systems associated with Dhanora Limbgaon Naleshwar Vitrika, which is 0 to 22.30 km.
Construction of the main canal and distribution system generally took place between 1960 and 1965.
At the above mentioned work site, the overbridge for the beneficiaries of the Sunegaon area to cross from the service point to the inspection line was in stone construction before 1965 and the roof of the compound was laid on it. At present, a portion of the roof of the bridge has collapsed into the canal, while the rest of the service track has weakened and the roof gauges are open and rusted. A portion of the roof was also ripped off and some of the roofs were ripped open. The residents of Sunegaon village have been demanding construction of a new bridge at the site. Former Minister Ma. Shri Jayaprakash Mundada has demanded that this bridge should be built again. In this regard, Refer No. A proposal was under consideration of the government to grant administrative approval under a special amendment for this work in exercise of the powers conferred on the department under the Government Decision No. 2, 3 and 4.
