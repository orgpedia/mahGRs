Introduction: - The minor project, Degree Storage Lake, is in the general area of Marathwada region under Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Under the scheme, it is proposed to construct an earthen dam with a capacity of 3.71 gallons on a local rivulet near Digras village in Hingoli taluka of Hingoli district. The total annual water consumption of the project is 3.8 dL. A total of 603 villages in Digras, Lohgaon and Dategaon of Hingoli taluka have been covered under this project. It is planned to give the benefit of irrigation to the area.
The project is worth Rs. The proposal for administrative approval of the 90.61 crore (rate list 2022-23) price was submitted to the government by the Executive Director, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar in a letter of reference.
The proposal has been approved by the Hon'ble Cabinet in its meeting dated 11/11/2025. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken:
Government Decision: - Degree Storage Lake, Ta. and G. Hingoli Project based on Water Resources Department Rate List 2022-23 at a cost of Rs. 90.61 crore (Rupees Ninety One Crore Sixty One Lakh only) at a cost of (Total Rs. 87.14 crore and ancillary expenditure of Rs. 3.47 crores) is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
