GOVERNMENT Circular: Under the Water Resources Department, Government Resolution, dated 05/12/2014, a committee consisting of 15 engineers/ members, both experienced and senior, was constituted under the chairmanship of Mr. S.N. Sahastrabuddhe (Retired Executive Director, Water Resources Department) to prepare an all inclusive separate manual for the Water Resources Department. Thereafter, as per Government Circular dated 16/01/2015, the composition of the Committee was partly modified on administrative grounds and Mr. A.P. Bhave, Retired Secretary (LaCEVI) Jasvini was appointed as the Chairman of the Committee.
The committee comprises of three members retired from the post of Secretary or equivalent, three members working in the post of Chief Engineer, two members working in the post of Superintending Engineer, Director, Accounts and Treasury, Divisional Accountant, etc. The committee prepared a report and submitted it. submitted to the Government vide letter dated 29/11/2016.
In the meantime, policy decisions on tendering have been taken at the government level. In view of this decision, it is necessary to amend the draft of the proposed rules. Accordingly, the following committee is being constituted to revise the draft draft of the overall separate manual for the Water Resources Department.
