Government's decision:
219 fixed temporary posts in the office of the Chief Engineer (Power), Hydel Project, Mumbai, under the Superintending Engineer, Vidarbha Hydel Power and Utilization Board, Nagpur and 4 Divisional and Sub-Divisional Offices under him and 1 post on converted temporary establishment are being extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 or till the date of joining of the staff, whichever is earlier. (Details are given in the accompanying report.) )
2. Establishment expenses on these posts The expenses on fixed temporary establishment and on converted temporary establishment respectively are as under: Demand No. I-5 (Fixed Temporary Establishment), Head of Accounts 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 190 Investments in Public Sector Undertakings and Other Undertakings, (04) Share Capital Contribution (Salary) to various Irrigation Development Corporations, (04) (02) Share Capital Contribution (Compulsory) to Irrigation Development Corporation (4701I 171) and Demand No. I5 (Converted Temporary Establishment) 4701- Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, 190- Investments in Public and Other Undertakings, (02) Share Capital Contribution to various Irrigation Development Corporations (02) Share Capital Contribution (Programme) to Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation (4701H665).
These Government Decisions are being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ APC, dated 11.09.2019 of the Finance Department and with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary.
