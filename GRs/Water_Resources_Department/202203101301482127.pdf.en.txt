Government Decision: Based on the results of the Combined Competitive Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai for the recruitment of Group-B posts of Assistant Engineer Grade-II in the Maharashtra Engineering (Architecture) Service Examination, 2018. This has been approved by the committee.
