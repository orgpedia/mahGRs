Preamble :
Dam Rehabilitation and Improvement Project (DRIP) is one of the flagship
projects of the Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation,
Government of India and the World Bank with the objective to improve safety and
operational performance of selected dams alongwith institutional strengthening with system
wide management approach.
Central government has decided to implement DRIP-II project with the
assistance of World Bank. In this project there are various dams from Maharashtra State.
Under this project Maharashtra is expected to receive fund about Rs. 940 Cr.
As DRIP-II project is being implemented with the assistance of World Bank,
it is mandatory to provide various administrative arrangements as per the Directives, Terms
& Conditions of World Bank for effective Project Management. Accordingly Central
Government has issued necessary instructions to participating states.
For Project Management at National level, Director, Dam Safety
Organisation, Central Water Commission, Seva Bhavan, New Delhi has formed Central
Project Monitoring Unit (CPMU). Accordingly, State Project Monitoring Unit (SPMU) has
formed as per Water Resources Department Government Resolution No. Misc
2017/(139/17)/IMW, dt.11/04/2019.

In addition to State Project Monitoring Unit (SPMU), it is necessary to
constitute State Level Steering Committee for effective project implementation.
Accordingly formation of State Level Steering Committee was under consideration of
Government of Maharashtra. Hence Government of Maharashtra has taken following
decision.

(B) Terms of Reference (ToR) of State Level Steering Committee for DRIP-II Project
i) Overall Project Planning, Management & Co-ordination at State level.
ii) Overall Administration for execution of works.
iii) According Administrative Approval to estimates of projects included in DRIP-II scheme.
iv) Deciding project wise budget allocation.
v) Co-ordinating with Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation, Government of India & other departments of State.
