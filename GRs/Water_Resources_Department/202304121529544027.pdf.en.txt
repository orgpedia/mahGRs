Government Resolution No. 2023/ P.S. 176/ MPR- (2)
The Government is approving the continuation of 13 posts in the establishment from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. The deadline has been extended under the following conditions:
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
3) These positions will be approved according to the figure.
4) All of the above will not be fulfilled.
(5) The proposals shall be submitted to the Government in the prescribed form, along with the certificate, along with the date of the period for which the certificate is to be submitted.
Expenditure on this account shall be covered under head Demand No. I-5, Head Account 4701, Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation, (80), General, 190- Investments in Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (04) Share Capital Contribution to various Irrigation Development Corporations, (04) (02) Share Contribution to Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation (Compulsory) (Computer Code 4701I 171) (Compulsory) and from the grants sanctioned thereunder.
This order was issued vide Government Order No. The posts are being issued vide No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 08.02.2023.
