Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said promotions are being given to the officers to be promoted without prejudice to the seniority of the officers who are senior in service and whose cases are kept open for consideration.
(ii) Promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) does not mean that the concerned Engineer is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior-most officers shall be demoted on last come first go basis without any notice.
(iii) The said promotion is being given subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary proceedings are going on or proposed against the concerned. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental enquiry is in progress or proposed to be conducted on the date of this order, the said promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
(iv) The said promotion order is purely temporary in nature. (vi) Promoted officers shall issue posting orders confirming submission of Statement of Obligations and Liabilities for the period ending 31.03.2021.
(vi) Engineers who are denied promotion on the ground that the promotions, though temporary, are regularised over a period of time, shall be informed in writing that the benefit of the Scheme of Assured Progress under Service shall be withdrawn. If an engineer refuses promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical), the report should be submitted to the government immediately. An engineer who has not rejected promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) shall not be deducted his salary from the month of June, 2022 for the post of Assistant Engineer Grade 2/ Minimum Engineer/ Branch Engineer. The Executive Engineer of the concerned department will be responsible for this.

In case of delay, the concerned Executive Engineer will be held responsible. If the promoted officer does not appear in this post within one month, the promotion given to him will be canceled on the assumption that he is not interested in the promotion. The information of the officers who will not appear in the promoted post within the stipulated time should be submitted to the government immediately by the concerned Chief Engineer/ Head of Office.
(ix) If the promoted engineers refuse the promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical), action will be taken against them as per the Government decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.
(x) The engineers who have come to join the post of posting after being relieved from the concerned department as per the promotion order should be recruited by the concerned without any delay. The Chief Engineer (Mechanical) should submit a consolidated report to the government immediately. In case of non-recruitment of an engineer leading to compulsory waiting period and payment of salary and allowances, the responsibility shall be fixed on the Executive Engineer/ Establishment Officer concerned.
(xi) These promotions are provisional and are subject to the publication of the final seniority of their parent cadre. Therefore, the concerned will not get any entitlement for seniority in the cadre of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical).
