Regarding the release of separate interim seniority of employees of the cadre of Junior Engineer (Electrical) in the Water Resources Department for the years 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021.
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources
Government Circular No: Seniority-1114/ (Q. No. 122/2014)/ A (Technical)
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of release: March 20, 2023
Read:- Government Circular, Water Resources Department, No. Seniority-1114/ (P. No. 122/2014)/ B. (Technical), Dy. 10.02.2020.
Government circular:
Department of Water Resources. 16.06.1981 to d. Junior Engineers (Electrical) who were appointed during 31.12.2014. The final seniority as on 1.1.2014 has been published vide Circular No. The government was considering publishing separate seniority for each year of the period from 2015 to 2021.
2. Junior Engineer (Electrical) The updated seniority is being published as a separate interim seniority for each year, taking into account the change due to promotion, retirement, death, etc., during the period from 2015 to 2021.
The Chief Engineer (Electrical) is hereby informed that this interim seniority should be brought to the immediate notice of all the Junior Engineers (Electrical). In case of any error/ discrepancy found in the said seniority list or any objection, the same should be intimated to the Government in writing within 15 days from the date of publication of this circular in a proper manner along with a certified copy of the required documents. Please note that any objections not received in a timely manner will not be considered.
