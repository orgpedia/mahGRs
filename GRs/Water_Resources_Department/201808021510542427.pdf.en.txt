The proposal-
The Superintending Engineer and Administrator, Benefit Area Development Authority, Aurangabad and the Superintending Engineer, Aurangabad Irrigation Board, Aurangabad are working under two Divisional Offices, namely, Jalna Irrigation Sub-Division No. 4, Watur Phata and Minor Irrigation Sub-Division, Mantha (Headquarter Watur), two Sub-Divisional Offices were under consideration for administrative reasons to be shifted from the operational Headquarter at Watur Phata to Partur and Mantha in Jalna District. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision
The Superintending Engineer and Administrator, Benefit Area Development Authority, Aurangabad is functioning under the Mandal Office, Jalna Irrigation Sub-Division No. It is hereby approved to shift the Sub Divisional Office of 4. Watur Phata to Partur and the Sub Divisional Office of Minor Irrigation Sub Division, Mantha (Headquarter Watur), functioning under the Divisional Office of the Superintending Engineer, Aurangabad Irrigation Circle, Aurangabad to Mantha for control and administrative convenience in the project works.
(2) Any such modification shall be subject to the following conditions:
(a) Jalna Irrigation Sub-Division No. 4. Two Sub-Divisional Offices viz., Watur FATA and Minor Irrigation Sub-Division, Mantha (HQ Watur) should be made functional with existing original charge and sanctioned establishment at Partur and Mantha HQ respectively.
(b) Until the provision of Government building for the Sub-Divisional Offices shifted to Partur and Mantha Headquarters is made, the offices shall be made functional for a limited period in the private building space on lease basis by alternative arrangement as per the prevailing rules and rates of the Public Works Department and shall be shifted to the Government space within 2 years.
(e) Materials in the working Sub-Divisional Offices shall be used for office use.
(e) There has been no change in the administrative and technical control of the transferred Sub-Divisional Offices.
