The proposal-
As per the decision of the government, the Bembla Rehabilitation Sub-division No. 5 has been taken for the work of Dehani Upas Drop Irrigation Scheme. 3, Thalegaon has been transferred as Dehani HQ, Dehani Uplift Irrigation Sub-Division, Dehani along with the sanctioned establishment of the Sub-Divisional Office. Under this scheme, some of the posts in the field of agriculture, marketing and information technology are not sanctioned in the field establishment of the department. However, during the discussion on the attention motion raised in the Budget Session of 2018 for providing establishment for construction of component works of the scheme, planned operation, etc., it has been assured in the House that action will be taken at the earliest for approval of the proposal.
Therefore, since the establishment is necessary for the work of the Dehani Upasana Drip Irrigation Scheme, the Finance Department, the Government has decided. In accordance with the provisions of paragraph 4 of 02.06.2015, Bembla Rehabilitation Sub-Division No. The matter of converting the posts in the cadre of equivalent pay scale in the Sub-Divisional Office of 3, Thalegaon and making these posts available to the Sub-Divisional Office of Dehni Upsa Irrigation Sub-Division, Dehni was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
3 As per the authority provided to the Administrative Department in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision of the Finance Department here, the Benbala Rehabilitation Sub-Division No. 5, which was transferred under the Government Decision in No. 5, has been transferred. It is hereby approved to convert 8 posts of 4 cadres out of 10 cadres of posts sanctioned to the Sub-Divisional Office, 3, Talegaon into equivalent pay grade cadre and make it available to the office of Dehani Irrigation Sub-Division, Dehani, which has been made functional by transfer.
