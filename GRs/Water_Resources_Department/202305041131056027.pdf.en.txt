Introduction: The second highest dam on the Mula river in the tribal area of Akole taluka is Kop Bandhara Khadki. There is a barrier. The construction of the dam was completed in 1996. The total number of sluices in the dam is 19 with 18 pillars, the length of the dam is 74m and the height of the dam is 6.15 m from the river bed. With an irrigated area of 263 hectares, the dam provides irrigation to villages like Khadki Budruk, Khadki Khurd, Pimpri and Vajulshet.
Every year during the rainy season, due to floods in the Mula river, the pillars of this dam have been damaged to a great extent. Water also seeps through the foundation. Therefore, it has been observed that the COP dam needs to be repaired immediately and the beneficiaries are also demanding in this regard.
Strengthening of the Khadki KoP dam will help in reducing the water leakage from the dam, making the dam safe and increasing the area under direct irrigation.
In view of the safety of the embankment, it is necessary to repair the embankment. P. Bandhara Khadki Ta. A proposal to give administrative approval to the repair work of Akole, G.
