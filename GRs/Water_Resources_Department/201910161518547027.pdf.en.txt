A total of 750 posts on fixed temporary establishment and 148 posts on converted temporary establishment functioning under the office of the Chief Engineer, Planning and Hydrology, Nashik, have been notified vide Government Resolution No. Posts-2019/ 140/ PR No. 41/2019/ under which the period of extension has been extended from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019.
Department of Finance, Government Resolution No. As per Regulation No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ P.C.D. 11/09/2019, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the Review earlier but which have not been included in the rolls. The powers to grant extension have been granted from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to extend the term of 731 posts on the fixed temporary establishment under the Chief Engineer, Planning and Hydrology Project, Nashik and 130 posts on the converted temporary establishment from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020.
