The Proposal:
Under the small-scale scheme. In Rajapur, Ratnagiri district, an earthen dam has been constructed on a local nullah with a capacity of 5.46 LMT. The project is located in the" Rest of Maharashtra" region under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation. A total of 255 villages of 3 villages of Rajapur taluka have been covered under this project. It is planned to provide irrigation benefits to the area through open canal and closed tube well.
