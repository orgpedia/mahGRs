The Central Government has decided to provide central assistance under a special package to 91 irrigation projects of the Water Resources Department in the suicide-affected districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada and drought-affected districts of the rest of Maharashtra. As per the Government decision dated 1.1.2019, it has been approved to be called" Baliraja Jal Sanjeevi Yojana."
Under the scheme, the Central Government will bear 25% of the project cost and the State Government will bear the remaining 75%.
