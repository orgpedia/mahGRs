Executive Director, Godavari Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad vide letter No.Gompavim/ Tan- 5/7088, dated 7.9.2017 Preamble: The Chief Engineer (South), National Water Development Agency, Hyderabad has stated in the pre-feasibility report that the Damanganga (one-lane) Godavari lift link scheme for diverting the surplus water of the western channel rivers of the Damanganga river basin to the Godavari basin in the east has been successful as per the pre-feasibility report.
As per the pre-feasibility report prepared by the National Water Development Agency, the benefit ratio of the scheme is 1.44. Under this scheme, 100 Dalhousie water can be made available for irrigation and 43 Dalhousie water can be made available for non-irrigation purposes. Under the scheme, 1 dam is proposed to be constructed, which is planned to be connected to the existing Gangapur Dam through a tunnel/ pipeline and 143 dams are proposed to be diverted to Godavari basin through lift system.
The National Water Development Agency (NWDA) had sent the proposal for the preparation of the detailed project report after conducting a detailed survey and investigation of the scheme. 1 was submitted to the government for administrative approval. Accordingly, the proposal for administrative approval of the expenditure incurred for preparation of Detailed Project Report was under consideration of the Government.
