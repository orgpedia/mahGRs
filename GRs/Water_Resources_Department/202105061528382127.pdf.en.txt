The Proposal:
The Department of Finance vide its Government Resolution dated 10th September, 2001 has laid down the policy on the use of vehicles in Government and other offices. For this, every administrative department has been directed to review the vehicles with them (including the regional office). Accordingly, a proposal to approve the review of vehicles with 8 offices of the Water Resources Department was submitted for the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee.
Reference No. 2 is here. As per the reference, the Finance Department has 8 offices in the Water Resources Department.
The review of vehicles has been approved and accordingly the Government has issued the above Reference No. The Finance Department has issued directions under 3-5. In accordance with this directive, the government was under consideration to issue a government decision to review the sanctioned vehicles of the offices of the Water Resources Department.
Government's decision:
The proposal to review the vehicles owned by the 8 offices of the Water Resources Department was submitted for the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee. Positions in these offices
Considering the duties and responsibilities, it has been decided to provide vehicle facilities for which posts, according to which the vehicle review is being approved with the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee. The vehicle inspection office is as follows. A brief description of the same is given in the accompanying Form A.
