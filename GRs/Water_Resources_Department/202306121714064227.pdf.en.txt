Girija Medium Project is located near Yesgaon village on the Girija river. Khultabad is located in Aurangabad district. Girija Medium Project was completed in 1986. The total live storage available in Girija Medium Project is 21.23 LHM and the total irrigated area of both the canals is 3447 hectares. Girija Medium Project has two canals, out of which the right canal is 10.15 km and has 10 distributaries. The left canal is 17.50 km long and has 16 distributaries.
