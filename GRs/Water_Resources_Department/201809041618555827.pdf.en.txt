The Proposal:
Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Shendurni under Jalgaon, Dist. The village of Jamner, G. Jalgaon is situated on the banks of the Sonad river. The Sonad River is a major tributary of the Waghur River. Due to the flood in the Sonad river, a large number of puddles are being formed on the banks of the river. This should not pose a threat to the Shani temple on the river bank and the life and property of some people, so this work is demanded by the Gram Panchayat, Shendurni, Ta. Jamner passed resolution No. 12, d. 1.5.2015 and Resolution No. 4/3 d. This was done on 27.11.2015. The Water Resources Department has approved the budget for the flood protection work near Shani Temple on the Sonad River. PRASHAMA-2016/ (Q. No. 66/2016)/ c. (M), D. As on 22.3.2016, Rs. Administrative approval was accorded for an estimated cost of Rs. 85,83,835/-.
With the implementation of the new common standard of the Irrigation Department from 01.11.2016, the matter of giving revised administrative approval to the work was under the consideration of the government.

(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
d) The cost of the work is to be completed within the limit of revised administrative approval from the available grant and within the scheduled time.
e) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be provided by the concerned without any cost, it should be ensured that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
e) After implementing the flood protection scheme, it should be ensured in the planning that the drainage of the area will be done in a proper manner.
e) After the completion of the project, the flood protection wall will be repaired. The Jamner Gram Panchayat has to take over and carry out future maintenance and repairs. Before starting the work, a written bond should be obtained from the Shendurni Gram Panchayat.
