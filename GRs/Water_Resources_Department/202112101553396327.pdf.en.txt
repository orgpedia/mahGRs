Reference No. 1 Subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission of the provisional seniority of 01.01.2021 in the cadre of Senior Geologist and Assistant Geologist in the Water Resources Department as per the instructions issued under the Government Decision herein, Reference No. 3 Government circulars were issued here. The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been mainly adopted in the preparation of the said final seniority.
