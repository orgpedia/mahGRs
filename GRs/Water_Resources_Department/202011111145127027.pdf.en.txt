Circular: The above Reference Circular No. As per Section 2 of the Act, the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur, was empowered to issue water availability certificates for projects in Gondia, Bhandara, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur and Nagpur districts of Vidarbha, subject to the condition that the existing/ under-construction projects would not be affected.
The Chief Engineer, Hydrology and Dam Safety, Nashik, has been entrusted with the task of updating the water registers of all rivers and revising the water registers according to the Integrated State Water Plan and maintaining a new sub-basin-wise standard water register and in order to bring uniformity in the work of issuing water availability certificates, the government has decided to hand over the power of issuing water availability certificates for projects in Gondia, Bhandara, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur and Nagpur districts of Vidarbha to the Chief Engineer, Hydrology and Dam Safety, Nashik.
Therefore, instead of submitting the necessary information to the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur, for obtaining the water availability certificate for the project in Gondia, Bhandara, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Nagpur districts of Vidarbha, the Chief Engineer, Hydrology and Dam Safety, Nashik, should submit the water availability certificate from them.
The Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur, vide Circular No. The documents of all the schemes for which water availability certificates have been issued as per the authority given under section 2 should be transferred to the Chief Engineer, Hydrology and Dam Safety, Nashik.
