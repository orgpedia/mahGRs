Preamble: The Shirgaon-Yesde Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka Murud, District Raigad, was accorded the original administrative approval under Government Resolution No. 2014/ (246/2014)/ Kharbhoomi dated 30/09/2015 at a total cost of Rs. 276.42 lakhs. While preparing this administrative approval proposal, the rates were used as per the 2011-12 tariff schedule. In the intervening period, the cost of the scheme has been increasing due to tariff differences, ownership charges, increase in carrying distance, increase in volume, etc., necessitating grant of revised administrative approval to the scheme. The Union Forest Ministry has exempted the scheme under the Coastal Regulation Act, 1991 (CRZ). The total length of the project is 2777 m. The project is located on the right bank of Kundalika river near Shirgaon Yesde village. The proposal to give first revised administrative approval to the Shirgaon-Yesde Kharbhoomi scheme, which was received through a reference letter from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai, was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: First Revised Administrative Sanction is hereby accorded to Shirgaon-Yesde Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka Murud, District Raigad for an estimated amount of Rs. 400.63 lakhs (Rupees Four Hundred and Sixty Three only) based on the 2011-12 tariff list. Sub-title provisions of Rs. 364.21 lakhs for works (Rs. Three hundred sixty four integer twenty one per cent only) and Rs. 36.42 lakhs for ancillary works (Rs. Thirty six integer forty two per cent only) are given in the Annexure attached to the Notes. The revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions:
