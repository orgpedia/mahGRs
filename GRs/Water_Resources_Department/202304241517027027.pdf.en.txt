Government Referendum: Reference No. 3 here. As per the Government Decision dated 11/04/2023, Ministry of Water Resources (M/ o Water Resources) has converted 80% of the total 409 posts into permanent posts as per the revised figure of posts in the establishment. The government's decision is as follows:
A total of 166 posts are in the provisional category, comprising 80% of the regular cadre i.e. a total of 327 posts in the permanent category and 20% of the regular cadre i.e. 82 posts and 84 posts in the deceased cadre. Instead, it should be read that 80% of the regular cadre i.e. a total of 327 posts are in the permanent classification and 20% of the regular cadre i.e. 82 posts and dead cadre 75 posts.
Also Reference No. 3 here. The total number of temporary posts in the statement accompanying the Government decision dated 11/04/2023 No. 28-Lokamudraka should be read as 1 instead of 10 and hence the total number of temporary posts should be read as 157 instead of 166.
