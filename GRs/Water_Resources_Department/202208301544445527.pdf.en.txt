Preamble: Creation of 30 posts on the fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur. The decision has been taken by the government. These posts have been approved to be continued from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government Resolution No. 2. As per the decision of the Finance Department vide Reference No. 4, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after approval of earlier review but not included in the rolls from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Now, as proposed under Reference No. 3 letter, the Government is approving the continuation of 30 posts on the fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 as indicated in the accompanying prospectus-" A." The deadline has been extended under the following conditions:
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
3) These positions will be approved according to the figure.
4) All of the above will not be fulfilled.
(5) The proposals shall be submitted to the Government in the prescribed form, along with the certificate, along with the date of the period for which the certificate is to be submitted.
