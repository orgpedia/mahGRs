Preamble: The control of the office of the Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Pune under the Department of Water Resources was handed over to the Director General, Concept, Training, Hydrology, Research and Safety, Nashik (DTHRS) under the Government decision in Reference No. However, since the works entrusted to the office of the Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Pune and its jurisdiction are all over Maharashtra, the Government has decided to bring the administrative control of the Directorate directly under the control of the Government as follows.
Government Decision:- In order to effectively carry out the works entrusted to the office of the Superintending Engineer and Director, Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Pune under the Water Resources Department, their administrative control is being handed over to the Chief Engineer (PA) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
Only the administrative control of the said Directorate is being changed and there is no change in the sanctioned establishment, office space and technical control.
