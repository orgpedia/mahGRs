The Proposal:
Patoda storage pond The project is located on the Ramtirth river upstream of Asun Patoda village in Manta taluka of Jalna district under the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation. The total storage capacity of the dam is 8.60 LMT. The project will irrigate 1199 hectares of land in Mantha taluka.
