The Proposal:
Khanapur, Bhudargad, Kolhapur is a village located on the right bank of the Vedganga river.
The land of the river bank on the upstream and downstream side of this Khanapur ghat is of soft layer.
The flood caused by heavy rainfall in the 2019-2021 monsoon season had caused extensive damage to both banks of the dam, threatening the standing crop and the embankment. The people's representatives have demanded that this work be done to prevent more erosion to the cultivated agricultural land and to protect the river banks and embankments on both banks from flood waters.
According to Mauje Khanapur, Ta. A fresh budget has been prepared by conducting a survey at the proposed site in Bhudargad, G. Kolhapur. Khanapur, Dist.
