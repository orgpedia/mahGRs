Introduction: Kalher Kevani, Motidiva Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka Bhiwandi, District Thane are included in the Kharbhoomi Scheme. The Union Ministry of Forests has notified 354 Kalher Keweni Motidiva Yojana under the Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) Act, 1991. are exempted for the area.
The original work of this scheme has been done by Kharland Board in 1957 and the renovation work of this scheme has been completed in 1989. The length of the embankment of the project is 5070 m.
Due to the onrush of water from the dam, the paddy crop has been damaged. The rain water is entering into agricultural fields and causing losses to farmers. The villagers have demanded that the project should be revived. The scheme has been proposed on the demand of the people's representatives and villagers and the name of the scheme has been given as Kalher Kevani Motidiva. The cost of the project is Rs. 1,85,227/- against the prevailing monetary norm of Rs. 1,92,950/- received from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai under reference letter. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
