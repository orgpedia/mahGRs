Proposal. -
The Urmodi Dam in Satara district, under the jurisdiction of the Water Resources Department, has led to a significant increase in irrigation in Satara, Manb Khatav taluka. The government had received a request for renaming the Urmodi dam reservoir as "Chhatrapati Abhaysinharaje WMA ASML."
At present, the government has no policy on naming the reservoirs of dams after great men. However, a proposal to rename the reservoir of Urmodi Dam as "Chhatrapati Abhaysinharaje Bhosale Reservoir" as a "special case" in this regard was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
The Government's Decisions:
The Urmodi Dam reservoir in Satara district is being renamed as "Chhatrapati Abhaysinharaje Bhosale Reservoir."
