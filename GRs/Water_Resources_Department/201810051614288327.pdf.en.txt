Preamble- The work relating to the Concept Picture is not pending under the Sub Divisional Office of the Deputy Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Concept Picture) Sub Division, Pune, functioning under the control of the Office of the Superintending Engineer, Pune Irrigation Board, Pune. Therefore, as the officers/ employees working in the sanctioned posts of this Sub-Divisional Office were included in the vacant posts in the Irrigation Management Offices under the Board under the order of the Regional Office, the matter of closing the office of Minor Irrigation (Sankalp Chitra) Sub-Division, Pune was under the consideration of the Government. Following this, the government decided:
Government's decision
It is hereby approved to close down the office of the Chief Engineer (Water Resources), Water Resources Department, Pune, under the control of the Superintending Engineer, Pune Irrigation Board, Pune, and the office of the Deputy Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Conceptual) Sub-Division, Pune.
2. Inclusion of officers/ staff working in sanctioned posts in Minor Irrigation (Sankalp Chitra) Sub-Division, Pune office has been done through the Regional Office in the vacant posts in the offices of Irrigation Management functioning under the control of Pune Irrigation Board, Pune. Hence, the Sub-Divisional Office of Minor Irrigation (Sankalp Chitra) Sub-Division, Pune has been established. The Chief Engineer (Water Resources), Water Resources Department, Pune should take immediate action to close all the records of the said office from 15/10/2018 and transfer them to other offices under the Board for administrative convenience.
Government Decision, Finance Department No. 1092/ CR-806/ Expenditure-6, Sec. This order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Department under the provisions of 15-10-1992 and with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary.
