Preamble: The Jigaon project of Buldhana Irrigation Project Board, Buldhana Division envisages creation of irrigation potential of 27,434 hectares by providing 182.89 LHM of partial water storage in the first phase by June 2024. The irrigation potential of the project No. It is expected to be built in the first phase of 1 to 15. The irrigation project No. It is necessary to complete the works of 1 to 15 and the works of closed tube distribution system in the first phase of all the schemes immediately. In the first phase of the Jigaon project, 233 metres of the lake has been constructed. 25 villages are to be rehabilitated to conserve water.
All these aspects need to be planned section wise and modified section wise to create irrigation potential by partially storing water.
