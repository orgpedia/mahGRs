The Proposal: -
Due to the flooding of the Waghur River, Ma. Pahur Peth, Ta. On the right and left bank adjacent to the village of Jamner, District Jalgaon, the Kevadeshwar Mahadev Temple, Jain Crematorium Bhumi, Ram Mandir, Mahadev Temple, Marwari Crematorium Bhumi, Veshi Darwaza, Khatik Wada, Buddhist Crematorium Bhumi, are the places where water enters the temple complex and causes extensive damage to the population. In this regard, the local public funds had informed about the construction of the flood protection wall, according to which the field office has made a physical inspection and submitted the budget to the government for approval. Administrative approval of the budget was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon. Pahur Peth, Ta. In Jamner, District Jalgaon, on the right and left bank of the Waghur river bank adjacent to the village, at Kevdeshwar Mahadev Temple, Jain Crematorium Bhumi, Ram Mandir, Mahadev Temple, Marwari Crematorium Bhumi, Veshi Darwaza, Khatik Wada, Buddhist Crematorium Bhumi, a flood protection wall has been constructed at a cost of Rs. 2,55,68, 560.00 - (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Five Lakh Sixty Five Thousand Sixty Five) The budget based on the tariff list of the Water Resources Department and the Public Works Department is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of the Decision No. P. 2019 / (Q. 115/2019) / S. (M) dated 31/08/2019.
b) Government Circular No. FDW - 1089/243/89 Simvya (Works), D. 21/09/1989 and d. The line of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn according to the 03/05/2018. Tenders and other proceedings should not be taken unless a flood line has been drawn.
c) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, tariff outlays and carrying distances at the competent level. The concept of the work should be approved only with technical approval from the competent level.
