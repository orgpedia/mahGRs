In Jamner, District Jalgaon, a proposal for construction of a flood protection wall on the right and left bank of the Waghur river bank adjacent to the village, at Nathjogi Samaj Cemetery, Muslim Cemetery, Boudh Vihar Crematorium, has been mooted at a cost of Rs. The following is the administrative approval given to the budget based on the tariff list of the Water Resources Department for the year 2022-23 and the Public Works Department for the year 2022-23 (Rs.
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of the Decision No. P. 2019 / (Q. 195/2019) / S. (M) dated 31/08/2019.
b) Government Circular No. FDW - 1089/243/89 Simvya (Works), D. 21/09/1989 and d. The line of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn according to the 03/05/2018. Tenders and other proceedings should not be taken unless a flood line has been drawn.
c) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, the externalities of the tariff list and the carrying distance at the competent level. Technical approval proceedings should be carried out only after the conception of the work has been approved from the competent level. If the concept results in a change in the cost or scope of the work, it will be mandatory to get it approved at the competent level.
d) The technical clearance authorities concerned shall be solely responsible for providing technical clearance in the budget excluding short work. The cost of the work is to be met from the available grants within the limits of administrative sanction and within the stipulated time. These works will not be given revised administrative approval.
f) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, ensuring that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
g) Plan to ensure proper drainage of water in the area after implementing the flood protection plan.
