Under the project, a total of 1.34 billion cubic feet of water is proposed to be used to irrigate 4,200 villages on the eastern and western sides of the river in Koregaon taluka. It is proposed to bring such an irrigated area under irrigation.
Accordingly, the proposal was placed for consideration in the meeting of the Expenditure Priority Committee dated 24/02/2022. The meeting decided to give administrative approval to the Second Revised Project Report of Rs. 125.06 crore based on the 2013-14 schedule of the Vangana Uplift Irrigation Scheme after taking into consideration the current status of construction, reasons for cost escalation, etc.
Government's decision:
With this decision of the Government, revised administrative approval is being accorded to the Second Revised Project Report of Wangna Utilization Irrigation Scheme based on the 2013-14 Schedule at a cost of Rs. 125.06 crore (Rs. Sub-heading wise provisions are being added to Goshwara.
The Second Revised Administrative Sanction granted to the Scheme has been entered in the Register of Medium Project Operations at serial number 01/2022.
The project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the revised administrative sanction.
The cost of the project shall be included in the head of the main account under the head I-5, capital expenditure on 4701 major and medium irrigation projects, investment in 190-public sector and other enterprises under (02) schemes, (02) (01) Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune share capital contribution (4701-H729) and shall be met from the grant-in-aid sanctioned thereunder.
