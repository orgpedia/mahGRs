Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune vide Reference No. 4 As per the request made under the aforesaid letter, considering the scope of work of the office of the Superintending Engineer, Sangli Irrigation Board, Sangli, it was under the consideration of the Government to make the post of Deputy Superintending Engineer, Pune Irrigation Board, Pune available on rotation basis to the Superintending Engineer, Sangli Irrigation Board, Sangli on temporary basis. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has decided to provide one post of Deputy Superintending Engineer in the cadre of Superintending Engineer under Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune and Executive Engineer in the office of Pune Irrigation Board, Pune to the Office of Superintending Engineer, Sangli Irrigation Board, Sangli on a temporary basis.
