Preamble: A total of 437 posts, including 423 posts of Superintending Engineer, North Konkan Irrigation Project Board, Kalwa-Thane and 14 posts of temporary establishment and 14 posts of temporary establishment in the divisional and sub-divisional offices under the Board have been notified in the notification. 1 As per the Government decision dated 06/04/2023, the extension was granted from 01/03/2023 to 31/08/2023. Now, a total of 433 posts of 423 posts on fixed temporary establishment and 10 posts on converted temporary establishment in the same office will be filled up from 01/09/2023. Reference No. regarding grant of extension upto 29/02/2024 2 The proposal has been received by the Government vide letter dated 28/08/2023. Finance Department Reference No. 3 In pursuance of the Government decision dated 31.08.2023, the following decisions are being taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: A total of 433 posts of Superintending Engineer, North Konkan Irrigation Project Board, Kalwa-Thane and 423 posts on fixed temporary establishment and 10 posts on converted temporary establishment in the Divisional and Sub-Divisional offices under the Board are being granted extension from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure' A' and' B'.
a) The fixed temporary posts should not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The posts on fixed temporary establishment shall be within the limit of 45297 posts as per the revised figure approved under the Government Decision dated 06.02.2003.
The expenditure on establishment of the above offices shall be covered under the head" Demand No. I-5,4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation, 80- General, 190- Loans to Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (04) Share Capital Contribution (Salary) to various Irrigation Development Corporations, (04) (03) Share Contribution (Compulsory) to Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation (4701 I-189)" and the same shall be met from the sanctioned grant for the year 2022-23.
