Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 20.06.2018, a Member Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri Shima Upase, Retired Secretary (Jasvya& Lakshevi) to study how it is possible to implement the Gunjavani Hydro Electric Project (1 X 2 MW) without disturbing the proposed concept of irrigating the canal/ distribution system of the Gunjavani Irrigation Project through pressurized closed pipes and drip method.
However, the matter of fixing the remuneration and allowances to be paid to Mr. Upase was under consideration.
Government's decision
Regarding the honorarium and allowances to be paid to Mr. Upase, retired Secretary, the following decisions are being taken: 1. A total of Rs. 85,000/- (Rupees Eighty Five Thousand only) is being sanctioned to the members of the one member committee for meetings and other activities for a maximum period of 20 days of the month, Rs. 3,500/- (Rupees Seventy Thousand only) per day and an estimated amount of Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand only) for stationery, telephone, fax, xerox, typewriting etc.
The amount of the honorarium shall include the boarding charges.
3. Daily allowance shall not be admissible separately as the rate of daily remuneration includes all other expenses.
4. Transport for the members to and from the place of work shall be provided by the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation.
The expenditure for this will be met from the grant sanctioned by the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune for the year 2018-19.
