Interim seniority of the office superintendents in the field offices under the Department of Water Resources at the level of Government for the period from 1st January, 2016 to 31st December, 2017
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources
Government Circular No.: Seniority 2017/456/2017/ AA (Intellectual) Ministry, Mumbai-400 032
Date: August 2, 2018
Circular: Interim seniority of office superintendents in the field offices under the Department of Water Resources for the period from 1st January, 2016 to 31st December, 2017 has been prepared and is attached herewith (Annexure A) for information. All Superintending Engineers are requested to bring this seniority to the notice of all the office superintendents under them immediately and give them 15 days time to file objections to the entries in the said seniority list. In case of objections raised by the concerned, the same along with necessary proof shall be intimated to the Government within 3 weeks from the date of issue of the seniority. Also, the office superintendents of backward classes who have not yet submitted the caste validity certificate should be informed about its immediate submission. Please note that the final seniority list will be published after considering the objections received by the government in the specified period regarding the entry in the seniority list.
