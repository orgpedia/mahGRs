Terms and Conditions:
(1) These promotions are being given to the officers to be promoted without prejudice to the seniority of the officers who are senior in service and whose cases are kept open for consideration.
(ii) Promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) does not mean that the Superintendent concerned is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, without giving any notice to the Junior Superintendents,
Last will be demoted on the principle of last come first go.
(iii) The said promotion is being given subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary proceedings are going on or proposed to be initiated against the above-named Superintendent. However, in case of any of these superintendents against whom a departmental enquiry is in progress or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
(iv) The detailed posting given by the Executive Director/ Chief Engineer after promotion from the Superintendent Cadre to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) shall not be changed in the mutual service category or any other change without the approval of the Government.
(v) Ma. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 as per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution dated 07.05.2021. This government has taken that decision. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. The promotions will be made subject to the final decision.
(vi) If a Superintendent promoted to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) does not appear in the post of promotion within one month from the date of this order, the promotion granted to him shall be cancelled on the ground that he is not interested in the promotion. Also, if the promoted Superintendent refuses to accept the promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B), action will be taken against him as per the Government Decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.
(vii) The Superintendents who refuse the promotion on the ground that the promotion is temporary in nature but gets regularised over a period of time, shall be informed in writing that the benefit of time-bound promotion/ Assured Progress Scheme under the service, if given, shall be withdrawn. If a Superintendent refuses promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B), the report should be submitted to the government immediately.
