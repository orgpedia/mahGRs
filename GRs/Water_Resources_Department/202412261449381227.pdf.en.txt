The Proposal:
Development of various types of hydroelectric projects in the state, from privatization to renewal and modernization of hydroelectric projects with life expectancy and revamping of development policy under privatization of small hydroelectric projects up to 25th May, are the three policies that need to be implemented in a harmonized manner, so as to formulate standard operating procedures / guidelines in accordance with the provisions mentioned in these three policies. The committee was constituted by a government decision on 26.04.2023.
After the formation of the committee, the Water Resources Department, in turn, constituted the Department of Water Resources. Development of various types of hydroelectric projects in the state under 20.12.2023. Renovation and modernization of hydroelectric projects having completed prescribed life standards under 21.08.2024 on Lease, Renovate, Operate & Transfer (LROT) basis, and Government policies have been expanded to include reforms in development policy under the privatization of small hydroelectric projects up to 25 MW capacity under the 08.10.2024.
Following the death of a member of the General Committee on D. 26.04.2023, the request made by the Chief Engineer (Project B Planning), General Management, to appoint his other officers as members on the Committee, as well as to revise the tenure of the Committee for implementation of policies, was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Mr. Sachin R. Dhumane, Deputy Engineer, Project B Planning, General Management is being appointed as a member of the committee in place of Mr. A. Shri Kulkarni, who has passed away.
