(2) The benefits incidental to the joining of the post of the transferor shall not be admissible to the above officers who have been transferred on request.
3. The date on which the concerned officers were relieved from their duties to appear in place of the new appointees and the date on which they appeared in place of the new appointees should be intimated to the Government immediately on the e-mail website doesttclass1.wrd@maharashtra. gov. in.
4. The concerned officers should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government.
Further, as per Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave application of a transferred officer shall be accepted by the parent office, if so received, the same shall be returned to the concerned officer by post on receipt of registration at the original address.
5. It may be noted that in case of failure to report to the place of posting immediately in accordance with the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
