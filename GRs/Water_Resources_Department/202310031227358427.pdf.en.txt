In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, framed vide Notification No. 1 dated 21.06.2021, dated 01.01.2023 of the Executive Engineer (Electrical and Mechanical) cadre, the provisional seniority (showing the position during 01.01.2022 to 31.12.2022) is being published in Annexure-A attached.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, the date of seniority is to be fixed on the date of joining the post of appointment within the prescribed period from the date of issue of the order of promotion. Also, since the officers promoted ad hoc are not eligible for the benefit of seniority in the rank, cadre or service of the promotion, the name of the officer concerned shall be included in the seniority list from the date on which the ad hoc promotion becomes regular to the vacant post in the quota of promotion. Accordingly, the officers of the cadre of Graduate Executive Engineer who are working on ad-hoc promotion from the quota of promotion of the Up-Davidhar cadre are not included in the said seniority. Similarly, in the seniority list dated 01.01.2023, the names of officers who were not working in the cadre of Executive Engineer (Electrical and Mechanical) before 01.01.2022 on account of retirement, voluntary retirement, death, promotion, resignation, etc. have been excluded.
