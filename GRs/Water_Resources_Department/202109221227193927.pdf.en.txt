GOVERNMENT DECISION: A total of 36 posts of Superintending Engineer, Thane Irrigation Board, Raigad Irrigation Department under Thane, 18 posts of temporary establishment in the office of Minor Irrigation Sub-Division, Mangaon under Kolad and 18 posts of temporary establishment in the office of Irrigation Works Sub-Division, Kolad are being sanctioned by extension from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure A.

(b) The posts on fixed temporary establishment shall be within the limit of 45297 posts as per the revised figure approved under the Government Decision dated 06.02.2003.
The expenditure on establishment of the above offices shall be met out of the grant sanctioned under the head Demand No. I-3 Account Head 2701 Large and Medium Irrigation, 80-General, (001) Operation and Administration, (04) Preservation Establishment (04) (05) Superintending Engineer, Thane Irrigation Board, Thane (Mandatory) (2701 4199).
