Reference No. The Water Resources e-Governance Committee has been constituted under the above Government Decision. Now the reference No. 11 As per the decision of the Government, the office of the Water Resources e-Governance Board has been shifted to Pune instead of Mumbai. Under Section 12 of the Act, all the functions of the Board office and e-governance have been transferred to the Chief Engineer (Wipro), Water Resources Department, Pune, instead of the Chief Engineer (Water Resources), Water Resources Department, Pune. The committee will be headed by the Deputy Commissioner and will have as members Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mumbai and Deputy Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mumbai.
