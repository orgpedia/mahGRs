Introduction: The Panzara Medium Project is built on the Panzara River near Pipalner village in Sakri taluka. The project was completed in 1972-73. The dam is an earthen dam with a length of 1140 metres, maximum height of 43.50 metres and a perimeter of 290 metres. The right canal is 21.80 km and the left canal is 6.80 km. I'm long. The discharge of the right canal is 6.71 cumecs and the discharge of the left canal is 0.71 cumecs. The total area of the project is 6868 hectares. A total of 15 distributaries are providing irrigation facilities to 20 villages from the right canal and 1 village from the left canal.
In 1982, the length of the right canal of this project was increased from 21.80 km to 6.60 km. increased to 28.40 km. The Government had accorded administrative approval under Extension and Revision dated 12/1/1982 for the extension up to M. Subsequently, TAPI Irrigation Development Corporation has accorded revised administrative approval to this work on 6/4/1999. Under this, the work of right main canal km 21.80 to 28.40 has been completed. As the work of sub-division of 18,20,21, 22 and 22 is yet to be taken up, an area of 1038.66 hectares in the Nilgavan, Nadse, Darkhel, Vitai and Behed etc. erosion prone and hilly areas of Sakri taluka of Dhule district has been deprived of irrigation.
