Preamble: Purna Barrage-II (Ner Dhamna) Medium Project is a project of Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation (VIDC) on the Purna River, 215.625 m south of Mauje Ner village in Telhara Taluka. The length of the barrage is 15 x 7.50 m. The size is proposed with 12 doors of vertical lifting mode. The barrage is proposed on raft foundation with diaphragm wall as there is permeable soft topography at the base of the barrage. Irrigation is proposed to be taken up from the left bank of the barrage through a pump house, lifted through a vertical tube and put into a distribution tank and further through a tube distribution system. The project covers 3838 villages in 18 taluks of Akola district. The area is 1397 of 5 villages in Balapur taluka. The area and 2 villages of Akot Taluka on the right bank is 110. Area and 7 villages of Telhara taluka is 1609. The total area is 6,954. It is proposed to provide irrigation benefits to the area.
A total of 8.17 million cubic metres of water will be produced in the project. For drinking water and industrial use and evaporation, the expenditure will be Rs. 2.54 lakh.
