the prelude
Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure Scheme by the Central Government Ministry of Finance, Government of India. The guidelines have been issued vide Office Memorandum dated 23.03.2021.
Accordingly, the Finance Department, Government of Maharashtra had informed about the opening of a new accounting head for the scheme.
Accordingly, a new head of account was under consideration of the Government for easy disbursement of non-interest bearing loans from the Central Government to the States for capital expenditure through the BEAMS system.
Government Decision The Central Government under the Department of Water Resources has approved the opening of new head of expenditure for the implementation of the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Expenditure to the State as follows.
