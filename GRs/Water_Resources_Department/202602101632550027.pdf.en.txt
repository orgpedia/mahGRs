The Proposal: -
Limbala is a Latur-style dam on the Terna River near the village of Libala in Latur district.
The barrage is located at a distance of 44.05 km from the site of the Terna Dam. The total projected water storage of the barrage is 0.283 Dalhousie and is 59. There is an irrigated area. The Limbala Latur system barrage was completed in 2015 and water was first stored by the construction department in 2015. The project has since been transferred to the Department of Management for maintenance and gate management in 2019.
Since the completion of the construction of the project, no repairs have been carried out to the gates and the high rise system in the dam. The Chief Engineer (Mechanics), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
On 12/9/2023, he visited various embankments / barrages on Manjra river, certified the need for repair of various machinery and electrical components and gave some suggestions in this regard, including the proposed green SAKATA. The Executive Director has directed that these works be proposed under special amendment as the required funds for carrying out the proposed works in connection with the repair of the mechanical and electrical components of the Libala Dam are not available at the corporation level. The Limbala Latur system dam was completed soon after the repair works of the dam's mechanical and electrical components were completed.

The leakage of water from the gate will stop. Also, the project proposed water storage will be available for irrigation during Rabi and Summer seasons at full capacity. The repair will also help in smooth operation of the gate and thus maintain the safety of the dam during flood control.
Limbala Dam is a specified dam as per the provision of the Dam Safety Act-2021. Therefore, since various mechanical and electrical components needed to be repaired for the safety of the Libala dam, a proposal was under consideration of the government to grant administrative approval for repair work of the mechanical component required for dam safety and easy gate operation of the Libala Latur system dam. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: - Limbala Latur system dam under Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation.
G. Latur, Dam Safety and Ease of Operation Special Repair of Mechanics and Electrical Components of the Dam at a cost of Rs. 1,77,39, 493 / - (Rs. The proposal for an expenditure of one crore seventy seven lakhs thirty one thousand four hundred and thirteen only) is being accorded administrative approval as per the accompanying form subject to the following conditions.
1) It shall be mandatory to comply with the prevailing Government Decisions / Circulars for technical approval, tendering process, planning and completion of work, expenditure within the limits of administrative approval, etc.
(2) No funds may be distributed or spent in excess of the generally approved administrative approval for any purpose for the purposes set out in this budget.
3) After the completion of this repair work, the zonal authorities will be fully responsible for transferring the benefit area by setting up a water use agency within the benefit area of the project.
4) Administrative approval means approval of the technical aspects of the report or the field level decision on the tender will not be considered.
5) Mechanical and electrical component budgets should be given technical approval at the competent level.
6) These special repair works should be undertaken by the Mechanical Association.
7) The revised cost of the thematic special repair work should be approved at the next meeting of the regulatory board.
8) Special efforts should be made by the zonal authorities to recover the catchment area.
The cost of the proposed work is being proposed under the head 2702 - Minor Irrigation, 01 - Maintenance and Repair, 03 - Minor Projects covered under Dam Safety Act 2029, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation (Marathwada) 2702 9729.
