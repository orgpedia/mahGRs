Government's decision
In order to ensure proper distribution of water in the upstream of Jayakwadi Dam, the guidelines have been prepared at the Government level taking into account the urgency, evaporation, transportation cost, etc. of the water in the Kharif season, as well as the difficulties encountered in implementation as per the report of the previous Committee dated 08.08.2013 and the changes in the water planning of the river basin in the intervening period. 1 study group was formed.
Mary, Director General, Nashik, the Chairperson of the Study Group, in her Reference No. As per the change suggested under Reference No. The Government's decision includes the following:
The Superintending Engineer, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad was appointed as a member of the study group. However, as the post is vacant, the Superintending Engineer, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad is being replaced by the Superintending Engineer and Administrator, Benefit Area Development Authority, Nashik as Member Secretary.
The following new members have been added as special invitees to the study group.
1) Superintending Engineer, Dams Board, Central Concepts Association, Nashik 2) Superintending Engineer, Benefit Area Development Authority, Aurangabad
The newly inducted special invitees will guide the committee as per the available information and studies related to the topic covered in the workshop of the above study group. They will also assist the study group in submitting the final report.
