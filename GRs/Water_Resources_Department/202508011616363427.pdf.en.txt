The Proposal:
Bhandardara Dam, considered the lifeline of the northern part of the Ahilyanagar district
(Early Revolutionary Veer Raghoji Bhangre Reservoir) on the Yar River at Ahilyanagar during the British period.
Constructed between 1910 and 1926, the dam completes 100 years in 2026.
Bhandardara Dam is located in the land of Akole Taluka with a natural and geographical background.
With the formation of this area, the taluks of Akole, Sangamner, Rahata, Shrirampur and Nevasa are irrigated.
The region has been heavily flooded, affecting agriculture, co-operation, and industry in the region.
Progress has been made. Also, it has led to the economic development of the Ahilyanagar district.
