The order:
Shrikant Madhukarrao Kulkarni, Branch Engineer (Jt. As the option of Revenue Department was not received by the Government within the stipulated time, he was promoted to the post of Sub-Divisional Officer (Architecture) and posted as Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur in the Nagpur Revenue Division as per the provisions of the" Maharashtra Government Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) Appointment of Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2015 for appointment by direct recruitment and promotion to the posts." However, Mr. Shrikant Madhukarrao Kulkarni, Branch Engineer (Jt. It has come to the notice of the government that the' B' bond of Aurangabad Revenue Department has been submitted to the office where he works. As per the provision of Rule No. 7 of the Maharashtra Government's Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) Appointments by Direct Service and Promotion in the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2015, Mr. Kulkarni is eligible to get the Aurangabad Revenue Department's preference rank given by him as there is less than 3 years left for his retirement.
Shrikant Madhukarrao Kulkarni has presented an option for permanent adjustment on the establishment of Soil and Water Conservation Department. In the list of Assistant Engineer Grade-II/ Branch Engineer submitted by the Department of Soil and Water Conservation in the press release dated 21.09.2017, Shri Kulkarni's Roll Number is 72.
As Shri Shrikant Madhukarrao Kulkarni is eligible to get the Aurangabad Revenue Division and has offered himself an option to be permanently absorbed in the establishment of the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the following changes are being made in the posting given to him by the Government vide order dated 25.04.2018.
