On 27/09/2018, regarding constitution of a committee to find out the reasons for the new Mutha right canal breach and to suggest measures.
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SAMRIDH-2018/ PR No. 379/18/ SAMRIDH (WORKS)
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 30/11/2018
Reference:- Government Resolution No. : Narikari-2018/ Q.No. 379/18/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 05/11/2018
Supplementary Government Decisions: Subject to the decisions of the Government in question. The work of the committee constituted to find out the reasons for the breach in 13, to find a solution plan accordingly and to propose guidelines, includes the following points: 1) Did the disaster management system at the Collectorate and Pune M.N.P. become operational immediately after the canal breach incident? Also to check what action was taken by the disaster management system, whether it was appropriate.
2) To check whether unauthorised water supply is being done by private water supply companies through tankers by taking unauthorised bore wells in the land adjacent to the New Mutha Canal.
The committee will submit its report to the government by December 15, 2018.
