Preamble: Flow diversion schemes and Upasa diversion schemes (river link projects) are being planned to divert water from the surplus basins in Marathwada or other deficit basins. To take these schemes step by step according to the availability of funds, it is necessary to determine the priorities of these schemes and to determine the priorities, first of all, the parameters of these turn schemes should be determined. For this purpose, the Executive Director, Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon, will head the committee and one chief engineer from each corporation will be appointed. The Study Group has been constituted as per the decision of the Government dated 13th August, 2018.
The mandate of the Study Group is to formulate parameters of Flow and Diversion Schemes for prioritization of river interlinking/ diversion schemes and to study the availability of water in which basins and which basins can be diverted sub-basin wise and to determine its primary viability and prioritize accordingly. The report of the study team should be in consonance with the State Water Plan. Accordingly, since the Marathwada region needs to study the proposed river diversion schemes for diverting water from other river basins, the tenure of the study group will be decided by the state government. It was released on September 15, 2018. Give it to him. Government Decision of October 12, 2018. The deadline has been extended to December 31, 2018.
