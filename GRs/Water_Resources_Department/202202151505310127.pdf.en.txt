The Proposal:
Under the Pali Bhutavali small scale scheme. An earthen dam with a capacity of 13.07 LM has been constructed at Karjat, G. Raigarh. The project is located in the" Rest of Maharashtra" region under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation.
A total of 950 people from 20 villages of Karjat taluka will be covered under this project. It is planned to provide irrigation benefits to the area through open canal and closed tube well.
