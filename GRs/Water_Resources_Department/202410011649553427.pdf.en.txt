The Proposal: -
Mojay Nilji-Bamni, Ta. In Miraj, District Sangli, the village suffered extensive damage due to river floods caused by heavy rains in the year 201920 and in the year 201920. The area is frequently flooded, causing damage to houses, roads, and graveyards along the river. Also, the land along the river bank is eroding due to erosion. Local representatives had informed the government about this. Accordingly, the field office has made a physical inspection and prepared the budget and submitted it to the government. Administrative approval of the budget was under the consideration of the government.
