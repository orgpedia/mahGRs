GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SUPRAMA-1013/ (92/2013) LAPA-1
This Government decision is based on the informal reference No.92/ K-1434 of the Planning Department, dated 25/04/2016 and informal reference No. 212/ Expenditure-12, D. As on 21/09/2018
As agreed by the Committee on Expenditure. As per the approval given in the meeting held on 05.11.2018 and vide reference No. 4 is being issued in accordance with the history issued in this regard.
The First Amended Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions:
4.1) Technical approval should be obtained for the cost involved in the proposed First Revised Administrative Sanction of the project.
4.2. Proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the project should be continued. The decision to grant the first revised administrative sanction shall not prejudice the proceedings of the departmental inquiry, whether ongoing or arising in future.
4.3 No additional expenditure or disbursement of funds shall be made beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for any purpose for the said scheme. In this regard, the prevailing government decisions, guidelines of CVC, limits of financial powers, prescribed tender procedures should be strictly implemented.
4.4) New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
4.5) The work of tenders in progress should be completed on priority. The priority of the remaining works should be determined in such a way that the water storage is maximized for irrigation and the works/ tenders should be processed accordingly.
4.6 The entire project should be completed within the proposed First Amendment Administrative Sanction Price.
4.7. The said revised administrative approval shall not be construed as approval of irregularities at various stages of implementation of the project. If any irregularity is found in this work before or after the grant of this revised administrative approval, it cannot be assumed that it has been regularized due to this revised administrative approval.
4.8) The project work should be completed quickly and the completion report of the project should be submitted to the government for approval.
The first revised administrative approval granted to the scheme is recorded in the register of administrative approvals at serial number (191).
