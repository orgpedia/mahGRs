Preface: The Pursuit, p. The village of Shahuwadi, Kolhapur, is situated on the banks of the Kadvi river. As the flood and water pressure are causing extensive damage to the houses near the nearby agriculture, village roads, public buildings and government property, a proposal has been received from the field level on the demand of the local people's representatives to get the work of flood protection wall done to protect the river bank and embankment on both banks from the flood water.
Mouze Perid, Ta. The Regional Office conducted a physical inspection at the proposed site in Shahuwadi, G. Kolhapur and submitted the budget of the flood protection wall work to the government. Mouze Perid, Ta. The proposal for construction of flood protection wall along the Kadvi river in Shahuwadi, Kolhapur was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government Resolution: Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune under Mauje Perid, Ta. Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Rate Based Budget of Water Resources Department for the year 2022-23 and Public Works Department for the year 2022-23 with an outlay of Rs.73, 14, 643.00/- (Rupees Seventy Three Lakhs Fourteen Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Three) subject to the following conditions: (a) The Government to take action as per the provisions of the Resolution No.
(b) Government Circular No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 21/09/1989 and dt. As per 03/05/2018, the alignment of flood protection wall should be fixed only after the flood line is drawn.
No work should be undertaken without proper planning.
