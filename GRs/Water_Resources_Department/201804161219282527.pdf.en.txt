The concerned Director General/ Executive Director/ Chief Engineer is informed that the orders for the detailed posting of the said officers should be circulated to the concerned immediately. Also, the officer should be relieved immediately to appear at the place of transfer. If any Deputy Engineer is working in the post indicated above, then the Deputy Engineer who has been transferred to such post should not be posted and his detailed proposal should be submitted to the Government for approval.
Arrangements should be made to send the confidential report, service book, and final salary certificate of the transferred officer to the new office immediately.
(5) No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned without the prior permission of the Government for any reason. If the transferred officer sends the leave application to the parent office, the application will not be taken into consideration.
(6) While appointing the officer transferred by this order, the concerned Administrative Officer/ Executive Engineer shall verify whether he was on leave earlier or how, and if found to be on leave, shall take action in accordance with Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 before taking him on leave and submit a report to the Government.
