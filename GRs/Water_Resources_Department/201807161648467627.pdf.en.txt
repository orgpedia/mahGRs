The proposal-
Bembla, a major project in Babhulgaon taluka of Yavatmal district, is one of the projects covered under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY). The project will create irrigation potential of about 47,000 hectares in 117 villages of Babhulgaon, Kalamb, Rathegaon and Maregaon taluks through a 113 km long canal under the PMKSY scheme. The project will create drip irrigation potential for 7,804 hectares in 15 villages of Ner and Babhulgaon taluks of the northern part of the district. The construction work of this drip irrigation scheme is being carried out by the Bembla Rehabilitation Sub-Division No. 3, Thalegaon under Sub-Divisional Office, the scheme covers component works like Architecture/ Mechanics/ Electrical/ Agricultural Marketing and Automation.
