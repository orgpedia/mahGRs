Introduction: - Hosur Bandhara is located on the Manjra River near Takalgaon Temple village in Latur district. The barrage is located at a distance of 145.85 km from the Manjra dam site. The total projected storage of the barrage is 2. 25 Dalhousie which is 299. There is an irrigated area. The Hosur Barrage was completed in 2016 and water was first stored by the Construction Department in 2016. The project has since been transferred to the Department of Management for maintenance and gate management in 2019.
Since the completion of the construction of the project, no repairs have been carried out to the gates and the high rise system in the dam. The Chief Engineer (Mechanics), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
On 12/9/2023, he visited various dams / barrages on Manjra river and certified the need for repair of various mechanical and electrical components therein and gave some suggestions in this regard, including the proposed Hosur dam. The Executive Director has directed that these works be proposed under special amendment as the required funds for carrying out the proposed works in connection with the repair of machinery and electrical components of Hosur Dam are not available at the corporation level. The Hosur High Level Dam will stop the leakage of water from the gate once the repair works of the mechanical and electrical components of the dam are completed. Also, the project proposed water storage will be available for irrigation during Rabi Summer season at full capacity.

As various mechanical and electrical components are therefore required to be repaired for the safety of Hosur Dam, the Hosur High Level Dam T.G.
