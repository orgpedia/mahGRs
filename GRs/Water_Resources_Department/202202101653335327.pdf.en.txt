(1) Government Decision No: Review 2019/ P.No. 95/19/ Aastha (Reorganization), D. July 21, 2020.
A total of 123 projects are under construction in Nagpur and Amravati divisions, including 7 projects under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) and 69 projects under Baliraja Jal Sanjeevi Yojana (BJSY) and 47 other regular projects. For quality control of the remaining works of Jigaon Bada Project under Amravati region, Katepurna in Akola district and Khadakpurna in Buldhana district, the Quality Control Department at Khamgaon has been made operational as per the decision of the government under reference and the departments, sub-divisional offices and laboratories under 3 circles of Quality Control Board, Pune, Aurangabad and Nagpur have been reorganized. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Superintending Engineer, Under Executive Engineer, Quality Control Board, Nagpur and the Quality Control Department, Khamgaon as Drawing and Disbursing Officers for all purposes. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Superintending Engineer, Under Executive Engineer, Quality Control Board, Nagpur, Quality Control Department, Khamgaon is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for all purposes. This Government decision has been taken as per the provisions of Rule No. 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 vide Informal Reference No. 2021/ 230/ TOSH-PRISA-5, D. is being issued as per the approval granted under 28.12.2021.
