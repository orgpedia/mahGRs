The Proposal: -
Socks have come, Ta. Shirol, District Kolhapur is located at a distance of 15.00 km from Shirol in Shirol taluka of Kolhapur district. The Krishna River flows on the western side of the Alas and floods occur every monsoon. The heavy rains in 2019 and 2021 have caused extensive economic damage to many houses along the river, temples, agriculture, cemeteries, roads, etc. Also, there is extensive erosion of agricultural land along the river. People's representatives have demanded the construction of a flood protection wall to prevent future damage to houses, cemeteries, and roads along the river, which are flooded every monsoon. Accordingly, the zonal office has conducted a physical inspection and submitted the budget to the government. The issue of administrative approval of the budget was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - Socks came under Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune, Dt. For the construction of the proposed flood protection wall over Krishna River at Shirol, District Kolhapur, an amount of Rs. 4,94,69, 244.00 / - (Rs. 2) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, the externalities of the tariff list and the carrying distance at the competent level.
