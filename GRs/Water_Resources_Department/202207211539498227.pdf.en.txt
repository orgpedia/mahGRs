Introduction: Mula Irrigation Project has two major canals namely Mula Right Bank Canal and Mula Left Bank Canal. The original right bank canal is 52 km long and was completed in 1972-73. The projected water carrying capacity from the origin is 1650 cusecs. The water management of the entire benefit area of the Mula Dam project has been transferred to the Water Utilization Agency since 2006. All water use agencies are functional and water is being metered as per demand at government rates.

Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation Limited, Aurangabad 00/00 to 24/00 km. A special budget of Rs. 3,65,22, 797/- (Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs.3 crore sixty five lakh twenty two thousand seven hundred and ninety nine only) subject to the following conditions in the accompanying proforma.
(1) It shall be mandatory to comply with the prevailing Government decisions/ circulars for technical approval, tender process, planning and completion of work, expenditure within the limits of administrative approval, etc.
2) Save by making full use of the construction materials available through excavation and demolition caused by the proposed works.
(3) There shall be no further disbursement of funds or any additional expenditure beyond the limit of administrative sanction generally approved for any purpose for the purposes of the said budget.
After the completion of the repair work, the entire responsibility of transferring the benefit area to the water utility will be of the field officer.
