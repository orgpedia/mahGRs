Preface: Savanas Tumbad, Ta. Khed, G. Ratnagiri The project is located on Dabhol creek on the bank of Jagbudi river.
Savanas Tumbde, Ta. Khed, G. Ratnagiri is included in the larger scheme of Kharbhoomi Development Scheme in Ratnagiri district. The scheme is included in the total of 575 (170 mangrove schemes in Ratnagiri district) mangrove schemes in Konkan which are exempted from the Marine Regulation Act, 1991 vide letter No. J 17011/32/2001/ I-3/ dated 22/8/2003 of Ministry of Environment and Forests, New Delhi. This scheme is Board time and the maintenance repair work of the scheme has been completed in 1968. The project is 53 years in the making. The project will be spread over an area of 57 acres. The total length of the embankment of the plan is 1940 meters, and on this plan 360 meters of the c. 660 m. And there are 3 such openings at 1470 m.
As the work of this scheme has been completed for 53 years and the saline water is entering the receiving area due to frequent tides and causing damage to the agricultural land, the local MLA has instructed that the work of the scheme should be done as per the broad plan and the beneficiary farmers on the scheme should get the work of the scheme. The cost of the project is Rs. 3,28,416/- which is Rs. is less than 3,58,020/-. This scheme fits into the criteria.
The letter was received from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai. The Chief Engineer has recommended to the government to give administrative approval to the renovation work of Khed, G. Ratnagiri. The proposal was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
