Proposal: - The Dry Storage Lake is a minor project under Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar in the general area of Marathwada region. Under the scheme, it is proposed to construct an earthen dam with a capacity of 4. 7 gallons on a local rivulet near Sukli village in Sengaon taluka of Hingoli district. The total annual water consumption of the project is 4.77 million litres. A total of 677 villages in Sukli and Datala of Sengaon taluka have been covered under this project. It is planned to give the benefit of irrigation to the area.
The project is worth Rs. The proposal for administrative approval of the 124.36 crore (rate list 2022-23) price was submitted to the government by the Executive Director, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar in a letter of reference.
The proposal has been approved by the Hon'ble Cabinet in its meeting dated 11/11/2025. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken:
