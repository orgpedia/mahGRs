The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the Central Government's Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Full Participation and Protection of Rights) Act, 1995, the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C and D in the direct service/ nomination quota in the Water Resources Department are referred to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
As per the decision of the government here 1 and 2 were ensured for the disabled. The Central Government has passed the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Full Participation and Protection of Rights) Act, 1995. The Maharashtra State Policy for Persons with Disabilities has been approved under the Government Resolution No. 4 of the General Administration Department, prescribing 4% reservation for Physically Handicapped persons in government service posts.
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Central Government vide notification dated 4.1.2021 in Reference No. 5, has notified the posts reserved for persons with disabilities.
