GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative approval is being accorded for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for Nar-Par-Girna Upsurge Link Scheme at an estimated cost of Rs. 13,51,25, 395/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Eleven Lakh Twenty Five Thousand Three Hundred and Ninety Five only).
