The Proposal: -
Moje Koti Taluka Karad is a village on the right bank of the Krishna River near the Pune Bangalore Highway. There is a demand from the local populist for the construction of a flood protection wall at the Koteshwar temple adjacent to the village cemetery B in Mauje Korti taluka Karad. The construction site is in Korti village on the right bank of the Krishna River, on the SA side of the K.P. Bandhara Konegaon, at the confluence of the Tarli and Krishna rivers. On the upstream side of Korti village on the Krishna River are the Dhaum, Kanher and Tarlo dams, respectively, which are flooded every year. The flood waters are causing damage to the river bank near the Koteshwar temple at the Koti crematorium site. Therefore, the people's representatives there have demanded that this flood protection wall should be built in view of safety. Accordingly, the zonal office has conducted a physical inspection and submitted the budget to the government.
The issue of administrative approval of the budget was under consideration of the government.
