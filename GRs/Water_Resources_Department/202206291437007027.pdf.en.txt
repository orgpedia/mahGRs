4. All the Circle Officers are informed that the decision of the Government, d. In the year 31.01.1989, when the category of Civil Engineering Assistant was created, the employees who did not have the necessary departmental training for inclusion in the post of Civil Engineering Assistant from the lower cadre have been included in the post of Civil Engineering Assistant. Apart from the technical and supervisory posts from the subordinate offices, it is also seen that the employees of labor or other non-technical posts have also been included in the post of Civil Engineering Assistant. Public Works Department Notification regarding Inclusion of such employees in the Civil Engineering Cadre, d. 18.06.1998 and dt. 0808.2001 and Government Resolution, Dt. Necessary action as per provisions of 31.07.2013 has been directed. The concerned offices are supposed to take action to cancel the inclusion of employees who have been included in the Civil Engineering Assistant Cadre in a wrong way. After the Government receives the order regarding cancellation of the inclusion of the wrongfully included Civil Engineering Assistants, if the name of those employees is included in the final seniority list of the Civil Engineering Assistant cadre, then the names of such Civil Engineering Assistants will be excluded from the final seniority list.
