The proposal: The National Green Tribunal (NGT), Western Zone, Pune Bench, in Petition No. Pursuant to the order dated 25/2014 issued vide letter dated 11th August, 2020 by the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune, it has been decided to fix the flood line of remaining length of river Seena from km to km 121, Khar from km 0 to 48, Vincharna from km 0 to 36 and other tributaries in Ahmednagar district. Administrative approval for the budget of Rs. 311.39 lakh was under consideration at the Government level.

Administrative approval is being accorded for the budget of Rs. 311.39 lakh.
The expenditure for the proposed works is to be made under the head Capital Expenditure on I-5, 4711 Flood Control Project, 01 Flood Control, 190, Investments in Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (01) Share Capital Contribution to various Irrigation Development Corporations (01) Share Capital Contribution to Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation (Programme) (4711 0209) and to be met from the sanctioned grants.
