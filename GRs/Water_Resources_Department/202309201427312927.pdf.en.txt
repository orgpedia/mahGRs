The project has been constructed on the Adla river in the Pravara sub-basin of the Godavari basin near Padoshi village in the hilly region of Akole taluka of Ahmednagar district. The project falls in the catchment area of Adla Medium Project. Under this project, a small irrigation tank has been constructed. Akole G. Ahmednagar and Flow Turning Scheme Waghera Tt. Trimbak G. Nashik is included in this scheme. L. P.
The project does not have a canal as it is a storage pond. Under this project, it has been planned to bring the hilly areas under irrigation. The beneficiaries of the project will get water at their own cost. The total water storage available in the project is 4.13 LHM.
Flow Turning Scheme Waghera Ta. Trimbak Ji Nashik is built on a local drain near Waghera village. In order to meet the ever increasing demand for non-irrigated water in the Godavari basin and consequent reduction in irrigated area, in order to reduce the water deficit in the Godavari basin to some extent, a diversion scheme has been worked out to divert the surplus water flowing westwards into the Godavari basin by impounding the Drainage Barriers on the Western Vahini River/ rivulets adjoining the Upper Godavari basin. Under this, Waghera village in Damanganga valley. Trimbak G. Nashik is included in this scheme. Under the scheme, 1.19 LHW of water has been diverted through link cuts.
