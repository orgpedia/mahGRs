The Department of Water Resources has taken a policy decision to privatize the development of small hydroelectric projects up to 25 MW capacity. PVT-1204/ (160/2004)/ JV, Dy. notified under 15.09.2005. In pursuance of this policy, Govt. The guidelines have been issued vide Government Circular dated 06.11.2007. However, it has been observed that there are doubts among the field officials as to the level at which action should be taken as per the provisions of the said policy. Therefore, the matter was under the consideration of the government to circulate the revised circular.
(2) Suggest the level of action required to be taken under the provisions of this policy to implement the policy of privatization of small hydroelectric projects in an expeditious and effective manner. The same is given under the Annexure to the Government Circular dated 06.11.2007 for further clarification. The Government letter dated 19.07.2016 has been issued. However, since the administrative time taken for decision making process is found to be long, the Annexure has been reviewed and a revised procedure has been prescribed and annexed as Annexure. However, action should be taken by the field officers at various levels as per this schedule and the action taken should be informed to the government from time to time.
