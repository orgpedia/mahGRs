the prelude
The 44.80-km long Pravara Right Canal, which starts from Bhandardara Dam on the Pravara River, and continues for 76.80-km, is the longest canal in the country. The Pravara Left Canal was completed during the British rule from 1926 to 1930. Through Pravara Right and Left Canal a total of 23077 hectare area of Sangamner, Shrirampur, Nevasa and Rahuri taluks of Ahmednagar district gets the benefit of irrigation.
As per the directions of the Hon'ble High Court dated 23/09/2016, in order to make the water distribution system suitable and efficient, as per the proposal received, the Bhandardara Mega Project has been completed.
