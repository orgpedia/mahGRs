The Department of Water Resources,

The Proposal:
Pachora G.
Jalgaon is a village situated on the bank of the river Hivra. There are some houses and religious places near the river bank. Hivra River is a tributary of Girni River and is one of the major rivers in Pachora taluka. The Saptashrungi Maroti temple complex in Mau, Sarola, B.T. Pachora has been washed away due to the discharge of Hivra river. Mr. A.T. (Nana) Patil, MP, Shri. As per the request made to the Minister (Water Resources) and the Gram Panchayat, Shri. As per the demand made by Sarola B. T. Pachora, the government was considering granting administrative approval to the work of the flood protection wall proposal.
Government's decision:
1) As per the decision of this Government, the Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon under Mauje Sarola Bu.
For the construction of flood protection wall near Saptashrungi Maroti Temple on Hivra River in Pachora G. Jalgaon to prevent danger to religious places, people's property and life, Rs. Administrative sanction is being accorded for the cost of Rs. 67,91,443/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Nine Thousand Four Hundred and Forty Three only) subject to the following conditions.
(a) Government Circular No.FDW-1092/ (170)/ CV (Works), d. Action should be taken in accordance with the provisions of 7.8.1992.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PRASHAMA-2019/ (P.S. 37/2019)/ Sec. V. (Revenue)
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
d) The cost of the work is to be completed within the limit of revised administrative approval from the available grant and within the scheduled time.
e) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, it should be ensured that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
e) After the implementation of the flood protection scheme, it should be ensured in the planning that the drainage of the area will be done in the proper manner.
e) After completion of the project, the flood protection wall will be constructed. Pachora, G. Jalgaon is to be taken over by the Gram Panchayat for future maintenance and repairs. Before starting the work, write a letter. Sarola Ta. Get it from the Gram Panchayat.
(f) To submit quarterly progress reports to the Government from the date of issue of the work order till the completion of the work. After the completion of the work, the certificate should be provided to the government immediately.
The expenditure for the proposed works is to be made under the head Demand No. I-5, 4711 Capital expenditure on Flood Control Project, 1 Flood Control, 190, Investments in Public Sector and other activities (1) Share Capital Contribution to various Irrigation Development Corporations (1) (2) Share Capital Contribution (Programme) to Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation (47110218) and to be met from the sanctioned grants.
(3) The administrative approval granted to this work is given in the register of the CV (Revenue) Office No. It was recorded in 2018-2019.
The decision is being issued with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and Joint Secretary and the financial powers vested in the departments as per the Financial Powers Rules, 1978.
