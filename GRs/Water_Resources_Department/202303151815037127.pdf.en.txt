The Proposal:
Domihira River Project is a minor irrigation scheme. An earthen dam of 14.49 LH capacity has been constructed on the Domihira river in Palghar. The project is located in the" Rest of Maharashtra" region under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation. The project envisages to provide irrigation facility through canals and closed pipes to a total of 685 hectares of tribal areas covering an area of 657 hectares in four villages of Jawhar taluka and 28 hectares in one village of Mokhada taluka.
