Government's decision:
With this decision of the Government, administrative approval is being accorded to the Fourth Revised Project Report of Rs. 169.14 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Seventy-Nine Crore Fourteen Lakh only) based on the 2016-17 schedule of the Dry Medium Projects. Out of this, a sum of Rs. 163.31 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Sixty Three Crores Thirty One Lakhs only) includes provision of Rs. 5.83 crore (Rupees Five Crores Thirty Three Lakhs only) for work expenses, establishment and incidental expenses. Sub-heading provisions are being added to the Goshawara.
The fourth revised administrative approval granted to the scheme has been recorded in the Medium Project Operations Register at serial number 03/2023.
The project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the revised administrative approval.

The Fourth Amended Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions:
5.1) The observations in point number 27 (2) of the report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee should be complied with.
5.2) Work should not be started until all statutory approvals have been obtained.
5.3. The concept of a tube distribution system should be examined by the Central Resolution Picture Organization and then the cost-effective alternative of tube distribution or open canal should be certified and selected by the Executive Director.
5.4) New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval. Also, make sure that the cost of the project does not increase.
