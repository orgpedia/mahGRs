The Proposal:
The Angie L.P. Project, Ta. Ralegaon, G. Yavatmal is a small project built in 1985 on a local canal near Anji village in Ralegaon taluka of Yavatmal district. The catchment area of the project is 10.10 sq.km. I'm getting rain. The length of the earth dam of the project is 385 meters. The maximum height of the earthen dam is 20.38 m. The length of the shaft is and the maximum projected discharge of the sludge is 291.50 km/ s.
The length of the main canal and its distributary is 2.50 km with a carrying capacity of 0.38 km/ s. The total water storage of the project is 2.80 Dalghami and the useful water storage is 2.69 Dalghami. The total area of the project is 858 hectares. The total irrigation potential of the project is 569 hectares. The head level of the dam is buried in some chains as the construction of the project has completed almost 30 years. Also, the main reservoir is overflowing due to damage to the inner and outer walls of the dam. The dam's shaft has also been damaged to some extent and minor repairs have been proposed for its safety. The proposal has also been made as a large number of trees have grown on the slopes on both sides of the dam and it is necessary to clean the dam in accordance with safety. The proposal also includes provision for pitching rearrangement as the ashfall on the upper side of the dam has been disturbed in some areas.
