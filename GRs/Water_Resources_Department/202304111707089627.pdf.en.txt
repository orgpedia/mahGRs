Finance Department Reference No. 1 here. As per the provision of the Government Decision dated 21/06/2006, the Ministry of Water Resources Department (itself) is under the consideration of the Government to convert 80 percent of the total 409 posts into permanent posts as per the revised figure of posts in the establishment.
Government Decision: As per the revised figure of posts in the Water Resources Department (itself) establishment, a total of 409 posts have been sanctioned. Out of these sanctioned posts, 327 posts are to be made permanent. (1) Approval is being granted subject to the conditions contained in the Government Resolution dated 21/06/2006. The classification of these posts as permanent/ temporary is being done as shown in the attached Form-A.
Also, the Department of Water Resources, Govt. As on 26.06.2022, all posts of Drivers and Trackers in Group-C and Group-D Cadre have been declared as dead cadre. The number of posts has been fixed as many employees will be working in the dead cadre. The employees of the posts declared as dead cadre will continue to work in the superannuation post till their retirement. The posts will be vacant on account of retirement/ resignation/ death of the employees.
