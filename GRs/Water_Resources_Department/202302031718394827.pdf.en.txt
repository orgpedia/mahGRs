Introduction: The Ambadi Medium Project has been constructed on the Ambadi River near Andhaner village in Kannad taluka and the construction of the project has been completed in 1980. The total irrigated area of the project is ICA 2147 hectares and the right canal is 11.58 km. M. and Left Canal 6.20 km. I'm long. The length of the earthen dam of Ambadi Medium Project is 1850 meters, the maximum height of the dam is 22.28 meters and the length of the sewerage is 295 meters.
The mud wall of Ambadi medium project is buried everywhere. The road is also in a bad condition. As water evaporates from the tank, it is lost. Therefore, it is necessary to repair the mud wall of the dam, as well as repair the shandas and guide walls. Also, some work needs to be done to make the necessary system for measuring water at various places on the canal.
Against this backdrop, the government was considering a proposal to grant administrative approval under a special amendment to the budget of the Ambadi medium project in order to carry out the main dam and embankment repair work and other works of the canal from the perspective of dam safety. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Ambadi Middle Taluk under Aurangabad. The project worth Rs. 3,75,93, 099/- (Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 3 crore, seventy five lakh, ninety three thousand ninety nine only) subject to the following conditions, in the accompanying proforma.
1) It will be mandatory to follow the prevailing government decisions/ circulars for technical approval, tender process, planning and completion of work, spending within the limits of administrative approval, etc.
2) Save by making full use of the construction materials available through excavation and demolition caused by the proposed works.
(3) There shall be no further disbursement of funds or any additional expenditure beyond the limit of administrative sanction generally approved for any purpose for the purposes of the said budget.
