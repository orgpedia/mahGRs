Preamble: Jui Dhaddi Kharbhoomi Scheme, Ta. Uran, G. Raigad is included in the Kharbhoomi scheme in the larger scheme. The Central Government through the Marine Regulation Act, 1991 vide its letter (English) No. J/ 170011/ (32) 2001-1 A-|| As on 22nd August 2003, 575 Kharbhoomi Scheme works have been exempted. This includes the plan. C. No. 175 m. 220 m. 2102 M.C. 2973 m. 3583 M.C. There are 5 such rectangular openings. The maximum height of the dam is 3.82 m. The top of the dam is 3.00 m wide.
Therefore, the government has decided to strengthen the embankment and repair the embankments. As on 19.06.2016, Rs. Administrative approval was granted for renovation works worth Rs. 3.17 crore. However, since the cost of completing the remaining work of the scheme will be more than the original administrative approval budget, the budget will have to get revised administrative approval. Based on the urgency of the work, the demand of the local people's representatives and the villagers, the scheme has been named Jui Dhaddadi Kharbhumi Yojana. The dam of the project is proposed to be double protected. The cost of the project is Rs. 1,66,021/- against the prevailing monetary norm of Rs. 1,92,950/- received from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai under reference letter. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
