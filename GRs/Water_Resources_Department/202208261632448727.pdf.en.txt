Out of the total 48 posts, 30 posts are in the office of Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai and 18 posts are in the office of Technical Cell (Water Works), Konkan Region, Mumbai. 1 The extension was granted vide Government Resolution dated 28.02.2022 from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022. Accordingly, a total of 48 posts comprising of 30 posts on the fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai and 18 posts on the fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Technical Cell (Water Works), Konkan Region, Mumbai, have been sanctioned for extension beyond 31.08.2022. The proposal has been submitted in 2 letters. Finance Department Reference No. In pursuance of the Government decision dated 18.08.2022, the following decisions are being taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 48 posts comprising of 30 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai and 18 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Technical Cell (Water Works), Konkan Region, Mumbai are being sanctioned by extension from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure A.
a) The fixed temporary posts should not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The posts on fixed temporary establishment shall be within the limit of 45297 posts as per the revised figure approved under the Government Decision dated 06.02.2003.
The expenditure on establishment of the above offices shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the head" Demand No. 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Projects, Loans to 80 General, 190 Public Sector and other Undertakings (04) Share Capital Contribution (Salary) to various Irrigation Development Corporations (04) (03) Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation Share Capital Contribution (Compulsory) (4701 I-189)."
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Finance Department, No. Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18.08.2022.
