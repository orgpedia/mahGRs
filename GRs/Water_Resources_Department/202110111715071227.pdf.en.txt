Preamble: Chorkhar Annapurna Kharbhumi Yojana, Ta. Bhiwandi, Thane district is situated between Maje Paye, Malodi, Kharbavan villages. The Chorkhar Annapurna Kharbhoomi Scheme is located on the right bank of the Ulhas creek on a tributary of the Kamwadi river. This scheme has been included in the broad outline of Kharbhoomi Development Scheme in Thane district vide No. 08 and Page No. Includes 34. The scheme is also included in the total 575 (108 Kharbhoomi Schemes in Thane District) Kharbhoomi Schemes in Konkan which are exempted from the Marine Regulation Act, 1991 vide letter No. J- 17011/32/2001/ I-3/ dated 22/8/2003 of the Union Ministry of Environment and Forests, New Delhi. This scheme is board time and was constructed by Kharland Board in 1958. The project was completed in 1988. The total length of the project is 4160 meters.
Due to continuous heavy rains, the flood level in Ulhas creek has increased and the water from the creek has entered the reclaimed area of the scheme, causing extensive damage to the embankment. Local farmers and local people's representatives have been repeatedly demanding the construction of the scheme. The cost of the project is Rs. 1,81,409/- against the prevailing monetary norm of Rs. 1,92,950/- respectively.
Chorkhar Annapurna Kharbhoomi Scheme, received under reference from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai. The proposal to give administrative approval to the renovation work of the Kharbhoomi scheme in Bhiwandi and G. Thane was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
