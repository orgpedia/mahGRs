The Proposal:
Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation Limited, Aurangabad.
Nanded village is located near the Vishnupuri project. The left bank of the 75-metre-long bridge has been eroded due to the flood in front of the Guide Wall constructed on the left side of the Vishnupuri project. Therefore, the construction of residential houses, auditoriums, etc. in the Kaleswar Temple (Shiva Temple) area of Kotitirtha village on the left bank may be threatened by the rain flood. This work needs to be done so that the area around the village does not get washed away and the people of the village can go down to the river for religious work. This was announced by the executive director of the Godavari Marathwada Development Corporation, Aurangabad. In view of the above situation, the matter of granting administrative approval to the above work was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
1) As per the decision of this Government, the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation Limited, Aurangabad, has constructed a special structure at Mauje Kotitirtha, Nanded, in front of the existing guide wall on the left side of the Shankarrao Chavan Vishnupuri Project. G.
Rs. Administrative Sanction is being granted for an expenditure of Rs. 4,49,62, 400/- (Rupees Four Crore Fifty-Eight Lakh Sixty-two Thousand Four Hundred) subject to the following conditions.
(a) Government Circular No.FDW-1092/ (170)/ S.V. (Works), d. to be dealt with in accordance with the provisions of 7.8.1992.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.

(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
