Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERRI), Nashik JO No. the letter dated 04.01.2023.
Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERI), Nashik is a premier engineering institute of the state of Maharashtra. It has been serving the State of Maharashtra and other States of India in the field of Water Resources Department, Public Works Department for the last 63 years. During his long service in the institute, Nagarkar has contributed immensely to the reputation of Mary, Nashik Institute by his great achievements in the field of research problems, test works, research articles, etc. Accordingly, the main building of the Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERI), Nashik (with a seating capacity of 200 persons) was inaugurated by Shri.
A proposal to rename the hall as' Nagarkar Hall" was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. The auditorium in the main building of Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERI), Nashik (with seating arrangement for 200 persons) is being named as" Shri.
