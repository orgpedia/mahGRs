the prelude
To complete 91 irrigation projects in the state, an eight-digit computer code is required to disburse the funds received from the state and the central government under the Baliraja Jal Sanjeevi Yojana (BJSY), as well as to repay the loan and interest received from NABARD on the state's share. As per the approval given by the Accountant General, Nagpur/ Mumbai, the Department of Finance has provided the following eight digit computer codes.
Government Resolution The Department of Finance has provided eight digit computer codes for head accounts 4701, 4702, 6003 and 2049 as follows.
