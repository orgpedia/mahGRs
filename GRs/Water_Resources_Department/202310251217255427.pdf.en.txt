The Proposal:
Creation of 30 posts on the fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Nagpur. 1 decision was made by the government. Reference No. 4, under the Government Order of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 for the temporary posts included in the figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the figure.
