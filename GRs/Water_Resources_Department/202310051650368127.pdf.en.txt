Preamble: A total of 55 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and Small Irrigation Cell, Thane under the Corporation have been notified vide Reference No. 1 The extension was granted vide Government Resolution dated 06.04.2023 from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Accordingly, a total of 55 posts, including 41 posts in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and 14 posts in the office of the Small Irrigation Cell, Thane, under the office of the Corporation, have been approved for extension beyond 31.08.2023. The proposal has been submitted in 2 letters. Finance Department Reference No. 3 In pursuance of the Government decision dated 31.08.2023, the following decisions are being taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 55 posts, including 41 posts in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and 14 posts in the office of the Small Irrigation Cell, Thane, under the Corporation, are being sanctioned for extension from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure A.
a) The fixed temporary posts should not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The posts on fixed temporary establishment shall be within the limit of 45297 posts as per the revised figure approved under the Government Decision dated 06.02.2003.
