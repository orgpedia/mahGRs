Preamble: To develop a computer system for conceptualizing canal structures for the Water Resources Department. The Committee was constituted as per the Government Resolution dated 31.5.2017. The committee Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 3.3.2018. The period has been extended up to 30.4.2018. However, since the committee's work was pending, the government was considering extending the term of the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to bring uniformity in the concept of construction on canals with a capacity of less than 1 hm/ s, the Committee constituted to study the concept prevalent under various Corporations and standardize the canal construction concept drawings for use at the State level is being extended till 31st May, 2018 subject to the condition that the report shall be submitted to the Government by 31st May, 2018.
