GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad Office Reference No. As proposed under section 8, 74 posts on temporary establishment in the office of the Executive Director, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, as shown in the accompanying Annexure A, are proposed to be filled up vide reference No. 7 In accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments by the decision of the Finance Department herein, the 01/09/2021 to Th. Extension up to 28/02/2022 is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
3) These positions will be approved according to the figure.
4) None of the above positions will be filled.
(5) Thereafter, the prescribed form shall be submitted to the Government along with the date of the proposal, the prescribed form, the certificate, the names of the posts in the converted temporary establishment and the date of retirement.
(2) The expenditure for the payment of salary for the said posts shall be covered under the following heads and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the same.
Fixed Temporary Establishment: Demand No. I-5, 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, (80) General, (190) Investment in Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (04) Share Capital Contribution (Salary) to various Irrigation Development Corporations, (04) (05) Share Capital Contribution (Compulsory) to Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation (4701 I 204).
This Government Decision is based on the Government Decision of the Department of Finance, No. 8/ 16/ A.P.C., D. As per the authorization granted to the administrative departments under 10/08/2021, the same is being issued with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary.
