Introduction Under the Government decision dated 2 nd March, 2021 of the Finance Department, it has been made mandatory to use the Integrated Finance Management System (IFMS), a self-provisioning accounting system, in all the Irrigation Corporations of the Water Resources Department from 1 nd April, 2022.
Accordingly, in order to start the use of this system in the stipulated time, the Water Resources Department, with the consent of the Finance Department, has issued a notification. As per the Government decision dated 28/12/2021, 129 Heads of Departments under Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane Corporation were declared as Disbursing Officers. As per the said Government decision (Annexure 2), 36 Executive Engineers of Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad were declared as" Disbursing Officer under PLA Account."
But reference No. As per the proposal received under Section 2, the Government has received a proposal for declaration of new disbursement figures for the 10 additional Executive Engineer Offices. Accordingly, the government was considering declaring 10 heads of departments under the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, as distribution officers for the system. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION 1. The present working of the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, has been transferred to the Private Private Accounts Account No. 9190 Scheme No. 84430081 Treasury Office Aurangabad
It's here. The Executive Director has been authorised to withdraw funds from the said personal provident fund account. However, since the newly developed Integrated Finance: Management System (FMS) requires the disbursement of funds by the Executive Engineer attached to the Aurangabad Treasury from the Corporation's own accounts, the 10 Executive Engineers listed in Annexure-A are being declared as" Disbursing Officer under PLA Account." They are also included in the list in Annexure-A.
