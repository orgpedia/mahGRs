Introduction The Government have decided to bring uniformity in the accounts and ancillary records of the Corporations and its offices under the Water Resources Department, to make the information available through it more useful and easy to use and to make it possible to update the accounts of the Corporations promptly. As on 22/07/2015.
In order to ensure effective control over the allocation of funds to the Corporations and their subordinate field offices at the government level and the expenditure incurred thereon, a committee of officers has been constituted to develop a system like BEAMS (Budget Estimation, Allocation and Monitoring System).
Accordingly, a new system called Integrated Finance Management System (IFMS) has been developed by the National Informatics Centre. After this system was applied to the corporation under the Water Resources Department, the government was considering declaring the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the corporation as the Controlling Officer and the Accounts Officer as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw the funds from the treasury. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision
Budget Estimation, Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) has been developed by the National Informatics Centre (NIC). To withdraw funds from the treasury through this system, the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the corporation is required to be declared as Controlling Officer and its Internal Accounts Officer as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Accordingly, the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer of the Corporation is declared as Controlling Officer and Senior Accounts Officer/ Accounts Officer is declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
This order has been issued vide order No. 18/2020/ TASCHE-PRASA-5 of the Department of Finance. Issued on 21/01/2020.
