Preamble: As per the Government Decision dated 18.11.2008, 13 posts have been sanctioned for the Executive Engineer, Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Special Team, Pune on internal rotation basis from the office of the Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation and the establishment which has been additional under the review of the Water Resources Department. Similarly, 4 additional posts have been made available as per Government Decision dated 01.08.2009. A total of 17 posts have been sanctioned for this team.
These positions are available at various institutions. It was under consideration of the Government to grant in-service approval to these posts from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have approved the continuation of the services of 17 posts in the accompanying prospectus on the fixed temporary establishment of the Chief Engineer, (JE) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Superintending Engineer under the control of the Ministry, Mumbai, Executive Engineer under the Kukadi Project Board, Pune, Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Special Squad, Pune from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 subject to the following conditions.
a) The posts on fixed temporary establishment are not vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The posts in the fixed temporary establishment. As per the revised figure approved under the Government decision dated 06.02.2003, 45297 posts are in the limit.
