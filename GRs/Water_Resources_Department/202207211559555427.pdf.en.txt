Preamble: Out of the 87000 ha benefit area of Warana Project, 46078 ha is planned to be brought under irrigation through Up-Sea Irrigation Scheme on River Warana. For this, a total of 9 K.P. embankments have been constructed from Warana Dam to Warana Krishna river confluence on Warana river. This dam- Mauje Chaunwadi Ta. Walwa, which is a one-kilometre barrage in Sangli, was built in 1963 and has been collecting water since 1964. The total length of the bridge is 90 metres. The maximum height is 8.90 m. There are a total of 26. (78.40 DLH) and the project irrigation potential is 4606 hectares. The dam was constructed about 59 years ago.
The river usually floods at least twice a year. Also, from the Rabi season to the end of the summer season, the dams have to store water from the water to be released for the Mhaisal Upasana Irrigation Scheme for irrigation projects from time to time, the water to be released to the state of Karnataka and the water to be released to the city of Latur. Out of the total 26 gates of the dam, the left end of the river flow is from Pillar No. 1 to 7 slab has been completely washed away and Pillar No. 10 and 12 slab has been broken and washed away up to seal level. Pillar No. 9,11,13, 14 and 16 have been broken and washed away from the masonry to the bottom seal level of about 1.5 m. So, the right end pillar of the river flow is Approach Pillar No. 21 to 25 are completely submerged in silt. The other pillar of the dam, the pointing on the u/ s and d/ s side is completely damaged. Also, there is a lot of stone construction. In the flood of 2019, the stone pitching on the u/ s and d/ s side of the river course has been completely washed away. Agriculture has also been severely affected by the flood. Overall, the dam is in a dilapidated condition.
Therefore, the T.B. A proposal to give administrative approval to the repair works of Walwa and G. Sangli under a special amendment was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune under Co. P. 4,49,19, 119/- (Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. Four Crore One Hundred and Fifty Five Lakhs (Rupees Four Crores One Hundred and Nineteen only) subject to the following conditions in the accompanying proforma.
