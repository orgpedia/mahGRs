Reference No. 1 by the decision of the Government here, the main gate construction team No. 3. Sub-Divisional Offices at Pune, Satara, Sangli and Kolhapur districts under Pune Divisional Office have been merged under Divisional Office. 3, Pune has been made operational with its headquarters at Pune. Under the control of this office, door opening, maintenance, repair, etc. are being handled in Pune, Satara, Sangli, Kolhapur, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg districts as well as in large and medium dams in the adjoining state of Goa. Considering the scope of this zonal team, the headquarters must be centrally located in terms of project and administrative convenience in the district. Accordingly, the headquarters of the team office was under the consideration of the government to be shifted to a central place on the recommendation of the Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
