Government's decision:
In the office of the Superintending Engineer, Jalgaon Irrigation Project Board, Jalgaon, 28 posts on the fixed temporary establishment as shown in the accompanying return A, 106 posts on the fixed temporary establishment as shown in the accompanying return B of the Executive Engineer, Waghur Dam Division, Jalgaon and 4 Sub Divisional Offices subordinate to him and 142 posts on the fixed temporary establishment as shown in the accompanying return A of the Executive Engineer, Lower Tapi Project Division, Amarnath have been converted into 276 posts on such total fixed temporary establishment as shown in the accompanying return D of the Executive Engineer, Waghur Dam Division, Jalgaon and 4 Sub Divisional Offices subordinate to him.
Expenditure on the posts in the designated temporary establishment should be met from the grants sanctioned under the head Demand No. I- 5-4701 Capital expenditure on major and medium irrigation projects, (190) Investment in public sector and other activities, (04) Share capital contribution for expenditure on post-plan wages (04) (04) Share capital contribution to TAPI Irrigation Development Corporation (4701I 198).
