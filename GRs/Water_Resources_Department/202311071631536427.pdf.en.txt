The Provisional Seniority List as on 01.01.2023 is annexed herewith as" Annexure-A." The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board vide Government Circular No. SRV-2016/ Q. No. 281/12, Dt. has been prepared as per the instructions issued vide 13.07.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been used in the preparation of this seniority list.
All the Heads of Departments of Water Resources Department are hereby informed to bring the matter to the notice of all the officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Electrical& Mechanical) of the present seniority. The officers of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Electrical and Mechanical), if they have any objections/ suggestions regarding this seniority list, they should submit them to the Government along with the comments of the concerned Head of Department within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular.
