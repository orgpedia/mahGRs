The Proposal:
HSA Medium Project, Ta. The Shahubari, District Kolhapur project is built on a tributary of the Kadvi River, the Pettapugi. The project was completed in 2012. The total storage capacity of the project is 71.24 Dalhousie (2.51 Aghfu).
The project has been approved by Government Decision No. The third administrative approval was granted under SUPRAMA- 2009/624 / (207/09) MP, dated 3/3/2011. 11012.15 LOCATION (Schedule 2008-09).
The project was completed in the year 2004 and the dam is receiving Yoo percentage of water. According to the approved project report, the benefit area of Kadvi project includes a total of 52 villages in 51B Panhala Taluka of VMAS Taluka. The total irrigated area of the project is 9219 as per III Supra. That's so much.
The total cost of the project is 9219. Out of the beneficiary areas, 2685 are receiving water from other projects.
This is saving the 19.09 Dalhousie (0.675 SAH) water storage in the Kadvi project. Out of this saving, 4000 are outside the benefit area by lifting the water from the Savai village limits on the Kadvi river through a tube well in the Julewadi pass. It will be possible to water the irrigated area.
In this regard, Ma. The Minister for Water Resources (Godavari and Krishna Basin Development Corporation), Shri.
At the request of MLA Mr. Vinay Kore, the meeting dated 12/2/2025 has directed that "Re-planning of Kadvi Medium Project should be done and water should be made available to the proposed project and it should be approved from the competent level."
Accordingly, the proposal for approval for re-watering of Kadvi Medium Project was received by the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune vide Reference Letter No. 01 has been received by the Government.
Accordingly, it has been decided to approve re-watering of Kadvi Medium Project.
Government Decision Re-watering of Kadvi Medium Project is being approved as per Annexure-A subject to the following conditions.
