Introduction: - The Rajegaon Latur system dam is on the Terna River near Rajegaon village in Dharashiv district.
The barrage is located at a distance of 12.50 km from the site of the Terna Dam. The total projected water storage of the barrage is 0.586 Dalhousie and is 150. There is an irrigated area. The Rajegaon Latur system barrage was completed in 2015 and water was first stored by the construction department in 2015. Subsequently, in 2019, the project was transferred to the Management Department for maintenance and gate management.
It has come.
Since the completion of the construction of the project, no repairs have been carried out to the gates and the high rise system in the dam. Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
On 12/9/2023, he visited various dams / barrages on Manjra river and certified the need for repair of various mechanical and electrical components and gave some suggestions in this regard, including the proposed Rajegaon dam. The Executive Director has directed that these works be proposed under special amendment as the required funds for carrying out the proposed works in connection with the repair of machinery and electrical components of the Rajegaon Dam are not available at the corporation level. The Rajegaon Latur system dam will stop the leakage of water from the gate once the repair work of the mechanical and electrical components of the dam is completed. Also, the project proposed water storage will be available for irrigation during Rabi Summer season at full capacity.
The repair will also help in smooth operation of the gate and thus maintain the safety of the dam during flood control.
