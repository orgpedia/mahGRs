The Proposal:
Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation Limited, Jalgaon. Jamner G.
Jalgaon is a village situated on the banks of the river Nachan (Khoka). There are some houses, markets and religious places near the banks of the river. The Nachan (Khoka) River is a tributary of the Waghur River and is one of the major rivers in Jamner Taluka. Due to the discharge of flood water from the Nachan (Khoka) river, the side near Sangameshwar Mandi in Nachankheda has been eroded. This is likely to pose a threat to the life and property of the people living on the left bank of the river near the Sangameshwar temple. Therefore, as per the demand made by the Gram Panchayat Nachankheda, the government was considering to give administrative approval to the work of the flood protection wall proposal.
Government's decision:
1) As per the decision of this Government, Mauje Nachankheda Taluka under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon. For the construction of flood protection wall near Sangameshwar Temple on Nachan (Khoka) river in Jamner G. Jalgaon for protection of people and market from flood, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being granted for an amount of Rs. 88,06,522/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Lakhs Six Thousand Five Hundred and Twenty Two only) subject to the following conditions.
(a) Government Circular No.FDW-1092/ (170)/ S.V. (Works), d. to be dealt with in accordance with the provisions of 7.8.1992.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
