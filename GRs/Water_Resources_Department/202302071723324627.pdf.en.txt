The Proposal:
Mouze Tambwe, Ta. Walwa village is situated on the right bank of the river Krishna and its main part is situated on the bank of the river. Around 200 to 250 houses in Kotbhaga were threatened by the floods in 2019 and 2021.
The Hon'ble Minister (Water Resources& Benefit Area Development), State of Maharashtra had ordered to submit a proposal on the demand for construction of a new flood protection wall near the existing wall at the said place and the ghats as well as where necessary.
Accordingly, the construction of the support wall to the old flood protection wall on the river in Mauje Tambwe and the construction of a new wall and wharf where necessary has been conducted by conducting a survey and preparing a new budget for the work. This time, The proposal for construction of a flood protection wall along the Krishna river in Walwa, which is in Sangli, was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
