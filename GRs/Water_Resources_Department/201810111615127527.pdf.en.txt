Circular:
In order to provide the benefit of Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme to the heirs of the Provident Fund Subscriber who are in government service and have passed away, it should be done at every stage.
The time limit for proceeding under reference No. 1 has been prescribed. Accordingly, necessary instructions have been issued to all the Controlling Authorities under Reference No. 3. However, as it came to the notice of the Government that the benefit of this scheme is not being given to the heirs of the subscriber by some office heads or it is being given late, the Government has issued a notification in this regard vide No. According to Circular No. 2. It has been observed that despite repeated instructions to comply with the timelines given in the Government Circular No. 1, some of the controlling authorities are not passing on the benefits of the funds under the Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme to the heirs till the end of the financial year and then the fund is dedicated on 31st March.
Therefore, all the controlling officers and heads of offices are again being instructed to take action to pay the amount due under the Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme to the heirs of the deceased government employees without delay. Action should be taken against them under the Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005.
