Government's decision:
The offices of the Superintending Engineer, Water Resources e-Administration Board, Pune and 4 other Divisions under this Board have been sanctioned as per Annexure No. 1 Out of 89 posts mentioned herein, 88 posts on fixed temporary establishment excluding 1 post of Superintendent, which does not have the order of classification, have been de-listed.
The continuation from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019 is being approved by this Government Resolution subject to the following conditions:
The expenditure of the posts extended under this Government decision should be spent under the head" Accounts Head 3451-Secretariat Financial Services 090 Secretariat (01) Water Resources Department Establishment (01) (03) Implementation of e-Governance Project (3451-2719)" and it should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2018-19.
This Government Decision is based on the Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C. of the Department of Finance.
This is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments vide order dated 07/09/2018 and with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary.
