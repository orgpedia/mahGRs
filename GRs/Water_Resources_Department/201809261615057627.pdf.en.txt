The Proposal:
Pimpri Minor Irrigation Lake is located on a local stream near Fattepur village in Jamner taluka. The project falls in the Waghur sub-basin under the Tapi valley. And the useful storage is 1.8015 DLH. Some area near the submerged area of the project belongs to the forest department. The farmers were using the road through the forest area to irrigate their fields. The area has recently been protected by the forest department with a wire fence, so farmers living near the submerged area are not able to carry out farm operations and transport agricultural goods. At present, the road for the farmers to irrigate their fields passes through a submerged area. The full storage level of the dam is 2.67 m at the point of crossing the road in the said drain. There is so much water. Therefore, it is not possible to transport the goods to the farm. Pimpri Small Projects Pvt. No provision has been made for this in the report. The local people's representatives, the water utility and the farmers of the area had demanded this work from time to time.
