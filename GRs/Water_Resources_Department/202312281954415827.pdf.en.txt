Government's decision
Based on the result of the Combined Competitive Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai for recruitment to the post of Group-A Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture) in the Maharashtra (Architecture) Engineering Services Main Examination, 2020 vide letter No: 374 (2)/ Architecture/ 2019/19, dated 17.08.2022, a recommendation has been made for appointment to the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture). Among the recommended candidates, candidates belonging to the economically weaker section category were not given appointment due to pending cases. 2722/2023 attached Interim Application No. 14323/2023 and other connected petitions. In view of the Judgment dated 22.12.2023 and instructions issued by the General Administration Department vide letter dated 22/12/2023, the candidates belonging to Economically Weaker Section (EWS) category recommended in Annexure-A for the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A in the pay scale S-20 (Rs. 56100-177500) in the revised pay matrix as per the 7th Pay Commission in the category indicated against their names from the date they join the Water Resources Department.
