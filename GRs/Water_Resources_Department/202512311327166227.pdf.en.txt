Introduction: - Kole Satandi Medium Irrigation Project under the project Mojhe Kunkawan, Ta. A 25.474 multi-layered earthen structure has been constructed on the Satandi drain at Deogarh, Sindhudurg District. The project is included in Wagotan ST under the Western Channel River Basin under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation. The project is under the Konkan Region under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane. The project covers 1695 villages in 10 taluks of Deogarh in Sindhudurg district. A total area of 2569 hectares comprising 94 of 3 villages of Kankavli Taluk and 780 of 8 villages of Rajapur Taluk of Ratnagiri District will be benefited by irrigation.

> This third revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions.
1) The corporation will be responsible for completing the work within the limits of the revised administrative approval.
2) Follow the prevailing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial constraints, prescribed tender procedures while implementing the scheme. The Corporation shall be solely responsible for rectifying the errors and directions given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in connection with the work of the project, as and when required.
3) This revised WITH approval does not mean that technicalities in the report, field decisions on the tender, or irregularities will not be considered as approvals. If any irregularity is noticed, the responsibility lies with the concerned zonal authorities. It shall be the responsibility of the Corporation to investigate such a case in accordance with the rules of the Government in force at the time.
4) Additional liability shall not be incurred on the tender of the project unless the authority incorporated under the Additional Matters List, Section 38, and the tender is approved at the competent level if additional liability arises. The responsibility will be of the Executive Engineer and the Superintending Engineer concerned.
5) The Integrated State Water Plan should be improved according to improved water use.
6) Example of all the components of the scheme. Concepts must be approved from the competent level before the closed tube distribution system works can be taken up.
7) Changes in the design of any component of the scheme should not be undertaken unless it is approved by the competent authority.
8) The Corporation shall be responsible for not disburse funds or incur any additional expenditure beyond the limits of the administrative sanction granted for the project for any purpose.
9) "Rehabilitation" by "land acquisition" required for the project needs to be taken up on priority. Proper precautions need to be taken while acquiring the land so that court cases do not arise or occur in the future.
10) Necessary statutory and technical approvals for the project should be taken up at the competent level.
