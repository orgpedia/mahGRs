Government's decision
Based on the result of the Combined Competitive Examination held by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai for the post of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) Group-A (Gazetted) under the Maharashtra Engineering (Architecture) Service Main Examination-2013, out of 96 candidates recommended vide letter dated 07.10.2015, 94 candidates who have qualified for appointment as Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) Group-A (Gazetted) have been recommended for appointment to the above mentioned posts.
