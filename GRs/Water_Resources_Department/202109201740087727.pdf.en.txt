The Adla Medium Project is built on the Pravara River and the Pravara River Basin is a sub-basin of the Godavari River. This includes the repair of pumps, fountains, flow measuring machines, construction of gavrasta bridges on the distribution system, as well as works at the level of approach roads and water utilities.
