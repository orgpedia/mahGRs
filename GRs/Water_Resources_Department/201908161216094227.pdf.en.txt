Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Act, 1996. Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation was established under the provisions of Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996.
As per Section 4 (1) (a) of the Act, the Vice-Chairman of the Corporation is Shri Sanjay (Kaka) Ramchandra Patil, Mr. Chinchani, Pt. Tasgaon, G. Sangli have been appointed. According to Shri.
Sanjay (uncle) Ramchandra Patil, vice-chairman, Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune, has been given the rank of a cabinet minister.
The Vice-Chairperson was appointed by the Government Decision No. S.A. No. 10.10/ P.S. No. 96/10/ S.A. of the Department of Finance. The services provided under 13/03/2012 shall continue to be permitted.
