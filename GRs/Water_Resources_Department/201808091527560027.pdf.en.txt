The Proposal:
Vehle-Pimplas Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka Bhiwandi, District Thane, Government Decision to this scheme, Water Resources Department No. The original administrative approval was granted under the Act/ EEC/ 2001/ (549/2001)/ Kharbhoomi, Ministry of Mines, Mumbai-32, dated 25/09/2001 at a total cost of Rs. 282.01 lakhs. While preparing this administrative approval proposal, the rates were used as per the 1999-2000 tariff schedule. In the interim period, the cost of the scheme has been increasing due to tariff differences, ownership charges, increase in carrying distance, increase in volume, etc., necessitating grant of revised administrative approval to the scheme. The total length of the project is 5000 m. The project is located on the right bank of Ulhas river/ main creek, south of Vehle Pimpalas village. The proposal for the first revised administrative approval to the Vehle Pimpalas Kharbhoomi Yojana, received under Reference Letter No. 1 from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai, was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: The Vehle-Pimplas Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka Bhiwandi, District Thane, is being accorded the first revised administrative approval with an estimated cost of Rs. 776.13 lakhs (Rupees Seven Hundred and Seventy Six point Thirteen only) for the year 2016-17 based on the table. An amount of Rs. 718.82 lakhs (Rupees Seven Hundred Eighty Eight Eighty Two percent of the target only) has been provided for works and Rs. 57.30 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Seven Eighty Three percent of the target only) for ancillary works. The said revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions:

The revised administrative approval means that the approval cannot be taken for irregularities at various stages of the project implementation.
