The Proposal:
On the fun, th. Tilganga river flows through the village of Walwa, which is Sangli. On one side of the river is an old flood protection wall made of stone by the local board under the then Satara district. There are also roads, anganwadis, houses of villagers on the river side. Due to the floods in 2019 and the torrential rains in April and May this year, the existing wall has been damaged and collapsed, posing a threat to the houses, temples, anganwadis and roads of the villagers on the river.
The Hon'ble Minister (Water Resources& Benefit Area Development), State of Maharashtra during his field visit on 24/5/2021 had directed for construction of new wall and ghats and where necessary, new flood protection retaining wall near the existing wall.
Accordingly, the construction of the base wall of the old flood protection wall on the river in Mauje Peth and the construction of a new wall and ghat where necessary has been done by conducting a survey and preparing a new budget for this work. On this occasion, Shri. The proposal for construction of flood protection wall at Tilganga Odhi in Walwa, which is in Sangli, was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
