The local populists had informed the government that the flooding of the Sone River in Jamner, District Jalgaon, was causing extensive damage to the Sri Pundalik Temple, Hanuman Temple, Mammadevi Temple premises, Weekday Bazar, and the temple complex. Accordingly, the field office has conducted a physical inspection and submitted the budget of the work to the government for approval. The issue of administrative approval of the budget was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon under Md. shendurni, Ta. For the construction of a flood protection wall at Sri Pundalik Temple, Hanuman Temple, Mammadevi Temple on the right and left banks on the banks of Sone River adjacent to the village in Jamner, District Jalgaon, Rs. 3,79,88, 658.00 - (Rupees Three Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Six Hundred and Eighty-Nine), the Water Resources Department's Rate Based Budget of Year 2022-23 B Public Works Department's Year 2022-23 is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of the Decision No. P. 2019 / (Q. 195/2019) / C. (M) dated 31/08/2019.
b) Government Circular No. FDW - 1089/243/89 Civya (Works), D. 21/09/1989 and d. The line of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn according to the 03/05/2018. Tenders and other proceedings should not be taken unless a flood line has been drawn.
c) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, the externalities of the tariff list and the carrying distance at the competent level. Technical approval proceedings should be carried out only after the conception of the work has been approved from the competent level. If the concept results in a change in the cost or scope of the work, it will be mandatory to get it approved at the competent level.
d) The technical clearance authorities concerned shall be solely responsible for providing technical clearance in the budget excluding short work. The cost of the work is to be met within the stipulated time within the limits of administrative sanction from the available grants. No revised administrative approval will be granted for these works.
