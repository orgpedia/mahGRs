Order: The Junior Engineer (Branch Engineer) holding Diploma mentioned in Annexure A is being promoted to the post of Sub-Divisional Officer (Architecture) in Group A of Maharashtra Engineering Service on Grade Pay of Rs. 5400 in the Pay Band (PB-3) of Rs. 15600-39100 subject to the following conditions, with effect from the date he joins the place of detailed posting.
2. The promotions are being given without affecting the seniority of the officers who are senior to them and whose cases are open for consideration. The said promotion to the post of Sub Divisional Officer (Architecture) does not mean that the engineers mentioned in Annexure A are eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to them by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior most officers shall be demoted without any notice, last come first go.
The promotions are being made subject to the condition that the departmental inquiry under Rule 8 and 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, against the officers in Schedule A is not ongoing or proposed. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental inquiry is ongoing or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
Similarly, the promotions are being made subject to the assumption that the Backward Class officers in Schedule A have submitted the caste validity certificate to the Government. However, if any of these officers does not produce caste validity certificate, in his case, these promotion orders will automatically stand cancelled.

7. The authority to give detailed posting to the engineers mentioned in Annexure A who are promoted is being delegated to the concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer under the direct control of the Government. The concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer will consider the urgency of the work in the department and the posts in irrigation management. As per the directions of the Governor for the budget year 2018-2019, detailed posting orders should be issued in the districts covered under the revenue division as per the divisional cadre allocation in the districts (Amravati, Akola, Washim and Buldhana) under the schedule. The concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer shall ensure that the detailed posting is done within 2 days from the date of issue of this order in the revenue department allotted to the engineers posted under him. On receipt of detailed posting orders for the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer, the promoted engineers should be relieved immediately, if it is not possible to relieve them immediately, in any case by May 8, 2018, and a consolidated report in this regard should be submitted to the Government by the concerned Director General/ Executive Director and the Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer under the direct control of the Government without fail by May 17, 2018. In this regard, the instructions given by the Department vide letter No. 173/17/ 2017/ (Class-II) dated 12th July, 2017 should be strictly followed.
8. Engineers who are denied promotion on the ground that the promotions, though temporary, are regularised over a period of time, shall be informed in writing that the benefit of the Assured Progress Scheme under the service will be withdrawn. If a junior engineer (branch engineer) refuses to be promoted to the post of sub-divisional officer, he should immediately submit a report to the government. The salary of a Junior Engineer (Branch Engineer) who has not been promoted, shall not be withdrawn from the post of Junior Engineer (Branch Engineer) with effect from 01 May 2018. The Executive Engineer of the concerned department will be responsible for this.
The Executive Engineer of the concerned department will be responsible for relieving the promoted engineers immediately after receiving the detailed posting order of the Sub-Divisional Engineer. If there is a delay in this regard, the concerned executive engineer will be held responsible. If the promoted officer does not appear in this post within one month, the promotion given to him will be canceled on the assumption that he is not interested in the promotion. The Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer concerned should submit the information of the officers who will not appear in the promoted post within the prescribed time to the government immediately.
10. If the promoted engineers reject the promotion to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer, action will be taken against them as per the Government decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.
11. The engineers who have been relieved from the concerned department and come to join the post of posting on promotion should be recruited by the concerned without delay. In case of non-recruitment of an engineer leading to compulsory waiting period and payment of salary and allowances, the responsibility shall be fixed on the Executive Engineer/ Establishment Officer concerned.
12. The Chief Engineer shall issue detailed posting orders to all the Sub Divisional Engineers mentioned in Annexure A after ensuring that they have submitted the Statement of Assignments and Liabilities for the period ending 31.03.2017.
13. These promotions are provisional and subject to the final seniority list to be published in their parent cadre. Therefore, they will not be entitled for seniority in the cadre of Sub Divisional Engineer (Architecture).
