Now, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to extend the tenure of the Chief Auditor of Accounts, Water and Irrigation, Maharashtra State, Aurangabad for a total of 86 sanctioned posts on the fixed temporary establishment in that office from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: It is hereby approved to extend the time limit from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019 subject to the conditions that the 86 sanctioned posts in the temporary establishment fixed as per the format in the office of the Chief Auditor of Accounts, Water and Irrigation, Maharashtra State, Aurangabad are not vacant for a period of more than 6 months as indicated in the accompanying proforma and are within the limit of 45297 posts as per the revised format.
