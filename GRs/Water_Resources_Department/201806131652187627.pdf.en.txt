Introduction: Jamkhedi Medium Project is under construction on Jamkhedi river near Navenagar village in Sakri taluka of Dhule district under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation. The work on the left canal and earth dam has been completed.
The Jamkhedi Medium Project has an availability of 14.952 Dalghami water and the project water storage is 13.28 Dalghami. The construction of the dam was completed in 2002. Under this scheme, 2750 hectares of irrigated area of 16 villages of Sakri taluka will benefit from direct irrigation and at present 2350 hectares of area is getting the benefit of irrigation. The water level in the river has increased due to floods.
