The Proposal:
The Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Board, Satara has handed over the control of Sardar Sarovar Project to the Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule.
Accordingly, the Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule has been declared as the Controlling Officer under the Government Order dated 27.11.2018 for all the ancillary proceedings in respect of the head of accounts, namely, Accounts (4801 3742) 53 (Major Constructions), Sardar Sarovar Project, Accounts (4801 3769)-53 (Major Constructions) Share of Gujarat State, Sardar Sarovar Project, Accounts (4801 3956) (Operation and Administration) Sardar Sarovar Project and (4801 3858)-52 (Machinery and Equipment) Sardar Sarovar Project. Therefore, it was proposed to open a new sub-heading for this office and make budgetary provisions under it. Accordingly, a new sub-heading under the head 4801, Capital Expenditure on Power Projects, was under consideration of the Government for this office.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Accountant General's Office, Nagpur has issued a circular to the Office of the Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule, to provide a sub-heading under the head' Capital Expenditure on Power Projects' for depositing the amount of recovery to be collected under the Sardar Sarovar Project under converted/ regular/ temporary establishments. As per informal reference dated 18.08.2021, Demand No. The new subheading is approved under I-5, Principal Title 4801. Details of the new title are as follows:
