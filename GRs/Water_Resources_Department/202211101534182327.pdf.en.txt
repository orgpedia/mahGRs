(ii) Promotion to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction) does not mean that the concerned Engineer is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior-most officers shall be demoted on last come first go basis without giving any notice.
(iii) The said promotion is being made subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary action is going on or proposed to be taken against the engineer. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental inquiry is ongoing or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.

(ix) The Executive Engineer of the concerned department shall be responsible for relieving the promoted engineers immediately after receiving the detailed posting orders for the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction).
