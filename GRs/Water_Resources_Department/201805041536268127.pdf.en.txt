Preamble :
Government of India vide Notifiction from Ministry of Water Resources dated 12 March, 2018 has constituted The Mahanadi Water Disputes Tribunal at New Delhi for adjudication of water disputes regarding the interstate river Mahanadi. The issue regarding the appointment of Advocate on Record for Government of Maharashtra to appear before the Mahanadi Water Dispute Tribunal was under consideration of Government of Maharashtra.
2.0 Government Resolution :
Shri D. M. Nargolkar, Advocate, Supreme Court, New Delhi is hereby
appointed as Advocate - on Record for Government of Maharashtra to appear before the Mahanadi Water Disputes Tribunal. The fees and other terms and conditions of appointment are as follows.
