The Proposal:
Mauje Nachankheda under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon. Jamner G.
Jalgaon is a village situated on the banks of the river Nachan (Khoka). There are some houses, markets and religious places near the banks of the river. The Nachan (Khoka) River is a tributary of the Waghur River and is one of the major rivers in Jamner Taluka. Due to the discharge of flood water of Nachan (Khoka) river the side near Shri Datta Mandi of Nachankheda has been eroded. Therefore, there is a possibility of danger to the property and life of the people on the left bank of the river and surrounding areas near Shri Datta Mandir. Therefore, as per the demand made by the Gram Panchayat Nachankheda, the government was considering to give administrative approval to the work of the flood protection wall proposal.
Government's decision:
1) As per the decision of this Government, Mauje Nachankheda Taluka under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon. For the construction of flood protection wall near Shri Datta Mandir on Nachan (Khoka) river in Jalgaon to protect the people and the market from floods, Rs. The administrative approval is being granted for an amount of Rs. 78,41,056/- (Rupees Seventy Eight Lakhs Forty Seven Thousand Sixty Five only) subject to the following conditions.
(a) Government Circular No.FDW-1092/ (170)/ S.V. (Works), d. to be dealt with in accordance with the provisions of 7.8.1992.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
