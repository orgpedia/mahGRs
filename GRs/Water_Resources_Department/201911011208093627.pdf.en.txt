the prelude
The Government has decided to bring uniformity in the accounts and ancillary records of the Corporations and its offices under the Water Resources Department, to make the information available through it more useful and easy to use and to make it possible to update the accounts promptly. As on 22/07/2015.
In order to ensure effective control over the allocation of funds to the Corporations and their subordinate field offices at the government level and the expenditure incurred thereon, a committee of officers has been constituted to develop a system like BEAMS (Budget Estimation, Allocation and Monitoring System).
Accordingly, a new system called Integrated Finance Management System (IFMS) has been developed by the National Informatics Centre. This system will be first implemented for the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Corporation. Before implementing the system, the government was considering declaring the heads of departments under the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Corporation as disbursing officers. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation (MKKVDC) has a self-financing Account No. 8541 of Scheme No. 84430081 Treasury Office is located in Pune. The Executive Director has been authorised to draw funds from the said personal provident account. However, for the newly developed Integrated Finance Management System (IFMS) (Extended Beams), the amount is required to be disbursed by the Corporation Subordinate Executive Engineer from the Corporation's own balance sheet in the Pune Treasury.
