The Proposal:
Mauje Nachankheda under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon. Jamner G.
Jalgaon is a village situated on the banks of the river Nachan (Khoka). There are some houses, markets and religious places near the banks of the river. The Nachan (Khoka) River is a tributary of the Waghur River and is one of the major rivers in Jamner Taluka. Due to the discharge of flood water of Nachan (Khoka) river, the side near Shri Maruti Mandhi in Nachankheda has been eroded. This is likely to pose a threat to the life and property of the people living on the left bank of the river near Maruti Mandir. Therefore, as per the demand made by Gram Panchayat Nachankheda, the matter of providing administrative approval to the work of the flood protection wall proposal was under the consideration of the government.
