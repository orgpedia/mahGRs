Government circular
In accordance with the Potnium 5 (1) of the notification dated 21.06.2021 of the General Administration Department, Provisional seniority of officers appointed between 01.01.2022 and 31.12.2022 is to be published. However, no new person has been appointed directly to the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Position) during this period. Provisional seniority as on 01.01.2023 has not been published.
The final seniority list of the officers of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A as on 01.01.2023 has been prepared and is annexed to this circular at" Annexure-A." The said seniority has been created in accordance with the instructions of the General Administration Department, Government Circular No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/12, dated 13.07.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been used in the preparation of this seniority list.
