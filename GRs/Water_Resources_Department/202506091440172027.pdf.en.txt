> This fourth revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions.
1) The issue number 6,9,10, 11,13,14 and 16 of the checklist prepared in accordance with the Dam Safety Act "0-2021" should be fulfilled.
2) The proposed water use change in the proposed Supra should be approved at the competent level and the "State Integrated Water Plan" should be revised accordingly.
3) Detailed budget should be prepared for construction of residential B office building on SH canal bed as well as at dam site B canal bed.
4) The remaining construction of the B-coil canal on the canal bed of the project shall be taken up only after detailed conceptualization and approval of the canal tunnel at the competent level.
5) Concept of closed duct distribution system (PDN) should be vetted by Central Concept Picture Association, Nashik.
6) A "Water Utilization Agency" should be set up after the proposed work of the project is completed.
(7) Additional liability shall not be incurred and expended on the tender of the project unless the tender is approved at the competent level in the event of additional liability arising out of the tender to the authority incorporated under the Additional Matters Rating List, Section 38. The responsibility will be of the Executive Engineer and the Superintending Engineer concerned.
8) The crop structure in the proposed Supramas should be approved from the competent level.
9) Proceedings for land acquisition and rehabilitation should be carried out by the faculty to take the project to completion.
10) Work on the proposed canal lining in Supra should not be taken up without approval from the competent authority.
11) Since the project is coming up on PMSKYSIDID, the remaining work of the project should be done within the prescribed time as per the directions received.
12) The work of the project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the limits of the revised administrative sanction provision.
