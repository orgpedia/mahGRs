Preamble: Makunsar Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka and District Palghar is included in the Kharbhoomi Scheme. The Union Forest Ministry has exempted the scheme under the Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) Act 1991. The total length of the project is 3250 m. The project is located on a tributary of Danda creek near Maikop village. In the absence of a dam, farmers are suffering losses due to regular tides, floods and heavy rains in the beneficiary areas of this scheme and therefore it is necessary to have this scheme. As there was a demand from the beneficiaries, gram panchayats and people's representatives to take up the scheme immediately, the proposal to give administrative approval to the Makunasar Kharbhoomi scheme, which was received under the reference letter from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai, was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: Administrative approval is being accorded to the Makunsar Kharbhoomi Scheme, Taluka and District Palghar for an estimated amount of Rs. 216.03 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred Sixteen integer Three only) based on the rates of the Department for the year 2011-12 and the Public Works Department for the year 2015-16. An amount of Rs. 196.39 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Ninety-four only) is provided for works and Rs. 19.64 lakhs (Rupees Nineteen Eighty-four only) for ancillary works. The said administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions:
The expenditure on the scheme works should be spent under the head Demand No. I5, Main Account Head" 4402 Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation (102) Soil Conservation (01) Khar Jameen Yojana (01) Constructions (Under Schemes) (44020384)" and it should be shared from the sanctioned grant.

As per No. 2& 3, before starting the construction of the Kharbhoomi Scheme, the beneficiaries should form a cooperative society and to complete the Kharbhoomi Scheme, the beneficiaries of the reclaimed area should be charged Rs. 500/- per hectare as cost of participation in the project (minimum Rs. 200/- in cash and Rs. 300/- in the form of wages or cash). After the completion of the project, the plan will need to be transferred to them for maintenance and repair.
3. It should be ensured that the expenditure for the said work is not incurred without budgeting the provision in the prescribed manner.
The actual work should be started only after obtaining all environmental and other statutory approvals for this work.
5. The said administrative approval shall not be deemed to be an approval for irregularities committed at various stages of project implementation. If any irregularity is found in this work before or after the grant of this administrative approval, it cannot be assumed that it has been regularized by granting this administrative approval.
Continue the proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the scheme. The decision to grant administrative sanction shall not prejudice the proceedings of the departmental inquiry, whether ongoing or arising in future.
No additional expenditure or disbursement of funds beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for any purpose under this scheme. In this regard, the existing government decisions, financial limits, prescribed tender procedures should be strictly implemented.
8. Technical approval should be obtained for the cost involved in the proposed administrative approval of the project.
The entire project should be completed within the limit of the administrative approval cost.
This Government decision is in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Department of Finance, Government Decision No. VP-1013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulation Part-II, dated 17.04.2015, Inter-Financial Adviser and Deputy Secretary of the Department of Planning (KAYARSAN-1434), in an informal reference No. 187/17, dated 03/05/2018 is being issued with the consent given.
The administrative approval granted to this scheme has been registered at serial number 184.
