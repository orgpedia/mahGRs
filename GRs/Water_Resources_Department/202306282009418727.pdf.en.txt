(3) The posts arising out of the aforesaid transfer, if the additional charge has been earlier assigned to other officers, shall cease to exist with effect from the date of this order.
4. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Further, as per Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave application of the transferred officer. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned officer at the original address. If the leave application of the transferred officer is sent to the parent office, then these applications will not be taken into consideration.
5. It may be noted that in case of non-appearance at the place of immediate appointment as per the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
