Circular:
As per the instructions issued under the Government Decision dated 01.01.2021 regarding the temporary seniority of the cadre of Senior Geo-Scientist and Assistant Geo-Scientist of the Water Resources Department, the same is being published under Annexure-A and Annexure-B attached.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted in the preparation of the said provisional seniority.
The Superintending Engineer, Central Conceptual Association, Nashik and the Superintending Engineer, Koyna Conceptual Board, Pune shall bring to the notice of all senior geoscientists and assistant geoscientists (including officers on leave/ deputation) of such seniority. Further, if any genuine errors/ omissions are found in the said Seniority Notes, the said provisional seniority of Senior Geo-Scientist and Assistant Geo-Scientist Cadres shall be finalised on the understanding that there are no objections if the same are not brought to the notice of the Government in a written manner within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular.
