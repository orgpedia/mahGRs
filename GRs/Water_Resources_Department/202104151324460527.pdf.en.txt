The Proposal:
The village is situated on the right bank of the Koyna River. The flood situation is caused by the rainfall in the upper free catchment area of the village and the water released from the Koyna dam.
Flooding is threatening homes, public buildings, and roads. Also, a large amount of land is being washed away and the river bank near the village is unstable.
