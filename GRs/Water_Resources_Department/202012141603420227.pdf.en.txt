Reference:- 1) Letter from the Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur. 18/09/2019 2) Government Decision No. SANKIRN 2013/ (415/13)/ SINVYA (POLICY) D. 16/8/2014 3) Government Decision No. SANKIRNA 2015/ (44/15)/ SINVYA (POLICY) D. 17/11/2016 4) Government Resolution No. Devudu 2015/ (836/15)/ Simvya (Works) Dt. 17/11/2016 5) Government Resolution No. SAMKIND 2018/ (511/18)/ SAMVYA (POLICY) Dy. 1/12/2018 Preamble: 1.0 Reference Letter No. Under Section 1, a proposal has been received from the Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur for sanction of Bulk Water Entitlement of 0.445 Dalghami from Kathani River to Nagar Panchayat Dhanora G. Gadchiroli. The government has taken a decision. As per 17/11/2016, instructions have been given to determine the sectoral allocation for different categories of irrigation projects and to approve the non-irrigated water reservation proposal. However, the power to approve the reservation of water from the river for non-irrigation purposes is not mentioned in the above Government decision. However, the notified river should be understood as a notified project. It is advisable to obtain approval in accordance with the classification of the institution and water use at the competent level, as stipulated in the Government Decision of 17/11/2016. In accordance with paragraph 2.2 of this Decision, With the approval of the Minister (s), the Government was considering to approve the water reservation of 0.445 Dalghams considering the population of 2050 for this scheme.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. BISINNA 2019/ (473/19)/ CE (DHO-2)
2.0 The above-mentioned proposal is being approved subject to the following conditions:
(1) The Government Decision No. Composite 2013/ (415/2013)/ SINVYA (POLICY), Dy. As per 16/8/2014, Zilla Parishads/ Gram Panchayats/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats/ Cuttack Mandals which are not economically viable should not be charged irrigation restoration cost for reservation of drinking water. Government Resolution No. Narrow 2015/ (P.No.274/ NV-33), Dy. As per 17/12/2016, the cost of irrigation restoration should be waived while approving the proposals of non-irrigation reservation of C and D category corporations in the state for domestic drinking water purposes.
2) Revised Model and Guidelines of Contract for Non-Irrigated Water Supply issued by Government of Maharashtra, Irrigation Department vide Government Circular No. Bipapu 1001/ (713/2001)/ Simvya (Dhow), Mumbai-32, Dist. Issued on 7/4/2003. An agreement should be reached.
3) Government Circular No. Papav-20156/ (P.S. 400/2015)/ Civil (Revenue) No. Action should be taken as per the guidelines issued under 15/12/2015 regarding signing of non-irrigation water usage contracts and timely renewal.
4) Guidelines on the imposition of penal water tariff on water users polluting water sources. Papav 2015/ (P.S. No. 88/2015)/ Simvya (Revenue) Dy. Water users will be obliged to comply with the conditions in 21/4/2016.
5) Government of Maharashtra, Water Resources Department, Government Resolution No. Narrow 2010/ (407/10)/ SINVYA (POLICY), Dy. Under the agreement dated 29/6/2011, the non-irrigated water users can plan the use of water in a phased manner as per the final water demand. Similarly, the Maharashtra Water Resources Regulatory Authority Order No. Action should be taken as per the provisions of 1/2018.
(6) Government Decision No. Devudu 2015/ (836/2015)/ Simvya (Works), Dy. As per the provisions of the Irrigation Development Corporation Act under 17/11/2016, a decision has been issued to transfer the management of irrigation projects to the Irrigation Corporation and to approve the expenditure of water table for irrigation management of the projects. Action should be taken accordingly.
7) A detailed memorandum should be issued immediately by the concerned Chief Engineer subject to the location of water lifting/ supply and other conditions as per the regional situation.
8)" This reservation is only the letter of support for the, water supply scheme or the industry as the case may be. The actual quantity of water that will be supplied every year shall be governed by the entitlement granted to the water user entity by the River Basin Agency based on the population and the reasonable use norm decided by this Authority and the annual allocation declared by the prescribed Authority on the basis of the reservoir contents of the year and shall be subject to various provisions in the agreement that will be signed by the water user entity with the River Basin Agency.
