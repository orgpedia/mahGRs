Introduction: The catchment area of the Vashishti River is also steep due to the extreme Western Ghats east of Chiplun city and the origin of the West Vahini Vashishti River in the Western Ghats.
From 22/7/2021 to 23/7/2021, Koyna 610 mm, Navja-946 mm and Koyna-610 mm respectively were constructed in the catchment area and ghats of river Vashishthi. Mahabaleshwar 556 m. There has been 200 mm of rainfall in the district. Rainfall has been recorded. The catchment area of the Vashishti river is extremely low-lying and the water in the catchment area is rapidly receding towards Chiplun city.
The discharge of water from Koyna dam is increasing in the catchment area. As a result, the flow of water from the catchment area was obstructed due to the formation of silt islands in the river basin, which led to the flood situation in Chiplun city. The dredging of these silt/ silt islands is planned to be done in a phased manner and the Phase-I work has been accorded administrative approval in 2021-22 and is in the final stages. In this regard, a proposal has been received from the field level to take up the work of the next phase-2. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government Decision: Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane under Vashishti River, Chiplun District Ratnagiri to remove silt and islands Rs. 48600186/- (Rupees Four Crores Eighty Six Lakhs One Hundred Eighty Six), the Technical Approval of the concerned Mechanical Department is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
c) Necessary action should be taken to remove the silt as per the prevailing policy of the government.
(b) All necessary permissions for the work shall be obtained from the competent authority.
c) Necessary clearances should be obtained in respect of Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ).
d) The silt removed from the Vashishthi river should be dumped in a safe place so that no citizen is harmed and it should be ensured that the silt is not again washed into the river.
The land should be provided by the municipality/ collector for dumping the silt at a suitable place and the land should be determined by the revenue department.
f) Necessary consent/ permissions of the concerned should be obtained in case of private islands in the river.
(g) The certificate should be provided to the Government immediately after the completion of the work.
