Government order:
Shri Narayan Namdeo Balpande (Shri. No.12A), Superintendent is being promoted to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) (Pay Scale 41800-132300 Pay Band (S-15) for the vacant post of Chief Engineer (JE), Water Resources Department, Nagpur on temporary basis subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(i) Mr. Balpande is being promoted without prejudice to the seniority of the officers who are senior in service and whose cases have been kept open for consideration.
(ii) Promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) does not mean that the Superintendent concerned is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in the future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior most Superintendent shall be demoted on Last Come First Go principle without any notice.
(iii) The said promotion is being given subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary proceedings are going on or proposed to be initiated against the concerned. However, in case of any of these superintendents against whom a departmental enquiry is in progress or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
(iv) Mr. Balpande has been promoted to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B). A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 as per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution dated 07.05.2021. This government has taken that decision. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court.
The promotion is subject to the final decision.

(v) The Chief Engineer concerned shall relieve Mr. Balpande forthwith to enable him to join the promoted post. The Chief Engineer (JE), Water Resources Department, Nagpur, should immediately appoint him to the post of promotion.
vi) If Mr. Balpande does not appear in the promoted post, the promotion given to him will be cancelled on the assumption that he is not interested in the promotion and action will be taken as per the Government decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.
(vii) Superintendents who refuse promotion on the ground that the promotion is temporary in nature but gets regularised over a period of time, shall be informed in writing that the benefit of time-bound promotion/ Assured Progress Scheme under the service, if granted, shall be withdrawn. If a Superintendent refuses promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B), the report should be submitted to the government immediately.
