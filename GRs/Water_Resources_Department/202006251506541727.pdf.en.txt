The total water storage available in the project is 49.85 LMT which is 47.47 LMT. 2528 under Alibaug district of Maharashtra State. The area will benefit from irrigation.
The proposal was placed for consideration in the meeting of the Expenditure Priority Committee dated 12/03/2020. The proposal was approved by the expenditure committee. It was proposed for consideration of the Cabinet. As on 06/05/2020.
The Cabinet decided to give administrative approval to the First Revised Project Report of Sambarkund Medium Project based on the 2018-19 schedule at a cost of Rs. 742.88 crore after taking into consideration the current status of construction, reasons for cost escalation, etc.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this decision of the Government, administrative approval is being accorded to the First Revised Project Report of Sambarkund Medium Project for the year 2018-19 at a cost of Rs. 742.88 crore (Rupees Seven Hundred Forty Two Crore Eighty Eight Lakh only) based on the schedule. Out of this, Rs. 692.78 crore (Rupees Six hundred and ninety two crore seventy eight lakhs only) is provided for works and Rs. 50.10 crore (Rupees Fifty crore ten lakhs only) for establishment and ancillary expenditure. Sub-heading wise provisions are being added to Goshwara.
The first revised administrative approval granted to the scheme has been recorded in the Medium Project Operations Register with serial number 01/ 2020.
The project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the revised administrative sanction.
The cost of the project should be included in the head of the main account, i-5,4701 Capital expenditure on major and medium irrigation projects, under 190-Public Sector and Other Undertakings Investment (02) Scheme, (02) (01) Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane Share Capital Contribution (4701-H729) and should be met from the grant sanctioned under it.
The First Amended Administrative Sanction is granted subject to the following conditions:
5.1) The observations in point number 1 to 27 in the report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee should be complied with.
5.2) Work should not be started until all statutory approvals have been obtained.
