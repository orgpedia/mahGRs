Regarding constitution of a Committee to submit a report on acquisition of Chain Cement Bonds/ Co. P. Bonds in place of Main Dam at the proposed Dam site.
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: COMMITTEE-2023/ (191/2023)/ LPA
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date:- 20/04/2023
Preamble: As per the demand of the people's representative, the Irrigation Development Corporation of the state gets the approval of the government to take over the above and below the dam in a chain system without cancelling the original quantity instead of the existing soil dam. At present, it is not possible to approve the proposals of these schemes as there is no policy to approve such schemes. Therefore, it is necessary to decide the policy on how to take up schemes with such changes and for this, the government was considering to form a committee. The government has decided to constitute a committee for the purpose.
Government Decision The basic objectives of the Department are to take up the above and below the dam in a chain manner without cancelling the original scale of the projects instead of the existing earthen dam or to prepare a report on how and the necessary policy in this regard and submit it to the Government.
