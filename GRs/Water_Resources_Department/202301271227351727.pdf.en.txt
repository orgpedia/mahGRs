The Proposal:
The General Administration Department has issued instructions to take necessary action in connection with the filling of vacancies in the government service. 2 decisions have been made by the government.
Accordingly, the Department vide Reference No. 3 Circle-wise Coordination Officers have been appointed as per the decision of the Government here. General Administration Department Reference No. The competitive examination process for direct recruitment to the nominated quota posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres (outside the purview of the Commission) will be conducted by TCS-ION (Tata Consultancy Services Limited) and ION (Tata Consultancy Services Limited). The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (BPS) has issued a notification regarding this. Further, as per the provisions of the said Government Order, the concerned Administrative Department/ Office shall enter into an MoU with the Company for conducting competitive examination/ online recruitment process for each post. As proposed by the Regional Selection Committee and the Superintending Engineer and Director, Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development and the Circle Officer, Pune Circle, Pune, the government is considering to conduct the recruitment process by a single regional selection committee to fill the vacant posts of nomination in Group-C cadre in all the offices under the Water Resources Department in the state without conducting a separate recruitment process at the regional level.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the formation of a Coordination Committee under the Chairmanship of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, to fill up the nominated vacancies in Group-C cadre in all the offices under the Water Resources Department in the State without different recruitment processes at the regional level in the State as follows:
Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region- Chairman 2. Superintending Engineer& Director, Irrigation Research& Development- Member Directorate, Pune

The functions of the above said Coordination Committee are being determined as follows: (1) The Coordination Committee shall enter into an agreement with the agency appointed by the Government for the recruitment process to fill the vacancies of Group-C nominees in all the offices of the State under the Water Resources Department and shall coordinate with the said agency.
(2) The candidates shall be given a choice as per the category and post. After the completion of the examination process by this Coordination Selection Committee, the selected examinees should be allocated under the circle according to the options given by them according to the merit. Thereafter, the orders of appointment shall be issued by the Board/ Board.
(3) Where court cases arise after the recruitment process has been carried out by this Coordination Committee, such court cases shall be dealt with by the Regional Selection Committee concerned.
