The Proposal:
The Bhiwapur mini-project is located in the Tivasa taluka of Amravati district, 25 km from Amravati on the Amravati-Kurha road. The total area of the project is 645 hectares. That's so much. The channel type of the project is heavily inundated downstream due to heavy rainfall and flood water flowing through the sewerage.
The building is also dilapidated. At present the puddle has reached the waste weir bar. The Dam Safety Organisation, Nashik, in its Dam Health Status Report-2013, had recommended immediate remediation of the silt in the wake of dam safety. Thereafter, the Superintending Engineer, Dam Safety Organization, Nashik, gave a presentation. Instructions have been given to inspect the dam on 26/06/2020 and repair the sewage immediately.
This work was done under the Water Resources Department Government Resolution No. 2013/ (785/13)/ Simvya (Works), No. As per 1/8/2019, as per priority-2 of the work type-1, the repair of the waste of Bhiwapur Minor Project is proposed to be carried out. The proposal to grant administrative approval under the special amendment budget of Tivasa, G. Amaravati was under consideration of the government.
