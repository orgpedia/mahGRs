The Proposal:
There are many reservoirs under the jurisdiction of the Water Resources Department in different parts of Maharashtra and this reservoir has the potential to provide high-quality and safe water travel. Boating can create jobs for local people and boost the local economy. Accordingly, there is a need for coordination between the Water Resources and Tourism departments to develop water tourism in the reservoirs of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, the government is considering to form an executive committee of officers of the Water Resources and Tourism Department to coordinate with the Water Resources and Tourism Department in order to develop water tourism in all the reservoirs under the Water Resources Department of Maharashtra and to study the water tourism project in the reservoirs and submit recommendations to the government from time to time.
Government's decision:
In order to develop water tourism in all the reservoirs under the jurisdiction of the Water Resources Department of Maharashtra, an executive committee of the officers of the Water Resources and Tourism Department is being formed to coordinate with the Water Resources and Tourism Department and to study the water tourism projects in the reservoirs and submit recommendations to the government from time to time:
