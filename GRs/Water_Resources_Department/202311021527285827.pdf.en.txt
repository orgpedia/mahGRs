The Proposal:
The project is planned to be implemented in two phases.
Jaikwadi Project Phase-I Major Irrigation Projects include 1. Jaikwadi Dam, 2.J. Paithan Dawa Canal of 208 km length of Kaikwadi Project, its branch canals, distributaries, sub and minor distributaries.
Under Jaikwadi Project Phase-I, works of Paithan Dam, Paithan Left Canal Km 0 to 208 and above, Vitrika Sub and Minor Vitrika have been completed. The Paithan Dam and Paithan Dawa Canal were originally constructed in 1976.
The total length of the main canal on the Paithan Left Canal is 208 km and the length of the 5-branch canal is 89 km. The total area under irrigation is 1,41,640 hectares (Aurangabad 7,620 hectares, Jalna 36,850 hectares, Parbhani 97,170 hectares).
Jaikwadi Project Phase-II consists of 132 km long Paithan Right Canal of Jaikwadi Project, Majalgaon Dam on Sindphana River, a tributary of Godavari River and 148 km long Jalpaiguri Dam. The Majalgaon Right Canal, which is 3 km long, comprises three major components and is located in the general area of the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation.
The project will connect Aurangabad (1,732 ha) with Paithan Right Canal. Ahmednagar (1,690). and Beed (38,260).
