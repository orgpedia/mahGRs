The Proposal:
In order to increase the participation of beneficiaries in the management of irrigation projects, the Canal Advisory Committees have been reconstituted as per the decision of the Government. The involvement of water use agencies in irrigation management is essential for efficient and optimum utilization of water. For this purpose, it is necessary to form canal advisory committees and hold their regular meetings.
As per the decision of the Government, for large projects category-A (where the benefit area of the project is more than 1 lakh hectare), Rs. Minister for Water Resources (Mo. The Minister of State (Water Resources) is the ex-officio Chairman of the Canal Advisory Committee, while for Category-B (if the project has a benefit area of less than 1 lakh hectares), the Chairman is the Member Secretary. Minister for Water Resources (Mo. Minister of State (Water Resources) The Chief Minister is the chairman of the Advisory Committee on the canal. Since it was not possible to meet the Canal Advisory Committee and approve the project in time, due to the large number of large projects in the state, the government was considering giving the chairmanship of the Canal Advisory Committee to the guardian ministers of the respective districts to conduct the annual water planning of the project, review the PIP compliances of the previous season and discuss and make recommendations on the water planning of the current season.
Government's decision:
In view of the above, the Government have decided to constitute a Canal Advisory Committee for Type-A (if the benefit area of the project is more than 1 lakh hectare) and Type-B (if the benefit area of the project is less than 1 lakh hectare) of major projects in the Annexure with the decision of the Government in this regard.
In addition, other provisions of the relevant government decision are being retained. However, according to this provision, the proceedings of holding the meetings of the Canal Advisory Committee should be done by all the ex-officio member secretaries, Canal Advisory Committee.
