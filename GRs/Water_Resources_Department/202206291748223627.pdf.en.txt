Introduction: Kelloshi Minor Irrigation Lake, Ta. In Radhanagari, Kolhapur district, an earthen dam is being constructed on the Londha Nala tributary of the Tulsi river. The project is being implemented by the Krishna Valley Development Corporation (KRDC). The total storage capacity of the dam is 5.603 LMT. A total of 19 borewells are proposed on the downstream side of the project. There are a total of 513 villages in 3 villages of Radhanagari taluka. The area will benefit from irrigation.
