Letter from the Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, vide No. 2020/ Cat 4/ Garga Suprama/ 2020 Dt. 18/03/2020 Preamble: Garga Medium Project, Taluka-Dharani District- It is proposed to construct a dam of 29.73 LM capacity on river Garga at Amravati. This project is Amravati falls under the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation. 4281 of Dharani Taluka of Amravati District. The area is planned to be brought under irrigation.
Garga Medium Project has been approved by the Corporation vide Resolution Vipavim/ KATAAN- 4/5172/ Garga MP/ PRAAM/ (64/2008) 2008 D.O. As on 14.11.2008, Rs. Administrative sanction was accorded for an amount of Rs. 140.24 crore.
In the meanwhile, due to increase in the cost of the project due to increase in tariff, increase in land acquisition cost, change in concept, etc., the Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, has submitted the first revised administrative approval proposal of 2016-17 based on tariff at a cost of Rs. 494.24 crore to the Government of Maharashtra under reference number 2. The project cost ratio is 0.64 and the project does not meet the financial parameters.
The proposed First Revised Administrative Sanction for the project is Rs. 494.24 crore higher by Rs. 354.00 crore than the First Revised Administrative Sanction of Rs. 140.24 crore. The main reasons for the price increase are:

The total water storage capacity of the project is 29.73 LMT and total live water consumption is 33.81 LMT. 4281 of Dharani Taluka of Amravati District. The area will benefit from irrigation.
The proposal was placed for consideration in the meeting of the Governing Body dated 28/01/2020. The Board of Directors proposed the proposal. It was proposed for consideration of the Cabinet. As on 24/03/2021. The Cabinet has decided to accord administrative approval to the First Revised Project Report of Garga Medium Project based on the 2016-17 schedule at a cost of Rs. 494.24 crore after considering the current status of construction, reasons for cost escalation, etc.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this decision of the Government, administrative approval is being accorded to the First Revised Project Report of Garga Medium Project based on 2016-17 schedule at a cost of Rs. 494.24 crore (Rs. Out of this, Rs. 457.45 crore (Rs. Four hundred and fifty seven crore and forty five lakh only) is provided for works and Rs. 36.79 crore (Rs. Thirty six crore and ninety nine lakh only) for establishment and ancillary expenditure. Sub-heading wise provisions are being added to Goshwara.
The first revised administrative approval granted to the scheme has been recorded in the Medium Project Operations Register at serial number 02/ 2021.
The project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the revised administrative sanction.
The cost of the project shall be included in the head of the main account, i.e., capital expenditure on I-5, 4701 major and medium irrigation projects, under the head 190-Public Sector and Other Undertakings Investment (02) Schemes, (02) (01) Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur Share Capital Contribution (4701-H729) and shall be met from the grants sanctioned thereunder.
The First Amended Administrative Sanction is granted subject to the following conditions:
(i) The State Level Technical Advisory Committee in its Technical Scrutiny Report has recommended that the issue No. The issues mentioned in 1 to 27 should be addressed.
(ii) Action should be taken in respect of irregularities in the tendering process of the said project as pointed out by the Secretary level Sub-Committee.
(ii) The first administrative approval shall not be deemed to be the approval of irregularities at various stages of project implementation.
(iv) Proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the project will continue. The decision to grant revised administrative sanction first will not hamper the proceedings of the departmental inquiry.
