Preamble: A total of 55 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and Small Irrigation Cell, Thane under the Corporation have been sanctioned vide reference No. 1 The extension was granted vide Government Resolution dated 30.08.2022 from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023. Accordingly, a total of 55 posts, including 41 posts in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and 14 posts in the office of the Corporation's Internal Minor Irrigation Cell, Thane, have been approved for extension beyond 28.02.2023. The proposal has been submitted in 2 letters. Finance Department Reference No. 3 In pursuance of the Government decision dated 08.02.2023, the following decisions are being taken:
Government Decision A total of 55 posts, including 41 posts in the office of Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and 14 posts in the office of the Small Irrigation Cell, Thane, under the Corporation, are being sanctioned for extension from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure A.
a) The fixed temporary posts should not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
