The third revised administrative approval granted to the project is recorded in the registry of the Middle Project Executive at serial number 03/2025.
The fourth revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions.
1) The provision in the State Integrated Water Plan should be revised according to the total water use of the project.
(2) The Government shall take decisions with respect to the distribution of water through taps. The officer competent to grant technical approval in the 13/01/2017 should approve the tube distribution plan and the concept and verify the financial viability of the proposal and seek the approval of the Executive Director. Without this, the work should not be undertaken.
3) The Central Water Commission's approval should be taken before the rest of the project is commissioned.
4) The project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the limits of the revised administrative sanction provision.
5) All issues in the Scrutiny Report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee should be addressed / complied with.
(6) The Corporation shall be responsible for not discharging funds or incurring any additional expenditure beyond the limits of the administrative sanction granted for the project for any purpose.
7) Necessary statutory and technical approvals for the project should be taken up at the competent level.
8) Revised WS approval means that no technicalities in the report or field-level decision on the tender or field irregularities will be considered. Such cases are required to be investigated in accordance with the prevailing Government decision at the time and the Corporation shall be solely responsible for the same.
9) The Corporation shall be fully responsible for the strict implementation of the prevailing Government decisions, rules, CVC guidelines, financial authority limits, prescribed tendering procedures while implementing the project, compliance with the directions and errors given by the State Level Technical Advisory Committee in line with the project work as required.
