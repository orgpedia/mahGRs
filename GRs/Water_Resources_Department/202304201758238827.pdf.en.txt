Introduction: The Palasgaon Amdi Upsa Irrigation Scheme, which is under construction, was given by the Corporation. The second revised administrative approval has been granted under 7.5.2021. For Amdi Barrage, 66.569 Dalghami water availability certificate has been obtained and for Palasgaon Amdi Upsa Irrigation Scheme, 21.58 Dalghami water availability has been mentioned in the revised administrative approval.
The corporation has proposed to store water in the proposed Amdi Barrage and use it for the Palasgaon Amdi Upasana Irrigation Scheme. The water from the barrage will be used for irrigation, industrial purposes and for water supply in Chandrapur, Rajura and Ballarpur towns.
Also, the concerned Chief Engineer has certified that the Amdi Barrage and Palasgaon Amdi Uplift Irrigation Scheme are viable if implemented together. Accordingly, the matter of providing administrative approval to the work budget for the remaining survey, investigation and preparation of detailed project report of the present scheme was under the consideration of the government.
