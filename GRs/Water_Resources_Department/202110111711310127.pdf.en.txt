Introduction: The Ovale Kharbhoomi Scheme. Thane G. Thane is located on the left bank of Ulhas creek between Mauje Mogar Pada and Bhayander Pada villages. Ovale Kharbhoomi Vikas Yojana is a scheme for development of Kharbhoomi in Thane district. Included in 45. The scheme is also included in the total 575 (108 Kharbhoomi Scheme in Thane District) Kharbhoomi Schemes in Konkan which are exempted from the Marine Regulation Act, 1991 vide letter No. J 17011/32/2001/ I-3/ dated 22/8/2003 of the Union Ministry of Environment and Forests, New Delhi.
The project is Board time and was constructed by Kharland Board in 1957. The project was completed in 1990. Due to the erosion of the old dam and the dilapidated structure of the masonry, the saline water of the creek is entering the fields and damaging the paddy crop. The villagers have demanded that the project should be revived. The per hectare cost of the project is Rs. 1,22,214/- against the prevailing monetary norm of Rs. 1,92,950/- respectively.
Owale Kharbhoomi Scheme received under reference from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai. The proposal to give administrative approval to the renovation work of Kharbhoomi scheme in Thane, G. Thane was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: Ovale Kharbhoomi Scheme, Ta. The administrative approval for the renovation of Thane, G.Thane is being accorded based on the tariff list of Kharbhoomi Department for the year 2018-19 at an estimated cost of Rs. 2,33,42, 931/- (Rupees Two Crore Thirty Three Lakhs Forty Two Thousand Nine Hundred and Thirty One only). 1,80,30, 450/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Lakh Thirty Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty only) for works and Rs. 18,03,045/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Three Thousand Forty Five only) for ancillary works, Rs. 21,63,654/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Sixty Three Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty Four only) for GST, Rs. 13,45,782/- (Rupees Thirteen Lakh Forty Five Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty Two only) for minor minerals. The said administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions:
