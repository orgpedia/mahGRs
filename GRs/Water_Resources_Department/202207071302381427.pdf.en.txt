The Proposal:
Government Decision No. (Dho), dated 31/08/2019, the right of non-irrigated bulk water for drinking water for Vasai-Virar City Municipal Corporation is 3.418 DLH.
The proposal has been approved. Accordingly, the project will be implemented for drinking water for Vasai Virar City Municipal Corporation. The government has taken a decision. A large number of water rights proposals have been approved to the Municipal Corporation under 31/08/2019. Accordingly, Rs. 57.39 crore (excluding royalty amount of Rs. 51.39 crore) revised budget was approved by Vasai Virar Municipal Corporation vide Administrative Resolution No. 51 dated 14/08/2020 has been accorded administrative approval.
Accordingly, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed on 08/03/2022 between the Executive Director, Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and the Commissioner, Vasai-Virar City Municipal Corporation, Virar, after considering the revised administrative sanction provisions, terms and conditions of the tender agreement and the amounts payable to the contractor under various clauses, the increase in the price, the forest land changing from time to time, the rules of land acquisition and rehabilitation and the amounts payable thereunder. As per Article 24 of the MoU, The government has constituted a committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
In the future, if the dispute arising between the Executive Director, Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane and the Commissioner, Vasai-Virar City Municipal Corporation, Virar is not resolved while carrying out the work of the Kholsapara-2 L.P. Scheme, then as mentioned in the Clause-24 of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) dated 08/03/2022, the Government of Maharashtra will not be able to resolve the dispute. The Government have approved the constitution of the following Committee under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
