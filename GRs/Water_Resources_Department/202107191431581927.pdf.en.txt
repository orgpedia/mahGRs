The Maharashtra State Water Policy 2019 has been revised on the lines of the National Water Policy 2012 with the objective of addressing the state-specific issues and challenges as decided by the above Governments. However, some amendments/ changes are required in the State Water Policy 2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Some amendments/ changes are being made in the State Water Policy 2019 as mentioned in the Annexure attached. These amendments/ changes have been made with effect from the implementation of Maharashtra Water Policy 2019 i.e. It will come into effect from September 5, 2019. These are the changes. The Minister, Water Resources Department is being issued with the approval of the State of Maharashtra.
