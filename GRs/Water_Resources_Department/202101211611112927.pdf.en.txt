The government has decided to celebrate the birth centenary of former Chief Minister Shankarrao Chavan through various programmes. Accordingly, the Department of Water Resources, under the Government Decision dated 12.06.2020, has decided to award the award to the persons who have done excellent work in the field of water resources, water conservation and water supply. On the occasion of the birth centenary of Shankarraoji Chavan, it has been decided to present the Jal Bhushan Award on the 14th of July through the Water Resources Department with the help of the Water Conservation and Water Supply Department.
Sadar Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 12/06/2020, the Director, Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune, in collaboration with the Director and Superintending Engineer, Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development (DIRD), Pune, has submitted to the Government, the nominations for selection of Jal Bhushan Awardees by awarding marks to the applications/ nominations received as per the criteria.
However, the applications/ nominations received for the Jal Bhushan Awards have been directed to re-verify the marks awarded as per the criteria. In pursuance of this direction, the matter of re-evaluation of the marks awarded to the applications/ nominations received for Jal Bhushan Award in the Water Resources Department was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has decided to award the applications/ nominations received for the Jal Bhushan Award. The following committees are being constituted to re-evaluate the marks awarded as per the criteria given in Annexure-I to the Government Resolution dated 12/06/2020.
Mr. H.L. Gunale, In-charge Chief Engineer (viz. Department of Water Resources, Pune. Shri P.R. Narvekar, Superintending Engineer, e-Governance Board, Pune. Mrs. Sunanda Jagtap, Superintending Engineer, Quality Control Board, Pune. Mrs. Pallavi Jagtap, Executive Engineer, Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Pune Member Secretary
