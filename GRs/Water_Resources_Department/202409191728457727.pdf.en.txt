The Proposal: -
Moose Varna, Ta. Khamgaon, District - Near Buldhana village flows the river Gyanganga and on the right bank of the river in the village is the ancient Mahadev temple. A large number of devotees from far and wide visit the temple every year during the rainy season in the month of Shravan and for the past few years, the floods during the monsoon season have eroded the side edge of the temple, posing a threat to the temple and causing inconvenience to the devotees. Local public representatives from Varna Gram Panchayat have demanded at the field level that a flood protection wall be constructed along the river bank of the required length and in accordance with this demand, the budget for this work has been submitted to the government as per its approval after inspection at the field level. The proposal for the construction of the flood protection wall was under the consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government Decision: - Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur under Mojave Varna, Dt. Construction of Flood Protection Wall at Khamgaon, District Buldhana at a cost of Rs. 3,46,49, 782.00 - (Three crore forty-seven lakh ninety-nine thousand and seven hundred and eighty-two rupees), the Water Resources Department's budget for the year 2022-2023 and the Public Works Department's rate-based budget for the year 2022-2023 are being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of the Decision No. P. 2019 / (Q. 115/2019) / S. (M) dated 31/08/2019.
b) Government Circular No. FDW - 1089/2423/89 Simvya (Works), D. 21/09/1989 and d. The line of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn according to the 03/05/2018. The tender process and other proceedings should be carried out only after the flood line has been drawn up.
c) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, the externalities of the tariff list and the carrying distance at the competent level. Technical approval proceedings should be carried out only after the conception of the work has been approved from the competent level. If the concept results in a change in the cost or scope of the work, it will be mandatory to get it approved at the competent level.
d) The technical clearance authorities concerned shall be solely responsible for providing technical clearance in the budget excluding short work. The cost of the work is to be met from the available grants within the limits of administrative sanction and within the stipulated time. These works will not be given revised administrative approval.
f) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, the construction of the flood protection wall should not obstruct the flow of the river, should be ensured.
