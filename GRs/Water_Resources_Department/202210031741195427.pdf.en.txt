The Proposal:
Small Irrigation Project Naikwadi is located in Nashik Taluka, 25 km from Nashik city.
It's distant. It is built on a local canal in the Godavari valley. The project was completed in 1973 and has created a water storage of 1.07 LH. The cultivable area of the project is 131 hectares and the irrigated area is 94 hectares. There is a 3.6-km canal on the left bank of the project.
The process of setting up a Water Utilization Agency on the project is in progress and at present the notification of the project has been circulated in 3 gazettes.
The project has completed more than 45 years and the average rainfall at the project site is about 1500 mm. Due to heavy rains, the main and upstream embankments of the dam have been breached at many places. The dam is 45 years old and is completely damaged. Also, the approach conduit on the upstream side of the well is weak and completely submerged under silt.
