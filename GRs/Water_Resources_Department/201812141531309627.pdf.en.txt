The Manikpunj Medium Project is being constructed on the Manyad River, a tributary of the Girna River, near Manikpunj village in Nandgaon taluka of Nashik district under the Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation.
The work on the project has been completed and the drainage and drainage works are in progress.
Manikpunj Medium Project has an availability of 24.07 Dalghami water (50% reliability) and the project water storage is 12.46 Dalghami. The water storage available in the dam is 14.01 LMT which is 9.46 LMT. The dam has been completed and the proposed water storage is being done since 2013. Under this scheme, a total of 2685 hectares of area comprising 2300 hectares in Nandgaon (Nashik) and 385 hectares in Chalisgaon (Jalgaon) taluks will be benefited by irrigation.
