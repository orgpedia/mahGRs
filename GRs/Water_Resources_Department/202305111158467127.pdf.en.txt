The functions of the Committee: 1. Service/ Establishment matters of the employees working in the Corporation Office/ Chief Engineer's Office/ Circle Office/ Division/ Sub-Division/ Branch/ Sub-Branch of the technical and non-technical cadre in Group-A and Group-D of the Regional Office under the Water Resources Department (e.g. To make necessary recommendations to the Government of the employees for dealing with the appointment authority, promotions, postings, transfers, inductions, compassionate appointments and other ancillary matters) and to facilitate the administrative functioning.
(2) While making recommendations, the Committee shall ensure that the recommendations are in conformity with the guidelines of the General Administration Department and the Finance Department.
The committee has to submit its report to the government within two months.
