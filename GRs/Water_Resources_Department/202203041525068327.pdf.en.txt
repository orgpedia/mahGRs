Preamble: Pura-Bolinj Kharbhoomi Scheme, Ta. Vasai, G. Palghar is one of the schemes included in the Board's long-term master plan. This scheme is on the right bank of the Vaitarna sub-catchment in Vasai taluka. The length of the dam is 2520 m. There are 225 m and 1500 m on the scheme. There are a total of 2 of them. The plan is 105. The area is being restored and the area has been notified by the Central Department of Environment and Forests vide letter No. J/ 170011/ (32) 2001-1 A-III, Dy. Exemption from CRZ has been granted as on 22.08.2003.
This scheme has been completed by Kharland Board in 1964. Since it has been about 56 years since the completion of this scheme and due to the frequent tides, the saline water entering the receiving area and damaging the agricultural land, the beneficiary farmers on the scheme have demanded to get the scheme renewed so that the work of the scheme included in the master plan can be done. The cost of the project is Rs. 1,49,891/- which is Rs. 1,92,950/- received from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai vide letter under reference. The proposal to give administrative approval to the renovation work of the Kharbhoomi scheme in Vasai, G. Palghar was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: Pura-Bolinj Kharbhoomi Scheme, Ta. The cost of renovation of Vasai, G. Palghar based on the cost of Kharbhoomi Department for the year 2018-19 is estimated to be Rs. 1,57,38, 586/- (Rupees One Crore, Fifty Seven Lakhs, Thirty Eight Thousand, Five Hundred and Eighty Six only)
