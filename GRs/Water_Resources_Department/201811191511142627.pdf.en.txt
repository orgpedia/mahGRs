Introduction: Ambulaga Minor Irrigation Project is constructed on a local canal near Ambulaga village in Deglur, Nanded district. The project was completed in 1974. The storage capacity of the project is 1.152 Dalghami and live storage is 1.007 Dalghami. The project has an irrigation area of 495 sq.km. Earlier in 2015, the soil work of the canal was done from 0 to 7.5 km in the work done under RRR. Also included was the renovation of the selected structure. At present, 6.0 km is being irrigated on the left canal.
But irrigation is not enough. For irrigation to be full capacity, for repairs on the canal and to increase the utility of the canal by reducing the carrying cost, works like construction of carriage way bridge, construction of aqueduct and selective lining on the upstream and downstream side of the construction have been proposed.
