The proposal: The National Green Tribunal (NGT), Western Zone, Pune Bench, in Petition No. In pursuance of the order dated 25/2014 issued vide letter dated 13.08.2020 by the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune, it has been decided to extend the length of Seena River (up to the confluence of Bhima River) in Solapur district from Km 141 to 297, Mangi River Km. from 0 to 40 km. Determination of flood line between M.M. and U.N.A. (Hydrological study, computational measurement of maximum flood line, design of prohibitory (blue) flood line and control (red) flood line). Administrative approval for the budget of Rs. 421.82 lakh was under consideration at the Government level.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under this Government decision, the length of the Seena river (up to the confluence of the Bhima river) in Solapur district has been increased from 141 km to 297 km. from 0 to 40 km. Determination of the remaining length of the flood line between M. and Upanale (hydrological study, computational measurement of maximum flood line, design of prohibitory (blue) flood line and control (red) flood line). Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 421.82 lakhs.
The expenditure for the proposed works is to be made under the head Capital Expenditure on I-5, 4711 Flood Control Project, 01 Flood Control, 190, Investments in Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (01) Share Capital Contribution to various Irrigation Development Corporations (01) Share Capital Contribution to Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation (Programme) (4711 0209) and to be met from the sanctioned grants.
