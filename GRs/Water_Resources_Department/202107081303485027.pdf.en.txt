Introduction: The village of Mauje Kasengaon Ta Walwa Ji Sangli is located on the Pune-Bangalore highway in the Walwa taluka of Sangli district. A large river runs along the north side of the village. During the rainy season, water is drawn from 10 to 12 villages on the western side of the village, which causes flooding in the river. Due to heavy rains in 2019 and the backwaters of the Krishna River, many houses on the east and west sides of the highway have been flooded and suffered huge economic losses.
Due to the frequent floods every monsoon, houses, temples, and streets with potholes have been damaged. Sarpanch Mauje Kasegaon and the villagers have demanded that a flood protection wall should be constructed at Odhayaka. Also in this case. The Minister of Water Resources and Benefit Area Development, Maharashtra State has instructed to take appropriate action. Considering this matter.
