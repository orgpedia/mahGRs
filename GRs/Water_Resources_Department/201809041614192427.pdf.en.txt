The Proposal:
Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Shendurni under Jalgaon, Dist. The village of Jamner, G. Jalgaon is situated on the banks of the Sonad river. The Sonad River is a major tributary of the Waghur River. The flood in the Sonad river caused extensive damage around the Kazipura mosque and created panic among the devotees. This work is necessary so that the Kazipura Mosque on the river bank and the lives of the people and devotees are not harmed. The demand for this work was made by the Gram Panchayat, Shendurni. Jamner's resolution No. 3/11. This was done on 26.1.2016. The Government have approved the proposal for construction of flood protection wall of 50 meters length on the right side of Kazipura Masjid on the right bank of Sonad River vide Government Resolution No.Prasama-2016/ (P.S. 545/2016)/ C.V. (Ms.), 2016. As on 7.12.2016, Rs. Administrative approval was granted for an estimated cost of Rs. 50,48,275/-.
With the implementation of the new Common Rating of the Water Resources Department from 01.11.2016, the matter of giving revised administrative approval to the work was under the consideration of the government.
