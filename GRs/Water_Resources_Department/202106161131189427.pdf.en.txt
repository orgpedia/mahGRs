The Proposal:
A Study Group has been constituted under Reference No. 1 to conduct preliminary studies, guide and monitor the projects proposed in the Integrated State Water Plan for diversion of surplus water from Western Vahini River Basin (Konkan) to Marathwada and Girna (Tapi) Sub-Basin. The term of office of the Committee shall be six months from the date of issue of this Order. It was 29/12/2020.
The committee has not submitted its report yet. The government was considering extending the deadline to complete the work of the committee. Accordingly, the report is being prepared.
Cleaning paper:
Considering the scope and scope of the work, the above committee has been appointed. The deadline has been extended till 30.09.2021.
