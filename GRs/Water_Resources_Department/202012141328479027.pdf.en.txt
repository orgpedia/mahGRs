Introduction: Mouje Darfal (Sena) under Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune. Maadhi ji.
Solapur is situated on the bank of river Seena. There are some houses, schools and religious places near the banks of the river. The Seena River is a tributary of the Bhima River and is one of the major rivers in Madha Taluka. Maruti temple, crematorium and school grounds are facing a threat as the water of the Seena river has washed away the banks of the river and flooded the river banks.
Therefore, as per the demand made by the Gram Panchayat Mauje Darfal (Seena), the government was considering giving administrative approval to the work of the flood protection wall and the proposal of the Ghatpayers.
GOVERNMENT DECISION 1. Subject to the decision of this Government, the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune under Mouje Darfal (Sena) Ltd. Rs. Administrative Sanction is being granted for the cost of Rs. 3,09,69, 628/- (Rupees Three Crore Nine Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Twenty Eight only) subject to the following conditions.
(a) Government Resolution No. 2019/ (p. No. 115/19) C.V. (M.) D. Action to be taken as per the provisions of 31/08/19.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
d) The cost of the work is to be completed within the limit of revised administrative approval from the available grant and within the scheduled time. These works will not be given revised administrative approval.

e) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, it should be ensured that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.

(f) To submit quarterly progress reports to the Government from the date of issue of the work order till the completion of the work. After the completion of the work, the certificate should be provided to the government immediately.
c) Before giving technical approval to the said work, Reference No. 1 should be strictly adhered to. No technical sanction can be given for the work till the completion of the work.
The cost of the proposed works is to be covered under the head I- 5-4711 Capital Expenditure on Flood Control Project, Flood Control, 190, Public Sector and Other Undertakings Investments, (01) Share Capital Contributions to various Irrigation Development Corporations (01) Share Capital Contributions to Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation (4711 0209) (Programme) and to be met from the sanctioned grants.
The administrative sanction granted to this work is recorded in the CV (Revenue) Office Record No. 16/ 2019-2020.
This Government Order is being issued in accordance with the Financial Powers and Powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 and the Government Order dated 7/8/1992.
