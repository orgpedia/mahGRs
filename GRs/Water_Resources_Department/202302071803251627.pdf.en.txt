The Proposal:
Mouje Madilge Khurd, Ta. Bhudargad, Kolhapur is a village located on the right bank of the Vedganga river. The land of the river bank on the upstream and downstream side of this Madilge ghat is of soft layer.
The flood caused by heavy rainfall in the 2019-2021 monsoon season had caused extensive damage to both banks of the dam, threatening the standing crop and the embankment. The people's representatives have demanded that this work be done to prevent more erosion to the cultivated agricultural land and to protect the river banks and embankments on both banks from flood waters.
Accordingly, enjoy. A fresh budget has been prepared by conducting a survey at the proposed site in Bhudargad, G. Kolhapur. This mauj is Madilge Khurd, Ta. The proposal for construction of flood protection wall along the Vedganga river in Bhudargad, Kolhapur district was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government Decision: Maharashtra Krishna Valley Irrigation Development Corporation, Pune
For the construction of flood protection wall under Gargoti embankment on Vedganga river at Bhudargad, District Kolhapur, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Rate Based Budget of Water Resources Department for the year 2022-23 and Public Works Department for the year 2022-23 with an outlay of Rs. 1,50,20, 353/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh Twenty Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty Three) subject to the following conditions.
(c) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of Decision No. PUSB-2019/ (Q.S. 115/2019)/ SINVYA (M) dated 31/08/2019. Since the Government decision does not include ghat work, the competent authority will be responsible for providing technical approval excluding the ghat works included in the proposal.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 21.9.1989 and dt.
As per 03/05/2018, the alignment of flood protection wall should be fixed only after the flood line is drawn.
(c) Technical approval of the budget shall be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
