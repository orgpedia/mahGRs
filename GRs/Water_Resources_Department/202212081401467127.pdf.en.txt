The Proposal:
A storage pond. The project is proposed to be constructed on a local canal at Pimpaladhava in Nanded district, which falls in the Sudha sub-basin of the Godavari basin. The total storage capacity of the dam is 5.847 Dalghami and the project covers 660 villages of Pimpaldhav, Daur, Jamdari, Siddharthnagar Wadi, Matool, Ballal, Laglud and Ravangaon in Bhokar taluka. The area will benefit from irrigation. State Level Technical Advisory Committee, Nashik In the 142nd meeting held on 23/04/2020, Rs. The original Administrative Sanction proposal for Rs. 73.76 crore (Tentative Year 2018-19) has been approved.
The project meets the parameters of profitability ratio and financial return. However, the price of water does not fit into the parameters of the reservoir. Therefore, the project was submitted for the decision of the Expenditure Priority Committee with the consent of the Planning and Finance Department. Accordingly, the Expenditure Priority Committee has approved the project in its meeting held on 19 th September, 2022. Based on this approval, the decision to grant basic administrative approval has been taken as follows.
Government's decision:
A storage pond. Bhokar, G.Nanded, this project was sanctioned by the Water Resources Department based on the 2018-19 tariff list at a cost of Rs. 73.76 crore (Rs. 61.83 crore and Rs. 2.59 crore, GST and insurance Rs. 3.36 crore and royalty charges of Rs. 5.98 crore) decided to grant original administrative approval subject to the following conditions: The details of sub-heading wise provisions of the project are annexed as Annexure-A.
1) The Corporation will be fully responsible for implementing the existing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial limits, following the prescribed tender procedures, as per the requirements of the State Technical Advisory Committee, the directions given in connection with the project and the work, and correcting the errors.
(2) The said administrative approval shall not be deemed to be an approval of the technical aspects, field decisions or irregularities in the tender. It will be the responsibility of the department to investigate such cases as per the rules of the then prevailing government.
3) The Corporation shall take action to obtain all necessary technical approvals at the competent level.
4) To ensure that the project is completed within the stipulated time and the farmers/ beneficiaries are benefitted, care should be taken to ensure that the project is completed within the stipulated time and funds are provided on priority as per the plan and that there is no improper irrigation capacity.
5) A beneficiary water use agency should be established on the project.
