Terms and Conditions:
(i) Government Circular No. The Corporation is obliged to follow the guidelines in the Proforma-2013/ (785/13)/ CENVIA (Works), 01/01/2015, keep records and take action.
(ii) The Corporation shall be solely responsible for making detailed survey and concept based provisions of the construction before the technical approval.
(iii) Technical approval of the budget should be provided at the competent level by examining the outlines of the rates and the carrying gaps in the plan/ concept envisaged in the budget.
(iv) To make necessary provisions for special repairs of the distribution system by conducting joint inspection by the concerned officers of the water utility bodies and the field officers and taking them and the water utility members of the beneficiary areas into confidence regarding the implementation of the Management of Irrigation System by Farmers (MMISF) as per the Maharashtra Irrigation Act, 2005. (v) The Superintending Engineer shall be solely responsible for transferring the entire benefit area to the Water Utilization Agency within the prescribed time frame after completion of the special repair works of the proposed distribution system.
(vi) Removal of soil and grass plants/ shrubs should be done by the available machinery of the Mechanical Department of the Water Resources Department.
(vii) The proposed works shall result in saving by making full use of available construction material by way of excavation/ demolition.
(viii) The cost of the work should be met from the available grant within the limit of administrative sanction and within the stipulated time.
