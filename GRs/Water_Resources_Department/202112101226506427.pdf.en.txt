Circular:
The last date of seniority of the cadre of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) in the Water Resources Department is 01.01.2020. 2 has been issued by the Government here.
Now, the provisional seniority of 01.01.2021 of this cadre is mentioned in Reference No. 1 As per the instructions given here under the Government Circular, it is being published under Annexure-A attached subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been mainly adopted while preparing the said provisional seniority list.
The Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik as well as all the heads of the concerned offices are requested to bring this seniority to the notice of all the Deputy Engineers (Mechanical) (including engineers on leave/ deputation). Further, if any genuine errors/ omissions are found in this seniority list, the said temporary seniority of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) cadre shall be finalised on the understanding that there are no objections if the same is not brought to the notice of the Government in a written manner within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular.
