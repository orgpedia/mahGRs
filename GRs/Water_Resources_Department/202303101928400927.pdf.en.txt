The Proposal:
Alewadi Minor Irrigation Project The project is being constructed on the Adas river near Alewadi village in Sangrampur taluka of Buldhana district under the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur. The total storage capacity of the dam is 10.7547 LHM and the project will irrigate 750 hectares of land in 4 villages of Sangrampur taluka of Buldhana district.
The scheme has been approved by Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur vide its Resolution No. 7/69/ Alewadi.
L.P./ P.M.A./ (884/2009) D. 27/02/2009/ (Rate Table 2008-09) amounting to Rs. 38.32 crore has been accorded original administrative sanction as per Schedule 2008-09. The Minister has agreed to appear before the Board.

Alewadi Minor Irrigation Project Sangrampur G. Buldhana Project is included in Baliraja Jal Sanjeevi Yojana. The increase in the cost of the project is due to the following factors: The increase in tariff, higher rate of tendering, increase in land acquisition, change in concept, inadequate provisioning, other reasons and incidental costs etc. Considering this, the project does not meet the parameters, but as a special case, the Water Resources Department has allocated Rs. 205.61 crore. 2) The State Level Technical Advisory Committee in the Technical Scrutiny Report has recommended the issue No. The issues mentioned in 26 and 27 should be addressed.
3) New elements that create financial liabilities cannot be included in the project without government approval.

(b) The project is completed as planned.

It should receive final approval and only then the tender on this component should be finalised/ spent.
8) Before providing funds to the project, the Maha Manthan should take timely action regarding the formation of a" Water Utilization Society" of the beneficiaries as per the" Maharashtra Management of Irrigation Systems by Farmers Act 2005" on the project.
9) As the remaining works of the project include dam construction, land acquisition and captive tube distribution system, the top works of the dam of the project should be completed on priority basis as per the availability of funds.
10) The Corporation shall be solely responsible for not disburse funds or incur any additional expenditure beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for any purpose for the said project.
