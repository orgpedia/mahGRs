Preamble: While granting permission for boating in the water bodies of the Water Resources Department, it is necessary to make changes in accordance with the above circular for the purpose of granting annual license, charging fees for it, determining reserve amounts as well as extending and re-contracting the contract period. Boating will also boost tourism and generate employment opportunities for project victims and local unemployed. Moreover, fixing the rates of the ferry services can increase the income of the Irrigation Development Corporations. Taking this into consideration, the state government has decided to revise the procedure and rates for allowing boating in the reservoirs of large, medium and small projects under the Water Resources Department.
Government Circular No. 08.12.2003 The matter of amendment of NOWWIP- 2003/1359/ (15/2003)/ CE (M) was under consideration of the Water Resources Department. Accordingly, the following committee has been formed.
