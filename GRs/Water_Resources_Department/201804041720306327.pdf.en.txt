Preamble Wagh Minor Irrigation Scheme, Taluka Mokhada, District Palghar, The scheme was accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution No. 1294/ (415/94)/ Kharbhoomi, dated 06/09/1994 at a total cost of Rs. 995.54 lakhs. While preparing this administrative approval proposal, the rates were used as per the 1992-93 tariff schedule. The scheme was also granted first revised administrative approval under Government Resolution No.Lapayo 1599/ (139/99)/ Kharbhoomi, dated 03/11/2001 at a total cost of Rs. 2801.98 lakhs. While preparing the revised administrative sanction proposal, the rates were applied as per the 1998-99 tariff schedule. In the interim period, the cost of the scheme has been increasing due to price variation, higher rate of tender acceptance, increase in land acquisition cost, change in concept, etc., which necessitated grant of revised administrative approval to the scheme.
