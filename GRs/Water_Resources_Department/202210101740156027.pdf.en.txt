The Proposal:
The offices of Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute, Nashik, Superintending Engineer and Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik and Superintending Engineer, State Technical Advisory Committee Office, Nashik are presently not under the control of any Chief Engineer but directly under the control of Director General, Concept, Training, Hydrology, Research and Safety, Nashik.
However, special pay cases in respect of additional charge handled by the Superintending Engineer, grant of 15 days earned leave/ leave of absence of the Superintending Engineer, service benefits, provident fund cases, fixation of pay, increments, deputation orders and all other administrative matters require the approval of the Chief Engineer or there are difficulties in dealing with matters which are not within the competence of the Superintending Engineer but are within the competence of the Chief Engineer. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, Government of Maharashtra has also issued a notification regarding the approval of tenders and technical control of the offices of Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute, Nashik, Superintending Engineer, State Technical Advisory Committee Office, Nashik and Superintending Engineer and Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik. 218/ Buildings-II dated 16th December, 2015 to the Superintending Engineer for architectural work amounting to more than Rs. 2,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Two and a Half Crores only).
Technical approval and tender cannot be granted as per Para 135 (A) of Schedule-13 above. The Chief Engineer has the authority to sanction more than two and a half crores. the Tenders of the aforesaid NAMUD Office

Approval of the chief engineer is also required in the matter of approval and technical control or there are difficulties in proceeding with the matters which do not fall within the competence of the superintending engineer but fall within the competence of the chief engineer.
As per the request of the Director General, Concept, Training, Hydrology, Research and Safety, Nashik, the Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute, Nashik, the Superintending Engineer and the Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik and the Superintending Engineer, State Technical Advisory Committee Office, Nashik, the Chief Engineer, Central Concepts Association, Nashik, was under the consideration of the Government to authorize the Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute, Nashik, the Superintending Engineer and the Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik, and the Superintending Engineer, State Technical Advisory Committee Office, Nashik, as the competent Chief Engineer for administrative matters and technical control of these offices.
Government's decision:
The Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute, Nashik, the Superintending Engineer and Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik and the Superintending Engineer, State Technical Advisory Committee Office, Nashik are entrusted with the administrative and technical control of the office of the Chief Engineer, Central Conceptual Association, Nashik.
