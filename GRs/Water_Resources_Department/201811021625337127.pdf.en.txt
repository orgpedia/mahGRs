Government Decision No.: KUK/ 1090/ SA-242/ (112/90)/ MOPR (1), d. 05/08/1994 Preamble: Kukdi Irrigation Project is a composite project of five dams namely Yedgaon, Manikdoh, Vadaj, Dimbhe and Pimpalgaon Joghe. The total water consumption of the project is 1114.84 DLH. Under the project, 1,44,912 houses will be constructed in seven drought-prone taluks of Ambegaon, Junnar and Shirur in Pune district, Parner, Shrigonda and Karjat in Ahmednagar district and Karmala in Solapur district. The area will get benefit of irrigation through various canals of length 718.50 km.
The Government has sanctioned Project No. The original administrative approval was granted at a cost of Rs. 31.18 crore based on the 1964-65 schedule.
Thereafter, the Government issued the project No. The first revised administrative approval was granted at a cost of Rs. 123.03 crore based on the 1975-76 tariff schedule. Also, the project No. The Second Revised Administrative Sanction was granted at a cost of Rs. 692.18 crore based on the 1989-90 tariff schedule.
In the intervening period, the project cost has increased due to increase in tariff rates, acceptance of excess tenders, increase in cost of land acquisition (especially under the new Land Acquisition Act), increase in change in concept, new/ dropped provisions, inadequate provisions and other factors and consequential cost overruns (installation and equipment). The Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation has submitted its third revised administrative sanction proposal to the government for approval.
The State Level Technical Advisory Committee has recommended the third revised project report of Kukadi Irrigation Project at a cost of Rs. 3977.85 crore.

The Cabinet, after considering the present status of construction, reasons for price increase, remaining works of the project, has taken a conscious decision to give administrative approval to the Third Revised Project Report based on the 2016-17 schedule at a cost of Rs. 3948.17 crore with the objective of completing the project to provide irrigation benefit to the areas of seven drought prone taluks namely Ambegaon, Junnar and Shirur in Pune district, Parner, Shrigonda and Karjat in Ahmednagar district and Karmala in Solapur district.
Government Decision Third Revised Administrative Sanction is being accorded to Kukadi Irrigation Project, G.Pune at a cost of Rs. 3948.17 crore (Rs. 3311.49 crore for direct works and Rs. 636.68 crore for incidental works) based on Water Resources Department Schedule 2016-17 and Public Works Department Schedule 2016-17 subject to the following conditions: The details of sub-heading provisions of the project are annexed as Annexure A.
1) The minimum required area should be acquired by considering the work done on the remaining land acquisition area in order to reduce the cost of the project.
2) The useful water storage of the project is proposed to be increased by 2.08%. Considering the above, the Department of Water Resources has issued a notification vide S.N.Mirakul 2015/ (425/2015)/ Medium Project, D.No. In accordance with the provision of 16/11/2016, the Maharashtra Water Resources Regulatory Authority should be confirmed.
3) Considering that the project has not obtained environmental clearance, action should be taken to send a proposal to the Ministry of Environment of the Central Government for confirmation of the requirement of environmental clearance.
4) Necessary provision should be made in respect of new provisions, inadequate provisions and other matters, CRT Estt, Arbitration Award.
5) Fulfill the points in the report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee.
6) New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
7) The project work should be completed as per the plan and within the revised administrative approval cost limit.
