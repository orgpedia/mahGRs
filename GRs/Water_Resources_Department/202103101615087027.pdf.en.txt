The Proposal:
As per the Government Decision dated 18.11.2008, 13 posts have been sanctioned for the Executive Engineer, Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Special Team, Pune on internal rotation basis from the office of the Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation and the establishment which has been additional under the review of the Water Resources Department. Similarly, 4 additional posts have been made available as per Government Decision dated 01.08.2009. A total of 17 posts have been sanctioned for this team. These positions are available at various institutions.
It was under consideration of the Government to grant in-service approval to these posts from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the continuation of service of 17 posts in the accompanying prospectus on the fixed temporary establishment of the Chief Engineer, (JE) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Ministry of Water Resources, Mumbai, Superintending Engineer, Kukadi Project Board, Pune, Executive Engineer, Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Special Squad, Pune, subject to the following conditions from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
a) Fixed temporary posts shall not be vacant for a period of more than 6 months.
(b) The posts in the fixed temporary establishment. As per the revised figure approved under the Government decision dated 06.02.2003, 45297 posts are in the limit.
