The construction, mechanical and electrical works of the main dam have been completed and the project water storage and irrigation capacity creation has been completed by the end of June 2009. The project was handed over to the Irrigation Management Department in June 2018.
The Superintending Engineer, Mechanical Board, Nanded has instructed to repair the ancillary components of the vertical lifters on the Raja Takli Dam. Also, from time to time, mechanical and architectural engineers have been instructed to carry out repair work during the project visit.
The King's Tower is a high level dam. On 05.06.2019, Deputy Engineer (s) Inspection Sub-Division No. 2, Aurangabad, gave a report after observing the pre-monsoon.
As per letter dated 01.07.2019 submitted to Executive Engineer (s), Inspection Team, Aurangabad. Accordingly, the Executive Engineer (s), Inspection Team, Aurangabad, issued the order.
As per the letter dated 15.07.2019, the inspection report of the pre-monsoon 2019 has been sent to the Executive Engineer, Jayakwadi Irrigation Department, Aurangabad. As per the Pre-monsoon 2019 Monitoring Report, there are 86 deficiencies in the Raja Tank High Level Barrage.
Considering the technical comments of the Superintending Engineer, Mechanical Board, Nanded, and the feedback of the Architecture Department on it, the difficulties faced by the Architecture Department in operating the dams, the errors in the Pre-Precipitation Inspection Report 2019, the government was considering giving administrative approval to the special repair work of the vertical lifters and other ancillary elements in the Raja Tankali High Level Dam in view of the safety of the dams and for the smooth operation of the dam's gates.
