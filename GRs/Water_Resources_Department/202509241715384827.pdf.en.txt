The Proposal: -
The Aurad Latur system dam is located on the Terna River near the village of Aurad in Latur district. The barrage is located at a distance of 66.15 km from the site of the Terna Dam. The total projected storage capacity of the barrage is 883 MWe 3.669. There is an irrigated area. The Aurad Latur system barrage was completed in 2019 and water was first stored by the construction department in 2019. The project has since been transferred to the Department of Management for maintenance and gate management in 2019.
Since the completion of the construction of the project, no repairs have been carried out to the gates and the high rise system in the dam. The Chief Engineer (Mechanics), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
On 12/9/2023, he visited various dams / barrages on Manjra river and certified the need for repair of various mechanical and electrical components and gave some suggestions in this regard, including the proposed Aurad dam. The Executive Director has directed that these works be proposed under special amendment as the required funds for carrying out the proposed works in connection with the repair of mechanical and electrical components of Aurad Dam are not available at the corporation level. With the completion of the repair work of the mechanical and electrical components of the dam, the water leakage from the gate will be stopped. Also, the project proposed water storage will be available for irrigation during Rabi Summer season at full capacity. Also, after this repair, the door will be easier to open.
