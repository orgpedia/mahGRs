A proposal for grant of administrative approval for works costing Rs. 4,73,00, 400/- has been received. In 2017-18, the river was in spate due to heavy rains and around 80 to 90 houses on the right and left sides of the stream have been inundated. There are people living on the banks of the river and it is necessary to build a protective wall on both sides of the river bed. Therefore, if the above villages along the Purna, Megha and Bahuda rivers are protected from the side of the river bed, the people living along the river banks will be protected from flood waters to a great extent, as well as the damage to houses and agriculture will be avoided and the subsidy to the concerned will be saved in accordance with the damage.
