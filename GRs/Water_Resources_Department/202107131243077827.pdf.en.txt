The Proposal:
The government has decided to celebrate the birth centenary of former Chief Minister late Shankarraoji Chavan through various programmes. The former Chief Minister also felicitated those who have done excellent work in the field of water resources, water conservation and water supply. The Department of Water Resources, Government of India has decided vide Government Decision dated 12.06.2020 to confer Jal Bhushan Award on the occasion of birth centenary of Shankarraoji Chavan through Water Resources Department in collaboration with Water Conservation and Water Supply Department.
The evaluation of 126 eligible applications by inviting online applications for this award is subject to Government Decision No. 2 and 3 respectively. Accordingly, the awardees for outstanding work in the field of water resources, water conservation and water supply will be selected for Jalabhushan Puraskar 2020. The State Level Selection Committee in the presence of the Minister for Water Resources, Shri. Meeting was held on 06/07/2021. In the meeting, the government was considering to announce the names of those selected for the Jal Bhushan Award 2020. A decision in this regard is being taken.
The decision was taken by the former Chief Minister. On the occasion of the birth centenary year of Shankarrao Chavan, the names of the following persons who have done outstanding work in the field of water resources, water conservation and water supply for the year 2020 are being announced for the Jal Bhushan Awards 2020.
