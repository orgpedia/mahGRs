Government circular
A provisional seniority list of officers of the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) in the Water Resources Department has been prepared as on 1.1.2021 and is annexed to this circular at Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Heads of Departments of Water Resources Department are hereby informed to bring the seniority of the present order to the notice of all the officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) of the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture). The officers of the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) should submit their objections/ suggestions regarding the said seniority list to the Government along with the views of the concerned Head of the Department by 16.02.2021. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
