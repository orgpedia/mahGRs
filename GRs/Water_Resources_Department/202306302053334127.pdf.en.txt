7. Since the said transfer is a request for transfer, the transfer related travel allowance and other ancillary benefits shall not be admissible to the concerned engineers.
Further, as per the provisions of the Water Resources Department, Government Notification, dated 25.04.2016, the concerned Chief Engineer is the competent authority for general transfers of Group-B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) cadre officers in the field offices and the concerned Executive Director/ Director General for transfers under two Chief Engineer offices in the same Corporation. Accordingly, he is informed that considering the transfers of the Deputy Engineer under him from the government level, care should be taken to strictly follow the provisions of Section 5 (2) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005 as well as other provisions of the Act while making transfers under him.
9. The concerned engineers should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government.
Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the transferred employees. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned employees at the original address. If the leave applications of the transferred employees are sent to the parent office, these applications will not be taken into consideration.
