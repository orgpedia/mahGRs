Preamble: The Central Government is mandated to effectively manage the funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds.
It has been directed to adopt the revised procedures under the memorandum dated March 23, 2019. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Public Finance Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, it is necessary to open a separate bank account in the authorized bank for the implementation of the centrally sponsored schemes by appointing a single nodal agency (SNA). Further, it has been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the centrally sponsored schemes of the Water Resources Department was under the consideration of the government.
