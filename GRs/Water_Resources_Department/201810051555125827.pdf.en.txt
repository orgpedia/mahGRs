Preamble: Under the control of the Superintending Engineer, Jalgaon Irrigation Project Board, Jalgaon Office, the Executive Engineer, Upasana Irrigation Works Department, Jalgaon, the work of Upasana Irrigation Scheme under the Divisional Office, Kurha-Vadhoda, Varangaon-Talwel and Bodwad Projects is in progress.
The nature of administrative and project work and expenditure under this Divisional Office is audited annually by the Accountant General, Nagpur Office. But due to the non-approval of the post of Divisional Accountant under the said Divisional Office, problems are arising in the administrative work related to accounting. Considering the need and urgency of the work in the divisional offices of the Irrigation Department, Jalgaon, it was under the consideration of the government to provide the post of Divisional Accountant. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision
It is hereby approved to make available the post of Divisional Accountant under Majalgaon Project Division, Kesapuri, which has been closed under the Government decision at Reference No.4, to the Divisional Office of the Executive Engineer, Irrigation Works Department, Jalgaon.
2.The salary and non-salary expenses of the employee working in this post should be shared with the Superintending Engineer, Jalgaon Irrigation Project Board, Jalgaon, from the approved account head.
Government Decisions, Finance Department, No. 1092/ CR-806/ Expenditure-6, Sec. This Order is being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Department under the provisions of 15.10.1992 and with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and the Deputy Secretary.
