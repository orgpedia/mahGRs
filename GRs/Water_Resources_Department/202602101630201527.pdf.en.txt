The Proposal: -
The Shivani High Level Dam is located on the Manjra River near Shivani village in Latur district. The barrage is located at a distance of 84.35 km from the Manjra dam site. The total projected water storage of the barrage is 9.810 Dalhousie, 1950. There is an irrigated area. The Sewage High Level Barrage was completed in 2015 and water was first stored by the Construction Department in 2015. Later in the year 2018
The project was transferred to the Management Department for maintenance, repair and operation.
It is.
Since the completion of the construction of the project, no repairs have been carried out to the gates and the high rise system in the dam. The Chief Engineer (Mechanics), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
On 12/9/2023 visit the various dams / barrages on the Manjra River and see the various mechanical and electrical
Some suggestions have been made to certify that the components need repair, including the proposed sewing dam. Repair of sewing dam machinery and electrical components.
Accordingly, the required funds for the proposed works are not available at the corporation level.

The Executive Director has directed that these works be proposed under a special amendment. The sewing high level dam will stop the leakage of water from the gate once the repair work of the mechanical and electrical components of the dam is completed. Also, the project proposed water storage will be available for irrigation during Rabi and Summer seasons at full capacity. The repair will also help in smooth operation of the gate and thus maintain the safety of the dam during flood control.
Sewani Dam is a specified dam as per the provision of the Dam Safety Act-2021. Therefore, as various mechanical and electrical components needed to be repaired for the safety of the sewing dam, a proposal was under the consideration of the government to grant administrative approval for repair work of the mechanical component required for dam safety and easy gate operation of the sewing high-level dam T.G. Latur. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
The Government's Decisions:
Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 1 crore for the construction of Sewani High Level Dam T.G. Latur, Dam Safety and Special Repair of Mechanical and Electrical Components required for Ease of Operating Door. 4,04,38, 7651 / - (Rs. The proposal is being accorded administrative approval in the accompanying form subject to the following conditions:
1) It shall be mandatory to comply with the prevailing Government Decisions / Circulars for technical approval, tendering process, planning and completion of work, expenditure within the limits of administrative approval, etc.
(2) No funds may be distributed or spent excessively for any purpose in this budget beyond the generally approved administrative approval limit.
3) After the completion of this repair work, the zonal authorities will be fully responsible for transferring the benefit area by setting up a water use agency within the benefit area of the project.
4) Administrative approval means approval of the technical aspects of the report or the field-level decision on the tender will not be considered.
5) Mechanical and electrical component budgets should be given technical approval at the competent level.
6) These special repair works should be undertaken by the Mechanical Association.
7) The revised cost of the thematic special repair work should be approved at the next meeting of the Board of Regulators.
8) Special efforts should be made by the zonal authorities to recover the catchment area.
