Read:- 1) Government Decision No. 291/2019/ CC. V. (m.) 23.08.2019.
Preamble: During the monsoon season of 2019, unprecedented flood situation had arisen due to excessive rainfall in the Krishna Valley during the months of July and August. Kolhapur and Sangli districts are the worst affected. There was a huge loss of life and property. Accordingly, a Committee was constituted vide Government Resolution dated 23rd August, 2019 to investigate the causes of floods in Bhima and Krishna basins in 2019 and to prepare a future remedial report.
The above Committee has so far met and studied the dimensions of rainfall and heavy rainfall warning, integrated reservoir operation, emergency action plan and mechanism, use of new technology and guidelines for flood control and forecasting, etc. and visited the flood affected areas of Sangli and Kolhapur districts on 23rd and 24th September 2019 and submitted the study report of Krishna Sub-Division to the Government. The committee made 18 recommendations. The recommendations of the committee were accepted by the government.
Government Decision: Krishna Valley Flood Study Committee It has been decided to accept, reject and reject the recommendations of the study report of Krishna sub-division submitted to the Government on 27th May, 2020. The details of the decisions taken by the Government are given in Annexure A.
