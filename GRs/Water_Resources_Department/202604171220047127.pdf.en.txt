The Proposal: -
The proposal for approval for re-planning of Tulsi, Dudhganga and Dhamani Medium Project was received by the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune vide Reference Letter No. The following has been received by the Government under section 01.
a) Tulsi Project - Tulsi Project, Ta. The Radhanagari, District Kolhapur project is built on the Tulsi, a tributary of the Bhogavati River. The project was completed in 1976 and the water use 2.588 Aghfu B irrigation area is 4720. The Government's Decisions:
Re-water planning of Tulsi, Dudhganga and Dhamani projects is being approved subject to the following conditions as per Annexure A, B and C respectively.
1) The Executive Director and Chief Engineer will be fully responsible for the re-water planning of the Dudhganga-Tulsi B Dhamani medium project submitted by the Corporation.
2) The Integrated State Water Plan should be suitably modified in accordance with this plan.
3) If the project area needs to be notified / de-notified due to this re-watering, appropriate action should be taken.
4) Any plan proposed in the future from the saved water reserves should be approved by an independent administrator.
5) Government Decision No. Sankarna-2014 / (96/14) / As (faith) d. Under 23.11.2016, approval should be obtained from the competent level.
6) The Corporation shall ensure that the proposed re-planning does not violate the provisions of the Krishna Basin Water Disputes Tribunal. Also, re-water planning of the combined Krishna basin should be submitted to the government.
