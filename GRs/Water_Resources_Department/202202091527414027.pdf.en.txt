The Proposal:
The Office of the Chief Engineer (Power), Hydropower Project, Mumbai and the Executive Engineer working under that Office, Narmada Team, Mumbai and the Executive Engineer, Control and Inspection Team, Mumbai, the Superintending Engineer, Tapi-Godavari Hydropower and Upasana Irrigation Board, Jalgaon, the Executive Engineer, Ghatghar Hydropower Divisional Team, Kalwa, Thane and the Superintending Engineer, Koyna (Vivayan) Sankalp Chitra Board, Pune are under the consideration of the Government for approval of a new sub-heading under 2801 (Programme) instead of the sub-heading under the head title 4801 (Programme) for the operation and administration of these Power Offices.
Government Decision: Chief Engineer (Power), Hydel Power Project, Mumbai and Executive Engineer, Control and Inspection Team, Mumbai, Superintending Engineer, Tapi-Godavari Hydel and Upasana Irrigation Board, Jalgaon, Executive Engineer, Ghatghar Hydel Division Team, Kalwa, Thane and Superintending Engineer, Koyna (Vivayan), Sankalp Chitra Mandal, Pune. The Office of the Accountant General, Nagpur has issued a new sub-heading under the head Accounting Head 2801 (Programme) under Heading I-3, Heading 2801. Approval has been granted vide informal reference dated 08.07.2021. Details of the new title are as follows:
