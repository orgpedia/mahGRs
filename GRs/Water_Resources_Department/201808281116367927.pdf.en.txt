Government Resolution Irrigation Department No. MIS 1091/37/91/ A (Training), dated 25.2.1993.
Preamble: The amount of remuneration to be paid to the probationers/ examiners/ coordinating officers/ other officers/ employees for various examinations conducted by Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik under the Water Resources Department has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 25.2.1993. The government was contemplating to revise the rates.
Government's decision:
The examiners/ examiners/ co-ordinating officers/ others to be appointed for the aforesaid examinations
All the orders issued earlier regarding the rate of remuneration to be paid to the officers/ employees are being superseded. The Government hereby approves the payment of honorarium at the revised rates for the examinations conducted by Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik under the Water Resources Department.
