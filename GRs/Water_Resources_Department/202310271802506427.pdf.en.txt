The works include plugging the leakages, desilting and re-construction of the dam. As a preventive measure against siltation of the dam, it has been proposed to fill the upstream side with black soil as per Indian Standard (8414) and to strengthen the dam wall on the downstream side. The proposed works are essential for the safety of the dam and after completion of these works, the life of the dam will be extended by strengthening the dam.
