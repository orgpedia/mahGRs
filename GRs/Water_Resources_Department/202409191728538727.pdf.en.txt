Mojay Hiwarkhed, Ta. The Bodi River flows through the center of the village of Khamgaon, district Buldhana, and there is a large Dalit settlement, Dalit Samaj Mandir, old Brihadwan Baba Mandir, and a health centre on both sides of the river.
The flood is threatening Dalit Basti, Dalit Samaj Mandir, the ancient Bridaban Baba Mandir as well as the health centre as the Bordi river floods every monsoon. Hence, a demand has been made at the field level by Gram Panchayat Hivarkhed and local public representatives for the construction of a flood protection wall of the required length on both the banks at the site and in accordance with this demand, the budget for this work has been submitted to the government after conducting an inspection at the field competent level. The proposal for the construction of the flood protection wall was under the consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government Decision: - Mouje Hiwarkhed under Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, Ta. Construction of Flood Protection Wall at Khamgaon, District Buldhana at a cost of Rs. 4,79,75, 615.00 - (Four crore eighty nine lakh seventy five thousand six hundred and fifteen rupees) are being granted administrative sanction subject to the following conditions as per the tariff list of the Water Resources Department and the Public Works Department. 2022-23
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of the Decision No. P. 2019 / (Q. 115/2019) / S. (M) dated 31/08/2019.
b) Government Circular No. FDW - 1089/243/89 Civya (Works), D. 21/09/1989 and d. The line of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn according to the 03/05/2018. Tenders and other proceedings should not be taken unless a flood line has been drawn.
c) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, the externalities of the tariff list and the carrying distance at the competent level. Technical approval proceedings should be carried out only after the conception of the work has been approved from the competent level. If the concept results in a change in the cost or scope of the work, it will be mandatory to get it approved at the competent level.
d) The technical clearance authorities concerned shall be solely responsible for providing technical clearance in the budget excluding short work. The cost of the work is to be met from the available grants within the limits of administrative sanction and within the stipulated time. These works will not be approved by the Revised Administrator.
f) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, ensuring that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
g) Plan to ensure proper drainage of water in the area after implementing the flood protection plan.
