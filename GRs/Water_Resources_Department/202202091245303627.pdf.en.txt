Introduction: Lower Manar Project is a major project in Nanded district. Under the project, a dam has been constructed near Varwant village in Kandhar taluka. There are two left and right canals on both banks of Lower Manar Project with a length of 69 km and 21 km respectively. 1 with a length of 67 km, and Manar Godavari Branch Canal No. The total length of the project is 65 km and the total area covered is 23,310 hectares. The proposal proposes to reconstruct one bridge and reconstruct two carriageway bridges and repair 14 outlet gates on both the branch canals.
