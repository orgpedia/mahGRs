The project visits under this training programme will be in the nature of one (1) week stay at the project site. After the project visit, the trainees studied the responsibilities of the electrical engineer on the project.
Submit a report.
1.0 The schedule of Vocational Training Courses at META, Nashik is given in the attached prospectus. The training will be conducted online by META, Nashik. During the training period, the trainees will be required to submit the daily diary online every day.
2.0 A certificate of successful completion of each training module should be provided to the trainees through META.
3.0 The cost of the training shall be borne by Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Nashik from the grant received from the Water Resources Department.
