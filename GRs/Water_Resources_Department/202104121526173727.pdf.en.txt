Government's decision:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the posts under the State Government are required to be fixed. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities notified under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification.
As per the notification, the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service, even if the designations of such posts are different.
The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department has given instructions to ensure the posts for the disabled. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021.
The central government has said this. The posts are ensured for the Divyang by the notification dated 04.01.2021. As decided for the post of peon in Group-D cadre in that list, posts are being ensured in the Water Resources Department (itself) as shown in the return A.
The decision was taken by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India. Notification dated 04.01.2021 of the Department of Social Justice& Empowerment, Govt. being issued as per the instructions issued vide Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
