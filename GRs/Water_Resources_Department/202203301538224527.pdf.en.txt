Introduction: The Mula Dam was completed in 1972 at Mauje Baragaon Nandur on the Mula River. There is a 6 m wide and 3 km long road on the wall of the Mula Dam for maintenance and maintenance and this road takes 250 m to connect to the main road from the left bank. The total length of the road is 3.250 km.
The Mula Dam area and the class tourist area have been cordoned off. The road is always crowded with tourists and locals. The residents of the village have been complaining to the authorities concerned to repair the road. The project was completed in 2010-11.
