Government's decision:
With this decision, the Arjuna Medium Irrigation Project will be implemented. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Fifth Revised Project Report of Rajapur, G. Ratnagiri at a cost of Rs. 1008.96 crore based on the 2018-19 Tariff. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 939.80 crore is provided for expenditure on works and Rs. 69.16 crore for incidental expenses. Sub-heading wise provisions are being added to Goshwara.
The fifth revised administrative approval granted to the scheme has been recorded in the Medium Project Operations Register with serial number 08/ 2021.
The remaining work of the project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the limits of the revised administrative approval.
The expenditure incurred on the operation of the scheme under the head Demand No. I-5, 4701 Capital expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation, 80 General, 190 Investment in Public Sector and Other Undertakings (02) Schemes, (02) (01) Share capital contribution to Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation (4701H629) and the subsidy sanctioned under the same.
The Fifth Amendment Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions:
5.1) The points in the Scrutiny Report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee-2 should be fulfilled.
5.2. The said revised administrative approval means that the approval cannot be taken for irregularities that have occurred at various stages of project implementation.
5.3) Proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the project should be continued.
The decision to grant revised administrative sanction will not affect the proceedings of the departmental inquiry.
5.4. New elements which create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
5.5. Work on tenders in progress should be completed on priority. The remaining works should be prioritized and works/ tenders should be processed accordingly so that the water storage is maximized for irrigation.
5.6 No additional expenditure or disbursement of funds for any purpose beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for the said project. In this regard, the existing government decisions, guidelines of CVC, limits of financial powers, prescribed tender procedures should be strictly implemented.
