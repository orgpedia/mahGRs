INTRODUCTION: The new Mutha Right Canal, 13 km, passing through the city of Pune. It broke between 11 am and 11.15 am on 27/09/2018. In connection with the accident, Minister of Water Resources. During the field visit on 28/09/2018, it has been directed to constitute a committee to find out the reasons for the canal breach. Accordingly, the government had constituted the committee.
Government decision: The following Technical and Administrative Committee is being constituted to find out the reasons for the breach in the new Mutha right canal km 13 between 11 am and 11.15 am on 27/09/2018, to find out the remedial plan accordingly and to propose guidelines:
1) Chief Engineer (Architecture), Hydro Power and Quality Control, Chairman Irrigation Building, Pune 2) Deputy Collector and Regional Officer, Pune City 3) Superintending Engineer, Pune Irrigation Project Board, Pune 4) Deputy Commissioner, Ghole Road Regional Office, Pune Municipal Corporation, Pune 5) Superintending Engineer, Koyna Sankalp Chitra Mandal, Pune Member Secretary
The mandate of the Committee is to: 1) To investigate the causes of the New Mutha canal breach.
2) To ascertain the reasons for the damage caused to the civilian population due to the flood caused by the breach of the New Mutha Canal.
3) To prepare a list of potentially dangerous places in the Pune city limits of the new Mutha canal and to suggest measures for the same.
4) Propose guidelines for maintenance of the canal within the limits of Pune city.
The necessary information to the above committee should be made available by the Pune Municipal Corporation and the Water Resources Department. Also, if the committee calls for a hearing or with information from time to time, the concerned should be present.
The committee will submit its report on the new Mutha right canal to the government by December 15, 2018.
