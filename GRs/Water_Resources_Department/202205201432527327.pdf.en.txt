The Proposal:
On the flood situation in Chiplun and Mahad towns, The Chief Minister presided over the meeting. The meeting was held on 15/12/2021. In the meeting, the Chiplun Rescue Committee member stated that the flood risk of Chiplun city increases due to the inflow of water from the Kolakewadi dam during the rainy season. A study group under the chairmanship of Mr. D. N. Modak, retired Chief Engineer, has been constituted to study the facts and suggest measures in this regard. The Prime Minister has given. As per this directive, the matter of forming a study group was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As per the minutes of the above meeting, the Government have approved the constitution of the following Study Group to suggest measures for planning the release of water from the Kolakevadi Dam during the monsoon season in view of the increase in flood risk due to the inflow of water from the Kolakevadi Dam during the monsoon season.

The work of the study group:
Impact of Koyna discharge on Chiplun town and surrounding areas during July 2021 Flood and quantification of discharge from Kolkewadi Dam during that period and reasons for discharge.
2. Study of maximum discharge of Kolakewadi water, its rise in the waterfall of Vashishti river in Chiplun city at the time of maximum tide level and its effect on Chiplun city area.
3) To determine the revised Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) regarding the water to be released into the Vashishti River after the power generation from the power house at the base of the Kolakevadi Dam or the Kolakevadi flood discharge water in coordination with the Water Resources Department, the Energy Department and the Revenue Department.
4. How has the excavation material along the river bed obstructed the digging of the tunnel of the then power house in the river bed from Kolakewadi to the confluence of the Vashishthi river? Also, after studying the feasibility of this river, at that time, the project report provides for the release of water or how to give feedback/ recommendations.
The duration of the study group:
The tenure of the study group will be two months from the date of issue of this order.
