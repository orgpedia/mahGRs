Introduction: The Purna Medium Project is built on the upstream of Vishroli village in Chandur Bazar Taluka of Amravati district. The main building was completed in 2007. The project is being implemented since 2008-09. The total length of the earthen dam of the project is 2822 m and the length of the stone dam (Jalotsarani) is 298 m with a maximum height of 45.395 m. From the left side of the project at 300m to 11.03 km. The canal is 6,275 km long and covers 36 villages in Chandurbazar taluka. The area is receiving irrigation water. Under this project, the Regional Rural Water Supply Scheme of 105 villages and the Chandurbazar Water Supply Scheme of the Municipal Council have been implemented.
A total length of 2822 meters has been constructed on the right and left sides of the project, with pitching on the U/ s side. The length of the dam ranges from 400 CE to 847 CE. No. 1350 to 1650 and c. Ashmapatla has collapsed between 1800 and 2500 AD. Cracks have also been found in the dam between 2190 and 2550 meters, both of which are also mentioned in the dam status report of the Dam Safety Organization.
The road leading to the dam has been damaged at various places. Therefore, it has been decided to delay the entry to the dam during the emergency period.
Hence, the road should be repaired immediately.
