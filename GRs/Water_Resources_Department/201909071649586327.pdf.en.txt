The Proposal:
The Chief Engineer, Hydrology Project, Nashik, under the Superintending Engineer, Base Material Separation Board, Nashik, has been entrusted with the task of laying down guidelines for proper diagnosis of discharge and flood planning in case of emergency due to flood situation in the State during the year 2005 and 2006. The Valley Conservation Department, Pune has been made operational as per the Government decision of 2. This Samarupan Vibhag is providing real time data of RTDAS, RTSF& ROS (Flood Forecasting Model) system to the concerned Superintending Engineers and Chief Engineers of Krishna and Bhima basins by conducting comparative studies on actual and predicted rainfall, rainfall, discharge, flood etc. and using it to control the flood situation. In order to make effective use of the Real Time Data Acquisition System (RTDAS) and Real Time System Flow& Reservoir Operation System (RTSF& ROS) model set up in the Krishna and Bhima basins, the Chief Engineer, Hydrology Project, Nashik was under the consideration of the Government to transfer the control of the technical aspects regarding the works to be done under the National Hydrology Project (NHP) of the Chief Engineer, Hydrology Project, Nashik, under the Base Material Separation Board, Nashik, the Basin Preparation Department, Pune, and the control of annual flood control and administrative matters to the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Pune.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: REVIEW-2019/ P.S. 66/2019/ ASTHA (Reorganization)
The government's decision:
It is hereby approved to transfer the control of the annual flood control and administrative matters of the Divisional Office of Basin Conservation Division, Pune under the Base Material Separation Board, Nashik to the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Pune for efficient utilization of the Real Time Data Acquisition System (RTDAS) and Real Time System Flow& Reservoir Operation System (RTSF& ROS) model set up in Krishna and Bhima basins.
The said Department shall function directly under the Chief Engineer in the Regional Office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Pune.
The administrative and technical control of the divisional office of the Valley Conservation Department, Pune has been changed and no change has been made in the sanctioned establishment and office space of the department.
However, the control of the technical aspects of hydrology for the new works to be taken up under the National Hydrology Project (NHP) will remain with the Chief Engineer, Hydrology Project, Nashik.
Since there is a change in the administrative and technical control of the Divisional Office of the Valley Samrupan Division, Pune, the Finance Division, Government Decision, No. TRW-1083/ CR- 611/83/ ADM-9, Dy.
