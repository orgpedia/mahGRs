The Proposal:
Ujani on river Bhima under Bhima (Ujani) project. The dam has been constructed at Madha, Solapur district with a capacity of 117.25 Aghfu (3320 Dalghami). The project is located in the' Rest of Maharashtra' region under the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation. The project is planned to benefit 181833 irrigated areas in a total of 13 talukas of Pune, Ahmednagar and Solapur districts. Out of 13 taluks, 6 taluks (Karjat, Karmala, Mohol, Madha, North Solapur and South Solapur) are drought prone while the remaining 7 taluks (Indapur, Daund, Shrigonda, Pandharpur, Malshiras, Mangalwedha and Akkalkot) are partially drought prone.
The live storage available in Bhima (Ujani) Project is 1520.87 Dalghami (53.71 LH) and combined storage of Bhima (Ujani) Project and 10 other schemes is 84.34 LH. Out of which, the total water utilization for irrigation under Bhima (Ujani) Project is 1193.15 Dalghami (42.14 Agfu).
The Government has sanctioned Project No. The original administrative approval was granted at a cost of Rs. 40.51 crore.
Thereafter, the Government issued the project No. The first revised administrative approval was granted at a cost of Rs. 113.08 crore under Section 2. The Corporation has also issued Project Reference No. The Second Revised Administrative Sanction was granted at a cost of Rs. 1405.67 crore based on the 2001-02 tariff schedule.
In the intervening period, the project cost has increased due to increase in tariff rates, increase in cost of land acquisition (especially due to new Land Acquisition Act), increase in new/ excluded provisions, inadequate provisions and other factors and consequential cost overruns (ETP and others). The Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation has submitted the third revised administrative sanction proposal of the project to the government for approval.
The proposal for third revised administrative approval of the project has been recommended by the State Level Technical Advisory Committee.
