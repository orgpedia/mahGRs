Preamble : -
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Water Resources Regulatory Authority Act, 2005, the State Water Resources Council has been constituted under Section 16, sub-sections (1) and (2), under the Chairmanship of the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Maharashtra. Further, under Section 15, sub-section (1) of the said Act, the State Water Board has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra.
The State Water Resources Council has been constituted to approve the Integrated State Water Plan. The Integrated State Water Plan (ISWP) envisages balanced sustainable and integrated water resources management through scientific planning, development, monitoring and assessment of all irrigation, drinking water and multi-purpose water resources projects across all river basins ॥ the State. The Plan also covers initiatives such as river rejuvenation, intra- and inter-basin water transfers, establishment of an integrated decision support system for water resources management, and climate-resilient interventions including water recycling and reuse, urban flood management, desalination, wetlands restoration and water tourism.
Further, to ensure sustainable water management as envisaged under the Integrated State Water Plan, key initiatives outlined in “Viksit Maharashtra 2047” include: (1) ensuring tap water access to all households; (11) expanding irrigation coverage through micro-irrigation and modernization of water storage infrastructure; (11) ensuring equitable water access across river basins to balance water demand and supply; (iv) implementing water recycling and reuse across sectors in Maharashtra; (४) developing financially sustainable water utilities through appropriate pricing mechanisms and digital governance and (v1) promoting inland river navigation for sea- going vessels to facilitate low-cost transportation of agricultural produce.
In order to ensure effective convergence, coordination and oversight of the initiatives envisaged under the Integrated State Water Plan, and to achieve the targets set out in “Viksit Maharashtra 2047”, the Hon’ble Chief Minister has directed the establishment of the State Water Resources Development and Management Committee (SWRDMC) to advise the State Water Resources Council on aligning the Integrated State Water Plan with the objectives of “Viksit Maharashtra 2047.”

Apart from the officers mentioned above, the Chairman of the committee may invite other state-level officers as ‘special invitee’, as required, in order to achieve objectives of the committee.
2. Objectives of the Committee: The objective of SWRDMC is advising on Integrated Water Resources Management on sustainable basis with scientific planning, development and monitoring of all aquifers, watershed, irrigation and drinking water Projects in all river basins in the State.
3. Functions: To achieve above objectives, the committee shall perform following functions:
1. Recommend worthy and appropriate amendments in the integrated state water plan for better and pragmatic management of water resources.
2. Monitoring of implementation of integrated state water plan to ensure optimum harnessing of natural water resources available with the state and its optimal utilization.
3. Recommendation to develop and monitor plans for making rivers perennial/sem1 perennial through watershed management Plan.
4. Recommendation to develop and monitor plans for stand-alone check dams micro storage tanks, barrages and command area development.
5. Recommendation to monitor planning of inter/intra basin water transfer interventions and monitoring implementation to ensure equitable water access across river basins to balance demand and supply of water and to ensure drinking water access from tap for all households
6. Recommendation to develop and Monitor plans for Inland River Navigation for sea going vessels.
7. Recommendation to develop and Monitor plans for development of an Integrated IT Decision Support System (DSS) for all water and land resources management through SWIC and monitoring implementation.
8. Recommendation to to develop and Monitor plans for management of Water Resources with climate resilience viz., water recycle & reuse; river rejuvenation; urban flooding - stormwater management; desalination, wetlands restoration; coastal & salinity management and water tourism etc.
