Final Seniority as on 01/01/2019 of Assistant Engineer, Grade-I (Architecture) Cadre in Water Resources Department
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources,
Government Circular No.-2019/ No.19/ A (Sany)
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 22.04.2019
Government circular:
Department of Water Resources, Government Circular No.-2019/ No. 19/ A (Sany), D. As on 2.2.2019, the provisional seniority as on 01.01.2019 for the post of Assistant Engineer, Grade-I (Architecture) cadre in Water Resources Department has been published.
The objections/ suggestions, if any, in respect of the said temporary seniority were communicated to be submitted to the Government. However, as the Government did not receive any objections/ suggestions in this regard, the provisional seniority list published vide the above circular was issued for the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture). The final seniority list of 1.1.2019 is being published.
