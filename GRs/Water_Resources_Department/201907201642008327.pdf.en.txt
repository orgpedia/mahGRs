The proposal-
The Maharashtra Act, 1996. The Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation has been constituted under the provisions of Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996. Section 4 (1) (a) of the Act provides for the appointment of two non-official members to the post of Vice-Chairperson to be nominated by the State Government in the composition of the Corporation.
The state government has decided to appoint a vice-chairman of the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation (MKKVDC).
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Act, 1996. The Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation has been constituted under the provisions of Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996. As per Section 4 (1) (a) of the Act, Mr. Sanjay (Kaka) Ramchandra Patil, Mr. Chinchani, Pt. Tasgaon and G. Sangli are being appointed as Vice-Chairperson in Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune.
As per Section 4 (2) of the Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996," the term of office of the nominated members shall be one year, unless terminated by the Government. The said Vice-Chairperson has been appointed by the Finance Department vide Government Resolution No. S.A. No. 10.10/ P.S. No. 96/10/ S.A., D. The services provided under 13/03/2012 shall continue to be permitted.
