A total of 48 posts comprising of 30 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai and 18 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Technical Cell (Water Works), Konkan Region, Mumbai are being sanctioned by extension from 01.03.2020 to 28.02.2021 subject to further conditions as indicated in Annexure A.
