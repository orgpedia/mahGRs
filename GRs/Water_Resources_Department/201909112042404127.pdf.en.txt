The Proposal:
The Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation (TAIDC) is constructing a flood protection wall for the Kapileswar Temple at the confluence of the Panjra and Tapi rivers in the village of Mauje Nimb (Kapileswar) in Jalgaon district. MLA Smita Uday Wagh and Shri Kapileshwar Temple Trust, Nimba. He asked the Minister for Water Resources. As per the demand made to the Minister (Water Resources), the government was considering granting administrative approval to the work of the flood protection wall proposal.
Government's decision:
1) Under this Government decision, the Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon has sanctioned Rs. Administrative approval is being granted for the cost of Rs. 2,96,40, 612/- subject to the following conditions:
(a) Government Decision No. Budget-2019/ S. No. 115/2019/ CV (Revenue), D. Action should be taken as per the provision of 31/8/2019.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 21.9.1989 and Government Circular No. FULL- 2018/ (182/2018)/ C.V. (M.) D. As on 03.05.2018, the alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
d) The cost of the work is to be completed within the limit of revised administrative approval from the available grant and within the scheduled time.

e) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, it should be ensured that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
e) After the implementation of the flood protection scheme, it should be ensured in the planning that the drainage of the area will be done in the proper manner.
e) After completion of the project, the flood protection wall will be constructed. Nimba tha. The Amarner G. Jalgaon Gram Panchayat has to take over and carry out future maintenance and repairs. Before starting the work, write a letter. Nimba tha. Get it from Jalgaon Gram Panchayat.
(f) To submit quarterly progress reports to the Government from the date of issue of the work order till the completion of the work. After the completion of the work, the certificate should be provided to the government immediately.
2) The cost of the proposed work shall be covered under the head of I- 5-4711 Capital Expenditure on Flood Control Project, 1 Flood Control, 190, Loans to Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (01) Share Capital Assignments to various Irrigation Development Corporations (01) (02) Share Capital Contributions to Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation (4711 0218) (Programme) and shall be shared from the sanctioned grant.
(3) The administrative approval granted to this work is given in the register of the CV (Revenue) Office No. 2018-2019 has been recorded.
(4) Such Government Decisions are issued under the authority conferred on the Department and with the concurrence of the Inter-Financial Adviser and the Joint Secretary.
