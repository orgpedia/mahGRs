The Proposal:
At present, a total of 2317 machine materials including scrapers, excavators, loaders, backhoes, crawler tractors, dumpers, transporters, tippers, water tankers, diesel tankers, motor graders, boring machines, cranes, diesel road roller generators, pumping sets, welding plants, etc. These works are carried out using various types of plants available in the department for the work done by the organization. For this, a large number of new machinery/ equipment have been procured with the Mechanics Association in the recent past.
New plants have also been purchased from the funds available under the District Planning Plan.
However, it has been brought to the notice of the government that while the machinery of the department is available, the machines are being hired under the Mechanics Association. The government also received complaints in this regard.
Therefore, the following instructions are being issued regarding the use of machinery/ plants of the Mechanics' Association for the works to be carried out by the Mechanics' Association in the future.
Government guidelines
Under the account for the works carried out by the mechanical organization of the Water Resources Department
All equipment, tools, tools, and supplies must be used. No machines should be taken on lease for the work to be done by the mechanical organization.
Even if there is any urgent demand from the architecture department regarding mechanical works, it should be met only with the help of the available machinery with the mechanical organization. Also, no work should be done by taking any plant on lease for work beyond the capacity of the soil work of the plant of the mechanical organization.

Also, if the concerned plant is not available with one board, the plan to make the plant available from the other board should be made at the level of Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
In exceptional/ emergency situations, where the dam/ canal is not safe to be operated using the equipment available under the Mechanized Organization, a detailed report should be submitted to the Government and prior permission of the Government should be obtained.
This order shall come into force from the date of issue of this order.
