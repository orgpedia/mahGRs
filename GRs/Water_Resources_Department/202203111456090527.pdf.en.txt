Introduction: Mogha L.P. Ahmedpur, G. Latur project has been completed in the year 1992.
The storage capacity of the project is 7.892 DLH and the project irrigation potential is 1255 hectare. Based on the Dam Safety Inspection Report 2018, Category 2 errors have been found in the case of Mogha dams. In order to correct these deficiencies and maintain the dam in a safe condition, the Mogha La Pa Lake was constructed. A proposal to give administrative approval to the special repair work of the Ahmedpur, G Latur scheme was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
