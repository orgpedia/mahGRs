Government circular
No new appointment has been made in the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) in Water Resources Department during the period from 01.01.2019 to 31.12.2019. Therefore, the Provisional Additional Seniority List dated 01.01.2020 for the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) has not been prepared.
Considering the objections received after publication of final seniority list dated 01.01.2019, the final seniority list of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) cadre officers of Water Resources Department dated 01.01.2020 has been prepared and is annexed to this circular at Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been used in the preparation of this seniority list.
