Preamble: Considering the water scarcity problem in the state, especially in Nashik and Aurangabad divisions, the decision of the government as a temporary arrangement until the formation of a separate post for the work of the river linking project, the detailed project report of the project is prepared and administrative approval is obtained. As on 10.12.2020, River Link Project Coordination Cell, Nashik has been set up. The project coordination cell has been made operational under the office of the Chief Engineer, North Maharashtra Region, Water Resources Department, Nashik.
