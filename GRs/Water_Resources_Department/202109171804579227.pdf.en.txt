The Government have decided to transfer the sanctioned post of Branch Engineer (Mechanical) in the establishment under the Mechanical Storage Department, Dapodi, Pune Division under the Board of Mechanics, Pune under the Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik to the Sub-Divisional Office of Mechanical Sub-Division, Kedgaon, under the Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar Division, for the work of construction, construction, repair, etc.
