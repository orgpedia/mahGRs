The Proposal:
Mauje Anturli Kh.P.P. under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon Pachora, G. Jalgaon is situated on the bank of a river. There are some houses and religious places near the banks of the river. The Saptashrungi temple side has been eroded due to the flood discharge of the nala. Member of Parliament Shri.
A.T. (Nana) Patil, Jalgaon As per the request made to the Minister (Water Resources) and the Gram Panchayat, Shri. Anturli Kh. P. P., Ta. As per Pachora's demand, the government was considering granting administrative approval to the proposed work of the flood protection wall.
Government's decision:
1) As per the decision of this Government, Mauje Anturli Khu under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon.
P., T. For the construction of flood protection wall near Saptashrungi Temple on the bank of a nala in Pachora, Jalgaon, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being granted for an amount of Rs. 21,19,441/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Four Hundred and Forty One only) subject to the following conditions.
(a) Government Circular No.FDW-1092/ (170)/ CV (Works), d. Action should be taken in accordance with the provisions of 7.8.1992.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 21.9.1989.
(c) Technical approval of the budget should be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.

The cost of the work is to be completed within the limits of revised administrative approval from the available grants and within the scheduled time.
e) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, it should be ensured that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
e) After the implementation of the flood protection scheme, it should be ensured in the planning that the drainage of the area will be done in the proper manner.
e) After completion of the project, the flood protection wall will be constructed. Pachora, G. Jalgaon is to be taken over by the Gram Panchayat for future maintenance and repair. Before starting the work, write a letter. Anturli B.P.P.T. should be obtained from Pachora Gram Panchayat.
(f) To submit quarterly progress reports to the Government from the date of issue of the work order till the completion of the work. After the completion of the work, the certificate should be provided to the government immediately.
