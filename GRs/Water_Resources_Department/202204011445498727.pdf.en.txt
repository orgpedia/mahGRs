Introduction: The Mula Dam was completed in 1972 at Mauje Baragaon Nandur on the Mula River.
Mauje Vanjarwadi and 8 other villages in the flood-affected areas have been shifted to Mauje Baragaon Nandur, Wavrath Jambhali and Kurnwadi. An alternative and nearby road has not yet been provided to this rehabilitated village to come to Ahmednagar.
The Ahmednagar-Manmad road is under the jurisdiction of the Water Resources Department. The total length of the road is 8.10 km. Mula Dam area and class have been declared as tourist areas. The road is frequented by locals and tourists. The villagers have been demanding that the road be repaired as the condition of the road is not good. The project was completed in 2010-11.
This road is important for reaching the dam for flood control and management during the rainy season. Hence, the road should be constructed in a safe manner. Rahuri under Ahmednagar district Manmad Highway to Mula Dam (8.10 km). A proposal to give administrative approval to the project under special amendment was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
