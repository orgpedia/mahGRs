Introduction: The Sribhuvan Minor Irrigation Project is proposed on the Ambika River flowing near Sribhuvan village in Surgana Taluka of Nashik district in the western channel Ambika valley under Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane.
Ambika river basin is the basin number 1 of the Paschim Vahini river and the project is adjacent to the Gujarat state border. The project has a capacity of 2.42 LM in tribal areas of hilly areas. The project will benefit 346 villages in Surgana taluk. The area will benefit from irrigation.
