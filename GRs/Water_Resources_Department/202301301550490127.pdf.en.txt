Introduction: The Sapan River Project is a medium project being constructed on the Sapan River in the Purna sub-basin of the Tapi valley near Wajjar village in Achalpur Taluka of Amravati district in the general area under Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur. The project is aimed at removing the backlog in the irrigation development sector in Amravati division. The project covers 6134 villages in 35 blocks of Achalpur and Chandurbazar taluks of Amravati district. The area will benefit from irrigation. Of these, 5678 are from 33 villages of Achalpur taluka. 456 of 2 villages in the area and Chandurbazar Taluk. area is included.
