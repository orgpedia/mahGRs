The Proposal:
The Main Gate Construction Team under the Mechanical Board, Nashik No. 5. Construction of gates, cleaning of gates, construction of gates and repair of gates of Mula Dam and Canal, Musalwadi, Mula Bhagada Char etc. projects in Godavari basin under sub-division of Nashik, Kedgaon and Ahmednagar. Since the works are closer to Rahuri than Kedgaon headquarters and it is convenient to maintain technical and administrative control, the government was considering to transfer the sub-divisional office from Kedgaon to Rahuri and attach it under the control of the Divisional Office of the Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar and to transfer the Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar under the Mechanical Board, Pune to the Mechanical Board, Nashik for the smooth functioning of the mechanical works under the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik under the Mechanical Board, Nashik. The Sub-Divisional Office of" Dwar Uparwani Sub-Division, Kedgaon" under 5, Nashik is being shifted from Kedgaon to Rahuri as" Mechanical Sub-Division, Rahuri" and attached under the Divisional Office of" Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar."
Further, in order to ensure the smooth functioning of the mechanical works under the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, the control of the Divisional Office of the Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar under the Mechanical Board, Pune is being transferred to the Mechanical Board, Nashik.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: REVIEW-2020/ PR. No.53/ A (Reorganization),
3. Changes in the headquarters and control of the aforesaid divisional and sub-divisional offices are being approved subject to the following conditions:
The Divisional Control of the said Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar, shall be vested in the Mechanical Board, Nashik and the Divisional Control of the Sub-Divisional Offices thereunder shall be vested in the Executive Engineer, Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar.
(2) Save as aforesaid, no change shall be made in the existing structure and approved establishment of the Board of Mechanics, Nashik and the Board of Mechanics, Pune.
3. The transferred Sub Divisional Office shall function at the place of transferred headquarters with original charge and sanctioned posts.
4. The relocated sub-divisional office should be made functional on government land and there should be no separate expenditure on construction. Also, only the material available in the existing functional sub-sector should be used.
5.To ensure that administrative and establishment expenses do not increase, so that there is no financial burden on the government.
The post-transformation structure of the Boards, Divisions and Sub-Divisional Offices, which are undergoing changes as above, shall be as per the Statement-A. Also, the above changes in headquarters and control. With effect from 01.10.2021, the Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik shall take all necessary action immediately.
Government Decision, Finance Department No. ERW-1083/ CR- 611/83/ ADM-9, Dy. As per the provisions of the Act dated 08.03.1984, the Regional and the Circle Office concerned shall take action for declaring the officers as Drawing and Disbursing Officers and Controlling Officers for the respective Divisions and Sub-Divisions under them. A report on the action taken should also be submitted to the government.
