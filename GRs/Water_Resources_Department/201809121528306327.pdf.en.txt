The Maharashtra State Government Act, 1997. Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation was established under the provisions of Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997. As per Section 4 (1) of the said Act, Dr. Sunil Panjabrao Deshmukh, Ashirwad Building, Rukmini Nagar, Amravati has been appointed as Vice-Chairman of the said Corporation vide Government Resolution dated 27/6/2018. Accordingly, Dr. Sunil Panjabrao Deshmukh, Ashirwad Building, Rukmini Nagar, Amravati, Vice President Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur are being given the status of Cabinet Minister.
The Vice-Chairperson was appointed by the Government Decision No. S.A. No. 10.10/ P.S. No.
96/10/ sec. The services provided under 13/03/2012 shall continue to be permitted.
