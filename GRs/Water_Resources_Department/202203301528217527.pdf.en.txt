Introduction: Mula Irrigation Project has two major canals namely Mula Right Bank Canal and Mula Left Bank Canal. The jurisdiction of the original right bank canal is under the control of this department. The original right bank canal is 52 km long and was completed in 1972-73. The projected water carrying capacity from Udhwa is 1650 cusecs (46.72 cumecs). The area includes the districts of Raigad, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Pune, Satara and Solapur. In the original right canal km 0/0 to 15/ 500, the canal lining, various constructions, canal filling have been damaged and the original project has been completed to reduce the transportation cost, leakage, waste during the canal rotation period.
