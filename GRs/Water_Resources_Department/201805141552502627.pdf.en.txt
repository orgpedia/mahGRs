The Proposal:
The establishment was created for the Godavari-Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation under the decision of the Government No. 1 of the Irrigation Department. Three positions have been created. Also, 31 posts have been made available for Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad office from the sanctioned posts under the zonal and 3 sub-divisional offices to be closed under Jayakwadi Project Board, Aurangabad, as per the decision of the Government No. 2. 34 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Corporation were approved to be continued from 01/10/2017 to 31/12/2017 under the Government Resolution No.3. A proposal was submitted to the Government for extension of 34 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad from 01/01/2018 to 30/09/2018 in accordance with No. 4. Re-proposal has been submitted to the Government for extension of 34 posts in the fixed temporary establishment in the office of Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad from 01/01/2018 to 30/09/2018 in accordance with No. 5.
Finance Department No. 6 Under the Government decision here, the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office of the temporary posts included in their profile as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the review earlier but which have not been included in the profile from 1/1/2018 to 28/02/2018. Also Reference No. 7 The Government hereby authorize the extension of time from 1/3/2018 to 30/09/2018.
