A proposal for grant of administrative approval for works costing Rs 1,90,56, 300/- has been received by the Government. A 50-metre long portion of the foundation of the Bara Jyotirlinga temple on the river bank at the confluence of the two rivers has been eroded due to the flood waters that flood the Purna river near Deurwada village. A 150-metre stretch near the Vitthal temple is likely to be submerged by the flood waters of the Purna river. There are people living on the banks of the river and it is necessary to build a safety wall on both sides of the river bed. So, if the Purna river bed is protected, the people living along the river will be protected from flood waters to a great extent, and the damage to houses and agriculture will be avoided and the subsidy to be paid to the concerned in accordance with the damage will be saved. Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur. Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Rate Based Budget of Water Resources Department for the year 2018-19 and Public Works Department for the year 2019-20 with an outlay of Rs. 190.56 Lakhs (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakh Fifty Six Thousand) subject to the following conditions:
(a) The Government shall take action in accordance with the provisions of Decision No. PUSB-2019/ (Q.S. 115/2019)/ SINVYA (M) dated 31/08/2019.
(b) Government Order No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 21.9.1989 and dt. The alignment of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn as per 3/5/2018.
(i) Technical approval of the budget shall be accorded after examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, schedule outlines and carrying gaps at the competent level.
ii) Before technical approval, the concept of retaining wall should be approved at the competent level considering the loft type or counterfort etc. in order to reduce the cost.
d) The cost of the work is to be completed within the limit of administrative approval and within the scheduled time from the available grant. These works will not be given revised administrative approval.
e) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, it should be ensured that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
e) After the implementation of the flood protection scheme, it should be ensured in the planning that the drainage of the area will be done in the proper manner.
After the completion of this scheme, the Sarpanch, Gram Panchayat Deurwada, Ta. Chandur Market, G. Amaravathi is to take over and do future maintenance and repairs. Before giving the technical approval, a written letter will be sent to the Sarpanch, Gram Panchayat Deurwada, Ta. Chandur Bazar, Dist.
The competent authority will be responsible for obtaining technical approval from Amravati.
f) The certificate should be provided to the government immediately after the completion of the work.
