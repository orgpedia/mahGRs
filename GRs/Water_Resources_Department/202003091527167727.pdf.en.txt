Government's decision:
Based on the final results of the Maharashtra Engineering Services Examination-2017, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission had recommended Mr. Vipul Balu Jadhav for the post of Assistant Executive Engineer (Architecture) Group A in the Water Resources Department. Pursuant to the said recommendation, vide Government Resolution, S. No. 26.12.2019, Shri Jadhav was provisionally appointed to the said post subject to prescribed terms and conditions. However, Shri Jadhav vide letter dated 10.02.2020 has informed that he is not able to join the post of appointee. Accordingly, vide Government Resolution, dated 26.12.2019, the temporary appointment of Shri Jadhav as Assistant Executive Engineer (Construction) Group-A is hereby cancelled.
