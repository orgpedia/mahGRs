Introduction: Shelarwadi Minor Irrigation Scheme The earthen dam is being constructed near Shelarwadi village on the Waki tributary of Jagbudi river in Khed district Ratnagiri. The project is being implemented by the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation (KIADC). The total storage capacity of the scheme is 16.234 DLH and irrigation is proposed through a closed tube distribution system at the downstream of the scheme, which will benefit 785 areas in 6 villages of Khed taluka.
