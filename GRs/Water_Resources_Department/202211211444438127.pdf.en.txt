The Proposal:
The office receives applications from various ministers, members of the assembly and other public representatives regarding the naming of dams, reservoirs and sub-irrigation schemes under the Water Resources Department. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to rename the Sangola Upsa Irrigation Scheme in Solapur district as" Late Balasaheb Thackeray Upsa Irrigation Scheme Sangola."
Government's decision:
The Sangola Upsa Irrigation Project in Sangola Taluka of Solapur district is being named as" Late Balasaheb Thackeray Upsa Irrigation Scheme Sangola."
