3. The Chief Engineer concerned is informed that he may, with the consent of the Executive Director, make local arrangements to relieve the concerned officers to attend to their new postings immediately till further orders regarding the assignment of additional charge of the posts vacated due to the transfers of the above officers.
4. The date on which the concerned officer was relieved to appear in place of the new appointment and the date on which he appeared in place of the new appointment should be informed to the government immediately on the e-mail website doesttclass1.wrd@maharashtra. gov. in.
(5) No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned without the prior permission of the Government.
Further, as per Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave application of the transferred officer. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authority at the original address. If the leave application of the transferred officer is sent to the parent office, then these applications will not be taken into consideration.
If any person fails to report to the place of posting immediately in accordance with the said transfer order or attempts to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them considering it as misconduct as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
