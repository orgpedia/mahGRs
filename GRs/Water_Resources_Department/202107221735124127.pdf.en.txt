Introduction: Sangli District is a district of Maharashtra State in west-central India. The river Krishna flows to the east of the village. In 2019, the Krishna river and its tributaries were in spate, causing huge losses to the exchequer.
The river is in spate every monsoon due to heavy rainfall. Sarpanch Mauje Kasegaon and the villagers have demanded the construction of a flood protection wall and a ghat so that roads, temples, cremation grounds, jackwell/ intake well etc. do not get submerged in water in the future. Also in this case. The Minister of Water Resources and Benefit Area Development, Maharashtra State has instructed to take appropriate action. Considering this, it is a matter of concern. The proposal for construction of flood protection wall and ghat on river Krishna at Walwa, Sangli was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
