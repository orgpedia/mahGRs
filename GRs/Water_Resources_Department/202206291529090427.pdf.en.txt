Introduction: The two states of Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh. The Chief Minister, Shri. As per the agreement signed on 16/05/1969, the Inter-State Control Board, Nagpur has been constituted to coordinate the inter-State inter-State inter-State river dams and hydroelectric projects. It has been in operation since July 3, 1971. A total of 20 posts have been sanctioned under reference No. 1 for the said Board and these posts are being extended from time to time. Out of the total 06 posts in the peon cadre, only 03 peon posts are required out of which 02 peons for the post of Secretary and Joint Secretary and 01 peon for other office work. 4 has been reported.
Therefore, the matter of dedicating the remaining 3 posts in the soldier cadre was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved the dedication of 3 posts out of 6 sanctioned posts in the peon cadre out of the total sanctioned 20 posts at the fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Inter-State Control Board, Nagpur.
