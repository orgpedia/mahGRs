Date: 9th October, 2024
Preamble: - The Government had received a request for renaming the dam "Upper Panganga Project Isapur" under the Water Resources Department as "Adi Krantiveer Raja Nobasaji Naik Reservoir."
Accordingly, a proposal to rename the reservoir as "Adya Krantiveera Raja Nobavasaji Naik Reservoir" was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - The dam "Upper Panganga Project Isapur" is being named "Adi Krantiveer Raje Novasaji Naik Reservoir."
The government decision has been posted on the website www.maharashtra.gov.in SUT HAT.
