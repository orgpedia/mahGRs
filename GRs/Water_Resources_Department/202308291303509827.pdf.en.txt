The Proposal:
Flow Turning Scheme, Chimanpada, Ta. Dindori, G. Nashik is one of the projects to divert water from Western Vahini rivers flowing into the Arabian Sea to Godavari basin through a diversion scheme. As per the original approved plan, the catchment area of the project is 0.857 sq km and two drains and two saddles are proposed. It is proposed to divert a total of 15.71 Dalghfu of water from these two saddles for local use and 29.29 Dalghfu of water to Godavari basin. The total water consumption of this diversion scheme is 1.334 Dalghami, which is 111 per cent of the Karanjawan Dam. Irrigation will be restored. The project will help in meeting the water shortage in Godavari basin. Also, Public Interest Litigation No. 173/2013 in this regard. The scheme is one of the 23 liquidated schemes in the High Court judgment. Pursuant to Public Interest Litigation No. 173/2016, Hon. The High Court vide its judgment dated 23/06/2016 has ordered to complete the scheme in one year. Therefore, the project needs to be completed as soon as possible.
