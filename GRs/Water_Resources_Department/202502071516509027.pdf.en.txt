the Water Resources Department
Government Circular No: - Seniority - 9424 / Q. 25 / Aastha (Technical)
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032.
Date: February 7, 2025
Government Circular: - In the Assistant Cadre of Architectural Engineering in the Regional Office under the Water Resources Department.
Date of Employee 1st January, 2019 to 31st December, 2019, 1st January, 2020 to 31st
December, 2020, dated 1st January, 2021 to 31st December, 2021, dated 1st January, 2022 to 31st December,
Formulation of temporary seniority for the period 2022 and from 1st January 2023 to 31st December 2023.
Attachments A, B, C, and F are attached to attachment E for information, respectively.
The Survey Circle Offices are requested to provide the relevant information under the said Circular Circle.
A signed copy of the notice should be placed on record so that it is brought to the notice of the employee immediately.
If there are any actual errors or omissions in this seniority report, the date should be set by the concerned.
Submit a statement to your circle office by 20.02.2025. Objections received after that date
Suggestions that will not be considered should be given to the concerned. Received as of 20.02.2025
The fact-checking of the statement is done by the Circle Offices by examining the service books and other available records of the concerned.
Submit to the government by the date 25.02.2025 with feedback from the zonal office.
