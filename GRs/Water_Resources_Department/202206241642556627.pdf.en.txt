Terms and Conditions:
(i) The said promotions are being given to the officers to be promoted without prejudice to the seniority of the officers in service and whose cases have been kept open for consideration.
(ii) Promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (s) does not mean that the Engineer concerned is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior most officers shall be demoted without any notice, last come first go.
(iii) The said promotion is being granted subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary action is going on or proposed to be taken against the concerned. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental inquiry is ongoing or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
(iv) The said promotion order is purely temporary in nature. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 are being issued as per instructions issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution dated 07.05.2021. This government has taken that decision. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. The promotions will be made subject to the final decision.
(v) Promoted officers. Issue detailed posting orders after verifying submission of last pay and liabilities statement as on 31.03.2021
(vi) Engineers who are denied promotion on the ground that the promotions, though temporary, are regularised over a period of time, shall be debarred from availing the benefits of the Scheme of Assured Progress in Service. If a graduate/ diploma holder cadre engineer refuses promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (VIVA), the report should be submitted to the government immediately. The salary of Assistant Engineer Grade-II/ Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer should not be deducted from the month of July 2022 unless the promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (s) is rejected by an engineer. The Executive Engineer of the concerned department will be responsible for this.
(vii) The Executive Engineer of the concerned department shall be responsible for relieving the promoted engineers immediately after the receipt of detailed posting orders for the post of Deputy Engineer (s). If there is a delay in this regard, the concerned executive engineer will be held responsible. If the promoted officer does not appear in this post within one month, the promotion given to him will be canceled on the assumption that he is not interested in the promotion. The information of the officers who will not appear in the promoted post within the stipulated time should be submitted to the government immediately by the concerned Chief Engineer/ Head of Office.
(viii) Promotions to the post of Deputy Engineer (s), if rejected by the promoted engineers, will be dealt with in accordance with the Government decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.

(ix) The engineers who have been relieved from the concerned department as per the promotion order and have come to join the post of posting on promotion should be recruited by the concerned without any delay. In case of non-recruitment of an engineer leading to compulsory waiting period and payment of salary and allowances, the responsibility of the same shall be fixed on the concerned Executive Engineers/ Establishment Officers.
(x) These promotions are provisional and are subject to the publication of the final seniority of their parent cadre. Therefore, the concerned will not get any rights for seniority in the cadre of Deputy Engineer (s).
