Government Circular: It is proposed to register online performance appraisal reports of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers from the year 2017-18 under the online performance appraisal reporting system, MahaPAR. In this regard, the Primary Custodian and Organization Unit (OU) have been fixed for the office under the Water Resources Department. The government circular had also mentioned the appointment of an additional custodian to prepare the performance appraisal report of Group-A and Group-B (gazetted) cadre officers in each Organisation Unit (OU). Information about the appointed Additional Custodian was also communicated to the government through a letter. However, there is no clarity from the district administration in this regard. For this, an Additional Superintendent of Police has been appointed. The Annexure to the Circular dated 06/02/2018 is being superseded and the revised Annexure shall be as Annexure A annexed to this Circular. The primary custodian of information gave the government. To be notified by 16/04/2018.
The Additional Conservator shall appoint the personnel dealing with the subject of Confidential Reporting in the Establishment Branch as Par Managers for preparing the performance appraisal reports of the officers in the Organization Unit (OU) under his control. (OU) may appoint one or more Par Managers depending upon the number of officers and they may be Class-C employees.
