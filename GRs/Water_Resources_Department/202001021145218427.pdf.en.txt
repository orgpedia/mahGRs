The Proposal:
The works are being carried out under the supervision of Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik, Superintending Engineer, Mechanical Board, Pune, Executive Engineer, Janai Shirsai Upasa Irrigation (Mechanical) Department, Ahmednagar. Therefore, the matter of changing the name of the department according to the changing nature of work was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government's decision:
It is hereby approved to change the name of the Divisional Office of the Executive Engineer, Janai Shirsai Upasa Irrigation (Mechanical) Division, Ahmednagar under the Superintending Engineer, Mechanical Board, Pune under the Regional Office of the Chief Engineer (Mechanical), Water Resources Department, Nashik to Executive Engineer, Mechanical Department, Ahmednagar subject to the following conditions:
There will be no change in the headquarters, functioning and structure of the said Divisional Office.
The change in name will not incur any additional expenditure on the government.
3. Proposals for extension of tenure should be submitted to the Government by making necessary changes in the name of the office.
4. With the change in the name of the office, the new name should again be declared as" Controlling Officer" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
Changes in the name of the office should be implemented from 01/01/2020.
All necessary action regarding the change of name as mentioned above should be taken by the concerned offices.
