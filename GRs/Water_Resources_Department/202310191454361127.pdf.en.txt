The Proposal:
The work of wetland/ Kharaba area clearance is done by four departments- Water Resources Department, Agriculture Department, Soil and Water Conservation Department and Employment Guarantee Scheme Department. All government departments should work in tandem. Accordingly, with the concurrence of the Planning and Finance Department, a Study Group has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Water Resources Department under the Government Resolution dated 25.05.2022 in consultation with all the four departments to finalize the" Char Scheme Parameters and Methodology Policy." It was decided that the Study Group would submit its report to the Additional Chief Secretary, Water Resources Department on 25.08.2022. However, as it was not possible to work within the given time frame for preparing detailed budgets for the proposed parameters as per the model budget, preparing the said budgets and submitting them to the Department of Agriculture, Department of Soil and Water Conservation and Employment Guarantee Scheme Department for feedback and to hold a meeting of the re-study group after determining the parameters for the elimination of saline zone under the Department of Water Resources as soon as the feedback was received from all the concerned departments, the said study group was given an extension of 3 months from the date of 25.02.2023 as per the Government decision dated 17.04.2023. However, the matter of extending the study group further is under the consideration of the government as it will take some time to study the report received from the Department of Agriculture, Department of Soil and Water Conservation and Department of Employment Guarantee Scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To determine the parameters and procedures of the Char Yojana Policy of the State of Maharashtra dated 25.05.2022
