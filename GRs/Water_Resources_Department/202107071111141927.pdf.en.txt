GOVERNMENT Circular No: EPRASA 2020/ (404/2020)/ LAPA dated 08.02.2021
Government Papers-
Various systems of e-governance for the Water Resources Department are developed under the e-Governance Board. According to the circular, a control committee has been formed for the system of" Stream" and" Integrated Irrigation Management System."
As the Committee is concerned with irrigation management activities, the scope of work mentioned in the relevant Government Circular is being implemented for the development of irrigation management activities in the newly developing" Master Database Module" system.
The terms of office, procedure and meetings of the said Committee shall be the same as set out in the said circular.
