Government's decision:
Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad Office Reference No. As proposed under section 7, 74 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Executive Director, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, as shown in the accompanying Annexure-A, are proposed to be filled up vide reference No. 6 In accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments by the decision of the Government of the Finance Department herein, the 01/09/2023 to D. Extension up to 29.02.2024 is allowed subject to the following conditions:
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
(3) The posts shall be sanctioned in accordance with the provisions of this Act and shall not be vacant for a period of more than six months and the posts shall not have expired.
4) Proposals for the next extension of all these posts should be submitted within the prescribed time.
(5) The proposals and the prescribed forms shall be submitted to the Government along with the date of retirement of the posts in the converted temporary establishment.
(1) The expenditure for the payment of salaries for the said posts shall be covered under the following heads and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the same.
Fixed Temporary Establishment: Demand No. I-5, 4701 Capital Expenditure on Major and Medium Irrigation Projects, (80) General, (190) Investment in Public Sector and Other Undertakings, (04) Share Capital Contribution (Salary) to various Irrigation Development Corporations, (04) (05) Share Capital Contribution (Compulsory) to Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation (4701 I 204).
(2) This Government Decision is based on the Government Decision of the Department of Finance, No. 8/ 16/ A.P.C., D. The same is being issued as per the authorization granted to the Administrative Departments under 31.08.2023 and dated 06.09.2023.
