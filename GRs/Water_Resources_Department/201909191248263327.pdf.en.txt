Introduction: Hiranyakeshi (Sarfnala) Medium Project, Ta. The 18.98-litre dam is being constructed at Parpoli village in Kolhapur district. The project is under the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation (MKKVDC). A total of 2644 hectares of area in Kolhapur district will be benefitted by the project.
Hiranyakeshi (Sarfnala) Medium Project, Ta. As per Reference No. 1 of 1996-97, the original administrative sanction for the project was Rs. 33.00 crore based on the tariff list. Thereafter, as per Reference No. 2, the first revised Administrative Sanction was granted for the year 2008-09 based on the Tariff List for an amount of Rs. 145.70 crore.
The Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune has submitted the second revised administrative sanction proposal of Rs. 221.38 crore based on the schedule for 2016-17 to the Government of Maharashtra under reference no. 3. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
With this decision of the government, Hiranyakeshi (Sarfnala) Medium Project, Tt. Revised Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Second Revised Project Report of the project at a cost of Rs. 221.38 crore based on the 2016-17 schedule of the project at Ajra, District Kolhapur. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 208.04 crore is provided for expenditure on works and Rs. 13.34 crore for incidental expenses. Sub-heading wise provisions are being added to Goshwara.
The Second Revised Administrative Sanction granted to the Scheme has been entered in the Register of Medium Project Operations at serial number 8/2019.
The remaining work of the project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the limits of the revised administrative approval.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. SUPRAMA- 2017/648/ (Q. No. 196/17)/ MIDDLE PROJECT 4. The cost incurred on the project under the head MAIN ACCOUNT 4701 MAJOR AND MEDIUM IRINFORMATION- Capital expenditure on this project should be covered under the head MIDDLE IRINFORMATION PROJECT, HIRANYAKESHI (SARPHNALA) MIDDLE IRINFORMATION PROJECT (Under Schemes) and should be met from the grants sanctioned thereunder.
The Second Revised Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions:
5.1) The State Level Technical Advisory Committee Report, Issue No. Observations from 1 to 27 must be met.
5.2. New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
5.3. In-progress tenders should be completed on priority. The priority of the remaining works should be decided and works/ tenders should be processed accordingly so that the water storage is maximized for irrigation.
5.4. transfer the management of irrigation to the Water Utilization Agency by establishing a Water Utilization Agency in the entire benefit area.
5. The said revised administrative approval does not justify regularisation of irregularities committed at various stages of project implementation.
