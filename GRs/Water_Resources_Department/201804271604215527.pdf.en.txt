A confidential report is a very important document regarding the service matters of government officers/ employees. Therefore, important instructions have been issued under the Government Decision, General Administration Department No. CFR-1210/ PR No. 47/2010/ XIII, dated 1st November, 2011 regarding the writing and preservation of confidential reports within the prescribed period. Accordingly, appropriate guidelines have been issued vide Government Circular dated 22nd July, 2013.
The General Administration Department has revised the existing pattern of performance appraisal report to ensure uniformity in the performance appraisal report of all State Government officers/ employees as per the Government Resolution, No: CFR-1211/ Q.No. 257/ XIII dated 7th February, 2018, which has been implemented from the year 2017-18. Similarly, during the review of the posts in the Water Resources Department, the designation/ name of the offices of some officers at the zonal level has been changed. Hence, the list circulated vide circular dated 22nd July, 2013, includes Principal Secretary (J& K) and Secretary (J& K). There is a need to improve the reporting and review of performance appraisal reports of the officers whose confidential reports are to be reported and reviewed. Hence, vide No. 2 The revised orders are being issued by superseding the circular here.
Guidelines are being issued as per Annexure' A' for reporting/ reviewing the annual performance appraisal reports of all officers/ employees within the prescribed time frame, which are to be strictly followed.
