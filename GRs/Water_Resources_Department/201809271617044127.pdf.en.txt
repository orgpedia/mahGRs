(2) Approval to proceed as aforesaid is being granted subject to the following conditions:
(1) The offices of the said Sub-Divisional Office shall be functional in the premises of the Government Building. There should be no extra expenditure on construction.
2) The services of the sanctioned Group-A and Group-B cadre officers of the Sub-Divisional Office to be transferred will continue to function at the headquarters of the Divisional and Sub-Divisional Office to be operationalized by the transfer.
3) Also, the services of the approved Group-C and Group-D cadre employees of the office to be operated in the relocated place should be made available through the local circle office.
4) The inclusion of the currently working employees of Group-C and Group-D cadres under the Sub-Divisional Office to be transferred should be done immediately in the vacant place of work in the office of the concerned Circle Office.
5) The process of handing over the project works and all the office records (judicial, accounting objections, other cases) related to the sub-divisional offices to other departments and sub-divisional offices under the board should be done by the respective region/ mandal office.
6) For offices to be made functional after relocation, only the material available in the existing working office should be used. Administrative and administrative costs should not be increased. This will not put any financial burden on the government.
