Preamble: Reference No. 4 As per the decision of the government here, the contours of the zonal establishment under the Irrigation Department have been decided. Accordingly, 289 posts of Superintending Engineer, Nagpur Irrigation Board, Nagpur and Executive Engineer under him, Lower Wardha Project Division, Wardha and Executive Engineer, Lower Wardha Canals Division, Wardha and Sub-Divisional Scheduled Temporary Establishment and 10 posts of Converted Temporary Establishment have been approved to be continued from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 under Reference No. As per the decision of the Department of Finance in Reference No.3, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier Review but not included in the rolls from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Now, as proposed under Reference No. 2 letter, the Government is approving the continuation of 289 posts on fixed temporary establishment and 7 posts on converted temporary establishment in the aforesaid office from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 as indicated in the accompanying returns 1 and 2.
The above extension is subject to the following conditions:
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
3) These positions will be approved according to the figure.
4) All of the above will not be fulfilled.
(5) Thereafter, the prescribed form shall be submitted to the Government along with the date of the proposal, the prescribed form, the certificate, the names of the posts in the converted temporary establishment and the date of retirement.
