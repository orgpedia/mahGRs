Introduction: - The village of Ambetakali is situated on the banks of the Torna River and has a population of 3500 to 4000 with a total of 500 houses. Of these, 40 to 50 houses are affected every year by the monsoon floods. Therefore, it is convenient to build a flood protection wall along this length. The demand has been made at the field level by two local public representatives from the Gram Panchayat of Khamgaon, District Buldhana and in accordance with this demand, the budget for this work has been submitted to the government after inspection at the field level. The proposal for the construction of the flood protection wall was under the consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government Decision: - Under Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, Mojave Ambetakali, Ta. Khamgaon, G.
For the construction of flood protection wall at Buldhana, Rs. 4,06,97, 512.00 - (Rupees Four Crore Six Lakh Ninety Seven Thousand Five Hundred and Twelve) are being granted administrative sanction subject to the following conditions as per the tariff list of the Water Resources Department and the Public Works Department.
a) The provisions of Government Decision No. PUSH-2019 / (Q. 115/2019) / SINVIA (M) dated 31/08/2019 shall be strictly complied with.
b) Government Circular No. FDW - 1089/243/89 Simvya (Works), D. 21/09/1989 and d. The line of the flood protection wall should be determined only after the flood line is drawn according to the 03/05/2018.
Tenders and other proceedings should not be taken unless a flood line has been drawn.
c) Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans / concepts envisaged in the budget, the externalities of the tariff list and the carrying distance at the competent level. Technical approval proceedings should be carried out only after the conception of the work has been approved from the competent level. If the concept results in a change in the cost or scope of the work, it will be mandatory to get it approved at the competent level.
d) The technical clearance authorities concerned shall be solely responsible for providing technical clearance in the budget excluding short work. The cost of the work is to be met within the stipulated time within the limits of administrative sanction from the available grants. No revised administrative approval will be granted for these works.
f) Land should not be acquired for the construction of the wall, the land required for the construction should be made available free of cost by the concerned, ensuring that the construction of the flood protection wall does not obstruct the flow of the river.
