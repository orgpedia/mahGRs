Government Circular: Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture), Group-B Cadre Officers of Water Resources Department. The provisional seniority list as on 01.01.2019 is annexed herewith as" Annexure-A." The list of such seniority is available in the General Administration Department, Government Circular No: SRV-2016/ PR No.281/ Function-12, No. has been prepared as per instructions dated 13.07.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
All the Heads of Departments of Water Resources Department are hereby informed to bring the seniority list submitted by them to the notice of all the officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture). If there are any objections/ suggestions regarding the seniority list of the officers of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture), they should be submitted to the Government along with the views of the concerned department heads within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular.
In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority list will be published assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority list are correct.
