The Proposal:
Karungli, Ta. The village is situated on the right bank of the river Varuna.
As the flood and water pressure are causing a lot of damage to the nearby houses, village roads, public buildings and government property, a proposal has been received from the field level in response to the demand of the local people's representatives to protect the river bank and embankment on both banks from flood water.
Karungali, Ta. The proposal for the flood protection wall has been submitted to the government after conducting a physical inspection at the proposed site in Shirala, Sangli. The proposal had received the government's approval.
Government Decision: Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune under Mauje Karungali, Ta. For the construction of flood protection wall on the bank of river Warana at Shirla, District Sangli, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Rate Based Budget of Water Resources Department for the year 2022-23 and Public Works Department for the year 2022-23 with an outlay of Rs.67, 12, 673.00/- (Rupees Twelve Thousand Six Hundred and Seventy Three only) subject to the following conditions: (a) The Government may proceed in accordance with the provisions of the Government Resolution, No.
(b) Government Circular No. FDW- 1089/243/89/ Simvya (Works), Dy. 21/09/1989 and dt. As per 03/05/2018, the alignment of flood protection wall should be fixed only after the flood line is drawn. No work should be undertaken without proper planning.
c) The Chief Engineer shall issue the inspection note after inspection and only after that the technical approval shall be granted.
Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining the plans/ concepts envisaged in the budget, externalities of the schedule and the carrying gaps at the competent level. The technical approval should be processed only after the concept of the work is approved from the competent level.
