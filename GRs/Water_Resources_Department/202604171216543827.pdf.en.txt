The third revised administrative approval granted to the project is recorded in the registry of the Middle Project Executive at serial number 01/2026.
The third revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions.
1) The project should be completed within the stipulated time and within the limits of the revised administrative sanction provision.
2) All the points in the Scrutiny Report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee should be met / complied with.
3) While disbursing the land acquisition funds, the concerned Superintending Engineer shall disburse the funds only after thoroughly examining each land acquisition case and ensuring that the amount due is reasonable.
4) The Corporation shall be responsible for not disburse funds or incur any additional expenditure beyond the limits of the administrative sanction granted for the project for any purpose.
5) Necessary statutory and technical approvals for the project should be taken up at the competent level.
6) Revised Administrative Approval means that no technical aspects of the report or field level decision on the tender or field irregularity will be entertained. Such cases are required to be investigated in accordance with the prevailing Government decision at the time and the Corporation shall be solely responsible for the same.
7) The Corporation shall be fully responsible for the strict implementation of the prevailing Government decisions, rules, CVC guidelines, financial authority limits, prescribed tendering procedures while implementing the project, compliance with the directions and deficiencies given by the State Level Technical Advisory Committee in line with the project work as required.
