Preamble: Reference No. 30 to be continued till 28.02.2023 for the posts of Chief Engineer, Gosikhurd Project, Water Resources Department, Nagpur. The decision was approved by 1. Reference No. 3. Under the Government Order of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in their format as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the format.
Government Resolution: Chief Engineer, Gosikhurd Project, Water Resources Department, Nagpur As proposed under Section 2, now 30 posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of Chief Engineer, Gosikhurd Project, Water Resources Department, Nagpur are being extended from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 subject to the following conditions as indicated in Annexure-A.
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
3) These positions will be approved according to the figure.
4) All of the above will not be fulfilled.
(5) The proposals shall be submitted to the Government in the prescribed form, along with the certificate, along with the date of the period for which the certificate is to be submitted.
