The Wadi Waghari scheme is a general area scheme under the Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation and is located in the Waghur sub-basin of the Tapi valley in Jalgaon district. The project is located on the Sur river near Wadi village in Jamner taluka, which falls in the catchment area of the Waghur project. The project proposes to irrigate an area of 690 hectares in Jamner taluka of Jalgaon district through private subsidy at the cost of farmers own money. The storage capacity of the dam is 2.886 tmcft. The total cost of the project is estimated to be Rs 2017-18 crore. 33.59 crore (Rupees Thirty Three Crores Eighty Six Lakhs Only).
