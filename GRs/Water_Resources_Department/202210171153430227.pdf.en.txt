Introduction: Refer No. 1 As per the decision of the government here, the review and demarcation of the zonal establishment under the Water Resources Department has been fixed. Ten posts on fixed temporary establishment in the office of the Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur have been filled up upto 31.08.2022 vide Reference No. The deadline has been extended by the government. All existing temporary posts in all the administrative departments included in the draft as well as those created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the draft are referred to in the Reference No. 4 Under the government decisions here, the power to extend the deadline has been given.
Government Decision: Now Reference No. 10 posts on the fixed temporary establishment in Annexure-A, as proposed by the Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur, w.e.f. 01.09.2022. The extension is being extended till 28.02.2023 subject to the following conditions:
1) All the above-mentioned posts will be in operation during the period proposed by the Department.
(2) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be filled up till the employees are employed.
3) These positions will be approved according to the figure.
4) All of the above will not be fulfilled.
(5) Thereafter, the prescribed form shall be submitted to the Government along with the date of the proposal, the prescribed form, the certificate, the names of the posts in the converted temporary establishment and the date of retirement.
