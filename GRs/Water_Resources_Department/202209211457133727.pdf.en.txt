Preamble: A total of 749 posts on the fixed temporary establishment functioning under the office of the Chief Engineer, Hydrology and Dam Safety, Nashik and a total of 80 posts on the converted temporary establishment under reference No. 1 d. As per the Government Decision dated 17/03/2022, the period has been extended from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022.
Reference No. 3 Department of Finance. 18/08/2022, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the Review earlier but which have not been included in the rolls. The powers to extend the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 have been granted. Accordingly, 749 posts on the fixed temporary establishment under the Chief Engineer, Hydrology and Dam Safety, Nashik and 71 posts on the converted temporary establishment have been sanctioned. The proposal to extend the period from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 was under consideration of the Government.
