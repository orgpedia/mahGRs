Regarding declaration of Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule as Controlling Officer

The Proposal:
There are two CRC codes in existence- (4801 3742) for the Sardar Sarovar Project (architectural and rehabilitation works) and (4801 3769) for the share of the State of Gujarat (on account of Maharashtra). The Executive Engineer, Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar is the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for these two computer labs.
At present, the Controlling Officer of these computer systems is the Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Board, Satara (the name of the Superintending Engineer, Koyna Construction Board, Satara is mentioned as the Controlling Officer in the budget publication). The Government was contemplating to declare 0074-Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule as Controller Officer instead of 0087-Superintending Engineer, Koyna Construction Board, Satara as Controller Officer (CO) only on Budget Distribution System (BDS) by keeping the existing computer data as Drawing and Disbursing Officer to hand over the financial control of the office of Narmada Development Department, Nandurbar to Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
The Superintending Engineer, Dhule Irrigation Project Board, Dhule is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer for all ancillary proceedings in respect of the following headings: Accounts Head 4801 3742 53 (Major Constructions), Sardar Sarovar Project, Accounts Head 4801 3769-53 (Major Constructions) Share of Gujarat State, Sardar Sarovar Project, Accounts Head 4801 3956 (Operation and Administration) Sardar Sarovar Project and 4801 3858-52 (Machinery and Equipment) Sardar Sarovar Project.
This decision was taken by the Government in reference to the Informal Reference No. 379/ 18/ TOSHA-5, dated 12.10.2018 and being issued with the consent of the Government.
