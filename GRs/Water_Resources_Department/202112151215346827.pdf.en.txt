Preamble: Reference No. A Multi Stakeholder Platform (MSP) Steering Board has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary for sustainable and efficient approaches and solutions in water management. The Steering Board is being supported by the Water Resources Group 2030 (WRG-2030), which is assisted by the World Bank. The Multi Stakeholder Platform is expected to guide all aspects related to Water Use Efficiency, Sustainability and Productivity in the benefit sector. For this, the Command Area Work Stream has been established.
The reference No. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, on the recommendation of the MSP, a Project Implementation Unit (PIU) has been set up under the Water Resources Department for a period of three years. The term of this cell has expired on 8/10/2021.
In the meantime, as per the decision of the Government of India, the cell has been re-established and the work related to PIU has been handed over to the Information Management and Training Department, Mumbai under the Water Resources e-Governance Board, Pune.
At present, the work of this cell includes conceptualizing, effective implementation and control of innovative projects in the state, guiding the field offices to implement the projects through social organizations and private participation (PPP), controlling the action from the field level to empower the water use agencies with the help of empaneled agencies and guiding them from time to time. Presently, the various works are in progress through the PIU set up in the wake of the relevant Government decisions and would take about 3 years to complete. Hence, the government decided to extend the deadline. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: 3 The Project Implementation Cell, which has been re-established under the decision of the Government here, has been given a period of three years at present. The deadline has been extended to 31/12/2024.
