Introduction: Hazrat Sayyad Jalaluddin Wali is a pilgrimage site near the Girna river in the Bhagurdi and Abhona Shivratas under the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad. This area is not fully utilised. Because of the Girna river in the north and the annual flood, the water of the river running throughout the year has caused a lot of damage to the land of this pilgrimage area. The rush of devotees in this pilgrimage area is daily, there is a possibility of harming a life along with the pilgrimage area. Therefore, the President, Hazrat Syed Jalaluddin Wali has requested to build a protective wall here. Gram Panchayat, Bhagurdi. Information g.
Nasik has been approved. As per the letter dated 16/11/2018 to the Deputy Superintending Engineer, Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation, Aurangabad, the Government was considering to give administrative approval for the construction of protective wall of Hazrat Syed Jalaluddin Wali Dargah on the bank of Girna River at a cost of Rs. 13.70 lakh.
Government's decision:
