Terms and Conditions: -
1) The promotion is being given to the officers to be promoted without affecting the seniority of the officers who are senior in service and whose cases have been kept open for consideration.
2) Promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) does not mean that the Superintendent concerned is eligible for regular promotion. Also, in future, in case of reduction of posts due to closure of certain divisions / subdivisions or any other reason requiring demotion, the Junior Superintendents shall be demoted without any notice, in accordance with the principle of Last come first go.

3) The promotion is being made subject to the condition that no departmental vigilance / disciplinary proceedings of any kind are ongoing or proposed against the above mentioned Superintendents. However, in the case of any of these superintendents, where a departmental inquiry is ongoing or proposed on the date of this order, these promotion orders will automatically stand quashed.
4) The detailed posting given by the Executive Director / Chief Engineer after his promotion from the Superintending Cadre to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) shall not be changed by the Mutual Service Class or any other change without the approval of the Government.
5) Ma. The High Court of Bombay in Petition No. Against the decision given in the 2797/2015 dated 04.08.2017, the State Government has issued a notification to the effect. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 is entered. The petition is still pending. All the vacancies in the quota of promotion as per the provision of the General Administration Department, Executive 16-B dated 07.05.2021 as per the status of the date 25.05.2004 shall be filled up in the net provisional form of superannuation in accordance with the service superiority. Fillings are being done subject to the decision of the Supreme Court.
6) If the Superintendent promoted to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) fails to appear in the post of promotion within 1 month from the date of this order, the promotion granted to him shall be cancelled on the ground that he is not inclined to the promotion. Also, if the promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B) is denied by the promoted Superintendent, action will be taken against them as per the Government Decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department.
7) The superintendents who refuse the promotion as it becomes regular over time, even if the promotion is made on a temporary basis, shall be informed in writing to the concerned that the benefit of the promotion / promotion scheme assured under the service, if granted, shall be withdrawn.
If a Superintendent refuses promotion to the post of Special Superintendent (Group-B), a report should be submitted to the Government immediately.
8) It shall be the responsibility of the Head of the Office concerned to relieve the Superintendent of Promotion immediately for joining the post of the Promoter. In case of delay, the head of the office concerned will be held responsible. The head of the office concerned should immediately submit to the government the information of the superintendents who will not appear for the post of promotion within the prescribed time.
9) As per the promotion order, the superintendents who have come to join the post of posting on promotion after being relieved from the concerned department should be appointed without delay by the concerned. In case of non-appointment of a Superintendent leading to compulsory waiting period and payment of salary and allowances, the responsibility in this regard shall be fixed on the Head of the Office / Establishment Officer concerned.
