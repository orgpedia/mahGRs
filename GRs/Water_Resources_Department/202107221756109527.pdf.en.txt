The Proposal:
L.Pa Lake Karunde Ta. Malshiras G. Solapur Project was constructed in the year 1971 on the canal flowing near Mauje Karunde village. This project has been transferred from the Works Department to this Department in 1972 for irrigation management. Under the project, the water storage capacity of the dam is 2.20 Dalghami and the useful water storage is 1.97 Dalghami. The length of the project is 2 km. The length of the road is 1.75 km. The length is mm. The project envisages to irrigate 373 hectares of land in two villages of Karunde and Morochi in Malshiras taluka.
The project is built to provide water through a canal for the Rabi season crops and if there is water left in the dam, it is used for the summer season crops.
Under this project, the main canal No. 172m. The bridge was constructed in 1970 and its pillars are made of masonry and trough RCC. For the Rabi season 2009-2008, the discharge from the canal was done on 12.11.2008. While the rotation was in progress, on 21.11.2008 at 6.00 am, pillar no. Irrigation through the canal has been stopped since 2008 due to the complete collapse of the other pillars 1 and 5 and the RCC trough above them.
