The Proposal:
Chief Engineer, Krishna Water Dispute Tribunal, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation for the purpose of Krishna Water Dispute Tribunal. The Water Resources Department was shifted to the Ministry as per the decision of the Government. The day-to-day work of the cell was under the control of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Pune under the above Corporation, Pune and the overall control from the government level was handed over to the Chief Engineer (Krishna Water Dispute Tribunal) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai. With a view to effectively represent the Government of Maharashtra before the Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal and to operationalize the State's competent machinery, Reference No. A special cell has been set up under the Krishna Water Distribution Disputes Tribunal 2.

A special cell was set up under the control of the Superintending Engineer, Konkan Irrigation Board, Ratnagiri under the Executive Director, Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation, Thane to present the claims of the State of Maharashtra regarding irrigation water demand before the Mahadayi Water Dispute Tribunal in a more vivid, coherent, effective and studied manner. As per the Government decision of Reference No. 4, the functions of the Mahadayi Water Disputes Tribunal Special Cell were transferred to the Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal Special Cell subject to certain conditions.
3. The post of Secretary (Project Coordination) in the cadre of Secretary and equivalent Executive Director/ Director General has been created vide Reference No. 5 by elevating the post of Chief Engineer and Joint Secretary (Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal) in order to ensure continuity in effective representation and follow-up of the issues related to the Central Government in the Water Resources Department on various issues of the Water Resources Department, such as environmental clearance, forest proposals, Central Water Commission clearances, Central Financial Assistance from schemes like AIBP, RRR, River Linkage Projects, Water Disputes Tribunal, etc.
4. The overall control of the office of the Superintending Engineer, Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal, Special Cell, Pune, to deal with the court proceedings in respect of Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal-II, has been delegated to the Chief Engineer (JE), Water Resources Department, Pune under the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Basin Development Corporation, Pune, in terms of reference No.6.
Now, most of the work of the Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal has been completed. The Government was considering handing over the administrative and technical control of the office of the Superintending Engineer, Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal, Special Cell, Pune, to the Secretary (Project Coordination) under the control of the Chief Engineer (JE) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai, for effective representation of issues related to the Central Government, continuity in follow-up and proper coordination. The government has taken the following decisions:
The administrative and technical control of the office of the Superintending Engineer, Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal, Special Cell, Pune is being entrusted to the Secretary (Project Coordination), Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai under the Chief Engineer (JE) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
The Superintending Engineer, Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal Special Cell shall have overall control of all the activities of the Chief Engineer (G) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Ministry of Water Resources, Mumbai. The final responsibility of presenting the State Government's case before the Tribunal will be of the Chief Engineer (JE) and Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
