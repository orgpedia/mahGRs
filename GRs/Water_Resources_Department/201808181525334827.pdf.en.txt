Preamble: Three Minor Irrigation Schemes, Tt. Mandangad, G. Ratnagiri, Government Resolution No. Lapayo 2005/1094/ (348/2005)/ Kharbhoomi, dated 19/09/2005 amounting to Rs. Administrative approval was granted at a cost of Rs. 21.37 crore. While preparing this proposal for administrative approval, the rates as per the 2003-04 tariff schedule were used. In the interim period, the cost of the scheme has been increasing due to price differential, increase in land acquisition cost, change in the concept, etc., which necessitates grant of revised administrative approval to the scheme.
Government Decision: Under the said Government Decision, three Minor Irrigation Schemes, Tt. Mandangad, G. Ratnagiri, First Revised Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the total estimated cost of the scheme at Rs. 93.95 crore (Rupees Ninety Three Integer Ninety Five Percent only). The total outlay for this work is Rs. 85.62 crore (Rupees Eighty Five Eight Sixty Two only) and the ancillary expenditure is Rs. 8.33 crore (Rupees Eight Eight Thirty Three only). For the remaining works in the budget, the rates have been used as per the schedule for the year 2013-14. The sub-heading provisions are annexed to the Annexure.
The First Amendment Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions: (i) The First Amendment Administrative Sanction means that the approval cannot be taken for irregularities at various stages of project implementation. If any irregularity has been noticed in the work before or after the issue of this first S.P.M. It cannot be assumed that by providing Q. M. it has become regular.
Proceedings of the ongoing or proposed departmental inquiry into the scheme should be continued.
The decision to grant the first revised administrative sanction shall not prejudice the proceedings of the departmental enquiry, whether ongoing or arising in future.
