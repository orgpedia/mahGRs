(e) The Executive Engineer of the concerned Department shall be responsible for the immediate retrenchment of the promoted engineers after the order of posting to the post of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction) is issued. In case of delay, the Executive Engineer concerned will be held responsible. The information of the officers who will not appear for the promotion post within the prescribed time should be immediately submitted to the government by the Executive Director / Chief Engineer / Superintending Engineer concerned.
(5) Engineers who have been relieved from the department concerned in accordance with the promotion order and come to join the position of posting on promotion shall be engaged without delay by the concerned. In the event of non-recruitment of an engineer leading to compulsory waiting period and payment of salary and allowances, the responsibility will be fixed on the Executive Engineer / Establishment Officer concerned.
