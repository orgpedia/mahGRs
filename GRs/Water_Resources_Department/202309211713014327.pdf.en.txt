Preamble: Shahbaz Kharbhoomi Scheme. Raigad is a part of the Raigad District Master Plan AK 39 page number 63 and the original work of this scheme was completed in 1953 by the Kharland Board. The renovation work of the project is being carried out under EEC Phase No. It was completed in 1991.
The scheme is governed by the Central Forest and Environment Coastal Regulation Act, 1991.
Since the dam is on the bank of the main Dharamtar creek, the dam has been damaged due to heavy inflow of flood water from the creek during the monsoon season and also due to the usual tidal surge. There are potholes everywhere. Due to heavy rains, water inundated the fields and caused damage to crops. Local villagers and public representatives are demanding renovation work to prevent damage to the structure, and the scheme has been named Shahbaz Kharbhoomi Yojana. As the plan is not working, it needs to be updated.
The Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Konkan Region, Mumbai has received a letter from the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Mumbai. The proposal to give administrative approval to the renovation work of Alibag G. Raigad Kharbhoomi Scheme was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
The government's decision The administrative approval is being accorded for the renovation of Alibag G. Raigad Project at an estimated cost of Rs. 14,23,96, 641/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Twenty Three Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Six Hundred and Forty One only) based on Kharbhoomi List 2022-2023 and Water Resources List 2022-2023. (3) The Government Decision, Water Resources Department dated 22.06.2015 vide Para No. As per the provisions of section 2 (1) and section 2 (2), before starting the construction of the Kharbhoomi scheme, the beneficiaries should form a cooperative society and to complete the Kharbhoomi scheme, the beneficiaries of the reclaimed area should be charged Rs.500/- per hectare as participation in the project (minimum Rs.200/- in cash and Rs.300/- in the form of wages or cash).
