Final Seniority as on 01.01.2020 for Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A cadre in Water Resources Department.
The Government of Maharashtra
The Department of Water Resources
Government Circular No: SJEE-2020/ PR No.26/ A (Sany),
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: February 15, 2020
Government circular
No fresh appointment has been made in the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A in the Water Resources Department during the period from 01.01.2019 to 31.12.2019. Therefore, the Provisional Additional Seniority List dated 01.01.2020 for the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A has not been prepared.
After considering the objections received after publication of final seniority list dated 01.01.2019, the final seniority list of officers of Grade-I (Architecture), Group-A cadre of Assistant Engineer, Water Resources Department, dated 01.01.2020 has been prepared and is annexed to this circular at Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been adopted in the preparation of this seniority list.
