The Proposal:
Under the small-scale scheme. In Lanja, District Ratnagiri, a earthen dam with a capacity of 5.5572 LMT has been constructed on a local nullah. The project is located in the" Rest of Maharashtra" region under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation. A total of 255 villages of 2 villages of Lanja taluka have been covered under this project. It is planned to provide irrigation benefits to the area through open canal and closed tube well.
