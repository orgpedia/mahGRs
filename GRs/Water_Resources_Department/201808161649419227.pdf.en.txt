Committee's mandate:
(1) Tourism under the Department of Water Resources and other important tourist attractions which can be developed
Listing of dams/ rest houses with priority and details.
(2) Drafting of policies of Water Resources Department for development and management of dams and reservoirs by private agencies on Build-Use-Transfer (BOT) basis.
(3) The type of contract to be entered into with the private developer for developing and managing the dam sites/ rest houses on Build-Use-Transfer (BOT) basis and the terms and conditions of the contract.
Preparation of draft agreement.
Committees and meetings.
The provisions of the various Corporations Acts of the Water Resources Department, the provisions of the Maharashtra Tourism Policy, 2016 of the State Tourism Department, the previous Government decisions on the subject, circulars, matters related to the safety of hydroelectric projects of dams, guidelines on boating and ancillary matters
Study the issues and submit the draft policy to the government.
Officials of the Tourism Department, Public Works Department, Home Department, Revenue and Forest Department should be invited as per requirement.
Term of the committee
The committee will have 30 days to submit its report to the government.
All the corporations are directed to provide the information sought by the said committee immediately.
