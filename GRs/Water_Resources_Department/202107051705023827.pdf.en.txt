INTRODUCTION The 110 km long project commencing from Nandur Madhyameshwara Unnayi Dam on Godavari River, 75 km from Darana Dam, will be completed in two phases. Godavari Right Canal of length 90 km. The Godavari Left Canal was built between 1911 and 1916 under British rule. The Godavari Right and Left Canal irrigates a total area of 33170 hectares in the talukas of Sinnar, Niphad and Yeola in Nashik district, Kopargaon, Rahata and Shrirampur in Ahmednagar district and Vaijapur in Aurangabad district.
Keeping in view the water supply to important towns and Gram Panchayats, water supply to sugar mills and industrial areas, etc., in order to reduce the carrying cost on Godavari canals, it is necessary to increase the carrying capacity by reducing the rotation period of the canal and strengthening/ repairing the canals as the old structures have started leaking due to about 100 years.
