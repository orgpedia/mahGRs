Government's decision
Based on the result of the Combined Competitive Examination held by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai for the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) Group-A (Gazetted) under the Maharashtra Engineering (Mechanical) Services Main Examination 2013 through direct recruitment, 26 candidates who have qualified for appointment out of 28 candidates recommended vide letter dated 19.05.2015, have been appointed as Assistant Engineers in the above mentioned No. As per the decision of the government, Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) has been appointed to the post of Group-A. As per the letter dated 27.04.2016 of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai, 5 candidates have been recommended for the post of Deputy Engineer (Mechanical) as per the merit from the waiting list in the said examination.
