Government Circular: The configuration of the field offices as per the functions under the Water Resources Department has been decided by the decision of the Government in question. Accordingly, a total number of 45,297 posts have been sanctioned for the region/ mandal/ division/ sub-division and branch offices. However, in the meantime, the concerned establishment offices of the department have revised the figure of field offices from time to time, which has created many difficulties in matching the sanctioned strength of 45,297 posts of the department. Accordingly, the following instructions are being issued in view of the approval of the revised configuration of the Regional Offices.
All the offices of the Water Resources Department are informed that the cases related to the following subjects related to the field offices of the Water Resources Department should not be submitted to each other by the respective establishment offices. Such cases should be submitted through the Establishment (Restructuring) Task Force.
(i) Cases of modification of the layout of the Regional Offices/ Establishments.
(2) the creation of new cases.
3) Change of name.
4) Cases of cadre consolidation.
(5) cases relating to elevation of posts.
6) cases of dismissal.
7) Relocation of offices/ closure of offices.
8) Construction or conversion of offices into irrigation works.
9) Cases of rotational availability of posts.
10) Matters relating to change in administrative/ technical control of offices.
(3) All matters relating to change in sanctioned posts or configuration of field offices under the Water Resources Department should be transferred to the Establishment (Re-organization) Department immediately.
