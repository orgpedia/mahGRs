Introduction: Anjani Medium Project is constructed on river Anjani near Palasdal village in Erandol Taluka of Jalgaon district under Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon.
The project is located in the Girna sub-basin of the Tapi valley. With this project, the total number of Arandol and Dharangaon taluks in Jalgaon district is 2831. The area will benefit from irrigation.
As per Reference No. 2 of 1991-92 to Anjani Medium Irrigation Project, based on the tariff of Rs. 17.96 crore (Seventeen crore wise target only) was given as fresh original administrative sanction. Under Reference No. 3, the Anjani Medium Irrigation Project has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs. 48.92 crore (forty eight crore only) has received the first revised administrative approval. Under Reference No. 4, the Anjani Medium Irrigation Project has been sanctioned for the year 2002-03 at a cost of Rs. 85.21 crore (Rupees Eighty Five Crores Twenty One Lakh only) has received Second Revised Administrative Sanction.
The Executive Director, Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation, Jalgaon has submitted the third revised administrative sanction proposal of Rs. 231.82 crore based on the price list of 2017-18 to the Government under Reference No.5. Profitability ratio of the project is 1.76.
As per the above, the proposed cost of 3rd Suprama Budget has increased by Rs. 146.62 crore over the approved 2nd Suprama Cost, registering a growth rate of 172.05 per cent.

5.2 The Corporation shall ensure that all the points in the Scrutiny Report of the State Advisory Committee are satisfied.
5.3 The Corporation shall be solely responsible for not disburse funds or incur any additional expenditure beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for any purpose for the said project. In this regard, the existing government decisions, rules, guidelines of CVC, financial powers, prescribed tender procedures must be strictly implemented and the responsibility for this will be entirely of the corporation.
5.4 The Government shall not be deemed to have given its consent to any financial and procedural irregularities, any illegalities or any other matters which may lead to financial misappropriation in the earlier period in respect of the said project and no such consent/ approval has been given by the Government in respect of the said project.
5.5 Revised Administrative Approval means that the approval of the technical aspects of the report or the decision at the field level regarding the tender or the field irregularities that have occurred will not be taken into account.
5.6 New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
5.7 The work of tenders in progress should be completed on priority. The priority order of the remaining works should be decided and works/ tenders should be processed accordingly so that the water storage is maximized for irrigation.
(6) The decision of the Government shall be effective from the date of issue.
