Introduction: The Shankarraoji Chavan Vishnupuri Project is under construction on the Godavari River in Nanded Taluka of Nanded district, in the general area under the Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation.
Part-I of the project comprises of Vishnupuri Barrage and Canal and 4 lift irrigation schemes on the canal. Part-II includes 13 dams on Godavari river. The project will irrigate 29,342 hectares in Nanded district, 11,572 hectares in Parbhani district, 11,136 hectares in Jalna district and 1,213 hectares in Aurangabad district. The project will benefit agriculture, irrigation, drinking water supply, industrial water use and tourism development.
The scheme was originally approved by the Government vide Government Resolution No. PIM/ 9576/ 677 (727)/ JSA-1, D. 25.05.1979 as against Rs. 32.24 crore, vide First S.P.M.Government Decision No. Nigopr 1084/2911/84 Mopr-2, Dy.
