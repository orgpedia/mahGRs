The transferred Deputy Engineers should not be posted and their detailed proposal should be submitted to the Government afresh for approval.
Arrangements should be made to send the confidential report, service book, and final salary certificate of the transferred officer to the new office immediately.
(5) No leave shall be granted to the officer concerned on any ground except with the previous sanction of the Government, except in accordance with Rule 30C of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981, where an application is received from the parent office that no leave application of the transferred officer shall be accepted.
The official should return the registration receipt to the original address by post. If the transferred officer sends the leave application to the parent office, the application will not be taken into consideration.
(6) While appointing the officer transferred by this order, the concerned Administrative Officer/ Executive Engineer shall verify whether he was on leave earlier or how, and if found to be on leave, shall take action in accordance with Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 before taking him on leave and submit a report to the Government.
