Introduction —
The Tapi River Basin was selected for developing a River Basin Management (RBM) Plan under Indo European Water Partnership (IEWP). For this, DoWR, RD & GR; Ministry of Jal Shakti constituted Tapi River Basin Committee having representations from CWC, CGWB, Tapi River Basin States (Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat) and the IEWP/GIZ Project Management Unit to support the technical work towards the Tapi RBM Plan.
As part of the collaborative efforts the Tapi RBM Plan was jointly developed with the active participation of the Tapi Basin States Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, and Gujarat using the principles of the European Union (EU) Water Framework Directive.
The Plan was subsequently adopted by the Tapi River Basin Committee during Phase-2 of the IEWP Action, with the recommendation that, as water is a State subject in India and the implementation of measures recommended in the Tapi RBM Plan 2023 has to be undertaken by the State Governments.
2. Building on this shared foundation, during the first meeting of the Joint Nodal Officers of the India-EU Water Initiative (IEWI) and EU Delegates, held on 17 January 2025 at CWC, New Delhi, it was opined that strong state-level coordination is required to consolidate the Action Plan for TEWI Phase-3 and to ensure the successful implementation of the Tapi RBM Plan.
3. The activities outlined as per the Action Plan include conducting dissemination workshops for Tapi River Basin Management Plan across Tapi Basin States, followed by stakeholder consultations with State Level Committees to prioritize the Programme of Measures (PoMs). Coordination with relevant districts, field missions, institutions in the solution forum and technical workshops will facilitate the implementation process.
4. Additionally, Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) will be prepared for the three prioritized PoMs out of 1) River Basin Management, 11) Reuse of Treated Wastewater, 111) Capacity Building activities, 1०) Urban Hydrology, and v) Impact of climate change one for each state or as per the interest from the states, with one DPR specifically addressing climate change impact management.
5. Under IEWI Phase-3, technical support will be extended to these State-Level Committees to develop Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for three prioritized Programmes of Measures (PoMs) identified in the RBM Plan. This will further strengthen state-level ownership and enable progress on ground-level implementation in alignment with the

Terms of Reference of committee:-
a. Conducting dissemination workshops for Tapi River Basin Management Plan across Tapi Basin States, stakeholder consultations with State Level Committees to prioritize the Programme of Measures (PoMs). Coordination relevant districts, field missions, institutions in the solution forum and technical workshops.
b. For each of the prioritized measures above and identified by the State, IEWI will provide technical support for the preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs).
c. The State-Level Committee shall perform the following key functions 1. Supervision: Oversee the preparation of DPRs for the three prioritized measures. These DPRs will be developed under the guidance of the Committee, with technical assistance from experts engaged by IEWI.
il. Steering: Provide strategic direction for the phased implementation of the prioritized measures from the Tapi RBM Plan (2023), ensuring alignment with both state-specific priorities and broader basin-level objectives.
11. Stakeholder Engagement and Coordination: Facilitate the active involvement of relevant stakeholders - including line departments, district authorities, and institutional actors. The Committee will ensure effective inter-agency and inter-governmental coordination for the smooth preparation and eventual implementation of the DPRs.
