Preamble: Shankarraoji Chavan Vishnupuri Project under reference No. The Fifth Revised Administrative Sanction has been accorded at a cost of Rs. 2982.243 crore subject to certain conditions as per the decision of the Government. Kivala storage pond is included under Vishnupuri project. In the revised administrative approval, the government decision regarding Kivala storage pond, condition no. 7 The following is as follows:- Since the Kivala storage pond near Nanded city, which is included in the project, is expected to be used for the drinking water of the city/ industrial estate of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, the concerned department will be required to bear the entire cost of the storage pond. There should not be any expenditure on the Kivala storage pond from the funds of the Water Resources Department.
The Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation has decided to cancel the condition and construct the Kivala storage tank through the Water Resources Department. 2 A proposal has been received through a letter here in which it has been mentioned that the work of Kivala storage pond should be done from the funds of the Water Resources Department. Considering this, the government was considering revoking the condition. To that Ma.
