The Proposal:
Sangli is situated on the left bank of the river Krishna. Sangli is a city on the banks of the Krishna river. Floods in 2005, 2006 and 2019 have caused extensive erosion of the riverbed and damage to agriculture and other structures along the river. The residents of the area have been demanding construction of a retaining wall to protect the property. Shri Jayant Patil, Minister, Water Resources Department, Government of Maharashtra, during his field visit on 26/01/2020, has instructed to submit the budget for the construction of the ghat with flood protection wall at the said place. Sangli is situated on the left bank of the river Krishna. The location of the work demanded is at the bottom, adjacent to the institutional government wharf.
