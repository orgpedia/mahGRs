Government order:
The Deputy Engineer (STA) cadre of Water Resources Department vide Government Resolution No. Establishment 2016/ S.No.88 (Part-9) Water-2D. He was working in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as per the provision of Para 8 (iv) of 31/05/2017. The services of these Deputy Engineers have been transferred from the Soil and Water Conservation Department to the Water Resources Department under the above Government Order. However, these sub-engineers need to be given postings.
(2) An engineer who has been transferred from the Soil and Water Conservation Department to the Water Resources Department shall be deemed to have been transferred in accordance with the provisions of section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005. The post is being posted in the post shown in 4.
