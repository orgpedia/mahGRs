The proposal: The National Green Tribunal (NGT), Western Zone, Pune Bench, in Petition No. 25/2014 filed by Mr. Sarang Yadavadkar and others. In the above petition, vide order dated 3/2/2016, a total of 1188 Kms. I have been directed to plan the flood line before 31/3/2017. Due to the failure of the flood plan in the state, The National Green Tribunal had given a deadline of 7/9/2018 for completion of the floodplain planning process. The court has taken a stand on it. In this regard, all the executive directors (all irrigation corporations) have been asked to take immediate action to map the flood-prone areas, draw flood lines and publish these maps on the website within the stipulated time. Accordingly, the Executive Director, Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune, vide letter dated 22.01.2020, submitted that the matter of granting administrative approval for the work of fixing the flood line of Koyna, Venna, Vasna, Urmodi, Tarali, Kolambi and Mand and their tributaries in the Krishna Valley in Satara district and the drains receiving them was under consideration of the Government.
