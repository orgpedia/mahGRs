Preamble: Participation of Beneficiaries in Irrigation Management for Successful Implementation of Water Utilization Agencies
An organization must always be self-sustaining and efficient. To promote water use organizations, to create competition in the management of water use organizations.
It has been decided to organize Mahatma Phule Pani Utpadan Sanstha Abhiyan every year and Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Pani Utpadan Sanstha Management Award Scheme under it as per the decision of the Government dated 21st August, 2009.
However, since it was not possible to evaluate the award competition in one year and submit the nomination proposal to the government, the government was considering holding the award competition biennially instead of annually. The following decisions have been taken:
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to confer the Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Water Utilization Society Management Award biennially (Two Yearly) under the Mahatma Phule Water Utilization Society Mission and its activities. Henceforth, the government will decide the nominees for this award competition scheme. 22/05/2017 and Govt. Bi-annual (Two Yearly) period from 2019, considering 23/10/2020
Be organized with this in mind.
