Introduction: - Bindigahal Latur style dam is on Manjra river near Bindighal village in Latur district.
The barrage is located at a distance of 95.13 km from the Manjra dam site. The total projected storage of the barrage is 1.35 gallons which is 208 ft. There is an irrigated area. The Bindigahal Latur Barrage was completed in 2019 and first commissioned by the Construction Department in 2011. Later in the year 2019
The project has been transferred to the Management Department for maintenance and gate management.
Since the completion of the construction of the project, the work related to the repair of the passages in the dam has not been done. The Chief Engineer (Mechanics), Water Resources Department, Nashik.
On 12/9/2023, he visited various embankments / barrages on Manjra river and certified the need for repair of various mechanical and electrical components and gave some suggestions in this regard, including the proposed missing embankment. The mechanical and electrical components of the missing dam
The Executive Director has directed that these works be proposed under the Special Amendment as the required funds required to carry out the proposed works are not available at the corporation level. The leakage of water from the gate will be stopped once the repair work of the mechanical and electrical components of the dam is completed. Also, the project proposed water storage will be available for irrigation during Rabi and Summer seasons at full capacity. The repair will also help in smooth operation of the gate and thus maintain the safety of the dam during flood control.
