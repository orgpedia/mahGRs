The Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune has decided to implement the scheme in Bhudargad, Kolhapur District. 299/ (462/1999)/ Rs. The original administrative sanction was granted at a cost of Rs. 1293.24 lakhs (Schedule Year 1999-2000). The revised administrative approval is required to be accorded to the project in the first instance as the project is undergoing an increase due to difference in tariff, increase in ownership charges and price, increase in acceptance of tenders at higher rates, increase in land acquisition cost and increase in incidental cost and other factors. A decision in this regard is being taken.

The estimated cost is given in the Annexure A attached with the Goshwara.
The First Amended Administrative Sanction is granted subject to the following conditions:
2.1) The top tasks of the plan should be completed first and then the rest of the tasks should be taken up. New factors that create financial liabilities should not be included.
2.2 The works of the scheme should be completed by June 2021.
2.3) If there is no government approval for spending more than the original administrative cost, then action should be taken to regulate the expenditure by first fixing the responsibility by making a disclosure accordingly.
2.4 No additional expenditure or disbursement of funds shall be made for any purpose beyond the limit of administrative sanction granted for the purpose.
