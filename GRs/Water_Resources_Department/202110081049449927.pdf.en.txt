The Proposal:
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater effectiveness in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has taken a number of steps, including: It has been directed to adopt the revised procedures under the memorandum dated March 23, 2019. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Regulation Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, a single nodal agency (SNA) is required to be appointed for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and a separate single bank account is required to be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as the nodal officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the centrally sponsored schemes of the Water Resources Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decisions: The Government Decisions dated 15 th July, 2021 and 05 th August, 2021 regarding appointment of Single Nodal Agency and Nodal Officer for Fund Distribution and Regulation Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes are being superseded by this Government Decision.
