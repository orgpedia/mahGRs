The Proposal: -
There is a Lingayat cemetery on the banks of the Krishna River near Sangli Haripur Road near Sangli city. There is also a civic settlement adjacent to the cemetery. At this place, the drain adjacent to the cemetery merges with the Krishna River, during the monsoon season, the city's rainwater is fed into the riverbed by this drain, and during the floods, the water of the Krishna River enters the urban settlements through this drain. In the floods of 2019 and 2021, the riverbank and drain near the cemetery have collapsed. As a result, graveyard land and civilian settlements are threatened. Local people's representatives had demanded the government to take up flood protection work at the site. According to Lingayat Crematorium, Jabal Ta Miraj, Dist. The budget was presented to the government by field officers after a physical inspection at the site of the construction of a flood protection wall on the river in Sangli. The issue of administrative approval of the said budget was under consideration of the Government.
