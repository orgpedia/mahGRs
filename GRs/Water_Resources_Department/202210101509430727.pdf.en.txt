Government's decision:
Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) and Baliraja Jal Sanjeevi Yojana (BJSY) under Water Resources Department
Problems faced in land acquisition, rehabilitation, forest land and other aspects in these Centrally Sponsored Projects.
A special project control room has been set up to ensure timely execution of the project.

Apart from the above members, the following officers will attend the meetings of the Special Project Control Room as special invitees.
1.Cr. Vidyut Varkhedkar, Ma. Deputy Secretary to Deputy Chief Minister 2. Mr. Kaustubh Dhavse, Hon. Officer on Special Duty to the Deputy Chief Minister
The head of the field offices concerned with the issues to be discussed and decided in the meeting will be invited to the meeting, if necessary.
Decisions of the Special Project Control Room shall be issued in the form of a circular and shall be subject to the orders of the Government and shall be acted upon by the concerned offices/ corporations of the Department within the prescribed time limit.
The concerned offices of the department will be responsible for issuing government decisions/ orders, if required, in accordance with the history, etc. issued from the Special Project Control Room.
The Special Project Control Room will be operated by the MOPR 2 functionary of the Water Resources Department.
