Date is April 2, 2019.
Read: Government Decision Revenue and Forest Department No. 2016/ P. No. 80-Part-2/ F-11, D. July 25, 2018.
Preamble: The Department of Revenue and Forestry, with the concurrence of the Department of Planning and Finance, has decided to make financial provision for" Tree Plantation and Maintenance" within the limit of 0.5% of the total budgetary provision of all the administrative departments. The decision was taken on July 25, 2018. Accordingly, the government was considering opening a new account title for the" Tree Plantation and Cultivation" programme of the Water Resources Department.
Government's decision:
Under this decision, the following new heads of accounts are being approved for making financial provisions within the limit of 0.5% of the expenditure on the" Tree Plantation and Cultivation" programme under the Water Resources Department.
