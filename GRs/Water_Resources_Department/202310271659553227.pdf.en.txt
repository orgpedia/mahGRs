Government's decision:
Urdhwa Penganga Project (based on Water Resources Department's 2022-23 tariff schedule) Rs. The Sixth Revised Administrative Sanction proposal for Rs. 7654.63 crore is being approved subject to the following conditions: The sub-heading of the project is as Annexure A.
1. The proposal is being approved subject to the condition that the issues raised by the Planning Department and the Finance Department are met and compliance is submitted.
2. Paragraph No. 1 of the Scrutiny Report of the State Level Technical Advisory Committee. Comply with all the points mentioned under sections 26 and 27.
3. Technical approval should be obtained for the cost involved in the 3rd S.P.M.Proposal of the project.
4. The existing Government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial limits, prescribed tendering procedures should be strictly implemented while implementing the project.
The report of the Technical Advisory Committee should be taken into account and accordingly the order of priority of investment on the construction of the project components should be determined and work should be done accordingly.
The decision of the revised administrative sanction will not affect the ongoing or proposed inquiries in respect of pending public interest litigations in the High Court as well as the proceedings to be conducted by the Special Investigation Committee.
If any irregularity is found in the implementation of the project, the responsibility will be on the concerned field officers.
8. Component-wise completion reports of the completed component works should be prepared and handed over to the management immediately.
9. The revised administrative approval means that the approval cannot be taken into account for irregularities at various stages of project implementation.
10.P. Appropriate action should be taken to use the option of irrigation through closed conduits in the areas where the works of distributaries/ minor canals have not been taken up on the project.
11.Sh. Technical Audit as ordered vide letter dated 09.03.2017.
Responsibility should be fixed as per the requirement after the receipt of the report of the technical audit.
Revised administrative approval shall not take into account the technicalities of the report or the regional level decision on the tender or the regional irregularities that have occurred.
Technical and tender matters must be examined in accordance with the government decision in force at the time and the corporation will be fully responsible for this.
13.Sadar Water Utilization Agency of the beneficiaries should be established on the project.
The expenditure on the project works should be included in the head of the main account head 4701 Capital expenditure on large and medium projects (80), general (190), investment in public sector and other enterprises (02) (05) Part capital contribution to Godavari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation (4701H899) and should be met from the grant approved by the government.
