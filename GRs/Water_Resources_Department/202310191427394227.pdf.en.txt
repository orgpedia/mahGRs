GOVERNMENT DECISION: Now, as proposed under Reference No. 4 and as indicated in Annexure-I attached, 36 posts of Superintending Engineer, Kolhapur Irrigation Board, Executive Engineer under Kolhapur, will be filled up on the fixed temporary establishment in the office. 01/09/2023 to D. The extension is being extended till 29/02/2024 subject to the following conditions:
(1) The posts in the converted temporary establishment shall continue to be held by the said employee till the time he is employed.
2) The above posts will be in service during the period proposed by the Department.
(3) The temporary posts for which extension is to be granted shall be in the prescribed format and shall not be vacant/ lapsed for a period of more than six months.
4) The number of posts/ reduction in posts/ increase in posts/ retirement etc. in the proposal made available by the Office of the Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Pune.
5) The proposal for further extension of all these posts should be submitted in the prescribed form along with the certificate without any delay.
