The Proposal:
A Committee has been constituted under the above said Government decision to suggest technical measures to fully utilize the water sanctioned as per the First Tribunal of the State's share in the Krishna basin. The term of the committee. expired on 28/10/2021. Also, considering the nature of the information to be collected in accordance with the work of the committee, the time frame and other aspects of the relevant government decision, as well as the time required to submit feedback in accordance with the report, the government was considering extending the term of the committee. Accordingly, the following document is issued.
Cleaning paper:
The term of the said Committee is being extended upto 31.03.2022.
All other terms and conditions shall remain the same.
