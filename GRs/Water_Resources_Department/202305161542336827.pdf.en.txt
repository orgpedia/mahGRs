Government circular:
Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Mechanical), Group-B Cadre Officers of Water Resources Department. The Provisional Seniority List as on 01.01.2023 is annexed herewith as" Annexure-A." The said seniority has been created as per the instructions of the General Administration Department, Government Circular No: SRV-2016/ Q. No. 281/12, dated 13.07.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been used in the preparation of this seniority list.
All the Heads of Departments of Water Resources Department are hereby informed to bring the present seniority to the notice of all the officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Mechanical). The objections/ suggestions, if any, regarding the seniority list of the officers of the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Mechanical) should be submitted to the Government along with the views of the Head of the Department concerned within fifteen days from the date of issue of this circular. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
