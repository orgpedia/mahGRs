The Proposal:
The creation of all the posts in the subordinate zonal office under the Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Uplift Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara) has been fixed as per the revised figure vide Government Resolution No. 4 dated 06/02/2003. The offices of Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Uplift Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara) on the field establishment, 536 posts on the fixed temporary establishment and 13 posts on the converted temporary establishment have been allowed to continue till 31/08/2021. The decision was approved by 1.
All the Administrative Divisions have been empowered to extend the term of the existing temporary posts from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 as per the Governing Decision of Reference No. 3 of the Department of Finance including the temporary posts included in their format as well as the existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier review but not included in the format.
Government's decision
The Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Upsa Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara) vide Reference No. Pursuant to the proposal of the Superintending Engineer, Gosikhurd Uplift Irrigation Board, Ambadi (Bhandara) and the following statement on the fixed temporary establishment and converted temporary establishment in the 4 divisions and subdivisions under him, the extension of the temporary posts indicated in Annexure A and B from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 is being approved subject to the following conditions.
