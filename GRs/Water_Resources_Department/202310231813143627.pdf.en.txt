Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said promotions are being given to the officers to be promoted without prejudice to the seniority of the officers in service and whose cases have been kept open for consideration.
(ii) Promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (s) does not mean that the Engineer concerned is eligible for regular promotion and no entitlement to regular promotion shall accrue to him by virtue of this temporary promotion. Further, in the event of reduction of posts due to closure of some departments/ sub-divisions in future or if there is a need to demote due to any other reason, the junior most officers shall be demoted without any notice, last come first go.
(iii) The said promotion is being granted subject to the condition that no departmental enquiry/ disciplinary action is going on or proposed to be taken against the concerned. However, in case of any of these engineers against whom a departmental inquiry is ongoing or proposed to be held on the date of this order, the promotion orders shall stand automatically cancelled.
(iv) The said promotion order is purely temporary in nature. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 are being issued as per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution dated 07.05.2021. This government has taken that decision. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. The promotions will be made subject to the final decision.
(v) Engineers who are denied promotion on the ground that the promotions, though temporary, are regularised over a period of time, shall be debarred from availing the benefits of the Scheme of Assured Progress in Service. If a graduate/ diploma holder cadre engineer refuses promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (VIVA), the report should be submitted to the government immediately. The salary of Assistant Engineer Grade-II/ Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer should not be withdrawn from the month of November 2023, unless the promotion to the post of Deputy Engineer (s) is rejected by an engineer. The responsibility of this will be taken by the executive engineer of the concerned department.
(vi) It shall be the responsibility of the Executive Engineer of the concerned department to relieve the promoted engineers immediately after receiving the detailed posting orders for the post of Deputy Engineer (s). If there is a delay in this regard, the concerned executive engineer will be held responsible. Promoted within a month's time

In case of non-recruitment of an engineer leading to compulsory waiting period and payment of salary and allowances, the responsibility of the same shall be fixed on the concerned Executive Engineers/ Establishment Officers.
(ix) These promotions are provisional and are subject to the publication of the final seniority of their parent cadre. Therefore, the concerned will not get any entitlement for seniority in the cadre of Deputy Engineer (s).
