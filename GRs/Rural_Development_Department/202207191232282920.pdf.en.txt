The proposal-
As per the decision taken by the Central Government on 14th January, 2016, the Government was considering to disburse a total amount of Rs. 1.50 crore to the concerned district for the development of the first group of villages, namely, Hartala Gaon Group, District Jalgaon, for the second phase of the CGF, namely, Account Head 25152539 (Central Share 60%) Rs. 0.90 crore and Account Head 25152548 State Share (40%) 0.60 crore.
Government's decision
As per this decision, a total amount of Rs. 1.50 crore has been disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Palghar (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) for the development of Hartala Village Group, District Jalgaon for the first phase of the Shyama Prasad Mukherjee National Rurban Mission, for the second phase of the CGF to be disbursed to the concerned district for the second phase, with an account head of Rs. 25152539 (Central share 60%) Rs. 0.90 crore and account head of Rs. 25152548 (State share 40%) 0.60 crore. The amount will be deposited in the State level accounts of the National Rural Livelihood Mission. The funds should be drawn from the headings as mentioned below.
