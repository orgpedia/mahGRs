Introduction: Read here for the transfers of Zilla Parishad Teachers under the district. The revised policy has been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 07.04.2021. As per the provisions of the said policy, an online system has been developed for the transfer of primary teachers in Zila Parishad and the transfer of primary teachers in Zila Parishad will be done through this system in the year 2022. Due to some technical issues, the project has been delayed. Therefore, while implementing the online transfer of teachers for the year 2022, the government was considering to reduce some of the time of the process mentioned in the government decision. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The time period as mentioned in the table below is being allotted to complete the following proceedings mentioned in the relevant Government Decision regarding the transfer of Zilla Parishad Teachers within the district. The online transfer of teachers for the year 2022 should be completed within this time frame. This decision will be applicable only for online transfer of teachers in 2022.
