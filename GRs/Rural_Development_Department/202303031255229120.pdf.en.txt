4. Before giving technical sanction to the said work and before commencing the work, the following should be done.
(i) Before granting technical approval, the scope of the road work, Pavement Design and Hydraulic Calculations should be strictly checked and the concept should be determined accordingly.
(ii) It shall be ensured that the land required for the said road is in the possession of this Department. It should be reaffirmed that no road works should be taken up on private land or where no-objection certificate of the forest department is required.
(iii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under the reservoir of any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.
iv) The budget should have a provision of a board showing information about the work in Marathi.
v) The description of each rate in the Administrative Sanction Budget: The gaps assumed in the assessment/ amount/ rate and other related aspects are mandatory to be examined at their level by the competent authorities granting technical approval. The government has no role in this.
vi) The competent authority granting technical approval shall ensure that the proposed work is not included in any other scheme/ programme and is not approved by the Public Works Department, Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation or any similar body.
