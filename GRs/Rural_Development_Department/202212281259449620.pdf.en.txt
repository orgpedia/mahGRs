Now, the Department of Finance has made available funds through BEAMS for the target of 31 Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried) for the financial year 2022-23 within the limit of 42% of the total budgetary allocation. 56.00 lakh (Rupees Fifty Six Lakh only) is being kept under the purview of the concerned Divisional Commissioner. 3. Expenditure Demand No. L- 3-2415 Agricultural Research Education- (03) Subsidiary Grants to Government Institutions (03) (01) Yashwantrao Chavan Development Administration Prabodhini Yashada, Pune" The approved provision for the year 2022-23 under the head" Main Account Head- 2415 0517" should be apportioned. Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure is spent under the same index and the divisional commissioner should inform the government every month. The Deputy Commissioner (Establishment) concerned will act as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
4. The concerned Deputy Commissioner (Aastha), Divisional Commissioner's Office will be responsible for the strict implementation of the distribution of the fund and the expenditure on the matters for which the fund has been sanctioned. Also, the utilization certificate of the fund should be submitted immediately.
The grant sanctioned by this Government decision shall be subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) Yashoda, Pune shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure incurred from this grant.
2) Yashoda, Pune shall spend the said grant for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned.
3) The amount of subsidy required by Yashoda, Pune every month should be withdrawn from the treasury every month.
The Accountant General (Accounts), Maharashtra-1, Mumbai shall be empowered to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure incurred by the respective training centres from this grant and shall provide assistance/ facilities to the respective training centres/ institutions to enable them to carry out their work in a proper manner.
5) When paying the amount approved through this, the relevant details on the payment should be mentioned without fail.
This Government Order is being issued in exercise of the financial powers conferred on the Administrative Department under sub-section (2) of section 4 of sub-section (3) read with Government Order dated 17.04.2015 under the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part I, sub-sections 1 to 5.
