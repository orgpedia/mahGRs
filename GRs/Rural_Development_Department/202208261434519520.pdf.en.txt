Preamble: In March, 2019, under the Mahabharti, the Department of Rural Development has issued a notification regarding the recruitment of Group-C category posts under Zila Parishad. The guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision dated 10 th May, 2022. Accordingly, the government has approved the filling up of all vacant posts under the Zila Parishad at the level of Zila Parishad through the District Selection Board as before. Under this decision, the government will take immediate action to fill the vacancies in 5 cadres of health workers, health workers, laboratory technicians, drug manufacturers, health supervisors related to the health department in the Zilla Parishads, and all the information collected for the organization of this examination (DATA) and the examination fee respectively. The information has been received from Trust Communication Pvt Ltd and Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner's Office, Konkan Division, Navi Mumbai and State Level Nodal Officer.
A study group was formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Satara, to study and submit a detailed report in connection with the recruitment of this post and to conduct the examination in this regard in a transparent and unbiased manner. In this regard, the chairman, state level coordination committee and chief executive officer, district council, Satara, were informed about the schedule of the examination for recruitment to Group C posts related to the health department in consultation with all the district councils and submit it to the government.
