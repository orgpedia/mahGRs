4. Before giving technical sanction to the said work and before commencing the work, the following should be done.
(i) Before granting technical approval, the scope of the road work, Pavement Design and Hydraulic Calculations should be strictly checked and the concept should be determined accordingly.
(ii) It shall be ensured that the land required for the said road is in the possession of this Department. It should be reaffirmed that no road works should be taken up on private land or where no-objection certificate of the forest department is required.
(iii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under any water body of any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.
iv) The budget should have a provision for a billboard showing information about the work in Marathi.
v) The gap and other factors assumed under the description of each rate/ rate/ rate: rate/ amount/ rate separation in the administrative approval budget are mandatory to be examined at their level by the competent authorities granting the technical approval. The government has no role in this.
vi) The competent authority granting technical approval shall ensure that the proposed scope of work is not included in any other scheme/ programme and is not approved by the Public Works Department, Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, etc.
(vii) Technical approval should be accorded to these works as per the prevailing tariff schedule.
(vi) These works should be recorded in the computer system prepared for the Mukhya Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana by the concerned Executive Engineer immediately.
ix) The works falling within the Dongri/ Tribal/ Naxalite affected areas or areas within a radius of 15 km from the sugar mills, as per the prevailing decision of the Government, shall be inspected by the competent authorities, who will give technical approval and submit a report to the Government for making provisions accordingly. The entire responsibility of this will be of the competent authority/ engineer who will provide the relevant technical approval.
