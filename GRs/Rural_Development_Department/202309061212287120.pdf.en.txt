4. Before giving technical sanction to the said work and before commencing the work, the following should be done.
Before granting technical approval, the scope of road work, Pavement Design and Hydraulic Calculations should be strictly examined and the concept should be determined by adopting the guidelines of Vision Documents on New Technology Initiatives and Guidelines-2022 issued by NRIDA.
(ii) It shall be ensured that the land required for the said road is in the possession of this Department. It should be re-affirmed that no road works should be taken up on private lands or where no-objection certificate of the forest department is required.
(ii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under the reservoir of any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.
iv) The budget should have a provision of a board showing information about the work in Marathi.
v) The description of each rate in the Administrative Sanction Budget: The gaps assumed in the assessment/ amount/ rate and other related aspects are mandatory to be examined at their level by the competent authorities granting technical approval. The government has no role in this.
(vi) The competent authority granting technical approval shall ensure that the proposed work is not included in any other scheme/ programme and is not approved by similar agencies such as Public Works Department, Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, etc.
(vii) Technical approval should be accorded to these works as per the prevailing tariff schedule.
(vii) These works should be recorded by the concerned Executive Engineer in the computer system prepared for the Mukhya Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana.
ix) The works falling within the Dongri/ Tribal/ Naxalite affected areas or areas within a radius of 15 km from the sugar mills as per the prevailing decision of the Government shall be inspected by the competent authorities who have granted technical approval and submitted to the Government for making the provisions accordingly.
