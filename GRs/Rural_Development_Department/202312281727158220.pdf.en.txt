Preamble:- The Rural Pilgrimage Development Scheme is being implemented with the objective of developing various pilgrimage/ pilgrimage sites in the state and providing necessary infrastructure facilities to the devotees there. At present, Reference No. 2 Funds are made available to build the necessary infrastructure as per the government decision here. The state government had proposed to increase the grant for the development of pilgrimage centres in rural areas and to rename them as Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Gramin Yatra Sthal Yojana (B-Class) and Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Gramin Yatra Sthal Vikas Yojana, respectively, instead of" Special Programme for Development of Pilgrim Places in Rural Areas" and" Special Programme for Development of Pilgrim Sites in Rural Areas."
