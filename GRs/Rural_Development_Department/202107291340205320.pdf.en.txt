Read: Government decision February 12, 2021 by: Considering the need to install a VC Set-up in the hall of the Ministry of State (Rural Development), read the Government Decision to purchase Polycom here. Administrative approval was granted on February 12, 2021. In the intervening period, when the payment for the purchase of Polycom was sent to the Subordinate and Accounts Office for approval, they had raised objections to the issuance of the revised financial authority. In the interim period, the payment is required to be sanctioned in the current financial year 2021-22 as the financial year 2020-21 is over. The Finance Department vide Government Resolution No. The Finance Commission has approved the payment of pending dues for the financial year 2020-2021 as per the provisions of the Finance Act, 2021/ P.No.48/ EARTH-3 dated 24th June, 2021, out of the available funds for the financial year 2021-2022. In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The government took the decision. 12/02/2021 is being revised. Revised administrative approval is being given for the purchase of Polycom required for installation of VC set up in the chamber of the Minister of State (Rural Development) in the Ministry. 4,92,765/- (including taxes) is being approved.
