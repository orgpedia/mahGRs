For the implementation of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II in the state, a committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Superintending Engineer (PRM), Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency of the concerned department through a government circular dated January 19, 2022, to prepare a core network of roads, conduct PCI survey and determine the priority of roads, detailed project report, draft tender etc.
Matoshree Gram Samruddhi Khet/ Panand Road Scheme is implemented by the Planning Department for construction of farm/ waterways of standard quality mainly for transportation of machinery required for agricultural activities and also for the purpose of making farm/ waterways fit for external use. After the completion of the work of this road, the proposal of the concerned district council is sent to the Public Works Department at the government level after the approval of the Executive Engineer, the concerned Public Works Department through the Executive Engineer, the concerned Superintending Engineer, the Public Works Board, the concerned Chief Engineer, the Public Works Regional Department.
