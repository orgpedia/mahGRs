Government Order: Subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure-II of Rule-40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assumption of Office, Non-Acceptance of Service, Suspension and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, the reference No. 1 of the Government Notification No. As per the decision of the Government, Shri Rajendra Pitambar Patil, Project Director, District Rural Development Agency, Nandurbar is being appointed as Deputy Commissioner, State Election Commission, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on deputation for a period of 3 years subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. Rajendra Pitambar Patil, Project Director, District Rural Development Agency, Nandurbar shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original charge of the Deputy Commissioner, complete his term of office by handing over the charge of the Deputy Commissioner three years thereafter.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
