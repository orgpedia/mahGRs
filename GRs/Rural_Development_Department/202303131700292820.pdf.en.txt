4. Before giving technical sanction to the said work and before commencing the work, the following should be done.
(i) Before granting technical approval, the scope of the road work, Pavement Design and Hydraulic Calculations should be strictly checked and the concept should be determined accordingly.
(ii) It shall be ensured that the land required for the said road is in the possession of this Department. The road works which require no-objection certificate from private land or forest department should be taken up.
(iii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under the reservoir of any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.

iv) The budget should have a provision of a board showing information about the work in Marathi.

ix) The works falling in the hilly/ tribal/ naxalite affected areas or areas within a radius of 15 km from the sugar mills, as per the prevailing decision of the Government, shall be inspected by the competent authorities, who will give technical approval and submit the same to the Government for making provisions accordingly.
