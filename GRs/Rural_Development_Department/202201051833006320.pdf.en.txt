Preamble: The Government of India has restructured the Rajiv Gandhi Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (RGSPA) scheme of the Government of India for strengthening the Panchayati Raj system and implemented the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) from 2018-19. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme which has been operationalized w.e.f. 1st April, 2018. It has been decided to implement the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan in the State as per the decision of the Government vide No. 102 (Part-II)/ Aastha-15 dated 20.02.2019 of this Department.

Government Decisions In the financial year 2021-22, as part of the second instalment of the first instalment of State share for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) (60:40), an amount of Rs. 2227.90 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Ninety Thousand only). 100.00 Lakh (Rupees One Crore only) for Non-SCST (Capital), Rs. 866.67 lakh (Rupees Eight Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Sixty Six Thousand only) for SC/ SP General, Rs. 394.56 Lakhs (Rupees Three Crores Ninety Four Lakhs Fifty Six Thousand only) towards SCST (Capital) and Rs. 866.67 lakh (Rupees Eight Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Sixty Six Thousand only) for TSP (General) is being provided to State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune.
The funds to be disbursed to the State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune are being placed under the Divisional Commissioner's Office, Pune. Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner Office, Pune will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Deputy Commissioner (Establishment) shall disburse the grant to the concerned State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune.
The State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune shall disburse the said funds to the Zila Parishads as per the approved Annual Action Plan.
The State Nodal Officer, State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune and all Chief Executive District Councils should take care that the expenditure from the grant approved by this Government decision will be done very strictly. The State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune shall distribute the funds made available for this scheme to the concerned as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the instructions in the letter of the Central Government in the said Reference No. 6 and ensure that the funds distributed are properly utilized. Similarly, a report should be sent to the government immediately after reviewing the expenditure incurred on controlling the expenditure and the targets set. Also, the cost utilization certificate should be submitted.
The State Nodal Officer shall have the powers to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure made by the State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune, to the Accountant General (Audit), Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General (Audit), Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and shall extend full assistance/ cooperation to the concerned institution/ office so as to enable them to carry out their work in a proper manner.
