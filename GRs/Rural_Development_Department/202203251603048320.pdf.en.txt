GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MOVISAY-1022/ PR No. 41/2022/ AASTA-3A, dated 25th March, 2022.
The said Civil Services Board shall, (1) come into existence from the date of the said Government Decision and: (1) The proposal for the posting of officers in the list shall be placed before the Civil Services Board for recommendations after the finalization of the departmental cadre allocation as per the provisions of the Government for appointment by promotion/ direct service to the posts of Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted).
(2) While placing the proposal for recommendations on transfers before the Civil Services Board, it shall be necessary to bring to the notice of the Civil Services Board the facts regarding the proceedings as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants Act, 2005 and the background/ facts regarding the proposed transfers.
(3) On a proposal for posting, on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board, the proposal shall be submitted to the competent authority for approval. The final decision will be taken by the competent authority.
(4) On a proposal for transfer, on recommendation by the Civil Services Board, the proposal shall be submitted for approval to the appropriate competent authority in accordance with the provisions of the Government Employees' Transfer Regulation Act. The final decision will be taken by the authorities concerned.
Proposals for transfers will be subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005.
