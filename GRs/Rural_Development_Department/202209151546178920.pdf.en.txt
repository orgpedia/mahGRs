Government Circular: The final seniority list of Maharashtra Development Service Group-B officers from 1.1.2010 to 31.12.2020 has been published on the website of Government of Maharashtra vide Government Circular dated 27.05.2021. However, the list of seniority was yet to include the officers who had joined directly from the State Service Examination-2016 (CPTP-4A), State Service Examination-2017 (CPTP-5) and State Service Examination-2018 (CPTP-6).
Therefore, on the basis of the information received from all the Divisional Commissioners and on the completion of the probationary period of the above officers provided by the General Administration Department, the provisional seniority for the period from 01.01.2018 to 31.12.2021 has been published through a circular dated 3.6.2022. The seniority list for the period from 01.01.2018 to 31.12.2021 is being finalised after making suitable corrections as per the objections/ objections received.
The final seniority list of officers of Maharashtra Vikas Seva Group-B published vide Government Circular dated 27.5.2021 for the period from 1.1.2010 to 31.12.2020 is being published by combining the final seniority of 1.1.2010 to 31.12.2017 and the final seniority of 01.01.2018 to 31.12.2021.
The merit list has been prepared on the basis of points available in the regular promotion quota of Group B cadre. The officers who have not passed the departmental examination within the prescribed time and within the prescribed opportunity have been deprived of their services. In case of such loss of service, the said officers have been appointed in Group-B after passing the examination or as per the date of exemption as per the rules of the government.
