The proposal-
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 31.03.2022 decided that instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development Agency Administration Scheme, the State Government should continue the scheme as 100% State Scheme for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027. For District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme, 8 posts have been earmarked against 15 posts with effect from 01.04.2022 and it has been decided to repatriate the services of the remaining officers/ staff to their parent establishment.
