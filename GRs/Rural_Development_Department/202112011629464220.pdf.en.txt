Government's stand:
The final seniority of the officers of Maharashtra Development Service Group-B, who are regular and serving, for the period from 1.1.2010 to 31.12.2020 has been published by the Government Circular dated 27.5.2021. The inclusion of the name of Mr. Arvind Gudde, Deputy Executive Officer (Panchayat), Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal in the seniority list was unintentional.
Based on the information submitted by Arvind Gudde, Deputy Executive Officer (Panchayat), Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal vide letter dated 14.8.2021, vide letter dated 27.5.2021, regarding inclusion of name in the final service seniority list for the post of Maharashtra Development Service, Group B, published by the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati vide letter dated 14.9.2021 and 22.11.2021, the seniority number 71-A in the service seniority list published on 27.5.2021 is admissible to Shri Gudde.
