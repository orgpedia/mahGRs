Nandurbar) New Administrative Building Construction of Rs. The administrative approval has been given for the construction of the building at an estimated cost of Rs 427.79 lakh. The Additional CEO, ZP, Nandurbar while presenting the proposal said that since the budget for the year 2017-18 has been prepared as per the prevailing tariff list and the tariff for the year 2019-20 has been increased, the construction of the present new administrative building has been completed at a cost of Rs. 427.79 lakhs is not possible. Further, Rs. 63.93 lakh is not available under the SVANidhi or other head of accounts of Panchayat Samiti Navapur or Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar and it is not possible to allocate such a huge amount.
Accordingly, the Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar has directed the Panchayat Samiti, Navapur (Dist. Nandurbar) New Administrative Building Construction of Rs. 491.72 lakh has been requested for grant of revised administrative approval.
