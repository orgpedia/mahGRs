Introduction: NIC has developed 11 commandments under the e-Panchayat Enterprise Suite, out of which the Local Government Directory (LGD) creates a unique code for each revenue village, gram panchayat, panchayat samiti, zila parishad, nagar panchayat, municipality, municipal corporation, etc. The LGD code is issued to gram panchayats, zilla parishads and panchayat samitis. However, this requires coordination with the Revenue Department, Urban Development Department and the Election Commission. The government was considering the formation of a state-level committee to coordinate the work related to the LGD Code. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: A state-level committee is being formed in connection with the Local Government Directory (LGD Code).
