Government circular
It has come to the notice of the government that if there is a vacancy in the post of Group-A and Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Development Service, then the additional charge of the post is handed over to the officers of other cadres.
Maharashtra Development Service Group-A consists of officers in the cadre of Additional CEO (Selection Category), Additional CEO/ Project Director, District Rural Development Agency, Deputy CEO and Block Development Officer. The Maharashtra Development Service Group-B includes officers in the cadre of Assistant Block Development Officer.
The above officers have been entrusted with various important responsibilities such as implementing the policies of the Rural Development Department, controlling and implementing the various schemes of the Rural Development Department and achieving the targets set by the government. It is the duty of the officers of Group-A and Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Vikas Seva to perform the duties and responsibilities in accordance with the orders issued by the government from time to time. Therefore, it is not appropriate to give additional charge of the above posts to officers/ employees of other cadres.
(4) In case of vacancy in Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadre posts or if the said officer is absent/ on leave for a long period, the charge of the concerned post shall lie with the officers of Maharashtra Development Service Group-A and Group-B cadre as per Annexure-A attached.
The responsibility of handing over will be of the concerned Divisional Commissioner and Chief Executive Officer, District Council. Accordingly, the concerned Divisional Commissioner and Chief Executive Officer, District Council should take action.
