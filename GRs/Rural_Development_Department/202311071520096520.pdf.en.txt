Through the office, various rural housing schemes such as the centrally sponsored Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin and the state sponsored Ramai Awas Yojana, Shabari Awas Yojana, Pardhi Awas Yojana, Adim Awas Yojana, Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana, Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana, Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Gharkul Yojana, Modi Awas Gharkul Yojana and their supplementary Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Gharkul Land Purchase Financial Assistance Scheme and regularization of encroachments in rural areas are being implemented.
According to the UN Sustainable Development Goal 11, work in the affordable housing sector will have a positive impact on at least 14 of the 17 SDGs. For this, the government aims to ensure that the progress of housing works is qualitative and not just quantitative, to provide all the facilities to the beneficiaries by implementing innovative ideas, and to take the initiative to build houses resistant to natural disasters.
The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin has been implemented in the country since November 20, 2016 and every year November 20 is celebrated as' National Housing Day'. This year, on the occasion of National Housing Day, the government was considering to implement the" Maha Awas Abhiyan 2023-24" in the year 2023-24 to make all rural housing schemes in the state more dynamic and quality enhancement.
Government's decision
With a view to expedite and bring quality in the effective implementation of all rural housing schemes namely Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Gramin and State Sponsored Ramai Awas Yojana, Shabari Awas Yojana, Pardhi Awas Yojana, Adim Awas Yojana, Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana, Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana, Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Gharkul Yojana, Modi Awas Gharkul Yojana by taking various initiatives during the campaign period under the Government's policy of" Housing for All-2024," the Government has approved the implementation of" Maha Awas Abhiyan 2023-24" in the State of Maharashtra from 20th November, 2023 to 31st March, 2024, coinciding with the National Housing Day, 2023.
