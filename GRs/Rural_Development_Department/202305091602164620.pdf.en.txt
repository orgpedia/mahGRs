Read- 1. Government Circular, Rural Development Department No. APT 2014/ PR. No. 139/ Aastha-8, dated 5th July, 2014.
Preamble: The Government Circular No. APT 2014 As per No. 139/ Aastha-8, dated 5th July, 2014, the format of the question paper, the standard of the question paper and the marks for the Group-C posts in the Zila Parishad have been explained.
Further, the General Administration Department issued a Government Resolution No: PRANIM 1222/ PR. K-54/ K. As per 13-A dated 4th May, 2022, consolidated guidelines have been issued for direct recruitment to the nominated quota posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) Group-C and Group-D cadres under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission).
In the Annexure A of the Government Circular dated 5th July, 2014, the questions and the marks assigned to them have been fixed in the category-wise Marathi, English, General Knowledge, Mathematics and Technical Questions. The standard of the question paper has also been fixed to understand the difficulty level of the above subjects.
The candidates were repeatedly asked about the quality of the question paper as they did not have sufficient understanding of the question asked in the question paper. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to determine the format and quality of the question paper for easy reference of the candidates.
Government Circular: Government Circular APT 2014/ PR from Rural Development Department. No. 139/ Aastha-8 dated 5th July, 2014 and Government Resolution No. K-54/ K. The consolidated guidelines are being prescribed considering 13-A dated 4 th May, 2022 as follows.
