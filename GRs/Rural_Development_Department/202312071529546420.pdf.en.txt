Government Circular District Technical Service, Class-III Assistant Accounts Officer in the cadre of District Council (Accounts), Reference No. 3 for publication of State Level Final Seniority List dated 01.01.2023. The letter of 1 was directed to submit objections/ objections/ information to be incorporated afresh etc. to the government.
Accordingly, as per the objections/ objections and new information/ amendment proposals received from the concerned Divisional Commissioners for inclusion in the final seniority list, the final seniority list of 01.01.2023 of the District Technical Services Assistant Accounts Officer in the Zilla Parishad is being published as per the list annexed.
The names of the employees who have not passed the Zilla Parishad Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Examination or have not been exempted from passing the said examination have not been included in the accompanying seniority notice. Also, the seniority is being published on the assumption that the information of the Divisional Commissioner's offices who have not sent the information to the government within the prescribed time is incomplete.
With regard to the seniority list published earlier by the Government, it has been observed that such seniority lists are not brought to the notice of the concerned. Therefore, the concerned could not file their objections in time. Often the concerned employees request for amendment in the seniority list at the time of their promotion.
