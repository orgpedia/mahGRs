Government Decision No: 2022/ PROCESS-20176/ FINANCE-4 The proposal for the distribution of funds to the rural local bodies in the state was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
The Central Government has released the second instalment of 15th Central Finance Commission tied grant for the financial year 2022-23 amounting to Rs. 1083.49 crore is being made available for distribution to Rural Local Bodies (Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat at all three levels) in the State of Maharashtra under the head of accounts (25152637/ 25152655/ 25152673) as shown in the accompanying statement (Form-A). Funds are being disbursed through Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to all the Chief Executive Officers of the Zila Parishads as per the prospectus. These funds are being distributed to the Panchayat Raj Institutions in the ratio of 10: 10: 80 to the Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat respectively.
All the District Councils are required to deposit the 15th Central Finance Commission funds in the ICICI Bank account of the Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat by submitting the payment to the Treasury through the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS-Treasury net. Also, the funds of the Zila Parishad should be deposited in the ICICI Bank account of the Zila Parishad by submitting the payment to the Subordinate and Accounts Office, Mumbai, through the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS Treasury net through the Department of Rural Development.
The Deputy Executive Officer (Gram Panchayat) will be responsible for planning, controlling and coordinating the distribution of funds at the Gram Panchayat level as per the guidelines given by the Government.
