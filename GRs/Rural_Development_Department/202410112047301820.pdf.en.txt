Read: - Government Decision No.: Department of Rural Development, No. - Tanda Reforms-2019 / Q. No.67 / Aastha-5, dated 23rd February, 2024.
Preamble: - Department of Rural Development, Government of India Decision No. Tanda Reforms-2019 / Q.67 / Aastha-5, D. A District Level Committee under the Chairmanship of the Collector has been constituted for the implementation of Sant Sewalal Maharaj Banjara / Laman Tanda Samriddhi Yojana for declaring Banjara / Laman Tanda habitations, declaring villages, proceeding to declare Aisa Revenue Village, proceeding to establish Gram Panchayat, etc. and other ancillary activities. Considering this, the government was considering appointing non-governmental members of the Banjara / Laman / Lalwan community to the committee headed by the tehsildar for a period of 3 years.
Government Decision: - Taluk level committees are being constituted under the chairmanship of Tehsildar to implement Sant Sewalal Maharaj Banjara / Laman Tanda Samriddhi Yojana by electing non-official members of Banjara / Laman / Lavan community for a period of 3 years.
Taluk Level Committee: - (The list of Taluk and Taluk-wise non-official members is annexed in Annexure-A. )
a) Tehsildar-Chairman
b) Representatives of Banjara / Laman / Lavana Samaj _ Non-official members
a) Assistant Group Development Officer - Member Secretary

Government Decision HATH: Tansuyo-2024 / Q. 44 (Taluk Level-1) / Aastha-5
The functions of the committee are: - To grant the status of separate revenue village to all Banjara / Laman / Lawan Tandas, to set up a separate Gram Panchayat for Tandas and to follow up on other ancillary works. Also to create awareness for the scheme.
The daily allowances and travel allowances payable to the non-governmental members of the said Committee on the day of the meeting shall be paid out of the 2% CAH fund admissible for the administrative expenses of the said scheme, as the case may be.
The non-official members shall submit the payments to the CEO, Zila Parishad, concerned district and the CEO, Zila Parishad shall take action regarding payment of daily allowances and travel allowances to the concerned non-official members as per government rules.
3. To make the said Government Decision available on the Government of Maharashtra website wwww.maharashtra.gov.in.
