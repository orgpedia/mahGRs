Government circular:
Contextually, vide Circular dated 21.10.2022 dated 21.10.2022, the provisional seniority list of officers in the cadre of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selection Grade) has been published with effect from 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2022.
2. Under this circular, the final seniority of the Additional CEO (Selection)/ Deputy Commissioner (Establishment/ Development) Pay Scale Cadre for the period from 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2022 is being released subject to further conditions.
i) The said seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority of the concerned officers in the rank of Additional Chief Executive Officer and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the selection list of the officers in the rank of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selection Grade).
(ii) In case of officers against whom departmental inquiry is pending, they will be included in the seniority list, as the case may be, after the final decision of the departmental inquiry.
