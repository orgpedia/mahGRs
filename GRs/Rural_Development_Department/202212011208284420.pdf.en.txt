The Proposal- M. In the meeting held on 11th June, 2013 under the chairmanship of Deputy Chief Minister, it was decided that 50% of the demand of the Gram Panchayat should be made available to the Gram Panchayats, Pune, Satara and Solapur Zilla Parishads on the Palkhi route to provide facilities to the Warkaris accompanying the palanquins of various saints passing through the districts of Pune, Satara and Solapur on the occasion of Ashadhi Ekadashi and the remaining 50% should be made available to the respective Zilla Parishads by the Village Development Department. The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune vide letter No. 2 funding is requested. Therefore, the matter of providing the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Rural Pilgrimage Development Scheme, for the financial year 2022-23, Rs. A provision of Rs. 22500.00 lakh has been made in the Budget.
2. Within the limit of funds available from the provision in this budget, Rs. 37.00 lakh is being approved under this Government decision to be made available to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune. Add the payment details.
(3) The District Councils shall submit to the Government and the Accountant-General the utilisation certificate that the funds have been spent on the necessary matters.
