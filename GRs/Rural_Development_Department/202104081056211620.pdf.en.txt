Government circular:
As per the circular dated March 15, 2021, the interim seniority list of officers in the cadre of Additional CEO/ Project Director has been published from 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2020.
The final seniority of Additional CEO/ Project Director cadre from 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2020 is being released subject to the following conditions.
i) The said seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority of the concerned officers in the post of Deputy CEO cadre and their selection in the post of Additional CEO/ Project Director cadre subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(ii) In case of officers against whom departmental inquiry is pending, they will be included in the seniority list, as the case may be, after the final decision of the departmental inquiry.
(iii) The said seniority list is provisional in nature. Petition No. 8452/2004 in T.A. 1/2014 and T. A. 2/2014, Ma. The High Court of Judicature at Mumbai, vide Writ Petition No. 2797/2017 (Stamp No. 7256/2015) in State of Maharashtra v. Vijay Ghogre and other connected court cases dated 4.8.2017.
