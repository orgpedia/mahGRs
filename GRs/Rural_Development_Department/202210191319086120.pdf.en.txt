Opening of new accounting heads for Central share and State share for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Rural Economic Transformation Project (NRETP).
The Government of Maharashtra
the Rural Development Department
Government Resolution No: UMED-2022/ PR No.37/ Scheme-3
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
The date is October 19, 2022.
Preamble: The National Rural Economic Transformation Project (NRETP) is being implemented by the Government of India through Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India with World Bank assistance in 13 States since 2019-20. The main objective of the scheme is to strengthen the Ward Sanghas and provide them with maximum livelihood opportunities, promote value chain development activity, enrich and provide financial support to rural enterprises and empower them for skill development and digital finance. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments.
PFMS SNA has been launched by the Central Government for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. A separate State Linked Scheme (SLS) for NRETP has been allocated by the Central Government.
Accordingly, guidelines have been issued vide letter dated 23.03.2021 for introduction of separate head of accounts for all Centrally Sponsored Schemes and schemes under them.
Accordingly, the Government was considering opening a new separate head of accounts for the Central share and State share to budget separate funds for the implementation of the scheme.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the opening of new separate head of accounts for the distribution of Central share and State share equivalent to the Centre for the National Rural Economic Transformation Project (NRETP).
