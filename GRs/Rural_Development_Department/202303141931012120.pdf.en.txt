Preamble: Due to COVID-19 pandemic and lockdown, the Government has not been able to appoint/ engage the candidates who were selected/ recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, District Selection Committees and other selection agencies after completing the prescribed procedure and verifying the documents required before giving them actual appointment before 9th September, 2020. In this, the candidates concerned were deprived of appointment as a victim of the situation without any mistake. Considering this fact. Before the interim stay of the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) Reservation Act, 2018 by the Supreme Court on September 9, 2020, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the Selection Boards and other recruitment agencies have not been able to make appointments in government services.

In order to provide appointment to the total number of 1553 candidates who were not appointed before the recommended date of 09.09.2020, the Acts read with Annexure 1 and Annexure 3 have been passed. Accordingly, under the Act at Roll No.3, a Schedule has been filed covering a total of 1553 posts, including 1064 existing posts and 489 newly created posts. The schedule includes a total of 12 posts in Maharashtra Development Service Group A and Group B cadres, 7 and 5 respectively, under the Rural Development Department.
Therefore, as per the directions of the General Administration Department in the Government Decision dated 31st January, 2023, the creation of a total of 12 supernumerary posts in 2 cadres of Maharashtra Vikas Seva Group A and Group B under the Rural Development Department in the Schedule to the Maharashtra Superannuation and Appointment of Selected Candidates (Amendment) Act, 2022 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Appointment of Superintendents of Posts and Selected Candidates (Amendment) Act, 2022 (Maharashtra Act No. 8 of 2023), published in the Gazette of India dated 13th January, 2023 and the instructions of the General Administration Department, the Government hereby approves the creation of a total of 12 Superintendents of Posts, namely 7 Superintendents of Posts for appointment to the post of Group-A Officer, Maharashtra Development Service, Block Development Officer, in the Department of Rural Development and 5 Superintendents of Posts for appointment to the post of Group-B Officer, Maharashtra Development Service, Assistant Group Development Officer, in the Department of Rural Development as follows:

Considering the above number of posts created under the Rural Development Department, the appointment of candidates selected/ recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will be processed separately as per the instructions of the Government Decision dated January 31, 2023 of the General Administration Department.
The expenditure on the salary of a total of 12 supernumerary posts in the Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadres, 7 and 5 respectively, should be met from the budgetary grants sanctioned under the head" 2053-District Administration, 093-District Establishment (3) Project Group Headquarters, Block Development Officer (Territory) (2053 0707)."
