Circular:
Gram Panchayats in the State which do not have a separate building for their office
A separate office building will be constructed for the gram panchayats. Balasaheb Thackeray Smriti Matoshree Gram Panchayat Bandhani Yojana Decisions 1 and 2 have been approved. Considering the requirement of infrastructure for rural areas and the limitation of resources available for it. Under the Balasaheb Thackeray Smriti Matoshree Gram Panchayat Bandhani Yojana, administrative approval has been granted to 1748 Gram Panchayats out of 4252 Gram Panchayats which do not have a separate building for their own office.
2. self. Under Balasaheb Thackeray Smriti Matoshree Gram Panchayat Bandhani Yojana, out of 4252 Gram Panchayats which do not have their own office, out of 1748 Gram Panchayats which have been given administrative approval, the construction of 256 Gram Panchayats has been completed. There are instructions from the top level to complete the construction of office buildings of the remaining 1492 gram panchayats and to complete the construction of 2504 gram panchayat buildings that have not been given administrative approval.
