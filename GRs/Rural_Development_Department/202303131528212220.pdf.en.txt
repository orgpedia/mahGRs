Preamble: Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana and National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme
The offices of the State Management Cells are located at Reference No. Under Section 1, the Government has approved the shifting of the administrative office at Gram Vikas Bhawan, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai and the two conference halls in front of it and the three rooms adjacent to it. However, the said space is inadequate for the establishment of both the offices and is inconvenient in terms of office functioning. Under Section 2, the Chief Operating Officer, State Management Cell, Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana has requested to provide space with necessary facilities for day-to-day office work. Accordingly, No. Under Section 3, the Superintendent Engineer, Pramangrao, Thane, has to tender the budget of the work for the office of the State Management Cells of both the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana and the National Rurban Mission.
He was informed about the preparation, as per reference no. After inspecting the site under letter No. 4, a budget for the renovation work in the available space has been prepared and submitted for administrative approval and availability of funds. The matter of administrative approval of the budget was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The State Management Cells of both Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana and Rashtriya Rurban Abhiyan have been made available at Gram Vikas Bhawan, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai
