Government circular:
As per the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission, each of the tied and tied grants of FY 2020-21 received by the Central Government is Rs. 2913.50 crore. 5827 crore and Rs. 861.40 crore has been disbursed to all the three tiers of rural local bodies (Zila Parishads, Panchayat Samitis and Gram Panchayats). Funds provided by the central government to the local bodies are mandatorily disbursed through the Public Finance Management System (PFMS) system. Also, two systems- PRIASoft-PFMS- have been integrated to deliver the funds directly to the beneficiaries.
The annual accounts of Gram Panchayats/ Panchayat Samitis/ Zila Parishads are recorded in the system of PRIASoft on the e-Gram Swaraj Portal of the Central Government. In this system, the accounts of that year are completed after taking entries in 3 stages- Day book, Month book, Year book. The Central Government has made it mandatory for the 15th Finance Commission to distribute and spend funds through PFMS.
Both PFMS and PRIASoft systems are integrated with each other. Therefore, it will not be possible to spend the 15th Central Finance Commission funds on the PFMS system without updating all the previous records on the system.
Government decides to provide PRIASoft-PFMS (PPI) solution through ICICI Bank for disbursement of 15th Central Finance Commission funds to Zila Parishads, Panchayat Samitis and Gram Panchayats
