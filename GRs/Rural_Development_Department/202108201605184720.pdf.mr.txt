Introduction -
Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana is a scheme launched by Government
of India for connectivity of Rural Habitations of + 500 Habitations (+250
populations of schedule-V areas). The Project Proposals for Road works for up
gradation was submitted for approval of Empowered Committee of Ministry of
Rural Development, New Delhi. It was discussed in the Empowered Committee
and the Empowered Committee has given approval to the above Project Proposals.
Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India accorded approval for the
proposals vide letter No.P-17024/15/2020-RC (FMS No. 371917), Dated 31st
March, 2021 mentioned under above reference at Sr.No.1.
In this regard it is to state that Administrative Approval for Batch-I (year
2021-22 under PMGSY-III) 2581.72 Km. road length (380 works) proposal has

Resolution:
The Project Proposals of PMGSY-III (Batch-I) has been cleared by the
Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India vide letter dated 24th May,
2021. Accordingly the Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord
Administrative Approval for the 34 road works. The Government of Maharashtra
is also pleased to accord sanction for proportional State share.
