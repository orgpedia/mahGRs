The Proposal:
The policy decision regarding the implementation of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II in the State has been taken as per the Government decision dated 06.01.2022. As per the provisions of the said Government Decision, vide Government Decision dated 23 rd February, 2022, a Committee under the Chairmanship of the then Guardian Minister was constituted at the district level to select the roads under Mukhya Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II. At present, with the formation of a new government in the state, the decision of the Government dated February 23, 2022 of the Rural Development Department to select the roads under the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2 was under the consideration of the Government to reconstitute the District Selection Committee to select the roads under the scheme by superseding the committee formed under the chairmanship of the then Guardian Minister of the concerned districts at the district level. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken to constitute the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: At present, with the formation of a new government in the state, the committee formed under the chairmanship of the then Guardian Minister of the concerned districts at the district level under the Government Decision dated February 23, 2022 of the Rural Development Department to select the roads under the Chief Minister Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2 is being superseded under this Government Decision.
