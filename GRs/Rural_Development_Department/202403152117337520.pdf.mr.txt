Introduction -
Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana is a scheme launched by Government of
India for connectivity of Rural Habitations of + 500 Habitations (+250 populations
of schedule-V areas). The Project Proposals for Road works for up-gradation
was submitted for approval of Empowered Committee of Ministry of Rural
Development, New Delhi. It was discussed in the Empowered Committee and the
Empowered Committee has given approval to the Project Proposals. Ministry of
Rural Development, Government of India accorded approval for the proposals of
roads and bridges vide letter No.P-17024/15/2020-RC (FMS No. 371917), Dated
15th March, 2024 with various condition.
Government Resolution:-
The Project Proposals of PMGSY-III (Batch- III) has been cleared by the
Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India vide letter dated

Administrative Approval for the 50 road works and 223 LSBs work. The
Government of Maharashtra is also pleased to accord sanction for proportional State
share.
