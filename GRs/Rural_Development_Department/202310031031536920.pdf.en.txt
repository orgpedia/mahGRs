The proposal to distribute the funds to the rural local bodies in the State was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Tied Fund of the Fifteenth Central Finance Commission for the Financial Year 2023-24
The first instalment of Rs. 1069.39 crore is being made available for disbursement to Rural Local Bodies (Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat at all three levels) in the State of Maharashtra under the head Accounts (25152637/25152655/25152673) as shown in the accompanying prospectus (Form-A). The funds are being disbursed through Budget Distribution System (BIMS) to the CEOs of all the Zila Parishads as per the prospectus. These funds are being distributed to the Panchayat Raj Institutions in the ratio of 10: 10: 80 to the Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat respectively.
All the District Councils are required to deposit the 15th Central Finance Commission funds in the ICICI Bank account of the Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat by submitting the payment to the treasury with the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS-Treasury net. Also, the funds of the Zilla Parishad should be deposited in the ICICI Bank account of the Zilla Parishad by submitting the payment to the Subordinate and Accounts Office, Mumbai, through the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS Treasury net through the Department of Rural Development.
The Deputy Executive Officer (Gram Panchayat) will be responsible for planning, controlling and coordinating the distribution of funds at the Gram Panchayat level as per the guidelines given by the Government.
Department of Finance (Expenditure) Central Finance Commission of India vide letter No. 15 (2) FC XV/ FCD/ 2020-25, Sec. 02/06/2022 and the Government of India's letter No. M 11015/161/2020-FD, Dy. All the three Rural Local Bodies as on 07/06/2022
The 15th Central Finance Commission will not be distributed to rural local bodies where the administrator is working at the level of Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat.
At present, there are 26 zila parishads, 289 panchayat samitis and 1200 gram panchayats in the state.
