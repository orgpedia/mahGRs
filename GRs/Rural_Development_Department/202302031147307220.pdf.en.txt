GOVERNMENT DECISION: 2. Mauje Hedgewar Nagar (Dharangaon), Ta. Construction of Memorial in memory of Krantiveer Khajyaji Naik at Dhargaon, District Jalgaon at a cost of Rs. The Plan and Budget amounting to Rs. 1499.96 lakhs (Rupees Fourteen Crores Nine Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution.
The memorial should be constructed as per the provisions of Government Resolution No. 2016/ PR No. 03/ Para-9 of Rural Development Department dated 11th August, 2016. Also, after the completion of the construction of the memorial, the responsibility of maintenance and repair of the memorial should be entrusted to the concerned institution.
District Planning Officer, Jalgaon is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Collector, Jalgaon is being declared as Controlling Officer to meet the expenditure on the construction of the memorial. The cost of the memorial will be borne by the District Collector, Jalgaon. He should take action to get the construction of this memorial done by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Amarnath, District Jalgaon. Also, information regarding the progress of the work of the memorial and its utility, utility certificate should be submitted to the government from time to time and action should be taken to match the expenditure with the accountant general's account. Also, ensure that you spend your money in a timely manner.
The District Level Memorial Committee will be responsible for controlling the construction of the memorial.
