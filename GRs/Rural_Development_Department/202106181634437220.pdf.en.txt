Introduction: To achieve planned and proper growth of tourism business in the state and to prioritize the areas with the highest tourism potential in the state. Some of the tourism units have been given industry status under the tourism business to attract entrepreneurs in view of rapid development. The hospitality sector is the main service industry in the tourism business and plays an important role in the context of economic development of the state of Maharashtra. This will create many employment opportunities. Tourism is an important part of the tourism industry. The government decision has been issued under 1 and 2 and it was under the consideration of the government to bring the issues in this government decision to the attention of all concerned and to give instructions regarding the implementation of the provisions in the said government decision. Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:
Government Circular: 1) Hoteliers registered with the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, should levy electricity tariff, electricity tariff, water tariff, property tax, development tax, increased floor area index and non-agriculture tax at industrial rates from April 1, 2021. Read about it. The instructions have been issued by the Department of Tourism and Culture under the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2020.
(ii) Hoteliers registered with the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, at industrial rates only after meeting the prescribed criteria for obtaining the minimum basic standards.
