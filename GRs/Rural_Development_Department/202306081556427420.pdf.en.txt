The Committee and Gram Panchayats are being advised as follows:
(i) The Central Government has kept 60% of the funds allocated to Gram Panchayats under the 15th Central Finance Commission in the bonded form and has directed that half of the funds i.e. 30% should be used for sanitation related activities and the remaining half i.e. 30% should be used for drinking water related activities. However, out of the 15 th Central Finance Commission tied funds for sanitation and drinking water schemes, 30% of tied funds for sanitation related activities should not be sanctioned to GPDP/ BPDP/ DPDP schemes of 15 th Central Finance Commission at that level unless the works/ activities to be taken up/ taken up in the villages are included in the plan for 2023-24.
In cases where the plans have already been approved, the District Coordinator (Swachh Bharat Abhiyan) and Deputy Executive Officer, Zila Parishad should verify the same and revise the plan with the approval of CEO, Zila Parishad, as per requirement.
(ii) Since there is a large amount of funds available under Jal Jeevan Mission for water supply schemes, and in villages where no water supply related works are now left to be taken from the 15th Finance Commission funds, 30% of the funds tied for drinking water schemes can be used for sanitary works, all such works should also be taken up in the 2023-24 Plan.

(iii) Out of the total permissible amount of administrative sanction works related to sanitation by the CEO, Zila Parishad and Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti, as per the guidelines of Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-III, 70% of the fund is allocated for the implementation of Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin). phase). If found violating the rules, legal action will be taken against them.
(iv) As mentioned in point number three above, the Gram Panchayats concerned are required to ensure that there is no delay in payment of such works to the contractor in accordance with the 15th Central Finance Commission norms of 30% of the funds. In respect of payment of these dues, the Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti at their level shall review the same from time to time and the same shall be followed up by the District Coordinator (Swachh Bharat Abhiyan) and Deputy Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad from time to time.
(v) In respect of the 15th Central Finance Commission funds to be spent under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase-II at Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samiti level, it will be mandatory to include these works in the respective level plan as per local needs.
It will be mandatory to inform the Sarpanch, WSC members and Gram Sevaks about the guidelines of the Center and the Government Decision of the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation dated November 19, 2020 and December 2, 2021 regarding which elements have been included under the bonded fund while preparing the GPDP plans.
(vi) The Local Bodies shall be responsible for maintaining records of works and expenditure made out of the 15th Central Finance Commission tied funds on the respective websites of the Central Government. Accordingly, the District Coordinator (Swachh Bharat Abhiyan) and Deputy Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad should take care to instruct the concerned local body to register the physical and financial progress of the works completed through the 15th Central Finance Commission Bonded Fund on the e-GramSwarajya website from time to time.
