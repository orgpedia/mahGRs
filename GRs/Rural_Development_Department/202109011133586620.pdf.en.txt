The concerned officers should take charge of their new post immediately. Also, they should not go on leave. Their attention is drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No.: CDR 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30.8.1982. In view of the instructions given in it, they should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
3. The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ CEO shall relieve the transferred officers from their present charge and ask them to join immediately in place of their appointment. The concerned Divisional Commissioner should submit a proposal to the government regarding disciplinary action against the officers who will not relieve the transferred officers within the stipulated time.
