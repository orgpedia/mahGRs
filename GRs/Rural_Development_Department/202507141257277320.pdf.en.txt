Preface: - Samma. The Supreme Court has ordered the immediate holding of local body elections under the order in reference No. 4. In pursuance of the order, the State Election Commission has informed that a large number of electronic voting machines will be required for the pending local body general elections in the state. Considering the number of upcoming local body general elections in the state, the Commission is in urgent need of 1 lakh more Electronic Voting Machines, out of which 50,000 have been requested by the State Election Commission in a letter dated 13th March, 2025 and 30th April, 2025 to be made available by the Rural Development Department. For this, it has been requested to transfer a fund of Rs 100.00 crore to the State Election Commission or to the Collector, Thane.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to immediately disburse a sum of Rs. 50.00 crore (Rupees Fifty Crore only) to the District Collector, Thane for payment to the State Election Commission through the District Collector, Thane for the purchase of Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) or related matters.
Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
