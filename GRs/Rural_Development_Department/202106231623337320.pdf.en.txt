Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, 4% reservation has been provided in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities. Accordingly, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. For this purpose, the Central Government has published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04/01/2021 under the heading' List of posts identification suitable for Person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04/01/2021' in the website of the Central Government www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated 02/02/2021 to ensure that the posts of Group-A to Group-D, which are in the list published by the Central Government in respect of the posts in the State Government Establishment (including posts in subordinate offices), are in the category of Divyang, even if the duties and responsibilities, nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are different from the posts in the State Government Service.
Accordingly, the posts of Health Worker (Male), Health Worker, Laboratory Technician, Drug Manufacturer, Health Supervisor in the Group-C cadre of Zilla Parishad will be filled up at the District Hospital at No. 6 under reference for the disabled. The Government's decision of 22 February 2021 has been confirmed. Thereafter, No. As on 08.04.2021, the posts of Group-C in Zila Parishad have been earmarked for PwDs in 7 cadres namely 1) Senior Assistant (Clerk), 2) Junior Assistant (Clerk), 3) Senior Assistant (Accounts), 4) Junior Assistant (Accounts), 5) Junior Engineer (Architecture), 6) Junior Engineer (Vidyut), 7) Junior Engineer (Mechanical).

Further, as per the provisions of the Central Government Disabilities Act, 2016 and the Central Government Notification dated 04.01.2021 and the Government Decision dated 02/02/2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, the Government was considering the issue of ensuring the post of Group-D Attendant in the Zila Parishad for the disabled.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities for the purpose of determination of posts under the State Governments as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the Central Government's Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, a committee has been constituted by the department under the chairmanship of Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner's Office, Pune to ensure the posts in Group-C and D cadres in the Zilla Parishad for the disabled. The Committee has submitted its report after verifying the posts in the notification dated 04/01/2021 of the Central Government and ensuring the posts for the disabled in the posts of Zila Parishad which are generally found equivalent in the notification of the Central Government for the recruitable posts of Zila Parishad.
The report submitted by the Committee as well as the equivalent posts in the notification dated 04/01/2021 of the Central Government are being verified to ensure that this post in the Group-D Attendant Cadre of the Zila Parishad is included in the new category for Divyang under this Government decision as shown in the return" A."
This Government Order is being issued pursuant to the notification dated 04/01/2021 issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India and the Government Order dated 02/02/2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice, Government of India.
