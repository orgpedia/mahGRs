The concerned officers should take charge of their new post immediately and they should not go on unauthorised leave. Their attention is drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No.: CDR 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30.8.1982. In view of the instructions given in it, they should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
3. The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ CEO shall ask the posted officers to join immediately at their place of posting.
4.The concerned officers should report to the new place of posting and inform the date of taking charge. The Chief Executive Officer concerned should submit a proposal to the government through the Divisional Commissioner for disciplinary action against the officers who will not join the post posted within the prescribed period.
