The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Central Government dated 14th January, 2016, the Government was considering to disburse a total amount of Rs. 1.50 crore for the development of the first phase of the Shyama Prasad Mukherjee National Rurban Mission in the State under the Shyama Prasad Mukherjee National Rurban Mission, for the second phase of the CGF to be disbursed to the concerned district.
Government's decision:
As per this decision, a total amount of Rs. 1.50 crore has been disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Palghar (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) for providing funds to the State Management Cell, National Rurban Mission through Budget Distribution System (BDS) for the development of Hartala Village Group, District Jalgaon under the first phase of Shyamaprasad Mukherjee National Rurban Mission, for the second phase of CGF to be disbursed to the concerned district. The amount will be deposited in the State level accounts of the National Rural Livelihood Mission. The funds should be drawn from the headings as mentioned below.
