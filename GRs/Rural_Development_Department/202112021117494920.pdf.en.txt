The officers who will be transferred to the superannuation post under the decision of the said Government have been directed to create the superannuation of the post in which they are currently working and temporarily remain in service for 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired from service or till the recommendations of the study group constituted by the Government in this regard are received and the Government takes a final decision, whichever is earlier.
