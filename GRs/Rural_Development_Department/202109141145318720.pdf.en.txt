The Proposal:
The Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana has been launched in the State of Maharashtra under the Government Decision dated 28th October, 2015 to connect the unconnected villages and improve the quality of roads in rural areas. The government was considering appointing a retired deputy engineer on contract basis for the implementation of the scheme and for the effective implementation of the work of the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency.
Order: In view of the decision of the selection committee for the effective implementation of the work of Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency, in view of the educational qualification, work experience, technical knowledge and physical ability required for the post of retired Deputy Engineer, the following retired Deputy Engineers are being appointed on contract basis for a period of 11 months from the date of their appearance as Deputy Engineer on contract basis for the Chief Minister/ Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana.
The concerned should reach the place of appointment immediately.

The duties and responsibilities of a retired Deputy Engineer during the period of his appointment to a contractual post shall be as follows:
(1) The retired Deputy Engineer shall assist in the discharge of the duties of the Head of the Office in the office concerned and shall also comply with the instructions given by the Senior Office from time to time.
2) Coordinate with all the Deputy Engineers under the Divisional Office as well as the officers of other offices at the district level.
(3) To deal with all correspondence received by the office concerned with the approval of the head of the office.
4) Technical aspects, detailed project reports, budgets and others received at the divisional office level
To examine the technical proposals in detail and submit them to the concerned head of the office.
5) Detailed examination of the draft tenders, all payments of works, tariff increases, extension proposals, etc. received from the Deputy Engineer level.
(6) To perform the work assigned by the Ministerial Officers, Chief Engineers, Superintending Engineers and other senior officers under the Department from time to time, and to submit periodic reports to the concerned office within the prescribed time.

03. Retired Deputy Engineer to be transferred to General Administration Department with the above provision. The following terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 17/12/2016 shall also apply.
(1) No person shall be entitled to be included in any cadre of the Government concerned or to receive any other benefit of regular service on account of being appointed on contract.
The competent authority for appointment shall have the power to terminate the contractual services of such officers at any time under special circumstances.
(3) The person appointed under the contract shall not be engaged in any commercial activity which may interfere with the performance of the service.
The person appointed under the contract will be required to declare the conflict of interest.
Contractual appointees are required to maintain confidentiality of documents/ information and supporting materials received by them.
The persons appointed on contract basis shall be required to complete the work assigned to them within the stipulated time. The appointing authority will periodically review and evaluate their performance.
The Chief Engineer, Chief Minister/ Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana will be the Controlling Officer.
The retired Deputy Engineer shall submit information/ proposals/ reports in respect of the above activities to the Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer/ Executive Engineer, Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana from time to time.
