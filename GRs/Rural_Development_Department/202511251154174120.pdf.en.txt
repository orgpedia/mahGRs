The Proposal:
The Cabinet of Ministers. The meeting on 29.07.2025 has approved the implementation of a new scheme "Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj Abhiyan" under the Rural Development Department with effect from this year 2025-26.
Also, it has been approved to open a new head of accounts for the scheme, providing additional outlays and provisions. Accordingly, the Government is to open a new head of accounts for the expenditure on the scheme.
