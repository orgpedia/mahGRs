(iii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under the reservoir of any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.

(v) The description of each rate/ rate/ rate differential/ amount/ assumed gap in rate differential and other related matters in the Administrative Sanction Budget are mandatory to be examined at their level by the competent authorities for grant of technical approval. (vi) The competent authority granting technical approval shall ensure that the proposed work is not included in any other scheme/ programme and is not approved by similar agencies such as Public Works Department, Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, etc.
(vii) Technical approval should be accorded to these works as per the prevailing tariff schedule.
(vii) These works should be recorded in the computer system prepared for the Chief Minister's Gram Sadak Yojana by the concerned Executive Engineer.
ix) The works falling within the Dongri/ Tribal/ Naxalite affected areas or areas within a radius of 15 km from the sugar mills, as per the prevailing decision of the Government, shall be inspected by the competent authorities, who will give technical approval and submit a report to the Government for making provisions accordingly. The entire responsibility of this will be of the competent authority/ engineer who will provide the relevant technical approval.
