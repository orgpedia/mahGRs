The above officers are being given ad-hoc promotions in the cadre of Additional CEO (Selection Grade) subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
3.1) The ad-hoc promotions to the above officers in the cadre of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selection Grade) are subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(ii) In case of non-availability of vacancies in the cadre after repatriation of senior officers on deputation, the said ad-hoc promotions are being made subject to demotion of the junior most officers of the order.
3.3) The said promotion is ad-hoc in nature and is being given in public interest as an administrative exigency.
3.4) This ad-hoc promotion will not entitle the officers concerned to any regular promotion i.e. seniority.
3.5) The officers promoted as above are required to join the post assigned to them within the prescribed period.
4. The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ CEO, Zila Parishad shall relieve the above officers who have been given posting by ad-hoc promotion in the cadre of Additional CEO (Selection Grade) immediately and instruct them to report immediately at the place of new appointment.
5. The officers promoted ad hoc in the cadre of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selective) should join their new place of posting immediately and submit a compliance report to the Government after assuming charge of the said post. The concerned officers should not go on unauthorised leave. Their attention is drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No.: CDR 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30.8.1982. In view of the instructions given therein, they should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the appointment, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
