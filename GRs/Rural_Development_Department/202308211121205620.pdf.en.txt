No. 510/ Work-12, dated 16th February, 2018; (5) Government Resolution, Rural Development Department No. No.66/ 2021/ Aastha-3A, dated 10th August, 2016; Preamble: As per the Government Resolution dated 04th July, 2016 of the Department of Rural Development, the department-wise break-up of posts in Maharashtra Development Service, Group A has been decided. Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A has a total of 4 cadres namely (i) Additional CEO (Selection Category) Cadre, (ii) Additional CEO/ Project Director Cadre, (iii) Deputy CEO Cadre and (iv) Group Development Officer Cadre. The posts in these three cadres will be filled with 100% promotion. A total of 295 posts are sanctioned in the Group Development Officer, Group-A cadre and these posts are filled in the form of direct service (50%) and promotion (50%).
The department-wise break-up of posts in Maharashtra Development Service, Group B has been decided as per the Government Decision dated 10 th August, 2022 of the Department of Rural Development. There are a total of 443 sanctioned posts of Assistant Group Development Officer, Group-B Cadre, which are to be filled in the ratio of direct service (50%) and promotion (50%).
Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadre officers for various schemes under Rural Development Department, Water Supply and Sanitation Department, Public Health Department and Planning Department.

Provision has been made for appointment by deputation in the order of formation of the concerned department/ rules of service/ government decision. In accordance with the policy of deputation issued in the Government Decision in the context of General Administration Department, in the provision of Paragraph No. Provided that where the services of officers of a particular cadre are required to be taken on deputation in accordance with the provisions of clause (a) of section 6, the policy of deputation shall not apply to such posts if such posts are created on the establishment of other administrative departments (in cases where the salary of the post is being paid from the Consolidated Fund of the State Government) or on the establishment of boards, corporations, companies, public undertakings and autonomous bodies under the control of the State Government (where the salary of the posts is not being paid from the Consolidated Fund of the State Government).
As per the above policy of the General Administration Department, the posts in the government offices which are filled by deputation only from the officers of the Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadres, such posts must be shown together in the Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadres. The government was considering the issue of determining the total strength of the Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadres by including the posts on deputation in the government offices in which the posts are filled by deputation only from the Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A and Group-B cadres.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the above proposal, the total strength of the Maharashtra Development Services, Group-A and Group-B cadres is being approved by this Government Resolution as follows.
