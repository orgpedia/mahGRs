In order to celebrate the successful movement of community organizations under the campaign on the occasion of World Women's Day, it was approved to implement the" Mahasmriddhi Mahila Sashaktikaran Abhiyan" in a time-bound manner from World Women's Day, 08th March 2021 to World Environment Day, 05th June 2021.
The" Mahasmriddhi Mahila Sashaktikaran Abhiyan" was launched with the objective of accelerating the campaign in the wake of the first wave of COVID-19. However, due to the second wave of Covid-19, the campaign did not get enough time to be implemented. However, as the situation is slowly returning to normal in the state, the campaign is being extended till 2 nd October 2021, coinciding with the birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi, to give a fair chance to the districts to excel in this campaign and compete.
