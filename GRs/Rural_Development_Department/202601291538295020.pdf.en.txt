6. The Divisional Commissioner's Office while disbursing the grant to the training centre shall ensure that the amount required is made available to them by verifying the actual amount of grant required on salary / non-salary basis. Care should be taken to ensure that no more than the above mentioned grants are disbursed. Also, the training centre should give prior intimation to the concerned Commissioner's office as to how much subsidy is required per month for salary / non-payment. Accordingly, the Commissioner's Office shall disburse the grant for salary / non-salary. The principal of the centre concerned will be solely responsible for the necessary grants, if they are not given prior notice.
With this grant, the Accountant General, (Accounts) Maharashtra-I Mumbai and / or Accountant General, Maharashtra-II Nagpur shall be empowered to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure made by the respective Training Centre and shall be assisted / facilitated by the respective Training Centre / Institutions so as to enable them to carry out their work properly.
