Government's decision:
The following decisions are being taken regarding the recruitment of all categories (excluding the posts of drivers and Group-D cadres) in Group-C under the Zila Parishad under the Rural Development Department.
The Government is approving the filling up of vacancies up to a limit of 80% of the vacancies in the direct service quota of all categories of Group-C (excluding posts of drivers and Group-D cadres) under the Zila Parishad under the Rural Development Department. The approval was granted by the Finance Ministry. It will remain in effect until August 15, 2023.
The subsequent recruitment process was decided by the Government of the Finance Department. As of September 30, 2022, the revised format can be done only after the government approves it.
The revised time-bound programme (tentative schedule of examination) for filling up of vacancies of all categories (excluding posts of drivers and Group-D cadres) in Group-C of Zila Parishads through District Selection Boards at their level has been fixed and added in Form-1 by fixing cadre-wise reservation as per rules. All district councils should strictly follow this time-bound programme. The District Selection Board and the Zilla Parishad will be responsible for filling up the vacancies (up to a limit of 80% of the total vacancies), ensuring their reservation, inviting nominations, selecting the company for conducting the said examination (if required), conducting the examination (online/ offline), etc.
