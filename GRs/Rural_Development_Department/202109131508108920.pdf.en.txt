The proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, 4% reservation has been provided in government jobs for persons with disabilities. Accordingly, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. For this purpose, the Central Government has published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04/01/2021 under the heading' List of posts identification suitable for Person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04/01/2021' in the website of the Central Government www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. As mentioned in the notification, the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which have been ensured by the Central Government for the disabled, are in the state government.

As per the approval of the expert committee of the Social Justice and Assistance Department, the government was considering to ensure a total of 9 cadres in the Group-C cadre of the Zila Parishad- 1) Construction Engineering Assistant 2) Junior Mechanic 3) Livestock Supervisor 4) Gram Sevak (Contractual) 5) Extension Officer (Panchayat) 6) Extension Officer (Statistics) 7) Extension Officer (Agriculture) 8) Anganwadi Supervisor and 9) Junior Accountant.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- As per the report of the Expert Committee of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance dated 07.07.2021, in accordance with Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, a total of 7 cadre posts of 1) Construction Engineering Assistant 2) Junior Mechanic 3) Livestock Supervisor 4) Gram Sevak (Contractual) 5) Extension Officer (Panchayat) 6) Extension Officer (Agriculture) 7) Anganwadi Supervisor in the Zilla Parishad for the disabled are being ensured under this Government decision as shown in the new category of Divyang as shown in the Statement-A.
Further, as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has published the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government. As per the Central Government's Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, a committee has been constituted by the department under the chairmanship of Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner's Office, Pune, to ensure the posts of Group-C and D cadres in the Zilla Parishad for the disabled. The Committee has submitted its report after verifying the posts in the notification dated 04/01/2021 of the Central Government and ensuring the posts for the disabled in the posts of Zila Parishad which are generally found equivalent in the notification of the Central Government for the recruitable posts of Zila Parishad.
After verifying the report submitted by the Committee as well as the equivalent posts in the notification dated 04/01/2021 of the Central Government, a total of 2 posts of 1) Extension Officer (Statistics) 2) Junior Accounts Officer in the Group C cadre of the Zila Parishad are being ensured under this Government decision along with the new category for Divyang as shown in the return" B."
Thus, as mentioned above, a total of 9 posts are being ensured for Divyangjans namely 7 posts as per return A and 2 posts as per return B.
