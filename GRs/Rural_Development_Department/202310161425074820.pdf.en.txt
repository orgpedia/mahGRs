The Proposal:
The combined probationary training programme for the candidates of Group-A and Group-B who are directly recruited every year since 2014 through the State Service Examination by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission is being implemented as per the decision of the Government dated January 20, 2014. Accordingly, considering the provisions of the Government Decision dated 20th February, 2016 of the Finance Department at Roll No. 2 in the above-mentioned text and the Office Orders and Government Circulars of the General Administration Department at Roll No. 3 to 5 in the said text, the Government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for the Group Development Officers, Group-A and Assistant Group Development Officers, Group-B probationary officers recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the State Service Examination-2021.

Government Resolution No: Mavise-1123/ P. No. 143/ Aastha-3A
Government's decision:
On the basis of the final result of the State Service Examination-2021, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has received a recommendation and under the office order of the General Administration Department, 14 candidates of Group-A and 15 candidates of Assistant Group Development Officer, Group-B have been appointed as Trainee Officers to provide training at various levels for a period of 2 years from October 3, 2023. Total 29 supernumerary posts of Group Development Officer, Group A Trainee Officers under Combined Competitive Training Programme (CPTP)-9 in the pay scale of S-20: 56100-177500 in the revised pay matrix as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission and Assistant Group Development Officer, Group B Trainee Officers in the pay scale of S-15: 41800-132300 in the revised pay matrix as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission.
Under the Combined Peripheral Training Programme (CPTP)- 9, 29 such supernumerary posts are being created in the establishment of the Rural Development Department.
The expenditure on salary for the period from 03.10.2023 to 02.10.2025 for the posts of Block Development Officer, Group-A and Assistant Block Development Officer, Group-B, in the total number of 29 posts in the cadre of Block Development Officer, Group-A and Group-B, shall be spent from the sanctioned grants for the years 2023-2024, 2024-2025 and 2025-2026 under the head" 2053-District Administration, 093-District Establishment (3) Project Group Headquarters, Block Development Officer (Territory) (2053 0707)."
