Introduction: The Government has decided to select smart villages and give District Smart Gram and Taluka Smart Gram awards to the Gram Panchayats of the state according to their ability, by implementing various schemes of the government on cleanliness, management, responsibility, non-conventional energy and environment and transparency and use of technology. This has been approved as per the Government Resolution dated 21.11.2016.
Former Deputy Chief Minister R. R. (Aaba) Patil has made valuable contributions to the development of rural Maharashtra. Considering their contribution to rural development, the Smart Village Scheme being implemented in the state should be named as R. R. (Aaba) Patil Sundar Gaon Puraskar Yojana. Cabinet meeting held on 27th February, 2020. The minutes of the Cabinet Meeting held on 5th March, 2020 have been approved. Accordingly, the decision to name the Smart Village Scheme as R.R. (Aaba) Patil Sundar Gaon Puraskar Yojana was taken in the context. This was decided in the Government Resolution dated 20.3.2020. Accordingly, it was considered to amend the title of the said scheme.
The government has decided to name the Smart Village Scheme as R. R. (Aaba) Patil Sundar Gaon Puraskar Yojana. This was decided in the Government Resolution dated 20.3.2020.
Accordingly, the Government is approving the following amendments in the head of accounts of the said scheme.
