Government circular:
As per the Government Circular dated 12th November, 2021, the final seniority list of officers in the cadre of Block Development Officers has been published from 1.1.2018 to 31.12.2020.
Under this circular, the provisional seniority of the Group Development Officer cadre from 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2022 is being published subject to the following conditions.
(i) The promotion in the said seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority in the post of Assistant Group Development Officer Cadre of the quota officers and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the selection list of their post of Group Development Officer Cadre.
(ii) Finalization of Departmental Inquiries in respect of officers against whom departmental inquiries are pending.
After the decision, they will be included in the seniority list as the case may be.

All Divisional Commissioners/ CEOs are requested to bring this seniority list to the notice of all concerned officers (including officers on deputation) working under their control and submit the same to the Department without any delay. After receiving such seniority, the concerned officer should submit the objections, if any, regarding his seniority to the government through the concerned Divisional Commissioner within 15 days and send an additional copy to the government immediately. All concerned officers in the seniority list are also advised to submit objections, if any, regarding their seniority as mentioned above within the prescribed time frame. They should note that objections received after the due date or late will not be considered. After receiving such objections, the Divisional Commissioner shall submit a copy of the objection along with the feedback to the Government within one week.
4. With regard to seniority list, it has also come to the notice of the Government that the copy issued by the Government is not being provided to the concerned officers in time. Therefore, all the Divisional Commissioners/ CEOs should take care to inform all the concerned officers under their control about the availability of this seniority from the government website to the officers on deputation under their department/ retired from their department and immediately inform the government about it.
