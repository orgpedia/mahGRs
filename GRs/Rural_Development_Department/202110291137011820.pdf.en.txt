Introduction: As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1, it has been made mandatory to adopt e-tendering process for procurement of services and goods required for various developmental works and development works in the Panchayati Raj Institutions with effect from 01.01.2012.
The revised procedure and time period for notification of tender under e-tendering system has been prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference No. 2 and 3.
As the code of conduct is likely to come into force due to the Legislative Council elections, Zilla Parishad elections, Gram Panchayat elections in the near future in the financial year 2021-22, it is necessary to complete the road works, bridge repair works under the Rural Development Department (excluding the works under the Prime Minister and Chief Minister Gram Sadak Yojana under the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency) and various other development works in public interest immediately and on time.

Government circular:
In order to expedite the works of road works and bridge repair works under Panchayat Raj Institutions (excluding works under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana and Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana under Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency), Zilla Parishad Self-Fund, Shrine, 2515 basic amenities and various other development works of public interest, the time limit prescribed for inviting the said tender in Paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2013 and Paragraph 1 of the Government Decision dated 26th July, 2016 of the Rural Development Department in the said context, vide Circular dated 10th August, 2021, read with Annexure 7 above, was approved till 31st October, 2021, fixing the short period as per the tender price of the works. However, vide Circular No.7 dated 10th August, 2021, the period of short notice under e-tendering system is hereby extended up to 30th November, 2021.
All the Chief Executive Officers, Zila Parishads and all the heads of departments under them as well as the people's representatives should bring the works under the rural infrastructure (2515 1238) to the notice of the agencies where the machinery under the Public Works Board is working and ensure that the above works are completed within the deadline. With effect from 01.12.2021, the above relaxation shall stand automatically cancelled and the provisions of the relevant Government Decisions dated 23.09.2013 and 26.07.2016 shall come into force again.
