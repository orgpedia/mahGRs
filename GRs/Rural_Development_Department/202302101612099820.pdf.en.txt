4. Before giving technical sanction to the said work and before commencing the work, the following should be done.

(ii) It shall be ensured that the land required for the said road is in the possession of this Department. It should be reaffirmed that no road work should be taken up on private land or where no objection certificate from the forest department is required.
(ii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.
