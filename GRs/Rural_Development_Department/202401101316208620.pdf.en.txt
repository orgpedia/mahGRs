Preamble: - Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A has a total of 4 cadres viz., (9) Group Development Officer (2) Deputy CEO (3) Additional CEO / Project Director and (4) Additional CEO (Selected Category), the number of posts is fixed for each cadre.
However, officers from Maharashtra Development Service Group-A are also deputed on the request of the department to the posts created by the department other than the sanctioned number of posts in the cadre of Group-A, Deputy CEO, Additional CEO / Project Director and Additional CEO (Selection).
The Department of Rural Development vide its Government Decision dated 04.07.2016 had fixed the division-wise profile of posts in Maharashtra Development Service, Group A. The issue of approving a revised profile of the post was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, a proposal for demarcation of post in Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A cadre was submitted to the Finance Department. In pursuance of this proposal, a proposal for framing a framework by the Department of Finance was submitted to the Sub-Committee in its meeting dated 20th March, 2023. As recommended by the Sub-Committee, the proposal was placed before the High-Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting dated 17th April, 2023. Following the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, the Government has taken the following decision regarding fixation of revised profile of posts in Maharashtra Development Service, Group A cadre.
Government Decision: - Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A has a revised profile of a total of 4 categories namely (9) Group Development Officer (2) Deputy CEO (3) Additional CEO / Project Director and (4) Additional CEO (Selection Category) as mentioned in the following table: - Group Development Officer, Group-A Cadre Pay Scale (S-20: 56100-177500)
