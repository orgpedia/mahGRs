Government's decision:
The Central Government has released the first instalment of Central share under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) (SCSS) for the financial year 2023-24 amounting to Rs. 9302.925 lakh and State share of Rs. 6201.950 lacs out of which Rs. 15504.875 lakh is being approved by the Government for spending and disbursement through Budget Distribution System.
The expenditure shall be met out of the approved allocation for the year 2023-24 and the Central Account Fund is required to be provided as per Demand No. N-3 2505 Rural Employment, 60 Other Programmes, 789 Special Component Schemes for Scheduled Castes (01) (04) Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) (Central Share 60%) (Programme) 31 Subsidiary Grants-in-Aid (2505 A102) and State Account Fund Demand No. N-3 2505 Rural Employment, 60 Other Programmes, 789 Special Component Schemes for Scheduled Castes (01) Under Scheduled Castes Component Programme
