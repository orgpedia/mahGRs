Government circular:
The Election Commission of India undertakes a programme of verification and correction of errors in the existing electoral rolls under the Special Summary Revision Programme every year and updating the electoral rolls by enrolling new voters as on the qualifying date i.e. 01st January. The Special Summary Revision Programme, 2023 envisages revision of electoral rolls by enrolling new voters who will attain the age of 18 years as on 1st January, 2023. From 2023, citizens who complete 18 years of age on or before the four qualifying dates of January 1, April 1, July 1 and October 1 every year will be able to register in advance in the electoral roll.
