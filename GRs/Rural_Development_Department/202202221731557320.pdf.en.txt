The Proposal:
The policy decision regarding the implementation of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II in the state will be taken in accordance with the government decision dated January 6, 2022.
has come. As per the provisions of this government decision, a committee under the chairmanship of the guardian minister at the district level has to be appointed to select the roads. The committee will consist of the Chief Minister, two members of the Legislative Assembly to be selected by the Guardian Minister of the district, the Superintending Engineer (PG) of the concerned department, and the Executive Engineer (PG) of the concerned district. The Maharashtra State Rural Road Development Corporation (MSRDC) will be the member secretary of the committee. It has been provided that the member secretary concerned will submit the taluk-wise list of roads in the district to the committee as per the criteria in the relevant government decision.
Accordingly, a district-level committee had been formed. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken to constitute the committee.
The following committee is being appointed at the district level under the chairmanship of the Guardian Minister to select the roads under the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2.

Steps should be taken to select the roads as per the criteria prescribed for the Chief Minister's Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2 through the committee formed as above.
The Executive Engineers (EE) and Superintending Engineers (EE) of the concerned departments will be appointed as per the list of roads selected by the committee. The M.G.S.O.) needs a detailed budget of the cost of this road
The documents should be submitted to the government through the Chief Engineer, Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana for government approval.
The committee constituted as above for selection of roads under Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II will be automatically terminated after the period of implementation of the scheme.
