GOVERNMENT Circular: Interim Seniority List for the period 1.1.2018 to 31.12.2020 of the officers in the cadre of Block Development Officers has been published vide Circular dated 30.07.2021.
Under this Circular, the final seniority of the officers of the cadre of Block Development Officers from 1.1.2018 to 31.12.2020 is being published subject to the following conditions.
(i) The officers who have come into direct service have been ranked as per the seniority determined by the General Administration Department and the officers in the promotion quota have been ranked as per the availability of points.
ii) The said seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority in the cadre of Assistant Group Development Officers of the concerned officers in the promotion quota and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the selection list of their Group Development Officer cadre.
(iii) The said seniority list is provisional in nature. Petition No. 8452/2004 in T.A.1/ 2014 and T. A. 2/2014, Ma. high
