The proposal: In the meeting held on 11th June, 2013 under the chairmanship of Deputy Chief Minister, it was decided that 50% of the demand of the Gram Panchayat should be made available to the Gram Panchayats, Pune, Satara and Solapur Zilla Parishads on the Palkhi route to provide facilities to the Warkaris accompanying the palanquins of various saints passing through the districts of Pune, Satara and Solapur on the occasion of Ashadhi Ekadashi and the remaining 50% should be made available to the respective Zilla Parishads by the Village Development Department. As per this decision, the government was considering providing 50% of the total demand of the Zila Parishads to the Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad, Pune, Satara and Solapur for this purpose.
