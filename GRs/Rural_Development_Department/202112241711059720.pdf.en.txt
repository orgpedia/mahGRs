Narikari- 2019/ S.No.794/ Aastha- 7, Dy. December 22, 2021 Introduction: Maharashtra Rajya Gramsevak Union has demanded to reduce the extra work. In response to this request, As per the discussion in the meeting held on 15th June, 2021 under the chairmanship of Minister of State (Rural Development), Shri. Additional Chief Secretary (Rural Development) A six-member committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune to examine and study and submit a report in accordance with the directions given in the minutes of the meetings held on March 19, 2021.
Government's decision:
Now the number of members in the committee formed to submit a report after examining and studying the demand to reduce the extra work of the village servants is being increased from 6 to 7 and the next member is being included in this committee.
