Preamble: Reference No. (1) Here. The posts of central heads have been created in the state under the Government Decision of 14/11/1994. According to this, the basic technical administrative department of the head of the center is the Department of School Education and Sports. Similarly, the School Education and Sports Department is also in the process of determining the policy regarding the post of the head of the center, appointment, educational qualification and promotion. However, since the post of the head of the centre is under the Rural Development Department, the School Education and Sports Department will appoint the head of the centre from time to time.

As per the policy decision, the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad will take action to make necessary amendments in the Service Entry Rules for the post of Head of the Centre in the District Services (Entry into Service) Rules, 1967, and until the revised notification in this regard is issued, the promotion of the Head of the Centre in the cadre will be subject to reference No. (3) The Department of School Education and Sports may, subject to reference No. (4) It is hereby notified by the Government.
There is confusion at the field office level about whether the process of promotion to the post of center head should be done according to the policy set by the School Education and Sports Department or according to the provision of the notification of the Rural Development Department. As a result, different procedures are carried out in each district using different procedures regarding the promotion to the post of head of the center. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines with a view to bring uniformity in the state regarding the process of promotion to the post of central head was under the consideration of the government. Government circular:

(2) Therefore, until the issuance of the revised notification regarding the necessary amendments in the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad District Services (Entry into Service) Rules, 1967, the reference No. 1 of the Department of School Education and Sports regarding the promotion to the Head of the Centre Cadre shall not be considered. (3) Action shall be taken in accordance with the amended provisions of the Government Decision referred to herein. Similarly, the matter pending for guidance to the government regarding the promotion to the post of head of the centre should also be dealt with by the appointing authority and the competent authority at their level as per the said guidelines.
