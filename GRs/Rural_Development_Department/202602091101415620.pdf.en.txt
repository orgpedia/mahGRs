The Proposal: -
The state government proposes to enact the Maharashtra Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Act, 2023, under the Central Government's Jan Vishwas Act, 2023, to make life easier for the common man in the state and easier for businessmen to do business. In the first phase, the "Maharashtra Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Bill 2025" to amend seven state Acts was introduced in the winter session of the legislature held at Nagpur. As approved by the Legislature, "The Maharashtra Public Trusts (Amendment of Provisions) Act No. 56, 2025 dated 31st December, 2025 has been notified. In the second phase, the "Maharashtra Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Bill 2026" is proposed to be introduced in the upcoming budget session to amend the remaining state Acts.
For this, the Department of Rural Development, Government of Maharashtra, in pursuance of the provisions of the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Act, 1959 and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961, has taken steps to simplify and decriminalise the THM, criminal, penal procedure-centric provisions. The government was considering setting up a state-level committee under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary, Rural Development Department.

The Government's Decisions:
The following State Level Committee is being constituted to recommend rationalization, simplification and decriminalization of the provisions of the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Act, 1959 and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 with a view to enhance the Ease of Doing Business under the Maharashtra Public Trust (Amendment of Provisions) Bill, 2026.
