02. The said order comes into immediate effect and the officer concerned shall join the post indicated in the order of posting forthwith. Also, the officers concerned should not go on unauthorised leave. The attention of the concerned authorities is being drawn to the Government Circular No: CDR 1082/2567/28 XI, dated 30.08.1982 of the General Administration Department. They should note that if the officers concerned submit any form of application for change of posting / cancellation of appointment, they will be liable for disciplinary action as per the instructions in the said Government Circular.
03. The Divisional Commissioner / CEO concerned shall ask the officer posted to join immediately in place of his / her appointment.
The officer concerned shall appear at the place of the new appointment and inform the Government of the date of assumption of office. The officer concerned shall not hold office within the prescribed period.
