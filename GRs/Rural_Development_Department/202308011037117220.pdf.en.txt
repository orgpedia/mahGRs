Rural Development: The Rural Development Department is implementing various schemes and schemes of the State Government and the Central Government. The administrative as well as technical skills of the concerned officers and employees are required while implementing such schemes/ projects. The scheme to honour such officers/ employees is decided by the Government of Rural Development and Water Conservation Department. It has been started from 2005-2006 with effect from 02.09.2005.
Accordingly, for the year 2020-2021, the Department of Rural Development, Ministry of Rural Development (itself) and District Level Officers/ Staff have been synchronized. The selection has been made as per the Government Circular dated 11.7.2023.
