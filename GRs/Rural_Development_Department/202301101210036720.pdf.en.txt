Government's decision:
The interest amount credited to the bank account on the Central Finance Commission Fund should be used by the rural local bodies for activities and related matters for which grants have been provided as per the guidelines issued by the Central Government and the State Government. The amount deposited in the ICICI Bank account is of the first installment of the tied fund of the financial year 2021-2022 to the rural local bodies of the state under the 15th Central Finance Commission. This amount is being distributed to 34 district councils.
The amount will be credited to the 15th Central Finance Commission ICICI Bank account of the Zilla Parishads as shown in the statement (Form-A) accompanying this Government decision.
As per the recommendations of the Fifteenth Central Finance Commission, the tied grant is to be used for the following infrastructure services: 1. Maintenance and repair of sanitary and hygienic local bodies.
2. Drinking water supply, water recharge/ rainwater harvesting, water recycling.
The tied grant is to be used in the ratio of 50% each for the above two items. If one of the above two items is fully implemented, the funds should be spent on the other item. The complete implementation in this regard needs to be certified by the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Gram Sabha.
