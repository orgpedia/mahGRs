Government's decision:
The entire budget for the financial year 2022-23 has been approved and a total of Rs. 20000.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget and the Finance Department has earmarked Rs. 4200.00 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System.
Subject to the prescribed limits of the powers of devolution of funds to the administrative departments, as mentioned in the circular dated 04.04.2022, the budgetary allocation for construction/ upkeep of administrative buildings of Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samiti is Rs. 4200.00 Lakhs (Rs. Forty-two crore only) is being approved by the Government to be placed under the respective Chief Executive Officers of Zila Parishads by sanctioning the Subsidiary Grants in the Cash Flow Month of July, 2022 as follows. The funds should be spent following the prescribed procedure for these works.
