As per the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission, the first instalment of tied grant (TID) for the year 2021-22 for rural local bodies in Maharashtra is Rs. 1292.10 crore has been received. The government has made it mandatory for rural local bodies to disburse funds through the Public Finance Management System (PFMS) system. Also, two systems- PRIASoft-PFMS- have been integrated to deliver the funds directly to the beneficiaries.
PES-4521/ P.No.50/ ASKDB to provide PRIASoft-PFMS (PPI) solution through ICICI Bank for disbursement of 15th Central Finance Commission funds to Zila Parishads, Panchayat Samitis and Gram Panchayats. 26.08.2021 has been issued.
Accordingly, the funds received by the State Government from the 15th Central Finance Commission will be disbursed to the rural local bodies of the State through Public Finance Management System (PFMS) system through ICICI Bank. The workshop was held in Pune on October 7 and 8, 2021.
