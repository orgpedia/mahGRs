With a view to expedite the completion of various development works of ZP self-financing, pilgrimage, 2515 basic amenities and other public interest works under the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency, and considering the possibility of holding elections to ZP, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat in the year 2022-23, in the said context of the Department of Rural Development, the time limit prescribed for inviting tenders in paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2013 and paragraph 1 of the Government Decision dated 26th July, 2016, vide Circular No. Public Works Department No. 12 As per the decision of the Government here, the time limit for publishing e-tender has been fixed considering the code of conduct in the places where elections to Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities, Nagar Panchayats, Gram Panchayats and Zila Parishads are to be held in 2022-23. Keeping this in view, the matter of fixing the period of publication of e-tender for the Village Development Department was under consideration.
Accordingly, vide Circular No. 11 dated 28th June, 2022, the period for issue of short-term e-tendering notices under e-tendering system shall be upto 31st January, 2023.
