The proposal: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 31.03.2022 has decided that instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development Agency-Governance Plan, the State Government should continue the scheme as 100% State Plan for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027. For the District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme, 8 posts have been earmarked against the earlier 15 posts with effect from 01.04.2022 and it has been decided to repatriate the services of the remaining officers/ staff to their parent establishment.
The Central share (60%) received from the Central Government for the year 2021-22 for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers and employees of the District Rural Development Agency. 6,07,89, 000/- (Rupees Six Crore Seven Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand only) and corresponding State Share (40%) Rs. 4,05,26, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Five Lakhs Twenty Six Thousand only) aggregating to Rs. 10,13,15, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Thirteen Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only) out of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 vide Government Decision No. As on 23.05.2022, the concerned CEO, Zilla Parishad has been allocated as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for payment of salaries and allowances of officers and employees in the District Rural Development Agency-Administration.
