4. Before giving technical sanction to the said work and before commencing the work, the following should be done.
(i) Before granting technical approval, the scope of the road work, Pavement Design and Hydraulic Calculations should be strictly checked and the concept should be determined accordingly.
(ii) It shall be ensured that the land required for the said road is in the possession of this Department. The road works which require no-objection certificate from private land or forest department should be taken up.
(ii) The Technical Sanctioning Authority shall ensure that the work is not likely to go under the reservoir of any irrigation/ hydroelectric water supply project or that the work is not affected by such projects.
iv) The budget should have a provision of a board showing information about the work in Marathi.
v) The gaps and other issues assumed under each rate description/ rate/ amount/ rate in the administrative approval budget are mandatory to be examined at their level by the competent authorities granting technical approval. The government has no role in this.
(vi) The competent authority granting technical approval shall ensure that the work proposed is not included in any other scheme/ programme and is not approved by similar agencies like Public Works Department, Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation etc.
(vii) Technical approval should be accorded to these works as per the prevailing tariff schedule.
(vii) These works should be recorded by the concerned Executive Engineer in the computer system prepared for the Mukhya Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana.
ix) The works falling in the Dongri/ Tribal/ Naxalite affected areas or areas within a radius of 15 km from the sugar factory as per the prevailing decision of the Government shall be inspected by the competent authority giving technical approval and the work shall be carried out accordingly in every case.
