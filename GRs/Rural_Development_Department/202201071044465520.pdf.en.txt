The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has been conducting Combined Probationary Training Programme (CBT) for the candidates of Group A and Group B who are directly recruited every year from the year 2014 through the State Service Examination. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, on the basis of the final result of the State Service Examination-2019, the Government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for the candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for the Maharashtra Development Service, Deputy Executive Officer/ Block Development Officer in Group-A cadre and Assistant Group Development Officer in Group-B cadre.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has released the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination, 2019.
