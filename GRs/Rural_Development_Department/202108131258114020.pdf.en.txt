The Maharashtra Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) and the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana (CMGSY) are long-term and ambitious schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency (MRRDA) under the Rural Development Department. Under the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana, the target is to upgrade 30,000 km of roads. Further, the Central Government has set a target of upgrading 6550 km of roads in the State of Maharashtra under Pramangrasyo Phase-III in all the districts under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana. For the implementation of this program, works like DRRP, creation of core network, facility survey, PCI survey, as well as field preparation for implementation of E-Marg software, etc. have been started simultaneously.
The central government has fixed a time frame for this. Experienced and competent officers must be employed in the districts for all these works. In view of this, the matter of continuation of the above mentioned total 1384 posts is under consideration of the Government.
