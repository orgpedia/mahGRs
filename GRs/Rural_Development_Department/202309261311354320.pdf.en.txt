Preamble: Shri Narayan Venkatrao Nagargoje attends asst. He joined the post of Junior Assistant on 18 August 2003.
Narayan Venkatrao Nagargoje has been appointed as Junior Assistant. He retired on 30/06/2013 with a total service period of 9 years 10 months and 13 days. However, as per Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, pensionary benefits are not admissible to them as they have not completed 10 years of continuous service for receipt of pension.
The Aurangabad bench of the Bombay High Court. With reference to the common judgment dated 13/08/2015 in 2582/2012 and other connected petitions, Shri Narayan Venkatrao Nagargoje has submitted a representation dated 27/02/2023 to the Government for addition of 1 month 18 days to his qualifying service as per Rule 54 (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 for the purpose of admissible retirement pension. In pursuance of this representation, the Government has decided as a" special case" to:
Government Decision: Mr. Narayan Venkatrao Nagargoje has served in the post of Junior Assistant for a total of 9 years, 10 months and 13 days continuously. """ Considering the unanimous decision dated 13.08.2015 passed by the Bombay High Court, Bench, Aurangabad in Petition No. 2589/2012 and other connected petitions, the Government is pleased to allow a total of 1 month and 18 days as a"""" special case"""" in the period of service of Shri Narayan Venkatrao Nagargoje as per Rule 54 (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, to make pensionary benefits admissible to him.""" Accordingly, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Latur, should take immediate action to start the retirement salary from the date of retirement of Shri Narayan Venkatrao Nagargoje.
