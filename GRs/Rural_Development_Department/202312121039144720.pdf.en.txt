The ruling:
The Central Government has released the first instalment of 15th Central Finance Commission Grant in the form of Untied Grants for the financial year 2023-24. 712.94 crore to the rural local bodies in the State of Maharashtra as shown in the accompanying statement (Form-A).
It is being made available for distribution to the self-government institutions (for all the three levels of Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat) under the head of accounts (25152628/ 25152646/ 25152664) respectively. The funds are being disbursed through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to the CEOs of all the Zila Parishads as per the prospectus. These funds are being distributed to the Panchayat Raj Institutions in the ratio of 10:10:80 to the District Councils, Panchayat Samitis and Gram Panchayats respectively.
All the District Councils are required to deposit the 15th Central Finance Commission funds in the ICICI Bank account of the Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat by submitting the payment to the treasury with the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS-Treasury net. With the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS Treasury net through the department, the funds are required to be deposited in the ICICI Bank account of the Zilla Parishad by submitting the payment to the Subordinate and Accounts Office, Mumbai.
The Deputy Executive Officer (Gram Panchayat) will be responsible for planning, controlling and coordinating the distribution of funds at the Gram Panchayat level as per the guidelines given by the Government.
