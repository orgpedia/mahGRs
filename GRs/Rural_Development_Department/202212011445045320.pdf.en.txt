The Proposal:
The policy decision to launch Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II for the upgradation of roads in rural areas on the lines of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana in the State has been taken as per the Government decision dated 06.01.2022. As per the decision of the Government dated 18th May, 2022 in the Table 2 of the aforesaid Readings of the Planning Department, for the 2 financial years 2022-23 and 2023-24, Rs. 1,000 crore has been approved to be provided under the District Annual Plan (General). In addition, instructions have been given to take necessary action as per the instructions given by the Rural Development Department as the administrative department of the scheme. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to decide the modalities for the transfer of the said funds.
Government circular:
The following procedures should be followed while allocating the funds available annually to each District Planning Committee under the District Annual Plan (General) for the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II in the 2 financial years 2022-23 and 2023-24 as approved by the Planning Department.
