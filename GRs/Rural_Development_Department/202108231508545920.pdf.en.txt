The Proposal:
Since the central share received from the Central Government, the instructions given by the Finance Department to revise the figure and the final review of the proposed posts will take some time to be submitted to the High Power Committee and to get approval and to issue orders regarding this, in view of the requirement of workable posts at present, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad and Chairman District Rural Development Agency and Project Director, District Rural Development Agency (all) have been given instructions to continue 15 posts in the District Rural Development Agency.
Accordingly, a total of 510 temporary posts of 15 posts each for 34 District Rural Development Agencies in the State were approved to be continued from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 under the Government Decision dated 03.03.2021 of the Department.
Now, a total of 510 temporary posts in 34 District Rural Development Agencies in the state were under the consideration of the government to continue from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
