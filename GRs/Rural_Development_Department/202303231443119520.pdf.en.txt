Currently, the GST rate on these items is 18% (9% CGST+ 9% SGST). This rate is applicable from 18 th July, 2022.
Government Circular: In view of the above changes, the following instructions are being issued regarding Goods and Services Tax in the contracts of District Councils.
(a) New Works Budgets:- As per the Government decision dated 15th February, 2021, instructions have been given to prepare the budgets as per the prevailing rates of Goods and Services Tax while preparing the new budgets. Accordingly, from the date of this Government decision, the Goods and Services Tax should be levied at 18% while preparing new budgets.
(b) Payments for ongoing works:- At present, tenders for ongoing works have been invited excluding Goods and Services Tax. Payment of such works subject to the terms and conditions of the tender
Payments to be made as per the changed 18% Goods and Services Tax.
While making payments to the Zila Parishads for the ongoing works for which tenders have been called along with the Goods and Services Tax, necessary action should be taken as per the tender terms and conditions taking into account the changed 18% Goods and Services Tax.
