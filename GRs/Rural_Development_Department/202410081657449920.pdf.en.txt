The Government Court:
In pursuance of the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, in the interest of public service, the Government, on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board and with the approval of the authorities competent to make the transfer, and in terms of the provisions of sections 4 (4), and 4 (5) of the said Act, is issuing orders for the posting of Shri Rajendra Pitambar Patil, Additional CEO / Project Director and Deputy Commissioner, State Election Commission, Mumbai, in the Maharashtra Development Services, Group-A Cadre. The officer concerned is being posted in the position indicated in column no. 3 in front of his name.
