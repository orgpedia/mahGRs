4.2. The said promotion is ad-hoc in nature and is being given in public interest as an administrative exigency.

4.4) Officers promoted as above are required to join the post assigned to them within the prescribed period.
5. The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ CEO, Zila Parishad shall relieve the Block Development Officer, the above-mentioned officers who have been given postings by ad-hoc promotion in Group-A cadre, immediately and instruct them to appear at the place of new appointment.
6. The Block Development Officer, the above officers who have been given ad-hoc promotion in Group-A cadre, should join their place of posting immediately and submit a compliance report to the Government after taking charge of the said post. The concerned officers should not go on unauthorised leave. Their attention is drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No: CDR 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30.8.1982. In view of the instructions given therein, they should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the appointment, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
