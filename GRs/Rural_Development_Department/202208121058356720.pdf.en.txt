The above officers are being given temporary promotion to the cadre of Additional CEO (Selection Grade) subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
3.1) Provisional promotion to the cadre of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selection Grade) to the above officers is subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
3.2 The said ad-hoc promotions are being made subject to demotion of the junior most officers of the order if there is no vacancy in the cadre for the senior officers on deputation after their repatriation.
3.3. The said promotion is temporary in nature and is being given in public interest as an administrative exigency.
3.4) This temporary promotion will not entitle the concerned officers to any regular promotion i.e. seniority.
3.5) The officers promoted as above are required to join the post assigned to them within the prescribed period.
4. The concerned Divisional Commissioner/ CEO, Zila Parishad shall relieve the above officers who have been given posting on temporary promotion in the cadre of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selection Grade) immediately and instruct them to report immediately at the place of new appointment.
The officers who have been temporarily promoted to the cadre of Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selective) should immediately report to the place of their new appointment and submit a compliance report to the government after taking charge of the said post. The concerned officers should not go on unauthorised leave. Their attention is drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No.: CDR 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30.8.1982. In view of the instructions given therein, they should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the appointment, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
