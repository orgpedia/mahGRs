The Proposal:
Government Resolution No. Smagrayo-2015/ S. No.151/ A/ Scheme-1, D. As per Scheme-11, dated 20th March, 2020, cash awards for the year 2019-20 for RR (Aaba) Patil Sunder Gaon Puraskar Scheme will be distributed to the first level Taluka Sunder Gaon Gram Panchayats. There is also a provision to publicise the scheme.
Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds for giving awards to Taluka Sunder Gaon Gram Panchayats in the state was under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
Under the RR (Aaba) Patil Sundar Gaon Award Scheme, 351 Taluka Sunder Gaon and 34 District Sunder Gaon Gram Panchayats of the state have been selected for Rs. 10.00 lakh and Rs. 40.00 lakh. In the year 2020-21, under the scheme of R.R. (Aaba) Patil Sundar Gaon Puraskar Scheme, under the head Demand No. L-3, 2515-Other Rural Development Programme, 198-Assistance to Gram Panchayats, (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants (Programme) to Panchayat Raj Institutions for implementation of Smart Village Scheme-31-Assistant Grants (Non-salaried) (2515 1677), Rs. While the budgetary allocation is Rs. 55,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Five Crores only), the Finance Department has made available 50% of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 27,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores Fifty Lakhs only) for disbursement on Budget Distribution System (BDS). However, for the year 2020-21, the Finance Department has approved that the amount disbursed on BEAMS is Rs. 27.50 crore out of the revised budget estimate provision of Rs. 41.25 crore under head 2515 1677 for the year 2020-21.
Under the RR (Aaba) Patil Sundar Gaon Award Scheme, 351 Taluka Sundar Gaon Gram Panchayats of 34 Zilla Parishads selected in the year 2019-20 were given financial assistance of Rs. 10.00 lakh per Gram Panchayat. 35,10,00, 00,000/- (Rs. Thirty five crore ten lakh only) so much has to be distributed. However, since the balance amount of Rs. 1,47,25, 772/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Seven Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy Two only) is left for Virna, the said fund is presently being distributed to Zilla Parishad, Thane on Budget Distribution System (BDS).
The Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the above funds and the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane will be the Controlling Officer in this regard. These funds should be kept under their control till further orders.
