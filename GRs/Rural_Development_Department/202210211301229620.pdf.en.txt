All Divisional Commissioners/ CEOs are requested to bring this seniority list to the notice of all concerned officers (including officers on deputation) working under their control and submit the same to the Department without any delay. After receiving such seniority, the concerned officer should submit the objections, if any, regarding his seniority to the government through the concerned Divisional Commissioner within 15 days and send an additional copy to the government immediately. All concerned officers in the seniority list are also advised to submit objections, if any, regarding their seniority as mentioned above within the prescribed time frame. They should note that objections received after the due date or late will not be considered. After receiving such objections, the Divisional Commissioner shall submit a copy of the objection along with the feedback to the Government within one week.
