Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 20.01.2014, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has decided to impart training to Group A and Group B officers recruited directly from the State Service Examination under a combined probationary training programme. Accordingly, a district level (75+4) weeks training programme is going on under the Combined Subordinate Training Programme-7 of the Subordinate Block Development Officers, Group-A of the Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A recommended from the State Service Main Examination 2019. Under the said (75+4) weeks training, the probationary officers are required to be posted for training of Group Development Officer, Group-A post on independent charge from 26th June, 2023 onwards (29 weeks).
Combined Subordinate Training Programme- 18 Subordinate Block Development Officers, Group-A of 7 Panchayat Samitis are being allocated for separate workload training for the post of Block Development Officer, Group-A as mentioned in Annexure-A.
