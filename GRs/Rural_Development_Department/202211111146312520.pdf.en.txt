Government circular:
As per the circular dated 21.10.2022, the provisional seniority list of officers in the cadre of Deputy CEO/ Block Development Officer (Selection Grade) has been published from 1.1.2021 to 31.12.2022.
Under this circular, the final seniority of the Deputy CEO/ Group Development Officer (Selection Category) cadre from 1.1.2021 to 31.12.2022 is being released subject to the following conditions.
i) The said seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority of the concerned officers in the post of Group Development Officer Cadre and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to their selection list in the post of Deputy CEO/ Group Development Officer (Selection Grade) Cadre.
(ii) In case of officers against whom departmental inquiry is pending, they will be included in the seniority list, as the case may be, after the final decision of the departmental inquiry.
(iii) The said seniority list is provisional in nature. high
