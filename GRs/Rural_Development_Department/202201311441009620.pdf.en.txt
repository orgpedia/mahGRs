Government's decision:
A total of Rs. 3008.00 lakh has been provided in the budget for the construction of residential buildings of Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samitis. 902.40 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System.
Subject to the prescribed limits of the powers of the administrative departments to disburse the funds, as mentioned in the circular dated 06.04.2021 and the Government Resolution dated 24.06.2021 of the Finance Department, the amount of Rs. 30,08,00, 000/- (Rs. Thirty crore eight lakh only) to be placed under the respective Chief Executive Officers of the Zila Parishad as follows, by sanctioning the same in the Cash Flow Month of January, 2022.
