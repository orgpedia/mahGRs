Earlier, the Water Conservation Department was part of the Rural Development Department. However, the Water Conservation Department has been created separately as Soil and Water Conservation Department and the same has been separated from the Rural Development Department. Therefore, it is necessary to reconstitute the Establishment Board constituted under the Government Resolution dated 12.5.2017.

Therefore, there is no state-level head in the rural development department like other departments. Therefore, it is necessary to reconstitute the Establishment Board constituted under the Government decision dated 12.5.2017 to appoint an officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary in the Rural Development Department as a member in the Civil Services Board (1).

Government Resolution No: Mavise-1021/ Q. No. 56/2021/ Astha-3A dated 12th August, 2021
Government Decision: The Civil Services Board (1) is being reconstituted to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the transfer and posting of officers of Group-B (Gazetted) cadre of Maharashtra Development Service under the control of Rural Development Department as follows:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1                                                                     | Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Rural Development& Panchayat Raj Department) | Chairman    |
| 2                                                                     | Deputy Secretary (Establishment-1), Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department                       | the members |
| Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Services), General Administration Department | the members                                                                                              |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The said Civil Services Board shall, (1) come into existence from the date of the said Government Decision and: (1) The Government shall, after finalising the allocation of Divisional Cadre as per the provisions of the Government, for appointment by promotion/ direct recruitment to the posts of Maharashtra Development Service Group-B (Gazetted), place the proposal for posting of officers in that list before the Civil Services Board for recommendations.
(2) While placing the proposal for recommendation of transfers before the Civil Services Board, it shall be necessary to bring to the notice of the Civil Services Board the facts regarding the proceedings as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants Act, 2005 as well as the background/ facts regarding the proposed transfers.
(3) On the recommendation of the Civil Services Board on the proposal for posting, the proposal shall be submitted to the competent authority for approval as per the recommendation. The final decision will be taken by the competent authority.
(4) On the recommendation of the Civil Services Board on the proposal for transfer, the proposal shall be submitted to the appropriate competent authority for approval in accordance with the provisions of the Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants Act. The final decision will be taken by the authorities concerned.
Proposals for transfers will be subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005.
