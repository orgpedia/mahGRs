Introduction: Asli to Charichapada road in NDR-97 of Nandurbar district, sanctioned under Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana. Work on the road. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2019, Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for an amount of Rs. 125.45 lakhs.
The work was ordered. 30.07.2019.
As mentioned in the proposal, a 7m 3 span small bridge was included in the budget in the Uday river bed at the thematic road no. 0/620. But due to heavy rainfall and flooding during the 2019-20 and 2020-21 monsoons, the width of the river bed has increased, making the 3m gap inadequate for the actual site conditions. Therefore, according to the revised hydraulic calculations, it is necessary to build a bridge of 4 lengths of 7 m.
The Chief Engineer, Pune. As per the proposal submitted vide letter dated 08/06/2021, the matter of granting revised administrative approval to the said work was under consideration of the Government.
