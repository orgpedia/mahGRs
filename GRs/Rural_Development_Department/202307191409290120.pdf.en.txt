Government's decision:
In pursuance of the Government decision dated 20th January, 2014, dated 20th January, 2014, it has been decided to impart training to the Group A and Group B officers to be recruited directly from the State Service Examination by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under a combined probationary training programme. Accordingly, a district level (75+4) weeks training programme is going on under the Combined Subordinate Training Programme-7 of Subordinate Assistant Block Development Officers, Group-B of Maharashtra Development Service, Group-B recommended from the State Service Main Examination 2019. Under the said (75+4) weeks training, the probationary officers are required to be posted for 29 weeks from 21st July, 2023 onwards for training as Auxiliary Group Development Officer, Group-B.
