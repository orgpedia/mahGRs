A District Level Committee under the Chairmanship of District Collector has been constituted for the implementation of Sant Sewalal Maharaj Banjara / Laman Tanda Samriddhi Yojana for declaring Banjara / Laman Tanda habitations, declaring villages, proceeding to declare Tandaia as revenue village, setting up of Gram Panchayat, etc., and other ancillary activities. Considering this, the government was considering appointing non-governmental members of the Banjara / Laman / Lavan community to the committee headed by the Block Development Officer for a period of 3 years.
Government Decision - To implement Sant Sewalal Maharaj Banjara / Laman Tanda Samriddhi Yojana, Taluk Level Committees are being constituted under the Chairmanship of Block Development Officer to select non-official members of Banjara / Laman / Lavana community for a period of 3 years.
Taluk Level Committee: - (The list of Taluk and Taluk-wise non-Taluk Members is annexed in Annexure-A. )
a) Group Development Officer - Chairman
b) Representatives of Banjara / Laman / Lavana Samaj - Non-official members
a) Assistant Group Development Officer - Member Secretary

The mandate of the committee is: - To follow up with every Banjara / Laman / Lavana Tanda so that all necessary infrastructure facilities are available in the next 3 years.
Since the daily allowance and travel allowance payable to the non-governmental members of the said committee on the day of the meeting shall be paid from the 2 per cent of the fund admissible for the administrative expenses of the said scheme, the concerned non-governmental members shall submit the payments to the CEO, Zila Parishad, concerned district and the CEO, Zila Parishad shall take action regarding payment of daily allowance and travel allowance to the concerned non-governmental members as per Government rules.
