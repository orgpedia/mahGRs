Preamble: The Government of India has restructured the Rajiv Gandhi Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (RGSPA) scheme of the Government of India for strengthening the Panchayati Raj system and implemented the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) from 2018-19. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme which has been operationalized w.e.f. 1st April, 2018. It has been decided to implement the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (Rastriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan) in the State as per the decision of the Government vide No.Ragraswa-2018/ PR.No. 102 (Part II)/ Aastha-15 dated 20th February, 2019 of the Department.
