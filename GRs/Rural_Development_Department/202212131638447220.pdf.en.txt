Preamble: The Central Government has restructured the scheme of" Rajiv Gandhi Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan" for strengthening the Panchayati Raj system and implemented the scheme of" Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan" from 2018-19 to 2021-22. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments.
Under the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan Scheme for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. 7334.35 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Seven Crores Thirty Four Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand only) and Rs. 4889.53 Lakhs (Rupees Forty Eight Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Fifty Three Thousand Only) aggregating to Rs. 12223.88 Lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Two Crores Twenty Three Lakhs Eighty Eight Thousand only) has been released. However, at the time of disbursement of State's share, the funds in the first and third instalments have been disbursed in integer in thousands, taking the total State share to Rs. 3666.67/- has been received.
Central Government Reference No. 2 Since the aforesaid balance amount of the State Account has been intimated to be received vide letter No. As per the letter, the State Project Director, Ragraswa, Pune has requested to disburse the remaining amount of State share. Pursuant to the said request, the balance amount of Rs. 3666.00/- (Rupees Three Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Six only) was under consideration of the Government.

Government Decision: In the financial year 2021-22, the balance amount in the State Account for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) (60:40) is Rs. 3666.00/- (Rupees Three Thousand Sixty Six only) is being provided to the State Nodal Officer, State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune.
The funds to be disbursed to the State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune are being placed under the Divisional Commissioner's Office, Pune. Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner Office, Pune will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Deputy Commissioner (Establishment) shall disburse the grant to the concerned State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune. The State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune shall disburse the said funds to the Zila Parishads as per the approved Annual Action Plan.
The State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune and all the Chief Executive Officers and Zilla Parishads should ensure that the expenditure from the grants sanctioned by this Government decision is done with utmost care. The funds provided to the State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune for this scheme are as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the said Reference No. 4 As per the instructions in the letter of the Central Government here, the concerned should be distributed and the expenditure should be controlled by ensuring that the funds distributed are properly used. After reviewing the expenditure incurred for the purpose of achieving the fixed target, a report should be sent to the government immediately and the utilization certificate of the expenditure should also be submitted.
The State Nodal Officer, State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune shall have the powers to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure made by the Accountant General (Audit), Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General (Audit), Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and the concerned office shall extend full cooperation to enable them to carry out their work in a proper manner.
