Introduction: The Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana has been launched in the state of Maharashtra as per the decision of the government dated October 28, 2015 to connect the unconnected villages and improve the quality of roads in rural areas. The government was considering appointing a retired executive engineer on contract basis for the implementation of the scheme and for the effective implementation of the work of the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency.
For effective implementation of the work of Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency, as per the decision of the Selection Committee, considering the educational qualification, work experience, technical knowledge and physical ability required for the post of Retired Executive Engineer, the following retired Executive Engineer is being appointed on contract basis for a period of 11 months from the date of his appearance as Executive Engineer on contract basis for the Chief Minister/ Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana. The concerned should reach the place of appointment immediately.

The duties and responsibilities of the retired Executive Engineer during the period of appointment to the post of Contractual shall be as follows:
(1) To assist the retired Executive Engineer in discharging the duties of the Head of the Office in the office concerned and also to follow the instructions given by the Senior Office as well as the Superintending Engineer from time to time.
(ii) To co-ordinate with all the Executive Engineers/ Deputy Engineers and officers of other offices under the Regional Divisional Office.
(3) To deal with all correspondence received by the office concerned with the approval of the head of the office.
4) To thoroughly examine the technical aspects, detailed project reports, budgets and other technical proposals received at the administrative department/ divisional office level.
5) To examine in detail the draft tenders, all payments of works, tariff increases, extension proposals, etc. received from all the executive engineers in the regional department.
(6) To perform the work assigned by the Ministerial Officers, Chief Engineers, Superintending Engineers and other senior officers under the Department from time to time, and to submit periodic reports to the concerned office within the prescribed time.
7) Submission of physical and financial reports of the scheme from the regional division level, other periodicals related to the establishment with the approval of the Superintending Engineer.
8) To carry out the tasks assigned by the government from time to time.
9) Collecting information about meetings at different levels, attending meetings, etc.
10) Work at the regional level, where necessary.
11) To inspect the works under the Chief Minister/ Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) on the orders of the administrative department of the Ministry/ Chief Engineer/ State Quality Coordinator and submit the inspection report.
12. Retired Executive Engineer posted at the Mumbai Headquarter of Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency to complete the works assigned by the Secretary (MD& CEO) and Financial Controller and Deputy Secretary from time to time as per the prevailing policy.

03. Retired Executive Engineer to be transferred to General Administration Department with the above provision. The following terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 17/12/2016 shall also apply.
(1) No person shall be entitled to be included in any cadre of the Government concerned or to receive any other benefit of regular service on account of being appointed on contract.
The retired officer/ employee should be physically, mentally and health-wise competent and should have the requisite competence for the proposed service.
The appointing authority shall have the power to terminate the contractual services of such officers at any time under special circumstances.
(4) No departmental inquiry shall be initiated or proposed to be initiated against the officer concerned.
The person appointed under the contract shall not engage in any commercial activity which may cause disruption in the performance of the services rendered.
The person appointed under the contract will be required to declare the conflict of interest.
Contractual appointees shall be required to maintain confidentiality of documents/ information and supporting materials received by them.
The persons appointed under the contract shall be required to complete the work assigned to them within the stipulated time. The appointing authority will periodically review and evaluate their performance.
The Chief Engineer, Chief Minister Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana will be the Controlling Officer.
The retired Executive Engineer shall submit information/ proposals/ reports in respect of the above activities to the Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer, Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana from time to time.
Remuneration and other allowances/ facilities of retired Deputy Engineer for a period of 11 months. It will be determined in accordance with the Governing Decision dated 17/12/2016.
