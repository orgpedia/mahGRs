The Proposal:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1, it has been made mandatory to adopt e-tendering process for procurement of services and goods required for various developmental works and development works in the Panchayati Raj Institutions with effect from 01.01.2012. The revised procedure and time period of notification of tender under e-tendering system has been prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference No. 2 and 3.
Due to the Covid-19 pandemic and the fact that the works of public interest are scheduled to be completed before the monsoon season, the work of roads under the Rural Development Department, the repair work of bridges (excluding the works of the Prime Minister and Chief Minister's Gram Sadak Yojana under the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency) and various other development works of public interest need to be completed immediately and on time.

In order to expedite various development works of the Council, private funds, pilgrimage sites, 2515 basic amenities and other public interests, the Department of Rural Development has issued a notification dated September 23, 2021, in this regard.
The time limit prescribed for inviting the tender in paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Resolution, 2013 and in paragraph 1 of the Government Resolution, dated 26th July, 2016, vide Circular No.4 of the above-mentioned Notes dated 13th January, 2021, was extended to 31st March, 2021 by fixing a short period as per the tender price of the works. However, vide Circular No.4 dated 13th January, 2021, the period of short notice under e-tendering system is hereby extended up to 31st May, 2021.
The circular shall be brought to the notice of all the Chief Executive Officers and the Heads of Departments under them to ensure that the above works are completed within the deadline. With effect from 01.06.2021, the above relaxations shall stand automatically withdrawn and the provisions of the relevant Government Decisions dated 23.09.2013 and 26.07.2016 shall come into force again.
