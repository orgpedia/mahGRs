The functions of the Committee constituted as aforesaid shall be as follows:
(i) A separate plan of the available space in accordance with the approved configuration in the A and B class Panchayat Samitis should be submitted to the Government along with the recommendation after determining the budgetary cost required for the same.
(ii) To submit to the Government the feasibility of the maximum permissible area of construction of the new administrative building.
(iii) Considering the policy of reduction in government expenditure, the model plan should be prepared according to the area mentioned in paragraph 4 and submitted to the government with a recommendation.
(iv) To recommend the model plan generally in accordance with the pattern of the District Councils by fixing the necessary budgetary cost for the same.
(v) To submit the model plan for the A and B class Panchayat Samitis to the Government with recommendations within two months.
