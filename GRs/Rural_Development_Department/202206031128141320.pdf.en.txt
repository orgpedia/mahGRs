Reference:- Government Circular No. 10/2020/ PR No.33/ Aastha-3A, dated 27.05.2021 Government Circular: The final seniority list of officers of Maharashtra Vikas Seva Group-B from 1.1.2010 to 31.12.2020 has been published on the website (computer system) of Government of Maharashtra vide Government Circular dated 27.05.2021. However, the list of seniority was yet to include the officers who had joined directly from the State Service Examination-2016 (CPTP-4A), State Service Examination-2017 (CPTP-5) and State Service Examination-2018 (CPTP-6). The provisional seniority list for the period from 01.01.2018 to 31.12.2021 is being published on the basis of the information received from all the Divisional Commissioners and on the completion of the probationary period of the above officers provided by the General Administration Department.
2. While declaring the merits of the above period of service, the officers who have come into Group-B cadre by regular promotion and direct entry into service. Also, officers who have been promoted on ad-hoc basis have been considered as officers who have received regular date by adjustment as per vacancies in the respective department as per the point list. Seniority of officers working on ad-hoc basis has not been considered. The officers who have not passed the departmental examination within the prescribed time and within the prescribed opportunity have been deprived of their service. Thus, in the event of loss of service, the said officers have been assigned Group-B as per the date on which they passed the examination or as per the rules.
The inclusion of the candidates awarded by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the Interim Seniority List for the years 2016, 2017 and 2018 has been made subject to regularization of their appointment after expiry of the period of probation. The officers recommended by the Commission and who are currently undergoing training but have not yet been appointed to the post of Maharashtra Development Service, Group-B, will be included in the seniority list in due course. Also, 11 officers who were appointed as Superintendents as per the Government Decision dated 04.12.2020 have not been included in the seniority list.
