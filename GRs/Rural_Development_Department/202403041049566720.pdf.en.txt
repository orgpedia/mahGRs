A proposal for inclusion in the Development Plan Year 2001-21 has been submitted to the government. The proposal states that land acquisition is not required for the sought-after road and that the road does not pass through forest land. The Zila Parishad, Solapur has also demanded that the road be included in the road development plan as a rural route.
Accordingly, considering the use of this road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Solapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade this road as a rural road.
Government Decision: - Taking into consideration the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the Government is approving the inclusion of the following road in Sangola Taluka of Solapur District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan Year 2001-21.
