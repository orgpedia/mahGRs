GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Central Government has released the second installment of the 15th Central Finance Commission (CFC) Untied Grants for the financial year 2021-22 amounting to Rs. 861.40 crore is being made available for disbursement to Rural Local Bodies (Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat at all three levels) in the State of Maharashtra under the head Accounts (25152628/ 25152646/25152664) respectively as shown in the accompanying prospectus (Form-A). The funds are being disbursed through the Budget Distribution System (BIMS) to the CEOs of all the Zila Parishads as per the prospectus. The funds are being distributed to all the Panchayati Raj Institutions in the ratio of 10: 10: 80 in Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat respectively.
