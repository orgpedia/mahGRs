The Proposal:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1, it has been made mandatory to adopt e-tendering process for procurement of goods and services required for various development works and development works in Panchayati Raj Institutions with effect from 01.01.2012. The revised procedure and time period of notification of tender under e-tendering system has been prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference No. 2 and 3.
Due to the Covid-19 pandemic and the fact that the works of public interest are scheduled to be completed before the monsoon season, the work of roads under the Rural Development Department, the repair work of bridges (excluding the works of the Prime Minister and Chief Minister's Gram Sadak Yojana under the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency) and various other development works of public interest need to be completed immediately and on time.

Government circular:
In order to expedite the works of road works and bridge repair works under Panchayat Raj Institutions (excluding works under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana and Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana under Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency), Zilla Parishad Self-Fund, Shrine, 2515 basic amenities and various other development works of public interest, the time limit prescribed for inviting the said tender in Paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2013 and Paragraph 1 1 of the Government Decision dated 26th July, 2016 of the Department of Rural Development in the said context, vide Circular dated 8th April, 2021, read with Annexure No.5 above, was approved till 31st May, 2021 by fixing a short period as per the tender price of the works. However, vide Circular No.5 dated 8th April, 2021, the period of short notice under e-tendering system is hereby extended up to 31st July, 2021.
The circular shall be brought to the notice of all the Chief Executive Officers and the Heads of Departments under them to ensure that the above works are completed within the deadline. With effect from 01.08.2021, the above relaxations shall stand automatically withdrawn and the provisions of the relevant Government Decisions dated 23.09.2013 and 26.07.2016 shall come into force again.
