The reports are scrutinized and compiled by the State Quality Coordinator (SQC). The post of Senior State Quality Monitor (SSQM) has been created for quality inspection of roads under Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana on the lines of the post of National Quality Monitor (NQM) for quality inspection of roads for Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana. The age limit for the post of National Quality Inspector was 67 years and for the post of Senior State Quality Inspector was 70 years. The State Quality Coordinator, Pramangrao, Pune has recommended raising the age limit of Senior State Quality Inspector to 67 years on the lines of National Quality Inspector. Accordingly, the matter of changing the age limit of Senior State Quality Inspector (SSQM) and State Quality Inspector-Grade-2 (SQM Gr-II) was under the consideration of the government.
Government circular:
The State Quality Coordinator, Pune, vide Reference No. 3 In view of the reasons stated in the letter, the age limit for the post of Senior State Quality Monitor (SSQM) is being fixed at 67 years as against the National Quality Monitor (NQM). The age limit of State Quality Monitoring Grade-II (SQM Gr-II) has also been increased to 67 years.
