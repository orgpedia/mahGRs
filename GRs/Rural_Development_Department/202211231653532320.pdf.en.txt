Introduction: The Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana has been launched in the state of Maharashtra under the Government decision dated October 28, 2015 to connect the unconnected villages and improve the condition of roads in rural areas. The government was considering the creation of a new accounting head for providing interest-free loans to the states under the special assistance scheme for capital expenditure by the central government for the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana.
Government Decision: For the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana, the Central Government is giving approval to the States to create the following head of accounts for non-interest loans under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment.
