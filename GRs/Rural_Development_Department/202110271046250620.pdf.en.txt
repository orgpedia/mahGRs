GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MEMORIAL-2016/ PR. NO. 03/ PR. 9, dated 11th August, 2016 GOVERNMENT DECISION: MAUJE-SONOSHI, BADGICHIMACHI, TATA. The government has approved the formation of a district-level committee under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Nashik, to recommend for approval the plan of construction of a memorial for revolutionary Raghoji Bhangare in Igatpuri, Nashik district.
