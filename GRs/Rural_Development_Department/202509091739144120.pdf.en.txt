Date 31.03.2022 of the month. In the cabinet meetings, instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development System-Administration Scheme, it has been decided by the state government to continue as a 100% state scheme for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027.
Also, in these meetings, 8 posts have been fixed for the District Rural Development Mechanism-Administration Scheme with effect from 01.04.2022 instead of the earlier 15 posts and it has been decided to certify the services of the officers / staff of the remaining posts at their original establishment.
A government decision in this regard has been issued on the date 01.04.2022. Accordingly, a proposal to extend the sanctioned posts for District Rural Development System-Administration Scheme from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026 was under consideration of the Government.
The Government's Decisions:
Subject to the powers conferred on the administrative departments by the Government Decision of the Department of Finance dated 26th August, 2025, a total of 272 posts of 8 posts for each District Rural Development System as mentioned in Annexure-A have been created with effect from the date 01.09.2025.
