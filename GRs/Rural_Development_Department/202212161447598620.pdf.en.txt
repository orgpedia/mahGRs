The proposal-
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 31.03.2022 decided that instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development Agency Administration Scheme, the State Government should continue the scheme as 100% State Scheme for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027. For District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme, 8 posts have been earmarked against 15 posts with effect from 01.04.2022 and it has been decided to repatriate the services of the remaining officers/ staff to their parent establishment.
The Central share received from the Government of India for the year 2021-22 for payment of salaries and allowances to the officers/ employees of the District Rural Development Agency is Rs.
6,07,89, 000/- (Rupees Six Crore Seven Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand only) and corresponding State share of 40% Rs. 4,05,26, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Five Lakhs Twenty Six Thousand only) aggregating to Rs. 10,13,15, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Thirteen Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only) Refer No. 4 and Rs. 9,50,00, 000/- (Rs. 9 crore only). By the Government Decision of 2022-23, the District Rural Development Agency (DRDA) has been allocated as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the payment of salaries and allowances of officers and employees in the administration.
