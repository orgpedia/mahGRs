The Proposal:
General Administration Department Reference No. 3 here. Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 03.03.2023
The upper age limit for candidates applying for all advertisements for direct recruitment in government service up to December 31, 2023 has been relaxed by two years.
Further, the Rural Development Department vide reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 21.10.2022, the candidates who have applied/ filled the application form earlier as per the advertisement dated March, 2019, and who are presently over the age, are not eligible to appear in the examination and such candidates should not be harmed, in order to accept the applications of such candidates and appear in the next examination only.
In the meantime, representations have been received from some public representatives. Through these representations, it has been demanded that due to various reasons (Corona outbreak, ban on recruitment, not enough advertisements being published, candidates not getting a chance to appear in the examination, candidates being ineligible for induction into service due to their age, etc.)

The age limit is over. Therefore, the government should take a positive decision on giving relaxation in the upper age limit.
In view of the above, considering the provisions of the Government decision issued earlier, the maximum age limit for direct recruitment to the Zila Parishad service has been increased from Rs. The government was considering giving relaxation till December 31, 2023.
Government's decision:
Considering the pros and cons of the reasons mentioned in the introduction, the following suggestions are being given by this government decision.
(1) From the date of this resolution. The upper age limit is relaxed by two years for candidates applying for all the advertisements for direct recruitment in Zila Parishad services till December 31, 2023.
2) Candidates who have applied/ filled up earlier as per the advertisement dated March, 2019, and are presently over the age, shall not be eligible to appear in the examination and such candidates shall not be disadvantaged. The candidates will be eligible to apply for all the advertisements to be published regarding direct recruitment in Zila Parishad service till December 31, 2023.
(3) In the recruitment of 10 percent posts in the Zilla Parishad Service from the Gram Panchayat employees, the Gram Panchayat employees who were eligible as per the advertisement of March, 2019 (i.e., the age of the candidates who were not more than 45 years as on January 1, 2019) and are now ineligible as they have crossed the maximum age limit, all such candidates will be eligible. Candidates will be eligible for the recruitment process to be held till December 31, 2023.
(4) For all such advertisements/ recruitment processes where the advertisement/ recruitment process has been started before the date of the said Government decision but the selection process has not yet been completed and the appointments have not been made, the same shall be notified in paragraph (a) of paragraph (b) above. Relaxation in upper age limit will be applicable as per 1, 2 and 3.
