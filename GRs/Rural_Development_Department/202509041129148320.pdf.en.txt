All Zila Parishads, Panchayat Samitis and Gram Panchayat Offices have been asked to submit compliance reports with respect to pending paragraphs made to the Accountant General's Office within the prescribed time frame as follows:
Suggestions are as follows: -
(1) The offices of the Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat are audited from time to time by the Accountant General (Audit) Mumbai / Nagpur in accordance with this audit.
All necessary information and documents should be made available to the Accountant General (Audit) Mumbai / Nagpur.
The Accountant General (Audit) Mumbai / Nagpur is fully responsible for processing the compliance reports on all paragraphs submitted from time to time during the audit within the prescribed time frame by the respective Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat.
The head of the department in the office will be the CEO / Chief Accounts and Finance Officer in respect of Zila Parishads.
Also, the compliance report on all paragraphs submitted by the Accountant General (Audit) Mumbai / Nagpur from time to time during the audit shall be processed within the prescribed time frame and the compliance report shall be submitted to the Accountant General (Audit) Mumbai / Nagpur office and the report thereon shall be furnished to the Accountant General (Audit) Mumbai / Nagpur during each quarter along with the attached statement "a" and "b."
The entire responsibility of submission in the respective Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat.
