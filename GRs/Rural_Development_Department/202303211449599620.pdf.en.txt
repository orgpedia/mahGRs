Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) is being implemented in the State of Maharashtra. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. Accordingly, on the utilization of funds received under the scheme, the Government was considering opening of new subordinate accounts for Central and State accounts as per the instructions of the Central Government.
