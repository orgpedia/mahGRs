The Proposal:
During the Corona pandemic, while performing duties related to survey, detection, tracking, prevention, testing, treatment and relief work, various categories of employees in various departments of the government are coming in close contact with the corona virus. The government is committed to take care of such employees and is determined to stand firmly behind them in this unsafe situation.
The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Govt. As per the order dated 28.3.2020, insurance cover scheme for health workers has been implemented in this regard.
The Department of Rural Development had implemented insurance cover to certain categories of employees working at Gram Panchayat level under Government Circular dated 8th July, 2020 to prevent the spread of Corona Virus. 50 lakh has been provided to the next of kin of the employees who died due to Corona virus infection while on duty after 30th September, 2020. The government was considering providing insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance of Rs 50 lakh. The government has taken the following decisions:

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Officers/ staff working in the establishment of Zila Parishads under the Rural Development Department, Gram Panchayat employees, computer operators and contractual employees/ workers working in local bodies are performing Covid related duties in large numbers. With a view to support such personnel who are on active duty to fight the global pandemic of COVID 19 and to stand by their families in case of unfortunate death of such personnel in the line of Corona duty, Rs. Insurance cover of Rs 50 lakh vide Government Circular No. IV-2020/ P. No.42/ Finance-4D As of July 8, 2020. Available until September 30, 2020.
The Government is approving a proposal to provide insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance of Rs. 50 lakh to the families of the officers/ employees working in the establishments of Zilla Parishads under the Rural Development Department, Gram Panchayat employees, computer operators and contractual employees/ workers working in the local bodies who died due to Corona infection till June 30, 2021.
2) In all cases of death of employees, Rs. 50 lakh will be provided subject to the following conditions:
(i) Copy of the order of the competent authority regarding appointment to Covid-related duties.
The employee must have been on duty for 14 days prior to the date of his hospitalization or death. This will be verified by the Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad or any other designated Head of Department.
3.Sadar deaths will be reported on the basis of the medical certificate related to Covid-19, reports received from government hospitals, district hospitals, municipal hospitals, private hospitals/ laboratories registered with ICMR.
4. All the officers/ employees working under the Zila Parishad, Gram Panchayat employees, computer operators and contractual employees/ workers working in local bodies i.e. their salary and its subsidy is given by the Rural Development Department. The proposals of the officers and employees whose salary and allowances are paid from the subsidy of the salary and allowances received from other departments should not be sent to the Rural Development Department. Regarding their insurance cover assistance and subsidy, the concerned department should take a decision after taking details from the district council.
