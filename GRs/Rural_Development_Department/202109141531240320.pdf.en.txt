The proposal:
The quality of education in primary and secondary schools of Zilla Parishad in Maharashtra has improved significantly in the last few years, which is having a positive effect on the students of Zilla Parishad schools. Most of the Zilla Parishad schools in Maharashtra are affiliated to ISO.
It has also received a rating. Improving the quality of education in Zilla Parishad schools, educating students with all the technologies of the world are also necessary in the era of globalization.
All the schools under the Delhi Government's Delhi Corporation have undergone major changes and improvements over time to improve the academic quality. A study team of experts was being sent to Delhi to study in detail the technology used to teach students in these schools, the facilities for students and teachers, the conduct and discipline of students and the teaching methodology of teachers.
Government's decision:
The Delhi government is setting up a study group of experts to study in depth the technology used to teach students, facilities for students and teachers, the conduct and discipline of students, and the teaching methodology of teachers in all schools under the Delhi Corporation.
