The Proposal:
The School Education and Sports Department has fixed the criteria of basic facilities like classrooms, separate toilets for boys and girls, drinking water facility, school special repair, protection wall, playground, ramp, kitchen etc. for all schools in the state. Also, under the Samagra Shiksha/ Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan scheme, basic physical facilities are provided to Zilla Parishad schools. Under this scheme, the construction and improvement of the Zilla Parishad Primary School building, maintenance and repair works have been included. The scheme for providing grants for the construction and repair of Zilla Parishad schools in the Rural Development Department is not working. Therefore, the construction/ building special repair/ reconstruction of Zilla Parishad schools under all schemes such as District Development Plan (DPDC), Finance Commission, Sports Scheme, Mining, CRC Fund, MP Local Development Program and MLA Local Development Program should be entrusted to an engineer working under Samagra Shiksha.
Government Decision: The funds sanctioned by the Central Government as well as the State Government under various schemes for all types of construction in all school subjects in the State are required to be sanctioned to the schools as per the available information in accordance with UDISE Plus by setting priorities so that unnecessary expenditure is not incurred. Further, in order to ensure that the engineers working under Samagra Shiksha are given the responsibility to complete the construction works as per the prescribed procedure and to ensure that the construction works are completed within the stipulated time, all the District Collectors/ CEOs/ Zilla Parishads have been instructed in the past by the State Project Director, Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Mumbai and Additional Chief Secretary (School Education) vide their letter dated 13.12.2019.

As on 29.6.2013, the norms for basic amenities like classrooms, separate toilets for boys/ girls, school special repairs, drinking water facilities, retaining walls, playgrounds, ramp, washrooms etc. have been prescribed for all schools in the State.
3. Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution, No. SMA-2615/ No.61/ SM-1, D. Under the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan Scheme, as on 9.3.2015, basic physical facilities are provided to Zilla Parishad schools. Under this scheme, the construction and improvement of the Zilla Parishad Primary School building, maintenance and repair works have been included.
4. General Administration Department, Circular No: DDS-1063-D, dated 24.4.1963 has issued instructions in accordance with the responsibilities of the affairs and ministerial departments of the Zila Parishad.
Accordingly, it has been clearly instructed that even if the schemes of the administrative department of the ministry are transferred to the district council for implementation, their regulation, financial management, fund distribution staff, all these matters should be handled by the administrative department of the concerned ministry and the starred questions, deduction suggestions, attention etc. in this regard should also be handled by the same department. The Maharashtra State Primary Education Council, Mumbai has the overall responsibility and control over the legislative affairs in respect of Zilla Parishad Primary School Building Construction and Improvement, Maintenance and Repair etc.
The scheme for providing physical facilities to Zilla Parishad Shallas and providing funds for construction and repair of Zilla Parishad Schools under the administrative department of the Ministry of Rural Development is not working.
There are a large number of vacant posts of engineer in the Zilla Parishad Construction Department. The Finance Department has imposed restrictions on the creation of new construction divisions/ sub-divisions in the Zilla Parishad Construction Department and accordingly sanctioning of new posts of engineers. Therefore, it is not possible to maintain uniformity in the maintenance/ repair and construction of Zilla Parishad school buildings through the Zilla Parishad Construction Department.
The responsibility of supervising the construction of new construction/ maintenance/ repair works of Zilla Parishad schools, special repair of Zilla Parishad schools, protection wall, playground, ramp, kitchen, etc. under all the schemes of the Central and State Governments such as District Development Plan (DPDC), Finance Commission, Sports Scheme, Mining, CRC Fund, MP Local Area Development Programme and MLA Local Area Development Programme, preparing the budgets and carrying out the construction works with technical approval, is being entrusted to the Junior Engineer, Deputy Engineer and Executive Engineer (Samagra) working under Samagra Shiksha.
