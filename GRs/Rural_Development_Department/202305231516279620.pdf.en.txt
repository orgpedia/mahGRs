Adoption is mandated by the Government Decision at Reference No. 1. The revised procedure and time period of notification of tender under e-tendering system has been prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference No. 2 and 3. With a view to expedite the works of road works and bridge repair works under Panchayat Raj Institutions, (excluding the works under Pradhan Mantri and Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana under Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency), Zilla Parishad Self-fund, Shrine, 2515 basic amenities and various other development works of public interest, and considering the possibility of holding elections to Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat in 2022-23, the time limit fixed for calling of tenders in paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2013 and paragraph 1 of the Government Decision dated 26th July, 2016 of the Rural Development Department in the said context, vide Circular No. Public Works Department No. As there is a one-month time left for the start of the monsoon season, as per the decision of the government here, the deadline for issuing e-tenders has been fixed in view of the pending tender process of the approved works under various headings for the year 2022-23. Keeping this in view, the matter of fixing the period of publication of e-tender for the Village Development Department was under consideration.
Accordingly, the period for issue of short-term e-tendering notices under the e-tendering system vide Circular No.13 dated 22nd February, 2023, read with the above, shall be upto 30th June, 2023.

The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad should bring the works under the Rural Infrastructure (2515 1238) suggested by all the heads of departments and people's representatives under them to the attention of the agencies where the machinery under the Public Works Board is working and ensure that the above works are completed within the deadline.
With effect from 01.07.2023, the above relaxations shall stand automatically withdrawn and the provisions of the relevant Government Decisions dated 23.09.2013 and 26.07.2016 shall come into force with immediate effect.
