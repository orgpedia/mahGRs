The Proposal:
As per the Government Order dated 15.09.2021, dated 15.09.2021, for the implementation of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana and effective implementation of the work of Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency, the temporary appointment of retired Executive Engineer on contract basis was done on contract basis for a period of 11 months. Among the retired executive engineers, Sadiq Shikalgar, a retired executive engineer, was under the consideration of the government for temporary appointment on contract basis.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per the order of the Department of Rural Development at serial number 5, the period of appointment of retired executive engineers on contract basis has ended.
Accordingly, subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Order dated 17th December, 2016 of the General Administration Department and the Government Order dated 15th September, 2021 of the Rural Development Department, the Government is approving the appointment of the following retired Executive Engineers on contract basis for a period of 11 months from the date mentioned in column No. 4 before their names as retired Executive Engineers on contract basis by giving technical break in their appointment on contract basis.
