The Proposal:
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1, it has been made mandatory to adopt e-tendering process for procurement of services and goods required for various developmental works and development works in the Panchayati Raj Institutions with effect from 01.01.2012. The revised procedure and time period of notification of tender under e-tendering system has been prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference No. 2 and 3.
In view of the Covid-19 pandemic and the prevailing situation in some districts of the state, the work of public interest is in order to be completed immediately, therefore, the work of roads under the Rural Development Department, the repair work of bridges (excluding the work of the Prime Minister and Chief Minister Gram Sadak Yojana under the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency) and various other development works of public interest need to be completed immediately and on time.

Government circular:
In order to expedite the work of road works and bridge repair works under Panchayat Raj Institutions, (excluding the works under the Prime Minister and Chief Minister Gram Sadak Yojana under the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency), Zilla Parishad Self-fund, pilgrimage sites, 2515 basic amenities and various other development works of public interest, the Department of Rural Development has issued a notification dated 23rd September, 2020 in this regard.
The time limit prescribed for inviting the tender under paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Resolution, 2013 and paragraph 1 of the Government Resolution, dated 26th July, 2016, vide Circular No.6 of the above mentioned Notes dated 09th June, 2021, was extended to 31st July, 2021 by fixing a short period as per the tender price of the works. However, vide Circular No.6 dated 09.06.2021, the time limit for submission of Short Term Tender Notice under e-tendering system is hereby extended upto 31.10.2021.
The circular shall be brought to the notice of all the Chief Executive Officers and the Heads of Departments under them to ensure that the above works are completed within the deadline. With effect from 1st November, 2021, the above relaxations shall stand automatically cancelled and the provisions of the relevant Government Decisions dated 23rd September, 2013 and dated 26th July, 2016 in this regard shall come into force again.
