Introduction:-
Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana is a scheme launched by Government
of India for connectivity of Rural Habitations of + 500 populations (+250
populations of schedule-V areas). The Project Proposals for Road works for
up-gradation was submitted for approval of Empowered Committee of Ministry of
Rural Development, New Delhi. It was discussed in the Empowered Committee
and the Empowered Committee has given approval to the Project Proposals.
Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India accorded approval for the
proposals of roads vide letter No.P-17024/15/2020-RC (FMS No. 371917),
Dated 12th September, 2024 with various condition.
Government Resolution:-
The Project Proposals of PMGSY-III, Batch-I of 2024-25 has been cleared
by the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India vide letter dated

12th September, 2024. Accordingly the Government of Maharashtra is pleased to
accord Administrative Approval for the 117 road works of 745.286 Km length.
The Government of Maharashtra is also pleased to accord sanction for proportional
State share.
