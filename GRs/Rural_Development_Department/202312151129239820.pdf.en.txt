Introduction: The villagers have to face many problems due to the lack of basic facilities within the village in the state. Therefore, Ma. People are demanding funds for basic amenities. The government is considering this matter. Providing basic amenities in rural areas suggested by the people's representatives (Head of Accounts 2515 1238) The scheme was launched in 2008-09. Also read this. The revised policy on the criteria and methodology of the scheme has been decided under the Government Decision No. 2. It has been noticed that the sanctioned works under the scheme are not completed within the stipulated time. People are deprived of basic facilities as the government has not completed the development works within the stipulated time. Therefore, the government was considering the formation of a committee to regularly review the progress of development works in rural areas approved under this scheme.
Government Circular: As mentioned in the above introduction, the works approved by the government under the scheme Title 2515 1238, which are to be done by the Zilla Parishad or the Public Works Department. Committees have been formed at district and taluk levels.

Action to be taken by the District Committee:
(1) Regular review of administrative approvals received from the Government.
(2) The works sanctioned under head of accounts 2515 1238 are mainly small in nature and are completed in a short period of time. Setting time-bound programmes for technical approval of works, implementation of tender process and completion of works in time.
(3) To inspect the works on a regular basis to ensure that the quality of works is met.
(4) The Committee shall meet on a monthly basis and review the work of the Committee.

Action to be taken by Taluk Level Committee:
(1) To resolve any difficulty in obtaining No-Objection Certificate or any other difficulty for further proceedings of the sanctioned works.
(2) Coordination between the Gram Panchayat and the implementing agency.
(3) To inspect the works on a regular basis to ensure that the quality of works is met.
(4) The Committee shall meet on a monthly basis and review the work of the Committee.
