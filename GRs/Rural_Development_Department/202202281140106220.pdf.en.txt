The Maharashtra Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) and the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana (CMGSY) are long-term and ambitious schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency (MRRDA) under the Rural Development Department. Under the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II, a target has been set to upgrade 10,000 km of roads. The Central Government has set a target of upgrading 6550 km of roads in the state of Maharashtra under Pramangrasyo Phase-III in all the districts under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana. The aim is to improve the quality of roads. In line with this program, the work of creating DRRP and Core Network, Facility Survey, PCI Survey, as well as field preparation for the implementation of E-Marg Software, etc. has been completed and the tender process for the approved works is underway on a large scale. Experienced and competent officers must be employed in the districts for all these works.
