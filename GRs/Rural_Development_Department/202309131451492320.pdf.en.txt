Preamble: Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal As per the letter dated 12.09.2022, the Panchayat Committee owned by Yavatmal Zilla Parishad, Government of Bombay State, in the name of Headmaster Government Middle School, in Vani City, is registered in the Revenue Records as Land Measurement No. 43/2 Area 2.43 H.R. Government land is requested to be transferred to District Sports Officer, Yavatmal for construction of Taluk Sports Complex.
On this proposal, the Revenue and Forest Department should take action to first hand over the proposed land to the Revenue Department and then the department which needs the land should demand the land by following the prescribed procedure. If a proposal is received from the Revenue and Forest Department for such a land, an appropriate decision will be taken on the proposal as per the rules. " This is how it is supposed to be done. Hence, the government should take steps in this regard.
Government Decision: The Panchayat Committee owned by the Yavatmal Zilla Parishad, the Government of Bombay State in Vani, the Headmaster Government Secondary School in the name of the land recorded in the Revenue Records No. The Government hereby approves the transfer of Government land in 43/2 Area 2.43 Ha to Revenue and Forest Department for transfer to District Sports Officer, Yavatmal for the purpose of construction of Taluk Sports Complex.
