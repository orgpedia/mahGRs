Government Resolution, Rural Development Department, No: Mugrayo-2021/ PR No.165/ Construction-4, dated 06.01.2022 Government Circular:
The policy decision to launch Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II for the upgradation of dilapidated roads in rural areas on the lines of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana in the State has been taken as per the Government decision dated 06.01.2022.
For the implementation of the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2 in the state, the government is approving the appointment of the following committees under the chairmanship of the Superintending Engineer (PRM) of the concerned department, Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency, to prepare the core network of roads, conduct PCI survey and determine the priority of roads, examine the detailed project report, draft tenders, etc.

The functions of the Committee constituted as aforesaid shall be as follows:
To prepare a core network of roads, conduct PCI survey and prioritize roads, examine detailed project reports, draft tenders, etc. for the implementation of Mukhya Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II.
Submission of proposals/ information prepared by the district-wise committee in connection with the Chief Minister's Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2 to the government/ competent committee for further action.
For the implementation of the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2, all the documents required or demanded for preparing the core network of roads, PCI survey and determining the priority of roads, detailed project report, draft tender, etc. should be made available to the committee immediately. The State Quality Inspectors will continue to be paid honorarium and other allowances at the prevailing rates.
For the implementation of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II, the committee formed as above to prepare the core network of roads, PCI survey and to determine the priority of roads, to examine the detailed project report, draft tender, etc., will be automatically terminated after the implementation period of this scheme.
