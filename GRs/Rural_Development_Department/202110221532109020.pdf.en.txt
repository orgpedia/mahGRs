Government's decision:
A total amount of Rs. 15000.00 lakh has been provided in the budget for the construction of administrative buildings of Zila Parishads and Panchayat Samitis. 4500.00 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System. Out of this amount, Rs. 50.00 lakhs to the Soygaon Panchayat Samiti (G. The new administrative building at Aurangabad) is proposed to be delivered after the technical approval for construction is granted and the remaining amount of Rs. 4450.00 lakhs is left for disbursement.
Subject to the prescribed limits of the powers of the administrative departments to disburse funds, as mentioned in the circular dated 06.04.2021 and the Government Resolution dated 24.06.2021, the budgetary allocation for the construction/ upkeep of administrative buildings of Zila Parishads and Panchayat Samitis has been increased from Rs. 4500.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 4450.00 lakhs (Rupees Forty Four Crores Fifty Lakhs only) in the Cash Flow for the month of October, 2021, to be placed at the disposal of the respective CEOs of Zila Parishads as follows:
