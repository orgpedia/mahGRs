The Divisional Commissioner's office shall ensure that the actual amount of subsidy on salary/ non-salary basis is ascertained while disbursing the subsidy to the training centre and the required amount is made available to them. It should be ensured that the subsidy is not disbursed in excess of the subsidy mentioned above. Also, the training center should give prior information to the concerned commissioner's office about how much subsidy is required per month for salary/ non-salary. Accordingly, the Commissioner's office should disburse the grant for salary/ non-salary. In case of non-payment of prior intimation regarding the required grant, the entire responsibility will be of the Principal of the concerned centre.
7. The Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and/ or the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur shall be empowered to examine the separate accounts and books of expenditure incurred by the respective training centres from this grant and shall be assisted/ facilitated by the respective training centres/ institutions to enable them to carry out their work in a proper manner.
