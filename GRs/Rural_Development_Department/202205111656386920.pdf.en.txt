Accordingly, the District Rural Development Agencies have been restructured and provided with improved manpower.
The District Rural Development Agencies (DRDAs) are implementing the Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Sponsored Schemes like Ramai Awas Yojana, Shabari Awas Yojana, Pardhi Awas Yojana, Adim Awas Yojana, Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana, Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasahat Yojana, etc.
As per the instructions of the Central Government, a State Level Direction Committee has been directed to be formed to review and control the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the State. In this regard, the State Level Steering Committee The committee will be formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister and will review various schemes of the central government at the state level. In this regard, various central schemes will be controlled at the state level and accordingly, there is a need to implement these schemes under one umbrella at the district level as well.
Reference No. The government has decided to reorganize and strengthen the district rural development agencies in the state under the Amrit Mahotsav of independence.
Accordingly, in addition to the existing schemes implemented through the District Rural Development Agencies, various schemes of the Central and State Governments will be implemented at the district level through the District Rural Development Agencies.
The matter of restructuring and strengthening the district rural development machinery was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to effectively implement the central and state sponsored schemes at the district level in the rural areas of the state, the government is approving the transfer of further schemes of the government (including manpower, salary and allowances, administrative funds, etc.) to the district rural development agencies in order to reorganize and strengthen the district rural development agencies of the state.
