Preamble: The General Administration Department vide its Government Resolution dated 31/03/2021 has issued instructions regarding the formulation of a policy on common matters regarding departmental examinations of various administrative departments of the State Government for State Government employees.
On the same lines, as proposed by the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division, the Department was contemplating to formulate a committee to recommend a common policy for the departmental examinations of employees of various cadres working in the Zilla Parishads under the Rural Development Department.
The government has decided to form a committee under the chairmanship of Deputy Commissioner (Development), Divisional Commissioner's Office, Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan, Belapur, Navi Mumbai to suggest a common policy on departmental examinations.
The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:
