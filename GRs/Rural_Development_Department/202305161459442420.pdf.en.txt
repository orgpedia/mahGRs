As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Development Services (Constitution, Classification and Appointment of Services) Rules, 1973, the rules for admission to the services of the Maharashtra Development Services, Group A and Group B cadres have been regulated. Thereafter, the Maharashtra Development Services (Constitution, Classification and Appointment of Services) Rules, 1973 have been amended from time to time.
As per the provisions of Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Development Services (Constitution, Classification and Appointment of Services) Rules, 1973, there is a provision for recruitment of officers from direct service and promotion quota in the Maharashtra Development Services, Group B. Accordingly, under the Maharashtra Development Services (Constitution, Classification and Allocation of Services) (Amendment) Rules, 1979, the ratio of direct recruitment to promotion in Group B of Maharashtra Development Services has been fixed at 50: 50.
