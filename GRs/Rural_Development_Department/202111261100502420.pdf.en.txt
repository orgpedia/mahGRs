Preamble: The Government of India has restructured the Rajiv Gandhi Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (RGSPA) scheme of the Government of India for strengthening the Panchayati Raj system and implemented the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) from 2018-19. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme which has been operationalized w.e.f. 1st April, 2018. It has been decided to implement the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (Rastriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan) in the State as per the decision of the Government vide No.Ragraswa-2018/ PR.No. 102 (Part II)/ Aastha-15 dated 20th February, 2019 of the Department.

The funds to be distributed to the State Nodal Officer, State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune are being kept under the Divisional Commissioner's Office, Pune. Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner Office, Pune will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Deputy Commissioner (Establishment) shall disburse the grant to the concerned State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune. The State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune shall disburse the said funds to the Zila Parishads as per the approved Annual Action Plan.
The State Nodal Officer, State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune and all Chief Executive District Councils should take care that the expenditure from the grant approved by this Government decision will be done very strictly. The State Nodal Officer State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, Pune should distribute the funds made available for this scheme to the concerned as per the guidelines of the Central Government and ensure that the funds distributed are used properly.
