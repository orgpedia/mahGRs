Ltd., Bhopal, Rs. 42.28 crore has been approved as on 23.06.2021 and accordingly, the Chief Engineer and Deputy Project Director have entered into an agreement with the concerned consultant under which a Project Implementation Assistant Consultant has been appointed.
The Project Implementation Assistant Consultant is mandated to appoint various experts as specified in the tender. Also, the experts concerned are expected to study the changes in the quality of life, economic and social progress of the concerned citizens as well as environmental changes in the case of Asian Development Bank-funded roads under the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana and submit a report to the Asian Development Bank from time to time.
The Government was contemplating to constitute a committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Rural Road Development Society, Pune to control the work as per the specifications mentioned in the tender by experts appointed under Project Implementation Assistant Consultant. Accordingly, the Government have appointed the following Committee.

The functions of the Committee as set out above are as follows:
Stay:
To exercise control over the activities of the officers/ engineers/ experts appointed under the Project Implementation Assistant Consultant.
2. Control over submission of reports to the Asian Development Bank within the stipulated time.
3. Organize meetings of Project Implementation Assistant Consultants as required to effectively control the work of the project and to periodically review and resolve the issues arising from time to time in this regard.
4. To examine the payments received from the Project Implementation Assistant Consultant before making payment and recommend payment as per the provisions of the tender.
