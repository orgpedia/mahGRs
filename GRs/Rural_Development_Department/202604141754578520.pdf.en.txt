Government circular:
The revised time period for submission of self-assessment proposals by Gram Panchayats under Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayat Raj Abhiyan and various levels of assessment for the year 2025-26 will be as follows: -
1. Submission of self-assessment proposals by Gram Panchayats: - D. From April 1, 2026 to d. April 25, 2026
2. Taluk Level Assessment: - D. April 26, 2026 to d. May 15, 2026
3. District Level Assessment: - D. May 16, 2026 to D. June 05, 2026
4. Departmental Evaluation: - D. June 06, 2026 to D. June 15, 2026
5. State Level Assessment: - D. From June 16, 2026 to d. June 30, 2026
