The government's decision:
The procedure for selection of Gram Panchayats under Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj Abhiyan will be as follows: -
The self-assessment proposals received from the Gram Panchayats will be evaluated by a Taluk Level Committee and the 3 Gram Panchayats with the highest marks at the Taluk level will be selected as Taluk Level Best Gram Panchayats. Also, 2 Gram Panchayats will be selected for the special award.

The best first 2 Gram Panchayats at the Taluk level will be evaluated by the District Level Committee and the 3 Gram Panchayats with the highest marks will be selected as the best Gram Panchayats at the District level.
The best first 2 Gram Panchayats at the district level will be evaluated by the Division Level Committee and the 3 Gram Panchayats with the highest marks will be selected as the best Gram Panchayats at the Division level.
The best first 2 Gram Panchayats at the divisional level will be evaluated by the State Level Committee and the 3 highest scoring Gram Panchayats will be selected as the best Gram Panchayats at the State level.
