3. Ma. The High Court, Bombay, Division Bench at Aurangabad, in Writ Petition No. 12245/2022 has directed to decide the policy regarding the earned leave and leave encashment of the Head of the Centre.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981. As per Section 54 of the Act, a government servant serving in the Long Leave Department shall not be entitled to claim any accrued leave in respect of the work done by him in any year in which he has taken full long leave. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981. (2) (a) If a Government servant has availed of any part of the long leave in respect of any one year, he shall be entitled to take the accrued leave of 30 days in proportion to the whole of the long leave in respect of the area not availed by him in that year.
In view of the above provision, the Government have decided that the Head of the Centre who has worked during the long leave period as per the written order of his superiors, if the superiors certify, in such a case, he will be given 10 days (30 days) each in respect of the work done during the long leave period
