In pursuance of the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, in the interest of public service, the Government, on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board and with the approval of the authorities competent to make the transfer and in terms of the provisions of sections 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the said Act, is appointing Shri Manik Diwe, Additional CEO (Selection), Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A Cadre.
