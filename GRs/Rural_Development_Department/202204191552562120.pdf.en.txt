Letter No. R. 20015/2/2020-DRDA, Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India. 01.11.2021 Proposal:
A total of 510 temporary posts, 15 posts each for 34 District Rural Development Agencies in the state, were extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 as per the decision of the Department dated August 23, 2021. As per the directions of the Central Government, the District Rural Development Agency was instructed to be closed from 1.04.2022.
dated 31.03.2022. In the Cabinet meeting, it was decided that instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development System Administration Scheme, the State Government will continue the scheme as 100% State Scheme for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027.
In the meeting, it was decided that from 01.04.2022, 8 posts have been earmarked for District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme instead of 15 posts and the services of the remaining officers/ staff will be returned to their original establishment. A Government Resolution in this regard has been issued on 01.04.2022. Accordingly, the proposal for extension of sanctioned posts for District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme from 1.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per Annexure-A attached to this GOVERNMENT DECISION, a total of 510 temporary posts in 34 District Rural Development Agencies of the State are being sanctioned for extension from 01.03.2022 to 31.03.2022.
