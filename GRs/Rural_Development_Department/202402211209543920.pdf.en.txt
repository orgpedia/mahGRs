The Proposal: -
The procedure and criteria for upgrading out-of-plan / unclassified roads in the state to rural roads and rural roads to other district roads are as under: No. 3 are determined by the government circular here.
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Zonal Department, Pune has identified the reference No. 4 and 5 here.
Rural roads in Satara, Khatav and Javli taluks of Satara district.
# Page 2
Government Decision No: RAVIO-2023 / Q. No.36 / Construction-5
Therefore, a proposal has been submitted to the government for inclusion in the Road Development Plan (ID1). The Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded that these roads be included in the road development plan as rural roads. The proposal also requires land acquisition for the roads demanded.
It is stated that no such road passes through forest land.
Accordingly, considering the use of these roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the matter of upgrading these roads as rural roads was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - Taking into consideration the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Satara, Khatav and Javli taluks of Satara district are being included in the Road Development Plan Year 2001-21 as rural roads.
