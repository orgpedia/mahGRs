Under the Government's policy of' Housing for All-2022',' Maha Awaas Abhiyan Gramin 2020-21' has been implemented in the State of Maharashtra from 20th November, 2020 to 05th June, 2021 to accelerate and bring quality in the effective implementation of all rural housing schemes such as Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin and State Sponsored Ramai Awas Yojana, Shabari Awas Yojana, Pardhi Awas Yojana, Adim Awas Yojana, Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana, Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasant Yojana by implementing various activities during the campaign period.
It has been decided to honour the organizations and individuals who have done excellent work by evaluating the activities carried out in this campaign with' Maha Awas Abhiyan Award' and' Maha Awas Abhiyan Special Award'. The decision was taken on June 10, 2021. Accordingly, the matter of awarding the' Maha Awas Abhiyan Award' and' Maha Awas Abhiyan Special Award' at the state level was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the State Level Implementation, Control and Evaluation Committee constituted by the Government of Rural Development Department vide its Decision No. PIO-2020/ PR No. 107/ Scheme-10 dated 19th November, 2020, the following State Level Maha Awaas Abhiyan Awards and Maha Awaas Abhiyan Special Awards are being announced to the institutions which have done excellent work by evaluating the activities in the' Maha Awaas Abhiyan-Gramin 2020-21'.
