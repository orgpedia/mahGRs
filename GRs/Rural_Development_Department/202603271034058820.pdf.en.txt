The Proposal:
In order to encourage and enhance the efficiency of Gram Panchayats, which are predominantly functioning as local self-government institutions in rural areas, by encouraging them to perform, Reference No.1 for implementation of "Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj Abhiyan" in the State from this financial year for Gram Panchayats, Panchayat Samitis and Zila Parishads doing excellent work. Approval has been granted by a Government Decision of 1. The duration of the mission for the year 2025-26 is as under Reference No. According to the Government Decision No. 2. Expanded to 31.03.2026. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to issue instructions for assessment period and assessment at all levels for the year 2025-26 under the Mission.
Government circular:
Under the "Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj" campaign for the year 2025-26, the assessment period and assessment at all levels should be carried out as per the following instructions: -
Submission of self-assessment proposals by Gram Panchayats: -
Duration: D. April 1, 2026 to D. April 15, 2026
It will be mandatory for all Gram Panchayats participating in the Abhiyaan to login to the official website of the Abhiyaan and submit their self-assessment proposals within the stipulated time. No corrections / updates can be made to the proposal after it is submitted. If any difficulties arise during the process, contact the state-level technical war room or the concerned group development officer, panchayat samiti.

2. Taluk Level Assessment: - Duration: - D. April 16, 2026 to d. May 05, 2026
The taluk-level assessment under Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj Abhiyan should be done as follows:
1. A Taluk Level Evaluation Committee should be constituted under the chairmanship of Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti. The committee will consist of one team member of journalists and 4 other members to be appointed by the Group Development Officer.
2. In order to ensure transparency and transparency, the Block Development Officer should evaluate the Gram Panchayats in other revenue division taluks without evaluating the Gram Panchayats in his / her taluka. Suggestions in this regard will be issued separately.
3. The Group Development Officer and his / her team will be in charge of the entire evaluation. It will be mandatory for the Assistant Group Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti to be present at the headquarters during the assessment period. In the case of a Panchayat Samiti for which the Assistant Group Development Officer is in charge, he / she should himself / herself be responsible for the inspection of the Taluka provided in the login of the Group Development Officer of the Panchayat Samiti concerned. 2. District Level Assessment: - Duration: - D. May 06, 2026 to D. May 25, 2026
The district level assessment under "Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj Abhiyan" should be done as follows:
A District Level Evaluation Committee under the Chairmanship of CEO, Zilla Parishad should be constituted. The CEO will appoint one team member and 4 other members to the committee.
In order to ensure transparency and fairness, the CEO should evaluate the Gram Panchayats in other revenue division districts without evaluating the Gram Panchayats in his / her district. Suggestions in this regard will be issued separately.
The first-ranking Gram Panchayat in each taluk will be eligible for the district level competition. These Gram Panchayats will be evaluated and the 3 highest scoring Gram Panchayats will be selected for the district level award. The first-ranked Gram Panchayat at the district level will be eligible for the competition at the division level.
The cost of the assessment shall be met by the concerned CEO, Zila Parishad from the grant received by their Zila Parishad under Reference H. 3. Also, the transportation for the assessment should be done by your district council.
The Zilla Parishad in which the assessment is to be carried out shall make arrangements for the reservation of a Government Hostel for the Evaluation Committee.
The District Level Evaluation Committee shall conduct an in-depth scrutiny of each component of the Evaluation Report of the Gram Panchayats by the Taluk Level Evaluation Committee along with evidence. The assessment process should be completed in a planned manner within the stipulated time.
The CEO should take action as per Annexure "A" based on the OTP based Self Validation & Authentication system as per the list of Gram Panchayats made available in the login.

State Level Assessment: - Duration: - D. From June 11, 2026 to d. June 30, 2026
The first-ranked Gram Panchayat in each division will be eligible for the competition at the state level. These Gram Panchayats will be evaluated and the 3 highest scoring Gram Panchayats will be selected for the state level award.
Reservation of Government Hostel at the place of assessment for the State Level Assessment Committee as well as transportation and refreshment expenses shall be borne by the concerned District Council.
The State Level Evaluation Committee shall conduct an in-depth and objective scrutiny of every element of the Evaluation Report of the Gram Panchayat by the Division Level Evaluation Committee along with evidence.
