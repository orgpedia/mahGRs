Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 3.8.2012, the posts of Assistant Block Development Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) have been created under the Maharashtra Development Service. Further, as per the Government Notification dated 7.12.2012, the cadre of the post of Assistant Block Development Officer (Group-B) has been included in the cadre of Assistant Block Development Officer (Group-B) in sub-rule 5 of Rule 3 of the Maharashtra Development Services (Constitution, Classification and Entry into Services) Rules, 1973 and the category of Assistant Block Development Officer includes the following posts: 1. Assistant Block Development Officer (Panchayat Samiti) 2. Assistant Block Development Officer (Administration) 3. Assistant Project Officer (Control) 4. Lecturer, Gramsevak Training Institute (Sindewahi, Chandrapur) 5. Other posts equivalent to Group B post in the Maharashtra Development Services (Constitution, Classification and Entry into Services) Rules, 1973.
The department-wise break-up of the posts in Maharashtra Development Service, Group B was decided by the Government Decision dated 25.3.2013 and 20.10.2014 of the Department of Rural Development. The matter of approving the revised format of the post was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the proposal for the post of Assistant Group Development Officer has been submitted to the Finance Department.

As recommended by the Sub-Committee, the proposal was placed before the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting dated 30.6.2022. After the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, the Government has taken the following decision regarding the demarcation of the post of Assistant Group Development Officer, Group B.
Government Decision: The establishment of Assistant Block Development Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) under the Maharashtra Development Service under the Rural Development Department, 443 posts (S-15 as per the pay scale of the 7th Pay Commission, Rs. 41800-132300) is hereby approved as per Annexure-A attached with the revised figure.
This Government Resolution is being issued as per the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 30.6.2022.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the approval given under the Informal Reference No. 418/ APC, dated 25.7.2022 of the Department of Finance.
