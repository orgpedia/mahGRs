Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, 4% reservation has been provided in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities. Accordingly, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. For this purpose, the Central Government has published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04/01/2021 under the heading' List of posts identification suitable for Person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04/01/2021' in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification of the Central Government on its website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated 02/02/2021 to ensure that the posts of Group-A to Group-D, which are in the list published by the Central Government in respect of the posts in the State Government Establishment (including posts in subordinate offices), are in the category of Divyang, even if the duties and responsibilities, nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are different from the posts in the State Government Service.
Accordingly, the posts of Health Worker (Male), Health Worker, Laboratory Technician, Drug Manufacturer, Health Supervisor in the Group-C cadre of Zilla Parishad will be filled up for various categories of Divyangs. The Government's decision of 22 February 2021 has been confirmed. Further, in the Government Resolution dated 02/02/2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance

Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities for the purpose of determination of posts under the State Governments as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the Central Government's Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, a committee has been constituted by the department under the chairmanship of Deputy Commissioner (Establishment), Divisional Commissioner's Office, Pune to ensure the posts in Group-C and D cadres in the Zilla Parishad for the disabled. The Committee has submitted its report after verifying the posts in the notification dated 04/01/2021 of the Central Government and ensuring the posts for the disabled in the posts of Zila Parishad which are generally found equivalent in the notification of the Central Government for the recruitable posts of Zila Parishad.
After verifying the report submitted by the Committee as well as the equivalent posts in the notification dated 04/01/2021 of the Central Government, the posts of 1) Senior Assistant (Clerk), 2) Junior Assistant (Clerk), 3) Senior Assistant (Accounts), 4) Junior Assistant (Accounts), 5) Junior Engineer (Architecture), 6) Junior Engineer (Vidyut), 7) Junior Engineer (Mechanical) in the Group-C cadre of Zila Parishad are being ensured as shown in the return" A" under this Government decision along with the new category for Divyang.
This Government Order is being issued pursuant to the notification dated 04/01/2021 issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India and the Government Order dated 02/02/2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice, Government of India.
