Government's stand:
Under the Ma Balasaheb Thackeray Smriti Matoshree Gram Panchayat Bandhani Yojana, the construction of 49 Gram Panchayats has been approved under the government decision dated 31 March 2022 at serial number 3 in Vacha.
The name of the Namud Gram Panchayat has been spelt as' Namud Gram Panchayat' in Rakana No. 4 of serial No. 4 of Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar district in the Government Resolution dated March 31, 2022 of the above mentioned Department of Rural Development. Therefore, the name in serial number 4 of Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar district is being changed and the name should be read as shown in the following table.
