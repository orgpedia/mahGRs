Reference No. 1 to be made is mandated by the Government Decision herein. The revised procedures and the time period for notification of tenders under the e-tendering system have been prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference No. 2 and 3. With a view to expedite the execution of various development works of Zilla Parishad Self-Fund, Shrine, 2515 Basic Amenities and other public interests under Panchayat Raj Institutions (excluding works under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana and Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana under Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency) and with a period of one month remaining for the start of monsoon season, the time limit prescribed for inviting tenders in Para-4 (2) of the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2013 and Para-1 of the Government Decision dated 26th July, 2016 of the Department of Rural Development in the said context. Public Works Department No. 15 As per the decision of the Government here, in the year 2023-24, where elections to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities, Nagar Panchayats, Gram Panchayats, Zilla Parishads/ Lok Sabha are to be held, the time has been fixed for the release of e-tenders in view of the pending tendering process of the sanctioned works under various headings in the year 2023-24. Keeping this in view, the matter of fixing the time limit for publication of e-tender for the Village Development Department was under consideration.
Accordingly, vide Circular No. 14 dated 18th May, 2023, the period for issue of Short Term E-Tender Notice under e-Tender System shall be upto 31st March, 2024 as under:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A.  |                                         |             | Cost of work   |             |   | Tender number and duration |
| First | the 2nd                                 | the 3rd     |
| 1     | Works costing less than Rs. 5.00 lakh   | It's 7 days | It's 4 days    | It's 3 days |
| 2     | Works from Rs. 5.00 to 50.00 lakhs      | It's 7 days | It's five days | It's 3 days |
| 3     | Works costing more than Rs. 50.00 lakhs | It's 7 days | It's five days | It's 3 days |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This order will be issued by all the chief executive officers, district councils, all the heads of departments under them and the people's representatives under the rural infrastructure (2515 1238) where the system under the Public Works Board will be working.
It should be ensured that such works are completed within the stipulated time.
With effect from 01.04.2024, the above exemption shall stand automatically withdrawn and the provisions of the relevant Government Decisions dated 23.09.2013 and dated 26.07.2016 shall come into force again.
