Government circular:
The final seniority list of officers in the cadre of Block Development Officers has been published from 1.1.2016 to 31.12.2017 vide order dated 09.10.2018.
Under this circular, the provisional seniority of the Group Development Officer cadre from 01.01.2018 to 31.12.2020 is being published subject to the following conditions.
(i) The promotion in the said seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority in the post of Assistant Group Development Officer Cadre of the quota officers and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the selection list of their post of Group Development Officer Cadre.
(ii) in the case of officers against whom departmental inquiry is pending.
They will be included in the seniority list as the case may be after the final decision of the inquiry.
(iii) The said seniority list is provisional in nature. high
