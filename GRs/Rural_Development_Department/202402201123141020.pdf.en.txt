The Proposal:
Under Gram Panchayat Sevagram, property tax was levied on productive buildings, Yatri Niwas and Karmikari Niwas excluding all Bapuji's huts at Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan.
Under this government decision, all the cottages of Bapuji at Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan Sevagram, Wardha, except the productive buildings, Yatri Niwas and Karmachari Niwas, have been imposed.
The grant for payment of financial assistance to Gram Panchayat Sevagram, T.G. Wardha has been approved with effect from this financial year as compensation for loss due to property tax of Gram Panchayat on account of exemption from incoming property tax.
The government's decision:
The amount sanctioned for this financial year for payment of financial assistance to Gram Panchayat Sevagram as compensation against property tax of Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan, Sevagram, Wardha is Rs. 34511 / - (Rupees Thirty-four thousand five hundred and eleven) to the Gram Panchayat Sevagram for payment on the Budget Distribution System by the Chief Executive.
Officers, Zila Parishad, Wardha are being distributed.
The CEO, Zilla Parishad, Wardha shall disburse the grant amount sanctioned under this Government Decision to Gram Panchayat Sevagram and forward a copy of the distribution order to the Government. Make sure that the grant is spent for this purpose.
In order to adjust the expenditure, the CEO, Zilla Parishad Wardha should coordinate with the Accountant General Maharashtra 1 & 2 Mumbai / Nagpur office regarding the expenditure statement and send a report to the government.
