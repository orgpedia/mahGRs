Government Resolution No: Budget 2015/ No.315/ Para-4, D. 11th March, 2016 Preamble: Property tax has been imposed on productive buildings, Yatri Niwas and staff quarters excluding all the huts of Bapuji at Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan under Gram Panchayat Sevagram. Under this decision, the Government has approved grant of financial assistance to Gram Panchayat Sevagram, T. G. Wardha from the financial year 2001-02 as compensation for the loss of property tax due to exemption of property tax levied on productive buildings, Yatri Niwas and Employees Niwas excluding all the huts of Bapuji at Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan Sevagram, Wardha.
Government Decision: Demand for financial assistance to Gram Panchayat Sevagram as compensation for property tax of Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan, Sevagram, Wardha for the financial year 2022-23 L-3, PradhanShiksha 2515 Other Village Development Programme 198- Assistance to Gram Panchayats (00) (06) Grant to Gram Panchayat as compensation for property tax of Mahatma Gandhi Pratishthan Sevagram Wardha (25152412)-31-Subsidiary Grants and the amount made available on the Budget Distribution System is Rs. 34511/- (Rupees Thirty Four Thousand Five Hundred and Eleven only) is being distributed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Wardha for payment to Gram Panchayat Sevagram.
The Chief Executive Officer, Zila Parishad, Wardha shall disburse the amount of subsidy sanctioned under this Government decision to Gram Panchayat Sevagram and send a copy of the distribution order to the Government. Be sure that the grant is used for this purpose.
To match the expenditure, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Wardha should coordinate with the Accountant General Maharashtra 1 and 2 Mumbai/ Nagpur office regarding the statement of expenditure and send the report to the government.
