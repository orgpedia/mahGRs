Government's decision:
Mauje-Yenikoni, Ta. The government has approved the formation of a district-level memorial committee under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Nagpur, to approve the construction cost plan and control the construction of the memorial of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in Narkhed, G. Nagpur.
