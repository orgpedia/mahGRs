The government's clean-up:
In order to encourage and enhance the efficiency of Gram Panchayats functioning primarily as local self-government institutions in rural areas, the Government has approved the implementation of "Mukhyamantri Samriddhi Panchayati Raj Abhiyan," an award campaign for promotion of Gram Panchayats, Panchayat Samitis and Zila Parishads doing excellent work at four levels - Taluka, District, Revenue Division and State from this financial year.
