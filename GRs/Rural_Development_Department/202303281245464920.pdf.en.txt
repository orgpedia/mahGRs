The contribution of Panchayati Raj Institutions in the development of rural Maharashtra is immense. All the development works in the state are being implemented by the Panchayati Raj Institutions. Then it is necessary to increase the work culture of the Panchayati Raj Institutions by encouraging them according to their performance. For this purpose, the award scheme" Yashwant Panchayat Raj Abhiyan" has been started at both the department and state level from the financial year 2005-2006 for the district councils and panchayat samitis who have done excellent work in administrative management and development work from the panchayat raj institutions in the state. Under the Yashwant Panchayat Raj Abhiyan, awards are given to Panchayat Samitis and Zila Parishads.
