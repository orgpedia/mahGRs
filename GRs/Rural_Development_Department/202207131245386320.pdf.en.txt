Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, the entire budget has been approved and a total of Rs. 3008.00 lakh has been allocated in the budget for the construction of residential buildings of Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samitis. 842.24 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System.
Subject to the prescribed limits of the powers of the administrative departments to disburse the funds, as mentioned in the circular dated 04.04.2022, the budgetary allocation for the construction/ sub-construction of residential buildings of Zila Parishad and Panchayat Samiti is Rs. 8,42,24, 000/- (Rs. The Government is approving the grant-in-aid amounting to Rs.8 crore forty two lakh twenty four thousand only) in the cash flow of the month of July, 2022 to be placed under the respective Chief Executive Officers of the Zila Parishad as follows. The funds should be spent following the prescribed procedure for these works.
