Introduction: Local Government Directory (LGD) system has been made available by the Ministry of Panchayati Raj, Government of India. Under this system, every revenue village, taluka, district, local body (rural and urban) is given a unique code.
In this system, the state revenue department has been given the responsibility of managing and updating the records of every revenue village, taluka, district. Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department and Urban Local Bodies Department
The records of self-government institutions are managed. However, this requires coordination with the Revenue Department, Urban Development Department, Election Commission and Census Directorate.
The government was considering the formation of a steering committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Maharashtra State, to coordinate the work related to the LGD Code. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The following Steering Committee is being constituted in respect of Local Government Directory (LGD Code).
