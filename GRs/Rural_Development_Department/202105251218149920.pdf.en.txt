The proposal: self. In pursuance of the Government decision dated 30.3.2017, funds were released to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Satara, for the construction of a memorial hall in memory of former Chief Minister Yashwantrao Chavan at Satara. The matter of allowing the spent funds was under consideration of the government as the funds were not spent within the stipulated time.
Government Decision: As per the decision of the Government dated 30.3.2017, a sum of Rs. 769.20 lakh (Rupees Seven Crores Seventy-Nine Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) was disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Satara for the construction of a memorial hall in memory of former Chief Minister Yashwantrao Chavan at Satara and the said amount was allowed to be spent till the end of 31.3.2018. However, since the said fund remains unutilized, the said fund is being allowed to be spent till the end of 31.3.2022. It should be ensured that the funds are spent by the end of March 2022 and the utilization certificate of the funds should be submitted to the government immediately.
It should be ensured that the said funds are spent by the end of March, 2022 and the utilization certificate regarding the spent funds should be submitted to the government immediately.
