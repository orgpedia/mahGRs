Government order:
As per the Government Order dated 15.09.2021, dated 15.09.2021, the period of appointment of retired Deputy Engineers on contract basis has expired. Accordingly, subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Order dated 17th December, 2016 of the General Administration Department and the Government Order dated 15th September, 2021 of the Rural Development Department, the following retired Deputy Engineers have been appointed on contract basis by giving technical break in their contractual appointments.
