Introduction: The above reference No. The Government has taken a decision in respect of the recommendations contained in Volume-II of the Report of the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017. The government has approved the recommendation of the State Pay Revision Committee and the information about the cadres who have been approved the revised pay levels has been given in the government decision.
In the above decision, the pay scale of Shorts (Upper Grade), Shorts (Lower Grade) and Extension Officers has been revised in the Statement-A. The employees of the above cadre are included in the Zilla Parishads of the state under the Rural Development Department.
The employees of the district councils are employees of the local bodies and not of the state government. Therefore, no rules, acts, government decisions, government circulars, etc. of the government are directly applicable to the employees of the Zilla Parishads in the state and if they are to be applied, the Rural Development Department makes separate orders and applies these matters to the Zilla Parishad employees. Hence, reference No. The government was considering a proposal to apply the revised pay scale to the employees of the cadres of shorthand (upper class), shorthand (lower class), extension officers in the Zilla Parishad of the state. Following is the government's decision:
Government Decision: Finance Department, Government of Maharashtra Decision No: Vepur 1121/ PR No.4/ Service-9, Dy. The revised pay scale (Statement-A) as on 13th February, 2023 is being made applicable to the employees of the cadre of Shortlists (Upper Grade), Shortlists (Lower Grade), Extension Officers in the District Councils of the State.
2. While applying the above revised pay scale, Reference No. All the terms and conditions of the Governing Decision No. 1 shall continue to apply to the above cadres.
3. The decision of the Government is not binding on the Finance Department. Reference No. 384/ Service-9, D. being issued with the approval and consent given under 30.10.2023.
