Rural Development Department: Under the Rural Development Department, many schemes and projects sponsored by the State Government are implemented.
Administrative and technical expertise of officers and staff involved in implementation of such schemes/ projects.
It takes work. Special individual skills and qualities are found in the officers/ employees while completing such projects. The scheme of honouring such officers/ employees has been started by the Government under the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation, Government Resolution, dated 2nd September, 2005 from the year 2005-2006. Accordingly, the Government has decided to honour the meritorious officers/ employees of the Ministry (itself) and at the field level in the Department of Rural Development for the year 2020-2021 by awarding them with certificates and medals.
In pursuance of the above decision, the following 37 meritorious officers/ staff have been finalised for the award for the year 2020-21.
