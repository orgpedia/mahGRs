Preamble: With a view to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring about efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued an order dated 23. Under the Memorandum dated 03.2021, it has been directed to adopt the revised procedure with effect from 01.07.2021. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, vide Circular Reference No. 3, has directed the Central Government to appoint a Single Nodal Agency for the implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme as per the revised procedures and to appoint a Deputy Secretary level officer as the Nodal Officer for the coordination of the scheme.
Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan is a centrally sponsored scheme. The scheme has been implemented in the State of Maharashtra since 2018-19 as per the Government decision in Reference No. 1. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. The State Management Cell, State Nodal Agency of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (SPMU-RGSA) and State Project Director, Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan were under consideration of the Government to be appointed as Nodal Officers for all activities to be implemented under the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan.
