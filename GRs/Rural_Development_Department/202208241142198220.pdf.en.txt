Under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana, the annual balance sheet of all three funds- Admin Fund, Maintenance Fund and Programme Fund- is required to be submitted to NRIDA, New Delhi. The Chief Minister Gram Sadak Yojana has been implemented in the state since 2015 on the lines of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana. At present, the work of both these schemes is going on in full swing, due to which the workload on the post of Financial Controller has increased. Therefore, the government has taken the decision. The post of Financial Controller created in the rank of Joint Director vide order dated 16.03.2009 is now in the cadre of Director in the pay scale of Rs. Rs 37,400. 67,000 to Rs. 8900/- (S-29 as per 7th Pay Commission, Rs. 1,31,100- Rs. 2,16,600) was under consideration by the government.
Government's decision
In view of the comprehensive nature of both the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana and the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana by the Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency under the Department of Rural Development, and in order to effectively handle the accounting matters, the post of Financial Controller has now been created in the cadre of Director in the pay scale of Rs. Rs 37,400. 67,000 to Rs. 8900/- (As per 7th Pay Commission S29, Rs. 1,31,100- Rs. 2,16,600) upgrade is being approved by the government.
