Preamble: The Central Government has been directed to adopt a revised methodology with effect from 01.07.2021 for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds.
Accordingly, the Finance Department vide Reference No. As per the revised procedures of the Central Government, a single nodal agency should be appointed for the implementation of every centrally sponsored scheme and a deputy secretary-level officer should be appointed as the nodal officer for the coordination of the scheme.
The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 (60% Central share and 40% State share) basis. The matter of appointing a single nodal agency for district rural development administration was under the consideration of the government.
