The Proposal: -
Subsidies are distributed to the District Collectors by the State Government from the budgetary provision for the conduct of Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti General and Bye-Elections in a free, fair and transparent manner as directed by the State Election Commission. From this grant, the Collector meets the expenditure on these elections. As of now, the programme for the upcoming 32 Zila Parishads and 336 Panchayat Samitis under it has been completed. This will be followed by the stages of reservation, electoral rolls, and physical elections. The issue of allocation of grants to all the concerned districts was under consideration of the Government in accordance with the letter No. 3 of the State Election Commission with reference to the forthcoming General Election to the Zilla Parishad and its Subordinate Panchayat Samitis in a phased manner. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
