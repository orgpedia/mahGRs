Government Resolution No. PAMVYA-2022/ Q. A proposal for the disbursement of funds to Rural Local Bodies (RLBs) in the State was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
The Central Government has released the second installment of the 15th Central Finance Commission (CFC) untied grant for the financial year 2022-23 amounting to Rs. 722.27 crore is being made available for disbursement to Rural Local Bodies (Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat at all three levels) in the State of Maharashtra under the head of accounts (25152628/251526646/ 25152664) respectively as shown in the accompanying prospectus (Form-A). Funds are being disbursed through Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to all the Chief Executive Officers of the Zila Parishads as per the prospectus. Panchayat funds
The government institutions are being distributed in the ratio of 10: 10: 80 in Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat respectively.
All the District Councils are required to deposit the 15th Central Finance Commission funds in the ICICI Bank account of the Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat by submitting the payment to the Treasury through the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS-Treasury net. Also, the funds of the Zila Parishad should be deposited in the ICICI Bank account of the Zila Parishad by submitting the payment to the Subordinate and Accounts Office, Mumbai, through the integration of e-Gramswaraj-PFMS Treasury net through the Department of Rural Development.
The Deputy Executive Officer (Gram Panchayat) will be responsible for planning, controlling and coordinating the distribution of funds at the Gram Panchayat level as per the guidelines given by the Government.

The 15th Central Finance Commission will not be disbursed to the Rural Local Bodies whose administrator is working at all the three levels i.e. Zila Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat under 7/6/2022.
At present, there are 26 zila parishads, 289 panchayat samitis and 635 gram panchayats in the state. All such Rural Local Bodies have not been disbursed the second instalment of 15 th Central Finance Commission undrawn funds for the year 2022-23 through this order.
