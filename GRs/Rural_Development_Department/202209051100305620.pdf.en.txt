Preamble: As on 31.03.2022. In the Cabinet meeting, it was decided that instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development System-Governance Plan, the State Government will continue the scheme as 100% State Plan for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027.
A total of 272 temporary posts, 8 posts each for 34 District Rural Development Agencies in the State, were approved to be continued from 01.04.2022 to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision dated 19 th April, 2022 of the Department.
In the meeting, it was decided that from April 1, 2022, 8 posts have been fixed for the District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme, instead of the earlier 15 posts, and the services of the remaining officers/ employees will be returned to their original establishment. A Government Resolution in this regard has been issued on 01.04.2022. Accordingly, the proposal for extension of sanctioned posts for District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
