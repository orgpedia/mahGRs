Government Decisions: During the session of the Maharashtra Legislature, various parliamentary instruments such as starred/ unstarred questions, promises, attention-grabbing suggestions, points of interest, special mention suggestions, etc. are raised by the honourable members in the Legislative Assembly/ Legislative Council. Accordingly, the replies/ representations in the prescribed format will be forwarded to the Secretariat of the Legislature. The time limit for sending members to the Legislative Assembly is prescribed by the rules. Action should be taken in this regard.
2. Also, regarding pending arms, the Legislative Council. The President and Mr. The Chairman also. Principal Secretary, Department of Parliamentary Affairs, Ministry of Rural Development, Govt. Meetings/ reviews in this regard are also held at the level of Additional Chief Secretary from time to time. During the review, it has been observed that in some cases, answers to unstarred questions/ assurances, etc. are not being sent to the Legislature Secretariat within the prescribed time frame.
It has been observed that despite giving instructions and reminders from the government level from time to time, the information/ report is not received from the field office within the prescribed time, so it is not possible to take action in the said case within the time limit.
4. When the legislature is supposed to do all the work with priority and seriousness, such action is not seen to be taken. However, it should be ensured that all priority activities are carried out within the stipulated time in accordance with the legislative business. If there is any delay, the concerned will be held responsible.
