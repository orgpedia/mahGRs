The revised procedures and the time period for notification of tender under the tender system have been prescribed under the Government Decision at Reference No. 2 and 3. With a view to expedite the works of road works and bridge repair works under Panchayat Raj Institutions, (excluding the works under Pradhan Mantri and Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana under Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency), Zilla Parishad Self-Fund, Shrine, 2515 basic amenities and various other development works of public interest and in view of the possibility of holding elections to the Zilla Parishad, Panchayat Samiti and Gram Panchayat in 2022-23, the time limit prescribed for calling of tenders in paragraph 4 (2) of the Government Decision dated 23rd September, 2013 and paragraph 1 of the Government Decision dated 26th July, 2016 of the Department of Rural Development in the said context, vide Circular No.
Public Works Department No. 13 As per the decision of the government here, the time has been fixed to publish e-tenders considering the code of conduct in the places where elections to municipal corporations/ municipalities, nagar panchayats, gram panchayats, zilla parishads are to be held in 2022-23. Keeping this in view, the matter of fixing the period of publication of e-tender for the Village Development Department was under consideration.
Accordingly, vide Circular No. 12 dated 19.12.2022, the period for issue of short-term e-tendering notices under e-tendering system shall be upto 31.03.2023.
