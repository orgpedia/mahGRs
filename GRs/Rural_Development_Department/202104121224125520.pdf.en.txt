Government circular:
As per the order dated 24.08.2020, the final seniority list of officers in the cadre of Deputy CEO/ Block Development Officer (Selection Grade) from 1.1.2018 to 31.12.2018 and from 1.1.2019 to 31.12.2019 has been published.
Provisional seniority of the Deputy CEO/ Block Development Officer (Selection Grade) cadre as per this circular is being published from 1.1.2020 to 31.12.2020 subject to the following conditions: i) The seniority list is being published subject to the finalization of seniority of the concerned officers in the post of Block Development Officer cadre and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the selection list of their Deputy CEO cadre post.
(ii) In case of officers against whom departmental inquiry is pending, they will be included in the seniority list, as the case may be, after the final decision of the departmental inquiry.

All Divisional Commissioners/ CEOs are requested to bring this seniority list to the notice of all concerned officers (including officers on deputation) working under their control and submit the same to the Department without any delay. After receiving such seniority, the concerned officer should submit the objections, if any, regarding his seniority to the government through the concerned Divisional Commissioner within 15 days and send an additional copy to the government immediately. All concerned officers in the seniority list are also advised to submit objections, if any, regarding their seniority as mentioned above within the prescribed time frame. They should note that objections received after the due date or late will not be considered. After receiving such objections, the Divisional Commissioner shall submit a copy of the objection along with the feedback to the Government within one week.
4. With regard to the seniority list, it has also come to the notice of the Government that the copy issued by the Government is not being provided to the concerned officers in time. Therefore, all the Divisional Commissioners/ CEOs should take care to inform all the concerned officers under their control about the availability of this seniority from the government website to the officers on deputation under their department/ retired from their department and immediately inform the government about it.
