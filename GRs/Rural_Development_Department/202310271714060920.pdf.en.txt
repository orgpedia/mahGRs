The Proposal:
As per the orders of the Government of Dinak No. 5 of the above mentioned readings of the Rural Development Department, the temporary appointment of retired Deputy Engineer on contract basis for the implementation of Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana and effective implementation of the work of Maharashtra Rural Road Development Agency was done on contract basis for a period of 11 months. The government was also considering temporary re-appointment of some of the retired sub-engineers on contract basis.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per the order of the Rural Development Department at serial number 5 in the above mentioned text, the period of appointment of retired Deputy Engineers on contract basis has ended.
Accordingly, subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Order dated 17th December, 2016 of the General Administration Department and the Government Order dated 15th September, 2021 of the Rural Development Department, the following retired Deputy Engineers have been appointed on contractual basis by giving technical break to their name as retired Deputy Engineer on contract basis.
