Preamble: A total of 272 temporary posts of 8 posts each for 34 District Rural Development Agencies in the State were approved to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 as per the Government decision dated 16th February, 2023 of the Department.
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 31.03.2022 has decided that instead of discontinuing the District Rural Development System Administration Scheme, the State Government should continue the scheme as 100% State Scheme for the next 5 years i.e. from 01.04.2022 to 31.03.2027.
In the meeting, it was decided that for the District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme, from April 1, 2022, 8 posts have been fixed in place of the earlier figure of 15 posts and the services of the remaining officers/ employees will be returned to their original establishment. Read the above reference. The Government Decision dated 01.04.2022 has been issued. Accordingly, the proposal to extend the sanctioned posts for the District Rural Development Agency-Administration Scheme was under the consideration of the government.
