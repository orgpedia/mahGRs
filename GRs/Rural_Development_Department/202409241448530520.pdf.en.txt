1) Rural Development Department Government Decision No. Narik-2019 / P.S. No.9796 / Aastha-7, dated 11th May, 2022
Introduction: - The post of Gram Sevak and Rural Development Officer is an important post and component of the system that connects and directly implements the government and the people for the overall and sustainable development of rural areas. The Gram Sevak / Village Development Officer functions as the Secretary of the Gram Panchayat and the ex-officio Secretary of the Gram Sabha. As both the posts of Gram Sevak and Rural Development Officer have the same nature of work, an expert committee was appointed under the government's decision to combine the two posts to create a single post. According to the report of the committee, the government was considering the idea of combining both the posts of Gram Sevak and Village Development Officer and nominating them as "Gram Panchayat Officers" who were competent in terms of administration.
The Government's Decisions:
The post of Gram Sevak (S-8) and Gram Vikas Adhikari (S-12) under the Rural Development Department is being renamed as "Gram Panchayat Officer" by amalgamating both the posts and retaining the original post of Gram Sevak in the pay scale of S-8 (25500-81100).
2. The existing posts of Village Development Officers shall be preserved in their pay scales and these posts shall be classified into the deceased cadre and thereafter new appointments shall be made to the same post in the S-8 pay scale for which the Gram Panchayat Officer is a nominee.
3. The post of Gram Panchayat Officer is guaranteed for 10 years under the PRAGATI scheme under the revised service.
First benefit after service Extension Officer S-14 (38600-122800), second benefit after 20 years of service
Auxiliary Group Development Officer S-15 (41800-132300) and Third Benefit Group after 30 years of service
A Development Officer S-20 (56100-177500) will be allowed.

As the promotion chain for the posts of Gram Panchayat Officers is being revised, the benefits of the Promotional Scheme as assured under the revised promotion chain will be admissible from the date of issue of the said Government Decision. Before the issue of this Government Decision, benefits under the Revised Assured Progress Scheme have been admissible to the employees in these posts but have not been actually paid, the benefits shall be admissible to the employees as per the earlier allowable promotion chain as on the date of this Government Decision and the benefits thereafter shall be paid by fixation of pay in the revised pay structure as per the said Government Decision.
5.सदर Considering the present service excellence of Gram Sevaks and Rural Development Officers, the Service Excellence List for the combined post should be prepared by the appointing authority at their level accordingly. This should ensure that it does not alter the existing service superiority.
6. The Maharashtra Zilla Parishad Zila Seva (Service Entry) Rules, 1967 shall be amended as required by creating the post of Gram Panchayat Officer by amalgamating both the posts of Gram Sevak and Village Development Officer.
