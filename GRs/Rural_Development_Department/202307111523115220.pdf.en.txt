Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 22.06.2023, 18 probationary Block Development Officers, Group-A of Maharashtra Development Service, Group-A have been allotted Panchayat Samitis for training of independent charge of the post of Block Development Officer, Group-A under the Combined Subordinate Training Programme-7, recommended from the State Services Main Examination 2019.
However, considering the vacancies of the post of Block Development Officer, Group-A in the Panchayat Samiti/ administrative reasons and requests, etc.
The revised allocation of Panchayat Samitis is being made for training of independent charge of the post of Block Development Officer, Group-A to some of the probationary officers as mentioned in Annexure-A by partially modifying the Government decision dated 22nd June, 2023 of the Department.
The remaining matters will remain as mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 22nd June, 2023.
All the concerned Divisional Commissioners/ CEOs, Zilla Parishad under their respective jurisdiction under the consolidated training programme.
The officers should be relieved immediately to join the revised independent charge.
Further, all the concerned Sub-Inspectors shall be required to indicate their names in Annexure-A.
