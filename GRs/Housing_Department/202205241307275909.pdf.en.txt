Preamble: Cadre-wise duties and responsibilities of employees working in Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority and Divisional Board. have been fixed under the order dated 12.04.2006. However, the government has received complaints about most of the chief officers and it has also been noticed that these chief officers are not using the powers given to them properly. Accordingly, to carry out the functions of Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority in a more effective and proper manner. In the order dated 12.04.2006, the Chief Officer was considering the issue of taking certain powers of Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur to the level of the government. The following decisions have been taken by the Government:
Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority As per the order dated 12.04.2006, the following powers vested in the Chief Officers, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur are being institutionalized:
9. To dispose vacant land or plots designated for residential use in approved scheme on lease as per provisions in MHAD Act, Rules/Regulations made thereunder prevailing Govt/MHADA / Board policies.
To take decision on bids of open plots, shops/ NR units etc. Which are advertised, in case powers are delegated to him.
3. To approve final sale price of T/s flats/ plots constructed by board and to approve upset price of shops / NR units /NR plots co-op plots to be used for hsg. which are to be advertised.
8. To do conveyance of properties for which NOC's for construction have been given for executing slum works, wherever necessary.
5.20% of all rights in respect of flats to be received by MHADA under Inclusive Housing and other schemes.
2. Chief Officer, Konkan, Pune, Nasik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur vide para. All pending cases mentioned in section 1 and all subsequent cases shall be submitted for Government approval with the approval and self-explanatory opinion of the Vice-Chairman and CEO, MHADA.
