As per Section 82 of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976, a repair cess is levied on buildings in Mumbai city and the island for repair/ reconstruction of old and dilapidated buildings. This amount of repair cess is appropriated, budgeted and transferred by the State Government to the Mumbai Building Repair and Reconstruction Fund under Section 86.
