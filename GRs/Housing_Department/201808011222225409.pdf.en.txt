Government Decisions The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana based on the theme Housing for All-2022.
(city) has begun. The scheme is being implemented in the state and the central government had provided funds for various projects in the state for 2017-18. The Central Government has re-validated these funds for 2018-19.
Accordingly, as approved by the Central Government, funds amounting to Rs. 3,10,80, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Ten Lakh Eighty Thousand only) are being released to the Nodal Agency and Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai for providing funds to the Municipal Council, Katol for construction of 601 houses in one of the projects in the accompanying returns under the BLC (Other than Schedule Castes& Component Scheduled Tribes) component.
So, after reviewing the current status of the project, the funds should be disbursed to the concerned agencies in a phased manner as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The report in this regard will be submitted to the government soon.
