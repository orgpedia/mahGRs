Government's decision:
Balasaheb Dnyandev Patil has been appointed as the part-time chairman of Konkan Housing and Area Development Board, one of the divisional boards under the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority.
By this decision of the government, Mr. Balasaheb Dnyandev Patil, Part-time Chairman, Konkan Housing and Area Development Board, is being given the status of Minister of State until further orders of the government. He is also entitled to the post of Minister of State for Services. 10.10/ E. No. 96/10/ CE., dated 13.03.2012 shall continue to be admissible.
