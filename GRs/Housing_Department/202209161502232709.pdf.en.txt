Government Circular: At present, the government has the power to approve and administer many development schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority. These powers need to be decentralized for speedy implementation of development works.
Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken to expedite the development works and to provide powers to MHADA at the authority/ zonal level: Development Control Regulations under Regulation 33 (7) of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority for redevelopment of buildings receiving cess 2.5 No-objection certificate for conversion of Floor Area Index Project 3.0 into Floor Area Index (FSI) Project with prior approval of the Government. REVIEW 2021/ Q. No.87/ DWP-1, Dy. The Government Circular dated 04.05.2021 issued is hereby cancelled and status quo ante as before the said Government Circular is being maintained.
