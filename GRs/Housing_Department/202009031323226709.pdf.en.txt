Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing) will be set up for the construction of houses in the state in addition to MHADA. This has been done as per the Government decision dated 11.12.2018. The government has also made a decision. As per the Government Decision dated 12.12.2018, Shri Rajendra Mirgane has been appointed as the Joint Chairman of the said Corporation. He has been given the status of Minister as on 13.08.2019.
It has been decided to reconstitute the boards and committees by cancelling the appointments of the members appointed to the government/ non-government boards and committees under each administrative division. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting. Accordingly, the matter of cancellation of appointments to non-government posts in Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing) was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: PRO-2018/ PRO 139/ GRIHANIDO-2
Government's decision:
The appointment of Mr. Rajendra Mirgane as the Joint Chairman of Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation has been cancelled with immediate effect.
With the cancellation of the appointment of Mr. Rajendra Mirgane to the post of Co-Chairman, the status of the Minister given to him is also coming to an end. Accordingly, the Government of India vide Decision No. S.A. 10.10/ P.S. No. 96/10/ C.U.D. The services permitted under 13.03.2012 will no longer be allowed.
