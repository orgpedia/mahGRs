The Proposal:
With a view to give impetus to Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, it has been decided to implement the scheme on Public-Private Partnership (PPP) and Joint Venture (JV) basis respectively. Decisions 1 and 2. Also, the procedure for distribution of houses to be constructed under the said schemes is under reference. The circular dated 08/02/2019 has been issued. Accordingly, under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, projects sanctioned under Public-Private Partnership (PPP) and Joint Venture of private developers have been allowed to be advertised by private developers, inviting applications from beneficiaries and allotting houses on first-come-first-serve basis.
However, in view of the large number of applications received under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in the recent past, it was not feasible to allocate houses only on the first-come-first-serve basis, and the matter was under the consideration of the Government to decide the exact policy. The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Resolution No: PRIO/ 2019/ P.No.21/ Part-1/ House Funds-2
Government Decision: Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), developers are being allowed to distribute houses in Public-Private Partnership (PPP) and Joint Venture projects by first-come-first-serve or lottery method. The developer will be able to distribute the houses by adopting the process which is convenient.
