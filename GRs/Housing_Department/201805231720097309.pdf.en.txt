The Proposal:
In exercise of the powers conferred on the Government under the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development (Allotment of Land) Regulations, 1982, vide Exchange No. 16 of 1982, for the distribution of residential/ non-residential plots of MHADA at the level of Govt. The decision was taken by a Cabinet sub-committee headed by the Minister of Housing. It was established on April 1, 2017.
The action taken regarding the revised policy of allotment and pricing of the remaining mat areas, break plots, no break plots or independently developable plots, recreation grounds etc. to be sold for reconstruction to the cooperative housing societies in the existing old colonies of MHADA. 6260, dated 4.6.2007, was being done mutually at the level of Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority without the approval of the Government. As per the Government Circular dated 14.07.2017, it has been ordered that the proposals for allotment of all these plots should be submitted for the approval of the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of Section 164 (1) of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976, the MHADA authority will have the power to distribute the adjacent Tit-Bit plot if the area of the original plot under the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority is up to 4000 sq.m. However, the area of the original plot of land in Abhinaya is 4000 sq.m.
It has been decided that the powers to distribute the alienated land, if it is more than the area, will remain with the Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted under Regulation 16 of the MHADA Act, 1982.
