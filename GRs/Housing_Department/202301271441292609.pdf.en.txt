Preamble: As per the directions of the Central Government, a total Central Share of Rs. 875.58 crore has been sanctioned under various components of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) for the financial year 2022-23.
Government Decision: Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), the Central Government Read No. It is hereby approved to disburse a total amount of Rs. 875.58 crore (Rupees Eight Hundred and Seventy Five Crores Fifty Eight Lakhs Only) to the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) for disbursement to the implementing agencies as approved under orders 1 to 3 and as prescribed in the said order.
Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), the government had approved the release of only the state share of funds from the Maharashtra Shelter Fund (Extra Budgetary Resources). However, since the funds are not reflected in the PFMS system and the letter No. 4 of the Central Government has informed about the disbursement of the State Account Fund from the State Budget Fund, the funds under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) will no longer be disbursed from the Maharashtra Shelter Fund.
