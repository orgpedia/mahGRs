Preamble: As per the policy of the Government, it was decided to adopt Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Aided Schemes of Housing Department. The government was considering declaring a State Scheme Manager (SSM) and a First Level Agency to implement the system.
Government has decided to declare a nodal officer for implementation of Public Financial Management System (PFMS), State Scheme Manager (SSM) and First Level Agency for each Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
Accordingly, State Level Scheme Manager (SSM) and First Level Agency for Centrally Sponsored Schemes and Centrally Aided Schemes of Housing Department are being declared as follows.
