As per Section 82 of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976, a repair cess is levied on buildings in Mumbai city and the island for repair/ reconstruction of old and dilapidated buildings. Also, under Section 85, it is recovered from the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and the remaining amount, excluding the deduction of 5 per cent of the total recovery, is deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. The amount of repair cess and contribution is appropriately, budgeted and disbursed by the State Government to be transferred to the Mumbai Building Repair and Reconstruction Fund as per Section 86 (1) and Section 97 (1).
