The proposal-
Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), the government was considering to allocate the funds made available by the central government for various projects in the state to MHADA, the steering agency for making available to the concerned agencies.
Government's decision
The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) based on the concept of Housing for All-2022. The scheme is being implemented in the State and Rs. 42,20,40, 000/- (Rs. Forty two crore twenty lakh forty thousand only) is being distributed to the Steering Agency, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai.
So, after reviewing the current status of the project, the funds should be disbursed to the concerned agencies in a phased manner as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The report in this regard will be submitted to the government soon.
Expenditure on this purpose shall be met from the provision budgeted under the head Demand No. Q-3, Head Accounts 2216, Housing General, Assistance to 103 Housing Boards etc. (08) Housing for All (08) (01) Grants to Housing Implementing Agencies for All (PRIs) Central Share (60%) (2216 2996) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried).
