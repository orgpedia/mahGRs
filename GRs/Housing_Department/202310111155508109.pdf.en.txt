The Proposal:
In order to ensure effective implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) in the State and to speed up the progress of the scheme and to provide houses to the beneficiaries within the prescribed time frame, Reference No. 3 A State Level Project Management Unit (PMU) has been set up through a Government Resolution dated 14 th August, 2023. As per the guidelines of the Central Government, 1% of the funds for administrative expenses are received from the Central Government. However, considering the time taken for receipt of the said funds from the Central Government, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds from Maharashtra Shelter Fund for administrative and other expenses for the State Level Project Management System (PMU) for various expenses such as travel expenses, system support, overhead expenses, etc., as per the guidelines of the Central Government.
Government's decision:

The State Project Management Unit (PMU) will be responsible for spending this fund for various administrative and other works such as travel expenses, system support, overhead expenses, etc. as per the guidelines of the Central Government.
The fund should be managed by the joint signature of two gazetted officers, namely, the Principal Officer and the Office Officer of the Housing Department (Grihanidho-2), appointed by the State Government for the State Level Project Management System (PMU).
All accounting records and records relating to the said fund should be maintained by the officers/ employees of the accounting branch of the Mahahausing in order to cut down the administrative expenses.
Monthly Reconciliation of the accounts of the fund should be conducted through the State Level Project Management Unit (PMU) and submitted to the MHADA Authority as the Housing Department as well as the Steering Agency.
The MHADA Authority will be responsible for reimbursing the expenditure incurred from the Maharashtra Shelter Fund from the funds received from the Central Government.
