Introduction: Under the New Chandrapur City Development Scheme, the construction of a bridge over the Irai River will be completed at a cost of Rs. 6519.30 lakh has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 27.02.2017. As the cost of construction of this bridge has increased due to various reasons, the proposal to give revised administrative approval to the cost of construction of this bridge was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Under the New Chandrapur City Development Scheme, the construction of a bridge over the Irai River will be completed at a cost of Rs. 8371.00 Lakhs has been provided by Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Board, Nagpur vide its letter No. 703/206/ 2018 dated 10th April, 2018 has been submitted to the Government for revised administrative approval.
The increase in the cost of construction of the bridge on the river Irai is due to the increase in the Goods and Services Tax, price increase, change in the design. The revised budget of the bridge is Rs. 8371.00 lakhs i.e., 5% of the amount considered for Centes charges of Rs. 364.53 lakh has been exempted by the Public Works Department while giving technical approval to the proposal.
