The Proposal:
Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, the State has set a target of constructing 19.40 lakh houses by 2022. In order to meet this target within the stipulated time frame, the Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing) has been formed under the Government decision of Reference No. 1, which can work full time in addition to MHADA for the construction of houses in the state in connection with the construction of large settlement projects under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. Similarly, Mr. Rajendra Mirgane has been appointed to the post of Joint Chairman of the Corporation as per the Government decision of Reference No. 2.
As per the provisions of the said Government Resolution, the Chairman of the said Corporation has the power to make changes in the structure and administrative structure of the Board of Directors of the Corporation. The Prime Minister has. Accordingly, the government was considering the appointment of Mr. Sanjay Upadhyay to the post of Vice President of Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation.
Government's decision:
Shri Sanjay Upadhyay, 703, Sadashiv Society, Shivaji Nagar, Shahaji Raje Marg, Vile Parle (East) Mumbai-57 has been appointed as Vice Chairman of Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation.
