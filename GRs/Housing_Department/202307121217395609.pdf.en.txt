Preamble: Appointment of developer in the stalled schemes for completion of slum rehabilitation scheme in Mumbai through tender process and implementation of Abhay Yojana. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 25.05.2022.
Proposals have been received from the Slum Rehabilitation Authority in respect of both the above schemes.
The government has approved the list of developers of the Slum Rehabilitation Authority to appoint the developer through a tender process.
Under the Abhay Yojana, 31 proposals of financial institutions have been received by the Slum Rehabilitation Authority. It is necessary to examine various aspects of the developers/ financial institutions and take a decision regarding granting approval to the concerned financial institution to implement the stalled slum rehabilitation projects funded by these financial institutions. Therefore, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary (Housing) to examine the tenders received from the shortlisted developers for completion of the stalled slum rehabilitation schemes and take a decision on the proposal submitted by the Slum Rehabilitation Authority and to approve the completion of the stalled schemes to the financial institutions/ competent agencies authorized by them.
Government Decision: A Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary (Housing) to examine the tenders received from the shortlisted developers for completion of the stalled slum rehabilitation schemes and take a decision on the proposal submitted by the Slum Rehabilitation Authority and to approve the completion of the stalled schemes to the financial institutions/ competent agencies authorized by them under the ABHAY Yojana as follows:
