The Proposal:
As the number of computers and printers was not sufficient for the office work in the housing department, the matter of purchasing new computers and printers was under the consideration of the government.
At present, according to the Government Policy, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Government Decision No. Narrow- 2016/ No.215/ Industries-4, Dy. As on 24th August, 2017, it has been made mandatory to procure goods and services from Government e-Marketplace (GeM) (https:// gem. gov. in) portal developed under National Procurement Policy of Government of India for procurement of all Government Departments and to ensure savings in procurement period, supply of quality goods, fair price and transparency in the entire procurement process. Accordingly, computers and printer materials have been purchased as per the prescribed procedure and the matter of approving the expenditure and grant for the said purchase was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: 1. 15 computers and 10 printers along with scanners have been purchased for the Housing Department and these purchases have been made from the Government-E-Marketplace (GeM) (https:// gem. gov. in) portal as per the prevailing policy of the Government. The purchase amount is Rs. Computers and printer materials have been procured from the lowest bidder (i. e., PERFECT HOUSE PRIVATE LIMITED, MUMBAI-400 034 and ICON INFOTECH PRIVATE LIMITED, MUMBAI-400 007) by adopting online bidding method for more than Rs. 3.00 lakhs. For the Rs. 12,77,485/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand Four Hundred and Eighty Five only) is being sanctioned. Below are the details of the purchase.
