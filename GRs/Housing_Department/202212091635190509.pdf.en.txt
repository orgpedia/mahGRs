GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PRIO-2022/ PR No. 153/ GRANIDHO-2, dated 5th December, 2022
Preamble: A committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Housing, under the relevant government for various difficulties faced in the construction of 39,760 flats approved under 6 projects on a total of 7 plot areas being implemented by the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), as well as for checking the financial and physical capacity of the tenderer, reviewing how many flats can be built directly on the approved site, etc. The report of the Committee is to be submitted to the Government by 20.12.2022. In view of the scope of work of the Committee, it was contemplated to constitute a Sub-Committee for site inspection and house site determination and financial feasibility study to provide useful inputs to the Committee.
Government Decision: Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), the following two sub-committees are being formed to examine 6 projects on a total of 7 plot areas being implemented by the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation.
