The Proposal:
In the above Reference No. 1 of this Department, under the Government Decision dated 4th March, 2015, guidelines have been issued for the construction of protective walls for the protection of slum dwellers living on hill slopes/ dangerous crevices in Greater Mumbai. Accordingly, the construction of retaining walls up to a height of only 9 meters is carried out by the Mumbai Slum Improvement Board as an operational mechanism and funds are provided to the Mutual Improvement Board through the Planning Department.
Also, the construction of retaining walls of more than 9 meters height is carried out by the Public Works Department or other appropriate agencies. Guideline No. 3 of this Government Resolution is as follows:
Instruction No. 3) The above mentioned retaining wall works up to a height of 9 meters should be carried out in the hill slope slums irrespective of the declared/ undeclared, authorized or unauthorized slum in order to avoid possible loss of life and property.
