The Proposal:
In 1998, the government decided to set up a new Chandrapur city on an area of 1391.71 hectares in Chandrapur district. The Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) has been appointed as the special planning authority for the development of the notified area in New Chandrapur. The scheme has been notified by the Urban Development Department, Govt. It was approved on June 30, 1998. The project includes power supply, water supply, main road works, land acquisition works and bridge work on Irai river in New Chandrapur colony. This is a long-term project and is being implemented as per the availability of funds. Various works under the scheme have been accorded administrative approval by MHADA from time to time.
2. Accordingly, the Chief Officer, Nagpur Housing and Area Development Board, Nagpur has issued reference No. 1 here. As per the representation made vide letter dated 03.07.2023, the matter of release of funds to MHADA Authority was under consideration of the Government.

Terms and Conditions:
1) The grant must be used for the purpose for which it was granted.
The Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) will have to submit a utility certificate to the government regarding the expenditure from this fund.
The funds should be drawn from the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai with the signature of the Controller of Finance, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai.
