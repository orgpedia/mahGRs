About 672 original residents of these chawls are still waiting for rehabilitation flats under the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) for the redevelopment of Siddhartha Nagar Cooperative Housing Society (Patrachal) in Goregaon (West), Mumbai. Similarly, there are complaints that the residents have not been paid rent for several months by the concerned developers. Also, this case. The process of redevelopment has been delayed as the High Court has jurisdiction.
Similarly, MHADA has released 306 flats constructed on the basis of the receipt of MHADA two years ago. However, the houses have not been handed over yet. Accordingly, in pursuance of the Government Decision dated 16.1.2020, a one-man committee headed by Mr. Johny Joseph, Retired Chief Secretary has been appointed to suggest measures within the next 15 days. However, the committee report is yet to be submitted. The committee has been asked to submit its report by the deadline. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the term of the committee.
