For the year 2019-20, a sum of Rs. 2,70,80, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Seventy Lakh Eighty Thousand only) for the five months August to December, 2019. 49,65,000/- (Rupees Ninety Five Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand only) as above. 2 Government circulars have been issued. Out of this, for the five months from August to December 2019, Rs. 29,79,000 (Rupees Thirty-nine Lakhs Eighty-nine Thousand only) is being distributed to the offices of the competent authority under the Maharashtra Rent Control Act at Konkan, Pune, Nashik and Nagpur as per the attached statement.
The necessary and mandatory expenditure from the provisions received by the concerned offices should be made for a period of five months from August 2019 to December 2019. While making such expenditure, the instructions of the Government Circular referred to in Reference No. 1 above should be strictly followed. Controlling authorities should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned grant. Also, make sure that each payment has the correct headings and 10-digit computer code. The expenditure incurred for each month in Form 10 should be made available to the government before the 5th of the next 3 months. Drawing and disbursing officers should coordinate the expenditure with the Accountant General's office. The details of expenditure on the programme should be displayed in the Scheme Information System (MP SIMS).
