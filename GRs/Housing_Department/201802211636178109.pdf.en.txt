Preamble In order to provide a house to every citizen of the country by 2022, the Central Government has issued a contextual Government Resolution in June, 2015 for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) in the State with the approval of the State Cabinet.
The central government has approved the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in 382 cities of the state. The State has set a target of constructing 19.40 lakh houses by 2022. Under this scheme, the eligible beneficiaries of the projects approved by the Central Sanctioning and Controlling Committee are entitled to receive Rs. The government was considering to use the grant of Rs 1 lakh from the Maharashtra Shelter Fund.
Government Decision The grant admissible to the eligible beneficiaries of the projects approved by the Central Sanctioning and Controlling Committee under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana is Rs. 1 lakh per beneficiary from Maharashtra Shelter Fund.
The amount of grant under the State Accounts will be disbursed to the concerned implementing agencies through the Mission Director with the approval of the Government.
