The Proposal:
A one-man committee under the chairmanship of Mr. Johny Joseph, Retired Chief Secretary, has been appointed to suggest measures within the next 15 days in accordance with the Government Decision, dated 16/01/2020, regarding the redevelopment of Siddhartha Nagar Cooperative Housing Society (Patrachal) in Goregaon (West), Mumbai under the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA). However, the committee report is yet to be submitted. Considering the demand of the concerned to submit their views to the said Committee, the said Committee has been given an extension of 15 days vide Government Resolution, dated 12/02/2020.
Meanwhile, the entire country has been put under a lockdown due to the coronavirus outbreak. The lockdown has not yet been lifted completely. So there is a delay in getting the required legal information, various analytical information required from the engineering officers. A lot of work to do on the committee
There are problems. Therefore, the ongoing lockdown in the country/ state should be relaxed or lifted.
The committee has requested the government to give at least a 30-day extension to the one-member committee since its arrival. Therefore, the government was considering to extend the term of the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 16/1/2020, read with the one-man committee appointed under the chairmanship of Mr. Johny Joseph, Retired Chief Secretary, to suggest measures regarding the redevelopment project of Siddhartha Nagar Cooperative Housing Society in Patrachar Colony, Goregaon, Mumbai, the State Government has decided to relax the lockdown in the State i.e. They are being given an extension of 30 days from 1.7.2020 to submit their report.
