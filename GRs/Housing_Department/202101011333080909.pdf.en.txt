The matter of appointing a supplier for recycling and supply of toner of computer printers for office work is under consideration of the Government for a period of 1 year from 31-12-2021. The Government of Industry, Energy and Labor Department Decision No. As per para 3.2.3 of the revised Rules of Procedure for procurement of Office by Government Departments under Section 82/ Part-II/ Industry-4, dated 1st December, 2016, the expenditure of the Department on toner recycling of computer printers in a financial year is Rs. 3,00,000/-, the tariff sheets were sought from the suppliers in a sealed cover. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a supplier who would recycle the toner with the lowest price by comparing the tariff received from the suppliers was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Since the rate of recycling and supply of toner for computer printers by R.K. Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai, is the lowest among the suppliers who have been asked for the tariff for toner recycling of computer printers in the department, the Housing Department (itself), Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of Maharashtra, has been directed to ensure that the price of toner for computer printers recycled by R.K. Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai, is at least Rs. 1-01-2021. R.K. Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai is being appointed as the supplier for recycling and supply of toner of computer printers required during the period of one year 31-12-2021 at the rate mentioned in the following statement.
