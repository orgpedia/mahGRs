Read: Government Decision, Housing Department, Mahahausing/ 2017/ p. Cr. 161/ Grinidhon-2, dated 11.12.2018.
Preamble: Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, the State has set a target of construction of 19.40 lakh houses by 2022. In order to achieve this target within the stipulated time frame, the Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing), which can work full-time in addition to MHADA for the construction of houses in the state in connection with the construction of large settlement projects under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, has been set up. This has been done as per the Government decision dated 11.12.2018. As per the decision of the government, a provision has been made to appoint non-official members to the post of co-chairman of the corporation.
The Maharashtra government has decided to appoint non-official members to the post of joint chairman of the Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 11.12.2018, Shri Rajendra Sukhdev Mirgane, Rt. Properties, Oil Mill Chowk, Barshi, G. Solapur are being appointed as co-chairpersons of Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation for a period till further orders of the government.
