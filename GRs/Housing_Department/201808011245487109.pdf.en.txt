To approve the release of first instalment of funds to Municipal Council Katol under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) for the financial year 2017-18 for the year 2018-19 for the project of Schedule Costs Component (BLC).
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Housing
Government Resolution No: PIO-2018/ P.No.33/ GRIHANS-2
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai-32.
Date is August 1, 2018.
For the financial year 2017-18, the Government was considering to allocate the funds made available by the Central Government for various projects in the State under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) to MHADA, the Steering Agency for making available the funds to the concerned agencies.
Government Decision- The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) based on the concept of Housing for All-2022. The scheme is being implemented in the state and the central government had provided funds for various projects in the state for 2017-18. The Central Government has re-validated these funds for 2018-19.
Accordingly, as approved by the Central Government, the Municipal Council, Katol has been sanctioned a sum of Rs. 18,60,000/- (Rs. Eighteen lakh sixty thousand only) is being disbursed to the Steering Agency and Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai for providing funds.
So, after reviewing the current status of the project, the funds should be disbursed to the concerned agencies in a phased manner as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The report in this regard will be submitted to the government soon.
