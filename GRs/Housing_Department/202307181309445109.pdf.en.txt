The Proposal:
A Study Group has been constituted under the coordination of Principal Secretary (Housing) under the Government decision dated 18.09.2020 in Reference No. 2 to study and submit a report to the Government on the rehabilitation of slums in major cities of the State except Slum Rehabilitation Authorities of Mumbai, Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad, Nagpur and Mumbai Metropolitan Region Slum Rehabilitation Authority. The meeting was presided over by the Additional Chief Secretary, Home Department. The 7th meeting was held on 01.02.2023. According to the minutes of the meeting, Comments were invited through a letter dated February 9, 2023. No comments were received from any other office except the comments of the CEO, Zopupra, Pune. Therefore, a semi-official letter has been sent with the signature of the Additional Chief Secretary (Housing) to send feedback immediately in accordance with the chronology of the meeting. After receiving the report, the committee is yet to take a decision.

It might take some time. Therefore, it is necessary to give an extension of a few more months for the further administrative work of the study group. Therefore, it is under consideration of the Government to grant extension of time from June 2022 to December 2023 for submission of report to the Government by the Study Group Committee constituted under the coordination of Principal Secretary (Housing) vide Government Resolution dated 18/09/2020.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Committee of the Study Group constituted under the coordination of Principal Secretary (Housing) under the Government Decision dated 18/09/2020 in Reference No. 2 to study and recommend to the Government regarding the establishment of Slum Rehabilitation Authority for Mumbai/ Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad and Nagpur and other areas of the State except Mumbai Metropolitan Region is being extended till December 2023.
