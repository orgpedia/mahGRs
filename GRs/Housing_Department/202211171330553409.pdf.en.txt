Preamble: As per the Central Government Order, a total amount of Rs. 61,93,200/- has been sanctioned in the financial year 2022-23 for carrying out Geo-Tagging activities for BLC Component under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban). Accordingly, the matter was under consideration of the government to disburse the funds to the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA), the nodal agency for providing the same to the respective implementing agencies.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the orders of the Central Government under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), a total amount of Rs. 61,93,200/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand Two Hundred only) is being sanctioned to be released to the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) for disbursement to the implementing agencies.
2. Demand for funds No. Q-3, Main Accounts Head 2216, Housing (08) Housing for All (08) (01) Grants to Implementing Agencies for Housing for All (PRIs) (Central Share 60%) (Programme) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non- Salary) are being disbursed under Account Head 22162996.
The said funds should be drawn from the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai with the signature of the Controller of Finance, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai. The funds should be utilised for the purpose for which they were sanctioned.
In this case, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Controlling Officer, the Controller of Finance, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority will remain.
These orders are being issued in concurrence with the Department of Finance vide its Informal Reference No. 304/ Expenditure-3 dated 28.10.2022.
