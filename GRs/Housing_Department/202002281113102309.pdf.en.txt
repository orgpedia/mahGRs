GOVERNMENT CLEARS: In the paragraph of the Government Decision dated 17th July, 2019, the Government shall have the power to make periodic and premature transfers of all officers of Group A and Group B (Class-I and Class-II) cadres in the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority and all Divisional Boards under it. Instead, the following paragraph should be read:" The Government shall have the power to effect periodic/ requisition transfers and premature transfers of all officers of Group" A" and Group" B" (Class-I and Class-II) cadres in the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority and all the Divisional Boards under it. Also, the proposals for postings to be made after promotion from the lower cadre to the post of Group' A" and Group' B" in MHADA will have to get the approval of the government. Similarly, approval of the government will be required before revoking the suspension of the concerned Group A and B employees/ officers in cases where a case has been registered/ proceedings are pending by the Anti-Corruption Bureau.
