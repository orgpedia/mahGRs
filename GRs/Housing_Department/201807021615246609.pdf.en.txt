The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Housing

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: 30th of June 2018
The Proposal:
A well-planned programme needs to be chalked out to accelerate the pace of housing construction in the state to provide houses to all. Further, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Committee to recommend effective and improved administrative measures such as construction of affordable houses for economically weaker sections and other sections, expediting the development of chawls and checking the influx of migrants in transit camps and what kind of effective administrative measures should be taken so that infiltration could be curbed and additional houses could be constructed at low cost within a short period.
Government's decision:
Mr. Johny Joseph (B.S.), Chairman, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA), has been appointed as the Chairman of the Authority to study the administrative difficulties in the functioning of the Authority and to recommend effective administrative measures to the Government for expediting the work. A single-member committee has been formed.
The term of the Committee is six months and the Committee shall be required to study the measures taken for administrative, financial and legal reforms in the above mentioned items and other ancillary matters and submit its report to the Government within six months.
Mr. John Joseph, Sr. Orders regarding remuneration and other concessions of the P.S. shall be issued separately.
4. The meeting of the said Committee shall be arranged and the facilities concerned shall be provided by the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority.
