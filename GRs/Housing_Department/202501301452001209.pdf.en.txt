The Proposal: -
The Demand No. RR, 2070-Other Administrative Services and Q-3, 2216-Housing are the mandatory expenses under the head 'Co-Other Expenditure'. 800-Other Expenditure The amount of the expenditure shown under the subheading 800-Other Expenditure is not known for what purpose it has been spent, and the expenditure is not covered under the subheading 800-Other Expenditure by the Accountant General (Lt. and H.) -1, selecting an alternate new subheading in "Annexure-10" made available by the Mumbai Office, seeking approval of the Finance Department and the Accountant General (LA & H) -1, Mumbai Office, as well as making available a new eight-digit notation.
The department was informed. It was under the consideration of the government to replace this 800-other-expenditure subheading with an appropriate alternative subheading.
The Government's Decisions:
The Demand No. FY-2, 1970 - Other Administrative Services FT RB, 2216 - The Government is approving the introduction of an alternate new subheading for the mandatory expenditure under the head Housing, 800 Other Expenditure instead of the subheading as follows. This is the title of the account
The 2025-26 will come into effect from this financial year.
