The Proposal:
The repair cess is levied on buildings in the city of Mumbai and the island under Section 82 of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976 for repair / reconstruction of old dilapidated cessed buildings in the city of Mumbai Island. Also, under Section 85, it is collected from the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and the remaining amount is deposited in the State Consolidated Fund, excluding 5% of the total revenue. The amount of the B contribution of the amendment tax is appropriated, budgeted and disbursed by the state government as per section 86 (1) b section 97 (1) to be transferred to the Mumbai Building Repair and Reconstruction Fund.
