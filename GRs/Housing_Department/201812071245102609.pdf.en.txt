94/ E-2, dated 22.11.2018 2) Application dated 26.11.2018 by Mr. Prasad Baliram Ukarde, Deputy Collector
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Government Duties Act, 2005 and subject to the prescribed terms and conditions of deputation as prescribed in Annexure 2 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Personal Retirement and Suspension, Dismissal, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri Prasad Baliram Ukarde, District Supply Officer, Palghar (Non-Resigning Officer) has been posted as Secretary, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai (by downgrading the post of Secretary, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority to the cadre of Sub-Collector). The deputation under the order dated 22.11.2018 has been appointed for a period of one year in the first instance.
As per the said order of the Revenue and Forest Department, the services of Mr. Prasad Ukarde have been handed over to the Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai for appointment to the said post.
Forest and Revenue Department. Mr. Ukarde, Deputy Collector has already been relieved from his earlier appointment to join the said post in pursuance of the order dated 22.11.2018. Accordingly, Mr. Prasad Ukarde has requested this department to present him in the said post.
Accordingly, Shri Prasad Baliram Ukarde, Deputy Collector and District Supply Officer, Palghar is being appointed as Secretary, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Bandra (East), Mumbai on deputation for a period of one year initially.
