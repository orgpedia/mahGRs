The proposal-
Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), the government was considering to allocate the funds made available by the central government for various projects in the state to MHADA, the steering agency for making available to the concerned agencies.
Government's decision
The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) based on the concept of Housing for All-2022. The scheme is being implemented in the state and the funds mentioned in the accompanying statement under the BLC and AHP (Scheduled Caste Component) components provided by the Central Government for various projects in the state are being distributed to the Steering Agency, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No.               | Component Number   | Funds sanctioned  |
| 1                    | BLC (SC component) | Rs. 8,30,40, 000/ |
| 2                    | AHP (SC component) | Rs. 98,40,000/-   |
| Total (ST Component) | Rs. 9,28,80, 000/- |
-----------------------------------------------------------------
(Rupees Nine Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Eighty Thousand only)
So, after reviewing the current status of the project, the funds should be disbursed to the concerned agencies in a phased manner as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The report in this regard will be submitted to the government soon.
