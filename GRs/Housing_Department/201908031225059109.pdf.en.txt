Preamble: Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, the State has set a target of constructing 19.40 lakh houses by 2022. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) is required to be approved by the Central Government by December, 2019 to achieve the target within the stipulated time frame. Meanwhile, the Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing) was set up to build houses in the state in line with the construction of large settlement projects under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. This has been done as per the Government decision dated 11.12.2018.
The Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MHDC) was supposed to participate in the meetings of the State Level Appraisal Committee (SLAC) and the State Level Sanctioning and Controlling Committee (SLSMC). Following this, the government decided:
