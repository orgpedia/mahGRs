The Proposal:
Housing Department (s). 01.01.2019 to D. The matter of appointing a supplier for supply of Recycling Toner for the period 31.12.2019 (1 year) is under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, invocation of the prevailing procedure was done on 11.12.2018 from the website of the Government of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the rates received by the Department were considered in the meeting of the Divisional Purchase Committee held on 26.12.2018. Representatives of the association were present at the meeting. In the meeting, a decision is being taken to appoint a supplier for the supply of recycling toner for this department from among the tenderers who have qualified in the technical examination.
