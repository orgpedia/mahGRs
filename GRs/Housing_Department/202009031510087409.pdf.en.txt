Read:- 1) Government Decision, Housing Department, No. Allotment 2018/ S.No.143/ Grnip, Dy. 28.3.2018 2) Government Decision, Housing Department, No. Meeting 2016/ No. 04/ Grnip, Dy. 16.1.2020, 3) Government Decision, Housing Department, No. Meeting 2016/ No. 04/ Grnip, Dy. 12.2.2020 and 4) Government Decision, Housing Department, No. Meeting 2016/ No. 04/ Grnip, Dy. 24.7.2020 Preamble: In pursuance of the redevelopment of Siddhartha Nagar Co-operative Housing Society (Patrachal), Goregaon (West), Mumbai under Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA), the Government of Maharashtra has decided to take up the construction of a new building at Vacha. As on 16.1.2020, a one-man committee under the chairmanship of Mr. Johny Joseph, Retired Chief Secretary has been appointed to suggest measures within 15 days. However, the committee report is yet to be submitted. After considering the request of the concerned to submit their views to the said committee, read the government decision here. As on 12.2.2020, 15 days extension has been granted.
Due to the new Corona epidemic in the state/ country, lockdown (lockdown) has been imposed and it has not been completely lifted yet. Therefore, the committee had requested the government to give an extension of at least 30 days to the one-member committee since the ongoing lockdown was relaxed or lifted in the state. Read on to the No. 4 the decision of the government. To submit a report to this Committee by 24.7.2020. The period has been extended by 30 days from 1.7.2020. However, considering that the lockdown has not yet been lifted completely and the limited attendance of office staff due to the lockdown, the committee has requested the government to give an extension of 2 months to submit a report to the committee. Accordingly, the matter of extending the last term of the committee was under the consideration of the government.
