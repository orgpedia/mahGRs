Read- Government Resolution No. 2004/ No. 31/ P.S. 3, D. 23.03.2005.
Preamble: Under the provisions of Section 40 of the Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 4 offices of the competent authority, Rent Control Act, have been created in the state of Konkan, Pune, Nashik and Nagpur to adjudicate any dispute between landlord and tenant falling under the Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999. The Competent Authority for Konkan Division in the said offices, the post is as per the aforesaid Government Resolution No. 2004/ No. 31/ PR.III, D. 23.03.2005 and the post of Competent Authority for Nagpur Division was not created.
The competent authority in the above 4 offices, regarding the empowerment of the Rent Control Offices. In the High Court against Shri Sanjay Jagtap, the Government of Maharashtra, in Writ Petition No. 5188/2014 has been filed. In this case The High Court had directed that for effective implementation of the Rent Control Act, rules should be made under Section 57 of the Act, rules should be made for regular appointment of posts of all competent authorities in the state, minimum staff and basic facilities in the office should be provided at the government level.

Government Resolution No: BHANIKA-2016/ PR No.45/ Part-1/ GRANIDHO-1
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the order of the Hon'ble High Court to regularize the posts of Competent Authority in all the offices of the Competent Authority, it is decided to revive the post of Competent Authority in Konkan Division and create a new post of Competent Authority for Nagpur Division. The approval was given by a high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the Rent Control Act Court, the Competent Authority in Konkan Division (Pay Scale 15600-39100 Grade.
5400) and the creation of a new post of Competent Authority (Pay Scale 15600-39100 Grade.Pay 5400) for the Rent Control Act Court, Nagpur Division and the expenditure on both the posts is being approved by this Government.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of these posts should be covered under the head" Demand No.Q-2, Accounts Title 2070 Other Administrative Services, 800 Other Expenses (00) (00) (02) Office of the Establishment of Competent Authority under the Bombay Rent Control Act (Dattamat) (2070 0512)" and it should be covered by the financial provision approved for the financial year 2017-18.
This Government Decision is being issued under the Finance Department's A.No. No. 66/ P.C., dated January 24, 2018.
