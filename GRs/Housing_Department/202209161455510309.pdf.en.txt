Government circular:
Currently, the government has the power to approve and administer several development schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority. These powers need to be decentralized for speedy implementation of development works. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken to expedite the development works and to provide powers to MHADA at the authority/ zonal level: Allotment 2021/ S.No.60/ Household, D. The Government Circular issued under 19.07, 2021 is hereby cancelled and the status quo ante as before the said Government Circular is being maintained.
