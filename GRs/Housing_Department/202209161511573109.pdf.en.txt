At present, the government has the power to approve and administer several development schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority. These powers need to be decentralized for speedy implementation of development works. Accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions with a view to empower MHADA at the Authority/ Regional level and to expedite the development works:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Regarding the establishment of a Civil Services Board to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the transfers of all officers of Group-A and Group-B (Class-1 and Class-2) cadres in the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority and all divisional boards under it, No. The Government Order issued on 20.05.2022 is hereby cancelled and the situation prevailing prior to the issuance of the said Government Order is maintained.
