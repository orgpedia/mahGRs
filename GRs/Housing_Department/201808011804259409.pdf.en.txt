For the financial year 2017-18, the Government was considering to allocate the funds made available by the Central Government for various projects in the State under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) to MHADA, the Steering Agency for making available the funds to the concerned agencies.
Government Decisions The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) based on the concept of Housing for All-2022. The Government of India has provided funds for various projects in the State for the year 2017-18. The Central Government has re-validated these funds for 2018-19. The Finance Department has also approved the proposal.
Accordingly, a total of 3805 housing projects in the 6 projects in the accompanying returns under AHP (SC Component) component as approved by the Central Government have been sanctioned at a cost of Rs. 4,32,43, 000/- (Rs. Four crore thirty two lakh thirty three thousand rupees) is being disbursed to the Steering Agency, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai for providing funds.
So, after reviewing the current status of the project, the funds should be disbursed to the concerned system in a phased manner as per the guidelines of the government. The report in this regard will be submitted to the government soon.
