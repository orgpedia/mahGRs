Preamble: The Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 302.50 crore has been sanctioned and the amount has been credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds received under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana to the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA), the nodal agency for providing funds to the concerned implementing agencies, was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA), the nodal agency for disbursing funds as approved by the Central Government to the implementing agencies mentioned in the reference order of the Central Government, has been allocated Rs. 302.50 crore (Rs. Three hundred and two crore rupees only) is being sanctioned through this.
The Demand No. Q3, Main Account Head 2216, Housing (08) Housing for All (08) (01) Grants to Implementing Agencies for Housing for All (PMAY) (Central Share 60%)
(Programme) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) are being distributed under head 22162996.
The funds should be drawn from the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai, under the signature of the Controller of Finance, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai. The funds should be utilised for the purpose for which they were sanctioned.
In accordance with this Government decision, the payments to be submitted to the Treasury will be counter-signed by the Controlling Officer of MHADA.
