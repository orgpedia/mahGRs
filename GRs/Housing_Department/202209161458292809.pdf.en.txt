At present, the government has the power to approve and administer several development schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority. These powers need to be decentralized for speedy implementation of development works.
Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken with a view to empower MHADA at the authority/ zonal level and to expedite the development works: Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA). The Government Sanction Sheet issued on 29.06.2021 is hereby cancelled and the status quo ante as before the said Government Sanction Sheet is being maintained.
