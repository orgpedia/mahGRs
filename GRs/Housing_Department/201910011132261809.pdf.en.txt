Government order:
The Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing) has been set up with the objective of expediting the construction of houses under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana and completing the set targets within the stipulated time frame. As per the orders of the Government, the said Corporation is being managed by Mr. Ramchandra Co. Dhanawade, Deputy Secretary, Housing Department, was given additional charge of the post of Managing Director. Additional charge of the post has been given to Mr. Ramchandra. Dhananjay today. It will be effective from September 30, 2019.
Shankar Bhise, Director of Administration, Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing).
