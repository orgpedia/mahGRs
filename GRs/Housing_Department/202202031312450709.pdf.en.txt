Preamble: A Study Group has been constituted under the coordination of Principal Secretary (Housing) vide Government Resolution dated 18.09.2020 in Reference No. 2 to study and submit a report to the Government on the rehabilitation of slums in major cities of the State except Slum Rehabilitation Authorities of Mumbai, Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad, Nagpur and Mumbai Metropolitan Region Slum Rehabilitation Authority. The Committee was required to submit its report within six months from the date of the decision. Since the formation of the Study Group Committee has completed six months on March 2021 and the work of the Committee is in the final stage, for administrative reasons, the Committee of the Study Group was given a nine-month extension as per the Government decision dated August 27, 2021. The extension has been extended till December 31, 2021. However, it was under consideration of the government to give another 6 months for further administrative work of the study group.
