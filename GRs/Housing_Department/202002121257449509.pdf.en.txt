Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation (MahaHousing) will be set up for the construction of houses in the state in addition to MHADA. This has been done as per the Government decision dated 11.12.2018. The government has also made a decision. As on 03.06.2019, Shri Sanjay Upadhyay has been appointed as Vice Chairman of Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation.
It has been decided to reconstitute the boards and committees by cancelling the appointments of the members appointed to the government/ non-government boards and committees under each administrative division. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting. Accordingly, the matter of cancellation of appointments to non-government posts in Maharashtra Housing Corporation (MahaHousing) was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Sanjay Upadhyay has been appointed as the vice-chairman of Maharashtra Housing Development Corporation.
