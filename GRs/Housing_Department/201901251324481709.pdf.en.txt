The proposal-
In the 8th Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) meeting held on 28.04.2016, the Central share of Rs. 40.13 crore had been sanctioned and disbursed to the State. Accordingly, the Government has sanctioned Rs. 5.685 crore and Rs. 34.267 crore has been disbursed. However, the project got delayed for some reasons. Subsequently, in the 30th Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) meeting held on 27.06.2018, the Central share for 10 projects (including the erstwhile Akola and Amravati cities) was increased from Rs. 33.486 crore (Other than SC/ ST Component) has been sanctioned and Rs. 40.13 crore has been communicated by the Central Government vide letter A. No. 3). Therefore, the Central Government has released funds for 10 projects in the State other than SC/ TSP component as per letter No. 3. It was under consideration of the government to approve the expenditure of the remaining funds to the concerned authorities by adjusting it from Rs. 40.13 crore.
Government Decision- Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, the funds sanctioned for 10 projects under the BLC component in the state for other than SC/ TSP component is Rs. 33.48 crore which was earlier released as per the Government decision dated 29.12.2016. The expenditure is being approved to the Steering Agency MHADA by adjusting it from the fund of Rs. 40.13 crore.
