The Proposal:
In 1998, the government decided to set up a new Chandrapur city on an area of 1391.71 hectares in Chandrapur district. The Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) has been appointed as the special planning authority for the development of the notified area in New Chandrapur. The scheme has been notified by the Urban Development Department, Govt. It was approved on June 30, 1998. The project includes power supply, water supply, main road works, land acquisition works and bridge work on Irai river in New Chandrapur colony. The project is a long-term project and is being implemented as per the availability of funds. Various works under the scheme have been accorded administrative approval by MHADA from time to time.
The Chief Officer, Nagpur Housing and Area Development Board, Nagpur has issued Reference No. According to the statement made under letter No. 1, the matter of releasing funds to MHADA for the New Chandrapur City Development Plan was under the consideration of the government.
