Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has notified the reservation of posts in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for Persons with Disabilities as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published. According to the notification, the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which have been ensured for the disabled by the Central Government, are in the state government establishments. Regarding such posts, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions to ensure that the posts in the state government service are reserved for the disabled, even if the designations of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service.
