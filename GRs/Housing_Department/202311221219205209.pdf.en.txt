The date is November 22, 2023.
Introduction: Many projects are delayed due to non-allotment of project-affected flats to the citizens affected by the infrastructure projects of public interest. The High Court in Writ Petition No.8973/ 2023. the order passed on 3.11.2023. In this regard, the Additional Chief Secretary (Housing) chaired the meeting to decide the policy at the government level. The meeting was held on 9.11.2023. The meeting was attended by officials of the Urban Development Department, Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Slum Rehabilitation Authority, Mumbai, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority and Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation. Many infrastructure projects are underway in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region and there will be a great need for PAP flats in the near future. However, in view of the fact that the number of PAP flats available is negligible, the meeting has directed to constitute a Task Force to make recommendations to the Government to decide a definite policy with regard to PAP flats. It was under the consideration of the Government to constitute this Task Force and to determine the scope of work of the said Task Force.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is approving the constitution of a Task Force to advise the Government on the policy with respect to PAP flats as follows:

The task force will have the following functions: 1) To study the rules and regulations of various departments and make recommendations to the government to decide the policy for increasing the construction of PAP flats.
2) To decide the priority for allotment of PAP flats, 3) To decide the prescribed fee, terms and conditions for allotment of PAP flats, 3. The Task Force shall study the above issues and submit its recommendations to the Government within one month.
4. If the prevailing policy is to be changed as per the recommendation of the Task Force to the Government, the Housing Department should take the approval of the concerned department in this regard.
5. Allowances/ honorarium to the Chairman and members of the Task Force shall be admissible as per the prevailing rates of the Government.
The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation shall make arrangements for the office, vehicle, staff, necessary office equipment, allowable honorarium, meeting arrangements, etc. for this task force.
