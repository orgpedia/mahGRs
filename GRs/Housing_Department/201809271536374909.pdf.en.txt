The Proposal:
The decision to purchase new computers and printers was taken by the department as the number of computers and printers was not enough for the office work in the housing department.
At present, according to the Government Policy, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Government Decision No. Narrow- 2016/ No.215/ Industries-4, Dy. As on 24th August, 2017, it has been made mandatory to procure goods and services from Government e-Marketplace (GeM) (https:// gem. gov. in) portal developed under National Procurement Policy of Government of India for procurement of all Government departments and for ensuring savings in procurement period, supply of quality goods, fair price and transparency in the entire procurement process. Accordingly, computers and printer materials have been purchased as per the prescribed procedure and the matter of approving the expenditure and grant for the said purchase was under consideration of the Department.
Government Decision: 1. 15 computers and 8 printers have been purchased for the Housing Department and the purchase has been made from the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) (https:// gem. gov. in) portal as per the prevailing policy of the Government.
