Preamble: As per the aforesaid letter, MHADA has been delegated powers at Authority/ Zonal level to ensure decentralization of powers for speedy implementation of various development works to be executed by Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority. However, it has come to the notice of the government that the provisions of The Maharashtra Housing and Areal Development (Execution of Contract) Regulations, 1979 are not being followed while executing tenders in MHADA. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines regarding the tender in MHADA authority was under the consideration of the government.
Government Order: While granting administrative approval, technical approval to all the projects/ development works implemented by MHADA Authority and various regional boards under it, the following guidelines regarding the tender process should be implemented: 1. As per Rule 3 (2) of The Maharashtra Housing and Areal Development (Execution of Contract) Regulations, 1979," No contract shall be
executed without the administrative approval and technical sanction have been obtained as provided in statement A (Delegation of Powers). " There is a clear provision. Accordingly, while publishing any tender under MHADA Authority, orders for administrative approval and technical approval are required to be issued at the competent authority level and the said orders are required to contain a detailed estimate of the estimated cost of the development works/ projects.
2. It is essential to complete the development works within the stipulated time keeping the required pace of the project development. However, if the work is not completed within the stipulated time by the contractor or the work has to be repeatedly extended, the cost of the project increases and the government/ authority has to bear the additional cost.
