Date: October 6, 2023
Department of Finance, Government Resolution No: VYAPRA-2013/ P.No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, D. 17.04.2015
Preamble: To amend the Financial Powers provided in the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-sections-I to V, read with the Notes issued by the Department of Finance. As per the Government Order dated 17.04.2015, the Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department has been delegated various financial powers as Head of the Department. Under consideration was the reallocation of financial and administrative powers in respect of non-policy matters out of the financial powers conferred on the Heads of Departments as per the said Government Resolution. The following decisions are being taken:
Government Decision: In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 17.04.2015 and subject to the conditions contained therein, the financial powers conferred on the Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary, Housing Department as Head of the Department are being conferred on the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Admn.
The Government Decision is being issued by nullifying the order/ Government Decision of this Department regarding the re-issue of financial powers issued earlier in this regard.
