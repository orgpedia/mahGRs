Preamble: It has been decided to take measures such as implementing the Abhaya scheme to complete the stalled slum rehabilitation scheme and appointing a new developer to complete the slum rehabilitation scheme by implementing the tender process in the stalled schemes.
The government was considering setting up a committee under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Housing) to select and recommend to the government the schemes to implement the Abhaya scheme from the list of stalled slum rehabilitation schemes, to recommend to the government to appoint a developer for a particular scheme from the list of developers prepared by the Slum Rehabilitation Authority, as well as to examine the competence of financial institutions willing to complete the stalled schemes.
Government Decision: A committee is being constituted to recommend the appointment of a developer through a tender process to complete the stalled slum rehabilitation scheme and to select a scheme from the stalled scheme to implement the Abhaya scheme and recommend to the government:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1) | Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Housing) | the Chairman         |
| 2) | the Chief Executive Officer, Slum Rehabilitation Authority           | the members          |
| 3) | the Chief Legal Adviser, Slum Rehabilitation Authority               | the members          |
| 4) | legal expert                                                         | the members          |
| 5) | Financial expert                                                     | the members          |
| 6) | Engineering Specialist                                               | the members          |
| 7) | the Controller of Finance, Slum Rehabilitation Authority             | the Member Secretary |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In the case of appointment of legal and economic experts, the willingness will be sought by the Slum Rehabilitation Authority and with the approval of the government, they will be appointed to the committee. So, the engineering expert will be nominated by the government.
