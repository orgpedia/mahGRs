Circular:
The Interim Budget for the financial year 2019-2020 has been presented to the Legislature on 27th February, 2019 and the detailed demands contained therein have been approved subject to the approval of the Legislative Council for the provision of expenditure for the first four months (April to July, 2019) and the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislature. The said Bill has been converted into an Act and published as Maharashtra Act No. 2 in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV, dated 08.03.2019.
The budgetary provisions relating to this Department as indicated in the aforesaid Act as mentioned in the Circular to the Government in question are being distributed to the Controlling Authorities under this Department as mentioned in column numbers 8,9,10 and 11 of the accompanying statement. The Controlling Authorities shall follow the rules mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book and Financial Powers Rule Book while disbursing the funds to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers under them. Similarly, the guidelines in paragraphs 5 to 17 of the circular in context should be strictly followed. (A copy of the circular dated 1.4.2019 of the Finance Department is available on the Government website. (iii) The expenditure required to be incurred during the period April, 2019 to July, 2019 and other expenditure to be incurred after receipt of full year provisions. Be sure to follow this instruction carefully. Controlling authorities should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned grant. Also, make sure that every payment has a valid account number and 10-digit computer code.
Also, the expenses incurred for each month should be made available in Form 10 before the 5th of the next 3 months. Also, contact the Office of the Auditor General to tally the expenses.
