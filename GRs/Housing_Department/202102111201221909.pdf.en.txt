The Proposal:
The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016. notified on 26.03.2016. In exercise of the powers conferred by section 20 of the said Act, the Government in reference No. As per the notification dated 08.03.2017, the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority has been constituted for the State of Maharashtra. As the post of Chairman, Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority is currently vacant, the matter of appointing a qualified person to this post was under the consideration of the Government:
Government's decision:
Mr. Ajoy Mehta is being appointed by the Government to the post of Chairman, Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority, as recommended by the Selection Committee constituted in exercise of all other powers conferred by Section 22 of the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 and as empowered thereunder.
After taking charge of the post, the provisions of the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 and the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority, Chairman, Members, Officers and Other Employees (Appointment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2017 will be applicable to his appointment.
