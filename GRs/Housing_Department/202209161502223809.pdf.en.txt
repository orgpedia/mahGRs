Government Circular: At present, the government has the power to approve and administer many development schemes implemented by the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority. These powers need to be decentralized for speedy implementation of development works. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken with a view to provide powers to MHADA at the authority/ zonal level and to expedite the development works: The Government Circular dated 09.03.2021 issued is hereby cancelled and the status quo ante as before the said Government Circular is being maintained.
