The proposal-
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government vide its Memorandum dated 23 rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt the revised methodology of PFMS (Public Financial Management System) for management. Accordingly, Finance Department vide Reference No. 2 Revised instructions have been given in respect of Government Circulars (PFMS). Accordingly, a single nodal agency (SNA) is required to be appointed for the implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and a separate single nodal bank account is required to be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least one officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary as Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) under the Housing Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) under the Housing Department, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority has already been designated as the Steering Agency. 3 has been appointed by the government. Central Government and Finance Department Referral No. The Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) is being appointed as the single nodal agency for the Fund Distribution and Appropriation Management (PFMS) system of Centrally Sponsored Schemes in accordance with the Government Circulars 1 and 2. Also, the Finance Controller, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) is being appointed as the nodal officer for fund distribution and utilization management.
