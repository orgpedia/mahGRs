(3) The scope of the study group shall be as follows:
(1) To simplify and streamline the process with a view to facilitate the delivery of notified services to the citizens.
Re-engineering the business process.
(2) Preparation of Common Application Form.
(3) To develop procedures for linking the service to Digi-Locker.
(4) Setting a time limit for the preservation of records in respect of notified services.
(4) The time period for the submission of the report -
The above study groups should submit their report to the government within three months.
(5) The study group may hold meetings as required.
