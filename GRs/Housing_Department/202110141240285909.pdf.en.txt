Read above. 2 here. Deadline for transfer of tenancies of BDD Chawls under Government decision dated 09.03.2021 This has been extended up to 01.01.2021.
So the. Tenants residing in Residential/ Non-Residential Blocks in BDD Chawls as on 01.01.2021. If the tenants of residential quarters in which penal charges have been levied as per the Government decision dated 24.04.2018 are not able to produce the receipt of payment of penal charges, but are fulfilling other eligibility conditions, the tenants of such residential quarters should be made eligible for temporary transition camp tenancies. However, it shall be mandatory for them to submit/ pay to the office of the Director, BDD, the receipt of payment of the penal amount levied on them for unauthorised transfer of lease, while entering into the actual agreement with them regarding the re-developed slabs in lieu of such original slabs. This condition should be clearly included in the agreement to be made with them regarding the transition camp.
Similarly, if penal charges have been imposed on the non-resident tenant in respect of unauthorised transfer, it shall be mandatory for such tenant to produce the acknowledgement of payment of such penal charges before entering into the agreement for redevelopment of such tenements or remit the penal amount to the office of the Director, BDD.
