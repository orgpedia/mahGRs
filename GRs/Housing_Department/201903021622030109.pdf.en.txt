The General Administration Department has issued a new policy regarding the appointments to be made by deputation from the concerned administrative department to the posts. This has been notified vide Government Resolution dated 17.12.2016.
The rules for the Aurangabad Housing and Area Development Board have not yet been approved. The post It has been vacant since 09.08.2017. Work on the Prime Minister's Housing Scheme is in progress. For this, it is necessary to appoint the Chief Officer, Aurangabad Housing and Area Development Board. Accordingly, Mr. Annasaheb Maruti Rao Shinde, Additional Collector, Parbhani, has been appointed as the Chief Officer, Aurangabad Housing and Area Development Board on deputation. The Chief Minister has approved it.
Therefore, by this order, Shri Annasaheb Maruti Rao Shinde, Additional Collector, Parbhani is being appointed as the Chief Officer, Aurangabad Housing and Area Development Board on deputation for a period of three years.
