Under the Slum Rehabilitation Scheme under 14.5.2008, 20.90 sq.m. (225 sq. ft) instead of 25 sq.m. (269 sq. ft.) size flats were made permissible. The Government of India has announced the policy of Housing for All-2022 under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. As per the policy, houses with a floor area of 30 sq.m. have been made permissible for the economically weaker sections. The government is considering to take a decision on the size of the rehabilitation flats in the slum rehabilitation scheme in the state so that the policy of the state government regarding the residential flats in the slum rehabilitation scheme should be in line with the policy of the central government. In the meeting held on 23.11.2017, Ma. The Chief Minister has directed to prepare an impact analysis report in accordance with the size of houses to be given to the hut owners.
Government Decision: The existing size of residential flats in the resettlement units in the Slum Rehabilitation Schemes being implemented in the Slum Rehabilitation Authority area under the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Eradication and Rehabilitation) Act, 1971 is 25 sq.m (269 sq. feet) is so permissible. Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana of the Central Government, the size of residential flats permissible for economically weaker sections has been made permissible up to 30 sq.m. In view of this, if a commensurate increase in the size of flats permissible under the Slum Rehabilitation Scheme is to be implemented, a committee as mentioned below is being constituted to prepare an impact analysis in order to study the various impacts on the slum rehabilitation schemes currently approved and under construction and those being introduced.
