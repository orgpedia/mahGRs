Preamble: Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), a total central share of Rs. 205.422 crore has been sanctioned for the financial year 2023-24 under the Other than SC/ ST component. The government was considering to allocate funds to the state through the budgetary provision.
Government Decision: Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban), the Central Government Read No. In pursuance of the orders dated 01-03, it is hereby approved to disburse a sum of Rs. 136.948 (Rupees One Hundred Thirty Six Crores Ninety Four Lakh Eight Thousand Only) to the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) out of the sanctioned amount of Rs. 205.422 crore to the State exchequer for disbursement to the concerned implementing agencies for the projects mentioned in the Central Government Order.
2. The said Fund, Demand No. Q-3, Main Accounts Head 2216, Housing, 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaried) (08) (02) Grants to Housing Implementing Agencies for All (PRIs) (State Share 40%) (Programme) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaried) are being disbursed under Account Head 22163007.
The said funds should be drawn from the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai with the signature of the Controller of Finance, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai. The funds should be utilised for the purpose for which they were sanctioned.
In this case, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Controlling Officer, the Controller of Finance, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority will remain.
These orders are being issued in concurrence with the Department of Finance vide its Informal Reference No.366/ Expenditure-3 dated 17.10.2023.
