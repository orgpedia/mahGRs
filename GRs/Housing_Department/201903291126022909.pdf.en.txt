As per Section 82 of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976, a repair cess is levied on buildings in Mumbai city and the island for repair/ reconstruction of old and dilapidated buildings. Also, under Section 85, it is recovered from the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and the remaining amount, excluding 5 per cent of the total recovery, is deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. This amount of repair cess is appropriated, budgeted and transferred by the State Government to the Mumbai Building Repair and Reconstruction Fund under Section 86. Also, the Government contributes an amount equal to the amount of cess collected under Section 97 of the MHADA Act, 1976, to the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority for the repair and reconstruction of the buildings in the Mumbai City Island as Government contribution to the Mumbai Building Repair and Reconstruction Fund.
