Corrigendum- The Government decision issued under the above reference should be read as follows.
Under the Jawaharlal Nehru National Urban Renewal Mission (JNNURM), houses constructed and lying unutilized under the Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP) and the Scheme for Providing Basic Amenities to the Urban Poor (BSUP) will be made available to eligible beneficiaries in the following 3 groups under the respective implementing agencies.
a) Original eligible beneficiaries who have been deprived of the benefit of housing under the Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP) and the Scheme for Providing Basic Amenities to the Urban Poor (BSUP),
(b) where houses are available under the Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP) and the Basic Amenities to the Urban Poor (BSUP), the eligible beneficiaries of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana under the Urban Local Bodies and
c) Beneficiaries affected by various development projects in the State.
However, since the responsibility of rehabilitating the project victims under the aforesaid C component lies with the concerned project developer, the permissible benefit under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana to the eligible families under this component (Central Govt. 1.5 lakh and the State Government Rs. 1 lakh as subsidy and Rs. 1000/-) will not be admissible. The cost of these houses should be deposited by the implementing agency of the development project to the steering agency MHADA as per the prevailing market rate chart.

In order to streamline the aforesaid (a), (b), (c), the vacant houses available under the Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP) and the Basic Facilities to the Urban Poor (BSUP) should be distributed to the beneficiaries as per the demand from (a), (b) and (c).
All the benefits of Central Government and State Government as admissible while distributing houses under Integrated Housing and Slum Development Programme (IHSDP) and Providing Basic Amenities to Urban Poor (BSUP) to the eligible beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) will continue to be paid.
