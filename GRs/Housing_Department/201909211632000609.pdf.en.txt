Preamble: The Government of India has announced a policy for Housing for All-2022 under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in 2015. A Government Resolution dated 9th December, 2015 has been issued regarding the implementation of this policy in the State. Under this scheme, the central government provides financial assistance of Rs. 1.50 lakh and Rs. 1.00 lakh.
The floods of August 2019 in three districts of Kolhapur, Sangli and Satara have affected many houses. It has been decided to provide benefits under the BLC component of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana to the citizens belonging to the economically weaker sections (EWS) for such houses. Accordingly, KPMG has been asked to conduct on-site survey of eligible EWS households as per the criteria of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) in the flood-affected households of Kolhapur, Sangli and Satara districts and submit the Detailed Project Report (DPR) of the affected households to MHADA immediately. This has been informed vide letter dated 27.08.2019. Usually, the first instalment of central and state government grants is disbursed after the Detailed Project Report (DPR) is approved by the Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC).

Government Decision: For the flood affected people of Kolhapur, Sangli and Satara districts, component no. 4 (BLC) to the eligible persons out of the State Government share i.e. Rs. 1 lakh (40%) of the first installment of Rs. 40,000/- is being approved as a special case from Maharashtra Shelter Fund.
The funds will be released after the State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (SLSMC) approves the Detailed Project Report (DPR) of the houses. Accordingly, the concerned implementing agencies should take immediate action to submit the detailed project report to the MHADA authority.
