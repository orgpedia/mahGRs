In 1995, the Government decided to set up a Slum Rehabilitation Authority for the area of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation under sub-section (1) of section 3-A of the Maharashtra Slum (Improvement, Eradication and Redevelopment) Act, 1971. Thereafter, the Government has constituted Slum Rehabilitation Authority Nagpur for Nagpur Municipal Corporation area vide notification dated 24/08/2004. In 2005, it was decided to set up a Slum Rehabilitation Authority in Pune. So, by the notification dated 11/09/2014 the Thane Municipal Corporation area has been included in the Slum Rehabilitation Authority, Mumbai.
Further, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 26/08/2020 has approved the recommendation of the Study Group constituted by Government Resolution dated 30/05/14 to study and submit recommendations to the Government on the formation of MMR SRA and by notification dated 08/09/2020, the Slum Rehabilitation Authority has been constituted in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (total 8 Municipal Corporations and 7 Municipalities excluding the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation). Further, a decision to appoint a study group for the establishment of Slum Rehabilitation Authority in other major cities of the State has been taken in the Cabinet meeting dated 26/08/2020.
In this background, it would be appropriate to establish a Slum Rehabilitation Authority for the areas other than Mumbai, Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad, Nagpur and Mumbai Metropolitan Region or to take any other measures for the rehabilitation of the slum dwellers, in accordance with which a study group was under consideration of the Government to make suitable recommendations on the following issues:
