Government's decision:
The online performance appraisal reporting system, MahaPAR, is proposed to provide online reporting of performance of all Group A and Group B (Gazetted) cadre officers from 2017-18 onwards. Accordingly, the General Administration Department, Govt. Under 15.12.2017, Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, who controls the Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers of the concerned service for recording performance appraisal report in the Mahapar system, has been declared as" Coordinating Officer," as well as" Cadre Controlling Officer." Also inform the Under Secretary/ Office Officer of the concerned office who controls the concerned service
EMD Manager has been declared. Accordingly, Deputy Secretary (Animal Husbandry) and Office Officer (Padum-1) have been declared as" Coordinating Officer" and" Information Manager" respectively.
