Read here. As per the Government Decision dated 05.02.2020, the Department of Tribal Development has released funds under the head Accounts (2401 A806) amounting to Rs. 7.26 lakh (Rs. Seven lakh twenty six thousand only) has been disbursed on the budgetary distribution system of the department. The matter of disbursing this fund to the Agriculture Commissionerate was under the consideration of the government. Following this, the government decided:
Government Decision: Financial approval is being given to spend an amount of Rs. 7.26 lakh (Rs.7 lakh 26 thousand only) within the limit of 33% of the budgeted funds for the implementation of the' Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation Scheme in the Neighborhoods of Tribal Families' in the 14 districts of Thane, Palghar, Pune, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Jalgaon, Ahmednagar, Nanded, Yavatmal, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Amravati and Gondia in 2020-21.
The expenditure for this scheme shall be met from the following head of accounts as per the provision approved and budgeted for the year 2020-21.
" Demand No. T-5, 2401- Crop Protection, (796), Tribal Area Sub-Plan (01) (36) Cultivation of Fruits and Vegetables in Adivasi Households (Programme) (2401 A806) 33 Financial Assistance. 3. Since the problem of malnutrition is rampant in tribal families, as a special case, the report of expenditure from the provisions made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information, fund utilization certificate, concerned field officer should send to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.

So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal sub-plan area.
