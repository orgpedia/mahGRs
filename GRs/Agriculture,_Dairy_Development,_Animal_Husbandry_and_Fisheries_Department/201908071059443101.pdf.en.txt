The Proposal:
For the year 2019-2020, the Central Government has released Rs.100 crore under the National Food Security Mission for commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane). As per the letter dated 09/04/2019, an action plan for an amount of Rs. 872.58 lakh has been approved. 523.55 Lakhs+ State share of Rs. 349.03 lakhs). The administrative approval has been granted as per the Government Decision dated 03.06.2019.
The Central Government has released Central share of Rs. 200.80 lakh in the first instalment under the Mission vide letter dated 18/07/2019. As per the demand made vide letter dated 20/07/2019, the Government is taking the following decisions regarding the release of required funds from the Central and State share
Government's decision:
For the year 2019-20, a total amount of Rs. 270.73 lakh (Rs.2.77 crore and seventy-three thousand only) from the Central and State share for the general category of commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) under the National Food Security Mission is being approved to be disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Budgetary Distribution System. The crop-wise and Central and State share of the fund is as follows:

5 The total amount of Rs. 270.73 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission under the Government decision should be disbursed district wise by the Director (Extension& Training) through PFMS system.
The Director (Extension and Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts 1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the withdrawal of funds to the tune of Rs. 270.73 lakhs made available under this Government decision.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Director (Extension and Training) should submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Secretary (Agriculture).
This Government decision is in accordance with the Government Circular No. 44/ Finance-3/ 2019 of the Department of Finance. As the conditions laid down as on 01.04.2019 are being fulfilled, the same is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on this Department under the said circular.
