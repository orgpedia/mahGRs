Introduction: Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana- Raftaar is being implemented with the funding of 60:40 ratio of Central and State Government respectively. As per letter dated 14/05/2018, Maharashtra has been sanctioned a total allocation of Rs. 57.30 crore (Central share 34.38 crore and State share 22.92 crore) for the year 2018-19 for SC category. In accordance with this allocation. The Scheme has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 15th September, 2018.
The central government. As per the letter dated 06.09.2018, a sum of Rs. 17.19 crore has been released in the first instalment for SC category under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana and a sum of Rs. 11.46 crore is available in the State Budget for the same. A sum of Rs. 28.65 crore is available for disbursement.

The Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Agriculture), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the withdrawal and disbursement of the amount of Rs. 28.65 crore from the treasury.
The Assistant Director (Accounts-I), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune shall deposit the amount of Rs. 28.65 crore received through Budget Distribution System in the Personal Ledger Account opened in the name of Commissioner (Agriculture), State of Maharashtra, Pune and report the same to the Government and thereafter the said funds shall be transferred to various implementing agencies for implementation of the projects.
In addition to this Government decision, Annexure-A shows the project-wise/ implementation mechanism-wise distribution of funds, as per which the Commissioner (Agriculture) is to disburse Rs. 28.65 crore to the Scheduled Castes category for the projects sanctioned under the scheme.
The above sanctioned funds should be withdrawn from the treasury for immediate utilization and all the departments should make immediate utilization of the funds received by them, the implementing agencies should not keep the funds in bank accounts without planning.
The remaining funds under the scheme will be released by the Central Government in the second phase after auditing and certifying the funds disbursed to various departments under the National Agriculture Development Scheme from 2007-08 to 2017-18.
8. The projects sanctioned under the National Agriculture Development Scheme of the Department of Agriculture are referred to as Advanced Agriculture- Prosperous Farmers. The guidelines prescribed under the Government Decision dated 27th April, 2018 should be implemented.
Under this Government decision, funds have been released only for the Scheduled Castes category and all the Departments/ Implementing Agencies should utilize the above amount for the funds required for the Scheduled Castes category under the projects sanctioned to them.
10.Further, all the Departments/ Implementing Agencies under individual benefit projects shall disburse the subsidy through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of the beneficiaries.
