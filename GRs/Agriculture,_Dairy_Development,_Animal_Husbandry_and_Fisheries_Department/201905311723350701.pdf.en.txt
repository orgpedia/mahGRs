Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme being implemented in the State in a modified form and weather based crop insurance scheme from Kharif season 2016 as per the guidelines of the Central Government. The plan
Area Approach is being adopted for notified crops in notified areas.
Under this scheme, the insurance companies appointed through the e-tendering process are in the process of paying compensation to the farmers as per the guidelines of the scheme. However, as per the provisions of the said Crop Insurance Scheme, if the amount of crop insurance compensation comes to a very small amount, then the farmers should be able to pay the amount of insurance reimbursement up to a minimum of Rs.1000/-.
The matter was under consideration of the government. Following decisions were taken in the cabinet meeting:
Government Decision: As per the provisions of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana and Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme, if the amount of crop insurance compensation is very less, then the farmers are required to pay the insurance amount up to a minimum of Rs. Therefore, if the amount paid by the insurance company for the crop insurance fruit crop insurance claim is less than 1000/-, the government is allowed to pay the difference up to Rs 1000/- through the state government.
The farmers who have received crop insurance and crop insurance claim amount less than Rs.1000/-, the concerned insurance company should submit the information along with the data to the Commissioner of Agriculture through the Agriculture Insurance Company of India. Thereafter, after the detailed proposal is submitted to the Government by the Agriculture Commissionerate, the State Government will pay the difference up to Rs.1000/- to the Agriculture Commissionerate. The amount will be transferred directly to the bank accounts of the eligible farmers after the Commissionerate of Agriculture disburses the amount to the respective insurance companies through Agriculture Insurance Company of India.
