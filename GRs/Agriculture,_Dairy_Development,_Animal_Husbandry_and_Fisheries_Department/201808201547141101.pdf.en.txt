the order
In accordance with the provisions of Section 12 (2) (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, this Department vide Order No. Makrupa 1018/ P.No. 131/6-O, Dy. Shri Sanjay Dhotre, MP has been appointed as Vice Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research by the Government with effect from 1st August, 2018.
Sanjay Dhotre has been given the status of Cabinet Minister during his tenure as Vice-Chairman, Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune.
