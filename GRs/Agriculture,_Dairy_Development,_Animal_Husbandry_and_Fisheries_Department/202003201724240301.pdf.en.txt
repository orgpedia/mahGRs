The Proposal:
The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State governments. Under this scheme, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 3 here. As per the letter dated 22/02/2019, the State has been sanctioned a total annual allocation of Rs. 418.45 crore for the year 2019-20, out of which Rs. 47.42 crore will be available for Scheduled Tribes. A total of Rs. 23.71 crore has been released as first instalment under National Agriculture Development Scheme (NADP) to the Scheduled Tribes (ST) category. 5 here. The Government Decision dated 27/08/2019 has been delivered.
Now the Central Government has released Rs. 14.23 crore to the State in the second installment for the Scheduled Tribes category. 6 here. The Government is taking the following decisions as per the letter dated 04/03/2020, wherein a total amount of Rs. 23.71 crore has been disbursed out of the corresponding State share of Rs. 9.48 crore.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, 60:40 financial assistance will be provided to the Scheduled Tribes for the implementation of the projects sanctioned under the scheme of Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana-Raftaar in 2019-20.
