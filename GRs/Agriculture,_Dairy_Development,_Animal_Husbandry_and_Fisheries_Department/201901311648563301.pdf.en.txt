In view of the importance of the agricultural development sector in the state, the Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute at Nagpur has been declared as the apex training institute and seven Divisional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institutes at Nagpur, Pune, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nashik, Kolhapur and Khopoli in the state have been declared as associate and affiliated institutes of the apex training institute. As per the decision of the Government dated 17th July, 2001, the apex training institute of Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur (VANAMATI) and its seven affiliated institutions of the department have been declared as autonomous institutions. In order to ensure the smooth functioning of the office of the institute, the director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Management and Training Institute, Nagpur (Vanamati) was considered by the government to be the head of the department and the principal, Divisional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (Rameti) was declared as the head of the regional office.
Government's decision:
Director, Vasantrao Naik State Institute of Agriculture Management and Training, Nagpur (Vanamati) has been declared as Head of Department and Principal, Divisional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (Rameti) as Head of Regional Office.
By this Government Order, the powers of the Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Management and Training Institute, Nagpur (Vanamati) as Head of the Department and the Principal, Divisional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (Ramati) as Head of the Regional Office shall be limited to financial matters only.
