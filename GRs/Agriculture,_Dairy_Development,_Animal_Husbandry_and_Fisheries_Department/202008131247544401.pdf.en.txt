Introduction: The Central Government has issued guidelines for the scheme by formulating a Central Sector Scheme' Financing Facility under Agriculture Infrastructure Fund'. Under this scheme, incentives and financial assistance will be provided for viable projects of post-harvest management infrastructure development and creation of group farm assets by taking medium to long term loans at the farm and at various farm collection centres (various executive cooperatives, farmer producer organisations, agri-entrepreneurs, agri start-ups, etc.).

Government's decision:
The State Level Monitoring Committee (SLMC) and District Level Monitoring Committee (DLMC) are being constituted to implement the Central Sector Scheme of' Financing Facility under Agriculture Infrastructure Fund' in the State.
