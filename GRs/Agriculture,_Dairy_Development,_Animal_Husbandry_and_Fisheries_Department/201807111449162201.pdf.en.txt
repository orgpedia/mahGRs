The Proposal:
Farmers should be provided with information about various agricultural schemes/ activities of the government, revised agricultural technologies, experiences of innovative experimental farmers, market management, agri-business, etc. Agricultural exhibitions, seminars on agriculture and lectures by experienced farmers/ entrepreneurs, direct interaction with successful farmers, scientists, technocrats and thinkers and clearing the doubts of ordinary farmers. Under the Government decision dated 5.1.2017, the State Government has approved the State Plan to celebrate 5 days District Agriculture Festival every year in the remaining 34 districts of the State except Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban districts to organize Food and Food Festival and Fruit, Flower and Vegetable Festival etc. based on the concept of direct selling from producer to consumer.
During 2018-19, Rs. A sum of Rs. 680.00 lakh has been allocated for the project. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to this fund and the Finance Department. As per the powers conferred on the Department vide Circular dated 02.04.2018, 70% of the sanctioned allocation i.e. Rs. 476.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the decision.
Government's decision:
In the year 2018-19, the budget allocated Rs. Administrative approval is being granted for the programme with an outlay of Rs. 680.00 lakhs (Rupees Six Crores Eighty Lakhs only).
Government Circular Finance Department No. Finance- 2017/ S. No.69/ Budget-3, D. As on 02.4.2018, a sum of Rs. 680.00 lakhs out of which 70% of the fund i.e. Rs. 476.00 lakhs (Rupees Four Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs only) to the Commissioner (Agriculture).
