Letter No. Adivi-15 (9)/ Temporary Posts/ 2018-19/4302, Dy. 29/09/2018 Preamble: The Government has notified the revised figure of total 4097 posts of Subordinate Officers/ Employees of Commissioner, Dairy Development Department. Approved by the Government Decision dated 30/08/2014.
After the approval of the revised figure, a total of 2640 temporary posts and 506 additional posts of officers and staff in various offices under the Commissioner, Dairy Development have been created. The continuation from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018 has been approved under the Government Decision of Reference No. The Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. Out of 2640 temporary posts, 69 posts have been retired and posted in other departments and the remaining 2571 temporary posts and 58 posts in 506 additional posts have been retired and the remaining 448 additional posts have been requested to be continued after adjustment in other departments. As per the approval given under letter No. 2, the matter of approval of continuation of temporary posts in the Dairy Development Department was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: A total of 2571 temporary posts and 448 additional posts of officers and staff of various offices under the Commissioner Dairy Development have been proposed. 1/10/2018 to D. Continuation up to 28/2/2019 is being approved subject to the following conditions. The details of temporary and additional posts are shown separately in the prospectus" A."
(1) The posts lying vacant for more than six months shall be removed.
Commissioner, Dairy Development reviewed all the total posts including 4097 posts sanctioned as per the revised figure and submitted the information to the government about the exact number of posts required. Before 31/01/2019, the revised format will need to be approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
Provisional and temporary posts should be adjusted in a phased manner to regular vacancies within the next two to three months. No further extension will be granted if the adjustment process is not completed.
