Preamble: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the service of officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity in order to test their eligibility (eligible/ ineligible) to remain in service at the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service period, whichever is earlier. Accordingly, the General Administration Department has issued reference No. 1. Under the Government decision dated 10.6.2019, the procedure and overall recommendations regarding service review have been laid down. Accordingly, for the review of services of Group-A and Group-B officers of Fisheries Department, Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 12.11.2020, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Padum).
A proposal has been made to review the services of Group-B cadre officers in the Fisheries Department at the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. This was submitted in the meeting of the Departmental Review Committee dated 18.12.2020 under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Padum). Officers who have completed 50/55 years of age or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, as recommended by the Departmental Review Committee after assessing the service of the said officers

At that time, the matter of approving those who have been decided eligible to remain in service was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee in its meeting held on 18.12.2020, the services of the officers mentioned in the attached form in Group-B cadre in the Fisheries Department are being approved to continue at the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service period, whichever is earlier.
The service book of Group-B cadre officers in the attached form should be taken as a record of their approval to continue after 50/55 th year of service or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
