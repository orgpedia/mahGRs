Government Decision: 1. Under the Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana in 2021-22 and 2022-23, Rs. Administrative approval is being given to the programme with a corpus of Rs. 100.00 crore. The project will be implemented on a 50:50 basis. Accordingly, to provide subsidy to individual beneficiaries as per the parameters of Integrated Horticulture Development Mission, Rs. 100.00 crore is being provided under the National Agriculture Development Scheme.
The project will be implemented over a period of two years with an outlay of Rs. An allocation of Rs. 100.00 crore is being approved. However, the Government shall have the power to increase/ decrease the fixed allocation to be made available year-wise in proportion to the demand received from the farmers.

Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the funds made available for the project for the legalization of individual farms should be deposited directly into the Aadhaar linked bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
The implementing agencies should ensure that these funds are not misused.
