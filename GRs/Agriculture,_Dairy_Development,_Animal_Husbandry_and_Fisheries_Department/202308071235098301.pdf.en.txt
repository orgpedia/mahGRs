As a measure to provide cost effective compensation to the farmers considering the factors, a restructured weather based crop insurance scheme will be implemented in the State for 9 crops namely Orange, Cashew, Pomegranate, Mango, Banana, Grape (A& B), Strawberry and Papaya (Ambia Bahar) on pilot basis in 30 districts over a period of three years i.e. 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24. Government Decision: In view of the recommendation made by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the restructured Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Ambia Bahar 2021-22, the State share insurance premium subsidy of Rs. 24,94,288/- is being approved by the Government to be made available to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for payment to the insurance companies as per the guidelines of the scheme. This amount is being disbursed for the Aambia Bahar season 2021-22 and will not be allowed for other seasons.
