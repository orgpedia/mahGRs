National Agriculture Development Scheme (NAVY): The scheme is being implemented on 60:40 sharing pattern between the Centre and the State Governments. The Public Financial Management System (PFMS) has been operationalized from 2021-22 onwards. On this system, Single Nodal Account (SNA) at the level of Commissioner (Agriculture) for the National Agriculture Development Scheme and Zero Balance Account for the implementing agencies have been made operational.
As per the instructions of the Central Government, for receiving the first instalment of the Central share of the approved outlay for the year 2023-24 under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the balance in the State SNA Account should be less than 25% of the Central share of the approved outlay. In pursuance of the above instructions of the Central Government, the Commissionerate of Agriculture vide Reference No. As proposed under Section 5, out of the funds deposited in the SNA account, Rs. 17.8259 crore was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Considering the funds available for disbursement on the SNA account of National Agriculture Development Scheme for the financial year 2023-24, Rs. 17.8259 crore (Rupees Seventeen Crores Eighty Two Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand only) is being disbursed as per the decision of the Government. The project-wise/ category-wise details of this distribution are mentioned in Annexure-A attached.
