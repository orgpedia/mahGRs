The Proposal:
It is proposed to organize a national level animal and bird show at Jalna in the month of January, 2019 for the year 2018-19. For this exhibition, it is proposed to set up stalls of the best cattle and birds of the state and outside the state, as well as the necessary machines and equipment for their scientific rearing, medicinal and products related to animal husbandry, and to honor the cattle farmers by selecting the best cattle and birds of the state.
Through the exhibition, the farmers will be guided on the importance of indigenous bovine conservation, genetic improvement of indigenous bovine breeds, providing self-employment opportunities to the educated unemployed through animal husbandry and thereby strengthening the rural economy, promoting milk, meat and egg production, reducing the cost of production by increasing the production capacity of animals, use of sex sorted semen, promotion of fodder production, Murgas/ Hydroponics/ Azolla technology, which will help in doubling the income of the farmers.
The matter of organizing a national level animal and bird show in Jalna headquartered Mahe in January, 2019 and providing administrative approval for the expenditure incurred was under consideration of the Government. Following is the government's decision:
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being accorded for organizing National Level Animal and Bird Show in Jalna Headquarter Mahe in January, 2019 and the expenditure involved.
The expenditure as above should be met from the funds sanctioned under the head Supplementary Demands for Grants during the year 2018-19.
