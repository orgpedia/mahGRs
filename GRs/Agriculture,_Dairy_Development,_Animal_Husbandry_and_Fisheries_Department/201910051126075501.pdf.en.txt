Letter of the Commissioner of Agriculture No. Kriya 1111/ SAPR/ 0261/19-20/ Q.No.-5/ Aastha-5 (NIP)/ 1989, Dy. 19 September 2019 Preamble: In the year 2019-20, a total of 248 temporary posts under the schemes in the Department of Agriculture were approved by the Government Decision No. Tabike 2019/ PR No. 58/13 A, d. It was approved to continue till September 30, 2019.
The Department of Finance, vide Government Resolution No. Designation-2016/ P.S. 8/16/ A.P.C., dated 11th September, 2019, has been empowered to extend the period up to 29.2.2020 for the temporary posts included in the administrative departments in their rolls and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said review but which have not been included in the rolls. Accordingly, the continuation of these temporary posts from 1st October, 2019 to 29.2.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner of Agriculture, No. Based on the request in the letter here, 248 temporary posts in the scheme of Taluka Bijagunan Region (2401 0261), (excluding the posts lying vacant for more than 6 months), from October 1, 2019 to February 29, 2020, the following temporary posts are being approved to continue.
