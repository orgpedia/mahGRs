The Government has approved the continuation of the posts/ items in the scheme related to the sub-heading/ group/ scheme and subject-wise funds disbursed by the Agricultural Universities and the Council of Agriculture as schemes under the scheme.
That is, the salary and allowances of those posts should be spent only for permissible matters. In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancy in certain posts or for any other reason/ reasons, the subsidy will be released in the next month.

(3) The grants/ revenue income distributed among each group shall be used to meet the expenditure on permissible items in that group and for carrying out development works. The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grant-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities is correct as per the grant formula, and how it is being spent as per the prescribed order/ procedure. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
8) While approving some new schemes of the University, the Government has approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme in an adjusted manner. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities shall be spent within the prescribed limits as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Accounts Code, 1991, and after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. While seeking grants for the year 2020-21, the University shall submit a report by 10th of every month regarding the plan-wise grants disbursed, actual expenditure and balance grants. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
11) Do not spend money on illegal schemes, posts and affairs.
