Preamble: The University Grants Commission (UGC) vide its notification dated 30th June, 2010 has prescribed the minimum educational qualification for appointment to the posts of Teachers and equivalent posts in Central Universities and Colleges/ Institutions and the measures to be taken to maintain the standards in higher education.
Also, instructions have been given regarding the pay scale, collateral benefits, incentive pay hike etc. of the teachers in these posts.
2. University Grants Commission. The notification dated 30th June 2010 has been published in the Gazette of India dated 18-24 th September 2010. The provisions of the said notification shall be applicable to teaching and equivalent posts in the Maharashtra Veterinary and Fisheries Sciences University and constituent colleges, as per the provisions of the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR). No.7 is given hereunder vide letter dated 2/08/2010. Accordingly, the matter of applying for the posts of teachers and equivalent posts in the Maharashtra Animal and Fisheries Sciences University and constituent colleges was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION- 3. After considering the recommendations/ directions of the University Grants Commission in its notification dated 30th June, 2010, the Government hereby make the following orders:
