As the financial position of the State is likely to be very critical due to COVID-19, the Controlling Authority should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the said funds. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information achieved, fund utilization certificate, the concerned field officer should be careful to send it to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
4. The Head of Department/ Controlling Officer shall ensure that the funds for the SC category are spent on the same category so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on these schemes.
While spending the funds distributed under this Government decision, the Head of the Department and the Controlling Officers should strictly follow the instructions given in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, the Financial Rules in the Financial Powers Rulebook as well as the Government Circular dated 16.04.2020 in the context of the Finance Department.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Informal Reference No. 282/2021/ Expenditure-14 of the Finance Department dated 24.03.2021 and Informal Reference No: 208/ Budget dated 30.03.2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance as agreed by the departments concerned.
