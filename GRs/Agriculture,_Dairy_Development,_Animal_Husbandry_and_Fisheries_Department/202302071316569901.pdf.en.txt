The Proposal:
Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RAFTAR) is being implemented by the Central and State Governments in the ratio of 60:40 and under this scheme, for the year 2021-22, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 2 here. The total annual allocation of Rs. 42.25 crore has been sanctioned vide letter dated 04.05.2021. This annual allocation includes Rs. 25.35 crore on Central share and Rs. 16.90 crore.
Reference No. 2. 4 is here. As per the letter dated 08.03.2022, the Central Government has released Rs. 12.67 crore to the State in the first instalment towards Central share for STs. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 21,11,66, 667/-was expected to be disbursed out of the Central share of Rs. 12,67,00, 000/- and corresponding State share of Rs. 8,44,66, 667/-. As per the Government decision dated 23 rd March, 2022, the Central share of Rs. 12.67 and Rs. 8.44 crore. 21.11 crore has been disbursed. 66,667/- is yet to be disbursed.
