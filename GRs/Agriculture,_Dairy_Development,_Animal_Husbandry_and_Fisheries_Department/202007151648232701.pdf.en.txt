INTRODUCTION: At the fish landing centres located in the coastal districts of Maharashtra, basic amenities are available at many places for the fishermen. The government has decided to provide basic facilities to the fishermen in the marine districts of the state under the National Agriculture Development Scheme. In this regard, in the 28th meeting of the State Level Project Approval Committee held on 26.02.2020 under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary (Agriculture), Rs. 2 works amounting to Rs. 44.00 crore have been sanctioned. Therefore, the matter of giving administrative approval to these works was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decisions Under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), for the year 2020-21, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for a budget of Rs. 44.00 crore (Rupees Forty Four Crores only).
The Commissioner, Fisheries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai shall take further action regarding the tender process as per the prevailing rules of the said works and obtain necessary level of no objection permission. Also, the government should review the expenditure and works done every quarter and submit a report.
This Government Decision is issued by the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Ministry of Agriculture, Mumbai Letter No. RKV-0120/ PR No. 14/14-A, D. 19.03.2020 is being issued accordingly.
Orders for the distribution of funds for these works will be issued separately.
