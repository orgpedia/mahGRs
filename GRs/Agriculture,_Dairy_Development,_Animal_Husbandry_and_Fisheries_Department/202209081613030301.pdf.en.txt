MA/ 20674/2022, Dy. 9/06/2022 Preamble: In order to ensure financial stability of farmers by providing insurance cover to their fruit crops against climatic hazards, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2020 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the year 2020 for 6 crops namely Orange, Pomegranate, Guava, Chikoo and Lemon (Mrig Bahar) in the district by holding the revenue board component.
Re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme Mrig Bahar under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2020 The administrative approval has been given as per the government decisions in 1 and 2.
The Commissionerate has issued Reference No. 6 of the State share of Rs. 4,75,00, 154/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In view of the recommendation made by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the restructured Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Mrig Bahar 2020, the State share insurance premium subsidy of Rs. 4,75,00, 154/- is being approved by the Government to be made available to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for payment to the insurance companies as per the guidelines of the Scheme. This amount is being disbursed for the Deer Spring Season 2020 and will not be allowed for other seasons.
