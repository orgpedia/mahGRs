S/ PUHAFPIVO/ 04/2019, Dy. 4.1.2019 Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against the climatic hazards and to help the farmers in maintaining their financial stability, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2016-17 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce.
Considering all these factors, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme has been implemented in 2016-17 with the Revenue Board as a component to provide effective compensation to the farmers.
Reference No. 17 for implementation of Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana in 2016-17 Administrative approval has been granted under Government Decisions 1 and 2. Under the scheme, the government was considering to give financial approval to the amount of the state share as per the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate for 2016-17 for Deer and Ambia Bahara.
Government's decision:
Under Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme, the Commissionerate of Agriculture has issued Reference No. As per the demand of Rs. 22,09,677/- for 2016-17 (Mrig and Ambia) to Reliance and Shriram.
