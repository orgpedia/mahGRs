Preamble: With a view to stabilize the production programme of the State and to maintain the participation of the farmers in the production programme, in view of the fact that the rate in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee for the crops produced in 2017-18 is less than the Minimum Support Price, a new scheme has been implemented as per the Government Decision dated 17th September, 2018 to pay the difference between the price based on the Agricultural Produce Market Committee and the Minimum Support Price announced by the Government to the farmers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIJ), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune. The scheme will be applicable for payment of difference between the quantity of seeds produced in Kharif and Rabi seasons 2017-18 and 2018-19 onwards. The amount will be transferred directly to the farmers" bank accounts through DBT.
The difference between the Agriculture Produce Market Committee (APMC) based price and the Minimum Support Price (MSP) announced by the Government for the seeds produced during Kharif and Rabi season 2017-18 is being paid to the seed producing farmers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIJ), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune. A proposal of Rs. 3100.23 lakh has been sent by the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune. The proposal was made by the Secretary (Agriculture). The State Level Committee has agreed to this in its meeting held on 19th December, 2018. The total amount available is Rs. 21.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.

Government Decision No: Purak-1518/ PROCESS 226/1 A
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 21.00 crore is being disbursed under the head of accounts 2401 O 922 (100% State Plan) to the seed producing farmers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIJ), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune for the seeds produced during Kharif and Rabi season 2017-18 against the difference between the Agriculture Produce Market Committee (APMC) based prices and the MSP announced by the Government.
The amount of this difference should be deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the farmer through Direct Benefit Transfer.
The conditions in the circulars dated 02/04/2018, 27/07/2018 and 05/12/2018 of the Finance Department should be fulfilled.
This Government Resolution is being issued pursuant to the approval given by the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 4/2019/ Expenditure-1 dated 09/01/2019.
