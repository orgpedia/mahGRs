प्रस्तावना: -
सहाय्यक अनुदानाच्या जलद संवितरण व संनियत्रणासाठी आहरण व संवितरण अधिकारी
आणि अन्य अशासकीय कार्यान्वयन प्राधिकारी यांचेकरिता "आभासी वैयक्तिक ठेव लेखा" (Virtual
Personal Deposit Account) या व्यवस्था व कार्यपध्दतीची अंमलबजावणी करण्याबाबत वित्त
विभागाच्या संदर्भाधीन क्र.०१ च्या शासन निर्णयान्वये सूचना दिलेल्या आहेत. सदर शासन निर्णयान्वये
कार्यान्वित करण्यात आलेली आभासी वैयक्तिक ठेव लेखा व्यवस्था व कार्यपध्दतीमध्ये ५२- यंत्रसामग्री
या उदिष्ट्ट शिषांचा समावेश करण्यास वित्त विभागाच्या संदर्भाधीन क्र.०२ च्या शासन निर्णयान्वये
मान्यता देण्यात आली आहे. आभासी वैयक्तिक ठेव लेख्यांचे कामकाज वित्त विभागाच्या संदर्भ क्र.०१
च्या शासन निर्णयान्वये विहित करण्यात आलेल्या कार्यपध्दतीव्दारे विनियमित होईल. त्यानुसार
आयुक्त दुग्धव्यवसाय विकास विभाग, मुंबई यांच्या अधिनस्त असलेले सहायक जिल्हा दुग्धव्यवसाय
विकास आधिकारी, अहिल्यानगर व सहायक जिल्हा दुग्धव्यवसाय विकास आधिकारी, नाशिक या

3. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
4. VPDA 15 applicable only for State Schemes and not for Central Sector and Centrally
Sponsored Schemes.
5. The Receipt to the said VPDA through Nil Bill procedure, shall be only for the bills
drawn under:
e MTR-31B for the object heads 27-Minor Works and 53-Major Works.
e MTR-45B for the object heads of expenditure 33- subsides and 50-Other Charges.
e MTR-44 for the object heads of expenditure 31-Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary) and 35-
Grants in Aid for creating of Capital Assets.
e MTR-29 for the object head of expenditure 52-Machinery and Equipment shall be applicable from 1 April 2025.
6. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate in the prescribed format
after incurring expenditure from the VPDA to the office of the AG (A&E)-I, Mumbai for the
Bills drawn under MTR-44 for object heads of expenditure 31-Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary),
35-Grants-in-Aid for creation of Capital Assets.
For the bills drawn under MTR-31B, MTR-45B, MTR-29 the Utilisation Certificate shall
be submitted to the concerned administrative departments.
7. The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the respective Grant.
8. The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund
after completion of one year (365 days) from the date of drawal of the funds/date of credit of amount to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever is
earlier.
9. The Monthly Plus Minus Memo shall be forwarded to this office by the respective
treasury officer.
10. The balances under the VPDA shall be reconciled monthly by the Administrator with
the District Treasury Officer.
11. The balances shall be reconciled with the AG office by the Treasury Officer who shall submit the Balance Certificate to the AG office for confirmation of their balances in the books of the AG office at the end of the Financial Year.
12. All the conditions/instructions in the Finance Department G.R. No संकीर्ण/2023 प्र.क्र.110/2023/ कोषा प्रशा-५ दिनांक १४-०२-२०२४: GR No. संकीर्ण/ 2023 प्र.क्र.110/2023/ कोषा प्रशा-५ दिनांक १२-०३-२०२५ and GR No. संकीर्ण/ 2023 प्र.क्र.110/2023/ कोषा प्रशा-५ दिनांक
१८-०७-२०२७५ shall be followed.
13. All Rules of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 shall be applicable to the VPDA.
