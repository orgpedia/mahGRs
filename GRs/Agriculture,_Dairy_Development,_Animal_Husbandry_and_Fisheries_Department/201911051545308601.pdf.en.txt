Government's decision:
For the year 2018-19, a total amount of Rs. 42.63 lakh (Rs. Forty Two Lakh Sixty Three Thousand only) is being approved to be re-appropriated from the unspent funds of Central and State accounts for commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) under the National Food Security Mission for the year 2019-20 for general category cotton and sugarcane crops.
For cotton crop as mentioned in para 1, a total of Rs. 12.15 lakhs (Rs. 12.15 lakhs only) is being released as Central share of Rs. 7.29 lakhs (Rs. 7 lakh thirty nine thousand only) and State share of Rs. 4.86 lakhs (Rs. 4 lakh eighty six thousand only).
3. A total amount of Rs. 30.48 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Lakh Forty Eight Thousand only) is being released for sugarcane crop as mentioned in Para 1, out of which Rs. 18.29 lakhs (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Thirty Nine Thousand only) is Central share and Rs. 12.19 lakhs (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Nineteen Thousand only) is State share.
