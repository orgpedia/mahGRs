Government's decision:
In 2020-21, the Central and State Governments will provide 60:40 financial assistance to the Scheduled Castes for the implementation of the projects sanctioned under the National Agriculture Development Scheme (NAV). A total of Rs. 20.06 crore (Rupees Twenty Crores Six Lakhs only) was disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture) as per the Government decisions dated 31/03/2021. Out of this amount, Rs. 17.16 crore (Rs. Seventeen crore and sixteen lakh only) is being distributed project-wise under this government decision. The details of the disbursement are indicated in Annexure-A attached and the Commissioner (Agriculture) shall make the said funds available to the concerned implementing agency.
2. National Agriculture Development Scheme- RAPTAR While utilising the funds available for the Scheduled Castes category for the implementation of the projects sanctioned under this scheme, the The instructions/ procedures in the Government Decision dated 31/03/2021 should be followed.
The funds to be disbursed under this Government decision are being disbursed as per the demand of the concerned implementing agencies however, as directed by the Committee in the 28th State Level Project Sanctioning Committee meeting of the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the concerned implementing agencies will be responsible for utilising the funds being disbursed during 2020-21 on priority basis for completion of old incomplete projects. The concerned implementing agencies and administrative departments should ensure that there is no liability of pending payments due to lack of funds under any new or old project.
