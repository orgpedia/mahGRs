Government's decision:
The difference between the Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) based price and the Minimum Support Price (MSP) announced by the Government for the seeds produced in Kharif and Rabi season 2019-20 will be paid to the farmers through Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited, Akola. 691.62 lakh is being disbursed to the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The expenditure for this scheme shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2021-22 and shall be covered under the following head:
Demand No. D- 3-2401, Crop Protection, 103, B-Seeds, (00) (28) On payment of difference between Minimum Support Price (MSP) and Agriculture Produce Market Committee (APMC) Support Price to seed producing farmers, (100% State Programme) 33- Financial Assistance, (2401 A922)
The amount of this difference should be deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the farmers through direct benefit transfer. It should be ensured that these funds are not kept in P.L.A./ bank accounts.
