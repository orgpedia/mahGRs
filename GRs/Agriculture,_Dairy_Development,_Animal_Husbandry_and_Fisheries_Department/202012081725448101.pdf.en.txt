The proposal:
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana is a group agriculture based scheme under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture under the Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan with 60% Central share and 40% State share.
In the year 2020-21, reference No. As per the above revised guidelines, under the Krishi Kalyan Mission, the Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana of the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture, as per the directions of the Central Government, a total of 20 groups of 4 each in the 4 Aspirational Districts of Nandurbar, Osmanabad, Washim and Gadchiroli have been established and implemented. Instructions are given in the letter above.
The scheme will be implemented in three districts of Nandurbar, Osmanabad and Gadchiroli by the respective Project Directors (ATMA) at the district level and in Washim district by the Project Director, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola at the district level.
