Introduction: To promote organic farming/ toxin free farming, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission is being implemented in six suicide/ depression affected districts of Vidarbha namely Akola, Amravati, Buldana, Washim, Yavatmal and Wardha in 4 years. Under this mission, in the financial year 2019-20, for the first phase of the scheme, Rs. A sum of Rs. 1000.00 lakh has been allocated for the project. Of this, 60 per cent will be funded. A sum of Rs. 600.00 lakh was disbursed. Of these, Rs. 5,41,30, 128/- (Rs. Five crore, forty one lakh, thirty thousand, one hundred and twenty eight only) is the amount of funds that have been spent. Last year's amount was Rs. 5,41,30, 128/- (Rupees Five Crores, Forty One Lakhs, Thirty Thousand, One Hundred and Twenty Eight only) has been requested by the Commissionerate of Agriculture to be approved for expenditure in the year 2020-21.

The following terms and conditions should be followed while implementing the scheme.
1) The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the State Government along with the progress report as soon as possible.
The physical and financial progress reports on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central and State Governments by the 5th of every month.
3) The balance sheet and the amount of the audit deposit related to the scheme should be reported by the implementing agency. It should clearly state the amount of interest and interest earned at the beginning of the year. So that the amounts can be considered in a transparent manner and there is no ambiguity.
4) It should be ensured that no programme beyond the approved programme is implemented under any circumstances.
