Government's decision:
The Government was considering to approve the allocation of 70% of the approved budgetary allocation for the year 2018-19 for establishment grants (mandatory), as follows:- Non-salaried assistants (Grants) to the District Councils under the scheme Title 24010163 (Non-Plan) being implemented by the Department of Agriculture.
The Finance Department vide Government Circular dated 02/04/2018 has approved the release of funds within the limit of 70% of the budgeted allocation. Therefore, in the following matters, the establishment grants (mandatory) to the District Councils under the Scheme Head 2401 0163 (Post-Plan), 31 Non-Paying Aids (Grants) for the year 2018-19, out of the sanctioned budgetary provision (Rs. 275.51 lakhs) with a limit of 70%. Financial sanction for disbursement of provisions amounting to Rs. 192,85,700/-lakh (Rupees One Hundred Ninety Five Lakhs Eighty Five Thousand Seven Hundred only) is being accorded by this Government Resolution.
