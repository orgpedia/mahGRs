The Proposal:
The state government has enacted the Cotton Seeds Regulation Act, 2009 for cotton seeds in view of the losses incurred by farmers due to complaints of poor cotton seeds. According to the law, farmers are to be compensated for the loss of their crops.
To frame a seed act on the lines of the Cotton Seeds Regulation Act, 2009 for the cases of possible cheating of farmers in respect of other seeds taken by farmers for agricultural purposes and to suggest preventive measures for the cases of adulteration in agricultural inputs like seeds, fertilizers, pesticides etc. The government was considering the formation of a cabinet sub-committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Agriculture).
Government Decision: To advise on the preparation of a seed law on the lines of the Cotton Seeds Regulation Act, 2009 for cases of possible cheating of farmers in respect of other seeds taken for agricultural purposes by farmers, and to suggest preventive measures for cases of adulteration in agricultural inputs such as seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, etc. A Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Agriculture).
(2) The composition of the Committee shall be as follows: Minister of Agriculture- Hon. Minister of State (Revenue, Animal Husbandry and Dairying) Minister of State (Rural Development and Panchayati Raj) Minister of Water Supply and Sanitation.
