Preamble: In order to stabilize the power generation program in the state and to maintain the participation of farmers in the power generation program, a new scheme to pay the difference between the Agricultural Produce Market Committee rate and the government declared base price (MSP) to the power producing farmers has been implemented from 2017-18 as per the government decision dated 17.09.2018.
Under this scheme, if the maximum daily rate of various crops in the district-wise prescribed period is less than the government declared base price (MSP), then the government provides subsidy to pay the difference to the seed producing farmers. This subsidy is given for certified and foundation seeds. In such cases, the difference amount is given to the farmers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (Mahabeej), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune as incentive subsidy. After considering the payment to the farmers, Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIS), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune submit the proposal to the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune. After scrutinizing this proposal, the appropriate proposal is submitted to the Agriculture Department through the Agriculture Commissionerate. Examine the proposal. The approval is given by a state-level committee headed by the Principal Secretary (Agriculture).
