The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries Scheme (PMMFPY) is a centrally sponsored scheme on the lines of One District One Product. The decision has been implemented in the state for the next five years from the year 2020-21. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments.
The entire expenditure (100%) for the financial year 2020-21 will be borne by the Government of India. Thereafter, the Centre will adjust the State's share for the financial year 2020-21 from the State's share in the next four years. In pursuance of the implementation of the said scheme, the Central Government has decided to establish a Project Management Cell for the financial year 2020-21, to prepare separate study reports, publicity and other planned items in the guidelines of the scheme.
