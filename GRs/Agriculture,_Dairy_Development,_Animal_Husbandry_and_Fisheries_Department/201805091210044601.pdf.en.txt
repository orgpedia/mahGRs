The Proposal:
The National Food Security Mission (NFSA) is a 60:40 sharing scheme between the Centre and the states for food security. The main objectives of the mission are to increase the production and productivity of the crops covered under it, increase the fertility and productivity of the farm land, create employment opportunities and increase the productivity of the farmers.
Central government. As per the letter dated May 01, 2017, an action plan of Rs. 23989.685 lakh has been approved. Accordingly, the Government is taking decision to accord administrative approval to the Annual Action Plan for food crops under National Food Security Mission-Foodgrains Crops with a total outlay of Rs. 23989.685 lakh under Krishi Unnati Yojana for the year 2018-19.
