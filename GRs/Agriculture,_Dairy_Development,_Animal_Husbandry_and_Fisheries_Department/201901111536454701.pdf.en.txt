Introduction: Refer No. As per the decision of the Government in 2, 3 and 4, the tuition fee reimbursement scheme for economically backward students admitted to private permanent unaided colleges of agriculture and allied subjects courses under agricultural universities in the state has been approved to be continued from the academic year 2015-16.
The State Government has launched the MahaDBT Portal to avail the benefits of various scholarships/ reimbursement schemes being provided to the students by the State Government. The Central Drawing and Disbursing Officer is required to be appointed for all the four Agricultural Universities of the State for reimbursement of educational fees/ payment of scholarships/ stipends to the economically backward students admitted under the Agricultural Universities of the State through this portal. Benefits of Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Allowance Schemes, etc.
The government was considering declaring the financial advisor at the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune office, as the central drawing and disbursing officer for making payments to students through MahaDBT.
Government Decision: To provide the benefit of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme (EBC) and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance Scheme to the students studying in the agriculture and allied courses under the four agricultural universities of the state.
