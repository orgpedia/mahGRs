Introduction: A new 100% state sponsored scheme, Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Yojana, will be implemented in the state for the next five years from the financial year 2017-18 with the objectives of promoting the food processing industry in the state, encouraging the establishment of projects based on modern technology through the participation of farmers to increase the value of agricultural products, to increase the quality of food produced, to promote the modernization of projects to save energy. This has been approved in the Cabinet meeting held on 30.05.2017. Accordingly, the plan has been approved. The Government has issued the decision on 20/06/2017. Similarly, the reference.
