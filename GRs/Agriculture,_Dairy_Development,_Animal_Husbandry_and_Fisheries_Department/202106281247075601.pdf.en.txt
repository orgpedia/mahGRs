The Proposal:
Students from economically weaker sections of all the four agricultural universities of the state are given Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance. In the year 2019-20, the scholarship was not paid for the students appearing in the spot admission round. As per letter No. 1, the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune has been informed that the scholarship is admissible to these students. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune has issued reference no. A proposal has been submitted to the government. In this regard, the Government was considering to disburse subsidy under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme under the Subsidiary Grant (24151139) (Programme) and Subsidiary Grant (2415 1148) (Programme) under the Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance Scheme under the 34- Discipleship/ Vidyavet.
The government has decided to provide scholarships to the students of all the four agricultural universities of the state under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme through the Maha DBT system.
