GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 0319/ PR. No. 88/14-A GOVERNMENT DECISION:
In 2019-20, the State had received a total amount of Rs. 418.44 crore under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) in the ratio of 60:40 Central and State funding. The funds were distributed to various projects sanctioned under the National Agriculture Development Scheme. During 2019-20, out of the general category funds of Rs. 315.38 crore released to various projects, the implementing agencies had dedicated Rs. 16.19 crore out of the sanctioned funds and Rs. 63.36 crore was pending for project wise disbursement as per the likely expenditure over the period. Thus, a total of Rs. 79.55 crore was available for re-distribution with the Commissioner (Agriculture).
National Agriculture Development Scheme. As per letter dated 03/03/2020 (Refer No. 9) As per instructions, re-distribution of funds to the tune of Rs. 38.65 crore out of the funds available for such re-distribution is provided in Reference No. 10 here. The remaining amount of Rs. 40.90 crore has been sanctioned vide Government Resolution dated 16/11/2020 and the Commissioner (Agriculture) has approved other projects sanctioned under the scheme vide Reference No. The redistribution has been carried out from time to time under the above orders 12 to 21, the redistribution is being approved by the government under this government decision. The project-wise/ implementation mechanism-wise details of the reallocated funds of Rs. 40.90 crore is at Annexure-B attached to this Government Resolution.
