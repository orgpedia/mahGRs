The Proposal:
For the year 2019-20, the Government of India has approved an Action Plan for food crops (Rice, Wheat, Pulses and Nutri Cereals) under National Food Security Mission for an amount of Rs. 22019.8155 lakh. 22019.8155 Lakhs (Central Share Rs. 13211.8893 Lakhs+ State Share of Rs. 8807.9262 lakhs) has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 03.06.2019. The Central Government has released Rs. 6840.53 lakh in the first instalment under the Mission and as per the demand of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, the Government is taking the following decisions to release the required funds from the Central and State share

3. The total amount of Rs. 1162.90 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission as per the decision of the Government should be disbursed district wise through PFMS system by the Director (Extension& Training).
The Director (Extension and Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the withdrawal of funds to the tune of Rs. 1162.90 lakhs made available under this Government decision.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Director (Extension and Training) should make use of the funds from time to time in accordance with the allocation.
The certificate should be submitted to the government. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Secretary (Agriculture).
