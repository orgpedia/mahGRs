Introduction- Kranti Singh Nana Patil Veterinary College, Shirwal, under Maharashtra Animal and Fisheries Sciences University, Nagpur. Students from all over the state and the country are admitted to this college. The campus of this college is 3 km away from Shirval village and due to increasing industrialization, there is no accommodation facility available around the college.
Therefore, students have to depend on the hostel of the college. Currently, there are approximately 470 students enrolled in the program. There are about 330 boys and 140 girls. The increased number of students necessitates hostel accommodation for the infrastructure of the college. Accordingly, it was necessary to build a hostel for the students of this college. Accordingly, the Registrar, Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, vide reference No. 3 here. Kranti Singh Nana Patil Veterinary College, Shirwal, Tt. The preliminary budget of the children's hostel building in Khandala, G. Satara has been submitted. The project is estimated to cost Rs. As the proposal is more than Rs. 15.00 crore, it will be scrutinized by the Public Works Department. The approval of the Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary was required. As per the approval given by the committee, the matter of administrative approval of the construction cost of the said college building was under consideration at the government level.
