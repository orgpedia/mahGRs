Considering the suggestions, the government was considering opening an ESCROW bank account in a nationalized bank at the level of Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune, to implement the new program Horticulture Cluster Development Program (HCCD) in the state through the National Horticulture Board.
The government is taking the following steps:
Government Decision: The Commissioner (Agriculture) and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune have been declared as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Horticulture Cluster Development in the State and approval is being given to open a separate PFMS account with e-banking facility at State Bank of India, University Road, Pune subject to the following conditions.
1) Do not make any other transactions in this bank account.
2) The account should be audited in accordance with the rules of this department.
3) The National Horticulture Board should submit a quarterly report to the government about the funds deposited in the account and the funds spent.
The Government decision was taken by the Department of Finance in its Informal Reference No. 100/ 2022/ Toshashan Prasar-5, No. 3. being issued pursuant to the consent given vide 10.05.2022.
