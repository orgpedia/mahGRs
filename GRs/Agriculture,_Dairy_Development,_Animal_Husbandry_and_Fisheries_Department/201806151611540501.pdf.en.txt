Government's decision:
Demand No. 2018-19 An annual provision of Rs. 10.00 crore (Rs. Ten crore only) has been budgeted under the head T-5,2401, Crop Protection, Schemes under Five Year Plans, 796, Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01) Schemes under Tribal Area Appropriation, Schemes under State Schemes, (01) (03) Subsidiary Grants (Programme) to Agricultural Universities, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (2401 8291). Out of the said budgetary provision, a sum of Rs. 20 lakh has been released for the completion of construction of Phase-I of Sonapur, District-Gadchiroli Agriculture College under Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Resolution dated 11th June, 2018 read with Rule 7 of the Tribal Development Department and the terms and conditions mentioned below. A grant of Rs. 5.00 crore (Rupees Five Crores only) is being sanctioned.
The Controller, Punjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola shall ensure that the said funds are spent during the year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing Government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made, the utilization certificate of the fund, the material receipts achieved in the head of accounts, etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, Tribal Development Department and this department by the 10th of every month.
