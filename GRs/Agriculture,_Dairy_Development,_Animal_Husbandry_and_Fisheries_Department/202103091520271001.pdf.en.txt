Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of release: March 9, 2021
Reference- 1) Letter No. 33015-03/2017-FY (H), Dy. 10.01.2020 2) Government Resolution No. 12.11.2020
The Government of India has proposed to set up a Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) to augment fisheries production and develop infrastructure in the fisheries sector. Under this, the Central Government vide Reference No. As per the letter, the states have sent a draft tripartite agreement for providing financial assistance through NABARD for the proposals for creation of necessary facilities. Accordingly, the scheme is being implemented in the State. 2 Administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the government decision here.
The government was considering the formation of a separate high-level committee at the department level to approve the projects being implemented under the FIDF.
Government Decision: To approve projects in the state under the Fisheries Infrastructure Development Fund Scheme. A high-level committee under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra is being constituted.
