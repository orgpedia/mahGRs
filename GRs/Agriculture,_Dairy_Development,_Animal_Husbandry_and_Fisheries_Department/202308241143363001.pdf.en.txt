Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400032
Date: August 24, 2023
Read- Government's decision As of August 3, 2023
Preamble: It has been approved to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Fisheries), Government of Wacha to determine the Fisheries Development Policy for the Groundwater and Marine Districts. The Government was considering to include the names of Shri Ram Naik, former Governor of Uttar Pradesh as Chairman and 2 members of Legislative Assembly and 1 member of Legislative Council from the Marine District and 2 members of Legislative Assembly and 1 member of Legislative Council from the Ground Water District in the Government decision.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the inclusion of Shri Ram Naik, former Governor of Uttar Pradesh as Chairman and 2 Members of Legislative Assembly and 1 Member of Legislative Council from Marine District and 2 Members of Legislative Assembly and 1 Member of Legislative Council from Marine District in partial changes in the committee constituted under the Government Decision to determine the Fisheries Development Policy in the Groundwater and Marine District.
