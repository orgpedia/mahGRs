The Proposal:
General Administration Department Under the Government decision dated 21/12/2019, instructions have been given to create more number of posts for the employees who are appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes and transfer their services to the higher post. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for a total of 46 employees in the Dairy Development Department.
Government's decision:
Employees who are appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category in the Dairy Development Department, but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of that category or have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes, and who have filed a claim in the court against the decision of caste certificate verification. Under this Government decision, 46 supernumerary posts are being created as shown in the return" 1" on the establishment of the office concerned for the employees in cases where the Court has not stayed the decision on caste certificate verification.
