The Proposal:
" Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries Scheme" is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme on the lines of" One District One Product" Reference No. The decision has been implemented in the state for the next five years from 2020-21. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. The scheme will be implemented in 2023-24. 3 here. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 11.05.2023. Department of Tribal Development No. 6 here. As per the Government decision dated 08.08.2023, the Central Share for Scheduled Tribes is Rs. 339.92 lakh and State share of Rs. 226.61333 lacs out of which Rs. 566.53333 lakh has been provided to the Agriculture Department. The matter of disbursement of funds to State Nodal Agency (PMFME) and Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune was under consideration of the Government. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Central share of Scheduled Tribes for the period 2023-24 for the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries Scheme in the State is Rs. 339.92 lakh and State share of Rs. 226.61333 lacs out of which Rs. 566.53333 lakh (Rupees Five Crore Sixty Six Lakh Three Thousand Three Hundred Thirty Three only) to State Nodal Agency (PMFME) and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The expenditure shall be met from the funds made available on the BDS system under the head of accounts.
