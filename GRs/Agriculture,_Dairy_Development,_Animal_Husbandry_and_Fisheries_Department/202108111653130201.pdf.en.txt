The Proposal:
" Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries Scheme" is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme on the lines of" One District One Product" Reference No. The decision has been implemented in the state for the next five years from the year 2020-21. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments.
The entire expenditure (100%) for the financial year 2020-21 will be borne by the Government of India. Thereafter, the excess expenditure on the State's account for the financial year 2020-21 will be adjusted by the State in the next four years. In pursuance of the implementation of the said scheme, for the financial year 2020-21, the Central Government has released a sum of Rs. 9,89,78, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crore Ninety-nine Lakh Seventy-eight Thousand only) as first instalment under Reference No. 2 dated 04.09.2020 to the Commissioner (Agriculture) for establishment of Project Management Cell, various study reports, publicity and other scheduled matters in the guidelines of the scheme.
