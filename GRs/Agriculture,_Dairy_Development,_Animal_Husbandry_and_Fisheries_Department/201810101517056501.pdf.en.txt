Introduction: The context. As per the decision of the Government on 27th April, 2018, guidelines have been issued for the implementation of Unnat Krishi Samriddhi Kisan Abhiyaan 2018-19. In section 4.5.1 a of the said guidelines, a basket of self-propelled agricultural machines and implements permissible under the mechanization component of the existing schemes has been prescribed. A proposal to include single/ double plough in the list of agricultural machinery and implements was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision: As per the Government decision dated 27.04.2018, single/ double ploughing implements are being included in the basket of machines/ implements prescribed in Annexure No. 4.5.1 under the Agricultural Mechanization Component.
