The Proposal:
As per the decision taken by the government, a committee has been approved to be formed under the chairmanship of the Director, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) to take action on the impact of water pollution on fish production. The Member Secretary of the Committee is the Deputy Director, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board. But due to the absence of the post of Deputy Director, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, in the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board headquarters office, the work of the committee is not being done properly and the purpose of the committee is not being achieved. Therefore, the Commissioner (Fisheries) has proposed to change the draft of the committee by changing the member secretary and the chairman. 2 has been submitted to the government. Read that No. 1 The issue of issuing revised government decisions by formalizing the government decisions here was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
The starred question No. During the discussion on the answer to the question 12178. On 14.03.2022, the Leader of the Opposition raised the issue of the need to take cognizance of the incidents of death of fish in the sea due to pollution of river/ sea water. In pursuance of the directions given in the discussion in this regard, the Government is approving the constitution of a Joint Committee of the Department of Fisheries and the Department of Environment as mentioned below.

The functions of the said Committee shall be as follows:
(a) To inquire into complaints relating to water pollution and environmental issues.
(b) To make necessary investigations as to whether or not the quality of water is poor due to release of chemical-laced water by the factory into the river/ sea.
e) To take necessary measures to prevent the occurrence of fish deaths due to water pollution.
(e) Propose action against the company/ employee for causing damage to fisheries due to water pollution.
The Committee shall meet once every month and submit its report to the Department of Environment and Fisheries.
The Director, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) shall take necessary measures to implement the objectives set out in the meeting of the said Committee and the Governing Decision of the Committee.
