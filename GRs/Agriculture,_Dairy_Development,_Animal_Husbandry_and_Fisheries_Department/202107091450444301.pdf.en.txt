The Proposal:
State level innovation of goat group distribution through Animal Husbandry Department and increase in the base price of goat and goat/ sheep and male sheep in the district level scheme. The approval was accorded by the Cabinet in its meeting held on 12.05.2021. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government's decision:
Read No. 1 here. The subsidy payable under the scheme at para 2 of the Government Resolution dated 16.11.2017 may be read as under:
Subsidy under the scheme
The total expected cost of the goat group (goat group and shed for goats) proposed to be distributed to each selected beneficiary is Rs. 2,31,400 respectively. At the time of forming the group, initially the beneficiary has to raise 100% of the funds through self-financing/ loans from financial institutions. However, for all categories, 50% of the actual expenditure is allowed subject to a ceiling of Rs. 1,15,700/- (Back ended subsidy).
