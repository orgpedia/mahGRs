Preamble: The Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry& Dairying Development and Fisheries will implement the component of Per Drop More Crop (Supplementary Water Management) under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) from 2019-20. The ratio between the Centre and the state is 60:40. Under this component, the government was considering opening a new head of account to provide for central and state accounts for the general category. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Per Drop More Crop (Supplementary Water Management Matters), the Government is approving opening of the following accounts for Central and State accounts for the general category.
