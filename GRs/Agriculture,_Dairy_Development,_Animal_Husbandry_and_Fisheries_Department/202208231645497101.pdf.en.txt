A committee has been formed to suggest measures to stop illegal fish traders in the Navi Mumbai area.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No: Parichay- 1022/ PR No. 162/ Padum- 14
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: August 23, 2022
Preamble- Second Session of 2022 (Monsoon Session) Ramesh Dada Patil and other Vipass, presented Starred Questions No. 13773-" Sale of fish by migrants in Navi Mumbai without license." In response to this question, The Minister made a statement in the House that a committee comprising the Commissioner and all the concerned honourable members would be constituted in this regard. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a committee to suggest measures to prevent illegal migrants from selling fish in the Navi Mumbai area.
Government Decision: The following committee is being constituted to suggest measures to prevent illegal migrants from selling fish in the Navi Mumbai area
