As per the provisions of the Drought Management Code, 2016, as per the decision of the Government in reference No. 4, the taluks of the State have been declared as drought hit during Kharif 2018.
Further, the Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted to suggest immediate measures/ take control/ decision for drought period had earlier sanctioned Rs. 2 crore to the Gaushalas under the scheme Govardhan Govansh Seva Kendra in the severely drought-affected districts of Marathwada. In the Gaushalas which have been selected for grant of Rs. 1.00 crore or where grant has been sanctioned, approval has been given to start Cattle Relief and Fodder Camps for the livestock of the area on a pilot basis with immediate effect. Accordingly, orders have been issued at reference No. 4.
