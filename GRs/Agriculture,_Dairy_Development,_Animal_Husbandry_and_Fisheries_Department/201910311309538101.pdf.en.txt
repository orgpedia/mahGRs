Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Krishi Unnati- Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission is being implemented in the State. The funding pattern is 60:40 between the Centre and the state. For 2018-19, the government has allocated Rs. 14,600 crore has been provided. Out of this amount, Rs. 2794.038 lacs remained unspent at the end of the financial year. This fund has been re-financed by the Government of India vide letter dated June, 2019 for the current financial year.
Out of the above unspent amount, the Central share of General, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes is Rs. 302.053 Lacs, Rs. 1430.604 Lakhs and Rs. 1061.381 lakh. The central share of Rs. 129.189 lakh has been deposited in PFMS Bank Account of Commissionerate of Agriculture and remaining Rs. 1301.415 lakh is deposited in the Government treasury. The Central Government has earmarked Rs. 1301.415 Lakhs and Rs. Rs 800 crore. A proposal to disburse Rs 2101.415 lakh to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Financial Management System (BDS) was under consideration of the government.
