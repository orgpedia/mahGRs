Balasaheb Prabhakar Dodatale has been appointed as the Chairman of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation Limited, Pune. .
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No.1019/ No.56/ Padum-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 21, 2019
As per the powers conferred by Article 72 of the Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation Limited, Pune, Mr. Balasaheb Prabhakar Dodtale, Mr. Chaturvedi, Pt. Ambassa, Ambajogai, G. Beed are being appointed. During the period of his appointment, he will hold the rank of Minister of State.
Travel allowance, daily allowance and other services to Mr. Balasaheb Dodtale vide Government Resolution No. S.A. U- 10.10/ P of Finance Department. 96/10/ CE, dated 13.03.2012.
