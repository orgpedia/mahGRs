The Proposal:
Chemical fertilizers, insecticides, fungicides and weedicides are widely used for the further production of cereals and hybrids and other crops. As a result, the ecological balance is disturbed and the soil is destroyed. This affects the health of animals and humans alike. Also, the use of chemical fertilizers deteriorates the texture of the soil. This has increased the cost of production. Also, there is a huge cost involved in the purchase of chemicals. Organic certification of farm produce produced through organic farming method, primary processing, market chain, formation of group groups through which agricultural products can get market and fair price. Organic farming is being implemented in the state under the Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission under the state sponsored scheme of Organic Farming/ Poison Free Farming so that the production cost of the farmers can be reduced, the net profit can be increased and the income can be increased.
