A total of 776 posts have been sanctioned at the state level in the cadre of Livestock Supervisor Group-C in the Department of Animal Husbandry, out of which 378 posts are vacant. In view of the outbreak of H1Five (bird flu) virus in poultry and other birds in some districts of the state, as an immediate measure, the matter of filling up of essential vacancies in connection with providing animal health services to the livestock farmers of the state was under consideration. However, since it will take some time to fill up these posts on regular basis, the vacancies in Livestock Supervisor (Group-C), Cadre, may be filled up as per reference no. As per the provisions of the Government Decision/ Circulars 2 to 6 6, the following decisions are being taken for immediate payment by Outsourcing.
Government's decision:
Out of 378 vacancies in the cadre of Livestock Supervisor (Group-C) in the Department of Animal Husbandry, 262 posts are available in Reference No. 2 to 6 are being approved by the Government to be made available through Outsourcing as per the procedures prescribed in the Government Decision/ Circular.

The period of Livestock Supervisor appointed from outside through this recruitment process shall be for a period of 11 months or till a regular candidate is found for the post of Livestock Supervisor, whichever is earlier. The appointment will be automatically terminated after 11 months.
(6) Even after this, the posts will remain vacant due to non-availability of regular candidates and if there is an urgent need for these posts for the functioning of the department, the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune shall give such information to the said selection process committee at least three months in advance. Thereafter, fresh recruitment process will be carried out by the said selection committee in accordance with the vacancies.
While revising the recruitment process, the work/ experience which was earlier hired by the candidate through external sources should be selected on the basis of merit only without any consideration while re-selecting. However, no extension of time will be admissible to the candidates appointed through the outsourced mechanism selected by the Selection Processing Committee under any circumstances.
(7) Since the appointment is temporary for a period of 11 months or till the time a recommendatory candidate is found for the post of Livestock Supervisor, the person concerned cannot claim any authority for the permanent absorption or retention of persons in the Government service who are serving through the outsourced system.
