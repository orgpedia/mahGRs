The Proposal:
The centrally sponsored National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP) is being implemented in the State since 2014-15 to increase the production of oilseeds. The Mission comprises of three mini-Abhiyan-1 (oilseeds), mini-Abhiyan-2 (oilseeds) and mini-Abhiyan-3 (tree-based oilseeds). However, at present only two missions, namely, Mission Laghu Abhiyan-1 and Mission Laghu Abhiyan-3, are being implemented. The ratio between the Centre and the state is 60:40.

The central government. As per the letter dated 08/03/2018, the erstwhile National Mission on Oilseeds and Oilseeds has been subsumed under National Food Security Mission from 2018-19 as Mission 1 (Oilseeds) and Mission 3 (Tree Oilseeds). The Centre's share and the state's share (60:40) will remain the same.
The Government of India has decided to implement this scheme during 2018-19. A sum of Rs. 6904.98 lakh has been sanctioned under the programme vide letter dated 02.05.2018. This includes Central share of Rs. 4174.98 lakh and State share of Rs. 2730.00 lakh. The programme The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 16th May, 2018.
As per the Government Resolution, dated 19.06.2018, a total of Rs. 2087.49 lakh has been released as Central share of the first instalment of funds under Mission for General Category for Micro-Abhiyaan-1 (Oilseeds) and Rs. 1391.66 lakh as State share and Rs. 18.10 lakh as Central share of funds for Micro-Abhiyaan-3 (Tree borne Oilseeds) as first instalment and Rs. 12.0666 lakh as State share amounting to Rs. 3509.3166 lakh.
For the year 2018-19, under Krishi Unnati Yojana-National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds and Tree Oilseeds), the remuneration for the post of Commissionerate Level Contractual will be paid by the District Agriculture Officer, Pune. The Commissionerate of Agriculture has said that a fund of Rs.56,000/- is required. As reported in the letter dated 02/08/2018. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds made in the budget for contingency expenditure at the level of Agriculture Commissionerate on the state's account under this campaign was under the consideration of the government, on which the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: 1. Krishi Unnati Yojana-National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds and Tree Oilseeds) for the year 2018-19. 56,000/- (Rupees Fifty Six Thousand only) to the Commissioner (Agriculture) is being approved for disbursement through Budget Distribution System.
