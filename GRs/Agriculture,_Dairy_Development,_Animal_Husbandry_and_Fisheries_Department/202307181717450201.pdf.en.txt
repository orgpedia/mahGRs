Introduction: Considering the difficulties faced by the farmers in the cultivation, processing and sale of cashew fruit, the" Cashew Fruit Development Scheme" has been implemented in the state under the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context of the overall development of cashew in Maharashtra. As per the decision, the government has included Ajra and Chandgad taluks in Kolhapur district. Director (Horticulture), vide letter No. 2, has requested to include Bhudargad, Gadhinglaj and Radhanagari taluks in the scheme as good quality cashew is being produced in large quantities in Bhudargad, Gadhinglaj and Radhanagari taluks and the demand for cashew cultivation and setting up of processing industries is increasing from these taluks. Accordingly, the proposal to include Bhudargad, Gadhinglaj and Radhanagari talukas of Kolhapur district in the cashew fruit development scheme was under consideration. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: It is approved to include Bhudargad, Gadhinglaj and Radhanagari talukas of Kolhapur district in the scheme of" Cashew Fruit Development Scheme" implemented under the Government Decision No. 1 under reference.
