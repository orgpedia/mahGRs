The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the state governments. As per the letter dated 12/05/2020, Maharashtra has been sanctioned a total allocation of Rs. 478.10 crore for the year 2020-21 with a Central share of Rs. 286.86 crore (General Rs. 226.96 crore, Scheduled Castes Rs. 34.55 crore and Scheduled Tribes Rs. 25.35 crore) and State share of Rs. 191.24 crore (General Rs. 151.30 crore, Scheduled Castes Rs. 23.03 crore and Scheduled Tribes Rs. 16.90 crore).
In view of the impact on the economy during the financial year 2020-21 due to the outbreak of COVID-19, the Department of Finance has approved the issuance of revised administrative approval orders to the Centrally Sponsored Schemes" within the limits of the total available funds and budgetary provisions for the previous financial year (Central share+ State share) and the first instalment received in the current financial year (Central share+ State share).
