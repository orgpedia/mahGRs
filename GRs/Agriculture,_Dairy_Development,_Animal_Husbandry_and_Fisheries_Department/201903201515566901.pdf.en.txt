Introduction: Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Ambia Bahar was launched in the State in 2017-18 by 3 insurance companies namely IFFCO TOKIO General Insurance Company, HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company and Bajaj Alliance Insurance Company. This is being implemented as per the Government decision dated 27.09.2017.
The scheme was launched in 2018-19 by two insurance companies- New India Assurance Company Limited and Agriculture Insurance Company of India Limited. This is being implemented as per the Government decision dated 28.09.2018.
