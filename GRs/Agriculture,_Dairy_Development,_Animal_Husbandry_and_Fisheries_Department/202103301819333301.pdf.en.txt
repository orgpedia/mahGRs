Preamble: In the total budget for the year 2020-21, provision under the head of accounts (8342 0141) for reimbursement of defined contribution pension scheme deposited by the employees of Agricultural Universities and their affiliated Aided Institutions has been increased from Rs. 3,03,03, 000/- out of which Rs. 2,27,27, 250/- (Rupees Two Crores, Twenty Seven Lakhs, Twenty Seven Thousand, Two Hundred and Fifty only) as Grants vide Reference No. 8 are distributed. As per the demand under reference number 4 to 7 regarding the distribution of the remaining grant, the matter of disbursement of funds was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following is approved:
Government Decision: The revised estimates have been approved for the return of defined contribution pension scheme deposited by the employees of agricultural universities and their affiliated aided institutions in the state under the head of accounts (83420141) as per the provision in the budget for the year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Government is approving the disbursement of the remaining amount of Rs. 75,12,431/- (Rupees Fifty Seven Lakhs Twelve Thousand Four Hundred and Thirty One only) as per the statement A attached with the grant.

2.This Agreement is subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The terms and conditions of the Government Decision at Reference No. 1 to 3 should be fulfilled.
The funds are being disbursed based on the demand and availability of funds of the universities under reference number 4 to 7.
The grants sanctioned to the University shall be spent within the prescribed limits as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Statutes, 1990 and the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Accounts Code, 1991 and after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the prevailing Government Order and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
(4) The grant shall not be deposited under any circumstances in the personal provident fund/ PLA bank account.
The grant should be spent in the financial year 2020-21 only after approval at the competent level for expenditure.
6) Submit to the government the utilization certificate of the funds being made available through the budget estimates.
(7) Where the funds distributed sub-heading wise, group wise, scheme wise and subject wise are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(8) The grants distributed among each group shall be used to meet the expenditure on permissible items in that group. Grants of one group should not be diverted for schemes/ activities of another group.
Each sub-heading/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts should be maintained.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether or not the grants sanctioned to the agricultural universities are being spent in accordance with the prescribed orders/ procedures. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
11) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
12) Don't spend money on illegal schemes, posts and activities.
13) While spending the funds, the guidelines and prevailing terms and conditions issued by the Planning and Finance Department and the instructions given from time to time should be strictly followed as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book and the Financial Rights Rule Book.
