Preamble: Out of the applications received for Micro Irrigation kits under National Micro Irrigation Scheme (NMMI) during 2013-14 and on Farm Water Management Scheme (OFWM) during 2014-15, the pending list of beneficiaries in Beed district on e-drip system was found to be at the level of bill invoice, moka scrutiny level, fixing of subsidy of some beneficiaries, due to which subsidy was yet to be disbursed to 521 beneficiary farmers during 2013-14 and 2014-15.
During the 2020 legislative session. The Minister (Agriculture) had assured that the pending subsidy would be examined and further action would be taken. Accordingly, the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune vide Reference No. 3 As per the letter, the proposal regarding pending subsidy of the beneficiaries for the year 2013-14 and 2014-15 for micro-irrigation in Beed district has been submitted to the Government. The government was considering to disburse the pending subsidy to the beneficiary farmers. Following this, the government decided:
Government Decision: 1. Received for Micro Irrigation kits under National Micro Irrigation Scheme (NMMI) for the year 2013-14 and on Farm Water Management Scheme (OFWM) for the year 2014-15 in Beed district
