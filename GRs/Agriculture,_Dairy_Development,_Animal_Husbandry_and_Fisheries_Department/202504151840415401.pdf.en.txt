Introduction: In order to increase the effectiveness of farmer extension services and disseminate agricultural knowledge to farmers seamlessly, it is necessary to develop comprehensive Farmer Field School (FFS) and Learning Management System (LMS) applications. The mobile app will be equipped with necessary facilities like Decision Support Systems (DSS) to help farmers take decisions on crop management and use of machinery, Integrated Feedback System to track farmers' progress, and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for each crop, etc., and will continue to serve as an important platform to improve the learning experience of farmers. In addition, the app will disseminate educational resources related to soil health, water management, and animal husbandry through digital farms, water schools, and animal schools.
