GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: DAHD- 14034/2/2023/ DAHD-AH-4 (E-664470)
The National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) is part of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Animal Health and Disease Control (LH& DC). Since the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune has been declared as the Implementing Officer of the scheme under 3.06.2023, the National Animal Disease Control Programme is required to be implemented in the State through the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored flagship scheme of Animal Health and Disease Control, various sub-schemes of National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) and Control of Critical Animal Diseases (CADCP), Establishment of Mobile Veterinary Units (ESVHD-MVU) and ASCAD should be implemented by the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune.
5. Transfer of CNA bank account opened for 100% central share at the level of CEO, Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur to Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune/ opening of new bank account for 100% central share in the name of Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune.
The implementation of this Centrally Sponsored Scheme should be subject to the actual funds available as per the guidelines issued by the Department of Animal Husbandry, Government of India in the year 2021 as well as guidelines to be issued from time to time in future.
