Preamble: The State Government has decided to implement Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (Organic Farming) under National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture from 2015-16 with Central share of 60% and State share of 40%. At present, a three-year programme of 932 groups is being implemented.
The 3rd Executive Committee meeting of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana was held on 11th May, 2017 at New Delhi. In this meeting, the central government has approved 326 new groups for the state of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the State Government has accorded administrative approval to implement the first year programme of 326 groups under the Government Decision No. 6 and No. 7.

The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the Central and State Governments along with the progress report as soon as possible by keeping the accounts of expenditure in order.
Physical and financial progress reports on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central and State Governments by the 5th of every month.
