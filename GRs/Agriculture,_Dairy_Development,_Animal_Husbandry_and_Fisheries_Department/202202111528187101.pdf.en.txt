The Proposal:
Read here. As per the Government decision dated 27.12.2021, administrative approval has been accorded to implement the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Livestock Mission in the State from the year 2021-22 as per the revised guidelines of the Central Government.
A large number of project proposals are being submitted by the farmers/ cattle rearers/ poultry traders of the state on the online portal developed by the Central Government under this scheme. The project proposals must be thoroughly scrutinized at the district level first and then at the level of the Commissioner of Animal Husbandry as to whether the guidelines issued by the Central Government in accordance with the scheme and how the terms and conditions are met. As the project proposals that have qualified after the scrutiny at the level of Animal Husbandry Commissioner are expected to be presented in the meeting of the State Level Executive Committee constituted at the state level, the government is issuing the following decision in connection with the formation of the Screening Committee at the level of District and Animal Husbandry Commissioner.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves the constitution of the following District Level and Animal Husbandry Commissionerate level Screening Committees for in-depth scrutiny of the project proposals received by the Commissionerate of Animal Husbandry, Pune on the online portal developed by the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored National Livestock Mission from the year 2021-22, as to whether the guidelines issued by the Central Government in pursuance of the scheme and the terms and conditions are being followed.
(a) District Level Scrutiny Committee:
District Animal Husbandry Deputy Commissioner (concerned district) Chairman District Animal Husbandry Officer, Zilla Parishad (concerned district) Member 3. Expert persons concerned with the project proposal (if required) Member 4. Office of the Assistant Commissioner Animal Husbandry, concerned District Animal Husbandry Deputy Commissioner Member Secretary

Functions of the District Committee:
1.To examine the project proposal as per the guidelines of the Central Government.
All the necessary documents along with the project proposal. Aadhaar card, PAN card, residential proof, detailed project report, proof of ownership of land or lease agreement, photographs of the actual site, proof of self-financing under the project, bank statements for the last six months, training and experience certificates, etc.
3. To inspect and ascertain the actual proposed site/ land as mentioned in the project proposal.
Ensure availability of water, electricity, transport, etc.
Ensure the availability of necessary infrastructure.
4. To examine the availability of necessary bank credit as per the project proposal.
The Committee shall give its views on the proposed project in terms of climatic conditions, available infrastructure and sales management in the district.
To provide suitable guidance to the concerned person or organization regarding incomplete/ defective project proposal in respect of points 1 to 5 mentioned above.
7. Submission of proposals which are complete only after inspection/ scrutiny as mentioned above to the Scrutiny Committee constituted at the level of the Commissionerate of Animal Husbandry, Pune within a period of 15 days from the receipt of the project proposal.
Functions of the State Board:
1.To ensure that the proposals submitted by the District Level Scrutiny Committee are accepted by the Central Government on the recommendation of the State Level Executive Committee.
2. Submit the complete proposals received from the District Level Committee to the State Level Executive Committee.
(iii) To revert the incomplete proposals online to the concerned persons/ organizations for rectification of the defects.
4. Review of proposals received district wise on regular basis and expeditiously scrutinize the proposals.
To co-ordinate with the Bank as required for sanction of bank loans, to inform the Bank about the scheme/ project and to co-operate with the person/ institution concerned for sanction of bank loans.
