Introduction: Under the State Sponsored Scheme of Organic Farming/ Poison Free Farming, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission will be implemented in six districts of Vidarbha namely Buldhana, Akola, Washim, Amravati, Yavatmal and Wardha. 1 Administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the above Government Decision. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission has been extended from 2022-23 to 2027-28 by expanding the scope of the scheme across the state.
Punjabrao Deshmukh Natural Farming Mission. Administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the above government decision.
The scheme is group-based and groups are formed in a group of 50 hectares. Under the scheme, the benefit is given to the selected group/ beneficiary once for 3 years.
