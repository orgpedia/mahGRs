The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 and 2, the cooperative and private milk powder plants for export of milk powder and liquid milk have been allocated Rs. 50/- per kg and Rs. 5/- per litre of milk and Rs. 5/ Subsidy scheme has been implemented. The budgeted funds for the grants to be distributed under this scheme are" Demand No. It was available under the headings D-5, 2404 Dairy Development, 202, Government Milk Scheme, Pune, Procurement (Mandatory) (2404 0804), 29- Milk Procurement. However, considering that this scheme is a separate and new scheme, the matter of opening a separate account for the scheme was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the opening of new accounting heads for the scheme of incentive subsidy for conversion of milk powder and liquid milk and surplus milk in the State implemented under the decision of the Government under reference as follows:
