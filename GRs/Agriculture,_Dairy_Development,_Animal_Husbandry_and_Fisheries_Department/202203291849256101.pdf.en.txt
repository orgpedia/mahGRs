The Proposal:
The scheme is being implemented on 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the state government. As per the letter dated 08.03.2022, the total central share of Rs. A sum of Rs. 120.55 crore (General Category- 90.60 crore, SC- 17.28 crore, ST- 12.67 crore) has been disbursed and the corresponding State share is Rs. 80.36 crore. Thus, a total of Rs. A proposal for grant of administrative approval for Rs. 200.91 crore is under consideration. The State Government will allocate Rs. 90.60 crore. A total amount of Rs.151 crore is available for disbursement out of which Rs. 60.40 crore is available for disbursement. In 2021-22, the administrative approval for the implementation of the National Agriculture Development Scheme as well as the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs.151 crore for the implementation of the projects sanctioned under the scheme were under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of a programme of Rs. 200.91 crore for Normal RKVM in the ratio of 60:40 between Central and State share under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana-Raftaar in 2021-22. This fund is being made available in the current year for implementation of the programmes under the projects approved by the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee as follows:
