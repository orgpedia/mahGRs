The Proposal:
The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and Commissionerates of Agriculture and subordinate offices.
Under the e-governance program, various activities are carried out by the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (itself) and the Agriculture Commissionerate, and various activities are planned.
The amount sanctioned under this scheme is distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, as per the requirement.
The e-Governance Programme is a scheme under the scheme and a total amount of Rs.25 crore has been budgeted for the scheme (3451 2782) for the financial year 2023-24. As per the circular, 70% of the budgetary allocation for the year 2023-24 for essential and programme expenditure i.e. Rs. 17.50 crore has been made available to the Department for the State e-Governance Programme for the first nine months and accordingly, the State e-Governance Programme has been launched. The administrative and financial approval has been granted under the Government Resolution dated 11th May, 2023. The Finance Department had released Rs. 99.40 lakhs in reference No. 2 It has been allocated to the Department of Agriculture (itself) by the government decision.
Therefore, the Commissioner of Agriculture, vide Reference No. 3 In pursuance of the demand made under the proposal herein, the limit of funds available under the State e-Governance Programme is Rs.
