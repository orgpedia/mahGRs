The Proposal:
The Panchayati Raj Committee of the Legislative Assembly reviews the audit of the Zila Parishads and the Annual Governance Report by taking the testimony of the concerned CEO and all other concerned officers. During this testimony, at times, deposition of Divisional Secretaries of the concerned Ministries is held to elicit further information in respect of an issue.
The Panchayati Raj Committee visited the Zilla Parishad, Sangli and Chandrapur and took the testimony of the Chief Executive Officer and all other concerned officers. A fine of Rs 25,000 has been imposed on him. The recommendation has to be approved by the concerned Divisional Commissioner.
For veterinary institutions at the ZP level, after procuring necessary medicines, weapons, implements, machinery, tools, fodder seeds, vaccines, etc. from the ZPs, the documents of such purchases should be properly put in the concerned register for availability for audit and it should be given a pagination number.
The schedule of audit in respect of Local Accounting Fund is communicated in advance to the Animal Husbandry Department of the District Council. Therefore, since there is definitely some time until the actual audit begins, it is important to keep all the documents/ documents required for the audit updated during this period.
The District Animal Husbandry Officer, Zilla Parishad is responsible for providing audit of purchase orders, delivery invoices, payments of supplies, stamped invoices of payments, etc.
