The Proposal:
State of Maharashtra's Agribusiness and Rural Transformation SMART has been approved to be implemented in all districts of the state over a period of 7 years (from 2019-20 to 2026-27).
For the year 2022-23, Rs. Rs 100 crore has been allocated for the project. Department of Finance As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 4 th June, 2022, the Department has been empowered to disburse funds@ 60% of the budgetary allocation. However, instructions have been received from the Department of Finance that the said funds will be made available to the Department at the rate of 7% per month on the Budget Distribution System.
