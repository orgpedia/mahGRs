The Proposal:
The Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI) at Solapur, Kolhapur, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nashik, Nagpur and Khopoli has been designated as the District Agriculture Officer. It was declared under the Government Resolution dated 31.10.2001. For the Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI) in Pune, Kolhapur, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nashik, Nagpur and Khopoli, the government was considering declaring the principal of the concerned institution, RAMETI (Gazetted Group A), as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer instead of the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer, Nagpur. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
Rameeti (Gazetted Group A) (sanctioned post), concerned Principal of Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute at Pune, Kolhapur, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nashik, Nagpur and Khopoli etc., are being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer as per the provisions of Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
