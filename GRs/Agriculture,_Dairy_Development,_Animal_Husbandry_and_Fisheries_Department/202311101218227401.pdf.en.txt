Introduction: The' Citrus Estates' set up so far in the State of Punjab have been set up keeping in view the parameters of the fruits and vegetables produced by the' Citrus Estates' set up in the State, especially in the area of post-harvest technology development and value addition.
In the state of Punjab, there is a criterion of 1 citrus estate per 10,000 hectares of satta cultivation area. So far, citrus estates have been set up at five places in the state.
Considering the possibility of similar demand in other places in the state, it is necessary to set criteria for setting up' Fruit Estate' for fruit growers.
A committee had been formed for this. Following this, the government took decision on:
Government Decision:- The following committee is being constituted to determine the criteria regarding the" Fruitful Estate" to be established in the state.
