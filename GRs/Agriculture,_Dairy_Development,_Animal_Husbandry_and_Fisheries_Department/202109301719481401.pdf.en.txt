Government's stand:
In order to provide veterinary services to the animal patients at the doorstep of the livestock rearers, the government has approved the establishment of new mobile veterinary teams in 81 talukas of the state under the state level scheme" Mukhyamantri Pashu Swasthya Yojana" in addition to the 65 mobile veterinary clinics currently functioning in the state.
2. Read paragraph No. 1 of the Governing Decision dated 25th February, 2019 at para. 8. For the said scheme, the District Deputy Commissioner of Animal Husbandry shall be the Executive Officer and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the concerned district, except for the two districts of Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban.
