The Proposal:
As per the provisions of Section 12 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research has been empowered to grant permission to the universities to start any new faculty, course or discipline or department, as well as to start additional elective subjects for any course. Also, the Department of Agriculture and Forestry, Govt. Instructions have been issued under 30.05.2018 regarding reservation of seats in undergraduate, postgraduate and pre-university courses under agricultural universities, the procedure for allocation of social reservation, fixing the admission procedure and the procedure for granting recognition to private agricultural colleges.
