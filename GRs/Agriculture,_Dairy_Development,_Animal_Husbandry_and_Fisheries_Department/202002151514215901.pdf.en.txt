The proposal:
The State Government has decided to implement Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (Organic Farming) under National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture from 2015-16 with Central share of 60% and State share of 40%. At present, a three-year programme of 932 groups is being implemented. Further, in the 3rd Executive Committee meeting of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana held on 11th May, 2017 at New Delhi, the Central Government has approved 326 new clusters for the State of Maharashtra.
