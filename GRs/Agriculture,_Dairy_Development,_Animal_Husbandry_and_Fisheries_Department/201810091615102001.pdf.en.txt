The Proposal:
The delay in the actual implementation of the schemes is due to more time in giving technical approval at the level of Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune for the implementation of District Planning Board Scheme, State Level Scheme, Centrally Sponsored Scheme etc. in all the Zilla Parishads of the state.
The Rural Development Department has issued its Readings-1 and 2 respectively. 7.10.2017 and D. As per the GO dated 01.03.2018, the schemes for which the Zilla Parishad receives funds from the District Planning Board or other departments of the Government, such schemes should get the technical approval of the State Level Commissioner or Director of the concerned administrative department of the Government and where the posts of State Level Commissioner/ State Level Director are not functioning under the administrative department, the technical approval of the concerned administrative department is provided.
The decision was taken by the Rural Development Department. 07.10.2017 and D. As on 01.03.2018, the matter of conferring the powers of technical sanction conferred on the Commissioner Animal Husbandry to the subordinate authority of the Commissioner Animal Husbandry subject to certain financial limits was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
