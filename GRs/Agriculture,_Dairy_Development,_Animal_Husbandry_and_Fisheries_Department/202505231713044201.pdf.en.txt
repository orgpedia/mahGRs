the Government Order
With the approval of the competent authorities in accordance with the provisions of 4 (1), 4 (2) and 4 (3) of the Maharashtra Prevention of Delay in the Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Assistant Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Department of Animal Husbandry, Group-A is conducting periodic transfers of officers of the cadre for administrative reasons through counselling-2025, in front of their names in column No. He is being replaced at the mentioned places indicated in section 4. The transferable officers, namely, Dr. Raju Kolte and Dr. Sunil Pawar, are being given an extension of 1 year in the existing post on administrative grounds.
2. The officers transferred in the return shall immediately appear at the place indicated by the transfer as soon as such transfer order is received. The Head of the Office concerned shall relieve the transferred officers forthwith (within 7 days from the date of issue of the order) as there is no need to issue separate orders at the field level for discharge. The officers concerned shall forthwith submit their joining report along with the transfer certificate to the Government through the Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune without any delay in the prescribed manner after appearing for transfer.
3. The head of the office concerned shall be solely responsible if the head of the office concerned is not relieved within a minimum period of 7 days in order to enable the officer concerned to join the post of transferor. The Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Pune is informed to immediately submit a report to the government proposing disciplinary action against the head of the office.
The salary of the transferred officers for the next month (for the month of June) should be drawn from the transferred office only. The head of the office concerned will be liable for financial dishonesty if the salary for the month of June is drawn from the office before the transfer without relieving the transferred officer and will be liable for disciplinary action in this regard.

5. The transferred officers shall take care to join the place of transfer after superannuation within the prescribed period as per the rules in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Tenure of Incorporation) Rules, 1981. Those concerned should note that otherwise, their period of absence will count as a "non-deceasing day."
6. Leave shall not be granted on any ground except with the prior permission of the Government of the officer concerned. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers transferred by the original office shall be entertained. If such applications are received, they should be returned by post to the concerned authorities at the original address.
7. They were provided with an opportunity to choose the places of their choice while conducting the transfer proceedings by counselling regarding the proposed transfer. It may, therefore, be noted by the concerned that no statement regarding the revised posting / partial change in posting will be entertained by the transferred officers till the completion of the regular tenure. Also, it may be noted that if any attempt is made by the political AAA or other office bearers to pressurize the administration regarding the appointment of a post in the modified transfer, disciplinary action will be taken against the officer on the ground that he is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil GAT (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
8. The Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Pune is informed that if the officer concerned does not join the place of transfer within the stipulated period or the head of the office does not take action to relieve / depute him immediately, disciplinary action should be proposed against him / her and a report should be submitted to the Government immediately. Request to submit the action taken report to the mansing.patil@nic.in e-mail address.
9. This Government Order of the Government of Maharashtra. 11. 615. 1.405.11 is available on this website and has the index 202505231713044201. The order is being authenticated with a digital signature.
