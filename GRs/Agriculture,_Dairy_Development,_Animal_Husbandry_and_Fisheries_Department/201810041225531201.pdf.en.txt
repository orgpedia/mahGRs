Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was implemented in the State during Rabi season 2017-18 by two insurance companies namely National Insurance Company and Oriental Insurance Company. Under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) Rabi 2017-18, the above 2 companies together contributed Rs. 92,13,89, 796/- has been demanded out of which an amount of Rs. 9,10,00, 000 has already been paid. As per the Government decision dated 22.03.2018. At present, it is Rs. 83,03,89, 796 was under the consideration of the Government to be disbursed to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India to make it available to them.
Government Decision: The details of subsidy to be demanded and disbursed by the two designated insurance companies namely National Insurance Company and Oriental Insurance Company during the Rabi season 2017-18 are as under:
