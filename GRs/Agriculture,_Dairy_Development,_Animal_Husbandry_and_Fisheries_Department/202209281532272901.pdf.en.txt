In the State: Mirkarwada, T.G. Ratnagiri, Anandwadi, T. Deogarh, G. Sindhudurg, Karanja, Ta. The construction of ports at Uran, G. Raigad and Sasundok, Mumbai city and fish landing centres under NABARD and National Agriculture Development Scheme are in progress. In addition, construction of port and fish landing centre under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana and under FIDF are proposed. The government was considering setting up a committee to study the technical issues arising out of the construction and suggest measures to resolve the issues.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: By this Government Decision, a Technical Committee is being constituted as follows to take action on the problems arising in connection with the ports and fish landing centers of the above-mentioned states and to suggest measures to the Government.
