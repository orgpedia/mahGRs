Kiran Buvasaheb Udmale and Bharat Parashuram Kolekar were working on deputation as Child Development Project Officers under the Women and Child Development Department. The tenure of these officers has expired and they have not been given any extension. Accordingly, in pursuance of the request made by the said officers, the Commissionerate of Agriculture, along with the recommendation of the Civil Services Board, terminated their deputation as Child Development Project Officer under the Department of Women and Child Development and reinstated them as Agricultural Officers (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) cadre. 3 here. The proposal has been submitted vide letter dated 15.05.2023. Accordingly, with the approval of the competent authority, the services of the following officers have been transferred from the post of deputation to the Agriculture Department and they have been posted at Pillar No. The government is approving the posting as indicated in Section 3.
