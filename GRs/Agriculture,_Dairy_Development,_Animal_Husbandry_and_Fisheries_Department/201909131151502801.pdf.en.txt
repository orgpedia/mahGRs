There are 24 laboratories in the state, including 3 seed testing laboratories, 5 fertilizer control laboratories, 4 pesticide testing laboratories, 2 pesticide residue testing laboratories and 10 biological pest control laboratories. During the financial year 2019-20, a basic provision of Rs. 2428.68 lakh has been made in the head of accounts 24010305 (mandatory) for the following items: 01 salary, 02 wages, 03 overtime allowance, 06 telephone, electricity and water charges, 11 domestic travel expenses, 13 office expenses, 14 lease and taxes, 17 computer expenses, 21 supplies and materials, 24 petrol, oil and lubricants, 27 minor constructions, 28 professional services, 50 other expenses, 52 machinery and equipment etc.
Out of these, 17 were for computer cost, 21 for supplies and materials, 50 for other expenses and 52 for machinery and equipment. Accordingly, in order to meet the expenditure of the above laboratories, a sum of Rs. 283.00 lakh has been provided. However, subject to the terms and conditions of the Finance Department Government Circular No: Finance- 2019/ P.No.92/ Finance-3, dated 08.07.2019, a sum of Rs. 169.80 lakh was under the consideration of the Government.
