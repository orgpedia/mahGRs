Preamble: With a view to increase fish production in the ground water, marine and brackish water areas of the State, the Government of India has set a target to double the fish production by utilising the resources available in these three areas. Under this policy, it is proposed to increase fish production in collaboration with the state's fish farmers, fish farmers and fish entrepreneurs by using the natural resources of the state's land, water bodies, marine and salt water areas to the fullest potential, in a sustainable manner, maintaining bio-security and ecological balance. For this, the Government of India has set an objective to make fisheries a complementary and priority occupation and to double the income of the socially and economically weaker fisherfolk in the land, water and marine sectors.
Under the Neelkranti Scheme, the Central Government has approved 21 schemes under the Fisheries Department of the State Government for the year 2016-17 subject to the guidelines of the Central Government for the Neelkranti 2016 and its terms and conditions. 31013-13/2016-FY (3), Dy. the State vide order dated 10.8.2016
