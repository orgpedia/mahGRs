The Central Government has approved the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) to address the problems of unorganized food processing industries in the State. In this backdrop, the Prime Minister's Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Enterprises (PMFME), sponsored by the Central Government, will be implemented in the State for the next five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 23.12.2020.
The Central Government vide letter dated 21.04.2023 has approved the Project Implementation Plan for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMMFPI) for the years 2023-24 to 2024-25. Accordingly, the letter has requested for administrative approval of the plan amount of Rs. 41907.76 lakh to be implemented in the state in the year 2023-24.
Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval within the limits of the provision budgeted for the year 2023-24 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme for implementation in the State in 2023-24 with an outlay of Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for the project at an estimated cost of Rs. 41907.76 lakh.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 2023/ PR. No. 131/9-A3. After making available the funds under the scheme during the current year, the same shall be utilised for the first time for the disposal of pending cases.
4. Referred Government Decision. The scheme should be implemented as per the provisions prescribed under 23.12.2020 and guidelines for implementation of the scheme in the year 2023-24 should be issued from the level of Commissionerate of Agriculture.
For the implementation of the scheme in 2023-24, the nodal agency, Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFME) and Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune are being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
Under this scheme, the funds provided by the Central Government should be utilized immediately and the nodal agency for the allocation of funds, Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PMEME) and the Commissioner (Agriculture) should submit the utilization certificate to the Central and State Government from time to time.
The guidelines, prevailing terms and conditions and instructions issued by the Planning and Finance Department as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book and Financial Powers Rule Book should be strictly followed while spending the funds.
