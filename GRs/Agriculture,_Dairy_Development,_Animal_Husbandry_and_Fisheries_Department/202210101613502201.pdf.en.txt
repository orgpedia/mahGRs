The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 27.09.2022 was apprised of the various measures being taken to control the viral and communicable lumpy skin disease in the bovine livestock in the State. To take urgent measures to control the outbreak of lumpy skin disease in livestock in the state, the Cabinet has directed the Collectors of all the districts to provide an additional amount of Rs. 100.00 lakh should be made available immediately and Rs. 3/- instead of Rs. 5/- each as honorarium. The directions have been issued with retrospective effect from 04.08.2022. The government was considering issuing a government decision as per the directions given by the cabinet in the cabinet meeting.
Government's decision:
In view of the above facts, the Government is issuing the following orders:
2.1 For immediate control of viral and contagious lumpy skin disease in bovine animals in the state, preventive vaccination to control lumpy skin disease and necessary equipment for it from the funds available with the District Planning Committee in 2022-23, treatment of infected animals as per the specific instructions of the Central Government, purchase of medicines and equipment, vaccination to the service providers
