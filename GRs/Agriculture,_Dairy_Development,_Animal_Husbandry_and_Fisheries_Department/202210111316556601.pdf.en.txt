The Proposal:
The above reference No. In a communication dated 01.01.2020, the Department of Finance has requested all the Administrative Departments to take action for opening of separate head of accounts for interest on unclaimed amount of Central and State share in SNA accounts of Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented by their Departments, as per requirement for crediting (01) interest and (02) other deposits under interest on unclaimed balance or other deposits from 801 grantees under functional head/ sub-head belonging to their departments.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to open a new head of account for depositing the interest and other deposits on the amount of the unallocated State share in the SNA account after the financial year out of the funds received for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented under the Sub-Division of Fisheries in this Division.
Government's decision:
The State Government has approved the opening of a new Account Head as follows for crediting the interest and other deposits on the amount of State share lying unclaimed in the SNA account after the financial year out of the funds received for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Sub-Division of Fisheries in the Department.
