Government Circular: -
Under the General Administration Department. As per the provisions of the Government Notification dated 21/06/2021, the Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A cadre officers are entitled to be transferred. 1/1/2004 is being published according to the appendix attached to the temporary registry located.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) shall bring the matter to the notice of all concerned officers (including officers on leave / deputation). If any objection is raised by the concerned authorities with regard to the present situation, the concerned authorities should submit a representation to the Commissioner (Agriculture) within 8 days. If no objections are received during the said period, the submission shall be finalised expeditiously assuming that the authorities concerned have no objections to the said order. Submissions received thereafter will not be considered.
After examining the objections and suggestions received from the concerned authorities, the Agriculture Commissioner's Office should immediately submit a complete proposal for publication of the final report with self-explanatory feedback.
