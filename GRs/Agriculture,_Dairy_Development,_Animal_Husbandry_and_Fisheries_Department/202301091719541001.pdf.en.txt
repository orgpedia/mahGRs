Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 and Rabi 2022-23 As on 1.07.2022, the scheme is being implemented through 5 insurance companies viz., Agriculture Insurance Company of India, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd., Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company and United India Insurance Company. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought the release of remaining State share of subsidy on crop insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 in combination with the above 5 companies. The Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (5) In response to a request for Rs. 724,51,46, 809/- towards crop insurance premium was under consideration of the Government for disbursement to the remaining State share subsidy insurance companies.
Government's decision:
Considering the demand submitted by Agriculture Insurance Company of India, the Agriculture Insurance Company of India, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd., Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Co., Ltd.

Government Resolution No: Propivio-2022/ P. No. 93/11 A
The Government is approving the release of balance State share subsidy amounting to Rs. 724,51,46, 809/- towards crop insurance premium to 5 insurance companies namely HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company and United India Insurance Company. This amount is being released for Kharif 2022 and its utilization will not be allowed for other seasons earlier.
Expenditure on the said items should be met from the budgetary provision approved for the year 2022-23 under the following head: Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 110, Crop Insurance (00) (08) Financial assistance for insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana State share (2401 A 664) Non-plan, 33- Financial assistance.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for spending the funds disbursed under this government decision in accordance with the guidelines of the Prime Minister Crop Insurance Scheme and the government decision issued by the government from time to time.
For this purpose, Assistant Director (Accounts), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
