The Proposal:
The Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur has been established under the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences Act, 1998. The University also has constituent colleges and other institutions of Animal Husbandry, Dairy Technology and Fisheries Sciences. University and College Tribunals have not been established under the said Act for the officers/ staff of the University as well as the constituent colleges and other institutions functioning under the University. In the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences Act, 2002, the provisions of Grievance Redressal Committee for redressal of grievances of the said officers/ employees have been made in the said Act and the Appellate Authority in the said Act. However, the above provision has led to some administrative difficulties as well as court cases.
Provisions have been made for inclusion of officers/ staff of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences and constituent colleges and other institutions under the jurisdiction of the University and College Tribunal established under the provisions of Section 80 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.

In pursuance of these provisions, the Executive Council of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences vide Resolution No. 96 (2) (22) to take over the jurisdiction of the Tribunal established under Section 80 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and to apply Sections 79 to 85 of the said Act.
(3) In view of the above circumstances, the grievances/ problems etc. arising/ arising in respect of service matters of the officers/ staff of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences and its constituent colleges and institutions should be resolved. The government was considering invoking the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 to make a legal provision in this regard.
(4) In view of the above, the Government hereby approve the applicability of the following provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 to the teachers, teaching equivalents and non-teaching staff on the establishment of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences and its constituent colleges and other institutions: (1) The University and College Tribunal, Nagpur, constituted under the provisions of Section 80 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, is being included in the jurisdiction of the Tribunal.
(2) Further, since section 80 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 is being applied, the provisions of sections 79 to 85 consistent with this section are being applied to the Maharashtra Veterinary and Fisheries University and its constituent colleges as the case may be.
(3) The said provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 shall automatically repeal the provisions of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences Act, 2002 which are inconsistent with the aforesaid provisions of the said Act.
(4) All the above provisions shall apply to the cases which are pending as on date.
In accordance with the provisions of Section 80 (7) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the Department of Higher and Technical Education vide Order No. As per Clause 2021/556/ PR No. 117/ VISHI5, dated 02.12.2021, all expenditure towards remuneration, pension, provident fund, contribution, leave allowance and other allowances and facilities admissible to the Presiding Officer and the staff assisting him/ her shall be borne by the University/ College.
