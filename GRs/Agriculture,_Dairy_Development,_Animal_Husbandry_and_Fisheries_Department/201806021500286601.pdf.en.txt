Introduction: Bijgunan Kendra, Taluka Bijgunan Kendra, Gale Borgaon, Taluka Khultabad, District Aurangabad has been created in 1958. The total area of the Bijgunan Center is 20.83 hectares and on this area, seed production programs, vermicomposting, nimboli powder making, urea briquette, dasaparni extract, nimark making, as well as farmer training programs are implemented. The government was considering the use of land/ some area in Taluka Bijgunan Kendra, Galle Borgaon, Taluka Khultabad, District Aurangabad, to grant permission to Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agriculture University, Parbhani for the cultivation and research of modified maize crop.
The government has approved the use of land/ some area in Taluka Bijgunan Kendra, Galle Borgaon, Taluka Khultadabad, District Aurangabad, for the University Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani for the cultivation and research of modified seeds of the maize crop.

2. The Department of Agriculture should continue the programme of seed production and other programmes on the remaining area of the land of the Agriculture Department in Taluka Bijgunan Kendra, Galle Borgaon, Taluka Khultabad, District Aurangabad, after making some of the area available for the cultivation of the modified seed of maize crop. Manpower, machinery, etc. required for maize research should be provided to Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani by the Head of the Center, Taluka Bijgunan, Gale Borgaon, Taluka Khultadabad, District Aurangabad, and the District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Aurangabad. The maintenance cost for the cultivation/ research/ maintenance/ harvesting, etc. of the modified crop of maize should be borne by Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani itself or if the work is to be done using the manpower of the taluka Bijgunan area, the funds should be given to the Agriculture Department as per the government norms.
