Similarly, all the teachers and non-teaching officers/ employees who have been/ will be appointed in the service of Maharashtra Animal and Fisheries Sciences University, Nagpur, have been covered under this scheme as per the Government decision dated 07.01.2011 in Reference No. 03. In pursuance of this decision, the Contributory Pension Scheme will henceforth be referred to as" National Pension Scheme (NPS)" and all the teachers and non-teaching officers/ employees engaged/ going to be engaged in the service of Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur have been directed to transfer the amount.
