The scheme includes promotion of commercial organic farming and use of organic manure, certification through participatory guarantee system, preparation of organic manure on farmers' field, making available chemical pesticides free residue free farm produce to the consumers, development of organic farming villages and training and demonstration based on organic farming, increasing economic income by reducing the cost of production of farmers by adopting low cost technologies.
