Introduction: In the Hingoli district, Ma. Balasaheb Thackeray Haridra (Turmeric) Research and Training Centre' to be set up as an autonomous institution on a non-profit basis as per Section 8 of the Companies Act.
This has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 14.09.2022. For the year 2022-23, Rs. 1000.00 lakh has been approved through Supplementary Demands in the Winter Session of the Legislature. The matter was under consideration of the Government to disburse the said fund to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, State of Maharashtra, Pune. Following this, the government took decision on:
Government Decision: 1. Balasaheb Thackeray Haridra (Turmeric) Research and Training Centre will be implemented in the financial year 2022-23 at a cost of Rs. 1000.00 lakh (Rupees Ten Crores only) to Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, State of Maharashtra, Pune on Budget Distribution System.

The Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Agriculture), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for drawing and disbursing the amount disbursed by this Government.
4) Implement the plan. Action should be taken as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 14.09.2022.
5) The Controller shall, in the course of spending the funds so disbursed, take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. It should be ensured that the expenditure in this year will be as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy.
(6) The report of the expenditure incurred from the provision made, the account-wise/ sub-account-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, the utilization certificate of the fund should be sent to the Commissioner (Agriculture) and the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Ministry by the 10th of every month by the concerned field officer.
