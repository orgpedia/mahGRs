Preamble: During the year 2020-21, the State Government had received Central share of Rs. 27,00,00, 000/- (Rs. Twenty Seven Crore) for the second installment for general category under reference No.1 letter from the Central Government for implementation of National Horticulture Mission under Krishi Unnati Yojana- Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State.
Thereafter, vide letter No. 2, Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune requested for administrative approval of the programme amounting to Rs. 15879.11 lakhs and disbursement of funds. Accordingly, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3, revised administrative approval was accorded to the programme for an amount of Rs. 15879.11 lakh for implementation of the said scheme in the year 2020-21.
Finance Department disburses 40% of the approved amount in the Revised Estimates 2020-21

Accordingly, under the Government Resolution No. 4 under reference, it was approved to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 2143.90 lakh as Central share amounting to Rs. 1286.50 lakh+ corresponding State share amounting to Rs. 857.40 lakh sanctioned for the second instalment of general category.
With a view to taking measures to address the impact of COVID-19 on the economy in the financial year 2021-22, the Department has been directed to plan the schemes under the programme subject to the condition that 60% of the budgeted allocation for 2021-22 will be available to the Department. Under Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (KUADM) in the State, the Central Government has approved a new programme of Rs. 12422.43 lakh and a Spill Over/ Committed programme of Rs. 5391.69 lakh under the National Horticulture Mission (NHM) with an annual outlay of Rs. 17814.12 lakh. Accordingly, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No.7, administrative approval has been accorded for the implementation of the said scheme for the year 2021-22 with a total outlay of Rs. 17814.12 lakh.
The Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune vide letter No. 8 dated 08.09.2020, has released the balance Central share of Rs. 14.1350 crore and the corresponding amount to the State Government from the second instalment received from the Central Government during 2020-21.
A total amount of Rs. 23.5610 crore has been requested to be disbursed as share of Rs. 9.4260 crore. In this background, the Government was considering to distribute the remaining Central share and the corresponding State share of the funds received in the second installment in the year 2020-21 to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government: 1. The Central share of Rs. 27,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores only) received in the second instalment of general category in the year 2020-21 for the implementation of National Horticulture Mission in the State under Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission, the Government is approving the disbursement of the remaining Central share of Rs. 14,13,50, 000/- and corresponding State share of Rs. 9,42,60, 000/- aggregating to Rs. 23,56,10, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Three Crores Sixteen Lakhs Ten Thousand Only) to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune.
