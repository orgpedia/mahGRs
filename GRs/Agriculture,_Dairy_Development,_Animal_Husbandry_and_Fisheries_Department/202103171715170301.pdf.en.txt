GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FAROVA-2021/ PR No. 28/13-A
The expenditure from the funds budgeted for 2020-21 under the head Vegetable Saplings, Fruit Plants and Small Constructions (Mandatory) (2401 1722) is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) There is a difference between the rates of the materials mentioned in the budget and the rates of the materials in the respective horticulture mission. Action should be taken to ensure that the rates of budget materials are as per the market prices currently available.
2) If other factors are included in addition to the given criteria under the Integrated Horticulture Mission, it should be ensured and acted upon at the level of the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer Pune according to the local conditions.
3) Spend within the limit of the approved grant.
(iv) To comply with the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 (as amended on 17/4/2015). Also, the funds provided for the same should be spent as per the prevailing rules.
