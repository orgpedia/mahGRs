The Proposal:
Reference No. 1 Under the decision of the Government here, in order to handle the continuous drought-like situation in the state and to control the expenditure on salary, the posts of agricultural assistants will be increased from Rs. The government had decided to fill up the post of agricultural servant at a fixed salary (consolidated honorarium) of Rs. 2500/-. After that, the salary of regular agricultural assistants was increased as per the recommendations of the Sixth Pay Commission which was implemented in 2009. Since all the duties and responsibilities of regular agricultural assistants are performed by agricultural servants, Reference No. 3 here. As per the Government decision dated 19.03.2012, the fixed pay (consolidated remuneration) of Agricultural Servants is Rs. 2500/- to Rs. 6000/- has been increased. Since then, Rs. 6000/- is working at a fixed salary. Therefore, the Maharashtra State Agricultural Cooperative Union and agricultural servants are demanding an increase in the fixed salary (consolidated remuneration) of agricultural servants in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission.
