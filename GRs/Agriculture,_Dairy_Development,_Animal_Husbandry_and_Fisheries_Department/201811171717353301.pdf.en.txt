With respect to delegation of powers to approve reimbursement of medical expenses of subordinate officers/ employees of the Commissionerate of Agriculture.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No: AGRICULTURE- 2118/ PR No. 323/16-A
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: November 17, 2018.
Read:- Department of Public Health, Government Resolution, No. 2016/ No. 16/2106/ RAKAVI 2/ D. 16th March, 2016.
Preamble: Government servants and their family members will be entitled to reimbursement of medical expenses in respect of 27 cases of casualities and 5 cases of serious ailments in private hospitals, subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961. Commissioner (Agriculture) as Head of the Department has the power to approve reimbursement within the ceiling limit of Rs. 3 lakh. Proposals for reimbursement of medical expenses above this limit are submitted to the Department of Agriculture, Mantralaya, Mumbai for approval. At present, the number of payments received from the 8 Divisional Joint Director of Agriculture in the Agriculture Department to the Office of the Commissioner (Agriculture) has increased significantly. The number of officers/ employees in the agriculture department is more than 27 thousand. As the medical payments received from the Regional Office take a long time for approval, the concerned officers/ staff get delayed in getting the reimbursement amount. In this regard, Rs. Under consideration of the Government, it was decided to empower the Joint Director of Agriculture concerned to approve reimbursement of medical expenses up to a limit of Rs. 1 lakh. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government Decision: Rs. For sanction of reimbursement of medical expenses up to a limit of Rs.1 lakh, the Joint Director of Agriculture (Pay Scale Rs. 15,600-39, 100 plus grade pay of Rs. 7600) is being declared as Head of the Department. These rights shall be limited to the aforesaid financial matters only.
This decision will also be applicable to pending cases.
The Government hereby directs that while approving cases at the level of Head of Department as mentioned above, the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued thereafter from time to time should be strictly examined and approved.
