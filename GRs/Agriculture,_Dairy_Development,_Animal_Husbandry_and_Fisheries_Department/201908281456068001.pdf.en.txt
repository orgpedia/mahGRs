9. The funds to be disbursed under this Government decision are being disbursed as per the demand of the concerned implementing agencies. However, as directed by the Committee in the 27th State Level Project Sanctioning Committee meeting of the National Agriculture Development Scheme, it will be the responsibility of the concerned implementing agencies to utilise the funds being disbursed during 2019-20 for completion of old incomplete projects on priority. The concerned implementing agencies and administrative departments should ensure that there is no liability of pending payments due to lack of funds under any new or old project.
Under individual benefit projects, all departments/ implementing agencies shall disburse the subsidy through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of the beneficiaries.
