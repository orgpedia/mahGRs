Government's decision
The Financial Powers Rules, 1978 (Amendment). As per section 134 of the Act, the Administrative Department has the power to grant administrative (financial) approval for general repairs and special repairs of buildings not under the control of the Public Works Department. As per the powers vested in the administrative department, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 3,10,64, 643/- (Rupees Three Crore Ten Lakh Sixty Four Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Three). GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FAROVA-2021/ PR No. 28/13-O,
The expenditure out of the funds budgeted under the head 27- Small Constructions under the head Vegetable Roses, Fruit Trees and Local Gardens (Mandatory) (2401 1722) for the year 2020-21 is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) There is a difference between the rates of the materials mentioned in the budget and the rates of the materials in the respective horticulture mission. Action should be taken to ensure that the rates of budget materials are as per the market prices currently available.
2) If the factors other than the criteria given under the Integrated Horticulture Mission are not included, it should be ensured and acted upon at the level of the District Superintending Agriculture Officer Aurangabad according to the local conditions.
3) Spend within the limit of the approved grant.
(iv) To comply with the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 (as amended on 17/4/2015). Also, the funds provided for the same should be spent as per the prevailing rules.
