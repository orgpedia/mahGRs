Introduction: In the Hingoli district, Ma. Balasaheb Thackeray Haridra (Turmeric) Research and Training Centre to be set up as an autonomous institution on a not-for-profit basis as per Section 8 of the Companies Act. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 14.09.2022. The project is scheduled to be completed by 2023-24. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune was under consideration of the Government. Following this, the government took decision on:
Government Decision: 1. Balasaheb Thackeray Haridra (Turmeric) Research and Training Centre will be implemented in the financial year 2023-24 with an outlay of Rs. 1099.60 lakh (Rupees Ten Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) to Commissioner (Agriculture), State of Maharashtra, Pune on Budget Distribution System.
