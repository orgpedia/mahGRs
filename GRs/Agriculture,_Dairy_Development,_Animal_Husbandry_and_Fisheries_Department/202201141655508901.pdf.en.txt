The Proposal:
A meeting was held under the chairmanship of the Minister of State (Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development, Sports and Youth Welfare) regarding the transfer of category-1 veterinary clinics from the Zilla Parishad to the State Government and the transfer of all category-2 veterinary clinics to the Zilla Parishad. In the minutes of the meeting, it was directed that a committee should be formed under the chairmanship of Additional Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune to study the matter in detail and the proposal to form this committee should be submitted to the government by the Commissioner, Animal Husbandry.
In pursuance of this direction, the Commissioner, Animal Husbandry Office has proposed to constitute a committee under the chairmanship of Additional Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune to study the above and submit a report to the Government on the comparative study of veterinary institutions under the control of the Zilla Parishad and the Government and what will be the effective mechanism for providing effective services in the future.
Government's decision:
The government is approving the formation of a committee as follows to conduct a comparative study of veterinary institutions under the control of the district council and the government and submit a report to the government on what will be effective in providing effective services in the future:
