Preamble: Centrally Sponsored Sub-Abhiyaan on Agricultural Mechanization is being implemented in the State. There are a total of eight components under this sub-mission. 1 and 2 are about demonstration, training and testing of plants developed with new technology. Component No. For 1 and 2, the central share is 100%. For the remaining 6 components, the ratio of Central and State funding is 60:40.
