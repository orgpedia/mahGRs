the order
In accordance with the provisions of Section 12 (2) (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, this Department vide Order No. Makrupa 1021/ P.No. 63/6-AD. As of May 18, 2021, Shri Prakash Anandrao Abitkar, Member of Legislative Assembly, has been appointed as the Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research by the Government.
The status of Cabinet Minister has been conferred by this Order with effect from the date of this Order during the tenure of Shri Prakash Anandrao Abitkar, Member of Legislative Assembly as Vice-Chairman, Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune. Mr. Prakash Anandrao Abitkar has been appointed as Secretary, Department of Finance, Government Resolution No. S.A. S.- 10.10/ P.S. No. 96/10/ C.E.
The service facilities as on 13.03.2012 will continue to be permitted.
