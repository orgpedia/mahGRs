As per the letter dated 18/11/2021, the Central Government has approved the reservation of 5 seats (Veterinary College, Mumbai-2, Shirval-1, Parbhani-2) in Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences for the students of the State of Goa from the academic year 2021-22. The Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences (MUFMS). 06/12/2021, recommended to the Government to submit a proposal for reserving 5 seats (Veterinary College, Mumbai-II, Shirval-I, Parbhani-II) in the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences for the students of the State of Goa from the academic year 2021-22. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to increase the reserved seats for veterinary degree courses for candidates from the state of Goa and reserve a maximum of 11 seats in addition to the approved intake capacity.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the Government Decision dated 11th July, 2019 of the Department of Agriculture and Padum, at para no. In place of the provision in 7A (vii), the following provision is being prescribed:" A maximum of 11 seats (College Mumbai-5, Shirval-4, Parbhani-2) shall be reserved for candidates from the State of Goa in addition to the sanctioned intake capacity for the Bachelor of Veterinary Courses in the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences. The decision will come into effect subject to the approval of the executive council of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences.
The decision will be effective from the academic year 2021-22.
