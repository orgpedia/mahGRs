Letter of the Commissioner of Agriculture No. Kriya 1111/ SAPR/ 0261/20-21/ PR No.-5/ Aastha-5 (NIP)/ 6666, Dy. 15 February 2020 Preamble: In the year 2019-20, a total of 248 temporary posts under the schemes in the Department of Agriculture were created by the Government Decision No. Tabike 2019/ PR No. 58/13 A, Decree No. It was approved to continue till 29th February, 2020 with effect from 4th October 2019.
The Department of Finance, vide Government Resolution No. Designation-2016/ P.S. 8/16/ G.P.C. dated 12th February, 2020, has been empowered to extend the period up to 31.8.2020 for the temporary posts included in their rolls of the Administrative Departments as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier Review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the continuation of these temporary posts from 1/3/2020 to 31/8/2020 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner of Agriculture, No. On the request of the letter here, 248 temporary posts in the scheme of Taluka Bijgunan Region (2401 0261), (excluding the posts lying vacant for more than 6 months), the following temporary posts are being approved to continue from 1/3/2020 to 31/8/2020.
