No. 150/ 3J., D. 16.8.2014 2) Department of Agriculture and Padum, Government Resolution No. 21.8.2018 3) Director (Extension& Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, State of Maharashtra vide letter No. Worms/ WIPR 4/ S.D./ 201/2019, Dy. 3.5.2019 Preamble: Padma Shri Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil should be remembered for his work for the farmers and as a mark of respect to all the farmers of the state, the government was considering to celebrate his birthday on the day of Coconut Pournami as Farmers' Day.
Government Decision: In memory of Padma Shri Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil, the Government Decision was taken by the Department of Agriculture No. Shedija-2012/ S.No.
150/3 O, D. As on 16.8.2014. It was approved to celebrate 29th August as" Farmers Day." Since then, this day is celebrated as Farmers' Day.
And now in 2019. The government has decided to celebrate 14th August 2019 as Farmers' Day. This event should be celebrated on a small scale. Technical guidance and assistance should be provided to farmers through Gram Sabhas and other programmes.
No additional funding will be provided for this program. The minimum expenditure for this programme should be met from the provisions under the Commissioner, Agriculture.
3. The Commissionerate of Agriculture should issue guidelines regarding the celebration of Farmers' Day.
