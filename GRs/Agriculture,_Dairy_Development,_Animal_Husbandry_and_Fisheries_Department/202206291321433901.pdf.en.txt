Preamble: For selection of private veterinarians for conducting pre-slaughter inspection of scheduled animals for APEDA authorized slaughterhouses, a selection committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Vice-Chancellor, Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur for selection of private veterinarians as per Government decision dated 14.7.2020. Further, vide Government Order dated 22.10.2021, in place of Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry Headquarters, Regional Joint Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Pune Division, Pune has been appointed as Member Secretary of the said Committee and Registrar, Maharashtra State Veterinary Council, Nagpur has been appointed as Member.
2. Now, the Chairman of the said Committee, Vice-Chancellor, Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur vide letter dated 5.5.2022 is finding it difficult to continue as Chairman of the said Committee due to his superannuation. Hence, he has been asked to resign from the post of chairman.
Therefore, the Government was considering to amend the existing selection procedure and to amend the committee constituted for selection of private veterinarians for conducting pre-slaughter inspection of scheduled animals for APEDA authorized slaughter houses.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 4. For selection of private veterinarians for conducting pre-slaughter inspection of scheduled animals for APEDA authorized slaughter houses, read No. 1 dated 14.7.2020 and Read No. 2 The Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 22.10.2021 has been abolished and a revised Selection Committee is being constituted as follows: (i) Principal Secretary (Padum) Chairman (ii) Additional Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune Member (iii) Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry (HQ) Member (iv) Registrar, Maharashtra State Veterinary Council, Nagpur Member (v) Regional Joint Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Pune Division, Pune Member Secretary

The procedure for selection of private veterinarians for conducting pre-slaughter inspection of scheduled animals for APEDA authorized slaughterhouses should be carried out as under: (1) There are presently 9 APEDA authorized slaughterhouses in the State.
(2) Advertisement should be made by the Member Secretary of the selection committee regarding the appointment of private veterinarians for 11 months for these 9 slaughterhouses.
(3) The selection committee shall interview the candidates received in accordance with this advertisement.
(4) The documents of the qualified candidates should be examined by the Registrar, Maharashtra State Veterinary Council, Nagpur.
(5) A list of candidates who are eligible and whose document verification report has been received favourably should be prepared.
(6) The company (slaughterhouse) which requires the appointment of private veterinarians shall make a requisition to the Member Secretary of the selection committee.
(7) On receipt of such requisition from the company, the member secretary of the selection committee shall intimate the names of the candidates selected by him to the concerned company in the ratio 1:3.
(8) The consent of the candidates obtained from the member secretary of the selection committee shall be processed by the company concerned.
(9) After receiving the consent of the candidate, the concerned company shall inform the Member Secretary of the selection committee before making his appointment.
(10) After receiving the letter from the company regarding the appointment of the candidate, the proposal should be submitted for the approval of the government regarding the appointment of the concerned veterinarian for 11 months.
(11) If after the completion of 11 months, the same veterinarian is to be appointed in the same slaughterhouse or other APEDA authorized slaughterhouse, the proposal for his appointment shall be submitted for the approval of the Government without re-interviewing him and without examining the document, with his prior consent.
