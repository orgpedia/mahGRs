Government Resolution No: Matsyavi-1023/ PR No. 228/ Padum-14
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: August 3, 2023
Introduction: The comprehensive policy of the Department of Fisheries does not exist in totality. Like textile policy of textile department and industrial policy of industries department. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of the following committees to determine the fisheries development policy in the landlocked and marine districts.
Government Decision: The following committee is proposed to determine the fisheries development policy for the land, marine and coastal districts.

1. Drafting of schemes after studying the schemes of different states. (Saline, Saline and Ground Water) 2. Preparation of separate plans for fisheries development after studying the district level/ state level plans.
