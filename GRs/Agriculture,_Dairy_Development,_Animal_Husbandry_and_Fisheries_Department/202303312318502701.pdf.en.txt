The Proposal:
Budget 2022-23 The Prime Minister and The Minister (Finance) said," As Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agriculture University, Parbhani and Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli are completing 50 years, they will be given Rs. A special grant/ fund of Rs. 50.00 crore will be provided. Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-23, under the head 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) to Agricultural Universities for Research expenditure under the programme, an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 25.00 crore will be provided for research. In this regard, the agricultural universities of the state were informed to submit research proposals. Accordingly, Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri, has allocated a total of Rs. Proposals worth Rs. 2815.50 lakh have been received by the Government through Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the proposals for research and to disburse funds for research in the financial year 2022-23.
Government's decision:
To speed up the research work in the agricultural universities of the state, a total of 13 research proposals of Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri, worth Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 2815.50 lakhs.
