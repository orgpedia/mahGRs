The Proposal:
Under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- More Crop Component Per Drop is being implemented in the state. Under this component, two aspects of micro-irrigation and water management are implemented. The ratio between the Centre and the state is 60:40.
The central government. As per the letter dated 20th August, 2019, for implementation of Micro Irrigation under Per Drop More Crop component, an amount of Rs. 174 crore. Out of this, Rs. 135.72 crore for Scheduled Castes and Rs. 20.88 crore and Rs. 17.40 crore.
The Central Government has allotted Rs. 20.88 crore and Rs. 13.92 crore. A proposal to disburse Rs. 34.80 crore to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Financial Management System was under consideration of the Government. The following decisions have been taken:
Government's decision:
