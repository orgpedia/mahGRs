Preamble: For the financial year 2022-2023, as per the instructions in the circular No. 1 and the semi-government letter in the context of the Finance Department, all the four agricultural universities in the state have been given the demand No. 1 status. Under the head' D-3, 2415 Agricultural Research and Education', funds have been disbursed on the BIMS system for the purpose of 31-Assistant Grants (Non-salaried) for the mandatory schemes with a ceiling of 21%.
Accordingly, the Controller, Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani has issued the above Reference No. A proposal has been submitted for disbursement of funds from the approved budgetary allocation for the mandatory schemes for Parbhani Agricultural University under letters 2 and 3 to 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary). The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government Decisions In the financial year 2022-23, for Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani, 2415-Agriculture Research and Education, 01-Crop Cultivation (2415 0052), 03-Animal Husbandry (2415 0203) (Compulsory), 31-Subsidized Grants (Non-salaried), 21% of the approved budgetary provision is as under: The Government is sanctioning a grant of Rs. 159,12,000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty-Nine Lakh Twelve Thousand only). 2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly implemented.
The funds allocated by the Agricultural Universities under the scheme/ non-scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the permissible items in the respective mandatory scheme.
(3) If the amount disbursed remains unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for any other group/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
4. Schemes under which the period of the scheme/ non-scheme has expired.
The expenditure under the schemes should not be continued without the approval of the government after the expiry date.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula, and how the expenditure is being incurred as per the prescribed orders/ procedures. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.

The government has approved the agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
The above terms and conditions shall be strictly complied with by the Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities.
The expenditure for this purpose should be shared within the limits of the monthly fund statement from the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts mentioned in the Budget 2022-23 and should be spent under the same head of accounts.
The Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, Parbhani, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall take necessary action regarding the payment of grants sanctioned under the respective sub-headings to the concerned Controller of Agricultural Universities as mentioned in the Government Decision No. Agriculture and Cooperation Department No. Ajiu 1072/18471/ V, dated 27th September, 1972 and Government Decision No. KrishiVi-2003/ CR. 35/20-O, dated 25/4/2003.
