Introduction: Former Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Shri. Vasantrao Naik's birth anniversary is celebrated as the birth anniversary of the great leader of the Indian agriculture. The government has decided to celebrate July 1 as Agriculture Day in the state. The decision was taken on June 21, 1989.
Government's decision:
July 1 is the birthday of former Maharashtra Chief Minister. The government has decided to celebrate Vasantrao Naik's birth anniversary as Agriculture Day.
2. d. The government is giving approval to celebrate July 1, 2022 as Agriculture Day in the state. A maximum of Rs. 10,000/- (Rs. 10,000 only) for 355 Panchayat Samitis Rs. 35,50,000/- (Rs. 50 lakh) and Rs. 20,000/- (Rs. 20 thousand only), for a total of 34 Zilla Parishads Rs. 6,80,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh Eighty Thousand only). 42,30,000/- (Rupees Forty Two Lakh Thirty Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) Maharashtra State, Pune should give guidelines to the concerned regarding the celebration of Agriculture Day. The expenditure should be based on the guidelines given by the Agriculture Commissioner. Also, expenses should be spent on screening of films, tea for farmers, small exhibitions at the district council level, etc. and for necessary things like mandaps, harbours, photos, etc.

4. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the grants available from the Zilla Parishad Cess Fund. To meet this expenditure, the Chief Executive Officers and Agriculture Officers of all the districts should take action as per the guidelines given by the Commissioner (Agriculture) for spending from the District Cess Fund. The Commissioner (Agriculture), Maharashtra State, Pune will be responsible for the implementation of this programme.
During the Kharif 2022 sowing season, the field staff/ officials of the state agriculture department, scientists of agricultural universities and extension agencies should guide the farmers by visiting the farmers' bunds. However, all the Agricultural Universities/ Agri-Departmental Mechanisms should prepare, implement and coordinate the action plan in this regard.
In the wake of Covid-19. Agriculture Day will be celebrated in the state on 1st July 2022.
