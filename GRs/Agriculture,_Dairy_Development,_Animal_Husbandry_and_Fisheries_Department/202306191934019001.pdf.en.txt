Introduction: With the objectives of promoting the food processing industry in the state, encouraging the establishment of projects based on modern technology through the participation of farmers to increase the value of agricultural products, encouraging the modernization of the project to increase the quality of the food produced, saving energy, the 100% state-sponsored scheme of Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Scheme was launched in the state for the next five years from the financial year 2017-18 under the decision of the No. 1 government. Thereafter, as per the Government Resolution No. 3, the scheme has been approved to be continued for the next five years from the financial year 2022-23. For the implementation of this scheme in the year 2023-24, the government has decided to provide Rs. Administrative approval has been accorded for the programme for an amount of Rs. 100.00 crore. Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune has requested for release of funds to the tune of Rs. 49.57 crore for the projects sanctioned under the said scheme. The Department of Finance vide Circular No.4 above has delegated powers to the Department to disburse funds within the limit of 20% of the total provision for the quarter April to June, 2023. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 10.00 crore (Rs.10 crore only) made available to the Department within the limit of 20% of the total provision was under consideration of the Government, the Government is now taking a decision as follows.
