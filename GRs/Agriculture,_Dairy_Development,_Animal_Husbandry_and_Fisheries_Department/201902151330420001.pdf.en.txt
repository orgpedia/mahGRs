The Proposal:
The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme to provide assured income to farmers. The scheme will be implemented in the state as per the norms prescribed by the central government and as per the directions received from the central government from time to time in this regard. The approval was accorded in the meeting held on 04.02.2019. The government was expected to take a decision in this regard.
Government Decision 1) The 100 percent centrally sponsored Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme launched by the Central Government to increase the income of small and marginal farmers has been implemented in the state. The approval is being granted with effect from 01.12.2018.
