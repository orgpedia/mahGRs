The Proposal:
It is proposed to organize a national level animal and bird show at Jalna from 2nd to 4th February, 2019. For this exhibition, it is proposed to set up stalls of the best cattle and birds of the state and outside the state, as well as the necessary machines and equipment for their scientific rearing, medicinal and products related to animal husbandry, and to honor the cattle farmers by selecting the best cattle and birds of the state.
Through the exhibition, experts will guide the cattle rearers on topics such as importance of indigenous cow conservation, genetic improvement of indigenous cow breeds, providing self-employment opportunities to educated unemployed people through animal husbandry and thereby strengthening the rural economy, promoting milk, meat and egg production, reducing the cost of production by increasing the production capacity of animals, use of sex sorted semen, promotion of fodder production, Murgas/ Hydroponics/ Azolla technology, which will help in doubling the income of cattle rearers.
