The starred question No. 13773-" Sale of fish by migrants in Navi Mumbai without license." In response to this question, The Minister has given a statement in the House that a committee of the Commissioner and all the concerned honourable members will be formed in this regard. Accordingly, Ma. With the approval of the Minister (Fisheries), a total of 12 member committee has been approved to be formed under the chairmanship of Commissioner (Fisheries) as per the government decision here. Now, the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat has sent the names of a total of 13 MLCs for this committee through a letter from Vacha-2.
Out of the total 13 MLCs sent by the Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, the names of 10 MLCs were included in the Read- 1 decision of the government here.
GOVERNMENT SUPPORT: The Government approves the inclusion of the following 3 MLCs in the Committee constituted under the Government Decision Read- 1 regarding prevention of illegal migrants selling fish in Navi Mumbai area.
