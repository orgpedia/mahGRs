State Plan Managers: State Plan Managers shall constitute a First Level Agency at the State level for implementation of the scheme and appoint/ modify as per requirement an Under Secretary/ Cell Officer subordinate to him as its Administrator and shall deal with the administrative/ technical aspects related to the scheme on PFMS system.
First Level Agency:- The administrator of the First Level Agency will be responsible for creating the Second Level Agency/ making changes as required. The Director (Education), Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune will be the competent authority to implement the scheme as the Second Level Agency.
Maker and Checker:- Joint Director (Finance) Agriculture Council, Pune will be the maker and Assistant Director (Accounts-1) Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune will be the checker for the implementation of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship and Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance through Public Financial Management System under Agriculture University.
