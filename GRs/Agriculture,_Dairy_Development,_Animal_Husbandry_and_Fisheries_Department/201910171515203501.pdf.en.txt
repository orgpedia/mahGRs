With a view to impart training in modern techniques to the concerned officers/ staff of agriculture and allied departments in view of the importance of the agricultural development program in the state, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur and 7 affiliated divisional agricultural training institutes of the said institute have been established in Pune, Kolhapur, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nashik, Nagpur and Khopoli. The programme of strengthening of these institutions was being implemented under the 100 centrally sponsored scheme of" Strengthening of Agricultural Training Institutes" as per the Action Plan till 2012-13. The government has stopped the implementation of the scheme since 2013-14. The programme is being implemented under the National Agriculture Development Scheme (NADP) since 2013-14. National Agriculture Development Project. On the 4th July 2019. The meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary decided to implement the training and capacity building programme of officers, field staff and members of the Aatma Samiti at Wanamati and Rameti. A sum of Rs. 749.00 lakh was sanctioned. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the programme was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 1 To implement the capacity building programme of training of officers, field staff and members of Atma Samiti at Vanamati and Rameti in 2019-20. 749.00 Lakhs (Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the programme for an amount of Rs. 7 crore only subject to the following terms and conditions.

2 The Director, ATMA shall issue necessary instructions to the concerned authorities for conducting various training programmes in accordance with the guidelines, guidelines and prevailing rules and conditions of the Government as provided under the said scheme.
3 The training programme will include training of officers at forestry level and members of the Spirit Regulating Board (GB) (officers and farmers representatives).
The training programme will include training of field officers and staff, newly appointed Krishi Sevaks, Atma District Farmers Advisory Committee (DFAC) members and Atma Taluk Farmers Advisory Committee (BFAC) members.
4 If it is not possible to implement the training program of the Farmers Advisory Committee Members (BFAC) of Atma Taluka at the Rameeti level, the program of such training should be implemented through the concerned Rameeti at the District Training Center (DTC), Agricultural Clinics, etc.
5 The programme of various trainings as above should be implemented by the Director, Vanamati in coordination with all the concerned Principals, Ramati.
The training of farmers in the respective areas of Konkan should be done in coordination with Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli.
7 The Director, ATMA shall ensure that the directions issued by the Director, Vanamati, Nagpur regarding the training calendar for the year 2019-20 are complied with.
8 The calendar of all these trainings should be prepared along with the training module and the process of getting it approved by the Commissionerate of Agriculture should be done by the Director, Vanamati.
9 All the concerned agencies should ensure that the funds under the National Agriculture Development Scheme are not wasted in 2019-20.
At the end of July 10, the amount used for training programs under this scheme from the accumulated funds of Vanamati, Nagpur and Rameti institutions was Rs. 66.54 lakh should be adjusted from the funds to be distributed under this order on a priority basis and the report of the funds allocated to Vanamati, Nagpur and Rameti institutions should be submitted to the government immediately. Also, all concerned should take care that in future, the accumulated funds will not be used for other purposes without the approval of the government.
11 A detailed report of the training programme conducted institution-wise/ month-wise in 2019-20 under the scheme should be submitted along with the information of expenditure incurred on it and no further programme under the scheme should be implemented until further orders of the Government in the subsequent period. In this regard, all concerned should note that the violation of the government order will be taken seriously. 12 The Director, Atma should submit utility certificates of the expenditure incurred in implementing the scheme to the government from time to time.
