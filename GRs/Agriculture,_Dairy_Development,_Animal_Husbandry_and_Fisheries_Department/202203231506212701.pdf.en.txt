2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The funds allocated by the Agricultural Universities under the scheme/ non-scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the permissible items in the respective mandatory scheme.
(2) If the amount disbursed is lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the amount so unutilized/ unutilized shall not be diverted or spent for any other group/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(3) The schemes/ schemes which have lapsed, shall not be continued beyond the period specified in the scheme.
(4) The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the financial year concerned, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula, and how the expenditure is being made in accordance with the prescribed order/ procedure. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
5) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income.
The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the universities are governed by the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991.
