Introduction: With the objectives of promoting the food processing industry in the state, encouraging the establishment of projects based on modern technology through the participation of farmers to increase the value of agricultural products, encouraging the modernization of the project to increase the quality of the food produced, saving energy, the 100% state-sponsored scheme of Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Scheme was launched in the state for the next five years from the financial year 2017-18 under the decision of the No. 1 government. Thereafter, in pursuance of the 5 th Government decision, the Scheme has been approved to be continued for the next 5 years from the financial year 2022-23. Administrative sanction for implementation of the scheme for the year 2022-23 has been accorded for an amount of Rs. 11500 lakh, out of which Rs. 345.91 lakh has been disbursed.
