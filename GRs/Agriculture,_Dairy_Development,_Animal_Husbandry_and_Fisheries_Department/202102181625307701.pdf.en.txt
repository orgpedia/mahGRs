Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published on the website of the Central Government www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability Notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of this decision, the government was considering to ensure the posts of Group-A to Group-D category in the establishment of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation, Akola under the Department of Agriculture and Padum for the disabled category.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for PwDs as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published.
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that the posts in Group' A" to Group' D," which have been fixed by the Central Government, will be ensured for the disabled in the state government service, even if the posts in the list published by the Central Government are different in terms of duties and responsibilities, nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service.
