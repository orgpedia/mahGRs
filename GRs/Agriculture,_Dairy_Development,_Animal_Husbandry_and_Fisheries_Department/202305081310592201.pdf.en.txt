The Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers Cadre) Act. The cadre-wise reservation policy has been implemented in direct service recruitment for teaching posts in Central Universities and Central Educational Institutions under the National Education Policy, 2019. As per the existing reservation policy in direct service recruitment in the teaching cadre in the educational institutions of the state, subject-wise reservation is applicable in the respective faculty cadre. Subject wise reservation is applicable to all reserved categories due to small size of subject wise cadre however, due to small size of subject wise cadre all reserved categories are not adequately represented in the faculty cadre. Also in some cases where there is a single subject-wise post, reservation is not applicable.
In this background, the cadre-wise reservation policy prescribed by the Central Government, the prevailing reservation policy of the State Government as well as the direct recruitment of teachers in colleges under the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Department of Medical Education, Department of School Education, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance will be included in the recruitment. A Cabinet sub-committee was formed to study the court's judgment and recommend to the government to implement the cadre-wise reservation policy prescribed by the central government in educational institutions in the state. The meeting of the sub-committee. It was held on 24.12.2021. In the meeting, it was decided to apply the present cadre-wise reservation to the educational institutions of the state. as well

The bill was introduced in the 2021 winter session of the state legislature. Also, the law It has been implemented in the State since 01.04.2022. Accordingly, the Government has notified the cadre-wise reservation policy in direct service recruitment for the posts of teachers in agricultural universities and affiliated colleges/ institutions in the State. It has come into force with effect from 16.02.2023. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines on the implementation of this reservation policy was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the implementation of the Cadre-wise reservation policy in direct service recruitment of teaching posts in Agricultural Universities and affiliated colleges/ institutions in the State, the following guidelines are being issued: a) Consideration of Cadre-wise reservation: 1) Government Notification No. As per the provisions prescribed in 16.02.2023, the sanctioned posts in all disciplines in the faculty cadre (of same pay scale) in each University as well as in all colleges under the control of the same management should be consolidated.
(2) Cadre-wise reservation shall be determined in accordance with the percentage prescribed in the prevailing reservation policy taking into account the total number of sanctioned posts in the cadre. Also, action should be taken as per the existing government orders regarding parallel reservation.
3) Considering the total sanctioned posts and functional posts in the cadre, the point list of the cadre should be kept in the prescribed format along with the list of posts. The Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers' Cadre) Act, 2021, provides for reservation of teachers in the educational institutions of the state. As per the provision of 18.10.1997, category-wise reservation of vacancies should be fixed and the approval of the competent authority shall be mandatory for the said list of points.
(b) On the implementation of the recruitment process in accordance with the cadre-wise reservation policy: 1) When the category-wise reservation of vacancies as above is determined and the point list is finalized, an advertisement should be published in accordance with the prevailing procedure of the government to fill the vacancies.
2) The advertisement should indicate the category-wise number of vacancies to be filled.
3) The advertisement should also indicate the total number of vacancies to be filled in the subject.
4) All the eligible candidates can apply for the posts according to the categories mentioned in the advertisement.
