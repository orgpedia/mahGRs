Preamble: Implementation of State Sponsored Scheme of Agricultural Mechanization. As a result of the Government Decision No. 1, administrative approval has been granted. Under this scheme, component number-3 of the Central Government's Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission, providing subsidy to individual beneficiary farmers for the purchase of agricultural implements/ machines and component number-4, providing subsidy to agricultural implements/ machines banks are implemented as per the guidelines of the Central Government's Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission.
Implementation of State Sponsored Scheme of Agricultural Mechanization Sub Mission on Maha DBT Portal disburses subsidy to the beneficiaries who are successful in the draw of lots and have completed the works under the scheme.
For the year 2023-24, Rs. Rs 300 crore has been allocated for this.
