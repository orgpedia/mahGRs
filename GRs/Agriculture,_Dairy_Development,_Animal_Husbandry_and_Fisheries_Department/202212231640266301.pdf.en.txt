As per the Government decision dated 11.10.2018, the cadre of Agriculture Officer, Group-C (Technical) in the Zila Parishad has been converted into a new cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) in the Department of Agriculture, Government of Karnataka. The Department of Agriculture has issued guidelines regarding the procedure of inclusion of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) in the Agriculture Department of the State Government. 2 here. issued pursuant to the Government Decision of January 9, 2019. Accordingly, 381 officers of the Agriculture Officer, Group-C (Technical) Cadre of the District Council, who gave the option to join the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zilla Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted), were transferred. As per the Government decision dated 11th January, 2021, 19 officers have been transferred. As per the Government decision dated 13 th May, 2022, the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries has been included in the State Government.

As per the provisions of the Government Decision of 11 January 2021, all the service matters of the newly established cadre officers will henceforth be handled by the Agriculture and Padum Department of the State Government. Accordingly, the Controlling Officer and Regional Head of the Department, Confidential Reporting Officer, Reviewing Officer and Conservator and Drawing and Disbursing Officer will be responsible for handling the service matters of the officers of the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted). The government decision of January 17, 2022 has been announced. However, the salaries of the officers of this cadre, who have been absorbed by the State Government, are still being paid by the Panchayat Raj Service System of the Zila Parishads under the head of Accounts No. 24010163 (compulsory) and 2401 2442 (programme). Since the salary of the officers of the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) is not being paid from the state government service system, there is a problem in providing salary and deduction of provident fund if these officers are transferred to other Zila Parishads at the field level.
Also, there are difficulties in providing pensionary benefits to retired officers. Therefore, the Government has decided to include these officers in the service of the State Government and provide their salary from the head of account 2401 0154 (mandatory) as follows.
Government's decision:
In reference to the above. Out of 400 officers of the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zilla Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) who were inducted in the Agriculture Department of the State Government under the Government Decision dated 11.01.2021 and dated 13.05.2022, the salary of 372 officers currently working in the Department of Agriculture has been increased from the financial year 2023-24. D-3, Account No. Approval is being given to provide from 2401 0154.
The total number of 134 officers of Group-C (Technical) cadre, who have not been given option for induction by the State Government, shall be paid in the same head of accounts under which the said officers are presently drawing salary (59 posts under head of accounts 2401 2442 and 75 posts under head of accounts 2401 0163). Also, the cadre of Agriculture Officer, Group-C (Technical) will henceforth be" expired posts" (dead cadre) and the posts falling vacant in that cadre will be included in the Agriculture Department of the State Government. The posts of Agriculture Officer, Group-C (Technical) and Agriculture Officer (Zilla Parishad), Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) are vacant.
