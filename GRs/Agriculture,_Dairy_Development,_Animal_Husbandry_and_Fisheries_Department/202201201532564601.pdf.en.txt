Introduction: Forestry can help in mitigating climate change to some extent by conserving natural resources, creating new sources of livelihood and increasing production. For this, the Central Government is running a sub-campaign on forestry under the National Sustainable Agriculture Mission with the aim of increasing tree plantation along with agricultural crops.
Reference No. The Government of India has approved the implementation of the centrally sponsored National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture- Vanasheti Sub Mission.
