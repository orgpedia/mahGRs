Preamble: The Government vide No. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Secretary (Padum) to decide the prices of milk to be bought and sold under the Government Milk Scheme. Reference No. 4 In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Commissioner Dairy under the letter here, the meeting of the said Committee was held. It was held on 11.11.2021. The meeting discussed the proposal to revise the sale price of' Aarey Shakti' cow milk and' Aarey Bhushan Tond' milk, revise the sale price of cow toned milk (3.5/8.5) and full cream milk (6.0/9.0) and revise the commission rate of milk given to the distributors under the government milk scheme.
As per the decision taken in the meeting, the government was considering to revise the sale price of" Aarey Shakti" cow milk and" Aarey Bhushan Tond" milk, revise the sale price of cow tond milk (3.5/8.5) and full cream milk (6.0/9.0) and revise the milk commission rate given to the distributors under the government milk scheme.
Government Decision: Under this decision, the Government approves to revise the sale price of' Aarey Bhushan" toned milk,' Cow Toned Milk,"' Aarey Shakti" cow milk and full cream milk sold through the Government Milk Scheme as follows. It will come into effect from 01/12/2021.
