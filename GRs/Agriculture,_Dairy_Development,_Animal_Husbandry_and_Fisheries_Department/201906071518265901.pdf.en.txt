Introduction: Department of General Administration. As per the notification dated 28.04.2015, a total of 18 officers of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-B Cadre have been transferred and posted in the Revenue Department. The appointment was made as per the Government Order dated 08.03.2019. There were no vacancies in Wardha and 3 officers namely Mr. Jayant Shankarrao Gaikwad, District Soil Survey and Soil Testing Officer, Buldhana were shown as posted. Therefore, the Commissioner (Agriculture) should not be relieved from his present post. This was communicated vide letter dated 18.03.2019. Subsequently, the Commissioner (Agriculture) had submitted a proposal to the Government for the posting of the said 3 officers by holding a meeting of the Civil Services Board in a circular manner with the recommendation of the revised posting by making partial changes in the postings. The proposal has been approved by the competent authority and accordingly, they are being posted as follows.
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Government, with the approval of the competent authority, is approving the transfer and posting of the following officers of the Maharashtra Agriculture Officer, Group-B cadre in the department as shown in the table.
