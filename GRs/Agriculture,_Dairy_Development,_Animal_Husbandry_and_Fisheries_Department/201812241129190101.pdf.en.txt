Preamble: In accordance with the provisions of sub-section (2) of section 73AAA of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, Govt. 6/4/2016 and dt. As per the notification dated 14/3/2017, the qualifications and experience of the Government representatives to be appointed on Government shareholding Co-operative Societies have been prescribed. Similarly, in the proviso to sub-section (2) of section 73AA, It was revised on 14/12/2018. According to the amendment, a government officer not below the rank of Assistant Registrar, Cooperative Societies, can be appointed as a Director in the Board of Directors of a cooperative society which is not a cooperative society and a private person who has experience in the functioning of the cooperative society of the type of cooperative society, can be appointed as a government-appointed Director.
As per the provisions of Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, Shri.
Varvand Ta. Daund G. Pune having fulfilled the educational qualification and experience, is eligible to apply for the post of District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, Pune. The matter of appointing a government appointed director on the team was under the consideration of the government.
