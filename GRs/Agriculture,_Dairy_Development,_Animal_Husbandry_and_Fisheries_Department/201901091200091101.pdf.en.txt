Introduction: The State Government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme for farmers who are not eligible to cultivate fruit orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The scheme will be implemented during 2018-19. 1 Administrative approval has been granted by the Government Decision dated 06/07/2018. The government was planning to provide funds for the project. The government is taking the following steps:
Government Decision: 1. For the year 2018-19, Rs. 700 crore (Rs. 7 crore only) is being made available to the Commissioner Agriculture on the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The funds shall be spent out of the provisions budgeted for the current year under the following headings:
Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 119, Horticulture and Vegetable Crops, (01) Fruits (33) Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Cultivation Scheme (2401 A889) 33 Financial Assistance
The Assistant Director (Accounts-I), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Agriculture) is being declared as the Controlling Officer for drawing and disbursing the amount of Rs.700 lakhs from the treasury.
The subsidy under the scheme is to be paid to the beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to their Aadhaar seeded bank accounts.
