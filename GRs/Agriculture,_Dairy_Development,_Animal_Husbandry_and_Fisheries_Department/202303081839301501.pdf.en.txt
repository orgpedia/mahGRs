The Proposal:
The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched for three years from Kharif 2020 and Rabi 2020-21. 29.06.2020 and dt. As per the Government decision dated 17.07.2020, Indian Agricultural Insurance Company, IFFCO Tokio General Insurance Co., Ltd., Reliance General Insurance Co., Ltd., and Reliance General Insurance Co., Ltd. have been permitted to offer the following insurance products: Bharti AXA General Insurance Co. Ltd. Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company Limited. and HDFC Insurance. It was being operated by 6 insurance companies. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought the remaining State Government share of subsidy for crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2021 from the above 6 companies. In the revised guidelines of the Central Government, Issue No. 13.1.11 Under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, the remaining State share of insurance premium for Kharif season 2021 is Rs. 78,11,844/- to Insurance Companies. The payment is required to be made by 31.03.2023. Accordingly, the Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (5) In response to a request for Rs. 78,11,844/- (Rs. Seventy eight lakh eleven thousand eight hundred and forty four only) was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the remaining State share subsidy on account of crop insurance premium to the insurance companies.
