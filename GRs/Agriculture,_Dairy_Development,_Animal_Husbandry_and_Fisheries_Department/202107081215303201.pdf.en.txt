The Proposal:
The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and Commissionerates of Agriculture and subordinate offices. The Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (DADF) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture carry out various activities under the e-governance programme. Under this scheme, a fixed amount is sanctioned by the state government every year and the sanctioned amount is distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune.
The e-Governance programme is a scheme under the scheme and a total of Rs.25 crore has been budgeted for the scheme (3451 2782) in the financial year 2021-22. An amount of Rs. 10 crore has been provided.
In 2021-22, Rs. The government was considering granting administrative approval to implement the e-governance program within a limit of Rs 10 crore and disbursing Rs 10 crore for it.
Government's decision:
The State e-Governance Programme is being implemented to computerize the Agriculture and Padum Departments, Ministry Offices and the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune and various subordinate offices. Administrative approval is being given to implement the e-governance programme within a limit of Rs 10 crore.
To implement the e-Governance programme in 2021-22, in the first phase, Rs. 10 crore (Rupees Ten Crores only) is being disbursed to Department of Agriculture (itself), Department of Agriculture and Padum, Ministry and Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune. The Government has decided to utilise the funds for the following purposes:
