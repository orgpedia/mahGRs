Preamble: In total, 15 districts including 14 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada and Jalgaon district, a total of 5142 villages with World Bank funding, about Rs. Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project (PoCRA) is being implemented at a project cost of Rs 4000 crore and a total of 805 posts have been earmarked for the project. It was approved by the Government on 28th September 2017. Reference No. As per the decision of the Government, two posts of Assistant Accounts Officer have been sanctioned for Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project and both the posts have been filled.
A total of 3 posts have been sanctioned and 2 of them have been filled. Reference No. As per the letter, the project director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project, had submitted a proposal to the government to abolish the post of deputy accountant for the project as it was vacant and create a new post in the cadre of assistant accounts officer instead. As per the 7th Pay Commission, it falls in S-13 and 9-15 categories respectively. Since the matter pertains to financial matters and requires approval of the Finance Department, the Finance Department was requested to approve the upgradation of the post of Deputy Accountant (Deputation) (S13; 35400-112400) to the post of Assistant Accounts Officer (Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service) (Deputation) (S-15; 41800-132300). While the above proposal was placed before the High Level Committee of Secretaries with the approval of the Department of Finance, the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its 2nd meeting held on 29 th April, 2022 has approved creation of 1 post in the cadre of Assistant Accounts Officer (S-15; 41800-132300) by abolishing 1 post out of 3 posts in the cadre of Sub-Accountant (S13; 35400-112400) sanctioned for Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project.
