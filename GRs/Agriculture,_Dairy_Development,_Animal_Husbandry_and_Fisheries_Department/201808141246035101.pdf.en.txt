Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission is being implemented in the State under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Krishi Unnati. The funding pattern is 60:40 between the Centre and the state. For 2017-18, the government has allocated Rs. A sum of Rs. 3412.80 lakh had been released. Out of this amount, Rs. 105.0586 lacs remained unspent at the end of the financial year. The scheme has been revived by the Government of India vide letter dated June, 2018 for implementation during 2018-19.
Out of the unspent amount, Rs. 37.6676, Rs. 52.9376 and Rs. 14.4533 lakh. The Central share of funds for Scheduled Tribes is Rs. 14.4533 panky Rs. 7.2880 lakh has been withdrawn by the Commissionerate of Agriculture from the government treasury and deposited in PFMS bank account. The remaining Rs. 7.1653 lakh is deposited in the Government treasury. The Central Government has earmarked Rs. 7.1653 lakh and Rs. 4.7768 crore. A proposal to provide funds to the Commissionerate of Agriculture on BDS system to implement the Revitalization Programme of Rs. 11.9421 lakh during 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION 1. Non-recurring Central share of Rs. 14.4533 lakh and Rs. 9.6355 lakh. The Government has approved the implementation of Revitalization Programme for 2018-19 with an outlay of Rs. 24.08 lakh.
