Preamble In order to increase the income of the farmers as well as to increase the energy consumption in the agriculture sector, the Centrally Sponsored Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Abhiyaan is being implemented in the State. Apart from this, under various centrally sponsored schemes like National Mission on Pulses, Oilseeds and Oilseeds, National Mission on Food Security and National Mission on Horticulture, some amount of funds are also provided for the agricultural mechanization component. However, adequate funds are not available for mechanization as per the demand of the farmers. Considering these factors, every year funds are also provided for agricultural mechanization under the National Agriculture Development Scheme. The board has approved a grant of Rs 60 crore.
The matter of administrative approval of the program to be implemented from the funds sanctioned from the National Agriculture Development Scheme-Raftaar was under consideration of the government.
