In this backdrop, the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) has been approved for sowing in the State for the next five years i.e. from 2020-21 to 2024-2025 as per the Government decision dated 23/12/2020.
The total outlay for implementation of the scheme for the year 2022-2023 is Rs. 175.90 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Seventy Five Crore Ninety Lakh only) with Central share and State share. The administrative approval has been given under the Government Decision 6. The Department of Finance has decided that the budget estimates for 2022-23 will be distributed through the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System within the limit of 60%.
