The release of subsidy amounting to Rs. 4,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crore Fifty Lakh only) is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions
(1) The funds shall be used for the purposes set out in Annexure A.
The guidelines and prevailing norms issued earlier by the Planning and Finance Department as well as the circulars of the Finance Department dated April 18, 2017, June 30, 2017, and August 8, 2017, as well as the instructions given from time to time, should be strictly followed while spending these funds.
(iii) If any of the sub-account, group, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
4) The grant should be used primarily to meet the expenses of the project. The rest of the grant should be spent in the order of certain amendments, general and special amendments.
(5) Each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
7) Do not spend money on illegal schemes, posts and affairs.
8) The Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
9) The Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, Ahmednagar has sanctioned Rs. 4,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crore Fifty Lakh only) to the Controller, Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the provision approved under the next head of accounts for the financial year 2017-2018 and shall be spent under the same head.
