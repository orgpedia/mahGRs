Introduction: As per the policy of the Government, a sound financial management system (PFMS) is adopted for the Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Aided Schemes of the Department of Agriculture. Nodal officers, State Plan Managers and First Level Agencies have been declared by the Government under the relevant Government decision for the implementation of this system. On the same lines, the government was considering to declare a nodal officer, state plan manager and first level agency, second level agency, maker and checker for the implementation of the financing of insurance premium under the Maharashtra State Agriculture and Rural Transformation Project and Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana through the Public Financial Management System (PFMS). Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: The Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare has issued a notification to the Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary (Budget and Planning) for the implementation of Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Aided Schemes of the Department of Agriculture. 16/6/2017 and D. As per the Government decision dated 9/8/2018, he has been declared as the Nodal Officer. The Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary (Budget and Planning) will be the nodal officer for implementation of the Maharashtra State Agriculture and Rural Transformation Project and financing of insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana through Public Financial Management System. Also, the State Plan Manager, First Level Agency, Second Level Agency, Maker and Checker for the above two schemes are being announced as follows.

State Plan Manager:- The State Plan Manager shall constitute a First Level Agency at the State level to implement the Plan and appoint/ modify as per requirement the Under Secretaries/ Cell Officers under him/ her as Administrator and to handle the administrative/ technical matters related to the Plan on PFMS system.
First Level Agency:- The administrator of the First Level Agency will be responsible for creating the Second Level Agency/ making changes as required. The Project Director (SMART), Pune will be the administrator of the Second Level Agency for Maharashtra State Agriculture and Rural Transformation Project. Also, there will be an officer of the rank of Director/ Joint Director/ Deputy Director who will be able to implement the scheme at the level of Agriculture Commissioner as the administrator of the Second Level Agency to finance the premium of insurance under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana.
Maker and Checker:- For implementation of Maharashtra State Agriculture and Rural Transformation Project through Public Financial Management System, Assistant Accountant (Smart), Pune will be the Maker, Specialist (Smart), Pune will be the Primary Checker and Additional Project Director (Smart), Pune will be the Final Checker. Further, under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), the Accountant (Accounts-1) will be the maker and Assistant Director (Accounts-1) will be the checker for the implementation of the scheme through Public Financial Management System (PFMS).
