Government's decision:
The difference between the Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) based price and the Minimum Support Price (MSP) declared by the Government for the seeds produced for the Kharif and Rabi Crops like Moong, Urad, Tur, Soyabean, Paddy, Gram and Karadai in the year 2019-20 will be paid to the farmers through Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited, Akola under Account Head 2401 A922 (100% State Scheme). 1879.24 lakh is being disbursed to the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21 and shall be covered under the following heads:
Demand No. D- 3-2401, Crop Protection, 103, B-Seeds, (00) (28) On payment of difference between Minimum Support Price (MSP) and Agriculture Produce Market Committee (APMC) Support Price to seed producing farmers, (100% State Programme) 33- Financial Assistance, (2401 A922)
The amount of this difference should be deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the farmers through direct benefit transfer. It should be ensured that these funds are not kept in P.L.A./ bank accounts.
