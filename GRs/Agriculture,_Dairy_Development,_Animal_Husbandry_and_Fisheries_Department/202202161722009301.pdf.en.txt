Introduction: In the citrus crops of the state, orange is followed by the fruit of the mulberry. Maharashtra stands first in the country in milk production. Considering the favorable climate for the crops of mung bean in Marathwada, mung bean is more beneficial for the fruit farmers. In Marathwada around 39370 hectare area is under the cultivation of black gram.
Out of them Aurangabad district 21525 hectare and Jalna district 14325 hectare are major mung bean producing districts. Considering the need to create clusters for sustainable production, processing and export of mung bean, which is important for Marathwada, the government has approved the establishment of" Citrus Estate" for mung bean on the area of Taluka Fruit Plantation, Paithan, District Aurangabad. Now the Director (Horticulture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune has requested to provide funds under letter No. 3 at Wacha. Accordingly, administrative approval for implementation of" Citrus Estate" at Taluka Fruit Garden, Paithan, G. Aurangabad in the year 2021-2022 and disbursement of funds to Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune was under consideration of the Government. The government will take a decision accordingly.
