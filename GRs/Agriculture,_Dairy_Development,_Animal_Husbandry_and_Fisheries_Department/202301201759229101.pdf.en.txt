The Proposal:
Integrated Poultry Development under District Annual General Plan is being implemented in the State since 2010 and under this scheme, Talanga groups (25 Talanga+ 3 male hens) are allotted for egg production at 50% subsidy and 100 groups of improved poultry birds are allotted in a day.
2. At present, the prices of boiled eggs, fried chicken, male chicken and one-day-old chicken have all gone up. Besides, the prices of fertilisers, pesticides and pesticides have also increased.
3. Since it has been a long time since the base price of the group of Talanga, male chicken and one-day poultry birds to be distributed under this scheme was fixed, the matter was under the consideration of the Government.
