Government Decision: Haribhau Bagade on the attention-grabbing suggestion raised under Rule 105 of the Legislative Assembly. Discussion was held in the Legislative Council on 17.3.2023. Replying to the questions raised by the members, Shri. The Minister of Dairying has assured that a committee will be constituted to make recommendations for taking measures for qualitative growth of dairy sector in the cooperative sector, bringing government control over private and cooperative dairies, prevention of milk adulteration, etc.
As per the assurance given by the Hon'ble Minister, Dairying to the House of the Legislature, approval is being given to form the study group as follows.

The mandate of the Committee will be: 1) To suggest measures for qualitative growth of cooperative dairy business.
(ii) Suggest measures to regulate the cooperative and private dairy sector.

3. Suggest measures to ensure good quality milk to the public and fair price to the milk producer through healthy competition among cooperative and private milk producers.
(iv) Suggest measures for providing financial assistance to dairy farmers in the cooperative sector for setting up infrastructure for milk processing and production of dairy products.
5) To make recommendations for more effective implementation of measures to prevent adulteration of milk.
