Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries Department. Under 09.10.2018, the Department of Animal Husbandry, Zilla Parishad has been empowered to grant technical approval to the schemes being implemented by the District Planning Board, State Level Schemes, Centrally Sponsored Schemes and other departments from the funds received from the department.
District Annual Plan (General) 2020-21. Deputy Chief Minister (Finance and Planning) 24.1.2020 to dt. In the department wise State level meetings held during 31.1.2020, Smt. The Deputy Chief Minister has directed that the powers of technical approval of district annual plans in the Department of Animal Husbandry are currently kept at the state/ directorate level, and a decision should be taken to give priority to them at the district/ division level and accordingly, the existing government decisions/ guidelines should be made.
Read No. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 9.10.2018 is hereby superseded and issued as follows:
Government's decision
Technical approval for schemes being funded by District Planning Boards, State Level Schemes, Centrally Sponsored Schemes and other Government departments and other sources.
