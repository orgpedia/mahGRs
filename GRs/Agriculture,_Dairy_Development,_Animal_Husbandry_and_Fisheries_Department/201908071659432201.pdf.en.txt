Introduction: The World Bank funded Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project will be implemented in selected 5142 villages of 14 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada and 15 districts of Jalgaon over a period of 6 years from 2018-19. 1 It has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 17.01.2018. Further, vide Government Order dated 16.09.2016, Reference No.2, Project Director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project has been declared as Controlling Officer for the project and Finance Specialist has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Project Management Cell. According to the Financial Management Manual for the project, at the district level, the District Superintending Agriculture Officer is the Controlling Officer and the Accounts Officer is the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the project. At the sub-division level, the sub-divisional agriculture officer is working as the Control Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the project. At present, the bank accounts of all the drawing and disbursing officers of the project are transacted with a single signature.
2. Annex-5 of the" Aid Memoire" of the World Bank Mission states that" The Project must ensure that the mode of operation of all bank accounts under the project is Joint Signatory." In accordance with the instructions of the World Bank Mission, it was proposed by the Project Director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani, Mumbai, under the letter of reference No. 3, that the transactions of the bank account of the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the Project Management Cell of the Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project should be done with the joint signature of the' Finance Specialist' and the' Accounts Officer', and the transactions of the DBT Bank account with the joint signature of the' Finance Specialist' and the' Project Deputy Director'.
3. As per the Government decision dated 16.09.2016, the Project Director, Project Management Cell, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project has been declared as the Controlling Officer for the project and the Finance Specialist has been declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer at the State level.

4. Transactions of the Bank Account of Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the Project Management Cell of Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project' Finance Specialist' and' Accounts' in consideration of the above mentioned instructions of the World Bank.
The government was considering as a special case to approve the transaction of DBT bank account with the joint signature of the' officer' and the joint signature of the' finance expert' and the' project deputy director'.
Government's decision:
As per the instructions of the World Bank, the transactions of the bank account of the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the Project Management Cell of Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project are being allowed with the joint signature of the' Finance Specialist' and' Accounts Officer', and the transactions of the DBT bank account with the joint signature of the' Finance Specialist' and' Project Deputy Director'.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the approval received under the Informal Reference No. Anouk/ 2019/ 268/ Koshaprasad-5 dated 09/07/2019 of the Department of Finance.
