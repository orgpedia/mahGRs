The Proposal:
The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched for three years from Kharif 2020 and Rabi 2020-21. 29.06.2020 and dt. As per the Government decision dated 17.07.2020, Indian Agricultural Insurance Company, IFFCO Tokio General Insurance Co., Ltd., Reliance General Insurance Co., Ltd. Bharti AXA General Insurance Co. Ltd. Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company Limited. and HDFC Insurance. It is operated by 6 insurance companies. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought State Government share subsidy for crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Rabi Season 2021. In the revised guidelines of the Central Government, Issue No. 13.1.6 states that" the insurance premium share of the Central and State Government is required to be paid to the company in advance (first installment) even if the data of the insured area is not available at the time of registration of the current season. The insurance premium subsidy amount should be 50 per cent of the total 80 per cent of the total state share insurance premium subsidy paid in the previous season in advance to the companies," it said. Accordingly, the Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (4) In response to a request for Rs. 117,26,33, 552/- was under consideration of the Government for payment of crop insurance premium subsidy to insurance companies.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The demand submitted by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India, the recommendation of the Agriculture Commissionerate and the revised guidelines of the Central Government are at issue No. 13.1.6.Considering the above, the State share of crop insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) for Rabi 2021-22 is Rs. The Government is approving disbursement of subsidy to the tune of Rs. 117,26,33, 552/-to the insurance companies. This amount is being disbursed for the Rabi season 2021-22 and its utilization will not be admissible for other seasons earlier.
Expenditure on the said items should be met from the budgetary provision approved for the year 2022-23 under the following head: Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 110, Crop Insurance (00) (08) Financial assistance for insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana State share (2401 A 664) Non-plan, 33- Financial assistance.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for spending the funds disbursed under this government decision in accordance with the guidelines of the Prime Minister Crop Insurance Scheme and the government decision issued by the government from time to time.
For this purpose, Assistant Director (Accounts), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
