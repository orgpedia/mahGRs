Government's decision:
Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli, has issued an order for payment of arrears as per the Seventh Pay Commission to the retirees as well as pension for the financial year 2021-2022. D-3, 2415 Agricultural Research and Education, 01- Crop Protection, 120 Assistance to other Institutions, (00) (02) Dr. B.S. Co. V. Subsidiary Grants (Compulsory) (24150034), 04- Pension Expenditure. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 9,20,92, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crores Twenty Lakhs Ninety Two Thousand only).
Terms and Conditions:
The grants sanctioned as above shall be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
2) While seeking grants for the year 2021-2022, the university should submit a report on the grants disbursed, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
3) Do not spend money on posts and affairs.
The Controller and other relevant competent authorities of the University shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
3. The Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, Dapoli, as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer, shall take necessary action in the matter as per rules.
