The Proposal:
The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and Commissionerates of Agriculture and subordinate offices. A total of Rs 2500 crore has been budgeted for the financial year 2021-22 under this scheme.
In view of the effective use of e-office system by the officers/ employees of the department, the Project Implementation Committee constituted under the State e-Governance Programme of the Department of Agriculture has been constituted. In the meeting held on 10 th February, 2022, approval has been given for procurement of computers/ hardware for Department of Agriculture (Ministry level). In pursuance of this decision, the amount required for the purchase of computers, printers and scanners for the Department of Agriculture (Ministry level) is Rs. The proposal to approve the payment of Rs. 51.20 lakh from the funds sanctioned under the State e-Governance Programme was under consideration of the Government, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2021-22, out of the funds sanctioned under the e-governance scheme, Rs. 51.20 lakh (Rupees One and Half Lakh Twenty Thousand only) is being sanctioned.
