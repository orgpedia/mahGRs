The Proposal:
Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli University for payment of salary to the employees of the university in the budget of the financial year 2021-2022 for the account head 2415 0081,36-Assistant Grant (Salary) (Program) has been allocated in the above reference No. 1 The decisions of the Government herein have been distributed from time to time. Accordingly, a total of Rs. 75% of the amount of Rs. 700.00 lakhs has been disbursed. The remaining 25% is being funded by the government.
Government's decision:
Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli has issued an order for payment of salaries to the employees of the university for the financial year 2021-2022. D-3, 2415 Agricultural Research and Education, 01- Crop Protection, 120 Assistance to other Institutions, (00) (07) Dr. The Government is sanctioning the release of funds amounting to Rs. 175,00,000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Five Lakhs only).
Terms and Conditions: 2. Subsidy under the said Government Decision is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The funds released by the Agricultural Universities and the Council of Agriculture in respect of sub-headings/ groups/ schemes and subject-wise expenditure should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the respective scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as non-plan schemes. In case the subsidy under a scheme remains unutilized due to vacancies or other reasons, the same may be adjusted in the next month's disbursement.

(2) If any of the sub-account, group, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
3. The Agriculture Council/ University should thoroughly examine whether the sub-title wise sanctioned schemes and the posts sanctioned under them have been properly grouped as per the revised pattern. No change in this classification should be made without the approval of the government.
(4) The income from the distributions and the revenue from each group shall be used to meet the expenditure on permissible matters of that group and to carry out development works. Subsidies and revenue income of one group should not be diverted for schemes/ activities of another group. If more grants are allocated for a group scheme, the amount of the excess grant should be taken into account in the subsequent demand for funds.
The grant will be made only for the salary and allowances of the posts sanctioned by the Government which have been filled as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statute, 1990 and the Government has approved the continuation of these posts at non-plan cost.
6) Separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained for each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise.
(7) The schemes/ schemes under which the period of non-plan period has expired shall not be continued after the relevant period at the cost of non-plan/ schemes without obtaining the approval of the Government.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula, and how the expenditure is being made in accordance with the prescribed orders/ procedures. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
The Government while approving some of the new schemes of the University, has approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme in an adjusted manner. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
10) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income.
The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.

The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
While making the demand for grants for the year 2021-22, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed scheme-wise, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
13) Don't spend money on posts and things that are out of order.
The above terms and conditions shall be strictly complied with by the Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities.
The expenditure for this purpose should be shared within the limits of the monthly fund statement from the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts mentioned in the Budget for 2021-22 and should be spent under the same head of accounts.
