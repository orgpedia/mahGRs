The Proposal:
A meeting was held on 04.12.2018 under the Chairmanship of Hon'ble Governor to promote cultivation of nutritious cereal crops and increase their use in food in order to address the problem of malnutrition in the tribal dominated areas of the State. At the meeting, The Governor had suggested the formation of a Task Force to suggest measures to promote cultivation of major nutritive cereals like Jowar, Bajra, Nachani and other nutritive cereals like Varai, Rala, Rajgira etc. and to increase their use in the diet.
Government's decision:
In order to address the problem of malnutrition in the tribal dominated areas of the State, a" Task Force" is being constituted with the participation of the following departments/ organizations to suggest measures to increase the production and increase the use of these nutritious cereals in the diet by promoting the cultivation of nutritious cereals (sorghum, millet, nachani being the major cereals and varai, resin, rajgira etc.

To create awareness among the people of tribal areas and other areas of the state to increase the use of nutritious cereals in their daily diet and to increase the production and productivity of these nutritious cereals, the" Task Force" should study the following aspects and submit its report to the government for taking policy decisions.
Current status of cultivation of nutri-cereals, their use in food, marketing system, demand and supply etc.
(ii) Existing varieties of nutri-cereals, their production potential, current status of adoption of modern agricultural technologies in their cultivation and production chain, analysis of yield gap and measures to bridge this gap, and analysis of economic returns from cultivation of nutri-cereal crops.
(iii) Availability of post-harvest management and processing infrastructure for nutri-cereals.
(iv) Minimum Support Prices for foodgrains and the prevailing policy of the Government regarding their procurement and distribution through Public Distribution System.
v. To make various food products by processing of nutritious cereal crops according to the needs of the people.
vi. Market linkage for marketing of nutri-cereals.
Section 3. In accordance with the points in point 2, meetings of the Task Force should be held around the time of the above mentioned meetings. The report of the Task Force is to be prepared by the Indian School of Business, Hyderabad under the guidance of the Commissioner (Agriculture) and for this all the departments should make available the information related to them to the Indian School of Business from time to time.
(4) The members of the Task Force may be invited to attend the meetings of the Task Force with the approval of the Chairman.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) shall convene a meeting of the Task Force on the report prepared by the Indian School of Business, Hyderabad, in this regard and submit the report to the Government along with its recommendations.
The Indian School of Business, Hyderabad, shall enter into a Non-Disclosure Agreement with the Governor's Secretariat before preparing a study report in the matter.
The Task Force in this case should submit its report to the Department of Agriculture within three months from the date of publication of the government decision.
