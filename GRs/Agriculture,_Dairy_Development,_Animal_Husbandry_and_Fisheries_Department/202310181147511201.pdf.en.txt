Read: 1) Department of Agriculture and Padum, Government Decision No. 2022/ No. 145/ Padum13, d. 15th February, 2023 Preamble: Considering the need to amend the Maharashtra Fisheries Act, 1960, in view of the increase in groundwater fisheries, a 16-member expert committee under the chairmanship of Commissioner, Fisheries, State of Maharashtra, has been constituted to amend the said Act. 1 here. The new government will be formed on February 15, 2023. It was under consideration of the Government to include Mr. Bandu Mengaji Hazare as a member of this Committee. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Supplementary Letter: Under this Government Supplementary Letter, Reference No. 1 here. Shri Bandu Mengaji Hazare is being included as a member in the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Fisheries, State of Maharashtra vide Government Resolution dated 15.02.2023.
