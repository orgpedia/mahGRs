Government Resolution No: Matsya 2013/ No.174/ Padum-12
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: November 13, 2020.
The Proposal:
The government was considering the implementation of the recommendations of the Sixth Pay Commission for the officers and employees working under the Maharashtra Fisheries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai. The government is taking the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The 6th Pay Commission for the officers and employees of Maharashtra Fisheries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai is being implemented from 1st April, 2018. Along with this, the names of the officers and employees, the pay scale of the Fifth Pay Commission and the Sixth Pay Commission have been added.
The Maharashtra Fisheries Development Corporation (MFDC) will have to bear the additional financial burden of implementing the Sixth Pay Commission for its officers and employees from its own income. No financial assistance will be provided by the government in this regard.
This decision was taken by the Finance Department. Reference No. 109/ s., dated 9.1.2020 as approved by them.
