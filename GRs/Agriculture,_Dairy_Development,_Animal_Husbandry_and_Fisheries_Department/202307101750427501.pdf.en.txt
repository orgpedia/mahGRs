The Proposal:
Under the government' s Aarey Dairy, milk of' Aarey Brand' is sold by packing. However, the production and distribution of the Aarey brand has been discontinued with effect from 28.8.2022. Considering the demand for Aarey milk in Mumbai city, the matter of packing and selling the milk from Mahananda Dairy was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Maharashtra government, Aarey milk will be sold at Aarey Milk Stations in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Thane and the rest of Maharashtra. The approval is granted on a pilot basis for a period of 1 year. This recognition will remain only for' milk'.
As per this approval, such terms and conditions as are favourable to the government should be issued by the Commissioner, Dairy, at his level. Also, the necessary agreement in this regard should be made by the Commissioner, Dairying, with Mahananda. The Commissioner, Dairy, should ensure that the government does not lose any revenue while signing the agreement.

A royalty of 1% of the selling price (distributor rate) of milk to be made by Mahananda should be charged. It will be mandatory to submit the details of milk sale every month, its price to the distributors, along with the certificate of chartered account on the 10th of the next month from Mahananda and pay the royalty amount to the government. The agreement will be reviewed after one year.
