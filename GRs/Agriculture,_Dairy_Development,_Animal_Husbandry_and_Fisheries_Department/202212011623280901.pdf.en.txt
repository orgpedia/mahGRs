As per the decision taken in the fifth meeting held on Sunday, while approving the outline of the Maharashtra Veterinary and Fisheries Sciences University, the revised outline of the university was approved for a total of 1922 posts, including 1745 regular posts for the university headquarters, 6 veterinary colleges affiliated to the university, 4 animal husbandry areas, 2 colleges of dairy technology, 2 colleges of fisheries science, 1 wildlife research and training center, Gorewada, 71 regular posts to be created, 57 posts revived in the College of Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur and College of Fisheries Sciences, Udgir, and 49 posts to be filled through external sources. This proposal The approval was given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting held on April 29, 2022. Accordingly, the matter of fixing the revised contours of the Maharashtra Animal and Fisheries Sciences University was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
8. The Government hereby approve the revised figure as per the norms of the Indian Council of Veterinary and Agricultural Research, New Delhi, for establishment of Maharashtra Veterinary, Fisheries Sciences University under the Headquarter, Subordinate Veterinary, Fisheries Sciences, Dairy Technology Colleges, Animal Breeding Farms and Wildlife Research and Training Centre, Gorewada, as shown in Annexure' A', a total of 1816 posts as shown in Annexure' B', a total of 1873 regular posts as shown in Annexure' B' (Teachers and Non-Teachers) and 49 posts to be filled by external agencies as shown in Annexure' A'.
As per the approval of the revised pattern for Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur, under this Government decision, the details of all the combined posts attached with Goshwara in Annexure' A' and the revived posts have been mentioned in Annexure' B'.
Further, details of 49 posts to be filled by external agencies are indicated in Annexure' C'. The details of sanctioned posts at the Headquarter, various constituent colleges, Animal Husbandry Department, Wildlife Research and Training Centre, Gorewada under the University are also shown in Annexure' D'-1 to' D'-16. The vacancies in the Cadre mentioned in Annexure' A' and' B' and Annexure' D' 1 to' D' 16 are being allowed to be filled up by the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur as per the powers conferred under the Universities Act, 1998 and Statutes, 2002. Further, the university in Annexure' C' is being allowed to make available manpower in the posts sanctioned by the external agencies by adopting the instructions issued by the Finance Department from time to time.
Some of the regular posts in Annexure' A' and Annexure' D'-1 to' D'-16 are shown as dead cadre. Page 3 of page 45 of the Appendix lists the cadres who have died.
