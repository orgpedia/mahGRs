3) Department of Agriculture and Padum, Government Decision, No. APO-2023/ No. 133/ 9O, Dy. 12/06/2023 4) Department of Finance, Government Circular No. 12/04/2023 5) Tribal Development Department, Government Resolution, No.BUD-2023/ No.No. 07/07/2023 and Corrigendum. 14/07/2023 Preamble: Under the Tribal Component Programme, with the objective of reducing malnutrition in the tribal areas of 14 districts of the State and to provide nutritious food to the tribal brothers and sisters, the scheme of" Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation in Tribal Household Bags" is being implemented in the State since 2003-04 to promote the selection and cultivation of nutritious vegetables and fruits in the family bag of the tribal families. For the financial year 2023-24, Rs. 36.30 lakh has been budgeted and administrative approval has been granted within the limits of the budgetary fund as per the Government Resolution No. 3 read above. The Department of Tribal Development, under the head Government Resolution (2401 A806) No. 5, read above, has allocated 10% of the budgetary allocation for the year 2023-24, amounting to Rs. 3.63 lakh has been transferred to the Agriculture Department. The matter of disbursing this fund to the Agriculture Commissionerate was under the consideration of the government. Following this, the government took decision:
Government Decision: Financial approval is being given to spend an amount of Rs. 3.63 lakh within the limit of 10% of the budgeted funds for the implementation of the' Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation Scheme in the Neighborhoods of Tribal Families' in the 14 districts of Thane, Palghar, Pune, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Jalgaon, Ahmednagar, Nanded, Yavatmal, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Amravati and Gondia in the year 2023-24.
