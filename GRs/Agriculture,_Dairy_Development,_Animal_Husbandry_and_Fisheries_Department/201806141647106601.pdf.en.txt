Witnessed on 29.05.2018. Implementation of schemes related to animal husbandry was discussed during the Sakshi. Accordingly, Ma. President of Panchayat Raj Committee has directed that" Animal husbandry related schemes are meant to improve the living standards of farmers. It is necessary to verify the implementation of the plans and evaluate the results. " The government has constituted a committee for this purpose. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Farmers are earning good incomes from the government schemes. However, to prevent malpractices in the implementation of the schemes, a committee has been formed at the government level to verify the implementation of the schemes and evaluate the results. As per the directions of the President of the Panchayat Raj Committee, the following committees are being formed.
a) Deputy Secretary (Animal Husbandry) Chairman b) Additional Commissioner Animal Husbandry Member c) Deputy Commissioner Animal Husbandry concerned Member d) District Animal Husbandry Officer concerned Member Secretary

The duties and responsibilities of the Committee are as follows:
i) The scope of the committee is the entire state and the beneficiaries of the schemes should be verified by making a sudden physical visit to the animals.
(ii) To examine the Plan Follow-up Report at the Taluk level.
(iii) To follow up and review the schemes at the Veterinary Clinic level.
(vi) To guide the livestock farmers and veterinarians in the implementation of the scheme.
(v) Implementation of the scheme at the district level and submission of the outcome report to the Government.
