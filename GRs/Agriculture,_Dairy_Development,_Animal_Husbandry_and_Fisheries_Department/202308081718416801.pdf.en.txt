In view of the above directions of the Finance Department, it was under the consideration of the Government to sanction defined contribution pension scheme/ National Pension System to the family of the employee who dies during the service period, family pension and death gratuity to the family of the employee who died during the service period and sickness pension and retirement gratuity to the employee who retired, as well as retirement gratuity to the employees retiring from Government service.
Government's decision:
Appointed and defined contribution pension scheme in Government service on or after 01.11.2005 The National Pension System (NPS) is applicable to the employee (a) Family pension and death gratuity to his family in case of death while in service, (b) Sickness pension and service gratuity to the employee who retired on sickness.
Accordingly, the family pension/ sickness pension as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 shall be applicable to the said family pensioner and the equalisation pensioner.
(c) Retirement allowance is also being applied to the employees retiring from Government service.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 shall continue to apply for the purpose of retirement gratuity and death gratuity to be provided in accordance with the above decision.
