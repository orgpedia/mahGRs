The proposal: The Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 (Chairman and Managing Director, FCI and Others Vs. Jagadish Balaram Behera and Others) and others. It has been decided that persons who have joined government service on the basis of reservation for backward castes as on 06.07.2017 and whose caste claims have been invalidated thereafter, will not be entitled to protection in government service. Instructions for implementation of this decision have been issued to the concerned administrative departments by the General Administration Department under reference No. 1 decision has been made by the government.
Accordingly, the services of 57 officers/ employees who were appointed to the reserved post of Scheduled Tribes working under the Commissionerate of Agriculture in the Department of Agriculture but whose claim of Scheduled Tribes was found invalid, have been temporarily transferred to the superannuated post as per the provisions of Government Decision S.P.V.D. 21.12.2019. Accordingly, the matter of creating more number of posts for the said officers/ employees was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the 57 officers and employees who were appointed to the reserved post of Scheduled Tribes in the Agriculture Department but were transferred to the superannuated post due to the invalidation of the claim of the Scheduled Tribes, a number of additional posts are being created as per the details mentioned in the accompanying returns" A" and" B."
