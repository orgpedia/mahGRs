The Proposal:
The Prime Minister's Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Enterprises (PMFME) will be implemented in the state for the next five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 23.12.2020. The scheme will be implemented in the state from 2023-24. Pursuant to Government Decision dated 11.05.2023, Rs. The administrative approval for the project has been accorded for an amount of Rs. 41907.76 lakh. The central government is reading it. As per the letter dated 21.06.2023, the first installment of the scheme has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State Government.
Therefore, the matter of disbursing the first installment of general category funds under the PMFME scheme was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Central share of the first installment of the Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme for the General Category for implementation in the State in 2023-24 is Rs. 57,40,06, 000/- and State share of Rs. 30,77,84, 667/- aggregating to Rs. 88,17,90, 667/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Crores Seventeen Lakh Ninety Six Hundred Six Hundred Sixty Six only) to State Nodal Agencies (PMFME) and Commissioner (Agriculture).

4. Referred Government Decision. The scheme should be implemented as per the provisions prescribed under 23/12/2020 and guidelines for implementation of the scheme in 2022-23 should be issued from the level of Agriculture Commissionerate.
