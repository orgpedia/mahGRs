Introduction: ICAR-Indian Institute of Sugarcane Research, Lucknow through its Regional Biological Control Centre at Pravaranagar has undertaken survey of pests and diseases on sugarcane, laboratory production of friend pests, etc.
The major activities are to impart information to the farmers about the latest technologies in sugarcane and to conduct new researches to control the major pests and diseases of sugarcane biologically. ICAR-Indian Institute of Sugarcane Research, Lucknow has sanctioned Rs. 457.53 lakh was submitted under the National Agriculture Development Scheme. The project has been approved by the state cabinet. The project will be funded with 100% grant of Rs. 128.81 lakh (Rs. 80 lakh and Rs. Rs 48.81 lakh)
The matter was under the consideration of the government, the government is taking the following decision.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, ICAR-Indian Institute of Sugarcane Research, Lucknow is being given approval to implement the project of Bio-Control Laboratory at Pravaranagar.
ICAR-Indian Institute of Sugarcane Research, Lucknow presented Rs. Rs 457.53 lakh
The project cost is Rs. 128.81 lakh is being sanctioned as grant and the remaining Rs. 328.72 lakh will be mandatorily invested as self-funding by ICAR-Indian Institute of Sugarcane Research, Lucknow.
