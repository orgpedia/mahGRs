Preamble: The report of the Empowered Committee constituted under the Union Ministry of Agriculture for doubling the income of farmers by 2022 is available on the website of the Union Ministry of Agriculture (www. agricoop. gov. in/ doubling-farmers. Volume 14 of this report contains a summary of the Committee's entire study and recommendations. Since the implementation of these recommendations was under the purview of the state, the government was considering setting up a committee for supervision. The following decisions are being taken:
Governance Decision: A Supervisory Committee is being constituted as follows to guide and monitor the interventions and impact of the programmes for implementation of the recommendations of the Central Empowerment Committee Report on Doubling Farmers Income by 2022.
Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune. Vice Chancellor, Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri, Ahmednagar Member 3. Vice Chancellor, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Agricultural University, Dapoli, Ratnagiri. Member 4. Kulguru, Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani. 5. Vice Chancellor, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola. Director (Extension and Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune. The Member Secretary
2.The above Supervisory Committee shall review the implementation of the programme from time to time and send a report to the Central Government. A cell is also being set up at the level of Agriculture Commissioner as a nodal agency at the state level in this regard. This cell will be under the control of Commissioner (Agriculture), State of Maharashtra.
