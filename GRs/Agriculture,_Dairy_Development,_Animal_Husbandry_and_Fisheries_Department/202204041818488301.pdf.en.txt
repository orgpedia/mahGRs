The proposal: The 23 rd State Level Project Sanctioning Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra had approved the implementation of the sub-irrigation scheme for seed production in central campus Rahuri under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana at Mahatma Phule Krishi Vidyapeeth, Rahuri University for a period of 3 years from 2017-18. Under the scheme, Rs. 1522.00 lakhs will be provided. The project is yet to be completed and will be completed by 2022-23. The matter of giving administrative approval to the program of 1522 lakh rupees was under the consideration of the government, following which the government is taking the following decisions.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Under the scheme of" Implementation of Subtidal Irrigation Scheme for Seed Production in Central Area Rahuri" under National Agriculture Development Scheme, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of the programme with an outlay of Rs. 1522.00 lakhs (Rupees Fifteen Crores Twenty Two Lakhs only).
The project should be implemented in Mahatma Phule Agricultural University as per the guidelines of the National Agriculture Development Scheme.
