Introduction: Maharashtra is a leading state in horticulture. This includes mainly citrus fruits. In Vidarbha, the area under orange and mango is the highest in the state. In order to increase the productivity and quality of oranges considering the various problems faced by the farmers in Vidarbha, administrative approval has been given to establish" Citrus Estate" in Nagpur, Amravati and Wardha districts of the state under the government decision mentioned at No. 1 here. A budget provision of Rs. 5973.97 lakh has been made for the year 2022-2023 for the implementation of the programme of" Citrus Estate" in the State. The Department of Finance vide Circular No. 3 has approved the release of funds within the limit of 60% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, the Director (Horticulture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, vide letter No. 4 to 8, Wacha, has requested to provide funds with administrative approval. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval and disbursement of funds to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune for the implementation of the scheme" Citrus Estate" in the state in the year 2022-2023 was under the consideration of the government. The government will take a decision accordingly.
