The Proposal:
The proposal to start post-graduate studies at Sharad Chandra Pawar Agriculture College, Baramati G. Pune was submitted to Mahatma Phule Agriculture University, Rahuri. As per the proposal, an expert committee was constituted under the chairmanship of Dr. C. D. Mayee, former Vice-Chancellor, Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani, with the approval of the Vidya Parishad, to examine the feasibility and feasibility of the affiliated colleges for starting post-graduate degree courses. Sharad Chandra Pawar Agriculture College, Baramati, Pune inspected the facilities required for starting post graduate courses in agriculture based on minimum criteria. Since the college has the necessary facilities to start the agricultural master's degree course, the expert committee has recommended to start the agricultural master's degree course subject to certain terms and conditions. The Board of Governors of Maharashtra State Agricultural University, Pune has approved the proposal to start a Post Graduate Degree course in Agriculture at Sharad Chandra Pawar College of Agriculture, Baramati, Pune.
