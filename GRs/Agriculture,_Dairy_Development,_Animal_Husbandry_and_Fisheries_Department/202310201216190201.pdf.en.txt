The proposal-
Various schemes are being implemented by the government to increase the income of the farmers. As a part of this, some farmers are increasing their production and income by using innovative activities/ technologies/ farming methods. It is necessary to keep the example of such farmers in front of other farmers, the universities should check and update this technology on scientific tests and the agriculture department should take up extension work to make use of these innovative initiatives/ technologies/ farming methods to other farmers and inspire others through such innovative farmers.
Various means of communication are effectively used for this. The policy decisions taken by the government for agriculture, the various schemes of the government, the progress achieved at the government level and at the field level, important government decisions and the schemes implemented by the government for the benefit of farmers also need to reach the farmers quickly and effectively. The campaign will be carried out through print, print, electronic, electronic, and electronic media, including newspapers, magazines, newspapers, magazines, newspapers, magazines, newspapers, magazines, newspapers, magazines, radio, radio, and television.
