The Proposal:
As per the provisions of Section 12 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research has been empowered to grant permission to the universities to start any new faculty, course or discipline or department, as well as to start additional elective subjects for any course. Further, the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, under Sections (40) to (44) of the Act, empowers the Agricultural University to grant affiliation and withdraw affiliation.
