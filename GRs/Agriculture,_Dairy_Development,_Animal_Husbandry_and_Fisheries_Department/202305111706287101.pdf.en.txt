Preamble: The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and the Commissionerate of Agriculture and its subordinate offices. The Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (DADF) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture carry out various activities under the e-governance programme. Under this scheme, a fixed amount is sanctioned by the state government every year and the sanctioned amount is distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, according to the need.
The e-Governance programme is a part of the scheme. A total amount of Rs.25 crore has been budgeted for the scheme (3451 2782) for the financial year 2023-24. 1 As per the circular, 70% of the budgetary allocation for the year 2023-24 for essential and programme expenditure i.e. Rs. 17.50 crore has been made available to the Department for State e-Governance Programme for the first nine months. The matter of disbursement of Rs. 99.40 lakh was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The State e-Governance Programme is being implemented to computerize the Agriculture and Padum Departments, Ministries and the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune and various subordinate offices.
