The Proposal:
The Controller, Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani has issued the above Reference No. Proposal for disbursement of funds under Section 3 has been submitted. Department of Finance As per the Government decision dated 17.03.2022, the allocation of funds for the year 2021-22 has been approved within the limit of 100% of the revised budgetary allocation for the year 2021-22. Accordingly, the government had decided to release the funds.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, the monthly salary dues under Dr. Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani, for the financial year 2021-22 have been paid as per the Account Head, Demand No. D-3, 2415 Agricultural Research and Education, 01 Crop Conservation, 120 Assistance to other Institutions, (00) (09) Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University for Assistant Grant (Programme) (24150105)," 36-Assistant Grants (Salary)" Rs. The Government is approving the release and expenditure of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only) for the financial year 2021-22.
2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
The grants sanctioned to the Universities as above are as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and as per the extant Government Orders and prescribed procedures, the competent authority may, in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1991, direct the concerned authorities to disburse such grants to the Universities.
