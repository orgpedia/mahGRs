Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2020 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the year 2020 for 6 crops namely Orange, Mango, Pomegranate, Guava, Chikoo and Lemon (Mrig Bahar) in the district by holding the revenue board component.
