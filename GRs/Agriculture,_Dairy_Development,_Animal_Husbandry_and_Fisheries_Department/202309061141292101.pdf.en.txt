It was approved by the Government on 30th May, 2023. Plan this action. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 05.07.2023.
For the year 2023-24, the Central Government has released a total Central share of Rs. 5059.59000 lakh as first instalment for foodgrains crops under Food and Nutrition Security for three categories. It is scheduled to be delivered on June 21, 2023. Out of which, the Government is deciding to disburse a total amount of Rs. 995.05000 lakh under the Food and Nutrition Security for Scheduled Castes category under the Central share of Rs. 597.03000 lakh and Rs. 398.02000 lakh under the State share, as demanded by the Commissionerate of Agriculture.
