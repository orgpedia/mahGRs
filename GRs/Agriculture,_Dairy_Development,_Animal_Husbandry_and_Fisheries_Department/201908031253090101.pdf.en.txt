Commissioner (Welfare of Persons with Disabilities), State of Maharashtra, Pune vide letter No. A/ c-7/ Grievance Redressal/ Appointment of Officer/ C. 20/2017-18/ 22, Dt. 20.06.2017 Preamble: As per the provisions of Section 23 (2) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in pursuance of Section 20 of the said Act, it is necessary to appoint a Grievance Redressal Officer in every establishment of the Government to redress service related problems arising out of disability of the concerned officers/ employees while in government service. The Commissioner (Disabled Welfare), Maharashtra State, Pune, has issued instructions to appoint a Group-A officer as Grievance Redressal Officer for the purpose. 2 vide letter dated 20.06.2017 to all the Ministries/ Administrative Departments. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Grievance Redressal Officer at the department/ district level office of the Agriculture Commissionerate and the subordinate office of the Agriculture Commissionerate was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: The following officers of Group-A are being appointed as Grievance Redressal Officers in the Regional Office under the Commissionerate of Agriculture and the Commissionerate of Agriculture to resolve the service related problems of the officers/ employees in government service.
