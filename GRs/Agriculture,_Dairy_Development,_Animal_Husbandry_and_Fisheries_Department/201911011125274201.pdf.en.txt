Preamble: Government servants and their family members will be entitled to reimbursement of medical expenses in respect of 27 cases of casualities and 5 cases of serious ailments in private hospitals, subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961. Commissioner (Agriculture) as Head of the Department has the power to approve reimbursement within the ceiling limit of Rs. 3 lakh. Proposals for reimbursement of medical expenses above this limit are submitted to the Department of Agriculture, Mantralaya, Mumbai for approval. Due to the large number of payments received from the 8 Divisional Joint Director of Agriculture in the Agriculture Department to the Office of the Commissioner (Agriculture), the medical payments received from the Regional Office were taking a long time to be approved. As a result, it has been observed that the concerned officers/ employees are facing delay in receiving the compensation amount. As per the Government Decision dated 17th November, 2018, a sum of Rs. The Joint Director of Agriculture concerned has been empowered to approve reimbursement of medical expenses up to a limit of Rs.1 lakh. After that, the Department of Public Health No. 3 Partial changes have been made in the financial powers under the government decision here.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: To approve reimbursement of medical expenses of subordinate officers/ employees of the Commissionerate of Agriculture subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and subsequent amendments made from time to time.
