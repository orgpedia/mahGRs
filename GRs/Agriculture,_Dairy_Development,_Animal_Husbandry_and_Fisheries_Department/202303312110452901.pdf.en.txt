2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly implemented.
(2) The funds allocated by the Agricultural Universities under the scheme and subject-wise allocation should be spent only for the permissible aspects of the scheme concerned.
(iii) If the amount disbursed is lying unspent/ unutilized for any reason, the amount shall not be diverted or spent for any other group/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(4) The schemes under which the period of the scheme has expired shall not be continued after the relevant period at the cost of the schemes without obtaining the approval of the Government.
(5) The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the financial year concerned, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula, and how the expenditure is being made in accordance with the prescribed orders/ procedures. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
6) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
