GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the above reference letter, the Commissioner, Dairying, on the request made by the office of the Collector and District Magistrate, Raigad, has decided to close the Government Milk Scheme, Mahad at 2.57.46 ha. A proposal has been submitted to the government to make these areas available to the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) for setting up permanent base camps. While presenting the proposal, the Commissioner, Dairy, has mentioned that since milk collection in the Government Milk Scheme, Mahad jurisdiction is not possible in the future, the building and the alternative land at the said place will not be used.
