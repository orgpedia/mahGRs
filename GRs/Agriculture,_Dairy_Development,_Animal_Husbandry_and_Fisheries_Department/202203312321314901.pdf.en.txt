Preamble: Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri A proposal for budgetary allocation of Rs. 1600.00 lakh has been received by the Government vide Reference No. 2. The proposal was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, as per the decision of the Government vide Reference No. 1 vide revised provision available under" Demand No.D-3, 2401, Crop Improvement (2401 9225), 33 Financial Assistance, for Centrally Sponsored National Agriculture Development Scheme," Mahatma Phule Krishi Vishwavidyalaya, Rahuri Vishwavidyalaya has been sanctioned 01- Crop Improvement, (00) (06) Subsidiary Grants (Programme) 2415 0072, 35- Capital Grants to Mahatma Phule Krishi Vishwavidyalaya for the financial year 2021-22 under the sub-heading of Rs. 16,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixteen Crores only) has been made available by reallocation. The following are being approved for disbursement of grants made available through this reallocation:
Government Resolution: Rs. The Government is approving the release of grant for the scheme amounting to Rs. 16,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixteen Crores only) subject to the terms and conditions mentioned below and as per Statement A attached with the Government Resolution.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities as above shall be spent within the prescribed limits after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991 and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
(2) The grant shall not be credited to the self-financed account/ PLA bank account under any circumstances.
3) The subsidy for the scheme sanctioned by the above reallocation should be spent in the financial year 2021-22 itself.
