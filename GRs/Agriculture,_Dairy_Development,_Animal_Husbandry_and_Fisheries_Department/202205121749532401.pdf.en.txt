10.The concerned District Superintending Agriculture Officers/ Taluk Agriculture Officers should select the beneficiaries for the campaign and compile the list of beneficiaries at the Taluk level for implementing the programmes covered under the campaign. In this list, the information of the farmer's name, village, survey/ group number, mobile/ telephone number, bank account number, Aadhaar number, etc. should be collected. Similarly, the District Superintending Agriculture Officer concerned will be responsible for making the list of beneficiaries selected under the campaign available on the website of the Agriculture Department within 15 days from the receipt of funds.
11. As per the list of beneficiaries, the agricultural assistants of the respective villages should physically inspect the program undertaken on the farm of all the beneficiary farmers and they should submit their report to the District Superintending Agriculture Officer through the Taluk Agriculture Officer. Similarly, as soon as the reports of the agricultural assistants are received, the Director (Extension and Training) shall prescribe in the guidelines the appropriate scale of supervisory inspection of the works implemented under the said programme.
