The Proposal:
Shirdi in March 2023. It is proposed to organize a state level animal and bird exhibition at Rahata, G. Ahmednagar. For this exhibition, it is proposed to set up stalls of the best animals and birds of the state and outside the state as well as the necessary machines, equipment, medicines and products related to animal husbandry for their scientific rearing.
The exhibition will focus on topics such as importance of indigenous cow conservation, genetic improvement of indigenous cow breeds, providing self-employment opportunities to educated unemployed through animal husbandry and thereby strengthening rural economy, promotion of milk, meat and egg production, reducing cost of production by increasing production capacity of animals, use of sex-determined semen in artificial insemination programme, introduction of manure production, Murgas/ Hydroponics/ Azolla technology.
