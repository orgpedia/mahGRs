After carrying out the necessary regular inspection, the Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) cadre has been appointed. The Government has decided to publish the seniority list as on 01.01.2022.
Accordingly, the Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) Cadres of Dy. The Interim Seniority as on 01.01.2022 is being published as per the Annexure attached.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) is requested to bring the said temporary seniority to the notice of all concerned officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) and place a copy of their signature on such intimation in the records. Also, if there is any objection/ error regarding this temporary seniority, the concerned should submit a statement within fifteen days.
Any objections received after that will not be considered. The facts of the representation received should be checked from the available records of the concerned and the representations should be sent to the government with detailed feedback. If no objection/ information is received within the prescribed period, the government should be informed about the publication of the final seniority considering that no one has any objection to this temporary seniority.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers of the said seniority shall be considered for determining the seniority of the Agricultural Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) cadre only as per the Government Notification, General Administration Department, dated 21/06/2021.
