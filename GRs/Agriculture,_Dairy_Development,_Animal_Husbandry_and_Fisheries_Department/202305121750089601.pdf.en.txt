2. The transferred officers of the above table shall report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the transfer order and shall be relieved forthwith by the concerned Head of Office. Local arrangements should be made to hand over the additional charge of the post vacated by the transfer to other officers nearby. The concerned officers, after appearing in the transferred post, should submit their joining report along with the transfer certificate to the government without delay through the Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune in the prescribed manner.
Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Pune is informed that if the concerned officer does not join within the stipulated period or if the action of relieving/ joining him by the head of the office is not done immediately, disciplinary action should be proposed against him and such report should be submitted to the government immediately. The action taken report in this regard should be submitted to the e-mail mansing. patil@nic. in.
