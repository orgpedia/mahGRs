Preamble: In accordance with the provisions of sub-section (2) of section 73AA of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, the Department of Co-operative Marketing and Textiles has issued the following orders: 06/04/2016 and D. Notifications have been issued under 14/03/2017. The Department of Cooperation also referred to Reference No. 2. The Ordinance has been issued on 8/6/2018. According to the notification and the Ordinance, in case of co-operative societies which are not aided by the Government in the form of shares, loans, guarantees, grants, government land or in any other form, whether in cash or kind, except for housing societies supported by the Government, a person not below the rank of Assistant Registrar, Co-operative Societies, may be appointed as a Director in the Board of Directors and a private person having experience in the functioning of the co-operative societies of the type in which the co-operative society is situated may be appointed as a Director appointed by the Government.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, Shri. Jalgaon Ta. Baramati G. Pune having fulfilled the eligibility criteria of educational qualification and experience, their Baramati Taluka Cooperative Milk Producers Union Limited. The matter of appointing a government-appointed director on Baramati was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Baramati Taluka Cooperative Milk Producers Union Limited. Dadaso Prakash Kokane Ra. appointed as the Director in the Board of Directors of Baramati Sangha. Jalgaon (C.P.) Baramati district Pune is being appointed.
