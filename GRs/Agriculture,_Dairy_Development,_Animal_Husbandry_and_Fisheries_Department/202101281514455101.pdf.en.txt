As per the proposal submitted under letter (6), the matter of disbursement of remaining funds under the said scheme was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to implement the Integrated Pest-Disease Survey and Advisory Scheme (CropSAP) for the management of pests and diseases on major crops such as soybean, cotton, rice, tur, gram, maize, sorghum and sugarcane, as well as fruits mango, pomegranate, banana, mango, orange and chikoo. A sum of Rs. 9.0 crore has been sanctioned.
