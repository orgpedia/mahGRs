Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State Government is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2019-20 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Crop Insurance Scheme 2019-20 has been implemented for 6 crops namely Orange, Moong, Pomegranate, Guava, Chikoo and Lemon (Mrig Bahar) in the district by holding the revenue board component.
Reference No. 1 for re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2019-20 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 2. The Commissionerate of Agriculture has submitted the demand for State share subsidy as demanded by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for the 2019-20 season. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: Considering the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate under the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2019-20 (Mrig Bahar), the amount to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India is Rs. 58,41,13, 934/- is being approved by the Government as follows:
