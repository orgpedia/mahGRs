Preamble: In the meeting of the Executive Council of Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, subject no. 8, Resolution No. 05/ 323 C.P.B. As on 06.12.2018, Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Agriculture College, Kolhapur is situated at 1.14 of S.No.604, off Shivaji University to Sambhajinagar road. It has been agreed to transfer this land for the construction of an administrative building for the offices of the Agriculture Department of the Government of Maharashtra. Further, the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune in its 97th Meeting held on 23.10.2018 (Subject No. The proposal has been approved under Resolution No. 53/97/2018.
The proviso to Section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983 provides that the leasing, sale or transfer of immovable property belonging to an agricultural university can be done with the prior consent of the State Government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision taken by the Executive Council of Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri and Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune in its 97th meeting held on 23.10.2018, the Associate Dean, Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Agricultural College, under the aegis of Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri, situated at S.No. 1.14 of 604, Shivaji University to Sambhajinagar Road, Kolhapur. Approval is being given to transfer this land for the construction of an administrative building for the Agriculture Office of the Government of Maharashtra.
Terms and conditions
(1) The land shall be used for the purpose for which it was transferred and the land shall not be transferred by the Department of Agriculture to any other person and shall not create any interest, right or interest of third parties.
