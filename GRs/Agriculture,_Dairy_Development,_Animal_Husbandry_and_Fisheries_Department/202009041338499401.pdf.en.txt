Introduction: A total of 248 temporary posts in the Department of Agriculture for the year 2020-21 have been approved by Government Decision No. Tabike 2019/ PR No. 58/13 A, D. It was approved to continue till 31.8.20 as on 3 rd March 2020.
The Department of Finance, vide Government Resolution No. Designation-2016/ P.S. 8/16/ G.P.C. dated 31st August, 2020 has been empowered to extend the existing temporary posts in the administrative departments included in their rolls after 31.8.2020, as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the continuation of these temporary posts from 1.9.2020 to 28.2.2021 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner of Agriculture, No. On the request of the letter here, 248 temporary posts in the scheme of Taluka Bijgunan Region (2401 0261), (excluding the posts lying vacant for more than 6 months), the following temporary posts are being approved to be continued from the date 1.9.2020 to the date 28.2.2021.
