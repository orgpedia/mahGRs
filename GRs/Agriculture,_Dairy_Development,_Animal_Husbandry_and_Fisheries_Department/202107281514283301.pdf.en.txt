Government Resolution No. 0118/ No. 32/14-A
Government's decision:
In 2018-19, the State received Rs. 508.02 crore under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) in the ratio of 60:40 between Central and State funding.
Out of this, Rs. 406.75 crore was released for the general category. However, a total of Rs. 32.36 crore was available with the Commissioner (Agriculture) for re-distribution out of the dedicated funds of Rs. 0.80 crore and the unallocated funds of Rs. 31.56 crore by the implementing agencies as per the expenditure likely to be incurred during the prescribed period. Out of which, the Government has decided to reallocate Rs. 18.63 crore in Reference No. 9 was approved and the remaining Rs. 13.73 crore was yet to be approved for reallocation.
Similarly, out of Rs. 56.51 crore allocated for Scheduled Castes category (Refer No. 5& 6) Rs. 15.37 crore dedicated by the implementing agencies as per the expenditure likely to be incurred during the period and Rs. 44.76 crore released for Scheduled Tribes (Refer No. 7& 8) Out of the fund, Rs. 14.27 crore has been dedicated by the implementing agencies. 38.85 crore was available with the Commissioner (Agriculture) for re-distribution.
National Agriculture Development Scheme. As per letter dated 15/02/2019 (Refer No. 10) As per the instructions, the Commissioner (Agriculture) has released the remaining amount of Rs. 13.73 crore to General Category, Rs. 15.37 crore to Scheduled Castes and Rs. 14.27 crore to Scheduled Tribes. 38.85 crore for other projects sanctioned under the scheme. 11 to 23 have been reallocated. This decision has been approved by the government. The category-wise/ project-wise details of the reallocated funds are given in Annexure-A, Annexure-B and Annexure-C respectively.
