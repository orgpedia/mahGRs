2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. the Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 12.04.2023 should be strictly implemented.
The funds allocated by the Agricultural Universities under the scheme/ non-scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the permissible items in the respective mandatory scheme.
(3) If the amount disbursed remains unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for any other group/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(4) Schemes/ Non-schemes whose period has expired shall not be continued beyond the relevant period at the cost of the scheme without obtaining approval of the Government.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula, and how the expenditure is being made in accordance with the prescribed order/ procedure. Also, the amount of unutilized funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
The government has approved the agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Also, action should be taken on the implementation of schemes to increase revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the universities are governed by the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code 1991.
