Preamble- Registration of Groundwater Fisheries Cooperative Societies/ Associations as per norms. In order to address the provisions of the amended Government Order issued under 03.07.2019, such as no fresh registration of a body on a lake/ water body where there is a registered body, prohibition of registration of more than one body on a newly transferred lake/ water body. Removing the shortcomings of the government decision of 03.07.2019, the government has decided to provide opportunities to the weaker sections of traditional Bhoi, Kahar, Dhiwar, Koli fishermen through fisheries from the water bodies created by the government and to include them in the mainstream. The Government Decision dated 12/05/2023 was issued.
However, the fishermen's organizations have demanded that there should be only one institution on one lake, there should be no other institution, and outside institutions within a radius of 10 km should not be registered on the lake. The reason It was requested to consider the Government decision dated 12/05/2023 to stay the said Government decision and to have a comprehensive new policy on the registration of Ground Water Fisheries Cooperative Societies and Federations. comply with this request. Read the Government Resolution issued under 12/05/2023 No. 2 Here the decision of the Namud Government has been postponed.
In response to the demand made by the fishermen's organizations, the government was considering the formation of a committee of representatives from the fishermen's organizations to submit a comprehensive new policy to the government for the registration of groundwater fisheries cooperative societies.
Government Decision- Through this government decision, the government is approving the formation of a committee of representatives from fishermen's organizations to submit to the government a new policy on the registration of groundwater fisheries cooperative societies and associations.
