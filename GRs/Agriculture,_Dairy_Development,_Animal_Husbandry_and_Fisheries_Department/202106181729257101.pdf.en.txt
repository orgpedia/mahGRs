The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government Resolution, out of 148 posts in the establishment of Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur under the Department of Agriculture and Padum, and in the internal colleges, 141 posts have been earmarked for the disabled. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that in respect of the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, the posts in the list published by the Central Government whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service, even if the designations of such posts are different, the posts in the State Government Service will be ensured for the disabled.
