Introduction: Till 2014-15, Micro Irrigation Scheme was being implemented under the Centrally Sponsored National Mission on Micro Irrigation. Since 2015-16, Micro Irrigation Scheme is being implemented under Per Drop More Crop (Micro Irrigation) component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana. As per the guidelines of National Mission on Micro Irrigation, the benefit of re-subsidy for micro irrigation was admissible after a period of 10 years for the area which had availed subsidy for micro irrigation. However, as per the guidelines of Per Drop More Crop (Micro Irrigation) component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana, the benefit of subsidy is allowed after a period of 7 years. Because, in the context. The Government has taken the following decision in view of the proposal under consideration of the Government to cancel the condition of Schedule III (VIII) of the Government Decision dated 30th August, 2019 and extend the benefit of subsidy for micro-irrigation to the same area after the completion of 7 years.
Government's decision:
1. under reference. The provisions of Article 3 (VIII) are hereby repealed by the Government Decision of 30 August 2019.
