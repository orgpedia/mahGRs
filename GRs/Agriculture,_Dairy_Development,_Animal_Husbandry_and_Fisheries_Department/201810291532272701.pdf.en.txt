Project Director, Vidarbha and Marathwada Milk Development Project, Nagpur vide letter No. Pras/ (V. 187/2018, Nagpur. dated 27.09.2018.
The Proposal:
The scope of various ongoing works under the Vidarbha and Marathwada Dairy Development Project is increasing day by day. Dr Satish Raju, Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry, Bhandara, who has earlier served as Project Director, Vidarbha and Marathwada Dairy Development Project. Therefore, in view of his experience in various works being carried out in the project, and in order to make the work of the project more fast and effective, the Government is issuing a decision to assign additional charge of the post of Additional Project Director, Vidarbha and Marathwada Dairy Development Project as follows.
Government's decision:
Additional Project Director, Vidarbha and Marathwada Dairy Development Project, Dr. Satish Raju, Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry, Bhandara is being given additional charge of this post. Since the post of Additional Project Director has not been sanctioned under the said project, the salary and allowances of Dr. Raju should be transferred to the post in which he is presently working. Also, no additional allowances or special pay will be payable to the Additional Project Director on account of his being entrusted with the additional charge of the post.
2. Dr. Raju, Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry, Bhandara, to take additional charge of the post of Additional Project Director, Vidarbha and Marathwada Dairy Development Project, immediately and submit a compliance report to the Government.
