Introduction: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Soil Health Management (SHM) under National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) is being implemented since 2014-15. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between Centre and State since 2015-16. Under this scheme, components like setting up of mobile/ permanent soil testing laboratory, strengthening of government soil testing laboratories, strengthening of fertilizer control testing laboratories etc. are implemented.
