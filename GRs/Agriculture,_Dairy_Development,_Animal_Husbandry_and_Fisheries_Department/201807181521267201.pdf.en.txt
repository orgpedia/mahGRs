Government's decision:
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) Kharif Season 2017 is being implemented by Agriculture Insurance Company of India in District Cluster No. 2 and 5 have been implemented. Agriculture Insurance Company of India Ltd. Earlier, the first and second instalments of Rs. 409,90,50, 192 and Rs. 151,20,87, 758 respectively. 4 and 5 have been issued by the Government. Now, for Kharif season 2017, the State share of crop insurance premium subsidy is Rs. 17,40,15269/- has been submitted by Agriculture Insurance Company of India. Accordingly, the State Government has allocated Rs. 17,40,15269/- has been requested by the Commissionerate of Agriculture to be spent from the funds already made available by the State Government to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India.
Crop insurance compensation for kharif 2017 could not be disbursed due to excess payment by some districts. These amounts are left with the Agriculture Insurance Company of India as savings.
The remaining Rs. 30,91,93, 331/- out of which the State Government's share towards crop insurance premium subsidy is Rs. 17,40,15, 269/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores Forty Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Two Hundred and Seventy Seven only) to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India Ltd. The expenditure is being approved by the government under the current government decision.
