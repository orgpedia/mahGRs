The Proposal:
The Government of India has decided to implement the Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIH) from 1st April, 2014 by converging all the schemes of National Horticulture Mission (NHM), Horticulture Mission for North Eastern and Himalayan States (HMNEH), National Horticulture Board (NHB), Coconut Development Board (CDB) and Central Institute of Horticulture (CIH). This scheme is for the development of fruits, vegetables, tubers, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew, cocoa and bamboo products. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between Centre and State since 2015-16.
Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State to be implemented in 2022-23 with an outlay of Rs. The Annual Action Plan for an amount of Rs. 199,33,97, 000/- has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution No. 2. The Central Government, vide letter No. 3 to the Government of India, has released Central Share of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,89,00, 000/- has been disbursed to the State Government. 1,26,00, 000/- amounting to Rs. 3,15,00, 000/- has been released by the Tribal Development Department vide Reference No. 5 has been transferred to the Department of Agriculture by the government decision. The matter of disbursement of the said fund to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:

Under Krishi Unnati Yojana- Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (MIDH), the Centre has allocated Rs. 1,89,00, 000/- to the Fund and the corresponding State share of Rs. 1,26,00, 000/- amounting to Rs. 3,15,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Fifteen Lakhs only) to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune.
The budgetary funds available for this scheme should be spent under the following head of accounts for the Scheduled Tribes as specified by the Central Government.

The Accountant, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune is being declared as the Procurement and Disbursement Officer and the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune as the Controlling Officer for the withdrawal of funds from the treasury.
