In order to ensure that the eligible graduates of Mumbai and Konkan divisions, as well as the eligible teachers of Mumbai and Nashik divisions, are enrolled in large numbers as graduate/ teacher voters in the respective divisions for voting in the aforesaid biennial elections, the Agriculture Universities and affiliated colleges of Mumbai, Konkan and Nashik divisions under the Department of Agriculture have been requested to carry out the following campaign. The concerned Divisional Commissioner is the Electoral Registration Officer for these elections, while the District Collector is the Assistant Electoral Registration Officer.
Agricultural Universities and colleges under them in Mumbai and Konkan region should carry out the campaign as follows:

(a) The Electoral College:
As the voter list is being prepared for the Graduate Legislative Council constituency in Mumbai and Konkan division, the eligible graduate college teachers of that division (Qualifications D. Candidates who have completed at least 3 years of graduation before 1.11.2023) are requested to register their names in the electoral rolls of the respective Graduates Constituency. It should also be encouraged.
Eligible graduate teachers of colleges in Mumbai and Konkan region will have to fill up Form 18 (Form 18) to register their names in the electoral roll of the respective Graduate Legislative Council Constituency. These forms will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer for this election), concerned Collector (Electoral Registration Officer for this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner as designated by the Divisional Commissioner, as well as in the office of the Tehsildar. Similarly, the format is also available on the website of the Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra https:// ceo.maharashtra.gov.in at Downloads/ DownloadForms/ Form- 18.pdf. The form can be downloaded from the website. (There is no option to register online).
The duly filled Form 18 (FORM 18), along with the required proof of educational qualification, should be submitted to the above mentioned concerned office. If the application is submitted in person, the concerned designated officer will have to show the original degree certificate/ mark sheet or other prescribed documents. In case of sending Sample 18 by post, the proof/ documents indicating the educational qualification concerned must be attested by the Designated Officer/ Assistant Designated Officer/ Gazetted Officer/ Notary Public of the concerned district.
(b) Teachers' Constituencies: Since the electoral rolls for the Teachers' Legislative Council constituencies are being prepared in the Mumbai and Nashik divisions, the State Government may, with the approval of the Election Commission, allot seats in educational institutions not lower than secondary schools, as determined under Section 27 (3) (b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1950, in that division. Teachers who have been working as teachers for at least 3 years in the six years preceding the qualifying date i.e. 1.11.2023 shall be called upon to register their names in the electoral rolls of the respective Teachers Constituencies. It should also be encouraged.
The eligible teachers of Mumbai and Nashik divisions will have to fill Form 19 (FORM 19) along with the certificate issued by the head of the concerned educational institution in the prescribed form to register their names in the electoral roll of the concerned Teachers Legislative Council Constituency.
