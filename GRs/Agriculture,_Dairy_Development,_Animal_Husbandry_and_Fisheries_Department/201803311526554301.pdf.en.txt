GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Supplementary Appropriation Bill passed in the winter session of the Maharashtra Legislature in December, 2017 was passed by the House. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published as the Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2017 (Maharashtra Act No.62 of 2017) in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV, dated 21st December, 2017. Under the sub-heading" 01- Crop Conservation (2415 0043)" for the renovation/ strengthening of Agricultural Colleges and Research Centre under Panjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola, the total amount of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) to Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola for the renovation/ repair of hostels/ repairs, renovation/ repair of university residences, renovation/ repair of office buildings, area development and conservation, electricity renovation/ repair, water supply pipelines, etc. 500.00 lakhs out of the sanctioned provision of Supplementary Demand within the limit of 90% of Rs. 4,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crore Fifty Lakh only) is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions
(1) The funds shall be used for the purposes set out in Annexure A.
The guidelines and prevailing norms issued earlier by the Planning and Finance Department as well as the circulars of the Finance Department dated April 18, 2017, June 30, 2017, and August 8, 2017, as well as the instructions given from time to time, should be strictly followed while spending these funds.
(iii) If any of the sub-account, group, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
4) The grant should be used primarily to meet the expenses of the project. The rest of the grant should be spent in the order of certain amendments, general and special amendments.
9) Each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities shall be spent within the prescribed limits as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Accounts Code, 1991, and after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
7) Do not spend money on illegal schemes, posts and affairs.
