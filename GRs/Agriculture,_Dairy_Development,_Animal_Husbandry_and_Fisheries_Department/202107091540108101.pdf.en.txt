Preamble: Under the Tribal Component Programme, with the objective of reducing malnutrition in the tribal areas of 14 districts of the State and to provide nutritious food to the tribal brothers and sisters, the scheme of" Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation in the Neighbourhood of Tribal Families" is being implemented in the State since 2003-04 to promote the cultivation of nutritious vegetables and fruits in the neighbourhood of the tribal families. In 2021-22, Rs. A sum of Rs. 30.00 lakh has been allocated for the project. In 2021-22, Rs. A proposal of Rs. 29.99808 lakh has been submitted by the Director (Horticulture) to the Government. Accordingly, the scheme has been extended to the State in 2021-22. 30.00 lakh within the limit of 60% of the fund i.e. Rs. Government's decision:
The scheme for the cultivation of fruits and vegetables in the backyards of tribal families in 14 districts of the state- Thane, Palghar, Pune, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Jalgaon, Ahmednagar, Nanded, Yavatmal, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Amravati and Gondia- has been implemented in 2021-22. 30.00 lakh within the limit of 60% of the fund i.e. Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for the programme with a corpus of Rs. 18.00 lakhs (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs only).
The Director (Horticulture), Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune shall complete the pending liabilities for the year 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 before the date of the award. After that, it should be ensured to implement the program within the limits of the remaining funds.
Since the problem of malnutrition is widespread in tribal families, as a special matter, the report of expenditure from the provisions made available, the information on account head-wise/ sub-head-wise and material objectives achieved, the certificate of utilization of funds, the concerned field officer should send to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik, the concerned Additional Commissioner, Tribal Development, Tribal Development Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
The Controlling Officer and Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area as well as in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area shall be recorded separately. So that the government can keep an eye on the expenditure on schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and outside the tribal sub-plan area.
Individual benefit schemes in tribal applications should be taken up from the provision made under the Tribal Component Programme and only individual benefit schemes should be taken up in areas outside the tribal sub-plan area.
