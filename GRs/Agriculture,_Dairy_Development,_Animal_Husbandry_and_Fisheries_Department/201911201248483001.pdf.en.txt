Introduction: Maharashtra is a leading state in horticulture. This includes mainly citrus fruits. In Vidarbha, the area under orange and mango is the highest in the state. In order to increase the productivity and quality of oranges considering the various problems faced by the farmers in Vidarbha, administrative approval has been given to establish" Citrus Estate" in Nagpur, Amravati and Wardha districts of the state under the government decision mentioned at No. 1 here.
The main objectives of the scheme are to move towards achieving the objectives in an organized manner by forming groups of primary producing farmers of orange, to disseminate new technologies through training and extension to orange farmers, to promote orange fruit processing, packaging, storage, marketing, processing, transportation and export, to guide farmers on technical aspects related to orange export, to create domestic and international export marketing chains, to develop orange growing farmers of Vidarbha, etc. The Finance Department has agreed to disburse funds to the extent of₹ 9.00 crore (₹ 9 crore only) out of the₹ 15.00 crore funds sanctioned through the fresh proposal for the implementation of the scheme" Citrus Estate" set up in Nagpur, Amravati and Wardha districts of the State. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval and disbursement of funds to the Commissioner (Agriculture) for the implementation of the scheme in 2019-20 was under the consideration of the Government.
