Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the state is implementing a redesigned climate-based Fruit Insurance Scheme on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production on a large scale. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide crop compensation to the farmers, the restructured weather-based crop insurance scheme has been implemented in the state for the three years 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24 for 8 crops of orange, mango, pomegranate, chikoo, guava, lemon, betel nut and grapes (C) (Mrig Bahar) in 26 districts by the Revenue Department.
Reference No. 1 for re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme Mrig Bahar under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2021 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 1. The Commissionerate has issued Reference No. 8 of the State share of Rs. 81,398/- was under consideration of the Government.
