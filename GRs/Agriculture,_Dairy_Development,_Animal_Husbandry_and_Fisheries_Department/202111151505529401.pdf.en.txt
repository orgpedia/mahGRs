The memo:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter, a proposal for grant of administrative approval for construction of Krishi Bhawan building at Nashik has been received.
The various offices of the Nashik-headquartered Agriculture Department are scattered at different places.
All offices must be located in the same building. Considering the public relations of the agriculture department and the dispersal of various offices, it is not convenient from the administrative point of view and from the point of view of the farmers. Also, you have to pay a lot of rent for the offices that are functioning in rented premises. Since different activities are being carried out for the farmers by the Agriculture Department, it is necessary to build a state-of-the-art agricultural complex under one roof.
There is space available at Shingada Lake, Gadkari Chowk, Nashik under the control of the Agriculture Department.
3. Shingada Lake, Gadkari Chowk, Nasik at Town Geology No. The Agriculture Department has an area of 7203.90 sq.m. in 210 and it is proposed to construct a new building for the convenience of farmers as well as various offices and laboratories of the Agriculture Department.
The proposed building will have offices of Joint Director of Agriculture, Nashik Division, Nashik, Taluk Agriculture Officer, Nashik, Directorate of Food Processing, Soil Testing Laboratory, Fertilizer Control Laboratory, Meeting Hall, Computer Room and Security Guard Cabin.
The Plan for the construction of the proposed building at Krishi Bhawan, Nashik along with the facilities required for the proposed building and the Schedule based Budget (Form-A) for the year 2021-22 has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik.
