The proposal-
The scheme is being implemented under the National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology (NMAET). The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing pattern between the Centre and the State governments. The total cost of the project is Rs 2020-21 crore. The State Action Plan (SEWP) amounting to Rs. 127.28 crore has been prepared and submitted to the Government by the Commissionerate of Agriculture for approval under the above reference letters.
Accordingly, under this government decision, the matter of approving the action plan submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate for the implementation of the scheme was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of Scheme of Assistance for Reforms in State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) under Sub Mission on Agriculture Extension of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET) during 2020-21.
The total outlay for the year 2020-21 is Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the State Action Plan for an amount of Rs. 127.28 crore.
