The following terms and conditions should be observed when implementing the scheme.
1) The scheme should be implemented in accordance with the guidelines of the Central Government and the terms and conditions of the release of funds by the Central Government.
2) The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the Central and State Governments along with the progress report as soon as possible by keeping the expenditure accounts in order.
The physical and financial progress report on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central and State Government by the 5th of every month.
4) The balance sheet and the amount of the audit deposit related to the scheme should be reported by the implementing agency. It should clearly state the unpaid amount at the beginning of the year and the income received by way of interest. So that the amount can be considered in a transparent manner and there will be no goodwill.
5) It should be ensured that no programme beyond the approved programme is implemented under any circumstances.
6) It should be ensured that the funds received for the scheme are spent for the same cadre for which it has been sanctioned.
Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer of the Scheme and Assistant Director, Accounts-1 in his office is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
