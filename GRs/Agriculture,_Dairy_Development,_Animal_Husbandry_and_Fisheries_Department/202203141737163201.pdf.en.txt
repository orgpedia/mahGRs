Government Decision: For the current year 2021-22, (01) (14) Krishi Unnati Yojana-Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana State Share of 40% (2401 B212) for the year 2020-21, Rs. 21.00 lakhs is being disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, State of Maharashtra, Pune subject to the following terms and conditions.
2) The following terms and conditions must be met:
1) The scheme should be implemented in accordance with the guidelines of the Central Government and the terms and conditions of the release of funds by the Central Government.
2) The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the Central and State Governments along with the progress report as soon as possible.
The physical and financial progress reports on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central and State Governments by the 5th of every month.
4) The balance sheet and the amount of the audit deposit related to the scheme should be reported by the implementing agency. It should clearly mention the amount of interest and interest earned at the beginning of the year. So that the amounts can be asked in a transparent manner and there is no ambiguity.
5) Ensure that no programme exceeds the approved programme.
6) The funds of the above scheme should be spent only for the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhists and it should be ensured that the state share is spent in proportion to the central share.
7) Since the economic condition of the state is likely to be very serious due to Corona, the control officer should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds in this distribution.
8) It should be ensured that the funds will be spent in this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy.
(9) The Controlling Officer shall ensure that the funds meant for the Scheduled Castes are spent on the same categories. So that the government can control the expenditure on these schemes.
(3) While spending the said funds, all financial laws/ tender rules and regulations/ procedures/ procedures/ within the limits of financial powers/ P.W.D./ Manual shall be complied with in accordance with the prescribed procedures. As per the V.C. Principle/ as per the directions of the CAG/ as per the prevailing Government Decisions/ Rules/ Circulars/ Provisions, the expenditure should be processed by the implementing agencies as per the Budget and Treasury Rules.
It will be the responsibility of the zonal office to ensure that no government decision/ authority is violated under any circumstances. All the funds that they have disbursed under these government decisions will have to be mandatorily disbursed through PFMS system to all the districts.
