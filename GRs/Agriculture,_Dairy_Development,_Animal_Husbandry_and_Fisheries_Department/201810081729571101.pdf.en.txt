Terms and Conditions
1) The actual cost of the above sanctioned grant, the number of sanctioned posts and the utilization of the disbursed grant should be submitted by the Agricultural University to the Government at the end of every month. The funds disbursed by the Agricultural University under the head/ group/ scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the respective scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as schemes under the schemes.
In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancies or other reasons, the subsidy shall be adjusted in the next month's disbursement. However, the grants made available during 2018-19 will have to be spent as per the approved monthly distribution statement.
(2) If any of the sub-account, block, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
3. The Agriculture Council/ University should thoroughly examine whether the sub-title wise sanctioned schemes and the posts sanctioned under them have been properly grouped as per the revised pattern. No change in this classification should be made without the approval of the government.
(4) The grants and revenues distributed among each group shall be used to meet the expenditure on permissible items within that group and for development activities. Subsidies and revenue income of one group should not be diverted for schemes/ activities of another group. If more grants are allocated for a group scheme, the amount of the excess grant should be taken into account in the subsequent demand for funds.
The grant will be made only for the salary and allowances of the posts sanctioned by the Government which have been filled as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statute, 1990 and the Government has approved the continuation of these posts at non-plan cost.

6) Separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained for each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise.
(7) The schemes/ schemes under which the period of non-plan period has expired shall not be continued after the relevant period at the cost of non-plan/ schemes without obtaining the approval of the Government.

Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
9) While approving some new schemes of the University, the Government has approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme in an adjustable manner. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
10) The government has approved the agricultural university to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the University shall be spent within the prescribed limits as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
12) While making the demand for grants for the year 2018-2019, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed scheme-wise, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month.
Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
13) Don't spend money on illegal schemes, positions and affairs.
The Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
