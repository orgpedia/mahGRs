Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 23.12.2020, the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Enterprises (PMFME) has been approved to be implemented in the State for a period of five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25. For the implementation of this scheme in the state in 2022-23, a plan of Rs. 175.90 crore has been given administrative approval as per the decision of the Government dated 26.07.2022.
Read the Central government's statement here. It has been communicated through e-mail dated 20.04.2022 that for the individual beneficiary/ group beneficiary project component under the scheme, the financial assistance of the State share shall be disbursed first to the Nodal Bank, after which the Central share will be disbursed by the Central Government. The Central Government has now deposited the Central share of Rs. 500.44 lakh in the Consolidated Fund of the State Government as per the letter dated 08.02.2023. in accordance with it

The Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Procurement and Disbursement Officer.
The funds provided by the Central Government under this scheme should be utilized immediately and the nodal agency for the allocation of funds, Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) and the Commissioner (Agriculture) should submit the utilization certificate to the Central and State Government from time to time.
The scheme should be implemented in accordance with the provisions prescribed in the Government Resolution issued here under dated 23.12.2020.
While spending the funds, the guidelines issued by the Planning and Finance Department as well as the guidelines mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and the Financial Rights Rulebook, the prevailing terms and conditions and the instructions given from time to time should be strictly followed.
This Government Decision is being issued as per the consent of the Finance Department, Circular No: Finance-2022/ PR No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 04.04.2022 and dated 08.02.2023, as well as the informal reference No. 118/ KA-1431 of the Planning Department, dated 16.03.2023 and informal reference No. 72/ Expenditure-1 of the Finance Division, dated 21.03.2023.
