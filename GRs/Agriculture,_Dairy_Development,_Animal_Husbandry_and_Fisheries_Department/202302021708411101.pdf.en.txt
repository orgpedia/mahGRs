Government Decision: 5 Under the Special State Level Scheme for providing supplementary income to farmers by rearing goats in a partially stationary manner in the State as per the Government decision dated 25.5.2021, the Scheme has been revised to supply 10+1 goats/ sheep group to each beneficiary such as Osmanabadi/ Sangamneri breed of goats or Madgyal/ Deccan breed of sheep or local breed of goats/ sheep/ male sheep to the beneficiaries (under General/ Scheduled Caste Sub Plan/ Tribal Applications). Read on to know how to implement this plan. 7 In a letter dated 25.10.2021, the Commissioner Animal Husbandry has proposed to make certain amendments in the said Government Resolution dated 25.05.2021. The government was contemplating to make necessary changes in this regard.
