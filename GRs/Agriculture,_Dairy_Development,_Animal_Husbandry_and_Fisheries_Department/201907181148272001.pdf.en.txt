In respect of ex-gratia grant and benefit payable to the family of a member of the scheme under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fishery Sciences in the event of his death during the service period.

Introduction: As mentioned above, No. In pursuance of the Government decision dated 01/11/2005 dated 29th September, 2018, the Department of Finance has decided to release an ex-gratia grant of Rs. 10.00 lakh plus accrued amount standing to the credit of the employee, if the employee who is appointed in the service of the Government on or after 01/11/2005 and is a member of the defined contribution pension scheme/ National Pension Scheme dies in service before completion of 10 years of service, to his legal heirs, unless nominated by his nominee. The proposal to extend the benefit of this ex-gratia grant to the members of the scheme under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In view of the above background, the Scheme of Grant-in-Aid Benefit and the provisions therein as permissible under the Government Decision dated 29th September, 2018 are hereby made applicable to the members of the scheme under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences as per the Government Decision.
(3) The decision of the Government, not of the Finance Department. Reference No. 74/19/ SERVICE-4, dated 10/04/2019 is being issued with their concurrence.
