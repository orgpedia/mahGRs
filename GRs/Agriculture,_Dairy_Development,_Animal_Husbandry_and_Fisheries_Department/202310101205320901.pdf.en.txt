Preamble: Administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of National Horticulture Mission under Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State for the year 2023-2024 with a budget outlay of Rs. 21207.25 lakh as per the Government Resolution No. Thereafter, vide letter No. 2, the first instalment of funds for STs has been received from the Central Government for the financial year 2023-2024. In this background, the Central share of the first installment of the Scheduled Tribes and the State share of the fund equivalent to it have been approved by the Tribal Development Department in Reference No. 5 has been transferred to the Department of Agriculture by the government decision here. The funds were to be distributed to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government: The Government has approved to disburse the first instalment of Central share of Rs. 188.50 lakh and corresponding State share of Rs. 125.67 lakh to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune for implementation of National Horticulture Mission in the State during 2023-2024 under Krishi Unnati Yojana- Integrated Horticulture Development Mission.
