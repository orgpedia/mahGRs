As per the provisions of the Government Notification dated 21/06/2021, the officers of the cadre of Joint Director of Agriculture, Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A, have been transferred. The Provisional Seniority Notice dated 01/01/2023 is being published as per Annexure attached.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) shall bring this seniority to the notice of all concerned officers (including officers on leave/ deputation). If the concerned officers have any objections regarding the said seniority, then the concerned officers should submit a representation to the Commissioner (Agriculture) within 15 days. If no objections are received during this period, the seniority will be finalised on the assumption that there are no objections from the concerned officers. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
After examining the objections and suggestions received from the concerned officers, the office of the Agriculture Commissioner should submit a complete proposal of the final seniority list with self-explanatory feedback within 15 days.
