The Proposal:
The Central Government, vide Reference No. In sub-rule 2.1.2 of the Food Safety and Standards (Food additives and food additives) Notification, under sub-rule 2 (b) of head column (4) and (5), revised standards for different categories of milk Minimum Fluid (FAT) and Minimum Non-Fluid Solids (SNF) have been specified. Based on this notification, the government was considering fixing the minimum viscosity (FAT) and minimum non-viscositic solids (SNF) in milk to bring uniformity in the quality recognition of different categories of milk to be collected in the state of Maharashtra.
Government Decision: Under this decision, the Government is fixing the standards of Minimum Fluid (FAT) and Minimum Non-Fluid Solids (SNF) in different categories of milk to be collected in the state of Maharashtra as follows.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The category of milk | Minimum Fat (%) | Minimum SNF (%) |
| 1    | cow's milk           | 3.2             | 8.3             |
| 2    | Cow's milk           | 6.0             | 9.0             |
| 3    | goat and sheep milk  | 3.5             | 9.0             |
