July 1/ 19/344, c. May 3, 2019 Introduction: Former Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Shri. Vasantrao Naik's birth anniversary is celebrated as the birth anniversary of the great leader of the Indian agriculture. The government has decided to celebrate July 1 as Agriculture Day in the state. The decision was taken on June 21, 1989.
Government decision: The government has decided to celebrate July 1 as Agriculture Day in the state. A maximum of Rs. 5000/- (Rs. 5,000 only) for 355 Panchayat Samitis Rs. 17,75,000/- (Rs. 17 lakh only) and Rs. 10,000/- (Rs. 10,000 only) for a total of 34 Zilla Parishads (Rs. 3,40,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Forty Thousand only). 21,15,000/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
Commissioner (Agriculture) Maharashtra State, Pune should give guidelines to the concerned regarding the celebration of Agriculture Day. The expenditure should be based on the guidelines given by the Agriculture Commissioner.
Also, expenses should be spent on screening of films, tea for farmers, small exhibitions at the district council level, etc. and for necessary things like mandaps, harbours, photos, etc.
The expenditure for this purpose should be met from the grants available from the Zilla Parishad Cess Fund. To meet this expenditure, the Chief Executive Officers and Agriculture Officers of all the districts should take action as per the guidelines given by the Commissioner (Agriculture) for spending from the District Cess Fund. The Commissioner (Agriculture), Maharashtra State, Pune will be responsible for the implementation of this programme.
