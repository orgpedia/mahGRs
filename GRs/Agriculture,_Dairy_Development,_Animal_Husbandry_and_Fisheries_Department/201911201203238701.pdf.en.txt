The officers of the corporation/ agriculture department should give instructions to all the farmers. Also, the consent letter of the farmers should be obtained to pay the subsidy amount to the company installing the micro-irrigation set.
8. District Superintending Agriculture Officer should provide the list of all farmers received on e-drip order to the concerned Executive Engineer of the Corporation.
9. The concerned Executive Engineer of the Corporation shall take action to install micro irrigation set from the concerned company in the fields of all the applicant farmers and submit a report to the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer, Yavatmal/ Wardha.
10.After receiving the micro irrigation set up report from the Executive Engineer of the Municipal Board, the District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Yavatmal/ Wardha should get the matter examined by the concerned officers and the necessary amount of subsidy payable should be considered and informed to the Executive Engineer of the Corporation.
11. The Corporation shall take action for payment of subsidy to the concerned company as considered by the District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Yavatmal/ Wardha. After the subsidy amount is paid to the company, all the farmers should be informed through SMS about the payment of the subsidy amount due to them to the company.
12.The Corporation shall submit the utilization certificate of the amount paid to the Municipal Corporation during the financial year to the District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Yavatmal/ Wardha by the end of April of the next financial year.
13.Sadar While allocating the fund, the guidelines issued by the Central Government for the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana- Per Drop More Crop (Micro Irrigation) component and the various criteria prescribed under it should be strictly followed.
14.Small irrigation set shall be mandatorily constructed by a Micro Irrigation Set Manufacturing Company registered with the Commissionerate of Agriculture.
