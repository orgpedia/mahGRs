The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 of the General Administration Department, instructions have been given to create more number of posts for the employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes and transfer their services to the higher post. The administrative departments have also been empowered to create the above number of posts through the Government Decision dated 30/6/2020 and the Corrigendum dated 28/9/2020 of General Administration Department. Therefore, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for a total of 8 employees in the fisheries department.
Government's decision:
However, for those employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category in the Fisheries Department, but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of that category or have submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes, as well as those who have filed a claim in the court against the decision to verify the caste certificate. Under this Government decision, 8 supernumerary posts are being created as shown in the return 1 on the establishment of the office concerned for the employees in cases where the court has not stayed the decision on caste certificate verification.
