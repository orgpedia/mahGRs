the order
Shri Haribhau Madhav Jawale is being appointed as Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research as per the provision of Section 12 (2) (b) of Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983.
The appointment of Shri Haribhau Madhav Jawale as Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall be for a period of 3 years from the date of such appointment or until the pleasure of the State Government, whichever is earlier.
Haribhau Madhav Jawale, Vice President, Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune. The rank of Cabinet Minister is admissible and he is assigned the Finance Department, Government Resolution No. 10.17/ p. No. 96/10/ c. Uh, the. Services will be available as of 13/3/2012.
