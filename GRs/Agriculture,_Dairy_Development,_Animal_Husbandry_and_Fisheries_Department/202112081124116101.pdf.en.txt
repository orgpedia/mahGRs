The Proposal:
As per the Government Resolution, Department of Finance, dated 29th May, 2020, insurance cover/ ex-gratia assistance is admissible to the employees who die due to COVID-19 virus infection while performing COVID-19 related duties.
2. The staff of Mahatma Phule Agriculture University, Rahuri, namely Mr. Machhindra Nivrutti Gagare, Agriculture Assistant, was infected with Corona Virus while performing COVID-19 related duties. He passed away on 10.09.2020.
3. c. Tehsildar cum Incident Commander, Rahuri has certified that Machhindra Nivrutti Gagare, Agriculture Assistant was on preventive duty in view of the pandemic" COVID-19" and was present on duty during the 14 days preceding the date of death.
4. c. The report of the concerned ICMR-authorized private laboratory has stated that the death of fisherman Nivrutti Gagare, agricultural assistant is related to the Covid 19 virus.
5. c. Since the death of Machhindra Nivrutti Gagare, Krishi Sahayak was due to COVID-19, the Registrar, Mahatma Phule Krishi Vishwavidyalaya, Rahuri has requested the Government to sanction ex-gratia grant of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) to the next of kin of the deceased as per the provisions of Government Decision, dated 29.05.2020.
