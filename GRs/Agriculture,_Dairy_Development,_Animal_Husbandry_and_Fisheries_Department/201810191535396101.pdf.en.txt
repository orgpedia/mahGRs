The Department of Finance, vide Government Resolution No. Designation-2016/ P.S. 8/16/ G.P.C., dated 7th September, 2018, has been empowered to extend the date of extension up to 28th February, 2019 for the temporary posts included in their rolls of the Administrative Departments and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the said Review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the continuation of these temporary posts from 1st October, 2018 to 28th February, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
267 temporary posts in Seed, Chemical Fertilizer Control and Pesticide Testing Laboratory, Remaining Degree Testing Laboratory, Biological Pest Testing Laboratory (2401 0305) and 248 temporary posts in Taluk Bijagunan Area (2401 0261), excluding 515 temporary posts vacant for more than 6 months, are being approved to continue from 1st October, 2018 to 28th February, 2019. Form A and Form B are attached.
The expenditure for this post shall be covered under the head of accounts mentioned above and shall be met from the approved budgetary provision for 2018-19.
