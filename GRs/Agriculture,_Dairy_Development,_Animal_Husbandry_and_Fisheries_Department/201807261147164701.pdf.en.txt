Read:- Order of the Commissionerate of Agriculture No. Cr./ Cr.- 1118 (B)/ General Changes/ 2018/738/ Astha-4, Dy. 09/. 06/2018
Government Decision: The general transfers of transferable officers of Maharashtra Agricultural Service Group-B (Junior) cadre have been made as per the order. Some of the officers affected by the transfers were referred to the competent authority on the grounds of medical reasons of their own/ family members, spousal integration, short period of retirement and proper utilization of the officers' functions. The following officers of the order under reference are being given revised postings by altering the postings given at the time of universal transfer with the approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Transfer Act.
