The Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Agriculture), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the withdrawal and disbursement of the amount of Rs. 22.855 crore from the treasury.
The Assistant Director (Accounts-I), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune shall deposit the amount of Rs. 22.855 crore received through Budget Distribution System in the Personal Ledger Account opened in the name of Commissioner (Agriculture), State of Maharashtra, Pune and report the same to the Government and thereafter the said funds shall be transferred to various implementing agencies for implementation of the projects.
In addition to this Government decision, Annexure-A shows the project-wise/ implementation mechanism-wise distribution of funds, as per which the Commissioner (Agriculture) is to disburse Rs. 22.855 crore to the Scheduled Tribes category for the projects sanctioned under the scheme.
The above sanctioned funds should be withdrawn from the treasury only for immediate utilization and all the departments should make immediate utilization of the funds received by them.
The remaining funds under the scheme will be released by the Central Government in the second phase after auditing and certifying the funds disbursed to various departments under the National Agriculture Development Scheme from 2007-08 to 2017-18.
8. The projects sanctioned under the National Agriculture Development Scheme of the Department of Agriculture are referred to as Advanced Agriculture- Prosperous Farmers. It should be implemented in accordance with the guidelines prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 13th April 2017.
Under this Government decision, funds have been released only for the Scheduled Tribes category and all the Departments/ Implementing Agencies should utilise the above amount for the funds required for the Scheduled Tribes category under the projects sanctioned to them.
