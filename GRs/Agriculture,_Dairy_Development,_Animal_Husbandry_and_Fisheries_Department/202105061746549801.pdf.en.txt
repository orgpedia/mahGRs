Preamble: A total of 5142 villages in Vidarbha and Marathwada, comprising 4210 vulnerable villages and 932 villages in the mangrove belt of the Purna river basin in Vidarbha, have been covered under the World Bank funded project over a period of 6 years with an estimated cost of Rs. Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjivani Project is being implemented at an estimated cost of Rs 4000 crore. Through this project, in order to develop climate change-friendly farming practices in these villages, projects are being implemented in selected villages under the project in a phased manner and the implementation of the project has started by preparing micro-planning, plans of the villages. A proposal for disbursement of funds has been received from the Project Director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project vide letter No. 2 of reference for implementation of various aspects in the selected villages under the project. 4. The Government decision to provide Rs. 250 (Rs. Two hundred and fifty million only)
Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project, Mumbai has been declared as Controller and Finance Specialist, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project, Mumbai as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Project Director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project, Mumbai shall submit the utilization certificate of the amount spent under the head 33-Finance Assistance to the Government from time to time.

The Project Director shall submit the progress report of the work done under the project to the Government from time to time.
