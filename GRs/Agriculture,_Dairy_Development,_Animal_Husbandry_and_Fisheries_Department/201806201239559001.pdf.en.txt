Preamble The Union Government had launched the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP) from 2014-15, replacing the Integrated Pulses, Oilseeds and Maize Development Programme. Since 2018-19, the Government of India has included the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oilseeds under the National Mission on Food Security (NMFS). In the campaign, No. 1 (Telbia), Mission No. 2 (Teltad) and Mission No. 3 (Tree borne oilseeds) are the three missions in the State. 1 (Telbia) and Mission No. 3 (tree oilseeds) will be implemented.
In order to implement the above schemes, the Government has approved the Annual Action Plan for implementation of National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds and Oilseeds) with an outlay of Rs.6, 904.98 lakh for the year 2018-19 in the State as per the decision of the Government in Reference No.3.
