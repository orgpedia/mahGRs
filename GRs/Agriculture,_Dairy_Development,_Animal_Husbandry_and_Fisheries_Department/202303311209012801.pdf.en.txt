Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help the farmers in maintaining their financial stability, the State is implementing a re-designed weather based Fruit Insurance Scheme on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production on a large scale. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, the restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme will be implemented in the State for 9 crops namely Orange, Moong, Cashew, Pomegranate, Mango, Banana, Grape (A& B), Strawberry and Papaya (Ambia Bahar) on a pilot basis in 30 districts over a period of three years i.e. 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24 by taking into account the Revenue Board, HDFC ERGO General Insurance Co.
