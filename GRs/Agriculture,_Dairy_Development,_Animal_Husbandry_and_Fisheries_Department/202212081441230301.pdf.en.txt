(4) As the term of office of the non-official members appointed to the Managing Committee of the Amravati District Animal Disease Prevention Society under the Government Decision dated 26.2.2019 herein has expired, the Government was considering the appointment of the Chairman of the Gaushala/ Panjalapola Society, members of the voluntary organization working on animal issues, persons nominated by the General Committee and persons working in the field of humanitarian work/ animal lovers etc. as non-official members to the said Managing Committee.
Government's decision:
In view of the proposal received from the District Collector, Amravati, the following persons are being appointed as non-official members on the Management Committee of the Animal Clash Prevention Society in Amravati District as per the provisions of clause (a) (3) of the notification dated March 14, 2017. The term of office of the member shall be three years from the date of his appointment.
