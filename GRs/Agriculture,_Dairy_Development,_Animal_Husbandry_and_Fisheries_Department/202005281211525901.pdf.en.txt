4. Krishi Unnati Yojana As envisaged by the Central Government under the National Food Security Mission, the benefit under this scheme should be provided to the beneficiaries of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and women farmers.
The Union Ministry of Agriculture has issued a Reference letter to the State Governments under the National Food Security Mission for the year 2020-21. It will be mandatory to implement the Mission as per the administrative approval given vide letter dated 20th May, 2020, as well as the action plan approved for the Mission during the current year and the guidelines issued by the Union Ministry of Agriculture from time to time. In this regard, the Director of the RASUA Mission and the Director (Extension and Training) should control the Mission at the state level and direct the subordinate offices from time to time for effective implementation and control of the Mission.
Priority should be given to small and marginal farmers or groups of such farmers while selecting beneficiary farmers under various components of the National Food Security Mission.

The concerned District Superintending Agriculture Officer/ Taluk Agriculture Officer should identify the beneficiaries for the campaign and compile the list of beneficiaries at Taluk level for implementing the programmes covered under the campaign. In this list, the information of the farmer's name, village, survey/ group number, mobile/ telephone number, bank account number, Aadhaar number, etc. should be collected.
