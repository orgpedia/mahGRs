The proposal-
The cyclone, the lockdown to prevent the spread of corona and the ban on monsoon fishing affected the livelihood of fishermen in Konkan as they got little time for fishing. A demand has been received from various fishermen's organizations and public representatives to announce compensation for the financial loss to the fishermen as well as for the destruction of the fish.
on 24.02.2020. A committee will be formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the government has approved the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Padum) regarding the fish crisis.
Since the functions of the Committee were purely technical, the Government was considering reconstitution of the Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Fisheries) and constitution of a Review Committee and Executive Committee under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Fisheries).
Government Decision: Regarding the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Padum) in connection with the fish disaster caused by the cyclone in the coastal areas of Konkan. The Government is pleased to approve the constitution of the following Committees by amending the Government Resolution dated 09.12.2020:

The committee will have the following functions:
(a) To make a detailed study of the declining fish production in the coastal areas of the State during the year 2019-2020 from the data of fish production of fish caught with the help of mechanized and non-mechanized fishing boats according to the type of fishing method and to study the fish drought situation in the coastal areas of the State and to recommend to the Government on the reforms to be made.

(b) to study the method of compiling marine fish production through the Fisheries Department of the State and the Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute, Cochin and recommend to the Government whether or not a comprehensive composite fish production compiling method can be implemented in the State.
(e) To recommend to the Central Government (SRDF/ NDRF) the rates and norms for assistance to be provided to persons affected by natural calamities, after studying the rates and norms for the duration of the period for which they would be useful to the marine fishermen on the lines of the norms of the Central Government for natural calamities (SRDF/ NDRF).
e) Matters which are not covered under the Compensation of (SRDF/ NDRF). Suggest a scheme of assistance for compensation on behalf of the State Government till it is approved by the NDRF.
e) To suggest measures for sustainable marine fishing by finding out the reasons for the decline in fish production in the coastal areas of Maharashtra State.
(f) to submit a report to the Committee headed by the Principal Secretary (Fisheries).
Remuneration, travel allowance and other allowances to the Chairman and Members of the Committee shall be admissible as per the policy prescribed by the Finance Department. The expenditure on this subject shall be apportioned from the head of account, Office Expenditure of the Office of the Commissioner (Fisheries). This should be done through the office of the Commissioner (Fisheries).
