The Proposal:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter, the Finance Department has directed all the administrative departments to
Functional Head of the Department/ Department concerned for interest on the unabsorbed amount of Central and State share in the SNA account of Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented by the Department.
Subtitle 801- Requests for action to be taken regarding the opening of deposits under (01) Interest and (02) Other Deposits as per the separate head requirement under interest on unpaid balances or other deposits from the grantee.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to open a new head of account for depositing the interest and other deposits on the amount of the State share remaining unclaimed in the SNA account after the financial year out of the funds received for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented under the Sub-Division of Animal Husbandry in this Division.
Government's decision:
Out of the funds received for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented under the Sub-Division of Animal Husbandry in the Division, the same will remain unutilized in the SNA account after the financial year.
