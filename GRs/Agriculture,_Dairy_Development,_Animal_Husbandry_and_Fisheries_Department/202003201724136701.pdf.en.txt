The Proposal:
For the year 2019-20, the Central Government has released Rs.200 crore under the National Food Security Mission for commercial crops (cotton, sugarcane). As per the letter dated 09/04/2019, an action plan for an amount of Rs. 872.58 lakh has been approved. 523.55 Lakhs+ State share of Rs. 349.03 lakhs). Under the National Food Security Mission, the Central and State share of funds for Scheduled Tribes in the first installment of commercial (cotton, sugarcane) crops will be provided. As per the Government Decision dated 14.01.2019. Now, the Central Government has released Rs. 110.86 lakh as Central share in the second instalment under the Mission vide letter dated 27/01/2020.
