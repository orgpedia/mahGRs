the order
As per the provisions of Section 12 (2) (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, Dr. Ram Khare was appointed to the post of Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research for a period of 3 years or until the pleasure of the State Government, by a Government Order dated 30.03.2015, subject to the schedule.
Dr. Ram Khare has been relieved from the post of Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research with immediate effect as the three-year term of his appointment as Vice-Chairman has ended.
This order comes into force with immediate effect.
