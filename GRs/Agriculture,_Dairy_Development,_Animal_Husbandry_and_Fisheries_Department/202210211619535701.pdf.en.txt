The Proposal:
The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and Commissionerates of Agriculture and subordinate offices. The Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (DADF) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture carry out various activities under the e-governance programme. Under this scheme, a fixed amount is sanctioned by the state government every year and the sanctioned amount is distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, according to the need.
The e-Governance programme is a scheme under the scheme and a total amount of Rs.25 crore has been budgeted for the scheme (3451 2782) in the financial year 2022-23. As per the circular, 60% of the budgetary allocation for 2022-23 for essential and programme expenditure i.e. Rs. 15 crore has been made available to the Department for State e-Governance Programme for the first nine months.
The Finance Department had released Rs. 414.45 lakhs in reference No. 2 and 3 have been distributed at the level of the Department of Agriculture (itself) and the Commissionerate under the decision of the government here.
Therefore, the Commissioner of Agriculture, vide Reference No. 4 In pursuance of the demand made under the proposal herein, the limit of funds available under the State e-Governance Programme is Rs. 460.55 lakh was under the consideration of the Government, the Government is taking the following decisions.
