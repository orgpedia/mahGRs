Government Circular: Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan Phase-I and other watershed development programmes have been approved under the Government decision to implement" Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan 2.0" for sustainable development of watersheds through public participation and available groundwater. Detailed instructions have been issued to implement the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan 2.0 programme.
2. Further, it has been decided to implement the" Silt Free Dam Sludged Sewer Scheme" in the State permanently for removing silt from the water bodies of the State and using it in the fields. According to this government decision, the procedure for removing silt from water bodies in the state has been mentioned.
3. As per the minutes of the meeting in Reference 5, suggestions have been received for active participation of the Agriculture Department in the implementation of both the schemes of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan 2.0 and Sludge Free Dam Sludge Free Shivar in an expeditious manner.
