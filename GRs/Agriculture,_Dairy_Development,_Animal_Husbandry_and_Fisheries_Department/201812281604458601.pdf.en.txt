Preamble: In connection with the implementation of special projects in collaboration with the National Dairy Development Board under the National Agriculture Development Scheme to increase milk production in the Vidarbha and Marathwada regions of the State, the Minister said that the Government of Maharashtra has decided to implement a special scheme for dairy development in the Vidarbha region. The Committee has directed to obtain the opinion of the Department of Animal Husbandry, Government of India, in the meeting of the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee held on 25.5.2017, to grant in-principle approval to the project and to provide Ration Balancing Advisory Services and Animal Induction under the project.
Accordingly, the project report was submitted for the Central Government's feedback, including two components- providing balanced animal feed advice and guidance services, and distribution of milch indigenous cows/ buffaloes. The central government is 8. 01.10.2018 has approved the implementation of these two components under the Special Project.

Government's decision:
Under the Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, the Government is providing administrative approval for implementation of special projects for increasing milk production in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions, providing balanced animal feed advisory and guidance services, and distribution of milch indigenous cows/ buffaloes. Also, the total cost of the project is Rs. Financial sanction of Rs. 45.44 crore is also being provided. Both these components are to be implemented over a period of two years, 2018-19 and 2019-20, in collaboration with the National Dairy Development Board, subject to availability of funds under the National Agriculture Development Scheme.
While spending on the following two components included under this project, the parameters of the NABARD assisted Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS), the guidelines of the National Agriculture Development Scheme should be taken into consideration.

b) Distribution of milch cows/ buffaloes.
Under the special project to increase milk production in Vidarbha and Marathwada region, one milch cow/ buffalo per beneficiary is to be distributed to a total of 16,400 beneficiaries as per NABARD assisted DEDS scheme norms. 27.88 crore is being approved for the next 16 months.
The project will be implemented in 2,936 villages in 11 districts of the Vidarbha region, including Akola, Amravati, Buldhana, Wardha, Nagpur, Yavatmal, Chandrapur and Latur districts of Marathwada region.
Under this special dairy project, the National Dairy Development Board is to implement the components of providing balanced animal feed advisory and guidance services. So, the distribution of milch animals these components are to be implemented by the District Animal Husbandry Deputy Commissioner of the concerned district.
The other aspects mentioned in the Government decision at Read- 7 for the implementation of this project, such as direct benefit transfer, technical criteria for the goods to be given to the beneficiaries under the project, beneficiary selection under the project, beneficiary selection committee and other ancillary instructions have not been changed and will remain in force as it is.
Detailed guidelines for successful implementation of the project should be issued by the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune from his level to all concerned immediately.
