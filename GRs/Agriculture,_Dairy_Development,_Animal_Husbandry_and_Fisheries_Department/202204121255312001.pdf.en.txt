(4) The Commissioner Dairy Development shall submit the draft of the tender prepared by the committee for the e-tendering process for the approval of the Government. After the approval of the government, further action should be taken as per the standing rules of the government to publish the tender through newspaper/ e-tender etc.
The decision to issue tenders at the government level or at the level of Commissioner Dairy will be taken at the government level on a case to case basis.
(5) Under the e-tendering process, the technical bid and the commercial bid should be opened in front of the State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee in the same format as above.
(6) The aforesaid State Level Administrative Departmental Procurement Committee shall take action in respect of the procurement process of various materials/ machinery/ services and all necessary items under various schemes of the Government Milk Scheme and Brihanmumbai Milk Scheme as well as various schemes of the Central/ State Government/ District level. For this, a dedicated purchase cell should be set up in the office of the Commissioner Dairy.
(7) In the course of the said procurement process, taking into account the financial provisions available in various schemes for the entire financial year, the Commissioner Dairy Office shall prepare a consolidated procurement plan for the year by the month of April every year and obtain the approval of the Government and take further action accordingly. This process should be completed within the first month of the financial year. Also, appropriate measures should be taken so that tenders are published in May.

Functions of the Technical Advisory Committee:
(1) To determine the technical specifications of the materials/ machinery to be procured under the Dairy Department.
(2) To determine the technical specifications of the material/ machinery to be procured under the Dairy Department.
(3) To select the material/ machinery etc. for procurement after taking into account the quality of material/ component parts of the machinery/ equipment to be procured under the Dairy Department.
