The proposal: In the 27th meeting of the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) of National Agriculture Development Scheme held on 04.07.2019 under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra, approval has been given for implementation of protected agriculture projects under National Agriculture Development Scheme in the State during 2019-20. The State Level Project Sanctioning Committee has sanctioned Rs. 11.00 crore has been sanctioned.
Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme (NADP), Rs. Administrative approval for the Rs. 11.00 crore programme was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme in 2019-20, Rs. Administrative approval is being given to implement the" Protected Agriculture Project" worth Rs. 11.00 crore in the State.
The project will be implemented in the state as per the guidelines of the Integrated Horticulture Development Mission.
The Central Government will contribute 60% of the total funds and the State Government will contribute 40%.
4. The total financial target to be achieved by the State shall be equitably distributed to all the districts/ taluks of the State and the farmers will be allowed to choose within the limit of this financial target. (Form A) The applications of the farmers should be invited online through the Hortnet system of the department. The beneficiaries of the project should be selected.
6. Subsidies to new units under protected farming projects should be given only after prior consent of the competent authority to the selected beneficiaries.

7. Geo-tagging should be done after installation of protected agriculture project by the beneficiary.
The subsidy should not be paid without the beneficiary being registered on 7/12 basis.
9. Preference to be given to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, women beneficiaries, small and marginal farmers as per guidelines.
10.P The disbursement of funds for the implementation of the project will be done by the National Agriculture Development Scheme Cell, Department of Agriculture and Padum, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
11.Equipment should be implemented by Agriculture Department under the control of Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune, shall ensure that the financial and physical progress report of the scheme is submitted to the Central Government as per the guidelines of the Central Government within the prescribed time.
Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the funds made available for the project of protected agriculture should be directly deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the beneficiaries. The implementing agencies should ensure that these funds are not misused.
14.Your audited utilization certificate of the fund expenditure under the scheme should be submitted by the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune to the Revenue Cell, Department of Agriculture and Padum, Mantralaya, Mumbai from time to time.
15. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall ensure that the said Government decision is strictly implemented. The approval is subject to compliance of all financial rules, regulations, financial governance decisions, notifications, orders, orders issued by the Central Government from time to time, guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the Comptroller and Auditor General of India in this regard, and to ensure that there are no irregularities in expenditure.
