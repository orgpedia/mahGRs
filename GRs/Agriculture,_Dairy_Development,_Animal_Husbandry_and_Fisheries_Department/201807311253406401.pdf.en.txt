Introduction: The context. As per the decision of the Government on 24th July, 2017, a scheme has been formulated to bring the area under sugarcane crop under micro-irrigation. Under the scheme, farmers will be provided loans at 7.25 per cent interest rate through district central cooperative banks by taking loans from NABARD under the long-term loan scheme. The interest rate on this loan is as follows:
1. Loan from NABARD to State Cooperative Cooperative Banks 5.50% 2. Loan from State Cooperative Banks to District Central Banks 6% 3. Loan from District Central Cooperative Banks to farmers 7.25% 7.25% The details of regular interest payment liability on loans are as follows:
The State Government 4%
Sugar Factory 1.25% 3. Farmers 2%
Some nationalised banks have also offered to provide loans under the scheme. Accordingly, in addition to providing loans to farmers through district central cooperative banks by taking loans from NABARD, the government was also considering to include nationalized banks in the scheme for providing loans.
The following decisions have been taken:

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SUSCINIO-2017/ PR No. 58/14 A
Government's decision:
1. under reference. Under the scheme of bringing the area under sugarcane crop under micro-irrigation, which has been administratively approved by the Government vide its decision dated 24.07.2017, it is being approved to include nationalized banks for providing loans to farmers, besides providing loans to farmers through State Cooperative Banks by taking loans from NABARD.
Nationalised banks will provide loans at the rate of interest determined for the purpose. However, 4% of the interest rate of the loan taken from the nationalized banks will be the responsibility of the state government, 1.25% of the interest will be the responsibility of the sugar factory and the rest of the interest will be the responsibility of the farmer.
