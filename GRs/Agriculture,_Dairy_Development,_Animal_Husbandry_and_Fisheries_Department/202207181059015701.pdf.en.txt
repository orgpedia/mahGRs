Preamble: Government Resolution No. Bhakhans-2014/ S.No.82/ Part-II/ Industries-4, Dy. Under 1/12/2016, the procedure for office procurement to be done by Government departments has been prescribed. The said Government Decisions are applicable to any institution/ boards/ corporations/ authorities/ societies established under the Act of the Legislative Assembly. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation (Mahanand) has been announced as the coordinator for the central government's schemes related to dairy business in the state. Under this scheme, the central government provides grants to the cooperative societies through the procurement federation. The government was considering the formation of a state-level administrative department purchase committee for this purchase.
Government Decision: The Government Decision of the Department of Industry, Energy No. Bhakans-2014/ No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, D. No. of 1/12/2016. As per the provision of 2.9.2, the State Level Administrative Department Purchase Committee is being constituted for the matters to be procured through Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited (Mahananda) as follows:

Committee's mandate: The Committee's mandate will be to procure machinery, equipment, materials, etc. to be distributed to the beneficiaries under the schemes of the Central and State Governments and to procure machinery, equipment, materials, etc. for the use of the Federation.
Before the final decision is taken, the government has to take a decision. All the action as prescribed as on 1/12/2016 will be taken at the level of Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited (Mahanand).
