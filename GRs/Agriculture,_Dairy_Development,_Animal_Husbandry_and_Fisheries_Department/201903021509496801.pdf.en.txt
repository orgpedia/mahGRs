Under the Integrated Horticulture Development Mission, funds to the extent of 5% of the expenditure on the scheme should be used for administrative expenses, consultancy, computerization, contingency expenses, etc., in the State level and Mandal level offices as well as in the District level offices for implementing the National Horticulture Mission.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) Pune shall submit the utilization certificate to the Central and State Government regarding the allocation of funds made available under the scheme from time to time.
The funds distributed under the scheme should be spent from the district level. This fund should be used for the welfare of the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
