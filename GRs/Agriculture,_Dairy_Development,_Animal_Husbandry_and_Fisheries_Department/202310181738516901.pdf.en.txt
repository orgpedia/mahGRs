Introduction: Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME), the Central Government has provided Rs. The first instalment of funds has been released to the state government through letter No. 4. The Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as the Procurement and Disbursing Officer and the State Nodal Agency, Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises Yojana (PMFME) and Commissioner (Agriculture) are being declared as the Controlling Officers for the collection and disbursement of the said amount from the Treasury. Also, the Accounts Officer in the office of the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer is being declared as the Collection and Disbursement Officer for collection and disbursement from the treasury at the district level.
4. While spending the said disbursed funds, the Controlling Authority shall take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent in this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information achieved, fund utilization certificate, should be sent to the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance by the 10th of every month.
The Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer should ensure that the funds meant for the SCs are spent on the same components so that the Government can keep a check on the expenditure on these schemes.
While spending the funds distributed under the said Government Decision, the Head of the Department and the Controlling Officers should strictly follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Powers, Rulebook and the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 12/04/2023 in the context of the Finance Department.
