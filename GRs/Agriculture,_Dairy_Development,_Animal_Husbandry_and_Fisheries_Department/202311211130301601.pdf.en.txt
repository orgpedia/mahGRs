Preamble: With the twin objectives of providing remunerative prices to the agricultural produce of the State and enabling the consumers to purchase agricultural produce at reasonable prices. As per the Government decision dated 23/04/2015, the State Agricultural Price Commission has been constituted by converting the State Agricultural Price Committee into State Agricultural Price Commission.
Shri Syed Pasha Osmansahab Patel has been appointed as the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission vide Government Resolution dated 18th October, 2023. However, considering the responsibilities given to the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission and the nature of his work in the State Agricultural Price Commission, as well as the representation of the State in the Central Agricultural Price Commission, the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision: Shri Syed Pasha Osmansahab Patel has been appointed as the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission vide Government Resolution dated 18th October, 2023. However, in view of the responsibilities given to the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission and the nature of the work of the State Agricultural Price Commission, as well as the representation of the State in the Central Agricultural Price Commission by the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission, in view of the need to accord the status equivalent to that of a Minister to the Chairman of the State Agricultural Price Commission, the aforesaid Reference No. 4 By passing the order dated 18/10/2023, the Government hereby approves to accord the status of Chairman, State Agricultural Price Commission equivalent to the rank of Minister in the State Government and appoint Mr. Syed Pasha Osmansahab Patel, Vittal Housing Society, Nanded Road, Latur-413512 as Chairman to the said post.
