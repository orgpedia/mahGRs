The following authorities are being declared for the implementation and utilization of Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement of funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes:
Nodal Officer:- Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Budget) at the State level will be the nodal officer for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Animal Husbandry and will coordinate the Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes for implementation of PFMS.
First Level Agency: At the level of Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Deputy Commissioner Animal Husbandry (Planning& Forecasting) will be the First Level Agency for Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Department of Animal Husbandry for implementation of PFMS. The First Level Agency will be responsible for creating a Second Level Agency for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Animal Husbandry as per the functioning of the Public Financial Management System. The First Level Agency will control the Second Level Agency under them for implementing the Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
Second Level Agency: The Chief Executive Officer for Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur/ Regional Joint Commissioner of Animal Husbandry/ Deputy Commissioner Animal Husbandry/ Assistant Commissioner Animal Husbandry will be the concerned officer.
Checker and Maker:- The officers/ employees looking after the affairs of the Accounts Department in the office of the Regional Officer will be responsible as Maker for the implementation of the schemes of the Animal Husbandry Department. Also, the head of the office will work as a checker. The staff looking after the affairs of the accounts department at the level of Commissioner Animal Husbandry will work as a maker and the accountant as a checker.
