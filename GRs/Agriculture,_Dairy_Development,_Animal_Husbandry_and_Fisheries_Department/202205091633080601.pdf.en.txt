Agricultural Scientist Award by the Department of Agriculture. Patangrao Kadam Agriculture Scientist Award.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No. Krishapu-2021/ P.S. 45/4 J,
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai-32.
Dated the 9th of May, 2022
Reference: 1. The Government of the Department of Agriculture and Padum vide Decision No. Kripa-2021/ PROCESS. 45/4 J, D. As of December 3, 2021:
The award is given to one outstanding agricultural scientist from each of the four agricultural universities of the state for their outstanding performance in the field of agriculture. The decision was taken on December 3, 2021.
Considering the contribution of Dr. Patangrao Kadam, who has made remarkable achievements in the field of agricultural science, technology and education and who has created opportunities for agricultural education and research for the youth, the government was considering to name the award after him. The government is taking the following steps:
Government Decision: 1. As per the Government Decision dated 03.12.2021, the' Krishi Vigyan Puraskar' to be conferred on one outstanding agricultural scientist in each of the four Agricultural Universities of the State for their meritorious performance in the field of agriculture will henceforth be conferred on Dr. The Patangrao Kadam Krishi Vigyan Puraskar will be named after him and the nature and criteria of the award will be as per the decision of the government concerned.
