The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State governments. A total of Rs. 45.71 crore has been sanctioned for the year 2018-19 under the scheme for the Scheduled Tribes (ST) category. The Scheme has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 15th September, 2018. Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, a total of Rs. 22.855 crore has been released to the Scheduled Tribes in the first instalment. 15/10/2018 (Refer No. 6) Is distributed by the decision of the government. Now, the Central Government has released Rs. 13.715 crore to the State in the second instalment for this category and the corresponding State share of Rs. 9.14 crore is expected to be released totalling Rs. 22.855 crore. However, as a total of Rs. 8.19 crore is available for disbursement to the State, the Government was considering to disburse a total of Rs. 21.905 crore out of which Rs. 13.715 crore is Central share and Rs. 8.19 crore is State share.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, a total amount of Rs. 21.905 crore (Rupees Twenty One Crore Ninety Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) in the ratio of 60:40 between Central and State funding for ST category for implementation of projects sanctioned under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana-Raftaar during 2018-19 through Budget Distribution System as under:
