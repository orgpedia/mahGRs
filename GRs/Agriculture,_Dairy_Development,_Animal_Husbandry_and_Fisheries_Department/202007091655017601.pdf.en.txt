Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in the State in Kharif 2019. The scheme is being implemented through two insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India and Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company as per the Government decision dated 22.05.2019. The Agriculture Commissioner's Office vide Reference Letter No.7 has released the remaining State share of crop insurance premium subsidy to the above 2 companies under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2019 amounting to Rs. 48,24,54, 132/- is sought to be disbursed. Accordingly, the government was considering to disburse this amount as crop insurance premium subsidy.
Government Decision: As recommended by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2019, the remaining State share of subsidy to be given to insurance companies for crop insurance premium subsidy is Rs. 48,24,54, 132/- is being approved by the Government. The details of crop insurance premium subsidy State share subsidy to be paid to the respective insurance companies earlier and now are as follows:
