Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) in Kharif Season 2019 under reference No. (1) The Central Government has approved the implementation of the Prime Minister Crop Insurance Scheme in all the districts of the State for the notified crops in the notified areas under the decision of the Government here. The reference No. (2) Two farmers' representatives have been included in the committee formed at the taluka level under the government decision here. However, to ensure that the committees constituted for redressal of grievances at the state level and divisional commissioner level through farmers, public representatives, etc. can play a neutral role, the representatives of the farmers in these committees as well as the state level committee should be empowered to address the grievances of the farmers.
The government was considering the inclusion of 2 members of the legislature as representatives of the people.
Government's decision:
The government is approving the inclusion of 2 farmer representatives in the department level committee and 2 farmer representatives in the state level committee and 2 members of the legislature as representatives of the people to resolve various complaints received regarding the Pradhan Mantri Crop Insurance Scheme.
