The Proposal:
The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme to provide assured income to farmers. 1 and 3 are started. The scheme will be implemented in the state as per the norms prescribed by the central government and as per the directions received from the central government from time to time in this regard. Approval has been accorded in the meeting dated 04.02.2019 and accordingly Reference No. The government has taken 5 decisions. As per the detailed guidelines issued by the Central Government for this scheme, a proposal was under the consideration of the Government to approve the opening of two separate bank accounts at the level of Commissioner (Agriculture). Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government's decision:
The Central Government has launched the 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) to enhance the income of small and marginal farmers. 3 As per the guidelines issued herein, two separate Joint Savings Accounts, one on the lines of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and one on the cost of Project Control Room to be set up at the State level for the said scheme, in the name of Commissioner (Agriculture), Central Bank of India, Pune, are being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
Joint Savings to be opened in Central Bank of India, Pune in the name of Commissioner (Agriculture) for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme in the State.
