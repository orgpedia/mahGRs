Also, the above-mentioned fish seed sale rates will be applicable only for Government fish seed production/ conservation centres. The government rates will not be applicable to fish seed producers/ breeders and fish seed production/ conservation centres leased out to private developers. However, it will be mandatory for the private developer to sell 50% of the total fish seed/ shrimp seed produced at the fish seed centers contracted by the contractor to the government at the above government rate.
