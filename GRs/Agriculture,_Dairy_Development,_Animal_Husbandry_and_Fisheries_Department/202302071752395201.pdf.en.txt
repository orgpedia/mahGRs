Introduction: In the citrus crops of the state, orange is followed by the fruit of the mulberry. Maharashtra stands first in the country in milk production. Considering the favorable climate for the crops of mung bean in Marathwada, mung bean is more beneficial for the fruit farmers. Considering the need to create clusters for sustainable production, processing and export of mung beans, which are important for Marathwada, the government has approved the establishment of" Citrus Estate" for mung beans on the area of Taluka Fruit Plantation, Paithan, District Aurangabad. After the promotion of Ramanath Popatrao Karle to the post of Agricultural Service Group-B, he was posted as the Chief Executive Officer of" Citrus Estate" at Taluka Fruit Nursery, Paithan, District Aurangabad. Due to non-payment of salaries since their posting, they are required to pay their pending salaries for the period March, 2022 to January, 2023. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune for payment of their outstanding salaries was under consideration of the Government. The government will take a decision accordingly.
Government's decision:
Shri Ramanath Popatrao Karle, Krishi Seva Group-B, posted as Chief Executive, Citrus Estate, Aurangabad with effect from 10.03.2022
