Introduction From the year 2017-18, the centrally sponsored National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) is implementing the Vanasheti Sub-Mission in the State. The ratio of central and state share is 60: 40. For 2018-19, the government has allocated Rs. Rs 200 crore was allocated for this. Out of this amount, Rs. 35.94 lakh remained unspent at the end of the financial year. The scheme was launched by the central government in 2019-20. It was re-released on April 29, 2019.
Out of the unspent amount, Rs. Rs. 12.31. 13.39 and Rs. 10.24 lakh.

GOVERNMENT DECISION 1.
