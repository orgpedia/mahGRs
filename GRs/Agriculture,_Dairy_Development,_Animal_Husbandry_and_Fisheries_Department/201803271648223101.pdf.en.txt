The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government, a special dairy project is being implemented in 3,023 villages of 11 districts of Nagpur, Wardha, Chandrapur, Akola, Buldhana, Amravati, Yavatmal, Jalna, Nanded, Osmanabad and Latur in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions in collaboration with the National Dairy Development Board. The duration of the project is 3 years. Read here 1. As per the letter dated 29.12.2017, Shri R.H. Thackeray (Bhapres) has been given the charge of the post of Project Officer, Vidarbha Marathwada Special Dairy Project. In order to expedite the implementation of the project, the matter of declaring the Project Director as the Head of the Department was under the consideration of the Government, in accordance with which the Government is issuing the following decision.
Government's decision:
Approval is being accorded to declare Project Director, Vidarbha and Marathwada Special Dairy Project as Head of the Department through this.
