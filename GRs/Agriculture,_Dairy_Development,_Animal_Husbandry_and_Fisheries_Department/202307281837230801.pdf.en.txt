Preamble: The Central Government has approved the Prime Minister's Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) to address the problems of unorganized food processing industries in the State. In this background, the Government of India has approved the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) in the State for the next five years i.e. from 2020-21 to 2024-2025.
The total amount of Rs. 41907.76 lakh, including Central share and State share, for the implementation of the above scheme in the year 2023-2024 is given in the above mentioned table. 4. While spending the said disbursed funds, the Controlling Authority shall take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent in this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of expenditure from the provision made, account-wise/ sub-account-wise as well as material objective-wise information achieved, fund utilization certificate, should be sent to the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance by the 10th of every month.
