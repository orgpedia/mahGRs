Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Ambia Bahar will allow farmers to file online insurance applications for banana crop in 2023-24. Extension up to 03.11.2023.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries
Government Supplementary Letter No: FAVIO-2021/ P. No. 81/10-A
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date of release: November 2, 2023
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. FAVIO-2021/ PR No. 81/10-A, dated 18th June 2021. 13012/10/2016-Credit-II, Dy. 02.11.2023 Government Supplementary Guidelines: As per the guidelines of the Central Government, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme is being implemented from the year 2021-22 to 2023-24 for the period of Mrug and Ambia Bahar as per the Government Decision dated 18th June 2021. Under this scheme, the last date for participation for Banana crop in Ambia Bahar 2023-24 has been extended. 31.10.2023 has been fixed. However, considering the technical difficulties faced by the farmers in joining the scheme, the Central Government has decided to provide adequate time for joining the scheme. (2) In accordance with the approval given under the terms and conditions mentioned in the letter, the restructured weather-based crop insurance scheme Ambia Bahar will be provided to the farmers to participate in the banana crop in 2023-24. It is being extended till 03.11.2023. However, for maximum participation of farmers during this period, necessary publicity and publicity campaign should be carried out by the concerned insurance company.
