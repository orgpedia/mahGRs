Introduction: The State Government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme under the Government Resolution No. 1 read above for the farmers who are not eligible to cultivate fruit orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The scheme has been given administrative approval for implementation during 2019-20. A sum of Rs. 456.84 lakh (Rs. 4 crore only) has been budgeted for the year 2019-20 for implementation of the scheme for the Scheduled Castes category through Account Grants. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has approved the spending of the entire amount. However, vide Circular No.7 above, the Department of Finance has clarified that the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure required for the first nine months of 2019-20 will be distributed through BEAMS Ratio within the limit of 60% of the total annual provision. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse 60% of the budgeted amount of Rs. 456.84 lakhs for the year 2019-20 to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Budget Distribution System. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
For the year 2019-20, 60% of the budgeted amount of Rs. 456.84 lakh for SC category for implementation of Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme, i.e. Rs. 274.104 lakh (Rs.2 crore, seventy four lakhs, ten thousand four hundred only) is being made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Budget Distribution System.
