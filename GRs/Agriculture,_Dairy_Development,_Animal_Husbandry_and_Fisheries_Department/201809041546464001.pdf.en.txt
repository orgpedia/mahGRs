Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Integrated Cereals, Pulses, Oilseeds and Maize Development Programme was being implemented in the State till the year 2013-14. The Central Government has restructured all centrally sponsored schemes in the 12th Five Year Plan. Accordingly, the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP) has been launched from 2014-15, replacing the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Pulses, Pulses, Oilseeds and Maize Development (ISOPOM). The Mission comprises of three mini-Abhiyan-1 (oilseeds), mini-Abhiyan-2 (oilseeds) and mini-Abhiyan-3 (tree-based oilseeds).
The funding pattern of the Mission was 75:25 between the Centre and the States during 2014-15. Since 2015-16, the ratio between Centre and States has been revised to 60:40.
