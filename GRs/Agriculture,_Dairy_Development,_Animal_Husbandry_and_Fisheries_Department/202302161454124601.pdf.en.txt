A State Level Poultry Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune to discuss and take measures to address the difficulties faced by farmers as well as poultry companies while rearing commercial poultry under relevant Government decisions.
Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune has requested the Government to include 2 members each from contractual poultry farmers as well as open-caste poultry farmers in the said Committee as proposed by the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune vide his letter dated 07.02.2023. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION 3. The State Level Poultry Co-ordination Committee constituted under the relevant Government Decision under the Chairmanship of Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune is being reconstituted as follows.
