Preamble: For the year 2019-20, the Government of India has approved an Action Plan for food crops (Rice, Wheat, Pulses and Nutri Cereals) under National Food Security Mission (NFSA) for an amount of Rs. 22019.8155 lakh. 22019.8155 Lakhs (Central Share Rs. 13211.8893 Lakhs+ State Share of Rs. 8807.9262 lakhs) has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 03.06.2019. The Central Government has released Rs. 6840.53 lakh as first instalment of Central share under Mission for 2019-20 and as per the demand of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, the Government is taking the following decisions to release the required funds from the Central and State share
Government Decision: 1. Krishi Unnati Yojana for the year 2019-20- Total Central and State share of Rs. 1014.68 lakh (Rs.10 crore 14 lakh sixty six thousand only) for ST category for foodgrains crops under National Food Security Mission

The total amount of Rs. 1014.68 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission under the decision of the Government should be disbursed district wise by the Director (Extension and Training) through PFMS system.
Director (Extension& Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts 1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the withdrawal of funds to the tune of Rs. 1014.68 lakhs made available under this Government Decision.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the utilization certificate issued by the Director (Extension and Training) from time to time in connection with the allocation of funds.
