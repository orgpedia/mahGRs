The government has decided to include additional professional courses and revise the eligibility conditions under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme for all four agricultural universities and affiliated colleges in the state.

Government's decision
The said scheme as per the decision of the Government dated 21st June, 2017 in the Reference No.1 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education is being implemented in the State for the eligible beneficiary students of Agricultural Universities and their affiliated colleges with appropriate modifications.
All the terms and conditions of this scheme shall be as per the provisions prescribed in the Government Order dated 21st June, 2017 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and issued accordingly from time to time.
