As per the guidelines of the Central Government, the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana will be implemented in the state for the notified crops in the notified areas from Kharif season 2023 to Rabi season 2025-26. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 26.06.2023. Under this scheme, the last date of participation for Rabi sorghum in Rabi season 2023-24 has been given. 30.11.2023 has been fixed. However, considering the technical difficulties faced by the farmers in joining the scheme, the Central Government has issued Reference No. (2) In accordance with the approval given under the letter herein, online insurance applications will be provided to the farmers for the sorghum crop under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, Rabi Season- 2023-24. 4.12.2023 and dt. The due date is being extended by two days i.e. 5.12.2023. However, necessary publicity and publicity campaign should be carried out by the concerned insurance company so that maximum number of farmers can participate in this period.
