Preamble: The Commissionerate of Agriculture has been requested to grant approval for auctioning through e-auction process, the materials like useless additional undocumented materials/ machinery, vehicles, old spare parts, tyre tubes, batteries, old loose furniture, agricultural implements and machinery etc. in the field offices of the State under the Commissionerate of Agriculture. General Administration Department As per the Government decision dated 03.12.2014, the e-auction system has brought transparency in the entire auction process and has led to a substantial increase in Government revenue.
The use of e-auction system has been made mandatory for all government departments, offices, rural and urban local bodies, public enterprises and corporations in order to increase government revenue and reach out to traders in more and more geographical areas by bringing transparency, flexibility, and promoting healthy competition in the auction of all resources in the state. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the use of e-auction system developed by National Informatics Centre (NIC) for auctioning of useless surplus un-scheduled materials/ machinery vehicles, old spare parts, tyre tubes, batteries, old loose furniture, agricultural implements and machinery etc. in the field offices of the State under the Commissionerate of Agriculture through e-auction process.

The Director (Soil Conservation and Watershed Management), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being authorized as the nodal officer for the operation of e-auction system in the Commissionerate of Agriculture and Subordinate Regional Offices. Necessary action in this regard should be taken in coordination with the regional offices.
The e-auction manual developed by the National Informatics Centre (NIC) should be used.
NIC should also conduct training for technical assistance in e-auction.
