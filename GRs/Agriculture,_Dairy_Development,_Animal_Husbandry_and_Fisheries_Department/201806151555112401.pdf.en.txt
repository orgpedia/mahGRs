Preamble: To regulate the transfer of Government servants and to perform the duties of the Government.
The Act, 2005 has been enacted to provide for prevention of delay in demolition. Section 6 of the Act specifies the competent authority for transfer of Group A and Group B officers. As per the provisions of proviso 2 to section 6 and section 7 of the said Act, the proposal for the transfer of the powers of the Group A and Group B cadres in the Department of Animal Husbandry was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per proviso 2 and clause 7 of section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the powers to transfer officers of Group-A and Group-B cadres in the Animal Husbandry Department are mentioned in the Annexure 3 and 4 respectively. 30.12.2014 and D. It was earlier extradited under the Government decision dated 05.05.2016. The powers to transfer Group A and Group B officers in the Department of Animal Husbandry are hereby delegated as follows.
