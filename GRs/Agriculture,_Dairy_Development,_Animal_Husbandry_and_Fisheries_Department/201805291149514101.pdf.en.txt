Preamble: The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and the Commissionerate of Agriculture and its subordinate offices. Various activities are carried out under the e-governance programme by the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (itself) and the Agriculture Commissionerate. A number of activities are also planned. Under this scheme, a fixed amount is sanctioned by the state government every year and the amount is distributed to the Agriculture Department (itself) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune.
The e-Governance programme is a scheme under the Scheme of Schemes and was launched in the financial year 2018-19 under the scheme Demand No. D- 7-3451, Secretariat-Financial Services, Under the Five Year Plan (02) Scheme (02) (04) Scheme for Implementation of e-Governance Programme (Agriculture) (3451 2782)" 17 Computer Expenditure A total of Rs. 300.00 lakhs has been budgeted for the purpose.
In 2018-19, administrative approval for implementing e-Governance programme with an outlay of Rs.300 lakhs and release of Rs.210 lakhs in the first phase was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: 1. The State e-Governance Programme is being implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Ministry of Agriculture and to computerize the offices of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune and various subordinate offices. Administrative approval is being given to implement the e-governance programme within a limit of Rs 300 lakh.
To implement the e-Governance programme in 2018-19, in the first phase, Rs. 210 lakh is being disbursed subject-wise as follows:

(b) Commissionerate of Agriculture Level I. Ernst& Young LLP, Mumbai has been appointed as the consultant for implementing e-governance programmes and adoption of latest information technology in Agriculture and Padum Department and Subordinate Offices. All aspects of the e-governance project, including the services and database work identified by the department, will be done in a phased manner by the company. It is being approved to pay the service charges of the said company and to pay the expenses incurred for various orders and development of IT related services as per their project report.
Management of Commissionerate Level Hardware- Various activities are carried out under the e-Governance programme by the Commissionerate of Agriculture. Under this, the work of developing computer systems is done by making available the services of technical manpower in a systematic manner. The Institute has been selected through AMC/ FMS method for maintenance of Hardware at Agriculture Commissionerate level, Management of LAN, WAN and Anti-Virus, VC HALL, Toll Free Arrangement, Maintenance and Repair. Their payments are being approved to be paid from this fund.
Connectivity- Under Commissionerate level connectivity and networking, BSNL is allowed to make advance payments for connectivity (internet leased line/ circuit data line etc.) and OFC cables, networking cables, LAN switches, modems, routers, etc. at the Commissionerate level and pay BSNL dues for VC units and toll free arrangements at the Commissionerate level.
Software Procurement- Crop Harvesting Experiment for Agriculture Department, MIS, e-Service Book, DBT, PFMS, Agriculture Web Portal (All Divisional and Regional Offices related to Agriculture Department) Intraagri Website Public Grievance, Horticulture Plants and Grafts and Agricultural Tools. Also, to prepare computer and information technology related programs of the agriculture department, to prepare various orders according to the report of the government appointed consulting company. Expenditure on technical manpower/ services and data entry operators is also being allowed to be paid from this fund.
NICSI Technical Manpower- NIC Pune-7 has been approved to pay the advance payment received by NICSI (New Delhi) through proforma invoice to the technical manpower appointed by NICSI on honorarium at NIC, Pune to ensure that the various mandates of the Department of Agriculture are developed and functioning properly, as well as to update the data from time to time and to avoid technical difficulties in it. In this way, it is being approved to pay the Proforma/ Tax Invoice fee from the fund for the website of the department developed through Maha Online and various orders and the technical manpower and other services appointed through them to update and improve the various services under the Public Service Guarantee.
