Introduction: Under the Tribal Components Programme, "Plantation of Fruits and Vegetables in Tribal Family Neighbourhoods" scheme is being implemented in the State with an aim to reduce the rate of malnutrition in tribal areas in 14 districts of the State and to provide nutritious food to tribal brothers and to encourage the cultivation of nutritious vegetables and fruits in tribal family neighbourhoods. In the financial year 2023-24, a sum of Rs. 36.30 lakhs has been budgeted and administrative approval has been given to the budgetary fund limit under the Government Decision No. 2 above. Under the said scheme, as per the above mentioned Government Decisions No. 3 herein, a total of Rs. 7.26 lakh has been disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture). Now read the above by the Tribal Development Department vide Government Decision No. 5 here (2401. 806) for the year 2023-24 under this head, Rs. 18.15 lakhs has been transferred to the Agriculture Department. The matter of disbursing the funds to the Agriculture Commissionerate was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: - Financial approval is being given to spend an amount of Rs. 1 lakh (Rupees Eighteen Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) for implementing the "Tribal Household Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation Scheme" in 14 districts of the state namely Thane, Palghar, Pune, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Jalgaon, Ahmednagar, Nanded, Yavatmal, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Amravati and Gondia in the year "ID2."
