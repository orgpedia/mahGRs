The detailed details of various schemes/ components to be implemented under Coconut Development Board, Kochi sponsored Coconut Development Scheme in the State during the year 2021-22 including financial/ material aspects are given in Schedule-I of the letter dated 29.06.2021 of the Coconut Development Board. The Coconut Development Board, Kochi vide its letter dated 29.06.2021 has accorded administrative approval for an amount of Rs. 70.67 lakhs for the implementation of the scheme in the State. Out of this, the share of Coconut Development Board is Rs. 63.95 lakh and the allocation of scheme/ component wise financial targets has been ensured for SC sub-plan, ST sub-plan and general category wise. The components/ items to be implemented by the State under the scheme are mentioned. Guidelines for implementation of the scheme have been issued. The funds of the Coconut Development Board will be available after receiving the administrative approval of the state government to implement the scheme in the state. For this, the government was considering giving administrative approval for the implementation of the scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Considering the approval given by the Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India to the scheme, administrative approval is being given for the implementation of Coconut Development Scheme sponsored by Coconut Development Board, Kochi in 2021-22 for a total amount of Rs. 70.67 lakh with Central share of Rs. 63.95 lakh, State share of Rs. 5.25 lakh and beneficiary farmer share of Rs. 1.47 lakh.
