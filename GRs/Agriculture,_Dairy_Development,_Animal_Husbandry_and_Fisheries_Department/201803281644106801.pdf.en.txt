Preamble:" Strengthening of Fisheries Sector Database and Geographical Information System (GIS) (100% Centrally Sponsored)" component of 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme" Collection of Statistics on Ground Water Fisheries" is under implementation in the State of Maharashtra. For this, the Central Government has approved 12 posts of part-time contractual material calculators in the year 2013. The Central Government has allotted 12 posts with a monthly allowance of Rs. 8,500/- per part-time content enumerator. The cost will be borne by the central government. The government was considering filling these posts through external sources.
Government Decision: Strengthening of Fisheries Sector Database and Geographic Information System (GIS) at the establishment of Fisheries Department The Government is approving creation of 12 notional posts of Part Time Contractual Data Enumerators to fill up the same through external sources for the purpose of collection of statistical information on ground water fisheries in 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme.
The expenditure on these posts will be borne by the central government. The above 12 notional posts should be filled up as per the provisions prescribed in the Government Resolution issued from time to time by the Finance Department and General Administration Department.
While making these recruitments, the existing procedures of the government and the government decisions issued from time to time by the General Administration Department should be strictly followed.
This decision was taken by the Government in reference to the Informal Reference No. 176/ APUK, dated 8.3.2018 as well as the General Administration Department's Informal Reference No. 157/ O/ o 12, dated 3.2.2018 is being issued pursuant to the consent received.
