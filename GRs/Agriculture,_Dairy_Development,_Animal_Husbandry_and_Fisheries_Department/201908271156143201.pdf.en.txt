The date: August 26, 2019.
Introduction: Due to vacancies in the cadre of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A Agriculture Deputy Director in the tribal and Naxal-affected areas of Gadchiroli, Chandrapur and Gondia in Vidarbha, it is not possible to effectively implement the government's schemes to the farmers there. In view of this, filling up of vacant posts. Review meetings were held with the Chief Secretary from time to time. In the meeting, The Chief Secretary has directed to fill up the vacant posts in Gadchiroli, Gondia and Chandrapur districts immediately.
Therefore, in order to effectively implement the various schemes of the Department of Agriculture in Vidarbha by providing adequate manpower in Vidarbha, the Government was considering to transfer the services of 9 officers working in the rank of deputation in the cadre of Group-A Deputy Director of Agriculture, Maharashtra Agricultural Service, to the parent Department of Agriculture and to post them in the vacant posts in Vidarbha. The government has taken the following decisions:
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Government, with the approval of the competent authority, is approving the transfer and posting of officers in the cadre of Deputy Director of Agriculture, Group-A, Maharashtra Agricultural Service in the department as shown in the table below.
