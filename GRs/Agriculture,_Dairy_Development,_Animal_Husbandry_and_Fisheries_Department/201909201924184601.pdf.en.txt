Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Krishi Unnati- Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission is being implemented in the State. The funding pattern is 60:40 between the Centre and the state. For 2018-19, the government has allocated Rs. 14,600 crore has been provided. Out of this amount, Rs. 2794.038 lakh remained unspent at the end of the financial year. This fund has been re-financed by the Government of India vide letter dated June, 2019 for the year 2019-20.
The central share of general, scheduled caste and scheduled tribe category in the unspent fund is Rs. 302.053 lakh, Rs. 1430.604 Lakhs and Rs. 1061.381 lakh. The central fund of Rs. 302.53 lakh out of Rs. 301.618 lakh has been withdrawn by the Commissionerate of Agriculture from the Government treasury and deposited in PFMS bank account. The remaining Rs. 0.434 lakh is deposited in the government treasury.
For the year 2018-19, the central share of Rs. 302.053 lakh and State share of Rs. 201.3687 Lakhs out of which Rs. 503.4217 lakh to be revived during 2019-20 and Rs. 0.434 lakh and Rs. 0.289 crore. A proposal to provide Rs 0.723 lakh to the Commissionerate of Agriculture on Financial Management System (BDS) was under consideration of the government.
