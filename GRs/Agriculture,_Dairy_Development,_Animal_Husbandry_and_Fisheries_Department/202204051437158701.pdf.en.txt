The proposal: The 23 rd State Level Project Approval Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra had approved the implementation of the project under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) for strengthening of laboratories at Kharif Soil Research Station, Panvel and capacity building of farmers in respect of soil health and fish production at Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli University during 2017-18 to 2020-21. Under the scheme, Rs. 419.00 lakhs will be provided. The project is yet to be completed and will be completed by 2022-23. The matter of granting administrative approval to the program of Rs. 419.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government, accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, strengthening of laboratories at Kharif Soil Research Centre, Panvel and capacity building of farmers regarding soil health and fish production. Administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of the programme with an outlay of Rs. 419.00 lakh (Rupees Four Crore Nineteen Lakh only).
