Introduction: Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana- Raftaar is being implemented with the financial assistance of the Central and State Governments in the ratio of 60:40. As per letter dated 28/11/2018, a total amount of Rs. 4.23 crore has been sanctioned to Maharashtra for the year 2018-19. Out of this annual allocation, a total allocation of Rs. 4.23 crore has been approved for Central share of Rs. 2.54 crore (General Category) and Rs. 1.69 crore for State share of the same. The projects are to be implemented within this year's budget.
The central government. As per the letter dated 28/11/2018, Rs. 1.227 crore has been released as first instalment for Animal Health Sub Plan under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, pursuant to which Rs. 0.818 crore is required to be released from the State exchequer.
Thus, a total amount of Rs. 2.045 crore is available for disbursement to the Department from Central and State share of Rs. 0.818 crore and Rs. 1.227 crore from Central share for the first instalment. The matter of distributing this fund to the Commissioner (Animal Husbandry) through the Commissioner (Agriculture) was under the consideration of the government, following which the government has taken the following decision.
