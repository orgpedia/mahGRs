Introduction Under the National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture, the Rain-fed Area Development (RAD) scheme is being implemented with a Central and State share of 60:40 subsidy.
A meeting of the Project Sanctioning Committee at the Central Government level was held on 6.08.2019 for implementation of Dryland Area Development (RAD) programmes under National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) in 2019-20. In the meeting, the Central Government vide letter dated 2.05.2019 sanctioned Rs. 2600.00 lakh and the State share of Rs. 1733.33 Lakhs out of which a total of Rs. Accordingly, the Annual Action Plan for the year 2019-20 of the RAD Programme under National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) (Rs. 4333.33 lakhs) was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.

The Annual Action Plan for the year 2019-20 has been approved with an outlay of Rs. 4333.33 lakh.
