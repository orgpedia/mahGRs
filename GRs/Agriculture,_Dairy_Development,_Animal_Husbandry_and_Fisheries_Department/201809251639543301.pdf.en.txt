In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of rule (3) of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Establishment and Regulation of Cruelty to Animals) Rules, 2001, for effective implementation of the provisions of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 of the Central Government, by notification dated the 14th March, 2017, in this behalf, an Animal Cruelty Prevention Society has been established in each district of the State.
2. The matter was under consideration of the Government to issue directions to create awareness among the public in this regard.
Government Circular: 3. In view of the above facts, the Government directs to create awareness in the State regarding Animal Clash Prevention Society as under: (i) Issue of Helpline The Helpline functioning in the Collectorate should be used.
(ii) Installation of wall-papers in the office of the Collector, District Animal Husbandry Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry Officer, Superintendent of Police, Chief Executive Officer (ZP), etc.
(ii) Organizing awareness programs in schools and colleges The headmasters of schools and principals of colleges should be directed to create public awareness in all schools and colleges of the district.
(iv) Public awareness through street plays
Awareness should be created through street plays if possible by the students of the college.
(v) Publicity in Gram Sabhas In the agenda of Gram Sabhas of all Gram Panchayats in the district, the topic of SPCA's awareness should be included and discussed in the Gram Sabha.

(vi) In respect of all such measures, the District Collector concerned shall, in such places as may be convenient to him, organise public awareness programmes and publish the same in the local newspapers at least.
(vii) Publication on the website The Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune and the concerned District Collector shall publish on their website about the Animal Clash Prevention Society.
The Collector and the Chairman of the District Animal Clash Prevention Society as well as the Deputy Commissioner of the District Animal Husbandry and the Member Secretary of the Animal Clash Prevention Society should take immediate action to implement all the above matters and report the action taken to the Commissioner Animal Husbandry Maharashtra State, Pune and the Government from time to time.
