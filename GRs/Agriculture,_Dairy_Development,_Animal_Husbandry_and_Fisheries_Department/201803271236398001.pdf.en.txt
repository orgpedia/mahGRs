Government Decision: For the purpose of 31 District Soil Survey and Investigation and Soil Analysis Chemical Laboratory in the State, under the head 24020032, rent and tax (14), Rs. A sum of Rs. 10.51 lakh (Rupees Ten Lakhs Fifty One Thousand) has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demands in the Winter Session 2017-18. Accordingly, the Government is giving financial sanction to spend the said amount for the purpose of rent and tax (14).
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2017-18 and shall be covered under the following heads:
Demand No. D-3 2402, Soil and water conservation.
Soil Survey and Testing (00) (02) Soil Survey and Investigation and Soil Analysis Laboratory (2402 0032) (14) Lease and Tax
The following terms and conditions shall be observed while making expenditure for the purpose of this lease and tax (14).
1) The audited report of the expenditure and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government as soon as possible along with the progress report. As such, it will be the responsibility of the implementing agency to keep the expenditure accounts as above.
(2) It shall be ensured that the said funds are spent for the purpose of lease and taxes (14) for the 31 District Soil Survey and Investigation and Soil Analysis Chemical Laboratory in the State.
3. The Director of Agriculture (Regulation of inputs and quality), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take necessary precautions to ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned amount under any circumstances.
4) It shall be the responsibility of the concerned Regional Office to ensure that no government rules/ authority are violated under any circumstances.
The orders are being issued in accordance with the powers conferred under the circular of the Finance Department dated 18th April, 2017.
