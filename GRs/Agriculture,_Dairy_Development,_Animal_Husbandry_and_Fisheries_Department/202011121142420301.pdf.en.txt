Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2019-20 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme 2019-20 has been implemented for 7 crops viz. Orange, Cashew, Pomegranate, Mango, Banana and Grape (Ambia Bahar) in the district by taking the component of Revenue Board.
Reference No. 20 for re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2019-20 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 1.
The Commissionerate of Agriculture has submitted a request for release of funds towards State share subsidy and administrative expenses as demanded by Agriculture Insurance Company of India for the year 2019-20 for Ambia Bahara in the scheme. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: Considering the demand submitted by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2019-20 (Ambia Bahar), the amount of Rs. 86,50,60, 285/- towards administrative and other incidental expenses. A total of Rs 20,00,000. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 86,70,60, 285/-.
