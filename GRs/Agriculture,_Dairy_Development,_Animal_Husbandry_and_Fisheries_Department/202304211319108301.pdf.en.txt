Government decision: During the discussion on the attention motion tabled in the Legislative Council on 10.3.2023, the issue of taking measures for the comprehensive development of Aarey area came up for discussion.
During the discussion. The Minister of Dairy said that considering the location of Aarey area in Mumbai city and its environmental and tourism importance, it is necessary to consult all the stakeholders for the development of this area and a consultant is being appointed for it. He assured that after receiving the report of the consultant to be appointed, a committee will be formed under his chairmanship to take a final decision after considering it.
As per the assurance given by the Hon'ble Minister, Dairying to the House of the Legislature, the approval is being given to constitute the committee as follows.

The mandate of the Committee will be as follows: 1. To study/ discuss and suggest changes/ amendments in the report of the consultant to be appointed for preparation of the development plan for Aarey area.
2. Suggest measures to make Aarey Dairy area encroachment free and use the land in public interest.
3. Provision of basic amenities to the residents of Aarey Colony by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation. (excluding the transgressor)
4. To guide the development of Aarey area as a tourist destination without disturbing the Aarey area as it is ecologically sensitive.
