Preamble: Under the Tribal Component Programme, with the objective of reducing malnutrition in the tribal areas of 14 districts of the State and to provide nutritious food to the tribal brothers and sisters, the scheme of" Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation in the Neighbourhood of Tribal Families" is being implemented in the State since 2003-04 to promote the cultivation of nutritious vegetables and fruits in the neighbourhood of the tribal families. The scheme will be implemented from 2023-24. 36.30 lakh has been proposed by the Director (Horticulture) in Reference No. 3 submitted to the government. Accordingly, this scheme will be implemented in the state in 2023-24 within the budgeted limit of Rs. The matter of giving administrative approval to the program of 36.30 lakh rupees was under the consideration of the government, according to which the government is taking the following decisions.
Decision of the Government: To implement the scheme of planting fruit trees and vegetables in the backyards of tribal families in 14 districts of Thane, Palghar, Pune, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Jalgaon, Ahmednagar, Nanded, Yavatmal, Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Amravati and Gondia in 2023-24, Rs. Administrative approval is being given for the programme with an amount of Rs. 36.30 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Six Lakhs Thirty Thousand only).
