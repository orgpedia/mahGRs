Government documents:
As per the guidelines of the Central Government, the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana will be implemented in the state for the notified crops in the notified areas in the Kharif season 2019. The approval has been accorded pursuant to the Government Resolution dated 23.05.2019. The last date for participation under the scheme is for Kharif season 2019. 24.07.2019 has been fixed. However, now considering the technical difficulties faced by the farmers in joining the scheme, the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) has been extended to cover the farmers in Kharif season 2019 with a view to provide them adequate time. from 25.07.2019. It is being extended till 29.07.2019.
However, for maximum participation of farmers during this period, necessary publicity and publicity campaign should be carried out by the concerned insurance company.
