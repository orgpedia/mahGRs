Government's decision:
As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the eligibility of officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, to ensure their continuation in Government service and to retire the officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity from Government service prematurely.
As per the said Review Policy, the following Committees are being constituted to review and recommend/ take further action as per rules, in order to test the eligibility (eligible/ ineligible) of the officers/ staff in the Fisheries Department to be eligible for review beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service.
(1) For Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Grade) Grade Pay 7600 and above but less than 10000, Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Grade) Grade Pay less than 7600 and Group-B (Gazetted) officers, the Departmental Review Committee shall be as follows.
