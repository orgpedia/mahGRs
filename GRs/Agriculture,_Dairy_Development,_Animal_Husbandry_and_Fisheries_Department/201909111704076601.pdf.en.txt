Introduction: In line with the Union Government's vision to double farmers' income by 2022, inputs like inputs, training, irrigation, mechanization, post-harvest technologies, agro processing and marketing etc. are critical to increase crop production. Considering the need for collective farming, modern methods of farming and strict planning for a substantial increase in the production of farmers due to the low land holding of farmers in the state, the scheme of" Promotion of Group Farming of Farmers for Promotion and Empowerment of Group Farming" has been implemented. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 5.10.2018. The implementation of the scheme is proposed to be carried out under Reference No. 4 for a period of two years. Extension from 1.4.2019 to 31.3.2021 has been approved. Under this scheme, 196 farmer groups selected in 2017-18 and 211 farmer groups selected in 2018-19 will be given Rs.
