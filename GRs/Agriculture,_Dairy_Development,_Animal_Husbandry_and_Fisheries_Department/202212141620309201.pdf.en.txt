Preamble: In order to stabilize the power generation program in the state and to ensure the participation of the farmers in the power generation program, a new scheme has been implemented from the year 2017-18 to pay the difference between the Agricultural Produce Market Committee rate and the government declared base price (MSP) to the power producers as per the government decision dated September 17, 2018.
Under the scheme, the government provides subsidy to the farmers for payment of the difference if the maximum daily rate of various crops in the prescribed period is less than the government declared MSP. This subsidy is given for certified and foundation seeds. In such cases, the difference amount is given to the farmers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (Mahabeej), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune as incentive subsidy. Considering the amount due to the seed producing farmers, Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIZ), Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune submit proposals to the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune. After examining the proposal, the appropriate proposal is submitted to the Agriculture Department through the Agriculture Commissionerate. Sadar
