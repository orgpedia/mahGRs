The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has been notified by the Government in this regard. Under Section 9 (1) and (2) of the said Act, the head of each head office shall empower the subordinate officer to take the final decision. Also, it has been directed to earmark three levels of officers who will submit cases for taking final decision on all matters related to the department/ office. Accordingly, the levels are being determined as shown in the accompanying table in respect of the subject matter related to this assignment. However, if there is a direction from higher authorities or if there is a need in some exceptional cases, it will be suitably modified on a case to case basis. In the above mentioned stages, it is not indicated to consult other departments where necessary.
