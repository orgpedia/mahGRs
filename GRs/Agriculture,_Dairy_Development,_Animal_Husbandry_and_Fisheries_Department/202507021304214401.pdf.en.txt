Introduction: Crop harvesting experiments are conducted under the Digital General Crop Estimation Survey under the Krishi Unnati Yojana in all the districts of the state of Maharashtra from the Kharif season 2024. For this, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 1000 / - will be made available. Under the scheme, it was proposed to open a single interest-bearing savings bank account for the "Digital General Crop SCART Survey" component, and the government was considering opening a bank account to conduct transactions on this account through the Public Financial Management System. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: Under the Krishi Unnat Yojana, approval is being accorded for opening of a new single interest-bearing savings bank account in a nationalised bank called "Bank of India" for transaction through Public Financial Management System for obtaining funding of crop harvesting experiment for General Crop SCHR Survey component from the Central Government subject to the following conditions.
The account shall be used only for the specific purpose for which it is proposed to be opened, as directed by the Central Government.
Under no circumstances should the account be used for any other purpose.
It shall be mandatory to keep the cash book up to date with respect to the said account.
4. If the balance in the said bank account is required to be paid to the appropriate government account, it shall be mandatory to act accordingly in accordance with the applicable rules and regulations.
5. Monthly adjustment of bank account transactions and audit shall be mandatory.
6. Care should be taken to ensure that funds are not left unutilised for a long period of time in this account.
This bank account will need to be closed immediately after certain purposes have ended.
