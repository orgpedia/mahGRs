The proposal-
" Chandrapur and Sindhudurg, at the two ends of Maharashtra, are blessed with mineral and natural resources. A special program of intensive planning and implementation will be implemented to ensure optimum utilization of natural resources in the district. In pursuance of the above announcement, the Planning Department has approved the pilot project of Chanda to Banda for economic development of Chandrapur and Sindhudurg districts, as per the decision of the Government in Read More
The government is considering to implement three schemes in Chandrapur district, such as encouraging fisheries cooperatives to produce fish seeds, providing fish feed mills and mobile vehicles for selling fish.
The following schemes have been included for Chandrapur district under the Chanda to Banda scheme.
