The proposal-
(2) In pursuance of the Government decision dated 14th July, 2020, a Committee has been constituted to select and recommend appointment of private veterinarians for pre-slaughter inspection of scheduled animals in the authorized slaughter houses in the State authorized by APEDA or similar authorities of Central/ State Government for meat export.
The matter of reforming this committee was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Read above. (2) The Government Resolution dated 14th July, 2020 is hereby amended as follows:
(1) The Joint Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, Pune Division, Pune is being appointed as Member Secretary in place of the Joint Commissioner, Animal Husbandry (Headquarters).
(ii) The Registrar, Maharashtra State Veterinary Council, Nagpur is being re-inducted as a member.
