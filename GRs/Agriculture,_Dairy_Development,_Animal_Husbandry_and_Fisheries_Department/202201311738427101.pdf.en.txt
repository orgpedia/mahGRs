The Proposal:
The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and Commissionerates of Agriculture and subordinate offices. Under this scheme, for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. 991 Lakhs to Fund Reference No. 1 has been issued by the Government here under the decision however, the Commissionerate of Agriculture vide reference No. Since the proposal received under Section 2 has changed the subject-wise demand for funds under the programme, the Commissioner (Agriculture) has requested to make available the unspent funds of some of the items to other units requiring funds under the programme by changing the subject. The matter of reallocation of funds distributed under this program was under the consideration of the government, following which the government is taking the following decisions.
Government's decision:
Under the State e-Governance Programme, in the financial year 2021-22, Rs. 991 crore out of which Rs. 585.16 lakh is being sanctioned as under: (Rs. million).
