The functions of the State Level Sanctioning and Controlling Committee shall be as follows: (2.1) The State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee shall collate the District Annual Fisheries Plans and prepare and approve the Annual Fisheries Action Plan of the State and be responsible for recommending the same to the Department of Fisheries, Government of India/ National Fisheries Development Board, Hyderabad.
(2.2) The implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana will require state-level approval and approval of the Control Committee. The committee will also be responsible for monitoring and evaluation.
(2.3) State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee for Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana will be supported by State Programme Unit (SPU).
(2.4) The State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee shall take information about the activities of the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana and also about the State level initiatives/ inventions or other schemes and programmes.
(2.5) The State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee shall encourage linkages with banks/ financial institutions for smooth implementation of the components of beneficiary oriented initiatives under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.
(2.6) The Chairman of the State Level Sanctioning and Controlling Committee may appoint additional State Level Officers, if deemed necessary. The Chairman shall also nominate a maximum of one or two non-government eminent persons associated with the fisheries sector in the State.

The functions of the District Committee shall be as follows:
(4.1) The District Level Committee shall be responsible for preparing and approving the Annual District Fisheries Plan and recommending the same to the State Level Sanctioning and Control Committee.
(4.2) The District Level Committee shall be responsible for the implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana at the district level and shall supervise and monitor the implementation of the scheme.
(4.3) The District Level Committee shall be responsible for the approval of the beneficiaries for individual group activities of the beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.
The District Level Committee shall be assisted by the District Programme Committee constituted by the Department of Fisheries, Government of India.
(4.5) The District Level Committee shall co-ordinate the district level activities including activities, other schemes and programmes under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.
(4.6) The District Level Committee will facilitate the linkage of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana with banks/ financial institutions.
(4.7) The above roles and responsibilities of the District Level Committee are for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme components or activities of the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.
