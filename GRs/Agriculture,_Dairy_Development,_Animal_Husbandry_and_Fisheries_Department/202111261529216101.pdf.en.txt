The Proposal:
The decision to implement the revised pay scale as per the 7th Pay Commission for the employees of the Public Sector Undertakings in the State has been taken by the Finance Department under the Government decision dated 04/08/2021. The Board of Directors of Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation in its 229th meeting held on 13/08/2019 has passed a resolution recommending to the Government to implement the revised pay scale as recommended by the Seventh Pay Commission to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation. Accordingly, the matter of implementing the revised pay scale as recommended by the Seventh Pay Commission to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation was under consideration of the Government. Following this, the government took decision on this issue.
Government's decision:
The Government has approved the revised pay scale for the State Government employees as per the 7th Pay Commission vide Government Resolution, Finance Division No.Vepur-2019/ PR.No.1/ Service-9, dated 30/01/2019 with effect from 01.01.2016. In the Annexure-A submitted by Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai, Rakana No. 3 A total of 1162 officers/ employees mentioned herein have been appointed as officers/ officials in the Appendix-A attached vide Rakaana No.1/ 2021 with effect from 1st July, 2021 subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Resolution dated 30/01/2019 of the Finance Department and the following terms and conditions: The Government is approving the implementation of the revised pay scales and pay metrics mentioned in Sections 8 and 9.
(1) The Corporation shall meet the expenditure incurred on the implementation of the revised pay scale as per the 7th Pay Commission from its own income and no financial assistance shall be provided to the Corporation by the Government to meet the expenditure now and in future.
