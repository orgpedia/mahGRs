The subsidy admissible for the above mentioned components should be transferred through Public Financial Management System to the bank account linked to the Aadhaar number of the concerned farmer.
While giving the benefit under this scheme, the applicant/ farmer should ensure that the demanded component has not been taken advantage of under any government scheme before.
The funds required for the allocation of the above components should be met from the budgetary provision made available from the relevant existing plan for the financial year 2023-24 and additional funds may be sought as per the demand of the above components.
The scheme should be promoted and publicized at the field level for implementation by the Department of Agriculture for the financial year 2023-24.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) shall issue guidelines at the field level in the above regard and the Commissioner (Agriculture) shall review the same on a regular basis.
