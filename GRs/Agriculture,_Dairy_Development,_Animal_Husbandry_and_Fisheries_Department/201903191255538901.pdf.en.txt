Preamble: In order to stabilize the power generation programme in the State and to maintain the participation of the farmers in the power generation programme, in view of the fact that the rate in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee for the crops produced in the year 2017-2018 is less than the minimum support price, a new scheme has been implemented to pay the difference between the minimum support price declared by the Government as per the Government decision dated 17th September, 2018, if it is higher than the rate based on the Agriculture Produce Market Committee for the prescribed period, to the power producers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited, Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune. A proposal was received from the Commissionerate of Agriculture to provide Rs. 3100.23 lakh for the difference amount of seeds produced by the farmers in Kharif and Rabi seasons 2017-18. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 21.00 crore was released as per the Government Decision dated 11.1.2019. The request for release of remaining funds was made by the Commissionerate of Agriculture vide letter dated 12.3.2019. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 3.00 crore.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PURCHASE 1518/ PR No. 226/1 A
A sum of Rs. 3.00 crore is being disbursed under the State Scheme of Account Head 2401A922 (100%) to the seed producing farmers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited, Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune to compensate the difference between the Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) based price and the Minimum Support Price (MSP) declared by the Government for the seeds produced during Kharif and Rabi season 2017-18.
The amount should be transferred directly to the farmers" bank accounts.
The conditions of the circulars dated 2.4.2018, 27.7.2018 and 5.12.2018 of the Finance Department should be fulfilled.
