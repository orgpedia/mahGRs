Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana is being implemented in the State from Kharif season 2016 as per the guidelines of the Central Government. It has been observed that while the crop insurance compensation amount fixed by the insurance company to the eligible farmers under this scheme was deposited electronically in the bank account, the amount was returned to the insurance company without being paid to the beneficiary farmers due to the bank account of the farmers not being linked to the Aadhaar number, having the wrong bank account details or other similar reasons.
The Central Government has issued reference No. 1 is reported here. The instructions issued by the Central Government in this regard and the reference of the Agriculture Commissionerate No. As per the request made in letter No. 2, the matter of opening an ESCROW bank account in a nationalized bank at the level of Commissioner (Agriculture) in the present case was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), the Government is approving the opening of an ESCROW Bank Account in a nationalized bank at the level of Commissioner (Agriculture) for depositing the amount returned to the beneficiary farmers due to non-payment of the amount to the insurance company due to the bank account of the farmers not being linked to the Aadhaar number, having incorrect bank account details or other similar reasons.
1) Do not make any other transactions in this bank account.
2) The amount should be deposited in the account of the eligible beneficiary farmers within 1 month after the insurance company has deposited the money in the account.
3) The account should be audited in accordance with the rules.
4) Quarterly report to the government about the funds deposited in the account by the insurance company and the funds deposited in the account of eligible beneficiaries by the Agriculture Commissionerate.
