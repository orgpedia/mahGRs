The Proposal:
As per the guidelines issued by the Central Government under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, project proposals are submitted to the State Project Scrutiny Committee and thereafter to the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee under the components of" Production and Growth,"" Infrastructure and Resources,"" Special Plan and Transformative Fund." Dattasai Multistate Co-op Milk and Milk Products Ltd., has been selected in the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee meeting held on 26.2.2020 for availing financial assistance under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana under the sub-segment of Dairy Infrastructure and Equipment. A proposal for setting up a new milk processing plant with a capacity of 10,000 litres was submitted by the team of Chilawadi T.G. Osmanabad.
According to the minutes of the meeting of the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee under reference letter, Dattasai Multistate Co-op Milk and Milk Products Ltd. Chilawadi T.G. Osmanabad Team Rs. 4.36 crore and the project proposal for 50% equity share of the Government of India and 50% equity share of the beneficiary on parallel financing basis as per the funding pattern prescribed for the NPDD programme.

Government's decision:
Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the government has decided to set up Dattasai Multistate Co-op Milk and Milk Products Ltd. The Government is granting administrative approval to the project proposal of Chilawadi T.G. Osmanabad for setting up a new milk processing plant with a capacity of 10,000 litres subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the said decision.

The duration of the project is 2 years. For the implementation of the project, it is mandatory for the association to submit the prescribed format of bond and work plan to the Commissioner, Dairying, for completion of the project within the stipulated time.
The Commissioner, Dairying, should write a miscellaneous bond on behalf of the above association.
Terms and Conditions: 1. The funds sanctioned shall be spent by the team for the approved components included in the Detailed Project Report of the project for the above mentioned aspects.
The team will bear the cost of the project in excess of the approved project cost. The government will not be responsible for the cost of the project. Also, no funds other than the project cost will be sanctioned by the government.
The decision of the State Government during the period of milk shortage/ excess milk production in the State shall be binding on the Union.
Separate accounts of expenditure in respect of the sanctioned funds shall be maintained in the manner prescribed under GFR Rule 149 and the report of expenditure incurred and the audit in respect of the accounts shall be submitted to the Commissioner, Dairying.
