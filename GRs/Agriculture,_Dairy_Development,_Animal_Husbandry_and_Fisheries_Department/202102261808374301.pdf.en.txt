Government's decision
For the financial year 2020-21, Demand No. D-3, 01, Crop Protection, 120, Assistance to other Institutions, (00) (22) Under Indian Council of Agricultural Research funded scheme Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University (State share 25%) (Programme) 2415 1112, 31- Assistance under Grants (Non-salaried) Rs. The Government is approving the release of grant-in-aid of Rs. 123.75 lakhs (Rupees One Crore, Twenty Three Lakhs, Seventy Five Thousand only) subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions
(1) The funds released by the Agricultural Universities under the head/ group/ scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the concerned scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as non-scheme schemes. In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancies or other reasons, the subsidy shall be adjusted in the next month's disbursement.
(2) If any of the sub-account, group, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
3) The University shall thoroughly examine whether the sub-heading wise sanctioned schemes and the posts sanctioned under the same have been properly grouped as per the revised pattern. No change in this classification should be made without the approval of the government.
(4) The income from the distributions of the subsidiaries and revenues from each group shall be used to meet the expenditure on permissible items in that group and for carrying out development works. Subsidies and revenue receipts of one group should not be diverted for schemes/ activities of another group. If more grants are allocated to a group scheme, the amount of the excess grant should be taken into account in the subsequent demand for funds.
The posts are filled as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Rules, 1990.

The grant will be provided only for the salaries and allowances of such posts as the government has approved to continue with the expenditure.
6) Separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained for each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise.
(7) The schemes/ schemes under which the period of non-plan period has expired shall not be continued after the relevant period at the cost of non-plan/ schemes without obtaining the approval of the Government.
(8) The assessment report of each financial year shall be submitted to the Government within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the Agricultural Universities are correct as per the formula and how the expenditure is being made in accordance with the prescribed orders/ procedures. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
9) The Government, while approving some new schemes of the University, has approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
10) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
While making the demand for grants for the year 2020-21, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed scheme-wise, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
13) Don't spend money on posts and things that are out of order.
The Controller and other relevant competent authorities of the University shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
The Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, Parbhani or the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall take necessary action regarding the payment of grants sanctioned under the respective sub-headings to the Controller of Agricultural Universities as mentioned in the Government Decision No. Agriculture and Cooperation Department No. OGU- 1072/18471/ V, dated 27th September, 1972 and Government Decision No. KrishiVi-2003/ CR- 35/20-O, dated 25/4/2003.
