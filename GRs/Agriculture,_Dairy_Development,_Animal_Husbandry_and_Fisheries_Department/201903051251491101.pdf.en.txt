8/16/ EC, dated 21st February, 2019 authorizes extension of date up to 30th September, 2019 for temporary posts included in the administrative departments in their rolls and all existing temporary posts created after approval of the said review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the continuation of these temporary posts from March 1, 2019 to September 30, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Taluka Bijagunan Area (24010261), 248 temporary posts in the scheme (excluding posts lying vacant for more than 6 months), are being approved to be continued as per Form-A from 1st March 2019 to 30th September, 2019.
The expenditure for this post shall be covered under the head of accounts mentioned above and shall be met from the approved budgetary provision for 2019-20.
This Government Decision is based on the Government Decision No. 1004/ Q.No. 12 (Part II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 04th October, 2004 of the Department of Finance, Government Decision No. 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ EC, dated 21st February, 2019 is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments.
