Letter No.4/ ACS/ 25/2018 of the Commissioner of Agriculture, D. 16.03.2019 Preamble: Scheme for Updation of Crop Statistics The Central Government has accorded administrative approval for the scheme for the financial year 2018-19 to the State of Maharashtra vide letter dated 18.05.2018. Accordingly, the state government has given a plan to improve the crop data for this scheme. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 13.06.2018.
Scheme for Improvement of Crop Statistics The Government has approved the continuation of 22 temporary posts in this 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme from 1st March, 2019 to 30th September, 2019 as per the above decision dated 27.02.2019.
The scheme is 100% Centrally Sponsored and the first, second and third instalments for 2018-19 have already been disbursed. Now the central government is. vide letter dated 15.03.2019 for the fourth instalment of Rs. A sum of Rs. 24.20 lakh has been sanctioned. The Commissionerate of Agriculture has requested to make available an amount of Rs. 24.20 lakh on Monthly Fund Distribution System (BEAMS). Accordingly, as per the proposal of the Commissioner of Agriculture, the fourth instalment of the scheme for the improvement of crop statistics has been released.
