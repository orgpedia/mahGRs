Introduction: Due to vacancies in the Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A cadre in the tribal and Naxal-affected districts of Gadchiroli, Chandrapur and Gondia in Vidarbha, it is not possible to effectively implement the schemes of the Agriculture Department to the farmers there. In view of this, filling up of vacant posts. Review meetings were held with the Chief Secretary from time to time. In the meeting, The Chief Secretary has directed to fill up the vacant posts in Gadchiroli, Gondia and Chandrapur districts immediately.
Therefore, in order to effectively implement the various schemes of the Department of Agriculture in Vidarbha by providing adequate manpower in Vidarbha, the Government was considering to transfer the services of Mr. Ganesh Kashinath Ghorpade, who was working in the post of Joint Director (Administration) in the cadre of Superintendent Agriculture Officer, Group-A, Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune, to the original Department of Agriculture and to give him a posting in the vacant post of District Superintendent Agriculture Officer, Gondia in Vidarbha. Accordingly, the Government have decided as under: GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Government, with the approval of the competent authority, hereby approve the transfer and posting of officers in the cadre of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A Superintending Agriculture Officer in the department as shown in the table below.
