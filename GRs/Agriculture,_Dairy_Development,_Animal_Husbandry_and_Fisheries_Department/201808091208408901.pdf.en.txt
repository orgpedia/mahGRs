The proposal-
In a meeting held on 11.6.2005 under the chairmanship of the then Union Agriculture Minister, it was decided to develop a total of eight small fishing harbours on the Konkan coast of the state. Matsya Bandar will be developed under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme in Deogarh, Sindhudurg. The Government have decided to provide Rs. 22.7.2004 for the work of this fishpond. The administrative approval was given for a budget of Rs. 29.13 crore. Further, as on 19.2.2008, Rs. The revised administrative approval was given for a budget of Rs. 35.89 crore. As the budgetary allocation had increased due to the revised plan, the matter of giving administrative approval to the revised project budget with the consent of the Expenditure Priority Committee was under consideration of the government.
