Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) has been implemented in the State from Kharif season 2016 as per the relevant Government Decision No. 2 is being implemented. It was under the consideration of the government to continue the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana for the notified crops in the notified areas of the state in the Kharif season 2019.
Government has decided to implement Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) in the State during Kharif season 2019 by adopting Area Approach for the notified crops in the notified areas as under:
Objectives of the scheme: To provide insurance cover to the farmers in case of crop loss due to adverse conditions like natural calamities, pests and diseases.
2. Maintaining financial stability of farmers even in the most difficult situation of crop loss.
Encourage farmers to use innovative and improved machinery technology and materials.
4. To ensure continuity of credit flow to agriculture sector, which will help in achieving the objectives of food security, crop diversification and accelerated growth and competitiveness of agriculture sector, besides protecting the farmers from production risks.
2.Yes the salient features of the scheme are: 1. The scheme will be applicable only to the notified crops in the areas notified under this Order.
2. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana is mandatory for loanee farmers for notified crops in notified areas and optional for non-loanee farmers.
Farmers who are cultivating the notified crops in the notified area on lease or lease in addition to the account holders are eligible to participate in the scheme.

7. Insurance Coverage: Under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, farmers will get insurance cover for losses due to unavoidable reasons.
7.1.Prevented sowing/ planting/ germination due to adverse climatic conditions.
The insurance cover will be payable if the sown/ un-sown area is more than 75% of the normal area only for the notified major crops which could not be sown/ transplanted in the notified area on a large scale due to adverse conditions of inadequate rainfall, climatic conditions etc.
7.2 On Account Payment of Claims due to Mid-Season Adversity Insurance cover shall be paid in case of adverse weather conditions during the season, but not earlier than 15 days before the normal harvest, if the expected production of the farmers is expected to fall by more than 50% of the average production due to floods, rain interruptions, drought, etc.

7.4 Localised Calamities: Under this, the loss of notified crop in a particular area due to damage caused by local natural calamities such as hailstorm, landslide, inundation of insured protected area, cloudburst or lightning, etc. will be determined at the individual level.

For this, the" Apni Sarkar Seva Kendra" (Digital Service Center) facility, operated by CSCE Governance Services India Limited in the state, has been made available to the farmers for filing applications under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana. Our government authorized service centers operating in the state will help the farmers in filling the crop insurance applications on behalf of the insurance company appointed by the Department of Agriculture and will accept the insurance premiums. Documents required for insurance application such as 7/12 extract, Aadhaar card, bank account details, etc. will be linked to the application form online. In order to bring ease, transparency and accountability in the process of delivery of government services, facilities, benefits and payments to the beneficiaries through the use of Aadhaar, the Central Government vide its Gazette dated 08.02.2017 has made it mandatory for all the borrower and non-borrower farmers participating in the scheme to get their Aadhaar card authenticated from Kharif season 2017 onwards.
Since Aadhaar card of farmers has been made mandatory for participation in the scheme, all banks are required to obtain Aadhaar number of their farmers. This will be applicable to non-loanee farmers registered with banks as well as insurance companies and insurance intermediaries. If Aadhaar card is not available, then it is necessary to register Aadhaar and submit its proof for participation in the crop insurance scheme.
As per the notification of Aadhaar Act, it is mandatory for all banks to obtain Aadhaar/ Aadhaar enrolment number while sanctioning crop loan/ Kisan Credit Card under the interest subvention scheme. Therefore, there is no question of loanee farmers joining the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana without Aadhaar.
