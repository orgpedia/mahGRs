The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 1, a" given committee" has been appointed under the chairmanship of Secretary (Padum) to determine the purchase and sale rate of milk for the Government Milk Scheme in the State. The reference No. The Committee met on 11.09.2020 in pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Dairying vide letter No. In the meeting, it was decided to revise the selling price of cow milk under" Aarey Shakti" scheme, cow toned milk (3.5/8.5) and full cream buffalo milk (6.0/9.0).
As per the decision taken in the meeting, the government was considering to revise the sale price of" Aarey Shakti" cow milk, revise the sale price of cow toned milk (3.8/8.5) and full cream buffalo milk (6.0/9.0) and revise the commission rate paid to milk distributors under the government milk scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby approves the following revision in the selling price of Aarey Shakti Cow Milk, Toned Milk and Buffalo Cream Milk for sale under the Brihanmumbai Milk Scheme with effect from 16.10.2020:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The type of milk         | Previous sales rate                    | The improved sales rate                |
| 1.   | " Aarey Shakti" cow milk | Rs.41/-per litre Rs.21/-per half litre | Rs.42/-per litre Rs.22/-per half litre |
| 2.   | Cow's milk toned         | Rs.37/- per litre                      | Rs.38/- per litre                      |
| 3.   | Full-fat milk            | Rs.46/- per litre                      | Rs.47/- per litre                      |
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
