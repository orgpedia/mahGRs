Accordingly, the officers/ employees of Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai under the aforesaid No. The revised pay scale has been implemented as per the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission. Maharashtra Insecticides Limited is a subsidiary of the Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation. The proposal received under letter No. 3 above for grant of revised pay scale to the officers/ employees of the Company, Akola, as per the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission, was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Insecticides Limited is a subsidiary of Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai. The officers/ employees of the establishment of the Corporation, Akola, shall be entitled to receive the benefits of the provisions of the Government Order dated 30/01/2019 of the Department of Finance and the norms of the Government Order dated 04/08/2021 of the Department of Finance, subject to the conditions mentioned below, with effect from 1st July, 2021. The government is approving the implementation of the revised pay scales and pay metrics mentioned in Section 9.

Maharashtra Insecticides Ltd. , Akola Company issued by Finance Department/ Government regarding 7th Pay Commission e.g. January 30th, 2019.
The terms and conditions of the Government Decisions/ Circulars issued on 20th February, 2019 and from time to time should be adopted as guidelines and the revised pay scales should be implemented accordingly.
(2) The benefit of the revised pay scale should not be more than the pay scale applicable to equivalent posts in the Government. The revised pay scales should be applicable within that limit.
It is clear that 11 out of 77 posts are working in the company. Therefore, the benefit of revised pay scale will be admissible only to the officers/ employees of the cadre approved at present. In case of creation of new cadre/ posts, prior approval of the Government will be required while allowing the said benefit.
(4) If any change is to be made in the posts mentioned in the proposal submitted by the company or the elevation of any post is to be downgraded, then the action regarding filling up of vacant/ lapsed posts/ creation of new posts in the company shall be taken with the prior approval of the Board of Directors and the Government.
The Company shall be fully empowered to take a decision on the payment of arrears for the period from 1st July, 2021 till the implementation of the revised pay scales, either in a lump sum or in a phased manner. The company will also be responsible for ensuring that the financial burden on the corporation does not increase significantly due to the implementation of the revised pay scale.
This decision has been taken with the concurrence of the Finance Department and in informal reference No. 22/ C.U., D. Issued on 17/03/2022.
