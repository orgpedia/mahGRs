Preamble: Administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of National Horticulture Mission under Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State for the year 2023-2024 with a budget outlay of Rs. 21207.25 lakh as per the Government Resolution No. Thereafter, vide letter No. 2, the first instalment of general category funds has been received from the Central Government for the financial year 2023-2024. In this background, the matter of disbursing the sanctioned central share of the first installment of the general category and the corresponding state share of the fund to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: 1. The Government has approved the disbursement of the first installment of Central share of Rs. 1589.25 lakh and corresponding State share of Rs. 1059.50 lakh for implementation of National Horticulture Mission in the State during 2023-2024 to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune.
