Supplementary Letter to the Government: Under the government decision here, a system has been created to appeal against the decision taken by the regional office in the office of the Dairy Development Department. These orders are being implemented by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Milk Federation Limited (MAHANAND). Accordingly, if the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Co-operative Milk Federation is aggrieved by the decision of Maryada (Mahanand), an appeal can be filed against it to the Principal Secretary (Animal Husbandry and Dairying) within 30 days.
