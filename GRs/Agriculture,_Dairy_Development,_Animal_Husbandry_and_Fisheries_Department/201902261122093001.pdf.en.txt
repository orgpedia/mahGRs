Preamble: In order to effectively implement the provisions of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 of the Central Government, the Managing Committees of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Societies have been constituted in each district of the State as per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Establishment and Regulation of Cruelty to Animals) Rules, 2001 vide notification dated 14th March, 2017. It was under consideration of the Government to appoint as non-official members the President of the Gaushala/ Cage Society, members of the charitable organization working on animal issues, persons nominated by the General Committee and persons working in the field of humanitarian work/ animal lovers etc.
In view of the proposal received from the Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry, Amravati, as per the provisions of clause (a) (3) of the notification dated 14/03/2017, the following persons have been appointed as members of the Management Committee of the Animal Clash Prevention Society, Amravati District:
Members of the board of directors of 2, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 are appointed as non-official members. The remaining members are being appointed subject to the condition that their character verification report is favourable. The term of office of the member shall be three years from the date of his appointment.
