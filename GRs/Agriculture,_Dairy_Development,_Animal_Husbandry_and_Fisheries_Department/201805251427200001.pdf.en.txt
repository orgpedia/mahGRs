The Proposal:
A total of 5142 villages with World Bank assistance in 15 districts including 14 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada and Jalgaon district have been covered with about Rs. 4000 crore Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project (PoCRA). The approval has been granted under the Government Decision dated 17/01/2018 and a total of 805 posts have been notified for the project. As per the Government Resolution dated 28/09/2017, 131 posts are to be filled up by appointment or deputation from Government officers/ employees. The state government has signed an agreement with the World Bank for the project. The World Bank on 06/05/2018. The Loan Effectiveness has been implemented as per the contract for the project since 18/05/2018.
The Project Director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project, Mumbai, vide Reference No. 4 and 5 have requested for sanction of Project Allowance to the Government officers/ employees of the project. The government is taking the following decisions regarding the matter of sanctioning project allowance to the officers/ employees.
Government's decision:
1. Government officers and employees working full time under Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjivani Project on deputation or appointment are being allowed to pay 25% of basic pay (basic pay+ grade pay) as project allowance.
The amount of Project Allowance payable to the Government officers and employees under the project shall be subtracted from the project cost and shall be reimbursable by the World Bank.
The state government has partnered with the World Bank for the Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project. The agreement was signed on April 6, 2018. As per the provisions of clause 3 of Annexure-II of the Loan Agreement, the eligible expenditure incurred during the period from the date of the Loan Agreement to the previous 12 months of the project will be reimbursed from the loan amount sanctioned by the World Bank for the project. Therefore, the government officials and employees working full-time under the project. The scheme will be implemented from April 6, 2017. Accordingly, the government officers/ employees who have been transferred to the sanctioned posts under the project. Must have been full-time prior to April 6, 2017. The project allowance should be paid from April 6, 2017, and the government officers/ employees who are transferred should be paid.
Those who have been appointed after April 6, 2017, should be paid project allowance from the date of joining the sanctioned post under the project.
4. Out of 805 posts sanctioned under the project, 131 posts have been sanctioned with project allowance and the list of such posts is annexed to this Government decision at Annexure-A.
The Project Allowance is independent and cannot be considered for any other consideration or purpose (including Dearness Allowance, Daily Allowance, Travel Allowance, Increase in Salary, Fixation of Pay, Retirement Allowance, etc.).
As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 28th September, 2017, if the sanctioned posts of Government officers/ employees under the project have been downgraded and filled up from the junior officers/ employees, the project allowance shall also be admissible to the junior officers/ employees.
7. Project allowance shall also be admissible to the Government officers/ employees if they have to be appointed in exceptional circumstances to the posts to be filled on contract under the project.
Project allowance will not be admissible to the employees working on contractual basis under Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project.
