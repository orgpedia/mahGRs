The Proposal:
The scheme is being implemented in 11 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada- Nanded, Latur, Osmanabad, Jalna, Akola, Amravati, Buldhana, Chandrapur, Yavatmal, Wardha and Nagpur. Accordingly, for effective implementation and control of the project, the Government was contemplating to transfer the services of the following officers as per Reference 3 to the Project Director for the project period.
Government's decision
The dairy development project is being implemented in 11 districts of the Vidarbha and Marathwada regions- Nanded, Latur, Osmanabad, Jalna, Akola, Amravati, Buldhana, Chandrapur, Yavatmal, Wardha and Nagpur. Accordingly, for effective implementation and control of the project, Dr. Abhay Ashok Bhalerao, Livestock Development Officer, Veterinary Clinic Grade-1, Butibori, Tt. Services of Nagpur are being transferred to Vidarbha-Marathwada, NDDB Project Director (for Nagpur Division) for assistance.
Dr Bhalerao's salary and allowances are at the basic level of the post he is currently holding. be appointed to the same position. No deputation allowance will be paid to them for this class of service.
The Livestock Development Officer shall submit all reports relating to the project directly to the Project Director.
The Project Director shall report the performance of the activities under the project to Dr. Bhalerao in his Annual Confidential Report.
