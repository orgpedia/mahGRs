The scheme should be implemented as per the guidelines of the Depletion Prone Area component in the guidelines of the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture issued by the Central Government and as per the guidelines of the Government Decision dated 13th April, 2017 of the Advanced Agriculture Prosperous Farmers Mission at No. 3 under reference.
Every month, the physical and financial progress of the scheme should be updated in Annexure-III along with the guidelines of the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture and the information should also be sent to the State Government.
The detailed quarterly progress report of the scheme should be sent to the Department of Agriculture and Cooperation, Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India as well as to the State Government within a period of 3 months after the end of the financial year.
