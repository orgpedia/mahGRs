Preamble: In order to effectively implement the provisions of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 of the Central Government, the Managing Committees of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Societies have been constituted in each district of the State as per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Establishment and Regulation of Cruelty to Animals) Rules, 2001 vide notification dated 14th March, 2017. It was under consideration of the Government to appoint a maximum of 12 persons as non-official members to the Managing Committee, including the President of the Gaushala/ Cage Society, members of the charitable organization working in the field of animal affairs, persons nominated by the General Committee and persons working in the field of humanitarian work/ animal lovers etc.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 2. In view of the proposal received from the Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry, Kolhapur and Member Secretary, Animal Clash Prevention Committee, Kolhapur, the following persons are being appointed as non-official members on the Management Committee of Animal Clash Prevention Society, Kolhapur District as per the provisions of clause (a) (3) of the notification dated 14/03/2017 under the said proviso. The term of office of the member shall be three years from the date of his appointment.
