Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority As per the letter dated 26/06/2018, out of the 9.54-hectare (95400 sq.m.) area available at Matri Durgashala, Kurla, 55806.84 sq.ft. is earmarked for entry and exit, biduct, site office and other ancillary activities of Metro Phase-II-B Project. The area has been requested to be given to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for 5 years by a temporary lease agreement.
In pursuance of the demand of the Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority and as recommended by the Commissioner Dairy Development, the Commissioner Dairy Development has communicated the decision to take a decision at the government level regarding the leasing of the space demanded by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for the casting yard of the Metro Phase 2B project, entry and exit from the station, biduct, site office, and other ancillary activities of the Metro. As the Metro project is an ambitious project of the government, the matter of accepting the demand of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) was under consideration of the government. Following this, the government took decision:
Government Decision To Allow Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) To Enter And Exit From Casting Yards, Stations, By-Ducts, Site Offices, and Other Metro Facilities For Phase-2B Project

For ancillary activities, Mother Milk Dispensary, Kurla, under the Dairy Development Department, has been allotted 55806.84 sq.ft. The Government is granting approval to lease out the area of the said land for a period of 5 years (five years) subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions
(1) The land shall be used only for the purpose for which it is allotted. If any part of the land is to be used for any other purpose, the prior approval of the Dairy Development Department of the Government will be required.
(2) The land shall not be transferred/ sub-let to any other person. Also, no financial transaction can be carried out in such a way that other persons/ institutions/ companies etc. will have any rights on this land or any part of the land.
Alternative arrangements for offices/ residences used by the Dairy Department on the proposed land will be required to be made by MMRDA.
4) It will be mandatory to take prior permission/ approval/ no-objection certificate etc. from the concerned department/ government machinery as per the existing rules and requirements for construction on the thematic land.
After the expiry of the five-year period, there will be no need for seeking an extension, so MMRDA will be required to start their work immediately and complete it within the prescribed time frame and return the land to the department as soon as the expiry date is over.
6) The MMRDA will be responsible for protecting the site from encroachment.
7) The rent of the said land shall be as per the rate fixed by the Public Works Department and as per the prevailing rules, the rate shall be fixed annually at the rate arrived at in the annual rate statement of the land concerned.
8) The General Manager, Brihanmumbai Milk Scheme, Worli, Mumbai shall be responsible for collecting the annual rental value of the said land in that year.
9. The General Manager, Brihanmumbai Milk Scheme, Worli, Mumbai is being authorized to enter into an agreement with MMRDA in this regard after the Commissioner (Dairy) certifies the agreement for temporary allotment of the proposed land for a period of five years.
After the expiry of the contract, the responsibility of making the said land freely available to the General Manager, Brihanmumbai Milk Scheme, Worli, Mumbai will be of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
