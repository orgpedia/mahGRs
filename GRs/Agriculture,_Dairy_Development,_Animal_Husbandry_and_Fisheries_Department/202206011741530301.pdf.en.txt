The proposal-
Various mechanisms are working to create infrastructure in the state. For example, the Public Works Department, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Mumbai Metropolitan Regional Development Authority, CIDCO, Maharashtra Maritime Board, Mumbai Port Trust, etc., all these agencies undertake infrastructure works in the coastal areas of the state as well as in the bay areas. The projects being constructed through these mechanisms affect the fishermen who earn their livelihood in the project area.
At present, the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation is constructing the Thane Creek Bridge-3 (TCB-3) in the Thane Creek area. The Marayi Fishermen Cooperative Society Limited, which was affected by this TCB-3 project. On the compensation. In the High Court of Bombay, Writ Petition No. 2016/2021.
The High Court dismissed the petition. The order dated 12.08.2021 mandates formulation of a State-wide policy on payment of compensation to fishermen affected by the project. Accordingly, a Committee under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner (Fisheries) was constituted under the relevant Government Order No. 1 to take necessary action
