Preamble: Reference No. 1 provides for opening of a total of 351 bank accounts for implementing 11 Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Agriculture through Public Financial Management System (PFMS) The decision was approved by 1. However, the government has not approved the opening of a bank account for the seed and planting material sub-campaign under the National Agriculture Extension and Technology Mission.
The subsidy received for the central share of the scheme is mutually received by the Maharashtra State Seed Certification System, while the equivalent state share is distributed by the state government.
The Government was considering to approve the opening of a separate Public Financial Management System (PFMS) bank account in the name of the Director, Maharashtra State Seed Certification System, Akola, to draw this grant and to appoint an Assistant Accountant in the office of the Maharashtra State Seed Certification System, Akola, as a Checker and Assistant Superintendent, Maker to handle the financial transactions of this bank account. Following this, the government took decision:
Approval for opening of a separate Public Financial Management System (PFMS) Bank Account in the name of Director, Maharashtra State Seed Certification System, Akola for seed and planting material sub-campaign scheme under National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NAMA) being implemented by the Department of Agriculture
is being provided. The account should be opened in State Bank of India.
