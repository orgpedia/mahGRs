Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2020 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production on a large scale. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, a restructured weather-based crop insurance scheme will be launched in the year 2020 for 6 crops of orange, mango, pomegranate, guava, chikoo and lemon (Mrig Bahar) in the district by taking the revenue board as a component of HDFC ERGO General Insurance Co. Ltd., Bajaj Allianz General Insurance. Agriculture Insurance Company of India Ltd. The insurance companies are referred to as No. Decisions 1 and 2 were implemented.
The Commissionerate of Agriculture has sought the release of State share subsidy for HDFC ERGO General Insurance Co. Ltd. as demanded by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for the year 2020 Mrig Bahar in the restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme. 7 of the State share of Rs. 9,38,21, 484/- was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FAVIO-2020/ PR No. 13/10-A
Government Decision: In view of the recommendation made by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the restructured Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Mrig Bahar 2020, the State share insurance premium subsidy of Rs. 9,38,21, 484/- is being approved by the Government to be made available to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for payment to the insurance companies as per the guidelines of the scheme. This amount is being disbursed for the Deer Spring Season 2020 and will not be allowed for other seasons.
