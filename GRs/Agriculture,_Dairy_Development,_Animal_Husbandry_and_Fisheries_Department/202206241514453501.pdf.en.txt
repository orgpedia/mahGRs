The Maharashtra Livestock Development Board has been set up as an autonomous body to provide specialized services to the livestock breeders by implementing the centrally sponsored National Cattle and Mahishya Breeding Project in the state. The main objective of the board is to preserve and conserve the indigenous bovine breeds of Deoni, Khillar, Gavalau, Lal Kandhari, Dangi and buffalo breeds of Pandharpuri and Nagpuri in their homeland.

(iii) The land of the above areas is being given to the Sheep and Sheep Development Corporation for temporary use and this will not register any organization in the original rights record. Records of land ownership will remain in original form. Also, there will be no change in the ownership of the land and the purpose of land use.
Similarly, this decision will not create any third party rights. The powers of the government to take away the use of the said land given for temporary use will remain intact.
(iv) The Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur and Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation, Pune shall develop the above mentioned areas and carry out programmes related to fodder production, rearing of cows and goats/ sheep etc. from the self-financing sources of the Board/ Corporation. It shall be mandatory for the said institutions to ensure that the land is not used for any other purpose or that the land cannot be used by any third party.
