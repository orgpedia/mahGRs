Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State Government is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2018-19 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the district for 9 crops viz. Grapes, Orange, Mango, Pomegranate, Banana, Mango, Cashew, Guava and Lemon in the year 2018-19.
Reference No. 18 for re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2018-19 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 2.
