General guidelines: 11.1. 3 The provisions of the revised rule book of procedure for office purchases to be made by the government departments issued under the government decision of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor here should be strictly followed.
11.2 All the Committees constituted under this Government Resolution shall carry out the procurement process within the limits of the financial powers conferred by the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-sections-I to V and the powers conferred by the said Financial Powers Rules, taking into account the amendments made/ to be made from time to time. Also, strictly follow the financial rules when carrying out the purchase process.
11.3 Canons of Financial Privacy should be scrupulously followed in the procurement process with a view to protecting the financial interests of the Government.
11.4 The Procurement Committee shall be required to carry out the procurement process by considering the funds available under various schemes during the relevant financial year through Financial District Annual Plans, State Level Schemes, Centrally Sponsored Schemes and other sources.
11.5 All veterinary clinics, disease investigation department, Pune, Veterinary Institute, Pune, State Level Training Centre, Pune, Food Analysis Laboratory, Pune, Quality Control Laboratory, Goregaon, Mumbai and other institutions under the control of the Department of Animal Husbandry should take action in accordance with the provisions of this Government Decision regarding the procurement process of all necessary items.
In accordance with the provisions of this Government Order, the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune shall issue necessary guidelines to all the offices under him forthwith.
