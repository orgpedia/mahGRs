Accordingly, a provision of Rs. 10,15,13, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crore Fifteen Lakhs Thirteen Thousand only) has been made in the Revised Budget 2018-19 for payment of interest on the amount due to the officers/ employees of all the four Agricultural Universities and affiliated institutions in the State from 01.04.2018 to 31.03.2019. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
As per the revised budgetary provision for payment of interest on provident fund for the financial year 2018-2019 to the officers/ employees of the Agricultural Universities and affiliated institutions in the State, an amount of Rs. 10,15,13, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crore Fifteen Lakhs Thirteen Thousand only) is being handed over to the Accountant General, Nagpur, Maharashtra-2 through Book Adjustment.
Senior Treasury Officers, Ahmednagar, Ratnagiri, Akola and Parbhani should take action to record and take note of the adjustment of the above interest amount.
An amount of Rs. 10,15,13, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Fifteen Lakhs Thirteen Thousand only) should be spent in the financial year 2018-19 towards payment of interest on provident fund of the employees of the Agricultural Universities and their affiliated institutions under the head of accounts mentioned below.
