Introduction: The distribution of Soil Health Cards (SHC) under National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) has been started by the Government of India since 2014-15. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between Centre and State since 2015-16. The Central Government has included the schemes of Soil Health Card and Soil Health Management in the sub-campaign of Soil Health and Fertility under the National Agriculture Development Scheme. Soil health cards are distributed to farmers after analysing soil samples. Through soil health cards, farmers are informed about the chemical properties of their land, the level of major food elements and the deficiency and condition of micro elements.
