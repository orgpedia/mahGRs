Out of this unspent amount of Rs. 5190.457 lakh, the Government was considering to approve the utilization of unspent amount of Rs. 2490.697 lakh for general category. Out of the unreserved funds for general category, Rs. 1431.41 lakhs (Centre Rs. 853.23 lakhs+ State Rs. 578.18 lakhs) is available in the PFMS linked bank account of the Mission and Rs. 641.197 lakhs is available at the Central level.
Therefore, the Government have decided to approve the utilization of Rs. 2072.607 lakh of General Category unutilized fund and also to disburse Rs. 641.197 lakh of Central share and Rs. 418.09 lakh of State share available at the Government level out of the said unutilized fund.
Government's decision
A total amount of Rs. 2490.697 lakh (Rs.14,44,49,90,42,77 only) out of the total Central share of Rs. 1494.427 lakh and Rs. 996.27 lakh of the corresponding State share of the unutilized Central share of the funds received for the General category of food crops under the National Food Security Mission in 2018-19 is being approved for utilization under the General category of food crops in 2019-20.
Section 2. Out of the total amount of Rs. 2490.697 lakh, Rs. 853.23 lakh on Central Account and Rs. 578.18 lakh on State Account, a total amount of Rs. 1431.41 lakh is lying in the PFMS linked bank account of National Food Security Mission, which is to be utilized during 2019-20.
Section 3. Out of the total amount of Rs. 2490.697 lakh, Rs. 641.197 lakh (Rs. Six crore forty one lakh nineteen thousand seven hundred only) in the Central Account and Rs. 418.09 lakh (Rs.
