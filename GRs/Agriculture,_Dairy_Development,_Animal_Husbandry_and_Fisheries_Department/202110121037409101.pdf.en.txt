Circular: In the context of the General Administration Department. As per the Government Decision dated 21.10.2011, a comprehensive policy has been formulated for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
General Group-B (Technical Group/ Administration Group/ Working Group Combined) in Dairy Development Department (Pay Scale S-15: Rs. 41800-132300). An interim seniority list showing the status of 1/1/2021 is being circulated through this circular. Also, if there are objections/ errors regarding this temporary seniority, the concerned should submit a statement within fifteen days. Any objections received after that will not be considered. The facts of the representations received should be examined from the records available to the respondents and these representations should be sent to the Government with detailed feedback. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the stipulated period, the final seniority will be published, assuming that no one has any objection to this provisional seniority list.
