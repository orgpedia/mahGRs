Government's decision:
As per the guidelines of the Central Government, Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana will be implemented in the state for the notified crops in the notified areas in Kharif season 2018. 24.05.2018 and D. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 13.06.2018. Under this scheme, the last date for participation of loanee farmers for Kharif season 2018 has been extended. As on 31.07.2018, the last date for participation of non-loanee farmers is Reference No. The government's decision 4. 24.07.2018. However, now considering the technical difficulties faced by the non-loanee farmers to join the scheme, with a view to provide adequate time to them, Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) has been extended to non-loanee farmers for Kharif season 2018. from 25.07.2018. The period is being extended upto 31.07.2018.
The schedule of the scheme for loanee and non-loanee farmers will be as mentioned below.
