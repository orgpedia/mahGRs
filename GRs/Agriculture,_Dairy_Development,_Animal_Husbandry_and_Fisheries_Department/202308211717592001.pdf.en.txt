The Proposal:
Details of various schemes/ components to be implemented in the State in the year 2023-24 under Coconut Development Board, Kochi Sponsored Coconut Development Scheme including financial/ physical aspects
This is given in Schedule-1 of the Board's letter dated 11.07.2023. The Coconut Development Board, Kochi vide its letter dated 11.07.2023 has accorded administrative approval for an amount of Rs. 132.52 lakhs for the implementation of the scheme in the State. Out of this, the share of Coconut Development Board is Rs. 112.43 lakh and the scheme/ component wise allocation of financial targets has been ensured for SC sub-plan, ST sub-plan and general category wise. The components/ items to be implemented by the State under the scheme are mentioned. Guidelines for implementation of the scheme have been issued.
The funds of the Coconut Development Board will be available after receiving administrative approval from the state government to implement the scheme in the state. for the implementation of this plan.
Administrative approval was sought from the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Centrally Sponsored Coconut Development Board, Kochi vide Reference No. Considering the approval given under letter 1, administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of Coconut Development Scheme in the State for the year 2023-24 with Central share of Rs. 112.43 lakh, State share of Rs. 18.00 lakh and beneficiary farmer share of Rs. 2.09 lakh for a total amount of Rs. 132.52 lakh (Rupees One Crore Thirty Two Lakh Fifty Two Thousand Only).

The action plan to be implemented in the State in the year 2023-24 under the Coconut Development Board, Kochi sponsored Coconut Development Scheme has been attached as" Schedule-I" with this Government decision. The plan should be implemented according to the approved components of the work plan.
As the area of coconut cultivation is more in the Konkan region, the office of the Accountant Divisional Joint Director of Agriculture, Thane, which is under the Divisional Joint Director of Agriculture, Thane, is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Director (Horticulture) as the Controlling Officer.
4. The Director (Horticulture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorized to issue target-wise guidelines for implementation of the scheme and to take action to make available funds from Coconut Development Board, Kochi for implementation of the scheme.
The Director (Horticulture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State Pune shall make a separate proposal for disbursement of the State share of funds after the availability of funds from the Coconut Development Board.
The list of beneficiaries (with mobile number) who have given personal benefits in the last two years should be posted at Taluk Offices/ Panchayat Samitis/ Collector's Office. The list should also be published on the website of the Agriculture Commissioner.
Monthly and quarterly reports of the implementation of the scheme as well as utility certificates from time to time should be submitted to the Coconut Development Board and the Government before the 10th of every month.
