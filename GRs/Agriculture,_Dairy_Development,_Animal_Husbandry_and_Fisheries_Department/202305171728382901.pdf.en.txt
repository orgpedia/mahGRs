(b) Since the promotion is temporary in nature, the entitlement to the benefit of service cannot be determined from the date of promotion.
(a) If the concerned officers refuse temporary promotion to the post of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A, the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ K. 12, D. 12/09/2016, General Administration Department, Government Decision No. SRV 2018/ Q.No.159/ Ka.12, D. As per the provision in 01/08/2019, the names of the concerned officers will be removed from the selection list for the year 2022-23 and their eligibility will be checked for temporary promotion to the post of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A in the third year selection list (2025-26) without considering their names in the selection list for the next two years (2023-24 and 2024-25).
