The 31 st State Level Project Sanctioning Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra has approved the implementation of the project' Anti Hell Net Cover for Pomegranate Crop under Protected Agriculture Component' in the National Agriculture Development Scheme in the year 2022-23. A total of Rs. 1072.22 lakh will be implemented and Rs. 541.42 lakh will be provided.
Accordingly, the State Level Project Sanctioning Committee has sanctioned Rs. 541.42 lakh has been sanctioned.

The government has taken the following decisions:
