Regarding the provision of head of accounts for the State share of Accident Group Insurance Scheme under the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No: Matsyavi-1121/ PR No. 156/ Padum-14
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of release: May 4, 2022
Reference- Government Resolution No. Matsyavi 1020/ P.No. 100/ Padum 14, Dy. 17.11.2020 Preamble- The scheme of" Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana" to usher in blue revolution through sustainable development of fisheries sector in the State has been accorded administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The scheme also includes an insurance scheme for fishermen. The scheme is 60 per cent funded by the central government and 40 per cent by state governments. Since the central share of the fund would be deposited directly with the insurance company, the government was considering opening a new head of account to provide for the state share.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Planning, Finance Department and Accountant General's Office have approved the opening of new head of accounts for the state share of Accident Group Insurance Scheme under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.
After the approval of the Accountant General, the Finance Department has been requested to provide statistics for these headings.
