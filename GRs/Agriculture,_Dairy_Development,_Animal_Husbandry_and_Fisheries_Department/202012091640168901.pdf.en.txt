Various fishermen's organizations and people's representatives had demanded that the fishermen of Konkan should be announced compensation for the economic loss and fish loss due to the impact on their livelihood due to the cyclone, the lockdown to prevent the spread of corona and the ban on monsoon fishing. Similarly, in the Budget Session of 2020, the Minister of State for Fisheries, Shri. on 24.02.2020. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary to this effect. Therefore, the matter of forming a committee under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Padum) regarding the fish crisis was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the formation of the following committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Padum) to take a decision on the fish disaster caused by the cyclone in the coastal areas of Konkan.

(2) The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(1) The State Government shall make a detailed study of the reduced fish production in the coastal areas of the State during the year 2019-20 from the data of fish production of fish caught with the help of mechanized and non-mechanized fishing boats according to the type of fishing method and recommend to the Government on the improvement of the fish drought situation in the coastal areas of the State.
(2) The Department of Fisheries of the State and the Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute, Cochin shall study the method of compiling marine fisheries production and recommend to the Government whether or not a comprehensive composite fisheries production compiling system can be implemented in the State.
(3) The rates and norms of assistance to persons affected by natural calamities shall be revised by the Central Government on the lines of the norms of the State Disaster Response Force (SDRF) and the State Disaster Response Force (SDRF) for recommending to the Central Government the rates and norms of assistance which would be useful to the marine fishermen.
(4) Matters which are not covered under the compensation of (SRDF/ NDRF). Suggest a scheme of assistance for compensation on behalf of the State Government till it is approved by the NDRF.
(5) To find out the reasons for the decline in fish production in the coastal areas of Maharashtra and suggest a solution plan for sustainable marine fishing.
The honorarium, travel allowance and related allowances to the chairman and members of the committee shall be admissible as per the policy prescribed by the Finance Department. The expenditure on the proposed item should be apportioned under the head Office Expenditure of the Commissioner, Office. The action in this regard should be taken through the Commissioner Fisheries Office.
