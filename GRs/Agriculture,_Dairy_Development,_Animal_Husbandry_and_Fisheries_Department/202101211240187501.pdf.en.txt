Preamble: The Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. of India has decided to extend the benefit of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme to eligible students from economically weaker sections. Pursuant to the Government decision dated 7.10.2017, the Department of Agriculture and Padum has issued Reference No. 1 here. The Government has issued a decision dated 14.1.2019.
The Cabinet 2. The General Administration Department has issued a notification regarding the action taken as per the decision taken on 22.9.2020. The Government has issued a decision on 9.10.2020. As per the order, the benefits of both Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme are admissible to the Socially and Educationally Backward Class (SEBC) students (after fulfilling the prescribed terms and conditions). The Government has issued a decision on 16.12.2020. As per the decision of the Higher and Technical Education Department, the government was considering to implement this scheme for eligible beneficiary students admitted to agricultural universities and colleges affiliated to them.
Government's decision:
The benefits of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme will be automatically admissible to the students belonging to Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) in the agricultural universities and colleges affiliated to them in the state.
