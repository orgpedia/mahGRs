the prelude
Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority As per the letter dated 26/06/2018, out of the 9.54-hectare (95400 sq.m.) area available at Matri Durgashala, Kurla, 10338.26 sq.km.has been earmarked for entry and exit, biduct, site office, and other ancillary activities of the Metro Phase IIB project. A request has been made to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for the allotment of the land.
In pursuance of the demand of the Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority and as recommended by the Commissioner Dairy Development, the Commissioner Dairy Development has communicated the decision to take a decision at the government level regarding the permanent allotment of the site sought by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for the casting yard of the Metro Phase 2B project, entry and exit from the station, biduct, site office, and other ancillary activities of the Metro. As the Metro project is an ambitious project of the government, the matter of accepting the demand of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) was under consideration of the government. Following this, the government took decision:

Government Resolution No. 1318/ PR No. 87/ Padum 9
Government's decision
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) has been allotted 10338.26 square feet of land no. 02 of Matri Dairy Hospital Kurla under the Dairy Development Department for the entry and exit from the casting yard, station, biduct, site office, and other ancillary activities of the Metro Phase-2B project. The Government is approving the transfer to the Revenue and Forest Department for the transfer of the m. m. area to the Urban Development Department on the following terms and conditions.
Terms and conditions.
(1) The land shall not be used for any purpose other than the purpose for which it is proposed to be constructed, nor shall any mortgage or mortgage be taken against the land, nor shall any transfer of the land by any other means be permitted.
Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Bandra, shall ensure that the transfer of land does not violate the provisions of the land related and other environmental laws.
3) As per the map submitted by MMRDA, 10338.26 sq.km. General Manager Matrudugshala Kurla should take action to give them control after counting so many worlds.
(4) The Government shall not be liable for any judicial or other proceedings arising out of this matter.
