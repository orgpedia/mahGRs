Introduction: As per the policy of the Government, Public Financial Management System (PFMS) is adopted for Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Aided Schemes of the Department of Agriculture. For this, the government has declared the accountant/ superintendent in the office of the district superintendent of agriculture officer as the maker and the project director (Atma) as the checker for the traditional agricultural development scheme. To implement the Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana through Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola through Public Financial Management System, the government was considering to declare the Accountant/ Superintendent in the office of the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer, Akola as Maker and Project Director, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola as Checker to handle the financial transactions of the bank account of Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
To implement the Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana through Public Financial Management System through Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola, the District Superintending Agriculture Officer, the Accountant/ Superintendent in the office of Akola will be the Maker and Project Director, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola will be the Checker to handle the financial transactions of the bank account of Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission, Akola.
