The Proposal:
Integrated Horticulture Mission is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the development of fruits, vegetables, tubers, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew, cocoa and bamboo products. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between Centre and State since 2015-16.
Krishi Unnati Yojana- Under the Integrated Horticulture Development Mission, the National Horticulture Mission is being implemented in the State. 4 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. The Central Government Reference No. 3 As per the letter, the Central share of funds for Scheduled Castes is Rs. 570.00 lakh and accordingly State share of Rs. 380.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 950.00 lakh to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune for implementation of National Horticulture Mission. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare, Government of India. As per the letter dated 25.5.2018, the central share distributed for SC category is Rs. 570.00 lakhs and accordingly 40% of the State share of Rs. 380.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 950.00 lakh, out of the approved budgetary provision, is being given financial sanction by the Government.
The funds available in the budget for this scheme should be spent under the following head of accounts for the general, scheduled caste and scheduled tribe categories as prescribed by the Central Government.
