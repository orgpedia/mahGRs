Preamble: The State Level Project Approval Committee of the National Agriculture Development Scheme. In the 24th meeting held on 15/12/2017, under the National Agriculture Development Scheme" Mechanization for Turmeric Crop
The project has been approved for implementation during 2017-18 and 2018-19. The matter of giving administrative approval to implement this scheme in the state was under consideration of the government, the government is taking the following decisions.
Decision of the Government: 1. Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the project titled" Mechanization and Post-Harvest Management for Turmeric Crop" is being given administrative approval for the financial years 2017-18 and 2018-19. A total of Rs. 10.01 crore is being sanctioned under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana and the funds will be made available to Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune in a phased manner.
The components approved under the Mechanization and Post-Harvest Management Project for Turmeric Crop and its financial and physical targets will be as follows.

| Turmeric Polisher      | 183                              | 183          |
| Turmeric storage house | 39                               | 487.50       |
| Total                  | 406                              | 1001.70      |
