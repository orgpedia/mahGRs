Government Order No. Under the Government Decision No. 1, the General Administration Department has specified the criteria for the eligibility of government officers/ employees to remain in service beyond the age of 50/55 years. Accordingly, reference No. Under Section 2, the Departmental Review Committee has been constituted to review the Group-A and Group-B cadre officers in the establishment of the Agriculture Department to review and recommend the eligibility of the officers eligible for review under the Agriculture Department to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service. Accordingly, the Review Committee has approved the continuation of the services of the following officers of the Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-A, Superintending Agricultural Officer Cadre before the age of 50/55 years.
