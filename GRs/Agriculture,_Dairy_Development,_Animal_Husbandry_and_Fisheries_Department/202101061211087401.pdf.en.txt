Introduction Under the National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture, the Rain-fed Area Development (RAD) scheme is being implemented with a Central and State share of 60:40 subsidy. As per the Government decision at Reference No.4, the Central Share for the year 2020-21 is Rs. 1191.09 lakhs and State share of Rs. 795.60 lakh. Administrative approval has been accorded for Annual Action Plan for an amount of Rs. 2615.52 lakhs.
The Central Government vide letter dated 11.9.2020 in Reference No.3 has sanctioned Rs. 56.05 lakh has been communicated to the Pay and Accounts Officer (Sector) for disbursement to the State Government.
Accordingly, under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA), the Centre has released the first instalment of Rs. 56.05 lakh and Rs. 37.37 lakh. 93.42 lakh to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Financial Management System (BDS) was under the consideration of the Government.
