Preamble: A new defined contribution pension scheme has been implemented for the teaching and non-teaching staff in the service of Maharashtra Veterinary and Animal Sciences University on or after 1st November, 2005 as per the decision of the Department dated 7th January, 2011. Finance Department No. 2 The Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS) of the State Government has been subsumed in the National Pension Scheme (NPS) of the Centre vide Government Resolution dated 27/08/2014 and the same has been subsumed in the National Pension Scheme (NPS) of the Government of India vide No. 3 The Government have, vide Government Resolution dated 06/04/2015, prescribed the (Level 1) procedure for National Pension Scheme applicable to State Government employees as well as All India Service officers of Maharashtra cadre. Accordingly, the No. The Maharashtra Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (MAMSU) has decided to implement the National Pension Scheme (NPS) for the teachers and non-teaching staff who will join the service on or after November 1, 2005. In addition, instructions were given to the concerned officers/ employees to implement the scheme by reforming the system of free service in accordance with all the provisions of the said Government Decision. Accordingly, the National Pension Scheme (NPS) has been implemented from April, 2020 with necessary reforms in the system of social security.
Now, the amount deducted, employer's share and interest thereon for the month of April, 2020 of the officers/ employees applying for the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS) of the State Government is required to be transferred to the National Pension Scheme (NPS). The government is considering to transfer the amount to the National Pension System (NPS).
