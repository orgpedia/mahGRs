As per the guidelines of the Government of India, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme is being implemented from the year 2021-22 to 2023-24 for the mrig and ambia bahar as per the Government Decision dated 18.06.2021. Under this scheme, Ambia Bahar has set the deadline for participation in the 2023-24 mango crop in Konkan and cashew and orange crops in all the states. 30.11.2023 has been fixed. However, considering the technical difficulties faced by the farmers in joining the scheme, the Central Government has decided to extend the period for joining the scheme. (2) In accordance with the approval given under the terms and conditions mentioned in the letter, the restructured weather-based fruit insurance scheme Ambia Bahar will provide online insurance applications to farmers for the mango crop of Konkan and cashew and orange crops of all states in 2023-24. 4.12.2023 and dt. The due date is being extended by two days i.e. 5.12.2023. However, necessary publicity and publicity campaign should be carried out by the concerned insurance company so that maximum number of farmers can participate in this period.
