As it has come to the notice that many seed producing companies have illegally produced and sold seeds using Transgenic Herbicide/ Glyphosate Tolerant Trait which is not allowed in cotton seeds, in this regard, under the Government decision dated 03.03.2018, Shri. Special Inspector General of Police (IGP) V.S. A Special Investigation Team (SIT) has been formed under the Mumbai Police Commissioner to investigate the case. The terms, conditions and conditions in this regard are as mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 07.02.2018.
The committee has been given a month's time to submit its report. In this regard, as per the Government decision dated 30th June, 2018, the time period for submission of the report to the said team has been extended by five months. However, during the extended period, the Maratha reservation agitation started at various places in the state, the law and order situation created by it, the security arrangements for VIPs in the state, the additional charge assigned to them and the investigation of police crimes registered earlier by the Special Investigation Team.
