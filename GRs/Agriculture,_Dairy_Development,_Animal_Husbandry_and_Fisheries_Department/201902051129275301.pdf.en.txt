Introduction: The recommendations of the B. K. Chaturvedi Committee appointed by the Central Planning Commission have been accepted by the Central Government. Accordingly, all Centrally Sponsored Schemes have been restructured into 66 Centrally Sponsored Schemes. The Government of India has decided to integrate some of these restructured schemes into a Mission mode from 2014-15 onwards.
Under the National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology (NMOET), a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, Seeds and Plantation Material Sub-Mission (SMSP) is being implemented.
This sub-mission is being implemented as per the guidelines of the Central Government with a view to improve the availability of quality seeds to the farmers at reasonable prices and in a time-bound manner by augmenting and expanding the existing scheme in the form of a mission to include new technologies in the production of quality or certified seeds for improving seed testing laboratories, assisting seed production institutions to adopt new seed production technologies, etc. within the scope of this mission

227/1 The entire seed chain ranges from production of centrifugal seeds to supply of certified seeds to farmers, to support creation of infrastructure for seed sector development, to deposit seeds in banks to meet the requirement of seeds during natural calamities. This sub mission includes the Seed Village Programme, support for certified seed production.
As per the letter dated 04.06.2018, Government of India has sanctioned Rs. 245.82 lakh for Centrally Sponsored Village Seed Production Programme (Central Share 60%) for STs and Rs. 24.20 lakh for Certified Seed Production Programme (Central Share 60%) under Village Seed Production Programme (CGSP). 270.02 lakh has been made available directly to Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (Mahabeej), Akola, thereby taking the State share (40%) to Rs. 180.01 lakh for STs.
As per the letter dated 20th November, 2018 in Reference No.5, the Central Government has provided Rs. 427.86 lakhs directly to Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIZ), Akola for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored" Village Seed Production Programme" in Rabi Season 2018-19 (Central share 60%).
In this regard, the State share of Rs. 465.25 lakh for STs for implementation of" Village Seed Production Programme" (Central and State share 60: 40) in the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Seeds and Plantation Material Sub Mission under National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NAMA) in 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 465.25 lakh is being released to Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIZ), Akola under the head of accounts 2401A 637 for Scheduled Tribes for the implementation of" Village Seed Production Programme" in 2018-19 season (Central and State share 60:40) under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Seeds and Plantation Material Sub Mission under National Agriculture Extension and Technology Mission.
The following conditions must be met when implementing the plan:
1) The scheme should be implemented in accordance with the guidelines issued by the Central Government and the terms and conditions of the release of funds by the Central Government.
2) The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the Central Government along with the progress report as soon as possible.
3) The physical and financial progress report on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central Government by the 5th of every month.
