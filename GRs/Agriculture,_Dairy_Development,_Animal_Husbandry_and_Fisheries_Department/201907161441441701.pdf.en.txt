Preamble: As per the official order of NITI Aayog, a high-powered committee comprising of Hon'ble Chief Ministers of various states of the country has been constituted to take various measures to improve the income of farmers by bringing about a radical change in the agriculture sector in the country. The Chief Minister of Maharashtra has been appointed.
The government has taken several steps to increase the income of farmers. In line with the above committee, the matter of appointing an experienced and expert person to advise the Agriculture Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Mr. Umesh Chandra Sarangi, former Additional Chief Secretary, Agriculture (Retired) and Chairman, NABARD is being appointed as an expert to guide and advise the Agriculture Department in taking various measures in the agriculture sector of the State. The terms, conditions and other terms and conditions of the appointment are as follows:
The appointment is initially for a period of three months. This period will be extended from time to time.
Umesh Chandra Sarangi, former Additional Chief Secretary, Agriculture and Chairman, NABARD (Retd.
