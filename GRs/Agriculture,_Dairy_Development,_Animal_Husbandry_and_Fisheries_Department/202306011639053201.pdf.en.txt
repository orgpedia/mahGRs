GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SANKIND-2023/ PR No. 16/12-A
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 1st of June, 2023
Government's decision: Chief Minister on various demands of farmers. Under the chairmanship of Shri. Minister of Agriculture. The meeting was held on May 9, 2023. The issues raised in the meeting included reduction in input costs, difficulties in setting up agro and allied processing units/ projects, fixation of FRP for sugarcane and suggesting reforms for farmer friendly implementation of various schemes. A committee has been formed to look into these issues. The Chief Minister has given instructions. The committee will hold discussions with the representatives of various farmers' organizations of the state. The following committees have been formed:
Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune Chairman 2. Commissioner (Sugar), Commissionerate of Sugar, Pune Member 3. Commissioner (Dairy Business), Worli, Mumbai Member 4. Director (Agricultural Processing and Planning), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune Member 5. Farmers Representative (3) Member
The committee will submit its report to the government.
