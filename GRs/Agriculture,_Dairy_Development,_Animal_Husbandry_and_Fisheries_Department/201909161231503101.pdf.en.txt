Distribution of 100 groups of eggs per district to a total of 1,000 women beneficiaries of 100 SHGs in 19 districts of Beed, Latur, Osmanabad, Nanded and Hingoli at 50% subsidy, providing 50% subsidy for 1,000 beneficiaries per district in 10 districts of Washim, Yavatmal, Hingoli, Amravati, Nagpur, Wardha, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gadchiroli and Gondia out of 19 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada and construction of new bird sheds to increase the existing bird rearing capacity of Central Egg Harvesting Centre, Nagpur by 2,000 birds, construction of new hatchers and setters and Ready Cement Sla for biosecurity.
Objectives of the scheme: To provide self-employment to women in rural areas.
2. To promote domestic egg production by promoting domestic poultry.
3. Strengthening of Self-Help Groups and providing source of income to women.
To empower women economically.
Increase availability of eggs and poultry meat in rural as well as urban areas.
To achieve better nutrition of the family by providing balanced diet through availability of eggs and poultry meat.
Format of the Scheme: The Scheme comprises of various three component schemes as mentioned below.
A. Component No. Construction of new bird sheds, new hatchers and setters to increase the present capacity of Central Egg Harvesting Centre, Nagpur by 2,000 birds and construction of 1 km long 8 feet high wall of Ready Cement Slabs for biosecurity.
B. Component No. 2 Distribution of 100 improved indigenous breeds (CARI approved) of poultry birds at 50% subsidy for 16 weeks to a total of 19,000 women beneficiaries of 100 SHGs (10 women members per SHG) per district in 19 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada namely Washim, Yavatmal, Akola, Amravati, Nagpur, Wardha, Bhandara, Buldhana, Chandrapur, Gondia, Gadchiroli, Aurangabad, Jalna, Parbhani, Beed, Latur, Osmanabad, Nanded and Hingoli.
Component Plan No. 3.
In 10 of the 19 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada region, namely Washim, Yavatmal, Hingoli, Amravati, Nagpur, Wardha, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gondia and Gadchiroli, 1,000 women beneficiaries per district will be provided protective shelter for rearing 100 hens per day at 50% cost i.e. Rs. 5,000/- as subsidy.
Mode of implementation of the scheme: Under the component scheme of Strengthening of Central Egg Harvesting Center, Nagpur, the new Setter and Hatcher should be purchased by adopting the government method in the scheme to increase the capacity of 2,000 more birds in the said institution. Component No. The component scheme should be implemented in cluster form by allocating 100 groups of poultry birds (CARI approved/ commercial layer birds) to 1,000 women beneficiaries per district in all the districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada divisions at 50% subsidy. In a village cluster, at least 5 to a maximum of 10 self-help groups should be selected on a cluster basis and their members should be given the benefit of the scheme. Priority should be given to women self-help group members. At least 10% of the beneficiaries under this scheme should be from Scheduled Castes and 7% of the beneficiaries should be from Scheduled Tribes.
If more number of applications of eligible beneficiaries are received as compared to the set targets, the beneficiaries should be selected by the selection committee in a staggered manner.
