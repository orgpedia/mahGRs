There are 24 laboratories in the state, including 3 seed testing laboratories, 5 fertilizer control laboratories, 4 pesticide testing laboratories, 2 pesticide residue testing laboratories and 10 biological pest control laboratories. During the financial year 2018-19, a total amount of Rs. 1926.37 lakhs was provided in the head of accounts 2401 0305 (mandatory) for the expenses of computerization of laboratories, supply and maintenance of materials and primers, chemicals, germination paper, wax paper, maintenance of laboratory equipment, provision of manpower on contract basis through outsourcing. 21. Supplies and Materials, 52. Since there was less provision for machinery and equipment, the Commissionerate of Agriculture was requested to provide funds through supplementary demand. Accordingly, to meet the expenses of the above mentioned laboratories, funds have been made available through supplementary demand in the Winter Session 2018 and the matter of providing these funds was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: To meet the expenses of Seed Testing Laboratories, Fertilizer Control Laboratories, Pesticide Testing Laboratories, Pesticide Residue Testing Laboratories and Biological Pest Control Laboratories in the State, a supplementary demand of Rs. 76.10 lakh has been approved for 21 supplies and materials and Rs. 44.05 lakh for 52 machinery and equipment in the head 2401 0305 (mandatory) for a total of Rs. 120.15 lakh. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of the circular dated 2nd April, 2018 of the Finance Department, an amount of Rs. 53.27 lakhs and 52- Rs. 30.83 crore. 84.10 lakh is being disbursed.
