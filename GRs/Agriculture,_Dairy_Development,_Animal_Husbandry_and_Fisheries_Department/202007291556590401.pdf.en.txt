Finance Department, Government Resolution No: Arthasam-2020/ PR No. 65/ Arth-3, dated 04th May, 2020. 128/9-O, dated 29th June, 2020 Preamble: The State Government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme for the farmers who are not eligible to cultivate orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), as per the decision of the Government in Reference No.1 above. As per the Government Resolution No.3, the Department of Finance has directed to take fiscal measures to address the impact of COVID-19 pandemic on the economy in the financial year 2020-21. Against this backdrop, as per the Government decision in Reference No.4, administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of the scheme in 2020-21 within the limit of 33% of the budgetary provision. Now the matter of distribution of funds for the general category was under consideration for the implementation of the said scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: 1. Administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Plantation Scheme in the State for the year 2020-21 within the limit of 33% of the budgetary provision in the year 2020-21 and at present, the Government is approving the disbursement of 20% of the budgetary provision for the general category i.e. Rs. 20.00 crore out of the budgeted amount of Rs. 100.00 crore for the year 2020-21 for the general category.
(2) The funds shall be spent out of the provisions budgeted for the current year under the following headings:
