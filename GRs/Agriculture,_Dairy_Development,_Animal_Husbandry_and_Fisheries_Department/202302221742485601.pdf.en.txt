Letter dated 07.02.2023 to the Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune vide G.O./ Accounts/ 2nd Installment Demand/ 4444/2023.
Preamble: Under the National Horticulture Mission under Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (KUADM) in the State for the year 2022-2023, administrative approval has been accorded for a programme with an outlay of Rs. 19933.97 lakh under Government Resolution No. 2. The first installment of general category funds has been disbursed as per the decision of the government at reference number 3. Thereafter, as per Reference No. 4, the second instalment of general category funds has been received from the Central Government for the financial year 2022-2023. In this background, the matter of disbursement of funds for the second installment of the general category to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune was under the consideration of the Government. The government is taking the following steps:
Government Decision: 1. The Government is approving the disbursement of funds to the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune for the second installment of Central share of Rs. 1701.00 lakh and corresponding State share of Rs. 1134.00 lakh, totalling Rs. 2835.00 lakh (Rupees Twenty-eight crore and Thirty-five lakh only) for the implementation of National Horticulture Mission 2022-2023 under Krishi Unnati Yojana- Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State.
