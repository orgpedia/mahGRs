The Proposal:
In line with the Union Government's vision to double farmers' income by 2022, inputs like inputs, training, irrigation, mechanization, post-harvest technologies, agro processing and marketing etc.are critical to increase crop production. Considering the need for collective farming, modern methods of farming and strict planning for a substantial increase in the production of farmers due to the low land holding of farmers in the state, the scheme of" Promotion of Group Farming of Farmers for Promotion and Empowerment of Group Farming" has been implemented. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 5.10.2018. The scheme will be implemented during 2017-18 and 2018-19.
As a result of increasing population and land fragmentation, the per capita area of cultivated land has declined.
Therefore, to make farming economically affordable to the farmers,' Driving Farmers' Group Farming' has been started to encourage and empower group farming. In view of this objective and the fruitful results of the scheme, the scheme" Promotion of group farming of farmers for promoting and empowering group farming" has been extended for two years. The matter of payment from 1.4.2019 to 31.3.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: 1 To implement the scheme" Promotion of Group Farming of Farmers for Promotion and Empowerment of Group Farming," a two-year time limit has been fixed. The period of extension is from 1.4.2019 to 31.3.2021.
2 This Order is in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Government Business Rules No. 15. are being issued subject to approval received under 29 (1).
