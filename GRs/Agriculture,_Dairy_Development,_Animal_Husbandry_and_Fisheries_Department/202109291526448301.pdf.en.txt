Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) to be implemented for three years from Kharif season 2020 and Rabi season 2020-21 29.06.2020 and dt. As per the Government decision dated 17.07.2020, Indian Agricultural Insurance Company, IFFCO Tokio General Insurance Co., Ltd., Reliance General Insurance Co., Ltd., and Reliance General Insurance Co., Ltd. have been permitted to offer the following insurance products: Bharti AXA General Insurance Co. Ltd. Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company Limited. and HDFC Insurance. It is operated by 6 insurance companies. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought State Government share subsidy for crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2021 from the above 6 companies. In the revised guidelines of the Central Government, Issue No. 13.1.6 states that" the insurance premium share of the Central and State Government is required to be paid to the company in advance (first installment) even if the data of the insured area is not available at the time of registration of the current season. The insurance premium subsidy amount should be 50 per cent of the total 80 per cent of the total state share insurance premium subsidy paid in the previous season in advance to the companies," it said. Accordingly, the Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (4) In response to a request for Rs. 973, 16,47,758/- was under consideration of the Government for payment of crop insurance premium subsidy to insurance companies.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PROPEVIO-2021/ P.S. 140/11 A
Government's decision:
The demand submitted by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India, the recommendation of the Commissionerate of Agriculture and the revised guidelines of the Central Government, point no. 13.1.6.Considering the above, the State share under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2021 is Rs. 973, 16,47,758/- is being approved by the Government for disbursement of subsidy to insurance companies. This amount is being disbursed for Kharif 2021 season and its utilization will not be admissible for other previous seasons.
Expenditure on the said items should be met from the budgetary provision approved for the year 2021-22 under the following head: Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 110, Crop Insurance (00) (08) Funding for insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana State Share (2401 A 664) Other than the Scheme 3. The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for spending the funds disbursed under the said Government decision in accordance with the guidelines of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana and the Government decisions issued from time to time.
For this purpose, Assistant Director (Accounts), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
