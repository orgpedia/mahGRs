Reference No. According to the Government Circular No. 2, the Senior Administrative Officer of Group-A Cadre. The final seniority list as on 01.01.2021 has been published.
Senior Administrative Officer, Group-A. 01.01.2021 to D. The names of the officers who have been appointed due to direct service recruitment, promotion or other reasons during the period 31.12.2021 will be included in this category. The provisional seniority list as on 01.01.2022 has been prepared and is being published as per Annexure-III annexed.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) is requested to bring this temporary seniority to the notice of all the concerned officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) and place a copy of their signature on such intimation in the records. Also, if there are objections/ errors regarding the provisional seniority list, the concerned should submit a statement within fifteen days.
The objections received thereafter will not be considered. The facts of the representation received should be checked from the available records of the concerned and the representations should be sent to the government with detailed feedback. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published, assuming that no one has any objection to this provisional seniority list.
