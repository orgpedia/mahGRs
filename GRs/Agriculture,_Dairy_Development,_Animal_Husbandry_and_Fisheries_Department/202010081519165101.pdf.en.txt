Introduction: The State Government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme for farmers who are not eligible to cultivate fruit orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. In 2020-21, the scheme is proposed to be implemented within the limit of 33% of the budgetary provision. 2 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. The government was considering providing funds to the Scheduled Tribes for the implementation of the scheme this year. The government is taking the following steps:
Government Decision: In the year 2020-21, for the implementation of Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme, Rs. 100.00 lakh (Rupees One Crore only) is being sanctioned to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Budgetary Distribution System.
