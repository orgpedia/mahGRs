The Proposal:
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in the state in Kharif 2019. The scheme is being implemented through two insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India and Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company as per the Government decision dated 22.05.2019. Under the scheme, the government was considering to disburse the state share subsidy as per the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissioner's Office for the Kharif season 2019.
Government's decision:
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana for Kharif Season 2019 Reference No. As per the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissioner's Office, Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company has received Rs. 29,51,801/- is required to be paid and the Government is approving the disbursement.
