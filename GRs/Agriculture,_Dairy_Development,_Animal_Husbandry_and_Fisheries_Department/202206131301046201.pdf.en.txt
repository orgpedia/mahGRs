MA/ 19645/2022, Dy. 31/05/2022 Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help the farmers in maintaining their financial stability, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, a restructured weather-based crop insurance scheme will be implemented in the state for 9 crops namely orange, blackberry, cashew, pomegranate, mango, banana, grape (A& B), strawberry and papaya (Ambia Bahar) on a pilot basis in 30 districts in the three years 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24.
