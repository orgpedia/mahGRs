The following are the objectives and functions of Maharashtra Marine Fishermen Welfare Corporation:
There will be. -
1) To raise the standard of living of fishermen in the marine sector.
2) To protect the interest of traditional fishermen.
3) The health of the brother and his family to the fishermen working in the marine sector; and
To take action on matters of education.
4) To create employment opportunities for fishermen working in the marine sector.
5) Suggest solutions to the government regarding fishing income, marketing, and processing industries.
