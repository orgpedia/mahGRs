Preamble: Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, an autonomous organization, has been established in the State to promote dairy production and rural employment generation by providing specialized veterinary services more efficiently and expanding the scope of hybrid breeding program, especially in the backward areas of the State. The decision has been taken by the government. As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, the shifting of the headquarters of Maharashtra Livestock Development Board from Pune to the premises of Post Graduate College of Veterinary Medicine, Akola has been approved.
Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Akola has a total of 3 packaged sand laboratories, 7 Government Livestock Farms and 2 Weed Breeding Centres. 2.04.2002 has been transferred. Creation of a proper system for controlling the activities (administrative, technical and financial) of the institutions. Also, since the designation of the head of the office needs to be consistent with the nature of the technical and administrative activities of the institutions, the Maharashtra Livestock Development Board's Board of Directors has decided to appoint a new head of the department. Resolution No.5 of the 5th Meeting dated 08.10.2004 As per Article 33, the Board of Directors has approved the revision of the nomenclature of the heads of the above institutions functioning under the Board as indicated below.
