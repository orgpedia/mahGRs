Preamble: Animal and Bird Charitable Trust and others against the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and others. Public Interest Litigation in the High Court of Bombay No. 36/ 2011 was recorded. According to the petition, The Home Department had constituted a Cabinet Sub-Committee under the Government Order dated November 9, 2016, to undertake a comprehensive study in terms of implementation of the order passed by the High Court on June 8, 2015. In view of the recommendations made by the said Cabinet Sub-Committee, orders have been issued under the Government Resolution dated 16.06.2017 regarding the relocation of drivers/ owners and horses dependent on the horse carriage business in Brihanmumbai.
In the Government Resolution dated 16th June, 2017. The following commands are given in Figure 2.
" The horse owners concerned should sell the horses for rehabilitation or hand them over to an NGO willing to take care of the horses through the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) with the consent of the horse cart owners. The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) should take action in this regard. Also, in order to ensure the safety and health of the horse in the entire process, the Department of Animal Husbandry is being given approval to formulate a comprehensive policy plan. " 3. In pursuance of the above decision of the Home Department, the Government was contemplating to frame a policy for the protection of the life and health of the horse till the entire process of rehabilitation of the horses belonging to the horse carriage owners in Brihanmumbai is completed as above.
Government's decision:
In view of the above facts, the Government hereby approve the following policy.
(i) The Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Mumbai Division, Mumbai shall take necessary measures with immediate effect to ensure the safety and health of the horses till the entire process of rehabilitation of the horses belonging to the horse cart owners in Brihanmumbai is completed as aforesaid.

(ii) In this regard, the Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Mumbai Division, Mumbai, in coordination with the concerned officers of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and the Joint Commissioner of Police (Traffic), Brihanmumbai, should conduct a joint inspection of the places where the horses are and get complete information.
Accordingly, in order to protect the life and health of the horses, the Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Mumbai Division, Mumbai, should entrust the responsibility of this work to the veterinary officers and staff under his control.
(ii) Seek the assistance of Veterinary Colleges located in Mumbai as well as Veterinary Officers/ Staff under the control of Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai, as required for this purpose. The facilities available in the dispensaries of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and veterinary colleges should be used for the prompt delivery of veterinary services.
(iv) The Police Department will cooperate in the rehabilitation process of horses in Brihanmumbai by handing over the custody of horses from the horse cart dealers to the Municipal Corporation.
(v) The Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Mumbai Division, Mumbai shall conduct health check-up of all the horses in the area of Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and submit a detailed report to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
The said Government Resolution is being issued as per the concurrence given by the Home Department dated 14.08.2017 and the Urban Development Department dated 08.02.2018.
