The Government of India has proposed to set up a Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) to augment fisheries production in the country and to develop infrastructure in the fisheries sector. Under this, the Central Government vide Reference No. As per the letter, the states have sent a draft tripartite agreement for providing financial assistance through NABARD for the proposals for creation of necessary facilities. Accordingly, the scheme is being implemented in the State. 2 Administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the government decision here.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Planning, Finance and Accountant General's Office has approved the opening of new accounting year for the Fisheries Infrastructure Development Fund Scheme.
After the approval of the Accountant General, when the Finance Department was requested to provide statistics for these headings, the Department has provided new headings as follows.
