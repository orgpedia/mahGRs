The Proposal:
The World Bank-funded" Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project (SMART)" is a Rs. 2220 crore ($ 300 million) project. 3 In principle approval has been given under the Government Resolution dated 19.01.2019 to set up a Project Management Cell with requisite manpower to prepare the project plan.
In the first phase, 37 full-time posts (14 government and 23 external) for the Project Management Cell with the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries for the preparation of the project plan have been created temporarily at the establishment of the Commissionerate of Agriculture. 5 It has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 27.05.2019. To implement this project and to sign an agreement with the World Bank, Reference No. 6 The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 11.09.2019 and the approval has been accorded for setting up of the Institute under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and implementation of the project by the said Institute.
Reference No. 7 As per the Government decision dated 11.09.2019, it has been approved to transfer 37 full-time posts (14 Government and 23 externally sourced) created temporarily on the establishment of the Agriculture Commissionerate to the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) Institute in the first phase for the project. Similarly, reference No. 8 As per the Government decision dated 17.02.2020, the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project has been renamed as" Ma. It has been approved to be called' Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project' and the name will be used for all official activities related to this project under the Government of Maharashtra.

The Agriculture Department is the main coordinating department for the Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project, which will include a total of 7 administrative departments and a total of 11 implementing agencies under them.
To create new posts for the manpower required for the project, the Project Director had submitted a proposal for creation of new posts on the establishment of Maharashtra State Institute of Agribusiness and Rural Transformation. The minutes of the meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary dated 23rd August, 2021 are attached at Reference No. 10 has been received from the Finance Department here and the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the creation of 467 new posts for this project. Accordingly, The government has further decided to sanction 467 new sanctioned posts for Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project, and a total of 504 posts for the project, including the total 37 sanctioned earlier.
Government's decision:
1. m. The government has approved the creation of 467 new posts for the Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project.
Out of the 467 posts created for the project under this Government decision, 200 posts are being approved to be filled up through appointment/ deputation and 167 posts through outsourcing mechanism in Phase-I.
A total of 504 posts are being sanctioned for the project, including 37 sanctioned posts for the project under the Government decision dated 27.05.2019 and 467 newly created posts under this Government decision.
