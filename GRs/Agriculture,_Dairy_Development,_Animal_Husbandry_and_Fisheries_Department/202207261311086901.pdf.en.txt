The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched for three years from Kharif 2020 and Rabi 2020-21. 29.06.2020 and dt. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought the remaining State Government share of subsidy for crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2021 from the above 6 companies. In the revised guidelines of the Central Government, Issue No. Under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), as per Section 13.1.11 of the Act, the State share of insurance premium for Kharif season 2021 will be paid to the insurance companies. Accordingly, the Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (5) In response to a request for Rs. 28,83,16, 428/- was under consideration of the Government for disbursement of crop insurance premium to insurance companies on account of State share subsidy.
