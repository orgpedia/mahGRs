During 2018-19, the Central Government has released Central share of Rs. 7.68 lakh for cotton crop out of commercial crops under National Food Security Mission and State share of Rs. 14.46 lakh amounting to Rs. 25.39 lakh. Further, in addition to the previous year's revived fund, a total of Rs. 9.21 lakh has been received for disbursement this year as Central share of Rs. 13.82 lakh (Rs. 7.68 lakh+ Rs. 6.14 lakh) and State share of Rs. 9.21 lakh (Rs. 5.12 lakh+ Rs. 4.09 lakh).
Out of the total amount of Rs. 9.21 lakh received for the scheme during the current year, a total amount of Rs. 10.58 lakh (Central share Rs. 6.35 lakh+ State share Rs. 4.23 lakh) has been provided in the Budget Estimates. The balance budgetary provision of Rs. 12.45 lakh for the distribution of the remaining amount under the National Food Security Mission (NFSA) out of the Potential Savings Fund under the head Food Crops is provided in Reference No. 12 The Government hereby make available, by way of reallocation, the following provisions with respect to the distribution of the said funds:
Government's decision:
Approval is being accorded to re-invest the unutilized Central share of Rs. 7.47 lakh and corresponding State share of Rs. 4.98 lakh out of the total Central share of Rs. 12.45 lakh (Rs. 12 lakh forty five thousand only) received for Scheduled Tribes for commercial crops (Cotton) under National Food Security Mission at the end of 2017-18 for utilization in 2018-19 for Scheduled Tribes for cotton crops under the Mission.
