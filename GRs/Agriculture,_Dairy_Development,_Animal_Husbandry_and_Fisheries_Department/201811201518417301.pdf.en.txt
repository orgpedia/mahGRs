In the Konkan region of the state, Sindhudurg and Ratnagiri districts produce a large amount of cashew. Considering the difficulties faced by the farmers from the cultivation to processing and sale of cashew fruit, the government should be advised to formulate a definite policy for the overall development of cashew fruit. As per the Government Order dated 18/07/2018. A committee was formed under the chairmanship of the Minister of State for Home (Rural), Finance and Planning. It was under the consideration of the government to extend the term of the committee and the office of the chairman as per the request of the chairman. The Government has decided to:
Government Decision: Considering the difficulties faced by the farmers from the cultivation to processing and sale of the cashew fruit, a definite policy should be formulated for the overall development of the cashew fruit. The Government is taking the following decisions regarding the committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Minister of State for Home (Rural), Finance and Planning.
(i) The scope of the Committee is being extended to cover the entire Konkan region of the State and also the Ajra and Chandgad taluks of Kolhapur district in addition to Konkan. Accordingly, the Committee should prepare its report on the complaints/ issues received in depth and what measures can be taken on them and submit it to the Government by 31/12/2018 with its self-explanatory opinion

Duties and Responsibilities of the Committee: The duties and responsibilities of the Committee shall remain unchanged as of 18/07/2018.
The Chairman of the Committee has been empowered by the Government Decision dated 18/07/2018 to invite the concerned dignitaries to the meeting as per requirement.
Accordingly, the chairman will have the right to invite tribal and other cashew growers and members of the processing entrepreneurs committee of Ratnagiri and Palghar districts to the meeting.
