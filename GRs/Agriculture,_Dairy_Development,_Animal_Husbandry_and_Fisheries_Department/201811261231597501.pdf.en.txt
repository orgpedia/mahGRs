Introduction: Veterinarians in Government service provide immediate veterinary services to farmers/ gopalkas at their doorstep in their own vehicle before office hours, during mid-day holidays, after office hours, during night hours and on weekly and public holidays on the request of farmers/ gopalkas.
Read here 1. As per the Government Decision dated 8.07.2010, revised rates for medical and surgical cases have been fixed for visits within the city limits and it has been decided to revise these rates every five years, if necessary.
Considering the increased prices of fuel (petrol/ diesel) since 2010 and the prevailing market prices, the revision of these prices was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
Decision: The government. The Government is hereby approving the following revision in the rates fixed for handling medical or surgical cases at the doorstep of the farmers as on 8.07.2010.

As the revised service charge includes the cost of travel of the veterinarian to the farmer's house/ area while handling medical or surgical cases for visiting the farmer's doorstep, it is not to be charged separately. All medications, tools, equipment, and supplies must be provided by the animal's owner.
The revised rates as above shall be applicable only to veterinary doctors in Government service from the date of issue of the said Government Order.
The Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune shall review the revised rates as aforesaid every 5 years and submit a proposal to the Government with suitable justification for revision, if required.
