As per the revised methodology of PFMS system, the Central Government has marked the commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) scheme SNA under National Food Security Mission. Accordingly, as per the demand of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, the Government is taking a decision to disburse a total of Rs. 16.43 lakh for the Scheduled Tribes category under this campaign for commercial crops (cotton, sugarcane) amounting to Rs. 9.86 lakh from the Central share and Rs. 6.57 lakh from the State share.
Government's decision:
For the year 2021-22, a total amount of Rs. 16.43 lakh (Rs.16 lakh and Rs.44,000 only) from Central and State share for Scheduled Tribes category for commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) under National Food Security Mission is being approved to be disbursed to Commissioner (Agriculture) on Budgetary Distribution System.
