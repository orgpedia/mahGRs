Read:- 1) Government Decision, Agriculture and Padum Department, No. Fig. 1018/ Q. No. 155/15-A, D. 08.06.2018 2) of Vasundhara Watershed Development Agency. 29th meeting held on 14.09.2018.
The proposal: The 29th meeting of the Vasundhara Watershed Development Mechanism was held on 14.09.2018 under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Soil and Water Conservation and Secretary (Agriculture). In the meeting, the number of projects under the district-wise Integrated Watershed Management Programme was reviewed and a resolution was passed to return the services of the Deputy Project Manager working in the Maharashtra Agricultural Service Group-B cadre of the Agriculture Department to the Agriculture Department in the districts where the number of projects is less.
As per the decision taken in the meeting, it has been decided to return the services of Deputy Project Managers working under the Vasundhara Project in Pune, Nandurbar, Satara, Nashik and Palghar in the Maharashtra Agricultural Service Group-B cadre to the Agriculture Department and to fill up the posts of Taluka Agriculture Officers on priority basis in the state.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority, the Maharashtra Agriculture Officer in the department, Group-B (Grade Pay Rs. 4600) The Government is approving the transfer and posting of the following officers in the cadre as shown in the table.
