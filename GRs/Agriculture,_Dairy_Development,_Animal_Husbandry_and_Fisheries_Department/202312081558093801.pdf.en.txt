In the meeting held on 05.09.2023 at Sahyadri Guest House, Malabar Hill, the Minister directed to conduct research and suggest measures for effective control of pest diseases on mango crop. The Minister (of Agriculture) should immediately convene a meeting and form a task force. The Chief Minister had issued directions in this regard. Accordingly, the matter of forming a task force for effective control of pest diseases on mango crops was under the consideration of the government. The government is taking the following decisions:
The government has decided to set up a task force to control pest diseases on mango crops in Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts.
