As per the Government decision dated 02.08.2021, the Foodgrain Crops Scheme (SNA) has been marked under National Food Security Mission (NFSA) for managing the fund distribution and utilization of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
The total amount of Rs. 582.80000 lakh provided for food crops under the National Food Security Mission as decided by the Government should be disbursed district wise by the Director (Extension and Training) through PFMS system.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Director (Extension and Training) should submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Principal Secretary (Agriculture).
