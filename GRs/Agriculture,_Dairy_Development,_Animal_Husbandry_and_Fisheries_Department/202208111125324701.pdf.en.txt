As per the revised modalities of PFMS system vide notification dated 23rd March, 2021 and in pursuance of the Government decision dated 02.08.2021, the Foodgrain crops scheme under National Food Security Mission (NFSA) has been marked as SNA for fund distribution and utilization management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
Under the National Food Security Mission, the total amount of Rs. 4622.282 lakh made available for food grain crops as per the decision of the Government should be disbursed by the Director (Extension and Training) through PFMS system district wise.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Director (Extension and Training) should submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Principal Secretary (Agriculture).
