As announced by the Finance Minister in the Budget Speech for 2023-24, the Government has approved the extension of the scheme for the financial year 2023-24 to cover the following areas: Fruit orchards, drip/ frost irrigation, farmsteads, field lining, sheds, greenhouses, modern sowing machines (BBF) and cotton shredders.
Keeping in view the contribution of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in the field of agriculture, the government was considering to name the extended scheme as" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Krishi Yojana" to provide orchards, drip/ drip irrigation, farmlands, field lining, shade nets, greenhouses, modern sowing machines (BBF) and cotton shredders to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj on the occasion of the 350th anniversary of his coronation
Government Decision: 1. The extended scheme of providing orchards, drip/ drip irrigation, farmlands, field lining, sheds, greenhouses, modern sowing machines (BBF) and cotton shredders to be implemented by the Agriculture Department for the year 2023-24 is being named as' Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Krishi Yojana" by this Government.
