the prelude
In reference to the above No. As per the guidelines in the Government Decisions 1 and 2, the vacant posts of agricultural assistants under the Commissionerate of Agriculture in the state are filled as agricultural servants at a fixed salary for the first three years. The period of three years spent in the post of Agricultural Servant is taken into account while calculating the qualifying service for payment of pension and retirement benefits. However, this period is not taken into account while calculating the total service while allowing the benefits of the Assured Progress Scheme. It was under consideration of the Government to consider the period of 3 years of honorarium service in the post of Krishi Sevak of the employees of the Agricultural Assistant Cadre working in the field office under the Commissionerate of Agriculture while calculating for the purpose of the Assured Progress under Service Scheme.
The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
It is hereby approved by the Government that the period of 3 years of service on honorarium in the post of Krishi Sevak of the employees of the Agricultural Assistant Cadre working in the field office under the Commissionerate of Agriculture should be taken into account while calculating the total service for the purpose of the Assured Progress Scheme under the service. The expenditure so incurred shall be met from the grants available under the head of accounts under which the salaries and allowances of the concerned are paid.
