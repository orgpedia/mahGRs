Government Order: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to retire officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in public interest after reviewing their services at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. General Administration Department. As per the procedure prescribed under the Government Order dated 10th June, 2019, the following Departmental Review Committee is being constituted to review the Group A and Group B cadre officers of the Agriculture Commissionerate and Subordinate Offices.
