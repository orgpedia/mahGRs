The Proposal:
Under the National Food Security Mission for 2022-23, Rs. 708.10 lakh (Central and State share 60:40). In pursuance of the approval given vide letter dated 09/05/2022, the said Action Plan has been referred to. The administrative approval has been granted under the Government Decision dated 13/05/2022.
For the year 2022-23, the Central Government has released a total Central share of Rs. 106.21 lakh to the three categories of farmers as first instalment under the Food and Nutrition Security (erstwhile National Food Security Mission) Commercial Crops (Cotton, Sugarcane). Crop wise distribution is given on 18/08/2022. Accordingly, as per the demand of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, the Government is taking a decision to disburse a total of Rs. 143.21 lakh under this campaign for commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) for the general category, amounting to Rs. 85.93 lakh from the Central share and Rs. 57.28 lakh from the State share.
