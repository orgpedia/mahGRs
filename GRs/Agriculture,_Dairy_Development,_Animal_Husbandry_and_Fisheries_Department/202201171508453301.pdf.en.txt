November 15, 2021 Preamble: In the above context of the Department of Rural Development. As per the Government decision dated 11.10.2018, the cadre of Agriculture Officer, Group-C (Technical) in the Zila Parishad has been converted into a new cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) in the Department of Agriculture, Government of Karnataka. The Department of Agriculture has issued the above guidelines and procedures for the existing officers of the Zila Parishad, Group-C (Technical) cadre who want to be included in the new cadre i.e. Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) cadre. issued pursuant to the Government Decision of January 9, 2019. Accordingly, the Rural Development Department had submitted to the Agriculture Department the information of the officers who gave options to be included in the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zilla Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted). According to the Rural Development Department, As on 11th January, 2021, 381 officers of Zila Parishad Agriculture Officers, Group-C (Technical) Cadre have been inducted in the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries of the State Government.
As per the provisions of this Government decision, all the service matters of the newly established cadre officers are henceforth required to be handled by the Agriculture and Padum Department of the State Government. The government has taken the following decisions:

GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: AGRICULTURE 1218/ PR No. 410/16 A
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In the newly created Agriculture Department of the State Government, the following action should be taken to deal with the various service matters of the officers included in the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zilla Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted). As the newly created cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) has been transferred to the Agriculture Department, the Agriculture Development Officer as the controlling officer of this cadre and the Chief Executive Officer as the head of the regional department, the concerned Zila Parishad are being declared.
Accordingly, henceforth, all matters relating to the establishment of this category such as leave sanction, service books, departmental inquiries, assured progress scheme under the revised service, retirement cases, etc. will be handled by the Chief Executive Officer, concerned Zila Parishad as the Controlling Officer of the category and the Regional Head of the Department as the Chief Executive Officer.
Confidential Report Reporting Officer, Reviewing Officer and Preserver: Confidential Reporting of officers included in the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) is being declared as Reporting Officer to District Agriculture Officer (General/ V.H.O.) and Reviewing Officer to Agriculture Development Officer, concerned Zila Parishad and Technical Officer (Personnel Welfare), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Processing Officer of the Confidential Report.
3. Drawing and Disbursing Officer: The District Agriculture Officer (General) concerned is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted). The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall henceforth take all necessary actions to withdraw the salary of the officers working in the cadre of Agriculture Officer (Zila Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) in the concerned district from the service system of the State Government such as taking out the service ID, adding all the previous accounts to the current account, opening/ adding group insurance, general provident fund account, opening NPS/ DCPS account and adding the previous account etc. Their salaries and allowances, retirement benefits, etc. should be kept in force in the earlier system till the process is completed.
