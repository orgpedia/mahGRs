The Proposal:
Budget 2023-24 As per the announcement made by the Deputy Chief Minister and Finance Minister in the budget speech, the Central Government's Pradhan Mantri Krishi Samman Nidhi Scheme for increasing the income of the Annadata Baliraj, the Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi, which will add another Rs. 3 here. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 15.06.2023. The scheme will be implemented through the Public Financial Management System by developing a separate mandali on the MahaDBT portal of the state government and opening a new bank account for the distribution of subsidy to the beneficiaries under the Namo Kisan Mahasanman Nidhi scheme in the state.

178/23/ COSHA PRASA-5, Dt. is being issued pursuant to the approval received vide 11.07.2023.
(1) The savings bank accounts to be opened for disbursement of subsidy to the beneficiaries under the" Namo Shetkari Mahasanman Nidhi" scheme as well as for the administrative expenses of the State Project Control Room shall be used only for that purpose.
(2) The funds deposited in the said savings bank account/ interest as well as the funds required as per the progress of the scheme should be reviewed from time to time and a report should be submitted on the same.
(3) The amount to be determined by reconciling the amount of interest on the savings bank account with the balance should be deposited in the government account.
(4) In order to ensure that the funds do not remain unutilized in the bank account for a long time, funds should be drawn from the treasury as soon as necessary for immediate appropriation.
(4) If the scheme is closed, the account of the scheme shall be closed. The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for this at the Commissioner level.
(6) (a) Since the transaction in the said account is to be done only through the Public Financial Management System, the competent authority shall issue the Fund Transfer Order in the name of the bank at the time when the funds are to be spent. For this, the Fund Transfer Order should be created by logging in to the Public Financial Management System. This will be the work of the maker.
(b) The Deputy Commissioner (Agriculture Enumeration) in the office of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune shall be the Maker and the Assistant Director (Accounts-1) shall be the Assistant Checker and the Commissioner (Agriculture) shall be responsible as the Final Checker for the transactions in the accounts through the Public Financial Management System.
