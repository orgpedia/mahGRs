Preamble: The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and the Commissionerate of Agriculture and its subordinate offices.
The funds available under the e-Governance programme through the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (DADF) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture are utilised for procurement of computers, IT initiatives, creation, development and maintenance of IT systems for the Department of Agriculture (Ministry level), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune and subordinate field offices. Under this scheme, a fixed amount is sanctioned by the state government every year and the sanctioned amount is distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, according to the need.
The e-Governance programme is a scheme under the scheme and a total amount of Rs.25 crore has been budgeted for the scheme (3451 2782) in the financial year 2022-23. As per the circular, 60% of the budgetary allocation for 2022-23 for essential and programme expenditure i.e. Rs. 15 crore has been made available to the Department for State e-Governance Programme for the first nine months.
Therefore, in the year 2022-23, the department is allocated Rs. Administrative and financial sanction for implementation of e-Governance programme with an outlay of Rs.15 crore and Rs. 3.50 crore was under the consideration of the Government, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Government's decision:
The State e-Governance Programme is being implemented to computerize the Agriculture and Padum Departments, Ministry Offices and the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune and various subordinate offices. Administrative approval is being given to implement the e-governance programme within a limit of Rs 15 crore.
