As per the decision of the Government dated 21.10.2011 in the context of General Administration Department, a comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in Government service has been prescribed every year.
2.A total of 2 posts are sanctioned in this cadre of Senior Administrative Officer, out of which 1 post is reserved. Vacancies as on 01.01.2021. So in this category. The list includes the names of officers who were promoted during the period 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2020. An Interim Seniority Notice dated 01.01.2021 has been prepared and the same is being published as per Annexure-A attached to the said Interim Seniority Notice as per the provisions of the UGC Regulation 21.10.2011.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) is requested to bring this temporary seniority to the notice of all the concerned officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) and to place a copy of his signature on such intimation in the records. Also, if there are objections/ errors regarding the provisional seniority list, the concerned should submit a statement within fifteen days. The objections received thereafter will not be considered. The facts of the representation received should be checked from the available records of the concerned and the representations should be sent to the government with detailed feedback. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published, assuming that no one has any objection to this provisional seniority list.
4. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the said seniority list shall be as per the Government Notification, General Administration Department, No. As per SRV- 1076/12, dated 21/06/1982, only Senior Administrative Officers will be considered for determining seniority in Group A cadre.
