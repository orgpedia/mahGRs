The following terms and conditions should be followed while implementing the traditional agricultural development scheme under the National Sustainable Agriculture Mission.
The scheme should be implemented in accordance with the guidelines of the Central Government and the terms and conditions of the release of funds by the Central Government and administrative approval.
The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the Central and State Governments along with the progress report as soon as possible by keeping the accounts of expenditure in order.
The physical and financial progress reports on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central and State Governments by the 5th of every month.
3. The balance sheet and the amount of the audit deposit expenditure relating to the scheme should be reported by the implementing agency. It should clearly state the amount of interest and interest earned at the beginning of the year. So that the amounts can be considered in a transparent manner and there is no ambiguity.
4. It should be ensured that under no circumstances more than the approved programme is implemented.
5. It should be ensured that the funds are spent for the same cadre for which the funds have been allocated.
