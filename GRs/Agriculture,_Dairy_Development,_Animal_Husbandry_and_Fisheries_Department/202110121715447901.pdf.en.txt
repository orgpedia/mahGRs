The Central Government has approved the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) to address the problems of unorganized food processing industries in the State. In this backdrop, the Prime Minister's Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Enterprises (PMFME), sponsored by the Central Government, will be implemented in the State for the next five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 23.12.2020.
Accordingly, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 21266.00 lakhs. The approval has been accorded vide Office Memorandum dated 30.09.2021. The scheme will be implemented from 2021-22. vide letter dated 08.10.2021 for Rs. 21266.00 lakh has been received. The funds have been provided by the Central Government through letters dated 04.09.2020, 19.03.2021 and 24.03.2021. Out of which, the funds made available under the letter dated 04.09.2020 to the Office of the Commissioner (Agriculture) were directly provided by the Central Government and the funds made available under the letter dated 19.03.2021 and 24.03.2021 were deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State Government. The funds have been made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) from the provision of Central share budgeted for 2021-22. Therefore, taking into account the Central share remaining after disbursing the Central share from the provision budgeted in 2021-22 and adjusting the amount of State share made available by the Central Government in the previous year.
