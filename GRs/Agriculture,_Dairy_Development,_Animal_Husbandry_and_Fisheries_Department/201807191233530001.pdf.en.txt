The Proposal:
Administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of Pest Disease Surveillance and Advisory Scheme for management of pests and diseases in major crops namely Soyabean, Cotton, Paddy, Tur, Gram, Mango, Pomegranate, Banana, Moong, Orange and Chikku from 2018-19. Para 2 (4) of the said Government Decision has approved the provision of computer technicians/ operators and other necessary manpower through NICSI, an Institute of National Informatics Centre. In order to make available the manpower, NICSI is required to pay the entire amount in advance and the payment for the same is submitted to the Pune Treasury, the Treasury has returned the payment with the objection that it cannot be paid in advance on the basis of proforma invoice. Therefore, the government is taking the following decisions with the consent of the Finance Department regarding the payment of advance on the basis of proforma invoice for the supply of manpower to the National Informatics Center.
Government's decision:
For the year 2018-19, under the Scheme of Kidney Disease Surveillance and Counselling Project, the National Informatics Centre Services Incorporation (NICSI) is being approved to provide manpower to the NICSI in advance on proforma invoice basis by making a lump sum payment of Rs. 56,58,231/- to the NICSI.
