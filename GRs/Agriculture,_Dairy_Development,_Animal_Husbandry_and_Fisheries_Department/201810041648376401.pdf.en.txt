The following conditions must be met when implementing the plan:
1) The scheme should be implemented in accordance with the guidelines of the Central Government and the terms and conditions of the release of funds by the Central Government.
2) The audit report and utilization certificates of the said expenditure should be submitted to the Central Government along with the progress report as soon as possible, keeping the accounts of expenditure in order.
3) The physical and financial progress report on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the Central Government by the 5th of every month.
