The proposal-
Krishi Unnati Yojana- Assistance for Reforms in State Agricultural Extension Programmes" (ATMA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme being implemented with Central Share of 60%: State Share of 40%. The Central Government has released the first instalment of Rs. 168.22 lakh. vide letter dated 20.6.2019 and second instalment of Rs. 168.22 lakh. As on 30.10.2019, a total of Rs. 336.44 lakh has been disbursed. The Central Government has allotted Rs. 336.44 lakh as compared to Rs. 224.29 lakh was required to be disbursed. However, in the current year, under the ATMA scheme, the Central share of ST category is Rs. 125.47 lakh and Rs. 84 lakh has been allocated to the Agriculture Department.
