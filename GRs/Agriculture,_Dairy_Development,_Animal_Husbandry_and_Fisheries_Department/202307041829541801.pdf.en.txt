The Proposal:
The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State governments. The Public Financial Management System (PFMS) has been operationalized from 2021-22 onwards. On this system, Single Nodal Account (SNA) at the level of Commissioner (Agriculture) for the National Agriculture Development Scheme and Zero Balance Account for the implementing agencies have been made operational.
