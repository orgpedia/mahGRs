Preamble: The Government of India has developed a Government e-Marketplace (GeM) Portal for procurement of goods and services by all Government Departments/ Agencies as per the changes made under the National Procurement Policy. The Department of Industry, Energy and Labour is responsible for: 1. As per the Government Resolution dated 24.8.2017, the State Government has been granted approval for procurement of goods and services. The procurement process through this portal will be completely online and without any human intervention. This approach will ensure transparency in the procurement process and supply of genuine and good quality goods at reasonable prices.
Under the Department of Animal Husbandry, the government was considering authorizing the officials to handle the related activities as Verifying Authority, Primary User, Payee/ DDO, Secondary User and Consignee for the purchase of goods and services through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) system.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The following designated officers are being declared as the responsible authority for handling the related activities as Verifying Authority, Primary User, Payee/ DDO, Secondary User and Consignee for procurement of goods and services through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) system under Department of Animal Husbandry.
