The Proposal:
The United Nations has declared the year 2023 as the" International Year of Millets." As per the decision of the Government in the context of celebrating the" International Year of Nutri Cereals 2023" in the State, a State Level Steering Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary and a State Level Task Force under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Agriculture) have been constituted to organize/ regulate various activities by all the concerned administrative departments in the State.
The meeting of the State Steering Committee was held under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. It was held on December 12, 2022. As per the decision taken in the meeting, instructions have been given to take the importance of nutritious cereals to all the people by taking active participation of the administrative departments of the concerned ministries in the activities to be organized as per the month-wise plan made by the Department of Agriculture. Accordingly, various programmes will be organized by all the concerned departments to celebrate the" International Year of Nutri Cereals, 2023" in the State.
There is a tradition of using nutritious cereal in the diet on the festival of Makar Sankranti-Bhogi in the state. In order to mark the International Year of Millets, 2023, and to build on this tradition and to highlight the importance of nutritious cereals in the diet, the Government was considering to promote the observance of this day as Nutritious Cereals Day in the State every year. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
The tradition of the state to use nutritious cereals in the diet on the festival of Makar Sankranti-Bhogi, as well as to highlight the importance of nutritious cereals in the diet, in line with the International Year of Nutritious Cereals 2023,' Makar Sankranti-Bhogi' day should be celebrated as' Nutritious Cereals Day' in the state every year.
2.Providing lunches, lunches and dinners to children, school and college students, workers and office staff and officials under various schemes.
