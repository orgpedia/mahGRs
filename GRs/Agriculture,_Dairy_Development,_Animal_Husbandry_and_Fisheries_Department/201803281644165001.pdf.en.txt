Introduction- A scheme to provide rebate in sales tax on high speed diesel for mechanized boats (1-6 cylinders) of the members of Fishermen Cooperative Societies is being implemented in the state since 1975. Since the Value Added Tax system has come into existence in the State of Maharashtra from 1st April, 2005, the scheme has been implemented in a revised form from 3rd September, 2005. During the year 2017-18, Rs. 6000.00 lakhs has been disbursed and balance Rs. 2500.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government decides to reimburse the sales tax on high speed diesel oil for mechanized boats belonging to the members of fishermen cooperative societies. The Government is approving to disburse an amount of Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores only) on Budget Distribution System.
The Commissioner, Fisheries shall disburse the said funds to all the marine districts in proportion to the pending proposals. For this, the Assistant Commissioner, Fisheries (Technical) of the concerned district is being declared as the Procurement and Distribution Officer. They should ensure that such expenditure is incurred within the limits of the provision approved in the current financial year.
The expenditure on this should be met from the available provision for 2017-18 under the head Demand No.D-6,2405-Fishing, (00) (103) Marine Fisheries, (01) Post-Plan Grants, (01) (03) Reimbursement of Sales Tax on High Speed Diesel Oil (State) (2405 1278), 33-Finance.
