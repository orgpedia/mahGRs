Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in Kharif 2018 by Indian Agricultural Insurance, Oriental General Insurance Co., Ltd., IFFCO Tokio General Insurance Co., Ltd. The scheme is being implemented in the state through 4 insurance companies. Under the scheme, the government was considering to disburse the state share subsidy as per the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissioner's Office for Kharif 2018.
Government's decision:
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) for Kharif Season 2018 Reference No. 8 As per the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissioner's Office, the Oriental General Insurance Co., Ltd. has been provided with State share crop insurance premium subsidy of Rs. 5,56,632/- is required to be paid and the Government is approving the disbursement of the same.
