By 30.10.2015, administrative approval has been given to the innovative State Level General, Scheduled Caste Sub-Plan and District Level Tribal Area Appropriations for allocation of six/ four/ two milch hybrid cows/ buffaloes groups to boost milk production in the State by superseding the earlier Government decisions. The government was considering the inclusion of indigenous breeds of milch cows in the milch animals to be distributed under the scheme. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
In order to promote milk production in the State, the Government is approving the inclusion of indigenous milch cows (Gir, Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, Rathi, Tharparkar Deoni, Lal Kandhari, Gawlau and Dangi) in the scheme for allocation of six/ four/ two groups of hybrid cows/ buffaloes to be allocated under State level General, Scheduled Castes Sub Plan and District level Tribal Area Appropriation.
