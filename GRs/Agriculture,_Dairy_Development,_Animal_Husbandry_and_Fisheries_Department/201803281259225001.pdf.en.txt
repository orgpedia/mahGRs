The Union Ministry of Agriculture has released the Annual Action Plan for 2017-18 under the National Food Security Mission (NFSA). As per the administrative approval given under the Government Resolution dated 6th May, 2017 and as per the action plan approved for the Mission during the current year and as per the guidelines issued by the Union Ministry of Agriculture from time to time, it will be mandatory for the Director (Extension and Training) to monitor the Mission at the State level and also to direct the subordinate offices from time to time for effective implementation and monitoring of the Mission.
The concerned District Superintending Agriculture Officers/ Taluk Agriculture Officers should select the beneficiaries for the campaign and compile the list of beneficiaries at the Taluk level for implementing the programmes covered under the campaign. In this list, the information of the farmer's name, village, survey/ group number, mobile/ telephone number, bank account number, Aadhaar number, etc. should be collected. Similarly, it will be the responsibility of the concerned District Superintending Agriculture Officer to make the list of beneficiaries selected under the campaign available on the website of the Agriculture Department within 15 days from the receipt of funds.
Priority should be given to small and marginal farmers or groups of such farmers while selecting beneficiary farmers under various components under the National Food Security Mission, as well as under all components of individual benefits under the mission, the process of direct benefit transfer should be done directly to the accounts of beneficiaries by linking Aadhaar number.
