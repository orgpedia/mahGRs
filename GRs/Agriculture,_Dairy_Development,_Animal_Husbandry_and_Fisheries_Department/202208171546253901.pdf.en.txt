Preamble: For the financial year 2022-23, according to the circular and semi-government letter of No. 1 under the reference of the Finance Department, all the four agricultural universities of the state have been given demand No. Under the head D-3,' 2415 Agricultural Research and Education', funds have been released on the BIMES system for the purpose of 31-Assistant Grants (Non-salaried) for the programme schemes with a ceiling of 21%. Accordingly, the Controller, Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri has issued the above Reference No. A proposal has been submitted to the Government for release of funds from the approved budgetary allocation for the scheme of Subsidiary Grants (State Share 25%) (Programme) to Mahatma Phule Krishi Vishwavidyalaya under the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) funded scheme under Letters 2 and 3. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, Demand No. D-3, 01, Crop Protection, 120, Assistance to other Institutions, (00) (19) Government approves release of grant-in-aid to Mahatma Phule Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (State share 25%) (Programme) 24151086, 31-Assistant Grants (Non-salaried) under Indian Council of Agricultural Research funded scheme amounting to Rs. 2,31,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Thirty One Lakh only)@ 21% of the sanctioned budgetary allocation. 2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly implemented.
(2) The funds released by the Agricultural Universities under the head/ group/ scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the respective scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as non-scheme schemes. In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancies or other reasons, the subsidy shall be adjusted in the next month's disbursement.
(iii) If any of the sub-account, group, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(4) The grant-in-aid shall be payable only for the salaries and allowances of the posts sanctioned by the Government which have been filled up as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990 and the Government has approved the continuation of such posts at non-plan expenditure.
(5) Each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained.
(6) The assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the Agricultural Universities are correct as per the formula, and how the expenditure is being incurred as per the prescribed orders/ procedures shall be furnished to the Government from the end of the financial year concerned.

be submitted within a period of months. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
7) The Government while approving some of the new schemes of the University, have approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme in an adjustable manner. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures.
It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
9) While seeking grants for the year 2022-23, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed, actual expenditure and balance grants as per the scheme by 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
10) Don't spend money on posts and things that are out of order.
The Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
The Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, Rahuri or the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall take necessary action regarding the payment of the grants sanctioned under the respective sub-headings to the Controller of Agricultural Universities as mentioned in the Government Decision No. Agriculture and Cooperation Department No. Ajiu- 1072/18471/ V, dated 27th September, 1972 and Government Decision No. Krishiwi-2003/ CR- 35/20-A, dated 25/4/2003.
The expenditure for this purpose should be shared within the limits of the monthly fund statement from the provision approved under the head of accounts mentioned in the Budget 2022-23 and should be spent under the same head.
