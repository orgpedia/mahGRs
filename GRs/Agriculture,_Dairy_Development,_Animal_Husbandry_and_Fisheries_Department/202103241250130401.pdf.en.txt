3) Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune will be the implementing agency for the National Horticulture Mission under the Krishi Unnati Yojana Integrated Horticulture Development Mission. This campaign should be implemented as per the guidelines of the Central Government and as per the directions given from time to time.

The funds should be disbursed to the District Superintending Agriculture Officers through PFMS system and they should deposit the subsidy through PFMS system in the Aadhaar linked bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
The Accountant, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune is being declared as the Procurement and Disbursement Officer and the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, and Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune as the Controlling Officer for drawing the funds from the treasury.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) shall submit to the Government the utilization certificate regarding the allocation of funds made available under the scheme from time to time.
