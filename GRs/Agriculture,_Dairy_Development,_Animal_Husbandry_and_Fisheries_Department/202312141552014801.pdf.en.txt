The Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers Cadre) Act, 2019 provides for direct recruitment to the posts of teachers in Central Universities and Central Educational Institutions.
Category-wise reservation policy has been implemented. As per the existing reservation policy in direct service recruitment in the teaching cadre in the educational institutions of the state, subject-wise reservation is applicable in the respective teacher cadre. Due to the small size of the subject-wise cadre, subject-wise reservation applicable to all reserved categories does not provide adequate representation in the faculty cadre. Also in some cases where there is a single subject-wise post, reservation is not applicable.
In this background, the cadre-wise reservation policy prescribed by the Central Government, the prevailing reservation policy of the State Government as well as the direct recruitment of teachers in colleges under the Department of Agriculture and Padum, Department of Medical Education, Department of School Education, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance will be included in the direct service recruitment. The cadre-wise reservation policy prescribed by the Central Government in the educational institutions of the state after studying the court's judgment.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: KRIVAPADUM- 30023/68/2023-MAG-ADF2 In direct recruitment to the posts of Teachers in Colleges/ Institutions/ Regions/ Krishi Vigyan Kendras etc., the said Cadre wise reservation policy has been implemented vide Government Notification dated 09/10/2023. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines on the implementation of this reservation policy was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the implementation of the cadre-wise reservation policy in direct service recruitment of teaching posts in the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences and constituent colleges/ institutions/ areas/ Krishi Vigyan Kendras etc., the following guidelines are being issued: (a) Consideration of cadre-wise reservation: 1. As per the provisions prescribed in the Government Notification dated 09/10/2023, the sanctioned posts in all disciplines in the faculty cadre (of same pay scale) in each University as well as in all colleges under the control of the same management should be consolidated.
2. Cadre-wise reservation shall be determined in accordance with the percentage prescribed in the prevailing reservation policy taking into account the total number of sanctioned posts in the cadre. Also, action should be taken as per the existing orders of the government regarding parallel reservation.
3. Considering the total sanctioned posts and functional posts in the cadre, the point list of the cadre should be kept in the prescribed format along with the details of the posts. Thereafter, as the cadre-wise reservation as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers' Cadre) Act, 2021 will be applicable to the total number of sanctioned posts in the cadre, the category-wise reservation of vacant posts should be determined as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 18/10/1997 after examining the point list. Approval of the competent authority will be mandatory.
(b) On the implementation of the recruitment process in accordance with the cadre-wise reservation policy: 1. Once the category-wise reservation of vacancies as above is determined and the point list is finalized, an advertisement should be published to fill the vacancies in accordance with the prevailing procedure of the Government.
2) The advertisement should indicate the category-wise number of vacancies to be filled.
3) The advertisement should also indicate the total number of vacancies to be filled in the subject.
4. All eligible candidates may apply accordingly, taking into account the category of posts and vacancies mentioned in the advertisement.
After receiving the application as above, the selection process should be carried out in accordance with the prevailing procedure.
