As per the decision of the Government, a committee has been constituted to study the problems of fishermen in Dharamtar Bay and the impact of coastal pollution on fish production. The committee was to have one member appointed by the president, a representative of the concerned district collector, a representative of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur, a representative of the Pen Alibaug Sangharsh Samiti, which is affected by the Dharamtar Bay Project, and a member who knows the problems of fishermen.
Accordingly, the Government have decided to constitute the following Committee:
