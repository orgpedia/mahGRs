In view of the importance of the agricultural development sector in the state, the Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute at Nagpur has been declared as the apex training institute and seven training institutes at Nagpur, Pune, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nashik, Kolhapur and Khopoli in the state have been declared as associate and affiliated institutes of the apex training institute. As per the Government decision dated 17th July, 2001, the Shikhar Training Institute, Nagpur and its seven affiliated institutions of the department were declared as autonomous institutions.
The existing hostel building at Rameti, Khopoli was constructed in 1974 and accommodates 40 trainees. As the accommodation is inadequate, a new hostel with 108 trainee accommodations with necessary facilities has been proposed.
