5. Accordingly, the aforesaid Committee shall comply with the following conditions while inspecting the Government Residences at Worli B Aare.
1) Check if the applicant is a qualified government employee and if he / she needs accommodation while inspecting government accommodation at Worli B Aarey.
2) Make sure the applicant does not have his or her own residence. A self-declaration of the same should be obtained from the applicant.
3) If the Ajandar is found to have given erroneous and misleading information, rent and other charges as well as penalty should be collected from the Ajandar as per prevailing Government rules.
4) General Administration Department Government Decision PMMA 1518 / P.S. No. 461/23 -A, D with respect to Government Residences at Worli and Aarey. The terms and conditions mentioned in the 7/1/2021 should be followed as well as the rules.
The Commissioner, Dairy Development Department, Worli shall submit the report of the meetings of the aforesaid Committee along with the details of vacant accommodation, applications received for allotment, details of eligible applicants and the accommodation allotted accordingly to the Government every month.
