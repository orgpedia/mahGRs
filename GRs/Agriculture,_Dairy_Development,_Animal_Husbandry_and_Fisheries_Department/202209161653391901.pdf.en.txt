Introduction: Lumpy skin disease is a scheduled disease under the Central Government's" Prevention and Control of Infectious and Contagious Diseases in Animals Act, 2009." in the state. On 4.08.2022, an outbreak of viral lumpy skin disease in bovine cattle was first reported in Jalgaon district. The disease has spread to 21 districts of the state. In view of the rapid spread of this contagious and viral disease, it is necessary to control this disease immediately. For this, as per the instructions of the Central Government, all bovine animals within a radius of 5 km from the epicentre of the disease must be vaccinated. The state government has taken necessary steps to control the spread of the disease. The fact has been conveyed to the Cabinet in the Cabinet meeting held on 12.09.2022. The District Collectors of all the districts have sanctioned Rs. The Cabinet has directed that Rs. 100.00 lakh be released immediately. The government was considering issuing a government decision as per the directions given by the cabinet in the cabinet meeting.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. In view of the above facts, the Government Decisions are being issued as under:
In order to control the viral and contagious lumpy skin disease in bovine cattle in the state immediately, preventive vaccination, treatment of infected animals as per the protocol of the central government, syringes, needles, PPE kits, spraying of medicine in and around the cattle sheds in the affected areas, necessary machinery and equipment for spraying the medicine, vaccination through vaccinators, interns in veterinary colleges, Rs. 3/- per district from the budgeted funds of the District Planning Committee in 2022-23 to meet the expenditure on ancillary matters in line with the control of the disease such as honorarium per vaccination, scientific disposal of dead livestock, etc. 100.00 lakh is being approved by the Government to be made available to the District Collectors of the concerned districts.
3. The said Government Decision is based on the informal reference of the Planning Department No. 49/1481, dt. 14.09.2022 as well as, in the Informal Reference No. 337/2022/ Expenditure-8, Dy. 16.09.2022 with their concurrence.
