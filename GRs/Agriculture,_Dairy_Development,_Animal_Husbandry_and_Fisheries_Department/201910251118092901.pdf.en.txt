The Proposal:
In the 27th State Level Project Sanctioning Committee Meeting held on 18.07.2019 under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the project of Goat Farming on Public-Private Partnership basis of" Srijan Organic Commodities Pvt. Ltd., Solapur" has been approved. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the Government is giving administrative approval to the project of" Shrujan Organic Commodities Pvt. Ltd., Solapur" to take up goat farming on public-private partnership basis. A total of Rs. 527.99 lakh out of which Rs. 163.00 lakh is being sanctioned. The remaining Rs. 240.34 lakh beneficiary share and Rs. 124.65 lakh will be the shareholding of Srijan Organic Commodities Pvt. Ltd., Solapur. The project will be implemented under the National Rural Livelihoods Mission.
Objectives of the project: The objectives of the project are as follows:
To provide self-employment and supply of 20 goats and 02 goats for goat rearing in a modern partially stationary system to empower the farmers economically.
To achieve qualitative and quantitative increase in goat meat production.

Implementation and control of the project:
The District Animal Husbandry Deputy Commissioner, Solapur will be the implementing officer of the project at the district level. The project will be monitored at the divisional level by Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune Division and Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune. The Commissioner, Animal Husbandry, State of Maharashtra, Pune shall provide technical approval for the project. The duration of the project is one year. According to the project report approved for the implementation of this project, it is mandatory for Srijan Organic Commodities Pvt. Ltd., Solapur to submit a detailed model bond and work plan to the District Animal Husbandry Deputy Commissioner, Solapur for completion of the project within the stipulated time. The draft of the bond should be prepared by the Commissioner Animal Husbandry.
The Deputy Commissioner, District Animal Husbandry, Solapur shall take a legal bond from the above said institution and submit the same to the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune.
