INTEGRATIVE Horticulture Mission is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the integrated development of fruits, vegetables, tubers, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconuts, cashews, cocoa and bamboo. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between Centre and State since 2015-16.
The main objectives of the scheme are to increase the productivity of crops covered under the mission, increase the fertility and productivity of agricultural land, create new employment opportunities and increase the overall income of farmers. Under the Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (IHDM) for 2018-19, Rs. 26333 lacs in 2017-18 and Rs. 6941.56 lakhs.
