Chief Minister's Animal Health Scheme
About starting a mobile veterinary team in the state to provide animal health services at the doorstep of the livestock farmers.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries
Government Sanitary Sheet No.- PAVIA-1017/ PR No. 87/ PADUM-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,

Date: 28th of June 2019
Read:- Department of Agriculture and Padum Government Decision No. 1017/ PR No. 87/ Padum-3, dated 25.2.2019
Government's stand:
In order to provide veterinary services to the animal patients at the doorstep of the livestock rearers, the Government has approved the establishment of new mobile veterinary teams in 80 taluks of the State under the state level scheme of" Mukhyamantri Pashu Swasthya Yojana" in addition to the existing 65 mobile veterinary clinics in the State.
