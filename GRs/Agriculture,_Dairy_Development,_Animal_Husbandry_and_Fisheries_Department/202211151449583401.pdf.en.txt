The Proposal:
To increase the income of the farmers as well as the use of energy in the agriculture sector, the Centrally Sponsored Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization is being implemented in the state. Apart from this, under various centrally sponsored schemes like National Mission on Pulses, Oilseeds and Oilseeds, National Food Security Mission and National Horticulture Mission, some amount of funds are also provided for the agricultural mechanization component. However, adequate funds are not available for mechanization as per the demand of the farmers. Considering these aspects, funds are also made available for agricultural mechanization through RKVY-RAFTAR. In the State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) meeting held on 22 nd August, 2022, the project of Agricultural Mechanization Project amounting to Rs. 291.37 crore was approved.
