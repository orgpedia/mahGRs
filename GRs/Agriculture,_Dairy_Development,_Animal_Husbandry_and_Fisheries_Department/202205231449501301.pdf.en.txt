The Proposal:
The rates of service charges for providing various veterinary services to the sick animals and birds belonging to the farmers/ cattle rearers through all types of veterinary clinics under the Department of Animal Husbandry have been fixed and are long overdue. The prices of all kinds of medicines, surgical instruments, equipment, implements etc. needed to treat sick animals have increased in the last few years.
2. The matter of revision of rates of service charges for various services presently being charged for the treatment of sick animals and birds under the Animal Husbandry Department was under consideration of the Government.
3. In view of the above facts, the Government is issuing the following Government Decisions by superseding the Government Decisions at serial numbers 1, 2 and 3 regarding the service charges for various services being provided by the Animal Husbandry Department.
The government's decision:
(1) The revised rates of service charges to be levied for providing various veterinary services to the sick animals and birds belonging to farmers/ livestock rearers through Veterinary Dispensaries in the State are as under:
