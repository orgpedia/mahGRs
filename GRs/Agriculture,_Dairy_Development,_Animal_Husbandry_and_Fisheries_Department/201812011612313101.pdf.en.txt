The scheme will be applicable for the whole day i.e. 24 hours every day of the prescribed period. During this period, the farmer will be eligible for the benefit of this scheme even if he meets with an accident or becomes disabled.
Under this scheme, farmers or any other organization on their behalf do not have to pay the insurance premium separately to the insurance companies. The entire premium amount will be paid by the government to the insurance companies. Also, if any separate insurance scheme has been implemented or insured by the farmers or any other organization on their behalf in the past, it will have no connection with this scheme. The benefits under this scheme will be separate.

Further, if the closure of the scheme is decided less than one year in advance and the insurance company is informed, the Commissioner (Agriculture) as the Controlling Officer shall return the remaining premium amount to the Government within 30 days by crediting the amount of the insurance premium per beneficiary per day to the company, taking into account the annual premium rate on a pro rata basis.
9) The insurance proposal shall be deemed to have been received by the insurance company on the date on which it is filed/ received in the office of the Taluk Agriculture Officer along with the prescribed documents.
10) It will be mandatory for the insurance company to consider the insurance proposal received at any time during the period of the scheme with the prescribed documents and to accept the proposal received from the Taluk Agriculture Officer for the accidents that occurred in the last days of the scheme, up to 90 days after the end of the approved period of the current year of the scheme. It will also be mandatory for the insurer to accept insurance proposals received after 90 days with justifiable reasons. The insurance company cannot reject the insurance proposal on the ground that the proposal has not been submitted within the stipulated time.
The insurance company cannot refuse an offer of insurance without a clear reason. It is mandatory to inform the farmers concerned in Marathi language only and send a copy of the same to the designated insurance consulting company/ Commissioner (Agriculture)/ concerned Divisional Agriculture Joint Director/ District Superintending Agriculture Officer/ Taluk Agriculture Officer.
12) Orders regarding the appointment of an insurance consulting company for the scheme and the brokerage to be given to them are being issued under this government decision. The insurance consultancy firm to be appointed shall examine the proposals received from them properly/ thoroughly and submit the complete proposal to the concerned insurance company for approval. The insurance company will be responsible for approving the proposal sent by the insurance consultant to the insurance company. The insurance company is required to process the claim within 60 days from the date of submission of the claim to the insurance company. However, in case the proposal is rejected by the insurance company, if the concerned approach the insurance consultancy company in case the required documents are completed, the insurance consultancy company will be required to file claims on behalf of the farmers at IRDA Consumer Grievance Redressal Cell/ Insurance Judge/ District Consumer Grievance Redressal Forum/ State Consumer Grievance Redressal Commission/ Court. No reimbursement of any expenditure incurred in such cases shall be made by the Government or by the farmers/ claimants.
13) The insurance consultant to be appointed should send the list of pending cases in the district and monthly progress report to the Commissioner (Agriculture)/ Divisional Joint Director of Agriculture/ concerned District Superintendent Agriculture Officer/ Taluk Agriculture Officer every month. In case of proposals pending/ rejected due to insufficient documents, the responsibility of completing the documents will be of the Divisional Agriculture Joint Director/ District Superintending Agriculture Officer/ Taluk Agriculture Officer. If they do not fulfill their responsibilities properly, they will be held personally responsible.
