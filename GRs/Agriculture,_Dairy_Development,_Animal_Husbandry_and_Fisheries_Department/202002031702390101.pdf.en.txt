Government Resolution No. D. 7.06.2018.
The Proposal:
The Principal Secretary (Home). As per the directions of the Chairman in the meeting of the Project Management Committee held on 2.08.2019 for increasing milk production in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions, the Government was considering to include the new villages proposed by Mother Dairy Fruits and Vegetables Pvt. Ltd. in the scope of special projects being implemented for increasing milk production in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Under the special dairy project being implemented in the Vidarbha and Marathwada regions under the government decision No. 3, a total of 2,936 villages were identified in the project area, excluding 91 villages out of a total of 3,027 villages in 11 districts.
In addition to the 2,936 villages included in the government decision in No. 3, Mother Dairy Fruits and Vegetables Pvt. Ltd. has been established in 1,326 villages of 11 districts under the project. The Government hereby approves inclusion of 1,326 villages mentioned in Annexure A in the project area for providing milk collection facilities through the Co-operative Milk Producers' Cooperative Milk Producers' Units Ltd. and for implementing productivity enhancement programmes.
