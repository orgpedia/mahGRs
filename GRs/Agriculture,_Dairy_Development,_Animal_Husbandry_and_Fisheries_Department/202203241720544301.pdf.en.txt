For the year 2021-22, the Government of India has released a total Central share of Rs. 110.75 lakh to the three categories of farmers under the Mission for the first instalment for commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane). Crop wise distribution has been done as on 05/01/2022. The total amount of Rs. 18.83 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission under the Government decision should be determined by the Director (Extension and Training) as the limit of district-wise expenditure on PFMS system.
