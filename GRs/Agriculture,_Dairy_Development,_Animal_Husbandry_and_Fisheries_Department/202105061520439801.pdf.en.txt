Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 6th of May, 2021.
Reference: 1. The meeting was presided over by the Chief Minister. Meeting held on 17.03.2021.
2) Letter from the Office of the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research No. Makrupa/ Education-1/ Q. No. 419/836/2021, Dy. 19.04.2021.
Introduction: The above reference No. 1 as mentioned here. on 17.03.2021. In the meeting held under the chairmanship of the Deputy Chief Minister regarding the agricultural admission process and the upcoming agricultural education policy in the state, he directed that a committee of experts should be formed under the chairmanship of Dr. S. N. Puri, former Vice-Chancellor, to determine the policy of agricultural education. The Prime Minister has given. Accordingly, the Agriculture Council has proposed to constitute a Committee vide Reference No. 2 submitted to the Government. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a committee to decide on the new agricultural education policy.
Government Decision: There are 4 Agricultural Universities functioning in the State of Maharashtra. All the four agricultural universities offer various diploma, degree, post-graduate and master's degree courses. With a view to enhancing the quality of agricultural education, a high level committee is being constituted to decide on a new policy on agricultural education.

The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within a period of two months subject to the following conditions:
The Committee shall be empowered to make available the manpower required for preparation of the report from any of the four Agricultural Universities on a temporary basis.
(ii) The financial expenditure incurred by the Committee from the commencement of its work till the submission of its report to the Government.
All the four agricultural universities should divide their revenue equally from their income and deposit it with the member secretary of the committee.
The entire administrative and financial work of the Committee shall be done by the Member Secretary.
