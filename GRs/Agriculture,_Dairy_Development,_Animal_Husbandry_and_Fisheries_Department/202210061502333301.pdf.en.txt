The Proposal:
Under the National Food Security Mission for 2022-23, Rs. 708.10 lakh (Central and State share 60:40). In pursuance of the approval given vide letter dated 09/05/2022, the said Action Plan has been referred to. The administrative approval has been granted under the Government Decision dated 13/05/2022.
For the year 2022-23, the Central Government has released a total Central share of Rs. 106.21 lakh under Food and Nutrition Security (erstwhile National Food Security Mission) for commercial crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) in the first instalment for three categories. Crop wise distribution is given on 18/08/2022.
