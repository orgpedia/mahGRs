Establishment of a study group to bring all available land under Maharashtra Livestock Development Board and Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation under Animal Husbandry Department under fodder cultivation and to increase the productivity of fodder per hectare.

Date: 21st of June 2022
The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur and Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation, Pune, are autonomous bodies under the Department of Animal Husbandry, with a total area of 2598.76 hectares and 1995.62 hectares respectively.
Of these, the government was considering to set up a study group to suggest necessary measures regarding the cultivation of fodder crops on the remaining land, except the land under buildings, livestock sheds, roads, ponds, and the land that is not under cultivation at present. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves the constitution of the following study groups to suggest measures to be taken for the utilization of available land in Amaravati and Pashupaidas Kshetra, Waghapur, G. Yavatmal for the cultivation of fodder crops.

The study group constituted as above should submit a report to the Government through the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune, after conducting a comprehensive study in accordance with the following points, along with necessary measures.
Use of additional fodder (Murgas, dried fodder bales) O. Cultivation of crops/ fruit trees other than fodder crops (wherever feasible, especially in the areas of Goat Corporation)
5. Resources required for all the above measures Source of estimated funds and availability of funds.
The members of the study group should visit all the areas physically and obtain necessary information to submit the report in accordance with all the above points. The expenditure for travel in respect of field visits shall be met from the funds available under the Domestic Travel Expenditure target to be made available to the concerned offices on a regular basis during 2022-23. Also, the office of the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune, Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur and Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation, Gokhale Nagar, Pune should provide vehicles to the members of the study group as per the requirement for travel to visit the areas.
The Study Group shall submit a report to the Government through the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune in respect of the above issues within a period of 2 months from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
