Preamble: In the context of the above, it is proposed to implement the scheme of" Citrus Estate" for the cultivation of fruits and vegetables on the Taluka Fruit Plantation-Paithan area of Aurangabad district and to establish" Citrus Estate." The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 13.01.2022. There is also a taluk centre in Isarwadi. Paithan, G. Aurangabad to convert the area into Taluka Fruit Plantation, Isarwadi. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 22.11.2022. As per the proposal received at reference number 3, the Taluka Fruit Plantation-Paithan area of Aurangabad district is not continuous and is not suitable for the healthy and sound growth of the mulberry crop, instead of the area of that nursery, the matter of establishing a" Citrus Estate" for mulberry fruit crop on the area of the Taluka Fruit Plantation converted at Taluka Seed Multiplication Center, Isarwadi was under the consideration of the government. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to set up a" Citrus Estate" for Mung bean crop on the area of Taluka Fruit Plantation converted at Taluka Seed Multiplication Centre, Isarwadi instead of Taluka Fruit Plantation-Paithan area in Aurangabad district. For the" Citrus Estate" established by this Government decision, the above reference No. All other provisions of the Government Order dated 13.01.2022 shall continue to apply.
