Circular: Under the Government decision dated 21.10.2011 in the context of the General Administration Department, a comprehensive policy has been decided to prepare and publish the seniority list of the officers/ employees in the government service every year.
Dairy Development Department General Group-A (Junior) (Pay Scale Rs. 15600 39100 Grade Pay Rs. 5400) Revised (Pay Scale S-20: Rs. 65100-177500). An interim seniority list showing the status of 1/1/2021 is being circulated through this circular. Also, if there are objections/ errors regarding this temporary seniority, the concerned should submit a statement within fifteen days. Any objection raised after that will not be considered. The facts of the representations received should be checked from the records available to the concerned and these representations should be sent to the Government with detailed feedback. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the stipulated period, the final seniority will be published, assuming that no one has any objection to this provisional seniority list.
