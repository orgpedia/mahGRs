Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project (SMART) has been approved to be implemented in all the districts of the state over a period of 7 years (from 2019-20 to 2026-27). The Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project Society has been set up under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 for the implementation of this project.
The World Bank funded the project. The project has been implemented with effect from 23rd March, 2020 and is to be completed in a period of 7 years. As per the Government decision at Reference No.4, for the year 2020-21, under the head of 31- Support Grant for the project, Rs. A sum of Rs. 3.94 crore had been released.
