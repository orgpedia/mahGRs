Re-constitution of Divisional Promotion Committee for giving promotion to the vacant posts in the promotion quota in Group-B cadre in the Agriculture Department.
The Government of Maharashtra
Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries Development Department
Government Resolution No. 1021/ PROCESS. 42/16 A
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madame Cama Road, Mantralaya, Mumbai-32
Date: February 8th, 2021
Read:- 1) Government Decision Department of Agriculture and Cooperation No. Agriculture- 4281/1583/14 O, Dy. 06.10.1981 2) Government Decision Department of Agriculture and Padum No. 0217/ PR No. 37/15, D. 22.02.2017 Preamble: Provide for promotion of lower cadre to the vacancies in the promotion quota in Group A and Group B cadres in the Department of Agriculture. 06.10.1981 and D. The Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 22.02.2017. In the meantime, due to the transfer of the Agriculture Officer of the Zilla Parishad to the State Government and other collateral reasons, the Government was considering to reconstitute the existing Divisional Promotion Committee for the promotion of the employees of the Agriculture Supervisor (Group-C) cadre to the Maharashtra Agricultural Service Group-B (Junior), as well as the Agriculture Officer (Zilla Parishad) Group-B (Junior) (Gazetted) and Maharashtra Agricultural Service Group-B (Junior) cadre officers to the Maharashtra Agricultural Service (Group-B) cadre. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Departmental Promotion Committee is being reconstituted to provide promotions to the vacant posts in the promotion quota in Group-B and Group-B (Junior) cadres in the Agriculture Department.
