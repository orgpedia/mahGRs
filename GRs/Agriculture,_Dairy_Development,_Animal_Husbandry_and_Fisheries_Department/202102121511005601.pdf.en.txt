Letter from the office of the Council of Agriculture, Pune No. 10/2/2021 Preamble: The day to day functioning of educational institutions has been disrupted since March, 2020 due to lockdown announced across the country due to outbreak of COVID-19 virus. However, the number of Covid-19 cases in the state is still on the rise. In this regard, the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. 1 Regular classes in all non-agricultural universities, deemed universities, self-financing universities and colleges affiliated to them. Approval has been given to start from February 15, 2021. On the lines of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, the Agricultural Council, Pune Office, has issued Reference No. The letter has requested to issue a government circular/ government decision regarding the resumption of regular classes in person in all agricultural universities and affiliated schools and colleges in the state. Accordingly, the matter of starting regular classes in all agricultural universities and affiliated schools and colleges in the state was under the consideration of the government.
Government circular:
Regular classes in all agricultural universities and their affiliated government and private unaided schools and colleges in the state are being approved from February 15, 2021.
Universities and their affiliated schools/ colleges located in containment zones in the State, which come under the jurisdiction of the Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Municipality or the Collector and the Chairman, District Disaster Management Authority, may consider the local level prevalence of COVID-19 disease and the local conditions and availability of health infrastructure, in consultation with the concerned universities and their respective authorities.
