Accordingly, a government order has been issued.

The" Accounts Officer" in the office of the District Superintending Agriculture Officer of the concerned districts is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer, who shall draw the funds and transfer them to the concerned Collectorate for providing to the families of the deceased farmers/ agricultural labourers.
