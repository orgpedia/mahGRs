General Administration Department Reference No. 1 As per the Government Decision dated 21/10/2011, a comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in Government service has been prescribed every year. According to reference no. 2 of the General State Service, Group A cadre of Administrative Officers. Provisional Common Seniority located on 01/01/2014 has been published.
No objections have been received from any of the Administrative Officer Cadre Officers after the publication of the Provisional Seniority Statement dated 01/01/2014. Accordingly, the Government decision is being published as per the provisions of the Government Order, dated 21/10/2011 in Annexure A attached with the last date of seniority of General State Service, Group-A, Administrative Officer Cadre dated 01/01/2014.
