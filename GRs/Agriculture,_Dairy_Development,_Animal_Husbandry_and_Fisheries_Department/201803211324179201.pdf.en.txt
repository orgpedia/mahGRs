Introduction: In the Budget Session of the Legislative Council 2018, Starred Questions No. During the discussion held in the House in accordance with 37653, it was assured that an inquiry will be conducted as it was found that the tender condition was not included in the contract during the procurement process of saliva-crushed cotton by the Department of Animal Husbandry in 2016-17. Hon'ble Members had raised the issue of the inquiry not being completed despite the fact that the Chief Minister had given it to the Legislative Council in the 4th Winter Session of 2017. Accordingly, an independent investigation should be conducted before the end of the 2018 budget session. The Speaker has directed the government to constitute a committee to look into the matter.
A one-member inquiry committee headed by Mukesh Khullar, Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department, has been constituted to probe the matter.
