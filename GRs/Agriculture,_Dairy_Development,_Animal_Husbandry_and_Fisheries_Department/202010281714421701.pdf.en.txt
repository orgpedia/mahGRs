The Proposal:
Details of various schemes/ components to be implemented under Coconut Development Board, Kochi sponsored Coconut Development Scheme in the State during the year 2020-21 including financial/ physical aspects
Schedule-1 in the letter dated 07.08.2020. The Coconut Development Board, Kochi vide its letter dated 07.08.2020 has accorded administrative approval for an amount of Rs. 72.935 lakh for implementation of the scheme in the State. Out of this, the share of Coconut Development Board is Rs. 61.535 lakh and the allocation of scheme/ component wise financial targets has been ensured for SC sub-plan, ST sub-plan and general category wise. The components/ items to be implemented by the State under the scheme are mentioned. Guidelines for implementation of the scheme have been issued. The funds of the Coconut Development Board will be available after receiving the administrative approval of the state government to implement the scheme in the state. Administrative approval for the implementation of the scheme
The matter was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Considering the approval given by the Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India to the scheme, the administrative approval is being given for the implementation of Coconut Development Scheme sponsored by Coconut Development Board, Kochi in the year 2020-21 for a total amount of Rs. 72.935 lakh with Central share of Rs. 61.535 lakh, State share of Rs. 8.900 lakh and beneficiary farmer share of Rs. 2.500 lakh.
