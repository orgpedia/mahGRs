The Proposal:
The State Government, vide Government Resolution dated 19/01/2019, has sanctioned World Bank-funded Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project worth about Rs. The project with an investment of Rs 2,220 crore (US$ 300 million) has been approved in principle for a period of 6 years. Similarly, the relevant government decision will be taken to prepare the project implementation plan. Under 27/05/2019, it has been approved to set up Project Management Unit under the Commissionerate of Agriculture with 37 posts of officers/ staff.
The Project Management Cell of the Project will prepare the project plan and finalize the draft of Project Implementation Report (PIP) and various manuals. Once the project is approved by the State Government and the World Bank, the Project Management Cell will be responsible for the actual implementation and control of the project. The funds for the project will be provided every year from the budgetary provision of the State Government from the current financial year till the completion of the project and the funds will be spent for the establishment/ office matters of the Project Management Cell and for various aspects related to the actual implementation of the project. For this, the project director of the project was to be declared as the head of the department and the government was considering declaring the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Controller to collect the funds from the treasury for the project from time to time.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. KRIVAPUR-4618/ PR.No. 102/8-A
Government's decision:
The Project Director, Project Management Cell, Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation, Pune, is being declared as the Head of the Department.
Project Director, Project Management Cell, Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the project.
Financial Specialist, Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the project.
The funds required for the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project will be budgeted every year under the following headings and will be made available to the Project Management Cell from time to time through the Budget Distribution System.
