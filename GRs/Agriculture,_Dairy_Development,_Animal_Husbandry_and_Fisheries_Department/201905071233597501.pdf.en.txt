Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani under Fruit Research Centre, Mauje.
Survey No. 0.39 r of 1 and Survey No. Vasantrao Naik Marathwada to transfer total 0.69 R area out of 3 to Aurangabad Municipal Corporation
In view of the approval given by the Executive Council of the Agricultural University and the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, as well as the concurrence shown by the Revenue and Forest Department, the transfer to the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and conditions
1) The actual value of the land to be transferred as per the prevailing market value.
The municipal corporation should pay a lump sum to the university before taking over.
(2) A cement concrete structure of at least 7 feet height along the side of the affected area.
The retaining wall should be constructed by the municipal corporation.
3) The land is used for the purpose for which it was transferred.
not to transfer the land to any third party.
Rights should not be created.
(4) The Agricultural University shall be entitled to take possession of the land in case of violation of the above conditions.
This Government decision is being issued with the approval of the Revenue and Forest Department/ 7G.
