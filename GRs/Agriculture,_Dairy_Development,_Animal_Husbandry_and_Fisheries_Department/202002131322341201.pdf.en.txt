Cancellation of appointments of non-official members made on District/ Taluka Cooperative Milk Producers Union in the State.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries, Government Resolution No.5/ 2020/ No.14/ Padum-8, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Mumbai-400 032
Date: February 13, 2020
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per sub-clause (2) of section 73AAA of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 and as amended by the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles vide order dated 14/12/2018, in the case of co-operative societies which are aided by the Government in the form of equity, loan, guarantee, grant, government land or in any other form, whether in cash or kind, other than housing societies supported by the Government, a Government officer not below the rank of Assistant Registrar, Co-operative Societies, may be appointed as a Director in the Board of Directors and a private person having experience in dealing with the affairs of such co-operative societies, as a co-operative society, may be appointed as a Government appointed Director. Also, the Department of Cooperation. 6/4/2016 and dt. The experience and educational qualifications for the person to be nominated have been prescribed vide Notification dated 14/3/2017.
The Government has appointed the following non-official members to the various 26 District/ Taluka Cooperative Milk Producers Union in the State under the authority of the above provisions.
