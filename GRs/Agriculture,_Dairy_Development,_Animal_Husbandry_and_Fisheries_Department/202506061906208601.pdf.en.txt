Introduction :-
As per the request from Department of Economics Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Government of India, New Delhi Mr. Jalindar Murlidhar Pangare, District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Kolhapur has been nominated by Agriculture
Department of Government of Maharashtra to participate in the launch event of scaling Next Generation Rice program of the World Bank Group Academy from 08" June, 2025 to 12" June, 2025 (including journey time) at the headquarters of the IRRI
in Los Banos Philippines. Accordingly, the proposal of sending Mr. Jalindar
Murlidhar Pangare to the said program was under consideration of Government of Maharashtra
Government Resolution :-
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord approval to the visit
Mr.Jalindar Murlidhar Pangare, District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Kolhapur to participate in the launch event of scaling Next Generation Rice program of the World Bank Group Academy from 08" June, 2025 to 12" June, 2025 (including
journey time) at the headquarters of the IRRI in Los Banos Philippines.
2. Government is further pleased to authorize the absence from duty of
Mr.Jalindar Murlidhar Pangare, District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Kolhapur be treated as on duty period until he resumes his duties on return to India
from the foreign tour.
3. They will be entitled to draw full pay and allowances which they would have drawn, had they remained on duty in India.
4. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation for the members of
delegation to be borne by the World Bank.
5. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions if any should be submitted to the government within one month from the date of
returning.
