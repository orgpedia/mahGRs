the Office Order
Various development programmes/ schemes are implemented by various administrative departments of the Government of Maharashtra and their subordinate and subordinate field offices. Evaluation exercise evaluates the implementation of these development schemes/ programmes as well as the office functioning, decision making process to identify the achievements and shortcomings in the implementation of the schemes and activities/ decision making. To address these gaps and suggest measures to improve the decision-making process and make the schemes/ programmes reach the common man better and more effectively, the Quality Council of India (QCI), a tertiary agency, has been mandated to conduct the evaluation. The approval has been given in accordance with the Government Resolution dated April 27, 2023.
General Administration Department. According to the circular dated August 11, 2014, the Department of Agriculture The Office Order dated 28th November, 2017 has decided to lay down the stages of case presentation to bring in flexibility and speed in the decision-making process and to ensure that the decision-making process is smooth.
Accordingly, the levels of performance are being decided for taking decisions on the subject matter related to this assignment as shown in the accompanying table. However, if there is a direction from higher authorities or if there is a need in certain exceptional cases, appropriate changes will be made on a case to case basis. Also in case of policy matters there will be more than three levels of presentation.
