Preamble: Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries The names of the Veterinary Institutions to be taken up under the District Annual Plan have been included in the Government Decision dated 30.08.2010. As the name of the Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Akola is not included in this, the government is issuing a decision in the following way to include the name of this board and the institutions under the control of the said board, including 3 sand laboratories, 2 rearing centers and 7 rearing areas.
Government Decision: In connection with the construction, strengthening and modernization of veterinary institutions under the District Annual Plan Read No. 1 In the Government Resolution dated 30.08.2010, page 1, the Government hereby approves the inclusion of the Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Akola and the three silt laboratories, two rearing centres and seven rearing areas under the control of this Board in the said paragraph. The approval will be applicable as long as the immovable property of the board is in the name of the government.
(2) The decision of the Government shall be subject to the concurrence of the Planning and Finance Department and the approval of the Finance Department. Reference No. 144/2020/ Expenditure-2, dated 18.03.2020.
