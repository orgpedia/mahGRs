Approval of State Level Farmers Advisory Committee (SFAC) under Assistance for Extension Reforms (ATMA) to State Agriculture Extension Programmes.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SAMRIDH-2020/ PR No. 52/3-A
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry Extension, Mumbai 400 032.
The date: November 18, 2021
Read: 1) Director (Atma), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra Pune, vide letter No. No.32 17/ A. 703/21 C.E. 11/11/2021 Preamble: Under the Guidelines of the Central Government under the Assistance for Extension Reforms to State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA), 2018, it has been notified to constitute various Committees at the State, District and Taluka level. Accordingly, guidelines have been issued by the office of the Commissioner (Agriculture) for the formation of these committees. Criteria were given to inform the name of a farmer from the district level to form a state-level farmer advisory committee. Following that. As per the proposal of Director (Aatma), Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune dated 11.11.2021, Shri. As recommended by the Minister of State (Agriculture), Shri. A proposal was under the consideration of the Government to approve the names of farmers selected for the State Level Farmers Advisory Committee (SFAC) under the Assistance for Extension Reforms (ATMA) to the State Agriculture Extension Programmes by incorporating the information received from the office of the Minister (Agriculture). Following this, the government decided:
Government Decision: Under the Assistance for Extension Reforms to State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA), the Government is approving the names of selected farmers as per Annexure A attached to the said Government Decision to the State Level Advisory Committee (SFAC).
2. Remaining categories should be filled while appointing members of the remaining districts.
