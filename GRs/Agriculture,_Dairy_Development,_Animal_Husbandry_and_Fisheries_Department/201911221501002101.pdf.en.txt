Preamble: In the context of Finance Department No. 2 The recommendations of the Committee of State Wage Revision Committee, 2017 have been accepted under the Government Resolution dated 01.01.2019 and are under reference No. As per the notification dated 30.01.2019, the revised pay structure has been made applicable to the State Government employees with effect from 01.01.2016 as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission.
2. Also in the context of Finance Department No. 2 The recommendations in this matter shall be applicable to eligible State Government employees, Zilla Parishads, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, non-judicial staff of courts, aided school staff, employees of Agricultural and Non-Agricultural Universities and their affiliated aided non-government colleges and employees of other aided institutions. It recommends that the date of implementation of these recommendations for employees of other offices/ institutions should be the same as that of the State Government employees and in column 4 it states that" Recommendation accepted, these recommendations should be implemented by the Government for employees of other aided institutions implementing the 6th Pay Commission and receiving salary subsidy."
In view of the aforesaid facts, the proposal for grant of revised pay structure in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission to the non-teaching cadres on the establishment of Maharashtra Veterinary and Fisheries Sciences University was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: MPV 2019/ PR No.36/ MafSU
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is now ordering that the non-teaching cadre on the establishment of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences be sanctioned a revised pay structure as indicated in Annexure 1 to 16 in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission.
(ii) Fixation of pay in the revised pay structure and payment of admissible arrears thereon vide Government Notification No. 3 dated 30th January, 2019, Department of Finance, vide No. Government Circular No. 4, Department of Finance, dated 20th February, 2019. Government Circular dated 14th May, 2019, No. 6 As per the provisions of the Government Circular, Department of Finance, dated 30th May, 2019.
The option required for fixation of pay in the revised pay structure should be given within one month from the date of issue of this order. The option once given will be final.
The additional expenditure arising out of the revised pay structure implemented under this Order shall be covered under the head of accounts under which the salaries and allowances of non-teaching staff are spent and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the same. The government order/ procedure in this regard should be followed.
This Government Resolution is being issued in concurrence with the Informal Reference No. 425/2019/ SERVICE-9 of the Department of Finance dated 14th October, 2019.
