2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 12.04.2023 should be strictly implemented.
(2) The funds allocated by the Agricultural Universities under the scheme and subject-wise allocation should be spent only for the permissible aspects of the scheme concerned.
(iii) If the amount disbursed remains unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for any other group/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(4) The schemes under which the period of the scheme has expired shall not be continued after the relevant period at the cost of the schemes without obtaining the approval of the Government.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grant-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities is correct as per the formula, and how it is being spent as per the prescribed order/ procedure. Also, the amount of unutilized funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
6) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the universities are governed by the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code 1991.
