The Proposal:
The Government of India has decided to implement the Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIH) from 1st April, 2014 by converging all the schemes of National Horticulture Mission (NHM), Horticulture Mission for North Eastern and Himalayan States (HMNEH), National Horticulture Board (NHB), Coconut Development Board (CDB) and Central Institute of Horticulture (CIH). Accordingly, the National Horticulture Mission being implemented in the state has been included in the Integrated Horticulture Development Mission. This mission has been included in the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Agriculture Promotion. Integrated Horticulture Mission is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the development of fruits, vegetables, tubers, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew, cocoa and bamboo products. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between the Centre and the States.
The main objectives of the scheme are to increase the productivity of crops covered under the mission, increase the fertility and productivity of farm land, create new employment opportunities and increase the overall income of farmers. In 2022-23, a new programme of Rs. 14000.00 lakh and a Spill Over/ Committed programme of Rs. 5933.97 lakh for implementation of National Horticulture Mission under Krishi Unnati Yojana- Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State with a total outlay of Rs. 19933.97 lakh has been approved by the Central Government vide letter No. Therefore, the Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune has requested for administrative approval of the Annual Work Plan of Rs. 19933.97 lakh for the year 2022-23 under reference No. 4.
Krishi Unnati Yojana- National Horticulture Mission under Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (IHDM) was under consideration for grant of administrative approval for implementation of the said scheme in 2022-23 considering the budgetary provisions for implementation in the State in 2022-23 and the Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government.
