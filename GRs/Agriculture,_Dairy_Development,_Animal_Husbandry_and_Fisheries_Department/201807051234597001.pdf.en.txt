the prelude
The World Bank funded Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project (Climate Resilient Agriculture Project) is being implemented over a period of 6 years in about 4000 drought affected villages of Vidarbha and Marathwada and about 900 villages of Kharpan belt of Purna river basin in Vidarbha. The Department. A Technical Advisory Committee under the Chairmanship of MAMS (Agriculture) has been constituted for effective implementation of the project as per the Government Order dated 13th June, 2017. There is a provision to appoint 2 non-official members in the total 15 member committee, according to which the matter of appointing a non-official member was under the consideration of the government.
the Governing
As per the provisions of the above Government Order, Mr. Vinayak Mete is being appointed to the vacant seat as a non-official member in the Technical Advisory Committee formed for the effective implementation of Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Yojana.
