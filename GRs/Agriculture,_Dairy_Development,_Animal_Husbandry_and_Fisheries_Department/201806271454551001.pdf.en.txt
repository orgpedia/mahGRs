Agriculture mechanization sub-mission is being implemented in the state. There are a total of eight components under this sub-mission. 1 and 2 are about demonstration, training and testing of the new technology developed plant. Component No. For 1 and 2, the ratio is 100%. For the remaining six components, the ratio of central and state funds is 60: 40. The Central Government has issued guidelines for this.
To develop the socially backward districts, the Central Government has taken up the programme of" Transformation of Aspirational Districts." In this programme, 111 backward districts of the country have been selected. Under this programme, 25 villages in each district are selected and the villages are to be completed. These districts are Osmanabad, Washim, Gadchiroli and Nandurbar.
The Central Government has decided to implement the Agriculture Mechanization Sub Mission under Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan in the selected districts under this programme. For this, 10 farmers from each of the 25 selected villages of the district will be selected.
