The following terms and conditions shall be followed while implementing the scheme.
1) Follow the guidelines and guidelines issued by the government.
(2) The accounts of the expenditure of the scheme should be kept in order and the audit report and utility certificates of the expenditure should be submitted to the State Government along with the progress report.
3) The physical and progress reports on various aspects of the scheme should be submitted to the state government at the beginning of every month.
4) The balance sheet and the amount of the audit deposit related to the scheme should be reported by the implementing agency. It should clearly state the amount spent at the beginning of the year and the income received for the mission.
5) Transparency should be maintained regarding the amount of the mission's accumulated expenses and it should be ensured that there is no ambiguity.
6) It should be ensured that no programme beyond the approved programme is implemented under any circumstances.
