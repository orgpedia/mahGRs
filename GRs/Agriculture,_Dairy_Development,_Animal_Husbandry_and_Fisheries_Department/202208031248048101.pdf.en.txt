Preamble: The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the Agriculture and Padum Departments (itself), Ministries, Commissionerates of Agriculture and subordinate offices. The funds available under the e-Governance programme through the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries (DADF) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture are utilised for the procurement of computers, IT initiatives, IT systems for the department, development and maintenance of the e-Governance system for the Department of Agriculture (Ministry level), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune and subordinate field offices. Under this scheme, the sanctioned funds are distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, as per the requirement.
The e-Governance programme is a scheme under the scheme and in the financial year 2022-23, a total amount of Rs. 25 crore has been earmarked for the project. As per the circular dated 04.04.2022, for the year 2022-23, 60% of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 15 crore has been made available to the Department for State e-Governance Programme for the first nine months.
