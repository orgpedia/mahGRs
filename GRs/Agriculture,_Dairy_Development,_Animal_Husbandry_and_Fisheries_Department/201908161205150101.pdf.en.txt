Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in the State in Kharif 2019. The scheme is being implemented through two insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India and Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company as per the Government decision dated 22.05.2019. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AIIO), under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2019, has provided crop insurance premium subsidy to the tune of Rs. 707,02,75, 658/- has been demanded. However, in the revised guidelines of the Central Government, point no. 13.2.3 states that" the insurance premium share of the Central and State Government is required to be paid to the company in advance (first installment) even if the data of the insured area is not available at the time of registration of the current season. The insurance premium subsidy amount should be 50 per cent of the total 80 per cent of the total state share insurance premium subsidy paid in the previous season in advance to the companies," it said. Accordingly, Rs. 682,29,56, 694/- is admissible as crop insurance premium subsidy to the State Government.
