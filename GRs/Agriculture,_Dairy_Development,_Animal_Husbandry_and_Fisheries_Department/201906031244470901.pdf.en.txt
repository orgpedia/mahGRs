The Proposal:
Vasantrao Naik State Institute of Agricultural Extension Management and Training (VANAMATI), Nagpur As per the Government decision dated 31.10.2001, the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer, Nagpur was declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Government was considering declaring the Senior Administrative and Accounts Officer (Gazetted Group A-Class 1) of the Vanamati Institute as Drawing and Disbursing Officer in place of the District Superintending Agriculture Officer, Nagpur.
The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Senior Administrative and Accounts Officer (Gazetted Group A-Class 1) on the establishment of Forest Institute for Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management and Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry. Reference No. 36/19/ Koshaprasad-5, Dy. being issued as per the consent given on 5.2.2019.
