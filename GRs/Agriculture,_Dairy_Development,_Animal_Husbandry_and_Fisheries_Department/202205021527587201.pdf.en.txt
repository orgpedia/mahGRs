The Proposal:
The Department of Agriculture implements various Central and State sponsored schemes as well as externally aided projects. After the annual action plan of the centrally sponsored schemes is finalised, the government receives funds in one or two phases in a year, similarly, the state sponsored schemes also receive funds in a phased manner. Though the agriculture department receives funds in a phased manner, the activities of the agriculture sector are season-wise. About 75% of the area under cultivation in kharif and rabi seasons is covered by various crops in kharif season itself. Therefore, the Kharif season is very important for the economy and farmers of the state. It is necessary to implement the elements that will complement the crops of this season in the pre-Kharif and post-season period. This includes tools and equipment.
As per the decision of the government, administrative approval has been given for the implementation of the State Sponsored Agricultural Mechanization Scheme. Under this scheme, component number-3 of the Central Government's Agriculture Mechanization Sub Mission, providing subsidy to individual beneficiary farmers for the purchase of agricultural implements/ machines and component number-4, providing subsidy to agricultural implements/ machines banks are implemented as per the guidelines of the Central Government's Agriculture Mechanization Sub Mission.
For the year 2022-23, Rs. Rs 400 crore has been allocated for this. Department of Finance As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 4 th June, 2022, the Department of Finance has been empowered to disburse funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary allocation. However, instructions have been received from the Department of Finance to make the said funds available to the Department at the rate of 7% per month on the Budget Distribution System
