Government's decision:
Read No. As per the decision of the Government of the 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th States Level Administrative Procurement Committee, Technical Specification Committee, Sub-Committee have been formed to determine the technical specifications for the purchase of medicines, weapons, implements, equipment, dyes, chemicals, machinery, vaccines, animal feed, fodder seeds, etc. required by the institutions under the Department of Animal Husbandry. The procedure has also been prescribed as per the provisions of the Revised Manual dated 01.12.2016.
During the meeting of the State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee, it was found that there were discrepancies in the terms and conditions prescribed on the GeM portal for the suppliers to purchase various items while conducting the e-tendering process from the GeM portal of the Central Government. It has also been observed that the said terms and conditions have not been approved by the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune or the competent authority.

Since there has to be uniformity in implementation, the terms and conditions laid down under the procurement procedure such as income tax returns of the tenderer for the last three years, documents relating to financial turnover for the last three years, determination of basic price of the item/ item to be procured, amount of EMD to be charged, duration of production/ delivery and experience of the tenderer in respect of the item/ item concerned, etc.
Before uploading it on the GeM portal, it should be approved by the Principal Secretary and Chairman, State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee.
