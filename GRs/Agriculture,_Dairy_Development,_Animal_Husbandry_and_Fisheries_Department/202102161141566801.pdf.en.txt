The Proposal:
The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retd. The Government have decided to accept the recommendations of the said Committee with certain modifications in terms of the Government Resolution at Reference No.2. Accordingly, along with other state government employees, non-teaching employees of agricultural universities were given Rs. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 have been notified under Reference No. 3 regarding the implementation of the revised pay structure with effect from January 1, 2016. Further, the detailed procedure for fixation of initial pay as per these rules has been prescribed vide Circular No. 4. Based on this, the government was considering a proposal to implement the revised pay structure for non-teaching staff of agricultural universities, full-time employees of agricultural colleges affiliated to agricultural universities, aided agricultural colleges, agricultural technical schools and rural education institutions under agricultural universities.
