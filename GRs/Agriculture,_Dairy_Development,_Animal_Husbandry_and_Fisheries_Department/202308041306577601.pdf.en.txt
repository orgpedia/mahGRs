Introduction: In order to increase the productivity of crops in the state, various experiments are carried out by the farmers and efforts are made to increase the productivity. If such experimental farmers are encouraged and honoured, their willpower, morale and enthusiasm to use the latest technology is further enhanced. Farmers contribute to agricultural production. In addition, crop competitions are organized within the state with the aim of providing guidance to other farmers in the area and adding value to the state's total agricultural production. Keeping in view the need to change the norms in the crop competition scheme in the year 2020-21. As per the Government decision dated 05.10.2020, the implementation mechanism for inter-State inter-crop marketing of foodgrains, pulses and pulses has been modified.
In the Rabi season 2021, the first, second and third rank results at the state, division, district and taluka levels have been received by the Agriculture Commissionerate and a total of Rs. 72,17,210 has been requested to provide funds as per the letters of reference No. 2 and 3 of the Commissionerate of Agriculture. The government has decided to allocate funds accordingly.
Government Decision: 1. Rs. 70,07,000 lakh (Rupees Seventy Lakhs Seven Thousand only) is being approved.
