Preamble: The Government, under the Government decisions at Reference No. 1 and 2, have sanctioned Rs.50/-per kg and Rs. The incentive subsidy of Rs.5/- per litre has been sanctioned subject to certain conditions.
The Scheme of Incentive Subsidy will be effective till 19th January, 2019.
Under the same government decision, the government had made a subsidy of Rs.5/- per liter per milk of 3.2/8.3 cow milk and above, excluding packaged milk produced in the state, permissible. The scheme has been implemented from 1st August, 2018 to 31st October, 2018. Keeping in view the response to the scheme, the government has decided to take a separate decision on the continuation of the scheme as per the need.
Commissioner, Dairy under reference number 3 has submitted a positive report to the government regarding the response to the scheme and has stated in his report that the farmers are satisfied with the scheme. The state is facing a drought-like situation. Dairy farming is seen as an important link with agriculture for dairy farmers. In view of the prevailing drought like situation in the State, the Government was considering to continue the subsidy scheme of Rs.5/- per litre for remaining 3.2/8.3 cow milk and above quality milk excluding packaged milk produced in the State beyond 31st October, 2018 in the interest of milk producing farmers.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby extend the validity of the Scheme B issued under Reference Nos. 1 and 2 with retrospective effect for a period of three months i.e. from 1st November, 2018 to 31st January, 2019. The rest of the terms and conditions of the Government Decision No. 1 and 2 in this regard shall remain in force.
