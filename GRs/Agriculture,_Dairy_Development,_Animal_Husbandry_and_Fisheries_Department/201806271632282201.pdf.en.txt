2. Letter from the Commissioner of Agriculture vide No. Cr./ V.P. 1/ RASUA/ NV 1819/235/18, D. 14.06.2018.
Cleaning paper:
The Government has approved the release of Rs. 7037.93333 lakh for the year 2018-19 for the general category crops under Krishi Unnati Yojana-National Food Security Mission. The Government may, in addition to para-3 of the said decision, read the following para-3:" 3. The Director (Extension and Training) is being authorized to allocate district wise/ office wise the amount of Rs. 7037.93333 lakh of Central and State share to be made available for National Food Security Mission under the Government decision and he shall disburse the funds to be allocated district wise under the Mission to the districts through BDS system through Divisional Agriculture Joint Directors and the District Superintending Agriculture Officer shall draw the funds received and deposit the same in the PFMS account and make the entire payment on PFMS. "
