Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State Government is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme for the year 2017-18 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent.
The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the year 2017-18 for 9 crops viz., Grapes, Orange, Mango, Pomegranate, Banana, Mango, Cashew, Chikoo and Lemon in the district by adopting the revenue board component.
Reference No. 18 for implementation of Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana in 2017-18 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 1. As per the demand of the participating companies for the year 2017-18, the State share subsidy was under the consideration of the Government for the financial sanction of the State share amount as submitted by the Commissionerate of Agriculture.
Government Decision: Considering the demand submitted by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2017-18 (Ambia Bahar), Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company has been allotted Rs. 2,66,321/- is being approved by the Government as follows:
