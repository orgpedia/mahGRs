No. 19/ Q. 17, dated 22nd September, 2020 2) Letter No. Kriya/ EXM/ Vilep/ Astha- 4/68/31796/ 2021, Dy. 27.09.2021 Preamble: The above Department of General Administration. As per the Government Circular dated 22.09.2020, all the Administrative Departments under whose jurisdiction the Departmental Examinations were being conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, have been asked to conduct their Departmental Examinations at their level from the year 2021. The departments have also been asked to set up separate cells for conducting these examinations in a time-bound manner. Since the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will not be conducting the departmental examinations from the year 2021, a separate cell under the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, for the work of the departmental audit examination for gazetted officers in the agriculture department and the post-service entrance examination for employees of Group-C cadre, and a committee under the chairmanship of the Commissioner (Agriculture) will be appointed to conduct the examination. The proposal has been submitted to the government. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government Order: Under this order, a separate cell is being set up for the following officers/ staff available in the office of the Deputy Director of Agriculture, Employees Welfare, Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune for the work of the departmental audit examination for gazetted officers of the Agriculture Department and the post-service entrance examination for employees of Group-C cadre.

2.1 The functions to be performed by the Committee in the aforesaid composition shall be as follows:
Select candidates for the examination and receive the question paper set from them in a sealed envelope.
Select the question paper of each paper from the question papers received from the candidates selected for the examination.
(iii) Determining the responsibility of confidentiality of the set of question papers.
(iv) Keeping the answer sheets safe and confidential after the examination.
V. To finalize the score and result of the examination.
The revised rules of departmental examinations will be notified by amending the existing departmental examination rules for gazetted officers in the agriculture department.
Till the said rules come into force, the examinations should be conducted as per the existing rules and even after the revised rules are notified, the above constituted committee should take action to conduct the departmental examinations.
