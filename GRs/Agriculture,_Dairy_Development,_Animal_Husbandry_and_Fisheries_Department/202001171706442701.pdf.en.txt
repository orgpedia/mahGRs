Preamble: For the year 2019-20, the Government of India has approved an Action Plan for food crops (Rice, Wheat, Pulses and Nutri Cereals) under National Food Security Mission (NFSA) for an amount of Rs. 22019.8155 lakh. 22019.8155 Lakhs (Central Share Rs. 13211.8893 Lakhs+ State Share of Rs. 8807.9262 lakhs) has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 03.06.2019. For the year 2019-20, the Central Government has released Rs. 3173.35 lakh as second instalment under the Mission and as per the demand of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, the Government is taking the following decisions to release the required funds from the Central and State share
Government Decision: For the year 2019-20, under the Krishi Unnati Yojana-National Food Security Mission, a total of Rs. 3855.184 lakh (Rs. Thirty eight crore fifty five lakh eighteen thousand four hundred only) of Central and State share for the general category of food crops is being made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Pune, under this Government Decision, crop-wise through the Budget Distribution System.

The total amount of Rs. 3855.184 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission as per the decision of the Government should be disbursed district wise through PFMS system by the Director (Extension& Training).
The Director (Extension and Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the withdrawal of funds to the tune of Rs. 3855.184 lakhs made available under this Government Decision.
The funds made available under this Government Decision should be utilised immediately.
The Director (Extension and Training) shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Secretary (Agriculture).
