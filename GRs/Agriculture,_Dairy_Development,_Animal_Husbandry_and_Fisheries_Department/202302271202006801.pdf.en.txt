2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly implemented.
The actual cost of the above sanctioned grant, the number of sanctioned posts and the utilization of the disbursed grant should be submitted by the Agricultural University to the Government at the end of every month. The funds disbursed by the Agricultural University under the head/ group/ scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the respective scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as schemes under the schemes. In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancies or other reasons, the subsidy shall be adjusted in the next month's disbursement. However, the subsidy made available in 2022-23 will have to be mandatorily spent as per the approved monthly distribution statement.
(iii) Where the funds distributed sub-heading wise, group wise, scheme wise and subject wise are lying unutilized/ remaining unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ balance funds should not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without the approval of the Government.
4. Each sub-heading/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained.
While seeking grants for the year 2022-23, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed scheme-wise, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
The Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
