Introduction: In the interest of the farmers, the farmers' magazine published since 1965 provides information to the farmers of the state and outside the state about the interesting and latest developments in the field of agriculture. The Commissioner (Agriculture) has approved the proposal to hand over the printing of Farmers Monthly Issue to Anand Publications, Jalgaon as the rates quoted by Anand Publications, Jalgaon in the tender process for printing of Farmers Magazine for the year 2019-21 are minimal. The letter dated 30.5.2019 has been submitted to the Government. In accordance with the proposal of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, a proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the tender process of the farmers' magazine, to provide the allowable amounts to the eligible tenderers according to the tender process and to approve the expenses incurred for the delivery of the issue post.
Decision of the Government: Commissioner (Agriculture) Sh. After considering the proposals received in the letter dated 30.5.2019, the Government has now decided as follows:
The tender rates for printing of Farmers Magazine, editing/ simplification of articles, layout-decoration of articles, four-colour printing of magazine, including the procedures for awarding the printing work of Farmers Magazine to Anand Publications, Jalgaon, the lowest tenderer in the e-tendering process conducted by the Commissioner (Agriculture) at his level for the period of two years i.e. April, 2019 to March, 2021 from the date of issue of the said order.
