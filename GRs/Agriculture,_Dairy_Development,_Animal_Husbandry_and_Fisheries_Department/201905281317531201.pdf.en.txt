The Government of the Department of Agriculture and Padum Decision No. KRIVAPUR-4618/ Q.No. 102/8-O, Dy. 19/01/2019 Preamble: In the Cabinet Meeting dated 8.01.2019, the World Bank (IBRD) funded" Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART)" was launched with an outlay of about Rs. The project with an investment of Rs 2,220 crore ($ 300 million) has been approved in principle for a period of six years. It has been approved to set up a Project Management Unit with requisite manpower to prepare the Project Implementation Plan.
The Cabinet of Ministers has approved the creation of 37 full-time posts (14 government and 23 contractual) in the Project Management Cell in the first phase on a temporary basis at the establishment of the Commissionerate of Agriculture for the preparation of the project plan.
In pursuance of the Cabinet approval, the creation of 37 new posts in the Project Management Cell has been approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary on a rotational basis. As per the approval, the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project's Project Management Cell was under the consideration of the government to be operationalized in Pune with 37 posts under the Commissioner (Agriculture). The government has taken the following decisions:
A Project Management Unit (PMU) under the Commissioner (Agriculture) should be set up in Pune to prepare the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project (PIP).
In order to prepare the project plan, the following 37 full-time posts (14 government and 23 contractual) are being approved in the Project Management Cell on a temporary basis at the establishment of the Commissionerate of Agriculture till the project period:

3. Filling up of the required posts for the next phase of Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project will be approved as soon as possible with the approval of the Finance Department.
After the approval of the project, the Commissioner (Agriculture) or a full-time independent officer in the Indian Administrative Service will be appointed as the Project Director of the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project.
Section 5. Out of the 37 posts mentioned in section 2, 14 posts mentioned in A.No. 1 to 9 are being declared as cadre posts of government officers/ employees in various departments of the government and these posts should be appointed by the respective departments. In order to ensure regular availability of officers/ staff of the concerned Administrative Department in these posts, all the posts mentioned in the above table as Cadre posts should be temporarily included by the concerned Administrative Departments in their rolls as Cadre posts till the project period.
6. Paragraph No. The posts of Superintending Agriculture Officer in the Maharashtra Agricultural Service as Group-A cadre posts, and the posts of A. No. 7 in the Maharashtra Agricultural Service as Group-B cadre posts shall be included in the figure of the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the Department of Agriculture as cadre posts temporarily for the project period.
The posts of Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Agriculture Officer Group-B as cadre posts, and the posts of A. No. 9 Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Agriculture Officer Group-C as cadre posts shall be included in the figure of Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries under the Finance Department as cadre posts temporarily till the project period.
8. Similarly, the General Administration Department shall take action to fill up the post at No. 6 by deputation from the cadre of Ministerial Cell Officers.
The Project Director, Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation shall fill up the 23 posts mentioned in A. No. 10 to 20 from time to time as per the requirement till the project period on contractual basis through the Outer Source System.
10.Parliament: At present, a total of 37 posts have been approved to be appointed in the Project Management Cell for preparing the project plan and 14 of these posts are to be filled by appointment or deputation from the officers and employees of various departments. In case of non-availability of cadre officers/ staff from the concerned administrative department for these posts, the officers/ staff who have retired from the said post are allowed to be appointed as per the requirement on contractual basis as per the prevailing policy of the Government.

11.Further, the Project Director may, if deemed necessary, downgrade the posts of senior officers/ staff created for the project and appoint officers/ staff in the rank of junior in the pay scale in the concerned administrative division.
12.The above 37 posts sanctioned for the State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project are being created temporarily at the establishment of the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the Department of Agriculture for the project period and these posts will be automatically abolished after the project period is over.
13.Sadar The posts to be filled on contract basis by the Outer Source System under the project shall remain in existence only for the duration of the project. After the expiry of the project period, the services of the officers/ employees appointed on contract basis will also be terminated and they will not be included in government service under any circumstances. The Project Director should take due care while signing the contract with the concerned and ensure that the contractual employees do not create any liability on the government.
14.Since the decision of the Government is being issued in accordance with the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries for the creation of the post in a cyclical manner as well as the approval received under the Informal Reference No. 92/ APUK of the Department of Finance, dated 15/03/2019.
