Government Order: The Government has decided to provide a total of 805 posts for the Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project. Approved on 28.09.2017. Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project is being implemented by the Government under No. 2 here. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 17.01.2018. The project director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project, has requested to fill the sanctioned posts immediately as there is an urgent need for the posts as the implementation of the project has to be started immediately at the field level. In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the following officers of Group-B (Junior) cadre of Maharashtra Agricultural Service are being transferred to the post mentioned in their name for Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project with the approval of the competent authority.
