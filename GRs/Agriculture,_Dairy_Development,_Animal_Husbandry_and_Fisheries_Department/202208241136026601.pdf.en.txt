Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 and Rabi 2022-23 As on 1.07.2022, the scheme is being implemented through 5 insurers viz., Agriculture Insurance Company of India, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd., Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company and United India Insurance Company. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought State Government share subsidy for crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 from the above 5 companies. In the revised guidelines of the Central Government, Issue No. 13.1.6 states that" the insurance premium share of the Central and State Government is required to be paid to the company in advance (first installment) even if the data of the insured area is not available at the time of registration of the current season. The insurance premium subsidy amount should be 50 per cent of the total 80 per cent of the total state share insurance premium subsidy paid in the previous season in advance to the companies," it said.
Accordingly, the Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (4) In response to a request for Rs. 842,17,84, 541/- was under consideration of the Government for payment of crop insurance premium subsidy to insurance companies.

Government Resolution No: Propivio-2022/ P. No. 93/11 A
Government's decision:
The demand submitted by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India, the recommendation of the Commissionerate of Agriculture and the revised guidelines of the Central Government, point no. 13.1.6.Considering the above, the State share under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 is Rs. 842, 17,84,541/- is being approved by the Government for disbursement to Agriculture Insurance Company of India for payment to insurance companies. This amount is being released for Kharif 2022 and its utilization will not be allowed for other seasons earlier.
Expenditure on the said items should be met from the budgetary provision approved for the year 2022-23 under the following head: Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 110, Crop Insurance (00) (08) Financial assistance for insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana State share (2401 A 664) Non-plan, 33- Financial assistance.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for spending the funds disbursed under this government decision in accordance with the guidelines of the Prime Minister Crop Insurance Scheme and the government decision issued by the government from time to time.
For this purpose, Assistant Director (Accounts), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
