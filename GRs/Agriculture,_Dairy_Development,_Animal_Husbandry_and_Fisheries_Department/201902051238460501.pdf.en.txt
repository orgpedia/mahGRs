The Proposal:
With the objectives of promoting the food processing industry in the state, encouraging the establishment of projects based on modern technology through the participation of farmers to increase the value of agricultural products, encouraging the modernization of the project to increase the quality of the food produced, saving energy, a new 100% state-sponsored scheme, Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Yojana, has been approved in the state for the next five years from the financial year 2017-18. Similarly, the State Agriculture and Food Processing Policy, 2017 has been notified vide Government Resolution dated 31st October, 2017.
Accordingly, a provision of Rs.5000 lakh (Rs.50 crore only) has been budgeted for the implementation of Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Scheme for the year 2018-19 and administrative approval has been accorded for the implementation of the scheme for the year 2018-19 vide Government Resolution dated 20.06.2018. The first installment of Rs. 627.975 lakh under the scheme was under consideration of the Government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, the first installment of Rs. 627.975 lakh is being made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) through the Budget Distribution System for the implementation of the projects sanctioned under the Chief Minister's Agriculture and Food Processing Scheme for the year 2018-19.
