The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 20.06.2017, a new 100% State Sponsored Scheme of" Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Scheme" has been approved to be implemented in the State for the next five years from the financial year 2017-18. An amount of Rs.5000 lakh (Rs.50 crore only) has been budgeted for the implementation of the scheme in 2018-19 and administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution No.4 dated 20th June, 2018. The Director of Agriculture (Agricultural Processing and Planning), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune has requested to disburse the first installment of Rs. 325.975 lakh under this scheme through his letter No. 9.
