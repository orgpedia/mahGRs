The Proposal:
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries 1 decision of the government. 2.06.2020 has accorded administrative approval for implementation of 100% Centrally Sponsored National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) in the State from 2020-2019 for control of Infectious Foot& Mouth Disease (FOBD) and Brucellosis. Under this decision, a state-level control committee has been formed for effective control of this scheme in the state.
In order to facilitate the implementation of this scheme, the government is issuing the following decision in connection with the restructuring of the committee by partially amending the structure of the said committee.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to implement 100% centrally sponsored National Animal Disease Control Programme in the State. The structure of State Level Sanitary Committees constituted under 2.06.2020 is being revised as follows:
