Circular: Under the Government decision dated 21.10.2011 in the context of the General Administration Department, a comprehensive policy has been decided to prepare and publish the seniority list of the officers/ employees in the government service every year.
Security Group-B in Dairy Development Department (Pay Scale S15: Rs. 41800-132300). An interim seniority list showing the status of 1/1/2021 is being circulated through this circular. Also, if there are objections/ errors regarding this temporary seniority, the concerned should submit a statement within fifteen days. Any objections received after that will not be considered. The facts of the representations received should be examined from the records available to the respondents and these representations should be sent to the Government with detailed feedback. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the stipulated period, the final seniority will be published, assuming that no one has any objection to this provisional seniority list.
