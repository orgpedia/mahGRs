Government Decision: 1 here. In the Government Resolution dated 27.01.2022, p. 2 The following paragraphs shall be read after the second paragraph of the above Governing Decision.
The procurement of various goods and services, including setting up of mobile veterinary centres under the 100 per cent cent centrally sponsored National Animal Disease Control Programme and Animal Health and Disease Control Group Group Scheme in 2021-22, should be done through e-tendering process on the e-tendering portal of the Government of Maharashtra.
From 2022-23 onwards, procurement of various goods and services under this programme should be done on priority from the GeM portal of the Central Government. If the required response is not received on this system, then the e-tendering process should be implemented on the e-tendering portal of the Government of Maharashtra and the purchase of goods and services should be done.
