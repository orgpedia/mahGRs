Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 and Rabi 2022-23 As on 1.07.2022, the scheme is being implemented through 5 insurance companies viz., Agriculture Insurance Company of India, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd., Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company and United India Insurance Company. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India, through the Commissioner (Agriculture), has sought the release of the remaining State share of subsidy on crop insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022 in the combination of the above 5 companies. According to reference no. 4 of the Government Rs. 724.52 crore has been disbursed. The Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. (6) As per the request made vide letter No. 244,86,25, 869/- was under consideration of the Government to disburse the remaining State share of crop insurance premium to the insurance companies for payment of subsidy.
Government's decision:
Considering the demand made by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India and the recommendation of the Commissionerate of Agriculture accordingly, 5 insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd., Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company and United India Insurance Company have been given the benefit of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2022.
