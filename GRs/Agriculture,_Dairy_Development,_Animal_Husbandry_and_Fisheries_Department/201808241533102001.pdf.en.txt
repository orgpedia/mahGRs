The proposal: The Supreme Court in Writ Petition (Civil) No. In pursuance of the directions given in 82/2011, it has been decided to constitute a Civil Services Board to make recommendations on the postings and transfers of Group A, Group B and Group C officers/ employees in the Government service. This decision has been taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 31.1.2014. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a Civil Service Board to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group-B (non-gazetted) and Group-C officers/ employees under the Agriculture Department. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
General Administration Department No. As per the instructions given under the Government Resolution No. 1, the following Civil Services Board is being constituted at the Ministerial Administrative level to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C officers/ employees under the Department of Agriculture.

The functions of the above mentioned Civil Service Boards shall be as follows:
To make recommendations to the competent authority in accordance with the proposal for inter-departmental transfer of agricultural supervisors, agricultural assistants, assistant superintendents, senior clerks, junior clerks, trackers and similar Group-C employees of Group-B (non-gazetted) and Group-C cadres under the Department of Agriculture at the zonal level.
(ii) to make recommendations on the proposal for transfer of employees in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005.
