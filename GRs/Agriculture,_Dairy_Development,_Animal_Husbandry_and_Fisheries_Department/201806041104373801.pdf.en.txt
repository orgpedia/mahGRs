Introduction: In line with the Union Government's vision to double farmers' income by 2022, inputs like inputs, training, irrigation, mechanization, post-harvest technologies, agro processing and marketing etc. are critical to increase crop production. Considering the need for collective farming, modern methods of farming and strict planning for a substantial increase in the production of farmers due to the low land holding of farmers in the state, the implementation of the scheme' Promotion of Group Farming of Farmers for Promotion and Empowerment of Group Farming' has been approved under the decision of the Government No. 1.
