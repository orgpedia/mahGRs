INTRODUCTION: In respect of 27-small structures under the head Vegetable nurseries, Fruit orchards and Local Gardens (Compulsory/ Non-Plan) (2401 1722), Taluk Fruit Plantation, Kanheri, Th. Rs. The proposal for an amount of Rs. 478.99 lakh has been accorded technical approval by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Pune. The proposal has been submitted to the government by the Commissionerate of Agriculture (Director, Horticulture) for administrative approval as it is beyond the financial powers conferred on the department heads. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the budget of this proposal was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the head Vegetable Plantation, Fruit Plantation and Local Gardens (Compulsory/ Non-Plan) (2401 1722), 27- Small Constructions, Rs. 478.99 lakhs (Rupees Four Crores Seventy Eight Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand) for the proposed works as per Form-A vide Finance Department Government Resolution No. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposed budget in accordance with the financial powers conferred under Annexure No.3 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-I, Sub-section 5 dated 17th April, 2015.
The funds shall be met from the provision sanctioned for the year 2019-20 under the head 27- Small Constructions under the head Vegetable Roses, Fruit Plants and Local Gardens (Compulsory/ Non-Plan) (2401 1722).
