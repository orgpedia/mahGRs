Government Decision: In the year 2023-24, approval is being given to implement the Integrated Pest-Disease Survey and Advisory Plan (CropSAP) scheme for management of pests and diseases in major crops such as soybean, cotton, rice, tur, gram, maize, sorghum and sugarcane, as well as fruits mango, pomegranate, banana, mango, orange, chikku, cashew, ladyfinger and tomato. In the year 2023-24, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the programme with a corpus of Rs. 25.00 crore (Rupees Twenty Five Crore only).
2. Government Reference No. The Commissioner (Agriculture) shall issue detailed guidelines for the implementation of the Pest and Disease Survey and Advisory Scheme in the year 2023-24 in accordance with the instructions issued under Government Decisions 1, 2, 4 and 6.

The planning of farmhouses should be done by preparing a convergence plan in coordination with the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project (POCRA) and other Centrally Sponsored Schemes. For this purpose, priority should be given to spend the eligible items from the funds available under various Centrally Sponsored Schemes National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) and Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani (POCRA) projects and thereafter, if the funds are short, the expenditure should be met from the provisions available under the said State Schemes as per requirement.
