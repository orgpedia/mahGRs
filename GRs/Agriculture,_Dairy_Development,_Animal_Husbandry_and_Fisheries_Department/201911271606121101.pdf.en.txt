Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in the State in Kharif 2019. The scheme is being implemented through two insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India and Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company as per the Government decision dated 22.05.2019. The Agriculture Commissioner's Office, vide Reference Letter No. 7, has released the second instalment of Rs. 532,72,92, 126/- is sought to be disbursed. Accordingly, the government was considering to disburse this amount as crop insurance premium subsidy.
Government's decision:
As recommended by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2019, the State share for the second installment of crop insurance premium subsidy to insurance companies is Rs. 532,72,92, 126/- is being approved by the Government.
