The proposal-
With a view to augmenting the income of small fishers and regeneration of fisheries, the Government has launched a new sub-component" Promotion of Sustainable Fisheries and Livelihoods through Artificial Reefs and/ or Sea Ranching" programme under PMMSY. The letter was written by Secretary (Fisheries), Government of India, New Delhi. Under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana for the State of Maharashtra, a target of installing 182 artificial reefs in the non-beneficiary component of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme has been proposed on 10/10/2022. 182 artificial reef projects along the coast of Maharashtra at a cost of Rs. 31.00 lakhs as against Rs. The Central Government has accorded administrative approval to the proposal of Rs. 5642.00 lakhs vide letter No. The central government also. vide letter dated 29.03.2023 for Rs. 845.00 lakhs has been released to the State Government. Therefore, a technical committee under the chairmanship of the Commissioner Fisheries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, was being considered by the government for successful and proper implementation of the project in the coastal areas of the state.
Government Decision: In order to successfully and properly implement the artificial reef in the coastal areas of the state, a technical committee is being formed under the chairmanship of Commissioner Fisheries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai as follows:
