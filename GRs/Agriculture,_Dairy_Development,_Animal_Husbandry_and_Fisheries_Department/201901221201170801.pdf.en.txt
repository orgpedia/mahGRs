Introduction: In order to increase the production and productivity of various crops in the agricultural sector, it is necessary to make necessary efforts to provide certified/ truthful seeds of high quality and high yielding varieties to the farmers at the local level. For this, there is an urgent need to provide the necessary facilities to the farmers at the village level to sow various crops. As per the recommendations of the Working Group of Secretaries on Seed Sector Reforms at the Central Government level, the Central Government vide its letter dated 13th October, 2017 has notified the setting up of 500 MT capacity Seed Processing Centres and Seed Storage Godowns at Gram Panchayat level under the Seed and Plantation Material Sub-Mission (SMSP) through Farmer Producer Companies (FPOs)/ Self Help Groups (SHGs)/ Foodgrains Producers Associations (CIGs)/ Cooperative Societies/ Agencies approved by State Governments and engaged in activities related to agriculture sector. The scheme is 100% Centrally Sponsored and targets 50 units for the State of Maharashtra.
