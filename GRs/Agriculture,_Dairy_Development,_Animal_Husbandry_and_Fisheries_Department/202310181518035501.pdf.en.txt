Preamble: With the twin objectives of providing remunerative prices to the agricultural produce of the State and enabling the consumers to purchase agricultural produce at reasonable prices. Under the Government decision dated 23/04/2015, the State Agricultural Price Commission has been constituted by converting the State Agricultural Price Committee into State Agricultural Price Commission. The Chairmanship of the State Agricultural Price Commission is mentioned in the above Reference No. The matter of appointing an expert person in the field of agriculture and a person with knowledge of agricultural prices/ agricultural commodity prices as per the criteria laid down in the Government Decision No. 1 was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision: Shri Syed Pasha Osmansahab Patel, Phoenix Foundation, Marwari Vyapari Sankul, Gandhi Chowk, Latur is being appointed as an expert in the field of agriculture and knowledgeable person on agricultural prices/ farm prices in the post of honorary chairman in the structure of the State Agricultural Value Commission constituted under the order dated 23rd April, 2015. The appointment is honorary and will be effective till further orders.
