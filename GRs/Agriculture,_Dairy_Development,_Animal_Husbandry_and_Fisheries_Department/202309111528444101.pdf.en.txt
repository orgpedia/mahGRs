2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 12.04.2023 should be strictly implemented.
The funds released by the Agricultural Universities in respect of sub-titles/ groups/ schemes and subject wise should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the respective scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as non-scheme schemes. In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancies or other reasons, the subsidy shall be adjusted in the next month's disbursement.
(iii) If any of the sub-account, group, scheme and subject-wise disbursed funds are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
The grant-in-aid shall be payable only for the salaries and allowances of the posts sanctioned by the Government which have been filled up as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990 and the Government has approved the continuation of such posts at non-plan expenditure.
Each sub-heading/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts should be maintained.
The assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the Agricultural Universities are correct as per the formula and how the expenditure is being incurred as per the prescribed mandate/ procedure shall be submitted to the Government within a period of three months from the end of the financial year concerned. Also, the amount of unutilized funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
The Government while approving some of the new schemes of the University, has approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme in an adjusted manner. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by way of internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991 and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is distributed.
While making the demand for grants for the year 2023-24, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed, actual expenditure and balance grants as per the scheme by 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and demand the same amount of subsidy as is actually required.
10) Don't spend money on posts and things that are out of order.
The above terms and conditions shall be strictly complied with by the Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities.
