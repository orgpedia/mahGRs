Preamble: It has been decided to implement the Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Agriculture through Public Financial Management System (PFMS). 1 has been adopted.
Reference No. 2. According to the guidelines of Reference No. 2. Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries (PMMFPI) Scheme, approval has been accorded for implementation in the State. The Government was considering the appointment of Accountant Accounts-1 as" Maker" and Assistant Director, Accounts-1 as" Checker" for conducting financial transactions in the current bank account opened in the name of Agriculture Commissionerate through Public Financial Management System. At the district level, the government was considering to approve the opening of a separate current bank account in the name of the District Superintending Agriculture Officer and to appoint the Taluk Agriculture Officer as the" Maker" and the Accountant/ Superintendent in the office of the District Superintending Agriculture Officer as the" Primary Checker" and the District Superintending Agriculture Officer as the" Final Checker" to conduct the financial transactions in the said bank account through the Public Financial Management System. Following this, the government took decision:
Government Decision: Approval is being accorded for appointment of Accountant Accountant Accountant-1 as" Maker" and Assistant Director Accountant-1 as" Checker" in the Commissionerate of Agriculture for making financial transactions through Public Financial Management System in the current bank accounts opened at the level of Agriculture Commissionerate for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries Yojana.
To implement this scheme through the Public Financial Management System at the district level, it is being approved to open a separate current bank account in the State Bank of India in the name of the District Superintending Agriculture Officer. The Taluk Agriculture Officer will be the' maker' and the District Superintendent of Agriculture Officer will be in the office to conduct financial transactions in the bank account through the Public Financial Management System.
