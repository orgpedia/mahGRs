Introduction: Refer No. As per Section 58 (2) (i) of the Act, the Chairman of the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research (MACER) shall be the Chairman of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Service Admission Board. Minister (Agriculture) has been provided. Accordingly, the matter of appointing the chairman of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Service Admission Board was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Mr. Prakash Anandrao Abitkar, Member of the Legislative Assembly, referred to vide No. He has been appointed as the Vice-Chairman of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research.
As per the provisions of Section 58 (2) (i) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983 (Second Amendment Act, 2013), the Government is pleased to approve the appointment of Shri Prakash Anandrao Abitkar, Vice-Chairman, Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune as Chairman, Maharashtra Agricultural University Service Admission Board.
The appointment of Mr. Prakash Anandrao Abitkar will be for a period of three years or until the consent of the State Government, whichever is earlier.
Necessary action in this regard will be taken by the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune.
