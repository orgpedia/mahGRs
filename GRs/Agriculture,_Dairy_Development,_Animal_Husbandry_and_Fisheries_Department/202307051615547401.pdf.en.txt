Under Food and Nutrition Security, priority should be given to small and marginal farmers or groups of such farmers while selecting beneficiary farmers under various components for food crops. Also, applications of farmers should be invited and beneficiaries should be selected in a transparent manner.
Under this campaign, for the purchase of various inputs (fertilizers, medicines, seeds, irrigation tools, etc.), the subsidy should be directly transferred to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries as per the prevailing policy of the government.
The beneficiary will have the freedom to buy these inputs from the open market. However, if the tenders produced by the Government agencies/ PSUs of the Government are being supplied under this campaign, the relevant Government decision will be taken. Action to be taken as on 19/04/2017.
