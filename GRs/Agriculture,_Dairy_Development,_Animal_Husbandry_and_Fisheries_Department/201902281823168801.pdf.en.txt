Agriculture and Padum Government Resolution No.Jaishemi 1018/ PR No. 125/17 dated 16.10.2018.
Introduction: Under the State Award Scheme of Organic Farming/ Poison Free Farming, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission has been approved under the Government Decision dated 16/10/2018. The headquarters of the mission will be in Akola. There is a provision to appoint an expert on organic farming as the chairman for this mission. Accordingly, the government was considering the appointment of Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh as the chairman of the Organic Farming Mission.
Prakash Pohare, Akola has been appointed as the Chairman of Punjabrao Deshmukh Organic Farming Mission till further orders.
