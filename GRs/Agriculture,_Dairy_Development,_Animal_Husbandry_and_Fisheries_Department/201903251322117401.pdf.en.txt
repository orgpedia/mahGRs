Terms and Conditions 1) While spending this fund, the conditions set out in the Government Resolution No. 4, 7, 8, 9 and 10 should be fulfilled and the funds should be spent only for the schemes and approved matters for which the funds have been allocated.
The grants sanctioned to all the four agricultural universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing government orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
(3) The Deputy Divisional Agriculture Officer, Ahmednagar, the Deputy Divisional Agriculture Officer, Dapoli, the Deputy Divisional Agriculture Officer, Akola and the Deputy Divisional Agriculture Officer, Parbhani shall file the return in column No. 1 of the return in paragraph 1 above. Necessary action should be taken regarding the payment of the grants distributed in the 9th to the Controller of the concerned Agricultural University as mentioned in the Government Decision No. AgriU No. 1072/18471/ V, dated 27 September 1972 and Government Decision No. AgriU No. 2003/ CR- 35/20 O, dated 25/4/2003.
4) The expenditure for this purpose should be shared within the limits approved under that head in the Budget 2018-19 and should be spent under that head.
These orders are being passed in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments under paragraphs 142 and 162 (a) of the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book (Part-I), 1977, and in accordance with the interpretation accepted by the Finance Department in the revised estimates.
