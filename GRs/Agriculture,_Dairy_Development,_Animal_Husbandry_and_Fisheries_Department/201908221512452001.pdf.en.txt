Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture, the Forestry Sub-Mission is being implemented in the State since 2017-18. The ratio of central and state share is 60: 40. For the year 2018-19, the Central Government has released Rs. Rs 200 crore was allocated for this. Out of this amount, Rs. 35.94 lakh remained unspent at the end of the financial year. The Central Government has released the funds for the year 2019-20. It was re-released on April 29, 2019.
The central share of general, scheduled caste and scheduled tribe category in the unspent fund is Rs. 12.31 lakh, Rs. 13.39 lakh and Rs. 10.24 lakh. The fund of Rs. 12.31 lakh and Rs. 8.54 lakh. A proposal was under the consideration of the government to refinance Rs. 20.85 lakh in the current year and make the fund available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on the Financial Management System (BDS) system.
Government's decision
1. Non-recurring central share of general category for the year 2018-19 of Forestry Sub-Mission Rs. 12.31 Lakhs (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Thirty One Thousand only) and State share of Rs. 8.54 lakh (Rs. 20.85 lakh is being disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Financial Management System (BDS) system by revising the expenditure during the current year.
