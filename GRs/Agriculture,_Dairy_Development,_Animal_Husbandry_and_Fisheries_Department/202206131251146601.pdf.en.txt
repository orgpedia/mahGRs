Preamble: As per the report submitted by the Commissioner (Agriculture) under reference, the proposed Taluka Seed Multiplication Centre at Narkhed, Ta. There is scope for establishing nursery as irrigation facility is available in the area of Narkhed, G. Nagpur. If the nursery is established, the requirement of saplings under the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme and the Maharashtra Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme will be met. If a nursery is established, no separate posts will have to be created for this nursery. It will not affect any sectoral component and will not be a financial burden. If nurseries are established on these areas, the area of orchard cultivation will be greatly increased by making available grafts/ saplings to the farmers of the area. This will help raise farmers" income. The area will benefit financially from the sale of saplings/ plants. As a result, local farmers in the surrounding areas will get a large number of employment opportunities, considering this, the matter of giving approval to set up a fruit orchard was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: Tabike-2020/ P.No. 149/13-O,
The Government is giving approval to set up a Fruit Plant on the area of the above Taluka Seed Multiplication Centre subject to the following conditions:
1) The work of the new orchard will be done by the officers/ staff present at the Taluk Seed Multiplication Center. No new posts of officers/ employees can be created for this fruit orchard.
(2) No fresh financial provision shall be required for the establishment of new orchards.
3) Ensure that there are no new charges.
4) Ensure that no area is affected.
