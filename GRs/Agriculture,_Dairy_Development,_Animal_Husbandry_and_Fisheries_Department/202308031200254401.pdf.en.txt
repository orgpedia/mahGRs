Preamble: In memory of Padmashri Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil, the Government has taken a decision. Shedija 2012/ Q.No. 150/3 J, Dy. As on 16.8.2014. The government has decided to observe August 29 as' Farmers Day'. Since then, this day is celebrated as Farmers' Day. Padma Shri Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil will be remembered for his work for farmers and all the farmers of the state will be honored. The government was considering to celebrate his birthday as Farmers' Day on August 30, 2023.
The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
In the year 2023. The government has decided to celebrate August 30, 2023 as Farmers' Day in the state.
No additional funding will be provided for this program. The minimum expenditure for this programme should be met from the provisions under the Commissioner (Agriculture).
3. The Commissionerate of Agriculture should issue guidelines regarding the celebration of Farmers' Day.
Conducting webinar/ guidance classes. Farmers' Day will be celebrated in the state on August 30, 2023.
