As per the guidelines of the Central Government, the Government has decided to extend the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana for a period of three years i.e. 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23. 29.06.2020 and dt. 17.07.2020. As per the decision of the government, the last date for participation for Kharif season-2021 has been given. It was fixed as 15.07.2021. However, in view of the restrictions in various areas in the wake of Corona in the state, the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana with the aim of providing adequate time to the farmers has been extended to enable them to participate in the Kharif season 2021. from 15.07.2021. 23.07.201 is being extended.
