The Proposal:
27 Small Constructions under the head Vegetable Plantations, Fruit Trees and Local Gardens (Compulsory/ Non-Plan) (2401 1722). 2,60,10, 000/- has been technically approved by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune in their work budget. The proposal has been submitted by the Commissionerate of Agriculture (Director, Horticulture) for administrative approval as it is beyond the financial powers conferred on the head of the department. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the budget of this proposal was under consideration of the government.
