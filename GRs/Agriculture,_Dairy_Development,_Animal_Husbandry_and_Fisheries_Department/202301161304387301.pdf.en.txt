Paddy Cultivation& Quality Improvement Project will be implemented in 9 districts of the state under the National Agriculture Development Scheme in 2022-23. The districts of Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Kolhapur, Sangli, Ahmednagar and Nandurbar are the major rice producing districts in the state. There is also scope for increasing the production of rice in these districts.
Accordingly, in the meeting of State Level Project Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) under National Agriculture Development Scheme held on 23 rd November, 2022, a sum of Rs. 357.22 lakh has been approved. 4 is here. Issued by letter dated January 6, 2023.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the government to approve the paddy cultivation and quality improvement project under the National Agriculture Development Scheme in 2022-23.
