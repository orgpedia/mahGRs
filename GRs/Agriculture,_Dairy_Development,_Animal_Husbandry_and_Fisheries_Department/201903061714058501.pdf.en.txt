Similarly, in the proviso to sub-section (2) of section 73AA, It was revised on 14/12/2018. According to the amendment, a government officer not below the rank of Assistant Registrar, Cooperative Societies, can be appointed as a Director in the Board of Directors of a cooperative society which is not a cooperative society and a private person who has experience in the functioning of the cooperative society of the type of cooperative society, can be appointed as a government-appointed Director.
