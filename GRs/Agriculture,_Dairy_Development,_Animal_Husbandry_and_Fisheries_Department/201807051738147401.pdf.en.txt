Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was implemented in the state in Kharif 2017 by 5 insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India, National Insurance Company, United India General Insurance Company, Reliance General Insurance Company and Oriental General Insurance Company. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. District No. National Insurance Company was appointed for 1 (Latur, Akola, Hingoli, Sangli, Thane, Palghar). The National Insurance Company has released the State share of crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2017 amounting to Rs. 48,27,25, 644/-. vide letter dated 22.06.2018 and accordingly, Rs. 48,27,25, 644/-to National Insurance Company through Agriculture Insurance Company of India. 48,27,25, 644/- has been requested by the Commissionerate of Agriculture in Reference No. 6 has been made. Accordingly, considering the demand submitted by the National Insurance Company and the recommendation of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, the State Government's share of crop insurance premium subsidy under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2017 has been increased to Rs. 48,27,25, 644/- was under consideration of the Government.
