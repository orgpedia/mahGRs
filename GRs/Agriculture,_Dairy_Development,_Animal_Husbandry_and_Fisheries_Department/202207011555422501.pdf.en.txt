The Proposal:
The State of Maharashtra's Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) project has been approved to be implemented in all districts of the state over a period of 7 years (from 2019-20 to 2026-27).
For the year 2022-23, Rs. Rs 100 crore has been allocated for the project.
Department of Finance As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 4 th June, 2022, the Department has been empowered to disburse funds@ 60% of the budgetary allocation. However, instructions have been received from the Department of Finance that the said funds will be made available to the Department at the rate of 7% per month on the Budget Distribution System. Accordingly, within the limit of 60% of the budgetary provision and 7% per month, the Project Director, pursuant to the request made under Reference No. 6, has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 2100 crore and Rs. 900 crore, a total of Rs. The matter of disbursement of 3000 lakh rupees was under consideration of the government, following which the government is taking the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. Project Director for 2022-23, Sh. Balasaheb Thackeray Agriculture and Rural Transformation Project to implement various aspects under the project. 2100 Lakhs (Rs. 21 crore) and Rs. 900 crore (Rs. Nine crore only) is being disbursed and the case-wise details are as follows.
