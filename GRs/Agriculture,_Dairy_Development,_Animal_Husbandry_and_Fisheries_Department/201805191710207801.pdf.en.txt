Introduction: In the interest of the farmers, the farmers' magazine published since 1965 provides information to the farmers of the state and outside the state about the interesting and latest developments in the field of agriculture. The Commissioner (Agriculture) has proposed to approve the handing over of printing of Farmers Monthly Issue to Anand Publications, Jalgaon as the rates quoted by Anand Publications, Jalgaon in the tender process conducted by the Commissioner (Agriculture) for printing of Farmers Magazine for the year 2018-19 are minimal. The letter dated 25.03.2018 has been submitted to the Government. In accordance with the proposal of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, a proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the tender process of the farmers' magazine, to provide the allowable amounts to the eligible tenderers according to the tender process and to approve the expenses incurred for the delivery of the issue post.
Decision of the Government: Commissioner (Agriculture) Sh. After considering the proposals received in the letter dated 25.03.2018, the Government has now decided as follows:
For the period of one year from the date of issue of the said order regarding printing of Farmers Magazine, i.e. for the period April, 2018 to March, 2019, the Government is approving the tenders for printing of Farmers Magazine, editing/ simplification of articles, arrangement of articles, decoration of magazine, four-color printing of magazine, along with the work of assigning the work of printing of Farmers Magazine to Anand Publications, Jalgaon, the lowest tenderer in the e-tendering process conducted by the Commissioner (Agriculture) at his level, as shown in the table below.
