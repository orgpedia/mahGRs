The Proposal:
Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri has formulated detailed guidelines/ norms for starting Post Graduate Degree Courses in Agriculture and affiliated colleges functioning on permanent non-aided basis. The said guidelines/ norms have been approved in the meeting of the Academic Council of the Agricultural University held on 28.02.2022. An expert committee was constituted under the chairmanship of Dr. C. D. Mayee, former Vice-Chancellor, Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani to examine the feasibility and feasibility of starting post-graduate courses in agriculture and allied colleges. The committee has proposed the necessary guidelines and criteria for the postgraduate course. The guidelines and criteria were approved by the Vidwat Parishad, Executive Council of the Agriculture University and the proposal was recommended to the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune for approval. Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune In the 107th meeting held on 16.06.2022, Resolution No. 03/107/2022 has been passed and the aforesaid Reference No. Under Section 2, the government was considering to approve the guidelines and criteria proposed by the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune, to start postgraduate courses on a permanent basis on a non-funded basis for private institutions.
