The awards are given to Punjabrao Deshmukh Krishi Ratna, Vasantrao Naik Krishi Bhushan, Jijamata Krishi Bhushan, Krishi Bhushan (Organic Farming), Vasantrao Naik Krishi Mitra, Vasantrao Naik Agrarian Farmer, Udyan Pandit, Padma Shri Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil, Krishi Sevaratna Award and State Level Crop Competition. Accordingly, the matter of honouring the farmers/ institutions by conferring various agricultural awards for the year 2015 and 2016 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The names of the awardees for the agricultural awards for the year 2015 and 2016 will be decided by the Hon'ble Minister. In the meeting held under the chairmanship of the Minister (Agriculture), the names of the following persons/ organizations have been finalized for the awards for the year 2015 and 2016 and the government is giving its approval to confer the awards.
