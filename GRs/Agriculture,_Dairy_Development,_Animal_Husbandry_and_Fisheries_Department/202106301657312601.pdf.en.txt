The memo:
In reference to the above No. Proposals for grant of administrative approval for construction of Krishi Bhawan buildings at Malegaon, Nashik and Jalna have been received vide letters No. 1 and 2.
In Nashik there are offices of sub-divisional agriculture officer, taluk agriculture officer, 4 divisional agriculture officer and agricultural clinic and orchard, Neelgavan etc. Some of these offices are currently in rented premises. Since the government spends a lot of money on rent and the farmers can connect with various offices at one place, it is proposed to build a Krishi Bhavan building in Malegaon G.
The offices of the District Agriculture Officer, the Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, the District Seed Certification Officer, the District Seed Certification Officer, the District Soil Surveyor and the Soil Testing Officer are also located in Jalna. Some of these offices are currently functioning in the government building of the Revenue Department and some offices are functioning in rented premises. Since the government spends a lot of money on rent, it is proposed to build a Krishi Bhawan building in Jalna and bring all the offices in one place so that the farmers can connect with different offices at one place.
