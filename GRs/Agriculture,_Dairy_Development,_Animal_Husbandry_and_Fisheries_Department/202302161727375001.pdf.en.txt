2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly implemented.
(2) The funds released by the Agricultural Universities under the head/ group/ scheme and subject-wise should be spent only for the salary and allowances and permissible matters of the posts/ matters in the respective scheme which have been approved by the Government to be continued as non-scheme schemes. In case of non-availability of subsidy under a scheme due to vacancies or other reasons, the subsidy shall be adjusted in the next month's disbursement.
(3) If for any reason, the funds distributed sub-heading wise, group wise, scheme wise and subject wise remain unutilized/ remaining/ remaining, the said unutilized/ balance funds
No sharing or sharing of expenditure for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
The posts sanctioned by the Government are filled as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1990 and the posts are filled at non-plan expenditure.

The subsidy will be paid only for the salaries and allowances of such posts which have been approved by the government for continuation.
(5) Each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained.
(6) The assessment report of each financial year shall be submitted to the Government within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year as to whether the grant-in-aid sanctioned to the Agricultural Universities is correct as per the formula or how it is being spent as per the prescribed order/ procedure. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
7) The Government while approving some of the new schemes of the University, have approved to make available/ fill up the posts under the said scheme in an adjustable manner. As a result of creation of new posts for the said new scheme, the funds released under this Order shall not be spent for payment of salary and allowances for the posts in the original schemes where the posts in the original schemes have been transferred/ made available to the new scheme by internal adjustments. Also, while seeking quarterly grant, the funds required for salary and allowances of such posts should not be demanded.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
9) While making the demand for grants for the year 2022-2023, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
10) Don't spend money on posts and things that are out of order.
The Controller and other relevant competent authorities of the University shall strictly comply with the above terms and conditions.
