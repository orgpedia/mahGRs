Administrative Sanction has been accorded for the programme for an amount of Rs. 11382.68 lakhs.
The Government of India has approved the scheme for 2018-19. As per the letter dated 1.2.2019, for the second installment of Rs. 922.53 lakh has been disbursed. Accordingly, Rs. 615.02 lakhs, as on date, a total of Rs. 1537.55 lakh is likely to be released in the second instalment for the general category in the current year. Accordingly, under this Government decision, the matter of disbursement of general category funds for the implementation of the scheme was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
In 2018-19, under the Centrally Sponsored Sub-Mission on Agriculture Extension of National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET), the Central share (60%) for the current year for the implementation of the Scheme of Assistance for Reforms in Agricultural Extension Programmes of the State (ATMA) was Rs. 922.53 lakh (Rupees Nine Crore Twenty Two Lakh Fifty Three Thousand only) is being disbursed on Budget Distribution System. 40% of the State share of Rs. 615.02 lakh should be made available from the District Planning and Development Committee (DPDC) fund. Thus, the total central and state share (from DPDC) of this scheme is Rs. 1537.55 lakh (Rs.15 crore, 37.55 lakh only) for general category is being approved for disbursement in the second instalment this year.
