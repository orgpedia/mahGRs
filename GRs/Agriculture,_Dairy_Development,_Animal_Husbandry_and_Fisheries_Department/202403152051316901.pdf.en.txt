Introduction: - In the state, the Krishi Unnati Yojana - Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (MIDH) has been launched as per the Government decision in Reference No. 1. The said scheme has been granted administrative approval for the programme with a funding of Rs. 21207.25 lakhs under the Government Decision at Reference No. 3 for implementation in the State in the year 2023-2024. The first and second instalment of funds of SC category for this financial year 2023-2024 have been disbursed under the Government Decision at Reference No. 4 & 5. Now the State has received the third instalment of funds of SC category from the Central Government for the financial year 2023-2024 as per the letter in Reference No.6. Against this backdrop, the remaining budgetary allocation of SC category (Central share and State share equivalent thereto) has been released by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide Reference No. 9 has been transferred to the Department of Agriculture by the government circular here. Also, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has not made adequate budgetary provisions for disbursement of the third instalment of funds (Central share and State share equivalent) to the SC category under the scheme vide Reference No. 10 The necessary funds have been made available to the Department of Agriculture through re-allocation as per the government decision here. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse the funds to the Commissioner (Agriculture) and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune. The government is taking the following decisions:
