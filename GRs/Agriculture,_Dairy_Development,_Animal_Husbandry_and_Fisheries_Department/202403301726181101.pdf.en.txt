2. The grant in this judgment is being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions.
1) Department of Finance. 12.04.2023, d. 05.02.2024 and d. The instructions given in the 04.03.2024 circular should be strictly implemented.
2) The funds disbursed by the agricultural universities on a plan and case-wise basis should be spent only for the permissible aspects of the scheme concerned.
3) If the funds disbursed are / remain unorganized for any reason, the unorganized / balance should not be shared or spent for other groups / schemes / matters without the approval of the Government.
4) Schemes under which the scheme has expired should not be continued at the cost under the scheme unless the approval of the Government is obtained after the relevant period.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report for each financial year as to whether the grant-in-aid granted to the agricultural universities is correct as per the grant formula or how it is being spent as per the prescribed orders / procedures. Also, immediately report to the government the amount of the last financial year's unfunded funds.
6) Agricultural universities have been approved by the government to use their revenue income. The university should take appropriate action in this regard as per the rules and procedures. Also, action should be taken to implement plans to increase revenue.
7) The grants sanctioned to the universities should be spent within the prescribed limits as provided in the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act 1983, Maharashtra Agricultural University Statute 1990, and Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code 1991 and after administrative and financial approval of the competent authorities in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. Don't spend just because there is a budgetary provision or because a grant has been disbursed.
The above terms and conditions shall be strictly complied with by the Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities.
