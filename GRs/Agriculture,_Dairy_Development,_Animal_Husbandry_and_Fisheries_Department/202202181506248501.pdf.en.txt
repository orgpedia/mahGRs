The Proposal:
In order to increase the production and productivity of various crops in the agricultural sector, it is necessary to make necessary efforts to provide certified/ truthful seeds of high quality and high yielding varieties to the farmers at the local level. For this, there is an urgent need to provide the necessary facilities to the farmers at the village level to sow various crops. As per the recommendations of the Working Group of Secretaries on Seed Sector Reforms at the Central Government level, vide letter dated 13th October, 2017, the Central Government has set up 500 MT capacity Seed Processing Centres and Seeds at Gram Panchayat level through Farmer Producer Companies (FPOs)/ Self Help Groups (SHGs)/ Foodgrain Producers Associations (CIGs)/ Cooperative Societies/ Agri-enterprises approved by the State Governments and working in the field of agriculture under the Seed and Plantation Material Sub-Mission (SMSP)
