The proposal-
The scheme is being implemented under the National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology (NMAET). Under this sub-mission, Assistance for Reforms in State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) is implemented in the ratio of 60:40 funding from the Central and State Governments. The scheme will be implemented from 2023-24.
