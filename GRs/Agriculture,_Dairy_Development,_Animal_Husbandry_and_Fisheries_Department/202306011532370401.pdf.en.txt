The posting has been given by promotion as per the order dated 18.01.2022.
2. Shri Vijay Harishchandra Rajurkar, Agriculture Officer, while promoting his mother to Group-B (Junior) cadre of Maharashtra Agricultural Service on the ground of her mental illness, had requested for posting in Amravati Division in lieu of the posting given in Pune Revenue Division. Considering the documents submitted by Mr. Rajurkar regarding change of Revenue Department and the fact that he has served in Pune Revenue Department for more than one year, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to transfer him. Q. Government notification. As per the provisions of Rule 12 (1) (2) of the Government Order dated 14.07.2021, the Government is approving the transfer of Revenue Division" Amravati" in place of" Pune" to Shri Vijay Harishchandra Rajurkar.
3. Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and delays in discharge of official duties As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Prevention of Corruption Act, 2005, Mr. Rajurkar is required to be transferred with the approval of the competent authority. The government is approving the posting as indicated in 5.
