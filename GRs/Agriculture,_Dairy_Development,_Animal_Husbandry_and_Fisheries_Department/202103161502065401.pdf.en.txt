Preamble: The State e-Governance Programme is implemented to computerize the offices of the Department of Agriculture and Padum (itself), Ministries and the Commissionerate of Agriculture and its subordinate offices. The Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (DADF) and the Commissionerate of Agriculture carry out various activities under the e-governance programme. Under this scheme, a fixed amount is sanctioned by the state government every year and the sanctioned amount is distributed to the Department of Agriculture (itself) as well as the Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune.
