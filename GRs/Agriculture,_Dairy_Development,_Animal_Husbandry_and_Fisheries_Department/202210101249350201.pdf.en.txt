As per the revised modalities of PFMS system vide notification dated 23rd March, 2021 and in pursuance of the Government decision dated 02.08.2021, the Foodgrain crops scheme under National Food Security Mission (NFSA) has been marked as SNA for fund distribution and utilization management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
Under the National Food Security Mission, the total amount of 508.532 lakh rupees made available for food crops under the decision of the government should be distributed district-wise by the Director (Extension and Training) through the PFMS system.
6. The funds made available under this Government Decision should be utilised immediately.
Also, the Director (Extension and Training) should submit the utilization certificate to the government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Principal Secretary (Agriculture).
This Government Resolution is being issued in compliance with the conditions laid down in the Government Circular No. 2022/ No. 43/ 2022, dated 04/04/2022 of the Department of Finance and in accordance with the powers conferred on this Department under the said Circular.
8. The said Government Decision is being issued with the concurrence of the Tribal Development Department.
