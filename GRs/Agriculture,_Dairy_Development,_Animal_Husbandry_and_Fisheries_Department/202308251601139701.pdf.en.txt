The general guidelines:
5.1 Technical approval of the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune will be required in respect of innovative schemes implemented under the State Level, Centrally Sponsored Schemes, Other Departmental and Other Funds received from other sources and other exceptional/ special cases/ District Annual Plans.
5.2 If the post of Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry/ Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry is vacant and the additional charge of the post is entrusted to an officer holding a junior post, in such a case, the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune shall provide technical approval.
5.3. The concerned Technical Sanctioning Authority shall be solely responsible for examining and giving technical sanction to the proposals as per the scheme norms.
5.4. The directions issued by the Government from time to time should be followed while granting technical approval.
5.5. While submitting the proposals for procurement under various schemes for technical approval, the Government of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour shall take a decision. The terms and conditions set forth under 01.12.2016 shall remain in force.
