The Proposal:
Considering the necessary aspects to increase the income of the Scheduled Castes and New Buddhist farmers of the state and to improve their living standards, as well as to make the Scheduled Castes and New Buddhist farmers self-sufficient, the reference was made. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana is being implemented in the State by revising the Special Component Plan as per the decision of the Government on 5th January, 2017. Under this scheme, Rs. Subsidy is given to SC farmers with annual income up to Rs. 1.50 lakh for" new well, old well repair, inwell boreings, power connection size, pump sets, plastic lining of farm ponds, micro irrigation sets."
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has sanctioned Rs. 23659.64 lakh has been made available at the district level. The matter of granting administrative approval to the program under the scheme within the limit of this fund for the current year was under the consideration of the government, in which the government has taken the following decision:
Under Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavlamban Yojana for Scheduled Castes and Newly Born Farmers in 2018-19, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for implementation of the programme within the fund limit of Rs. 23659.64 Lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Thirty Six Crores Sixty Nine Lakh Sixty Four Thousand only).

7. It should be widely publicized at the district level that 90% subsidy is available for micro-irrigation under the scheme. For this, the District Agriculture Development Officer should publish an advertisement in the major newspapers of the district with detailed details of the subsidy to be made available to the farmers under this scheme and the subsidy to be received for it and inform the interested farmers to apply for the available items under the scheme.
The funds to be made available to the districts under this scheme in the current year should be distributed taluka-wise in an equitable manner. For this, according to the agricultural census, the economic target of each taluk should be determined in proportion to the number of farmers of the said category in the respective taluk and within the limit of this target, eligible beneficiaries should be selected from the interested farmers of the respective taluk.
Under the scheme, the facility of applying is being provided on the Maha-Online website. At least 1 month's notice is required to apply.
10.If more applications are received than the financial target of the concerned Taluk, the District Selection Committee shall select the beneficiaries in a staggered manner. This process should be carried out at the district level in the presence of applicants and public representatives. In this way, the selection list of beneficiaries in each taluk through draw of lots as well as the waiting list should be published in order. Copies of the list of taluka-wise lists should be put up on the notice board of the panchayat samiti and the zilla parishad and also published on the website of the zilla parishad.
11.The beneficiaries selected for the new well and old well repair through the scheme shall be informed in writing within 7 days of their selection. Thereafter, on-site inspection of the area of the beneficiaries should be done and after the technical feasibility study, the location of the new well to be drilled by the beneficiaries as well as the proposals for the repair of the old well by the beneficiaries should be technically examined.

If found suitable, the process of approving the budget of the said items and giving prior approval to the proposals of the concerned beneficiaries should be done within 45 days from the date of draw of lots.
Similarly, the selection of beneficiaries whose proposals are found to be technically unsuitable in the site inspection should be cancelled and the next eligible beneficiaries in the waiting list should be selected.
Under the scheme, for other than new well and old well repair, the selected beneficiaries should be given prior consent within 7 days after release and the benefit of the scheme should be given to them in the current financial year.
The prior consent of the beneficiaries who do not implement the matters under the scheme within the prescribed period should be cancelled and thereafter the next eligible beneficiaries should be selected as per the rank in the waiting list. Thus, the scheme should be implemented by selecting the beneficiaries under the scheme in a transparent manner.
A maximum period of 2 years will be admissible to the beneficiaries who jointly implement all other aspects including the digging of a new well under the scheme.
15. The beneficiaries selected for other purposes other than drilling of new well will be required to complete the implementation of the relevant activities in the current financial year itself.
The beneficiaries should be given prior consent within the limit of the financial target to be achieved for the district/ taluk under the scheme.
17. To provide supplementary subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Per Drop More Crop Scheme to all the beneficiaries selected for micro irrigation under Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana, the Agriculture Development Officers should fill up the applications of the concerned beneficiaries on e-drip system and send the list of such beneficiaries to the District Superintending Agriculture Officer for approval.
18. District Superintending Agriculture Officers should preferably approve the proposals of the beneficiaries who have applied on e-drip system under Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana and provide them with permissible supplementary subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana Per Drop More Crop Scheme. Similarly, they should send the list of beneficiaries who have been transferred the supplementary subsidy to the Agriculture Development Officers so that they are able to pay the subsidy amount admissible under Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana to the said beneficiaries.
The amount of allowable subsidy under the scheme should be transferred electronically to the Aadhaar linked bank account of the beneficiaries.
