The Proposal:
The Minister of State (Revenue, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development) chaired a meeting on Thursday regarding the problems faced by commercial poultry farmers and poultry companies. The meeting was held on 03.11.2022 at Vidhan Bhavan, Pune. The meeting was attended by representatives of poultry farmers and various poultry companies from different parts of the state. In the meeting, the problems faced by farmers and poultry companies in commercial poultry farming were discussed. At the end of the discussion. The Minister (Revenue, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development) has directed that a State-level poultry coordination committee should be constituted under the chairmanship of Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune with representation from Vidarbha, Marathwada, Western Maharashtra, North Maharashtra and Konkan regions of the state, including poultry farmers, egg producers and representatives of poultry companies.
The government was considering the formation of a state-level poultry coordination committee to address the problems faced by private commercial poultry farmers as well as poultry companies in the state. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 3. The Government hereby approve the constitution of State Level Poultry Coordination Committee as follows to address the problems faced by the private commercial poultry farmers and poultry companies in the poultry business in the State.
Composition of State Level Poultry Coordination Committee
1) Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune-Chairman 2) Additional Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Commissionerate of Animal Husbandry, Pune-Member 3) Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Disease Investigation Department, Pune-Member 4) Assistant Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Central Egg Harvesting Centre, Pune-Member 5) Coordinator, State Level Bankers Committee, Pune-Member 6) 3 Farmers engaged in contract farming-Member 7) 3 Farmers engaged in open farming-Member 8) 5 Farmers engaged in poultry egg production business,-Member 9) 5 representatives of private poultry business (chicks and feed) companies. - Member 10) Representative of NECC- Member 11) Deputy Commissioner Animal Husbandry (Livestock& Poultry), Commissionerate of Animal Husbandry, Pune- Member Secretary
4. The Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune shall appoint representatives of the farmers engaged in contract/ open farming and egg business of Vidarbha, Marathwada, Western Maharashtra, North Maharashtra and Konkan regions in the above committee. Also, if there is a need to change the name of the representatives, then the decision should also be taken by the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune.
Organization of State Level Poultry Coordination Committee Meetings:
The State Level Poultry Coordination Committee should meet regularly every 3 months and submit the minutes of the meeting to the Government.
