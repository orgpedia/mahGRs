The Proposal: -
According to the Maharashtra Fire Prevention and Life Safety Measures (Amendment) Act, 2006 and the Maharashtra Fire Prevention and Life Safety Measures (Amendment) Act, 2023, SAAT systems are required to be operationalized in all buildings and surrounding, industrial buildings.
Under Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani, various colleges, student hostels, various departments, etc. buildings have been constructed. Officers, staff under various departments are working in the office building. Also, arrangements have been made in the office for various documents, important records, etc. Since these buildings have been electrified, inadvertent large-scale power outages can cause a fire in the building by causing a shot circuit, which can result in significant damage to building property and life if the building does not have firefighting measures in place. For this, each building is required to be fire audited and fire safety measures required by the building are put into operation, according to Reference No. Under Sections 1 and 2, a proposal has been submitted for administrative approval for installation of fire and fire protection system in various colleges, student hostels under Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani.
