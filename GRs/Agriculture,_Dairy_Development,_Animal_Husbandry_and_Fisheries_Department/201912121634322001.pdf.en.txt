Approval to open a new head of account to budget funds for the construction of various office buildings under the Commissionerate of Agriculture.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No: AGRICULTURE- 1219/ PR No. 142/16-A
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is December 12, 2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Public Works Department, the Planning Department, the Finance Department and the Accountant General's Office have agreed to open a new head of capital expenditure to budget the necessary funds for the construction of new buildings for various offices operating in rented premises under the Department of Agriculture. Accordingly, the following new accounting standards are being granted for the construction of office buildings in the Agriculture Department.
