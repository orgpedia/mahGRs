Preamble: For the financial year 2022-2023, as per the instructions in the circular No. 1 and the semi-government letter in the context of the Finance Department, all the four agricultural universities in the state have been given the demand No. 1 status. Funds under the head D-3,' 2415 Agricultural Research and Education have been disbursed on the BIMES system for the purpose of 31-Assistant Grants (Non-salaried) for the mandatory schemes with a ceiling of 21%.
The Controller, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli has issued the above Reference No. As per letter No. 2, a proposal has been submitted for the disbursement of funds from the approved budgetary allocation for the mandatory schemes for Dapoli Agricultural University in the form of 31-Assistant Grants (Non-salaried).
The matter was under consideration of the government for release of funds.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: During the financial year 2022-23, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli has been sanctioned 21% of the sanctioned budgetary allocation for the following activities: 2415-Agriculture Research and Education, 01-Crop Cultivation (2415 0034), 03-Animal Husbandry (2415 0179) and 05-Fish Farming (2415 0241) (Compulsory) 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary). The Government is sanctioning a grant of Rs. 87,63,000/- (Rupees Eighty Seven Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand only). A detailed description is given below.

1) Department of Finance. The instructions given in the circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly implemented.
2. Funds to be mandatorily disbursed by the Agricultural Universities in respect of scheme/ non-scheme and subject-wise.
Spend only for the permissible items in the plan.
(3) If the funds disbursed are lying unutilized/ remaining unutilized for any reason, the amount of such funds shall be
Unallocated/ balance funds should not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
4. Schemes under which the period of the scheme/ non-scheme has expired.
The expenditure under the schemes should not be continued without the approval of the government after the expiry date.
The grants-in-aid sanctioned by the Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research to agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula or how, and to be spent as per the order/ procedure prescribed.
The assessment report of each financial year, whether forthcoming or not, shall be submitted to the Government within a period of three months from the end of the financial year concerned. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
