Introduction: Former Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Shri. Vasantrao Naik's birth anniversary is celebrated as the birth anniversary of the great leader of the Indian agriculture. The government has decided to celebrate July 1 as Agriculture Day in the state. The decision was taken on June 21, 1989.
The Kharif 2020 sowing season has begun. During the lockdown in the wake of COVID-19, the Department of Agriculture, Agricultural University has tried to reach out to the farmers by using online platforms for technology dissemination. This will be necessary in the future as well.
This needs to be planned for. The agriculture department has done a good job of supplying fertilizers and seeds on the dam. Farmers have responded in a big way.
Government Decision: 1. The government has decided to celebrate July 1 as Agriculture Day in the state. A maximum of Rs. 5000/- (Rs. 355 crore), Rs. 17,75,000/- (Rs. 17 lakh) and Rs. 10,000/- (Rs. 10,000 each) to 34 Zilla Parishads. 3,40,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Forty Thousand only). 21,15,000/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Fifteen Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
