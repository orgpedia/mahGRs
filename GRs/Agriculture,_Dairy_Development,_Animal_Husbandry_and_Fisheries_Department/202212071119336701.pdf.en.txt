The Proposal:
In the meeting of the 25th and 29th State Level Project Approval Committee of the National Agriculture Development Scheme, Shri. The Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra had approved the implementation of the project titled" Production of Silkworm Hybrid Treasure and Promotion of Silk Farming Technology in Marathwada Region" under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana at Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agriculture University, Parbhani for a period of 3 years from 2017-18 to 2019-20. This project worth Rs. 36.00 lakhs will be provided. The project is yet to be completed and will be completed by 2022-23. Post-facto administrative approval for the programme of Rs. 35.653 lakh was under consideration of the Government. Following this, the government decided:
Government's decision:
