The Proposal:
M.B. Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project (earlier named' Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project') has been approved to be implemented in all the districts of the state over a period of 7 years (from 2019-20 to 2026-27). The Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation Project Society has been set up under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. The total cost of the project is around Rs 300 crore. 2100 crore (@ 1 US$= Rs. 70/- each) is expected to be invested (excluding beneficiary share), out of which 70% of the funds (US$ 210 million) is being sanctioned as a low interest rate loan from the World Bank, 26.67% of the funds (US$ 80 million) is being invested from the State Government's self-funding. Similarly, 3.33 per cent of the project cost (US$ 10 million) has been approved for private sector participation (from Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds) through the Gram Samajik Parivartan Foundation.
