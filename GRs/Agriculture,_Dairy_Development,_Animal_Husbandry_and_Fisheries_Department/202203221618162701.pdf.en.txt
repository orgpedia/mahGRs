The Proposal:
Marayi Fishermen Co-operative Society Limited, Vashigaon, Navi Mumbai has decided to file a writ petition against the State of Maharashtra and others regarding the compensation to be paid to the fishermen affected by the Vashi Bridge Project to be constructed in the Thane Bay area. Writ Petition No. 2016/2021 has been filed in the High Court, Mumbai. Regarding this petition, The High Court, Bombay has directed that (i) a State-wide policy should be prescribed regarding the compensation to be paid to the fishermen affected by such projects (ii) a committee should be constituted to decide the compensation to be paid to the fishermen affected by the Thane Creek Bridge-3 project. Ordered on 12/08/2022.
Accordingly, a state-wide policy will be formulated to provide compensation to the fishermen affected by the projects being undertaken by various agencies in the coastal areas of the state. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The committee has prepared a draft of the state-wide policy. The department has received representations from various fishermen cooperative societies to make the draft" State-wide Policy" transparent.
However, to finalize the draft" State-wide Policy" for providing compensation to the project affected fishermen and to take appropriate decision on the demands and representations received, the Government has decided to implement the draft policy. The government is considering the formation of a" high-level committee" under the chairmanship of the Minister (Fisheries).
Government Decision: To provide compensation to the fishermen affected by the projects being undertaken by various agencies in the coastal areas of the state. A committee headed by the Principal Secretary (Fisheries) has been constituted to finalise the draft of the state-wide policy and take appropriate decisions on the demands and representations received from various fishermen cooperative societies. A" High Level Committee" is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Fisheries) as follows:
