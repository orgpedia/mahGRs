The Central Government has approved the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) to address the problems of unorganized food processing industries in the State. In this backdrop, the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) will be implemented in the State for the next five years i.e. from 2020-2021 to 2024-25. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 23.12.2020.
A total amount of Rs. 175.90 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Seventy Five Crores Ninety Lakh only) has been budgeted for the year 2022-2023 for implementation of the said scheme with Central share and State share. A proposal has been received from the Commissioner (Agriculture) for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 20.00 crore for implementation of the scheme during 2022-2023. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the amount budgeted for the implementation of the scheme in the year 2022- 2023 was under consideration of the Government, accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions.
