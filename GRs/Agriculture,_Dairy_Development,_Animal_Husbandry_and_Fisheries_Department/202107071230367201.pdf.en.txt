2) Department of Finance, Government Circular No. 2121/ No. 35/ Koshaprastha-4, dated July 1, 2021
Preamble: In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has directed to adopt a revised methodology vide order dated 23rd March, 2021. Accordingly, Finance Department vide Reference No. 2 The Government Circular here has given revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, a single nodal agency (SNA) is required to be appointed for the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes and a separate single bank account is required to be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as the nodal officer for coordination of these centrally sponsored schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Resolution: Reference No. 2 As per the instructions issued vide Circular No.2, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) is being appointed for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries.
