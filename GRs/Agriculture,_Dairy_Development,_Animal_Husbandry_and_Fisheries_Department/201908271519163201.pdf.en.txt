The Proposal:
Funding is sanctioned mainly through the Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi, for the implementation of teaching, research and extension education schemes under all the four agricultural universities in the state. With this funding, education, research and extension projects are implemented in agricultural universities in the state. The research projects submitted by the universities are sanctioned on a grant basis of 75:25 (75% by ICAR and 25% by State Government) for a period of 2 to 5 years. Reference No. Under Section 2, the Central Government/ Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi/ Institutions funded by the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare may grant approval of the Government, where necessary, for implementation of the project/ scheme/ programme under the Agricultural University and for making full utilization of the funds. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. It was held on 4th July, 2019 at Mantralaya, Mumbai. In the meeting, 25 percent grant was approved as the state government's share for research projects under agricultural universities in the state with 75:25 percent financial assistance from the Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi and the state government respectively.
Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning 25% share of the State Government for implementing 02 projects under Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola was under consideration of the Government.
