Introduction: The above-mentioned Department of General Administration. The detailed procedure for recruitment to the post of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres in the nomination quota in the office of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission) has been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 13th June, 2018. In the said Government Decision, (b) 1, it is directed that Regional Selection Committees headed by Regional Head of Department and State Level Selection Committees headed by Head of Department shall be constituted by all the Ministries/ Administrative Divisions for selection of candidates to the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D at the State level. Accordingly, the Commissionerate of Agriculture has submitted a proposal to the Government to constitute Selection Committees to fill up the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres at the State level in the Commissionerate of Agriculture and Subordinate Regional Offices by nomination. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government Order: The following Selection Committees are being constituted to fill up the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres at the State level in the Commissionerate of Agriculture and Subordinate Regional Offices by nomination.
