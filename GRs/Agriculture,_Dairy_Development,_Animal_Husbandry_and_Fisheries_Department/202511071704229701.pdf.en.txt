Preamble: To provide accurate weather information in all villages of the state, to provide weather-based agricultural advice and guidance to farmers, to provide necessary weather information for research in the agro-climatic field as well as for natural calamities, etc. In line with the central government's WINDS (Weather Information Network Data $559]) project, an automated weather centre will be set up at the gram panchayat level in the state. There is a reversal in the approval under the Government Decision of 27.06.2025. The government was considering opening a savings account to receive funds under the project.
Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: The opening of a new interest bearing savings bank account with Bank of Maharashtra, a nationalised bank, is being permitted subject to the following conditions for availing funds under the central government's WINDS (Weather Information Network Data System) project for setting up of automated weather stations at Gram Panchayat level in the state.
The account shall be used only for the specific purpose for which it is proposed to be opened, as directed by the Central Government.
Under no circumstances should the account be used for any other purpose.
It shall be mandatory to keep the cash book up to date with respect to the said account.
4. If the balance in the said bank account is required to be paid to the appropriate government account, it shall be mandatory to act accordingly in accordance with the applicable rules and regulations.
5. Monthly adjustment of bank account transactions and audit shall be mandatory.
6. Care should be taken to ensure that funds are not left unutilised for a long period of time in this account.
This bank account will need to be closed immediately after certain purposes have ended.
