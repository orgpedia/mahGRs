Government's decision:
In 2017-18, the State received Rs. 664.36 crore under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) in the ratio of 60:40 Central and State funding.
Out of the said fund, Rs. 539.16 crore was released for general category, however, as per the expenditure likely to be incurred during the period prescribed, the implementing agencies have released Rs. 24.17 crore out of which the balance of Rs. 77.13 crore, totalling Rs. 101.30 crore, was available with the Commissioner (Agriculture) for re-distribution. Out of which, the Government has decided to reallocate Rs. 85.04 crore in Reference No. 12 to 14 was approved and the remaining Rs. 16.22 crore was pending for re-appropriation.
Similarly, out of the Rs. 63.76 crore allocated for SCs, the implementing agencies had dedicated Rs.25 crore as per the expenditure potential within the stipulated time period and out of the remaining Rs. 5.67 crore, a total of Rs. 30.67 crore was available with the Commissioner (Agriculture) for re-distribution.
Further, out of the fund of Rs. 61.44 crore released for Scheduled Tribes, the implementing agencies had dedicated Rs. 14.27 crore as per the expenditure likely to be incurred during the period prescribed and out of the remaining Rs. 0.18 crore, a total of Rs. 14.45 crore was available with the Commissioner (Agriculture) for re-distribution.
National Agriculture Development Scheme. As per letter dated 15/02/2021 (Refer No. 5) As per the instructions, the Commissioner (Agriculture) has released the remaining amount of Rs. 16.22 crore to the General Category, Rs. 30.67 crore to the Scheduled Castes and Rs. 14.45 crore to the Scheduled Tribes out of the total amount of Rs. 61.34 crore to the other sanctioned projects under the scheme vide Reference No. 16 to 28 have been reallocated. This decision has been approved by the government. The category-wise/ project-wise details of the reallocated funds are given in Annexure-A, Annexure-B and Annexure-C respectively.
