The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities.
As per the directions of this Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure posts in Government service for Divyang for the posts of Group A to Group-D cadre in the Commissionerate of Animal Husbandry and Regional Office under the Department of Agriculture and Padum and to ensure these posts for the newly Divyang category by reviewing the list of seats in Government service ensured for Divyang at a fixed period not exceeding 3 years.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021.
As mentioned in the notification, the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in the Group A to Group D posts in the state government establishments, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale of the state government.
