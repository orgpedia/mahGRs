Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 19.August, 2019, the implementation of Mukhya Mantri Sustainable Agriculture Irrigation Scheme in the drought prone areas as well as in the suicide affected and Naxal affected districts was approved with the objective of providing sustainable irrigation facilities to the farmers of the State. The government has decided to implement this scheme in the remaining taluks of the state from 2021-22.
Under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana, 55% subsidy is given to small and marginal farmers of the state and 45% to other farmers (within the limit of 5 hectares). Apart from this permissible subsidy, the government has decided to provide 80% and 75% total subsidy for micro irrigation by providing 25% supplementary subsidy to small and marginal land holding farmers and 30% to other farmers under the" Mukhya Mantri Sustainable Agriculture Irrigation" scheme.
