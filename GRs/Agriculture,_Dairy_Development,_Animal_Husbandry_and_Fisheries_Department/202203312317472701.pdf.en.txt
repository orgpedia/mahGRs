Preamble: A demand for additional grant in wages under the heading of Accounts (2415 0043) has been made to Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Agriculture University, Akola vide letter No. 2. The proposal was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the balance of 31-Assistant Grants (Non-salary) under the mandatory head of Accounts in all the four Agricultural Universities namely 24150025, 2415 0034, 2415 0043 and 2415 0052 has been increased to Rs. 12,99,35, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Nine Lakh Thirty Five Thousand only) has been provided by way of re-appropriation. The following are being approved for disbursement of grants made available through this reallocation:
Government Resolution: Rs. The Government hereby approve the release of grant in aid amounting to Rs. 12,99,35, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand only) subject to the terms and conditions mentioned below and as per the prospectus A attached to the Government Order.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities as above shall be spent within the prescribed limits after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991 and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
(2) The grant shall not be credited to the self-financed account/ PLA bank account under any circumstances.
3) The subsidy for the scheme sanctioned by the above reallocation should be spent in the financial year 2021-22 itself.

4) Further, while making the demand for grant, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed scheme-wise, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. 5) Don't spend money on posts and affairs.
