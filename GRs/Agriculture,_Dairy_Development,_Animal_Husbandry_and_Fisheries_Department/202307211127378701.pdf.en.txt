Government Resolution No: Propivio-2023/ P. No. 85/11 A
Government's decision:
Considering the demand made by the Commissionerate of Agriculture and the guidelines of the Central Government, the Crop Insurance Companies implementing the Comprehensive Crop Insurance Scheme under Kharif Season 2023 have been given an amount of Rs. 1265,74,78, 478/- is being approved by the Government.
The amount is being released for Kharif 2023 season and will not be allowed for other seasons earlier.
Expenditure on the said items should be met from the budgetary provision approved for the year 2023-24 under the following head: Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 110, Crop Insurance (00) (08) Financial assistance for insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana State share (2401 A 664) Non-plan, 33- Financial assistance.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for spending the funds distributed under this government decision in accordance with the guidelines of the Prime Minister Crop Insurance Scheme and the government decision issued from time to time by the government.
For this purpose, Assistant Director (Accounts), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
