Preamble In order to increase the income of the farmers as well as to increase the energy consumption in the agriculture sector, the Centrally Sponsored Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization is being implemented in the State. Apart from this, under various centrally sponsored schemes such as National Mission on Pulses, Oilseeds and Oilseeds, National Mission on Food Security and National Mission on Horticulture, some amount of funds are also provided for the agricultural mechanization component. However, adequate funds are not available for mechanization as per the demand of the farmers. Considering these factors, funds are also made available for agricultural mechanization through RKVY-RAFTAR. The project was sanctioned at a cost of Rs 150 crore.
