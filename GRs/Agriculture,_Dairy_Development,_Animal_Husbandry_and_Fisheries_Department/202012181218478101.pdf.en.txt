Preamble: There was a demand from the economically backward students of all the four agricultural universities of the state as well as the colleges concerned to pay the pending amount of scholarships for the year 2019-20. 21.98 crore from Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune. Proposals 1 and 2 were submitted to the government. In this regard, the government was considering to disburse subsidy under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme 24151139 (Programme) 34- Discipleship/ Vidyavate.
Government decision: Under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme, scholarships are provided to the students of economically backward classes in all the four agricultural universities of the state through the Maha DBT system. For the financial year 2020-21, under the head" 2415 Agricultural Research and Education," assistance will be provided to 120 institutions under the head" 01 Crop Protection." The Government is approving the release of subsidy amounting to Rs. 13,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Fifty Lakhs only).
