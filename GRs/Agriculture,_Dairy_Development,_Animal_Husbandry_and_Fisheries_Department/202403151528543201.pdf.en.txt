3. Taluka Seed Counting Centre, Mauje Saidapur, Ta. Out of the 13.03 hectare area of Karad, District Satara, the 10.00 hectare land was transferred to Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri for Agricultural College, Karad by the Department of Revenue. According to the government circular dated 8/9/2008, restrictions have been imposed on the transfer of land without the permission of the revenue and forest departments. Also, if such lands are not required by the government departments in respect of the government land provided to the government departments, the department is required to hand over such lands to the revenue department with the consent of the revenue department.
4. There are a total of 357 taluks in the state with only 192 Taluk Bijgunan Kendras.
The land of Taluka Bijgunan Kendra is requisitioned by various private, commercial, cooperative, government, semi-government, organizations for different purposes as these taluks are coming up in urban areas during the development of these taluks. Therefore, the Government vide Government Circular No. As per TABIKE 2011 / Q. Considering the importance of agricultural education and extension as the Agricultural College is an initiative of the Agriculture University under the Department of Agriculture, the transfer of 10.00 hectare of land out of total 13.03 hectare area in Taluka Seed Multiplication Area Saidapur to Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri for Agricultural College, Karad is being approved under the following terms and conditions: -
1) Out of the 13.03 hectare area in the name of Department of Agriculture, Government of Maharashtra, Seed Counting Centre, Saidapur, 10 hectares of government land will be required to be counted as per rules before transfer to Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri.
2) Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri will hold the land on a tenancy of Subsidiary Class-II.
3) It will be mandatory to use such government land for the purpose of Agricultural College Karad in this area.
4) The transfer of the land cannot be done without the prior approval of the Revenue Department / Collector, Satara, Government of India.
