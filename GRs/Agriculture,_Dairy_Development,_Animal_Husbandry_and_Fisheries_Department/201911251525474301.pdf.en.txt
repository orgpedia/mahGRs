Government's decision
A total amount of Rs. 1233.46 lakh (Rs. 12 crore thirty three lakh forty seven thousand only) out of the Central share of funds received under National Food Security Mission for foodgrains crops in 2018-19, amounting to Rs. 740.08 lakh in Central share and Rs. 493.38 lakh in State share, is being approved for utilization under this Mission for foodgrains crops in 2019-20.
Section 2. Out of the total amount of Rs. 1233.46 lakh as Refund under NFSA, a total amount of Rs. 71.87 lakh comprising of Rs. 49.10 lakh from Central Account and Rs. 22.77 lakh from State Account is lying in the PFMS linked bank accounts of NFSA to be utilized during 2019-20.
Section 3. Out of the total amount of Rs. 1233.46 lakh, Rs. 690.98 lakh (Rs. Six Crore Ninety Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand only) is being released to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on Budgetary Distribution System (BDS), out of which Rs. 1161.59 lakh (Rs. Four Crore Seventy Lakh Sixty Nine Thousand only) is being released to the Central Government and Rs. 470.61 lakh (Rs. Four Crore Seventy Lakh Sixty Thousand only) to the State Government, to be spent during 2019-20.
