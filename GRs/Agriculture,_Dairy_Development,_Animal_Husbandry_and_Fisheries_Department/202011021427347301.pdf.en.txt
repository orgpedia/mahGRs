Preamble: The Government has approved the revised figure of 4097 posts of Subordinate Officers/ Employees of the Commissioner, Dairy Development Department. Approved by the Government Decision dated 30/08/2014. After the approval of the revised figure, a total of 2616 temporary posts and 353 additional posts of officers and staff in various offices under the Commissioner, Dairy Development Department have been sanctioned. 01/03/2020 to dt. Reference No. to be continued till 31/08/2020. The decision was approved by the government. The Commissioner's Office vide Reference No. The letter of 4 has requested for an extension of the continuation of 2611 temporary posts and 315 additional posts.
Finance Department Reference No. Under the government's decision of April 3, the authority to extend temporary posts has been given to the concerned administrative departments. The figure of 4097 posts in the establishment of the Commissioner, Dairy, is given in Reference No. In view of the approval given by the Government under section 1, the continuation of temporary posts, permanentization of temporary posts, etc. should be carried out through the Commissioner Dairy by revising the 2611 temporary posts and 315 additional posts indicated under this figure. Since it is likely to take some time to complete the process, the government was considering to extend 2611 temporary posts and 315 additional posts on the establishment of the Commissioner Dairy Department so that the salary of the officers and employees would not be adversely affected.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this decision, the Government has decided to fill up a total of 2611 temporary posts and 315 additional posts of officers and staff in various offices under the Commissioner, Dairy Development. 01/09/2020 to D. Approving the extension till 31/01/2021. The details of temporary and additional posts are shown separately in the Statement A.
