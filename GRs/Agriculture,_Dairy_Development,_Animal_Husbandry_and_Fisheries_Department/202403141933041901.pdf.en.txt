2. Active Conditions -
The recognition given to the college will be on a perpetual non-grant basis.
i.The college should fulfil the points / errors mentioned in the findings of the University Screening Committee report within the stipulated time.

iv. Fixation of fees for undergraduate courses shall be prescribed by the Fee Regulatory Authority, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai / Govt. of India as may be prescribed from time to time.

The norms and guidelines prescribed by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi, the State Government and the concerned Agricultural University / Council of Agriculture from time to time will be mandatory for affiliation, inspection and evaluation of the show college.
