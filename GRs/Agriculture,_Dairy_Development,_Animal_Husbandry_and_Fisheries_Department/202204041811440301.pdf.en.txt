The proposal: The 29 th State Level Project Sanctioning Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra has approved the implementation of the project for production of various biocontrollers using advanced technology for healthy growth and higher yield of crops under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana at Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli University in the year 2021-22 and 2022-23. Under the scheme, Rs. Rs 190 crore. The project is yet to be completed and is expected to be completed by 2022. The matter of giving administrative approval to the program of 190 lakhs was under the consideration of the government, following which the government is taking the following decisions.
Government's decision:
1. Under the project titled" Production of various biocontrollers using advanced technology for better growth and higher yield of crops" under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, Rs. Administrative approval is being given to implement the programme with a fund of Rs.190 lakh (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakh only).
2. The project is being implemented as per the guidelines of National Agriculture Development Scheme by the Department of Plant Pathology, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University should be implemented.
