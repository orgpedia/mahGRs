The proposal-
To provide veterinary services to the livestock belonging to farmers/ cattle rearers, Read No. 1 here. A total of 03 posts viz., Livestock Development Officer, Group-A, 01 post, Cardiologist, Group-D, 01 post and Attendant, Group-D, 01 post have also been sanctioned for establishment of Category-I Veterinary Dispensaries.
Delighted at the 6th meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries held on 26.08.2021. Government's decision
Approval of the Government is being accorded for establishment of a new Category-I Veterinary Clinic at Mauje Lahavit, T.G.
