The Government Decisions 3 and 4 are being superseded and the Consolidated Government Decisions are being issued as follows:
GOVERNMENT DECISION The Verifying Authority for Agriculture Commissionerate and Subordinate Regional Offices is being declared as the authority responsible for approving the registration on the primary user portal for purchase of all types of goods/ services including vehicle related activities at the Commissionerate and Divisional level and opening of the secondary user account on the portal after the primary user receives the approval of the Verifying Officer and to act as the controller while carrying out the procurement process as per the guidelines given on the Government e Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the procedures prescribed by the Government from time to time.

1.Department of Industries, Energy and Labour. 01.12.2016, Dy. The provisions mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 24.08.2017 should be strictly followed.
To purchase goods/ services through GeM Portal, each Purchasing Office shall create a User on the GeM Portal in such a manner that at the time of creating the User, there can be only one Buyer and Receiver of goods/ services in the respective office, but the Buyer and Receiver of goods and services shall not be the payee.
3. Demo for implementation of procurement of goods/ services through GeM portal is available at geM.gov.in/ training. However, the users of this system should follow the procedure of procurement of goods/ services as per the terms and conditions of procurement prescribed by the Government and the terms and conditions on the GeM portal, based on the training material.
In case of technical/ other difficulties in the implementation of the said procedure, the following Coordination Cell of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour may be contacted.
Email: ak. rai@gem. gov. in, dicspo@maharashtra. All purchases of goods and services in the office should be made through the GeM portal by strictly following all the instructions on the contact number 02222023505.
