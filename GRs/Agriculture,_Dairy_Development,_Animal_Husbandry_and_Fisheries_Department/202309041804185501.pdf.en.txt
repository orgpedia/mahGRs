Preamble: Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana is being implemented in the State under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture with 60% Central share and 40% State share. The scheme includes promotion of commercial organic farming and use of organic manure, creation of organic manure in farmers' fields, organic certification, training based on organic farming, increasing economic income by reducing the cost of production for farmers by adopting low-cost technologies.
The scheme is group based and groups are formed in a group of 20 hectares. Under the scheme, the benefit is given to the selected group/ beneficiary once for 3 years.
Under the scheme, 350 groups have been formed in six districts of Vidarbha in 2020-21 and 20 groups in 4 aspirational districts of the state and 500 groups have been formed in all districts of the state in 2022-23. Administrative approval has been granted under 2 to 4. From 2022-23 onwards, funds are being made available for Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana from National Agriculture Development Scheme.
