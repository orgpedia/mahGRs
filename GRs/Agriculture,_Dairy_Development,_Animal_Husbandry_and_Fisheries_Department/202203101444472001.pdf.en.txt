(b) Mr. Hitendra Patil has been appointed as a member of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Provisions in respect of period of assumption of office, post-retirement service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981 vide Rule 40, Annexure-II, Clause No.

(b) Since the promotion is temporary in nature, the entitlement to the benefit of service cannot be determined from the date of promotion.
(a) If the concerned officers refuse temporary promotion to the post of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-B, the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ K. 12, Dy. 12/09/2016, General Administration Department, Government Decision No. SRV 2018/ Q.No.159/ Ka.12, D. (d) Provisional promotion of the said officers to the post of Maharashtra Agricultural Service, Group-B is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against the said officers till the date of this order.
The above officers should immediately join the post of promotion by handing over the charge of their present post to the nearest senior officers of the department.
