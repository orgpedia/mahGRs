Government's decision
Integrated Pest-Disease Surveys and Advisory Schemes (CropSAPs) are being implemented in 2020-21 under the Crops Disease Surveys and Advisories Project (CropSAP) for management of pests and diseases in major crops like soybean, cotton, paddy, tur, gram, maize, sorghum and sugarcane and fruits like mango, pomegranate, banana, mango, orange and chikoo. Funds have been released under the scheme as per administrative sanction. Under the Pest-Disease Surveys and Advisory Project (CropSap) scheme, the funds are being approved for these crops from the savings on other aspects of the project, including cashew, ladyfinger and tomato.
The implementation of the scheme shall be as per the guidelines contained in the Government Resolution dated 27th May, 2020.
