4. Krishi Unnati Yojana- Benefit under the scheme should be provided to the beneficiaries of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and women farmers as envisaged under the National Food Security Mission by the Central Government.
The Union Ministry of Agriculture has issued a Reference letter to the State Governments under the National Food Security Mission for the year 2019-20. As per the administrative approval given vide letter dated 18/04/2019 and as per the action plan approved for the mission during the current year and as per the directions of the Union Ministry of Agriculture from time to time, it will be mandatory for the Director (Extension and Training) to control the mission at the state level and also to direct the subordinate offices from time to time for effective implementation and control of the mission.
Priority should be given to small and marginal farmers or groups of such farmers while selecting beneficiary farmers under various components of the National Food Security Mission.
Under this campaign, for the purchase of various inputs (fertilizers, medicines, seeds, irrigation tools, machines/ tools, etc.), the subsidy should be directly transferred to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries as per the policy of the government. The beneficiaries will have the freedom to purchase these tenders from the open market, however, if the tenders produced by the government agencies/ PSUs of the government are being supplied under this campaign, action should be taken as per the relevant government decision dated 19/04/2017.
The concerned District Superintending Agriculture Officer/ Taluk Agriculture Officer should select the beneficiaries for the campaign and compile the list of beneficiaries at the Taluk level in order to implement the programs covered under the campaign. The list includes the name of the farmer, village, survey/ group number, etc.
Data such as mobile number, bank account number, etc. should be collected. Similarly, the list of beneficiaries selected under the campaign has been 15 since the receipt of funds.
