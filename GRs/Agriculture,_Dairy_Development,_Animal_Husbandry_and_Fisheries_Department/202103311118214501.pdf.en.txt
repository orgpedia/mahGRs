Circular: In 2020-21, online applications from farmers have been accepted through Maha-DBT system for various schemes of the Department of Agriculture. Similarly, farmers have also been selected in online draw of lots through Maha-DBT system. The entire process of scrutiny and approval of the applications of the selected farmers is being completed online by the field officers/ employees of the Agriculture Department on the Maha-DBT system and it is being recommended to pay the admissible subsidy amount to the eligible beneficiaries at the district level.
Under reference The modalities for implementation of the schemes of the Department of Agriculture on Maha-DBT system have been laid down vide circular dated 4th November, 2020. As per the provisions of Para 4.9 of the said Circular, the modalities for disbursement of subsidy to the beneficiary farmers through Maha-DBT portal have been prescribed. Accordingly, the payment of every state level scheme from the level of Agriculture Commissionerate is to be sent to the treasury and the subsidy disbursed to the beneficiaries from the funds made available by the treasury is to be deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the farmers through the Maha DBT system. However, the integration of the schemes of the Department of Agriculture on Maha-DBT system and the system of Accounts Treasuries Office is in progress and is expected to be completed soon.
Therefore, for the year 2020-21, the Department of Agriculture will implement the Maha-DBT portal
