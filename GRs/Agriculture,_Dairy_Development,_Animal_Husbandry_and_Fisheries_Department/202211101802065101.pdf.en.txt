The Proposal:
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) is a group farming based scheme under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) which provides benefits to selected groups/ beneficiaries for three years. The scheme is 60 per cent funded by the central government and 40 per cent by state governments.

3. The balance sheet and the amount of the audit deposit expenditure relating to the scheme should be reported by the implementing agency.
