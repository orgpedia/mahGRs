Preamble: Under the Tribal Component Programme, with the objective of reducing malnutrition in the tribal areas of 14 districts of the State and to provide nutritious food to the tribal brothers and sisters, the scheme of" Fruit and Vegetable Cultivation in the Neighbourhood of Tribal Families" is being implemented in the State since 2003-04 to promote the cultivation of nutritious vegetables and fruits in the neighbourhood of the tribal families. For the year 2022-23, Rs. A sum of Rs. 33.00 lakh has been allocated for the project. Accordingly, the scheme will be implemented in the state in 2022-23 at a cost of Rs. 33.00 lakh has been proposed by the Director (Horticulture). 2 submitted to the government. This scheme will be implemented in the state in the year 2022-23 within the budgeted limit of Rs. 33.00 lakh for the above programme. The administrative approval has been granted under the Government Decision dated 11/05/2022. Further, for the implementation of the said scheme in the year 2022-23, the Tribal Development Department has allocated Rs. 33.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs only) has been disbursed to the Principal Secretary (Agriculture) as Head of Administrative Department on Budget Distribution System (BEAMS). The matter was under consideration of the Government to disburse the said fund to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune. The government is taking the following steps:
