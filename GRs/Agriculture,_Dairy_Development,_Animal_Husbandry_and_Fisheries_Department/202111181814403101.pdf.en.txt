The Proposal:
Under reference Administrative approval has been given to implement the" Mukhyamantri Saswat Krishi Sinchai Yojana" under the Government Decision dated 19th August, 2019. The scheme is being implemented in all the 17 taluks of 14 suicide-prone and 3 naxal-affected districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada, as well as in the drought-prone taluks of the rest of Maharashtra. The government is planning to implement the scheme in all the districts of the state.
Government's decision:
1. under reference. As per the decision taken on 19th August, 2019, administrative approval has been given to implement the Mukhyamantri Sashakt Krishi Sinchayee Yojana in all the taluks of 17 districts of Vidarbha and Marathwada, including 14 suicide-affected districts and 3 Naxal-affected districts, as well as in the drought-prone taluks of the rest of Maharashtra.
2.The implementation of the Chief Minister Sustainable Agriculture Irrigation Scheme in these 107 talukas should be done from the date of this government decision, the list of these talukas is being co-signed with this government decision.
This Government Decision was issued vide No. No. 309/21/ Expenditure-1, D. Issued pursuant to approval received by November 12, 2021.
