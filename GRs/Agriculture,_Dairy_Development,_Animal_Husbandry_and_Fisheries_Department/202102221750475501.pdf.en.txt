Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. In this regard, the Central Government has published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 4.1.2021 under the heading' List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 4.1.2021' in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification of the Central Government on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. A list of vacancies has been published for the reserved posts. As stated in the order, the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in Group A to Group D posts which are in the state government establishment and the posts in the list published by the Central Government whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the post in the state government service, even if the designations of such posts are different, the posts in the state government service will be ensured for the disabled.
Accordingly, the posts as indicated in the Return-A under this Government decision are being ensured for the posts from Group" A" to Group" D" in the Disabled Cadre Fisheries Department in the list published for the posts ensured for Divyang in the notification dated 4.1.2021 issued by the Central Government.
