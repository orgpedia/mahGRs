The total amount of Rs. 13.02902 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission under the decision of the Government should be disbursed district wise by the Director (Extension& Training) through PFMS system.
Director (Extension& Training), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts 1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the withdrawal of funds to the tune of Rs. 13.02902 lakhs made available under this Government decision.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Director (Extension and Training) should submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Secretary (Agriculture).
This decision was taken by the Government of the Department of Finance vide Circular No. 69/ 3D/ 2018. As the conditions laid down as on 02.04.2018 are being fulfilled, the same is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on this Department under the said circular.
