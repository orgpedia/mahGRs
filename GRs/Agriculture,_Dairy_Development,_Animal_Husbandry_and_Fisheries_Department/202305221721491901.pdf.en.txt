6) The allocation of targets and funds under the project should be made from the district-wise mandal level according to the area of the grape crop.

10) The National Agricultural Development Plan Cell, Department of Agriculture and Padum, Mantralaya, Mumbai will be responsible for the distribution of funds for the implementation of the project.
The Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune, shall ensure that the financial and physical progress report of the scheme is submitted to the Central Government as per the guidelines of the Central Government, as well as to the National Agriculture Development Scheme Cell, Agriculture and Padum Department, Ministry of Agriculture within the prescribed time.
13) The funds disbursed for the implementation of the said project should be directly credited to the Aadhaar seeded bank accounts of the beneficiaries. The implementing agencies should ensure that these funds are not wasted.
14) The Drawing and Disbursing Officer should ensure that the decision of the Government is strictly implemented. The expenditure for the scheme is being approved subject to compliance of all financial rules, regulations, financial governance decisions, notifications, orders, orders issued by the Central Government from time to time, guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the Comptroller and Auditor General of India.
