The proposal-
Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries Department. 01.11.2021, the Department of Animal Husbandry, Zilla Parishad, through the District Planning Board, State Level Schemes, Centrally Sponsored Schemes and other Departmental Funds, the Deputy Commissioner of Animal Husbandry, concerned Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Additional Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune and Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune have been prescribed the powers to grant technical approval to the schemes. The Additional Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune and Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune have been delegated the powers to grant technical sanction for schemes amounting to Rs. 50,00,000/-and above. Proposals of Rs. 50,00,000/-lakh and above have to be submitted to the higher office for technical approval and this definitely takes some time and hence delays the implementation of the scheme.
2. In view of the above, the Department of Animal Husbandry, Zilla Parishad through the District Planning Board, State Level Schemes, Centrally Sponsored Schemes and other Departments, to provide technical sanction for the schemes to be implemented from other sources. The Government of this Department has decided to confer powers up to Rs. 3.00 crore to the concerned Regional Joint Commissioner Animal Husbandry. The following Government Decision is being issued by superseding 01.11.2021.
Government Decision The Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Pune and various authorities under him are hereby authorized to grant technical approval to the District Planning Board, State Level Schemes, Centrally Sponsored Schemes and Schemes receiving funds from other sources from other departments of the Government subject to the following financial limits.
