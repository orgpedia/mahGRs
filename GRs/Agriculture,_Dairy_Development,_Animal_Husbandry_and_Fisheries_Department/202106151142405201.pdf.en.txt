The Proposal:
" Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Industries Yojana" is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme on the lines of" One District-One Product" implemented in the State for the next five years from the year 2020-21 as per the Government decision dated 23.12.2020. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments.
The entire expenditure (100%) for the financial year 2020-21 will be borne by the Government of India. Thereafter, the excess expenditure on the State's account for the financial year 2020-21 will be adjusted by the State in the next four years. In pursuance of the implementation of the said scheme, for the financial year 2020-21, the Central Government has made available a sum of Rs. 9,89,78, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crores Ninety-nine Lakhs Seventy-eight Thousand only) as first instalment under Reference No. 1 dated 04.09.2020, through PFMS system to the Office of the Commissioner (Agriculture) for establishment of Project Management Cell, various study reports, publicity and other scheduled matters in the guidelines of the scheme.
The project implementation plan for the year 2020-21 has been prepared at a cost of Rs. 4473.00 lakh. It was submitted for the approval of the Central Government vide letter dated 25.01.2021 with the approval of the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the Central Government vide letter dated 18.03.2021 has approved the project implementation plan for an amount of Rs. 4431.593 lakh (Rupees Forty Four Crores Thirty One Lakh Sixty Nine Thousand Three Hundred only).
