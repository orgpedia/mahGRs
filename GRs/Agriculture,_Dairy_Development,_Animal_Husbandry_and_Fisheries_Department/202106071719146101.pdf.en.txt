Preamble: All India Agricultural Service Union and others against the Government of Maharashtra and others. A Public Interest Litigation No. 4 In 2005, having regard to the directions issued on 06.10.2005, the Government of Maharashtra have enacted the Maharashtra Animal Welfare Act, 1976, the Central Government's Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 and other ancillary Acts to regulate the implementation of the provisions of the Act in the State. A committee was set up to regulate the Animal Welfare Act under the chairmanship of Chandrashekhar S Dharmadhikari (retired Justice, Bombay High Court).
The post of the chairperson of the committee has fallen vacant due to the death of Justice Chandrashekhar S Dharmadhikari (retired). The government was considering appointing a chairman for the vacant post. Also Ma. The High Court of Bombay, vide Public Interest Litigation No. Civil Application (Stamp) No. 4/2005 4561/2019 dated 17.7.2019 directing the Government to appoint the Chairperson of the said Committee. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 3. To regulate the implementation of the provisions of the Maharashtra Animal Welfare Act, 1976, the Central Government Act for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals, 1960, and other ancillary Acts in the State. Justice Abhay Thipsay (Retd.) of Bombay High Court and Allahabad High Court is hereby appointed as the Chairman of the Committee constituted vide Government Resolution dated 1st December, 2005.
All other provisions of the said Government Order dated 5th December, 2005 shall continue to apply.
