The Supreme Court As per the provisions of Section 23 (2) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 vide order dated 18/9/2018, it is necessary to appoint a Grievance Redressal Officer in every establishment of the Government to resolve service related issues arising out of disability of the concerned officers/ employees while in government service in accordance with Section 20 of the said Act.
The Commissioner (Disabled Welfare), Maharashtra State, Pune, has issued instructions to appoint a Group-A officer as Grievance Redressal Officer for the purpose. 2 vide letter dated 20.06.2017 to all the Ministries/ Administrative Departments.
Accordingly, the government was considering appointing a Grievance Redressal Officer at the department/ district level office in the subordinate offices of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune and all agricultural universities.
Government Order: The following officers of Group-A are being appointed as Grievance Redressal Officers to resolve the service related problems of officers/ employees working in the subordinate zonal offices of Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune and all agricultural universities.
