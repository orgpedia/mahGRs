Introduction: In the interest of the farmers, the farmers' magazine published since 1965 provides information to the farmers of the state and outside the state about the interesting and latest developments in the field of agriculture. The Commissioner (Agriculture) has approved the proposal to hand over the printing of the Farmers Monthly Issue to Anand Publications, Jalgaon as the rates mentioned by Anand Publications, Jalgaon in the tender process for the printing of Farmers Magazine for the year 2021-23 are minimal. The letter dated 20.05.2021 has been submitted to the Government. In accordance with the proposal of the Commissionerate of Agriculture, a proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the tender process of the farmers' magazine, to provide the allowable amounts to the eligible tenderers according to the tender process and to approve the expenses incurred for the delivery of the issue post.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Commissioner (Agriculture) Sh. After considering the proposals received by letter dated 20.5.2021, the Government has now decided as follows:
For the period of two years from the date of issue of the said order regarding printing of Farmers Magazine, i.e. for the period from September, 2021 to August, 2023, the Government is approving the tender rates for printing of Farmers Magazine, editing/ simplification of articles, arrangement of articles, four-color printing of magazine, including the procedures for assigning the work of printing of Farmers Magazine to Anand Publications, Jalgaon, the lowest tenderer in the e-tendering process conducted by the Commissioner (Agriculture) at his level.
