The proposal-
The Central Government has accorded administrative approval for implementation of the Scheme of Assistance for Reforms in State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) under Sub-Mission on Agricultural Extension of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET) during 2019-20. As per letter dated 30.10.2019, Rs. 1410.19 lakh has been disbursed. Accordingly, Rs. 940.12 lakhs. 2350.31 lakh is likely to be released in the second instalment for the general category in the current year. Accordingly, under this Government decision, the matter of disbursement of general category funds for the implementation of the scheme was under consideration of the Government.
