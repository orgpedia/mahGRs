Regarding adjustment of interest amount on the newly defined contributory pension scheme of the officers/ employees under all the four agricultural universities in the state for the financial year 2020-21
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairying Development and Fisheries
Government Resolution No.Agriculture-3721/ PR No. 31/7-A
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400032
Date: 25th of March 2021
Read:- 1) Government Decision, Agriculture and Padum Department No. KRIVAYA 1110/ P.No. 100/6, D. D. 27.02.2009 and D. 05.03.2011.
2. Circular No.Finance-2020/ No.64/ Finance-3 of the Department of Finance. 15.03.2021
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has decided to introduce a new defined contribution pension scheme for the officers/ employees under all the four Agricultural Universities in the State. It came into force on 27.02.2009. The modalities for implementation of the scheme have been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 05.03.2011.
For the financial year 2020-21, under the head Demand No.3, 2049-Interest Payments, 60-Interest on Other Liabilities, 101-Interest on Deposits, (00) (05)-Interest on Contributions to Contributory Pension (2049 2246) 45-Interest (in weight), an amount of Rs. 32,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores only) is being handed over to the Accountant General, Mumbai/ Nagpur, Maharashtra by way of Book Adjustment.

2. Under this Government decision, to meet the cost of interest on the consolidated deposits of regular monthly contributions and equivalent contributions of the Government from 01/04/2020 to 31/03/2021 of the officers/ employees of the University under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme, the District Treasury Officers, Ahmednagar, Ratnagiri, Akola and Parbhani have released a total amount of Rs. 32,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores only), the amount of interest should be adjusted and the proceedings for recording and recording should be taken up.
3. It may be ensured that this expenditure is met through book adjustments from the approved budgetary provision for 2020-21 under the head" Demand No. G-3, 2049- Interest Payments, 60- Interest on Other Liabilities, 101- Interest on Deposits, (00) (05)- Interest on Contributions to Contributory Pension (2049 2246) 45- Interest (in weightage)" and does not exceed the sanctioned grant.

The grants approved in this Government Resolution are subject to the following terms and conditions:
(i) All the four Agricultural Universities of the State shall maintain separate accounts of the expenditure made from this grant to the DCPS.
(ii) The purpose for which these grants have been sanctioned by all the four Agricultural Universities of the State. For the same purpose, the interest on the combined amount of regular monthly contribution and equivalent government contribution of the officers/ employees of all the four agricultural universities of the state should be spent [Head of Interest Deposit 8342-Other Deposits, 117 Definitive Contribution Pension Scheme for Employees (01) (01)- Definitive Contribution Pension Scheme 45- Government Interest (8342 5099)].
(iii) The Department of Finance has issued Government Decisions fixing the rate of interest every year.
Accordingly, the interest should be charged and while considering the interest, care should be taken to act according to the instructions given by the circular issued by the Finance Department from time to time. Also, the utility certificate should be sent to the Accountant General's office as well as to this department.
