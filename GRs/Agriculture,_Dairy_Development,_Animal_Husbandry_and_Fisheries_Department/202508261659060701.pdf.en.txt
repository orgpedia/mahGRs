The Proposal: -
Agriculture is the most important source of livelihood in Maharashtra. However, the area is small and
fragmented land, lack of markets, low productivity and income, as well as farmer debt
There are many such problems. These problems have been exacerbated by climate change. or
An ecosystem to overcome problems, improve the quality of life of farmers and move towards prosperity.
It is essential to create.
Maharashtra has been a pioneer in terms of Farmer Producer Organizations. 17th Prime Minister of India
It was mentioned in the Cooperative Congress and that FPOs are a means to empower small farmers.
NITI Aayog also called for strengthening FPOs for the "Developed India @2047" approach.
It is considered essential.
Since 2022, Pani Foundation has organized and disseminated knowledge through the "Satyamev Jayate Farmer's Cup" initiative.
It has worked to improve the lives of farmers. More than 50,000 farmers have been affected.
Half of these organizations are women farmers. That's why this platform is for women.
It has also been effective for empowerment.
At present, the Farmer's Cup is held in 46 taluks of Maharashtra. However, every district of the state
And it needs to be expanded in the village so that there is a mass movement for the organization of agriculture.
And rural prosperity can be achieved.

2.1 Action plan prepared for expansion of "Farmer's Cup" initiative to every village of Maharashtra
To achieve the following objectives.

o Enthusiastic participation of farmers for organization and formation of FPOs o Comprehensive adoption of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) in agriculture o Advancement in value chain through FPOs o Adoption of climate resilient farming practices o Adoption of organic farming by reducing chemical use
(ii) Formulate implementation plans based on government support but with the participation of farmers.
2.3) Setting goals for the activity, creating regulatory mechanisms, and regularizing with the participating stakeholders.
Setting a schedule for review.

Funding and training.
