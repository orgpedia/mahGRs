The following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA)
1. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant G.R. have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
3. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
# Page 3
4. The VPDA is applicable only for State Schemes and not for Central Sector and Centrally
Sponsored Schemes.
5. The Receipt to the said VPDA through Nil Bill procedure, shall be only for the bills drawn
under:
e MTR-31B for the object heads 27- Minor Works and 53- Major Works;
e MTR-45B for the object heads of expenditure 33- subsides and 50- other charges;
e MTR 44 for the object heads of expenditure 31 Grants-in-Aid (Non salary) and
35- Grants in Aid for creating of Capital Assets:
e MTR-29 for the object heads of expenditure 52- Machinery and Equipments shall be
applicable from 1April 2025
6. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate in the prescribed format after
incurring expenditure from the VPDA to the offices viz. O/o. PAG (A&E)-I, Mumbai, O/o
PAG (A&E)-II, Nagpur or O/o PAO, Mumbai as per the respective jurisdiction for the Bills drawn under MTR-44 for object heads of expenditure 31 Grants-in-Aid (Non salary),
35- Grants in Aid for creating of Capital Assets.
For Bills drawn under MTR-31B, MTR-45B, MTR-29 the utilization certificate shall be
submitted to the concerned administrative departments
7. The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the respective Grant.
8. The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund after completion of one year (365 days) from the date of drawl of the amount of funds /date of credit of the amount to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever
is earlier.
9. The Monthly Plus Minus Memo shall be forwarded to this office by the respective treasury officer.
10. The balances under the VPDA shall be reconciled monthly by the administrator with the
District Treasury Officer.
1 — . The balances shall be reconciled with the AG Office by the Treasury Officer who shall submit Balance Certificate to A.G. office for confirmation of their balances in the books of A.G at
the end of the Financial Year.
12. All the conditions/instructions in the Finance Department G.R. No. संकोर्ण / 2023 /
प्र.क्र.११० / 2023 / कोषा प्रशा-५, दि.१४.०२.२०२४ and G.R. No. संकीर्ण / 2023 / प्र.क्र.११० /
2023 / HINT प्रशा-५, दि.१२.०३.२०२५ shall be followed.
13. All Rules of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 shall be applicable to the VPDA.
