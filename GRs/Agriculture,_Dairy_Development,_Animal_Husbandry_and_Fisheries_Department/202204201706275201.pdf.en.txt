It was decided to construct a building for Women Hostel (G+ 1) at Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur with the objective of providing necessary infrastructure for Class-II and Class-III officers/ staff to implement the State Training Policy as well as the training programmes under Integrated Schedules. The above reference No. 1 crore for construction of G+ 1 Hostel building. Administrative approval was granted for a cost of Rs. 125.00 lakhs. However, in view of the increasing scope of training, the capacity of the Vanamati Women's Hostel will be insufficient, therefore, it was decided in the 28th meeting of the Vanamati Regulatory Board to construct the Vanamati Women's Hostel as Ground Floor+ 5 (G+ 5). was notified vide letter dated 11.02.2019. The construction cost of the women's hostel building has increased as it is proposed to construct (G+ 5) instead of (G+ 1).
At present, the slab and wall construction of the women's hostel has been completed up to 5 floors, therefore, in order to give revised administrative approval for the construction of the building of the present women's hostel and to complete the rest of the construction immediately, Vanamati, Nagpur has been given Rs. 600.27 lakh. 448.07 lakh has been released by the Commissioner (Agriculture). The request was made vide letter dated 10.02.2020.
