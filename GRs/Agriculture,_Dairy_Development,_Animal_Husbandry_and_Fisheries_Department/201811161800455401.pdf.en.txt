For effective implementation of the provisions of the Central Government's Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960, Animal Cruelty Prevention (Prevention of Cruelty to Animals) in every district of the State.
As per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Establishment and Regulation) Rules, 2001, the Managing Committees of Animal Cruelty Prevention Societies have been constituted in all the districts vide notification dated 14th March, 2017. It was under consideration of the Government to appoint as non-official members the President of the Gaushala/ Panjalapola Sanstha, members of the voluntary organization working on animal issues, persons nominated by the General Committee and persons working in the field of humanitarian work/ animal lovers etc.
Government's decision:
In view of the proposal received from the Collector, Gadchiroli, the following persons are being appointed as non-official members on the Management Committee of Animal Clash Prevention Society, Gadchiroli District as per the provisions of clause (a) (3) of the notification dated 14.03.2017 under the said proviso. The term of office of the member shall be three years from the date of his appointment.
