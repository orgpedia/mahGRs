The Proposal:
The Central Government has accepted the recommendations of the B. K. Chaturvedi Committee appointed by the Central Planning Commission. Accordingly, all Centrally Sponsored Schemes have been restructured into 66 Centrally Sponsored Schemes. The Government of India has decided to integrate some of these restructured schemes into a Mission mode from 2014-15 onwards.
Under the National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology (NMOET), a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, Seed Planting and Material Sub-Mission (SMSP) is being implemented.
As per the letter dated 06.06.2018 in Reference No.3, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 408.39 lakh for Centrally Sponsored Village Seed Production Programme (Central Share 60%) for SC/ ST category and Rs. 41.60 lakh for Certified Seed Production Programme (Central Share 60%) under Village Seed Production Programme (CSP). 449.99 lakhs has been made available directly to Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (Mahabeej), Akola, thereby taking the State share (40%) to Rs. 299.99 lakhs for SCs.

As per the letter dated 20th November, 2018 in Reference No.5, the Central Government has provided Rs. 987.35 lakhs directly to Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited (MAHABIZ), Akola for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored" Village Seed Production Programme" in Rabi Season 2018-19 (Central share 60%) for SC farmers, taking the State share (40%) to Rs. 658.23 lakhs.
The State share of Rs. 77.00 lakh was released as per the Government decision dated 18.09.2018 of the Department of Agriculture& Farmers Welfare, Reference No.6 for implementation of" Village Seed Production Programme" and" Certified Seed Production Programme" under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Seed& Planting Material Sub Mission under National Mission on Agriculture Extension& Technology.
In this regard, the State share of Rs. 1105.29 lakh for the Scheduled Castes component of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Seed and Plantation Material Sub Mission (SAMBAM) under National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NAMA) for the year 2018-19 (Central and State share 60: 40) for" Village Seed Production Programme" and" Certified Seed Production Programme" was under consideration.
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 1105.29 lakh is being released to Maharashtra State Seeds Limited (MAHABIJ), Akola for the State share of SC (SC) component (Account Head 2401A 332) for the season 2018-19 (Central and State share 60: 40) for" Village Seed Production Programme" and" Certified Seed Production Programme" under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Seeds and Planting Materials Sub Mission under National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology.
The scheme should be implemented as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the terms and conditions of the release of funds by the Central Government. Care should be taken to ensure that this fund is spent for the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Caste category.
The conditions of the circulars dated 02.04.2018, 27.07.2018 and 05.12.2018 of the Finance Department should be fulfilled.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the approval of the Finance Department vide its Informal Reference No. 28/2019/ Expenditure-1 dated 06/03/2019.
