The Proposal:
Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani is an agricultural university established by Vasantrao Naik. It happened on May 18, 1972. The jurisdiction of Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University covers eight districts. Beed district is located in the Marathwada region and comprises Beed, Shirur (Kasar), Parli Vaijnath, Ambajogai, Kej, Dharur, Gevrai, Majalgaon, Patoda, Ashti and Vadwani taluks.
Beed district's fertile soil and favorable climate make it suitable for the cultivation of various crops. Different crops are grown in the kharif and rabi seasons in Parli Vaijnath taluka. Cotton and soybean are important cash crops during the kharif and rabi seasons. Other crops include maize, wheat, barley, oats, rye, barley, potatoes, turnips, potatoes, turnips, and potatoes. Crops grown include apples, pears, plums, pomegranates, and peaches. The district is known for producing high quality sugarcane which is used for sugar production. Also, Beed district is leading in the production of oilseeds and vegetables. The market in Parli Vaijnath town is quite large and is considered to be a center of grain trade. Parli Vaijnath railway station is located on the broad gauge railway line.
