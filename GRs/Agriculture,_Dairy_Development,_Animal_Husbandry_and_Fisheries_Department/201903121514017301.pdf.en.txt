The Proposal:
Integrated Horticulture Development Mission (IHDM) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the development of crops such as fruits, vegetables, tubers, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew, cocoa and bamboo. The scheme is being implemented in the ratio of 60:40 between Centre and State since 2015-16.
The reference No. 4, the Central Government has released the second installment of Rs. 450.00 lakhs has been released. Following this, the government took decision.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The Central Government vide letter dated 30.01.2019 has released Central share of Rs. 450.00 lakh (Rs. Four Crore Fifty Lakh only) for the second installment of Integrated Horticulture Development Mission for 2018-19 to the Scheduled Tribes.
Under this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. 750.00 lakhs (Rs. Seven Crore Fifty Lakhs) is being distributed to the Scheduled Tribes with Central share of Rs. 450.00 lakhs (Rs. Four Crore Fifty Lakhs) and State share of 40% i.e. 300.00 lakhs (Rs. Three Crore Only) as follows.
