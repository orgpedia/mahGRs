Department of Finance As per the Government decision dated 14.10.2021, the allocation of funds for the year 2021-22 has been approved within the limit of 50% of the budgeted funds for the programme expenditure. Accordingly, the Controller, Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri has issued the above Reference No. A proposal for the distribution of funds under 2 has been submitted. Government Decision: For the financial year 2021-22, Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri, has been sanctioned the remaining 30% grant within the limit of 50% of the sanctioned budgetary provision under the head' 01 Crop Conservation', 24150072, for 2415 Agricultural Research and Education. The Government is sanctioning a grant of Rs. 59,40,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakh Forty Thousand only).
2. The grant in this Government Resolution is being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions:

(1) The funds allocated by the Agricultural Universities under the scheme and subject-wise allocation should be spent only for the permissible aspects of the scheme concerned.
(2) If the amount disbursed is lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the amount so unutilized/ unutilized shall not be diverted or spent for any other group/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(3) The schemes under which the period of the scheme has expired shall not be continued after the relevant period at the expense of the schemes without obtaining the approval of the Government.
(4) The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the financial year concerned, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether the grants-in-aid sanctioned to the agricultural universities are correct as per the grant formula, and how the expenditure is being made in accordance with the prescribed order/ procedure. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
5) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income.
The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
The grants sanctioned to the Universities should be spent within the prescribed limit after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural University Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural University Statutes, 1990, and the Maharashtra Agricultural University Accounts Code, 1991, and in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
The above terms and conditions shall be strictly complied with by the Controller of the University and other relevant competent authorities.
The expenditure for this purpose should be shared within the limits of the monthly fund statement from the provision sanctioned under the head of accounts mentioned in the Budget for 2021-22 and should be spent under the same head of accounts.
The Sub-Divisional Agriculture Officer, Rahuri, G. Ahmednagar, shall take necessary action regarding the payment of the grants sanctioned under the respective sub-headings to the concerned Controller of Agricultural Universities as mentioned in the Government Decision No.AZIU 1072/18471/ V, dated 27th September, 1972 and Government Decision No.AZIU-2003/ CR- 35/20 O, dated 25/4/2003.
