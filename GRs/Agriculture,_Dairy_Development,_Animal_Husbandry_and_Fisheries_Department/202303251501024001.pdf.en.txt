Preamble: For the farmers who are not eligible to cultivate orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, the State Government has issued the above Reference No. 1 decision of the government. Bhausaheb Phundkar has started a fruit plantation scheme. Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of the said scheme in the year 2022-23 as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3. 4200.00 lakhs has been disbursed to Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune as per the demand received from Director (Horticulture), Commissionerate of Agriculture vide letter No. 4, vide Government Order No. 5, as per reference.
The Planning Department and the Finance Department were requested to make available to the Department for disbursement of the remaining funds of the general category in the fund demand made under Reference No. 4. Accordingly, the Finance Department has agreed to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 700.00 lakhs out of the remaining funds. The matter was under consideration of the Government to disburse the said fund to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decisions: 1. Approval is being given to make available funds to the tune of Rs. 700.00 (Rupees Seven Hundred Lakh only) for the general category to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune on Budget Distribution System for implementation of Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Plantation Scheme in the year 2022-23.
