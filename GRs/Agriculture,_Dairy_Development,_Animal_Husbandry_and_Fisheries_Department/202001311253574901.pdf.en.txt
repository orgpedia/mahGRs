The Proposal:
Implementation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
The season has been running since 2016. The proposed scheme will be implemented in the state as per the guidelines received from the Central Government regarding the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana. The government is considering to form a cabinet sub-committee to resolve the problems and complaints received while implementing the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana and Fruit Crop Insurance Scheme in the state and to implement the scheme effectively.
It was followed. As per the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 15th January, 2020, the following Government Decisions are being issued:
Government's decision:
Cabinet sub-committee is being constituted for effective implementation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana and Fruit Crop Insurance Scheme in the State as under:

The Cabinet sub-committee will consider the following issues and take a decision to formulate a remedial plan.
1) To take measures to take practical policy decisions in the districts where the insurance company could not be appointed for Rabi 2019.
(ii) To suggest and decide on the measures to be taken in respect of Crop Insurance Scheme and Fruit Crop Insurance Scheme, considering the possibility of a similar situation in Kharif 2020.
3) Suggest measures to remove various deficiencies under the scheme at present.
