Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State Government is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2018 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent.
The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the year 2018 for 5 crops namely Orange, Moong, Pomegranate, Guava and Chikoo (Mrig Bahar) in the district by holding the revenue board component.
Reference No. 1 for re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2018 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 1. As per the demand of the participating companies for 2018 Mrig Bahar in the scheme, the demand for State share subsidy was under the consideration of the Government to give financial approval to the amount of State share as submitted by the Commissionerate of Agriculture.
