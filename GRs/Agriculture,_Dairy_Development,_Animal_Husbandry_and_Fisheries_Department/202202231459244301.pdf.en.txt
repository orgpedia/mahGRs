Circular:
The government has decided to implement the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana. State Level Project Review Committee constituted on 15th February, 2019. Chief Secretary, Maharashtra. In pursuance of the directions received in the meeting held on 20.1.2022, the following instructions are being issued:
To carry out the work of data correction of pending applications under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana, the concerned farmers should be informed about the reasons for which their application is pending by sending a message through NIC. To complete the data correction work and provide immediate benefits to the eligible beneficiaries, a camp should be organized at the village level on the fourth Friday of March in the presence of Talathi, Gram Sevak, Agriculture Assistant until the data of all eligible beneficiaries is corrected. Applicants should bring with them all the necessary documents such as 7/12 extract, Aadhaar card, bank passbook, etc.
The work of data correction should be carried out under the leadership of the Collector in coordination with the Revenue, Agriculture and Rural Development departments. Also, the concerned district collector should take necessary action in order to organize the camp successfully by publicizing the campaign for this.
2) Under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana, physical verification of the beneficiaries of the scheme should be done at the village level through Krishi Mitras by giving honorarium from the amount received for administrative expenses. After physical verification of the beneficiaries through Krishi Mitras, cross checking should be done through Talathi, Krishi Sevak and Gram Sevak and the physical verification form should be submitted to the Tehsil Office and the inspection should be completed 100%. The detailed procedure in this regard should be decided by the Commissioner (Agriculture).
3) The Revenue Department under the control of the Collector should carry out the work of recovering the benefit given to the beneficiaries who are ineligible due to income tax and other reasons. The funds collected under this scheme should be immediately transferred to the central government.
4) The social audit of the beneficiaries of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Yojana should be completed under the leadership of the District Collector in coordination with the Revenue Department, Rural Development Department and Agriculture Department by displaying the beneficiary lists at the village level and taking certificates at the village level as per the instructions of the Central Government.
