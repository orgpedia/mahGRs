Introduction: The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Samman Nidhi (PM KISAN) scheme to provide assured income to farmers. (1) It shall be implemented in the State by the decision of the Government. (2) The procedure for implementation of the scheme has been revised in accordance with the Government Decision.
In the Budget Speech of Hon'ble Finance Minister for the year 2023-24, it has been announced to implement the scheme of" Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi," which will add the subsidy of the State Government to the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Samman Nidhi Yojana, to increase the income of the Annadata Baliraj. The state government is implementing the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme. This has been approved in the Cabinet meeting held on 30.05.2023. Following this, the government took decision on:
Government's decision:
As announced in the Budget Speech of 2023-24, the scheme of" Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi," which will supplement the state government's subsidy under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Samman Nidhi Yojana for increasing the income of Annadata Baliraj, is being approved to be implemented from 2023-24 as follows.
The eligibility and eligibility criteria for the scheme are as follows:
(i) The guidelines of the Central Government for the said scheme shall be taken into account.
Centre and States registered on PM Kisan Portal under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

All the beneficiaries who are eligible for the benefit as per the government norms will be eligible for the benefit of the Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi scheme. Also, the changes made by the Central Government from time to time in the criteria for eligibility of beneficiaries will also be applicable to the beneficiaries of the Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi scheme with immediate effect. There will be no need to issue a separate government decision from the Maharashtra government for these changes.
Eligible beneficiaries who have been newly registered on the PM Kisan portal will also be the beneficiaries of this scheme.
2. the plan of action:
The beneficiaries who are eligible for the benefit at the time of disbursement of each installment under the PFMS system of PM Kisan Yojana will be eligible for the benefit of Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi Yojana. The funds will be transferred directly to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through the portal/ system to be developed by the State Government.
3. Namo Shetkari Mahasman Nidhi Yojana Portal/ System: Since only beneficiaries of PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana will be given benefits from the state funds under Namo Shetkari Mahasman Nidhi Yojana, the Department of Agriculture and Department of Information Technology for the state should jointly take action to develop a portal/ system for the scheme.
With the consent of the Central Government, the portals/ systems of both PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana and Namo Kisan Mahasamman Nidhi Yojana should be integrated so that the change in the number of beneficiaries eligible for subsidy can be realized on both portals simultaneously.
4. Mode of disbursement of funds: Under the scheme of" Namo Kisan Mahasman Nidhi Yojana," Government of India has launched PM-KISAN Yojana.
As per the scheme, the benefit will be disbursed through Commissioner (Agriculture) through direct transfer to the bank account of eligible beneficiaries as per the following schedule.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | the number of weeks | period of time          | amount     |
| 1    | The 1st week        | From April to July      | Rs. 2000/- |
| 2    | The 2nd week        | From August to November | Rs. 2000/- |
| 3    | The 3rd week        | From December to March  | Rs. 2000/- |
---------------------------------------------------------------------
5. Recovery to be made if ineligible beneficiary is provided benefit under the scheme:
If the benefit is provided to ineligible beneficiaries under this scheme, the recovery to be made from the beneficiary should be done through the revenue system and deposited to the government through the Commissioner (Agriculture).

Project Control Room: (1) As per the decision of the Government, a Project Control Room has been set up to implement the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana in the State. The project control room will implement and monitor the implementation of the Namo Farmers" Fund.
(2) The control of manpower and other necessary activities in the scheme should be done by the Project Control Room of the State.
Administrative expenses: The amount of up to 1% of the annual provision as required for manpower to be taken by external agencies as per the Government decisions issued by the Finance Department/ Government from time to time, including the administrative expenses of the Namo Kshetra Mahasman Nidhi Yojana.
8. Controlling Committee for implementation:
Under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana, the control of this scheme should be done through the control committees formed at the village level, taluka level, district level and state level.
9. Appointment of Nodal Officer: Under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme, officers appointed as Nodal Officers at village level, taluka level, district level, block level and state level will look after the work of Nodal Officer for the scheme.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for the strict implementation of the scheme.
The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be required to review the implementation of the scheme every three months and submit a progress report to the government.
Detailed guidelines on the scheme should be issued by the Commissioner (Agriculture).
