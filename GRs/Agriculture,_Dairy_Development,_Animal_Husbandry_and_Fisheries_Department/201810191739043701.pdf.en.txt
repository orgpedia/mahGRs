the prelude
The Department of Agriculture organizes study tours outside the country for the farmers of the state to know about the developed and modern technologies of different developed countries, to know the export of agricultural products/ marketing of agricultural products and market demand, to process agricultural goods as well as to know the latest technologies used in those countries and to help the farmers of the state to use them on their farm/ farm, to know about the agricultural technologies developed by different countries and related aspects by talking to the farmers of that country directly and to increase the knowledge and capacity of the farmers through field visits, visits to the concerned institutions, etc. The scheme has been in operation since 2004-2005.
During 2018-19, Rs. Rs. 50.00 crore has been allocated for this. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of this fund and the Finance Department. As per the powers conferred on the Department vide Circular dated 2nd April, 2018, 70% of the sanctioned allocation i.e. Rs. 35.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the decision.
