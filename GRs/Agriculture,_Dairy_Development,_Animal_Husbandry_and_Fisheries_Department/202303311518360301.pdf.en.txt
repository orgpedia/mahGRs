Preamble: Administrative approval has been accorded for implementation of National Horticulture Mission under Krishi Unnati Yojana-Integrated Horticulture Development Mission in the State for the year 2022-2023 with a budget outlay of Rs. 19933.97 lakh under Government Decision No. 1. The first and second installments of the general category have been released under the government decision at reference number 2 and 3 respectively. Thereafter, vide letter No.5, the third instalment of General Category funds has been received from the Central Government for the financial year 2022-2023. In this background, the matter of disbursement of the third installment of general category funds to the Commissioner (Agriculture) and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune was under consideration of the Government. The government is taking the following steps:
