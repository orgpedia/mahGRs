Read:- (1) Finance Department Government Resolution No. Shasau-2019/ No.13/ CE dated 04.08.2021 (2) Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation, Ltd. The letter No. PUAMMENVVIM/ PRASH/ 836/2021, dated 27.08.2021 Preamble: Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation Ltd. as State Government employees. The Government was considering to implement the revised pay scale as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission to the officers/ employees of the establishment.
Government Decision: Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation Ltd. The Government is approving to implement the revised pay structure from 1st July, 2021 as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission as mentioned in Annexure-A and Annexure-B attached with 301 posts in Sheep and Goat Development Sector and 14 posts in Traders Scheme at the Headquarter of the Corporation subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: (1) The notification dated 30th January, 2019 and the circular dated 20th February, 2019 issued by the Finance Department in respect of the 7th Pay Commission and the terms and conditions of the Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Government from time to time shall be adopted as guidelines and accordingly the revised pay scales shall be implemented with effect from 1st July, 2021.
(2) In giving the benefit of the revised pay scale, the benefit to the officers/ employees of the corporation shall not be higher than the pay scale applied to the equivalent posts in the government.
The revised pay scales should be applied within that limit.

(3) The annual accounts of the Corporation shall be laid before the Legislature within the prescribed time limit.
(4) The creation of vacancies/ new posts in the Corporation shall be subject to the approval of the Government.
(5) As per the rate of dividend declared by the Government (at least 5%), the Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation shall be required to pay to the Government regularly at least 5% or more of the profit received in every financial year.
(6) Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation to implement the schemes in coordination with the central and state level agencies in a time-bound manner.
Prepare a program and take appropriate measures to realize the projected profit as per the projected profit figures submitted by the Corporation.
And implement the plans.
The expenditure for 301 posts in the sheep and goat development sector of the corporation at the revised rate of pay from 1.7.2021, as well as the amount of the difference in the pay increase due from 1.7.2021 onwards, should be made from the government's subsidy.
Similarly, the expenditure for the 14 posts in the Trader Scheme at the headquarters of the Corporation at the revised rate of pay with effect from 1.7.2021, as well as the amount of difference of increment payable from 1.7.2021 onwards, shall be met from the Trader Programme of the Corporation.
