Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government Resolution No. 01/11-O, dated 22.05.2019
Preamble: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is being implemented in all the districts of the state for the notified crops in the notified areas under the decision of the government in the Kharif season 2019, through insurance companies approved by the central government.
However, since a large number of complaints were being received from various public representatives and farmers at the local level regarding the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, the government was considering the formation of a Grievance Redressal Committee at the taluka level as well, with a view to resolving these complaints at the local level.
Government Decision: With a view to resolving the complaints received from various people's representatives and farmers at the local level regarding the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, the Government is approving the formation of the following Grievance Redressal Committee under the chairmanship of the Tehsildar at the Taluka level:
1) Tehsildar: 2) Block Development Officer, Zila Parishad: 3) Taluk Agriculture Officer: 4) Farmer Representative (2): Member 5) Taluk Level Representative of Agrani Bank: Member 6) District Central Cooperative Bank Representative: Member 7) Representative of Insurance Company concerned: Member 8) Representative of your Government Service Centre Operators (2): Member 9) Taluk Agriculture Officer: Member Secretary

Duties and Responsibilities of the Committee:
(i) To take action for redressal of grievances of farmers received in connection with the implementation of the scheme, subject to the guidelines of the scheme.
2) To take action on complaints received regarding the scheme.
3) To keep control over the work of your government service center at the taluk level.
4) To regulate the participation in the scheme by all nationalized/ private/ co-operative bank branches in the taluk.
5) Verify the complaints regarding registration and make recommendations to the department level/ district level committee as required.
