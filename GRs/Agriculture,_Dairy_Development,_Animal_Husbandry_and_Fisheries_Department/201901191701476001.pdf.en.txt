The Proposal:
The World Bank funded Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project (POCRA) is being implemented in a total of 5142 villages, 4210 villages in Vidarbha and Marathwada and 932 villages in the mangrove belt of Purna river basin in Vidarbha, which are highly vulnerable to climate change.
The government has decided to implement the project. Initially 23 posts were sanctioned as on 10.08.2016. Thereafter, the government took decision on it. As on 28.09.2017, a total of 805 posts were sanctioned for the project out of 782 new posts sanctioned and 290 posts were sanctioned to be filled in the first phase.
The Project Director, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project had submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference No.5 to fill up the remaining sanctioned 492 posts required for the next phase of the project. In pursuance of this proposal, the Sub-Committee comprising of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department met on 7.01.2019 and approved filling up of 14 posts from regular officers/ staff (by appointment/ deputation) and 478 posts on contract basis through external mechanism in the second phase of the project. In line with this approval, the Government is taking a decision to approve the filling of posts in the second phase for Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project as follows:
