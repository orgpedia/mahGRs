Objectives of the Project:
(i) Promotion and conservation of indigenous breeds of cows and buffaloes.
2) Rapid genetic improvement in economically important breeds of cows and buffaloes in the country.
3) Promote selective breeding of high milk yielding breeds of cows and buffaloes 4) Increase milk productivity of cows and buffaloes.
5) To improve the economic condition of milk producers.
6) Supply of male calves to the semen laboratory at Goti for semen production of high milk yielding improved breeds of cows and buffaloes.
