Ma./ 10604/2021, D. 18.03.2021 Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2020 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent.
The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the year 2020 for 6 crops namely Orange, Mango, Pomegranate, Guava, Chikoo and Lemon (Mrig Bahar) in the district by holding the revenue board component.
Re-designed weather based crop insurance scheme Mrig Bahar under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2020 The administrative approval has been given as per the government decisions in 1 and 2. It was under consideration of the government to disburse the state share amount as submitted by the Commissionerate to the participating companies for the year 2020 Mrig Bahara in the scheme. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: Considering the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate for the restructured weather based Fruit Crop Insurance Scheme Mrig Bahar 2020, the Agriculture Insurance Company of India has been allocated an amount of Rs. 29,46,89, 839/- is being approved by the Government.
