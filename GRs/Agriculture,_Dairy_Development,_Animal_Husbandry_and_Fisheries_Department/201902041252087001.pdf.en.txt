Section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983 provides that" every University shall be competent to acquire and hold property, both movable and immovable, to lease, sell or otherwise transfer or dispose of any movable or immovable property which is vested in or acquired by it for the purposes of the University and to do all other things as may be necessary for the purposes of this Act."
As per this provision, Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University Executive Council In the meeting held on 8.12.2018, Resolution No. 59/8.12.2018, the Executive Council of the University has in principle recommended to make available the land of Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani under the Agricultural College, Latur for the Government Multispecialty Hospital, Latur, taking into consideration the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983 and the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Ordinance, 1990, the Government Decisions and Standing Orders issued in this regard from time to time and for the purpose of the subject matter under the exclusive authority of the Government. Terms and conditions
1) The amount that comes according to the prevailing market price table of the land should be made available for the development of the agricultural college, Latur and the university.
2) In this case. If the High Court or other court orders to pay the excess amount to the concerned, the amount should be compensated by the District Hospital, Latur and alternatively by the Public Health Department.
(3) The land shall be used for the purpose for which it was transferred and shall not be transferred to any third party.
(4) The Agricultural University shall be entitled to take possession of the land in case of violation of the above conditions.
