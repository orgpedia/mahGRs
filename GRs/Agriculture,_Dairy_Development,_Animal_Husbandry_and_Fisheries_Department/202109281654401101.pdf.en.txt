Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme for the year 2020-21 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent.
The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide crop compensation to the farmers, a restructured weather-based crop insurance scheme for the year 2020-21 for 8 crops namely Orange, Cashew, Pomegranate, Mango, Banana, Grapefruit and Strawberry (Ambia Bahar) on pilot basis has been launched in the district by the Board of Revenue through its constituent entities namely HDFC ERGO General Insurance Co.
