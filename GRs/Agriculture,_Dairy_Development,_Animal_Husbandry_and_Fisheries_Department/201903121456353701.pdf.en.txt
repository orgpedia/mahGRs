The Proposal:
The state government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme for farmers who are not eligible to cultivate fruit orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The scheme will be implemented during 2018-19. 1 Administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 06/07/2018.
Decision of the Government: 1. During the financial year 2018-19, Rs. 84.4970 lakh (Rupees Eighty Four Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand Seven Hundred only) is being made available to the Commissioner Agriculture on Budget Distribution System.
(2) The funds shall be spent out of the provisions budgeted for the current year under the following headings:
Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection (00) 789 Scheduled Castes Component Scheme (00) (33) Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme- Scheduled Castes Component Programme (2401 A904) 33 Financial Assistance.
(4) It shall be ensured that the benefit of the said provisions is extended only to the beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist sections.
While spending the funds, the guidelines and prevailing terms and conditions issued by the Planning and Finance Department and the instructions given from time to time should be strictly followed as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book and Financial Rights Rule Book.
