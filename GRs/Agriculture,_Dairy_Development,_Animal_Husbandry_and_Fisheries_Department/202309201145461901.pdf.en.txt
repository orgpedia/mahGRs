Various development programmes/ schemes are implemented by various administrative departments of the Government of Maharashtra and their subordinate field offices. The implementation of these development schemes/ programmes as well as the functioning of the office, the decision making process are evaluated and through the evaluation exercise, the success of the implementation of the schemes and the shortcomings in the functioning/ decision making are identified. To address these gaps and suggest measures to improve the decision-making process and make the schemes/ programmes reach the common man better and more effectively, the Quality Council of India (QCI), a tertiary agency, has been mandated to conduct the evaluation. The approval has been given in accordance with the Government Resolution dated April 27, 2023.
