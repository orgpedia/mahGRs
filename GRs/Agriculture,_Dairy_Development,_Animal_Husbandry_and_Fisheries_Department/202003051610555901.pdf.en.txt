Preamble: Government Resolution No. Under 1, the undergraduate courses under the Agricultural University have been declared as professional courses. Council of Agriculture. In the 94th meeting held on 11.9.2017, Resolution No. 13/94/2017 A resolution has been passed and recommended to the Government to declare Diploma in Agriculture, Agribusiness Management and Animal Husbandry Degree Courses, Post Graduate Degree in Agriculture and Acharya Degree Courses as Professional Courses. Further, in the 98th meeting of the Council of Agriculture, a resolution was passed regarding the declaration of postgraduate and master degree courses related to professional degree courses under agricultural universities as professional. 3/98/2019 has been passed and recommended to the Government. The Agricultural Council No. A proposal has been submitted to the government under section 3 and 4.
The views of the Planning and Finance Department have been obtained in pursuance of the said proposal. Since these courses are not declared professional, there is resentment among a large number of students as the tuition fees are not reimbursed to the students of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Vimukt Jati nomadic tribes along with the children of economically weaker sections of the open cadre. Accordingly, the matter of declaring courses under agricultural universities as professional was under the consideration of the government.
