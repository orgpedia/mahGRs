The Proposal:
The special dairy project to be implemented in the Vidarbha and Marathwada regions has been given administrative approval under the government decision in Wacha-1, which covered a total of 3,027 villages in 11 districts. Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Shri. As per the letter dated 2.04.2018, out of the above 3,027 villages, some villages have been de-notified, some villages have been de-notified and since there are a total of 91 such villages, a proposal has been submitted to the Government to exclude these villages from the purview of the project. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Under the special dairy project being implemented in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions, out of a total of 3,027 villages in 11 districts, a total of 91 villages mentioned in the annexure are being excluded, leaving a total of 2,936 villages in the project area.
