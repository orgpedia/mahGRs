Read:- 1. Government Resolution, Department of Agriculture and Padum, No. 165/9-O, D. 07.09.2020 2. Ma. Minister of Agriculture. Letter dated 22.10.2020
Preamble: To advise the government to decide the turmeric research and processing policy considering the difficulties faced by the farmers from turmeric cultivation to processing and sale. A Study Committee was constituted under the Government Resolution dated 07.09.2020. The government was considering including new members in the study committee. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: In order to determine the turmeric research and processing policy considering the difficulties faced by the farmers from the cultivation of turmeric crop to processing and sale, a meeting was held under the chairmanship of Mr. Hemant Patil, Member of Parliament, Hingoli Lok Sabha Constituency. The following members are being re-inducted in the Study Committee constituted under the Government decision dated 07.09.2020.
