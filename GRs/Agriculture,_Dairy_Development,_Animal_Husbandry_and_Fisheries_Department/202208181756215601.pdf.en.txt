Preamble- Agriculture Extension Sub Mission is being implemented under the Centrally Sponsored National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology (NMAET). Under this sub-mission, Assistance for Reforms in State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) is implemented in the ratio of 60:40 funding from the Central and State Governments. The total outlay for implementation of the scheme in 2022-23 is Rs. The State Agriculture Extension Work Plan (SEWP) for an amount of Rs. 15318.19 lakh has been prepared and the Commissionerate of Agriculture has prepared the detailed action plan in this regard. The letter dated 28.04.2022 has been submitted to the Government for approval. The State Project Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) meeting was held on 05.07.2022. The meeting approved the annual action plan of the SLSC in this regard.
Accordingly, under this government decision, the matter of approving the action plan submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate for the implementation of the scheme was under the consideration of the government. Government Decision: Administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of the Scheme of Assistance for Reforms in Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) of the State under the Sub-Mission on Agricultural Extension of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET) during 2022-23.
2. The total amount of Rs. The State Agriculture Extension Action Plan for an amount of Rs. 15318.19 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Five Crores Eighteen Lakhs and Nineteen Thousand only) is being accorded administrative approval.
