Introduction: In order to increase the production and productivity of various crops in the agricultural sector, it is necessary to make necessary efforts to provide certified/ truthful seeds of high quality and high yielding varieties to the farmers at the local level. For this, there is an urgent need to provide the necessary facilities to the farmers at the village level to sow various crops. As per the recommendations of the Working Group of Secretaries on Seed Sector Reforms at the Central Government level, the Central Government vide letter dated 13th October, 2017, has decided to establish Farmers Producer Companies (FPOs)/ Self Help Groups (SHGs)/ Foodgrain producers at Gram Panchayat level under the Seed and Plantation Material Sub-Mission (SMSP).
