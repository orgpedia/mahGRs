Introduction: The Animal Husbandry and Dairying Department under the Commissioner, Dairying, for the Mumbai-Ahmedabad High Speed Rail Project, has set up a Animal Husbandry and Dairying Centre at Survey No. 0.46.03 H.R. of 672 and Survey No. Request for transfer of 0.36.69 HaR of 154 to Commissioner, Dairy Development Read No. 2 was written here. In pursuance of this request, the Commissioner, Dairying has proposed to make the said land available for High Speed Rail Project. 1 submitted to the government. The proposal had been approved by the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Animal Husbandry and Breeding Centre under the Commissioner, Dairy Development, Maharashtra State, is located at Survey No. 0.46.03 H.R. of 672 and Survey No.
Considering the consent of the Commissioner, Dairy Development, to transfer 0.36.69 ha out of 154 ha to High Speed Railway Project, the said area is being handed over to the Revenue and Forest Department subject to the condition that the amount assessed as under by the Joint District Registrar, Class-I and Stamp Collector, Palghar, while transferring the same to High Speed Railway Project, should be received from the High Speed Railway Project.
