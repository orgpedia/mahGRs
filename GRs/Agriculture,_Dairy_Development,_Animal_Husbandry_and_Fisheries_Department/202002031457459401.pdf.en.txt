The University Grants Commission (UGC) vide Notification dated 30th June, 2010, has prescribed the minimum educational qualification for appointment to the posts of Teachers and Teacher Equivalents in Central Universities and Colleges/ Institutions and the measures to be taken to maintain quality in higher education. Instructions have also been given regarding pay scale, collateral benefits, incentive pay hike etc. for the teachers in these posts.
The notification of the University Grants Commission dated 30th June 2010 has been published in the Gazette of India dated 18-24 th September 2010. The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has issued a circular to the Maharashtra Veterinary and Fisheries Sciences University (MAFSU) and the constituent colleges of the university. No.7 is given hereunder vide letter dated 2/08/2010. Accordingly, the posts of teachers and equivalent posts of teachers in the Maharashtra Animal and Fisheries Sciences University and constituent colleges have been notified in the notification of the University Grants Commission dated 30th June, 2010 vide Para No. Subject to the provisions of Articles 6.0.4, 11.0, 16.0 and 17.0. The Dinakas at No. 8 have been implemented by the Government Decision dated September 27, 2018.
