| The Proposal -                                                                                                                       |
| Financial stability of farmers if farmers' fruit crops are insured against weather hazards                                           |
| Priority under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana in the state is to provide assistance from the point of view of sustainability.  |
| Restructured weather-based Fruitful Insurance Scheme is being implemented. Due to various weather hazards                            |
| The productivity of the fruit is adversely affected, leading to a large-scale decline in production.                                 |
| The farmers face a financial crisis as they do not get the expected yield.                                                           |
| Under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, as a measure to provide farmers with fruitful compensation                               |
| Restructured Weather-Based Fruit Insurance Scheme in the State for 2 years in the year 2024-25, 2025-26                              |
| Bahara has 26 districts for 8 fruits: oranges, pomegranates, pomegranates, chikoo, guava, lemon, citrus, and grapes (C).             |
| The Board of Revenue consists of the Agricultural Insurance Company of India, Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, Future        |
| Through General Insurance Company and Universal Sompo General Insurance Company at Reference No.1                                    |
| The decision is being taken by the government.                                                                                       |
