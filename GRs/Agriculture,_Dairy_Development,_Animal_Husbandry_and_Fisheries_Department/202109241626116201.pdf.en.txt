Guidance on taking appropriate cognizance of self-immolation/ suicide or other types of letters.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries Development
Government Circular No. Parichay- 2021/235/ PR No. 08/ Padum- 17
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is September 24, 2021
Government circular
It has come to the notice of the Government that some persons/ organizations are giving suicide/ suicide or other similar kind of letter for some reason. But after receiving the letter, due to not noticing it at the right time or ignoring it, some untoward incident may occur. For this, the concerned Assistant Commissioner/ Regional Deputy Commissioner/ Commissioner/ Managing Director of the corporations should personally take timely cognizance of such letters. On receipt of such letter, immediate action should be taken as follows: 1. Letter should be issued to all police stations concerned to take appropriate preventive action. Also, the concerned Commissioner of Police/ Deputy Commissioner of Police/ Inspector of Police/ Sub-Inspector of Police/ Police Station should be informed in writing within 24 hours of receiving the letter regarding self-immolation/ suicide.
2. Police bandobast should be made in and around the concerned office as a precautionary measure.
3.The Government/ Office should inform the stakeholders in writing about the reasons for which such steps have been taken. If necessary, their demands are fulfilled by hearing them and if they are in accordance with the government decision/ circular/ law/ rule, or if they are not in accordance with the law, they should be informed in writing to the concerned and efforts should be made to discourage them from committing suicide/ self-immolation. If they do not desist from committing suicide/ self-immolation even after making efforts, appropriate preventive action should be taken against them by the police and the action taken report should be immediately reported to the Government/ Commissioner's office. A report will also be submitted to the Home Department in this regard.
(2) If such correspondence relates to the office of the Commissioner, the Commissioner shall take all reasonable precautions as aforesaid. Also, if such correspondence pertains to the Ministry, the Joint Secretary/ Deputy
Secretary as the nodal officer shall take appropriate precautions and measures and shall inform the security officer of the Ministry in writing and shall take appropriate preventive/ necessary measures in liaison with them and ensure that no untoward incident takes place. A report in this regard will be submitted to the Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ A.M.S. (Padum) immediately.
