Circular:
As per the notification dated 26.03.2001 issued by the Central Government, provisions have been made for the issue of transport and health certificates of animals. Accordingly, vide Government Circular dated 11.08.2014, under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960, as per the provisions of the Transport of Animals Rules, 1978, the competent authorities of the State were authorised to issue transport and health certificates to bovine and buffalo animals as per Rule No. 47 and 96.
Now, as per the provisions of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 read with Rules No. 65 and 96 of the Transport of Animals Rules, 1978, the officers mentioned in the circular dated 11.08.2014 are being authorized to issue transport and health certificates to the goats and sheep.
(3) The competent authority shall, under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960, issue transport and health certificate to the goats and sheep as mentioned in Schedule-J (Rule-65) and Rule-96 of the Transport of Animals Rules, 1978, in accordance with the attached form.
4. The Government of this Department has decided to issue a certificate after conducting medical examination of goats and sheep.
Service charge as on 1099/ PR No.183/ Padum-4, dated 06.04.2000 and 04.01.2001.
