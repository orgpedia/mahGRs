The Proposal:
Budget 2022-23 The Prime Minister and The Minister (Finance) said," As Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agriculture University, Parbhani and Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli are completing 50 years, they will be given Rs. A special grant/ fund of Rs. 50.00 crore will be provided. Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-23, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli has been sanctioned Rs. 28.00 crore out of which Rs. A sum of Rs. 25.00 crore will be provided for research.
In this regard, the agricultural universities of the state were informed to submit research proposals. Accordingly, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli, has allocated a total of Rs. 2500.00 lakhs has been received by the Government through Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the proposals for research and to disburse funds for research in the financial year 2022-23.
Government's decision:
In order to speed up the research work in the agricultural universities of the state, Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Agricultural University, Dapoli, a total of 13 research proposals of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 2500.00 lakhs.
