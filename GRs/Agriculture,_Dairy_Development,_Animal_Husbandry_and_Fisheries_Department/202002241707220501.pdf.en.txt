The Proposal:
For the year 2019-20, the Central Government has released Rs.200 crore under the National Food Security Mission for commercial crops (cotton, sugarcane). As per the letter dated 09/04/2019, an action plan for an amount of Rs. 872.58 lakh has been approved. 523.55 Lakhs+ State share of Rs. 349.03 lakhs). The administrative approval has been granted as per the Government Decision dated 03.06.2019.
Under the National Food Security Mission, central and state share of general category funds for the first installment of commercial (cotton, sugarcane) crops will be provided. As per the demand made vide letter dated 04/02/2020, the Government is taking the following decisions regarding the release of required funds from the Central and State share
