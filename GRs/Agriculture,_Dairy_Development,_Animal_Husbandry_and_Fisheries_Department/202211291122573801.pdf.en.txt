Government's decision
Under the Centrally Sponsored National Millets Programme, two different heads of accounts have already been made available for 40% State share. As the remaining 60 per cent of the central share of funds under the programme is directly contributed by the central government to the Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur, the accounting for 60 per cent of the central share was not made available. However, the Centre No. As per the letter, 60 per cent of the central share will be provided by the central government through the PFMS system. The Government is approving two new heads of accounts as follows for 60% central share required for mapping of the scheme on PFMS system.
