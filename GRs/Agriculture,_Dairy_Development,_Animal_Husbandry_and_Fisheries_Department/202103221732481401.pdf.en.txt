Ma./ 10604/2021, Dy. 18.03.2021 Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2020-21 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent.
The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide crop compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2020-21 has been implemented for 8 crops namely Orange, Moong, Cashew, Pomegranate, Mango, Banana, Grape and Strawberry (Ambia Bahar) on pilot basis in the district by holding the revenue board component.
Reference No. 1 for implementation of Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana in 2020-21 The administrative approval has been given as per the government decisions in 1 and 2. The Commissionerate of Agriculture has submitted a request for release of funds towards State share subsidy and administrative expenses as demanded by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for the year 2020-21 for Ambia Bahara under the scheme. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: Considering the demand submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate under the restructured weather based crop insurance scheme Ambia Bahar 2020-2021, the amount of Rs. 10,65,30, 652/- is being approved by the Government. The costs incurred in this regard shall be covered under the following headings:
