Maharashtra Public Service Commission. vide letter dated 09.10.2017 to the Livestock Development Officer, Maharashtra Animal Husbandry Service (Advertisement No. 24/2017), 69 candidates have been selected and recommended to the Government for appointment. Out of the total 69 candidates recommended by the Commission, 67 candidates whose medical examination, conduct and antecedent report have been received, have been provisionally appointed to the post of Livestock Development Officer, Maharashtra Animal Husbandry Service (Group-A) (Pay Scale 15,600-39, 100 Grade Pay-5400) in the Department of Animal Husbandry as per the definition of the term subject to the purview prescribed in Rule (43) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981. Of these 69 candidates, Dr Jagdish Shivaji Bagul (G. No. 49) was not issued his appointment order as the police verification report of his residence in Nagpur was not received. However, the police report of the said period has been received by the government and orders for the appointment of Dr Bagul are being issued.
The term of appointment of the candidates will be for two years. The first increment will be given after the completion of the probation period of 1 year and the next increment will be given after the completion of the probation period.
