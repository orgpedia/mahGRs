Preamble: In the wake of the locust infestation in Gujarat and Rajasthan, the government was considering to approve the use of funds for locust control from the funds sanctioned for pest control in emergency situations under the CropSap scheme being implemented in the state, as it was necessary to take precautionary measures to prevent damage to the state due to this pest infestation.
Government's decision:
Pest Disease Surveillance and Advisory Project (CropSAP) scheme is being implemented for management of pests and diseases on major crops and fruit crops. Under the Government decision of 2019-20, the scheme has been approved to be implemented through convergence of various schemes. Rs 20 crore has been set aside for this. Commissioner's Office No. As per letter (4), the funds required for locust control are being approved to be utilised from the funds sanctioned for pest control in emergency situations under CropShape Scheme as well as from the savings on other aspects.
The scheme should be implemented as per the guidelines contained in the Government Resolution dated 31st May, 2019.
