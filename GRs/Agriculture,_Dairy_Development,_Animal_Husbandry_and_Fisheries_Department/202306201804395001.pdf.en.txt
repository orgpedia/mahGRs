The proposal-
The scheme is being implemented under the National Mission on Agriculture Extension and Technology (NMAET). Under this sub-mission, Assistance for Reforms in State Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) is implemented in the ratio of 60:40 funding from the Central and State Governments. The total outlay for the scheme for 2023-24 is Rs. The State Agriculture Extension Work Plan (SEWP) has been prepared at a cost of Rs. 6014.58 lakh. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. The State Project Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) meeting was held on 17.05.2023. The meeting approved the annual action plan of the SLSC in this regard. The Commissioner of Agriculture, in the context of the above, As per the letter dated 16.06.2023, the plan has been submitted to the government for administrative approval.
Accordingly, under this government decision, the matter of approving the action plan submitted by the Agriculture Commissionerate for the implementation of the scheme was under the consideration of the government. The government is taking the following steps:
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being accorded for implementation of the Scheme of Assistance for Reforms in Agricultural Extension Programmes (ATMA) of the State under the Sub-Mission on Agricultural Extension of the Centrally Sponsored National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET) in 2023-24.
