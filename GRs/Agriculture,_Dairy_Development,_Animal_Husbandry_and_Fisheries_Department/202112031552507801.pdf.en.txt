The Proposal:
Accidents due to natural calamities like lightning, flood, snakebite, scorpion bite, electric shock, road accident, vehicle accident, and any other cause, while doing agricultural business, cause death or disability to many farmers. A car accident can cause a family to lose income and create a difficult situation. In order to provide financial benefit to such accident-affected farmers/ their families, all the tenant farmers of the state and any 1 member of the family of the farmer who is not registered as a tenant account holder (parents, spouse of the farmer, son and unmarried daughter), for a total of 2 persons between the age of 10 to 75 years, Gopinath Munde Farmers Accident Insurance Scheme Reference No. (1) The scheme is being implemented as per the decision of the Government. Guidelines and guidelines have been issued. The government has issued a circular in this regard. Changes/ amendments were under consideration of the Government in 19.09.2019.
