S/ o Puh Phi Vio/ A.Ma/ 24624/2022, Dy. 14/07/2022 Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help the farmers in maintaining their financial stability, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme has been implemented in the State for the three years 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24 for 8 fruit crops namely Orange, Pomegranate, Pomegranate, Chikoo, Guava, Lemon, Saffron and Grape (C) (Mrig Bahar) in 26 districts by the Revenue Department.
Reference No. 1 for re-designed Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2021 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 1. The Commissionerate has issued Reference No. 6 of the State share of Rs. 42,36,461/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In view of the recommendation made by the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the restructured Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Mrig Bahar 2021, the State share insurance premium subsidy of Rs. 42,36,461/- is being approved by the Government to be made available to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for payment to the insurance companies as per the guidelines of the scheme.
