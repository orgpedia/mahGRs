The Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 is applicable to co-operative societies in the state of Maharashtra and the functions and functions of all co-operative societies in the state are governed by the provisions of the said Act and the Co-operative Societies Rules, 1961 made thereunder. Under this provision, various authorities have been provided under section 152 and section 154 of the Act in respect of appeal/ revision on resolution of disputes.
The Department of Law and Justice was requested to hear the appeals/ revision applications filed by cooperative societies related to fisheries under the department and clarify the powers to dispose of the applications. The Department of Law and Justice has the right to hold a hearing on the basis of the opinion given by the Department of Fisheries in consultation with the Department of Cooperation. It was proposed to be handed over to the Minister (Fisheries).
On that The Minister (Cooperation), Shri. The Minister (Fisheries) has agreed to be extradited. Accordingly, the following circular is being issued.
GOVERNMENT Circular: Power to hear and dispose of appeal/ revision applications filed by Fisheries Cooperative Societies functioning under the Department of Fisheries. As agreed by the Minister (Cooperation), Shri. Minister (Fisheries) has been transferred. Therefore, the appeal/ revision application filed under this Act under the provisions of Section 152 and Section 154 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 and the Appellate/ Revision of the Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961 made under this Act shall be heard by the Hon'ble Supreme Court. Applications will be taken to the Minister (Fisheries) and disposed of.
