Introduction: In order to increase the production and productivity of various crops in the agricultural sector, it is necessary to make necessary efforts to provide certified/ truthful seeds of high quality and high yielding varieties to the farmers at the local level. For this, there is an urgent need to provide the necessary facilities to the farmers at the village level to produce seeds of various crops, so the Central Government has provided seeds of 500 metric tons capacity through FPO/ SHG/ CIG/ cooperative society at the gram panchayat level under the Seed and Plantation Material Sub Mission (SMSP).
