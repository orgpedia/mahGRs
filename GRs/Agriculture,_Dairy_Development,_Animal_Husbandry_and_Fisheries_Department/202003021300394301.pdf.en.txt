Preamble: With the twin objectives of providing remunerative prices to the agricultural produce of the State and enabling the consumers to purchase agricultural produce at reasonable prices. As per the Government decision dated 23/4/2015," State Agricultural Price Committee" has been converted into" State Agricultural Price Commission." The State Agricultural Price Commission. As per the Government Resolution dated 1st July, 2017, the Chairman and the Chairperson of the Commission, Shri. Farmer representatives have been appointed as members under the Government Resolution dated 30th July, 2018.
Instructions have been issued to revoke the appointments of the members appointed on the government/ non-government boards and committees established by various laws/ acts/ rules under each administrative division and to reconstitute the boards and committees. Accordingly, a proposal to cancel the appointments of the chairman and farmer representatives as members of the State Agricultural Price Commission was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Shri Syed Pasha Osmansahab Patel has been appointed as Chairman, State Agricultural Price Commission vide Government Order dated 01.07.2017. The appointments of the following representatives of farmers as members under the Government Order dated 16th November, 2017 are hereby cancelled.
