The Accounting Officer, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune as the Controlling Officer for drawing the funds disbursed under this Government decision from the treasury.
The beneficiaries under this scheme should be selected in a transparent manner and the list of beneficiaries should be published on the official website of the Agriculture Department with complete details from time to time.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be distributed at the district level through the PFMS system and eligible beneficiaries should be paid subsidy in their Aadhaar linked bank account. The Managing Director, Maharashtra State Horticulture and Medicinal Plants Board, Pune shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in connection with the allocation of funds under the scheme.
The order was issued by the Government Circular Finance Department, No: Finance- 2017/ PR No. 75/ Finance- 3, No. Subject to fulfillment of the conditions dated 18.04.2017, the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the same are being issued.
