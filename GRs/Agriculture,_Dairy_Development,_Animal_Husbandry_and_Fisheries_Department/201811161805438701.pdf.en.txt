Appointment of non-official members to the Managing Committee of the District Animal Clash Prevention Society.
District- Thane
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries, Government Resolution No. 1018/ No. 255/ Padum-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: November 16, 2018
(1) Department of Agriculture and Padum, Notification No. 2014/ PR No. 414/ Padum-3, dated 14.03.2017 (2) History of the meeting of the President, District Animal Clash Prevention Society and Collector, Thane dated 8.1.2018.
Preamble: In order to effectively implement the provisions of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 of the Central Government, the Managing Committees of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Societies have been constituted in each district of the State as per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Establishment and Regulation of Cruelty to Animals) Rules, 2001 vide notification dated 14th March, 2017. It was under consideration of the Government to appoint as non-official members the President of the Gaushala/ Panjalapola Sanstha, members of the voluntary organization working on animal issues, persons nominated by the General Committee and persons working in the field of humanitarian work/ animal lovers etc.
Government's decision:
In view of the proposal received from the District Collector, Thane, the following persons are being appointed as non-official members on the Management Committee of Animal Clash Prevention Society, Thane District, as per the provisions of clause (a) (3) of the notification dated 14/03/2017, subject to the condition of receiving the character verification report from the Police. The term of office of the member shall be three years from the date of his appointment.
