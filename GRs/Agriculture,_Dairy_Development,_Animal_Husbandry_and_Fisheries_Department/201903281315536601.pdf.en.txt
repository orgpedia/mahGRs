The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research (MACER), Pune, has received approval from the State Bank of India (SBI) to open two separate accounts for the two schemes- Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Allowance Scheme- through the Public Financial Management System (PFMS). Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision:
The State Bank of India (SBI) has approved the opening of two new accounts for the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Allowance Scheme.
These bank accounts shall be operative subject to the following conditions:
1) The accounts to be opened for the two schemes- Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme- should be in the current form.
