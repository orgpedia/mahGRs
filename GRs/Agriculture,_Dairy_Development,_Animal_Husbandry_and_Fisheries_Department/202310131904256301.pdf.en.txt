The Proposal:
Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani is an agricultural university established by Vasantrao Naik. It happened on May 18, 1972. The jurisdiction of Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University covers eight districts. Beed district is located in the Marathwada region and comprises Beed, Shirur (Kasar), Parli Vaijnath, Ambajogai, Kej, Dharur, Gevrai, Majalgaon, Patoda, Ashti and Vadwani taluks.
Beed district's fertile soil and favorable climate make it suitable for the cultivation of various crops. Different crops are grown in the Kharif and Rabi seasons in Parli Vaijnath taluka. Cotton and soybean are important cash crops during the kharif and rabi seasons. Other crops include maize, wheat, barley, oats, rye, barley, potatoes, turnips, turnips, potatoes, turnips, and potatoes. Fruits like apple, grapes, banana and orange are being planted. The district is known for producing high quality sugarcane which is used for sugar production. Also, Beed district is leading in the production of oilseeds and vegetables. The market in Parli Vaijnath town is quite large and is considered to be a center of grain trade. Parli Vaijnath railway station is located on the broad gauge railway line.
2. Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Mauje Jirewadi, Dist. With the opening of the College of Agricultural Business Management in Parli Vaijnath, Taluka, the facility of agricultural graduate course will be created for the youth of the district, agribusiness, agro-business and agro-industry according to the changing climate, which will make available extension workers, gram sevaks, agricultural assistants and expert trainers at the village level to spread agricultural technology in this area.
By providing agricultural education, agricultural advice and services to the farmers to do farming in a modern way, the productivity of the farmers' crops will increase and in turn, their economic status will be raised and the country will progress.

Government's decision:
1. Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Mauje Jirewadi in Beed district under Parbhani, Dist. Approval is being given to start a Government College of Agribusiness Management with an intake capacity of 60 students per annum on government land at Parli Vaijanath.
Creation of 16 teaching and 24 non-teaching posts for the College of Agricultural Business Management is being approved subject to the approval of the High Power Committee. Approval is being given to take the faculty manpower required for agriculture related subjects from the newly established Agriculture College, Parali Vaijanath.
3) Salary for Teacher/ Non-Teacher posts Rs. 6422.00 lakhs, recurring expenditure Rs. 99.25 lakh and non-recurring expenditure of Rs. 6768.67 lakh. An expenditure of Rs. 13289.92 lakh is being approved.
