Terms and Conditions: 1. The grant shall be utilized for the scholarship of the beneficiary students during the current financial year.
The amount should be disbursed through the MahaDBT system to the bank accounts of eligible students of all the four agricultural universities in the state by the Director (Education), Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research, Pune.
(2) Where the funds distributed scheme-wise and case-wise are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, such unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for any other purpose.
While making the demand for grants for the year 2022-23, the University shall submit a report on the grants disbursed scheme-wise, actual expenditure and balance grants by the 10th of every month. Further, while submitting the demand for subsidy, the balance of subsidy disbursed earlier should be clubbed with the proposed demand and the actual amount of subsidy should be sought.
3. In column No. 1 of the return in paragraph No. The Assistant Director (Accounts-I), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune, as mentioned in clause (3) of the said sub-heading, may refer to column No. 6 Necessary action should be taken regarding the payment of grants approved below.
The expenditure for this purpose should be shared within the limits of the monthly fund statement from the provision sanctioned for the year 2022-23 under the head of accounts mentioned in column number 2 of the statement in paragraph 1 and should be spent under the same head of accounts.
The order was issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book (Part-I), 1977, as well as the circular of the Finance Department No. are being issued at the department level as per the approval given under 04.04.2022.
