5. terms and conditions:
The funds sanctioned will be the responsibility of the Maharashtra State Veterinary Council to spend on the items mentioned in the above table and included in the Detailed Project Report of the project.
The Maharashtra State Veterinary Council will bear the cost of the project beyond the approved project cost. The government will not be responsible for the cost of the project. Also, no funds other than the project cost will be sanctioned by the government.
Separate accounts of expenditure on the funds sanctioned by the Maharashtra State Veterinary Council should be maintained in the manner prescribed under GFR Rule No. 149 and the report of expenditure made should be submitted to the Commissioner of Animal Husbandry regarding the audit done in respect of the accounts.
4. The Maharashtra State Veterinary Council shall submit the utilization certificate of the funds made available to the Institute to the Government through the Commissioner Animal Husbandry. The remaining funds will not be disbursed until the utilization certificate of the disbursed funds is received.
The project will be cancelled at any stage if the allocated funds are not spent within the stipulated time.
The Maharashtra State Veterinary Council, Nagpur will be responsible for the successful implementation of the approved project and the Commissioner Animal Husbandry at the state level will be the Controlling Officer.
To facilitate the implementation of this project, an account of Maharashtra State Veterinary Council, Nagpur should be opened in the bank. The Maharashtra State Veterinary Council should collect the sanctioned amount from the bank and distribute it to the Public Works Department.
7. Under this scheme, the institution should adopt the tender process as per the government procedure for all materials purchased and projects to be constructed other than construction.
The material to be installed under this scheme, as well as the project to be constructed under the scheme, shall be prominently mentioned on the facade as if the project is under the National Agriculture Development Scheme.
After the implementation of this project, the Commissioner Animal Husbandry, Maharashtra State, Pune should submit the results of the project to the government along with their feedback.

The project should be implemented in accordance with the decisions of the State Level Project Approval Committee in its meeting dated May 10, 2021, the guidelines of the National Agriculture Development Scheme as well as the Government's Animal Breeding Policy.
The project should be included in the District Agriculture Plan (C-DAP) of the concerned district and the same should be reported to the National Agriculture Development Plan Cell as well as the government.
The project information should be entered on the RDMI/ RDMIS system developed by the Central Government and geo-tagging should be done. Also, monthly progress reports of the project's financial cost and material resources should be submitted to the government on a regular basis.
The implementing agencies shall maintain necessary coordination with the Commissioner of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune in the implementation, control and evaluation of the project.
The said Government Decision is being issued in the authority conferred on the Administrative Department by the circular dated 11.6.20218 of the Planning Department.
