Accordingly, the following is approved:
Government's decision:
The Government has approved the release of Rs. 2,27,27, 250/- (Rupees Two Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Twenty Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) out of the budgetary provision for the year 2020-21 for the head of accounts (8342 0141) for the return of defined contribution pension scheme deposited by the employees of the Agricultural Universities and their affiliated Aided Institutions in the State as per statement A attached.

2.This Agreement is subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The terms and conditions of the Government Decision in Reference No. 1 to 5 should be fulfilled.
2) The funds should be spent as demanded under reference number 6 to 9.
The grants sanctioned to the University shall be spent within the prescribed limits as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Act, 1983, the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Statutes, 1990 and the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Accounts Code, 1991, and after obtaining administrative and financial approval of the competent authority in accordance with the prevailing Government Orders and prescribed procedures. It should not be spent only because there is a budgetary provision or a subsidy is disbursed.
(4) The grant shall not be deposited under any circumstances in the personal provident fund/ PLA bank account.
The grant should be spent in the financial year 2020-21 only after approval at the competent level for expenditure.
6) Submit to the government the utilization certificate of the funds being made available through the budget estimates.
(7) Where the funds distributed sub-heading wise, group wise, scheme wise and subject wise are lying unutilized/ unutilized for any reason, the unutilized/ unutilized funds shall not be diverted or spent for other groups/ schemes/ activities without prior approval of the Government.
(8) The grants distributed among each group shall be used to meet the expenditure on permissible items in that group. Grants of one group should not be diverted for schemes/ activities of another group. If more grants have been disbursed for a scheme in a group, then the amount of the excess grant should be considered while seeking further funds.
9) Each sub-account/ group/ scheme and subject-wise separate accounts/ accounts shall be maintained.
The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research shall submit to the Government, within a period of three months from the end of the relevant financial year, an assessment report of each financial year as to whether or not the grants sanctioned to the agricultural universities are being spent in accordance with the prescribed orders/ procedures. Also, the amount of unpaid funds of the previous financial year should be immediately reported to the government.
11) The government has given approval to agricultural universities to use their revenue income. The University shall take appropriate action in accordance with the order and procedure laid down. Action should also be taken to increase the revenue.
12) Don't spend money on illegal schemes, posts and activities.
