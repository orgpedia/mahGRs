As per the powers conferred by Article 72 of the Memorandum of Association& Articles of Association of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Maharashtra Sheep and Goat Development Corporation Limited, Pune, Shri Balasaheb Prabhakar Dodtale was appointed as the Chairman of the said Corporation by the Government decision dated 21.02.2019.
Now, as per the authority given under Article 73 (3) of the Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association of the Corporation, the appointment of Mr. Balasaheb Prabhakar Dodtale as the Chairman of the Corporation is being terminated with immediate effect.
