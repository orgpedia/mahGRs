The proposal-
The state of Maharashtra Based on the concept of the Chief Minister, the policy of implementing the schemes of the Agriculture Department has been decided. As a part of these policies of the State Government, the concept of providing better price to the farmers' goods in the market, availability of fresh fruits and vegetables at reasonable prices to the consumers should be started in the State. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 13.11.2020. Under this initiative, farmers/ farmer groups are directly connected to the consumers through this system. The campaign is coordinated and controlled at the district level by Project Director (ATMA) and Divisional Deputy General Manager, Maharashtra State Marketing Board and at the state level by Director (ATMA) and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board.
Dated 15.06.2023. As per the directions given in the meeting chaired by the Chief Minister, the matter of transferring the scheme of" Sant Shiromani Savta Mali Rayat Bazar Abhiyan" to the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (Marketing) was under the consideration of the Government. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision
Department of Agriculture. The Government hereby approves transfer of the scheme" Sant Shiromani Savta Mali Rayat Bazar Abhiyan" approved vide Government Resolution dated 13.11.2020 to the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing.
The earlier procedure shall continue till necessary Government decisions/ guidelines are issued by the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing regarding the transfer.
