Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif 2020 29.6.2020 and D. As per the Government decision dated 17.7.2020, Indian Agricultural Insurance Company, IFFCO Tokio General Insurance Co., Ltd., Reliance General Insurance Co., Ltd. Bharti AXA General Insurance Co. Ltd. Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company Limited. and HDFC Insurance. It is operated by 6 insurance companies. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India is the coordinating company of insurance companies in the state. The Agriculture Insurance Company of India (AAI) has sought State Government share subsidy for crop insurance premium subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2020 from the above 6 companies. In the revised guidelines of the Central Government, Issue No. 13.1.6 states that" the insurance premium share of the Central and State Government is required to be paid to the company in advance (first installment) even if the data of the insured area is not available at the time of registration of the current season. The insurance premium subsidy amount should be 50 per cent of the total 80 per cent of the total state share insurance premium subsidy paid for the previous season in advance to the companies, it said. Accordingly, Rs. 496.14 crore was under consideration of the Government for disbursement of crop insurance premium subsidy.
Government's decision:
The demand submitted by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2020, the recommendation of the Agriculture Commissionerate and the revised guidelines of the Central Government, is item No. 13.1.6.Considering this, the State share of crop insurance premium subsidy to insurance companies is Rs. The government has sanctioned Rs. 496.14 crore for the purpose.
