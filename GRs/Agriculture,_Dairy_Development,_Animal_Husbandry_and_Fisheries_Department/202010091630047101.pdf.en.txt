The Proposal:
In order to stabilize the power generation program in the state and to maintain the participation of the farmers in the power generation program, in view of the fact that the rate in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee is less than the minimum support price for the crops produced, as per the decision of the government dated September 17, 2018, the difference between the minimum support price declared by the government and the base rate of the Agricultural Produce Market Committee for the prescribed period has been implemented as a new scheme for payment to the power producers of Maharashtra State Seeds Corporation Limited, Akola and National Seeds Corporation, Pune.
A total of Rs. 10,000 crore will be provided to the farmers who have taken Kharif/ Rabi crops for the year 2018-19 to pay the difference between the Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) based prices and the Minimum Support Price (MSP) announced by the Government. Proposal of National Seeds Corporation, Pune for sanction of subsidy amounting to Rs. 120.76 lakh 4 was obtained. The proposal was approved by the state committee. It has been approved in the meeting dated 20.03.2020. Accordingly, Rs. The government was planning to disburse Rs 120.76 lakh.
