4. The total amount of Rs. 930.221 lakh made available for National Food Security Mission as per the decision of the Government should be disbursed district wise by the Director (Extension& Training) through PFMS system.
The funds made available under this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Director (Extension and Training) should submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds. On the basis of this utilization certificate, the utilization certificate of the fund will be issued to the Central Government with the signature of the Secretary (Agriculture).

7. This Government Decision is based on the Government Circular No. Finance-2020/ No. 64/ Finance-3, D. As the conditions laid down as on 16th April, 2020 are being fulfilled, the same is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on this Department under the said circular.
