Introduction: Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, a project to provide facilities for artificial insemination of cows and buffaloes at the doorstep of farmers through veterinary graduates/ veterinary diploma holders and other educated unemployed and voluntary/ voluntary organizations has been approved. The approval was accorded at the 20th State Level Project Clearance Committee Meeting held on 18.06.2015. Accordingly, the project of setting up artificial sanding centers in 26 districts of the state through the service organization M.J.K. Trust, Thane, under the e-tendering process convened at the level of CEO, Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Akola, was given administrative approval under the government decision in Wacha-1. The duration of the MoU signed in this regard with M.J.K. Trust, Thane. expired on 30.06.2017.
In order to continue the project in the state in the next period, e-bids were invited from voluntary/ voluntary organizations at the level of CEO, Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Akola. Accordingly, as per the relevant Government decision, administrative approval has already been granted to the project to provide artificial sanding facilities in 11 districts through the service organization BAIF, Pune.
