Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in the State in Kharif 2019. The scheme is being implemented through two insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India and Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company as per the Government decision dated 22.05.2019. The Agriculture Commissioner's Office, vide Reference Letter No. 9, has released an amount of Rs. 2 crore to the State Government from the fund available through supplementary demand for crop insurance premium subsidy to the above mentioned 2 companies under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2019. A sum of Rs. 500,00,00, 000/- is sought to be disbursed. Accordingly, the government was considering to disburse this amount as crop insurance premium subsidy.
