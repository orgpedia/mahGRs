The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the State governments. A total of Rs. 57.30 crore has been sanctioned for the year 2018-19 under the scheme for the Scheduled Castes category. The Scheme has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 15th September, 2018. Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, a total of Rs. 27.86 crore has been released to the Scheduled Castes in the first instalment. 12/11/2018 (Refer No. 5) is distributed by the decision of the government. Now that the Central Government has released Rs. 17.19 crore to the State in the second instalment for this category, and a total of Rs. 28.65 crore, amounting to Rs. 11.46 crore on the corresponding State account, was under consideration of the Government, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. 28.65 crore (Rupees Twenty-eight crore fifty-five lakh only) is being made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) in the ratio of 60:40 Central and State funding for the Scheduled Castes category for the implementation of the projects sanctioned under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana-Raftar in 2018-19 through the Budget Distribution System as follows:
