Introduction: The Motherland, Ta. Mulshi, Survey No. of Animal Husbandry Department, Pune. Since 0.15 hectares of land out of 50 was reserved for the police department, the matter of handing over the land to the revenue and forest department was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 2. Valu Mata Prakrama, Tathawade, Ta. Mulshi, District Survey No. 0.15 hectare of land in Reservation No. 46 is reserved for the police department.
As the land is required by the Deputy Commissioner of Police, Circle-2, Pimpri Chinchwad Police Commissionerate, Pune, the land is being handed over to the Revenue and Forest Department under the following terms and conditions.
The terms and conditions are as follows: (i) The land shall be sold at the Government rate of Rs. 1,40,55, 000/- (Rs. One crore forty lakh fifty five thousand only), the police department should build a pucca structure of the same price on the remaining land of the area at Tathawade.
(ii) The construction of a protective wall in the entire area, as well as wire fencing on the 10-foot high wall should be done by the police department.
(iii) The said space shall be used for the purpose for which the space is being dedicated.
