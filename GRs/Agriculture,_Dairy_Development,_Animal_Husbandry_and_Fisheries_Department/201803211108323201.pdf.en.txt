Introduction: Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the project of providing facilities of artificial insemination in cows and buffaloes at the doorstep of farmers through veterinary graduates/ veterinary diploma holders and other educated unemployed and voluntary/ voluntary organizations has been approved. The approval was accorded at the 20th State Level Project Clearance Committee Meeting held on 18.06.2015. Accordingly, during the 12th Five Year Plan period, a project to provide facilities of artificial sanding in cows and buffaloes at the doorstep of farmers through the educated unemployed was launched. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 17.10.2012.
Since the concept of Five Year Plan has not been in existence since the financial year 2017-18, the Government is formulating the following decisions in order to implement the project in the State in the next period as well.
Government Decision: Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, a project to provide facilities for artificial insemination of cows and buffaloes at the doorstep of farmers through veterinary graduates/ veterinary diploma holders and other educated unemployed is being given administrative approval through this project and the project should be implemented in the state until further orders.
Read- 1 Government decision mentioned here regarding the implementation of this project in the state. As on 17.10.2012, the financial parameters of the project, detailed guidelines, evaluation committee and Appendix A have not been changed and are being retained.
