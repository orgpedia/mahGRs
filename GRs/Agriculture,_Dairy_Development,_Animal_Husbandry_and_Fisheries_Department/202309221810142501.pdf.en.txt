The scheme aims to promote commercial organic farming and use of organic manure, standardize the participation guarantee system, prepare organic manure in farmers' fields, provide chemical pesticides free residue free farm produce to the consumers, develop organic farming villages and conduct training and demonstration based on agriculture, adopt low cost technologies and increase economic income by reducing the cost of production to the farmers.
The scheme is group based and a group of 20 hectares is being formed. Under the scheme, the benefit is given to the selected group/ beneficiary once in 3 years. Under the scheme, 86.20 per cent of the total funds are received by the general category, 7.50 per cent by the Scheduled Castes and 6.30 per cent by the Scheduled Tribes. From 2022-23 onwards, funds are being made available for Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana from National Agriculture Development Scheme.
