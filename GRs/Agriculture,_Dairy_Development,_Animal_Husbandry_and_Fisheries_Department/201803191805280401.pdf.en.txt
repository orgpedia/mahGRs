Introduction The Central Government has decided to implement the National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)- Forest Farming sub-campaign from the year 2017-18. The guidelines for this have been issued by the central government. As per the guidelines of the central government, the ratio of central and state share in this sub-mission is 60:40. The sub-campaign will be carried out in the states which have relaxed the rules regarding tree felling and transportation of timber. The state of Maharashtra has been included in the implementation of the Forest Agriculture Sub-Abhiyan. This campaign will be carried out across the state. The administrative approval has been given as per the government decision dated December 8, 2017.
