Government order:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has released a recruitment notification for the positions of Group A. 28/4/2015, dt. 16/7/2015 and dt. The provisions of 15/6/2017 are applicable, as per which the concerned candidates are required to complete 6 years of service in the appointed revenue department. However, as per the provisions of section 12 (a), 12 (b) and 12 (c) of the said Act, they can apply for transfer by changing the Revenue Department after completing one year of service in the Revenue Department appointed for medical, spousal integration and mutual transfer. As per the said provision, some officers of the cadre of Livestock Development Officer, (Group A) have applied to the government for transfer by changing the revenue department. Similarly, in similar cases, the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Nagpur, has directed to take a decision on inter-departmental transfer by changing the revenue department as per the provisions of the said notification. Ordered on 9/3/2018. In view of the above, after considering the representations received from the Government at the Government level as per the provisions of the notification, the following officers of the rank of Livestock Development Officer (Group-A) mentioned in the following statement are being transferred and posted to the post mentioned in column No. 3 by changing the Revenue Department, as per the recommendation of the Civil Services Board and as per the approval of the competent authority.
