The Proposal:
The Department of Agriculture has developed an integrated computer system on Maha-DBT portal under the head" Farmers Scheme" to facilitate the farmers to avail the benefits of all the schemes" through a single application." At present. As per the Government decision dated 04.11.2020, the flagship schemes of the Department of Agriculture are being implemented through Maha DBT portal.
General Administration Department The modalities for distribution of benefits through Maha DBT system were prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 12th October, 2018. Accordingly, a Central Bridge Account has been opened for the implementation of various schemes of the Agriculture Department. It was approved by the government on May 21, 2021. General Administration Department. The Government of India has decided to revamp the functioning of Maha DBT system by opening a separate Central Pool Account in State Bank of India for each scheme with effect from 30th November, 2021. As per the amendment, there will be a separate centralized facility for each scheme at the level of Commissioner of Agriculture for transfer of subsidy to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of eligible beneficiaries under all the schemes being implemented through Maha DBT portal under the Department of Agriculture as well as the future schemes to be implemented through Maha-DBT portal.
