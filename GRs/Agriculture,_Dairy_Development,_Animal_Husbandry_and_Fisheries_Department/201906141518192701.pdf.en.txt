Government's decision:
In the interest of the Maharashtra Public Service, the Government is approving the posting of officers of the category of Group-A, namely, Deputy Director, Maharashtra Agricultural Service, in this Department, who are eligible for periodic transfers, with the approval of the competent authority, as per the provisions of Section 4 (1), 4 (2), 4 (3) and Section 5 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, as shown in the table below. The following officers were eligible for general transfer, but due to some exceptional circumstances, the orders of transfer could not be issued within the prescribed period. Accordingly, the said orders are being issued with the approval of the senior competent authority as per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and 4 (5) of the Transfer Act.
