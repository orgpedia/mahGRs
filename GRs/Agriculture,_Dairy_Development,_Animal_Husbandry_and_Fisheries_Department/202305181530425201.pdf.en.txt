Introduction: In line with the Union Government's vision to double farmers' income by 2022, inputs like inputs, training, irrigation, mechanization, post-harvest technologies, agro processing and marketing etc. are critical to increase crop production. Considering the need for collective farming, modern methods of farming and strict planning for a substantial increase in the production of farmers due to the low land holding of farmers in the state, the scheme of" Promotion of Group Farming of Farmers for Promotion and Empowerment of Group Farming" has been implemented. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 5.10.2018. As per the decision of the government, the scheme was approved for implementation in 2017-18 and 2018-19. Therefore, to make farming economically viable for the farmers,' Driving Farmers' Group Farming' has been started to encourage and empower group farming. In view of this objective and the outcome of the scheme, the scheme titled" Fostering group farming among farmers for promoting and empowering group farming" has been introduced in the context.
