The order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Employees Act, 2005, the Government hereby approves the transfer and posting of officers in the cadre of Dairy Engineer in Engineering Group-A (Senior) in the Dairy Development Department with the approval of the competent authority.
