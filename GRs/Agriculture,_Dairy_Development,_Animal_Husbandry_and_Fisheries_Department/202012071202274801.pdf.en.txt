Dr. Somavanshi Committee report with recommendations after studying the marine watershed area of the state
submitted to the government. Pursuant to the said recommendation, Persecine has been regulated vide Government Order dated 05.02.2016. As there are no rules or guidelines on how to reduce the number of Persecine licenses to 182, it has become difficult to take a decision on it. Also, to protect the interests of traditional fishermen, the government was considering appointing a study group to study the environmental impact on marine fish stocks and fishing practices.
Government's decision
In order to protect the interests of traditional fishermen, environmental impact on marine fish stocks and fisheries, study fishing practices and to determine guidelines on how to reduce the number of persisin licences to 182, the Government is approving the appointment of the following study groups.
