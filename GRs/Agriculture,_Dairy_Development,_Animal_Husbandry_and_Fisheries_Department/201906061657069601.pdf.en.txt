Introduction: The State Government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme for farmers who are not eligible to cultivate fruit orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The scheme will be implemented from 2019-20. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 03.06.2019. A sum of Rs. 4000.00 lakhs (Rupees Forty Crores only) has been budgeted for the general category for implementation of the scheme in the current year. The matter of distributing this fund to the Commissioner of Agriculture on the budget distribution system was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: For the year 2019-20, a sum of Rs. 4000.00 lakh (Rupees Forty Crores only) is being made available to the Commissioner of Agriculture on Budget Distribution System for the general category for implementation of Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Plantation Scheme.
(2) The funds shall be spent out of the provisions budgeted for the current year under the following headings:
Demand No. D-3 2401- Crop Protection 119, Horticulture and Vegetable Crops, (01) Fruits (33) Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Cultivation Scheme (2401 A889) 33 Financial Assistance

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FUFFLA-2019/ P.S. NO. 84/9-A,
The Assistant Director (Accounts-I), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as the Procurement and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Agriculture) as the Controlling Officer for drawing and disbursing from the Treasury the amount of Rs. 4000.00 lakhs (Rupees Forty Crores only) disbursed by this Government Resolution.
The subsidy under the scheme is to be paid to the beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to their Aadhaar seeded bank account.
The funds made available by this Government decision should be utilized immediately and the Commissioner (Agriculture) should submit the utilization certificate to the Government from time to time in accordance with the allocation of funds.
While spending the funds, the guidelines issued by the Planning and Finance Department as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and the Financial Powers Rulebook and the prevailing terms and conditions and the instructions given from time to time should be strictly followed. Also, ensure that the funds are appropriated.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department subject to compliance of the terms and conditions contained in Circular No: Fince-2019/ PR No.44/ Fince-3, dated 1st April, 2019 of the Finance Department.
