Under the State sponsored scheme of Agricultural Mechanization No. As a result of the Government Decision No. 1, administrative approval has been granted. Under this scheme, component number-3 of the Central Government's Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission, providing subsidy to individual beneficiary farmers for the purchase of agricultural implements/ machines and component number-4, providing subsidy to agricultural implements/ machines banks are implemented as per the guidelines of the Central Government's Agriculture Mechanization Sub-Mission.
The State Sponsored Agriculture Mechanization Scheme is implemented through Maha DBT portal.
