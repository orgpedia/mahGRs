The Proposal:
The World Bank-funded Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjivani project is being implemented in 15 districts of the state. Under this project, various climate adaptation components are being implemented. The World Bank is organizing a workshop on" South-South Knowledge Exchange on Climate Smart Agriculture" in Nairobi (Kenya) from 12th to 13th June, 2019. The objective of the workshop is to disseminate Indian knowledge/ experience on Climate Smart Agriculture/ Climate Resilient Agriculture to various African countries and to facilitate exchange of knowledge/ technology in this regard. Considering that African countries should be informed about the knowledge/ experience of various components of climate adaptation being implemented under the project and exchange of experience on climate adaptation with different countries participating in the workshop, the World Bank has invited Mr. Vijay Subrao Kolekar, an agricultural scholar, to the above mentioned workshop. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: 1. The Government approves the visit of Shri Vijay Subrao Kolekar, Agricultural Scholar, Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjeevani Project to attend the workshop on" South-South Knowledge Exchange on Climate Smart Agriculture" organized by the World Bank from 12th to 13th June, 2019 at Nairobi (Kenya).
The duration of the visit will be from June 11 to 16, 2019.
Vijay Subrao Kolekar will bear the expenses for travel, accommodation, food etc. from the World Bank, while the visa related expenses will be borne by Mr. Kolekar.
