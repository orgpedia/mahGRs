खाता
The following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit Account
(VPDA) 1. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant G.R. have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
3. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
4. The VPDA is applicable only to the State Schemes and not for Central Sector or Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
5. The Receipt to the said VPDA through Nil Bill procedure, shall be only for the bills drawn under:
e MTR-31B for the object heads 27-Minor works snad 53-Major Works; e MTR-45 B for the object heads of expenditure 33- subsides nad 50-Other Charges; e MTR-44 for the object heads of expenditure31-Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary) and 35 Grants in Aid for creadting of Capital Assets.
e MTR-29 for the Object head of expenditure52-Machinery and Equipment shall be applicable from 1 April 2025
6. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate in the prescribed format after incurring expenditure from the VPDA to offices viz. O/o PAG(A&E)-I, Mumbai O/o PAG(A&E)-II, Nagpur or O/o PAO, Mumbai as per the respective jurisdiction for the Bills drawn Undert MTR-44 for object heads of expenditure 31- Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary), 35-Grant-in-Aid for creation of Capital Assets.
For bills drawn under MTR-31B, MTR-45B,MTR-29 the Utilisation Certificate shall
be submitted to the concerned administrative departments.
7. The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as_ per the Sanction order of the respective Grant.
8. The suspent balances under the VPAD shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund after completion of one year (365 days) from the date of drawal of the funds/date of credit of amount to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever is earlier.
9. The monthly Plus Minus Memo shall be forwarded to this office by the Administrator treasury officer.
