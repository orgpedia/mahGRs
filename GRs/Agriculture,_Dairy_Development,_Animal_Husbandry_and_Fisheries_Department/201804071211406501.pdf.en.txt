The Proposal:
It is stated in the Circular that the amount specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been approved by the Legislature and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and has been published in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV dated 27th March, 2018 as Maharashtra Act No. 22 of 2018.
Demand for the Financial Year 2018-19. The Budget Estimates have been approved under D-2, D-3, D-7 and D-11. As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, the allocation is being made on the basis of the Budget Estimates 2018-19 (White Book) book as approved by the Finance Department.
Government's decision:
For the schemes under mandatory expenditure as per the circular in reference to the Finance Department, the details are given in the Annexure-A attached to this Government Decision in Column No. The approved provision in column no. 5 being handed over to the Implementing/ Drawing and Disbursing Officers, they should ensure that the budgetary provision for the 9th year 2018-19 is spent in the prescribed manner within the limit of 70% after fulfilling the conditions mentioned in the circular under reference to the Finance Department as well as the prevailing terms, conditions and conditions. Under the mandatory expenditure, financial assistance for the premium of insurance under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, auxiliary grants to vegetable nurseries, orchards and local parks and agricultural universities, and house-building advance will be provided for these schemes and the scheme-wise administrative and financial sanction orders for the schemes under the programme will be issued separately by the concerned authorities.
(2) This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments.
