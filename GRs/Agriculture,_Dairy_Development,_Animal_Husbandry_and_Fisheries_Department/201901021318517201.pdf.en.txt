As the Metro Rail project is an ambitious project of the government, the matter of accepting the demand of one of the two authorities was under consideration. Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision
The government, under this decision, is rejecting the demand of the Pune Metropolitan Region Authority to provide 4 hectares of land permanently for the Hinjewadi to Shivajinagar Metro, and the Government Dairy Development Scheme under the Dairy Development Department, Survey No. The Government has approved the leasing out of the Pune Metro Rail Project for a period of 3 years for relocation of ST Station and its administrative office at Shivajinagar, Pune, subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions
(1) The land shall be used only for the purpose for which it is allotted. Any use of this land or any part of the land for any other purpose will require prior approval of the Dairy Development Department of the Government.
(2) The land shall not be transferred/ sub-let to any other person. Also, no financial transaction can be carried out in such a way that other persons/ institutions/ companies etc. will have any rights on this land or any part of the land.
3) Alternative arrangements for offices/ residences used by the Dairy Department on the proposed land will be required to be made by Maharashtra Metro Rail Corp. Ltd.
(iv) Prior permission/ approval/ no-objection certificate, etc. of the concerned department/ government machinery as per the rules and regulations in force for construction on the subject land.
