GOVERNMENT REGULATION: In order to provide veterinary services to the animal patients at the doorstep of the livestock rearers, the Government has approved the establishment of new mobile veterinary teams in 80 taluks of the State under the state level scheme of" Mukhyamantri Pashu Swasthya Yojana" in addition to the existing 65 mobile veterinary dispensaries in the State.
