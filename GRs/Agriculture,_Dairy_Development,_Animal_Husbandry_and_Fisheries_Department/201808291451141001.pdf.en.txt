GOVERNMENT ORDER: Ordered periodic transfers of Livestock Development Officers, Group-A cadre officers of Animal Husbandry Department for the year 2017. has been issued on 31.5.2017. Some of the officers transferred under this order have submitted their request to the government for partial modification of their transfer orders on account of their family problems, imminent retirement and medical reasons. After considering the request from the government level, considering the provisions of section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005, on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board and with the approval of the competent authority, the following officers of the Animal Husbandry Department, Livestock Development Officer, Group-A, in the following cadre, have been transferred and posted in the place indicated in column 4 before their names by partially modifying the transfer order dated 31/5/2017:
