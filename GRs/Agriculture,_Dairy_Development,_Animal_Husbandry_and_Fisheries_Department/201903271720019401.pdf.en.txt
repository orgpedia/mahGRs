Introduction: The State Government has launched the Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme for farmers who are not eligible to cultivate fruit orchards under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 09/01/2018, for the year 2018-19, for the implementation of Bhausaheb Phundkar Fruit Garden Plantation Scheme, Rs. 700 crore (Rs. 7 crore only) has been made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on the Budget Distribution System. As per the revised estimates of the Finance Department, an additional Rs. 100.00 lakh has been sanctioned by the Government for the implementation of the scheme. The government is taking the following steps:
Government Decision: 1. For the year 2018-19, Rs. 100 lakh (Rs. 1 crore only) is being made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on the Budget Distribution System.
(2) The funds shall be spent out of the provisions budgeted for the current year under the following headings:
