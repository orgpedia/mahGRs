Introduction: Mukhyamantri Krishi and Food Processing Scheme is a 100% state sponsored scheme implemented for the next five years from the financial year 2017-2018. The said scheme has been accorded administrative approval within the limit of 100% of the budgetary provision under Government Resolution No. 2 for implementation in the State in the year 2021-2022.
As per the Government decision in Reference No. 3 of the Department of Finance, the year-end limit of budgetary allocation for schemes under the programme has been fixed at 60% and the Department is directed to plan expenditure accordingly. As per the Government decision at Reference No.4, a sum of Rs. 445.00 lakhs (Rs. Four Crore Forty Five Lakhs in letter) has been made available to the Commissioner (Agriculture) towards pending liabilities under the scheme. A proposal for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 4193.25 lakhs has been received by the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 3305.00 lakh (Rs. Thirty Three Crore Five Lakhs) was under consideration of the Government to be disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune for payment of the liability under the said scheme.
The government is taking the following steps:

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MUAPR-2021/ P. No. 90/9-A
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Approval is being given to disburse a sum of Rs. 3305.00 lakh (Rs. Thirty Three Crore Five Lakhs) to the Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune towards the liability of pending proposals for implementation of Mukhya Mantri Agriculture and Food Processing Scheme in the State during 2021-2022.
(2) The funds shall be spent out of the provisions budgeted for the current year under the following headings:
Demand No.D-3, 2401- Crop Protection (00) (102) Food Crops, (00) (34) Chief Minister Agriculture and Food Processing Scheme (100% State Scheme) (2401 A791) 33, Financial Assistance
After the funds are made available under this scheme in the current year, it should be used to meet the liabilities of pending cases in the first place.
The scheme should be implemented as per the provisions prescribed under the Government Decision dated 20/06/2017 in Reference No.1 and guidelines for implementation of the scheme in 2021-2022 should be issued from the level of Commissionerate of Agriculture accordingly.
The Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Agriculture Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the implementation of the scheme in 2021-2022.
6. Submit to the State Government the utilization certificate of the fund disbursement after conducting the audit following the rules/ instructions prescribed by the State Government.
While spending the funds, the guidelines issued by the Planning and Finance Department from time to time, the prevailing terms and conditions and the instructions given from time to time should be strictly followed as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book and the Financial Rights Rule Book.
This Order is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Resolution Finance Division No: 2021/ No.48/ 3 dated 14th October, 2021.
