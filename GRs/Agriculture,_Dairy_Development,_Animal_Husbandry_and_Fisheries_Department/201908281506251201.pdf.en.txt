The above sanctioned funds should be withdrawn from the treasury for immediate utilization and all the departments should make immediate utilization of the funds received by them, the implementing agencies should not keep the funds in bank accounts without planning.
The remaining funds under the scheme will be released by the Central Government in the second phase after auditing and certifying the funds disbursed to various departments under the National Agriculture Development Scheme from 2007-08 to 2018-19.
Under this Government decision, funds have been released only for the' Scheduled Castes Category' and all the Departments/ Implementing Agencies should utilise the above amount for the funds required for the Scheduled Castes category under the projects sanctioned to them.
The funds to be disbursed under this Government decision are being disbursed as per the demand of the concerned implementing agencies however, as directed by the Committee in the 27th State Level Project Sanctioning Committee meeting of the National Agriculture Development Scheme, the concerned implementing agencies will be responsible for utilising the funds being disbursed during 2019-20 on priority for completion of old incomplete projects.
The concerned implementing agencies and administrative departments should ensure that there is no backlog of payments due to lack of funds under any new or old project.
Under individual benefit projects, all departments/ implementing agencies shall disburse the subsidy through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of the beneficiaries.
