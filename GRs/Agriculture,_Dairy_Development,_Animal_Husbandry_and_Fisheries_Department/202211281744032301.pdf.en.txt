Preamble: In order to provide insurance cover to the farmers against climatic hazards and to help in maintaining the financial stability of the farmers, the State is implementing the Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme 2019-20 on priority. Due to various climatic hazards there is adverse effect on the crop productivity and there is reduction in the production to a large extent. The farmers are facing financial crisis as they are not getting proper price for their produce. Considering all these factors, as a measure to provide effective compensation to the farmers, the Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme 2019-20 has been implemented for 7 crops viz. Orange, Cashew, Pomegranate, Mango, Banana and Grape (Ambia Bahar) in the district by taking the component of Revenue Board.
Reference No. 1 for re-designed Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana to be implemented in 2019-20 The administrative approval has been given in accordance with the government decision in 1. The Commissionerate of Agriculture has submitted a letter vide Reference No. 7 to the State Share Grant as demanded by the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for the year 2019-20 for Ambia Bahara in the scheme. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:

Government's decision:
Considering the recommendation of the Commissionerate of Agriculture under the restructured Climate Based Fruit Insurance Scheme Ambia Bahar 2019-20, the State share insurance premium subsidy of Rs. 16,25,040/- is being approved by the Government to be made available to the Agriculture Insurance Company of India for payment to the insurance companies as per the guidelines of the Scheme. This amount is being disbursed for Aambia Bahar 2019-20 and will not be allowed for other seasons.
The expenditure on the said item shall be met from the budgetary provision approved for the year 2022-23 under the following heads:
Demand No. The D-3
2401- Crop Protection
110, crop insurance.
(00) (07) State share of Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme
(2401 9402) 33- Financial assistance
For this purpose, Assistant Director (Accounts), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
The fund should be spent as per the guidelines of Restructured Weather Based Fruit Insurance Scheme and Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Government from time to time.
