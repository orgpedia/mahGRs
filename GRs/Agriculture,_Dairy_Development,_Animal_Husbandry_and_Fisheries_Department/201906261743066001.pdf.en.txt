It has been decided to adopt (PFMS) mode. The memorandum dated March 22, 2019, directs the implementors of the scheme to implement safety measures.
The students of the constituent colleges of Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur have been allowed the benefit of two schemes- Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance Scheme. The nodal officer for the use and implementation of this system should be declared as the State Plan Manager, First Level Agency, Second Level Agency, Maker and Checker. For this, the matter of declaring the six-level competent authority was under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
In view of the above facts, the Government have decided to issue the following orders:- (1) The competent authority for the implementation of the two schemes namely, Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance Scheme applicable to the students of the constituent colleges of the Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur is being notified as under:
(i) Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary, (Padum-Budget) Nodal Officer (ii) Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary, (Padum-Mafsu) State Level Scheme Manager (iii) Cell Officer/ Under Secretary (Padum-Mafsu)- First Level Agency (iv) Registrar,- Second Level Agency Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur (v) Director Student Welfare,- Maker Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur (vi) Controller (Finance and Accounts),- Checker Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Nagpur (ii) Duties/ Responsibilities of Competent Authority: (i) Nodal Officer:- Nodal Officer is the Public Provider for Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr.
(ii) State Plan Manager:- The State Plan Manager will handle the administrative/ technical aspects related to the scheme on PFMS system for implementation of the said schemes. Also, if there is a need to change the First Level Agency at the state level, then the Under Secretary/ Cell Officer under his control will be appointed.
(iii) First Level Agencies: First Level Agencies will deal with the administrative/ technical aspects of their activities related to the scheme on the PFMS system to implement the said scheme. Also, if there is a need to change the second level agency at the state level, then the officers under their control will be appointed.
