Proposal- Reference to the Central Government. The Central Government has accorded administrative approval to the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Animal Health and Disease Control (LH& DCP) vide letter dated August 23, 2021. The Animal Health and Disease Control Programme (LH& DCP) is a group scheme comprising both National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) and Animal Health and Disease Control (LH& DC). For implementation of the Centrally Sponsored National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) in the State, Referral No. 3 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here.
The Animal Health and Disease Control (LH& DC) scheme covers the following three applications.
Critical Animal Disease Control Programme (CADCP) (100%): Under this application, two diseases namely PPR and CSF are to be controlled and eradicated by 2030 through preventive vaccination.
Funds will also be made available to provide private manpower (Pashumitra/ Pashusakhi/ Pashubandhu) in addition to the government machinery to make available the manpower that is inadequate while carrying out the preventive vaccination drive.

Before preventive vaccinations it is mandatory to register them on the eNAF system by putting billets in the ears of the animal.
Establishment and Strengthening of Mobile Veterinary Units (ESVHD MVU): From the year 2021-22 onwards, the establishment of Mobile Veterinary Units instead of construction and strengthening of Veterinary Dispensaries has been included in this scheme.
Each mobile veterinary team will provide animal health services at the doorstep of the animal husbandry workers, mainly in the areas of disease diagnosis, medication, vaccination, surgery and audio-visual and extension services.
The mobile veterinary teams to be set up under the scheme are to be managed by State Level Call Centre.
Setting up of Mobile Veterinary Team Under this deployment, 100% of the non-recurring cost of providing 80 four wheelers and ancillary items (fabrication and equipment) has been funded at a total cost of Rs. 1280.00 lakh has been deposited by the Central Government in a separate account in the National Animal Disease Control Programme opened by the Office of the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Livestock Development Board, Nagpur in the year 2021-22.
Under ASCAD (Assistance to States for Control of other Economically Important, Exotic, Emergent and Zoonotic Livestock and Poultry Diseases), the following 6 activities are to be implemented.
National Animal Disease Reporting System (NADRS) (Central share 60% and State share 40%) under Modernization of Zonal Disease Investigation Laboratories Prevention of Emerging and Exotic Diseases (Central share 60% and State share 40%), (Central share 50% and State share 50%).
Government Decision-: The Government is giving administrative approval for implementation of Livestock Health& Disease Control Programme in the State from 2021-22 which includes CADCP, ESVHD-MVU, various sub-schemes under ASCAD.
