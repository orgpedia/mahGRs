With regard to the abolition of the provision for appeal and review of cases in the jurisdiction of the administrative department of Padum (Dairy Development Department) in which there is no provision for appeal/ review in the Act, Rules, Regulations and Government decisions.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries
Government Order No.No.Duvipra-1023/ PR No. 177/ Padum-6
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: August 9, 2023
Read:- Government Order No. 2021/ No. 8/ Padum-17, D. 2nd September 2021.
Note: Read the above. Based on the Government decision dated 02.09.2021, a large number of cases of salary/ time-bound promotion/ assured progress scheme/ regularization in service/ date of remuneration etc. of employees of Class-3 and Class-4 cadres of Dairy Development Department are being submitted to the Government for consideration. The government wastes a huge amount of time and effort in conducting hearings, issuing hearing orders, processing review petitions, etc. Hence, the government decided to withdraw the decision.
Government Decision: In the cases under the jurisdiction of the Dairy Development Department, due to the Act, Rules, Regulations and Government Decisions, the salary of Class-3 and Class-4 cadre employees/ time-bound promotion/ promised progress scheme/ regularization in service/ due date etc., the provision for the aggrieved person to appeal/ review against the order of the Commissioner, Dairy Development (Appellate Authority) to the Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary (Padum) (except for the punishment awarded after the departmental inquiry of Class-3 and Class-4 only) is not included in any Maharashtra Civil Service Rules.
