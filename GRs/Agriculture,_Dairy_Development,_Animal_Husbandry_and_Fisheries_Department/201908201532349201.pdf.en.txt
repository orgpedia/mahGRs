Transfer of General Group-A (Junior) Cadre Officers in Dairy Development Department
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries
Government Order No.Duwipra-1119/ PR No. 24/ Padum-6
Ministry Extension, Mumbai 400032
Date is August 20, 2019
The order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority for transfer, the Government is approving the posting of officers of the cadre of General Group-A (Junior) in the Dairy Development Department as District Dairy Development Officer/ In-charge Manager, as shown in the table below.

(2) The order shall come into force immediately.
3.The above officer should join the place of transfer immediately and take charge of the new post immediately. They should not be absent. Otherwise, their absence will be treated as unauthorised absence and will be made without pay.
4. The Commissioner, Dairy Development is informed that the date of assumption of charge by the concerned officers should be intimated to the Government immediately. If the concerned officer does not join within the prescribed time, the same should be informed to the government immediately, so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned. As per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the transfer officers' leave applications should not be approved.
