Preamble The Union Government had launched the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP) from 2014-15, replacing the Integrated Pulses, Oilseeds and Maize Development Programme. Since 2018-19, the Government of India has included the National Mission on Oilseeds and Oilseeds under the National Mission on Food Security (NMFS). In the campaign, No. 1 (Telbia), Mission No. 2 (Teltad) and Mission No. 3 (Tree borne oilseeds) are the three missions in the State. 1 (Telbia) and Mission No. 3 (tree oilseeds) will be implemented. Under the Mission, the Central share of subsidy will be 60:40 except for basic seed procurement (100% Central share).
The Central Government vide letter No. 1 has approved the Annual Action Plan for implementation of National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds and Oilseeds) in the State for the year 2018-19 with an outlay of Rs.6, 904.98 lakh. As per the Annual Action Plan, the Mission will be implemented in the State during 2018-19 for Mission-1 (Oilseeds) and Mission-3 (Tree-Borne Oilseeds) with a total outlay of Rs.6, 798.74 lakh for Mission-1 and Rs. 106.24 lakh for Mission-3. The Central share is₹ 4, 174.98 lakh and the State share is₹ 2, 730.00 lakh. The matter of administrative approval of the program of Rs.6, 904.98 lakh (Rupees Seventy-nine Crore Four Lakh Eighty-eight Thousand Only) approved by the Central Government was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision 1. Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Krishi Unnati Yojana, the Central Government has approved Mission-1 (Oilseeds) and Mission-3 (Tree Based Oilseeds) under the National Food Security Mission (Oilseeds and Oilseeds).
