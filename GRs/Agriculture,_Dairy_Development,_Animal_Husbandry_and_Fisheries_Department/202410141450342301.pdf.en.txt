Temporary appointments are being made subject to verification of the validity of their caste certificate for candidates who have been placed in seats reserved for the category and have not submitted a validity certificate to the government before appointment. On receipt of the appointment order, such candidate shall be required to get his / her caste certificates validated by the concerned caste verification committee within six months from the date of the appointment order. All the concerned candidates shall, as soon as they accept the appointment, submit a proposal for validation of caste to the respective Caste Verification Committee and a certified copy of the receipt given by the Caste Verification Committee to the appointing authority. If the caste verification committee finds the caste certificate invalid, the appointment orders issued to the candidate concerned should be cancelled forthwith, and the appointing authority should take immediate action against the candidate in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Denotified Castes, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes (Regulation of Issue and Verification of Caste Certificates) Act, 2000. The Commissioner (Agriculture), Agriculture Commissionerate, Pune will be responsible for taking necessary action in this regard. It will be mandatory for all concerned candidates to submit a proposal for caste verification to the caste verification committee as soon as they join the appointment post.
