The government has decided to implement the Pest and Disease Surveillance and Advisory (CropSAP) scheme for the management of pests and diseases on major crops such as soybean, cotton, paddy, tur, gram, maize, sorghum and sugarcane, as well as fruits mango, pomegranate, banana, mango, orange, chikoo, cashew, ladyfinger and tomato. A sum of Rs. 5.25 crore has been sanctioned.
The expenditure for implementation of the CropSap Scheme in 2022-23 should be met from the approved provision for 2022-23 under the following head: Demand No. D-3 2401, Crop Protection, 102-Foodgrains, (00) (30) Pest and Disease Prevention and Advisory Project on Crops (24019242) State Plan, 33 Financial Assistance. The Commissioner (Agriculture) will be responsible for expenditure as per provisions of 10.06.2022.
