The Proposal:
To implement Maha-Agritech projects using satellite and drone technology in the State from Kharif and Rabi season 2019-20, Reference No. The decision was approved by the government.
The project will use satellite and drone technology under the Maha-Agritech project to assess the damage caused to limited area at the local level, accurately calculate the crop-wise area of cultivation in the state, determine the area correction factor for kharif and rabi seasons of all major crops up to the level of revenue board, provide advice and guidance to farmers on crop protection from pests and diseases by conducting regular surveys of crop health, make yield estimation of crops and based on that, plan the production of farm produce. The project will be implemented in collaboration with the Government of Maharashtra, Maharashtra Remote Sensing Solutions Centre, Nagpur (MRSAC) and National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC ISRO).
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Remote Sensing Measures Centre, Nagpur (MRSAC) has proposed to implement the Maha-Agritech project in collaboration with National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC-ISRO), Hyderabad for crop sowing, crop health and crop production using satellite and drone technology and localized damage to agriculture due to various natural calamities in the State from Kharif and Rabi season 2019-20. 17.68 crore is being disbursed.
