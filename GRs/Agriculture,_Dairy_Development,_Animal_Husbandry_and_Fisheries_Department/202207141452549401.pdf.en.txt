Introduction Under the National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture, the Rain-fed Area Development (RAD) scheme is being implemented with a Central and State share of 60:40 subsidy.
The Government of India has notified implementation of Dryland Area Development (RAD) programmes under National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) in the National Agriculture Development Scheme (RKVY) Cafeteria from 2022-23. The meeting of the Project Sanctioning Committee at the Central Government level for the year 2022-23 was held on 10.06.2022.
In the meeting, the meeting was apprised of the Central share of Rs. 2000.00 lakhs and the State share of Rs. 1333.00 lacs as against a total of Rs. 3333.33 lakh has been provided in the Annual Action Plan (AAP) vide Government of India Reference No. Approved by letter of 5. Further, the inclusion of National Agriculture Development Scheme under RKVY Cafeteria has been approved in the meeting of SLSC dated 5.7.2022 under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, RKVY.
Accordingly, under the National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA), the Central share for the year 2022-23 under Dryland Area Development (RAD) is Rs. 2000.00 lakhs and State share of Rs. 1333.33 lakh. The matter of providing administrative approval to the annual action plan of Rs. 3333.33 lakh was under consideration of the Government.

Priority should be given to small and marginal farmers or groups of such farmers while selecting beneficiary farmers under various components under the RAD scheme. Also, applications of farmers should be invited and beneficiaries should be selected in a transparent manner.
Under this campaign, for the purchase of various inputs (fertilizers, medicines, seeds, irrigation tools, machines/ tools, etc.), direct benefit transfer should be done directly to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries as per the prevailing policy of the government. Beneficiaries will have the freedom to buy these items from the open market. However, the Government may take a decision in this regard if the inputs produced by the Government agencies/ PSUs of the Government are being supplied under this Mission.
