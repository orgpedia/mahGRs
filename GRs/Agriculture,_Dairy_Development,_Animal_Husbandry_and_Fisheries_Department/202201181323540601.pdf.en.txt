Preface- Anandwadi, Ta. Rs. The administrative approval has been given for the work worth Rs. 149.80 crore. It was under consideration of the government to constitute a committee to verify the difference between the results of the filling mentioned in the administrative approval of Anandwadi fishpond, the results of the filling mentioned in the tender and the results required on the site of the entire project during the actual construction and to ensure the results of the filling required to complete the project.
Government Decision: A committee is being constituted to ascertain the cost of the increased quantity of filling work at the fishponds in Anandwadi, Ta Devgad, G. Sindhudurg as follows:

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To confirm the actual quantity of laterite stone material required for reclamation to create a land parcel to accommodate all necessary off-shore facilities as per Administrative Approval dated 28/03/2018 accorded by Govt. of India for Fishing Harbour at Anandwadi Tal. Deogad Dist. Sindhudurg.
2) The committee should submit the report within 15 days.
