Circular: Students from economically weaker sections (EBCs) studying in professional and higher education courses approved by the Government are required to apply for admission under Section No. 1 of the Act in the context of Higher and Technical Education Department. Under the decision of the government dated 13th October, 2016," Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Reimbursement Scheme" and" Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme" have been started for the maintenance of hostels. Both these schemes have been implemented under the Government Decision dated 26th December, 2016 under Article 2 of the Constitution of India regarding students from economically weaker sections studying in colleges under Maharashtra University of Animal and Fisheries Sciences.
The Department of Higher and Technical Education vide circular dated 14th February, 2018 vide Reference No. 3 dated 14th February, 2018, while admitting students belonging to economically weaker sections, shall not insist that 100% of the tuition fee or the full amount be paid by the eligible students at the time of admission. Students belonging to economically weaker sections who are eligible for 100% reimbursement of tuition fees should not be forced to pay tuition fees at the time of admission.
Also, students who are eligible for 50 per cent reimbursement of tuition fee will be eligible for admission if they pay 50 per cent of the tuition fee at the time of admission. In this regard, strict instructions have been issued that if the educational institutions insist or demand the full payment of tuition fees at the time of admission from a deserving student belonging to the economically weaker section, action will be taken against such colleges.
