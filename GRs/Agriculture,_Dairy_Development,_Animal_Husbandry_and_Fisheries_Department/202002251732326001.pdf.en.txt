Introduction: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched in the State in Kharif 2019. The scheme is being implemented through two insurance companies namely Agriculture Insurance Company of India and Bajaj Allianz Insurance Company as per the Government decision dated 22.05.2019. The Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, vide Reference Letter No. 9, has released the balance amount under the head" Supplementary Grants under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, (2401B052)" to the above mentioned 2 companies under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Kharif Season 2019 towards crop insurance premium subsidy amounting to Rs. 42,46,02, 197/- is sought to be made available through reallocation under the head" State share of insurance premium under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (2401A664)." According to reference no. (11) Funds have been made available by way of reallocation under the decision of the Government. Therefore, the matter of disbursing this amount as crop insurance premium subsidy was under the consideration of the government.
