Environment Service Scheme (ESS) was launched in 2011. The scheme is being implemented at the state level by the Department of Environment through the Centre for Environment Education (CEE), Pune, declared as a" Centre of Excellence" by the Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change. During the financial year 2018-19, a sum of Rs. 88,57,936/-has been sanctioned under Reference No.2 to the Budget received from the Centre for Environment Education, Pune on 26.08.2018 for implementation of the above programme. 50,00,000/- has been disbursed as first installment.
