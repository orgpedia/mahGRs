Preamble: Under the powers conferred under Section 2 and Section 4 of the Maharashtra Control of Dissolved and Non-Dissolved Waste (Control) Act, 2006, it is necessary to carry out an awareness campaign on plastic and thermocol products throughout the state. Various media should be used to spread awareness. The government was considering providing a grant for the purpose.
Government Decision: The Planning and Implementation Committee has approved a comprehensive media plan to create awareness among the people about the ban on plastic and thermocol through various media.
As per the media plan prepared by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has issued a notification regarding the publication of the advertisement. 3A has been issued.
