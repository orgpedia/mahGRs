Preamble: The State Electric Vehicle Policy, 2021 has been issued as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. In order to promote the purchase of electric vehicles, various incentives have been announced in this policy. This includes the Early Bird Incentive.
It was also decided that from April 1, 2022, all government, semi-government, local self-government institutions and vehicles purchased from government funds will be battery electric vehicles. In this decision, the condition has been made applicable from 01.01.2022 instead of 01.04.2022 by amending the Government Resolution at Reference No. Therefore, after January 1, 2022, it has become necessary to buy and use electric vehicles through all government, semi-government, local self-government institutions and vehicles purchased from government funds.
(3) Under the Government Decision No. 3 above, it has been made mandatory for some government, semi-government and local self-government institutions to purchase electric vehicles. However, the government officials who have to make frequent visits as part of official work have been asked to report to their offices. The government has exempted the purchase of electric vehicles till March 31, 2023. The proposal to extend the period was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Having regard to the matters mentioned in the Preamble, Government vide Government Resolution No.3 dated 12.04.2022 has decided to extend the period of exemption from purchase of electric vehicles. Starting from April 1, 2023. It will be extended until March 31, 2025.
