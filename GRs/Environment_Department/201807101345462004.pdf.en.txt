The proposal-
Under the State Lake Conservation Scheme, the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Sangli, will take up the work of conservation of the lake. The proposal for ecological conservation of the lake at Walwa was accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 13th March, 2013. In order to implement the works sanctioned in connection with the conservation of the aforesaid lake, a total amount of Rs. 100.00 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred Lakh only) was released by the Government vide its decisions dated 28th March, 2013 and 28th November, 2014 respectively, aggregating to Rs. 50.00 lakhs and Rs. 50.00 lakhs respectively. The utilization certificate for the full utilization of this amount has been submitted by the District Planning Committee, Collector's Office, Sangli. On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, the matter of releasing the remaining funds for the expenditure incurred on environmental conservation of the lake in Kuralap Ta, Walwa, District Sangli was under the consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government- 1. Under the State Lake Conservation Scheme under the State Plan, the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Sangli, shall make arrangements for the conservation of Kuralap, Ta. To conserve the lake at Walwa ecologically, an amount of Rs. 202.65 lakhs has been administratively approved vide Government Resolution dated 13th March, 2013 and out of the total amount of Rs. 182.39/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Two Lakhs Forty Nine Thousand only) payable to the State Account, an amount of Rs. 100.00 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred Lakh only) has been disbursed so far. An amount of Rs. 50,00,000/-lakh (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) is being sanctioned as the next instalment for carrying out the remaining works for the conservation of the lake at Walwa.
2. Kuralap under State Lake Conservation Scheme. Out of the total amount of Rs. 182.39 lakh (Rs.1 crore crore twenty two lakh forty nine thousand only) payable to the State for the environmental conservation of the lake at Walwa, District Sangli, the Government has decided to release the next instalment for the implementation of the remaining works.
