the prelude
The State Government has launched the State River Conservation Plan dated 01/03/2014 to conserve the rivers of the State ecologically. A typical program typically includes the following:
(i) Measures for collection, interception, diversion and transportation of sewage from the point where it is discharged into the river from the city/ village are not included in the city's underground sewerage scheme.
2) Installation of appropriate technology based sewage treatment system and its ancillary components, etc. for treatment of wastewater, taking into account the quantity, quality and local geographical conditions of wastewater.
(3) Establish the necessary systems/ facilities/ arrangements etc. to enable recycling and reuse of treated wastewater in the project area or nearby agriculture, industrial units, water bodies, etc., so that the revenue generated can be used to meet the cost of project maintenance, etc.
4) Construction of low cost toilets in the vicinity of the river, connecting the effluents to the sewage treatment system or taking similar measures for treatment, etc.
5) River valley development, measures to prevent erosion of river banks, conservation of river resources, etc.
Provisional appointment of Consultants/ Specialist Engineers etc. as per requirement for capacity building of technicians, training, research as well as for the success of the scheme as per prescribed standards.
Other important activities for pollution abatement as per the local conditions of the scheme, such as management and disposal of urban solid waste in a scientific manner, generation and utilization of non-conventional energy, public participation, etc.

The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
For the completion of the works mentioned in the' Form-A' attached with the Detailed Project Report for Ecologically Conservation of Ulhas River at Gram Panchayat Maharal and Gram Panchayat Kamba received from the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane under the State River Improvement Scheme, administrative approval is being given for the total amount of Rs. 6,30,09, 750/- for the proposal at Gram Panchayat, Maharal and Rs. 5,62,50, 000/- for the proposal at Gram Panchayat, Kamba. Of the total amount sanctioned, 80% will be the share of the State Government and 20% will be the share of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Thane. Separate orders will be issued by the government regarding the distribution of funds.
