The Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 8th February, 2022 in Reference No. (2) to give administrative approval to the Detailed Project Reports of the works to be taken up from the prize money of my Vasundhara Mission, as per the recommendation made after considering the proposal submitted by the Chief Officer, Hingoli Municipal Council, in Annexure-I to the Government Decision in Reference No. (4), for installation of on-grid solar panels at No. 7 in A. 1,85,45, 009/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Five Lakhs Forty Five Thousand Nine only) for installation of on-grid solar panels at six locations as indicated in Annexure-I attached to this Government Resolution. The Government is granting administrative approval to the works costing Rs. 1,87,46, 457/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Seven Lakhs Sixty Six Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Seven only) subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 4 below.
Terms and Conditions 1) The Hingoli Municipal Council shall be the implementing agency for the said works.
2) The economic and physical progress report of the implementation of the works in the detailed project report should be sent to the Directorate of my Vasundhara Mission by the 5th of every month.
3) The implementation of the work in the detailed project report should be completed within the next 6 months from the date of administrative approval.
4) If the expenditure is more than the prize money, it should be done from the self-fund.
(5) In case of any increase in the original project cost in the Detailed Project Report, no additional funds/ grants will be provided for the same.
