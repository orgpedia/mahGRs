The proposal-
The proposal for ecological conservation of Khandala Lake received by Lonavala Municipal Council, District Pune under State Lake Conservation Plan has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 11th March, 2013. In order to implement the works sanctioned in respect of conservation of the aforesaid lake, vide Government Resolution dated 11th March, 2013, a total amount of Rs. 32,00,000/- (Rs. 32 lakhs) and Government Resolution dated 6th March, 2017 amounting to Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rs. one crore). 1,32,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Two Lakhs only) was released. The Lonavala Municipal Council has submitted utilization certificates, physical and financial progress reports of the work as well as the minutes of the meeting of the Central Control Committee. On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, the government was considering to release the remaining funds for the expenditure incurred on environmental conservation of Khandala Lake in Lonavala Municipal Council area.
