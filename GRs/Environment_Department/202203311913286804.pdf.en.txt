Government Decision- Under the State Lake Conservation Plan received by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, for the environmental conservation of Kondi Lake, under the Government Decision dated 21st November, 2017, a total of 1,21,84, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty One Lakh Eighty Four Thousand Only) has been sanctioned.
