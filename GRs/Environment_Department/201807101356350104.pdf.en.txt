The proposal-
The proposal for the ecological conservation of the lake at Glen Ogle received by Mahabaleshwar Girishthan Municipal Council, District Satara, under the State Lake Conservation Plan, has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 17.02.2014. In order to implement the works sanctioned in connection with the conservation of the aforesaid lake, a sum of Rs. 250.00 lakhs (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakhs only) was released by the Government vide its decision dated 27th March, 2014 and dated 19th September, 2016, aggregating to Rs. 150.00 lakhs and Rs. 100.00 lakhs respectively. The utility certificate for the full utilisation of the amount has been submitted by the Mahabaleshwar Girishthan Municipal Council.
On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, the government was considering to release the remaining funds for the environmental conservation of the lake at Glen Ogle in Mahabaleshwar Girishthan Municipal Council.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- 1. Submitted by the Mahabaleshwar Giristhan Municipal Council under the State Lake Conservation Scheme under the State Plan, the amount payable to the State exchequer by the Government Decision dated 27th March, 2014 under the context of environmentally sound conservation of the lake at Glen Ogle is Rs. 3,20,76, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Twenty Lakhs).
