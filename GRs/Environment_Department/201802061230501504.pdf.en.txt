The proposal-
The proposal for conservation of Karpureswar Lake at Mauje Wadi Ratnagiri received under the State Lake Conservation Plan by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 7th January, 2016. A sum of Rs. 50.00 lakh (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) was released as per the Government Resolution dated 7th January, 2016 for the implementation of the works sanctioned in respect of conservation of the aforesaid lake. The concerned have submitted utility certificate, water quality report, physical and financial progress report of the work, third party audit report, copies of the order for the formation of the project control committee, etc. by the Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur. On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, the government was considering to release the remaining funds for the environmental conservation of Karpureswar Lake in Mauje Wadi Ratnagiri within the limits of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- 1. Under the State Plan for the conservation of Karpureswar Lake at Mauje Wadi, Ratnagiri, under the purview of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, an amount of Rs. 2,15,02, 814/- (Rupees Two Crore Fifteen Lakhs Two Thousand Eight Hundred and Fourteen Only) was administratively approved by the Government through a decision dated 7th January, 2016.
Out of the sanctioned amount, a sum of Rs. 50.00 lakh (Rupees Fifty only) has so far been disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur.
