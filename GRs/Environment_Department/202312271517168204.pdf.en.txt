the prelude
The State Government has launched the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07 to conserve the lakes, tanks and major water bodies in the State ecologically. A typical program typically includes the following:
1.Take measures to prevent pollution by identifying sources of water pollution.
2. Remove the harmful and organic sludge that has accumulated in the soil.
To maintain the quality of the lake, a. to take measures to control the growth of unwanted and nuisance plants in the lake.
(b) Improving the quality of water in the lake through biological processes in the lake.
Beautification of beaches, development of green belts, fencing, recreation parks, boating, low cost toilets etc.
4.Programmes for public awareness and measures to be taken according to local conditions to prevent pollution.
Under this scheme, the proposal of Katrapgaon Lake Lake submitted by the Chief Officer, Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council, District Thane, in the financial year 2023-24 was under the consideration of the government for in-principle approval and release of corresponding funds.

Government's decision:
Under the State Lake Conservation Scheme, the proposal for the environmental conservation of Katrapgaon Lake submitted by the Chief Officer, Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council, District Thane, was approved by the Steering Committee. Discussions were held in the meeting dated 04.07.2023 and subject to the decision in the minutes of the meeting, in-principle approval is being accorded in the limit of Rs. 4,53,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crore Fifty Three Lakh only). Of the Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) to the Principal, Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council, District Thane, subject to the following terms and conditions.
A table of basic information about the lake in the prescribed format, current status of the water in the lake, a resolution regarding the share to be paid by the local body, a resolution regarding the maintenance and repair of the lake after the conservation is completed, current photographs of the lake and information about the ownership of the lake should be given (copy of the seven twelve document)- the seven twelve attached to the proposal does not mention the name of the lake.
It has been noticed that a fountain and aerator with higher maintenance requirements have been proposed. Since the main objective of the State Lake Conservation Plan is to rejuvenate the lake, reduce pollution and conserve the lake, the work of planting trees/ afforestation, solar street lights, benches, nirmalikalash should be included instead of the main work of conservation.
As it has been observed that the proposed works involve various aspects such as insurance lab and testing charges, only GST will be admissible from now on.
As per the above, the detailed project report related to the lake should be submitted to the department, following which the administrative and final approval will be taken at the government level.
2.The Chief Officer, Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council, District Thane should take care of spending the funds distributed under this government decision only after receiving administrative and final approval to the proposal.
