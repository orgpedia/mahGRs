Preamble To remove the ambiguity regarding the level at which cases relating to direct service, promotion or deputation of government employees should be submitted and at which level the final decision should be taken, the Department of General Administration has decided to conduct the examination of the cases under the direct service, promotion or deputation. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 19.11.2016, the matter of declaring the Secretary of the Department as the" appointing authority" for the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D in the Department was under consideration.
Government Decision The following are being declared as" Appointment Authority" for Environment Department for taking final decision in the matter of appointment/ posting of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees in direct service, promotion or deputation.
