The functions of the State Level Committee shall be as follows:
(1) The Committee shall be responsible for compliance of the provisions of the said Order and the relevant provisions of the Air Pollution (Prevention and Control) Act.
(2) The Committee shall prepare a plan of action and ensure its implementation.
(3) The Chairman of the State Committee shall have the power to summon or summon from time to time the officers/ experts of other departments or research institutions for effective implementation of the mission policies of the Committee. It is mandatory for such persons/ experts to appear before the committee and give that information.
