Introduction- The proposal for the conservation of Shirsinghe Lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, in the financial year 2018-19 under the State Lake Conservation Plan. It was approved by the Steering Committee on 17.12.2018. Reference No. As per the decision of the Government, administrative approval was given to the proposal worth Rs 59,84,900/- for the conservation of the lake and Rs 26,93,205/- was disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri as the first installment.
Now, reference no. Under Section 3, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, has requested for approval to spend the amount of savings in some of the works sanctioned under the proposal. Accordingly, the proposal has been approved. It has been placed before the Steering Committee on 27.02.2023. As per the decision of the steering committee, the matter of approving the change in the proposal of Shirshinge Lake was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the State Lake Conservation Plan, the revised proposal for the environmental conservation of Shirsinghe Lake presented by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, was approved by the Steering Committee. Discussion was held on 27.02.2023. Subject to the decision in the minutes of the meeting, it is being approved to implement the approved works by including 8% Centage Charge/ Contingency Charge as per the guidelines of the National Lake Conservation Plan (NLCP Guidelines) in place of the GST, Centage Charge, Contingencies, Laboratory Charge included in the proposal without changing the original cost of the proposal for the conservation of Shirshinge Lake and to spend the amount saved in some of the approved works under the proposal for other approved works in the proposal.
(2) The Government Resolution submitted to the Government Circular No. 2023/ PROCESS. 40/ EARTH-3, dated 12th April, 2023 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
