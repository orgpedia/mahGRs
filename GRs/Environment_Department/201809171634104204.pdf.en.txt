Environment Service Scheme (ESS) was started in the middle and higher secondary schools of the state of Maharashtra by the Department of Environment with the aim of creating an" environmentally sensitive and aware" future generation by inculcating the importance of environmental conservation and protection in the students at the school level. In the first phase, 50 schools in 12 districts of the state will be covered. The scheme is being implemented at the state level by the Department of Environment through the Centre for Environment Education (CEE), Pune, declared as a" Centre of Excellence" by the Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.
