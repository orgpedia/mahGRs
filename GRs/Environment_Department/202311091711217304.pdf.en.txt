In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07.
Under this scheme, in the financial year 2021-22, the proposal of Daivagya Bhavan Lake submitted by the Chief Officer, Municipal Council Ratnagiri, G. Ratnagiri, was approved at a cost of Rs. In-principle approval within the limit of Rs. 40.00 lakhs and a corresponding grant of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only). The Government decision was issued on 31.03.2022. Further, Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) has been disbursed to the Principal, Municipal Council Ratnagiri, G. Ratnagiri.
In the meantime, Daivagya Bhavan, submitted a detailed project report of Rs 37,83,376/- to the department by the Chief Officer, Municipal Council Ratnagiri, G. Ratnagiri. It was placed before the Steering Committee on 4th July, 2023. As per the decision of the Steering Committee, the Steering Committee has given final approval to the proposal of Rs. 37,83,376/- for the conservation of Daivagya Bhawan Lake. Therefore, the matter of giving final administrative approval to the proposal for conservation of Daivagya Bhawan Lake and sanctioning the expenditure of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) disbursed was under consideration of the Government.
