The proposal: The National Green Tribunal (NGT) vide its original application No. 384/2016 in Khalid Ashraf v. Union of India and Others. In compliance with the order of the National Green Tribunal, it is necessary to prohibit the use of such plastic or synthetic synthetic yarn in order to avoid adverse effects on human beings and the parties.
Accordingly, in exercise of the powers conferred under Rule 4 of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, the Government of Maharashtra has issued a notification dated 01.03.2023 to indicate the adverse impact of the use of such synthetic yarn on the general safety and biodiversity and to promote the use of alternative eco-friendly yarns in place of such yarn. The matter of preparing a modality for the effective implementation of this notification was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The following modalities have been worked out for effective implementation of the notification issued by the Department of Environment and Climate Change dated 01.03.2023.
