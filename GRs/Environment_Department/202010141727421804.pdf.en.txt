Government's decision:
Considering the factors mentioned in the Preamble, the Government is approving the implementation of" Majhi Vasundhara" Mission based on the five elements of nature namely Earth, Air, Water, Fire and Sky in the following 667 Local Bodies of the State during the period of six months from 2nd October, 2020 to 31st March, 2021.

The Commissioner, Municipal Corporation, Chief Officer, Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats and Gram Sevaks, Gram Panchayats will be responsible for the effective implementation of this campaign in the respective local bodies. The District Collectors, District Rural Development Officers and the Chief Executive Officers of the respective Zilla Parishads have been entrusted with the responsibility of monitoring the progress of the programme.
Under this campaign, 1500 marks have been kept for evaluating the work done by the local bodies on the five elements related to nature- earth, air, water, fire and sky.
