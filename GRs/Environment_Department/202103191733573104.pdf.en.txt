Preamble- State Lake Conservation The proposal for conservation of Motiram Lake received from the Yonjetarganta Pen Municipal Council, District Raigad has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 5th December, 2012. In order to implement the works sanctioned for the conservation of the aforesaid lake, funds amounting to Rs. 125.00 lakh, Rs.21 lakh and Rs. 71,43,882/- were released under the Government Decisions dated 19th March, 2013, 31st March, 2015 and 19th September, 2016 respectively.
The concerned have submitted utilization certificate, physical progress report and financial progress report for the full utilization of the amount. On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, the matter of releasing the remaining funds for the environmental conservation of Motiram Lake in Pen Municipal Council limits was under the consideration of the government.
