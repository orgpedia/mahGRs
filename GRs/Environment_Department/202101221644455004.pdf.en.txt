Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 14.10.2020, the" Majhi Vasundhara" campaign based on the five elements of nature- Earth, Air, Water, Fire and Sky- has been approved to be implemented in 667 local bodies in the State. This includes 272 Panchayat Raj Institutions (Gram Panchayats). Gram Panchayats with a population of more than 10,000 and Gram Panchayats with a population of more than 5,000 in districts where there is no Gram Panchayat with a population of more than 10.000 have been included. The gram panchayats of Sindhudurg district were not included in this. Whereas, the request for inclusion of certain Gram Panchayats in the Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council areas in some of the districts covered in the Mission and certain Gram Panchayats with a population of more than 10,000 is contextual.
