Preamble: - Central Government's' Mission Life '? This theme is accepted. An eco-friendly lifestyle has been adopted in Mission Life. In line with this, the state government has taken up a campaign to celebrate the eco-friendly festival of Diwali this year. Under this campaign, special attention will be paid to celebrating an air pollution-free and noise pollution-free Diwali.
In accordance with the procedure prescribed in the Government Decision dated 13/08/2021 of the General Administration Department, the Media Planning and Implementation Committee of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations in its meetings held on 10/10/2025 has approved the Publication Media Plan prepared at a cost of Rs. 14,98,00, 000 / - after deliberations on the action plan proposed for wider publication and dissemination of the policy for celebrating the environment friendly Diwali festival. The issue of granting administrative approval to the media plan was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
1. Media Plan Planning and Implementation Committee, Directorate General of Information and Public Relations is giving administrative approval to a Publicity Media Plan of Rs. 14,98,00, 000- / - (Rupees Fourteen Crore Ninety-eight Lakh only) (including GST) approved for implementation in the State in connection with the celebration of the environment friendly Diwali festival. (With Annexure-A) 2. In pursuance of the said Framework, the Media Plan Planning and Implementation Committee is approved to proceed in accordance with the recommendations of the Committee in its decision in the minutes of its meeting dated 10/10/2025.
3. Maharashtra Pollution Control Board is being directed to meet the expenditure for this purpose from the funds of Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, Mumbai.

4. Action should be taken at the level of the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board to ensure that the work on the said plan is completed in accordance with the recommendations of the said Media Plan Planning and Implementation Committee decision.
