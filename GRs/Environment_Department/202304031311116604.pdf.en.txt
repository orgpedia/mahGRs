The proposal-
The State Government has launched the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07 to conserve the lakes, tanks and major water bodies in the State ecologically.
Under this scheme, in the financial year 2021-22, the proposal of Ramdharaneshwar Lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Raigad, was approved at a cost of Rs. 500.00 lakhs in principle and Rs. 2,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Lakhs only) to release funds. The Government decision was issued on 31.03.2022.
Meanwhile, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Raigad, submitted a revised detailed project report of Rs 4,98,40, 339/- for the conservation of Ramdharaneshwar Lake to the department. It was placed before the Steering Committee on February 27, 2023. As per the decision of the Steering Committee, only 8% centage charge/ contingency charge will be admissible as per the guidelines of the National Lake Conservation Plan (NLCP Guidelines) in place of GST, Royalty, Contingencies, and Testing charges etc. In view of this, the Steering Committee has given final approval to the proposal of conservation of Ramdharaneshwar Lake at a total cost of Rs. 4,30,34, 511/-. Therefore, the proposal for the conservation of Ramdharaneshwar lake was under the consideration of the government.
