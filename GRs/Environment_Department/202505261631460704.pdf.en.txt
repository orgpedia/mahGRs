The Proposal:
The procedure for the award of cash prizes and the appropriation of the said prize money to the winning local bodies in the 12 groups of Majhi Basundhara Abhiyan 4.0 has been notified vide Government Decision dated 27.09.2024 at Reference No. (1). Accordingly, a Detailed Project Report of the works to be taken up by the local bodies out of the award amount has been prepared vide Government Decision dated 08.02.2022 at Reference No. Administrative approval has to be obtained from a committee headed by the Secretary, Department of Environment and Climate Change.
2. Accordingly, for grant of administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report received from the Awardee Local Bodies, Ms. The Committee has made recommendations after discussing the Detailed Project Report submitted by the Local Bodies in its meetings held on 02.05.2025 under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Environment & Climate Change. It was under the consideration of the government to grant administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report of the respective local bodies as recommended by the committee.
The government's decision:
