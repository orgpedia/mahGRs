The Proposal:
The project is being implemented under the National Plan for Conservation of Aquatic Ecosystems (NPCA). Under this Centrally Sponsored Scheme, with the administrative approval of the Department of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India, dated 12.09.2018, an amount of Rs. 12.21 crore has been approved under which Rs. 7,32,67, 800/- (60%) has been approved for Central Government and Rs. 4,88,45, 200/- (40%) for Solapur Municipal Corporation.

Government's decision
The Central Government has started disbursement of funds through Public Financial System (PFMS) for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in fund utilization.
