Preamble: Winners of 4 categories of Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan 1.0 were felicitated on 5th June, 2021 and the modalities for the award of cash prizes and allocation of the said prize money to these winning local bodies have been announced vide Government Resolution No. Accordingly, a detailed project report of the works to be taken up by the local bodies out of the award money has been prepared and submitted to the Government vide Government Resolution dated 8th February, 2022 at Reference No. Administrative approval has to be taken from the committee constituted under the chairmanship of Secretary, Environment and Climate Change.
2. Accordingly, for giving administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report received from the local bodies which have received the award. The Committee has made recommendations after discussing the Detailed Project Report submitted by 10 Local Bodies in its meeting held on 1 st November, 2022 under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Environment and Climate Change. As per the recommendation of the committee, the government was considering giving administrative approval to the detailed project report of the concerned local body.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the points mentioned in the Preamble, the detailed project report of the work to be undertaken by the Karjat Nagar Panchayat from the prize money of Rs. 2.00 crore under the Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan has been submitted by the Chief Officer, Karjat Nagar Panchayat with the technical approval of the City Engineer, Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation. The proposal submitted by the Karjat Nagar Panchayat. The date of constitution of the Committee under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Environment and Climate Change was considered in the meeting held on 1 st November, 2022. As per the recommendation of the Committee, the Government is granting administrative approval to the works in the Detailed Project Report submitted by Karjat Nagar Panchayat at a total cost of Rs. 2.00 crore subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 3 below as indicated in Annexure-I to this Government Resolution.
