Preamble- State Lake Conservation The proposal for conservation of Kavandal Lake received from Yonjetargant Pen Municipal Council, District Raigad has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 5th December, 2012. In order to implement the works sanctioned for the conservation of the aforesaid lake, funds amounting to Rs. 125.00 lakh and Rs. 20.90 lakh respectively were released under the Government Decisions dated 16th March, 2013 and 30th March, 2015 respectively. The concerned have submitted utilization certificate, physical progress report and financial progress report for the full utilization of the amount. On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, the matter of releasing the remaining funds for the environmental conservation of Kavandal Lake in the Pen Municipal Council area was under the consideration of the government.
