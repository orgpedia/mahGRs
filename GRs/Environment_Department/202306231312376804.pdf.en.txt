The proposal-
The Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change has published the Wetland (Conservation and Management) Rules, 2017 vide notification dated 26/09/2017. As per the provisions of this notification, the Maharashtra State Wetland Authority has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 06/02/18. Chairman of the Authority, Shri. Minister (Environment and Climate Change) The Principal Secretary (Environment and Climate Change) and the Director (Environment and Climate Change) are the Member Secretaries of the Authority. Further, under Rule 5 (6) of the said notification, a Technical Committee and Grievance Redressal Committee have been constituted to assist the Authority.
Government's decision
The Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change has published the Wetland (Conservation and Management) Rules, 2017 vide notification dated 26/09/2017. As per the provisions of this notification, the Maharashtra State Wetland Authority has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 06/02/18. Chairman of the Authority, Shri. Minister (Environment and Climate Change) The Principal Secretary (Environment and Climate Change) and the Director (Environment and Climate Change) are the Member Secretaries of the Authority. Further, under Rule 5 (6) of the said notification, a Technical Committee and Grievance Redressal Committee have been constituted to assist the Authority. The government was considering setting up a wetland committee at the district level to assist the Maharashtra State Wetland Authority. Accordingly, the following committees were established:
