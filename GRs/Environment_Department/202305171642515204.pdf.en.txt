As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 21.6.2006, the Ministry of Environment and Climate Change (MoEF& CC) is considering to convert 80% of the total 48 (43 regular sanctioned+ 5 supernumerary posts) posts into permanent and 20% of the posts into temporary posts as per the revised figure of posts in the establishment.
Government Decision: Ministry of Environment and Climate Change (MoEF& CC) has sanctioned a total of 48 posts (43 regular sanctioned+ 5 supernumerary posts) as per the revised figure of posts in the establishment. Out of the sanctioned posts, 37 posts are permanent and 11 posts are temporary. (1) The approval is being granted subject to the conditions contained in the Government Resolution dated 21.06.2006. The classification of these posts as permanent/ temporary is being done as shown in the attached Form-A.
2. The Department of Environment and Climate Change also issues Government Decisions. As on 6th April 2023, all posts of Group-C Drivers and Group-D Cadre have been declared as dead cadre. The number of posts has been fixed as many employees of the deceased cadre will be employed. The employees of the posts declared as dead cadre will continue to work in the superannuation post till their retirement. The posts will be vacant on account of retirement/ resignation/ death of the employee. Therefore, the classification of the posts declared dead into temporary posts is being done as shown in the attached Form-A.
Therefore, 80% of the regular cadre i.e. a total of 37 posts are considered permanent and 20% of the regular cadre i.e. 6 posts and 5 posts of dead cadre i.e. a total of 11 posts are considered temporary.
