In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07.
Under this scheme, in the financial year 2021-22, the proposal of Teli Alli Lake submitted by the Chief Officer, Municipal Council Ratnagiri, G. Ratnagiri, was approved at a cost of Rs. 20.00 lakhs in principle and Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) to release such funds. The Government decision was issued on 31.03.2022. Also, Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) has been disbursed to the Principal, Municipal Council Ratnagiri, G. Ratnagiri.
In the meantime, the Chief Officer, Municipal Council Ratnagiri, G. Ratnagiri, submitted a detailed project report of Rs. 18,90,600/- to the department. It was placed before the Steering Committee on 4th July, 2023. As per the decision of the Steering Committee, the Steering Committee has given final approval to the proposal of conservation of Teli Alli Lake worth Rs. 18,90,600/-. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to give final administrative approval to the proposal for conservation of Teli Alli Lake and to approve the expenditure of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only).
