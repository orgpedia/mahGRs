The proposal-
Keeping in view the local needs of the state for environmental conservation, innovative programs are being implemented by the Department of Environment with the participation of the people since 2010-2011. Accordingly, a meeting of the Steering Committee was held on 30th August, 2019. As per the decision taken in the meeting, the matter of selecting suitable proposals from the proposals received from June 5, 2019 to August 21, 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Out of the proposals received for implementation of Environment Conservation Innovative Programmes through Public Participation, the approved proposals (under Round XIII) and the State share (75%) of funds to be disbursed from the proposals received during the period from 5th June, 2019 to 21st August, 2019 are as follows:
