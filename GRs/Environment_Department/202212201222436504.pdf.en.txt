Introduction: The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act has been enacted with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their increasing expectations from the local administration. Accordingly, the Public Service, Designated Officers, First and Second Appellate Authorities and Rules time limits are required to be notified under this Act.
Government Decision: In order to provide the benefits of various services provided by the Department of Environment and Climate Change to the citizens of the state, the Maharashtra Public Service Rights Act, 2015 is determining the following public service.
