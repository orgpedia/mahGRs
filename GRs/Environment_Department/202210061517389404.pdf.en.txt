The Proposal:
The project is being implemented under the National Plan for Conservation of Aquatic Ecosystems (NPCA). Under this Centrally Sponsored Scheme, with the administrative approval of the Department of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India, dated 12.09.2018, an amount of Rs. 12.21 crore project for conservation of Dharmaveer Sambhaji Lake has been approved under which 60% (Rs. 7,32,67, 800/-) of Central Government and 40% (Rs. 4,88,45, 200/-) The participation of Solapur Municipal Corporation has been approved on behalf of the State Government. The first instalment of Rs. 3,66,33, 900/- (Rs. Three thousand nine hundred rupees) three hundred and sixty-six lakhs to the Solapur Municipal Corporation. It has been delivered as per the Government Decision dated 7th March, 2019. The Solapur Municipal Corporation has submitted the utilization certificate of the expenditure to the Central Government and the State Government.
The Central Government has released the second installment of Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) for this project which has been credited to the SNA account for the scheme. The matter was under consideration of the government to distribute this fund from the SNA account to the account of Solapur Municipal Corporation's Bank of Maharashtra, Ashirwad, Chhatrapati Rang Bhavan Road Camp Solapur branch (account number- 60405798218, IFSC-MAHB0000163).
