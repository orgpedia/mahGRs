In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07.
Under this scheme, in the financial year 2021-22, the proposal of Shiv Mandir (Chandore) Lake submitted by the District Planning Officer, District Planning Committee Raigad, was approved at a cost of Rs. 342. 90 lakh and in principle approval of Rs. 1,71,45, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy One Lakh Forty Five Thousand Only) to release the same amount. The Government decision was issued on 31.03.2022. Also, Rs. 1,71,45, 000/- has been distributed to the District Collector, G. Raigad.
Meanwhile, the District Planning Officer, Raigad District Planning Committee, has submitted to the department Rs. 3,19,65, 189/- as detailed in the Project Report.
