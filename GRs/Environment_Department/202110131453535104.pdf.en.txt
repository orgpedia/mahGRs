circular
(1) As per Rule 5 (4) of the Wetlands (Conservation and Management) Rules, 2017, the State Wetlands Authority shall have the following powers: The minutes of the meeting held on 26.8.2021.
The Proposal:
The third meeting of the State Wetland Authority constituted under Rule 5 (4) of the Wetland (Conservation and Management) Rules, 2017 of the Ministry of Environment and Forests was held on Friday. It was held on 26.8.2021. A summary of the meeting. The letter dated 23.9.2021 has been sent to all concerned.
2. In the meeting, the issue of complaint filed by Vanashakti with the Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change regarding construction in the area of Powai Lake, which is included as a wetland in the National Wetland Atlas, and the action to be taken accordingly, was discussed. Accordingly, a report was submitted to the state government. It was decided to constitute an expert committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Change Department. Accordingly, an expert committee was formed.
Government circular:
In response to the complaint filed by Vanashakti with the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change regarding construction in the area of Powai Lake, which is included as a wetland in the National Wetland Atlas, the following expert committee is being constituted to submit a report to the State Wetland Authority.

It will be mandatory to submit to the State Wetland Authority by 15.11.2021. Once the committee submits its report to the state wetland authority, the term of the expert committee will automatically end.
