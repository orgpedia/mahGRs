In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state from the environmental point of view, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan from the financial year 2006-07 under the State Plan. A typical program typically includes the following:
1. Identify sources of pollution of lake water and take measures to prevent pollution.
2. Removal of hazardous and organic sludge from the lake.
To maintain the quality of the lake, a. to take measures to control the growth of unwanted and nuisance plants in the lake.
(b) Improving the quality of water in the lake through biological processes in the lake.
Beautification of beaches, development of green belts, fencing, recreation parks, boating, low cost toilets etc.
Programmes for public participation and awareness and measures to be taken according to local conditions to prevent pollution.
Under this scheme, in the financial year 2022-23, the proposal of the lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Raigad, was under the consideration of the government for in-principle approval and release of corresponding funds.
Government's decision
Under the State Lake Conservation Plan, the proposal for the environmental conservation of the lake was examined by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Raigad. Discussion was held on 27.02.2023. 65,00,000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Lakh only) to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Raigad is hereby approved by this Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
In line with the work being proposed to conserve and protect the lake environmentally, instead of a complete protection wall, retaining wall, Gabion wall or similar RCC structures, the retaining structure/ Gabion wall/ retaining wall should be included with chain-link fencing at the required place and at the required height. Also, the work of tree fences, Sewage treatment plant (STP), Drainage Management System, Desilting, Dewatering, Plantation/ Afforestation, Aerators cum fountains, Solar Lightening, Immersion Ghats, Nirmalya Kalash and Public Awareness should be included as per the need.
As per the National Lake Conservation Plan (NLCP) guidelines, only centage charge/ contingency charge will be allowed.
Submit a letter of undertaking for depositing the share of the local body.
Current status of water in the lake- Water quality report prepared by collecting water samples from various places (at least 5 to 6) in the lake area and from a recognized laboratory should be submitted to measure the pollution level of the lake.
In the resolution of the Gram Panchayat, it has been mentioned that the pond should be cleaned and desilted. Instead, a request should be made to conserve the lake under the State Lake Conservation Plan.
The ownership of the said lake should be informed (copy of seven-twelve documents) The fund required for works related to lake beautification like non-core work (construction of ghats, gardens, pathways, installation of benches) etc. should not be more than 25%. The proposed fund should be based on the District Schedule Rate (DSR) issued by the Water Resources Department and similar authorities.
As per the above, the detailed project report related to the lake should be submitted to the department, following which the administrative and final approval will be taken at the government level.
The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Raigad shall ensure that the funds distributed under this Government decision are spent only after the administrative and final approval of the proposal.
