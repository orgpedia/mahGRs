The scope of the study group will be as follows:
1. Simplification of procedures with a view to providing the notified services to the citizens with greater ease.
Re-engineering the business process to make it smoother.
(2) Preparation of Common Application Form.
(3) Setting a time limit for the preservation of records in respect of notified services.
Duration of submission of report: -
The above study group should submit its report to the government within three months.
The study group may hold meetings as needed.
