Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory to fix the posts in the government service for persons with disabilities. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities notified under the Notification dated 4.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts Identification Suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 01.01.2021 in Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. Reference No. 5 As per the directions of the Government of Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, The proposal submitted for fixation of posts vide letter dated 16.02.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. The posts in the Group A to Group D category, which have been ensured by the Central Government for the disabled, are in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service.
Though the designations of the posts are different, the State Government is committed to ensure that such posts in the service are reserved for the disabled.
The notification was issued by the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021.
2. Par. In pursuance of the directions contained in clause (1), 29 cadre posts are being ensured for Divyangjans as indicated in the Statement A accompanying this Government Resolution on the establishment of Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
