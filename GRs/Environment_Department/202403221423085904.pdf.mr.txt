The Terms of reference of the said State Level High Power Committee will be as follows.
1) To apply the principle of environmental compensation to the industries/organizations not complying with the environmental standards and any directives related thereto. To deposit the fund in a separate bank account. Similarly, to create a guiding system for spending the said amount for environmental protection .
2) To ensure that, Environmental Damage Compensation Fund Plans are consistent with the State/District Environmental Plans as per order dated 26.09.2019.
3) Plans and progress in execution to be placed on websites of the States/UTs after every six months 4) To ensure that, part of funds to be provided for environment awareness through eco clubs in all Schools and Colleges up to grassroots levels in the State through the Education Department.
5) Committee has a liberty to coordinate with the Law and Justice Department 1n accordance with some legal points in this regard.
6) The committee will meet at least once in three months.

The Terms of Reference of the said Internal Committee will be as follows.
1) To prepare plans for utilization of Environmental Damage Compensation Funds and recommend them to the State Level High Power Committee .
2) The following schemes have been identified for utilization of Environmental damage Compensation funds which may be considered: a) Compliance with environmental standards and relevant directives for verification Industrial Inspection .
b) Establishment of continuous Water and Air Quality Monitoring Centers to strengthen the existing monitoring network .
c) Preparation of comprehensive industry documents on industrial sectors/clean technologies.
d) Investigation of environmental damage , preparation of DPR.
e) Remediation of contaminated areas .
f) Capacity building of Urban Local Bodies ( ULBs) / SPCBs / PCCs
