Government Decision: Considering the points mentioned in the preamble, I am pleased to inform that under the Vasundhara Abhiyan, the Gadhinglaj Municipal Council will receive Rs. Rs. 5.50. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) of Rs. 5,50,10, 588/- has been submitted by the Chief Officer, Gadhinglaj Municipal Council with the technical approval of the City Engineer, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation. The proposal was put forward by the Gandhinagar Municipal Council.
The date of constitution of the Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Change was considered in the meeting held on 23rd August, 2023. As per the recommendations of the committee, a sum of Rs.
5,50,10, 588/- (Rupees Five Crores Fifty Lakhs Ten Thousand Five Hundred Eighty Eight only), the Government is granting administrative approval to the works in the Detailed Project Report as indicated in Annexure-I to this Government Resolution, subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 3 below.
2. Under Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan, Gadhinglaj Municipal Council has been awarded the first prize at the state level for" Nagar Parishad and Nagar Panchayat 25 thousand to 50 thousand population group" and the prize for the highest performance in the land thematic, totaling Rs. A reward of Rs. 5.50 lakh was also announced. Out of this amount, Rs. The first instalment of Rs. 2.75 crore has been released to them. Since the detailed project report of the works to be taken up from the said prize money has been accorded administrative approval by the Government as per para 1 above, the Government is approving the release of the second installment of the prize money of Rs. 2.75 crore (Rupees Two Crore Seventy Five Lakh only) to the Gadhinglaj Municipal Council through the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
Terms and Conditions 1) The Gadhinglaj Municipal Council will be the implementing agency for the works in this Detailed Project Report.
2) The financial and physical progress report of the implementation of the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report should be sent to the Directorate of my Vasundhara Mission by the 5th of every month.
3) The implementation of the work in the detailed project report should be completed within the next 6 months from the date of administrative approval.
4) If the expenditure is more than the prize money, it should be done from the self-fund.
