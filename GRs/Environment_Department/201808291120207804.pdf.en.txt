The proposal-
The proposal for conservation of Pandharbodi Lake at Nagpur under the State Lake Conservation Plan was approved with the approval of the Planning and Finance Department vide Government Resolution dated 2nd January, 2013 at a cost of Rs. 3,33,79, 992/- (Rupees Three Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety Two only). Out of which, 70% of the admissible State share i.e. Rs. 2,33,65, 994/- (Rupees Two Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand Nine Hundred Four Hundred and Nine only) has been disbursed so far amounting to Rs. 1 50.00 lakh as per the Government Decision dated 17.01.2014 and Rs. 50.00 lakh as per the Government Decision dated 26.10.2015. The Nagpur Municipal Corporation has submitted a utilisation certificate for the full utilisation of the amount. On the basis of the above document and in accordance with the total expenditure, it was under the consideration of the government to release funds for the next installment of the expenditure incurred for the environmental conservation of Pandharbodi Lake in Nagpur.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- 1. Under the State Plan for Ecologically Conservation of Pandharbodi Lake under the State Lake Conservation Scheme, the Government had accorded administrative approval for the environmental conservation of Pandharbodi Lake under Nagpur Municipal Corporation vide Government Resolution dated 2nd January, 2013 for a total amount of Rs. 2,33,65, 994/- (Rupees Two Crore Thirty Three Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety Four only). Out of this amount, Rs. 150.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) has been disbursed so far.
