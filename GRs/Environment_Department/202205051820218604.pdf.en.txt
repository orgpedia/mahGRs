The Proposal:
The winners of the four categories of Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan 1.0 were felicitated on 5 th June, 2021 and the modalities for the award of cash prize and allocation of the said prize money to the winning local bodies have been announced vide Government Decision No. Accordingly, the detailed project report of the works to be undertaken by the local bodies out of the award money will be prepared and submitted to the Government vide Government Resolution dated 8 th February, 2022 at Reference No. Administrative approval has to be taken from the committee constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Change.
2. Accordingly, to approve the Detailed Project Report received from the local bodies which have been awarded. In the meeting held on 3 rd March, 2022 under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Change Department, the detailed project report submitted by 10 local bodies was discussed and on the recommendation of the committee, the matter of administrative approval of the detailed project report of the concerned local body was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
After considering the points mentioned in the introduction, a detailed project report of Rs. 3.00 crore worth of work to be undertaken from the prize money to be received by the local bodies under my Vasundhara Abhiyan was submitted by the Chief Officer, Shirdi Nagar Panchayat to the City Engineer, Ahmednagar.
