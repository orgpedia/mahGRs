Preamble: Under the powers conferred under Section 2 and Section 4 of the Maharashtra Control of Dissolved and Non-Dissolved Waste (Control) Act, 2006, it is necessary to carry out an awareness campaign on plastic and thermocol products throughout the state. Various media should be used to spread awareness. The government was considering providing a grant for the purpose.
Government Decision: The Planning and Implementation Committee has approved a comprehensive media plan to create awareness among the people about the ban on plastic and thermocol through various media.
As per the media plan prepared by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has issued a notification regarding the publication of the advertisement. 3A is defined as follows. Under this, the payment for the advertisement regarding the broadcast has been directed to be sent to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, Mumbai.
In line with the above mandate of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, sanction is being accorded for release of funds to the tune of Rs. The fund is being distributed to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board. For this, a cheque of Rs 1,36,416/- should be drawn in the name of Member Secretary, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, Mumbai. As per the payment received from the Member Secretary, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, the work of publication has been done as per the above plan. After verification, the payment is to be made.
