the prelude
The State Government has launched the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07 to conserve the lakes, tanks and major water bodies in the State ecologically. Under this scheme, for the financial year 2021-22, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nashik, Dist.
To approve the proposal of Mahadev Temple Storage Pond submitted by Nashik and to release the corresponding funds. The Government decision was issued on 31.03.2022. However, due to technical glitches in the bill portal, the payments for disbursement of funds could not be prepared and submitted to the accounts and accounts office. The funds have not been disbursed for the year 2021-22. The Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- for the financial year 2022-23 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the State Lake Conservation Scheme, the proposal for ecologically conservation of Mahadev Temple Storage Lake submitted through the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nashik, G. Nashik, is being approved in principle with a limit of Rs. 200.00 lakh. Of the Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nashik District.
In order to conserve the environmental aspect of the lake, works such as sewer interception and diversion, installation of Phytoride Technology, installation of STP, silt trap, aeration, low cost sanitation, water drainage should be proposed in accordance with the priority. As per the guidelines issued under the National Lake Conservation Plan by the Central Environment and Forest Department, the limit of core work should be 75% and non-core work should be 25%. It should be noted that the proposed fund should be based on the District Schedule Rate (DSR) issued by the Water Resources Department and similar authorities.
