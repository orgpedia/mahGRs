Government's decision:
Considering the points mentioned in the introduction, the detailed project report of Rs. 50.00 lakh worth of work to be undertaken from the prize money of Rs. 50.00 lakh to be received by Sindkhed Gram Panchayat under my Vasundhara Abhiyan has been submitted by the Gram Sevak, Gram Panchayat Sindkhed with the technical approval of the Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad, Buldhana.
The proposal was submitted by the Sindkhed Gram Panchayat. The date of constitution of the Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Change was considered in the meeting held on 23rd August, 2023. As per the recommendation of the said Committee, the detailed project report submitted by Sindkhed Gram Panchayat at a total cost of Rs. 50.00 lakh (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) includes construction of well at a cost of Rs. The Government is granting administrative approval to the remaining works costing Rs. 39,14,450/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Fourteen Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Five only) excluding the expenditure of Rs. 10,85,550/-, subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 4 below as indicated in Annexure-I to this Government Resolution.
2. Sindkhed Gram Panchayat Rs. An amount of Rs. 10,85,550/-is to be used for increasing the green cover in Gram Panchayats by planting trees of indigenous species. Detailed project
The committee recommended that the report be prepared and approved for implementation at the local body level with the technical approval of the concerned executive engineer, district council. No administrative approval will be required.
3. Under Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan, Sindkhed Gram Panchayat has been announced a reward of Rs. 50.00 lakh at the level of Amravati Division for" Gram Panchayat: Group with population less than 10,000." Out of this prize money, the first installment of Rs. 25.00 lakhs has been distributed to them. Since the detailed project report of the works to be taken up from the said prize money has been accorded administrative approval by the Government as per para 1 above, the Government is approving the release of the second installment of the prize money of Rs. 25.00 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) to Sindkhed Gram Panchayat through Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
4 Terms and Conditions 1) The Sindkhed Gram Panchayat will be the implementing agency for the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report.
