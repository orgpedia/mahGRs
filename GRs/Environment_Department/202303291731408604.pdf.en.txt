" Introduction:"" Jaanta Raja,"" written and directed by Padmavibhushan Shivshahir Babasaheb Purandare, will be held at Shivaji Park, Dadar from March 14, 2023 to March 19, 2023."
Government Decision: To consider the event and support the noble cause for maximum possible sponsorship from the government level for the successful conduct of the event.
Ashish Shelar, Member of Legislative Assembly has requested.
This largest play of Asia (Jaanta Raja) depicts the life history of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. More than 250 artists will perform on stage with a 5-storey set. More than 10,000 people are expected to attend the event every day. The Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) is the producer of the programme. Considering the importance of the life history of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the government has decided to accept the sponsorship of the above program by the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
