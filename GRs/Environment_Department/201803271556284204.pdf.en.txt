In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies ecologically, the State Government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07.
Under this scheme, under the Government decision dated 6th January, 2015 regarding the Sangam Lake Conservation Proposal submitted by the Buldana Municipal Council, a fund of Rs. 25.00 lakh has been distributed as Mobilization Fund for the works related to environmental matters like De-wedding, De-silting, Compound Wall, Drainage-Sewer Interception,& Diversion Sewage Treatment Plant (Phytoride), as well as preparation of Detailed Project Report, obtaining technical approval, selection of expert consultant agency, various tender process, selection of good environmental expert consultant agency etc. The Detailed Project Report submitted by the stakeholders under reference dated 2.7.2016 was placed for consideration in the Steering Committee meeting held on 4th July, 2016. The proposal was approved by the board with certain conditions. Further, the Principal, Buldana Municipal Council submitted a report dated 1.2.2017 stating that out of the Rs. 25.00 lakh distributed, Rs. 9.89 lakh has been spent and the Municipal Council, Buldana is ready to spend an amount of Rs. 2, 38232.12 crore over and above the proposed Rs.3 crore by submitting the Utilization Certificate. This information was given in the Steering Committee Meeting held on 13th April, 2017
