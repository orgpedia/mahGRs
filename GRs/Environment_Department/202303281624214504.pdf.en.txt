Government's decision
Under the State Lake Conservation Plan received by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Sangli, for the environmental conservation of Kavlapur Lake, under the Government Decision dated 18th September, 2019, a total amount of Rs. 2,50,25, 214/- (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Five Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Two Hundred Four Hundred Four Only) was sanctioned against the State share of Rs. 2,25,22, 692/-. Out of the sanctioned amount, a sum of Rs. 1,22,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Two Lakh only) has been disbursed so far and now through this Government decision, the amount of Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Two Lakh only) has been released as the next instalment for carrying out the remaining works of conservation of Kavlapur Lake.
