The proposal-
The State Government has launched the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07 to conserve the lakes, tanks and major water bodies in the State ecologically. A typical program typically includes the following:
1. Identify sources of pollution of lake water and take measures to prevent pollution.
2. Removal of hazardous and organic sludge from the lake.
To maintain the quality of the lake, 6. (a) To take measures to control the growth of unwanted and infested plants in the lake.
(b) Improvement of water quality through biological processes in the lake.
Beautification of beaches, development of green belts, fencing, recreation parks, boating, low cost toilets, etc.
Programmes for public participation and awareness and measures to be taken according to local conditions to prevent pollution.
