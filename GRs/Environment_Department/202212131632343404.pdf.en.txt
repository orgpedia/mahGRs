The proposal-
Pollution of the Panchganga River. In the meeting held on 09/11/2022 under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, Mr. The Chief Minister has given instructions. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a solar control committee for the prevention of pollution of the Panchganga River. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision

The above committee should find out the source of pollution of the Panchganga river and take action to remove the pollution, and the above committee should meet at least once in three months and submit a complete report of the action to the government. Thereafter, a review meeting will be held at the government level once in six months.
The Regional Officer/ Deputy Regional Officer, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, Kolhapur, as the Member Secretary of the above mentioned Committee, shall coordinate with the members of the Committee and conduct a meeting and take action so that the report of the action taken is submitted to the Government within the prescribed time.
