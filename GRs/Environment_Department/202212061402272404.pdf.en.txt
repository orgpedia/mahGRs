The Proposal:
The winners of the four categories of Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan 1.0 were felicitated on 5 th June, 2021 and the modalities of distribution of cash prizes and the said prize money to the winning local bodies have been announced vide Government Resolution No. Accordingly, a detailed project report of the works to be taken up by the local bodies out of the award money has been prepared and submitted to the Government vide Government Resolution dated 8th February, 2022 at Reference No. Administrative approval has to be taken from the committee constituted under the chairmanship of Secretary, Environment and Climate Change.
2. Accordingly, for giving administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report received from the local bodies which have received the award. The Committee has made recommendations after discussing the Detailed Project Report submitted by 10 Local Bodies in its meeting held on 1 st November, 2022 under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Environment and Climate Change. As per the recommendation of the committee, the government was considering giving administrative approval to the detailed project report of the concerned local body.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the points mentioned in the introduction, the detailed project report worth Rs. 1.50 crore to be undertaken from the prize money received by the local bodies under the Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan has been submitted by the Gram Sevak, Pimpalgaon Baswant Gram Panchayat with the technical approval of the City Engineer, Nashik Municipal Corporation. The proposal was submitted by the Pimpalgaon Baswant Gram Panchayat.
