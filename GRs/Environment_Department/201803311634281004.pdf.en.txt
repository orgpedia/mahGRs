In order to protect and conserve the coastal area of the State ecologically, the Ministry of Environment and Forests, Government of India, vide notification dated 31/12/2008, has constituted the Maharashtra Coastal Zone Management Authority in the State. Under this, the work of identifying eco-sensitive areas in the coastal areas of the state, preparing its management plans, protecting the mangrove forest and coordinating the environmental management of the coastal areas as a whole is done by this authority. An agreement in this regard has been signed with the Centrally Sponsored Agency for preparation of Maritime Zone Management Plans and they are paid based on the progress of the work.
