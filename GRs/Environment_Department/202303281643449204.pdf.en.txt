In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07. Under this scheme, in the financial year 2021-22, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, Dist.
Approval for the proposal of the diversion lake submitted by Ratnagiri and release of funds to the tune of Rs. The Government decision was issued on 31.03.2022. However, due to technical glitches in the bill portal, the payments for disbursement of funds could not be prepared and submitted to the accounts and accounts office. The funds have not been released for 2021-22. Now it is Rs. 30,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government for disbursement during the financial year 2022-23.
Government's decision:
Under the State Lake Conservation Scheme, the proposal for the environmental conservation of the lake was submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri. Discussion was held in the meeting dated 17.03.2022. Subject to the decision in the minutes of the meeting, a proposal for the environmental conservation of the diversion lake was made. As per the Government decision dated 31st March, 2022, in-principle approval has been accorded for an amount of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakh only). Now, out of this amount, a sum of Rs. 30,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Lakh only) is being sanctioned to be distributed/ released to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri subject to the following terms and conditions.
In order to conserve the environmental aspect of the lake, works such as sewer interception and diversion, installation of Phytoride Technology, installation of STP, silt trap, aeration, low cost sanitation, removal of water hyacinth should be proposed. These works are being carried out as per the guidelines issued by the Central Environment and Forest Department under the National Lake Conservation Plan.
