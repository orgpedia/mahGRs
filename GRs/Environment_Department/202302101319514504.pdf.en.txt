Preamble:" Sumangalam Panchamahabhoota Mahotsav" will be organized by Shri Kshetra Siddhagiri Mahasansthan, Kanerimath, Kolhapur from 20th February, 2023 to 26th February, 2023. In connection with the festival, Ma. The meeting was held on 10th January, 2023 under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. In the meeting, Pujya Sri Aashaya Kandasiddeshwara Swamiji had requested for proper cooperation from the government level for the successful conduct of this Lokotsava. According to The Chief Minister had assured the cooperation from the government level in connection with the proposed Sumangalam Panchamahabhoota Lokotsava. All Ministries/ Departments have been directed to set up stalls in the Lokotsav to disseminate information about various Government schemes, development works undertaken by the Government through audio-visual and other mediums to about 30 lakh citizens visiting the festival.
Keeping in mind that the five elements of Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Sky are complementary to solve the emerging problems based on Indian culture and tradition, the Panchmahabhoot Mahotsav is also being organized from 20th to 26th February, 2023 to spread awareness, organize exhibitions, lectures, competitions, activities, etc. and to encourage people to change their lifestyle. The festival will have five thematic themes- Earth (Earth), Water (Water), Energy (Fire), Air (Air), and Sky (Sky)- with their significance, properties, uses, uses, and abuses, good and bad effects/ side effects, questions/ problems, solutions, necessary lifestyle changes, etc.
The government was planning to provide funds for the event.
