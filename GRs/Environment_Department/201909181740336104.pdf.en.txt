The committee will work as follows:
To review the draft interventions and citizen action plans submitted by the expert committee of IIT (B)/ NEERI and forward them to the State Level Air Quality Screening Committee.
Monitoring the progress of components/ activities under the National Clean Air Programme to ensure strict implementation of the programme.
To ensure that all interventions under the National Clean Air Programme are properly implemented in accordance with the programme documentation/ operational guidelines and to achieve the desired outcomes for the implementing agencies/ partners as per the city/ unit.
Review any other matters referred to them and make recommendations on these matters as necessary.
Encourage collaboration among organizations with specific components/ activities in the city under the National Clean Air Programme.
The civic-level committee will hold a meeting every month to review the progress of the above action.
The Oversight Committee shall select any other person as and when required.
The civic-level committee will be responsible for day-to-day monitoring and implementation.
The civic-level committee will submit a monthly progress report to the state-level Air Quality Control Committee (AQMC).
