Preamble- The proposal for the conservation of Vinhe Lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, during the financial year 2018-19 under the State Lake Conservation Plan was approved by the Cabinet. It was approved by the Steering Committee held on 06.03.2019. Reference No. As per the decision of the Government, administrative approval was given to the proposal worth Rs 52,79,562/- for the conservation of the lake and Rs 23,75,803/- was disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri as the first installment.
Now, reference no. Under Section 3, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, has requested for approval to spend the amount of savings in some of the works sanctioned under the proposal. Accordingly, the proposal has been approved. It has been placed before the Steering Committee on 27.02.2023. As per the decision of the steering committee, the matter of approving the change in the proposal of Vinhe Lake was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the State Lake Conservation Plan, the revised proposal for the ecological conservation of Vinhe Lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, was approved by the Steering Committee. Discussion was held on 27.02.2023. Subject to the decision in the minutes of the meeting, it is being approved to implement the approved works by including 8% Centage Charge/ Contingency Charge as per the guidelines of the National Lake Conservation Plan (NLCP) in place of the GST, Centage Charge, Contingencies, Laboratory Charge charges included in the proposal without changing the original cost of the proposal for the conservation of Vinhe Lake and to spend the amount of savings, if any, in some of the approved works under the proposal for other approved works in the proposal.
(2) The Government Decision made in accordance with the Government Circular No. 2023/ PROCESS. 40/ EARTH-3, dated 12th April, 2023 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
