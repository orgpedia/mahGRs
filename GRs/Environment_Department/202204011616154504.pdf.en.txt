Government Sanction Sheet: To prepare an action plan for the elimination of single-use plastic. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Reference No. A State-level Special Task Force has been set up as per the decision of the Government of India.
As the issue of sanitation in Gram Panchayats in line with the elimination of single use plastic is being handled by the Water Supply and Sanitation Department, the Principal Secretary, Water Supply and Sanitation Department is being included as a member in the State Level Special Task Force.
