Environment Service Scheme (ESS) was started in the year 2011 in the secondary schools of the state of Maharashtra by the Department of Environment with the aim of creating an environmentally sensitive and aware future generation by inculcating the importance of environmental conservation and protection in the students at the school level. In the first phase, 50 schools in 12 districts of the state will be covered. The scheme is being implemented by the Centre for Environment Education (CEE), Pune.
