Synchronized Governance of the Department Decision dated 14th February, 2018 Preamble- To conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the State ecologically, the State Government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07. Under this scheme, the proposal of Teleshwaranagar Lake submitted by Ratnagiri Zilla Parishad in the financial year 2017-18 has been given administrative approval as per the decision of the Government dated 14th February, 2018.
In pursuance of the above approval, the Government was contemplating to release funds for the conservation of lakes under the Teleshwaranagar Talavas Rajya Sarovar Samrakshana Yojana in Gram Panchayat Burundi, District Ratnagiri.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Out of the total amount of Rs. 1,13,92, 300/- (Rupees One Crore Thirteen Lakhs Ninety Two Thousand Three Hundred Only) for the environmental conservation of Teleshwaranagar Lake submitted by the CEO, ZP, Ratnagiri under the State Sarovar Yojana, the State Government has decided to disburse/ release Rs. 30.00 lakh (Rupees Thirty Lakh Only) to the CEO, ZP, Ratnagiri out of the total amount of Rs. 1,02,53, 070/- (Rupees One Crore Thirteen Lakhs Ninety Two Thousand Three Hundred Only) to the State Government, subject to all the guidelines, terms and conditions of the Government decision dated 14th February, 2018.
For this, a cheque of Rs. 30.00 lakh (Rupees Thirty Lakh only) should be drawn in the name of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri.
Mr. M. B. Hazari, Deputy Secretary, Department of Environment, Ministry of Environment is being declared as the Controller Officer. He shall take action to disburse Rs. 30.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Lakhs only) to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, by way of a separate cheque from the office of the Registrar and Accountant, Mumbai.
