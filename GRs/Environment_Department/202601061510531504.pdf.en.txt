The Proposal: -
The Union Ministry of Forests and Environment has issued the Air Pollution Control (Approval, Rejection or Cancellation of Consent) Guidelines, 2025 under GSR 84 (E), dated 29th January, 2025. The said guidelines relate to the air pollution control consent process for industrial projects under the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.
The said guidelines explain the procedures, fees, rules and responsibilities required for industrial pollution control. In the said guidelines, Paragraph No. 8 provides for the time limit for grant of consent under the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. If the consent letter application is not disposed of by the State Pollution Control Board within the said time limit, the matter will be dealt with in the said instructions in paragraph no. Instructions have been given to submit to the State Level Control Committee constituted under section 15. Paragraph No. 1 of the said Guidelines provides for the constitution of a State Level Control Committee to monitor and regulate the implementation of the said Guidelines for Settlement of Consent Petitions as well as the process of Settlement of Consent Petitions. Suggestions are given in 15.
Accordingly, a State Level Control Committee has been constituted under the Government Decision No. 2 in the context of the Air Pollution Control (Regarding Approval, Rejection or Cancellation of Consent) Guidelines, 2025 to monitor and regulate the process of disposal of consent forms as well as implementation of the guidelines for disposal of consent forms. The issue of a Government Corrigendum is under the consideration of the Government with a view to revising the post of Member Secretary of the Committee mentioned in the said Government Decision.
