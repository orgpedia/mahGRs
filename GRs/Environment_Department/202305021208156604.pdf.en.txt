Preamble- The proposal for the conservation of Khamb Lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, during the financial year 2018-19 under the State Lake Conservation Plan was approved by the Cabinet. It was approved by the Steering Committee on 17.09.2019. Reference No. As per the decision of the Government, administrative approval was accorded to the proposal for conservation of the said lake at a cost of Rs. 60,33,300/- and reference no. As per the decision of the Government of Tamil Nadu, a sum of Rs. 26,93,205/-was disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri as first instalment.
Now, reference no. Under Section 4, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, has requested for approval to spend the amount of savings on other approved works under the proposal as there is a saving in some of the works approved under the proposal. Accordingly, the proposal has been approved. It has been placed before the Steering Committee on 27.02.2023. As per the decision of the Steering Committee, the matter of approving the change in the proposal of Khumb Lake was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Under the State Lake Conservation Plan, the revised proposal for the environmental conservation of Khamb Lake presented by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri, was approved by the Steering Committee. Discussion was held on 27.02.2023. Subject to the decision in the minutes of the meeting, it is being approved to implement the approved works by including 8% Centage Charge/ Contingency Charge as per the guidelines of the National Lake Conservation Plan (NLCP Guidelines) in place of the GST, Centage Charge, Contingencies, Laboratory Charge included in the proposal without changing the original cost of the proposal for conservation of Pillar Lake and to spend the amount saved in some of the approved works under the proposal for other approved works in the proposal.
