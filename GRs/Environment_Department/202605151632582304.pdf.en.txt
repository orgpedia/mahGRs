Preamble :-
The Union Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change issued notification for the implementation of the Construction and Demolition Waste Management Rules 2025 vide notification dated 02 "April, 2025. The said rules came into force with effect from 1 ° 'April, 2026 across the country. As per Section 22 (3) of the said rules, directions have been given to the State Government to constitute a Monitoring Committee at the State Government level under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Environment & Climate Change Department to oversee the implementation of Construction and Demolition Waste Management Rules 2025 in the State.
In view of the above, State Government has decided to constitute State Level Monitoring
Committee for effective implementation of Construction and Demolition Waste Management
Rules 2025.
Government Resolution:-
In Consonance with above mentioned references, Government hereby constitutes
following State Level Monitoring Committee for effective implementation of Construction and
Demolition Waste Management Rules 2025. The Constitution of the Committee as follows :-

B) Term of Reference -
1. To supervise the implementation of Construction and Demolition Waste Management Rules 2025 in the State of Maharashtra.
2. The Non Official members of the Monitoring Committee shall be paid allowances for attending the meeting of the Committee as determined by the State Government and expenditure for the same will be borne by Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
3. The committee shall monitor the implementation of these rules and also take such measures as required for removal of difficulties at the state level.
4. The said committee shall meet from time to time as per the necessity of work mentioned above, however, at least once 1n three months.
