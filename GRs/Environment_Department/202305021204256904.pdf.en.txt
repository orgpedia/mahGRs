Introduction: The Steering Committee had approved the conservation proposal of Kelin Lake submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri during the financial year 2018-19 under the State Lake Conservation Plan.
Reference No. As per the decision of the Government, administrative approval was given to the proposal worth Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- for the conservation of the lake and Rs. 45,00,000/- was disbursed to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri as first installment.
Now, reference no. Under Section 3, the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, has requested for approval to spend the amount of savings in some of the works sanctioned under the proposal. Accordingly, the proposal has been approved. It has been placed before the Steering Committee on 27.02.2023. As per the decision of the steering committee, the matter of approving the change in the proposal of Kelin Lake was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Under the State Lake Conservation Plan, a revised proposal for the ecological conservation of Kelain Lake was submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad Ratnagiri. Discussion was held on 27.02.2023. Subject to the decision in the minutes of the meeting, it is being approved to implement the approved works by including% Centage Charge/ Contingency Charge as per the guidelines of the National Lake Conservation Plan (NLCP Guidelines) in place of the GST, Centage Charge, Contingencies, Laboratory Charge included in the proposal without changing the original cost of the proposal for the conservation of Kelin Lake and to spend the amount saved in some of the approved works under the proposal for other approved works in the proposal.
(2) The Government Resolution submitted to the Government Circular No. 2023/ PROCESS. 40/ EARTH-3, dated 12th April, 2023 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
