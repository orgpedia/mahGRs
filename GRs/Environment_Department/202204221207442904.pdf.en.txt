The competition will be held from 18 April 2022 to 28 February 2023. The evaluation of the work done under this competition will be done through a tripartite mechanism from March 1, 2023 to March 31, 2023 and the prize giving ceremony of this competition will be held on World Heritage Day i.e. April 18, 2023.
Detailed instructions regarding guidelines for participation in the competition, methodology of marking, evaluation etc. will be issued separately to the local bodies.
