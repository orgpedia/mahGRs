The Proposal:
The winners of the four categories of Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan 1.0 were felicitated on 5 th June, 2021 and the modalities of distribution of cash prizes and the said prize money to the winning local bodies have been announced vide Government Resolution No. Accordingly, a detailed project report of the works to be taken up by the local bodies out of the award money has been prepared and submitted to the Government vide Government Resolution dated 8th February, 2022 at Reference No. Administrative approval has to be taken from the committee constituted under the chairmanship of Secretary, Environment and Climate Change.
2. Accordingly, for giving administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report received from the local bodies which have received the award. The Committee has made recommendations after discussing the Detailed Project Report submitted by 10 Local Bodies in its meeting held on 1 st November, 2022 under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Environment and Climate Change.

Government's decision:
After considering the points mentioned in the introduction, the detailed project report worth Rs. 0.50 crore to be undertaken from the prize money to be received by the local bodies under my Vasundhara Abhiyan has been submitted by the Village Development Officer, Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat with the technical approval of the City Engineer, Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation. The proposal submitted by Loni Badruk Gram Panchayat. The Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Environment and Climate Change in its meeting held on November 1, 2022. As per the recommendation of the committee, the detailed project report submitted by Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat at a cost of Rs. 0.50 crore, excluding the works proposed at a cost of Rs. The Government is granting administrative approval to the works costing Rs.49, 02, 994.00 subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 4 below as indicated in Annexure-I to this Government Resolution.
2. Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat is required to utilise the amount of Rs. 8,97, 006.00 of Gram Panchayat towards increasing the green cover in the Gram Panchayat. Accordingly, the government is approving the preparation of a revised Detailed Project Report of this amount and its implementation at the local body level with the technical approval of the concerned City Engineer. The Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat will not need to seek administrative approval again for this.
Under the Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan, a reward of Rs. 0.50 crore has been announced to Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat on the basis of the best performance in the Gram Panchayat group. The first instalment of Rs. 0.25 crore has been disbursed to them.
Since the detailed project report of the works to be taken from this prize money has been given administrative approval by the Government as above, the Government is approving the second installment of the prize money of Rs. 0.25 crore to the Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat through the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
Terms and Conditions 1) The Loni Budruk Gram Panchayat will be the implementing agency for the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report.
2) The economic and physical progress report of the implementation of the works in the detailed project report should be sent to the Directorate of my Vasundhara Mission by the 5th of every month.
