The proposal was to issue a government decision on the sanction and disbursement of grants-in-aid to the Maharashtra Coastal Zone Management Authority.
GOVERNMENT DECISION In pursuance of the above proposal, the Government have decided to sanction and disburse grant-in-aid to the Maharashtra Coastal Zone Management Authority.
The Ministry of Environment and Forests, Government of India has constituted the Maharashtra Coastal Management Authority (MCZMA) under the Environment Protection Act, 1986 to protect and conserve the coastal areas of the state ecologically.
Maharashtra Coastal Management Authority (MCMA) has been entrusted with the task of identifying ecologically sensitive areas in the coastal areas of the state, preparing its Marine Zone Management Plan (C.Z.M.P.), protecting the mangroves and coordinating the environmental management of the coastal areas as a whole. through C.Z.M.A.)
