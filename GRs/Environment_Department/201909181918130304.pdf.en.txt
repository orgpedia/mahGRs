In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07. Under this scheme, generally the following activities are included 1. Identifying sources of pollution of lake water and taking measures to prevent pollution.
2. Removal of hazardous and organic sludge from the lake.
3. to maintain the quality of the pool,
(a) To take measures to control the growth of unwanted and infested plants in the pond.
(b) Improving the quality of water in the lake through biological processes in the lake.
Beautification of beaches, development of green belts, fencing, recreation parks, boating, low cost toilets etc.
Programmes for public participation and awareness and measures to be taken according to local conditions to prevent pollution.
