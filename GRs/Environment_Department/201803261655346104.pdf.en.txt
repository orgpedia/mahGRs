In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07. Under this scheme, the proposal of Kondi Lake submitted by Ratnagiri Zilla Parishad in the financial year 2017-18 has been given administrative approval as per the decision of the Government dated 14th February, 2018.
In accordance with the above approval, Gram Panchayat Jamge, Tt. The Government was considering to release funds for the conservation of Kondi Talavas in Dapoli, District Ratnagiri under the State Sarovar Scheme presented by the CEO, ZP, Ratnagiri.
For this, a cheque of Rs. 30.00 lakh (Rupees Thirty Lakh only) should be drawn in the name of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri.
