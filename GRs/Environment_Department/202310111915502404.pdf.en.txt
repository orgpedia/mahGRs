The Government is granting administrative approval to the works in the Detailed Project Report at a cost of Rs. 50,01,112/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs One Thousand One Hundred and Twelve only) under the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 3 below as indicated in Annexure-I to this Government Resolution.
2. Murgud Municipal Council under Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan has been awarded the third position at the state level in the" Municipal Council Group" High Jump with a cash prize of Rs. A reward of Rs. 50.00 lakh has been announced.
Out of this amount, Rs. The first instalment of Rs. 25.00 lakhs has been disbursed to them.
Since the detailed project report of the works to be taken up from the said prize money has been accorded administrative approval by the Government as per Para 1 above, the Government is approving the release of the second installment of the prize money of Rs. 25.00 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) to Murgud Municipal Council through Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
Terms and Conditions 1) Murgud Municipal Council will be the implementing agency for the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report.
2) The financial and physical progress report of the implementation of the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report should be sent to the Directorate of my Vasundhara Mission by the 5th of every month.
3) The implementation of the work in the detailed project report should be completed within the next 6 months from the date of administrative approval.
4) If the expenditure is more than the prize money, it should be done from the self-fund.
(5) In case of any increase in the original project cost in the Detailed Project Report, no additional funds/ grants will be provided for the same.
