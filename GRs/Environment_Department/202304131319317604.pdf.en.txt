The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Environment and Climate Change
Government Circular No: SEIAA-2022/ PR No.30/ SEIAA
New building, 15th floor
Ministry, Mumbai-32.
Dated- 12.04.2023.
Government circular
The National Green Tribunal, Western Bench, Pune, in its original application No. 13/2021 and Original Application No. As per the order dated 14/2021, it was under consideration of the Government to issue guidelines for effective and efficient implementation of the Environment Impact Assessment Notification, 2006 by evaluating the functioning of the State Level Environment Impact Assessment Authority.
For effective and efficient implementation of the Environment Impact Assessment Notification, 2006, all the administrative departments shall inform the local bodies/ planning authorities under them about the strict compliance of the said Standard Operating Procedure.
