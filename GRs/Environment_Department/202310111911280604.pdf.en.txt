Government Decision: Considering the points mentioned in the introduction, I have decided to provide Rs. Rs. 2.50. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) of Rs. 2,49,85, 231/- has been submitted by the Chief Officer, Satara Municipal Council with the technical approval of the City Engineer, Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City Municipal Corporation. The proposal was submitted by the Satara Municipal Council. The date of constitution of the Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Environment and Climate Change was considered in the meeting held on 23rd August, 2023. As per the recommendations of the committee, a sum of Rs.
2,49,85, 231/- (Rupees Two Crores Nineteen Fifty Five Lakhs Eighty Five Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty One only), the Government is granting administrative approval to the works in the Detailed Project Report as indicated in Annexure-I to this Government Resolution, subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 3 below.
2. Under Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan, Satara Municipal Council has been awarded the third prize (divided) at the state level in the" Amrit Group" with Rs. A reward of Rs. 2.50 lakh was also announced. Out of this amount, Rs. The first instalment of Rs. 1.25 crore has already been released. As the detailed project report of the works to be taken up from this prize money has been accorded administrative approval by the Government as per para 1 above, the Government is approving the disbursement of the second installment of the prize money of Rs. 1.25 crore (Rupees One Crore Twenty Five Lakh only) to Satara Municipal Council through Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
Terms and Conditions 1) The Satara Municipal Council shall be the implementing agency for the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report.
2) The financial and physical progress report of the implementation of the works mentioned in the Detailed Project Report should be sent to the Directorate of my Vasundhara Mission by the 5th of every month.
3) The implementation of the work in the detailed project report should be completed within the next 6 months from the date of administrative approval.
4) If the expenditure is more than the prize money, it should be done from the self-fund.
(5) In case of any increase in the original project cost in the Detailed Project Report, no additional funds/ grants will be provided for the same.
