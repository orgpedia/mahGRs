In order to conserve the lakes, lakes and large water bodies in the state ecologically, the state government has started the State Lake Conservation Plan under the State Plan from the financial year 2006-07. A typical program typically includes the following:
1. Identify sources of pollution of lake water and take measures to prevent pollution.
2. Removal of hazardous and organic sludge from the lake.
To maintain the quality of the lake, a. to take measures to control the growth of unwanted and nuisance plants in the lake.
(b) Improving the quality of water in the lake through biological processes in the lake.
Beautification of beaches, development of green belts, fencing, recreation parks, boating, low cost toilets etc.
Programmes for public participation and awareness and measures to be taken according to local conditions to prevent pollution.
Under this scheme, the government was considering to approve the proposal of' Kelin' lake submitted by the District Council in the financial year 2018-19 and to release funds.
A total amount of Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) is being accorded administrative approval for completion of works mentioned in the" Form A" attached as per the Detailed Project Report for Ecologically Conservation of the lake submitted by the CEO, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri under the State Sarovar Yojana subject to the following conditions and the conditions in the" Form B" attached. Out of this amount, 90% will be provided by the State Government and 10% by the Gram Panchayat. The financial outlay of the scheme is as follows:
