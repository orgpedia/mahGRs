The proposal-
Keeping in view the local needs of the state for environmental conservation, innovative programs are being implemented by the Department of Environment with the participation of the people since 2010-2011. Accordingly, a meeting of the Steering Committee was held on 8th March, 2019. As per the decision taken in the meeting, it was the discretion of the Government to select suitable proposals from the proposals received during the period from June 5, 2018 to September 30, 2018 and sanction funds to them.
Government's decision
Out of the proposals received for implementation of Environment Conservation Innovative Programmes through Public Participation, the approved proposals (under Round XII) and the funds to be disbursed (Government share 75%) from the proposals received during the period from 5th June, 2018 to September, 2019 are as follows: -

Under this Government decision, approval is being given for sanction of Rs. 33,71,250/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs Seventy One Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) to the concerned. As per the Government decision dated 08/11/2016, in addition to the 75% share of the Government, the concerned institution should open a separate bank account with a nationalized bank and deposit 25% of its share in it. This amount will be deposited in the nationalized bank in the name of the institution and after informing the government about it, 75% of the government share will be distributed to the institutions. Also, make sure that the expenses incurred for this scheme should be done from this account. The terms and conditions for disbursement of these funds are detailed in the accompanying Memorandum of Understanding.
