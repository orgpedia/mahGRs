The Proposal:
The State Government implements various subsidy schemes under the Sports Policy, 2012 for the development of sports. Among them, the stadium development grant scheme, financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities.

Schemes, grant schemes for construction of block/ district/ taluka sports complexes, grant schemes for residential and non-residential Government Sports Academy, District Sports Training Centre Scheme, etc. and State level sports training camps are also organized. Under all these schemes, the procurement of sports materials, training equipment and other materials is done at the level of Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune. These sports materials should be certified, quality and in accordance with the appropriate standards. For this, it will be appropriate to procure sports materials collectively for various schemes under the sports department. in order to provide a good and consistent quality
Sports materials will be made available to all the trainees/ players of the state and their performance will be enhanced.
Procurement of Sports Materials and Sports Equipment under various schemes at State level is governed by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Government Resolution, dated 01.12.2016, Rule No. 3.2 Under Purchase Type No. As per clause 5, it was proposed to" procure from empanelled/ contracted suppliers." Under this procurement process, the Government was considering to give administrative approval to invite necessary proposals in order to determine the manufacturer/ authorized distributor/ authorized supplier of the original material and to authorize the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune for this purpose.
Government's decision:
Under the State Sports Policy, 2012, a separate procurement process is carried out by the Directorate of Sports for the purchase of sports materials and training materials for the trainees/ players and coaches from the Government grants, Stadium Development Grant Scheme, Financial Assistance Scheme for the construction of sports facilities, Grant Scheme for the construction of Block/ District/ Taluk Sports Complexes, Grant Scheme for Residential and Non-Residential Government Sports Academy, District Sports Training Centre Scheme, etc. In addition to this, sports kits are also procured at the Directorate level for the state level sports training camps.
