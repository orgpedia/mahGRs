Nashik, English medium permanent unaided (primary, upper primary, secondary and higher secondary (ICSE 1st to 10th and state board 11th and 12th) school, Arjun Nagar, Gangapur Road, Chandshi, T. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to G. Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to inadequate physical facilities at the present location of the above school, it is proposed to shift to a spacious building which has been taken on lease for 30 years. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: 1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
2) The school will be required to ensure in writing that the parents of the students consent to the relocation, as well as the adjustment of all students in the school to the place of relocation.
3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
4) The school will be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the government from time to time. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
