Reference:- The Principal Secretary, Higher and Technical Education Department, Ministry of Education, Government of Maharashtra vide letter No. NEP 2023/ Q.No.150/ UE-3, D. 12.10.2023
Introduction: As per the provisions of the new National Education Policy, 2020, inclusion of Internship component in undergraduate and postgraduate courses has been made mandatory.
The Department of Higher and Technical Education of the State is striving to provide opportunities to the students of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the higher education institutions of the State to complete the internship and thereby acquire work experience and tangible skills that will be useful to them in the future. The department has been asked to chalk out a definite programme to implement such an internship policy in collaboration with all government departments in the state. The MoU was signed with EY LLP. A proposal has been submitted to the Department of School Education and Sports to agree on the possibility that the offices and institutions under the Department of School Education and Sports can become a platform for providing opportunities to graduate and post-graduate students of the state to complete their internship.
Government Decision: To provide opportunities for completion of internship to the students of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the higher education institutions of the state, it has been decided to make an effective use of the offices/ schools/ institutions etc. under this department as a platform. Director of Education (Planning), Directorate of Education (Planning), State of Maharashtra, Pune is being declared as the nodal officer by the Department to extend best possible support to EY LLP and to implement it on a regular basis after the blueprint is finalised, initially through a pilot project as per requirement and thereafter.
May EY LLP is advised to consult with the above mentioned nodal officers to finalize the outline and implementation plan of the Internship Programme.
