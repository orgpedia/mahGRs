The Proposal:
Six states (Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Kerala) have been selected on the basis of their performance in the Performance Grading Index for the project titled Strengthening Teaching Learning and Results for States funded by the World Bank.
The total cost of the project is Rs. 976.39 crore has been sanctioned with Central share and State share in the ratio of 60:40. Out of this, the central share of Rs. 585.83 crore. 390.56 crore.
