Preamble: Shri. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the English Medium Self-Financed Higher Primary (E1st to 8th) School, Sangli, to Malgaon Road, Dutt Colony East, Indiranagar Miraj, Dist.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from a building with inadequate physical facilities to a self-owned building with full facilities. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
