Government Order Regulating the Transfers of Government Employees of Maharashtra and Performing Government Duties
The Prevention of Delay Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 1st July, 2006.
Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and their performance of official duties.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared as per Section 6 of the Prevention of Delay Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority transferring under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the power of transfer of officers of various cadres in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
The officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred on administrative grounds with the prior approval of the senior authority in the presence of the competent authority as per the recommendation of the Civil Services Board and are being posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 before their names.
