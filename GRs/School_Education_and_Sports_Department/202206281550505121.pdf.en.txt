Introduction: Education is the foundation of all-round development of society. The development of the society and the nation is possible only through dedicated teachers. Every year, teachers with such a dedicated attitude are awarded the State Model Teacher Award by the Department of School Education and Sports.
Proposals for the State Model Teacher Award are invited online from eligible teachers by the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the existing guidelines/ norms, the proposals are scrutinized by the District Selection Committee at the district level and category wise eligible proposals are forwarded to the State Selection Committee constituted at the level of Director of Education. Based on the recommendations, the final selection of teachers is made by the State Selection Committee and the proposals are submitted for the approval of the government. The awards are usually announced on August 15 and distributed on September 5, Teacher's Day.
3. Reference No. As per the Government Circular dated 21st July, 2016, the criteria for State Model Teacher Award has been revised. The matter of implementing this scheme in the name of Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Gunagaurav Award by amending these criteria was under the consideration of the government. The letter No. The proposal has been received from the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune as on 2 nd February, 2022.
The Government Decision is being superseded by the Government Circular PTC 2016/ PR No.98/ TNT-4 dated 21st July, 2016.
The government has approved the implementation of this scheme by revising the criteria for the State Model Teacher Award under the name of Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award. Formation of State Selection Committee: For the Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award, the State Selection Committee will call the eligible teachers in the category-wise merit list from the district by the District Selection Committee in the physical interview. The State Selection Committee shall consider the educational, social status of the teachers as well as the sensitivity towards social equity, gender equality, special efforts made for this purpose, national perspective and work/ activities while making the final selection of teachers through online verification and scrutiny as per the existing norms. The final selection of teachers on the basis of this recommendation should be sent to the government for approval from the state selection committee.
A State Selection Committee should be constituted at the State level for selection to the Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award.
