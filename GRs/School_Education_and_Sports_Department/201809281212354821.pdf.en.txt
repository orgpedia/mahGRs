Government Decision: To pay 25% of the grant amount to be made available by the State Government to the Rashtriya Chhatra Sena offices in Maharashtra for organizing camps and trainings, Rs. 11.00 crore within the limit of 70% of the provision of Rs. 7.70 crore. 7,00,409/- in respect of Fund Reference No. It is hereby approved to disburse by Government Circular No. 1 and to place the said amount under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
