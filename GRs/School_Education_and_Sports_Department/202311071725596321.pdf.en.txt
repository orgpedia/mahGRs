Preamble:- All the part-time librarians working in the state will be provided to the government within the limit of 2118 full-time librarian posts available in the state. On the basis of the report received under section 5, the government decided to upgrade part-time librarians to the posts available after meeting the criteria of 1001 students as per the school mapping report by merging two and three schools, and to upgrade the remaining part-time librarians to full-time librarians in a phased manner. 13.04.2023 has been approved. The Government Resolution No. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Backward Classes and Minorities Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff Association, Maharashtra State has requested the Government to amend the conditions of the Government decision as the serving part-time librarians are not getting financial benefits as per the increased workload while upgrading (conversion) to the minimum pay level.
As per the conditions of this decision, the senior part-time librarians who have completed their service period in the post of part-time librarian for a long time are not getting financial benefits according to the increased workload when they are upgraded (converted) to full-time librarian at least in stages. It has come to the notice of the Government that the concerned Librarians are facing difficulties in obtaining certificates from the concerned school/ institution regarding the total service rendered in the past in accordance with the provisions of Section 2. Following that. The matter of amending the terms of the Government Resolution dated 13.04.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
