Reorganisation of School Tribunals
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No. 2021/ P. No. 139/21/ P.S.-5
Madama Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Extension Building, Mumbai 400 032
Date: June 2, 2022
Read:- Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution No. Review-2021/ P. No. 139/21/ P.S.-5, dated 20.m., 2022
Government Decision: As per the Government Decision dated 20 th May, 2022, it has been decided to close down the School Tribunals at Mumbai, Chandrapur and Solapur. The school tribunal in Mumbai has also been shifted to Navi Mumbai.
The decision to close down the school tribunals at Chandrapur and Solapur under the Government decision dated 20th May, 2022, mentioned herein, is hereby temporarily stayed until further orders.
