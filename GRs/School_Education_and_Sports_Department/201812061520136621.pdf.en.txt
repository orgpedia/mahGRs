the prelude
Satara Military School was established. Established on June 23, 1961, it is the first military school in the country. The main objective of this school is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children of Maharashtra at the grassroots level and at all levels to get a post of officer in the Indian Army through the National Defence Academy. A large number of students from this school have so far been admitted to the National Defence Academy.
The Satara Sainik School is sanctioned 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-salaried) Grants every year for the purpose of building maintenance, food allowance, horse maintenance, advertisement expenses, municipal taxes.
A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between the Sainik School Society and the State Government on 30th December, 2016 regarding the Satara Sainik School. According to the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Pune, Rs. A case-wise budget of Rs. 9.88 crore has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 12.10.2018. The total expenditure of Rs. 2,15,02, 538/- towards food allowance, building maintenance, horse maintenance, advertisement expenses, municipal taxes was under consideration of the Government.
