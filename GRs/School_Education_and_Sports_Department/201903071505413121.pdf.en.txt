Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Khelo India- National Programme for Development of Sports was implemented throughout the country vide Circular dated 16/10/2017. Under this scheme, annual sports competitions are organized for the youth. Accordingly, the Government of Maharashtra in association with the Government of India organized the Khelo India Youth Games-2019 for U-17 and U-21 boys from 9th to 20th January, 2019 at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mhalunge Balewadi, Pune.
Maharashtra State has secured the first position in the competition with 280 medals.
The tournament is being organised by the Maharashtra State Sports Council for the medal winners, coaches and managers. The felicitation ceremony was organized by the Minister on 31st January, 2019 at Mumbai. During the event, Ma. The Minister announced cash awards to the medal winners.
