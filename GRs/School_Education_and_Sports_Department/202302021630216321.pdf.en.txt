In order to honour the social work done by the youth of the state/ district of Maharashtra and to encourage them to do youth development work, the Department of School Education and Sports, Government of Maharashtra has approved the award of Youth Awards at the state and district level every year vide Government Resolution No.UKYO-2012/ PR.S. 65/ CRUSE-3 dated 12th November, 2013. For the year 2022-23, an amount of Rs. 125.00 lakhs out of the sanctioned amount of Rs. 52.50 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Two Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2022-23, a budget of Rs. 125.00 lakhs out of the sanctioned amount of Rs. 52.50 lakh (Rupees Fifty Two Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) is hereby approved to be released and placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
