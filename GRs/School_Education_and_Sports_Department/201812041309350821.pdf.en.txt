The Chhatrapati Shivaji Trophy Kabaddi Tournament is organized by the Government of Maharashtra every year at the state level to encourage the Kabaddi players of the state as per the point number 5.4 of the State Sports Policy 2012 and to provide them maximum opportunities to play in the state level tournament and to make the sports loving audience of the state get to see the skills of the quality players. Regarding the above competition No. As per the government's decision of 01, the rules have been set and the matter-wise expenditure for organizing the Chhatrapati Shivaji Trophy Kabaddi Tournament has been fixed.
