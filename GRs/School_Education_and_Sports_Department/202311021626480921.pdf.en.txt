The functions of the committee will be as follows: To prepare the draft of the Sports University Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra Act, to make recommendations on the necessary matters in line with the Sports University, to recommend the introduction of courses under the Sports University, etc.
The Committee shall meet at least once in a month and submit its report to the Government within three months.
