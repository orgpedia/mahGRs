The government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Basketball Tournament 2018-19 was held at Sabina Chandrashekhar Recreation Ground, Mumbai from 25th to 28th January 2019. The exam was conducted on January 3, 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Rs. 67,450/- (Rs. Only four hundred and fifty thousand) is being approved for this expenditure.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office shall relieve the player coaches/ managers for the training camp from 21st to 23rd January, 2019 and for the tournament from 25th to 28th January, 2019 with effect from 19th January, 2019 (M.No.) and provide the services of these players to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
