As it is not possible to actually start schools in the state in the situation arising out of the outbreak of COVID-19 in the state, the Government has decided to close the schools. The concerned District Collector, Municipal Commissioner and the concerned School Management Committee have been given the authority to take a decision regarding the opening of physical schools in accordance with the local conditions by starting the academic year from June 15, 2020. As per the Government Decision dated 15th June, 2020.
The government has announced the date of reopening of schools for classes 1 to 12. Also, online classes have been started. Now, online education should be provided to students from pre-primary to Class XII as mentioned in the table below.
