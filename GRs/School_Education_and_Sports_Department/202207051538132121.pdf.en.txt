The Principal of Arunrao Kalode Primary School, Khamla, Nagpur, which is run by Saibaba Public Service Institution, Nagpur, has been transferred to Hirabai Gaikwad Educational Institution, Nagpur.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No: PRASHAH 3022/ PR No.310/ SM-1
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: July 5, 2022
School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.MASHHA 2011/ 210/11)/ MASHI-3 dated 17th February, 2012
Reference: Letter dated 10th May, 2022 to the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur vide G.O. No.M./ B/ 3240/2022
Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the Marathi medium aided primary school (1st to 4th standard) school run by Saibaba Public Service Institution, Nagpur, Principal Arunrao Kalode Primary School, Khamla, Nagpur, to Hirabai Gaikwad Educational Institution, Nagpur, a minority institution, was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Hirabai Gaikwad Educational Institute, Nagpur on the following terms and conditions:
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall examine all the matters and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
