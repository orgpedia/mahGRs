Introduction: The players of the state's hockey game should get the maximum opportunity to play in the state tournament. Further, to enable them and the sports loving audience to witness the skill games of quality players, it has been decided by the Government on 19.11.2008 to organize State level Hockey Tournament for boys and girls under 19 years of age every year. The Government decision dated 3rd January, 2012 has increased the grant limit to Rs. 50.00 lakh for organizing State level Hockey Games.
The amount of grant for organizing this competition was decided in 2012 and considering the increase in expenditure on various aspects related to organizing the competition, the government was considering increasing the grant for this competition. The government has taken the following decisions:
