Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for shifting of the Art (Marathi Medium) Unaided and Science (English Medium) Junior College of Smt. Panchphulabai Fakirrao Bangar Junior College, Khatkali, Hingoli, run by Satya Shodhak Gahan Sevabhavi Sanstha, Aundha (Na), G. Hingoli, to Hingoli, beside Khaki Baba Math, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The said Junior College is proposed to be shifted from the existing rented building to its own self-contained building. The transfer of the said Junior College to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions:
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred Junior College shall not be relaxed for any reason.
2) It will be necessary to confirm in writing the consent of the parents of the students of the junior college to the migration, as well as to adjust the maximum number of students in the school to the place of migration.
3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
4) As per the orders issued by the Government from time to time, the pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the junior college. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
6) There will be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original government permission and approval in the case of the migrated junior college.
7) The Junior College will function at the relocated place on the same basis as the grant-in-aid at the place of origin.
8) It will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not more than that as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff. Also, since the Science stream (English medium) is always on a non-subsidised basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in it will remain with the institution.
(9) The Government shall have the right to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall take necessary further action regarding approval of shifting of the said Junior College and inform the Government about the action taken.
