Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. In this regard, the Central Government vide notification dated 04/01/2021 has published the list of posts for persons with benchmark disability notified on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of posts identification suitable for persons with benchmark disability notified on date 04/01/2021. It states that the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in the posts of Group A to Group D which are in the state government establishment and the posts in the list published by the Central Government whose duties, responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations are different.
The instructions to be ensured for the disabled are given under the Government Decision at Reference No. 7
