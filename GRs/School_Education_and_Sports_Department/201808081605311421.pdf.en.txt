The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of the Marathi medium unaided primary and upper primary school, Aurangabad, to Rajmata Jijau Multi-purpose Institute, Sillod, District Aurangabad, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the school where the management is changed for any reason.
(2) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Jalna shall ensure that the financial position of the institution is good as per the bank balance certificate and tenure of the institution.
(3) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts shall remain in operation and not more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.

It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy regarding filling up of vacancies from the permissible posts by additional staff.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(8) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(9) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall examine all the aspects and take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school including other necessary conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
