As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 of this Department, it has been decided to develop 488 schools connected with this Government decision as Model Schools by including the schools of the local bodies of the State, 81 schools of the erstwhile Maharashtra International Education Board, Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya, Government Educational Institutions and schools in urban areas. A total of 355 schools, including 293 primary and upper primary schools and 62 secondary and higher secondary schools, have received budgetary allocations for renovation.
The government was considering to allocate funds for the construction of these schools. Accordingly, the following decision was taken:
Government's decision:
A total of 355 schools, including 293 schools in Schedule-A and 62 schools in Schedule-B, linked to this decision of the government, will be given Rs. The Government is approving an expenditure of Rs. 53,97,15, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Three Crores Ninety Seven Lakh Fifteen Thousand only).
