Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for shifting of Marathi medium aided upper primary (class 1st to 7th) school, Phoenix Public School, Hirvi Nagar, Nagpur, run by Pramod Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur, to Bhandewadi Road, Bidgaon, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted to the land of the owner due to the dilapidation of the building and termination of the lease agreement. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
