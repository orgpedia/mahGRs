Washim, Marathi medium aided higher secondary (E. 11th to 12th Arts) School, Smt. Sarubai Tulshiram Devale Multi-purpose Educational Institution, Shevati, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the management of the institution to Karanja, G. Washim was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of this school is Mrs. Sarubai Tulshiram Devale Multi-purpose Educational Institution, Shevati Ta. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to G.Vashim, Karanja on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school in which the management has changed will not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management.

The Government will have the power to cancel the transfer if the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution.
