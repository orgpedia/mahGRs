The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the Marathi medium aided primary and upper primary school, Nagpur, to Om High School, Lalganj, Mehndi Bagh Road, Dist.
Government Decision: The above school is being shifted from a building with inadequate physical facilities to a self-sufficient building. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to accommodate all the students of the school at the place of shifting and to ensure that there is no objection/ objection from anyone regarding the shifting of the school.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The building in which the said school is proposed to be shifted, is being run by an aided school of another institution from class V to X. Two schools will be set up in the same building.
Therefore, it will be necessary to ensure that there are no disputes/ problems between the two institutions or otherwise.
