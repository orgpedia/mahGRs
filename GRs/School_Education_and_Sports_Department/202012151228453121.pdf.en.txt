Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution No. Abhanase 3820/ No.13/ Creuse-2, Dy. 5.3.2020 Preamble: Every year, All India Civil Services Competitions for about 15 sports at National level are held in different States of the country. The team from Maharashtra will be given a grant by the government to participate in the tournament. The State teams participated in the All India Civil Services National Games 2019-20. The government has passed and approved government decisions from time to time to provide the necessary expenses for the participation of this association. However, due to non-availability of funds allocated for the scheme last year, it was not possible to provide funds for the expenditure incurred for the year 2019-20. The government was planning to provide funds for this in the current financial year.
Government's decision:
Participation in the All India Civil Services Competition for the year 2019-20. 10.00 lakh (Rupees Ten Lakh only) and the same is being placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
