The proposal: self. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the English medium self-financed primary school, Tejsingrao Raje Bhosale Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur, operating Mamta English Primary School, Hudkeshwar Road, Nagpur, to Rural Development Multi-Purpose Institute, Bela, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Rural Development Multipurpose Institute, Bela, Nagpur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(3) As the school is self-financing English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
