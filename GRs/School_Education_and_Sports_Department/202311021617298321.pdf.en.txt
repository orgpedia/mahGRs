Introduction Various sports competitions at school level sponsored by the Sports Federation of India are organized every year from Taluka to National level. Maharashtra is represented by teams/ players in national level competitions in various sports. This competition is very useful to develop the love of sports among school children and to see their sportsmanship and this is what makes the best players. Sports events are equally important to create opportunities for the youth, especially in rural areas, to participate in various sports events. The best players of the state are selected for the national competition. The Government has taken the decision in accordance with the recommendations of the Sports Policy 2012. The modalities for conducting the School Games have been decided as on 24.12.2014.
