Government's decision:
Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan with 60% funding by the Central Government and 40% by the State Governments. The salary of the officers and employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state is paid from this. Out of the provision made in the budget under 01 wage target under General Account Head 2202I 612 for payment of salary to the working posts for the said scheme, an amount of Rs. 9,06,36, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crore Six Lakh Thirty Six Thousand only), as per the aforesaid reference No. The approval has been given under the Government Decision dated May 13, 2022.
This decision has been taken by the state government.
