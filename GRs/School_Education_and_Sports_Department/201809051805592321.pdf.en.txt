(20), (21) and (22) of the Government Decision dated 1st July, 2016 and 2nd July, 2016, the grant should be distributed to the remaining primary/ secondary schools and units excluding the schools and units in Form A and Form B with the Government Decision dated 9th May, 2018. The following orders shall apply:
(1) All the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 19th September, 2016 regarding the concerned school, weightage shall be required to be fulfilled.
(2) All documents must have been examined in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated May 9, 2018 and No. (3.1) to (3.8).
(3) No grant-in-aid shall be disbursed to a primary/ secondary school batch which is undergoing separate inspection/ proceedings in respect of complaints received regarding recognition/ shifting or transfer of the batch.
(4) The teachers and non-teaching staff of the said primary/ secondary schools and batches should be provided subsidy at the rate of 20% from April, 2018. Grants in excess of 20% should not be sanctioned under any circumstances.
(5) It should be checked at the level of the District Treasury Officer that the grant has not been made available to the said primary/ secondary school, units, at the level of the field officers.
The 20 per cent subsidy should not be given to primary/ secondary schools/ units which have already been provided with the subsidy. Also, departmental inquiry/ criminal proceedings should be initiated against such schools/ officials concerned.
The Commissioner of Education will be responsible for this.
(6) Government Resolution No. PRAMA-2018/ PR No. 73/ SM-4, dated 09th May, 2018 in Form A and Form B, 373 posts of teachers and 178 posts of non-teaching staff in 54 batches of 51 secondary schools and 19 secondary schools should not be disbursed subsidy until further orders of the Government.
(7) No expenditure shall be incurred for purposes other than those for which the fund/ grant is sanctioned. Also, funds cannot be transferred to other accounts.
(8) The progress report of the said expenditure shall be submitted by the Controlling Authorities to the Government by the 10th of every month as per the monthly fund statement.
(9) No further grant shall be disbursed unless the drawing and disbursing officer has received the utilisation certificates of the previous grant.
