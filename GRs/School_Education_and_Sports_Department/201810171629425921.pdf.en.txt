Shiusam/ Navi/ Prath-1/ Khaprasha/ Sth/ 2018/ 9040/41, dated 7/05/2018 The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for the shifting of the Marathi medium aided primary and upper primary school, Dattanagar, Chunchale Shivar, Ambad, to Nashik district was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As the Kazi Garhi area has been declared dangerous, the Nashik Municipal Corporation has issued a notice regarding the relocation of the school, the government has decided to approve the relocation of the school to a self-owned spacious building. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nashik shall ensure that all the pre-migration students are accommodated in the nearest school/ place of migration and there is no complaint/ objection in this regard.
(3) The migration is agreed to by all the teachers/ non-teaching staff, and all the qualified teachers and non-teaching staff have been accommodated in the migration place and their service conditions have been changed and will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(4) The Chairman/ Secretary of the institution shall provide all physical facilities and educational facilities required for the school as per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and orders issued from time to time by the Government. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(5) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission and recognition of the Government in respect of the migrated school.

The organization shall be bound to follow the laws, rules, government decisions, orders, etc. prescribed by the government from time to time in this regard.
(7) The school should be sanctioned at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was originally sanctioned.
(8) In accordance with the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as permissible posts will continue to be employed in the school and action will be taken as per the Government policy regarding the filling up of the vacancies from the additional staff.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
(10) It shall be necessary for the institution to ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
2. Clause No. On fulfilment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nashik Division, Nashik shall take necessary further action in respect of approval of relocation of the said school and the action taken shall be intimated to the Government.
