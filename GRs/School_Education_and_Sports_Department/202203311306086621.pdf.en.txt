Government Resolution No. Samagra-2021/ S. No. 111 Part-3/ S. The D-1 project should be spent for recurring/ non-recurring expenditure of ongoing schemes of primary and secondary education under the Samagra Shiksha approved by the Approval Board (PAB).
4. It should be ensured that the fund is spent in the current year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure.
The report of the expenditure incurred from the provision made under the head Scheduled Tribes, the certificate of utilization of funds, the material objectives achieved in the head/ sub-heading, etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and School Education Department without fail by the 10th of every month.
6. It should be ensured that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area, is recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
7. The provision made under the Tribal Component Programme should be taken up for creation of infrastructure and individual benefit schemes in the tribal sub-plan area and only individual benefit schemes should be taken up in the tribal sub-plan peripheral area.
8. Expenditure to be borne by Finance Department, Government Decisions/ Circulars. 6/04/2021, Dy. 24/06/2021, Dy. 29/07/2021 and D. to be done as per instructions dated 17/03/2022. Under Secretary/ Cell Officer (Accounts Branch), Department of School Education and Sports, Ministry of Education is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Administration), Department of School Education and Sports, Ministry as" Controlling Officer" for collection and disbursement of the said funds.
The concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer should draw the funds from the treasury and deposit the funds in the bank account of Samagra Shiksha.
