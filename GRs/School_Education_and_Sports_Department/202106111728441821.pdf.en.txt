The Proposal:
Orders have been issued under the decision of the Government of General Administration Department in reference No. 1 regarding the formation of a Civil Service Board to recommend transfers and postings in the field offices under the Administrative Department. Accordingly, the matter of forming a Civil Service Board (2) for the recommendation of transfers and postings in Group-C cadre in the office of National Chhatra Sena under the School Education and Sports Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Civil Services Board is being constituted for transfer and posting of Group C Cadre in the National Cadet Corps Office as shown in the table below:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | designation                                                                                        | The Chairman/ Member |
| 1.   | Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services                                                         | Chairman             |
| 2.   | The concerned Group Head, Group Headquarters, National Cadet Corps Office or their representative. | the members          |
| 3.   | the District Sports Officer of the concerned Department                                            | the members          |
| 4.   | the Office Superintendent of the concerned Department                                              | the members          |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(The decision on the election/ appointment of members in A.No. 3 and 4 shall be taken by the Chairman.) )
