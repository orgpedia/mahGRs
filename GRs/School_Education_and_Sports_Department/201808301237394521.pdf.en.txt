Introduction: Urooj-e-Urdu Education Society, Kalamb, Ta. Baba Kambal Posh Urdu High School, Arni, Dist. and G. Yavatmal Urdu Medium Aided School, Ideal Education Society, Dhanaki, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the institution to Umarkhed, G. Yavatmal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Higher Primary and Secondary School, Ideal Education Society, Dhanki, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Umarkhed, G. Yavatmal on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of a change of management of the school.
(2) The Chairman/ Secretary of the new institution will be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities for the school as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their service conditions shall not change. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the Government decision, dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, and not to employ more teaching/ non-teaching staff than the permissible posts.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
