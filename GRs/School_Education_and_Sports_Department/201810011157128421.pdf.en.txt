Preamble: The South Indian Association, Kalyan, G.Thane runs Shri Vani Vidyashala High School, Shri Vani Vidyalaya Marg, Rambagh, Gali No. 5, Welfare (p. ), G. Thane, English Medium Higher Primary and Secondary School, Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation, opposite B Ward Office, Khadakpada, Kalyan (Pt. ), G. The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for the relocation to Thane was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the relocation of the school from the dilapidated, dangerous and inadequate building to the spacious building of the institution on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The Education Officer (Primary/ Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Thane shall ensure that all pre-migration students are accommodated at the place of migration or in the nearest secondary school and there is no complaint/ objection or court case of the parents in this regard.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Further, all the eligible teaching and non-teaching staff have been accommodated at the migrant place and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and orders issued from time to time by the Government, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided for the school. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3217/ PR. No. 263/ SM-1 (5) No change in any of the terms and conditions of the original government permission and approval in the case of a migrant school.
(6) On the basis of the grant-in-aid of the said English medium classes/ batches, as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as may be permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, shall continue to work in the school and action shall be taken as per the policy of the Government to fill up the vacancies of these posts by adjustment from the additional staff. Also, the financial responsibility of teachers/ non-teaching staff working on unaided classes/ batches will remain on the institution.
The organization shall be bound to follow the laws, rules, government decisions, orders, etc. prescribed by the government from time to time in this regard.
(8) The classes/ batches of the school shall continue to be on the same basis as it was on the basis of grant before grant of permission for relocation of the said school.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
(10) It shall be necessary for the institution to ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
2. Clause No. On fulfilment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai shall take necessary further action for grant of approval for relocation of the said school and shall apprise the Government of the action taken.
