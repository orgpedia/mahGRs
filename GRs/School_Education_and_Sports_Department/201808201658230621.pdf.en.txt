Government circular:
National Means cum Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Financially Weaker Section Scheme (NFWS) implemented by Department of School Education and Sports (DSES), Government of India since 2008. The Central Government has revised the guidelines of this scheme as follows:
1) The scholarship amount is Rs. 6000/- to Rs. 12000/-.
The scholarship amount which was earlier given in 4 installments in a year will now be given in one installment in a year.
The eligibility criterion for the scholarship is that the candidate must have a minimum of 40% (32% for SC/ ST students) marks in both Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT) and Mental Ability Test (MAT) in the NMMSS examination. Therefore, the earlier condition of 40% marks in each subject should be considered as void.
Director of Education, Secondary and Higher Secondary, Maharashtra State, Pune shall issue instructions to the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Education Officer regarding the revised guidelines of the above scheme. Also, elaborate advertisements should be made to give improved information to the parents/ students so that more and more students can take advantage of this scheme by qualifying in the NMMSS exam. (Additional instructions are attached.)
