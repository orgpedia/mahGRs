Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is as under. (2) The Government decision herein has been made effective as of March 1, 2014. Accordingly, the educational institutions, local/ civic bodies, schools of local self-government institutions, junior colleges, ashramshalas of the Tribal Development Department, divisional/ district/ taluka sports institutions of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act 1950 or the Societies Registration Act 1860, are provided financial assistance in the ratio of 75:25 (75% of the government and 25% of the eligible institution concerned) for the construction of sports facilities.
