Introduction: Shri Shivaji Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Barshi, G. Solapur Manipal Bharat Vidyalaya, Osmanabad Talim Galli, Khaja Nagar, T. Osmanabad Marathi Medium Aided Higher Primary and Secondary School, Survey No. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the premises to 350/1, Narayan Bagh, behind Government Technical College, T.G. Osmanabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing building with inadequate physical facilities to the new spacious building owned by the school. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It should be ensured that the class of class 10 is recognized.
(3) It shall be ensured that all the parents of the students in the school consent to the migration.
(4) The existing students in the school should be adjusted at the place of migration.
(5) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(6) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, the school shall be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(8) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(9) The school shall function at the place of relocation on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.

2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3), (5) and (6), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur shall take necessary further action regarding approval of relocation of the said school and inform the Government about the action taken.
