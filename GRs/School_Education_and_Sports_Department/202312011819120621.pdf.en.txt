The Proposal:
The centrally sponsored scheme, Navbharat Saksharta Karyakram, will be implemented from April 1, 2022, to March 31, 2027. The objective of the scheme is to provide continuing education to the illiterate persons of the age group of 15 years and above in the country, which will include art, science, technology, culture, sports, entertainment, as well as subjects of interest to the locals.
In line with the National Education Policy 2020 and Budget Announcements for 2021-22, a new scheme of Adult Education," Nava Bharat Saksharta Karyakram" will be implemented in the State from 2022-23 to 2026-27 as per the instructions of the Central Government.
