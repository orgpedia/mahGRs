Regulation of transfers and performance of official duties of Government of Maharashtra
The Prevention of Delay Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and their performance of official duties.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared under Section 6 of the Prevention of Delay Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority transferring under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Education Service Group-A and Group-B have been transferred.
The decision has been taken by the government decision dated May 30, 2015.
As per the provisions of the said Act, a proposal for transfer of officers who have completed three years of service as on 31st May, 2018 was submitted to the Civil Services Board. urban
The officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 against their names with the prior approval of the competent authority as per the recommendation of the Service Board.
