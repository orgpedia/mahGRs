School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: Departmental Deputy Director of Education, Pune Division, Pune vide letter No.
Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of English Medium Self-financed Primary School, Rajmata Jijau Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Landewadi, Bhosari, Pune, Intelligent Cadet International School, Landewadi, Pune, to Gyanada Mission of Education, Landewadi, Bhosari, Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Gyanada Mission of Education, Landewadi, Bhosari, Pune on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred school shall not be relaxed on any ground.
(3) As the school is self-financing English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(4) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the transferred school.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Order, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said transfer or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
