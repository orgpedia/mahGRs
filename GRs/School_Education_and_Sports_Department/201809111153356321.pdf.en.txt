The proposal-
The government took the decision. dated 15th November, 1985. Under section 1, the scrutiny of pension case is explained. This Section No. As per section 1, the pension documents of the board employees are to be scrutinized by the office of the Chief Auditor, Local Accounts, Maharashtra after preparing it at the board level. However, in view of the scope of the audit of local bodies and the inadequate manpower with the Directorate, it has now been informed under the mandate of the Department of Finance under reference that on the lines of the Zilla Parishad, the pension documents of the employees of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education should be scrutinized by the Assistant Director (Group-A) and Accountant (Group-B Gazetted) of the Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Cadre in their office. In view of this fact, the matter of determining the amended provisions excluding the above mentioned provisions in the relevant Government decision was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
the reference ruling. dated 15th November, 1985. The provision regarding the scrutiny of pension documents of the employees of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education in 1 through the office of the Chief Auditor, Local Accounts of the State of Maharashtra, is being excluded under this government decision.
Now, the process of scrutiny of pension cases/ documents should be done by the Accountant (Group-B Gazetted) of the Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Cadre in the Board.
The government has decided. The remaining provisions of 15th November 1985 shall continue to operate.
