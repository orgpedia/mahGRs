Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of Vishal Vidyalaya, Nandurbar run by Indira Gandhi Mahila Seva Society, Nandurbar to Manashri Educational and Agricultural Institute, Nandurbar was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason for the school where the management has changed.
(2) The recognition of class 7 and class 10 of the school should be verified based on the order. The Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar, should verify the financial position of Manasri Educational and Agricultural Institute, Nandurbar, on the basis of the balance in the bank account and the term loan.
(3) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) In accordance with the revised criteria of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as in the orders issued from time to time by the Government regarding schools, the number of teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts shall remain in operation and not more than that number of teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.

Government Resolution No. Mashah 3118/ PR No. 378/ SM-1 shall remain in force and action shall be taken in accordance with the policy of the Government to fill up the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time. Students will also be required to fill in details such as Aadhaar card number, biometric information, etc.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.

Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nashik Division, Nashik, shall examine all the aspects and take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school, including other necessary conditions, and inform the government about the action taken.
