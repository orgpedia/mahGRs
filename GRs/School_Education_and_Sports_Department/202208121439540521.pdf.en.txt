The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme through Yashoda, Pune for Group A posts and Vanamati, Nagpur for Group B posts. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for the candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the State Service Examination-2020 and undergoing training in the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-8 (CPTP-8).
Government's decision:
Based on the final results of the State Service Examination-2020, 23 candidates out of 25 candidates selected for appointment to the post of Deputy Educational Officer and similar, Group-B (Administration Branch) by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission have appeared for training at Vanamati, Nagpur
The Government is approving creation of 23 supernumerary posts for the period from 23.08.2022 to 22.08.2024 for 23 candidates who joined under the said combined probationary training programme-8.
