School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No: Masha Sth 3321/ PR No.401/ SM-1 dated 28th July, 2021.
Reference: Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur vide No. Letter dated 11.04.2023.
The proposal received under the letter was to grant ex-post facto approval for shifting of a partially aided primary (Class 1st to 4th) school of Marathi medium, located at Babasaheb Ambedkar Road, opposite Dr. P. R. Patil Hospital, Ganesh Nagar, Sangli, to 100 Feet Road, Shamraon Nagar, Sangli.
Government Decision: As the school building is in a dilapidated and dangerous condition, the relocation of the school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
