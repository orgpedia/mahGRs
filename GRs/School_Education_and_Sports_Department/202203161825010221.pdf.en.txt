As per the policy of the Central Government, two Sainik Schools have been established- Satara Sainik School and Chandrapur Sainik School. The main objective of these schools is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children of Maharashtra at the grassroots level and at all levels to get a post of officer in the army from India through the National Defence Academy.
