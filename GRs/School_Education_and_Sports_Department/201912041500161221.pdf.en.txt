The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 13.06.2013, it was decided to provide basic training to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) sponsored candidates by appointing them to the post of Education Officer and similar in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A cadre from 01.07.2013. The officers who have completed the training have been posted as Education Officers and equivalent posts as per the Government decision dated 08.08.2013.
Accordingly, these officers have been appointed as Education Officers and equivalent posts for a period of 2 years.
The Commissioner (Education) has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 06.10.2017 regarding the completion of the probationary period of the officers appointed to the post of Education Officer and equivalent in Group-A of Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch).
