Preamble: Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution No: AST-112/ PR No.472/ PRISA-2, dated 4.03.2013, a total of 54 candidates were appointed directly to the posts of Superintendents in General State Service, Group-B Cadre. Out of the officers who have accepted the appointment in this order, 5 officers have not passed the departmental examination held from the date of their joining in 4 occasions. Therefore, an extension was granted to him vide order dated 31.05.2018. 1 candidate has been declared elected while 4 others have been declared elected. The government was considering providing another opportunity to such failed candidates. Therefore, the Government has decided to extend the period of probation by one year
Government's decision
The following 4 officers, who were directly appointed to the posts of Superintendents in General State Service, Group-B, have not passed the departmental examination held on their joining date in 5 occasions. However, these officers will have to sit for the departmental examination.
The period of probation is extended by one year on the ground that an extension is being granted
The following 4 officers are required to pass the departmental examination in this extended opportunity, otherwise their services will be terminated without any prior notice.
