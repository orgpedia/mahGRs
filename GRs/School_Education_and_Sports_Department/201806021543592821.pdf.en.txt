The proposal-
The defined contribution pension scheme for teachers and non-teaching staff of 100% aided posts in private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools recognized by the Zilla Parishads. The government has taken a decision. The procedure for refund of contribution amounts of the new" Defined Contribution Pension Scheme" vide Reference No. The government has taken 3 decisions. The matter of disbursement of contribution refund for teachers and non-teaching (secondary and higher secondary) employees under the defined contribution pension scheme in 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme for the financial year 2018-19, a sum of Rs. 170.94 has been provided. A total of Rs. 1, 70.94 lakh is being approved to be disbursed through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) to the Commissioner, Education, State of Maharashtra, Pune as per the following statement.
