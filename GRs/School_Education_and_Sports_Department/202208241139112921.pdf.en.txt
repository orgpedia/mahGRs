Preamble- The Department of School Education, Government of India has decided to include the defined contribution pension scheme applicable to teachers and non-teaching staff in 100% aided posts in private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools recognized by the Zilla Parishads in the National Pension Scheme of the Central Government. The government has taken a decision. The amount collected under the defined contribution pension scheme of teachers/ non-teaching staff of Zilla Parishad and recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools will be transferred under the National Pension Scheme. 2 has been approved. In 2021-22, Rs. 9061,47,80 thousand to disburse 50% of the grant. 3 was approved. In the year 2022-23, from the budget provision in the head Accounts 83420257, 83420266, 83420275, reference No. Funds have been allocated for 4. Now, as per the approval of the Finance Department, the government was considering to disburse 50% of the funds from the budget provision in the headings 83420257, 83420266 and 21% from the budget provision in the headings 83420275.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- For the financial year 2022-23, the following statement in column No. 3 has been provided. With this provision, No. A total of Rs 4.
