The selection trial was held on 03.02.2023 at Shramik Gymkhana, NM Joshi Marg, Near Curry Road Station, Mumbai to select the Maharashtra Government team to participate in the All India Civil Services (Women/ Men) Kabaddi Tournament 2022-23 to be held at Dehradun, Uttarakhand from 20th to 24th February, 2023. The list of male/ female officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying statement" C" and the team statement of the State selected through the selection test is as per the accompanying statement" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 2,19,500/- (Rupees Two Lakh Twenty One Thousand Five Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office shall relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 13th to 15th February, 2023 and for the tournament from 20th to 24th February, 2023 with effect from 17th February, 2023 (M.No.) and the services of these players shall be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
