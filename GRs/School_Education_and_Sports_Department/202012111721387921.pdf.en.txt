Preamble: For non-teaching staff in all private aided/ partially aided secondary/ higher secondary schools in the State, subject to reference No. 7 here. As per the Government decision dated 28th January, 2019, the revised figure has been implemented and the criteria for filling up these posts have been laid down. Separate orders are being issued regarding the figure for the posts of Class IV employees. This was made clear in the government decision of January 28, 2019.
