The tender was invited vide Notice dated November 10, 2021 for entering into a one-year contract with the supplier for the purchase of computer accessories required for the School Education and Sports (Self) Department. A total of 7 bids were received. Out of these 7 rates, the rates offered by Apex Enterprises for cartridges are the lowest. Also, the lowest prices for SanDisk (32 GB) and iBall Keyboard and Mouse have been offered by Naganeci Stationery, Fort, Mumbai. Accordingly, the Purchase Committee at its meeting held on 24th November, 2021 has selected two suppliers namely Apex Enterprises and Naganeci Stationery, Fort, Mumbai subject to the following terms and conditions.
The period of procurement of computer spare parts required for the department will be from 1.12.2021 to 30.11.2022.
(2) It shall be mandatory to supply spare parts of computers of the company mentioned in the tender or of better quality at the rate mentioned in the tender immediately as per the demand registered by the department from time to time.
(3) If the spare part of the computer of a company other than the one mentioned in the list is demanded, it will be mandatory to supply it immediately. Also, it will be mandatory for the supplier to supply such goods at a rate which is 5% less than the market price.
