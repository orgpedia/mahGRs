the prelude
The Government is planning to give cash awards to the medal winners of Olympic, Commonwealth and Asian Games. Accordingly, the government was considering to honor the medal winning athletes of Maharashtra and their mentors with cash awards for their outstanding performance at the international level.
Government's decision:
Under the Sports Policy of Maharashtra, 2012, the Government is approving the sanction of special cash awards to the medal winning players and their mentors of Maharashtra State as indicated in their names for their outstanding performance at the international level and for bringing glory to the State.
