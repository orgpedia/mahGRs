Read:- 1. Government Decision No: Shichhaphu 2022/ PR No.93/ CRUSE 2 dated 14.12.2022.
Introduction: The Government has implemented the scheme of giving Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards to the best sportspersons of the State of Maharashtra since 1969-70 and the Best Sports Mentor Award to the sports mentors who have made the country proud at the national and international level since 1988-89. The Government of Maharashtra has implemented the scheme of giving Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar from the year 2001-02 to honour the senior sports persons associated with the field of sports who have spent their life for the development of sports by giving their all for the field of sports.
2. To revise the rules for the award of Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar, Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Puraskar (Best Sports Mentor/ Jijamata), Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Puraskar (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Puraskar (Differently Abled Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Puraskar (Adventure Activities). As per the Government decision dated 01.08.2019, another 15-member Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri Dhanraj Pillay, Padma Shri Awardee.
The committee has submitted its report to the government. As per the Government decision dated 24.01.2020, the rules for Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards and other Sports Awards have been amended. The representations received by the Directorate during the preliminary scrutiny of the proposals received for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards for the year 2018-19 are being considered
However, some points in the award rules need to be clarified. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, was asked to submit a proposal to amend the said Rules. Accordingly, the matter of amending the award rules was under the consideration of the government.
Sportspersons, organizers/ workers and mentors of the state are honoured with Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Pride Award, Best Sports Mentor Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson, Organizer/ Worker), Eklavya State Sports Award (Divyang), Adventure Sports Award and Jijamata Sports Award. All about the award and the past. The Government Decision dated 14.12.2022 is being superseded. The rules attached to this government decision are being approved.

(1) In the Rules appended to the General Rules, Rule No. General rules for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards have been fixed under Section 1.
(2) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Jeevan Gaurav) In order to honour the great sportspersons, sports mentors, sports organizers/ activists and other persons associated with the field of sports who have dedicated their life for the development of sports and sports, rules No. Under Section 2, the rules for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Lifetime Achievement Award are being determined.
(3) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Best Sports Mentor/ Jijamata): In order to honour the sports mentors whose guidance in the field of sports has helped in the participation and formation of sportspersons at the national and international level, as well as those whose guidance has produced sportspersons at the national and international level, the rules of the accompanying rules No. Under Section 3, the rules for the Best Sports Mentor/ Jijamata Award are being decided.
(4) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson): In order to honour the sportspersons of Maharashtra who have excelled in the field of sports, the rules mentioned in the accompanying rule No. Under Section 4, the rules for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson) are being decided.
(5) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards (Adventure Sports):- In order to honour the sportspersons of the State who have excelled in adventure sports, in the accompanying Rules, Rule No. Under Section 5, the rules for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards (Adventure Activities) are being decided.
(6) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Differently Abled Sportspersons): In order to honour the sportspersons of Maharashtra (Differently Abled) who have excelled in the field of sports, in the accompanying Rules, Rule No. Under Section 6, the rules for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Divyang Sportsperson) are being fixed.
The details of expenditure incurred in connection with the above award distribution should be submitted to and approved by the Commissioner, Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune before the award distribution ceremony.
The expenditure for the above purpose shall be met out of the approved provision made in the said financial year under the head Main Account 2204-Sports and Youth Services-104-Sports and Sports- (03) (01) Organizing State Sports Award Programme- 50-Other Expenditure Scheme, Computer No. (2204-1569).
