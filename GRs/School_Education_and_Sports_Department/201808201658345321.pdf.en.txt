Government Circular- Commencement of 150th Birth Anniversary Year of Mahatma Gandhi from 2nd October, 2018. In this Golden Jubilee Year of Centenary, the following programmes should be organized in all the schools of the State from 2nd October, 2018:
The event will be held on October 2, 2018. Also, Gandhi Vandana, garlanding of Gandhiji's portrait, etc. are organised.
Thereafter, the following programmes may be organized during the next year up to 02.10.2019 as per the accompanying schedule:
Organizing essay competition on Gandhiji's Non-Cooperation Movement, Civil Disobedience Movement, Quit India Movement.
Science fairs/ exhibitions should be organized in all schools. This should include projects focused on Swachh Bharat Mission, water conservation and solar energy.
3) Organize painting competitions based on Gandhiji's thoughts.
4) To organize a quiz competition on Gandhiji's contribution to the freedom movement.
5) Lectures by Gandhian thinkers should be organised for students during this post-centenary golden jubilee year.
The programmes mentioned in the plan are exemplary, and in addition, various innovative programmes should be organized at the school level to ensure that the ideas of Mahatma Gandhi reach the students, parents and the public.
