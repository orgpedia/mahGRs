Ahmednagar and Sahyadri Primary School, Sangamner, Dist.
Two schools (4+4 posts) of D.N. Nagar have been declared eligible for grant-in-aid under the Government decision dated 30th November, 2013 at Reference No. As per the decision of the Cabinet in its meeting held on 14th June, 2016, the Government have issued orders under the Government Resolution dated 19th September, 2016 in the Reference No. (6) regarding sanction of 20% grant in aid from 1st September, 2016 to the Primary/ Secondary School batches declared eligible for grant before 14th June, 2016.
Accordingly, 20 per cent subsidy was permissible for 8 posts in these 2 schools from September 1, 2016.
However, it has been observed that the salaries of the teachers of the said school for the period from June, 2012 to October, 2016 have been paid without due approval of the Government. In this regard, it is clearly stated in paragraph 4.2 of the Government Decision, dated 19th September, 2016, that
