Preamble: Hon'ble Supreme Court of India in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 (Chairman and Managing Director, FCI and Others Vs. Jagadish Balaram Behera and Others) and other petitions dated 06.07.2017, has ruled that the protection in Government service is not payable to persons who have joined the Government service on the basis of reservation for Backward Castes and whose caste claims have been invalidated thereafter. 3140/2008 vide order dated 28.09.2018. In pursuance of the order of the Hon'ble Supreme Court, the proceedings have been ordered to be completed by 31.12.2019. General Administration Department to implement the decision of the Supreme Court. As per the provisions of the Government decision issued on 21.12.2019, the Government was considering the creation of additional number of posts for teachers/ non-teaching staff in aided schools under this department who were appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but could not produce caste validity certificate.
Government's decision:
The Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 and other petitions. Teachers/ non-teaching staff who have been transferred to superannuation on the ground of non-submission of caste validity certificate of Scheduled Tribes are being appointed to superannuation on a temporary basis for a period of 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier, from a humanitarian point of view and to remove the difficulties faced by the administration.
The concerned appointing authority i.e. the concerned Education Officer of the above mentioned returnee employees shall immediately issue the appointment order for only the individual approved/ permanent employees as per the pattern in Annexure C and Annexure D attached with the Government Decision dated 21st December, 2019 in the context of the General Administration Department and transfer their services to the higher post immediately and submit a report to the Government.
The monthly salary and allowances received by the employees of the above-mentioned return before their transfer to the post of service head. Salaries and allowances will continue to be paid till further orders.
