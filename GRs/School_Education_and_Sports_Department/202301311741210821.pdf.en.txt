The best sportspersons, sports activists and sportspersons/ individuals who have worked for the progress of the sports sector in Maharashtra with an aharnish and service-minded attitude are awarded every year with Shivchhatrapati State Krida Jeevan Gaurav Award, Best Sports Guide Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Adventure Sports Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Disabled Sportsperson) and Jijamata Award. The revised rules for these awards are as follows: The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 14.12.2022.
The government has decided to constitute a state-level selection committee for the award. Accordingly, the proposal to constitute a selection committee for the award for the years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government Decision: Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Time Honour Award, Best Sports Guide Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Adventure Sports Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Differently Abled Sportsperson) and Jijamata Award for giving reference No. Annexure A to Rule No. 1. As per the provisions of section 6, the selection committee is being constituted as follows and the Government is approving the appointment of the following non-official (expert) members. :

Non-official members of the selection committee shall be appointed for a period of three years. The meeting allowance as per the Government Resolution, Finance Division, dated 24.05.2012 and travelling allowance and daily allowance as per the provisions of Para 12 of the Government Resolution, Finance Division, dated 03.03.2010 shall continue to be admissible to the above members. The expenses should be met from the funds collected from Shivchhatrapati Kridapeeth, Balewadi, Pune.
The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune and the Member Secretary, Award Selection Committee shall take necessary further action regarding the convening of the meeting of the Committee immediately.
