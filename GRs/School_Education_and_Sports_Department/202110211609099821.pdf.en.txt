Reference: Letter dated 01.09.2021 from the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, vide No.
Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter to shift the building of Mahanagar Palika Primary School, Cidco N-6, Aurangabad on self-financing basis from the English medium unaided primary school (classes 1 to 8) of The World School, Garkheda Campus, Aurangabad, which was transferred from Shri Laxminarayan Multi-purpose Educational Institution, Aurangabad to Shri Sai Knowledge Solution, Aurangabad, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted due to inadequate physical facilities due to increasing number of students. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
