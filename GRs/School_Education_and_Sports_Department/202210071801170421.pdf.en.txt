The above promotion to the post of Taluk Sports Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) is being made subject to the following conditions. All the posts in the quota of promotion subject to the final decision of 28306/2017. As per the service superiority as on 25.05.2004, only temporary filling up is to be done as per the provisions of Sec. V./ 16B. The said promotion is being given as per the instructions issued vide Government Decision dated 07.05.2021. The said promotion is subject to final decision as challenged in the High Court, Mumbai.
(ii) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the said temporary promotion will not entitle the officers in the select list to any regularisation.
III. in the case of officers in the said public service who refuse promotion. P.S. The Government Decision. Action will be taken as per 12.09.2016.
3. The said promotion is being made subject to the condition that the departmental enquiry under Rule 8 and 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, against the above mentioned officers is not ongoing or pending in the proposed/ pending court case. However, if a departmental inquiry/ court case is registered against any of these officers on the date of this Government decision, the order shall stand automatically quashed in their case.
