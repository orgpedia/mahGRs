Preamble: Sri Visva-Bharati Institute of Vocational Education, Ruikar, Osmanabad. Bhoom, G. Osmanabad, Marathi Medium Unaided Higher Secondary School, Bhoom Nagar, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of the land to G. Osmanabad was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: It is proposed to shift the said High School from a building with inadequate physical facilities to a spacious building. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated Higher Secondary School shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(5) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in the case of Higher Secondary School.
(6) The Higher Secondary School shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(7) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible in accordance with the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(8) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfilment of clauses (2) and (3), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur, shall take further action within six weeks regarding approval of relocation of the said school and shall inform the Government of the action taken.
