Government circular:
Periodic inspection and supervision of schools is an important factor in the process of achieving the goal of providing quality education. The centre plays an important role in conducting such investigations as well as providing training and support to students and teachers. In this context, it is necessary to develop an appropriate pedagogical and curricular support system for continuous professional improvement of teachers.
For better academic performance of the schools, regular visits and monthly meetings are required to be organized through Kendrashalas to discuss various academic issues and prepare various activities based on it. Thus, each Kendriya Vidyalaya has been provided with funds for the year 2019-2020 under Project Approval Board (PAB) for providing direct educational assistance to the schools under its jurisdiction by making regular visits to the schools under it and sending the report to the Education Officers (Primary/ Secondary& Higher Secondary). The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has prepared a detailed questionnaire and instructions for the Head of the Centre for periodical inspection of infrastructure in schools, teachers, students, other administrative and academic aspects etc. They're as follows:
The Centre School will function as an educational research institute for teachers.
2) The head of the center should increase the performance of the school by holding a meeting every month on educational problems in the school.
3) The head of the centre should visit the members of the school management committee and local bodies and hold meetings for the progress of the school.
