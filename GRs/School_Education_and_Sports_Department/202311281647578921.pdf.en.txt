The proposal to transfer the management of Akola Marathi medium aided secondary school (class 6th to 10th) to Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Shikshan Prasarak and Multipurpose Institute, Akola, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the management of the said school to Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Shikshan Prasarak and Multi-purpose Institute, Akola on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school whose management has been changed shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
