The Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012, has come into force with effect from 19.01.2013. Accordingly, the permission of the government is required to start a new school or to upgrade an existing school in the state as per the provisions of this Act. After this, the concerned school will have to take a no-objection certificate from the government to get affiliation to any board in the state, country or abroad. In pursuance of this, the Government was contemplating to amend the Government Resolution at Reference No. (1) and issue an order regarding the procedure for grant of No Objection Certificate to the schools desirous of affiliation to the Board's curriculum in the country and abroad.
