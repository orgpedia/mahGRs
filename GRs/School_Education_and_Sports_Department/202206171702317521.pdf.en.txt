Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Music, Dance and Short Dramatic Competition 2021-22, held in New Delhi from 23rd June to 27th June, 2022, was held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai. The selection test was conducted on June 3, 2022. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per the prospectus" A." The selection of this competition will be done as per the prospectus' B' for the test, training camp and participation of the state team (Music Rs. 92,200/-+ Dance Rs. 99,100/-+ Rs. 81,300/- (Rs. 2,72,600/- (Rupees Two Lakh Seventy Two Thousand Six Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 16th to 20th June, 2022 and for the tournament from 23rd June to 27th June, 2022 with effect from 15th June, 2022 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. Absence from office during the period from 16th to 27th June, 2022 (Training Camp, Competition and Travel) shall be treated as" Duty Period." as well as their

In order to avail the benefit of this concession, the certificate of attendance of the officers and employees appearing for the selection test issued by the Honorary Secretary, Secretariat Gymkhana, will have to be submitted by the concerned employees in their establishment office.
The absence from office of the officer/ employee (Return-C) appearing for the selection test of the competition should be treated as duty under the Government Decision, Finance Division No.-LVE/ 1492/46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992.
The Secretarial Gymkhana shall submit the statement of actual expenditure to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of the order. In case of balance, the balance amount should be returned immediately to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
The expenditure for this should be met from the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Competition, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Competition (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
The Secretariat Gymkhana is approving the expenditure as per return" B" subject to fulfillment of all conditions in the report of the competition, details of actual expenditure incurred, utilization certificate, examination statement and sanction order.
