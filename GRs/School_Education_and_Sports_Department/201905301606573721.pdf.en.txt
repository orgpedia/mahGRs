The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for the relocation to Kalyan, G.Thane was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the present building of the above school is not sufficient, the institution has shifted to a spacious and all physical facility building taken on a lease agreement of 50 years. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no complaint regarding the shifting of this school and all the students of the school are accommodated.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Further, all the eligible teaching and non-teaching staff have been accommodated at the place of migration and their service conditions have not been changed. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The school shall have a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and orders issued from time to time by the Government.
