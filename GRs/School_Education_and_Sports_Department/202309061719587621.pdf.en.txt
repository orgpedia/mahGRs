The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is mentioned above. The decision was taken on March 1, 2014. 3 Here the information of the eligible institutions for the scheme is mentioned.
Accordingly, financial assistance is provided to eligible institutions in the State on 75:25 basis (Government share 75% and eligible institution share 25%) for construction of sports facilities. Reference No. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraphs 4 (a) and (b) of the Government Decision.
