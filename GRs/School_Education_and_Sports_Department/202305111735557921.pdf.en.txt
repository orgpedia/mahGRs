Introduction: The proposal received under the letter of reference to grant ex-post facto approval for the relocation of Jai Parvati Mata Junior College (formerly known as Shaheed Vitthal Bhalsingh Junior College), Ahmednagar, an unaided junior college of Arts, Commerce, Marathi Medium and Science-English Medium, to Barati Karanja, Maliwada, G. Ahmednagar was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Institute has shifted the building of the above said Referral Junior College to Bara Toti Karanja, Maliwada, Dist. Ahmednagar due to old and dilapidated condition. Therefore, the transfer of the said Junior College is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
(6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
