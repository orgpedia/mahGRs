Government order
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2), 4 (5) and Section 6 (a) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the following Taluk Sports Officers are being transferred on administrative grounds and posted in the place indicated in Column-4 against their names
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The name of the officer | Current position                                 | Post-transfer                                    |
| 1    | Mr. Sanjay Gadhave      | Taluka Sports Officer, Ardhapur, District Nanded | Taluka Sports Officer, Bhokardan, District Jalna |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Authorities about the relieving of the said officers from their present posts and ensure that the transferred employees join the given place of posting within the prescribed period and submit a report to the Government.
As per the provisions of Rule 28 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment and Removal of Service) Rules, 1981, the period of assumption of office for joining the post and place of new posting shall not be admissible to Mr. Gadave on his request for transfer and the period of travel allowance and other incidental allowances shall not be admissible.
It may be noted that if the above transferred officers attempt to cancel the transfer or seek to cancel the transfer by directly or indirectly exerting pressure for the same, it will be considered as misconduct as the action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
No leave shall be granted to the officer so posted without prior permission and no leave applications of the officer so posted shall be entertained by the parent office in accordance with Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned officer by sending the registration fee to the original address.
Further, the non-compliance of the above transferred officers with the orders passed with the approval of the competent authority under the Transfer Act is serious in nature and the non-compliant officer shall be liable to be punished under Rule No. 1 of the M.N.S. (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. 4 shall be liable to be suspended.
