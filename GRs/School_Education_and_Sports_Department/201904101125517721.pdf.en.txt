November 1, 2018 Government Circular: Regarding reimbursement of arrears of schools which have given admission under 25% under the Right to Education Act. The revised instructions are being issued as per the discussions held with the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education Department along with the CEOs of various organizations and Zilla Parishads.
1) The government, d. The government circular of November 1, 2018 is being superseded.
2) The amount of reimbursement of 25 percent admission should be distributed to the concerned schools after the approval of the Chief Executive Officer of the Zilla Parishad.
The departmental education deputy director, Mumbai division, has to pay the reimbursement dues of schools affiliated to other boards in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.
The pending reimbursement amounts of the schools whose reimbursement proposals are pending from the commencement of the online admission process till the year 2016-17 shall be paid as per the rates fixed for that year.
The fee reimbursement for the academic year 2017-18 should be made as per the approved rate for the year 2016-17.
6) 50% of the school reimbursement amount for 2017-18 should be paid to the schools immediately as per the fixed rate.
The following should be verified while paying 50% of the amount in 2017-18.
The schools which are providing 25 per cent admission under the Right to Education Act, the details of the fee to be charged for the students of classes 1 to 8 will be available on the Saral portal.
