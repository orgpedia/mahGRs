Krishna College (Junior Division), Shivnagar, Dist. Satara Marathi Medium Aided Junior College (Class 11th to 12th, Arts, Commerce and Science Branch), Sou. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to G. Satara was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The above school is proposed to be shifted from inadequate building to perfect building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
