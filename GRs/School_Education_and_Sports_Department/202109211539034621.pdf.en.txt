Maharashtra has been selected along with other states for the World Bank-funded project Strengthening Teaching Learning and Results for States (STARS) based on its performance in the Performance Grading Index. Under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan programme, STARS aims to bring about fundamental reforms in the education sector at all levels, from pre-primary to class 12, by providing quality education and enhancing learning outcomes, improving teacher training practices, improving assessment methods, etc. Strengthening and up-gradation of business education from class 6 has been included in this scheme. The project will be implemented across the state. The Cabinet has approved the project. 1 Government decision has been made.
