Government Circular: The last date of seniority up to 01.01.2021 for the post of Deputy Educational Officer in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service, Group-B (Administration Branch) has been released on 17th April, 2023. Now the provisional seniority as on 01.01.2022 is being published in the accompanying Form.
The said seniority has been created vide Government Decision No. SRV 2016/ PR No. 281/12 dated 21.06.2021.
The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall bring the above seniority to the notice of all concerned. The officers who have objections regarding seniority should submit their representations in this regard to the Government through the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune within two weeks from the date of issue of this order. Any submissions received after the deadline will not be considered.
The Commissioner (Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune should immediately forward the representations received within the prescribed time frame to the Government along with their feedback.
