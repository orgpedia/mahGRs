Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Junior College (Arts), Gawandhala, Dist. Kamgaon, G. Buldana of Marathi medium unaided junior college, Lakhanwada, Tt. The proposal received under reference letter for relocation to Khamgaon, G. Buldana was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing inadequate building to a self sufficient building on rent. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure in writing the consent of the parents of the students in the school to the relocation, as well as to adjust the maximum number of students in the school to the place of relocation.
(3) It will be necessary to submit documents related to the ownership of the land/ building taken for the junior college at the place of relocation for a period of at least 5 years.
(4) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The school shall be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
