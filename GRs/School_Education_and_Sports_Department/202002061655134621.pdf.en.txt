No. 36/ SM-6, dated 13th September, 2019 Preamble In order to provide military education up to the secondary and higher secondary level in every district of the State, the Government has allowed the opening of military schools through private competent and voluntary managements under the Government decision dated 26th September, 1995.
The Government vide Government Order dated 24.12.2007, has granted permission to start a separate batch of 45 students from Class V onwards for imparting military education to tribal students in the said Sainik School.
Shri Durgamata Multi-purpose Sports and Educational Institute, Chincholi, G. Yavatmal has been established as Prithviraj Deshmukh Girls Military School, Dharmapuri, Dist. In connection with the opening of Girls Military School at Parbhani, No. (3) It is hereby approved by the Government vide letter dated 21st August, 2013. As per the decision of the Government dated 24.12.2007, the Government was contemplating to provide a separate batch of 45 tribal students for imparting military education from the academic year 2019-20.
The Government has decided to establish Shri Durgamata Multi-purpose Sports and Educational Institute, Chincholi, Yavatmal as Prithviraj Deshmukh Girls Military School, Dharmapuri, Dist. G. Parbhani Sainik School is being approved to start tribal batch of Class V from the academic year 2020-21 subject to the following conditions:

(a) After approval of a general batch, while sanctioning a tribal batch, it shall be prescribed that there should be a minimum of 45 students in the general batch and at least 30 students in the tribal batch or 45 students in the tribal batch and at least 30 students in the general batch. When the total number of general students and tribal students is 45, the second batch will not be allowed automatically for further admission.
(b) If the educational qualification of the teachers of the General Secondary School and the Sainik School is the same, action should be taken to fill the necessary posts for this (additional) tribal batch by adjustment from the additional teachers.
(c) If the posts are not to be filled up from amongst additional teachers, the selection of teachers for these posts shall be made in accordance with the prescribed procedure and the same shall be followed in future.
