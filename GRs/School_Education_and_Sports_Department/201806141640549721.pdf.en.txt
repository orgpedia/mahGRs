The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 1st July, 2006.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared as per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005. (2) The competent authority may, by general or special order, transfer, in accordance with proviso to this section, any of its powers under this section to any of its subordinates.
Provision has been made that it can be handed over to the authority. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the power of transfer of officers of various cadres in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
In accordance with the proposal submitted to the Government by the Director, Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training, Pune and with the prior approval of the senior officer in the presence of the competent authority, in accordance with the recommendation of the Civil Services Board, the officers mentioned in the following table are being transferred on administrative grounds and posted to the post indicated in column No.-3 before their names.
