Preamble: To amend the various clauses of the proposal regarding grant of further extension to the existing 20% grant-in-aid and 20% grant-in-aid to the private primary, secondary and higher secondary schools/ batches as approved by the Cabinet in its meeting dated 26th August, 2020. The Ministerial Errors Committee was set up under the Government Resolution No. 11. The date of the error committee is 16th September, 2020. A meeting chaired by the Minister (Revenue) had submitted the report of the recommendations made by the committee to the government. The report was discussed in the Cabinet meeting held on 14th October, 2020 and it was decided to examine the proposals submitted by the Commissioner (Education) to the Government through the School Education Department instead of the Finance Department before giving direct grants to the Primary, Secondary and Higher Secondary Schools which were declared eligible for grants under the Government decision dated 13th September, 2019. Accordingly, an inspection team was appointed by the School Education Department. The inspection team visited the higher secondary schools/ junior colleges/ more batches/ additional branches etc. at the field level. Also, the proposals for filling up the gaps in higher secondary schools/ junior colleges/ additional batches/ additional branches, which have been shown in Form B and are ineligible due to lack of certain documents, should be submitted to the government within 30 days.
