The Proposal:
The office building of Regional Education Authority, Mumbai is from 1950. Current situation
The building is in a dilapidated condition. The total area of the building is 274 square meters.
But half of it is not being used due to the dilapidated condition of the office.
The Regional Education Authority, Mumbai office consists of Mumbai Division and Konkan Division.
A total of 7 districts are responsible for the quality of education. There are often different in the office
type of meetings are held. However, due to the dilapidated condition of the office, meetings were held
At each location, you need to find a different place.
In view of all the above, the matter of renovation of this office building
was under consideration.
Government's decision:
Office Building of Regional Education Authority, Mumbai for Renovation and Repair
