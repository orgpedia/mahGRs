The Proposal:
Procedure for recruitment of teachers through the holy portal Reference No. Ruling from 1 to 4
has been prescribed by the decision. Most of these provisions further the recruitment process.
It was intended to be carried out in an easy and transparent manner. However, due to certain provisions
Unnecessary complications have been noticed in the recruitment process. This is the case
With a view to implementing the amended provisions by overriding certain relevant provisions.
The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
Teacher recruitment through the holy portal as shown in the table below.
To supersede the provisions relating to procedure and to apply the amended provisions instead.
Government approval is being granted.
