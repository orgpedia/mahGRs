Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is as under. (1) The Government decision herein has been made effective as of March 1, 2014. 75:25 for the construction of sports facilities to educational institutions, local/ civic bodies, schools of local self-government institutions, junior colleges, ashramshalas of the Tribal Development Department, divisional/ district/ taluka sports complexes of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act 1950 or the Societies Registration Act 1860. The approval has been granted on the basis of the terms and conditions prescribed under the Government Decision No. 1. Proposals for financial assistance to sports facilities/ sports materials of various institutions received by the Government from the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune, are being considered under reference No. Government Decisions 2 to 5

In principle, administrative approval has been given. Further, the proposal for demand of remaining funds of the institutions which were earlier given partial grants (Phase I/ II) is under reference No. 6 submitted to the Government by the Commissioner, Directorate of Sports and Youth Services. It was under consideration of the Government to release funds for providing financial assistance during the year 2022-23 for providing first phase grant to the institutions which have been granted administrative approval as well as the next phase grant to the institutions which have been granted partial grant (first/ second phase) earlier.
Government's decision:
Financial assistance for construction of sports facilities for the financial year 2022-23 out of the budget provision of Rs. 457.29 lakhs (Rupees Four Hundred and Fifty Seven Lakhs only) as shown in Form A, Form B and Form C attached, are hereby approved to disburse the remaining grants, grants for construction of sports facilities and grants for purchase of sports materials respectively to the respective institutions as shown against their names. Acceptance is subject to the following terms and conditions.
The Government shall take action to disburse the grants to the concerned institutions in accordance with all the terms and conditions of the approval of grants for sports facilities projects and sports materials respectively in paragraphs 4 (a) and 4 (b) of the Government Decision dated 1st March, 2014.
It shall be mandatory to submit the project completion certificate along with the photographs of the project and the utilization certificate as soon as possible and within a maximum period of 1 year after the disbursement of the grant as shown in the accompanying Form A, B and C to the eligible institutions concerned, otherwise the distributed grant will be recovered from the date of disbursement of the grant at the prevailing penal interest rate of the Government. Also, the concerned institutions in Form B will be required to take a bank guarantee for 10 per cent of the total grant amount to be sanctioned. Accordingly, the funds available for disbursement of the sanctioned amount should be disbursed to the concerned institution through RTGS system after the bank guarantee and affidavit is submitted to the concerned District Sports Officer. Subsidies for sports materials should be disbursed to eligible institutions as indicated in the prospectus in Form C subject to the terms and conditions of the Government decision. The terms and conditions contained in the Government Resolution, item 4 (b), shall continue to apply to the institutions that have provided grants for these sports materials.
