Introduction: Vidya Pratishthan, Vidyanagari, Baramati, G. Pune run Vidya Pratishthan English Medium School, built Bhigwan Tt. Indapur, G.Pune, an English Medium Self-Financed Upper Primary (Class 1st to 8th) School, Chincholi, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate Daund G. to Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the rented building to the land owned by the institution. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
