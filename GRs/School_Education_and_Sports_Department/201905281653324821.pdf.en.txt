Preamble: (1) By virtue of the 103rd Constitutional Amendment Act, 2019, the Government of Maharashtra, vide Government Order, S. No.
R.A.D-4019/ PR No. 31/16-A, dated 12/2/2019, mandates reservation of 10% seats in Government Services and Educational Institutions for Economically Weaker Sections of the Open Category as under: (a) 10% reservation in seats for admission to Government Educational Institutions/ Aided Schools, Colleges, All Higher Educational Institutions, Unaided Schools/ Colleges and Educational Institutions in the total number of seats for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) in the State, other than the categories provided for under Article 15 (4) and (5) and Article 16 (4) of the Constitution of India This reservation will not be applicable to minority institutions as per Article 30 (1) of the Constitution.
As per the above order, 10% seats are required to be reserved for the students belonging to economically weaker sections in the open category in the institutions running D.El.Ed. courses under the Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune.
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has been published in the Gazette on 28/12/2016. Section 32 of the Act provides as under: (9) All Government Institutions of Higher Education and other Higher Education Institutions receiving Aid from the Government shall reserve not less than 4% seats for persons with benchmark disabilities.
(2) The person with benchmark disabilites shall be given an upper age relaxation of five years for admission in institutions of higher education.

As per the provisions of the said Act of the Central Government, 5% seats are required to be reserved for persons/ students with significant disabilities for admission to Government and non-Government aided institutions running D.El.Ed. courses under Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune.
10% seats will be reserved for the economically weaker section in the state for admission to government/ aided/ unaided D.El.Ed. However, this reservation will not be applicable to minority institutions. The terms and conditions of the said reservation shall be as prescribed in the Government Resolution No. R.A.D.-4019/ R.S. 31/16-A, dated 12/2/2019.
As per the provisions of Section 32 (1) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, 5% seats will be reserved for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwDs) for admission to Government and Non-Government aided institutions running D.El.Ed. courses under Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune. The provisions of this Act regarding Persons with Benchmark Disabilities should be considered.
The above decisions should be implemented at the level of Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune.
