2. The Department of School Education and Sports vide notification dated 26.02.2018 has issued the Rules 2018 regulating the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Resident (Group-A) and Senior Resident (Group-B) in the cadre of" Maharashtra Shiksha Seva (Education Empowerment Branch)" under the control of Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State Pune, Government of Maharashtra.
3. The following procedure should be followed while sanctioning benefits under the Revised Assured Progress Scheme.
(1) In the case of officers who have completed 12/24 years of regular Government service before 01.01.2016, read the case-wise details as on 01.04.2015 and page 1 of page 2

Government Resolution No: EST-4518/ PR No. 177/ PRISA-2 dated 01.01.2019 to be taken up as per the provisions of the Government Resolution.
(2) In case of officers who have completed 10/20/30 years of regular government service after 01.01.2016, action should be taken in accordance with the provisions of the government decision dated 01.01.2019.
However, till the period prescribed for passing the examination in sub-rule (1) of rule 3 of the Rules 2018, for the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Advocate (Group-A) and Advocate (Group-B) in the" Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch)," those who have completed 12/24 years of service or 10/20/30 years as per the Seventh Pay Commission, as the case may be, shall be granted benefits under the Assured Progress Scheme, as per rules. However, if the Government extends the period of passing the examination within the period specified in the rules or in exceptional circumstances, the officers who do not pass the examination within such extended period shall not be granted permissible benefits under the revised Assured Progress Scheme for the period up to which they are exempted from passing the said examination.
