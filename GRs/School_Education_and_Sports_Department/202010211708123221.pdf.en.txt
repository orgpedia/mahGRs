Preamble: Andheri Education Society, S.V.Road, Andheri (Pt. Sheth M.A. High School, Andheri (Mumbai) ), Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal, Vile Parle, Mumbai, an English Medium Aided Secondary School. The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the institution to Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal, Vile Parle (Pt. The Government has decided to approve the transfer to the Institute of Management, Mumbai on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai, after examining all the aspects and including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions, should take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
