Introduction: Pragya Surya Shikshan Sanstha, Nanded runs Matasaheb Primary School, Cidco, Nanded, a Marathi medium aided primary (class 1st to 4th) school, Sonkhed, Tal. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift Loha, G. to Nanded was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted as the building is dangerous and the lease agreement has been terminated. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
