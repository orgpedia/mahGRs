The Proposal- M. As per the directions of the Chairman, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, a joint committee was constituted under the decision of the Government under reference to study the implementation of the old pension scheme for all teachers and non-teaching staff appointed before November 1, 2005 on aided, unaided, partially aided posts and batches in schools in the State. The government has directed to submit a proposal to the Finance Department to take a policy decision regarding the employees who have retired from the defined contributory pension scheme, and the teaching and non-teaching staff who have a few years of service left but have not been deducted from the defined contributory pension scheme. An in-depth proposal needs to be prepared for such action. Hence, the government has decided to form a committee to look into the issue.
Government Decision- A" Samyak Vichar Samiti" is being appointed to decide the nature of the proposal to be submitted to the Finance Department regarding the teachers and non-teaching staff who have retired from the employees applying the defined contributory pension scheme and who have some years of service left but have not been deducted from the defined contributory pension scheme.
