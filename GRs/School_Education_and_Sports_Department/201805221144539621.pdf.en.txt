The proposal-
With the advancement in technology, easy availability of technology, teachers' self-motivation to use technology, a large number of schools in the state have become digital.
The technology of QR code is being used in the new textbooks being produced by Bal Bharati. This technology provides free text-based videos.
Considering all the above circumstances, the government has decided to close down the State Institute of Educational Technology, Pune (Balchitravani) and establish a new institute e-Balbharti. The decision was made on May 31, 2017. In this government decision, it has been mentioned that the administrative officer should take action to transfer all the movable and immovable property of Bal Chitravani to Bal Bharati with all the records of the organization.
School Education Department, Bhamburda, Shivajinagar, Pune City Geo-measurement No. 173-B/ 1 Sheet No. The total area covered by 832 is 67, 878.66 square kilometres. I have about 20,000 children. I decided to rule this place. 03/09/1983.
Since it was decided to transfer all the movable and immovable properties of Balchitravani to Balabharati, the matter of transferring the land given to Balchitravani to Balabharati was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decision was taken:
The decision was taken by the School Education Department, Bhamburda, Shivajinagar, Pune City Geo-measure No. 173B/ 1 Sheet No. Out of the total area of 832 hectares, the government has decided to set up a children's film institute. Approximately 20,000 sq.ft. allotted as on 03/09/1983. I want this space to be transferred to Maharashtra State Textbook Board (Balbharti). The Director, Maharashtra State Textbook Board (Balbharti) shall take necessary action to get the property deed of the said land from the Revenue Administration in the name of Maharashtra State Textbook Board (Balbharti).
