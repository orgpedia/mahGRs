Government's decision:
It is hereby approved to designate the Public Works Department as the Implementing Agency for the construction of the project Olympic Bhawan in the premises of Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex. Acceptance is subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) Reference No. 1 decision of the government. 27.3.2020, Refer No. Decisions 2 to 4. 27.3.2020, Dy. 14.1.2021 and dt. All the provisions of 25.3.2021 as well as the terms and conditions shall remain in force and shall be required to be complied with.
(2) Reference No. According to 5 and 6, the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune will submit the budgets and plans regarding the project and further action will be taken regarding the construction of the project after the finalization of these plans and budgets at the specified level.
(3) The disbursement of funds should be done in accordance with the progress of the project. Also, the progress report of the work and the utility certificates regarding the funds distributed should be submitted to the government from time to time.
(4) All financial and other related regulations shall be complied with.
