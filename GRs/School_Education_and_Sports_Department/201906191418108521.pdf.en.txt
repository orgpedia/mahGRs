Introduction: Swami Vidyamandir Primary School, Indurkar Wada, in front of Bhosla Veda School, Mahal, G.Nagpur, Marathi medium aided primary and upper primary school, Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter for relocation to 32 and 33, Dnyaneshwar Nagar, Manewada, G.Nagpur was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: As the present rented building of the above Marathi medium aided primary and upper primary school is dilapidated and dangerous, the landlord has issued a notice to vacate it, the school is proposed to be shifted to a spacious building taken on a 30-year lease. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. : 1) Qualified teachers and non-teaching staff working in the school before the relocation will be accommodated in the relocated place and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building and all necessary physical facilities and educational facilities. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
