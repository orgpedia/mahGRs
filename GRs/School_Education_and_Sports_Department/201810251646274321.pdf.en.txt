Date: 25th of October, 2018
Government circular
The Government of India has decided to celebrate the 31stOctober, 2018 as" National Unity Day" to commemorate the birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
Sardar Patel is known as the' Iron Man of India' for his contribution to the freedom struggle of the country.
The schools where the exams will be held on the occasion of his birth anniversary should organize the oath ceremony by skipping the exam period. Schools that do not have exams should organize the following programs.
To organize elocution/ essay competition at school level on the topic" Importance of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel in present-day India."
Music, drama and solo competitions should be organized based on the character and integrity of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. The most creative theme will be awarded.
3) Organize the most creative slogan and painting competition on the theme of Unity.
On the occasion of Rashtriya Ekta Diwas, a pledge taking ceremony should be organized in all schools at 11.00 am on 31st October, 2018.
5) Students should be encouraged to participate in the" Run for Unity" to be held in connection with the National Unity Day.
The Commissioner (Education) and the Director of Education (Primary/ Secondary) shall take action at their level to issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the above activities in all the schools of all the mediums of management.
