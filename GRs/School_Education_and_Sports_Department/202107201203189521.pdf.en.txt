Preamble: Satara Sainik School is sanctioned ex-gratia (non-salary) every year for training and administrative expenses, building maintenance, food allowance, maintenance of horses, advertisement expenses, municipal taxes, etc.
For the year 2019-20, Rs. 1,36,44, 723/-for the financial year 2020-21 and Rs. 2,62,66, 000/- aggregating to Rs. 3,99,10, 723/- is proposed to be sanctioned by the Director of Education, Secondary and Higher Secondary vide Reference No. (5) It was submitted to the Government by letter. Among them, the reference to Satara Sainik School No. (3) The Government has decided to provide Rs. 1,64,21, 250/- and Refer No. (4) The Government has decided to provide Rs. 82,10,625/- aggregating to Rs. 2,46,31, 875/- has been disbursed as Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried). Out of the amount demanded for Satara Sainik School from the available provision for the financial year 2021-22, the remaining grant-in-aid (non-salary) of Rs. 1,52,78, 848/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2021-22, from the available budgetary provision, Satara Sainik School will be given a grant-in-aid (non-salary) of Rs. 1,52,78, 848/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Two Lakh Seventy Eight Thousand Eight Hundred and Forty Eight only) is being approved by the Government.
The expenditure should be met from the headings" Demand No.E-2, 2202-General Education, 02 Secondary Education-110, Assistance to Non-Government Secondary Schools (00) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Poorvadayan Sainik Schools (22020469), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
The Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Satara is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said funds.
The various guidelines issued by the Finance Department from time to time as well as the orders mentioned in the Finance Department Government Decision No:-Finance-2021/ PR No. 48/ Finance-3, dated 24.06.2021 shall be strictly followed.
