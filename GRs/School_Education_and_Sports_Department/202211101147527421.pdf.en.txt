Maharashtra Creation of Superannuation Posts and Appointment of Selected Candidates Act, 2022 General Administration Department, Government Decision No. BCC-1122/ Pr. No. 128/16-B, dated 21.09.2022 Preamble: The Maharashtra Public Service Commission may, by competitive selection process, select candidates for the Government, Government Departments and their Subordinate Offices or Authorities from among the candidates belonging to the Educationally and Socially Backward Classes (ESBC) and Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) including Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Tribes, Denotified Tribes, Nomadic Tribes-B, Nomadic Tribes-C, Nomadic Tribes-D, Special Backward Classes, Others. Many candidates in the said category were given appointments on the basis of their merit in the competitive examinations.
High Court, Shri. After the judgment of 27th June, 2019. The Supreme Court Appointments made in the Public Service to the candidates belonging to Socially and Educationally Backward Classes till the stay order dated 09.09.2020 shall be treated as admissible. The order was upheld by the Supreme Court. However, following due process, the concerned selection authorities have decided to hold the examination. Earlier on September 09, 2020, some candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Vimukt Jati (A), Nomadic Tribes-B, Nomadic Tribes-C, Nomadic Tribes-D, Special Backward Classes, Other Backward Classes, Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) and Economically Weaker Section (EWS) were selected. However, the selected candidates could not be given appointment letters due to the COVID-19 pandemic and lockdown in the state.
The original selection lists were revised by the respective selection authorities after the Supreme Court's judgment. Due to revision of the original selection lists, the candidates belonging to the said categories which were at the bottom of the original selection lists could not make it to the revised selection lists and hence they have been deprived of appointments in Government services. The Cabinet of Ministers has decided to introduce a bill in the legislature to create a maximum number of posts for the candidates who have been deprived of such appointments.
Accordingly, the Maharashtra Legislature has passed the" Maharashtra Creation of Overwhelming Number of Posts and Appointment of Selected Candidates Act, 2022." Under Section 3 of the Act

Provision has been made for creation of supernumerary posts for the above candidates, and appointment to the department-wise and cadre-wise posts mentioned in the Appendix to the Act, by the concerned competent authority. Detailed instructions have been issued under section 4 (4) of the" Maharashtra Creation of Supernumerary Posts and Appointment of Selected Candidates Act, 2022" vide Government Resolution dated 21.09.2022 of the General Administration Department.
Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts in the Maharashtra Education Service Group-B (Administration Branch) Deputy Educational Officer and similar cadres on the establishment of the School Education Department.
Government Resolution: In the Schedule accompanying the" Maharashtra Creation of Superintendence Posts and Appointment of Selected Candidates Act, 2022," there are 4 candidates for the post of Deputy Education Officer and similar cadre under the School Education Department. General Administration Department As per the guidelines in the Government Decision dated 21.09.2022, 4 supernumerary posts are being created in the Maharashtra Education Service Group-B (Administration Branch) and similar cadre on the establishment of the School Education and Sports Department.
2. Expenditure on the salary of the above 4 number of posts of Deputy Educational Officer and similar, Group B cadre is included in this Department's" Demand No. E-2, 2202 GENERAL EDUCATION- 01- Elementary Education, 104 OBSERVATION (00) (01) Inspection of elementary schools, to be made from the grant approved under the head' Mandatory 22020021'.
After receiving the names of the 4 candidates who were affected in the selection process (State Service Examination 2019) from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, these candidates will be appointed as per the prevailing rules to the post of Deputy Education Officer and similar posts in Maharashtra Education Service Group-B (Administration Branch).
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the instructions in the Government Decision dated 21.09.2022 issued by the General Administration Department in accordance with the Maharashtra Government Maharashtra Creation of Superannuation Posts and Appointment of Selected Candidates Act, 2022.
