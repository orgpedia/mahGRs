Procedure to be followed in respect of Deductible Over and Under Contributions: The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for recovering the prescribed 10% (Basic Pay+ Dearness Allowance thereon) contribution amount every month from the salary dues of the employee and depositing the employer's 10% Equated Monthly Contribution. The Principal/ Principal and Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the concerned school will be responsible for adjusting the excess amount in the next month in case the amount is credited to the employee's account. In case of less amount deposited, the Headmaster/ Principal and Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the concerned school will be responsible for depositing the required amount in the next month.
18.4 It shall be the responsibility of the employee to ensure that the contributions made to the National Pension Scheme account are correct every month. If any error is found in it, the employee must give a written statement to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Complaints will not be considered if they are not made in writing.
18.5 The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for ensuring that in case of unauthorised absence, extraordinary leave, the payment of salary of the employee is not withdrawn from the salary system. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be responsible for ensuring that the provisions of Rules 264 and 265 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, are strictly followed. Further, the State Controlling Officer shall give necessary instructions to the field officers regarding the action to be taken under the Shalarth System as per the provisions of the Government Decision Finance Division No. 1009/ PR No. 39/ Koshaprasad-5, dated 4/10/2013 and the accompanying annexure. In case of non-compliance of these rules, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Treasury Officer will be liable for administrative action.
18.6 The Annual Returns with respect to Permanent Pension Account Number (PRAN) of the employees shall be sent to the concerned employee by the Central Record Keeping Agency every year. Also, the Central Record Keeping Agency will provide the facility to the registered employees to view the details of the amount deposited in the account of the concerned employee on their website at any time.
18.7. The cases relating to the refund of contributions to the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme up to 30/11/2019 shall be disposed of in accordance with the provisions of Government Decision 20/12/2017. Further, from 01/12/2019 onwards, cases of such returns shall be dealt with by the Central Record Keeping Agency.
