Government Supplementary Orders: As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, orders for transfer of officers who have completed three years as on 31st May, 2019 have been issued vide order dated 28.05.2019. According to the order, some of the transferred officials had requested for a change in their postings. Considering their request, with the approval of the competent authority, orders have been issued vide order dated 13.08.2019 for partial modification in the postings of 10 officers.
The order dated 13.08.2019 shall not apply to the officers who have joined the place of transfer as per the order dated 28.05.2019.
