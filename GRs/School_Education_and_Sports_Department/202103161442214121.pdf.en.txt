Introduction In order to improve the quality of students in the state, the curriculum concept taught to them is easily communicated to the children by the teachers. There is no offline/ online method to assess the extent to which teachers have used physical training every year by the State Government for learning, whether the training has made learning/ teaching easier, whether the quality of students has increased due to improvement/ change in the learning method. Therefore, a proposal to create an online teacher training platform for imparting training in various skills through online mode was under consideration of the government. High-level committee The proposal for Online Teacher Training Platform has been approved in the meeting held on 19/10/2020.
Government's decision
The high-level committee has approved the creation of an Online Teacher Training Platform. The state government has approved the creation of an online teacher training platform to provide online training to teachers in the state.
