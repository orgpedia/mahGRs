The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme through Yashoda, Pune for Group A posts and Vanamati, Nagpur for Group B posts. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for the candidates who were selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the State Service Examination-2017 and the State Service Examination-2018 and who underwent training in the combined probationary training program-5 and 6.
Government's decision:
Based on the final results of the State Service Examination-2017 conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, out of 59 candidates selected for appointment to the post of Deputy Educational Officer and similar, Group-B (Administration Branch), 36 candidates have appeared for training at Vanamati, Nagpur.
