Educational institutions/ schools in Mumbai, Nashik and Konkan division should take action as follows.

Eligible graduate teachers working in educational institutions/ schools in Mumbai and Konkan region will have to fill Form 18 (Form 18) to register their names in the electoral roll of the respective Graduate Legislative Council Constituency. The duly filled Form 18 (FORM 18), along with necessary proof of educational qualification, may be submitted to the concerned office mentioned above. If the application is submitted in the physical office, the concerned designated officer will have to show the original degree certificate/ mark sheet or other prescribed documents. In case of sending Sample 18 by post, the proof/ documents indicating the educational qualification concerned must be attested by the Designated Officer/ Assistant Designated Officer/ Gazetted Officer/ Notary Public of the concerned district.
(b) Teachers Constituency Since the electoral rolls are being prepared for the Teachers Legislative Council Constituency in Mumbai and Nashik Division, the schools in that Division, which are not below the middle school level, as determined by the State Government with the approval of the Election Commission under Act 27 (3) (b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1950, shall be affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education. Teachers who have been working as teachers for at least 3 years out of the 6 years preceding the qualifying date i.e. 1.11.2023 should be encouraged to enrol themselves in the electoral rolls of the respective Teachers Constituencies.
The eligible teachers of Mumbai and Nashik divisions will have to fill Form 19 (Form 19) along with the certificate issued by the head of the concerned educational institution in the prescribed form to register their names in the electoral roll of the concerned Teachers Legislative Council Constituency.
The sample 19 and the sample of the prescribed certificate will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), the concerned District Collector (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner and Tehsildar as designated by the Divisional Commissioner.
