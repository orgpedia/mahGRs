In order to improve the standard of physical education and sports in the state, as a first step to establish a sports university, a central sports center at Shivchhatrapati Kridangari in Pune and sports presidiums at 14 different places in the state have been established. The decision was approved by 1. At present, there are a total of 9 sports academies in the state and a total of 526 resident athletes are expected to be admitted in 2020-21.
The demands include provision for food, medical treatment, participation in competitions, education, sports uniforms, purchase of sports equipment, staff honorarium, building rent, hostel maintenance and repair, office and administrative expenses etc. 5 is written here. Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds to meet the expenses of the sports academies is under the consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
In order to meet the expenditure on food, medical treatment, competition participation, academic and sports uniform, purchase of sports equipment, staff honorarium, building rent, hostel maintenance and repair, office and administrative expenses, etc., of the existing sportspersons of the State, in the financial year 2020-21, 10% of the sanctioned amount under this head i.e. Rs. 120.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Twenty Lakhs only) is hereby approved to be released and placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
2. In the year 2020-2021, the Sports University and Sports Academy, Sports University were provided Rs. 120.00 lakh (Rupees One Crore Twenty Lakh only) should be disbursed to the Sportspersons.
The above funds should be spent as per the agreed criteria as per the requirement. The funds should be withdrawn at the time when they are actually to be spent. The amount should not be deposited in any bank account.
Approval is being accorded to disburse the aforesaid provision subject to utilization of the aforesaid provision by implementing the various orders/ rules/ Government decisions/ financial rules issued by the State Government from time to time in respect of finance.
Expenditure on the above matters shall be met from the provision available for the current financial year under the head Demand No.E-3" 2204, Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (33) Sports University and Sports Academy, (33) Establishment of Sports University, Non-Plan Scheme, 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary), (2204 2064)."
Action should be taken to spend the above provisions as per the instructions of the Finance Department, Government Circular, dated 16th April, 2020 and dated 4th May, 2020. In this case, the conditions set out in Annex No. 9 of the Finance Department Government Circular are being fulfilled. All other provisions of this Order shall not apply. Accounts Officer, Sports and Youth Services, Divisional Deputy Director Office, Aurangabad, District Sports Officer, Thane, Kolhapur, Nagpur, Amravati, Gadchiroli, Nashik, Ahmednagar are declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers and Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services are declared as Controlling Officer.
