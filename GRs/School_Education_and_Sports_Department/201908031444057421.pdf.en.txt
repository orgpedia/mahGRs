Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of approval for relocation of Acharya Multi-Purpose Service Institute, Parbhani operating Acharya Global Academy, Saba, Vasamat Road, Parbhani to English Medium Self-Financed Primary and Upper Primary School, Dattadham Area, Vasamat Road, Parbhani was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The relocation of the above school has been proposed due to the absence of a paved road in the place and the remote area. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The consent of the parents shall be necessary for the transfer of the said school. Similarly, since the school is on self-financing basis, the institution will be required to submit a registered lease deed for a period of at least 30 years for the land taken on rent in the municipal area (minimum 500 sq.m.) at the relocated place.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(6) As the said school is an English medium self-financing institution, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in it shall be on the institution.
