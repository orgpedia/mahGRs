Government Decision: Bhalerao Rambhau Patil Multipurpose Institute, Marwar, Ta. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to the Registrar, G. Jalgaon on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of change of management of the school.
(2) The Junior College shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(3) It shall be ensured that in case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government.
(4) Since the Junior College is on self-financing basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(5) As the recognition of the said Junior College is on self-financing basis, it is deemed to be deemed to be deemed to be a Government School as per the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012.
