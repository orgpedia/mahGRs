The Proposal:
For the Department of School Education and Sports (itself), a new high duty (fast speed) duplex digital copier machine with all-in-one facility of a very good model of a reputed company providing maximum back-up copies per minute was under the consideration of the Government for supply on lease basis (including supply of spare parts, papers, toner ink and machine operator facility along with maintenance of the machine) for a period of one year from 01.09.2022 to 31.08.2023. Accordingly, invoking the prevailing procedure, the sealed tenders were invited from the private suppliers vide Notice dated 28.07.2022. Accordingly, a total of 4 tenders received by the Department were considered in the Purchase Committee meeting dated 11 th August, 2022. In the meeting, it was decided to enter into an annual service contract agreement with the supplier who submitted the minimum tariff from the suppliers qualified in the technical examination, and the government is issuing orders accordingly
