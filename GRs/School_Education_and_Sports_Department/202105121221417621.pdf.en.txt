Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) 10) Cancellation of examination.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No:- Examination 0521/ S.No.39/ SD-2
Ministry, Extension Building, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 12th of May 2021
Introduction: Certificate in Secondary Schooling (ECS) through Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) 10th) examination is conducted. The exam was scheduled to be held in the month of May 2021 for the academic year 2020-21. However, due to the extraordinary situation arising out of the outbreak of COVID-19 disease, the matter of cancellation of certificate examination in secondary schools conducted by the State Board was under consideration of the Government.
Considering the interest of all the students appearing for the Class X examination and all the factors related to the examination, the Government has decided as follows:
The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) has released the timetable for SSC and HSC examinations 2020-21. 10th) The government is approving the cancellation of the examination. Accordingly, the Board should take appropriate action to make necessary changes in the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Regulations 1977.
Separate instructions will be issued regarding the issuance of mark sheets/ certificates and the admission process for Class 11.
