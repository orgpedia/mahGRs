Reference: Letter dated 13th October, 2016 to the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, vide G.O.S.S./ PRIV-5/ Transfer/ 2016-2017/1883/ 85.
The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the permanent English medium unaided primary school Aurangabad to Godavari Educational and Social Institute, Gangapur, District Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Godavari Educational and Social Institute, Gangapur, G. Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) The school will be run on self-financing basis.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(3) The school shall function on a self-financing basis in English medium and the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval are not changed in the case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
