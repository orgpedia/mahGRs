Preamble: The Government has decided to implement the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Inclusive Education for Disabled. It was made operational for classes 9 and 12 as on 31.08.2009. In case of non-compliance of any administrative provisions while approving the unit of 1185 special teachers and 72 sepoys under this scheme, the unit recognition granted to the said special teachers and sepoys and the individual recognition provided by the Divisional Deputy Director of Education in accordance with it should be cancelled from the Director of Education (Primary) level by following the prescribed procedure and the concerned persons should be demoted from the post of special teacher/ sepoy accordingly. dated 07.07.2015.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune, issued an order in 2015 cancelling the recognition and termination of service of the concerned unit. In the case of the Disabled Inclusive Education Scheme (Secondary) Scheme, the special teachers protested against the cancellation of unit recognition and the individual recognition given by them and the termination of service order. High Court, Bombay, Bench at Aurangabad in Writ Petition No. 1030/2016 was filed. In this case, The court gave. " The order dated 25.08.2016 quashing the order of termination of service of the Director of Education and reinstatement of the concerned petitioner in service and payment of honorarium for the said period and arrears, if any, be paid," the bench said.

4. Ma. The High Court of Judicature at Bombay, Aurangabad. During the hearing on 02.05.2023, it was ordered that the outstanding honorarium of the special teachers who are appointed as per rules and who have held the educational and professional qualifications as per the provisions of the scheme within the prescribed period, in respect of which there is no dispute, should be paid before the next hearing. The court has given. In pursuance of this order, a proposal for payment of arrears of honorarium to a total of 130 special educators has been submitted by the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune to the Government vide his letter dated 7.06.2023.
Under the Disabled Inclusive Education Scheme (Secondary Level), out of the 161 teachers who were found eligible for appointment in the inquiry of the Secretary Level Committee, Rs. 11,46,43, 670/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Out of the 161 teachers who were found eligible for appointment in the inquiry of the Secretary Level Committee under the Disabled Inclusive Education Scheme (Secondary Level), 130 teachers were paid Rs. Government approval is being granted for disbursement of subsidy amounting to Rs. 11,46,43, 670/-.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune should pay honorarium to 130 special teachers out of 161 teachers who were found eligible for appointment in the inquiry of the Secretary-level committee under the Disabled Inclusive Education Scheme (Secondary Level). The amount of outstanding honorarium should be deposited in the accounts of only 130 special educators who have qualified in the inquiry of the Secretary Committee. Special educators should not be paid salaries. The Director of Education (Primary), Pune will be held responsible for this.
3.After the payment of the honorarium as above, if the funds remain, the report should be submitted to the government. Prior approval of the government should be taken before utilising the balance funds.
The utilization certificate of the disbursed funds should be submitted to the government within a month.
The expenditure should be met from the budgetary provision available in the financial year 2023-24 under the head Demand No.E-2, 2202. General Education, 01 Primary Education, 106 Teachers and other expenditure, (01) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, (01) (02) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (General) (State share 40%), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried), (2202I612).
The funds are being handed over to the State Project Director, Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Charni Road, Mumbai. The Under Secretary/ Cell Officer (Cash Branch/ Accounts Branch), School Education and Sports Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, School Education and Sports Department, Mumbai will be the Disbursing Officer.
