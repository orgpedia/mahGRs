The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the permanent unaided primary school of English medium, Ghanshyamji Manvatkar Multi-Purpose Institute, Yavatmal, to Dadasaheb Gavai English Primary School, Vadgaon Road, G. Yavatmal, operated by Upakhya Dadasaheb Gavai Education Foundation, Yavatmal, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Ghanshyamji Manavtar Multi-Purpose Institute, Yavatmal on the following terms and conditions: (1) In case the school is not transferred along with the movable property, it will be necessary to submit a notarized certificate with the photograph and signature of all the members of the parent institution.
(2) It will be necessary to complete the simple online information of the school.
(3) Transfer fee of Rs. 10,000/- is required to be paid.
(4) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) As the school is a permanent English medium unaided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(10) If any fresh complaints are received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or the terms and conditions regarding the change of management are violated.
