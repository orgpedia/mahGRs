The Proposal:
As Dattarao Nande has applied for the post of Statistical Officer/ Research Officer Group-B (Gazetted) in the prescribed manner, as per the request of Shri Nande, he has been relieved as per the provisions of Para 2 (b) (1) of the General Administration Department 02.12.1997 vide Government Memorandum dated 05.04.2019 to join as Statistical Officer/ Research Officer Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Regional Office, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Aurangabad. Mr. Nande has been relieved from his original post i.e., the post of Superintendent, Group-B in the General State Service (Administration Branch) by retaining the retention power as per the provisions of Rule 22 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981.
Shri Dattarao Chandan Nande vide his application dated 08.07.2019 has requested to be reinstated in the post of Superintendent, Group-B in the General State Service (Administration Branch) to which he was originally entitled due to his family problems. General State Service to Shri Dattarao Chandan Nande in his original capacity.

Government Resolution No. 3819/ PR No. 76/ PR-II (Administration Branch) was under consideration of the Government for re-appointment to the post of Superintendent Group-B. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Mr. Dattarao Chandan Nande, Statistical Officer, Regional Office, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Aurangabad is being recruited subject to the terms of his original appointment order as he continues to hold the post of Superintendent Group-B in General State Service (Administration Branch) in his parent cadre. Dattarao Chandan Nande has been posted as Superintendent (Sarase), Primary Department, G. P. Parbhani. Mr. Nande should join the post immediately.
Commissioner (Education), Pune shall ensure that the officers shall join the place of posting as per this order immediately and submit a report to the Government.
