Preamble: The Department of School Education and Sports, Government of India has accorded administrative approval for the supply and installation of Innovative Science Centres in Zilla Parishad Schools in the State, with effect from February 9, 2023. Accordingly, the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to provide funds vide letter dated 11/4/2023. The matter was under consideration of the government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PARIOUS- 2023/ Q.No.23/ SD-1
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. As proposed by the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 11/4/2023, the Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 23,11,95, 800/- (Rupees Twenty Three Crores Eleven Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand Eight Hundred only) for the supply and construction of Innovative Science Centres in Zilla Parishad Schools in the State.
2. Expenditure on the matter" Demand No. E-2, 2202, General Education, 800-Other Expenditure, (00) (10) Providing Educational Materials to Students (22025679), (Programme) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment)" from the budgetary provisions made available during the year 2023-24 under the head' Budgetary Provisions."
For this purpose, Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, School Education and Sports Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Under Secretary/ Cell Officer (Cash Branch), School Education and Sports Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall draw the said fund from the treasury and hand it over to the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune for implementation. All funds must be used for the purpose for which they were provided.
4. The grant is a conditional grant and its terms and conditions are as per the decision of the said Government. Utilization certificate of funds already disbursed has also been submitted.
