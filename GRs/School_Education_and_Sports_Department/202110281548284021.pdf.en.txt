The Proposal:
Section 12 (1) (c) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 provides for reservation of 25% seats at the entry level in private unaided schools for children belonging to disadvantaged and weaker sections. Further, as per Section 12 (2) of the Act, there is a provision for reimbursement of fees for elementary education of 25% admitted children.
