Government order:
Smt. Ashwini Rajendra Bharud, Sub-District Programme Coordinator (MGNREGA) vide Government Order dated 27.01.2022 in the said context. Zilla Parishad Nandurbar has been posted as Assistant Commissioner, Maharashtra State Council of Examination, Pune on deputation for a period of one year initially. Smt. Bharud joined the post on 09.02.2022. His term of deputation ended on 08.02.2023 and the period of deputation is being extended for a further period of one year.
The terms and conditions of the Government Order dated 27.01.2022 in respect of the deputation of Smt. Ashwini Rajendra Bharud shall continue to be applicable as before.
