A total of 5281 temporary posts in the office were extended from March 1, 2018 to September 30, 2018. As the said period has expired, the Commissioner, Education, State of Maharashtra, Pune has issued Reference No. As proposed under Section 3 of the Finance Department Reference No. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Section 2, the Government was considering the extension of a total of 5281 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner of Education and all field offices under him.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, 5281 temporary posts existing in the Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and its Subordinate Regional Offices will be filled up with effect from 01.10.2018
The government has extended the deadline till February 28, 2019. The establishment-wise details in this regard are given in Form A and Form B.
Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and all the offices under him and all the offices of the Divisional Deputy Director of Education should take care to attach the office-wise and post-wise statements of the posts in his office while submitting the salary of the temporary posts.
