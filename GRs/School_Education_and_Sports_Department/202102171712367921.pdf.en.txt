GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 1219/115/ TNT-3 prescribes the procedure for depositing the Sixth Pay Commission arrears in Level-2 of the employees covered under the New Definitive Contribution Pension Scheme for the teaching and non-teaching staff of 100% aided posts in Secondary, Higher Secondary and Teachers Schools. However, since the provident fund or national pension or defined contributory pension scheme is not applicable to the teaching and non-teaching staff of partially aided private schools, the matter of payment of arrears of the 6th Pay Commission in cash was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision Approval is being given to pay the arrears of the difference of the 6th Pay Commission for the period from 01.01.2006 to 31.03.2009 to the teachers and non-teaching staff of partially aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools recognized in the State in lump sum cash as the Provident Fund or National Pension or Defined Contributory Pension Scheme is not applicable.
