As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the transfer orders of officers who have completed three years of service as on May 31, 2019, have been issued on May 29, 2019.
Kamladevi Shridhar Awate, Principal, District Institute of Educational Continuing Professional Development, Pune has been transferred and posted as Principal, District Institute of Educational Continuing Professional Development, Thane. Considering the request of Smt. Kamaladevi Shridhar Awate, her transfer vide order dated 29th May, 2019 stands cancelled and she is being given one year extension in the post of Principal, District Institute of Educational Professional Development, Pune.
(2) These orders are being issued with the approval of the competent authority.
If the Commissioner (Education), Pune has issued an order dated 29th May, 2019 relieving the said officers from their present posts, the same should be cancelled. Appropriate orders should be issued to the concerned officers regarding the extension of their original post and a report should be submitted to the government.
4. If Mrs. Kamaladevi Shridhar Awate, Principal has joined the post of Principal at the place of transfer made by the Government Order dated 29th May, 2019 i.e. District Educational and Professional Development Institute, Thane, then this order will not be implemented.
