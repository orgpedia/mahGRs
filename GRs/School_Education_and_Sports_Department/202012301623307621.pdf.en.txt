Government's decision:
The existing shooting range at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mhalunge, Balewadi, Pune has been upgraded by replacing the damaged target with a new 25m range. Shooting Range SIUS Make Automatic Electronic Scoring Target Systems ISSF Phase III Approved Model LS 25/50 for 25 M Model for 40 Lane Targets Rs. 370.00 (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Lakhs only) from Swiss Escore, Switzerland, the only authorized dealer in India. The administrative approval for procurement from Gem International, Faridabad through single source procurement mode is being provided subject to the following conditions:
A proposal for grant under Khelo India State Centre of Excellence for upgradation of Shooting Range at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mhalunge, Balewadi, Pune has been submitted to the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India. The expenditure for this should be met from the funds received from the Central Government.
