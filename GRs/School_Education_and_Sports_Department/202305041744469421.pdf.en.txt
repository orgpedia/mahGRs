Terms and Conditions:
(1) The officer concerned shall commence his deputation on the date on which he enters upon his office of deputation and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes charge of his original office.
(2) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(3) If their services on deputation are not found necessary by the non-employer, the non-employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the self-employed employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority.
(4) The officers concerned shall be transferred to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to go back to the parent department.
