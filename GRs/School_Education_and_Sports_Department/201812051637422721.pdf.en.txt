Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Administration Branch) Deputy Director of Education and similar post
The Provisional Seniority Notice dated 01.01.2018 was published vide the Notice dated 05.04.2018. If there are objections from some officers regarding this seniority, they were instructed to send it to the government through the office of the Commissioner (Education), Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune within 1 month from the date of publication of the provisional seniority. However, the officials did not respond on the issue.
(2) The said seniority has been maintained among the officers who have joined the promoted post within the prescribed period (after the period of assumption of office) as per the provisions of rules 4 and 5 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982.
The said seniority is being issued subject to the decision of the Supreme Court, the High Court and the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal on any other special civil application/ writ petition filed earlier or if this seniority list has to be changed.
In view of all the facts and circumstances mentioned in the above paragraph, the final seniority of the post of Deputy Director of Education and similar post in Group-A (Administration Branch) of Maharashtra Education Service as on the end of 31.12.2017 is being published in the form attached. This should be brought to the notice of the Commissioner of Seniority (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune without any delay by all the concerned officers.
