Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of approval to shift the English Medium Self-Financed Primary (Classes 1 to 7) School, The Royal Public School, Shamnagar, Bhagwan Nagar Road, Nagpur, run by Navshakti Educational and Cultural Institute, Nagpur, to Kalamna, Old Toll Naka, Kamthi Road, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to insufficient space of the above school, it is proposed to shift to Kalamna, Old Toll Naka, Kamthi Road, Nagpur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
