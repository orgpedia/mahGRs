Panchsheel Primary Vidyamandir, Government Doodh Dairy, opposite Bhaiji Nagar, Gokul Nagar, G. Dhule, a Marathi medium aided primary school (1st to 4th grade), which was transferred from Dhule Minority Institution to Rashtramata Jijau Sevabhavi Sanstha, Dhule Minority Institution. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Sakri, G. Dhule was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing rented building to a self-owned building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
