The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the Institute to Parli, G.Beed on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Deputy Director of Education, Aurangabad, shall ensure that the financial position of Vivekanand Vidya Pratishthan is good. Also, the president/ secretary will have to submit an undertaking that there is no court case against the two institutions.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued from time to time by the Government, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) It shall be ensured that the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for the transfer of the school. transfer
Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff who have given consent will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be obligatory for the new institution to comply with the acts, rules, government decisions and orders prescribed from time to time by the government in respect of schools, teachers/ non-teaching staff, salary/ post-payment grants, batches, students, etc. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
