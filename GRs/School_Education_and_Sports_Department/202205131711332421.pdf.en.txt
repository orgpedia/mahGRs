Government's decision:
The Army Public School, Raipur, is affiliated to the Indira Gandhi School of Military Science, Aurangabad. As per the decision of the government, the tribal batch of Class 12 has been approved to start from the academic year 2021-22. With the amendment, it is being approved to start the tribal batch from the academic year 2015-16 instead of 2021-22 subject to the following conditions:
Though the Sainik School, Aurangabad, has been granted recognition to a separate batch of tribal students of Class XII from the academic year 2015-16, the salary arrears of the eligible teachers for the period 2015-16 to 2020-21 will not be paid. The organization will be responsible for paying the salaries during this period.
This Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 2 of the Tribal Development Department. 2/ Q-11, being issued pursuant to the approval received vide dated 14.03.2022.
