Preamble: Sarvodaya Multi-purpose Education Training Institute for Backward Classes, Wardha runs Nishatai Vankhade, Upper Primary School, Khamla, Nagpur, a Marathi medium aided upper primary (class 1st to 7th) school, Isasani (Vagadara), Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Hingna, G.Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The above school is proposed to be shifted from the rented building to the land owned by the institution. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above-mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
