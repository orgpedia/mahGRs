The Proposal:
The ratio of Central and State share for Samagra Shiksha (Primary Education) Scheme is 60:40. The Central Government vide order dated 05/08/2020 has released the ad-hoc ad-hoc ad-hoc ad-hoc share (TSP) of Rs. 19,55,66, 000/- has been sanctioned. Out of this amount, Rs. 13,03,77, 333/-. The Finance Department has approved the release of funds within the limit of 15 per cent of the budgetary allocation. Accordingly, the Tribal Development Department vide Reference No. 5 Under the head Central Share, 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Payment) under Government Resolution (2202 I 648), Rs. 1089.66 lakhs and (2202I 657) State share, under the head 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) Rs. Rs. 500 crore has been allocated for this.
The Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Mumbai has issued a notification regarding the same. Under the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to disburse the central share and state share funds. Department of Finance As per the instructions issued vide circular dated 18/05/2020, the Central Govt. of ST Appropriation sanctioned vide letter dated 05/08/2020 amounting to Rs. 19,55,66, 000/- has actually been credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State. E-mail received on 04/09/2020. The government was contemplating to release the funds.
The government's decision is as follows:
