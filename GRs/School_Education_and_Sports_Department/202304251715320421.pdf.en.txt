Reference: Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur vide No.
Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for shifting of the English Medium Self-financed Primary (Class 1st to 5th) School, Shubham Nursery, Shamnagar, Kupwad, District Sangli, run by Maharani Ahilyabai Holkar Education Society, Sangli, to Kids Paradise English Medium School, Vasantnagar, Sangli, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to inadequate physical facilities in the above school, the school has been shifted to a full-fledged building, Shubham Nursery, Shamnagar, Kupwad, District Sangli. Therefore, the relocation of the said school is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is an English medium self-financing institution, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
(6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur should take further action regarding the relocation of the school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
