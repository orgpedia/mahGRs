As per the circular dated September 24, 2021, in the academic year 2021-2022, schools from class V to class XII in rural areas and from class VIII to XII in urban areas of the state will be opened. The guidelines have been issued to start from October 4, 2021. As the corona outbreak in the state is decreasing day by day, classes 1 to 4 in rural areas of the state and classes 1 to 7 in municipal areas have been closed. Approval has been given to start from December 1, 2021.
