Regarding extension of time for continuation of manual typewriting courses in Government recognized typewriting and shorthand commercial institutions in the State.
The Government of Maharashtra

Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai- 400 032.

Reference:- 1) Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. SPE-1012/ (108/12)/ Sashi-1, dated: 31st October, 2013 No. SRIKH- 2015/ (116/15)/ SD-2, Dated: 30th September, 2015 3) Govemment Certificate No., School Education& Sports Department, No. Narrow- 2016/ (116/15)/ SD-2, dated: 13th July, 2016 4) GOVERNMENT DECISION School Education and Sports Department No. The 16th November 2017
Preamble: Considering the policy adopted by the Government to use computer in the functioning of all government/ semi-government offices and to adopt e-governance/ e-office system in all departments of the government, the Government has decided to start computer typing courses in government recognized commercial institutions in the state. The decision was made on October 31, 2013. While starting the computer typing course, the manual typing course is given. The decision to close was taken in a phased manner by November 30, 2015. However, after that. Manual typing course has been started from 30th September, 2015. The deadline has been extended to May 31, 2016. After that. Manual Typewriting Course as on 13th July, 2016. The deadline has been extended to May 31, 2017.
After that. As per the Government decision dated 16th November, 2017, Government recognized Miniature Typewriting in the State is being implemented in commercial establishments along with Computer Typewriting. The government has approved the continuation of manual typing courses till November 30, 2019. However, some organizations have demanded the government to continue the manual typing course along with computer typing. According to this, the government was considering to extend the time to continue the manual typewriting course along with computer typewriting in the government-recognized small typewriting trade institutions.
Government Decision: Manual typing courses along with computer typing will be provided in the government-recognized business institutes of minting and typing in the state. The government has decided to continue the scheme beyond November 30, 2019. All Government recognized typewriting and shorthand commercial institutions should make necessary changes in the institutions to start the course of computer typewriting along with manual typewriting.
