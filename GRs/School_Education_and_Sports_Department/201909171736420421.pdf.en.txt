Introduction: Maharashtra Sevabhavi Sanstha, Beed runs New Vision English School, Neknur, T.G. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the institution to Annasaheb Patil Sevabhavi Sanstha, Chandegaon, T. G. Beed was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Annasaheb Patil Sevabhavi Sanstha, Nandgaon, T.G. Beed on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Beed shall be required to examine the order of recognition of classes 4th to 6th of the said school and ensure that the consent of the President and members of the institution is obtained as per Annexure-I of the Maharashtra Sevabhavi Sanstha, Beed.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(3) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(4) As the school is a permanent English medium unaided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(5) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(7) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(8) If any fresh complaint is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or the terms and conditions regarding the change of management are violated.

The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution prior to the change of management have been breached by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, shall examine all the matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
