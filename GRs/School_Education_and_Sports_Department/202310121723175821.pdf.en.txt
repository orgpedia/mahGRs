Pr/ RTE/ RTE 520/2023/4009, d. 25.05.2023 3) Government Resolution No. The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education (RTE) Act, 2009, provides for reservation of at least 25% of seats in private unaided schools at the entry level for children belonging to disadvantaged and weaker sections. Further, under Section 12 (2) of the said Act, provision has been made for reimbursement of educational fee of the student admitted under RTE 25% by the State Government. Accordingly, Joint Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune has requested to provide funds in the aforesaid Reference No. 2 letter to the government. For the academic year 2023-2024, Rs. A sum of Rs. 200.00 crore has been allocated for this. The government has already taken the decision. As on 20th July, 2023, an amount of Rs. 40.00 crore has been disbursed. Now, in accordance with the approval given by the Finance Department for reimbursement of educational fee of the student who has been given RTE 25% admission, an amount of Rs. The government is planning to spend Rs 36.75 crore for the purpose.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the provisions made under Section 12 (1) (c) and Section 12 (2) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, RTE has decided to provide Rs. 36.75 crore (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) to the Director of Education (Primary), State of Maharashtra, Pune.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune shall disburse the funds in proportion to the reasonable demand made by the districts. The funds allocated will be used only for 2022-23 and

3. Government circular for reimbursement of educational fees. As on 21.8.2019, the following should be verified.
The details of the fees to be charged for the students of class 1 to 8 should be declared on the Saral portal or the RTE portal. The school has its own website. Such schools should also declare the details of fees on the website.
The school should attach a certificate of recognition of RTE under the Right to Education Act.
3) Students who have already been admitted to the school through the online lottery system should ensure that they are studying in the concerned school through the Saral and RTE portal.
All students should have their Aadhaar card registered on the Saral portal. Only the number of students enrolled should be considered. Also, only 25 per cent of the total number of students will be considered for reimbursement.
(5) No school shall be entitled to reimbursement of tuition fee on the ground of obtaining any land, building, equipment or other facilities free of cost or at a concessional rate as provided in the proviso to section 12 (2) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, to the extent of the number of children to whom the condition of free education is imposed.
