Preamble: In order to provide financial assistance for modernizing the old talmis/ akhadas which have kept the tradition of wrestling in the modern times in the state, and to provide financial assistance to the concerned institutions for promoting the development of Talim Wrestling Center, Reference No. 1 is given here. The development of Talim Wrestling Centre has been approved under the Government Resolution dated 01.03.2014. Under this, the Commissioner Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government through a letter at Reference No. 2, 3 and 4 to grant administrative approval for the grant to various institutions for the development of Talim Wrestling Center. In pursuance of the said proposal, it was under consideration of the Government to grant administrative approval for sanction of financial assistance to the concerned institution for development of the centre for Talim Kusti.
