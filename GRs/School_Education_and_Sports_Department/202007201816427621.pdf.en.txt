/ CISCE/ I. B./ I. The procedure for grant of No Objection Certificate to the schools in the State desirous of affiliation to the Board syllabus/ CIE etc. has been laid down under the Government decision in Reference No. Further, the fee for scrutiny of proposals received at the Government level from educational institutions for grant of No Objection Certificate and renewal of No Objection Certificate issued, has been fixed under the letter of reference (1). The government was considering to increase the tax.
Government's decision:
CBSE/ CISCE/ IB/ ICSE Proposals for grant of No Objection Certificate and renewal of No Objection Certificate issued for affiliation to a course of the Board/ CIE etc. are received at the Government level. To examine the proposals
It is hereby decided to increase the charges levied and fix the amount of revised charges as follows:
