Introduction: Vidyavardhini Shikshan& Krida Prasarak Mandal, Buldana, runs Raje Chhatrapati Junior Arts College, Ramnagar, G. Buldana, a Marathi medium unaided higher secondary school (class 11th to 12th- Arts Branch), Mauje Dhamangaon Badhe, Ta. The proposal received under reference letter for relocation to Motala, G. Buldana was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted to its own building in hilly and drought prone areas. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) In respect of schools, teachers and non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grant, students.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
