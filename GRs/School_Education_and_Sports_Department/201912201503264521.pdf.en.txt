Reference:- Letter from Commissioner Education, Commissionerate of Education, Pune vide No. S. Aashi/ 2019/ Xerox Machine Kherdi/ Aastha No. 143/4823 dated 7th August, 2019
Introduction: Samsung SCX 8128NA/ XIP is a single Xerox machine for taking Xerox copies of daily office work in the office of the Commissionerate of Education, Maharashtra State Pune, which has been continuously malfunctioning for a long time and is causing problems in daily work. As the Xerox machine is continuously getting damaged, a proposal has been received under the reference letter for obtaining administrative approval for dismantling the new Xerox machine. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the purchase of this Xerox machine was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision- In accordance with the proposal submitted by the Commissionerate of Education vide letter dated 07.08.2019, the estimated cost of replacement of defunct Xerox machines with new Xerox machines is Rs. 1,51,502/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty One Thousand Five Hundred Two only) is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
1) Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions contained in the Revised Manual of Procedures for Office Procurement by Government Departments published under Section 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016.
The conditions set out in Annexure to Regulation No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 read with Rule No. 76 of Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division II, No. 26-A, shall be complied with.
3) It should be ensured that the required technical manpower and other facilities are available to operate the said xerox machine. Also, ensure that the machine is not left unattended.
4) The expenditure for the said procurement shall be met from the approved and available provision under section 13 Office Expenditure for the year 2019-20 (Head 2202I432).
5) It should be ensured that there is no duplication of the purchase of this equipment.
The Commissioner of Education will be fully responsible for fulfilling the above condition.
30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 read with Annexure to Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division II A.S. 26-A, Rule No. The proposal is being approved as per the powers conferred on the administrative department under Section 76.
