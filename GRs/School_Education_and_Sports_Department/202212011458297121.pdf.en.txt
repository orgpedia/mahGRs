The Maharashtra Private Schools Employees (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act, 1977 has been enacted to regulate the conditions of service of employees in order to provide stability and stability in service to the employees of private management schools in the state so as to enable them to perform their duties effectively and efficiently. The Maharashtra Private Schools Employees (Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981 have been framed under this Act. According to Rule No. Sub-Rules (41-1) regarding transfer of vacancies from unaided or partially aided schools or batches to partially aided or fully aided schools or batches respectively have been filed vide Government Notification No.Sanikya-2019/ PR No. 341/ TNT-1, dated 08.06.2020.
It states that the provisions must be followed to ensure that there are no additional teachers, compliance with service excellence, considering the need of the subject, the appointment of the teacher must be approved before the transfer, the transfer must be made to the vacant post. In this regard, detailed instructions have been issued by the Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution No. 341/ TNT-1, dated 01.04.2021.

Irregularities have been noticed in transferring teachers from unaided post to partially aided or aided school or batch without compliance and paying salaries to such teachers from 100 per cent government aided post. Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:
Government Circular- During the period of the ban on the recruitment of teachers and non-teaching staff in private schools in the state, the by-law included in the original rule under the Sun Notification, dated 08.06.2020, No. (41-1) and the Government Decision issued pursuant thereto, in view of the fact that there is a transfer from an unaided or partially aided school or batch to a vacant post in a partially aided or fully aided school or batch respectively without complying with the provisions of the Government Decision, dated 01.04.2021, the following instructions are being issued:
Government Notification No.Sankrit-2019/ PR No.341/ TNT-1, dated 08.06.2020 vide Rule No. 341/ TNT-1, dated 01.04.2021 is hereby stayed until further orders.
(2) Wherever, after such adjournment, the Deputy Director of Primary/ Secondary and Divisional Education takes action for grant of personal recognition, disciplinary action shall be taken against the educational officer in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Conduct) (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
(3) If any court case arises during the moratorium period and it is found that there is an urgent need to resolve the matter, a detailed proposal should be submitted to the Government through the Commissioner, Education for order and decision.
The Commissioner (Education) Maharashtra State should submit a detailed report to the Government within 3 months from the date of the Government Circular with recommendations as to whether or not the provisions of the Reference Notification, dated 08.06.2020 and Government Decision, dated 01.04.2021 regarding the transfer of teachers in all the districts of the State have been complied with, as well as whether or not the postings have been transferred during the ban period.
