Proposal- Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Govt. of India The guidelines of the Scheme" Khelo India- National Programme for Development of Sports" Use and Creation/ Upgradation of Sports Facilities" have been issued vide circular dated 27/11/2017. Accordingly, the proposals received by the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services of the state for the creation of sports facilities are submitted to the government. After examining the proposal and the accompanying documents, the detailed application and the detailed certificate of the proposal are sent to the Central Government with the copy of the Additional Chief Secretary, School Education and Sports Department. Considering the facilities available in the district under Khelo India, the matter of setting up a committee to scrutinize the proposals at the government level was under consideration.
Government's decision
" Khelo India- National Programme for Development of Sports was launched under the scheme" Utilization and Upgradation of Sports Facilities." As per the Central Government guidelines dated 27/11/2017, the following Scrutiny Committees are being constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Sports& Youth Affairs) to scrutinize the proposals received by the Government at the State level regarding grant for construction of sports facilities.
