The Proposal: -
Mahatma Jotirao Phule was a pioneer of social revolution in modern India, a great social reformer, educationist and a champion of human values. He fearlessly fought against caste inequality, superstition, and the ban on education in the 19th century. He spread the idea of equality, social justice and human emancipation to the common man by establishing the Satya Shodhak Samaj.
Mahatma Phule and Savitribai Phule, the pioneers of Sri Shiksha, demonstrated in real action that education is the effective medium of social change. He struggled throughout his life for education, dignity and equality for the deprived, oppressed society. That is why Mahatma Phule's thoughts continue to be a great guide to democracy based on constitutional values, social equality and equality.
The year 2027 marks 200 years of the birth anniversary of Mahatma Phule. In this regard, Ma. The Chief Minister has announced in the 2026 budget session to celebrate the bicentennial year of Mahatma Jotirao Phule in 2027. Accordingly, the Government decided to take up the Rababaya initiative to celebrate the bicentennial year of Mahatma Jyotirao Phule in 2027. The committee has been constituted with effect from 10th March, 2026. Mahatma Jotirao Phule through the School Education Department. From April 19, 2026 to d. Government Circular: - Mahatma Jotirao Phule's work in the fields of social equality, women's education, farmer's welfare and social justice in Indian society is unparalleled. In line with the National Education Policy 2020, it is essential to promote value-based, inclusive and skill-based education among students. Accordingly, various activities, programmes should be celebrated throughout the year in all the schools of the state on the occasion of his bicentennial.
