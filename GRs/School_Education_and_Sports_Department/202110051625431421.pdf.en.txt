Read: 1.I letter from the office of the Deputy Commissioner (Education) No. Aastha-B124/ JESU/ Shishas/ 2021/3848 dated 07.09.2021
The final seniority list for the post of Joint Director of Education in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Administration Branch) dated 01.01.2020 has been published on 04.10.2021.
Now the provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2021 is being published in the accompanying Form-A.
2. The said seniority has been created vide General Administration Department Notification No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII dated 21.06.2021.
3. Ma. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015 dated 04.08.2017, has set aside the Government decision dated 25.05.2004 and declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. To challenge the decision. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. The seniority is being issued subject to the final decision in the Special Leave Petition. Accordingly, the seniority list is being published subject to the condition that if there is a change in the selection list after 2004, the same will be changed accordingly.
The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall bring the said seniority to the notice of all concerned, the officers having objections regarding seniority shall submit a representation in this regard to the Government through the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune, within two weeks from the date of issue of this order. Any submissions received after the deadline will not be considered.
The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall forward the representations received within the prescribed time frame to the Government immediately along with their comments.
