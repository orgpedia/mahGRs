Regulation of transfers and performance of official duties of Government of Maharashtra
The Prevention of Delay Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and their performance of official duties.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared under Section 6 of the Prevention of Delay Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority to be transferred under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the powers of various cadre officers in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of the said Act, a proposal for transfer of officers who have completed three years of service as on 31st May, 2020 was submitted to the Civil Services Board. urban

The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. Also, the concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted to cancel the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
Commissioner (Education), Pune, as per this order, shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Officer about the immediate release of the said officers from their present posts and shall ensure that the transferred officers join the given place of posting within the prescribed period and submit a report to the Government.
In order to ensure effective implementation of this order, the salary of the transferred officers should not be drawn by the office of the transferor under any circumstances from the date of September 1, 2020.
