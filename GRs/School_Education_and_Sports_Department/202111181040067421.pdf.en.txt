Introduction: Vishwavadhi Jangli Maharaj Trust, Palghar runs Gurudev Chaitanyaswarup High School and Junior College (Arts), Ambhai, Tt. Kurz High School, Kurz, Palghar, an unaided junior college (Class 11th 12th Arts) of Wada, G. Palghar. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift to Vikramgad, Palghar district was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The above Junior College has been proposed to be shifted as the English medium residential students have been sent to Ambhai for education under the project of the Tribal Department, due to insufficient space. Therefore, the transfer of the said Junior College to the above-mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.

Government Resolution No. Mashastha 3319/ PR No. 425/ SM-1 (4) The school will continue to function at the shifted place on the same principle of grant-in-aid as at the original place.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
