Preamble: It was decided under the Government Decision dated 25.11.2005 to appoint the newly appointed non-teaching staff in the private aided schools in the State on honorarium basis for the first three years. As per the decision of the Government, the following allowances were made admissible to the non-teaching staff:
----------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | designation                      | Remuneration (Rs.) |
| 1.   | Junior Scribe                    | 2000/              |
| 2.   | part-time librarian              | 1500/-             |
| 3.   | a full-time librarian            | 2500/-             |
| 4.   | A part-time laboratory assistant | 2000/              |
| 5.   | A full-time laboratory assistant | 2500/-             |
| 6.   | The 4th Grade Employee           | 1700/-             |
----------------------------------------------------------------
The salary of the non-teaching staff has not been increased since 2005. Considering the increase in the salary of regular non-teaching staff as per the 7th Pay Commission, rising inflation, the government was considering increasing the honorarium of non-teaching staff. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
The government has decided to pay honorarium to the non-teaching staff of private aided schools in the state for the first three years from their first appointment as follows:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | designation                                        | Remuneration (Rs.) |
| 1.   | librarian                                          | 14,000/-           |
| 2.   | A laboratory assistant                             | 12,000/-           |
| 3.   | the Junior Clerk                                   | 10,000/-           |
| 4.   | Class IV employees (only on compassionate grounds) | 8,000/-            |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
