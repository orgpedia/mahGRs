As per the decision taken by the Government in the context No. 1, 2 and 3, full-time teaching and non-teaching staff in the recognized private aided primary, secondary, high school, junior college, teachers' school, military school, teachers and non-teaching staff in the secondary section of Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats and part-time teachers and non-teaching staff have been implemented the revised pay structure as per the Seventh Pay Commission. Since the revised pay structure has come into effect from 1st January, 2016, the arrears admissible for the period from 1st January, 2016 to 31st December, 2018 will be paid in 5 equal installments over a period of 5 years from the financial year 2019-20. Detailed instructions regarding payment of arrears have been issued under Government Resolution No. 4.

Government Resolution Number: Salary is 1019/105/ TNT-3. In this background, the matter of postponing the payment of the second installment due on July 1, 2020 to the teachers and non-teaching staff of private aided schools in the state was under the consideration of the government.
The government is now ordering that the payment of the second installment due on July 1, 2020, to the full-time teaching and non-teaching staff of recognized private aided primary, secondary, high school, junior colleges, teachers' schools, military schools, teachers and non-teaching staff of the secondary section of municipal corporations, municipal councils and nagar panchayats and part-time teachers and non-teaching staff in urban local bodies in the state be postponed by one year. Separate orders will be issued for payment of this instalment.
