Introduction: Vishwavadhik Jungli Maharaj Ashram Trust, Thane runs Gurudev Chaitanya Swaroop High School and Junior College, Ambhai, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the unaided junior college of Wada, G. Palghar to Kudus Vibhag Shiksha Sewa Sangh, Palghar was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Kudus Vibhag Shiksha Sewa Sangh, Palghar on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Secondary School and Junior College of the said institution are functioning and only the Junior College of the said institution is being transferred. Therefore, the junior college will be required to obtain a separate U-Dias number.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of a junior college where the management has changed.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in respect of the Junior College where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said transfer or there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management.
