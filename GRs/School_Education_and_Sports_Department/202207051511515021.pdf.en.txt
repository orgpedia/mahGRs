Introduction: Om Shree Sai Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur runs Raisne Convent, Hudkeshwar Road, Nagpur, an English medium self-financed primary school (Class 1st to 4th), Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to 1, Mauja Kharbi, Samarth Colony, Saibaba Nagar, Kharbi Road, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the dilapidation of the above school building and inadequate physical facilities in the school, the plot No. It is proposed to be shifted to 1, Mauja Kharbi, Samarth Colony, Saibaba Nagar, Kharbi Road, Nagpur. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
