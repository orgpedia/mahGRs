Government's decision:
Section 18 (5) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 provides that" any person who establishes or operates a school without obtaining a certificate of recognition or continues to operate a school even after withdrawal of recognition shall be liable to a monetary penalty which may extend to one lakh rupees and for every day during which such contravention is continued, to a monetary penalty which may extend to ten thousand rupees." As per the said provision, directions were issued under the Government Resolution at Reference No. 3 regarding action against unauthorised schools in the State. Nevertheless, the President
The government has stayed the order till further orders under reference (4). The said stay is in conflict with the provisions of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the Government Order in Reference No. 4 is hereby quashed.
