The proposal: Shri Ramchandra Hindi Primary School, Ranjangaon (SC), Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad). The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of management of Hindi medium unaided upper primary (1st to 7th standard) school at Gangapur, G. Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad) to Advaita Pratishthan, RH 72/ 2, Bajajnagar, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of this school is Advaita Pratishthan, RH 72/ 2, Bajajnagar, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar
(Aurangabad) The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated, or

The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer of teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management if the conditions of service are violated by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad) Division, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad), after examining all the aspects and including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions, has directed the school to provide the necessary facilities to the students.
Further action on the change of management should be taken within 15 days and the government should be informed about it.
