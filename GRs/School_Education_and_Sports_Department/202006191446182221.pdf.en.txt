2) Government Decision Department of School Education, No. 3419/ No. 43/ TNT-6, D. 26/02/2020.
Government Decision- A Joint Committee has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 24/7/2019 to study the applicability of the old pension scheme to all teachers and non-teaching staff who were appointed before November 1, 2005 on all private aided, unaided partially-aided and batches in the state. Now the date for making recommendation to the Government to the Joint Committee is being extended till 31/07/2020.
