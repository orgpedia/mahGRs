Introduction: Mahatma Jotiba Phule Higher Primary School, Wanera, Nagpur, operated by Sandip Education Society, Nagpur. Parashivani, G. Nagpur, a Marathi medium aided higher primary school, near bus stop Parashivani, Ta. The proposal received under reference letter for relocation to Parsivani, G.Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is presently functioning in a rented building and it is proposed to shift the school after the expiry of the lease agreement. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
