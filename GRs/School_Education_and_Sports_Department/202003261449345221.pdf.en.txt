Introduction: Khwaja Garib Nawaz Minority Education Institute, Yavatmal runs Khwaja Garib Nawaz Junior College, Babhulgaon, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Urdu medium unaided junior college, Yavatmal, to Gar-e-Hira Multi-Purpose Educational Institute, Digras, G. Yavatmal, was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Junior College to Gar-e-Hira Multi-purpose Educational Institution, Digras, G. Yavatmal on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred Junior College shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the transferred Junior College.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Order, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said transfer or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
