Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for shifting of the Urdu Medium Partially Aided Primary School (Classes 1 to 7) of Paradise Social Society (Minority Institution), Beed run by Shahjahan Urdu Primary School, Ektanagar, Jatwada Road, Harsul, Aurangabad to Darul Uloom Campus, Beed, T.G. Beed was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The aforesaid Primary School, run by a Minority Institution, has proposed relocation as the owner of the building has informed about the demolition of the school building by selling the land.
Therefore, the relocation of the said primary school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
