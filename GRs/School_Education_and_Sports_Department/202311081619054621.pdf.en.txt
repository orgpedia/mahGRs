The Proposal:
The Finance Department has approved the recruitment of 140 posts on contract basis for the offices of the National Cadet Corps. The matter of providing funds for the salary/ honorarium of the said 140 posts was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Finance Department has approved the recruitment of 140 posts on contract basis for the offices of the National Cadet Corps. The 140 contractual employees of the Rashtriya Chhatra Sena Office appointed by the Ministry of External Affairs have been paid honorarium up to June 2023 and for the period of eight months from July 2023 to February 2024, the salary/ honorarium of 140 posts has been increased by Rs. Under this Government decision, a sum of Rs. 2,17,77, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Seventeen Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand only) is being sanctioned.
The expenditure shall be met from the provisions available in the head of account Rashtriya Chhatra Sena, Demand No.E-3, 2204, Sports and Youth Services, 102, Youth Welfare Programme for Students (01) Youth Welfare Programme for Students (01) Rashtriya Chhatra Sena 10 Contractual Service (22040016). The concerned Commanding Officer, Group Headquarters, Rashtriya Chhatra Sena is being authorized as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw this amount from the treasury. Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune is being authorized as Controlling Officer.
(3) The Government may, subject to the condition that no more funds shall be expended than the provision made under the aforesaid heads of accounts for the year, and in accordance with the provisions of the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 977/2023/ Expenditure-5, No. being issued with the consent received as on 02.11.2023.
