The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 28.6.2012, it was decided to provide basic training to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) sponsored candidates by appointing them to the post of Deputy Education Officer and equivalent in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A cadre. Accordingly, the said officers were appointed as Deputy Educational Officers and equivalent post for a probationary period of 2 years.
2. Mr. Balasaheb Yamaji Dhanve, Group Education Officer, while working in this post, was given a contract by the Anti-Corruption Department. He was arrested on 24.3.2017 (BCE) and a case was registered against him at Police Station Ambhora and as per the order dated 24.4.2017 issued by the Office in this Division, under Rule 4 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, he was suspended from the date of arrest and his probation period was not completed due to a court case/ departmental inquiry in his case. He was later reinstated in government service. Since the irregularities were not during their probation period, the General Administration Department As per the Probation Policy dated 22.6.2021, it was under consideration of the Government to terminate the probationary period of Shri Dhanve. Following this, the government decided:
Government's decision:
The proposal regarding completion of probationary period of the officers appointed to the post of Deputy Educational Officer and equivalent in Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-B has been submitted by the Commissioner (Education) vide letter dated December 8, 2017 and dated July 20, 2018. The following officers, who have successfully completed their basic education and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the Government, are being completed their probation period on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 7 in front of their name and the Government is approving to continue their service on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 8.
