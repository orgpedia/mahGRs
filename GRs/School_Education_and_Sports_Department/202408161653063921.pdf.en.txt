The Proposal: -
Millat Education Society (Minority Society), Darwa District Yavatmal Operated Millat English
Primary and Secondary High School, Darwa G. Yavatmal, an English medium self-financing secondary school
(1st to 10th) School to be shifted to Allied Society, Anant Nagar, Nagpur District Nagpur
The proposal received under the reference letter was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The relocation is proposed as the aforementioned school building is dilapidated and dangerous. So
The relocation of the school to the above mentioned places is permitted on the following terms and conditions:
It is: -
(1) The condition of minimum marks of a migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Interested students will also be required to be accommodated at the relocation site.
(2) The school operates on the self-financing principle of English medium.
The entire responsibility of the teaching / non-teaching staff will rest with the institution.
(3) In respect of schools, teaching and non-teaching staff, salary / non-salary grants, students, etc., by the Government.
Acts, rules, orders, etc., as may be prescribed from time to time, shall be required to be complied with by the institution.
(4) There shall be a change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
