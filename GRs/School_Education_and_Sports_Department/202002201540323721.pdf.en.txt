Introduction: The Higher Secondary Certificate (Class 12) examination conducted by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) is very important from the point of view of students. The success of the exam will determine the future of the students. Failure to pass the Higher Secondary Certificate (Class 12) examination has a negative impact on the mental and physical health of the students.
