Introduction: Jivan Jyoti Rural Education and Social Reforms Committee, Amravati (Minority Institution) runs J.J. Urdu Primary School, Hanuman Nagar, Amravati, an Urdu (Semi English) medium non-aided primary school (Class 1st to 4th), Plot No. 25, Muglai Pura, Paratwada, Ta. Achalpur,
The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Amaravati was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Due to the dilapidated and dangerous building of the above school, the plot of the school No. 25, Muglai Pura, Paratwada, Ta. Relocation to Achalpur, G. Amravati is proposed. Therefore, the relocation of the said school is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is on non-aided basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
