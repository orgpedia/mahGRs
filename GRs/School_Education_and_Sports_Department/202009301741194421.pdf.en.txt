The Maharashtra government has made the teaching and learning of Marathi language compulsory in all schools in the state. Further, as per Section 16 (1) of the Act, the State Government shall have the power to make rules for carrying out the purposes of the Act. This Act has come into force with effect from 01/04/2020. The Department of School Education and Sports has issued Reference No. 3 decision has been made. The government was considering setting up a committee to prepare rules under the Marathi Language Act, which is mandatory at the school level.
Government Decision: It has been decided to constitute the following committee to prepare the rules in accordance with the provisions of the above Act and Government Decision.
