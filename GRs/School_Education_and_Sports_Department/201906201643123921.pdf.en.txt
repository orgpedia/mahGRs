GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3019/ PR. NO. 308/ SM- 1 5) In case of a school where there is a change of management, care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(8) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the new institution has violated the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune shall examine all the matters in accordance with the above terms and conditions and take further action within 6 weeks regarding the change of management of the school, including other necessary conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
