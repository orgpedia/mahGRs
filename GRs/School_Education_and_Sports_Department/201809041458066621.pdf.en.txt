As on 16.08.2014, a total of 57 candidates were appointed directly to the posts of Superintendents in General State Service, (Administration Branch) Group-B, Cadre. Since 6 of the officers who accepted the appointment in this order were not able to get the right opportunity for the departmental examination from the date of their joining, the government was considering to extend the probationary period of these candidates by one year.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 6 officers who have been given direct appointment to the posts of Superintendent in the General State Service (Administration Branch) Group-B, cadre have not passed the departmental examination conducted from the date of their joining in 2 occasions. These 6 officers got the opportunity to appear in the departmental examination
They were unable to complete their term. However, in order to provide more opportunities to the below mentioned officers for departmental examination, the period of their probation is being increased by one year.
