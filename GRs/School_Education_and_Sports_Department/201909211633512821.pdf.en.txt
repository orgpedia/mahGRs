Government Decision School Education and Sports Department, vide No. 4215/ (38/15)/ Training, dated 24th July, 2017 2) Government Circular Finance Department, vide No. 19/ 2019/ 92/ Finance-3, dated 08th July, 2019 3) Letter dated 26/06/2019 of Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune Preamble- As per the State Training Policy, training is to be imparted to the officers/ staff of the School Education Department in the academic year 2019-20. For this purpose, Vidya Authority, Pune submitted a proposal to the Government on 26/06/2019. As per the Government decision dated 24th July, 2017, the training venue and fee per trainee has been fixed. A total of 1000 trainees will be trained under this training. The cost of the training is Rs. 1,06,83, 000/- was under consideration of the Government for sanction and sanction of expenditure. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision- In the financial year 2019-20, a total of Rs. A budget of Rs. 2.00 crore has been allocated for the project. Department of Finance As per the Government Circular dated 08.07.2019, the Department of Finance has allowed release of funds within the limit of 60% of the total budgetary allocation. Accordingly, Rs. 1,06,83, 000/- is being approved by the Government. This expenditure should be included in the target of 50% of other expenditure under the head of main account head, 2251, secretarial-social services, as follows: (00) (01) Training of Government Employees (Programme) (22511002). In this regard, the directions issued from time to time by this Department and the instructions given by the Finance Department in the Government Circular dated July 8, 2019, should be followed carefully.
