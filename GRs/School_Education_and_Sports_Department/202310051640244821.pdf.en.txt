2. Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mhalunge-Balewadi, Pune. The Budget Estimate of Rs. 72.78 crore (Rupees Seventy Two Crores Seventy Eight Lakh only) is being accorded fresh administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions. :
(1) The proceedings relating to the construction of the said project and the appropriate agreement and other ancillary proceedings relating to the leasing out of the premises thereof to the Maharashtra Olympic Association, affiliated bodies and other offices after the completion of the building shall be carried out under the supervision and control of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, State of Maharashtra, Pune.
(2) A detailed and detailed proposal for making available the space therein free of cost to the Maharashtra Olympic Association and its affiliated institutions for starting their offices and ancillary activities after the construction of the Olympic Building is completed should be submitted separately for the approval of the High Powered Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary.
3) The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services will have control over the maintenance and day-to-day operations of the Olympic building. The income from the rented offices in Olympic Bhawan, Olympic Museum and the facilities at Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex, Pune should be deposited in the account to be booked. The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services will have control over the revenue generated from the Olympic building and its utilization.
The International Olympic Committee (IOC), in collaboration with the Olympic Foundation for Culture and Heritage and the Indian Olympic Association (IOA), will set up an international-level Olympic museum and other ancillary facilities in the proposed Olympic building.
The necessary standards for regular management, day-to-day operation and maintenance should be set. An agreement should also be signed.
