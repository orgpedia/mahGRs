Preamble: Sarvodaya Multi-purpose Educational Institution for Backward Classes, Wardha, operating South West Point School, Somalwada, G.Nagpur, English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary School, Plot No. 377 and 400, Pa. The proposal received under the reference letter regarding relocation to Bidgaon, Nageshwar Nagar, G.Nagpur was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: As the present building of the above English medium school is dilapidated and dilapidated, the school is being shifted to a spacious building taken on lease for 30 years. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. 1) The Aadhaar number and other information of the school students will be required to be filled on a simple online system.
2) It should be ensured that there are no complaints about school transfers and that all students in the school are accommodated.
3) Qualified teachers and non-teaching staff working in the school will be accommodated at the place of relocation and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building and all necessary physical facilities and educational facilities. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
