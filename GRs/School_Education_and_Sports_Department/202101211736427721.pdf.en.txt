The posts of Director of Education in Group-A (Administration Branch) cadre of Maharashtra Education Service will be filled up with temporary additional seniority with effect from 31.12.2018 i.e. 01.01.2019 and 31.12.2019 i.e. 01.01.2020.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya Extension Building, Mumbai
Date: January 21, 2021
Read: 1. Letter from the Office of the Commissioner (Education) No. Ashika/ Astha-B124/ JESU/ Shisham/ 2018/ 849, dated 02.02.2019 2. Letter from the Office of the Commissioner (Education) No. Aastha-B/ 124/ JESU/ SC/ 2019/452, dated 27.01.2020 Government Circular The final seniority list for the post of Director of Education in Group-A (Administration Branch) cadre of Maharashtra Education Service has been published on 26.08.2018.
Now the Provisional Additional Seniority List dated 01.01.2019 is being published in Form-A alongwith the date 01.01.2020 in Form-B.
The seniority has been prepared in accordance with the Government Decision No. SRV/ 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department.
3. Ma. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015 dated 04.08.2017, has set aside the Government decision dated 25.05.2004 and declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. To challenge the decision. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. The seniority is being issued subject to the final decision in the Special Leave Petition. Accordingly, this provisional additional seniority is being released subject to the condition that if there is a change in the selection list after 2004, the said seniority list will change accordingly.
The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall bring the said seniority to the notice of all concerned, the officers having objections regarding seniority shall submit a representation in this regard to the Government through the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune, within two weeks from the date of issue of this order. Any submissions received after the deadline will not be considered. The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall forward the representations received within the prescribed time frame to the Government immediately along with their comments.
