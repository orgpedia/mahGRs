Introduction: The Department of School Education and Sports is functioning on the fourth floor in the extended building of the Ministry. The building and its furniture are very old. If the environment and facilities in the office are of good quality, it will definitely have a good effect on the work of the employees and will help in increasing efficiency. Accordingly, in the Ministry of the Department, in the Extended Building, Hall No. 413, 415, 417, 419, 432, 434, 436,438,440 and 443 and Hall No. Renovation of 446, 447 and 459 halls was under the consideration of the government.
The decision was taken by the School Education and Sports Department, Extension Building, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
413, 415, 417, 419, 432, 434, 436,438,440 and 443 and Hall No. 446, 447 and 459 Extension, Rs. 1,94,32, 947/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Four Lakh Thirty Two Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty Seven only) and Rs. 17,18,289/- (Rupees Seventeen Lakhs Eighteen Thousand Two Hundred and Nineteen only) aggregating to Rs. 2,11,51, 236/- (Rupees Two Crores Eleven Lakhs Fifty One Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Six only) is being sanctioned by the Government subject to the following conditions specified by the Public Works Department.
