Little Flowers English Medium School, No. 176/9, Dhamalwadi, Fursungi, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of Pune English Medium Self-Financed Higher Secondary School (all branches from class 1st to 12th) to Bhosale Village, Bhekarainagar, Fursungi G. Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has proposed to shift to the new building at Bhosale Village, Bhekarainagar, Fursungi, G. Pune due to inadequate physical facilities at the present location. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
