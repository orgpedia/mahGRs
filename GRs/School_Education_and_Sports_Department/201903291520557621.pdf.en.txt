As a follow up to the above decision of the Government, the revised monthly honorarium to the national/ international level outstanding sportspersons and medal winning wrestlers has been disbursed at 70% of the budgeted amount in 2018-19. The government was considering sanctioning the remaining funds for the scheme.
Government Decision: For the year 2018-19, Rs. 51,67,800/- out of which Rs. 40,19,400/- has already been disbursed. The remaining Rs. 11,48,400/- (Rupees Eleven Lakhs Forty Eight Thousand Four Hundred only) and to be placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
