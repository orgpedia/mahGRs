The Proposal:
The Government of India has decided to implement a new scheme namely Inclusive Education for Disabled Children at Secondary Stage (IECS) instead of Disabled Inclusive Education Scheme from 2009-10. In addition, as the Integrated Education for Disabled Children Scheme (IEDS) has been discontinued by the Central Government, 595 special teachers of the units working under this scheme have been absorbed in the post of teachers in primary schools as per the decision of the Government dated 15th September, 2010.
