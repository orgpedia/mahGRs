GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Selection Trials were held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai from 30th to 31st January, 2023 to select the Maharashtra Government team to participate in the All India Civil Services Table Tennis Tournament 2022-23 to be held at Table Tennis Hall, Chandigarh from 24th to 28th February, 2023. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,13,800/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirteen Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office shall relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 20th to 22nd February, 2023 and for the tournament from 24th to 28th February, 2023 with effect from 20th February, 2023 (MP) and the services of these players shall be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
Each player and manager of the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department Office under which they will be working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The period of absence from office from February 20 to 28, 2023 (training camp, competition and travel) should be treated as" duty period." as well as their

In case of balance, the balance amount should be returned immediately to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
The expenditure for this should be met from the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Competition, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Competition (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
