Preamble: As there is a need to have a separate Credit Head to match the funds received from the Central Government for 25% admission under Section 12 (1) (c) of the Right to Education Act, 2009, the Government was considering a new head of account for the scheme.
Government Decision: As per the approval given by the Planning and Finance Department, approval is being given to open new account titles as follows.
0202-Education, Sports, Art and Culture, 01-General Education, 101-Primary Education, (02) Reimbursement under RTE 25%.
