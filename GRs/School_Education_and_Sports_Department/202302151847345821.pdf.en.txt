The Proposal:
The Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award for the year 2021-22 has been announced as per the Government Decision dated 29 th December, 2022. It will be held in Mumbai on the birth anniversary of Savitribai Phule on January 3, 2023. The Model Code of Conduct came into force with immediate effect with the Election Commission of India announcing the election schedule for two Graduates' and three Teachers' seats of Maharashtra Legislative Council on 29th December, 2022. So read here. In the government decision dated December 30, 2022, it has been stated that the program of distribution of the said award will be postponed and the next date will be notified as soon as possible.
Government Decision: Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award for the year 2021-22 was presented in Mumbai on Friday. It will take place on February 24, 2023.
