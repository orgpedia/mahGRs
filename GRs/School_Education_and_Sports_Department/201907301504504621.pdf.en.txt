Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of a Marathi medium aided primary school, Datta Primary Vidyamandir, Veduwadi, Upalai Road, Barshi, G. Solapur, operated by Sarvodaya Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Solapur, to Chhatrapati Krida Shikshan Samajik Sanstha, Solapur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said Primary School to Chhatrapati Sports Education Social Welfare Society, Solapur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(3) It shall be ensured that in case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government.
(4) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as permissible posts and not more than that.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.

(7) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune shall examine all the aspects and take further action within 6 weeks including other necessary conditions regarding the change of management of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
