Preamble: As per the Government Decision dated 19th September, 2016, total 20% grant has been sanctioned to the posts of teachers/ non-teaching staff in 1628 schools and 2452 batches which were declared eligible for grant by the Government Decisions issued from time to time before 14th June, 2016 subject to the prescribed terms and conditions. Further, the Primary/ Secondary and Higher Secondary Schools, which are pending for declaration of eligibility for grant-in-aid at Government level, have been declared eligible for grant-in-aid vide Government Decision dated 13th September, 2019. The people's representatives and organizations had demanded to provide grants to the schools as per the terms and conditions of the original government decision of evaluation as the conditions prescribed in the government decision and the conditions of educational quality were not met in this regard. The government has revoked the terms and conditions
The Government was considering the issue of grant-in-aid to eligible schools as per the terms and conditions of the decision dated 15th November, 2011 and dated 4th June, 2014.

Government Decision: The Cabinet in its meeting dated 28th August, 2019 has approved 20% grant in aid to 276 unannounced primary schools and 2851 teaching and non-teaching posts in 1031 batches, 128 unannounced secondary schools and 2160 teaching and non-teaching posts in 798 batches with effect from 1st April, 2019.
Further, a total of 753 teachers/ non-teaching posts in 123 schools and 60 blocks/ additional branches of Higher Secondary and 9097 non-teaching posts in 1656 schools, 523 blocks and 1929 additional branches of Higher Secondary have been declared eligible for grant-in-aid and 20% subsidy has been sanctioned with effect from 1st April, 2019.
As per the Government Decision dated 19/9/2016, 20% grant has been sanctioned for 28217 teaching/ non-teaching posts in 2417 schools and 4561 batches, who were declared eligible for grant as on 1st and 2nd July, 2016 and were sanctioned 20% grant, with effect from 1st April 2019.
Since all these conditions and conditions have been fixed with the approval of the Cabinet, the Government should take action to provide grants to the primary/ secondary and higher secondary schools/ batches declared eligible for grants under the Decision dated September 13, 2019 and to the primary and secondary schools/ batches receiving 20% grant as per the terms and conditions dated November 15, 2011 and June 4, 2014.
