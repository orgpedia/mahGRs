Introduction: The Maharashtra State Board of Textbook Production and Curriculum Research, Pune has been publishing Kishore monthly for the past 47 years for the benefit of students. The Board is making special efforts through this initiative to promote knowledge through entertainment, keeping in mind the child's age. As per the list of 2001, at present 53,585 Zilla Parishad Primary Schools are being provided with Adolescent marks. Since it does not include schools newly opened after 2001, those schools are deprived of this scheme, so the matter of providing funds for the supply of juvenile magazine to these schools was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being given to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 49,32,560/- (Rupees Nineteen Fifty Five Lakh Thirty Two Thousand Five Hundred Six only) per annum for distribution of Kishore Digits to 61,657 ZP Schools from the financial year 2018-19 for supply of Kishore Magazines to all ZP Primary Schools in the State.
