Introduction: Shri Saraswati Bhuvan Education Society, Aurangabad At the Centenary Celebrations held on 04/05/2015, Ma. The Chief Minister had announced to provide assistance for the future plan of the institute. After that Ma. The Minister (Finance), in his Budget Speech 2018-19, said," On the occasion of the centenary year of Shri Saraswati Bhuvan Shikshan Sanstha, Aurangabad, a sum of Rs. 2 crore has been earmarked for the purpose," he said. Accordingly, a proposal has been received by this Department under Reference No. 6. The proposal was under the consideration of the government to provide grants to the institute by giving administrative approval along with financial approval. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government has decided to set up 121 smart classrooms in 12 schools of Shri Saraswati Bhuvan Shikshan Sanstha, Aurangabad. The proposal for grant of Rs.2 crore as a special case is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The Institute shall take care that the prices mentioned in Annexure I are estimated in nature and the total final cost of the project after tendering process shall not exceed the estimated cost.
(2) The total cost of the project is Rs. 333.60 lakh, out of which special grant of Rs. 2 crore will be provided by the government and the remaining amount will be raised by the institute.
(3) An undertaking shall be obtained from the institution that the expenditure will not exceed the amount sanctioned and if the expenditure is in excess, it will be made by the institution out of its own funds.
(4) The Department of Industry, Energy and Labour. As per the Government decision dated 01/12/2016, the tender process should be carried out and the contractor/ supplier should be selected in consultation with the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad.

(5) After providing the commissioning order, the institution should request funds from the government through the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Aurangabad. Funds will be disbursed as per the progress of the work.
(6) 50% of the funds will be disbursed after the actual work starts. Further 25% of the funds will be released after completion of three-fourths of the work and after completion of the smart classroom project, it will be mandatory for the institution to submit the utilization certificate before demanding the remaining 25% of the funds.
(7) The institution shall ensure that the quality of the works proposed and the quality of computers and similar systems are appropriate.
The estimated cost and works of the Smart Classroom project are mentioned in the Annexure along with the details.
The said expenditure should be met from the grants sanctioned during the financial year 2019-2020 under the head Demand No.E-2, 2202, General Education, 02 Secondary Education, 110, Assistance to Non-Government Secondary Schools and Junior Colleges (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to General Secondary Schools (DATAMAT) (22020442).
The Commissioner (Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune is hereby declared as the Controlling Officer and the Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad is declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
(6) The said administrative sanction is granted only as a one-time exceptional case and shall not be applicable as a precedent in other cases.
7. The said Government Decision, Informal Reference No. 424/19/ VY-5, dated 29/06/2019 is being withdrawn with the concurrence of Finance Department.
