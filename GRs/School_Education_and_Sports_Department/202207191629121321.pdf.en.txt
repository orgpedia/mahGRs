The Proposal:
National Commission for Protection of Child Rights The Supreme Court in Suo-Moto Writ Petition No. 04/2020 and 06/2021, the State Government has sought information on the education of out-of-school students and students whose parents died during the Covid period. The Supreme Court in Suo-Moto Writ Petition No. The affidavit pursuant to the hearing of 04/2020 and 06/2021 is to be prepared by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights, for which it is important to submit information at the state level. In order to discuss the above and take further action, a meeting was organized by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights on 16 th July, 2022 at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi.
As per the directions given by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights in its meeting held on 16 th July, 2022 and vide No. As mentioned in the Supreme Court's petition 04/2020, it is necessary to conduct regular review of out-of-school students in every district of the state, admit students found out of school, guide the parents of the students and appoint nodal officers at the district level to complete other works related to out-of-school students in each district. As per the Government decision dated 23 rd June, 2022, Child Development Project Officer for the age group of 3 to 5 years, Education Officer (Primary) for the age group of 6 to 14 years and Education Officer (Secondary) for the age group of 14 to 18 years have been appointed as Nodal Officers for action on out of school children. However, it was under consideration of the Government to appoint Education Officers (Primary) as Nodal Officers at each district level to compile the entire data at the district level and register it on the online portal of the Central Government.

GOVERNMENT Circular No. Sankeerth-2022/ PR No. 92/ SD-4 GOVERNMENT Circular: Education Officers (Primary) are being appointed as Nodal Officers at every district level to identify out of school students in every district of the State, conduct regular review of out of school students, admit students found out of school, guide parents of students and complete other works related to out of school students in every district.
The nodal officer will be responsible for guiding the schools in the district on the matter related to out-of-school students, controlling their activities, implementing the instructions issued from the state level. The list of nodal officers appointed district-wise, their names, e-mail IDs and telephone numbers are given in" Form-A."
