The Proposal:
The Hon'ble Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 (Chairman and Managing Director, FCI and Others Vs. Jagadish Balaram Behera and Others) and other petitions.
The Supreme Court has ruled that persons who joined government service on the basis of reservation for backward castes on July 6, 2017, and whose caste claims have since been invalidated, will not be entitled to protection in government service. The Hon'ble High Court, Bombay Bench, Nagpur has disposed of the writ petition No. 3140/2008 vide order dated 28.09.2018, to proceed in accordance with the orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court till 31.12.2019. The Government was considering creation of more number of posts in the Commissioner Education and its subordinate offices under this department to implement the Supreme Court verdict
Government's decision:
The Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 and other petitions dated 06.07.2017, it is hereby approved to create 1 post of Superintendent, General State Service, Group-B (Administration Branch) as a supernumerary post where the caste certificate of Scheduled Tribe is invalidated on the establishment of the Commissioner Education and his subordinate office under this department.
