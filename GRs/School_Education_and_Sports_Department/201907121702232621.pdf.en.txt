Jalna of Marathi medium aided primary school, Bhalchandra Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the institution to Aurangabad was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Bhalchandra Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad, T.G. Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) It shall be mandatory to submit a notarized affidavit on stamp paper with the photographs and signatures of all the members of both the institutions that the transfer of the said school is not done with immovable property.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The joint agreement of the two institutions on the transfer of the school on stamp paper shall be required to be notarized and submitted with the signature of the President/ Secretary. Also, there was no financial transaction in the transfer of the school, the signature of the President/ Secretary of both the institutions was Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(6) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.

The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
