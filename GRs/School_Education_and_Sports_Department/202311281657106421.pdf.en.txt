Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation of Royal Education Society, Vidyanagar, Bhandara to Royal Public School, Vidyanagar, Bhandara, a self-financed English medium Secondary School (Classes 1 to 10), Mouza Bela, G. Bhandara, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has been shifted as there is not enough space. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school is self-financing English medium.
The entire responsibility of teaching/ non-teaching staff will be on the institution.
(3) The Government shall provide to the schools, teachers and non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grant, students, etc. The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
(6) If any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The government will have the right to cancel the migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
