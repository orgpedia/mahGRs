Gajanan Maharaj Multi-purpose Institute, Kingaon Raja, G. Buldana Managed Kamakshadevi Secondary School, Ward No. King of Qingdao, 1. Buldana is a Marathi medium high school (5th to 10th aided and 11th to 12th self-financed), Group No. 580, King of Qingdao. The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for the relocation of Sindkhed Raja, G. Buldana was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has been shifted from inadequate building to self-sufficient building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(3) School, teachers and non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grant, students in this regard.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.

The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
