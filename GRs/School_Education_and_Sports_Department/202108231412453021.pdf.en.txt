The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval to the relocation of G. Satara was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the building of the above school being very dangerous and no alternative arrangements in the drought-hit village, the children of the hilly area have been shifted to Chikhli, T.G. Satara. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
