Introduction: Maa Durga Shakti Mahila Sanstha, Kondhwa, Pune runs National English Medium School, Dattanagar, Katraj, Pune. 70, No. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift to 2, Varad Building, Santosh Nagar, Shree Complex, Katraj, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted on the grounds of insufficient space and inadequate physical facilities. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Pune shall ensure that the simple online information of the said school is filled and the U-Dice number is obtained.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(6) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the English medium as the school is on self-financing basis.
