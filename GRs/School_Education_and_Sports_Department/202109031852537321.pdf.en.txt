The Proposal:
As per the Government Order dated 27.03.2017 of the Department of School Education and Sports, the post of Senior Advocate in Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre has been appointed for a period of 2 years.
The proposal to complete the period of probation has been submitted vide letter dated 24.03.2021 and 30.06.2021 of Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government, the officers mentioned in the accompanying proforma working in the post of Senior Advocate in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre as per the Government Decision dated 02.05.2018 mentioned above, who are fulfilling the conditions of probationary period, are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in the Rakkana No. 7 in front of their names.
