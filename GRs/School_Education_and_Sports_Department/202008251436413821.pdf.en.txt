Satara Military School was established. Established on June 23, 1961, it is the first military school in the country. The main objective of this school is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children of the bottom rung of Maharashtra at all levels to become officers in the Indian Army through the National Defence Academy.
A large number of students from this school have so far been admitted to the National Defence Academy.
The Satara Sainik School is sanctioned ex-gratia (non-salary) every year for training and administrative expenses, building maintenance, food allowance, horse maintenance, advertisement expenses, municipal taxes, etc.
Finance Department No. (2) The circular here has approved the disbursement of 25% of the funds.
For the financial year 2019-20, Rs. 2,04,67, 084/- to the fund under reference no. (1) is distributed. 22020469 for the financial year 2020-21. The budget provision is Rs 6,56,85, 000. The balance amount for the financial year 2019-20 and advance grant for the financial year 2020-21 is Rs. 3,99,10, 723/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Out of the budgetary provision available for the financial year 2020-21, Satara Sainik School has been allocated balance fund of Rs. 1,36,44, 723/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Six Lakh Forty Four Thousand Seven Hundred and Twenty Three only) and advance grant for the financial year 2020-21 is Rs. 27,76,527/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Lakh Seventy Six Thousand Five Hundred and Twenty Seven only) aggregating to Rs. 1,64,21, 250/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty-four Lakhs Twenty-one Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) to Satara Sainik School for the financial year 2020-21.
