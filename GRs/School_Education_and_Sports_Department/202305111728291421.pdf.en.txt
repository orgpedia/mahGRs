Introduction: Al-Falah Minority Development Educational Multi-Purpose Institution, Washim operates Rising Stars English Primary School, Hivra Rohilla, G. Washim, an English medium self-financed primary (class 1st to 5th) school, near Eidgah, Kekat Umra Road, Ta. The proposal received under the letter regarding the relocation of G. Washim was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the old rented building to the physical facility building owned by the Institute Secretary. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
