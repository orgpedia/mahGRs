Preamble: Sadguru Manajibaba Educational Broadcasting Society, Palashi, Ta. Sadguru Manajibaba Vidyalaya, Palashi, Satara. Siddhanath School, G. Satara, a Marathi medium aided secondary school. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the institution to Khatau, G. Satara was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Siddhanath Education Board, Palashi, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Khatau, G. Satara on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason for the school where the management has changed.
(2) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(3) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3119/ PR. No. 356/ SM-1 (7) The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur, after examining all the aspects and including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions, should take further action on the change of management of the school within 4 weeks and inform the government about the action taken.
