(e) The constitution of the Student Safety Committee: -
1) Occasional incidents of misconduct regarding the safety of the schoolboy, especially sexual harassment, are reported. The society's approach to such events is sensitive, and such events have a negative impact on students, their families, and society as a whole. Such unfair practices need to be completely eradicated.
(ii) Student Safety Committees should be constituted in schools under the Chairmanship of the Principal. The committee will have two representatives of parents, two representatives of teachers (one female teacher representative). The Committee shall review the implementation of Government decisions / circulars prescribed by the Government from time to time regarding student safety at school level every month and submit a report to the concerned Education Officer / Inspector of Education. The committee should also certify and affix a certificate on the facade of the school in the form prescribed by the government from time to time regarding the implementation of measures regarding student safety.
