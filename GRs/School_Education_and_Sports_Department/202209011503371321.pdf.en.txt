The award was instituted by the Government of India for the year 1983-84 in the State of Maharashtra for outstanding work in the field of female adult literacy. Maharashtra State Institute of Women's Adult Education was established under the government decision of 1. After the establishment of the institution, the actual work begins according to the government directives from time to time. The benefit of Dearness Allowance given by the Government to the Government employees from time to time as per the Government decision and the revised pay scale implemented since January 1986 has been given to the employees of the Institute from time to time. The officers/ employees working in this institution were being paid salaries and allowances as per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra. This process was regular from the inception of the Institute till the implementation of the 5th Pay Commission. These employees were given the benefit of the 5th Pay Commission from February 1999 to June 1999. After this, a resolution was passed in the Aurangabad meeting that the salaries according to the 5th Pay Commission should not be paid to the employees.
In view of the above fact, the staff of the institution, Mrs. Malti Gudde and others gave the above information. On 16.05.2008. Writ Petition No.1 at High Court, Nagpur. 2125/2008 filed
