A proposal to transfer the Matoshree Parvatai Vanjari Primary School (earlier known as Matoshree Primary School), Kuhi, G. Nagpur, a Marathi medium unaided primary school, to Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Education Officer (Primary), Nagpur shall ensure after inspection at the place of the present school that the school is not closed at the present place and is functioning there regularly.
(2) In the documents submitted by the institution, two different names of the said school are mentioned. With the approval of the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, it will be necessary to take proper action on the name of the school and take care that the same name of the school is registered on all online systems and government documents like U-Dice, Saral, Set Approval Order, etc.
(3) A notarized affidavit on bond paper shall be required to be taken along with the signatures of all the members in Annexure-I regarding" non-transfer of this school with immovable property and no objection to the transfer."
(4) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(5) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(6) The eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their service conditions shall not be changed. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(7) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(8) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff than that.
(9) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(11) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
2. Clause No. (1) and (3). On fulfillment of clauses (5) and (6), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall examine all the matters and take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school including other necessary conditions and inform the Government about the action taken.
