Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of' Khelo India- National Programme for Development of Sports" has been implemented in the State as per Government Decision, dated 20.11.2018. Under this scheme, an annual sports meet is organised for the youth of the country. The fifth edition of the Khelo India National Championships will be held in Madhya Pradesh from January 31 to February 11, 2023. The tournament will feature 27 different individual and team sports, including kabaddi, kho-kho, basketball and Mallakhamb.

The selection of players in individual sports disciplines will be done by the State and National level recognized associations and the selection of players in team sports disciplines of Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Basketball and Mallakhamb will be done by the Sports Director through district and state level selection trials. A 15-day pre-tournament training camp has been organized at Shri Shivchhatrapati Kridapith, Balewadi, Pune for the players selected by the Sports Director. The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune has sanctioned airfare for the entire selection trial programme and training camp and for the travel of approximately 650 persons including team players, sports guides, assistant guides and managers to the venue in Madhya Pradesh, as well as the cost of accommodation and food, travel expenses, medical expenses, purchase of sports uniforms and sports equipment etc. 2 to Rs. 2,10,97, 700/- has been requested to the Government.
The matter was under consideration of the government for release of funds.
Government's decision:
For selection of players from the State for the 5th Khelo India National Youth Championship 2022-23 to be held at Madhya Pradesh from 31st January to 11th February, 2023, District and State Level Selection Trials and Training Camps at Shri Shivchhatrapati Kridapith, Balewadi, Pune, Rs. 2,10,97, 700/- (Rs. 2 crore, 10 lakh, 90,000, 700 only), as per the Government Decision, dated 09.12.2022, for the 4th Khelo India National Games, the amount of Rs. 867.50 lakh is being approved as per the concurrence of the Department of Finance out of the available funds.
Once the programme for the award ceremony and felicitation of medal winners of the 4th Khelo India Youth Games, 2021 in Haryana is finalised, the proposal for the funds required for the same will be submitted separately to the Finance Department.
3. In respect of the matter shown in the following statement, Rs. 2,10,97, 700/- (Rupees Two Crores, Ten Lakhs, Ninety Seven Thousand, Seven Hundred only) is hereby approved to be disbursed towards expenditure and placed under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
