The Proposal:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 11 (1) of the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act, 2011, in Reference No. 2 here. Shri A.M. Dhawale, retired Judge of High Court and Joint Director of Education (Secondary& Higher Secondary), Directorate of Education, State of Maharashtra, Pune has been appointed as ex-officio Secretary to the Chairman of the Review Committee vide notification dated 25.02.2020. As per the provisions of Section 7 (4) and 11 (2) of the Act, the Government was considering the constitution of a selection committee for appointment of other members on the Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee and on the Revision Committee.
As per the provisions of Section 7 (4) and 11 (2) of the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Rules, 2011, the Selection Committee is being constituted as mentioned below:
Shri A.M. Dhawale, Former Justice, President of High Court 2) Additional Chief Secretary, School Education& Sports 3) Principal Secretary, Law& Justice
