Introduction: Established in the year 1914, Shri Hanuman Gymnasium Prasarak Mandal, Amravati is a sports organization having developed various sports facilities such as Ananta Sports Temple, Indoor Stadium, Open Stadium, Various Sports Grounds, Swimming Pool, 10m Shooting Range, Auditorium. The college is accredited by NAAC and is accredited by NAAC. Considering the achievements of the Institute in the field of sports and considering the sports facilities available in the Institute, the Degree College of Physical Education, Amravati will be given the status of" Deemed Sports University." The Deputy Chief Minister and Finance Minister have presented the State Budget for the year 2023-24 in the Legislative Assembly. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of forming a committee in coordination with the relevant departments and the Higher and Technical Education Department to take further necessary action.
Government Decision: It is hereby approved to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Amravati to take necessary further action in connection with grant of grant of status as" Deemed Sports University" to the Degree College of Physical Education, Amravati, run by Shri Hanuman Vyayam Prasarak Mandal, Amravati as per the budget announcement.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To prepare a proposal to be submitted to the Cabinet for granting the status of Deemed Sports University to the Physical Education College of Hanuman Gymnasium Prasarak Mandal, Amravati.
(ii) To prepare a proposal for the creation of new posts in accordance with the rules of the deemed university after reviewing the existing grant-in-aid and other posts.
3) To prepare a proposal for taking decisions regarding grants and ancillary matters after the termination of the affiliation of Amravati University as the said University will come under the purview of the Sports Department.
4) To study and report on the financial burden of recurring and non-recurring expenditure on the upgradation of infrastructure, creation of new facilities and salaries for posts and non-salary grants.
(5) Any other matters which may arise in connection with the proposals to be prepared as aforesaid.
(3) The term of office of the said Committee. Necessary action will be taken within 1 month.
