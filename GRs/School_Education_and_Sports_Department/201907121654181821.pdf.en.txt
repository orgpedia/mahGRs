Introduction: Yashoda Shikshana Prasarak Mandal, Solapur runs Sojar English School, Kurduwadi, Ta. Madha, G. Solapur, English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary and Upper Primary School, Group No. 240/01, Akulgaon, Th. The proposal received under the reference letter regarding relocation to Madha, Dist. Solapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the above schools are filling up in the land owned by the Railways, the Railways has proposed to relocate the school as it is struggling to vacate the land. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure in writing the consent of the parents of the students in the school to the relocation, as well as the adjustment of all the students in the school to the place of relocation.
(3) The migration shall be agreed upon by all the teachers/ non-teaching staff, and all the qualified teachers and non-teaching staff shall be accommodated in the migrated place and their service conditions shall not be changed. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted. It should be ensured that the school is filled in the space meant for educational use, the school should be kept functioning in the ground floor space for the safety of the students.
