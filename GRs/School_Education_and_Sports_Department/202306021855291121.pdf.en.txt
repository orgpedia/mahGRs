Preamble: Shri. Ramgiri School near Barshi G. Solapur. 1 (Amba). Barshi G. Solapur, a Marathi medium aided secondary school (5th to 10th standard), Tadwale (M. ). The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate Madha to Solapur district was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school is dangerous, it is proposed to relocate the school. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason. All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(3) School, teachers and non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grant, students in this regard.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
