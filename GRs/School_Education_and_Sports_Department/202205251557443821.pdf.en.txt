As per the scheme, Khelo India Youth Games are being organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India from 2018-19 to tap the talent pool in various sports disciplines from across the country.
The 4th Khelo India Youth National Games will be held in Haryana (Panchkula) from 4th June 2022 to 13th June 2022. Contest is open to children under the age of 18. In order to ensure that Maharashtra remains at the top of the country's medal tally in the national championship, district, zonal and state level selection trials will be conducted in the first phase to select teams for the team sports of kabaddi, kho-kho and basketball selected for the state.
The team selection has been done. The selection of the athletes in the individual sports disciplines will be done by the National Federation of the respective sport. A total of 420 players will be selected for the tournament. A team of 130 officials including mentors, assistant mentors, office bearers, managers and assistant managers will accompany him to the venue. The second phase will include a pre-competition training camp and a physical national competition. A 13-day training camp for the players selected in the state team through selection trials is being held at Shri Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune from 18 th May, 2022.
