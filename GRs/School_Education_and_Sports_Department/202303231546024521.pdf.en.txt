Preamble: All India Civil Services Athletics Championships 2022-23 will be held at Shree Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune from 26th to 28th March, 2023. The Central Civil Services Cultural and Sports Board has approved the conduct of the Athletics meet with effect from 20.02.2023. Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services The proposal has been submitted vide letter dated 09.03.2023. Accordingly, Shri Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune has been constructed. The approval for the event to be held from March 26 to 28, 2023 was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: All India Civil Services Athletics Championships 2022-23 to be held at Shri Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune from 26th to 28th March, 2023 and expected to cost Rs. 26,02,000/- (Rupees Twenty Six Lakhs Two Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
2. Subject to the said expenditure, the Secretariat Gymkhana shall submit a report of the competition, subject-wise details of actual expenditure incurred, utilization certificate, compliance of all the conditions of the sanction order, etc. The approval of the Government is being accorded for subject-wise expenditure as per Annexure A on the conditions of meeting the accounting objections raised by the Office of the Accountant General, Mumbai.
