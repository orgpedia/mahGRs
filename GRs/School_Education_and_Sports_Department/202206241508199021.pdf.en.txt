Government's decision
In pursuance of the amendment made in the Financial Officers Rules Manual 1978, Part-I, Sub-Divisions I to V dated 17.04.2015 to the Heads of Offices at Zonal level under the Department of School Education and Sports, it was contemplated to declare the officers as Heads of Department, Heads of Regional Offices and Heads of Offices for financial matters. Accordingly, the Government is now declaring Heads of Departments, Heads of Regional Offices and Heads of Offices as follows:
