The Proposal- M. As per the directions of the Minister (School Education& Sports), a meeting was held on 2 nd March, 2019 with the officials of the Sports Department, Government of Maharashtra and Sports Authority of India regarding the sports facilities at Kandivali (East), which are being taken over and developed by Sports Authority of India. A review of the sports facilities set up by the Sports Authority of India and the future facilities proposed by the Sports Department of the Government of Maharashtra was also taken. The proposed facilities and the facilities already created by the Sports Authority of India and the Sports Department, Government of Maharashtra, need to be used in a coordinated manner.
For this, a coordination committee was to be set up to coordinate the facilities being built and used by the Sports Authority of India and the Government of Maharashtra in Kandivali (East).
Government's decision
The following Coordination Committee is being constituted to coordinate the development of Sports Facilities by Sports Authority of India at the site at Kandivali (East) and the proposed facilities being developed by Sports Authority of India and Government of Maharashtra.

The completion of the proposed facilities in the said complex should be planned with the funds received from the scheme and sponsors.
The Sports Authority of India and the Sports Department, Government of Maharashtra shall submit all proposals regarding the development of facilities at the campus in Kandivali (East) for the approval of the said Committee.
The Committee shall ensure that the facilities already created or proposed in the premises of the said complex are not repeated.
The Committee shall provide necessary suggestions and guidance to the Sports Authority of India and the Department of Sports regarding the future proposed facilities.
The Committee shall meet once in three months.
