Preamble: To provide 25% of the grant-in-aid to the Rashtriya Chhatra Sena offices in Maharashtra for conducting camps and trainings, the budget outlay for implementation of the above activities during 2018-19 is Rs. 11.00 crore within the limit of 70% of the provision of Rs. 7.70 crore. 3,43,890/- was under consideration.
Government's decision:
The budget outlay for implementation of the above activities during 2018-19 has been increased from Rs. 11.00 crore within the limit of 70% of the provision of Rs. 7.70 crore. 3,43,890/-for 7 Maharashtra Battalion, Rashtriya Chhatra Sena, Nashik Unit vide Reference No. It is hereby approved to disburse by Government Circular No. 1 and to place the said amount under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
The expenditure in respect of the above should be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No: E-3, 2204-Sports and Youth Services 102, Youth Welfare Programme for Students (01) (01) Rashtriya Chhatrasena Yojana (2204 0016-21)." The grant should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned.
