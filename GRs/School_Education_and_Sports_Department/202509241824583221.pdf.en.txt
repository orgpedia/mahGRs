2. The following is the sanction for handling the financial affairs of the Zonal Sports Complex Committee, District Sports Complex Committee and Taluka Sports Complex Committee: -
(1) The Zonal Sports Complex Committee: - The Chairman of the Committee is the Zonal Commissioner and the Member Secretary.
It is being approved to handle the financial transactions with the joint signature of the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, as well as with the approval of the Committee.
However, Latur district falls under Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar revenue division and Kolhapur district.
Since Pune falls under the revenue division, the Zonal Sports Complexes, Latur and Kolhapur, are the campuses.
The financial affairs of the Committee shall be governed by the approval of the said Complex Committee, namely, the Treasurer and the
District Collectors Latur and Kolhapur and Member Secretary and concerned Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Affairs.
It is being approved to handle financial transactions with the joint signature of the Youth Services, as well as with the approval of the committee.
(2) District Sports Complex Committee: - Chairman and Collector and Member Secretary and
Financial transactions with the joint signature of the District Sports Officer, as well as with the approval of the Committee.
The handling is being approved by this.
(3) Taluka Sports Complex Committee: - Provincial Commissioner and Member Secretary and Taluka Sports Committee.
It is allowing financial transactions to be handled with the joint signature of the officer, as well as with the approval of the committee.
