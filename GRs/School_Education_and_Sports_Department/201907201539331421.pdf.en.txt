Government Decision: A committee under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur has been constituted under the decision of the Government in the context to make recommendations regarding the provision of facilities in the premises of Chandrapur Sainik School to other institutions and general public and the appropriation of revenue accordingly.
