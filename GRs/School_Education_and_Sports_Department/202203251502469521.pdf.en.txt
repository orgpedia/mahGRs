Preamble- The Department of School Education, Government of India has decided to include the defined contribution pension scheme applicable to teachers and non-teaching staff in 100% aided posts in private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools recognized by the Zilla Parishads in the National Pension Scheme of the Central Government. The government has taken a decision. The amount collected under the defined contribution pension scheme of teachers/ non-teaching staff of Zilla Parishad and recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools will be transferred under the National Pension Scheme. 2 has been approved. The government had earmarked Rs. 2021-22 crore for the purpose.
