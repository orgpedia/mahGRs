Introduction: For the most effective use of DIKSHA App in the state and for the creation of quality mathematics e-material for the students of class 1 to 10 of Marathi medium in the state, Reference No. The proposal was submitted by the Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Pune. Reference No. 2 has been approved. Mathematics is a very important subject in school. Various efforts are being made at the state level to increase the acquisition of mathematics by the students of the state. It highlights the importance of e-literature on DIKSHA App. The content on this app is delivered to the students through the QR code in the textbook. The e-content on the DIKSHA App is created with the help of tech-friendly teachers of the state to make the e-content updated, quality and qualitative. In this process, the cooperation of various non-governmental organizations specializing in this subject is also taken. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of e-content for mathematics for students in the state. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: The State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra State, Pune is producing e-content for students from class I to X based on the State Curriculum. The e-Sahitya Deeksha App, the Q.R.Code in the textbook will be made available through the website and YouTube channel of the conference. An amount of Rs. 16.50 crore has been sanctioned. Administrative approval is also being given for the purchase of necessary materials, video production, translation experts and other incidental expenses for the production of e-material. Approval is subject to the following conditions.
1) The purchase of e-material should be done in the prescribed manner from the GeM portal.
2) The funds provided should be used for the same purpose.
