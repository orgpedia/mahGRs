Proposal by the Central Government. The draft of the new National Education Policy was released on June 17, 2019. The draft of the new education policy is being prepared at the central government level and it is likely to be finalized this year. A meeting was held at Sahyadri Guest House under the chairmanship of the Minister (School Education). At the time of the meeting, a study group of experts from the education sector, representatives of NGOs working in the education sector, and scholars and officials were appointed at the level of Commissioner (Education). The work of preparing the report of this study group is going on at various levels. A State-level coordination committee had been formed for the purpose.
Government's decision:
It is being approved to constitute a Coordination Committee as follows to coordinate with the Study Groups appointed to conduct studies in line with the recommendations of the new National Education Policy.
