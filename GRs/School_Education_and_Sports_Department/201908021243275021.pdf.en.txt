The Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides (MSBSGM) has decided to change the designation of its six cadres- District Organising Commissioner, District Organiser, Assistant Organising Commissioner, District Scout Organiser, District Guide Organiser and District Scout Guide Organiser- to District Organiser (Scout/ Guide).

The Proposal:
Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai, to provide 100% subsidy to meet the expenditure on salaries of all employees on the establishment of the organization, the matter of changing the designation of 6 cadres of District Organization Commissioner, District Organizer, Assistant Organization Commissioner, District Scout Organizer, District Guide Organizer and District Scout Guide Organizer to a single designation.
Government's decision:
A total of 74 posts have been sanctioned on the establishment of Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai in 6 cadres- District Organization Commissioner, District Organizer, Assistant Organization Commissioner, District Scout Organizer, District Guide Organizer and District Scout/ Guide Organizer. The salary band is Rs. 5200 20200 and Grade Pay Rs. 2800/- is admissible. Also, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in this cadre are also the same. They are required to visit primary and secondary schools throughout the district, guide the teachers on scouts and guides, prepare at the district level for the district/ state/ national and international level events, and meet the objectives set by the state and national office. In order to bring uniformity in the designation, the Government is approving to change the designation of 6 cadres namely (i) District Organizing Commissioner, (ii) District Organizing, (iii) Assistant Organizing Commissioner, (iv) District Scout Organizing, (v) District Guide Organizing and (vi) District Scout/ Guide Organizing to the new designation of" District Organizing (Scout/ Guide)." The Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai shall pass necessary resolution in the State Executive Committee and communicate the same to this Department.
