The Proposal: -
Gujarati Education Institute Latur-run Shri Gujarati English School, Lokhand Gali Latur
Self-financed English medium secondary school (e.g. 1st to 10th) Sikanderpur (Katpur Road), Latur
A proposal received under the memorandum to approve the migration here was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
As the above school building is dilapidated and lacks physical facilities, Sikanderpur (Katpur Road),
Relocation to Latur is proposed. Hence, the school has been shifted to the above mentioned places.
Acceptance is granted on the following terms and conditions. : -
(1) The condition of minimum marks of a migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students will also be required to be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school operates on the self-financing principle of English medium.
The entire responsibility of the teaching / non-teaching staff will rest with the institution.
(3) In respect of schools, teaching and non-teaching staff, salary / non-salary grants, students, etc., by the Government.
Acts, rules, orders, etc., as may be prescribed from time to time, shall be required to be complied with by the institution.
(4) There shall be a change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
