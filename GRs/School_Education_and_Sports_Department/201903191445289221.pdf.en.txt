The Proposal:
For the financial year 2018-19, 70% of the budgetary allocation for the educational concessions/ scholarship schemes implemented by the School Education Department has been released as per reference No. 1 has been issued by the Government. Department of Finance As 100% of the budgetary provision has been provided for the period of the current financial year by the circular dated 25th January, 2019 for the head 34, Scholarships/ Scholarships and 80% for the head 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary), the proposal for administrative approval for spending the remaining amount of the budgetary provision was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The budgetary allocation for the academic scholarship scheme implemented by the School Education Department has been approved in the financial year 2018-19 as given in the table below.
The government has sanctioned the remaining funds for the project.
