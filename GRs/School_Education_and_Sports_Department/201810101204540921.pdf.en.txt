Introduction: The students with disabilities at the secondary level should be able to study with the general students.
For this purpose, special educators have been appointed under the 100% centrally sponsored Inclusive Education for Disabled (Secondary Level) Scheme launched by the Central Government in 2009. The scheme has been increased to 60 (Central share): 40 (State share) from 2015-16. The State Government has released the salary of 840 (512+328) Special Teachers for the year 2015-16.
Out of 40% Government share, vide Government Resolution dated 08.11.2017, Rs. 1075 million
Funds have been released and the remaining funds were under the consideration of the government.
Also, Ma. The High Court, Bench at Aurangabad in Writ Petition No. Contempt Petition No. 452/2015 369/2016 and other contempt petitions. As per the directions of the High Court dated 05.09.2018 and 25.09.2018, out of the total number of writ petitioners in the said contempt petitions, the Central Government was contemplating to disburse 40% of the State Government share of funds to meet the expenditure on salaries of the Special Educators (petitioners) for the financial years 2016-17 and 2017-18.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government, vide this Order, is sanctioning grant-in-aid to the extent of Rs. 27.09 lakh (Rupees Twenty Seven Lakhs Nine Thousand only) to the 840 (512+328) Special Teachers, who have been declared eligible for salary for the year 2015-16 by the Central Government, against the State Government's share of 40%, to meet the expenditure on their salary for the financial year 2015-16.
Also, 2. The High Court, Bench at Aurangabad in Writ Petition No. Contempt Petition No. 452/2015 369/2016 and other contempt petitions. As per the directions of the High Court dated 05.09.2018 and 25.09.2018, the Central Government is sanctioning a grant of Rs. 185.19 lakh (Rupees One Crore Eighty Five Lakhs and Nineteen Thousand only) to all the writ petitioners in respect of the said contempt petitions, on 40% share of the State Government, to meet the expenditure on the salaries of the Special Educators (petitioners) for the financial year 2016-17 and 2017-18, who have been declared eligible for the salary for the year 2016-17 and 2017-18.
The expenditure shall be met from the provision made available in the financial year 2018-19 under the head Demand No. Accounts Head E-3, 2235 B891, 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
The Under Secretary/ Officiating Officer (Cash Branch), School Education and Sports Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Administration), School Education and Sports Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as the" Controlling Officer" for collection and disbursement of the said funds.
The fund should be withdrawn from the treasury by the Under Secretary/ Officiating Officer (Cash Branch), School Education and Sports Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai and deposited in the bank account of RMSA IEDSS of Maharashtra Primary Education Council Society immediately.
The process of payment of salary to the concerned special teachers should be done by the State Project Director, Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, Charni Road, Mumbai.
