2. Governance Circular of the Finance Department. All the things mentioned in 04/04/2022 are being fulfilled. This grant is a conditional grant and the utilization certificate of the funds released earlier has been submitted. The terms and conditions of this grant have been set out in the said Government Decision.
3. The funds disbursed under the said Government Decision shall be borne by the Central Government. As per the directions given in the order dated 13/12/2022, the Annual Plan and Budget for the year 2022-23 should be spent for recurring/ non-recurring aspects of ongoing schemes of Samagra Shiksha Yojana approved by the Project Approval Board (PAB).
4. It should be ensured that the fund is spent in the current year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure.
The report of the expenditure incurred from the provision made under the head Scheduled Tribes, the certificate of utilization of funds, the material objectives achieved in the head/ sub-heading, etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and School Education Department without fail by the 10th of every month.
6. It should be ensured that the expenditure incurred in the tribal sub-plan area, outside the tribal sub-plan area, is recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
