Government's decision
Considering the number of tribal students in the Sainik School, as per the criteria, the Rashtramata Indira Gandhi Sah Shiksha Sainik School, near the Railway Gate, Beed Bypass Road, Aurangabad, has been selected as the Sainik School.
1 (one) tribal batch of Class XII is being recognized from the academic year 2021-22.
The Military School is being approved to start a Tribal Unit (Science Branch) as per Para 1, subject to the following conditions:
1) The Tribal Development Department will be responsible for the entire expenditure on this tribal unit.
The minimum number of students should be 30.
3) To avoid academic loss to the students, the tribal students in the Sainik Schools where the strength of students for tribal batches is less than 30 should be adjusted in the general batches and the cost of the adjusted tribal students should be borne by the Tribal Development Department.
4) The institution/ school shall ensure that all the tribal students admitted in the Sainik School are able to appear in the Board of Higher Secondary Education Examination (Class 12 Science) and no student is disadvantaged and are eligible for the examination of the National Academy of Sciences.
5) The educational institution will be responsible for providing physical facilities in these military schools.
No separate grant will be given for this.
3. Reference No. The Tribal Contingent in the said Military School has been sanctioned subject to fulfilment of the conditions laid down under the Government Decision No. 1 to 3 as well as the number criteria and considering the availability of physical facilities. The matter should be reported to the concerned education officer (secondary).
4. Recognition of the post of teacher in the said batch will be decided separately.
