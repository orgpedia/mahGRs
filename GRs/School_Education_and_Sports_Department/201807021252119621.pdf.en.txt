Preamble: Pravin Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Gaudgaon (Vijaynagar), Ta. Ravindranath Tagore Primary School, Shrinagar Colony, Udgir, Latur. The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the institution to Udgir, Latur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Thirumalla Multi-purpose Service Institutions of the said Primary and Upper Primary School, Yenaki, Ta. The government has decided to approve the transfer of Udgir, G. Latur on the following terms and conditions. :
(1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management.
(2) The Chairman/ Secretary of the new institution will be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities for the school as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) It shall be ensured that all the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the school agree to the transfer.
Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented for transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) Regarding the objections raised by the teachers of the school in the transfer process, In the writ petition filed in the High Court, Aurangabad Bench, Tirumalla Multipurpose Service Society, Yenaki, Ta. Udgir, President/ Secretary of Latur Institute Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(5) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard, and not to employ more teaching/ non-teaching staff than the permissible posts.
(6) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(7) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) No. 3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(10) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
