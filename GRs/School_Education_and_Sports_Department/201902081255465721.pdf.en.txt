Nanded was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to provide free education to the students of these schools. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to Muched, G. Nanded on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason for the schools where the management has changed.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) It shall be ensured that the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for the transfer of the school. Also, the eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(4) No financial transaction has taken place in the transfer of these schools. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The schools shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff than that.
(7) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in respect of schools where management has changed.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.

Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur shall examine all the aspects and take necessary further action regarding the change of management of the school, including other necessary conditions, and inform the government about the action taken.
