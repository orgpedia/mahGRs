Introduction: As mentioned above, No. Private primary and secondary schools, which have been approved on a non-subsidized and permanent non-subsidized basis under the government decision 1 and 2 and are continuing to receive 20 percent subsidy, have been approved an increased 20% subsidy from November 1, 2020. Also referred to as No. Under the 3rd Government decision, an increased 20% subsidy has been disbursed. Though the teachers and non-teaching staff of these schools have been provided with salary IDs, the facility to pay the outstanding salary through the salary system has not been provided yet. Therefore, since the salaries of such teaching and non-teaching staff for the period November 2020 to February 2021 could not be paid before 31/3/2021 from the salary system, the government was considering to approve the payment of salaries for these four months by March 31, 2021 in the offline mode.
Government Decision: Private primary and secondary schools, which are recognized on non-subsidized and permanent non-subsidized basis and are running with 20% subsidy, which have been approved with increased 20% subsidy from November 1, 2020, are being approved to pay the salaries of teachers and non-teaching staff from November, 2020 to February, 2021 through offline mode.
