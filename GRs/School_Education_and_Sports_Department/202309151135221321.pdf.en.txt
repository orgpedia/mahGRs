Circular Preamble dated 12.04.2023: In order to encourage meritorious students of the State to pursue education, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse the sanctioned funds for the financial year 2023-24 for the purpose of 31-Additional Grants/ Post-Graduation, 34-Scholarship/ Scholarship and 50 other expenses.
Government Decision: To encourage meritorious students in the state, various schemes are being implemented to provide scholarships to the students in the schools of the state. The Government is approving the release of total amount of Rs. 29,17,82, 200/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores Seventeen Lakhs Eighty Two Thousand Two Hundred only) for the schemes mentioned in paragraph 2 out of the approved budgetary allocation for the financial year 2023-24 and make the same available on the Budget Distribution System.
