On the proposal received under Reference No.1 regarding ex-post facto approval of transfer of Urdu medium aided secondary school, Osmanabad to Hind Shikshan Sanstha, Umarga, G. Osmanabad, Reference No. 2 The action is proposed in accordance with the court decision here.
The proposal had been sent to the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the Hindu Educational Institution, Umarga, G. Osmanabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school whose management has been changed shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) The schools shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(3) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.

The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur, shall examine all the matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the schools, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, and inform the government about the action taken.
