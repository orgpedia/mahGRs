Preamble: Tirthankar Education Society, Th. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the English medium permanent unaided primary school, Mahavir State Academy, Sangli, run by Miraj, G. Sangli, to Banyan Tree Swami Education Society, Vishrambaug, Sangli, was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Banyan Tree Swami Education Society, Vishrambaug, Sangli on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Tirthakar Education Society, Th. Miraj, G. Sangli regarding the non-change in the executive of the institute in the year 2006, the chairman/ secretary of the institute paid Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) As the school is a permanent English medium unaided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(8) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
