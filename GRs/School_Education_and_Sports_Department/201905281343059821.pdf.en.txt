Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of Jai Hind Junior College, Tukum, Chandrapur, an English Medium Permanent Unaided Junior College run by the Indian Society for Social Development, Chandrapur, to Shri Balaji Grace Multipurpose Education Society, Chandrapur, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Junior College to Sri Balaji Grace Multipurpose Education Society, Chandrapur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the Junior College where the change of management has taken place for any reason.
(2) The Chairman/ Secretary of the new institution will be paid Rs. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff who have consented to transfer to junior colleges shall be accommodated and their service conditions shall not be changed. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) A certified copy of the constitution of Sri Balaji Grace Multipurpose Education Society, Chandrapur should be taken.
Also, the certified copies of the last 3 years' batch accreditation of the said junior college will be required to be submitted to the office of the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur.
(5) The Junior College shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in the case of a junior college where there is a change of management.
