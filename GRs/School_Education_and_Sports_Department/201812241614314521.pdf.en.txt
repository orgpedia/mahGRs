Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009
There is a provision for every State to set up a State Advisory Committee with a maximum of 15 non-official members and to select experts and experienced persons in the field of education and child development. A notification in this regard has also been issued by the School Education and Sports Department. As per Article 25 of the Constitution of India, dated 11th October, 2011, the Council shall consist of the Minister of School Education and the Minister of State for School Education as Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the State Advisory Council.
