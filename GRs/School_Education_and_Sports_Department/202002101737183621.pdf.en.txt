Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Chess Championship 2019-20 was held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai from 20th to 28th February, 2020. The exam was conducted on 20th and 21st January 2020. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 46,920/- (Rupees Forty Six Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty only).
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of Department/ Head of Office concerned should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 15th to 17th February, 2020 and for the tournament from 20th to 28th February, 2020 with effect from 14th February, 2020 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. Absence from office during 15th to 29th February, 2020 (Training Camp, Competition and Travel) shall be treated as" Duty Period." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned. Also, the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai are covered under Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9, dated 11th May, 1989 and Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1404/ PR.
