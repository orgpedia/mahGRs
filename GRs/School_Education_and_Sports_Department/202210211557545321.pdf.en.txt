Introduction- The Central Government started the Disabled Integrative Education Scheme (Primary Level) from the year 1978 to enable the students with disabilities with special needs to study in normal schools along with the general children. However, the salary of Special Teachers working on Secondary Units appointed under the Scheme for the period March, 2017 to May, 2019 has been paid as per the Government decisions in Reference-1. However, subsequently, the salaries of these special educators for the period from June, 2019 to March, 2022 were overdue due to technical reasons. Accordingly, the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune has released the pending salary of 57 Special Teachers for the period from June, 2019 to March, 2022 and the second installment of 7th Pay Commission amounting to Rs. 13,22,95, 820/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty Five) has been requested to the Government under Reference-4.
On the request made by the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune, a proposal to provide necessary funds for the salary of 57 special teachers under the head (2202 I 612), 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Paying) was submitted for the approval of the Planning and Finance Department. The Planning and Finance Department has sanctioned Rs. The matter of disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 13,22,95, 820/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty) was under consideration of the Government. The government's decision is as follows:
However, 57 Special Teachers working on Secondary Unit who were appointed under the scheme of Disabled Integrated Education Scheme (Primary Level) as demanded by the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune, have been given arrears of salary for the period from June, 2019 to March, 2022 and a total of Rs. 13,22,95, 820/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty Five) is being approved.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune shall deposit the amount of outstanding salary in the account of 57 special teachers concerned. Make sure that the amount of salary is credited to the account of the teacher concerned.
The list of 57 Special Educators is furnished in Annexure-A. A teacher's name appears twice in the list.
