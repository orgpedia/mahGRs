Preamble: The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, is organizing the National Youth Festival to promote holistic development of youth, preserve culture and traditions, promote inherent qualities of youth and promote national integration. The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India vide letter No.4 has entrusted the responsibility of organizing the 27th National Youth Festival 2024 to the State of Maharashtra and the venue of the event has been fixed at Nashik.
The National Youth Festival will be attended by about 8000 participants comprising of youth teams from States and Union Territories, volunteers of National Service Scheme/ Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan, Examiners and other officials and staff. The Prime Minister will. Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune Considering the directions of the letter No. 3 and the letter No. 4 of the Central Government, the Government was considering the formation of a National Youth Festival Organizing Committee and Executive Committee in connection with the organization of this Youth Festival.
Government Resolution: The National Youth Festival is being organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India from 12.to 16th January, 2020 to promote holistic development of youth, preserve culture and traditions, promote inherent qualities of youth and promote national integration.
