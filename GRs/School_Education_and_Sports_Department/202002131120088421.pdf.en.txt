Preamble: Bright Star Education Society, Aurangabad operates Disha Secondary School (earlier name- Late. The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for the shifting of the Marathi medium unaided secondary (classes 5th to 10th) school (Vasant Kale High School), Devlai area, Aurangabad to Jhalta Phata, T.G. Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The said school has been shifted from the dilapidated and dangerous building to a self-sufficient building at the above mentioned place. The transfer is subject to the following conditions: :
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more than that shall be employed.
