The Proposal:
The ratio of Central and State share for Samagra Shiksha (Primary Education) Scheme is 60:40. The Central Government vide order dated 05/08/2020 has released ad-hoc ad-hoc instalment of Rs. 121,87,91, 000/- has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Out of this amount, Rs. 81,25,27, 333/-.
The State Project Director, Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Mumbai has issued Reference No. Under the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to disburse the central share and state share funds. Department of Finance As per email dated 04/09/2020, feedback has been received that the Central share of funds has been credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State Government. Informal Reference No. 433/ Expenditure-5 dated 30/09/2020 of the Department of Finance has approved the release of 25% of the total budgetary allocation. The government was contemplating to release the funds.
The government's decision is as follows:
Government's decision
The Central Government has allotted Rs. 189,77,91, 667/- (Rupees One Hundred and Nineteen Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Ninety One Thousand Six Hundred and Sixty Seven only) to the State Project Director, Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Mumbai for programme wise expenditure as shown in the accompanying return.
