Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Volleyball Tournament 2018-19 was held at Dharavi Sports Complex, Dharavi, Mumbai from 24th to 28th February, 2019. The exam was conducted on 11th February 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The selection test, training camp and participation of the state team as per the prospectus' B' is Rs. 56,950/- (Rupees Ninety Five Thousand Nine Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
4. The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned shall inform the player-coach/ manager to participate in the tournament. Training camp from 20th to 22nd February 2019. The competition will be held from February 24 to 28, 2019. They should be relieved from duty from 18th February, 2019 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. All the players and the manager.
