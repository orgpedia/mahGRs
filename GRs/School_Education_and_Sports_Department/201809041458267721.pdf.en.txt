(3) However, if the officers mentioned in the above table have joined the place of posting as provided under the order dated 18.05.2018, this order shall not be effective.

Those concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the transfer in view of the instructions given in it, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
