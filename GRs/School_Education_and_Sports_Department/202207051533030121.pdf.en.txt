Solapur Marathi Medium Secondary School (5th to 7th unaided, 8th to 10th partially aided), Mauje Anandnagar, Manegaon, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter for relocation to Madha, Dist. Solapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school is dilapidated and dangerous, the institution has been shifted to its own building at Mauje Anandnagar, Manegaon, Ta. The school is proposed to be shifted to Madha, Solapur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(3) The entire liability of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the unaided units of the said Marathi medium school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(5) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
