Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme" Rajiv Gandhi Khel Abhiyan" was implemented vide Government of India letter dated 16th July, 2014. Under this programme, the State Government has planned to organize sports competitions for Taluk, District, State, National, Rural, Women and Naxal affected areas in the year 2014-15. The Scheme" Rajiv Gandhi Khel Abhiyan" has been re-designed and implemented by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India vide its circular dated 26/05/2016, by merging three separate schemes namely, 1. Rajiv Gandhi Khel Abhiyan, 2. Urban Sports Infrastructure Scheme and 3. National Sports Talent Search, which were earlier being implemented by the Central Government, into a Centrally Sponsored Scheme" National Programme for Development of Sports in Khelo India" with effect from 16/10/2017.

The expenditure on various aspects related to the conduct of the Games shall be borne by the Organising Committee for the Khelo India Youth Games, 2019 from the funds made available by the Government of Maharashtra, Sports Authority of India or the Central Government by adopting various procedures and rules.
All the authority in this regard is being provided to the organizing committee.
The Organizing Committee shall have the power to call for tenders, finalize, sign contracts, issue work orders, inspect, impose penalties, make payments, deduct or deny payments, audit expenses, make final decisions on whether to spend or not, and enter into agreements, etc. with respect to matters incidental to the conduct of various competitions.
Operation of Bank Account:- The grants received from the Central and State Governments for the Khelo India Youth Games as well as the financial assistance received from the sponsors should be deposited in the account of Shivchhatrapati Kridapeeth. The amount will be spent as per the approval of the organising committee.
However, the expenditure incurred on the Khelo India Youth Games should be kept separate.
Audit of the Khelo India Youth Games, 2019 after completion of the event.
The responsibility of the organizing committee should be done by a private chartered accountant
will remain.
The nominated officers of the Central Government and Sports Authority of India shall be required to reside in Pune. The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services, Pune shall requisition the services of such officers/ staff under him as may be required by him.
The tenure of the above Committees shall be for a period of 6 months.
