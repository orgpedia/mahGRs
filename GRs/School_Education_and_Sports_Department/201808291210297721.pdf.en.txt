Circular:
The National Teacher Awards are given annually by the Government of India to teachers of primary and secondary schools in the country. This year's National Teacher Award for 2017-18 is being given by the Government of India. As on June 1, 2018, six nominations for the National Teacher Award were notified online to the Central Government.
Vikram Sonba Adsul, Assistant Teacher, Zilla Parishad Primary School, Bandagarvasti, Tt. Karjat, G. Ahmednagar, has been selected by the Central Government in the above context. The letter was dated August 24, 2018. (Attached is the link. The Central Government is implementing a scheme to provide 50% concession in second class fare to teachers who have been awarded National Teachers Award in recognition of their meritorious work in the field of education. The concerned teachers will be issued with a photo identity card in the form of a durable form, signed by the Deputy Director of Education. This scheme is not applicable to teachers who have received the State Teacher Award.
