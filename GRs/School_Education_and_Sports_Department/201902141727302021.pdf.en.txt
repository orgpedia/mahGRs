4) Payment of the arrears of wage subsidy under Rule 4, Rule 39 (b), Note 4, Rule 5, Rule 39, (b), Note 5 and Rule 6, Rule 40 shall be made with the approval of the competent authority.

(8) Subsidies for the Zilla Parishads shall not be disbursed without their grants being determined.
9) The approval of the Finance Department is required in respect of the disbursement of funds for the schemes provided under the new head in accordance with the new expenditure.
10) Before sanctioning the grant-in-aid to the various Government/ Administrative Institutions as well as the aided institutions, the Department should obtain the utilization certificates of the expenditure of the grant-in-aid disbursed earlier. Further, no further subsidy shall be released until 75% of the subsidy given 3 months ago is utilized.
