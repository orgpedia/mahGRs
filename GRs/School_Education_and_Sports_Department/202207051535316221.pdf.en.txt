Sant Dnyaneshwar English Medium School, Kopargaon, District Ahmednagar is an English medium self-financed primary school (1st to 8th standard), Matoshrinagar, Jeur Patoda, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Kopargaon, G. Ahmednagar was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Due to the inadequate physical facilities available in the existing premises of the above school, the land owned by the founders of the said school is situated at Matoshrinagar, Jeur Patoda, Tt. Relocation to Kopargaon, Ahmednagar has been proposed. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is a self-financing English medium school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
