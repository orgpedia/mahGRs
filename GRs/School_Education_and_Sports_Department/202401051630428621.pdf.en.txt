Preamble: In the year 1948-49, the National Cadet Corps has been constituted as a GAA for secondary, higher secondary and college students in educational institutions and the National Cadet Corps Act, 1948 has been enacted to provide and structure the National Cadet Corps. Various activities such as training, camps are conducted for the students of the National Cadet Corps. It is located at Rajpath, New Delhi. The government was considering distributing funds for the felicitation ceremony held to boost the morale and morale of the Rashtriya Chhatra Sena students from Maharashtra who took part in the parade on Republic Day and the Prime Minister's rally on 26.01.2024.
Government Decision: D. The felicitation ceremony held to boost the morale and morale of the National Cadet Corps students from Maharashtra who participated in the parade at Rajpath, New Delhi on Republic Day on 26.01.2024, was held at a total cost of Rs. 37.40 Attention (Rs.
