Introduction: Shri Hanuman Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad runs Mahatma Phule Primary School, New Hanuman Nagar, Aurangabad. Shiur Bungalow, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation to Vaijapur, G. Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As the above school does not have adequate physical facilities at the original location, M.P.Shiur Bungalow, Tt. Relocation to Vaijapur, Aurangabad has been proposed. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the said Secondary School is a Marathi medium non-aided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) If any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The government will have the right to cancel the migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
