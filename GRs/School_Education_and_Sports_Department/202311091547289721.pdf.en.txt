Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure and eligibility criteria of the institution in this regard are mentioned in the above Reference No. The Government's decision herein has been dated 01.03.2014. Accordingly, eligible institutions are provided financial assistance in the ratio of 75:25 (Government share 75% and eligible institution share 25%) for construction of sports facilities. Reference No. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraphs 4 (a) and (b) of the Government Decision herein. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune has submitted the proposals of eligible institutions to the Government under Reference No. 3 along with recommendations for grant of administrative approval for financial assistance for construction of sports facilities. Following the recommendations, the government took the decision. Subject to the terms and conditions as on 1.3.2014, the proposal for grant of financial assistance for construction of sports facilities was under consideration of the Government for grant of in-principle administrative approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In principle, administrative approval is being given to the proposal for providing financial assistance to the following institutions as per the State Government approved grant limit (75%) as per Pillar-6 for construction of sports facilities mentioned in Pillar-3, subject to fulfilling the eligibility for grant in the following Annexure-A, in compliance with all the terms and conditions of grant approval dated 01.03.2014, examining the project and budgetary cost and considering the expenditure limit prescribed subject to these and other relevant conditions.
