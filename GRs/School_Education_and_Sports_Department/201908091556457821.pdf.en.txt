Introduction: Sane Guruji Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Nashik operated by Sou. Indumati Bhalchandra Joshi Primary Vidyamandir, Chehendi Bu. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Marathi medium primary and upper primary school, T. G. Nashik, to Pravin Joshi Social and Educational Multi-purpose Development Institute, Nashik, was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said School to Pravin Joshi Institute of Social and Educational Multipurpose Development, Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) In case of consent of all the members of the Executive Board of Sane Guruji Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Nashik and no financial transaction has taken place in the transfer, Rs. A notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- shall be required to be submitted along with the signatures of all the members.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nashik will be required to ensure that the information of the said school is finalized by filling it on a simple online system.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts shall remain in operation and not more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.

It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy regarding filling up of vacancies from the permissible posts by additional staff.
(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.

Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (1), (2) and (3), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nashik Division, Nashik shall examine all the matters and take further action within 6 weeks regarding change of management of the school by including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
