Terms and Conditions:
Since the said 03 Higher Secondary Schools have been approved to start classes on self-financing basis, no financial assistance/ grant, either direct or indirect, shall be provided by the State Government for any expenditure incurred in respect of the said schools.
It shall be mandatory to comply with the prevailing policy and norms of the Government regarding secondary/ higher secondary schools and to provide necessary classrooms, all physical and academic facilities in the school.
It shall be mandatory to comply with all the standards, terms and conditions prescribed under the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012 and the directions given by the Government under this Act or the standards or terms and conditions specified by the Government from time to time.
