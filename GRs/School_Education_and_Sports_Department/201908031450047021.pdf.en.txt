GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Kisan Gyanodaya Mandal, Gudhe, G.Jalgaon on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Government has decided to transfer the said school to Kisan Gyanodaya Mandal, G.Jalgaon, on the following terms and conditions. Bapuso Pun K. Patil Multi-purpose Educational Institution, Gudhe, Ta. It will be necessary to ensure that the Chairman and members of the Appendix-1 of the institution of Bhadgaon, G. Jalgaon have consent to the transfer of the school and there is no objection to the transfer of the teacher/ non-teaching staff of the school.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(3) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(4) As the school is self-financing English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(5) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(7) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.

(8) As the said school is a self-financing school, as per the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012, the Kisan Gyanodaya Mandal, Gudhe, Jalgaon will be required to purchase the land/ land for the said school or take it on a registered lease of at least 30 years.
