The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 16th October, 2015, an Executive Committee under the Chairmanship of Joint Director (Sports) was constituted to prepare and implement an action plan to ensure maximum participation of players from Maharashtra in the 2020 Olympic Games to be held in Tokyo, Japan and to ensure that Maharashtra gets at least 20 medals. The committee decided to prepare an action plan for the selection of potential talent in 16 selected sports of Maharashtra for the 2020 Olympics by determining the eligibility criteria and finalizing the selection of the athletes. A total of 175 players were selected.
After reviewing the performance of the prospective players in it, the selected players will be given financial assistance for upgrading the sports facilities. (2) Here. As per the Government decision dated 17.11.2018, a total of Rs. 189.00 lakhs has been disbursed. Also, since the Tokyo Olympics include the Para Games, the athletes with disabilities selected for the same as well as the general athletes, will be provided sports facilities to upgrade the reference no. (6) The Government has decided to provide Rs. 33.00 lakh has been disbursed. The matter of sanctioning financial assistance to the remaining general and disabled athletes who have qualified for the Tokyo Olympic Games was under the consideration of the government.
