Introduction: Iqra Educational Institute, Jalgaon, Jalgaon operating Iqra Urdu Secondary School, Pratapnagar, Ward No. 23, G. Jalgaon, Urdu Medium Higher Primary and Secondary School (Classes V to X), Near Ajanta Chowfooly, Behind Laddha Farm House, Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation of 82, Salar Nagar, Jalgaon was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from a rented building with inadequate physical facilities to a building owned by an institution with physical facilities. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. Fee: (1) Rs. 10,000/- is required to be paid.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who were working before the migration have been accommodated at the place of migration and they have no complaints/ objections regarding the migration. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
