The Proposal:
In pursuance of the Government decision dated 18.12.2017, Ms. Vaishali Pankaj Vir (Mrs. Vaishali Dattatraya Jhankar) has been appointed in the direct service of the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A cadre as Education Officer and equivalent for a period of two years.
The proposal to complete the probationary period has been submitted vide letter dated 06.10.2017 from the Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government, which meets the conditions of probationary period, the officers mentioned in the accompanying form working in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A Cadre as Education Officer and similar, under the Government Decision dated 18.12.2017 mentioned in Annexure No. 1 herein, are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in Rakkana No. 7 before their names.
