Preamble: With a view to encourage the teachers who serve the society selflessly and loyally and do excellent work in the field of education in their committed work and to honor their merits, 109 teachers of the state will be awarded with Krantijyoti Savitrimai Rajya Shikshak Gunagaurav Puraskar as per the terms and conditions of the Government decision dated June 28, 2022. The award ceremony is proposed to be held in the first/ second week of October 2022. The proposal for the estimated expenditure for the award ceremony has been submitted to the Government by the Director of Education (Secondary& Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 18 th August, 2022. The matter of administrative approval of the expenses to be incurred for the award ceremony was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule Rajya Shikshak Gunagaurav Puraskar Scheme, for the year 2022-23, out of the provision announced against the following heads, Rs. 1,95,55, 700/- (Rs. One crore ninety five lakh fifty five thousand seven hundred only) administrative approval is being granted.
