Terms and Conditions:. No financial assistance/ grant, direct or indirect, shall be provided by the State Government for any expenditure required to be incurred for upgrading the primary school from secondary to higher secondary level.
It shall be mandatory to comply with the prevailing policy and norms of the Government regarding secondary schools and to provide necessary classrooms, all physical and educational facilities in the school.
It shall be mandatory to comply with all the standards, terms and conditions prescribed under the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012 and the directions issued by the Government under this Act or the standards or terms and conditions specified by the Government from time to time.
