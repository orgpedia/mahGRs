Preamble: The Director has requested for administrative approval and grant for installation of heat pump to supplement the solar system in the guest house building of State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune-30. The guest house has 33 rooms with accommodation for 99 trainees. All the trainees are provided with hot water in their rooms. The solar system was installed on 05/12/2004. The solar system does not provide hot water on rainy days and some days in winter. Also, the solar system was installed in 2004 and in the current situation, there are shortcomings. There have been frequent complaints from trainees about the lack of solar hot water when the guest house is running at full capacity. A few rooms have a separate bathroom. The purchase of geysers is also usually dated to 2014, which is more than five years old. In order to provide all-year-round hot water facility in all the rooms of the guest house, it is necessary to install a heat pump of 5.5 HP capacity as a supplement to the solar water system. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to provide necessary grant amount of Rs. 6,50,000/- (13) towards" Accounting for Office Expenditure" for the year 2022-23 for installation of Heat Pump to supplement the Solar System in the Guest House Building of State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune-30.
Government Decision: State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune-30, has approved installation of 5.5 HP capacity heat pump as a supplement to solar water system to provide round the year hot water facility in all the rooms of the building.
