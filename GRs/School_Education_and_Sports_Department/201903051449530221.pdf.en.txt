Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Wrestling Championship 2018-19 was held at Chandigarh from 28th to 30th January, 2019. The exam was conducted on January 5, 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Rs. 37,400/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Thousand Four Hundred only).
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated 11th May, 1989 Curve No.LVE/ 1492/46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be paid Rs. Absence from office during the period from January 23 to February 1, 2019 (training camp, competition and travel) shall be treated as" period of duty." The expenditure on travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned.
