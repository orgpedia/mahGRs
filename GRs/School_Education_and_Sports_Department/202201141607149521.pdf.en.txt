In order to produce international level sportspersons from the state of Maharashtra and to provide educational facilities to the sportspersons at their place of practice, the Maharashtra Board was permitted vide Government Decision dated 27.03.1997 to start and run a school for residential sportspersons at Shri Shivchhatrapati Sports University, Balewadi, Pune on 100% grant basis only.
