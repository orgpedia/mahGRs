The state government has declared a holiday for primary, middle and high schools.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Circular No: SANKIND-2023/ PR No.105/ SD-4
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: April 20, 2023.
Thiruvananthapuram: The temperature has been rising across the state steadily in the past few days. Therefore, as a preventive measure, the matter of declaring a holiday for students and issuing instructions regarding the opening of schools was under the consideration of the government.
2.In this regard, the following recommendations are made:
The students of all the schools of the state board in the state are being declared a holiday from 21.04.2023.
2) If the schools of other boards of the state are running according to the schedule or if important educational activities are being carried out in such schools, the school administration should take an appropriate decision at their level to declare a holiday for the students.
In order to maintain uniformity in the opening of schools in the state, schools in all divisions except Vidarbha shall open on 15th June every year and on the next day if that day is a holiday.
