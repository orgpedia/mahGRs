23. Considering the prayer of the Petitioners as well as the Management, that such grants should be made available by virtue of the GR dated 19th September, 2016, that this Writ Petition is allowed. The payment of grants to be extended to the sanctioned posts as per the staffing pattern, shall commence by virtue of the GR dated 19th September, 2016, beginning with 20% grants per year. It is undisputed that the Government has introduced resolutions which mandates an increase of 20% grants in each academic year, for illustration, 20% in the first year, 40% in the

made by the Competent Authority of the State Government considering the staffing pattern
