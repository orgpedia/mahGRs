Preamble: As per Rule 34 of the Memorandum of Association of the Maharashtra Primary Education Council, there is a provision to hold the meeting of the Executive Committee at least once in 3 months, as per the requirement. Samagra Shiksha is a programme launched by the Government of India in 2018-19, which combines three schemes- Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan, Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan and Teacher Education. The scheme is being implemented by the Maharashtra Primary Education Council.
The Executive Committee is the highest decision-making body for planning, implementation, control and monitoring of the scheme. Due to various reasons, the meeting of the executive committee could not be held within the stipulated time. Accordingly, a sub-committee of the Executive Committee under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, School Education and Sports Department and Vice-Chairman (Executive Committee) has been constituted. The meeting was chaired by the Chief Secretary. This matter. Approval for constitution of Sub-Committee under Issue No. 933 in the 65th meeting held on 01/12/2018 and under Issue No. 956 in the 68th meeting of the Executive Committee held on 19/08/2019. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a sub-committee of the executive committee. The government's decision is as follows:
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Government is approving the formation of the following Executive Committee Sub-Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, School Education and Sports Department and Vice-President (Executive Committee) for the implementation of Samagra Shiksha Scheme.

The meeting of the sub-committee of the Executive Committee shall be held with the approval of the Chairman of the sub-committee and the decisions taken in the meeting of the sub-committee shall be approved by the Executive Committee at the next meeting. However, important policy and similar decisions should be submitted to the Executive Committee for approval.
Functions of the Sub-Committee: (i) To plan the implementation of the Samagra Shiksha Programme.
(b) To decide on the proposal before the Committee for implementation of the Programme.
3) To guide the State Project Director for implementation as per the decision of the Executive Committee.
4) Monitor the implementation of the program.
(5) To approve the decisions taken by the sub-committee at the next meeting of the Executive Committee.
Where the Additional Chief Secretary and the State Project Director are the same person, the State Project Coordinator or Joint Director (Administration) shall act as the Member Secretary of the Committee.
