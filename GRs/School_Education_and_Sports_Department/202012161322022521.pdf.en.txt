Proposal- Reference No. 01 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.10.2013," Rajiv Gandhi Vidyarthi Accident Compensation Scheme" is implemented on regular basis to provide compensation to the children studying in class 1 to 12 for the loss due to accident. Under this government decision, the permissible ex-gratia grant in case of an accident of a student and the necessary documents for this have been mentioned and a committee has been formed at the district level under the chairmanship of the Collector to resolve the proposal.
In the financial year 2020-21, Rs. 360.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government has sanctioned Rs. 1200.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 360.00 lakhs is being approved for disbursement.
