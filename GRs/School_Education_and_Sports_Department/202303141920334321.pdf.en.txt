Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is as under. (1) The Government decision herein has been made effective as of March 1, 2014. 75:25 for the construction of sports facilities to educational institutions, local/ civic bodies, schools of local self-government institutions, junior colleges, ashramshalas of the Tribal Development Department, divisional/ district/ taluka sports complexes of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act 1950 or the Societies Registration Act 1860. It was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds for providing financial assistance during 2022-23 for providing next tranche of grants to the institutions which have been granted administrative approval as well as those which have been granted partial grants (Phase I/ II) earlier.
Government's decision:
Financial assistance for construction of sports facilities for the financial year 2022-23 out of the budget provision of Rs. 179.21 lakhs (Rupees One Crore, Nineteen Lakhs, Twenty One Thousand only) is hereby approved to be released to the respective institutions for grant as shown in the accompanying return. Acceptance is subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2014 in the matter No. 4 (a) to take action for disbursement of grants to the concerned institutions in accordance with all the terms and conditions of sanction of grants for sports facilities.
(2) It shall be mandatory to submit the certificate of completion of the project along with the photographs of the project and the utilization certificate within a maximum period of 1 year after the disbursement of the grant as indicated in Annexure-A with the concerned institution, otherwise the distributed grant will be recovered from the date of disbursement of the grant at the prevailing penal interest rate of the Government.
3) It will be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate by paying the government subsidy and self-contribution.
2. Subject to the above conditions, out of the provision budgeted for the Financial Assistance Scheme for Construction of Sports Facilities for the financial year 2022-23, within the revised estimate limit, Rs. 179.21 lakh (Rupees One Crore, Nineteen Lakhs, Twenty One Thousand only).
