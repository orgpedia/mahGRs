The best sportspersons, sports activists and sportspersons/ individuals who have worked tirelessly and with a spirit of service for the progress of the sports sector in Maharashtra are awarded every year with Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Pride Award, Best Sports Guide Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Adventure Sports Award, Eklavya State Sports Award (Disabled Sportsperson) and Best Sports Organizer/ Activist and Jijamata Award. The revised rules for these awards are as follows: This has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 16.10.2017. In this decision, there is a provision to form a selection committee for the selection of Shivchhatrapati awardees. Accordingly, the proposal to constitute the Award Selection Committee for the year 2017-18 was under consideration of the Government and the Government has now decided as follows:
Government Decision: Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Time Honour Award, Best Sports Guide Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Adventure Sports Award, Eklavya State Sports Award (Disabled Sportsperson) and Best Sports Organizer/ Activist and Jijamata Award for giving Reference No. The selection committee has been formed as per the decision of the government. The non-official members of the selection committee shall be appointed for a period of three years. The above members will be entitled to meeting allowance as per Government Decision, Finance Division dated 24.05.2012 and travelling allowance and daily allowance as per the provisions of Para 12 of Government Decision, Finance Division dated 03.03.2010. The expenses should be met from the funds collected from Shivchhatrapati Kridapeeth, Balewadi, Pune.
The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune and the Member Secretary, Award Selection Committee shall take necessary further action regarding the convening of the meeting of the Committee immediately.
