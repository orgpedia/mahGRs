The Proposal:
A preliminary structural audit of all the schools of the local self-government institutions in the state has been done by the Samagra Shiksha system. Rajmata Jijau Educational Development Quality Mission is proposed to be implemented for the reconstruction of classrooms and repair of schools in the local body schools of the state and under this campaign, it is proposed to repair/ new construction (reconstruction) of schools of the Nizam-era local bodies in the districts of Aurangabad division in the first phase. There are a large number of government schools in Marathwada. The Maharashtra Primary Education Council has submitted a proposal to the government to rebuild and repair classrooms of Nizam-era government schools in the Marathwada region on priority as there is an urgent need to rebuild and repair classrooms in schools in the Marathwada region. Accordingly, a proposal is under the consideration of the government to give administrative approval for the reconstruction and repair of classrooms of Nizam-era government schools in the Marathwada region in the first phase under the Rajmata Jijau Educational Quality Development Mission.
Government's decision:
The Rajmata Jijau Educational Quality Development Mission is being implemented to rebuild and repair the classrooms in the schools of the local self-government institutions in the state and in the first phase, 1623 out of 718 schools in the Marathwada region have been demolished.
