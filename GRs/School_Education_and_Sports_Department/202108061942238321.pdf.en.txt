The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 1st July, 2006.
Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and their performance of official duties.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared under Section 6 of the Prevention of Delay Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority transferring under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Education Service Group-A and Group-B have been transferred.
The decision has been taken by the government decision dated May 30, 2015.
(2) As per the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the proposal for transfer of transferable officers who have completed three years in the post of posting as on 31.05.2021 has been approved by the Civil Services Board No. 1 was presented. the Civil Service Board
The Counsel for the General Administration Department in the Government Resolution dated 09.04.2018 has made recommendations regarding the transfer of Group-B officers after taking into consideration the guidelines regarding transfer.
