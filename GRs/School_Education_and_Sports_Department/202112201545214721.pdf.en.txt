The Proposal:
Section 12 (1) (c) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 provides for reservation of 25% seats at the entry level in private unaided schools for children belonging to disadvantaged and weaker sections. Further, as per Section 12 (2) of the Act, there is a provision for reimbursement of fees for elementary education of 25% admitted children. With reference to No. The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a request for funds to the government. A sum of Rs 200 crore has been allocated in the budget for 2021-22 for this purpose. Reference No. 4 The Government has decided to provide Rs. Rs 100 crore has been allocated for this. The Finance Department has now approved the release of 75 per cent of the funds in the budget. Accordingly, Rs. The government had earmarked Rs 50 crore for the purpose. The government's decision is as follows:
Government Decision: As per Section 12 (1) (c) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, a sum of Rs. 50 crore (Rupees Fifty Crores only) to the Director of Education (Primary), State of Maharashtra, Pune.
