The government's decision:
Bharat Ratna Shri Rajiv Gandhi District Sports Complex, Dharavi, Mumbai for selection of Maharashtra Government team for All India Civil Services Kabaddi Tournament 2019-20 to be held at Shimla, Himachal Pradesh from 15th to 19th November, 2019. The exam was conducted on October 31, 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Participation in the State Team for the said Tournament as per Form B (Parush Sangha) Rs. 88,940/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty only) and as per return" B-1" (Women Team) Rs. 1,05,740/- (Rupees One Lakh Five Thousand Seven Hundred and Forty only) aggregating to Rs. 1,94,680 (Rupees One Lakh Ninety Four Thousand Six Hundred Eighty Only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.

The absence from the office of the officer/ staff appearing for the selection test of the tournament shall be deemed to be for the purpose of duty under the Government Decision, Finance Division No. 1492/ 46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992 and the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai shall be as per the Government Decision No. LVE-1485/ PR. No. 833/ A/ Service-9, dated 11th May, 1989 and the Government Decision No. LVE-1404/ PR. No.8/ A/ Service-8, dated 27th February, 2004 be passed as a special case. Also, if the amount is left, it should be returned to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai immediately.
