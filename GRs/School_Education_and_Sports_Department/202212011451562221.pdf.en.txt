Introduction In the State of Maharashtra, 4860 Central Primary Schools have been established to ensure micro planning, continuous attendance and quality education for all in all primary schools of Zilla Parishads.

The decision to set up one post of Head of Centre for 10 Zilla Parishad Primary Schools was taken in the said Reference No. The decision was taken by the government. Accordingly, a total of 4860 posts of Head of Centres have been created. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2010, the ratio of recruitment to the post of Head of the Centre was fixed at 40: 30:30 through direct service, promotion and departmental limited competitive examination respectively. According to the Department of Rural Development. The Rules of Service Admission of the Head of the Department were notified vide Notification dated 10.06.2014. Reference No. 8 The procedure for filling up these posts was decided in accordance with the decision of the government here. However, the government has said that the posts are not being filled. Therefore, the government was considering issuing a decision to change the recruitment rate to fill 100 percent posts through promotion and 50 percent posts through limited departmental competitive examination.
Government's decision:
The earlier Government decision dated 16.02.2018 in respect of recruitment to the post of Head of the Centre is being superseded and the corresponding Government decision/ Government Sanction Letter in this regard is being superseded.
It is approved to fill up the existing vacancies of the Head of the Centre as well as the posts falling vacant due to retirement, resignation, dismissal, etc., as soon as those posts become vacant, 50 percent of the posts by promotion and 50 percent of the posts by limited departmental competitive examination within the limits of that quota.
All the Chief Executive Officers, Zila Parishads should determine the posts of the Head of the Centre taking into account the number of Urdu schools in their district.
The following action should be taken to fill the vacant posts of the head of the center by departmental limited competitive examination and promotion:
4.1 Zonal Limited Competitive Examination:- The Zonal Limited Competitive Examination will be conducted by the Maharashtra State Council of Examination.
4.2 Conduct and Format of Examination:- The conduct of Aptitude and IQ Test for selection of Head of the Centre through Departmental Limited Competitive Examination will be conducted by such examination system as may be determined by the Government. The examination will be conducted at all the examination centres across the state. The format of the test will be as follows:

Redressal of Grievances:- Responsibility of redressing the grievances of the candidate lies with the concerned district.
He will be the chief executive of the council. Applicants must submit a written complaint to the relevant authorities. Complaints must be addressed within 15 working days. The Director of Education (Primary) will be the appellate authority in this regard.
The entire process of filling up the posts through Limited Departmental Competitive Examination of the Head of the Center will be done under the control of the Director of Education (Primary). Detailed instructions in this regard will be issued from the Directorate level from time to time.
According to the order, 50 per cent of the sanctioned posts of the head of the centre will be filled through promotion and 50 per cent of the posts will be filled through limited departmental competitive examination. At present, if more than 50 per cent of the posts in the promotion quota are vacant, the posts will be filled through a limited departmental competitive examination.
As the posts of the head of the centre are in the establishment of the Zilla Parishad, the Department of Rural Development should take action to make necessary amendments in the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad District Service (Entry into Service) Rules, 1967 regarding their entry into service.
The districts which have completed the process of filling up the posts of the Union President by the date of the proposed Government decision shall be valid.
The said Government Decision is being issued by the Rural Development Department in accordance with the consensus of Informal Reference No. 48/ Aastha-14 dated 17.11.2022.
