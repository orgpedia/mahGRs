Closure of Government Practice Pathshala provided to the students of Government Teacher Schools in the State
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 15th of September 2020
Reference:- Letter from the Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune, No. RAS/ Pre-Service Education/ 2020-21 Dated 29.7.2020
Preamble: The affiliated practice schools were made available to the students of the Government Teachers' Schools in the State for taking practice lessons. At present, considering the decreasing number of students in the government teachers' school and the number of students in some practice schools has become zero, as well as the primary education facility available in the nearby area for the remaining practice school students, the government was under consideration to permanently close the 10 government practice schools and adjust the working teacher and non-teaching staff in the school as per the rules.
Government Decision: As proposed by the Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune, a total of 10 practice schools mentioned in the table below are being closed. Out of these 10 practice schools, the next 2 practice schools are being closed immediately due to lack of students. The teaching and non-teaching staff working in these 2 Practice Pathshalas should be adjusted in equivalent teaching and non-teaching vacancies respectively before 30th November, 2020.
