The circular was issued by the Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Administration Branch) of the post of Education Officer and similar.
The Government Circular No. of Provisional Seniority dated 01.01.2016 was issued vide Government Circular No. Seniority-2116/ PR No. 121/ PR-II dated 27.05.2016.
If there are objections from some officers regarding this seniority, they were instructed to send it to the government through the office of the Commissioner (Education), Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune within 1 month from the date of publication of the provisional seniority. The corrigendum for naming Bachchav after Shri Sandeep Kumar Sureshrao Sontakke, S.No.105 has been published on 18.01.2017.
(2) The said seniority has been maintained among the officers who have joined the promoted post within the prescribed period (after the period of assumption of office) as per the provisions of rules 4 and 5 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982.
The said seniority is being issued subject to the decision of the Supreme Court, the High Court and the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal on any other special civil application/ writ petition filed earlier or if this seniority list has to be changed.
In view of all the facts and circumstances mentioned in the above paragraph, the final seniority of the Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Administration Branch) Education Officer and similar post as on the end of 31.12.2015 is being published in the form attached.
This should be brought to the notice of the Commissioner of Seniority (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune without any delay by all the concerned officers.
