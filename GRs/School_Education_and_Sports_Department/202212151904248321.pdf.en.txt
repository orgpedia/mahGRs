Kolhapur is a Marathi medium partially aided secondary (Class 5th to Class 10th) school, located near Tasgaon Road, Minche, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation of Hatkanangale, Dist. Kolhapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The institute has shifted the above school to a self-sufficient building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
