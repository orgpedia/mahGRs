The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme through Yashoda, Pune for Group A posts and Vanamati, Nagpur for Group B posts. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, in the category of Deputy Educational Officer and similar, Group B, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has created 6 supernumerary posts for the 6 candidates selected on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2016 and undergoing training in the Combined Subject Training Programme-4 by the Government Decision dated August 1, 2017. However, 3 candidates have been given extension to join the post of Deputy Education Officer. The 3 candidates who have been given extension will attend the training programme from 16.1.2019. As the matter of creating more number of posts in the cadre of Deputy Education Officers to pay their salaries was under the consideration of the government, the following decision is being taken.
Government's decision:
Out of the 12 candidates selected for appointment to the post of Deputy Educational Officer and similar, Group-B (Administration Branch) on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2016 by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, 6 candidates have already appeared for the training.
