The Proposal:
In reference to the above No. Under the decision of the government here, the salaries and allowances of teachers and non-teaching staff in private aided primary, secondary and high schools as well as district councils, municipal corporations and municipalities in the state are paid through the Shalarth system. The salaries of the teaching and non-teaching staff working in 49 aided secondary schools under the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) were being paid by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) for the year 2016-2017 and their reimbursement was being done by the office of the Inspector of Education (South), Mumbai, the next year. Reference No. 2 As per the Government decision not to pay the salary of the employees who do not have the school number, the salary grant for the years 2016-2017 to 2019-2020 was not reimbursed to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.

Government's decision:
The Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Pune, has approved the payment of salary for the period 2016-2017 to 2019-2020 as per the salary of teachers and non-teaching staff of 49 aided schools under the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC).
