The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Marathi medium aided primary and upper primary school Solapur to Vinay Gram Vikas Multi-purpose Society, Solapur, was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Vinay Gram Vikas Multi-purpose Institute, G. Solapur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The batch approval orders of the said school should be verified by the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune regarding the consent of all the teachers for transfer and the quality of the school.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(3) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the basis of the grant in aid granted to it.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If any fresh complaints are received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3019/ Q. No. 316/ SM-1: The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it comes to notice or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution.
On fulfillment of the above condition number (1), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune, shall examine the matter and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
