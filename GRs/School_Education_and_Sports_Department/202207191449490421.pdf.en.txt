Preamble: A meeting of the Divisional Promotion Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (School Education) was held to give promotions in the selection list 2021-22 to the vacant posts of Administrative Officer Cadre in Group-A (Administration Branch) of Maharashtra Education Service. It was held on 24.02.2022. In the meeting, the eligibility-disqualification for promotion to the post of Administrative Officer (Administration Branch) from the Superintendent Cadre was decided. Accordingly, after approval of the selection list of the General Administration Department, the orders of promotion have been issued on 26.05.2022 after approval of the appointing authority on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board. Rajmohan Sharma, Superintendent Pay and Provident Fund Team (Secondary) vide Government Order dated 31.08.2020, in view of the nature of the allegations made against Shri Sharma, it is hereby ordered that the increment of the immediate salary of Shri Sharma shall be withheld for one year without affecting the next increment. Since the completion of the sentence is due in July 2022, the order of promotion of Shri Rajmohan Sharma as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 15.12.2017 of the General Administration Department, will be issued after the expiry of the sentence in July 2022, stated the Government Order dated 26.05.2022.
The proceedings against Rajmohan Sharma have been completed. Shri Rajmohan Sharma has been promoted to the rank of Administrative Officer. Therefore, the government has decided to give promotion to Mr. Rajmohan Sharma as follows.
Government Order: The following officers working in the post of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-B (Administration Branch), Superintendent, Salary and Provident Fund Team, will be appointed as Administrative Officers in the Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-A (Administration Branch) (Pay Scale S-20 to 56100-177500) as per the selection list approved by the Government.
