Arvind Satav, Headmaster, ZP High School Pimpalgaon, Buldhana has been promoted and posted. Mr. Arvind Satav has refused to be promoted on the basis of his date of birth due to family problems. As per the provision mentioned herein 5 of the Governing Decision dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department, the name of Shri Arvind Satav is being excluded from the Selection List for the year 2020-21. Therefore, the order dated 29.12.2022 regarding promotion of Shri Arvind Satav is being cancelled.
