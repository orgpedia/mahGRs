(2) This is permitted by the Government Decision dated 5th July, 2002. Additional (Tribal) batches to be added to the general batch of Military Schools for imparting education to the tribal students in these Military Schools from the year 2008-09. (3) Here. It was approved on December 24, 2007.
Considering the number of tribal students in the Sainik School, a total of 8 tribal units, namely 6 tribal units of 1 each of Class XI from 2014-15 and 2 tribal units of 1 each of Class XII from 2015-16, have been constituted as reference No. (4) It has been approved under the Government Resolution, dated 08.09.2017. 23 posts of teachers in the 11th and 12th batches for tribal children in military schools. The High-Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the creation of the emoluments from 29.08.2019. The matter of approving the creation of necessary teaching posts for these units was under the consideration of the government.
