Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Lawn Tennis Association, Kuprej, Colaba, Mumbai has invited applications for selection of Maharashtra Government Team for All India Civil Services Lawn Tennis Tournament 2023-2023 to be held at Maharana Pratap Khel Gaon Stadium, Udaipur, Rajasthan from 23.9.2023 to 28.9.2023. The selection test was conducted on 25.08.2023. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be held as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,56,850/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Six Thousand Eight Hundred and Fifteen only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the athletes/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 18.9.2023 to 20.9.2023 and for the competition from 23.9.2023 to 28.9.2023 with effect from 15.9.2023 (M.No.) and the services of these athletes should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Every player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department Office under whom they will be working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989 and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be paid Rs. 18.09 2023 to d. Absence from office during the period 28.09.2023 (Training Camp, Competitions and Travel) should be treated as" Duty Period." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of head accounts of the concerned department/ head of office. Travel/ Daily Allowance and others as admissible to players outside Mumbai.
