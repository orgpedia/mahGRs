The proposal to form a committee was under the consideration of the government. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
The government has decided to give away the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Eklavya State Sports Award (Sportsperson with Disability) and Best Sports Organizer/ Activist and Jijamata Award to the sportspersons. The following Committees are being constituted to suggest amendments in the norms/ rules after studying the norms prescribed under the Government Decision dated 16.10.2017.
The Committee shall submit a report to the Government within one month regarding the amendments to be made by the Committee after studying the norms/ rules prescribed under the above Government Decision. The committee will have a two-month time frame.
