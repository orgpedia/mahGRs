Introduction: Shri Shaneshwar Educational Institutions, Nimbendur-Vihire, G. Bapu Budhwant Secondary School, Nimbendur-Vihire, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of the aided middle school of Marathi medium, Ahmednagar, to Shri Shaneshwar Samajik Sanstha, Nimbendur-Vihire, G. Ahmednagar, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Sadar Secondary School, Shri Shaneshwar Social Institute, Nimbendur-Vihire, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Shevgaon, G. Ahmednagar Institute on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) It shall be ensured that the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for the transfer of the school. Also, the eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, the number of teachers/ non-teaching staff as permissible posts shall remain in operation and not more than that number of teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.
