As per the directions given in the order dated 28/09/2019, the Annual Plan and Budget for the year 2019-20 shall be spent for recurring/ non-recurring expenditure of ongoing schemes of elementary education under the Samagra Shiksha approved by the Project Approval Board (PAB). The funds will also be used for the welfare of the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
4. It should be ensured that the fund is spent in the current year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure.
5. The expenditure is under the purview of the Finance Department. As per the instructions of the Government Circular dated 08/07/2019. The Under Secretary/ Cell Officer (Accounts Branch), Department of School Education and Sports, Ministry is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and the Deputy Secretary (Administration), Department of School Education and Sports, Ministry as the" Controlling Officer" for the purpose of collection and disbursement of funds.
