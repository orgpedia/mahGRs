The Proposal:
The government has taken the decision. through December 29, 2023. The government has decided to celebrate January 15, the birth anniversary of late Olympian Khashaba Jadhav, as the" State Sports Day." The government has also issued instructions regarding various sports activities to be carried out on the occasion of the State Sports Day.
National Sports Day is being celebrated across the country. National Sports Day and Sports Week 1 here. As per the Government Decision dated 31.07.1997, each district has been allocated a total of Rs. 10,000/- will be provided.
This grant is being increased in accordance with the current situation. Also, with the decision of State Sports Day as above, it is necessary to include State Sports Day along with the objectives of Sports Week, National Sports Day. In view of this, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to increase the existing grants for the Sports Week and National Sports Day and to fix a per district grant for the same, including the State Sports Day.
Government's decision:
The government has decided to celebrate the birthday of Olympian Khashaba Jadhav, i. e., 15th January every year as the" State Sports Day." Accordingly, considering the need to increase the existing grants for the Sports Week and National Sports Day, the Government has taken the following decisions:
Reference No.1 dated 31.07.1997 regarding grant for celebration of National Sports Day and Sports Week. 1 The government decision here is being overridden by this.
