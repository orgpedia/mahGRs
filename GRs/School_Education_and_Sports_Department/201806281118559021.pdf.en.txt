Introduction: Shri Mankeshwar Educational and Social Institute, Karad, G. Satara operated Academic Heights Public School, Undale Road, Kalatek Kale, Ta. Karad, G. Satara, English Medium Self-Financed Primary School, Mt. Nandlapur, The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for the relocation to Karad, G. Satara was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the original location of the school did not have a paved road and other facilities, the children had to go through the forest area, mud roads, so the government has decided to approve the relocation from the unsafe place near the forest area to a safe place built on 2 acres of land taken on a 30-year lease on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Satara shall ensure that all the pre-migration students are accommodated in the nearest school/ place of migration and there is no complaint/ objection in this regard and the building at the place of migration is safe for the students.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Further, all the eligible teaching and non-teaching staff have been accommodated at the migrant place and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.

(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and orders issued from time to time by the Government, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided for the school. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(5) In the case of a self-financing migrant school, there shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission and approval of the Government.
The organization shall be bound to follow the laws, rules, government decisions, orders, etc. prescribed by the government from time to time in this regard.
(7) The school should be sanctioned at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was originally sanctioned.
(8) As the school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the school shall be on the institution.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
(10) It shall be necessary for the institution to ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
2. Clause No. On fulfilment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur shall take necessary further action regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and the action taken shall be communicated to the Government.
