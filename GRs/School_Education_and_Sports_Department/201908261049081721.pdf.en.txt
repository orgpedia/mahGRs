Government circular:
Every citizen of the society should do physical exercise or participate in sports.
The central government has decided to implement the Fit India Movement across the country.
The Fit India Movement was launched on the occasion of National Sports Day on August 29, 2019. The Prime Minister will address the gathering from Indira Gandhi Stadium, New Delhi. This time The Prime Minister will take oath on physical fitness. The oath will be administered on 29th August, 2019 at 10-00 AM. It will be made available to all people.
Following instructions have been issued to all the concerned.
1) The schools concerned should make necessary arrangements for the students of all schools, colleges and universities to take the oath at that time. All students and teachers are expected to attend.
(2) All the members of Gram Panchayats/ Municipal Councils/ Municipal Corporations shall make necessary arrangements in their offices/ halls for taking oath on physical fitness. At that time, the maximum number of citizens of the village/ town were present
Be sure to stay. Sportspersons/ general public/ other dignitaries and public representatives should be invited.
3) Organize activities like morning walk for men and women, short distance marathon races for youth, yoga, etc.
