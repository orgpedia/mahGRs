School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: Departmental Deputy Director of Education, Pune Division, Pune vide letter No.
Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of Marathi medium partially aided secondary school, Shree Sant Tukaram Maharaj Secondary School, Mhetrevasti, Chikhli, T. G. Pune, run by Navnagar Education Board, Akurdi, Pune, to Saraswati Bhavan Education Society, Moshi Authority, Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Saraswati Bhavan Education Society, Moshi Authority, Pune on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the transferred school.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Order, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said transfer or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune should examine the incidental matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
