Preamble- There should be a conducive atmosphere for the promotion, promotion, propagation and nurturing of sports culture in the state. It is in this role that the State Sports Policy, 2012 has been announced and the Government Decision dated 14.6.2012 has been issued to implement it. Point No. C- 4.7 (c) of the Sports Policy deals with the revision of the Stadium Development Grant Scheme.
Accordingly, the grant limit of the District Level Scheme of the Stadium Development Grant Scheme has been increased and revised in the criteria. 2 d. The decision was made on February 13, 2014. Reference No. 3 and 4 have been amended from time to time. With all these aspects, the matter of amending the rules for this scheme was under the consideration of the government.
The government has decided to implement the revised rules of the District Level Scheme of Stadium Development Grant Scheme through this government decision.
The Government Decision dated 16.08.2014 and Government Corrigendum dated 3.10.2016 are being superseded.
The rules contained in Annexure' A' shall be applicable along with the decision of the Government to provide the benefit of this scheme. The revised subsidy limits and rules will be applicable from the date of the government decision.
District Sports Officer of the concerned district is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for this district level scheme.
At present, the title of the district level scheme of playground development grant is available. This plan will continue.
