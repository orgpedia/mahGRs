Also, the institution concerned will be required to provide a bank guarantee of 10% of the total grant amount to be sanctioned. Accordingly, after depositing the bank guarantee and affidavit with the concerned District Sports Officer, the funds available for the disbursement of the sanctioned amount will be transferred to the concerned institution through RTGS.
