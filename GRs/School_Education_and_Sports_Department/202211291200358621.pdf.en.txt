Mumbai: The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has decided to start 12 new primary and high schools in the city on a self-financed basis.

Date of release: November 29, 2022
Government Resolution, Department of School Education and Sports, No: NAMASA-2009/ (497/09/ MASHI-1, dated 11th June, 2009.
The Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012, dated 04.01.2013. Proposal dated 28/10/2021.
The proposal for recognition of 12 new primary to higher secondary schools affiliated to CISCE and CISCE programmes under the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012 was approved by the Additional Municipal Commissioner, Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, Mumbai vide Reference No. (3) submitted to the Government. The government was considering allowing these 12 schools to start classes on a self-financed basis.
12 new primary to higher secondary (ECS) courses of CISCE Schools from 1st to 12th standard are being allowed to start from the academic year 2022-23 on self-financing basis, subject to the following conditions.

Terms and Conditions: I. Since the opening of these 12 new primary to high schools has been permitted on self-financing basis, no financial assistance/ grant, either direct or indirect, will be provided by the State Government for any expenditure incurred in respect of the said school.
It shall be mandatory to comply with all the standards, terms and conditions prescribed by the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the Maharashtra Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Rules, 2011 and the directives issued by the Government under this Act or the standards, terms and conditions specified by the Government from time to time.
It shall be mandatory to comply with all the standards, terms and conditions prescribed by the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012 and the directions issued by the Government under this Act or the standards or terms and conditions specified by the Government from time to time.
As per the directions issued by the Government from time to time, it will be mandatory to obtain a no-objection certificate from the Government for CBSE/ CISCE Board affiliation.
