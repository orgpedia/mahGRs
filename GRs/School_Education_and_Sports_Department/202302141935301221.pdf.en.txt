Introduction: The players of Kho-Kho game in the state should get maximum opportunity to play in the state tournament. In order to enable them and the sports enthusiasts to witness the skill games of the elite athletes, the Government has decided to organize State level Kho-Kho Games for boys and girls under 19 years of age every year. Bhai Nerurkar Trophy to be held as State level Kho Kho Sports Tournament. It was approved by the government on August 6, 2009. As per the Government decision dated 03.01.2012, the grant limit has been increased to Rs. 50.00 lakh for the conduct of this competition.
The amount of grant for organizing this competition was decided in 2012 and considering the increase in expenditure on various aspects related to organizing the competition, the government was considering to increase the grant for this competition. The government has taken the following decisions: The Bhai Nerurkar Trophy, a state-level kho-kho tournament, currently gets Rs. 50.00 lakh per annum to Rs. 75.00 lakhs is being approved by the Government.
