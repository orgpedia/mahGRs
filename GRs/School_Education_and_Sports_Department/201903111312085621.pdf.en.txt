As per the provisions of the School Education and Sports Department (erstwhile Education Services and Youth Services Department) Government Notification dated 20.02.1980, the Combined Departmental Examination is conducted for the Gazetted Officers of Group A and Group B in Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) and General State Service Group B (Administration Branch) Cadre. The Department of School Education and Sports, vide Government Decision dated 31.07.2008, decided to conduct the Departmental Examination for the Principal, Senior Advocate Group-A and Advocate Group-B in the District Institute of Education and Training in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Teacher Training Branch). The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Aurangabad, in its original application No. 774/2009, dated 06.07.2010, states that the examination prescribed for the administration branch cannot be applied to the officers of the Teacher Training Branch.
As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 31.07.2008, the candidates who have passed the departmental examination conducted by the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) under the notification dated 20.02.1980 are not required to take the examination again.
