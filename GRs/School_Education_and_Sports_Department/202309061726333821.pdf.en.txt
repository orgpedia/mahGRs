March 16, 2022 Preamble:" To provide financial assistance for construction of sports facilities" is one of the important recommendations of the Sports Policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is mentioned above. The decision was taken on March 1, 2014. 3 Here the information of the eligible institutions for the scheme is mentioned.
Accordingly, financial assistance is provided to eligible institutions in the State on 75:25 basis (Government share 75% and eligible institution share 25%) for construction of sports facilities. Reference No. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraphs 4 (a) and (b) of the Government Decision. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune has submitted the proposals of eligible institutions to the Government under Reference No. 3 along with recommendations for grant of administrative approval for financial assistance for construction of sports facilities. Following the recommendations, the government took the decision. Subject to the terms and conditions as on 1.3.2014, the proposals for providing financial assistance for construction of sports facilities were under consideration of the Government for grant of in-principle administrative approval.
Government Decision: The Government Decision referred to in Annexure-A below shall ensure compliance with all the terms and conditions of eligibility for grant and grant approval as mentioned in the Government Decision, dated 1st March, 2014 and the expenditure limit prescribed for the project subject to scrutiny of the project and budgetary cost and other conditions, for the project for construction of sports facilities mentioned in Pillar-3.
