The Proposal:
The Hon'ble Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 (Chairman and Managing Director, FCI and Others Vs. Jagadish Balaram Behera and Others) and other petitions dated 06.07.2017, has held that protection in Government service is not payable to persons who have joined Government service on the basis of reservation for Backward Castes and whose caste claims have since become invalid. 3140/2008 vide order dated 28.09.2018. In pursuance of the order of the Hon'ble Supreme Court, the proceedings have been ordered to be completed by 31.12.2019. General Administration Department to implement the decision of the Supreme Court. As per the provisions of the Government decision issued on 21.12.2019, the Government was considering the creation of additional number of posts for teachers/ non-teaching staff in aided schools under this department who were appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but could not produce caste validity certificate.
Government's decision:
The Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 and other petitions. In pursuance of the decision dated 06.07.2017, it is approved to create supernumerary posts for 11 months for a total of 3 employees mentioned in the following prospectus who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but the concerned Education Officer in the cadre of Assistant Teacher who has not submitted the caste validity certificate has submitted the certificate of being a 100% aided post employee in a 100% aided school.
