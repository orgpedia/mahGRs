The High Court has directed on July 8, 2021, to publish the detailed addresses, e-mails and telephone numbers of Punarikshan Samiti and Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee in a comprehensive manner on the website of the School Education Department and all zonal offices, on the website and notice boards of all schools and in the newspaper.
