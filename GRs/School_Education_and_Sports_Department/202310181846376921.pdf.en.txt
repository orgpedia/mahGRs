Introduction: Annual maintenance repair of computers and printers in use in the office of the Directorate of Sports, Pune, as well as the purchase of stationery, computer and printer spare parts was done by the contractor. However, since the Directorate does not have sufficient funds every year under the Computer Expenditure target, the said contractor has to incur an expenditure of Rs.
1093.882 thousand could not be paid. For this purpose, the matter of reallocation of necessary funds under the target of 22041371-17 computer expenditure was under consideration.
Government's decision:
The annual maintenance and repair of computers and printers used in the office of the Directorate of Sports, Pune, as well as the purchase of stationery, computer and printer spare parts have been done by the contractor. However, since the Directorate does not have sufficient funds under the Computer Expenditure target, the said contractor has to incur an expenditure of Rs.
1093.882 thousand could not be paid. For this purpose, the expenditure on 22041371-17 computer is Rs. 1093.882 thousand is being approved to be made available through redistribution as follows:
