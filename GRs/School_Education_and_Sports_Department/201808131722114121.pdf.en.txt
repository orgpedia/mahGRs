Government Order: Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and their duties in the performance of government duties
The Prevention of Delay Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 1st July, 2006. Regulation of transfers and delay in discharging official duties of Maharashtra Government employees
The competent authority for transfer has been declared under Section 6 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority transferring under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the powers of various cadre officers in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the proposal for transfer was submitted to the Civil Services Board on the request of the officers of the Senior Advocate Cadre of Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A. The officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 against their names, with the prior approval of the competent authority and the concerned senior authorities, as per the recommendation of the Civil Services Board.
