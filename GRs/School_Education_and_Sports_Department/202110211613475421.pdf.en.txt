Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of the English Medium Primary (Class 1st to 8th) School of Sri Lakshminarayan Multi-Purpose Education Society, Garkheda Campus, Aurangabad, run by The World School (formerly Jijamata English Primary School), Garkheda Campus, Aurangabad, on a self-financed basis, to Sri Sai Knowledge Solution, Garkheda Campus, Aurangabad, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Shri Sai Knowledge Solution, Garkheda Campus, Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school whose management has been changed shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) Since the transfer of the English medium school is being done on a self-financing basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school will be on the institution.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated, or
