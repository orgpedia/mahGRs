Referred No. According to the letter of 1, the Central Government has set revised guidelines in line with the National Teacher Award 2017. Accordingly, the Government is approving the appointment of the following District Selection Committee for the National Teachers Award 2017.

The Central Government has given the National Teacher Award. Guidelines issued vide letter dated 15.6.2018 should be strictly followed. The selection committee should ensure that the secrecy of the selection process is maintained.
The District Selection Committee has submitted three nominations to the State Selection Committee through the online portal. Must be submitted by July 24, 2018. The login/ password for the District Selection Committee will be intimated to the concerned District Collector through e-mail. Therefore, all the education officers (secondary)/ teacher inspectors (for Mumbai) should contact the district collector. Also, the nomination of the members of A. No. 3 should be obtained.
