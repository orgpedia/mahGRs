Preamble: A new" Defined Contribution Pension Scheme" for teachers and non-teaching staff in 100% aided posts in Zilla Parishads, recognized private aided primary, secondary and higher secondary schools and teachers" schools was under consideration of the Government for the procedure of depositing the arrears of the 6th Pay Commission in Level-2 of the employees.
Government Decision: The new" Defined Contribution Pension Scheme" for teachers and non-teaching staff of 100% aided posts in Zilla Parishads, recognized private aided primary, secondary and higher secondary schools and teachers" schools has been approved to open new head of accounts for depositing 6th Pay Commission arrears in Level-2 of the employees.
As per the approval given by the Accountant General's Office as well as the Finance Department, approval is being given to open new account titles as follows.
(1) 8342-Other Deposits, 117- Defined Contribution Pension Scheme for Government Employees, (03) Defined Contribution Pension Scheme, (03) (11) Level-2 Contribution of Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff in Recognized and Aided Private Primary Schools (8342 5473)
