Yavatmal Managed Urdu Primary School, Galva, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the Urdu Primary School Yavatmal to Pobaru Layout, Nagpur Road Bypass, Yavatmal, G. Yavatmal was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from a building with inadequate physical facilities to a building provided by the Chairman of the institution in his possession free of cost. Also, the school is proposed to be shifted as the minority community of this village has migrated to Yavatmal.
The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The existing students of the school should be accommodated in the nearest Urdu medium school or at the place of transfer.
(3) It shall be ensured that the consent of all the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for migration and all the eligible teachers and non-teaching staff are accommodated at the place of migration and there is no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided for the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(7) The school shall function at the place of relocation on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(8) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati shall take necessary further action regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the Government about the action taken.
