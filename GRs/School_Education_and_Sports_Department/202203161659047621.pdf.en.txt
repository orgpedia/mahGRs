Preamble- Director, Directorate of Minority and Adult Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide No. In accordance with the letter of August 4, the Government Decision dated August 24, 2021 of the Department of School Education and Sports is being superseded. In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater effectiveness in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has taken a number of initiatives under the Contextualization of Schemes. The office memorandum dated 23 rd March, 2021 directs to adopt the revised procedures. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement and utilization of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate single bank account in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary level Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a single nodal agency (SNA) and nodal officer for centrally sponsored schemes under the School Education and Sports Department was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the instructions given by the Government Circular in Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, for the implementation of the ongoing/ future Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of School Education and Sports, the Central Government has decided to implement the following Centrally Sponsored Schemes with a view to effectively manage the funds and bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds. The office memorandum dated 23 rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt the revised procedure. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement and utilization of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) will be appointed for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the bank authorised for the scheme.
