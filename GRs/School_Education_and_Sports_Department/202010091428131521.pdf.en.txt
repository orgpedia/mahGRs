Nutan Ganesh Vidyalaya, Budhwar Peth, G. Kolhapur, a Marathi medium aided primary school, Vasgade Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Vasgade, T. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of the institution to Karveer, G. Kolhapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Board of Education, Govt. The Government has decided to approve the transfer to Kolhapur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Divisional Education Deputy Director, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur shall ensure the approval of the order by the natural growth of Nutan Ganesh Vidyalaya, Budhwar Peth, District Kolhapur School.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
