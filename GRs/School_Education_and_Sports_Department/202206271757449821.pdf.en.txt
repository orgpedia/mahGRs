circular
The 44th Chess Olympiad was held in Chennai, India. It will be held from 28.07.2022 to 10.08.2022. This is the first time the tournament is being held in India.
This year, the country is celebrating the 75th anniversary of independence. The first flame of this game of chess is starting from India. This flame will be taken around 75 cities of the country on the occasion of the 75th Amrit Mahotsav of Independence. The torch will be taken to three cities in Maharashtra. Coming up on 02.07.2022. For this, the following coordination committee groups are being formed to coordinate between various departments and institutions.
