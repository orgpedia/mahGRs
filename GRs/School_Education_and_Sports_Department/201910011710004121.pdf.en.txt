Preamble: The Consolidated Guidelines on the procedure to be followed for promotion of State Government Officers/ Employees have been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 1.8.2019 of the General Administration Department. As per the guidelines in this government decision, the government is considering to form a promotion committee for the promotion to the Group B post in the Maharashtra Education Service under the School Education and Sports Department. Following this, the government decided:
Government's decision:
The decisions of the Government issued earlier to form a Promotion Committee to recommend for promotion to the post of Group-B in Maharashtra Education Service under the School Education and Sports Department are being superseded and a new Divisional Promotion Committee is being formed as follows:

Necessary quorum for the meeting of the Departmental Promotion Committee:
A minimum of 3 members must be present at the meeting: the President, the Secretary, and the Treasurer. However, if the Secretary/ Member Secretary is absent from the meeting, the officer to be directed by the Chairman of the Committee shall act as Secretary/ Member Secretary.
The jurisdiction of the Divisional Promotion Committee shall be as follows:
The Grade Pay of the 6th Pay Commission is Rs. To consider the proposals for recommending promotion for Maharashtra Education Service Group-B posts which is Rs.4800/- or less and to prepare the selection list of eligible officers for promotion and recommend to the Department in accordance with the provisions of the Service Admission Rules and the orders/ guidelines issued by the General Administration Department from time to time.
