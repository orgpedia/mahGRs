GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SSA-2019/ PR No. 111 PART-3/ SD-1 3. Funds disbursed under the said Government Decision shall be borne by the Central Government. As per the directions given in the order dated 30/04/2019, expenditure should be incurred for recurring expenditure of ongoing schemes of primary education under Samagra Shiksha. The provision made under the Tribal Component Programme should be taken up for individual benefit schemes as well as for creating infrastructure in the tribal sub-plan areas and only individual benefit schemes should be taken up in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan areas.
4. It should be ensured that the fund is spent in the current year as per the approved plan in accordance with the existing government policy on expenditure. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made, the utilization certificate of the fund, the material objectives achieved in the headings/ sub-headings etc. should be sent by the concerned field officer to the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Nashik; Tribal Development Department and School Education Department without fail by the 10th of every month.
5. It should be ensured that the expenditure on various schemes in the tribal sub-plan area, in the additional tribal sub-plan area and in the areas outside the tribal sub-plan area is recorded separately so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on schemes in the tribal area and outside the area of application.
6. The expenditure is under the purview of the Finance Department. 01/04/2019 and D. As per the instructions of the Government Circular dated 08/07/2019. The Under Secretary/ Cell Officer (Accounts Branch), Department of School Education and Sports, Ministry is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and the Deputy Secretary (Administration), Department of School Education and Sports, Ministry as the" Controlling Officer" for the purpose of collection and disbursement of funds.
7. The said Government decision shall be subject to the approval of the Finance Department. 01/04/2019 and D. As per the Government Circular dated 08/07/2019, the Finance Branch of the Finance Department vide Informal Reference No. 230/ p. Issued pursuant to the approval granted under 17/06/2019.
