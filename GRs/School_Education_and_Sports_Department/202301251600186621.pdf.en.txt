The Proposal:
The Government of India has launched the Nav Bharat Literacy Programme for the period April 2022 to March 2027. As per the Road Map of the programme, it has been mentioned to set up a State Literacy Mission Authority (SLMA). According to As per the approval of the Cabinet, the Centrally Sponsored" New India Literacy Programme" will be implemented in the State as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The government was considering setting up committees at the state level, district level, block level and school level for the implementation of the Nav Bharat Literacy Programme.
Government Decision: It has been decided to constitute and constitute the following Committees for the implementation of Navbharat Literacy Programme: 1) State Literacy Mission Authority (SLMA) A) Governing Council

(a) The School Level Committee
The School Management Committee/ School Management and Development Committee at the school level will act as the School Level Committee for this programme.
The School Level Committee will be responsible for all policy decisions and implementation at the school level.
The School Level Committee shall be bound to act in accordance with all the instructions given by the State, District and Block level.
The committees will be formed at the district, block, district and state levels.

4) Monitoring the planning of the districts.
