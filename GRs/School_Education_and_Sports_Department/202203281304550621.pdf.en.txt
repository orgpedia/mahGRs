Government's decision:
The selection of Maharashtra Government team for All India Civil Services (Women/ Men) Kabaddi Tournament 2021-22 to be held at Motilal Khel School, Rai, Senipat, Haryana from 28th to 30th March, 2022 will be done at" Godrej Ground, Curry Road, Station Adjacent Mumbai." The selection test was conducted on 06 March 2022. The list of male/ female officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying statement" C" and the team statement of the State selected through the selection test is as per the accompanying statement" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,75,300/- (Rupees One Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Three Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the athletes/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 24th to 25th March, 2022 and for the competition from 28th to 30th March, 2022 with effect from 25th March, 2022 (M.No.) and the services of these athletes should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. Absence from office during the period from March 24 to March 31, 2022 (training camp, competition and travel) shall be treated as" duty period." as well as their daily wages

Expenditure on other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the office of the head of the department concerned. Also, the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai are covered under Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9, dated 11th May, 1989 and Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1404/ PR. No.8/ A/ Service-8, dated 27th February, 2004 should be approved. In order to avail the benefit of this concession, the certificate of attendance of the officers and employees appearing for the selection test issued by the Honorary Secretary, Secretariat Gymkhana, will have to be submitted by the concerned employees in their establishment office.
The absence from office of the officer/ employee (Return-C) appearing for the selection test of the competition should be treated as duty under the Government Decision, Finance Division No.-LVE/ 1492/46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992.
The Secretarial Gymkhana shall submit the statement of actual expenditure to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of the order. In case of balance, the balance amount should be returned immediately to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
The expenditure for this should be met from the budgetary provision under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Competition, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Competition (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)" for the financial year 2021-22.
The Secretariat Gymkhana is approving the expenditure as per return" B" subject to fulfillment of all conditions in the report of the competition, details of actual expenditure incurred, utilization certificate, examination statement and sanction order.
