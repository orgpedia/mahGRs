The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate Birajdar Prashala, Saraswati Nagar, Shelgi, G. Solapur was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing dilapidated and dangerous building to a spacious and well-equipped building with physical facilities. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure in writing the consent of the parents of the students in the school to the relocation, as well as to adjust the maximum number of students in the school to the place of relocation.
(3) It shall be ensured that there is no dispute as to whether two schools of two different institutions are to be housed in the same building.
(4) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
