Preamble In pursuance of the Government decision in Reference No. 1 and 2 above, the Department of Finance had revised the pension of State Government employees retiring after 1/1/2006 as per the recommendations of the Sixth Pay Commission and pensioners as per the Maharashtra Civil Services Pension Rules. These provisions have been made applicable to teachers/ non-teaching staff of Zilla Parishads, recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools in the state under the Government decision in Reference No. 3 of the School Education Department.
Department of Finance Some of the provisions of the Government decision dated 30/10/2009 were made effective from 27/2/2009 instead of 1/1/2006. The government was considering to implement this provision for teachers and non-teaching staff in Zilla Parishads, recognized private 100% aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools.
GOVERNMENT DECISION- 1. Reference No. 4 is here. The provision as amended under the Government Decision dated 27/12/2018 is being applied to teachers and non-teaching staff in Zilla Parishads, recognized private 100% aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools as usual.
(2) The expenditure shall be incurred under the head of the budget account under which the pension of the pensioners is spent and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under that head.
