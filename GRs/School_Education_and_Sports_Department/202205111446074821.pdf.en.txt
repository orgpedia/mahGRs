Letter from the office of Commissioner (Education) No. Ashika/ Pariksha/ 21/ Aastha-A/ 105/1101 dated 24.02.2022 Preamble: As per the Government Order dated 27.03.2017 of the Department of School Education and Sports, in direct service, the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre has been appointed to the post of Senior Advocate for a period of 2 years.
The proposal to complete the period of probation has been submitted vide letter dated 09.11.2021 and 24.02.2022 of Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government.
Accordingly, the Government have decided as follows: GOVERNMENT DECISION: In compliance with the conditions of probationary period as per the prevailing policy of the Government, read with the above mentioned Government Decision dated 27.03.2017, 3 officers working in the post of Senior Advocate in Group-A cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in the name of their name in Rakana No. 7 and the Government is approving their continuation on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 8.
