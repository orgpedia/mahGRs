Introduction: Goda Union Charitable Society, Nashik runs T.S. Dighole Primary School, Naigaon, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the primary and upper primary school of Sinnar, G. Nashik (classes 1 to 7) to Krantiveer Vasantrao Narayanrao Naik Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Nashik, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Krantiveer Vasantrao Narayanrao Naik Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same principle of grant-in-aid.
(2) In accordance with the revised criteria of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as in the orders issued from time to time by the Government regarding schools, the number of teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts shall remain in operation and not more than this number of teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.
It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy regarding filling up of vacancies from the permissible posts by additional staff.
(3) It shall be ensured that in case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management
