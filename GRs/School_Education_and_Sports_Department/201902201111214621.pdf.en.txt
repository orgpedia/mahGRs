Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Lawn Tennis Tournament, 2018-19, which was held at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune from 14th to 20th February, 2019, was held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai. The exam was conducted on 14th January 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The selection test, training camp and participation of the state team as per the prospectus' B' is Rs. 82,425/- (Rupees Eighty Two Thousand Four Hundred and Twenty Five only).
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
4. The Head of the Department Head Office concerned has directed the player coaches/ managers to participate in the tournament. Training camp from 11th to 13th February 2019. The competition will be held from February 14 to 20, 2019. To be made functional from 08.01.2019 (M.No.) and the services of these players are available at Secretariat Gymkhana
