The Proposal:
Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan with 60% funding by the Central Government and 40% by the State Governments. The salary of the officers and employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state is paid from this. In view of the difficult situation of the COVID-19 pandemic, it was necessary to pay the salaries of the working posts, so the matter of disbursement of allowable funds under the State Share Account Head 2202 I 612 under 01 salary target was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
The ruling:
Out of the budget provision made under the target of 01, salary for the salary of regular teachers and other employees in the District Institute of Education and Training in the state, to meet the expenditure on salary of the employees whose salary was from the service system, Rs. 8,84,37, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Eighty Four Lakhs Thirty Seven Thousand only). This amount is being disbursed from the provisions made in the budget under the head of accounts mentioned below.
