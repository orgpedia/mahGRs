The Proposal:
Jyoti Savitri International School, Khanvadi, Pune, operated by Zilla Parishad, Pune. Purandar, Upper Primary to Higher Secondary (E.P.) in Pune. The proposal to allow the commencement of classes from 6th to 12th (English Medium) on self-financing basis has been approved by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Pune vide Reference No. (2) submitted to the Government. The government was considering granting permission to upgrade the school from upper primary to high school.
