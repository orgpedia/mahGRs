Preamble: Gurukul Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad is a minority institution run by Shri. Sahabrao Patil Sultanpurkar Vidyamandir, Gadana, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for shifting of Marathi medium unaided Higher Primary School (Class 1 to 7), Khulatabad, G. Aurangabad to Hina Nagar, Chikalthana, Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Institute has shifted the above school to Hinanagar, Chikalthana, Aurangabad due to inadequate physical facilities. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3320/ PR No. 444/ SM-1 (6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
