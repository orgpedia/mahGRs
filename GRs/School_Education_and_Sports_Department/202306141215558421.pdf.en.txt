Preamble: The Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 29th July, 2020 has approved the new National Education Policy. To provide state level coordination, control and guidance for the implementation of the National Education Policy, 2020. The Inter-Departmental Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 16th January, 2023. The government was considering including more members in the committee.
Government Decision: For effective implementation of National Education Policy-2020, the following reconstituted committees are being constituted by including new members in the inter-departmental committees for coordination, control and guidance at the state level for the work mentioned in the policy.
