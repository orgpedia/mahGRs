The Proposal:
The State Project Director, Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Mumbai has issued Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to disburse central share and state share funds for the Samagra Shiksha (primary and secondary education) programme.
The ratio of Central and State share for Samagra Shiksha (Primary and Secondary Education) is 60:40. The Central Government vide order dated 28/03/2022 has released the first installment of Rs. 271,86,79, 000/- for SAP and Rs. 10,10,71, 000/- and Rs. 34,73,61, 000/- aggregating to Rs. 316,71,11, 000/- has been sanctioned.
