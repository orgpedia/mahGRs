The Proposal:
To meet the budget deficit for the financial year 2017-18 to 2019-20, Satara Sainik School has been given Rs. 6,24,94, 819/- (Rupees Six Crores Twenty Four Lakhs Ninety Four Thousand Eight Hundred and Nineteen only), as per the demand No. (3) is provided here.

Government Decision: Satara Sainik School has been given Rs. The Government is approving the release of funds amounting to Rs. 65,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Five Lakhs only).
