Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of Jagnath High School, Jagnath Budhwar, Timki, Nagpur, a Marathi medium aided secondary school, run by Bahujan Vikas Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur, to National Workers Education Society, Somwar Peth, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said School to National Workers Education Society, Somwar Peth, Nagpur on the following terms and conditions: (1) It shall be ensured that the Chairperson and Members of the updated Annexure-I of the Bahujan Vikas Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur shall consent to the transfer of the School. Also, the education officer (secondary), zilla parishad, Nagpur, should verify that there is a recognition order for classes 9 and 10.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(3) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(4) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as permissible posts and not more than that.
(5) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.

(7) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur, after examining all the aspects and including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions, should take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
