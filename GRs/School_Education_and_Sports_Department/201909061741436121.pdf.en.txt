The proposal for transfer of three Marathi medium schools, Sangamner (unaided) (classes 8 to 10), to Sahyadri Bahujan Vidya Prasarak Samaj, Sangamner, G. Ahmednagar, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said schools to Sahyadri Bahujan Vidya Prasarak Samaj, Sangamner, G. Ahmednagar on the following terms and conditions: (1) The approval of all the classes of the said schools shall be required to be verified by the Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar.
(2) The condition of minimum strength of schools where the management has changed shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(3) The schools shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(4) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as permissible posts and not more than that.

(5) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in respect of schools where management changes have taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(7) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune shall examine all the aspects and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the schools by including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
