Teachers Awards are announced every year to honour the teachers who have served the society selflessly and loyally and who have excelled in the field of education.
2. The various categories of awards are District Teacher Award, State Teacher Award and National Teacher Award. The scheme of giving" Model Female Teacher" award at the state level is in operation. Teachers who have received the District Teacher Award or minor teachers can be selected for the National/ State Teacher Award. The District Teacher Award Scheme is implemented by all the District Councils of the State. The award is a district level award and its final implementation is carried out at the Zila Parishad level by the District Selection Committee at the district level headed by the Zila Parishad Chairperson.

The National Teacher Award Scheme is operational in the state of Maharashtra since 1958-59 and is implemented by the School Education Department, an administrative department. The Central Government has announced a grant of Rs. 50,000/- respectively. As per the directions of the Central Government, the names of 6 teachers from the state were sent to the Central Government for this award.
The State Teachers Award Scheme is operational in the state of Maharashtra since 1962-63 and is implemented by the School Education Departments. The State Award of Rs. 10,000/-. Further, National/ State awardee teachers will be given a lump sum amount of Rs. 1.00 lakh (Rupees One Lakh only).
The committee was appointed as per the decision of the Government dated 16th July, 2018 for final selection of teachers for the State Teachers Award. The State government had announced the award for teachers.
For the year 2017-18, the Government has selected 108 teachers at the school education and sports department level, including 38 primary teachers, 39 secondary teachers, 18 primary teachers who have excelled in tribal areas, 8 Savitribai Phule Adarsh teachers, 2 special teachers (arts/ sports), 1 disabled teacher/ teacher of a school for disabled students, 1 guide teacher and 1 scout teacher. The names of the teachers selected for the State Model Teacher Award are being announced as shown in the accompanying Annexure 1 to 7. Award distribution ceremony. It will be held on September 5, 2018.
