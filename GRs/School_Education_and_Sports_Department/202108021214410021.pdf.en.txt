The Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995, dated 1.1.1996. Composite 2007/ Q.No.58/ Amendment-3, dated 26.3.2007
Section 47 of the Central Government Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995, makes provisions for employees who suffer disability while in service.
In this regard, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Mumbai vide letter dated 26.3.2007 in Reference-2, has directed all the establishments under the Department to take action considering the provisions of Section 47 of the said Act. Accordingly, necessary action should be taken in respect of employees who have suffered disability while in service in accordance with the provisions contained in Section 47 (1) and its proviso to the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995.
