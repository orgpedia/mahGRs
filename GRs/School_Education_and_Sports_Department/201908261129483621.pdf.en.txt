The Proposal:
To nurture the sports culture of the state of Maharashtra and to create a sports environment among the people, every year a sports day is organized in every district of the state. The Sports Week is celebrated from 12th to 18th December. These include various sports activities, competitions, felicitation of sportspersons, seminars, exhibitions, etc. Major Dhyan Chand's birthday (August 29) is celebrated as National Sports Day. The events include events/ lectures highlighting the life of Major Dhyan Chand, scientific information on sports, sports activities, road races/ cycle races etc.
