The Proposal:
In order to ensure that maximum number of students of the State enter the National Defence Academy (NDA) competitive examination and join the armed forces and serve the country, as well as to provide the facility of disciplined, confident team spirit and leadership oriented Sainik education at the secondary level, the competent and voluntary organizations have been approved to start thirty schools imparting military education in every district of the State and to start a batch of 40 students from the 5th grade level from the year 1996-97 at 100% subsidy.

Government's decision:
As per the order of the Finance Department, a grant of 20% subsidy has been sanctioned to 36 teacher posts in a total of 18 batches in 3 Sainik Schools, which meet the criteria for evaluation of government-recognized private primary and secondary schools in the state, which are recognized on a permanent basis without subsidy and have excluded the word permanent. 30.46 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Lakhs Forty Six Thousand only) out of which 70% i.e. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 21.32 lakh (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Thirty Two Thousand only) for the financial year 2018-19.
2. The expenditure will be met out of the head" Demand No.E-2, 2202- (00) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Sainik Schools, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries), (2202 0502) during the financial year 2018-19. The Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" and the Assistant Director (Accounts), Secondary and Higher Secondary, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for disbursement of these funds.
3. This Government decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 778/2018/ Expenditure 5, dated 3.10.2018.
