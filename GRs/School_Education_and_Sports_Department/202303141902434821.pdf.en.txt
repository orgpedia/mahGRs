Government's decision
A total of 445 temporary posts in the offices of Pune Educational Division under the School Education Department have been given. 1st of March 2023. The government has approved a six-month extension till August 31, 2023. The office-wise and post-wise details in this regard are indicated in the statement accompanying this government decision.
The Government Decision, dated 31/7/2017 and the Government Decision, dated 7/12/2018, have restructured the Education Authority, in which Regional Education Authorities have been created, and the posts in the District Educational Continuing Professional Development Institute (presently known as District Institute of Education and Training) have also been restructured. In the District Institute of Education and Training, 1 post of Short Writer, 2 posts of Junior Clerk and 1 post of Peon were not abolished or transferred. Therefore, the said posts will continue to be in the same establishment till the finalization of the demerger. The government has also made a decision. After 7/12/2018, if the posts have been transferred elsewhere as per requirement, they shall continue in the place to which they were transferred by the decision of the Government.
