The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the Marathi medium aided secondary school, Nagpur, to Rani Durgavati Chowk, Anandnagar, G. Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The present building of the above school is proposed to be shifted as it is dilapidated, dangerous and damaged. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 4 weeks and inform the government about the action taken.
