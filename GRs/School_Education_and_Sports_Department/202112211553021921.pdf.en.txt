Government's decision:
Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai, Mumbai, will be conducting the All India Civil Services Basketball Tournament 2021-22 from 22nd to 25th December, 2021 at Sabina Chandrashekhar Recreation Ground, Recreation Society, Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Chowk, Mumbai. The exam was conducted on 10th December 2021. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 78,150/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Thousand One Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of Department/ Head of Office concerned should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 20th to 26th December, 2021 and for the tournament from 22nd to 25th December, 2021 from 20th December, 2021 (M. No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The absence from office during the period from December 20 to December 26, 2021 (training camp, competition and travel) should be treated as" duty period." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grant under the head of the head of the office of the department concerned. Also, the concessions such as travel/ daily allowance etc. admissible to players outside Mumbai, will be covered by the Finance Department vide Government Resolution No. LVE-1485/ PR. No.833/ A/ Service-9,

The Secretarial Gymkhana shall submit the statement of actual expenditure to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of the order. In case of balance, the balance amount should be returned immediately to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
The expenditure for this should be met from the budgetary provision under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Competition, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Competition (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)" for the financial year 2021-22.
The Secretariat Gymkhana is approving the expenditure as per return" B" subject to fulfillment of all conditions in the report of the competition, details of actual expenditure incurred, utilization certificate, examination statement and sanction order.
