Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for shifting of Shri Sharda Bhavan Education Society, Nanded run Shri Sharda Bhavan Secondary School, Chirgalli, Old Mondha, Nanded to Marathi medium aided secondary school (class 5th to 10th), Ardhapur, Dist.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school is dilapidated and dangerous, it is proposed to relocate the land owned by the institution to Ardhapur, Dist. Nanded. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
