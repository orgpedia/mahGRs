Preamble: In order to provide financial assistance for modernizing the old Talmis/ Akharas which have kept the tradition of wrestling art in the state in the modern times, and to provide financial assistance to the concerned institutions for promoting the development of Talim Wrestling Center. The scheme for development of Talim Wrestling Centre has been approved under the Government Decision dated 01.03.2014. In this regard, the Commissioner Sports and Youth Services, State of Maharashtra, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government through a letter dated 02.09.2017 for sanction of grants to various institutions for the development of Talim Wrestling Centre.
In pursuance of the said proposal, the matter of granting administrative approval for sanction of financial assistance to the concerned institution for the development of a centre for Talim Kusti was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Reference No. 1 to the Government Decision. The following eligible institutions are being given administrative approval under the Development of Training Wrestling Centre Grant Scheme subject to the terms and conditions as on 01.03.2014:
