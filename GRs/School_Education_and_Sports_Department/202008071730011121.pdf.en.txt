The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has come into force with effect from 01.07.2006. The competent authority for transfer has been declared as per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority transferring in accordance with proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the power of transfer of officers of various cadres in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
In the interest of public service, with the prior approval of the senior officer next to the competent authority, the officer mentioned in the following table is being posted to the post indicated in column number-4 before his name, either administratively or by transfer on request, in accordance with the provisions of section 4 (4) (2) and section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005.
