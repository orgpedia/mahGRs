Beed, Siddheshwar Pratishthan, Beed, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the institution to G. Beed was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Primary School to Siddheshwar Pratishthan, Beed, T.G. Beed on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) In case of transfer of signature of the Chairman/ Secretary of both the institutions, Rs. Notarized Consolidated Agreement should be submitted on a stamp paper of Rs.100/-.
(3) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff who have consented to transfer in the school will be accommodated and their service conditions will not be changed. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) The school will be required to submit a xerox copy of the Aadhaar card or list of Aadhaar numbers of the students.

(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
(8) Since the said English medium school is permanently unaided, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(10) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(11) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
