The Proposal:
In the Maharashtra Legislature, Mr. Kishore Patil and other members of the Legislative Assembly had raised attention to the irregularities in the issuance of school numbers to teachers/ non-teaching staff in seven educational institutions in Jalgaon district. During the discussion on this topic, Shri. Minister of Education. In the House, it was assured that disciplinary action will be initiated against the concerned after investigating all the school numbers issued in the last one year in the Nashik division and a thorough investigation will be conducted regarding the school numbers issued in the last one year in the state.
Accordingly, an inquiry committee was formed under the decision of the government to investigate the irregularities in the issuance of school numbers in the Nashik division as well as in the state.
However, considering the scope of the committee's work and the problems that would arise, the matter of reconstituting the committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Inquiry Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated August 14, 2020 to investigate the irregularities in the issuance of school marks to teachers and non-teaching staff in private aided primary, secondary and higher secondary schools in Nashik Division and in the last one year in the State is being reconstituted as follows.
