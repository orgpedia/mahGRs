The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 04.08.2017, 23.02.2018 and 02.05.2018, the above mentioned post has been appointed in direct service in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-B cadre for a period of 2 years.
The proposal to complete the probationary period has been submitted vide letter dated 27.07.2022 from the Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
As per the prevailing policy of the Government, the officers mentioned in the accompanying proforma working in the post of Resident Officer in Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva (Education Empowerment Branch) under the Government Decree dated 04.08.2017 dated 23.02.2018 and dated 02.05.2018 mentioned above, who are fulfilling the conditions of probationary period, are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in the Rakana No. 7 before their names.
