The Proposal:
As per the Government Order dated 27.03.2017 of the Department of School Education and Sports, the post of Senior Advocate in Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre has been appointed for a period of 2 years.
The proposal to complete the period of probation has been submitted vide letter dated 16.09.2022 from the Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
Shri Ganesh Sakharam Shinde, working as Senior Advocate in Group-A cadre of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva (Education Empowerment Branch), as per the Government decision dated 27.03.2017, mentioned above, who is serving the term of probation as per the prevailing policy of the Government, has been appointed as Senior Advocate.
The period of probation is being completed on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 7 before the name and the Government is granting approval to continue their service on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 8.
