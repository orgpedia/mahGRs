Preamble: Rozabag Education Society, Primary English School, Karimnagar, Pishor Road, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the permanent unaided primary and upper primary school of Kannada, G. Aurangabad to Sara Education and Welfare Society, Aurangabad was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Sara Education and Welfare Society, Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(2) The eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff of the school shall be accommodated and there shall be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) As the school is a permanent English medium unaided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.

(8) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(9) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (1) and (2), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, after examining all the aspects and including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions, the school shall be allowed to open the school.
Further action on the change of management should be taken within 4 weeks and the government should be informed about the action taken.
