Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of English Medium Primary School (Class 1st to 7th), Udaan English Medium School, Dindori, G. Nashik, run by Vindhya Education Foundation, Dindori, to Balesh Education Society, Nashik on self-financing basis was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Balesh Education Society, Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
