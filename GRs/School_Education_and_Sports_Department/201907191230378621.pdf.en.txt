Government's decision:
1) Under the School Nutrition Scheme, the following meals are being provided to the students of primary and upper primary classes and the revised rates for the same are being approved.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| The Beneficiary Group      | The amount of food                   | Food expenditure per beneficiary per day (Rs.) |
| Primary (1st to 5th)       | 450 calories and 12 grams of protein | 4.48                                           |
| Upper Primary (6th to 8th) | 700 calories and 20 grams of protein | 6.71                                           |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Under the School Nutrition Scheme, rice along with other cereals are supplied at the school level in rural areas. It is being approved to divide the food expenditure earmarked for the primary and upper primary beneficiaries for making food from these foodgrains as follows.
Distribution of food expenditure in rural areas
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| the beneficiary   | Food expenses (Rs.) | Cost of Food (Rs.) | Fuel and Vegetables (Rs.) |
| primary           | Rs. 4.48/           | Rs. 2.82/-         | Rs. 1.66/-                |
| the high-priority | Rs. 6.71/-          | Rs. 4.22/          | Rs. 2.49.                 |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Under this scheme, prepared food cooked by NGOs/ SHGs in urban areas i. e. Municipal Corporations, Municipalities is supplied to the students in schools.
