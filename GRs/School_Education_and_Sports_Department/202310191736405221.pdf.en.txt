The Proposal:
The defined contribution pension scheme applicable to teachers and non-teaching staff of Zilla Parishad and recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools in the state has been included in the National Pension Scheme of the Center. PFX Document Signer Certificate is required to start the NPS facility in Shalarth system. Accordingly, a proposal to procure PFX Document Signature Certificate for NPS under e-Governance Programme from e-Mudhra Company was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the request made by the Commissioner, Education, State of Maharashtra vide letter under reference, e-Mudhra Ltd., a company registered under the Ministry of Electronics& Information Technology, Government of India, is being approved to disburse an amount of Rs. 38,348/- by giving administrative approval for purchase of PFX Document Signer Certificate for NPS Module for a period of next 3 years.
The expenditure should be met from the demand number E-2, 2202 General Education, Scheme (02) (51) e-Governance Programme (2202H454) 31 Subsidiary Grants under 80-General Five Year Plans from the available allocation for the year 2023-24 under this head. The Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune has been declared as the Controlling Officer and the Assistant Director (Accounts), Directorate of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, has been declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
