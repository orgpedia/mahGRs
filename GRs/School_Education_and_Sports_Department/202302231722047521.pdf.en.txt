The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education (RTE) Act, 2009 provides for reservation of at least 25% of the total number of seats in private unaided schools at the entry level for children belonging to weaker sections. Further, Section 12 (2) of the said Act provides for reimbursement of educational fees of students admitted under RTE 25% by the State Government. Accordingly, the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune has requested for the release of funds in the aforesaid Reference No. 5 has been sent to the government. For the academic year 2022-2023, Rs. A sum of Rs. 200 crore has been earmarked in the budget. 84 crore to the government. 21.10.2022 has been distributed. At present, the Finance Department has released Rs. 36 crore. Therefore, as per the request made by the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune, the matter of disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 36.00/-crore towards reimbursement of educational fees of the student who has been given RTE 25% admission is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
In accordance with the provision made under Section-12 (1) (c) and Section 12 (2) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, the RTE has sanctioned Rs. 36.00/-Crore (Rupees Thirty Six Crores only) to the Director of Education (Primary), State of Maharashtra, Pune.
