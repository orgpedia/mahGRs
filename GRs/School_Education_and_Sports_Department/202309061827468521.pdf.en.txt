Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is as under. (2) The Government decision herein has been made effective as of March 1, 2014. Accordingly, the educational institutions, local/ civic bodies, schools of local self-government institutions, junior colleges, ashramshalas of Tribal Development Department, divisional/ district/ taluka sports institutions of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act, 1950 or the Societies Registration Act, 1860 are provided financial assistance in the ratio of 75:25 (75% of the government and 25% of the eligible institution concerned) for the construction of sports facilities. In this regard, No. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraphs 4 (a) and (b) of the Government Decision. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune has submitted the proposals of eligible institutions to the Government under Reference No. 3 along with recommendation for grant of administrative approval for financial assistance for construction of sports facilities. Following the recommendations, the government took the decision. Subject to the terms and conditions of 1.3.2014, the proposal for grant of financial assistance for construction of sports facilities was under consideration of the Government for grant of in-principle administrative approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In principle, administrative approval is being given to the proposal for providing financial assistance under the State Government Grant-in-Aid (75%) as per Pillar-6 for the project for construction of sports facilities mentioned in Pillar-3, subject to compliance of the terms and conditions of eligibility for grant and sanction of grant as mentioned in the Government Decision, dated 01.03.2014 and to examine the budgetary cost of the project and to consider the expenditure limit prescribed subject to other conditions.
