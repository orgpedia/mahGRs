The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of Chandwad, G. Nashik to the Institute was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Shrikarbhai Mangalchand Jain Education Welfare Society, Waghdardi, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Chandwad, G. Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of a change of management of the school.
(2) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(3) The number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools shall be as many as the posts are permissible and not more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.
It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy regarding filling up of vacancies from the permissible posts by additional staff.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.

(7) If any fresh complaints are received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution prior to the change of management have been breached by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nashik Division, Nashik, after examining all the aspects and incorporating the above terms and conditions, should take further action on the change of management of the school within 4 weeks and inform the government about the action taken.
