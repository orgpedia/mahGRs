Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan with 60% funding by the Central Government and 40% by the State Governments. The salary of the officers and employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state is paid from this. The Government was considering the issue of disbursement of admissible funds under the State Share (General) Account head 2202 I 612 for payment of salary to the posts working for the said scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Out of the provision made in the budget under the target of 01, salary for the salary of regular teachers in the District Institute of Education and Training of the state and to meet the expenditure on the salary of other employees whose salary was from the service system, Rs. 1,76,87, 400/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Six Lakh Eighty Seven Thousand Four Hundred only). This amount is being disbursed as per the revised estimates approved by the Department of Finance under the head of accounts mentioned below.
