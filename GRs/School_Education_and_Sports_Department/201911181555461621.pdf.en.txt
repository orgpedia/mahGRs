Government Resolution, School Education Department, No.Isaisha 1095/ (278/95)/ Mashi-8, dated 26th September, 1995 2) Preamble of Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Pune vide No.Saisha/ 2017-18/ K.Vina Anu/ 303/3954-174, dated 18.06.2018:
The government has decided to start private schools in the state. (1) This is permitted by the Government Order dated 26th September, 1995. The Director of Education (Secondary& Higher Secondary), Pune vide letter dated 18.06.2018 has informed that Shri Durgamata Multi-purpose Sports and Educational Institute, Chincholi-2 is running Prithviraj Deshmukh Girls Sainik School, Dharmapuri, Dist. A proposal has been sent to the government to sanction an additional batch of class 5 to G. Parbhani School on a permanent basis on a non-subsidized basis. In pursuance of this proposal, the Government was considering sanctioning an additional batch of Class 5 to the school in the academic year 2019-20 and thereafter to Class 6 to Class 10 next year on a self-financing basis.
Government's decision:
Shri Durgamata Multi-purpose Sports and Educational Institute, Chincholi-2 operated by Prithviraj Deshmukh Girls Military School, Dharmapuri, Dist. G. Parbhani School is being given permission by the Government from the year 2019-20 onwards for additional batch up to Class 10th from this academic year to Class 5th and thereafter to Class 6th next year on self-financing basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The batch sanctioned under this Order shall not be entitled to any grant.
2) The teaching/ non-teaching staff required for this batch should be provided by the institution from its own funds on a permanent basis. The government will not be responsible in future.
