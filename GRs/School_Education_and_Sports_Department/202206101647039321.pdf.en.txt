Introduction: The South Indian Association, Dombivali runs D.S.I.A. Junior College, Dombivali (Pt. Higher Secondary Classes (Class 11th to 12th- Arts, Science and Commerce stream) of Thane English Medium Aided School, MIDC, Dombivli (PU. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the G.T. to Thane was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has shifted the higher secondary classes from the inadequate building to the building full of physical facilities at MIDC, Dombivali (St. It is proposed to be shifted to Thane. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) Even after the transfer of the above classes, the administrative work will continue from the original school.
Also, the institution cannot demand an independent principal.
