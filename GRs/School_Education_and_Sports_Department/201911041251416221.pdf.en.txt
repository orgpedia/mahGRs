The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 16.10.2015, an Executive Committee under the Chairmanship of Joint Director (Sports) was constituted to prepare and implement an action plan to ensure maximum participation of players from Maharashtra in the Olympic Games to be held in Tokyo, Japan in 2020 and to ensure that Maharashtra gets at least 20 medals. The committee decided to prepare an action plan for the selection of potential talent in 16 selected sports of Maharashtra for the 2020 Olympics by determining the eligibility criteria and finalizing the selection of the athletes.
The Mission 2020 Olympic Executive Committee met on 6/8/2019. After reviewing the progress of the sports performance of the probables, the Committee has recommended for category wise selection of 52 sportspersons for allocation of funds during the financial year 2019-2020. The matter of sanctioning financial assistance to these players was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2019-20, out of the budgetary provision for the Scheme for Improving the Quality of Special Education in Sports Skills and Techniques at the National Level (Olympic Mission),
