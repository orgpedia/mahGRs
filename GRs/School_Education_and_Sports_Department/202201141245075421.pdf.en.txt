Introduction: Jai Khanderao Maharaj Multipurpose Institute, Dhule runs Shri Vinay Primary School, Kapdane, T.G. Dhule of Marathi medium partially aided primary (1st to 4th grade) school, Nanasaheb Yuvraj Patil Multipurpose Institute, Kapdane, T.G. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of G. Dhule to this institution was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Nanasaheb Yuvraj Patil Multipurpose Institute, Kapdane, T.G. Dhule on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) The institution has submitted an affidavit to the government that the classes of 5th to 7th classes started by the school have been closed. Accordingly, classes functioning on U-DIS and Saral system should be revised from 1st to 4th instead of 1st to 7th.
(6) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated.
