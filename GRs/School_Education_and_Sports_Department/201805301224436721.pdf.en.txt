The proposal was submitted by the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department to the School Education Department to grant the equivalency of Class 10 to students who have not passed Class 10 and have completed business courses from Industrial Training Institutes.
A decision in this regard is being taken at the government level after studying all the aspects through a committee of officials from both the departments.
Government Decision: The Government hereby approves grant of equivalency to the students passing out of Industrial Training Institute with vocational subjects in Class 10 and Class 12. The procedure for grant of equivalency to Class 10 and Class 12 will be as mentioned below.
1) Students who have not passed the 10th class and I. in the T.I.

2) Students who have passed 10th class. 2 years Business Course in which the entry qualification is 10th pass. Those who pass from the state board will be given credits for the equivalency of the E-12th certificate.
3) For this, all ITIs in the state will be required to obtain board certificates from the state board on the lines of secondary schools.
