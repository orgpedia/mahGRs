Introduction: Balaji Yuva Krida Mandal, Shri Samarth School (Former name- Balaji Marathi Primary School) Kasarkheda, Gram Panchayat Sheel, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of Akola Marathi medium primary and upper primary unaided school to Shri Gajanan Maharaj Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, G. Washim was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions:
(1) The consent for the transfer of the said school shall be signed by the President and all the members of the Balaji Youth Sports Board, G. Akola, and a sum of Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(2) The consent for the transfer of the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the said school shall be required to be verified by the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Amravati Division, Amravati on the basis of the original consent letter.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of a school where the management has changed.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their service conditions shall not be changed. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(6) It will be necessary to submit the information of the school set approval copy, Saral.

(7) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(8) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff than that.
(9) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(11) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.

Clause No. After the fulfillment of (1), (2), (4), (5) and (6), the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Amravati Division, Amravati, after examining all the aspects and including other necessary conditions, should take necessary further action regarding the change of management of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
