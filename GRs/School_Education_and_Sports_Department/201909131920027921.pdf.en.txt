Introduction: Shri Bhagwant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Mandegaon, Ta. Barshi, New Secondary School, Mandegaon, Dist. Barshi, G. Solapur, Aided Secondary School, Belgaum, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter regarding relocation to Barshi, G. Solapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing rented inadequate building to a self-sufficient building. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions:
1) Plastering of school buildings and roofing of toilets should be completed.
(2) The condition of minimum marks in respect of migrant school will not be relaxed for any reason.
3) Government grants to schools, teachers/ non-teaching staff, non-salaried, students, etc.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original government permission and approval regarding the school.
5) The school will function on the same basis as it was functioning at the original location.
