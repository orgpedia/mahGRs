The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) had issued an advertisement on 21.10.2010 for the recruitment of 74 posts of Education Officer and similar posts. Accordingly, the Commission had conducted the examination and sent the recommendation of eligible candidates to the Government vide letter dated 16.02.2013. The appointment to the post of Education Officer was made by the Government Decision dated 13.06.2013 subject to the examination of educational qualifications and other documents submitted by Mr. Ashok Nanasaheb Kadus.
Dattatraya Karbhari Gunjal had complained to the government that Mr. Kadus, the education officer, had submitted a false affidavit that he had two children when he entered government service despite having three children. Based on the complaint, Shri.
Ashok Nanasaheb Kadus was dismissed from Government service vide order dated 02.08.2017 for having three children on 7.5.2010. Against the said dismissal order, Shri Ashok Nanasaheb Kadus has submitted a representation to the Government vide application dated 10.08.2017. In pursuance of Shri Kadus submission, Section 6 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Affidavit of Small Family) Rules, 2005 states that" Notwithstanding anything contained in these rules, the Government may, in such circumstances as it may deem just and equitable and in such manner as may be prescribed, relax any of the provisions of this rule by recording in writing the reasons for the relaxation. " In accordance with this provision, with the approval of the appointing authority, after giving written reasons, Mr. Ashok Nanasaheb Kadus, the dismissed education officer, dated 08.12.2017

As per the order of the Government of the day, Mr. Ashok Nanasaheb Kadus, Education Officer, the period from the date of his dismissal to the date of his re-joining the Government service was under the consideration of the Government. The government has issued an order in this regard:
Government order:
The period of 5 months and 17 days from the date of direct dismissal as per the order of dismissal of Shri Ashok Nanasaheb Kadus, Education Officer, i.e., from 5th August, 2017 till the date of actual joining as per the order of reinstatement i.e., 22nd January, 2018 shall not be counted for any service purpose.
