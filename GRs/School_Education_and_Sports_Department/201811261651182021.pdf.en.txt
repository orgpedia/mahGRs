the prelude
Under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan scheme, the ratio of share of Central and State Governments (Centre: State) for the activities of Girls Hostel, Vocational Education for the ongoing programme of Purvashrami Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan is 60:40. The Central Government vide order dated 11/09/2018 has released the first instalment (all of Rs. 29,08,08, 000/-+ SAP Rs. 3,93,000/- (Rs. 29,12,01, 000/- (Rupees Thirty-nine crore twelve lakh one thousand only) has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, the Government of India has launched the Purvashrami Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (PMSSY) for providing residential and vocational education to girls. 2 The State Project Coordinator, Samagra Shiksha, Mumbai, has proposed to sanction the funds of the State Account in proportion to the funds deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State as per the letter herein. 3 submitted to the government. This amount has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State, the Finance Division of the Finance Department said in an informal reference No. 466/ Orthopay, Dy. Comments have been made under 15/10/2018. The State share of this fund is Rs. 19,38,72, 000/-+ SAP Rs. 2,62,000/- (Rs. 19,41,34, 000/- (Rupees nineteen crore forty one lakh thirty four thousand only).
