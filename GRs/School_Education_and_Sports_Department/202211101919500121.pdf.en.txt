Introduction: Established in the year 1914, Shri Hanuman Gymnasium Prasarak Mandal, Amravati is a sports organization having developed various sports facilities such as Ananta Sports Temple, Indoor Stadium, Open Stadium, Various Sports Grounds, Swimming Pool, 10m Shooting Range, Auditorium. The college is accredited by NAAC and is accredited by NAAC. This college is getting grant from the government. The Association has also requested the Government to grant recognition to the Degrees College of Physical Education, Amravati, as a" Sports University" and provide financial assistance for the same. According to the demand of the organization. The Minister for Sports and Youth Welfare has directed to form a committee under the chairmanship of Commissioner, Amaravati and submit a report. The government had constituted a committee for the purpose.
Government Decision: It is hereby approved to constitute the following committee to examine and study the demand for recognition of Sri Hanuman Gymnasium Prasarak Mandal, Amravati run Degree College of Physical Education, Amravati as a" Sports University" and to submit a complete report to the Government regarding the related issues.
