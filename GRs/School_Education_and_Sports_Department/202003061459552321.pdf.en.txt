The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Mumbai-400 052 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the dilapidated and dangerous building of the above school, the school's Emerald Building, near Shamrao Vitthal Bank, S.V. Road, Khar (Pt. ), Mumbai- 400 052 has been proposed to be relocated temporarily. The temporary relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) It shall be ensured that the consent of the parents is obtained for the transfer of the said school. Further, Rs. 100/- should be taken by the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
(2) The migration shall be for a period of not less than three years and the institution shall be required to construct a suitable building at the place of origin in the meantime and shall be required to obtain prior approval of the Government before shifting to the place of origin.
(3) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
