Introduction: The Secondary Book Bank Scheme has been implemented for the students of recognized and aided secondary schools and government secondary schools run by private as well as district councils, municipal corporations, municipalities and municipal councils in the state. Under the scheme, textbooks are provided to eligible students on a reimbursement basis within a limit of 25 per cent of the student strength and are withdrawn at the end of the academic year. All students belonging to Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Tribes and economically weaker sections are eligible for the scheme. Under this scheme, the set of books given under the scheme is changed every 3 years and new copies are given instead.
