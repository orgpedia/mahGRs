Organizing and participating in All India Civil Services Competition.

Date: 17th of October, 2022
The All India Civil Services Competitions are conducted annually by the Central Board of Civil Services, New Delhi in various sports and cultural sports disciplines. These tournaments are organized by the various state governments under the direct control of the Central Sports Board. Maharashtra government teams in various sports and cultural categories are selected and sent to participate in the competition. Also, the Secretariat Gymkhana organizes All India Civil Competitions in the state from time to time.
The Secretariat Gymkhana has been entrusted with the responsibility of selecting the teams of various sports and cultural sports of the government for this competition, training and organizing these competitions in the state. Accordingly, the Secretariat Gymkhana conducts selection trials for selection of teams for All India Civil Services Competition as well as training camps for selected sportspersons.
Accordingly, the expenditure required for selection trials, training camps and participation in team competitions is sanctioned by the State Government to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
However, no official order has been issued yet in this regard. It was under consideration of the government to issue orders in this regard and to appoint Secretariat Gymkhana as the coordinating mechanism/ institution for the selection and practice/ training of various sports and cultural teams of the Government of Maharashtra for participation in this competition. The government has taken the following decisions:
The Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai is being appointed as the coordinating mechanism for selection of Maharashtra Government teams in various sports and cultural disciplines for the" All India Civil Services Tournament" organized by the Central Board of Civil Services Cultural and Sports, New Delhi. The expenditure of the participating teams is being approved by the School Education and Sports Department of the state government.
All India Civil Services Competitions are organized by the Central Government or by various States. If these competitions are to be held in the state, for this, Ma. Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports, Shri. Minister (Finance). A decision in this regard will be taken by the Chief Minister.
