The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Examination Council, Pune conducts the examination for the recruitment of candidates to the post of Clerk-Typist in Central/ State Government/ Paramilitary Offices and Private Establishments. It is mandatory for the employees of government/ semi-government services to obtain typing qualification as per the prevailing policy of the General Administration Department. For some years, computers have been used in office work instead of typewriters.
Reference No. Under the decision of the government here, the government has approved to start computer typing courses as follows.
Basic Course in Computer Typing Marathi/ English/ Hindi 40 S.P.M. (6 months) 3) Special Skill in Computer Typing for Instructors and Students (3 months)
Also, the nature of the above course and the scoring of the examination were determined as per Annexure-I. In view of the demand of the institute owners and the need to change the syllabus from time to time, the Maharashtra State Council of Examination has proposed to change the present syllabus and marking system. 2 has been submitted to the Government for approval.
Therefore, the government was considering a change in the curriculum and marking system.
