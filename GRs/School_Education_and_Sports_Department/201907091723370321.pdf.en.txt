Also, in case of any court case in future, the institution transferring the school and the institution accepting the transfer will be jointly responsible. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of both the organizations have contributed Rs. A notarized bond of Rs 100/- will be required to be taken.
(3) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be ensured that the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for the transfer of the school. Also, the eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(5) No financial transaction has taken place in the transfer of the said school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff.

The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(11) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
