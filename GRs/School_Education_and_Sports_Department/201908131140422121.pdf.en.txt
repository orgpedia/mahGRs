The Proposal:
As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government in the public interest to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them prematurely in Government service. As per the said policy, the Government has decided to constitute a committee to review the services of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers under the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services as follows:
Government's decision:
A Committee is being constituted to review the services of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre Officers under the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services as under:
