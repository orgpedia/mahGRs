GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF SCHOOL EDUCATION AND SPORTS, NO. IEDS-2016/ (Q. No. 21/16)/ TNT-5, dated 19th October, 2016.
Introduction: The Central Government has started the Disabled Integrative Education Scheme (Primary Level) since 1978 to enable students with disabilities with special needs to study in normal schools along with the general children.
The 71 special educators appointed under the scheme and working on the secondary school unit were in arrears due to non-availability of grants due to some technical reasons. The government was considering making provisions for payment of arrears.
Government's decision:
A grant of Rs. 4.40 crore (Rupees Four Crore Forty Lakh only) is being sanctioned for meeting the outstanding salary expenditure of 71 Special Educators working under the Disabled Integrated Schooling Scheme (Primary Level) for the period from March, 2017 to May, 2019.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune shall take the following action while paying the salary to the said special teachers:
(a) Only 40% of the monthly allowable salary of the Special Educators for the period from March 2017 to May 2019 shall be paid.
All aspects regarding appointment/ recognition of these special educators should be ensured. Only the period worked should be paid as mentioned in 2 (a) above, after ascertaining whether the unit starts during the pay period or how and whether they have worked during the pay period or not.

If the salary has been paid to the special teachers earlier in pursuance of a court case, the balance amount shall be paid by adjusting the amount from the amount admissible at the end of the consideration as mentioned in clause (a) above.
(c) to submit to the Government a report on the balance of funds for the period from March, 2017 to May, 2019 for payment of salaries as mentioned in clause (a) above.
Prior approval of the government should be obtained before utilising the funds.
d) Ensure that the salary of special educators is not paid twice in any period. Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune will be held responsible for the excess payment.
e) The report on the salary paid should be submitted to the government within one month.
The said expenditure shall be met from the provision available in the financial year 2019-20 under the head Demand No. E-2, 2202- General Education, 01 Elementary Education, 106 Teachers and other services, (00) (00) (01) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, 2202I 612, General, State Share 40%, Programmes, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries). For this, the Director of Education (Primary) is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
4. The said Government decision has been taken under the Informal Reference No. 134/1471 of Planning Department dated 18.06.2019 and Informal Reference No. 490/19/ Expenditure-5 of Finance Department dated 29.07.2019.
It is being issued as per the approval.
