GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government, the officers mentioned in the table below, who have completed the period of probation as on the date mentioned above, are appointed as Superintendents in Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) under the Government Decisions dated 04.08.2017, 25.09.2017, 23.02.2018 and 02.05.2018.
