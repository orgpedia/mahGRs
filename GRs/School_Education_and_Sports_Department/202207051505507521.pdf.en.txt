Introduction: Blue Sky Social Multipurpose Institute, Latur operates Lavesh English School, Latur, G. Latur, an English medium self-financed primary school (class 1st to 8th), Group No. The proposal received under the letter was under consideration of the government to relocate it to 24, Behind Siddheshwar Temple, Malvati Road, Hanmantwadi, T.G. Latur.
Government Decision: Due to the termination of the lease of the current location of the above school, the place of the institution owners in Group No. The school is proposed to be shifted to 24, Behind Siddheshwar Temple, Malvati Road, Hanmantwadi, T.G. Latur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
