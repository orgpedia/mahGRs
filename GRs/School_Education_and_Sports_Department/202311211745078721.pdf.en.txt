Date of release: November 21, 2023
Preamble: In order to create a sense of responsibility towards the school among the teachers, parents and students of all the management and all the medium schools of the state and to provide a pleasant and inspiring environment for the students to learn from the competitive environment, the government is considering to implement the campaign" Mukhyamantri Majhi School, Sundar School" under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Model School Scheme. A State-level Steering Committee for the formulation, monitoring and control of the Mission and a State-level Executive Committee for effective implementation of the Mission were also under consideration.
Government Decision: Under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Adarsh Vidyalaya Yojana, the Government has approved the formation of a State Level Steering Committee for the formulation and monitoring and control of the' Mukhyamantri Majhi School, Sunder School" campaign and a State Level Executive Committee for the effective implementation of the campaign.
