Preamble: The Scheme of Award of Scholarship to encourage the best cadets (boys and girls) of the Senior and Junior wings of National Cadet Corps in the State of Maharashtra was started under the Government Decision Social Welfare, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Tourism Department No:- NCC- 1973/30617-B, dated 21/10/1974. The order was subsequently amended. The reference No. 1 The matter of increasing the rate of scholarship award to the best student soldiers (boys and girls) of the National Student Army, which has been approved under the government decision here, is under consideration.
Government Decision: It is being approved to increase the rate of scholarship award for the best student soldiers of the National Student Army as follows.

2. The revised rates of scholarship for the best cadets (boys and girls) of the National Cadet Corps should be implemented from the academic year 2019-20.
The selection of the National Cadet Soldiers (Boys and Girls) for the award of scholarships shall be made by the concerned after evaluating the performance of the National Cadet Soldiers during the academic year from 1st June to 31st May.
After the recommendations of the Group Heads of the Group Headquarters of all the National Cadet Corps, the Deputy Director General, Directorate of National Cadet Corps, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall submit a proposal to the Government through the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune for the award of scholarship by selecting the best National Cadet Corps (boys and girls) within the prescribed limit.
Expenditure in respect of Demand No.5. E-3, 2204-Sports and Youth Services-102 Youth Welfare Programme for Students (01) (01) National Cadet Corps, Non-Plan, Sub-Scholarships/ Scholarships (2204 0016-34) to be made from the grants sanctioned in that year under the head of account.
