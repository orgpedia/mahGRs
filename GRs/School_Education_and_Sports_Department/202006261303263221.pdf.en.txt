Preamble: Due to the outbreak of Corona virus (COVID-19) in the state, the situation has become adverse. In such a situation, the government has decided to start the academic year on time. Commencement of academic session 2020-21 The guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision dated 15th June 2020. Responsibilities of various departments have been defined. In the appendix of this government decision, instructions have been given about what action should be taken within a fortnight of the school pre-preparation. The government was trying to give more clarity on the issue.
1.1 Public transport in Mumbai, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Palghar districts of the state has been started for essential services only. This can make it difficult for teachers to attend school. In view of this, the work from home concession is being given to the teachers.
However, for school readiness and e-learning, principals should attend two days a week, if called.
1.2 Female teachers, those with serious ailments like diabetes, respiratory disorders, blood pressure, heart disease, etc. and male teachers above 55 years of age are being given temporary work from home concession without calling them to school till the period of commencement of physical school.
1.3 Further, the concerned Headmaster/ School Management Committee shall take a decision as per the situation to call such teachers to the school whose attendance is mandatory. Such teachers should preferably not be called more than once or twice a week and all teachers should not be called to school on the same day.
1.4 Regarding the release of teachers whose services have been requisitioned for Covid related work, the concerned education officer should contact the local administration and release them.
