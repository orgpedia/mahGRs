Preamble: Banasraj Singh Thakur Education Society, Nagpur operates Thakur's Green Valley Primary School, Mou. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the Marathi medium aided primary school at Bakulkhed, Narendra Nagar to Old Tuesday, Sriharinagar Layout, Dhoble Jal, near Dnyaneshwar Mandir, Gangabai Ghat Road, T. G. Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the above school building being dangerous, the Nagpur Municipal Corporation and the landlord have issued a notice to vacate the building, the students are shifting to the rented building for safety reasons. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid place is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The students of the said primary school shall be accommodated at the place of relocation and no complaint shall be entertained in this regard. The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur should ensure this.
(2) Any change in the name of the school shall require correction in the records and on the computer systems of the Education Department. This should be verified by the Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur.
(3) The merger of the two companies. It should be ensured that the name of the new institution is registered by verifying the orders of the Charity Commissioner's office.
(4) It should be verified that the school building at the place of relocation is a long-term (at least 5 years) lease agreement.
(5) Personal endorsements of teachers shall be verified by the Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur.
(6) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(7) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(8) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(10) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(11) The school shall function at the place of relocation on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(12) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(13) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (1), (2), (3), (4), (5), (7) and (8), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall take necessary further action regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the Government about the action taken.
