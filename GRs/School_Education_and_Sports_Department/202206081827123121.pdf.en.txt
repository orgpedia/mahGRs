the prelude
The Government is planning to give cash awards to the medal winners of Olympic, Commonwealth and Asian Games. Accordingly, the government was considering to honor the medal winning athletes of Maharashtra and their mentors with cash awards for their outstanding performance at the international level.
Government's decision:
Under the Sports Policy 2012 of the State of Maharashtra, the Government is approving to sanction special cash awards to the medal winning players and their mentors of the State of Maharashtra for their outstanding performance at the international level and for bringing glory to the State.
