This Marathi medium (8th to 10th aided, and 11th to 12th, partially aided) junior college and Shri Babasaheb Dhabekar Secondary School Yaverdi, Ta. Karanja, G. Washim, Marathi medium aided secondary (E. Class 8th to 10th) School, Paramhansa Sant Jholebaba Bahu. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the management of the institution to the Educational Board, Y (Karanja) G. Washim was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of these schools is Paramhans Sant Jholebaba Bahu. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the Board of Education, Wai (Karanja), to G. Washim on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the change of management has taken place shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.

(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati, shall examine all the matters and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
