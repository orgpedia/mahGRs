Government Resolution Shri Prakash Janardan Pawar, Vishwagunj Pictures, Pune has requested vide letter dated 23/06/2023 to allow screening of Marathi film" Baloch" in schools of the State. According to No. 3 The report of the Committee constituted for this purpose has been received.
A historically well-crafted Marathi film on the valour, courage and achievements of the Marathas who fought across the border,' Baloch' will be screened in schools across the state. This Marathi film is being allowed to be shown to the students/ girl students in the schools of the state for one year in the academic year 2023-24 subject to the following conditions.
Conditions: 1) The film" Baloch" is being allowed to be screened in schools of the state only for students above the age of 10 years.
No student will be forced to watch the film.
3) Ensure that students do not miss out on the course.
The Producer, Vishwagunj Pictures, Pune shall not enter into any agreement with any other organization or appoint any representative for the screening of the said film on the basis of the permission of this Government and shall not be permitted to do so.
Students who wish to watch the film will not be charged more than Rs.20/- (Rupees Twenty only).
