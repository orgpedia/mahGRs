Preamble: Hirabai Gaikwad Educational Institution, Poh, Ta. Kalmeshwar, G. Nagpur, Principal Arunrao Kalode Vidyalaya, Khamla, Nagpur, a Marathi medium aided secondary (class 5th to 10th) school, was under the consideration of the Government for shifting to Nageshwar Nagar, Bidgaon, Nagpur.
Government Decision: The above school has been proposed to be shifted after the landlord informed about the demolition of the building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above-mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
