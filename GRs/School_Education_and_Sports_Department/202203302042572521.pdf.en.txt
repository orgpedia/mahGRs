The Proposal:
Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan with 60% funding by the Central Government and 40% by the State Governments. The salary of the officers and employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state is paid from this. For the scheme
The matter of disbursement of funds admissible under State Share (General) Account Head 2202 I 612 for payment of salary for the working posts was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
To meet the expenditure on the salary of regular teachers and other employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state, whose salary was from the service system, a provision available with the approval of the Finance Department under the target of 01, salary is Rs. 9,20,13, 804/- (Rupees Nine Crore Twenty Lakh Thirteen Thousand Eight Hundred and Four only) is being approved for disbursement.
