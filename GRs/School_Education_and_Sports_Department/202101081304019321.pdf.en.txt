The proposal: self. Rajaramji Dubey Multi-purpose Educational Institute, Trimurti Nagar, Nagpur N.K. International Public School, Bazargaon, Dist. G. Nagpur English Medium Self-Financed Primary School, Tribal Multi-Purpose Development Institute, Deoli (Kalbande), Dist. The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the institution to Hingna, G. Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The School, Tribal Multi-purpose Development Institute, Deoli (Kalbande), Ta. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to Hingna, G.Nagpur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred school shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(3) As the school is self-financing English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(4) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the transferred school.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Order, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such transfer or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
