However, in the case of such cases. Petitions in court The following amendments are now being made in accordance with the judgment of the court.

If they are not received within the prescribed time, the concerned organization will be notified by the Divisional Deputy Director to submit the records within 7 days. Further, the concerned Organizing Body/ Sports Association/ concerned Government Department shall send the proofs in the prescribed format to the Deputy Director, Sports, as requested from time to time, within a period of 7 working days by e-mail, in person or by post. If the report is not received within this period, the concerned Divisional Deputy Director will not be able to send the verification report of his certificate to the player concerned and the sports organization concerned will be responsible for the loss of the player.
III. Action to be taken by the concerned Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services: Once the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services receives the application for verification of the athlete certificate, he/ she shall call for the necessary records for verification of the qualification from the concerned tournament organizing bodies/ sports associations/ government offices within 3 working days. Thereafter, based on the report received from the concerned Organizing Body/ Sports Association/ concerned Government Department, the Deputy Director, Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services shall take action within 8 working days to inform the concerned athletes stating the reasons for the issuance or rejection of the certificate. The Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services shall be bound to provide the certificate verification report to the concerned athlete within 30 days of the receipt of the certificate of the athlete for verification. The report/ decision should be uploaded on the website by the Deputy Director, Sports. Hence, in future, if the player's appointing authority asks for verification of the certificate, they can be made available immediately. In future, if there is a delay in the appointment due to the delay in the verification of the certificate of the selected player or if there is a court case, the Deputy Director, Sports, will be held responsible and disciplinary action will be taken against them.
Action to be taken by the Selection Authorities of various departments of the Government/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission: If the application received from the sportsperson category is not accompanied by the sports certificate issued by the appropriate sports authority or the copy of the application for verification of the sports certificate to the said authority, the applications should be rejected in the first phase of the examination.
