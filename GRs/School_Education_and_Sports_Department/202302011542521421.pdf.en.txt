Introduction: The salary of teachers and non-teaching staff in private partially and fully aided schools in the state is paid through the Shalarth system. The Shalarth system has been developed by Maha IT and regular salaries of teaching and non-teaching staff are paid online through this system. However, the facility of online payment of arrears of these employees, medical payments (advance and reimbursement), dues of Lokayukta and court cases and dues to be paid in 5 installments after the implementation of the revised pay structure as per the Seventh Pay Commission has not been provided by Maha IT. At present, there is no facility available in the Shalarth system to pay outstanding salary payments, medical payments, difference in salary (arrears) due after the implementation of the revised salary structure, etc. through online mode.
Government's decision:
Offline payment of regular and other outstanding dues, medical dues, difference in salary (arrears) due after the implementation of the revised pay structure, increase in dearness allowance, etc. to the working and retired teachers/ non-teaching staff of private partially and fully aided schools and teachers' schools in the state is being approved till the end of March 2023.
This includes the following types of payments:
(a) the payment of arrears of salary of the Sixth Pay Commission to the teachers and non-teaching staff of partially aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools recognized in the State in lump sum cash in the absence of provident fund or national pension or defined contributory pension scheme. The Government decision has been issued on 17.02.2021. Paying the dues.
