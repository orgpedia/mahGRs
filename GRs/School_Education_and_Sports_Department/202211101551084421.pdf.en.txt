In order to provide equal opportunity to all candidates for selection to the vacant posts of teachers in government and aided schools, primary, upper primary, secondary, higher secondary schools, night schools and D.L.Ed. colleges declared eligible for grant-in-aid by all local self-government institutions and private managements in the state, and to select high-quality candidates for the post of teaching staff, the recruitment of a teaching staff should be done through a computer system based on the marks obtained in the" Aptitude and Intelligence Test" (PAVITRA). 3 A transparent system has been laid down under the government decision here. In the meantime, it was observed that while implementing the recruitment process through Pavitra, there was a need to modify/ amend the said procedure and add some new provisions. For this, based on the meetings held at various levels and the proposal received from the Commissioner, Education, dated 12.10.2022, the Government has issued Decision No. CET-2015/ Q.No.149/ TNT-1, Dy. 07.02.2019, the matter was under consideration of the Government to take a decision to delete/ amend some of these provisions and add new provisions. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:

Government Decision- Government Circular No. SSC- 1398/223028/1097/ Umashi-1, Dy. 22/06/2000 and being superseded by Government Resolution No.HSC 1305/ (63/05/)/ Umashi-1, dated- 1/12/2005.
The approval of the Government is being given to amend and delete the following provisions of the Government Decision dated 07.02.2019 at No. 3.
Government Decision No.1: CET2015/ No.149/ TNT-1, D. Paragraph No. 07.02.2019 3.4 In place of the provision that" a candidate shall have a maximum of five attempts to improve his/ her score," it shall be mandatory for the candidate to appear in a fresh Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test every time. The marks obtained by the candidate in the previous test shall not be considered for fresh advertisement after the publication of the final result of the new test.
Government Decision No.2: CET2015/ P.No.149/ TNT-1, D. Paragraph No. 07.02.2019 3.6 The provision herein is hereby deleted. However, the rest of the provisions will remain in force.
The following provisions are being incorporated in respect of Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test to be conducted through the Pavitra Portal:
The age of the candidate as on the date of advertisement published by the management for recruitment to the post of Teacher shall be taken into consideration. However, the age of the candidates appearing for the Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test to be held in the year 2022 is being relaxed for 2 years in view of COVID-19.
To be eligible for the Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test, the candidate must possess minimum educational and professional qualifications.
The medium chosen by the candidate for Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test shall be limited to the Test of Proficiency. The medium of this test will not be considered for selection process.
Advertisements will be taken on the portal by the management once in three months as per the requirement for recruitment to the post after the Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test. those
