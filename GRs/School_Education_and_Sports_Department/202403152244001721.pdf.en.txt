The Proposal: -
Hiranandani Trust, Mumbai-run Hiranandani Trust School, Talegaon, Ta. Mouje Bhokarpada, Ta., of Khalapur District Raigad, an English medium self-financing Higher Secondary School (E1st to 12th).
The proposal received from the Government through a letter regarding relocation to Panvel District Raigad
was under consideration.
The Government's Decisions:
A relocation to a building with physical facilities of the above school is proposed.
Hence, the shifting of the school to the above mentioned places is being approved on the following terms and conditions. : -
(1) The condition of minimum marks of a migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Interested students will also be required to be accommodated at the relocation site.
(2) Since the school is self-financing in the English medium, the school is functional.
The entire responsibility of the teaching / non-teaching staff will rest with the institution.
(3) The Institute shall be required to comply with the Acts, Rules, Regulations, etc., as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time in respect of schools, teaching and non-teaching staff, salary / non-salary grants, students, etc.
(4) There shall be a change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
