In view of the COVID-19 pandemic, instructions have been issued regarding the changes to be made in the current financial year 2021-22. The General Administration Department has been instructed that no transfers should be made under the Transfer Act till June 30, 2021, as per the Government Decision dated May 10, 2021.
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 provides for transfer in exceptional circumstances in accordance with the provisions of Rule 4 (4) 2 and Rule 4 (5). In pursuance of the said provision, the officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 against their names, as per the approval given by the competent authority and the concerned senior officers on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board.
