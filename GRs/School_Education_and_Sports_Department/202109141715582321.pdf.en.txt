Introduction: Victoria's Kids Educare Pvt. Ltd., Kharadi, Pune is running Victoria's Kids Educare School, Pune, an English medium self-financed Higher Secondary School, Survey No. The proposal received under the reference letter for ex-post facto approval for relocation of 53, 54 and 58 shares at 2/1 A Fountain Road, Nagar Road, Kharadi, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Institute has decided to conduct the survey due to insufficient space by the above school. 53, 54 and 58 have been shifted to 2/1 A Fountain Road, Nagar Road, Kharadi. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) As the said school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
