If the concerned officer has joined the concerned post as per the order dated August 5, 2020, then these orders will not be implemented. The Commissioner (Education) should first ensure this and then issue the above orders.
The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. Those concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
Commissioner (Education), Pune, as per this order, shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Authorities about the immediate relieving of the said officers from their present charge and the transferred officers.
It should be ensured that the officers join the given place of posting within the stipulated time and submit a report to the government.
