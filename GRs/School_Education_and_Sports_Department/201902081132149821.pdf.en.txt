Preamble: The Department of General Administration has implemented the policy of transfer of Group-B officers and Group-C and D employees through counselling through a Government Resolution dated 9.4.2018. The process of transfer is to be carried out as mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 9.4.2018 of the General Administration Department. However, considering the upcoming elections, it has been decided to implement the process of transfer early. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken to publish the list of vacant posts and the list of transferable officers and to seek preferences from the transferable officers.
Government Decision: The list of transferable officers and vacancies in the following cadres of Maharashtra Education Service and General State Service (Administration Branch)/ Education Empowerment Branch, Group-B under the School Education Department is being attached with this Government Decision.

However, the transferable officers should submit the information of the preferred course in the prescribed format as per the return-1 to the Commissioner of Education by February 15, 2019 without fail. The Office of the Commissioner of Education should submit the consolidated information along with the order of preference to the Department of School Education on February 18, 2019.
Group B officers under this department who have submitted request forms in the past will not be considered. Also, the officers who do not give preference to the Commissioner of Education by February 15, 2019, will be given posting as per the urgency to fill the vacant posts of administration. Also, such officers will not be able to apply for premature transfer till the period of posting is over. Also, applications submitted by such officers will not be considered.
The officers who will specify the priority will have to attach proof of certificates and documents issued by the competent authority. If these evidences are not attached, the priority of such officers will not be taken into account. Also, while specifying the preferred order of preference, if the Revenue Department Allotment Rules are applicable to the concerned officer, such officer shall submit the preference order considering the vacancies in the Revenue Department allotted to them. Preference given to transferable officers outside the revenue division allotted to them as per the Revenue Division Allotment Rules shall not be taken into consideration.
