Circular:
Department of School Education and Sports, Government Circular, d. 8th to 12th grade. 8th to 12th grade. As per the circular dated September 24, 2021, in the academic year 2021-2022, schools from class V to class XII in rural areas and from class VIII to XII in urban areas of the state will be opened. The guidelines have been issued to start from October 4, 2021. As the corona infection is decreasing in the state, classes 1 to 4 in rural areas and classes 1 to 7 in municipal areas have been closed. Approval has been given to start from December 1, 2021. Meanwhile, in view of the increasing corona infection in the state, Reference No. Under the order of the Disaster Management Department, all schools in the state have been closed. The closure order was issued till February 15, 2022. However, the increasing prevalence of corona is not the same in all parts of the state and there is a constant talk about starting schools from those areas of the state where the incidence of corona is low.

As per the circular dated January 20, 2022, schools in the state for classes 1 to 12 will be opened after considering the local conditions. The authority to reopen schools from January 24, 2022, has been given to the Municipal Commissioner in the Municipal Corporation area and the District Disaster Management Authority and the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad in other parts of the state.
2. Corona outbreak in the State is decreasing day by day and Corona related restrictions on all establishments/ events in the State are being lifted in a phased manner. The government is now ordering the following to compensate the educational loss of students due to the corona outbreak:
2.1. Normally, schools are allowed to function in the morning session from March every year. In this academic year, instead of starting schools in the morning session from March to the end of April, schools from classes 1 to 9 and classes 11 should be opened full time.
2.2.Saturday schools to be continued full-time till the end of April. Schools can also be closed on Sundays.
2.3 The examinations for the students of classes 1 to 9 and class 11 should be held in the third week of April and the results should be declared in May.
2.4. If it is necessary to have school in the morning session, the teaching hours should be taken as full-time school.
2.5. 100% attendance of students is being allowed every day.
The powers conferred on the Municipal Commissioner in the Municipal Corporation area and the District Disaster Management Authority and the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad in other parts of the State as per the earlier circular dated January 20, 2022, shall remain intact.
