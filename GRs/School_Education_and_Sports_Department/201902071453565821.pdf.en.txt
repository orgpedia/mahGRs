Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Badminton Championship, 2018 was held at the Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai from 14th to 20th February, 2019. The exam was conducted on 14th January 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The selection test, training camp and participation of the state team as per the prospectus' B' is Rs. 82,820/- (Rupees Eighty Two Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
The Head of Department/ Head of Office concerned for participation in the tournament should relieve the Sports Coach/ Manager for the training camp from 11th to 13th February, 2019 and for the tournament from 14th to 20th February, 2019 with effect from 08th January, 2019 (M.No.) and the service secretariat of these players.
