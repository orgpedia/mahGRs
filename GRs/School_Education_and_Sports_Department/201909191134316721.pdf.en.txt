Introduction: Under the State Sports Policy 2001, the scheme of providing minimum facilities for various sports in the Taluk Sports Complex at the Taluk level, providing national level sports facilities under the District Sports Complex at the District level, as well as setting up international level sports facilities under the Divisional Sports Complex at the Divisional level has been implemented under the Government Decision dated 26th March, 2003.
As per the Government decision dated 21st March, 2009, the grant limit of Taluk Sports Complex is Rs. 25.00 lakhs to Rs. 1.00 crore, District Sports Complex grant limit is Rs. 4.00 crore to Rs. 8.00 crore and Rs. 16.00 crore to Rs. 24.00 crore.
