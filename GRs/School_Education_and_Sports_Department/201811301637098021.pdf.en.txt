Preamble: The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the permanent non-aided primary school of English medium, namely Royal Oaks World School, Devlai Chowk, Beed Bypass, Aurangabad, run by Sou. Gandharibai Pratishthan, Aurangabad, to Eden Education and Research Institute, Aurangabad, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said Primary School to Eden Education and Research Institute, Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff who have consented to transfer in the school will be accommodated and their service conditions will not be changed. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The Deputy Director of Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall ensure that the financial position of Eden Education and Research Institute, Aurangabad is good on the basis of its bank balance/ balance in the bank.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.

(7) As the said English medium school is permanently unaided, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(10) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall examine all the aspects and take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school including other necessary conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
