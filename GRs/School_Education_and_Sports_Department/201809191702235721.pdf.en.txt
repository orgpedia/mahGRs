Government Decision: Out of the vacancies in the sanctioned posts of drivers and constables for the offices of Rashtriya Chhatra Sena under the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai, 5 posts of drivers and 2 posts of constables are approved to be filled through outsourcing and to meet the expenses for the same, subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in Circular No. 1 and 2 of the Government in question.
The said approval is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the powers mentioned in the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-II, Sub-Division-IV, No. 11.
