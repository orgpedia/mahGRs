The birth anniversary of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, the architect of the Indian Constitution, is celebrated across the country on April 14. In order to make the students aware of the qualities of Dr. Ambedkar's perseverance and the importance of his social, educational, political and economic work, various programs will be organized in every school of the state on the occasion of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar's birth anniversary.
Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar was a versatile personality who brought light to the dark lives of Scheduled Castes and Tribes, migrants, labourers, oppressed and women by giving them a message of wisdom.
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of such a great man, programs like painting, monologue, video production, monologue, storytelling, essay writing, collection of rare photographs, elocution, poetry reading should be implemented level-wise/ group-wise for students of classes 1 to 12 in all management and media schools in the state.
Participate in the program and upload videos and materials on#AmbedkarJayanti2021,#drbrambedkar2021 HASHTAG (#). Also, follow us on social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram).
Teachers and parents should help them in this.
