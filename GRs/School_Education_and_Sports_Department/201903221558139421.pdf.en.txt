The Proposal:
Satara Military School was established. Established on June 23, 1961, it is the first military school in the country. The main objective of this school is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children of the bottom rung of Maharashtra at all levels to become officers in the Indian Army through the National Defence Academy. A large number of students from this school have so far been admitted to the National Defence Academy.
Satara Sainik School is sanctioned ex-gratia (non-salary) grant every year for the purpose of building maintenance, food allowance, horse maintenance, advertisement expenses, municipal taxes, etc. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between the Sainik School Society and the State Government on 30th December, 2016 regarding the Satara Sainik School. Accordingly, the expenditure on training and administrative matters is the responsibility of the State Government. Accordingly, for these matters, a total of Rs. 2,13,92, 242/- has been disbursed. For the financial year 2018-19, Satara Sainik School will get the remaining 30% i.e. Rs. 90,80,796/- was under consideration of the Government.
