Government Decision: Sadar Secondary School, Srigonda Taluka Agricultural Service Union, Chikhli, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of Mr. Shrigonda, G. Ahmednagar on the following terms and conditions: 1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of a change of management of the school.
(2) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government.
3) Padma Shri Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation, Vadgaon Gupta, Ta. It should be ensured that there is no change in the Chairman and all the members of Annexure-I of the institution in Nagar (Uladghat), G. Ahmednagar after the year 2017, as well as the consent for the transfer of the individually approved permanent teaching/ non-teaching staff of the school.
4) Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff of the school will be accommodated and their service conditions cannot be changed.
5) The school will continue to function on the same grant-in-aid basis after the transfer.
