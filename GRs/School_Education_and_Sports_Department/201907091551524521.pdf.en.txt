The Proposal:
The evaluation pattern of the Class 10 examination held in March, 2019 has been changed. The decision to discontinue internal assessment in language and sociology was taken to improve the quality of class 10 students and to ensure proper evaluation of students. However, in other boards (e.g. CBSE and I. The internal assessment of the students is underway. Therefore, there is a need to rethink the internal assessment as well as the content structure.
Government's decision
A Committee under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Education) is being constituted to study the subject structure and evaluation pattern for classes 9th to 12th from the academic year 2019-20.
