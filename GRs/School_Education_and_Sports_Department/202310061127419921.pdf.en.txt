Introduction: Ahmed Naseem Minangari Education Society, Malegaon, G. Igatpuri, G. Nasik, Urdu Medium Partially Aided Secondary School (Class 8th to 10th), Group No. 98, Malegaon, Maharashtra. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Malegaon, G. Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted as the building is dilapidated and there are no physical facilities. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
