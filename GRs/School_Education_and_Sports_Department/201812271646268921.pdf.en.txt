the prelude
The ratio of Central and State share for Vocational Education is 60:40 for the ongoing programme of Purvashrami Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan Scheme. The Central Government vide order dated 11/09/2018 has released the first instalment of Rs. 98,64,000/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Nine Lakhs Sixty Four Thousand only) has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyaan, the Central Government has taken up the ongoing Vocational Education initiative of Purvashrami Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan with reference No. 2 The State Project Coordinator, Samagra Shiksha, Mumbai has proposed to sanction the State Account Fund in proportion to the funds deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State as per the letter herein. 3 submitted to the government. This amount has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State, the Finance Division of the Finance Department said in an informal reference No. 466/ Orthopay, Dy. Comments have been made under 15/10/2018. The State share of this fund is Rs. 65,76,000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Lakhs Seventy Six Thousand only). The first instalment of Rs. 98,64,000/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Nine Lakhs Sixty Four Thousand only) and the State share of Rs. 65,76,000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Lakhs Seventy Six Thousand only) aggregating to Rs. 1,64,40, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Four Lakh Forty Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government. The government's decision is as follows:
