Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure and eligibility criteria of the institution in this regard are mentioned in the above Reference No. The Government's decision herein has been dated 01.03.2014. Accordingly, eligible institutions are provided financial assistance in the ratio of 75:25 (Government share 75% and Affiliated Institutions share 25%) for construction of sports facilities and support for sports materials. Reference No. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraphs 4 (a) and (b) of the Government Decision herein. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune has issued reference No. Under Section 3, the proposal of the eligible institution has been submitted to the government with a recommendation. Following the recommendations, the government took the decision. Subject to the terms and conditions of 1.3.2014, the proposal for grant of financial assistance for construction of sports facilities was under consideration of the Government for grant of in-principle administrative approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In principle, administrative approval is being given to the proposal for providing financial assistance to the following institutions as per the State Government's grant-in-aid limit (75%) as per Pillar-6 for the construction of sports facilities mentioned in Pillar-3 subject to the following Government Decision, dated 01.03.2014, regarding completion of eligibility for grant-in-aid, compliance of all the terms and conditions of grant-in-aid, examination of the project and budgetary cost and other relevant conditions.
