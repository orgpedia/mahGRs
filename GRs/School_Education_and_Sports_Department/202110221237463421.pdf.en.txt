School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No: Masha Sth 3321/ PR No.401/ SM-1 dated 28th July, 2021.
Reference: Letter dated 25th September, 2019 to the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur vide No. Pr/ B/ 8568/2019
Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter to shift the permanent non-aided primary (class 1st to 4th) school of English medium, Nityanand English Convent, Tajnagar, Bidgaon, Nagpur, run by Tribal Social Multi-purpose Education Society, Nagpur, to Kalamna Road, Yerkhada, Nagpur, on self-financing basis, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from an inadequate and drain-side building to a self-contained building on rent. The above migration of the said school is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) Since the shifting of the English medium school is being done on a self-financing basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school will be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
