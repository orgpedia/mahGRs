Department of School Education and Sports
Government Circular No: RTE-2018/ PR No.145/ SD-1
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
The date: November 20, 2019
Government circular:
Educational rehabilitation of the children of the martyred soldiers considering their service to the country
It is the duty of the nation. Hence, the children of the martyred soldiers are in government, aided schools
They should be given direct entry as per their demand. as well as admission to private unaided schools.
The wards of the martyred soldiers should be given priority access if they show willingness.
Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune and Director of Education (Secondary and Higher
Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned.
