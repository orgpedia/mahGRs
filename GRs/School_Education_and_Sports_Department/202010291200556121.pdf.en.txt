Government circular:
November 14 is celebrated as Children's Day. First Prime Minister of India
It is the birth anniversary of Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. Nehru was very fond of children and flowers.
Being a child among children Pandit jee used to spend hours. to know his soft heart.
It was dull. According to him, children are the bright future of the country and the future of the country depends on the bright future of the children.
Children's Day Week for all the students of classes 1 to 12 in all the schools of the state from November 8 to 14
The festival is celebrated in November. This will be done in the following 7 steps.
The students of the state should upload their videos and photos from the Facebook account of their parents and teachers using the hashtag#baldivas 2020 while participating in this initiative.
Students will also be able to upload these activities on Instagram, Twitter using#baldivas 2020.
