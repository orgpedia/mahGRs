| Government's decision:                                                                                                                  |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) to fill the posts of teachers in primary, middle and high schools in local self-government institutions and private educational institutions in the state, according to the prescribed reservation (TET), a concession of 15 percent marks is being approved for the wives and family members of the martyred soldiers. Candidates need to score 45 per cent marks to qualify the exam.
