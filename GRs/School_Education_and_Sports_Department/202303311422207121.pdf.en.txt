Preamble: The Government have decided to start Military Schools at the secondary level for private and competent voluntary organizations in the State. (1) This is permitted by the Government Order dated 26th September, 1995.
The government has given permission from 1996-97 to start the science stream course of class 11 and class 12 with natural growth in the military schools where there are classes up to class 10. (2) This is permitted by the Government Decision dated 5th July, 2002.
The 11th Science stream and the 12th Science stream with natural increase from 2011-12 have been allowed to start. It was under consideration of the Government to give ex-post facto approval to the 5 non-teaching posts filled up in the school during the restriction period of the Finance Department during the year 2010-11 as per the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in the meeting held on 13.12.2022.
Government's decision:
The 5 non-teaching posts which were filled up during the restriction period of the Finance Department during 2010-11 in the Sainik School, Vaghaye Patil Sainik Higher Secondary School, Lakha, G. Bhandara are hereby approved post-facto subject to the following conditions:
(1) All such matters as reservation, provisions of the Service Admission Act, point list and other matters which are required to be examined while granting individual recognition to the said posts shall be examined.
2) There will be no fee for these posts.
2. The said Government Decision is in Informal Reference No. 119/ APUC, being issued with the approval dated 01.03.2023.
