Preamble: The National Cadet Corps was established in 1948-49 for the provision and formation of the National Cadet Corps.
Various activities are carried out by the National Cadet Corps offices in the state through training/ camps for national cadets. Out of the budgetary provision available for the year 2023-24 for this activity, a total of Rs. 38,31,946/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Lakhs Thirty One Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty Six only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The offices of National Cadet Corps in the State are provided grants by the State Government to carry out various activities through training/ camps for National Cadet Corps. Out of the budgetary provision available in the financial year 2023-24 for this activity, a total amount of Rs. 38,31,946/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Lakhs Thirty One Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty Six only) and to be placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
