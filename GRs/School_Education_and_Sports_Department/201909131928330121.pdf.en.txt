Reference:- Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, No. SC/ ST 1/2014-15/ 938/40 dated 27th March, 2019.
A proposal has been submitted for transfer of an Urdu medium unaided school, namely, Al Manhal Educational, Cultural and Social Service Institute, Aurangabad, operating from Zikra Urdu Primary School, Deulai Chowk, Beed Bypass Road, Aurangabad, to Al Faisal Education and Welfare Society, Aurangabad. The proposal was then sent to the government for consideration.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the subject school to the above institution on the following terms and conditions:
1) It should be ensured that the chairman of Al Manhal Educational, Cultural and Social Service Institute agrees to the transfer of the school.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued from time to time by the Government, a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) There will be no change in the conditions of service of qualified teaching and non-teaching staff working in the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The number of posts as admissible in accordance with the revised norms of Government Resolution, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time in this regard.

Government Decision No. Mashah 3015/ PR No. 444/ SM-1: The institution will be obliged to keep the teaching/ non-teaching staff working and not to employ more than that number of teachers/ non-teaching staff, and it will be necessary to take action according to the government policy to fill the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
5) The condition of minimum marks will not be relaxed for any reason in case of a school where the management has changed.
(6) The school will continue to function on the same basis as it has been on grant-in-aid.
Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the government permission/ approval in case of a school where the management has changed.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
8) The change of management will be implemented in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated February 17, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
2. Clause No. (1) On the completion of clauses (3) to (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall complete the necessary further proceedings regarding the change of management of the school within 4 weeks after examining all the aspects and including other conditions required and shall inform the Government about the action taken.
