Deulgaon Raja, G. Buldhan of Marathi medium aided secondary school (8th to 10th standard), Mauje Nimgaon Guru (Rohana Shivar), Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation to Deulgaon Raja, Buldhana was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: To move from the rented building of the above school to the place of the members of the organization, Mauje Nimgaon Guru (Rohana Shivar), Ta. It is proposed to be built at Deulgaon Raja, G. Buldhana. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of transfer.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
