Introduction: The Government has implemented the scheme of giving Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards to the best sportspersons of the State of Maharashtra since 1969-70 and the Best Sports Mentor Award to the sports mentors who make the country proud at the national and international level since 1988-89. The Government of Maharashtra has implemented the scheme of conferring the Shiv Chhatrapati State Sports Life Time Honour Award since 2001-02 to honour the senior sportspersons, sports mentors, organizers, activists and other persons associated with the field of sports who have spent their entire life for the development of sports.
2. To revise the rules for the award of Shivchhatrapati State Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar, Best Sports Mentor Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson, Organizer/ Activist), Eklavya State Sports Award, Adventure Sports Award and Jijamata Sports Award. As per the Government decision dated 01.08.2019, a Committee of 15 members was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri Dhanraj Pillay, Padma Shri Awardee. The committee has submitted its report to the government. As per the Government decision dated 24.01.2020, the rules for Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Awards and other Sports Awards have been amended. During the preliminary verification of the proposals received for the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards for the year 2018-19 and considering the representations received in the Directorate, it was observed that certain aspects of the said Award Manual need to be clarified.
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services was asked to submit a proposal for amendment in the said Award Rules. Accordingly, the proposal submitted by the Commissioner Sports and Youth Services was considered. Accordingly, the matter of amending the award rules was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Sportspersons, organizers/ activists, mentors of the state are honoured with Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Pride Award, Best Sports Mentor Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson, Organizer/ Activist), Eklavya State Sports Award (Divyang), Adventure Sports Award and Jijamata Sports Award. About the award The Government Decision dated 24.01.2020 is being superseded. Following are the guidelines issued by the government:

(1) Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar: The Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar shall be conferred as per the rules indicated in Annexure' A' and' A-1', in order to honour such Krida Maharishi, who has spent his life for the development of sports by giving his all for sports and games, such senior sportspersons, sports mentors, sports organizers/ activists and persons associated with sports.
(2) Outstanding Sports Mentor Awards: In order to honour the sports mentors whose guidance in the field of sports has helped in participation and development of sportspersons at national and international level, the outstanding sports mentor awards will be given as per the rules indicated in Annexure A and A-2.
(3) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards (Sportspersons): The said awards shall be conferred in accordance with the rules set out in Annexure-A and A-3 to honour the sportspersons of Maharashtra who have excelled in the field of sports.
(4) Shivchhatrapati State Adventure Sports Awards: The awards will be given as per the rules mentioned in Annexure-A and A-4, to honour the sportspersons of the State who have excelled in adventure sports.
(5) Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards (Divyangjans): The awards will be given as per the rules mentioned in the Annexure-A and A-5 to honour the outstanding sportspersons of Maharashtra (Divyangjans) in the field of sports.
(6) District Sports Awards: The District Sports Awards will be conferred as per the rules indicated in Annexure A-6 to evaluate and honour the work/ contribution of meritorious sportspersons and sports mentors of the district.
The details of expenditure incurred in connection with the above award distribution should be submitted to and approved by the Commissioner, Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune before the award distribution ceremony.
The expenditure for the above purpose shall be met out of the approved provision made during the financial year under the head Main Account Head 2204-Sports and Youth Services-104-Sports and Sports (03) (01) Organizing State Sports Award Programme 50-Other Expenditure Scheme, Computer No.
