Introduction: Mauli Multipurpose Institute, Akola runs Mauli Science Junior College, Somthana, T.G. Akola is an English medium self-financed higher secondary school located at Shastri Nagar, Akola, T.G. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation to G. Akola was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing building with inadequate physical facilities to a spacious rented building. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) As the school is a self-financing school, it shall be necessary to submit a registered lease deed in the name of the institution at the place of relocation for a period of at least 30 years for at least 1 acre of land or the same amount of land or at least 500 sq.m. of land if the place is in the area of Municipal Corporation/ A.C.M.C.C.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(6) As the said school is an English medium self-financing institution, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in it shall be on the institution.
