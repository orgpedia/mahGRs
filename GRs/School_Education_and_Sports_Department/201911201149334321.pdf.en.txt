The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Marathi medium aided secondary school, Dhule, to Shri Shivarajyabhishek Multi-Purpose Institute, Amkhel, G. Dhule, was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has decided to approve the transfer to Dhule on the following terms and conditions: (1) The consent of all the members of the Dhanai Punai Legislative Council, Nizampur, G. Dhule to the transfer, the citizens of the school premises, the students and their parents, the teaching/ non-teaching staff, and other schools in the area should be ensured that there are no objections.
(2) The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nashik should ensure that the financial position of Shri Shivarajyabhishek Multi-Purpose Institute, Amkhel, G. Dhule Institute is good on the basis of its bank balance/ deposit.
(3) As per the orders issued by the Government from time to time, a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(6) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(7) The number of posts of teachers/ non-teaching staff as admissible under the revised norms of Accreditation dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time in respect of schools.

The staff will remain employed and no more teachers/ non-teaching staff will be employed.
It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy regarding filling up of vacancies from the permissible posts by additional staff.
(8) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(10) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(11) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
On fulfilment of the aforesaid conditions (1) to (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nashik Division, Nashik shall examine all the matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding change of management of the school including necessary conditions in addition to the above conditions/ conditions and inform the Government about the action taken.
