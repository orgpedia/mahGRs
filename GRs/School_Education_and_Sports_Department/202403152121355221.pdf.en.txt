Preamble: - The song "Jai Jai Maharashtra Majha, Garja Maharashtra Majha," written by Mr. Raja Neelkanth Bade and sung by Shahir Sable, an iconic icon who inspires the youth of the state as well as all the citizens of the society and describes the valour of Maharashtra, coinciding with the "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," has been adopted as the state anthem under the aforesaid Government Decision. The state anthem is highly meaningful, evocative and narrates the story of the noble and valiant traditions of the state. It was under the consideration of the Government to issue instructions so that the National Anthem would be played / sung along with the National Anthem / Ritual / Prayer / Pledge before the commencement of the daily session in all medium and all management schools in the State.
Government Circular: - Guidelines have been issued by the General Administration Department under the above relevant Government Decisions for compliance with the propriety with respect to singing / playing of the State Anthem. Notice No. 4 of the said instructions prescribes that the State Anthem shall be played / sung along with the National Anthem / Ritual / Prayer / Pledge before the commencement of the daily session in the schools of the State. Accordingly, the National Anthem adopted under the aforesaid relevant Government Decision shall be played / sung along with the National Anthem / Ceremony / Prayer / Pratigya before the commencement of the daily session in all the management schools of all mediums in the State, accordingly, the following instructions are being issued: -
The National Anthem / Ceremony / Prayer / Vowel shall be played / sung along with the National Anthem / Rituals / Prayer / Vowel before the commencement of the daily session in all medium and all management schools in the State as per the Government Decision of General Administration Department dated 01.02.2023 above.
The school management shall ensure that the National Anthem / Ritual / Prayer / Pledge is played / sung along with the State Anthem before the commencement of the daily session in all medium schools of the State.
