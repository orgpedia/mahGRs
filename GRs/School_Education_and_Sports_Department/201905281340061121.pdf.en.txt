School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: No. Med/ C/ 1090/2018, Dy. Letter from February 7, 2018.
Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of the permanent unaided English medium primary and upper primary school, Nutan English School, Gondia (formerly known as V.N.V. Convent), run by Smt. Vimaladevi Narayan Vishwakarma Multi-Purpose Education Society, Gondia, to Shri Rashtriya Shikshan Sanstha, Gondia, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Primary and Upper Primary School to Shri Rashtriya Shiksha Sansthan, Gondia on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(3) It shall be ensured that in case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government.
(4) Since the said English medium school is permanently unaided, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
