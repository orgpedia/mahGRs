The Proposal: -
Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan was implemented across the country from the year 2000-2001. Under the Government Decision dated 18.1.2002 of the Department of School Education and Sports, it was decided to implement Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan in the state and the responsibility of implementing the mission was entrusted to Maharashtra Council of Primary Education, Mumbai. The society is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the Bombay Trusts Act, 1950.
Samagra Shiksha Scheme, a Central Sector Scheme, has been implemented with effect from the year 2018-19 by amalgamating the Central Government schemes of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan, Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan and Teacher Education. The Samagra Shiksha Scheme has been restructured in line with the recommendations in the National Education Policy, 2020.
The ratio of the central and state share of the scheme is 60:40.
With a view to implementation of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan, new posts should not be created while sanctioning posts under Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan under Rule 37 (Management Cost) of the Manual on Financial Management and Procurement 2004 issued by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, New Delhi and the posts created should be filled only by contract or deputation. It is stated that filling up of these posts should not result in permanent liability to the society or the state government.
4. For effective implementation of the project based on the above mentioned guidelines of Ministry of Human Resource Development, New Delhi, some of the sanctioned posts under management as per Maharashtra Primary Education Council Services Rules, 1994 have been filled up by deputation and some posts by contract / contractual mode. The prescribed procedure mentioned in the Maharashtra Primary Education Council Service Rules, 1994 has been adopted while filling up these posts on contract / deputation. Also, the services of the employees appointed on contract basis as per the mentioned procedure have been continued with a break of one day every six months. These contractual employees are entitled to remuneration at the unit rate approved by the Central Government.
5. Regarding various queries of contractual employees working under the scheme, Shri. The meeting was chaired by the Chief Minister. The meeting was held at Sayadri Guest House on 22.7.2024. During the meeting, Shri. Chief Minister Madodaya made recommendations to the government regarding various issues of these employees.
