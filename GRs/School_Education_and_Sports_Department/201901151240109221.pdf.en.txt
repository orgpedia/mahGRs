Council, Pune vide No. Marshaisamprap/ Jishaisavya/ V.P./ 2018 Aastha/ 5419, dated 28.12.2018 Preamble: The Rules 2018 regulating the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal (Group A), Senior Advocate (Group A) and Advocate (Group B) in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) have been published vide notification dated 26.02.2018. As per proviso to Rule 4 (1) (a) of the said Rules, an officer who has attained the age of 55 years shall be exempted from passing the departmental examination. Accordingly, the Office of the Commissioner (Education) vide letter dated 21.09.2018, dated 02.02.2018, had submitted a proposal to the Government to exempt the officers of the Education Empowerment Wing who have completed 55 years of age from the departmental examination. As per the proposal of the Commissioner's office, 5 officers who have completed 55 years of age as per the Government Order dated 04.10.2018 mentioned herein are exempted from the departmental examination. 2. Here the name of Mr. Sheikh Kalimuddin Aziz, Principal is included.
Director, Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training, Pune.
4 In the proposal of the Commissioner's Office under the letter mentioned herein, the date of birth of Shri Sheikh Kalimuddin Aziz, Principal has been inadvertently mentioned as 25.08.1962 and his date of birth is 25.08.1965. Therefore, by amending the date of birth of Mr. Sheikh Kalimoddin Aziz, Principal, he has been appointed as Principal (Group-A), Senior in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch).
