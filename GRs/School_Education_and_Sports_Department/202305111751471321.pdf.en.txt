Preamble: Sant Bhagwanbaba Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Ta. Ashti, G.B.D. Vasudev Baluram Madhavi, New English School, Lodha Haven, Nilje, Ta. Kalyan, G.Thane is an English medium self-financed primary (Std. 1st to 7th) School Year Education Charitable Trust Lodha Haven, Nilje, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the management of the institution to Kalyan, G.Thane was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of this school is Varsha Education Charitable Trust, Lodha Haven, Nilje, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Kalyan, G.Thane on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai shall examine all the aspects and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
