Introduction: Carmel Jyoti Education Society, Khopoli, Raigad runs Nirmala Convent School, Chandannagar, Vadgaon Sheri, Pune. The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval on self-financing basis for relocation to Haveli, G. Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The school has shifted to a self-contained building owned by the institution due to insufficient space.
Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3221/ PR No. 469/ SM-1 (6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
