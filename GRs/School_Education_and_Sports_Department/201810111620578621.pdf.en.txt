Government Resolution, Department of School Education and Sports No. Isaiasha 1095/ (278/95)/ Mashi-8 dated 26th September, 1995 2) Government Resolution, Department of School Education and Sports No. Isaiasha 2015/138/ SM-6, dated 9th December, 2015 Preamble: 1.1 A total of 30 Military Schools, one in each district of the State imparting Military Education, are being established by NGOs from 1996-97 under reference No. 1 This has been permitted vide Government Resolution dated 26th September, 1995. Under this scheme, it has been decided at the government level to start a boys' Sainik School in the district through private, competent and voluntary organizations and to start a girls' Sainik School in every revenue division. These schools are purely residential schools and at present there are 39 aided and 3 unaided Sainik Schools in the State.
1.2 As per the direction of the State Cabinet in its meeting dated 29.5.2018, the Government was considering to appoint a Secretary level committee to review how many students have been recruited as soldiers from the military schools in the State.
Government's decision:
The following committee is being constituted to review how many students have been recruited as soldiers out of the students who have studied from military schools in the state so far.
The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:

The mandate and duration of the Committee is as follows: (i) To oversee the functioning/ performance of the private voluntary affiliated Military Schools in the State.
(ii) To make recommendations to the Government for improving the functioning and performance of these schools.
The term of office of the Committee shall be 6 (six) months from the date of the order.
