Introduction: Gyanranjan Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad runs Netaji Subhash English Primary School, Hadko, G. Aurangabad, an English medium unaided primary school (1st to 7th standard), on self-financed basis, under the aegis of Hallmark Welfare Society for Quality Education and Research, T. The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the institution to Paithan, Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Hallmark Welfare Society for Quality Education and Research The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the Institute to Paithan, Aurangabad on self-financing basis on the following terms and conditions:
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) As the school is a self-financing English medium school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated, or
