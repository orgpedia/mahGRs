The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 13.06.2013, it was decided to provide basic training to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) sponsored candidates by appointing them to the post of Education Officer and similar in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A cadre from 01.07.2013. The officers who have completed the training have been posted as Education Officers and equivalent posts as per the Government decision dated 08.08.2013.
Accordingly, these officers have been appointed as Education Officers and equivalent posts for a period of 2 years.
Shri Nitin Popatrao Bachhav has been re-appointed as Education Officer and equivalent in Group-A cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) vide Government Order dated 06.01.2016 at No.3 mentioned above. the Commissioner (Education)

The proposal for completion of the period under review has been submitted vide letter dated 06.10.2017.
The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
1 In pursuance of the Government Order dated 13.06.2013 and the Government Order dated 06.01.2016, the officers mentioned in the accompanying proforma working in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A Cadre as Education Officer and similar are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in Rakkana No. 7 before their names.
2. Annual increment should be given to the officers who complete the probation. This should also be recorded in the service book of the concerned.
