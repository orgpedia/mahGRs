School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.Masha 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Letter dated 10/04/2017, No. 164/2016-17/ 610-11.
Preamble: The proposal received under the said letter for transfer of Adarsha Vidyaniketan Shikshan Sanstha, Koparkhairane, Navi Mumbai operating Visva-Bharati English Primary School, Koparkhairane, Navi Mumbai to Gurudas Seva Samiti, Ghansoli, Navi Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said Primary and Upper Primary School to Gurudas Seva Samiti, Ghansoli, Navi Mumbai on the following terms and conditions: 1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the school where the management has changed.
2) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued from time to time for the school. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
3) Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff who have consented to transfer in the school will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
4) In the updated Annexure-I of Adarsh Vidyaniketan Education Society, Koparkhairane, Navi Mumbai, with the signature of all the members, consent to the transfer of the school is Rs. Notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
5) The school is being transferred without any immovable property and the records will be transferred to the new institution after the transfer. A notarized affidavit signed by the President and Secretary of both the organizations in this regard is Rs. 100/- should be taken on the stamp paper.
6) The school will continue to function on the same grant-in-aid basis.

7) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the government permission/ approval in case of a school where the management has changed.
8) Since the English medium school is permanently unaided, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school will be on the institution.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
10) The change of management will be implemented in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated February 17, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
