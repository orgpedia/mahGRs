Shree Samarth Films, Navi Mumbai As per the Government decision dated 11th April, 2023, the Marathi film" Let's Change" has been allowed to be screened for the students/ girl students in the schools of the State for two years of the academic year 2023-24 and 2024-25 subject to conditions. The Government Resolution No. 4 The following is as follows: Students who wish to watch the said film shall not be charged more than Rs.20/- (Rupees Twenty only). Instead, students who wish to watch the film should note that they will not be charged more than Rs.10/- (Rupees Ten only).
