3. Letter from the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai No. Sub-Division/ Abhanase/ 18-19/ Ka- 4/749, D. 06.03.2019
Government decision: The All India Civil Services Table Tennis Tournament 2018-19 was held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai from 13th to 17th March, 2019. The exam was conducted on 1st and 2nd March 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The selection test, training camp and participation of the state team as per the prospectus' B' is Rs. 86,325/- (Rupees Eighty Six Thousand Three Hundred and Twenty Five only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
4. The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned shall inform the player-coach/ manager to participate in the tournament. Training camp from 9th to 11th March 2019. The competition will be held from 13th to 17th March 2019. They should be relieved with effect from 8th March, 2019 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to Secretariat Gymkhana. Every player in the team

As per the provisions of the Government Resolution No.LVE-1485/ Q.No.833/ A/ SEWA-9 dated 11th May, 1989 Curve No.LVE/ 1492/46/ SEWA-9 dated 30th July, 1992 of the Finance Department, the players, coaches and managers of the team are required to be registered with the Board. The expenditure on travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ head of the office concerned.

In order to avail the benefit of this concession, the certificate of attendance of the officers and employees appearing for the selection test issued by the Honorary Secretary, Secretariat Gymkhana, will have to be submitted by the concerned employees in their establishment office.
The Secretarial Gymkhana shall submit the statement of actual expenditure to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune and Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of the order. Also, if the amount is left, it should be returned to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai immediately.
8. The expenditure for the year 2018-19 shall be met from the budgetary provision under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Tournament, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Tournament (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
9. Secretariat Gymkhana is approving expenditure as per return" B" subject to fulfilment of all conditions in the report of the competition, item wise details of actual expenditure, utilization certificate, audited statement of accounts and sanction order.
