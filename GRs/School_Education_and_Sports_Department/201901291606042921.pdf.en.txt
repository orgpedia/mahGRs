Introduction: Savitribai Phule Mahila Mandal, Ta. Rajmata Jijau Junior College (Arts and Science), Parbhani. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the Marathi medium unaided junior college, Parbhani, to Ughada Mahadev, Karegaon Road, G. Parbhani, was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The above Junior College is being shifted from the existing dilapidated and dangerous building to an upgraded building. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the relocated Junior College shall not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure that no one has any objection/ objection regarding the adjustment of all the students in the junior college as well as the transfer of the junior college.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the orders issued from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in the case of a transferred Junior College.
(7) The Junior College shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(8) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall take necessary further action regarding approval of relocation of the said school and inform the Government about the action taken.
