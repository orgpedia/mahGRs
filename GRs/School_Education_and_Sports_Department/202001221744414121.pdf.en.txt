Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Basketball Tournament 2019-20 was held at Sabina Chandrashekhar Recreation Ground, Mumbai from 28th to 31st January, 2020. The exam was conducted on January 2, 2020. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Rs. 57,621/- (Rupees fifty seven thousand six hundred and twenty one only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office should relieve the Sports Coach/ Manager for the training camp from 23rd to 25th January, 2020 and for the tournament from 28th to 31st January, 2020 with effect from 22nd January, 2020 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they will be working.
