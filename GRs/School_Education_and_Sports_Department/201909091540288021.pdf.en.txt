The Proposal:
As per the recommendation of the Task Force Group constituted for the mission of Universalization of Primary Education and Literacy, it has been decided to establish a total of 4860 Central Primary Schools in the primary schools of the Zilla Parishads for better planning of education for all, maintaining continuous attendance and raising the educational standard. The decision was taken by the government. Accordingly, one post of Head of Centre each has been created for 10 primary schools of Zila Parishad.
The reference No. As per the Government Notification No. 3, the service entry rules for the recruitment of the post of Head of the Centre were fixed. Accordingly, it was decided to fill up 30 per cent and 40 per cent of the total posts of Head of the Centre through limited departmental examination and competitive examination respectively. Accordingly, the revised procedure for filling up the vacancies of Head of the Centre through competitive examination has been prescribed vide Government Resolution No. 4. However, since the syllabus of the said examination is not in consonance with the duties of the Head of the Centre and is not concise and complete, the matter of rescheduling the said syllabus was under consideration of the Government.
Government Clearance Sheet: The Government has decided to fill up the posts of Head of the Centre through competitive examination. The revised procedure has been prescribed w.e.f. 16.02.2018. Paragraph No. 5 of this Government Decision is hereby omitted. 5 are included:

(2) The direct service and limited departmental examination for filling up the vacancies of the head of the center should be conducted by the system selected by the Department of Information Technology according to the above syllabus.
