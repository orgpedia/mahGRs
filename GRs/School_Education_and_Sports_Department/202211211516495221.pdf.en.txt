Preamble If the workload required for a part-time teacher for a subject is not available from a recognized middle school, high school and junior college in the state, then a qualified person is appointed on an hourly basis to teach such subject and they are paid on a clock-hour basis. Reference No. 5 decision of the government. The honorarium to be paid to teachers appointed on clock hour basis in secondary, higher secondary and junior colleges has been fixed as on 27.11.2006. The government was considering increasing the amount.
The government has decided to increase the salary of teachers working on a clock-hour basis in recognized private aided secondary and higher secondary/ junior colleges in the state as follows.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | level                       | Modified Rate (Per Hour) |
| 1    | High School/ Junior College | Rs. 150/-                |
| 2    | Secondary                   | Rs. 120/-                |
-----------------------------------------------------------------
2. Teachers appointed on hourly basis should be qualified as full-time teachers.
Teachers who do not meet the eligibility criteria should not be appointed on an hourly basis. The government has taken the decision. It will come into effect from 01/12/2022.
