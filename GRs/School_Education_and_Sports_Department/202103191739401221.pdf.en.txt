Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mantralaya Extension Building,

Date of release: March 19, 2021
Introduction: Regarding the establishment of the" International Sports University," Ma. In pursuance of the announcement made by the Deputy Chief Minister (Finance and Planning) in the Budget Speech for the year 2020-2021, with the concurrence of the Department of Law and Justice, with the approval of the Cabinet and both the Houses of Parliament, the Government has decided to introduce the Bill. As per the Governor's assent, the Maharashtra Act No. 35 Therefore, the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV dated 28.12.2020 has been published. Accordingly, the matter of flying a new title for the International Sports University was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As per the approval given by the Planning and Finance Department, approval is being given to open new account titles as follows.
Demand No. E-3 2204- Sports and Youth Services, 104- Sports and Sports, (41) International Sports University (41) (01) Assistance for the creation of International Sports University (Program) (22045831)
This decision has been taken by the Principal Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts), Mumbai. No: KV/ CH-1/ School Education& Sports Department/ UOR- 67/2020-21/ 450, Dy. The approval received under 18/02/2021 is being issued vide Informal Reference No. 131/ Expenditure-5, dated 09.02.2021 of the Department of Finance.
