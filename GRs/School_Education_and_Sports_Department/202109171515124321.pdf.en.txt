Maharashtra Recognized Aided Secondary Schools Provident Fund Rules, 1977 (2) G.O.No.2412/ (81-A/ 12)/ Budget, dated 7.11.2012.
The Provident Fund of the full-time approved teaching and non-teaching staff of recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools is controlled by the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune and the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune. All the activities like sanctioning of advances/ refunds, depositing of contributions, maintaining accounts of provident fund, accounting of provident fund and issuing of letters to the concerned every year, etc. are carried out by the Superintendents, Salaries and Provident Fund Teams under the control of the Education Officers at the district level.
The salary of the teaching and non-teaching staff of recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools is being paid through online system as per the Government Decision dated 7.11.2012. However, the accounts of the provident fund are recorded in the register. Therefore, it has been observed that in some cases, accounts/ returns of provident fund accounts are not being paid on time. Therefore, in order to keep the accounts of the provident fund, give accounts, pay advance/ refund payments, etc. up-to-date, accurate and timely decisions, the government was considering to make the entire functioning of the provident fund online by linking it to the system.
The Government has decided that the maintenance of accounts, accounting, payment of advance/ refund of provident fund of full-time recognized teaching and non-teaching staff of recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools will be done online by linking it with the Shalarth system from October 1, 2021.
The Superintendent, Salary and Provident Fund Team should report the initial balance of the Provident Fund of the teacher and non-teaching staff of recognized private aided primary, secondary, high school and teacher schools on the Provident Fund System by 31 December 2021. The deduction of provident fund deduction from salary from April 1, 2021 will be automatically recorded in the provident fund system.
