The Proposal:
The revised syllabus, theme, assessment and hourly schedule for Class 10 have been revised based on the curriculum revision in the academic year 2017-18.
Accordingly, vide Circular dated 14th June, 2018 of State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune, an alternative provision/ facility has been provided to the students of Marathi medium schools to study English (First) language instead of English (Third) for class 10 at school level.
b) At the school level, for class 10th, students of English medium schools have been provided with an alternative provision/ facility to study Marathi as the first language instead of Marathi (II). The government was considering making English the first language of instruction in minority educational institutions.
Government circular:
At the school level, for class 10, students of schools having other mediums (Hindi, Gujarati, Urdu, Kannada, Sindhi, Telugu, Tamil, Bengali) are provided with an alternative provision/ facility to study English as a first language along with their first language.
