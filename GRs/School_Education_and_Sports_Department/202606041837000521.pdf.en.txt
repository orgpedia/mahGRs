I. Analyzing the training-related needs of the personnel in the department and capacity building.
To act as the 'Nodal Body' for implementing the plan.
II. Taking into account the duties and responsibilities of the curtains of the various cadres of the department, for them
Develop a capacity building plan based on the skills required. This plan
When preparing, consider the goals for the current fiscal year and future needs. as well as
For this, use of AI based tools available on the platform iGOT-Karmayogi.
III. Online training and hands-on training by the departments, instead of relying only on traditional training.
Adopt a blended training approach such as in-person and on-the-job training.
IV. Creation of Capacity Building Plan of the Department as per the objectives set by the State and
Implementation, periodic review.
Onboarding of all officers / employees of the department on the portal V.IGOT Karmayogi
and supervise training, as well as the timely completion of online courses, and
Ensure that staff are involved in offline workshops / trainings.
VI. Coordination with General Administration Department and YASDA for all types of training.
VII. Review the progress of the capacity building framework and hold periodic meetings accordingly.
