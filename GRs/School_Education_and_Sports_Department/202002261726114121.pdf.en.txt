Government Resolution Department of School Education, No. 3419/ No. 43/ TNT-6, D. 24/7/2019.
Government Decision- A Joint Committee has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 24/7/2019 to study the applicability of the old pension scheme to all teachers and non-teaching staff who were appointed before November 1, 2005 on all private aided, unaided partially-aided and batches in the state. The committee is expected to submit its recommendations to the government within three months. Now the date for the Joint Committee to make recommendations to the Government is being extended till 01/05/2020.
