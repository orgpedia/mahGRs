The Proposal:
Satara Military School was established. Established on June 23, 1961, it is the first military school in the country. The main objective of this school is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children at the grassroots level and at all levels in Maharashtra to become officers in the Indian Army through the National Defence Academy.
A large number of students from this school have so far been admitted to the National Defence Academy. As per the Government Decision dated 5th July, 2013, students of these schools will be paid Rs. Meal Allowance has been sanctioned at the rate of Rs.72/-. As per the MoU between the Centre and the state government, it is the responsibility of the state government to provide food allowance to the students of Satara Sainik School. In view of the increase in the price of food grains and the overall increase in inflation since the price of food allowance was fixed, the rate of food allowance for the students of Satara Sainik School is Rs. 72/- to Rs. 79/- (Rupees Ninety-nine only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Students will be given Rs. Approval is hereby given for sanction of food allowance as per Rs.79/-. The revised rates of food allowances will be applicable from the academic year 2019-20. Also, the rates of the food allowance will be applicable to the students of Chandrapur Sainik School.
The expenditure shall be met from the grant sanctioned under the head" Demand No.E-2, 2202-General Education, 02-Secondary Education-110, Assistance to Non-Government Secondary Schools (00) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Poorvadayan Sainik Schools (22020469), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
