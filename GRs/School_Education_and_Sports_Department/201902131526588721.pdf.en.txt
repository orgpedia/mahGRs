the prelude
The rules regarding the award of Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Pride Award, Best Sports Mentor Award, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson, Organizer/ Activist)/ Jijamata Sports Award, Eklavya State Sports Award and Adventure Sports Award to the best sportspersons of the state of Maharashtra have been amended and the revised rules have been framed based on the decision of the Government in the above context 1 to 3. Accordingly, the Government had constituted the Award Selection Committee for the year 2017-18 vide Government Resolution dated 06.02.2019. Considering the recommendations of the Award Selection Committee, the matter of approving the distribution of awards for the year 2017-18 was under consideration of the Government and the Government has now decided as follows.
The Government has decided to confer the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Life Pride Award to the senior athletes, sports mentors, sports organizers/ activists and other persons associated with the field of sports in Maharashtra who have made an unparalleled place in the sports life of Maharashtra by dedicating their all to sports and games and whose personality's sports activism has made a memorable impact on the sports life of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the Government is pleased to confer the' Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar' for the year 2017-18 to the senior sports personalities mentioned in Annexure A.

The" Best Sports Mentor Award" is given to the sports mentors who have performed exceptionally in the field of sports in Maharashtra and have inspired the players to participate at the national and international level. Accordingly, it is approved to honour the persons mentioned in Annexure' B' by conferring" Best Sports Mentor Award" for the year 2017-18 and" Jijamata State Sports Award" to Women Mentors.

In order to honour the best male and female sportspersons of the State of Maharashtra with the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award and to honour the disabled sportspersons with the Eklavya State Sports Award, the Government hereby approves the conferment of the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson) and Eklavya State Sports Award (Sportsperson with Disability) for the year 2017-18 to the male and female sportspersons referred to in Annexure' C'.
4. It is approved to confer the Shivchhatrapati State Adventure Sports Award and the Shivchhatrapati State Sports Organizer/ Activist Award for the year 2017-18 respectively to the players in adventure activities and sports organizers/ activists who have done exceptional work in the field of sports as indicated in Annexure D.
The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services, State of Maharashtra should take immediate action for the conferment of Shivchhatrapati State Sports Awards for the year 2017-18.
