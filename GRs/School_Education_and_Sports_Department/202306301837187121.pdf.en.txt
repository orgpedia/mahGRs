The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. The transfer is made on administrative grounds with the prior approval of the senior authority next to the competent authority as per the provisions of section 4 (4) (2) and section 4 (5) of the said Act. By the Government Order dated 23.06.2023, the officers of the rank of Deputy Education Officer and similar posts were promoted and posted. In view of the partial change in the said post, the post is being posted at the place of the revised post shown in column No. 4 against their names mentioned in the table below.
