Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Athletic Meet 2020-21 was held at Priyadarshini Park, Nepeansea Road, Mumbai from 28th to 30th September, 2021. The exam was conducted on 22nd September 2021. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,01,800/- (Rupees One Lakh One Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers from participating in the tournament from 28th to 30th September, 2021 with effect from 24th September, 2021 (M. No.) and the services of these players should be made available to Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The period of absence from office from September 24 to October 1, 2021 (training camp, competition and travel) should be treated as" duty period." as well as their travel,
