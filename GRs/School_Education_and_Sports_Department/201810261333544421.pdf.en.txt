Cleaning paper:
The government is implementing the school feeding programme. Measures/ provisions have been taken to reduce the liability of the Principals in respect of School Nutrition Scheme vide Circular dated 26.02.2014. In this government decision, responsibility has been fixed for the taste of food in serial number 5.
This is being improved as follows:
The taste of the food should be recorded in the Taste Book by first taking the cook who prepared the food, then the school management committee members or their representatives and then the principal/ teacher associated with the scheme at three levels. " Instead
The food shall be tasted by (i) a member of the School Management Committee, or his representative (if present) or (ii) one of the Principals/ teachers associated with the scheme and the same shall be recorded in the Taste Book.
As per the amendment, the Director of Education (Primary Education) has made necessary changes in the records in the Taste Registry to be provided at the school level. ).
