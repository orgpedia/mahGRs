2.This Agreement is subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) The Government shall take action to disburse the grant to the institution concerned in accordance with the terms and conditions of the grant approval regarding financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities vide Government Resolution No. 4 (a) dated 1st March, 2014.
(2) After the disbursement of the grant to the institution concerned, it shall be mandatory to submit the certificate of completion of the project along with the photographs of the project and the utilization certificate as soon as possible and within a maximum period of 1 year, otherwise the disbursed grant shall be recovered from the date of disbursement of the grant at the prevailing penal interest rate of the Government. Also, the institution concerned will be required to provide a bank guarantee of 10% of the total grant amount to be sanctioned. Accordingly, after depositing the bank guarantee and affidavit with the concerned District Sports Officer, the funds available for the disbursement of the sanctioned amount should be disbursed to the concerned institution through RTGS.
(3) It shall be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate by paying the government subsidy and self-contribution.
