The salaries of such teaching and non-teaching staff up to June 2020 have been allowed to be paid offline due to non-issuance of salaried marks.
However, since some of the above employees are yet to receive their salary numbers, the matter of approval for payment of salary up to September, 2020 to such teaching and non-teaching staff, who have not received their salary numbers, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The following teachers/ non-teaching staff of private partially and fully aided, local self-government institutions and teachers' schools in the state who have not yet received their school marks are being approved to pay their salaries till September, 2020 through offline mode. This includes the following types of payments:
1). Salaries of 276 teachers approved individually and 171 teachers approved by Supplementary Demand in Higher Secondary Schools under Government decision dated 13.3.2018.
2) As per the Government decision dated 6th February 2019, out of the 1000 posts approved by the High Level Committee for the year 2003-04 to 2010-11 in the private aided higher secondary and junior colleges in the State, out of the 428 posts that have lapsed, 68 posts have been revived.
