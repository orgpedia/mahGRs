The Selection List of vacancies in the General State Service Group-B (Administration Branch) in the Superintendents, Cadre has been issued by the promotion of Shri Krishna Naganna Thotavar, Superintendent General State Service in 2019-20. Shri Thotawar has declined the promotion under his application dated 22.7.2022 due to his family problems. In the Government Resolution dated 12.9.2016 of the General Administration Department, Issue No. As per the provisions mentioned herein, the name of Shri Krishna Naganna Thotavar is being excluded from the list for the year 2019-20. Therefore, the order dated 26.5.2022 regarding the promotion of Shri Thotawar is hereby cancelled.
