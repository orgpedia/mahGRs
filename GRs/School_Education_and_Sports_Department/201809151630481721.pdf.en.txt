Preamble: The defined contribution pension scheme for teachers and non-teaching staff in 100% aided posts in private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools recognized by the Zilla Parishads. The government has taken a decision. For the year 2018-19, the matter of disbursement of the government's share and interest for teachers and non-teaching staff in Zilla Parishad and private aided primary schools was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- For the financial year 2018-19, the following statement in column No. 3 has been provided. Out of this, Rs. 1,97, 59.62 lakhs is being approved for disbursement through Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS) to the Commissioner, Education, State of Maharashtra, Pune as per the following statement.

(6) The Department of Finance has issued Government Decisions fixing the rate of interest every year.
Also, while considering the interest, the Finance Department Circular No. Care should be taken to take action as per Annio 1010/ Q.No.67/ Service-4, dated 16th November, 2012. Accordingly, the Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency should take action regarding the levy of interest at the prescribed rate and submit the payment of the specified amount to the Treasury for the purpose of drawing the amount of interest in Sample 45A. There is no need to add the list of employees whose accounts are to be credited with the payment.
However, the payment should be certified by the Sub-State Record Keeping Agency to ensure that the amount is accurate. The information in this regard should be sent to the Controller by the Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency. Also, the Controlling Officers should send the report to the Deputy Secretary (Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff), School Education Department and State Records Agency, Mumbai without fail every month.
(7) The Accountant General, Maharashtra-1 and 2 Mumbai/ Nagpur shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts for audit.
(8) The utilization certificate of the funds being made available shall be submitted to the Government.
This Government Decision was taken under the authority of the Administrative Department to distribute 70% of the funds at their level as per the circular dated 2/4/2018 of the Finance Department and Government Decision No. Annio 1006/ (23/06) Mashi-2, dated 29/11/2010 is being issued subject to the terms and conditions.
