Introduction: Mahatma Phule Samata Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur runs Keshavrao Pawar Vidyalaya, Katol, Dist.
Nagpur Marathi medium aided secondary school (class 5th to 10th) Plot No. The proposal received under reference letter to shift 767 IUDP to Sharda Nagar, Nagpur Road, Katol was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is functioning at Kuntlapur Industrial Co-operative Colony, Katol and the institution has proposed to shift the school after giving a notice to shift the school. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) In respect of schools, teachers and non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grant, students.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
