Government's stand:
The final paragraph under the heading Powers and Functions of the Committee in the Government Resolution No.Sanikya-2020/ PR No. 56/ SD-6, dated 30th March, 2020 is being amended and may be read as follows:
State International Curriculum Advisory Committee at State level
After making a comparative study of the textbooks prepared by the Research and Training Council, Maharashtra, Pune and the textbooks prepared by the Maharashtra International Education Board, the State Level Curriculum Preparation Committee should be guided to provide improved curriculum of international standards to the students of all the schools under the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, including the 81 schools selected by the Maharashtra International Education Board.
