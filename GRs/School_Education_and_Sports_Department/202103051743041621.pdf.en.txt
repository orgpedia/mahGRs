The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act, 2011 and the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Rules, 2016 and the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) (Amendment) Act, 2018 have been framed by the government. The government is also considering amending the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act in view of the continuous complaints from parents regarding school fees.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: A Committee is being constituted to suggest amendments in the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Acts/ Rules as follows

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
# Page 2
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SASAISHU-2021/ PR No. 39/21/ SM-6
(a) to amend the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act, 2011 and the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Rules, 2016;
2) To suggest a coherent policy for the state by studying the laws and rules regarding fees in other states.
3) Study the nature of grievances of parents and suggest ways to redress the grievances.
4) To study the report submitted to the Government by the Shri V. G. Palshikar Committee earlier and suggest amendments.
5) To suggest measures to ensure uniformity by studying the rules related to self-financing schools, capitation fee act, minority educational institutions, etc., judicial decisions, laws and rules etc.
The Committee shall convene the meeting as required and take into consideration the suggestions received from the parents, the directors of the institutions and the NGOs. Discuss with them as required and prepare a comprehensive report and submit it to the government within three months. The committee can take invited members as per requirement and consult experts in the field.
(4) This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
