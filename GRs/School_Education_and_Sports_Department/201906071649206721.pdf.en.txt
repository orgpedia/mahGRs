Preamble- As per the Government decision in Reference No. 1 and 2 above, the revision of pension/ family pension of the pensioners who were in retirement before 01/01/2016 as per 7th Pay Commission and enhancement of pension for 80 years and above with effect from 01.01.2019.
This provision was under consideration to apply to teachers and non-teaching staff in Zilla Parishads, recognized private 100% aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools.
Government Resolution No. The provision as amended under Sections 1 and 2 is being made applicable to teachers and non-teaching staff in Zilla Parishads, recognized private 100% aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools.
(2) The expenditure in respect thereof shall be covered under the head of the budgetary account under which the pension of the pensioners is to be spent and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under that head.
