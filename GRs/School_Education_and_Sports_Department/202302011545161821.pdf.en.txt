The proposal-
General Administration for speedy disposal of promotion proposals
The department The instructions have been issued vide circular dated 9 th March, 2022.
With a view to create awareness for speedy disposal of promotion proposals, the General Administration Department has issued a notification. As per the guidelines issued regarding the promotion proceedings under the Government Decision of 1st August 2019, it was under the consideration of the Government to finalize the proposal within the prescribed period and to fix the responsibility for the same. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government circular
The General Administration Department, Government of Odisha, after examining the proposals for promotion to the posts in Group C and Group D in all the field offices under the School Education Department, recommends eligible persons for promotion. The Departmental Promotion Committee should be constituted as mentioned in August 1, 2019.
2. Considering the guidelines issued by the General Administration Department, the time limit is being fixed as follows for the immediate completion of the promotion process.

3. In order to ensure that Group C and Group D employees in the subordinate offices of the School Education Department are promoted without delay within the stipulated time, the responsibility is being fixed on the Deputy Director of Education of the concerned department as the Cadre Controlling Officer. Also, the responsibility is being fixed on the Joint Director (Establishment) in the Commissioner's Education Office as the Cadre Controlling Officer in order to ensure that the employees of Group C and Group D at the Commissionerate and Directorate level get promotions without delay. The Cadre Controller must strictly adhere to the above timelines. The supervisory officers of the concerned officers should take information about the work done on promotion every year and record it in the confidential report/ performance appraisal report.
4. Record the pending work of all service matters of Group C and Group D employees in the subordinate offices of the School Education Department and the service books/ service charts of all employees. Special campaigns must be completed or updated before April 30, 2023. The Joint Director handling the establishment matters in the office of the Commissioner of Education and all the Divisional Deputy Directors of Education should complete the pending work related to the service by taking care of themselves.
