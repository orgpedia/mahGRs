The Proposal:
The Revising Committee and Zonal Fee Regulatory Committees have been constituted vide notification dated 20.12.2020 and dated 7.6.2021 as per Section 7 and 11 of the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act, 2011. Further, as per the Government Decision dated 20.12.2021, the Chairman and Non-Official Members of the said Committee have been allotted honorarium per sitting day. Reference No. 5 to the Chairman and non-official members of the Review Committee and Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee. The demand is for disbursement of honorarium of Rs. 5,76,500/-. Accordingly, the matter of payment of honorarium to the chairman and non-official members of the Revision Committee and Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Chairman and Non-Official Members of the Revision Committee and the Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee, S.No. 3 here. As per Government decision dated 20.12.2021, for payment of honorarium at the prescribed rate of Rs. 5,76,500/- (Rupees Five Lakh Seventy Six Thousand Five Hundred only) is hereby approved by the Government.
