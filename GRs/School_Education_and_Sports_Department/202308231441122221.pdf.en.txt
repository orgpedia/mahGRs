Introduction: Priyadarshani Indira Gandhi Educational Institute, Umarkhed, G. Yavatmal Managed Secondary School, Marlegaon, Ta. Umarkhed, G. Yavatmal, Marathi medium aided secondary (E. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the school (8th to 10th Aided) to Umarkhed, G. Yavatmal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The relocation is proposed as the above school building is going into the highway. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) In the present case, the temporary approval for relocation has been granted and it will be mandatory for the institution to complete the buildings with amenities as per the prescribed norms within 18 months and submit the occupancy certificate to the Government, if this condition is not met within the stipulated period, the said order will be considered null and void.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(3) The school shall continue to function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
