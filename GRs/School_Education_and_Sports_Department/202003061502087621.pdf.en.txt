N. G. Barate Educational Trust, Pune, to Ankush Paygude Company, NDA Road, Kondhave-Davade, Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has been shifted from the existing inadequate building to the self-sufficient building owned by the founder. The transfer is subject to the following conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) As the school is a permanent English medium unaided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
