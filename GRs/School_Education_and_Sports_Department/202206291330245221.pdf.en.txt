Introduction: JSPM, Latur run Maharashtra Primary School, Majge Nagar, Latur, a Marathi medium aided primary school (1st to 4th standard), Swami Dayanand Vidyalaya, Rajivnagar, Kava, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Latur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: It is proposed to shift the said school from the building having inadequate physical facilities to the building owned by the institution at Swami Dayanand Vidyalaya, Rajivnagar, Kava, Dist. Latur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3322/ PR No. 443/ SM-1 (5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
