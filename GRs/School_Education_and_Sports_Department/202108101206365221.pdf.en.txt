Preamble: A proposal for grant of temporary promotion to the lone post in the quota to be filled by promotion in the cadre of Joint Director, Sports and Youth Services, Pune, Group A (Pay Scale-S-25: 78800-209200) on establishment of Directorate of Sports and Youth Services was under consideration of the Government.
The Government have decided to temporarily promote Shri Chandrakant Ramdas Kamble, Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, to the post of Joint Director, Sports and Youth Services, Pune, Group-A (Pay Scale-S-25: 78800 209200) subject to the following conditions:
I. All the pending court petitions regarding the promotion on the date of the said Government decision. Special Leave Petition No. The said temporary promotion is being granted subject to the decisions dated 28306/2017.
II. The Hon'ble High Court of Bombay has issued Writ Petition Stamp No. 10876/2021 and Writ Petition Stamp No. 10878/2021. The said promotion shall be subject to the decision on this writ petition as per the decision dated 25.05.2021.
(2) The order shall come into force immediately.
