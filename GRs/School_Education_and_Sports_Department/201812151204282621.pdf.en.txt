the prelude
Maharashtra is a progressive state and the government has decided to build 100 schools of international educational standards in the state in the first phase on October 14, 2016, to ensure that the schools in the state should be of international educational standards and the children of the state should get the best education of international standards. Accordingly, the Government has decided to set the criteria for the creation of schools of international educational standards as per the decision dated 30th November, 2017.
As per the Government Decision dated 02.01.2018, there is a need to have adequate academic freedom and autonomy in the teaching-learning in the schools of international standards to be built in the State. The Maharashtra International Education Board has been set up in the backdrop of this.
The Maharashtra International Education Board (MIEB) is a new board with a tenure of 10 years to become financially viable. (Class 1 to Class 10) After 10 years, the said Board will generate their own sources of income. However, at present, the Maharashtra International Education Board is in the primary stage. The subject of providing grants-in-aid and other powers for the functioning of the said Board. Cabinet of Ministers The Cabinet meeting was held on December 11, 2018.
