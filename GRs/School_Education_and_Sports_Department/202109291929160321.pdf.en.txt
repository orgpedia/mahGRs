Regarding declaration of Controlling Authority for reconciliation of the expenditure of grants and revenue collection to be disbursed by the School Education and Sports Department.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Circular No.3321/ (54-A/ 21)/ Budget,
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mantralaya Extension Building,
Mumbai- 400 032.
Date of release: September 29, 2021
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Budget Manual, the Controller of School Education and Sports Department is being declared as Form-A to reconcile the expenditure of the subsidy and the amount collected in the financial year 2021-2022.
The Controlling Officer and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall strictly follow the following instructions for reconciling the expenditure and accruals as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Budget Rules: 1) The accounts maintained by the Head of Office/ Drawing and Disbursing Officer in his office with the accounts maintained by the Treasury shall be reconciled by the 10th of the month to which the accounts relate, at the latest by the 2nd day of the following month. A copy of the statement must be sent to the Controller.
(2) The Controlling Authorities shall collate all the returns and compare them with the monthly revenue returns received by the Accountant General after every quarter and if there is any discrepancy, the same shall be reconciled.
(3) In view of the monthly fund distribution system, the head of the office/ Drawing and Disbursing Officer should keep a tab on the amount of expenditure to be disbursed by the government till the 10th of every month. A copy of the statement must be sent to the Controller.
4) The statements received from the Controller General of Accounts from the Head of Office/ Drawing and Disbursing Officer in respect of the accounting of expenditure should be classified sub-heading wise/ index wise and matched with the accounts maintained in the Accountant General's Office after every quarter.
5) The Head of the Department/ Office handling the expenditure should ensure that monthly entries are made in the Budget Rule Book Sample No. 10. or the registration.
