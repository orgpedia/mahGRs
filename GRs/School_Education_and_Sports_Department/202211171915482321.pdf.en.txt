Introduction: The Shiv Chhatrapati Sports City has been built in an area of 151 acres near the village of Mhalunge-Balewadi in Pune on the occasion of the National Games held in Pune in 1994. After the National Games, the stadium was used to host various sports events and sports activities at various levels in the state. On the occasion of 3rd Commonwealth Youth Games 2008, various international level sports facilities at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex have been renovated and some new facilities have been created. The 9 sports facilities include shooting, badminton, athletics, boxing, wrestling, table tennis, lawn tennis, weightlifting, swimming, sports science centre, fitness centre and hostels. The sports complex has 15 different types of facilities like kabaddi, kho-kho, velodrome, football ground etc. Since all these facilities are used regularly, the use of these facilities often leads to expenditure on day-to-day maintenance and repair of the sports complex.
