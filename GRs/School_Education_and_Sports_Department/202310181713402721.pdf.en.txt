Preface: The mother. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the English Medium Self-Financed Higher Secondary (E1st to 12th) (All Branches) School of Lila Multi-Purpose Social Education Institute, Narsala, Nagpur run Ansh Public School and Junior College, Mauje Vihirgaon, G. Nagpur to Hudkeshwar Road, Pimpalaphata, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school building is proposed to be shifted as it is dilapidated and dangerous. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) As the school is a self-financing English medium school, the entire responsibility of the working teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
