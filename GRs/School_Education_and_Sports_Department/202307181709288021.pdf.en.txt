Introduction: To select a new Consulting Company for e-Governance projects for School Education Department. The presentation was made at the Project Implementation Committee meeting on 5 th August, 2022. Two e-Governance consulting firms participated in the presentation. The project implementation committee has decided to appoint Ernst& Young for the department. The reference No. Ernst& Young has been selected as the E-Governance Consultant for a period of one year and 5 Technical Consultants have been approved for appointment. Now the matter of approving the appointment of 5 more advisors was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As per the decision taken by the Project Implementation Committee in its meeting dated 5 th August, 2022, Ernst& Young has been selected as the e-Governance Consultant for a period of one year. The reference No. Under this, 5 technical advisors of Ernst& Young have been approved for appointment. The appointment of five new advisors is now being approved.
