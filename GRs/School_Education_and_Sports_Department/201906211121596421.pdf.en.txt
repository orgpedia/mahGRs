The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Marathi medium aided primary and upper primary school in Nanded to Shri Manikrao Shinde Sarvagina Shikshan Vikas Mandal, Nanded was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Shri Manikrao Shinde Sarvagina Shikshan Vikas Board, Nanded on the following terms and conditions: 1. The eligible teaching and non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be adjusted and there will be no change in their service conditions. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
2) Personal approval of the qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be required. It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff in the school as per the revised norms of accreditation dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government policy regarding the adjustment of additional teachers and filling the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
4) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
The new institution will be required to provide a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities to the school as per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the Government Orders issued in this regard from time to time.

6) The school will continue to function on the same grant-in-aid basis after the transfer.

The change of management will be implemented in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated February 17, 2012.
(10) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
