Government Circular: The following instructions are being issued regarding reimbursement of tuition fees for the academic year 2018-19 for the schools which have given admission under 25% under the Right to Education Act.
The amount of reimbursement of RTE 25% admission should be distributed to the concerned schools after the approval of the Chief Executive Officer of the Zilla Parishad.
The departmental education deputy director, Mumbai division, has to pay the reimbursement dues of schools affiliated to other boards in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.
The pending reimbursement amounts of the schools whose reimbursement proposals are pending from the commencement of the online admission process till the year 2017-18 shall be paid as per the rates fixed for that year.
4) The fee reimbursement for the academic year 2018-19 is Rs. 17,670/- should be approved.
5) 50 per cent of the school reimbursement amount for the year 2018-19 should be paid to the schools as per the fixed rate.
The following should be verified while paying 50% of the amount in 2018-19.
The schools which are providing 25 per cent admission under the Right to Education Act should have declared the details of the fee to be charged for students of classes 1 to 8 on the Saral portal or the RTE portal. Schools that have their own website should also declare the details of the fee on it.
The school should have obtained a certificate of recognition of RTE under the Right to Education Act.
