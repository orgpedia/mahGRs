The Proposal:
In order to prepare and implement an action plan for the participation of maximum number of players from Maharashtra in the 2020 Olympic Games to be held in Tokyo Japan and to get at least 20 medals for Maharashtra, the reference No. (1) The decision of the Government. The Executive Committee was constituted on 16th October, 2015 under the Chairmanship of Joint Director (Sports). The committee decided to prepare an action plan for the selection of potential talent in 16 selected sports of Maharashtra for the 2020 Olympics by determining the eligibility criteria and finalizing the selection of the players. A total of 175 players were selected.
After reviewing the performance of the probables in the said list, the selected athletes were invited for financial assistance for upgradation of sports facilities vide Reference No. (2) Here.
As per the Government decision dated 17.11.2018, a total of Rs. 189.00 lakhs has been disbursed. Further, since the Tokyo Olympics include Para Games, the sportspersons with disabilities selected for the same as well as general sportspersons will be provided sports facilities to upgrade their sports facilities. (3) The decision of the Government, d. 02.12.2020, Dy. 18.12.2020 and D. As on 03.02.2021, a total of Rs. 183.00 lakhs has been disbursed.
The matter of sanctioning necessary financial assistance for upgrading sports facilities to the remaining general athletes who have qualified for the Tokyo Olympic Games was under the consideration of the government.
