The construction of the Olympic Building has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs.1 crore, which was made available through supplementary demand in the Winter Session-2020. The Finance Department has approved the release of the remaining 50% of the additional funds amounting to Rs. 4455.00 lakhs.
Accordingly, Rs. 4455.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 2227.50 lakh (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) was under consideration for release of funds by making available on Budget Distribution System (BDS).
Government's decision:
Rs. Administrative approval has been accorded for the project at a cost of Rs. 44.70 crore. For the purpose of this construction, the amount available by way of Supplementary Demand under the head Demand No.E-3, Principal Accounts Head 2204, Sports and Youth Services, 104, Sports and Games, (16) (02) Establishment of Sports Complexes, (2204 1827), 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) is Rs. 4455.00 lakhs out of which the balance amount of Rs. 2227.50 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) and placed under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, State of Maharashtra, Pune.
(2) The purpose for which the funds are provided. It should be spent on the same.
The funds should be released from the budgetary provision made for the establishment of sports complexes in 2019-20.
The above funds should be spent as per the agreed criteria as per the requirement.
The funds must be used at the same time as they are spent. The money should not be kept outside the government account.
