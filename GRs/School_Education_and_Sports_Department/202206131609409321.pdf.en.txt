The Secretary of School Education and Sports Department has his office in the fourth floor of the Ministry Extension Building at hall number 424, which is broken and the furniture is old. Also, since personal assistants and clerical typists sit very tightly, their seating arrangements and furniture need to be revamped.
Therefore, for the renovation of this place, for the construction works and furniture etc. work, the Secretary, School Education and Sports Department, prepared by the Executive Engineer, Department of City Department, Public Works Department, Mumbai, has given Rs. 28,21,649/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Six Hundred and Nineteen only) and Rs. 7,77,261/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty One only) has been submitted.
2. Under this Government decision, the expenditure for the renovation of Building No. 424 (Extension Building) allotted to the Office of the Secretary (School Education and Sports Department) and Office in Mantralaya Extension Building will be Rs. 28,21,649/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Six Hundred and Nineteen Fifty only) and the cost of electrification with air-conditioning is Rs. 7,77,261/- (Rupees Seven Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty One only) aggregating to Rs. 35,98,910/- (Rupees Thirty Five Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand Nine Hundred and Ten only) is being accorded administrative approval.
