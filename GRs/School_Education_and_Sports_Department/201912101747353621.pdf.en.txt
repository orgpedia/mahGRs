Introduction: The Sports Policy 2012 has been formulated to implement the welfare schemes for the players in the state by keeping in mind that the state of Maharashtra should be a leader in the field of sports, the interest of exercise should be nurtured among the citizens, sports-related technical training for the players of the state to perform well at the national and international level, improvement in the quality of sports, glory to the players, quality sports facilities. A Government Resolution dated 14.06.2012 has been issued to implement the same. As per the recommendations of the committee, a District Sports Training Centre will be set up in every district to provide training facilities to the sportspersons of the state.
