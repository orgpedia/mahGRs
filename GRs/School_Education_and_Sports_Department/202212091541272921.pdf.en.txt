The Proposal:
A total of 5276 temporary posts in the zonal offices under the School Education Department have been notified under reference No. The deadline was extended from September 1, 2022, to February 28, 2023. As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, the office of Directorate of Minority and Adult Education, Pune has been renamed as Directorate of Education (Scheme). As per the Government decision dated 22.6.2022, the Directorate of Minority and Adult Education, Pune has been renamed as Directorate of Education Scheme. Commissioner of Education No. Issue of purification letter under section 5 was under consideration.
Accordingly, the following is issued.
GOVERNMENT CREDIT: The existing 5276 temporary posts in the Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and its subordinate field offices have been extended from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 as per the Government Decision dated 8th September, 2022. The establishment-wise information in this regard is given in the Statement-A accompanying the Government Decision.
