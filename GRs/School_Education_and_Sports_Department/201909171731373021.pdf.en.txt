Solapur, a Marathi medium unaided secondary school (class 5th to 10th), Ausa, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter regarding relocation to Ausa, Latur district was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted due to managerial difficulties. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. : (1) It will be necessary to finalize by filling in simple, set approval, U-Dias report, etc. information about the school.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
