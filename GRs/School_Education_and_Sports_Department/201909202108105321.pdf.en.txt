Introduction: Sri Nilambika Basaveshwara Educational Institutions, Sankh, Ta. New English School, Akkalwadi. Jath, G. Sangli, a Kannada medium unaided secondary school, Jalihal, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate Jt. G. Sangli was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted due to managerial and technical difficulties. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) A fee of Rs. 10,000/- is required to be paid.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff.
