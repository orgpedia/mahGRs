Preamble: - Government officers / employees and their families may, subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued thereunder from time to time, seek hospitalization for 27 co-morbidities and 5 critical illnesses arising out of emergencies. The Government Decision of 19.03.2005 prescribes the procedure for approving a medical expense reimbursement proposal. On the same lines, medical expense reimbursement benefits are provided to in-service teaching / non-teaching staff and their families in private aided primary schools under the Education Board of Municipal Corporations / Municipalities / Municipal Councils in the state of Maharashtra.
