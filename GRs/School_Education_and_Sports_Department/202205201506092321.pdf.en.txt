Department of School Education and Sports, Government Notification No.STR-2021/ PR No. 139/ PRISA-5, dated 20th May, 2022
Read the government's decision here. As per the notification dated 20 th May, 2022, 8 School Tribunals have been constituted in the State by reorganizing the School Tribunals.
The School Tribunal for Mumbai Division will be located at CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai. 3 existing school tribunals in Mumbai, Chandrapur and Solapur are being closed.
The process of taking over the records from the closed tribunal and transferring them to the concerned school tribunal should be done by the concerned Deputy Director of Divisional Education. The services of the presiding officers of the 3 school tribunals that have been closed. The Under Secretary, PRSA-2, School Education and Sports Department should take necessary action regarding the transfer to the High Court. Thereafter, the process of transfer of Group C and Group D employees in the said 3 School Tribunals to the equivalent vacant post in the department should be done by the concerned Deputy Director of Divisional Education.
