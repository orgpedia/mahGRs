The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Circular No: Sashaishu-2021/ PR No. 39/21/ SM-6
Madame Cama Road, Ministry of Expansion
Mumbai- 400032
Date: 16th of March 2021
circular
The Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act, 2011 and the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Rules, 2016 and the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) (Amendment) Act, 2018 have been framed by the government. In view of the continuous complaints of parents regarding school fees, a committee has been formed under the chairmanship of Joint Secretary, School Education Department under the government decision dated March 5, 2021, to suggest amendments in the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act and suggestions are being sought from parents/ parents associations/ educational institutions as well as the general public for the consideration of this committee.
The suggestions to be submitted to the Committee can be submitted online at www.research.net/ r/ feeregulation.
Therefore, all the concerned should submit their suggestions for suggesting changes/ amendments in the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Acts on the said website within a period of 1 month from the date of this circular.
