Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Volleyball Championship 2019-20 was held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai from 20th to 25th February, 2020. The exam was conducted on January 30, 2020. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." Selection Test, Coaching Camp and State Team for the tournament as per Form B (Men's Team) Rs. 60,920/- (Rupees Sixty Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty only) and as per return' B-1' (Women Team) Rs. 72,490/- (Rupees Seventy Two Thousand Four Hundred and Ninety only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the athletes/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 14th to 17th February, 2020 and for the competition from 20th to 25th February, 2020 with effect from 13th February, 2020 (M.No.) and the services of these athletes should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager of the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department Office under which they will be working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The period of absence from office from 14th to 24th February, 2020 (Training Camp, Competition and Travel) shall be treated as" Duty Period." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned. Also, the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai are as per the Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ SEWA-9 dated 11th May, 1989.
