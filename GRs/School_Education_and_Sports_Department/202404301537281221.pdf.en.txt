The working class of the study group shall be as follows: -
(1) To simplify the process with a view to providing the notified services more easily to the citizens and
Re-engineering the business process to make it smoother.
(2) Preparation of Common Application Form.
(3) Develop procedures for linking the service to Digi-Locker.
(4) Setting a time limit for the preservation of records in respect of notified services.
Duration of submission of report: -
The above study groups should submit their report to the government within three months.
The study group may hold meetings as needed.
