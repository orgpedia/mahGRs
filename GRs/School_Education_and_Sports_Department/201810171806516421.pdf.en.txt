Government Circular: Maharashtra Education Service, Group-C and District Technical Service, Group-C Cadre Employees have been selected for appointment to the post of Deputy Educational Officer and similar posts under the Maharashtra Education Service, Group-B (Administration Branch) under the School Education and Sports Department. The relevant Government Notifications and Circulars are available on the Government of Maharashtra website.
The notification of the School Education and Sports Department has indicated category-wise reservation for 123 posts of Deputy Education Officer and similar posts to be filled through Limited Departmental Examination-2017.

Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court. The Supreme Court has not given any stay. No orders have been issued to maintain status quo.

In view of the Supreme Court's order, reservation will not be applicable for the limited departmental examination conducted for promotion to the post of Deputy Education Officer and similar posts. Therefore, the 123 posts to be filled through the Limited Departmental Examination-2017 for the post of Deputy Educational Officer and similar posts announced by the above Government Notification/ Circular should be filled as per the norms.
