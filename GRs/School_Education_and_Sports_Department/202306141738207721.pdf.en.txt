Preamble: Sufia Educational Association, Nanded runs Al Sufia Urdu Primary to Higher Secondary School, Brahmanwada, T.G. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Nanded was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school is in a dilapidated condition, the owner has proposed to shift the building as he has given notice to vacate the building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the said school is on self-financing basis of Urdu medium.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
