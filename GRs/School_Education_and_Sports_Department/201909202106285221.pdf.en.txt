Preamble: Afkar Education Society, Khasbagh, Beed operated Pokhari Urdu Primary School, Hirapur, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of Urdu medium unaided primary and upper primary school, Beed, to Bundelpura, Old Sabzi Mandi, G. Beed was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted due to inadequate physical facilities. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. Fee: (1) Rs. 10,000/- is required to be paid.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(3) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Beed shall ensure that there is a need for an Urdu medium primary school at the place of relocation of the said school.
(4) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(7) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(8) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(9) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff shall continue to be employed.
It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy to fill the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
(10) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (1), (4) and (5), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall take further action within 4 weeks regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the government about the action taken.
