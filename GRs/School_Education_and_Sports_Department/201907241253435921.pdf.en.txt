The Proposal- M. The Chairman, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat had convened a meeting on 26/6/2019 to discuss the implementation of the old pension scheme for all the teachers and non-teaching staff appointed before 1st November, 2005 in all the aided, unaided, partially aided posts and batches in the State. At the meeting, The Speaker, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat directed to form a joint committee of the Education Department, Finance Department and the Secretary of the Law and Justice Department. Accordingly, the matter of forming a joint committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
A Joint Committee is being constituted to study the applicability of the old pension scheme to all teachers and non-teaching staff appointed before 1st November, 2005 in all the aided, unaided, partially aided posts and batches in the State. The committee is as follows:
