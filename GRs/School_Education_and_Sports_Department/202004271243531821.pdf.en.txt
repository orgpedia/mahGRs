The aforesaid funds/ grants made available by the Department of Finance on the BEAMS system during the financial year 2020-21 are being released subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. No expenditure shall be incurred for purposes other than those for which the funds/ grants are sanctioned. Also, you can't switch to other titles.
2) The progress report of the said expenditure should be submitted to the government by the 10th of every month by the controlling authorities as per the monthly fund statement.
3) The rules mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget rule book should be followed while disbursing the subsidy. Further, the Finance Department shall pay the subsidy after obtaining the approval of the competent authority in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I as provided under Government Decision No. V.P.-2013/ PR. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015.
(4) It shall be ensured that action is taken in accordance with the suggestions given by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries/ Cabinet as well as the Finance Department, where necessary, with regard to the allocation of funds on salary in accordance with the creation of posts.
(5) When providing grants to private NGOs, the grant should be paid with the approval of the competent authority in accordance with the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-1, Sub-Division I, serial number 27, rule 149.
(6) In the case of the distribution of provisions for which an administrative sanction order is required, the grant shall be disbursed after the order is issued with the approval of the competent authority as provided under Part-I, Sub-Division-III, Schedule-IV, Rule 27 (2).
7) The post-salary grants to the Zila Parishads should not be disbursed without their grants being determined.
8) The approval of the Finance Department is required in respect of the disbursement of funds for the schemes provided under the new head in accordance with the new expenditure.
9) Before sanctioning the grants-in-aid to various Government/ Administrative Institutions and Aided Institutions, the Department should obtain utilization certificates of the expenditure of the grants-in-aid disbursed earlier. Further grants should not be disbursed unless the utilization of the earlier grant is completed. The treasury officers should not pass the payment unless a copy of the bank account statement for the previous month is attached with the payment submitted for drawing funds from the subsidiary grant.
10) The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
11) Before disbursing grants to local self-government institutions and corporations, the amount coming to the state government from these institutions should be reviewed and recovered.
