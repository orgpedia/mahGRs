The Proposal:
As per the Government Decision dated 23.02.2018 and 02.05.2018, the post of Addl. Secretary in Group B cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) has been appointed for a period of two years.
The proposal to complete the period of probation has been submitted vide letter dated 25.11.2021 and 10.01.2022 of Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per the prevailing policy of the Government, the officers mentioned in the accompanying form working in the post of Addressee in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-B Cadre under the Government Decision dated 23.02.2018 and 02.05.2018, mentioned above, who are fulfilling the conditions of probationary period, are being completed the probationary period on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 7 in front of their names and the Government is approving their continuation of service on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 8.
