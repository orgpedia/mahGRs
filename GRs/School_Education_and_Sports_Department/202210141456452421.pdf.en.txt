Government Decision: Orders have been issued for the promotion of Shri Rajmohan Hariprasad Sharma, Superintendent Mashiye in the selection list 2021-22 for the vacant posts of Administrative Officer Cadre in Maharashtra Education Service Group-A (Administration Branch). Shri Sharma has declined the promotion under his application dated 26.07.2022 due to his family problems.
As per the provision mentioned herein in the Government Resolution dated 12.09.2016 of the General Administration Department, the name of Shri Rajmohan Hariprasad Sharma is being excluded from the Selection List for the year 2021-22. Therefore, the order dated 19.07.2022 regarding promotion of Shri Sharma is being cancelled.
