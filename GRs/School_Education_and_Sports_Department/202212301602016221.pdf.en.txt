The government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Basketball Tournament 2022-23, to be held at Chhatrasal Stadium, New Delhi from 05th to 08th January, 2023, will be held at Sabina Chandrashekhar Recreation Ground, Mumbai. The selection test was conducted on 13th December, 2022. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Rs. 59,700/- (Rupees Nineteen Thousand Seven Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
