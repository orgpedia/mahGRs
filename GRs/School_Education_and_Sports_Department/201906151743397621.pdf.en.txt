Introduction: The Vidarbha Multipurpose Society, Bhandara runs Gangabai High School, Chaprad, Ta. A proposal to transfer a Marathi medium unaided secondary school in Lakhandur, G Bhandara, to the Maharashtra Backward Classes Educational and Social Development Board, Bhandara, was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said Primary School to Maharashtra Backward Classes Educational and Social Development Board, Bhandara on the following terms and conditions: 1.
(2) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason by the school where the management has changed.
3) The school will continue to function on the same grant-in-aid basis as before the transfer.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the new institution has violated the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management.
2. Clause No. (1) On completion of this section, the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur, shall examine all the matters and take further action within six weeks, including other necessary conditions, regarding the change of management of the school and inform the Government about the action taken.
