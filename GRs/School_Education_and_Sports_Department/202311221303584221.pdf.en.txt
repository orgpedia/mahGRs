Tenders were invited from the Government website vide Notice dated 12.10.2023 for entering into a one-year contract with the supplier for supply of stationery and essential office materials for the School Education and Sports Department (itself). of the proposals received. Devish
The tender submitted by Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai is technically sound and is less expensive than other bidders. May for a period of one year from 01st November, 2023 to 31st October, 2024. Devish Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai has been unanimously selected to tender and contract with them subject to the following terms and conditions. Department of School Education and Sports
The period of supply of high quality stationery for (self) will be from 1.11.2023 to 31.10.2024.
(1) It shall be obligatory to supply materials and stationery of the quality of the company or better mentioned in the tender, as per the demand registered by the Department from time to time, at the rate mentioned in the contract immediately.
On requisition of stationery as required by the Department, it shall be incumbent upon the supplier to supply the requisitioned stationery as per the prescribed rates and standards. Any change in the quality or price of the supplied stationery will be acceptable to the supplier, who will decide to reject the goods and terminate the contract if the compliance of the contract is not satisfactory.
(ii) If the Department is in urgent need of goods other than those mentioned in the list, it shall be obliged to supply the same immediately after registering the demand for the said goods. Also, it will be mandatory for the supplier to supply such goods at a rate less than 5 per cent of the market price (MRP).
(iv) Since the rates mentioned by the suppliers are in addition to GST, the rate of GST admissible on the goods concerned shall be paid additionally.
