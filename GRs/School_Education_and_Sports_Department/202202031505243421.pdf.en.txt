Regarding the distribution of funds for the payment of salaries to the teaching and non-teaching staff of Krida Maharishi Prof. D. B. Deodhar Sports School.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No. 1721/ PR No. 142/ CRUSE-2,
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 3, 2022.
Read:- Commissioner Sports and Youth Services, Pune Shikshipi/ PRASHA/ Salary/ Provision/ 2021-22/ KA- 11/831 dated 23.12.2021.
Preamble: In a letter addressed to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, a proposal has been received to provide funds for payment of salaries to the teaching and non-teaching staff of Krida Maharishi Prof. D. B. Deodhar Sports School for the month of November 2021 to February 2022.
Accordingly, the said award has been received by the Government for the above purpose. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to make available the said funds for the above purpose under the heading" Demand No. E-3, 2204, (33) (01) Establishment of Sports University (2204, 2064), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)."
Government Decision: Administrative approval is being given to sanction funds to the tune of Rs. 43,16,187/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs Sixteen Thousand One Hundred and Eighty Seven only) for payment of salaries to the teaching and non-teaching staff of Krida Maharishi Prof. D. B. Deodhar Krida Prabodhini's Secondary and Higher Secondary School, Pune for the month of November, 2021 to February, 2022.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No. E-3, 2204, (33) (01) Establishment of Sports University (2204, 2064), 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)."
(3) The grant shall be used for the purpose for which it is granted.
