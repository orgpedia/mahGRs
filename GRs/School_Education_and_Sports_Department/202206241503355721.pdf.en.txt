Government order:
Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) to regulate the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Advocate (Group-A), and Advocate (Group-B) in the cadre
The Rules, 2018 have been notified vide Notification dated 26.02.2018. As per the proviso to Rule 4 (1) (a) of the said Rules, the officers mentioned in the bracket below, who are working in the Education Empowerment Branch and have completed 55 years of age, are exempted from the departmental examination.
