Latur, Urdu Medium Unaided Primary School, Al-Huda Social Welfare Society, Malegaon, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the institution to G. Nashik was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Al-Huda Social Welfare Society, Malegaon, T.G. Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(2) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) The financial position of the Al Huda Social Welfare Society, Malegaon, G. Nasik should be ensured on the basis of the balance/ balance in the bank account of the institution.
(4) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff than that.

(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (1) and (2), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur shall examine all the matters and take further action within six weeks regarding change of management of the school, including necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
