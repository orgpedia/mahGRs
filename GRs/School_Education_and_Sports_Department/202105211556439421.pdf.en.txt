Government order
37 employees of the Chief Clerk Cadre of the selection list for the year 2019-20 have been promoted to the post of Superintendent, General State Service, Group-B (Administration Branch) under the Government Order No. 1 under reference.
Orders regarding the posting have been issued and in this order, at 2 respectively, Shri.
India includes Shivdas Dhawale. However, in pursuance of the decision of the Government at Reference No. 2, Shri.
The services of Bharat Dhawale have been transferred to higher ranks.
Since Mr. Bharat Dhawale's services have been transferred to the post of Superintendent, General State Service, Group-B (Administration Branch), his name is eligible for promotion to the post of Superintendent, General State Service, Group-B (Administration Branch) from the selection list of Chief Clerk Cadre for the year 2019-20 as well as Reference No. 1.
The same is being omitted from the order dated 27.01.2021.
