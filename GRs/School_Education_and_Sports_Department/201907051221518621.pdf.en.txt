As per the decision of the Government in the aforesaid Reference No. 1, the" State Teachers Awards" will be announced on 15th August, 2019 to the teachers who have done excellent work in the field of education in the State. In the context of the State Teacher Award 2018-2019, the criteria for the selection of award proposals have been issued by the Government Circular No. 2 and the State Model Teacher Award proposals are being invited online. The Department of School Education will be organizing the award distribution and felicitation of these teachers at the government level on Teachers' Day on September 5, 2019. The proposal for the estimated expenditure for the function has been submitted to the Government by the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 7th June, 2019. The matter of administrative approval of the expenses to be incurred for the award ceremony was under consideration of the government.
The government has decided to give State Awards to Primary and Secondary Teachers, Savitribai Phule Awards and State Awards to Teachers for the year 2018-2019 under the State Award Scheme for outstanding work in the tribal areas and to organize award ceremonies for them. 181.50 lakhs (Rs. One crore eighty one lakh fifty thousand only) is providing administrative sanction for such expenditure.
