Structure, Appropriation and Management of State Sports Development Fund by Regulating
The orders are as follows:
Sources of State Sports Development Fund:
1) An amount to the extent of Rs. 50.00 lakh (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) per annum from the budgetary provision of the State Government shall be deposited in the State Sports Development Fund.
2) Donations from various industrial or commercial establishments will be credited to the fund. However, at present, no concessions will be allowed to the institutions concerned in lieu of this, 3) 40 per cent of the amount to be deposited in the District Development Fund by the Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation and Municipality from the district, 4) 40 per cent of the amount to be deposited in the District Sports Development Fund by the educational institutions.
5) The amount collected from the events like exhibition matches, cultural programmes, etc. will be deposited in this fund.
Objectives/ Objectives of the Fund:
1) To provide financial assistance for pre-international training to the talented, talented and needy players of the state.
2) To finance the purchase/ import of Government approved foreign quality sports equipment, 3) To finance the athletes/ mentors/ umpires for international seminars/ competitions/ camps, 4) To finance seminars/ workshops/ lecture classes conducted by the Government, 5) To finance the construction of sports facilities and similar equipment.
(6) In addition, the scheme for the development of sports as may be considered appropriate by the Committee constituted to regulate the said Fund shall be funded out of this Fund, subject to the prior approval of the Committee.
1) The account of the State Sports Development Fund is in the official national bank of Bank of Maharashtra, Mantralaya Branch, Mumbai and the savings account number of the fund is 20045735604.
