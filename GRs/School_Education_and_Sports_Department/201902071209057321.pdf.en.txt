Latur of Marathi medium unaided primary and upper primary school, Dattanagar, Madlapur, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Udgir, Latur district was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is being shifted from the existing inadequate space to a self-sufficient building. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure that no one has any objection/ objection regarding the adjustment of all the students in the school as well as the relocation of the school.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- is to be submitted and duly filled.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(7) The school shall function at the place of relocation on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(8) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfilment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur shall take necessary further action for approval of relocation of the said school and shall apprise the Government of the action taken.
