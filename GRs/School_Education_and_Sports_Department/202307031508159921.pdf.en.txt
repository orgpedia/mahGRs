Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure and eligibility criteria of the institution in this regard are mentioned in the above Reference No. The Government's decision herein has been dated 01.03.2014. Accordingly, eligible institutions are provided financial assistance in the ratio of 75:25 (75% Government share and 25% related institution share) for construction of sports facilities and financial assistance for sports materials. Reference No. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraphs 4 (a) and (b) of the Government Decision. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune has issued reference No. Under Section 3, the proposal of eligible institutions has been submitted to the government along with the recommendation. Following the recommendations, the government took the decision. Subject to the terms and conditions of 1.3.2014, the proposal for grant of financial assistance for construction of sports facilities was under consideration of the Government for grant of in-principle administrative approval.
Government Decision: The Government Decision referred to in Annexure-A below, subject to the eligibility for grant as on 01.03.2014, compliance of all the terms and conditions of grant approval, scrutiny of the project cost and other conditions.
