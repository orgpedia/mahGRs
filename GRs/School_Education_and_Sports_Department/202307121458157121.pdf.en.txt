Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is as under. (1) The Government decision herein has been made effective as of March 1, 2014. 75:25 for the construction of sports facilities to the educational institutions, local/ civic bodies, schools of local self-government institutions, junior colleges, ashramshalas of the Tribal Development Department, divisional/ district/ taluka sports complexes of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. which are registered under the Public Trust Act, 1950 or the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and are engaged in the development of various sports. The approval has been granted on the basis of the terms and conditions prescribed under the Government Decision No. 1.
Proposals of various organizations received by the Government from the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune, vide No. (2) Administrative approval has been given in principle under the government decisions here. It was under consideration of the Government to disburse from the budgetary fund for the financial year 2023-24 to provide grants to the institutions which have been given the above administrative approval.

Government's decision:
Financial assistance for construction of sports facilities for the financial year 2023-24 out of the budgeted provision for the scheme is Rs. 74.00 lakhs (Rupees Seventy Four Lakhs only) to the concerned institutions as shown in the accompanying return A. Acceptance is subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2014 in the matter No. 4 (b) to take action for disbursement of grants to the institutions concerned subject to all the terms and conditions of sanction of grants for purchase of sports equipment.
(2) It shall be mandatory to submit the certificate of completion of the project along with the photographs of the project and the utilization certificate within a maximum period of 1 year after the disbursement of the grant as indicated in Annexure-A with the concerned institution, otherwise the distributed grant will be recovered from the date of disbursement of the grant at the prevailing penal interest rate of the Government.
3) It will be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate by spending the government subsidy.
4) Refer No. 1 decision of the government. 01.03.2014. 4 (b) (10) The condition of self-assurance shall not be applicable to government institutions for grant of sports materials.
2. Subject to the above conditions, out of the provision budgeted for Financial Assistance Scheme for construction of sports facilities for the financial year 2023-24, an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 74.00 lakhs (Rupees Seventy Four Lakh only) should be disbursed.
3. Financial Assistance for Construction of Sports Facilities Expenditure in respect of the Scheme is estimated to be Rs. The E-3 accounts should be made from the available provision under the head" 2204, Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (40) Sports Facilities, (40) (01) Assistance for Construction of Sports Facilities (Programmes) (2204 5706), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
4. Action should be taken to spend the above provision as per the directions given by the Finance Department from time to time regarding the distribution of funds. The District Sports Officer of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune as the Controlling Officer.
