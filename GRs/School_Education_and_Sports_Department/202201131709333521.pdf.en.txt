Government's statement:
Smt. Manjiri Pramod Salvi, Principal of 100% aided schools of Maratha Mandir, Mumbai, run by Maratha Mandir, S.R.Desai, Adhyapak Vidyalaya, Ratnagiri on 31.5.2020
The post of principal has been lying vacant since his retirement. Therefore, Shri Netaji Shivaji Kumbhar, the best serving teacher of the school, has been appointed as the headmaster of the school vide order dated 30.5.2020.
The post of Principal has been promoted with effect from 1.6.2020. The proposal to approve the said promotion was submitted by the concerned institution to the Deputy Director of Teachers, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur vide letter dated 30.6.2020. However, no decision has been taken on the proposal yet.
Before receiving the proposal from the institution regarding the approval of the promotion to the post of Principal, the Deputy Director of Teachers,
Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur vide letter dated 28.1.2020 had requested the Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra State, Pune to provide guidance on specific issues regarding service excellence. Accordingly, the Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted the proposal to the Government vide letter dated 16.3.2020.
""" 2. Pursuant to the proposal under reference, it is informed that the seniority list of teachers of secondary/ higher secondary and teacher schools should be maintained in the same manner as mentioned in Schedule"""" F"""" of the Maharashtra Private Schools Employees (Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981.""" However, considering the qualifications prescribed by the National Council for Teacher Education regarding the eligibility for the post of Principal in a Teacher School, the
Teachers cannot be promoted to the post of principal on the basis of seniority list. For this promotion, both the eligibility and seniority list prescribed for the post of principal by the National Council for Teacher Education will have to be taken into account.
