Atfal Urdu Primary School, Gulzar Bagh, Gali No. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift the Urdu medium aided primary (class 1st to 7th) school, 6, Nanded, to Nimrah Colony, Gadegaon Roll, near Alu Onion Market, Nanded, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The relocation has been proposed due to the termination of the lease of the above school. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above-mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
