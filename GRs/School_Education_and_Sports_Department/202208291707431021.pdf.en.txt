The Proposal:
The State Selection Committee has been constituted for final selection of Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award under Para 6 of Government Decision No. PTC-2022/ PR No. 34/ TNT-4 dated 28.06.2022. As per Clause 6 of the Constitution of the State Selection Committee, two government-appointed representatives with outstanding achievements in the field of education are to be appointed as members.
The Government has received a proposal from the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune regarding the appointment of two non-official members with outstanding achievements in the field of education on the State Selection Committee for the selection process for the Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award for the year 2021-22.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following two non-official members with outstanding achievements in the field of education are being appointed on the State Selection Committee for the selection process for Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award for the year 2021-22.
