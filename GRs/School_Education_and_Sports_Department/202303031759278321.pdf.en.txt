Introduction: The Government of India. Bhaskarraoji Shingane, Sports and Multipurpose Institute, Palaskhed, Jhalta, Ta. Deulgaon Raja, G. Buldhana Governing Government Maharishi Late. Bhaskara Raoji Shingane Secondary School, Giroli, Ta. Kulswamini Tuljabhavani Education Sports and Rural Development Multi-Purpose Institute, Khalyal Gawan, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of management to Deulgaon Raja, G. Buldhana was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of this school is managed by Tuljabhavani Education Sports and Rural Development Multi-purpose Institute, Khalyal Govan, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Deulgaon Raja, Buldhana on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.

(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated, or
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer of teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management if the conditions of service are violated by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati, shall examine all the matters and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
