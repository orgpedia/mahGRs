No.12/ TNT-2, dated 11th December, 2020 Preamble: Reference No. (1) As per the Government decision, dated 19th January, 2013, on 1st April, 2008, the teachers/ non-teaching staff of private primary/ secondary and higher secondary schools in the State under the School Education Department who were on salary subsidy were given a post-pay subsidy (4% post-pay subsidy and 1% building rent/ maintenance subsidy) of about Rs. 266.82 crore has been approved to be made available from 1st April, 2013.
Also, reference No. (2) Instead of applying the revised figure for the Class IV employees of the recognized private partially/ fully aided middle and high schools in the State as per the Government decision, dated 11th December, 2020, the Per-shala peon allowance has been applied for the post which will lapse after the Class IV employees retire as per the prescribed age. Accordingly, for the financial year 2021-22, the government is considering to sanction additional grant-in-aid to the private aided primary/ secondary and higher secondary schools in the state to meet their non-salary expenses and to pay peon's allowance to the private partially/ fully aided secondary and higher secondary schools in proportion to the posts vacant as per the prescribed age of the class IV employees.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of the Government Resolution, dated 19th January, 2013, the recognized primary, secondary and higher secondary schools in the State are entitled to receive Rs. 26,75,20, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Seventy Five Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) as non-salary grant.
