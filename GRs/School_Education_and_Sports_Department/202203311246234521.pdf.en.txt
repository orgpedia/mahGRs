Preamble: Subject to grant in the ratio of 75:25 (Government and related institutions) for the construction of sports facilities and sports materials to educational institutions run by private/ government in the State and recognized by the concerned departments of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act, 1950 or the Societies Registration Act, 1860. 1 has been approved by the government. One of the important recommendations of the sports policy is to provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities in the state through educational institutions, private clubs, sports boards, authorities, local bodies and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons.
The detailed procedure in this regard is as under. (1) The Government decision herein has been made effective as of March 1, 2014. The proposals of various organizations received by the Government from the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune, vide No. (2) and (3) are hereby granted administrative approval in principle. Further, the proposal for demand of remaining funds of the institutions which were earlier given partial grants (Phase I/ II) is under reference No. (4) The Commissioner, Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, vide Reference No. 4 submitted to the Government. By providing the next tranche of grants to the institutions which have been granted the above administrative approval as well as to the institutions which have been granted partial grants (Phase I/ II) earlier.

The Government is planning to provide financial assistance for the year 2021-22.
Government's decision:
For the year 2021-22, Rs. 1800.00 lakh out of which 60% of the provision of Rs. 1080.00 lakhs (Rupees Ten Crores Eighty Lakhs only) is hereby approved to be disbursed towards grant-in-aid to the respective institutions as shown in the accompanying statement A and B. Acceptance is subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2014 in the matter No. 4 (b) to take action for disbursement of grants to the concerned institutions as per the terms and conditions of sanction of grants for sports materials.
(2) It shall be mandatory to submit the certificate of completion of the project, along with the photographs of the project and the utilization certificate within a maximum period of 1 year after the disbursement of the grant as indicated in the statement in Forms" A" and" B" accompanying the institution concerned, otherwise the grant distributed shall be recovered from the date of disbursement of the grant at the prevailing penal interest rate of the Government. Also, the concerned institution in Form B will be required to obtain a bank guarantee for 10% of the total grant amount to be sanctioned.
Accordingly, after depositing the bank guarantee and affidavit with the concerned District Sports Officer, the funds available for the disbursement of the sanctioned amount should be disbursed to the concerned institution through RTGS.
(3) It shall be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate by paying the government subsidy and self-contribution. The reference No. 1 of the Government Decision dated 1st March, 2014, No. 4 (b) (10) does not apply to government institutions.
(4) The remaining amount due to the concerned institution from the Regional Office/ Directorate shall be disbursed in the next financial year subject to the provision made by the concerned institution only after first furnishing a statement of the cost of the 25% contribution from the concerned institution in Form' B' and the subsidy disbursed under this Government Decision, along with the cost of signing the Chartered Accountant with the photographs of the present status of the said construction.
(5) The bank guarantee of the institution concerned shall be released after the institution concerned has first spent 25% of its share and submitted the return to the concerned District Sports Officer as indicated in the above table (2).
