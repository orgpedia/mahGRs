Government's decision:
The selection of Maharashtra Government team for All India Civil Services Powerlifting/ Weightlifting& Bodybuilding Championship 2019-20 held at Patan, Bihar from 4th to 8th March, 2020 was done at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai. The exam was conducted on February 18, 2020. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,52,180/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Two Thousand One Hundred and Eighty only such expenditure is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 28th February to 1st March, 2020 and for the tournament from 4th to 8th March, 2020 with effect from 27th February, 2020 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The period of absence from office from 28th February to 10th March, 2020 (Training Camp, Competition and Travel) shall be treated as" Duty Period." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned.
