The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 1st July, 2006.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared as per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005. Provided further that the competent authority transferring in accordance with proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the power of transfer of officers of various cadres in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of the said Act, a proposal for transfer of officers who have completed three years of service as on 31st May, 2020 was submitted to the Civil Services Board. urban
The Services Board has taken into consideration the guidelines issued in the order dated 9th April, 2018 regarding transfers through counselling of General Administration Department and made recommendations regarding transfer of transferable officers in Group B. In view of the above recommendation, the officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 against their names, with the prior approval of the competent authority.
