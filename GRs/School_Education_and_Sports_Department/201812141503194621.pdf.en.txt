The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of Phulambri, G. Aurangabad to the Institute was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the school where the management is changed for any reason.
(2) The order of approval of the said school for classes 6 and 9 and 10 shall be required to be verified by the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Aurangabad.
(3) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be ensured that the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for the transfer of the school. Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.

(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff than that.
(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(10) If any fresh complaints are received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or the terms and conditions regarding the change of management are violated.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution prior to the change of management have been breached by the new institution.

Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall examine all the aspects and take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school including other necessary conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
