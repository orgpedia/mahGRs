Preamble: The Commissioner of Education had submitted a proposal to the Project Implementation Committee to procure 20 laptops under e-Governance to expedite the work of the offices of the Commissionerate of Education and the Subordinate Director, as well as the e-Governance Cell and NIC, Pune.
Following that. It has been approved in the meeting of the Project Implementation Committee held on 11.05.2022. Accordingly, the matter of approving the proposal for the purchase of laptops was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative and financial approval is being given for the purchase of 20 laptops for the Commissionerate of Education and Subordinate Director's Offices, e-Governance Cell and NIC, Pune from the funds sanctioned under the e-Governance Scheme in the financial year 2022-23. The specifications of the laptop to be purchased are mentioned in Annexure-A. The purchase of this laptop should be done by the Commissioner, Education Office as per the prevailing procurement policy of the government.
