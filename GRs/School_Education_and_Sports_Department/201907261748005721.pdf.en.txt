Introduction: Art Education Prasarak Mandal, Kolhapur operated Kedarling High School, Shindewadi, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of Karveer, G. Kolhapur, a Marathi medium unaided primary school, to Janata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Kolhapur, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Secondary School to Janata Shiksha Prasarak Mandal, Kolhapur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The said School shall continue to function on the same principle of grant-in-aid even after the transfer.
(2) It shall be ensured that in case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the teacher/ non-teacher working in the institution before the change of management
