GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A meeting was held at the Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai to select the Maharashtra Government team to participate in the All India Civil Services Table Tennis Tournament 2019-20 held at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune from 7th to 11th February, 2020. The exam was conducted on 13th and 14th January 2020. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,30,450/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of Department/ Head of Office concerned should relieve the athletes/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 4th to 6th February, 2020 and for the competition from 7th to 11th February, 2020 with effect from 03rd February, 2020 (M.No.) and provide the services of these athletes to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
