Proposal Procedure for sanction of Higher Secondary Schools/ Junior Colleges vide Order No. 1 and vide Order No. 2 is set out in the letter. Accordingly, the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary) has proposed to sanction additional batches/ additional branches to the secondary schools/ junior colleges in the state. Letters 3 to 10 are provided.
At present, it is the policy of the government to approve additional batches/ additional branches of class 11 on a self-financing basis. The proposal for sanctioning additional batches of Class 11 to these higher secondary schools/ junior colleges was under consideration of the government.

Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune
After examining the proposal, a total of 236 Higher Secondary Junior Colleges mentioned in the form accompanying this Government decision are being approved by the Government on a self-financing basis for a total of 283 additional batches/ additional branches of Class XI from the academic year 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20. The said additional batches/ branches are being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
The Deputy Director of Education of the concerned department should ensure that the required classrooms and physical facilities are available in these junior colleges.
2. The Deputy Director of Education concerned shall ensure that the strength of these additional batches is as per the prescribed norms.
The concerned Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad/ Inspector of Education, Brihanmumbai will have to ensure that the necessary educational facilities are available in these junior colleges.
(4) If the institution concerned has not already deposited the fee for the said additional batch by challan, it shall be required to submit a copy of the same to the Deputy Director of Education of the concerned department.
The institution will be required to declare the educational fee and ensure that all students have an Aadhaar card.
