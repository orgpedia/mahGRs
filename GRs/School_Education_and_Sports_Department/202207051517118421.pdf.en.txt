Introduction: Jai Kranti Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Latur, runs Maharashtra Junior College, Majgenagar, Latur, a Marathi medium aided higher secondary school (Arts, Science and Commerce stream), Plot No. The proposal received under the letter of reference to relocate 74, MIDC Kalamb Road, Latur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: From the building with inadequate physical facilities of the above higher secondary school, Plot No. It is proposed to be shifted to a self-contained building owned by the Institute at 74, MIDC Kalamb Road, Latur. Therefore, the relocation of the said High School to the above-mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred Junior College shall not be relaxed for any reason. All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The Junior College shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the Junior College.
