The Proposal:
The birth anniversary of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule, the first female teacher, is celebrated on January 3 in the state. The educational contribution made by Savitribai Phule, the emancipator of women, the values for women's emancipation practiced by her are very important for the future generation.
Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule's contribution to women's education, her work should reach all sections of the society and the educational values instilled by her should be transmitted to the next generation. Recognizing this need, the government was considering to celebrate the birth anniversary of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule as" Women Education Day."
Government's decision:
It has been decided to celebrate the birth anniversary of great social reformer and educationist Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule on 3 rd January as" Women Education Day" in all medium schools of all managements as well as in government, semi-government offices in the state. On this occasion, various activities and programs should be celebrated as follows: 1. The statue of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule should be worshipped in all government, semi-government offices and schools.
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule, every student and teacher should resolve to read a book about her work.
Lectures based on the works of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule should be organized.
4. Seminars on Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule should be held in groups of students.
Organize online webinars and seminars on the importance of women's education, the inspirational writings and thoughts of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule.
6. Recognizing the importance of women teachers, officials and social workers in women's education.
