Introduction: Saakshar Bharat Yojana was launched in the State in January, 2010.
As per 2001 census, 10 districts of the state where the female literacy rate is less than 50% have been included in the Saakshar Bharat Yojana, which was being implemented at four levels- state, district, taluka and gram panchayat. The scheme is being implemented by the central government. As per the order dated 07.03.2019, for the general component of Central share of Rs. 1243.80 lakh has been disbursed.
