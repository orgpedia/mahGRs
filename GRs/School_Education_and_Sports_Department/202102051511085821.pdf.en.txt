Preamble As per the decision of the Government under reference No. 1, the Department provides free education in Zilla Parishads, recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers schools. Defined Contribution Pension Scheme has been implemented for the teaching and non-teaching staff appointed on and after 1st November, 2005.
The Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS) has been subsumed in the National Pension Scheme (NPS) of the Centre as per the Government of India decision No. 2. The defined contribution pension scheme has been renamed as National Pension System (NPS). The reference No. The Department of Finance has prescribed the procedure required for implementation of Level-1 of the National Pension Scheme in respect of Government employees as per the Government Decision No. 3. As per the decision of the Department of Finance, vide Government Resolution No. 4. The modalities for implementation of National Pension Scheme (NPS) (Level 1) for teachers/ non-teaching staff who are members of the New Defined Contribution Pension Scheme have been laid down.
As per the agreement entered into by the State Government with the Central Record Keeping Agency (CRA), for the services provided by the agency under this scheme, the DTO will have to pay service charges to the Central Record Keeping Agency for the following:
PRAN opening at Rs. 30/ 2) Annual PRAN Maintenance Rs. 95/ 3) Transaction charges Rs. 3.75 The Government was considering to approve a new head of accounts to provide subsidy to all DTOs for payment of service charges as above.
