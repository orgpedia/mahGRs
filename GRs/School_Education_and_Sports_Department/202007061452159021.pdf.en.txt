Government Circular: The Government Advocate/ Assistant Public Prosecutor, after taking appropriate decision regarding the legal assistance to the Director of Education under the administrative control of the Commissioner (Education), if he has been made a party in a court case by name or by name and designation in respect of the official work performed by him in good faith while discharging his official duties.
The services of Special Counsel or Non-Panel Counsel in the approved list or Private Lawyers who are not empanelled by the Government but are willing to accept fees equivalent to the Lawyers fees payable to the Public Prosecutors.
The Commissioner (Education) was authorised on behalf of the Department to instruct them not to delay in appearing before the Court. However, this circular is being superseded as per the guidance of the Department of Law and Justice.
2. Department of Law and Justice dated 20.12.2011. As on 18.07.2014, a proposal for providing assistance of Public Prosecutor in cases where officers have been named as respondents should be submitted for approval of the Head of the Department.
