In view of the COVID-19 pandemic, instructions have been issued regarding the changes to be made in the current financial year 2021-22. Instructions have been issued by the General Administration Department to not make any transfers (all transfers and transfers due to some exceptional circumstances or special reasons) under the Transfers Act till June 30, 2021 under the Government decision dated May 10, 2021. Further, in accordance with the provisions of Rule 4 (4) II and Rule 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, a provision has been made for transfer in exceptional circumstances. Accordingly, on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board, with the approval of the competent authority and the corresponding senior authorities, the officers mentioned in the following table are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 in front of their names.
