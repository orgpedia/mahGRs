Introduction: Vasgade Education Prasarak Mandal, Vasgade runs Nutan Ganesh Vidyalaya, Old Budhwar Peth, Kolhapur.
The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation to Karveer, G. Kolhapur was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Since the above school has to be shifted to the building of the owner, M.P. Vasgade, Tt. Karveer, G. Relocation to Kolhapur is proposed. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
