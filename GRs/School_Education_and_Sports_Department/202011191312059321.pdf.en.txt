Government Decision: The above school has been shifted to a full-fledged building owned by the institution due to non-availability of space at the present location. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
(6) The institution shall take care that the school is not shifted without the approval of the government in future.
