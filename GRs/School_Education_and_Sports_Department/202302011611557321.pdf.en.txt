Preamble:- The Directorate of Education (Scheme) Office has been created by reorganizing the Directorate of Minority and Adult Education under the Government Decision dated 22.06.2022 of the Department of School Education and Sports. Similarly, the office of District Level Education Officer (Continuous) has been reorganized and the office of District Level Education Officer (Plan) has been created. In the absence of the office of the Education Officer (Continuous) in Washim, Hingoli, Gondia, Nandurbar and Palghar districts, a new office of the Education Officer (Plan) has been created in the said district. A proposal was under consideration to declare a" Head of Office" and a" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" to handle administrative and financial matters in the newly created office.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government Decision dated 24.06.2022 of the Department of School Education and Sports, the Heads of the Regional Level Offices under the Department of School Education and Sports have been declared as Divisional Heads, Regional Heads of Offices and Head of Offices in accordance with the amendments made in the Financial Powers Rules Manual 1978, Part-I, Sub Divisions I to V dated 17.04.2015. Now that the Directorate of Minority and Adult Education as well as the Office of the Education Officer (Continuing Education) GP have been reorganized under the Government Decision dated 22.06.2022,
