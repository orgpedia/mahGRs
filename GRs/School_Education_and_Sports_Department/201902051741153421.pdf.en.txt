The following orders shall apply:
(1) All the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 19th September, 2016 regarding the concerned school, weightage shall be required to be fulfilled.
(2) All the conditions of the Government Decision dated January 5, 2019 must be fulfilled and all the documents must have been examined in accordance with the terms and conditions.
(3) No grant shall be disbursed to a secondary school/ batch which is undergoing separate inspection/ proceedings in respect of complaints received regarding recognition/ transfer or transfer of secondary school/ batch.
(4) The teachers and non-teaching staff of the said secondary schools and batches should be provided subsidy at the rate of 20 percent from April, 2018. Grants in excess of 20% should not be sanctioned under any circumstances.
