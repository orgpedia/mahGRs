Introduction: Shri Bhairavnath Education Society, Pune Ambekar Primary School, 1359, Shukrawar Peth, Thorale Bajirao Road, Pune is a Marathi medium aided primary (class 1st to class 4th) school located at No. 1586, Property No. 1446, Padmavati Road, Alandi Devachi Gramin, Ch Holi Khurd, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation to Khed, G. Pune was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Since the aforesaid school has been informed by the previous institution about vacating the school site, A.No. 1586, Property No. 1446, Padmavati Road, Alandi Devachi Gramin, Ch-Holi Khurd, Ta. Relocation to Khed, Dist. Pune is proposed. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.

The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
