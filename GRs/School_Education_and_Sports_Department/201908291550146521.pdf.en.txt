Preamble: The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retd. The Government have decided to accept the recommendations of the said Committee with certain modifications. Accordingly, in respect of the revised pay structure applicable to the employees of Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai with effect from 01.01.2016, the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 vide Reference No. 3 has been announced. Also Reference No. As per Circular No. 4, a detailed procedure has been prescribed for determination of initial salary and payment of arrears. Based on this, the matter of implementing the revised pay structure for the employees of the establishment in the Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai Institute was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is passing an order that, in reference No. The revised Pay Structure and Fixation of Pay Rules under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 notified under Section 3 and Reference No. 4 In addition to the instructions issued regarding fixation of pay and payment of arrears, the Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai, has approved the implementation of the revised pay structure for the employees of the institution with appropriate modifications.
The option required for fixation of pay in the revised pay structure should be given within 2 months of the issue of this order. The option once given will be final.
(3) The additional expenditure arising out of the revised pay structure implemented under this Order shall be covered under the head of accounts under which expenditure is incurred for the payment of salaries to the employees of the establishment of Maharashtra State Bharat Scouts and Guides, Mumbai and shall be met from the following approved grants. The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune should consider this additional expenditure while preparing the revised estimates.
