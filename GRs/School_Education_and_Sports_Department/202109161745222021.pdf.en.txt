Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan with 60% funding by the Central Government and 40% by the State Governments. The salaries of the officers and employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state have not been paid since June 2021.
In view of the difficult situation of the COVID-19 pandemic, it is very important to pay the salaries of the working posts, considering this, the government was considering to disburse the allowable funds under the 1st Pay Target under the General Accounting Title 2202 I 612. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Out of the budget provision made under the target of 01, salary for the salary of regular teachers and other employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state, to meet the expenditure on salary of the employees whose salary was from the service system, Rs. 12,38,11, 800/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Thirty Eight Lakhs Eleven Thousand Eight Hundred only). These amounts are being disbursed out of the provisions made in the Budget under the head of accounts mentioned below.
