Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 26th September, 1995, 30 Sainik Schools/ NGOs have been permitted to open in the State with effect from 1996-97, one in each district. Under this scheme, private, competent and voluntary organizations will be engaged at the district level.
A policy decision has been taken at the government level to start a boys' military school and a girls' military school in every revenue division. These schools are purely residential schools and presently there are 39 aided and 3 unaided Sainik Schools functioning in the State.
On the lines of the Satara Sainik School, the private institutions in the state subsidized Sainik
A separate set of rules for schools will help in improving their standards and enable more students to join the National Defence Academy. For this purpose, the government was considering the formation of a committee to prepare separate rules for the aided military schools of private institutions in the state.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. A Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune to prepare separate rules for Sainik Schools aided by private institutions on the lines of Satara Sainik School as follows:

The mandate of the Committee shall be as follows:" To prepare and submit to the Government a comprehensive report in accordance with the preparation of separate rules for aided military schools of private institutions in the State. The committee will have two months" time to submit their report.
Two office bearers of Maharashtra State Sainik Schools Association should be invited to the meeting of the above committee and given an opportunity to present their views and submit a memorandum. The committee can make appropriate recommendations after studying the issues raised by them in the meeting and their statements. The Maharashtra State Sainik Schools Association should submit the names of the concerned 2 office bearers to the said committee.
