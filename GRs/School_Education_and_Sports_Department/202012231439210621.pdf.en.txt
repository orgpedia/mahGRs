Shishamma/ EBC/ 2018-19/10820/ Scholarship/ T.No. 224/6648, Dy. 4th October, 2019 Preamble: Considering the academic difficulties of the students studying in the border areas of Devchand College, Arjun Nagar, District Kolhapur, EBC concession is given to them as a special case from the year 1964-65. As per the earlier terms and conditions of the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 30th March, 1993 and Government Letter dated 5th March, 1965, the EBC concession was granted to the students of the said institution on the income of their parents in Maharashtra till the year 2018-19. Now, the proposal for administrative approval for grant of EBC concession for the next year 2019-20 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the Maharashtra-Karnataka border dispute is still not resolved, the EBC concession to the students of Devchand College run by Janata Shikshan Mandal, Arjun Nagar, District Kolhapur, must continue as a special matter. Therefore, the Government is permitting the continuation of EBC concession to the students of the said college for the year 2019-20. Further, for the year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 1,99,078/- (Rupees One Lakh Nine Thousand Eighty Eight only) subject to the terms and conditions as aforesaid.
