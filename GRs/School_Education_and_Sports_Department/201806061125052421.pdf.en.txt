Introduction: In the context of the above. As per the Government decision dated 25.10.2012, data entry operators working under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of School Nutrition Aahaar will be given Rs. 9600/- per month. The matter of increasing the remuneration given to these data entry operators was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Data Entry Operators working under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of School Nutrition Aahar will be paid a monthly honorarium of Rs. 10932/- is being approved. The hike will come into effect from the date of the announcement of the decision by the government.
The expenditure for this should be met from the grants sanctioned by the Central Government for the Management, Control and Evaluation (MME) component of the School Nutrition Scheme.
