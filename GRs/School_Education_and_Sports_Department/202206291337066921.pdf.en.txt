Introduction: Nari Pragati Sanstha, Shende Nagar, Nagpur runs Shanti Vidya Mandir Higher Primary School, Nagpur. 1st to 7th) on self-financing basis at Menons Trust, Plot No. The proposal received under the letter of reference for transfer of the institute to 84, Jai Durga Housing Society, Ujjwal Nagar, Everest Silver-2, Godhni Road, Nagpur was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: School, Plot No. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Jai Durga Housing Society, Ujjwal Nagar, Everest Silver-2, Godhni Road, Nagpur on self-financing basis on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school whose management has been changed shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) As the said school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
