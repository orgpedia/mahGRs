Satara Military School was established. Established on June 23, 1961, it is the first military school in the country. The main objective of this school is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children of the bottom rung of Maharashtra at all levels to become officers in the Indian Army through the National Defence Academy. A large number of students from this school have so far been admitted to the National Defence Academy.
