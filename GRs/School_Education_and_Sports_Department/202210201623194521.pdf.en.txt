Introduction: Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyaan (60:40) Under this scheme, the State share (General) for payment of salary to the working posts of officers and staff in the District Institutes of Education and Training in the State is Rs. The Finance Department has approved the release of Rs. 7,17,89, 160/- (Rupees Seven Crore Seventeen Lakh Ninety-Nine Thousand One Hundred Sixty only). As per the guidance given by the Department of Finance vide letter dated 6 th May 2022, the matter of issuance of Government Resolution as under was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan with 60% funding by the Central Government and 40% by the State Governments. The salary of the officers and employees of the District Institute of Education and Training in the state is paid from this.
The State share for payment of salary to the working posts for the said scheme under General Account Head 2202 I 612 is Rs. 7,17,89, 160/- (Rupees Seven Crores Seventeen Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand One Hundred Sixty only) is being approved for disbursement.
