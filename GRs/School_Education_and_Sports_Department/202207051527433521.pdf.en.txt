Reference: Letter dated 18th January, 2022 from the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati vide G.O.S.S./ M.S./ B/ 406/2022.
The proposal: self. Shankarrao Chapke Multipurpose Institute, Shrirampur, Dist. Gurukul Convent, Vadsad, Dist. Pusad, G. Yavatmal, English Medium Self-Financed Secondary School (Class 1st to 10th), Plot No. 50, Sriperumbudur. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Pusad, G. Yavatmal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the inadequate physical facilities in the above school, the school is located in Plot No. 50, Sriperumbudur. Relocation to Pusad, G. Yavatmal is proposed. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
