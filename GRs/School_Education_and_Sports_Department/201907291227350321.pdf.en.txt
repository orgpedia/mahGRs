Government Decision: It is proposed to shift the above High School from inadequate space to a self-contained building on rent. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated Higher Secondary School shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and recognition in the case of Higher Secondary School.
(4) The Higher Secondary School shall continue to function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, Department of School Education and Sports, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard shall be as many as the permissible posts and more teachers/
