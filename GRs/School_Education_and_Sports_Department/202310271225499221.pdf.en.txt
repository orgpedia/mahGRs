The Proposal:
Grants to Municipal Corporations and Municipalities for expenditure on primary education through School Education Department. By amending the Government Resolution dated 16th December, 1987 vide Reference No. (1) The decision of the Government, The revised order was issued on July 7, 2015. The reimbursement of expenditure on approved aspects of elementary education to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Municipal Councils/ Boards in the State is admissible by the State Government as per the subsidy formula prescribed under this Government decision.
The proposal submitted by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation has been examined by the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai and the permissible grant amount has been determined after the examination.
As per the recommendations of the Commission for expenditure on elementary education, the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has been given a total of Rs. 191,98,95, 370/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ninety One Crore Eighty Nine Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand Three Hundred and Seventy only) is admissible. Also, the total amount of reimbursement of the allowable salary subsidy of private primary schools for the previous period and the reimbursement of the outstanding subsidy with the difference in the amount actually paid.
