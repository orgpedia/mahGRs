Preamble: The proposal for disbursement of funds sanctioned under 34 scholarships/ scholarships for the financial year 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government. The government's decision is as follows:
Government Decision: To encourage meritorious students in the state, various schemes are being implemented to provide scholarships to the students in the schools of the state. The Government is approving to disburse a total amount of Rs. 3,28,75, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Twenty Eight Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand) for the purpose of 34 scholarships/ scholarships for the schemes mentioned in paragraph 2 from the approved budgetary allocation in the financial year 2021-22 and to make these funds available on the Budget Distribution System.
