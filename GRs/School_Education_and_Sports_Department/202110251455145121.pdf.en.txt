Government's decision
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has released the final results of the State Service Examination-2017 and State Service Examination-2018 for the 54 candidates who were selected on the basis of the final results of the State Service Examination-2017.
54 supernumerary posts have been created by the Government Decision of 2019.
However, the result of the examination of Deputy Training Officer, Group-B Trainee Officers in CPTP-5 and 6 for Session 3 and Session 4 has not been declared yet.
