Maharashtra: Students from Maharashtra's Rashtriya Chhatra Sena take part in the Republic Day parade in New Delhi. A variety of campsites to choose from
The event will be held in Delhi. Selected students from these camps participate in the Republic Day Parade at Rajpath, New Delhi. The cost of the unit is Rs. 47,13,968/- (Rupees Forty Seven Lakhs Thirteen Thousand Nine Hundred Sixty Six only) and the same is hereby approved to be placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
