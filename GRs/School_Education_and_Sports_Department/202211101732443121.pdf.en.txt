2.This Agreement is subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2014 in the matter No. 4 (b) to take action for disbursement of grants to the concerned institutions as per the terms and conditions of sanction of grants for financial assistance for sports facilities.
(2) After the disbursement of the grant to the institution concerned, it shall be mandatory to submit the certificate of completion of the project along with the photographs of the project and the utilization certificate as soon as possible and within a maximum period of 1 year, otherwise the distributed grant will be recovered from the date of disbursement of the grant at the prevailing penal interest rate of the Government. Also, the institution concerned will be required to provide a bank guarantee of 10% of the total grant amount to be sanctioned. Accordingly, after depositing the bank guarantee and affidavit with the concerned District Sports Officer, the funds available for the disbursement of the sanctioned amount should be disbursed to the concerned institution through RTGS.
(3) It shall be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate by paying the government subsidy and self-contribution.
(4) The remaining amount of grant payable to the institution concerned shall be disbursed by the Regional Office/ Directorate subject to the financial provision available to the institution concerned only after submitting the cost statement of 25% of the amount received from the institution concerned and the grant disbursed under the previous Government decision, along with the photographs of the present status of the construction at the first cost.
