Introduction: On the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Independence, Ma. The Minister of Finance, in the 2022-23 budget speech, had announced the development of schools in 10 historic villages associated with great men. Also, three historical villages were included in it as per the demand of the legislator. Under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" programme, the central government has sanctioned funds to the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, which will provide funds for the development of schools in historical villages. to build historic schools. The Gabha Committee chaired by the Minister of Cultural Affairs has approved the reference No. The Department of Cultural Affairs has informed to submit a proposal for seeking funds with administrative approval under Section 1. Accordingly, under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, Rs. The Chief Engineer (Project) Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Mumbai has given technical approval to the proposal at a cost of Rs. 1430.20 lakh. A proposal for administrative approval was submitted to the government under Section 2. A total of Rs. 13 crore has been earmarked for this. The matter of providing administrative and financial approval to the fund amounting to Rs. 1430.20 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
1) Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1430.20 lakh (Fourteen Crore Thirty Lakh Twenty Thousand). The details are attached with the government's decision.
(2) The expenditure required in this regard shall be met from the funds provided by the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs.
(3) The said Government have made provisions in the Foreign Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division V, para. 134 is being issued as per the powers vested in the department.
