Introduction: A total of six States (Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Kerala) have been selected for the project Strengthening Teaching Learning and Results for States funded by the World Bank on the basis of their performance in the Performance Grading Index. The total cost of the project is Rs. 976.39 crore has been sanctioned and the Central and State share is 60:40. Out of this, the central share of Rs. 585.83 crore. 390.56 crore. The central government. Order dated 13.11.2020 approves the year-wise funds to be released by the Central Government to the State of Maharashtra for the period 2020-21 to 2024-25. The programme is scheduled to begin in early 2021.
2. the central government. The Central share of funds has been made available as second tranche for the year 2023-24 under the Fund Distribution Order dated 19.09.2023. Accordingly, the matter of distribution of the central share of funds and the corresponding state government funds was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: To implement the World Bank funded project Strengthening Teaching Learning and Results for States (STARS), the Central Government has allocated Rs. 4350.00 lakhs and corresponding State share of Rs. 2900.00 Lakhs out of which a total amount of Rs. 7250.00 lakhs to the State Project Director, Maharashtra Primary Education Council, Charni Road, Mumbai.
