Preamble: The proposal received under the letters of reference to approve the shifting of classes (11th and 12th Science and Combined Arts, Commerce stream) of Marathi medium aided junior college Milind Junior College, Shahu Chowk, Latur, run by Samata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Latur, to Ausa Road Latur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to lack of physical facilities in the building of the above school-junior college, the institute has shifted the classes of the junior college to Ausa Road, Latur District. Therefore, the relocation of the junior college classes of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) Even after the transfer of the above classes, the administrative work will continue from the parent school and the institution will not be able to demand an independent principal.
(6) The institution shall take care that the school is not shifted without the approval of the government in future.
(7) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
