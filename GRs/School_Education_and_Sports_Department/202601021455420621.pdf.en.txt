Gaddekar Enterprises, Bhandup (p. ), Mumbai has been unanimously selected to tender and enter into service with them subject to the following terms and conditions. The terms and conditions of the agreement are as follows:
2.1 The supply of material b. stationery of the company or better mentioned in the tender shall be made immediately on the demand recorded by the Department from time to time at the rate specified in the contract.
When the Department orders stationery items as required by it, the supplier shall be obliged to supply the requested stationery items as per the prescribed rates and standards. Any change in the quality or price of the stationery supplied will be subject to the Plaster's decision to refuse the item and terminate the contract if compliance with the contract is not satisfactory.
2.3 If the Department is in urgent need of any ARAMA NT other item mentioned in the list, it will be immediately obligated upon placing an order for such item and the supplier will be obliged to supply such item at 5% below the market price (MRP).
