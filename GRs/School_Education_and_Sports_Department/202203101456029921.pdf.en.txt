Introduction: The salary of teachers and non-teaching staff in private partially and fully aided schools in the state is paid through the Shalarth system. Shalarth system has been developed by MahaIT and regular salaries of teaching and non-teaching staff are paid online through this system. However, the facility of online payment of arrears of these employees, medical payments (advance and reimbursement), dues of Lokayukta and court cases and dues to be paid in 5 installments after the implementation of the revised pay structure as per the 7th Pay Commission has not been provided by MahaIT so far. MahaIT has been informed from time to time about providing such facilities. However, as such a facility is not yet available in the Shalarth system, the matter of allowing offline payment of these payments was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Due payments of working and retired teachers/ non-teaching staff in private partially and fully aided schools and teachers' schools in the state, medical payments, implementation of the revised pay structure in the Seventh Pay Commission. 01.01.2016 to D. It will include the following types of payments a) To the teachers and non-teaching staff of the state as per the Government Decision dated 10.01.2020. 1.1.2016.
