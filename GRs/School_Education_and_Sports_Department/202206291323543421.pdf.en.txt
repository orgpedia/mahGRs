Introduction: Att Deep Bhava Educational Board, Kamthi, Nagpur is running Nalanda Primary School, Gawalipura, Ramnagar, Kamthi, G.Nagpur, Marathi medium aided primary school (1st to 4th class), Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to 63, Shriram Suman Nagar, Kalamna, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The building of the above school is dilapidated and the owner has asked to vacate the rented building of the school. It is proposed to be shifted to 63, Shriram Suman Nagar, Kalamna, Nagpur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3322/ PR No. 440/ SM-1 (5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaints or objections are received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
