The proposal to transfer the institution to Kandhar, G. Nanded, received through a letter under reference, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions:
(1) It shall be mandatory for the institution to activate the U-DIAS number of the school and fill in the details of the school on the U-DIAS and Saral online system.
(2) As the said school is a self-financing English medium primary school, the entire financial liability of the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the school shall be on the institution.
(3) All the conditions on which the said school has been recognized on a self-financing basis shall continue to apply to the institution and the school even after the transfer of the school.
(4) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(5) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(6) The eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their service conditions shall not be changed. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(7) Care should be taken that in the case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(10) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. On fulfilment of clauses (5) and (6), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur shall take necessary further action regarding the change of management of the school after examining all the aspects and including other necessary conditions and inform the Government about the action taken.
