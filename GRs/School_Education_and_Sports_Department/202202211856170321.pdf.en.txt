The Central Government's Fund Demand No. N-2, 2202- General Education, (01), Primary Education, (789), Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, (01) Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, (01) (03) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (Central share 60%) Programme Title 2202I 746 Below Rs. 50.10 lakh and State share (01) (04) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (State share 40%) Programme Account Head 2202I 755 below Rs. 33.40 lakh should be spent in the financial year 2021-2022 from the provision made in the budget.
The instructions given in the Government Circulars dated 6.4.2021, 24.6.2021, 14.10.2021 and 28.01.2022 of the Finance Department should be strictly followed. The funds are being disbursed as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by this circular and as approved by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide Government Resolution dated 18.02.2022.
The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said funds. Also, the funds should be used for the purpose for which they were allocated. The details of the beneficiaries of this scheme should be updated on MPSIMS.
