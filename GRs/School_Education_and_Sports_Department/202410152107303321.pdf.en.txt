The government's decision:
The management of the junior college was transferred to the Kurne Education Society, Latur.
The Government has decided to grant approval on the following terms and conditions: -
(1) The condition of the minimum number of students of a minor college whose management has changed for any reason.
It will not be relaxed.
(2) Any permission / approval of the Government in case of a minor college where the management has changed.
Care should be taken to ensure that the terms and conditions do not change.
(3) From time to time, in respect of schools, teachers / non-teaching staff, salary / non-salary grants, students, etc. by the Government.
The new body will be bound to comply with the prescribed Acts, Rules, Orders, etc.
(4) These Management Changes, Government Decisions, Department of School Education and Sports, No: Mashah 2011 / (210/11)
MASHI-3 shall come into force in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed on 17th February, 2012.
(5) Fresh complaints to the Government or to the field officers of the Education Department regarding this change of management.
