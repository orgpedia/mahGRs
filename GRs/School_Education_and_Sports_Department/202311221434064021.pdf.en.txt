Government circular:
The Additional Provisional Seniority List for the post of Deputy Director of Education in Group-A (Administration Branch) Cadre of Maharashtra Education Service from 01.01.2022 to 31.12.2022 i.e. 01.01.2023 has been published by the Government on 05.06.2023. Instructions were given to submit objections, if any, on the seniority list through the Commissioner (Education) within the stipulated time.
2. The said seniority has been created vide General Administration Department Notification No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII dated 21.06.2021.
3. Ma. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015 dated 04.08.2017, has set aside the Government decision dated 25.05.2004 and declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. To challenge the decision. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. The seniority is being issued subject to the final decision in the Special Leave Petition. Accordingly, the seniority list is being published subject to the condition that if there is a change in the selection list after 2004, the same will be changed accordingly.
