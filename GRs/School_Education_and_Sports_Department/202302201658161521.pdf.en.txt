Reference: 1) Letter dated 30.06.2022 and 20.12.2022 of the Director of Education, Secondary and Higher Secondary, Maharashtra State, Pune 2) Department of Finance No. Circular dated 08.02.2023
Preamble: In order to encourage meritorious students of the State to pursue education, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds sanctioned for the purpose of 34 Scholarships/ Vidyavadhan in the financial year 2022-23.
Government Decision: To encourage meritorious students in the state, various schemes are being implemented to provide scholarships to the students in the schools of the state. The Government is approving the release of total amount of Rs. 5,43,21, 200/- (Rupees Five Crore Thirty Three Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred only) for the schemes mentioned in paragraph 2 from the approved budgetary allocation in the financial year 2022-23 and make the same available on the budgetary distribution system.
