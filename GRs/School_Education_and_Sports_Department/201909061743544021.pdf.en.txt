Introduction: Goda Union Charitable Society, Nashik operates T.S. Dighole Secondary and Higher Secondary School, Naigaon, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Secondary and Higher Secondary School, Sinnar, G. Nashik (Class 8th to 12th) to Krantiveer Vasantrao Narayanrao Naik Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said School to Krantiveer Vasantrao Narayanrao Naik Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) The School shall continue to function on the same principle of grant-in-aid even after the transfer.
(2) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff.
(3) It shall be ensured that in case of a school where there is a change of management, there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
