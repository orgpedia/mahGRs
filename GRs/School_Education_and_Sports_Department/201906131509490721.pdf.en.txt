Introduction: Dombivali Progressives, Thane run Greens English School, Dombivali (East), Krishna Nivas, in front of Dr. Oak Hospital, Chiplunkar Path, Ramnagar, Ta. Kalyan, G. Thane, English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary, Upper Primary and Secondary School, Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval to the relocation of Dombivali (East), G. Thane was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school is dangerous, the Dombivali Municipal Corporation has issued a notice to vacate the building and due to the safety of the students, the school has been shifted to the building constructed on the land leased by the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. The relocation of the school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The institution shall charge the prescribed fee for the relocation.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure that all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff are accommodated in the migrant place and their service conditions are not changed.
(3) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(5) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(6) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
