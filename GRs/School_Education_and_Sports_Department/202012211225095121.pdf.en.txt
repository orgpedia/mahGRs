As per the Government decision in the Preamble Reference No. 1, a grant of 4% of the previous year's salary expenditure based on the 4th Pay Commission allowable salary is sanctioned to the schools of the local bodies to meet their marginal expenditure. Also Reference No. Under Section 2, the permissible expenditure out of the simple grant amount has been revised.
However, many Zila Parishads are not disbursing the amount as the Government is not in the process of collecting the amount from them.
However, due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the state was put under a lockdown. At present, the process of unlocking is underway and detailed instructions have been issued through the government circular dated June 15, 2020, regarding the phased reopening of schools in the state. Accordingly, the schools need to be provided with funds for taking necessary actions such as preparation before starting the school, cleaning of school and toilets, vigilance after starting the school, planning to avoid crowding of students, etc.
Deputy Chief Minister (Finance and Planning) As per the directions given in the meeting with the Minister (School Education), considering the situation of COVID-19 outbreak, it has been agreed as a special case to disburse 100% funds for the year 2020-21 under the head of Accounts 22020173 for simple grants to prepare for the opening of schools.
Accordingly, the government was considering the issue of disbursement of grants for Zilla Parishad schools.
