Introduction: Swodharak Vidyarthi Sanstha, Dhule runs Maasheb NayanKuvarji Rawal Secondary School, Dhawde Ta. Shindkheda is a Marathi medium partially aided secondary school (classes 5 to 10). The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation to Shindkheda, District Dhule was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has been shifted from a building with inadequate physical facilities to a building with full facilities. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
