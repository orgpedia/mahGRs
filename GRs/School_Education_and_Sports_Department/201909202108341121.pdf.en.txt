Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of Shahjahan Urdu Primary School, Ekta Nagar, Jatwada Road, G. Aurangabad, running by Saif Multipurpose Society, Aurangabad, to Paradise Social Society, Beed, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Paradise Social Society, Beed on the following terms and conditions:- (1) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Beed shall ensure that the financial position of the school is sound on the basis of the written consent of all the teachers of the school and the bank balance/ deposit of Paradise Social Society, Beed.
(2) The Paradise Social Society, Beed shall be required to submit a notarized affidavit that no financial transaction has taken place in the transfer of the said school.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible in accordance with the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools and not to employ more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff.
Further, it shall be mandatory to take action as per the policy of the Government to fill up the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.

The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(8) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(9) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, shall examine all the matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
