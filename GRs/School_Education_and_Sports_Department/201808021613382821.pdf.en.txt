Read:- 1) School Education and Sports Department Government Decision No. Ukyo 2012/ Q. No.65/ Kriuse-3D. on the 12th November 2013.
Preamble: Under the State Youth Policy 2012, the Government has decided to confer the Youth Awards at the State and District level every year to honour the social welfare work done by the youth of the State/ District and to encourage them to do youth development work. Ukyo 2012/ Q. No.65/ Kriuse-3D. It was approved on November 12, 2013. As the criteria for selection of Yuva Puraskar are not clear, the distribution of the award for the year 2016-17 has been put on hold and the matter of setting up a committee to decide the governance of Yuva Puraskar on the lines of Shivchhatrapati Puraskar and on the basis of Youth Social Reputation Index was under consideration.
Government Decision: The following Committee is being constituted to formulate the revised criteria for selection of the Youth Award.
