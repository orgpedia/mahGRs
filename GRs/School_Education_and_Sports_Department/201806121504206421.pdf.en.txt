English medium permanent unaided primary and upper primary schools in Belapur, Navi Mumbai, G.Thane, Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for the relocation to 3, Sector-8, Kharghar, G. Raigad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the relocation of the school from the building with inadequate physical facilities to the new self-sufficient building leased from CIDCO on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
2) All pre-migration students have been accommodated in the nearest school/ place of migration and there is no complaint/ objection in this regard. Also, the education officer, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, Education Department, Navi Mumbai, G. Thane will have to ensure that the building at the relocated place is safe for the students.
3) The migration should be agreed by all the teachers/ non-teaching staff, and all the qualified teachers and non-teaching staff have been accommodated in the migrant place and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and orders issued from time to time by the Government, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
