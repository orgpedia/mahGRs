The proposal-
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to provide financial assistance to the registered organizations/ associations working at various levels for the development of sports and games in the State of Maharashtra for organizing official State/ National/ International competitions from the funds of the State Sports Council. The government is working according to the decision of 1.
The 63rd Senior Group Gadi and Mitti State Championship Wrestling Tournament and Maharashtra Kesari Title Fight 2019-20, organized by the Maharashtra State Wrestling Council from December 2 to January 7, 2020, were under the consideration of the Government for providing funds.
Government decision: 63rd Senior Group Gaddi and Mathi State Level Championship and Maharashtra Kesari Title Fight 2019-20 organized by Maharashtra State Wrestling Council at Shri Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mhalunge Balewadi, Pune from 2nd to 7th January, 2020. Administrative sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 42.18 lakhs (Rupees Forty Two Lakh Eighteen Thousand only). It is hereby approved to place the said amount under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services. The payment of Rs. 42.18 lakh (Rupees Forty Two Lakh Eighteen Thousand only) should be drawn in the name of Maharashtra State Wrestling Association. This amount has to be paid.
