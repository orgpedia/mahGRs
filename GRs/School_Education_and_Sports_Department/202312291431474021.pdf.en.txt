As per Section 12 (1) (c) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, at least 25% of the total number of seats in the respective classes at the entry level in private unaided schools shall be reserved for children belonging to disadvantaged and weaker sections. Further, under Section 12 (2) of the said Act, provision has been made for reimbursement of educational fee of the student admitted under RTE 25% by the State Government.
Accordingly, Joint Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune has requested to provide funds in the aforesaid Reference No. 2 letter to the government. For the academic year 2023-2024, Rs. A sum of Rs. 200.00 crore has been allocated for this. The government has taken a decision. 20th July, 2023 amounting to Rs. 40.00 crore and Govt.
12th October, 2023 amounting to Rs. 36.75 crore. 76.75 crore has been disbursed. Now, in accordance with the approval given by the Finance Department for reimbursement of educational fee of the student who has been admitted to RTE 25%, the amount is Rs. 62.00 crore is under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In accordance with the provisions made under Section 12 (1) (c) and Section 12 (2) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, RTE has decided to provide Rs. 62.00 crore (Rupees Sixty Two Crores only) to Director of Education (Primary), State of Maharashtra, Pune.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune shall disburse the funds in proportion to the reasonable demand made by the districts. The funds allocated will be used only for the year 2022-23.
