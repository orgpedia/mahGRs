The Proposal:
The Department of School Education and Sports has developed a comprehensive policy and vision document till the year 2025, according to which this project under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (Science City) will be set up in Pimpri-Chinchwad. This was decided at the Cabinet meeting.
To inculcate scientific temper among students, a 7.5-acre site (30,000 sq.m.) is available for setting up a Science Centre in Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, District Pune. Of these, one Science Centre has already been set up by the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation on 1.75-acre (5,1,79 sq. The remaining 5.75 acres (24,821 sq.m.) is available for the construction of Science City. In the next five years, with the consent of the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation Rs. The government was considering the formation of a committee for the construction of a world-class" Bharat Ratna Rajiv Gandhi Science City" worth Rs 191 crore.
The government has approved the formation of a committee to take necessary decisions immediately for the construction of' Bharat Ratna Rajiv Gandhi Science City' in Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.
