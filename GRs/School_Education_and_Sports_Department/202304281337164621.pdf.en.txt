'Let's Change' film to be allowed to be screened in state schools
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Decision No.: Parichay-2023/ PR No.18/ Training
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date of release: April 28, 2023
Read:- 1) Government Resolution No: PARIK-2023/ PR No.18/ Training dated 11th April, 2023 2) Government Sanction Letter No: PARIK-2023/ PR No. 18/ Training dated April 19, 2023
Government's decision:
Shri Samarth Films, Navi Mumbai Marathi film" Let's Change" has been allowed to be screened in schools across the state for two years, 2023-24 and 2024-25, subject to conditions as per the Government decision dated 11th April, 2023. The government has taken the decision.
The Government Corrigendum dated 19th April, 2023 issued in this regard is hereby cancelled.
