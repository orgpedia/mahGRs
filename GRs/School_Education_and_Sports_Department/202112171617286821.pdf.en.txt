Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation of Shams Urdu Primary School, Khana, Gandhibagh, an Urdu medium aided primary (Class 1st to 4th) school run by Women's Education Society, Ganjakhet, Nagpur, to Vanjara Layout, Near Yellow River, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school has become dilapidated, the Municipal Corporation has shifted it to Vanjara Layout, near the Yellow River, Nagpur due to the notice for repair. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
