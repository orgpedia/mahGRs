Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary School, Tulsi Mahila Mandal, Beed, Kids Care School, Jaisinghpura, Aurangabad to Shri Samarth Multi-Purpose Institute, Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Shri Samarth Multipurpose Institute, Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) Since the school is permanently unaided in English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall remain with the institution permanently.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Order, School Education and Sports Department, No: MASHAH 2011/ (210/11) MASHI-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said transfer or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the service conditions of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, should examine the incidental matters and take further action regarding the transfer of the school within 4 weeks and inform the government about the action taken.
