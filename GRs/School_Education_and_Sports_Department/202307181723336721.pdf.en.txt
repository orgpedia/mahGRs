The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. The officer who is competent to be transferred in accordance with the provisions of section 4 (4) (2) and section 4 (5) of the said Act has been transferred and posted on administrative grounds with the prior approval of the senior officer next in line. Accordingly, with the approval of the competent authority, the officers mentioned in the table below are being posted in the place of posting indicated in column No. 3 against their names in the transfer posting.

The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. Also, the concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted to cancel the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
The Commissioner (Education), Pune shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Authorities about the immediate release of the said officers from their present posts as per this order and ensure that the transferred officers join the given place of posting within the prescribed period and submit a report to the Government.
