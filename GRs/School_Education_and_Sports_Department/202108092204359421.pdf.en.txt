Government order:
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. As per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the competent authority for transfer has been declared.
Provided further that the competent authority transferring in accordance with proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the powers of various cadre officers in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the proposal for transfer of transferable officers who have completed three years in the post of posting as on 31.05.2021 has been approved by the Civil Services Board No. 1 was presented.
