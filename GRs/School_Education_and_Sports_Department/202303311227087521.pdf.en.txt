Preamble: The Government has decided to encourage the schools/ senior colleges affiliated to the School Games Federation of India to participate in the School Games organized by the State Government at the district level in the age group of 14, 17 and 19 years. The grant is given as on 26.02.2014. The Government of India has decided to postpone the School Games to be organised by the School Games Federation of India. As per letter dated 16.09.2022, approval has been accorded for conducting school sports competitions up to State level.
