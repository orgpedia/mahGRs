A complaint regarding migration to Shindkheda, District Dhule was received at the field level. In accordance with the notice issued to the school in this regard, the school had requested for fresh approval for the relocation. In the meeting held on 7/08/2018 regarding the relocation order of the said school, the Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Dhule was directed by the Minister for School Education to submit a fact-finding report. The report has been submitted to the government and a decision on it is awaited.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Keeping in view the educational interest of the students of the above-mentioned schools, the Government has decided to grant ex-post facto approval to the relocation of the said Secondary School to the above-mentioned places on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Dhule shall ensure that the building at the place of relocation is safe for the students and necessary physical facilities are available there.
(2) All qualified teaching and non-teaching staff have been accommodated at the place of migration and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(3) As per the orders issued by the Government from time to time, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided for the school. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission and recognition of the Government in respect of the migrated school.
