The government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Wrestling and Greco-Roman Championship 2022-23, to be held at Mani Marja Sports Complex, Chandigarh from 12th to 15th January, 2023, will be held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai.
The selection test was conducted on 10th December, 2022. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" and the team of the state selected through the selection test is as per the prospectus" A." Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Rs. 1,35,800/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Five Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Distribution Officer.
The athletes who have received kit bags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kit bags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years, this rule will be strictly followed by the Secretariat Gymkhana.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office shall relieve the player coaches/ managers for the training camp from 7th to 9th January, 2023 and for the tournament from 12th to 15th January, 2023 with effect from 06th January, 2023 (M.No.) and the services of these players shall be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they will be working.
