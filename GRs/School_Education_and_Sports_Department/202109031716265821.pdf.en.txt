Introduction: Mahatma Phule Samata Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur runs Arunoday Primary School, Kuntlapur Vasahat, Katol, G.Nagpur. The proposal received under reference letters to shift 767 IUDP to Sharda Nagar, Nagpur Road, Katol was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is functioning at Kuntlapur Industrial Co-operative Colony, Katol and the institution has proposed to shift the school after giving a notice to shift the school. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The two schools will be merged with the middle school of Keshavrao Pawar Vidyalaya, Katol, Nagpur, run by Mahatma Phule Samata Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur.

The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
