A total of 142 special educators appointed under the schemes of" Disabled Inclusive Scheme (Secondary Level)" and" Disabled Integrated Education Scheme (Primary)" under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan" have been paid their outstanding salaries. The High Court, Bombay, Nagpur Bench, in Writ Petition No.
3696/2018 and other petitions, the following judgment has been passed under Reference-1:..."
(i) That the Respondent State of Maharashtra will forthwith pay to each of the Petitioners Rs. 75,000/- ( rupees seventy- five thousand) per month as an ad hoc payment subject to further orders, adjustment etc, commencing from 1 April 2022 on every 15th day of the month till further orders. The amount shall be credited directly in the accounts of the Petitioners."
2. m. In order to implement the above decision of the High Court, a proposal to provide necessary funds for payment of arrears of salary for the two months of April and May to 142 special teachers from the State share of Samagra Shiksha Scheme under the head of Accounts (2202I 612) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) was submitted for the approval of the Planning Department and Finance Department. As per the approval of the Planning Department and Finance Department, Rs. 2,13,00, 000/- (Rs. The government had earmarked Rs two crore for the purpose. The government's decision is as follows:

A total of 142 special teachers appointed under the" Disabled Inclusive Education Scheme (Secondary Level)" and" Disabled Integrated Education Scheme (Primary)" have been given a total of Rs. 2,13,00, 000/- (Rs. Two lakh (Rs. 75,000/- per teacher per month).
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune shall deposit the amount of outstanding salary in the account of the concerned 142 special educators. Make sure that the amount of salary is credited to the account of the teacher concerned. The list of 142 Special Educators is furnished in Annexure-A. If a teacher's name is found twice in this list, it should be ensured that the salary will be paid once in such a case. Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune will be held responsible if it is found that excess salary has been paid.
(3) If the salaries of the special educators mentioned in Annexure A have been paid earlier in connection with a court case, the amount shall be adjusted from the amount admissible after taking into account the amount mentioned in paragraph 1 of the said Government Order.
4) If the funds remain after paying the salary as per the above, the report should be submitted to the government. Prior approval of the government should be obtained before utilising the funds.
The expenditure should be met from the provision available in the financial year 2022-23 under the head" Demand No. E-2, 2202, General Education, 01 Elementary Education, 106-Teachers and other expenses, (01) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, (01) (02) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (General) (State share 40%), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried), (2202 I 612)." For this, the Director of Education (Primary) is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
All the conditions mentioned in the Finance Department, Government Circular dated 4.4.2022 are being fulfilled.
In this case, the Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune should take immediate action and submit a compliance report to the government.
8. Informal Reference No. 186/1475, dated 12.5.2022 and the Finance Department vide Informal Reference No. 526/ Expenditure-5, dated 18.5.2022 is being issued as agreed.
