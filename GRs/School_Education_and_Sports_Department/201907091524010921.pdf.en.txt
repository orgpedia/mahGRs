Government Decision: Online applications are invited for State/ Savitribai Phule Adarsh Teacher Award for the year 2018-2019. After receiving the online proposal, as per the letter dated July 3, 2019 of the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune, the government is approving the appointment of the following District Selection Committee to send category-wise recommendations from the district level to the State Selection Committee.

2. While recommending for the State/ Savitribai Phule Teacher Award, Dy. The provisions of the Government Circular dated 21st July 2016 should be strictly followed. The Selection Committee shall take care to maintain necessary confidentiality with regard to the recommendations. In this regard, the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary) Maharashtra State, Pune should receive the recommendations from the District Selection Committee within the prescribed time limit and submit them to the State Selection Committee.
