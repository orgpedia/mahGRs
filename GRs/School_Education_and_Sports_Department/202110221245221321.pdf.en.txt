Introduction: Nari Pragati Sanstha, Kamthi Road, Nagpur operates Shanti Vidyamandir Higher Primary School, Shende Nagar, Kamthi Road, Nagpur on a self-financed basis of English Medium Upper Primary (Class 1st to 7th) School, Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter for relocation to 84, Jaydurga Housing Society, Ujjawal Nagar, near Everest Silver 2 Govind Lawn, Godhni Road, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has proposed to relocate as the building owner has asked to vacate the rented building. The above migration of the said school is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) Since the shifting of the English medium school is being done on a self-financing basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school will be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PRASHTHA 3220/ PR No. 447/ SM-1 (5) The school will continue to function at the shifted place on the same basis as it was functioning at the original place on grant-in-aid basis.
(6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
