The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation of English Medium Self-Financed Primary School (Class 1st to Class 7th), Plot No.6, Bhausaheb Surve Nagar, Nagpur, run by Savji Education Society, Ramdaspeth, Nagpur, Speak and Span Convent, Sawalambi Nagar, G.
Government Decision: As the landlord asked to vacate the rented building of the above school, the institution has shifted to Plot No. 6, Bhausaheb Surve Nagar, Nagpur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
(6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
