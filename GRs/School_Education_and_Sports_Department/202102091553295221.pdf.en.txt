Government's decision:
The School Education and Sports Department (Khudia) has sanctioned 22 posts of constables in the direct service quota and 27 posts of 1 constable and 4 naiks in the promotion quota in the Group-D cadre. Reference No. 1 of the Finance Department. As per the Government decision dated 14th January, 2016, 25% of the sanctioned posts i.e. 7 posts are required to be abolished. According to reference no. Under Section 2, out of 27 sanctioned posts of Group-D cadre, 3 posts out of 7 posts to be abolished have been abolished. The remaining four positions are now being eliminated.
