The government has decided that the final selection of education servants from among the highest scoring candidates on the basis of marks in aptitude and intelligence tests in private management schools in the state will be done by the respective institutions through interview. In order to bring more transparency in the selection process of teaching staff in the schools of private managements who have opted for recruitment to the post of teachers on the basis of Teacher Eligibility and Aptitude Test, 2022 and onwards, including interview, the following procedures are being revised:
(a) The list of candidates who have secured the highest marks in the order of preference, order of merit, medium of posting, category, subject and point name of the candidates in the ratio of 1:3 instead of 1:10 as prescribed under the Government Decision, dated 7.2.2019, with parallel reservation, if available for each vacant teaching position for final selection through interview with the teaching staff of the selected private management schools.
