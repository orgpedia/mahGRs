Introduction: The Sports Federation of India organizes various sports competitions at school level from Taluka to National level every year. Maharashtra is represented by teams/ players in national level competitions in various sports. Competitions are a great way to develop a love of sports among school children and to develop sportsmanship among them, which in turn produces excellent sportspersons. Sports events are equally important to create opportunities for the youth, especially in rural areas, to participate in sports events. Players from various states will be selected for the national-level competition. Taking into consideration the recommendations of the Sports Policy 2012, Reference No. 2 the government's decision. The modalities for conducting the School Games have been decided as on 24.12.2014.
The School Games Federation of India (SSFI) has announced the participation of Maharashtra in the pending National Games for boys and girls under-19 to be held in Delhi, Bhopal and Gwalior (Madhya Pradesh) during the 2022-23 school year. The government has taken the decision. As per the provision dated 24.12.2014, the Government is considering to provide subsidy for the expenses incurred for selection process of State teams for National School Games being organized by School Games Federation of India, pre-National Competitions training camps, accommodation and food expenses, material expenses etc.
