Government's decision:
The registration branch of the department is functioning in the fourth floor of the Ministry of School Education and Sports extension building in hall number 421. Since the seating arrangement of the officers/ employees of the registration branch is not proper, there is a need to make new arrangements and furniture for their seating. Therefore, for the renovation of this place, for the construction works and furniture etc., the Executive Engineer, Department of City Department, Public Works Department, Mumbai, prepared Rs. 36,50,319/- (Rupees Thirty Six Lakh Fifty Thousand Three Hundred and Nineteen only) and Rs. 5,48,453/- (Rupees Five Lakh Forty Eight Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Three only) has been submitted by the Executive Engineer (Electrical), South Mumbai Electricity Department, Public Works Department, Mumbai.
2. Under this Government decision, the expenditure for the renovation of Building No. 421 (Extension Building) allotted for the officers and staff of the Registration Branch in Mantralaya Extension Building will be Rs. 36,50,319/- (Rupees Thirty Six Lakhs Fifty Thousand Three Hundred and Nineteen only) and the cost of electrification is Rs. 5,48,453/- (Rupees Five Lakh Forty Eight Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Three only)
