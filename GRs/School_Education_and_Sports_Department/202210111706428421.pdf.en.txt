The proposal-
In order to implement the Teacher Education Scheme under the Centrally Sponsored Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment has been requested to disburse funds to the tune of Rs.84 lakh for the salary of officers and staff of District Institutes of Education and Training in the State for the financial year 2022-23 under the target of 01 salary under the head 2202I 755. Accordingly, the Department of Social Justice has disbursed a provision of Rs.84 lakh to the Department of School Education as per the Government decision dated 3 rd October, 2022. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Teacher Education, the budgetary allocation for the financial year 2022-23 under the head SC Sub-Plan Account is Rs. 84 lakh (Rs. Eighty four lakh only) is being approved by the government to be disbursed on the budgetary fund distribution system.
2) State share of Rs. 84 lakh. N-2, 2202- General Education, 01, Elementary Education, 789, Special Component Plan for Scheduled Castes, (01) Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, (01) (03) Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (State Share 40%) (Programme) (2202/755).
Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said funds. Also, the funds should be used for the purpose for which they were allocated. The details of the beneficiaries of this scheme should be updated on MPSIMS.
