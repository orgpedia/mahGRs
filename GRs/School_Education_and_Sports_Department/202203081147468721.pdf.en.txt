Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for shifting of English Medium Self-financed Higher Secondary School (Classes 11 to 12- Arts, Science and Commerce stream), Kirakatwadi, Pune to Pritam Prakash School and Junior College, Hadapsar, Pune, run by Dhanvantari Education and Social Foundation, Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Institute has shifted the said School to Kirkatwadi, Pune in place of the Institute Secretary due to its location in the commercial complex of Mega Centre, Hadapsar and inadequate physical facilities. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The institution shall ensure that the school is not shifted without the approval of the Government in future.
(6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
