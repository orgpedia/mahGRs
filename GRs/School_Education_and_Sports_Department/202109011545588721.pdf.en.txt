Proposal- Reference No. As per the decision of the Government, a total of 5279 temporary posts in the zonal offices under the School Education Department were extended from March 1, 2021 to August 31, 2021. As the said period has expired, the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune Office vide Reference No. As proposed under Section 3 of the Finance Department Reference No. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Section 2, the Government was considering the extension of tenure of a total of 5279 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune and all the field offices under him.
The Government has decided to fill up 5279 temporary posts in the office of the Government Resolution Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and its subordinate field offices. As of September 1, 2021. The government has decided to extend the deadline till February 28, 2022. The establishment-wise details in this regard are given in Form A and Form B.
Commissioner (Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune and all the offices under him and all the offices of the Deputy Director of Divisional Education should take care to attach the office-wise and post-wise statements of the posts in his office while submitting the pay scales of the temporary posts.
(3) The expenditure on these temporary posts should be under the same head of account as was being incurred earlier and the same should be met from the sanctioned provision.
