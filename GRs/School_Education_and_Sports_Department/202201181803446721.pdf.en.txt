Preamble: The Government was contemplating to disburse funds for the felicitation ceremony to boost the morale and enthusiasm of the students of Rashtriya Chhatra Sena from Maharashtra who will be participating in the march past at Rajpath, New Delhi on Republic Day, 26th January, 2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To boost the morale and enthusiasm of the students of National Cadet Corps from Maharashtra participating in the march past at Rajpath, New Delhi on Republic Day, 26th January, 2022, a sum of Rs. The Finance Department has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 26.00 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Six Lakh only). Accordingly, this Government decision is being approved to disburse this fund and place it under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
The expenditure shall be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No.E-3, 2224-Sports and Youth Services-102, Youth Welfare Programme for Students (01) (01) Rashtriya Chhatra Sena Yojana (2204-0016)-21 Supplies and Supplies. All funds must be used for the purpose for which they were allocated.
The felicitation should be held subject to the guidelines, orders, etc. issued by the Central Government as well as the State Government in the circumstances arising out of COVID-19. If the number of students participating in it decreases, the amount of funds should be spent accordingly.
