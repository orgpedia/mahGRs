Introduction: Seva Das Maharaj Education Society, Kasarkhed, Ta. Barshitakali, G. Akola operated Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Secondary and Higher Secondary School, Jodga (Tathod), Tt. Paramhans Gangadhar Maharaj Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Karanja, G. Vashim, Aided Secondary and Unaided Higher Secondary School, Jodga, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of the institution to Karanja, G. Washim was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Paramhans Gangadhar Maharaj Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Jodga, Ta. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to G.Vashim, Karanja on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in the case of change of management of the school.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If any fresh complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management.

The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution prior to the change of management have been breached by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati, shall examine all the matters and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
