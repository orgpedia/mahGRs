Introduction: There is no plan to modernize the old talmis/ akhadas that have kept the tradition of wrestling in the state in modern times. Therefore, in order to provide financial assistance to such sports institutions for providing modern facilities to keep alive the traditional sports like wrestling, a scheme for providing financial assistance to such institutions by promoting the development of Taleem Wrestling Centre has been notified vide Reference No. 1 As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2014, the scheme for development of Talim Wrestling Centre has been approved.
2. Proposal for sanction of grants to various institutions for development of Talim Wrestling Centre was approved by the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra, Pune vide reference No. 2, 3 and 4 have been submitted to the Government. According to the proposal, the government was considering giving administrative approval to various institutions for the development of the center of Talim Kusti.
Government's decision:
Reference No. 1. the decision of the government. The following eligible institutions are being given administrative approval under the Development of Training Wrestling Centre Grant Scheme subject to the terms and conditions as on 01.03.2014.
