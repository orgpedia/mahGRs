16) Work-wise funds are required to be budgeted after obtaining administrative approval/ technical approval in respect of the construction proposal as well as approval of the Committee of Secretaries, where necessary. The old works should be reviewed and completed on priority and the funds should not be provided to the Public Works Department in the form of deposit construction.
