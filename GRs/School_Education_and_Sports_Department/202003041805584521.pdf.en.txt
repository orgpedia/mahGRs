Government Order As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, orders for transfer of officers who have completed three years as on 31st May, 2019 have been issued on 28.05.2019. A decision has been taken to make partial changes in the postings of some officers at the government level. The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. Those concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
Commissioner (Education), Pune, as per this order, shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Officer about the immediate relieving of the said officers from their present posts and shall ensure that the transferred officers join the given place of posting within the prescribed period.
