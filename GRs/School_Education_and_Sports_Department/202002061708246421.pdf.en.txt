Preamble to Order No. 925/2017 dated 5.9.2019:
As per the decision of the Government dated 13.06.2013, it was decided to provide basic training to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) sponsored candidates by appointing them to the post of Education Officer and similar in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A cadre from 01.07.2013. The officers who have completed the training have been posted as Education Officers and equivalent posts as per the Government decision dated 08.08.2013. Accordingly, these officers have been appointed as Education Officers and equivalent posts for a period of 2 years.
The Commissioner (Education) has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 06.10.2017 regarding the completion of the period of probation of the officers appointed to the post of Education Officer and equivalent in Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A. Those who successfully complete the basic training and meet the conditions of the probationary period as per the prevailing policy of the government.

In accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government, the officers mentioned in the accompanying proforma serving in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A Cadre as Education Officer and similar in direct service under the Government Order dated 13.06.2013 mentioned herein are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in Rakana No. 7 before their names.
