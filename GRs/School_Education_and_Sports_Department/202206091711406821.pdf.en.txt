The here. Orders have been issued under the Government Decision dated 28.05.2009. This order has been amended from time to time in accordance with the relevant government decisions. The scope of online admission to Class 11 has also been increased.
Pursuant to the decision of the Supreme Court and Reference No. 13 In accordance with the decision of the Government here and the report received by the Government from the Commissioner, Education, Pune, the Government was considering to include some points in the revision of the online admission process for Class 11 from the academic year 2022-23. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: 1. Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) Action should be taken in accordance with the government decision dated July 5, 2021.
2) Class 11 admission process is carried out on the basis of class 10 marks. If the senior student is the same as the previous year and the date of birth are the same, the full name (name- father's name surname) of the student should be taken in English and the order of the letters should be decided.
3) Facility should be provided for entry and extradition at any stage under Inhoun Quota.
4). 28.03.2016 and D. The villages which were earlier excluded from the MMR (Mumbai Metropolitan Region) and Pune Municipal Corporation areas as per the decision of the Government dated 27.01.2017 should be included in the online admission process for Class XI.
5) Students who are debarred for three regular rounds for the following reasons can be included in the fourth special round.
(a) In case of non-admission with first preference, (b) in case of cancellation of admission, (c) in case of denial of admission by the school, the students concerned shall be debarred for the next round by modifying the existing provision.
The Director (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State shall take necessary action in the matter of making necessary changes in the online admission process and online admission system of Class 11 in accordance with the provisions of the said Government Decision.
