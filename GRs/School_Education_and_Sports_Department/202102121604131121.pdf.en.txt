Government order:
The Rules, 2018 regulating the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Advocate (Group-A), and Advocate (Group-B) in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) have been published vide notification dated 26.02.2018. As per the proviso to Rule 4 (1) (a) of the said Rules, the officers mentioned in the table below, who are working in the Education Empowerment Branch and have completed 55 years of age, are exempted from departmental examination.
