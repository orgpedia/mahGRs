Latur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Sadar Secondary School, Navjeevan Sevabhavi Sanstha, Ausa, Ta. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of Ausa, G. Latur to the Institute on the following terms and conditions: (1) The school should be inspected and appropriate action should be taken regarding the approval of the first account.
(2) The U-Dias number of the school, the name of the institution, etc. details should be updated on the Saral system.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(4) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) It should be ensured that the transfer of the school has the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, the eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(6) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.

(7) It shall be necessary to examine the set recognition of the school, the index number of the board and the personal recognition of the teachers appointed in the school in the prescribed manner and take appropriate action in this regard.

The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(11) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
