Introduction: Shri Tulja Bhavani Education Trust, Dhule runs Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji Sainik Vidyalaya, Morane, Pr. Indubai Bhadane Pratishthan, Borkund, T.G. Dhule, a Marathi medium aided higher primary, secondary and higher secondary (5th to 12th standard) school. G. Dhule has submitted a proposal for transfer to this institution. The proposal was then sent to the government for consideration.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the subject school to the above institution on the following terms and conditions:
1) Shri Tuljabhavani Education Trust, Dhule. The appeal filed before the Joint Charity Commissioner, Nashik vide No. The said transfer is being allowed subject to the above decision dated 17/2017.
2) The name of the school is Raje Sambhaji Military School, Morane, PR. Ner, T. G. Dhule instead of" Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji Sainik Vidyalaya, Morane, Pr. Ner, T. G. Dhule" has been changed. It will be necessary to issue an order by the Education Officer (Secondary), ZP, Dhule regarding this change.
3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.

Government Resolution No. Mashah 3118/ PR No. 342/ SM-1 4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time, the school will be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
5) The approved and working qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff of the school will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding military schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to keep as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as permissible posts and not more than that.
(7) The school shall continue to function on the same basis as it was established after the transfer.
8) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the government permission/ approval in case of a school where the management has changed.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution.
