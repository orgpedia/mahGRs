Preamble: Smt. S. V. Mohta Semi English Primary School, Karode Bhavan, Panchpavali Road, Agrasen Chowk, Nagpur, run by Adarsh Vidya Mandir, Nagpur, is a Hindi medium aided primary school. The proposal received under reference letter for relocation to 1616/ A, Panchsheel Nagar, Binaki Layout, G.Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school building has been proposed to be shifted as the landlord has given a notice to demolish it. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.

The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action regarding the relocation of the said school within 15 days and inform the government about the action taken.
