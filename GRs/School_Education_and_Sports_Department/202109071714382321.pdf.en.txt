The Proposal:
The Public Works Department will repair the office building of Rashtriya Chhatra Sena, Group Headquarters, Mumbai-B with an immediate amount of Rs. 27,33,820/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty only) has been submitted to the Government under reference letter for administrative approval and availability of funds. The budget is based on the schedule for the year 2020-21 and the works have been certified by the Executive Engineer, Department City Division, Mumbai. The matter of granting administrative approval to the budget was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The nature of repair of the office building of Rashtriya Chhatra Sena, Group Headquarters, Mumbai-B is as follows:

| 1.   | Repairs to Flooring, Partition, Storage unit, table  |  chair and plaster and painting at Group Headquarter Mumbai (B) at N.C.C. Building, Mumbai. | Rs. 27,33,820/ |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A total of Rs. 27,33,820/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty) is being granted administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
1.Public Works for these works before inviting tenders for these works.
Technical approval of the competent authorities of the department will be required.
2. In case of provision made in a lax manner, the work should be taken up only after sanctioning detailed budgetary rates.
The work should not be started without the sanction of funds.
