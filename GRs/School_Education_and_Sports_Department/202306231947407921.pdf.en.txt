The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
The competent authority for transfer has been declared as per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005. (2) The competent authority may, by general or special order, transfer his powers under this section.
It has been provided that it can be handed over to any secondary authority. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the power of transfer of officers of various cadres in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the proposal for transfer of transferable officers who have completed three years in office of posting as on 31.5.2023 has been approved by the Civil Services Board No. 1 was presented. urban
