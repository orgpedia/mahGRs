In view of the Dahi Handi festival, the government was considering providing insurance cover to 50,000 Govindas in the state. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision:
Govindas participating in the Dahi Handi festival/ Pro-Govinda League are prone to accidents/ mishaps while erecting human towers. At times, Govinda's life is threatened by unfortunate accidental deaths or serious injuries. As a result, the victims and their families are suffering. Accordingly, to provide insurance cover to Govindas, 50,000 Govindas will be provided Rs. 75/- for a total of Rs. 37,50,000/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) to the concerned Insurance Company. The organization is hereby approved to distribute.
