The government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Football Tournament 2018-19 was held at the Oval Ground, Churchgate, Mumbai from 17th to 22nd December, 2018. The exam was conducted on 4th December 2018. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and Rs. 1,01,550/- (Rupees One Lakh One Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
For participation in the tournament, the concerned Head of Department/ Head of Office shall relieve the player coaches/ managers for the training camp from 12th to 14th December, 2018 and for the tournament from 17th to 22nd December, 2018 with effect from 11th December, 2018 (M.No.) and the services of these players shall be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. All the players and the manager.
