Marashasvaprap/ DIECPD/ V.P./ 2018/ Aastha, Dy. 25.03.2019 GOVERNMENT ORDER: The Rules 2018 regulating the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Advocate (Group-A), and Advocate (Group-B) in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) have been published vide notification dated 26.02.2018. Accordingly, it is mandatory for the officers directly appointed to the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Advocate (Group-A), and Advocate (Group-B) in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to pass this departmental examination.
As per the proviso to Rule 4 (1) (a) of the Departmental Examination Rules, 2018 for the posts of Principal (Group A), Senior Advocate (Group A) and Advocate (Group B) in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch)-" A person who has completed 55 years of age shall be exempted from passing the said examination. " As per the said provision, the officers mentioned in the following bracket who are working in the Education Empowerment Branch and have completed 55 years of age are exempted from the departmental examination.
