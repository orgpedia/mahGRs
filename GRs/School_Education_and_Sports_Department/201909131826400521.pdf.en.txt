the prelude
The government has decided to start private schools in the state. (1) This is permitted by the Government Order dated 26th September, 1995. In the Military Schools approved by the Government since 1996-97, where there are classes up to 10th standard, it is proposed to start the Science stream course of class 11th and class 12th by natural growth. (2) This is permitted by the Government Decision dated 5th July, 2002. Additional batches to be added to the regular batch of Army Schools to impart education to the tribal students in these Military Schools from the year 2008-09. (3) Here. It was approved on December 24, 2007.
Additional Tribal Contingent transferred from Tatyasaheb Kore Military Academy, Warnanagar, G. Kolhapur (which the institution does not want) to Rajiv Gandhi Military School and Higher Secondary School, Kolwad, Buldana, to be started from 2008-09. Approval has been accorded vide letter dated 09.06.2009. It was under consideration of the Government to grant approval to start one batch of Class XI Science for tribal students from 2019-20 and one batch of Class XII Science with natural growth from 2020-21.
Government's decision
Keeping in view the number of tribal students in the school, the following Sainik Schools are being sanctioned a total of 2 tribal batches, one batch of Class XI from 2019-20 and one batch of Class XII from 2020-21.

3. The said Military Schools are being approved to start Tribal Corps (Branch Science) as per Para 2, subject to the following conditions:
The minimum number of students should be 30.
(2) The institution/ school shall ensure that all tribal students admitted to Sainik Schools are able to appear in the Board of Higher Secondary Education Examination (Class XII Science) and that no student is disadvantaged and are eligible to appear in the examination of the National Academy of Sciences.
After the start of the tribal batch in these military schools, the financial responsibility of the expenditure on the tribal batch, which is permissible due to natural growth, for the next academic year will be completely with the Tribal Development Department.
4) The educational institution will be responsible for providing physical facilities in these military schools. No separate grant will be given for this.
4. Reference No. The Tribal Contingent in the said Military School has been sanctioned subject to fulfilment of the conditions laid down under the Government Decisions 1& 2 and the criteria of strength and considering the availability of physical facilities. The matter should be reported to the concerned education officer (secondary).
5. Recognition of the post of teacher on the said batch will be decided separately.
This Government decision is based on the informal reference No.10/ Q.11 of the Tribal Department dated 14.06.2019 and informal reference No.6 of the Finance Department. 421/ Expenditure-5/ Finance Department, dated 25.06.2019.
