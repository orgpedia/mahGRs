The procedure for inclusion of teachers/ non-teaching staff of primary, secondary, higher secondary and teacher schools in the National Pension Scheme (NPS) in respect of 100% aided posts of Zilla Parishad and 100% aided posts of recognized private schools appointed on or after 1st November, 2005 has been prescribed under the decision of the Government in this regard.
In this regard, due to the delay in getting the registration number of DTA, DTO, DDO under NPS, technical difficulties in filling the registration form of the employee in the CSRF-1 format, as well as the possibility of delay in making necessary changes in the salary system, the dates of implementation of the procedure prescribed under the above Government decision were changed by the Corrigendum on Reference No. 2.
However, it will take some time to complete the process.
However, wherever the date is mentioned as" 31/01/2020," the date should be replaced by" 31/03/2020" and where the date is mentioned as" 01/02/2020," the date should be read as" 01/04/2020."
