Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra was considering the issue of a new account head to implement the ambitious program of planting 50 crore trees to realize the concept of Green Maharashtra.
Government Decision: As per the approval given by the Planning and Finance Department, approval is being given to open new account titles as follows.
Demand No. E-3 2406- Forestry and Wildlife 04- Forestry and Environmental Development, 101- National Programme for Forestry and Environmental Development, (00) (01) Tree Plantation and Cultivation under the Sustainable Development Goals (Goal 15- Living on Land) (100% State Plan) (Expenditure on the Programme)
(2406 A283)
This Government decision is being issued with the approval of the Principal Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts), Mumbai vide informal reference no. KV/ Cha-1/ School Education and Sports Department/ UOR- 116/2018-19/ 962 dated 27/12/2018 and also vide informal reference no. 898/ Expenditure-5 of Finance Department dated 29th October, 2018.
