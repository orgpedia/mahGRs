The Government Circular No. Government Circular No.- Seniority No. 2118/ PR No. 103/ PR-II dated 31.07.2018 containing the comprehensive five-year provisional seniority of the post of Education Officer and similar in Group-A (Administration Branch) of Maharashtra Education Service, dated 01.01.2012 to 31.12.2016 was published. If there are objections from some officers regarding this seniority, they were instructed to send it to the government through the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune within 1 month from the date of publication of the provisional seniority. However, the officials did not respond on the issue.
(2) The said seniority has been maintained among the officers who have joined the promoted post within the prescribed period (after the period of assumption of office) as per the provisions of rules 4 and 5 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982.
The said seniority is being issued subject to the decision of the Supreme Court, the High Court and the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal on any other special civil application/ writ petition filed earlier or if this seniority list has to be changed.
In view of all the facts and circumstances mentioned in the above paragraph, the final seniority of the Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Administration Branch) Education Officer and similar posts from 01.01.2012 to 31.12.2016 is being published in the form attached.
