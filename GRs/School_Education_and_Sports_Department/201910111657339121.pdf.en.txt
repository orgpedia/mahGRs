Preamble: Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2019, a total of 5279 temporary posts in the Regional Offices under the School Education Department have been filled up from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019
The deadline was extended. As the said period has expired, the Commissioner, Education, State of Maharashtra, Pune has issued Reference No. As proposed under section 4 of the Finance Department Reference No. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Section 3, the Government was considering the extension of a total of 5279 temporary posts in the office of the Commissioner of Education and in all field offices under him.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The existing 5279 temporary posts in the Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and its subordinate field offices will be filled up by this Government under this decision from 1st October, 2019 to 29th October, 2019.
The government has extended the deadline to February 2020. The establishment-wise details in this regard are given in Form A and Form B.
Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and all the offices under him and all the offices of the Divisional Deputy Director of Education should take care to attach the office-wise and post-wise statements of the posts in his office while submitting the salary of the temporary posts.
