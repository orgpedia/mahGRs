Preamble 4217/ (93/2017)/ Training/ dated 25th October, 2017 Preamble: As per the decision of the Government dated 25th October, 2017, the Department of Vocational Guidance and Psychological Counselling has been started at Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune. The government was considering to start a" Business Guidance Cell" in every District Educational and Continuing Professional Development Institute to provide vocational guidance and psychological counseling to the children at the secondary level in the state, as well as to start diploma and certificate courses in business guidance and psychological counseling.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to start a Business Mentoring Cell in every District Educational and Sustainable Professional Development Institute.
Those who complete the first level of continuous courses will be recognized as a Career Scientist Certificate and those who complete the 4 levels will be recognized as a Diploma.
