Waiving of examination fee of students who have lost their parents, surviving parent or legal guardian/ adoptive parents due to outbreak of COVID-19 virus as a special case.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No:- SCEO-2021/ S.No.84/ SD-5
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai 400032
Date: December 3, 2021.
Reference: 1) Letter dated 25.11.2021 from Secretary, Government of Maharashtra, (Secondary& Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune
Some students have lost their parents, surviving parents or legal guardians/ adoptive parents due to the outbreak of COVID-19 virus in the state of Maharashtra. Such students may face difficulties in paying the examination fee of Secondary School Certificate Examination (Class 10)/ Higher Secondary Certificate Examination (Class 12) due to financial situation. Therefore, if the student is deprived of the examination due to not being able to pay the board examination fee, there is a possibility of hindering their academic progress and further academic progress. The government was considering waiving the examination fee for the Secondary School Certificate Examination (Class 10)/ Higher Secondary Certificate Examination (Class 12) of such affected students.
Government Decision: Considering the larger interest of the affected students who have lost their parents, surviving parents or legal guardians/ adoptive parents due to the outbreak of COVID-19 virus in the State, the Government is approving waiver of examination fee as a special case for regular students appearing in the Secondary School Certificate Examination (Class 10)/ Higher Secondary Certificate Examination (Class 12) to be conducted by the Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Maharashtra in the year 2021-22.
The expenditure for this should be met from the funds available with the Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Pune.
