The Proposal:
Reference No. (1) Teachers/ non-teaching staff of private primary/ secondary and higher secondary schools in the State under the School Education Department who are on salary subsidy as on April 1, 2008 as per the Government decision, dated January 19, 2013.
This year, after freezing the wage subsidy payable at the stage at which wage subsidy was admissible as per the Fifth Pay Commission, the post-pay subsidy (4% post-pay subsidy and 1% building rent/ maintenance subsidy) at the rate of 5% of the pay at which the wage subsidy was admissible has been increased to about Rs. 266.82 crore has been approved to be made available from 1st April, 2013.
Reference No. (2) Instead of applying the revised figure for the Class IV employees of the recognized private partially/ fully aided middle and high schools in the State as per the Government decision, dated 11th December, 2020, the Per-shala peon allowance has been applied for the post which will lapse after the Class IV employees retire as per the prescribed age.
Reference No. (3) As per the Government Decision, dated 3rd September, 2021, the recognized aided primary, secondary and higher secondary schools in the State will be given Rs. 2675.20 lakh has been disbursed.
For the financial year 2021-22, the government is considering to sanction additional grant-in-aid to the private aided primary/ secondary and higher secondary schools in the state to meet their non-salary expenses and to pay peon's allowance to the private partially/ fully aided secondary and higher secondary schools in proportion to the posts vacant as per the prescribed age of the class IV employees.
