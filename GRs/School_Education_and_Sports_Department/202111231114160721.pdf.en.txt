The proposal received under the reference letter for shifting of Amravati, a Marathi medium unaided secondary (class 8th to 10th) school, to Mauje Anjangaon Surji, G. Amravati, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Relocation of the above secondary school has been proposed in accordance with the request of the people's representative. Therefore, the relocation of the said secondary school to the above-mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
