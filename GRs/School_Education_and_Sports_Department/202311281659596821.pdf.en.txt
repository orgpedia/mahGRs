Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for shifting of the Marathi medium aided primary (Class 1st to 4th) school, Nanded Road, Latur, run by Shivchhatrapati Education Society, Latur, to Yashwant Primary Vidyamandir, Sallegalli, Latur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has been shifted from a rented building to a self-owned building of the institution. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved post facto on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
