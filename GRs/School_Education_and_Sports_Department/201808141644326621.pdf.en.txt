Preamble: The proposal received under the reference letter for shifting of the Urdu medium unaided higher primary school, Mouza Limbala Makta, Hingoli, T. G. Hingoli, run by Matriulya Rural Development Society, Yavatmal, to Sharifa Urdu Primary School, Dhamangaon Road, Yavatmal, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from Dhamangaon Road, Yavatmal to Mauja Limbala Makta, Hingoli, T.G. Hingoli. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) It is to be ensured that the said school is functioning at the original location.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(3) The parents of all students in the school shall be required to give written consent to the relocation.
(4) For the adjustment of such students, it shall be necessary to have an Urdu medium upper primary school within 3 km and to accommodate all such students therein.
(5) The written consent of all teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be required for migration. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(6) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, the school shall be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(7) Since the migration of the said minority school is taking place at a distance more than the prescribed norms, it will be necessary to ensure that there is a requirement of Urdu medium upper primary school at the place of migration and that there is no objection to the relocation of this school.

The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(9) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(10) The school shall function at the place of relocation on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(11) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible in accordance with the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(12) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (1), (3), (4), (5), (6) and (7), the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Amravati Division, Amravati shall take necessary further action regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the government about the action taken.
