Introduction: Integrated Institute of Rural and Agricultural Development, Parbhani runs Junior College of Entrance of Science and Arts, Khatkali Campus, Hingoli, Dist. Hingoli is a partially aided higher secondary school. 11th to 12th, Branch- Arts and Science), Social Harmony Service Institute Morthalwadi (Halli), Ta. The proposal received under reference letters for transfer of management to Udgir G. Latur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of this school is a social harmony service organization Mortalwadi (Hali), Tt. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to Udgir G. Latur on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
