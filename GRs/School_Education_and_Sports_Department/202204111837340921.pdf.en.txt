the prelude
In the history of India, many great personalities have worked day and night for the country and the society, fostering social commitment and patriotism. The legacy of his service continues to be a model, guide and guide for the countrymen even today. Among these great personalities, the name of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar has to be taken first.
Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar was an Indian jurist, economist, politician, social reformer and politician. She inspired the movement of social revolution and advocated for the rights of women and workers. He was independent India's first law minister and the principal architect of the Constitution of India.
His contribution to various fields of knowledge is unparalleled.
Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar created a sense of self in the society, created a fighting spirit among them and ensured that the society got its rights. The Satyagraha of Mahad, the burning of the Manusmriti, the Round Table Conference, the Pune Accord, the formation of the Swatantra Mazdoor Party, the formation of the Bahishkrit Hitakarini Sabha, the formation of the People's Education Society, the fundamental work in terms of gender equality, etc. were some of the important things that Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar did in his life.
The main focus was on the lower strata of society. " Learn, unite and fight," he said. India is a country with various religions, languages, castes and cultures.
Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar had a great love for reading. We cannot live without books. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar was a scholar of law, economics, political science, sociology, journalism, and water conservation.
