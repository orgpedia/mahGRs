Regulation of transfers and performance of official duties of Government of Maharashtra
The Prevention of Delay Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
As per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the competent authority for transfer has been declared. (2) The competent authority may, by general or special order, transfer his powers under this section.
It has been provided that it can be handed over to any secondary authority. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the power of transfer of officers of various cadres in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of the said Act, a proposal for transfer of officers who have completed three years of service as on 31st May, 2018 was submitted to the Civil Services Board.
The officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 against their names with the prior approval of the competent authority as per the recommendation of the Civil Services Board.
