Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Football Tournament 2022-23 to be held at Bhubaneswar, Odisha from 18th to 23rd March, 2023 will be held at Oval Ground, Churchgate, Mumbai and Karnataka Sports Ground, Churchgate, Mumbai. The selection test was conducted on 4th and 5th March, 2023. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,52,400/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Two Thousand Four Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 13th to 15th March, 2023 and for the tournament from 18th to 23rd March, 2023 with effect from 11th March, 2023 (M.No.) and provide the services of these players to the Secretariat Gymkhana.
Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The period of absence from office from March 13 to 23, 2023 (training camp, competition and travel) should be treated as" period of duty." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned. Also, the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai shall be governed by the Finance Department Government Decision No. LVE 1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9 dated 11th May, 1989 and the Finance Department Government Decision No. LVE.
