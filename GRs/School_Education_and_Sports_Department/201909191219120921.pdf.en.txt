Introduction: Vikas Shikshan Sanstha, Mumbai runs Gyandeep Hindi Night School or Pantnagar Municipal School No. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of Hindi medium aided secondary night school at 3, Ghatkopar, Mumbai-75 to Municipal Corporation School, Lal Bahadur Shastri Marg, Mulund, Mumbai-80 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above night school is proposed to be shifted in the interest of students' convenience. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The institution shall be obliged to provide the necessary classrooms and necessary physical facilities for the classes to be held in the night school.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff shall continue to be employed.

The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai shall take further action within 4 weeks regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the government about the action taken.
