Preamble: Shri Datta Meghe Bal Kalyan Shikshan Sanstha, School of Scholars, Dhamangaon Railway, G. Dattapur, No. 134/2, Dhamangaon Railway, the proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation to Amravati was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from the existing inadequate building to a self-sufficient building. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the English medium school as it is on permanent non-subsidized basis.
(6) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding this migration or if it is found that there is a violation of the conditions regarding migration.
