The Department of School Education and Sports, Government vide Notification dated 26.02.2018, has notified the Departmental Examination Rules, 2018 for the posts of Principal Group-A, Senior Supervisor (Group-A) and Supervisor Group-B in the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) with effect from 26.02.2018. As per Rule 8 of the Rules, 50 per cent of the total marks in each paper is required to pass the examination. As per Rule 11, a candidate who fails in the examination for exemption in a particular paper but obtains 60 or more marks in any subject, shall be exempted from appearing and passing the examination for that paper in the subsequent attempt.
2. Under the Government Decision dated 31.03.2021 of the General Administration Department, the policy on" Common Matters regarding Departmental Examinations" of various administrative departments of the State Government has been fixed. To pass the exam, a candidate must have scored at least 50 per cent marks in each subject.
In accordance with the said provision of the General Administration Department, the Department of School Education and Sports is considering to amend the" Departmental Examination Rules 2018" for the posts of Principal Group-A, Senior Advocate (Group-A) and Advocate Group-B in the" Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch)" cadre. The government is taking the following decisions so that the candidates appearing for the examination do not suffer till this reform is finalized.

Government's decision:
The provision of Rule 11 in the" Departmental Examination Rules, 2018" for the posts of Principal Group-A, Senior Advocate (Group-A) and Advocate Group-B in the cadre of" Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch)" is being amended as follows.
The candidates will have to appear for a departmental examination
A minimum of 50% marks in each subject is required to pass the exam. If a candidate fails to score 50% or more marks in any of the subjects in that examination, that candidate is exempted from appearing and passing the examination for the question paper of that subject in the subsequent attempt. "
(2) This Decision shall come into force on the date of its publication.
