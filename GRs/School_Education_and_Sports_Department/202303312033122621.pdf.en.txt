Introduction: The Sports Federation of India organizes various sports competitions at school level from Taluka to National level every year. Maharashtra is represented by teams/ players in national level competitions in various sports. This competition is very useful for the school children to develop a love of sports and to see the sportsmanship in them and this is what produces the best players. Sports events are equally important to create opportunities for the youth, especially in rural areas, to participate in various sports events. The best players of the state are selected for the national competition. The Government has taken the decision in accordance with the recommendations of the Sports Policy 2012. The modalities for conducting the School Games have been decided as on 24.12.2014. The tournament was not held for the last two years due to the COVID-19 pandemic. government this year.
