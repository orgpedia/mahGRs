Establishment of Satara Military School. Established on June 23, 1961, it is the first military school in the country. The main objective of this school is to provide opportunities to the poor and meritorious children of the bottom rung of Maharashtra at all levels to become officers in the Indian Army through the National Defence Academy. A large number of students from this school have so far been admitted to the National Defence Academy.
The Satara Sainik School is sanctioned ex-gratia (non-salary) every year for training and administrative expenses, building maintenance, food allowance, horse maintenance, advertisement expenses, municipal taxes, etc. 22020469 for the financial year 2020-21. The budget provision is Rs 6,56,85, 000/-. Satara Sainik School No. (1) 25% of the funds have been disbursed as per the decision of the Government. For the financial year 2020-21, the Finance Department has released an additional 25% of funds, which is 12.5% i.e. Rs.
82,10,625/- has been approved for disbursement. For the financial year 2020-21, Rs. 82,10,625/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: From the budgetary provision available in the financial year 2020-21, Satara Sainik School has been given a grant of Rs. 82,10,625/- (Rupees Eighty Two Lakh Ten Thousand Six Hundred and Twenty Five only) is being approved by the Government.
The expenditure should be met from the headings" Demand No.E-2, 2202-General Education, 02-Secondary Education-110, Assistance to Non-Government Secondary Schools (00) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Poorvadayan Sainik Schools (22020469), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
The Commissioner, Education, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Satara is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said funds.
