The Maharashtra Olympic Association has decided to organize a" Maharashtra Mini Olympic" tournament in the state to create a sporting environment in the state and to encourage various sports, so that the players who are showing their skills at the international, national and state level get maximum opportunities to play in the competition and the players are prepared for the national sports competition. The estimated cost of Rs. 19,07,94, 600/- is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
1) The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services and the Chief Event Organizing Committee should take care of the expenditure in 2022 as compared to 2014 in terms of accommodation and food.
(2) The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services and the Chief Event Organizing Committee shall be responsible for approving the purchase of sports materials and other ancillary items in the prescribed manner and ensuring that the expenditure is incurred in the prescribed manner.
