The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval for the relocation of Nagpur English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary School, Premnagar, Navapur, Dist.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from inadequate building to self-sufficient building.
The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. - (1) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur shall ensure that the school was functioning before the transfer and has not been closed or de-recognised after the transfer and that the institution has passed a resolution regarding the transfer approval with the consent (including signature) of all the members of the valid executive board.
(2) It shall be mandatory for the institution to notarize the details of the duration, amount of rent, etc. by entering into a lease agreement with the owner of the building at the place of relocation of the school and to fill up the complete details of the students and teachers of the school on the online system and U-DIAS system.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who were working before the migration have been accommodated at the place of migration and they have no complaints/ objections regarding the migration. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, a pucca building for the school at the place of relocation

(6) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(7) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.

(9) It shall be made clear to the institution to take care that the school is no longer relocated without prior approval of the Government.
(10) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur should take further action within 4 weeks regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the government about the action taken.
