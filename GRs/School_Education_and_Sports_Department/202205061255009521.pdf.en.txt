The 4th Khelo India Youth National Games will be held in Haryana (Panchkula) from 4th June 2022 to 13th June 2022. Contest is open to children under the age of 18. In order to ensure that the State of Maharashtra remains at the top of the medal tally in the 4th Khelo India Youth National Games, the State Government has decided to conduct District, Zonal and State Level Selection Trials for the selection of teams for the State of Maharashtra in Kabaddi, Kho-Kho and Basketball. Personal
The selection of the athletes in the sports disciplines will be done by the National Federations of the respective sports. A total of 420 players have been selected for the tournament. With them, 130 guides, assistant guides, office bearers, managers, assistant managers, etc.
A team of officials will accompany the players to the venue. For the first phase of district, division and state level selection trials, the Sports Director has issued Reference No. 4 as on 10.12.2022 amounting to Rs. The budget of Rs. 61,41,000/- has been submitted to the Government.
The second phase will include a pre-competition training camp and a national competition.
For this, a 13-day training camp for the players selected in the state team through selection trials will be held at Shri Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune from 18 th May, 2022. The Directorate of Sports has issued Reference No. 5 vide letter dated 06.01.2022, Rs. Budget of Rs. 1,48,12, 800/- submitted to the Government for approval
Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. A request has been made to approve the budget of Rs. 2,09,53, 800/-.
The distance from Pune to Haryana (Panchkula) is about 30 hours by train. To ensure that the athletes of the state participating in the national competition do not feel the fatigue of the journey and adversely affect their performance, 420 players have been selected for the national competition this year as well as last year.
