The Proposal:
The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 has been in force in the State since April 2010. Every child between the ages of 6 and 14 has the right to quality education. Under this Act, the state government has been entrusted with the responsibility of providing necessary basic educational facilities to the students. Accordingly, as per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra dated 22nd June 2015, the Advanced Education Maharashtra Programme has been launched in the state and it is being effectively implemented.
Students need to be encouraged and provided with opportunities to develop interest in science and mathematics and develop curiosity and creative qualities.

In view of the above, the Director of Education has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 31/1/2023 regarding the establishment of the said Innovative Science Centre in the Zilla Parishad Schools in the State. As per the proposal of the Director of Education, the government was considering to set up innovative science centers in the schools of the Zilla Parishads in the state.
Government Decision: 1. Administrative approval is being accorded at the level of Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune for supply and construction of Innovative Science Centres in Zilla Parishad Schools in the State within the limit of provision of Rs. 46.25 crore.
The Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune shall execute the e-tendering process for the establishment of the said Innovative Science Centre by following the provisions of the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour and the prescribed procurement rules. The process should be completed by the State Level Procurement Committee under the chairmanship of Director of Education (Primary), Maharashtra State, Pune.
