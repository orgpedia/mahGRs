The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has come into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
As per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the competent authority for transfer has been declared. Provided further that the competent authority transferring under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the powers of various cadre officers in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.
As per the provisions of Rule 4 (4) II and Rule 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the proposal for transfer was submitted to the Civil Services Board on the request of the officers of the Senior Advocate Cadre of Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A. The officers mentioned in the table below are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 3 against their names, with the prior approval of the competent authority and the concerned senior authorities, as per the recommendation of the Civil Services Board.

The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. Their attention is drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No.- CDR 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30th August, 1992. Those concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the transfer in view of the instructions given in it, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
Commissioner (Education), Pune, as per this order, shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Officer about the immediate release of the said officers from their present posts and shall ensure that the transferred officers join the given place of posting within the prescribed period and submit a report to the Government.
