School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.: Government Resolution No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: Departmental Education Deputy Director, Aurangabad No. Preamble to the letter dated 24/04/2017: The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the permanent unaided English medium primary and upper primary schools of New Kids Paradise English Primary and Upper Primary School, Aurangabad, run by Shri Hanuman Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad, to New Sahara Education and Welfare Society, Aurangabad, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the necessary documents for transfer have not been completed in this case, the proposal to transfer the permanent unaided primary and upper primary school of English medium New Kids Paradise English Primary and Upper Primary School, Aurangabad, operated by Shri Hanuman Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad, to New Sahara Education and Welfare Society, Aurangabad, is being rejected.
