The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Shirpur, District Dhule was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided that there will be no parking facility for students and accommodation for teachers at the school premises.
Therefore, the institute has decided to set up the school at Group No. 1 for the benefit of the students in the hilly areas. 22, loki, t. Relocation to Shirpur, Dhule has been proposed. Therefore, the Government Decision No.
As per Para-5 of Masha Sth-3321/ PR No.401/ SM-1, dated 28.7.2021, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the said school is a Marathi medium non-aided school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
