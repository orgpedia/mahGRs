(5) For payment of the amount of matching contribution to the Government, Sample No. R7 The Education Officer/ Inspector of Education (Primary, Secondary)/ Chief Accounts and Finance Officer shall get the certificate attested by the concerned Treasury and send the copies of the certificate to the Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency every month. The Sub-State Records Maintenance Agency shall send a copy of the challan of the amounts collected from the employees and deposited in the Government Accounts along with the information in Sample R2 to the Controller. Copies of the invoices should also be sent to the Controller without fail after the employer's contribution is paid.
The Controller shall collect the information monthly from the Deputy State Record Keeping Agency and send such information to the Deputy Secretary (Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff), School Education Department and State Records Agency, Mumbai without fail every month. Also, the utility certificate regarding the expenditure incurred for these purposes should be sent to the Accountant General's Office, as well as to this department.
(6) The Department of Finance has issued Government Decisions fixing the rate of interest every year.
Also, while considering the interest, the Finance Department Circular No. Care should be taken to take action as per Annio 1010/ Q.No.67/ Service-4, dated 16th November, 2012. Accordingly, the Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency should take action regarding the levy of interest at the prescribed rate and submit the payment of the specified amount to the Treasury for the purpose of drawing the amount of interest in Sample 45A. There is no need to add the list of employees whose accounts are to be credited with the payment.
However, the payment should be certified by the Sub-State Record Keeping Agency to ensure that the amount is accurate. The information in this regard should be sent to the Controller by the Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency. Also, the Controlling Officers should send the report to the Deputy Secretary (Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff), School Education Department and State Records Agency, Mumbai without fail every month.
(7) The Accountant General, Maharashtra-1 and 2 Mumbai/ Nagpur shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts for audit.
(8) The utilization certificate of the funds being made available shall be submitted to the Government.
(4) This Government decision was taken with the concurrence of the Finance Department and in Informal Reference No. 100/18/ Service-4, dated 5/05/2018, vide Government Resolution No. Annio-1006/ (23/06) Mashi-2 is being issued subject to the terms and conditions dated 29/11/2010.
