Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the school where the management is changed for any reason.
(2) The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati shall be required to verify that there are no objections/ objections of the Executive Board members regarding this transfer.
(3) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be ensured that the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff is obtained for the transfer of the school. Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
