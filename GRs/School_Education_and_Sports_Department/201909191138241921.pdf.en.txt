GOVERNMENT ORDER Shri Anil Narayan Kolhe, Deputy Education Officer has been transferred and posted as Education Officer, Municipal Corporation, Amravati vide Government Order dated 14.06.2018
However, claiming that the post was a promotion from the municipal corporation employees, Dr. Abdul Razik Abdul Hamid said that the post was a promotion from the municipal corporation employees. Writ Petition No. 3527/2018 was filed in the High Court, Nagpur. In this petition, The High Court, Nagpur vide its order dated 30.07.2019 has passed an order for the promotion of Dr. Abdul Razik Abdul Hamid to the rank of Additional Director within six weeks.
2. m. In view of the above order of the High Court, Nagpur, the Government vide order dated 14.06.2018, has appointed Mr. Anil Kolhe, Education Officer, Municipal Corporation, Amravati as the Commissioner of Municipal Administration, Amravati.
As per the administrative arrangement, he is being posted as Deputy Educational Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Amravati.
Anil Narayan Kolhe should immediately take charge as Deputy Educational Officer (Secondary), G. P. Amravati and should not go on unauthorised leave.
Commissioner (Education), Pune shall immediately inform the concerned Controlling Authorities about the immediate action taken as per this order and ensure that Mr. Anil Kolhe joins the place of posting immediately and submit a report to the Government in this regard.
