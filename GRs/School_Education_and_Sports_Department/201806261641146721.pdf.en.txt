Proposal- Reference No. As per the Government decision of 01.01.2020, a scheme is being implemented to honour the medal winners of Olympic, Commonwealth and Asian Games by giving them cash awards. The state government was considering awarding cash prizes to the players and their mentors who brought laurels to the state of Maharashtra by winning medals in the Commonwealth Games-2018 held in Gold Coast, Australia.
