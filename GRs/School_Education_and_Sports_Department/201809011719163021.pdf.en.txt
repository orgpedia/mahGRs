Acceptance is subject to the following terms and conditions:
The Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary) at the state level should prepare a service excellence list of part-time librarians. Apply for it online.
The date of first appointment should be taken into account while preparing the service excellence list. The appointment must be approved by the Education Officer (Secondary)/ Inspector of Education.
The concerned staff should upload the information on the website and authenticate themselves. After this, the headmaster of the working school should authenticate the information and the education officer (secondary)/ education inspector should check the information/ records uploaded online and forward it to the director of education (secondary and higher secondary).

Accordingly, the institution-wise/ school-wise/ reservation-wise list of vacant posts of full-time librarian in the district should be published online on the website by the Education Officer (Secondary)/ Education Inspector within 60 days.
While upgrading the part-time librarians, adjustments should be made in the institutions/ schools that are vacant in the first taluk. If the post of full-time librarian is not vacant in the taluka, adjustment should be made at the department level if the post of full-time librarian is not vacant in the district.
At the time of upgrading the part-time librarian to full-time librarian as per the service excellence, an undertaking should be taken that he is ready to attend the school/ institution obtained online as per the service excellence. Failure to appear in the institution/ school will disqualify them for upgradation in future.
