Reference: Departmental Deputy Director of Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad vide No. Letter dated 17.05.2023.
Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for shifting of Vidyadham Pratishthan, Aurangabad-run Vidyadham Public School, Saraswati Bhuvan Colony, Aurangabadpura, Aurangabad to Chikalthana, Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted due to inadequate physical facilities. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
