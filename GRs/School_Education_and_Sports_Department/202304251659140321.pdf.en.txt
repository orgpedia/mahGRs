Preamble: The proposal received under the letter of reference for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation of Blue Heavens English School, Katraj, Pune, run by Revaben Nagindas Mehta Foundation, Pune to 30/4/3/ 1 Shelar Malla, Mahadev Nagar, Katraj Kondhwa Road, Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to rent and inadequacy of the above school building, it has been shifted to 30/4/3/ 1 Shelar Malla, Mahadev Nagar, Katraj, Kondhwa Road, Dist. Therefore, the relocation of the said school is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason. All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
