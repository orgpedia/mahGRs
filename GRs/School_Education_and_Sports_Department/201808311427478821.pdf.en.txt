Introduction: Shri Venkatesh Education Society, Ahmednagar operates Strawberry English Primary (Class 1st to 7th State Board) and Secondary (Class Vth to 10th- CBSE Board) School, Sangamner City, G. Ghulewadi, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for the relocation to Sangamner, G. Ahmednagar was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the relocation of the school to a spacious building on the grounds of inadequate physical facilities and insufficient space on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrant school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar shall ensure that all pre-migration students are accommodated in the nearest school/ place of migration and there are no complaints/ objections in this regard and the building at the place of migration is safe for the students.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Further, all the eligible teaching and non-teaching staff have been accommodated at the migrant place and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
(4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and orders issued from time to time by the Government, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided for the school. A notarized affidavit should be taken on the stamp paper of 100/-.
