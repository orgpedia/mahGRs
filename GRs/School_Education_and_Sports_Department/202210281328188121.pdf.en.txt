Date of release: October 27, 2022
Government order:
The e-tenders were invited from the Government website vide Notice dated 12.09.2022 for entering into a one-year contract with the supplier for supply of stationery and essential office supplies for School Education and Sports Department (itself). Out of the five e-tenders received, The tender submitted by Devish Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai is technically sound and is less expensive than other bidders. Also by letter dated October 17, 2022. Devish Enterprises has reduced the prices of some items wherever possible. May for a period of one year from 1st October, 2022 to 30th September, 2023. Devish Enterprises, Fort, Mumbai has been unanimously selected to tender and contract with them subject to the following terms and conditions.
The period of supply of high quality stationery for School Education and Sports Department (itself) will be from 1.10.2022 to 30.09.2023.
It shall be obligatory to supply the materials and stationery of the company mentioned in the tender or of better quality as per the demand registered by the department from time to time at the rate mentioned in the contract.
(ii) The supplier shall be bound to supply the requisitioned stationery as per the rates and standards fixed by the Department on requisition of stationery as required. Any change in the quality or price of the supplied stationery will be acceptable to the supplier, who will decide to reject the goods and terminate the contract if the compliance of the contract is not satisfactory.
(iv) If the Department is in urgent need of goods other than those mentioned in the list, it shall be obliged to supply the same immediately after registering the demand for such goods. Also, it will be mandatory for the supplier to supply such goods at a rate less than 5 per cent of the market price (MRP).
