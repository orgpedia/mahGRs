Introduction: Siddheshwar Sports Board, Sillod, G. Aurangabad operated Dayanand Secondary School, Kaigaon, Ta. Sillod, G. Aurangabad of Marathi medium aided secondary school, Ghatambri, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to shift to Sillod, G. Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted to a spacious rented building at the present location due to lack of infrastructure and basic amenities. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure in writing the consent of the parents of the students of the school to the migration and to make the adjustment of all the students of the school at the place of migration or at the nearest distance to the aided school. The education officer (secondary), zilla parishad, Aurangabad should ensure this.
(3) It shall be ensured in writing that the consent of all teachers/ non-teaching staff is given for migration. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The school shall be provided with a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(7) The certificate of minority status of the said institution shall be verified.
(8) The school shall function at the place of relocation on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(9) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(10) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (3), (4) and (7), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall take necessary further action regarding approval of relocation of the said school and inform the Government about the action taken.
