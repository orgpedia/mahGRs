Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for the construction of Olympic Bhawan building in the premises of Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mhalunge-Balewadi, District- Pune, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 44.70 crore (Rs. Forty Four Crores Seventy Lakh only) subject to the following conditions:
1) The tender process for the construction of the Olympic building in the Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune should be carried out and the work order should be issued immediately.
2) A suitable agreement will have to be signed between the Sports Department and the Maharashtra Olympic Association regarding the transfer and use of the building by constructing the Olympic Building on the said site.
