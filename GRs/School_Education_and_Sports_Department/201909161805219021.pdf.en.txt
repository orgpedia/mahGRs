The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Jawahar Bal Bhavan, Mumbai has been granted approval for registration under the Registration Act, 1860 and its Memorandum of Association and its Rules vide Government Resolution dated 6th April, 1977. According to this, the Maharashtra State Jawahar Bal Bhavan, Mumbai is a registered society under the Societies Registration Act. As per the provisions of the Memorandum of Association of the Institute, a Governing Body has been constituted, which includes government and non-government members. This is the governing body. It is headed by the Minister of Education. The government was considering the appointment of 5 non-official members in this regulatory board. The government's decision is as follows:
Government's decision
The following non-official members are being approved by the Government to be appointed in the Governing Body of Maharashtra State Jawahar Bal Bhavan, Charni Road, Mumbai.
