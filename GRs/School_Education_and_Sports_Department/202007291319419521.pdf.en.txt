The following 3 officers have been appointed to the post of Direct Service in the cadre of Training Institute vide letter dated 4th August, 2017 and since the period of one year has elapsed from the date of joining the post, they have been requested to be transferred to the Revenue Department as per Rule-12 (A) of the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2015 for appointment to the posts of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted and Gazetted) of the Government of Maharashtra by direct service and promotion.
As per the provisions of Rule 12 of the notification dated 28.04.2015 of the General Administration Department, the concerned officers can be transferred to another revenue department only after completing at least one year of service in the allotted department. As per the request of these officers, their request for change of revenue department and posting was placed in the meeting of the Civil Services Board. In pursuance of the recommendations of the Civil Services Board, the competent authority, with the prior approval of the senior authorities concerned, transfers and postings of the officers in Table (3) to the Revenue Department shown in column (6) and to the post shown in column (7) before their names
