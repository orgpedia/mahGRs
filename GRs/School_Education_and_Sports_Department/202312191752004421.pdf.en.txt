The proposal-
The defined contribution pension scheme applicable to the teaching and non-teaching staff of 100% aided posts in private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools recognized by the Zilla Parishads has been included in the National Pension Scheme of the Central Government. The government has taken a decision. The amount collected under the defined contribution pension scheme of teachers/ non-teaching staff of Zilla Parishad and recognized private aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools will be transferred under the National Pension Scheme. 2 has been approved. Also, the Finance Department's reference No. (3) Financial powers have been amended by the Government Circular here. The government is planning to release funds under this head for the financial year 2023-24.
Government's decision
For the financial year 2023-24, the following statements are given in column No. 3 has been provided. The provision No. A total of Rs 4. 3823,73,64/- (Rupees Three thousand eight hundred and twenty three crore seventy three lakh sixty four thousand only) is being approved for disbursement as per the following statement.
