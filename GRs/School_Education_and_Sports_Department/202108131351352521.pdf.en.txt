Circular:
India got Independence on 15th August 1947. Today, 15th August is celebrated as Independence Day across the country. This year marks the 74th anniversary of India's Independence. On this occasion, various programs will be organized for the students of class 1 to class 12 in all management schools of the state with various objectives to make the students aware of the sacrifices made by the revolutionaries for the country and to instill a sense of national integration in them. During the day, various activities such as tree plantation, debate competition through online mode at the inter-school/ inter-college level, quiz/ patriotic essay and poetry, painting competition, singing of patriotic songs, elocution competition, collection of rare photographs of freedom struggle etc. should be organized online/ offline.
The following programmes will be organized on the occasion of 74th Anniversary of Indian Independence:
