the prelude
The ratio of Central and State share for Samagra Shiksha (Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan) scheme is 60:40. The Central Government vide order dated 28/09/2019 has released the first instalment of Central Government's Recurring/ Non-recurring (ST Sub Plan share) amounting to Rs. 39,87,73, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Eighty Seven Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Out of this amount, Rs. 26,58,48, 667/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Fifty Eight Lakhs Forty Eight Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Seven only).
