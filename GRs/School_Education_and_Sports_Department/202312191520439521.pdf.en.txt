Introduction: With a view to inculcate scientific temperament in children and to provide a platform to students in the age group of 14-18 years to showcase their creativity, National Children's Science Exhibition is organized by National Council of Educational Research and Training, New Delhi every year for children in a rotational manner with the help of any State. The 49th National Children's Science Exhibition-2022 was held in Guwahati, Assam. This year, the 50th National Children's Scientific Exhibition-2023 will be held in the state. The 50th National Children's Science Exhibition-2023 will be organized at an estimated cost of Rs. 4,91,00, 000/- (Rs. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. In this regard, the Government was considering to allocate funds for the 50th National Children's Scientific Exhibition-2023.
Government Decision: 50th National Children's Science Exhibition-2023 to be funded at a cost of Rs. 4,91,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Ninety One Lakhs) is being approved under this Government decision.
