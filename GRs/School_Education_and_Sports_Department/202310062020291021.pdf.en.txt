Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for shifting of the English Medium Self-Financed Primary (E 1st to 5th) School, Ashirvad Kala Mandir, Nagpur run Success Point School, Suyognagar, Nagpur to Sun Flag Society, Beltarodi, T.G. Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has proposed to shift to the building owned by the institution operators after being informed by the landlord about the demolition of the rented building. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school as the school is an English medium self-financing institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
