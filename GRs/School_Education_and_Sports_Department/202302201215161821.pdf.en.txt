Preamble: Teachers who have served the society selflessly and loyally and who have excelled in the field of education in the state will be awarded the Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award in accordance with the terms and conditions and criteria of the Government decision dated June 28, 2022, with the aim of promoting their work and honoring their merits.
The Krantijyoti Savitrimai Phule State Teacher Merit Award for the year 2021-22 has been announced as per the Government Decision dated 29 th December, 2022. The award ceremony will be held in Mumbai on the birth anniversary of Savitribai Phule on January 3, 2023. However, the Model Code of Conduct came into force with immediate effect with the Election Commission of India announcing the election schedule for two Graduates and three Teachers seats of the Maharashtra Legislative Council on December 29, 2022, and therefore, the award distribution programme of January 03, 2023, was postponed.
