Preamble- The State Sports Policy Issue No. 3.8 The scheme of honouring the sportspersons and their mentors who have brought laurels to the State by their outstanding performance at the international level is being implemented as per the decision of the Government in this regard. In the said Government Resolution, in Annexure-A, Rule No. The format of the award has been decided as per 03. The winners and runners-up of the competition will be awarded with cash prizes. Since there is no clarity in the names of these competitions, the Directorate is facing difficulties in scrutinizing the proposals received. Therefore, the matter of amending the names of the competitions mentioned in the said Government Decision was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:

Rule No.4 in Annexure A of the above decision of the Government proposes to add the following rules to the eligibility criteria:
(iv) In all the above competitions, if there is a team event, there must be teams from eight countries. Also, in individual sports, athletes from eight countries are required to participate in the respective sports category/ age group.
(v) Sports/ games covered in the Olympic Games, Commonwealth Games and Asian Games, as well as indigenous games like Kabaddi, Kho-Kho will be eligible for cash prizes in the above mentioned competitions.
(vi) Applications should be made to the Directorate within 15 days of receiving the medal in the above mentioned competition. Applications will only be accepted for cash prizes in the financial year in which the competition was held and until the following financial year.
