The Proposal:
Teachers who have done exceptional work for the education of women and girls will be awarded the' Thorat Social Reformer Savitribai Phule Adarsh Sthree Teacher Award" and' Rajya Shikshak Puraskar" will be given to teachers who have done excellent work in the field of education in the state. Teachers Day is celebrated every year on September 5 in the state to honour teachers. The estimated expenditure for the function was proposed by the Director of Education, (Secondary& Higher Secondary), Maharashtra State, Pune vide the aforesaid Reference No. 4 submitted to the government. The expenditure for the award ceremony is mentioned in the above Reference No. 5 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. However, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve a lump sum payment to meet the expenses of the programme.
Government's decision:
The State Awards to Primary and Secondary Teachers, Savitribai Phule Adarsh Sthree Teacher Award and State Award to Teachers for outstanding work in tribal areas have been announced for 2018-19. 181.50 lakh (Rupees One Crore Eighty One Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being sanctioned by the Government.
