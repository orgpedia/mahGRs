Introduction: Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji Vidya Vikas Mandal, Kolegaon, Osmanabad operated Shri Samarth International School, Osmanabad, Dist. G. Osmanabad English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary School, Sri Samarth Education and Social Foundation, Kot Galli, Osmanabad, Dist. The proposal received under the letter of reference to transfer the institution to G. Osmanabad was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Shri Samarth Education and Social Foundation, Kot Gali, Osmanabad, T.G. Osmanabad on the following terms and conditions:
(1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(2) As the said school is permanently unaided in English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management

The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if it is found that the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution prior to the change of management have been breached by the new institution.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur, shall examine all the matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, and inform the government about the action taken.
