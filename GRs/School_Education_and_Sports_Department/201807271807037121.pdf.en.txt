Introduction: Nath Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Pingali, T.G. Parbhani Managed Gokulnath Junior College (Arts Branch), Babhulgaon Peth, T.G. Stone, stone. The proposal received under reference letter for shifting of this unaided Junior College to Karegaon Road, T.G. Parbhani was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the dilapidation of the existing building of the above junior college, it is proposed to shift to a building with physical facilities and rented for 15 years. The transfer of the said Junior College to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions:
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the transferred Junior College shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure in writing the consent of the parents of the students of the junior college to the migration, as well as to accommodate the maximum number of students of the school at the place of migration.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) As per the orders issued from time to time by the Government, the pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities for the Junior College shall be provided. The Chairman/ Secretary of the Board will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in the case of a transferred Junior College.
(7) The Junior College shall function at the place of transfer on the same basis as the grant in aid at the place of origin.
(8) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible as per the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall take necessary further action regarding approval of shifting of the said Junior College and inform the Government about the action taken.
