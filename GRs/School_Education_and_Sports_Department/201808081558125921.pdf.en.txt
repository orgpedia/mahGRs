Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of Marathi medium unaided primary and upper primary schools of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Primary School, Bhimnagar, Bhavsinghpura, Nandanvan Colony, Aurangabad, operated by Lokhitwadi Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad, to Palak Haripriya Educational and Social Multipurpose Development Society, Cidco, Aurangabad, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the school where the management is changed for any reason.
(2) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time for the school, the school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) It shall be ensured that all the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the school agree to the transfer.
Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) Teachers/ non-teaching posts equivalent to the posts admissible as per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time in respect of schools.

It will be mandatory for the institution to keep the staff employed and not to employ more than the number of teachers/ non-teaching staff and it will be necessary to take action according to the government policy regarding filling up of vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(8) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(9) If any fresh complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2) and (3), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad shall examine all the matters and take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school including other necessary conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
