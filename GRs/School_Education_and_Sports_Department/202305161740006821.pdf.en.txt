Introduction: Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (60:40) Under this scheme, the State share (General) Account for payment of salaries to officers and staff of District Institutes of Education and Training (DIETs) in the State is 20% of the budgeted amount for the year 2023-24 for the quarter April to June, 2023 under 01 pay target under heading 2202I612. The Department of Finance has approved the release of an amount of Rs. 9,33,89, 600/-. As per the guidance given by the Department of Finance vide letter dated 6 th May 2022, the matter of issuance of Government Resolution as under was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Teacher Education is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (60:40). 9,33,89, 600/- (Rupees Nine Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Six Hundred only) is being approved for disbursement.
