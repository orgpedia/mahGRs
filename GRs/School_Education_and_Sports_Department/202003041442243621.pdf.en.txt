As per the circular dated 22 nd January, 2020, till the revised estimates for 2019-20 are finalised, the extent of fund disbursement for 2019-20 is being worked out and it has been agreed to allocate the funds on Budget Estimate Allocation and Control System (BEAMS). The provisions of the circular are as follows:
(i) The limit of fund disbursement for the revenue and capital account components of the budgeted fund has been prescribed at 80%. However, the distribution and expenditure of funds under the target of 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) will need to be pre-approved by the Finance Department. Please be aware that proposals for these matters will be submitted through the Department's Budget Branch.
(2) The limit on the disbursement of funds for programme expenditure shall be kept in abeyance till the revised estimates are finalised. This will be effective as of July 8, 2019. However, the proposals for the distribution of additional funds for the programme under which the expenditure matters have been taken into account by the department, should be submitted to the Finance Department through the Planning Department/ Social Justice and Special Assistance Department/ Tribal Development Department with concrete support and ensuring that the available funds are spent. Please be aware that proposals for these matters will be submitted through the Department's Budget Branch.
3. Before disbursing the Supplementary Demands Fund, it should be approved by the Planning Department/ Social Justice Department as well as the Finance Department as per the requirement. Please ensure that these cases are submitted in the prescribed format as well as complete proposals through the Budget Branch of the Department.
