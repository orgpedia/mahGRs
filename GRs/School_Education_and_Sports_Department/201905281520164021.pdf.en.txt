In order to provide quality sports facilities, technical training, improvement in the standard of sports, to enable sportspersons to perform at national and international level, under the State Sports Policy-2012, educational institutions run by private/ government and recognized by the concerned departments of the State Government, working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act, 1950 or the Societies Registration Act, 1860, will be provided with sports facilities and subsidy for sports activities. 1 has been approved by the government.
In the 2nd Khelo India Youth Games-2019 held in January, 2019 for U-17 and U-21 boys, the State of Maharashtra emerged as the joint winners with 288 medals. In line with such competitions, along with the sports skills of the youth of the state, sports promotion and health enhancement through sports has been promoted to a great extent.
Further, it has been decided at the Government level to provide only sports subsidy as mentioned in Form B under the Scheme of Financial Assistance for Construction of Sports Facilities for the year 2018-19.
As these aids are available to a large number of players, children of different groups will be motivated to play games, which will increase their inclination towards sports and it will affect their health. So that is the main objective.
The terms and conditions laid down in the Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2014 in this regard for the construction of sports facilities are for the creation of movable assets. The terms and conditions for creation of movable assets mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2014 for providing sports incentives were under consideration for the financial year 2018-19.
