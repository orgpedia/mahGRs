School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No: Masha Sth 3321/ PR No.401/ SM-1 dated 28th July, 2021.
Reference: Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar Division, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Letter dated 17.03.2023.
Preamble: The proposal received under the reference letter for shifting of Kranti Junior Teachers College, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar run Nandanvan Vidyamandir, Shantipura, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Marathi medium aided primary (class 1st to 7th) school, Nisarga Colony, Bhavsinghpura, G. Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school building is proposed to be shifted as it is dilapidated and dangerous.
Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
