The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 30th August, 2016 had decided to revise the grant-in-aid formula to the extent of 20% to be given to the recognized private primary and secondary schools which are permitted and declared eligible for grant-in-aid on' non-subsidy' and' permanent non-subsidy' basis. Accordingly, as per the Government Decision dated 19th September, 2016, a total grant of 20% has been sanctioned to 19,247 teaching and non-teaching staff of 1,628 schools and 2,452 batches, who were declared eligible for the grant before 14th June, 2016, subject to the prescribed terms and conditions.
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 17th April, 2018 has decided to grant 20% subsidy to the recognized private primary and secondary schools and class units, which have been approved on the basis of" permanent non-subsidy" and declared eligible for subsidy under the Government Decision dated 1st and 2nd July, 2016, until further orders of the Government.
