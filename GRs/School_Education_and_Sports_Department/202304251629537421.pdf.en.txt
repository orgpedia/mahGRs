Introduction: Qari Maulana Mohammad Ayub Azmi Education Society Shahada Ta. Shahada G. Nandurbar Managed Kari Maulana Private Urdu Primary School Shahada Ta. Shahada G. Nandurbar, Urdu Medium Partially Aided Primary (Class 1st to 4th) School, Nishat Welfare and Education Society, Kukdel, Dist.
The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of management to Shahada, G. Nandurbar was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The management of these schools is Nishat Welfare and Education Society, Kukdel, Ta. The Government have decided to approve the transfer of Shahada to Nandurbar on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated, or
