In respect of adjustment in other institutions vide Reference No. 09 A total of 212 teachers who were verified by the Director's Office were informed about the adjustment. The reference No. As per the proposal received by the Government vide letters 7& 9, the Government was considering the matter of adjustment of total 283 (211+72) eligible teachers who were working in the incremental posts in the Higher Secondary/ Junior Colleges in the State during the period 2003-04 to 2018-19.
Government Decision: Adjustment of total 283 eligible teachers included in Annexure' A' and Annexure' B' working in higher secondary/ junior colleges of the State in incremental posts from 2003-04 to 2018-19 is being approved under the following conditions.
(i) As per the prevailing policy/ rules of adjustment and Government decisions, adjustment of teachers working in the incremental posts in the list attached herewith shall be made by the concerned Divisional Deputy Director in the same department, if it is not possible in the same institution first, as per the availability of the post. If this is not possible, the Director (Secondary and Higher Secondary) should take action to make adjustments outside the department in other institutions.
(ii) In making such adjustments, the reservation of backward classes should be made in accordance with the policy and point list.
(ii) Since the said teachers were earlier working in extended posts, they should be exempted from the Shiksha Sevak Scheme and their salary after adjustment should be fixed in the regular pay scale taking into account their service from the date of their appointment. However, no salary arrears or retirement salary and other benefits will be admissible to the said teachers for the service rendered by them in the earlier increment.
(iv) The norms of the sacrosanct system shall not be applicable to the teachers working in the incremental posts in the minority higher secondary/ junior colleges recognized under the said Government decision. However, the norms of the sacred system will continue to apply to other teachers.
2.This Government decision is being issued with the approval of the Finance Department of Maharashtra.
