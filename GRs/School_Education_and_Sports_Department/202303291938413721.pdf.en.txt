In order to support various youth welfare activities to empower the youth of the State, in view of the need to provide incentives and financial assistance to the youth to effectively carry out youth development activities in the State and to enhance bright future opportunities for the budding youth of the State, the Government has approved the establishment of the State Youth Development Fund. For the year 2022-23, Rs. The matter of approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 14.00 lakhs (Rupees Fourteen Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The State Youth Development Fund has been allocated Rs.1 crore for the financial year 2022-23 to support various youth welfare activities to empower the youth of the State, to provide incentives and financial assistance to the youth to effectively implement youth development activities in the State and to enhance the bright future opportunities for the budding youth of the State. It is hereby approved to disburse the amount of Rs. 14.00 lakhs (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs only) and place the same under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Pune.
The expenditure for the said activities shall be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No: E-3, 2204 Sports and Youth Services-103, (01) (09) Subsidiary Grants (Programmes) to State Youth Development Fund (2204 5671) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)." The grant should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned.
