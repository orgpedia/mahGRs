Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) The consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff presently working in the school for the transfer should be confirmed in writing. Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) Shri Vitthal Devasthan Trust, Chem, Ta. All the members of Karmala, G. Solapur have agreed to transfer Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(6) Teachers/ non-teaching posts equivalent to the posts admissible as per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time in respect of schools.
