Government circular:
In the wake of COVID-19 outbreak, schools in rural and urban areas of the state have started in offline mode from 04/10/2021. Schools for classes 1 to 12 in the state have been declared holidays for various religious festivals/ celebrations.
In the academic year 2021-22, schools in the state are being declared a holiday from October 28, 2021 to November 10, 2021 in view of the Diwali festival. During this period, online and offline teaching-learning activities will remain closed.
