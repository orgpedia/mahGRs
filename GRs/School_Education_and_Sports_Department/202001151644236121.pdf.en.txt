The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Pune has cancelled the recruitment of non-official members for various posts in various cadres.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No:- SSC-2019/ S.No.222/ SD-2
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: January 15, 2020
Reference:- 1. Government Resolution No. SSC-2019/ No.222/ SD-2, D. The September 13, 2019
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 5 (1) (c) and section 6 (1) (i) of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Pune, and all the Divisional Boards, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 5 (1) (c) and section 6 (1) (i) of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, 1965, as amended by the said Government, the following appointments have been made: He was nominated for a term of 4 years.
However, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 8 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Act, 1965, the appointment of non-official members to the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Pune and all Divisional Boards is hereby cancelled. A list of members who have been suspended is attached.
