Preamble: The Government of Maharashtra has decided to set up a Marathi medium primary and upper primary school in Matoshree Jijau Primary School, Beed Bypass, Aurangabad, under the aegis of Godavari Multipurpose and Charitable Trust, Hadko, Aurangabad. 212, behind Harsiddidevi Temple, Harsul, District Aurangabad, the proposal received under the letter was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The relocation is proposed due to the dilapidation of the school building and the expiry of the lease agreement. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more than that number of teachers/ non-teaching staff shall continue to be employed.
It will be mandatory for the organization to take action as per the government policy to fill the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
