Introduction: Shri. M.R. School, Burud Galli, Shaniwar Peth, Kolhapur is a Marathi medium aided primary school (1st to 7th grade) located in Kasba Beed, Taluka. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Karveer, G. Kolhapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Since the above primary school building is dilapidated and dangerous, relocation is proposed. Therefore, the relocation of the said primary school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
