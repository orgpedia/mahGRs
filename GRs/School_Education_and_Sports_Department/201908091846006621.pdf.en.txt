the prelude
The Government is planning to give cash awards to the medal winners of Olympic, Commonwealth and Asian Games. Accordingly, the government was considering to honor the medal winning athletes of Maharashtra and their mentors with cash awards for their outstanding performance at the international level.
Government's decision:
Under the Sports Policy of Maharashtra, 2012, the total amount shown in front of their names as cash awards to the medal winners and their mentors from the state of Maharashtra for their outstanding performance at the international level as well as for bringing glory to the state is Rs. 92.875 lakh and the expenditure on organizing Rs. 4.00 lakh in total. 96.875 lakhs (Rupees Eighty Seven Thousand Five Hundred only) is being approved by the Government:
