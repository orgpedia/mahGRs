Read paragraph 2 as follows:
(1) After giving both the parties a reasonable opportunity of being heard, the Grievance Redressal Committee shall, within a period of six months from the date of filing of the complaint, hear the complaint in accordance with law and dispose of the same with due process. In giving such a judgment, the policy of the government, relevant acts/ notifications, government decisions/ circulars/ orders, judicial decisions related to the subject matter should be taken into consideration.

(2) The Chairman of the concerned Committee shall invite the Education Officer/ Inspector of Education of other districts as members in the Committee for Primary and Secondary Schools. An independent education officer/ education inspector can be called for each hearing.
(3) If no other member of the Committee is present at the hearing, the Chairman of the Committee shall have the right to hold the hearing and to take a decision.
(4) The proceedings of the Committee shall be quasi-judicial.
(5) The Committee shall commence its proceedings as soon as possible.
