Reference No. 1 The Commissioner (Education) has been authorized to permanently close the 10 Government Practice Pathshalas, which have been provided to the students of Government Teachers Schools in the state to take practice lessons, and to adjust the teachers and non-teaching staff in these Practice Pathshalas to the equivalent vacant posts.
The Director, State Council of Educational Research and Training, Maharashtra, Pune vide No. 2 In order to protect the services of the employees of Government Practice School vide letter dated 15th September, 2020, in the Government Decision No. 3 has been requested to issue a corrigendum by amending it. In view of the above request, Paragraph No. 3 of the Government Decision dated 15.9.2020 is being amended.
3. Department of School Education and Sports, vide Government Resolution dated 15.9.2020, vide Para No. 3 should be read as follows:
3. Commissioner (Education) is being authorised to make adjustment of teachers and non-teaching staff in these practice schools to equivalent vacancies.
Instead of
3. In view of the non-availability of government primary schools in the state at present and to protect the services of teachers/ non-teaching staff in these schools, the Government has decided to adjust them to the vacant equivalent posts in the local bodies in compliance with all the existing laws and rules. Commissioner (Education) is being authorized.
