Government's decision:
School Nutrition Scheme at the level of Directorate of Elementary Education
Director of Education proposes to declare State Coordinating Officer as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the separate class constituted under the Government Decision dated 06/03/2017
In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Principal, the matter of declaring the State Coordinating Officer, School Nutrition Cell, Directorate of Primary Education, Pune as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
The State Coordinating Officer, School Nutrition Cell, Directorate of Elementary Education, Pune has been appointed as the Nodal Officer under Rule No. 1 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. 15 (3) is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." Also, the" State Coordination Officer" is being authorized to draw the funds of the School Nutrition Scheme from the treasury and distribute them to the districts. The order will be limited to financial matters.
