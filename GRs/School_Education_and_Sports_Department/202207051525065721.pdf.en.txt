Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of Urdu Medium Unaided Primary School (Class 1st to 7th), Gevrai, G. Beed (Minority Institution) operated Urdu Primary School, Hinanagar, Chikalthana, Aurangabad to Gurukul Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad, a Minority Institution, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Gurukul Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has been changed shall not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) As the school is on non-subsidized basis with minority status, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(3) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school where the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated, or
