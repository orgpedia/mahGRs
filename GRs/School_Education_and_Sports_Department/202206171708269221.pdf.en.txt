Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Hockey Tournament 2021-22, to be held at Major Dhyan Chand Hockey Stadium, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh from 21st June to 30th June, 2022, will be held at Mahendra Stadium, Churchgate, Mumbai. The selection test was conducted on 11th June, 2022. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,07,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Seven Thousand only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned for participation in the tournament should relieve the players/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 17th to 19th June, 2022 and for the tournament from 21st to 30th June, 2022 with effect from 16th June, 2022 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.
