The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the college, Washim (Class 11th and 12th Jointly Aided and Science Unaided), to Kashibai Maganlalji Bagadia Multi-Purpose Society, Risod, G. Washim, was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has decided to approve the transfer to WASHIM on the following terms and conditions: (1) Annexure-I of the updated (existing) Board of Trustees of the transferring organization (Janata Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Barshitakali, G. Akola) should be taken up. Also, if there is a change in the trustees, an affidavit of consent should be taken to approve the transfer of new directors.
(2) An affidavit signed by all the members of both the organizations along with their photographs should be taken that the transfer is not taking place with immovable property.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.

(7) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
After the fulfillment of the above condition number (1) and (2), the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Amravati Division, Amravati, should take further action on the change of management of the school within 15 days after examining all the aspects and including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms/ conditions and inform the government about it.
