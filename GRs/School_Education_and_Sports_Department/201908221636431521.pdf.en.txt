The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of Urdu medium aided primary and upper primary school, Nasik, to Ansar Colony, Malegaon, Dist.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted from a rented building to a spacious building owned by the institution. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more than that number of teaching/ non-teaching staff shall continue to be employed.
