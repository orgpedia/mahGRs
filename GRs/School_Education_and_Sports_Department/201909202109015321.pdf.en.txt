The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Aurangabad was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The above school building is proposed to be shifted as it is dilapidated and dangerous.
The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) School transfer fee of Rs. 10,000/- should be remitted to the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Aurangabad.
(2) It shall be ensured that educational loss to the existing students does not occur due to relocation of the said school.
(3) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(5) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(6) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
