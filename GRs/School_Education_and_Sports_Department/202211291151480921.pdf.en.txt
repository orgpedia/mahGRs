Preamble: The proposal for approval of Amravati Municipal Corporation, Amravati to start secondary classes in 8 schools and higher secondary classes in 1 school in the periphery was approved by the Director of Education (Secondary and Higher Secondary), Pune in Reference No. (2) submitted to the Government. The matter of granting permission to these 9 schools to start secondary/ higher secondary classes was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012 (01 of 2013), the Government of Amravati Municipal Corporation, Amravati is permitting the following 8 primary schools in the area of Amravati to be upgraded to the status of Higher Secondary Class on Self-Financed basis by adding 1 Middle and 1 Middle School from the academic year 2022-23 subject to the following terms and conditions:

Terms and Conditions:. Since these schools have been permitted to be upgraded to Secondary/ Higher Secondary level on self-financing basis, no financial assistance/ grant, either direct or indirect, shall be provided by the State Government for any expenditure incurred in respect of the said school.
It will be mandatory to comply with the prevailing policy and norms of the government regarding secondary/ higher secondary schools and to provide all physical and educational facilities in the school.
It shall be mandatory to comply with all the standards, terms and conditions prescribed under the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012 and the directions issued by the Government under this Act or the standards or terms and conditions specified by the Government from time to time.
