Introduction: Maharashtra Mahila Udyam Trust, Pune runs Pratibha Prathmik Vidyamandir and Pratibha Madhyamik Vidyalaya, Jalgaon is a Marathi medium aided primary and secondary school (1st to 7th Std. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the management of the schools from 5th to 10th standard to the Debt Relief Trust, Jalgaon was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of management of these schools to the Debt Relief Trust, Jalgaon on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum strength of the schools whose management has been changed shall not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in respect of schools where management changes have taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
