Introduction: The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 is being implemented. Every child has the right to quality education. To enhance the quality of education of students, the learning environment in the classroom and in the school premises should be vibrant, joyful and constantly active. Through hands-on activities, the curriculum objectives of the students are being achieved through the teaching material in a practical manner, thereby increasing the acquisition level of the students. Also, the school should have adequate facilities. Accordingly, the government has approved the implementation of the following scheme through the decision of the government in the context of providing infrastructure in the schools to create a vibrant and happy educational environment in the Zilla Parishad schools of the state and providing educational materials and technology to increase the quality of education.

In order to implement the above schemes on priority basis, the Planning Department has approved to earmark at least 5% of the total outlay of the District Planning Committee (except Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban districts) for the schemes related to the School Education Department (excluding Sports Scheme). It was under the consideration of the Government to issue all inclusive guidelines for effective implementation of the said schemes.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has decided to restructure the schemes related to the School Education Department under the District Annual (Sarva Sadharan) Plan (DPC) and reserve at least 5% of the funds for the same. All the inclusive guidelines regarding the activities to be carried out at the school level through the above schemes and their modalities will be as follows.
1) Special repair of buildings and classrooms of primary/ secondary schools in the area of Zila Parishads/ Sanitary repair.
1.1.Repairing or re-construction of school building/ classrooms due to natural calamities or expiry.
1.2) Repair/ construction of separate toilets for boys/ girls as per the provisions of Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009.
2) Construction of primary/ secondary school buildings and classrooms/ construction of toilets/ construction of ramps and toilets for differently abled students/ construction of drinking water facility/ school sanitation/ library/ educational wall/ construction of separate classrooms/ school playground/ patio facilities for girl students in ZP area/ construction of retaining wall for primary/ secondary schools in ZP area.
2.1. Construction of ramps and separate toilets for differently abled students in schools.
(ii) Construction of hand wash stations for providing drinking water facility to school children or providing water purifiers.
2.3. To make arrangements for regular cleaning of schools and school premises by providing manpower through a tri-service organization.

5.1 The above Committee shall prepare proposals for the necessary aspects of the above schemes taking into account the resources/ facilities available in the Zilla Parishad schools and the necessary resources/ facilities and submit them to the District Planning Committee.
5.2 The said Committee shall have the power to incorporate the necessary nutritive activities for the creation and quality enhancement of the academic environment in addition to the matters specified as above for the effective implementation of the schemes.
The plan prepared by the above committee will be submitted to the District Planning Committee and once it is approved, it will be implemented as per the prevailing procedure.
