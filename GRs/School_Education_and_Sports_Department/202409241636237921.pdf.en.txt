All educational institutions in the state shall submit to the Government proposals for school affiliation / upgradation / new class affiliation / No Objection Certificate to various board schools / fixation of grant phase / sanction of additional batches / change of medium etc. along with the prescribed checklist along with the accompanying Annexure-A. Only proposals received in the prescribed manner along with this "Annexure-A" shall be suitably dealt with by the relevant Executive.
