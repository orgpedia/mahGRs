Introduction: Jeevan Shiksha magazine is published by the Media Division of Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran). Government's policy and objectives
Jeevan Shiksha magazine is called the mouthpiece of the government's school department as it plays the role of a guiding mediator between the work program and the expected school level activities.
In May 1861, the first issue of the magazine was published as Pune Pathshala Patrakar. This magazine has a glorious tradition of about 160 years and started publishing in October 1956 under the name of Life Education. The life of Mahatma Gandhi
The name is the background of the project. The theme of this magazine is Education for Life.
Due to the increased expenditure on printing, printing and paper of the Jeevan Shiksha magazine, the matter of increasing the retail price of the issue as well as the annual subscription of the issue was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The cost of printing, printing and paper etc. for Jeevan Shiksha magazine considering the increased prices due to inflation, the retail selling price of one digit is Rs. 20/- to Rs. The proposal submitted by Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (Vidya Adhikaran), Pune to the government regarding the increase from Rs 200/- to Rs 300/- is being approved by the government.
