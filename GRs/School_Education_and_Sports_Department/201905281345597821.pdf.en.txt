Bhiwapur, G.Nagpur, a Marathi medium unaided higher primary and secondary school, Mauje Nand, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Bhiwapur, G.Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school is dilapidated, dangerous and damaged, the school is being shifted to a rented building. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to adjust all the students of the school at the place of migration and to ensure that there is no objection/ objection to the migration of the school and the recognition of classes 6th and 7th of the school.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be necessary to ensure that the construction of the building at the place of relocation is completed and all necessary physical facilities are available there. Further, as per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided at the place of relocation. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.

(6) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.

Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(9) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (4), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall take necessary action within six weeks regarding approval of the relocation of the said school and shall inform the Government about the action taken.
