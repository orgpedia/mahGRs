Government Decision No. Anuvyee 4921/ (32/21)/ Budget 2. During the financial year 2021-2022, the aforesaid funds/ grants made available by the Finance Department on the BEAMS system are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) The Finance Department shall disburse the house building advance within the minimum amount permissible to the concerned, taking into account the provisions of the Appendix-26 of the Bombay Finance Act, 1959 and the prescribed instructions/ conditions contained in the Government Decision/ Government Circular issued from time to time, subject to the sanction of advance to the Government employees. The concerned controller should ensure that there is no lapse in this.
Applicants having more than two children on or after 01.05.2001 (except second time conjoined children) will not be able to avail this benefit.
(3) The expenditure shall not be incurred for any purpose other than the purpose for which the fund/ grant is sanctioned. Also, you can't switch to other titles.
4) The progress report of the expenditure should be submitted to the government by the 10th of every month by the controlling authorities as per the monthly fund statement.
5) The rules mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget rule book should be followed while disbursing the subsidy. Further, the Finance Department shall pay the subsidy after obtaining the approval of the competent authority in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I as provided under Government Decision No. V.P.-2013/ PR. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015.
(6) It shall be ensured that action is taken in accordance with the suggestions given by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries/ Cabinet as well as the Finance Department, where necessary, in respect of allocation of funds on salary in accordance with the creation of posts.
7) When providing grants to private NGOs, the grant should be paid with the approval of the competent authority in accordance with the Financial Powers Rule Book 1978, Part-1, Sub-Division I, serial number 27, rule 149.
(8) In the case of the distribution of provisions for which an administrative sanction order is required, the grant shall be disbursed after the order is issued with the approval of the competent authority as provided under Part-I, Sub-Division-III, Schedule-IV, Rule 27 (2).
(9) Subsidies for the Zilla Parishads shall not be disbursed without their grants being determined.
10) The approval of the Finance Department is required in respect of the disbursement of funds for the schemes provided under the new head in accordance with the new expenditure.
11) Before sanctioning the grants-in-aid to various Government/ Administrative Institutions and Aided Institutions, the Department should obtain utilization certificates of the expenditure of the grants-in-aid disbursed earlier. Further grants should not be disbursed unless the utilization of the earlier grant is completed. Funds drawn from Treasury Officers, Auxiliary Grants

Government Decision No.4921/ (32/21)/ Payments should not be passed if a copy of the bank account statement of the previous month is not attached with the payment submitted for budgeting.
12) The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be transferred to the bank account of the beneficiaries through ECS. Under no circumstances should the salary subsidy amount be disbursed in lump sum and offline mode.
13) Before disbursing grants to local self-government institutions and corporations, the amount coming to the state government from these institutions should be reviewed and the remaining grants should be disbursed only after recovering those amounts. Also, detailed information should be collected about the utilization of the grants distributed by the government to these institutions and where the unutilized amount is kept. If the unaccounted money is kept in the bank, then its bank account statement should be submitted to the government. If the funds made available for expenditure on a programme are unutilized, the funds should not be released for the same purpose in the current year without the approval of the Finance Department.
14. While drawing payments to beneficiaries under the individual beneficiary programme, grants to individual beneficiaries shall not be drawn unless the list of beneficiaries is fixed and linked to the payment with proper details. The programmes of individual beneficiaries should be linked with Aadhaar number and the subsidy should be disbursed through Electronic Clearance System (ECS).
The payment of scholarship/ tuition fee reimbursement, individual beneficiaries as well as subsidy etc. should be made through Electronic Clearance System (ECS) only. All such details should be linked to Aadhaar.
16) Work-wise funds are required to be budgeted after obtaining administrative approval/ technical approval in respect of the construction proposal as well as approval of the Committee of Secretaries, where necessary. Once the availability of funds for new works is ensured, the work should be started by issuing tender notices and commissioning orders, and the funds should be distributed as needed. The old works should be reviewed and completed on priority and the funds should not be provided to the Public Works Department in the form of deposit construction.
17) The concerned department should ensure the receipt of funds from the Central Government in respect of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme from the Finance Branch of the Finance Department (no need to refer the funds to the Expenditure/ Budget Branch for this) and thereafter, if the funds have been received, the administrative department should disburse the balance share of the State Government at their level. There is no need to refer the fee separately to the Finance Department for concurrence. However, if the funds of the Central Government for the previous year are to be spent in the current year, it will be mandatory to obtain the consent of the Planning, Tribal Development Department and Social Justice and Special Assistance Department as well as the Finance Department, as the case may be.
