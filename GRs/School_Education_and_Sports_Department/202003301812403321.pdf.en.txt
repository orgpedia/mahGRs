Relaxation in school fee collection during the lockdown period due to Corona virus.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Circular No: SRIKH- 2020/ PR No.33/ SM.6,
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
The date: March 30, 2020.
circular
The lockdown has been imposed in the country to prevent the spread of coronavirus. The state government has imposed a lockdown in the entire state till April 14, 2020, and has advised all the citizens to stay indoors and ensure that there is no transmission of COVID-19 corona.
Many parents have requested the state government to extend the period of depositing school fees for the current year as well as for the upcoming years i.e. 2019-2020 and 2020-2021. Considering the current situation of the Corona epidemic in the state, the ban imposed on the entire movement, the availability of money felt by the citizens, all the schools of all boards and all the managements are requested to show empathy while collecting the school fees for the current year and the upcoming year from the students and parents. There should be no compulsion to pay this fee during the lockdown period. After the end of the lockdown period
Instructions should be given to the concerned regarding the payment of school fees.
