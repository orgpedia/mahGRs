Government Corrigendum: -
The Government decision in the said context is in Paragraph No. Special teachers under the offices of the "Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai and Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai," which are not Zila Parishads, at 05 shall be accommodated at the vacant centre in the Taluka under the Zila Parishad of the adjoining Raigad District and their services shall be deployed at the centre where they are presently functioning. For this, the concerned responsible officers should take necessary action in coordination with the CEO of Raigad Zilla Parishad. Instead, "Adjustment of Special Teachers under the offices of the Deputy Director of Zonal Education, Mumbai and Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai, which are not Zila Parishads, as per their preference, shall be made at the vacant centre in the Taluk under Zila Parishads of Thane, Palghar and Raigad districts and their services shall be deployed at the centre where they are presently functioning." For this, the concerned responsible officers should take necessary action in coordination with the CEOs of Thane, Palghar and Raigad Zila Parishads. Such adjustment proceedings should be made on the basis of the following conditions.
1) Preferences should be taken for the above mentioned three districts by the candidates to be adjusted as per their preference.
2) First preference should be given to the candidate who is the best in service based on the date of appointment of the said candidates in the scheme for adjustment.
3) If the number of posts available is less than the number of candidates to be accommodated in a district after giving preference, their second and third preference, as the case may be, should be considered.
