Introduction: Jivan Jyoti Rural Education and Social Reform Committee, Amravati (Minority Institution) runs K.G.N. Hindi Primary School, Amravati, a Hindi (Semi English) medium non-aided primary school (Class 1st to 4th), Plot No. The proposal received under the reference letter for shifting to 62, 63 and 68, Sahu Nagar, Akkalkot, Old City, Akola was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the dilapidated and dangerous building of the above school, the plot of the school No. It is proposed to be shifted to 62, 63 and 68, Sahu Nagar, Akkalkot, Old City, Akola. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) As the school is on non-aided basis, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
