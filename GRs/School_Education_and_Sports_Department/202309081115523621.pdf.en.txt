Preamble: As per the Department of Finance, Government Resolution, dated 31st March, 2023, vide Reference No. 2, on and after 01.11.2005, teachers/ non-teaching staff in 100% aided posts in Zilla Parishads and recognized private 100% aided primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers schools in the State who are members of the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme, (a) Family Pension and Death Gratuity to the family of the teacher/ non-teaching staff in case of death in service, (b) Sickness Pension and Retirement Gratuity to the teacher/ non-teaching staff who has become sick and retired. Accordingly, the family pension/ sickness pension will be applicable to the said family pensioner and sickness pensioner as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982. Retirement allowance has also been made applicable to teachers/ non-teaching staff retiring from service in 100% aided schools.
The Department of Finance vide Circular No. 3 has laid down the modalities with respect to the benefits which are permissible as per the Government decision at Reference No.1. The above decision shall be applicable to the employees of Zilla Parishads, recognized and aided non-government primary and secondary schools, non-agriculture universities and affiliated recognized and aided non-government colleges and agricultural universities and similar aided institutions, with appropriate modifications. However, separate orders in this regard have been issued by the concerned administrative departments.
