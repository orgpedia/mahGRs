Government circular:
As it is not possible to start schools in the state in the situation arising out of the outbreak of COVID-19 in the state, the concerned District Collector, Municipal Commissioner and the concerned School Management Committee have been empowered to take a decision regarding the opening of schools in accordance with the local conditions by starting the academic year from June 15, 2020. Also, as per the instructions of the government circular dated July 22, 2020, online education has been started for the students of classes pre-primary to 12.
According to Rule 52.2 of the Secondary School Code, the total number of holidays in the academic year shall not exceed 76 days, and the total number of working days shall be 230 days.
For classes 1 to 5, the minimum number of working days for school teachers in an academic year should be 200 and for classes 6 to 8, the minimum number of working days for school teachers in an academic year should be 220. Schools will remain closed from November 12 to November 16 on account of Diwali. During this period, online classes will remain suspended.
