Regulation of transfers and discharge of official duties of Government of Maharashtra
The Prevention of Delay in Demolition Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. As per Section 6 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the competent authority for transfer has been declared. Provided further that the competent authority transferring under proviso 2 to this section may, by general or special order, delegate its powers under this section to any of its subordinate authorities. Accordingly, the decision to transfer the powers of various cadre officers in Group-A and Group-B of Maharashtra Education Service has been taken by the Government Decision dated 30th May, 2015.

The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. Also, the concerned should note that if any form of application is submitted to cancel the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
