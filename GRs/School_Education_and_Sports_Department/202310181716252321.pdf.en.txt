The proposal: self. Ganesh Dhatrak Education Society, Nashik Road operates Angel English Medium School, Wadi Varhe, Ta. Igatpuri, G. Nasik is an English medium self-financed upper primary (1st to 8th grade) school run by Pragati Multi-purpose Education Society, Hatheed, T. The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of management to Chopra, G. Jalgaon was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The management of this school is the Multi-Purpose Educational Institute, HATED, Tt. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Chopra, G.Jalgaon on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
(3) As the school is an English medium self-financed school, the entire responsibility of the working teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be on the institution receiving the transfer.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(5) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(6) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
