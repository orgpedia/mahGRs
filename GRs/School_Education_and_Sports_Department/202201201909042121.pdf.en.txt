Government Circular No: 2021/ PR No.213 PART-1/ SD-6 2. In the Circular dated 07.07.2021 of this Department, a Committee under the Chairmanship of Sarpanch has been constituted to start schools in rural areas. So the. As per the circular dated August 10, 2021, committees have been formed under the chairmanship of the Commissioner for Municipal Corporation areas and the Collector for the opening of schools in municipalities/ nagar panchayats/ rural areas.
2.1 The Committee should consider the following aspects before starting the school.
(i) The prevalence of COVID in the respective town/ village should have been reduced before the opening of the school.
(ii) Vaccination of all teachers and non-teaching staff (both doses) must be completed.
The district administration should make a plan in this regard. The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad/ Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Principal, Municipal Council and Education Officer should follow up with the District Collector and make efforts to ensure 100% vaccination.
(ii) Parents of students should not be allowed to enter the school premises to avoid crowding.
(iv) Strict adherence to all COVID related norms. There should be a safe distance between two students. Wash hands with soap regularly, use masks, and if there are any symptoms, students should be sent to the medical officer or the nearest health center.
(v) If the student is found to be infected with corona, the school should be closed immediately and the class and teachers of corona-infected students should be quarantined. Also, the school principal should conduct disinfection and quarantine of the students and start medical treatment on the advice of the medical officer and the students who came in close contact with the corona infected students should also be tested.
2.2. Children should be called to school in a staggered manner while starting the school, e.g. 50:50 attendance on the day of shifting/ morning-afternoon/ morning shift, priority for certain core subjects etc.
2.3) The teachers of the school concerned should preferably stay in the same town/ village or they should be careful not to use public transport as much as possible.
2.4) All the above matters should be reviewed by the Municipal Commissioner in urban areas and in other areas by the Collector, Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad/ Principal, Municipal Council along with the Education Officer, Health Officer and where necessary, the concerned.
The guidelines issued vide Government Circular dated 7th July, 2021, dated 10th August, 2021 along with the additional guidelines in Circular dated 24th September, 2021 and Circular dated 29th November, 2021 have been incorporated in Annexure-A and Annexure-B attached for opening of schools as above, with necessary modifications. Be sure to follow these instructions carefully.
