Preamble: Saba Minorities Educational and Welfare Society, Chhaya Nagar, Amravati operates Paramveer Chakra Shaheed Abdul Hamid Higher Primary School, Bhosa Road, Yavatmal, Urdu medium aided primary school (1st to 7th standard), Ward No. The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of 25, Gulista Nagar, Yasmin Nagar, Phase Colony, Usman Nagar, T.G. Amravati was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As the rented building of the above school is dilapidated and inadequate, Ward No. 25, Gulista Nagar, Yasmin Nagar, Phase Colony, Osman Nagar, T.G. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
