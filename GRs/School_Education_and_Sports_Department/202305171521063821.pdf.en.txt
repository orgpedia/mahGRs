The Proposal:
In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 27.03.2017, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has appointed the following candidates as Senior Advocate in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre for a period of two years. Mr. Rahul Barve gave recommendations and appointments of some of the officers who have been given this appointment. Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai in Original Application No. 1209/2016 was filed. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 22.06.2021 of the General Administration Department, the original Application No. The period of probation of the officers who are respondents in 1209/2016 is the same as that of the original application No. 1209/2016 has been extended till the final decision.
