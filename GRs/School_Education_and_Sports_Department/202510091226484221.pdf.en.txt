The Proposal:
Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Education Board, Mumbai-run National High School Ghatkopar, Mumbai or Marathi
Medium Aided Secondary (e.g. Devgiri Pratishthan of this school (5th to 10th) Shahunagar Beed, T.G. Beed
The proposal received under the memorandum regarding transfer of management to the institute was under consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
To transfer the management of the school to Devagiri Pratishthan Shahunagar Beed, T.G. Beed.
The Government has decided to grant approval on the following terms and conditions: -
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) Changes in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission / approval in case of a school where management has changed.
Care should be taken not to do so.
(3) As may be prescribed by the Government from time to time in respect of schools, teachers / non-teaching staff, salary / non-salary grants, students, etc.
The new body will be obliged to comply with the acts, rules, orders, etc. to be made.
(4) These Management Changes, Government Decisions, Department of School Education and Sports, No: MASHAH 2011 / (210/11) MASHI-
3, dated 17th February, 2012 shall come into force in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed.
(5) Received fresh complaints from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding this change of management.
In the event or if a breach of the terms and conditions regarding a change of management is noticed, or the management
In the event of a breach by the new institution of the service allowance of the teaching / non-teaching staff working in the pre-change institution,
The government will have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Zonal Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai has examined all the aspects and in addition to the above terms / conditions.
Further action on the change of management of the school should be taken within 15 days, including the necessary conditions.
