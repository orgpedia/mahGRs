The modalities for inclusion of teachers/ non-teaching staff of primary, secondary, higher secondary and teachers' schools in 100% aided posts of Zilla Parishad and 100% aided posts of recognized private schools appointed on or after 1st November, 2005 in the National Pension Scheme (NPS) have been prescribed under the decision of the Government in this regard. The dates of implementation of the procedures prescribed under the above Government Decision were changed by reference No. 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 Corrigendum.
Due to technical difficulties in this regard, as well as delays in implementation due to the lockdown imposed to prevent the spread of the corona virus, it will take some time to complete this process.
However, in the Government Corrigendum No. 6, where the date is mentioned as" 31/12/2020," the date should be read as" 28/02/2021" instead and where the date is mentioned as" 01/01/2021," the date should be read as" 01/03/2021."
