the prelude
In order to encourage various sports by creating a sports environment in the state and to ensure that the players who are showing their skills at the international, national and state level get maximum opportunities to play in the competition and to prepare the players for the national sports competition, it has been decided to jointly organize and sanction funds for the Maharashtra State Olympic Games at Shivchhatrapati Sports Complex, Mahlunge-Balewadi, Pune in the year 2022. The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services has suggested some changes in this government decision. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 27.05.2022 is being superseded and revised orders are now being issued as under:
Government's decision
With a view to encourage various sports by creating a sporting environment in the state and to ensure that the players who are showing their skills at the international, national and state level get maximum opportunities to play in the competition and to prepare the players for the national sports competition, the Government of Maharashtra and the Maharashtra Olympic Association have jointly decided to organize the" Maharashtra State Olympic Games" in the state. The estimated cost of Rs. 19,07,94, 600/- is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
1) The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services and the Chief Event Organizing Committee should take care of the expenditure in 2022 as compared to 2014 in terms of accommodation and food.
(2) The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services and the Chief Event Organizing Committee shall be responsible for approving the purchase of sports materials and other ancillary items in the prescribed manner and ensuring that the expenditure is incurred in the prescribed manner.

3) The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services and the Chief Tournament Organizing Committee should ensure that the expenditure of Rs. 1.80 crore shown in the advertisement, banner, poster and beautification of the tournament is more and keep it within the limit.
4) The Commissioner Sports and Youth Services and the Chief Event Organizing Committee will be responsible for the expenditure in the prescribed manner as per rules.
5) The details of the cost of the competition must be submitted to the government within 1 month of the competition and the remaining amount after the competition must be dedicated to the government. This responsibility will be of the Chief Tournament Organizing Committee and the Commissioner Sports and Youth Services.
The item-wise estimated cost of the competition is given in Annexure-A. The Organising Committee of the main event will be required to decide the format, time and dates of the event after taking into consideration the availability of the sports complex and other factors.
3.1 The Maharashtra State Olympic Games, 2022 will be held at the Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex, Mahlunge Balewadi, Pune and at various venues in the districts of Pune, Mumbai City, Jalgaon, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur. The competition will comprise of a total of 32 games as mentioned in Annexure-B. The Organising Committee of the main event shall have the power to cancel any of the games and to increase or decrease the number of players/ mentors/ managers/ umpires as may be required.
3.2. Arranging food for the required manpower (Police, Health, Fire, Sports Department, unskilled workers, various committee members) for this competition.
3.3 Order regarding the formation of a committee for the well-planned and successful organization of the Maharashtra State Olympic Games. As per the Government decision dated 01.06.2022.
Financial Transactions of the" Maharashtra State Olympic Games" Competition:
The Controlling Officer for the funds received from the Government for the Maharashtra State Olympic Games will be the Accountant, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State.
Also, the relevant funds should be withdrawn and transferred to the bank account of the main tournament organizing committee. The financial transaction is being approved by the Joint Signatory of the Member Secretary of the Main Event Organizing Committee (Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services Headquarters, Pune) and the Secretary General, Maharashtra Olympic Association.
The competition will be held at the state level.
