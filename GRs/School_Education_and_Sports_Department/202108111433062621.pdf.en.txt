Introduction: Gyandeep Education Institute, Sakoli, G. Bhandara Managed by Kunjilal Bisen Science Junior College, Pandhari, Ta. Sadak/ Arjuni, G. Gondia, English Medium Permanent Unaided Higher Secondary School (Class 11th& 12th), Mouza Khatiya, Dist. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to G. Gondia was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted to its present location due to insufficient space. Also, the proposed location of the relocation is in a remote and inaccessible area and there is a need for a higher secondary school. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
