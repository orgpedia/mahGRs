Government's decision:
The selection of Maharashtra Government team for All India Civil Services Music and Dance Competition 2020-21 to be held at New Delhi from 15th to 17th September, 2021 was done at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai. The exam was conducted on 26th August 2021. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,33,628/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Six Hundred and Twenty Eight only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of Department/ Head of Office concerned should relieve the athletes/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 08th to 12th September, 2021 and for the competition from 15th to 17th September, 2021 with effect from 13th September, 2021 (M.No.) and provide the services of the athletes to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager of the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department Office under which they will be working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. The absence from office during the period from 08th to 18th September, 2021 (training camp, competition and travel) shall be treated as" duty period." as well as their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances.

Government Resolution No: Abhanase-3821/ No.92/ Kriuse-2,
The expenditure to be incurred should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned. Also, the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai are covered under Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9, dated 11th May, 1989 and Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1404/ PR. No.8/ A/ Service-8, dated 27th February, 2004 should be approved. In order to avail the benefit of this concession, the certificate of attendance of the officers and employees appearing for the selection test issued by the Honorary Secretary, Secretariat Gymkhana, will have to be submitted by the concerned employees in their establishment office.
The absence from office of the officer/ employee (Return-C) appearing for the selection test of the competition should be treated as duty under the Government Decision, Finance Division No.-LVE/ 1492/46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992.
The Secretarial Gymkhana shall submit the statement of actual expenditure to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of the order. In case of balance, the balance amount should be returned immediately to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
The expenditure for this should be met from the budgetary provision under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Competition, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Competition (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)" for the financial year 2021-22.
The Secretariat Gymkhana is approving the expenditure as per return" B" subject to fulfillment of all conditions in the report of the competition, details of actual expenditure incurred, utilization certificate, examination statement and sanction order.
