Letter from Commissioner Education No. Kala/ Group-A/ 2017/4684, dated 06.10.2017 Preamble: In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13.06.2013, it was decided to provide basic training to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) sponsored candidates by appointing them to the post of Education Officer and similar posts in Group-A cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) with effect from 01.07.2013. The officers who have completed the training have been posted as Education Officers and equivalent posts as per the Government decision dated 08.08.2013. Accordingly, these officers have been appointed as Education Officers and equivalent posts for a period of 2 years.
The Commissioner (Education) vide his letter dated 06.12.2017 has submitted a proposal to complete the period of probation of officers in the rank of Education Officer. It was under consideration of the Government to complete the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government, the officers mentioned in the accompanying form who have fulfilled the conditions of probationary period, mentioned above under the Government Decision dated 13.06.2013, in direct service in the Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) Group-A Cadre in the post of Education Officer and similar, shall be placed before their names. The period of probation is being completed on the date mentioned in Section 7. Government approval is being granted to continue their services on the date mentioned in Section 8.
