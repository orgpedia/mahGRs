Government's decision:
The selection of Maharashtra Government team for All India Civil Services Athletics Championship 2021-22 to be held at Tau Devi Lal Sports Stadium, Gurgaon, Haryana from 28th to 30th March, 2022 will be done at Priyadarshini Park, Nepeansea Road, Mumbai. The selection test was conducted on 14th March, 2022. The list of officers and staff involved in this test is as per the accompanying statement" C" and the team statement" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The tournament will be conducted as per the prospectus' B" for selection trials, training camp and participation of the state team. 1,25,700/- (Rupees One Lakh Twenty Five Thousand Seven Hundred only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
3. The Head of the Department concerned to participate in the tournament, the Head of the Office shall inform the players/ coaches/ managers. Training camp from 24th to 25th March, 2022. The competition will be held from March 28 to 30, 2022. They should be relieved with effect from 25th March, 2022 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager of the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department Office under which they will be working.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. Absence from office during the period from March 24 to March 31, 2022 (training camp, competition and travel) shall be treated as" period of duty." The expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances is borne by the Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned.
