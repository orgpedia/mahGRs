Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for grant of approval for shifting of the Gyan Bal Vikas Shikshan Sanstha, Pune-run Gyanada Prathmik Vidyamandir, Pimple Nilakh, Pune, a Marathi medium unaided primary school to More Vasti-Sharad Nagar, Chikhli, District Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school has proposed to shift from rented building to office-bearers building, More Vasti-Sharad Nagar, Chikhli, Dist. Pune. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
