Preamble: The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme through Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur for the posts in Group-B recruited directly by the State Service Examination every year since 2014. The decision is being implemented as per the government order. Accordingly, the Deputy Educational Officers and similar Group B officers selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the State Service Examination-2021 have been appointed as Trainee Officers and their training will be conducted at Vanamati, Nagpur under the Combined Pre-Trial Training Programme-9 (CPTP-9). It has started from 03.10.2023. Instructions have been issued through the Reference No. 4 circular of the General Administration Department regarding the creation of a maximum number of posts within the limit of the number of candidates who appeared for this training. Reference No. 9 for fulfillment of service matters of Sub-Inspectors in the cadre of Government Labour Officers (Group-B) under CPTP-9 As per the letter, Additional Labour Commissioner, Nagpur has been appointed as the nodal officer.

Government Decision: The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC), under the School Education Department, has approved the creation of a total of 24 supernumerary posts for a period of two years from 03.10.2023 to 29.09.2025 for a total of 24 candidates who were selected on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2021 and joined Vanamati, Nagpur for training.
These 24 supernumerary posts are being created on the establishment of the Commissioner of Education, Pune under the School Education Department.
3. The expenditure on the salary of the probationary officers for the period of two years from 03.10.2023 to 29.09.2025 of the above 24 Superintending Officers in the cadre of Deputy Educational Officer and similar, (Group-B) above the establishment of the Commissioner of Education, Pune, shall be apportioned from the head of account" 2202 All General Education- 80 All General 001 Operations and Administration (00) (01) Director of Education (22021091)" and it shall be apportioned from the grant sanctioned for that year.
The guidelines for the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-9 (CPTP-9) have been issued under the order of the General Administration Department at reference number 4. Accordingly, instructions have been issued to register these trainee officers in the service system, obtain the DCPS number, appoint Drawing and Disbursing Officers for their salary, provide regular salary of the concerned post to the probationers from the first month of training, prepare service books of the probationers, etc. in the first 4 weeks of training. Therefore, taking into consideration the directions of this circular, necessary action should be taken by the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Nagpur as the nodal officer.
This Government Resolution is being issued by the Department of Finance under the provisions of Government Resolution No. 2016/ No. 7/16/ Fiscal Reform 1 dated 20.02.2016.
