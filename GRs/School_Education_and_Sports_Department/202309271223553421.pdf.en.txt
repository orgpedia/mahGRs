Introduction:- Regarding the eligibility for the post of Central Head. Considering the petitions filed in the court and the representations received, as well as the qualifications for government employees, the Government has decided to issue a Government Corrigendum in the qualification for the post of Head of the Center as of 09.03.2023. Paragraph No. as amended w.e.f. 01.12.2022 5.1. regarding the abolition of the condition of having at least 50% marks in the graduation examination for the limited departmental competitive examination here, as well as the Government Decision. Paragraph No. 01.12.2022 5.2 The Government was considering the abolition of the minimum age limit of 50 years prescribed for the Limited Departmental Competitive Examination. Accordingly, the Government has decided to revise the eligibility criteria for selection to the post of Head of the Centre as follows:
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the selection to the post of Head of the Centre through Limited Departmental Competitive Examination and Promotion, Reference No. 2 The Government hereby amends the Government Order dated 09.03.2023. Also Reference No. 1 in the Government Decision dated 01.12.2022 vide Para No. 5.1, 5.2 and 6 are hereby omitted. However, the other provisions will remain in force.

The educational and professional qualifications for selection to the post of Head of the Centre through Limited Departmental Competitive Examination and Promotion are being determined as follows:
2.1 Selection through Limited Departmental Competitive Examination:
The candidate must have passed B.Com/ B.Sc. and must have completed at least 6 years of uninterrupted service in the post of Trained Graduate Teacher (Primary) or Trained Teacher (Primary) of Zilla Parishad.
2.2 For appointment by promotion:
The candidates who have completed at least 6 years of continuous service in the post of Trained Graduate Teacher (Primary) of Zilla Parishad will be appointed on the basis of merit and merit.
Since the posts of the head of the Zila Parishad are in the establishment of the Zila Parishad, the Department of Rural Development should take action to make necessary amendments in the Maharashtra Zila Parishad District Services (Service Entry) Rules, 1967 regarding their entry into service.
4. The said Government Decision is being issued with the concurrence of the Rural Development Department.
