Preamble: The Central Government has directed to include vegetables and other products produced from these vegetables in the school nutrition diet by creating parasagas in schools under the Pradhan Mantri Poshan Shakti Nirman Yojana. Guidelines as on 15th October, 2019. Accordingly, instructions have been issued to implement these activities at all school levels in the state. Also, excellent Parasbagh competition is organized by the department for effective implementation of Parasbagh activities. Vegetables, fruits and vegetables are being provided to the students. This initiative is serving various good purposes such as creating eco-friendly environment, providing nutritious food to the students and eliminating malnutrition. It is seen that this initiative is not being implemented due to the unavailability of space in some schools in rural and urban areas of the state. The central government has given guidelines to implement these activities through various options even in less space in schools. Accordingly, the following action should be taken regarding the creation of parasagas in the schools of the state and the vegetables grown in those parasagas.
