Introduction:" To provide financial assistance for the construction of sports facilities" is an important recommendation in sports policy. The objective of the scheme is to promote the creation of national and international sportspersons by creating quality sports facilities and thereby enhancing the performance of sportspersons. The detailed procedure in this regard is mentioned above. The decision was taken on March 1, 2014. Accordingly, the educational institutions, local/ civic bodies, schools/ junior colleges of local self-government institutions, ashramshalas of Tribal Development Department, divisional/ district/ taluka sports institutions of the State Government, gymnasiums, sports boards, sports associations etc. working for the development of various sports and registered under the Public Trust Act, 1950 or the Societies Registration Act, 1860 are provided financial assistance in the ratio of 75:25 (75% of the government and 25% of the eligible institution concerned) for the construction of sports facilities. 2 The terms and conditions are explained in paragraph 4 (b) of the Government Decision. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, Pune has submitted the proposals of eligible institutions to the Government under Reference No. 3 for grant of administrative approval for financial assistance for sports materials. Following the recommendations, the government took the decision. Subject to the terms and conditions as on 1.3.2014, the proposal for grant of financial assistance for sports material was under consideration of the Government for grant of in-principle administrative approval.
