The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of Nagpur Hindi and Marathi medium aided primary school, Savitribai Phule Nagar, near Jaripatka police station, to Jaripatka, Dist.
Government Decision: The relocation has been proposed due to the difficulty in providing physical facilities for the students due to insufficient space of the above school. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time by the Government, a pucca building for the school and all physical facilities and educational facilities shall be provided at the place of relocation. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) School, teachers and non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grant, students in this regard.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time.

(5) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(6) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(7) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff.
(8) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
2. Clause No. On the fulfillment of clauses (2) and (3), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall take further action regarding the shifting of the said school within 4 weeks and inform the Government about the action taken.
