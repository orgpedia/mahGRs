Preamble: The proposal for disbursement of funds sanctioned under 34 scholarships/ scholarships for the financial year 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government. The government's decision is as follows:
Government Decision: To encourage meritorious students in the state, various schemes are being implemented to provide scholarships to the students in the schools of the state. The Government is approving to disburse a total amount of Rs. 4,60,25, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand only) for 34 scholarships/ scholarships for the schemes mentioned in paragraph 2 out of the approved budgetary provision in the financial year 2021-22 and make the said funds available on the budgetary distribution system.
