The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Regulation of Fee) Act, 2011 has been implemented across the state. As per the provisions of the said Act, the Departmental Fee Regulatory Committee and Revision Committee are to be constituted. As per the provisions of sub-section (1) of section 7 of the said Act, vide Government Notification dated 7th June, 2021, Divisional Fee Regulatory Committees have been constituted for Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, Nagpur and Aurangabad Divisions and the matter of constitution of Divisional Fee Regulatory Committees at Kolhapur, Amravati and Latur was under consideration of the Government.
The Government have decided to entrust the functions of the Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee constituted under the relevant notification till the formation of separate Divisional Fee Regulatory Committees in Kolhapur, Amravati and Latur.
