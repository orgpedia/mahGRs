INTRODUCTION: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 27.03.2017, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has appointed the following candidates as Senior Advocate in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre for a period of two years. Mr. Rahul Barve gave recommendations and appointments of some of the officers who have been given this appointment. Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai in Original Application No. 1209/2016 was filed. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 22.06.2021 of the General Administration Department, the original Application No. 1209/ 2016 covering the period of probation of the respondent officers vide original application No. 1209/2016 has been extended till the final decision.
2. m. In pursuance of the order dated 24.02.2023 passed by the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, vide original Application No. 1209/2016 has been disposed of. The court did not issue any order. Therefore, the matter of completing their probation period on a regular basis was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
As per the prevailing policy of the Government, the above-mentioned officers have been appointed to the post of Senior Advocate in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-A cadre as per the Government Decision dated 27.03.2017.
