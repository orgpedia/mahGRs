Regarding transfer of Amarjyot Tarun Mandal Education Society, Bhosari, Pune running Shri Swami Samarth Primary Vidyamandir, Indrayaninagar, Bhosari, Pune to Shri Tagore Education Society, Bhosari, Pune.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of School Education and Sports
Government Resolution No: PRASHAH 3020/ PR No.340/ SM-1
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: January 27, 2021
Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution No.Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi- 3-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: Departmental Deputy Director of Education, Pune Division, Pune vide letter No.
Preamble: The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the Marathi medium aided primary school namely Amarjyot Tarun Mandal Shikshan Sanstha, Indrayaninagar, Bhosari, Pune-39 operated by Shri Swami Samarth Prathmik Vidyamandir, Indrayaninagar, Bhosari Pune-39 to Shri Tagore Shikshan Sanstha, Indrayaninagar, Bhosari, Pune-26 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Shri Tagore Education Society, Indrayaninagar, Bhosari, Pune-26 on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed under any circumstances.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune shall examine all the matters and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
