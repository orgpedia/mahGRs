School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.Masha 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012
Reference: Letter dated 17.01.2020 from the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur to Shri.
Preamble: Maratha Ekta Shikshan Sanstha, Nanded, operating Santosh Kanya Primary School, Navin Nanded, a Marathi medium aided primary (1st to 5th standard) school, Shri Sai Multipurpose Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Kalegaon, T.G. The proposal received under reference letter for transfer of the institution to Ahmedpur, Latur district was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Shri Sai Multi-purpose Educational Broadcasting Board, Kalegaon, Tt. The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the school to Ahmedpur, Latur District on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks criteria of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the school in which the change of management has taken place.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Latur Division, Latur, shall examine all the matters and take further action regarding the change of management of the school, including the necessary conditions in addition to the above terms and conditions, within 15 days and inform the government about it.
