The Trust is running a Marathi medium aided upper primary (class 1st to 7th) school at New Jimmy Bagh, Kalyan (East), run by Vadakka Vidyamandir, Matoshree Nagar, Chinchpada Road, Kalyan (East), near Nandivali Lake, Nandivali College, Tt. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Kalyan, G. Thane was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Relocation is proposed as the owner has informed to vacate the rented dilapidated building of the above school. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
Also, interested students will have to be accommodated at the place of migration.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
