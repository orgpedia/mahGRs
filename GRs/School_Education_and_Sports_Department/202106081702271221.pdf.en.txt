Introduction: Shri Suresh Hiraman Gaikwad of Marathi medium aided secondary school, Thakur Green Valley School, Punapur, Pardi, Nagpur, run by Bansraj Singh Thakur Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur, is a resident of Plot No. The proposal received under reference letter to relocate 377 and 400 to Nageshwar Nagar, Mauja Bidgaon, Pardi, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the owner has given a notice to vacate the existing building site of the above school, the school is proposed to be shifted. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department in respect of such migration or if it is found that the conditions relating to migration have been violated.
