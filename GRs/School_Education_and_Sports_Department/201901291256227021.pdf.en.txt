(1) The term of the Board of Directors shall be for a period of five years or until a new Board is constituted, whichever is later. The proposal for reconstitution of the Selection Board should be submitted to the Joint Director, Sports and Youth Services, State of Maharashtra, Pune before completion of the 5 year period or at an appropriate time as may be required.
(2) With a view to streamline and facilitate the recruitment process by conducting interviews at one place to fill up the vacancies of part-time officers, the Joint Director, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune is being approved to conduct these interviews at Pune.
(3) The non-official members attending the proceedings of the said Selection Board shall be informed by the Finance Department, Government Resolution No. 1010/ No. 2/ Service-5, D. As of March 3, 2010, No. Travel allowance and daily allowance as mentioned in section 12 shall be admissible. No separate fee will be charged for the meeting.
(4) The Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Sports and Youth Services, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to sign the copy of payment of Travel Allowance to non-official members.
