Government decision The matter of amending the condition in para 3 (a) of 01.07.2016 was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision-Referred No. (1) In paragraph 3 (a) of the Government Decision dated 01.07.2016, the condition is as follows:" In international competitions, a team/ individual event shall consist of a minimum of 12 athletes from at least 12 countries in the team/ individual event (individual sport category/ each weight category) separately. Minimum 60% of States/ Districts are required to participate in all National/ State Championships The condition is being revised as follows:" In International Competitions, at least 12 teams from 12 countries in team/ individual events/ at least 12 teams in individual events (individual sports/ each weight category) separately. In all National/ State Championships, at least 60% of the States/ Districts affiliated to the Unitary Sports Federations of the respective sport shall be participating.
(2) This Order shall apply to the recruitment process commencing on the date of this Government decision and in cases where the selection process has commenced and the final list (merit list) has not yet been declared by the Selection Authority. However, this order will not be applicable in cases where the final results of the recruitment process have been declared.
The orders issued by the Government from time to time in respect of sports qualification, sports, competition and other terms and conditions in relation to the 3.5% reservation for sportspersons will also remain in force.
