Preamble: In pursuance of the Government Resolution No. 1, all the posts in the Bhosla Military School, Nashik have been brought under the revised pay scale as per the recommendations of the Sixth Pay Commission.
Subject to Government Resolution No. (2) Under this, the revised pay scale has been applied to all the posts in Bhosla Military School, Nashik as per the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission. However, since the revised pay scale has not been applied to the posts of (1) Commandant, (2) Chief Training Officer, (3) Training Officer, (4) Assistant Training Officer, (5) Mess Manager as per the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission, the matter of applying the revised pay scale as per the recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Now, the Government hereby order that the Government hereby approve the revised pay scale for the posts of (1) Commandant, (2) Chief Training Officer, (3) Training Officer, (4) Assistant Training Officer, (5) Mess Manager in Bhosla Military School, Nashik subject to the following conditions as prescribed in the following statement.
