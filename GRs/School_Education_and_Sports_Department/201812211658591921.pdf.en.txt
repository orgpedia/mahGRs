Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of Paradise English Secondary School, Camp Amaravati, an English medium permanent unaided secondary school, to Shantai Multipurpose Institute, Shanthinagar, Amaravati, run by Abbas Ali Haji Education and Welfare Society, Camp Amaravati, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the Sadar Secondary School to Shantai Multipurpose Institute, Shantinagar, Amravati on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teachers/ non-teaching staff who have consented to transfer in the school will be accommodated and their service conditions will not be changed. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
