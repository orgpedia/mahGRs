School Education and Sports Department, Government Resolution No.Masha 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: Departmental Deputy Director of Education, Amravati vide No. HSC/ 1971/2018, dt. Letter dated 26/03/2018.
The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Urdu medium unaided Junior College, Amravati, to the Muslim Minority Multi-Purpose Institute, Achalpur, G. Amravati, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Junior College to the Muslim Minority Multi-Purpose Institute, Achalpur, G. Amaravathi on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) The Chairman/ Secretary of the new institution will be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities for the school as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs 100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their service conditions shall not change. In this regard, the Chairman/ Secretary of the new organization will receive Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the Government decision, dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time in this regard, and not to employ more teaching/ non-teaching staff than the permissible posts.
(5) It shall be ensured that the financial position of the Muslim Minority Multi-Purpose Organisation is sound on the basis of its bank balance and fixed deposits.
(6) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.

(7) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(9) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(10) If any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the conditions of service of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. On fulfillment of clauses (2), (3) and (5), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Amravati Division, Amravati, shall take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school after examining all the aspects and including other necessary conditions and inform the government about the action taken.
