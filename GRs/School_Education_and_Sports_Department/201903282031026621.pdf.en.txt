the prelude
The ratio of Central and State share for Vocational Education (Centre: State) is 60:40 for the ongoing programme of Purvashrami Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan. The Central Government vide order dated 18/3/2019 (General) has released Rs. A sum of Rs. 2741.51 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores Forty One Lakh Fifty One Thousand only) has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyaan, the Central Government has taken up the ongoing Vocational Education initiative of Purvashrami Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan with reference No. 2 The State Project Coordinator, Samagra Shiksha, Mumbai has proposed to sanction the State Account Fund in proportion to the funds deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State as per the letter herein. 3 submitted to the government. This amount has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State, the Finance Division of the Finance Department said in an informal reference No. 151/ Arthopay, Dy. Comments have been submitted as on 26.3.2019. The State share of this fund is Rs. 1827.67 lakhs (Rupees Eighteen Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Sixty Six Thousand Only).
