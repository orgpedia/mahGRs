Department of School Education and Sports, Government Resolution No.Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012 Reference: Departmental Deputy Director of Education, Pune Division, Pune vide letter No.
The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer to Dugad Trust, G. Pune was under consideration of the Government.
The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the school to Dugad Trust, G.Pune on the following terms and conditions:- (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school transferred shall not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) It shall be ensured that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in respect of the transferred school.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said transfer shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Order, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) If any fresh complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the said transfer or if it is found that the terms and conditions regarding the change of management have been violated or the service conditions of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management have been violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
The Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Pune Division, Pune should examine the incidental matters and take further action within 4 weeks regarding the transfer of the school and inform the government about the action taken.
