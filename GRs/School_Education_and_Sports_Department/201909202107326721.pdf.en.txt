INTRODUCTION: Kids R Kids National English Medium School, Crystal Centre, 2nd Floor, Opposite Konark Puram, Kondhwa, Pune, operated by Maa Durga Shakti Mahila Sanstha, Kondhwa, Pune. The proposal received under the said letter for shifting to 6, Mauje Kondhwa Khurd, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The above school is proposed to be shifted on the grounds of insufficient space and inadequate physical facilities. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Pune should ensure that the simple online information of the school is filled and the U-Dice number is obtained.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) The institution shall be fully responsible for the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the English medium as the school is on self-financing basis.
