Preamble: The proposal received under reference letters to shift the Marathi medium aided primary (class 1st to 7th) school of Swami Vidya Mandir, Bhosla, opposite Veda School, Mahal, Nagpur, run by Swamikripa Education Society, Nagpur, to Nageshwar Nagar, Bidgaon, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The present building of the above school is dilapidated, dangerous and damaged. The transfer of the said school to the aforesaid location subject to the condition of recognition of the year 2019-20 is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
