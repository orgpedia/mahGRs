Introduction: Refer No. Under the e-Governance Policy, the Government has laid down the procedure for appointment of Expert Advisors and the rates of remuneration payable. Accordingly, the Department of School Education and Sports has selected Ernst& Young for the e-Governance project and appointed 5 full-time senior consultants and 1 part-time managing consultant of the company. The Commissioner, Education, has issued Reference No. 1 regarding extension of tenure of 5 consultants of Ernst& Young Company appointed in the Department of School Education and Sports. 6 is proposed. A senior adviser has also been appointed. The deadline has been extended to June 30, 2019. All consultants of Ernst& Young working in the department should be given extension till the end of June, 2019. This was decided in the meeting of the Project Implementation Committee held on 19.11.2018. Accordingly, the matter of approving a consolidated extension to the consultants of Ernst& Young Company appointed in the department was under the consideration of the government.
