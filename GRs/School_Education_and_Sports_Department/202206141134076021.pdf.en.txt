The Proposal:
In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 04.08.2017 and 25.09.2017, the above mentioned officers have been appointed in direct service in the cadre of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-B for a period of two years.
The proposal to complete the probationary period has been submitted vide letter dated 09.11.2021 from the Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the Government Decision dated 04.08.2017 and 25.09.2017, mentioned above, the officers mentioned in the accompanying proforma for the post of Advocate in the Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch) Group-B cadre, who have fulfilled the conditions of probationary period as per the prevailing policy of the Government, are being allowed to continue their service on the date mentioned in the Rakana No. 7 before their names.
