The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 13.06.2013, it was decided to provide basic training to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) sponsored candidates by appointing them to the post of Education Officer and equivalent in Group A cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Administration Branch) with effect from 01.07.2013. The officers who have completed the training have been posted as Education Officers and equivalent posts as per the Government decision dated 08.08.2013. Accordingly, these officers have been appointed as Education Officers and equivalent posts for a period of 2 years. The proposal to complete the probationary period has been submitted vide letter dated 06.10.2017 from the Commissioner (Education). The matter of completing the probation period of the officers who successfully completed the basic training and fulfilled the conditions of probation period as per the prevailing policy of the government was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government of Maharashtra in direct service under the Government Decision dated 13.06.2013 at A.No.1 mentioned herein above, which fulfils the conditions of the probation period as per the prevailing policy of the Government.
