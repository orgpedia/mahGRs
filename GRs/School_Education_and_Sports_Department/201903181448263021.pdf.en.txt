Government's decision:
The selection of Maharashtra Government team for All India Civil Services Carrom Competition 2018-19 held at New Delhi from 11th to 15th March, 2019 was done at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai. The exam was conducted on March 2, 2019. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the prospectus" C" along with the team prospectus" A" of the state selected through the selection test. The selection test, training camp and participation of the state team as per the prospectus' B' is Rs. 56,225/ (Rs. Only two thousand five hundred and fifty) such expenditure is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The athletes who have received kitbags and tracksuits in the team will be eligible to receive kitbags and tracksuits for any All India Civil Services Sports Meet once in two years.
4. The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned shall inform the player-coach/ manager to participate in the tournament. Training camp from 6th to 8th March 2019. The competition will be held from 11th to 15th March 2019. They should be relieved with effect from 5th March, 2019 (M.No.) and the services of these players should be made available to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain permission from the head of the department head office under which they will be working.
The players of the team as per the provisions of the Government Decision No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated 11th May, 1989 Curve No.LVE/ 1492/46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992.
