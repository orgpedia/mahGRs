Introduction: Under the State Sports Policy 2001, the scheme of providing minimum facilities for various sports in the Taluk Sports Complex at the Taluk level, providing national level sports facilities under the District Sports Complex at the District level, as well as setting up international level sports facilities under the Divisional Sports Complex at the Divisional level has been implemented under the Government Decision dated 26th March, 2003. As per the Government decision dated 21st March, 2009, the grant limit of Taluk Sports Complex is Rs. 25.00 lakhs to Rs. 1.00 crore, District Sports Complex grant limit is Rs. 4.00 crore to Rs. 8.00 crore and Rs. 16.00 crore to Rs. 24.00 crore.
The State Sports Development Committee has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 26.03.2003. The Committee has the power to grant technical, administrative and financial approvals for various sports facilities at Zonal/ District/ Taluka Sports Complexes. The State Sports Development Committee in its meeting held on 02.11.2018 has approved the release of funds to the Divisional Sports Complex, Latur and 6 District Sports Complexes at Pune, Baramati, Satara, Osmanabad, Nashik, Aurangabad and Amravati. According to the Directorate of Sports and Youth Services, No. 3 for Rs. A request has been made to disburse a provision of Rs. 1624.00 lakhs. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the allocation made in the budget for the year 2018-19 for the District Sports Complexes.
