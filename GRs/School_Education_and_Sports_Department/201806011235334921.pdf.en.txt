As per the provisions of Section 29 of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009, the Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (MSERT), Pune has been renamed as Maharashtra Vidya Authority, Pune. Now, as per the Central Government letter dated 27.2.2018, in order to have uniformity in academic activities, all the States must have the same name of the institutions discharging the same educational responsibilities.
The Maharashtra government has decided to change the name of the Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research and Training (MSCERT).
