Preamble: The Consolidated Guidelines on the procedure to be followed for promotion of State Government Officers/ Employees have been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 1.8.2019 of the General Administration Department. As per the guidelines in this government decision, the government is considering the formation of a divisional promotion committee for the promotion of Group A posts in the Maharashtra Education Service under the School Education and Sports Department. Following this, the government decided:
Government Decision: The Government Decision issued earlier to form a Divisional Promotion Committee to recommend for promotion to the post of Group-A in Maharashtra Education Service under the School Education and Sports Department is being superseded and a new Divisional Promotion Committee is being formed as follows:

If there is no member representing the Backward Classes in the above Divisional Promotion Committee, an officer representing the Backward Classes will be included as an invited member with the consent of the Chairman of the Committee.
Necessary quorum for the meeting of the Departmental Promotion Committee:
A minimum of 3 members must be present at the meeting: the President, the Secretary, and the Treasurer. However, if the Secretary/ Member Secretary is absent from the meeting, the officer to be directed by the Chairman of the Committee shall act as Secretary/ Member Secretary.
The purview of the Departmental Promotion Committee shall be as follows: The Grade Pay of the posts as per the 6th Pay Commission is Rs. 7599/- or less for Maharashtra Education Service Group-A posts.

To consider the proposal for recommending promotion for all such posts which are Rs.7,599/-or less and to recommend to the appointing authority for promotion by preparing the select list of officers eligible for promotion in accordance with the provisions of the Service Admission Rules and considering the orders/ guidelines issued by the General Administration Department from time to time.
