The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. List of Posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, available on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in, under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification
Identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability Notified on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In accordance with the directions of the said Government Order, Group-A and Group-B on the establishment under the Commissioner (Education), State of Maharashtra, under the School Education and Sports Department
The matter of ensuring posts in the cadre for the disabled category was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification to ensure reservation of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
The list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published vide notification dated 04.01.2021.
It states that the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been ensured by the Central Government for the disabled, are in the state government establishments and the posts in the list published by the Central Government.
The duties and responsibilities of the posts, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are the same as that of the posts in the state government service
