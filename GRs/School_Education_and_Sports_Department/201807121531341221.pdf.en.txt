Preamble: The Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India has sanctioned Rs. A proposal of Rs. 310.00 lakh has been approved and funds have been made available by the Central Government to the District Collector, Latur. To ensure continuity in the education of girls from minority communities, to provide educational opportunities to girls from these communities and to ensure educational upliftment of the community, it was necessary to build a hostel for minority girls.
Government's decision:
Out of the 3.09-hectare land owned by Government Teachers School, Udgir, Latur, 1.20-acre land is being sanctioned for construction of hostel for 100 girls belonging to minority community subject to following conditions.
1) The ownership of this land will remain with the Government Teachers School, Udgir.
2) This land is being made available for use only by the Minority Department for the construction of hostels.
3) The Minority Department should not construct any other construction on the said site other than the hostel.
