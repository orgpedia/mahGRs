Preamble: Banasraj Singh Thakur Education Society, Nagpur operates Thakur's Green Valley Secondary School. The school is located at Rajabhoj Colony, Punapur, and is affiliated to a Marathi medium aided school, Randhir Higher Primary School, Bakulkhed, Narendra Nagar, Nagpur. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to Nagpur was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to the danger of the above school building, the Nagpur Municipal Corporation and the landlord have issued a notice to vacate the house, the students are shifting to the rented building for safety reasons. The shifting of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur shall ensure that maximum number of students of the said middle school shall be accommodated at the place of shifting and those students who are not willing to be admitted at the place of shifting shall be accommodated in the nearest aided school.
(2) The said school is proposed to be shifted to the school building of Randhir Higher Primary School, Rajabhoj Colony, Punapur, Dist. The objections of the concerned institution and the principal should be taken by the Certified Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur. Two schools will be set up in the same building. Institutions will have to ensure that this does not create any disputes/ difficulties in the future.
(3) The long-term tenancy of the school building should be verified.
(4) Personal recognition of teachers as well as recognition of classes 6th, 7th, 9th and 10th must be verified by the Education Officer (Secondary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur.
(5) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(6) Merger of institutions. It should be ensured that the name of the new institution is registered by verifying the orders of the Charity Commissioner.

(7) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. (10) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school. Also, the school will function on the same grant-in-aid basis as the one in the original location.
(11) It shall be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teaching/ non-teaching staff as are permissible in accordance with the revised criteria of set approval prescribed by the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, dated 28/08/2015 and orders issued from time to time by the Government in this regard. Also, it will be necessary to take action according to the government's policy regarding filling the vacant posts from the permissible posts by adjusting the additional staff.
(12) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
