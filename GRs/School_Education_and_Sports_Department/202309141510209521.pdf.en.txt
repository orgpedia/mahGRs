Preamble 01.09.2023: The National Cadet Corps was constituted in 1948-49 for secondary, higher secondary and college students in educational institutions on a nationwide basis and the National Cadet Corps Act, 1948 has been enacted to provide for and structure the National Cadet Corps.
Various activities are carried out by the National Cadet Corps offices in the state through training/ camps for national cadets. Out of the budgetary provision available in the financial year 2023-24 for this initiative, a total of Rs. 56,33,139/- (Rupees Fifty Six Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand One Hundred and Forty Nine only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision:
Various activities are carried out by the offices of the National Cadet Corps in the state through training/ camps for national cadets. Out of the budgetary provision available in the financial year 2023-24 for this activity, a total of Rs. 56,33,139/- (Rupees Fifty Six Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand One Hundred and Forty Nine only) and to be placed under the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
