AND (303)/ 3253, dated 25.05.2015 Preamble: As per the decision of the Government dated 30.04.2004, the cost of food for the students in the Government Educational Institutions in the State is Rs. 500/- has been fixed.
Considering the current inflation index and the increase in the prices of essential and other things since 2004, the government was considering increasing the food expenses of students in government schools.
Government's decision:
At present, the State Government is spending Rs. 500/- per month. The government is approving the food expenses of Rs.2160/- (Rupees Two Thousand One Hundred Sixty only).
The increase will be effective from the academic year 2019-20.
(3) This Government decision was taken with the concurrence of the Finance Department and in its informal reference No. 583/ Expenditure-5, dated 17.9.2019.
