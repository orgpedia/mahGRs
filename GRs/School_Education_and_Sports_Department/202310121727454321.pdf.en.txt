Introduction: Under the Samagra Shiksha programme of the Central Government, all girls in the class 1st to 8th in government and local body schools, all children of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes and children of parents below the poverty line are given the benefit of free uniforms. The government was considering opening a new account head to provide free uniforms to the children of poverty line parents who were deprived of this scheme of the central government, as well as to provide the benefit of one pair of shoes and two pairs of trousers to all eligible students under the free uniform scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the approval of the Planning and Finance Department, approval is being given to open a new account head for the" Free Uniform, Shoes and Socks" state level scheme as follows.
Demand No.E-2 2202, General Education 01- Elementary Education 103- Assistance to Local Bodies for Elementary Education (01) Assistance to Local Bodies for Elementary Education (01) (20) Free Uniform, Boot and Footwear Scheme (State Level Scheme) (2202 K838)
