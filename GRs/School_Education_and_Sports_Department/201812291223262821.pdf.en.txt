The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC)-recognized candidates have been appointed for a period of 2 years for the post of Senior Advocate in Group-A cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (Training Branch) vide Government Decisions dated 30.01.2013 and 11.02.2013 respectively. The departmental examination was not conducted for the officers appointed in the cadre of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-A and Group-B (Education Empowerment Branch- formerly Teacher Training Branch). Hence, the salaries of these employees have been withheld. As a result, service matters like termination of probationary period of all these officers, continuation of appointments, regularization of increments, preparation of seniority etc. are also pending. The proposal to complete the period of probation has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 19.04.2018 from the Commissioner (Education).

The Department of School Education and Sports vide notification dated 26.02.2018 has notified the Rules 2018 regulating the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal Group-A, Senior Superintendence Group-A and Superintendence Group-B in the cadre of" Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch)."
Government's decision:
The candidates have been appointed in direct service as per the Government Decision dated 30.01.2013 and 11.02.2013 mentioned herein for the post of Superintendents (Group-B) in Maharashtra Education Service (Training Branch before Education Empowerment Branch). Also, read here mentioned at Annexure No.3, candidates have been appointed directly to the post of Senior Advocate (Group-A) as per the Government decision dated 01.03.2013.
2. The Department of School Education and Sports vide notification dated 26.02.2018 has notified the Rules 2018 regulating the Departmental Examination for the posts of Principal Group-A, Senior Superintendence Group-A and Superintendence Group-B in the cadre of" Maharashtra Education Service (Education Empowerment Branch)." In view of the provisions of the said Rules, the Government has decided to regularise the probationary period of 25 officers who were appointed in the year 2013 on regular basis. The officers mentioned in the accompanying Form-A appointed to the post of Senior Advocate and Advocate in the Maharashtra Education Service (Training Branch) Cadre are to be mentioned in front of their names in the form No. The period of probation is being completed on the date mentioned in Section 7. Government approval is being granted to continue their services on the date mentioned in Section 8.
The officers completing the probation should be given annual increment subject to the rules dated 26.02.2018. This should also be noted in the service book of the concerned.
4. The" Zonal Examination Rules 2018 for the posts of Principal (Group-A), Senior Advocate (Group-A) and Advocate (Group B) (Education Empowerment Branch) in Maharashtra Education Service" have been issued on 26.02.2018 and published in the Gazette dated 27.02.2018. As per Rule 3 (1) of the said notification, officers who have completed their period of probation on regular basis (excluding officers who are exempted on completion of 55 years of age) are required to pass the departmental examination.
