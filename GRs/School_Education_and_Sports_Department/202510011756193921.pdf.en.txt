Preamble: - Additional Chief Secretary (Power). In a letter to the semi-government dated 01.09.2025, Md. The Chief Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today chaired the meeting of the Union Ministry of Power. The directive of the 01.07.2022 directs action to be taken immediately to start the implementation of the composite billing system for timely payment of electricity bills of various government departments as mentioned in the directive. Accordingly, action under the School Education and CRODA was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
A composite billing system is being implemented to ensure timely payment of electricity bills to various government departments. The Government is approving the appointment of the Under Secretary (Budget) as the Nodal Officer and the Deputy Secretary (Budget) as the Controlling Officer for implementing the Composite Billing System for timely payment of electricity bills of Government Primary Schools.
