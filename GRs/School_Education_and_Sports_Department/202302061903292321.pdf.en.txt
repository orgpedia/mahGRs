Preamble: The General Administration Department, under reference No. 2 here. In accordance with the instructions issued by the Government Circular dated February 2, 2023, all administrative departments and field offices of the Ministry will be allowed to function. The e-office system is to be implemented with effect from April 1, 2023. To ensure that the mail received in the ministerial departments and offices under them is processed promptly and no time is wasted. Instructions have been issued to strictly implement the e-mailing process in all the administrative departments and field offices of the Ministry from 1st February, 2023. Accordingly, the matter of issuing instructions on the basis of this circular to all the regional offices of the School Education Department was under consideration. A circular is being issued in this regard.
Circular: In order to increase the use of e-office in government offices, the following instructions are being issued to all the field offices under the School Education Department.
Strict action should be taken to send the mail received in the offices under the School Education Department through every postal e-office while dealing with the mail so that immediate action is taken on the mail and time is not wasted. All the field offices under the School Education Department, except the following, should send all correspondence through e-office only, through Government e-mail (NIC/ GOV). Only the following mail should be sent by hand.
(a) maps, building plans, (b) books, (e) original documents or documents in cases where the sending of such documents is mandatory, e.g. Service books of officers and employees, original medical bills, etc.
