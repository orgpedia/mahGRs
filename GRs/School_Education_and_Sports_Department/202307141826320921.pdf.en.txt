Preamble- The revised rules have been prescribed under the Government decision dated 14/12/2022 for the award of Shivchhatrapati State Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar, Best Sports Guide/ Jijamata Krida Puraskar, Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Sportsperson), Shivchhatrapati State Sports Award (Disabled Sportsperson) and Adventure Sports Award to the best sportsperson of the State of Maharashtra. Accordingly, a Selection Committee was constituted vide Government Resolution dated 31/01/2023 to select the awardees for the years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22. Considering the recommendations of the selection committee, the government was considering to approve the distribution of awards for the years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22.
Government Decision: As a mark of respect to the veteran sportspersons, sports mentors and sports personalities of the state who have made an unparalleled place in the sports life of Maharashtra by giving their all for sports and games and whose personality and sports activism has made a memorable impact on the sports life of Maharashtra, the Krida Maharishi is honoured with the Shiv Chhatrapati State Sports Life Pride Award. Accordingly, the Government is approving conferment of" Shivchhatrapati Rajya Krida Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar" for the years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 to the veteran sports personalities mentioned in Annexure A.
