The Government's Decisions:
Mr. Praveen Taide, Producer, Samata Films, Akola has requested for permission to screen Marathi film "Satya Shodhak" from schools in the state in a letter dated 10/01/2024 under reference. Accordingly, the No. 3 The report of the Review Committee constituted for the proceedings referred to in the letter herein has been received.
The film "Satyashodhak" depicts Mahatma Jyotirao Phule's struggle for social change, his contribution to the development of modern Maharashtra, the labour movement, educational development of women and construction of modern buildings, Phule's substantial achievements in all these fields, and the support given to him by Mata Savitribai Phule in that struggle, showing the social enlightenment that the couple achieved beyond their lifetimes. Hence, students will be able to see the history of Mahatma Phule and Maharashtra through this film. Hence, the screening of the film "Satya Shodhak" to the students / girl students of the schools in the state is being permitted for the academic year 2023-24, 2024-25 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Conditions: - 1) The film "Satya Shodhak" is being allowed to be screened in schools in the state for students of all ages from classes 1 to 12.
2) No student will be forced to watch the film.
