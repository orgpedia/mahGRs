The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. List of posts for persons with disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 is available on the website of the Central Government www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification
Identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability Notified on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering the matter of ensuring posts in Group-A and Group-B cadres for the disabled category in the establishment under the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, under the School Education and Sports Department.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published vide notification dated 04.01.2021. As mentioned in the notification, the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in the Group A to Group D posts in the state government establishments.
