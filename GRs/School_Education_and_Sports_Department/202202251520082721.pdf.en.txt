The Proposal:
The Department of School Education, No. In the 1. As per the decision of the Government on 8th August, 2019, the revised syllabus and revised assessment scheme for Class XI and XII have been fixed. Reference No. 2 of Government Order and Reference No. 3 and 4 have been amended from time to time by Government Decisions. Considering the demand of various sections of the junior colleges, 1) To give an opportunity to the students enrolled in the subject of Pedagogy, which was excluded from Group C for the Science stream, to appear in the Higher Secondary Certificate (Class 12) Examination of 2022 2) To reconsider the limits of the number of optional subjects to be taken from Group B and Group C.
Government's decision:
In this regard, the content of class 11 and class 12 is being revised as follows.
For Arts and Commerce
1) The subject of Pedagogy for Arts stream will be included in Group C.
2) While selecting eight subjects for Arts and Commerce streams, four subjects from Group A will be compulsory as before.
At least two subjects from Group B will have to be selected while selecting the remaining four optional subjects.

For the remaining two subjects, a total of two subjects can be selected from Group B and Group C within the limit of zero, one or two from Group B and zero, one or two from Group C.
For Science stream:
The subject of Pedagogy has been excluded from Group C for Science stream, but keeping in view the academic interest of the student, the students who have opted for subjects as per the earlier subject scheme are being given the only opportunity to appear in the Higher Secondary Certificate (Class 12) Examination, 2022. However, if a student does not pass in the subject of education in this examination, then he/ she can enter the examination with the same subject as per the previous subject.
No such concession will be available for Class XI from the academic year 2021-22 and Class XII from the academic year 2022-23.
