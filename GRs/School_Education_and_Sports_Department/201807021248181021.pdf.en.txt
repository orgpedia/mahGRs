Introduction: Matoshree Tulsi Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Aurangabad runs Super Thirty English School, Jatwada Road, Harsul area, District Aurangabad, a permanent non-aided primary and upper primary (1st to 7th standard) school of English medium, Survey No. The proposal received under the reference letter for shifting to 187/1, Plot D, Ushakiran Housing Society, Mhasoba Nagar, Harsul T Point, G. Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Due to inadequate physical facilities at the present location of the above school, it is proposed to shift to a rented self-sufficient building. The relocation of the said school to the aforesaid location is being approved on the following terms and conditions: (1) The minimum marks requirement of the relocated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
(2) It shall be necessary to ensure in writing the consent of the parents of the students in the school to the relocation, as well as the adjustment of all the students in the school to the place of relocation.
(3) The migration must be agreed upon by all teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, all qualified teachers and non-teaching staff will be accommodated in the migrant place and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(4) It will be necessary to ensure that there is no objection/ objection to the opening of the school in the concerned building.
The lease agreement must be valid for at least 5 years.
