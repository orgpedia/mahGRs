The proposal-
Department of School Education (Education, Sports and Social Justice) The provisions relating to the posts of Junior Clerk/ Clerk in the Entry into Service Rules dated 17th September, 1971 have lapsed. Therefore, the government is considering amending the service entry rules regarding the post of junior clerk or clerk-typist by amending the said provisions. In order to ensure that there is no difficulty in the recruitment of posts till the revised rules are issued, the government has decided as follows:
# Page 2
Government's decision
School Education (then Education, Sports and Social Justice Department). The provisions of the Entry into Service Rules for the post of Junior Clerk/ Clerk in the Entry into Service Rules dated 17th September, 1971 are being superseded.
2. The process of prescribing revised service entry rules for the post of Junior Clerk or Clerk-Typist is in progress. However, in order to ensure that the recruitment to the posts of Junior Clerk and Clerk-Typist in the government offices of the School Education Department is not hampered, the following service entry rules prescribed for the post of Clerk-Typist by the General Administration Department are being implemented with amendments therein.
(a) Clerk-Typist in Government Offices outside Greater Mumbai, Group-C (Entry into Service) Rules, 2017 (b) Clerk-Typist in Government Offices in Greater Mumbai, Group-C (Entry into Service) Rules, 2018
(3) The Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action in respect of the recruitment to the post of Junior Clerk or Clerk-Typist as per the rules of entry into service prescribed by this Government. Also, the matter should be brought to the notice of all the concerned appointing authorities.
