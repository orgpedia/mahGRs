The Proposal:
The Government Resolution in Reference-1 has been passed in respect of service matters of teachers and non-teaching staff of night schools, accreditation of night schools, etc. However, in view of the fact that certain provisions of the Government Resolution dated 17.5.2017 are found to be inconsistent with the provisions of the Maharashtra Private Schools Employees (Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981, and in view of the attention drawn to the matter in the third session of the year 2021, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to amend the provisions of Notice No. In pursuance of the discussion on 405, the Government was contemplating to set up a High Level Committee to review the Government Resolution dated 17.5.2017 and decide on a comprehensive policy on night schools.
Government's decision:
A Committee is being constituted to review the Government Decision dated 17.5.2017 to formulate a comprehensive policy on night schools as under:
1. the President. Minister of School Education
2. the Vice President. Minister of State, School Education Department

Functions of the Committee:
To reconsider the Government decision dated 17.5.2017.
(ii) To lay down a comprehensive policy on night schools.
The Committee shall hold as many meetings/ hearings as may be necessary in the matter and make a comprehensive study of the night schools and submit it to the Government on its own within eight weeks.
