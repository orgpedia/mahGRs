Introduction: Cultural Education Society, Nagpur operates Kayande Primary and Upper Primary School, Badkas Chowk, T.G. The proposal received under the reference letter to grant ex-post facto approval to the relocation at Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The institution has shifted to its own building to avoid loss of life and education of the students due to the dangerous construction of the above school building. The transfer is subject to the following conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(3) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the migrated school.
(4) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(5) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 and as per the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more than that number of teachers/ non-teaching staff shall continue to be employed.
