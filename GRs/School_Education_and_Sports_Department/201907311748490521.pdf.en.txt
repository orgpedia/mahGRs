Introduction: Swanand Education Society, Chincholi Kaladat, G. Ahmednagar operated New English School, Rekhuri, Ta. The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the Marathi medium unaided secondary school, Karjat, G. Ahmednagar, to Kohinoor Vidya Prabodhini, Nashik, was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said Secondary School to Kohinoor Vidya Prabodhini, Nashik on the following terms and conditions: (1) The said School shall continue to function on the same principle of grant-in-aid even after the transfer.
(2) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the permission/ approval of the Government in the case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(4) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(5) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015 as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not to employ more teachers/ non-teaching staff than that.
