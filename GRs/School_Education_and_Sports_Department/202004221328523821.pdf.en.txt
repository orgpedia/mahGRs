Introduction- The progress of any country is not measured only on the test of economic development, but it is now measured on the basis of criteria such as education, health, employment. Also, education is a priority. Sport is an integral part of education. The progress of any country depends to a large extent on the quality of education, including sports, of that country. That is why, considering sports as the human face of the nation, international organizations have set some new standards of development of the nation. One of the important criteria is the Human Development Index (HDI) of the proposed International Sports University in Maharashtra.
International Sports University, Maharashtra The Minister of Animal Husbandry, Dairying, Sports and Youth Welfare. The meeting was held on 02.03.2020. The meeting discussed the International Sports University Maharashtra Act and the formation of a committee to make necessary suggestions. The Minister has given instructions.
Government's decision
A committee is being formed at the government level to make necessary suggestions on the International Sports University Maharashtra Act.
