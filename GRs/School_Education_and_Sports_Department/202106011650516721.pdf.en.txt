The matter of ensuring the disabled category of the posts of Group-C and Group-D employees in the office was under the consideration of the government. These positions are as follows:
Government Decision- As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities has been published as per the notification at No. 4 under the context of determining the posts under the State Government. As mentioned in the order, the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in Group A to Group D posts which are in the state government establishment and the posts in the list published by the Central Government whose duties, responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations are different, the posts in the state government service will be ensured for the disabled.
2. The posts for Group' C" and Group' D" posts in the offices under the administrative control of Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune under the School Education and Sports Department are being ensured for the disabled as shown in the statement attached with this Government decision.
