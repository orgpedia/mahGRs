The proposal received under the reference letter to transfer the permanent unaided primary school of English medium Kamthi, G. Nagpur to Tribal Social Multi-Purpose Education Institute, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said English Primary School to the Tribal Social Multi-Purpose Education Society, Nagpur on the following terms and conditions: 1. A notarized Consolidated Agreement on stamp paper of Rs.100/- must be submitted along with the signature/ signature.
(2) There has been no financial transaction between the two entities. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- with signature/ signature will be required to be submitted.
3) The conditions on which the old institution has been allowed by the government, the original conditions will not change after the transfer of the school to the new institution, in this regard, the chairman/ secretary of the new institution will have to pay Rs. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- with signature/ signature will be required to be submitted.
4) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued in this regard from time to time, a pucca building and all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- with signature/ signature will be required to be submitted.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 3018/ PR. No.369/ SM-1 5) The eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff of the school will be adjusted and their service conditions will not be changed. A notarized affidavit on a stamp paper of Rs.100/- with signature/ signature will be required to be submitted.
6) The financial position of the Tribal Social Multi-Purpose Educational Institute, Nagpur should be ensured on the basis of bank account balance and tenancy certificates.
7) It shall be mandatory for the institution to comply with the acts, rules, government decisions and orders prescribed by the government from time to time in respect of schools, teachers/ non-teaching staff, salary/ non-salary grants, students, etc.
8) As the school is a permanent English medium unaided school, the entire financial responsibility of the teachers and non-teaching staff of the school will be on the institution.
9) The condition of minimum marks will not be relaxed for any reason for the school where the management has changed.
10) The school will continue to function on the same grant-in-aid basis after the transfer.
11) There will be no change in any of the terms and conditions of government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
12) The change of management will be implemented in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No. Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated February 17, 2012.
The Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer if any complaint is received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if it is found that there is a violation of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management or if the new institution violates the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management.
2. Clause No. (1) On the completion of clauses (1) to (6), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall, after examining all the aspects and including other necessary conditions, take further action within six weeks regarding the change of management of the school and inform the Government of the action taken.
