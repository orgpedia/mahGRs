Preamble: Government Decision Social Welfare, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No. Rakridho 2002/ Q. No.93/ Kriuse-1D. As on 3rd September, 2003, the State Sports Development Fund (SSDF) is functioning at the Directorate level to develop sports facilities in the State of Maharashtra for effective promotion and development of sports culture in the State, to provide services of foreign sports mentors to quality sportspersons of the State and to provide financial assistance to international sportspersons of the State, to provide financial assistance for training and to effectively carry out sports activities. Under the scheme, for 2017-18, Rs. Rs. 50.00 crore has been allocated for this. In 2018-19, Rs. 50.00 lakhs from the Finance Department. As per the Government decision dated 2/4/2018, 70% of the limit of Rs. 35.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government for sanction of funds.
Government Decision: For the year 2018-19, Rs. 50.00 lakhs is budgeted. Of these, Rs. 35.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Five Lakhs only) is hereby approved to be disbursed and the same is placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
The expenditure in respect of the above should be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No: E-3, 2204 Sports and Youth Services-104, (35) (01) Contribution to State Sports Fund (2204, 2082), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)." The grant should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned.
