Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for students belonging to Minority Communities is being implemented in the State since 2008-09. In pursuance of the aforesaid Order at Annexure-II, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 1,47,75, 006/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Seven Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Six only) has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Out of the said amount, the Government was contemplating to disburse the budgeted amount of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) under the respective heads of accounts and to give administrative approval to the expenditure.
Government's decision:
Under the Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for the students belonging to Minority Communities, the Central Government has provided financial assistance of Rs. An amount of Rs.1, 47,75,006/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Seven Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Six only) has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Out of the said amount, the Government is approving the expenditure by disbursing the budgeted amount of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) under the respective head of accounts. The funds are being distributed to the Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State Pune. He should hand it over to the Director, Minority and Adult Education, Maharashtra State Pune.
The above funds should be disbursed immediately subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The Fund may not be used for purposes other than those for which it is provided or diverted under any other head.
