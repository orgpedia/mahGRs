Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of English Medium Permanent Unaided Primary and Upper Primary School, Learners Academy English School, Diva (East), G.Thane, Navi Mumbai, operated by Manav Vikas Shikshan Sanstha, Navi Mumbai, to Gunjan Memorial Trust, Airoli, Navi Mumbai, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Gunjan Memorial Trust, Airoli, Navi Mumbai on the following terms and conditions: (1) As per the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 and the orders issued from the Government from time to time in this regard, a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities will be provided for the school. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(2) Qualified teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer shall be accommodated and their conditions of service shall not change. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(3) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for any reason in case of change of management of the school.
(4) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(5) As the said school is permanently unaided in English medium, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(6) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
