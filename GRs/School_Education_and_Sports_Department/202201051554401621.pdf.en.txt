The Proposal:
The Finance Department has allocated Rs. A budget of Rs. 3.00 crore has been allocated for the project. Vanamati Nagpur vide her letter dated 23.01.2021 had requested for reimbursement of the cost of Rs. 111.72 lakhs for the technical training of the trainee officers of the cadre of Deputy Training Officers, Group-B under CPTP 4A, 5 and 6. Accordingly, the Government has approved the release of 40% grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 44,68,000/- (Rupees Forty Four Lakh Sixty Five Thousand only) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur under the Integrated Peripheral Training Programme 4A, 5& 6 during the financial year 2021-22 as per Government Decision dated 13.07.2021. Now the matter of disbursing the remaining 60% funds amounting to Rs. 67,04,000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Lakhs Four Thousand only) was under the consideration of the Government.
