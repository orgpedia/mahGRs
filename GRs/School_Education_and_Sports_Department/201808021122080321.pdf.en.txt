Government's decision:
To reduce the stress of the students in stress free studies and examinations, psychology, etc.
Manashakti Lab, Rest New Way, Lonavala in collaboration with selfless youth workers, doctors, engineers, educationists, thinkers, guides on various science based experiments, easy methods of study, patience during exam time, how to increase memory as well as make efforts for holistic development.
Manashakti Lab, Rest New Way, Lonavala are being given permanent permission to take this initiative from various schools of the state subject to the following conditions:
No fee will be charged from students, parents and teachers for this program. This program should be free.
2) No student will be forced to study Manashakti.
3) Principals should ensure that there is no adverse impact on the school curriculum.
4) Prior approval of the school principal will be required for this program.
5) The permission is being given to implement this initiative in all schools voluntarily.
6) The district-wise information of how many education and students have benefited from this program in the state should be submitted to the government every year.
7) If any complaint is received regarding this activity, the permission given will be cancelled.
