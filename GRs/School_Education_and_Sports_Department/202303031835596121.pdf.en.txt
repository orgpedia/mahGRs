The schemes of the Directorate of Primary Education and the Directorate of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education have been transferred to the Directorate of Education (Scheme) by making it the Directorate of Education (Scheme). Also, the office of Education Officer (Scheme) has been created in a total of 35 districts to implement the schemes of the department at the district level. In the said Government Decision dated 11.10.2022 and dt. 21.2.2023
Daily updates have been made.
The posts for the Office of the Education Officer (Scheme) as mentioned in paragraph 6 of the Government Decision dated 22.6.2022 have been approved. The posts were to be distributed district-wise. The posts are being allotted on the basis of district-wise division.

The Directorate of Minority and Adult Education has been renamed as Directorate of Education (Scheme). The office of Education Officer (Continuing Education) has also been renamed as Education Officer (Planning). The government will decide the posts required for this office. Allotments are due June 22, 2022. However, some posts in the Directorate of Native Minority and Adult Education as well as the Office of the Education Officer (Continuing Education) are originally approved, until the figure is finally revised, as follows is being decided.
(a) The 13 posts originally sanctioned on the establishment of the Directorate of Minority and Adult Education and mentioned in Form A are being sanctioned on the establishment of the Directorate of Education (Scheme) on a temporary basis.
179 posts have been transferred out of the total 269 posts sanctioned in the Office of the Education Officer (Continuing Education) with the designation of Programme Assistant/ Assistant Project Officer. The remaining 90 seats are vacant. Therefore, the 90 posts mentioned in the attached Form-B will be sanctioned on the establishment of the office of the Education Officer (Scheme) of that district until further orders.
Out of the total 52 sanctioned posts of Senior Clerk in the Office of the Education Officer (Continuing Education), 35 posts have been transferred to the Education Officer Scheme Office. The remaining 17 seats are vacant. Therefore, the 17 posts mentioned in the attached Form-A will be sanctioned on the establishment of the office of the Education Officer (Scheme) of that district until further orders.
