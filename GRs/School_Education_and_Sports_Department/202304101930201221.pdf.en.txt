Introduction: Jnana Vaibhava Educational Institute, Parali (V.V. Bhagwan Mahavir Primary School, Main Road Mukundwadi, Dist.
The proposal received under the reference letter to approve the relocation of the unaided Marathi primary and upper primary school in Aurangabad to Venkatesh Nagar, Jadhavwadi, G. Aurangabad was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Due to the dilapidated and old building of the above school, it is proposed to shift to Venkatesh Nagar, Jadhavwadi, District Aurangabad. The relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. (1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.

(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
