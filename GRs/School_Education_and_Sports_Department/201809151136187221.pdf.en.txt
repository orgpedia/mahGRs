Introduction- Every year, All India Civil Services Competitions are held in various states of the country for about 10 sports at the national level. Competitions for 1 (or 2) of these sports are held in the state of Maharashtra. The government is providing grant for the event. Accordingly, the Government of Maharashtra was contemplating to disburse the funds budgeted for the conduct of All India Civil Services Competitions for the year 2018-19 in the State.
Government Decision: All India Civil Services Competition for the year 2017-18. The budget is Rs. 25.00 crore. Of these, Rs. 17.50 lakh (Rupees Seventy Lakh Fifty Thousand only) and the same is hereby approved to be placed at the disposal of the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services.
