Government's decision:
The All India Civil Services Volleyball (Men& Women) Championship 2021-22 was held at Secretariat Gymkhana, Mumbai from 24th June to 28th June, 2022. The selection test was conducted on June 6, 2022. The list of officers and staff who participated in the test is as per the accompanying prospectus" C" and the team of the State selected through the selection test is as per prospectus" A." Five women, who could not appear for the selection trials of the volleyball (women) event due to unavoidable reasons, have expressed their willingness to participate in the competition. The women's volleyball team has been selected as a special case for this tournament. Selection Test, Coaching Camp and State Team for the event as per Form B (Men's Team) Rs. 68,300/- (Rupees Sixty Eight Thousand Three Hundred only) and as per return' B' (Mahila Sangha) Rs. 70,050/- (Rupees Seventy Thousand only). 1,38,350/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Eight Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved.
For this, the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office concerned should relieve the athletes/ coaches/ managers for the training camp from 19th to 21st June, 2022 and for the competition from 24th to 28th June, 2022 with effect from 22nd June, 2022 (M.No.) and provide the services of these athletes to the Secretariat Gymkhana. Each player and manager in the team must obtain the permission of the Head of Department/ Head of Office under whom they are working.

Government Resolution No: Abhanase-3822/ No.77/ Kriuse-2,
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1485/ Q. No. 833/ A/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance, dated May 11, 1989, and No. 1492/46/ Service-9, dated July 30, 1992, the players, coaches and managers of the team will be registered. Absence from office during the period from 19th to 28th June, 2022 (training camp, competition and travel) shall be treated as" duty period." Also, the expenditure on their travel allowance, daily allowance and other allowances should be met from the grants under the head of the head of the department/ office concerned. Also, the travel/ daily allowance and other concessions admissible to the players outside Mumbai are covered under Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE-1485/ PR No. 833/ A/ Service-9 dated 11th May, 1989 and Finance Department Government Resolution No.LVE 1404/ PR. No.8/ A/ Service-8, dated 27th February, 2004 should be approved. In order to avail the benefit of this concession, the certificate of attendance of the officers and employees appearing for the selection test issued by the Honorary Secretary, Secretariat Gymkhana, will have to be submitted by the concerned employees in their establishment office.
The absence from office of the officer/ employee (Return-C) appearing for the selection test of the competition should be treated as duty under the Government Decision, Finance Division No.-LVE/ 1492/46/ Service-9, dated 30th July, 1992.
The Secretarial Gymkhana shall submit the statement of actual expenditure to the Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of the order. In case of balance, the balance amount should be returned immediately to the Deputy Director, Sports and Youth Services, Mumbai Division, Mumbai.
The expenditure for this should be met from the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 under the head" 2204 Sports and Youth Services, 104 Sports and Games, (06) All India Civil Services Competition, (06) (02) Participation in All India Civil Services Competition (2204 1981), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)."
The Secretariat Gymkhana is approving the expenditure as per return" B" subject to fulfillment of all conditions in the report of the competition, details of actual expenditure incurred, utilization certificate, examination statement and sanction order.
