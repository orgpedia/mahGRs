Preamble: The proposal received under the Reference Letter for transfer of the Hindi Medium Aided Primary School, Nav Gyan Vikas Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur, operating as Nav Gyan Vikas Primary School, Itwari, G. Nagpur, to Lotus Cultural and Spotting Association, Nagpur, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to approve the transfer of the said school to the subject institution on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks shall not be relaxed for the school where the management is changed for any reason.
(2) The change in the name of the said school shall be ensured by the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur and the Education Officer (Primary), Zilla Parishad, Nagpur and it shall be ensured to fill simple online information accordingly.
(3) It shall be necessary to ensure that no objection is received to the transfer of the said school.
(4) The school shall be provided with a pucca building, all physical facilities and educational facilities as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.
(5) It should be ensured that the transfer of the school has the consent of the teachers/ non-teaching staff. Also, the eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff who have consented to the transfer will be accommodated and there will be no change in their service conditions. Notarized affidavit on stamp paper of Rs.100/- will be required to be submitted.

(6) The school shall continue to function after transfer on the same basis as the grant-in-aid.
(7) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding schools, it will be mandatory for the institution to employ as many teachers/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and not more than that.
(8) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(10) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(11) If any fresh complaints are received with the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding the change of management or if any breach of the terms and conditions regarding the change of management is noticed or if the service conditions of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in the institution before the change of management are violated by the new institution, the Government shall have the power to cancel the transfer.
2. Clause No. (2) On fulfilment of clauses (5) to (5), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall, after examining all the aspects and including other necessary conditions, take necessary further action regarding change of management of the school and inform the Government about the action taken.
