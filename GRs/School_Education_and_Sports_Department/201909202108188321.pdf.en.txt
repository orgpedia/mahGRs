Introduction: Shri Samarth Education Society, Sonawadi-Gajwadi, T.G. Satara operated Raosaheb Bhausaheb Wangde Secondary School, Nitral, T. G. Satara Secondary School. Khandwadi, Pt. The proposal received under the reference letter to relocate to G. Satara was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The relocation of the school has been proposed due to the migration of the people of the village due to the Urmodi project of the above school. The transfer of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions. Fee: (1) Rs. 10,000/- is required to be paid.
(2) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
(5) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
(6) As per the revised norms of accreditation in the Government Decision, dated 28/08/2015, as well as the orders issued from time to time by the Government in respect of schools, the number of teaching/ non-teaching staff as per the permissible posts and more teachers/ non-teaching staff shall be employed.

Government Decision Number: There will be no MASSHATH 3318/ PR No. 481/ SM-1, it will be mandatory on the organization and it will be mandatory to take action according to the government policy to fill the vacancies from the permissible posts with additional staff.
(7) The Government shall have the power to cancel the migration if any complaint or objection is received from the Government or the field officers of the Education Department regarding such migration or if it is found that the conditions regarding migration have been violated.
On fulfilment of the above condition number (1), the Deputy Director of Divisional Education, Kolhapur Division, Kolhapur shall take further action within 4 weeks regarding the approval of the relocation of the said school and inform the Government about the action taken.
