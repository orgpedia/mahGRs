To vote in the aforesaid biennial elections, eligible post-graduates of Nashik and Amravati divisions as well as eligible teachers of Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions should register in large numbers as graduate/ teacher voters in the respective divisions. The concerned Divisional Commissioner is the Electoral Registration Officer and the District Collector is the Assistant Electoral Registration Officer.
Educational institutions/ schools in Nashik, Amravati, Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions should take action as follows.
(a) Graduate Constituencies Since the electoral rolls are being prepared for the Graduate Legislative Council constituencies in Nashik and Amravati divisions, the eligible graduate teachers of those divisions (date of eligibility d. Candidates who have completed their graduation at least 3 years before 1-11-2022) are to be called and encouraged to register their names in the electoral rolls of the respective Graduates Constituencies.

Eligible graduate teachers of Nashik and Amravati divisions will have to fill up Form 18 (Form 18) to register their names in the electoral roll of the respective Graduate Legislative Council Constituency. These forms will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), concerned District Collector (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner and Tehsildar as designated by the Divisional Commissioner. The same is also available on the website of the Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra https:// ceo.maharashtra.gov.in at Downloads/ DownloadForms/ Form-18.pdf. The form can be downloaded from the website. The duly filled in Form 18 (FORM 18) along with necessary proof of educational qualification may be submitted to the above mentioned concerned office. If the application is submitted in person, the concerned designated officer will have to show the original degree certificate/ mark sheet or other prescribed documents. In case of sending Sample 18 by post, the proof/ documents indicating the educational qualification concerned must be attested by the Designated Officer/ Assistant Designated Officer/ Gazetted Officer/ Notary Public of the concerned district.
(b) Teachers Constituencies- As the electoral rolls for the Teachers Legislative Council constituencies are being prepared in Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions, the State Government may, with the approval of the Election Commission, allot seats in schools not lower than secondary school, as determined under Section 27 (3) (b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1950, in those divisions. Teachers who have been working as teachers for at least 3 years out of the six years immediately preceding the qualifying date 1-11-2022 should be urged and encouraged to enrol their names in the electoral rolls of the respective Teachers Constituencies.
Eligible teachers of Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions will have to fill Form 19 (FORM 19) along with the certificate issued by the head of the concerned educational institution in the prescribed form to register their names in the electoral list of the concerned Teachers Legislative Council constituency. The sample 19 and the sample of the prescribed certificate will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), the concerned District Collector (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner and Tehsildar as designated by the Divisional Commissioner. Similarly, the sample 19 and the prescribed certificate are also available on the website of the Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State at https:// ceo.maharashtra.gov.in and at Downloads/ DownloadForms/ Form- 19.pdf Downloads/ DownloadForms/ Certificate-Form- 19.pdf respectively.
