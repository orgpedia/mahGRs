Introduction: A total of six states including Maharashtra (Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Kerala) have been selected on the basis of their performance in the Performance Grading Index for the World Bank assisted Strengthening Teaching-Learning and Results for States (STARS) project.
Under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan programme, the STARS project aims to bring about fundamental reforms in the education sector at all levels, including providing quality education from pre-primary to class 12 and enhancing learning outcomes, improving teacher training, improving assessment and assessment. Strengthening and up-gradation of business education from class 6 has been included in this scheme. The project will be implemented across the state. The Cabinet has approved and in this regard, the matter of issuing a new accounting title for the Scheduled Tribes was under the consideration of the government.
