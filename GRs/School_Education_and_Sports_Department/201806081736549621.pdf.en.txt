The United Nations has declared June 21 as the International Day of Yoga.
Yoga is India's gift to the world with a tradition of more than 5000 years. Yoga helps in the physical and mental development of a person. 4th International Day of Yoga. It will be held on 21.06.2018. The programme is aimed at involving the youth and the general public. Organize yoga related programs through youth organizations like Nehru Yuva Kendra etc. Details of the International Day of Yoga are available on the website www.ayush.gov.in.
District Collectors at the district level and Divisional Commissioners at the divisional level should take the initiative to ensure the success of this programme.
The Commissioner, Sports and Youth Services should be appointed as the State Level Nodal Officer for this initiative.
3 Pratyasha Yoga Institutions have agreed to provide support to celebrate the above International Day of Yoga by providing training to trainers, guidance on organizing the Yoga Day, etc. Instructions have been issued to all the zonal offices under the School Education and Sports Department to celebrate the International Day of Yoga in all the schools of the state. The government has decided to celebrate International Yoga Day in all schools as well as government offices in the state. Yoga Day will be celebrated on June 21, 2018 in all schools of the state. Similarly, International Yoga Day should be celebrated in all government offices.
