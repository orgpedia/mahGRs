The proposal: The proposal received under the reference letter for transfer of Phoolchand Chate Marathi Madhyamik Vidyalaya, Dhankawadi, Pune, run by Udhavrao Tulsiram Jadhavar Foundation, Pune, to Ninaidevi Shikshan Sanstha, Dhankawadi, Pune, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to approve the transfer of the said school to Ninaidevi Educational Institutions, Dhankawadi, Pune on the following terms and conditions: (1) The condition of minimum marks of the school where the management has changed will not be relaxed on any ground.
(2) The school shall continue to function after the transfer on the same basis as permitted.
(3) As the said school is a self-financing Marathi medium school, the entire responsibility of the teachers/ non-teaching staff working in the school shall be on the institution.
(4) Care should be taken to ensure that there is no change in any of the terms and conditions of the Government permission/ approval in case of a school where there is a change of management.
The new institution shall be bound to comply with the Acts, Rules, Orders, etc., as may be prescribed in this behalf from time to time.
(6) The said change of management shall be effected in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed in the Government Decision, School Education and Sports Department, No: Mashah 2011/ (210/11) Mashi-3, dated 17th February, 2012.
(7) As the said school is a self-financing school, the existing land for the said school, as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2012, shall be in the name of Ninaidevi Shikshan Sanstha, Dhankavadi, Pune or it shall be acquired by this institution (3 years of the previous institution out of 30 years period).
