The Proposal:
Grants to Municipal Corporations and Municipalities for expenditure on primary education through School Education Department. By amending the Governing Decision of 16/12/1987, vide reference No. (1) The decision of the Government, d. The revised order was issued on July 7, 2015. The reimbursement of expenditure on approved items of elementary education to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Municipal Councils/ Boards in the State is admissible by the State Government as per the formula prescribed under this Government decision.
The proposal submitted by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation has been examined by the Divisional Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai and the permissible grant amount has been determined after the examination.
3. The total amount of compensation to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation as reimbursement of wage subsidy for the first, second and third instalments for the financial years 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 as recommended for expenditure on elementary education. 191,98,95, 370/- (Rupees Ninety One Crore Eighty Nine Lakh Ninety Five Thousand Three Hundred and Seventy only) is admissible. Also, the allowable wage subsidy of private primary schools for the previous period.

The total amount of reimbursement of outstanding subsidy which is the difference between reimbursement and actual amount paid is Rs. 5,63,20, 379/- (Rupees Five Crore Sixty Three Lakh Twenty Thousand Three Hundred and Nineteen only) is admissible. Accordingly, the admissible wage subsidy for the 1st, 2nd and 3rd instalments for the year 2018-19 to 2022-23 and the outstanding wage subsidy for the previous few years aggregating to Rs. 197,62,15, 749/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ninety Seven Crores Sixty Two Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Seven Hundred and Nineteen only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As recommended for expenditure on elementary education to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, the allowable wage subsidy for the first, second and third instalments for the year 2018-19 to 2022-23 and the outstanding wage subsidy for the previous few years, a total of Rs. 197,62,15, 749/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ninety Seven Crores Sixty Two Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Seven Hundred and Nineteen Fifty Five only) is admissible.
Under the respective heads, the Budget Estimate for 2022-23 is Rs. 34,22,40, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Four Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Forty Thousand only) has been approved and the same is retained in the revised estimates.
Accordingly, the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has been provided with a salary grant of Rs. 34,22,40, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Four Crores Twenty Two Lakhs Forty Thousand only) towards expenditure on elementary education as per" Demand No. E-2, 2202 General Education, 01 Elementary Education, 191, Assistance to Local Bodies (01) Financial Assistance to other Local Bodies (01) Grants to Municipal Corporation of Mumbai (Compulsory) (2202 0191) 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries)" are hereby approved to be paid out of the sanctioned provision for the year 2022-23 under the heading" Grant-in-Aid (Compulsory) to Municipal Corporations of Mumbai."
For this purpose, Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai Division, Mumbai is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and Commissioner (Education), Maharashtra State, Pune as" Controlling Officer."
4. The said Government Decision was issued by the Department of Finance in its Informal Reference No. 357/ Expenditure- 5, D. being issued as per the agreement dated 27.03.2023.
