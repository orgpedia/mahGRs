S.S. Baddapure Marathi Vidyalaya, Solapur, a Marathi medium aided primary (class 1 to class 7) school run by Baddapure Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Vagdari, Ta.
The proposal received under the reference letter for grant of ex-post facto approval for relocation to Akkalkot, Dist. Solapur was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As the building of the above school has become dilapidated and dangerous, and as the notice of the M.N.P. He has been shifted to Akkalkot, Solapur. Therefore, the relocation of the said school to the above mentioned place is being approved on the following terms and conditions.
(1) The condition of minimum marks of the migrated school shall not be relaxed for any reason.
All students must be accommodated at the transfer location.
(2) The school shall continue to function at the place of relocation on the same basis as it was functioning at the place of origin.
The organization shall be required to comply with the laws, rules, orders, etc. prescribed from time to time in this regard.
(4) There shall be no change in any of the terms and conditions of the original Government permission and approval in respect of the school.
