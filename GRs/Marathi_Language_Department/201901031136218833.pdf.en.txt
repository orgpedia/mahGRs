The Proposal:
The Government was contemplating to announce Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2018 to the eminent litterateur who has done substantial and valuable work in the field of Marathi literature/ literature and Shri P. Bhagwat Puraskar for the year 2018 to the outstanding publishing house who have made significant achievements in the field of Marathi literature production.
Government's decision:
Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for outstanding contribution in the field of Marathi literature. As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the Government Decision of 2018, the Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2018 has been conferred on veteran litterateur" Mahesh Elkunchwar" and for the publication house which has made significant achievements in the production of Marathi literature," Shri Pu Bhagavata Puraskar" has been conferred. As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the Government Resolution No. 2, the Shri P. Bhagwat Award for the year 2018 is being announced to the" Sahitya Prasar Kendra, Nagpur."
