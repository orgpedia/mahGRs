Government's decision:
The day is celebrated to commemorate the birth anniversary of former President of India Dr APJ Abdul Kalam. On the occasion of Reading Inspiration Day, the government has taken a decision to increase awareness about Marathi among the Marathi/ Amarathi people. The competition was held as per the government decision of November 3, 2022. The results of the competition were announced.
