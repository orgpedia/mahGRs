The Marathi Language Day is observed every year in all government and private offices, boards, corporations, public sector undertakings, banks, educational institutions, universities, colleges, etc. in the state. It is celebrated from January 14 to January 28.
In line with the main policy of the Department of" Promotion and Expansion of Marathi Language," instructions are given to organize various programs every year to celebrate the" Marathi Language Promotion Fortnight." Accordingly, this year also, the government was considering to instruct all the offices to organize various programs related to Marathi language in the Marathi Language Conservation Fortnight.
Government circular:
Instructions are being given to all the offices to celebrate the Marathi Language Preservation Fortnight by organizing appropriate programs as follows:
(a) All the offices of the State:
Organizing various seminars/ lectures/ workshops/ camps/ poets' meet/ mono-dramas/ plays/ book release function/ literary award distribution related to Marathi language and literature.
2) Essay/ Quiz/ Story-telling/ Story-writing/ Poetry-writing/ Handwriting/ Reflections/ Speech/ Slogans/ Speech/ Debate/ Antakshari/ Dictionary/ Interpretation/ Review/ Paragraph Translation etc.
To organize guidance, seminars, workshops by eminent persons/ writers/ speakers specializing in Marathi language to introduce the classic books of Marathi language with a view to promote Marathi reading culture.

According to the Maharashtra Official Language Act, 1964, 100% of the government business must be done in Marathi, except for prohibited purposes. According to the three-language formula, Marathi is mandatory to be used in all central government offices/ public undertakings in the state.
Therefore, all the district collectors should take various initiatives through the District Marathi Language Committee regarding the promotion and dissemination of Marathi language and bring the orders, rules, and acts issued by the government from time to time to the notice of the concerned.
