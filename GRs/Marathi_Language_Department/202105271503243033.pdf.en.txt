Introduction: A total of 31 members, including the president and 30 members associated with different fields, have been appointed to the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board. The 2. The appointment was made under the Government Order dated 05.03.2019 for a period of three years or until further orders of the Government. Dilip Karambelkar has resigned from the post of president of the Maharashtra Congress. This has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 30.07.2020. Accordingly, the proposal to reconstitute the board was under the consideration of the government.
The Government are pleased to approve the appointment of the following Chairman and Members on the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board for a period of 3 years from the date of this order or until further orders of the Government.
