The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Marathi Language
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. SRIKH- 2019/ PR No. 04/ BUDGET
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mantralaya, Mumbai-032
Date: 16.11.2019
See: Government Resolution, Revenue and Forest Department, No. 2016/ P.No.80 (Part-II)/ F-11, dated 25.7.2018.
Introduction: The Revenue and Forest Department has taken up a massive tree plantation programme to combat the adverse effects of climate change. As per the Government Resolution dated 25.7.2018 of the Department of Revenue and Forest, the Planning and Finance Department has agreed to make financial provision for" Tree Plantation and Maintenance" within the limit of 0.5% of the total budgetary allocation of all the administrative departments from the expenditure on the programme.
Accordingly, the opening of a new title for the programme" Tree Plantation and Nurturing" of the Marathi Language Department was under consideration.
Government's decision:
As per the approval given by the Department of Planning and Finance to the scheme" Tree Plantation and Maintenance," approval is being given to open new account titles as follows.
