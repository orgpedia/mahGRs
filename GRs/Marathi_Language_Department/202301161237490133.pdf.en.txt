Government circular:
""" To preserve the glory of the Marathi language and to promote the Marathi language, the Government of India is celebrating"""" Marathi Bhasha Samvardhan Pakhwada"""" from 14th January to 28th January, 2023.""" The instructions have been issued vide circular dated 11.11.2022. In line with that. The following competitions are being organized for the officers/ staff of the 4 offices under the Ministry and Marathi Language Department from 23.01.2023 to 25.01.2023
Competitions on the following four topics are being organized on the occasion of Marathi Language Promotion Fortnight. Students can read any subject in Marathi.
1. patriotism.
2. any part of the story
3.A few articles on language for me
Parts of the historical book
The officers/ staff of the Ministry and the subordinate offices of the Department who wish to participate in the above-mentioned competition should submit their names. Send e-mail to aarti. sane@nic.in latest by 19.01.2023. Contestants must be in attendance at the conference.
All the participants of the competition were present in the conference hall of the Ministry. The physical bidding competition will be held on 24.01.2023 from 2 pm to 5 pm. The first, second and third prize winners will get Rs. 3000/-, 2500/- and 1500/- and two incentives of Rs. 1000/- will be awarded.

Competition rules are as follows:
Contestants can choose to read any of the above-mentioned topics. Contestants can submit their entries. But it cannot be a play or a performance. The duration of the test will be 5 to 7 minutes. As the textual performance of the contestant is of paramount importance, elements such as colour, hairstyle, background music, etc. cannot be used.
The judges' decision will be final.
Officers/ staff of all Ministries/ Departments and Subordinate Offices of the Department of Marathi Language are requested to participate in the challenge.
Apart from this, the following programs have been organized for the promotion and propagation of Marathi language.
2)" Chalta-Balta"- the program in question was held on Monday at the Trimurti premises of the Ministry. On 23.01.2023, from 11 am to 4.00 pm, a quiz programme on Marathi proverbs, idioms, alternative Marathi words for English words and questions based on them and Marathi literature/ literary will be organized. Those who participate in this competition and give the correct answer will be immediately rewarded.
3. a comedy show:
Smt. Deepali Kelkar's programme" Hasya Sanjeevani" is scheduled on 25th January, 2023 from 4.00 pm to 5.15 pm at Trimurti Auditorium, Mantralaya.
The rules set by the government to control the Covid epidemic will be applicable for this competition.
