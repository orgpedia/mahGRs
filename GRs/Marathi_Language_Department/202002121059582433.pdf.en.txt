The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 18th February, 2016, it has been decided to confer the" Bhasha Samvardhan Puraskar" by the State Marathi Development Agency to a person or institution who has made valuable contribution for the promotion of Marathi language. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to announce the name of Dr. Ashok Kelkar, a senior linguist and literary critic of international repute, as" Marathi Bhasha Adhyaksh Puraskar, 2019" and the name of poet Mangesh Padgaonkar as" Marathi Bhasha Sanvardhak Puraskar, 2019."
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the decision of the Government in the context of conferring the Marathi Language Preservation Award, the award for the year 2019 will be conferred on Dr. The award was given to Anil Gore, the Marathi language activist, and poet Mangesh Padgaonkar.
