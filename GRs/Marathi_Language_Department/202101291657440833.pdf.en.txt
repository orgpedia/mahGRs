Government order
The post of" Divisional Assistant Director of Languages" in the Directorate of Languages, Divisional Office, Navi Mumbai is lying vacant since 27.05.2016. The additional charge of the said post has been given to other officers from time to time. Reference No. The Government has approved to continue the additional charge of the said vacant post for a period of one year beyond 27.05.2019 or till the post is filled up in due course, whichever is earlier. This period has ended on 26.5.2020.
Reference No. 2. 3 In accordance with the letter of the Directorate of Languages, the Government hereby approve the continuation of the additional charge of the post of" Divisional Assistant Director of Languages" in the Office of the Directorate of Languages, Divisional Office, Navi Mumbai for a period of one year beyond 27.05.2020 or till the said post is filled up in the prescribed manner, whichever is earlier.
