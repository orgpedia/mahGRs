Government circular:
The birth anniversary of poet and freedom fighter Subramania Bharathi is celebrated as' Indian Languages Day." The initiative aims to celebrate the diversity of Indian languages and connect with the language by encouraging the youth.
In this context, it is mentioned that" Indian Language Day" is a great concept to promote awareness of mother tongue. Through this concept, the language can be propagated in various mediums. In view of the above, it was contemplated to issue instructions to all the field offices under the Ministry to celebrate" Bhartiya Bhasha Diwas" as mentioned above.
2. Keeping in view the objective of the" Bhartiya Bhasha Diwas" initiative, various activities are to be organized by all the Ministries/ Departments and all the Government/ Semi-Government offices, Government Institutions/ Boards, Public Undertakings, etc. The objectives/ activities are as follows:
(a) This year. It is targeted to cover one crore students by 11.12.2023 by celebrating 75 days of celebrations at all levels in schools, colleges and other institutions.
The following activities are expected to take place during the program:
The festival will have various cultural programmes, including art, craft, food, folk dance, drama, essay writing and poetry recitation.

3. The following general suggestions are being given as to what programmes should be organized to celebrate" Indian Language Day" this year.
(1) The programme shall be organised in accordance with the activities mentioned in paragraph 2, taking into account the various types of messages, lectures, seminars, invitations, group readings, book exhibitions, etc., as well as other linguistic innovations.
2) The event should be conducted through social media/ physical mode (online/ offline)
So that the scope of the program can be expanded and the message of' Indian Language Day" can be spread everywhere at the same time.
3) In line with" Indian Language Day," everyone should encourage our friends, family members, etc. by gifting them at least one book to promote" Reading Culture."
On the occasion of" Bhartiya Bhasha Diwas," programmes on various themes and other such forms should be organized to promote" Reading Culture" and to attract more youth through employment oriented activities with the objective of promotion and propagation of mother tongue.
5) Through social media platforms like WhatsApp, Internet, Facebook, Twitter, etc., the message that" Bhartiya Bhasha Diwas" will enhance culture should be spread.
6) All institutions should organize" Virtual/ Live Literary Conference of Indian Languages" at their level.
7) Workshops should be organized so that there is more writing on mother tongue on media like Wikipedia.
