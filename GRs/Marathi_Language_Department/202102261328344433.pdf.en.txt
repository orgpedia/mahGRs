3) Marathi Language Department Government Resolution No. 2021/ No. 10/ Language-3, D. 26th February, 2021 Preamble: As per the decision of the Government dated 18th February, 2016, it has been decided to confer the" Bhasha Samvardhan Puraskar" to an individual or institution who has made valuable contribution for the promotion of Marathi language through the State Marathi Development Agency.
Under reference As per the decision of the Government dated 26 th February, 2021, it has been decided to confer this award separately to individuals and institutions from this year.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to announce the name of Dr. Ashok Kelkar, a senior linguist and literary critic of international repute, as" Marathi Language Scholar Award, 2020" and the name of poet Mangesh Padgaonkar as" Marathi Language Preserver Award, 2020."
Government Decision: As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the decision of the Government in the context of conferring the Marathi Language Preservation Award, the award for the year 2020 will be given to Dr. Ashok Kelkar, Marathi Language Scholar Award" Dr. Sudhir Rasal is being announced.
The award was also given to Sanjay Janardan Bhagat and Marathi Sahitya Parishad, Andhra Pradesh.
2) Each award carries a cash prize of Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs only) in cash, a memento and a certificate.
3. The said Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. Anusankra- 133/2021/ Expenditure-4, Dy. being issued pursuant to the approval received under 26.2.2021.
