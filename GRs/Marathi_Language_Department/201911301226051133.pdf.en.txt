Terms and Conditions
1) Candidate must be a citizen of India and must have at least 4th pass. He can read, write and speak Marathi fluently.
The age of the candidate should be between 18 to 40 years.
3) Candidate must have a valid Aadhar Card/ Voter ID/ PAN Card.
4) The personnel provided by the organization should not be of criminal background or tendency. The self-declaration in this regard will have to be submitted by the candidate as well as the concerned institution.
5) The Government will not be responsible for any medical expenses or compensation in case of serious injury to the candidate. It will be the responsibility of the concerned authorities.
6) Reference No. 05 As per the provisions mentioned in the Government Circular dated 22.02.2019 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, it will be mandatory for the organization to pay the salary as per the salary determined by the Government as well as the taxes and allowances applicable from time to time. The statutory financial liability payable to the manpower provided is included in the salary and the organization will be responsible for depositing the statutory liability with the concerned authority. As per the demand, it will be mandatory for the institution to submit the documents to the Marathi Language Department from time to time.
The Marathi language department will have no responsibility in this regard.
7) In case the applicant is not available for the period demanded, alternative arrangements should be made by the institution with acceptable conditions. Otherwise, a fine will be imposed.
The working hours of each day will be from 9.30 am to 6.00 pm. (8.30 p.m.) For this, the concerned candidate will be required to record the time of his coming to the office and leaving the office in the register to be kept in the Establishment Branch of the Marathi Language Department. Apart from this, allowance will be paid at the rate mentioned in the government decision for additional work.
9) Rs. A one-year contract with the government on a stamp paper of Rs 200/- will be required.
10) The candidate will be requisitioned for a specific period as per the requirement of the government.
11) Holidays will be permissible as per government rules. Also, if necessary, you can go to work on holidays.
12) Termination of the contract at any time if the performance of the contract is not satisfactory. The final decision regarding the extension of the contract period to one year (if the government deems it necessary) will be taken by the Marathi Language Department.
The expenditure for the above purpose will be met from the grant sanctioned under the head Demand No.ZF-01, 2052-Secretariat General Service, (090) Secretariat General Service, (090) Secretariat (00) (01) Marathi Language Department, (13) Office Expenditure (2052 5054).
