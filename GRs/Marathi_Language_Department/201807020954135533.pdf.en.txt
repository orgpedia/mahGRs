Government has started a separate department of Marathi language since 2011 with a view to develop and promote Marathi language. Under the aegis of this Department, four offices namely (i) Directorate of Languages, Mumbai (ii) Rajya Marathi Vikas Sanstha, Mumbai (iii) Maharashtra Rajya Sahitya and Sanskriti Mandal, Mumbai (iv) Maharashtra Rajya Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai are functioning. At present, these offices are functioning at different locations in Mumbai and are required to be located in one building to coordinate the activities and work of these offices. For this, the main center of Marathi Bhasha Bhavan in Mumbai area. A decision has been taken to form a separate committee under the chairmanship of the Minister of Marathi Language. A committee has been formed in this regard.
