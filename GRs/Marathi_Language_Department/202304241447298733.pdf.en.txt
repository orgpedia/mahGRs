Terms and Conditions: 1) The above candidates are being appointed on temporary basis subject to medical examination and antecedent verification (within six months).
(2) Caste validity certificate as required and non-criminal (non-criminal) certificate in case of non-criminal category shall be submitted within a period of six months from the date of this order. If these certificates are not fulfilled within a period of six months, their appointment will be automatically terminated. The appointment is being made subject to the completion of the above certificates within the stipulated time.
(3) The above candidates are required to complete the probation period of two years from the date of their appointment. In case they do not achieve the expected standard of work during this probation period or their conduct is found to be unsatisfactory, they will be eligible to terminate their services as Assistant Director of Languages (Translation and Terminology) without any prior notice.
4) All the terms and conditions mentioned in the government decision will be applicable to the candidate appointed to this post during the probation period.

5) The seniority of the above candidate in the post of Assistant Language Director (Translation and Vocabulary) will be as per the provisions of the prescribed rules and according to the merit number of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(6) The above candidate should join the post of his appointment in the Directorate of Languages within one month from the date of this order. Candidates should note that if they do not join the post of appointment within this period, their appointment orders will be cancelled without any prior notice.
If they need an extension to join for some unavoidable, reasonable reason within the prescribed period, they must seek an extension from the government, and if they do not take such an extension or do not get such an extension from the government, they will not get seniority as per the Maharashtra Public Service Commission's scorecard and their seniority will be taken into account from the date they appear. The candidates who do not join the service within the prescribed period and do not take extension of time to appear, will be deemed to have cancelled their appointment orders automatically on the ground that they are not willing for appointment.
7) If the above-mentioned candidate wants to resign from the government service for any reason after the appointment, he must give a written notice to the government one month in advance.
Otherwise, they will have to deposit one month's basic salary with the government.
The above candidate is required to submit the affidavit in the prescribed Form" A" as per Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Small Family Affidavit) Rules, 2005.
9) The candidate must have been appointed by the Finance Department, Government Decision No. Anniyo- 1005/126/ Service-4, Dy. The scheme of" Defined Contribution Pension" will continue to be applicable as per the provisions of 31st October, 2005.
The above candidates are eligible for transfer from Directorate of Languages (HQ, Mumbai) to the Divisional Offices.
11) The above candidates have passed the Shalaant Certificate Examination, Marathi (Higher Level) and Hindi along with these subjects, they are exempted from passing the Marathi and Hindi language examination of the Board.
12) The above candidate has the computer qualification prescribed by the government.
13) The above-mentioned candidates are required to strictly follow the rules of conduct prescribed for government officers/ employees.
14) It is mandatory for the above candidate to complete the training prescribed by the government from time to time.
The above candidate must declare his/ her native place within six months from the date of his/ her appointment. After that, if they declare their home village, the home village cannot be changed during the entire service period.
