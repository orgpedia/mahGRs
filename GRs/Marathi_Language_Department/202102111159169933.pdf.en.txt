The Proposal:
The Government was contemplating to announce Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2020 to the eminent litterateur who has done substantial and valuable work in the field of Marathi literature/ literature and Shri P. Bhagwat Puraskar for the year 2020 to the outstanding publishing house who has done remarkable work in the field of Marathi literature production.
Government's decision:
In order to confer Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar on a noted litterateur for his outstanding performance in the field of Marathi literature and Shri P. Bhagwat Puraskar on a publishing house for its remarkable performance in producing Marathi literature, Reference No. The Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2020 has been conferred on Shri. The Bhagwat Award is being given to' Shabdalaya Prakashan," Shrirampur, G. Ahmednagar.
