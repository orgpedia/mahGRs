Mr. A.V. Gite, Assistant Director of Languages (Translation and Terminology), Directorate of Languages, has been appointed as Assistant Director of Languages (Translation and Terminology) in the 7th Pay Commission. The post of Deputy Director of Languages (Law) Group-A in the pay scale of Rs. 56100 177500 is being given only temporary promotion from the date of this order and after promotion, he is being posted to a vacant post in the Directorate of Languages (Headquarters), Mumbai.
The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015. Against the judgment dated 04.08.2017, the State Govt. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. In this regard, the General Administration Department/ Office No. 16B of No. BCC-2017/ Q.No.312A/ 16B, Dy. As per the instructions issued vide letter dated 29.12.2017, the vacancies in the open category in the quota of promotion will be filled only on temporary basis and as per the merit of the service. Subject to the decision of the Supreme Court, the Government at an earlier stage decided to provide the Backward Class employees in the service merit list of the category of Assistant Director of Languages (Translation and Terminology). Provisional promotion under consideration has been granted excluding the employees who have been placed in the top of the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provision of 25.05.2004. In addition, the promotion is subject to the following conditions:
1.The said promotion shall be purely temporary in nature as an administrative exigency.
(2) They shall not be entitled to any regular promotion or promotion.
