Preamble: Under the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
" Revised Manual of Procedure for Purchase of Offices by Government Departments" has been issued. Para 2.9.3 of the said rule book provides for constitution of the Ministerial Administrative Department (itself) Procurement Committee. Considering this, the government was considering the formation of a purchase committee, the administrative department of the ministry (itself), to purchase the necessary items for the Marathi language department.
Government Decision: The above No. 2 of this section is here. The Office Orders dated 18.03.2016 are being superseded. It is now the Department of Energy and Labor. Procedure for procurement of offices by Government Departments attached with the Government Decision dated 1.12.2016
