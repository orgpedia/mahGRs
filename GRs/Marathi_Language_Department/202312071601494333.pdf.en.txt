Specifications were sought from the Directorate of Information and Technology for the purchase of computers and scanners for the officers/ employees of the department. After receiving this specification from the Directorate of Information and Technology, the department's Divisional Procurement Committee met on Tuesday. Taken in the Deputy Secretary's chamber on October 10, 2023. The meeting was attended by the president and the vice-president. It was decided that the computers and scanners required by the department should be purchased from the Government-e Marketplace at the lowest rate.
