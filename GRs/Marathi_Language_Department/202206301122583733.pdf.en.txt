Government order
The post of" Secretary," Group-A cadre in the office of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai, under the Department, is lying vacant since 01.03.2018 as the recruitment is under adjudication. The additional charge of this post has been given to other officers from time to time. Reference No. The approval granted for the additional charge of the said vacancy under Section 2 has expired on 28.02.2022.
In view of the above, the Government hereby approve the continuation of the post of" Secretary," Group-A in the office of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai for a further period of 6 months with effect from 01.03.2022 in additional charge or till the said post is filled up as per rules, whichever is earlier.
