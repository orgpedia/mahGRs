Government's decision:
Department of Marathi Language. Shri Madhav Rajguru, Member of the Marathi Certification Fixation Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 29.09.2021, has written a letter to Shri. On 22.02.2022, I have violated my privacy by writing an article regarding standard writing in Marathi language. Therefore, the membership of Shri Madhav Rajguru in the Marathi Certification Committee is being cancelled.
