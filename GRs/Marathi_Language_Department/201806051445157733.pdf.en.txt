Introduction: To promote Marathi language, every year from 1st to 15th January is celebrated as" Marathi Bhasha Samvardhan Pakhwada" in the state. Also, the. Marathi Language Pride Day is celebrated on 27 February and Reading Inspiration Day on 15 October. During this period, seminars, workshops, lectures by experts, book exhibitions, storytelling, poetry reading, documentary, elocution, writing competitions etc. will be organized across the state and other innovative initiatives for the development of Marathi language will be launched. As per the Government decision dated 16.01.2016, a separate head of accounts for Marathi Language Department (itself) vide Demand No. " Marathi Language Promotion and Outreach Programme (2052 5232)" has been created under ZF 01. However, due to non-availability of all the necessary expenditures required by the department under the target of" 50 other expenses" under this head, it was not possible to make payments from this head even after spending for the promotion of Marathi language. Therefore, the Government was considering including 11 more items under this head with the objective of" 50 other expenses."
Government Decision: To promote and propagate Marathi language. 1st to 15th January), Marathi Language Pride Day (d. 27th February is the Reading Inspiration Day. 15th October) and language studies/ research/ organizing programmes in memory of veteran litterateurs. Under ZF-01, 11 more targets have been included under the head of Marathi Language Promotion and Outreach Programme (2052 5232) with the target of" 50 other expenditures"
