Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra has awarded the best Marathi literature in the Marathi language. The Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award scheme is in operation. Under this scheme, books of different literary genres are accepted from writers who have produced literature in Marathi language in the state and outside the state. Marathi literature produced/ first published in 2017. September 10, 2012 and d. As of February 2, 2016. The proposal to announce the Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award-2017 was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Marathi literature produced/ first published in the year 2017 will be published by Swat. This Government has decided to declare the names of 32 writers/ litterateurs who have been declared eligible for the award as per the result of the Screening Committee constituted for conferring the Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award as per the list in the prospectus attached with the award and to confer the award to the eligible litterateurs as per the decision of the Government dated 10th September, 2012 and 2nd February, 2016, respectively.
