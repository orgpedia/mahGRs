in the service of the Government in accordance with the rules, 10 (4) and 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services Pension) Rules 1982
In accordance with the policy of the Government to review the eligibility of officers and employees to remain in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service as per the prescribed criteria, the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board under the Marathi Language Department is reconstituting the following committee in accordance with the Government's policy in reference 1 above to review and recommend the services of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees who are eligible for review in the office.
