Date: February 15, 2022
Read: 1) Decision to be taken by the government 29.09.2021
Preamble: The" Committee for Determination of Scripts" was constituted to reconsider the issues of script, alphabet etc. regarding Marathi language. It has been established under the Government Decision dated 29.09.2021. The committee will need more time to submit its report along with the bill on fixing Marathi certification. Therefore, the government was considering an extension of six months for the committee to submit its report along with the bill on fixing Marathi certification.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the extension of the term of the" Certification Committee" appointed to reconsider the matters of standardization, alphabetization etc. regarding Marathi language from 29th December, 2021 to 6 months i. e. 28th June, 2022.
