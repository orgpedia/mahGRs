The Government has decided to celebrate 15th October, the birth anniversary of former President of India Late Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam as" Reading Inspiration Day" with an aim to preserve his memory. In pursuance of this decision, instructions were issued earlier through the relevant Government Circulars to encourage reading and develop the love of reading among school and college students. Similarly, this year also, to mark the Reading Inspiration Day, all Ministries/ Departments and all Government/ Semi-Government Offices under them, Government Institutions/ Boards, Public Undertakings, etc.
The offices are also being instructed to organize various activities as follows.
Government/ semi-government offices, schools should organize the program on 15th October, every officer/ employee as well as school students, teachers and non-teaching staff should devote at least half an hour during working hours for reading, the group should sit together for reading and spend a few hours continuously for reading as a" reading habit."
2. To nurture the reading culture, everyone should gift at least one book to their friends, family members etc.
