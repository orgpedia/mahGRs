The Proposal:
V. V. Shirwadkar alias Kusumagraj has made a significant contribution to the cultural field of Maharashtra and has worked tirelessly to make Marathi language a language of knowledge. As a mark of respect to our mother tongue, instructions have been given earlier under the Government Decision dated 21st January, 2013 to celebrate the birthday of V. V. Shirwadkar alias Kusumagraj on 27th February as" Marathi Bhasha Gaurav Din." In pursuance of the above decision, all the Ministries/ Departments of the State Government and all the Heads of Offices under them are being informed to celebrate the" Marathi Bhasha Gaurav Divas" on Sunday, the 27 th February, 2022 in a befitting manner by organizing the following activities.
2. While the event of" Marathi Bhasha Gaurav Din" is being celebrated along with other Marathi language outreach programmes of" Marathi Vachan Prerna Din" and" Marathi Bhasha Samvardhan Padhwada" in line with the main policy of promotion and propagation of Marathi language, instructions have been given earlier to celebrate more and more programmes online as a preventive measure due to the current outbreak of COVID-19. (Wherever it is possible to hold live events, the Covid guidelines laid down by the Central Government/ State Government should be strictly followed.) )
The promotion/ propagation of Marathi language is the main policy of Marathi Language Department. In line with this policy, along with the traditional way of propagation/ dissemination, the following programs will be organized for the promotion/ propagation of Marathi language using various mediums by combining it with modern technology:
