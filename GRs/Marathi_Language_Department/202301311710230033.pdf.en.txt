Preamble: Reference No. Under the decision of the government here, approval has been given to set up the Knowledge Boards for 3 years. Reference No. 2 decision of the government. The period has been extended up to 22.02.2022. As per the guidance of the Chairman, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, changes are proposed in the prevailing working of the Board. But, it's going to take some time. Therefore, the government was considering extending the existing activities of the Knowledge Board so that the work of the Encyclopedia does not get interrupted and the payments for the works yet to be received by the Knowledge Boards are transferred from the office of the Grants and Accounts Officer and paid to the concerned.
Government Decision: Various Knowledge Boards have been set up for subject-wise upgradation of Marathi Encyclopedia as well as for taking notes of new concepts, new streams of thought in various disciplines.
The Encyclopaedia is published on the website of the Board by the Office of the Board after examining the records received from the Knowledge Boards. However, changes are expected in the near future.
Therefore, the period of extension of Gyan Mandalas from 23.02.2022 to 22.02.2023 is being extended. In accordance with this extension, 4 posts of Assistant and 1 sanctioned for the work of Knowledge Board
