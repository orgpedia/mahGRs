The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board is engaged in the editing of children's encyclopaedias and Kumar's encyclopaedias along with the editing of encyclopaedias in Marathi language. For the writing and editing of the entries in the Marathi Encyclopedia volume, the assistance of experts/ writers from different subjects is taken as a visiting editor. As it is not technically convenient to appoint permanently the experts required for the work of the Encyclopedia as editors, the reference No. Under the decision of the government here, it is in the process of taking the services of senior professors of university and college as well as senior professors, researchers, etc. experts in some technical subjects for the work of writing and editing the entries in the Marathi Encyclopedia volume. Accordingly, the No. 4 decision of the government. from April 1, 2018. It was decided to continue the practice of hiring the services of Visitor Editors for various subjects till March 31, 2021. Since the work of encyclopaedia is still in progress, the government was considering to continue the practice of hiring the services of visiting editors. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:

Government Resolution No: Exchange-2021/ PR No.114/ Language-2
Government Decision: As per the powers given to the Secretary, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai, the services of senior professors, researchers, editors etc. of various universities and colleges in connection with the editing of Marathi Encyclopedia will be taken as" Visiting Editor" for writing and other editorial work at Encyclopedia Production Board Office, Wai, G. Satara. from April 1, 2021. Government approval is being accorded for continuation of the scheme during the period up to 31st March, 2022 subject to the conditions mentioned below:
At No. 1: While submitting the expenditure proposal, the detailed details of the total expenditure should be made available to the government on the basis of rules/ orders.
At No. 2: These services should be taken on a temporary basis and the honorarium of the visiting editors should be paid according to the rates prescribed by the government for the services.
The Secretary, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopaedia Production Board, Mumbai shall plan the works of the Visiting Editor in such a way that the above conditions are complied with and the necessary criteria are fixed in this regard.
For this purpose, the Secretary, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Assistant Accounts Officer as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and they are being approved to sign the payment in this regard.
The expenditure for this should be shared as per the availability of monthly funds from the sanctioned provision under both the headings of Demand No.ZF-02, Principal Heading-2205-Art and Culture, 102 Art and Culture Operation, (01) (02)-Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, (22053181) Post-Plan-16 Publications and ZF-02 Principal Heading 2205 Art and Culture 102 Art and Culture Operation (02) (03) Development Programme Scheme of Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board (2205 3223) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Pay Matters).
