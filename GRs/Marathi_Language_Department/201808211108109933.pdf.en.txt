Preamble: The Government has been providing a grant of Rs. 10,000 per annum from the financial year 2002-2003 for the conduct of Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan organized by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Nigam. 25.00 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) was being sanctioned. There was a demand from the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Parishad as well as the president of the conference and the people's representatives to increase the grant. In this background, the amount of financial assistance given by the government for the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan to be organized by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Nigam has been doubled to Rs. The proposal for sanction of financial assistance to the extent of Rs. 50.00 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, it is hereby approved to sanction financial assistance to the extent of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) for organizing the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan to be organized by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Mahasangh every year at the proposed venue of the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan subject to the following terms and conditions.

(1) The All India Marathi Sahitya Parishad shall submit a proposal to the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture for providing financial assistance to the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan.
(2) The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture may provide financial assistance to the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan only after the Board receives a proposal from the All India Marathi Sahitya Mahasangh. 50.00 lakh should be disbursed to the All India Marathi Sahitya Mahasangh.
(3) The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture shall ensure that in the event of the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan not being organised by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Nigam in any year, the provision sanctioned for the same shall not be used elsewhere without the approval of the Government.
(4) The Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board shall deposit an amount of the financial assistance made available to the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan in the account of the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Mahamandal before Dussehra every year. In this regard, the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board should take care to request the details of the proposed conference from the All India Marathi Literary Corporation in advance.
(5) The financial assistance being made available by the Government of Maharashtra should be utilised only for the various activities to be organized in the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan on the subject of Marathi language and literature.
(6) The Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Mahamandal shall be fully responsible for the proper fulfillment of the rules, regulations and financial matters regarding the organization of the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan. The Maharashtra government will not be responsible for this.
(7) The certificate of appropriation of funds made available by the Government for the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan, the Sammelan report, the statement of the articles examined by the Chartered Accountant should be submitted by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Nigam to the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board under the Government within 6 months after the Sammelan. Also, the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture should submit a compliance report to the government along with a utility certificate.
(8) The Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or his officers shall have the right to examine all the documents and accounts relating to the financial assistance sanctioned by the Government for the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan.
