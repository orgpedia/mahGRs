Government circular:
To commemorate the memory of former President late Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, his birthday on 15th October is celebrated as" Reading Inspiration Day." In pursuance of this decision, instructions were issued earlier through the relevant Government Circulars to encourage reading and develop the love of reading among school and college students. Similarly, in order to celebrate Reading Inspiration Day this year, all the Ministries/ Departments and all the Government/ Semi-Government offices under them, Government Institutions/ Boards, Public Undertakings, etc.
Government/ semi-government offices, schools should organize the program on October 15. Every officer/ employee as well as school students, teachers and non-teaching staff should devote at least half an hour during working hours for reading
For reading, the group should sit together and spend their time reading for a few hours in a row as a reading hobby.
2. To nurture the reading culture, everyone should gift at least one book to their friends, family members etc.
Through social media such as WhatsApp, Internet, Facebook, Twitter, etc., messages that promote reading culture/ inspire reading should be spread.
4. Schools, colleges, libraries and other institutions to install' Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam" reading kattas in their premises.
Special care should be taken to ensure that the model code of conduct is not violated while celebrating this event.
