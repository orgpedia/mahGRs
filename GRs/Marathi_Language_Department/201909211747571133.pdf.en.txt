Government has started a separate department of Marathi language since 2011 with a view to develop and promote Marathi language. Under this department, four offices are functioning (1) Directorate of Languages, Mumbai (2) State Marathi Development Society, Mumbai (3) Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai (4) Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai. At present, these offices are functioning at different locations in Mumbai and are required to be located in one building to coordinate the activities and work of these offices. For this purpose, under the decision of the Government of Reference No. 2, a suitable place for the main center of Marathi Bhasha Bhavan in the area from South Mumbai to Bandra-Kurla Vasahat has been selected. It was decided to form a separate committee under the chairmanship of the Minister, Marathi language.
The period of the said Committee was 3 months from the date of issue of the Government Resolution.
However, the deadline. The said Committee was dissolved on 29.09.2018 and no further business of the Committee could be transacted during the said period. The period is being extended up to 31.12.2019.
