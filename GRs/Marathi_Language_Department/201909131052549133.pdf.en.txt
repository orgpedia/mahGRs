Introduction: The concept of" village of books" came into being on the lines of" Hay on Way," a book village in Wales (England). Considering the international nature of the scheme, the concept of" Village of Books" was formed with the objective of development, promotion, propagation and promotion of Marathi language, reading culture and to increase the interest in the language. Reference No. As per the decision of the Government of India, a committee was constituted to determine the criteria for selecting the houses to be included in the scheme. After the committee's report, the government directed to select the houses and implement the initiative through the State Marathi Development Agency. As per the norms of the scheme, book stalls were made available to the readers on various subjects with the participation of the local people. Inauguration of this innovative project. It was released on May 4, 2017. This initiative has been launched in collaboration with the government and public participation to promote the reading culture and attract a large number of tourists to the book city. Considering the increasing scope of the" Village of Books" initiative, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to implement this initiative in a comprehensive manner as a separate scheme.
Government Decision: With a view to develop, propagate, propagate and nurture the reading culture of Marathi language and to promote Marathi language and to implement the" Village of Books" initiative in a comprehensive manner, the Government has decided to implement the scheme" Village of Books" Bhilar from the year 2019-20. 2 in the order. The Government approval is being given as per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting held on 3.09.2019.
Readers and tourists are visiting in large numbers the various book galleries in the village of books.
In view of the features of this scheme, the State Marathi Development Agency should take action to implement various activities in a comprehensive manner to use this platform to organize various literary activities, literature publication, workshops as well as book release programs, seminars, literature fair, literary programs of eminent litterateurs in Bhilar.
