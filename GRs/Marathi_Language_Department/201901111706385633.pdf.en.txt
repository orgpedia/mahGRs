Government's stand:
As per the Government decision dated 04.09.2018 regarding sanction of subsidy for payment of outstanding rent of the office of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, the outstanding rent for the period September, 2013 to February, 2018 (54 months) is Rs. 47,33,856/- (Rupees Forty Seven Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Eight Hundred and Fifty Six only) to be paid in respect of Encyclopaedia Production Board. The proposal was approved on 29.05.2018.
However, the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopaedia Production Board has issued a circular in this regard. vide letter dated 22.10.2018, the rent for the above mentioned period of March, 2017 is Rs. 87,664/- (Rupees Eighty Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Sixty Four only) already paid and in view of the fact that it was left out unnecessarily during the consideration, the amount of payment of rent for the remaining 53 months excluding the rent of March, 2017 for the period September, 2013 to February, 2018 has been increased to Rs. 46,46,192/- (Rupees Forty-six Lakhs Forty-Six Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Two only) was the art of the proposal to be approved.
In view of the above proposal, the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board has to pay the rent for the remaining 53 months excluding the rent for the period from September, 2013 to February, 2018.
