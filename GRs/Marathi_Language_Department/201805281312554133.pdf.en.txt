Introduction: The Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board is working on the editing of encyclopaedias in Marathi language as well as the editing of Bal Vishwakosh and Kumar Encyclopaedias. For the writing and editing of the entries in the Marathi Encyclopedia volume, the assistance of experts/ writers from different subjects is taken as a visiting editor. Since it is not technically convenient to appoint permanently the experts required for the work of the Encyclopedia as editors, the reference No. Under the decision of the government here, it is in the process of taking the services of senior professors of the university and college as well as senior professors, researchers, etc. experts in some technical subjects for the work of editing the entries in the Marathi Encyclopedia volume. Accordingly, the No. 3 decision of the government. As of April 1, 2015. It was decided to continue the practice of engaging the services of Visiting Editors for various subjects up to 31st March, 2018 (3 years). Since the work of encyclopaedias is still in progress, the government was considering to continue this practice of hiring the services of visiting editors for a period of next 3 years. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: As per the powers given to the Secretary, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai, the services of senior professors, researchers, editors etc. of various universities and colleges in connection with the editing of Marathi Encyclopedia will be taken as" Visiting Editor" for writing and other editorial work at Encyclopedia Production Board Office, Wai, G. Satara. before 31.03.2018. The Government is granting approval for continuation of the scheme during the period from 1st April, 2018 to 31st March, 2021 subject to the following conditions:
At No. 1: While submitting the expenditure proposal, the detailed details of the total expenditure should be made available to the government on the basis of rules/ orders.
At No. 2: These services should be taken on a temporary basis and the honorarium of the visiting editors should be paid according to the rates prescribed by the government for the services.
