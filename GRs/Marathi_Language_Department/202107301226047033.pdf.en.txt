Government order
The post of" Assistant Director of Languages (Translation& Terminology)" in the Directorate of Languages, Mumbai is lying vacant since 27.05.2016. The additional charge of this post has been given to other officers from time to time. Reference No. The Government have approved the continuation of the additional charge of the said vacant post for a period of one year and six months beyond 27.11.2019 or till the date of filling up of the post, whichever is earlier. This period has ended on 26.05.2021.
In view of the above, the Government hereby approve the continuation of the additional charge of the post of" Assistant Director of Languages (Translation and Terminology)" in the Directorate of Languages for a further period of 6 months with effect from 27.05.2021 or till the said post is filled up as per rules, whichever is earlier.
