Preamble The Secretary, Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai, vide the reference letter, has stated that the computers in the office of the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai, under the Marathi Language Department, are outdated and non-existent, the need for a scanner to scan the books published by the Board and the need for the purchase of these materials has been necessitated by the frequent shutdown of the office printers due to their continuous use. Administrative approval was also granted for this and the required funds were requested to be made available under the Account Head e-Governance Project Implementation (2052 5134) (Programme) from the Detailed Head 17-Computer Sheet.
In pursuance of the above demand, the office of the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai was informed after receiving a case-wise representation from the office of the Executive Information and Technology, Sapravi. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai, issued a reference letter. A revised proposal was submitted under the letter dated 31.10.2024.
As per the revised proposal, the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai, will purchase 2 computers (all-in-one), 4 printers and 1 scanner at a cost of Rs. 00 lakh (letters Rs.

Government Decision - Since the computers in the office of Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board, Mumbai are old and not of up-to-date nature, a scanner is required to scan the books published by this board and the printers in the office are frequently switched off due to continuous use.
