The Proposal:
The Government Transaction Cell was created in May 1973 with the definition and semantics that are useful in governance and administrative affairs. So far, several editions of this book have been published. The Government Transaction Directory is prepared and updated by the Directorate of Languages. The Government Transaction Folder is available on https:// shabdakosh. marathi. gov. in website of Directorate of Languages and contains 24470 terms. In the changing times, some of the English terms and meanings in the government's dictionary were given new options and meanings in Marathi. However, in order to ensure that they are not included in the Sasan Vyavasaya Kosh, the expert members of the Sasan Vyavasaya Kosh (Revised) Sub-Committee, after thorough discussion, have fixed 7489 terms with their Marathi alternatives. Finally, an updated Government Transaction Fund is to be published by incorporating the agreed terms and their Marathi alternatives in the existing Government Transaction Fund. In order to simplify the Government Transaction Fund, the above mentioned 7489 terms and their alternatives are directed to be sent for the perusal of all the administrative officers. The Minister has given.
Government circular:
