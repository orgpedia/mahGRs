Introduction: Shri Sant Namdeo Maharaj's work in Bhagwat Dharma and his abhangas and kirtanas
Considering the awareness created among the people of the entire India, especially in the Maharashtra and Punjab regions, the importance of his literature and the awareness generated by it has not diminished even today.
Therefore, even today the book" Shri Namdev Gatha" is an important text of the Warkari sect. That is why the government was considering to approve the proposal to make the book" Shri Namdev Gatha" available to the general public as well as general readers at a discounted price.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, the book" Shri Namdev Gatha" will be available to the general public and general readers at a discounted price i.e. Rs. 945/- instead of 50% discount of Rs. 472.00/- is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The cost of reprinting the book" Shri Namdev Gatha" shall be met from the provision approved by the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture for the year 2019-20.
(2) The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture shall ensure that the frequency of proposals for making available Shri Namdev Gatha at a discounted price is not increased by reducing the prescribed selling price of the books/ texts.
(3) The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture should take action to make the book" Shri Namdev Gatha" available on Mahajala and make its audio CDs available to reach more and more readers and common readers.
The expenditure for the same shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-2020 under the head Demand No. ZF-02, 2205, Art& Culture, 102 Art& Culture Operations- (01) (Mandatory) (01) State Board of Literature& Culture (16 Publications) (2205 3172).
