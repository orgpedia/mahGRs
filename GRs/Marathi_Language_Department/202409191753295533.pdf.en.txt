The post of Deputy Director in the State Marathi Development Institute has been vacant since 2009. The State Marathi Development Agency has been established with the objective of preserving, nurturing and promoting the Marathi language and culture of Maharashtra. Various initiatives are undertaken independently by the State Marathi Institute to develop the Marathi language at all levels with the aim of making the use of Marathi more and more qualitative in various fields and to keep the process of Marathi language development in a planned pace. However, since the important post of Deputy Director in the institute is currently vacant, it is adversely affecting the activities and functioning of the institute.
Read the above mentioned No. In a statement here today, Smt. N. J. Dhammal, Deputy Commissioner of State Taxes, Goods and Services Tax Department, Pune had requested for appointment as Deputy Director, State Marathi Development Agency on deputation. Read No. According to the letter, the Department of Finance has agreed to relieve Mrs. N.J. Dhammal, Deputy Commissioner of State Taxes, to join the deputation if the Department of Finance appoints Mrs. N.J. Dhammal, Deputy Commissioner of State Taxes, to the post on deputation.
In view of the above facts, as a special case, the post of Deputy Director, State Marathi Development Agency, Mumbai is being held by Smt. N. J. Dhammal, Deputy Commissioner of State Taxes, Goods and Services Tax Department, Pune on deputation for a period of 2 years with immediate effect.
