Government's decision:

Pursuant to this decision, in order to create motivation and enhance the interest of reading, the Government vide Circular No. In pursuance of the" Reading Motivation Day" programme, all Ministries/ Departments and all Government/ Semi-Government offices under them, Government Institutions/ Boards, Public Undertakings, etc. have also been advised to organize various activities.
The Maharashtra Local Authorities (Official Language) Act, 2022 is binding on local authorities. The local authority may make suitable provisions under section 4 for effective implementation of this Act. As part of this provision, the local authority can take various measures.

As per the above and in the context of Reading Motivation Day, the Government has decided to organize the following competitions at the municipal level this year to increase affection/ awareness about Marathi among Marathi/ Amarathi people. The competition will be held at the level of Municipal Commissioner. Competition rules and regulations are as follows:
Competition rules and regulations.
1) In line with the theme of" Official Language of Maharashtra- Marathi," programs should be organized based on innovative ideas to increase affection/ awareness about Marathi among Marathi/ Amarathi people. In the innovative idea, various mediums like music, photography, drawings, slogans, themes based on modern technology, etc. can be used.
