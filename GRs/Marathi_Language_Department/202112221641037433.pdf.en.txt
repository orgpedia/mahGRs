All government and private offices, educational institutions, banks, post offices, etc.
Every year,' Marathi Bhasha Samvardhan Pakhwada' is being organized in all the offices/ institutions of the state, including institutions/ universities/ colleges, to maximize the use of Marathi language, which is the official language of the state, and to promote Marathi language. It is celebrated from January 14 to January 28.
In line with the main policy of the department of promotion and propagation of Marathi language, instructions are given to organize various programs every year to celebrate the" Marathi Language Preservation Fortnight." However, due to the current outbreak of COVID-19- Omicron, certain restrictions need to be followed as a preventive measure. Therefore, while a large number of physical events cannot be held during this fortnight, more and more events can be celebrated online using modern technology. While celebrating this event, the government was considering organizing programs for the promotion and dissemination of Marathi through the effective implementation of social media, television, etc.
Government Decision- All the offices are being instructed to celebrate the fortnight by organizing appropriate programs on the occasion of Marathi Language Preservation Fortnight as follows: a) Programmes to be held by all the offices in the state: Online programs are the need of the hour in the wake of COVID and modern technology.
In view of this, maximum programs for the promotion and propagation of Marathi language are as follows:
