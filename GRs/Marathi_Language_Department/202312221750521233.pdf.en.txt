Background:
The conference is being organised by the All India Marathi Sahitya Akademi. As per the Government Decision dated 23rd August, 2018, Rs. Rs 50 lakh has been provided in relief.
The All India Marathi Literary Meet is a literary meet organised by the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan. The main policy of the Marathi Language Department is the promotion, dissemination, development and preservation of the Marathi language. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 50.00 lakhs to Rs. 2.00 crore was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision As per the decision of this Government, the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Parishad will provide a grant of Rs. 2 crore (Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for sanction of financial assistance to the extent of Rs.2 crore only subject to the following conditions:
(1) The All India Marathi Sahitya Parishad shall submit a proposal to the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture for providing financial assistance to the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan.
(2) The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture may provide financial assistance to the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan only after the Board receives a proposal from the All India Marathi Sahitya Mahasangh. 2 crore to the All India Marathi Literary Association.

(3) The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture shall ensure that in the event of the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan not being organised by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Nigam in any year, the provision sanctioned for the same shall not be used elsewhere without the approval of the Government.
(4) The amount of financial assistance to be made available by the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture for the All India Marathi Literary Conference shall be deposited in the account of the All India Marathi Literary Corporation in one lump sum before Dussehra every year. In this regard, the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board should take care to request the details of the proposed conference from the All India Marathi Literary Corporation well in advance.
(5) The financial assistance being made available by the Government of Maharashtra should be utilised only for the various activities to be organized in the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan on the subject of Marathi language and literature.
(6) The Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Mahamandal shall be fully responsible for the proper fulfillment of the rules, regulations and financial matters regarding the organization of the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan.
(7) The certificate of appropriation of funds made available by the Government for the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan, the Sammelan report, the statement of the articles examined by the Chartered Accountant should be submitted by the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Nigam to the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board under the Government within 6 months after the Sammelan. Also, the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture should submit a compliance report to the government along with a utility certificate.
(8) The Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or his officers have the right to examine all the documents and accounts regarding the financial assistance sanctioned by the Government for the All India Marathi Literary Conference and if they demand, all the documents should be submitted to him for examination.
For this purpose, the Assistant Accounts Officer, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Further, Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer and he or a subordinate officer under him authorised by him to sign the payment in this regard.
The expenditure for the same shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the head Demand No.ZF-2, 2205-Art and Culture-00, 102 Art and Culture Operations, (01) (10) Grants to Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sahitya Sammelan (Programme) (2205 3448) 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Salary) and as per the monthly fund statement.
