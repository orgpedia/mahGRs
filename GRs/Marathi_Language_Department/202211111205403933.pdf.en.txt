The Marathi Language Day is observed every year in all government and semi-government offices, boards, corporations, public sector undertakings, private and commercial banks, educational institutions, universities, colleges and other institutions in the state. It is celebrated from January 14 to January 28.
In line with the main policy of the department of promotion and propagation of Marathi language, instructions are given to organize various programs every year to celebrate the" Marathi Language Preservation Fortnight." Accordingly, this year also, the government was considering to instruct all the offices to organize various programs related to Marathi language in the Marathi Language Conservation Fortnight.
Government circular:
Instructions are being given to all the offices to celebrate the Marathi Language Preservation Fortnight by organizing appropriate programs as follows:
(a) Organizing various seminars/ lectures/ workshops/ camps/ poets' meet/ mono/ children's plays/ plays/ book release function/ literary award distribution related to Marathi language and literature.
