Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, the revised layout of the Marathi Language Department and the subordinate field offices of the department has been fixed. Accordingly, a total of 27 temporary posts have been sanctioned for the office of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai, which is under the department. Out of these, 6 temporary posts have been sanctioned for the headquarters at Mumbai and 21 temporary posts for the office at Wai. A total of 27 temporary posts in Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board have been extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
