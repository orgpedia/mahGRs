Government order
The post of" Divisional Assistant Director of Languages" in the Directorate of Languages, Divisional Office, Navi Mumbai is lying vacant since 30.05.2016. The additional charge of the said post has been handed over to other officers from time to time. Reference No. 2 vacant post
The Government has approved continuation of the additional charge for a period of one year beyond 27.05.2020 or till the date the post becomes vacant, whichever is earlier. This period has ended on 26.05.2021.
In view of the above, the Government hereby approve the continuation of the additional charge of the post of" Divisional Assistant Director of Languages" in the Directorate of Languages, Divisional Office, Navi Mumbai for a further period of six months with effect from 27.05.2021 or till the said post is filled up in the prescribed manner, whichever is earlier.
