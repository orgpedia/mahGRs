The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 18.02.2016, it has been decided to confer the" Bhasha Samvardhan Puraskar" to a person or institution who has made valuable contribution for the promotion of Marathi language through the State Marathi Development Society. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to announce the name of Dr. Ashok Kelkar, a senior language scientist and literary critic of international repute, as" Marathi Bhasha Adhyaksh Puraskar, 2017" and the name of poet Mangesh Padgaonkar, who has done significant work in promoting Marathi language in an easy-to-understand language through poetry, as" Marathi Bhasha Sanvardhak Puraskar, 2017."
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the decision of the Government in the context of conferring the Marathi Language Preservation Award, the award for the year 2017 was conferred on Dr. Ashok Kelkar and Avinash Biniwale have been awarded the Marathi Language Scholar Award.
2) Each award carries a cash prize of Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs only) in cash, a memento and a certificate.
