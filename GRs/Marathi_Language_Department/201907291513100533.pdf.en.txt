The Proposal:
Among the major objectives of the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, one of the important objectives is to promote literary culture. In order to promote and propagate literature, encourage and guide the new writers of rural areas of Maharashtra, improve the writings of the new writers so that there are no errors in their writings, create good writers in rural areas, they get expert guidance, for this purpose 7 workshops/ seminars are organized every year for the new writers implemented by the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture and Rs. 3,50,000/- is provided in the budget. The number of workshops could not be increased as the funds were insufficient. The proposal to increase the number of workshops was under the consideration of the government due to the response from budding writers to this initiative and the increase in the subsidy of this scheme to make it possible to implement the same in other parts of Maharashtra.
Government's decision:
Under this initiative, a total of 10 workshops/ seminars will be organized every year by the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture. 50,000/- (Rs. The government has approved to provide only 50 thousand).
