Introduction: The Office of Directorate of Languages, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has launched the Mobile App" Government Dictionary Part-1." It was released on Google Play Store on 29.05.2021. The Government Dictionary Part-I is a mobile application which has four dossiers available in digital format viz., Secretary, Government Interpretation, Government Transactions and Judicial Transactions, which can be viewed in English x Marathi and Marathi x English. This mobile application can be used with or without internet. The words in the Government Dictionary Part-1 are very useful for government affairs and judicial affairs and the government was considering to give wide publicity to this mobile phone user at the ministerial level as well as at the state level.
