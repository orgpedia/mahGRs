Introduction: The General Administration Department will decide how to use the standardized Marathi alphabet, alphabets and numerals in Devanagari script in all government offices, semi-government offices, educational institutions, universities, boards, corporations, authorities, Marathi textbooks, etc. where Marathi language is used in handwriting, typing, printing, etc. The decision was taken on 6.11.2009. The Department has received reduction suggestions in the Budget Session 2019-20 regarding simplification of existing rules and changes in rules. Also, some teachers and writers have expressed their opinion to the government that there is a need to make some amendments, changes in the total number of characters, consonants, vowels, swaradis, rules regarding similar and related words in the Marathi language alphabet. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a committee to reconsider the matter and suggest measures to promote the study and research of the Devanagari script.
Government Decision: The Government Decision is issued by General Administration Department. Reconsideration of the need to revise the standardized Marathi alphabet and alphabets in Devanagari script mentioned in 06.11.2009
