The Proposal:
As per the Maharashtra State Cultural Policy 2010, on the lines of the All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan, the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture implements the scheme of" Grants to Other Marathi Sahitya Sammelans" to provide grants to literary gatherings of people belonging to different streams and marginalized sections based on the overall nature of the gathering. As a special case to the All India Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan, Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, a proposal has been submitted to the Planning/ Finance Department to make available a fund of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) for the organization of Akhil Bhartiya Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan through Warkari Sahitya Parishad, Maharashtra by reallocation of funds under Computer Samples (22053214).
1) Demand for the year 2022-2023 No. Village of ZF-02 Books (100% State Plan) (Programme) (2205 3653) Under the head 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Munerative), Rs. Rs 25 lakh. The prescribed procedure shall be followed while reallocating and allocating the said funds under the head of ZF-02, State Board of Literature and Culture (Programme) (22053214) (Programme).
