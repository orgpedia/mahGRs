Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory to fix the posts in the government service for persons with disabilities. The central government has given. The list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disability notified under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 may be seen in the website of the Central Government www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Published on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In accordance with the directions of the said Government decision, it was under the consideration of the Government to ensure the posts of constables in Group-D cadre for Divyangs in the Marathi Language Department (itself), Mantralaya, Mumbai and to ensure these posts for the newly Divyang category by reviewing the list of seats in Government service ensured for Divyangs after a period of not more than 3 years.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for PwDs as per notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published.
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions to ensure that the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which have been fixed by the Central Government, are in the state government establishment, the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations of such posts are different. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
