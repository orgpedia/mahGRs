Government order
The post of" Secretary" in Group-A cadre in the office of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai under the Department is lying vacant since 01.03.2018. The additional charge of this post has been given to other officers from time to time. Reference No. The approval granted to continue the additional charge of the said vacant post under Section 2 has expired on 28.02.2021.
In view of the above, the Government hereby approve the continuation of the post of" Secretary" in Group-A cadre in the office of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopaedia Production Board, Mumbai for a period of one year beyond 01.03.2021 in addition to the existing charge or till the said post is filled up as per rules, whichever is earlier.
