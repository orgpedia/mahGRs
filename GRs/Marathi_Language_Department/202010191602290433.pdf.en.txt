The Proposal:
Amendments to the Maharashtra Official Language Act, 1964. The Committee has been constituted as per the Government Resolution dated 24.08.2020. The committee has been asked to submit its report within two months. The term of the said Committee will expire on 23.10.2020. In the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic, it is becoming difficult to organise meetings. Also, the committee will need more time to submit its report as the information sought from the ministry departments regarding the use of Marathi language is not available. The government had sought three months" time to submit the report.
Government's decision:
The Government has approved the extension of the term of the Committee appointed to amend the Maharashtra Official Language Act, 1964 from 24th October, 2020 to 23rd January, 2021.
