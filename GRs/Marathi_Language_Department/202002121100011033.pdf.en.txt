The Proposal:
The Government was contemplating to announce Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2019 to a renowned litterateur who has made significant and valuable contributions in the field of Marathi literature/ literature and Shri Pu Bhagwat Puraskar for the year 2019 to an outstanding publishing house which has made significant achievements in the field of Marathi literature production.
Government's decision:
Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for outstanding contribution in the field of Marathi literature. As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the Government Decision No. 1, the Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar for the year 2019 has been conferred on the veteran litterateur Anuradha Patil and the publication house for the significant achievement in the production of Marathi literature has been awarded the Shri Pu Bhagwat Puraskar. As per the decision of the Committee constituted under the Government Resolution No. 2, the Shri Pujya Bhagwat Puraskar for the year 2019 is being announced to Padmagandha Prakashan, Pune.
