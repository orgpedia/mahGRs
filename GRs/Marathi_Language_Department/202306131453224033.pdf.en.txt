Government circular
The Department of Marathi Language was established with effect from 22.07.2010. After the formation of the department, the seniority list of language officers (Hindi), Group B (Gazetted), lone cadre on the establishment of the Directorate of Languages under the department is to be published afresh. The comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in Government service is contained in Reference No. 1 has been prescribed by the notification here. Accordingly, the provisional seniority for the period from 01.01.2011 to 01.01.2023 for the cadre of Language Officer (Hindi), Group-B (Gazetted) has been issued vide Department Circular dated 29.03.2023. The objections have been sought from the Directorate of Senior Secondary Languages within the stipulated time. The Directorate has suggested an amendment in the name of No. 1-Category in Form-D in the said Seniority List and the same has been amended. Accordingly, the provisional seniority of Language Officer (Hindi), Group-B (Gazetted) cadre for the period from 01.01.2011 to 01.01.2023 vide circular dated 29.03.2023 is being finalized in the form of 4 returns in Form A to D attached to this circular. Form-A and Form-A for the period from 01.01.2011 and 01.01.2012 respectively.

Reference No. 2. As per the provisions of section 1, the names of the officers who were appointed on regular/ ad-hoc promotion/ simple service in the Directorate of Languages during the period from 01.01.2010 to 01.01.2022 have been included in the seniority list. The names of officers who retired or died before 31.12.2009 have been excluded from the list. Also, the names of officers who retired during the relevant year from 01.01.2010 to 01.01.2022 have been excluded from the list of the respective year.
The seniority of the officers of the cadre of Language Officer (Hindi), Group-B (Gazetted) in the Directorate, attached with the 4 prospectus, is being finalized under the provisions of the General Administration Department," Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021," dated 21.6.2021, subject to the following conditions.
(a) The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list shall be taken into consideration for determining seniority in the cadre of Language Officer (Hindi) only in accordance with the provisions of the M.N.S. (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 as well as Government Decision, General Administration Department, dated 21.06.2021. Permission for financial benefits in respect of the said date shall be subject to the prevailing policy.
(b) M. The High Court, Bombay in Petition No. 2797/2015 has quashed the Government Order dated 25.5.2004 and declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. The state government has appealed against this decision. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court. The said final seniority is being issued subject to the final decision in the said Special Leave Petition.
The Director of Languages, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is requested to bring the said circular and the accompanying seniority to the immediate notice of the concerned Language Officer (Hindi).
