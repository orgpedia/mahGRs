Proposal - Government Letter No. Vyapam 2017 / P.No.19 / Languages-2, D. A mobile phone user named "Government Dictionary Part-9" has been approved under 16.06.2017. The cell phone user included four HMAs, namely, the Secretariat, the Office of Administration Speech, the Office of Government Transaction, and the Office of Justice. The contact address has been given to the mobile user to give him wide publicity at the ministerial level and at the state level.
The Office of the Directorate of Languages had submitted a proposal to the Hon'ble Government regarding the inclusion of two new directories, namely, "Designation Dictionary" and "Financial Terminology," in the "Government Dictionary Part-I" mobile application (APP) and administrative approval of the expenditure fixed as per Minimum Deduction / Tariff by calling for the Tariffs for the expenditure incurred for providing information on various examinations conducted by the Directorate of Languages to the mobile user through the Press Release.
Accordingly, the Government was considering the inclusion of two new directories, namely, "Designation Dictionary" and "Financial Terminology," in the "Government Dictionary Part-I" mobile application (APP) and administrative approval of the cost of providing information on various examinations conducted by the language directory to the mobile user through a press release.
In pursuance of the proposal submitted under the letter of the Director of Government Decision-Language, State of Maharashtra, an amount of Rs.77,880 / - (including Goods and Services Tax) (Rupees Seventy-seven thousand and Eighty-eight only) is being granted for inclusion of two new directories, namely, "Designation Box" and "Financial Glossary," in the "Government Dictionary Part-I" mobile application (APP) and for providing information on various examinations conducted by the language directorate to the mobile user through a press release.
