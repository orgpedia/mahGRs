Government Resolution: Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs As per the Government decision dated 28.12.2006, the Government have approved the relocation of two Government offices of Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture and Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai on the second floor of Ravindra Natya Mandir in the premises of P. L. Deshpande Kala Academy at the rate fixed by the Public Works Department on Maha Rent basis. However, since the offices of the Mandals are government offices, the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board and the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture have been in communication with the government in this regard. However, the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture has not approved the waiver of rent. Orders have been issued under the office order dated 22.11.2016 to enter into a lease agreement with P. L. Deshpande Kala Academy and make budgetary provisions accordingly. As per the lease agreement between the Maharashtra State Sahitya and Sanskriti Board and the P. L. Deshpande Kala Academy, the proposal to pay the outstanding rent of the office of the Maharashtra State Sahitya Sanskriti Board was under the consideration of the government.
The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture has been given administrative approval to pay the rent for the period from March 2007 to March 2018. 1,76,91, 000/- (Rs. The Government is approving the payment of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs.1.77 crore (Rupees One Crore Seventy Seven Lakhs Ninety One Thousand only) as approved through Supplementary Demand in the Winter Session of December, 2017.
The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture shall ensure that the funds are spent only for the approved purpose.
For this purpose, the Assistant Accounts Officer, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai as the Controlling Officer.
