The Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board has 23 members associated with various fields. The appointment was made under the Government Order dated 5.8.2015 for a period of 3 years or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier. Two of the posts are lying vacant due to non-appointment of members. The two members were appointed by the Government on July 5, 2016. The term of Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board has expired on 04.08.2018. As per the Government decision dated 28.08.2018, the existing Board was extended till fresh restructuring. The proposal to reconstitute the board was under the consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of the following Chairman and Members on the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board for a period of 3 years from the date of this order or until further orders of the Government.
