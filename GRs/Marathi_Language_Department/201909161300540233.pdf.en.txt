Introduction: The Directorate of Languages under the Department of Marathi Language, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board and the Maharashtra State Society and Culture Board have produced a number of books on various definitions, administrative writings, encyclopaedias in Marathi language on the lines of Britannica Encyclopedia, as well as original commentaries on various topics such as Marathi language, literature, culture, science, modern technology, sociology. Although these publications are informative, at present, these publications are sold only from 5 Government Libraries in Mumbai, Pune, Aurangabad, Kolhapur and Nagpur and through authorized dealers in Maharashtra and outside Maharashtra appointed by the Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publications. However, in order to make these publications easily accessible to educational institutions, students and general readers, the government was considering a proposal to make these publications available at a discounted price of 40 per cent to private vendors in addition to the authorized dealer appointed by the Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publications.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, all the publications published so far and to be published henceforth by the Directorate of Languages under the Department of Marathi Language, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board and Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board will be sold through the official and private dealers outside Maharashtra and Maharashtra appointed by the Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publishing and the Government is giving a total discount of 40 percent on all the publications to these sellers
