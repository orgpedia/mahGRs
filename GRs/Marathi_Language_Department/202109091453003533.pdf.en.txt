The Proposal:
Reference No. Under the Hindi Language Examination, the Board was reconstituted. As the term of the said Board has expired, a proposal to reconstitute the said Board appointed for the Hindi language examinations prescribed for the Government officers and employees was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The said Board, which has been appointed to conduct the prescribed Hindi language examinations for Government officers and employees and to declare the results of those examinations, is being reconstituted as follows:
Hindi Language Test

The Board shall advise the Government on the changes in the syllabus prescribed for Hindi Language Examinations as may be required and on other questions relating to the examination. The Board shall continue to exist until it is reconstituted or until further orders.
The non-official members of the Board shall hold office for a term of three years and shall be entitled to travel allowance, daily allowance and sitting allowance as per the extant orders of the Finance Department. The cost of this should be met from the grant sanctioned by the Directorate of Languages.
