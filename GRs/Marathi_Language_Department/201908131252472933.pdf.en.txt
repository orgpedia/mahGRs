Preamble: The revised pay structure for the State Government employees has been implemented with effect from 01.01.2016 in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission. On the same lines, a proposal to implement the revised pay structure as per the Seventh Pay Commission for the officers/ employees of the 100% government-funded institution, Rajya Marathi Vikas Sanstha, has been submitted for approval at the government level.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving to provide direct financial benefit of revised pay from 01.01.2019 to the officers/ employees of" Rajya Marathi Vikas Sanstha," a 100% Government funded organization under the Department of Marathi Language, by approving the revised pay structure from 01.01.2016 as shown in the attached" Form-A."
The fixation of pay in this revised pay structure should be done in accordance with the Government Notification, Finance Department, dated 30.01.2019 issued under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 and Government Decision, Finance Department, No.Vepur-2019/ PR No. 8/ Service-9, dated 20.02.2019. The option to draw salary in the revised pay scale permissible under this rule should be given to the said employee within 01 month from the date of issue of this order.
Separate orders will be issued in respect of the allowable dues for the period from 01.01.2016 to 31.12.2018.
