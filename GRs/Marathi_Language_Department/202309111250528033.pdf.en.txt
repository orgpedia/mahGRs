Government Circular: With a view to preserve the memory of former President late Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, his birthday on 15th October is celebrated as" Reading Inspiration Day." Pursuant to this decision, in order to encourage reading and promote the love of reading among school and college students, the Government vide Circular No. In pursuance of the" Reading Motivation Day" programme, all Ministries/ Departments and all Government/ Semi-Government offices under them, Government Institutions/ Boards, Public Undertakings, etc. are also advised to organize various activities every year.
The main policy of the Marathi Language Department is the promotion, development, preservation and conservation of the Marathi language.
Various activities/ programmes/ schemes are implemented in accordance with this policy. Since the above schemes alone are not enough to expand the scope of this policy, instructions are given to organize different programs for the promotion of Marathi on the occasion of Reading Inspiration Day or other programs.
In the context of the ongoing and expanding initiatives being undertaken in respect of Marathi language, The administrative approval has been given in pursuance of the Government decision dated 24 th June, 2022. As per the decision of the government, the Marathi Language Department and the subordinate offices have been instructed to organize various types of messages, lectures, seminars, speeches, group reading, book exhibition, etc. to promote reading culture on the occasion of Reading Inspiration Day. Under the extended initiative, it has been suggested to organize various programs for the promotion of Marathi language. As per the decision of the government, the concerned commissioner and collector have to organize various programs.
Also, all the Ministries/ Departments and all the Government/ Semi-Government Offices under them, Government Institutions/ Boards, Public Undertakings, etc. are to organize various activities on the occasion of Reading Inspiration Day.

The following general suggestions are being made in terms of what programmes should be organized to celebrate Reading Inspiration Day this year:
1. To mark the 350th Shivarajyabhisheka this year, programmes should be organized on the occasion of Reading Inspiration Day by fixing the central theme of" 350th Shivarajyabhisheka of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Celebration of Shivcharitra, reading of books."
Various events are organized every year to promote the culture of reading through cultural programs/ seminars/ training etc. Similarly, this year too, the programmes should be conducted through social media/ physical mode (online/ offline).
So that the scope of the program can be expanded and the message of Reading Inspiration Day can be spread everywhere at once. While organizing this program, the main policy of the Marathi Language Department should be considered for the promotion, development, preservation and conservation of Marathi language.
3. To foster" Reading Culture," everyone should encourage by gifting at least one book to their friends, family members etc.
4. Not limiting the programme to fine arts, the time-befitting subjects like science, technology, management, environment, health, computers, websites, space science etc. should also be included in the programme.
5. In order to increase the" reading culture" and to attract more youth through employment oriented activities with the aim of promoting Marathi language, the following topics and other programs of such nature should be organized.
1) Translation 2) Professional Writing 3) Book Production and Publication 4) E-Book 5) Self-Publishing 6) Use of Unicode 7) Online Book Sales 8) Author Publication Agreement 9) Code Writing 10) Information Workshop on Encyclopedia 11) Short Film, Documentary
Social media platforms like WhatsApp, Internet, Facebook, Twitter etc., should be used to spread messages that promote reading culture/ inspire reading.
To preserve the Marathi reading culture, literature in Marathi should be sent to the readers spontaneously by making maximum use of all available media.
8. All institutions should create" Marathi reading katta" on social media.
All institutions should organize Marathi virtual/ physical literary conferences at their level.
10.Workshops should be organized so that more and more writings can be done in Marathi on media like Wikipedia.
On the occasion of Reading Inspiration Day, various programs should be organized for the promotion/ propagation/ development of reading culture in Marathi.

The objective of the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture is to promote and assist in the fulfillment of the multifaceted literary plans for the subjects covered by science, modern technology, sociology, as well as the language, culture and history of Maharashtra in Marathi, and to provide basic knowledge books and translations of classics at affordable prices, as a means for the development of knowledge and culture and to inform about the latest advances in modern science and technology. Accordingly, the board should review and include in the book publication of the institution, along with the fine literature as a means for the development of knowledge and culture, as well as modern science, technology and other new topics.
The State Marathi Development Society should start a new initiative to promote reading culture and submit a proposal to the government to start this initiative according to its year-long schedule.
The Encyclopaedia Production Board shall determine the programme for continuous training and increase in the number of entries for updating the records of the Encyclopaedia and submit the report of implementation to the Government.
The promotion and propagation of Marathi language is the main policy of the Marathi Language Department. In view of the above, this policy is being implemented.
