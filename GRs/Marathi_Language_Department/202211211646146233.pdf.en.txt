Government's decision
It was proposed to organize a world conference in Mumbai with the noble intention of" Marathi Tituka Mela" to promote the Marathi language at a global level, to create interest in communicating in Marathi among all, to encourage people to use Marathi language more in their daily use. Accordingly, Ma. The Minister of Marathi Language, Shri. In the meeting held on 28.10.2022, it was decided to organize a world conference in Mumbai and invite members of Maharashtra Board from abroad and other states for the conference.
Marathi language, literature, culture and art have a long tradition and separate identity.
In order to celebrate this long tradition of Maharashtra, to revive Marathi culture, to listen to classic Marathi literature, to express traditional arts, to exchange business ideas, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to organize a" Marathi Tituka Malava (World Conference)" of Marathi speakers from India and abroad in Mumbai on 4th, 5th and 6th January, 2023.
The offices of the Directorate of Languages, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board and State Marathi Development Society under the Department are obliged to participate in the organization of this World Conference by taking action as per the directions given from the government level from time to time.
4. Two Committees are being constituted as follows to plan the programme and outline the important tasks such as appointment of various institutions for other works to be done in connection with the World Conference and selection of private institutions for other ancillary works.
