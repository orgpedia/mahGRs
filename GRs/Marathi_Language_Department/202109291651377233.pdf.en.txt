The Proposal:
Reference No. The Board was reconstituted for the purpose of Marathi Language Examination. As the term of the said board has expired, a proposal to reconstitute the said board appointed for the Marathi language examinations prescribed for government officers and employees was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: To conduct the Marathi language examination prescribed for government officers and employees and to
The Etherth Board, which has been appointed to conduct the results of the examinations, is being reconstituted as follows: Marathi Language Examination- Etherth Board
Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Department of Marathi Language) Chairman
Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Department of Marathi Language) Member
Prof. R.R. Shetkar (Department of Marathi) Sathe College, Mumbai Non-Governmental Member
Divisional Secretary, Maharashtra State Secondary and Higher Secondary. Board, Vashi Member
Language Director, Maharashtra State, Member Secretary, Mumbai
The Board will advise the Government on the changes to be made in the syllabus prescribed for the Marathi language examinations as per the requirement and other questions related to the examination. The Board shall continue in existence till the Board is reconstituted or until further orders in this regard.
