Terms and Conditions: 1) The above candidates are being appointed on temporary basis subject to medical examination and antecedent verification (within six months).
(2) Caste validity certificate as required and non-criminal (non-criminal) certificate in case of non-criminal category shall be submitted within a period of six months from the date of this order. If these certificates are not fulfilled within a period of six months, their appointment will be automatically terminated. The appointment is being made subject to the completion of the above certificates within the stipulated time.
(3) The above candidates are required to complete the probation period of two years from the date of their appointment. In case they do not achieve the expected standard of work during this probation period or their conduct is found to be unsatisfactory, they will be eligible to terminate their services as Assistant Director of Languages (Translation and Terminology) without any prior notice.
4) All the terms and conditions mentioned in the government decision will be applicable to the candidate appointed to this post during the probation period.
5) The seniority of the above candidate in the post of Assistant Language Director (Translation and Vocabulary) will be as per the provisions of the prescribed rules and according to the merit number of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
