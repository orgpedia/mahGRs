Introduction: Many eminent personalities have made their significant contribution in the social, cultural, literary, political, educational, art etc. fields of Maharashtra, as well as many enlightening journals have made their important contribution by doing remarkable work in the intellectual formation of progressive Maharashtra. The Government of Maharashtra has already celebrated the birth centenary of such people from time to time who have done remarkable work in different fields and have made their valuable contributions. On the same lines, a proposal was under consideration of the government to celebrate the centenary year of Prabodhanpar magazine, which has made an important contribution in the ideological formation of progressive Maharashtra.
Government Decision: To carry forward the legacy of Maharashtra's ideological movement and progressive tradition, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to celebrate the centenary year of" Prabodhan" initiated by Prabodhankar Keshav Sitaram Thackeray from 16th October, 2020 to 15th October, 2021 through various activities.
In this regard, the following programmes will be organized by the Government of Maharashtra during the centenary year of Prabodhan, which has made a significant contribution in the progressive ideological formation of Maharashtra, from 16th October, 2020 to 15th October, 2021:
