GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. RASANDHO-1012/ PR No. 126/2012/ Language-3 dated 17th April, 2015.
As per the Maharashtra State Cultural Policy 2010, on the lines of All India Marathi Sahitya Sammelan, the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture is implementing the scheme of" Grants to Other Marathi Sahitya Sammelans" to provide a platform to literary gatherings of people belonging to different streams and marginalized sections based on the overall nature of the gathering. In pursuance of the announcement made at the 8th Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan held at Kolhapur on 28th December, 2019, the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan has received a one-time special grant of Rs. The proposal for sanction of financial assistance to the extent of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government Decision, it is hereby approved to sanction one time special financial assistance of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) for organizing Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan through Warkari Sahitya Parishad, Maharashtra subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture Rs. 25.00 lakh should be disbursed to Warkari Sahitya Parishad, Maharashtra and since this is a one-time special case, it should not be treated as a precedent.
The financial assistance provided by the Government of Maharashtra should be used only for the activities to be organized in the All India Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan on Marathi language and literature.
(3) The certificate of appropriation of funds made available by the Government for the Akhil Bharatiya Marathi Sant Sahitya Sammelan, the Sammelan report, the statement of the articles examined by the Chartered Accountant should be submitted by the Warkari Sahitya Parishad to the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board under the Government within 1 month.
Also, the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture should submit a compliance report to the government along with a utility certificate.
