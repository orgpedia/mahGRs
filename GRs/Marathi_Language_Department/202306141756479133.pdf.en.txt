The Proposal:
Reference No. for update of Marathi Encyclopedia. Under the decision of the government here, approval has been given to set up the Knowledge Boards for 3 years. Reference No. 2 decision of the government. up to 22.02.2022 and Reference No. 04 Under the decision of the Government here, the term of the Knowledge Boards has been extended till 22.02.2023. As per the guidance of the Chairman, Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board, changes are proposed in the prevailing working of the Board. But, it's going to take some time. Therefore, the government was considering extending the existing activities of the Knowledge Board so that the work of the Encyclopedia does not get interrupted and the payments for the works yet to be received by the Knowledge Boards are transferred from the office of the Grants and Accounts Officer and paid to the concerned.
Government's decision:
Various Knowledge Boards have been set up for thematic upgradation of Marathi Encyclopedia and to take notes of new concepts, new thought streams in various disciplines. The records received from the Gyan Mandal are examined and published on the website of the Board by the office of the Maharashtra State Marathi Encyclopedia Production Board. Since the information in the volumes of the Encyclopedia needs to be updated in a timely manner, the period of extension of Gyan Mandalas from 23.02.2023 to 22.02.2024 is being extended. As per this extension, 4 posts of Assistant and 1 post of Computer Officer have been sanctioned for the work of Gyan Mandal.
