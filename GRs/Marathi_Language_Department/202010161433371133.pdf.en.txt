The government has decided to confer Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar to a renowned litterateur for his outstanding work in the field of Marathi literature/ literature and Shri Pu Bhagwat Puraskar to an outstanding publishing house for its work in the field of literature production. The Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar carries a cash prize of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only), a cash prize of Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh only), a medal and a citation.
The scheme, which is being implemented by the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, has been transferred to the Marathi Language Department since 2012-13. The Selection Committee has been reconstituted as per Government Decisions 3 and 4 and the criteria for selection of awardees have been prescribed. As the new Council of Ministers has come into existence in the State of Maharashtra with effect from 28.11.2019, the proposal to reconstitute the selection committee for Vinda Karandikar Lifetime Achievement Award and Shri P.
