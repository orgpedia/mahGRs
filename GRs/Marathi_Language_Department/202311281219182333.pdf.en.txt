Background:
The event is being organised by Konkan Marathi Sahitya Parishad in association with Mauritius Marathi Mandal Federation, Mauritius Marathi Cultural Centre Trust and Marathi Speaking Union under the Ministry of Art and Cultural Heritage, Government of Mauritius. In connection with the Marathi Sahitya Sammelan scheduled to be held on 2nd and 3rd December, 2023, a financial assistance of 1.5 crore has been sanctioned to Konkan Marathi Sahitya Parishad, Ratnagiri. The proposal was submitted vide letter dated 16.10.2023. Konkan Marathi Sahitya Parishad has been invited to present literary activities in Mauritius. In pursuance of this proposal, Konkan Marathi Sahitya Parishad, Ratnagiri has been given Rs. The government had proposed to provide Rs. 1.50 crore for the purpose.
Government's decision:
Konkan Marathi Sahitya Parishad, Ratnagiri. To participate in the Marathi Literary Meet scheduled on 2nd and 3rd December, 2023 in Mauritius. The government has approved the release of Rs 1.50 crore through the State Marathi Development Society as a" special case."
The government's approval cannot be taken as precedent. Approval is subject to the following terms and conditions.
