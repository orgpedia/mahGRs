Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra has awarded the best Marathi literature in the Marathi language. The Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award scheme is in operation. Under this scheme, books of different literary genres are accepted from writers who have produced literature in Marathi language in the state and outside the state. Marathi literature produced/ first published in 2020. September 10, 2012 and d. As of February 2, 2016. The proposal to announce the Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award-2020 was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Marathi literature produced/ first published in the year 2020 will be published by Swat. As per the decision of the Screening Committee constituted for conferring the Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award, the names of the 31 writers/ litterateurs who have been declared eligible for the said 35 awards as per the list in the accompanying prospectus and the awardees who have been declared eligible for the said award, as per the decision of the Government dated 10th September, 2012 and 2nd February, 2016, respectively.
2. the self. The amount of cash award for the Yashwantrao Chavan State Literary Award-2020, as mentioned in Annexure-I of the Government Resolution dated 10th September, 2012, subject to a maximum of Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One Lakh only) and not less than Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only). The award comprises a cash prize, a memento and a certificate. The list contains the name of the literary category for which the award has been given, the name of the author, the book and the publishing house concerned, and the amount of the award, along with the statement of the names of the 31 authors/ litterateurs who have been declared eligible.
For this purpose, Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
