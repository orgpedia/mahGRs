The Proposal:
Reference (2) Under the Government decision herein, a" Marathi Certification Committee" has been constituted to reconsider the standardization, alphabetization etc. in respect of the Marathi language. The committee In the first meeting held on 18.11.2021, discussions were held regarding" Suggesting measures to promote the study and research of Devanagari script and inclusion of script experts and computer expert persons in the Marathi Certification Fixation Committee in line with the government decision in the context (1)." In pursuance of this discussion, the Chairman of the Marathi Certification Committee recommended the inclusion of Mr. Girish Dalvi, a scriptologist and computer expert as a member of the Committee.
