The government is planning to introduce Marathi language in the state. In this regard, the following recommendations are made:
1. On the occasion of 350th Shivarajyabhisheka this year, various programs such as lectures, symposiums, discussions on books/ literature on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj based on the central theme" 350th Shivarajyabhisheka of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Celebration of Marathi Language Pride Day" should be organized to awaken the thoughts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in Marathi mind.
2. The Regional Offices should disseminate information about the progress and progress of the Marathi Language Department and the Regional Offices through various mediums.
The Marathi Language Committee of the respective districts should select and honour the Marathi language promoters, scholars, researchers, writers and litterateurs at the local level who have been working continuously for the promotion and promotion of Marathi language and literature in the respective districts of Maharashtra.
The Marathi Language Officer in the District Collector's Office of the respective district should publish the information about the activities and schemes implemented by the Marathi Language Department and the four offices under the Marathi Language Department for the promotion, preservation and promotion of the Marathi language through all the publications in the respective district.
Various competitions, media campaigns, discussions on books and dictionaries, seminars, lectures, etc. should be organized for the promotion and propagation of Marathi language.
Considering the need of the hour, instructions have already been given to conduct as many events, seminars, training as possible online. The program should be organized by all government offices to attract more and more young generation through employment-oriented activities with the aim of promoting Marathi language.
The Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai, the State Marathi Development Institute, Mumbai, the Maharashtra State Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai, the Directorate of Languages, Mumbai should announce the schedule of online workshops to be conducted during the year as mentioned above. Accordingly, a report will be prepared and submitted to the government.
All government offices, Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture, Mumbai, State Marathi Development Society, Mumbai, Maharashtra State Encyclopedia Production Board, Mumbai, Directorate of Language, Mumbai should organize online workshops with the help of experts on how to effectively promote Marathi language online.
