Government circular
After the formation of the Department, a provisional common seniority list for the period from 01.01.2011 to 01.01.2022 of the Assistant Director of Languages (Training and Establishment), Group B (Gazetted), on the establishment of the Directorate of Languages under the Department, has been prepared vide Reference No. 1 as per the provisions of Reference No. 2 has been published here.
Reference No. 2 from the Directorate of Language. 2 The Government has not received any objections to the provisional seniority list within the prescribed time frame. 2 The provisional seniority of Assistant Director of Languages (Training& Establishment), Group B (Gazetted), Officers of the lone cadre for the period from 01.01.2011 to 01.01.2022 is being finalized as per Form A, A-1 and A-2 attached herewith. During the year 2011 and 2012, there was no regular officer in the cadre of Assistant Director of Languages (Training and Establishment). Therefore, the Assistant Director of Languages (Training and Establishment) for the year 2011 and 2012 is the" Nirank" of seniority in the cadre. Seniority in Form A, A-1 and A-2 attached with the cadre of Assistant Director of Languages (Training& Establishment) is being finalised subject to the following conditions.
