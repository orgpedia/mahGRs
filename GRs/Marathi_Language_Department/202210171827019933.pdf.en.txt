Government circular:
General Administration Department, Notification No. Tamlap 1081/ CR-344/ WIS-B, Dy. Under 06.05.1991, provisions have been made to make Marathi shorthand/ Marathi typewriting examination compulsory for English shorthand writers, English shorthand writers and English typists. It contains instructions as per the notifications, government decisions, government circulars mentioned in reference number 2 to 8 from time to time.
