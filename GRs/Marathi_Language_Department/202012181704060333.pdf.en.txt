The Proposal:
Section 3 of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 provides that the State shall take steps to create an enabling environment for the right to equality and dignity of persons with disabilities and to protect the right to equality for women with disabilities as per Section 4. The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 provides for 4% reservation for physically challenged persons in government jobs. However, in order to take measures to ensure representation of persons with disabilities in various schemes/ activities of the Government, it was under the consideration of the Government to take action to provide benefits of the schemes of this Regional Office of the Maharashtra State Board of Literature and Culture under the Department.
Government's decision:
Under this decision, the government is giving priority representation to the following persons with disabilities in various schemes implemented by the Maharashtra State Literary and Cultural Board.
