Pursuant to the approval given by the Central Government and proposal received from MTCIL dated 14/02/2019, Rs. Administrative approval for the budget of Rs. 3759.20 lakh was under consideration.
Memorandum of Understanding: As per the approval given by the Central Government to implement the Smart Class project in the minority concentration schools of the recognized groups in the state, a total of Rs. The budget of Rs. 3759.20 lakh is being accorded administrative approval subject to prescribed terms and conditions.
2. Terms and Conditions: 1. MTCIL shall supply smart class materials to the concerned school at its own cost wherever required. The complaint should be made to the principal of the school or to an officer appointed by the school education and sports department. This acknowledgement will be treated as a delivery report.
2. MeTCIL should start supply of materials, project construction and project training for smart classes in a phased manner. The list of schools under the project will be provided by the School Education Department. In the schools mentioned in the list, the MTCIL company should complete the work of supplying materials within 120 days.
(iii) If there is any defect/ defect in the material within three months of supply to the school concerned, the company shall be obliged to replace the material at its own cost.
IV. MTCIL should implement the tender process very transparently while selecting the hardware.
In case of any change in the syllabus while the project is in progress by MTCIL, updated e-content approved by SCERT should be provided.
The MTCIL shall submit monthly progress report of the project to the Department of School Education and Sports and the Department of Minority Development during the implementation of the project and also inform both the departments by letter on completion of the project.
VII. MTCIL shall set up a state level help desk to resolve any doubts/ issues/ technical difficulties arising out of the hardware. The doubts/ problems/ technical difficulties will need to be resolved within three days.
The MTCIL shall appoint one Technical Assistant for every 50 schools.
IX. MTCIL shall ensure that while procuring the material required for the project, the same is as per the GFR applied by the State Government. If any dispute arises between the Minorities Development Department and TCIL during the project, it should be resolved amicably by the Project Implementation Control Committee. In case of a bilateral dispute, they can appeal in the court under the Bombay High Court. 84% of the amount will be paid after the purchase of materials for the project, delivery, construction of the project and the remaining 6% will be paid after the completion of the project.

The responsibility of completing the Smart Class project on time and resolving the difficulties in it will be of the Controlling Committee appointed under the chairmanship of Joint Secretary (Student Development), School Education and Sports Department.
