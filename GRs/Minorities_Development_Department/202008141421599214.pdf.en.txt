Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, New Delhi. Rajiv Gandhi's birth anniversary is celebrated as Sadbhavana Diwas on August 20 every year. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has also issued an Office Memorandum No. F- 28-22/2006-V. S4, the. In pursuance of the directions issued under 18th July, 2006, in pursuance of the aforesaid Sadbhavana Diwas, the Hon. from August 20, 2020. The Government has decided to observe 5 th September, 2020 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada." The main objectives of this fortnight are to promote the spirit of unity, harmony and avoidance of violence among the people of different religions and different languages of the state.

Further, to celebrate the fortnight from 20th August, 2020 to 5th September, 2020 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" in the State, following programmes should be organized as per the guidelines of the Central and State Governments in the wake of COVID-19.
A) The concerned District Collectors under their chairmanship should organize interaction/ guidance programs through online/ webinar etc. in the wake of COVID-19 to spread the message of social unity in the district headquarters of the state.
