Government's decision
Maharashtra State Wakf Tribunal, Aurangabad
The opening of a self-financing account for the amounts is being approved subject to the following conditions.
i) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit ii) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
iii) The amount related Waqf claims, Waqf disputed rent amount, penalties, amount received through appeal, amount collected from litigants pertaining to claim petition only is to be credited to this PLA.
iv) Amount deposited by the litigants may be refunded as per orders of the tribunal.
v) No revenue receipts/Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PLA.
