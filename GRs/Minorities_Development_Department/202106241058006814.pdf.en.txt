The Maharashtra State Minorities Commission Act, 2004.
The Maharashtra State Minorities Commission has been constituted under the Maharashtra State Minorities Act, 2004 to address the problems of the minority community and bring them into the mainstream of society with a view to the economic and social development of the sections of the minority groups in the state. For the Office of the Commission
As per the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the government was considering ensuring posts in Group A to Group D cadres from the sanctioned posts.
Government's decision:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory to ensure a post in the government service for the disabled. The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has issued a notification in this regard. The List of Posts to be ensured for Persons with Disabilities as per the Notification dated 4th January, 2021 has been published under the heading List of Post Identification suitable for Person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 at Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. As per the notification, the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which have been ensured by the Central Government for the disabled, are in the state government establishment. For such positions,
