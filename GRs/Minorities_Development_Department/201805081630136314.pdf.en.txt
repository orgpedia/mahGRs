The Proposal:
The Department of Minority Development has implemented Area Development Programme for Minority Concentrated Urban Areas in the State since 2008-09. The Scheme has been approved for implementation during 2015-16 with effect from 18th June, 2015. Also Government Supplementary Letter, Minority Development Department, Kshevika-2015/ No.5/ K. 9, d. As of March 9, 2016. The Government have decided to insert a paragraph after paragraph (b) (ii) under the heading of Scheme Procedure in the Government Resolution dated 18th June, 2015. According to The matter of approving the proposals received by the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat from the MPs and MLAs and the Collector is under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
During 2018-19, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 26,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Forty Lakhs only) has been made. The Finance Department As on 02.04.2018, for the first nine months (ending December, 2018), 70% of the budgetary allocation has been approved. Thus, the total budget of Rs. 26,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Six Crores Forty Lakhs only) i.e. 70% of the provision amounting to Rs. 18,48,00, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Forty Eight Lakhs only) is available for disbursement till the end of December, 2018. These funds will be used for the 2018-19 financial year. The proposals received by the Government from Ministers/ MLAs/ Khasadars and Collectors and proposals for works in Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats will be approved by the Government under the Area Development Programme as indicated in Annexure A to this Government Resolution. A sum of Rs. 30,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Lakh only) is being sanctioned with the approval of the Government. The funds are being handed over to the respective Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats located in the respective districts.

The concerned Collector will act as Controlling Officer and Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursing the grants sanctioned to the respective Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Town Panchayats. The Collector shall ensure that the works mentioned in Annexure A have not been taken up from other schemes and if the work is sanctioned from other schemes, the Collector of the concerned district shall have the power to make changes in lieu of the other works of the scheme within the limits of the sanctioned funds. If it is not possible to do other works under this scheme in lieu of the above work, the funds allocated for it should be deposited in the government treasury immediately. Also, the concerned collector should get the budget of the above works from the construction department.
The Collector should take action to get all the documents in accordance with the terms and conditions of the government decision. After this, they should be given administrative and financial approval and the funds should be handed over to the concerned Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat. These works should be carried out by the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Town Panchayat through the Construction Department.
It is mandatory for the respective Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats to spend the funds only for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned as demanded by the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats in the State. After the disbursement of the grant by the concerned District Collector, the concerned Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayats are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal within a period of six months and the utility certificates in this regard are required to be submitted by the District Collector to the Minority Development Department in the format prescribed in Annexure' B" of this Government decision. The District Collectors concerned should monitor the progress of development works undertaken by the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats in the State under the Area Development Programme and submit a report to the Government.
