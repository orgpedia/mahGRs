Government's decision
As per the provisions of proviso 2 under Section 14 (1-A) of the Waqf Act, 1995, the members appointed to the Maharashtra State Waqf Board must include at least two women members. The government has decided to reserve one post each for women in Mutawallis of Waqf institutions with an income of Rs 1 lakh or more.
