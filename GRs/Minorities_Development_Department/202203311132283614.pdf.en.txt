The grant is being handed over to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad/ Public Works Department/ Public Works District Council through the respective District Collectors.
The District Collectors concerned shall act as Controlling Officers and Drawing and Disbursing Officers for disbursement of grants sanctioned to the minority dominated Gram Panchayats. The Collector concerned shall ensure that the works mentioned in Annexure-A are not taken up from other schemes and if the work is sanctioned from other schemes, the Collector of the concerned district shall have the power to make changes in lieu of the other works within the limits of the sanctioned funds. If it is not possible to do other work in lieu of the above work, the funds allocated for it should be deposited by the government. Also, the concerned collector should get the budget of the above works from the construction department.
The Collector should take action to get all the documents in accordance with the terms and conditions of the government decision. After this, they should be given administrative and financial approval and the funds should be handed over to the concerned CEO, Zila Parishad/ Public Works Department/ Public Works District Council.
3. It is mandatory for the respective Gram Panchayats to spend the funds only for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned. After disbursement of the grant by the concerned Collector/ CEO, concerned Zilla Parishad/ Public Works Department/ Public Works District Council, the Gram Panchayats are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal and submit the utility certificates in the prescribed format in Annexure' B" of this Government decision to the Minority Development Department.
