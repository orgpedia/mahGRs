The Proposal:
The scholarship scheme for economically weaker and meritorious students of minority communities of the state pursuing higher professional education and studying in all courses after Class XII is being implemented as per the Government decision dated 14th October 2011.
As per the decisions of the Government in the Reference Table 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7, under this scheme for the financial year 2019-20, Rs. 49,99,01, 072/-Rs. 10,41,28, 278/-Rs. 1,09,36, 829/-Rs. 60,32,154/-Rs. 21,55,244/- and Rs. 54,11,503/-Rs. 62,85,65, 080/- (Rupees Sixty Two Crore Eighty Five Lakhs, Sixty Five Thousand Eighty Eight only) has been provided for the distribution of scholarships to Minority students for the academic year 2019-20, from the sanctioned amount for the financial year 2019-20 and 2020-21. 35,62,33, 000/- has been disbursed. However, it was not materialised due to some technical reasons.

Now, vide letter dated 6th May, 2021 addressed to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, it is requested to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 53,51,09, 113/- (Rupees Fifty Three Crores, Fifty One Lakhs, Nine Thousand, One Hundred Thirteen only) for the allotment of scholarships to the students of the year 2019-20 and 2020-21. The following decisions are taken by the government:
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2020-21, an original budget provision of Rs. 120.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crores only) has been approved under the head of Subsidiary Grants (2235 A338) for the Scholarship Scheme for Minority Students pursuing higher professional education and all courses after 12th. The amount has not been disbursed yet.
The Government hereby approve the release of funds amounting to Rs. 53,51,09, 113/- (Rupees Fifty Three Crores, Fifty-one Lakhs, Nine Thousand, One Hundred and Thirteen only) for the distribution of scholarships to the remaining students of the academic year 2019-20 and 2020-21, dated 6th May, 2021 at serial number 9 in the context of Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra, State, Mumbai.
The amount is being handed over to the concerned Directorate for sanction of scholarship to the students belonging to minority communities who have been eligible for scholarship for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 for which the concerned Coordinating Officer will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and its Director as Controlling Officer. Since the disbursement of this scholarship is to be done expeditiously, the question of furnishing the monthly expenditure statement of this fund does not arise.
Further, it is hereby permitted to use the balance amount, if any, of the funds released for the scholarship for the year 2018-19, for the scholarship for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21, if required, and to use the balance amount for the scholarship for the year 2021-22, if any, from the funds released under the said Government Decision. The details of expenditure and utilization certificate of the above mentioned accounts should be submitted to the government within the stipulated time. Also, all the directions/ rules/ government decisions/ orders issued in this regard by the government from time to time and the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the supervision of the Comptroller and Auditor General should be followed while spending this grant.
