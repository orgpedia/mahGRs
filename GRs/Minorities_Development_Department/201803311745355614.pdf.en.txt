The Proposal:
In order to provide opportunities to the candidates belonging to minority communities of the State in Government/ Paramilitary Services, for competitive examinations like Central Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission/ Banking Service Recruitment etc. as well as for the preparation of Common Entrance Examinations for admission to professional courses and for starting special tuition classes for the minority candidates who have failed in class 10th and 12th, Reference No. 1 has been approved.
Under this scheme, training centers have been selected for training minority candidates in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane and Pune cities of the state. 2 has been implemented. In pursuance of the above decision of the Government, the training institutes in the above mentioned training centres have submitted their proposals seeking grants to the Government. It was under consideration of the Government to sanction grant-in-aid in the first phase of 2017-18 to those institutions which have started physical training classes.
Government Decision: Under the scheme" Maulana Azad Free Tuition and Related Schemes," it was found that the training institutes selected for the training center in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane and Pune have started physical training classes.
