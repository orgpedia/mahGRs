The Finance Ministry has approved the release of funds for the financial year 2018-19 for the Self Help Group Scheme for Minority Women in Minority Concentration Areas.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Minority Development
Govt. Clearing Paper No: Mavim 2018/ Q. No. 61/18/ K. 9
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: August 1, 2018.
Government Resolution, Minority Development Department, MAVIM 2018/ No. 61/18/ K. 9, D. June 8, 2018
Government's stand:
Under the scheme, the Government has allotted Rs. 1,40,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Lakh only) has been approved. In paragraph 4 of this Government Resolution, it is stated as follows:
Expenditure for the year 2018-19 is Rs. ZE-1, 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 200 Other Programmes, Schemes under State Schemes (01) Assistance to Minorities (01) (10) Subsidiary Grants to Self Help Groups (2235 A 169), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried).
Instead, read the following paragraph 4.
Expenditure for the financial year 2018-19. Expenditure from grants sanctioned under the head ZE-1, 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 200 Other Programmes, (01) Minority Assistance (01) (10) Subsidiary Grants to Self Help Groups (Expenditure on Programmes) (2235 A169), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
