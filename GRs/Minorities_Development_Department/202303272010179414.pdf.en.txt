The Proposal:
The scholarship scheme is being implemented by the state government since 2008-2009 for students belonging to Muslim, Buddhist, Khishwan, Jain, Sikh, Parsi and Jewish/ Jewish minority communities in the state.
To expand the scope of the scheme and to increase the amount of scholarship and to implement the scheme in a revised form from 2022-23, the above No. The decision has been approved by the government.
