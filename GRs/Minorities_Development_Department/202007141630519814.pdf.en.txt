Introduction: The scholarship scheme for economically weaker and meritorious students of minority communities in the state who are pursuing higher professional education and studying in all courses after Class XII is being implemented as per the government decision dated 14 October 2011.
Under this scheme, the Nodal Officer of the scheme under the Government decision at Table No. 6 under reference, Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai; Director, Medical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune, will be paid Rs.
49,99,01, 072 (Rupees Nineteen Fifty Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs, One Thousand Seventy Two only) has been provided for the year 2019-20 for the purpose of distribution of scholarships to the students belonging to minority communities. Now Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 8th May, 2020 has requested to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 10,21,50, 331/- (Rupees Ten Crore Twenty One Lakh Fifty Thousand Three Hundred and Thirty One only) and Director, Directorate of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide e-mail dated 12th May, 2020 has requested to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 19,77,947/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakh, Seventy Seven Thousand, Nine Hundred and Forty Seven only) from the sanctioned grant for the allotment of scholarships for the financial year 2019-20. The following decisions are taken by the government:

GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the head' Subsidiary Grants (2235 A338) for Scholarship Scheme for Minority Students pursuing Higher Vocational Education and all courses after XII," a basic budget provision of Rs. 120.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crore only) has been approved for the financial year 2020-21. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 12.00 crore (Rupees Twelve Crore only) has been provided as 10% of the cost for the period up to June, 2020.
Out of this amount of Rs. 12.00 crore, no amount has been released for any purpose so far. Hence, the entire amount of Rs. 12.00 crore is available for disbursement through Budget Distribution System. As per the letter dated 8th May, 2020 issued by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra, State, Mumbai vide Roll No.7 dated 8th May, 2020, out of 21,839 students sanctioned in the year 2019-20, a sum of Rs. 25,000/- per student for 21,612 students allotted on DBT portal for generating scholarship bills and another Rs. 10,21,50, 331/- for 8,774 students for providing second installment amount is required and requested to be made available. Further, vide letter dated 12.05.2020 dated 8.05.2020 addressed to Director Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune, it has been requested that a total amount of Rs. 19,77,947/- may be required and made available to the total 630 students belonging to minority community pursuing higher education.
Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the disbursement of a sum of Rs. 10,21,50, 331/- (Rupees Ten Crores, Twenty One Lakhs, Fifty Thousand Three Hundred and Thirty One only) to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and Rs. 19,77,947/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs, Seventy Seven Thousand, Nine Hundred and Forty Seven only) to the Directorate of Higher Education, Pune, aggregating to Rs. 10,41,28, 278/- (Rupees Ten Crores, Forty One Lakh, Twenty Eight Thousand Two Hundred and Seventy Eight only).
The amount is being handed over to the concerned Directorate for sanction of scholarship to the students belonging to minority community who have been eligible for scholarship for the year 2019-20, for which the concerned Coordinating Officer will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and its Director as Controlling Officer. Since the disbursement of this scholarship is to be done expeditiously, the question of furnishing the monthly expenditure statement of this fund does not arise.
The expenditure of the two Directorates shall be limited to the amount of funds sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21.
Also, if any amount is left out of the funds allocated for the scholarship for the year 2018-19 and if necessary, it should be used for the scholarship for the year 2019-20.
Otherwise, it is also being allowed to use this fund later for the scholarship of this year i.e. 2020-21. The details of expenditure and utilization certificate of the above mentioned accounts should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time. Also, all the directions/ rules/ government decisions/ orders issued in this regard by the government from time to time and the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the supervision of the Comptroller and Auditor General should be followed while spending this grant.
