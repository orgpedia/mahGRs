The Proposal:
In order to make the administration of the state more dynamic and people-oriented, the e-governance policy of the state has been decided in accordance with the Government Resolution No. 3. Computerization of Government departments/ offices. As per the Government Decision dated 29.10.2001
A comprehensive action plan has been prepared. Also, under the said Government decision, it has been directed to form a Project Implementation Committee in every administrative division to implement the project of computerization under the offices under the administrative department.
Accordingly, the matter of forming a Project Implementation Committee to implement the e-governance project of the Minority Development Department and the field offices under the department was under the consideration of the government.
