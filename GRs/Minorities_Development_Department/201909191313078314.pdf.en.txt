The Government has approved the proposals of Gram Panchayats received from the Ministers/ Ministers of State/ MPs/ MLAs and District Collectors out of the provision made in the Budget for 2019-20. Accordingly, as stated in Annexure-" A" to this Government Decision, Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh only) is being sanctioned under the Rural Area Development Programme and the grant is being handed over to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad through the District Collector of the concerned district.
The District Collector of the concerned district shall act as the Controlling Officer and Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursing the grants sanctioned to the respective minority dominated Gram Panchayats. The Collector shall ensure that the works referred to in Annexure-A are not taken up under any other scheme and if the work is sanctioned under any other scheme, the Collector of the concerned district shall have the power to make changes in respect of such work within the limits of the sanctioned funds. If it is not possible to do other work in lieu of the above work, the funds allocated for it should be deposited by the government. Also, the concerned collector should get the budget of the above works from the construction department. The Collector should take action to get all the documents in accordance with the terms and conditions of the government decision. After this, administrative and financial approval should be given to them and the funds should be handed over to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, District Council.
3. It is mandatory for the respective Gram Panchayats to spend the funds only for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned. After disbursement of the grant by the District Collector/ CEO, concerned Zilla Parishad, the Gram Panchayats are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal and submit the utility certificates to the Minority Development Department in the prescribed format in Annexure-B of this Government Decision. The District Collectors concerned should monitor the progress of development works taken up by the respective minority-dominated gram panchayats in the State under the Rural Area Development Programme and submit a report to the Government.
