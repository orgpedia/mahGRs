The Proposal:
The Grant-in-Aid Scheme for providing infrastructure facilities in Government recognized Private Aided/ Unaided Schools, Junior Colleges, Industrial Training Institutes, Municipal/ Municipal Council Schools and Disabled Schools having a student population of Religious Minorities is being implemented as per Government Decision dated 7th October, 2015.
Under the said scheme, the eligible schools in the proposal received from the concerned Collectorate to the Government are being approved for sanction of grants for the financial year 2022-23 as follows.

Government Decision: The decision was taken by a high-level selection committee headed by the concerned District Collector.
The applications received from them as on 7.10.2015 have been scrutinized and after scrutiny, the list of eligible schools, recommendations and proposals along with demand for necessary funds have been sent to the Government for approval. All the proposals received from the respective Collectorate will be scrutinized at the government level and the number of schools that qualify in it and they will be approved (maximum limit is Rs. 2.00 lakh) The amount of grant is as follows: Rs. 1,80,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore, Eighty Lakhs, Only) is hereby approved by the Government.
The funds will be handed over to the respective district collectors. The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Controlling Officer for this fund. The names of the eligible schools for which grants have been sanctioned along with the details of the nature of the demand for the school, the year in which the grant has been availed earlier, are given in the Annexure to this Government Decision.
