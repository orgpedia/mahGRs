The proposal-
A Government Resolution dated 2nd March, 2010 has been issued for construction of hostels for girl students of minority community in all the districts of the State.
The modalities for construction of hostels for girls belonging to minority communities on the land available in various universities in the State have been decided as per the Government decision dated 20.12.2011. Further, the modalities for the management of hostels being constructed for students belonging to minority communities have been decided as per the Government Decisions dated 21st June, 2013 and 28th July, 2015.
