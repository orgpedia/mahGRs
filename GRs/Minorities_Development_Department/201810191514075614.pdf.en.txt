Government's decision:
In accordance with the procedure prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 17.12.2016, Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department, for deputation of employees of offices other than the Ministerial Department to Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C posts in Maulana Azad Minorities Economic Development Corporation, the following Committee is being constituted:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | Joint/ Deputy Secretary (A.V.V./ Office- 8) | Chairman             |
| 2. | Under Secretary (A.V.V./ Office- 8)         | the members          |
| 3. | Officer of the Cell (A.V.V./ Office)- 8     | The Member Secretary |
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
After the selection of one such candidate for one post by the said Committee, orders will be issued for deputation to the respective Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C posts after approval of the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary of the Minority Development Department as the appointing authority.
