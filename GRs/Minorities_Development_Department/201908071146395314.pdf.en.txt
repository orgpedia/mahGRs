The Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Punjabi language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through ideological exchange between the writers of Punjabi and Marathi languages. Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi is implementing various programmes under the above reference No. 2 The Government has decided to provide Rs. Administrative approval has been accorded for the expenditure of Rs. 9,00,000/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs only) for the period of five months from August, 2019 to December, 2019. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2019-20 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The above reference No. 20 provides for the release of grants-in-aid for the period of 5 months from August, 2019 to December, 2019 out of the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20. 1 and 2 have been approved by the government. Considering this, Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi has been allocated Rs. 15,00,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) out of the subsidy available for disbursement of Rs. The grant under the programme amounting to Rs. 9,00,000/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs only) is being disbursed to the office of Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy, Mumbai. The Superintendent-cum-Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi, Mumbai shall ensure that the expenditure incurred on the implementation of various programmes shall be within the prescribed limits. Also, the government should take action according to the terms and conditions as prescribed.
The amount of ECS should be drawn in the name of the Under Secretary (1), and Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Minority Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai. Also, the deputy secretary (establishment) of the department is being declared as the controlling officer.
