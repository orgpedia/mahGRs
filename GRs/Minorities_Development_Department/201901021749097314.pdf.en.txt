The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through intellectual exchange between Urdu and Marathi language writers. With reference to the matter of (compulsory) expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi, vide the above Reference No. 3 As per the decision of the Government, out of the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2018-19, Rs. Administrative approval has been granted for an expenditure of Rs. 3.00 lakh. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision- To disburse subsidy within the limit of 70% of the subsidy sanctioned for the financial year 2018-19, as per the above Reference No. 1 and 2 have been approved. In view of this, the expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy (Mandatory), which was sanctioned in 2018-19, is Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs only) subject to 70% of the subsidy i.e. Rs. 14.00 lakhs is available for distribution.
