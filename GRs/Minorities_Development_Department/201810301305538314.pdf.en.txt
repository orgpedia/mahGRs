The proposals received by the Government from the MPs/ MLAs and the District Collectors and the proposals for works in the limits of Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats will be approved by the Government under the Area Development Programme as indicated in Annexure A to this Government Decision. A sum of Rs. 10,33,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Thirty Three Lakhs only) is being sanctioned. The funds are being handed over to the respective Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats located in the respective districts.
The concerned Collector will act as Controlling Officer and Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursing the grants sanctioned to the respective Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Town Panchayats. The Collector shall ensure that the works mentioned in Annexure A have not been taken up from other schemes and if the work is sanctioned from other schemes, the Collector of the concerned district shall have the power to make changes in lieu of the other works of the scheme within the limits of the sanctioned funds. If it is not possible to do other works under this scheme in lieu of the above work, the funds allocated for it should be deposited in the government treasury immediately. Also, the concerned collector should get the budget of the above works from the construction department. The Collector should take action to get all the documents in accordance with the terms and conditions of the government decision. After this, they should be given administrative and financial approval and the funds should be handed over to the concerned Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat. These works should be carried out by the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Town Panchayat through the Construction Department.
It is mandatory for the respective Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats to spend the funds only for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned as demanded by the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats in the State. After the disbursement of the grant by the concerned District Collector, the concerned Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayats are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal within a period of six months and the utility certificates in this regard are required to be submitted by the District Collector to the Minority Development Department in the format prescribed in Annexure' B" of this Government decision. The District Collectors concerned should monitor the progress of development works undertaken by the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats in the State under the Area Development Programme and submit a report to the Government.
