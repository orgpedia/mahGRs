During the year 2018-19, the Collector, Latur, has been given the charge of the office of the Deputy Commissioner in Column No. This Government Decision authorizes drawing and disbursing from the budget distribution system within the limit of the total amount sanctioned as per (08) and (09).
The Collector, Latur should immediately disburse the amount collected for the construction of Sadbhav Mandap/ CSC for minorities at Udgir in Latur district under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) to the implementing agencies concerned for the purpose of such development works.
The District Collector of the concerned district will be the Chief Controller and Enforcement Officer for the implementation of Sadbhav Mandap/ CSC construction under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK).
Construction of Sadbhav Mandap/ CSC for Minorities at Udgir approved under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) should be done by the Municipal Council, Udgir as per the type plan of the Central Government and the detailed budget for the same as per the technical approval given by the competent authority of the Public Works Department.
The concerned implementing agency should expeditiously complete the necessary aspects for the implementation of Sadbhav Mandap/ CSC construction approved under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK). As per the government norms and the technical approval given by the competent authority for the construction of Sadbhav Mandap/ CSC, the tender process should be started immediately and the work should be informed to the government.
