To provide scholarships to students studying in 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th classes and Industrial Training Institutes, thereby enhancing the educational quality of students, increasing their employability and helping to improve their economic status, the Government has decided to implement the Dr. Zakir Hussain Madrasa Modernization Scheme by providing infrastructure for modernization of Madrasas, providing grants for library and grant for honorarium to teachers. No. 152/10/ KA-6 dated 11th October, 2013 has been approved. A sum of Rs. 08.00 crore has been sanctioned for the year 2019-20 for this scheme. As per Government Circular, Finance Division No:-Finance- 2019/ P.S.92/ Finance-3, dated 08.07.2019, 60% of the annual budgetary allocation under these schemes i.e. Rs. 4.80 crore. Accordingly, the Government is issuing the following decisions for the allocation of grants to Madrasas in the financial year 2019-20 under this scheme.

Random Government approval is being given for sanction of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 1,47,50, 000/- (Rupees One Crore, Forty Seven Lakh Fifty Thousand only). The grants mentioned in front of the districts mentioned in the joint letter with this government decision are being handed over to the concerned district collectors. It is also being approved to disburse the funds handed over to them by the concerned District Collectors through the Budget Distribution System (BDS). For this, the concerned Collector will act as Controller and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The amount sanctioned at the Government level shall be credited to the account in the Nationalized Bank of the Madrasa concerned in the form of a letter of contribution attached by the concerned Collector or any other officer of his office authorized by him for this purpose.
The remaining amount should be deposited in the government treasury.
Under this scheme, the madrassas concerned should spend the funds for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned in the current year.
After disbursement of the grant to the madrasas, the madrasas concerned are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal within a period of three months from the actual receipt of the grant and thereafter submit the utilization certificate in the prescribed format to the respective district collectors.
