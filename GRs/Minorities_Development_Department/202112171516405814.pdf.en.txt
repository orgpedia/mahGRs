The Proposal:
Application for inclusion in the Hajj pilgrimage including training, transportation etc. for Hajj pilgrims as per the Hajj Act, 2002 of the Central Government through the office of the Maharashtra State Hajj Committee.
After receiving the necessary training on it, the Central Haj Committee takes action to determine the number of pilgrims within the limit of the number allocated for the state. Approximately 50,000 applications are received each year. The work goes on till late in the night during the processing period in order to scrutinize these applications within the prescribed time limit. The Haj Committee has brought to the notice of the government that there is difficulty in making available photocopies of necessary documents on time as the process is going on till late in the night. To provide a separate Xerox machine for the office of the Committee.
The proposal has been received from the Maharashtra State Haj Committee. It was for the government to approve it.
Government's decision:
