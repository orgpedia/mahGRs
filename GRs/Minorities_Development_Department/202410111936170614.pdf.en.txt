The Proposal: -
Maharashtra State Punjabi Literature aims at the development, preservation and progress of Punjabi language in the state and to strengthen the spirit of national integration by providing ideological exchange among Punjabi and Marathi language writers.
The Academy was established by Government Decision, No. Pansa-
2017 / Q. No. 176 / Karyasana-4, dated 05.10.2018. Maharashtra State Punjabi
The reorganisation of the Sahitya Akademi and the appointment of non-official members and office bearers on it was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Akademi has been reconstituted with the following appointments of Executive and Non-Governmental Members as nominated on the Academy:
