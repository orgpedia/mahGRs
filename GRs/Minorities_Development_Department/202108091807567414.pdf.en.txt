Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, New Delhi. Rajiv Gandhi's birth anniversary is celebrated as Sadbhavana Diwas on August 20 every year. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has also issued an Office Memorandum No. F- 28-22/2006-V. S4, the. In pursuance of the directions issued under 18th July, 2006, in pursuance of the aforesaid Sadbhavana Diwas, the Hon. from August 20, 2021. The Government has decided to observe 5 th September, 2021 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada." The main objectives of this fortnight are to promote the spirit of unity, harmony and avoidance of violence among the people of different religions and different languages of the state.

d) Considering the background of the Corona virus, the concerned should take care that the Sadbhavna Day program is completed by following the rules regarding social distancing.
e) Masks are mandatory for all. Be sure to follow all safety precautions. The event will be live-streamed on the website so that more people can watch it at home.
e) In the wake of COVID-19, Director, Cultural Affairs, State of Maharashtra should organize interaction/ guidance programs through online/ webinar etc. on the subject of Sadbhavana with the participation of the people, especially the youth, in Brihanmumbai.
As universities, colleges and schools are closed in the wake of COVID-19, no events can be held there this year.
All Covid-19 guidelines issued by the central and state governments will be strictly followed.
Further, to celebrate the fortnight from 20th August, 2021 to 5th September, 2021 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" in the State, as per the guidelines of the Central and State Governments in the wake of COVID-19, the following programmes should be organized.
The concerned District Collectors should organize interaction/ guidance programmes through online/ webinar etc. in the wake of COVID-19 to spread the message of social unity in the district headquarters of the state under their chairmanship.
(b) The District Collector should invite eminent freedom fighters of the district to speak in the interactions/ guidance programmes organized through online/ webinars etc. in the wake of COVID-19.
Director, Cultural Affairs, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to organize interaction/ guidance programmes through online/ webinar etc. during this fortnight (in the wake of COVID-19).
All Covid-19 guidelines issued by the central and state governments will be strictly followed.
4. Directorate General of Information and Public Relations should contact the local authorities of Doordarshan and All India Radio for publicity of the above-mentioned" Sadbhavana Diwas" and" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" programmes and give adequate publicity to the programmes.
