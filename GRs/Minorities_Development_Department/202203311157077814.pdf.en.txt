Introduction: A new website/ Case Information Management System is being prepared by the National Informatics Centre (NIC) for the Maharashtra State Waqf Tribunal and the government is considering providing a grant to the National Informatics Centre (NIC) as the first phase development cost (Design Approval) of 20% of the total proposal.
Government Resolution: Maharashtra State Wakf Tribunal has been accorded the status of a Civil Court and the functioning of this office is similar to that of a Court. The National Informatics Centre (NIC) has issued Reference No. A government proposal was sought. Accordingly, the National Informatics Centre (NIC) issued its Reference No. 2 for Rs. 82,08,652/- (Rupees Eighty Two Lakh Eight Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty Two only) has been submitted to the Government. Reference No. 3 Administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the government decision here. Now, as per the approval given by the Planning Department and the Finance Department, the National Informatics Centre (NIC) will have to bear 20% of the total proposal as the first phase development cost (Design Approval) to start the work of the website. 16,41,730/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Forty One Thousand Seven Hundred and Thirty only) should be handed over to the Under Secretary (K-1), Minority Development Department for payment.
