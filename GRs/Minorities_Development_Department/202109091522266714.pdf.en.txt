The Proposal:
The building of the Nagpur Haj House was constructed by the Nagpur Improvement Trust. It was transferred to Maharashtra State Haj Committee on 29/09/2012. The fire department was informed about the incident. Thus, the firefighting system for this building needs repairs and upgrades. Accordingly, based on the 2020-21 schedule for the construction of the fire fighting system, Rs. 1,03,81, 500/- has been prepared.
Government's decision:
Haj House, Nagpur for installation of Fire Fighting System at a cost of Rs. The budget of Rs. 1,03,81, 500/- (Rupees One Crore Three Lakh Eighty One Thousand Five Hundred only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
1.The work shall be carried out by an electrical contractor licensed and registered with the Government in accordance with the procedure prescribed by the Government.
The electrical material used for the work should be of ISI mark.
