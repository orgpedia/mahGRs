Government circular
As per the decision taken by the Central Government in 1986, the week from 19 th November, 2022 to 25 th November, 2022 is to be celebrated as" Qaumi Ekta Week" in the State and maximum number of people are to be involved in the following programmes to be organized in the week. Accordingly, this year, the period from Saturday, 19 th November, 2022 to Friday, 25 th November, 2022 should be observed as" Qaumi Ekta Week" and maximum people should be involved in it. In view of the guidelines issued by the Central and State Governments from time to time in the wake of COVID-19, the following programmes should be organized: 1) National Integration Day on Saturday, November 19, 2022: Meetings, Seminars and Symposiums with emphasis on secularism, anti-communalism and non-violence should be organized.
Sunday, November 20, 2022- Minority Welfare Day: Emphasis should be laid on a 15-point programme for the welfare of minorities, special processions should be taken out to build brotherhood from the cities where communal riots are taking place.
Monday, 21st November, 2022- Language Harmony Day: Special events and poets' meets should be organized to introduce the language heritage of the people of other parts of India.
Tuesday, 22 nd November, 2022- Weaker Section Day: Meetings and rallies should be held to highlight the programmes to help persons belonging to SCs and STs and weaker sections under the 20-point programme.
