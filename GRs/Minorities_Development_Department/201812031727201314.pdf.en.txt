The Proposal- M. The main objectives of the Prime Minister's 15 Point Programme are to increase educational opportunities for minorities and to ensure equal share in economic growth and employment for minorities. Also, the judges appointed by the central government to study the social, economic and educational condition of the minority community in the country and take measures for it. In line with the recommendations made by the Sachar Committee on the need for education in this community, a proposal to start a new Government Industrial Training Institute for minority students at Malegaon, Dist-Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
The Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai vide letter dated 21.09.2016 had proposed to start 10 Vocational Courses of 2 batches each with an intake capacity of 460 for students belonging to Minority Communities at Malegaon, Distt.
In this regard, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 18.11.2018 has decided to set up a new Government Industrial Training Institute at Malegaon, District Nashik for the students belonging to minority community.
Government Decision- It is hereby approved to start a new Government Industrial Training Institute for the students of minority community at Malegaon, Dist-Nashik with an intake capacity of 460 and 10 business courses of 2 batches each.

Reservation of seats for admission to Industrial Training Institute: In the said Industrial Training Institute, 70 per cent of the total strength of seats shall be reserved for students belonging to minority community and the remaining 30 per cent of seats shall be filled by candidates belonging to general and reserved categories as per the prevailing admission rules. If there are not enough candidates for admission under the 70 per cent quota, then general and reserved category candidates will be admitted to such vacant seats.
The admission process in this Industrial Training Institute will be carried out by the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship following the prescribed procedure.
The authority to inspect the Industrial Training Institute shall be vested in the officers of the Minority Development Department and the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department or a representative appointed by the Department.
This Government Resolution is being issued in pursuance of the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 18.11.2018.
