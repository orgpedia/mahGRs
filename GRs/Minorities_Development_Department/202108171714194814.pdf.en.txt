Introduction: The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi was established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting the Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. The decision has been taken by the government. For the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, the expenditure for the financial year 2021-22 is Rs. 19, 60,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) has been sanctioned and the mandatory provision of Rs. 3 d. As on 24.6.2021, out of the sanctioned allocation for 6 months from April to September, 2021, Rs. 4,00,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh only) is available to be spent. Therefore, the matter of granting administrative approval to spend from this available fund was under the consideration of the government.
