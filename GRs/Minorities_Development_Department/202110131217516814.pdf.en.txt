Introduction: In order to provide opportunities for vocational education and training to the unemployed and promising youth of the minority community of the state and to make them employable in different areas of daily life, the skill development-based employment-oriented training program being implemented by the Minority Development Department has been revised and notified in Reference No. 1 d. The Employment Oriented Training Fee Reimbursement Scheme is being implemented as per the Government Decisions dated 4th December, 2015. Reference No. 2 d. As per the Government Decision dated 31st March, 2018, it has been decided to implement this scheme through Maharashtra State Skill Development Society functioning under the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. In addition, the skill development training programme being implemented to provide employment and self-employment opportunities to women and youth belonging to minority communities will be improved. 3 d. As per the Government decision dated 10th December, 2020, it has been decided to implement this programme through Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, functioning under the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. Since the expenditure of both the above training programmes is being done under the same head, the matter of changing the name of the scheme in the head was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the change in the name of the scheme in the accounting of the employment oriented fee reimbursement scheme being implemented for minority candidates in the state as follows.
