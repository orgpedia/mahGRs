The Proposal:
In order to provide opportunities to the candidates belonging to minority communities of the State in Government/ Paramilitary Services, for competitive examinations like Central Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission/ Banking Service Recruitment etc. as well as for the preparation of Common Entrance Examinations for admission to professional courses and for starting special tuition classes for the minority candidates who have failed in class 10th and 12th, Reference No. 1 has been approved.
Under this scheme, training centers have been selected for training minority candidates in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane and Pune cities of the state. 2 has been implemented. In pursuance of the above decision of the Government, the training institutes in the above mentioned training centres have submitted their proposals seeking grants to the Government. The above training institutes namely MT Educare, Mumbai, a training institute, have been allocated Phase Grants for the Common Entrance Test for the year 2015-16 as per the Government decision in Reference No.5 above. Now, the institute has demanded the remaining grant for 2015-16, the entire grant for 2016-17 Common Entrance Test for Class XI and XII, and the entire grant for 2016-17 for the 10th failed students. Reference No. 1 for Banking Courses in Garjato Marathi, Borivali Mumbai at Mumbai Centre The first phase of the grant has been disbursed as per the decision of the government. The institute has sought the remaining grant and the entire grant for 2016-17.

The first phase of grant for the courses of Union Public Service Commission, State Public Service Commission and State Public Service Commission (Non-Government) has been disbursed to the Institute of Farmers Education, Amaravati at Amravati Centre as per the decision of the Government of Reference No.5. The institute has sought the remaining grant for 2015-16 and the entire grant for 2016-17.
After examining the proposal of the demand for grant submitted by the above institution, the matter of sanctioning the grant in accordance with the provisions mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 25.06.2013 of Reference No. 1 and the number of students who have attended 85% of the actual attendance was under the consideration of the State Government.
MT Educare, Mumbai, an institution based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, which is fulfilling the provision of Government Decision, dated 25.06.2013 under the scheme" Maulana Azad Free Tuition and Related Scheme," has not completed the private coaching classes of class 12th failed students. The matter was brought to the notice of the Government vide reference No. Under the Government Decision on 3,4,5 and 6, Rs. 12,50,000/- is being decided to be recovered. Accordingly, MT Educare, Mumbai, Garjato Marathi, Borivali, Mumbai and Farmers Education Institute, Amravati will be given Rs. 45,64,000/ (Rupees Forty Five Lakhs Sixty Four Thousand only) is being approved to be disbursed in the financial year 2017-18 as mentioned in Annexure-A out of the budgetary provision for the said scheme. In this case, the Under Secretary, Minority Development Department Controller and Drawing and Disbursing Officer will be there to collect this amount.
The Electronic Clearing Service (ECS) of the said amount should be drawn in favour of the Under Secretary (1), Minority Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
The institutions concerned will be inspected from time to time. If any irregularity is found in the implementation of the scheme, penal action will be taken against the institution.
The training institute concerned should maintain year wise accounting details in this regard and spend only on the items for which the grant has been disbursed. The utilization certificate of this amount will have to be submitted to the government by the concerned training institute. It shall be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate of these items to the Government as mentioned in Annexure' B'. Officials of the Minority Development Department will visit the concerned training institute.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the guidelines contained in the Circulars dated 18.04.2017, 06.02.2018 and 30.06.2017 respectively and the accompanying Annexure in Reference No. 8 and 9 of the Department of Finance.
