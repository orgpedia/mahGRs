Preamble: The Empowerment Committee constituted under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karykram (PMJVK), Ministry of Minority Affairs, Govt. The 31st meeting held on 19/02/2021 has approved the proposal to implement the Smart Class project in the recognized group schools in the minority dominated areas of the state. The project will be implemented under the guidance of the School Education Department. According to the proposal, the government was considering the formation of a control committee to implement the smart class project in batches of 6 to 10 in schools belonging to the recognized groups in the minority-dominated areas of the state.
The order:
A committee is being constituted for the implementation of the Smart Class project under the control and guidance of the School Education Department in batches of 6 to 10 in recognized group schools in minority-dominated areas of the state.
