Introduction: The scholarship scheme for economically weaker and meritorious students of minority communities in the state who are pursuing higher professional education and studying in all courses after Class XII is being implemented as per the government decision dated 14 October 2011.
The Nodal Officer of the Scheme under the said Scheme, Director, Directorate of Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, as per the Government decisions at the reference serial numbers 2, 3, 4 and 5, respectively, for the financial year 2019-20, Rs. 37,52,40, 655/-, Rs. 10,21,50, 331/-, Rs. 1,05,97, 399/- and Rs. 37,45,554 totalling Rs. 49,17,33, 939/- (Rupees Nineteen Fifty Seven Crore Seventeen Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty-Nine only) has been provided to the Minority students for the allocation of scholarships for the academic year 2019-20, from the sanctioned amount for the financial year 2019-20 and 2020-21. Now, as per the letter dated 27th November, 2020 issued by the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, the amount of Rs. 21,55,244/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh, Fifty Five Thousand, Two Hundred and Forty Four only) for the allotment of scholarship to the remaining students for the year 2019-20, and one percent of the total scholarship amount allotted by the Director, Technical Education for the financial year 2019-20, has been requested to be made available as administrative expenses. The following decisions are taken by the government:

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The original budgetary provision of Rs. 120.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crore only) for the financial year 2020-21 under the head' Subsidiary Grants (2235 A338) for Scholarship Scheme for Minority Students pursuing Higher Professional Education and all courses after XIIth" has been approved. Out of this amount, a total amount of Rs. 12.10 crore has been disbursed during the year 2020-21 as per the Government decisions at Reference No.3, 4 and 5.
2. Now, as per the letter dated 27th November, 2020 issued by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra, State, Mumbai vide Roll No.6 dated 27th November, 2020, for the allotment of scholarships for the year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 21,55,244/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Four only) is required for further enhancement of the scholarship amount. However, at present, considering the inadmissibility shown to the administrative expenses while disbursing the funds under Finance Department Informal Reference No.9/ 2021/ Expenditure 1, dated 12th January, 2021, the Government is approving to disburse the total amount required by the Directorate of Technical Education for the allotment of scholarships to its students only by way of Rs. 21,55,244/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh, Twenty Five Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Four only).
The amount is being handed over to the Directorate of Technical Education for sanction of scholarship to the students belonging to minority communities who have qualified for the scholarship for the year 2019-20, for which the concerned Coordinating Officer will act as Drawing Dispensing Officer and its Director as Controlling Officer. Since the disbursement of this scholarship is to be done expeditiously, the question of furnishing the monthly expenditure statement of this fund does not arise.
Further, it is hereby permitted to use the balance amount, if any, of the funds released for the scholarships for the year 2018-19 and, if required, for the scholarships for the year 2019-20 and also to use the balance amount, if any, for the scholarships for the year 2020-21.
The details of expenditure and utilization certificate of the above mentioned accounts should be submitted to the government within the stipulated time. Also, all the directions/ rules/ government decisions/ orders issued in this regard by the government from time to time and the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the supervision of the Comptroller and Auditor General should be followed while spending this grant.
This Government decision is being issued under the authority received from the Administrative Department under the Finance Department, Government Decision No. 2013/ No. 30/ 2013/ Regulations, Part-2 dated 15th April, 2015/ as per the authority provided to the Department from time to time under the Financial Authority Rule Book Part-2 and as per the approval given by the Planning Department Informal Reference No. 8/ 1471, dated 8th January, 2021 and Finance Department Informal Reference No. 9/ 2021/ Expenditure-1 dated 12th January, 2021.
