relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.

(4) The Receipt to the said VPDA shall be only such grants drawn under MTR 44 for 31

2235A741. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate after incurring
expenditure from the VPDA.
(5)The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the respective
Grant.
(6) The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated
Fund after completion of one year from the date of drawl of the funds/date of credit of amount of funds to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever is earlier.
(7) The Administrator with the District Treasury Officer shall reconcile the balances
under the VPDA at the end of every calendar month.
