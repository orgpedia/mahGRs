Government circular
Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, New Delhi. Rajiv Gandhi's birth anniversary is celebrated as Sadbhavana Diwas on August 20 every year. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India vide its Office Memorandum No.F- 28-22/2006-VAS-4, D. In pursuance of the directions issued under 18th July, 2006, in pursuance of the aforesaid Sadbhavana Diwas, the Hon. from August 20, 2019. The Government has decided to observe 5th September, 2019 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada." The main objectives of this fortnight are to promote the spirit of unity, harmony and avoidance of violence among the people of different religions and different languages in different regions of the state.
2. d. To celebrate 20th August, 2019 as" Sadbhavana Diwas," the following programmes should be organized.
(a) In the Ministry. " Sadbhavana Diwas" should be celebrated on 20th August, 2019 and all the participants should be asked to take pledge of Sadbhavana Diwas and a Sadbhavana Run should also be organized on this day. A copy of the pledge and how to act on it is attached.
(b) The Director, Cultural Affairs, State of Maharashtra, shall organize group songs and cultural programs on the subject of Sadbhavana with the participation of the people of Brihanmumbai, especially the youth.
(c) The Commissioner of Revenue Department shall organise a Sadbhavana Day pledge taking event from his office including organising Sadbhavana Run. (The modus operandi of the race and the pattern of the pledge is attached. (d) An all-religion prayer meeting should be held in all government offices, district headquarters and under the chairmanship of the concerned Collector and all the attendees should be asked to take the goodwill pledge. Also a goodwill race should be organized (the sample is attached). A cultural programme was also organised with the participation of the youth.

Similar programmes should be organised in universities, colleges and schools. Orders in this regard have been issued by the School and Mass Education Department and the Medical Education Department. Nehru Yuva Kendra, Bharat Scouts and Guides and National Service Scheme should also be involved in the programme.
In addition, the following programmes should be organized to celebrate the fortnight from 20th August, 2019 to 5th September, 2019 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" in the State.
(a) Programmes such as human chain to spread the message of social unity should be organized in the district headquarters of the state under the chairmanship of the concerned collector.
(b) The District Collector shall organize youth conferences and invite eminent freedom fighters of the district to speak in them.
The District Collectors should also organise other cultural programmes in the districts during the fortnight.
e) Director, Cultural Affairs, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to organize cultural programmes in Mumbai during this fortnight.
4. Directorate General of Information and Public Relations should contact the local officials of Doordarshan and All India Radio to give adequate publicity to the above-mentioned" Sadbhavana Diwas" and" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" programmes.
5. All the concerned officers should take care of taking all the necessary measures for the success of the above mentioned programmes and submit a report to the government after the completion of the programme.
