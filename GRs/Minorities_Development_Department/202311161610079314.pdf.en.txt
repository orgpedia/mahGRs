Introduction: The government has decided to increase the proportion of candidates from minority communities in the police recruitment in every district of the state. The pre-recruitment training scheme was being implemented from July 27, 2009. The government has decided to amend the scheme. It has been decided to implement the scheme on a residential basis from December 17, 2021. As per the revised Government decision, the subsidy payable to the training institutions is 50% at the beginning of the training as per the phase method, 25% after two months of the training and the remaining 25% after the completion of the training after the trainees submit the proof of participation in the police recruitment process (written test, physical test test). The programme will be implemented from 2022-23. Accordingly, the Collectors, Gondia and Nagpur have submitted a proposal for disbursement of 75% of the total grant under the above Reference Letter, which is 50% of the initial training and 25% after two months from the commencement of training. Whereas Collector, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar has submitted a proposal to disburse the initial 50% subsidy of training under the above reference letter. The government has approved the proposal. It was under consideration of the Government to disburse the said grant from the funds budgeted and available for the scheme for the year 2023-24 after scrutinizing the same as per the prescribed schedule dated 17.12.2021.
