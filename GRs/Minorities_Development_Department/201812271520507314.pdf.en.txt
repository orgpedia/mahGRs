Preamble: In order to provide opportunities for vocational education and training to the unemployed and promising youth of the minority community in the State and to make them employable, the Minority Development Department has issued Reference No. mentioned in 1. The revised Employment Oriented Training (ETS) reimbursement scheme has been implemented as per the Government decision dated 04.12.2015. Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission is being implemented to empower the youth of the state through skill development and provide them opportunities in employment/ self-employment in industries, services and similar sectors where there is a high demand. mentioned in 2. The decision was taken on September 2, 2015.
The Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission and the Employment Oriented Training Fee Compensation Scheme, both run by the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship and the Department of Minority Affairs respectively, have largely the same objectives and curriculum. In order to ensure uniformity in the implementation of skill development programme and to prevent duplication in the scheme in respect of skill development, the Government has decided to implement the scheme of Employment Oriented Training Fee Compensation Scheme of the Minority Development Department through Maharashtra State Skill Development Agency functioning under the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship and to allocate the provision for the scheme to Maharashtra State Skill Development Agency. The decision was taken on March 31, 2018. Accordingly, the Government was considering to allocate the budgeted allocation for the above scheme for the year 2018-19 to Maharashtra State Skill Development Agency.
