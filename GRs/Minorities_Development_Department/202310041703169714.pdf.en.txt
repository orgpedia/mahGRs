The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. Out of the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2023-24 for the establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Rs. An expenditure of Rs. 3,92,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) has been approved. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2023-24 was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: Out of the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2023-24, it has been approved to disburse the necessary grants for the period of 5 months from April 2023 to August 2023. Budget for 2023-24 is Rs. 19,60,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) out of which Rs. A grant of Rs. 3,92,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) is being distributed to the office of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai for the following purposes. Accordingly, an amount of Rs. 3,92,000/-is being disbursed to the office of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai for the limited period from April, 2023 to August, 2023 for meeting the expenses as under:
