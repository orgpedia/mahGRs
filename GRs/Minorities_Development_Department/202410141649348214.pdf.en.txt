1) To solve the social problems of the Sikh community.

2) Guidance to the Sikh community for availing the benefits of minority schemes and
Suggesting measures.
3) To study the difficulties of the Sikh community for their economic and social upliftment.
Designing plans
4) To make appropriate recommendations for future demands from the Sikh community.
5) To recommend various activities / events / festivals to be organized on the birth anniversaries of Guru Gobind Singh, Guru Tegh Bahadur and other Sikh Gurus / dignitaries.
The Committee shall have the composition and functions as aforesaid.
4. The Government shall determine the term of office of the representatives of the Sikh community to be elected as members.
No remuneration or allowances shall be payable to the non-official members of the said Committee.
6. This Government decision shall be binding on the Government of Maharashtra.
