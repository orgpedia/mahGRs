The proposal was to approve the form of application for appointment as Kazi, Chief Kazi and Additional Kazi in the state of Maharashtra, the application to be given to the bridegroom at the time of marriage (nikah), the approval of the form of marriage certificate, as well as the fee for the sample of these applications and the approval to publish an advertisement in the leading Urdu, Marathi and English newspapers of the state for inviting applications from aspirants for the appointment of Kazi.
Government Decision- As per the provisions of Section 2 of the Kazi Act, 1880 (12 of 1880), guidelines have been prescribed to prescribe the eligibility criteria and regulate the appointments of one or more eligible persons as Kazis in the areas having significant Muslim population in the State. As per the guidelines, the government has decided to approve the application form (Annexure-1), marriage form (Annexure-2), marriage certificate (Annexure- 3-4) and the fee to be charged for it and to publish an advertisement in the leading Urdu, Marathi and English newspapers of the state for inviting applications from aspirants for the appointment of Kazi, Chief Kazi and Additional Kazi in the state of Maharashtra.
