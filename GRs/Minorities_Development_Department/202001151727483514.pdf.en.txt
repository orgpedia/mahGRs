About the establishment of the Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Akademi with the objective of promoting the development of Punjabi language in the state, the preservation and progress of the language, as well as strengthening the spirit of national integration through ideological exchange between the writers of Punjabi and Marathi languages. on 25.09.2018.
As per the decision taken at the Cabinet meeting, No. Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy, Mumbai has been established by the decision of the Government of Maharashtra. Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy, Mumbai at Reference No. 2 The 11 non-official members were appointed by the government decision here. The matter of cancellation of the appointments of these non-official members was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy, Mumbai vide Reference No. 2 The appointments of the following non-official members appointed by the Government are hereby cancelled:
