Preamble: Grant Scheme for providing infrastructure facilities in Government recognized Private Aided/ Unaided Schools, Junior Colleges, Industrial Training Institutes, Municipal/ Municipal Council Schools and Disabled Schools having a student population of Religious Minorities is being implemented as per Government Decision dated 7th October, 2015. Under the said scheme, the Government is issuing the following decision to be distributed from the approved budgetary grants for the financial year 2023-24 to the eligible schools in the proposal received from the concerned Collectorate to the Government in the year 2022-23.
Government Decision: A high-level selection committee headed by the concerned District Collector will take a decision in this regard. The applications received from them as on 7.10.2015 have been scrutinized and after scrutiny, the proposal along with the list of eligible schools, recommendations and demand for necessary funds have been sent to the Government for approval. All the proposals received from the respective Collectorate will be scrutinized at the government level and the number of schools that qualify in it and they will be approved (maximum limit is Rs. 2.00 lakh) The amount of subsidy is as follows:

The funds will be handed over to the respective district collectors. The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Controlling Officer for this fund. The names of the eligible schools for which grants have been sanctioned along with the details of the nature of the demand for the school, the year in which the grant has been availed earlier, are given in the Annexure to this Government Decision. The Government hereby grants approval for the release of the said funds to the respective District Collectors, subject to the fulfillment of the following paragraphs, to be disbursed by drawing the same from the Budget Distribution System (BDS).
(2) The decision of the Government. Subsidy will be provided for only one U DEIS CODE/ INSTITUTE CODE/ LICENSE NUMBER as per the terms and conditions dated 07.10.2015. Also, in case of different schools/ colleges in the same building/ premises of the same institution, which are in different shifts/ sessions according to different schedules of the day and have different U DEIS codes, the grant will be sanctioned for only one DEIS code for the same building/ premises. Also, aided schools are not self-financed, the concerned collector should give the government a decision before distributing the grant. The terms and conditions as prescribed in 7.10.2015 shall be adhered to. Under this scheme, the schools concerned should spend the funds for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned to the schools in the current year.
After disbursement of the grant to the schools, colleges, industrial training institutes, disabled and municipal/ municipal schools, the concerned schools, colleges, industrial training institutes, disabled and municipal/ municipal schools are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal within a period of three months from the actual receipt of the grant. After this, it is mandatory to submit the utility certificate in the prescribed format of the allocation of the grant to the concerned district collector. Also, the Collector shall submit to the Government in the prescribed form as per the' Annexure-B' accompanying this Government Decision.
