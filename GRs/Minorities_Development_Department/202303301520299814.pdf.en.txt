Preamble: Implementation of the scholarship scheme for economically weaker and meritorious students studying in all courses in higher professional and class XII from minority communities in the State. The date mentioned here is being done as per the Government decision dated 14th October 2011.
The requisition has been made vide letter dated 6th February, 2023 under serial number 11 in respect of Director, Education, Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and vide letter dated 14.2.2023 under serial number 12 in respect of Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.

Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the concerned offices for the scholarship of the students who applied in the academic year 2019-20,2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the requisition made vide letter dated 6th February, 2023 dated at Roll No. 11 under reference to Director, Education, Directorate of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and vide letter dated 14.2.2023 dated 12th February, 2023 under reference to Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, the concerned offices are directed to disburse Rs. 25,57,05, 324/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Fifty Seven Lakhs Five Thousand Three Hundred and Twenty Four only) is hereby approved by the Government.

The amount is being handed over to the Director, Director of Education, Higher Education, Maharashtra State Pune and Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai for disbursement of scholarships to the students belonging to minority communities who have become eligible for scholarships for the four academic years 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23.
The funds disbursed through this are being approved to meet the pending dues of the students whose scholarship amount is pending for the academic year 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22 from the funds disbursed under this order. Also, if any balance is left after disbursement of the pending amount from the said amount, it should be utilized for the scholarship of beneficiary students for the year 2022-23. The details of expenditure and utilization certificate of the above mentioned accounts should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time.
