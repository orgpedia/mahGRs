Government's decision:
Two posts of drivers have been sanctioned and one post is vacant at the establishment of the Minority Development Department (Khud). As per the provisions of the circulars dated 02.02.2013 and 02.12.2013 of the Finance Department, the tenders were invited by implementing the prescribed tender process for filling up one vacant post of Driver through external agency. of the proposals received. The tender of Krishna Motors, Mumbai was accepted as the lowest bidder and the said supplier has been selected to provide one driver on monthly basis.
