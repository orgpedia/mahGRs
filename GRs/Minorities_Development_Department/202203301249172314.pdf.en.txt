The Government Decision regarding construction of hostels for girl students has been issued on 2nd March, 2010. The modalities for construction of hostels for girls belonging to minority communities on the land available in various universities in the State have been decided as per the Government decision dated 20.12.2011. Further, the modalities for the management of hostels being constructed for students belonging to minority communities have been decided as per the Government Decisions dated 21st June, 2013 and 28th July, 2015.
