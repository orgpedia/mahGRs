The Proposal:
The Minorities Development Department has decided to increase the number of minorities in the state in the state service, central service and all-India service. The competitive examination training scheme is implemented as per the Government decision dated 20.3.2010. The scheme is being implemented by the State Minorities Commission through the State Administrative Service Training Institute under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Mumbai/ Kolhapur/ Nagpur/ Aurangabad.
