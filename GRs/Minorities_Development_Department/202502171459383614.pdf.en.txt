The Proposal:
Hostels in all districts for girls pursuing higher education from minority communities in the state.
The construction has been approved under the Government Decision dated 02.03.2010. In pursuance of that
Hostels for girls from minority communities have been started in the state. The procedure for admission to the hostel has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 21.06.2013. as well as the date
The criteria for admission to the hostel have been set by the Government Circular of 1.11.2021.
Hostels built by the department for minority girls pursuing higher education.
Being for girls only, girls who have passed Class XII and are enrolled in the next course can take admission in this hostel. The benefit of this hostel scheme is maximum for the minority community.
Students should take classes 10th, 11th, 12th, diploma, diploma equivalent as well as industrial.
The admission of minority girl students from training institutes and technical institutes to the hostel was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
All built for girls pursuing higher education from minority communities.
The hostels are vacant after the completion of the admission process for girls pursuing higher education after 12th standard.
In these seats, minority girl students from class 10th, 11th, 12th, Diploma, Diploma equivalent as well as Industrial Training Institutes and Technological Institutes are allowed to be admitted to the hostels.
