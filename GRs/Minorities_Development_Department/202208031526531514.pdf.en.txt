Preamble: It has been approved to set up Self Help Groups of women belonging to minority communities with a view to provide adequate credit by mobilizing them for economic empowerment of women belonging to minority concentrated areas of the State and to develop entrepreneurial skills among them by providing them necessary information about entrepreneurship. In the first phase, the government has decided to set up self-help groups for women belonging to minority communities in Mumbai city, Mumbai suburbs, Nanded, Malegaon, Karanja, Parbhani, Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Bhiwandi, Mumbra-Kausa and Miraj. It was approved on February 28, 2012. Under this scheme, 2400 Self Help Groups of minority women will be formed and their 13 Common Resource Centres will be set up within a period of 8 years. Women Economic Development Corporation has established 3200 Self Help Groups and 8 Community Resource Centres in a span of 8 years as on 31st March, 2020.
