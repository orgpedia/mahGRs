The Proposal:
The government has taken a decision in every district of the state to increase the proportion of police recruitment of candidates from minority communities in the state. The pre-recruitment training scheme was being implemented from July 27, 2009. The duration of the programme was two semesters. However, the benefit of this scheme is maximum for minority candidates.
The government has decided to recruit a large number of candidates from the minority community for the post of police constable in the police force in the form and duration of this scheme. 17
Changes have been made for December 2021. Accordingly, it has been decided to implement this scheme on a residential basis and for a period of three months instead of two months.
The programme will be implemented from 2022-23.
Accordingly, the District Collectors, Nandurbar and Beed have submitted a proposal to disburse the initial 50% subsidy for the training. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to disburse this subsidy from the funds available for the scheme during 2022-23.
