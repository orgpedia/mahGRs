Government circular
As per the decision taken by the Central Government in 1986, from November 19, 2019. The week of November 25, 2019 is to be celebrated as" Qaumi Ekta Week" in the State and more and more people are to be involved in the next programme to be organized in it. This year, it's Tuesday. From Monday, November 19, 2019. November 25, 2019 should be observed as" Qaumi Ekta Week" and the following programmes should be organized by involving maximum number of people.
Tuesday, November 19, 2019 National Integration Day: Organize meetings, seminars and symposiums with emphasis on secularism, anti-casteism and non-violence.
Wednesday, November 20, 2019- Minority Welfare Day: A 15-point programme for the welfare of minorities should be emphasized, special processions should be taken out to build brotherhood from the cities where communal riots have erupted.
Thursday, November 21, 2019- Language Harmony Day: Special events and poets' meets should be organized to introduce the language heritage of people in other parts of India.
Friday, November 22, 2019- Day of the weaker sections: Meetings and rallies should be held to highlight the programmes set out to help persons belonging to Scheduled Castes and Tribes and weaker sections under the 20-point programme. The following points should be noted at this time:
a) Indira Awas Yojana and allotment of house sites and loans.
(b) Distribution of surplus land to landless labourers.
c) Programmes for providing legal aid to the poor should also be organized.
Saturday, 23rd November, 2019 Cultural Unity Day: Cultural programmes should be organized emphasizing on the unity in diversity of Indians and presenting Indian tradition of cultural preservation and integration.
November 24, 2019- Women's Day: The importance of women in Indian society and their role in the development of nation building should be emphasized.
