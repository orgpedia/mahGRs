To provide scholarships to students studying in 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th classes and Industrial Training Institutes, thereby enhancing the educational quality of students, increasing their employability and helping to improve their economic status, the Government has decided to implement the Dr. Zakir Hussain Madrasa Modernization Scheme by providing infrastructure for modernization of Madrasas, providing grants for library and grant for honorarium to teachers. No. 152/10/ KA-6 dated 11th October, 2013 has been approved. Under this scheme, Rs. Rs. 5.40 crore. Under this scheme, the government is issuing the following decisions to allocate grants to madrassas in the financial year 2021-22.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Dr. Zakir Hussain Madrasa Modernization Scheme for modernization of registered Madrasas in the State has been operationalized vide GOVERNMENT DECISION dated 11.10.2013. Accordingly, a committee was formed under the chairmanship of the District Collector of the concerned districts on the recommendation of the government for the grant of madrasas eligible under the scheme

2. An annexure showing the names of the madrassas sanctioned as above and the details of grants sanctioned to them is annexed to this Government Resolution. A total of Rs. 142. The Government hereby approves grant in aid of Rs. 5,37,30, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores, Thirty Seven Lakhs Thirty Thousand only). The grants mentioned before the districts mentioned in the letter of agreement with this government decision are being handed over to the concerned district collectors. It is also being approved to disburse the funds handed over to them by the concerned District Collectors through the Budget Distribution System (BDS). For this, the concerned Collector will act as Controller and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The amount sanctioned at the government level should be deposited through ECS in the nationalized bank account of the madrasa concerned as per the letter of association attached by the concerned Collector or other officials of his office authorized by him for this purpose, and the remaining amount should be deposited in the government treasury.
Under this scheme, the madrassas concerned should spend the funds for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned in the current year.
After disbursement of the grant to the Madrasas, the concerned Madrasas are required to complete the works mentioned in the proposal within a period of three months from the actual receipt of the grant and thereafter submit the utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the grant to the concerned District Collector. The Collector concerned should submit the report of utility certificates received from all the madrasas concerned to the government as well as the Accountant General's office at the earliest. The Collector as Controlling Officer issued a circular to the Government vide Finance Department No.:- AUD-1082/ CR- 90/84/ Fund-5, Dy. The expenditure of the Fund as on 24th September, 1986 should be reconciled and a report submitted to the Government. Also, the appropriation account report of the relevant head for the financial year 2021-22 should be submitted to the government by September 30, 2022.
Students studying in madrasas will be evaluated by the teachers appointed for them every three months as per Schedule 8 of the Terms and Conditions of the Government Decision dated 11th October, 2013. In this, written/ oral examination of the students will be conducted and the report will be submitted to the government through the education officer every May.
The High-Level Selection Committee as mentioned in the Government Decision dated 11.10.2013 under the Chairmanship of the District Collector of the concerned district is being authorized to resolve the grievances arising under the scheme.
The expenditure under this scheme should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2021-22 under the head" Demand No.ZE-1, Principal Accounts Head 2235, (01) (31) Subsidiary Grants (Programme) for providing infrastructure to the registered Madrassas in the State and for payment of salaries to the teachers," (2235B 227) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
