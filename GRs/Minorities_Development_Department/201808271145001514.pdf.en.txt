The Maharashtra State Minorities Commission, Mumbai was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the sanctioned funds to meet the expenditure for four months from April, 2018 to July, 2018 for the financial year 2018-19 for the following schemes for the candidates belonging to the minority community: (i) Police Constable Pre-recruitment Training Class Scheme, (ii) Marathi Language Foundation Class Scheme, (iii) Union Public Service Commission Examination Training Class Scheme (through YASHAM) and (iv) Competitive Examination Training Scheme (through State Administrative Services Training Institute).

A budget of Rs seven crore has been earmarked for the project.
