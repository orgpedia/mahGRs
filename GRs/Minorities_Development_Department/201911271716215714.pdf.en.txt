The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Urdu language in the state and to strengthen the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy is implementing various programmes. 4 As per the decision of the Government, out of the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20, Rs. Administrative approval has been granted for an expenditure of Rs. 32,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Lakhs only). Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2019-20 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The above reference No. 20 provides for release of grants-in-aid for the 9 months from April to December, 2019 out of the grants-in-aid sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20. 2 Approval has been granted by the Government Supplementary Letter. Of these, reference No. 3 d. As on 12.6.2019, a sum of Rs.40 lakh has been disbursed. In respect of expenditure on various schemes of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi, during the year 2019-20," Demand No. ZE-1, 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 200 Other Programmes, 01- Assistance to Minorities, (01) (23) Subsidiary Grants (Programmes) for various schemes of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy (2235B094) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried). 72,00,000/- (Rupees Seventy Two Lakh only) for a limited period of 5 months from August to December, 2019. 32,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Lakhs only) is being released. The Superintendent-cum-Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai shall ensure that the sanctioned expenditure remains within the prescribed limits.
