the prelude
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government vide its Office Memorandum dated 23rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology, pursuant to which Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Appropriation Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. Accordingly, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) has been appointed for the department for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme and it has been informed to open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for the scheme. It has also been notified to appoint a Deputy Secretary level officer as Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the appointment of a nodal officer of the Minority Development Department for the control and coordination of the activities under the PFMS in the case of Centrally Sponsored Schemes was under consideration of the Government.
Government Resolution Reference No. 2 As per the instructions issued by the Government Circular herein, Deputy Secretary, Minority Development Department, Ministry of Minority Affairs, Mumbai as the Nodal Officer for controlling and coordinating the activities under PFMS in respect of Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Minority Affairs.
