No.16/ USA/ Q. 13/8/2021 Preamble: The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy has been established with the objective of development, promotion and progress of Urdu language in the state and to strengthen the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. For the matters relating to (compulsory) expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi, refer No. Under the Government decision, out of the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. Administrative approval has been granted to spend Rs. 4.00 lakh/- (Rupees Four Lakh only).
Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Reference No. 243/2021/ Expenditure-1, Dy. Approval has been granted under the informal reference dated 6/8/2021. In view of the above, the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been asked to submit a report in the year 2021-22 for the matters of (mandatory) expenditure on the establishment of the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi vide Demand No. ZE 1,2205 ART AND CULTURE, 102- ART AND CULTURE OPERATION (00) (01) Establishment of Urdu Academy/ Dattmat, 31 Auxiliary Grant (Compulsory) (2205 3027)" sanctioned under the head Rs. 19,60,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) for a limited period from April, 2021 to September, 2021. 4.00 lakh is available for distribution and is being distributed to the office of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai. The Superintendent-cum-Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai shall ensure that the sanctioned expenditure remains within the prescribed limits.
The ECS of the said amount should be drawn in the name of Under Secretary (1), Minority Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
