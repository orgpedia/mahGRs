The Proposal:
The Maulana Azad Minorities Economic Development Corporation has been set up by the state government for the upliftment of the economically weaker sections of the Muslim and minority communities in the state. The authorized share capital of Maulana Azad Minorities Economic Development Corporation is Rs. 500.00 crore. It is from this stake that loans are made available under various schemes for the social and economic upliftment of minorities. The matter of increasing the official shareholding of the corporation was under the consideration of the government.
On this occasion Ma. The Cabinet gave. The said Governing Decision is being issued pursuant to the approval given in the meeting dated 20 th January, 2022.
Government Decision- Increase in the authorized share capital of Maulana Azad Minorities Economic Development Corporation to Rs. 500.00 crore to Rs. 700.00 crore is hereby approved subject to the following conditions:
(a) An additional Rs. 200.00 crore will be made available to the Corporation in a phased manner.
(2) There shall be a regular review of the schemes implemented by the Corporation as well as the shareholding in the distribution at the level of Chairperson/ Department of the Corporation. The details should be submitted to the Planning and Finance Department.
