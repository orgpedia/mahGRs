Decision Minority Development Department, No.USA-2022/ P.No.15/ USA/ KA-4D 28.6.2022 Preamble: The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy has been established with the objective of development, promotion and progress of Urdu language in the state and to strengthen the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. For the matters relating to (compulsory) expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi, refer No. 2 As per the decision of the Government, out of the sanctioned grant for the financial year 2022-23, Rs. Administrative approval has been accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 2,74,400/-lakh (Rupees Two Lakh Four Hundred Seventy Four only). Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2022-23 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Reference No. 2 Administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the government decision here. Accordingly, for a limited period from April, 2022 to June, 2022, Rs. 2,74,400/- lakh is being disbursed to the office of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai. The Superintendent-cum-Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai shall ensure that the sanctioned expenditure remains within the prescribed limits.
The ECS of the said amount should be drawn in the name of Under Secretary (1), Minority Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
3. Expenditure on" Demand No. ZE 1,2205 Art& Culture, 102- Operation of Art& Culture (00) (01) Establishment of Urdu Academy (Compulsory), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (2205 3027) to be spent under the head" Expenditure to be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2022-23.
The Superintendent-cum-Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai shall be required to submit the details of the expenditure and utilization certificate of the fund within the prescribed period.
This Government Decision is based on the Finance Department Government Circular, No: Earths-2021/ No.48/ Budget-3, No. 4.4.2022 is being issued as per the authority received.
