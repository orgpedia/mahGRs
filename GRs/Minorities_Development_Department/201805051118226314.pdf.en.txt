In order to provide opportunities to the candidates belonging to minority communities of the State in Government/ Paramilitary Services, for competitive examinations like Central Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission/ Banking Service Recruitment etc. as well as for the preparation of Common Entrance Examinations for admission to professional courses and for starting special tuition classes for the minority candidates who have failed in class 10th and 12th, Reference No. 1 has been approved.
Under this scheme, training centers have been selected for training minority candidates in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane, Pune and Aurangabad cities of the state. 2, 3, and 4 are included.
In pursuance of the above decision of the Government, Prabodhan Multipurpose Institute, Nashik, a training institute in the above mentioned training centre, has submitted proposals seeking grants to the Government. Prabodhan Multipurpose Institute, the first phase grant has been disbursed to this institute under the Government decision of Reference No. 10 for the selected courses in Nashik, Thane, Pune and Aurangabad. The institute has sought release of the remaining grants for the above four centres and the CET course for the year 2016-17.
After examining the proposal of the demand for grant submitted by the above Enlightenment Multipurpose Institute, the matter of sanctioning the grant in accordance with the provisions mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 25.06.2013 of Reference No. 1 and the number of students who have attended 85% of the actual attendance was under the consideration of the State Government.
Government Decision: Under the scheme" Maulana Azad Free Tuition and Related Schemes," Government Decision, dated 25.06.2013, grants are being sanctioned to the training institutes of Prabodhan Multipurpose Institute at Nashik, Pune, Thane and Aurangabad based on the number of students who have completed the provision and have 85% attendance.
