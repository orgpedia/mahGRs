Introduction: The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi was established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting the Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. The decision has been taken by the government. For the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, an amount of Rs. A mandatory provision of Rs. 19,60,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) has been approved and the same has been approved in the Finance Department Reference No. 3 d. As on 4.4.2022, out of the sanctioned allocation for 3 months from April to June, 2022, Rs. 2,74,400/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Seventy Four Thousand Four Hundred only) is available for expenditure. Therefore, the matter of granting administrative approval to spend from this available fund was under the consideration of the government.

Administrative approval is being granted to spend an amount of Rs. 2,74,400/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Seventy-four Thousand Four Hundred only) as per the request of the Academy.
