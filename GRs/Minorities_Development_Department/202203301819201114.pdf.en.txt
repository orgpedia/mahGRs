The Central Government has started disbursing funds through the Public Financial System (PFMS) of the Central Government in order to effectively manage the funds of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more effectiveness in the utilization of funds. Accordingly, Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme and as per the revised modalities of PFMS system, the Under Secretary, Minority Affairs Department, Ministry has been appointed as the Single Nodal Agency for the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram.
The SNA account of this scheme has been opened in the State Bank of India, Mantralaya branch.
The scheme has been mapped on PFMS system and funds are being provided by the Central Government through PFMS system.
The funds released by the Central Government for the implementation of Smart Class project in minority dominated schools under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram during 2021-22 shall be deposited in the SNA account opened in the State Bank of India, Mantralaya branch of the scheme. The SNA account no. 40533345678 and IFSC Code- SBIN0061524. To deposit the said amount in the SNA account, the Under Secretary (1), Minority Development Department is required to send the amount to the following address in column No. (10) and (11), the balance of Rs. 7,51,84, 000/- (Rs. seven crore fifty one lakh eighty four thousand
Only) With this decision, it is being approved to draw and distribute such funds from the budget distribution system within the limit of the amount. The funds to be disbursed for the construction are being handed over to the Under Secretary (1), Minority Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai, who will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Deputy Secretary (Budget), Minority Development Department as Controlling Officer. This amount should be withdrawn in the name of ECS Under Secretary (1), Minority Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
The Under Secretary, Minority Development Department shall deposit the amount drawn for implementation of Smart Class project in Minority Concentration Area Schools under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram in the SNA account of the scheme opened in State Bank of India, Mantralaya, Branch. The SNA account no. 40533345678. The amount is then transferred through the PFMS system through the Budget Branch. It will be operated by Telecommunications Consultants India Limited (TCIL).
The amount collected by the Under Secretary, Minority Development Department for implementation of Smart Class project in minority dominated area schools of recognized groups in the state under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram will be handed over to the concerned implementing agency i.e. Telecommunications Consultants India Limited (TCIL). The amount distributed cannot be used for any other purpose. It should be ensured that funds are not distributed twice for the above development works.
The concerned should ensure that there is no violation of any government decision/ terms and conditions as per the guidelines of C.V.C./ direction of CAG within the limits of financial powers to make such expenditure.
