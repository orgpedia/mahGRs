Under this scheme, at the end of March 31, 2018-19, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai, as mentioned in the above statement, will receive Rs. 06.25 crore (Rupees Six Crore Twenty Five Lakh only) and Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Maharashtra State, Mumbai Rs. 02.50 Crores (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Lakhs only) aggregating to Rs. 08.75 crore (Rupees Eight Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) is being approved by this Government. The amount is being handed over to them for sanction of scholarship to the students belonging to minority community who have been eligible for scholarship for the year 2018-19 and they will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Controller.
The Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai and the Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall spend the funds sanctioned for the year 2018-19 only up to the limit. The details of expenditure and utilization certificate of the above mentioned accounts should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time. Also, all the directions/ rules/ government decisions/ orders issued in this regard by the government from time to time and the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the supervision of the Comptroller and Auditor General should be followed while spending this grant. This Government decision is based on the Financial Powers Rules Manual, Part-I, Finance Division, Government Decision No. V.P.1008/ P.No. 70/2008/ Regulations, D. Under the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide 15.05.2009, the financial powers are being issued to the Department from time to time as per the powers conferred under Rule Book Part-II and as approved by the Principal Secretary, Minority Development Department in his capacity as Head of the Administrative Department.
