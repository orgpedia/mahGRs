Introduction For economic empowerment of women belonging to minority communities living in minority dominated areas of the State, it is necessary to provide adequate credit by organizing them and imparting necessary entrepreneurial skills to them.
For the purpose of development, it has been approved to set up Self Help Groups of women belonging to minority communities. Under this, in the first phase, Self Help Groups of women belonging to minority communities have been approved to be set up in Mumbai city, Mumbai suburbs, Nanded, Malegaon, Karanja, Parbhani, Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Bhiwandi, Mumbra-Kausa and Miraj. Under this scheme, 2400 Self Help Groups of minority women will be formed and their 13 Common Resource Centres will be set up within a period of 8 years. For this Rs. 27,54,32, 639/- (Rs. Twenty seven crore fifty four lakh thirty two thousand six hundred and forty nine only) the expected expenditure has been approved. The scheme is being implemented through the Women's Economic Development Corporation. For the year 2018-19, Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.00 crore has been earmarked for the project.
