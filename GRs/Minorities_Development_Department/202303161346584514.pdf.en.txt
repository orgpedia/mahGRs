Memorandum: Advertisements were published in various newspapers of the state for the publication of two schemes at the state level, namely, the Grant Scheme for the year 2019-20 and the Dr. Zakir Hussain Madrasa Modernization Scheme, 2019-20 for providing infrastructure facilities in minority-dominated government-recognized private schools, junior colleges and disabled schools in the state. The Ministry of Information and Public Relations has informed this. Accordingly, after the publication of the advertisement, the original payment for the advertisements of the concerned newspapers has been received by the Minority Development Department. The payment of the principal amount is approved as follows:
