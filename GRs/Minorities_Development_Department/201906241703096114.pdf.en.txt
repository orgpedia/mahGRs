The District Collector is in charge of the concerned Chief Executive Officer and the District Council.
The District Collectors concerned shall act as Controlling Officers and Drawing and Disbursing Officers for disbursement of grants sanctioned to the respective Minority dominated Gram Panchayats. The Collector shall ensure that the works referred to in Annexure-A are not taken up under any other scheme and if the work is sanctioned under any other scheme, the Collector of the concerned district shall have the power to make changes in respect of such work within the limits of the sanctioned funds.
