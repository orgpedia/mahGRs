The Proposal:
The Waqf Act, 1995 is an Act of the Government of Maharashtra. This Act has come into force with effect from 01/01/1996 and as per the provisions of section 13 and 14 of this Act, the Government Notification, Revenue and Forest Department, S. No. 10/2001/ S. No.154/ L-3, D. The Maharashtra State Board of Waqfs has been established on 4th January, 2002 with its headquarters at Panchakki, Aurangabad. The Government was considering declaring the Chief Executive Officer as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the office of Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad and the Deputy Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad in place of the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad.
Government's decision
The Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad has been appointed as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the office of Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad.
All the orders issued earlier in this regard are being superseded and the appointment of the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad is being cancelled and the Deputy Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Waqf Board, Aurangabad is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer instead.
