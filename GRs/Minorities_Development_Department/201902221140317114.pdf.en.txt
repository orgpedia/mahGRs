Preamble: Indian Administrative Service (IAS) The General Administration Department has been instructed to issue a government decision and prescribe a rapporteur/ review officer in each department regarding the reporting/ review of confidential reports for POS and all other services in the state. There are 2 posts of members sanctioned on the establishment of the Regional Office of Maharashtra State Wakf Tribunal, Aurangabad under this Department. Out of these 2 posts, officers of the rank of Deputy Secretary are appointed on deputation to one post" an officer of the State Civil Service and a Government officer of equivalent rank if such officer is not available" and to the other post" a person having knowledge of Muslim law and legal philosophy." It was under consideration of the government as to who should report and review the confidential report of the officers of the rank of Deputy Secretary, who are working as" Members" of the Regional Office of Maharashtra State Wakf Tribunal, Aurangabad.
Government's decision:
Now, under this Government decision, the Chairman, Maharashtra State Waqf Tribunal, Aurangabad is being declared as the Reporting Officer and the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Minority Development Department) as the Reviewing Officer for writing confidential reports of officers of the rank of Deputy Secretary working in the capacity of Member on the establishment of the Regional Office of the Maharashtra State Waqf Tribunal, Aurangabad under this Department.
(2) The proceedings relating to the reporting/ review of confidential reports etc. shall be governed by all applicable Government rules relating to confidential reports.
