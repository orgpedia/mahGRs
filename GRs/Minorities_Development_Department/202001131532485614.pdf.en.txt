Preamble: The Empowered Committee constituted by the Ministry of Minority Affairs. In the 101st and 106th meetings held on 30.01.2013, under the Centrally Sponsored Multi-Sectoral Development Programme (MSDP), a total of 116 Anganwadi Centres and 211 Educational and Health related works in Shegaon, Chikhli, Khamgaon and Buldana blocks of Buldana district have been completed at a cost of Rs. A proposal of Rs 1547.40 lakh has been approved.
In Shegaon, Chikhli, Khamgaon and Buldana blocks of Buldana district, 116 Anganwadi centres have been constructed and 211 educational and health related works have been completed at a total cost of Rs. 1547.40 lakh has been accorded administrative sanction vide Government Order dated 6.6.2016.
Further, as per the Government Decisions dated 31/03/2016 and 30/04/2016, the first instalment of Central and State share of Rs. 773.70 lakh has been disbursed.
The construction of 116 Anganwadi Centres in Shegaon, Chikhli, Khamgaon and Buldana blocks of Buldana district under the MSDP, out of which 64 Anganwadi Centres and 31 educational and health works out of 211 educational and health works could not be started due to technical reasons.
The government has decided to cancel the administrative approval of the construction of 64 Anganwadi centers and 31 educational and health related works in Shegaon, Chikhli, Khamgaon and Buldana blocks of Buldana district under the Centrally Sponsored Multi-Sectoral Development Programme (MSDP) in the 12th Five-Year Plan. The approval was given by the State Level Minority Welfare Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting held on 20/07/2019. The details are given in Annexure A and Annexure B.
