Disbursement of funds received through Supplementary Demand to meet the expenditure incurred on Khadimul Hujja who was deputed to Saudi Arabia for Haj 2018.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Minority Development
Government Resolution No: Haj-2018/ P. No. 169/ Ka-5
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 04.01.2019
Government Circular, Finance Department, No:- Supplementary-2018/ No. 95/ Finance-3, D. July 27, 2018.
Informal Reference to Finance Department No. 300/2018/ Expenditure-1, dated 11.12.2018.
Preamble: Since the expenditure on Khadimul Hujja, who was deputed to Saudi Arabia for Haj 2018, could not be met from the funds made available in the financial year 2018-19, a sum of Rs. Supplementary Demand of Rs. 71,28,000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Seventy Eight Lakhs Twenty Eight Thousand Only)" vide Demand No. ZE-1, 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 200 Other Programmes (01) (18), Expenditure on Pilgrims (Non-Plan), (2235A267) was submitted to the State Legislature for approval. Accordingly, Rs. 71,28,000/- has been approved by the State Legislature in the Monsoon Session, 2018 and with the approval of the Finance Department, Rs. 49,89,600/- was under consideration of the Government for disbursement to Maharashtra State Haj Committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Circular to the Government, Finance Department, No. Supplementary-2018/ No. 95/ Finance-3, No. Funds sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demand as per clause (a) of 27.07.2018 to meet the expenditure incurred on Khadimul Hujja deputed to Saudi Arabia for Haj 2018 (Rs. 71,28,000/-) out of which 70% is Rs. 49,89,600/- has been issued by the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 300/2018/ Expenditure-1, Dy. Approved on 11.12.2018. Accordingly, Rs. 49,89,600/- is hereby approved.
(2) This Government Decision shall be governed by the Government Circular of the Finance Department, d. being issued under the authority received on 27.07.2018.
