The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Urdu language in the state and to strengthen the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. With reference to the matter of (compulsory) expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi, vide the above Reference No. 3 As per the decision of the Government, out of the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21, Rs. Administrative approval has been granted for an expenditure of Rs. 3,96,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Sixty Nine Thousand only). Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2020-21 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Out of the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21, the grants required for the financial year April, 2020 to September, 2020 have been released as per the above Reference No. 2 Approval has been granted under the informal reference here. In view of the above, the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been asked to submit a report in 2020-21 on the matter of (mandatory) expenditure on establishment of Urdu Sahitya Akademi.
