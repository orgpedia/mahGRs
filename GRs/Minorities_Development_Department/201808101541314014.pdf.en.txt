Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, New Delhi. Rajiv Gandhi's birth anniversary is celebrated as Sadbhavana Diwas on August 20 every year. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has also issued an Office Memorandum No. F- 28-22/2006-V. S4, the. In pursuance of the directions issued under 18th July, 2006, in pursuance of the aforesaid Sadbhavana Diwas, the Hon. from August 20, 2018. The Government has decided to observe 5th September, 2018 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada." The main objectives of this fortnight are to promote the spirit of unity, harmony and avoidance of violence among the people of different religions and different languages in different regions of the state.
2. d. To celebrate 20th August, 2018 as" Sadbhavana Diwas," the following programmes should be organized.
(a) In the Ministry. " Sadbhavana Diwas" should be celebrated on 20th August, 2018 and all the participants should be asked to take pledge of Sadbhavana Diwas and a Sadbhavana Run should also be organized on this day. A copy of the pledge and how to act on it is attached.
(b) The Director, Cultural Affairs, State of Maharashtra, shall organize group songs and cultural programs on the subject of Sadbhavana with the participation of the people of Brihanmumbai, especially the youth.

Further, to celebrate the fortnight from 20th August, 2018 to 5th September, 2018 as" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" in the State, the following programmes should be organized.
(a) To spread the message of social unity, programmes such as human chain should be organized in the district headquarters of the state, under the chairmanship of the concerned collector.
(b) The District Collector shall organize youth conferences and invite eminent freedom fighters of the district to speak in them.
e) The District Collectors should also organise other cultural programmes in the districts during this fortnight.
e) Director, Cultural Affairs, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to organize cultural programmes in Mumbai during this fortnight.
4. Directorate General of Information and Public Relations should contact the local officials of Doordarshan and All India Radio to give adequate publicity to the above-mentioned" Sadbhavana Diwas" and" Samajik Aikya Pakhwada" programmes.
5. All the concerned officers should take care of taking all the necessary measures for the success of the above mentioned programmes and submit a report to the government after the completion of the programme.
