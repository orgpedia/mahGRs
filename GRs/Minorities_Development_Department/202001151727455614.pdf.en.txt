Introduction: The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy was established in the first place by the Government Decision, General Administration Department, No. EUA-1075/ L-JJ, d. It was established on 16.04.1975. Since then, the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy has been reconstituted from time to time. Reference No. The matter of cancellation of the appointments of non-official members/ working presidents appointed to the said academy by the government decision at 1 and 2 was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai vide Reference No. The appointments of the following non-official members/ office bearers appointed by the Government in 1st and 2nd place are hereby cancelled:
