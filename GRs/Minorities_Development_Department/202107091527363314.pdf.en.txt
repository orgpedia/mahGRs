GOVERNMENT DECISION: The original budget provision of Rs. 120.00 crore (Rs. The amount has not been disbursed yet.
The Government hereby approve the release of funds amounting to Rs. 12,02,96, 700/- (Rupees Twelve Crores, Two Lakhs, Ninety Five Thousand, Seven Hundred only) for the distribution of scholarships to the remaining students of the academic year 2019-20 and 2020-21 as requested under letter No.10 dated 27th May, 2021 addressed to the Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Maharashtra, State, Mumbai.
The amount is being handed over to the concerned Directorate for sanction of scholarship to the students belonging to minority communities who have been eligible for scholarship for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 for which the concerned Coordinating Officer will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and its Director as Controlling Officer. Since the disbursement of this scholarship is to be done expeditiously, the question of furnishing the monthly expenditure statement of this fund does not arise.
Further, it is hereby permitted to use the balance amount, if any, of the funds released for the scholarships for the year 2019-20 and, if required, for the scholarships for the years 2019-20 and 2020-21 and also to use the balance amount, if any, for the scholarships for the year 2021-22. The details of expenditure and utilization certificate of the above mentioned accounts should be submitted to the government within the stipulated time. Also, all the directions/ rules/ government decisions/ orders issued in this regard by the government from time to time and the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the supervision of the Comptroller and Auditor General should be followed while spending this grant.
