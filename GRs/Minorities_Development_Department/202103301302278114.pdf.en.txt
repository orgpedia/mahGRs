The Proposal:
In order to provide opportunities to the candidates belonging to the minority community of the state in the government/ semi-government services, for competitive examinations like UPSC/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission/ Banking Service Recruitment, etc. as well as for the preparation of Common Entrance Examinations for admission to professional courses and for starting special tuition classes for the minority candidates who have failed in class 10th and 12th. 1 has been approved by the government.
Under this scheme, training institutes will be set up in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane and Pune. 2 has been selected. Government decision to the institutions which have started physical training. The first tranche of subsidy has been disbursed as per Government Decision dated 11.11.2020 as per the Phase-I subsidy pattern notified on 25th June, 2013. These include MT Educare, Mumbai, Siddique Education and Welfare Trust, Mumbai, Adarsh Multipurpose Society, Amravati, Excel Foundation, Amravati, Anjuman Hami A Islam, Nagpur, Spandan Multipurpose Education Society, Nagpur, Navdisha Backward Classes Multipurpose Society, Akola-based UPSC Academy, Pune and the Pune Gujarati Kalevani Mandal, Pune. The proposal was under consideration of the Government for sanction of second phase grant to the above 8 institutions during the year 2020-21 after examining the same as per the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 25.06.20213.
The government has decided to provide free training to the selected institutes in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane and Pune under the scheme.
The Government is approving the grant as per the terms and conditions laid down on 25th June, 2013 as well as the Phase Grant Methodology.
