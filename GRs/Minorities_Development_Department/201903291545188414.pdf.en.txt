Preamble For economic empowerment of women belonging to minority communities living in minority dominated areas of the State, it has been approved to set up Self Help Groups of women belonging to minority communities with the objective of organizing and providing adequate credit and developing entrepreneurial skills among them by providing them necessary entrepreneurial knowledge. Under this, in the first phase, Self Help Groups of women belonging to minority communities have been approved to be set up in Mumbai city, Mumbai suburbs, Nanded, Malegaon, Karanja, Parbhani, Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Bhiwandi, Mumbra-Kausa and Miraj. Under this scheme, 2400 Self Help Groups of minority women will be formed and their 13 Common Resource Centres will be set up within a period of 8 years. For this Rs. 27,54,32, 639/- (Rs. Twenty seven crore fifty four lakh thirty two thousand six hundred and forty nine only) the expected expenditure has been approved.
