Preamble: A proposal was under consideration of the government to open a new head of account for minority educational institutions in the state as the amount of fees paid online while submitting an application on the" Apne Sarkar" portal for obtaining minority status must be credited under the head of account.
Government decision: Minority educational institutions in the state should come to" your government" to get minority status
The Government is approving the opening of new accrual head for the payment of fees paid online at the time of submission of application on the portal as follows:
