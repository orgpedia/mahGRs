The term of the non-official members appointed to the Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi shall be three years from the date of their appointment. However, after the expiry of the term of the Academy, the said Academy shall continue to function till the actual restructuring of the Academy or until further orders of the Government. The Government shall have the power to terminate the appointments of non-official members of the Academy at any time before the expiry of the term of the said Academy. If the appointment of non-official members is cancelled by the government, the chairman, vice-chairman and government members of the academy will continue the work of the academy and in the alternative, the existence of the academy will continue.
The Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Akademi will work for the overall development of the Punjabi language, as well as implement various schemes for the exchange of ideas through translation or other literary endeavours in the two languages of the state language, Marathi and Punjabi. The Academy Office will be responsible for organizing various programs of the Academy. It shall be the responsibility of the officers of the Academy to organize the programmes or spend the funds permissible for these programmes under the guidance and cooperation of the President/ Vice President/ Executive Director of the Academy and in accordance with the prevailing government rules and regulations. The members appointed to the Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi are appointed by the Government Decision, Finance Department, No. Travel 1010/ Q.No.2/ Service-5, 1 Para No.1 of 03/03/2010. Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance will be admissible to the non-official members appointed to the State Level Committee/ Board/ Commission at the rates prescribed. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Minority Development Department, who will be appointed by the Government, will have the authority to sign the copy on the payment of Travel Allowance.
