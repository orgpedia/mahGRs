The Proposal:
In order to provide opportunities to the candidates belonging to minority communities of the State in Government/ Paramilitary Services, for competitive examinations like Central Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission/ Banking Service Recruitment etc. as well as for the preparation of Common Entrance Examinations for admission to professional courses and for starting special tuition classes for the minority candidates who have failed in class 10th and 12th, Reference No. 1 has been approved.
Under this scheme, training centers have been selected for training minority candidates in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane and Pune cities of the state. 2 has been implemented. In pursuance of the above decision of the Government, Diamond Charitable and Educational Trust, Nagpur, one of the above training institutes, has submitted a proposal for the remaining demand of grant for the training of CET, Special Tuition to the failed students of 10th and 12th standards for the year 2017-18. As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3 above, the first tranche of grant has been disbursed to Diamond Charitable and Educational Trust, Nagpur. As demanded by the above training institute, the reference number for this course for CET and special tuition for 10th and 12th failed students for the year 2017-18. The matter of disbursement of the remaining phase subsidy as per the provision of Government Decision No. 1 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the scheme" Maulana Azad Free Tuition and Related Schemes," the Government has decided to sanction grants for the year 2017-18 to Diamond Charitable and Educational Trust, Nagpur as per the strength of 85% attendance and final results of the students who have completed the provision as on 25.06.2013.
