Introduction: The Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Akademi was established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting the Punjabi language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through ideological exchange between the writers of Punjabi and Marathi languages. The decision has been taken by the government. The Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy has been allocated Rs. A mandatory provision of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only) has been approved and Finance Department has issued Reference No. 2 and Section No. 3, d. As per Circular dated 23.7.2019, for the 5 months period August to December, 2019, an amount of Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh only) is available for expenditure. Office expenses, travel and daily allowances of non-official members, honorarium of working presidents, refreshments for meetings, etc. are being borne by the Punjabi Academy for a period of 5 months from August, 2019 to December, 2019. 3,00,000/- is required.
