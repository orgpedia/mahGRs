The term of non-official members to be appointed to the Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi shall be three years from the date of the order of appointment. However, after the expiry of the term of the Academy, the said Academy shall continue to function till the actual restructuring of the Academy or until further orders of the Government.
Similarly, the Government shall have the power to terminate the appointments of non-official members of the said Academy at any time before the expiry of the term of the said Academy. If the appointment of non-official members is cancelled by the government, the chairman, vice-chairman and government members of the academy will continue the work of the academy and in the alternative, the existence of the academy will continue.
Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy will work for the overall development of Punjabi language. Similarly, for exchange of ideas through translation and other literary endeavours in the two languages of the State, Marathi and Punjabi, the Government shall implement such schemes as may be prescribed from time to time, strictly adhering to the ceiling prescribed for such schemes. The schemes to be implemented by the Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi and their maximum expenditure limit are prescribed as follows:
