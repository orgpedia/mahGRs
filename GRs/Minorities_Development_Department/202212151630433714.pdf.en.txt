The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages.
Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy is implementing various programmes. 2 from here. Administrative approval has been granted for the expenditure of subsidy amounting to Rs. 61,00,000/- (Rupees Sixty One Lakh only) during the period. Out of this, Rs. 55,02,000/- (Rupees Fifty Five Lakhs Two Thousand only) for the financial year 2022-23 was under the consideration of the Government for disbursement of grant to Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy.
Government's decision:
