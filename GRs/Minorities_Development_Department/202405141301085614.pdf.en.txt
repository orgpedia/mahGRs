The government was considering opening a new head of accounts to handle the budgetary aspects of the scheme.
Government Decision - To make budgetary provisions in connection with the implementation of scholarship schemes for postgraduate degree and PhD courses abroad for students belonging to minority communities in the State, the following new headings are being approved to be opened: -
