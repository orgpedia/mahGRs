The Proposal: -
The department implements the centrally sponsored Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram.
The funding ratio of the Centre B State share under the scheme is 60:40. SNA SPARSH is an alternative fund to fund centrally sponsored schemes to achieve greater efficiency in cash management at both central and state levels.
