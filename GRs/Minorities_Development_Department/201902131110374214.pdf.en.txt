Preamble: With a view to strengthen the spirit of national integration through the development of Punjabi language in the State, promotion and progress of the language and exchange of ideas among the literati of Punjabi and Marathi languages. Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy has been established as per the decision of the government. For the establishment of Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi, for 4 posts, the salary as per the prevailing pay scale is Rs. 11,80,000/- and Rs. 5,00,000/- and to implement various schemes of Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahitya Academy.
Under the grant of Rs. The Government has approved a budgetary provision of Rs. 30,00,000/-. The government was considering opening a new account.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Punjabi Sahit Akademi is pleased to approve the opening of two new Accounting Titles for the implementation of various schemes of the Academy:
