Introduction: With a view to strengthen the spirit of national integration through the development of Urdu language in the State, promotion and progress of the language and exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages, Reference No. The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy was established by the Government of Maharashtra. Since then, the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy has been reconstituted from time to time. Reference No. 4 In view of the death of Mr. Rauf Khan Majid Khan, Khadka Road, Bhusawal, Jalgaon, who was appointed as the working president of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy under the decision of the government here, the matter of appointing the working president of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy was under the consideration of the government.
The government has decided to appoint Mushfiq Ahmed Siddiqi alias Dr Rana, who will stay at Mira Road, district Thane.
The term of office of the Chairperson appointed to the Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi shall be until the actual reconstitution of the Akademi. However, the Government shall have the power to terminate the appointments of non-official members at any time. If the appointment of non-official members is cancelled by the government, the chairman, vice-chairman and government members of the academy will continue the work of the academy and in the alternative, the existence of the academy will continue.
Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy will work for the overall development of Urdu language, as well as implement various schemes for exchange of ideas through other literary endeavors through translation into the two languages of the state language Marathi and Urdu.
