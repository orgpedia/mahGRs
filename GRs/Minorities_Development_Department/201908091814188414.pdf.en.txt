Accordingly, as stated in Annexure-" A" to this Government Decision, Rs. 5,90,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Ninety Lakhs only) is being sanctioned under the Rural Area Development Programme and the grant is being handed over to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad through the respective District Collectors.
The District Collectors concerned shall act as Controlling Officers and Drawing and Disbursing Officers for disbursement of grants sanctioned to the respective Minority dominated Gram Panchayats. The Collector shall ensure that the works referred to in Annexure-A are not taken up under any other scheme and if the work is sanctioned under any other scheme, the Collector of the concerned district shall have the power to make changes in respect of such work within the limits of the sanctioned funds.
If it is not possible to do other work in lieu of the above work, the funds allocated for it should be deposited by the government. Also, the concerned collector should get the budget of the above works from the construction department. The Collector should take action to get all the documents in accordance with the terms and conditions of the government decision. After this, administrative and financial approval should be given to them and the funds should be handed over to the concerned Chief Executive Officer, District Council.
3. It is mandatory for the respective Gram Panchayats to spend the funds only for the specific purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned.
