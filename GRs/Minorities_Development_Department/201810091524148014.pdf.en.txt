State Plan Manager:- The State Plan Manager shall constitute a First Level Agency at the State level for the implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram and appoint officers/ employees subordinate to him as its administrator.
First Level Agency:- The administrator of the First Level Agency will be responsible for creating the Second Level Agency according to the fund distribution procedure of the Department of Minority Development.
The Second Level Agency will have officers of the rank of District Collector/ Director concerned, Directorate of Minority and Adult Education, Pune, who will be able to implement the scheme at the district level.
The Second Level Agency should take similar action from declaring the Third Level Agency as per the distribution of cash flow for these schemes currently operating on the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS) and similarly the next level agencies should be declared.
The final level agency through which the benefit is to be directly transferred to the beneficiary's bank account (e.g. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer (DDO) will be required to register the beneficiary or vendor first to provide the beneficiary or vendor at their level.
For this, they will have to register their bank account in the PFMS system. Also, it will be necessary to create a Login-ID of the Maker and Checker at their level.
