The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. In respect of expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy (Mandatory), the above Reference No. 3 Under the Government decision, out of the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20, Rs. Administrative approval has been granted for an expenditure of Rs. 6,70,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh Seventy Thousand only). Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of grant for Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy in the financial year 2019-20 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The above reference No. 20 provides for the release of grants-in-aid for the four months from April to July, 2019 out of the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20. 2 Approval has been granted by the Government Supplementary Letter. In view of the above, in the year 2019-20, in the matter of (compulsory) expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, a Demand No. ZE 1,2205 ART AND CULTURE, 102- ART AND CULTURE OPERATION (00) (01) Establishment of Urdu Academy/ Dattmat, 31 Auxiliary Grant (Compulsory) (2205 3027)" sanctioned under the head Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh only) for a limited period of 4 months from April to July, 2019. 6,70,000/- is available for disbursement. A grant of Rs. 6,70,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh Seventy Thousand only) is being disbursed to the office of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai for the month of April, 2019 to July, 2019. The Superintendent-cum-Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy, Mumbai shall ensure that the sanctioned expenditure is within the prescribed limits.
