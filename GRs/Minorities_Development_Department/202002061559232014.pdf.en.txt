Preamble: It has been approved to set up Self Help Groups of women belonging to minority communities for economic empowerment of women belonging to minority communities in the State with a view to provide them adequate credit by mobilizing them and developing entrepreneurial skills among them by providing them necessary information about entrepreneurship. Under this, in the first phase, approval has been given to set up self-help groups of women belonging to minority communities in Mumbai city, Mumbai suburbs, Nanded, Malegaon, Karanja, Parbhani, Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Bhiwandi, Mumbra-Kausa and Miraj. Under this scheme, 2400 Self Help Groups of minority women will be formed and their 13 Common Resource Centres will be set up within a period of 8 years. For this Rs.
