Government circular
As per the decision taken by the Central Government in 1986, through November 19, 2023. The week of November 25, 2023 is to be celebrated as" Qaumi Ekta Week" in the state and more and more people are to be involved in the next program to be organized in it. This year, it was Sunday. From Friday, November 19, 2023. November 25, 2023 should be observed as" Qaumi Ekta Week" and maximum people should be involved in it.
Sunday, November 19, 2023 National Integration Day: Organize meetings, seminars and symposiums with emphasis on secularism, anti-communalism and non-violence.
Monday, 20th November, 2023- Minorities Welfare Day: Emphasis should be laid on a 15-point programme for the welfare of minorities, special processions should be taken out to build brotherhood from the cities where communal riots are taking place.
Tuesday, November 21, 2023- Language Harmony Day: Special events and poets' meets to be organized to introduce the language heritage of people in other parts of India.
Wednesday, November 22, 2023- Day of the weaker sections: Meetings and rallies should be held to highlight the programmes set out to help persons belonging to Scheduled Castes and Tribes and persons belonging to weaker sections under the 20-point programme. The following points should be noted at this time:
a) Indira Awas Yojana and allotment of house sites and loans.
(b) Distribution of surplus land to landless labourers.
c) Programmes for providing legal aid to the poor should also be organized.
Thursday, 23rd November, 2023 Cultural Integration Day: Cultural events should be organized emphasizing on unity in diversity of Indians and presenting Indian tradition of cultural preservation and integration.
