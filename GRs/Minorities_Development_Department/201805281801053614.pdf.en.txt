5) Government Circular, Finance Department No. Finance-2018/ Q.No.69/ Finance-3, D. 2nd April, 2018 Preamble: In order to provide opportunities to the candidates belonging to minority communities of the State in Government/ Paramilitary Services, in order to prepare them for competitive examinations like UPSC/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission/ Banking Services Recruitment etc. as well as for the Common Entrance Examinations for admission to professional courses and to start special tuition classes for the minority candidates who have failed in 10th and 12th standards, Reference No. 1 has been approved.
Under this scheme, training centers are selected for training minority candidates in Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Thane, Pune and Aurangabad cities of the state. According to reference no. 2. As per the Government decision dated 12.05.2016, Prabodhan Multipurpose Institute, Nashik has been selected as the training institute for Aurangabad Centre for a period of 3 years. Under reference number 3 4, the grant for the year 2016-17 has been released to the Institute. The institute has now sought disbursal of the grant for 2017-18.
3. The above Enlightenment has examined the proposal of the demand for grant submitted by the Multipurpose Institute and referred it to Reference No. 1, the matter of sanctioning the grant in accordance with the provision mentioned in the Government Decision dated 25.06.2013 and the number of students who have attended 85% of the actual attendance was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the scheme" Maulana Azad Free Tuition and Related Schemes," Government Decision, dated 25.06.2013, grants are being sanctioned to the Training Institute at Aurangabad, Nashik of Prabodhan Multipurpose Institute as per the number of students meeting the provision and having 85% attendance.
As per the Budget 2018-19, Rs. 3.00 Crores, 70% of which is Rs. 2.10 crore is available for disbursement. Out of this available fund, as per the Government Decision of Reference No. 4, Rs. 32,62,000/- has been disbursed. The remaining Rs. 1,77,38, 000/- out of the balance provision, the training institute at Aurangabad, a centre of Prabodhan Multipurpose Institute, as mentioned in Annexure-A, will get a total amount of Rs. 42,01,250/- (Rupees Forty Two Lakhs One Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved. In this case, the Under Secretary, Minority Development Department Controller and Drawing and Disbursing Officer will be there to collect this amount.

3. The training institution concerned should maintain year wise accounting details in this regard and spend only on the items for which the grant has been disbursed. The utilization certificate of this amount will have to be submitted to the government by the concerned training institute. It shall be mandatory to submit the utilization certificate of these items to the Government as mentioned in Annexure' B'. Officials of the Minority Development Department will visit the concerned training institute. If it is found that the institution has not complied with the provisions of the Government Decision, dated 25.06.2013, action will be taken against the institution and the subsidy disbursed will be recovered.
