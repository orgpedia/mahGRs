The proposal: on 18.06.2019. The Minister (Finance& Planning) in his Budget Speech announced a scheme for providing skill development programmes, technical education and other employable materials to provide various employment opportunities to women and youth belonging to minority communities. The Legislative Assembly has approved the demand of Rs. 50.00 crore. The Planning and Finance Department has agreed to disburse 60% of the funds for July-December. The matter of distribution of funds in accordance with this agreement was under the consideration of the government.
