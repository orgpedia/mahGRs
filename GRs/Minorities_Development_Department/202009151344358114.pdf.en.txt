Introduction: The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi was established with the objective of developing, preserving and promoting the Urdu language in the state and strengthening the spirit of national integration through exchange of ideas among the writers of Urdu and Marathi languages. The decision has been taken by the government. The Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Akademi has been allocated Rs. 19, 80,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) has been sanctioned and the same has been approved in the Informal Reference No. 135/2020/ VY-1D. As on 3.8.2020, 20% of the sanctioned allocation for the 6 months April to September, 2020 amounting to Rs. 3,96,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Sixty Nine Thousand only) is available for expenditure. Therefore, the matter of granting administrative approval to spend from this available fund was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the financial year 2020-21, for the matter of expenditure on establishment of Maharashtra State Urdu Sahitya Academy," Demand No. ZE 1,2205 ART AND CULTURE, 102- ART AND CULTURE OPERATION (00) (01) Establishment of Urdu Academy/ Dattmat, 31 SUPPORT Grants (Non-salaried) (Mandatory) (2205 3027) Under the head" Rs. 19,80,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) as 20% of the budgeted provision for two quarters from April to September, 2020. Administrative approval is being accorded for the expenditure of Rs. 3,96,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Ninety Six Thousand only) as proposed by the Academy.
