Preamble: The Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India, under the Multi-Sectoral Development Programme (MSDP), on the basis of the parameters of population, socio-economic status and availability of basic amenities, has identified four minority-dominated district headquarters of Parbhani, Buldana, Washim and Hingoli in Maharashtra under reference No. Under this, IT cells have been set up for four districts- Parbhani, Washim, Buldana and Hingoli.
Government's decision:
A sum of Rs. 2.00 crore has been sanctioned for setting up of an Information Technology Cell (IT CELL) at the above mentioned Parbhani district headquarters for the purpose of compiling data, analysing data, updating statistical information of schemes, collecting data by visiting the scheme site physically, preparing report on the proposal of the scheme, sending quarterly progress report to the Central Government etc. A proposal of Rs 3.40 lakh has been submitted by the Ministry of Minority Affairs.
