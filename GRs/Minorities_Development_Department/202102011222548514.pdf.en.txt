GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the head' Subsidiary Grants (2235A 338) for Scholarship Scheme for Minority Students pursuing Higher Vocational Education and all courses after 12th standard," an original budget provision of Rs. 120.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crore only) has been approved for the financial year 2020-21. Out of which, a provision of Rs. 39.60 crore (Rupees Forty Nine Crore Sixty Lakh only) has been allowed to be spent as 33% of the total expenditure for the entire year. However, at present, only Rs.12 crore has been made available through the Budget Distribution System as 10% of the amount. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 10,41,28, 278/- has been released as on 14.07.2020 and an amount of Rs. 1,09,36, 829/- has been released as on 6.11.2020. The remaining Rs. 49,34,893/- is available for disbursement through Budget Distribution System.
As per the letter dated 27th November, 2020, issued by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra, State, Mumbai vide Roll No.5, a sum of Rs. 37,45,554/- has been sought for the allotment of scholarships to the remaining students for the academic year 2019-20.
Further, vide letter dated 11.11.2020 dated 11.11.2020 addressed to the Director Medical Education& Research, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, it has been requested that a total amount of Rs. 22,86,600/- may be required and made available to the remaining student belonging to minority community pursuing higher education.
In pursuance of the requisition made in paragraphs 2 and 3 above, the Government hereby approve the disbursement of the amount of scholarship to be distributed to the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra, State, Mumbai and the Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the minority students of their respective schools by way of grant in aid of grant in aid of Rs. 37,45,554/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty Four only) and Rs. 22,86,600/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Eighty Eighty Thousand Six Hundred only) respectively amounting to Rs. 60,32,154/- (Rupees Sixty Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand One Hundred and Fifty Four only).
The amount is being handed over to the concerned Directorate for sanction of scholarship to the students belonging to minority communities who have been eligible for scholarship for the academic year 2019-2020, for which the concerned Coordinating Officer will act as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and its Director as Controlling Officer. Since the disbursement of this scholarship is to be done expeditiously, the question of furnishing the monthly expenditure statement of this fund does not arise.
6. Both the said Directorates shall comply with the orders and the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission and the observations of the Comptroller and Auditor General.
