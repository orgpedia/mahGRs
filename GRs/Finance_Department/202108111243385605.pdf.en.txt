As per the Government Decision dated 15.02.2021, all the Administrative Departments have been directed to fill up the non-permanent posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing non-permanent posts which have been created after the approval of the earlier Review but which have not been included in the rolls. The extension was granted till 31.08.2021. However, the process of finalising the revised contours of all administrative divisions is yet to be completed. In view of this, the government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government- All the administrative departments shall be given temporary posts included in their figures as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the figures. 01.09.2021 to dt. The extension is being granted till 28.02.2022.
During this period, all the administrative departments, in any case, are required to review the sanctioned posts in their establishment and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control and obtain the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised pattern. All the departments should note that the time period for fixing the revised figure will not be extended. All the administrative departments have been asked to fill up the posts in their own establishment as well as in the establishment of the zonal office under their control.
