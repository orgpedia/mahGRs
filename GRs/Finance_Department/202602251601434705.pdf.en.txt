The Government's Decision
State Government employees drawing salary in an undefined pay structure (as per 5th Pay Commission) and others
The question of revising the allowable dearness allowance rate of eligible employees was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is ordering that the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on basic pay in the non-defined pay structure as per the 5th Pay Commission be reduced from 466% to 474% with effect from 1st July, 2025.
This Dearness Allowance increase should be paid in cash with effect from 1st February, 2026.
The arrears for the month of July, 2025 to October, 2025 should be paid in cash in the month of March, 2026. Orders regarding arrears for the remaining period will be issued separately.
