The government decision. In view of the possibility of changes in the audit report of District Central Cooperative Banks every year, the list of these banks will be revised by the Finance Department in consultation with the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing every year. Accordingly, the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing was under the consideration of the Government to approve 14 District Central Cooperative Banks with Audit Report A for the last 5 years recommended for the year 2023-24 under the present circumstances, to carry out government banking transactions and to invest additional funds from the Public Enterprises Corporation.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS 1. Feedback was taken from the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing in respect of District Central Cooperative Banks which are financially sound and have professionalism as per rules and fulfill certain other criteria, considering the safety of Government funds. The Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing has recommended the following 14 District Central Banks with Category A status in the audit report for the last 5 years.
Thane District Central Co-operative Bank 2. Raigad District Central Co-operative Bank 3. Ratnagiri District Central Co-operative Bank 4. Sindhudurg District Central Co-operative Bank 5. Ahmednagar District Central Co-operative Bank
