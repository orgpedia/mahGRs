Government Circular: Instructions regarding payment of refund of the total amount of contribution deposited in the defined contribution pension account of the employees, who are covered under the defined contribution pension scheme, in case of termination of service on account of forced retirement/ dismissal/ dismissal from service etc. 3 GOVERNMENT DECISION DATED 12/11/2010 AND ANNEXURES AND REFERENCE NO. 4 here. Circulars dated 28/07/2017 have been issued.
However, the orders did not mention the recovery of all kinds of advances from the employees and other government collections. Accordingly, instructions are being issued in respect of recovery from Government employees, who are covered under the defined contribution pension scheme as per this circular, on termination of service on account of forced retirement/ dismissal/ dismissal etc. from Government service, as follows: 1. Recovery of all advance amounts due to the Government and other Government dues, if any, shall be made from the amount of defined contribution and interest thereon credited to the defined contribution pension account of the employee.
(2) The liability for pecuniary loss to the Government incurred by the employee concerned, if ascertained by a departmental inquiry or by a judicial inquiry/ claim, shall be recovered from the amount of the return of the contribution deposited in the defined contribution pension account of the employee concerned.
This circular shall be applicable to the employees of Zilla Parishads, recognized and aided non-government primary and secondary schools, non-agricultural universities and affiliated recognized and aided colleges and agricultural universities, etc. However, separate orders in this regard should be issued by the administrative departments of the concerned ministries and the action should be taken at their level.
