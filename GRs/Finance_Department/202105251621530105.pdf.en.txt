Introduction: Dated from 1/1/2016 to d. The cases of retirement of the employees who retired from the service of the State Government during the period 31/12/2018 are being submitted to the Accountant General's Office after verifying the salary fixation as per the provisions of the 7th Pay Commission. Inadequate staff strength with the Pay Scrutiny Team is causing delay in checking the service books of the employees who retired during the above period. There is a shortage of officers/ employees who are helpful for this work, due to which the work of salary verification has been hampered. Considering the administrative urgency to remove it, it was under consideration of the Government to appoint some retired officers/ employees on contract basis as per requirement. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to expedite the settlement of wage settlement cases at the establishments of the Pay Scrutiny Team, Mumbai under the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries, the Government is approving, as a special case, the appointment of a total of 5 additional employees as required from the Accounts Officers/ Assistant Accountants/ Assistant Superintendents/ Deputy Accountants/ Junior Accountants on contract basis for a period of 11 months.
