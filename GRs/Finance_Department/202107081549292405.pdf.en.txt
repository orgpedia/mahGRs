The proposal: d. The Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 has come into force with effect from 01.07.2017.
The Goods and Services Tax (GST) has to be paid online on the website of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Department. Due to lack of legal and technical knowledge, the Directorate of Insurance is facing many difficulties in paying Goods and Services Tax. Therefore, the Directorate of Insurance, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is permitted to engage the services of a Chartered Accountant for matters relating to Goods and Services Tax. The approval has been given as per the Government Decision dated 03.06.2021.
To set up a Divisional Office of the Directorate General of Insurance at Nagpur. The approval has been given as per the Government Decision dated 01.03.2019. It has become operational from 23.09.2019. Goods and Services Tax (GST) on insurance premiums collected at the Mumbai and Nagpur offices was being credited to the GST number of the Mumbai office. Now a separate GST number has been taken for the Nagpur office and the Goods and Services Tax will be paid separately from the Nagpur office. Accordingly, the matter of allowing the Nagpur Regional Office of the Directorate General of Insurance to engage the services of Chartered Accountants for the activities related to payment of Goods and Services Tax was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Directorate of Insurance, Nagpur Divisional Office is permitted to engage the services of a Chartered Accountant only for legal and technical work in connection with the payment of Goods and Services Tax subject to the following conditions: (1) The Director of Insurance shall be responsible for sanctioning the necessary provision under sub-heading" (01) Management of Insurance Fund (22350534)" for meeting the expenditure incurred in this regard. The service provider should not be appointed unless budgetary provision is available.
(2) The Directorate of Insurance shall submit a separate proposal as may be necessary for approval of the detailed expenditure to be incurred in this regard.
