Government's decision
The question of revising the rate of dearness allowance admissible to state government employees and other eligible full-time employees was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st January, 2022, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on the basic pay in the revised pay structure as per the 7th Pay Commission be increased from 31% to 34%. The Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash with arrears for the period from 1st January, 2022 to 31st July, 2022 along with the salary for the month of August, 2022.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
