Introduction:
As per Ref (1) above, three State Benches of the Goods and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal (GSTAT) have been constituted for the State of Maharashtra and Goaas per section 109 (4) of the Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (CGST Act).
The Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017, Section 110 (4) (a) provides for constitution of a Search-cum-Selection Committeefor the recommendations in respect of selection of Technical Member (State) of the State Bench of the GSTAT.
Resolution:
As per the provisions of Section 110(4)(a) of the Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017and nominations received from Hon’ble High Court of Judicature at Bombay and Chief Secretariat , the Search-cum Selection Committee constituted for making recommendations for the appointment of Technical Member (State) of the State Benches of the GSTAT shall consist of:-
a) Hon’bleChief Justice of the High Court, Bombay(Chairperson) b) Hon’ble Justice (Retired) Shri Sunil B. Shukre (Member) c) Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra (Member) d) Additional Chief Secretary,General Administrative Department -Services, Government of Maharashtra (Member) e) Additional Chief Secretary/Principal Secretary/Secretary ,Financial Reforms, Government of Maharashtra (Member Secretary)
4. Secretarial assistance to the Committee shall be provided by Finance Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
