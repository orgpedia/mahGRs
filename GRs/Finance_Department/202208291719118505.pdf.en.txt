circular
The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2022-2023 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 17 th August, 2022. This bill has already been approved by the Lok Sabha. The Supplementary Appropriation Bill, to be included in the appropriations for the financial year ending 31st March, 2023, is proposed to be introduced in the House by Hon. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV, dated the 26th August, 2022 as the Maharashtra (Payment of Gratuity) Appropriation Act, 2022 (Maharashtra Act No. 40 of 2022).
All the Ministries/ Departments are being informed that they may seek approval of the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department, as the case may be, regarding the Supplementary Demand for Expenditure under the programme and the Finance Department regarding the Supplementary Demand for Compulsory Expenditure, before allocating the amounts of Supplementary Demands sanctioned under the Supplementary Appropriation Act. Proposals for such approval should be submitted on the basis of the original of the supplementary demands. If the original provision is not valid, the proposal should not be sent to the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department unless there are certain reasons for disbursing the supplementary demand.
