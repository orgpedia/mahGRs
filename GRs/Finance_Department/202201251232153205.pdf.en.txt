As of December 31, 2021
According to Government Order No. 1 Allow the state government pensioners to submit the life certificate by the government circular here. The deadline has been extended to December 31, 2021.
In view of the COVID-19 outbreak, the Central Government has issued the No. 2 Submit a proof of residence. The deadline has been extended to February 28, 2022.
On the lines of the Central Government, the time limit for submission of life certificate to State Government pensioners is also being revised as follows.
(1) Allow all State Government pensioners to submit proof of life. The deadline has been extended to February 28, 2022.
(2) These orders shall also apply to the pensioners of aided educational institutions, non-agriculture universities and their affiliated non-government colleges and agricultural universities and district councils.
