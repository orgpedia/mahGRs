The government was considering exempting posts under the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission from the restrictions on recruitment. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. Administrative Departments may fill up 100% vacancies in the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission. The following posts are being exempted from the restrictions imposed on recruitment under Government Decisions 1 to 7.
(i) Posts in the revised format as approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
2. New posts to be created by the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
3. Vacancies approved by the Sub-Committee for filling up of the existing sanctioned posts.
Accordingly, the administrative departments should complete the process of sending the requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission by September 30, 2021, after preparing the point list for these posts and obtaining appropriate approval. Also Ma. A high-level committee of secretaries headed by the chief secretary should review the action taken by the administrative departments from time to time.
