The Proposal:
While presenting the budget for 2022-2023, the government was considering to give administrative approval for the purchase of hard top, 4 wheeler, luggage trolley bags (cabin size) for the distribution of bags as per the prevailing practice.
Government Decision: While presenting the budget for 2022-2023, 841 hard top, 4-wheeler, luggage trolley bags (cabin size) will be purchased through tender process for distributing budget publications to the members of the legislature and others along with the bags from the pen drive. To present the budget speech of the Deputy Chief Minister (Finance) and Minister of State (Finance), 2 small leather bags (LD file case flap DLX) will be purchased through tender and the total estimated expenditure including advertisement of procurement tender and other miscellaneous expenses will be Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 62.55 lakhs (Rupees Sixty Two Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand only). Purchase No. 1 In accordance with the provisions contained in the" Revised Manual of Procedure for Purchase of Offices by Government Departments" read with the Government Order dated 01.12.2016, herein and under reference No. 2 The administrative department of the Ministry constituted under the decision of the Government here (itself) will be done through the Purchase Committee.
