The Proposal:
In order to ensure timely payment of dues to the farmers for the cotton procured by Cotton Corporation of India during the season 2019-20 under Minimum Support Price Scheme, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation, Mumbai (NIDCO) has received a total amount of Rs. 1800 crore (Rupees One Thousand Eight Hundred Crores) to be raised under reference serial number 4. The Government Guarantee has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 24.2.2020.
The said Government guarantee was valid for a period of 6 months from the date of issue of the Government Resolution. However, since the repayment of the loan taken on the government guarantee is pending, the government guarantee as proposed by the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles is not applicable in the case of A. No. 6. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 27.5.2021. The deadline has been extended to 30.6.2021. The government is yet to take a decision on repaying the loans. The Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles has proposed to extend the Government Guarantee under 24.2.2020 for another 6 months.
The government was considering extending the deadline.
Government's decision:
In order to ensure timely payment of dues to the farmers for the cotton procured by Cotton Corporation of India during the season 2019-20 under Minimum Support Price Scheme, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation, Mumbai (NIDCO) has received a total amount of Rs. 1800 crore (Rupees One Thousand Eight Hundred Crores) to be raised under reference serial number 4.
