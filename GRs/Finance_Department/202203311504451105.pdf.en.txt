the decision of the government. The rate of increase in Dearness Allowable to State Government Pensioners/ Family Pensioners drawing Pension/ Family Pension as on 07.10.2021 is Rs. It has been revised from 17% to 28% with effect from July 1, 2021. The increase in inflation has been ordered to be paid in cash from October 1, 2021.
(2) The Government are pleased to order that the State Government Pensioners/ Family Pensioners who are drawing State Government Pension/ Family Pension should be paid Rs. As of July 1, 2021. The arrears of inflation for the period of three months i.e. 30th September, 2021 should be paid in cash along with the monthly pension/ family pension of March, 2022.
The Pension Disbursing Authority, i.e., the Payment and Accounts Officer, Mumbai/ Treasury, as the case may be, shall be responsible for calculating the amount of dearness allowance payable in each individual case.
The Government also order that the above decision shall be applicable to the pensioners/ family pensioners of recognized and aided educational institutions, agricultural/ non-agricultural universities and non-government colleges affiliated to them, to whom the pension scheme is applicable, with appropriate modifications.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 248 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (Maharashtra Act V of 1962) and all other powers conferred thereunder, the Government hereby order that the above decision shall apply to the pensioners/ family pensioners of the Zilla Parishads.
