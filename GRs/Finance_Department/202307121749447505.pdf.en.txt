Preamble: The State Government in its Budget Speech for 2023-24 has proposed to raise Rs. 16,000 crore from Green Bonds to address the issue of climate change. 5,000 crore will be raised from green bonds for the state by 2024. This will include renewable energy, green buildings, green hydrogen, electric mobility and charging facilities. The state government has set up the Maharashtra Green Finance Working Committee (MGFWC).
In view of the above, the Maharashtra Green Finance Working Committee (MGFWC) is being constituted at the state level as follows:

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MIS- 10.23/ S.No.27/ EARTHPAY
The functions of the Maharashtra Green Finance Working Committee (MGFWC) will be as follows: 1) To finalize the framework for green bonds of the state.
2) Approval of works/ projects to be undertaken under Green Bonds.
3) To regulate the time-bound process under the green bond framework.
(iv) To take action in accordance with the other terms of reference of the Committee set out in the Framework.
