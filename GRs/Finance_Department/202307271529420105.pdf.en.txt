Government Decision: The Department of Finance, Government of India, vide its decision dated 14.03.2023, has decided to conduct a comparative study of the National Pension System and the Old Pension Scheme and submit a report to the Government recommending measures to ensure assured financial and social security at the time of retirement for employees appointed on or after 1st November, 2005.
A committee of officials has been formed. The committee has been asked to submit its report within three working days. However, the Committee is being extended for a further period of two months with effect from 14.06.2023 to analyse the information received by the Committee in statistical, economic, scientific and technical terms and submit a complete recommendation/ report to the Government.
