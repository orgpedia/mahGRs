the prelude

Adjustment of interest is carried out in accordance with the capital cost. The interest rate required for this is fixed by the government to the Accountant General's office.
While determining the rate of interest, the Budget Branch of the Department of Financial Affairs under the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government fixes the rate of interest for loans given by the National Small Savings Fund to the Central Government and the State Government by increasing it by 0.5% and reporting it to the Accountant General's Office.
Thereafter, the Office of the Accountant General has issued a notification in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, under Paragraph No. 25, 92 and Sec. The adjustment of interest is carried out in accordance with the procedure prescribed under Rule 7 of Return No. 153 and Annexure 8. In this background, the government was considering to fix the rate of interest for carrying out the interest adjustment process for the financial year 2022-23 in accordance with the capital expenditure on irrigation projects.

Government's decision
The rate of interest for carrying out the interest adjustment for the financial year 2022-23 is being fixed at 7.8 per cent per annum in line with the capital expenditure on irrigation projects.
