In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (2) of sub-section (3) of section 11 of the Maharashtra Value Added Tax Act, 2002 (MAHA-9 of 2005) read with rule 6 of the Maharashtra Value Added Tax Rules, 2005, and in exercise of all other powers, the Government of Maharashtra has constituted a Committee for selection of non-judicial (technical) members on the Maharashtra Sales Tax Tribunal. On the recommendation of the Selection Committee, the following officers or retired officers are being appointed to the Bench of the Maharashtra Sales Tax Tribunal as Non-Judicial (Technical) Members for a period of two years from the date they assume charge of the said office or till they attain the age of sixty years, whichever is earlier.
