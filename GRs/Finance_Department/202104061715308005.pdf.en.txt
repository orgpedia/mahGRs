All the Ministries/ Departments are hereby informed that the" Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021" (including the amounts specified in Column-4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2021) has been approved by the Legislature and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is hereby published in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, D. It was released on March 15, 2021.
While allocating funds for mandatory and programme expenditure included in the State Budget to all the concerned administrative departments at the beginning of the year, the Department of Finance is considering to fix the expenditure limit after taking into account the trend of the previous year's expenditure and the revenue received in phases during the year as well as the unexpected/ urgent expenditure that may occur suddenly during the year.
Before finalization of the matter, limited funds for the following objectives are being released on Budget Estimate Allocation and Monitoring System as a contingency arrangement for the urgent committed expenditure for the month of April.
