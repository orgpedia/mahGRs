The proposal-
The Government has decided to increase the rates of travel and daily allowance payable to State Government officers/ employees in accordance with the recommendations of the Sixth Pay Commission. Amendments have been made as of March 3, 2010. In paragraph 9 of this Government Resolution, Table No. The rates of daily allowances prescribed for hotel accommodation at the rates prescribed under clause (b) of clause (d) above. Amendments have been made in accordance with the Government Decision dated 02.05.2013. However, it has been observed that it is not possible to meet the daily expenses from the daily allowance payable to the officers/ staff on official tour staying in hotels of Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore and Hyderabad at the rates prescribed. The government has taken the decision. The rates of daily allowance (hotel accommodation and food and miscellaneous expenses) as prescribed under the Seventh Central Pay Commission, w.e.f. The matter was under consideration of the government to amend the office order dated July 13, 2017.
