Judgment dated 15.09.2021 4) Government Circular No. 22.11.2021 It has been brought to the notice of the Government that excess payment of Government dues has been made to the State Government officers and employees belonging to Group-A, Group-B, Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D on various occasions during their service for various reasons, not being payable to them as per rules or as per law.
In respect of recovery or remission of overpaid amount, read the above as mentioned in the Annexure 1 and 2. The Supreme Court has given the verdict.
Therefore, it is necessary to make timely payments.
Considering the legal opinion regarding the overstaying of state government employees during their service, the government circular is being issued as follows.
Government circular
The head of the office is required to add the certificate of service to the service book by conducting an annual verification of the service books and service films in the month of May every year as per Rule 45 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981. The departments should ensure that action is taken accordingly, so that there is no incident of excess harassment in the future.

(2) The service books/ service films of every officer and employee should be verified by the Pay Scale Verification Team at the age of 50 years without fail. So that the errors in their salary fixation can be rectified in time. The Head of the Office will be responsible for sending the service books of the officers and employees who have completed 50 years of age to the Pay Scrutiny Team. It will be the responsibility of the Pay Scrutiny Team to verify these books on time. Responsibility will be fixed on the officers/ staff (office/ pay verification team) responsible for this. So that there will be no time to recover the previous overpayment after the retirement of the employee.
Generally, the service books/ service films are verified by the Pay Scrutiny Team after the implementation of the Pay Commission. This will help them in clearing their salary arrears and also in recovering the excess payment.
The departments are required to take appropriate action as per Rule No. 41 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981.
