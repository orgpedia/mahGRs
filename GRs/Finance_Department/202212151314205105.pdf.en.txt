As per the provisions of Rule 849 (b) of the Bombay Civil Services Rules, 1959, dated 19th April, 2011, the licence fee is collected for the type of accommodation actually occupied by the government employees irrespective of their eligibility for residential accommodation. The Government is directing that the fee be collected at the existing rate as per the existing provisions till the final order is issued in this regard. The concerned employee. The basic pay in the unscheduled pay scale as on 01.01.2016 should be taken into account. After the revised order of license fee is issued, if the arrears in this regard are cleared, then it should be recovered.
