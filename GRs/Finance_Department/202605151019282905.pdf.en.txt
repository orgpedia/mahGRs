A Principal, Deputy Principal, Subordinate or Secondary within the limits of the same grant / allocation in the Act.
The administrative department is able to redeploy funds from the heads to other heads in the same manner. Administrative
The department should consider the following when approving proposals for redeployment.
1. Redeployments should be made to the books of accounts of the same demand number. Different demands
No redeployment can be made to numbered ledgers.
2. There can be no redeployment from weighted matters to weighted matters or vice versa.
3. Redeployment from the program ledger to the mandatory ledger or vice versa.
It won't come.
4. The provision cannot be redirected from the following objectives to any other objectives.
01 - Salary 04 - Pension expenses
06 - Telephone, Electricity and Water Charges 10 - Contractual Services
14 - Leases and Taxes 34 - Delegations / Scholarships
36 - Auxiliary Grants (Salary)
5. Central to Central share of Centrally Sponsored Scheme and State to State share.
The prior approval of the Planning and Finance Department will be required if the redeployment is to be made.
6. Budgetary provision under the objectives under which the redeployment is to be carried out.
Without it, there can be no redeployment.
7. The purpose for which the funds sanctioned by the Supplementary Demand have been made available.
It is necessary to spend for the purpose itself. Therefore, the funds sanctioned by the Supplementary Demand are elsewhere.
Cannot be reallocated.
8. Supplementary to the objectives under which the funds have been redeployed.
Funds cannot be demanded by demand.
