Government's decision
As per the Government Decision dated 7th October, 2021, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible to State Government employees and other eligible employees was revised from 17% to 28% with effect from 1st July, 2021 and the amount of increase in Dearness Allowance was decided to be paid in cash from 1st October, 2021. Further, as per the amendment, it was also decided to issue separate orders regarding the allowable arrears for a period of 3 months from July 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
The Government is now ordering that the arrears of 11% Dearness Allowance hike for the period of 3 months from 1st July, 2021 to 30th September, 2021 to the State Government employees and other eligible employees should be paid in cash along with the salary for the month of March, 2022.
The existing provisions and procedures relating to the payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply as it is.
