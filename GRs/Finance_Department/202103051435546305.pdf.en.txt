The Proposal:
Orders prescribing revised pay structure with effect from 1st January, 2006 and Rules prescribing initial pay in the revised pay structure. Issued on 22.04.2009. The guidelines for fixation of pay in the revised pay structure have been issued vide Government Circular No: Vepur-1209/ PR No.69/ Service-9, dated 29.04.2009 of this Department. the above. As per the Government decision dated 06.11.2009, a Pay Scrutiny Committee has been constituted to verify the fixation of pay under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2009 and the discontinued Pay Scrutiny Committees have been revived. Look at the posts in these teams, the government decisions here have been overturned from time to time. The deadline was extended till 28.02.2021. The, however. Since the verification of pay fixation done in the revised pay structure from 1/1/2006 is still pending, the question of further extension of temporary posts in this team was under consideration of the Government, taking into account the revised pay fixation works as per the 7th Pay Commission.
Government's decision:
Department of Finance, S.No. No. 2016/ S.No. 8/16/ A.P.C., Dy. 15.02.2021, all the Administrative Departments have been given temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the rolls. The authority has been given the power to extend the deadline till 31/08/2021.
