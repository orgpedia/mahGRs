Government's decision
The Government is ordering that the State Government pensioners/ family pensioners who are still drawing pension/ family pension in the unscheduled pay scale (as per the 6th Pay Commission), the rate of increase in allowable inflation from 212% to 221% with effect from 01.01.2023 on their original pension/ family pension amount. This increase in DA should be paid in cash along with the pension/ family pension of July 2023 with arrears from January 1, 2023.
The Pension Disbursing Authority i.e. the Payment and Accounts Officer, Mumbai/ Treasury Officer, as the case may be, shall be responsible for calculating the amount of dearness allowance payable in each individual case.
The Government also directs that the above decision shall be applicable to the pensioners/ family pensioners of recognized and aided educational institutions, agricultural/ non-agricultural universities and non-government colleges affiliated to them, to whom the pension scheme is applicable, with appropriate modifications.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to section 248 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (Maharashtra Act V of 1962) and all other powers conferred thereon, the Government hereby order that the above decision shall also apply to the pensioners/ family pensioners of the Zilla Parishads.
