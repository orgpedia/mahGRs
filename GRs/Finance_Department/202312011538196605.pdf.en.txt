The Proposal:
The list of 14 District Central Cooperative Banks in the State for the financial year 2023-24 has been announced as per the recommendation of the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing regarding District Central Cooperative Banks which are financially sound and professional as per rules and meet certain other criteria, considering the safety of government funds.

Relaxing the norms prescribed under the Government Decision dated 13th August, 2020, for the financial year 2023-24, the Government has decided to provide Rs. Mumbai District Central Bank has been directed to be included in the list of District Central Banks announced under the Government Decision dated October 17, 2023.
In this background, the Government Decision mentioned in front of No. 4 in the said chapter is being amended as follows.
The supplementary letter:
In accordance with the facts stated in the said Preamble, as a one-time special case, the Mumbai District Central Co-operative Bank will be incorporated as a co-operative bank for the financial year 2023-24 by the Department of Finance, Govt. of India. This Government Supplementary Letter of Approval is being provided to the list of District Central Banks notified vide Government Resolution dated 17th October, 2023.
