Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision in Reference No. 3 above, the procedure for obtaining Permanent Retirement Account Number (PRAN) from the Central Record Keeping Agency (CRA) under the National Pension System (NPS) applicable to State Government employees and All India Service officers of Maharashtra cadre, who join Government service on or after 01.11.2005, has been prescribed. Also refer to section 4 of section 4. Under section 1, the time limit has been given to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Treasury Officer to send the complete registration form (Common Subscriber Registration Form (CSRF) to the Treasury Office and the Treasury Officer to send the form to the Central Records Maintenance Agency or to the institutions appointed by them.
However, it has come to the notice of the Government that despite giving the time limit restrictions as mentioned above, the same is not being followed by the concerned, due to which the employees are not receiving the PRAN numbers on time.
Accordingly, the government was considering providing the facility of processing Permanent Retirement Account Number (PRAN) through Online PRAN Generation Module (OPGM) by the Central Record Keeping Agency.
