The decision was taken to revise the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible to State Government employees and other eligible full-time employees drawing salary under the unscheduled pay structure (as per the 5th Pay Commission).
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st January, 2023, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on the basic pay in the unscheduled pay structure as per the 5th Pay Commission be increased from 396% to 412%. The Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash with arrears for the period from 1st January, 2023 to 31st May, 2023 along with the salary for the month of June, 2023.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
4. The expenditure thereon shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the head of accounts under which the salaries and allowances of the Government servants concerned are spent. In the case of aided institutions and ZP employees, the expenditure should be covered under the sub-heading of the head of account under which the expenditure on their ancillary subsidy is covered.
The decision has been taken by the Maharashtra government.
