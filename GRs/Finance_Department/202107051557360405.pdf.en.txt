Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04/01/2021.
2. The posts for persons with disabilities have been ensured by the said notification of the Central Government. A total of 55 posts in all cadres for the disabled from Group-A to Group-D in the Goods and Services Tax Department under the Finance Department, as determined for the post of Group-A to Group-D cadre in that list. These have been ensured as indicated in the accompanying Return A vide Government Resolution dated 25th February, 2021.
