Department of Finance, Government Resolution No: NIMWA-2022/ PR No.47/ Service 4, dated 17.8.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is ordering that the rate of increase in allowable inflation from 203% to 212% with effect from 01.07.2022 on the basic pension/ family pension amount of State Government pensioners/ family pensioners who are still drawing pension/ family pension in the unscheduled pay scale (as per 6th Pay Commission). This increase in DA should be paid in cash along with the pension/ family pension of January 2023 with arrears from July 1, 2022.
The responsibility of calculating the amount of dearness allowance payable in each individual case shall be with the Pension Disbursing Authority i.e. the Payment and Accounts Officer, Mumbai/ Treasury Officer, as the case may be.
The Government also directs that the above decision shall apply, with suitable modifications, to the pensioners/ family pensioners of recognized and aided educational institutions, agricultural/ non-agricultural universities and non-government colleges affiliated to them to whom the pension scheme is applicable.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to section 248 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (Maharashtra Act V of 1962) and all other powers conferred thereon, the Government hereby order that the above decision shall also apply to the pensioners/ family pensioners of the Zilla Parishads.
(5) The expenditure in respect thereof shall be covered under the head of the budgetary head under which the pension of the pensioners referred to in the above paragraph is to be spent and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under that head.
All existing orders regarding payment of Dearness Allowance (DA) on pension, as approved by the Government from time to time, with suitable modifications, shall also apply to the Dearness Allowance approved now.
