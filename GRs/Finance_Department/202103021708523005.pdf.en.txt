The proposal: d. The Goods and Services Tax (GST) came into effect from July 1, 2017, both at the Centre and the states. The head of the department of the administrative department and the state tax commissioner are both of the rank of principal secretary. In such a situation, as the proposals of expenditure submitted by the Commissioner of State Taxes were not yet scrutinized by the Head of Administrative Department i.e. equivalent level officers, the matter of reallocation of financial powers of the Administrative Department to the Commissioner of State Tax was under consideration.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to reallocate the financial powers of the administrative department in respect of the total 2 financial powers in sub-division-1, 12 financial powers in sub-division-2, 1 financial power in sub-division-4 and total 2 financial powers in sub-division-5 in the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-II for the Goods and Services Tax Department under the Finance Department to the Commissioner of State Tax, Maharashtra State as per the time period and also to the Special Commissioner of State Tax, Additional Commissioner of State Tax, Joint Commissioner of State Tax, Deputy Commissioner of State Tax and Head of Department as per the principle of decentralization of powers as required.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Commissioner of State Taxes, Department of Goods and Services Tax, under the Finance Department, has been re-allotted a total of 2 financial powers in Sub-Division-I under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-II as mentioned in Annexure-A to this Government Resolution and 12 financial powers in Sub-Division-II as mentioned in Annexure-B and 1 financial power in Sub-Division-IV as mentioned in Annexure-A and a total of 2 financial powers in Sub-Division-V as mentioned in Annexure-D subject to the conditions mentioned in that Annexure. as well as decentralization of power

Government Resolution No.: Parichay- 1020/238/ P.No. 73/ V.P.-4
In principle, the Government hereby grant permission to re-appoint Special Commissioner of State Taxes, Additional Commissioner of State Taxes, Joint Commissioner of State Taxes, Deputy Commissioner of State Taxes subject to the conditions mentioned in the Annexure and the following conditions.
(1) The powers conferred shall be subject to the actual financial provision made by the Government taking into account the relevant terms, conditions and conditions.
(2) The financial powers conferred must be exercised in an adequate and responsible manner.
(3) Before issuing the sanction order under the financial powers provided in Annexure-B to this Government Decision, it will be necessary to obtain a certificate from the Assistant Director Accounts in the Goods and Services Tax Office regarding the availability of funds.
(4) A copy of each order issued under the financial powers conferred shall be sent to the Sales Tax Administration-4, Expenditure-7 and Regulation Offices of the Finance Department by mentioning the serial numbers mentioned in the Annexure accompanying this Government decision.
The Commissioner of State Taxes shall be required to review every six months the exercise of the financial powers conferred.
(6) The officer concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action if it is found that the financial powers conferred are being misused.
