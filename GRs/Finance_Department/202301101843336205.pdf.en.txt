Government's decision
The question of revising the rate of dearness allowance admissible to state government employees and other eligible full-time employees was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st July, 2022, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on basic pay in the revised pay structure as per the 7th Pay Commission be increased from 34% to 38%. The Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash with arrears for the period from 1st July, 2022 to 31st December, 2022 along with the salary for the month of January, 2023.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
