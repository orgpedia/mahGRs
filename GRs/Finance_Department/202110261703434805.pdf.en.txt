Preamble: The Government of India has appointed Cotton Corporation of India as the Principal Agent for procurement of cotton at MSP for the year 2020-21. The Cotton Corporation of India has appointed Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation Limited, Mumbai as Deputy Manager in the State for implementation of the scheme. In order to ensure timely payment of dues to the farmers for the cotton procured under this scheme, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers' Producers' Federation has been asked to provide a guarantee to the tune ofâ‚¹ 600 crore (Rupees Six Hundred Crores only) from UCO Bank, Mumbai at an interest rate of 6.00% and waive the guarantee fee on the government guarantee. The Cabinet gave. It has been approved in the meeting dated 22/09/2021. Accordingly, the matter of issuing a government guarantee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government is sanctioning Guarantee for loans aggregating to Rs.600 crore (Rs.600 crore only) to the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation, Mumbai (NIDCO) from UCO Bank, Mumbai (DHANCO) at an interest rate of 6.00% for timely payment of dues of cotton procured by Cotton Corporation of India during the season 2020-21 under Minimum Support Price Scheme.
The guarantee will be valid for a period of 6 months.

The following terms and conditions should be included in the agreement with Dhanco Bank regarding the loan taken on government guarantee.
3.1) Government guarantee will be limited to a total loan amount of Rs.600 crore.
3.2. Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers' Federation Ltd. is a subsidiary of DANCO Bank, which provides loans to the farmers for the purpose of repayment of loans received from the bank, the amount received from the Cotton Corporation of India and the amount to be disbursed to the farmers. A separate Escrow A/ C will be opened in the name of Mumbai and the loan taken from this account will be repaid.
3.3 The Government Guarantee will not be applicable for penal interest and any other dues on account of delay in repayment of loan from Dhanco Banks.
3.4) Dhanco Banks will sanction loans only after obtaining adequate security for recovery of loans from Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation. This will be checked by the concerned bank from time to time and at least once in every six months.
3.5) In case of default in repayment of loan by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation, the process of recovery of loan should be completed by selling/ auctioning the movable/ immovable property of the Federation. Also, banks cannot use government-guaranteed securities for collateral without making all efforts to recover the loans.
3.6) Dhanco Banks will be required to inform the Government about the delay in repayment of loan or payment of interest by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Federation within 90 days.
3.7 Dhanko Banks shall not, without prior approval of the State Government, extend loans to Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation beyond the limits prescribed in the order. Also, the borrower will not take any loan from any other financial institution for this purpose.
3.8) If a decision is taken to recover the sale of movable/ immovable property of Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation from Dhanco Bank, a committee should be formed for this purpose and one representative each from the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles and the Department of Finance should be included in that committee. The valuation of movable/ immovable property at the time of sale/ auction will be required to be done by the tertiary institution as per the rules of the government. Also, the finalization of the auction process with the report of both the representatives of the government in the said committee will require the approval of the government.
A breach of any of the conditions (3.1) to (3.8) above shall invalidate the Government Guarantee.
The Government Guarantee of Rs.600 crore to the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation will be subject to the conditions mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 5.11.1999 at No.1 as well as the following terms and conditions:
4.1) The Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers' Producers' Federation will be the Principal Debtor.

4.2. Loans from Dhanco Banks should be used only for timely payment of cotton dues.

5.Payment of Fee
As per the decision taken by the Cabinet, the guarantee fee on this government guarantee is being waived off. However, the responsibility of controlling the repayment of the loan taken on the government guarantee will be of the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Cooperation, Marketing and Textile Department, Ministry, Mumbai-32.
