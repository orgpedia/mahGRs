The Pay Scrappage Redressal Committee 2024 has been constituted to examine and make recommendations on the deficiencies arising out of the Seventh Pay Commission as well as other categories received from the Ministries / Administrative Departments.
The Committee shall have a period of six months from the date of its appointment (d. 15th September, 2024) has been given. The work of the committee is still going on. Hence, the committee has been given an extension to complete the remaining work and submit a report.
The matter is under the consideration of the government.
