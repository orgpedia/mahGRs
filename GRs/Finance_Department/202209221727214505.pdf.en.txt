The Proposal:
As per order No. 1 dated 1st October, 2003, the cadre of Ministerial Cell Officers have been sanctioned non-functional pay scale after four years of regular service.
As per Para 2 (b) (3) of the Government Decision dated 1st April, 2010, in respect of Assured Progress Scheme under the Revised Service, the inoperative pay structure to be provided to the cadre of Cell Officers after four years of regular service is part of the Assured Progress Scheme under the Revised Service.

In view of the above paragraphs 3 and 4, it is justified to apply the provision of the Government Decision dated 7th October, 2016 to consider the provision of temporary continuous service in the concerned post along with regular service for the benefit of the Assured Progress Scheme under the service (i.e. for sponsorship of non-formal pay scale) and also the non-formal pay structure to be sanctioned after four years of regular service to the cadre of Cell Officers as provided for in the Decisions dated 17th June, 2013, 12th March, 2018, 28th March, 2018 and 11th April, 2018.
Provisional service is considered for grant of remuneration and grant of remuneration in accordance with the Government Order dated 12th August, 1999 in Reference No. 9 for the purpose of meeting the minimum service requirement for promotion in the ministerial cadre.
In view of all the above background and the Government Orders in reference Nos. 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8, it was contemplated to amend the condition of four years regular service to prescribe four years uninterrupted service for sanction of non-permanent pay scale to the cadre of Cell Officers.
Government's decision
Considering the fact that the policy has been adopted to consider Provisional Service, Service on Remuneration Basis, Service on Contract and Service on Contract mode for the purpose of grant of Informal Pay Scale (i.e., for the purpose of grant of Informal Pay Scale) to the in-service Assured Progress Scheme/ Teacher Cadres, the revised condition of' four years continuous service' instead of the condition of' four years regular service' as prescribed under Order dated 27.02.2006 in the Reference No.1 for grant of Informal Pay Scale to the Ministerial Cell Officer Cadre is being prescribed with effect from 1.10.2003.
Further, in case of Cell Officers who have been given temporary/ ad-hoc/ local technical breaks in promotion, 4 years of continuous service shall be considered for sanction of non-functional pay scale by condoning the said technical breaks subject to the conditions contained in the letter dated 12th August, 1999 of the General Administration Department.
The Cell Officers who have been granted inoperative pay scales up to the date of these orders on or after the 1st day of October, 2003, taking into account their regular service of four years, shall be granted inoperative pay scales in accordance with the conditions as amended in paragraphs 1 and 2 above, taking into account their continuous service of four years in the rank of Cell Officer (in the case of promoted Cell Officers, continuous service of temporary/ temporary promotion) and shall be determined accordingly. The arrears of difference between allowable pay and allowances as per the said fixation of pay (adjusted against the amount paid after sanction of earlier inoperative pay scales) should be paid. Also, the allowable arrears should be paid by revising the pension and pensionary benefits, as the case may be.
