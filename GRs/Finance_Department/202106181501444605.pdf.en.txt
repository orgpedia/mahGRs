Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory to fix the posts in the government service for persons with disabilities. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for the disabled has been published under the notification dated 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the matter of ensuring the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the establishment of the Directorate of Insurance, Maharashtra State, under the Finance Department, was under the consideration of the Government for the disabled category.
Government's decision:
The central government. Notwithstanding the difference in the designations of the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the State Government establishments, and the posts in the list published by the Central Government, which have similar duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale as those in the State Government services, as notified under the notification dated 04.01.2021,

Those posts in the state government service will be ensured for the disabled, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has instructed. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
Accordingly, the central government issued the notification. The list published for the Group-A to Group-D cadres ensured for the disabled in the notification dated 04.01.2021 is for the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadres on the establishment of the Directorate of Insurance under the Finance Department. As per the Government decision dated 03.03.2021, the posts have been ensured as indicated in the accompanying Return-A. Also, the post of Clerk-Typist (Group-C) category has been decided by the General Administration Department, Govt. 26.02.2021.
2. the central government. The Department of Social Justice, under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Social Justice Department), has been mandated to ensure that the posts for persons with disabilities are in accordance with the nature of work, duties and responsibilities of the posts on the establishment of the State Government, in addition to the posts prescribed for persons with disabilities under the notification dated 04.01.2021. vide Circular dated 12.11.2020 before the Expert Committee. The proposal of the Finance Division was presented in the meeting held on February 22 and 23, 2021. In pursuance of the proposal of the Department and the recommendation of the Expert Committee, the posts for persons with disabilities are being ensured for the remaining cadres from Group-A to Group-D in the insurance operations as indicated in the accompanying prospectus.
