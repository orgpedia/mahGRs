The Maharashtra Zilla Parishad Finance and Accounts Service Class-III exam is conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) for the recruitment of Accountants in Group-B posts. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) will conduct the Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Group-C examination.
Therefore, the Rural Development Department has issued a proposal regarding the eligibility of employees of Maharashtra Zilla Parishad Finance and Accounting Service Class-3 to appear in this examination. 2 has been sent to this department by the nurse here.
Directorate Reference No. As per the feedback received from the letter here, the matter of conducting the examination prescribed for the promotion of the employees of Maharashtra Zilla Parishad Finance and Accounts Service Class-3 to the post of Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service through the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries was under the consideration of the government.
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has decided to conduct the departmental examinations conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) through the respective administrative departments. Accordingly, the decision to conduct the Maharashtra Finance and Accounting Service Class-1 and Class-3 departmental examinations from the level of Director, Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries has been taken in Reference No. 1 has been adopted.
The Directorate has agreed to conduct' Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Class-III Examination', which is mandatory for the promotion of employees of Maharashtra Zilla Parishad Finance and Accounts Service Class-III under the Rural Development Department to the post of Accountant Group-B in Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service under the Finance Department. Accordingly, it is approved to conduct the Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service Class-3 examination, which is mandatory for promotion to the post of Assistant Accounts Officer from Finance and Accounts Service, Group-B, at the level of Directorate, Accounts and Treasuries, Mumbai for Junior Accountants in Zilla Parishads.
