GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government have decided to revise the rate of Dearness Allowance from 154% to 164% with effect from 1st July, 2019 for State Government and other eligible employees drawing salary in the unscheduled (as per 6th Pay Commission) pay scale with effect from 1st December, 2019. It has also been decided to issue separate orders regarding allowable arrears as per this amendment.
The Government is now ordering that the arrears of 10% Dearness Allowance hike admissible to State Government and other eligible employees drawing salary in the unscheduled (as per 6th Pay Commission) pay structure for a period of 5 months from 1st July, 2019 to 30th November, 2019 be paid in cash along with the salary for the month of October, 2021.
The existing provisions and procedures relating to the payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply as it is.
