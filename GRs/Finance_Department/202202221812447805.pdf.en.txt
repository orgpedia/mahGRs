As per the Government decision dated 10.08.2021, all the Administrative Divisions have been directed to fill up the non-permanent posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing non-permanent posts which were created after the approval of the earlier review but which have not been included in the rolls. The extension was granted till 28.02.2022. However, the process of finalising the revised contours of all administrative divisions is yet to be completed. In view of this, the government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government- All the administrative departments shall be given temporary posts included in their figures as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the figures. 01.03.2022 to Dy.
The extension is being granted till 31.08.2022.
During this period, all the administrative departments, in any case, are required to review the sanctioned posts in their establishment and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control and obtain the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised pattern.
