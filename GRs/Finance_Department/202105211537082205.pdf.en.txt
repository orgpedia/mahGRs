Appointments in the cadre of Accounts Officer in the Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service are made by promotion, nomination and selection from Zilla Parishad employees in the ratio of 65:20:15.
Dated 1.1.2015. Officers working in the cadre of Accounts Officer during the period 31.12.2015. Provisional Seniority Reference No. 1.1.2016. Circular No. 1 was issued and objections were invited from all concerned.
2. Considering the objections received accordingly, the Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service, Accounts Officer (Group B Gazetted) Cadre has been appointed. The final seniority chart showing the position as on 1.1.2016 is hereby published.
The Directorate, Accounts and Treasuries are informed that this circular should be brought to the notice of all concerned.
