Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure a post in the government service for the disabled. Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities, as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, is published on the website of the Central Government at www.disabilityaffiras.gov.in under the heading List of posts identification suitable for person with Benchmark Disability Notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. According to the notification, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has been directed to ensure posts for the disabled in the state government establishments. The instructions have been issued under the Government Decision dated 02.02.2021. As per the instructions in the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure the posts of Group-C cadre driver and Group-D cadre peon/ dabtari/ naik/ xerox machine driver in the establishment of the Insurance Directorate, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under the Finance Department.
Government Decision: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the State shall ensure reservation of posts in Government service for persons with disabilities and review the list of seats in Government service ensured for persons with disabilities at regular intervals not exceeding 3 years. Central government. The posts of Group-A to Group-D, which have been prescribed for persons with disabilities under the notification dated 04.01.2021, are in the establishment of the State Government, although the designations of the posts in the list published by the Central Government in respect of such posts are different, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to those in the State Government Service.
