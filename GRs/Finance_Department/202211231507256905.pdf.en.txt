GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby order that the rate of increase in admissible inflation on the basic pension/ family pension amount of the State Government pensioners/ family pensioners who are still drawing pension/ family pension in the unscheduled pay scale (as per the Fifth Pay Commission) be revised as under:
-----------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The date of the inflation | Permissible Inflation Rate |
| 1    | As of July 1, 2018        | 284% (274% to 284%)        |
| 2    | As of January 1, 2019     | 295% (from 284% to 295%)   |
| 3    | As of July 1, 2019        | 312% (295% to 312%)        |
| 4    | As of July 1, 2021        | 356% (312% to 356%)        |
| 5    | As of July 1, 2021        | 368% (356% to 368%)        |
| 6    | As of January 1, 2022     | 381% (368% to 381%)        |
-----------------------------------------------------------------
The allowable arrears as per the above inflation should be paid in cash along with the pension/ family pension for the month of November, 2022.
The responsibility of calculating the amount of dearness allowance payable in each individual case shall be with the Pension Disbursing Authority i.e. the Payment and Accounts Officer, Mumbai/ Treasury Officer, as the case may be.
The Government also order that the above decision shall be applicable to the pensioners/ family pensioners of recognized and aided educational institutions, agricultural/ non-agricultural universities and non-government colleges affiliated to them, to whom the pension scheme is applicable, with appropriate modifications.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to section 248 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (Maharashtra Act V of 1962) and all other powers conferred thereon, the Government hereby order that the aforesaid order shall also apply to the pensioners/ family pensioners of the Zilla Parishads.
The expenditure in this behalf shall be covered under the head of the budgetary head under which the pension of the pensioners referred to in the above paragraphs is to be spent and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under that head.
