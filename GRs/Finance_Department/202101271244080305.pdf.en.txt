circular
The statutory development board wise mandatory actual expenditure is required to be submitted to the legislature along with the integral part. The Statutory Development Board wise mandatory actual expenditure (including integral part) for the financial year preceding the financial year for which the Budget is to be presented in this Budget Session is required to be submitted to the Legislature. All the administrative departments are required to place on the table of the House, the mandatory physical expenditure of their respective statutory departments, in the form of a single item (without sub-section) in the White Book and separately before the general discussion on the budget. Circulars dated May 8, 2014 have been issued.
The budget session of the Maharashtra legislature for 2021-22 is scheduled to begin in the first week of March. The state budget will be presented in the assembly during this session. In view of the above, in pursuance of the circular in this regard, it is hereby informed to all the concerned administrative departments that all the administrative departments shall bear the entire responsibility of submitting to the Legislature within the prescribed time frame the Statutory Development Board wise mandatory direct expenditure for the financial year 2019-20, excluding the year immediately preceding the financial year for which the budget is to be presented in this Budget Session.
