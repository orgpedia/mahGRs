Preamble -
Cabinet in its meeting held on dated 28.06.2024 has given guarantee to raise loans
from HUDCO of Rs. 5,500.00 crore for land acquisition of Pune Ring Road (East) project.
It was under consideration to give guarantee to the loan to be raised from HUDCO.
Government Resolution -
1. Government Guarantee shall be furnished to HUDCO as per HUDCO's format for
securing the loan of MSRDC at the rate of interest mentioned in HUDCO's letter at
reference no. 3.
2. Budgetary provision shall be made through P.W.D., Government of Maharashtra
for repayment of HUDCO's dues every year during the currency of loan covering
Principal, Interest and any other charges under the respective Loan Agreements
executed/to be executed between MSRDC and HUDCO.
3. Any additional amount that may be required for ensuring financial closure of the
project of land acquisition of Pune Ring Road (East) shall be provided by the P.W.D.,
Government of Maharashtra.
4. This G.R. issues with reference to the proposal of P.W.D. received vide file no.
KKS-2024/C R 175/Road-8.
5. The Joint Secretary/Deputy Secretary concerned of P.W.D. and the Joint Secretary
/Deputy Secretary (Arthbal) of Finance Department are hereby authorised to sign the Deed
of Guarantee for loan to be raised by M.S.R.D.C.
