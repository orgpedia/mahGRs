Read:- 1) Government Resolution, Finance Division No. 10.02/ S.No. 128/ Arthopai, Dy. 06.06.2008.
Preamble: Considering the difficulties faced by the District Councils, Municipal Councils, Municipal Corporations, Authorities in fully spending the funds released in a financial year, Reference No. As per the Government Decision No. 1, these institutions are allowed to spend the funds released and remaining unspent in a financial year till the end of the next financial year. Apart from this, the funds allocated to other government departments in a financial year must be spent by the end of March 31 in the same financial year. However, clear instructions have been given under the above government decision that it will be mandatory to return the unutilized funds to the government during the above period. However, proposals are being submitted to the Department of Finance for extension of time to spend the funds which are expected to be spent but unutilized till 31st March, 2023. A policy decision in this regard was pending with the government.
Government's decision:
Municipal Corporations, Municipal Corporations, Zilla Parishads, Municipal Corporations, etc. Funds disbursed before 31.03.2022 and lying unutilized till 31st March, 2023 are being allowed to be spent till 28th February, 2024.
(2) The Municipal Corporations, Municipal Corporations, Zilla Parishads and other authorities. Allow the funds drawn from the treasury but lying unutilized in the bank account before 31.03.2023 to be spent. The deadline has been extended to February 28, 2024.
Departments which are not in existence after 28th February, 2024 are required to deposit the amount with the Government by 5th March, 2024. Disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officers of the department responsible for non-deposit of unutilized funds by the administrative departments to the government within this period.
