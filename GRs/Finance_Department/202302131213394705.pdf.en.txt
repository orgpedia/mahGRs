The Proposal:
The Central Government had decided to revise the pay scales of Central Government employees with effect from January 1, 2016, based on the recommendations of the 7th Central Pay Commission. The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra under Read No. The members of the committee were Principal Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department.
The Committee submitted its report to the Government. It was released on December 5, 2018. Recommendations in this Report have been accepted in accordance with the Government Decision at Read No. 2. The State Pay Revision Committee submitted its report to the Government. Submitted with corrections on February 8, 2021. The government is expected to take a decision on the recommendations of the report. This report The proposal was submitted to the Cabinet.
Government's decision:
The Government has taken a decision in respect of the recommendations contained in Volume-II of the Report of the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017. The details of the recommendations of the Committee and the decisions taken by the Government thereon are given in Annexure-I and Annexure-II.
