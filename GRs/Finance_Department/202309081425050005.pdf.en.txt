As per the Government Decision dated 08.02.2023, all the Administrative Divisions have been directed to fill up the non-permanent posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing non-permanent posts which have been created after the approval of the previous Review but which have not been included in the rolls. The extension was granted till 31.08.2023. However, the process of finalising the revised contours of all administrative divisions is yet to be completed. In view of this, the government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government- All the administrative departments shall be given temporary posts included in their figures as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the figures. 01.09.2023 to dt. The extension is being granted till 28.02.2024.

Government Decision No. Designation 2016/ No. 8/16/ PC During the said period, all the Administrative Departments are required to review the sanctioned posts in their establishment and in the establishment of the offices under their administrative control in any case and obtain the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised figure. All the departments should note that the time period for fixing the revised figure will not be extended. All the administrative departments should review the posts on their (own) establishment as well as the posts on the establishment of the zonal office under their control and give instructions to the concerned officials to take immediate action by planning to submit the proposal of the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately.
