GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government Decision, dated 4th January, 2020, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible to State Government employees and other eligible employees has been revised from 12% to 17% with effect from 1st July, 2019 and the amount of increase in Dearness Allowance has been decided to be paid in cash from 1st December, 2019. It has also been decided to issue separate orders regarding allowable arrears as per this amendment.
The Government is now ordering that the arrears of 5% Dearness Allowance hike for the period of 5 months from 1st July, 2019 to 30th November, 2019 for the State Government employees and other eligible employees should be paid in cash along with the salary for the month of October, 2021.
The existing provisions and procedures relating to the payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply as it is.
