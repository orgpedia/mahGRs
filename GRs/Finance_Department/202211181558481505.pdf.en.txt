(3) The following terms and conditions shall be included in the agreement to be entered into with the financial institution for loans on Government guarantee.
3.1) Government Guarantee Total Rs. Rs 12,000 crore.
3.2 The Government Guarantee will not be applicable for penal interest and any other dues levied by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for delay in repayment of loans taken from financial institutions.
3.3 The financial institution concerned shall be obliged to give notice to the Government of the delay in repayment of the loan or payment of interest by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority within 90 days.

3.4.Dhanco shall not, without prior approval of the State Government, grant loans to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority in excess of the limits prescribed in this Order.
3.5) Future assets arising from loans taken from Dhanco institutions should be kept as collateral.
3.6) A separate Escrow A/ C will be opened in the name of Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for the loan received from the financial institution, the amount received from the project and the repayment of the financial institution and the loan taken from this account will be repaid.
3.7) In case of default in repayment of loan by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, the process of recovery of the loan should be completed by selling/ auctioning the mortgaged/ immovable property of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority. Also, the government guarantee cannot be used as a security for guaranteeing the financial institution concerned without making all efforts to recover the loan.
3.8) If a decision is taken to recover the sale of mortgaged movable/ immovable property of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority from Dhanco, a committee should be formed for this purpose and one representative each from the Urban Development Department and the Finance Department should be included in that committee. While selling/ auctioning the mortgaged movable/ immovable property, the valuation will be required to be done by the tertiary institution as per the rules of the government. Also, the finalization of the auction process with the report of both the representatives of the government in the said committee will need the approval of the government.
A breach of any of the conditions (3.1) to (3.8) above shall invalidate the Government Guarantee.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) will be given Rs. 12,000 crore under the Government Guarantee Scheme. 5.11.1999 shall be subject to the following terms and conditions:
4.1 The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall be the Principal Debtor.
4.2 The loan from Dhanco shall be used for the purpose for which the loan is taken.
4.3) Dhanco will sanction the loan only after obtaining adequate security from the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority. The financial institution concerned shall examine the proposal from time to time and at least once in every six months.
4.4) The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall take over as much of the loan as may be required.
4.5) The Urban Development Department/ Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) will be solely responsible for ensuring that the interest rate is minimum considering the prevailing bank rate of the Reserve Bank while taking loans from Dhanco.
4.6 The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall be solely responsible for the repayment of the loan (principal+ interest).
4.7) The Urban Development Department should take care that the government will not be responsible for the loan given on the government guarantee.

5.Payment of Fee
As per the decision taken by the Cabinet, the guarantee fee on this government guarantee is being waived off.
