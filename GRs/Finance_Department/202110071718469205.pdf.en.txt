the decision of the government. The rate of increase in Dearness Allowable to State Government Pensioners/ Family Pensioners drawing Pension/ Family Pension as on 7.1.2020 is Rs. (2) The Government are pleased to order that the State Government Pensioners/ Family Pensioners who are drawing State Government Pension/ Family Pension should be paid Rs. From July 1, 2019. The arrears of inflation for the period of five months i.e. 30th November, 2019 should be paid in cash along with the monthly pension/ family pension of October, 2021.
