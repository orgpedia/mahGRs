Government circular:
In terms of the procedure to be followed by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Treasury/ Sub-Treasury Officer as well as the Accounts Officer, Mumbai in respect of deduction of TDS@ 2% from the total amount payable to the Supplier/ Contractor in respect of goods or services or both as per Section 51 of the Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 and the Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017, the above Reference No. 3 were suggested.
As per the circular, it has been made mandatory for the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to submit a certificate in the form of Annexure' D' along with the payment for deduction of 2% TDS from the total amount of payment due to the supplier/ contractor. Further, instructions have been issued to examine whether TDS of 2% has been deducted from the payment at the time of sanction of payment by Treasury/ Sub-Treasury Officer and Accounts Officer, Mumbai and in case of non-deduction, to object to the payment.
The reference No. 3 In addition to the instructions given in the circular, the following specific instructions are being issued at present.
(a) Procedure to be followed by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer: (1) The Drawing and Disbursing Officer is required to submit a certificate in the form of Annexure' E' attached with the payment.
