Preamble: In order to ensure timely payment to the farmers for the cotton procured by Cotton Corporation of India during the season 2019-20 under Minimum Support Price Scheme, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation, Mumbai (LANIKO) has received a loan from Bank of India, Mumbai (DHANIKO) at an interest rate of 7.75%. 1800 crore (Rupees One Thousand Eight Hundred Crores) to be raised as loans under reference serial number 4. The Government Guarantee has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 24.2.2020. The said Government guarantee was valid for a period of 6 months from the date of issue of the Government Resolution. However, the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles has submitted a proposal to extend the government guarantee in view of the fact that the loan taken on government guarantee is yet to be repaid and in view of the demand from Dhanco Bank to renew the government guarantee. The government was considering extending the deadline.
Government's decision:
In order to ensure timely payment of dues to the farmers for the cotton procured by Cotton Corporation of India during the season 2019-20 under Minimum Support Price Scheme, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation, Mumbai (NIDCO) has received a total amount of Rs. 1800 crore (Rupees One Thousand Eight Hundred Crores) to be raised under reference serial number 4. The Government Guarantee issued vide Government Order dated 24.2.2020 is being extended till 30.6.2021.
