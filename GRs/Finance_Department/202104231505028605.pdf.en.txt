In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Maharashtra is pleased to amend the Schedule to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019, issued vide Government Notification, Finance Department, No: Vepur 2019/ PR No.1/ Service-9, dated the 30th January, 2019, as follows:
In the heading Commissioner State Excise under Home Department (10) on page 137, the designation at No.5 should be read as" Director (Enforcement& Vigilance)/ Director (Implementation& Vigilance)" instead of" Director/ Director."
The designation at No.6 should be read as" Deputy Commissioner (Senior) (Group-A)/ Deputy Commissioner (Senior) (Group-A)" instead of" Divisional Deputy Commissioner/ Divisional Deputy Commissioner."
The designation at No.9 should read" Deputy Commissioner (Junior) (Group-A)/ Deputy Commissioner (Junior) (Group-A)" instead of" Deputy Commissioner/ Deputy Commissioner."
