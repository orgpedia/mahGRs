Considering the duties and responsibilities of the officers/ employees on establishment of Directorate, Accounts and Treasuries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and Subordinate Offices, a total of 11 vehicles have been sanctioned for such posts, 4 vehicles for the office of Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 6 vehicles for each of the 6 Subordinate Regional Offices and 1 vehicle for the Subordinate and Accounts Office, Mumbai.
Also, the State Level Vehicle Review Committee under the decision of the Government at No. 3 in the above' Read'
As per the approval, some of the Sub-Treasury offices under the District Treasury Offices are located in forested, Naxal-affected areas with tribal population. These taluks are very remote and remote from the headquarters in the hilly areas and there are hardly any public transport facilities. Therefore, in order to ensure effective administrative control, 14 such District Treasury Offices have been provided with jeeps in the first phase.
Vehicles are allowed.
Accordingly, as per the request of the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries at No. 5 in the above mentioned' Vacha' for office use in the second phase, a total of 21 vehicles were under the consideration of the Government, including 20 vehicles for the remaining 20 District Treasury Offices and 1 vehicle for the State Records Maintenance Agency, Mumbai Office.
Government's decision:
As per the approval given by the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, a total of 21 vehicles are being permitted for 20 District Treasury Offices under the Directorate, Accounts and Treasuries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 1 vehicle for the office of State Records Maintenance Agency, Mumbai.
