Government Decision- The question of revision in the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible to State Government employees and other eligible full-time employees drawing salary in the unscheduled pay structure (as per the 6th Pay Commission) was under consideration of the Government.
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st July, 2022, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on the basic pay in the unscheduled pay structure as per the 6th Pay Commission be increased from 203% to 212%. The Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash with arrears for the period from 1st July, 2022 to 31st December, 2022 along with the salary for the month of January, 2023.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
