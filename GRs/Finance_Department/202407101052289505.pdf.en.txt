The Government's Decision-
The question of revising the rate of dearness allowance admissible to state government employees and other eligible full-time employees was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is ordering that the 7th Pay Commission be revised with effect from 1st January, 2024.
The rate of allowable dearness allowance on basic pay in the pay structure should be increased from 46 per cent to 50 per cent. The increase in Dearness Allowance is for the month of July with arrears for the period 1st January, 2024 to 30th June, 2024.
Payments should be made in cash along with the 2024 salary.
