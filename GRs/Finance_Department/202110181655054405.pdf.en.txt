Circular: In the Budget Session 2022 of the Legislature, the Programme Budgets for the year 2022-23 are to be made available to the Members of the Legislature on the same day or on the next day after the Budget is presented to the Legislature.
Since 1989-90, the programme budgets are printed only in Marathi. The brochures/ charts are printed in both English and Marathi languages. 50 additional copies of these brochures/ charts are printed and bound with the English version printed by the Ministries/ Departments.
Considering the fact that from 2017-2018, the under-plan and non-plan expenditure have been combined and now divided into programme and mandatory expenditure respectively, care should be taken to prepare the programme budgets for 2022-23.
4. All the suggestions submitted so far should be taken into consideration while preparing the programme budgets. In particular, since the publication of" Financial Assistance in the Budget of the Government of Maharashtra" has been discontinued, the information provided in this publication, while being included in the Programme Budget 2022-23 as last year, is not included in the above reference No. Be sure to follow the instructions in Section 2.
Steering Committee for the formulation and implementation of schemes for women implemented by various Ministries. In the meeting held on 21/08/2013 under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary, it has been decided that while preparing the programme budget, all the departments should show the information related to women in their schemes in a separate statement and give necessary details therein. All the authorities should act accordingly.
Officers of the Finance Department deposed before the Estimates Committee on 23.2.2021. In this testimony, the committee has presented the budget of all the departments, the budget of all the departments, the economic survey of Maharashtra, the boards under various departments, corporations, autonomous bodies, companies, etc.

The report, as well as other related information, has been instructed to be submitted online to the legislature. Reference No. 5 vide letter dated 26.2.2021, all the Departments are requested to take immediate action for making available their aforesaid publications/ information online to the Legislature.
Similarly, since the publication of the Programme Budget is to be made available to the members of the Legislature on the same day or the next day after the presentation of the Budget to the Legislature in the Budget Session, the Programme Budget should be made available online to the Legislature within the prescribed time and only the necessary copies of the records should be printed. The department should make the program of its department available on the website of the budget department. The online link should be made available on the website of the state government and the legislature. This link should also be made available to the sites where the budget publications of the Finance Department are available. The department should take immediate action in this regard.
The Ministry departments are required to print the Programme Budgets for the year 2022-23 in the Government Press designated for printing the Programme Budgets. Regarding the printing system of the program budget, the Directorate of Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai, Shri. Letter dated 13th November, 1995 No. Karyasana-6/ Mun-4 (Ka. (b) By 1941, all the Ministries were notified. There has been no change in it. If the departments have any problem regarding this printing system, then the program budgets for the year 2022-23 should be printed after discussing with the Director, Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai.
8. For contacting the concerned officers of the department regarding the printing of the program budget, information about the names of the officers, their office addresses, telephone numbers should be sent to the printing press assigned to the departments and a copy of it should be sent to the Director, Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai and the Finance Department for information. Similarly, the handwriting/ printout of the programme budget should be sent to the Director, Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai and the Finance Department.
The schedule for preparation of the Programme Budget for 2022-23 and its presentation to the Legislature is attached. However, all the Ministries/ Departments shall prepare their manuscripts keeping in view the instructions in the above circulars and strictly adhere to the following schedule by completing the works indicated therein in time and sending the manuscripts to the designated Government Printing Press in time for the publication of the programme budget of their department. Primarily, the Estimates Committee should consider the recommendations made from time to time on the programme budget and re-examine their publications before sending them for final printing.
During the year 2013-14, due to late submission of programme budgets by some departments to the Legislative Assembly and before discussion on their budgetary demands in the House, the Government has decided to withdraw the programme. Opposition leaders raised the issue with the group leader as the members were not available. At that time, Ma. The Assembly Speaker had suggested action against the departments which do not submit the programme budget within the time limit.
Accordingly, the Head of the Department of each department should ensure that the publications of the" Programme Budget" on the Programme Budget are submitted to the Legislature on the same day or on the next day after the presentation of the Budget for 2022-23. In this regard, the instructions given under paragraph 2 of the circular at Reference No. 3 should also be followed.
