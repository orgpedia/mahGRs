Government Circular No.: SRIKH- 2021/ PR No. 99/21/ KOSHA- PRASH 5
India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) has launched the facility of online submission of digital life certificate.
To apply for this service, you can download the" Postinfo App" from the Google Play Store. Pensioners/ family pensioners who are unable to use mobile phones should visit the nearest post office. After registering the request with the Department of Posts, the postman will come to the pensioner's residence along with the required material and take fingerprints. Be aware that this service is free of charge. Details of submission of Digital Life Certificate online through postman can be found on You Tube at https:// youtu. be/ cERwM U7g54 is available at this link.
Pensioners/ family pensioners residing abroad can submit the life certificate through the authorized officers of the Indian Embassy, High Commission of India or Indian Consulate.
Pensioners/ family pensioners can submit the life certificate in any of the above ways to the concerned Treasury Office, Subordinate and Accounts Office. For more details regarding submission of online life certificate, please visit the above mentioned website or contact the Pension Branch of the concerned District Treasury/ Overdue and Accounts Office.
