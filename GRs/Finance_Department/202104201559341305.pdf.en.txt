Chief Secretary's letter No. Divyang- 2021/ Parivartan 11/ Dec.2, dated 06.02.2021 Government Decision The Central Government is required to ensure the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi vide notification dated 04.01.2021 has notified the list of posts for persons with disabilities and published the same on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of posts identification suitable for person with Benchmark Disability Notified on 04.01.2021 in Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt.
As per the Central Government Notification dated 02.02.2021 dated 251/ 2019/ P.S.D.2, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to issue a Government Resolution regarding the posts in Group A to Group D in the State Government establishments which have been identified by the Central Government for Divyangjan and has been forwarded to the General Administration Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune.
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi vide notification dated 04.01.2021 has ensured posts for Divyangjan. As decided for the posts of Drivers in Group C cadre and Constables in Group-D cadre in that list, the posts for Divyang as shown in the statement-A in the Finance Department (itself) are being ensured through this Government decision.
