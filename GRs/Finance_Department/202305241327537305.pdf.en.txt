As per the Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019, it has been decided to credit the arrears of 7th Pay Commission to the Employees' Provident Fund Scheme account in 5 equal installments over the next 5 years from the year 2019-20 and pay the same in cash to the retired employees. The procedure in this regard has been prescribed by the Government Circular Finance Department, dated February 20, 2019. Further, instructions have been issued vide Government Circular dated 30th May, 2019 regarding payment of arrears in cash in 5 equal installments over a period of 5 years to the employees who are covered under National Pension Scheme or Defined Contributory Pension Scheme.

Also, the payment of pension arrears in cash in five years, in five equal instalments, has been ordered as per the Government Decision dated 24th January, 2019 and 1st March, 2019.
Considering the situation arising out of the COVID-19 (Corona) pandemic in the State and its adverse impact on the revenue collection of the State, the Government has decided to disburse the 3rd installment of 7th Pay Commission arrears due to State Government and other eligible employees and pensioners as on 1st July, 2021, as per the decision of the Department of Finance, dated 9th May, 2022. Also, no decision was taken regarding the payment of the remaining installments due in this government decision.
In the above background, the question of payment of remaining instalments of arrears was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The Government is now ordering that the amount of 4th instalment of arrears of 7th Pay Commission payable to State Government and other eligible employees and retired employees as on 1st July, 2022, respectively, be credited to the account of Provident Fund Scheme or paid in cash as mentioned below:
(a) Pensioners should be paid the 4th installment of pension arrears in cash along with the pension for the month of June, 2023.
(b) Payment of the 4th installment of arrears of State Government employees to be made along with the salary for the month of June, 2023.
The 4th installment of arrears of eligible employees in all Zilla Parishads, Government aided schools and all other Government aided institutions should be paid along with the salary for the month of June, 2023.
