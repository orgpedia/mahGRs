The government was considering the question of revising the rate of dearness allowance for state government employees and other eligible full-time employees.
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st July, 2021, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on basic pay in the revised pay structure as per the 7th Pay Commission be increased from 28% to 31%. This Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash along with the salary for the month of March, 2022, with arrears from the date of increase, 1st July, 2021.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
4. The expenditure thereon shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the head of accounts under which the salaries and allowances of the Government servants concerned are spent.
