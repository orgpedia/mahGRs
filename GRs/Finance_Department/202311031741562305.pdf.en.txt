The Proposal:
The list of 14 District Central Cooperative Banks in the State for the financial year 2023-24 has been announced as per the recommendation of the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing regarding District Central Cooperative Banks which are financially sound and professional as per rules and meet certain other criteria, considering the safety of government funds.
Subsequently, the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing has recommended to the Finance Department to include Gondia District Central Co-operative Bank in the list of District Central Co-operative Banks notified under the above mentioned Government Decision for the financial year 2023-24. In this background, the Government Decision mentioned in front of No. 4 in the said chapter is being amended as follows.
Government Supplementary Paper:
In accordance with the facts stated in the said Preamble, the Bank, Gondia District Central Co-operative Bank, will be incorporated for the financial year 2023-24 by the Department of Finance, Govt. of India. This Government Supplementary Letter of Approval is being provided to add these districts to the list of Central Banks announced under the Government Decision dated 17th October, 2023.
