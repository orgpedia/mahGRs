7) Elimination of the minus authorization from the budgetary system:
In order to avoid delays in the financial transactions of the government, it has been allowed to issue minus authorization letter from the budget distribution system to spend on certain types of targets. The issuance of minus authorization letter from the budget distribution system will be stopped after the receipt of funds as per the eighth revised budget. However, if payments are made with a letter of authorization, the Treasury Officer shall promptly notify the Directorate of Accounts and the Treasurers of such payments.
Such payments should not be passed without receiving orders from them.
8. Controlling Authorities to make available the grants received through Supplementary Demand on BEAMS system expeditiously:
After the government approves the grants to various departments as per the eighth revised budget and through supplementary demands, the administrative departments and department heads provide the grants to the drawing and disbursing officers on the BEAMS system. Then there is a delay in processing the payments, submission to the sub-treasury, etc. Due to the increased load. On 31st March, there are instances of breakdown of BEAMS system as well as Treasury Net system due to additional stress. To overcome this, all the departments/ controlling authorities should ensure that the revised budget/ supplementary demand received subsidy is made available on BEAMS system at the earliest.
