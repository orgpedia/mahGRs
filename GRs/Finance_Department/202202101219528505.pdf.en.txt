The government decision. In view of the possibility of changes in the audit report of District Central Cooperative Banks every year, the list of these banks will be revised by the Finance Department in consultation with the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing every year. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant approval to 14 District Central Co-operative Banks having Audit Report A for the last 5 years as recommended by the Department of Co-operation, Textiles and Marketing in the present situation to carry out Government banking transactions and to invest additional funds from the Public Enterprises Corporation.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Feedback was taken from the Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing in respect of District Central Cooperative Banks which are financially sound and have professionalism as per rules and fulfill certain other criteria, considering the safety of Government funds. The Department of Cooperation, Textiles and Marketing in its audit report for the last 5 years has recommended the following 14 District Central Banks with Category A status.
Thane District Central Co-operative Bank 2. Raigad District Central Co-operative Bank 3. Ratnagiri District Central Co-operative Bank 4. Sindhudurg District Central Co-operative Bank 5. Ahmednagar District Central Co-operative Bank 6. Pune District Central Co-operative Bank 7. Satara District Central Cooperative Bank
