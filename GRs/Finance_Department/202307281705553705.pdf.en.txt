circular
The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2023-2024 was presented to the Legislature on 17th July, 2023. This bill has already been approved by the Lok Sabha. The Supplementary Appropriation Bill, to be included in the appropriations for the financial year ending 31st March, 2024, was introduced by Hon.
The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published as the Maharashtra (Second Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2023 (Maharashtra Act No. 31 of 2023) in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV, dated 27th July, 2023.
All the Ministries/ Departments are being informed that before allocating the amount of Supplementary Demands approved under the Supplementary Appropriation Act, they should obtain the approval of the Department of Planning, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Department of Tribal Development and Department of Finance, as the case may be, in respect of the Supplementary Demands for Expenditure under the Programme and the Department of Finance in respect of the Supplementary Demands for Compulsory Expenditure. Proposals for such approval should be submitted on the basis of the original of the supplementary demands. If the original provision is not valid, the proposal should not be sent to the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department unless there are certain reasons for disbursing the supplementary demand.
