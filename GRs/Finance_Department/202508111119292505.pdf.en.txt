State Government employees drawing salary in an undefined pay structure (as per 6th Pay Commission) and others
The question of revising the allowable dearness allowance rate for eligible full-time employees was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is ordering that the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on basic pay in the non-defined pay structure as per the 6th Pay Commission be reduced from 246% to 252% with effect from 1st January, 2025.
This Dearness Allowance increase is inclusive of the arrears for the period 1st January, 2025 to 31st July, 2025.
