Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for filling up of vacancies in the State Government under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published vide notification dated 04/01/2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has been instructed to ensure that the posts in the state government service for the disabled, although the designations of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, are different in respect of the posts in Group' A' to Group' D' posts which are in the state government establishment. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
2. Central Government Reference No. 3 mentioned here. The posts are ensured for the disabled through the notification dated 04/01/2021. As decided for the posts in Group-A to Group-D cadre in that list, all the posts in all the cadres in Group-A to Group-D in the Goods and Services Tax Department under the Finance Department are being ensured as shown in the return.
