Preamble: Decision of the Government. As per the approval given by the Committee on Financial Powers dated 19.10.2020, the Finance Department as the administrative department concerned and the officers of the Directorate of Insurance have been conferred financial powers subject to the prescribed financial limits as indicated in the Annexure annexed to the said decision and subject to the prescribed terms and conditions therein. Department of Finance, Govt. As on 01.03.2019, the Regional Office of the Directorate of Insurance, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has been established at Nagpur. However, the government decided to. As the Director of Insurance is vested with full powers to provide for payment of Goods and Services Tax and fees levied from time to time on various matters by the Central Government in relation to the conduct of insurance business under Schedule 9 to the Annexure attached to 19.10.2020, administrative approval is required to be obtained from the Mumbai Headquarter for payment of Goods and Services Tax of the Nagpur Regional Office. Therefore, it was difficult and cumbersome to deliver goods and services to the Nagpur Regional Office within the stipulated time. If the goods and services tax is not paid within the stipulated time, it is mandatory to pay additional penalty and interest on it. Therefore, the government has decided to ensure timely delivery of goods and services. As on 19.10.2020, the financial powers regarding payment of Goods and Services Tax were also being delegated to the Head of Regional Office, Nagpur, Directorate General of Insurance.
Government's decision:
As per the approval given by the Financial Powers Committee, the Government is granting financial powers to the Finance Department as the concerned administrative department and to the officers of the Directorate of Insurance as well as to the Head of the Nagpur Regional Office subject to the prescribed financial limits as indicated in the Annexure No. 9 annexed to this decision and subject to the prescribed terms and conditions therein.
