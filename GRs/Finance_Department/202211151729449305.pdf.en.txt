Application: Original No. 858/2009 by the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal. As per the directions given in the Judgment dated 22/02/2019, the Assistant Director of the Cadre, Dy. From 01/01/1997 to 01/01/2007, the seniority has been revised as per the zonal examination criteria. The original application filed in respect of revision of seniority of Deputy Director Category No. 155/2009 by the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal. In the judgment dated 25/03/2019, the original application No. 858/2009 has directed to revise the seniority of the Assistant Director Cadre in accordance with the same guidelines as the seniority of the Deputy Director Cadre.
As noted above, the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal in original Application No. 858/2009. As per the Judgment dated 22/02/2019, all seniority of Assistant Director Cadre since 1997 has been revised. Accordingly, it is proposed to revise the seniority of the Deputy Director cadre. Earlier, the promotions to the cadre of Deputy Director from 1997 to 2015 were made taking into account the disproportionate seniority of the Assistant Director cadre. However, since the seniority of the Assistant Director cadre has been revised since 1997, it has become necessary to revise the list of candidates who have been earlier selected for promotion to the Deputy Director cadre.
While including the officers in the seniority list in case of non-existent post of Assistant Director, it was not checked whether they have passed the departmental examination/ obtained exemption within the stipulated time. Therefore, it was observed that the seniority of the officers who have not passed the departmental examination within the prescribed time from the regular date of their appointment as Assistant Director has not been reduced by fixing the seniority of such officers on the next date of exemption from passing the examination. Therefore, it was observed that officers who did not qualify for seniority in the selection list of promotion prepared earlier on the basis of the abnormal seniority list of the Assistant Director cadre were also promoted to the post of Deputy Director.
Therefore, while preparing the revised selection list, it has been examined whether the officers of the cadre of Assistant Director in the field of consideration have passed/ obtained exemption from the examination prescribed as a pre-requisite in the Service Admission Rules, 1965 as per the principle of seniority-cum-merit as per the revised seniority list.

Accordingly, the matter of regularization of promotions in the cadre of Deputy Director is under the consideration of the Government.

The officers in the cadre of Assistant Director who have been found eligible to be included in the revised selection list as per the principle of seniority-cum-merit as per the revised seniority list and as per the criteria of examination prescribed as a pre-requisite in the Entry into Service Rules, 1965, shall be eligible to be included in the revised selection list in front of their names in column No. The date mentioned in clause 7 is being given as the date of his regular promotion to the cadre of Deputy Director.
