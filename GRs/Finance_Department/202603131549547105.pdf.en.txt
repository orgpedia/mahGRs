At the end of the financial year, in the Budget Session in March every year, by supplementary demand and revised
Funds are made available to various departments through the budget. The funds that have been made available
At the end of 31st March at the Subordinate & Accounts Office, Mumbai as well as at the District Treasury / Sub Treasury Offices.
