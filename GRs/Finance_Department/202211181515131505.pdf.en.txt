The Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 have approved the revised pay structure for the state government employees and other eligible employees from January 1, 2016, in line with the 7th Pay Commission. However, the question of revision in the rate of Dearness Allowance for the State Government employees and all other eligible full-time employees who are entitled to Dearness Allowance is still being paid in the pay scale approved under the 5th Pay Commission.
The Government now directs that the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on the basic pay of the State Government employees and all other eligible full-time employees who are entitled to Dearness Allowance, who are still drawing salary in the pay scale approved under the 5th Pay Commission, be revised as follows:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No. | Date of increase in dearness allowance | Permissible rate of inflation allowance |
| 1      | As of July 1, 2018                     | 284% (274% to 284%)                     |
| 2      | from January 1, 2019.                  | 295% (284% to 295%)                     |
| 3      | As of July 1, 2019                     | 312% (from 295% to 312%)                |

4. The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned under the head of accounts under which the salaries and allowances of the concerned government employees are spent. In the case of aided institutions and ZP employees, the expenditure should be covered under the sub-heading of the head of account under which the expenditure on their ancillary subsidy is covered.
