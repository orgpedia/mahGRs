3.7) If a decision is taken to recover the sale of movable/ immovable property of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation from Dhanco, a committee should be formed for this purpose and one representative each from Public Works Department, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation and Finance Department should be included in that committee. Also, the finalization of the auction process along with the report of the government representatives in the said committee will need to be approved by the government.
