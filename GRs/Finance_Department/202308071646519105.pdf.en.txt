As per the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries and Local Funds Audit has been mandated to ensure reservation of posts for persons with disabilities in Government services for the posts of Group-A, B, C and D cadres in its offices and subordinate offices.
