circular
The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2021-2022 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 22 nd December, 2021. This bill has already been approved by the Lok Sabha. The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been amended to include the appropriations for the financial year ending March 31, 2022. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published as the Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2021 (Maharashtra Act No. 17 of 2021) in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV, dated 30th December, 2021.
All the Ministries/ Departments are being informed that they may seek approval of the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department, as the case may be, regarding the Supplementary Demand for Expenditure under the programme and the Finance Department regarding the Supplementary Demand for Compulsory Expenditure, before allocating the amounts of Supplementary Demands sanctioned under the Supplementary Appropriation Act. Proposals for such approval should be submitted on the basis of the original of the supplementary demands.
