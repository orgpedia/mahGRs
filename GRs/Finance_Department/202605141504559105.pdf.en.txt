The Proposal: -
Under the Central Government's "Special Assistance to States for Capital Investments 2026-27" scheme, financial assistance will be provided to the state governments in the form of 50-year interest-free loans for capital investment projects. In this regard, the Department of Expenditure, Government of India has issued guidelines for the scheme vide letter dated 27.03.2026 in reference (1) above. The funds provided to the states under the scheme are to be used for capital projects for the long-term benefit of the state. The project proposals submitted by the state government will be scrutinized by the central government, after which the sanctioned projects will receive loan funds.
All funds released to the States under the Special Assistance for Capital Investments Scheme (55891) in the year 2026-27 as per the guidelines dated 27.03.2026 of the Central Government are to be provided through SNA.
As per the instructions of the Central Government, a single SNA (Single Nodal Agency) is to be appointed for all the areas. The SNA is supposed to open an account with a scheduled commercial bank.
