Finance Department Reference No. In case of death of an employee in service before completion of 10 years of service, defined contribution pension scheme/ National Pension Scheme applicable as per the Government decision of 01.01.2020, an amount of Rs. 10 lakh ex-gratia is given to the accumulated amount deposited in the accounts of more employees. The head of account shown for expenditure in this Government decision is only for Government employees. However, it has been noticed by the government that this head of account is also being used to provide benefits to the heirs of deceased employees of aided institutions.
(2) The following head of accounts referred to in the aforesaid Government Decision shall be used only to recover the ex-gratia amount payable to the next of kin of State Government employees working under the National Pension Scheme, who die while in service before completion of 10 years of service.
Head of Account- Demand No. G-6, 2071- Pension and other retirement benefits 01 Civil 103 Compassionate Allowances (00) (06)- Defined Contributions/ Ex-gratia under National Pension Scheme (20710722)
The District Councils, as well as recognized aided schools, non-agricultural universities and affiliated aided non-government colleges, agricultural universities and all recognized aided institutions shall be required to provide separate head of accounts for providing grants-in-aid in accordance with the decision of the Government in the above context.
