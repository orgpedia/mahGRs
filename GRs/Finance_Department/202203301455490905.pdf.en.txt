GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of Government Decision, dated 7th October, 2021, it was decided to revise the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible to State Government and other eligible employees drawing salary in the unscheduled (as per 6th Pay Commission) pay scale from 164% to 189% with effect from 1st July, 2021 and to pay the increased amount of Dearness Allowance in cash from 1st October, 2021. It was also decided to issue separate orders regarding the arrears admissible from July 1, 2021 to September 30, 2021.
The Government is now ordering that State Government and other eligible employees drawing salary in the unscheduled (as per 6th Pay Commission) pay structure should be paid arrears of 25% Dearness Allowance hike admissible for a period of 3 months from 1st July, 2021 to 30th September, 2021 in cash along with the salary for the month of March, 2022.
The existing provisions and procedures relating to the payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply as it is.
