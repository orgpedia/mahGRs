3. The scope of the study group shall be as follows: -
I. Business process re-engineering to simplify and streamline the process with a view to providing notified services more easily to the citizens.
II. Fixation of time limit for preservation of records in respect of notified services.
Duration of submission of report: The aforesaid Study Group shall submit its report to the Government within three months.
The study group may hold meetings as needed.
6. This Government Circular shall be issued by the Government of Maharashtra.
