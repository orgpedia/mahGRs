The Proposal:
While presenting the budget for 2021-2022, the government was considering to give administrative approval for the purchase of cabin size hard top, 4 wheeler, luggage trolley bags for the distribution of bags as per the prevailing practice.
Government's decision:
After the presentation of the budget for the year 2021-2022 in the Legislative Assembly, 842 cabin size hard top, 4 wheelers, luggage trolley bags will be purchased through tender process for the distribution of budget publications to the members of the legislature and others along with the bags from the pen drive. To present the budget speech of the Deputy Chief Minister (Finance) and Minister of State (Finance), 2 small leather bags (LD filecase Flap DLX) will be purchased through tender and the total estimated cost of the procurement tender will be Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 58.82 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Eight Lakhs Eighty Two Thousand only).
Purchase of this cabin size bag is subject to reference No. 1 In accordance with the provisions of the" Revised Manual of Procedure for Purchase of Offices by Government Departments" contained herein along with the Government Order dated 01.12.2016, the Government has decided to go ahead with the process of e-tendering and issue of tenders vide No. 2 The administrative department of the Ministry constituted under the decision of the Government here (itself) will be done through the Purchase Committee.
