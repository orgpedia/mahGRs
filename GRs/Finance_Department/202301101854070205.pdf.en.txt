Government's decision
The question of revision in the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible to State Government employees and other eligible full-time employees drawing salary as per the 5th Pay Commission was under consideration of the Government.
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st July, 2022, the rate of Dearness Allowance permissible on the basic pay in the pay scale approved as per the 5th Pay Commission be increased from 381% to 396%. The Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash with arrears for the period from 1st July, 2022 to 31st December, 2022 along with the salary for the month of January, 2023.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
4 Expenditure on the salaries and allowances of the Government servants concerned under the head.
Expenditures are to be covered under the head of expenditure and to be met from the grants sanctioned under it. In the case of aided institutions and ZP employees, the expenditure should be covered under the sub-heading of the head of account under which the expenditure on their ancillary subsidy is covered.
