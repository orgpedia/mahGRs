In the Budget Session 2024 of the Legislature, the Programme Budgets for 2024-25 are to be made available to the Members of the Legislature on the same day or on the next day after the Budget is presented to the Legislature.
Since 1989-90, the programme budgets are printed only in Marathi. The brochures/ charts are printed in both English and Marathi languages. 50 additional copies of these brochures/ charts are printed and bound with the English version printed by the Ministries/ Departments.
Considering the fact that the under-plan and non-plan expenditure have been consolidated since 2017-2018 and now divided into programme and mandatory expenditure respectively, care should be taken to prepare the programme budgets for 2024-25.

The Ministerial Departments are required to print the Programme Budgets for the year 2024-25 in the Government Press designated for printing the Programme Budgets. Regarding the printing arrangement of the programme budget, the Directorate of Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai vide letter No. (b) By 1941, all the Ministries were notified. There has been no change in it. If the departments have any problem regarding this printing system, they should print the program budgets for 2024-25 in consultation with the Director, Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai.

For contacting the concerned officers of the department regarding the printing of the program budget, information about the names of the officers, their office addresses, telephone numbers should be sent to the printing press assigned to the departments and a copy of it should be sent to the Director, Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai and the Finance Department for information. Similarly, those who send the manuscripts/ printouts of the programme budget to the printing press should inform the Director, Government Printing and Stationery, Mumbai and the Finance Department about it.
The schedule for preparation of the programme budget for 2024-25 and its presentation to the Legislature is attached. However, all the Ministries/ Departments should complete the works indicated in this schedule in time and send the manuscripts to the designated Government Press in time and prepare for the publication of the programme budget of their department. In doing so, you should prepare your manuscripts keeping in mind the instructions in the above circular and follow the following schedule carefully. Primarily, the Estimates Committee should consider the recommendations made from time to time on the programme budget and re-examine their publications before sending them for final printing.
