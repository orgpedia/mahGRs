No: 1/1/2023-C. || (b), dated April 03, 2023
Government Decision- The question of revising the rate of dearness allowance admissible to state government employees and other eligible full-time employees was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is ordering that the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on basic pay in the revised pay structure as per the 7th Pay Commission be increased from 38% to 42% with effect from 1st January, 2023. The Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash with arrears for the period from 1st January, 2023 to 31st May, 2023 along with the salary for the month of June, 2023.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
4. The expenditure thereon shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the head of accounts under which the salaries and allowances of the Government servants concerned are spent. In the case of aided institutions and ZP employees, the expenditure should be covered under the sub-heading of the head of account under which the expenditure on their ancillary subsidy is covered.
