the Office Order
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has been enacted in the State. Section 9 (2) of the Act provides that the head of each office or department shall prescribe three levels of officers, as may be, to be submitted to him for taking a final decision on all matters relating to that office.
Accordingly, levels of performance are being fixed for taking decisions in respect of subjects related to Taxation-01, Taxation-02 and Taxation-03 functions as indicated in the accompanying statement.
However, if there is a direction from a senior officer or if there is a need in some exceptional cases, appropriate changes will be made on a case to case basis. Also in case of policy matters, there will be more than three levels as mentioned in the circular dated August 11, 2014.
