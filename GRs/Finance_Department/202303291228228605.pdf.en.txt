INTRODUCTION: The National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has accorded sanction to the Government of Maharashtra under Rural Infrastructure Development Fund Phase XXVIII (RIDF-XXVIII) for completion of 2 irrigation projects of Water Resources Department as mentioned in Annexure-I attached to the Reference Letter. 664.0758 crore (Rupees Six hundred sixty four crore seven lakh fifty eight thousand only). The format of the Loan Scheme is as under: (9) The Loan is for two irrigation projects as mentioned in Annexure-I, namely, Talodhi Mokasa Project dated 31/03/2026 and Wakurde Project D. Available for completion by 31/03/2027.
(2) The interest on the loan shall be payable at the rate of 5.25 per cent after every three months of the calendar year until further directions from NABARD.
(3) The loan amount payable on the basis of actual expenditure incurred after 01/04/2022 on the projects for which this loan has been sanctioned shall be sanctioned by NABARD. The amount of loan (excluding last instalment) to be withdrawn at each time will be in multiples of Rs.1 lakh and each demand will be Rs. Not less than 10 lakhs.
(4) The amount of the loan taken out at each time shall be deemed to be a separate loan for repayment.
The loan is to be repaid in five equal annual installments within seven years. This seven-year period includes an initial grace period of two years.
(4) The total financial provision for the completion of the project as per the sanction order of NABARD shall be made by the Government in the first instance. For this, the Water Resources Department should ensure that the necessary budgetary provisions are made in the prescribed time and in the prescribed manner. After the actual expenditure, the loan amount from NABARD will be taken as reimbursement.

The Government have taken the following decisions in this regard:

(2) The terms and conditions prescribed by NABARD in its letter dated 14/03/2023 have been accepted by the Government and the suggestions made by NABARD have been taken on record by the Government. The Finance Department will act as the coordinating department for this loan scheme.
(3) The Government shall pay to NABARD interest at the rate of 5.25 per cent per annum on the existing loan.
The Government will pay the interest to NABARD as per the directions received from NABARD from time to time. The interest rate will be paid on the total loan balance for the quarter period. Interest will be paid from the date of maturity at the rate of interest applicable at that time.
(4) On projects for which loans have been sanctioned. Demand Forms/ Claims in the form of reimbursement of expenditure incurred so far from 01/04/2022 onwards will be submitted to NABARD.
(4) NABARD has relaxed the grace period to be considered as the initial two years from the date of withdrawal of the loan. NABARD has allowed the government to not repay the loan during this period. The government has decided to provide this facility. Therefore, the amount withdrawn at each time will be a separate loan and the loan will be repaid in equal installments over five years after the grace period of two years.
(6) For coordination of the above loan from NABARD, Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary, Water Resources Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being appointed as Coordinating Officer at the administrative department level and Chief Engineer, Water Resources Department, Irrigation Service Building, Civil Lines, Nagpur is being appointed as Coordinating Officer at the regional level. The concerned Coordinating Officer will be responsible for the implementation of the programme at the administrative department level and at the regional level.
(7) Out of the 2 irrigation projects mentioned in Annexure-I attached to this Government decision, the works of Talodhi Mokasa Project were completed before 31/03/2026 and the works of Wakurde Project were completed before 31/03/2026. Completion certificate of work completed before 31/03/2027 is required to be submitted by the Water Resources Department to NABARD by 30/04/2026 and 30/04/2027 respectively.
