Government's decision:
The National Pension System (NPS) (formerly Defined Contribution Pension Scheme (DCPS)) has been introduced for the employees who are appointed in the service of the State Government on or after 1st November 2005. The government was considering setting up a committee to make a comparative study of the National Pension System (NPS) and the old pension scheme. Accordingly, the following committee was formed.
