Government Decision- The question of revising the rate of dearness allowance of State Government employees and other eligible full-time employees drawing salary under the unscheduled pay structure (as per the 6th Pay Commission) was under the consideration of the Government.
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st July, 2021, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on the basic pay in the unscheduled pay structure as per the 6th Pay Commission be increased from 164% to 189%. This includes the DA hike from January 1, 2020, July 1, 2020 and January 1, 2021. However, the rate of Dearness Allowance will remain at 164% for the period from 1st January, 2020 to 30th June, 2021. The increase in Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash from October 1, 2021.
Separate orders will be issued regarding arrears of dearness allowance for the period of 3 months from 1st July, 2021 to 30th September, 2021.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
