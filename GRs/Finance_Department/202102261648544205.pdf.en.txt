The proposal: The Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 and others. The General Administration Department has decided to implement the decision given on 06.07.2017 in the state. The Government has issued a decision dated 21.12.2019. An independent Study Group shall be constituted to study in detail the service/ retirement benefits to be admissible to the officers/ employees concerned after appointment to the post of Superintendents and make recommendations to the Government. This has been done in accordance with the Government Decision dated 15.06.2020. Action should be taken as per the orders issued by the government only after the recommendation of this study group. Meanwhile, many offices are asking the officers/ employees who have been transferred to the post of Service Superintendents, what action should be taken regarding the transfer of the State Government Employees Group Personal Accident Scheme and providing benefits accordingly. Following are the recommendations:
Circular: All the administrative departments are being informed that the Government Decision, General Administration Department, Dept. Subscription to the State Government Employees Group Personal Accident Insurance Scheme should be recovered immediately from the officers/ employees who have been transferred to the post of service head as per 21.12.2019.
All other provisions of the Government decision regarding the Accident Insurance Scheme to the said officers/ employees will continue to be applicable as regular employees.
