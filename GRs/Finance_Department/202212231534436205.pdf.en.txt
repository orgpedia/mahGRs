circular
The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2022-2023 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 19.12.2022. This bill has already been approved by the Lok Sabha. The Supplementary Appropriation Bill, to be included in the appropriations for the financial year ending 31st March, 2023, is proposed to be introduced in the House by Hon. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary, Part IV, dated 23rd December, 2022 as the Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2022 (Maharashtra Act No. 49 of 2022).
2. All the Ministries/ Departments are being informed that before allocating the amount of Supplementary Demands approved under the Supplementary Appropriation Act, they should seek the approval of the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department, as the case may be, in respect of the Supplementary Demands for Expenditure under the Programme and the Finance Department in respect of the Supplementary Demands for Compulsory Expenditure. If the original provision is not valid, the proposal should not be sent to the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department unless there are certain reasons for disbursing the supplementary demand.
