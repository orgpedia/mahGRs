The Proposal:
The Government of Maharashtra has received a request for waiver/ reduction of stamp duty of 0.1% levied on gold and silver imported into the State of Maharashtra.
In this regard, the Government was considering to constitute a committee to comprehensively study the possible impact on the State revenue if the stamp duty levied as per Article-29 of Schedule-1 of Maharashtra Stamp Act is waived/ reduced on the delivery order documents of gold and silver imported in the State of Maharashtra and make recommendations to the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to conduct a comprehensive study on the possible impact on the revenue of the State if the stamp duty levied as per Section-29 of Schedule-1 of the Maharashtra Stamp Act is waived/ reduced on the delivery order documents of gold and silver imported into the State of Maharashtra and make recommendations to the Government. The committee will be headed by Additional Chief Secretary (Finance).
