circular
The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2021-2022 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 22nd December, 2021 and the Supplementary Statement of Expenditure contains Supplementary Demands for Contingency Fund Compensation. A statement showing the advance amount taken from the Contingency Fund is being sent along with this. Since the Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2021 has now been passed, the concerned Ministries/ Departments are requested to ensure that the expenditure on the items mentioned in the statement accompanying this circular is not credited to the Contingency Fund accounts and the same is reported to the concerned Controlling Authorities.
2. Supplementary appropriations in respect of advances sanctioned from the Contingency Fund have been approved by the Legislature and incorporated in the" Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2021" (Maharashtra Act No. 17 of 2021, dated 30.12.2021).
The Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts)-I, Maharashtra, Mumbai is requested to reimburse the amount of expenditure incurred from the amount mentioned in column-4 of the return accompanying this circular to the Contingency Fund.
