Government's decision
The Government was considering revision of Dearness Allowance rates for State Government employees and other eligible full-time employees drawing salary in the unscheduled pay structure (as per the 6th Pay Commission).
The Government is ordering that with effect from 1st July, 2021, the rate of Dearness Allowance admissible on the basic pay in the unscheduled pay structure as per the 6th Pay Commission be increased from 189% to 196%. This Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash along with the salary for the month of March, 2022, with arrears from the date of increase, 1st July, 2021.
The existing provisions and procedure for payment of Dearness Allowance shall continue to apply.
