Read- 1) Government Circular No: Finance Department, Government Circular No. Supplementary-2023/ PR No. 94/ Finance-3, D. 19.12.2023
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2023-24 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 07.12.2023. The bill has already been approved by the Assembly.
The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been approved by the Cabinet for inclusion in the Appropriation Bill for the financial year ending March 31, 2024. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published as the Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2023 (Maharashtra Act No. 47 of 2023) in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV dated 18th December, 2023.
2. Supplementary demand as shown in the accompanying statement under the Supplementary Appropriation Act aggregating to Rs. 1176,96,42, 000/- (Rupees One thousand one hundred and seventy six crore nine hundred and forty two thousand only) (under the programme) (mandatory) (Datta Mat). After the approval of the Department of Planning and Finance, the financial sanction orders for the related schemes regarding the expenditure of Supplementary Demand will be handed over to the concerned Implementing/ Controlling Officer in column 5 of the statement accompanying the provision, after being separately issued by the concerned Plan Implementation Offices.
3. Proposals for disbursement of funds for Supplementary Demands approved under the programme (on original lease) should be approved by the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department, as the case may be. If the original provision is not valid, the proposal should not be sent to the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department and Finance Department, unless there are certain reasons for distributing the supplementary demand.
