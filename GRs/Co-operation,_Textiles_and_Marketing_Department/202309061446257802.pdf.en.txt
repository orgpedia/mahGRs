Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision at Annexure 2 above, a scheme for providing capital subsidy to self-financing textile projects has been introduced under the new Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra. Under this scheme, the government was considering to disburse capital subsidy to the textile units in the" Appendix" accompanying this government decision.
Government Decision: Under the Textile Policy of the State, capital subsidy to textile units for self-financing textile projects will be provided under this scheme. The projects approved in the meeting of the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Textiles), as per the Statement in the Annexure accompanying this Government Decision for the year 2023-24, have a total outlay of Rs. An amount of Rs. 8,08,02, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Eight Lakhs Two Thousand only) is sanctioned and sanctioned for disbursement. In the case of projects which have been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team in the group under the decision of the government here in reference number 6 and in which irregularities have been found, the grant will be disbursed only after the final decision or further action will be taken according to the decision. Also, the projects which have not yet been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team will be inspected soon in the near future and if any irregularity is found in the inspection of that project, the subsidy sanctioned through this order will be recovered with interest.
