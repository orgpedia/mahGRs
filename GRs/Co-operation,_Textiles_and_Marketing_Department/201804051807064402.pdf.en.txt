Preamble: Shri Shekhar Charegaonkar, President, Maharashtra State Co-operative Council. The proposal for appointment was under consideration for the next three years with effect from 21.2.18. The following decision has been taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In exercise of the powers conferred by the provisions of section 154 (a) of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Council is being reconstituted by this Order. The term of the Council will be for a period of three years from 21.02.2018 or until further orders, whichever is less.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of section 154 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, the Government hereby appoint the Maharashtra State Co-operative Council (MSCSC) as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Company. Council) Shri Shekhar Charegaonkar is being appointed as Chairman with effect from 21.02.2018.
The functions of the Maharashtra State Co-operative Council shall be governed by the provisions of Section 154 (a) (3) of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 as follows:
1) To advise the State Government on all such matters relating to the cooperative movement.
(ii) To review the cooperative movement and suggest ways to coordinate the functioning of cooperative societies in the State.
3) Suggest ways and means to overcome the difficulties faced by the cooperative societies.
(4) To report to the State Government on all matters which the State Government may refer to the Council.
5) To recommend schemes and policies for the development of the cooperative movement in the state.
To evaluate the existing projects and suggest new projects for the development of the cooperative movement, especially for the development of backward classes and economically weaker sections of the society.
