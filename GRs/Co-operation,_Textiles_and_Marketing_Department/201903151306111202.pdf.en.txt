Under this policy, the scheme for creation of infrastructure and strengthening of existing facilities for the growth of sericulture and sericulture has been implemented as per Government Decision, dated 3rd May, 2018. Sasmira, a Centrally Sponsored Society, has proposed to set up a Gas Chromatography Mass Spectroscopy facility in collaboration with the State Government for fabric quality certification for determining the quality of agro-based textile/ agrotextile products. Submitted on January 31, 2018. Accordingly, a sum of Rs.70 lakh has been sanctioned and released to Sasmira Institute for studying Agrotextile products in Maharashtra under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, Government Resolution, No.Purukar 2018/ R.No.72/ R.C.C., dated 14th November, 2018. The government was planning to allocate funds for the project.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: SASMIRA has disbursed a sum of Rs.70,00,000/- (Rupees Seventy Lakh only) to SASMIRA for setting up a Gas Chromatography Mass Spectroscopy Testing Facility through the Director (Silk), Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, Government Resolution No. The remaining amount of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) required for the project is being sanctioned to Sasmira through this Order.
