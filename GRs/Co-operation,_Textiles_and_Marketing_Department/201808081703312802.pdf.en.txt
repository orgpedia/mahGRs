the Office Orders
In view of the transfer of certain officers of the Department of Co-operative Marketing and Textiles to other departments of the Ministry and consequent change in the charge/ designation of the officers of the Department, the Office Orders dated May, 2015 are being superseded and revised as per the said changes and the requirement of the Central Government to declare Assistant Public Information Officers, State Public Information Officers and First Appellate Authorities under Section 5 (1) and Section 5 (2) of the Right to Information Act, 2015.
The designations of Assistant Public Information Officer, State Public Information Officer and First Appellate Authority in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the Right to Information Act, 2005 of the Central Government are being declared in the attached Form' A' for effective realization of the right to information to the public.
If there is more than one Assistant Public Information Officer in an office, the Assistant Public Information Officer to whom the information is to be given should immediately provide the necessary information to the State Assistant Public Information Officer. If the applicant is not provided with the correct information within the time limit and as a result, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned Assistant Public Information Officer and the concerned State Assistant Public Information Officer. The State Assistant Public Information Officers, State Public Information Officers and First Appellate Authority of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (Private) shall strictly comply with the orders/ circulars, etc. issued by the Central Government from time to time in relation to the Right to Information Act.
