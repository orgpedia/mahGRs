Introduction: Read here. Kapus producing taluks in the State have been declared as per the Government decision dated 28th August, 2018. After that. As per the Government notification dated 30th August, 2018, some more taluks have been added to the list of Kapus producing taluks, taking the total number of Kapus producing taluks in the State to 118.
Now, as the cotton production of Chimur Taluka of Chandrapur district in the last 3 years is more than 9600 tons, the matter of including Chimur Taluka in the list of Kapus Producing Taluka was under the consideration of the government, according to which the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decision: Chimur Taluka of Chandrapur district has been included in the list of Kapus Producing Taluka as the average cotton production of the taluka in the last 3 years is more than 9600 tons. In the table titled Kapus Producing Taluks, in the Government Resolution dated 28th August, 2018:- In the list of Kapus Producing Taluks of Chandrapur District at Table 18, Chimur Taluka is being included. With this, the total number of Kapus producing taluks in the State has gone up to 119.
