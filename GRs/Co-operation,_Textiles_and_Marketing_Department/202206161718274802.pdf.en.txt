The said promotions and postings of the above promoted officers are being made only on temporary basis subject to the following:
This promotion. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 is being made subject to final decision.
(i) The promotion is purely temporary in nature and does not entitle the candidate to any regularity and eminence in service.
As per the provisions of clause (d) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, officers to be appointed by promotion shall join the post of promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of issue of the said promotion order.
The attention of the promoted officers is being drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said Rules, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any application for change in the post.
5. Promoted officers to give salary fixation option within one month from the date of joining the post of promotion. In case such option is not given within the prescribed time, their salary will be determined as per Rule No. 1 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
Before such officer joins the post of promotion, a written bond from the concerned officers that he will not be entitled to promotion to the post given to him only on a temporary basis should be taken by the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune and then the order of retirement should be issued.
The Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune shall ensure that the said orders shall come into force with immediate effect and the said officers shall join the place of posting within the prescribed time. General Administration Department. The authorities concerned should also ensure that the statement of interest and liability is submitted as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 2/6/2014.
