The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2021 (including the amounts specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2021) has been approved by the Legislature and assented to by the Governor of Maharashtra and is published in the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV dated 15th March, 2021 as Maharashtra Act No. 5 of 2021. The circular dated 06.04.2021 has been notified.
While disbursing the funds for mandatory and programme expenditure under the Act to all the concerned administrative departments at the beginning of the year, the Department of Finance is considering to fix the expenditure limit after taking into account the trend of the previous year's expenditure and the revenue generated during the year in a phased manner and the unexpected/ urgent expenditure arising during the year. Before finalization of the matter, as a temporary arrangement for the April month, funds have been allocated on Budget Estimate Allocation and Control System in the range of 01- Salary, 04- Pension related expenditure and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salary)- 10%, 02- Wages, 10- Contractual Services, 06- Telephone, Electricity and Water charges, 13- Office expenses, 14- Lease and Taxes- 5%.
Also, if the funds are budgeted through a new item, the proposal for the distribution of funds will need to be approved by the Planning, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department, and the Finance Department. It shall be the responsibility of the Department to comply with the provisions of the Rules of Business of the State Government, the Financial Rules, the Budget Rule Book and all the terms and conditions before drawing and spending the funds available on the Budget Estimate Allocation and Control System.
