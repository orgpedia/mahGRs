Government's decision:
The 67 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative consumer institutions in the State were extended till 31st August, 2023 as per the Government decision, dated 21st February, 2023. The proposal for extension of these 67 temporary posts from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
