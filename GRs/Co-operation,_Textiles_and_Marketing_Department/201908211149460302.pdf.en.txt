The Proposal:
The Government has approved to provide Government shareholding in the ratio of 1:9 of the member shareholding keeping the ratio of loans and shareholding to 1:1 to finance the projects to be set up during the Eighth Five Year Plan period and submitted to the Government by the selected co-operative spinning mills for Government funding. In view of the progress made in the construction of the project in accordance with the project report submitted by these yarn mills to the government, and considering the share capital collected from the members so far, the Chopra Taluka Farmers Cooperative Yarn Factory Limited. The proposal to provide government equity to the spinning mills of Taluka Chopra and District Jalgaon in the financial year 2019-2020 was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Chopra Taluka Farmers Co-operative Society Ltd. Sootgiri, Taluka Chopra, District Jalgaon have been included in the Eighth Five Year Plan and selected for government funding. The project cost is Rs. 6100.00 lakhs. According to the project report, Rs. 305.00 lakhs is required to be deposited. Accordingly, Rs. 305.00 Lakhs has been deposited as Members Share. Accordingly, Rs. 2745.00 lacs is the Government share capital permissible to the mill.
So far, Rs. 2624.67 lakhs has already been disbursed in 8 installments. Details are attached with the letter.
