Preamble: Under the State Small and Medium Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) Scheme for Assistance to Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (2425 2524), the interest subvention of 2.5 per cent is sanctioned by the State Government to the District Central Cooperative Banks for making available crop loans at 6 per cent interest rate to the farmers through the District Central Cooperative Banks. Under this scheme, in the financial year 2022-23, Rs. 100.00 crore is available. Of these, Rs. 30.00 crore in reference No. 5 dated 21.06.2022 has been issued. Commissioner of Cooperation, Pune vide Reference No. 6 vide letter dated 15.09.2022, it is proposed to disburse the balance amount for the said scheme. Accordingly, the proposal was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Out of the funds sanctioned under the Programme Expenditure Plan of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles for the financial year 2022-23, an amount of Rs. 30.00 crore (Rs. Thirty crore only) is being approved for disbursement under the State Small Finance Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) Scheme titled Assistance for Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (State Level) (Programme), (2425 2524) 33 Financing.

The Assistant Registrar (Estimates and Planning), Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune, under the Commissioner for Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune, is being authorized as Controlling Officer to spend the said provision (V0004). Also, the Accountant, Under Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune are being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers. The Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune should ensure that the funds are withdrawn and spent on time. A report on the expenditure should also be submitted to the government.
The expenditure should be made from the provision available in the financial year 2022-23 under the head Demand No.V-02, 2425-Sahakar (00) (107) Assistance to Cooperative Credit Societies (01) (16) Financial assistance for empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (2425 2524), 33 Financial assistance under Small Cooperative Credit Structure in the State.
The said Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the concurrence given by the Planning Department vide Non-official Reference No. 325/1431, dated 13.10.2022 to the Finance Department vide Non-official Reference No. 492/ Expenditure-2, dated 21.10.2022.
