Introduction: Restructuring of Maharashtra State Cooperative Council. This was decided by the Government on 5.4.2018. A proposal to include other members in the Maharashtra State Cooperative Council was also under consideration. The following decision has been taken:
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of section 154 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, the Government hereby reconstitute the Maharashtra State Co-operative Council. This was decided by the Government on 5.4.2018. The tenure of the Council is for a period of three years from 21.02.2018 or until further orders, whichever is less. In this cooperative council, Shri Dilip Gopalrao Rajurkar, the Government of Amravati, passed a resolution No. Narrow- 2018/ Q.No. 22/22-S, Dy. He was appointed on April 16, 2018. This appointment is being cancelled and Mr. Dilip Baburao Patange, Solapur is being appointed in his place. The computer code of the website is 201808041222594602.
