With a view to encourage the handloom weavers of the State and to promote the best varieties produced by the weavers, the Government of Maharashtra has been implementing the Handloom Textile Competition and Prize Scheme since 1972-73 for the best samples of handloom fabric produced by the handloom weavers of the State. The Directorate of Textiles has proposed to allocate funds from the budgetary provision for implementation of the reward scheme in 2022-23. as per letter dated 07.12.2022. Accordingly, the government had decided to allocate funds for the project.
