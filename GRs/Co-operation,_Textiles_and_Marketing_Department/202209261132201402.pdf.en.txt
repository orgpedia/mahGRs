Introduction: Refer Note No. Under Section 3, a total of 52 temporary posts including 39 temporary posts under Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under Commissioner Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune were approved to be continued till 31st August, 2022. Now, the Department of Finance has been empowered to extend the tenure of these posts from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023. There are 4 administrative departments. Accordingly, a total of 52 temporary posts, including 39 temporary posts under Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune, are under consideration of the Government for extension from September 01, 2022 to February 28, 2023.
Government's decision:
By this Government decision, a total of 52 temporary posts, 39 temporary posts under the Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune, have been filled up. The extension is being approved from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
