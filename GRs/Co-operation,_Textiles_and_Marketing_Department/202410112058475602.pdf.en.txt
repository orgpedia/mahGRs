The State Cooperative Development Committee (€5901) and the District Cooperative Development Committee (€91 ') have been constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary and at the district level under the chairmanship of the District Collector concerned respectively.
Under the central government's "Sahakar se Samriddhi" scheme, NABARD, NDDB, NBFCs and state governments have set a target of setting up and strengthening 9,218 new cooperative societies in the first phase (2024 to 2029). To meet this objective, the Central Government has directed the constitution of a sub-committee, the Joint Executive Committee (10/0) under the District Cooperative Development Committee.

Functions of the Committee: -
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To obtain information about the revenue villages in the district where Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies, Dairy Development Co-operative Societies, Fisheries Co-operative Societies do not exist.
2) To obtain information about the revenue villages in the district where Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies, Dairy Development Co-operative Societies, Fisheries Co-operative Societies are closed.
3) To prepare an action plan for registration of new Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies, Dairy Development Co-operative Societies, Fisheries Co-operative Societies in these revenue villages after obtaining the above information.
4) To place before the District Cooperative Development Committee (91) an action plan for the establishment of new Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies, Dairy Development Co-operative Societies, Fisheries Co-operative Societies.
5) Submit a proposal to the District Cooperative Development Committee for the formation of a district-level association in coordination with the relevant stakeholders as per the requirement and feasibility assessment, if it does not exist in the district.
6) To unite farmers to form new Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies, Dairy Development Co-operative Societies, Fisheries Co-operative Societies as per the action plan.
7) Formation of the Board of Directors and accumulation of necessary shareholding for registration of cooperatives and ensuring registration of cooperatives in coordination with the stakeholders as per the objectives and timelines defined in the Action Plan.
The aforesaid Joint Executive Committee will have a review meeting every month. Meetings will also be held as needed.
