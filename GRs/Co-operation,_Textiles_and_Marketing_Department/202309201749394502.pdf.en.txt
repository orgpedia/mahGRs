Introduction: Refer Note No. Under Section 3, a total of 52 temporary posts, including 39 temporary posts under Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune, were approved to be continued till August 31, 2023. Now, the Department of Finance is empowered to extend the tenure of these posts from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024. 4 and 5 are assigned to administrative departments. Accordingly, a total of 52 temporary posts, including 39 temporary posts under Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune, are under consideration of the Government for extension from September 01, 2023 to February 29, 2024.
Government's decision:
By this Government decision, a total of 52 temporary posts, including 39 temporary posts under the Commissioner (Textiles), Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune, have been created. 4& 5 are being approved for extension from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
