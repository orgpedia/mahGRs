Preamble: Under the Group Insurance Scheme of the Government, loans were sanctioned for the construction of houses for 42 members of the housing society Atharva Cooperative Housing Society Limited, Amravati.
After sanctioning the first instalment to 42 members of Atharva Cooperative Housing Society Limited, Amravati for construction of houses under the Group Insurance Scheme of the Government, the Society has mortgaged 14 plots of total area 2589.65 sq.mtrs to the Government at Mauje Amravati, including plot no. 7A, 7B, 8A, 8B, 9A, 9B in Survey No. 195/3 and plot no. 2A, 2B, 21B, 22 in Survey No. 195/6 A and plot no. 29A, 29B, 30A and 30 in Survey No. 195/6.
After completing the construction of houses for 42 members who were sanctioned loans, the institution has 442.78 sq.m. area without construction. The government has not sanctioned any loan for the construction of the building. Therefore, the Institute has requested the Government to allow the transfer/ sale of the said un-constructed area to others free from mortgage. Accordingly, the request for approval of the institution was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
A total of 442.78 sq.m. of unbuilt area of Plot No. 21B& 22 in Survey No. 195/6 A, owned by Atharva Co-operative Housing Society Ltd., Amaravati, which is mortgaged to the Government, is being excluded from the mortgage subject to the condition that the Government will not sanction a housing loan to the Society under the Group Insurance Scheme in future.
Further, permission is being granted to transfer/ sell the said unbuilt area to others.
