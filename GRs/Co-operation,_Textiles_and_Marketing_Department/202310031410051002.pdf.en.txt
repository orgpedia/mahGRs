Preamble: The Directorate of Silk has been established by the Government Resolution dated 27.08.1997 in the context of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour and the posts have been transferred from various establishments to the Directorate of Silk. A total of 379 posts in the Directorate of Silk have been sanctioned vide Government Resolution dated 22nd March, 2011 and 22nd March, 2012 respectively.
A total of 303 posts have been made permanent and 76 are temporary.
However, after reviewing the posts in the establishment of Silk Operations, the Government vide its decision dated 09.03.2022, as many as 311 regular posts and 54 outsourced services have been notified.

Such a revised format has been approved. Also, 12 posts of drivers and 56 posts of constables have been declared as dead. And it has been approved to count the maximum number of posts as many employees will be working in this dead cadre.
The continuation of temporary posts in the Directorate of Silk from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 has been approved by the Government decision dated 14.03.2023. Now, with reference to Director (Silk) vide letter dated 31.07.2023 and 21.09.2023, a proposal has been received for continuation of temporary posts in Directorate of Silk from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
The Department of Finance has been empowered to extend the term of the temporary posts included in the figure of the Department as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure, from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024. the Government Decision dated 31.08.2023 and its follow-up The corrigendum dated 06.09.2023 has been provided to the Administrative Departments.
Accordingly, the matter of extension of temporary posts on the establishment of the Directorate of Silk was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 63 temporary posts as indicated in the accompanying Form on Establishment of Directorate of Silk are hereby approved to be continued from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024. Further, as per the Government Decision, Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department, dated 09.03.2022, the cadres of Drivers and Constables have been declared dead. Therefore, the number of posts in this category should be taken into account. These posts will be automatically abolished after they fall vacant due to retirement/ resignation/ death of the employees working thereon.
The said expenditure shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the respective financial year under the next head of accounts.
