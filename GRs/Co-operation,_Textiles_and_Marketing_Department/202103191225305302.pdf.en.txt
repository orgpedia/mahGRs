The Proposal:
The Vita Merchant. Operational Bank Ltd. Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune has given approval for" Special one-time loan repayment scheme" to Vita (Sangli) Bank. The proposal has been submitted to the Government on 17.3.2021.
Bank loans must be repaid in order for the borrower to be able to repay the loan.
Approving a special one-time loan repayment scheme will help the bank reduce its mounting NPAs. Keeping this in mind, The Vita Merchant. Operational Bank Ltd. The matter of sanctioning" Special one-time loan repayment scheme" to Vita (Sangli) Bank was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Pune. The Vita Merchant Co. vide letter dated 16.3.2021. Operational Bank Ltd. The proposal to approve the special one-time loan repayment scheme submitted in respect of Vita (Sangli) Bank is being implemented by The Vita Merchant Co., Ltd., by way of exemption from the provisions of Rule-49 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961, as per the powers vested in the Government under the provisions of Section 157 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960.
Operational Bank Ltd. Vita (Sangli) Bank is being granted approval for Special one-time loan repayment scheme mentioned in Annexure A upto 30.6.2021.
The implementation of the above scheme is reviewed every month by the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune and the terms of reference of the Reserve Bank of India are communicated to The Vita Merchant Co. Ltd. Operational Bank Ltd.
It should be ensured that the Vita (Sangli) bank is strictly followed.
