Krishipat-4/ Crop Loan/ Coordination Committee/ 2022, dated 15.06.2022
Crop loans are provided to the farmers of the state through public sector banks, private sector banks, regional rural banks and district central cooperative banks. However, it has been observed that farmers are not getting loans from banks, especially public and private banks, as per the targets set by the State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC). Also, the loans should be disbursed to the farmers in the month of May/ June every financial year before the start of the Kharif season. However, the banks are not providing loans to the farmers on time. The people's representatives have expressed their resentment over this.
Accordingly, the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperatives, Pune, has proposed to form a Taluka Crop Loan Distribution Review Committee under the chairmanship of the local Member of Legislative Assembly at the taluka level to ensure the supply of crop loans to the farmers of the state within the stipulated time. submitted vide letter dated 15.06.2022. According to this. The government was considering the formation of a taluk-level crop loan distribution review committee under the chairmanship of the Member of Legislative Assembly (Local).
The government has decided to form a Taluka Crop Loan Review Committee under the chairmanship of the local MLA at the taluka level to ensure adequate and timely supply of crop loans to the farmers of the state.

The functions of the Committee are as follows:
(i) To review the disbursement of crop loans as per the targets set to the banks at the taluka level and the action taken to provide fresh loans to the farmers.
(ii) Simplification of the procedure for granting loans and concessions to farmers by banks. (e. g. online crop loan requisition facility for farmers, etc.) 3. The Committee shall hold monthly review meetings during Kharif season and may hold such meetings during Rabi season as may be required.
