Cancellation of appointment of Additional Registrar rank officer on deputation in Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles
Government Resolution No. Complex 1221/ Q.No. 153/2-C
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400032
Date of release: May 18, 2022
Reference- 1. Government Decision No. 23.12.2021 Letter No. 111/ Banking/ Portion Department/ 256/2021-22 of Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank, dated 31.12.2021
The Proposal:
Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank Civil Appeal No. 3445/2011 has been filed in the Supreme Court. In pursuance of the said petition, in pursuance of the Government decision dated 23.12.2021, Reference No. 1, dated 23.12.2021, regarding the appointment of an officer of the rank of Additional Registrar under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Maharashtra State, Pune, as a deputee in the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank, was approved.
In pursuance of the above petition, the State Government has sanctioned the Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank the amount of Rs. 270.00 crore out of which Rs. 100.00 crore was disbursed as equity. The said funds have been returned to the Government by Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank vide letter dated 31.12.2021. Therefore, as there is no need to appoint an officer of the rank of Additional Registrar in the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank, the matter of cancellation of the government decision dated 23.12.2021 was under the consideration of the government.
Maharashtra: The Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank Ltd. In pursuance of Civil Appeal No. 3445/2011 filed in the Supreme Court, the Government hereby approve to cancel the Government Order dated 23.012.2021 issued in respect of appointment of an officer of the rank of Additional Registrar under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Maharashtra State, Pune as a deputationist in Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank.
