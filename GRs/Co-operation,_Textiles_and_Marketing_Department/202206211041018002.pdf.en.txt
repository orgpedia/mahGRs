Preamble: Under the Scheme for Support to Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (2425 2524) under the Small and Marginal Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) in the State, the State Government approves 2.5 per cent interest subvention to the District Central Cooperative Banks for providing crop loans to farmers at 6 per cent interest rate through the District Central Cooperative Banks. Under this scheme, in the financial year 2022-23, Rs. 100.00 crore is available. Accordingly, the Commissioner of Cooperation, Pune vide reference No. 5, vide letter dated 26.04.2022, for the said scheme Rs. 30.00 crore has been proposed to be released. Accordingly, the proposal was under the consideration of the government.

The Government is approving the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 30 crore only under the State Small and Medium Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) Assistance for Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (State Level) (Programme), (2425 2524) 33 Financial Assistance under this head.
