The Proposal: -
Unseasonal rains and hailstorms have caused some loss of life and property in the state during February-March 2015. Agricultural land and crops have also been damaged to some extent. To provide assistance to the victims of untimely rains and hailstorms in the state during February and March 2015 at an affordable rate. The Cabinet of Ministers.
In pursuance of the decision taken on 9.4.2015, the Government has decided to provide assistance as a special case to the victims of loss of life and property due to rain and hailstorms during the months of February and March 2015. It was taken under 13.04.2015. Accordingly, the Office of the Accountant General has directed that the title of the account created to provide this assistance be revised.
2. The issue of revision of head of accounts was under consideration of the Government as per the directions of the Accountant General.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government is approving the creation of a new revenue expenditure ledger for the scheme of interest subvention on loans to those affected by untimely rains and hailstorms as follows:
