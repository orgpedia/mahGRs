GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. JIMS 0617/ PR No. 126/2-C
The following is being approved in respect of the appointment of Shri J. N. Patel as Authorized Officer and the expenditure of honorarium and other allowances in respect thereof: (i) The Authorized Officer shall be entitled to a remuneration of Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One Lakh only) per day for the conduct of the enquiry.
(ii) Airfare from Mumbai to Nagpur and Nagpur to Mumbai shall be admissible to the authorised officer.
(iii) Provision of conveyance shall be permissible to the authorised officer for the conduct of the enquiry.
(iv) Accommodation shall be permissible to the authorised officer. Accommodation should be arranged according to the convenience of the authorized officer in this regard.
v) The necessary stationery, computers with internet facility, printers, scanners, telephones and accessories should be arranged through the bank as per the demand of the authorized officer.
vi) At present, the Nagpur District Central Co-operative Bank shall make available the funds required for the conduct of the enquiry from time to time and after completion of the enquiry, the cost of enquiry under Section 88 (2) of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 shall be recovered and deposited in the bank against the expenditure incurred by the bank.
vii) Ms. Jyoti Vajani, Advocate, be appointed as an Assistant to assist the Authorized Officer in the enquiry. Separate orders will be issued regarding the terms/ conditions of their employment and remuneration.
