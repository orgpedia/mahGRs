Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 25/09/2020, the Government had approved the continuation of 118 temporary posts in the offices of the Commissionerate of Sugar and Regional Joint Director (Sugar) under it from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021. Now, the office of the Commissioner of Sugar has submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference Read 8 to approve the continuation of the said 118 posts with effect from 01/03/2021. Accordingly, at present, the proposal to continue these 118 temporary posts from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: It is hereby approved to continue 118 temporary posts as shown in Table A and B attached in the office of the Sugar Commissioner and 8 Regional Joint Director (Sugar) offices under him from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021. In the meantime, the offices of the Office of the Administrator (Aastha-1) and Office of the Administrator (5-C) shall, in any case, review the sanctioned posts on their establishment and on the establishment of the offices under their administrative control and obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised figure. It should be noted that after this, the period for determining the revised figure will not be extended. The Office Bearers (Aastha-I) and Office Bearers (5-C) should review the posts in the establishment of the Administrative Department (itself) and the establishment of the Regional Office under their control, plan to submit the proposal for the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately and take immediate action and take care to get the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the revised figure.
