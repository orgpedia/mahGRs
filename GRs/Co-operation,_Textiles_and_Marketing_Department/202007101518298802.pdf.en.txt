The Proposal:
Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 25 posts. The above reference No. 25 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies under Section 3 of the Act. The Government had approved the continuation till 29/02/2020. In view of the expiry of the said extension, 25 temporary posts created for the audit of the Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies for the period of 2020-21 for the implementation of the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme have been continued for the audit department to work as per the 97th constitutional amendment. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies. Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the proposal in Section 2, the following 25 temporary posts created for audit in connection with the implementation of the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme of the Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies will be filled up. from 01/03/2020. The extension is being granted by this Government decision till 31/08/2020.
