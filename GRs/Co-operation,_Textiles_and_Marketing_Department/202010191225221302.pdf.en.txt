The Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project will be implemented in all districts of the state for a period of six years (from 2020 to 2026). The Cabinet meeting was held on 29/07/2020. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to constitute a State Level Steering Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Maharashtra to approve the final design of the Magnet project, make necessary changes in the project plan at the time of implementation with the consent of Asian Development Bank, make policy changes in the project implementation, review the project, approve the budget and administrative matters and make changes as per the need.
The Maharashtra government has decided to set up a state-level steering committee under the chairmanship of the chief secretary to implement the Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project.

The Steering Committee shall have the power to make changes, review the project, approve budgetary and administrative matters and make changes thereto as may be required.
The Annual Action Plan of the project will be approved by the State Level Steering Committee every year and after the Annual Action Plan is approved, the Project Director will implement the project physically.
The progress of the project will be reviewed by the State Level Steering Committee.
