Introduction: To ensure fair and remunerative prices to the farmers of the state and to generate employment at the local level. As per the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1, the cost of the project to the Agricultural Processing Cooperatives in the State is Rs. The financial assistance is sanctioned in the ratio of 1:9 subject to a ceiling of Rs. 500.00 lakh.
7 Agro-processing cooperatives have submitted a proposal to the Central Government to set up a cluster of Agro-processing cooperatives under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana. This is the first such request. As these 7 institutions will be set up in clusters, they will be given grants by the Central Government for the construction and maintenance of infrastructure together under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1, the proposals of the said 7 Parliaments were recommended by the State Government for grant of financial assistance on priority basis as a special case for grant of financial assistance to the National Cooperative Development Corporation. Read about these projects. Approval has been accorded for grant of financial assistance from National Cooperative Development Corporation through letters 2 to 8.
The matter of administrative approval of the projects of 7 agricultural processing cooperatives approved by the National Cooperative Development Corporation was under consideration of the government. The government has issued orders towards this.
Government Decision: National Cooperative Development Corporation has sanctioned financial assistance to the following 7 Agro Processing Cooperative Societies. Accordingly, under the scheme of disbursement of financial assistance in the ratio of 1:9 to the Agro Processing Cooperatives, administrative approval is being accorded to the projects of the following 7 Agro Processing Cooperatives.
