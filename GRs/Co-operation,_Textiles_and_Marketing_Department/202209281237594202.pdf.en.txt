Government's decision:
The 67 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative consumer institutions in the state were extended till August 31, 2022, as per the Government decision, dated March 2, 2022. The proposal for extension of these 67 temporary posts from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of the temporary posts to be extended for the period from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 are as follows:
