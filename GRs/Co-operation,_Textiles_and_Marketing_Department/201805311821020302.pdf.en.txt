Read: Maharashtra State Excise Department Maravam/ Establishment/ Pension Scheme/ A- 1/276, dated 5th February, 2015 Preamble: Under the Central Warehousing Act, 1962, the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation has been established with equal (50%-50%) share capital of the Central Warehousing Corporation and the Government of Maharashtra for the scientific storage of agricultural produce and scheduled commodities in the State.
The Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation has been consistently making profits and the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation has been paying dividends to the state government and the Central Warehousing Corporation on the profits every year. The Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation (MSRDC) employees' salary, allowances and other daily expenses are being met by the corporation from its own income and there is no financial burden on the state government for this. The Central Warehousing Corporation has given a gift to its employees. Contributory Pension Scheme has been implemented with effect from 01.01.2007. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation had submitted a proposal to implement the defined contributory pension scheme revised by the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation on the lines of the Central Warehousing Corporation. Since the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation has been continuously in profit since its inception, the matter of approval for the implementation of the contributory pension scheme for the employees of the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation was under consideration of the Government. Considering the proposal of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation, the following decisions are being taken.
Government Decision: The Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation (MSWC) has approved the following" Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation Defined Contributory Pension Scheme."
The scheme will be called" Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation Defined Contributory Pension Scheme-2018." The scheme will come into effect from the date of the government's decision.
Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation (MSCB)- Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation Contributory Pension Trust under the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation-Indian Trust Act, 1982 to manage and regulate the pension fund under the defined contributory pension scheme.

Warehousing Corporation will set up a Contributory Pension Trust. The Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation Defined Contributory Pension Scheme-2018 Scheme shall be implemented and regulated by the Board of Directors of the Corporation in accordance with the terms and conditions of the said Government Decision by registering the Trust under the Income Tax Act, 1961.
3) Under the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation Defined Contributory Pension Scheme, every employee of the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation will have to contribute 5 per cent of his" basic salary plus dearness allowance." This amount will be deducted from the employee's salary payment every month. The Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation will contribute 5 per cent of the equivalent amount i. e." Basic Pay plus Dearness Allowance."
This scheme is being made compulsory for the employees in the service of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation as on July 1, 2007. Therefore, the voluntary employees should be credited with the corporation's account only after depositing the due instalment of their monthly contribution. The stages and manner of payment of the contribution up to the date of this Government decision shall be determined by the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation Contributory Pension Trust to be constituted as per Rule No. 2 above.
If the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation incurs losses in the future, the monthly contribution of the corporation will not be deposited. The monthly contribution will be credited only after the corporation becomes profitable.
6) The Corporation shall meet the expenditure arising out of the implementation of the scheme from its own income.
There will be no financial assistance from the government for this.
(7) In case the defined contribution pension scheme applicable to the Central Warehousing Corporation is discontinued or amended in future, the proposal regarding the discontinuation or continuation of the said scheme of the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation shall be required to be submitted by the Corporation through the Department for prior approval of the Finance Department.
