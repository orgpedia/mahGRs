Proposal: - Assistance for Empowerment of District Central Co-operative Banks under Short Term Co-operative Credit Structure in the State (2425 2524) Under this scheme, 2.50% interest subvention is sanctioned by the State Government to District Central Co-operative Banks for providing crop loans to farmers at 6% interest through District Central Co-operative Banks. A sum of Rs. 512.92 crore has been budgeted for this scheme in the financial year 2025-26. Out of this fund, the reference No. 3 and No. 5 vide Government Decision dated 03.07.2025 and 23.12.2025 hereunder respectively, Rs. 187.75 crores and Rs. 200 crores has been disbursed to the Commissioner of Cooperation, Pune. Accordingly, the entire funds disbursed have been diverted for the purpose of sponsorship and the utilization certificate has been submitted to the Government by the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation. Now the Cooperative Commissioner & Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune has issued Reference No. Under letter 6, the balance for the scheme is Rs. A proposal to disburse the 125.17 crore fund has been submitted. Accordingly, the proposal for distribution of funds was under consideration of the Government.
