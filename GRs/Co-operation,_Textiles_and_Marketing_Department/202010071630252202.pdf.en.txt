Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai Bench has passed an order to continue the temporary posts of two members at Nagpur and Pune till 31/8/2020. 2 Approval has been granted pursuant to the Government Decision dated 31/3/2020. For administrative reasons, these temporary positions must continue and be extended for 2020-2021. Therefore, subject to the condition that the said posts are included in the format fixed under Government Resolution dated 5/5/2011, the Government approves the continuation of the temporary posts of two members at Nagpur and Pune from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021.
