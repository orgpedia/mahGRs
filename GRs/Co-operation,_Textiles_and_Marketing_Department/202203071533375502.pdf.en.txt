Government's decision:
The Directorate of Silk has been established by the Government Resolution dated 27th August, 1997 in the context of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour and the posts have been transferred from various establishments to the Directorate of Silk. The review of 373 posts under Director (Silk) has been approved by the High Level Committee and orders in this regard have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 22.03.2011. Further, vide Government Resolution dated 22.03.2012, 6 new posts have been sanctioned with creation of new District Silk Office at Jawhar, District Palghar. A total of 379 posts are sanctioned under the Directorate of Silk. A total of 303 posts have been made permanent and 76 are temporary. The continuation of these temporary posts from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 07.09.2021. Now, a proposal for continuation of the said 76 temporary posts has been received vide letter dated 02.03.2022 from the Director (Silk).

All the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all the existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 vide Government Order dated 22.02.2022 in the context of Department of Finance. Accordingly, it is hereby approved to continue a total of 76 temporary posts from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 with a total of 55 technical posts as shown in Form A and a total of 21 non-technical posts as shown in Form B on establishment of Directorate of Silk.
The said expenditure shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the respective financial year under the next head of accounts.
