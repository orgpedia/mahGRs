Government's decision
As per the Government decision, dated 28.09.2022, the period of extension of 67 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Co-operative Consumer Societies in the State was extended till 28.02.2023. The proposal for extension of these 67 temporary posts from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of temporary posts to be extended for the period from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023 are as follows:
