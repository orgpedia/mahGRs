Preamble: As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the State Government has decided to provide financial assistance to the farmers of the State in proportion to the supply of crop loans by the Government to empower the Primary Agricultural Credit Societies for the supply of crop loans. The government has decided to provide financial assistance ranging from 0.5% to 1.5% of the loan disbursement by these institutions every financial year. The primary agricultural credit cooperatives to be financed are bound to meet the conditions/ criteria mentioned in the government decision.
In line with the policy of the State Government, the State Government was considering to sanction funds under the Scheme of Assistance for Empowerment of Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies under Short Term Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) in the State.
Government Decision: Under the Scheme of Support to Empowerment of Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (SACS) under Short Term Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) in the State, in the year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 2000.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 1200.00 lakhs (Rupees Twelve Crores only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
