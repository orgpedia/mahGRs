Preamble: To implement the construction of 31 new grain sieving machines in each of the 31 market committees in the state. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 04.09.2018. As the implementation of the project is wide-ranging and it will take a long time to prepare a separate section 12 (1) approval proposal for each market committee and submit it to the Director of Marketing, Maharashtra State, Pune, the government will provide the work of setting up grain sieving machines to the 31 market committees included under the projects.
The issue of granting blanket permission of section 12 (1) from the level was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Maharashtra to set up 31 new grain sieving machines in 31 market committees of the state for the cleaning and percolation of incoming grain
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 59 of the Agricultural Produce Marketing (Operation and Facilitation) Act, 1963, the Government hereby grant blanket permission under section 12 (1) of the Act. Therefore, no separate approval of Section 12 (1) shall be required from the Director of Marketing, State of Maharashtra
