As per the Government Circular dated 20th July, 2021 of the Department of Finance for disbursement of funds under Contractual Service, the expenditure of hard work/ remuneration of the employees of the Contractual Service shall be covered under the objective of 10-Contractual Service.
For the purpose of disbursement of funds sanctioned under this Government Resolution, Rs. 22,46,000/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Forty Six Thousand only), Assistant Registrar (Estimates and Planning), Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune as Controlling Officer and Joint Assistant Election Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The amount to be disbursed for contractual services under the election authority will be spent under the head 10 Contractual Services under State Cooperative Election Authority, 2070 0882.
This Government Resolution is being issued pursuant to the approval given by the Finance Department vide No. No. 376/2021/ Expenditure-2, dated 11.08.2021.
