GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government had extended the period of appointment of 70 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Co-operative Consumer Societies in the State till 30th September, 2018 vide Government Decision, dated 15/03/2018, read with para. The proposal for extension of these 70 temporary posts from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of temporary posts to be extended for the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 are as follows:
