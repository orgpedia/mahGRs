The Proposal:
The Project Implementation Committee of the Department has decided to develop a new website instead of the various websites of the Department of Cooperation. was held on 08.04.2022.
Department of Information Technology. The institutions listed in the Government Decision dated 20.03.2018 have been approved by the Project Implementation Committee of the Department. Presented in the meeting dated 25.05.2022. M/ s PwC was selected on the basis of the score obtained in the evaluation report. Accordingly, 4 technical personnel have been appointed for this project. M/ s PwC was issued to the Institute on 08.06.2022. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) prepared by M/ s PwC on the said project was submitted to the Project Implementation Committee of the Department. It was agreed in the meeting dated 17.02.2023 and M/ s PwC was directed to submit Request for Proposal (RFP) in the next meeting. Accordingly, the Request for Proposal (RFP) prepared by M/ s PwC Institute was submitted to the Project Implementation Committee of the Department. It was presented in the meeting dated 28.04.2023. The meeting discussed various modules and related issues mentioned in the Detailed Project Report (DPR).
