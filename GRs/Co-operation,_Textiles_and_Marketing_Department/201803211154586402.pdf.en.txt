The Proposal:
As per the revision of the permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the relevant Government Decision No. 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the 26 posts presented in the figure. Under the above Reference No. 5, 26 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies have been de-notified. The Government had approved the continuation till 28/2/2018. In view of the expiry of the said extension, 26 temporary posts created for audit in respect of implementation of Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme of Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies will be continued for the period 2017-18. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies. Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the proposal in section 2, the following 26 temporary posts created for audit in connection with the implementation of the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme of the Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies will be given to the following: from 01/03/2018. The extension is being granted by this Government decision till 30/9/2018.
