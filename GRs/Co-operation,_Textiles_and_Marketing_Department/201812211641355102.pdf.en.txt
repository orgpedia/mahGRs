2) Sh. Corrigendum No: Policy 2017/ P.S.No.6/ Tex-5D. 16th March, 2018 Preamble- The Textile Industry Policy 2018-23 of the State has been announced as per the decision of the Government in the context.
The government was contemplating to amend the Act. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision- Textile Policy 2018-23 Issue No. 3.3, 3.4, 3.12, 7.3 and 7.4 are being revised as follows:
The issue No. Definition 3.3. Expansion Projects If an existing project installs machinery at 10% or more of the Book Value of machinery installed in the previous financial year, the investment will be considered as expansion or diversification/ modernization. This expansion and/ or diversification/ modernization will be with forward/ backward process.
3.4 Fixed Capital Investment- Investment in machinery for expansion/ diversification/ modernization and/ or in projects sanctioned by financial institutions/ banks and set up with self-financing.
3.12 Composite Unit- This definition is being dropped.
The issue No. 7.3.3 followed by 7.3.4 is being incorporated as follows.
7.3.4 Giving an option to Textile Projects to avail the benefits of Textile Policy 2011-17 or Textile Policy 2018-23
Dated 1.8.2017. The projects which have been sanctioned long term loan during the period 14.2.2018 or the projects which have placed orders for procurement of machinery in respect of self-financing projects will be given the benefit of the schemes in Textile Policy 2011-17 or Textile Policy 2018-23. Projects will be offered during this period.

The issue No. In column 3 of 7.4, the position of forward backward integration is being modified as follows.
The Composite Unit in Pillar 3 and all subsidies for it are being cancelled. Instead, an additional subsidy of 5% for forward and backward integration is being applied to textile projects in all departments of the state.
The issue No. 7.4.a. It is included as follows.
7.4.a. Payment of capital subsidy on the funds used for purchase of machinery by textile units.
The capital subsidy will also be provided on the amount used from self-funding for purchase of machinery by the bank-aided projects under the Capital Subsidy Scheme in lieu of the interest subsidy in the Textile Policy 2018-23.
