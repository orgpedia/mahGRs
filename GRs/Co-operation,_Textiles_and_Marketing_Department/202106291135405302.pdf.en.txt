Note dated 23rd June, 2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, posts in Government service are required to be reserved for persons with disabilities. Accordingly, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has published the list of posts ensured for Divyangjan vide notification dated 04.01.2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Decision dated February 2, 2021, to ensure that the posts in the State Government Service are reserved for persons with disabilities, even if the designations of the posts in this list are different, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service. Accordingly, by the Government decision dated 23rd February, 2021, out of the total 20 cadres on the establishment of the Directorate of Silk, 16 cadres have been designated for the disabled. As the posts of Accounts Officer, Group-A in the Directorate of Silk are filled by deputation from the Finance Department, the posts of Senior Superintendent, Group-B (A.R.A.) and Superintendent (Accounts and Aastha), Group-C are to be promoted and their lower level (feeder cadre) posts are ensured for the disabled. In the meeting held on 17th June, 2021 under the chairmanship of Secretary, it was directed to submit the proposal regarding reservation of posts for the disabled in the said 4 cadres to the Expert Committee constituted vide circular dated 12th November, 2020 under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Social Justice).
Accordingly, the proposal regarding the reservation of posts for the disabled of these four cadres was sent to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance through a letter dated June 17, 2021, for submission to the Expert Committee. Accordingly, the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department has decided that since the post of Accounts Officer, Group-A in the Directorate of Silk is being filled by deputation from the Finance Department, the post should be ensured by the Finance Department as the parent establishment department, the posts of Senior Superintendent, Group-B (A.R.A.) and Superintendent (Accounts and Aastha), Group-C should be reserved for the disabled as per their lower level (feeder cadre) post and in view of the duties and responsibilities of the post of Driver, Group-A, the expert committee on disability has decided that the cadre should be excluded from the reservation of posts for the disabled. Accordingly, the Government was considering the matter of ensuring the posts of Senior Assistant, Group-A on the establishment of the Directorate of Silk, Superintendent (Accounts and Aastha), Group-C and Senior Superintendent, Group-B (A.R.A.) in the chain of promotion to 2 cadres for the disabled.
Government's decision:
Out of the total 20 Cadres on establishment of Directorate of Silk, 16 Cadres have been earmarked for Divyang by the Government decision dated 23rd February, 2021. Out of the remaining 4 cadres, the post of Accountant, Group-A is to be filled by deputation from the Finance Department.
Further, in view of the duties and responsibilities of the post of Driver, Group-C, it has been decided by the Expert Committee on Differently Abled Posts to exclude this cadre from the list of Differently Abled Posts.

In terms of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, instructions have been issued that the posts which have been ensured for the disabled at the lower (feeder cadre) level under the Government decision dated 2 nd February, 2021 shall be deemed to have been automatically ensured for the said category of disabled for the post of promotion. Accordingly, the remaining 2 cadre posts in the establishment of Directorate of Silk are being ensured for Divyangjan as per Annexure attached. All other provisions/ instructions mentioned in the notification dated 23 rd February, 2021 shall continue to apply as applicable.
