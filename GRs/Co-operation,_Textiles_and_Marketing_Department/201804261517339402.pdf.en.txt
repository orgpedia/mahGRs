Preamble: Under the above mentioned read No. 1 and 2, committees were formed for the approval of tenders for the purchase of machinery and construction of buildings of the machinery cooperatives. However, in paragraph 7.3 of the new Textile Policy 2018-23 released under Read No. 3, it has been mentioned that in order to simplify the administration of the schemes under the Textile Policy 2011-17, the powers to approve the construction of machinery and purchase of machinery will be decentralized in order to be administratively convenient and to avoid wastage of time. Accordingly, the matter of decentralization of the rights of construction and purchase of machinery and equipment of the machinery cooperatives was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, the revised financial limit as mentioned in the table below is being approved to approve the tender proposals for the construction and purchase of machinery and equipment of the Machinery Cooperative Societies in the State.
