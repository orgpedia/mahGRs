Opening of new accounting heads for marketing infrastructure support (State share 40%) in various Market Committees in the State under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana.

Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date of release: October 19, 2018
Read: 1) Letter No. House No.0317/ No.14/ House Room, Dy. 31/01/2018 2) Executive Director, Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board Kripanam/ Engineering/ PRO/ State Fund Office/ 459/2018 Dy. Letter dated 20.02.2018
Preamble: Under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, funds will be provided by the Centre for the construction of marketing infrastructure in various market committees of the state with 40 percent of the funds as the share of the state government as per the investment determined by the State Level Project Approval Committee. Under the scheme, the central government will provide 60% of the total subsidy amount to the state government and the remaining 40% will be provided by the state government. For this, the government was considering opening a new account head for disbursement of financial assistance under the state share.
Government Decision: Under the Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board (MSAMB) has been approved to open a new account head for disbursement of the State Government's share of 40% of the financial assistance for the ongoing and new projects for the construction of marketing infrastructure in various market committees in the state from the financial year 2018-19.
