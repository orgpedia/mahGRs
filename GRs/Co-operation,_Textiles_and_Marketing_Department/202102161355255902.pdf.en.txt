Introduction: Read the above. As per the decision of the Government of India, a State Level Accounting Committee has been constituted for Cooperative Societies. At present, the post of chairman of the state-level accounting committee for cooperative societies is vacant and the matter of appointing the chairman of this committee was under the consideration of the government. The government has issued orders towards this.
