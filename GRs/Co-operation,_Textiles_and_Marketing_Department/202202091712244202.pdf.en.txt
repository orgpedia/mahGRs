The Proposal:
The Vita Merchant. Operational Bank Ltd. Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune has given approval for" Special one-time loan repayment scheme" to Vita (Sangli) Bank. The proposal was submitted to the Government on 16.03.2021. Accordingly, the Government vide Reference No. The Government had approved a special one-time loan repayment scheme to this bank till 30.06.2021. Also Reference No. The scheme has been extended till 31.12.2021 through a Government Resolution dated 02.02.2021.
During this period, about Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.51 crore had been disbursed. Accordingly, the extension of the scheme will reduce the accumulated losses of the bank and provide more liquidity to the bank. Therefore, keeping in mind the interest of the bank, the government was considering extending the scheme.
Government Decision: Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Pune. The Vita Merchant Co. vide letter dated 31.01.2022. Operational Bank Ltd. The proposal for extension of the special one-time loan repayment scheme submitted in respect of Vita (Sangli) Bank has been approved by The Vita Merchant Co., Ltd. by way of exemption from the provisions of Rule-49 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961, in accordance with the powers conferred on the Government under the provisions of Section 157 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960. Operational Bank Ltd. Vita (Sangli) Bank has been granted extension of Special one-time loan repayment scheme mentioned in Annexure A till 30.04.2022.
