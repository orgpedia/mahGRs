Preamble: To decide on the assistance to the disaster victims affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the state from July 2019 to August 2019. A cabinet sub-committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. The committee meeting. The meeting was held on 19.8.2019 at 2.00 pm under the Chairmanship of Hon'ble Chief Minister.
In the meeting, it was decided to waive off the crop loans of the affected farmers in the districts affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the state from July 2019 to August 2019. Accordingly, Government Decision dated 23.08.2019 and Government Decision dated 27.08.2019 were issued for loan waiver of flood victims.
The Government was considering creation of a new head of account for expenditure on the" Loan Waiver to Farmers Affected by Natural Calamities (State Level) Scheme" to waive off crop loans of farmers affected by floods and other natural calamities in the districts affected by heavy rainfall during the period July 2019 to August 2019 at the State level.
Government's decision:
Under the Government decision, approval is being given to create a new head of account for expenditure on the scheme" Loan Waiver to Farmers Affected by Natural Calamities (State Level) Scheme" as follows:
