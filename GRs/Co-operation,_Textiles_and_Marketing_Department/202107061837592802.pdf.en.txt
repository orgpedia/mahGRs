The Proposal:
The government has taken a decision. As on 11th January, 2017, the Co-operative Yarn Growers have been provided financial assistance of Rs. 3,000/- per month for a period of 5 years and the Government will pay interest up to a maximum of 12% for 5 years on this loan. Co-operative yarn mills which have repaid the interest free loan taken from the government earlier will be considered eligible for this scheme.
