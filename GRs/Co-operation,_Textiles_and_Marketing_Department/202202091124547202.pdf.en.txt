Preamble: In pursuance of the proposal of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, a proposal was submitted to the Legislative Assembly for the payment of monthly dues and regular hard work of contract clerks, typists, contract peons, retired contract officers and employees through external sources at the establishment of the Authority for the purpose of contractual service in accordance with the demand for the winter session December, 2021. Among them, No. V-2, 2070 Other Administrative Services (00) (800)-Other Expenditure (00) (01) State Cooperative Election Authority (Compulsory) (2070 0882) 10- Rs. 19,11,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Eleven Thousand only) for contractual services has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demand.
As per the Government Decision dated 14.10.2021 of the Department of Finance, it has been agreed to disburse 75% of the budgetary provision for mandatory expenditure up to December, 2021. The Government was contemplating to disburse 75% of the total budgeted (basic+ supplementary) provision of Rs. 41.58 lakh in the head 20700882,10-Contractual Service for the period August 2021 to February 2022 for the remuneration of contractual officers/ employees working on the establishment of State Cooperative Election Authority.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the objectives of Accounting Title (2070 0882) (Mandatory), 10-Contractual Service for payment of dues of Contract Clerks, Typists, Contractual Peons and retired Contractual Officers and Employees working on the establishment of State Cooperative Election Authority, a sum of Rs.1000/-

A budget has been allocated. Further, in the July 2021 session, a supplementary demand of Rs. 22.46 lakh has been approved and in the December 2021 session, a supplementary demand of Rs. 19.11 lakh has been approved. 75% of the provision of Rs. 41.58 lakhs under the Objectives (Basic+ Supplementary) of Head of Accounts (2070 0882) (Mandatory), 10- Contractual Services is to be disbursed.
Out of this, an amount of Rs. 22.46 lakh has been released as per the Government decision dated 06.09.2021. Accordingly, the Government have approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 8,72,500/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Seventy Two Thousand Five Hundred only).
For disbursement of funds under Contractual Service, as per the Government Circular dated 03.01.2022 of the Department of Finance, the expenditure of hard work/ remuneration of the employees of the Contractual Service shall be covered under the objective of 10-Contractual Service.
The Assistant Registrar (Estimates and Planning), Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer (V0004) and the Joint Assistant Election Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 8,72,500/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Seventy Two Thousand Five Hundred only) sanctioned under this Government Decision. The amount to be disbursed towards contractual services under the election authority will be spent under the head 10-Contractual Services under the State Cooperative Election Authority, 2070 0882.
This Government Resolution is being issued in pursuance of the approval given by the Department of Finance vide No. 13/2022/ Expenditure-2, dated 11.01.2022.
