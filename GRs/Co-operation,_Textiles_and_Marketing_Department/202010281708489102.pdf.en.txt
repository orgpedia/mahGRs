Preamble: To decide on the assistance to the disaster victims affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the state from July 2019 to August 2019. A cabinet sub-committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. The committee meeting. 19.8.2019 and dt. on 03.09.2019. It was presided over by the Chief Minister. In the meeting, it was decided to waive off the crop loans of the affected farmers in the districts affected by heavy rains and floods in the state from July 2019 to August 2019. Accordingly, Government Orders dated 23.08.2019, 27.08.2019 and 12.02.2020 were issued.
For the implementation of this scheme, the Government was considering to disburse the funds from the funds sanctioned by the Supplementary Demand for Financial Year 2019-20 under the head 33 Financial Assistance to Farmers Affected by Natural Calamities (State Level), (2435 0133) from the available provision for the financial year 2020-21 as per the demand of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
