Preamble: The Marketing Federation of India was appointed as the State Nodal Agency by NAFED for procurement of pulses and oilseeds under Price Stabilization Fund and Minimum Support Price (MSP) at guaranteed prices during 2016-17 and 2017-18. Under this scheme, funds were made available by raising loans from nationalized banks through government guarantees approved by the Finance Department from time to time to pay the dues of the farmers as soon as possible. The total due interest and penal interest on the Government Guarantee sanctioned by the Finance Department from time to time to the Marketing Federation for procurement at guarantee has been paid from the Self-Fund. The Federation has paid a total amount of Rs. 31.6641 crore towards interest on loans provided on various Government Guarantees, out of which only Rs. 20.50 crore has been made available as per Government Decision, dated 21/1/2020. The remaining amount of Rs. 11.1641 crore has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demands for Grants in the 2nd (Monsoon) Session, 2022 for making available the remaining amount to the Federation. The matter of disbursing the funds sanctioned through this supplementary demand to the Federation is under the consideration of the Government.
