The Proposal:
The Government of India has decided to provide assistance to the farmers who supply sugarcane to the mills during the crushing season 2014-15 for payment of FRP for sugarcane. The soft loan scheme was announced as per the notification dated June 23, 2015. Under this scheme, 50% of the total cane price paid as FRP for the crushing season 2014-15 is to be paid by the farmers. The Cabinet decided to provide interest subsidy for the next four years to 148 eligible cooperative and private sugar mills in the State, which have paid their dues as on 30th June, 2015 and which have paid their dues during the 2013-14 and 2014-15 seasons, as per the rate of interest for the first year at the rate of 10% of the direct interest or the rate of interest charged by the banks, whichever is lower. Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 30.07.2015 and Government Sanction Letter No. was held on 28.09.2015.
Thereafter, the State Government has released interest subsidy for 22 cooperative and private sugar mills which had taken up crushing season in 2014-15 only. The decision was taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 05.09.2015. The government has decided to implement the soft loan interest subsidy scheme for 6 cooperative and private sugar mills that do not fit into the two schemes. 09.12.2015.
