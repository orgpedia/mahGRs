Preamble The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated 7th October, 2016 to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service.
For this, the Central Government has released the list of posts reserved for Divyangjan as per the notification dated January 4, 2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Decision dated February 2, 2021, regarding the posts of Group-A to Group-D posts fixed by the Central Government in the state government establishments, the posts of which the duties and responsibilities, nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, although the designations of such posts in the list published by the Central Government are different. The posts under this Department have not been ensured in the posts ensured for the disabled persons as per the notification dated 4th January, 2021 of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. As per the order dated 28.01.2021 in case 58/ 2021, an expert committee has been appointed to ensure posts in government services for the disabled.

As per the recommendation of the Expert Committee for ensuring posts in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities in its meeting held on 22nd February, 2021, the posts of Senior Marketing Officer, Machine Manager and Clothing Inspector in Group-C cadre in the Commissionerate of Textiles, Nagpur, under this Department, have been filled up. 6 The disabled have been ensured in the government service under the government decision here.
Now, in the meeting held on 21st June, 2021, the Expert Committee for ensuring posts in Government Services for Divyangs has recommended to ensure the posts of Clothing Specialist, Assistant Commissioner (Technical), Junior Marketing Officer, Junior Industrial Inspector, Assistant Clothing Inspector, Stamp Inspector, Technical Assistant in the cadre of Group-A to Group-C in the Commissionerate of Textiles, Nagpur under this Department.
Government Decision The posts of Clothing Experts, Assistant Commissioners (Technical), Junior Marketing Officers, Junior Industrial Inspectors, Assistant Clothing Inspectors, Stamp Inspectors, Technical Assistants in the cadre of Group-A to Group-C under this Department are being ensured as shown in the new category of Persons with Disabilities under this Government Decision.
