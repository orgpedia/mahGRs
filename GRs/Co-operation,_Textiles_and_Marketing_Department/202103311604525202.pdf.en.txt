Preamble: In order to facilitate payment of FRP for sugarcane to the farmers supplying sugarcane to the mills during the 2014-15 crushing season, the Central Government has decided to provide Rs. The soft loan scheme was announced as per the notification dated June 23, 2015. Under this scheme, 50% of the total cane price paid as FRP for the crushing season 2014-15 is to be given to the farmers. The 147 eligible cooperative and private sugar mills in the State which have paid the dues at the end of 30/6/2015 and which have paid the dues in the 2013-14 and 2014-15 seasons have been given the opportunity to apply the scheme announced by the Central Government. A Government Resolution has been issued on 30.07.2015. Thereafter, the State Government has released interest subsidy for 22 cooperative and private sugar mills which had taken up crushing season in 2014-15 only. The decision was taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 05.09.2015. The government has decided to implement the soft loan-interest subsidy scheme for 6 cooperative and private sugar mills that do not fit into the two schemes.
