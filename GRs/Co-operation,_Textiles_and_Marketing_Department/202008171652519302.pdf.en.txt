Preamble: The Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Tribunal, Mumbai. The existing court building of the office of the Co-operative Court, Nanded, was in a dilapidated condition. Therefore, the Assistant Commissioner Wazirabad, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation, Nanded has issued a notice to the concerned land owner regarding the demolition of the dilapidated structure. According to the letter dated 21.6.2020 of the Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai, due to rain falling on some parts of the existing building and many case papers getting wet due to rain, the Co-operative Court, Nanded is not able to conduct its office work at the said place. Therefore, the court has granted permission to shift to the new rented premises and take possession of the said new premises. Thus, approximately 1900 sq. ft. is required for the office of this court. The owner of the building, Mr. Jitendra Hariprasad Daima, has decided to take the new private building on lease for a foot space. Devashish Complex, 1st Floor, SBI
The proposal submitted by the Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai, under reference letter, regarding financial and administrative approval to enter into an agreement with Ghamodia Factory, Doctor Lane, Nanded, Nanded, Waghala Mahapalika Hada, Nanded, and to pay rent certified by the concerned Public Works Department, was under consideration of the government.
