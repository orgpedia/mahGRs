The Proposal:
As per the revision of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the relevant Government Decision No. 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 2 posts. Under the aforesaid Reference No. 4, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule has sanctioned a total of 2 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in various executive services cooperative societies and 7 other institutions. The government had approved to continue till 28/2/2018. With the expiry of the said extension, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule has decided to continue the 2 temporary posts created for audit in various executive services cooperatives and 7 other organizations for the period 2018-19 for carrying out the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune under the letter of 2.
Government's decision:
Commissioner for Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies in consideration of the above. Maharashtra State, Pune.
