Preamble: In pursuance of the proposal of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune vide WATCH 1, a supplementary demand for an amount of Rs. 40,11,000/- (Rupees Forty Lakhs Eleven Thousand only) was submitted to the Legislative Assembly through Supplementary Demand for the Monsoon Session, August, 2022 for payment of monthly dues and regular wages of contract clerks, typists, contract peons, retired contract officers and employees as well as for payment of advocate fees under Professional Services Act, 2022 on the establishment of the Authority through external sources.
The matter of disbursement of funds sanctioned through supplementary demand as approved by the Legislature was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Demand No. The Supplementary Demands for Funds (Rs. 40,11,000/-) have been sanctioned for the following: V-2, 2070 Other Administrative Services (00) (800)-Other Expenditure (00) (01) State Cooperative Election Authority (Compulsory) (2070 0882)-Bab 10- Rs. 33,81,000/-for Contractual Services and Bab 28- Rs. 6,30,000/-for Professional Services.
2. Considering the details of the aforesaid sanctioned Supplementary Demand, 30% of the funds sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demand under Chapter 10- Contractual Services i.e. Rs. 10,14,300/- (Rupees Ten Lakh Fourteen Thousand Three Hundred only) and 100% of the funds sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demand under Chapter 28- Commercial Services i.e. Rs. 6,30,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh Thirty).

A total amount of Rs. 16,44,300/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Forty Four Thousand Three Hundred only) is being approved under this Government decision.
For disbursement of funds under contract services, the Department of Finance vide its circular dated 29th August, 2022 has mandated that the expenditure on wages/ wages of contractual employees shall be covered under the objectives of contract services.
The Assistant Registrar (Estimates and Planning), Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer (V0004) and the Joint Cooperative Election Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 16,44,300/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Forty Four Thousand Three Hundred only) sanctioned under this Government Decision. The amount disbursed for contractual services and professional services under the Election Authority will be spent under the Election Authority, 2070 0882, under the head of Chapter 10 Contractual Services and Chapter 28- Professional Services.
This Government Resolution is being issued in pursuance of the approval given by the Department of Finance vide informal reference No. 469/2022/ Expenditure-2 dated 23.09.2022.
