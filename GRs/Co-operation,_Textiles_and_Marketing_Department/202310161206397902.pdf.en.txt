The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has released an official notification for the recruitment of Assistant Director (Silk) in Group A of Maharashtra Silk Service. 1 The candidates recommended here under vide letter dated 12.07.2023 are appointed as Assistant Director (Silk), Group A in the pay scale S-17 (Rs. 47600-151100) in the revised pay matrix as per the 7th Pay Commission with effect from the date of their joining and are being appointed by nomination for a period of two years subject to verification.

The candidates will be appointed on the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1. The candidate shall, within 30 days from the date of this Government decision on his appointment to the post or if for a specified reason the request for extension of this period is made and accepted by the Government on merit, appear before the Director (Silk), Directorate of Silk, Nagpur, with all the original documents required. If they fail to appear within the stipulated time, their appointment will be cancelled.
The appointment of the candidate will be in the nature of probationary for a period of 2 years from the date of his assumption of office as per the provisions of General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 22nd June, 2021. If the candidate does not achieve the expected standard of work during the period under review or if his conduct is found to be inappropriate, his service will be terminated without any prior notice.
