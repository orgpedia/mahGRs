The Proposal:
The Director of Marketing under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, Maharashtra State, Pune has been given the target for the plantation and cultivation of trees by the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the Government decision at A. No. (1) under reference for the main premises and sub-market premises of Agricultural Produce Market Committees. The government was contemplating to allocate funds for the plantation and maintenance of these trees.
Government Decision: Out of the total budgetary provision available for the year 2019-2020 for tree plantation and plantation, a maximum of 0.5% of the expenditure on the programme (under the earlier schemes) has been provided under 02 Wages i.e. Rs. 48.75 lakhs and 21 Supplies and Materials Rs. 48.75 lakh for the year 2019-2020. The Rs. 97.50 lakh out of which Rs. 83,45,805 is being disbursed.
The Managing Director, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of the said funds. Also, the existing Drawing and Disbursing Officers at their District level offices under the control of the Controller General are being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers for the scheme. Accordingly, the required expenditure should be met from the approved provision for the year 2019-2020 under the following head.
