9. Department of Government Decisions, Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
No: SAPAS-2018/ Q.No. 367/24 S dated 8th March, 2019
Preamble: In view of the fall in onion prices in the State since October, 2018, the Government vide its decision dated 26/12/2018, has sanctioned subsidy to the extent of Rs.200 per quintal and upto a maximum of Rs.200 per quintal per farmer for the period from 1st November, 2018 to 15th December, 2018 to the farmers who have sold onion in the Agricultural Produce Market Committees/ Private Market Committees in the State. The period of grant-in-aid has been extended from time to time and the revised grant-in-aid period is from 1st November, 2018 to 26th February, 2019.
Lakshmi Sopan Agriculture Produce Marketing Company Ltd. The inclusion of Barshi, a private market committee, was under the consideration of the government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SAPAS-2018/ PR No. 367/24 S
Government Decision: Lakshmi Sopan Agriculture Produce Marketing Company Limited (LPAML) has been selected as a beneficiary of the Onion Subsidy Scheme for the period 2018-2019 from 1st November, 2018 to 28th February, 2019. Barshi is a private market committee.
The terms and conditions of implementation of the scheme for the said Private Market Committee shall remain the same as mentioned in the Government Decision, dated 26/12/2018.
