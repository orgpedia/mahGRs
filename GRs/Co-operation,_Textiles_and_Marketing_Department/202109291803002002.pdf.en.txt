Preamble: As per the provisions of Section 154 (a) (1) of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Council, Pune has been reconstituted and the Council is headed by Mr. Vidyadhar Vamanrao Anaskar, R. Riturang Co-operative Housing Society Ltd.
Phase-II, City Light Bakery, Aranyeshwar, Tavare Colony, Pune has been appointed as the Chairman of the Board as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The duration of the said Council has been allotted for a period of three years with effect from 06.08.2021 or until further orders, whichever is less. Earlier, the government had accorded the post of the president with the status of a minister of state and provided all the facilities permissible for the post of minister of state. Similarly, the issue of giving the post of' Minister of State" status was still under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In exercise of the powers conferred by the provisions of section 154 (a) (1) of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, it is hereby notified that the Maharashtra State Co-operative Council (M.S.C.C.C.C.) Vidyadhar Vamanrao Anaskar, President, Maharashtra State Cooperative Council, Pune, is hereby approved by the Government to be accorded the status of" Minister of State."
The functions of the Maharashtra State Co-operative Council shall continue to be as per the provisions of Section 154 (a) (3) of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960.
Vidyadhar Vamanrao Anaskar, President, Maharashtra State Cooperative Council, Pune vide Government Resolution, Finance Division No., S.A. S.- 10.10/ p. 96/10/ CE, dated 13.03.2012 and all the services as admissible to the post of Minister of State.
