Introduction: Shree Solapur Readymade Textile Manufacturers Association, Solapur had requested the Government of Maharashtra to be the sponsor for the" 3rd Uniform Expo-2018" to be held in Bangalore. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to sanction the fee for participating as a sponsor of the exhibition.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Shree Solapur Readymade Textile Manufacturers Association, Solapur has decided to participate as Sponsor in the 3rd Uniform Expo-2018 to be held at Bangalore.
Approval is being given to sanction partnership fee of Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh only). The Rs. 20,00,000/- by deducting 10% TDS and the remaining amount is Rs. 18,00,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs only) should be paid to Shri Solapur Readymade Textile Manufacturers Association, Solapur. The Institute shall make the expenditure in the prescribed manner and submit the report to the Director Textiles, Nagpur.
