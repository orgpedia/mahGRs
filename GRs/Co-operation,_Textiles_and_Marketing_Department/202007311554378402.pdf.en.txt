Preamble: The office of the District Special Auditor Class-I, Co-operative Society, Thane has been functioning for the last 25 to 30 years under the Thane Municipal Corporation Employees Co-operative Credit Society Limited. The building, owned by the Thane Cooperative Society, is functioning on lease at Mahatma Phule Sahakar Bhavan, Kadwa Galli, Jambhali Naka, Thane (P). The building has been declared" extremely dangerous" by the Thane Municipal Corporation since 2007. Therefore, the Municipal Corporation has given notices to the above office from time to time to vacate the building within 24 hours. Considering the potential risk of this dangerous building, it was absolutely necessary to shift the thematic office to the new premises immediately to avoid future loss of life and finances.
