Government Decision: Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 26 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in the Department of Financial Services, Mumbai were extended till 28/2/ 2020 vide Government Resolution dated 25th September, 2020. The proposal for extension of these 26 temporary posts from 1st March, 2021 to 31st August, 2021 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
