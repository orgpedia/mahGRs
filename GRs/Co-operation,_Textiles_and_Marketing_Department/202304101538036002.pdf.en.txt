Introduction Textile Policy 2018-23 of the State of Maharashtra Reference No. 2 Here, as per the decision of the government, various schemes have been launched in the state under this policy. The policy will expire on March 31, 2023. The process of formulating the proposed new textile policy of the state is also underway. The government was considering extending the existing textile policy till the proposed new textile policy was announced.
Considering the facts in the Government Resolution-Preamble, the State Textile Policy 2018-23 is being extended till the announcement of the proposed new Textile Policy.
