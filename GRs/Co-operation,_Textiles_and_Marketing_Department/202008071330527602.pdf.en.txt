Order: Read No. B.B. Chavan, Joint Secretary (Textiles) was appointed as Government Director in Maharashtra State Handloom Corporation. However, Shri B. B. Chavan, Joint Secretary (Textiles) has retired from Government service. Therefore, his appointment as Government Director on Maharashtra State Handloom Corporation has been cancelled and Mr. Vishal Madane, Deputy Secretary (Textiles) Additional Charge is being appointed in his place. Also Read No. Shri V.R. Thakur, Under Secretary (Textiles-Tex-3) was appointed as Government Director in Maharashtra State Handloom Corporation. However, Shri V.R. Thakur, Under Secretary (Textiles-TEX-3) has been transferred from the Department of Textiles to the Department of General Administration. Therefore, his appointment as Government Director in the Maharashtra State Handloom Corporation was cancelled and he was replaced by Shri. Gauri Mhaske, Cell Officer (Tex-3), Additional Charge
The shares of Maharashtra State Handloom Corporation should be transferred in the name of Mr. Vishal Madane, Deputy Secretary (Textiles) Additional charge in the name of Mr. Chavan, Joint Secretary (Textiles). Further, shares of Maharashtra State Handloom Corporation in the name of Shri V.R. Thakur, Under Secretary (Textiles-Tex-3) have been transferred to Shri. Gauri Mhaske, Cell Officer (Tex-3) should be transferred to the additional charge.
