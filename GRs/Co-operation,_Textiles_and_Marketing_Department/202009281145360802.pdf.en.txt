Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 26.03.2020, 118 temporary posts in the offices of the Commissionerate of Sugar and Regional Joint Director (Sugar) under it have been created. The Government had approved the continuation from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. Now, the office of the Commissioner of Sugar has submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference to Note 7 for approval to continue the said 118 posts with effect from 01.09.2020. Accordingly, at present, the proposal to continue the 118 temporary posts from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval is being given for continuation of 118 temporary posts as shown in Table A and B attached with the office of the Sugar Commissioner and the offices of Regional Joint Director (Sugar) under him from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. In the meantime, the offices of the Office of the Administrator (AASTA-1) and the Office of the Administrator (5-C) may, in any case, review the sanctioned posts on their establishment and on the establishment of the offices under their administrative control, and revise the figure.
