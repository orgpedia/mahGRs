The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015
The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act has been enacted with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their growing expectations from the local administration. Accordingly, the Public Service, Designated Officers, First and Second Appellate Authorities and the prescribed time limit are required to be notified under this Act. Accordingly, the matter of notifying the services provided to the people under the Directorate of Silk was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
In order to make the benefits of various services provided by the Directorate of Silk available to the citizens/ farmers/ industries of the state, the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 specifies the following categories of public servants, designated officers, first and second appellate authorities and timelines:
