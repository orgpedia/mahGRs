Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Agricultural Rural Multipurpose Development Bank, Mumbai has been allotted a total of 50 posts, including 9 posts for audit of apex bank and 41 posts for audit of district banks. The Government has approved the continuation of the lockdown till 31.08.2023.
2. Figure on Review of posts in Audit Department under Cooperation Department includes 50 posts for Audit of Maharashtra State Cooperative Agricultural Rural Multipurpose Development Bank, Mumbai (Headquarter) (9 posts) and District Banks (41 posts).
Maharashtra State Cooperative Agricultural Rural Multi-Purpose Bank Limited under the process of fixing the revised figure of sanctioned permanent/ temporary posts under the department. The revised pattern of continuous and concurrent audit posts in the Mumbai (Headquarter) and District Banks has been approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries and issued vide Government Resolution No.Rajas-1008/ PR No. 538/5 dated 5.5.2011.
In accordance with the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the list of Chartered Accountant Firms/ Chartered Accountants/ Certified Auditors and Auditors of Accounts has been published for the audit of cooperative societies in the state of Maharashtra. This includes most of the employees and those who are ineligible to be empanelled as per the terms of empanelment will assist the empanelled officers/ employees.
Further, in view of the changes made in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 in accordance with the said Constitutional Amendment, the proposal for the re-employment of a total of 3995 posts in the Audit Department as per the approval in the figure is under examination and noting that this scrutiny will take time, the Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government for the continuation of a total of 50 posts in the Maharashtra State Cooperative Agricultural Rural Multipurpose Development Bank, Mumbai, 9 posts for audit of the apex bank and 41 posts for audit of district banks in the year 2023-2024. Department of Finance Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 31.08.2023. As per the Corrigendum dated 06.09.2023, 50 temporary posts in the Statement-A for audit as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments. w.e.f. 01.09.2023 The Government hereby approve the continuation up to 29.02.2024.
