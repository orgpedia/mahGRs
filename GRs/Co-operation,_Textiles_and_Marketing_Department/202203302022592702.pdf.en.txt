Introduction: During the four consecutive years from 2015-16 to 2018-19, due to drought-like situation in various parts of the state, a large amount of less than 50 paise was released.
The farmers of the state have been badly affected due to scanty rainfall in the state. Due to the loss of farmers in the wake of this natural disaster, the loan related to agriculture could not be repaid in time for a few years. As a result, the farmers are in debt and are unable to get loans for their crops. To get farmers out of the vicious cycle of debt. The Chief Minister announced the" Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme 2019" in the Winter Session, 2019. The government decision on debt relief scheme has been issued under Section 1.
Under the Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers' Debt Waiver Scheme 2019 (State Level) (Programme), (24350142), 33 financial assistance for the financial year 2021-22, the Government was considering to disburse funds as per the demand of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
