The Proposal:
The Government has issued a decision to elaborate the nature of Atal Financial Assistance Scheme for Cooperative Societies to provide financial assistance for the growth of business of cooperative societies by promoting projects based on new cooperative societies under Articles 3 and 4 in the context, especially on agriculture, agriculture, allied and non-agriculture innovative industries.
The scheme envisages financial assistance to promote agricultural, agricultural, allied and non-agricultural innovative projects of cooperative societies and is to be implemented through the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation.

Government's decision:
Under the Atal Financial Assistance Scheme for Cooperative Societies, for the year 2018-19, Rs. 50000.00 lakhs out of which Rs. 8004.69 lakh (Rupees Eighty Crores Four Lakhs Seventy Nine Thousand only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
The amount includes project management charges of Rs. 10.00 crore to Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation and loan amount to be paid by Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation at the rate of 12.50% of the project cost under the scheme. The funds sanctioned as share contribution as above should be spent only for the purpose specified and separate accounts should be maintained by the Corporation.
The Commissioner for Cooperation and the Registrar, Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune are hereby declared as the Controlling Authorities for the payment of this provision to the concerned institutions. Also, the Accountant, Under Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune are being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers. The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune should ensure that this expenditure is done in time by withdrawing this fund. Also, the report and development board-wise expenditure should be sent to the government from time to time by the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation.
4. While disbursing the above sanctioned funds to the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall obtain the utilization certificate in respect of the said amount and submit a copy thereof to the Government.
