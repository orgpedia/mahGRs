In the above background, the Government was considering to extend the deadline for online application under the scheme" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017" for those farmers who have not applied earlier or their family members who have been deprived of applying.
Government's decision:
Under the scheme" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017," the facility of applying online for farmers who were deprived of applying in the previous period is being extended till 20th May, 2018.
