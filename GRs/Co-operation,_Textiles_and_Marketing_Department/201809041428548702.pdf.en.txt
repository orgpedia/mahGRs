The Proposal:
Maharashtra State Cooperative Textile Federation Limited, Mumbai is the representative body of cooperative societies in the textile industry. The Textile Federation is registered on 22.12.1970 under the provisions of Maharashtra State Cooperative Societies Act, 1960. The main objective of the institute is to provide financial and technical guidance to the cooperative yarn mills as well as other cooperative societies in the textile industry in the state and to act as a link between the Directorate of Textiles and the State Government and the cooperative societies in the textile industry. As per the Government Order dated 22nd June, 2010, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Textile Federation Limited, Mumbai has been receiving a total amount of Rs. The Government has decided to sanction a grant of Rs. 50.00 lakh as financial assistance. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the Federation for the financial year 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government.
