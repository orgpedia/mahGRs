Preamble: Under section 39 (b) (vii) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963, the State Government shall appoint one member each from the Revenue Department concerned and from the Chairman of the Market Committees. As per the Government decision dated 6th June, 2017, non-official members have been appointed for four revenue divisions of Amravati, Nashik, Pune and Konkan.
Also from the Nagpur revenue department. The non-official members have been appointed as per the Government Resolution dated 18.01.2018. As per the provisions of Section 39-D of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963, the state government has the power to cancel the appointment of these members. As per the provisions of Section 39 (d) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963, the appointments of non-official members in the revenue divisions of Amravati, Nashik, Pune, Konkan and Nagopar, presently appointed to the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board, were under consideration of the Government.
As per the powers conferred on the Government under Section 39 (d) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963, the following non-official members of the Amravati, Nashik, Pune, Konkan and Nagupar revenue divisions presently appointed to the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board are hereby cancelled:
