The Proposal:
As per the revision of the permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the relevant Government Decision No. 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the 21 posts presented in the figure. As per the above, 2 temporary posts out of 21 created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks under No. 5 have been given.
The Government had approved the continuation till 28/02/2018. In view of the expiry of the said extension, in pursuance of the 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of 2 temporary posts for the period 2018-19, out of 21 created for the audit of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks, in connection with the implementation of the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme, the Audit Department has been asked to work as per the requirement. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies. Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the proposal, the following 2 temporary posts created for the purpose of continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks are functional. These posts are being approved for extension from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018 through this Government decision.
