The Proposal:
The state government has been providing relief to the farmers who have been reeling under debt burden due to continuous drought and drought since 2009-10. As per the approval of the Cabinet in its meeting held on 24.6.2017, Govt. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana-2017 has been announced on 28.6.2017. After that, in order to ensure that more and more farmers get the benefit of this scheme, the scheme has been modified from time to time according to the government decisions and recommendations of the cooperative department mentioned in the above" Read More"
Under the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana-2017 (Scheduled Caste Sub-Plan), the loan waiver to farmers (24350091) was under the consideration of the government.
