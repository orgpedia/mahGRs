The Proposal: -
Section 39 (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963 provides for every revenue from the Chairman of the Market Committees in the State as a member on the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board.
There is a provision to nominate 6 members, one from the department. In accordance with the
Chairman of the Market Committee of Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Sillod from Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar Revenue Department
The Government was considering the appointment of Shri Keshavrao Yadavrao Taide as a nominated member on the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board.
The Government's Decisions:
Agricultural income from the Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar Revenue Department as a member on the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board as per Section 39 (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963
Market Committee Sillod with the nomination of the Chairman of the Market Committee, Shri Keshavrao Yadavrao Tayde.
The appointment is being approved by the government.
2. Shri Keshavrao Yadavrao Taide, Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Corporation from the date of this Government decision.
Will be eligible to work as a member on the board.
