Preamble: In 2013-14, Government of India appointed NAFED as the lead agent for procurement of various pulses, cereals and oilseeds at MSP. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai is appointed as the Deputy Agent for the implementation of this scheme in the state. Under the Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme, NAFED has made a provision of Rs. 566.00 lakh for the financial year 2018-19 under the head Financial Assistance for Contingent Expenditure for procurement being done by Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation (24252417). Out of which Rs. 70.00 lakhs has been disbursed to the Federation as per the Government decision dated 13/6/2018.
The circular dated 8/3/2019 of the Department of Finance has approved the disbursement of budgetary provisions (including supplementary demands) for the financial year 2018-2019. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to release funds to the tune of Rs. 476.00 lakh for reimbursement of Government guarantee fee and Rs. 20.00 lakh for payment of advertisement dues, totalling Rs. 496.00 lakh, under the head' Financial Assistance to Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation for Contingency Expenditure for Payment of Agricultural Produce procured through NAFED' (2425 2417).
