26/2-C, a) The Government of the Revenue and Forest Department at No. 2 under reference. Approval for restructuring of short term crop loans into medium term loans (for 5 years) in all the villages of the districts mentioned in Annexure A with effect from 28.2.2018 where the seasonal payment is less than 50 paise and moratorium on recovery of farm loans. Accordingly, Nationalized Banks, Commercial Banks, Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank Ltd. Mumbai and the concerned District Central Co-operative Banks should take necessary action.
For easy reference, see above. The Government Resolution dated 28.2.2018 is annexed herewith as Annexure A.
The date of repayment of Kharif Crop Loan is 31st March, 2018 and the date of repayment of Rabi Crop Loan is 31st March, 2018. The farmers of the above affected villages who are not able to repay the crop loan within the stipulated time as on June 30, 2018, should be restructured into medium term loan (duration 5 years) with interest for the Kharif and Rabi seasons of 2017-18 with the consent of the farmers. All the banks have taken steps to restructure the kharif crop loans. Crop loans for Kharif 2018 should be provided to such farmers by 30th July, 2018. The Co-operation Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune as well as the Coordinator, State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC) Bank of Maharashtra, Pune should ensure that this Government circular is implemented scrupulously.
