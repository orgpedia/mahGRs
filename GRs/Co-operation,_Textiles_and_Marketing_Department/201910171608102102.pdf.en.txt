Decision of the Government: 70 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative consumer organizations in the State were extended till 30th September, 2019 as per the Government Decision, dated 12th March, 2019, read with (5) above. (1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
