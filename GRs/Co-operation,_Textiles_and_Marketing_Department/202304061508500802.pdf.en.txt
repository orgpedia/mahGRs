Read the preface here. Ichalkaranji Powerloom Mega Cluster Ltd. was approved in the 97th meeting of the High Powered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary. It has been decided to constitute a committee to accord the status of mega project under the Collective Incentive Scheme.
Accordingly, Ichalkaranji Powerloom Mega Cluster Ltd. The government was considering the formation of a committee to give the project the status of a large project under the collective incentive scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the information in the Preamble, Ichalkaranji Powerloom Mega Cluster Ltd. A committee is being formed to give this project the status of a mega project under the collective incentive scheme.
The composition of the Committee will be as follows: 1. Principal Secretary (Industries), Department of Industrial Energy and Labour. Secretary (Textiles), Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles. 3) Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. Member 4) Commissioner (State Goods and Services Tax), Mazgaon, Mumbai. Member (Textiles) Commissionerate of Textiles, Nagpur.
The committee is headed by Ichalkaranji Powerloom Mega Cluster Ltd. The project was funded under the Textile Policy 2018-23 in line with the demands made by the Textile Sector.
