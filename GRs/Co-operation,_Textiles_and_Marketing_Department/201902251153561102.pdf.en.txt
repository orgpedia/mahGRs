Preamble: On the Establishment of the State Co-operative Election Authority Read No. 1 here.
The creation of the posts has been approved under the Government Decision dated 31/10/2013. Accordingly, 12 posts of clerks and typists have been sanctioned for these offices, 6 posts are to be filled on deputation and 6 posts on contract basis. There are 6 vacancies on contract basis and the government was considering to do the work of the clerk typist posts for this office through an external system.
Decision of the Government: It is being approved to fill up the posts on contractual basis in the establishment of Clerk Typists approved in the establishment of State Cooperative Election Authority for a period of 11 months by externment subject to the following conditions.
(c) The tender for the said work shall be invited from the contractor/ company/ institution in the prescribed manner and the external agency shall be selected in a very transparent manner and with the approval of the competent authority.
(b) The State Co-operative Election Authority shall enter into an agreement with the concerned company/ institution to work through an external agency. The terms and conditions of employment must be stated. It should be ensured that the government will not have any responsibility for the services of the officers and employees working on behalf of the company/ institution.
(c) The payment of work to be done by external agencies should be shown under the heading' office expenses' instead of the detailed heading' salary'.
