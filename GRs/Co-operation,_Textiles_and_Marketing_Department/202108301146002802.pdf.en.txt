Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 26 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in the Department of Financial Services, Mumbai were extended till 31/8/2021 as per the Government Decision, dated 30th March, 2021, in the above mentioned table. The proposal for extension of these 26 temporary posts from 1st September, 2021 to 28th February, 2022 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
