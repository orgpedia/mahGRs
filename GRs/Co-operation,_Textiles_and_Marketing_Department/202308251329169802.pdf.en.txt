Government circular:
General Administration Department Reference No. 1 The Rules for the Regulation of Seniority of Officers/ Employees in the Government Service have been published under the Government Notification. As per the provisions of the said Rules, a comprehensive policy has been prescribed for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
As per the provisions of Rule 3 (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2011, the Additional Registrar of Cooperative Societies shall be responsible for the registration of Group A category of cooperative societies. Seniority as on 1/1/2023 has been revised to temporary Seniority at the level of Office of the Cooperation Commissioner. It was published on 21/6/2023. No objections have been received in this regard. Therefore, the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies have directed the Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies to appoint officers working in Group-A cadre. A proposal has been submitted to the Government to finalize the seniority as on 1/1/2023. Accordingly, the Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies Group-A has been appointed. The final seniority as on 1/1/2023 is being published as per Annexure-A attached.
The date of seniority mentioned against the name of the officer of the said seniority shall be as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No.SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII, D. As on 21/6/2021, only Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies will be considered for determining seniority in Group-A cadre.
The final seniority shall be brought to the notice of all the concerned Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies Group-A (including the officer on leave/ deputation) by the Cooperation Commissioner.
