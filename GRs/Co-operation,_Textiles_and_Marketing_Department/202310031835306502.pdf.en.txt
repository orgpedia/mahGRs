GOVERNMENT DECISION: A study group to examine various aspects relating to private market yards in the State has been approved through a decision of the Government in this regard. Under this Government decision, the following Alternative Marketing Channels are being approved to be included in the scope of the study group constituted under this Government decision.
(i) Direct Marketing (ii) Farmers-Consumers Market (iii) Contract Farming Agreements (iv) Marketing through Electronic Trading Platform
Further, since the above mentioned additional matters are being entrusted to the Study Group constituted in respect of the said Private Market Complex, the time limit of 45 days for the Study Group to submit its report to the Government is being extended from 30 days to 75 days.
Further, this Government decision approves the inclusion of the following members in the Study Group constituted under the decision of the Government in question.
