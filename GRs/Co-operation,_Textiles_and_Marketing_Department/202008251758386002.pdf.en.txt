The Proposal:
Dated 1.4.2015. """ Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme 2019"" is a debt relief scheme for providing relief to the farmers who have taken short term crop loans for the period upto 31.3.2019 and have restructured/ restructured their short term crop loans during this period.""" The decision was announced by the government. The benefit of this scheme should be immediately given to the eligible beneficiaries. For this purpose, Reference No. 2 from the contingency fund of Rs. Rs 900 crore has been allocated for this. The matter was under consideration of the government for sanction of funds.
Government's decision:
Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme 2019 The government has sanctioned Rs 900 crore for the project.
In the order/ letter regarding handing over the amount to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending from the Contingency Fund by the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune, the payment to be submitted to the Treasury at the time of spending from the advance amount will be considered as" Contingency Fund Advance Finance Department, Memorandum No. Clear instructions should be written in red ink as' Approved under 24.8.2020'. This instruction should be underlined with red ink.
