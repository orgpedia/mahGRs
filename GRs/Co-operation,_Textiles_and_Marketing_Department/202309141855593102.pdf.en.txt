Preamble: To resolve the outstanding statutory dues and other financial difficulties for the rehabilitation of the under production spinning mills in the State, financial assistance in the form of loan is sanctioned by the Government. Accordingly, the rehabilitation loan is sanctioned to the co-operative yarn millers as per the terms and conditions prescribed in the decision dated 26.3.1987 in the context of the Government.
Jawahar Shetkari Sahakari Sootagirani Morane, District Dhule is a cooperative yarn mill established during the 6th Five Year Plan. The mill is in financial trouble due to the recession in the textile sector and the burden of the then increased interest rate. The government loan has already been given to the mill to meet the dues from the financial institutions. The loan has not been repaid. To repay this loan, it is necessary to improve the production capacity of the spinning mill and the quality of yarn. Since the textile industry as a whole is in distress due to COVID and the employment of the workers employed in the mill, the mill needs a rehabilitation loan as a special case for the statutory dues and equity of the mill. In view of this and the report of the Commissioner (Textiles), Nagpur, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to finance the mill in the form of a rehabilitation loan for the statutory dues and playing stock of the mill of Jawahar Shetkari Sahakari Sootagirani Morane, District Dhule. During the Financial Year 2023-2024, during the Monsoon Session, July 2023, an amount of Rs. 5.00 crore has been provided in the Head of Accounts under Chapter No.253
