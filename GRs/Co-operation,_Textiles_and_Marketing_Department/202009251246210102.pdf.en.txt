Government's decision:
Commissioner& Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune vide No. 3 here. As per letter dated 16/9/2020, 692 temporary posts (regular posts) in Audit Cell of Cooperation Department have been sanctioned. The deadline has been extended to 31/8/2020. As per the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the audit department has to work as per the requirement, so it is necessary to continue these posts. The government has taken a decision. The said posts are included in the format prescribed under 5/5/2011, subject to the condition that the Government approves the continuation of the said 692 temporary posts from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021.
