The Proposal:
Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 21 posts. Read above. Under Section 3 of the Act, 2 out of 21 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Co-operative Banks will be filled up.
The Government had approved the continuation till 28/02/2021. In view of the expiry of the said extension, accordingly, the Audit Department is directed to work as per the requirement of 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of 2 out of 21 temporary posts created for audit in respect of implementation of Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme of Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks for the period 2021-22. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The following 2 temporary posts created for the purpose of continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Co-operative Banks are functional as per the proposal in Section 2. The extension of these posts from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 is being approved through this Government decision.
