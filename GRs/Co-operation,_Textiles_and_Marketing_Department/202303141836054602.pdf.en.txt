Preamble: The Directorate of Silk has been established by the Government Resolution dated 27.08.1997 in the context of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour and the posts have been transferred from various establishments to the Directorate of Silk. A total of 379 posts in the Directorate of Silk have been sanctioned vide Government Resolution dated 22nd March, 2011 and 22nd March, 2012 respectively.
A total of 303 posts have been made permanent and 76 are temporary.
However, after reviewing the posts in the silk handling establishment, the Government vide its decision dated 09.03.2022 has approved the revised pattern of 311 regular posts and 54 outsourced services. Also, 12 posts of drivers and 56 posts of constables have been declared dead. And it has been approved to count the maximum number of posts as many employees will be working in this dead cadre.
The continuation of 67 temporary posts from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 has been approved by the Government decision dated 26.09.2022 as per the revised figure under the Government decision dated 09.03.2022. A total of 67 temporary posts comprising of 63 temporary posts and 4 temporary posts in the deceased cadre have been proposed to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.

Government's decision:
Finance Department Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 06.03.2023, a total of 67 posts including 63 temporary posts as shown in the accompanying Form on Establishment of Directorate of Silk and 4 posts of non-permanent posts in the deceased cadre are hereby approved to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
The said expenditure shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the respective financial year under the next head of accounts.
