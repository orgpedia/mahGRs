The Proposal:
Under the various schemes sponsored by the National Cooperative Development Corporation for the Cooperative Division under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, the State Government provides Government share and loan to the Cooperative Societies for the construction of projects as per the approved financial plan of that scheme. The funds made available to the state government under this scheme through the National Cooperative Development Corporation are considered as a complete loan on the state government. The National Cooperative Development Corporation, New Delhi has issued a reference letter to the State Government for repayment of the loan sanctioned by NCDC to the State Government upto the year 2019-20. The State Government has been informed vide letter dated 19.06.2020. According to the letter, the principal amount of the loan sanctioned to the state government till 2019-20 is Rs. 7,18,00, 000/- and interest of Rs. 2,57,65, 408/- aggregating to Rs. 9,75,65, 408/- to the National Cooperative Development Corporation. On payment before 20.09.2020, interest of Rs. 23,79,028/- will be exempted. That is Rs. 7,18,00, 000/- in principal and Rs. 2,33,86, 380/- (Rupees Two Lakh Eighty Five only). 9,51,86, 380/- to NCDC. Payment is due by 20.09.2020. Accordingly, Rs. 9,51,86, 380/- (Loan Amount Rs. 7,18,00, 000/-+ Interest Rs. 2,33,86, 380/-) to NSS.
2. Care to be taken in passing payments to the Treasury. A circular dated 10.05.2016 has been issued by the Department of Finance, in which the following information has been given regarding the proposal:
(1) Financial powers of the Department of Finance. As per Circular dated 16.04.2020, funds have been disbursed within the limit of 15 to 25% of the total budgeted amount. The amount under consideration to be disbursed is not in the range of 15 to 25%.
2) Sanctioned Fund:- Rs. 7,18,00, 000/- and Rs. A total of Rs 2,57,00, 000. An amount of Rs. 9,75,00, 000/- has been provided.
