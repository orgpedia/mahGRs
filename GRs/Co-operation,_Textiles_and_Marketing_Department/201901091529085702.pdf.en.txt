The Proposal:
The Director, Pune, Shri. 1 here. According to the circular dated December 16, 2014, electronic forks have been installed in the premises of the market committee. In such places, since the weight of the farm produce is on the electronic fork, it was directed not to charge any kind of tillage there and not to recover the deduction from the payment of the farm produce of the farmers. The government is implementing the order. It was postponed on 22.12.2014. The postponement Petition No. 6238/2016 filed in the Bombay High Court, Mumbai, and Contempt Petition No. 477/2018. Consider the court's ruling. has been raised on 1.12.2018. However, how can the agricultural produce be weighed on electronic weighing sticks as well as on packaged agricultural produce? Since it was necessary to take a decision after considering everything, the government was considering the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of an officer of the rank of Additional Registrar in the zonal office under the control of the Excise Department to make recommendations in this regard.
Government's decision:
A committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of an officer of the rank of Additional Registrar in the Marketing Department to recommend whether or not to levy a levy on the agricultural produce being weighed and packaged on the electronic weighing board in the market committee.
