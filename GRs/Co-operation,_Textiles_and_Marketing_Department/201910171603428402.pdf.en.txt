Government Decision: Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 27 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in the Central Board of Direct Taxes, Mumbai were extended till 30th September, 2019 vide Government Order, dated 12th March, 2019. The proposal for extension of these 26 temporary posts from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
