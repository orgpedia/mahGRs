Find out the reasons and try to make Maharashtra the top sugar producing state again. For this, a committee has been formed at the government level under the chairmanship of the sugar commissioner. Accordingly, the Commissioner of Sugar, Pune, issued a notification. In pursuance of the proposal submitted vide letter dated 18.8.2020, the Government has decided to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Sugar Commissioner, Pune to make efforts to regain the top position in sugar production in Maharashtra.
