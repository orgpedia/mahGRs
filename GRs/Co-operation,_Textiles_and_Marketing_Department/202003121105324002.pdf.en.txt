GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2019-20 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 24.02.2020. The bill has already been approved by the Assembly.
The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been approved by the Cabinet for inclusion in the Appropriation Bill for the financial year ending March 31, 2020. The governor has agreed. And it has been published as Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2020 (Maharashtra Act No. 1 of 2020) in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV dated 04.03.2020.

4.Also, while distributing and spending the fund of supplementary demands, the guidelines and prevailing norms issued earlier by the Planning and Finance Department as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and Financial Rights Rulebook, the delegation of financial powers and the instructions given by the Finance Department from time to time should be strictly followed.
