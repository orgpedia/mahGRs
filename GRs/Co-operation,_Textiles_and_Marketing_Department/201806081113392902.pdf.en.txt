Government's decision:
In point number 3 of the Government Resolution dated 30/12/2017 at Reference No.4, the Cabinet has delegated all the powers to the Empowered Cabinet Sub-Committee to take a decision to change the retail price of Tur to Rs. 55/- (MRP) per kg in the event of a near revision of the MSP of pulses in the open market and to sell Tur to private markets/ malls as there is less possibility of selling all Tur from cheap grain stores.
