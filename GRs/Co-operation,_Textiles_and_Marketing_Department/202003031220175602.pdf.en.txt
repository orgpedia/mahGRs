The Proposal:
Dated 1.4.2015. """ Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme 2019"" is a debt relief scheme for providing relief to the farmers who have taken short term crop loans for the period upto 31.3.2019 and have restructured/ restructured their short term crop loans during this period.""" The decision was announced by the government. The benefit of this scheme should be immediately given to the eligible beneficiaries. For this purpose, Reference No. 2 in the form of Rs. 1,000 crore and reference no. 4 in the form of Rs. 2,500 crore and Reference No. 6 of the Rs. 2,500 crore from the Contingency Fund. 3, 5 and 7 have been issued.
