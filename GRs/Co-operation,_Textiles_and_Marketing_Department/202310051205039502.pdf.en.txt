Government Decision: The Government have extended the tenure of 86 permanent posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative societies up to 31.08.2023 as per the Government Decision, dated 21/02/2023. The proposal for extension of the said 86 temporary posts from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
