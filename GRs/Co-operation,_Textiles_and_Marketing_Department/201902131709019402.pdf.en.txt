2. Agriculture loans (crop loan and medium term loan) have been included in the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana for benefit. The NABARD. In the circular dated 30.3.2012, guidelines have been issued to all banks regarding lending up to a limit of 10% of the limit of crop loan for cultivated area for post-harvest/ domestic requirement. Commercial banks disburse crop loans through Kisan Credit Cards. The crop loan limit of such Kisan Credit Cards also includes the khawati loan. Therefore, commercial banks do not disburse loans to farmers under the head' khawati loan' separately after disbursing crop loans. However, the crop loan at the time when the District Central Cooperative Banks disburse crop loans to the farmers does not include the khawati loan. Therefore, the District Central Cooperative Banks which want to disburse loans to farmers without crop loans should be allowed to do so.

They make such provisions in the potnium. Accordingly, the Siddhudurg District Central Cooperative Bank and Kolhapur District Central Cooperative Bank have changed the potnium and given short-term khawati loans. Government's decision:
The Government has approved the following changes in the scope of the scheme" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017."
1) Under the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana-2017, it is being approved to include the Khoti loan given by the District Central Cooperative Banks for the benefit.
2) Under this scheme, a total of Rs. An estimated expenditure of Rs. 24.04 crore is being approved.
In this case, the responsibility of verifying that such loans were given for the" Khawati Loan" matter while giving the benefit will be of the concerned district sub-registrar, cooperative society. The application for this has to be done online. It will be held from February 14 to February 24, 2019. The scheme is only available to farmers in Sindhudurg and Kolhapur districts. The terms, conditions and criteria set out earlier under this scheme will remain in place for this benefit.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. 87/2019/ Expenditure-2, dated 2.2.2019 of the Department of Finance.
