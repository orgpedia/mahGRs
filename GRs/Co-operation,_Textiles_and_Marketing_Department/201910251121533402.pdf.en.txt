Introduction: Refer Note No. Under Section 2 of the Act, 32 temporary posts in the Agricultural Processing Cooperative Society under the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Pune were allowed to continue till 30/9/2019. Now, the Finance Department is authorized to extend the period from 1/10/2019 to 02/ 09/ 2020. There are 3 administrative departments.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: By this Government Resolution, it is being approved to extend the 31 temporary posts for audit of Agricultural Processing Cooperative Societies under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune from 1/10/2019 to 29/2/2020.
2. The details of the posts extended as above are as per Annexure A to the said Government Resolution.
