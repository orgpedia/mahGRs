Read:- 1) Decision to help the disaster victims affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains between July 2019 and August 2019 The Chief Minister presided over the meeting. 19.8.2019 at 2.00 PM. The meeting of the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
Preamble: To provide relief measures to the flood affected people in the State during the period from July 2019 to August 2019. A cabinet sub-committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. The committee meeting. The meeting was held on 19.8.2019 at 2.00 pm under the Chairmanship of Hon'ble Chief Minister. In the meeting, it was decided to waive off the crop loans of the affected farmers in the districts affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the state from July 2019 to August 2019. The government was contemplating to take a decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
The state government has decided to waive off crop loans of farmers affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the state from July 2019 to August 2019. The government is giving approval to waive crop loans of the above affected farmers subject to the following conditions.
1) The scheme will be applicable to the affected farmers in the affected taluks of the affected districts of Maharashtra state.
2) The affected farmers will be given the benefit of crop loan waiver under this scheme as per the criteria set by the Relief and Rehabilitation Department and the panchnama made by the District Revenue System.
Under this scheme, only primary agricultural credit availed by the affected farmer through cooperative societies, nationalized banks, private banks, rural banks and district central cooperative banks will be eligible for crop loan benefit in Kharif 2019 season.
