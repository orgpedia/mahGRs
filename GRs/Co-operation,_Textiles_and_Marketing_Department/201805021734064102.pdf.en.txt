Preamble: A proposal to approve new cooperative spinning mills in Maharashtra during the Eighth Five Year Plan was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, Prabodhankar Thackeray Weaver Co-operative Weavers Limited. , Solapur, Dist. 2 here. As per the decision of the Government dated 17.3.1997, it was approved to be included in the Eighth Five Year Plan and selected for Government funding. During the same period, most of the cooperative spinning mills included in the Eighth Five Year Plan initiated the process of setting up, actual setting up and production. Solapur, has not made any progress in the construction of the project despite completion of 21 years from the date of registration, as per the provisions of section 102 (1) (a) (i) and (iv) of Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960. The final order for decommissioning of the mill was issued on 22.3.2017.
Against this order, the mill filed a revision application with the government under Section 152 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960. The review petition was heard by the Directorate. Revoking the order of dissolution dated 22.3.2017, the Board of Directors of the mill were ordered to appoint an ad-hoc committee to look after the day-to-day affairs of the institution till the new Board of Directors takes over. It was passed on 21.2.2018. Pursuant to this order, the Director, Textiles, Nagpur, Shri. As per the order dated 27.2.2018, 4 temporary members have been appointed on the Board of Directors. Considering all these factors, the matter of re-selecting the said yarn mill for government financial assistance was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Prabodhankar Thackeray Weaver Co-operative Weavers. Solapur district was included in the Eighth Five Year Plan and was selected for government financial assistance. Now, the government is approving a fresh selection to provide government funding to the yarn mill.
