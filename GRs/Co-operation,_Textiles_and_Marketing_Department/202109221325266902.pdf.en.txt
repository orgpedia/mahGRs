The State Co-operative Election Authority is an independent and autonomous election authority and is responsible for superintendence, operation and control of electoral rolls as per Section 73 (cb) (1) of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, and for declaring the election schedule, conducting physical polls, checking whether the code of conduct is followed during the election, conducting elections in a smooth, peaceful and fearless atmosphere and publishing the electoral rolls on the website of the Authority as per Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Election) Rules, 2014. Also, the State Cooperative Election Authority has been entrusted with the statutory responsibility of conducting elections to all Agricultural Produce Market Committees in the state.
The Authority had submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to create a website for the smooth functioning of the above activities. The Authority submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative and financial combined approval for the work and expenditure of a total amount of Rs 13, 56, 120/- for Cloud Computing and Original Proposal given by Maha IT. In the above case, a meeting of the Project Implementation Committee (PIC) was held on 16.02.2021, wherein administrative and financial approval was accorded for creation of a new website for the State Cooperative Election Authority at a cost of Rs. 7,82,417/- plus cloud computing at a cost of Rs.
Since the State Level State Cooperative Election Authority, which has its controlling office for the elections to the cooperative societies and the Agricultural Produce Market Committee, does not have a website, the government was considering giving administrative approval to start a website for the smooth functioning of the State Cooperative Election Authority.

Government Decision: The Project Implementation Committee in its meeting held on 16th February, 2021 has accorded administrative and financial approval for the construction of new website for State Cooperative Election Authority at a cost of Rs. 7,82,417/- plus cloud computing at a cost of Rs. As per Section 73 (cb) (1) of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the State Cooperative Election Authority is entrusted with the superintendence of the electoral roll, as well as the statutory responsibility of conducting elections to all cooperative societies in the state and the statutory responsibility of conducting elections to all agricultural produce market committees in the state. The State Level State Cooperative Election Authority, which has its controlling office for the elections to the Cooperative Societies and the Agricultural Produce Market Committee, does not have a website, for the smooth functioning of the State Cooperative Election Authority, administrative approval is being given for the launch of a website, Rs. 7,82,417/- for the creation of a new website, and Rs. 5,73,703/- for cloud computing for one year, at a total cost of Rs.
