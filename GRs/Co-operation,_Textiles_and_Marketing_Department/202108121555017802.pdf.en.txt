The Proposal:
The Vita Merchant. Operational Bank Ltd. Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune has given approval for" Special one-time loan repayment scheme" to Vita (Sangli) Bank. The proposal was submitted to the Government on 16.03.2021. Accordingly, the Government vide Reference No. The Government has approved a special one-time loan repayment scheme to this bank till 19.03.2021.
The bank currently has 103 NPA loan accounts and the extension of the scheme will help recover these accounts, reduce the bank's accumulated losses and provide it with more liquidity. Therefore, keeping in mind the interest of the bank, the government was considering extending the scheme.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Pune. The Vita Merchant Co. vide letter dated 26.07.2021. Operational Bank Ltd. The proposal to approve the special one-time loan repayment scheme submitted in respect of Vita (Sangli) Bank is being implemented by The Vita Merchant Co., Ltd., by way of exemption from the provisions of Rule-49 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961, as per the powers vested in the Government under the provisions of Section 157 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960. Operational Bank Ltd. Vita (Sangli) Bank has been granted extension of Special one-time loan repayment scheme mentioned in Annexure A till 31.12.2021.
