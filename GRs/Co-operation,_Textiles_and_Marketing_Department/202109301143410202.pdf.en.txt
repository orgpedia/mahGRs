Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 25/09/2020, the Government had approved the continuation of 118 temporary posts in the offices of the Commissionerate of Sugar and Regional Joint Director (Sugar) under it from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021. Now, the office of the Commissioner of Sugar has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 09.09.2021 to approve the continuation of the said 114 posts with effect from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022.
Accordingly, at present, the proposal to continue these 114 temporary posts from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Approval is being accorded for continuation of 114 temporary posts as shown in Table A and B attached with the 8 Regional Joint Director (Sugar) Offices in the Commissionerate of Sugar and under his control from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022. In the meantime, the offices of the Office of the Administrator (Aastha-1) and Office of the Administrator (5-C) shall, in any case, review the sanctioned posts on their establishment and on the establishment of the offices under their administrative control and obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised figure.
