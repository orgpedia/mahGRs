The Maharashtra State Co-operative Union is a co-operative educational, training, promotion and dissemination organisation in the state of Maharashtra. Dhananjayrao Gadgil Institute of Cooperative Management, Nagpur was established in 1972 by the National Council for Cooperative Training, New Delhi and the cost sharing formula has been prescribed by the Central Government and the State Government in the ratio of 50:50.
A proposal is under consideration of the Government to pay 70% of the amount due to Dhananjayrao Gadgil Cooperative Management Institute, Nagpur for the year 2018-19 on 50% share of the State Government.
Government Decision: Considering this matter, the amount payable to Dhananjayrao Gadgil Cooperative Management Institute, Nagpur, a cooperative training college under the Maharashtra State Cooperative Union, on 50 percent of the state government's share, is Rs. 50.00 lakh out of which 70% i.e. Rs. An amount of Rs. 35.00 lakh is being provided to the Institute. Department of Finance, No.Finance-2018/ P.K.69/ Finance-3, Dy. As per the circular dated 2.04.2018, 70% of the total annual provision i.e. Rs. 35.00 lakhs is being agreed to be disbursed (through BEAMS).
