With a view to encourage the handloom weavers of the state and to promote the best varieties produced by the weavers, the Government of Maharashtra has been implementing the Handloom Textile Competition and Prize Scheme since 1972-73 for the best samples of the fabric produced by the handloom weavers of the state. As per the decision of the Government of Department of Textiles dated 17.10.2016, the Scheme has been restructured and the prize money has also been increased and its annual figure has been approved. The proposal for release of funds from the budgetary allocation for implementation of the BAMSHA Scheme in 2019-20 has been received from the Commissioner of Textiles vide letter dated 28.05.2019. Accordingly, the government had decided to allocate funds for the project.
