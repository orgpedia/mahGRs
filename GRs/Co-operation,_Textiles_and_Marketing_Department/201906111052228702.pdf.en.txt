The Proposal:
Maharashtra Act No. 31 of 2015 was enacted with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely service to the eligible persons in the state of Maharashtra and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their growing expectations from the local administration. It came into effect on 21 August 2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Act, every public authority is required to notify under this Act, within a period of 3 months from the commencement of the Act and thereafter from time to time, the Public Service Designated Officers, First and Second Appellate Authorities and the time limits prescribed.
Accordingly, in accordance with the report sent by the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation, dated 4.7.2015, considering the citizen as the focal point of the various services of the Department of Cooperation under the administrative authority under the Government Decision dated 18.9.2015 in the Reference Roll No. 2 of this Department, the Government on 18.9.2015 notified through the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and the Registrar Cooperative Societies Pune, 5 services that are continuously needed in the daily life of the citizens and which have been taken advantage of by the maximum number of citizens and which are important services.
3. The list of public services to be provided as per Schedule 4 of the said Government Order shall be published by the Cooperation Commissioner on the notice board of the office as well as on its website or portal with the details of time limit, prescribed pattern, designated officers, first appellate officers and second appellate officers.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Act, 2015, No. In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of section 3 of section 5, the office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune, hereby makes the following changes in the composition of the Designated Officers, First and Second Appellate Authorities in respect of the public services being provided to the eligible citizens:
