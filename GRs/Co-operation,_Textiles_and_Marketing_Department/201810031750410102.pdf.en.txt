Preamble: For the offices of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Courts, Mumbai and Co-operative Courts No. 1 to 4, Mumbai, a third floor, fifth and sixth floor space of 14660.15 sq.ft. has been taken on lease at Apeejay House, Dinshaw Vachha Road, Churchgate. The Executive Engineer, South Mumbai Electricity Division, Public Works Department has submitted the budget for giving administrative and financial approval to install the fire alarm system for this office. The proposal was under consideration.
Government Resolution: For the offices of the Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Courts, Mumbai and Co-operative Courts No. 1 to 4, Mumbai, 14660.15 square feet of space has been taken on rent at Apeejay House, Dinshaw Vachha Road, Churchgate, including the third, fifth and sixth floors. The Executive Engineer, South Mumbai Electricity Department, Public Works Department has sanctioned Rs. 7,60,905/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Sixty Thousand Nine Hundred Five only). The Registrar, Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai has requested for administrative and financial approval of the said budget.
