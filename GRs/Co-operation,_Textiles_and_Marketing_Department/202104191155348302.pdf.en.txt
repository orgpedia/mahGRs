The Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project is to be implemented in the state. The total cost of the project is estimated to be US$ 142.9 million over a period of 6 years (approx. 1,000 crore), of which 70% (US$ 100 million) is a loan from the Asian Development Bank.
And 30% of the funding (US$ 42.9 million) will be from state government self-funding. The government was considering opening two separate accounting heads for the scheme- state share and external share.
The government has approved the opening of two separate new accounting heads- state share and external share- for the Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project.
