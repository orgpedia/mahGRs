Finance Department, Government Circular No. No. 64/ Finance-3, dated 15/03/2021 Preamble: As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the State Government has decided to provide financial assistance to the farmers of the State in proportion to the supply of crop loans by the Government to empower the Primary Agricultural Credit Societies for the supply of crop loans. The government has decided to provide financial assistance ranging from 0.5% to 1.5% of the loan disbursement by these institutions every financial year. The primary agricultural credit cooperatives to be financed are bound to meet the conditions/ criteria mentioned in the government decision.
