Preamble The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated 7th October, 2016 to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service.
For this, the Central Government has released the list of posts reserved for Divyangjan as per the notification dated January 4, 2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Decision dated February 2, 2021, regarding the posts of Group-A to Group-D posts fixed by the Central Government in the state government establishments, the posts of which the duties and responsibilities, nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, although the designations of such posts in the list published by the Central Government are different. The posts of Senior Marketing Officer, Machine Managers and Clothing Inspectors in Group-C cadre under this Department have not been included in the posts ensured for the disabled persons as per the notification dated 4th January, 2021 of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. Posts in Government Service for Persons with Disabilities as per order dated 28.01.2021 in case 58/2021
An expert committee has been appointed to look into the matter.
The Expert Committee for Ensuring Posts in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities in its meeting held on 22nd February, 2021 has recommended for ensuring posts in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities in Group-C Cadre in the Commissionerate of Textiles, Nagpur under this Department and accordingly the matter was under consideration of the Government.
