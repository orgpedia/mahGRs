Conditions: 1) Before starting the work, the sample map, as well as the detailed map, should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
2) While working on the provision made in the general form, a detailed budget should be prepared and the work should be taken up only after obtaining the approval of the competent authority/ engineer concerned.
3) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No. 177/ T. Action should be taken as per the guidelines mentioned in No. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
4) Action should be taken under the Government Decision, Public Works Department No. BDG-2017/ PR No. 60/ Ima-2, dated 07.07.2017.
5) The tender notice of the work should be published in the leading newspaper only after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
6) Do not start construction without a budget.
7) It should be ensured that the area of the proposed building is in accordance with the permissible standards of the staff.
8) The concerned Controlling Officer shall get the architectural plans and working plans prepared by the Chief Architect and the necessary certificates, approval and no-objection certificates from the Municipal Corporation, similar authorities etc. obtained before starting the construction.
9) The electrification and other ancillary works should be properly planned and completed during the construction period.
10) Action should be taken in accordance with the instructions given by the government from time to time regarding the various facilities to be made available in the building considering the disabled persons and senior citizens.
11) Thematic work will not require revised administrative approval, so special care should be taken in this regard.
The cost of construction shall be covered under the head of Demand No. H-8," Capital Expenditure on Public Works 4059." Detailed article titles and computer statistics in this regard will be issued separately.
