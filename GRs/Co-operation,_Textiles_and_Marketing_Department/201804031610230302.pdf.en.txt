Preamble: As per the revision of the permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the relevant Government Decision No. 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes 490 posts. Further, 26 functional and 464 vacant temporary posts out of 490 created for continuous and concurrent audit of all District Central Cooperative Banks under the aforesaid Reference No. 5 have been filled up. The Government had approved the continuation till 28/2/2018. Since the above extension is expiring, No. As per letter No.2, a proposal has been submitted to the Government through the office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune to work as per the requirement of the Audit Department in accordance with the 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of 28 working and 462 vacant temporary posts for the period 2018-19. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the proposal, 26 functional and 464 vacant temporary posts out of 490 created for continuous and concurrent audit of all district central cooperative banks will be given. from 01/03/2018. The extension is being approved by this Government through a decision dated 30/09/2018.
