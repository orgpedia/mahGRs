Section 24-A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 provides that every institution shall conduct cooperative education and training for its directors, members, officers and employees through the State Federal Institutions or State Apex Training Institutions as may be specified by the State Government by notification in the Official Gazette.
As per the Economic Survey 2020-21 of Maharashtra, as on 31st March, 2020, there are about 2.12 lakh cooperative societies in the State with a total membership of about 5.5 crore. The number of directors of these institutions is more than 25 lakh and the number of employees is about 3.5 lakh. As per Section 24A of the Co-operative Act, it is mandatory for a co-operative society to impart co-operative education and training to its members, directors and officers/ employees.
In view of the above, for recognition of Cooperative Training Institutions as State Apex Training Institutions, Reference No. 1 The criteria are set out in the circular. As per Section 24-A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, some institutions in the state have been notified as State Apex Training Institutes. However, in view of the large number of members of cooperative societies in the state, and the obligation to train these members at least once in 5 years, it is still necessary to notify some institutions as training institutions. Karad Urban Co-op Bank Ltd. The proposal to notify the Institute as State Apex Training Institute under Section 24-A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 was under consideration of the Government.

In this regard, in pursuance of the decision taken in the meeting dated 13.06.2016 of the Committee examining the proposal of the Institutions to be notified as State Apex Training Institutions for imparting cooperative education and training as per Section 24-A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Government has decided as under in the proposal submitted by the Commissioner for Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune vide letter No. 2 (i).
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 24-A of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, it is hereby notified that" The Karad Urban Co-op Bank Ltd. The Government hereby approve to notify the Institute as State Apex Training Institute, Karad. A separate notification in this regard is being issued. Approval is subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The Karad Urban Co-op Bank Ltd. The Co-operative Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Pune should immediately change their bye-laws to" Entire Maharashtra Area of Operation" in their area of operation and get the approval of the Co-operative Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Pune.
The Institute shall enter into a registered lease agreement for a period of at least five years in respect of the building, hostel, accommodation or other facilities of the training centre.
After this, the agreement should be made for the next five years and its report should be submitted to the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune.
(3) A quarterly report on the training provided by the above institution should be submitted to the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune and a copy of the report should be sent to the Government.
