Preamble: The Government have decided to provide incentive concession to the farmers of the State in connection with the recovery of interest rates by the Cooperative Agricultural Credit Societies vide Government Resolution dated 24/11/1988. Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 02/11/1991, the Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Interest Subvention Scheme has been made applicable to the farmers who have taken crop loans from 01/04/1990. Under this scheme, interest subvention is being provided to farmers who take crop loans from cooperative agricultural credit institutions and repay it in full by June 30 every year.
The scheme is also applicable to farmers taking loans from nationalised banks, rural banks and private banks. However, this scheme is not applicable to overdue loans, as well as medium-term and long-term loans. Under this scheme, interest subvention is being provided to farmers linked to the interest rate on crop loans availed by cooperative agricultural credit societies and banks.
