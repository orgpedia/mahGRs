Preamble: In order to provide financial assistance to the soybean farmers of the state in the Kharif season 2016-2017, a subsidy has been sanctioned to the farmers who sold soybean in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee of the state from the month of October, 2016 to December, 2016 at the rate of Rs.200/- per quintal and a maximum of 25 quintal per farmer as per the Government decision, dated 10th January, 2017. In addition, farmers who have sold soybean to 11 private market committees and 16 direct marketing licensees under the government decision, dated August 8, 2017, have also been included in this subsidy scheme. In addition, four more Private Market Committees have been included as per the Government Decision dated 8th March, 2019. A total of 15 private market committees and 16 direct marketing

Out of this, a sum of Rs. 396.12 lakh has been sanctioned in the second (Monsoon) Session of 2019 through Supplementary Demands for Grants to the farmers who have sold soybean to 3 Private Market Committees and 5 Direct Marketing Licensees received from the Director of Marketing, State of Maharashtra, Pune. The matter of distributing the funds received through supplementary demand to the concerned market committees and direct marketing licensees was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is providing Rs. 396.12 lakh (Rupees Three Crores Ninety Six Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) for disbursement of sanctioned soybean subsidy to 11 Private Market Committees and 16 Direct Marketing Licensees approved as per Government Decision dated 8th August, 2017.

The Government is authorizing the Accountant, Director of Marketing, Office, Maharashtra State Pune as the Drawing and Distribution Officer and Director of Marketing, Maharashtra State, Pune as the Controlling Officer to scrutinize all the proposals received under this scheme and distribute the subsidy to the eligible beneficiaries district-wise and to withdraw and distribute the amount sanctioned in the government decision to the concerned.
The Director of Marketing shall disburse the subsidy amount from the Government treasury to the eligible beneficiary farmers on behalf of the Government before 31st August, 2019 and submit a report to the Government.
