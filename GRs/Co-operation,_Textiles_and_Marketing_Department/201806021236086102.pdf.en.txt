The Proposal:
The state government has been providing relief to the farmers who have been reeling under debt burden due to continuous drought and drought since 2009-10. As per the approval of the Cabinet in its meeting held on 24.6.2017, Govt. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana-2017 has been announced on 28.6.2017.
