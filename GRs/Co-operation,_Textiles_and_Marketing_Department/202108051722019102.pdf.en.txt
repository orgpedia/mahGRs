The proposal:
Considering the difficulties in the marketing of agricultural produce to the farmers during the lockdown period due to the COVID-19 situation in the state in April and May 2021 and the FRP still due to the farmers from the sugar mills, the District Central Cooperative Banks and Commercial Banks of the state have decided to recover the crop loans given to the farmers in the year 2020-21. Read the extension till 31/07/2021 The decision was announced by the government.
