Government's decision:
770 temporary posts in the audit cell of the Cooperative Department under reference No. 3 here. Further continuation up to 28/02/2019 has been approved vide Governance Decision dated 14/03/2019.
The reference No. Since 148 posts of Group-D cadre in the Audit Cell have been abolished as per the decision of the Government here, a request for sanction of 692 posts excluding 78 posts in the regular scheme has been made in the reference No. 4 has been proposed. As per the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the audit department has to work as per the requirement, so it is necessary to continue these posts. The government has taken a decision. The said posts are included in the format prescribed under 05/05/2011, subject to the condition that the said 692 temporary posts are not filled up. Government approval is being given to continue from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020. Details of the posts are as follows:
