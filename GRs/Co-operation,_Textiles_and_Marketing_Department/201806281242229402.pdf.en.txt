The Proposal:
Shri Shrikant Namdeo Maindale, Textile Specialist Class-I, Directorate of Textile Industries, Nagpur, has issued Reference No. The application dated 29th May, 2018, hereby gives a prior notice of voluntary retirement from Government service on family grounds.
Director (Textiles), Directorate of Textiles, Government of Maharashtra, Nagpur vide Reference No. Shri Shrikant Namdeo Maindale, Textile Specialist Class-I has submitted his application for voluntary retirement vide letter dated 30th May, 2018 to the Government.
Therefore, the matter of taking voluntary retirement of Shri Shrikant Namdeo Maindale, Textile Specialist Class-I and relieving him from government service was under the consideration of the government. The government has issued orders to this effect:
Government order:
In accordance with the provisions of Rule 66 (4) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 and the Government Order dated 2nd June, 2003 and the Government Notification dated 12th April, 2016 of the Finance Department, Shri Shrikant Namdeo Maindale, Textile Specialist Class-I, Directorate of Textiles, Nagpur has been advised to take voluntary retirement from service with effect from 29th June, 2018. The government is giving approval to release from government service.
2. Director (Textiles) has certified that no departmental enquiry is pending against Shri Shrikant Namdeo Maindale, Textile Expert Class-I and no official communication is pending from him.
