The Proposal:
A Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Finance) has been constituted to fix the rates of collateral cost for procurement under Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme under the Government Order, dated 3/9/2019. The Committee in its meeting held on Thursday, 12.1.2023, discussed in detail the determination of the transport rates for the season 2022-2023 and other related issues. As per the decision taken in the meeting, the matter of fixing the transportation rate and fixing the transportation rate for Osmanabad and Latur districts from the season 2022-23 on the lines of adjoining Nanded and Parbhani districts as well as approving the proposal of transportation rate jointly submitted by NAFED, Mumbai and Panan Mahasangh, Mumbai was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Steps should be taken to fix the rates for transportation of pulses and oilseeds procured under the Minimum Support Price (MSP) scheme from the procurement centre to the warehouse of the Warehousing Corporation as follows:
