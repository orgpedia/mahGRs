Preamble: On the representation made by Maharashtra State Mazdoor Sahakari Sangh and other District Mazdoor Sahakari Sangh regarding the various problems/ demands of the Mazdoor Cooperative Societies and to take policy decisions with the aim of improving the functioning of the Mazdoor Cooperative Societies and solving the problems faced by the institutions, Shri. A meeting was held at the Government level on 15/09/2022 under the Chairmanship of Minister (Cooperation). In pursuance of the directions of this meeting, the Government Order dated 14/10/2022 in Reference No. 2 was issued for constitution of the Committee. The Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Upper Registrar (Inspection& Elections) had submitted its report to the Government vide Reference No. 3 dated 30/11/2022.
The Commissioner for Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune had conveyed their views to the Government through a letter at Reference No. 4 on this report. The report of the committee constituted, the opinion of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperatives, Cooperative Societies, Pune, the demands/ objections of the labor cooperatives were all under the consideration of the government to take a decision.
Government's decision:
Based on the report of the committee formed on the problems/ demands of the labor cooperatives, the following decisions are being taken.
