Preamble: Scanning and Digitization of records of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai and Co-operative Courts, Mumbai Rs. The proposal for grant of administrative sanction for expenditure of Rs. 2,95,75, 000/- has been submitted by the Chairman, Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai. A proposal for administrative and financial approval of the expenditure was under consideration.
Governing Decision: The Chairman, Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai has sanctioned Rs. 2,95,75, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand only) has been requested for administrative and financial sanction.
Scanning and Digitization of records of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai and Co-operative Courts, Mumbai at a cost of Rs. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 2,95,75, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand only).
As per the order dated 4.05.2018. The High Court directed the Chairperson, Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai to send a proposal to the State Government for necessary grant for scanning and digitization of records of Co-operative Appellate Courts and Co-operative Courts.
Accordingly, in pursuance of the proposal received, the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles has been allocated Rs. 3 crore has been sanctioned. The exact number of pages to be scanned during this process should be determined under the guidance of experts. The proposal should include tagging, retraining and indexing.
A consultant should be appointed for this purpose and e-tendering process should be carried out, feedback of Directorate of Information and Technology should be taken on the tender draft.
