The Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project has been approved to be implemented in all the districts of the state for a period of 6 years as per the Government Decision Cooperation, Marketing and Textile Department No. Kripa Manam-0118/ PR No. 13/21-S dated 30.9.2020. The State share for the year 2022-23 for the Magnet project is Rs. 100.74 crore and Rs. 155.25 crore. Rs 255.99 crore has been allocated in the Budget. The government was planning to allocate 20% of the funds.
Government Decision: Out of the amount disbursed for the Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project in the financial year 2021-22, the remaining amount of Rs. 12,02,90, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Two Lakh Ninety Thousand only) from the State Share (4435 0048) of the budgetary provision for the financial year 2022-23 subject to the condition of depositing the same in the Government Accounts with interest. 20,14,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Crores Fourteen Lakhs only) and External Share (4435 0057). 31,05,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty One Crore Five Lakhs Only) aggregating to Rs.
