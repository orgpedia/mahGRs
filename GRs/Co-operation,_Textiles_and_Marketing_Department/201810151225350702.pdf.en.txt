Preamble: As per Article 243ZK (2) of the 97th Constitutional Amendment Act of the Central Government, it was provided for the creation of an independent election authority to conduct and monitor/ control elections to all cooperative societies in the State. Accordingly, vide notification dated 30/03/2013, in line with the State Election Commission under Section 73C of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Government has decided to constitute a separate State Cooperative Election Authority for conducting elections to the Cooperative Societies in the State and create 47 posts for the same.
The Cabinet has approved the same in its meeting dated 30/1/2013.
The decision was taken at a high-level meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary. As per the decision of the Government, out of the 47 posts approved by the Cabinet, the creation of the remaining 44 posts excluding 3 posts has been approved. Accordingly, the functioning of the State Co-operative Election Authority has commenced from 26/05/2014. Then read the government's decision. 44 temporary positions.
