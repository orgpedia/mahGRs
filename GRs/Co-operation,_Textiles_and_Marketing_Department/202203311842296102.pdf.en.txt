Government Resolution It is proposed to approve the reallocation under the programme/ mandatory provisions in the year 2021-22 under the head of Demand Number V-2," 2070 Other Administrative Services, 2425-Sahakar, 2435-Other Agricultural Programmes, 2851-Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 2852-Industries, 3451 Secretarial Financial Services" under the control of Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
Under the year 2021-22, under Demand Rank V-2, Principal Accounts Head" 2425- Cooperation, 2435- Other Agricultural Programmes, 2851- Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 2852- Industries, 3451- Secretarial Financial Services (Under Programme/ Compulsory), Rs. 30753.14 lakhs (Rupees Three Hundred Seven Crores Fifty Three Lakh Four Thousand Only) as shown in the attached statement-2, the Government is approving the reallocation for the scheme as shown in the attached statement-1 under Head of Accounts, 2070 Other Administrative Services (Compulsory), 2435-Other Agricultural Programmes (Under Programmes).
This Government has taken this decision in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments under paragraphs 162, 163, 164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules, 1977 (Part-I). Additional Chief Secretary (Cooperation), Sh. It is being issued with the approval of the Principal Secretary (Textiles) Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department.
