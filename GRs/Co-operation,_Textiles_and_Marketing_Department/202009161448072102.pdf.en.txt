Introduction: Refer Note No. Under Section 3, a total of 52 temporary posts, including 39 temporary posts under Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under Commissioner Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune, were approved to be continued till 31/08/2020. Now, the Finance Department is authorized to extend the period from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021. There are 4 administrative departments. Accordingly, a total of 52 temporary posts including 39 posts under Commissioner (Textiles), Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune have been extended from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021.
Government's decision:
By this Government decision, a total of 52 temporary posts, 39 temporary posts under the Commissioner (Textiles) Nagpur and 13 temporary posts under the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune, have been filled up. The extension is being approved from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021.
