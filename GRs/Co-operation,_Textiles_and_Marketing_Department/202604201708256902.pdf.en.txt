The Proposal: -
The meeting was chaired by the Chief Minister. At the State Summit meetings on 14/07/2025, discussions were held on implementing various initiatives under the International Year of Cooperation-2025. The meeting directed the Department of Cooperation to appoint an expert committee to make necessary changes in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 from time to time, considering the challenges in the cooperative sector in the changing circumstances. The Chief Minister has given.
With a view to bring about favourable changes in the cooperative law of the state, the Cooperative Commissioner B, headed by the Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune, has been appointed. The committee has been constituted as per the Government Order dated 02/01/2026. The government was considering revamping the committee.
Government Orders: -
The government has issued orders to make favourable changes in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961 and the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies (Election of Committees) Rules, 2014. The Government is approving the inclusion of the following member in the High Level Committee constituted under 02/01/2026 under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operatives, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The Government Order is available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra, www.maharashtra.gov.in.
