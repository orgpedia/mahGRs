Government Resolution It is proposed to approve the reallocation of funds from the provisions under the programme for the year 2022-23 under the head Demand No. V-3," 4425, Capital Expenditure on Cooperation, under the control of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
The capital expenditure under the head" 4435 Other Agricultural Programmes" (under the programme) under the head Principal Accounts 2022-23 is Rs. 7241.99 lakhs (Rupees Seventy Two Crores Forty One Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand only) as shown in the attached Statement-1 along with savings as shown in the attached Statement-2 in the Head of Accounts" 4425, Capital Expenditure on Cooperation" (under the Programme).
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under paragraph 162,163,164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules Book (Part-I), 1977 and with the approval of the M.A.M.S. (Co-operation and Marketing), Co-operative Marketing and Textile Department.
