Preamble: Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 21 posts. Read above. Under Section 3 of the Act, 2 out of 21 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Co-operative Banks will be filled up.
The Government had approved the continuation till 29/02/2020. In view of the expiry of the said extension, in pursuance of the 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of the 21 temporary posts created for the audit of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank for the period 2020-21, the Audit Department has been asked to work as per the requirement. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operative Societies. The State of Maharashtra, Pune. The following 2 temporary posts created for the purpose of continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Co-operative Banks are functional as per the proposal in Section 2. The extension of these posts from 01/03/2020 to 28/02/2021 is being approved through this Government decision.
