Preamble: In line with the Government's policy to promote post-harvest processing in the State in order to provide market access to the silk produced by the silk farmers of the State, the Government has approved the grant from the District Annual Plan for the production of silk yarn by Multi-End Reeling Unit, Automatic Reeling Unit and Tasser Reeled Yarn. The criteria, guidelines and modalities for implementation of the scheme have been prescribed vide letter dated 30th August, 2018. Now, in terms of the letter dated 04.07.2019 of the Planning Department, the matter of determining the modalities was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The following modalities are being prescribed to implement the Silk Yarn Production Scheme from the District Annual Plan: 1) Sericulture Officers, Grade-1/ Grade-2, concerned District Silk Offices will be the functional machinery at the district level to implement the scheme.
The Silk Development Officer, Grade-1/ Grade-2, of the concerned district will have the power to give technical approval to the proposal for providing subsidy to silk yarn production under the scheme and the concerned Collector will have the power to give administrative and financial approval.
