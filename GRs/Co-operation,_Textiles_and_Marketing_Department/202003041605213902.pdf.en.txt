The Proposal:
Under the Scheme for Financial Assistance for Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (2425 2524) under the Small and Marginal Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) in the State, the State Government approves 2.5 per cent interest subvention to the District Central Cooperative Banks for making available crop loans to farmers at 6 per cent interest rate through the District Central Banks.
