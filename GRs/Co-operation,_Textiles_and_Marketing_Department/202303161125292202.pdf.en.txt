GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government Decision dated 16.09.2022 in Reference No.2 above, the Government had approved the continuation of 59 temporary posts of Audit Staff in Urban Cooperative Banks till 28.02.2023. A total of 59 posts were under the consideration of the government for the year 2023-24 in accordance with the proposal submitted by the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Under the process of fixing the revised figure of the sanctioned permanent/ temporary posts under the department, the revised figure of the posts of continuous and concurrent auditor of the civil cooperative banks has been approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries and the Government has issued a decision in this regard. Rajah 1008/ Q. No. 538/5-S, Dy. Issued on 05.05.2011. This includes 59 posts. In view of the changes in the law as per the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the proposal for the re-employment of a total of 3995 posts in the audit department is under examination as per the approval in the figure, noting that this scrutiny will take time, the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government to continue 59 posts for the audit of the Urban Cooperative Bank in 2023-24. Department of Finance As per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments vide Government Order dated 08.02.2023, 59 temporary posts in the Statement-A for Audit of Urban Co-operative Bank have been sanctioned. w.e.f. 01.03.2023 It is hereby approved by the Government to continue till 31.08.2023.
3. The Co-operative Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take full care of the collection of audit fee. Also, the process of collection of audit fee equivalent to the minimum salary of the employees for audit should be done immediately and the total accumulated arrears of audit fee should be fully recovered at the time of submission of the proposal for further extension.
4. The district-wise allocation of posts in respect of the above mentioned urban banks is shown in the accompanying prospectus' A'. The details of the withdrawal and disbursement officer-wise allocation of these posts should be sent directly to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Subordinate and Accounts Officer, Mumbai and the concerned District Treasurer by the Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune and a copy of it should be sent to the Government for information.
5. Expenditure on all the above staff should be met from the provision collected by the respective cooperative societies in the form of audit fee. The Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune should ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
