Under the various schemes sponsored by the National Cooperative Development Corporation under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, the State Government provides Government share and loan to the Cooperative Societies for the construction of projects as per the approved financial plan of that scheme. The funds made available to the state government under this scheme through the National Cooperative Development Corporation are considered as a loan on the state government. The National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC), under reference, has been mandated to reimburse to the State Government, the principal amount of loan eligible for repayment and the interest payment instalments on the sanctioned loan, out of the loan sanctioned by NCDC upto 2018-19. 1.07.2019 to the State Government. According to the letter, the principal amount of the loan sanctioned to the State Government till 2018-19 is Rs. 17,93,31, 600/- and interest of Rs. 6,58,46, 500/- aggregating to Rs. 24,51,78, 100/- to the National Cooperative Development Corporation. On payment made before 20.09.2019, interest of Rs. 56,92,628/- will be exempted. That is Rs. 17,93,31, 600/- in principal and Rs. 6,01,53, 872/- towards interest. 23,94,85, 472/- to NCDC. Payment is required by 20.09.2019. Therefore Rs. 23,94,85, 472/- (principal of loan Rs. 17,93,31, 600/-+ interest Rs. 6,01,53, 872/-) to the NSS.
