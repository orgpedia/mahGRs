Regarding the formation of a committee to organize the departmental examination of gazetted officers in the Department of Cooperation.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles
Government Resolution No.Rajas-2021/ P.No. 252/5 S
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400032.
Date: December 2, 2021
Reference:- General Administration Department, Government Order No. 2620/ No. 19/ No. 17, Dy. 22/9/2020
Preamble: Under the General Administration Department. As per the Government Order dated 22/9/2020, all the Administrative Departments under which the Departmental Examinations for the officers were being conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission have been asked to conduct their Departmental Examinations at their level from the year 2021. The departments have also been asked to set up separate cells for conducting these examinations in a time-bound manner. Since the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will not conduct departmental examinations from the year 2021, the government was considering the formation of a committee at the level of Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune to conduct the departmental examination of gazetted officers in the cooperative department.
Government Decision: Since the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will not be conducting departmental examinations from the year 2021, the following committee is being formed at the level of Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune to conduct the departmental examination of gazetted officers in the cooperative department:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.       | Chairman             |
| 2. | Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies (Administration), Headquarters, Pune. | the members          |
| 3. | Sub-Registrar Cooperative Societies (Establishment), Headquarters, Pune.         | The Member Secretary |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The functions to be performed by the Committee in the aforesaid composition shall be as follows: 1. Selection of question papers for the examination and receiving the question paper set from them in a sealed cover.
2. Select the question paper of each paper from the question papers received from the candidates selected for the examination.
3. Determining the responsibility of confidentiality of the set of question papers.
4) Keep the answer sheets safe and confidential till the results are out.
5.To finalize the score and result of this examination.
