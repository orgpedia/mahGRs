GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 0722/ PR No. 114/18-C
Preamble: The State Government under the Government Resolution No. 1
The Government has decided to provide financial assistance in proportion to the crop loan supply to empower Primary Agricultural Credit Societies to provide credit to farmers. The Government has decided to provide financial assistance in the ratio of 0.75 to 2% of the loan disbursement made by these institutions every financial year. The primary agricultural credit cooperatives to be financed are bound to meet the conditions/ criteria mentioned in the government decision.
