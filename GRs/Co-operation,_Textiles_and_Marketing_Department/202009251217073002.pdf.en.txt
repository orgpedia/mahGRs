Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 26 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in the Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs), Mumbai, were extended till 31st August, 2020 vide Government Order, dated 22nd July, 2020. The proposal for extension of these 26 temporary posts from 1st September, 2020 to 28th February, 2021 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
