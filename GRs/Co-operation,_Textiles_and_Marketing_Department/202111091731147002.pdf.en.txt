3. Finance Department Circular dated 20.07.2021 Preamble: During the four consecutive years from 2015-16 to 2018-19, a large number of payments of less than 50 paise were released due to drought like situation in various parts of the State.
The farmers of the state have been badly affected due to scanty rainfall in the state. Due to the loss of farmers in the wake of this natural disaster, the loan related to agriculture could not be repaid in time for a few years. As a result, the farmers are in debt and are unable to get loans for their crops. To get farmers out of the vicious cycle of debt. The Chief Minister has announced the" Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme 2019" in the Winter Session, 2019. Accordingly, the Government have issued a Government Resolution (No. 1).
Under the Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers' Debt Waiver Scheme 2019 (State Level) (Programme), (24350142), 33 financial assistance items for the implementation of this scheme were under the consideration of the Government to disburse funds as per the demand of the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune for the financial year 2021-22.
