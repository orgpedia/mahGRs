The Union Cabinet chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has given its approval for implementation of Central Sector Scheme" Silk Samagra-2" during 15th Finance Commission period (2021-22 to 2025-26).
The scheme will be implemented by the Central Silk Board for research and development, capacity building and training, strengthening of silkworm seed production, marketing system and quality certification system etc. for the beneficiaries of the silk sector in the state. In order to achieve the objectives envisaged under the scheme, the Central Silk Board has reconstituted two Committees, namely, (i) Project Control Committee (PMC) headed by Director, Silk, of the concerned State and (ii) State Level Silk Coordination Committee (SLSCC) headed by Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary. Nominations of silk and other allied departments and entrepreneurs are required to be made on these committees. Following this, the Director (Reshim) gave the reference. As proposed vide letter dated 27.04.2022, State Level Silk Coordination Committee (SLSCC) and Project Controlling Committee (PMC) are being constituted as follows:
