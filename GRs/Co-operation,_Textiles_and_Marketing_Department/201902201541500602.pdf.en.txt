Government's stand:
In the season 2017-2018, under the Minimum Support Price Scheme, financial assistance in the amount of Rs.1000 per quintal has been sanctioned to the farmers who have registered on the NeML portal for the purchase of Tur and Urad but have not purchased Tur and Urad from them as per the Government Decisions dated 19/06/2018 and 01/11/2018 respectively. Subject to the conditions (f) of the Government Decision No. 2 and No. 3 relating to the disbursement of the said grant, the revised conditions shall read as under:
Revised Condition Number:- The subsidy should be disbursed to the farmers registered on the NeML portal through the portal itself in their available bank accounts.
