Introduction: Under the National Agriculture Development Scheme, a Silk Treasure Market will be set up at Mauje Hiraj G. Solapur. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 15.11.2018, an expenditure of Rs. 694.27 lakh was administratively approved. Accordingly, the Directorate of Sericulture had received funds to the tune of Rs. 694.00 lakh from the Department of Agriculture for setting up this market during 2018-19. Of this, the Directorate of Silk had transferred Rs 647.80 lakh to the Public Works Department, Solapur, and the Directorate of Silk had returned Rs 45.75 lakh to the Agriculture Department as the construction of the market building was not completed. The remaining₹ 0.45 lakh (₹ 694 lakh-₹ 647.80-₹ 45.75 lakh) has been spent towards audit fees.
The entire fund has been spent for the construction of the main building as the impact of some aspects has increased while working as per the administrative approval. As a result, the provision for furniture and other items in administrative sanction is getting reduced. The Administrative Sanction Order has been received as per Schedule 2017-18 and the Technical Sanction is for 2018-19.
