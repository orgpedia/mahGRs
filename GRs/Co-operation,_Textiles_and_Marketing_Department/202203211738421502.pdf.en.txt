Introduction: Read the above. 10 As per the Government decision dated 30.09.2021, the Government had approved the continuation of 114 temporary posts in the offices of the Commissionerate of Sugar and Regional Joint Director (Sugar) under it from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022. As per the letter dated 01/03/2022, the Sugar Commissioner, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to approve the continuation of these 114 posts. Accordingly, at present, the proposal to continue these 114 temporary posts from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval is being given for continuation of 114 temporary posts as shown in Table A and B attached with the 8 Regional Joint Director (Sugar) Offices in the Commissionerate of Sugar and under its control from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022. In the meantime, the offices of the office bearers (Aastha-1) and office bearers (5-C) are required to review the sanctioned posts in their establishment and in the establishment of the offices under their administrative control in any case and get the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised pattern. It should be noted that after this, the period for determining the revised figure will not be extended. The Office Bearers (Aastha-I) and Office Bearers (5-C) should review the posts in the establishment of the Administrative Department (itself) as well as the establishment of the Regional Office under their control, plan to submit the proposal for the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately and take action immediately and take care to get the approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee of the revised figure.
The expenditure on this should be shown under the head Demand No." V-2, 2425, Cooperation (01) (08) Strengthening of Sugar Commissionerate (24251054) 01-Salary" and should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2022-23.
