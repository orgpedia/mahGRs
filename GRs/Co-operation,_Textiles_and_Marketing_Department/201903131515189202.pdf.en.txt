The Proposal:
Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of the permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the 26 posts presented in the figure. The above reference No. 26 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies under Section 3 of the Act. The Government had approved the continuation till 28/02/2019. In view of the expiry of the said extension, in pursuance of the 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of the 26 temporary posts created for audit in the Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies for the period 2019-20, the Audit Department has been asked to work as per the requirement. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies. Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the proposal in section 2, the following 26 temporary posts created for audit in connection with the implementation of the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme of the Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies will be given to the following: from 01/03/2019. The extension is being granted by this Government decision till 30/09/2019.
