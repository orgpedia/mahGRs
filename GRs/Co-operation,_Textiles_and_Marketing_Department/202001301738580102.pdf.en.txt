The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles
GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. ASNI- 2019/1028/ P.No. 131/22-C,
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry (Extension), Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: January 30, 2020.
Read:- Letter No. of the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation: JA No. SAA/ PRASA-2/ SASAKA/ A. CSI/ 2019, dated 24.12.2019
Preamble: The matter of cancellation of appointments of Government nominated Directors of various Cooperative Sugar Mills during the period from November, 2014 to October, 2019 was under consideration of the Government in accordance with the provisions of Section 73AA of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 and the revised order issued accordingly.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of Section 73AA of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 and the revised order issued accordingly, the appointments of Government nominated Directors to various Cooperative Sugar Mills under the following heads are hereby cancelled. The Sugar Commissioner's Office, Pune, should immediately implement this order and submit a compliance report to the government immediately.
