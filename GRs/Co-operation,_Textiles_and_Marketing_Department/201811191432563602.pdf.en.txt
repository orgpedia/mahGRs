The Proposal:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the circular dated 4.6.1992, instructions were issued to collect 20% of the audit fee payable to the auditors appointed for the audit of cooperative societies as administrative fee and pay it to the government. The circular was issued by the Maharashtra Certified Auditors Association and others. The said circular was challenged in the Supreme Court, Bombay. Order dated 14.10.2004 The Supreme Court in SLP No. 2353-2354/2005, SLP No. 16820/2005, SLP No. 17549/2005 were filed. The SLP was filed in 2013 under Civil Appeal No. 82,83,84 and 85 respectively.
While deciding on. The Supreme Court has directed that 20% of the audit fee be refunded to the auditor within four weeks. Ordered on 13.7.2017. The Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has requested the Government under their Reference No. 1.
Government's decision:
Considering this, 20% of the audit fee collected to be refunded to the respective auditors is Rs. 36,54,000/- has been approved in the 2nd (Monsoon) Session, 2018 through Supplementary Demands and in the context of Department of Finance vide No. According to the circular, Rs 3. 36,54,000/- is hereby approved for disbursement through BEAMS.
