As per the provisions of Section 154-A (1) of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Council, Pune has been reconstituted with Mr. Vidyadhar Vamanrao Anaskar, Pune as Chairman (MoS Status) vide Reference No. (1) The decision was made by the Government. The term of office of the Council shall be for a period of three years from 06.08.2021 or until further orders, whichever is less. The matter of cancellation of the appointment of Mr. Anaskar to the post of President (Minister of State Status) in the Maharashtra State Cooperative Council was under the consideration of the Government vide Government Resolution No. 1.
As per the provisions of Section 154-A (1) of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Council, Pune is headed by Mr. Vidyadhar Vamanrao Anaskar, Pune as its Chairman (MoS Status). (1) The Government have hereby decided to cancel the appointment made under the Government Decision from the date of the Government Decision. The Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune should immediately implement the order and submit a compliance report to the government immediately.
