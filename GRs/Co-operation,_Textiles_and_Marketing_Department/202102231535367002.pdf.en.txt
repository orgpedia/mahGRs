GOVERNMENT DECISION: The 14 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Farmers Cooperative Union, Kolhapur in the state were extended till 28th February, 2021 as per the Government Decision, dated 25/09/2020 in the above mentioned (2). (1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
