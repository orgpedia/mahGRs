Preamble: The Central Government vide notification dated 06/07/2023, has notified the Minimum FRP Sugarcane Rate for the crushing season 2023-24. In view of the sugarcane price announced through this notification, the FRP sugarcane price announced for the crushing season 2022-23 has been revised. In view of these changes and the directions in the Government Resolution dated 21/02/2022, the Government has decided as follows, that till the final sugar cane price for the season 2023-24 is decided, it is necessary to fix the minimum sugar cane price to be supported while fixing the initial FRP cane price for sugarcane crushed from the start of the season.
Government Decision Until the final sugar cane price for the crushing season 2023-24 and beyond is determined, the sugar cane price to be supported at the time of payment of the initial FRP cane price for the cane distilled since the start of the season has been determined as follows.
