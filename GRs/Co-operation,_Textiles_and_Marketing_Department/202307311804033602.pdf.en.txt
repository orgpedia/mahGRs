Read- 1) Government Circular No: Finance Department, Government Circular No. Supplementary-2023/ No. 62/ Budget-3, No. 28.07.2023 GOVERNOR'S DECISION- Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2023-24 was presented to the Legislature on 17th July, 2023. This supplementary expenditure has now been approved by the Legislative Assembly. The governor has agreed. And it has been published as Maharashtra (Second Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2023 (Maharashtra Act No. 31 of 2023) in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV dated 27th July, 2023.
2. Supplementary demand as shown in the accompanying statement under the Supplementary Appropriation Act aggregates to Rs. 1130, 70,68,000/- (Rupees One thousand one hundred and thirty crore seventy lakh sixty eight thousand only) (under the programme) (mandatory) (datta). After the approval of the Department of Planning and Finance, the financial sanction orders for the related schemes with regard to the expenditure of Supplementary Demand will be handed over to the concerned Implementing/ Controlling Officer in column 5 of the statement accompanying the provision, after being separately issued by the concerned Plan Implementation Offices.
3. The approval of the Planning and Finance Department should be obtained on the basis of the supplementary demands as per the delegation of financial powers, where necessary.
