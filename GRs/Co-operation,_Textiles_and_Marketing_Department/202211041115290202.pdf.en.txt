The Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2021 have been issued for the appointment of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts of the Government of Maharashtra under 14/7/2021 by direct service and promotion. As per Rule 5 of the notification, each administrative division of the government shall determine the total number of sanctioned posts in each cadre of Group-A and Group-B in their respective revenue divisions. Accordingly, the Commissioner of Cooperation and the Registrar of Cooperative Societies were under the consideration of the Government to determine the revenue department-wise number of sanctioned posts in the group-A and group-B cadres on the establishment of the departmental cadre to which the departmental cadre allocation is applicable.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under this Government decision, the total number of sanctioned posts in Group-A and Group-B cadres in the establishment of Co-operative Commissioners and Registrar Co-operative Societies, to which the departmental cadre allocation is applicable, is being determined as follows.
