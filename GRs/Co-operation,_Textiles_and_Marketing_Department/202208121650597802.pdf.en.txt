Preamble: A Committee of experts in the field of Textiles was constituted under the Government decision dated 02.02.2022 to study the problems of the machinery owners in the State and find a solution. Three new members were added to the committee as per the Government decision dated 11 th March 2022. The committee has not submitted its report to the government despite the lapse of four months, when the committee was supposed to submit a detailed report within 30 days. The Chief Secretary read out the No. 4 was provided. Accordingly, the government was considering the dissolution of a committee of experts in the textile sector to study the problems of the machinery owners in the state and take measures to address them.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has constituted a Committee of experts in the field of Textile Industry under the Government Decisions dated 02.02.2022 and 11.03.2022 to study the problems of the machinery owners in the State and to find a solution.
