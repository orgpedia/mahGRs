Introduction: Rupee Co-op. The Bank Ltd. Commissioner for Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune has issued a circular regarding the sanction of" Special one-time loan repayment scheme" to the bank. The proposal was submitted to the Government on 14.3.2017. Accordingly, the Government vide Reference No. A special one-time loan repayment scheme has been approved to this bank through a government decision in 1. Reference No. In accordance with the Government Decisions No. 2 and No. 3. The due date for submission of corrections in Form 4 has been extended to 31.5.2018.
The number of depositors in the bank is around 6.22 lakh. 1516 crore. Efforts are being made at the bank level to merge this bank with other banks. Despite best efforts as per the one-time loan repayment plan approved for the said bank, recovery has not been made in NPA cases. The merger of this bank with other banks will be easier if the accumulated losses of the bank are reduced by recovering the outstanding loans. However, the special one-time loan repayment scheme approved for the bank has expired on 31.5.2018. However, keeping in mind the interest of the members and depositors of the bank, the government was considering extending the term of the scheme with some modifications.
Government Decision: Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Pune. Rupee Co-op vide letter dated 7.6.2018. The Bank Ltd. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has decided to extend the validity of the special one-time loan repayment scheme offered in respect of Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 for the period for which the Government is empowered under the provisions of Section 157 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Rules, 1961, by exempting the RBI from the provisions of Rule 49 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Rules, 1961.
