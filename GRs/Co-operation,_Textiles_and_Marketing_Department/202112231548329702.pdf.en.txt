Preamble: A budgetary provision of Rs. 150.00 lakh has been made for the financial year 2021-22 for the promotion and publicity programme of the silk industry. The Finance Department has approved the disbursement of 10 per cent of this provision i. e.₹ 15 lakh. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 10,26,920/- has been made available to the Director (Silk) on Budget Distribution System for payment of pending dues for the financial year 2019-20, i.e. Rs. 8,56,920/- under the Government Decision dated 04.08.2021 and Rs. 1,70,000/- under the Government Decision dated 07.12.2021. Therefore, out of the 10% (₹ 15 lakh) of the budgetary provision for 2021-22 approved by the Finance Department, a sum of₹ 10,26,920/- has been released and a sum of₹ 4,73,080/- is available for disbursement to the Department. Now, the Director (Sericulture) vide letter dated 17.12.2021 has requested to make available funds to the tune of Rs. 2,09,920/-for setting up a stall of Directorate of Sericulture at Agrovision Agri Expo-2021 to be held at Nagpur from 24.12.2021 to 27.12.2021. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to disburse funds to meet the expenses incurred for setting up the stall of the Directorate of Silk at the Agrovision Exhibition-2021.
Government's decision:
Approval is hereby given for disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 2,09,920/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Nine Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty only) for setting up of Directorate of Sericulture stall at Agrovision Agri Expo-2021 to be held at Nagpur from 24th December, 2021 to 27th December, 2021 under the Promotion and Publicity Programme of the Silk Industry.
