The Proposal: -
The State Summit Committee meetings held under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Chief Minister discussed various initiatives under the International Year of Cooperation-2025. The meetings directed the Department of Cooperation to appoint an expert committee to make necessary changes in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 from time to time, taking into account the challenges in the cooperative sector in the changing circumstances. The Chief Minister has given.
Accordingly, the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperatives, Co-operative Societies, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government for constitution of a high-level committee with a view to making favourable changes in the Co-operative Policy and Co-operative Act of the State in line with the National Co-operative Policy, 2025. Read No. 4 with a view to formulating the State Cooperation Policy in line with the National Cooperation Policy, 2025. The government has approved the constitution of a high-level committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Cooperation), State of Maharashtra.
The Commissioner of Cooperation and the Registrar. As per the proposal dated 12/08/2025, the government was considering setting up a committee under the chairmanship of the Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operatives, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune with a view to making favourable changes in the Co-operative Act of the state.
Government Orders: -
With a view to making favourable changes in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961 and the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies (Election of Committees) Rules, 2014, the Government is approving the constitution of the following High Level Committee under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
