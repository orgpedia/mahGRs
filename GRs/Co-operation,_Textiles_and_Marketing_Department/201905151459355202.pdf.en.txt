Introduction: The Agriculture Export Policy announced by the Central Government has set a target of doubling the country's agricultural exports. To achieve the goal of agricultural export of the country it is necessary to have agricultural export policy of every state. Maharashtra is the largest exporter of grapes, pomegranate and onion in the country. Also, a large number of bananas, vegetables, rice etc. are exported from Maharashtra. The government was considering the formation of a committee to formulate a separate agricultural export policy of the state to increase the export of the state.
Government circular:
As per the agricultural export policy of the central government, the following committee is being formed to decide the agricultural export policy of the state.
