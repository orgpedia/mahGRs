The Maharashtra State Co-operative Union is a co-operative education, training, promotion and dissemination organisation in the state of Maharashtra. Dhananjayrao Gadgil Institute of Cooperative Management, Nagpur was established in 1972 by the National Cooperative Training Council, New Delhi. The Centre and the state governments share the cost in a 50:50 ratio.
A sum of Rs. 35.00 lakh (70%) has been disbursed to Dhananjayrao Gadgil Sahakari Prabandh Sansthan, Nagpur, Cooperative Training College for the year 2018-19 out of the provision of Rs. 50.00 lakh sanctioned for the year 2018-19 on 50% share of the State Government. Now the Department of Finance. As the circular dated 25.01.2019 had approved the disbursement of funds within the limit of 80%, the matter of disbursement of the remaining 10% funds to the said institution was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The funds due to Dhananjayrao Gadgil Cooperative Management Institute, Nagpur, a cooperative training college under the Maharashtra State Cooperative Union, on 50% of the state government's share, will be increased from the remaining 10% of the budgetary provision approved for 2018-19 under the scheme of training (under state schemes) (02) (02) State Cooperative Union (2425 0218) 31, Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried). A sum of Rs. 5.00 (Rupees Five Lakhs only) is being disbursed to the above institution.
