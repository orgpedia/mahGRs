Reference:- 1) Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai Letter No: NAFED Purchase/ Advertisement/ 2021-22/412, dated 4th August, 2021 2) Government Resolution, Finance Department,
For details: Preamble to the Constitution of India: In the year 2013-14, the Central Government appointed NAFED as the lead agent for procurement of various pulses, cereals and oilseeds at MSP. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai, was appointed as the deputy agent for the implementation of the scheme in the state. As per the revised guidelines of the Central Government issued in October, 2018, the scheme is advertised at the level of the State Government. Advertising fees are paid by the association. For the financial year 2021-22, a budget provision of Rs. 1.00 crore has been made under the head' Financial Assistance to Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation (MSCSM) for meeting the expenditure incurred on the purchase of farm produce through NAFED (2425 2417). From this budget provision, the government was considering to release funds for payment of pending advertisement dues from the season 2017- 18 to the season 2019-20.
Government's decision:
The Government have approved payment of Rs. 75.00 lakh (Rupees Seventy Five Lakh only) to the Federation for payment of the payment of the advertisement in the newspapers regarding procurement under the Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme from the season 2017-18 to the season 2019-20.
The expenditure shall be met from the budgetary provision for the year 2021-22 under the following head: Demand No. V-2
