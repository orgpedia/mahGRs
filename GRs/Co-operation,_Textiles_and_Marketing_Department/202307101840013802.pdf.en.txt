Introduction: Under the Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra, the guidelines of the scheme of Capital Subsidy Scheme in lieu of Interest Subsidy to Textile Units have been prescribed under the Government decision at Table No. 3 above. Accordingly, under the Textile Policy, the Government was considering to disburse subsidy to the proposals received under the Capital Subsidy Scheme instead of interest subsidy to the textile units as per the provision available in the year 2023-24 as per the statement in the' Annexure' accompanying this Government decision.
Government Decision: Under the Textile Policy of the State, instead of interest subsidy to the textile units, capital subsidy scheme is being implemented. The projects approved by the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Textiles), out of the provision approved for expenditure by the Department of Finance for the year 2023-24, as per the statement in Annexure A accompanying this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. An amount of Rs. 15,10,08, 000/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores Ten Lakh Eight Thousand) is sanctioned and sanctioned for disbursement. However, the amount will be disbursed only after the final decision in the case of the projects that have been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team under the decision of the government here in Reference No. 6 and in the case of the projects in which irregularities have been found, or further action will be taken according to the decision. Also, the projects which have not yet been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team due to lack of time will be inspected soon in the near future and if any irregularity is found in the inspection of that project, the subsidy sanctioned through this order will be recovered with interest.
