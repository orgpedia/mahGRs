To provide relief to the farmers having outstanding and unpaid dues up to Rs. 2.00 lakh, the Government has decided to launch the" Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme 2019." The Cabinet meeting was held on 24.12.2019. According to the A Government Resolution for the scheme has been issued on 27.12.2019.
In the said Government Resolution, after the following paragraph (iii) under the heading" Government Resolution," the following text is inserted after" A. No. 6) Officers and Office Bearers (Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Board of Directors) of Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Cooperative Sugar Factories, Cooperative Societies, Urban Cooperative Banks, District Central Cooperative Banks and Cooperative Milk Unions (having combined monthly salary more than Rs. 25000)."
" The various Departments/ Offices/ Corporations under which the information of the persons included in the above category of ineligible persons falls, will process the information collected from the banks about the pending loan accounts subject to the availability and accuracy of the Aadhaar number of the persons belonging to such category. Also, a public appeal will be made to the people included in the category of ineligibility not to take advantage of the scheme. "
