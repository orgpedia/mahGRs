Mango and Banana are the most important fruits of India. Indian mangoes are
being exported to various parts of the world including Middle East, Europe, UK, USA,
Australia and Far-East. Banana is currently being exported to Iran, UAE and the nearby
countries. The Central Asia is a potential market for Indian mangoes and banana.
Therefore, attempts are to be made to explore this part of the world for mango and banana
marketing especially Uzbekistan, which is the strategic business relationship with India.
There is immense potential for trade, especially in agricultural products between
the India and Uzbekistan. Recently, Uzbekistan has cleared Indian mangoes and bananas
for export from India following the process of Pest Risk Analysis of these fruits from
India. This opens up new opportunities of export of mango from India, which is
extremely popular in this country. Therefor Mango and Banana exporter in State and they
may be encouraged to explore the market in Uzbekistan.
A delegation led by Additional Chief Secretary (Cooperation and Marketing)
through Asian Development Bank has visited Tashkent, Uzbekistan from 28th to 30th
November, 2022. During the said visit, a meeting was held with the Deputy Minister of
Agriculture for Uzbekistan. During this meeting Hon. The Deputy Minister of
Agriculture for Uzbekistan has assured cooperation through the Government of
Uzbekistan for mango promotion Programme in Tashkent, Uzbekistan for mangoes from
India. Also, in the year 2020, India's Ambassador to Uzbekistan requested to Hon. the
Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra regarding organizing Mango Promotion
Programme at Tashkent, Uzbekistan.
Maharashtra being the horticulture bowl of the country and having premium
varieties of mangoes and Banana, the Uzbekistan market need to be tapped. Therefore,
considering the potential of mango and Banana, a Mango & Banana Promotion
Programme is proposed at Tashkent, Uzbekistan. The objectives of the Programme are to
