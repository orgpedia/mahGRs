Introduction: As per the recommendation of the committee appointed under the chairmanship of Mr. Tidke, Member of Legislative Assembly to study the problems of the handloom business in the state and suggest measures to solve them, to encourage handloom weavers, a scheme to organize competitions at various levels for weavers producing exquisite fabrics of traditional and non-traditional designs and giving prizes to them has been implemented under reference No. 1 Approved by the Government Decision dated 5.10.1972. In the scheme launched by the Government decision dated 5.10.1972, the amount of the award was increased by the Government decision dated 29th September, 2008. Till the year 2015-16, the scheme of Best Textile Production Competition for Handloom Weavers was being implemented at the prize money as mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 29th September, 2008. Meanwhile, considering the decline in the number of handloom weavers, non-availability of scheduled caste weavers for competition, the demand of weavers for increase in prize money, increase in the cost of the event, etc., the above-mentioned date of 17th October, 2016 was taken into account by the Government.
