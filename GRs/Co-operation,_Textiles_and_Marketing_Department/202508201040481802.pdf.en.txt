The election process for the cooperative societies in the state is carried out by the State Cooperative Election Authority under the provision of Section 73KB of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960.
Office of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune.
According to the report received by the government on 25/07/2025, the total number of co-operative societies currently in the electoral process is 3,188, while the number of co-operative societies in categories A and B is 285.

Finally, 30 districts in the state received above-average Co% rainfall, out of which 15 districts received above-average 100% rainfall. As such, farmers in most of the districts of the state are likely to face difficulties in participating in the election process of the cooperative societies to which they belong as they are busy with kharif farming activities. Considering this, the government is of the view that it would be appropriate to postpone the election process of the cooperative societies in the state till the end of the rainy season (September 30, 2025).
As per the provision in Section 73AA of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, the government has the power to postpone the election of co-operative societies during the monsoon season. For the above-mentioned reasons, the Government is issuing the following orders.
