Preamble A Selection Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Textiles) for promotion to the posts of Group A and Group B in the Commissionerate of Textiles and under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Textiles), Nagpur has been constituted vide Government Order dated 29th March, 2008. General Administration Department, State Government Officers/ Employees as on 1st August, 2019
Consolidated guidelines on the procedure to be followed for their promotion have been issued. In paragraph (a) (ii) of the said Government Decision, with regard to the constitution of the Divisional Promotion Committee,
provisions have been set. As per the said provisions, the Government was contemplating to constitute Committees for giving promotions to the posts of Group-A to Group-C cadre in the subordinate offices of the Commissionerate of Textiles and the Commissionerate of Textiles.
Government Decision The following Committees are being constituted by dissolving the Committees constituted under the Government Decision dated 29th March, 2008 for giving promotions to the Gazetted and Non-Gazetted posts in the Office of the Commissionerate of Textiles, Nagpur:
Committee for promotion to Group-A cadre posts in the Commissionerate of Textiles, Nagpur and subordinate offices of the Commissionerate which are not under the jurisdiction of the Establishment Board.
