The Proposal:
Read No. Under the decision of the government here, criteria have been set for the implementation of the revised pay scale as per the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission for employees of public enterprises in the state. Since the Government is fulfilling the criteria laid down in the said decision, the Board of Directors of the Corporation vide Board Resolution No. 669 dated 26th March, 2012, has implemented the pay scales based on the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission with effect from 1st January, 2006 for the officers and employees of the Corporation.
Also, the 5th Pay Commission has been given to the employees of the Machinery Corporation. Board of Directors effective January 1, 1996. It was approved on January 25, 2008.
As the above proposals have not been approved by the Administrative Department and the Finance Department, the Maharashtra State Machinery Corporation was under the consideration of the Government to give ex-post facto approval to the recommendations of the 5th and 6th Pay Commission implemented under the resolution of the Board of Directors to its officers/ employees.
Government's decision:
Since the Maharashtra State Machinery Corporation has applied the 5th and 6th Pay Commission to its officers/ employees mutually, the employees of this PSU are being given ex-post facto approval to apply the revised pay scale as per the recommendations of the 5th and 6th Pay Commission subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) With the implementation of the present pay scale, the government will not provide financial subsidy to the corporation for any kind of salary allowance now or in the future.
