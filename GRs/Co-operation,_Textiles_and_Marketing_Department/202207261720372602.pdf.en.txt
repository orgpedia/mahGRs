The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 31st January, 2014 in the context of General Administration Department, Shri. The Supreme Court in Writ Petition (Civil) No. 82 in the year 2011. In pursuance of the directions dated 31.10.2013, orders have been issued for constitution of a Civil Services Board for making recommendations to the competent authority regarding posting/ transfer of officers/ staff belonging to Group-A, Group-B and Group-C in the State Government Services. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Civil Services Board (1) to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers on the establishment of the Textile Commissionerate under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
Government's decision:
The Civil Services Board is hereby constituted to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) cadre officers on the establishment of the Textile Commissionerate as follows:
