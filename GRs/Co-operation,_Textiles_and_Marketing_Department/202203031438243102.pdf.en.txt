State Support for Empowerment of District Central Co-operative Banks (2425 2524) under State Small and Marginal Co-operative Credit Structure (STCCS) The State Government has sanctioned 2.5 per cent interest subvention to the District Central Co-operative Banks (DCCBs) for making available crop loans to farmers at 6 per cent interest rate through the District Central Co-operative Banks (DCBs). For the implementation of the Scheme" Support for Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks under State Small and Medium Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) (2425 2524)," it has been decided to disburse funds from the available allocation for the financial year 2021-22 as follows.
