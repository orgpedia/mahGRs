GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government had extended the validity of 67 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Co-operative Consumer Societies in the State till 31.08.2021 vide Government Decision (2) dated 3.03.2021. The proposal for extension of these 67 temporary posts from 1st September, 2021 to 28th February, 2022 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of temporary posts to be extended for the period from 1st September, 2021 to 28th February, 2022 are as follows:
