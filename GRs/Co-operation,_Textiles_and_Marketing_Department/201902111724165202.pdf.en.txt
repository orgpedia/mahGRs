Preamble- As per the decision of the Government in the context dated 15/12/2018, out of the budgetary provision of Rs.60 crore under the head" Marginal Amount (Programme) for Implementation of Cotton Procurement Scheme (64250064)" for the season 2018-19, the Government had approved to make available an amount of Rs.42 crore to the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation as a non-interest bearing loan with the approval of the Finance Department. However, due to some administrative reasons, the matter of cancelling the government decision and disbursing the revised funds was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision No. Cotton-2018/ P. No. 39/10-S dated 15/12/2018 regarding the disbursement of funds under the head" Marginal Amount (Programme) for Implementation of Cotton Procurement Scheme (64250064)" in the 2018-19 season is hereby cancelled. The Government is approving to provide Rs.20 crore (twenty crore) to Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation as interest free loan out of the budgetary provision of Rs.60 crore available for payment of assured price of cotton procured/ to be procured from farmers by Cotton Growers Marketing Federation under Cotton Purchase Scheme 2018-19.
The Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Federation should enter into a formal agreement with the Cotton Corporation of India in this regard.
The amount will be spent only on the purchase of cotton under the minimum support price, after which the amount of cotton purchased will be paid to the concerned farmers, which will be the responsibility of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Producers Marketing Federation.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the provisions available during 2018-19 by meeting the expenditure under the headings given below:
