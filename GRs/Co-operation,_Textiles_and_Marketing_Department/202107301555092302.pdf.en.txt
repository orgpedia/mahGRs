All Co-operative Societies having more than 50 members shall hold Annual General Meeting through VC or OAVM by following procedure: (a) Arrangement should be made to inform every member of the society about the date, time, place and link of the Annual General Meeting at least seven days in advance through SMS/ mail/ WhatsApp.
(b) The notice of the Annual General Meeting shall be posted on the Notice Board of the Institute/ Offices of the Branches of the Institute etc.
(c) It shall be the responsibility of the Co-operative Society concerned to communicate by letter within seven days to the members who do not have an email address or mobile number for contact.
d) All cooperative societies shall publish the information regarding their Annual General Meeting in at least one local newspaper/ one district newspaper or Marathi or English newspaper of state status, taking into account the jurisdiction of the organization. Advertising in the newspaper should include the following items:
(i) Which of the following is the medium through which the VC or OAVM (Other Audio Visual Means) is to be conducted for conducting the Annual General Meeting?
(ii) the date and time of the meeting.
(iii) Members who have not registered their email ID or mobile number with the concerned Co-operative Society, should mention the details of where to submit the information.
(e) to conduct the Annual General Meeting of the Co-operative Societies through VC or OAVM (Other Audio Visual Means), the concerned Co-operative Society shall conduct the proceedings of the meeting through the advice of the expert person available with them or by selecting the agency.
f) The agency using Marathi software should be considered on priority while selecting the said agency.
(i) It shall be the responsibility of the institution concerned to maintain proper records of the attendance of the members at the Annual General Meeting and to preserve the records of the meeting in accordance with the provisions in force.
(j) The institutions should immediately take action to hold their Annual General Meeting by strictly adhering to the above guidelines.
