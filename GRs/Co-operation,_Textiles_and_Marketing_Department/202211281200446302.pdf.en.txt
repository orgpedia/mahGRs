Dhananjayrao Gadgil Institute of Cooperative Management, Nagpur was established in 1972. Accordingly, the second Cooperative Training Centre in Maharashtra has been set up at Nagpur. The cost will be shared equally by the Centre and the state government (50:50). Accordingly, 50% of the subsidy amount from the State Government is provided in the Budget under the head Demand No. V-2, 2425, Cooperation 003 (2) State Cooperative Union (02) (02) State Cooperative Union (Programme) (24250218) 31-Support Grants.
For the year 2022-2023, a sum of Rs. 35.00 lakh has been provided to the said Training Institute by the Central Government through a letter dated 26.07.2022.
