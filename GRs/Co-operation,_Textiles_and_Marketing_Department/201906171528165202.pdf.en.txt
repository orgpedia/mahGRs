The Proposal:
Cabinet of Ministers As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 18.11.2018, Atal Financial Assistance Scheme for Co-operative Societies for financing the business/ projects of Co-operative Societies under No. (1) and (2) of Wacha was being implemented by Maharashtra Co-operative Development Corporation during 2018-19.
The scheme is expected to be launched in 2019-20.
Accordingly, a decision was taken in the Cabinet meeting held on 04.06.2019.
Government Decisions: (a) In the year 2018-19, the Government has decided to provide Rs. on 18.11.2018. Atal Financial Assistance Scheme for Cooperative Societies approved by the Cabinet. The Minister of Finance, Mr. Minister (Cooperation) Government Resolution dated 02.01.2019 with final amendments made by the three-member Committee of Chief Secretary. The Government has approved the continuation of the scheme of" Atal Financial Assistance to Cooperative Societies" for the year 2019-20, which is due for approval as on 23.01.2019.
(b) Under this Scheme, Read No. 3 Under the scheme, during 2018-19, an amount of Rs. 8004.69 lakh is being sanctioned for the year 2019-20.
