Preamble: The Board of Directors of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers and Traders Federation on 22.11.2019 has approved the implementation of the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission on the lines of the State Government to the employees of the Federation. Earlier, the State Government has approved the implementation of the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission to the officers/ employees of the Federation on the lines of the officers/ employees of the State Government vide Government Resolution dated 11.03.2013. Further, the criteria for applying the revised pay scale to the PSUs in the State as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission has been decided under the Government decision dated 04.08.2021 of the Department of Finance. Considering these aspects, the proposal to implement the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission for the employees of the Cotton Marketing Federation was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Pursuant to the norms laid down in the Government Order dated 04.08.2021 of the Department of Finance, the officers/ employees of Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation are being paid emoluments as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission in the Annexure A to this Government Order. The revised pay scale is being approved with effect from 01.07.2021 subject to the following conditions:
(1) The benefit of the officers/ employees of the Federation should not be more than the pay scale applied to the equivalent posts in the Government while giving the benefit of the revised pay scale.
The revised pay scales should be applicable within that limit.
(2) The Federation shall be required to submit a separate proposal to the Government while allowing the assured progress scheme under the revised service as per the 7th Pay Commission.

3) The figure of 1131 posts of the Federation. Approved by the Directorate of Marketing, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 9th August, 2007. However, the federation will now have to submit a proposal to the government for approval of the format.
4) The benefit of the revised pay scale will be admissible only to the officers/ employees of the cadre as approved in due course. In case of creation of new cadre/ posts, prior approval of the Government will be required while allowing the said benefit.
It will be mandatory to take action regarding filling up of vacant/ expired posts/ creation of new posts in the federation with the prior approval of the government.
6) The Cotton Marketing Federation should take an appropriate decision at their level regarding the payment of arrears in a lump sum or in a phased manner from July 1, 2021 till the implementation of the revised pay scale or deposit it in the Provident Fund. However, with this and the introduction of the revised pay scale, the federation will not incur losses in the future and the responsibility of the financial burden will be entirely with the federation. There is no grant from the government in this regard.
7) It will be necessary to make positive efforts to increase the income of the federation and to prove the usefulness and efficiency of the federation.
8) The appropriate time-bound programme of the schemes/ works to be implemented by the federation should be prepared and its review should be submitted to the government from time to time.
The federation shall implement the revised pay scale as per the terms and conditions of the 7th Pay Commission issued by the Finance Department/ Government, dated 30th January, 2019, dated 20th February, 2019, dated 04th August, 2021 and Government decisions/ circulars issued from time to time.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the consent of the Finance Department and under the informal reference No.59/ CE, dated 02.09.2021 of that Department.
