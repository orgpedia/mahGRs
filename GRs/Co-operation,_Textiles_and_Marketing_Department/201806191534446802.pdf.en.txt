The Proposal:
Due to the large-scale production of tur in the state in the 2016-17 season, the market price of tur has increased and the farmers are getting less than the minimum support price fixed for tur.
The State Government has procured 25.25 lakh quintals of Tur under Market Intervention Scheme from its own funds. It was decided to sell this rice to the cheap grain shops and government departments. The decision has been taken by the government. The government was considering some reforms to make the sale of this tur dal faster.
Government's decision:
This was discussed at the Cabinet meeting. As per the decision taken in the review meeting held under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary with the Secretary of the concerned department, the following instructions are being given to sell tur dal.
