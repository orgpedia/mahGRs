Government Clarification-
In the aforesaid Government Resolution No. 5." 1) Considering the production capacity of all the institutions here, they should be given orders for supply in almost equal proportion to the production capacity. Also, a circular system should be adopted while placing orders for supply of textiles so that all institutions receive orders for supply of textiles.
" No. 1 above) The institutions which have acquired the demand for textile supply themselves should be given the work of textile supply. However, if the Maharashtra State Machinery Corporation itself acquires the textile demands from the Government/ Semi-Government Office or if the textile demands are received directly from the Government/ Semi-Government Office to the Corporation, then the work of supplying such textiles should be done as per the above No. 1) All the institutions here should be ordered to supply in almost equal proportion to the production capacity and all the institutions should adopt a cyclical method while ordering the supply of textiles so that the orders for the supply of textiles are received.
