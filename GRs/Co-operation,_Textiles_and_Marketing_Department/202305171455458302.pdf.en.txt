The Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963. Kripa Man/ Administration/ Shri Thakur/ Nomination/ 1717/2022 Dy. Katol/ Shri Thakur/ 114/2 D. 10.1.2023
Section 39 (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963, provides for the appointment of six members to the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board as members by nomination from among the chairpersons of the market committees in the state, one member from each revenue division. Accordingly, the Government was considering the appointment of Shri Charan Singh Thakur, Chairman, Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Katol, from Nagpur Revenue Division as a member of the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board by nomination.
As per Section 39 (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963, the Government is approving the appointment of Shri Charan Singh Thakur, Chairman, Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Katol, from Nagpur Revenue Division as a member of the Maharashtra State Agricultural Produce Marketing Board.
Shri Charan Singh Thakur shall be eligible to hold office as a member of the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board with effect from the date of this Government Order.
