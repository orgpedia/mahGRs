The decision m. The Supreme Court in Writ Petition (Civil) No. 82/2011. Group-A, Group-B and Group-C in the State Government Services to comply with the directions given on 31.10.2013
Orders have been issued to constitute a Civil Services Board to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of officers/ employees. Accordingly, the Directorate of Silk under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles was established.
The Government was contemplating to constitute a Civil Services Board (1) to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of officers of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadres.
Government's decision:
The Civil Services Board (1) is hereby constituted to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Silk.
