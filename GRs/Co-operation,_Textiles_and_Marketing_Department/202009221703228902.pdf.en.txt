GOVERNOR'S DECISION: The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2020-21 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 7th September, 2020. The bill has already been approved by the Assembly.
The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been approved by the Cabinet for inclusion in the Appropriation for the financial year ending March 31, 2021. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published as the Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2020 (Maharashtra Act No. 18 of 2020) in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary, Part-IV, dated 10th September, 2020.
2. Supplementary demand as shown in the accompanying statement under the Supplementary Appropriation Act aggregating to Rs. 10584,72,23, 000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand Five Hundred and Forty Four Crores Seventy Two Lakh Twenty Three Thousand only) (Programme/ Compulsory) (Datta Mat). After the approval of the Planning and Finance Department in respect of this provision, the orders for financial approval of the schemes in respect of this Supplementary Demand shall be handed over to the concerned Implementing/ Controlling Officer in column V of the statement accompanying the provision, after being separately issued by the concerned Scheme Implementing Offices.
Proposals for disbursement of funds for Supplementary Demands for Compulsory Expenditure (on original lease) should be submitted to the Finance Department for approval. Proposals for Supplementary Demands for Programme Expenditure (on original lease) should be submitted to the Finance Department with the approval of the Planning Department, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Tribal Development Department, as the case may be, before disbursement of funds.
