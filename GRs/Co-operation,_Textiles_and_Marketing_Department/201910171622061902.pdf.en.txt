GOVERNMENT DECISION: The 14 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Farmers Cooperative Union, Kolhapur in the State were extended till 30th September, 2019 as per the Government Decision, dated 12th March, 2019. The proposal for extension of these 14 temporary posts from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
