Introduction: Whether the agricultural produce is weighed on electronic weighing machines and how the packaged agricultural produce should be weighed? A committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Dr. Kishare Toshniwal, Additional Registrar, Administration and Civil Society, Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune to make recommendations in this regard. A single representative of the traders has been appointed to represent the traders in this committee. The proposal to appoint 1 more trade representative in the committee was under the consideration of the government to avoid injustice to the traders.
Government Decision: In the above mentioned committee, Mr. Walchand Devichand Sancheti, Dy. The Federation of Association of Traders (Maharashtra), Pune is being appointed as a member.
