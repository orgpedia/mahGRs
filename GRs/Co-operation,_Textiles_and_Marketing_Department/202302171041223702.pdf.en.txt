Preamble: In pursuance of the proposal of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, vide Supplementary Demand dated December, 2022 for payment of Advocate Fee under the Professional Services Act, 2022, a proposal for Supplementary Demand amounting to Rs. 16,06,000/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Six Thousand only) for the Professional Services Act, 2022 was submitted to the Legislature. The matter of disbursement of funds sanctioned through supplementary demand as approved by the Legislature was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the winter session of December, 2022 for the office of State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, Demand No. The Supplementary Demands for Grants for V-2, 2070-Other Administrative Services (00) (800)-Other Expenditure (00) (00) (01), State Cooperative Electoral Authorities (2070 0882)-28- Professional Services amounting to Rs. 16,06,000/- have been approved.
Considering the details of the above sanctioned Supplementary Demands, the Government are pleased to approve the release of funds amounting to Rs. 16,06,000/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Six Thousand only) sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demands under Chapter 28 of the Commercial Services.
The Assistant Registrar (Estimates and Planning), Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer (V0004) and the Joint Assistant Election Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 16,06,000/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Six Thousand only) sanctioned under this Government Decision. The amount to be disbursed for contractual services and professional services under the Election Authority will be spent under the head of Chapter 28 Professional Services under the Election Authority, 2070 0882.
