The Government's Decisions:
The Government is giving its approval for implementation of "Tasar Silk Development Project (2026-2029)" in Gadchiroli district with a view to promote tussar silk development and generate sustainable employment in tribal and rural areas with the following conditions and conditions:
Project Area and Beneficiaries: The project will be implemented in 8 Taluks namely Itapalli, Bhamragad, Aheri, Kurkheda, Armori, Perimili, Sironcha and Mulcherra in Gadchiroli District. A total of 2270 beneficiaries will be selected under the project. Priority will be given to Scheduled Tribes, women and self-help groups.
2) Project components and physical specifications:
Basic seed cell production: A total of 180 seed cell producers will be developed in > 3 years (60 per annum).
Support will be provided for necessary materials and tree conservation.
> By the end of the third year, 2.5-3.00 lakh egg-laying capacity will be created.
2.2 Commercial fund generation: > 500 beneficiaries will be supported in the first year and 700 each in the next two years.
> A total of 1900 commercial fund manufacturers will be created.
2.3 Private Egg Production Centres: > Total Yo Centres to be set up.
> 10 centres will be set up in the first year and 15 each in the next two years.
About 5000 egg-laying capacity will be developed per centre.
2.4 Tasar Silk Yarn Manufacturing Groups: > 3 yarn manufacturing groups to be set up in 3 years.
Employment will be provided to local women's self-help groups.
2.5 Manpower (Silk Envoys): > 100 Silk Envoys will be appointed in a phased manner through CSR funds.
> 5 Raring Operatives will be appointed by the Directorate of Sericulture.
Training and Capacity Building: > 180 Seed Fund Producers, Woo Professional Producers, 40 Egg Holders and 150 Yarn Manufacturing Trainees will be trained.
Study tours and demonstrations will be held.
> The training will be provided by the Directorate of Silk.
3) Financial Plan: The project is expected to cost ₹1335.00 lakh for a period of 03 years.
