The Proposal:
The policy on implementation of co-generation projects by cooperative sugar mills in the state is referred to in Reference No. The date herein has been fixed by the Government Decision dated 20.02.2008. As per the policy, the state will be able to set up 1000 MW of power generation projects in the next three years through cooperative sugar mills. An example has been set for the generation of Watt power. Also, as per the policy, 5 per cent government shareholding of the project cost will be sanctioned to cooperative sugar mills implementing co-generation projects.
