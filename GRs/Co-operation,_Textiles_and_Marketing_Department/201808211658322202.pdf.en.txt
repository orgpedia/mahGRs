Preamble:" Atal Mahapan Vikas Abhiyan" is being implemented under the Government Decision, dated 20/12/2016, to improve and strengthen the work of Cooperative Marketing Federations, District/ Taluka Procurement Unions and various Executive Cooperative Services (VCAS) Institutions in the State. Issue No. 10 of the decision states that the institutions and individuals who have done excellent and innovative work in this campaign will be awarded. In the first year of the Mission, the Government is considering to give awards to the institutions and individuals who have done excellent and innovative work under the Mission from 25/12/2016 to 25/12/2017.

(b) Criteria for Award Selection: Based on the activities carried out under Atal Mahapan Abhiyan from 25th December, 2016 to 25th December, 2017, institutions and individuals should be selected for Atal Mahapan Awards through the following criteria:
1) Membership of the V.K.S. organization to the farmers in the field.
(ii) the playing stock raised by way of recovery of dues, utilisation of full capacity of warehouses and other means.
3) New businesses launched based on agricultural inputs to farmers- fertilizers, seeds, medicines e.
5) New businesses of other types (essential goods) and innovative types.
7) Investments in new businesses of all kinds.
8) Start a new business.
9) Organized workshops, meetings, study tours, etc.
10) The due publicity given to this work by various media and its documents, etc.
Awards will be given to the institutions/ individuals who secure maximum marks out of the total 100 marks by awarding 10 marks each to each of the above criteria.
(a) Appraisal Committees for selection of Awards: The Appraisal Committees for selection of three types of Atal Mahapan Awards on the basis of the given criteria will be as follows: 1) State Level Appraisal Committee 1) Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune Chairman 2) Managing Director, Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai- Member 3) Executive Director, Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board, Pune- Member 4) Marketing Director, Maharashtra State, Pune- Member Secretary
The committee is to select the best officers/ employees of the Atal Mahapanchayat Development Mission in the state.
