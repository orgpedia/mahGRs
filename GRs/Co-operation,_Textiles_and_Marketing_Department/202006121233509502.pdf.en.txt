Preamble: In the context of General Administration Department, by the Government decision dated 21.12.2019, instructions have been given to create more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or have submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes and transfer their services to the higher post. Accordingly, the Director (Silk) has transferred the services of a total of 6 employees in the Directorate of Silk and subordinate offices of the Directorate to the supernumerary post with effect from 31.12.2019 and has proposed to create 6 supernumerary posts for them.
Accordingly, a total of 6 additional posts for employees in the Directorate of Silk and subordinate offices of the Directorate were under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Those who are appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to produce the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes.
