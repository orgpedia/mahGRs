The Proposal:
NAFED has been appointed as the lead agent for procurement of various pulses, cereals and oilseeds at MSP by Government of India during 2013-14. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai is appointed as the Deputy Agent for the implementation of this scheme in the state. Under the Minimum Support Price Scheme, NAFED has provided Rs. 100.00 lakh for the financial year 2018-19 under the head Financial Assistance for Contingent Expenditure on Procurement by Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation (2425 2417). The circular dated 2/4/2018 of the Department of Finance empowers the administrative departments to disburse the budgetary allocation on essential and programme expenditure required for the first nine months of 2018-2019 (ending December, 2018) within the limit of 70% of the total annual provision. Accordingly, to provide funds for payment of advertisement dues to the Pawnee Federation, the scheme titled" Financial Assistance to Maharashtra State Co-operative Pawnee Federation for meeting the incidental expenses for payment of agricultural produce purchased through NAFED" has been introduced.
