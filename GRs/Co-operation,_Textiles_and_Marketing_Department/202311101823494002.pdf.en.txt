In order to contribute to the measures, the Government of Maharashtra has announced an Integrated and Sustainable Textile Policy 2023-28 in 2023 for the next 5 years.
Government Decision The Central Government provides 15% subsidy on yarn (DBT Linked Bank Account) with a numerical restriction.
An additional 15% subsidy will be provided by the government. A total subsidy of 30% (15% Central share and 15% State share) will be admissible on cotton hank yarn, home-made silk, woolen and linen yarn and blended yarn of natural fibres.
Eligibility of the scheme: 1) A weaver receiving 15% subsidy under the Central Government's Raw Material Supply Scheme will be eligible for 15% subsidy under the State Scheme.
(2) The applicant must be a resident of Maharashtra.
3) The concerned weaver will be eligible for the benefit under this scheme as long as the weaver is employed in the business.

4) It will be mandatory for the beneficiary weaver to do the weaving himself. If it is found that the interest cannot be sold/ transferred to others under any circumstances, then the scheme will be disqualified.
Scheme Criteria: (1) The State Government's share shall be paid in proportion to the amount received from the Central Government.
2) The amount will be credited directly to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
3) The weavers who will be benefited under this scheme will be inspected by the Textile Department from time to time and if it is found that they are taking advantage of the scheme illegally, the subsidy given will be recovered and legal action will be taken.
