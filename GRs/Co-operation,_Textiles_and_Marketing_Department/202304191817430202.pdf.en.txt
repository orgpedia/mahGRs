Section 24-A of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 provides that every institution shall conduct co-operative education and training for its directors, members, officers and employees through the State Federal Institutions or State Apex Training Institutions as may be specified by the State Government by notification in the Official Gazette. There are about 2,22,738 lakh cooperatives operating in the state of Maharashtra as on March 31, 2022, with a total membership of about 5.90 crore. The number of directors of these institutions is more than 25 lakh and the number of employees is about 3.5 lakh. It is mandatory for the concerned cooperative society to provide cooperative education and training to its members, directors, and officers/ employees as per Section 24-A of the Cooperative Act.
In view of the provisions of the said Cooperative Act, for notifying the institutions imparting cooperative education and training as State Apex Training Institutions, Reference No. 1 Here the criteria are laid down by the government circular. As per Section 24-A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, some of the institutions in the state have been notified as State Apex Training Institutes. However, in view of the large number of members of cooperative societies in the state, it is still necessary to notify some cooperative societies as training institutions as it is mandatory to provide cooperative education and training to the members of cooperative societies at least once in 5 years. In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 24A of the said Act, Maharashtra Urban Co-op Banks Federation Ltd. Approval is hereby accorded to notify the Institute as State Apex Training Institute, Mumbai subject to the following terms and conditions.
Quarterly report on the training provided by the above institution should be submitted to the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune and a copy of the report should be submitted to the Government.
