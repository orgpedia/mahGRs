The Proposal: -
Section 39 (b) of the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963
The Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board has every revenue from the Chairman of the State Market Committees.
There is a provision to nominate 6 members, one from the department.
Accordingly, Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Malkapur, Dist. Buldhana was formed from Amravati Revenue Division.
The Chairman of the Market Committee, Mr. Sanjay Sadashiv Kajale (Patil) has been transferred to the Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board.
The matter of appointment as a nominated member was under the consideration of the government.
