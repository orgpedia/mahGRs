Whereas, as COVID-19 (Corona Virus), declared as Pandemic by WHO, is spreading in the country as well as in Maharashtra, the Department of Public Health, vide its notification dated 13.03.2020, has declared a public health emergency in the State, by invoking the provisions of Sections 2, 3 and 4 of the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1987, read with Section 73A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, namely," The State Government shall have the power to postpone the elections to the Cooperative Societies in the interest of the public in the event of any natural calamity,"
The State Co-operative Election Authority shall be entrusted with the responsibility of conducting, supervising and controlling the elections to all the co-operative societies (excluding co-operative housing societies with less than 250 members) in the State in accordance with the provisions of Section 73AB (1) of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960.

Government Order No: SAMIKHU0320/ PR No. 70/13-C
The State Co-operative Election Authority may have issued an order dated 13th September, 2021 regarding the commencement of the election process for the first phase of the Co-operative Societies (excluding the Co-operative Housing Societies with a strength of 250 members and below and excluding the District Central Co-operative Banks where the election process is underway) in the" District Election Plan" prepared for the election of the Authority as per the authority.
Accordingly, a new section has been inserted in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, for cooperative housing societies with a strength of 250 or less members. Therefore, since the period of suspension of elections to these cooperative housing societies has expired, it is necessary to start these elections immediately.
Therefore, since it is necessary to start the process of elections to cooperative housing societies with a strength of 250 members and less, the action to start the elections to cooperative housing societies with a strength of 250 members and less should be taken immediately from this order by adopting the provisions mentioned in the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies (Election to Committees) (Amendment) Rules, 2019.
