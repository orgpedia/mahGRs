Go to the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperatives, State of Maharashtra, Pune. 3 SA/ 2/ VC Empowerment/ Sub-Certificates/ 2022, dated 07/03/2022 Preamble: As per the Government Resolution No.1, the State Government has decided to provide financial assistance to the farmers of the State in proportion to the supply of crop loans by the Government to empower the Primary Agricultural Credit Societies for supply of crop loans. The government has decided to provide 0.75 to 2% financial assistance in proportion to the loan disbursement made by these institutions every financial year.
The primary agricultural credit cooperatives to be financed are bound to meet the conditions/ criteria mentioned in the government decision.
