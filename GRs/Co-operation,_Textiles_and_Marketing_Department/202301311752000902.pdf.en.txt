The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015.
The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act has been enacted with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their increasing expectations from the local administration. Accordingly, the Public Service, Designated Officers, First and Second Appellate Authorities and Rules time limits are required to be notified under this Act. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to amend the bye-laws of the sugar mills to include this service as a list of public services and to notify the Regional Joint Director (Sugar) as a Designated Officer.
Government's decision:
In order to make the benefits of various service facilities provided by the Sugar Commissionerate available to the farmers and citizens/ industries of the state, the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 stipulates the following public service.
