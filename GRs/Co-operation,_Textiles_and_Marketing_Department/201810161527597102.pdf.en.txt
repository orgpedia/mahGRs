Growth/ 18/1377 D. 17/09/2018 GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government Decision dated 27th August, 1997 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, the Directorate of Silk has been established and the posts have been transferred from various establishments to the Directorate of Silk. The High-Level Committee has approved the review of 373 posts under the Director (Reshim). It was established on March 22, 2011. Reference No. The government has approved six new posts along with the creation of a new district silk office in Jawhar, Palghar.

So there is a figure of 379 posts under the Directorate of Silk. Of these, reference No. 4 is here. A total of 303 posts have been made permanent including 29 posts which were made permanent vide Government Order dated 14.10.2014. The remaining 76 are temporary posts. Accordingly, the Administrative Department has been empowered under the Government Decision, Finance Division, dated 7.9.2018 to extend the period of temporary posts on the establishment of Directorate of Silk from 1st October, 2018 to 28th February, 2019. A proposal for grant of approval for continuation of these 76 temporary posts with effect from 1st October, 2018 has been received from Directorate of Silk vide letter dated 17.9.2018.
All Administrative Divisions have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all existing temporary posts created after approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls. Accordingly, the Government is approving the continuation of 76 temporary posts as shown in the accompanying Forms A and B from 1.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
The expenditure incurred on this post should be covered under the following head of accounts and should be met from the sanctioned grant for the financial year 2018-2019.
