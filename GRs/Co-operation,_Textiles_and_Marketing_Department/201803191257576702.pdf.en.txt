Read 1) GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: GOVERNMENT MEMORY NO. AASUA-2018/ PR No. 14/18/17 C.D. 17.3.2018
Government Resolution It is proposed to approve the reallocation of funds from the provisions under the programme for the year 2017-18 under the head Demand V-5," Loans for Co-operation, 6425" under the control of the Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles.
The Principal Accounts under the year 2017-18 under the heading" 6425- Loans for Cooperation," (under the Programme) has an outlay of Rs. 103.69 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Three Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand only) to the Cooperative Farm Processing Societies under the head' 6425-Loans for Cooperation' (under the Programme) as shown in the Saving Annexure-I.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under paragraph 162,163,164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book (Part-I), 1977 and with the approval of the Additional Chief Secretary (Marketing), Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department.
