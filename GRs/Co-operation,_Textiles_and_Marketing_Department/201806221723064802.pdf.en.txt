Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai vide letter No. MH/ LQN/ 30151/ Dr. No.320/ No. M/ s./ 16/4722, Dy. 15.11.2017 Preamble: Registration of the Nagpur Weavers Co-operative Weavers Society Limited, Nagpur. It was made on 15.06.1962 and the working capital of the spinning mill was exhausted. On 24.06.1996, the yarn mill was removed from service by an interim order of the Director Textile, Nagpur.
The period of closure of the yarn mill ended on 5.5.2011. On 23.06.2013, the registration of the spinning mill was cancelled under the provision of Section 21 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960. Thereafter, the property of the spinning mill has been placed under the custody and control of the Regional Deputy Director, Textiles, Nagpur on behalf of the Registrar. The remaining 20.20 acres of land with the yarn mill would be sold to MHADA at the ready reckoner rate and the amount would be deposited in the Textile Development Fund and used for implementing the textile policy.
Government Decision: Nagpur Weavers Co-operative Yarn Factory Limited, Nagpur registered under the name of Director Textiles, Nagpur. The Government is approving the sale of 20.20 acres of land of this spinning mill, which is under the custody of Regional Deputy Director, Textiles, Nagpur, on behalf of the Registrar, to MHADA at the ready reckoner rate, as cancelled vide order dated 2.7.2013. Necessary action in this regard should be taken by the Housing Department, MHADA and the Director Textile Industry, Nagpur.
Subject to the final outcome of the petitions/ court cases pending in various courts in respect of the Nagpur Weavers Co-operative Yarn Mill, Nagpur.
