The Government Decision is proposed to approve the reallocation from the mandatory provisions in 2021-22 under the head of Demand Number V-2," 2070 Other Administrative Services," under the control of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
For the year 2021-22, under Demand No.V-2, Principal Account Head" 2425-Sahakar" (Compulsory), the Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Compulsory), (24250011) 50 other expenses amounting to Rs. 13.60 lakh (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) is being approved by the Government for making available the same as mentioned in the Supplementary Statement-I along with the savings as shown in the Demand No.V-2, Head of Accounts 2070-Other Administrative Services, State Cooperative Election Authority (Mandatory), (2070 0882) 17-Computer Spending as shown in the Supplementary Statement-II.
This Government has taken this decision in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under paragraph 162,163,164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules, 1977 (Part-I). It is being issued with the approval of the Additional Chief Secretary (Cooperation and Marketing), Cooperation, Marketing and Textile Department.
