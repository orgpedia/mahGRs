The proposal: The Cabinet Decision taken at the meeting held on 29 July 2020. The Asian Development Bank (ADB) funded-Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project with a total outlay of US$ 142.90 million as on 30.09.2020 has been approved to be implemented by MAGNET Institute in all the districts of the State for a period of 6 years. Of this, 70% i.e. USD 100 million will be received as a low-interest loan from the Asian Development Bank (ADB).
The remaining 30% of the funding, US$ 42.9 million, will be self-funded by the state government. The loan agreement was signed between the Central Government and the Asian Development Bank and the project agreement was signed between the Government of Maharashtra, the Asian Development Bank and the Magnet Institute. The loan has become effective from 31st December 2021 and the actual operation of the project has started.
Considering the medium-term credit and working capital needs of the state's farmer producer organizations and value chain investors, the Asian Development Bank has included the Financial Intermediation Loan (FIL) component in the magnet project. In the Project Management Manual of the Magnet Project, regarding the disbursement of funds, para No. The Asian Development Bank (ADB) will provide a loan to the state government through the central government and the entire amount will be provided by the Maharashtra government as a grant to the Magnet Institute to cover the cost of the project. Under this component, loans will be made available to selected participating financial institutions (Nationalized Banks/ Commercial Banks/ Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs)) by the Magnet Entity.
Considering the above facts, the Government was considering to disburse the funds for the Financial Intermediation Loan (FIL) component under the Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project as a grant to the Government's Magnet Institute.
Government Decision: The Government of Maharashtra has decided to set up a Magnet Institute to implement the Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project. The decision has been approved by the government.
