The Proposal:
Budgetary provisions are being made under the head Demand V-2, 2425 Cooperation, (00) (001) Operation and Administration, (01) Operation and Administration, (01) (04) Directorate of Handloom, Machinery and Co-operative Textiles (Mandatory) (24250058) for the mandatory items under the Directorate of Textiles. The Directorate of Textiles has been renamed as Textile Commissionerate as per the Textile Policy 2018-23. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to change the name of the account to Demand Number V-2, 2425-Sahakara, (00) (001) Operation and Administration, (01) Operation and Administration, (01) (04) Textile Commissionerate (Mandatory) (2425 0058).
Government Decision: Budgetary provisions are being made under the head of Demand Number V-2, 2425 Cooperation, (00) (001) Operation and Administration, (01) Operation and Administration, (01) (04) Directorate of Handloom, Machinery and Co-operative Textiles (Mandatory) (2425 0058) for mandatory matters under the Directorate of Textiles. The Directorate of Textiles has been renamed as Textile Commissionerate as per the Textile Policy 2018-23. Accordingly, the Government hereby approves the change in the name of the said head of account to Demand No. V-2, 2425-Cooperation, (00) (001) Operation and Administration, (01) Operation and Administration, (01) (04) Commissionerate of Textiles (Compulsory) (2425 0058).
