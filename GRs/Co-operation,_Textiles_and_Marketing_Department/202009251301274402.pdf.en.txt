Government's decision:
Commissioner& Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune vide No. 3 here. The temporary posts created for the audit of Padum Cooperative Societies under the letter dated 16/9/2020 have been requested to be extended for the period from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021. Reference No. 2 here. The 415 temporary posts created for the audit of Padum Cooperative Societies under the Government decision dated 26/6/2020 have been given. The deadline has been extended to 31/8/2020. Considering the vacancies in the Group D cadre of the Padum Scheme, it is proposed to extend the 408 (415-7) temporary posts from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021. 3 here. Submitted by letter dated 16/9/2020.
A Government Resolution dated 5/5/2011 has been issued to fix the revised pattern for the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles. As per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 1/9/2004 of the Department of Finance, the figure is required to be reviewed every three years. Accordingly, the figure bond issued vide Government Resolution dated 5/5/2011 has completed three years as on 5/5/2014. The proposal to fix the revised figure was received by the Government from the Cooperation Commissioner. After the approval of the high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary, the revised proposal was submitted to the Finance Department. This process is expected to take some time. Since it is necessary to decide on these posts in the interim period, the 408 temporary posts created for the audit of Padum Cooperative Societies in accordance with the decision of the Finance Department dated 31/8/2020 are being extended for the period from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021.
