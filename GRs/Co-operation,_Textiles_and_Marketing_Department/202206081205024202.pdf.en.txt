Introduction: Jaikisan Vegetable and Fruit Growers Farmers Cooperative Society Limited. Harsul Sawangi, Tt. G. Aurangabad was registered on 2.11.1987 with registration number- OGD/ RSR/ SR/ 301/87. The organisation's area of operation is limited to Aurangabad, Khuldabad, Kannad and Sillod talukas. The institute was established on 10th May under the scheme of National Cooperative Development Corporation. Pre-cooling unit of ton capacity and 50m. A total of Rs. 84.94 lakh has been sanctioned for construction of cold storage with tonnage capacity at a cost of Rs. 11.36 lakh from National Cooperative Development Corporation, New Delhi and Rs. 73.58 lakh from Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (Office 24C). Jaikisan Vegetable and Fruit Growers Cooperative Society Ltd. With the closure of all the activities of the Aurangabad Institute, the Institute was taken into existence on 19.10.2006 and was headed by Mr. S.N.Bargaje, Tatka.
The Cooperation Officer, Grade-2, was appointed as the Assistant Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Khuldabad. Sriram A. Sonne, Tatka. The Assistant Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Paithan was appointed as an ex-officio member.
As per Section 109 (1) of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the period of dissolution must be completed within 6 years. However, even after a period of 13 years to complete the demilitarized operation, the demobilization work of the institute has not been completed.

The above-mentioned fact is covered by the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961, Chapter No. 9 In view of the rules and procedures regarding the closure of the institution as well as the circulars issued from time to time by the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies and the period of stay of the institution, the Jaikisan Vegetable and Fruit Growers Farmers Cooperative Society Limited. It was under consideration of the government to approve the sale of land, construction and machinery of the Aurangabad Institute as per the procedure prescribed for the sale of the property of the Land Development Bank.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Rules, 1961. According to the rules and procedures regarding the closure of the organization in 9th and the circular issued by the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies from time to time, the Jaikisan Vegetable and Fruit Growers Farmers Cooperative Society Limited. The sale of the land, construction and machinery of the Aurangabad Institute as per the procedure prescribed for the sale of the property of the Land Development Bank is being approved under this Government Decision subject to the following conditions:
The instructions issued by the Commissionerate of Cooperation vide circular dated 27.9.2019 to maintain the sale of properties of State/ District Cooperative Agricultural and Rural Multipurpose Development Banks (SIKHAR/ DISTRICT LAND DEVELOPMENT BANK) in Avasayana should be followed.
I. In respect of the functioning of the Custodian/ Conservator appointed on the cooperative society whose registration has been cancelled vide circular dated 8.6.2020, the appointment of the Custodian/ Receiver on the cooperative society whose registration has been cancelled and the procedure to be followed by them shall be dealt with accordingly.
If there is any change in the amount of dues fixed by the institution after 25/9/2022, action should be taken at the level of the Director of Marketing, Maharashtra State, Pune. But the government should tell the truth.
IV. Jaikisan Vegetable and Fruit Growers Farmers Co-operative Society Limited. The total value of the land, construction and machinery of Aurangabad is Rs. 5,31,95, 000/-. While selling the property of the organization through online tendering, the upset price should be kept at Rs 5,31,95, 000/-.
V. The amounts due to the Government from the institution after the sale of the property of the institution should be adjusted in the first place. After that, other funds will be allocated.
