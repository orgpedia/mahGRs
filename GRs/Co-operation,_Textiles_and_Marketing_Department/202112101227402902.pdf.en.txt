The Proposal:
As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the State Government has decided to provide financial assistance to the farmers of the State in proportion to the supply of crop loans by the Government to empower the Primary Agricultural Credit Societies for the supply of crop loans. The government has decided to provide financial assistance ranging from 0.5% to 1.5% of the loan disbursement by these institutions every financial year. The primary agricultural credit cooperatives to be financed are bound to meet the conditions/ criteria mentioned in the government decision.
In line with the policy of the State Government, financial assistance for empowering Primary Agricultural Cooperative Societies under Short Term Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) in the State (Head of Accounts 24252515) under the scheme amounting to Rs. 4000.00 lakh has been approved for the financial year 2021-22. Hence, the government had decided to allocate funds for the project.
