Preamble: The Finance Department has decided to implement the revised pay scale as per the Seventh Pay Commission for the employees of the State Public Sector Undertakings. 1 hereunder vide Government Decision dated 04.08.2021. The Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation in its 238th meeting held on 19.02.2019 has passed a resolution to implement the revised pay scale as per the 7th Pay Commission to the officers/ employees on the establishment of the Corporation. Accordingly, it has been recommended to implement the 7th Pay Commission to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation. Accordingly, the implementation of the 7th Pay Commission for the officers/ employees of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Pursuant to the norms laid down in the Government Resolution dated 04.08.2021 of the Finance Department, the officers/ employees of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation have been paid emoluments as per the recommendations of the 7th Pay Commission in accordance with the provisions of the Annexure A to this Government Resolution vide Raka No. The revised pay scale of 7th Pay Commission as on 01.07.2021 is being approved subject to the following conditions:
The Corporation shall implement the revised pay scales as per the terms and conditions of the Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Finance Department/ Government in respect of the 7th Pay Commission, e.g., dated 30th January, 2019, dated 20th February, 2019 and the time to be issued.

(2) The benefit of the officers/ employees of the Warehousing Corporation should not be more than the pay scale applied to the equivalent posts in the Government while giving the benefit of the revised pay scale. The revised pay scales should be applicable within that limit.
3) The benefit of revised pay scale will be admissible only to the officers/ employees of the cadre sanctioned at present. If new cadre-wise posts are created, prior approval of the government will be required while allowing this benefit.
(4) It shall be mandatory to take action with the approval of the Government in filling up the vacant/ expired posts/ creation of new posts in the Corporation.
(5) The Corporation shall henceforth ensure that the annual accounts for each year are completed on time and submitted to both Houses of the Legislature within a reasonable time.
6) Now the first corporation has paid dividends to the central and state governments.
Every year, the board makes decisions for the finance department. The appropriate dividend should be paid to the Government regularly at the rate of dividend to be declared by the Government on and after February 22, 2012. The corporation should take a positive decision to increase the percentage of this dividend from time to time.
The Corporation shall be fully empowered to take a decision on payment of arrears for the period from 1st July, 2021 till the implementation of the revised pay scales, either in lump sum or in a phased manner. However, care should be taken that the corporation does not incur losses in the future due to this and the implementation of the revised pay scale. Also, the financial burden will be completely the responsibility of the corporation and no subsidy will be available from the government in this regard.
The said Government Resolution is being issued with the concurrence of the Finance Department and in terms of the Informal Reference No.67/ CE, dated 22.09.2021.
