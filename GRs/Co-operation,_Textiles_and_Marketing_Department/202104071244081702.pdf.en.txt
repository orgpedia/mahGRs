Introduction: Read the above. The Government had approved the continuation of 248 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative sugar mills in Maharashtra till 28.02.2021. Now, the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to continue these 248 posts with effect from 01.03.2021. Accordingly, at present, the proposal to continue the said 248 temporary posts from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Read above. The Government had approved the continuation of 248 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative sugar mills in Maharashtra till 28.02.2021. Now, in the proposal received vide letter dated 16.03.2021 from the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune, the following 248 temporary posts are being approved to be continued from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. In the meantime, the offices of the Office of the Administrator (Aastha-1) and Office of the Administrator (5-C) shall, in any case, review the sanctioned posts on their establishment and on the establishment of the offices under their administrative control and obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised figure. It should be noted that after this, the period for determining the revised figure will not be extended.
The Office Bearers (Aastha-I) and Office Bearers (5-C) should review the posts in the establishment of the Administrative Department (itself) and the establishment of the Regional Office under their control, plan to submit the proposal for the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately and take immediate action and take care to get the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the revised figure.
