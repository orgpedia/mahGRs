Preamble: In pursuance of the proposal made by the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, to pay the regular remuneration of the officers and employees working on the establishment of the Authority, the monthly dues of the hardworking, retired contract officers and employees and the regular remuneration of the regular workers, the Authority had submitted a proposal to the Legislature for the winter session, December, 2020, for a total amount of Rs. 60,13,755/- (Rupees Sixty Lakh Four Thousand only in integer) for the contractual service and Rs. 46,57,635/- (Rupees Sixty Lakh Four Thousand only) for the computer cost. Out of these, Supplementary Demand No. V-2, 2070 Other Administrative Services (00) (800)-Other Expenditure (00) (01) State Cooperative Election Authority (Compulsory) (2070 0882) 10- A sum of Rs. 46.58 lakh has been sanctioned for contractual services. The issue of disbursement of funds sanctioned through supplementary demand was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Granted by Supplementary Demand in the Winter Session of December, 2020 for the office of State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune vide Demand No. 2070 Other Administrative Services (00) (800)-Other Expenditure (00) (01) State Cooperative Election Authority (Compulsory) (2070 0882) 10- Supplementary Demand for funds amounting to Rs. 46.58 lakhs for contractual services has been sanctioned.
2. Considering the details of the above sanctioned supplementary demand, the Government have decided to disburse a sum of Rs. 46.58 lakhs (Rupees Forty Seven Lakhs Fifty Eight Thousand only) for the 10-contract service.
