The Proposal:
As per Article 243ZK (2) of the 97th Constitutional Amendment Act, an independent election authority has been created to conduct and supervise elections to all cooperative societies in the state. Accordingly, vide notification dated 30.03.2013, in line with the State Election Commission under Section 73Q of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Government has decided to constitute a separate State Cooperative Election Authority for conducting elections to the cooperative societies in the State and create 47 posts for the same. It was approved by the Cabinet at its meeting held on 30th January, 2013.
The decision was taken at a high-level meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary. As per the decision of the Government, out of 47 posts approved by the Cabinet, the creation of remaining 44 posts excluding 3 posts has been approved. Also, 44 temporary posts as per the Government Decision No. 4 here
