READ:- GOVERNMENT DECISION, SAMNAGAR DATED 16.02.2018.
Preamble: Shushrusha Citizens Cooperative Hospitals Ltd. A proposal to provide share capital to Mumbai Co-operative Society for providing affordable hospital services to the needy at Vikhroli, Mumbai was presented in the Cabinet meeting on 22.11.2017. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting. A Cabinet Sub-Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Cooperation). The cabinet sub-committee of the deceased. Pandurang Phundkar. The Minister (Agriculture and Horticulture) is not a member of the Cabinet at present. Also, the matter of re-constitution of the Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted in the wake of the retirement of Member-Secretary Mr. S.S. Sandhu, Additional Chief Secretary (Cooperation) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Shushrusha Citizens Co-operative Hospitals Ltd In pursuance of the proposal to provide share capital to Mumbai Co-operative Society for providing affordable hospital services to the needy at Vikhroli, Mumbai, the Cabinet Sub-Committee is being reconstituted with the following designation.
