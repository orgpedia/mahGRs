The Proposal:
The state government has been providing relief to the farmers who have been reeling under debt burden due to continuous drought and drought since 2009-10. As per the approval of the Cabinet in its meeting held on 24.6.2017, Govt. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017 has been announced on 28.6.2017. Thereafter, in order to ensure that more and more farmers get the benefit of this scheme, the scheme has been modified from time to time as per the decisions and recommendations of the Government of Cooperative Department at A. No. 1 to 6 mentioned above.
The Government has decided to disburse funds for implementation of the scheme" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017 (State Level) (24350082)" as per the subject-wise expenditure for the financial year 2019-20.
