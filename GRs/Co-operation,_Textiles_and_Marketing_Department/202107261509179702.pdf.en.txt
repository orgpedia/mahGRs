The central government has given. The list of posts for persons with disabilities notified vide Notification dated 4/1/2021 is published under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for person with Benchmark Disability notified on 4/1/2021 in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 7/10/2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure posts in Government service for Divyangs for the posts in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D on the establishment of Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, and to ensure these posts for the new Divyang category by reviewing the list of seats in Government service ensured for Divyangs within a stipulated period not exceeding 3 years.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that in respect of the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the state government establishment, the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the duties and responsibilities of which are similar to the prevailing pay scale, the nature of work and the designation of such posts, although different, will be ensured for the disabled in the state government service.
