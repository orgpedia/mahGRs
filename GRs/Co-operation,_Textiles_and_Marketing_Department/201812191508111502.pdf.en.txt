Government circular:
Directives have been issued to declare drought in the Kharif season of 2018 through the Revenue and Forest Department (Relief and Rehabilitation) and to implement measures and concessions in drought-hit areas. Read the above. As per the Government decision dated 23.10.2018, the State Government has been directed to declare drought like situation in 180 taluks of the State where Trigger-II of Kharif 2018 has been implemented and to implement various relief schemes and concessions.
Then the As per the decision of the Government dated 6.11.2018, the Government has issued instructions to declare drought in 268 revenue circles in the State other than the taluks declared drought.
The relief measures announced in the above mentioned Government Decisions No. 1 and No. 3 include reliefs/ measures related to the cooperative sector such as 1) restructuring of cooperative loans and 2) moratorium on recovery of farm loans.
2. (a) Read in context A. No. 1. In the Government decision dated 23.10.2018, Trigger-2 was implemented in 180 taluks during the Kharif season of 2018-19.

(b) Read in context A. No. 2. In respect of 112 taluks which have been declared as severe drought and 39 taluks which have been declared as moderate drought under the Government decision dated 31.10.2018 (c) read with reference to Annexure 3, the details as given herein are as under: The Government has decided to extend loan waiver for Kharif 2018 season in 268 Revenue Circles of other taluks which have been declared drought hit under the Government decision dated 6.11.2018. Approval is being given for moratorium till 31.10.2019 and restructuring of short term crop loans into medium term loans (tenure 5 years). Accordingly, Nationalized Banks, Commercial Banks, Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank Ltd. The concerned District Central Co-operative Banks should take necessary action.
The annexures to the above Government decisions are attached herewith for easy reference, the relevant Government decisions are available on the website of Government of Maharashtra.
As the date of repayment of crop loan for Kharif 2018 season is 31st March 2019, the crop loan for Kharif 2018 season should be restructured into medium term loan with interest for a period of 5 years with the consent of the farmers of the above affected villages who are not able to repay the crop loan within the stipulated time.
All the banks have undertaken the process of restructuring of crop loans in Kharif 2018. It should be completed by July 31, 2019 and crop loans should be made available to such farmers for the next season. The Co-operation Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Coordinator, State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC) Bank of Maharashtra, Pune should ensure that this circular is strictly implemented.
