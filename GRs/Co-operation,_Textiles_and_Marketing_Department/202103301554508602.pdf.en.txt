The Proposal:
The scheme of financing the Cooperative Consumer Societies in rural and urban areas of the State is being implemented since 1994-95 as per the Government Decisions dated 30/5/1994 and 14/3/1995, considering the important aspects such as providing reasonable price to the consumers of the urban/ rural areas of the State, as well as to the producers for the consumer products produced by them and to provide employment to the unemployed by the consumer organizations. The nature of the scheme, the pattern of funding, the criteria for sanctioning the funding, the terms, conditions etc.
This has been stated in a government release.
Government's decision:
The Scheme of Financial Assistance to Cooperative Consumer Societies in Urban/ Rural areas has been transferred to the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune vide Government Resolution, dated 26/12/2007.
