The Proposal:
The above reference No. 1 As per the Government decision dated 20.02.2008, the policy for implementation of cogeneration projects through cooperative sugar mills in the State has been decided. As per the policy, 1000 units of co-generation plants will be set up in the state in the next three years through cooperative sugar mills. The target is to generate electricity. Also, the government will provide 5% of the project cost to the cooperative sugar mills implementing the cogeneration project under this policy.
