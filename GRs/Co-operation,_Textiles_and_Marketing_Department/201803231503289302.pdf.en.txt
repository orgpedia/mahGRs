The Proposal:
NAFED has been appointed as the lead agent for procurement of various pulses, cereals and oilseeds at MSP by Government of India during 2013-14. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai has been appointed as the Deputy Agent for the implementation of the scheme in the state. Under the Minimum Support Price Scheme, a nominal provision of Rs.1000/- was made under the head Financial Assistance (2425 2417) for the collateral expenses incurred for the procurement being done by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation on behalf of NAFED. Thereafter, in the revised estimates for 2017-2018, a total of Rs. 146.63 lakh has been provided as supplementary demand during the Winter Session. 147.37 lakh has been provided. Rs. 146.63 lakhs sanctioned through Supplementary Demand has been disbursed to Panan Federation. Now to pay the advertisement payments made for the purchase under the minimum support price scheme. An amount of Rs.100.73 lakh has been provided in the Revised Estimates 2017-2018. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse the amount to the federation.
