GOVERNMENT REPRESENTATIVE: In order to provide financial assistance to the onion growing farmers of the State, an amount of Rs. 114.80 crore (Rs. One Hundred and Fourteen Crore Eighty Lakh only) has been made available from the Contingency Fund under the Government Decision, dated 16/2/2019, for disbursement of subsidy to the extent of Rs.200/-per quintal and maximum of 200 quintals per farmer for the period from 1st November 2018 to 15th December 2018 to the farmers who sold onions in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee. Paragraphs 3 to 3 (a) of this Government Resolution read as follows:
3 (a) The Accountant, Office of the Director of Marketing, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Director of Marketing, State of Maharashtra, Pune as the Controlling Officer to draw and disburse the amount sanctioned in the Government Resolution to the concerned.
