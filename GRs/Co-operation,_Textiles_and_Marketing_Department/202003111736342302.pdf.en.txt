Supplementary paper:
Farmers in Vidarbha and Marathwada have to pay Rs. 65.00 crore to the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. A sum of Rs. 6.25 crore has been sanctioned vide Government Resolution MLA 1620/ PR No. 62/7-S dated 5th March, 2020.
As per the Government Order No. The following paragraph is being inserted after the first paragraph under the heading Governing Decision in Chapter 3.
" The Assistant Registrar (Estimates and Planning), Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation, Maharashtra State, Pune, under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune, has been declared as the Controlling Officer for the expenditure of this provision (V0004).
The Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune should ensure that this expenditure is done in time by withdrawing this fund. Also, the report should be sent to the government from time to time. "
Government Resolution No: MLA-1620/ PR No. 62/7-S, Dy. Orders other than those amended as above under 5.320.20 remain in force.
