Preamble: As per Article 243ZK (2) of the 97th Constitutional Amendment Act of the Central Government, it was provided for the creation of an independent election authority to conduct and monitor/ control elections to all cooperative societies in the State. Accordingly, vide notification dated 30.03.2013, in line with the State Election Commission under Section 73Q of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Government has decided to constitute a separate State Cooperative Election Authority for conducting elections to the cooperative societies in the State and create 47 posts for the same.
The Cabinet has approved the same in its meeting held on 30th January, 2013.
The decision was taken at a high-level meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary. The Cabinet has approved the creation of remaining 44 posts excluding 3 posts out of the approved 47 posts.
