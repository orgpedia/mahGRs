The Proposal:
Sugar industry plays a major role in the economy of Maharashtra. In the last 50 years, the sugar industry under the cooperative sector in Maharashtra has made great strides in the agricultural economy. However, some sugar mills in the state are facing financial difficulties. Sugar mills have repeatedly requested the government to get government guarantee for loans from sugar mills. For this, it is necessary for the development of the state to consider and act on the proposals submitted by the factories to get government guarantees for loans.
Considering the above, the State Government through National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) is providing margin money loan to the distressed cooperative sugar mills. 2 The repayment of the loan was subject to the terms and conditions specified in the Government Resolution. Now, on the same lines, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank has decided to provide term loans to the distressed cooperative sugar mills on government guarantee. dated 06.09.2023.
