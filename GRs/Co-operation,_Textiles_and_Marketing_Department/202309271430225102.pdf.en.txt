The Proposal:
Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 21 posts. Read above. 2 temporary posts out of 21 created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks under Section 3. The Government had approved the continuation till 31/08/2023. In view of the expiry of the said extension, in pursuance of the said extension, the Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank has been constituted as one of the 21 banks to conduct audit for implementation of Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme.
02 In continuation of the temporary posts for the period 2023-24, in accordance with the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the Audit Division has been asked to work as per the requirement. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The following 2 temporary posts created for the purpose of continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Co-operative Banks are functional as per the proposal in Section 2. These posts are being approved for extension from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024.
