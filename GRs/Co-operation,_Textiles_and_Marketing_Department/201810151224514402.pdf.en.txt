Introduction: Elections to all cooperative societies in the state are conducted by the State Cooperative Election Authority. In the wake of these elections. Petitions are filed against the Authority in the High Court, Mumbai and Bench Aurangabad, Nagpur. Further, for payment of advocate fees admissible at Government rate to the advocates appointed by the Authority for election disputes filed under Rule 78 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies (Committee Election) Rules, 2014, Rs. Supplementary demand of Rs. 15,00,000/- was approved in the Monsoon Session of July 2018 and the matter was under consideration of the Government.
Kadam and Mr. Amit Borkar for the payment of Rs. 70 per cent of the agreed supplementary demand for payment of advocate fee in respect of the payments made by them. 10,50,000 (Rs. Ten lakh fifty thousand only) is being approved under this government decision.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: Finance 0618/ PR No. 60/13-C
The government has sanctioned Rs. 10,50,000/- (Rs. 10 lakh fifty thousand only) should be disbursed by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the office of the Secretary, State Cooperative Election Authority, Pune. For this, the Secretary, State Cooperative Election Authority, Pune is being authorized as the Controlling Officer.
The expenditure towards professional fees of the advocates shall be met from the provision of Supplementary Demand approved for the financial year 2018-19 under the head" 2070 Other Administrative Services, (00) (800)-Other Expenses, (00) (01), State Cooperative Electoral Authority (20700882) 28 Professional Services."
This Government has taken this decision in accordance with the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division II, Roll No. 49, Rule No. 166 is being issued under the powers vested in the administrative departments.
The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry vide reference No. 497/ 2018, Expenditure-2, D. is being issued with the consent given under 1.10.2018.
