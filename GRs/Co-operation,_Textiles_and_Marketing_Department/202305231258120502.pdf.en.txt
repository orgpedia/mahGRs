As per the provisions of clause (d) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, officers to be appointed by promotion shall join the post of promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of issue of the said promotion order.
The attention of the promoted officers is being drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said Rules, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any application for change in the post.
Before such officer joins the post of promotion, a written bond stating that he will not be entitled to promotion to the post given to him by the concerned officers on an ad-hoc basis should be taken by the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune and then the order of retirement should be issued.
The Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune shall ensure that the said orders shall come into force with immediate effect and the said officers shall join the place of posting within the prescribed time.
