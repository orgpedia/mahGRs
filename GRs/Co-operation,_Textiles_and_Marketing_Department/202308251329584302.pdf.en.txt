Government Circular: Reference No. 1 The Rules for the Regulation of Seniority of Officers/ Employees in the Government Service have been published under the Government Notification. As per the provisions of the said Rules, a comprehensive policy has been prescribed for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
As per the provisions of Rule 3 (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2011, the Deputy Registrar, Co-operative Societies Group-A, Office of the Commissioner of Temporary Seniority Cooperation dated 1/1/2022. The circular was issued on 9/6/2023. The objections received to this seniority list have been verified/ verified and resolved at the level of the office of the Cooperation Commissioner. Accordingly, the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Cooperative Societies, has directed the Sub-Registrar of Cooperative Societies Group-A to identify the officers working in this cadre. A proposal has been submitted to the Government to finalize the seniority as on 1/1/2022. Accordingly, the Sub-Registrar Cooperative Societies of Group-A. The final seniority as on 1/1/2022 is being published as per Annexure-A attached.
The date of seniority mentioned against the name of the officer of the said seniority shall be as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No.SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII, D. As on 21/6/2021, only Sub Registrar Cooperative Societies will be considered for determining seniority in Group-A cadre.
