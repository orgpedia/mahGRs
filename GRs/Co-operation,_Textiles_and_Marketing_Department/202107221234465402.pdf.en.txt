Preamble: To determine the amount due to the Government on account of Government dues of loans given by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank to various cooperative sugar factories and other co-operative societies.
A committee under the chairmanship of Shri Rajgopal Deora, Principal Secretary (Financial Reforms), Department of Finance, Ministry of Finance, Government of India, has been constituted.
Government's decision
The Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank (MSSCB) has decided to pay the dues to the government for the loans given to various cooperative sugar factories and other cooperative societies.
A committee has been formed. The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:
