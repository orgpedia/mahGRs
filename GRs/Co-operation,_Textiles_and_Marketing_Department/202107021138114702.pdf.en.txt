The Proposal:
Farmers are provided crop loans through district central cooperative banks and commercial banks. Farmers who have taken loans from the bank. The government is implementing the Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Interest Subvention Scheme to provide an incentive rebate on interest for repayment of crop loans to farmers who repay their crop loans by June 30. Due to this scheme, most of the farmers of the state are repaying their crop loans within the stipulated time. This will help banks in recovering their loans.
In view of the difficulties in the marketing of agricultural produce to the farmers during the lockdown period due to the Covid-19 situation in the state in April and May 2021 and the FRP still due to the farmers from the sugar mills, the district central cooperative banks and commercial banks in the state have given the crop to the farmers for the year 2020-21
Repay the loan. The matter of extension till 31/07/2021 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In view of the specific circumstances mentioned in the above Preamble, the District Central Co-operative Banks and Commercial Banks of the State have decided to disburse crop loans to the farmers for the year 2020-21. The deadline has been extended to 31/07/2021. Farmers who repay their crop loans within this extended period will be eligible for the benefit of the government's Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Interest Subvention Scheme.
