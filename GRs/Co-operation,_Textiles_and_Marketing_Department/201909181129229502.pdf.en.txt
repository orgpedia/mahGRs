The Proposal:
The Textile Policy of the State has been announced as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 1. Under this policy, a concession in electricity rates has been announced for textile projects. According to the referenced. As per the Government Decision dated 21.12.2018, machinery units of 27 horsepower and above but less than 201 horsepower will be paid Rs. 3.00 per unit electricity tariff concession has been implemented. In this regard, various organizations, entrepreneurs and public representatives have submitted representations to the government to provide additional power subsidy to these industries. Keeping this in view, the government was considering to provide additional concession in power tariff to the mill units having a combined load of 27 horsepower and above but less than 201 horsepower.
Government's decision
Machinery units of 27 horsepower and above but less than 201 horsepower will be charged Rs. 3.00 per unit of electricity. 3.40 Electricity rebate is being implemented through this government order.
