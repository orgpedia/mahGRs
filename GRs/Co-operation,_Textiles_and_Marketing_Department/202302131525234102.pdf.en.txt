Preamble- In order to announce the new Textile Policy 2023-2028 of the State under the Government decision in Reference No. 2, an all inclusive committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Commissioner (Textiles), to consider the recommendations for the new Textile Policy in consultation with the experts related to the textile industry and other experienced persons in the field, taking into account their useful suggestions from the experts and informants related to the textile industry and to submit recommendations to the Government accordingly. The Government was considering the inclusion of the following representatives/ members in the said Committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the information in the Preamble, the Committee constituted to suggest recommendations for the Textiles Policy 2023-2028 under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Textiles), includes the following members.
Mr. Rais Shaikh, Member of Legislative Assembly. Shri Damodar Kulkarni, former Deputy Secretary, (Textiles), Ministry of Textiles, Mumbai. Member 3. Mr. Puneet Khemsia, Bhiwandi, Thane. Member 4. Mr. Rajendra Vasam, Bhiwandi, Thane. Amit Kumar Jain, Solapur, Shri.
