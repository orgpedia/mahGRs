The Proposal:
Under the schemes sponsored by the National Cooperative Development Corporation under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, the State Government provides Government share and loan to the Agro Processing Cooperative Societies as per the approved financial plan of the scheme. The funds made available to the state government under this scheme through the National Cooperative Development Corporation are considered as a loan on the state government. To repay the instalments of interest on loan sanctioned and the loan amount eligible for repayment, out of the loan sanctioned by the National Cooperative Development Corporation to the State Government up to the year 2019-20, the National Cooperative Bank has issued a circular under reference No. 5 here. The same has been communicated to the State Government vide letter dated 19.06.2020. According to the letter, the principal amount of the loan sanctioned to the state government till 2019-20 is Rs. 2,45,01, 500/- and interest of Rs. 1,39,63, 492/- aggregating to Rs. 3,84,64, 992/- has been requested to be paid by National Cooperative Development Corporation. Government's decision:
