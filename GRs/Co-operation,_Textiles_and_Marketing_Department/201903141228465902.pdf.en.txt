Government's decision:
770 temporary posts in the audit cell of the Cooperative Department under reference No. 2 here. Further continuation has been approved vide Government Decision dated 19/09/2018 up to 28/02/2019.
As per the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the audit department has to work as per the requirement, so it is necessary to continue these posts. The government has taken a decision. The said posts are included in the format prescribed under 05/05/2011, subject to the condition that the said 770 temporary posts are not filled up. Government approval is being given to continue from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019. Details of the posts are as follows:
