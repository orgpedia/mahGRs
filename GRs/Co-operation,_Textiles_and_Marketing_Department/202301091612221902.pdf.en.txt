Introduction: Under the promotion and publicity program of the silk industry, the Director (Silk) has submitted a proposal to provide information about the sustainable income from the silk industry to the farmers of the state and the government schemes through electronic sign boards at the bus stands of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. Accordingly, the Media Planning and Planning and Implementation Committee of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has been formed. The Media Framework has been prepared in the meeting dated 11 th October, 2022. Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation
