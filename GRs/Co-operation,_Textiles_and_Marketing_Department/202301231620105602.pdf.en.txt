The Proposal:
The scheme" Atal Financial Assistance Scheme for Cooperative Societies" was approved under No. (1) and (2) of the Act to provide financial assistance to the business/ projects of the cooperative societies in the State. The scheme was implemented by the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation during 2018-19.
The Scheme was taken up in the Cabinet Meeting held on 04.06.2019
As per the decision, the extension was granted for the period 2019-20 under Read Here No.
However, the scheme was not extended due to the Covid-19 pandemic in the state. At present, the government was considering extending the implementation of the scheme for 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 with retrospective effect and further extension in 2023-24.
The Government's Decision:
