Government's decision
70 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative consumer organizations in the State were extended till 28th February, 2019 as per the Government decision, dated 17/10/2019, read with (2) above. The proposal for extension of 67 temporary posts mentioned in the proposal received from the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune from 1st March, 2020 to 31st August, 2020 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
