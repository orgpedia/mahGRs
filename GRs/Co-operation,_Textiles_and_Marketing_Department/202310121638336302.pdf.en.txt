State Agricultural Produce Market Committee. A Study Committee has been constituted as per the decision of the Government under reference to provide the benefit of Retirement Salary to the employees retired after 25.05.1967. The committee has been asked to submit its report to the government. Since the term ended on 26.09.2023, it is under consideration of the Government to grant extension of three months to the said Committee to submit its report to the Government.
The decision was taken by the state government's Agriculture Market Committee. To take a policy decision on providing the benefit of retirement pay from the government level to the employees retired after 25.05.1967. The Study Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 26.06.2023 has been given three more months i.e. It is being extended till 25.12.2023.
