INTRODUCTION: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 1st March, 2012 in the above Reference Table 1, the interest subvention scheme for long term loans related to Centrally Sponsored TUFS has been announced under the Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra.
It was under consideration of the Government to disburse subsidy to the approved proposals under the scheme from the available provision during 2022-23 as per the statement in the' Annexure' accompanying this Government decision.
Government's decision:
Under the Textiles Policy 2011-2017, interest subvention subsidy to textile units has been provided in various meetings under the scheme.
Out of the sanctioned projects, the total outlay for the year 2022-23 is Rs. An amount of Rs. 55,49,18, 200/- (Rupees Fifty Five Crores Fifty Nine Lakhs Eighty Two Thousand Two Hundred only) is sanctioned and sanctioned for disbursement. Similarly, the projects which are being implemented by the Vigilance and Control Team constituted under the Government decision in Reference No. 7.
Inspections have been carried out and only after a final decision is taken in cases where irregularities have been found.
The grant will be disbursed or further action will be taken as per the decision taken. Also, the projects which have not yet been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team due to lack of time will be inspected soon in the near future and if any irregularity is found in the inspection of that project, the subsidy sanctioned through this order will be recovered with interest.
The amount so sanctioned to the Textile Sector should be disbursed through RTGS to the accounts of the banks mentioned in the Annexure accompanying this Government Resolution as per the guidelines of the Scheme. Approved as above
