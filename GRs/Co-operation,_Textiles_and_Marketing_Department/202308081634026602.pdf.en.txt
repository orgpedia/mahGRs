Preamble: In order to make Primary Agriculture Credit Co-operative Societies in the country self-reliant on the lines of" Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan," the Central Government has approved the scheme" Computerization of Centrally Sponsored Primary Agriculture Credit Co-operative Societies" on 29/06/2022. The scheme for computerization of Centrally Sponsored Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies (CACS) is being implemented with an outlay of Rs. The Cabinet in its meeting held on 12/09/2022 has approved the Government Resolution No. 1.
Under this scheme, a total of 12,000 Primary Agricultural Credit Cooperative Societies (PACS) will be computerized, with a target of 4,000 units per year over a period of 3 years from 2022-23 to 2024-25. Under the scheme, the Centre will bear 60% of the component cost of hardware, digitisation and supporting systems and the state will bear 40%.
Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 153.25 crore. This amount has been approved under the Government Decision dated 07/10/2022 to be made available over three years from 2022-23 to 2024-25.
