Government Resolution, Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department No: Policy 2017/ No.6/ Tex-5, dated 15th February, 2018.
Preamble: Textile Policy 2018-23. The decision was announced on February 15, 2018. In this policy, a figure of 10:30:60 has been fixed for co-operative spinning mills. In the new textile policy, a one-time exit scheme has been adopted for cooperative machinery societies and cooperative spinning mills. The decision regarding the One Time Exit Policy was under the consideration of the government, according to which the decision is being taken as follows:
Government Decision: As per Textile Policy 2018-23," One Time Exit Policy" is being implemented for the cooperative machinery societies and cooperative spinning mills in the state. To take advantage of the" One Time Exit Policy," it will be mandatory to meet the following conditions.
These conditions are as follows: 1) If the one-time exit scheme implemented by the State Government for the Cooperative Machinery Societies and Cooperative Yarn Mills is to be availed by the said institutions, such institutions will be allowed to exit from the Government if they return the share capital, Government loan/ non-Government loan, interest amount on loan, amount of non-interest loan provided by the Government, etc. to them.
(2) In case of change in the industrial use of land available with the cooperative spinning societies/ cooperative machinery, it will be mandatory to pay the amount to the government as per the prevailing rules of the government, only after paying the amount, such proposals will be approved by the government.
