Preamble: In order to maintain uniformity, impartiality and uniformity in the work of filling up the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the nomination quota by direct service and to provide equal opportunities to the candidates, the General Administration Department has issued consolidated orders regarding the procedure of recruitment through a Government Resolution dated 13.06.2018. In pursuance of the said order, the Government was contemplating to constitute a State Level Selection Committee for direct recruitment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the nomination quota in the Directorate of Silk and subordinate offices of the Directorate.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government Decision dated 13.06.2018 in the context of General Administration Department has prescribed the procedure to be followed for direct recruitment to Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre posts in the nomination quota under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board. Accordingly, the Department has instructed to constitute (a) District Selection Committee and (b) Regional and State Level Selection Committees for direct recruitment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the nomination quota under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board. Accordingly, a State Level Selection Committee is being constituted to directly fill up the Nomination Quota posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C in the Subordinate Offices of the Directorate of Silk and the Directorate as under:
