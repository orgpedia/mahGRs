Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the posts in the government service are required to be fixed. The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has prepared and published the list of posts ensured for Divyangjan through a notification dated 04.01.2021 on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated 02.02.2021 to ensure that the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been fixed by the Central Government for the disabled, are in the state government establishment and the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, although the designations of such posts are different. Government Resolution No: Aastha-2021/ No.18/ Silk Room
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has prepared and published the list of posts for persons with disabilities on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. In addition to the above list, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, in pursuance of the instructions given by the Government Order dated 02.02.2021, is ensuring that the posts in Group A to D on establishment of Subordinate Offices of Directorate of Silk and Directorate of Handicrafts are made available to the PwDs as per the Annexure attached.
This Government Order is being issued in accordance with the notification dated 04.01.2021 issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India and the Government Order dated 02.02.2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of India.
