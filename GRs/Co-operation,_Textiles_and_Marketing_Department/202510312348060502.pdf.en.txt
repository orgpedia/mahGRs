2. The following procedure shall be followed in respect of preference to local candidates in recruitment:
The jurisdiction of district central cooperative banks is limited to each district. Also, all the members of these banks are from that district. Hence, if local candidates are preferred in district central cooperative THAT recruitment, such employees will be able to provide better pal to the bank's customers / members / depositors.
Directions in this regard are as follows:
70% of the seats should be reserved for candidates having Domicile Certificate / Domicile Certificate in the respective district where the concerned District Mediation Co-operative Bank has jurisdiction.
The remaining 30% of the seats will be open to candidates from outside the district. However, if candidates from outside the district are not available in sufficient numbers, these seats can be filled by local candidates.
The said Government decision shall also apply to the District Central Co-operative Banks which have published the recruitment advertisement before the date of this Government decision.
