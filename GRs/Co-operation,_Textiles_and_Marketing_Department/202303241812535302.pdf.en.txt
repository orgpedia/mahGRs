Read 1) Government Resolution No. Government Memorandum No. 2223/ PR No. 16/2023/17 Sd. 17.03.2023 It is proposed to approve the reallocation under the programme/ mandatory provisions in the year 2022-23 under the head of Demand No.V-2," 2235 Social Security and Welfare 2425-Sahakar, 2851-Village Industries and Small Scale Industries," under the control of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
The Principal Accounts under the year 2022-23 under the head" 2425- Cooperation, 2435 Other Agricultural Programmes 2851- Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 2852- Industries, 3451 Secretarial Financial Services," (Under Programme/ Compulsory) include Rs. 25383.79 lakh (Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty Three Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand only) is being approved by the Government for making available for redeployment to these schemes as shown in the attached statement-2 in the head of the head Accounts" 2235 SOCIAL SECURITY AND WELFARE 2425- COOPERATIVE, 2851- VILLAGE INDUSTRIES AND SMALL INDUSTRIES," (Under Programme/ Mandatory) as shown in the attached statement-1.
This Government has taken this decision in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under paragraph 162,163,164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules, 1977 (Part-I). It is being issued with the approval of Secretary (Textiles), Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
