Government's decision:
The 86 permanent posts for continuous and concurrent audit of the cooperative societies have been extended till 28th February, 2023 as per the Government decision dated 28/09/2022. The proposal for extension of these 86 temporary posts from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
