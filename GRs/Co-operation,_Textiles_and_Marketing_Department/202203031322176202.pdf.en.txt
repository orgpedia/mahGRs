Introduction: In various government offices and corporations, only officers with knowledge of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 and the Maharashtra Agricultural Produce (Development and Regulation) Act, 1963 are required. Hence, these posts are filled up by the officers of the Cooperation, Marketing and Textile Departments.
The posts of deputation in the department are basically from the organizations related to the functioning of the department and under the jurisdiction of the department. These posts are to be filled from their original cadre. The government has already taken the decision in this regard. There are also provisions in the by-laws of some institutions in this regard. Appointment, promotion and transfer to these posts are carried out in the same manner as in regular posts.
Therefore, only officers/ employees of the Co-operative, Marketing and Textile Departments are appointed on deputation to such office/ corporation. In accordance with the policy of deputation issued in the government decision in the context of the General Administration Department.
