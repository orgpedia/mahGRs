Government's decision:
Continuation of 1467 temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation (Administration) up to 31/8/2020 vide reference No. 2 Approved by the Government Decision dated 12/3/2020. for administrative reasons.
These temporary positions need to be continued and they need to get an extension for the year 2020-2021.
Therefore, as per the Government Decision dated 31/8/2020, the Government is approving the continuation of these 1467 temporary posts from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021.
The details of the said 1467 temporary posts are shown in Form' A' and the expenditure thereon is shown in the heading' B'.
Accordingly, the expenditure should be met from the provision approved for the year 2021 under the respective headings.
This Government Order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Government Order dated 31/8/2020 of the Finance Department.
