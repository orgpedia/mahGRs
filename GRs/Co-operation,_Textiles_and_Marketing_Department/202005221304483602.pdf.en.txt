6) Government Decision, Revenue and Forest Department, No. SCY 2019/ S.No.220/ M-7, Dy. 31.10.2019 7) Letter from the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune No. Krishipat-02/ Excessive Rain/ Damage/ Deferment of Recovery of Loan/ 2181/2019, Dy. 02.11.2019 Preamble: As per the Drought Management Code, 2016, a detailed methodology has been prescribed under the relevant Government Decisions No. 1 and 2 to assess the drought situation in the district. The second wave of drought has been imposed in 3 talukas of the state based on the situation in the Kharif 2019 season as per the criteria laid down in the Reference Government Decision and the Drought Management Code. Ground truthing survey has been conducted in 3 taluks where the second wave of drought has been affected and based on the proposal received from the State Level Drought Monitoring Committee, instructions have been issued under Government Decision No. 6 in the state to declare drought in the taluks of the state. The relief measures announced in the said Government decisions include 1) restructuring of cooperative loans and 2) moratorium on recovery of farm loans.
The standing crops in the state have also been damaged due to heavy rains during the kharif 2019 harvest season. Currently, the loan recovery season is going on and if the loan is recovered in this season, the farmers are likely to be in trouble. In view of this, the matter of deferring the recovery of loans was under consideration of the government.
2. The matter of restructuring of cooperative loans and imposition of moratorium concessions/ measures on recovery of farm loans in accordance with the Government decision in the above No. 6 of the Revenue and Forest Department was under consideration of the Government.
Government circular:
In the Kharif season of 2019, the Revenue and Forest Department (Relief and Rehabilitation) has been directed to declare drought in the taluks of the state and implement measures and concessions in drought-hit areas.

2. It is hereby notified by this circular that, subject to the provisions of section 6 of the said Act, Approval is being given for moratorium on recovery of farm loans for Kharif 2019 season and restructuring of short term crop loans into medium term loans (duration 5 years) in the 2 blocks of the Annexure attached to the declaration of drought under Government Decision dated 31/10/2019. Accordingly, Nationalized Banks, Commercial Banks, Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank Ltd. And the concerned district central banks should take necessary action.
The Annexures to the above Government Decision are annexed herewith for easy reference. The relevant Government decisions are available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra.
As the date of repayment of crop loan for Kharif 2019 season is 31st March 2020, the farmers of the above affected taluks who are not able to repay the crop loan within the stipulated time should be restructured into medium term loan with interest for a period of 5 years with the consent of the farmers.
Also, the recovery of loans related to agriculture in the above-mentioned taluks of the state for the Kharif 2019 season is being postponed until further orders. Accordingly, Nationalized Banks, Commercial Banks, Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank Ltd. And the concerned district central banks should take necessary action.
All the banks have taken action for the restructuring of crop loans in Kharif 2019 in the above taluka. It should be completed by July 31, 2020 and crop loans should be made available to such farmers for Kharif 2020 season. The Co-operation Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Coordinator, State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC) Bank of Maharashtra, Pune should ensure that this decision is implemented with care.
