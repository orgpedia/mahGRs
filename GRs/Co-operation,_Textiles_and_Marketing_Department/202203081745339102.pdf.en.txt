Introduction: Read the above. The Government had approved the continuation of 248 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative sugar mills from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 under the Government decision dated 16/09/2021. Now the office of the Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune, under reference Read No. As per the letter dated 01/03/2022, a proposal has been submitted to the Government to approve the continuation of these 248 posts. Accordingly, at present, a total of 248 temporary posts were under the consideration of the Government to continue from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022.
Government's decision:
Read above. The Government had approved the continuation of a total of 248 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative sugar mills in Maharashtra from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 as per the Government decision dated 16/09/2021. Now the Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune vide the above mentioned No. In the proposal received vide letter dated 04/02/2022, a total of 248 temporary posts mentioned below are being approved to be continued from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022. In the meantime, the offices of the Office of the Administrator (Aastha-I) and Office of the Administrator (5-C) shall, in any case, review the sanctioned posts on their establishment and on the establishment of the offices under their administrative control and the revised pattern shall be considered by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries. It should be noted that after this, the time for determining the revised figure will not be extended. The Office Bearers (Aastha-I) and Office Bearers (5-C) should review the posts in the establishment of the Administrative Department (itself) and the establishment of the Regional Office under their control, plan to submit the proposal of the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately and take action immediately and take care to get the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised figure.
