GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 25/10/2017, permission has been granted for the first time for the proper utilization of 25.25 lakh quintals of Tur procured by the State Government from its own funds under the Market Intervention Scheme during the season 2016-2017 in the State. Accordingly, the Government has decided to sell Tur dal from the cheap grain shops in the State, as per Article No. 3 dated 28/11/2018, from the cheap grain shops in the State, on all types of ration cards, as per the demand of the cardholders and all the education cards of the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority families who are currently covered under the National Food Security Scheme.
Out of the total 25.25 lakh quintals of Tur procured under the Market Intervention Scheme, about 15 lakh quintals have been sold through toddy and e-tendering. Apart from Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority family beneficiaries, other cardholders are not distributed through the Pos machine, but are sold through the receipt. For this purpose, the Government is pleased to amend Article 3 of the Government Decision dated 28/11/2017 as follows:
Article 3: All the beneficiaries of Antyodaya Anna Yojana and priority families covered under the National Food Security Scheme will be registered through PoS machine and sold tur dal.
The distribution of toor dal as mentioned above shall be effective from the date of issue of the Government Order.
