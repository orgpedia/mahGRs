Read- 1) GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: GOVERNMENT MEMORY NO. AASUA-2018/ Q. No. 13/19/17 C.D. 14.3.2019
It is proposed to approve the reallocation under the programme/ mandatory provisions in 2018-19 under the head Accounting Head Demand No.V-2," 2425-Sahakar," under the control of Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
The Principal Accounts under the year 2018-19 under the head" 2070-Other Administrative Services, 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 2425-Cooperatives, 2851-Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 2852-Industries, 3451-Secretariat Financial Services" (Under Programme/ Compulsory) have been revised to Rs. 27945.96 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Nineteen Crores Forty Five Lakhs Ninety Six Thousand only) is being approved by the Government for making available by way of redeployment for these schemes as shown in the attached statement-2 in the head' 2425-Sahakar," (Under Programme/ Compulsory) as shown in the attached statement-1.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments under paragraph 162,163,164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules Book (Part-I), 1977 and with the approval of the Principal Secretary (Cooperation/ Marketing) and Secretary (Textiles), Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department.
