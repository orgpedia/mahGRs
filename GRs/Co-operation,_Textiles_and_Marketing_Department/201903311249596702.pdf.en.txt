As on 11th January, 2017, the Co-operative Yarn Growers have received financial assistance from Financial Institutions/ N.S.V.M. amounting to Rs. 3,000/- per month for a period of 5 years and the Government will pay interest up to a maximum of 12% for 5 years on this loan. Co-operative yarn mills which have repaid the interest free loan taken from the government in the past will be considered eligible for this scheme. As per the decision of the Government, Mahalaxmi Backward Class Co-operative Sootagirani Mariya, Kadepur, G. Sangli has donated Rs. 3000/- to Rs. 6,04,80, 000/- as 12% of the loan amount. It was under consideration of the Government to provide 12% permissible interest for the period 1.7.2018 to 31.3.2019 to Sangli District Central Co-operative Bank, Sangli.
