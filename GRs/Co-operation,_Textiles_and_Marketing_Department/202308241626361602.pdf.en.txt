Preamble: The original budgetary provision of Rs. 3,80,000/- was made in the year 2023-24 under the head of Accounts 20700882, Sub-section (28) Professional Services for payment of advocate fees of the prosecutors appointed by the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune Office. In view of the inadequacy of this provision, a proposal was submitted to the Legislative Assembly for payment of advocate fees under the Professional Services Act, 2023 vide Supplementary Demand dated July, 2023 for an amount of Rs. 8, 14, 000/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Fourteen Thousand only) for the post of Accountant, Chapter 20700882, Chapter 28- Professional Services Act, 2021 in pursuance of the proposal of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune. As approved by the legislature, the matter of disbursement of funds sanctioned through supplementary demand was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Demand No. Supplementary demand for funds amounting to Rs. 8,14,000/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Fourteen Thousand only) for V-2, 2070-Other Administrative Services (00) (800)-Other Expenditure (00) (00) (01), State Cooperative Election Authority, (20700882) 28 Professional Services has been sanctioned.
In addition to the above sanctioned Supplementary Demand, the amount of Rs. 2,66,000/- out of the original budgetary provision made in the year 2023-24 under the head of Accounts 2070 0882, Chapter 28- Professional Services or in the year 2023-24, out of the provision of Rs. 8,14,000/- sanctioned by the Supplementary Demand under the head of Business Services and in the year 2023-24, the amount of Rs. 5,48,000/- is taken into account.
