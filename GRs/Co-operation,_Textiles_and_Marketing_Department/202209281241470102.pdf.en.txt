Government's decision:
86 permanent posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative societies have been extended up to 31st August, 2022 as per the Government Decision, dated 2/03/2022, read with (2) above. The proposal for extension of the said 86 temporary posts from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions: 1. Vacancies for more than six months excluding the post of Head of the Office shall be excluded.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of the temporary posts to be extended for the period from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 are as follows:
