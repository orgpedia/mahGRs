Dhananjayrao Gadgil Sahakari Prabandh Sansthan, Nagpur was established in 1972 as the second cooperative training institute in Maharashtra by the National Cooperative Training Council (NCCT), New Delhi with the prior approval of the Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India and the Department of Cooperation, Government of Maharashtra to cater to the growing training needs of cooperative departments/ institutions in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions of Maharashtra. The said Institute was recognized as a Cooperative Training Institute subject to the condition that the recurring expenditure of the said Institute shall be borne equally (50:50) by the Central Government and the State Government. Since its inception, the Institute has been imparting quality, on-demand and on-demand cooperative training, research and consultancy training to the officers/ employees, office bearers, farmers, tribal youth etc. of the Cooperative Department of the State Government and various cooperative institutions.
The Memorandum of Reference (1) of the Central Government directs the Central Government to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds. Accordingly, the Finance Department has issued revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Government Circular No. 2. To implement these Centrally Sponsored Schemes, the said institution shall be required to appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for the funds received from the Central and State Governments and to open an independent Single Bank Account in the authorized bank for the scheme and to coordinate with the said Centrally Sponsored Scheme.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SNO-2022/ PR No. 246/22-C
In view of this, the Finance Department has issued a circular regarding the appointment of an officer of at least the rank of Deputy Secretary as a nodal officer. As per the directions of the Finance Department, in order to bring more effectiveness in the process of allocation of recurring expenditure of the Central and State accounts of the said organization, the Government was considering the appointment of Additional Registrar, Cooperative Societies (Recovery and Planning), Pune under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune as a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Deputy Secretary (22-C) of this department as the Nodal Officer.
Government's decision
As per the instructions in the circular at Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, the Additional Registrar, Cooperative Societies (Recovery and Planning), Pune is being appointed as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) under the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune for the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Financial Assistance to Dhananjayrao Gadgil Cooperative Management Institute, Nagpur under the Cooperation Department and the Deputy Secretary (22S), Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department is being appointed as the Nodal Officer for coordinating the scheme.
