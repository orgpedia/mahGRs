Government's decision:
The 67 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative consumer institutions in the state were extended till February 28, 2022, as per the government decision in the above-mentioned (2), dated September 6, 2021. The proposal for extension of these 67 temporary posts from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
