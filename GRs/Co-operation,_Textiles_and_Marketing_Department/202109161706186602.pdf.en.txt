Introduction: Read the above. The Government had approved the continuation of 248 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative sugar mills under the Government decision dated 07/04/2021 till 31/08/2021. Now, the office of the Commissioner of Cooperation, and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune, under reference. As per the letter dated 10. 8.2021, 248 posts have been given. A proposal has been submitted to the government for approval to continue from 1.9.2021. Accordingly, at present, the proposal to continue these 248 temporary posts from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government had approved the continuation of 248 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative sugar mills in Maharashtra till 31.8.2021 as per the Government decision dated 07/04/2021. Now, in the proposal received vide letter dated 10/08/2021 from the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune, the following 248 temporary posts are being approved to be continued from 01/09/2021 to 28/02/2022. In the meantime, the offices of the Office of the Administrator (Aastha-I) and Office of the Administrator (5-C) shall, in any case, review the sanctioned posts in their establishment and in the establishment of the offices under their administrative control and the revised pattern shall be considered by the High Level Committee of Secretaries. It should be noted that after this, the time for determining the revised figure will not be extended. The Office Bearers (Aastha-I) and Office Bearers (5-C) should review the posts in the establishment of the Administrative Department (itself) and the establishment of the Regional Office under their control, plan to submit the proposal for the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately and take action immediately and ensure the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the revised figure.
