Government's decision:
State Farmers Co-operative Union Ltd. The 14 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Kolhapur had been extended till 31st August, 2020 as per the Government decision dated 22/7/2020 in the aforesaid (4). The proposal for extension of these 14 temporary posts from 1st September, 2020 to 28th February, 2021 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
