Preamble: Turi has been procured from Panan Federation, Mumbai and Vidarbha Panan Federation, Nagpur on behalf of NAFED in the State during the season 2017-2018. 200.00 crore was sanctioned as Government Guarantee under Department of Financial Services (DFS) Guarantee Scheme dated 14/2/2018 for procurement of Tur on behalf of NAFED during 2017-18. On the basis of this government guarantee, the Panan Mahasangh has provided loans from Indian Overseas Bank, Branch Nariman Point and paid the dues to the farmers. However, due to some reasons, the loan account of Indian Overseas Bank has not been cleared by the PNB. Accordingly, an amount of Rs. 47.50 crore has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demands for Grants in the 4th Winter Session of 2019 for the purpose of resolution of the loan account and payment of interest and guarantee charges. The matter of disbursing the funds sanctioned through this supplementary demand to the Federation is under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under this decision, an amount of Rs. 47.50 crore (Rupees Forty Seven Crore Fifty Lakh only) has been sanctioned by the Government through Supplementary Demand in the Fourth (Winter) Session of 2019 against the Government Guarantee of Rs.200 crore sanctioned to the Panan Mahasangha for procurement of Tur at Guarantee Price for the season 2017-2018, to repay the loan provided by the Panan Mahasangha from Indian Overseas Bank, Branch Nariman Point, to settle the loan account, reimburse the interest paid to the Bank and to pay the Guarantee Fee.
