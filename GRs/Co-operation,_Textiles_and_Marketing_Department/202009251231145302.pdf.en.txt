Government's decision:
70 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative consumer institutions in the State were extended till 31st August, 2020 as per Government Decision, dated 22/7/ 2020, read with para. The proposal for extension of 67 temporary posts mentioned in the proposal received from the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune from 1st September, 2020 to 28th February, 2021 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
