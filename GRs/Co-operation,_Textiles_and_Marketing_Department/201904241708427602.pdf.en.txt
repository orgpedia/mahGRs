The Proposal:
Read above. The State Government has announced the Textile Industry Policy 2018-23. Under this policy, it has been decided that the Directorate of Textiles will be renamed as Textile Commissionerate on the lines of Rohyo Commissioner, Skill Development Commissioner, Livestock Development Commissioner with a view to effective governance.
In line with the above, the Government was considering changing the name of the Directorate of Textiles to Textile Commissionerate and the name of the post of Director in that office to Commissioner and accordingly the designation of other officers in the chain of promotion also by the word Commissioner instead of Director.
Government's decision:
In line with the decisions taken in the Textile Policy 2018-23, the Government is approving the renaming of Directorate of Textile Industries as Textile Commissionerate. The Government is also approving the change in the designation of the following officers in the following table:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No. | Current designation                                             | New designation                                                         |
| 1.     | Director (Textiles)                                             | the Commissioner (Textiles)                                             |
| 2.     | Joint Director (Textiles)                                       | Joint Commissioner (Textiles)                                           |
| 3.     | Deputy Director (Textiles)/ Regional Deputy Director (Textiles) | Deputy Commissioner (Textiles)/ Regional Deputy Commissioner (Textiles) |
| 4.     | Assistant Director (Textiles)                                   | the Assistant Commissioner (Textiles)                                   |
| 5.     | Assistant Director (Technical)                                  | Assistant Commissioner (Technical)                                      |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
