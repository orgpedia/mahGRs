GOVERNMENT DECISION: This Government has approved the extension of 31 temporary posts for audit of Agricultural Processing Co-operative Societies under the Commissioner of Co-operation and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Pune from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022 subject to the following conditions.
(a) The extension shall not be applicable to temporary posts lying vacant for more than six months.
b) The reasons for which the temporary positions have been approved should be followed.
c) Take necessary action to finalize the revised figure as directed by the Finance Department from time to time.
d) If the process of revision of the revised pattern/ posts is not completed within the deadline, the temporary posts will not be extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officer/ employee, it will be the responsibility of the concerned office.
