Preamble- Ministry of Textiles. As per the order dated 14.10.2020, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation has been appointed as the Deputy Manager of the Cotton Corporation of India Limited (CCI) to implement the Minimum Support Price Scheme for cotton in the State on certain conditions. However, it has been informed that no Line of Credit, Letter of Comfort or Advance will be provided to the Cotton Marketing Federation by the Central Government/ CCI for this purpose. Therefore, the Government was considering to sanction margin money to Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation for availing loans from financial institutions/ banks for payment of cotton dues to farmers after procurement of cotton at MSP in 2020-21 season.
Government Decision During the Financial Year 2020-21, a budgetary provision of Rs. 186,58,94, 000 has been made under the head' Marginal Amount for Implementation of Cotton Procurement Scheme (64250064)'. As per the Government Resolution dated 4th May, 2020 issued in the context of the Department of Finance, instructions have been issued for fiscal measures to be taken in the financial year 2020-21 to address the impact of COVID-19 on the economy. As per the decision of the Government, the overall department will get only 33% of the budgetary allocation for the year 2020-21 for the schemes under the programme, it has been clarified to plan accordingly. However, in view of the fact that the market price of cotton has remained below the Minimum Support Price (MSP) in the wake of COVID-19 and the Cotton Marketing Federation has to purchase cotton at a higher guaranteed price in the season 2020-21, with the prior approval of the Finance Department, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Producers Marketing Federation has decided to borrow from financial institutions/ banks for timely payment of outstanding dues of cotton purchased at MSP to the farmers, from the budgeted amount of Rs. 186.5894 crore (Rs. 93.2947 crore (Rs.
