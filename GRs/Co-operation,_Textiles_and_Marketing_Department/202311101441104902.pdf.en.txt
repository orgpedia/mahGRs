Regarding fixing of the motto and motto of the Textile Department.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles,
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. 37012/91/2023-DESKTEX-2
16th floor, new administrative building
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date of release: November 10, 2023
Introduction: The Department of Textiles has to use attractive posters, flags, seminars and media to publicize and publicize various schemes related to the textile industry and to make the various programs sponsored by the Department reach the people more effectively. Also, information about the various activities of this department
Advertising is delivered to the public. Effectively in posters, flags and advertisements.
The matter of making the logo and motto of the department to convey the complete identity of the department to the people and from which the work and role of the department can be seen was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, it is being approved to use the logo and motto of the joint letter attached with this Government Resolution in the promotion and publicity of various schemes related to the textile industry by the Department and various programs sponsored by the Department, posters, flags, seminars and advertisements of the Department to be given in the press.
2. The said Government Resolution is being issued with the concurrence of the Home Department.
