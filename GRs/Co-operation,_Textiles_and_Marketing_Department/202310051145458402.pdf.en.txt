Government Decision: Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 26 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in the Central Board of Direct Taxes, Mumbai were extended till 31st August, 2023 as per the Government Decision, dated 21st February, 2023. The proposal for extension of these 26 temporary posts from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of the temporary posts to be extended for the period from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024 are as follows:
