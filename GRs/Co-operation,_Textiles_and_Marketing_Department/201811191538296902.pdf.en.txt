Introduction: As per the decision of the government, the procedure has been set for the purchase of machinery for cooperative spinning mills. The scheme was launched in 2011. Therefore, the matter of forming a committee to prepare a list of machinery manufacturers by fixing the prices of machinery in connection with the purchase of machinery for cooperative spinning mills was under the consideration of the government, following decisions are being taken.
Government Decision: A committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Director Textiles, Nagpur to fix the rates for the purchase of machinery for cooperative spinning mills in the state and to prepare the list of manufacturers of machinery. The composition and functions of the Committee shall be as follows:

Procedure: The Committee shall complete the entire process of procuring machinery for the co-operative spinning mills by preparing a list of manufacturers and a sample draft of interest and advertising it in the newspaper.
The committee should complete the process of preparing the list of machinery manufacturers within 3 months by fixing the price of machinery.
