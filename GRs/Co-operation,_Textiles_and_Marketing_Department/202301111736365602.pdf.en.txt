General Administration Department Reference No. 1 The Rules for the Regulation of Seniority of Officers/ Employees in the Government Service have been published under the Government Notification. As per the provisions of the said Rules, a comprehensive policy has been prescribed for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
As per the provisions of Rule 3 (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2011, from the date of appointment, the seniority of the Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies Group-A category has been revised from the year 2011 and the temporary seniority has been increased to the level of the Office of the Cooperation Commissioner. It was published on 12/12/2022. The objections received to this seniority list have been verified/ verified and resolved at the level of the office of the Cooperation Commissioner. Accordingly, the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies have appointed Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies Group-A. 1/1/2011 to d. A proposal has been submitted to the Government to finalize the seniority as on 1/1/2022. Accordingly, the Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies Group-A has been appointed. 1/1/2011 to d. The final seniority as on 1/1/2022 is being published as per Annexure-A attached.
The date of seniority mentioned against the name of the officer in the said seniority shall be as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No.SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII, D. As on 21/6/2021, only Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies will be considered for determining seniority in Group-A cadre.
