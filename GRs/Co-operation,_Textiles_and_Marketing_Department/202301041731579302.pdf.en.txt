As per the Government decision dated 22.07.2021, a committee under the chairmanship of Shri Rajgopal Deora, then Principal Secretary (Financial Reforms), Finance Department, Ministry of Finance was constituted by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank to determine the amount due to the Government on account of Government dues of loans given to various cooperative sugar factories and other cooperative societies. Sadar The final report has been submitted to the Government as on 20.10.2022. The committee report
The matter was under the consideration of the Department to be placed before the Cabinet for consideration and approval. According to
The Cabinet gave. In the meeting held on 13.12.2022, the following decisions were taken and the proposal was approved.
Government Resolution: The Government has decided to fix the amount due to the bank on account of the government dues of loans given by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank to various cooperative sugar factories and other cooperative societies. The matter was discussed in the Cabinet meeting held on 13.12.2022. Accordingly, the amount payable to the Bank as recommended by the Committee on account of Government dues of loans given to 12 Cooperative Sugar Factories and 1 Khandsari Cooperative Society as shown in Annexure-A is being approved.
The amount will be paid to the bank only after the recommendations of the Deora Committee and the following terms and conditions incorporated by the Finance Department are strictly complied with.
1.The concerned bank should withdraw the claims/ petitions filed in various courts against the Government for recovery of this loan in respect of receipt of the amount as guaranteed by the Deora Committee.

2) Submit to the government an undertaking that there will be no dispute in the future.
3. On receipt of guarantee from the Government, all the accounts of the loan against the guarantee should be verified and the same should be submitted to the Government.
4. In future, if any amount is received by the bank after recovery of the loan from the amount received by the bank on rent or sale of these factories, the amount should be returned to the government as a guarantee loan.
5. While giving the amount recommended by the committee to the bank, the Finance Department should decide on the payment of the amount as per the availability.
Besides, the Finance Department has also included the following two conditions.
Proposals for restoration or appropriate disposal of assets of sick cooperative sugar factories/ societies for recovery of loans will be submitted to the above mentioned company MAHA ARC LIMITED for consideration.
2. No government guarantee will be given to any cooperative sugar factory in future on account of government dues.
In this case, the Commissioner of Sugar, Pune should ensure strict compliance of the above terms and conditions by the concerned cooperative sugar factory/ cooperative society and after this fulfillment, the complete proposal should be submitted to the government for the availability of funds.
This Government decision was taken by the Committee of the Finance Department and its Informal Reference No. No. 654/2022/ Expenditure-2D. Issued on 03.01.2023.
