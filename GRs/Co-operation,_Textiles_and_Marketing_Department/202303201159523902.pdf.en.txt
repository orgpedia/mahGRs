Government Circular: Reference No. 1 The Rules for the Regulation of Seniority of Officers/ Employees in the Government Service have been published under the Government Notification. As per the provisions of the said Rules, a comprehensive policy has been prescribed for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
(2) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 3 (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2011, the officers working in the cadre of Co-operative Societies shall be appointed by the Government of Maharashtra. 1/1/2013 to d. The request for publication of provisional seniority as on 1/1/2022 has been made by the Commissioner Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies. This has been communicated to the Government vide letter dated 21/2/2023.
3. Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operative Societies. 1/1/2013 to d. The provisional seniority as on 1/1/2023 is being published as per the Annexure attached.
The date of seniority mentioned against the name of the officer in the said seniority shall be as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No.SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII, D. As on 21/6/2021, only Co-operative Societies will be considered for determining seniority in Group-A cadre.
The Commissioner of Cooperation shall bring this temporary seniority to the notice of all the concerned Co-operative Societies Group-A (including the officer on leave/ deputation).
Any error/ objection regarding the said seniority shall be brought to the notice of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies in the prescribed manner along with necessary documents in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. If the error/ objection is not received within the stipulated time, the concerned will not have any say in the finalization of the seniority.
