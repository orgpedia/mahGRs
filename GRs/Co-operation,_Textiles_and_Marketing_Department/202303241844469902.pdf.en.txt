The Proposal:
In order to make the Primary Agriculture Credit Co-operative Societies of the country self-reliant on the lines of" Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan," the Central Government has approved the scheme" Computerization of Centrally Sponsored Primary Agriculture Credit Co-operative Societies" on 29/06/2022. The scheme for computerization of Centrally Sponsored Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies (CACS) is being implemented with an outlay of Rs. The Cabinet in its meeting held on 12/09/2022 has approved the above. 1 has been approved by the government.
Under this scheme, a total of 12,000 Primary Agricultural Credit Cooperative Societies (PACS) will be computerized, with a target of 4,000 units per year over a period of 3 years from 2022-23 to 2024-25. Under the scheme, the Centre will bear 60% of the component cost of hardware, digitisation and supporting systems and the state will bear 40%. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 153.25 crore. The amount has been approved to be made available over three years from 2022-23 to 2024-25.
The corresponding Central Share (2425 2571) of Rs. 5563.00 lakhs and State share (24252589) Rs. The matter of sanctioning Rs. 3708.67 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
