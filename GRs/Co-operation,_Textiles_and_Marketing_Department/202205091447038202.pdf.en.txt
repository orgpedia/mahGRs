The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles

Government circular:
Sericulture Development Officer, Grade-I, Group-B Officers of the Directorate of Sericulture. The provisional seniority list as on 01.01.2022 (Form-A) is being published vide this circular.
The Director (Reshim) is hereby informed to bring the provisional seniority list to the notice of all the concerned officers and to place a signed copy of the intimation in the records of the Directorate. If any genuine errors or errors are found in this seniority list, the objections of the concerned should be submitted to the government within 15 days from the date of issuance of this government circular along with your opinion. Please note that if the objections of the concerned are not received by the government along with the opinion of the Director (Silk) within the prescribed time, the seniority list will be finalized assuming that the concerned have no objections.
