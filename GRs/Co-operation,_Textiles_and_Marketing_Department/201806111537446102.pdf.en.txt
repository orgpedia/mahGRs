Government Resolution No: CCR 1117/ No. 162/7-CD 8.12.2017 Corrigendum:
A Committee has been constituted under Government Resolution No. CCR 1117/ P. No. 162/7-CD. 8.12.2017 to conduct an in-depth study on the evaluation and qualitative improvement of the functioning of Urban/ Rural Non-Farm Cooperative Credit Societies in the State.
Dr P L Khandagale, Additional Registrar, Cooperative Societies (Credit Societies), Cooperative Commissioner's Office, Pune has been appointed as the Chairman of the Committee in place of Gyandev Mukne, Additional Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Office of the Commissioner for Cooperation, Pune.
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: CCR 1117/ Q.No. 162/7-S, Dy. Orders made under 8.12.2017, other than the above amendments, remain in force.
