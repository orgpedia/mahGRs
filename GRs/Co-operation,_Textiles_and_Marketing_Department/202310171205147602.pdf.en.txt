RASNIPRA/ ELECTIONS/ ACCOUNTS/ CHALLENGE-15/ SUPPORT DEMAND/ 6115/2023, dated 04.09.2023.
Preamble: The original budgetary provision of Rs. 3,80,000/- was made in the year 2023-24 under the head of Accounts 20700882, Sub-section (28) Professional Services for payment of advocate fees of the prosecutors appointed by the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune Office. As this provision is not sufficient, in pursuance of the proposal of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, vide Supplementary Demand dated July, 2023 for payment of Advocate Fee under the Professional Services Act, a proposal for a sum of Rs. 8,14,000/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Fourteen Thousand only) for the post of Accountant, 20700882, Chapter 28, Professional Services Act, 2023 was submitted to the Legislature. As per the approval of the Legislature of the proposal, the Finance Department has been requested to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 8,14,000/- (Rs. 5,48,000/- out of the original budgetary allocation of Rs. 2,66,000/- made in the year 2023-24 and Rs. 8,14,000/- out of the supplementary demand approved in the Monsoon Session, July, 2023) under the head of Accounts 2070 0882, Chapter 28- Professional Services. Under the above headings, the Government was contemplating to disburse the balance amount of Rs. 2,61,000/- out of the budgetary provision for payment of advocate fees.
