Preamble: Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies (PACS) are primarily comprised of farmers and rural artisans with the primary objective of promoting savings and mutual assistance among the members, meeting their credit needs, providing credit linked inputs, storage and marketing facilities. The need has arisen for these institutions to provide a wide range of computer technology services to their members and make them viable business ventures. Keeping this in mind, the Central Government has approved the scheme" Computerization of Centrally Sponsored Primary Agricultural Credit Cooperative Societies" on 29 th June, 2022 to make Primary Agricultural Credit Cooperative Societies in the country self-reliant on the lines of" Atmanirbhar Bharat" mission. The scheme for computerization of Centrally Sponsored Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies (CACS). The Cabinet in its meeting held on 12/09/2022 has approved and accordingly the Government Resolution dated 07/10/2022 has been issued.
Under this scheme, a total of 12,000 Primary Agricultural Credit Cooperative Societies (PACS) will be computerized, with a target of 4,000 units per year over a period of 3 years from 2022-23 to 2024-25. Under the scheme, the Centre will bear 60% of the component cost for hardware, digitisation and supporting systems and the state will bear 40%.
Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 153.25 crore. This amount is to be made available over a period of three years from 2022-23 to 2024-25, out of which Rs. 51.08 crore has been approved.
The government was considering the creation of a new head for expenditure on this scheme as follows.
Government Decision: The Government has approved the creation of new head of accounts for the expenditure on the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Primary Agricultural Credit Co-operative Societies (CAPS).
