2. Supplementary demand as shown in the accompanying statement under the Supplementary Appropriation Act aggregating to Rs. 3808,71,88, 000/- (Rupees Three Thousand Eight Hundred Eight Crores, Seventy One Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand only) (Compulsory/ Programme) (Datta Mat). After approval of the Planning and Finance Department in respect of this provision, the orders for financial sanction of the schemes relating to the expenditure of the said Supplementary Demand shall be handed over to the concerned Implementing/ Controlling Officer in column V of the statement accompanying the provision, after separate issuance of the same by the concerned Scheme Implementing Offices.
Proposals for disbursement of funds for Supplementary Demands for Compulsory Expenditure (on original lease) should be submitted to the Finance Department for approval. Proposals for Supplementary Demands for Programme Expenditure (on original lease) should be submitted to the Finance Department for approval with the approval of the Planning Department/ Social Justice and Special Assistance Department/ Tribal Development Department, as the case may be, before disbursement of funds.
