Preamble: Farmers producing Tur and Gram who had registered on NeML portal for procurement under Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme during 2017-2018 season. Subsidy of Rs.1000/- per quintal has been sanctioned to the farmers who could not procure Tur and Gram from them vide Government Decisions dated 19th June, 2018 and 1st November, 2018 respectively. The limit for the subsidy is 10 quintals per hectare and 2 hectares per farmer. Initially, Rs 70 crore has been made available to the federation from the Contingency Fund for disbursing the subsidy to the farmers and the remaining Rs 480 crore is to be provided as loan from the nationalised banks through the government guarantee sanctioned by the Finance Department. A total of Rs 340 crore has been borrowed at an interest rate of 8.50 per cent and a subsidy of Rs 399.50 crore has been disbursed to 3,13,987 beneficiaries under the scheme so far. As per the Government Decision dated 30th March, 2019, Rs.20 crore has been provided to the Federation to repay the loan taken by the Federation from the Bank. The government has to pay the interest on the loan and the interest on it. In order to reduce the financial burden on the government due to higher interest rates on bank loans provided by the Pannon Federation for providing subsidy to tur and gram farmers, a sum of Rs. 320.07 crore has been sanctioned as per the demand of the Pannon Federation in the second (monsoon) session of 2019 through supplementary demand.
The circular dated 9th July, 2019 of the Department of Finance has approved the disbursement of budgetary provisions (including supplementary demands) for the financial year 2019-2020. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 288.00 crore has been made available to the Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation (MSCMMF) under the head' Financial Assistance for payment of incidental expenses for purchase of farm produce' (2425 2542) under the Government Order No.9 (C), covering 90% of the sanctioned supplementary demand. The remaining 10% i.e. Rs. 32.07 crore was under consideration of the Government.
