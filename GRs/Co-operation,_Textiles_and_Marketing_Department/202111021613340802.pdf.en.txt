Constitution of State Level Selection Committee for direct recruitment to the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C in the nomination quota in the offices of the Commissioner of Textiles.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles
Government Resolution No.Astha-2021/ P.S. No.321/ Tax-2
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is November 2, 2021
Read: Government Decision, General Administration Department, No. PRINM- 1216/ (P.No. 65/16)/ 13-A D. 13.06.2018
Preamble: In order to maintain uniformity, impartiality and uniformity in the work of direct recruitment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the nomination quota and to provide equal opportunities to the candidates, the General Administration Department has issued consolidated orders regarding the procedure of recruitment through a Government Resolution dated 13.06.2018. In pursuance of the said order, a State Level Selection Committee was under the consideration of the Government for direct recruitment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre in the nomination quota in the office of the Commissioner of Textiles.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government Decision dated 13.06.2018 in the context of General Administration Department has prescribed the procedure to be followed for direct recruitment to Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre posts in the nomination quota under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board. Accordingly, the General Administration Department has instructed to form (a) District Selection Committee and (b) Regional and State Level Selection Committees to directly fill up the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre in the nomination quota under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board. Accordingly, the following State Level Selection Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner (Textiles) for direct recruitment to the Group B (Non-Gazetted) and Group C nominated quota posts in the office of the Commissioner of Textiles.
