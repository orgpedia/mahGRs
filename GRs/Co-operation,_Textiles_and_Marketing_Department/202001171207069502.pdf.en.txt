The Proposal:
The Department of Finance vide its Reference Government Decision dated 10.9.2001 has laid down the policy on use of vehicles in Government and other offices. For this, the Finance Department has directed each administrative department to review the vehicles they have (including the regional office). Accordingly, the matter of reviewing the permissible vehicles under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (itself), Mantralaya, Mumbai was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Considering the duties and responsibilities of all the posts in the establishment of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (DoT), the Government, as per the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, is approving the permitting of 6 vehicles as shown in the table below for the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (DoT) subject to the following conditions:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
