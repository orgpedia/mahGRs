The Proposal: -
The registration of Primary Agricultural Credit Supplementary Cooperative Societies (PACS) in the state has been approved by the government. Government Decision of 23.09.2013, d. 16.01.2015 wbd. The criteria for institution registration are laid down in the Government Certificate of ID1. However, considering the policy of the Central Government in this regard, the above criteria need to be revised, the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperatives, Co-operative Societies, Pune, vide Notification No. Under Section 4, a proposal has been submitted to the government to revise the criteria for registration of Primary Agricultural Credit Subsidiary Societies (PACS) in the state. Following this proposal, Shri. The meeting was chaired by the Minister (Cooperation), Shri. The meeting concluded on 4 November 2025. In pursuance of this ISH decision, the government was considering revising the criteria for registration of primary agricultural credit co-operative societies in the state.
The Government's Decisions:
The following criteria are being prescribed for the registration of new Primary Agricultural Credit Supply Cooperative Societies (PACS) in the state.
1) There should be only one primary agricultural credit co-operative in a revenue village.
2) Plan as per the crop loan rate fixed by Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank, District Central Co-operative Banks, Public Sector Banks, Private Sector Banks or Regional Rural Banks in the financial year immediately preceding the filing of the proposal for registration.
