Preamble: The Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted proposals under reference 1 to 4 letters for the continuation of temporary posts sanctioned under the Continuous and Concurrent Scheme for the year 2019-20. A total of 33 vacancies are on offer. 01.10.2018 to D. Read No. 3 dated 25.09.2018 was continued under the Government decision to continue the period 28.02.2019. As per the decision of the Government in Reference-2, the revised figure of total 9,319 posts has been approved as per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Subordinate Regional Offices of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles. In pursuance of the 97th Constitutional Amendment Act, 1960, the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act was amended. issued on 13.08.2013. As per the provisions of the said amended Act, the Audit Department is required to be restructured. This is likely to take some time. A total of 33 posts are on offer. The proposal for extension of the period from 01.03.2019 onwards was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government is now issuing orders in this regard as follows: To conduct continuous and concurrent audit of the cooperative societies, a total of 33 temporary posts in the 4 schemes shown in the table below have been notified in Reference No. 3 in the order. The period of suspension was extended up to 28.02.2019. Department of Finance, Govt. The temporary posts included in the Annexure as notified in the Notification dated 21.02.2019 and all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier Review but not included in the Annexure.
