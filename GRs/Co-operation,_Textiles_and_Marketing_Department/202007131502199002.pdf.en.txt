Preamble: For the newly created office of Regional Silk Office, Aurangabad, under the Directorate of Silk, under the The Assistant Director (Group A) has been declared as Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer vide Government Order dated 09.01.2013. However, in view of the response from the farmers to sericulture in Aurangabad Division and the various infrastructure facilities to be set up in Aurangabad Division mainly for sericulture and industry, the Regional Sericulture Office at Aurangabad is being strengthened. By a Government Resolution dated 18.09.2019, the post of Deputy Director (Mulberry), headquartered at Nagpur, has been transferred to Regional Silk Office, Aurangabad and the office head of the said office has been designated as Deputy Director, Aurangabad. The post of Assistant Director, Regional Silk Office, Aurangabad has been shifted to Directorate of Silk, Nagpur and the post of Assistant Director (Mulberry) in Directorate of Silk has been changed to Assistant Director (Mulberry). Therefore, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Deputy Director (Group-A), Regional Silk Office, Aurangabad as the" Regional Office Head" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" of the office of Regional Silk Office, Aurangabad.
