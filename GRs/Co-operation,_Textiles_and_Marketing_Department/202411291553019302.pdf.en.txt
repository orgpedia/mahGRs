The Proposal: -
National Cooperative Organic Organization Limited is a national level organization for the procurement, certification, testing, processing, storage and marketing of organic products produced in the country. {National Cooperative Organic Limited (NCOL)}} has been established. At present, about 4000 organizations from all over the country are members of NCOL.
The National Cooperative Organic Limited (NCOL) will provide all necessary facilities to make the organic products produced in the country available to the world market under the brand "Bharat Organics" through cooperatives and these organic products will be exported to the world market through National Cooperative Exports Limited (NCEL).
Various schemes are in place to flood organic produce in the state. However, the rate of return to productive farmers is low as the beneficiaries of these schemes do not have access to an institutionalized market. In view of this fact, it has been directed to designate the appropriate agency as the "Nodal Agency" for communication between the State Government and NCOL for the purpose of obtaining information of the organic farmers of the State, standardization of these products, creation of necessary laboratories for testing the products, etc. The Minister of Cooperation, Government of India has provided.
The Government's Decisions:
Certification of organic products in the state, as required to test the products
AAA (Organic) Institute Ltd., a state government and national cooperative for the creation of laboratories, etc. National Cooperative Organic Limited (NCOL). Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation (MCDC), Pune is being declared as the "Nodal Agency" to maintain GAIT and enter into necessary Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between them.
