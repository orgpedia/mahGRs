The committee was headed by the Chief Secretary. This committee. The meeting was held on March 23, 2021.
The committee has decided that in order to complete the construction of the yarn mill in time and to come under the yarn production, the government share should be sanctioned to the cooperative yarn mills in two phases and the government should take action to issue a decision accordingly. The matter of sanctioning government share in two phases to the cooperative spinning mills to be done as per this decision was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the above Preamble, henceforth Government shareholding to the Co-operative Yarns will be sanctioned in two phases as mentioned below: 1) In the first phase, the Co-operative Yarns will first deposit at least 50% of their 5% self-fund and purchase the land required for the project.
After that, the cooperative should submit a proposal to the government. However, at the time of submission of proposal for Government shareholding in the Co-operative Yarn Mill to the Government, it should be certified by the Bank as bankable and viable and the Bank should have received a letter of sanction for a term loan of 50% of the project cost. After receiving the government's share from the government, the spinning mill should complete the construction of the entire 25200-foot project.
2) In the second phase, the cooperative spinning mill should deposit the remaining 50% of the 5% self-fund and pay the advance for the machinery. Then the cooperative spinning mill should take the entire loan amount, acquire and install the machinery and then submit the proposal for the remaining amount of the government share to the government and after receiving the share amount, start the project for the entire 25200 square feet.
3) The cooperative spinning mill should be set up within a period of 2 years.
In exceptional cases, the period may be extended by one year with the approval of the Government.
3. The said Government decision shall be applicable to the yarn mills which are yet to be paid Government shareholding out of the yarn mills selected for financial assistance earlier as well as the yarn mills to be selected for financial assistance henceforth.
