GOVERNMENT REPORT: Out of the funds sanctioned under the Programme Expenditure Plan of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles for the financial year 2023-24, an amount of Rs. 50.00 lakhs (Rs. 50 lakh only) has been approved to be disbursed under the head of Assistance (State Level) (Programme), (2425 0133) 33 Financial Assistance for waiver of crop loans of affected farmers in the districts affected by the flood situation caused by heavy rains in the State during July 2019 to August 2019.
In this Government decision, in the fourth line of paragraph 1, the words assistance (State level) (programme), (24250133), 33 financial assistance should be replaced by assistance (State level) (programme), (24350133), 33 financial assistance.
