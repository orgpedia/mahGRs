Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017 has been announced on 28.06.2017. At present, a total of 50.60 lakh loan accounts under this scheme have received Rs. 24,737 crore has been sanctioned and 44.04 lakh loan accounts have actually received Rs. 18,762 crore has been released. Benefits are proposed to be provided to the remaining loan accounts eligible under the scheme.

The Hon'ble High Court, Bombay, Bench- Aurangabad vide order dated 27.09.2022 has directed to provide the benefit of the scheme Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Shetkari Samman Yojana 2017 to the petitioners.
