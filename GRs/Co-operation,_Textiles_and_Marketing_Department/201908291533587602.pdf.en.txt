The Proposal:
As per the Government decision in A. No. 1,2,4, 6 and 7 here, instructions have been given to declare drought in the villages mentioned in the appendix and to implement the concessions by declaring concessions in the taluks. The relief measures announced in the said Government decisions include 1) restructuring of cooperative loans and 2) moratorium on recovery of farm loans.
In accordance with the above Government Decisions of the Department of Revenue and Forest No. 1,2,4, 6 and 7 and based on the instructions of the Reserve Bank of India dated 20.03.2019, the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, Government Circular No. 19.12.2019 and D. 07.03.2019 and Govt. Repayment of loan for Kharif-2018 as on 14.06.2019 Approval has been given for moratorium till 31.10.2019 and restructuring of short term crop loans into medium term loans (for a period of 5 years) and action has been communicated to Nationalized Banks, Commercial Banks and Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank and concerned District Central Cooperative Banks.

Government circular:
The due date for processing of crop loan restructuring in Kharif-2018 is being extended from 1st August, 2019 to 30th September, 2019.

In pursuance of Government Circular No: SRIKH- 0819/ PR No. 91/18-C, all the Banks have decided to proceed with the restructuring of crop loans in Kharif 2018. This should be completed by September 30, 2019 and crop loans should be made available to such farmers for the next season. The Co-operation Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune and the Coordinator, State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC) Bank of Maharashtra, Pune should ensure that this circular is strictly implemented.
