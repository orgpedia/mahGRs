Preamble: In order to provide financial assistance to the onion farmers in the State in view of the fall in the onion market in October 2018, the Government has sanctioned a subsidy of Rs.200/-per quintal to the farmers who sold onions in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee from 1st November 2018 to 15th December 2018 as per the decision dated 26/12/2018. This subsidy is applicable to farmers who have sold onions in the Agricultural Produce Market Committees of the state as well as in the private market committees of Prasanna Agriculture Market Paldi Aale, G. Ahmednagar. Thereafter, the period of Onion Subsidy has been extended from time to time and the revised period of Onion Subsidy Subsidy has been fixed from 1st November, 2018 to 28th February, 2019 as per Government Decision, dated 8/3/2019.

An amount of Rs. 114.48 crore has been provided as contingency fund for 1,60,697 eligible beneficiary farmers who have been sold between 1st November, 2018 and 15th December, 2018. As per the proposal received from the Director, Marketing, Maharashtra State, Pune, a sum of Rs. 387,30,31, 000 has been sanctioned as Supplementary Demand in the 2nd (Monsoon) Session of 2019 for disbursement of subsidy to the farmers for the remaining period from 16th December, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.
The government was considering to distribute the funds received through the supplementary demand to eligible onion farmers in the state at the rate of Rs 200/- per quintal.
Government Decision: State Agricultural Produce Market Committee Prasanna Agricultural Market Paddali, Dist. The Government is providing an amount of Rs. 387,30,31, 000 (Rupees Three Hundred and Eighty Seven Crore Thirty Lakh Thirty One Thousand only) for the distribution of subsidy to the extent of Rs.200/-per quintal and up to a maximum of 200 quintals per farmer for the period from 16.12.2018 to 28.02.2019 to the farmers who have sold onions in Ahmednagar Private Market Committee.
Expenditure under the scheme shall be met from the funds made available under the following headings: Demand No.V-2 2425 Support 107 Assistance to Cooperative Credit Societies (01) Assistance to Cooperative Credit Societies (01) (08) Financial assistance to Onion growing farmers Datta Mat (Compulsory) (24251421) 33 Financial assistance.
The Government is authorizing the Accountant, Marketing Director, Office, Maharashtra State Pune as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Marketing Director, Maharashtra State, Pune as the Controlling Officer to scrutinize all the proposals received under this scheme and to disburse the subsidy to the eligible beneficiaries district-wise and to withdraw and disburse the amount sanctioned in the Government Decision to the concerned.
The Director of Marketing shall disburse the amount of subsidy from the Government treasury to the eligible beneficiary farmers on behalf of the Government before 31st August, 2019 and submit a report to the Government.
