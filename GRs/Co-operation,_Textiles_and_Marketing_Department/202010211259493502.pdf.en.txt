Preamble: Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 25 posts. Also refer to no. 25 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies under Section 3 of the Act.
The Government had approved the continuation till 31/08/2020. Since the above extension is expiring, No. A proposal has been submitted to the Government through the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune, to work as per the requirement of the Audit Department in accordance with the 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of 25 temporary posts for the period 2020-21. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the proposal in Section 2, 25 temporary posts created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Tribal Development Corporation and Tribal Cooperative Societies will be given to the new posts. from 01/09/2020. The extension is being granted by this Government decision till 28/02/2021.
