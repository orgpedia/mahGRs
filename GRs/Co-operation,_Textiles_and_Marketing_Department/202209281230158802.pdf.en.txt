Government Decision: Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 26 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit in the Department of Financial Services, Government of India, Mumbai were extended till 31/8/2022 vide Government Resolution, dated 2 nd March, 2022. The proposal for extension of these 26 temporary posts from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
1) The temporary posts should be filled in the prescribed manner.
2) Ensure that the audit fee is paid in full.
