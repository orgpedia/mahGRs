Government's decision:
State Farmers Co-operative Union Ltd. The 14 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit of Kolhapur were extended till 28th February, 2020 as per the Government decision, dated 17/10/2019, read with (2) above. The proposal for extension of these 14 temporary posts from 1st March, 2020 to 31st August, 2020 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
