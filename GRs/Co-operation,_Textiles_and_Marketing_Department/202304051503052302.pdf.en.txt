Government Circular: Reference No. 1 The Rules for the Regulation of Seniority of Officers/ Employees in the Government Service have been published under the Government Notification. As per the provisions of the said Rules, a comprehensive policy has been prescribed for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
As per the provisions of Rule 3 (b) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2011, the Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies (Audit) Group-A/ Co-Managing Cooperative Societies (Audit) Group-A and Special Auditor Class-I of officers working in the cadre of Cooperative Societies Group-A. The request for publication of final seniority as on 1/1/2022 was made by the Commissioner Cooperation and Registrar Cooperative Societies. As per letter dated 8/12/2022 to Govt.
Additional Registrar on the establishment of Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies Group-A/ Co-Managing Cooperative Societies (Audit) Group-A and Special Auditor Class-I. The final seniority as on 1/1/2022 is being published as Annexure-A, B and C attached.
The date of seniority mentioned against the name of the officer in the said seniority shall be as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No.SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ XII, D. As on 21/6/2021, only Additional Registrar Cooperative Societies (Audit) Group-A/ Co-Managing Cooperative Societies (Audit) Group-A and Special Auditor Class-I Cooperative Societies will be considered for determining seniority in Group-A cadre.
