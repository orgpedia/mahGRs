Introduction: Refer No. As per the decision of the Government dated 5th October, 2018, 23 temporary posts required to implement the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme for the Co-operative Yarn Mills were approved to be continued from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
The proposal for continuation of 23 temporary posts in the scheme from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 5th May, 2011, the revised figure of 9319 posts has been approved as per the review of permanent and temporary posts in this department.
The Government is approving the continuation of the 23 temporary posts from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 as they include the 23 temporary posts shown below under the Continuous and Concurrent Audit Scheme.
