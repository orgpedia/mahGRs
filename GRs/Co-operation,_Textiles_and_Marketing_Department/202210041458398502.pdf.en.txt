The Proposal:
Subject to Government Resolution No. As per the review of permanent/ temporary posts in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under Section 1, the revised figure of the Department of Cooperation has been fixed and the figure includes the following 21 posts. Read above. 2 temporary posts out of 21 created for continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks under Section 3.
The Government had approved the continuation till 31/08/2022. In view of the expiry of the said extension, accordingly, the Audit Department is directed to work as per the requirement in accordance with the 97th Constitutional Amendment regarding the continuation of the 2 temporary posts for the period 2022-23 in the Maharashtra State Cooperative Banks. The proposal has been submitted to the government through the office of the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies. The State of Maharashtra, Pune. The following 2 temporary posts created for the purpose of continuous and concurrent audit of Maharashtra State Co-operative Banks are functional as per the proposal in Section 2. The extension of these posts from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 is being approved through this Government decision.
