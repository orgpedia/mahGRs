GOVERNMENT DECISION: To constitute a study group to examine various aspects regarding private market yards in the State. 1 has been approved by the Government here. Under this Government decision, it is being approved to include the study of the difficulties of traders and farmers in the following selected market committees in the scope of the study group constituted under this Government decision.
Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Navi Mumbai.
Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Nashik.
Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Nagpur.
Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Pune.
Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Sangamner, Ahmednagar.
Further, the Government have approved the inclusion of Mr. D.M. Palodkar, Retired Joint Registrar as a member of the said Study Group. Accordingly, the composition of the study group will be as follows:
