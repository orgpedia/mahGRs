Government Decision: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity in view of their eligibility/ ineligibility to remain in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, to continue the service of the competent and efficient officers/ employees and to prematurely retire the officers/ employees of incompetent and doubtful integrity from Government service.
In accordance with the said Review Policy, the Commissioner of Cooperation and the Registrar Cooperative Societies are constituting the following Divisional Review Committee to review the officers who are eligible for review on establishment of Cooperative Societies with a view to test their eligibility/ ineligibility to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and to take further action as required by rules:
(a) For Group-A (Gazetted) (Excessive Senior Grade) Grade Pay of Rs.10,000 and above 2) Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Grade) Grade Pay of Rs.7,600 and above but less than Rs.10,000 3) Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Grade) Grade Pay of Rs.7,600 and below 4) Group-B (Gazetted) Officers, the Departmental Review Committee shall be as follows:
