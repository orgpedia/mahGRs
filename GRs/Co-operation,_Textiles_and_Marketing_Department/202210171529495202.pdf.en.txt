The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 26.12.2018, initially, subsidy has been sanctioned to eligible farmers at the rate of Rs.200 per quintal and maximum of Rs.200 per quintal per farmer for the period from 1st November, 2018 to 15th December, 2018 for the sale of Onions in the Agricultural Produce Market Committees and Private Market Committees of Prasanna Agricultural Market, Padliyale, G. Ahmednagar.
The period of Onion Subsidy under the Scheme has been extended from time to time and the revised due date has been fixed from 1st November, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.
