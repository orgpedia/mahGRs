Preamble: Out of the total 17 zonal offices in Aurangabad city under the Department of Cooperation, 13 zonal offices are functioning on lease in private premises. A large amount of government money is spent on the annual rent of this regional office. In order to reduce the cost of renting, all the regional offices of the cooperative department in Aurangabad city will be established under one roof. The proposal received through a letter of reference from the Commissioner for Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune for construction of" Cooperative Complex" building at 17370 Sectors at an estimated cost of Rs. 14077.75 lakh was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative and financial sanction.
Government Decision: Out of the total 17 zonal offices under the Cooperative Department, 13 zonal offices are functioning in private space on lease basis. For this purpose, all the zonal offices of Aurangabad city will be established under one roof at Kranti Chowk, Aurangabad. It is proposed to construct a building of" Sahakar Sankul" at Aurangabad on the land measuring 7683.10 sq.m. in 17370. The proposal for construction of the building" Sahakar Sankul," with an estimated total budget of Rs. 14077.75 lakhs (as per the current schedule), was submitted for technical/ administrative approval by the office of the Commissioner of Cooperation, Pune under reference letter. Accordingly, the proposal. A High Level Committee of Secretaries under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary was constituted on 11.07.2023.
