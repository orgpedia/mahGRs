GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Seven temporary posts in the Directorate of Marketing were extended till 28th February, 2022 vide Government Decision (2) dated 30th August, 2021.
The proposal for extension of these 7 temporary posts from 1st March, 2022 to 31st August, 2022 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
