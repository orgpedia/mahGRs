It should also be ensured that the utilization of the said amount is completed before the end of the project period i.e. by 31.10.2018.
All the Controlling Authorities shall ensure that the funds sanctioned under this Government Decision are utilized for the implementation of the approved components/ sub-components under the project and within the approved expenditure limit in the manner prescribed under the project.
The compliance report and utilization certificate in this regard should be submitted to the Project Director, Maharashtra Competitive Agriculture Development Project.
