The Proposal:
In the meeting held on Wednesday, 15.9.2021, the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Planning) to fix the rates of collateral cost for procurement under Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme under the Reference Government Order, dated 3/9/2019, discussed in detail the fixation of transportation rates for the season 2020-21, fixation of collateral rates for the season 2021-22 and other related issues. Transport rates have been fixed for procurement under Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme for the season 2019-2020 as per Government Decision, dated 19/3/2021. Government's decision:
Steps should be taken to fix the rates for transportation of pulses and oilseeds procured under the Minimum Support Price Scheme from procurement centres to warehouses of Warehousing Corporation as under: i) The proposed transportation rates for the season 2020-21 jointly submitted by NAFED, Mumbai and Panan Mahasangh, Mumbai are being approved. The rates shall be as per Annexure-A.
(ii) The rates for traffic for a distance of 50 km and above as prescribed under the guidelines of the Minimum Support Price Scheme shall also be determined in accordance with the formula specified in paragraph 2.
The rates fixed for the season 2020-21 shall continue to be applicable for the season 2021-22 also.
(iv) Transport rates shall be determined in accordance with the formula referred to in paragraph 2 if the purchase is made in a district other than the district in which the transport rate is agreed.
(v) These rates shall be applicable to all Central Nodal Agencies in the State.
