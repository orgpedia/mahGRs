GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. In respect of waiver of loans taken by farmers of Vidarbha and Marathwada from licensed money lenders, in terms of the eligibility criteria of the beneficiaries of the Government Decision referred to above, clause No. 1 (3) is being relaxed. However, all other conditions of the scheme will remain the same.

2. Condition No. in the decision of the Government in context. As per clause (3) of clause (1), only those farmers who are ineligible outside the purview of the list submitted by the Taluk/ District Level Committee during the implementation of the original scheme will be eligible for the loan waiver scheme after the relaxation of the condition. However, such farmers will be required to fulfil all other conditions as may be prescribed in the relevant Government Order.
(3) No new lists to be submitted shall be considered while implementing the scheme.
The Taluk/ District level committees mentioned in the relevant Government decision should examine/ re-examine the applications of the farmers who were ineligible due to the condition of jurisdiction in the list of loanee farmers submitted in the original loan waiver scheme.
5. After the scrutiny/ re-verification of the above mentioned lists by the District Level Committee, in the cases where eligible, the licensed money lenders shall consider the loan disbursed to the out of the jurisdiction loanee farmers as on 30.11.2014 and the interest accrued till the date of sanction by the District Level Committee and pay the amount accordingly to the concerned.
The condition of the work area is being relaxed only one time.
The implementation of the scheme should be completed by 31.3.2020. Accordingly, the District Level Committees should complete the verification/ refund of the list of loanee farmers by 29.2.2020 and the process of payment to the concerned should be completed by 31.3.2020.
