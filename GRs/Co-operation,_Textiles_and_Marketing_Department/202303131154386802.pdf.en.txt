Preamble: Creation of storage capacity with all associated facilities, especially in rural areas, to help the farmers of the State to store farm processing and agri-inputs, promotion of quality of agricultural processes to improve their marketability, prevention of wastage and deterioration of farm produce by providing access to finance and marketing credit, creation of infrastructure for small farmers with respect to farm produce that can be stored in godowns, revival of the process of agricultural investment by encouraging private and cooperative sectors to invest in the construction of agricultural godowns in India, so that they are easily accessible to farmers for storing their farm produce. To implement the scheme at the state level, the objectives, criteria, guidelines, subsidies mentioned in the Rural Storage Scheme of the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) need to be deliberated at the primary level.
Accordingly, the matter of forming a' Study Committee' for the' Village There Godown' scheme was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to enable the farmers of the State to store their farm produce safely, to prevent loss of produce and to ensure that they do not have to sell their produce at a lower price, a Study Committee is being constituted for the proposed scheme of' Gaon Vahit Godown" as follows:
