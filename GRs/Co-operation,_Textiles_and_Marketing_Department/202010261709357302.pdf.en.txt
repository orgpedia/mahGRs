Introduction: Refer Note No. Under Section 2, the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune had approved the continuation of 31 temporary posts in the Agricultural Processing Cooperative Society under this scheme till 31/08/2020. Reference No. 4 As per the letter here, the Cooperation Commissioner has requested for the extension of 31 temporary posts in the scheme. Now, the Finance Department is authorized to extend the period from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021. There are 3 administrative departments.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: This Government has approved the extension of 31 temporary posts for audit of Agricultural Processing Cooperative Societies under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021 subject to the following conditions.
(a) The extension shall not be applicable to temporary posts lying vacant for more than six months.
b) The reasons for which the temporary positions have been approved should be followed.
c) Take necessary action to finalize the revised figure as directed by the Finance Department from time to time.
d) If the process of revision of the revised pattern/ posts is not completed within the deadline, the temporary posts will not be extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officer/ employee, it will be the responsibility of the concerned office.
