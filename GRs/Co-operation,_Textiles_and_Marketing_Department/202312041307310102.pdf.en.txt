Date: December 4, 2023.
Introduction: The role of urban cooperative banks in the economic development of Maharashtra has been very important. At present, there are 435 urban cooperative banks in the state.
Through urban cooperative banks, loans are provided to businessmen and common people of the state.
After the outbreak of Covid-19 in the state, the business of urban cooperative banks has suffered a lot. To solve these problems. A meeting was held under the Chairmanship of Minister (Cooperation) on 19.10.2023 at Mantralaya, Mumbai. In the meeting, it was decided to form a committee to study the problems faced by the urban cooperative banks in the state and submit a recommendation report to the government.
Accordingly, a proposal to set up a committee to study the problems faced by urban cooperative banks was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
To help the urban cooperative banks in the state, the following committee is being formed to study the problems faced by these banks and submit a report to the government.
