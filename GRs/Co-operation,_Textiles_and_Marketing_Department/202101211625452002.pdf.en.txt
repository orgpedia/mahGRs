Preamble: The New Textile Policy 2018-23 of the State has been notified vide Government Resolution dated 15th February, 2018. Under this Textile Policy, it has been decided to create infrastructure and strengthen existing facilities under the Silk Development Programme to boost sericulture and industry in the state. Accordingly, the reference was made. Under the Government decision dated 3 rd May, 2018, a new scheme" Creation of Infrastructure and Strengthening of Existing Facilities under Silk Development Programme" has been launched.
At present, only one government egg production centre is functioning in Gadhinglaj, Kolhapur. As about 20 lakh eggs produced at this centre are insufficient for supply to the farmers, more eggs are being ordered from the Central Silk Board, Bengaluru. There should be no shortage of eggs in the state.
