To sanction interest free loan to Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation for payment of cotton dues to farmers for 2018-19 season.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles
GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. KAVAS-2018/ PR No. 39/10-C
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 15th of December 2018
As per the order dated 24.09.2018 of the Ministry of Textiles, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Federation has been appointed as the Deputy Manager of Cotton Corporation of India Limited (CCI) for implementation of Minimum Support Price (MSP) scheme for cotton in the State. However, no Line of Credit, Letter of Comfort or Advance will be issued for this purpose. For this, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers' Marketing Federation was under the consideration of the government to sanction an interest-free loan to pay the amount to the farmers after the purchase of cotton in the 2018-19 season.
Government Decision During the financial year 2018-19, a budget provision of Rs. 60.00 crore has been made under the head" Marginal Amount (Programme) for Implementation of Cotton Procurement Scheme."
The Government is approving to provide an amount of Rs. 42.00 crore (Rupees Forty Two Crores only) to Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Marketing Federation as interest free loan from the budgeted amount of Rs. 60.00 crore (Rupees Sixty Crores only) for payment of guarantee price of cotton procured/ to be procured from farmers by Cotton Growers Marketing Federation under Cotton Purchase Scheme 2018-19.
The Maharashtra State Cooperative Cotton Growers Federation should enter into a formal agreement with the Cotton Corporation of India in this regard.
