Introduction: Under the Centrally Sponsored Pilot Scheme of In-Situ Upgradation of Plain Powerloom for SSI Sector, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 15,000 and up to Rs. 1,20,000 is being provided by the State Government@ 33.33 per cent of the total cost of machinery or upto a maximum of Rs. 10,000 up to a maximum of Rs. The scheme is being implemented in Nagpur, Malegaon, Ichalkaranji, Solapur, Bhiwandi and Vita clusters. Under the pilot scheme of In-Situ Upgradation of Plain Powerloom for SSI Sector, the rate of subsidy has been revised for a maximum of eight units.
Considering the reforms made by the Central Government, the State Government as an auxiliary scheme to the Central Government Scheme. The scheme has been included in the State Textile Policy 2018-23. Accordingly, the matter of making changes in the existing state government scheme was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 1 The Government hereby authorize the State Government to launch the following scheme of financial assistance to the machinery owners who have received financial assistance from the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Pilot Scheme of In-Situ Upgradation of Plain Powerloom for SSI Sector, as per the price fixed by the Central Government.

Under this scheme, each machine owner will be eligible for financial assistance for a maximum of eight machines. Also, if the Central Government revises the scheme/ subsidy amount, the State Government will also revise the scheme/ subsidy amount accordingly.
Method of implementation of the plan:
The Director (Textiles), Nagpur will receive the list of beneficiaries of the Centrally Sponsored Pilot Scheme of In-Situ Upgradation of Plain Powerloom for SSI Sector from the Commissioner of Textiles, Government of India every three months along with all the details.
(2) The Director shall prepare a priority list of the machinery owners who are to be financed and shall display the same on the notice board in his office as well as in the office of the concerned Regional Deputy Director Textiles.
The Director (Textiles), Nagpur shall examine the beneficiary-wise list of beneficiaries of the machinery procured from the Commissioner of Textiles, Government of India and submit the same to the Government for approval.
4) The proposal received from the Director (Textiles), Nagpur, will be examined at the government level and the admissible subsidy from the government will be deposited in the bank account of the eligible beneficiaries.
5) The Director (Textiles), Nagpur shall ensure that the admissible subsidy from the government is properly utilized after it is credited to the bank accounts of eligible beneficiaries.
Terms and Conditions of the grant:
1) The beneficiary should have been sanctioned subsidy by the Central Government under the Centrally sponsored" Pilot Scheme of In-Situ Upgradation of Plain Powerloom for SSI Sector."
2) The officer of the textile department will have to provide the machinery for inspection by the beneficiaries.

The Director (Textiles), Nagpur shall receive the application from the beneficiary in the list of beneficiaries received from the Commissioner of Textiles, Government of India, along with their identity card (election letter, Aadhaar card, electricity bill or other identity card approved by the State Government) and submit it along with the proposal.
4) Details of the beneficiary's bank account (name of the bank branch, account number, IFSC code, MICR code, etc.) and a photocopy of the first page of the bank passbook should be submitted along with the proposal to the Director (Textiles), Nagpur.

The Appropriation Certificate has to be submitted to the State Government within two months after the grant is sanctioned.
