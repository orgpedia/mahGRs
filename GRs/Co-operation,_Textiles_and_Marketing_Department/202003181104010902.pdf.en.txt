Read- 1) Government Resolution No. Government Memorandum No. 1819/ PR No. 48/19/17 S.D. 13.03.2020. It is proposed to approve the reallocation under the programme/ mandatory provisions in the year 2019-20 under the head of Demand No.V-2," 2425-Sahakar, 2851-Village Industries and Small Scale Industries," under the control of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles.
The Principal Accounts under the year 2019-20 under the head" 2425-Cooperatives, 2851-Village Industries and Small Scale Industries," (Under Programme/ Compulsory) with an outlay of Rs. 7560.61 lakhs (Rupees Seventy Five Crores Sixty Lakh Sixty Seven Thousand only) is being approved by the Government for making available the same as shown in the attached Statement-1 along with savings as shown in the attached Statement-2 in the Head Accounts Head" 2425 Cooperation, 2851- Village Industries and Small Scale Industries" (Under Programme/ Compulsory).
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under paragraph 162,163,164 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules Book (Part-I), 1977 and with the approval of the Principal Secretary (Marketing/ Cooperation/ Textiles), Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department.
