A subsidy of Rs 200 per quintal has been sanctioned to eligible farmers and a maximum of 200 quintals per farmer for the period from 2018 to December 15, 2018.
Under this scheme, the period of onion subsidy was extended from time to time. The revised deadline was set from November 1, 2018 to February 28, 2019.
A sum of Rs. 114.80 crore was provided from the Contingency Fund for disbursement of first phase of Onion Subsidy vide Government Resolution dated 16/2/2019. Thereafter, Rs.387.3031 crore has been provided for the second phase of subsidy through Supplementary Demands for Grants issued vide Government Resolution, dated 7/8/2019. So far, a total of Rs. 504.40 crore has been disbursed to 5,53,857 farmers.
The auditor had re-examined and approved the applications received by the farmers who were disqualified from the applications received for onion subsidy by the Screening Committee, Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Solapur. The approved application was left to be included in the beneficiary list due to technical reasons. The Directorate of Marketing vide letter dated 13.12.2019 has demanded to provide subsidy of Rs. 2,64,59, 700/-for 1172 beneficiaries of Agriculture Produce Market Committee, Solapur. Accordingly, a supplementary demand for Rs. 2,64,59, 000 has been approved in the Monsoon Session of August, 2022 for making available and disbursing the pending onion subsidy for the year 2018-19 for Agriculture Produce Market Committee, Solapur. It was under consideration of the Government to approve disbursement of the sanctioned amount of Rs. 2,64,59, 000 and the budgetary provision of Rs. 1000 for the year 2022-23 to the eligible beneficiaries who have sold onions in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Solapur.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The State Cabinet in its meeting dated 20/12/2021 has decided to sanction subsidy to the extent of Rs.200 per quintal and maximum of Rs.200 per quintal to the eligible farmers who have sold onion in the Agricultural Produce Market Committees and Prasanna Agriculture Market, Padliyale Private Market Committees of the State from 1st November, 2018 to 15th December, 2018.
