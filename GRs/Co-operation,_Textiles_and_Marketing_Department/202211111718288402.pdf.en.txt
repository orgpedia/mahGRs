Preamble: As per the 97th Constitutional Amendment to the Constitution of India, the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 was amended and the State Cooperative Election Authority was constituted as per the notification dated 30th March, 2013 in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 73CB of the said Act. Accordingly, the State Cooperative Election Authority was entrusted with the constitutional responsibility of conducting elections to cooperative societies in the state.
A total of 44 posts have been created for the functioning of the Authority as per the Government Decision dated 31st October, 2013. For the post of Commissioner on the State Cooperative Election Authority, the provision has been made in Section 73AB Potkalm 2 of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act 1960, according to which the order for the recruitment of the post of Commissioner, State Cooperative Election Authority has been issued. Further, Rule 4 of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Election Authority (Conditions of Service of Commissioners) Rules, 2014 provides for expenditure on salary and allowances, accommodation, leave, leave sanctioning authority, gratuity, conveyance, travel allowance, telephone facility, medical treatment, office meetings and guest allowance, other allowances etc. However, as there was no clarity on the selection of the post of Commissioner, State Cooperative Election Authority, the government was considering the formation of a selection committee to facilitate the selection of a suitable candidate for the post.
Government's decision:
For the selection of candidates as Commissioner, State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, a short list of candidates will be prepared from the applications received in accordance with the advertisement. The Selection Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra as follows:

(a) The selection committee. With the approval of the Minister (Cooperation), Shri. The proposal will be forwarded to the Chief Minister.
(a) Ma. In accordance with the directions of the Chief Minister, the proposal for appointment to the post of Commissioner, State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, as per the provisions of sub-section 2 of section 73AB of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, is being sent to the Chief Minister's Office. It will be submitted to the Governor for approval.
