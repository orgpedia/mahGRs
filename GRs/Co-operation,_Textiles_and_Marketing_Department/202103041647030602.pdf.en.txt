Government's decision:
The Directorate of Silk has been established by the Government Decision dated 27.08.1997 in the context of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour and the posts have been transferred from various establishments to the Directorate of Silk. The review of 373 posts under Director (Silk) has been approved by the High Level Committee and orders in this regard have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 22.03.2011. Further, vide Government Resolution dated 22.03.2012, 6 new posts have been sanctioned along with creation of new District Silk Office at Jawhar, District Palghar. A total of 379 posts are sanctioned under the Directorate of Silk. A total of 303 posts have been made permanent and 76 are temporary. The continuation of these temporary posts from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 10.09.2020.
Now, vide letter dated 01.03.2021 addressed to Director (Silk), a proposal has been received for continuation of the said 76 temporary posts.
All administrative departments are empowered to extend the term of all temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the rolls from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
