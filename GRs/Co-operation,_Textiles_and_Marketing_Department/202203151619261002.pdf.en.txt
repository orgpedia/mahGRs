Preamble: The Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Government decision dated 12th April, 2018 to examine the eligibility/ ineligibility of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on establishment of Directorate of Silk to continue in Government service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service. As per the Government Decision dated 10th June, 2019 in the context of General Administration Department, a consolidated procedure has been prescribed for the review of services of Government officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service. Annexure-A to the said Government Decision prescribes the constitution of Divisional Review Committees for reviewing the services of Government officers/ employees and accordingly instructions have been issued for the constitution of Divisional Review Committees in each administrative division.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Departmental Review Committee to examine the eligibility/ ineligibility of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on establishment of Directorate of Silk beyond 50/55 years of age or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
