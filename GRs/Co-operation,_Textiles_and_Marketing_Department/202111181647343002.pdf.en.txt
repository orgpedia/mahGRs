Dhananjayrao Gadgil Institute of Cooperative Management, Nagpur was established in 1972. Accordingly, the second cooperative training school in Maharashtra has been started in Nagpur. The cost will be shared equally by the Centre and the state government (50:50). Accordingly, funds are being provided in the budget under the head Demand No. V-2, 2425, Cooperation 003 (2) State Cooperative Societies (02) (02) State Cooperative Societies (Programme) (24250218) 31 Subsidiary Grants in accordance with the 50% grant amount from the State Government.
As per the Government decision of Reference No. 4 of the Department of Finance, it has been approved to disburse funds within the limit of 60% of the expenditure on the programmes. However, a sum of Rs. 18.00 lakh has been provided by the Central Government to the said Training Institute for the year 2021-2022 as per their letter dated 01.07.2021.
Accordingly, as per the Central Government, the State Government's equal share for the year 2021-22 under the scheme of State Cooperative Union (24250218) has been increased from Rs. 50.00 lakh out of which Rs. 18.00 lakh to Dhananjayrao Gadgil Cooperative Management Institute, Nagpur.
