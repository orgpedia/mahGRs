Supplementary Demands for Compulsory Expenditure:- The proposals (on the original sheet) shall be submitted to the Finance Department for approval, as the case may be, before the allocation of the amounts of Supplementary Demands for Compulsory Expenditure sanctioned under the Supplementary Appropriation Act.
Supplementary Demands for Programme Expenditure:- Before allocating the amount of sanctioned supplementary demands for programme expenditure (on original lease), the proposals should be submitted to the Finance Department with the approval of the Planning Department/ Social Justice and Special Assistance Department/ Tribal Development Department, as the case may be.
