The Proposal: -
Reference No. 3 The government decision here prescribes guidelines for the scheme of capital subsidy instead of interest subsidy to textile units under the Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse an equal amount of subsidy to the proposals received under the scheme of capital subsidy instead of interest subsidy to the textile units under the Textiles Policy, as per the provision available in the year 2023-24, as per the statement in the Annexure with this Government decision.
