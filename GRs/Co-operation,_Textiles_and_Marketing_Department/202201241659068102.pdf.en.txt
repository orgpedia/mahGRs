Preamble: Under the Scheme for Support to Empowerment of District Central Cooperative Banks (2425 2524) under the Small and Marginal Cooperative Credit Structure (STCCS) in the State, the State Government approves 2.5 per cent interest subvention to the District Central Cooperative Banks for providing crop loans to farmers at 6 per cent interest rate. For the implementation of the Scheme" Assistance for Empowerment of District Central Co-operative Banks under State Small Finance Co-operative Credit Structure (STCCS) (2425 2524)," it has been decided to disburse funds from the available provision through Supplementary Demand in the financial year 2021-22 as follows.
