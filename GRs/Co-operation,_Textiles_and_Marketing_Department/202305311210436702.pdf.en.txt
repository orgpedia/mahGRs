Introduction to the Co-operative Housing Societies Co-operative Council. The Deputy Chief Minister and Minister (Housing) have made some important announcements to bring dynamism and transparency in the functioning of cooperative housing societies. Self-development of cooperative housing societies is a very important issue in this regard. Earlier, a comprehensive Government Resolution dated 22.06.2018 was issued with the concurrence of the Revenue Department, Urban Development Department, Housing Department and Rural Development Department. In it. As announced by the Deputy Chief Minister and the Minister (Housing), the inclusion of some more issues was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision After submission of the proposal for transfer of the person referred to in paragraph 2 (c) (iv) of the Government Decision dated 22/6/2018, it is referred to Sample No. It is provided that the application will be disposed of within 6 months from the date of notice of 10th. However, now if the institutions which decide to self-redevelopment pass a resolution in the general meeting regarding this and submit a proposal along with the prescribed format of transfer of manpower, the competent authority will consider the proposal in the form of Sample No. It will be mandatory to take a decision within a period of 1 month from the date of giving notice of 10.
In respect of the proposal received, all the concerned District Deputy Commissioners, Joint Director CIDCO and all other competent authorities shall expeditiously dispose of the proposal within the stipulated time by taking action as per the provisions of the rules.
