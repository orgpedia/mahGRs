Preamble: In pursuance of the relevant Government Decisions No. 3 and 4, the Government has issued a decision to define the nature of Atal Financial Assistance Scheme for Cooperative Societies to provide financial assistance for the growth of business of cooperative societies by promoting projects based on new cooperative societies, especially in agriculture, agricultural ancillary and non-agriculture innovative industries.
The scheme envisages financial assistance to promote agricultural, agricultural, allied and non-agricultural innovative projects of cooperative societies and is to be implemented through the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation.
