Read: - Provisions in section 73A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960.
The election process for the cooperative societies in the state is carried out by the State Cooperative Election Authority under the provisions of Section 73A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960.
At present, municipal and town panchayat elections have been announced in the state, and due to the upcoming elections to other local bodies, the respective administrative and police agencies are involved in the election process. This is likely to create difficulties in terms of manpower required for the cooperative elections, police deployment, and availability of polling stations.
According to the provisions of Section 73A of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the government has the power to postpone the elections to the cooperative societies in cases where, in the opinion of the state government, it is not in the public interest to hold elections to any institution or class of institutions as any election programme of the state assembly or legislative council or Lok Sabha or local authority falls during the elections to any class of institution. Considering this provision, the government is issuing the following orders.
the order
Taking into account the above considerations, as per the provisions of Section 73A of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, in the context of the ongoing elections to Municipalities and Nagar Panchayats in the State and the ensuing elections to other local bodies, in respect of which elections to the Co-operative Societies are to be held, Shri. the High Court / M. The elections to the Co-operative Societies (excluding Co-operative Housing Societies) in the State, excluding the Co-operative Societies as ordered by the Supreme Court, are being postponed from the date of this order till the completion of the local body elections in the State at the stage at which they are at present in place.
However, the order exempts the following:
1. To elect the first office bearers after the election of the co-operative societies.
