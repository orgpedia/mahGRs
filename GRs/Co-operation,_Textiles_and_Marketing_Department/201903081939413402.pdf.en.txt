GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In 2018, in view of the fall in the prices of onion in the State since October, 2018, the Government under the decision dated 26/12/2018, has sanctioned subsidy to the extent of Rs.200 per quintal and maximum of Rs.200 per quintal per farmer for the period from 1st November, 2018 to 15th December, 2018 to the farmers who have sold onion in Agricultural Produce Market Committees/ Private Market Committees in the State. The period of Onion Subsidy has been extended from time to time and the revised period was from 1st November, 2018 to 31st January, 2019. However, after that, there was a demand from the farmers, farmers' organizations, local representatives to extend the period of onion subsidy till February 28, 2019 due to the low market price of onion. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the period of onion subsidy till February 28, 2019.
Government Decision: Government Decision dated 26/12/2018, dated 31/01/2019 and dated 15/2/2019 extending the period of Onion Subsidy from 1st November, 2018 to 31st January, 2019
