OFFICIAL ORDERS Due to the transfer of some officers of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the Ministry and retirement of some officers, there has been a change in the charge/ designation of the officers of the Department. As per this change, the General Administration Department, Government Circular No.Ramaa/ 2019/ PR No. 18/ 6D. 12
Since the provision of Para 1.2 of February, 2019 requires declaration of updated information of Assistant Public Information Officer, State Public Information Officer and First Appellate Authority, the Office Orders dated 2nd July, 2018 at Reference No.1 of this Department are being superseded and revised orders are being circulated as under: 2. The designation of Assistant Public Information Officer, State Public Information Officer and First Appellate Authority in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles under the Central Right to Information Act, 2005 is being declared in the attached Form A for effective realization of Right to Information to the public.
If there is more than one Assistant Public Information Officer in an office, the Assistant Public Information Officer to whom the information is to be given should immediately provide the necessary information to the State Public Information Officer. If the applicant is not provided with the correct information within the time limit and as a result, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned Assistant Public Information Officer and State Public Information Officer. The Assistant Public Information Officer, State Public Information Officer and First Appellate Authority of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (itself) shall strictly comply with the orders/ circulars, etc. issued by the Central Government from time to time in relation to the Right to Information Act.
