The Proposal:
The scheme of financing the Cooperative Consumer Societies in rural and urban areas of the State is being implemented since 1994-1995 as per the Government Decisions dated 30/05/1994 and 14/03/1995, considering the important aspects such as providing fair price to the consumers of the urban/ rural areas of the State, as well as to the producers of the consumer products produced by them and to provide employment to the unemployed by the Consumer Societies. The details of the scheme, the pattern of funding, the criteria for sanctioning the funding, the terms and conditions, etc. are set out in the Government Resolution of the scheme.
Government's decision:
The Scheme of Financial Assistance to Cooperative Consumer Societies in Urban/ Rural areas has been transferred to the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, State of Maharashtra, Pune vide Government Resolution, dated 26/12/2007. The Director of Marketing, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted to the Central Co-operative Consumer Dispensary Limited for approval of financial assistance under the scheme. The proposal of Walwa G. Sangli is being approved. Orders for this should be issued at the level of the Directorate General of Labour.
During the financial year 2018-2019, the expenditure under this scheme shall be met from the available provision under the head of accounts mentioned below:
