Preamble: In order to provide financial assistance to the onion farmers of the State, a subsidy of Rs.200/-per quintal and a maximum of 200 quintals per farmer to the farmers who sold onion in the Agricultural Produce Market Committee from 1st November 2018 to 15th December 2018 has been approved under the Government decision dated 26/12/2018. The government was considering providing funds for distribution of subsidy under the scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is providing an amount of Rs. 114.80 crore (Rupees One Hundred Fourteen Crore Eighty Lakh only) from the Contingency Fund for sanction of subsidy to the extent of Rs.200/-per quintal and maximum of Rs.200/-per quintal to the farmers who have sold onion in the Agricultural Produce Market Committees/ Private Market Committees in the State during the period from 1st November, 2018 to 15th December, 2018.
Expenditure under the Scheme shall be met from the Contingency Fund provided under the following head: Demand No.V-2 2425 Support 107 Assistance to Cooperative Credit Societies (01) Support to Cooperative Credit Societies (01) (08) Financial assistance to Onion growing Farmers (Mandatory) (24251421) 33 Financial assistance Contingency Fund Index (8000 7547)
The Director of Marketing, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized by the Government to disburse grants to eligible beneficiaries district-wise after scrutinizing all the proposals received under the scheme. Withdraw the amount of subsidy to the directors from the government treasury.
