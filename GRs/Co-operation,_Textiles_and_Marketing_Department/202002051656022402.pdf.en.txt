The Proposal:
Kalaskarwadi Miscellaneous Executive Services Co-operative Societies Limited. and others against the government.
The High Court of Judicature at Mumbai, vide Writ Petition No. 3712/2017 was filed. The Maharashtra government is the petitioner in the case. In view of the importance of the case, Mr. S.U. Kamdar was appointed by a letter under reference as a special counsel to represent the government properly.
In this case, the matter of payment of agreed professional fees to Mr. S.U. Kamdar, Special Counsel, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Petition No. 3712/2017 on behalf of the respondent. High Court, Mumbai. Shri S.Kotwal to appear before the Government during the hearing dated 09/01/2020 and to represent the Government.
U. Kamdar, Special Advocate, was awarded Rs. 1,35,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Thirty Five Thousand only) towards Professional Fee (Advocate Fee).
The court fee of Mr. S.U. Kamdar, Public Prosecutor should be paid from the funds available for the year 2019-20 from the head of accounts under the head" V-02A 3451 Secretariat Financial Services (00) (090) Secretariat (01) (01), Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (34510304) (Dattamat)-28 Professional Services" of this Department.
As per this Government decision, for the hearing dated 09/01/2020, Rs. 1,35,000/- from this fund subject to 10% total income tax deduction as per Income Tax Rules, 1961 (Rs. 13500/-) and 4% of the total deduction of education surcharge (Rs. 540/-) and Rs. 1,20,960/- should be drawn in favour of" Mr. S. U. Kamdar."
