The High-Level Committee has approved the review of 373 posts under the Director (Reshim). It was established on March 22, 2011. Reference No. The government has approved six new posts along with the creation of a new district silk office in Jawhar, Palghar. So now there is a figure of 379 posts under the Directorate of Silk.
