Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court, Mumbai Bench has directed to continue the temporary posts of two members at Nagpur and Pune till 28/2/2021. 2 Approved by the Government Decision dated 7/10/2020. For administrative reasons, these temporary positions must continue and be extended for 2021-2022. Therefore, subject to the condition that the said posts are included in the format fixed under Government Resolution dated 5/5/2011, the Government approves the continuation of the temporary posts of two members at Nagpur and Pune from 1/3/2021 to 31/8/2021.
