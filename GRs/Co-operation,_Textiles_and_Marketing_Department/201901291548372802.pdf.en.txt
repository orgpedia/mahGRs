Under the Advertisement and Publicity Scheme, the State Government provides financial assistance for the exhibition of handloom with the objective that the handloom weavers of the State should get the benefit of this scheme and the variety produced in the State should get the necessary publicity and thereby contribute to the livelihood of the families of poor handloom weavers.
Under the Centrally Sponsored National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) scheme, district level Handloom Textile Exhibitions along with National and State level exhibitions have been organized during 2015-16. However, since the year 2016-17, the Central Government has not granted permission for organizing district level Handloom Textile Exhibition. Therefore, in view of the difficult situation of handloom weavers, to get work in their hands and to make the variety produced by them famous and to sell their goods and contribute to their livelihood, Director Textiles, Nagpur, vide No. 2018-19 to 2022-23 as per the proposal received vide letter dated 30th May, 2018.
