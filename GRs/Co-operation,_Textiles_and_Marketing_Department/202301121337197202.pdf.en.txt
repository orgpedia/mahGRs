In order to announce the new textile policy of the state 2023-2028 under the government decision, an all inclusive committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Commissioner (Textile Industry), to consider the recommendations for the new textile policy in consultation with the experts related to the textile industry and other experienced persons in the field, considering their useful suggestions from the experts and textile industry informers and to submit recommendations to the government accordingly. The committee will have representatives from the Bombay Textile Research Association (BTRA), Vidarbha Industries Association and the Directorate of Industries, Department of Industries, Government of Maharashtra.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the information in the Preamble, the Committee constituted to suggest recommendations for the Textile Policy 2023-2028 under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Textiles), includes the following members.
Representative of The Bombay Textile Research Association (BTRA), Member 2) Representative of Vidarbha Industries Association, Nagpur, Member 3) Joint Director (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Government of Maharashtra, Member
