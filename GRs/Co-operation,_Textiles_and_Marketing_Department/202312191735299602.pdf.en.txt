Introduction: In the third session of 2023, under Rule 105 of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly, attention-grabbing suggestions were raised regarding the difficulties in the machinery industry in the state.
During the discussion of this attention-grabbing suggestion, a committee will be formed to study the problems of the machinery owners in the state and suggest a solution plan. The Minister (Textiles) has announced/ assured in the House. In pursuance of the said announcement/ assurance, the Government was contemplating to constitute a committee of people's representatives to study the problems of the millers in the State and suggest a solution.
Government Decision: A study of the problems of the machinery owners in the state and a solution plan
