The State Cooperative Election Authority was established by a Government Notification under Section 73AAB of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 to conduct elections to all cooperative societies in the State. After this, 44 posts have been created on the establishment of the State Cooperative Election Authority under the government decision at Read No. 2, which includes the post of State Cooperative Election Commissioner.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of section 73Q of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 and Rule 18 of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Election Authority (Conditions of Service of Commissioners) Rules, 2014, the State Cooperative Election Authority, with the approval of the General Administration Department, hereby notifies under Rule 10 that the post of Commissioner State Cooperative Election Authority is equivalent to the post of Additional Chief Secretary in the Indian Administrative Service. Further, in view of the implementation of the 7th Pay Commission with effect from 1st January, 2016 for the officers and employees of the Central and State Governments, in Rule 5 of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Election Authority (Conditions of Service of Commissioners) Rules, 2014, under the notification at Read No. 11, the text" Rs. 80,000/- per month" has been substituted with the text" Salary of Additional Chief Secretary to Government, per month with effect from 1st January, 2016." Therefore, as per the 7th Pay Commission, the salary of the post of Commissioner, State Cooperative Election Authority has been fixed at Rs. 2,25,000/- (equivalent to the salary of Additional Chief Secretary). Accordingly, in pursuance of the proposal sent to the Government vide letter No. 12, the matter of fixing the salary and allowances of the Commissioner State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
