Preamble: The Government of India has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Strengthening of Cooperatives through IT Interventions for a period of three years from 2023-24 to 2025-26. has been approved on 06.10.2023. A total of 3 projects are included in the program:
(a) Computerization of Agriculture and Rural Development Banks (b) Computerization of office of RCS of States/ UTs (c) Central Project Management Unit (PMU) Under the said Centrally Sponsored Project, it is proposed to computerize the offices of Registrar Cooperative Societies (RCS) of the State. As per the revised methodology for Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes, the concerned administrative department shall appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for that scheme. In addition, at least Deputy Secretary level in terms of coordination of the scheme
The Finance Department has appointed an officer as the nodal officer. The instructions have been issued vide Government Circular dated 01.07.2021. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the implementation and coordination of the said Centrally Sponsored Scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Government Circular, Finance Department, Dy. As per the instructions given under 01.07.2021, the Government is approving the appointment of Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the implementation and coordination of the Centrally Sponsored Project" Strengthening of Cooperatives through IT Interventions" under which the offices of Registrar Cooperative Societies will be computerized.
