Preamble: As per Article 243ZK (2) of the 97th Constitutional Amendment Act of the Central Government, it was provided for the creation of an independent election authority to conduct and monitor/ control elections to all cooperative societies in the State. Accordingly, vide notification dated 30.03.2013, in line with the State Election Commission under Section 73C of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, a separate State Cooperative Election Authority has been constituted to conduct elections to the Cooperative Societies in the State and 47 posts have been created for the same. It was approved by the Cabinet at its meeting held on 30th January, 2013.
02. Ma. The decision was taken at a high-level meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary. As per the decision of the Government, out of 47 posts approved by the Cabinet, the creation of remaining 44 posts excluding 3 posts has been approved. Also, as per the Government decision at Read 4, 44 temporary posts have been approved to be continued till 31 st August, 2022. Deadline is August 31, 2022
