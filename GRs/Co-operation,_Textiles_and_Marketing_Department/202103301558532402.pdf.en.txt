Preamble: NAFED has been appointed as the lead agent for procurement of various pulses, cereals and oilseeds at MSP by the Government of India during 2013-14. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai has been appointed as the Deputy Agent for the implementation of the scheme in the state. The scheme is advertised at the level of State Governments as per the guidelines of the Central Government issued on 31.7.2014. Advertising fees are paid by the association. The Government was considering to disburse the funds received by the Federation through Revised Estimates for payment of payments for the advertisement given in the newspapers regarding procurement at guaranteed price under the Minimum Support Price Scheme for the season 2017-18 to 2019-20.
Government's decision:
The Government have approved payment of Rs. 50.00 lakh (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) to the Federation for payment of payments for advertisements in newspapers regarding procurement under Minimum Support Price Scheme (MSP) from 2017-18 to 2019-20.
