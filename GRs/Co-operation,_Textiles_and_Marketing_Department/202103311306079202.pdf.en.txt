Preamble: The Director (Sericulture) vide letter dated 01.02.2021 has proposed a sum of Rs. 6,37,696/- (including GST) for making available the services of 02 Expert Database Admin (Experience 4 to 9 years) from Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHAIT) for hiring technical manpower for a period of 3 months as per the Government Decision dated 24.03.2017 in respect of Directorate of Sericulture, Nagpur and for updating and developing Silk Online System (MIS). Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Government Circular dated 16th April, 2020 and the Government Decision dated 04th May, 2020, under reference to the Department of Finance, the Finance Department was requested to approve the release and expenditure of the said funds from the provision received under e-Governance by the Department for the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has approved the release of 40% of the budgetary allocation under e-Governance. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to approve the expenditure to be incurred by the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHAIT) to provide the services of 02 expert database admin to the Directorate of Silk.
Government's decision:
6,37,696/-for providing the services of 02 Expert Database Admin (Experience 4 to 9 years) for updating and developing Silk Online Control System (MIS) and website of Directorate of Silk, Nagpur for a period of 3 months as requested by Director (Silk).
