The Proposal:
The guidelines of the scheme prescribe a capital subsidy scheme in lieu of interest subsidy to the textile units under the Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra, as per the Government decision at Table No. 3 above. Accordingly, instead of interest subsidy to the textile units under the Textile Policy, the Government was considering to disburse subsidy to the proposals received under the Capital Subsidy Scheme as per the provision available for the year 2023-24 as per the statement in the' Annexure' accompanying this Government decision.
Government's decision:
Under the Textile Policy of the State, instead of interest subsidy to the textile units, the capital subsidy scheme is being implemented. The projects approved by the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Textiles), out of the provision approved for expenditure by the Department of Finance for the year 2023-24, as per the statement in Annexure A accompanying this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. An amount of Rs. 17,54,37, 000/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores Fifty Four Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand) is sanctioned and sanctioned for disbursement.
However, this amount is collected by the Vigilance and Control Team as per the decision of the Government in reference No. 6.
In cases where projects have been inspected and irregularities have been found, the grant will be disbursed only after a final decision is taken or further action will be taken as per the decision taken. Also, the projects which have not yet been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team due to lack of time, will be inspected soon in the near future and if any irregularities are found in the inspection of the project, the subsidy sanctioned through this order will be recovered with interest.
