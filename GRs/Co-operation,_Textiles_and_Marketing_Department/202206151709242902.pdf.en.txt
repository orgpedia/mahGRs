Preamble: Maharashtra State Cooperative Sugar Factories Union, Ltd., has requested that there is a need to make a state law to control jaggery production as per the Maharashtra Khandsari Production Licensing Order 1979. Mumbai and West Indian Sugar Mills Association, Pune and accordingly a proposal has been received from the Commissioner of Sugar, Pune.
Under the Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966, the state government has issued the Maharashtra Khandsari Production License Order, 1979. At present, there are 11 licensed Khandsari projects in the state. In view of the various issues that have arisen in the case of the Khandsari projects, there is a need to address these issues in a comprehensive manner.
Government Decision: In view of the proposal received from the Sugar Commissioner, the Government has decided to constitute the following Study Group under the Chairmanship of the Sugar Commissioner, Pune with a view to examine the policy on grant of recognition and production license to Khandsari industries and the possibility of implementing the Sugarcane Control Order 1966 for jaggery production plants.

To suggest amendments in the Maharashtra Khandsari Production Licensing Order, 1979 in consonance with the provisions of the Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966.
