The Director (Reshim) shall take action to obtain the approval of the concerned department of the government, approval of the budget, ownership of the site, construction map, layout map and detailed map of the architect for the purpose for which the funds are to be spent.
4. The Director (Reshim) shall make the appropriation of the fund only after preparing a detailed budget on the basis of which the outline, nature, duration and subject-wise expenditure criteria of the programme have been determined with regard to the provisions made in the form of a budget.
5. While spending the said fund, the Director (Reshim) shall exercise complete control over the expenditure on appropriate matters in accordance with the financial rules of the government and the media plan approved by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations and strictly following the prescribed criteria.
6. The Director (Reshim) shall ensure that the said funds are spent within the limit of monthly cash flow.
7. The funds should be withdrawn as per requirement by following the prescribed procedure while making the actual expenditure. Also, the terms and conditions laid down by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed.
It should be ensured that the utilization certificate of this fund is submitted to the Accountant General and the Government within the prescribed time.
8. The said fund should be met from the subsidy available during the financial year 2019-20 under the head" Demand No. V-2, (01) (01) Promotion and Publicity of Silk Industry under State Textile Policy 2018-23 (Programme) (2851 6574), 26 Advertisement and Publicity."
The Director (Reshim) is being authorised as the Controlling Officer to draw and disburse the said funds from the Treasury. The said fund is being handed over to the subordinate of Director (Silk) on BDS system.
