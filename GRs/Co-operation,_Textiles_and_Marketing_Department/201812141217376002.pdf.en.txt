The Supreme Court As per the order dated 27/03/2018, the interim recommendations of the 2nd National Judicial Pay Commission to the President/ Members of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Appellate Tribunal and the Judges of the Cooperative Courts, Pensioner Judicial Officers and Family Pensioners have been issued. With effect from 1/01/2016, the Government approves the following:
(1) The Chairman/ Members of the Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court and the Co-operative Court
to the judges. Implementation of Interim Pay Hike with regular increment by 30% increase in Basic Pay with effect from 1/01/2016. to be done from 1/05/2018.
2) The increase in salary should be considered separately. No dearness allowance will be allowed.
3) The amount due should be provided as mentioned below.
(a) The. Chairman/ Members of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court and Judges of Co-operative Court as on 1/05/2018, whose provident fund account is in existence, should deposit 60% in cash and 40% in their provident fund.
(b) The. The amount of arrears should be paid in cash to the judicial officers whose account of Provident Fund of the Chairman/ Member of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Appellate Court and the Judge of the Cooperative Court does not exist as on 1/05/2018.
(4) The retired Chairman/ Members of the Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court and the Judges of the Co-operative Court and Family Pensioners are also entitled to the same benefits as the serving judicial officers. They should be given an interim pay hike on the basic pay with effect from 1/01/2016 and the arrears should also be paid. Payment will be made by 30/06/2018. (The interim relief shall be given to the pensioners and family pensioners, on the same basis as given to the serving Judicial Officers, with effect from 1/01/2016 and arrears shall be paid to them in cash by 30/06/2018)
(5) The Commission shall, after making an interim determination, submit a final report on the determination of wages.
After that, the amount will be adjusted. The expenditure for this should be met from the grants sanctioned for those financial years.
