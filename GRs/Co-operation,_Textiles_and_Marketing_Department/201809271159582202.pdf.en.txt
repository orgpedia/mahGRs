Reorganisation of the Board of Directors of the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation nominated by the State Government.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Co-operation, Textiles and Commerce.
Government Resolution No. Kempuyo/ Wakhar2018/ P. No. 146/1-C.
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 0032.
Date:- 26.9.2018
The Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation was established under the Maharashtra Warehousing Corporation Act, 1962. There are a total of 11 Directors as per the provisions of Rule 20 (1) of the Act. The composition of the Board of Directors is the same for all State Warehousing Corporations.
Accordingly, 5 Directors are nominated by the Central Warehousing Corporation and 5 Directors are nominated by the State Government. Also, 1 Managing Director is appointed by the State Government. The matter of reconstitution of the Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation to be nominated by the State Government was under consideration of the Government.
As per the provisions of Rule 20 (1) of the Warehousing Corporation Act, 1962, the State Government is to appoint 5 Directors of the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation by nomination. Accordingly, the Government have approved the appointment of the Board of Directors of the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation as follows.
