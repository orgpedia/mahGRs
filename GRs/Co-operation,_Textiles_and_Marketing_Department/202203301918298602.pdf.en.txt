The Proposal:
The Director (Sericulture) has proposed to make available the unutilized Central share of Rs. 31.107 lakh and supplementary State share of Rs. 12.455 lakh out of the funds received for SC/ ST/ General category beneficiaries under CSS Scheme in 2014-15 and 2015-16, out of the total budgetary provision of Rs. 43.562 lakh for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme" Silk Samagra" for the financial year 2021-22. Submitted vide letter dated 27.07.2021. The Central Silk Board, Bangalore has decided to utilize the Central share lying unutilized with the State for the financial year 2021-22. Approval has been accorded vide letter dated 14.07.2021. Accordingly, as requested by the Director (Reshim), the matter of disbursement of the said fund from the budgetary provision of 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government.
