The Proposal:
As per Article 243ZK (2) of the 97th Constitutional Amendment Act, an independent election authority has been created to conduct and supervise elections to all cooperative societies in the state. Accordingly, vide notification dated 30/03/2013, in line with the State Election Commission under Section 73C of the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, the Government has decided to constitute a separate State Cooperative Election Authority for conducting elections to the Cooperative Societies in the State and create 47 posts for the same. The Cabinet has approved the same in its meeting dated 30/1/2013.
The decision was taken at a high-level meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary. As per the decision of the government, the creation of remaining 44 posts excluding 3 posts out of 47 posts approved by the Cabinet has been approved. Accordingly, the functioning of the State Co-operative Election Authority has commenced from 26/05/2014. Then read the government's decision. 44 temporary positions. It was approved to continue till 29.02.2019.
The deadline The request for approval of the Secretary, State Cooperative Election Authority, Pune for continuation of the said 44 posts as it is expiring on 01.03.2019 Read No. 5 has been done as per the letter here and the matter of approving it was under consideration of the government.
