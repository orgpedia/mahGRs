The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Cashew Board was set up to develop the cashew fruit crop in the state. The government decision has been made on May 16, 2023. The headquarters of the cashew board will be in Navi Mumbai, while the divisional offices will be in Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg.
Considering the production of cashew fruit crop in Chandgad and Ajra taluks of Kolhapur district, the government was considering to set up the divisional office of Maharashtra State Cashew Board in Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg as well as in Chandgad in Kolhapur district.
Government's decision:
Under the context of the development of cashew fruit crop in Maharashtra. The Cabinet has approved the establishment of Divisional Office of Maharashtra State Cashew Board at Chandgad in Kolhapur district along with Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg, which has been established under the Government Resolution dated 16.05.2023.
