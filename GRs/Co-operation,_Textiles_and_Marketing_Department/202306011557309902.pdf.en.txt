The Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960. In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of section 73CB (as amended by Maharashtra Ordinance No.2 of 2013), the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune was constituted vide notification dated 30th March, 2013. The office is responsible for conducting elections to cooperative societies in the state.
The authority has been entrusted with the responsibility of preparing the list of eligible members during the election, announcing the election schedule, conducting physical voting, checking whether the code of conduct is followed during the election, conducting the election in a smooth, peaceful and fearless environment. The State Cooperative Election Authority has brought to the notice of the government that the Xerox machine available in the office was purchased in 2015 and is currently in need of frequent repair. For the smooth conduct of elections to cooperative societies, the government was considering granting administrative approval for the purchase of new xerox machines for the office of the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative approval is being accorded for the purchase of a new Xerox machine for the office of State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune at an estimated cost of Rs. 4,63,800/- (Rupees Four Lakh Sixty Three Thousand Eight Hundred only) subject to the following conditions/ conditions.
Guidelines have been issued for adoption of the modalities of the Government e-Marketplace portal developed by the Central Government under the Government Decisions dated 24.08.2017 and 08.12.2017 in accordance with the provisions contained in the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour. Accordingly, the procurement process should be carried out from the government e-marketplace.
Purchase of Xerox machines should be done from the L-1 supplier on the Government e-Marketplace web portal, taking into account the quality of the goods, specifications, supply of quality goods and reasonable price.
While purchasing a new Xerox machine, the difference between the price in the open market and the price on the Government e-Marketplace web portal should be studied by comparing the quality of the goods, specifications, supply of quality goods, fair price and transparency in the entire procurement process.
4. Procurement proceedings to be carried out by verifying whether the estimated amount proposed for the purchase of the xerox machine in question is correct.
After the purchase of a new xerox machine, the State Cooperative Election Authority, Maharashtra State, Pune, should specify the xerox machine, which has to be repaired frequently, purchased in the year 2015 in the office, within 3 months according to the prescribed government procedure.
