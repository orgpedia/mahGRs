Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 7/10/2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure posts in Government service for Divyangs for the posts in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D on the establishment of Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies, Pune under the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, and to ensure these posts for the new Divyang category by reviewing the list of seats in Government service ensured for Divyangs within a stipulated period not exceeding 3 years.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification to ensure reservation of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
