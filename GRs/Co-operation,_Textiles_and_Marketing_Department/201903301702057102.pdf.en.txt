Introduction: The Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank has provided various types of loans to cooperative sugar factories, cooperative spinning and agricultural processing cooperatives since 1993, which were guaranteed by the state government from time to time. The Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank had appealed for guarantee from time to time as these institutions could not repay the bank. About this Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank Ltd. Mumbai Civil Appeal No. 3445/2011 is filed in the Supreme Court, New Delhi.
