The Proposal: -
As per the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context, the State Government has decided to provide short-term crop loans to farmers in the State at 6 per cent interest. As per the decision, as per the policy of the central government, banks are to provide loans to farmers at 7 per cent interest rate, instead of 7 per cent, the state government's policy is to provide loans to farmers at 6 per cent interest rate. For this purpose, the financial burden of the 1 per cent interest differential amount is on the government. The decision is being extended to nationalised banks and regional rural banks in the state during the Kharif and Rabi seasons from January 1 to April 1 and private banks disbursing short-term crop loans up to Rs.
The government was considering sanctioning funds under the 1% subsidy scheme to provide short-term crop loans to farmers for the fourth financial year.
