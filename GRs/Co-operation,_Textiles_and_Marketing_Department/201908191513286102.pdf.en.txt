Introduction: 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 28th August, 2018, 115 taluks in the State have been declared as Kapus Producing Taluks. After that. The government's notification dated August 30, 2018. As per the Government decision dated 26.07.2019, 4 more taluks have been added to the list of Kapus Producing Taluks. With this, the total number of Kapus producing taluks in the state has gone up to 119.
Now, since the cotton production of Karjat and Jamkhed taluks of Ahmednagar district in the last 3 years is more than 9600 tonnes, the matter of inclusion of Karjat and Jamkhed taluks in the list of Kapus producing taluks was under consideration of the Government, accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions.
Karjat and Jamkhed taluks of Ahmednagar district have been included in the list of Kapus producing taluks as the average cotton production in the last 3 years is more than 9600 tonnes. Karjat and Jamkhed are included in the list of Kapus producing taluks of Ahmednagar district in the Table of Kapus Producing Taluks, dated 28th August, 2018. With this, the total number of Kapus producing taluks in the State has gone up to 121.
