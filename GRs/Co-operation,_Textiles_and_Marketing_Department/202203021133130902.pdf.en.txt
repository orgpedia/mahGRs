Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 23/8/2021, read with (2) above, 86 permanent posts for continuous and concurrent audit of cooperative societies were extended till 28th February, 2022. The proposal for extension of the said 86 temporary posts from 1st March, 2022 to 31st August, 2022 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
The details of temporary posts to be extended for the period from 1st March, 2022 to 31st August, 2022 are as follows:
