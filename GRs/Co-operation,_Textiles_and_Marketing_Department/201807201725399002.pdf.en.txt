Preamble: During the Eighth, Tenth and Twelfth Five Year Plans, the selected cooperative spinning mills to be set up and financed by the Government are provided with Government shareholding in the ratio of 1:9 to the self-share accumulated by the spinning mills as per the earlier figure of 5:45:50. Also the. As per the New Textile Policy 2018-2023 under the Government decision dated 15.2.2018, the process of sanctioning Government shareholding to the Yarn Mill as per the figure 10:30:60 and sanctioning of Government Loan/ NFS Loan/ Rehabilitation Loan to the Yarn Mill is carried out. As per the instructions of the Finance Department, the condition has been included that the cooperative weavers belonging to the general category and backward class category should be allowed to withdraw the amount after mortgaging the personal property of the Board of Directors to the extent of 20% of the amount while sanctioning government shareholding/ government loan/ non-government loan/ rehabilitation loan. However, the people's representatives have brought to the notice of the government that it is difficult to give 20% mortgage from personal property in this way and the limit of 20% should be relaxed.

Accordingly, the government was considering to take a mortgage of 10% instead of 20% from the personal property of the board of directors of general category yarn mills (Government shareholding/ Government loan/ NSS loan/ rehabilitation loan amount to be disbursed) and 5% instead of 20% in the case of yarn mills of backward class.
Government Decision: The co-operative spinning mills to be set up during the Eighth, Tenth and Twelfth Five Year Plan period and selected for Government financial assistance will be funded as per the 5:45:50 ratio. In accordance with the New Textile Policy 2018-2023 as per the Government decision dated 15.2.2018, the Government is permitted to approve the withdrawal of the Government shareholding to be granted to the yarn mill as per the self-share deposited by the yarn mill as per the figure 10:30:60 and the amount of 10% for general category yarn mill and 5% for backward category yarn mill after taking the personal property of the yarn mill directors instead of taking the mortgage from the personal property of 20% of the amount of Government loan/ NSS loan/ rehabilitation loan subject to the following conditions.
(a) 10% should be taken as collateral for cooperative spinning in general category. A maximum of 2% of personal assets per director must be pledged when mortgaging the board of directors. While taking the mortgage of the board of directors, it will be necessary to take a registered mortgage like a bank. It will also be necessary to register with the Taran Mortgage Registration Office.
(b) 5% should be taken as collateral for co-workers of backward classes.
A maximum of 1% of personal assets per director must be pledged when pledging the board of directors. While taking the mortgage of the board of directors, it will be necessary to take a registered mortgage like a bank. It will also be necessary to register with the Taran Mortgage Registration Office.

The amount of loan/ rehabilitation loan shall not be charged to the mill or overdraft to the mill till the amount of 10% of the loan amount for general category yarn and 5% for backward category yarn is received as per the above personal guarantee. The Director Textile Industries shall pass on this obligation to the concerned bank from time to time. The Director of Textiles should take strict action in this regard.
