Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 07.03.2022 in the aforesaid Reference No. 2, the Government had approved the continuation of 59 temporary posts of Audit Staff in Urban Cooperative Banks till 31.08.2022. A total of 59 posts were under the consideration of the government for the year 2022-23 in accordance with the proposal submitted by the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Under the process of fixing the revised figure of the sanctioned permanent/ temporary posts under the department, the revised figure of the posts of continuous and concurrent auditor of the civil cooperative banks has been approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries and the Government has issued a decision in this regard.
Rajah 1008/ Q. No. 538/5-S, Dy. Issued on 05.05.2011. This includes 59 posts. In view of the changes in the law as per the 97th Constitutional Amendment, the proposal for the re-employment of a total of 3995 posts in the Audit Department as per the approval in the figure is under scrutiny and noting that this scrutiny will take time, the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government for the continuation of 59 posts for the audit of the Urban Cooperative Bank in 2022-23. Department of Finance As per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments vide Government Decision dated 18.08.2022, 59 temporary posts in the Statement' A' for the audit of the Urban Cooperative Bank have been sanctioned. from 01.09.2022. Continuation up to 28.02.2023 is hereby approved by the Government.
