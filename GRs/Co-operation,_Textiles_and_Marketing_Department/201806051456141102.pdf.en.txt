Preamble: Textile Policy 2018-23. The decision was announced on February 15, 2018. In this policy, provisions have been made with a view to rejuvenate the cooperative spinning mills, in which it has been provided that the cooperative spinning mills can be taken on deputation by a government officer in the post of managing director in view of human resource development. In accordance with the matter mentioned in the Textile Policy, the matter regarding the appointment of a government officer on deputation to the cooperative spinning mill was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As per the Textile Policy 2018-23, a Group-A Government Officer in the service of the State Government can be appointed as Managing Director on yarn by the Co-operative Yarn Mills in the State on deputation.
The terms and conditions and procedure for the appointment of a government officer on deputation shall be as follows:
Terms and Conditions for Appointment by Deputation: 1) A Group-A rank officer in the service of the State Government shall be appointed on the yarn selected for Government financial assistance.
