Preamble: Proposal for procurement of computer content under e-Governance Scheme for Directorate of Sericulture and Subordinate Regional Offices Reference No. 4 submitted to the Government. The proposal has been received from the Directorate of Information Technology and approved by the Project Implementation Committee of the Department in its meeting held on 24.07.2023.
Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the proposal to purchase computer materials under the e-governance scheme for the Directorate of Sericulture and the subordinate zonal office was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the procurement of computer content under the e-Governance scheme, it is estimated that the e-office system will be implemented efficiently as per the specifications (as per the attached form) approved by the Department of Information Technology for the Directorate of Silk and the area office under it.
Rs. 1,24,65, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Four Lakh Sixty Five Thousand only)
The proposal is being accorded administrative approval under this Government decision subject to the following terms and conditions.
