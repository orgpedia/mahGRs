The Proposal:
Under the Promotion and Publicity Programme of the Silk Industry, a sum of Rs. 192.83 lakh was sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20 as per the Government Resolution dated 22.08.2019. However, the payments for the financial year 2019-20 were kept pending as the Directorate of Sericulture did not receive payments from the concerned in time due to the outbreak of COVID-19 in the State. The Finance Department has approved the release of 10% of the budgetary allocation i. e.₹ 15 lakh for the financial year 2021-22 for the promotion and publicity programme of the silk industry. Accordingly, under the Promotion and Publicity Programme of the Silk Industry, for payment of pending dues for the financial year 2019-20, a sum of Rs. 8,56,920/-under Government Decision dated 04.08.2021, and Rs. 1,70,000/-under Government Decision dated 07.12.2021, as the case may be, has been released to the Government of Maharashtra and Nagpur.

A total amount of Rs. 12,36,840/- has been made available to Director (Sericulture) on Budget Distribution System as per the Government decision dated 23rd December, 2021 for setting up of stall of Directorate of Sericulture at Agrovision Agri Expo-2021 held here. Now, the Director (Sericulture) vide letter dated 23rd March, 2022 has requested for release of funds to the tune of₹ 19,470/- towards payment of pending dues for the financial year 2019-20 under the Promotion and Publicity Programme. Hence, out of the 10% (₹ 15 lakh) funds sanctioned earlier by the Finance Department, so far₹ 12,36,840/- has been disbursed and₹ 2,63,160/- is available for disbursement with the Department. Therefore, under the promotion and publicity program of the silk industry, the government was considering to release funds to meet the expenses of pending payments for the financial year 2019-2020.
Government's decision:
It is hereby approved to disburse and spend a sum of Rs.19,470/- (Rupees Nineteen Seventy only) towards payment of pending dues for the financial year 2019-20 under the Promotion and Publicity Programme of the Silk Industry.
