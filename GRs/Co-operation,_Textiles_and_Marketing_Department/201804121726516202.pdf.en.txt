8. Finance Department Government Circular, No.Finance-2018/ PR No. 69/ Finance-3D. 02/04/2018 Introduction:
In pursuance of the Government decision dated 1st March, 2012, under the New Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra, a scheme for providing 10% capital subsidy to new and expanding textile units in Vidarbha, Marathwada and North Maharashtra has been introduced. Under the scheme, the government was considering to provide 10 per cent capital subsidy to the textile component of the statement in the' Annexure-A" accompanying this decision.
Government's decision:
Under the State Textile Policy 2011-2017, under the scheme of 10% capital subsidy to the new and expanding textile units in Vidarbha, Marathwada and North Maharashtra, the textile units in the statement in" Annexure-A" accompanying this Government decision will get a total of Rs. 3,55,75, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand only) at 10% Capital Concession.
