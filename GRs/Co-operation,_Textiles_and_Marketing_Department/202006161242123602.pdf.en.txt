A subsidy of Rs 200 per quintal has been sanctioned to eligible farmers and a maximum of 200 quintals per farmer for the period from 2018 to December 15, 2018.
The period of Onion Subsidy under the Scheme has been extended from time to time and the revised due date has been fixed from 1st November, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.
An amount of Rs. 114.80 crore was made available from the Contingency Fund for disbursement of first phase of Onion Subsidy vide Government Resolution, dated 16/2/2019. Thereafter, Rs. 387.3031 crore has been provided as grant-in-aid for the second phase through Supplementary Demands for Grants vide Government Resolution, dated 7/8/2019. In view of the delay in disbursement of first phase onion subsidy, the Government vide its decision dated 19/8/2019, has approved the release of second phase onion subsidy through ICICI Bank. So far, a total of Rs. 503.55 crore has been disbursed to 5,53,204 farmers.
As per the Government Decision, dated 19/8/2019, while approving the distribution of onion subsidy through ICICI Bank, in the terms and conditions specified by the Finance Department, the condition No. It is as follows:
The number of " The interest received from this savings account should be used only for the implementation of this scheme. "
The interest amount of Rs. 30.09 lakh has been credited to the account of Directorate General of Revenue at the end of 4/5/2020 from the funds deposited in ICICI Bank.
The Directorate of Marketing had submitted the proposal for Onion subsidy for Washim, Chandrapur and Solapur districts vide letter dated 13.12.2019 and for the pending beneficiaries of Jalgaon and Yavatmal districts vide letter dated 6/1/2020. The proposal was expected to be tabled during the supplementary demands to be submitted before the winter session. However, the proposal was delayed due to non-availability of funds through supplementary demand.
Considering the situation created by Corona in the state, it will take a lot of time to make funds available by submitting a new supplementary demand for onion subsidy. The Finance Department has given permission to use the interest for the implementation of the scheme. In fact

The subsidy will be paid through ICICI Bank.
