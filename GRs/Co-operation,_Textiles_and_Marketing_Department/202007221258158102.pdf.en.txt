Government's decision
Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. The 26 temporary posts for continuous and concurrent audit at Mumbai were extended till 29th February, 2020 vide Government Resolution dated 17th October, 2019. The proposal for extension of the said 26 temporary posts from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The temporary positions shall be filled in the prescribed manner.
3) Ensure that the audit fee is fully recovered.
