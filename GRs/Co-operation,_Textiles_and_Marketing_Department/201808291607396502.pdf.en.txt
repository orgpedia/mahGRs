Preamble- To regulate the rate of sugarcane supplied to sugar mills in the State of Maharashtra. As per the notification dated 24.12.2013, the Maharashtra Regulation of Sugarcane (Supply to Factories) Rates Act, 2013 has been framed and the relevant provisions have been made. As per the notification dated 23rd May, 2014. It has come into effect from 01.6.2014. In accordance with the provisions of Section 3 of the said Act and Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Sugarcane Rates (Supply to Factories) Rules, 2016, the Government of Maharashtra has issued a notification to the effect. Two posts of members of private sugar mills in the Sugarcane Control Board constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary are vacant. Also, 2 posts of members of cooperative sugar factories are vacant. The matter of appointing non-official members to these vacant posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In accordance with the provisions of Section 3 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Sugarcane Rates (Supply to Factories) Act, 2013 and Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Sugarcane Rates (Supply to Factories) Rules, 2016, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to impose a new rate of Rs.
