The Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project will be implemented in all districts of the state for a period of six years (from 2020 to 2026). The Cabinet meeting was held on 29/07/2020. Accordingly, the project will be implemented. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 30.09.2020. The Maharashtra Agribusiness Network Project (MAGNET) was set up under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.
Government has approved the establishment of" Maharashtra Agribusiness Network Project (MAGNET) Institute" under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 (XXI of 1860) to implement the Asian Development Bank (ADB)-funded Maharashtra Agribusiness Network (MAGNET) project in all the districts of the state for a period of 6 years (from 2020 to 2026).
