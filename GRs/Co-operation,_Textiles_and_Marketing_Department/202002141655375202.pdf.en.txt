For this, the list of eligible beneficiaries will be issued by processing the loan account information uploaded by the banks through the portal. The benefit amount will be credited directly to the loan account of the farmers included in such a list after they go to the nearest Common Service Center/ Cheap Grain Shop/ Your Government Center and get it approved through Aadhaar authentication.
Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme-2019 The Chief Minister presided over the meeting. A Video Conference was held on 7.02.2020. In the meeting, various aspects of the project were discussed. At that time, in the context of Aadhaar authentication of farmers in Gadchiroli district, it was observed that the villages in Gadchiroli district lacked internet and other similar facilities as they were located in extremely difficult hilly terrain. Therefore, there are difficulties in authenticating the eligible beneficiaries while implementing this scheme. Considering this problem, the government was considering providing additional funds to Gadchiroli district for the provision of vehicles to transport beneficiaries to the nearest villages where internet facilities are available for Aadhaar authentication of eligible beneficiaries.
Government Decision: For the purpose of Aadhaar authentication of farmers in the Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers Debt Waiver Scheme, IEC activity to take farmers of villages in Gadchiroli district which do not have internet facility to the nearest place with internet facility through vehicle system has been made available to them as per the demand of the Collector, Gadchiroli. 5 lakh in addition to Rs. 8,02,000/- (Rs.
