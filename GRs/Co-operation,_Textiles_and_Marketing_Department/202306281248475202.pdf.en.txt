Introduction: It was pointed out that hailstorms and unseasonal rains in the state during 2021 and 2022 caused damage to agricultural crops. It was also observed that due to COVID-19 outbreak and other reasons, some of the proposals seeking funds were not processed. Farmers have been badly hit due to scarcity of water due to natural calamities. As per the decision of the State Executive Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting held on 31/03/2023, the funds have been disbursed to provide relief to the affected farmers as per the decision of the Government in the above mentioned (6). As per clause (v) of paragraph 2 of the said Government Resolution, no bank shall recover any amount from the amount of assistance while crediting the amount of assistance for the loss of agricultural crops and perennial crops directly to the bank account of the account holder.
In this regard, the Cooperation Department has issued necessary orders. This directive
The matter was under consideration of the government to issue an order in this regard.

GOVERNMENT Circular No.: SRIKH- 0623/ PR No. 146/18-C GOVERNMENT Circular: In the wake of hailstorm and untimely rains in the state during the year 2021 and 2022, various precautions have been directed to be taken while distributing funds to the farmers and perennial crops.
In accordance with the said direction, it is hereby notified that no bank shall recover any amount from the amount of assistance while depositing the amount of assistance allocated for the loss of crops and perennial crops due to hailstorm and untimely rains in the bank accounts of the account holders. The Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune should ensure that the implementation of this will be done carefully.
