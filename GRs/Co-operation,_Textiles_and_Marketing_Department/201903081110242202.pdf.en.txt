The Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department has issued a circular to the villages where drought has been declared as on 21.2.2019. As per the instructions dated 19.12.2019, the amount of Kharif Crop Loan for the year 2018 has been increased from Rs. Approval is being given for moratorium till 31.10.2019 and restructuring of short term crop loans into medium term loans (tenure 5 years).
For easy reference, see above. 8.1.2019 and dt. The annexures to the Government Decisions dated 21.2.2019 are attached herewith, the relevant Government Decisions are available on the website of Government of Maharashtra.
As the date of repayment of crop loan for Kharif-2018 season is 31st March, 2019, the crop loan for Kharif-2018 season should be restructured into medium term loan with interest for a period of 5 years with the consent of the farmers of the above affected villages who are not able to repay the crop loan within the stipulated time.
All the banks have initiated the process of restructuring of crop loans in Kharif-2018. It should be completed by July 31, 2019 and crop loans should be made available to such farmers for the next season. The Co-operation Commissioner and Registrar, Co-operative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune as well as the Coordinator, State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC) Bank of Maharashtra, Pune should ensure that this Government circular is implemented scrupulously.
