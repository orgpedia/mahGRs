Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in the government service for the disabled. The central government has given. List of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, may be seen in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in and in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification, the list of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on the website. Published on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure the posts of' peon' in Group-D cadre in the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (itself), Mantralaya, Mumbai Division and to ensure the posts for the newly disabled category by reviewing the list of seats in the Government service ensured for the disabled after a period of not more than 3 years.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, has issued a notification to ensure reservation of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published.
As mentioned in the notification, the Central Government has ensured the posts for the disabled by ensuring the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which are in the state government establishments, the posts published by the Central Government.
