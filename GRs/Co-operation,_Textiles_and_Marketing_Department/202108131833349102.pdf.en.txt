The Proposal:
The Textile Policy of the State has been announced as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 1. Under this policy, a concession in electricity rates has been announced for textile projects. Accordingly, the power concession has been applied to textile projects as per the government decisions in Reference No. 4. In this regard, the matter of amending the procedure for applying the electricity tariff concession was under consideration of the Government in accordance with the request made by the Commissioner (Textiles), vide letter No. 7.
Government's decision:
As per the Government decision of Reference No. 4, power subsidy has been applied to textile projects.
The part of it. 4 prescribes the procedure for distribution of electricity tariff concession. This process is improved as follows:
Procedure for implementation of Power tariff concession: (1) Proposals for machinery plants having load less than 27 HP shall be examined and approved at the level of Commissioner (Textiles).
(2) After receiving proposals from the Commissioner (Textiles) for the textile units other than the machinery units mentioned in No. 1 above, such as machine mills, co-operatives as well as private spinning/ knitting/ hosiery/ garmenting/ processing industries and all other textile projects, the power subsidy will be applied to these proposals with the approval of the Government.
