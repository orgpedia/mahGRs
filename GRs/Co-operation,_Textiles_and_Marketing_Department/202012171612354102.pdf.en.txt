Preamble: DONGAON Miscellaneous Executive Services Co-operative Societies Limited. and others against the government. The High Court of Judicature at Mumbai, vide Writ Petition No. 1967/2017. The Maharashtra government is the petitioner in the case. In view of the importance of the case, Shri Rajendra Raghuvanshi was appointed by a letter under reference as Special Counsel to represent the Government properly.
In this case, the matter of payment of agreed professional fees to Mr. Rajendra Raghuvanshi, Special Counsel, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Petition No. In the Writ Petition 1967/2017, on behalf of the respondent, High Court, Mumbai. Shri Rajendra Raghuvanshi, Special Counsel for appearing before Shri Shinde during the hearing dated 02/04/2019 and 23/04/2019 and to represent the Government, a sum of Rs. 3,00,000/ (Rupees Three Lakh only) towards professional fees (Advocate Fee).
The fees of Shri Rajendra Raghuvanshi, Public Prosecutor in respect of Court work shall be paid from the funds available for the year 2020-21 from the head of accounts under the head" V-02, 3451 Secretarial Financial Services (090) Secretariat, (01) Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department, (34510304) (Dattamat) 28 Professional Services" of this Department.
The hearing dated 02/04/2019 and 23/04/2019, which was approved as per this Government Decision, has been allotted Rs. 3,00,000/- deduction under the Income Tax Rules, 1961@ 10% of the total income tax (Rs. 30,000/-) and 4% education surcharge of Rs. 1200/-) and Rs. 2,68,800/- (Rupees Two Lakh Sixty Six Thousand Eight Hundred only) through RTGS.
