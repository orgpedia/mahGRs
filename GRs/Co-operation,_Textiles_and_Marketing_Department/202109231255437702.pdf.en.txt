GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per the Government Order dated 24.8.2020, the rate of collateral cost was fixed at Rs.40/- per quintal for procurement by NAFED and FCI at MSP for Kharif season 2020 and Rabi 2021.
The Committee constituted under the Government Order, dated 3/9/2019 to fix the rates of collateral cost for procurement under Minimum Support Price (MSP) Scheme at the meeting held on Wednesday, 15.9.2021 under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Cooperation& Marketing), has recommended Rs.44/-per quintal for Kharif 2021 and Rabi 2022 seasons
It has been decided that the quintal rate will continue to be applicable.
