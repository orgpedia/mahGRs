As per the decision of the Government on 28.08.2018, the taluks where the production of cotton is more than 9600 tonnes for 2 consecutive years will be included in the list of cotton producing taluks after reviewing it every year. The average production of cotton in the year 2020-21 and 2021-22 in Chandrapur district is 7982.73 tonnes. GOVERNMENT DECISION: As mentioned in the above Preamble, Chandrapur in Chandrapur district is being included in the list of Kapus Producing Taluks as a special case. Chandrapur is included in the list of Kapus producing taluks of Chandrapur district at Table 18 of the Table of Kapus Producing Taluks dated 28th August, 2018. With this, the total number of cotton producing taluks in the State has gone up to 123.
