2. Shushrusha Citizens Co-operative Hospital Ltd. The Co-operative Society, Mumbai has been given the mandate to provide the services mentioned in their proposal dated 29.8.2019. As per the decision of the Cabinet, Rs. Approval is being given to make available 25 crore financial assistance in the form of Redeemable Preference Shares. Also, to provide the said share capital," Shushrusha Citizens Co-operative Hospital Ltd. Approval for the new scheme of' Shareholding in Mumbai Co-operative Society' and approval for opening of new head of accounts in line with the same is being approved subject to the following conditions.
(1) Before providing funds to the Institute, the opinion of the Public Health Department regarding the eligibility and suitability of the Institute shall be taken. If the Department of Public Health gives proper feedback on the eligibility and suitability of the institution, for the financial years 2019-20, the first Rs. 10.00 crore will be distributed. Thereafter, after verifying the performance of the institution and the amount of reimbursement from the institution to the government for the year 2019-20, each of the next three financial years, Rs. A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore will be disbursed to the institute. Also, if the institution does not repay the government share capital as per schedule, no further funds will be disbursed by the state government to the institution.
(2) Shushrusha Citizens Co-operative Hospital Ltd. The company will create a capital redemption reserve fund to return Rs 25 crore.
Further, before calculating the profit every year, the institution shall deposit with the Government a certain amount as mentioned in the following table.

(3) A Director appointed by the Government in accordance with section 73AA of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Societies Act, 1960 shall be appointed on the Board of Directors of the said Hospital.
(4) The Institute shall provide free of cost in-patient services to the below poverty line patients as well as to the above poverty line patients at 50% rate.
