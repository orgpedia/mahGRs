Government's decision:
Under the Textiles Policy 2011-2017, interest subvention subsidy to textile units has been provided to the extent of Rs. 12000.00 lakhs out of which as per the statement in the Annexure accompanying this Government decision, a total of Rs. An amount of Rs. 86,99,45, 000/- (Rupees Eighty Six Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Forty Five Thousand only) is sanctioned and sanctioned for disbursement.
Similarly, the projects which are being implemented by the Vigilance and Control Team constituted under the Government decision in Reference No. 7.
In cases where inspection has been done and irregularities have been found in the projects, the subsidy will be disbursed only after the final decision or further action will be taken according to the decision.
Also, the projects which have not yet been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team due to lack of time will be inspected soon in the near future.
If any irregularity is found, the grant sanctioned through this order will be recovered along with interest.
The amount so sanctioned to the above Textile units shall be disbursed by R.T.G.S. to the accounts of the banks mentioned in the Annexure accompanying this Government decision as per the guidelines of the Scheme.
