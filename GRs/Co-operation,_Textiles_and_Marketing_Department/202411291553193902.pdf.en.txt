The Proposal: -
In order to create an inclusive environment for the export of goods and services produced by cooperatives in the country, the Union Ministry of Cooperation, as per the provisions of the Multi-State Cooperative Societies Act, 2002, has established National Cooperative Export Organizations Limited at the national level. {National Cooperative Exports Limited (NCEL)} has been established. The principal objective of NCEL is to make the goods and services produced through cooperatives and surplus produced to meet local needs globally through exports. Membership of NCEL ranges from primary agricultural credit co-operative to apex bodies in the three-tier credit structure. At present, about 5500 institutions across the country are members of NCEL.
Directives to designate appropriate agency as "Nodal Agency" for coordination between State Government and NCEL for export of exportable products from agriculture and allied sectors of the State through NCEL. has been given by the Minister of Cooperation, Government of India.
Given the corporation's experience in this area, it would be advisable to designate the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation (MCDC), Pune, as the "nodal agency" for communication between the state government and NCEL.
