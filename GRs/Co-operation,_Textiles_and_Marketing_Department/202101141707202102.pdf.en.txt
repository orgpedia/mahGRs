The proposal: 156/2011 in the High Court of Bombay. The High Court, Bombay vide its order dated 5/5/2017, has directed that" a separate set up be approved for the Cooperative Court and Cooperative Appellate Courts on the lines of District Courts and Civil Court. Also, a separate cadre should be created separate from the Commissionerate of Cooperation," it said. In pursuance of this decision, separate cadre pattern of 255 posts of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Tribunal and Co-operative Tribunal has been approved vide Government Order No.4 dated 28/2/2019. However, since the post of" Assistant Registrar" mentioned at serial no. 4 of paragraph 1 of the said Government Order is not in existence in the Maharashtra Co-operative Appellate Tribunal and other Co-operative Courts, the Finance Department vide its letter dated 31/8/2020 has approved necessary amendment in the minutes of the meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries held on 8/2/2019 in this regard. Therefore, in view of the above, the matter was under consideration of the Government to fix the revised staffing pattern by abolishing the separate staffing pattern of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court and Co-operative Court for 255 posts fixed as on 28/2/2019.
Government's decision:
The Government is giving approval for separate staffing pattern of 255 posts of Maharashtra State Co-operative Appellate Court and Co-operative Court subject to the following conditions:
The conditions for reduction of 2 posts of Assistant Registrar (9300 34800+ Grade Pay 4400) cadre from the establishment of Cooperative Commissioner and Registrar Cooperative Societies and higher pay structure in the future will not be demanded. Subject to this condition, the establishment of the Co-operative Appellate and Co-operative Court shall be governed by the provisions of the" Manager Co-operative Appellate Court" (Rs. The Government is approving creation of 2 new posts in the cadre of 9300-34800+ Grade Pay 4400- 6th Pay Commission (S- 15:41800-132300- 7th Pay Commission).
