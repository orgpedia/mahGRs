Preamble: The State Government has issued a" Revised Manual of Procedure for procurement of offices by Government Departments" vide Government Resolution dated 01/12/2016. Paragraph No. 2.9.3 of the said rule book provides for constitution of a Procurement Committee for the Administrative Department of the Ministry (itself). Considering the above, the Purchase Committee for the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (Department of Textiles) has decided to procure the items required for the department through the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (Department of Textiles), for the Administrative Department of the Ministry (itself) vide Government Resolution No. It was launched on March 18, 2016. Under the e-Governance Programme, a Project Implementation Committee has been constituted on 24.08.2011 under the Chairmanship of Secretary. As per General Administration (IT) Department, dated 29.10.2001, the procurement of computer materials and other ancillary items required by the Department is done with the approval of the Project Implementation Committee. However, after the purchase of the department under the e-governance program, the government was considering the formation of a revised procurement committee under the chairmanship of the concerned Joint/ Deputy Secretary to check the technical tender and financial tender of the goods purchased through the GeM portal or by calling for a tariff sheet.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of paragraph no. 2.9.3 of the revised Rules of Procedure for procurement of offices by Government Departments of the State Government, the following Ministerial Administrative Department (Self) Procurement Committees are being constituted for the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles (Self):

| 2. | Representative of the Information and Technology Corporation | The Invited          |
| 3. | Representative of the Directorate General of Industry        | The Invited          |
| 4. | Representative of the Directorate of Accounts and Treasury   | the members          |
| 5. | the Under Secretary/ Cell Officer of the concerned office    | The Member Secretary |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Committee shall take action to complete all the procedures as per the provisions of the rule book dated 01/12/2016.
