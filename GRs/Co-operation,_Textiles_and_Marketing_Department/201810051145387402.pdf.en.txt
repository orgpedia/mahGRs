Letter from the Project Director (JFPR), AIDIP Project, Pune No. JFPR/ Supplementary Demand 2018-19/1080/ 2018, D. 13.08.2018 Preamble: The Agricultural Trade Infrastructure Development Investment Project (AIDIP) is being implemented in the State with financial assistance from Asian Development Bank. The JFPR-assisted project to connect small farmers in Maharashtra to markets is funded by the Asian Development Bank in the form of a 100% grant.
