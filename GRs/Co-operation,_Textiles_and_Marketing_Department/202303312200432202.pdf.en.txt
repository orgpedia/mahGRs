Preamble: In pursuance of the above Government decision, the guidelines for the scheme of Capital Subsidy Scheme in lieu of Interest Subsidy to Textile units under the Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra have been prescribed. Accordingly, under the Textile Policy, the Government was considering to disburse subsidy to the proposals received under the Capital Subsidy Scheme instead of interest subsidy to the textile units as per the provision available in the year 2022-23 as per the statement in the" Annexure" accompanying this Government decision.
Government's decision:
Under the Textile Policy of the State, the capital subsidy scheme in lieu of interest subsidy to the textile units is being implemented. Approved by the Committee chaired by Principal Secretary (Textiles),
The projects will be funded out of the provision approved for expenditure by the Finance Department in 2022-23. 10850.96 lakhs out of which as per the statement in Annexure A accompanying this Government Resolution, a total of Rs. An amount of Rs. 108,50,96, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eight Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand) is sanctioned and sanctioned for disbursement. However, this amount will be disbursed only after the final decision in the case of the projects that have been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team constituted under the decision of the government here in Reference No. 6 and in the case of the projects in which irregularities have been found, or according to the decision made.

Further action will be taken. Also, the projects which have not yet been inspected by the Vigilance and Control Team due to lack of time will be inspected soon in the near future and if any irregularity is found in the inspection of that project, the subsidy sanctioned through this order will be recovered with interest.
Similarly, due to the low outlay available under the scheme, the sanctioned projects have been disbursed subsidy in proportion to their sanctioned subsidy. The remaining grants for these projects will be disbursed as and when funds are available for expenditure under the scheme.
The amount so sanctioned to the Textile Sector should be disbursed through RTGS to the accounts of the banks mentioned in the Annexure accompanying this Government Resolution as per the guidelines of the Scheme. The amount of interest concession sanctioned as above should be deposited by the concerned bank in the loan account of their respective textile unit in the" Annexure" accompanying this Government decision and the utilization certificate should be submitted online to the Government on the website.
