Preamble: As per the Government Decision dated 26th December, 2018, initially, subsidy has been sanctioned to eligible farmers at the rate of Rs.200 per quintal and maximum of Rs.200 per quintal per farmer for selling onions in Agricultural Produce Market Committees and Private Market Committees of Prasanna Agriculture Market, Padliyale in the State from 1st November, 2018 to 15th December, 2018. The period of Onion Subsidy under the Scheme has been extended from time to time and the revised due date has been fixed from 1st November, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.

The Finance Department has given permission to use the interest amount for the implementation of the scheme. Accordingly, the matter of approving the disbursement of the amount remaining in the ICICI Bank account of the Directorate of Marketing and the interest received on this amount to the eligible beneficiaries of Sangli district for onion subsidy was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Since the amount remaining unspent in the ICICI Bank account and the interest received on the said amount is required to be utilized for the purpose of Onion Subsidy, it is being approved to disburse the pending Onion Subsidy to the eligible beneficiaries of Sangli district from the amount remaining unspent in the ICICI Bank account and the interest received on the said amount.
The subsidy will be paid through ICICI Bank.
