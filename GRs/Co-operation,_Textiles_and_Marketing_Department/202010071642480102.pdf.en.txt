Government's decision:
A total of 185 temporary posts of Class 1, Class 3 and Class 4 in the Cooperative Courts have been allowed to continue till 31/8/2020. 2 Approval has been granted pursuant to the Government Decision dated 31/3/2020. The approval for continuation of these 185 temporary posts from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021 has been accorded by the Cooperation Commissioner and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune vide reference No. The request has been made vide letter dated 29/9/2020.
The revised pattern of posts in the field establishment of this Department has been approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 21/1/2011. Accordingly, the Government Decision dated 5/5/2011 has been issued for the revised pattern and the revised pattern includes the said temporary posts. As per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 1/9/2004 of the Department of Finance, the figure is required to be reviewed every three years. Accordingly, the figure bond issued vide Government Resolution dated 5/5/2011 has completed three years as on 5/5/2014. Proposal to fix revised figure The process of submitting the report to the Finance Department with the approval of the high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary is underway.
As per Department of Finance, Government Decision dated 31/8/2020, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to grant extension of tenure from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021 to all the existing temporary posts included in their rolls as well as those created after approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls.
Meanwhile, the government has also decided that all administrative departments should take care of getting the approval of the high-level committee of secretaries to the revised figure.
