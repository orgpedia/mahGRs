Introduction: Refer Note No. Under Section 2, the Agriculture Processing Cooperative Societies under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune, 31 temporary posts in the scheme were approved to be continued till 28/02/2021. Reference No. 4 As per the letter, the request for extension of 31 temporary posts under the scheme has been made by the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies. Now, the Finance Department is authorized to extend the tenure of these posts from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021. There are 3 administrative departments.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: By this Government Decision, it is being approved to extend the tenure of 31 temporary posts for audit of Agricultural Processing Cooperative Societies under the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Pune from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 subject to the following conditions.
(a) The extension shall not be applicable to temporary posts lying vacant for more than six months.
b) The reasons for which the temporary positions have been approved should be followed.
c) Take necessary action to finalize the revised figure as directed by the Finance Department from time to time.
d) If the process of revision of the revised pattern/ posts is not completed within the deadline, the temporary posts will not be extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officer/ employee, it will be the responsibility of the concerned office.
