With a view to encourage the handloom weavers of the state and to promote the best varieties produced by the weavers, the Government of Maharashtra has been implementing the Handloom Textile Competition and Prize Scheme since 1972-73 for the best samples of the fabric produced by the handloom weavers of the state. As per the decision of the Government of Department of Textiles dated 17.10.2016, the Scheme has been restructured and the prize money has also been increased and its annual figure has been approved. The proposal for release of funds from the budgetary provision for implementation of the BAMSHA Scheme in 2018-19 has been received from the Directorate of Textiles vide letter dated 23rd October, 2018. Accordingly, the government had decided to allocate funds for the project.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the heading" Demand No.V-2, Accounts Head 2851 Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 110 Composite Village Industries and Small Scale Industries and Co-operative Societies, (02) (50) Exemption and Other Financial Assistance for Development of Handloom Industry (28510119) 05 Prizes" Rs. 3,60,000/- (Rs. three hundred and sixty thousand only).
