Letter from the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune vide No. Krishipat-8/ Demand for Fund/ 2022-23/2022/ 972, dated 09/06/2022 Preamble: Under the context of the Government Decision No. 1, the State Government has decided to provide Government financial assistance to the farmers of the State for the supply of short term crop loans at 6% interest rate. As per the policy of the central government, banks should provide loans to farmers at 6% interest rate instead of 7% where banks are providing loans to farmers at 7% interest rate. For this purpose, the financial burden of the 1% interest differential amount is on the government. The benefit of this decision is being given to the District Central Co-operative Banks, Nationalized Banks and Regional Rural Banks in the State during Kharif and Rabi seasons from 2006-07 and to the private banks who have disbursed short term crop loans up to Rs. 3.00 lakh to the farmers since 2013-14.
