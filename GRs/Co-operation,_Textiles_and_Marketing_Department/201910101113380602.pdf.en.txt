The Proposal:
NAFED has been appointed as the lead agent for procurement of various pulses, cereals and oilseeds at MSP by Government of India during 2013-14. The Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation, Mumbai is appointed as the Deputy Agent for the implementation of this scheme in the state. An amount of Rs. 100.00 lakh has been provided for the financial year 2019-2020 under the head Financial Assistance for Contingent Expenditure (2425 2417) for the procurement being done by NAFED through Maharashtra State Cooperative Marketing Federation under Minimum Support Price Scheme. The circular dated 8th July, 2019 of the Department of Finance empowers the administrative departments to disburse budgetary allocations on essential and programme expenditure required for the first nine months of 2019-2020 (ending December, 2019) within a limit of 60% of the total annual provision. Accordingly, the Government was considering to disburse 60% of the budgetary provision of the scheme (2425 2417) to provide funds to the Maharashtra State Co-operative Marketing Federation for meeting the expenditure incurred on payment of farm produce purchased through NAFED.
