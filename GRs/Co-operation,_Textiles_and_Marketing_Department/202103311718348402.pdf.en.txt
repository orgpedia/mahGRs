INTRODUCTION: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 1st March, 2012 in the above Reference Table 1, the interest subvention scheme for long term loans related to Centrally Sponsored TUFS has been announced under the Textile Policy of the State of Maharashtra. Under this scheme, the government had issued a decision to disburse subsidy to the proposals received under this scheme in the year 2019-20 as per the available provision. However, the Finance Department did not approve the disbursement of the grant and the funds could not be disbursed to those projects. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the subsidy to the projects under the said Government Decision from the available provision of 2020-21 as per the statement in Annexure A to N accompanying this Government Decision.
