Department of Finance As per the provisions of the Governing Decision dated 1/9/2004, the framework is required to be reviewed every three years. The government has taken the decision. Figures issued as of 5/5/2011. Three years have passed on 5/5/2014. Proposal to fix revised figure The process of submitting the report to the Finance Department with the approval of the high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary is underway.
Finance Department, Govt.
