Introduction: During the four consecutive years from 2015-16 to 2018-19, due to drought-like situation in various parts of the state, a large amount of less than 50 paise was released. The farmers of the state have been badly affected due to scanty rainfall in the state. Due to the loss of farmers in the wake of this natural disaster, the loan related to agriculture could not be repaid in time for a few years.
As a result, the farmers are in debt and are unable to get loans for their crops. The Mahatma Jotirao Phule Farmers' Debt Waiver Scheme 2019 has been announced to bring farmers out of the vicious cycle of debt and to relieve them of worries. The government decision on debt relief scheme has been issued under Section 1.
So far, Rs. 20472.75 crore has been disbursed. The proposal to disburse loan waiver to the remaining eligible beneficiaries was under consideration of the Government as per the demand received vide letter No. 3 dated 20.01.2023 from the Commissioner of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Maharashtra State, Pune.
