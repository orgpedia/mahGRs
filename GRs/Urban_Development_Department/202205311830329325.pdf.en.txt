Government's decision:
The Sewerage (Phase-I) Project of Khopoli Municipal Council submitted under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahaabhiyan is being approved by this Government Resolution as mentioned in the following statement subject to the terms and conditions of the relevant Government Resolution and the technical approval granted by the Maharashtra Life Authority.
The State Government has decided to implement the Sewerage Project in two phases considering the fund limit for implementing the Sewerage Project and to focus on creating sewage treatment capacity on priority basis. 4), as well as reference No. The decision was taken on December 31, 2018. According to this policy, in the first phase, the sewage generated at present should be treated on priority by creating a sewage treatment system according to the future needs. The STP capacity required to treat the entire waste water should be sanctioned in the first phase.
These include a sewage treatment plant, a sewage treatment plant, a water supply station and a sewage treatment plant. Along with this, arrangements should also be made to collect waste water in some of the different wards of the city. For the rest of the ward, the sewage from the Trunk Main should be collected in a drain and the drain should be blocked near the Sewage Treatment Plant (STP) and the sewage should be taken to the Sewage Treatment Plant at that place and treated. In the second phase, a sewerage system should be constructed and connected to the sewage treatment system created in the first phase. The state government has adopted a policy that this phase will include second-level sewers. Accordingly, in-principle approval is being given to the entire sewerage project of Khopoli town in a phased manner. One of the above
