The Proposal:
The Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority vide letter dated 10.09.2018 had submitted the project on the basis of Swiss Challenge Methodology to the Maharashtra Infrastructure Development Competent Authority under the Maharashtra Infrastructure Development Competent Authority Act, 2018. Pursuant to the decision of the Maharashtra Infrastructure Development Authority in its meeting dated 14.09.2018, the Pune Mumbai Hyperloop Project has been designated as" Public Infrastructure Project" along with DP World FZE& Hyperloop Technologies, Inc. The proposal of the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority to declare it as an original project proponent was under the consideration of the government. The Government have taken the following decisions in this regard:
Government's decision:
The Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) has approved the Pune Mumbai Hyperloop Project as a" Public Infrastructure Project" along with DP World FZE& Hyperloop Technologies, Inc. The following are being approved for declaration as Original Project Proponent:
1) The Pune-Mumbai Hyperloop project is being approved to be declared as an" infrastructure project."
DP World FZE and Hyperloop Technologies, Inc. This partnership company is being recognized as an Original Project Proponent.
3) As per the provisions of Section 5 (3) of the Maharashtra Infrastructure Development Authority Act, 2018," the Authority may, in such manner as may be prescribed, entrust its functions in respect of specified infrastructure projects to the concerned department or authority of the Government, Government agency or local authority, as the case may be," the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority is being approved to appoint a Transaction Advisor for the implementation of the said project.
