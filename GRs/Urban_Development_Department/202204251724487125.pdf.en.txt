Introduction: Refer No. 1 The policy regarding the inclusion of the employees of the respective then Gram Panchayats in the newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats has been laid down under the decision of the Government. Also, amendments have been made from time to time to include more and more eligible Gram Panchayat employees.
As per the prevailing policy, the eligible employees are inducted in the respective newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats as well as in other Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the district. Nevertheless, which
The Directorate of Municipal Administration had submitted a proposal to the government to take a policy decision at the department level for the inclusion of employees who are not included in the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats as well as other Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the district due to non-availability of staff posts. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: In view of the inclusion of the then eligible Gram Panchayat employees, if the vacant posts are available in the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats of the concerned revenue division despite the appointments under the Heritage, Lad Pages and Compassionate Scheme and the appointment/ inclusion of eligible daily wage employees already approved by the Government as per the policy decision, the inclusion of the then eligible Gram Panchayat employees in the newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the said Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats of the concerned revenue division is being approved.
