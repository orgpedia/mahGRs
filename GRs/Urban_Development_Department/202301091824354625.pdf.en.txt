About 50 per cent of the state's population lives in urban areas and the state has 28 municipal corporations and more than 400 municipalities/ nagar panchayats.
Urban facilities are provided to the citizens through the institutions. Financial empowerment of these Urban Local Bodies is of utmost importance to enable them to provide these facilities more effectively. Similarly, in the national level conference of Chief Secretaries held at Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh from 15th to 17th June, 2022, instructions have also been given to take measures for financial empowerment of Urban Local Bodies across the country. Accordingly, the matter of forming a state-level committee to suggest measures was under the consideration of the government, on which the government is taking the following decisions.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. A State Level Committee is being constituted to suggest measures for financial empowerment of Municipal Corporations in the State as follows.
