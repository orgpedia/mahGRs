The Goods and Services Tax (GST) has been fixed at 12% on most government offices.
Now, certain provisions relating to Government functionaries which were earlier notified vide Notification dated 28.06.2017 through Government Notification of Reference No. 6 and 7 have been omitted. Now, the Goods and Services Tax (9% CGST+ 9% SGST) rate has been fixed for services. Under Reference No.7, the Finance Department has introduced 18% rate in the State with effect from 18th July, 2022.
Accordingly, the government was considering to implement the revised rates in water supply/ sewerage and other approved schemes under the centrally sponsored AMRUT, AMRUT 2.0 campaign and Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan of this department.
Circular:
In view of the above changes, following instructions are being issued regarding GST in the contracts of water supply/ sewerage and other sanctioned schemes under Centrally Sponsored AMRUT, AMRUT 2.0 Mission and Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan.
New Works Budgets:- As per the Government decision dated 25th June, 2021, instructions have been given to prepare the budgets as per the prevailing rates of Goods and Services Tax while preparing the new budgets. Accordingly, the Goods and Services Tax should now be levied at 18% while preparing the new budgets.
Payments for ongoing works: Payments for ongoing works, which are called for excluding tenders and GST, are made by calculating 12% GST. Now payments for such ongoing works shall be made subject to the tender terms and conditions from the date mentioned in the notification No. 7 under reference of the Department of Finance to pass the payments as per the changed 18% Goods and Services Tax.
While passing the payments for the ongoing works for which tenders have been called along with Goods and Services Tax, necessary action should be taken as per the tender terms and conditions taking into account the changed 18% Goods and Services Tax.
