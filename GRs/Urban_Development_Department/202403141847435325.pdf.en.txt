Preamble: - The 225 sq km area covered by Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation and Ambernath and Kulgam Badlapur Municipal Councils has a population of 33.43 lakhs as per 2011 census.
The number of private vehicles, as well as other private vehicles, is increasing in the area, leading to traffic jams and pollution. These Municipal Corporations / Municipal Councils have 12 Railway Stations spread over 225 sq.km area, with lakhs of people commuting daily from these stations and about 10 km in and around the perimeter of these Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils. The rural areas of the M. periphery have been converted into urban areas similar to the city.
Private vehicles cause a lot of traffic congestion on the road. Therefore, the speed of public buses was usually 16 km / hr in the east, it is now 9 km / hr. I'm so slow. In view of this, with the specific objective of motivating the citizens to use more public vehicles on the roads and maintaining the ecological balance of the city and controlling fuel-saving traffic congestion, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, with the combined participation of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation and Ambernath Municipal Council and HAMA Badlapur Municipal Council, had submitted a proposal to the government under read 1 and 2 to form an authority for the area and start public transport through it.
2. Considering the resolutions passed by Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation and Municipal Councils of Ambernath and Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Councils regarding introduction of combined transport services, no-objection mark given and the objective of providing easy, affordable, efficient, reliable, safe and equitable transport services, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government to approve the proposal for creation of an Authority for the area and introduction of public transport through it with the combined participation of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation and Ambernath and Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Council.

4. The following four points have been used while managing a separate special vehicle and the joint venture will be responsible for compliance.
Planning of Bus Trains: - Buses should be planned for each local body on the basis of population.
7 Planning of Staff: - The staff on the route should be transferred on deputation by external mechanisms and the administrative staff by the respective local body.
7 Asset Utilization and Planning: - The property within the concerned local body will be made available for transportation purposes. The property will be appropriated through a planning company. The Company shall have exclusive rights in this regard.
7 Planning of Movement Deficit: - Operational Losses in passenger traffic in public sector undertakings to be borne by the respective Municipal Corporations / Municipal Councils as per the number of buses which will be operating within the limits of the Municipal Corporation / Municipal Council and, in case of profit, the Transport Service Development works within the limits of the Municipal Corporation / Municipal Councils involved in the Company to be used for up-gradation.
The Board of Directors in charge shall complete the proceedings relating to the establishment, administrative and financial matters relating to the formation and operation of the AGM Company.
