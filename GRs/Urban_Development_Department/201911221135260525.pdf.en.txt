The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 26 Municipal Corporations in the State will result in an accumulation of Rs. 206,00,69, 566/- to the Municipal Corporations is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the month of July and August, 2019, the total amount released to the Municipal Corporations against the subsidy available on the Budget Distribution System for the month of July, 2019 towards 1% stamp duty is Rs. 206,00,69, 566/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Six Crores Seventy-Nine Thousand Five Hundred and Sixty Six only).
