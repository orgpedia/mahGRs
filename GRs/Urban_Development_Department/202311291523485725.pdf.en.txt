The Proposal:
Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment by Government of India Financial assistance is being provided to State Governments for capital investment projects under the Scheme 2023-24. The Ministry of Finance, Government of India has issued guidelines in this regard on 03.02.2023. Further, the guidelines of the Scheme" Financing Reforms in Urban Local Bodies to make them creditworthy for issuance of Municipal Bonds and for issue of Municipal Bonds under Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment 2023-24: Part-IV" dated 19.06.2023 have been prescribed. Under this scheme, if the urban local bodies submit proposals to the central government under Milestone 1 to 4, they will get an incentive grant from the central government. The government was considering opening a new account head for this fund in the budget.
Government's decision:
The Central Government under the Scheme" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment 2023-24" has approved the opening of the following heads of accounts for budgeting the funds for the Scheme" Financial Reforms in Urban Local Bodies to make them creditworthy for issuance of Municipal Bonds and for issue of Municipal Bonds Under Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment 2023-24: Part-IV."
