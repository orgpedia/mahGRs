Introduction: The Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation was established in the year 1992. It was started on 23.01.1996. At present, the transport undertaking is providing passenger services on a total of 70 different routes including Navi Mumbai, Brihanmumbai, Thane, Dombivali, Badlapur, Kharghar, Kalamboli, Panvel, Uran. It caters to over 2.5 lakh passengers daily.
2. Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking (NMMCT) has been given the mandate for transport activities in pursuance of various proposals submitted earlier. 20.07.2006 and D. The Government have approved the sanction of a total of 1489 posts as per the Government decisions dated 31.08.2009.
Considering the administrative functions of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, the day-to-day changes in the transport service, modernization and increase in the transport service, it was necessary to create a new designation and designate the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking. The Commissioner of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has issued a notification in this regard.
