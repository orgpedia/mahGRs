Preamble: Under the" Golden Jubilee Urban Employment Scheme" at the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, the group organizer working on contractual basis on wholesale wages has been included in the permanent list of employees working on wholesale wages in the same way as in other municipal corporations. On the same lines, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has requested to take a decision at the government level regarding regular inclusion of temporary group organizers working on contractual basis in the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation.
2. Figure of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation. It has been approved as per the Government Decision dated 01.06.2021. In this figure, 3 posts of group organizer have been approved. In view of the proposal of the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, the appointments of 03 group organizers working on contractual basis (contract) at wholesale rates have been made through this process (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview). Out of them, 1) Mrs. Suvarna Dagdu Gaikwad and 2) Mrs. Anita Laxman Kadam are eligible for the post of Group Organizer as they fulfill the educational qualification prescribed in the Service Entry Rules. Mr. Kishore Pandurang Gaikwad

They are not eligible as they do not meet the educational qualifications in the rules. Therefore, the matter of retaining the appointments of the 2 group organizers appointed in the prescribed way was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
Since the appointment of 02 Group Organizers namely 1) Smt. Suvarna Dagdu Gaikwad and 2) Smt. Anita Laxman Kadam on the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has been made by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview) and fulfilling the educational qualification prescribed in the Service Entry Rules, the approval of the Government is being given to continue their appointment on the approved and vacant posts on the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation on the pay scale of S-8: 25500-81100 as per the 7th Pay Commission subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) These employees should be appointed to the post of' Group Organizer' approved in the figure from the date of the government decision.
(2) The employees will continue to be paid the benefits of permanent service (pay, seniority, pension, etc.) from the date of posting.
(3) No benefit or arrears of service rendered earlier shall be admissible to such employees.
4) No funds will be provided by the government for the salary of these employees.
5) Before being reinstated in service, it will be clearly mentioned that no previous benefit will be received by taking a bond from the concerned employee.
While giving permanent appointment to the above mentioned 02' Group Organizers', permanent appointment will be given as per the rules after checking the attendance default record, inquiry, service attendance, caste validity verification certificate, medical certificate, etc. It will be mandatory for the concerned employees to submit the character verification report within a period of 06 months from the date of order of permanent appointment.
7) No one other than the above mentioned 2 employees on wholesale honorarium, who are currently working in Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, can be appointed to this post.
Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation should ensure that the pay scale of these employees while appointing them to the post of' Group Organizer' will not be higher than the pay scale equivalent to that post.
9) Since the establishment cost of the Municipal Corporation is more than 35% while including the group organizer, the condition of 35% establishment cost is being relaxed in this case.
