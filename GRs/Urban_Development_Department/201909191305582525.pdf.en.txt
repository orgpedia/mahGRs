Preamble: Establishment of Thane Municipal Corporation. The said Municipal Corporation was formed on 01.10.1982 and is a' B' class Municipal Corporation. At present, there are 9 wards of the Municipal Corporation, with a total area of 147 sq km. As per the 2011 census, the Thane Municipal Corporation has a population of 18.41 lakh and a floating population of 24 lakh.
2. The ever-increasing population of Thane Municipal Corporation, due to which there is a pressure on the infrastructure, effective implementation of various schemes of the state and central government, increasing demand from the citizens for public welfare and transparent work, and in order to carry out the increased administrative work, there is a need to create new posts in various departments of Thane Municipal Corporation. Therefore, the matter of creating new posts on the establishment of Thane Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.

The posts outside the establishment of Thane Municipal Corporation are being created subject to the following terms and conditions:

The posts will continue to be in existence till they are promoted, after which such posts will automatically lapse.
