The proposal:
Neha Vithalrao Jivtode, Clerk, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation has requested to transfer her services from Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to Nagpur Municipal Corporation, due to her family reasons. Accordingly, the Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed General Body Resolution No. 56, d. Approved by Nagpur Municipal Corporation on 10.08.2016. The transfer of services of Ms. Jivatode has been approved vide letter dated 19.12.2019.
Since the rules for the entry into service of the Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation and the Nagpur Municipal Corporation have not yet been finalized, Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Municipal Services (Recruitment and Classification of Services) Rules, 2008, specifies the following provisions for transfer from one Municipal Corporation to another.
" Notwithstanding anything contained elsewhere in this rule, if an employee of another corporation submits an application for transfer to a post in a corporation, the transfer may be made subject to the following conditions, in accordance with the provisions of the Act and on such terms and conditions as may be agreed upon by the appointing authorities of both the corporations with mutual consent:
(a) There shall be no transfer of posts. Appointments should be made only to the posts reserved for appointment by nomination.

(b) On such appointment, the seniority of the incumbent shall be zero seniority in the cadre of the post as on the date of appointment.
(c) The transfer of officers of Category-I and Category-II from one Municipal Corporation to another shall be decided by mutual consent of both the Municipal Corporations on definite terms and conditions with the approval of the Government. "
Considering the mutual correspondence and subsequent information regarding the transfer of Ms. Jivtode, both the Municipal Corporations are positive about the transfer/ transfer of Ms. Neha Vithalrao Jivtode. Also, Ms. Neha Jivtode is a Class-3 employee in the clerical cadre and the expenses of her salary will be borne by the concerned Municipal Corporation.
In the above background, the matter of transferring the services of Ms. Neha Vithalrao Jivtode, Clerk on the establishment of Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to Nagpur Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Ms. Neha Vithalrao Jivtode, Clerk, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, to work in the pay scale equivalent to the service of the above mentioned post. Subject to the terms and conditions of the Maharashtra Municipal Services (Recruitment of Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2008, the transfer of Nanded-Waghala City from the Municipal Corporation to the Nagpur Municipal Corporation is being approved by the Government.
Commissioner, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation shall relieve Ms. Neha Vithalrao Jivtode, Clerk, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to join the Nagpur Municipal Corporation immediately after verifying her service matters. Also, the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation should appoint Ms. Neha Vitthalrao Jivtode as Junior Clerk in the Municipal Corporation.
