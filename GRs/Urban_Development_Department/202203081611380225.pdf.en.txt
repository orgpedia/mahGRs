The MUTP-3 project has been approved to be implemented by Mumbai Railway Vikas Corporation (MRVC) in partnership with Indian Railways on the lines of Mumbai Urban Transport Project Phase-I and Phase-II with 50:50 financial participation. The project is being implemented by the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC), the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) and the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). The Second Financial Agreement and Supplementary Agreement were signed on 9th July, 2020. The project is funded by the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB). 3500 crore (USD 500 million) has been sanctioned.
The loan agreement was signed between the Government of India and AIIB Bank and the project agreement was signed between AIIB Bank, Government of Maharashtra and MRVC. It was signed on 24.08.2020.
The project is being implemented by the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC), the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC), and the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). As per the Financial Agreement and Supplementary Agreement signed on 9th July, 2020, the financial contribution to be borne by MMRDA and CIDCO on behalf of the State Government in the said project is to be made available to MRVC in advance in various installments based on the estimated cost. Similarly, if the external debt component is spent on projects through MRVC, such expenditure has to be met by MMRD. MRVC submits reimbursement claims to AIIB Bank for the expenses incurred. Once the reimbursement claims are approved by the AIIB, the same amount is disbursed by the AIIB Bank to the Central Government in the form of a loan. The amount is deposited by the Central Government in the Consolidated Fund of the State. The amount of AIIB loan received by the Maharashtra government as additional central assistance from the central government on the funds made available by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority to the Mumbai Railway Vikas Corporation (MRVC) on account of the loan of the state government is to be disbursed by the state government to the MMRDA.
The amount collected by the Railways on account of surcharge on suburban train tickets will be utilised for repayment of loan taken from AIIB for the project and for payment of lease charges against the loan.
