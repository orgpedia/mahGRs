A committee under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division was constituted at the government level under reference number 2 to take policy decisions under the inclusion of employees from the gram panchayat of Purvashrami in the Panvel Municipal Corporation. Following the report of the committee, the Commissioner and Director of Municipal Administration Directorate had submitted a report to the government in this regard.
