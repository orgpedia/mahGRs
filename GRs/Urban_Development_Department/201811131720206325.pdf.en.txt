On the lines of the Central Government's" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)," the implementation of" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is going on in mission mode in the State as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 1. The two major components of this engineering include making cities" open defecation free" and" clean" under solid waste management.
So far, 245 cities in the state have been given administrative approval for the Detailed Project Report under Solid Waste Management under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban). Detailed project
The report includes the scientific process of bio-mining of waste stored in cities for many years.
While giving administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report of these cities, it was instructed not to spend the funds sanctioned for bio-mining under the Solid Waste Management Project until a state-level policy is decided to implement the bio-mining process under the Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban).
As bio-mining is an innovative process to scientifically process and dispose of the solid waste stored without processing in the cities of the state, a committee was formed under the chairmanship of the Commissioner and Director, Municipal Administration under the Government Decision dated 27.10.2017 and dated 17.11.2017 to study the process and determine the modus operandi.
