Preamble: The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and to improve their quality.
The water supply project of Arvi Municipal Council under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotna Abhiyan was submitted to the State Level Approval Committee for approval. The project has been given technical approval by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Nagpur. The project has been approved by the State Level Project Approval Committee. As per the recommendation made in the meeting held on 09.01.2018, the matter of granting administrative approval to the said project was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The water supply project of Arvi Municipal Council submitted under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Nagarothana Mahabhiyana is being approved by this Government Decision as mentioned in the following statement subject to the terms and provisions of the relevant Government Decision and the technical approval given by the Maharashtra Life Authority. The details of the project and its cost are as follows:

As per the decision of the Government No. 1 under reference, the administrative approval is being given to the water supply project of Arvi Municipal Council subject to the following conditions: a) Project Implementation Mechanism:• The project should be implemented by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
• It will be mandatory for the implementing agency to fulfill all the conditions prescribed by the State Level Project Approval Committee while approving the project.
b) Fulfillment of Reforms: In the context of Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahabhivana No. As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the Urban Local Bodies will be required to complete the following binding and optional reforms. Of these, 80% of the optional conditions must be met by the second year of the project.
1) Compulsory Reforms: It will be mandatory for the Municipal Councils to fully computerize their activities in the first year of project approval. This will include 100% implementation of e-governance, accounting, and birth and death registration reforms.
To recover at least 80% by applying appropriate consumer tax.
Provision of certain funds in the budget for the poor in urban areas.
• The respective local civic body will be required to complete the two-letter registration process within six months.
• The concerned Urban Local Bodies will be required to complete the property tax assessment in their area within a period of one year from the date of sanction of the project, if it has not been done.
It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Bodies to ensure proper management of solid waste in their jurisdiction as per the provisions of the Solid Waste Management Rules, 2016.

It will be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Bodies to collect at least 80% of the property tax and water charges in their jurisdiction in the first year. In the following year, the balance 90% will be required to be recovered.
(ii) the following amendments:
Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) undertaking sewerage projects should include in their Detailed Project Report (DPR) the components of reuse and treatment of sewage in urban areas.
The concerned urban local bodies should store rainwater on the building owned by them.
Regular water audits should be carried out by the respective urban local bodies. In respect of rectification of defects mentioned in the Water Records Inspection Report, the concerned Urban Local Body shall be required to complete the defects within the prescribed time frame by formulating necessary action programmes in this regard.
(c) Mode of disbursement of funds: The State Government's permissible share for the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyana will henceforth be disbursed in two installments.
After the administrative approval for the projects in the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, the concerned civic body should submit the financial closure report of the project to the Urban Development Department. After that, the first installment of the project will be released.
After disbursing the first installment of funds to the projects approved under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks prescribed by the government as per the prevailing policy.
The second installment of the State Government will be disbursed only after the utilization certificate of 70 percent of the total amount of the first installment of subsidy disbursed by the State Government and the first installment of self-assurance deposited by the urban local body is submitted to the Government through the Directorate of Municipal Administration.
The urban local bodies should submit the proposal to the government through the Directorate of Municipal Administration while demanding the second installment. The Directorate of Municipal Administration should scrutinize the proposals and submit them to the government with appropriate feedback.

Tendering process, work orders and implementation: It will be mandatory to issue tenders within one month after the release of the first installment of funds to the projects approved under this mission and to issue work orders within three months and to start the work physically before the 101st day.
The implementing agency shall be bound to follow the instructions contained in the Government Resolution No. 3 in the context while implementing the tender process of the said project.
In case of non-compliance, the concerned urban local body will be required to return the funds sanctioned for such project to the government along with interest thereon.
• While completing this water supply project, the State Government under reference No. 4 It will be mandatory to follow the procedures prescribed by the government circular here.
• It will be mandatory for the concerned implementing agency to complete the project within the prescribed time frame.
• After the work order of the project, the purchase of the pipes, etc. included in the project should be done according to the requirements of the project, under the guidance of the project management system.
• The purchase of unnecessary pipes for the project initially and providing it from the project fund will be considered as a serious financial irregularity and the concerned executing agency and project management consultancy will be held responsible for it.
After the State Government has sanctioned the share of the State Government to the concerned local body for the project approved under this mission, the local body concerned shall provide the amount of its own share in each payment at each stage of expenditure as per the amount due from the State Government.
With this, there will be no time to pay the share of the local body in such a scheme at once.
The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can only be used for the same project and cannot be diverted or used for any other purpose. Any such diversion of the principal amount or interest thereon, whether permanent or temporary, will be treated as a serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned local bodies/ concerned authorities.
e) Tertiary Technical Examination of Projects: Government/ Paramilitary Engineering from time to time at various stages of implementation of the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Maha Abhiyan.

(c) Maintenance and Repair of the Project: The necessary planning regarding the maintenance and repair of the project sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan should be done by the concerned executing agency so that the results from the said projects are sustainable.
The responsibility of maintenance and repair of this scheme will be of the concerned urban local body. No funds will be received from the government for maintenance and repair of the scheme.
Other terms and conditions: After the project is approved by the state government for the urban local body, the entire responsibility of any increase in the original project cost for any reason will be of the concerned urban local body.
No additional grant will be provided by the State Government for the same.
