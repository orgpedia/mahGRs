NABRAS/ NULM/ 2023-24/ Cell-10/ Demand for Funds/ 4838, Dy. 01.09.2023 Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored" Suvarna Jayanti Shahari Rozgar Yojana" has been restructured and converted into" National Urban Livelihoods Mission" under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana and is being implemented in the State.
The scheme is being implemented on a 60: 40 ratio between the Centre and the state. Under this mission, measures are being taken to provide employment or self-employment opportunities by providing skill training to the persons belonging to urban poor families, providing shelter facilities to urban homeless, solving the problems related to livelihood of street vendors in urban areas, reducing urban poverty by improving the living standards of urban poor families, etc.
