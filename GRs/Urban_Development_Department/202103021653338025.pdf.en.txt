The Proposal:
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) is currently executing the ambitious Mumbai Metro Rail Project, Mumbai Parbandar Project and other important projects. The post of Municipal Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority is very important from the administrative point of view and the responsibility of controlling the projects and other activities carried out by the Authority is carried out by the officers of this post. S. S. 28.02.2021 (M. No.) has retired from the Government Service as the Municipal Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, as per the prescribed age limit. Considering the experience and efficiency of Mr. R. A. Rajeev, the Government was considering to appoint him on a contract basis to the post of Municipal Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, so that his experience and efficiency would be benefited to carry out the projects being implemented by the Authority.
Government's decision
Rajiv, Shri. (1987) (Retired). For a period of 3 months from 1st March 2021 (d. As on 31st May, 2021) are being appointed as Municipal Commissioners, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority on contract basis.
The terms and conditions of the appointment on the basis of the said contract shall be as follows:
The amount to be paid to Shri Rajiv after deducting his present pension (including Dearness Allowance and Dearness Allowance) from the total salary (including basic pay and Dearness Allowance) received by him before his retirement, shall be fixed as monthly remuneration.
