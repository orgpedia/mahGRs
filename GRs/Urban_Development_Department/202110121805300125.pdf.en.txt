Reference:- General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. Mavz-2020/ Q.No.33/ Ka-34 dated 16.03.2020.
The Proposal:
The Government was considering to form a committee at the Government level to select an institution from the institutions listed in category A and B on the panel mentioned in the Government Decision dated 16.03.2020 of the Information and Public Relations Department for controlling, evaluating, analyzing and documenting the news through a third party system in the case of positive and negative news coming in the online media (print media and online TV channel only) about the Urban Development Department.
Government's decision:
In this regard, the Government is approving the constitution of the following Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Navi-2) to select the Institute through e-tendering process.
