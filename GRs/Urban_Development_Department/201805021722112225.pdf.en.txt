As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, the Kalyan-Dombivli City Sewerage (Phase-II) Project is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
4.1 Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation will be the implementing agency for the project.
4.2 The share of the local body for the project should be paid by the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation.
4.3 The implementing agency shall be bound to implement the said project as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the decision of the Government in the context of the State Government.
4.4 It will be mandatory for the concerned executing agency to complete the tender process and implementation of the project within the stipulated time as per the procedures prescribed by the State Government vide Circular No. AMRUT-2016/ PR No. 163/ NV-33, dated 16th May, 2016 and 12th July, 2016. All types of pipes should also be included in the tender for this project.
4.5 It shall be mandatory for the executing agency to tender the said project as per the model tender form issued by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for sewerage projects under AMRUT Mission.
4.6 The implementing agency shall ensure that the work orders for the said project are issued within 45 days from the date of order of administrative sanction.
4.7 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the mission city to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.
4.8. Mode of disbursement:- The Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%) as per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission. Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.

4.9 While setting up the Self-Help Fund for the projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, the Urban Local Bodies will be required to converge with the 14th Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the 14th Finance Commission.
4.10 After releasing the first instalment of Central and State share of funds for the project, the Centre
After submission of Utilization Certificate to the Central Government as prescribed by the Government, the next instalment of Central share and State share will be disbursed accordingly.
4.11 As per the financial framework of the Mission, it will be mandatory for the concerned implementing agency to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments and submit the utilization certificate of the said expenditure to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
4.12 As per the instructions issued at the time of disbursement of funds for the said project, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks allocated in accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government. The funds allocated for the project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, it will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
4.13 After the project is approved for the Urban Local Bodies by the State Government, the entire responsibility of any increase in the original project cost for any reason will be of the concerned executing agency. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government for this purpose.
4.14 It shall be the responsibility of the concerned implementing agency to complete the project within the stipulated time.
4.15 Once the project work is started, the concerned executing agency will be responsible to make the payment of the work to the concerned contractor within 15 days within the limit of available funds after the project manager consultant certifies the quality of work and payments.
4.16 As per the guidelines of AMRUT Mission, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran will act as Project Manager Consultant for the project. Accordingly, the implementing agency will pay the fee of 3 per cent to the Maharashtra Life Authority as per the procedure prescribed by the government.
4.17 Necessary decisions regarding the raising of the necessary municipal share funds for the project and the implementation of improvements under the AMRUT Mission should be taken accordingly.
18 The process of obtaining all No Objection Certificates required for the completion of the project should be done before the project is awarded.
4.19 In order to ensure sustainability of the project, necessary action should be taken with the approval of the General Assembly regarding the levy of Sewerage Tax as mentioned in the Detailed Project Report of the project.
4.20 a. m. The project work should be completed within the stipulated time to achieve reasonable achievement in accordance with the order received from the National Green Tribunal.
