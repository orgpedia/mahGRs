Reference:- Government Resolution, Urban Development Department, No. Policy-2017/ No. 96/ No. 16, dated 12th December, 2017.
Preamble: As Nagpur is the Deputy Capital of Maharashtra, a special grant of Rs. 15 crore per annum was sanctioned to the Nagpur Municipal Corporation from the approved budget for Urban Development Sub-Zone for a period of 5 years from 1995-96 to meet the development expenditure in respect of the Sub-Capital of Nagpur city. On the same lines, the government was considering sanctioning a special grant in 2018-19 for the development of Nagpur city.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to sanction a special grant-in-aid of Rs.150 crore for 2018-19 on the pattern of 1995 for the overall development of Nagpur as it is the Deputy Capital City.
The special grant will be disbursed from the approved budget of the Urban Development Department through the Municipal Infrastructure Account Head. The nature of works to be taken up under this special grant, administrative approval etc. shall be as prescribed under Government Resolution No. 2017/ PR.No. 96/ NV-16 dated 12th December, 2017. However, the condition of setting up 25 per cent share of the municipal corporation as intended in the Municipal Infrastructure Scheme will not be applicable for this special grant.
