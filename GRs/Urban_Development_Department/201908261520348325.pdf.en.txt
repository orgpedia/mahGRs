Government Resolution dated 04.09.2017.
Preamble: In pursuance of the Ease of Doing Business, the documents required for services under 4 components namely Property Tax, Water Lease, No-Objection Certificate and Business License, Fee, Periodic Limit, Designated Officer, First and Second Appellate Officer etc. have been notified under the Right to Public Service Act vide Government Order dated 04.09.2017. However, under the said Government Decision," matters relating to services under Business License have not been notified to these Municipal Councils.
The documents required for 10 services under" Business License," fees, tenure limits, Designated Officers, First and Second Appellate Officers have been notified to the Municipal Corporation under the Government Decision dated 04.09.2017. As per Section 280 of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Towns Act, 1965, services under" Business License" are also required to be provided in the jurisdiction of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats.
Government's decision:
Considering the points mentioned in the Preamble, 10 services under" Business License" are also to be notified by the" A,"" B" and" C" class Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats as per the Ease of Doing Business, as decided to notify the services of Property Tax, Water Slab, No-Objection Certificate to the Municipal Councils as on 04.09.2017.

The relevant public authorities (Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats) shall not be able to change the services, documents and periodicity mentioned in the return accompanying the decision of the Government.
The concerned public authorities will be free to fix the fee to be charged for providing services under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015. Also, the concerned public authorities can revise the posts of Designated Officer, First Appellate Officer and Second Appellate Officer etc. taking into account the composition of the concerned urban local body.
All the principals, municipal councils/ nagar panchayats in the state should take action regarding the notification and implementation of services as per Section 3 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015.
It shall be the responsibility of the concerned Principal, Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats to immediately start the implementation of the above order.
