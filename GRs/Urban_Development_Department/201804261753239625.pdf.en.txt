The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is being implemented in the State since 2015-16. Under this campaign, infrastructure facilities like water supply, sewerage, storm water drains, urban transport and greenfield development will be created in the cities.
The detailed project report of Miraj Water Supply Project, which is included in the plan for the financial year 2016-17 under AMRUT Mission, was prepared by Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation. As per the instructions of the Central Government, the project report has been approved by the State Level Technical Committee constituted under AMRUT Mission and subsequently by the State Level High Powered Committee.
The Government has accorded administrative approval to Miraj Water Supply Project on 13/12/2016 as recommended by the State Level High Powered Committee.
Resolution No. 2 of the General Assembly regarding the implementation of the Miraj Water Supply Project. 120, dated 30/11/2016, Resolution No. 33 dated 04/07/2017 and Standing Committee Meeting Resolution No. 78, dated 12/07/2017 has been passed. At the time of passing this resolution,
Since certain terms and conditions are inconsistent with the provisions prescribed by the Central Government and the Government of Maharashtra, the Government is of the opinion that the said resolution is ultra vires of the powers conferred.
Resolution No. 120, dated 30/11/2016, in part, pursuant to General Assembly Resolution No. 33, dated 04/07/2017 in full and Standing Committee Meeting Resolution No. 78, dated 12/07/2017 the matter of partial suspension was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
As certain terms and conditions of the said resolution passed by the General Body and Standing Committee of the Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation are contrary to or ultra vires the said power and in the larger public interest for the implementation of the said scheme within the stipulated period, the above mentioned resolutions are being suspended in the first instance under the provisions of Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 as follows:

The issue No. 1," If any amount is to be paid in excess of the budget, the amount shall be paid by the Government."
The issue No. 2." On the condition that the Government will pay the project management fee and other taxes."
As per Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the concerned can make a representation to the state government within one month from the date of the said decision. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further appropriate action will be taken as per Section 451 (3) of the Act.
