Preamble: A total of 1246 posts have been sanctioned for Latur City Municipal Corporation vide Government Resolution dated 28.5.2013. As per the rules, the post of" City Engineer" is 100 per cent promoted and there is a provision to fill it up on deputation till the availability of suitable officers.
Shri Navnath Kakasaheb Kendre, Municipal Council is an officer of the cadre of Engineering Service (Architecture) Grade-A and has been appointed on deputation to the Municipal Council Administration Branch, Collectorate, Parbhani vide order dated 12.1.2022. As per the request of Shri Kendre, the Commissioner and Director Municipal Administration, Mumbai vide letter dated 27.1.2022 has recommended for the post of" City Engineer" in Latur City Municipal Corporation on deputation basis.
On the request of Navnath Kakasaheb Kendre and on the recommendation received from the Commissioner and the Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai, the matter of appointing Shri Kendre on deputation to the vacant post of" City Engineer" in Latur City Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Shri Navnath Kakasaheb Kendre, Municipal Council Engineering Service (Architecture) Grade-A is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially to the vacant post of" City Engineer" in Latur City Municipal Corporation.
The said appointment of Mr. Kendre is being made by the Deputy Commissioner for a period of one year from the date of assumption of charge of the office of Deputy Commissioner as provided in Annexure-II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Automatic Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981 subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mr. Kendre shall be deputed to the vacant post of' City Engineer' in Latur City Municipal Corporation until the officer on promotion becomes available.
(b) If their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
