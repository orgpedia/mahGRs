Animal and Bird Charitable Trust and others have filed a case against the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and others. The Bombay High Court, vide Petition No. 36/2011 was filed. In this regard, Ma. The High Court has passed orders dated 8th June, 2015. In pursuance of the orders of the Hon'ble Court, a series of meetings were held under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary and Additional Chief Secretary (Home). Accordingly, a Cabinet Sub-Committee was constituted vide Government Resolution dated 09/11/2016 to comprehensively study the proposal submitted for consideration of the State Cabinet. In pursuance of the recommendations made by the said Cabinet Sub-Committee, the Home Department has issued a Government Resolution dated 16/06/2017. As per the decision, the financial assistance to be made available to the affected for the rehabilitation of horse-cart drivers and owners is to be provided in the budget of the Urban Development Department and distributed to the concerned beneficiaries through the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
The matter of transferring the financial assistance to be made available for the rehabilitation of the horse carriage drivers and owners to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation as mentioned in the relevant Government decision of the Home Department was under consideration. The following decision has been taken:

Government's decision
In pursuance of the recommendations made by the Cabinet Sub-Committee on the rehabilitation of drivers/ owners dependent on the horse carriage business in the city of Mumbai, the Home Department has issued a Government Resolution dated 16/06/2017. According to the government decision, the financial assistance to be made available to the affected for the rehabilitation of horse carriage drivers and owners will be provided in the budget of the Urban Development Department and it will be distributed to the concerned beneficiary through the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation. The transfer of funds amounting to Rs. 5,96,70, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Ninety Six Lakhs Seventy Thousand only) to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation is being approved by the Government on the basis of the following terms and conditions.
(1) The funds so made available shall not be used for any purpose other than the purpose for which they are made available.
2) The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation shall be solely responsible for providing financial assistance, determining eligibility/ disqualification, providing street vendor certificate, and for court cases arising out of other ancillary matters.
3) Ensure that the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation appoints a responsible officer and takes immediate action in the case presented.
4) The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation shall ensure that the proceedings are carried out in accordance with the terms, conditions and rules mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 16/06/2017 of the Home Department.
The process of disbursement of financial assistance to the affected should be completed within 6 months.
Also, after ensuring that financial assistance reaches all the affected, if the funds remain, the remaining funds should be deposited with the government immediately.
6) The detailed details of the financial assistance distributed to the victims and other action taken should be submitted to the Urban Development Department and Home Department of the Government immediately.
The Office of the Commissioner (Navi-5), Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said amount. The Deputy Secretary (Navi-5), Urban Development Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer.
