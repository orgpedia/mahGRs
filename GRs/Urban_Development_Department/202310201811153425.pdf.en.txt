The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of India has launched Swachh Bharat Mission Urban 2.0 with a view to sustaining the" Swachh Bharat Mission" and making the entire urban India garbage free. The Mission will be implemented from 2 nd October, 2021 to 1 st October, 2026.
All urban local bodies are required to complete all sanctioned solid waste management projects and sewerage related projects under Swachh Bharat Mission-20 within the prescribed time frame and in a proper manner. For this purpose, guidelines have been issued to the Urban Local Bodies through circular No. 3 in the above context for expeditious and efficient implementation of these projects.
3. Paragraph No. 3 of the Circular under reference. In this guideline, 16 project management consultants prepared by the Maharashtra Life Authority under the letter dated April 8, 2021, have been accepted by the department.
2.Now the No. 4 In view of the request made by the following eight Project Management Consultants in the NAMICA list prepared by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the letter, the Department is appointing the following eight Project Management Consultants as Project Management Consultants for the implementation of Sewerage Projects under the Centrally Sponsored Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0.
