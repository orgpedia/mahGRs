Preamble: - The Central Government has started the implementation of "Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, hygienic and livable and to provide clean and good health and hygienic environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation facilities to all the constituent cities of the country and household toilets to the citizens of these cities. On the lines of this mission, "Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0" is being implemented in the state under the Government Decision No. 1 in the context.
The Central Government has issued Reference No.2 for Capacity Building, Skill Development and Knowledge Management component under Swachh Maharashtra Mission 2.0. 12 vide order dated 13.02.2026, the Central Government has disbursed to the State through Mother Sanction an amount of Rs. 18,10,06, 937 / - (Rupees eighteen crore ten lakh six thousand nine hundred and thirty-three only) as per allowable share. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse the funds to the Directorate of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan under the SNA SPARSH system.
Government Decision: As per the letter from the Central Government in Reference 12, the Government is approving the allocation of funds for capacity building, skill development and knowledge management components under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 and accordingly the permissible share of the State Government as follows.
