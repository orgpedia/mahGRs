The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is being implemented in the State since 2015-16 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Under this campaign, the construction of water supply, sewerage, storm water drains, urban transport and green area development will be done in the cities.
Accordingly, the Central Government has approved the State Annual Action Plan for the financial year 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 under this Mission, which includes the Green Area Development Project of Nashik city. Now the matter of implementing an additional green area development project for Nashik city was under consideration.
The detailed project report of the green area development project included in the State Annual Action Plan under AMRUT Mission was prepared by Nashik Municipal Corporation. The Chief Engineer, Nashik Municipal Corporation has given technical approval to this project. As per the instructions of the Central Government, the State Level High Powered Committee has authorized the State Level Technical Committee to approve the DPRs of Greenfield Development Projects as per the reference No. 4 meeting. Accordingly, the project report has been approved in the 5th meeting of the State Level Technical Committee constituted under AMRUT Mission. In accordance with the decision of the Government No. 1 under reference, administrative approval is being given to the Green Area Development Project of Nashik City subject to the following conditions:
4.1 Nashik Municipal Corporation will be the implementing agency for the project.
4.2 The implementing agency shall be bound to implement the project as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the Government Decision No. 1 and Government Circulars No. 2 and 3 in the context of the State Government.
4.3 It shall be incumbent upon the concerned implementing agency to complete the implementation of the said project within the prescribed time frame as per the procedure prescribed in paragraph 3 of the reference.
__ 4.4 The implementing agency shall ensure that the tender process for the project is completed and work orders are issued immediately.
4.5 Mission cities and implementing agencies shall be bound to fulfill the conditions mentioned in the minutes of the State Level Technical Committee meetings.
4.6 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the Mission City to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.

4.7 Methodology of Fund Distribution: As per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, the Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%).
Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.
4.8 The first installment of funds for the project from the Central and State Government will be distributed to the Municipal Corporation after the work order is issued. Accordingly, after submission of Utilization Certificate to the Central Government as prescribed by the Central Government, the next instalment of Central share and State share will be disbursed accordingly.
4.9 While raising self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) will be required to converge with the 14th Finance Commission Grants/ Goods and Services Tax Compensation received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the 14th Finance Commission/ Goods and Services Tax Compensation Subsidy.
4.10 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
4.11 As per the instructions issued while disbursing the funds for the said project, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks allocated as per the prevailing policy of the Government. The funds allocated for the project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, it will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
4.12 After the project is approved by the State Government for the Urban Local Bodies, any increase in the original project cost for any reason shall be the sole responsibility of the concerned executing agency. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government for this purpose.
4.13 The concerned implementing agency shall be fully responsible for completing the project within the stipulated time.
4.14 After the commencement of the project, it shall be the responsibility of the concerned executing agency to make payment of the work to the concerned contractor within 15 days within the limit of available funds after the project manager consultant certifies the quality of work and payment.
4.15 Mission cities should develop a mechanism for meeting the maintenance and repair cost of greenfield development projects.
4.16 It shall be incumbent upon the concerned implementing agency to implement the green field development projects under the guidance of the consultants assigned to you.
