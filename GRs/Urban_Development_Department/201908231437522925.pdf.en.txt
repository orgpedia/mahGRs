Introduction: The Urban Dalit Habitat Improvement Scheme is implemented since 1996 for the welfare of the special category population in the urban areas of the state. The Urban Dalit Habitat Improvement Scheme (UDBY) was implemented by superseding the Government Decisions issued prior to 1996. A revised Government Resolution was issued and the guidelines of the scheme were prescribed by handing over the control of the scheme to the District Collector. Reference No. 8 As per the decision of the government here, the name of this scheme was" Lokhandir Anna Bhai Sathe Urban Habitat Improvement Scheme." However, the government is considering changing the name of the scheme.
