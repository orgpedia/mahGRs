Date: 30th of December 2020
Read:- Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation's letter No. 424/ Establishment, dated 20/02/2020
Preamble: As per the proposal sent by the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation in a letter, the selection list and waiting list for the post of Assistant Commissioner/ Ward Officer (AY) to be recruited directly on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation has been finalized by conducting interviews by a committee constituted to conduct interviews as per Section 54 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. Thereafter, with a view to approve the merit-wise selection list and waiting list and since the said post is of Class-01 status, the appointment authority for this post as per Section 53 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act is the" Mahasabha" of the Municipal Corporation, the proposal for appointment of the above candidates would have been considered by the House in its meeting dated 20/06/2019. On the basis of the suggestions made by the members, after taking legal opinion, the re-proposal was placed before the General Assembly. Based on the suggestion made by the members," a committee of 5 members under the chairmanship of the Mayor should be appointed and interview should be conducted by that committee and the new Assistant Commissioner should be appointed as per the decision of that committee. " Decision No. 29 dated 20/01/2020 passed.
In this case, as per Section 54 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the selection list and waiting list have been decided by conducting interviews by the constituted committee for conducting interviews. Therefore, the Nagpur Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 29 dated 20/01/2020, the reconstitution of the Committee of Members headed by the Mayor is inconsistent with the provision of Section 54 (1) of the Municipal Act.
In view of the aforesaid facts, since the resolution passed by the General Body of the Nagpur Municipal Corporation is inconsistent with the provision of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the said resolution as per the provision of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, No. 29 dated 20/01/2020 should be cancelled, stated the letter from the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation.
In view of the above facts, in accordance with the administrative discipline of the Municipal Corporation, a proposal to take a decision on the proposal sent to the Government was under consideration at the Government level. Following this, the government took decision:

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: NAMPA-2020/ RESOLUTION/ P.No.22/ NO.26
Government Resolution: No. 29 dated 20/01/2020 is deemed to be temporarily suspended under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
However, as per Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, an opportunity is being given to the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation as well as the Municipal Corporation within one month from the date of the order. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further action will be taken as per Section 451 (3) of the Act.
