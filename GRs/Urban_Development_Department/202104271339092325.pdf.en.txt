Preamble: Subsidy is distributed to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For this purpose, out of the budgetary allocation of₹ 2050 crore for the financial year 2021-22, a sum of₹ 174 crore has been approved.
As per the above, the said grants are to be disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Administration through BEAMS system.
Government Decision: Subsidy is given to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For the year 2021-22, out of the budgeted funds for this purpose,₹ 174 crore received through the distribution system is being disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Council Administration for disbursement to the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats/ Municipal Corporations.
2. Subsidiary Grants received through BEAMS system for Municipal Councils. As per the Government decision dated 16.06.2017, the Municipal Council Administration Directorate should directly deposit the amount in the bank account of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat.
3. The Joint Commissioner and Joint Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration are being declared as Controlling Officers and Assistant Directors as Drawing and Disbursing Officers for disbursement of Subsidiary Grants to Municipalities directly into the bank accounts of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats by the Directorate of Municipal Administration.
