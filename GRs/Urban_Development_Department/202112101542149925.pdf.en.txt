Date: 10th of December 2021.
The proposal: As per the decision taken in the meeting chaired by the Chief Secretary, the income tax levied on mobile towers in different municipal corporations and municipal councils is different, the government was considering the matter of appointing a committee at the government level to uniform/ rationalize the income tax and what should be the procedure for authorizing unauthorized mobile towers in the state and how long to complete the process.
Government Decision: It is being approved to constitute a committee as follows to suggest necessary measures to rationalize the property tax levied on mobile towers in the state as well as to determine the procedure for authorizing unauthorized mobile towers and to prescribe the time period for this.
