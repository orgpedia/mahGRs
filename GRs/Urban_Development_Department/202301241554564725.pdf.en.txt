The Proposal:
The Commissioner of Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation had submitted a proposal to the government through a letter of reference to approve a loan bond of Rs 200 crore for various development projects related to the revival of Pavana and Indrayani rivers in Pimpri Chinchwad city. It was for the government to approve it.
Government's decision:
The project will be implemented in connection with the revival of Pavana and Indrayani rivers of Pimpri Chinchwad city. The estimated cost of the project is Rs 2757 crore.
Accordingly, the standing committee of the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and the municipal assembly have approved a budget of Rs 1557 crore for the Pavana river project and Rs 1200 crore for the Indrayani river project. Accordingly, the approval of the State Government is being given under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act to raise bonds up to Rs 200 crore in the initial phase in connection with the said project.
The responsibility of repaying the loan amount and interest on it raised through bonds will be completely with the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation. There is no guarantee from the government.
2. For repayment of bonds, the Municipal Corporation should open an escrow account in a nationalised bank and ensure that repayment is made on time. There is no help from the government.
