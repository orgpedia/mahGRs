The Proposal:
As per the order dated 01.03.2021, under the 15th Finance Commission, for Non-Million Plus Cities, Rs. 305 crore has been received as 2nd instalment of Basic Subsidy for 2020-21. The grant was to be distributed to the urban local bodies in the state.
Government's decision:
The second instalment of Basic Grant to Non-Million Plus Cities for the financial year 2020-2021 released by the Central Government under the Reference Order on the recommendation of the 15th Central Finance Commission is Rs. 305 crore is being approved for distribution to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in Non-Million Plus Cities group and Cuttack Mandal on the basis of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
2) Rs. 305.00 crore is being handed over to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the Urban Local Bodies as per Annexure. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration shall take immediate action to disburse their share of funds to the respective Urban Local Bodies through RTGS as per the annexure.
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai, shall immediately submit to the Government an appropriation certificate in the prescribed format of the 15th Finance Commission grant received from the Central Government for the financial year 2020-2021. As per the provisions of the Government Decision, the Government will issue while spending from this subsidy.
