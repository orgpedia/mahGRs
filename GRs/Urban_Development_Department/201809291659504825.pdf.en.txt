4. In view of the above, the Standing Committee of the Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 42, d. 29/05/2017 was under consideration of the Government to suspend the resolution as the Government was of the view that it was against the larger public interest.
Government's decision:
In view of the facts mentioned in the above-mentioned Preamble, the Standing Committee of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation vide its Resolution No. 42, d. 29.05.2017 is against the larger public interest. Therefore, this resolution is being suspended in the first instance to dissolve it under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
(2) If the resolution is to be submitted to the concerned for suspension, it must be submitted to the Government within 30 days from the date of the suspension order.
