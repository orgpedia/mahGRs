For the Navi Mumbai project, the State Government has constituted CIDCO Corporation as the Planning Authority under Section 113 of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966. To discuss various issues related to the development of land distributed by Cidco Corporation on lease in and around Navi Mumbai and to decide on the problems related to the business through relevant government decisions.
A committee has been formed. The committee has been asked to submit its report.
Government's decision:
1. Cidco Corporation As informed vide letter dated 28.04.2023, the report to be prepared by the Committee is at the final stage and will take some time for analysis. The last date is being extended till 31.05.2023.
The Committee is required to submit its report within the aforesaid time frame and no further extension of time shall be granted.
