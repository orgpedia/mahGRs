Introduction: The above reference No. 1 On the letter dated 12.7.2022 received from Shri Prataprao Chikhlikar, Member of Lok Sabha, Shri. In pursuance of the directions of the Deputy Chief Minister, the following works were suspended under the relevant Government Order dated 7.9.2022.
(i) Suspension of the agreement entered into by the Municipal Corporation with the developer regarding the Mahatma Phule Commercial Complex.
(ii) Suspension of tender, approval of Janata Market Shivajinagar Commercial Complex.
However, in view of the financial condition of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation, it will not be possible for the corporation to build the commercial complex through self-funding, so the general meeting of the corporation has approved to take up the project on BOT/ PPP basis. Accordingly, the Municipal Corporation is taking action as per the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act and is strictly following the rules in the entire process, so the Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation has requested to lift the stay granted by the Government immediately vide letter dated 9.9.2022. Therefore, the matter was under consideration of the government.
