Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk

The date: March 9, 2018.
The Proposal:
It was under consideration of the Government to take a notional decision to bear 50% of the total expenditure of the State Government on the implementation of Communication Base Train Control (CBTC) system on the suburban railway lines of Churchgate-Virar (Dhima Marg) and CST-Panvel in the ratio of 15:15:15: 5 by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA), the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai and the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
The State Government will bear 50% of the total cost of implementation of Communication Base Train Control (CBTC) system on Churchgate-Virar (Dhima Marg) and CST-Panvel suburban railway lines in Mumbai.
