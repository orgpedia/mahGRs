Government's decision:
Detailed instructions and timelines regarding action to be taken in respect of funds allocated to Urban Local Bodies under various schemes by the Urban Development Department for non-utilization within the prescribed time frame have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 12th March, 2018. This includes allocations made by the Urban Development Department to Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State during the year 2013-14 and earlier.
The Government has been advised to deposit the funds lying unutilized immediately and in any case before 17.03.2018.
During the review, it was observed that most of the Urban Local Bodies are yet to refund such unpaid amounts. For this, they have pointed out the ambiguity in the accounting of the unaccounted deposits. The necessary orders in this regard have been issued by the Government Circular Finance Department No. BGT-2015/ PROCESS. Budget as on March 4, 2015
