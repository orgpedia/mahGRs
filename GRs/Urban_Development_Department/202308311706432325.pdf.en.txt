2. Terms and Conditions for construction of Aspirational Toilets mentioned above:
2.1 The implementing agency for the said projects shall be the respective Urban Local Bodies.
2.2) The work of the above-approved project should be carried out immediately through e-tendering process.
2.3) All the relevant rules to be followed while implementing the e-tendering process.
It will be mandatory for the institution to adopt the model tendering process and procedures prescribed by the central government.
2.4) It should be ensured that the expenditure incurred on the work under the said project does not violate the Model Code of Conduct.
