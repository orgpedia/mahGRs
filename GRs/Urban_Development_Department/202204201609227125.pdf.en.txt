The Proposal:
The Director, Town Planning and Valuation, Pune, has issued the following orders: In pursuance of the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 09.09.2021, the matter of shifting the office of Development Plan Special Unit, Karad Municipal Council was under consideration of the Government as the office of Development Plan Special Unit, Karad Municipal Council has ceased to function. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
In view of the geographical location of Dhule city, the total area and the speed/ scale of development of Dhule city, considering the scope and importance of the work of revising the development plan for the said Dhule Municipal Corporation area, and also because the development plan special unit, Karad Municipal Council Office has ceased to function, it is hereby approved to transfer by nomination as follows:
