Mr. Pandurang Rakhmaji Shinde, Audit Officer, Local Fund Audit (Municipal Corporation Lt. ) Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai is being appointed as "Accounts Officer" in the vacant post of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation on deputation for a period of 9 years.
Mr. Pandurang Rakhmaji Shinde, is being deputed for a period of 1 year from the date he assumes charge of the office of the deputation subject to the following conditions: - a) If his services are not deemed necessary by the non-individual employer, the non-individual employer shall have the right to return his services.
b) They will be allowed to return to the original department after giving at least 3 months' written notice to the government that they intend to return to the original department.
3. The Finance Department should take necessary action for the discharge of Mr. Pandurang Shinde for joining the post of deputation.
4. Mr. Pandurang Shinde to join his deputation and submit a compliance report to the Government. The deputation is being made subject to the terms and conditions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, Non-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
