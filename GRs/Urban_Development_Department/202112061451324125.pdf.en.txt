As the regular posts of safai karamcharis have not been approved in the newly created municipal councils and nagar panchayats, the sanitation workers working in the gram panchayats have not been included. There are about 1477 Safai Karamcharis working in various newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats. As per the existing policy of the Government regarding the inclusion of the then Gram Panchayat employees in the newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, the proposal for the inclusion of the Safai Karamcharis in the then Gram Panchayats, which were functioning since before the initial announcement of the formation of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the posts of Class-3 and Class-4, which were sanctioned and vacant even after the completion of the process of inclusion, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Inclusion of Safai Karamcharis of the then Gram Panchayats working since the initial announcement of the formation of the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the posts of Class-III and Class-IV sanctioned and vacant despite completion of the process of inclusion as per the policy laid down under the relevant Government decisions in the newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats is being approved as follows: (a) Inclusion of the then Gram Panchayat employees in the posts in the approved diagram of the newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, after following the procedure laid down under the Government Decision and accordingly, the vacant and available posts of Class-III and Class-IV which remain vacant despite inclusion, in the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats.

It is being approved to induct willing Safai Karamcharis holding educational and technical qualifications in the existing Gram Panchayats functioning from the date of initial announcement of the Nagar Panchayats.
(b) Sanitation workers who have not completed the technical qualification prescribed for the posts of Class-III and Class-IV are being approved for inclusion in the sanctioned and vacant posts of Class-III and Class-IV after completing 6 months training on the technical qualification required for the respective post in the Maharashtra Environmental Engineering Training and Research Institute (MITRA), Nashik.
(2) Such inclusion shall be permissible subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The Government may take a decision regarding the inclusion of former Gram Panchayat employees in the newly created Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats. The policy terms and conditions laid down under the Consequential Government Decisions dated 19.01.2017 and thereafter shall continue to apply.
(b) the stages and process of inclusion previously set out under the Government Policy.
(c) The inclusion should be made according to the seniority of the sanitation workers.
(d) The procedure for such inclusion shall be carried out in the order in which the vacancies in the approved figure are taken into account, in accordance with the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, the concerned district and thereafter the vacancies in the department.
