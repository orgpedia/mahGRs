GOVERNMENT Circular No. 2023/ No. 18/ No. 29 (3) The mandate of the Study Group will be as follows:
(1) Re-engineering the business process to simplify and streamline the process to provide the notified services to the citizens more easily.
(2) Creation of Common Application Form.
(3) Developing procedures for attaching services to Digi-locker.
(4) To fix the time limit for maintaining records in respect of notified services.
(4) The time limit for the submission of the report:
The committee will submit its report to the government in three working days.
(4) The study group may hold meetings as needed.
