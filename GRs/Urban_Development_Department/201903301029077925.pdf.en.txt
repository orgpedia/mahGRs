The Proposal:
The Mumbai Urban Transport Project is a joint initiative of the Government of India and the Government of Maharashtra to improve transport and transportation in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region. This project includes the railway project as well as the rehabilitation of the people affected by this project.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) has been appointed as the nodal agency for the Mumbai Urban Transport Project. 5,300 crore. The work under the Mumbai Urban Transport Project-IIA has been taken up with financial assistance from the World Bank. The World Bank has allocated Rs. 1,910 crore.

The Mumbai Railway Development Corporation (MRDC), the implementing agency for the project, pays 50% of the cost to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) on behalf of the Government of Maharashtra. The Mumbai Railway Development Corporation submits the reimbursement claims of the expenses incurred to the World Bank. After the World Bank approves the reimbursement claims, the same amount is disbursed by the World Bank to the Central Government. Out of this, the amount accounted by the State Government is disbursed under the head of regular accounts for book adjustment of expenditure under the head of suspension of the State Government and the cash provided by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority on behalf of the State Government to the Mumbai Railway Development Corporation is disbursed by the Government of Maharashtra to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
The Central Government has so far released Rs. 864,53,25, 500/- has been disbursed to the State Government and an amount of Rs. 575,26,78, 500/- has been disbursed by the State Government till date.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) has informed the Government under the above reference letter that an amount of Rs. 133,05,70, 000/- is to be disbursed in cash to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) from the amount of additional loan assistance disbursed by the Central Government to the State Government. Against this backdrop, the Government was considering to sanction an amount of Rs. 133,05,50, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Thirty Three Crores Five Lakh Fifty Thousand only) to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority in cash within the limit of the sanctioned budget provision for the year 2018-19. The following decisions are being taken:
Government's decision
Mumbai Urban Transport Project (MUTP-IIA LOAN NO. The Government of India has approved the release of additional Central Assistance of Rs. 133,05,50, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Thirty Three Crores Five Lakh Fifty Thousand only) in cash to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) from the Budget Estimates 2018-19.
The above amount should be met from the budgetary provision available in the financial year 2018-19 by spending under the head" Main Account Head-2217-Demand No.F-2, 80, General, 191 Assistance to Municipal Corporations, Centrally Sponsored Schemes Special Programme for Development of Mumbai (00) (37) Additional Central Assistance for Mumbai Urban Transport Project (2217 1241), 31 Subsidiary Grants,-Non-salary."
