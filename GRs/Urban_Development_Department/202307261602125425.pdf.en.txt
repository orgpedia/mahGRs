Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, dated 28.04.2015 Preamble: In order to provide transparent, efficient and timely service to the eligible persons of the state and with a view to providing prompt and quality service to the people in view of the growing expectations from the local administration, the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was passed. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28.04.2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority shall, within a period of three months from the date of promulgation of the Ordinance and from time to time thereafter, notify the public servants, designated officers, first and second Appellate Authorities and the time limits prescribed by it under the Ordinance. Accordingly, the matter of notifying the time limit, designated officers, first and second appellate officers for the services of Kolhapur Urban Area Development Authority was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. The Government hereby approve the notification of Schedule-A services in Kolhapur Urban Area Development Authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015. The details of the duration of public service, office to which the service is notified as well as the designated officer, first appellate officer, and second appellate officer for the services provided by the Kolhapur Urban Area Development Authority under the Urban Development Department are mentioned in Schedule-A. Accordingly, the Kolhapur Urban Area Development Authority should take action to notify this service in the government gazette.
(2) The designated officer responsible for providing services mentioned in Schedule' A', the First and Second Appellate Officer shall take action to provide services to the citizens within the stipulated time.
