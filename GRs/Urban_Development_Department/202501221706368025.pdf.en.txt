Instructions regarding the implementation of the scheme are as follows.
a) Project Implementation Mechanism and Implementation: 1) Projects under the scheme should be implemented by the respective Municipal Councils / Nagar Panchayats / Municipal Corporations.
(2) In the execution of these projects, it shall be incumbent upon the implementing agency to follow the guidelines in this regard of the Central Government and the State Government.
3) It shall be the responsibility of the Principal / Administrator concerned to re-verify that the project work under the scheme has not been carried out under any other scheme. In the event of non-compliance, the concerned civic body will be required to return the funds sanctioned for such a project, along with the interest thereon, to the government.
4) In case of any increase in the original cost of the project under the scheme, the entire responsibility shall lie with the concerned Urban Local Body. It is to be noted from all the concerned operational agencies that no additional expenditure will be made available for the same either through the State Government or from the Central Government funds.
5) It shall be the responsibility of the implementing agency to ensure that there is no duplication of funds from the Central Government for the projects sanctioned out of this fund.
6) The progress of the work should be maintained as indicated in the tender. The management will be responsible for processing the payment as per the availability of funds.
7) The responsibility of GI for the same purposes as the funds available under the scheme and the quality of works as per the standards issued by the Government from time to time shall lie with the implementing agency.
8) It shall be the responsibility of the implementing agency to maintain a Geotag photo of the pre-commissioning status, various stages of work and post-completion status and submit it on demand.
9) The funds under the scheme shall be spent in accordance with the procedure prescribed, adhering to all financial laws and regulations as well as the procedures prescribed. It shall be the responsibility of the implementing agency to comply with the instructions of the government decisions issued by the government from time to time in this regard.
10) The Principal / Administrator / Commissioner concerned should immediately submit the details of utilization certificate B expenditure in the prescribed form to the Director, Town Planning, Maharashtra State, Pune after spending the funds for the project under the scheme. Also, a copy of the same should be sent immediately to the Director, Directorate of Municipal Councils, Navi Mumbai and the operational machinery will be responsible in respect of malpractices / accounting objections, Public Accounts Committee, Legislative business, etc. under the scheme.
11) The action to register the properties arising out of the projects under the scheme in the Real Estate Registry should be taken by the Commissioner / Principal concerned.
