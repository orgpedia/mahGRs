The Proposal:
Subsidy is distributed to the municipal councils in lieu of the abolition of VAT and dearness allowance of the municipal councils in the state. For this purpose, the Finance Department has approved a revised estimate of Rs. 1999.52 crore out of the budgeted amount of Rs. 2024.52 crore for the year 2020-2021. Out of this amount, the Department of Finance had made available funds to the tune of Rs. 1822.06 crore on BEAMS system. Reference No. Out of this, a sum of Rs. 1811.84 crore has been approved for disbursement to the Directorate of Municipal Administration. Of this, Rs. 10.22 crore is yet to be released. Further, Rs. 177.45 crore has been released by the Department of Finance.
As per the above, the balance grants received and disbursed are to be distributed to the Directorate of Municipal Administration through BEAMS system.
Government Decision: Subsidy is given to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For the year 2020-21, out of the budgetary allocation for this purpose, a total of Rs. 187.67 crore is being released to the Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement of Auxiliary Grants to the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats/ Municipal Corporations, out of the total amount of Rs. 177.45 crore received through the disbursement mechanism and Rs. 10.22 crore released earlier.
