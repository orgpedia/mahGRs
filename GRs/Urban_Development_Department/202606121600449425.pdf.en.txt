The Proposal: -
The General Administration Department studied the admission rules of all categories of various administrative departments to greatly improve the recruitment process, simplify the recruitment process, and provide transparent, forward-looking, and citizen-centric manpower to meet the goal of "Minimum Government and Maximum Governance." It has declared PHT as "dead cadre," which is administratively unnecessary in view of the increased use of IT systems in administrative work, in government affairs, the expiry of duties and responsibilities of certain categories in the context of administrative TAT, modern technology, and has directed the administrative departments to take necessary action in this regard. Accordingly, the issue of declaring the Short-Literate Cadre of the various Zonal Offices at the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation as a dead cadre was under consideration.
The Government's Decisions:
Revised profile of the cadres in the Subordinate Zonal Office on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation. Approved on 10.04.2023.
The cadre of "Personal Assistant, Miniature Writer (Upper Class) B Miniature Writer (Lower Class)" at the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation included in this figure is being declared as "dead cadre."
Service benefits of officers and staff working in the cadre of "Personal Assistant, Miniature Writer (Upper Class) B Miniature Writer (Lower Class)" at the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation shall remain intact.
