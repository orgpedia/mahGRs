Introduction: The 15th Finance Commission has been constituted for the period 2021-22 to 2025-26. 2 Under the 15th Finance Commission Order dated 01.06.2023, Rs. 191.00 crore has been received as second installment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2022-23 and it was under consideration of the Government to disburse the same to the Urban Local Bodies in the State.
Government Decision: As per the recommendation of the 15th Central Finance Commission, the Central Government has decided to implement the 2 The second installment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2022-2023 released under the order is Rs. 191.00 crore to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in Non-Million Plus Cities group and Cuttack Mandal on the basis of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
2) Rs. 191.00 crore is being handed over to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the Urban Local Bodies as per Annexure. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration should immediately disburse their share of funds to the respective Urban Local Bodies through the PEMS system as per the annexure.
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai shall immediately submit to the Government the utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the amount of 15th Finance Commission Untied/ Basic Grants received from the Central Government for the financial year 2023-2024. While spending from this subsidy, it will be mandatory to do as per the provisions of the government decision issued by the government as well as the instructions given from time to time. The grant is being distributed as" conditional."
