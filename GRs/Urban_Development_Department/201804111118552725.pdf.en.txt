The Proposal:
Commissioner Pimpri-Chinchwad, Municipal Corporation, Pimpri, Pune. As per the letter dated 23.06.2015, to revise (for the second time) the approved development plan of Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad, Nawanagar Development Authority areas, the development plan of 18 temporary posts under the Deputy Director, Town Planning has been requested to be established under the name of" Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Nawanagar Development Authority Unit." In this regard, the Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Unit, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation, Aurangabad office along with the staff working there should be shifted to Pimpri-Chinchwad, Municipal Corporation, Pimpri, Pune for revising the development plan approved by the Pimpri-Chinchwad Development Authority. 30.04.2016 and D. Necessary recommendations have been made vide letter dated 30.11.2017. Considering this request, the Government has issued a Government Resolution dated 28th March, 2018. Since it is necessary to revise (for the second time) the development plan of both Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority by the special unit to be relocated, the Government has decided to do so by superseding the Government Decision (Refer No. 4) dated March 28, 2018.
