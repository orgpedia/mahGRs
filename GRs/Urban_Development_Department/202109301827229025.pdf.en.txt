Introduction: As the population of Parbhani city is more than 3 lakh as per the 2011 census, the Parbhani Municipal Council was converted into Parbhani City Municipal Corporation. As the Municipal Corporation needs officers and employees to implement various development works and schemes in Parbhani City Municipal Corporation, it had invited applications for the recruitment of contractual engineers on a contractual basis/ fixed remuneration for 11 months on a temporary basis. The engineers were appointed temporarily by the Municipal Corporation after receiving applications, checking educational qualifications and interviewing eligible candidates.

The Government is approving regular induction of 16 officers/ employees of the following 4 cadres in the sanctioned and vacant posts in the Municipal Corporation in the following manner (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview) on remuneration/ contract basis as mentioned in the proposal dated 5.5.2020 in the context of Parbhani City Municipal Corporation.

Regular induction of the above officers on the establishment of Parbhani City Municipal Corporation is being done subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The said officer should be inducted into the service of the Municipal Corporation from the date of the decision of the Government.
(2) The regular service of the officers inducted shall be deemed to have been rendered for all purposes from the date of their induction.
(3) The benefits of seniority, pension, etc. shall be admissible to them from the date of regular service.
(4) No arrears of salary, allowances and other incidental financial benefits for the period of service preceding the date of incorporation shall be admissible to them.
