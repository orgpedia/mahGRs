According to the report submitted by the two-member committee of Shri Krishna and Shri B.N. As per the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 18.11.2018. The Government decision has been issued on 1.8.2019. As per the Cabinet decision, it is necessary to create a new head of account to deposit the funds available to the government from various purposes in the Consolidated Fund. Accordingly, the issue of creating a new account head was under consideration of the government.
The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the report submitted by the two-member committee on the lands and other matters exempted under section 20 of the principal Act, taking into account the provisions of the Urban Land (Ceiling on Holdings and Regulation) Repeal Act, 1999, As per the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 18.11.2018, the Government is approving the creation of new head of accounts as per the following head of accounts, sub-head of accounts, sub-head of accounts and objectives to deposit the funds available to the Government from various purposes in the Consolidated Fund.
