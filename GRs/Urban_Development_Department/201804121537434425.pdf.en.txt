The Proposal:
The Navi Mumbai International Airport Limited (NMIAL), a public-private partnership (PPP) company, has won the bid for the project. have been provided as per the Government Decision dated 6.11.2017. The work on the Navi Mumbai International Airport project is also in progress. However, to complete the work of the airport within the stipulated time frame, coordination between the concerned departments of the Central and State Governments is required in the field of security issues, power supply, water supply, rail/ metro/ road connectivity to the airport, traffic control, etc. In view of this, Navi Mumbai International Airport has decided to coordinate with all the concerned departments involved in the process. In the meeting dated 24.01.2018, it has been suggested to constitute a Task Force under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a task force for the successful implementation of the Navi Mumbai International Project.
Government's decision:
Task Force is being constituted for speedy and successful implementation of the Navi Mumbai International Airport Project as under:

The Task Force constituted for implementation of the Navi Mumbai International Airport Project will have the following functions:
Coordinate with all departments on various aspects for implementation of Navi Mumbai International Airport project in a time bound manner.
(b) To take steps for timely completion of all ongoing projects related to road, rail, water supply, power supply, metro and Mumbai Trans-Harbour project in connection with connectivity to Navi Mumbai International Airport.
Obtaining necessary licenses from Government of Maharashtra/ Subsidiary offices for development of Navi Mumbai International Airport.
