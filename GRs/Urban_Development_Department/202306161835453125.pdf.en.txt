Government Decision: Subsidy is given to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For the year 2023-2024, out of the budgeted funds for this purpose, Rs. 727.768 crore received through the distribution system is being disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats/ Municipal Corporations.
2. Subsidiary Grants received through BEAMS system for Municipal Councils. As per the Government decision dated 16.06.2017, the Municipal Council Administration Directorate should directly deposit the amount in the bank account of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat.
3. The Joint Commissioner, Directorate of Municipal Administration is being declared as the Controlling Officer and Assistant Commissioner (Accounts), Directorate of Municipal Administration as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of Subsidiary Grants to Municipalities/ Town Panchayats directly into their bank accounts.
4. Circular to the Government, Finance Department, while disbursing the said funds. The funds should be disbursed to the respective Municipal Councils only after fulfilling the conditions as on 02.04.2018 and on submission of 75% Utilization Certificate.
