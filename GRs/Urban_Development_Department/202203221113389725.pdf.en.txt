The Proposal:
The 15th Finance Commission has been constituted for the period 2021-22 to 2025-26. 2 Under the 15th Finance Commission Order dated 17.03.2022, Rs. 184.40 crore has been received as 2nd instalment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2021-22. The grant was to be distributed to the urban local bodies in the state.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission, Govt. 2 The second installment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2021-2022 released under the order is Rs. 184.40 crore is being approved for distribution to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in Non-Million Plus Cities group and Cuttack Mandal on the basis of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
2) Rs. An amount of Rs. 184.40 crore is being handed over to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the Urban Local Bodies as per Annexure. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration should immediately disburse the amount to the concerned Urban Local Bodies through PFMS system as per the annexure.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FFC-2022/ No.24/ NO.4, 3. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai shall forthwith submit to the Government the utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the amount of 15th Finance Commission Untied/ Basic Grants received from the Central Government for the financial year 2021-2022. While spending from this subsidy, it will be mandatory to do as per the provisions of the government decision issued by the government as well as the instructions given from time to time. The grant is being distributed as" conditional."
As per the recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission, the second instalment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2021-2022 is being disbursed to the D category Municipal Corporations, Municipalities and Nagar Panchayats as well as Cuttack Mandal as mentioned above and the expenditure is being met from Demand No. F-2, 2217 Urban Development, 05, Other Urban Development Schemes, 191, Assistance to Urban Local Bodies, Urban Development Authorities, Urban Development Boards etc., (00) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Urban Local Bodies (Compulsory) as per the recommendations of the Fifteenth Finance Commission, (2217 A314) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried).
The Joint Commissioner, Directorate of Municipal Administration is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the concerned Assistant Commissioner (Accounts), Directorate of Municipal Administration as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the purpose of collection and disbursement of the said funds.
6. The said Government Decision is a non-official reference of Finance Department No. 21/ Finance Commission Cell, dated 21/03/2022 is being issued with the concurrence received.
