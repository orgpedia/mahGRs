The Proposal:
The Director, Town Planning and Valuation, Pune, has issued the following orders: As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 07.01.2022, the functions of Town Planner, Development Plan, Special Cell, Beed Office have been terminated and the matter of shifting of Town Planner, Development Plan Special Cell, Beed Office was under consideration of the Government. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
Considering the geographical location of Latur city, the total area and the speed/ scale of development of Latur city, the scope and importance of the work of revising the development plan for the said Latur City Municipal Corporation area, as well as the development plan special unit, the Beed office has ceased to function, it is proposed to be transferred by nomination as follows:
