The Proposal:
Implementation of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 in the State under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) of Government of India vide Reference No. It has been launched as per the Government decision dated 15 th July, 2022. The key components of the Mission are sustainable solid waste management, sustainable sanitation, treatment of stagnant water, wastewater treatment and legacy waste bioremediation.
Under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0, it is necessary to dispose of the old accumulated waste in the urban local bodies by scientific processing and recovery of the land by the urban local bodies.
Under Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban) 10, administrative approval has been given for the scientific processing of untreated solid waste in 180 cities of the state through bio-mining process under the Government Decision dated 13/11/2018 and under Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban) 2.0, Biomining (Legacy Waste Remediation) Processing Project in 28 Urban Local Bodies of the state under the Government Decision dated 10/08/2022.
It is also proposed to approve 158 more urban local body biomining projects as per the City Solid Waste Management Action Plan (CSWAP).

Old accumulated garbage in the city should be scientifically treated and disposed of and the land should be reclaimed by the Urban Local Bodies. However, in many places, the environment does not recover nutritionally once the biomining process is completed on the old accumulated waste. Therefore, the matter of providing necessary instructions to the government to take further action with the aim of reducing the waste of the place was under consideration.
Government's decision
As mentioned in the Preamble, the old accumulated waste in the city needs to be scientifically processed and disposed of and the land recovered by the Urban Local Bodies. The Government is giving approval for creation of gardens/ playgrounds or setting up of solid waste management processing units and sewage treatment plants on the land to be reclaimed after biomining of the legacy waste.
