Government circular:
The Swachh Bharat Mission is being implemented in the state on a mission mode. In the year 2018, 260 cities of the state participated in the" Swachh Survekshan-2018" conducted by the Central Government in connection with cleanliness. Out of these, 9 cities have received the second-highest number of national awards.
Swachh Survekshan 2019 by the Central Government. It will be held across the country from January 4 to January 31, 2019. A total of 384 cities, including the newly created Nagar Panchayats in the state, will be participating in the survey and these cities will have to submit their data on the central government portal every month from October 2018. Also, the targets set under the survey are to be completed within the stipulated time period.
3. The activities of Swachh Survekshan-2019 in all the cities of the State are currently being monitored at the block level by the Regional Deputy Director, Municipal Administration. However, considering the large number of cities that participated in the survey and the targets to be met within the stipulated time frame, it needs to be controlled at the district level as well. Accordingly, the District Administrative Officer of the concerned district is being declared as the" District Coordinating Officer" for" Swachh Survekshan-2019."
The duties and responsibilities of the District Coordinating Officer for Swachh Survekshan-2019 shall be as follows: (1) To guide the ongoing activities in respect of Swachh Survekshan-2019 in all the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats of the district. Also, coordinate and review by visiting the concerned city directly as per requirement.

(2) To collect the necessary information in accordance with the Swachh Survekshan-2019 of all the cities of the district from time to time and to send it to the Regional Deputy Director of the Municipal Council Branch of the concerned department and to the Government from time to time.
