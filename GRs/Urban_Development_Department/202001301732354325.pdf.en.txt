Government Decision: Under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), Jalna Municipal Council's Urban Solid Waste Management Rs. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) for Rs. 16.5239 crore has been accorded administrative approval as per the Government Resolution No. 3.
2. While the implementation of the Detailed Project Report was going on through Jalna Municipal Council, they noticed that the Detailed Project Report did not cover certain aspects in terms of solid waste management in the city. Therefore, the Jalna Municipal Council had submitted a proposal to include those items in the detailed project report.
The proposal submitted by Jalna Municipal Council was examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Powered Committee held on 30.08.2019. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
In accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee to the proposal submitted by the Jalna Municipal Council, the Government approves the matters mentioned in the Annexure 4 and Annexure 5 to this Government Resolution amounting to Rs. 2,71,80, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores One Hundred and Seventy-Four Lakhs Eighty Thousand Only).
The amount of this increase shall be borne by the Jalna Municipal Council from the 14th Finance Commission Fund or from its own funds. The government will not provide any additional funds to the concerned municipal council for this.
It shall be mandatory to comply with the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated 12.04.2018 at Reference No. 3.
With this change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of Jalna Municipal Council will be Rs. 19,24,19, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores Twenty Four Lakhs Nineteen Thousand only).
