The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, in respect of immovable property situated in the city, if any, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent on the value of such property situated in the case of sale or encumbrance of the same and on the amount secured by the encumbrance of the encumbrance of the same in the case of encumbrance of the same. Accordingly, the Government has decided to deduct 1% of the amount due to the 26 Municipal Corporations of the State as stamp duty surcharge for the financial year 2022-23. As on 29.04.2022, Rs. 147,00,00, 000/- as first instalment and Rs. 483,00,00, 000/- crore has been released to the respective Municipal Corporations as on 14.09.2022. 284,25,00, 000/- crore was under consideration of the Government for disbursement to the concerned Municipal Corporations.
Government's decision:
For disbursement of 3 rd instalment of 1% Stamp Duty for the year 2022-23, the total amount released to the Municipal Corporations within the limit of subsidy available on Budget Distribution System is Rs. 284, 25,00,000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Forty Four Crores Twenty Five Lakhs only) is hereby approved by the Government as follows:

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations. The Collector shall draw the said grant without any deduction from the amount due to the Municipalities (without intimation to the Government) and disburse the same to the respective Municipal Corporations within 10 days.
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be maintained separately by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
The expenditure to be incurred in this regard shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2022-23 by incurring expenditure under the head" Demand No.F-4,3604, payment of compensation and stamp duty amounts to Local Self Government Institutions and Panchayat Raj Institutions, 102, Stamp Duty (01) Stamp Duty due to abolition of VAT (01) (01) Stamp Duty due to restoration of VAT in lieu of Local Body Tax (36040924) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) to Municipal Corporations for payment of 1% surcharge on stamp duty in the Municipal Corporation area."
