Distribution of grants-in-aid for GIS based survey and mapping of properties in the limits of all Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats to' C' and' D' category Municipal Corporations.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Order No: BMC-2517/ PR No.734/ NV-21
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: April 13, 2018.
Government Resolution, Urban Development Department No.BMC-2517/ PR No.734/ NV-21, Dy. 31.03.2018
The Government has issued orders to the Municipal Corporations of' C' and' D' categories to pay the dues of MahaIT submitted by the Directorate of Municipal Councils for the GIS-based survey and mapping of properties within the limits of Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats. 100.00 crore (Rupees one hundred crore only) has been disbursed to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2018.
The funds provided shall be used on the basis of the following terms and conditions:
The funds provided should be used only for property-wise survey. Funds should not be used for any other purpose.
Also, the conditions and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated 31.03.2018 must be followed.
