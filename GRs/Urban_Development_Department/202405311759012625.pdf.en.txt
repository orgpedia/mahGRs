The Proposal: -
The Mumbai Metro Line-6 (Swami Samarthnagar-Jogeshwari-Vikhroli) has been sanctioned vide Government Decision dated 21.12.2017. The completion cost for Metro Line-6, estimated by the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation Limited, is Rs. A price of 6716.00 crores has been approved. The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority has contributed Rs. 3195.02 crore, State Government participation Rs. 1820.58 is crores. Also, an external loan of Rs. 1699.69 crores will be charged. A similar loan will be taken by the authority from the New Development Bank. A new FARTS SISTA proposal was under consideration of the government to disburse the loan amount of the New Development Bank to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for the Mumbai Metro Line-6 project, to recover the loan as well as to recover the interest on the loan taken. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
The Government's Decisions:
The following new head of accounts are being approved by this Government for disbursement of New Development Bank loan amount to Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for Mumbai Metro Line-6 project, recovery of loan and recovery of interest on loan taken: -
