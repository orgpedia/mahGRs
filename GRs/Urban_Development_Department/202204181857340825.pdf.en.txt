The above promotion is being given subject to the following conditions: (1) The said promotion is an ad-hoc promotion.
(2) The officer/ employee so promoted shall not be entitled to any benefit of promotion.
(3) The said promotion shall be valid for a period of 11 months from the date of receipt of the promotion order or till the availability of the candidate by the Commission through a simple process. upto 17.03.2023) whichever is earlier and shall automatically terminate after the period specified in the promotion order.
(4) These ad-hoc promotions shall be continued after every 11 months.

As per the provision of 07.05.2021, the vacancy in the promotion quota will be filled only on a temporary basis as per the merit of the service. (6) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the said temporary promotion will not result in any entitlement of regularity and eminence to the officers in the select list.
