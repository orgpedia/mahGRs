GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government decision dated 18th September, 2019 regarding the release of Central and State Government funds for the construction of individual household and community/ public toilets to the Urban Local Bodies in the State under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) is hereby revoked.
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
The Central Government has released funds to the tune of Rs. 33,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Crores Forty Lakh only) for the construction of individual household toilets and community/ public toilets under Swachh Bharat Mission to the State under Order No.5 of 2019-20.
Accordingly, the Central Government has released Rs. 33,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Crore Forty Four Lakh only) for construction of individual household toilets and Rs. 50,65,37, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Crore Fifty Five Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand only) for construction of community/ public toilets under Swachh Bharat Mission.
