The Proposal:
Nagpur Municipal Corporation is an A-category municipal corporation. It was held on 02.03.1952. The area of Nagpur Municipal Corporation is 227 square kilometres and the population of Nagpur city at present is approximately 30 lakhs.
The necessary posts have been sanctioned to the Nagpur Municipal Corporation in view of the increase in urbanization of the Nagpur Municipal Corporation area, the industrial sector, the stress on infrastructure due to important initiatives/ institutions/ projects of the central and state governments, as well as the role of the urban local body as a people's welfare and dynamic administration. According to the reference. A total of 17,981 posts of Nagpur Municipal Corporation have been approved by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 10.05.2023. Accordingly, vide letter No. Under section 2, the Commissioner and Administrator, Nagpur Municipal Corporation had requested for approval of the service entry rules of these sanctioned posts. Accordingly, the matter of approving the" Service Entry Rules" of the said posts under Section-457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Nagpur City Municipal Corporation has submitted the" Nagpur Municipal Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2023" to the Government for approval under Section-457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
(2) The said rule shall be called the" Nagpur Municipal Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2023."
(3) These rules shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
