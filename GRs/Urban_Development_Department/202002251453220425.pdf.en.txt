As per the Government of India notification dated 29.11.2019, the Central Finance Commission will submit two reports to the Government of India for the year 2020-21 and for the years 2021-22 to 2025-26. The recommendations of the Commission's final report will come into effect from April 1, 2021. The final report of the Commission will be published by 30th October, 2020. Out of these, the first report for the year 2020-21 and the clarificatory note of the Central Government have been received.
