Government Decision: The Transport Committee passed and passed a resolution to reject the proposal of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking to construct new 250 stainless steel bus shelters on BOT (build, use, transfer) basis and to grant advertising rights. Resolutions suspended under the Government Decision dated 13.09.2019 No. 119, c. 31.05.2019, being against the financial interest of the transport undertaking, in exercise of the powers vested in the Government, the said Corporation is being finally dissolved under Section-451 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. The proposal submitted by the transport manager to the transport committee is being approved.
