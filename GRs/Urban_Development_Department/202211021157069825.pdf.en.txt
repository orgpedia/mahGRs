The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission is being implemented in the State from the year 2021-22 as per the decision of the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Under this mission, infrastructure such as water supply, rejuvenation of lakes and green area development will be created in all the urban local bodies of the state and sanitation facilities will be provided in 44 cities of the state which were covered under the previous AMRUT Mission. Accordingly, Rs. The Central Government has approved the State Water Resources Plan (CAPEX cost only) with a project cost of Rs. 18236.39 crore. This includes the Ichalkaranji Municipal Water Supply Project. As per the technical approval given by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Pune, it has been approved by the State Level Technical Committee in its meeting dated October 10, 2022. The project was under the consideration of the government.
