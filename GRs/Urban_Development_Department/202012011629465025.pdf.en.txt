Considering the above, the Government is taking the following decisions: Government Decision: Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik Zonal Centre, Mumbai has requested for a free of cost plot of land in Thane Municipal Corporation area for the Mumbai Zonal Centre of Open University.
The above proposal of the Thane Municipal Corporation has been approved by the General Body of the Municipal Corporation vide its resolution dated 27.08.2019. Accordingly, pursuant to Section 79 (c) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the proposal of the Thane Municipal Corporation to lease out a plot of at least 1500.00 sq.m. for educational purposes to the Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, at a nominal rent of Rs.1/-per annum for 30 years, subject to the following terms and conditions, subject to the availability of the plot of facilities available to the Thane Municipal Corporation or the plot of land reserved for educational purposes.
Terms and Conditions: 1) Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University shall be bound to construct at its own cost a building for educational purposes of at least 1500.00 sq.m. on the plot to be distributed by the Thane Municipal Corporation.
The Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University will be obliged to set up a separate centre for Thane within two years after the land is given.
(3) If the construction is not started within six months of the delivery of the plot, the Municipal Corporation shall take back possession of the plot on the assumption that the lease agreement has been terminated.
4) If the Mumbai Zonal Center of Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University is set up in the Thane Municipal Corporation area, Yashwant Chavan Open University will continue to provide excellent educational facilities to the students as long as it is established. Till then or 15 years, whichever is earlier, the lease will be admissible at a concessional rate of Rs.1/- per annum for this period.
5) If the lease is to be extended after 15 years, the government's approval will be required.
6) If the Thane Municipal Corporation cancels the lease agreement between the Thane Municipal Corporation and Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University for violating the terms and conditions of the lease agreement, the lease will be terminated and the ownership of all the buildings and other equipment on the said plot will be transferred to the Thane Municipal Corporation free of cost.
7) Also, if after the expiry of the lease, the Thane Municipal Corporation/ Government decides not to extend the said lease, the Public Works Department
