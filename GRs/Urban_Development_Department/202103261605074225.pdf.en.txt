Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored" Suvarna Jayanti Shahari Rozgar Yojana" has been restructured and converted into" National Urban Livelihoods Mission" under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana and is being implemented in the State. The scheme is being implemented on 60: 40 basis between the Centre and the State. Under this mission, measures are being taken to provide employment or self-employment opportunities by providing skill training to the people of urban poor families, providing shelter facilities to the urban homeless, solving the problems related to livelihood of street vendors in urban areas, reducing urban poverty by improving the living standards of urban poor families, etc. Reference No. 6 In a letter, the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, has requested for release of Centre-State share (3rd/ final tranche) for this mission.
For the year 2020-2021, the Centre: State share of Rs. 6000.00 Lakhs and Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 4000.00 lakh has been approved. So far, 85% of the provisions of Reference No. 5 has been distributed here as per the government decision. The remaining 15% of the final stage available provision is Rs. 900.00 lakhs and Rs. 570.00 lakhs was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Final tranche of 15% available allocation made by Finance Department for the programme under National Urban Livelihood Mission under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana Centre-State share respectively
