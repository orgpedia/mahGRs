The Proposal:
The Government has given in-principle approval for development of Ramtek Pilgrim Centre at a cost of Rs.150 crore for development of the historical monuments and precincts of Ramtek in Nagpur district. Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 21/5/2018 for works to the tune of Rs. 49.28 crore to be taken up in the first phase of the project. For this purpose, a Supplementary Demand of Rs.14 crore has been approved in the Winter Session of December, 2020 and the amount is to be disbursed as per the Government Circular dated 22/12/2020 of the Finance Department at Reference No.5. The Finance Department has agreed to disburse an amount of Rs 7 crore (subject to a ceiling of 50%) out of the said amount of Rs 14 crore. The matter was under consideration to hand over the amount to the Collector, Nagpur. Accordingly, the Government is approving the handing over of funds to the Collector, Nagpur:
