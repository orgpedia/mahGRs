The decision was taken by the state agriculture and horticulture department. As on 09.10.2020, Virkarwadi (Dist. Approval has been given to set up a category-1 veterinary clinic here. Request made vide letter dated 13.10.2020. This was informed by the municipal council. Resolution No. 04.08.2021 The meeting has approved the transfer of the above 10 gunthas to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Satara free of cost by passing 485 unanimous resolutions.
As per the provisions of Section 92 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Town Panchayats and Industrial Towns Act, 1965, no municipality shall transfer its immovable property without the approval of the State Government. Also, as per Rule 21 of the Maharashtra Municipalities (Transfer of Immovable Property) Rules, 1983, there is a provision for leasing out land for public purposes and the municipal council can lease out land for public purposes to government departments at a concessional rate decided by the council from time to time with the prior approval of the government.
