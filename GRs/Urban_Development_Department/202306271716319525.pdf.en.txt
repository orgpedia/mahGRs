Approval of the Thane Municipal Corporation Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2023.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No.:- Sthakpa-2122/ P.No. 41/ NV-23
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
4th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: June 27, 2023
Reference:- Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation dated 04.01.2023
Preamble: Establishment of Thane Municipal Corporation. The said Municipal Corporation was formed on 01.10.1982 and is a' B' class Municipal Corporation. At present, there are 9 wards of the Municipal Corporation, with a total area of 147 sq km. As per the 2011 census, the Thane Municipal Corporation has a population of 18.41 lakh, with a floating population of 24 lakh.
2. The creation of posts of various cadres on the establishment of Thane Municipal Corporation has been approved under the Act dated 11.04.2022, in view of the increase in urbanization of the Thane Municipal Corporation area, the stress on infrastructure due to important initiatives/ institutions/ projects of the Central and State Governments, as well as the role of the urban local body as a public welfare and dynamic administration.
Thane Municipal Corporation General Assembly Resolution No. 403/1 dated 07.04.2021 and Administrative Resolution No. 167 dated 15.02.2023.
The Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation vide letter dated 04.01.2023 has submitted the said Service Access Rules to the Government for approval.
Accordingly, the matter of giving unanimous approval to the Service Admission Rules of the Thane Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: As per section 457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the Government is approving the rule" Thane Municipal Corporation Service (Classification of Service Access and Services) Rules, 2023" under section 454, 455, 456, 457 (3) and 465 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, as per the draft service access rules submitted for government approval, with necessary amendments.
2. The said rules may be called the" Thane Municipal Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2023."
