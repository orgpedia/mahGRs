Government circular
It has been decided to celebrate" Foundation Day" in all the Urban Local Bodies of the State through the Urban Development Department and all the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats of the State shall celebrate" Foundation Day" every year on the date on which they are constituted by notification. Various programmes will be held during the course of the day. All expenses must be paid out of your own funds.
