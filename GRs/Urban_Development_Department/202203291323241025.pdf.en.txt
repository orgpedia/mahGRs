2. The guidelines for effective implementation of the schemes mentioned in returns A and B above are as follows: (1) The Commissioner/ Principal of the Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat shall be paid Rs. The limit is Rs 10 lakh.
However, Rs. The Commissioner/ Principal concerned is being empowered to approve the scheme and implement the scheme up to the financial limit of Rs.25 lakh per scheme. However, it is necessary to follow the financial rules.
(2) The Commissioner, the Municipal Corporation, the Chief Officer, the Municipality/ Town Panchayat shall finalize the budgetary provision for the next year only after ensuring that the programme for implementation of the scheme is prepared from the beginning of each year.
(3) The proposal for expenditure on the schemes/ programmes/ works mentioned in the aforesaid returns shall require the recommendation of the Women and Child Welfare Committee in the Urban Local Bodies. No proposal/ fund should be sanctioned without this recommendation.
(4) The District Collector shall first ascertain whether the funds of the previous year have been spent or not while approving the budget of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat. If there is insufficient funds, it should be carried forward in the next year's budget. Carry forward facility in the next year's budget will be available only for one year.
If the necessary expenditure is not made within the stipulated time, the concerned Commissioner/ Principal should be held responsible and recorded in his confidential report.
(5) The plans/ programmes/ works mentioned in the above statement are in the form of guidelines/ indicators for the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats to undertake,
