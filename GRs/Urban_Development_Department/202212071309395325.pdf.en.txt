The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission is being implemented in the State from the year 2021-22 as per the decision of the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Under this mission, infrastructure such as water supply, rejuvenation of lakes and green area development will be created in all the urban local bodies of the state and sanitation facilities will be provided in 44 cities of the state which were covered under the previous AMRUT Mission. Accordingly, Rs. The Central Government has approved the State Water Resources Plan (CAPEX cost only) with a project cost of Rs. 18236.39 crore. This includes the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation's Sewerage Project. As per the technical approval given by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Thane under Section 5, the same has been approved by the State Level Technical Committee in its meeting dated November 22, 2022. The project was under the consideration of the government.

The Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation is required to set up the Opex Price of the said Sewerage Project. Completion of the project is expected in 12 months.
Administrative approval is being granted to the Sewerage Project of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation subject to the following conditions: 4.1 Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation shall be the implementing agency for the project.
4.2 The share of the local body for the project should be paid by the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation.
4.3 The implementing agency shall be bound to implement the said project as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the decision of the Government in the context of the State Government.
4.4 The State Government vide No. It shall be incumbent upon the concerned executing agency to complete the tendering process and execution of the project within the prescribed time frame as per the procedures prescribed under Sections 2 and 3. D.I. for this project.
All types of pipes, including pipes, should also be included in the tender.
4.5 The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will be required to publish the model tender booklet issued for water supply and sewerage projects under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
4.6 The implementing agency shall ensure that the work orders for the said project are issued within 45 days from the date of order of administrative sanction.
4.7 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the mission city to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.
4.8. Mode of disbursement: As per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission, the Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%). Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.
4.9 While setting up self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) are required to match the Finance Commission funds received by them.

4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on the operational outcomes. Also, the
Funds will not be allocated to the project if the functional outcome is not met. The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation will be fully responsible for fulfilling these requirements.
4.11 The first instalment of funds from the Central and State Governments for the project will be disbursed to the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation after the issuance of the work order. Accordingly, after submission of Utilization Certificate to the Central Government as prescribed by the Central Government and after achieving the operational results, the next instalment of Central share and State share will be disbursed accordingly.
4.12 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
4.13 As per the instructions issued while allocating funds for the project, it will be mandatory for the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation to open a separate bank account and keep funds in it in proportion to the funds released by the Central and State Governments for the project.
The funds allocated for the project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, it will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
4.14 After the project is approved by the State Government for the Urban Local Bodies, the original project cost will be included in the Government Decision No.
In case of extension of Grapadho-2021/ PR No. 122/ PAPU-06, dated 29th June, 2022, other than the stated reason, the entire responsibility will be of the concerned implementing agency. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government for this purpose.
4.15 The overall responsibility of completing the project within the stipulated time will be shared by the concerned implementing agency, Project Management Consultant and Project Management Development and Management Consultant.
4.16 After commencement of the project, the Project Manager shall make payment of the work within the limit of funds available after the consultant certifies the quality of work and payment.
