Introduction: The Integrated Web Based Project (IWBP) project for the Urban Development Department to develop and implement the services offered under the Urban Local Bodies in the state of Maharashtra in online format was approved by the High Power Committee in its meeting dated 27 June 2016 and 17 March 2017. Accordingly, Maha-IT has issued the commissioning order for the project on 2nd February, 2017. A total of 27 modules have been developed under the project, out of which 6 modules have been implemented in 386 ULBs. The original cost of the project was Rs. 144.01 crore and the tenure was 5 years. Subsequently, the project cost was revised to Rs. The approval for the revised project cost of Rs. 43.79 crore and extension of the project period was accorded by the High Powered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting dated 22 nd August, 2022. The Government of India has decided to implement the National Urban Digital Mission (NUDM) in the country from 2022-23.
