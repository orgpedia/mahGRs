Correction:
The Water Supply Scheme for Jamkhed (Dist. Ahmednagar) under Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 02.03.2020. The following are the provisions of the Government Order No. 3 (a) is being amended.
(a) Implementation of the project:
The project should be implemented on a full deposit basis by the Maharashtra Life Authority.
The municipal council should provide a fee equal to 3% of the project cost to the Maharashtra Life Authority.
The implementing agency shall be bound to fulfil all the conditions prescribed by the State Level Project Approval Committee while approving the project.
