The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015. 18.12.2018
Introduction: The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 has been enacted with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their increasing expectations from the local administration. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28.04.2015. As per Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority is required to notify under this Ordinance the public service, designated officers, first and second appellate authorities and the time limits to be prescribed by it from time to time after the promulgation of the Ordinance. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to notify the time limit, designated officers, first and second appellate authorities for public services in the CIDCO Corporation.
Government's decision:
As per Section-3 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, the services in Schedule-A of CIDCO Corporation are being notified under this order.
The details of the duration of public service, office of service notifying, designated officers, first appellate officers, and other appellate authorities for the public services provided by the CIDCO Corporation under the Urban Development Department are mentioned in Schedule-A.
(2) The designated officer, first and second appellate officer responsible for providing services under the Schedule-A should take action to provide services to the citizens within the stipulated time.
