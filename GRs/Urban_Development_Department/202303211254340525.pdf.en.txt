The Proposal:
Mumbai Metro Line 4 (Wadala-Ghatkopar-Mulund-Thane-Kasarvadavali) project is being implemented by the state government. It was approved on October 25, 2018. The project is being implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). The cost of the project is Rs. 5961 crore, 50% of Central Taxes Rs. 867 crore, interest during construction period Rs. 47 crore and 20% of the system cost of Rs. 979 crore. 50% of the project cost of Rs. 867 crore and Rs. 994 crore and Rs. 1832 crore. 3,693 crore is to be provided by the State Government in the form of non-interest bearing secondary loans.
