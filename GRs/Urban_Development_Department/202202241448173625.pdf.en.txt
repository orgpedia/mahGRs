The Proposal:
The total length of Mumbai Metro Line-5 (Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan) is 24.9 km. I, Stations- 15 and estimated Rs. The project is being implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority at a cost of Rs 8416.51 crore. The Government has decided to implement this project by the Urban Development Department, Government Decision No. MRD-3316/ PR No. 139/ NV-7, dated 21 December,
2017 has been approved. As per the approved financial plan of the project, 50 per cent of central taxes and 100 per cent of state taxes and land cost will be borne by the state government.
Total Rs. 1352.25 crore is to be provided by the State Government to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority. Urban Development Authority No. MRD
3317/ S.No. 138/ NV-7, dated 02.11.2018, it has been decided to provide the secondary loan to be given by the State Government for Mumbai Metro Line-5 in the form of interest free loan.
To disburse this secondary loan fund to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
The new authority to repay the issue of this loan to the state government
