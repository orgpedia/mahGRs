The Urban Development Department sanctions funds to the Municipal Corporations for various schemes like Municipal Infrastructure, Municipal Infrastructure (Block), Extension Fund to Municipal Corporations and Special Schemes to Municipal Councils, New Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, Extension of Municipal Councils, Development of Pilgrimage Areas, Special Road Grants, Road Grants. The matter of giving instructions regarding the utilization of the interest received on the funds distributed by the government on such schemes was under consideration.
In order to ensure that the interest amounts lying with the Urban Local Bodies are properly utilised and help in generating capital from them and such amounts are not unnecessarily lying unutilized with the Urban Local Bodies, the Government has decided to:
(a) The amount of interest received by the Urban Local Bodies on the State Account Fund disbursed to them under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagrothan Abhiyan shall be adjusted from the same at the time of disbursement of the last instalment of the projects to such Urban Local Bodies.
In addition, the Urban Development Department will be able to use the interest received on the funds sanctioned for various schemes such as Urban Local Bodies, Municipal Infrastructure, Municipal Infrastructure Block, Extension Fund to Municipal Corporations and Special Schemes to Municipal Councils, New Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, Extension of Municipal Council Boundaries, Special Road Grants, Road Grants etc. For this, all the guidelines of the original scheme will be applicable to them. Similarly, it will be mandatory to obtain administrative approval from the competent authority applicable to the funds in the original plan.
