The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Abhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and raising their standards.
Under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission, the water supply project of Sillod city was granted administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 2. The matter of approving the revision of the Goods and Services Tax rate of the Sillod City Water Supply Project, which was submitted to the government under reference number 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration, was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval has been granted to the Sillod City Water Supply Project under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotna Abhiyan under the Government Resolution No. 2. The proposal submitted to the Government under reference No. 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration regarding the revision of the rate of Goods and Services Tax in the water supply project is being approved by this Government decision as mentioned in the following statement.
