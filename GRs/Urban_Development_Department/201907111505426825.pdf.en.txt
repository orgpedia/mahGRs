(a) The benefit of the Compassionate Appointment Scheme shall be applicable to the eligible families of the following categories of employees in the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats: 1. Permanent/ temporary employees working in the establishment of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat.
Employees who have been appointed in Group-C or Group-D in the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat service and are on the establishment list of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat.
3. Employees who are engaged as daily wagers in the service of Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat. Employees who were working before 27.03.2000 and have been absorbed and appointed by the Government/ Directorate of Municipal Administration/ Divisional Commissioner.
4. Daily wage workers. Labour Court/ M. the Industrial Court. High Court/ High Court. Employees who have been regularized in the Municipal Council/ Town Panchayat service but are working in superannuation posts, (b) Action on retention of waiting list on compassionate grounds: The Collectorate concerned shall keep separate waiting lists for Group-A and Group-D for appointment of eligible heirs of employees of Municipal Councils/ Town Panchayats in the district under the scheme.
2.The concerned headmaster should inform the relatives of the deceased employees/ children about the scheme, receive the proposal as per the rules, and send the complete proposal to the Municipal Council Administration Branch, Collector's Office.
3. The District Administration Officers with the approval of the District Collector shall include the name of the eligible heritage in the waiting list.
The benefit of the Compassionate Appointment Scheme shall be applicable to the following employees of the Municipal Corporation: 1. Municipal employees on permanent and temporary establishment converted into Municipal Corporation.
(ii) Municipal Corporation employees who have been regularised in service but are working in superannuation.
Educational Qualification for Compassionate Appointment in Municipal Corporations: 1. Educational qualification as provided in the relevant Service Entry Rules for direct service posts in Group-C and Group-D may be so appointed.
2.Also, in cases where the Municipal Corporation does not have its own service entry rules, it is necessary to have the educational qualification prescribed under the service entry rules of the equivalent post in the government.
