Corrigendum:
Navi Mumbai International Airport Ltd. The Concession Agreement has been signed on 8.1.2018. Earlier, the development of the airport was undertaken by CIDCO from April, 2017 and the land was handed over to the concessionaire.
A Grievance Redressal Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Navi-I) to resolve disputes or differences arising during the implementation of the said work. has been fixed as on 31.10.2018. The following changes have been made in the Government Order:
" Instead of the words" Concession Agreement/ Contract Agreement" in the said Government Decision.
The Land Development Works Substitution Agreement (LDSWA) should be read as" Land Development Works Substitution Agreement." "
There is no change in the rest of the text.
