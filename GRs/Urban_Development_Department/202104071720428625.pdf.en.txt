Reference:- Urban Development Department, Government Resolution No. 2017/ No. 139/ NV-14, D. 12.02.2018.
Preamble: It has been approved to fix the proportion of the posts notified in the Maharashtra Municipal Council Cadre in the Municipal Council and Nagar Panchayat wise and to fix the revised figure accordingly. As per the provisions of this decision, the revised figure of the posts has been fixed taking into account the class and population of the Municipal Council. With the conversion of Nagpur and Nagar Panchayats into Municipal Councils, it was under the consideration of the Government to determine the contours of the Armori Municipal Council, as per the population of Gadchiroli, as per the permissible annexure in the relevant Government Resolution.
Considering the population of Gadchiroli which has been converted into a Municipal Council, it is being approved to implement Annexure-5 of the said Government Resolution for Armori Municipal Council, District Gadchiroli.
