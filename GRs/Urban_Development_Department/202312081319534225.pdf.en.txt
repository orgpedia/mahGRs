In accordance with the decision of the Government No. 02, the said project of Nagpur Municipal Corporation is being approved subject to the following conditions:
3.1 The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation has certified that the said land acquisition project is in the area of Nagpur Municipal Corporation.
3.2 In respect of the work of flyovers, ROBs in urban areas, it is the primary responsibility of the respective Urban Local Bodies/ Municipal Corporations to acquire land which is not the statutory responsibility of NHAI/ Central Government.
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation shall certify that the responsibility of acquiring the proposed site lies with the Nagpur Municipal Corporation.
3.3 As per the provisions of the relevant Government Decision, the Municipal Corporation is required to acquire the land by issuing TDR for such work and also take action to make changes in the Development Plan where necessary.
The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation shall ensure that the action is taken in accordance with the requirement of the concerned Collector, Nagpur to certify that it is not possible to acquire the site despite such action.
3.4 The work of the Centrally Sponsored Project of the flyover, road widening should have been started by the Central Government after technical and administrative approval of the project and the project should be completed.

The process of distribution of funds:
5.1 After the administrative approval for the projects in the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, the concerned civic body should submit the financial closure report of the project to the Urban Development Department. After that, the first installment of the project will be released.
5.2 After disbursing the first installment of funds to the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks prescribed by the Government as per the prevailing policy of the Government. The Nagpur Municipal Corporation should be formed by the Municipal Corporation from the 14th and 15th Finance Commission's Goods and Services Tax grants or self-funding.
5.3 The second installment of the State Government will be disbursed after submitting the utilization certificate of 75 percent of the total amount of the first installment of the subsidy distributed by the State Government and the matching self-assurance fund of the Municipal Corporation to the Government through the concerned Collector.
5.4 Proposal for demand of second installment by Urban Local Bodies The concerned Collector should scrutinize the proposals and submit the proposal to the Government with appropriate feedback.
Non-Direction of Funds:- The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can be used only for that project and it cannot be diverted or used for any other work. Any such diversion of the principal amount or interest thereon, whether permanent or temporary, will be treated as a serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned Urban Local Bodies/ concerned authorities.
