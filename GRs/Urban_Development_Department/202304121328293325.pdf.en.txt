Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Campaign is being implemented in the state. Under the scheme, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pariyojana (MJPA) or a private project management consultant is appointed as the project management consultant (PMC) for water supply and sewerage projects.
2. The water supply and sewerage projects under the Mission are being taken up under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission as per the provisions of the Government Circular No. Under Section 3 of the Act, urban local bodies are allowed to choose one of the three options for the appointment of a Project Management Consultant (PMC). The options are as follows:
(a) The Maharashtra Life Authority may be appointed as the Project Management Consultant.
(b) In terms of letter from the Maharashtra Life Authority No. As per the letter of 1, the Maharashtra Life Authority has created a total of 16 empanelment of Project Management Consultants (PMC), out of which any one of them will be selected as a Project Management Consultant.
c) Urban Local Bodies will be allowed to appoint Project Management Consultant (PMC) through tender process as per Model RFP prescribed by Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
