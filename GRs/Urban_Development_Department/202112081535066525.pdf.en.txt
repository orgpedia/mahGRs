GOVERNMENT DECISION, UDD, SANKARNAMA dated 14th July, 2021.
Preamble: The modalities for the disbursement and utilization of grants for the purpose of Air Quality Improvement to Urban Local Bodies in Million Plus Cities under the 15th Finance Commission have been decided vide Government Resolution dated 14th July, 2021. The matter of determining the procedure for granting administrative approval in respect of the said grant was under the consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: The following procedure is being laid down for grant of administrative approval in respect of grants for Air Quality Improvement to Urban Local Bodies in Million Plus Cities under the 15th Finance Commission
Municipal Corporations:- The Commissioner of the concerned Municipal Corporation in the case of Municipal Corporations will be empowered to give administrative approval to the proposals of works to be taken from the grant for the purpose of air quality improvement in the group of million cities under the 15th Finance Commission.
Municipal Councils: (i) Power to grant administrative approval to proposals for works to be taken up under the 15th Finance Commission Grants-in-Aid for Air Quality Improvement in Group of Million Cities. 30 lakh, Rs. 20 lakh and Rs. 10 lakh will be given to the head of the respective Municipal Councils.
(ii) The District Collector concerned shall have the power to grant administrative approval to all proposals for works other than these.

The concerned Chief Executive Officers (CEOs) in the case of Cuttack mandals will be empowered to give administrative approval to the proposals for works to be taken up under the 15th Finance Commission grants for the purpose of Air Quality Improvement in Million Cities.
The concerned authorities at (1), (2) and (3) above shall exercise the powers of the said administrative sanction for the Air Quality Improvement component in accordance with and subject to the guidelines of the 15th Finance Commission, instructions/ orders issued from time to time by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Central Pollution Control Board, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board.
