A proposal was under consideration of the government to create a competent authority to address the transportation challenges in the Pune Metropolitan Region and suggest integrated and effective solutions by coordinating all schemes, initiatives and related agencies. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
With a view to further strengthen the transport system in the Pune Metropolitan Region and to suggest measures to meet the transport challenges through integrated and mutually complementary plans and initiatives in coordination with all the concerned agencies, it is hereby approved to set up Pune Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority under the Chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Pune as follows:

(3) The jurisdiction of the Authority:
The defined geographical area of the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Area shall be the jurisdiction of the Pune Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority. The authority will function under the jurisdiction of Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
(2) The Authority shall co-ordinate with the agencies under it, mainly in the field of transport.
As the Empowered Transport Authority, the Authority shall make decisions and make recommendations with respect to the overall transport plan, modal priorities, infrastructure priorities, financial build-up and allocations and modalities of operation.
However, the said Authority shall not take any decision which would unduly interfere with the autonomy provided by the Constitution to the local bodies.
(4) The Authority may make recommendations on the following matters:
All-inclusive transport plan for the city.
Regional and city development plans and regional transport plans.
Coordination.
