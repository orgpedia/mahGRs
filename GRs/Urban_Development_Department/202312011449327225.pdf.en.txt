Preamble: The Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has requested to take a decision at the government level regarding the inclusion of the employees of the post of' Headmistress and Surgical Assistant/ Attendant' on wholesale basis in the establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation on a temporary basis, as a total of 12 employees of the post of' Headmistress and Surgical Assistant/ Attendant' are working on wholesale basis at Bharat Ratna Indira Gandhi Hospital and the employees of the post of' Headmistress and Surgical Assistant/ Attendant' on wholesale basis in other Municipal Corporations have been included on a permanent basis.

Provisional appointments on wholesale basis for 12' Superintendents and Surgeon Assistants/ Attendants' cadre under Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Bharat Ratna Indira Gandhi Hospital have been made by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview). A diagram of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation. As per the Government decision dated 26.02.2019, the posts of' Paricharika' cadre are sanctioned in the pay scale of S-13: 35400-112400 and' Surgical Assistant/ Attendant' cadre are sanctioned in the pay scale of S-5, 18000-56900. Accordingly, the matter of appointing them to these vacant posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Since the appointment of 12 employees of the' Superintendents and Surgical Assistants/ Attendants' cadre working on a temporary basis at Bharat Ratna Indira Gandhi Hospital on a wholesale basis on the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation establishment has been made by following the route of the (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), the Government is approving the appointment of them to the sanctioned and vacant posts of the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, by making them permanent and regularizing them in the Municipal Corporation service.

The 12 staff of the cadre of' Superintendant and Surgical Assistant/ Attendant' as per return' A' are being given permanent appointment to the sanctioned and vacant posts in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation diagram subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) These employees should be appointed to these posts from the date of the decision of the government.
(2) The employees will continue to be paid the benefits of permanent service (pay, seniority, pension, etc.) from the date of posting.
(3) No benefit or arrears of service rendered earlier shall be admissible to such employees.
4) No funds will be provided by the government for the salary of these employees.
5) Before being reinstated in service, it will be clearly mentioned that no previous benefit will be received by taking a bond from the concerned employee.
6) While giving permanent appointment to the above mentioned 12' Superintendents and Surgical Assistants/ Attendants', permanent appointment will be given as per the rules after checking the attendance default record, inquiry, serviceability, caste validity verification certificate, medical certificate, etc. It will be mandatory for the concerned employees to submit the character verification report within a period of 06 months from the date of order of permanent appointment.
7) Contractual employees shall be deemed to be permanent employees from the date of order without any retrospective effect at the time of appointment.
8) Relaxation in age and educational qualification is allowed if the age of the' Superintendents and Surgical Assistants/ Attendants' on the proposed wholesale rate is more than the age limit prescribed in the Service Recruitment Rules.
9) The Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation should ensure that the pay scale of the said employees while giving appointment is not more than the pay scale equivalent to that post.
10) Since the establishment cost is more than 35%, the condition of 35% of establishment cost is being relaxed as a one-time matter.
