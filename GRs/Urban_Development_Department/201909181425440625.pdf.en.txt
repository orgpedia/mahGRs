Government Decision: The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
2. Under Swachh Bharat Mission, the State Government has been implementing the scheme of Solid Waste Management. With the approval of the High Powered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary, proposals worth Rs. 318,02,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred Eighty Eight Crores Two Lakhs Only) for 92 cities were submitted to the Central Government. The Central Government vide Reference No. 4 As per the order dated 13.09.2019, out of the Central Government's permissible share of Rs. 108,80,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Eight Crores Eighty Lakh only), a sum of Rs. 54,42,00, 000/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Forty Two Lakh only) has been released to the State as 2nd installment.
The Central Government has released the second installment of Rs. 54,42,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Four Crores Forty Two Lakhs only) to the State for the Urban Solid Waste Management Projects of 92 Urban Local Bodies in the State under Swachh Bharat Mission. The expenditure shall be met from the provision made during the financial year 2019-2020 under the respective head of accounts as shown in the table below.

The said Government Decision is being issued under the authority conferred on the Administrative Department vide Finance Circular No. 2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3 dated 1st April, 2019 of the Finance Department.
As per the Government Circular Finance Division No. 31/ Finance No. 44/ Finance No. 3 dated 1st April, 2019, the Department has been authorized to incur the following expenditure in respect of non-salaried Subsidiary Grants, which are not applicable in the case of 7 (1) b, 7 (3), 7 (4) out of paragraphs 7 to 18 of the said Circular. It is stated in paragraph 7 (2) of this circular that the amount coming from the local bodies cannot be adjusted as the funds of the Central Government account are to be disbursed for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. The conditions mentioned in paragraph 7 (5,6,7, 8,9) of this circular have also been fulfilled.
The Government Circular No. 2, 3 of the Annexure to the Finance Department No.Finance-2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3, dated 1st April, 2019 is not applicable in this case. It is also mentioned in Annexure 4 that the Scheme is not a Centrally Sponsored Externally Aided Scheme, however, the receipt of funds from the Central Government has been confirmed under the Informal Reference Number of the Finance Department: ANU-332/ Arthopay, dated 16/09/2019.
In pursuance of Annexure III to the Government Circular Finance Division No.Finance-2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3 dated 1st April, 2019, it is stated that no change has been made in the form or scope of the said scheme. Further, the conditions mentioned in the Annexure No. 1, 2, 3, 4 of the Check List with reference to Annexure No. 8 to the said Circular dated 2nd April, 2018 are being fulfilled. Also, the reference to serial number 8 is not applicable in the case of serial numbers 5 to 10 in the checklist. Further, all other conditions as contained in the Government Circular Finance Division No. 2019/ PR No. 44/ Finance-3, dated 1st April, 2019 are being complied with.
The Department has been empowered to incur expenditure under Section 7 (1) (a) of the Government Circular Finance Division No. 2019/ No. 44/ Finance-3, dated 1st April, 2019.
A total amount of Rs. 90,70,18, 000/- (Rupees Ninety Crores Seventy Lakh Eight Thousand only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the approval received under the Informal Reference Number: ANU-332/ Arthopay, dated 16/9/2019 of the Department of Finance.
