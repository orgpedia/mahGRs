Government's decision:
As per the facts mentioned in the above preamble, a departmental inquiry has been conducted against Mr. Sambhaji Madhavrao Castewad, Superintendent of Excise and his period of suspension has been considered as a period of suspension and the Municipal Corporation has imposed a lenient punishment for him.
