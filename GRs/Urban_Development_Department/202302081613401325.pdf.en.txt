Government Resolution No.CID- 3322/913/ P.No.155/ NV-10, D. 27.10.2022 2. Cidco Corporation Ltd. Letter dated 31.1.2023
For the Navi Mumbai project, the State Government has constituted CIDCO Corporation as the Planning Authority under Section 113 of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966. The committee has been constituted by the relevant government decision to discuss various aspects related to the development of land distributed by Cidco Corporation in and around Navi Mumbai on lease and to decide on the problems related to the business.
Government's decision:
Cidco Corporation, under reference. It has been observed that the Committee may take some more time to provide the documents sought in the meeting as informed vide letter dated 31.1.2023, analyse the same and prepare the final report.
The committee has submitted a report regarding this. The period is being extended up to 30.4.2023.
