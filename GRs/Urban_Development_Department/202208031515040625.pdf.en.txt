Government Decision: To make all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens.
The Government of India had launched the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) with the objective of providing household toilet facilities to the citizens of the cities.
The guidelines for implementation of Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) 2.0 as a Mission in the country have been issued by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of India. The campaign is to be implemented in all the statutory cities of the country. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan 2.0 will be implemented with a vision to achieve" Garbage Free City" status for all cities.
