The Proposal:
The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation has notified the revised Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019. In the said figure of the Municipal Corporation, the posts of' Chief Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-2) and' Senior Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-3) for the Public Health Department were not approved. Also, the eligibility criteria for appointment to the post of' Chief Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-II) have been prescribed in the Service Admission Rules. However, for the post of Senior Pharmaceutical Officer (Class-III), the eligibility criteria for appointment has not been prescribed in the Service Admission Rules.
In view of the increasing scope of health work in Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, the Municipal Corporation of Mira-Bhayandar has decided to create a total of 5 new posts for the Public Health Department on the establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, namely, 1 post of' Chief Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class 2) and 4 posts of' Senior Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-3).
The approval has been accorded by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 25.09.2023. For this, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, gave the relevant information. As per the letter dated 17.04.2023, the Government has proposed to include the procedure of entry into service and qualifications for the posts of Chief Pharmaceutical Officer (Class-II) and Senior Pharmaceutical Officer (Class-III) in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019.

Government Decision: Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued the relevant orders. As per the letter dated 17.04.2023, the proposal submitted for inclusion of the method of entry into service and qualifications for the posts of' Chief Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-II) and' Senior Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-III) in the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation (Amendment of Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019, is being approved by the Government under Section-454, 455, 456, 457 (A) and 465 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, as per Annexure-II, Schedule-I.
The said Rules may be called the" Amended Part of the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Amended Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019.
The amended rules shall be published by the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation in the Government Gazette immediately. These rules will come into force from the date of their publication in the official gazette.
