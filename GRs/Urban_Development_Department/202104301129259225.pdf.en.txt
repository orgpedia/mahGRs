The Proposal:
The final report of the 15th Central Finance Commission has been released and under the 15th Finance Commission, funds will be sanctioned to urban local bodies in the state under two components- Million Plus Cities and Non-Million Plus Cities. As per the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission under the relevant Government decision, the procedure for the distribution and allocation of grants received for the Urban Local Bodies in the Non-Million Plus Cities group in the State has been fixed and the Urban Local Bodies in the Million Plus Cities and Non-Million Plus Cities group have been classified. The government was considering to amend the Act.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission regarding classification of Urban Local Bodies in the category of Million Plus Cities and Non-Million Plus Cities, the following classification is being approved by the Government.
Million Plus Cities: Under the 15th Finance Commission, the following Urban Local Bodies of the State have been included in the Million Plus Cities category.
