The Proposal:
Considering the long service rendered by Mrs. Rajkumari Laxman Gaikwad, who is working as a group organizer on consolidated pay in Aurangabad Municipal Corporation, the general meeting of the Municipal Corporation passed a resolution to give her regular permanent appointment to the vacant post of group organizer on the establishment of the corporation. 591 A.D. Approved on 6.8.2018.
As per the resolution of the general meeting, the proposal to include Mrs. Rajkumari Laxman Gaikwad in the service of the Municipal Corporation in the post of Group Organizer has been approved by the Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation. It was submitted for Government approval vide letter dated 6.1.2021. As per the proposal received from the Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation, the matter of regular inclusion of Mrs. Rajkumari Laxman Gaikwad, Group Organizer on consolidated pay, in the service of the Municipal Corporation was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Smt. Rajkumari Laxman Gaikwad has been appointed as Group Organiser, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation on consolidated pay basis in the prescribed manner. Aurangabad Municipal Corporation General Assembly Resolution No. 591, Sec. the approval granted under 6.8.2018 and the Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation, issued the relevant d. In view of the proposal received vide letter dated 6.1.2021, the Government is pleased to approve the regular induction of Smt. Rajkumari Laxman Gaikwad in the post of Group Organizer in the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation as per the following prescribed terms and conditions:
