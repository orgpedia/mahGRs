Objectives of the scheme:
The restructured Pradhan Mantri Street Vendor Atmanirbhar Nidhi Yojana is a housing and urban works scheme.
It is administered by the Ministry and 100% sponsored by the Central Government. The objectives of the scheme are as follows:
I. Identification of Street Vendors.
II. To provide playing capital to street vendors in the form of loans.
III. Making UPI-enabled credit cards available to street vendors.
IV. Promotion of digital transactions of street vendors.
V. Capacity building of street vendors.
VI. Affiliation of street vendors with social security scheme.
