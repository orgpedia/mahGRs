2. Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Cell, Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Navanagar Development Authority Unit, Pimpri-Pune vide letter No. Aastha/ Deputy Director, Town Planning and Panchayat Raj Department/ 23, dated 15.06.2018.
INTRODUCTION: As per the decision of the Government dated 28.03.2018, the Government has approved the establishment of a Special Unit Office under the name of" Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority Unit." It was under the consideration of the government to declare the office as a Drawing and Disbursing Officer (DDO). The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Ordinance, 1968, the office of the Town Planner (Gazetted-Group-A), Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navanagar Development Authority, Pimpri, Pune
Chinchwad Navanagar Development Authority Unit, Pimpri, Pune is hereby declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for drawing salary, allowances and other amounts from the treasury.
All concerned should take care to take action as mentioned in the Government Circular, Finance Division No. 1, 1009/ PR No. 26/ Koshap-5, dated 24.06.2009.
