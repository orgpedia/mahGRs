Introduction: In order to make the administration of the state more dynamic and people-oriented, the e-governance policy of the state has been decided under the Government Decision No. 4 in the context. Computerization of Government departments/ offices. A comprehensive action plan has been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 29.10.2001. Further, under the said Government decision, it has been directed to constitute a Project Implementation Committee under each administrative division to implement the computerization projects under the offices under the administrative division. Accordingly, a Project Implementation Committee (PICC) under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Navi-1) was constituted to implement the e-governance project under the Urban Development Department. However, since there are two Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries under the Urban Development Department and the nature of work under them is different, the matter of forming two separate project implementation committees was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
(9) The General Administration Department has issued the above Reference No. Considering the guidelines/ directions given under the Government Decision mentioned herein 1-4, the following Project Implementation Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary (Navi-1) to implement the e-Governance project under the Urban Development Department.
