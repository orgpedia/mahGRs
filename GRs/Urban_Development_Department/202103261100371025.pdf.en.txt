Mumbai Metro Line-7 (Andheri East) and Dahisar (East) and Mumbai Metro Line-2 Project Phase-II (DN Nagar-Mandalay) are the elevated metro rail projects to be implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). Approved by 1 to 3. The project will be funded by the state government, central and state governments.
Loans will be available from two external financial institutions, New Development Bank and Asian Development Bank, for the cost of system components of Mumbai Metro Line 2A, 2B and 7 metro projects.
The Asian Development Bank (ADB) has approved a loan of$ 926 million for the projects. It was given. Project Agreement between ADB Bank, Government of India and Government of Maharashtra and Loan Agreement between ADB Bank and Government of India have been signed on 01.03.2019.
Similarly, the New Development Bank has approved a loan of$ 260 million for these projects. It was given. Project Agreement signed between New Development Bank, Government of India and Government of Maharashtra on 26.12.2018
