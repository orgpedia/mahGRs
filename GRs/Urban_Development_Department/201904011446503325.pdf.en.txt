2. Municipal Administration under its purview. In the letter dated 29.11.2018, it has been mentioned that the said employees are working as male labourers in Municipal Sanitary Establishments. The posts of male/ female workers in Municipal Corporation Safai Establishments are in the category of Class 4 and the pay scale of this post is 4440-7440 Grade Pay 1300. The post of Sanitary Inspector is in the cadre of Class 3 and the pay scale of the post of Sanitary Inspector is 5200-20200 with Grade Pay of 2400.
There are a total of 14 posts of Sanitation Inspector sanctioned in the Municipal Corporation. Out of these, 12 sanitary inspectors are currently working and 2 posts of sanitary inspector are vacant. However, since the post of Sanitation Inspector does not fall in the chain promotion, as well as the concerned employee being from the cadre of Class 4 Male Labourer, the designation of all concerned cannot be changed or promoted. The said resolution passed by the Standing Committee of the Municipal Corporation has been requested to be quashed under Section 451 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act by the Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation vide letter dated 29.11.2018.

The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Standing Committee of the Municipal Corporation has passed a resolution to change the designation of six male workers working at the sanitation establishment to sanitary inspectors and to give them the pay scale of that post. The 85. 12.10.2018 has been approved. The posts of male labourer are in the category of Class 4 cadre and the post of sanitary inspector is in the category of Class 3 cadre. Also, the pay scale of both the posts is different. In view of all these facts, the Standing Committee of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 85, c. 12.10.2018 as it is not compoundable and not constitutive, the said resolution is being suspended in the first instance for dissolution under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
If the resolution is to be submitted to the concerned in the matter of suspension, they must submit it to the government within 30 days from the date of the suspension order.
