A proposal was under consideration of the government to create a competent authority to address the transportation challenges in the Nagpur Metropolitan Region and suggest integrated and effective solutions by coordinating all the schemes, initiatives and related agencies. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
With a view to further strengthen the transport system in the Nagpur Metropolitan Region and to suggest measures to meet the transportation challenges through integrated and mutually complementary plans and initiatives in coordination with all the concerned agencies, it is being approved to constitute the Nagpur Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur as follows:

(2) Any person or officer, other than those mentioned in the above paragraph, who is required to attend the meeting of the Nagpur Integrated Metropolitan Transport Authority may be invited by the Chairman or with the permission of the Chairman.
(3) The jurisdiction of the Authority:
The defined geographical area of Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Area will be the jurisdiction of Nagpur Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority. The authority will function under the jurisdiction of Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
(2) The Authority shall co-ordinate with the agencies under it, mainly in the field of transport.
As the Empowered Transport Authority, the Authority shall make decisions and make recommendations with respect to the overall transport plan, modal priorities, infrastructure priorities, financial build-up and allocations and modalities of operation.
However, the said Authority shall not take any decision which would unduly interfere with the autonomy provided by the Constitution to the local bodies.
(5) The Authority may make recommendations on the following matters:
1 All inclusive transport plan for the metropolis.
Coordination of regional and urban development plans and regional transport plans.
3. Modal prioritization and integration.
Prioritization and integration of infrastructure.
5. Selection of implementing agency to implement the infrastructure.
Fast bus service.
Financial planning and allocation.
Implementation mechanism, participation of government and private agencies (PPP).
9. To create consensus among various organizations.
Transport research and knowledge.
Training in the transport sector.

Other functions assigned by Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
(6) The Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall provide staff and technical support to the Nagpur Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority.
(a) To establish the Committee:
The Transport Authority shall constitute the following committees with a view to carrying out in-depth studies on specific issues in the transport sector.
Planning Committee 2. Finance Committee 3. Transport Transport Engineering Committee 4. Transport Operations and Management Committee 5. Regulation, Safety and Environment Committee 6. Vehicle Base Committee 7. Law Committee
The Chairman of the Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall appoint the Chairman and Members of these Committees. In addition to this committee, other committees can be constituted by the transport authority as per the requirement.
(3) The Committee may, with the approval of the Chairperson, invite such persons or institutions as may be necessary to represent it.
These committees will study the issues determined and directed by the transport authority and submit their recommendations to the transport authority.
(8) The Nagpur Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority shall be given statutory existence in due course.
(9) The non-official members shall be paid travel and daily allowance as per rules.
The expenditure on Nagpur Integrated Metropolitan Transport Authority will be borne by Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
