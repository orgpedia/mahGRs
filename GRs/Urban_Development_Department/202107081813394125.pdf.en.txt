Preamble: Considering the increasing scope of the jurisdiction of the Pune Municipal Corporation, the figure of the remaining departments excluding the Primary Education Department of the Pune Municipal Corporation is given in Reference No. 1 was approved by the government. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation, had submitted proposals to the government from time to time under reference letters regarding the increase in the posts of non-teaching officers/ staff and teacher cadre in the primary education department, creation of new posts and approval of the figure.
Therefore, the matter of approving the figure of the Primary Education Department by creating the necessary posts in the Primary Education Department on the establishment of the Pune Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the above mentioned facts, considering the limit of establishment cost of Pune Municipal Corporation, the Government is giving approval for the creation of additional posts as per section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, for the Primary Education Department of Pune Municipal Corporation, in addition to the figure of 989 posts mentioned in Annexure' A' attached with this Government Decision in the non-teaching cadre and a total of 1104 posts in the non-teaching cadre and other cadres as mentioned in Annexure' B'. The details of the posts in the diagram will be as per Annexure A and B attached.
The number of posts in the teacher category, which are approved by the School Education and Sports Department of the State Government, will be fixed according to the approval of the set from time to time according to the number of students.

The Pune Municipal Corporation has also requested for the abolition of a total of 35 posts out of the government sanctioned posts in the primary education department. These positions are not shown in the accompanying prospectus. These positions are approved.
(2) The pattern of the Primary Education Department of the Pune Municipal Corporation is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions: (1) It shall be mandatory for the Pune Municipal Corporation and the Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation to ensure that the pay scale of the posts to be approved in the pattern shall be at par with the government equivalent posts.
(2) If the corporation applies a higher pay scale than the equivalent posts of the government at their level, it will not get the approval of the government under any circumstances.
(3) The Pune Municipal Corporation may submit a proposal to the Government if it is of the opinion that it is necessary to create more posts or additional posts than the number of posts approved in the approved diagram of the Department of Elementary Education in the classification and the establishment cost of the corporation is within the prescribed percentage.
(4) The services which are possible and necessary to be done through out-sourcing should be done through out-sourcing.
(5) The Pune Municipal Corporation and the Commissioner, Pune Municipal Corporation shall ensure that the establishment cost within the prescribed limit of 35% due to the creation of new posts.
