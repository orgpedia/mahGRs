Preamble: - With various metro rail and other ambitious infrastructure projects being implemented in the state under the Urban Development Department, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to open a new head of accounts under capital expenditure to disburse the amount of 1 stamp duty surcharge to be collected in the municipal areas for the ambitious projects notified under the provisions of the Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 and the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
Government Decision: - Various Metro Rail projects as well as other ambitious infrastructure projects are being implemented in the State and the Government hereby approves opening of new consolidated ledger for disbursement of 1 Stamp Duty Surcharge amount to notified ambitious projects as per the provisions of the Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 and the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 in the municipal areas as under: -
