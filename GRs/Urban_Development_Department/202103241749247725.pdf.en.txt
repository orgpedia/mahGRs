The Proposal:
Guidelines for implementation of the District Plan for Urban Dalit Habitat Improvement in Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats have been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 8th February, 2021. In this decision, the government was considering to bring more clarity about the implementation mechanism under this scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The District Planning and Development Committee of the concerned district is being empowered to decide on the implementation mechanism regarding the works to be undertaken from the funds distributed under the Urban Non-Dalit Habitat Improvement District Plan.
The responsibility of maintenance and repair of the works taken up from the funds distributed under the District Plan for Urban Non-Dalit Habitat Improvement shall also be of the concerned operational machinery.
The District Collector will be responsible for this.
Other terms and conditions of the Government Resolution dated 8th February, 2021 will remain unchanged
