The Proposal:
The Central Government has repealed the Urban Land (Maximum Tenancy and Regulation) Act, 1976 under the Nazakdha Repeal Act, 1999. Accepted on 29.11.2007. Section 20 of the Repeal Act protects the following exemption orders for various purposes, the proceedings under it and the proceedings on the lands acquired under section 10 (3) and 10 (5).
In this regard, it was brought to the notice of the Government that the District Collector and the competent authority's office had taken action under Section 10 (3) and 10 (5) of the Act in respect of the lands under Sooty without due diligence and entered the names of the Government in the record of rights of such lands.
As per the provisions of Nazakadha Act, the proceedings under both the sections on the same land are invalid as the additional declared lands are only required to be exempted under section 20 or the proceedings of acquisition under section 10 (3) and 10 (5).
