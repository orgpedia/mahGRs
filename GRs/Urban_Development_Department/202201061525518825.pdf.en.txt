The Proposal:
Due to the increasing urbanization in the state, the work flow in the urban development department is increasing day by day. Also, the number of Nagar Panchayats has increased significantly and new Municipal Authorities have been constituted. Therefore, the high-level committee of secretaries has agreed to create a total of three new posts- Deputy Director General, equivalent to the post of Deputy Secretary, 1 post in the cadre of Town Planning and Deputy Secretary and 2 posts in the cadre of Assistant Director, Town Planning and Under Secretary, equivalent to the post of Under Secretary, to handle the technical work as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966.
After that Ma. The Cabinet also The Cabinet in its meeting held on 15.12.2021 has approved the creation of the posts of Deputy Director and Deputy Secretary in the cadre. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
In view of the formation of new city authorities under the Urban Development Department and the development plans of the growing cities, new posts are being sanctioned in the cadre of Deputy Director, Town Planning and Deputy Secretary and Assistant Director, Town Planning and Under Secretary as shown in the table below on the establishment of the Town Development Department (itself) for the Town Planning Cell.
