Rahul B. Gethe, Medical Officer Group-A, Public Health Department, has been appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation vide Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Administrative Resolution No. 4457, No. Approved on 02.05.2023. Accordingly, a proposal was submitted by the Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation to the Government for his permanent inclusion in the post of" Deputy Commissioner" Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. As per the proposal submitted by the commissioner to the government, a total of 11 posts of" Deputy Commissioner" cadre have been approved on the establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, out of which 5 posts are appointed by the government officers on deputation. It was under consideration of the Government to make one of the available posts out of the said 5 deputation quota available for induction only till Dr. Rahul Gethe continues to hold the said post and thereafter to approve the transfer of the said post to the deputation quota.
