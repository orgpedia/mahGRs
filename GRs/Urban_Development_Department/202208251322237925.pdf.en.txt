The Proposal:
The Cabinet also approved the construction of Mumbai Metro Line-9 (Dahisar to Mira-Bhayander) and Line-7A (Andheri-Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport). Approved on 24/10/2017. Accordingly, the implementation of the said project has been approved vide Reference Government Decision dated 2nd November, 2018. The project is being implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). The project will be funded by Central taxes (50%), State/ Local taxes (100%), land cost and Rs. 1631.24 crore is to be given to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) as a non-interest bearing secondary loan. The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) will be the implementing agency for the project.
