The Proposal:
As Nagpur is the city of the Deputy Capital, it has been decided to sanction special grant-in-aid to the Nagpur Municipal Corporation for the overall development of the city. Accordingly, the matter of opening a new account was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to open a new account head for the scheme" Special Grant for the overall development of Nagpur as it is the city of Deputy Capital" as under:
