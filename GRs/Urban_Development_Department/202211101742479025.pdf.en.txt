The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Decision No. 1& 2 in the context of providing basic infrastructure and raising their standards in the urban areas of the state.
Under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission, the underground sewerage project of Solapur city was granted administrative approval under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2. The matter of approving the inclusion of Goods and Services Tax in the Solapur City Underground Sewer Project submitted to the Government under Reference No. 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagrothan Abhiyan, the underground drainage project of Solapur city has been given administrative approval under the Government Decision No. 1 and 2. The proposal submitted to the Government under Reference No. 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration regarding the inclusion of Goods and Services Tax in the underground sewerage project is being approved by this Government decision as mentioned in the following statement.
