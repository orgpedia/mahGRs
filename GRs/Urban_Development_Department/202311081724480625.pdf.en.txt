The Proposal:
To provide sanitation and toilet facilities in all cities, to process, dispose and recycle used water and waste, to manage all types of solid waste etc. The Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 has been launched under the Government decision dated 15.07.2022 and will be implemented from 2nd October, 2021 to 1st October, 2026.
Under the Mission, funds will be released to the State Governments by the Union Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India. Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Govt. The letter dated 20.10.2022 has informed about the opening of separate SNA accounts component-wise as mentioned below in pursuance of the said mission.
Component 1 Individual household/ community/ public and aspirational toilets.
Part 2- Water management.
Part 3- Management of waste.
Component 4- Information learning dissemination and change in habits.
Component 5- Capacity building, skill development and knowledge management.
The Central Government is allocating funds for the scheme. The Government Circular No. 2021/ PR No. 35/ Koshasra-4, dated 23.03.2021, issued by the Department of Finance, Govt. The procedures prescribed under 01.7.2021 need to be followed precisely. the Central Government
The Directorate of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 is required to budget the funds under it by taking separate headings for the expenditure on the works to be done under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0. The government was considering to open a new accounting head for the state and central share of the funds received under this campaign, component-wise and under it, and municipal corporation, municipal council and town panchayat-wise.
