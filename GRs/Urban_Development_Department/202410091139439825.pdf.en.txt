In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to sub-section (1) of section 456 (a), co-section 79 (a) of section 59 of 1949, the Government of Maharashtra is making the following rules by amending the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation (Transfer of Immovable Property by Tenancy and Renewal of Tenancy) Rules, 2023: -
These rules may be called the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation (Transfer of Immovable Property by Lease and Renewal of Lease) Amendment Rules, 2024.
The Maharashtra Municipal Corporation (Transfer of Immovable Property by Lease and Renewal of Lease) Rules, 2023 will have the following rule instead of rule 6:
6- The corporation and the lessee shall, by mutual agreement, fix the duration of the lease or renewal. The lease term should not exceed thirty years. "

NOTIFICATION
MAHARASHTRA MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS ACT.
No.NAMPA-1221 / CR 166 / UD-26. - In exercise of the powers conferred by proviso to clause (c) of section 79 and sub-section (1) of section 456A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (LIX of 1949), the Government of Maharashtra hereby makes the following rules, further to amend the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations (Transfer of Immovable Property by Lease and Renewal of Lease) Rules, 2023, as follows nominally: -
1. These Rules may be called the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations (Transfer of Immovable Property by Lease and Renewal of Lease) Amendment Rules, 2024.
For rule 6 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations (Transfer of Immovable Property by Lease and Renewal of Lease) Rules, 2028, the following rules shall be substituted, namely: -
Duration of lease - The duration of lease or renewal of lease shall be decided mutually by the Corporation and the lease holder. The total period of lease may not be more than thirty years. "
