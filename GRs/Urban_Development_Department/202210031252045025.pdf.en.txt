Introduction: The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has been published by the Central Government. As per Section-37 of the said Act, 5% of the funds earmarked for persons with disabilities in the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities of the State shall be earmarked for spending on individual as well as group schemes for persons with disabilities. The Government has issued a decision on 10.05.2018.
2. Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities under the Urban Development Department were facing difficulties in spending the remaining 5% of the funds reserved for the welfare of persons with disabilities in the next financial year due to lack of guidelines.
Accordingly, as per the proposal of the Commissioner, Divyang Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune, the matter of issuing guidelines for spending 5% of the unutilized funds reserved for Divyangs in the next financial year for the schemes of Divyangs was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT Circular: As per the proposal of the Commissioner, Differently Abled Welfare, the following instructions are being issued regarding the 5% unutilized funds reserved for the welfare of Divyangjan in Urban Local Bodies under Urban Development Department: (a) The funds reserved for the welfare of Divyangjan in Urban Local Bodies under Urban Development Department should be spent only for the welfare of Divyangjan in the next financial year, if not spent in that financial year.
(b) The Nodal Officer of the concerned Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat as well as the Accounts and Finance Officer will be responsible for spending the said unutilized fund for the welfare of the disabled.
Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats shall take necessary action for the strict implementation of the directions given under the Circular/ Government Decision in respect of the reservation of funds for the welfare of the disabled and for the expenditure thereof.
