Government's decision:
Latur Municipal Corporation's Solid Waste Management Project worth Rs. 33.6286 crore has been approved under Reference No. The decision was approved by the government. While the project was being implemented by the Municipal Corporation, a request was made by the Municipal Corporation to improve certain aspects involved in the project.
The proposal submitted by the Latur Municipal Corporation has been examined at the level of the State Mission Directorate and submitted for the approval of the High Power Committee held on 8/11/2021 and it has been approved by the High Power Committee.
The Government is approving the items and their prices mentioned in the statement accompanying this Government decision in accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee. With this change, the revised cost of the Municipal Solid Waste Detailed Project Report will be as shown in the table 9 of the statement accompanying this Government Decision. The revised Detailed Project Report (DPR) should be funded by the Municipal Council from the 14th Finance Commission funds or from its own funds.
