Government's decision:
The sewerage project (Phase-I) of Gangapur Municipal Council, submitted under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, is being approved by this Government Resolution subject to the conditions and provisions of the relevant Government Resolution and the technical approval given by the Maharashtra Life Authority.
The State Government has decided to implement the Sewerage Project in two phases considering the fund limit for implementing the Sewerage Project and to focus on creating sewage treatment capacity on priority basis. 4) is accepted. According to this policy, in the first phase, the sewage generated at present should be treated on priority by creating a sewage treatment system according to the future needs. The STP capacity required to treat the entire waste water should be sanctioned in the first phase. These include a sewage treatment plant, a sewage treatment plant, a water supply station and a sewage treatment plant. Along with this, arrangements should also be made to collect waste water in some of the different wards of the city.
For the rest of the wards, the waste water from the Trunk Main should be collected in a drain and such a drain should be blocked near the Sewerage Treatment Plant (STP) and the waste water should be taken to the Sewerage Treatment Plant at that place. In the second phase, a sewerage system should be constructed and connected to the sewage treatment system created in the first phase. This phase will include the second phase. This policy has been adopted by the government. Accordingly, in-principle approval is being accorded to the entire sewerage project of Gangapur city for implementation in a phased manner.
