Preamble: The resolutions passed by the general body of Akola Municipal Corporation in the last three years should be examined in this regard. A report was received on 01. Accordingly, the case No. 02 Under the Government letter, the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division was informed with the approval of the Government to conduct a thorough investigation. Accordingly, the Divisional Commissioner, under reference No. 03 Along with the letter, the government has received an inquiry report that a total of 139 resolutions passed by the general assembly of Akola Municipal Corporation in the last three years have been passed in violation of the provisions of the Municipal Corporation Act. Based on the inquiry report, the government had initiated action. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: (1) As a total of 139 resolutions (along with Annexure-A) passed by the General Body of Akola Municipal Corporation during the period of 03 years from 01/01/2018 to 31/12/2020 have been passed against the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, all such resolutions are being temporarily suspended under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. The list of these resolutions is annexed as Annexure-A.
However, on this, an opportunity is being given to make representations to the Municipal Corporation as well as the Commissioner, Akola Municipal Corporation within one month from the date of the order as per Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further action will be taken as per Section 451 (3) of the Act.
