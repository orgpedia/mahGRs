Preamble: The Government have decided to create a post of" Special Executive Officer" on the establishment of Solapur Municipal Corporation. It has been approved by the Government Decision dated 20.10.2000. Also, the number 2. As per the Government Resolution dated 17.12.2007, it has been approved to be called as" Assistant Commissioner" and to be accorded the status as per Section 45 of the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949. Section 45 (2) of the Act provides that the corporation may, with the approval of the State Government, create the post of Assistant Commissioner and appoint a suitable person to such posts. Considering the matter, Solapur Municipal Corporation has passed a resolution under Section 45 (2) of the Act vide Resolution No. 74, d. As on 25.06.2013, it has been approved to appoint Mr. Sunil Narayan Mane, Superintendent of Import Tax to the permanent post of Assistant Commissioner (erstwhile Special Executive Officer (City Extension)) lying vacant with the Municipal Corporation. The proposal has been submitted to the Government on 19.12.2017. In view of this, the government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Commissioner of Solapur Municipal Corporation in his letter No. SAS Cell-1/ Aastha 1/680, Dy. Considering the proposal submitted to the Government under 19.12.2017, the Government is pleased to approve the appointment of Mr. S.N. Mane, Superintendent of Import Tax, Solapur Municipal Corporation to the permanent post of" Assistant Commissioner," as per the provisions of Section 45 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
