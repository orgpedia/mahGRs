The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 25 Municipal Corporations of the State for the month of April and May, 2018 is Rs. 220,68,85, 437.00 crore is under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: For the month of April and May, 2018-19, the total amount of 1% stamp duty of Municipal Corporations is Rs. 220,68,85, 437.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Crores Two Hundred and Sixty Five Lakh Eighty Five Thousand Four Hundred and Thirty Seven only) is approved for disbursement as under.
