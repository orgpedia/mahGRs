Government's decision:
The" Chief Minister Saksham City Competition-2023" at the city level in all Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats and" Chief Minister Saksham Ward Competition-2023" at the ward level in other Municipal Corporations of the State will be held from September 15 to December 15, 2023.
The main features of the competition are:
Chief Minister Competitive City- 2023 will be held in two levels. At the first division level, 3 cities with the highest category-wise merit will be selected and awarded at the division level. The 3 best performing cities will be selected at the state level and awarded.
2. Mukhyamantri Saksham Ward Competition-2023 will be held at the state level, in which 3 wards with the highest merit category will be selected after evaluation at the state level and they will be given state level prizes.
All eligible cities are required to participate in the competition.
The competition will be held from September 15 to December 15, 2023.
The criteria and marks of the Chief Minister Competent City Competition will remain as mentioned in the Statement-1 of this Government Decision.
6. The criteria and marks of the Competitive Competitions of the Competent Wards shall be as mentioned in the Statement-2 of the Government Decision.
7. The expenditure for the competition should be met from the self-funds of the Urban Local Bodies, CSR funds and public contributions.
