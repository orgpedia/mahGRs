The Proposal:
The green belt development project of Bhiwandi-Nizampur town, which was included in the plan for the financial year 2015-16 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, was accorded administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Subsequently, the matter of revising the administrative approval of the Green Area Development Project of Bhiwandi Nizampur City was under the consideration of the Government in view of the change in the cost of the components of the project.
Government's decision:
The administrative approval granted under the Government Resolution dated 5th May, 2017 to the Greenfield Development Project of Bhiwandi-Nizampur City included in the State Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government during the financial year 2015-16 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, subject to the conditions and provisions contained in the relevant Government Resolution, is as under: 2 is being amended as mentioned in the prospectus.
