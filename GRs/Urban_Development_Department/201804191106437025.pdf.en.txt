The project is being implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) and the Thane Municipal Corporation.

Government's decision:
As per the decision, the Thane Municipal Corporation will take up the construction of a creek bridge between Vitawa and Kopri in the jurisdiction of the Thane Municipal Corporation, and the widening of the road between Kalwa and Atmaram Patil Chowki and the service road.
If the Thane Municipal Corporation needs technical assistance for the construction of this bridge, the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority should provide technical assistance as per the requirement.
