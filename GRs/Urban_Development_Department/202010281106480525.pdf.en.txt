Government's decision:
The Directorate of Town Planning and Assessment has issued the Parts of Town Development Plan and Town Planning Plan etc., to the public and Government Offices (excluding State Government Offices) vide Charter, Reference No. 1 here. The rates were being provided as per the Government decision dated 27.06.1984.
Thereafter, in view of the increase in the cost of paper used by the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation for preparation of part maps/ charter of town planning etc. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 07.02.2000, the revised rates were revised for the next three years.
Now, the proposal to increase the rate was under consideration due to increase in the cost of paper for preparing part maps/ zone maps for the citizens/ stakeholders as per the regional plan and development plan approved by the concerned branch office in all the districts under the Directorate of Town Planning, increase in the salary of employees etc. The Government is now approving the revised rates for making available (a) Portion Maps/ Zone Registers to the public through offline mode and (b) Portion Maps/ Zone Registers to the public through online mode, as mentioned in the attached prospectus, on the condition that the revised rates are revised for the next three years or earlier, if deemed necessary.
4. This Order is in Informal Reference No. 85/-, Rs. being issued with their concurrence as on 15.05.2020.
