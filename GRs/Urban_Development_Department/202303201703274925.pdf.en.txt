Introduction Under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), the detailed project reports of solid waste management of a total of 152 Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) comprising 9 Municipal Corporations and 143 Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats have been prepared. The administrative approval was granted in accordance with the Government Decision No. 4-155.
The project is being implemented by the Urban Local Bodies and the concerned Urban Local Bodies were requested to approve the new items to those cities excluding some of the items included in the project.
The proposal submitted by a total of 152 urban local bodies was examined at the level of the State Mission Directorate. It was submitted for approval in the meeting dated 3/3/2023 of the High Powered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
As per the above approval of the High Powered Committee, the Government was considering granting revised administrative approval to the Detailed Project Report of the said 152 Urban Local Bodies by granting new items to those cities excluding some items included in the approved Detailed Project Report.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As mentioned in the Preamble, in pursuance of the approval given by the High Powered Committee to the proposal submitted by the said 152 Urban Local Bodies, the Government hereby approves the deletion of the items of value mentioned in Ranks 4 and 5 respectively and the inclusion of the items of value mentioned in Ranks 7 and 8 respectively in place of the items mentioned in the accompanying Return.
5. With the above change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the concerned Urban Local Bodies for solid waste will be as shown in the Annexure 11 of the Statement A accompanying this Government Resolution.
