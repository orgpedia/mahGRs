The Central Government has allowed mobile towers under the Ease of Doing Business with regard to the inclusion of this service under the Right to Public Service Act, 2015 and the documents required for this service, fees, fixed time limit, designated officers as well as first and second appeals.
The proposal to notify the officers etc. was under consideration of the Government, accordingly, the Government is taking the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Mobile Tower Permission is to be notified by all the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats of the State as per Section 3 of the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015 as per the statement' A' and' B' attached with the service.
The relevant public authorities (Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats) will not be able to change the service requirement documents mentioned in the prospectus' A' and' B' accompanying this decision.
The concerned public authorities will be able to fix the fee to be charged for providing services under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 and the posts of Designated Officer, First Appellate Officer and Second Appellate Officer etc. can be revised by the concerned public authorities taking into account the structure of the concerned urban local body.
All the Commissioners, Municipal Corporations and Chief Officers, Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats of the State shall take action to notify and implement the service as per Section 3 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015.
