Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored" Suvarna Jayanti Shahari Rozgar Yojana" is being restructured and converted into" National Urban Livelihoods Mission" and implemented in the State. Under the Mission, for the year 2022-2023, an amount of Rs. 85.00 lakh has been sought to be released by the Deputy Commissioner, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai, vide letter under reference.
Budgetary provision for salaries for 2022-2023 has been made and the matter of disbursement of salary subsidy for the posts working under National Urban Livelihood Mission (NULM) was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-2023, the Department of Finance has released Rs. 85.00 lakh is being disbursed to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration and State Mission Director, National Urban Livelihood Mission.
