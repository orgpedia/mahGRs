Government's decision:
Under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), the project of the price indicated in Rakana 4 of the urban solid waste management statement of Chandrapur Municipal Corporation was approved as per the government decisions at Reference No. 2.
While the project was being implemented by the corporation, a request was made by the corporation to approve new items excluding some of the items included in the project. (The exclusions and costs of the approved projects are set out in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively of the Statement accompanying this Government Decision.) The items to be approved and their cost in place of the excluded items are indicated in Rakaana 7 and 8 respectively.
The proposal submitted by Chandrapur Municipal Corporation was examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Power Committee held on 25.02.2021.
The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
Accordingly, in accordance with the approval of the High Powered Committee to the said proposal, the Government hereby approve the deletion of the items of value mentioned in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively and the deletion of the items of value mentioned in paragraphs 7 and 8 respectively in the accompanying statement.
5. With the above change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the Chandrapur Municipal Corporation Solid Waste will be as shown in para 10 of the statement accompanying this Government Resolution.
6. It shall be obligatory to comply with the terms and conditions mentioned in the administrative approval decision at Reference No. 2.
