Preamble: The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation's Revised Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 have been amended vide Reference No. 2, here. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019. In this service entry rule, there is a condition of 5 years of service in the lower cadre for promotion. In the state government, generally, 3 years of experience in a lower cadre post is required to be eligible for promotion. Accordingly, the revised Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation have been amended to provide for 3 years experience in lower cadre post for promotion. The decision was approved by the government.
Before the 2014 Entry into Service Rules of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, Diploma holders have been appointed as Junior Engineers as the degree/ diploma in engineering branch of a recognized university is a condition for the appointment of Junior Engineer. In view of the change in the Service Entry Rules of 2019, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, has issued a relevant notification regarding the change in the Service Entry Rules, as the condition of a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering for promotion to the post of' Additional City Engineer' has been kept, 3 officers currently working in the post of' Executive Engineer' have not held a degree from a recognized university, and since they are diploma holders, the opportunity for promotion has been closed. The proposal was submitted to the Government for approval vide letter dated 07.09.2022.
The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019. 28.05.2019 and D. As per the Government Decision dated 25.11.2021, for the post of' Additional City Engineer' on the establishment of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation, it has been provided that an officer who has completed at least 3 years of service in the post of' Executive Engineer' and holds an engineering degree of a recognized university can be appointed by promotion on the basis of merit and merit. Therefore, one of the officers currently serving in the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation establishment in the post of' Executive Engineer' is a diploma holder, who has completed 15 years of regular service in the post of' Executive Engineer', but is still in the post of' Additional City Engineer'.

By making it mandatory to hold a degree for promotion, the opportunity of promotion of a serving engineer has been closed. Since the other 2 officers are also diploma holders, it will not be possible to promote them as per the provisions of the Service Entry Rules. Accordingly, in view of the provisions of the Municipal Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019, regarding the provision of opportunity for promotion to the said 3 officers as per the proposal of the Municipal Corporation, it is decided to appoint the concerned Deputy Commissioners.
