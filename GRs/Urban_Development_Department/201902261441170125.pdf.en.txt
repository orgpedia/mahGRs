The Proposal:
Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation was established on 28.02.2002.
Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation is a category D Municipal Corporation with a total area of 79.40 sq km. According to the 2011 census, the population of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation is more than 12 lakh.
A total of 2442 posts have been sanctioned on the establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation in the past under various government decisions. The General Assembly of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 103.
The Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has approved their design as on 30.01.2016. As per the proposal dated 05.03.2016, the layout of the Municipal Corporation has been submitted to the Government for approval. The proposal was being examined at the government level.
Thereafter, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation was informed to submit the revised figure for the required posts. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, referred to the above. The revised figure vide letter dated 24.12.2018 has been submitted to the Government for approval.
