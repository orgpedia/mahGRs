The government's decision:
Under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), Jalgaon Municipal Corporation's Urban Solid Waste Management project at the price indicated in the statement of Rakana 4 was approved as per the Government decisions at Reference No. 2.
While the project was being implemented by the corporation, a request was made by the corporation to approve new items excluding some of the items included in the project. (The exclusions and costs of the approved projects are set out in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively of the Statement accompanying this Government Decision.) The items to be approved and their cost in place of the excluded items are indicated in Rakaana 7 and 8 respectively. )
The proposal submitted by Jalgaon Municipal Corporation was examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Power Committee held on 25.02.2021. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
