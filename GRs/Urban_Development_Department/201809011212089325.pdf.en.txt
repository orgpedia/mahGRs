Date is September 01, 2018.
In the fourth meeting of the Authority, the Authority has approved a plan to take concrete programs to determine the water requirement for the Pune Metropolitan Region, how it will be supplied and how much water will be needed in the future, to prepare a plan for the next long-term policy on water planning, management and control, considering the limited availability of water and the future shortage of water, and to consider the reuse of water. Pune Water Supply and Sewerage Board The Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority vide letter dated 06.06.2018 has requested for constitution of such a Committee by approving the constitution of a Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Urban Development.
Accordingly, the Government has decided to constitute a Water Distribution and Water Utilization Committee for the Pune Metropolitan Region by the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, considering the water availability for the Pune Metropolitan Region.
The government has approved the formation of a water storage allocation and utilization committee in the Pune metropolitan region.
The Committee shall consist of the following members:
