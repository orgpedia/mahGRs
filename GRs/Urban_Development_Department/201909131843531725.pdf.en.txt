Preamble: In order to provide basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the State and to improve their quality, in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision at Reference No. 1 and accordingly, the Government of the State under Reference No. As per the decision of the government issued under section 2, 3 and 4, the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Nagarotna Maha Abhiyan is being implemented in the state.
As per the Government decision dated 21.8.2014 of Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarotthan Mahavidyan, the duration of the Mission was five years from the date of the Government decision. Accordingly, the period of this campaign has come to an end on 20.08.2019. As per the provisions of the said decision, the Government was expected to review the progress of the Mission and take a decision on continuation of the scheme.
Accordingly, as per the provisions of the decision of the Government in the context, the increasing response from urban local bodies to the Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan scheme, the need to complete the remaining projects approved under the campaign in order to provide facilities to the citizens, the scheme is a comprehensive single scheme for small and medium urban infrastructure projects, considering the current demand in line with the mission, the government was considering to review the campaign and decide on its continuation.
Government's decision:
In the light of the above mentioned in the Preamble, the Government has taken the following decisions in pursuance of the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahabhiyana.

Government Resolution No: Nagarro-2019/ P.S. No.243/ NV-33
(a) The duration of the Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarothana Mahabhiyana is 5 years as per the Government decision dated 21.8.2014. In view of the revision of the scheme mentioned in the above Preamble, the scheme is being extended for a further period of 5 years with effect from 20.8.2019.
Subsequently, the Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarotna Abhiyan will be reviewed and a decision will be taken according to the situation.
