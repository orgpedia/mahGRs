As per the Government Decision dated 30 th September, 2022 and taking into consideration the guidelines issued by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India, a Committee is being constituted at the Zonal level and State level to scrutinize the performance of the cities during the period of" City Beautification and Sanitation Competition-2022."

The Commissioner/ Principal of the cities which are to participate in the City Beautification Competition shall prepare a presentation of 15 slides regarding the work done by that city during the competition period by classifying the work done in accordance with the four points mentioned in paragraph 3 and submit it to the Division Level Scrutiny Committee headed by the Divisional Commissioner of their division with the necessary information. Each of these four subjects will carry 25% marks.
After receiving the presentation, the department-level screening committee of each division will scrutinize the proposals and if necessary, visit the cities and receive them in their division.
The report of one urban local body securing the highest marks in each of the categories mentioned in the above paragraph 2 of the proposals shall be submitted to the State Level Evaluation Committee along with the recommendation.
The State Level Evaluation Committee shall scrutinize the proposals received from the Block Level Screening Committees of all the departments and prepare a category wise list of awardee cities.
