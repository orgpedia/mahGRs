The Director, Local Fund Audit is responsible for certifying the annual accounts of all Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State as per the provisions of the Act/ Rules/ Government Decisions indicated in the above table. However, as per the testimony of the Public Accounts Committee, the work of certifying the annual accounts of various urban local bodies is pending on a large scale. The Directorate of Local Fund Audit has brought to the notice of the Government that the annual accounts of the concerned Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats are not being submitted for authentication. Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:
Government circular:
In order to clear the backlog of annual accounts, all the Commissioners, Municipal Corporations and Principal Commissioners, Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats shall submit their accounts for authentication to the Director, Directorate of Local Fund Audit, Belapur, Navi Mumbai.

The Director, Directorate of Local Fund Audit shall plan a time-bound programme for validation of pending annual accounts of Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities and communicate the same to all the concerned Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities and a copy of the same shall be forwarded to the Government.
3. The detailed procedure for submission of accounts for authentication and the documents required to be submitted along with the same shall be prescribed by the Director, Directorate of Local Fund Audit, Belapur, Navi Mumbai and shall be circulated to all the Urban Local Bodies. Accordingly, the concerned Urban Local Bodies should submit their proposals.
4. The process of authentication of accounts of all urban local bodies should be completed in a time-bound manner.
In this regard, the Director, Directorate of Local Fund Audit, Belapur, Navi Mumbai, shall ensure that the progress report of the work of authentication of accounts as well as the details of non-responding Urban Local Bodies are submitted to the Principal Secretary, Urban Development Department, (Navi-2).
