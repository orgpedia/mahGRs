Preamble: Implementation of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission in Context No. The government has been in power in the state since 2015-16. Under this campaign, the construction of water supply, sewerage, storm water drains, urban transport and green area development will be done in the cities. Accordingly, the Central Government has approved the State Annual Action Plan for the financial year 2016-17 under this Mission, which includes the Green Area Development Project of Panvel City.
The revised Detailed Project Report of the Green Area Development Project included in the plan for the financial year 2016-17 under the AMRUT Mission was prepared by the Panvel Municipal Corporation. The project has been given technical approval by the executive engineer, Panvel Municipal Corporation. The matter of administrative approval of the revised green area development project of Panvel city included in the State Annual Action Plan for the financial year 2016-17 of AMRUT Mission was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The revised Green Area Development Project of Panvel City included in the State Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government in the financial year 2016-17 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Decision under the following paragraph No. The administrative approval is being given by this government decision as mentioned in the prospectus in 2.
