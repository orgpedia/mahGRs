4.8. Mode of disbursement: As per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission, the Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%). 4.9 While setting up self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on the operational outcomes. Also, the project will not be funded if it does not meet the functional outcome. The Commissioner, Bhiwandi Nizampur Municipal Corporation will be fully responsible for fulfilling these requirements.
