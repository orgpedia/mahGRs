The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 26 Municipal Corporations in the State will result in an accumulation of Rs. 105,00,00, 000/- to the Municipal Corporations is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The total amount to be released to the Municipal Corporations within the limit of subsidy available on Budget Distribution System for disbursement of 1st installment of 1% Stamp Duty for the year 2020-21 is Rs. 105,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Five Crores only) is hereby approved by the Government as under:
