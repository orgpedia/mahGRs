Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in the government service for the disabled. The central government has given. List of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, may be seen in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in and in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification, the list of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on the website. Published on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In pursuance of the directions contained in the said Government Resolution, it was under the consideration of the Government to ensure the post of' peon' in the Group-D cadre of Urban Development Department (itself), Mantralaya, Mumbai Division and to ensure that the list of seats in the Government service ensured for Divyang at intervals not exceeding 3 years should be reviewed and the posts should be reserved for the newly Divyang category.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification to ensure the posts of the posts under the State Government as per Section-33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published. It states that in respect of the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are the same as those in the State Government service. Although the designations of such posts are different, the posts in the state government service will be ensured for the disabled, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance said. The following are the Government Decisions dated 02.02.2021.
