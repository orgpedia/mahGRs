The Proposal:
Reference to the Service Entry Rules of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019. In this service entry rule, a condition of 5 years of service in the lower cadre has been prescribed for promotion. To be eligible for promotion in the state government, there is a condition of service of 3 years in the post of lower cadre. This condition has been included in the service entry rules of many municipal corporations.
Due to the condition of 5 years of service in the lower cadre for promotion in the service entry rule of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, some employees do not get promotion opportunities at the establishment.
Similarly, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued a notification regarding the revision of eligibility and appointment procedure for promotion to the post of Executive Engineer as approved in the Service Entry Rules of 2019 of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation. The proposal has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 17.06.2021. Accordingly, regarding the approval of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Service Entry Rules 2019, the The matter of amendment in respect of eligibility and mode of appointment for appointment to the posts mentioned in Annexure-II to the Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019 was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Ref. In the" Annexure-II" attached with the Service Entry Rules of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation approved under the Government Decision dated 28.05.2019, the Government is approving the condition of 3 years of service in lieu of the 5 years of service in the lower cadre for promotion in the authority under section 454, 455 and 465 (1) (l) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949. Similarly, amendment in the eligibility criteria for promotion to the post of Executive Engineer is being approved under Annexure-II, Schedule-A attached.
