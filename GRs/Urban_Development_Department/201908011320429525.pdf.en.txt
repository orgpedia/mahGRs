Government's decision:
In line with the above, the Government has decided to constitute a" Working Group" as follows: :
(a) The working group shall be as follows:
(1) The Director, CSG, All India Institute of Local Self Government, Mumbai. Chairman (2) Advisor, Centre for Safety Engineering, IIT, Gandhinagar. Member (3) Surveyor and Loss Assessor Member (4) Joint Secretary, Urban Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai Member (5) Chief Fire Officer and Director, State Fire Service Member
(b) The Working Group shall study the issue in a holistic manner and submit its recommendations to the Government within a period of three months.
(a) No remuneration shall be payable to the Chairman or a member.
