The Parbhani City Municipal Corporation has submitted the" Parbhani City Municipal Corporation Services (Entry of Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2015" for the approval of the Government under Section 457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The composition of the posts of Parbhani City Municipal Corporation has been decided by the Government Decision dated 28.5.2013. For the sake of smoothness and administrative convenience in the posts in the Municipal Corporation, it is necessary to adjust some posts in this figure to other posts, as well as to change the designation of some posts. The details are given in Schedule A.
3. The said Rules may be called the" Parbhani City Municipal Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2021."
(4) These rules shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
