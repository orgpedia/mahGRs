Preamble: - Subsidized grants are distributed to these Municipal Councils on account of abolition of excise duty and dearness allowance of Municipal Councils in the State. For this purpose, out of ₹3261.4824 crore budgeted for the financial year 2025-2026, ₹1663.356 crore has been made available on the Budget Estimates, Allocations and Control System.
Now, the Directorate of Municipal Administration has to disburse the funds of ₹249.7056 crore made available by the Finance Department out of the original budgeted provision through the BEAMS system.
Government Decision: - Tacit is a grant-in-aid to the Municipal Councils due to the abolition of excise duty and dearness allowance of the Municipal Councils in the State. For the year 2025-2026, out of the budgeted funds for this purpose, a grant-in-aid of 249.7056 crore received through the distribution system is being disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Council Administration for distribution to the respective Municipal Councils / Nagar Panchayats / Municipal Corporations.
