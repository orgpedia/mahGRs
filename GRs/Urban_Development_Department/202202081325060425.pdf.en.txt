Government's decision:
The Hinganghat Municipal Council's Solid Waste Management Project worth Rs. 4.6318 crore has been approved under Reference No. The decision was approved by the government. While the project was being implemented by the Municipal Council, a request was made by the Municipal Council to amend certain aspects involved in the project.
The proposal submitted by Hinganghat Municipal Council has been examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Power Committee held on 8/11/2021 and it has been approved by the High Power Committee.
The Government is approving the items and their prices mentioned in the statement accompanying this Government decision in accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee. With this change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the Municipal Council's Solid Waste will be as shown in the table in the accompanying statement. The revised Detailed Project Report (DPR) should be funded by the Municipal Council from the 14th Finance Commission funds or from its own funds.
