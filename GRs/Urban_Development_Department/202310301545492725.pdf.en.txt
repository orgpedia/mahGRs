Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC): Implementation of Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015. In the meeting held under the chairmanship of the Chief Commissioner, State Right to Service Commission, it was directed to appoint a study group of expert persons of the department to re-engineer the business process of the notified services under the Urban Development Department. Pursuant to the said direction, a Study Group was constituted for services rendered by CIDCO, MMRDA and other Regional Development Corporations as per (1) of the Circular to the Government in question.
Instead of Shri Kailash Shinde, Joint Managing Director, CIDCO as indicated in clause 2, it should be read as Shri Shantanu Goyal, Joint Managing Director, CIDCO.
