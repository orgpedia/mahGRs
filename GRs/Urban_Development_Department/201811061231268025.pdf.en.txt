The Director, Town Planning, Pune is hereby informed that action should be taken to bring the draft seniority list to the notice of the concerned authorities.
Officers of the cadre of Deputy Director (Group-A, Gazetted) on establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation are advised to verify the details mentioned in the draft seniority list. Also, if there are any objections/ suggestions/ objections in the said seniority list, they should be submitted to the government through the Director, Town Planning, Pune, within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
If objections/ suggestions/ objections are not received within the stipulated time regarding the draft seniority list, the seniority list will be published finally, assuming that the concerned do not have any say in it.
