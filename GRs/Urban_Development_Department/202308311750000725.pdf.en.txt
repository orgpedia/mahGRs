The Proposal:
In paragraph 2 of the Government Decision dated 05/02/2019, referred to at Roll No. 1 in the above context, the Junior Engineer working as a daily wager in various Municipal Councils of the State was approved for induction by giving the option of induction in the post of Clerk, if the option is accepted by the concerned employees. In the meantime, the claims of these employees were filed in various courts, in which orders have been passed in the following court cases.
1. m. Industrial Court, Jalna vide Complaint (ULP) No. 145/2011, Dy. 30.03.2015
2. m. The Nagpur bench of the Bombay High Court in its writ petition No. 6374/ 2005, Dt. 01.07.2017 3. Ma. The High Court, Aurangabad Bench, in Writ Petition No. 10084/ 2016, Dt. 07.12.2018
4. Ma. The Nagpur bench of the Bombay High Court, vide its order No. 3537/2019 D. 14.01.2020

In order to maintain uniformity in the regularization of the 8 Junior Engineers working in various Municipal Councils of the State after considering the above court cases, the Government has already approved the Government vide Grants dated 10/12/2021 and 10/08/2022 mentioned in the aforesaid Reference 3 and 5 and the order has been implemented with effect from 10/12/2021. Now the matter of approving the government decision regarding the inclusion of the 8 junior engineers in that post was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
In view of the decisions of various courts regarding Junior Engineers working as daily wagers in various Municipal Councils of the State, the Government, as per the decision taken by the Government under the Government Resolution dated 05/02/2019 at Reference Wall No. 1, hereby approves the following 8 Junior Engineers to be included in the post of" Junior Engineer" instead of accepting the option of clerical post and to maintain uniformity in the regularization of their services, subject to the conditions mentioned below.

Terms and Conditions:
1) The 8 junior engineers should be appointed to the posts currently vacant out of the 25 percent posts to be filled from the eligible employees of the municipal councils as per the service entry rules fixed for the post of junior engineer in the municipal councils of the state.
(2) As the posts in which these engineers are working belong to the state cadre, they will have to apply where there are vacant posts in the state and they will be eligible for transfer, this should be brought to the notice of the said engineers and with their written consent, the process of regularization should be done by the Directorate of Municipal Administration, Belapur, Navi Mumbai.
(3) The service of the said engineers from the first appointment shall be deemed to be payable only on retirement. Also, their salary should be fixed considering that the appointment was made on that day. as well
