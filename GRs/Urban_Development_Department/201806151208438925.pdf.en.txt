The Proposal:
Prakash Sahebrao Gaikwad was working as a senior clerk in the property department in the Sangli-Miraj-Kupwad city municipal office. While working in this post, he was given a salary due to the financial loss of the Municipal Corporation due to negligence and negligence in the work of daily market fee collection, rent collection, etc. On 14.05.2009 he was suspended from the service of the Municipal Corporation. However, they were later subjected to a departmental inquiry. Restored to service by order dated 06/03/2010.
A departmental inquiry was initiated against Mr. Prakash Sahebrao Gaikwad, Senior Clerk and charges were framed against him in the suspension case. As 4 out of the 5 charges were proved by the departmental inquiry, Mr. Prakash Sahebrao Gaikwad was held responsible for the financial loss of the Municipal Corporation and brought to the basic pay of the post of Senior Clerk as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
3. Mr. Prakash Sahebrao Gaikwad against the punishment given to him. Industrial Court at Sangli ULP No. 23/2011 filed a complaint. Subsequently, Mr. Gaikwad. Retired from the service of Municipal Corporation on 31.05.2011. 23/2011 by the Court. Decision dated 25.10.2012. According to the On 30.08.2013, Shri. Gaikwad was penalised to be brought on the basic pay scale and accordingly, he was paid a stipend and pension was started.
4. Mr. Prakash Sahebrao Gaikwad filed an appeal before the Standing Committee after his retirement against the punishment given to him. The Standing Committee on 21, d. 26.11.2013 was approved as follows:
The decision to promote Mr. Gaikwad on the basic pay scale is wrong and unjustified and therefore, the meeting is cancelling the decision and accepting the appeal made by Mr. Gaikwad to the Standing Committee. Mr. Gaikwad has been given his original post as the punishment given by the Commissioner has been revoked.

Government's decision:
Standing Committee of Sangli Miraj Kupwad Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. The 21st. 26/11/2013 has been suspended prima facie under the provisions of Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, as it is against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation as well as the larger public interest.
As per Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the concerned can make a representation to the state government within one month from the date of the said decision. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further appropriate action will be taken as per Section 451 (3) of the Act.
