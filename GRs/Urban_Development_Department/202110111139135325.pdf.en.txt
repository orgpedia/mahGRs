List of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, may be seen in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in and in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification, the list of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on the website. Published on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In pursuance of the directions in the said Government Resolution, it was under the consideration of the Government to ensure that the posts in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D on the establishment of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority and the list of seats in the Government service ensured for the disabled should be reviewed after a period of not more than 3 years to ensure that the posts are reserved for the newly disabled category.
