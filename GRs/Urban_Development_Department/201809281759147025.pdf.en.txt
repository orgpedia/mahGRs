Introduction: The implementation of Goods and Services Tax (GST) across the country has started from 1st July, 2017 as notified by the Central Government. Accordingly, the implementation of Goods and Services Tax has also started in the State and the entry tax, duty, local body tax, cess or any other tax levied and collected under the earlier entry 52 of List II (State List) of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India has been discontinued. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax (Compensation to Local Authorities) Act, 2017 has come into force with effect from 1st July, 2017. (1) The Finance Department has published in the Government Gazette of Maharashtra.
