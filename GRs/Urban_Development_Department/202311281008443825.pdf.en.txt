The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Abhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and raising their standards.
The sewerage project of Sillod city under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission was granted administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 2. Sillod City Sewerage Project submitted to the Government under reference No. 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration
The matter of approving the revision of the rate of Goods and Services Tax was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval has been granted to the Sillod City Sewerage Project under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagrothan Abhiyan as per the Government Resolution No. 2. The proposal submitted to the Government under reference No. 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration regarding the revision of the rate of Goods and Services Tax in the sewerage project is being approved through this Government Decision as mentioned in the following statement.
