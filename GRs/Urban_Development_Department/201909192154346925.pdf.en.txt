The Proposal:
Latur City Municipal Corporation was established. 25.10.2011. Latur City Municipal Corporation is a Class D Municipal Corporation with a total area of 32.49 km2.
That's it. As per the 2011 census, the city has a population of 3,82,940.
Due to the infrastructure being created in the Latur City Municipal Corporation area day by day, various projects being carried out by the state and central governments, the ever-growing population, the increasing expectations of the citizens about the welfare of the people and transparent work by the municipal administration, the relevant government has already decided on the establishment of the municipal corporation in accordance with the administrative process of creating/ eliminating/ ensuring or modifying the figure and preparing the service access rules for these posts. As on 28.5.2013, a total of 1,246 posts have been sanctioned for various departments of Latur City Municipal Corporation. However, the service entry rules for the posts at the establishment of the Latur City Municipal Corporation were not prepared. Therefore, the corporation was facing difficulties in recruiting services due to the absence of the corporation's service entry rules.
Therefore, it was necessary to formulate service entry rules for the officers/ employees of the Municipal Corporation.
Accordingly, the Government vide Government Circular No. 2002/821/ No. 86/ NO. Model Service Entry Rules for Municipal Officers/ Employees have been framed as on 02.07.2008. Based on this model service access rule, the service access rule of Latur City Municipal Corporation was approved by the general meeting. Resolution No. 23.9.2015 64 has been approved. Following the reference. As on 9.5.2016, Latur City Municipal Corporation has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval of the said Service Access Rules. Therefore, the matter of approving the proposal on the basis of the said service access rule submitted by the Latur City Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Latur City Municipal Corporation has submitted to the Government for approval under Section-457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.

The details of adjustment of certain posts in the said figure to other posts and change in the designation of certain posts for the purpose of administrative convenience and for the purpose of streamlining the posts in the Municipal Corporation are shown in the accompanying Schedule-A. It has been approved by the government.
