The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 26 Municipal Corporations in the State will result in an accumulation of Rs. 167, 32, 13, 560/- to the Municipal Corporations is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The total amount to the Municipal Corporations within the limit of subsidy available on Budget Distribution System for the year 2019-20 on account of 1% stamp duty is Rs. 167, 32, 13, 560/- (Rupees One Hundred and Sixty Two Crores Thirty Two Lakhs Thirteen Thousand Five Hundred and Sixty only) is hereby approved by the Government as under:

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations. The Collector should withdraw the grant without any deduction from the amount due to the Municipalities (without informing the Government) and immediately disburse it to the concerned Municipal Corporations.
The expenditure to be incurred under the head" Demand No.F-4,3604, Payment of Compensation and Stamped Amounts to Local Bodies and Panchayat Raj Institutions, 102, Stamp Duty (01) Stamp Duty due to abolition of VAT (01) (01) Stamp Duty due to restoration of VAT in lieu of Local Body Tax (36040924) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) to Municipal Corporations in respect of 1% surcharge on Stamp Duty in Municipal Corporations area" shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2019-2020.
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be separately accounted for by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
