The Nashik Municipal Corporation has six divisional offices, sub-offices, hospitals, dispensaries, medical, street lights, roads, education, water and sanitation facilities. According to the 2011 census, the city has a population of 14.86 lakh. Considering the increasing number of urban settlements, there is a huge increase in the demand for providing civic amenities to the citizens. Therefore, the provision and maintenance of facilities by the municipal corporation is increasing. Besides, considering the requirement of officers and employees for the implementation of various schemes, the corporation had submitted proposals from time to time for the creation of posts at the establishment.
As per the directions issued by the government, the Nashik Municipal Corporation has to create new departments such as traffic planning, operationalizing the urban police station for encroachment elimination, implementing the national hawking policy, disaster management. Nashik city has been selected under the Smart City project of the central government.
In view of the above, the nature of work of all the departments of the Municipal Corporation and the requirement of officers and employees required by those departments, the creation of new posts in various cadres on the establishment will be submitted from time to time.

After reviewing the proposals made, a department-wise diagram of the posts required in Group-A to Group-D of the Municipal Corporation has been prepared, which has been approved by the General Assembly Resolution No. 586, D. Approval has been granted as on 14.10.2016.
The matter of creating essential posts on the establishment of Nashik Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the proposals submitted by the Commissioner, Nashik Municipal Corporation, vide letters under reference, at present, the approval of the Government under Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act is being accorded for creation of a total of 635 new posts on the establishment of Nashik Municipal Corporation as mentioned in the Annexure-A attached to this Government Decision and for sanctioning of 05 posts as mentioned in Annexure-B by way of adjustment.
The newly created posts on the establishment of Nashik Municipal Corporation, after the service entry rules of these posts are finalized and if the establishment cost of the Municipal Corporation is within the prescribed limit, then these posts can be filled by the Municipal Corporation.
