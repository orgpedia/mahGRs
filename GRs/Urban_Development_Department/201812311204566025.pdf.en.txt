The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide for levy of stamp duty on sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958), if any such deed is in respect of immovable property situated in the city, in the case of sale or deed of gift, in respect of such property situated in the city.
A surcharge at the rate of one per cent shall be levied on the value and, in the case of a leveraged mortgage, on the amount secured by that instrument introduced in the contract. Accordingly, the 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to the newly formed Panvel Municipal Corporation in the state for the month of April 2018 to November 2018 is Rs. 43,68,43, 416.00 crore to Panvel Municipal Corporation is under consideration.
Government's decision:
For the month of April 2018 to November 2018, the total amount of 1% stamp duty of Panvel Municipal Corporation is Rs. 43,68,43, 416.00 crore (Rupees Thirty Three Crores Sixty Two Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Four Hundred and Sixteen only) as follows:

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for disbursement of this grant to the above-mentioned Municipal Corporation. The Collector should withdraw this grant from the treasury and immediately distribute it to the concerned Municipal Corporations.
The expenditure to be incurred under the head" Demand No.F-4,3604, Payment of Compensation and Stamped Amounts to Local Bodies and Panchayat Raj Institutions, 102, Stamp Duty (01) Stamp Duty due to abolition of Tax (01) Stamp Duty due to restoration of Tax in lieu of Local Body Tax (01) 1% Surcharge on Stamp Duty in the Municipal Corporation Area with a view to provide for Municipal Corporation (36040924) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary)" shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2018-2019.
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be separately accounted for by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
