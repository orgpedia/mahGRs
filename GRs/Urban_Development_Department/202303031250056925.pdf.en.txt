The Proposal:
The Commissioner, Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, had submitted a proposal to the government under a letter of reference to approve the setting up of a loan bond of Rs 200 crore to implement various development projects in connection with the revival of the Mula river in Pimpri Chinchwad city. It was for the government to approve it.
Government's decision:
The project will be implemented in connection with the revival of the Mula river in Pimpri Chinchwad city. It is expected to cost Rs 750 crore. Accordingly, the standing committee of the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and the municipal assembly have approved a budgetary amount of Rs 750 crore for the Mula River Project. Accordingly, the approval of the State Government is being given under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act to raise bonds up to Rs 200 crore in the initial phase in connection with the said project.
The responsibility of repaying the loan amount and interest on it raised through bonds will be completely with the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation. There is no guarantee from the government.
2. For repayment of bonds, the Municipal Corporation should open an escrow account in a nationalised bank and ensure that repayment is made on time. There is no help from the government.
