Allocation of funds to Urban Local Bodies
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No.: Parichay-1198/ P.No.30/ NV-04
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date is March 12, 2018
Government's decision:
The Government has taken the following decisions regarding the action to be taken in respect of the funds allocated to the Urban Local Bodies under various schemes of the Urban Development Department for non-utilization within the stipulated time.
The funds disbursed by the Urban Development Department to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State during the year 2013-14 and previous years and still lying unutilized should be deposited immediately and in any case before 17.03.2018.
The last date for spending the funds disbursed and yet to be released by the Urban Development Department to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State during the financial years 2014-15 and 2015-16 is being extended up to 30th June, 2018.
The last date for spending the funds disbursed and yet to be released by the Urban Development Department to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State for the financial year 2016-17 is being extended till 31st December, 2018.
d. The funds remaining unutilized after the expiry of the extension granted under (b) and (c) above shall be deposited by the Government within one week thereafter.
The Commissioner, Municipal Corporation and the Chief Officer, Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat shall be solely responsible for taking action in accordance with (a) above. He shall submit a report on the action taken in this regard to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai by 20.03.2018. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai shall submit a consolidated report to the Urban Development Department by 31.03.2018.
The Commissioner and the Principal of the concerned Municipal Corporation shall be fully responsible for completing the development works within the time limit prescribed in (a) and (b) above.
Similarly, the Collector should also review the development works within this time frame once in every fortnight and the pending proposals at his level should be disposed of immediately.
The Commissioner and the Director of Municipal Administration shall review the completion of development works once in every fortnight and complete the works within the prescribed time. A report should be submitted to the government.
