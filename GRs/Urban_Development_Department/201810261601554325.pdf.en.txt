The committee had appointed a high-level committee to study the road safety in a scientific manner and suggest measures. It has been observed that in the meetings held at the government level on the recommendation of this committee, it is necessary to give instructions to take necessary measures on priority to prevent accidents in the areas of the black spot identified in the jurisdiction of urban local bodies. It was also under consideration to issue instructions to the Urban Local Bodies to ensure effective and expeditious implementation of road safety related works.
The Delhi Integrated Multimodal Transport System (DIMTS) has submitted its report to the high-level committee on road safety, based on various recommendations. The recommendations of the committee have been accepted and are being implemented. Accordingly, the following committees are being constituted with a view to ensure effective action in the urban local body areas.

The Committee shall take the following actions from the safety point of view in accident prone areas:
The Committee shall prepare an audit report on road safety in urban areas on the lines of the Public Works Department, as per the standards of the Indian Road Congress or through a third party. The safety of roads in urban areas should be reviewed twice a year. Also, the necessary measures should be taken by the concerned urban local bodies as per the recommendations of this report.
Separate instructions will be issued on the standards of Indian Road Congress with regard to road safety in urban areas.
The Committee shall be required to review the measures taken in pursuance of the Audit Report on Road Safety on a regular quarterly basis.
