The Proposal:
In view of the traffic problems arising out of the increasing population and number of vehicles in Nagpur city, and considering that the existing roads will become inadequate and congested in the near future, the Government, under the relevant Government Decision dated 30.01.2014, has decided to construct Line No. 1" Automotive Chowk to Mihan" and Route No. 2 Metro Rail projects from Prajapati Nagar to Lokmanya Nagar have been approved. The project was sanctioned by the Central Government vide letter dated 21.08.2014.
The project has been sanctioned. The project is being implemented by the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC). This special purpose vehicle is manufactured by the company. The Nagpur Metro Rail Corporation Limited (NMRCL), a joint venture between the Centre and the state government, is executing the project. The tripartite MoU was signed in July, 2015. The Government has taken a decision in the context of the said tripartite MoU. Post facto approval has been granted as on 31.7.2017.
Out of the total length of 38.215 km, 13.70 km has been completed as on 08.03.2019. M., dated 29.01.2020 at 11.00 Km. Me and another 1.50 km on 21.08.2021 totalling 26.20 km. I have started the work on a long metro line. The remaining 12.015 km of the project is expected to be commissioned shortly.
The Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited (MMRCL) is implementing the Nagpur Metro Rail Project. Nagpur
A revised budget has been submitted to the government. Accordingly, the proposal for revised expenditure was under consideration of the State Government for approval. The government has taken the following decisions:
