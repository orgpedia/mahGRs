The Proposal:
Sangli Miraj and Kupwara City Municipal Corporations are D category Municipal Corporations. It was established on 09/02/1998. According to the 2011 census, the population of Sangli Miraj and Kupwad Municipal Corporations is more than 5 lakh and the total area is 118.18 sq.km.
2. Considering the huge increase in urbanization of Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporation areas, the increasing population resulting in stress on infrastructure, various important projects/ initiatives being undertaken by the State and Central Government, implementation of various public welfare schemes by the Municipal Administration leading to reduction in the working staff, the requirement of trained manpower for the Municipal Corporation for carrying out dynamic administrative work, it was necessary to create new posts for administrative and technical work in Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporation.
3. General Meeting of the Municipal Corporations of Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City vide Resolution No. 146, c. The Municipal Corporation has been approved under 18/02/2022. Commissioner, Sangli Miraj and Kupwara City Municipal Corporation As per the letter dated 06/05/2022, a proposal has been submitted to the government to approve the formation of the Municipal Corporation.
In pursuance of this proposal, the Commissioner, Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City Municipal Corporation, Shri. Revised information sought under Government letter dated 22/08/2022. Received through letter dated 04/01/2023. Accordingly, the matter of approving the consolidated structure of the Municipal Corporation along with the creation of new necessary posts on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SAMIK-2022/ PR. NO. 80/ NO. 19 GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Commissioner, Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City Municipal Corporation have issued the following orders to the concerned DMs. 06/05/2022 and D. In pursuance of the proposal submitted vide letter dated 04/01/2023, the Government has issued a notification in column No. The creation of 1114 new posts mentioned in (6) is being approved by the Government as per Section-51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The Government is approving the relaxation of the condition of 35% establishment cost of Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporation as a one-time matter while creating the 1114 new posts mentioned above.
3. The details of inclusion of certain posts of Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporations in other cadres in line with the contours of other Municipal Corporations and from the point of view of convenience of hierarchy are set out in Form B-1 accompanying the said Government Resolution.
Also, the details of changes in the name, pay scale and grade pay of some posts have been indicated in Form-" B-2," which is being approved by the government.
The Government is approving the abolition of a total of 70 posts, including 4 working staff-wise posts and 66 vacancies indicated in Form-B-3 attached to this Government decision.
The Government is approving the figure of total 3,454 posts (4 functionally lapsed) as mentioned in Form-" A" of Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporation excluding 66 vacancies to be exhausted out of total 3520 posts including 2406 posts sanctioned by the Government and 1114 posts newly created in Form-" A."
6. The drawing of lots of the Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City Municipal Corporation is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions: (i) The drawing of lots of the Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City Municipal Corporation shall be ensured keeping in view the prescribed percentage (35%) of the establishment cost of the Municipal Corporation as required.
(ii) Considering the establishment cost of Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporations, the post should not be filled up without obtaining the approval of the Government.
(ii) The Commissioner shall take a decision to implement the change in pay scale only after verifying/ verifying the pay scale as per the Sangli Miraj and Kupwad City Municipal Corporation diagram.
(iv) No funds/ grants of any kind shall be made available by the Government to the Municipal Corporation for the posts/ increments in the said figure.
