The Swachh Bharat Mission Urban 2.0 has been launched by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India, with a view to sustain the Swachh Bharat Mission and to make urban India garbage free, keeping in view the need to ensure continuity in investment for individual, community and public toilets, treatment, disposal and recycling of used water and sewage, management of all types of solid waste and disposal and management of legacy waste, plastic waste, construction and demolition waste etc. The Mission will be implemented from 2 nd October, 2021 to 1 st October, 2026.
All Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) are required to complete all projects related to solid waste management and sewerage for all sanctioned projects under Swachh Bharat Mission- 2.0 within the prescribed time frame and in a proper manner. For this, guidelines are being issued to Urban Local Bodies for speedy and efficient implementation of these projects.
Solid Waste Management Projects- A department-wise empanelment of agencies has been created to prepare detailed project reports of the projects sanctioned under the Mission, to carry out the tendering process and to act as project management consultants (Annexure-I). Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) should prepare a detailed project report on solid waste management from the designated agencies. The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority is being authorized to grant technical approval to these Detailed Project Reports for which a technical approval fee of 1% of the project cost shall be paid to the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority and the amount of the said 1% technical approval fee shall be included in the project cost. Once the technical approval is received, the detailed project report should be submitted to the State Level Technical Committee immediately for approval. Once the tender process is completed, it will be approved.
