In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the Telhara Municipal Council Water Supply Project is being given administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
a) Project Implementation Mechanism: The project should be implemented by the Telhara Municipal Council.
It will be mandatory for the project to appoint the Maharashtra Life Authority as the Project Management Consultant (PMC). A decision in this regard will be taken by the city corporation.
• The Project Management Consultancy Charges (PMC Charges) equivalent to 3% of the project cost should be provided by the Municipal Council to the Maharashtra Life Authority from its own funds.
• It will be mandatory for the implementing agency to fulfill all the conditions prescribed by the State Level Project Approval Committee while approving the project.
