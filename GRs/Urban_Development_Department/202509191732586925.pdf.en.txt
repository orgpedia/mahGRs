Introduction - Simhastha Kumbh Mela is being held in Nashik and Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik in the year 2027-2028. A large number of devotees / tourists are expected. In order to ensure the successful conduct of the Kumbh Mela, planning and effective implementation are in order to provide large-scale connectivity, transport, water supply, accommodation, health care, electricity, law and order, parking, etc.
The Kumbh Mela is an important religious and cultural event that is attended by a large number of devotees. Coordination among various government departments, provision of necessary infrastructure, and management of safety and health facilities is crucial for the successful organisation of the event. Considering all these aspects, it is necessary to form the Kumbh Mela Ministerial Committee to comprehensively review the preparations and organisation of the Kumbh Mela, as well as to take important policy decisions. This committee will streamline the functioning at various levels and make the decision-making process more effective.
Maharashtra Act No. 33 of 2025 Nashik-Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority Act, 2025. Published in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette on 10.07.2025.
Section 3 of the Act provides for the constitution of the Kumbh Mela Ministerial Committee.
In view of the above facts, the Government was contemplating the formation of the Kumbh Mela Ministerial Committee with a view to operationalising the administrative machinery for better planning and effective implementation.
