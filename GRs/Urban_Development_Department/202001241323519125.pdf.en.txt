Approval has been granted vide 28.07.2004. The request has been made vide letter dated 22.01.2020. The government has taken the decision. In the Annexure 28.07.2004, instead of Ushatai Damodar Bhadbhade mentioned at No.59, his name is being corrected as Ushatai Damodar Bhadbhade and the name should be read as per the correction in the list.
