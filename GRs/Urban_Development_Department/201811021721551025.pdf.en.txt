As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the Rajapeth Railway Subway Project of Amravati City is being given administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
a) Project Implementation Mechanism: The works of the sub-components approved by the Railways in the said project should be implemented by the Railway Administration and the works of the sub-components approved by the Amravati Municipal Corporation should be implemented by the Amravati Municipal Corporation.
However, after the completion of these two works, the Commissioner, Amravati Municipal Corporation will be responsible for ensuring proper coordination with the railway administration so that the subway can be used smoothly.
• It will be mandatory for the implementing agency to fulfill all the conditions prescribed by the State Level Project Approval Committee while approving the project.
Fulfillment of Reforms: In the context of Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Nagarothana Mahabhivana No. As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the Urban Local Bodies will be required to complete the following binding and optional reforms. Of these, 80% of the optional conditions must be met by the second year of the project.

(i) Compulsory Reforms: It will be mandatory for the Municipal Councils to fully computerize their activities in the first year of project approval. This will include 100% implementation of e-governance, accounting, and birth and death registration reforms.

It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Bodies to ensure proper management of solid waste in their jurisdiction as per the provisions of the Solid Waste Management Rules, 2016.
It will be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Bodies to collect at least 80% of the property tax and water charges in their jurisdiction in the first year. In the following year, the balance 90% will be required to be recovered.
(ii) the following amendments:
Urban Local Bodies undertaking sewerage projects should include in their Detailed Project Report the components of re-treatment and re-use of sewage in urban areas.
The concerned urban local bodies should store rainwater on the building owned by them.
Regular water audits should be carried out by the respective urban local bodies. In respect of rectification of defects mentioned in the Water Records Inspection Report, the concerned Urban Local Body shall be required to complete the defects within the prescribed time frame by formulating necessary action programmes in this regard.
(a) The method of distribution of funds:
After the administrative approval for the projects in the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, the concerned civic body should submit the financial closure report of the project to the Urban Development Department. The first installment of the project will be released after the completion of the project. The Municipal Corporation should set aside the amount from the current year's grant of the 14th Finance Commission.
• After disbursing the first installment of funds to the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Campaign, the installment and the local body's share of the amount will be released.

Tendering process, work orders and implementation: It will be mandatory to issue tenders within one month after the release of the first installment of funds to the projects approved under this mission and to issue work orders within three months and to start the work physically before the 101st day.
The implementing agency shall be bound to follow the instructions contained in the Government Resolution No. 3 in the context while implementing the tender process of the said project.
In case of non-compliance, the concerned urban local body will be required to return the funds sanctioned for such project to the government along with interest thereon.
• It will be mandatory for the concerned implementing agency to complete the project within the prescribed time frame.
After the State Government has sanctioned the State Government's share to the concerned local body for the project approved under this mission, the local body concerned shall provide self-assurance to the extent of the amount to be provided from the State Government's account in each payment at each stage of expenditure. With this, there will be no time to pay the share of the local body in such a scheme at once.
The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can only be used for the same project and cannot be diverted or used for any other purpose. Any such diversion of the principal amount or interest thereon, whether permanent or temporary, will be treated as a serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned local bodies/ concerned authorities.
e) Third Party Technical Audit of Projects: Under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotna Abhiyan, it will be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Bodies to conduct" Third Party Technical Audit" of the sanctioned projects at various stages of implementation by one of the mechanisms set up by the Government from time to time. Also, while submitting the proposal for sanction of second installment under the project, it will be necessary to submit a certificate regarding the quality of the said" third technical examination." After that, the second installment will be released.
