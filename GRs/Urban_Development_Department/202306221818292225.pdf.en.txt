The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No: PMC-3010/ PR No.394 (Part-II)/ NV-22
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: June 22, 2023
Reference:- (1) Urban Development Department, Govt. 18.02.2017 (2) Urban Development Department, Govt. 14.11.2022 (3) Directorate of Municipal Council No.NAPPRASAM-2015/ PIL No. 115/2021/ PR No. 03/ Cell 10/2588, Dy. dated 10.08.2018.
Preamble: Order to formulate an all-inclusive policy for the purpose of regulating and controlling the display of sky signs and advertisements in the Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats. The High Court of Bombay, vide Public Interest Litigation No. 155/2011 dated 31st January, 2017. In pursuance of the said order, the matter of framing separate guidelines for regulation and control of display of sky signs and advertisements in Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats was under consideration at the Government level.
Government Decision: Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Councils, under reference No. The Government has received the draft guidelines for regulation and control of display of sky signs and advertisements in the Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats along with letter No. 3. Guidelines have been prepared on the basis of this bill and it is being approved by the government under this decision. In addition, guidelines in Marathi have been prescribed in Annexure A and English in Annexure B.
