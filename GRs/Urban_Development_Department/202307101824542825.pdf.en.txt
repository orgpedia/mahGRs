Mumbai Trans-Harbour Link (MTHL) to be renamed as Atal Setu.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No:- MRD-3323/ P. No. 81/ NV-7
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai-32.
Date: July 10, 2023.
Read the government's decision. Meeting/ MRD-3308/ No.110/ NV-7, Dy. June 08, 2011.
The proposal: Cabinet of Ministers In the meeting held on June 28, 2023, it has been approved to rename the Sewri-Nhava Sheva Mumbai Parbandar (MTHL) Setu being constructed by Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority as" Atal Bihari Vajpayee Sewri-Nhava Sheva Atal Setu," henceforth.
Government's decision:
The government has decided to rename the Mumbai-Parbandar Sea Link (MTHL) as Atal Bihari Vajpayee Sewri Nhava Sheva Atal Setu.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) should take immediate action in this regard.
