Introduction: The Finance Department has given its decision. The policy on the use of vehicles in government and other offices has been fixed under 10.9.2001. For this, every administrative department has been directed to review the vehicles with them (including the regional office).
Accordingly, the proposal of the Urban Development Department (itself) to approve the review of vehicles was submitted to the State Level Vehicle Review Committee for approval. The state-level vehicle review committee has approved the vehicle review of the department and the matter of issuing a government decision in this regard was under the consideration of the government.
