The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, in respect of any such immovable property situated in the city, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent on the value of such property situated in respect of the sale or encumbrance of the same and on the amount secured by the encumbrance of the encumbrance of the same in respect of the encumbrance of the same. Accordingly, in the financial year 2021-22, the 1% stamp duty due to the 26 municipal corporations of the state will be Rs. 235,71,98, 809/- as 1st installment vide Government Resolution No.Stamp-2020/ P.No.13/ NV-32, Dt. 31.12.2021 has been issued. It is now Rs. The second installment of Rs 210,00,00, 000/- to the Municipal Corporations was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The total amount to be released to the Municipal Corporations within the limit of subsidy available on Budget Distribution System for disbursement of 2nd instalment of 1% Stamp Duty for the year 2021-22 is Rs. The Government approve the release of Rs. 210,00,00, 000 (Rupees Two Hundred and Ten Crores only) as follows:

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations.
