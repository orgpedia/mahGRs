As per the provisions of the Urban Local Bodies Act regarding exemption of property tax to the property holders in Urban Local Bodies, the matter of exemption of property tax to the citizens/ housing societies of the local civic bodies for setting up charging stations for electric vehicles belonging to Urban Development Department (NAVI-2) was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In view of the provisions of the Urban Local Bodies Act regarding exemption of property tax, the Government has decided to provide exemption of property tax to the citizens of the local civic bodies/ housing societies who are setting up charging stations for electric vehicles till the implementation of the Maharashtra Electric Vehicle Policy-2021
(1) A 2% rebate in property tax should be given if a charging station is set up for own electric vehicle and it provides charging facility to other electric vehicle owners. However, the property owner should ensure that there is no obstruction to traffic.
(2) Property tax should be exempted up to a maximum limit of 5% if the members are provided with electric vehicle charging facility under common facilities in housing societies.
(3) If the housing societies provide electric vehicle charging facility on a commercial basis in the premises owned by them, in the open space other than the original parking, but for emergency work, it shall be provided.
