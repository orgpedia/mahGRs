The Proposal:
Under the Scheme" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment 2023-24" of Government of India, financial assistance in the form of 50 years non-interest bearing loan for capital investment projects will be provided to the State Governments. In this regard, the Department of Expenditure, Government of India, dated 03.02.2023 and The guidelines of the scheme have been issued vide letter dated 02.05.2023. Detailed guidelines for the construction of Unity Mall under Part VI of the scheme. issued on 16.06.2023. Ministry of Commerce and Industry. As per the letter dated 10.05.2023, the nodal department for the construction of Unity Mall as well as the state-level Single Point of Contact information, the site of Unity Mall construction and the DPR for the construction of Unity Mall should be submitted.
Under this scheme, non-interest bearing loans/ funds will be available and the expenditure on the works to be done under it will be borne by the concerned departments by making a separate budgetary provision for the expenditure. The new modalities prescribed for allocation of funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide letter dated 23.03.2021 and in respect of Finance Department vide No. 3, d. The procedures prescribed under 1.7.2021 are to be followed. Special Assistance to the States

The funds received under the Capital Investment Scheme will have to be budgeted by the concerned department and distributed to the operational agencies. The funds received by the Urban Development Department for the distribution of budget No. Nodal officers have been appointed as per the decision of the government.
The proposal to open a separate account of Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for construction of Unity Mall and zero balance account for implementing agencies under Part VI of the" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme 2023-24" was under consideration of the Government of India.
The decision is as follows:
Government's decision:
1.Government Order of the Finance Department and Central Government. 23.03.2021, Dy. 03.02.2023, Dy. Guidelines dated 02.05.2023 and the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Govt. Guidelines dated 10.05.2023 The Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary (Navi-10) is being declared as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Construction of Unity Malls under Part VI of the" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme 2023-24" for coordination and control of State Governments and implementing agencies for implementation of PFMS system.
Under this scheme, a separate bank account of the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and the implementing agencies are being approved to open a Zero Balance Subsidiary Account in Bank of Maharashtra, Mantralaya Branch for the funds received from the Central Government.
These accounts should be used only for the purpose of the said scheme.
Department of Finance, Government Decisions. 13.3.2020 and dt. The Government has issued instructions regarding opening of accounts in nationalized and private banks under 3.3.2021 and these instructions should be followed while opening SNA and Zero Balance Subsidiary Account.
The SNA account and the Zero Balance Subsidiary account should be linked to the PFMS system.
The funds received for each scheme from the treasury shall be linked to the PFMS system of the respective scheme in the first instance for the purpose of disbursement of the funds of the scheme to the concerned agencies in a centralized manner.
