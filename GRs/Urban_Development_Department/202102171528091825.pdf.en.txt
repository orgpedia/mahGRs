Introduction: Primary and Secondary School Teachers under Urban Local Bodies
There has been a persistent demand from a large number of concerned teachers for transfer/ transfer of services on account of family and specific difficulties in urban/ rural local body schools. In this regard, the recommendations of the local public representatives as well as the honorable MLAs/ MPs are also being followed up.
Earlier, the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, were referred to for such transfers/ transfers. The action was being taken at the level of Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, under the standing orders from 1 to 3. Hence, there was no need for the government to take such a step. However, since the order dated 31.05.2017 issued by the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, cancelling the said standing order, confusion has arisen regarding the transfer of teachers in Urban Local Bodies.
The Department of Rural Development, vide Government Resolution No: JIPB-4817/ PR No.280/ Aastha-9, dated 29.06.2017, has approved transfer/ transfer of services of Zilla Parishad teachers to Urban Local Bodies (ULBs). However, since no decision has been taken in this regard by the Urban Development Department, there were difficulties in transferring/ transferring services to urban or rural local bodies under the family problems/ specific circumstances of teachers in urban local bodies. Government's decision:
The Government has decided to grant approval for permanent transfer of services of teachers in Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Nagar Panchayats subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) Necessary certificate from both the appointing authorities concerned will be required for transfer of services by the primary/ secondary teachers under the jurisdiction of the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat.
(2) It shall be mandatory for the teachers concerned to accept the service-related and salary-related provisions applicable to the place of appointment sought.
3. Service seniority at the place of original appointment to the place of new appointment of the replacement
The prior consent of the teachers concerned will be required. Their seniority will be lower in the new place.
Primary/ Secondary teachers seeking transfer to the service should have been appointed on a regular basis. Also, the probationary period must be completed.
(5) No departmental inquiry or criminal proceedings should be initiated against the teacher concerned.
6. It shall be mandatory to adjust the audit objections and all advances in their name.
The amount deposited in the teachers' provident fund account on the date of transfer of services, pension and leave transfer will be required to be transferred to the transferred urban or rural local body.

How to submit a proposal:
1) The teacher who has demanded transfer/ transfer of service by the middle/ primary teacher of the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat should submit a complete application to the concerned administrative officer.
(2) On receipt of the proposal, the Administrative Officer shall, subject to the provisions mentioned above, examine and submit the proposal for approval of the Commissioner, if it is a Municipal Corporation, and after approval, the teacher shall submit the proposal to the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Municipal Panchayat Administration Officer to whom the teacher has sought to transfer the service to the Municipal Council/ Municipal School and to the Education Officer, if it is a Zilla Parishad, to the concerned Zilla Parishad.
(3) The concerned Administrative Officer of the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat to which the transfer is sought and the concerned Education Officer, if there is a Zilla Parishad, shall verify whether the above conditions are being fulfilled or how and accordingly submit a proposal to the concerned Commissioner/ Principal/ CEO for obtaining approval regarding the transfer of services.
4) Both the appointing authorities shall forward the copy of the order to the Director of Education as well as the concerned Deputy Director of Education, Primary/ Secondary and the Urban Development Department for necessary action after the completion of the process of transfer of services as mentioned above.
Right to consent to the provision of services:
(1) The approval of the concerned Municipal Commissioner/ Chief Executive Officer of the Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Municipal Council shall be necessary for the transfer of the services to the District Council. They will be required to approve by following the prescribed procedure and taking into consideration the prescribed terms and conditions.
(2) In case of transfer of service to a vacant post, the appointing authority shall issue an order for transfer of service by the appointing authority after following the prescribed procedure in compliance with all the terms and conditions.
