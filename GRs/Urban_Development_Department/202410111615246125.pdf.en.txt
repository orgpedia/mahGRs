The Proposal: -
The state government is receiving funds from the central government under the central government's "Special Assistance to States for Capital Investments" scheme. Under this scheme, the central government had approved to provide funds for the works in the development plan framework in the financial year 2022-23. Also, funds have been provided for the ongoing works in the development plan framework of various Municipal Councils / Nagar Panchayats / Municipal Corporations as per the approval given by the Central Government in this financial year. An Institute of Architects for projects under the Scheme has been appointed from the level of Divisional Joint Director, Town Planning, Office to implement the Scheme from the proposed fund of 2024-2025 depending upon the prescribed modalities. Approval has been given at the level of Joint Director, Town Planning / Assistant Director, Town Planning in respect of the proposed development works maps prepared by the Institute of Architects. Technical approvals for such development works have been received by the Architects' Institute by preparing the budgets for the development works under the said project and submitting the proposals for technical approval to the concerned Principal / Administrator, Municipal Council / Nagar Panchayat to the "concerned Office and / or Director of Public Works Department, Uplands and Parks." Instructions regarding the implementation of the scheme are as follows.
a) Project Implementation Mechanism and Implementation: 1) Projects under the scheme should be implemented by the respective Municipal Councils / Nagar Panchayats / Municipal Corporations.
(2) In the execution of these projects, it shall be obligatory on the part of the implementing agency to follow the guidelines in this regard of the Central Government and the State Government.
3) It shall be the responsibility of the Principal / Administrator concerned to re-verify that the project work under the scheme has not been carried out under any other scheme. In the event of non-compliance, the concerned civic body will be required to return the funds sanctioned for such a project, along with the interest thereon, to the government.
4) In case of any increase in the original cost of the project under the scheme, the entire responsibility shall lie with the concerned Urban Local Body. It is to be noted from all the concerned operational agencies that no additional expenditure will be made available for the same either through the State Government or from the Central Government funds.
5) It shall be the responsibility of the implementing agency to ensure that there is no duplication of funds from the Central Government for the projects sanctioned out of this fund.
6) The progress of the work should be maintained as indicated in the tender. The management will be responsible for processing the payment as per the availability of funds.
7) The management will be responsible for spending the funds available under the scheme for the same purposes and the quality of the works as per the standards issued by the government from time to time.
8) It shall be the responsibility of the implementing agency to take a photograph (660192 0 0 050) of the status before the commencement of these works, at various stages of the work, with latitude and longitude of the status after the completion of the work.
9) The funds under the scheme shall be appropriated in accordance with the procedure laid down, in accordance with all financial laws and regulations as well as the procedures laid down. It shall be the responsibility of the implementing agency to comply with the instructions of the government decisions issued by the government from time to time in this regard.
10) The Principal / Administrator / Commissioner concerned should immediately submit the details of utilization certificate B expenditure in the prescribed form to the Director, Town Planning, Maharashtra State, Pune after spending the funds for the project under the scheme. Also, a copy of the same should be sent immediately to the Director, Directorate of Municipal Councils, Navi Mumbai and also to the Abuse / Accounts Objection, Public Accounts Committee, under the scheme.
The executive machinery shall be responsible in respect of legislative business, etc.
11) The action to register the properties arising out of the projects under the scheme in the Real Estate Registry should be taken by the Commissioner / Principal concerned.
b) Tendering Process: 1) Tenders will be floated immediately after the project gets the approval of the administrator under the scheme.
