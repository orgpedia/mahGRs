Introduction: The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation (Amendment of the Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 has been notified. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019.
In the said figure of the Municipal Corporation, the posts of" Multipurpose Health Worker" (Class-3) were not sanctioned for the Public Health Department. Also, for the post of" Multi-Purpose Health Worker" (Class-3), the eligibility criteria for appointment as well as the mode of appointment have been prescribed in the Service Admission Rules. However, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has requested for a partial change in the educational qualification in the service entry rules for the said post.

Government Resolution No: MIBHAM-1123/ No. 987/ No. 54/ NV-28
In view of the increasing scope of health work in Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, the Government was considering to revise the educational qualification for the post of" Multi-purpose Health Worker" in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation Revised Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019.
Government Decision: Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued the relevant orders. In pursuance of letter dated 17.04.2023, the Government is pleased to approve the amendment in the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Amended Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019, as per Annexure-II, Schedule E, submitted for inclusion of entry into service procedure and qualifications for the posts of Multi-Purpose Health Workers (Class-III). This is an improvement. The rules of entry into service are being amended only for" Multi-Purpose Health Workers" who have been included in the service as per the Government decision dated 02.11.2023. Thereafter, the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 will be applicable for the said post.
