City Beautification and Sanitation Competition-2022 State Level Evaluation Committee Meeting dated 05.4.2023
Preamble: Guidelines have been issued under Reference No. 2 regarding the implementation of the City Beautification Campaign in the Urban Local Bodies of the State. As a part of the said campaign, the Government had organized" City Beautification and Sanitation Competition-2022" at the city level in Urban Local Bodies of the State from 2 nd October, 2022 to 31 st December, 2022 under Reference No. The amount of the award was also fixed. In order to evaluate the performance of the local bodies in the competition, a Scrutiny Committee and a State Level Evaluation Committee were constituted at the Division and State level respectively. Accordingly, the State Level Evaluation Committee in its meeting held on 05.04.2023 has identified the respective awardee Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats for the award.
