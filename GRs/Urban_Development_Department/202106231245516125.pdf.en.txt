The Proposal:
The Central Government has repealed the Urban Land (Ceiling on Holdings and Regulation) Act, 1976 under the Nazakdha Nidhi Nidhi Act, 1999 and the said Act has been repealed by the State Government. Accepted on 29.11.2007. Under the Repeal Act, the following exemption orders and proceedings under Section 20 of the principal Act as well as the proceedings on the lands acquired under Section 10 (3) and 10 (5) are protected.
A two-member committee constituted to suggest measures to make available for development the lands exempted under Article 20 of the Constitution. Report submitted on 9.8.2018. The Supreme Court vide Appeal No. 558/2017. Judgment dated 2.7.2019. The policy decision has been taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 1.8.2019. In the light of this decision, the Government, with the approval of the Cabinet, has taken the following decision after taking into consideration the concurrence expressed by the Department of Industrial Energy and Labour regarding the lands exempted for industrial purposes under Section 20 of the Act, which have been leased out by the lessee to the industrial units under the purview of the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation.
Utilization of lands leased out to industrial units in the jurisdiction of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation and exempted for industrial purposes under section 20 of the Act

Government decision issued regarding change/ transfer charges. 3.2.2018, the following decisions are being taken in respect of such lands:
""" 1. The lands exempted for industrial purposes shall be transferred only for industrial purposes without changing the purpose of use and no transfer fee shall be required to be collected under section 20 for the lands exempted under section 20, unless the entry\"" area under section 20 exempted for industrial purposes\"" taken in other title to the land is required to be reduced."""
(2) If the land exempted under section 20 for industrial purposes is to be used/ transferred for other purposes, the Government may take a decision. As per the provision of 1.08.2019, 60% of the amount of overage (15%) to be recovered will be deposited with the Government and the remaining 40% with MIDC. The entry of" Area under Industrial Holiday under Section 20" in other rights of such land will be reduced and the land will be developed as per the terms and conditions of the Industrial Development Corporation as well as the provisions of the Development Control Rules.
(3) The Government shall take a decision with respect to the land exempted under section 20 for industrial purposes. Out of the amount of the over-value to be determined as on 1.8.2019, 60% of the amount to be deposited with the Government shall be paid by the Government in accordance with the First Government Order. 10.10.2019 and the balance 40% shall be credited to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. For this, the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation is being authorized.
