Government Decisions: The urban sector in the state is constantly growing. Considering the increasing urbanization as an opportunity and in order to take action on important schemes of the Central and State Governments for effective development in urban areas in a time-bound and time-bound manner, the following Key Result Areas are being prescribed for the Commissioners, Municipal Corporations and all the Principals, Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State for the year 2018-19.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | K.R.A.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| 1    | Prioritization of Urban Sanitation: Considering increasing urbanization as an opportunity, effective implementation of Urban Sanitation has assumed great significance. The cities of the State have performed better than last year in Swachh Survekshan 2018 conducted by the Union Government.                           |
| 2    | Recovery of various taxes: Recovery of more than 90% (current and past) of property tax and other taxes and substantial improvement in the financial position of Urban Local Bodies.                                                                                                                                        |
| 3    | Special Measures for Self-Reliance: Increase the income of the Urban Local Bodies by strengthening the source of income base. Adoption of GIS system for collection of property tax, bringing all properties under the net of tax. Revision of rates of rent for shops as per the prevailing policy of the Government, etc. |

| 4 | Completion of infrastructure projects: Completion of stalled projects of Jawaharlal Nehru National Urban Renewal Mission (JNNURM) and Nagarothana in a time-bound manner including effective implementation of all ongoing and newly sanctioned projects of AMRUT and Nagarothana schemes and implementation of Smart Cities Mission in a time-bound manner.                                         |

Based on the performance and progress in the above mentioned areas of the Commissioner, Municipal Corporation and all the Principals, Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats of the State, the concerned officers will be evaluated for the Performance Appraisal Report (PAR)/ Annual Confidential Report (ACR) for the year 2018-19.
