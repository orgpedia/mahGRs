NO. 264/ NV-34, dated: 28th December, 2018 Preamble: Swachh Maharashtra Mission is being implemented in the State on the lines of Swachh Bharat Mission of the Central Government. Under this campaign, two major aspects are to make the cities" open defecation free" by providing toilet facilities (individual or community) to the families in the cities who do not have toilet facilities, and to make the city" clean" by managing the solid waste of the city.
To encourage people to participate in Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan and build sustainable capacity of cities in terms of openness and cleanliness and the journey towards Swachh Maharashtra. In order to create awareness among all sections of the society to increase their participation in the Swachh Survekshan-2018, Swachh Survekshan-2018 was conducted from January 2018 to March 2018 in 4041 Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) across the country.
The reward scheme was announced under the government decision in Reference No. 2 to encourage cities to achieve the highest performance of all cities in the state in the Swachh Survekshan-2018. The performance of the State in Swachh Survekshan-2018 has been excellent. The state has received the highest number of awards (10 out of 46) in the country. The Central Government has announced the results of Swachh Survekshan-2018 competition in May 2018. Accordingly, an amount of Rs. 120,88,00, 000/- out of Rs. 325,00,00, 000/- of the prize eligible cities has been disbursed as per the Government decision at Reference No.3. Out of the remaining amount, a sum of Rs. 14,00,00, 000/- was under consideration of the Government
Government Decision: Considering the points mentioned in the Preamble, the Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 14,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores only) out of the total prize money of Swachh Survekshan-2018 to the prize winning cities.
