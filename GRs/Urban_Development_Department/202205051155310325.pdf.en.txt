The Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam Board (TTD) had sought land for the temple in Navi Mumbai on the lines of the temples built in various cities across the country such as Hyderabad, Chennai, Kanyakumari, Bengaluru, Visakhapatnam, Bhubaneswar, Jammu, New Delhi, Kurukshetra and Rishikesh.
With the construction of Sri Venkateswara Temple under a special project as a city level facility in Navi Mumbai, the temple will become a tourist attraction in the future. This will give the area religious and social importance and will help in promoting communication and business in the area. Many social activities will also be organised in the area. Considering the request of the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) Board for allotment of land for the construction of Sri Venkateswara Temple, the Board of Directors of Cidco has decided to allot a plot of land to Sri Venkateswara Temple Board at No. 12456, d. The decision was taken on 2.4.2022. The decision was taken by the CIDCO. This was decided in the Cabinet meeting held on 20.4.2022. The decision was taken at a meeting of the state cabinet.
Government Decision: As per the request of Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams Board (TTD), the following decision is being taken regarding allotment of land by CIDCO Corporation for the construction of Sri Venkateswara Temple in Navi Mumbai.
As a special case for the construction of Sri Venkateswara Temple under the special project as City Level Facility in Navi Mumbai, at a nominal rate of Re.1 per sq.m., at Sector-12, Plot No.1, Ulwe Node. 3 (total area 40,400 sq.m.) with 1.00 floor area index is being approved for direct allotment by lease.
