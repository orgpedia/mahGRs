The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA), the project implementing agency, pays the first instalment and submits the reimbursement claims to the Asian Development Bank. After the Asian Development Bank approves the claim, the same amount is deposited by the Asian Development Bank in the Consolidated Fund of the State through the Central Government. The loan amount is to be disbursed as a loan from the state government to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority through the budget.
