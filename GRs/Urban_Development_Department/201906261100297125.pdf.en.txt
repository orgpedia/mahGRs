The Proposal:
""" Mumbai Urban Transport Project-III (MUTP-III), Virar-Dahanu Road Railway Project and Panvel-Karjat New Suburban Line Project have been declared as\"""" Vital Government Projects\"""" for the purpose of sub-section (3) of section 36 (A) of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966.""" He appealed to the government. In view of this request, the government was considering to approve the metro, mono and railway projects in the state to be declared as" ambitious urban transport projects." Also, as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966, the Maharashtra Regional Planning and Town Planning Act, 1966 and the Maharashtra and Mumbai and Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, the ambitious urban transport projects undertaken by the Central/ State Government through their authorities, government companies, are required to be notified by the State Government in the Gazette. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the Government to declare the approved projects like Metro Rail, Mono Rail, etc. taken up by the Central/ State Governments through their authorities and Government companies under the Urban Development Department as" Aspirational Urban Transport Projects." The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The Government's Decision:
In the above background, the following urban transport projects approved by the State Government are being approved to be declared as" Aspirational Urban Transport Projects."
