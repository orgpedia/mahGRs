Preamble: Implementation of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 in the State on the lines of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 of the Central Government Reference No. 1 has been started here as per the government decision. The main objective of this campaign is to make all the cities of the state garbage-free, which includes scientific management of all types of solid waste in the city and sustainable sanitation and treatment of used water.
Proposals received under Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban) 2.0 for biomining of legacy waste in solid waste management units of Urban Local Bodies in the State were reviewed by the State Level Technical Committee headed by Principal Secretary (Navi-II), Urban Development Department. 4th and 4th of 06.04.2023. This has been approved in the 5th meeting held on 18.05.2023. Similarly, the proposals were approved by the State High Powered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary, Maharashtra. 4th and 25.04.2023. This has been approved in the 5th meeting held on 05.07.2023. The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
