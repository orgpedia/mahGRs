Preamble: The Pune Municipal Transport Undertaking and the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Transport Undertaking were amalgamated under the Indian Companies Act, 1956. The Board of Directors of Pune Metropolitan Transport Corporation has been constituted and a total of 13 Directors were appointed.
The Pune Metropolitan Transport Corporation (PMC) provides bus services to the citizens of Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad and Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) limits. The Corporation also needs financial assistance from the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) and a place to set up a depot in the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) area, due to the increasing population, industrialization and the increase in the corporation's circulation in the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) area.
Therefore, it is requested to include the Municipal Commissioner and Chief Executive Officer, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Pune in the composition of the Board of Directors as in the case of Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporations. Chairman and Managing Director, Pune Metropolitan Transport Corporation Ltd. has done. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to create 1 post of Director in the Board of Directors constituted under the Government decision dated 19.04.2007 and appoint it by the Municipal Commissioner and Chief Executive Officer, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
Government Decisions: Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) Chairman and Managing Director, Pune Metropolitan Transport Corporation Ltd. As proposed by the Governor, in the Board of Directors constituted vide Government Resolution dated 19.04.2007, one post of Director is being created and the post of Director is being held by the Commissioner and CEO, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Pune.
