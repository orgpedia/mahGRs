GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have taken a decision regarding the formation of Aurangabad Municipal Corporation with a new designation. Government approval has been granted as on 15.2.2021. In this regard, the Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation has proposed to create posts in the cadre of Livestock Development Officer and Assistant Livestock Development Officer for the Zoo and Veterinary Department. 19.3.2021 has been submitted to the Government. As per the proposal, the following posts will be created in the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation.
