Against the backdrop of the strict restrictions imposed due to Covid-19 in the state, the authorized cycle rickshaw drivers in the state will get Rs. 1500/- as financial assistance.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No:- SANKIRNA-2021/ P.S. NO. 130/ NV-20
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400032
Date: June 2, 2021.
The Proposal:
In the wake of COVID-19, strict restrictions have been imposed in the state to break the chain of transmission of COVID-19 in April 2021. Under this, the business of cycle rickshaw drivers has also been banned in the state. This has led to problems for the livelihood of bike rickshaw drivers and weaker sections. Considering this. The Chief Minister said. Package for weaker sections announced on 13/04/2021. Under this, Rs. A financial assistance of Rs 1500/- has also been announced. Accordingly, Rs. 1500/- as financial assistance from the Government.
Government's decision:
In view of the strict restrictions imposed due to Covid-19 in the state, the cycle rickshaw drivers in the state will get Rs. 1500/- is being approved by the Government.
In view of the strict restrictions imposed in the state due to COVID-19, cycle rickshaw drivers will be given Rs. 1500/- to Rs. 7.63500 lakh (Rupees Seven Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand Five Hundred only) to the Commissioner and Director, Municipal Council, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai through RTGS or by cheque from the Transport Department as per their procedure. The Transport Department should make the funds available to the Urban Development Department/ Commissioner and Director Municipal Council Administration Directorate, Worli, Mumbai.
Cycle Rickshaw Drivers will be considered for the above financial assistance from the information submitted by the Urban Local Bodies after verifying their eligibility/ license.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SANKIRN-2021/ PR. NO. 130/ NO. 20-04. The Commissioner and Director, Municipal Administration, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai are instructed to take necessary further action regarding the deposit of this fund in the bank account of the beneficiaries. While disbursing the funds from the level of Commissioner/ concerned Urban Local Bodies, care should be taken to deposit the funds in the bank accounts of the concerned beneficiaries.
The Finance Department has agreed to incur the expenditure from the provision available for the current financial year 2021-2022 under the head Administration, (01) Transport Commissioner, (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner (Datta Mat) (Mandatory) 20410018, 33- Financial Assistance.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the concurrence of the Transport Department, Planning Department and in accordance with the concurrence given by the Finance Department vide its informal Reference No. 185/ Expenditure-3 dated 25/05/2021.
