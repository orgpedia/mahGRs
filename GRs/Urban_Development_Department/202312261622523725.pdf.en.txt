Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Infrastructure Development of Small and Medium Towns (UIDSSMT), an amount of Rs. The Shegaon City Augmentation Water Supply Scheme worth Rs. 42.18 crore was sanctioned with 80% share of Central Government, 10% share of State Government and 10% share of Municipal Council. Due to the financial position of the Municipal Council, the total amount of Rs. 7,58,35, 000/-, which is 10% of the share of the Municipal Council in the scheme and 10% of the water of 1.00 MLD to be paid to the Shegaon Sansthan for lifting water, has been paid by the Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan, Shegaon and in this regard, the Municipal Council has decided to supply water to the Sansthan without charging any water tariff tax of the Municipal Council for 100 MLD water as per the agreement dated 7/3/2009 with the Sansthan passed by the Municipal Council Shegaon vide Resolution No.2 dated 28/11/2008. On the same basis, the Government is giving approval to supply water to Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan, Shegaon for supply of 0.51 MLD water in addition to 1.00 MLD water without charging any waterline tax from the Municipal Council.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The State will be required to pay the water lifting charges to the Irrigation Department for the supply of 0.51 MLD of water.
(2) The other terms and conditions of the agreement dated 7/3/2009 entered into by the Municipal Council with Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan, Shegaon shall remain in force.
