Preamble: Under the Central Government's Scheme of" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment 2022-23" for the year 2022-23, financial assistance is being provided to the State Governments in the form of 50-year non-interest bearing loan for capital investment projects. In this regard, the Department of Expenditure, Government of India, vide letter dated 06.04.2022, has issued guidelines for the scheme. The funds provided to the States under the scheme are to be used for new and ongoing capital projects for the long-term benefit of the State.
The project proposals submitted by the state government have been scrutinized by the central government, after which the loan funds have been received for the approved projects. The Central Government has proposed a total of seven tranches of funds under this scheme, out of which Part-1 is the Special Fund for Capital Expenditure, Part-2 is the Urban Reforms under the PM GatiShakti Yojana, and Part-7 is the Disinvestment and Monetization.
2. Under the above scheme, interest-free loans will be available and the expenditure on the works to be done under it will be borne by the concerned departments by making a separate budgetary provision for the expenditure.
