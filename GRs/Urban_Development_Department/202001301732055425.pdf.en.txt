Government Decision: Under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), the projects of the 13 Municipal Councils shown in the accompanying statement, at the price indicated in Rakaana 4 of the statement of Urban Solid Waste Management, were approved as per the Government Decisions at Reference No. 3 to 15.
While the project was being implemented by the Urban Local Bodies, the concerned Urban Local Bodies were requested to approve new items to the cities excluding some items included in the project. (The exclusions and costs of the approved projects are set out in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively of the Statement accompanying this Government Decision.) The items to be approved and their cost in place of the excluded items are indicated in Rakaana 7 and 8 respectively. )
The proposal submitted by these 13 Urban Local Bodies was examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Powered Committee held on 30.08.2019. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
