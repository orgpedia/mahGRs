Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is being appointed as the Project Development and Management Consultant (PDMC) of the Mission for speedy and efficient implementation of various projects sanctioned under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission. Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Abhiyaan 20 Scheme, an amount of 0.50% of the total project cost will be paid to Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as Project Development and Management Consultancy Fee as per the stage of progress of the projects.
All Urban Local Bodies shall pay 1% of the project cost to the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for immediate technical approval from the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority for the projects sanctioned under AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
