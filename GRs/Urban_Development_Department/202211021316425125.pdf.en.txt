Government Decision: In order to ensure uniformity in the work related to town planning in the Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in the state and to control the work related to town planning in the cities for the planned development as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966 and the Development Control Rules, it has been decided to fill 455 sanctioned posts in the cadre of Maharashtra Municipal Council Town Planning and Development Service Group-A with 100% deputation from the officers of the respective cadres on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation.
Now, in order to have uniformity in the sanctioned posts related to Town Planning in the Municipal Councils, the decision of the Contextual Government is being amended to provide that the posts of Maharashtra Municipal Council Town Planner and Development Service Group-C cadre on the establishment of the Directorate of Municipal Administration should be filled up by securing 100% deputation from the officers of Town Planner, Assistant Town Planner and Assistant Design Cadre on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation as indicated in the following statement.
