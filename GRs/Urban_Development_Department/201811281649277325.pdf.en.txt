The Proposal:
Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission Implementation No. The government has been in power in the state since 2015-16. Under this campaign, the construction of water supply, sewerage, storm water drains, urban transport and green area development will be done in the cities. Accordingly, the State Annual Action Plan for the financial year 2017-18 has been approved by the Central Government under this Mission, which includes the Greenfield Development Project of Hinganghat City.
Hinganghat Municipal Council prepared the revised Detailed Project Report of the Green Area Development Project included in the Plan for the financial year 2017-18 under AMRUT Mission. The project has been given technical approval by the Municipal Engineer, Chandrapur Municipal Corporation. The matter of administrative approval of the revised green area development project of Hinganghat town included in the State Annual Action Plan for the financial year 2017-18 of AMRUT Mission was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The revised Green Area Development Project of Hinganghat City included in the State Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government in the financial year 2017-18 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, subject to the conditions and provisions of the Government Decision under paragraph No. The administrative approval is being given by this government decision as mentioned in the prospectus in 2.
