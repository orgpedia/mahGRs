The Proposal: -
The Goods and Services Tax has been implemented throughout the country with effect from 1st July, 2017 as notified by the Central Government. Accordingly, the Goods and Services Tax has also started to be implemented in the state and entry tax, excise, local body tax, cess or any other tax levied and collected under the earlier entry 52 of List-II (State List) of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India has been abolished. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax (Compensation to Local Authorities) Act, 2017, with effect from 1st July, 2017, No. (1) As published by the Department of Finance in the Government Gazette of Maharashtra. For this reason, the issue of disbursement of compensation grants to the state's municipal corporations under the "Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax (Payment to Local Authorities) Act-2017" was under the consideration of the government.
