Introduction: Mr. B.K. Parihar, Branch Engineer, Water Supply and Sewerage Department, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation was on deputation from 1.7.2004 to 11.6.2006. Shri Parihar passed away suddenly on 11.6.2006 while on duty. His family is in distress as no one earns in his house and his wife is a heart patient. The general body of the Municipal Corporation passed a resolution to appoint Mr. Satyam Singh Baldev Singh Parihar, son of Branch Engineer, as a clerk on compassionate grounds as a special case. 82 dated 22.8.2012.
2. Mr. B. K. Parihar, Branch Engineer is an officer in Government service under Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran and was working as a deputation with the Municipal Corporation. Therefore, the Municipal Corporation has forwarded the application dated 11.1.2013 to the concerned office for giving employment to Mr. Parihar's son on compassionate grounds.
The Superintending Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Aurangabad has rejected the proposal of appointment on compassionate grounds as the application for appointment on compassionate grounds was not submitted by Shri Satyam Singh Baldev Singh Parihar within the prescribed time i.e. within one year after the death of his father.
Since the said resolution passed by the general body of the Municipal Corporation is not a joint and charter, the request for the dissolution of the said resolution under section 451 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act has been made by the Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation vide letter dated 2.12.2017.
5. In view of the above, the general body meeting of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 82, dated 22.08.2012, the matter of suspension was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: NAVASHMANPA-2017/ PR No.386/ NAVI-24
Government's decision:
The general meeting of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed a resolution to appoint Satyam Singh Baldev Singh Parihar as a clerk on compassionate grounds. 82 dated 22.8.2012 is not a joint and charter, therefore, the said resolution is being suspended prima facie under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
If the resolution is to be submitted to the concerned in the matter of suspension, they must submit it to the government within 30 days from the date of the suspension order.
