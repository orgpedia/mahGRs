The Proposal:
On 01.11.2018, while rescuing three persons who had fallen into a well at Chakkinaka, Kalyan (East), Shri. Anant Vasant Shelar and Shri. Pramod Waghchaude, both firemen, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation and Dy. on 29.11.2018 at Golden Park, Kalyan (Pt. ) While extinguishing the fire in the shop and doing rescue work. Jagan Govind Amle, the leading fireman, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, was martyred. The general body of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation passed a resolution to confer the status of" martyr" to these 3 firemen of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation after their death. 1 passed through. Similarly, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has requested to provide" Shaheed" status to these 3 jawans who were martyred as well as the concessions and benefits permissible to the martyrs. 2 lettered here. In view of this, the following decisions have been taken by the Government:
Government's decision:
The following employees of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, who lost their lives while carrying out fire and rescue operations in accidents in Kalyan, are being awarded the' martyr' status after their death.
1) K. Anant Vasant Shelar, Fireman.
2) K. Pramod Waghchaude, Fireman.
3) K. Jagan Govind Amle, Leading Fireman.
