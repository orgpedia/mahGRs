The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, in respect of immovable property situated in the city, if any, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent on the value of such property situated in the case of sale or encumbrance of the same and on the amount secured by the encumbrance of the encumbrance of the same in the case of encumbrance of the same. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the government to disburse the arrears of 1% stamp duty surcharge due to 24 municipal corporations in the state from the financial year 2015-16 to 2021-22 to the respective municipal corporations.
Government's decision:
As per the amount available on the Budget Distribution System for disbursement of arrears from 2015-16 to 2021-22 towards 1% stamp duty, the total amount to the Municipal Corporations is Rs. 395,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred and Ninety Five Crores Fifty Lakhs only) is hereby approved by the Government as follows:

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations. The Collector shall draw the said grant without any deduction from the amount due to the Municipalities (without intimation to the Government) and disburse the same to the respective Municipal Corporations before 31st March, 2023.
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be maintained separately by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
The expenditure to be incurred in this regard shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2022-23 by incurring expenditure under the head" Demand No.F-4,3604, payment of compensation and stamp duty amounts to Local Self Government Institutions and Panchayat Raj Institutions, 102, Stamp Duty (01) Stamp Duty due to abolition of VAT (01) (01) Stamp Duty due to restoration of VAT in lieu of Local Body Tax (36040924) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) to Municipal Corporations for payment of 1% surcharge on stamp duty in the Municipal Corporation area."
