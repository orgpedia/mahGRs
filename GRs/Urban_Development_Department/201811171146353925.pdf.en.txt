Preamble: The Government was considering filling up of the vacant posts of Assistant Director Town Planning, Srirampur and the Arbitral Town Planning Scheme by transfer/ deputation from the Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted) cadre officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation. The following Government Orders are being issued regarding the transfer/ deputation of Assistant Director Town Planning:
Government Resolution: As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, as per the terms and conditions and for administrative reasons, the officers of the cadre of Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted) are being transferred and posted on deputation as indicated in the following statement.

The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the transfer of the above officers to the place of posting.
The officers transferred under this order should join the place of deputation immediately on receipt of the executive order regarding the deputation of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Since the above officers are being appointed on transfer in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of permissible posting and joint transfer grant should be sanctioned to them as per rules.
If the officers transferred under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers. It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
