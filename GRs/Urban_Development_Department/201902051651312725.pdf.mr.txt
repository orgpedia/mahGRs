Government Resolution:
Considering the importance of effective Municipal Solid Waste Management the Hon'ble National Green Tribunal (NGT) vide order in (O.A. No. 606 of 2018) dated 20 August, 2018 had directed all the states/UTs to constitute Regional Level Monitoring Committee for effective implementation of the SWM rules, 2016. Accordingly this Government had constituted Regional Level Monitoring Committee (Western Zone) by Government Resolution No-SWM-2018/C.R.-306/UD-34, Dated 21st September 2018 for effective monitoring of the SWM rules, 2016.
2. Further in accordance with Hon'ble National Green Tribunal (NGT) order in O.A. No.
606 of 2018 in matter of compliance of SWM rules, 2016, dated 16 Januray, 2019, the Regional Level Monitoring Committee (Western Zone) formed by Government Resolution No-SWM 2018/C.R.-306/UD-34, Dated 21st September 2018 is hereby dissolved.
