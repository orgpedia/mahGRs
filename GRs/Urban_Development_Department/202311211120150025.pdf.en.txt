The Proposal:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Mission AMR- 2.0, The Government decision dated 07.12.2022 is being implemented in the State from the year 2021-22. Under this mission, the Government has approved the underground sewerage scheme Phase-II (Part-I) for Mira-Bhayander city. The financial outlay of the scheme is as follows:
---------------------------------------------------------
| Central Government (33.33%)       | Rs 58.39 crore    |
| State Government (36.67%)         | Rs 64.24 crore    |
| Share of the corporation (30.00%) | Rs. 52.56.        |
| Total:                            | Rs. 175.19 Crores |
---------------------------------------------------------
2. 30% of the amount of the above Municipal Corporation Account i.e. Rs. 52.56 crore, out of which 80% i.e. 42.04 crore is required to be raised in the form of borrowings from financial institutions.
The time period for completion of the scheme is 2 years as per the decision of the Government. For this purpose, Rs. 42.04 crore is required to be raised in the form of loan from financial institutions. The necessary administrative resolution for this has been passed and the Government was considering to sanction a loan to the Municipal Corporation for the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Underground Sewerage Scheme Phase-II (Part-I) Scheme under Section 109 of the Mumbai Regional Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 from the financial institution Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, which has the lowest interest rate.
Government Decision: Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation 06.07.2023 and D. In pursuance of the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 27.07.2023, after careful consideration, the Project for Subway Sewerage Scheme Phase-II (Part-I) for Mira-Bhayander City has been sanctioned by the Financial Institution at a cost of Rs. 42.04 crore.

As per Section 109 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the government approval should be given subject to the following terms and conditions.
The loan received from MMRDA is to be used only for the project of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Underground Sewerage Scheme Phase-2 (Part-1). The loan amount cannot be used for any other purpose.
2) The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation should take the loan from the concerned financial institution as per the requirement.
(3) The Government shall not guarantee the repayment of the loan.
4) The terms and conditions suggested by the lending financial institution should be mutually agreed upon by the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation and the concerned financial institution.
5) The Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation should take care that the underground sewers constructed from this loan fund will not be dug back for other reasons.
6) The Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation is obliged to take necessary action to increase the income of the corporation and take action to repay the debt fund and the debt fund raised earlier within the prescribed time.
7) No property owned by the government or requiring government approval can be mortgaged in connection with this loan.
The repayment period should not be more than 60 years.
(9) The portion borrowed for carrying out the construction shall not be utilised for payment of salary or allowances to any other officer/ employee other than the municipal officer or employee engaged on the construction.
10) The municipal corporation should establish a debt relief fund for debt relief.
The Commissioner/ Administrator of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation will be responsible for depositing the instalments of repayment of the loan amount to the concerned financial institution within the prescribed period.
