The Proposal -
In view of the digital policy of the state government, the government should maximize the use of computerization of government work.
Consequential to overuse, as well as inconvenience to officers / staff and transfers
In order to avoid delays in the process as well as to ensure consistency in the transfer process and to save precious time of the Government, the Director, Town Planning, Pune as the Secretary / Principal Secretary / Additional Chief Secretary (New-I) is the competent authority to make general / regular transfers of officers / staff belonging to Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadres at the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation. It was under the consideration of the Government to conduct the proceedings of such cadre officers / transfers online. The decision will be taken accordingly.
