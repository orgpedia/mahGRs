Government Decision: Under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), the 11 Municipal Councils which were shown in the accompanying statement, were approved as per the Government Decisions at Reference No. 3 to 14 for the projects of the value shown in Rakaana 4 of the Urban Solid Waste Management Statement.
While the project was being implemented by the Urban Local Bodies, the concerned Urban Local Bodies were requested to approve new items to the cities excluding some items included in the project. (The exclusions and costs of the approved projects are set out in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively of the Statement accompanying this Government Decision.) The items to be approved and their cost in place of the excluded items are indicated in Rakaana 7 and 8 respectively. )

The proposal submitted by these 11 Urban Local Bodies was examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for the approval of the High Powered Committee held on 25.02.2021. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
In accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee to the proposal submitted by the said 11 Urban Local Bodies, the Government hereby approves the deletion of the price items mentioned in Ranks 5 and 6 respectively and the deletion of the price items mentioned in Ranks 7 and 8 respectively.
If the revised Detailed Project Report results in an increase in the cost of the Detailed Project Report as originally approved, the increased cost shall be met by the concerned Urban Local Bodies from the 14th Finance Commission Fund or from the Self-Fund. No additional funds will be provided by the government for this.
(6) With the aforesaid modification, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the respective Urban Local Bodies for solid waste will be as indicated in paragraph 10 accompanying this Government Decision.
It shall be mandatory to comply with the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision on Administrative Sanction of the concerned Urban Local Bodies at Reference No. 3 to 13.
