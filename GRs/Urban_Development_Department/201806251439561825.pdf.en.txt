The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the transfer of the above officers to the place of posting. On receipt of the Executive Order regarding the transfer of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune, the concerned officers should immediately join the place of transfer.
Since the above officers are being transferred in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of postings and joint transfer grants admissible to them as per rules should be sanctioned.
If the officers transferred under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers. It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, State of Maharashtra, Pune, shall entrust the additional charge of the post falling vacant in pursuance of the said transfers to an officer of the same or an equivalent rank in the nearest office or to a senior officer in the same office in the cadre which is junior to the cadre of the officer to be transferred. However, it should be ensured that in no case the transferring officer is assigned additional charge of the post falling vacant due to his transfer.
