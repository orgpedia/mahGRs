In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 1, administrative approval is being given to the water supply project of Tumsar Municipal Council subject to the following conditions: a) Project Implementation Mechanism:• The project should be implemented by Tumsar Municipal Council.
It will be mandatory for the project to appoint Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as Project Management Consultant (PMC). A decision in this regard will be taken by the city corporation.
The project management consultancy charges (PMC charges) equivalent to 3% of the project cost should be provided by the Municipal Council to the Maharashtra Life Authority.
• It will be mandatory for the implementing agency to fulfill all the conditions prescribed by the State Level Project Approval Committee while approving the project.
b) Fulfillment of Reforms: In the context of Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahabhivana No. As per the Government Resolution No. 1, the Urban Local Bodies will be required to complete the following binding and optional reforms. Of these, 80% of the optional conditions must be met by the second year of the project.
(i) Compulsory Reforms: It will be mandatory for the Municipal Councils to fully computerize their activities in the first year of project approval. This will include 100% implementation of e-governance, accounting, and birth and death registration reforms.
To recover at least 80% by applying appropriate consumer tax.
Provision of certain funds in the budget for the poor in urban areas.
• The respective local civic body will be required to complete the two-letter registration process within six months.
• The concerned Urban Local Bodies will be required to complete the property tax assessment in their area within a period of one year from the date of sanction of the project, if it has not been done.

(a) The method of distribution of funds:
After the administrative approval for the projects in the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, the concerned civic body should submit the financial closure report of the project to the Urban Development Department. After that, the first installment of the project will be released.
After disbursing the first installment of funds to the projects approved under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks prescribed by the government as per the prevailing policy.
The second installment of the State Government will be disbursed only after the utilization certificate of 70 percent of the total amount of the first installment of subsidy disbursed by the State Government and the first installment of self-assurance deposited by the urban local body is submitted to the Government through the Directorate of Municipal Administration.
Urban local bodies should submit a proposal to the government through the Directorate of Municipal Administration seeking the second installment. The Directorate of Municipal Administration should scrutinize the proposals and submit them to the government with appropriate feedback.

The bidding process and implementation:
It will be mandatory to issue tenders within one month of release of first instalment of funds to the projects approved under this campaign and to issue work orders within three months and to start the work physically before the 101st day.
The implementing agency shall be bound to follow the instructions contained in the Government Resolution No. 3 in the context while implementing the tender process of the said project.
In case of non-action, the concerned urban local body will be required to return the funds sanctioned for such project to the government along with interest thereon.
• While completing this water supply project, the State Government under reference No. 4 It will be mandatory to follow the procedures prescribed by the government circular here.
• It will be mandatory for the concerned implementing agency to complete the project within the prescribed time frame.
• After the work order of the project, the purchase of the pipes, etc. included in the project should be done under the guidance of the project management consultant according to the requirements of the project.
• The purchase of unnecessary pipes for the project initially and providing it from the project fund will be considered as a serious financial irregularity and the concerned executing agency and project management consultancy will be held responsible for it.
After the State Government has sanctioned the State Government's share to the concerned local body for the project approved under this mission, the local body concerned shall provide self-assurance to the extent of the amount to be provided from the State Government's account in each payment at each stage of expenditure.
With this, there will be no time to pay the share of the local body in such a scheme at once.
The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can only be used for the same project and cannot be diverted or used for any other purpose. Any such diversion of the principal amount or interest thereon, whether permanent or temporary, will be treated as a serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned local bodies/ concerned authorities.
