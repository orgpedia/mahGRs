Introduction: Mr. Pravin Uttam Rathod, Fireman, Amravati City Municipal Corporation has requested to transfer his services from Amravati City Municipal Corporation to Nagpur Municipal Corporation. Accordingly, the Amravati City Municipal Corporation passed General Body Resolution No. 15, d. 17.02.2022 has been approved by the Nagpur Municipal Corporation vide reference No. Under letter No. 3, it has been approved to transfer the services of Mr. Praveen Uttam Rathod, Fireman.
Since the rules for the entry into service of the Amravati City Municipal Corporation and the Nagpur Municipal Corporation have not yet been finalized, Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Municipal Service Recruitment and Classification of Services Rules, 2008, specifies the following provisions for transfer from one corporation to another.
" Notwithstanding anything contained elsewhere in this rule, if an employee of another corporation submits an application for transfer to a post in a corporation, the transfer may be made subject to the following conditions, in accordance with the provisions of the Act and on such terms and conditions as may be agreed upon by the appointing authorities of both the corporations with mutual consent:"
(a) There shall be no transfer of posts. Appointments should be made only to the posts reserved for appointment by nomination.
(b) On such appointment, the seniority of the incumbent shall be zero seniority in the cadre of the post on the date of appointment.
