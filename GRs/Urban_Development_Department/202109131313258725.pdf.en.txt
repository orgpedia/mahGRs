The Proposal:
The civic amenities are provided by various urban local bodies in the state.
For this, various development works are being carried out by the urban local bodies to create infrastructure. In view of the complaints that the quality of the works being carried out is not up to the expected standards, the decision to conduct a technical audit of the quality of such development works by a third party has been taken under the above mentioned government decision. the reputed Engineering Institutions/ Government Engineering in the State to make the aforesaid audit more effective and efficient.
The colleges/ Government Technicians have been selected through" Form-A,"" Form-B" and" Form-C" in the relevant Government Decisions No. 2, 3 and 4. The government was considering to include some more government engineering colleges and government technical colleges in the state in this form.
Government's decision:
To ensure that the development works carried out by the urban local bodies in the state are carried out in a quality manner and generate sustainable value, the quality of these works is enhanced by the tertiary system.
