Preamble: The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has requested to take a decision at the government level regarding the inclusion of temporary employees of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation on the regular establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation in the form of temporary employees on pay scale, minimum pay and wholesale remuneration.
The General Administration Department has issued a circular as 506 employees of 41 cadres out of 545 employees working on temporary pay scale, minimum pay and bulk pay in Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation have been appointed without following the prescribed procedure (advertisement/ written test/ interview). 25.08.2005 and Ma.
In view of the Supreme Court's order in this regard, the said officers/ employees are not eligible for regular induction into the service of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation.
However, the Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has informed that the provisional appointments of a total of 39 officers/ staff in the remaining 5 cadres have been made by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview) and that these staff are holding the educational qualification and qualification prescribed for their post. Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. As per the Government Decision dated 21.08.2017, necessary vacancies are available for these employees.
Similarly, if the services of these employees are regularized, the establishment cost of the corporation will be limited to 35%. Therefore, the matter of regular inclusion of these employees in the service of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.

Regular induction of the said employees on the establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation is being done subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
2.1 The said employees shall be inducted into the service of the Municipal Corporation from the date of the decision of the Government.
2.2 The regular service of all the inducted employees shall be deemed to have been rendered for all purposes from the date of their induction.
2.3) The benefits of Service Merit Pension etc. shall be admissible to them from the date of regular service.
2.4) No arrears of salary, allowances and other incidental financial benefits for the period of service preceding the date of incorporation shall be admissible to them.
2.5) Before induction, the bond should be taken from the concerned employee and it should be clearly mentioned that no benefit will be received in the past service and no demand can be made in the future.
2.6) After the inclusion of these employees, no fund will be made available by the government for their salary.
2.7 The Municipal Corporation shall ensure that the establishment cost shall be within the limit of 35%.
