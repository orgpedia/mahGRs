The Proposal:
The civic amenities are provided by various urban local bodies in the state. For this, various development works are being carried out by the urban local bodies to create infrastructure. In view of the complaints that the quality of the works being carried out is not up to the expected standards, the decision to conduct a technical audit of the quality of such development works by a third party has been taken in accordance with the government decisions in the above context. In order to make the above audit more effective and effective, the Government in the context of reputed engineering institutions/ Government Engineering Colleges in the State vide Government Resolution No. Out of the 2, form A has been selected. The government was considering to include some more government engineering colleges in the state in this form.
Government's decision:
In order to ensure that the development works carried out by the Urban Local Bodies in the State are carried out in a quality manner and generate sustainable value, the concerned Urban Local Bodies have decided to conduct a more effective and effective technical audit of the quality of these works by a third party.
