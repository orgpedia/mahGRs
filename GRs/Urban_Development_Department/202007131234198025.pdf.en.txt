The proposal-
The Supreme Court in Writ Petition No. 82/2011 dated 31.10.2013, the General Administration Department, Government has decided to constitute a Civil Services Board to make recommendations to the competent authority for transfer and posting of Group-A, Group-B, Group-C officers/ employees in the State Government Service. Orders have been issued vide SRV 2014/ PR No. 23/12, dated 31.01.2014. Accordingly, the Urban Development Department, vide Government Decision No. TPV-2414/ No. 31/ NV-27 dated 22.04.2014, has constituted the Civil Services Board-I to recommend to the competent authority for transfer/ posting of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation. The government has decided to change the structure of the Civil Service Board and the following orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
To make recommendations to the competent authority for transfer/ posting of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation. The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary of Tribal Development Department is being appointed as a member in place of Director, Town Planning, Pune in the Civil Service Board-1 established under the Government decision dated 31.01.2014.
Accordingly, the Civil Services Board-I will be responsible for making recommendations to the competent authority for transfer/ posting of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation.

Proposals for posting/ transfer of Group' A' and Group' B' (Gazetted) officers on the establishment of Town Planning and Valuation Department will be submitted to the competent authority/ Government through the Department after the recommendation of the above Civil Service Board-1.
The Director, Town Planning, Maharashtra State, Pune shall submit to the Town Development Department, along with the list of department-wise vacancies in the respective categories, the information including the period of service of the officers eligible for transfer for the postings/ transfers of Group" A" and Group" B" (Gazetted) officers on the establishment of Town Planning and Valuation Department as mentioned above, for the last 10 years.
Proposals for mid-term transfers/ postings to be made in cases of extreme urgency and exceptional circumstances will be submitted to the members of the above mentioned Civil Services Board-1 on a rotational basis.
