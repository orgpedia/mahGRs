In accordance with the decision of the Government No. 02, the said project of Nagpur Municipal Corporation is being approved subject to the following conditions:
3.1 The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation has certified that the said land acquisition project is in the area of Nagpur Municipal Corporation.
3.2 In respect of the work of flyovers, ROBs in the urban areas, it is the primary responsibility of the local bodies/ Municipal Corporations to acquire the land which is not the statutory responsibility of the NHAI/ Central Government.
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation shall certify that the responsibility of acquiring the proposed site lies with the Nagpur Municipal Corporation.
3.3 As per the provisions of the relevant Government Decision, the Municipal Corporation is required to acquire the land by paying TDS for such work and also take action to make changes in the Development Plan where necessary.
Despite such action, it is not possible to edit the place, the concerned collector
Nagpur will be required to certify. The approval is being given subject to the action being taken.

3.4 The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation shall verify and certify that the work of the Centrally Sponsored Project of the flyover and road widening has been started by the Central Government after obtaining technical and administrative approval and that the Central Government has made budgetary provision for the completion of the project.
3.5 The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation shall ensure and certify that the proportion of the consequential investment made by the Central Government in such projects or being made by the Central Government shall not exceed 25% of the total cost of the land to be acquired by the Municipal Corporation.
3.6 Such land acquisition should be done in accordance with the land acquisition policy of the State Government. As per the proposal, the Collector, Nagpur, shall ensure that while considering the compensation to be paid for land acquisition, it is done as per the rates prescribed in the land acquisition policy of the State Government and certify the same.
3.7 As per the provisions of the Government Decision at Reference No. 2, the District Collector of the concerned district shall, before providing funds for the purpose, make necessary scrutiny regarding eligibility of funds required as per the Land Acquisition Policy of the State for land acquisition for the projects, and the demand of the Urban Local Body for funds required for the purpose shall be certified by the Collector of the concerned district. Accordingly, the amount included in the proposal should be considered and certified by the Collector, Nagpur and a certificate should be submitted accordingly. In accordance with this certification, the price of the project shall be revised within the price limits set out in paragraph 2.
3.8 It is necessary for the Municipal Corporation to take a decision to acquire land for this purpose. The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation will be responsible for making such a resolution and submitting it.
3.9 The funds to be disbursed for this purpose will be mainly distributed in three phases. After the administrative approval, the first phase of funds will be disbursed by the state government after the proposal is received from the municipal corporation.
The funds should be made available to the concerned land holder.
3.10 The funds of the first instalment and the share of the Nagpur Municipal Corporation in that proportion, as per the financial figures, shall be borne by the Urban Local Bodies concerned from the Consolidated Fund.

The process of distribution of funds:
5.1 After the administrative approval for the projects in the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, the concerned civic body should submit the financial closure report of the project to the Urban Development Department. After that, the first installment of the project will be released.
5.2 After disbursing the last installment of funds to the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks prescribed by the Government as per the prevailing policy of the Government. The self-financing corporation of the Nagpur Municipal Corporation should be set up from the 14th and 15th Finance Commission Grants in Goods and Services Tax or from self-funding.
5.3 The second instalment of the State Government will be disbursed after submitting the utilization certificate of 70% of the total amount of the first instalment of subsidy and the equivalent self-assurance fund of the Municipal Corporation to the Government through the concerned Collector.
5.4 Proposal for demand of second installment by Urban Local Bodies The concerned Collector should scrutinize the proposals and submit the proposal to the Government with appropriate feedback.
Non-Direction of Funds:- The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the sanctioned projects under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can only be used for that project and it cannot be diverted or used for any other work. Any such diversion, whether in any other form or temporary, will be considered as serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned local bodies/ concerned authorities.
