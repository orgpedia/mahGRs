The Proposal: -
Under section 147-A (9) of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils and Industrial Towns Act 1965, in the case of articles in a municipal area, in the case of sale or donation of property so situated in a municipal area, a surcharge of 1 per cent is levied on the value of the property and, in the case of a consumable mortgage account, on the amount secured by that article, as specified in that article.
The Government's Decisions:
For disbursement of arrears to the respective Municipal Councils / Nagar Panchayats for the financial year 1 2014-15 to 2024-25, the Finance Department has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 200,61,24, 000 / - (Rupees Two Hundred Crores Sixty One Lakh Twenty Four Thousand Only) is made available for distribution on BDS.
The funds are being sanctioned for disbursement to the respective Nagar Panchayats / Municipal Councils from the amount of arrears reported by the Inspector General of Registration and Controller of Stamps, Pune for the period 2014-15 to 2024-2025.
However, the 119 Municipal Councils of Ahmedpur Yavatmal, Osa, Bhokar, Morshi, Beed, Parli, feel, Mudkhed, Sindkhed Raja, Bhadgaon are yet to fully repay the loan taken from the Maharashtra Urban Infrastructure Development Company Limited (MUIDCL) company for various projects.
