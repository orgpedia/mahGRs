The Proposal:
Detailed instructions and timelines regarding the action to be taken in respect of the funds allocated to the Urban Local Bodies under various schemes by the Urban Development Department for non-utilization within the prescribed time frame are given in the context of Schedule to Government Decision No. 1,2,3 and 4 respectively. It was under consideration of the government to revise the deadline.
Government's decision:
The funds released to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats by the Urban Development Department during the financial years 2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 are yet to be spent.
The deadline has been extended to September 30, 2019.
Reference No.2. This Order shall not apply to funds already deposited by the Government under the Government Decisions 1,2,3 and 4.
