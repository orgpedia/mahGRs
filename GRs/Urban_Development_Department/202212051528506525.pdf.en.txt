The Proposal:
The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation's Revised Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 have been amended vide Reference No. The 4. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019. In this service entry rule, there is a condition of 5 years of service in the lower cadre for promotion. In the state government, generally, 3 years of experience in a lower cadre post is required to be eligible for promotion. Accordingly, the amended Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation have been amended and the revised portion of the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 has been made available in the Rules vide Reference No. The decision has been taken in accordance with Government Order No. 5.
