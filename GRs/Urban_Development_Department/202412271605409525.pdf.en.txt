Introduction: - The Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2021 have been amended. Approval has been granted under the Government Decision of 09.04.2021. A proposal to amend the Service Admission Rules for the post of Assistant Manager (Theatre) in the said Service Admission Rules is under consideration. 12.08.2024 and d. The VARA of 21.09.2024 has been submitted by the Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation for government approval.
Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. 12.08.2024 and d. Proposals for 21.09.2024 b. Admin Resolution No. 6852 In view of this, the Government was considering amending the Service Admission Rules for the post of Assistant Manager (Theatre) to facilitate administrative work.
The Government's Decisions:
As per Annexure-I '* Schedule-III * of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the Government is approving amendments in the Admission to Service Rules under Sections 454, 455 and 457 (3) in the procedure for appointment of Qualifications B of the post of Assistant Manager (Theatre) on establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation.
This rule may be called as the amended part of the "Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Revised Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2021."
