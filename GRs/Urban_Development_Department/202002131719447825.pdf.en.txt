Government Decision: Under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), Aurangabad Municipal Corporation has decided to spend Rs. The Civil Solid Waste Management Project costing Rs. 91,79,50, 000/- (Rupees One Ninety Nine Crore Eighty Nine Lakh Fifty Thousand only) has been accorded administrative approval as per the Government Resolution at Reference No.2.
While giving administrative approval to the project, it was proposed to process all solid waste generated in Aurangabad city in a centralized manner. However, as the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation did not have the space required for such centralized processing, the committee formed under the chairmanship of the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, decided to set up processing centers at three places in the city in a decentralized manner. Accordingly, the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation (AMC) has requested for a revised administrative approval by making changes in the approved project report.
Accordingly, the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation has allocated Rs. The revised budget of Rs. 148,79,03, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Forty Eight Crores Eighty Nine Lakhs Three Thousand only) has been submitted to the State Mission Directorate with the technical approval of Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
After the proposal was scrutinized at the level of State Mission Directorate and approved by the State Level Technical Committee, the proposal was placed in the meeting of the High Powered Committee held on 30/08/2019. In the meeting, the high-powered committee has approved the revised project report of Aurangabad Municipal Corporation.
