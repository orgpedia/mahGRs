2.R. State Level Technical Committee. Minutes of the 6th meeting held on 04.08.2023.
Preamble: Implementation of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 in the State on the lines of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 of the Central Government Reference No. 1 has been started here as per the government decision. The main objective of this campaign is to make all the cities of the state garbage-free, which includes scientific management of all types of solid waste in the city and sustainable sanitation and treatment of used water.
The proposal received for the solid waste management project of Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0 was approved by the State Level Technical Committee of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban). This has been approved in the 6th meeting held on 04.08.2023.
The proposal had received the government's approval.
Government Decision: State Level Technical Committee approved the proposal of Solid Waste Management Project of Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0. As per the approval given in the 6th meeting dated 04.08.2023, the administrative approval is being granted subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph 2 as mentioned in the following table.

(Additional Cost will be borne by ULB) 2. Terms and Conditions regarding Solid Waste Management Project of Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation as mentioned above:
The implementing agency for the said projects shall be the concerned Urban Local Bodies.
2.2) The work of the above-approved project should be carried out immediately through the e-tendering process.
2.3. It shall be mandatory for the concerned ULBs to follow all the extant rules while implementing the e-tendering process and to adopt the model tendering procedure and procedures prescribed by the Central Government for the same.
2.4. It shall be ensured that the expenditure incurred under the said project does not violate the Model Code of Conduct.
2.5 The operational machinery shall maintain a record of the status of the works under the project before commencement and after completion of the works.
2.6) The guidelines prescribed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) and the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) for the implementation of solid waste management projects in the cities of the country should be followed.
2.7) The progress report of the works of the above projects should be submitted to the Directorate every month. The concerned Commissioner will be responsible for the implementation of the project within the stipulated time.
The distribution of funds:
3.1 The share of the Centre, the States and the Urban Local Bodies for the said project shall be as mentioned in the Table to paragraph 1. The excess amount excluding the share of the Centre, State and Urban Local Bodies should be paid by the respective Urban Local Bodies from their own funds.
After the project is approved for the urban local body, the entire responsibility of any increase in the original project cost for any reason will remain with the concerned urban local body. No additional funds will be provided for this.
