Introduction: - The country's first school for women was started on 9 January 1848 by Krantijyothi Savitribai Phule in the income of Pune Peth Wednesday Gharana 257 / C1 and 257 / C2 Bhidewada.
With this income, a memorial of Krantijyothi Savitribai Phule is being built by the Pune Municipal Corporation. Also Mahatma Phule Memorial at Peth Ganj and Krantijyothi Savitribai Phule Memorial are being consolidated and expanded. It was under the consideration of the government to set up an advisory committee to coordinate the works of the proposed monuments and other ancillary matters.
Government Decision: - This Government is approving the constitution of "Mahatma Phule Wada and Bhide Wada Memorial Advisory Committee" by Pune Municipal Corporation at Bhidebada for coordination of Krantijyothi Savitribai Phule Memorial at Peth Ganj and Mahatma Phule Memorial and Krantijothi Savitribai Phule Memorial at Bhidebada for amalgamation and expansion works and other ancillary matters. Mr. Nilesh Dashrath Girme is being appointed as the Chairman of this Advisory Committee. The other members will be appointed separately by the B Member Secretary.
The government decision is available on the Maharashtra government's www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
