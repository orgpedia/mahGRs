The Proposal:
As per the resolution of the general body meeting of the Amravati Municipal Corporation, the Commissioner, Amravati Municipal Corporation, Amravati, issued the order. Prashant Gavner 3) Ms. Sharada Ra. A proposal has been submitted to get the government's approval to accommodate Gulhane in the vacant post of health inspector in the municipal corporation. These temporary health inspectors have been appointed as Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation's Referral No. 2 and Municipal Corporation No. The matter of approving the recruitment to the vacant post of Health Inspector in the Amravati Municipal Corporation on the lines of the decision of the previous government was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Sharda Gulhane 3) Mr. Prashant Gavner has been appointed as the temporary Health Inspector in the prescribed manner following the prescribed procedure. The appointment of Sharda Gulhane as one of the 2 vacancies for the post of Immovable and Shri Prashant Gavner as one of the 2 vacancies for the post of Immovable is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
1.1 The service of the said employees shall be regularized in the service of the Municipal Corporation from the date of the decision of the Government.

(2) The regular service of such employees shall be deemed to have commenced from the date of their regularisation for all purposes.
1.3) The benefits of Service Merit Pension etc. shall be admissible to them from the date of regular service.
1.4) No arrears of salary, allowances and other incidental financial benefits for the period of service before the date of regularisation shall be admissible.
1.5) Take a bond from the said employee before regularization. It should be clearly mentioned in the bond that no previous service/ financial benefit will be available in it, nor can it be demanded in the future.
1.6) No funds will be provided by the Government for the salary of the employee after his regularization.
1.7) The Municipal Corporation shall ensure that the establishment cost shall be within the limit of 35% after the induction of the said employees.
