The Proposal: -
With a view to meet the shortfall in fire service of D-category Municipal Corporations / Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in the State and to take various necessary measures to empower the Fire Service in the State. The government has decided to implement the Maharashtra Fire Protection Fire Protection Mission on 31.08.2009. Reference No. 2 The newly formed D-category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats have been included in the Maharashtra Fire Protection Mission as per the government decision here. Reference No. Under Sections 3 and 4, the type of fire station and fire fighting vehicle, the expenditure limit, and the share of government / urban local bodies have been determined according to the standards of urban local bodies.
Under the present scheme, a proposal for administrative approval / grant-in-aid is submitted to the government through the concerned Urban Local Bodies Fire Service Directorate after technical approval from the Public Works Department / Maharashtra Life Authority regarding the fire station and Maharashtra Fire Service Directorate regarding the fire vehicle. Administrative approval in this regard is given and the Government share is handed over to the Directorate for making the funds available to the respective Urban Local Bodies. The construction, procurement, tendering process, etc. are then carried out by the respective Urban Local Bodies.
