However, since the posts of' computer drivers' are not approved in the figure, it is necessary to create a maximum number of posts equal to the number of such employees on the municipal corporation establishment. The pay scale of the post will be S-6, 19900-63200 as per 7th Pay Commission. If the services of these employees are regularized, the establishment cost of the corporation will be limited to 35%. Therefore, the matter of creating more number of posts for these employees and appointing them to those posts was under the consideration of the government.
Decision of the Government: Since the appointment of 51 employees of the' Computer Driver' cadre working on a temporary basis in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation establishment on wholesale remuneration was made in 2007 by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), the Government is approving the creation of a maximum number of posts equal to the number of these employees in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation establishment, their permanent appointment in the pay scale of S-6, 900-63200 as per the 7th Pay Commission, with the designation of' Computer Driver' and regularizing them in the Municipal Corporation service.
The said 51 employees as per Return' A' are being given permanent appointment to 51 supernumerary posts created on the establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1) The number of posts of computer operators created for these employees should be given permanent appointment from the date of the decision of the government.
(2) The employees shall be entitled to the benefits of permanent service (pay, seniority, pension, etc.) from the date of their posting to the subordinate posts.
(3) No benefit or arrears of service rendered earlier shall be admissible to such employees.
4) No funds will be provided by the government for the salary of these employees.
5) Before being reinstated in service, it will be clearly mentioned that no previous benefit will be received by taking a bond from the concerned employee.
6) The above 51 computer drivers will be given permanent appointment as per the rules after checking the attendance, default record, inquiry, service attendance, caste validity verification certificate, medical certificate, etc. It will be mandatory for the concerned employees to submit the caste verification, character verification report within a period of 6 months from the date of order of permanent recruitment.
7) No one other than the 51 computer driver employees on wholesale wages currently working in Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation can be appointed to this number of posts.
(8) If any of the said office falls vacant for any reason, it shall automatically lapse with effect from that date.
9) The Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation should ensure that the pay scale of these employees while appointing them to the post of Superintendents should be as per the pay scale prescribed by the government.
