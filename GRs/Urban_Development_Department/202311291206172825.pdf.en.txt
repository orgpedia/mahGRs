Preamble: Mr. Kiran Prabhakar Babade, on the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, has violated Rule-16 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, by accepting a private job, despite being employed as a peon in the service of Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Corporation. Therefore, they have been given the permission under Section-56 (2) (c) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has been reduced from service vide order dated 04.02.2015.
2. In pursuance of the appeal filed by Mr. Babade before the Standing Committee against the impugned order of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, the Standing Committee has decided to file a status report in the matter. Resolution No. 25.03.2015 As per Section 135, Mr. Babde is disabled, his license is gone and he should be hired.
However, against Mr. Court (5th), Kalyan at Summary No. A case under section 1631/2013 has been registered and the matter is pending before the Kalyan court. The action taken by the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation is appropriate and in accordance with the rules. Therefore, since the resolution passed by the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Standing Committee Meeting is inconsistent with the Act, the implementation of this resolution will not be joint. Stating that the action of the municipal administration is appropriate, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, has requested the government to cancel the resolution as per Section-451 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
In view of the aforesaid facts as well as the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, the Standing Committee on Re-employment of Mr. Kiran Babde, Safai Karmacharis passed Resolution No. 135, p. 25.03.2015 as it is administratively and legally against the Municipal Corporation, the matter of suspension of the said resolution under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act was under consideration of the Government. Following this, the government took decision on this issue.
