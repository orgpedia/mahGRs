As per the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Services (Recruitment and Classification of Services) Rules, 2010, the following provisions have been made in respect of appointment by permanent transfer: Notwithstanding anything contained elsewhere in this rule, if an employee of another Municipal Corporation submits an application for appointment by transfer to a post in a Municipal Corporation, the appointment may be made by transfer subject to the following conditions as per the provisions of the Act and on such terms and conditions as may be mutually agreed upon by the appointing authorities of both the Municipal Corporations: a) No transfer shall be made to the posts to be filled by promotion. Appointments should be made only to the posts reserved for appointment by nomination.
(b) On such appointment, the seniority of the incumbent shall be zero seniority in the cadre of the post as on the date of appointment.
