Preamble: The Central and State Governments have embarked on an ambitious mission to provide houses to all homeless families by 2022. For this, the Central Government has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in 382 cities and adjoining planning areas of the state. Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, beneficiaries in urban areas are selected on the basis of their ownership and annual income and the approved action plan is implemented through an operational mechanism. For this, the Housing Department has issued the above mentioned No. Policy 1 is defined. According to this policy, development is expected to be done where the slums are.
However, according to this policy, the eligible beneficiary who does not have his own place or such eligible beneficiary cannot take advantage of this scheme. As a result, such a person can be deprived of this ambitious scheme.
For this. As per the decision of the Cabinet in its meeting held on 13/11/2018, in respect of encroachers of any type of government land under the jurisdiction of the Revenue Department, the above mentioned No. Guidelines have been issued under the government decision here. Accordingly, it has been seen that the effective implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana is helping in a big way.
On the same lines, the above mentioned No. 02 Proposal to make the guidelines in the Government decision here applicable to all the remaining departments of the State Government (excluding the Forest Department) in order to provide the benefit of this scheme to the encroachers of the land in the urban areas.
