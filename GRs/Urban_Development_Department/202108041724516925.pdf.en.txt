Preamble: In pursuance of the order dated 28.03.2020, the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India has implemented the Insurance Coverage Scheme for Health Care Workers.
The state government has also announced a compensation of Rs. 50 lakh insurance cover/ ex-gratia subsidy scheme has been implemented.
However, the scheme was not applicable to those who died due to corona infection while performing corona-related duties, except health workers in urban local bodies. Under this, the government was considering to implement a scheme of compassionate assistance like the above scheme to the employees who are conducting survey of corona patients, their detection, tracking, prevention, testing, treatment and ancillary relief work.
Government's decision:
