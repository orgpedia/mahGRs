The Director, Municipal Administration shall immediately transfer the incentive amount to the bank account of the concerned Urban Local Bodies through RTGS as indicated in para 10 of the accompanying prospectus.
The second instalment of the remaining 50% of the funds of Swachh Survekshan 2018 and Swachh Survekshan-2019, as mentioned in the Government Resolution at Reference No.3, will be admissible in case of non-Hindustan cities having achieved at least ODF+ status and 3 star rating, after having been ranked 1st to 10th nationally in Swachh Survekshan 2020. So, in case of non-Amrit cities, it will be permissible for that city to achieve at least ODF+ status and 3 star rating, after it comes in the 1st to 50th rank at the national level in Swachh Survekshan-2020.
The Commissioner, Municipal Corporation as well as the Chief Officer, Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat concerned should ensure that the amount of these incentive prizes to be given to the Urban Local Bodies is utilized only on the matters prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 25th July, 2019 in reference No. 4.
