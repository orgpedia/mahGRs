Reference- Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, dated 28.04.2015
The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was promulgated with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their growing expectations from the local administration. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28.04.2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority shall, within a period of three months from the date of promulgation of the Ordinance and from time to time thereafter, notify the public servants, designated officers, first and second Appellate Authorities and the time limits prescribed by it under the Ordinance. Accordingly, the government was considering to notify the time limit, designated officer, first and second appellate officer for the services of Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The Government hereby approve the notification of Schedule-A services in the Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015. The details of the period of public service for the services provided by the Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority under the Urban Development Department, the office of the service notifier as well as the Designated Officer, First Appellate Officer, and Second Appellate Officer are mentioned in Schedule-A.
Accordingly, the Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority should take action to notify this service in the government gazette.
(2) The designated officer responsible for providing services mentioned in Schedule' A', the First and Second Appellate Officer shall take action to provide services to the citizens within the stipulated time.
