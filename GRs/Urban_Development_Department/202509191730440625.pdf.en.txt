Introduction - Simhastha Kumbh Mela is being held in Nashik and Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik in the year 2027-2028. A large number of devotees / tourists are expected. In order to ensure the successful conduct of the Kumbh Mela, planning and effective implementation are in order to provide large-scale connectivity, transport, water supply, accommodation, health care, electricity, law and order, parking, etc.
In view of the above facts, the government was considering operationalising the administrative machinery for better planning and effective implementation. Accordingly, Government Decision No. Aquarius 2023 / Q. No. 183 / Nov-25 Formed under 14.12.2023. Also, the Government affidavit No. Kumbhamay 2022 / P.No. 183 / NV-25D. Under the 03.09.2024, a clean sheet has been issued for the planning of Simhastha Kumbh Mela by amending the members and member secretaries of various committees as well as the scope of the committees. Also, the Nashik-Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority Act, 2025 dated 10 July 2025 has been published in the Government Gazette of Maharashtra to establish the Simhastha Kumbh Mela Authority to coordinate with the Central Government, State Governments and other relevant agencies.
The government decided to consolidate the decision. 14.12.2023 and the government certificate. The issue of issuing a revised governance decision by overturning the 03.09.2024 was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government has decided to set up the following various Committees by superseding the said Government Decision.

(b) The office of the Executive Committee: -
1) Scrutinize and recommend the planning framework for approval by the summit committee.

3) Scrutiny of the report submitted by the District Level Committee on the quality of work and submission to the Summit Committee.

5) Suggest all necessary measures to ensure that the implementation of the project is completed within the stipulated time.
6) To approve the proposal for creation of necessary posts for Nashik-Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority.
