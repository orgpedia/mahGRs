The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is being implemented in the State since 2015-16 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Under AMRUT Mission, the Water Supply (Akkalpada Udyog) Project of Dhule City, which was included in the plan for the financial year 2017-18, was given administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in the context. Subsequently, the administrative approval of the Dhule City Water Supply (Akkalpada Udyog) project was under consideration of the government in view of the change in the price of the components of the project.
Government's decision:
The administrative approval granted under the Government Resolution dated 14.02.2019 to the Dhule City Water Supply (Akkalpada Udyog) Project included in the State Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government for the year 2017-18 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is subject to other terms and conditions of the relevant Government Resolution as under: 2 and 3 are being revised as mentioned in the prospectus.
