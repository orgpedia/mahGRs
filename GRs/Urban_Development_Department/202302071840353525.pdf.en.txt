Introduction: Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation is a' D' class Municipal Corporation. The total area covered by the Municipal Corporation is 29.84 square kilometres. During the period of Ichalkaranji Municipal Council, the office of the Commissioner and Director Municipal Administration, Mumbai, as their competent authority, had sanctioned the figure for a total of 1883 posts as per the orders at serial numbers 1 to 4 referred to. In the meantime, Ichalkaranji Municipal Council has been converted into a Municipal Corporation under the Government Notification No. 5. For the immediate performance of the functions and duties of the Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation, the government had approved the delineation of 43 posts in 34 cadres under the government decision at serial number 6.
In the meantime, with the conversion of Ichalkaranji Municipal Council into a Municipal Corporation, it is necessary to determine the contours by determining the number of posts for Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949. Accordingly, the Commissioner and Administrator, Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation, vide Administrative Resolution No. 1/2022 D. It has been approved on 04.01.2023 and it is proposed to approve the figure of total 1744 posts.

As per the provisions of Section 51 (1) (2) (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, the Municipal Commissioner is required to prepare a statement and place it before the Standing Committee stating the officers/ employees, their number, their designations, ranks, salaries, fees, allowances etc. After this, the standing committee will have to submit the approved statement along with the changes to the government. The state government then has the power to approve the posts under Article 51 (4).
Considering the above, the Commissioner and Administrator has proposed to the Government to abolish 459 posts and create 277 new posts by combining the figure of 1883 posts approved for the then Ichalkaranji Municipal Council and the figure of 43 posts approved by the Government under the Government Decision at serial number 6. It was for the government to approve it. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the resolution No. 01/2023 passed by the Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation on 04.01.2023 and its letter dated 04.01.2023 proposed to the Government, in exercise of the powers vested in the Government under Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, the Government hereby approves the sanction of a figure of 1744 posts for a total of 40 offices for the Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation as mentioned in Annexure-4.
The revised figure shall include the 1883 posts (as per Annexure-I) sanctioned earlier by the then Commissioner for Municipal Councils and Director, Directorate of Municipal Council Administration vide orders in serial numbers 1 to 4 referred to from time to time and the figure of 43 posts (as per Annexure-II) sanctioned by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 27/7/2022.
As per the proposal of the Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation, a total of 459 posts as mentioned in Annexure III are being approved for abolition and 277 newly created posts as mentioned in Annexure V are being approved by the Government.
As per the government policy, the establishment cost of the municipal corporation should be 35%. Considering the reasons given in the letter dated 06.01.2023, the Government is approving the relaxation in the condition of establishment cost. However, according to the revised figure of the Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation, the expected establishment cost is 56.39 percent, so the corporation has to pay the post to keep a limit on this cost.
