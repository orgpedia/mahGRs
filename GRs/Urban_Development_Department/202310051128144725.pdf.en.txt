The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was promulgated with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their growing expectations from the local administration. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28.04.2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority shall, within a period of three months from the date of promulgation of the Ordinance and from time to time thereafter, notify the public servants, designated officers, first and second Appellate Authorities and the time limits prescribed by it under the Ordinance. Accordingly, the details of the public service provided by the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority from time to time, as well as the documents required for the public service, fees, fixed time limit, designated officers, first and second appellate officers, are to be notified as per section (3) under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act 2015.

Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to notify the services under Schedule-A of the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015. The details of the duration of public service, office to which the service is notified, as well as the designated officer, first appellate officer, and second appellate officer for the services provided by the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority under the Urban Development Department have been mentioned in the Schedule-August. Accordingly, the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority should take action to notify this service in the government gazette.
(2) The designated officer responsible for providing services mentioned in Schedule' A', the First and Second Appellate Officer shall take action to provide services to the citizens within the stipulated time.
3) The details of the services mentioned in Schedule' A' should be prominently displayed in legible letters on the board of every office of the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
4) The services mentioned in Schedule' A' should be made available on the Dashboard of Right to Service on the web portal of" Your Government" with the help of" MahaIT."
The user ID and password should be provided to the concerned designated officers of Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority through MahaIT.
MahaIT will be responsible for providing technical support to make this service available on the" Apna Sarkar" portal.
