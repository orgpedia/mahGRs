Preamble: The Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation vide reference No. Through letters No. 1 and No. 2, a proposal was submitted to the government for a plot for the establishment of a hospital in the Thane Municipal Corporation area for the medical institution" Sankara Nethralaya."
In pursuance of the above proposal of the Thane Municipal Corporation, it is proposed to allot a plot of land to Sankara Nethralaya for setting up of a hospital in the Thane Municipal Corporation area at a lease rent of 30 years at a rate lower than the market rate. Approval has been granted subject to the conditions specified in the Government Decision No. 3. However, the Thane Municipal Corporation, under reference No. 4 of the said Government Order vide letter No. Amendments to sections 1, 7 and 8 are requested.
