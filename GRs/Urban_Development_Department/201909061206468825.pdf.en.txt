Preamble: In the 110th meeting of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority held on 28th May, 2004, the broad contours of the Mumbai Metro line prepared by the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation Limited and its implementation phase were approved. The total length of 9 metro lines is 146.50 km and was expected to be completed by 2021.
While formulating the detailed project report and finalising the implementation strategy, the government has approved 275.3-km-long metro line as per the revised metro route master plan.
The Kalyan-Taloja section of Mumbai Metro Line 12 is 20.756 km (elevated) and includes 17 stations. Detailed Project Report (DPR) of the project has been prepared.
The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has been appointed as the consultant and the consultant has submitted a brief working report of the project to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority in the month of October, 2018 along with the detailed project report of the metro project submitted by the consultant to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for approval of the Government. Accordingly, the state government had approved the project. Following this, the government decided:
