Preamble: The matter of posting of Director, Assistant Director on Establishment of Town Planning and Value Assessment Department, officers of Town Planning Cadre by mid-term transfer/ deputation was under consideration of the Government. The government has issued the following orders in this regard:

The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the above officers to join the place of posting by transfer/ deputation.
The officers who have been posted on deputation under this order should join the place of transfer/ deputation immediately on receipt of the executive order regarding the transfer/ deputation of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune.

Since the above officers are being transferred/ deputed in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of permissible posting and joint transfer grant should be sanctioned to them as per rules.
If the officers transferred/ posted on deputation under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers. It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune, shall entrust the additional charge of the post falling vacant in pursuance of the said transfer/ deputation to an officer of the same or an equivalent rank in the nearest office or to a senior officer in the same office in the cadre which is junior to the cadre of the officer to be transferred. However, it should be ensured that in no case the transferring officer is assigned additional charge of the post falling vacant due to his transfer.
