The Proposal:
The Government was contemplating to constitute a Committee to amend the provisions of the Land Acquisition and Compensation of Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 in accordance with the provisions of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 and the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2018 and to incorporate the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 in the same after considering the provisions of Section 105A of the Right to Transparency Act, 2013. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
It is being approved to constitute a committee to amend the provisions of the Land Acquisition of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974. The composition and functions of the Committee are as follows:
(a) Composition of the Committee: A. No. Designation of Member 1. Additional Metropolitan Commissioner, Chairman Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority
Additional Collector, Member, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority
Joint Secretary (Land Acquisition), Revenue and Forest Department Member 4. Deputy Secretary (NV-7), Urban Development Department Member 5. Under Secretary (NV-7), Urban Development Department Member Secretary

(a) The functions of the Committee:
The proposal to amend the land acquisition provisions of the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 in accordance with the provisions of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 and the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2018, and to incorporate the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 in the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013, after taking into account the provisions of Section 105A of the Act, shall be submitted to the Government within one month.
