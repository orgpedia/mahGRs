Government Resolution No. AMRUT-2023/ PR No. 46/ NV-33 4.1 The Aurangabad Municipal Corporation will be the implementing agency for this project.
4.2 The share of the local body for the project should be paid by the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation.
4.3 The implementing agency shall be bound to implement the said project as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the decision of the Government in the context of the State Government.
4.4 The State Government vide No. It shall be incumbent upon the concerned executing agency to complete the tendering process and execution of the project within the prescribed time frame as per the procedures prescribed under Sections 2 and 3.
4.5 The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will be required to publish the model tender booklet issued for the projects under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
4.6 The implementing agency shall ensure that the work orders for the said project are issued within 45 days from the date of order of administrative sanction.
4.7 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the mission city to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.
The Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%) as per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission. Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.
4.9 While raising self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on the outcome of functional outcomes. Also, the project will not be funded if it does not meet the functional outcome. The Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation will be responsible for this.
4.11 The first instalment of funds for the project from the Central and State Governments was disbursed to the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation after issuing the work order.

It will be mandatory for the municipal corporation to open a separate bank account and keep funds in it. The funds allocated for the project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, it will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
The overall responsibility of completing the project within the stipulated time will be shared by the concerned implementing agency, Project Management Consultant and Project Management Development and Management Consultant.
4.15 Once the project work is started, the concerned executing agency will be responsible to make the payment of the work to the concerned contractor within 15 days within the limit of available funds after the project manager consultant certifies the quality of work and payment.
4.16 As per the guidelines under AMRUT 2.0 Mission, under reference No. A project manager should be appointed for the project. As mentioned in it, it will be mandatory for the concerned urban local body to make a decision on the payment of the project manager fee.
4.17 All No Objection Certificates required for the completion of the project and the process of making available the site should be done before giving the project work order.
4.18 It shall be the responsibility of the concerned Urban Local Bodies, the Management Consultant of the project and the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran to ensure that there is no duplication between the sub-plans of the project and the sub-plans of the project which have been approved under any other scheme earlier.
