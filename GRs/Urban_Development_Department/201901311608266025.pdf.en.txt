The Proposal:
Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation has been selected in the Smart City Mission on September 25, 2017. Smart City Mission Guidelines No. Reference No. 10.6 for implementation of Smart City Scheme. Pimpri-Chinchwad Smart City Limited, a Special Purpose Vehicle, was set up by the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation on July 13, 2017.
Reference No. Issue No. 9. 3B (2) specifies that the Commissioner of Police, Pune shall represent the Government on the Board of Directors of Pimpri-Chinchwad Smart City Limited.
However, reference No. As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, the Commissioner of Police, Pune City and the Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural have been divided and the work of the Pimpri-Chinchwad Police Commissionerate has been started.
Accordingly, Reference No. Issue No. 9. The state government was considering amending section 3B (2).
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: SmartC-2018/ P.S.No.507/ NV-33
Government's decision:
Reference No. Issue No. 9. As per Section 3B (2), the board of directors of Pimpri-Chinchwad Smart City Limited is being amended to include the Commissioner of Police, Pimpri Chinchwad, instead of the Commissioner of Police, Pune, as a representative of the government.
The Chief Executive Officer, Pimpri-Chinchwad Smart City Limited should make the said changes in all the records immediately.
