The Proposal:
The Central Government has released Rs. 1489.51 crore towards the first instalment of the basic grant for the year 2019-2020 to be distributed to the Urban Local Bodies in the State as per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission vide order No. The grant was to be distributed to the urban local bodies in the state.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission, the Central Government has released the first instalment of the basic grant of Rs. 1489.51 crore i) Population ii) Area based allocation to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats as indicated in the Annexure.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FFC-2019/ PR. No.51/ NV-4. (2) and (3) shall be taken into account in the procedures set out in the Government Decision and prescribed by separate orders in this behalf.
2) Rs. 1489.51 crore is being handed over to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the Urban Local Bodies as per the Annexure. Immediate action should be taken for disbursal through NEFT/ ECS. Further instructions on the disbursement of these funds are being issued separately.
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai shall immediately submit to the Government the utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the basic grant received from the Central Government for the financial year 2019-2020.
While spending from this subsidy, it will be mandatory to do as per the provisions of the government decision issued by the government as well as the instructions given from time to time. The grant is being distributed as" conditional."
As per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission, the first instalment of the basic grant received for the year 2019-2020 is being disbursed to the D category Municipal Corporations, Municipalities and Nagar Panchayats as mentioned above and the expenditure is being met from" Demand No. F-2, 2217Urban Development, 800, Other Expenditure, (00) (03) As per the recommendations of the Fourteenth Finance Commission, the expenditure to be incurred under the head of Subsidiary Grants to Urban Local Bodies (KSPYO) (Compulsory), (22179741) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) shall be deducted from the provision available for the current financial year 2019-2020.
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration are being declared as Controlling Officers and the concerned Assistant Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the purpose of collection and disbursement of the said funds.
