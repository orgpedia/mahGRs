(1) Government Resolution No.24/ No.16 dated 27th June, 2012.
Preamble- Special financial assistance is provided for the development of civic amenities in the newly demarcated areas of Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities in the State.
The criteria in this regard have been prescribed by the Government under reference No. (1). The matter of amending the power to grant administrative approval under the said scheme was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION- In the context of the special financial assistance scheme for the development of civic amenities in the newly demarcated areas of Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities in the State, No. (1) The decision of the Government. (2) (i) regarding grant of administrative approval is being amended as follows:
(i) The Collector shall be empowered to grant administrative approval for the works identified at the government level under the scheme from the financial year 2020-2021 and within the limit of the funds sanctioned by the government as per the implementation mechanism.
3. Reference No. (1) All other provisions of this Act shall remain in force.
