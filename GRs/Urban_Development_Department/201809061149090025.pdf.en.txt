Government's decision:
The Government of Maharashtra vide its letter dated 4th May, 2018, has sanctioned a project of Rs. 6.64 crore for acquisition of land which is not the statutory responsibility of the Railway Authority and is the responsibility of the Amravati Municipal Corporation, in accordance with the Rajapeth Railway Flyover in the limits of Amravati Municipal Corporation submitted under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotna Maha Abhiyan.
Administrative approval is being accorded subject to the provisions of the resolution and the amendments proposed therein.
The project will be funded 70% by the state government and 30% by the Amravati Municipal Corporation.

Non-Direction of Funds:- The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the sanctioned projects under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can only be used for that project and it cannot be diverted or used for any other work. Any such diversion of the principal amount or interest thereon, whether permanent or temporary, will be treated as a serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned local bodies/ concerned authorities.
7. Third Party Audit of Projects:- It will be mandatory for Pune Municipal Corporation to conduct third party audit of the projects sanctioned under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission at various stages of implementation by the Director Local Fund Accounts.
Other Binding Terms and Conditions:- After the project is approved by the state government for the urban local body, the entire responsibility of any increase in the original project cost for any reason will be with the concerned Pune Municipal Corporation. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government for this purpose.
