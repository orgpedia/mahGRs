Government Circular The final seniority list of the Principal (Group-A) (Selection Category), Principal (Group-A) and Principal (Group-B) Cadre was published on 01.01.2019 vide Circular dated 27.11.2020 of Urban Development Department.
Now, the provisional seniority list of the Maharashtra Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat Principal (Group-A) (Selection Category), Principal (Group-A) and Principal (Group-B) Cadre as on 01.01.2022 (Dated 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2021) is being published as shown in Annexure-A, Annexure-B and Annexure-C.
The concerned Divisional Commissioner and Regional Director should bring this seniority to the notice of the Divisional Commissioner's office, Municipal Council, Nagar Panchayat, Collector's office and the Chief Officer working in the Municipal Corporation in their revenue division.
Objections/ suggestions, if any, regarding the seniority list of the concerned officers should be submitted through the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Navi Mumbai by 21.10.2022. Applications received after this date will not be considered.
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Navi Mumbai shall constitute a Committee under his Chairmanship and submit the objections received thereunder along with their comments to the Government by 26.10.2022.
