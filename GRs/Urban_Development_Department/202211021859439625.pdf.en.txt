Administrative approval is being given to the Old Navghar Sarovar Rejuvenation Project of Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation subject to the following conditions: 4.1 The implementing agency for the project will be Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation.
4.2 The share of the local body for the project should be paid by the Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation.
4.3 The implementing agency shall be bound to implement the said project as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the decision of the Government in the context of the State Government.
4.4 The State Government vide No. It shall be incumbent upon the concerned executing agency to complete the tendering process and execution of the project within the prescribed time frame as per the procedures prescribed under Sections 2 and 3.
4.5 The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will be required to publish the model tender booklet issued for the projects under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
4.6 The implementing agency shall ensure that the work orders for the said project are issued within 45 days from the date of order of administrative sanction.
4.7 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the mission city to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.
4.8 Methodology of Fund Distribution: As per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 20 Mission, the Central share of funds will be distributed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%). 4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on the operational outcomes. Also, the
Funds will not be allocated to the project if the functional outcome is not met. The Commissioner, Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation will be fully responsible for fulfilling these requirements.

It will be distributed to Bhayander Municipal Corporation. Accordingly, after submission of Utilization Certificate to the Central Government as prescribed by the Central Government and after achieving the operational results, the next instalment of Central share and State share will be disbursed accordingly.
4.12 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
4.13 As per the instructions issued while allocating funds for the project, it will be mandatory for the Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation to open a separate bank account and keep funds in it in proportion to the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments for the project.
The funds allocated for the project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, it will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
4.14 The overall responsibility of completing the project within the stipulated time will be shared by the concerned implementing agency, Project Management Consultant and Project Management Development and Management Consultant.
4.15 Once the project work is started, the concerned executing agency will be responsible to make the payment of the work to the concerned contractor within 15 days within the limit of available funds after the project manager consultant certifies the quality of work and payment.
4.16 As per the guidelines under AMRUT 2.0 Mission, under reference No. A project manager should be appointed for the project. It will be mandatory for the concerned urban local body to make a decision on the payment of the project manager fee as mentioned in it.
4.17 All No Objection Certificates required for the completion of the project and the process of making available the site should be done before giving the project work order.
