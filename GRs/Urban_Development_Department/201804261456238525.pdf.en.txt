In order to improve the financial position of the Urban Local Bodies in the State, it has been decided to implement the Geographical Information System (GIS) based property tax system in the" C" and" D" category Municipal Corporations and all Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State as per the Government decision dated 20.07.2015. Accordingly, the Municipal Council Administration with regard to GIS survey and mapping of properties within the limits of these local bodies
A common tender process is being carried out for all Municipal Councils/ Municipalities for implementation of the scheme through the Directorate. The Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation Limited (MAIT) has been appointed by the Directorate of Municipal Administration. The payments have been submitted to the Directorate of Municipal Councils through MahaIT to meet the expenditure on the scheme. The matter of payment of the said dues was under consideration of the Government as per the proposal submitted by the Directorate of Municipal Council to the Government.
The government has taken the following decisions:
