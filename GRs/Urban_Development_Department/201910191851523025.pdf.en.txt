GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government decision dated 18th September, 2019 regarding the release of Central and State Government funds for the construction of individual household and community/ public toilets to the Urban Local Bodies in the State under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) is hereby revoked.
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
3. Under Swachh Bharat Mission, the State Government has been implementing the scheme of Solid Waste Management. With the approval of the High Powered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary, proposals worth Rs. 318,02,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred Eighty Eight Crores Two Lakhs Only) for 92 cities were submitted to the Central Government. The Central Government vide Reference No. 5 As per the order dated 13.09.2019, out of the Central Government's permissible share of Rs. 108,80,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eighty Crores Eighty Lakh only), a sum of Rs. 54,42,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Four Crores Forty Two Lakh only) has been released to the State as 2nd instalment.
The second instalment of funds released by the Central Government to the State for Urban Solid Waste Management Projects of 92 Urban Local Bodies in the State under Swachh Bharat Mission is Rs. 54,42,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Four Crore Forty Two Lakh only).
