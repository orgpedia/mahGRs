The Proposal: -
The Pune Mero Railway Phase-I project has been approved by the State Government on 23rd December, 2014.
Similarly, approval has been granted by the Central Government vide order dated 09.01.2017. The central and state government's participation in the project is in the form of equity and secondary debt support. The project is also supported by the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and the Pune Municipal Corporation. The project has been financed by the European Investment Bank (EIB) and the French Development Agency (AfD). All such funds are provided by the project implementing agency, Maharashtra Mero Rail Corporation Limited. (Mahamero) is being handed over to this special purpose vehicle company.
The project has been declared as a public project of urgency and an ambitious urban transport project by the state government. The project is in the final stages of implementation through Mahamero and is expected to be completed by the end of December 2023. At present, the physical progress of the project has been achieved at 90 per cent. A tripartite Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed in July 2017 between the Central Government, State Government and Mahamero for implementation of Pune Mero Project. The Government has taken a decision with regard to the said Tripartite Memorandum of Understanding. Subsequent approval has been granted under 31.07.2017.
Out of the total length of the project 33.28 km, 12 km was completed on 06.03.2022. m., 11.66 km on the date 01.08.2023. I have a total of 23.66. I have started the operation of the long Mero corridor.
The remaining 9.62 km of the project is expected to be commissioned soon.
The cost of the Pune Mero Rail project is increasing, leading to the formation of the Maharashtra Mero Rail Corporation Ltd. Nagpur has submitted a proposal for revised expenditure to the government. Accordingly, the proposal for revised expenditure was under consideration of the Government for approval by the State Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
