Government's decision:
The Government decision dated 18th September, 2019 regarding devolution of Central and State funds for capacity building and administrative and office expenses to Urban Local Bodies in the State under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) is hereby rescinded.
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
The Central Government has released the second installment of Rs. 4,76,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy Six Lakh only) to the State under Order No.5 of the said Order for the financial year 2019-20 for the purpose of capacity building, administrative and office expenses under Swachh Bharat Mission.
The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 4,76,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy Six Lakh only) to the Urban Local Bodies in the State. The expenditure shall be met from the provision made under the relevant head of accounts for the financial year 2019-20 as shown in the table below.
