Government Decision, Department of Environment and Climate Change, No.Plastic-2021/ Q.No.53/ T.Q.1, dated 01.11.2021 Government Decision: In pursuance of the Central Government Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the following amendment rules, a Municipal Corporation Level Task Force is being constituted for effective implementation of the ban on single-use plastic in the Municipal Corporations of States with more than one million population as under:
Municipal Commissioner/ Additional Commissioner Municipal Chairman 2. Deputy Commissioner (Solid Waste Management)/ Head of Department dealing with Solid Waste Management matters Member, Secretary 3. Collector Nominated Revenue Officer Member 4. Police Commissioner Nominated Police Officer Member 5. Environment Officer Municipal Corporation Member 6. Education Officer Municipal Corporation Member 7. Social Development Officer Municipal Corporation Member 8. Principal Legal Advisor Municipal Corporation Member 9. District Level Officer of Maharashtra Pollution Control Board Member 10. District Industries Officer Member 11. Public Relation Officer, Municipal Commissioner (Municipal Commissioner may appoint additional members as per requirement)
The Solid Waste Management Department of the Municipal Corporation will act as the nodal department for taking measures to ban single-use plastic. This committee should meet at least once a month.
Duties of the Task Force: To prepare a comprehensive action plan for the implementation of the Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the amendment rules thereunder and for the implementation of the Single Use Plastic Ban with coordinated efforts and resources of various departments at the city level.
