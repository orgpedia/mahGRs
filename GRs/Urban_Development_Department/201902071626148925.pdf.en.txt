The Proposal:
The Blood Transfusion Department under the Public Health Department of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) and other major hospitals of the BMC is responsible for supplying blood to the patients and other patients admitted in the hospital. The department requires a post in the cadre of" Blood Transfusion Officer" as per the Food and Drug Administration norms.
The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation has created posts in the cadre of Blood Transfusion Officer with effect from 11th November, 2004 and the qualifications for these posts have been approved with effect from 6th January, 2005. Computer knowledge and knowledge of Marathi language have been included in the new qualifications and the pay scale of the post has been revised to Rs 15,600, Rs 39,100 and Rs 6,000 as per the 6th Pay Commission. The matter of approving the revised qualifications for the post was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The revised qualifications for the post of" Blood Transfusion Officer" in the establishment of major hospitals and public health department of the Mumbai Municipal Corporation are being approved as per the provision of section 80 (b) (4) of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 as per the attached prospectus" A."
