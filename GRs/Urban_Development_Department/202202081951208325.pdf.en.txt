Government Decision: The detailed project report of the Municipal Councils of Savda, Gadhinglaj and Malegaon-Jahangir for the management of urban solid waste has been published in Reference No. The decisions 2, 3 and 4 were approved by the government. A request was made through the municipal councils to revise the items mentioned in the detailed project report and their cost.
The proposal submitted by the said Municipal Councils has been examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and the proposal has been submitted for approval of the High Power Committee held on 8/11/2021 and it has been approved by the High Power Committee.
The Government is approving the items and their prices mentioned in the statement accompanying this Government decision in accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee. With this change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the Municipal Councils Solid Waste will be as shown in the Annexure 4 of the Statement accompanying this Government Decision. The revised Detailed Project Report (DPR) should be funded by the Municipal Councils from the 14th Finance Commission funds or from their own funds.
