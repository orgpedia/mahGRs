The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. Accordingly, the Central Govt. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities notified under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Post Identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Acts/ Rules-Notification. the above reference. As per the decision of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure the posts in the establishment of subordinate offices of the State Government for the disabled. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring the posts on the establishment of municipal corporations in the state under the Urban Development Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Issued by the Central Government. As per the list published in the Notification dated 4.01.2021 for the Group-A to Group-D Cadre ensured for Persons with Disabilities, the posts for Group-A to Group-D Cadre in the Municipal Corporations under Urban Development Department are being ensured for the persons with disabilities as shown in the table in Annexure-" A."
