02. The Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide letter dated
15/01/2026 has approved the said visit of Maharashtra delegation to Davos, Switzerland.
03. All the members of the official delegation will be entitled for travel expenses and per diem allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
04. Government of Maharashtra is pleased to authorize the absence of the above mentioned delegation from 17" January to 26" January, 2026 (including travel time) and be treated as on duty
until they resume their duties on return to India from their foreign tour.
OS. Government of Maharashtra is further pleased to approve the sanction for processing fees of visa for Switzerland, as per actual and medical facilities admissible, under the revised AMA Scheme continued in the Ministry of External Affairs O.M.No.Q/GA/653/1/74 dated 5/3/1979 for the members
of the delegation. The Government is also pleased to approve the sanctioned charges for medical insurance for them.
06. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation etc. for the members/officers at serial 1, 2
and 4 of the delegation will be borne by Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority
(MMRDA) and for the member/officer at serial No. 3 of the delegation will be borne by City and
Industrial Development Corporation (CIDCO)
07. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions if any should be submitted to the government within one month from the date of returning.
