Government's decision:
A total of 795 temporary posts in various cadres on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, indicated in the" Annexure-A" accompanying this Government Decision, have been sanctioned. 01.03.2019 to D. The period is being extended up to 30.09.2019.
During the said period, all the Administrative Departments shall, in any case, review the sanctioned posts in their establishments and in the establishment of offices under their administrative control and obtain the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries to the revised pattern.
All departments should note that after this, the time period for fixing the revised figure will not be extended. All the administrative departments have been asked to review the posts in their (own) establishment as well as the posts in the establishment of the zonal office under their control and plan to submit the proposal of the revised figure to the Finance Department immediately and take immediate action. The Government Decision dated 21.02.2019 has been issued and should be strictly followed.
