As per section 75A (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Towns Act, 1965, the employees of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Towns shall be officers in the service of the State Government and all rules, regulations or orders made under section 75A (3), as amended from time to time and in force at that time, and applicable to the concerned class of officers in the service of the State Government, are applicable to the employees appointed in the service of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Towns. Article 75A (5) of the Constitution of India provides that the salaries and allowances of municipal employees shall be paid directly from the municipal fund.
