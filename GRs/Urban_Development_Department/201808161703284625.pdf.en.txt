Government Resolution No: MUP-2018/ PR No. 133/ NV-19
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As some of the terms and conditions of the said resolution passed by the General Assembly and Standing Committee of the Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation are inconsistent with the provisions laid down by the Central Government and the State Government, and are beyond the scope of the powers conferred and against or beyond the powers vested in the State Government, in the larger public interest, in order to implement the said scheme within the stipulated period, the powers vested in the Government under Section 451 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, are hereby finally revoked as follows:
(a) The General Assembly Resolution No. 120, dated 30/11/2016 (in part), the following Notice: Notice No. 4" Since the present scheme will be funded from the budgetary provision of Sangli Miraj and Kupwar City Municipal Corporation and from the collection of taxes from the citizens, the tender process will be carried out with the approval of the General Assembly and the Municipal Corporation will have the power to process the tender process, approve the work order, check and save the payments, etc. The Maharashtra government has nothing to do with it. " Notice No. 5" Tenders for the present scheme should not be accepted at a rate higher than the working budget. " Notice No. 7" Since the Government of Maharashtra has approved the appointment of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran as the implementing agency, a fee equal to 3% of the scheme as project management fee should be paid by the Government. The government should not be burdened with that. " (b) The General Assembly, Resolution No. 33, dated 04/07/2017 in full: (a) Standing Committee Meeting, Resolution No. 78, dated 12/07/2017 (partly) in the following matter.
The issue No. 1," If any amount is to be paid in excess of the budget, the amount shall be paid by the Government."
The issue No. 2." On the condition that the Government will pay the project management fee and other taxes."
