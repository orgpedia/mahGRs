Preamble: There are 1176 posts of various cadres as per the approved figure as on 01.03.2006 in the establishment of Town Planning and Valuation Department under Urban Development Department. A proposal to upgrade 12 branch offices in 12 districts of Sindhudurg, Ratnagiri, Dhule, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gadchiroli, Wardha, Beed, Jalna, Latur, Osmanabad and Buldhana under the control of Assistant Director, Town Planning, and to create 60 regular posts and 18 notional posts in various cadres was submitted to the approval of the high-level committee of secretaries of the Finance Department.
The High Level Committee of Secretaries of Finance, in its meeting held on 08.02.2019, vide its minutes dated 20.02.2019, has approved creation of following 78 new posts in various cadres in addition to 1176 posts in various cadres sanctioned earlier in the establishment of Town Planning and Valuation Department. A government order has already been issued in this regard.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the decision of the High Level Committee of Secretaries, Finance Department in its meeting held on 08.02.2019, it is hereby approved to upgrade 12 Branch Offices in 12 districts of Sindhudurg, Ratnagiri, Dhule, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gadchiroli, Wardha, Beed, Jalna, Latur, Osmanabad and Buldhana, which are headed by the Office of the Town Planner on the establishment of Town Planning and Valuation Department under the Urban Development Department, and to create 60 regular posts and 18 notional posts in various cadres under the Directorate as indicated in the following statement.
