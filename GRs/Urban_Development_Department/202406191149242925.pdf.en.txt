The Centrally Sponsored PM e-Bus Service Scheme has been approved on 16th August, 2023. Under this scheme, the central government proposes to run 10,000 e-buses across the country through various states and union territories. The central government has implemented this scheme for 169 cities in the country, including 23 municipal corporations in the state.
