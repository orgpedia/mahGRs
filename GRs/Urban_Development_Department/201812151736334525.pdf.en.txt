The Proposal:
Detailed instructions and timelines regarding the action to be taken in respect of the funds allocated to the Urban Local Bodies under various schemes by the Urban Development Department for non-utilization within the prescribed time frame are given in the context of Schedule to Government Decision No. 1, 2 and 3 are given. It was under consideration of the government to revise the deadline.
Government's decision:
The last date for disbursement of funds released to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats for the financial years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17 by the Urban Development Department is being extended till 31st March, 2019.
Reference No.2. These orders shall not apply to the funds already deposited by the Government under the Government Decisions No. 1, 2 and 3.
