Government circular:
Articles 39 (a) and 41 of the Directive Principles of State Policy of the Constitution of India
Senior citizens should be in good health. Considering the position of senior citizens in the society, the State Government has taken several steps to ensure that they spend their old age in a better way, have a comfortable life in the society, have good physical/ mental health, their economic potential in old age, right to work, right to education and to get public assistance. As per the Government decision dated 09.07.2018, it has been decided to implement the Comprehensive Senior Citizen Policy-2013 of the State. The government's decision
Administrative departments have been instructed to take action in this policy decision.
In pursuance of the implementation of the said policy, instructions are being issued to all Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats under the Urban Development Department as under:
1) Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. All the aspects related to this section of the Senior Citizen Policy as decided under the Government decision dated 09.07.2018 should be strictly implemented.
(2) Every Municipal Corporation shall entrust the responsibility of effective implementation of the Senior Citizen Policy to one Deputy Commissioner/ Assistant Commissioner at its establishment.
