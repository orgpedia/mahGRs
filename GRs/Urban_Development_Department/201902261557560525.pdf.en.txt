GOVERNOR'S DECISION (PRECIOUS PAPER): Some of the terms and conditions of the Government Decision issued in the above context are being amended and the conditions in this regard should now be read as follows. All other terms and conditions mentioned above must be read in the same way as before. - (1) In terms of the Government Order No. 03 (a) reads as follows: :
(a) The amount of possession shall not be charged from the holder of encroachments belonging to Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes/ Other Backward Classes/ Economically Weaker Sections while regularising such encroachments.
(2) In the Government Resolution No. 06 should be read as follows:
(6) While regularising such encroachment, such encroached land shall be reserved for any public/ semi-public purpose in the approved/ draft development plan.
If the land is required for the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana and if the urban local body submits such a proposal, the Collector shall submit the necessary proposal regarding reservation on this area to the Director, Town Planning, Maharashtra State, Pune for appropriate action.
(3) In terms of the Government Resolution No. 07 (a) It may be read as follows: (1) In the first stage, the encroachments of the eligible beneficiaries shall be regularized subject to the above terms and conditions.
(2) Thereafter, such encroached land shall be prepared and approved by the local body concerned for collective management.
(3) Thereafter, it shall be binding on all concerned to act in accordance with this approved model while carrying out the redevelopment of the area.
