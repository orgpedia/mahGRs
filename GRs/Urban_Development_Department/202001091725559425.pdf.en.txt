Live Election Schedule for the General Election to the posts of President and Members of the newly created Shirol Municipal Council in Kolhapur District- 2018 The State Election Commission, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide its letter dated 11th September, 2018 has declared the date of the ensuing General Election to the Legislative Assembly of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the counting of votes of Shirol Municipal Council was held on Sunday, 21st October, 2018 and the results were declared on Monday, 22/10/2018. In this election, Ward No. 6, Seat No. 6-A (Scheduled Tribe category) was reserved. In this, Mr. Dadaso Jaisinghrao Koli was declared elected.
However, they have not yet submitted the caste validity certificate as per the provisions of the Act.
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats Industrial Towns, 9A of the Maharashtra Industrial Townships Act, 1965 and the rules made thereunder. In view of the amendment made under the Ordinance dated 27.09.2018, a person contesting election to the posts reserved for Backward Classes shall be required to submit an undertaking to produce such certificate of validity within one year from the date of his election, if he does not have a caste validity certificate at the time of filing of nomination. There is a provision in the rules to disqualify them by cancelling their membership with retrospective effect if they do not submit the caste validity certificate within a period of 1 year from the date of election. Orders in this regard are issued by the state Urban Development Department on the directions of the State Election Commission. Accordingly, the matter of issuing an order that the election of Mr. Dadaso Jaysingrao Koli as a member of the Shirol Municipal Council has been cancelled with retrospective effect was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:

GOVERNMENT ORDER: Dadaso Jaisinghrao Koli, Corporator, Shirol Municipal Council, Ward No. 6, No. 6A (Scheduled Tribes) has amended the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats Industrial Towns Act, 1965 vide No. As per the provisions of the amendment made under the Ordinance dated 27.09.2018, his election to the Shirol Municipal Council on the reserved post of" Scheduled Tribe" is being declared void with retrospective effect and he is disqualified to continue as a member of the Municipal Council for not producing the caste validity certificate within a period of 12 months from the date of declaration of his election.
