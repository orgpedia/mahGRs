Preamble Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has released a notification for the recruitment of 1 post of" City Programme Manager" on Municipal Corporation Establishment for Medical Health Department as per 6th Pay Commission with Pay Band of Rs.
9300-34800/- Grade Pay Rs. 4400/- subject to the proposal for grant of approval by the Government under Section-51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 for permanent creation in this pay scale. submitted to the Government vide letter dated 15.07.2021.
2. d. As per the Government decision dated 18.03.2008, while creating 51 posts for regularization of employees working under RCH Phase-II, the post of" City Programme Manager" has not been created. Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has decided to create the post of" City Programme Manager" on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation for the Medical Health Department on a permanent basis.
85, c. As per the Government decision dated 01.06.2021, the layout of Kalyan Dombivali Municipal Corporation has been approved and it does not include the post of" City Programme Manager." As proposed by the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, the matter of creating 1 post of" City Program Manager" on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution: Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Resolution received vide letter dated 15.07.2021 and Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation General Body Resolution No. 85, c. In view of the approval given for creation of 1 post of" City Programme Manager" under 20.11.2013, one post of" City Programme Manager" on establishment of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation for Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Corporation Medical Health Department in the pay band of Rs. 9300-34800/ Grade Pay Rs. 4400/- is being approved by the Government under Section-51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
The condition of 35% establishment cost while creating the post is being relaxed as a one-time matter.
