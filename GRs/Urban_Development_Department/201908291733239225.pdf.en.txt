The Proposal:
Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, No. The Jalgaon City Water Supply Project has been approved under the Government Decision No. 2. The government has decided to implement the solar power sub-station work in the project on full deposit basis through MEDA as per the decision of the Government No. 3.
Accordingly, the DPR of the solar power sub-station in the Jalgaon City Water Supply Scheme approved under the AMRUT Mission was prepared through MEDA and submitted to the government for approval after providing technical approval. Accordingly, the DPR of the solar power project submitted through MEDA has been approved by the concerned Urban Local Bodies as well as the State Level Technical Committee constituted under AMRUT Mission. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the solar power project component of the Jalgaon City Water Supply Scheme sanctioned under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The cost of solar power sub-station was approved as mentioned in Annexure 7 of the Statement of Technical Sub-stations in Para 1 of the Administrative Sanction Order dated 3rd May, 2018 of Jalgaon City Water Supply Phase-II Project under Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission in the sanctioned plan for the financial year 2016-17.

According to the statement mentioned in paragraph number 2 of the said Government Decision, the Jalgaon City Water Supply Scheme sanctioned under the AMRUT Mission has an outlay of Rs. The Solar Energy Appendices costing Rs. 2,33,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores, Thirty Three Lakh only) are being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
The project will be implemented by the Maharashtra Energy Development Authority (MEDA).
After the administrative approval of the project is issued, the Maharashtra Energy Development Authority (MEDA) should conduct the tender process, scrutinize it, give work orders and take further action regarding the implementation of the project. The project should be completed and commissioned by the concerned tenderer before 31st December, 2019.
Accordingly, it will be obligatory on Meda to publish the tender of this project within 7 days and complete the tender process and issue the work order immediately.
After the work order is issued, the funds from the project fund should be transferred to the Maharashtra Energy Development Authority (MEDA) by the concerned urban local body.
It shall be binding on the concerned urban local body to pay a fee of 3% to the Maharashtra Energy Development Authority (MEDA) for the implementation of this sub-section on full deposit basis, from its own funds as per the stage of work prescribed by the Government Decision referred to in Reference No. 3.
It shall be obligatory on the Centre to transfer the project to the urban local body by supervising the implementation of the project for at least 1 year after the implementation of the project as mentioned in the Government Decision No. 3 under reference.
