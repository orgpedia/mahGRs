Preamble: In the wake of the global Corona crisis, the resolution to waive the rent of municipal buildings and property tax of citizens for six months from April 2020 to October 2020 was passed. 164. 12.6.2020 has been passed by the Standing Meeting of Latur City Municipal Corporation.
2. Salaries of Municipal Corporation employees/ officers, electricity dues, water tariff dues are pending due to the pathetic financial condition of Latur City Municipal Corporation. Similarly, the exemption granted under the said resolution is against the provisions of Section 79 (a) and (d) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 and the Government Order dated 20.5.2018. The Commissioner, Latur City Municipal Corporation vide letter dated 6.1.2021 has requested the Government to cancel the said resolution as it is against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation and will cause financial loss to the Municipal Corporation.
The financial condition of Latur City Municipal Corporation is pathetic and the salaries of the officers and employees for the last three months have not been paid by the corporation. A protest was held at the city corporation office in this regard. The Standing Committee passed Resolution No. 164 dated 12.6.2020 is a financial loss to the Municipal Corporation. Therefore, the Standing Committee of the Municipal Corporation passed the said Resolution No. 164 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act is against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation and the exemption granted under the said resolution is against the provisions of Section 79 (a)& (d) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, 1949 and the Government Resolution dated 20.5.2018. Therefore, the said resolution needs to be suspended in the first instance to be set aside as mentioned in Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: LAMANPA-2021/ PR No.15/ NO-24
7. In view of the above, the said Resolution No. 164 dated 12.6.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the facts mentioned in the above-mentioned Preamble, the said Resolution passed by the Standing Committee of Latur Municipal Corporation No. 164 dated 12.6.2020 is against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act and the exemption granted under the said resolution is against the provisions of Section 79 (a)& (d) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, 1949 as well as the Government Decision dated 20.5.2018. Therefore, the said resolution is being suspended prima facie for disaffiliation under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
If the concerned want to submit a petition regarding the suspension of this resolution, they will be suspended.
The application must be submitted to the government within 30 days from the date of the order.
