Non-Direction of Funds:- The funds sanctioned by the State Government for the sanctioned projects under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyana can only be used for that project and it cannot be diverted or used for any other work. Any such diversion of funds, either in principal or interest thereon, whether permanent or temporary, will be considered as serious financial irregularity for which action will be taken by the concerned local bodies/ concerned authorities.
Third party audit of projects: It will be mandatory for the Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation to conduct a third party audit of the approved projects under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission at various stages of implementation by the Director Local Fund Accounts.
Other Binding Terms and Conditions:- After the approval of the project for the Urban Local Bodies by the State Government, the original project cost may be increased for any reason.
The Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation will be responsible for this. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government for this purpose.
