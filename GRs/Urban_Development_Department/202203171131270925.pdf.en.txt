The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, in respect of immovable property situated in the city, if any, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent on the value of such property situated in the case of sale or encumbrance of the same and on the amount secured by the encumbrance of the encumbrance of the same in the case of encumbrance of the same. In this regard, the government has decided to implement the scheme for the financial year 2021-22. 31.12.2021 and dt. As on 17.02.2022, 1% Stamp Duty Cess payable to 26 Municipal Corporations in the State is Rs. 235,71,98, 809/- and Rs. 210,00,00, 000/- has been disbursed as first and second instalment. It is now Rs. The third installment of Rs 210,00,00, 000/- to the Municipal Corporations was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The total amount to be released to the Municipal Corporations within the limit of subsidy available on Budget Distribution System for disbursement of 3rd installment of 1% Stamp Duty for the year 2021-22 is Rs.
