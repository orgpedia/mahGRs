Government of Maharashtra
Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No. MRD-3318/C.R. 17/UD-7
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: -9th May, 2018
Read: Government Resolution No. BRD-1095/1384/220/95/UD-10, Dt. 25.08.1995.
Resolution: -
In Exercise of the powers conferred by Section 7(1) (vii), (viii) and (ix) of the Mumbai
Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 (Mah. Act. No. IV of 1975), the Government of Maharashtra has decided to discontinue the membership of Shri D. M
Sukhathankar of the Executive Committee and appoints Shri Jayant Kumar Banthia (Rtd. IAS)
former chief secretary to GoM, as member of the Executive Committee in his place with affect from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
