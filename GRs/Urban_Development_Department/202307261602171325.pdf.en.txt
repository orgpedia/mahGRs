Reference- Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, dated 28.04.2015
The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was promulgated with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their increasing expectations from the local administration. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28.04.2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority shall, within a period of three months from the date of promulgation of the Ordinance and from time to time thereafter, notify the public servants, designated officers, first and second Appellate Authorities and the time limits prescribed by it under the Ordinance. Accordingly, the government was considering to notify the time limit, designated officers, first and second appellate officers for the services of Nashik Metropolitan Region Development Authority. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
(1) In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, the Government is pleased to notify the services under Schedule-A of the Nashik Metropolitan Region Development Authority under this Order. The details of the period of public service, office to which the service is notified as well as the designated officer, first appellate officer, and second appellate officer for the services provided by the Nashik Metropolitan Region Development Authority under the Urban Development Department are mentioned in Schedule-A. Accordingly, the Nashik Metropolitan Region Development Authority should take action to notify this service in the government gazette.
