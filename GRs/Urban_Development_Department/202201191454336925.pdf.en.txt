(1)" Heritage tree": The definition of tree of any species having an estimated age of 50 years or more has been fixed by Government Notification, Department of Environment and Climate Change dated 22nd September, 2021.
Age of Trees: The criteria and method for determining the estimated age of trees have been prescribed by the Government Notification, Department of Environment and Climate Change dated September 22, 2021.
(3) Compensatory plantation: It is mandatory to plant as many trees as the estimated age of the trees to be felled. A minimum of 6 feet should be planted.
If it is not possible to plant a tree as compensation, then the provision of depositing an amount not less than the valuation of the trees to be cut.
Large-scale tree felling: Proposals for felling of more than 200 trees of five years and above age are required to be sent to the Maharashtra State Tree Authority.
The local tree authority should ensure that the projects are not divided into small areas to keep the number of trees within the prescribed limits.
Maharashtra State Tree Authority: The notification for the constitution of Maharashtra State Tree Authority at the state level has been issued by the Department of Environment and Climate Change on 10th January, 2022. The Maharashtra State Tree Authority has proposed to cut more than 200 trees and heritage trees that are five years old or older.
(6) Local Tree Authority: In the case of a Municipal Council, the Chairman of the Tree Authority shall be the" Principal Officer."
(7) Community land for cultivation: Compensatory plantation can be done on public land identified by the local body in case of non-availability of space.
(viii) Replantation of trees: Replantation should be done only under the guidance of experts and compensatory plantation should be done as per the approximate age of the tree to be replanted.
