The Proposal:
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government vide its Office Memorandum dated 23rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement and utilization of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Under Reference No. 3 Office Memorandum and Reference No. 4 letter, the State Government has been directed to appoint a separate Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for each Smart City. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate single bank account in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary level Nodal Officer for coordination of this Centrally Sponsored Scheme. Accordingly, the government was considering appointing a single nodal agency (SNA) and a nodal officer for the Smart City Mission, a centrally sponsored scheme under the Urban Development Department.

Government's decision:
As per the instructions given by the Government Circular at Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, the Central Government has been directed to adopt revised procedures under the Office Memorandum dated 23rd March, 2021 for the implementation of the ongoing/ future Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Urban Development Department in order to effectively manage the funds of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes as follows and to bring more effectiveness in the process of allocation of funds. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement and utilization of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate single bank account in the authorized bank for that scheme.
It has also been directed to appoint at least a Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary level Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) is being appointed for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Smart City Mission under Urban Development Department as shown in the table below.
