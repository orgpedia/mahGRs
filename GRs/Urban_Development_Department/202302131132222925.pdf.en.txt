GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SWMA-2023/ PR. No.18/ NV-34 GOVERNMENT DECISION: Solid Waste Management of 29 Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) covered under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) in the attached return A, the cost of the project shown in Rakkana 4 of the return A is given as No.
The administrative approval was granted in accordance with the Government Decision No. 4-32.
While the project was being implemented by the Urban Local Bodies, the concerned Urban Local Bodies were requested to approve new items to the cities excluding some items included in the project. (The matters relating to the exclusion of approved projects and their cost are set out in Annexure-A to this Government Resolution in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively.) In place of the excluded items, the items to be approved and their cost are indicated in Rakaana 8 and 9 respectively. )
3. The proposal submitted by these 29 Urban Local Bodies will be examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and accordingly, the proposal will be sent to the State Government. It was submitted for approval in the meeting dated 2/1/2023 of the High Powered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
In accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee to the proposals submitted by the said 29 ULBs, the Government hereby approve the deletion of the price items mentioned in Ranks 5 and 6 respectively and the approval of the price items mentioned in Ranks 8 and 9 respectively in place of the deletion items in the accompanying Statement A.
(5) With the above change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the concerned Urban Local Bodies for solid waste will be as shown in the Annexure 12 of the Statement-A accompanying this Government Resolution.
It shall be mandatory to comply with the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision on Administrative Sanction of the concerned Urban Local Bodies at Reference No. 4 to 32.
The responsibility of obtaining technical approval for the Detailed Project Report will be of the concerned Urban Local Bodies.
Also, the increase in the revised Detailed Project Report amount should be paid by the local civic bodies from their own funds.
