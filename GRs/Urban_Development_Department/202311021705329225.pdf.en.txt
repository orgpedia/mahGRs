The Proposal:
Without cleanliness, beautification of the city is impossible. For the same reason, the City Beautification and Sanitation Competition 2022 was held last year from October 2 to December 31, 2022. In order to encourage the cities to maintain their quality, this year also, the Government has issued a decision to organize" Chief Minister Saksham City Competition-2023" at the city level in all the Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats of the State and" Chief Minister Saksham Ward Competition 2023" at the ward level in other Municipal Corporations from September 15 to December 15, 2023.
Considering the guidelines issued to the cities in the competition presented in this Government Decision, the urban local bodies that have performed well in the" Chief Minister Saksham City Competition-2023" and" Chief Minister Saksham Ward Competition-2023" competition, a department-level committee has been formed to scrutinize the performance of the cities during the" Chief Minister Saksham City Competition-2023" and" Chief Minister Saksham Ward Competition-2023" as follows:
