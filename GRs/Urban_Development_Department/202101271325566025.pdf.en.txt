Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored" Suvarna Jayanti Shahari Rozgar Yojana" has been restructured and converted into" National Urban Livelihoods Mission" under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana and is being implemented in the State. The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the state. Under this mission, measures are being taken to provide employment or self-employment opportunities by providing skill training to the people of urban poor families, providing shelter facilities to the urban homeless, solving the problems related to livelihood of street vendors in urban areas, reducing urban poverty by improving the living standards of urban poor families, etc. Reference No. 4 The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, have requested for release of Centre-State share (Phase-II) for this mission.
