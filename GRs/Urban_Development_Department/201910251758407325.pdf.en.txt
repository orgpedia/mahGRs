Government's decision:
To make all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to ensure clean and good health and environment to all the citizens and to keep all the constitutional cities of the country clean.
And with the objective of providing household toilet facilities to the citizens of these cities, the Central Government has started implementing the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)." On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context. Accordingly, the project of solid waste management of Aurangabad Municipal Corporation under the said scheme has been given administrative approval as per the decision of the government in reference number 2.
For the detailed project report of Rs. 91.7949 crore sanctioned for Solid Waste Management of Aurangabad City under Swachh Bharat Mission, the second installment of Rs. 16,06,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixteen Crore Six Lakh only) from the Central Government's account has been released to the State vide Reference No. 10,70,66, 667/-to the State Government.
