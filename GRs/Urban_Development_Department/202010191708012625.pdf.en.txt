Introduction: Refer No. 1 In order to reduce the inefficiency in the service of the principals under the government decision here, a policy has been decided to give promotion to the cadre principals and appointment in the corporation.
Reference No. It is clarified that 50% of the posts of Assistant Commissioner, Deputy Commissioner and equivalent posts sanctioned in the corporation under the government decision here will be appointed by the equivalent and appropriate officer in the cadre of Chief Officer. Also, the number of posts in the category of headmaster cadre. It has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 12.04.2007. Thereafter, in the backdrop of increasing urbanization, the designations for the newly created Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in the State have been made by passing the relevant Government Decisions and Government Policies from time to time. Considering the number of posts sanctioned under various government decisions, the matter of marking the updated number of officers in the cadre of Principal was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The government has taken a decision. Approval is being accorded to earmark the following posts for the Principal Cadre as per the sanctioned posts at present on the lines of the number of posts marked under 12.04.2007.
