Government's decision:
As per the facts mentioned in the above-mentioned Preamble, the Standing Committee of the Nanded-Waghala Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 40 d. 29.5.2017 as it is inconsistent with the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, the said resolution is being suspended prima facie for being struck down under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, 1949.
If the resolution is to be submitted to the concerned in the matter of suspension, they must submit it to the government within 30 days from the date of the suspension order.
