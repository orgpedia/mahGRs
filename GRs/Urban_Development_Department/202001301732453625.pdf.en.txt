Government's decision:
Under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), Rs. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) of Rs. 3.9286 crore has been approved as per the Government Resolution No. 3.
While the implementation of the Detailed Project Report was going on through the Basmatnagar Municipal Council, it was noticed that the Detailed Project Report did not cover certain aspects in terms of solid waste management in the city. Therefore, the proposal to include those items in the detailed project report was submitted by the Basmatnagar Municipal Council.
The proposal submitted by the Basmatnagar Municipal Council was examined at the level of the State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Power Committee held on 30.08.2019. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
In accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee to the proposal submitted by the Basmatnagar Municipal Council, the Government approves the matters mentioned in the Annexure 4 and Annexure 5 to this Government Resolution amounting to Rs. 1,01,23, 000/- (Rupees One Crore One Lakh Twenty Three Thousand only).
The amount of this increase shall be borne by the Basmatnagar Municipal Council from the 14th Finance Commission Fund or from its own funds. The government will not provide any additional funds to the concerned municipal council for this.
It shall be mandatory to comply with the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Decision dated 16.11.2018 at Reference No. 3.
