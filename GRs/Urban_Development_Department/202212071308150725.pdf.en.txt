4.8 Methodology of Fund Distribution: As per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 20 Mission, the Central share of funds is to be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%). Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.
4.9 While setting up self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on the operational outcomes. Also, the project will not be funded if it does not meet the functional outcome. The Commissioner, Sangli Miraj and Kupwad Municipal Corporation will be responsible for this.
4.11 The first instalment of funds from the Central and State Governments for the project will be disbursed to the Municipal Corporations of Sangli Miraj and Kupwad after the issuance of the work order. Accordingly, after submission of Utilization Certificate to the Central Government as prescribed by the Central Government and after achieving the operational results, the next instalment of Central share and State share will be disbursed accordingly.
4.12 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
4.13 As per the instructions issued at the time of disbursement of funds for this project, it will be mandatory for the Sangli Miraj and Kupwad Municipal Corporations to open and keep separate bank accounts in proportion to the funds released by the Central and State Governments for this project.
