The Proposal:
Detailed instructions and timelines regarding the action to be taken in respect of the funds allocated to the Urban Local Bodies under various schemes by the Urban Development Department for non-utilization within the prescribed time frame are given in the context of Schedule to Government Decision No. 1.2.3, 4,5,6 and 7. It was under consideration of the government to revise the deadline.
Government's decision:
Extending the date for spending the funds released for the financial years 2016-2017, 2017-2018, 2018-2019 and 2019-20 upto 28.02.2022.
The funds disbursed by the Urban Development Department to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State during the year 2015-16 and earlier and which are yet to be released should be immediately deposited to the Government.
