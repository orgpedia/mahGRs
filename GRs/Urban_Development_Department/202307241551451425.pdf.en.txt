Government's decision:
To make all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens and to make all the constitutional cities of the country clean and hygienic.
The Government of India has launched the Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) 2.0 with the objective of providing household toilet facilities to the citizens of the cities. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
Under the Swachh Bharat Mission. The proposals of the Urban Local Bodies of the State under this component were submitted to the Central Government for management of water used with the approval of the High Powered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The Central Government vide Reference No. In pursuance of this proposal vide order dated 30.03.2023, the Central Government's admissible share of Rs. 19,55,93, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Fifty Five Lakh Ninety Three Thousand only) has been released to the State. Sheet No. 1 of the Finance Department confirms the receipt of funds from the Central Government to the State Government. 52 (2022-23) in Finance Department/ Finance, dated 03.04.2023.
The State Mission Director, Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban), vide letter dated 31st March, 2023, has informed that under the Swachh Bharat Mission, the Central Government has released the first installment of Rs. 19,55,93, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Fifty Five Lakh Ninety Three Thousand only) to the State Government for the management of water used by the Urban Local Bodies in the State and the State Government's admissible share in this regard is Rs. 17,20,89, 865/- (Rupees Seventeen Crore Twenty Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand only).
