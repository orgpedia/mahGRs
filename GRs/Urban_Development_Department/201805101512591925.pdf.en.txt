Committee's mandate:
The Committee shall visit the Eversmile Project at Mahul at least twice to review the existing infrastructure facilities as well as facilities in the field of education, health, transport etc.
2. Submit an objective report to the government on whether the facilities available at present are adequate.
If there is a need to increase the existing infrastructure and other facilities, a definite recommendation should be submitted to the government.
4. A time bound action plan should be submitted to the government for providing enhanced facilities.
In this plan, the responsibilities and responsibilities of the concerned authority (Municipal Corporation/ ZP/ BEST etc.) providing the facilities should be fixed.
The report shall in any case be submitted to the Additional Commissioner (City), Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and the Chairman of the Committee on or before 21/05/2018.
The said report shall be submitted by the Additional Commissioner (City), Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation with the approval of the Commissioner, Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation on or before 25/05/2018. submitted to the Chief Secretary.
The Deputy Commissioner (Improvement), Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation will be responsible for providing necessary information to the committee and coordinating it till the report is submitted to the government.
The department heads concerned will take a decision regarding the officers mentioned as nominated officers in the table of the above committee formation and provide the details of the names, designations, etc. of the nominated officers to the government within two days.
