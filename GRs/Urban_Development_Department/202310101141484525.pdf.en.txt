The Proposal:
The Mumbai Urban Transport Project (MUTP) Phase-III is a Rs. Cabinet approves Rs 10,947 crore projects Approved on 30.11.2016. Accordingly, the Ministry of Railways has approved the project No. 1 here. The project has been approved vide letter dated 20.12.2016. The State Government vide No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 07.01.2017, the MUTP-3 project has been approved to be implemented by Mumbai Rail Vikas Corporation (MRVC) in partnership with Indian Railways on the lines of Mumbai Urban Transport Project Phase-I and Phase-II with 50:50 financial participation. The project is funded by the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB). 3500 crore (USD 500 million) has been sanctioned.
The loan agreement was signed between the Government of India and AIIB Bank and the project agreement was signed between AIIB Bank, Government of Maharashtra and MRVC. It was signed on 24.08.2020.

If the external debt component is spent on projects through MRVC, such expenditure has to be met by MMRD. The MRVC submits reimbursement claims for the expenses incurred to the AIIB Bank. Once the reimbursement claims are approved by the AIIB, the same amount is disbursed by the AIIB Bank to the Central Government in the form of loan.
The amount is deposited by the Central Government in the Consolidated Fund of the State. The amount of AIIB loan received by the Maharashtra government as additional central assistance from the central government on the funds provided by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority to the Mumbai Railway Vikas Corporation (MRVC) as part of the state government's loan, is to be disbursed by the state government to the MMRDA. The amount collected by the Railways on account of surcharge on suburban train tickets is to be used for repayment of loan taken from AIIB for the project and for payment of lease charges against the loan.
