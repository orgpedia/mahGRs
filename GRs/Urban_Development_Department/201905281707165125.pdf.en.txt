Preamble: Establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation. The Municipal Corporation was formed on 28.02.2002 and is classified as' D' category Municipal Corporation. The total area of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation is 79.40 sq.km.
Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation is under the jurisdiction of Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
Due to the proximity to other municipal areas, the increasing population, resulting in stress on infrastructure, various projects being carried out by the state and central governments, increasing expectations of the citizens about the welfare of the people and transparent work by the municipal administration, the government has decided to reduce the working staff. As on 26.02.2019, a total of 2,561 posts have been sanctioned for various departments of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation.
The Service Access Rules of the Municipal Corporation have been approved in the year 2014. It has come into effect from 13.03.2014. Nevertheless, the service entry

Differences in the designation of posts and sanctioned posts in the rules, some posts not indicated in the service entry rules, were causing difficulties in the recruitment and appointment of employees.
In this regard, a committee was formed at the Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation level to study the Service Access Rules of 2014 and suggest measures. The general body meeting of the Municipal Corporation passed a resolution to this effect. 103, p. Approved on 30.01.2016. Following this, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, gave a reply. 05.03.2016 and D. As per the letter dated 24.11.2017, a proposal was submitted to the Government for amendment and amendment in the Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Services (Entry of Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2014.
The approval of the Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation (Amendment of Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 was under consideration of the Government to correct the errors in the Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation Service Entry Rules, 2014 and to bring it in line with the service entry rules of other Municipal Corporations.
The proposal submitted by Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation under section 457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act and the rules (Annexure-1, 2, 3, 3-A, 3-B, 3-A and 4) of the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation (Amendment of Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019, which have been added to the Municipal Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2014, with necessary amendments, are being approved by the Government under sections 454, 455, 456, 457 (3) and 465 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
2. The said rules may be called the" Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation Amended Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019."
(3) These rules shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
