Government's decision:
Detailed instructions and timelines regarding action to be taken in respect of funds allocated to Urban Local Bodies under various schemes by the Urban Development Department for non-appropriation within the prescribed time frame have been issued vide Government Decision dated 12th March, 2018. The State Government has given time till 30th June, 2018 to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats to spend the funds released by the Urban Development Department during the financial years 2014-15 and 2015-16. Also, in 2013-14 and earlier years, the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State have been allotted by the Urban Development Department and are yet to be notified.
Instructions have also been given to deposit the funds to the government before 17.03.2018.
