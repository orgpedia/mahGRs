The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 25 Municipal Corporations of the State for the month of June, July and August 2018 is Rs. 331,03,28, 154.00 crore is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the month of June, July and 2018 in the year 2018-19, the total amount of Municipal Corporations towards 1% stamp duty was Rs. 331,03,28, 154.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Thirty One Crores Three Lakh Twenty Eight Thousand One Hundred and Fifty Four only) is being approved by the Government as follows:
