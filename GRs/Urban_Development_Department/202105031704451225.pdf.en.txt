Introduction: The 15th Finance Commission has come into effect from 2020-21. As per the recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission, separate funds have been sanctioned to Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) for Million Plus Cities and Non-Million Plus Cities under the 15th Finance Commission. The urban local bodies in the state have been classified into Million Plus Cities and Non-Million Plus Cities under the government decision. Under the 15th Finance Commission, funds have been sanctioned for Million Plus Cities Ambient Air Quality& Improving Conservation, Supply and Management of Water and Efficient Solid Waste Management measures.
Under the Government Decision No. 3, the procedure for the distribution and appropriation of grants received for urban local bodies in the Non-Million Plus Cities group in the State under the 15 Finance Commission has been fixed and the procedure for the distribution and appropriation of grants received for urban local bodies in the Million Plus Cities group was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
