The Proposal:
Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation was formed. The Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking was formed on 16.11.1995. It was started on 23.05.1999. At present, the transport undertaking is providing passenger services on various routes including Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, Panvel City Municipal Corporation, Ambernath Municipal Council and Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council.
2. Regarding the pending issues of the employees of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, the then Hon. The Minister of Urban Development. The meeting was held online (through video conference) on 31.05.2022. As per the discussion held in the meeting, the proposal to approve the formation of the post of conductor on the establishment of the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking under Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, as well as the proposal to approve the figure was directed to be submitted.
3. For transport activities in pursuance of various proposals submitted earlier by Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, the 03.04.1998, Dy. 25.07.2011 and D. A total of 575 posts have been sanctioned as per the Government decisions dated 29.02.2012.
Considering the administrative work of the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, the changes in the transport service day by day, the modernization and the increase in the transport service, it was necessary to create a new designation for the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, as well as to determine the contours. For this, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, gave the relevant information. The proposal for creation of new posts vide letter dated 04.08.2022 has been submitted to the Government for approval.
Accordingly, after examining the proposal, the government was considering the creation of new posts for various departments of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation (KDMC).

Government Resolution: Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Pursuant to the proposal for sanction of new posts submitted vide letter dated 04.08.2022, the Government has issued notification in Table No. As per Section 346 (2) (a) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the government is approving the creation of a total of 68 posts of Class-1, Class-2 and Class-3 mentioned in Section 6.
The government is approving the relaxation of the condition of 35% establishment cost of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking as a one-time matter while creating the above mentioned 68 new posts.
Out of 575 posts sanctioned earlier for Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, 24 posts in various cadres mentioned in Form-' C' are being abolished under this Government decision.
The establishment of the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking will have a total of 575 posts sanctioned as per Form-A and 68 posts sanctioned as per Form-B of this Government Decision and 24 posts as per Form-A to be lapsed.
This figure of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) The posts in the Annexure attached with this Government Resolution should be filled up keeping in view the percentage of establishment cost of Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking as per requirement or higher.
(2) If more than the number of officers/ employees sanctioned in the revised figure of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, are presently working permanently in the Municipal Corporation, the posts will continue to exist till such time such officers/ employees are in service or promoted, after which such posts will automatically lapse.
Since the electric buses of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking will be operated under the NCAP scheme in the future, after the posts of conductors/ drivers in this cadre are retired, they should be filled through an external system.
4) The services of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking that are possible and necessary to be done through external sources, such services should be done through external sources.
The Transport Manager, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, vide Para No. Further necessary action should be taken in accordance with the revised diagram by complying with the terms and conditions in Section 5.
