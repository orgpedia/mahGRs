03. Ma. In pursuance of the Supreme Court's direction regarding appointment of Custodian for permanent preservation of Advance Medical Directives for citizens in urban areas, the Government was contemplating to appoint Custodian in all Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats of the State for permanent preservation of Advance Medical Directives for citizens in urban areas and to create a common digital platform for permanent preservation of legal documents such as the present Living Will.
Government Decision: As Registrar of Births and Deaths in urban areas, it is appointing Commissioner, all Municipal Corporations, Principal Commissioners, all Municipal Councils / all Nagar Panchayats as Custodian for permanent preservation of living will of citizens in Urban Local Bodies for citizens in urban areas of the State. It shall be the duty of the Custodian to protect and preserve this Living Will.

For citizens of urban areas in all Municipal Corporations, Municipalities, Nagar Panchayats in the state.
A living will module has been created on the mahaulb portal as a uniform digital platform to preserve their living crops permanently.
The procedure to be followed for the permanent preservation of the living will of the citizen as well as for the custodian to view the living will as and when required and provide a copy thereof to the concerned shall be as follows:
Procedure for making online living will: 1) The applicant has to visit the official website of the government mahaulb.in.
2) Fill out the application form on the relevant Living Will window on the website. (This will contain the applicant's basic (primary) information.) 3) Uploading of their legally made living will by the applicant.
4) Taking an online appointment of the Custodian regarding the preservation of their living will by the applicant.
5) The Custodian to give the applicant actual appointment time within seven days.
6) Custodian's confirmation of the living will uploaded by the applicant for preservation online as well as the documents required to verify their identity as per the rules. If all of the required factors are correct, the applicant must then take out a copy of the living will made by 0119 and provide the applicant with the signature of the Custodian, the RIRINTS Custodian, on the copy of the original living will made online, if requested by the applicant.
Procedure for making Offline Living Will: 1) The applicant has to visit the concerned Custodian's Office of the Government and submit the application.
2) The Custodian must actually give the applicant an appointment within seven days.
3) The applicant must submit to two Custodians an ATEN Affidavit along with the Living Will application they are required to make. The custodian shall, as a rule, confirm the living will filed by the applicant with the custodian in person offline as well as the documents required to verify their identity. Upload a copy of the Living Will submitted offline by the applicant to the Custodian in the Living Will module on the digital portal mahaulb.in, if all the required details are correct. Also, the original notarized living will submitted offline by the applicant must be delivered to the applicant by the Custodian with the Custodian's signature on the copy.
Procedure for Cancelling a Living Will: If the living will is to be annulled, the applicant must take the original copy of their living will to the Custodian. The Custodian shall return to the applicant the signed BRINE copy of the Cancel on the original copy and similarly the Custodian shall also cancel the Living Will of the concerned on the Online Portal.
