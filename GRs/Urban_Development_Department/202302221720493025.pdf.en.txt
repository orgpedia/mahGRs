Preamble: The decision to accommodate the project victims displaced due to the raising of the height of the Barvi Dam on the establishment of the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Thane, Mira-Bhayander, Kalyan-Dombivali, Navi Mumbai, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation and Ambernath Municipal Council in accordance with the equitable principle of water use is referred to in Reference No. The above decision has been taken by the government. As per the decision of the government, if there are no or few posts available to accommodate the 7% posts reserved for the victims of the Barvi dam project finalized by the Collector, Thane, then the necessary number of additional posts in Group C and Group D has been approved.
Accordingly, the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a list of 16 candidates to the Ambernath Municipal Council to provide jobs to the affected families of the Barvi Dam project. Submitted by letter dated 15/12/2022. Divisional Commissioner and Regional Director Office Reference No. The report was submitted to the Directorate of Municipal Administration with their recommendation under the letter here. The Directorate of Municipal Administration vide Reference No. A total of 16 supernumerary posts, 7 of Group-C and 9 of Group-D, were proposed to be created on the establishment of Ambernath Municipal Council under Section 3. Accordingly, a total of 16 supernumerary posts of Group-C to Group-D were under the consideration of the government to accommodate the establishment of Ambernath Municipal Council.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As mentioned in the above Preamble and as recommended by the Directorate of Municipal Administration, the Government is approving the creation of a total of 16 supernumerary posts, namely, 7 of Group-C and 9 of Group-D, as follows, to accommodate the Barvi Dam project victims on the establishment of Ambernath Municipal Council.
