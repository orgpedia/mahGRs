(c) Maintenance and Repair of the Project: 1. The necessary planning regarding maintenance and repair of the project sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan should be done by the concerned executing agency and the Municipal Council so that the results from the said projects are sustainable.
