The Proposal:
As per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission, the Central Government has released Rs. 1102.35 crore towards the first instalment of basic grant for the year 2018-2019 to be distributed to the Urban Local Bodies in the State, vide order No. The grant was to be distributed to the urban local bodies in the state.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission, the Central Government has released the first instalment of the basic grant for the financial year 2018-2019 amounting to Rs. 1102.35 crore is being approved to be distributed to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats based on the criteria of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
