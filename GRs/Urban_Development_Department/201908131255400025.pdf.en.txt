The Proposal:
The matter of posting of Director, Assistant Director on Establishment of Town Planning and Value Assessment Department, officers of Town Planning Cadre on interim transfer/ deputation on administrative grounds was under consideration of the Government. The following orders have been issued by the government regarding the transfer and posting of officers on deputation:
Government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005, and in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment of Services and Provisions during Suspension, Removal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, in terms of Annexure-III, Rule 40 (1) (2) (3) (4) and Annexure-II, and for administrative reasons, the Director, Assistant Director on Establishment of Town Planning and Value Assessment Department, Officers of Town Planning Cadre are being transferred/ posted by transfer/ deputation on interim basis as indicated in the following statement:

| 1.     | Mr. M.Y.Bhargave, Assistant Director, Town Planning | Assistant Director, Town Planning, Mira Bhayander Municipal Corporation (PRNC) | Assistant Director, Town Planning, Nashik Municipal Corporation (SMC) |

The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the above officers to join the place of posting by transfer/ deputation.
The officers who have been posted on deputation under this order should join the place of transfer/ deputation immediately on receipt of the executive order regarding the transfer/ deputation of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Since the above officers are being transferred/ deputed in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of permissible posting and joint transfer grant should be sanctioned to them as per rules.
If the officers transferred/ posted on deputation under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers.
It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall hold the additional charge of the post falling vacant in accordance with the said transfer/ deputation to the officers of the same or equivalent rank in the nearest office or to be transferred.
