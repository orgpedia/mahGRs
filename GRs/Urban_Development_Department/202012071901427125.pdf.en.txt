The Proposal:
It is the primary responsibility of the respective local bodies/ Municipal Corporations to acquire land which is not the statutory responsibility of the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) in respect of the work of Civil Sector Flyovers, ROBs, RUBs on National Highways. Therefore, the land was acquired by the respective local bodies/ municipal corporations by issuing TDR.
If necessary, the development plan should be changed. After the concerned Collector certifies that it is not possible to acquire the place despite taking action in this way, the Central Government in the area of the concerned urban local body
The project is being implemented by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) under the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways.

The decision to provide the funds required for land acquisition of the land required to be acquired from the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarottan Mahaabhiyan has been taken as per the Government decision dated 04.05.2018. Also, improvements are being made on a regular basis.
The Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation had requested the Government to provide funds as per the Government decision dated 04.05.2018 through Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarottan Mahabhiyana for acquisition of land which is not the statutory responsibility of National Highways Authority of India and is the responsibility of Nagpur Municipal Corporation in connection with the flyover to be constructed by NHAI on the Nagpur-Raipur Highway (NH-6) passing through the city limits from Pardi Octoy Naka to Sant Tukaram Chowk to Kalamna Marg, Sant Tukaram Chowk to Rani Prajapati Chowk to Vaishnodevi Chowk. Accordingly, the administrative approval was accorded under the Governing Decision dated November 17, 2018, subject to the proposed amendment in the Governing Decision dated May 04, 2018. Thereafter, Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation vide their letter dated 13.01.2020 had requested for sanction of enhanced cost as the area of affected land was increasing. It was for the government to approve it.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the Nagpur flyover to be constructed by the National Highways Authority of India in the Nagpur Municipal Corporation area submitted under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotna Maha Abhiyan, the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) will construct a flyover on the road from Pardi Octoy Naka to Sant Tukaram Chowk to Kalamna Marg, Sant Tukaram Chowk to Rani Prajapati Chowk to Vaishnodevi Chowk on the Nagpur-Raipur Highway (NH-6), which is not the statutory responsibility of the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) and is the responsibility of the Nagpur Municipal Corporation (NMC), in accordance with the original Rs. 82.87 crore project to acquire the land, the committee headed by the District Collector, Nagpur and the Commissioner, Nagpur Municipal Corporation, certified the progress of the project in the affected areas
The project will be funded 70% by the state government and 30% by the Nagpur Municipal Corporation. Accordingly, as per the revised cost of the project, the revised financial figure for the state share and municipal share will be as follows.
