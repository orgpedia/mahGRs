The Proposal:
Detailed instructions and timelines regarding the action to be taken in respect of the funds allocated to the Urban Local Bodies under various schemes by the Urban Development Department for non-utilization within the prescribed time frame are given in the context of Schedule to Government Decision No. 1,2,3, 4,5,6, 7 and 8. It was under consideration of the government to revise the deadline.
Government's decision:
The date for completion and utilization of funds in respect of funds released during the financial years 2017-2018,2018- 19,2019-20 and 2020-2021 is being extended upto 31.03.2023.
The funds disbursed by the Urban Development Department to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State during the year 2016-17 and earlier years and which are yet to be released should be immediately deposited to the Government.
Reference No.3. This order shall not apply to the funds already deposited by the Government under the Government Decisions No. 1,2,3, 4,5,6, 7 and 8.
