Government's decision:
The government has taken a decision. As on 19.09.2019, approval has been given for creation of posts in various cadres on the establishment of Thane Municipal Corporation. As per this decision, one post of Deputy Director, Town Planning has been created.
In view of the need to fill the post of Deputy Director, Town Planning, as the Cluster Development has to be done immediately through the Thane Municipal Corporation, the Government has issued Circular No.S.A.V-2018/ PR.No. 208/ Work.12, D. As per the provisions dated 05.09.2018, for administrative reasons, the post of Deputy Director, Town Planning in Thane Municipal Corporation is being temporarily upgraded to the post of Joint Director, Town Planning and the additional charge of this post is being handed over to Mr. N. R. Kavale, Joint Director, Town Planning, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Mumbai till further orders of the Government. Mr. Kavale shall hold the additional charge of the post of Joint Director, Town Planning, Thane Municipal Corporation along with the duties and responsibilities of his original post and shall work as per the directions of the Commissioner, Thane Municipal Corporation.
Compliance report of this order should be submitted immediately by the concerned office/ Director, Town Planning, Pune on the e-mail address nitin. shelke@nic.in.
