The Proposal:
Implementation of" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0" in the State on the lines of" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0" of the Central Government vide Reference No. 2 has been started here as per the government decision. The component of Bioremediation of Legacy Waste under Solid Waste Management is included in this Mission.
Under the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) 2.0, it is necessary to dispose of the old waste stored in the urban local bodies by scientific processing and recovery of the land by the urban local bodies. For this purpose, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 86.76 crore out of its share of funds for biomining project for scientific treatment of legacy waste accumulated in 28 cities of AMRUT in the State. 1 has been sent to the State Government.
4. Before disbursing the aforesaid funds received from the Central Government to the 28 ULBs, the matter of granting administrative approval to the said legacy waste remediation project was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the matter mentioned in the Preamble, in order to scientifically process the old accumulated waste in the 28 Urban Local Bodies of the State, the following statement has been issued vide No. Administrative approval is being granted subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in paragraph number 2 to implement the biomining project as per the cost indicated in section 7.
