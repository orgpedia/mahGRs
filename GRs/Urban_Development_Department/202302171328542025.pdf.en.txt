Preamble: Establishment of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation. It was established on 21/03/2003 and is a' D' category Municipal Corporation. The Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation has a total area of 64 sq km and a population of 4,60,228 as per the 2011 census.
The Commissioner, Jalgaon Municipal Corporation has proposed to create posts for administrative and technical work in Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation due to the large increase in urbanization of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation area, the network of MIDC industries located in the area, the stress on infrastructure due to important initiatives/ institutions/ projects/ schemes of the Central and State Government, as well as the role of Urban Local Bodies as a people's welfare and dynamic administration, and to eliminate the need for certain posts and to change the designation/ pay scale of certain posts.
Jalgaon City Municipal Council Resolution No. 12/08/2021 The revised figure of Municipal Corporation has been approved under section 550. Commissioner, Jalgaon Municipal Corporation As per the letter dated 06/06/2019, a proposal has been submitted to the government to approve the formation of the Municipal Corporation. Additional information sought from the Commissioner, Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation in pursuance of this proposal. 01/11/2021 and D. Received by letter dated 15/09/2022. Accordingly, the matter of approving the consolidated structure of the Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation along with the creation of a new post on the establishment of the Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.
The decision was taken by the Commissioner, Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation. 01/11/2021 and D. As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 15/09/2022, the details of the sanctioned posts available with the Jalgaon Municipal Corporation as well as the details of the posts sanctioned by the Government have been mentioned in the" Form (A)" attached with this Government Resolution.
2. Establishment of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation in Form (B) accompanying this Order, Column No. As per Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the government is approving the creation of 336 new posts mentioned in Section 07.

The details of changes in the names, pay scales and grade pay of certain posts of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation have been indicated in Form (a), which is being approved by the Government as per Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, after taking into account the pay scales consistent with the figures of other Municipal Corporations as well as the pay scales of the posts mentioned while approving the figures for' D' class Municipal Corporation by the Government and from the point of view of convenience of hierarchy.
4. The Government is approving the abolition of a total of 842 posts as proposed by the Commissioner, Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation in the" Form (d)" attached to this Government Resolution. Out of the 842 vacancies, 292 posts are vacant due to retirement or other reasons.
The Government is approving the delineation of a total of 2157 posts of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation as mentioned in the" Form (E)" excluding the existing sanctioned 2663 posts on the establishment of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation and 842 lapsable posts recommended by the Municipal Corporation and taking into account the increase of 336 newly created posts. The pay scale mentioned for these posts is as per the 6th Pay Commission.
The layout of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) Care should be taken to fill up the posts available as per the revised figure of Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation keeping in view the prescribed percentage of establishment cost of the Municipal Corporation (35%) as required.
(2) No fund/ grant of any kind shall be made available to the Municipal Corporation by the Government for the post/ increment in the said figure.
(3) The officers/ employees of the Administrative Service under the Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation shall not be appointed to the posts in the Technical and Accounts Service or the officers/ employees of the Technical and Accounts Service to the posts in the Administrative Service.
(4) The services of the Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation which are possible and necessary to be done through external sources, such services should be done through external sources.
7. The Commissioner, Jalgaon City Municipal Corporation shall take further necessary action in accordance with the revised figure in compliance with the terms and conditions in paragraph 6.
