The Proposal:
The Green Area Development Project of Ulhasnagar City, which was included in the Plan outlay for the financial year 2016-17 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, was accorded administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Subsequently, the matter of revising the administrative approval of the Green Area Development Project of Ulhasnagar City was under the consideration of the Government in view of the change in the cost of the components of the project.
Government's decision:
The administrative approval granted under the Government Resolution dated 09.10.2017 to the Greenfield Development Project of Ulhasnagar City included in the State Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government during the financial year 2016-17 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is subject to the other terms and conditions of the relevant Government Resolution as under: 2 is being amended as mentioned in the prospectus.
