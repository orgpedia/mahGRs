The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the above officers to join the place of posting by transfer/ deputation.
The officers who have been posted on deputation under this order should join the place of transfer/ deputation immediately on receipt of the executive order regarding the transfer/ deputation of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Since the above officers are being transferred/ deputed in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of permissible posting and joint transfer grant should be sanctioned to them as per rules.
If the officers transferred/ posted on deputation under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers.
