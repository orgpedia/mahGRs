A total of 840 flats have been constructed by Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Ltd. The Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation (DFCCIL) was asked to submit a representation on why it should not be made available at market price.
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, has issued orders to this effect. As per the letter dated 30.09.2019, it is stated that the proposal in this regard was placed before the general body meeting of the Municipal Corporation. However, the meeting was adjourned. Hence, the government should take a decision in this regard.
Representation submitted by Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation and representation submitted by Delhi to JNPT.
As the State Government was of the view that it was necessary to direct the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation to include the market price and other permissible taxes to DFCCIL for the remaining houses in the project, the matter was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Kalyan Dombivali Mahanagarpalika is being directed under Section 450A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act to implement the Central Government's Dedicated Freight Corridor Project from Delhi to Uran as follows:
Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited (DFCCIL) is implementing the Delhi-Uran Dedicated Freight Corridor Project. As demanded by DFCCIL, they should be given 840 flats in the first phase at market rate (including administrative charges and all other taxes as per rules) out of the total number of flats in the BSUP project of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation.
(b) Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Ltd. Funds received from DFCCIL should be deposited by opening a separate bank account with the Directorate of Municipal Administration (DMA), Mumbai. The Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai (DMA) should disburse this fund and pay their share to the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation and the State Government immediately.
c) Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Ltd. Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation after receiving funds from Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai (DFCCIL).
