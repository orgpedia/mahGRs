Introduction: Refer No. A proposal was submitted to the government to implement the revised pay scale as per the 7th Pay Commission for the officers/ employees of Pimpri-Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority.
The proposal mentions the financial position of the Namud Pimpri-Chinchwad Navanagar Development Authority, additional liabilities, establishment expenses within the prescribed limits, provisions for meeting additional liabilities, and details of the figure. Also, there are no government subsidies. In view of the above and the government's approval to implement the Sixth Pay Commission, the Pimpri-Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority was considering to approve the revised pay scale for the officers/ employees as per the 7th Pay Commission. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government Decision: Officers/ employees of Pimpri-Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority will be given revised pay scale as per the 7th Pay Commission. Effective from 01.01.2016 and payable directly. With effect from 01.09.2019, the pay scales to be fixed as per the 7th Pay Commission shall not exceed the pay scales applicable to the respective equivalent posts in the State Government. Also, the revised pay scales as per the 7th Pay Commission will be applicable to the officers/ employees of the Authority on par with the pay scales approved under the 6th Pay Commission. The authority will not make any increase in the revised pay scale.
(2) If any difficulty arises in determining equivalence in respect of pay scales, it should be resolved by prior approval of the Government.
