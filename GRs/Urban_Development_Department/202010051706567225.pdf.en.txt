Gopal Uttareshwar Kale, Assistant Town Planner, Dahanu Municipal Council, Palghar, Shri. The transfers made to the Thane Municipal Corporation, Thane (Cluster Cell) under the Government Order dated 14.08.2020 are hereby cancelled.
The prescribed terms and conditions of deputation shall continue to apply to the officers who have been posted on deputation.
The head of the office in which the above officers are working shall, on receipt of this order, relieve the above officers immediately to join the place where they have been transferred/ deputed.
The officers who have been posted on deputation under this order should immediately report at the place of transfer/ deputation and the concerned office/ Director, Town Planning, Pune should immediately submit a report on the transfer/ deputation to the email address nitin. shelke@nic. in.
If the officers transferred/ posted on deputation under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers. It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
