Government's decision:
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and clean environment to all the citizens. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
The Central Government has released Rs. 9,79,00, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crore and Eighty-nine Lakh only) as second instalment to the State under Order No. 5 of 2019-20 for the purpose of Publicity, Publicity and Public Awareness under Swachh Bharat Mission.
The Central Government has released a sum of Rs. 9,79,00, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crore Eighty-nine Lakhs only) and accordingly, the State Government has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 6,52,66, 667/- (Rupees Sixty-six Lakhs Sixty-six Thousand Six Hundred Sixty-six only) to the State Government, totalling to Rs. 16,31,66, 667/- (Rupees Sixteen Crore Sixty-six Lakhs Sixty-six Hundred Sixty-six only). The expenditure shall be met from the provision made under the relevant head of accounts for the financial year 2019-20 as shown in the table below.
