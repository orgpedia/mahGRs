The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Abhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and raising their standards.
Under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission, the water supply project of the city of Chalisgaon was granted administrative approval under the Government Decision No. 2. The matter of approving the inclusion of goods and services tax of the Chalisgaon City Water Supply Project, which was submitted to the government under reference number 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration, was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Water Supply Project of Chalisgaon City under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan has been accorded administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 2. The proposal submitted to the Government under reference No. 4 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration regarding the inclusion of Goods and Services Tax in the water supply project is being approved by this Government decision as mentioned in the following statement.
