Government's decision:
The water supply project of Gevrai Municipal Council, which was submitted under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Campaign, was given administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 5.
The Chief Officer, Gevrai Municipal Council, regarding the revised administrative approval of the said project, issued Reference No. A request was made to the government by letter. The Revised Estimate is being accorded administrative approval by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, Aurangabad as per the revised technical approval under Reference No.7 as mentioned in the following statement.
