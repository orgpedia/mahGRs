The Proposal: -
Guidelines for "Expansion and Modernization of Fire Services" * Scheme in States through planned allocation of Preparedness and Capacity Building Fund window under National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) as recommended by the 15th Finance Commission have been issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India dated 22.04.2022. Accordingly, the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India has notified the submission of proposal under date 11.07.2023 setting the criteria for the scheme under date 04.07.2023.
Under this, an amount of Rs. The Rs 4,500 crore scheme has been rolled out to all states. Out of the present fund, Rs. 615.48 crore (10.26%) has been provided for funding. The central government accounts for 75 per cent and the state government 25 per cent. The recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission are for the period 2020-21 to 2025-26 and the funds will be available in a phased manner once the proposals submitted by the states are approved. The implementation of the scheme has to be approved under the relevant Government Decision. The government was considering opening a new head of accounts to budget the funds.
