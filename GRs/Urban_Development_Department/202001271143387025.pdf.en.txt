The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 27.05.2019, the Government has approved the establishment of the Office of Development Plan Special Unit under the name of" Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Unit, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur." It was under the consideration of the government to declare the office as a Drawing and Disbursing Officer (DDO). The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Ordinance, 1968, the office of the" Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Unit, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur" is hereby declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the office of the" Town Planner (Gazetted-Group-A), Development Plan, Special Unit, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur" for drawing salaries, allowances and other amounts from the treasury.
All concerned should take care to take action as mentioned in the Government Circular, Finance Division No. 1, 1009/ PR No. 26/ Koshap-5, dated 24.06.2009.
