The Municipal Council shall be bound to provide Project Management Consultancy Charges (PMC Charges) to the concerned consultant from its own funds as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 04.06.2018 and subject to the provisions of that Government Decision. The city council must make a decision within seven days.
