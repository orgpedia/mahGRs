In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the water supply project of Manmad Municipal Council is being given administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
Project Implementation Mechanism: Considering the urgency of the water supply project in Manmad city, the project is currently being sanctioned under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan. In future, after the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission in the State, the funds will be disbursed under AMRUT 2.0 Mission. Therefore, the implementation guidelines applicable to AMRUT Mission projects will continue to be applicable for this project.
The project should be implemented by Manmad Municipal Council as per the request of Manmad Municipal Council.
The Municipal Council shall appoint a Project Management Consultant for the said project within seven days subject to payment of fee within a maximum limit of 3% as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 04.06.2018. A decision in this regard will be taken by the municipal council.
The Municipal Council shall be bound to provide Project Management Consultancy Charges (PMC Charges) to the concerned consultant from its own funds as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 04.06.2018 and subject to the provisions of that Government Decision. The city council must make a decision within seven days.

Under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotna Maha Abhiyan, it will be mandatory for the concerned urban local body to conduct a" third party technical audit" of the approved projects at various stages of implementation by one of the mechanisms set up by the government from time to time. Under the Maharashtra Suvarnajayanti Nagarotna Abhiyan, the concerned urban local bodies will be allowed to borrow from nationalised banks, the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA), the Maharashtra Urban Infrastructure Development Company Limited (MUIDCL) and government-approved financial institutions to raise their own funds for the sanctioned projects.
