The Proposal:
The Director, Town Planning and Valuation, Pune, has issued the following orders: As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 09.11.2020, after the completion of the development plan Amaravati (Second Amendment), the development plan was submitted to the Government for approval, the matter of shifting the offices of Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Cell, Municipal Corporation, Amaravati was under consideration. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
In view of the geographical location of Thane city, the total area and the speed/ scale of development of Thane city, considering the scope and importance of the work of revising the development plan for the said Thane Municipal Corporation area, and since the development plan of Amravati (2nd Amendment) has been completed, it is hereby approved to transfer by nomination as follows:
