The pay scale will be S-8: 25500-81100. If the services of these employees are regularized, the establishment cost of the corporation will be limited to 35%. Therefore, the matter of creating more number of posts for these employees and appointing them to those posts was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
Since the appointment of 34 employees of the" Multi-Purpose Health Workers" cadre working on a temporary basis in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation establishment on a wholesale basis has been made by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), the Government is approving the creation of a maximum number of posts equal to the number of these employees in the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation establishment by appointing them permanently in the pay scale of S-8: 25500-81100 as per the 7th Pay Commission with the designation of" Multi-Purpose Health Workers."
The said 34 employees as per Return A are being given permanent appointment to 34 supernumerary posts created on the establishment of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The majority of the posts of the designation" multipurpose health worker" created for these employees should be appointed permanently from the date of the government decision.
(2) The employees shall be entitled to the benefits of permanent service (pay, seniority, pension, etc.) from the date of their posting to the subordinate posts.
(3) No benefit or arrears of service rendered earlier shall be admissible to such employees.
4) No funds will be provided by the government for the salary of these employees.
5) Before being reinstated in service, it will be clearly mentioned that no previous benefit will be received by taking a bond from the concerned employee.
The above 34" Multi-Purpose Health Workers" will be given permanent appointment as per the rules after checking the attendance, default record, inquiry, service attendance, caste validity verification certificate, medical certificate etc. It will be mandatory for the concerned employees to submit the caste verification, character verification report within a period of 6 months from the date of order of permanent appointment.
