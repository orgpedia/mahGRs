Preamble: The Panvel Municipal Corporation has been established on 01.10.2016. The total area of Panvel Municipal Corporation is 110.16 sq.km.
The revised figure of a total of 1042 posts has been approved under the Government Decision dated 16.03.2021 regarding the increase in urbanization of the newly created Panvel Municipal Corporation area, the stress on infrastructure due to important initiatives/ institutions/ projects of the Central and State Government, as well as the posts required by the Panvel Municipal Corporation in fulfilling the role of an urban local body as a public welfare and dynamic administration.
The Panvel Municipal Administration has prepared the Service Access Rules of the Municipal Corporation as per the procedure in the Urban Development Department, Government Circular dated 02.07.2008.
The Panvel Municipal Corporation General Body Resolution No. 31, dated 06.08.2018 has been approved. The Commissioner, Panvel Municipal Corporation has submitted the said Service Admission Rules to the Government for approval vide letter dated 29.08.2018.
Accordingly, the matter of giving combined approval to the service admission rules of administrative and technical posts of Panvel Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The draft service access submitted by Panvel Municipal Corporation for government approval as per section 457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act," Panvel Municipal Corporation Service (Classification of Service Access and Services) Rules, 2022" as added by making necessary amendments.
