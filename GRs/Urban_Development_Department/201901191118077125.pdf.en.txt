The Proposal:
Under the relevant Government decision, it has been approved to make financial provision for" Tree plantation and rearing" in the limit of 0.5% from the" Programme expenditure" out of the overall budgetary provision of the Administrative Department. Accordingly, the matter of opening a new account was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to open a new head of account for" Tree plantation and rearing" within the limit of 0.5% of the expenditure on the programme of the Urban Development Department as follows:
