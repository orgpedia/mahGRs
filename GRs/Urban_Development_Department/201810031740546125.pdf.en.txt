The release of funds to the tune of Rs. 105,29,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Five Crores Thirty Nine Lakhs only) is being sanctioned under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions.
The State Government will bear 100% of the project cost. Therefore, the technical and administrative approval of the competent authority within the limit of the sanctioned amount for the works identified under the said project should be obtained under the Government Decision No. (1) to (5).
The implementing agency shall be the Town Panchayat or the Public Works Department subject to the appointment of a Project Management Consultant (PMC) in consultation with the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration.
Orders regarding Nagar Panchayats to be disbursed funds under the scheme will be issued separately.
4. Project cost to be determined at the end of e-tendering of works under the said project. If the project cost is more than the project cost as per the technical and administrative approval, no additional grant in any form will be provided by the Government for such additional cost.
If the project cost at the end of the e-tender is less than the project cost as per the technical and administrative approval, the State Government shall deposit its share of the reduced cost to the Government. This will be the responsibility of the collector and the collector.

The responsibility of the Collector shall be as follows: (a) to ensure that the works under the said project are carried out in publicly owned premises, the nature of the work is public, and it adds to the basic amenities of the general public, and to ensure that no private works are approved.
(b) to ensure that the works under the said project are in conformity with the development control rules and development plan of the city concerned.
(c) The works under the said project shall be mandatorily subjected to e-tendering process. The latest guidelines issued by the Public Works Department and the government in this regard should be strictly followed.
To ensure that the" e-tendering" mechanism is being followed.
(d) to see that the funds provided under the said project are spent for the same purpose, the work is done according to the quality or how the sanctioned funds are spent on the sanctioned work. Also, every six months, review the work done under this scheme under the district.
(e) to see that the expenditure incurred on the work under the said project does not violate the Model Code of Conduct.
(e) the expenditure of the Fund shall be made in accordance with the prescribed procedures and in accordance with all financial laws and regulations. To see that the instructions issued by the Finance Department from time to time regarding the spending of funds and the limits of financial power are not violated and no government rule/ authority is violated.
The responsibilities of the implementing agency shall be as follows: (a) To ensure that no repair and maintenance work is undertaken under the said project.
(b) to take a photograph of the status of the work under the said project before commencement and after the work and submit it on demand.
