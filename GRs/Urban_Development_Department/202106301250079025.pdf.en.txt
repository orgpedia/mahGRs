Introduction: The context. Classes B, C and D vide Government Order dated 28th March, 2011
The post of Additional Commissioner has been included in the figure of Municipal Corporations.
The amended section 39A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act provides as follows:
(1) The State Government may create one or more posts of additional
Municipal Commissioners in the Corporation and appoint suitable persons on such posts, who shall, subject to the control of the Commissioner, exercise all or any of the powers and perform all or any of the duties and functions of the Commissioner.
(2) Every person so appointed as the Additional Municipal Commissioner shall be subject to the same liabilities, restrictions and terms and conditions of
service, to which the Commissioner is subjected to as per the provisions of this Act.

As per the Act, the state government is empowered to create one or more posts of" Additional Commissioner" in any municipal corporation and appoint suitable persons to these posts.
2. in context. In pursuance of the powers conferred on the Government under Section 39A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, vide Government Order dated 11.11.2011, one post of Additional Commissioner each has been created in all the D category Municipal Corporations and provision has been made to fill up the post by deputation only. The officers working in the Municipal Corporation are not eligible for promotion to this post. the decision of the government. As on 06.01.2015, it has been approved to create two posts of Additional Commissioners on the establishment of' C' class Municipal Corporations. Out of the two sanctioned posts, one post is to be filled by deputation from the officers of the State Government service and the remaining one post is to be filled by selection from the officers of the rank of Deputy Commissioner of the Municipal Corporation and equivalent officers from the Technical and Accounts Service.
3. under reference. As per the Government decision dated 1.09.2014, the Municipal Corporations in the State of Maharashtra have been classified on the basis of 2011 Census.
Accordingly, for' C" category Municipal Corporations, the population and per capita income for the category of 10 lakh to 15 lakh is Rs. The criterion is more than 3000/- while the population criterion is 3 lakh to 10 lakh for the classification of' D' category Municipal Corporations.
However, at present, there is a considerable increase in the population of D category Municipal Corporations and to handle the increased workload and other administrative matters due to the outbreak of Corona, it is necessary to create an additional post in addition to the sanctioned post of Additional Commissioner in some D category Municipal Corporations with a large population.
The Census 2021 has been postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic. Therefore, the Government was considering creation of one more post of Additional Commissioner in the Municipal Corporations whose population is more than 5 lakh as per the 2011 Census. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Resolution: Under the powers conferred on the Government under Section 39-A and Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, the Government is approving the creation of one additional post in addition to the sanctioned one post of Additional Commissioner in the following 10 D category Municipal Corporations with a population of more than 5 lakh as per 2011 Census.
