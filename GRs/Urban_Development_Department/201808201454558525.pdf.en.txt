Improvement in Educational Qualification for the post of Scribe:
(a) Employees who, on the date of their appointment to the Gram Panchayats, possess the educational qualification of having passed the Shalalat Examination (10th pass) prescribed in accordance with the provisions of Rule 4-A of the Bombay Gram Panchayat Conditions of Service Rules, 1960, shall be deemed to be eligible for inclusion if they fulfil the other conditions prescribed under the Government Decision in A. No. 1 and 2, as the case may be.
Date fixed for Inclusion, Service Merit and Pensionary Benefits:
(a) In determining the seniority of service of an employee absorbed in the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat, the date fixed by the Gram Panchayat for the concerned employee shall be taken into account.
(b) to hold office from the date of establishment of the Municipal Council/ Panchayat for the benefit of pension, assured promotion and other pensionary benefits after the induction of the employee concerned.
Provision for inclusion in the post of Mukadam:
(a) A Gram Panchayat employee holding a prescribed educational qualification and working in the post of Mukadam in the Sanitary Department (Health Department) shall be considered eligible for inclusion in the post of Mukadam.
For matters other than the above, the contours of the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats have already been decided.
