In order to achieve the goal of providing clean environment and good health to all the citizens of the country, the Government of India is implementing the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) throughout the country from 2nd October, 2014 till the 150th birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi i. e. 2nd October, 2019. In line with the Central Government's" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)," the implementation of" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" has started in the State as per the Government Resolution dated 15th May, 2015 at Reference No.
To create awareness among people to encourage them to participate in Swachh Bharat Mission and to enhance the sustainable capacity of cities in terms of cleanliness and sanitation and to increase the participation of all sections of society in the journey towards Swachh Bharat/ Maharashtra, the Central Government is conducting a Swachhta-based survey of all Urban Local Bodies in the country.
Since the period of Swachh Bharat Mission is till the 150th birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation i. e. 2 nd October, 2019, all the Urban Local Bodies in the State have been given the target of achieving at least" ODF+" status and at least" 3 Star Rating" in the star rating of Garbage Free Cities by 2 nd October, 2019, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No.

4. Distribution of Incentive Prizes to top performing cities in Swachh Survekshan on 23rd July, 2019. A package of Rs.500 crore for the best performing Urban Local Bodies in Swachh Survekshan 2020 has been announced by the Government vide Government Resolution No.4 dated 11.09.2019.
Despite this, some of the urban local bodies in the state are not participating in the Swachh Survekshan-2020 to get at least" ODF+" status and" 3 star rating" in the rating of garbage-free cities.
In view of the above, the Government is giving instructions that it will be mandatory for all the Urban Local Bodies of the State to participate in the' Plastic Free City Challenge' by obtaining at least" ODF+" status and" 3 Star Rating" in the rating of Garbage Free Cities under the Swachh Bharat Mission.
The Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) which have achieved at least" ODF+" status and at least" 3 Star Rating" and are participating in Swachh Survekshan-2020 will be given priority in the form of Discretionary Grants other than the statutory grants given by the Government.
