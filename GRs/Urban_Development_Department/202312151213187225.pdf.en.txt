Preamble: Subsidy is distributed to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For this purpose, out of the total fund of Rs. 2881.69 crore, provision of Rs. 2695.44 crore in the original budget under the head 2217 8066 for the financial year 2023-2024 and provision of Rs. 186.25 crore made through supplementary demand under One Time Settlement Scheme in the Monsoon Session, 2023 for providing the amount of the issue of public water supply and street light electricity dues of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats to MSEDCL. 1717.3128 crore has already been released as per the Government decisions.
Now, the Finance Department is required to disburse a total amount of Rs. 480.7688 crore to the Directorate of Municipal Administration through the BEAMS system, out of the original budgetary provision of Rs.473 crore and the earlier balance of Rs. 7.7688 crore.
Government Decision: Subsidy is given to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For the year 2023-2024, out of the budgeted funds for this purpose, Rs. 480.7688 crore received through the distribution system is being disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the respective Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats/ Municipal Corporations.
2. Subsidiary Grants received through BEAMS system for Municipal Councils. As per the Government decision dated 16.06.2017, the Municipal Council Administration Directorate should deposit the amount directly in the bank account of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat.

The Deputy Commissioner, Directorate of Municipal Administration is being declared as the Controlling Officer and Assistant Commissioner (Accounts), Directorate of Municipal Administration as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursing the Subsidiary Grants to the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats directly into the bank accounts of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats.
4. Circular to the Government, Finance Department, while disbursing the said funds. The funds should be disbursed to the respective Municipal Councils only after fulfilling the conditions as on 02.04.2018 and on submission of 75% Utilization Certificate. Further, the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran has issued reference no. 5 Action should be taken according to the instructions in the government decision here.
The said fund should be spent under the head Demand No.F-2, 2217, Urban Development, 192, Assistance to Municipal Councils, Schemes under Five Year Plans, (00) (60) Subsidiary Grants to Municipal Councils on account of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance, 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (22178066).
6. Withdrawal and disbursement wise settlement of pending utilization certificates as per the prospectus on the website of the Accountant General should be done within one month. These certificates should be submitted to the Accountant General's office instead of submitting them to the Adhiveshan office.
7. Settlement of pending short payments should be done within one month as per the prospectus on the website of the Finance Department.
8. The Directorate of Municipal Administration should recover the excess amount from the concerned Municipal Councils at the earliest. Also, the recovery should be made under the head 911, recovery of overpayment, (00) (02) recovery of overpayment (non-plan) (22179957) 70, minus recovery.
9. The said Government Decision is in accordance with the provisions of Circular No. Earths-2018/ P. No. 69/ Earth-3, D. being issued under the authority given on 02.04.2018.
