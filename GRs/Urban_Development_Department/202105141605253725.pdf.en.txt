The government's decision:
Under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), the project at the cost indicated in Rakana 4 of the urban solid waste management statement of Nanded Municipal Corporation was approved as per the government decisions at Reference No. 2.
While the project was being implemented by the corporation, a request was made by the corporation to approve some new aspects in the project. (The new projects to be sanctioned and their cost are shown in Rakaana 5 and 6 respectively.) )
The proposal submitted by Nanded Municipal Corporation was examined at the level of State Mission Directorate and submitted for approval of the High Power Committee held on 25.02.2021. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
Accordingly, in accordance with the approval given by the High Powered Committee to the said proposal, the Government hereby approve the cost aspects mentioned in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively in the statement accompanying this Government Resolution.
5. With the above change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of Nanded Municipal Corporation Solid Waste will be as shown in para 8 of the statement accompanying this Government Resolution.
(6) If the cost of the original approved Detailed Project Report increases due to the revised Detailed Project Report, the cost of the increase shall be borne by the concerned Urban Local Bodies from the 14th Finance Commission Fund or Self-Fund. No additional funds will be provided by the government for this.
