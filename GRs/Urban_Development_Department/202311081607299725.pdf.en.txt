Preamble: The Assistant Teachers (Primary) and Assistant Teachers (Secondary) teachers working on pay scale, minimum salary and wholesale remuneration in the establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Education Department on contractual basis have been permanently included in the daily wage employees of other Municipal Corporations. On the same lines, the Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has requested to take a decision at the government level regarding the regular inclusion of temporary teachers working on contractual basis in the sanctioned and vacant posts in the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Education Department's establishment diagram.
2. Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Education Department. This has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 15.12.2022. As many as 131 posts of assistant teachers (secondary) and 372 posts of primary teachers have been sanctioned. Considering the proposal of the Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, a total of 50 teachers have been appointed through this process (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), who are working on contractual basis on the establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Education Department on temporary basis on pay scale, minimum salary and bulk remuneration. As the appointments of the remaining teachers in the proposal were made without following the prescribed procedure (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), they are not eligible for regular induction into the services of the Municipal Corporation. A total of 50 teachers- 29 assistant teachers (primary) and 21 assistant teachers (secondary)- were appointed through the prescribed route.

The government's decision:
Since the appointment of a total of 50 teachers, 29 Assistant Teachers (Primary) and 21 Assistant Teachers (Secondary) on the Navi Mumbai Municipal Education Department's establishment has been made by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), the approval of the Government is being given to continue their appointment on the approved and vacant posts of Primary Teacher S-10, 29200-92300 and Secondary Teacher S-14, 38600-122800 on the Navi Mumbai Municipal Education Department's establishment, Figure, Assistant Teacher (Primary) Statement" A" and Assistant Teacher (Secondary) Statement" B" subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) These teachers should be appointed to the sanctioned and vacant posts in the figure of Navi Mumbai Municipal Education Department from the date of the government decision.
(2) The teachers shall continue to be entitled to the benefits of permanent service (pay, seniority of service, pension, etc.) from the date of their appointment.
(3) No benefit or arrears of service rendered earlier shall be admissible to the said teachers.
4) No funds will be provided by the government for the salary of these teachers.
5) It will be clearly mentioned that no previous benefit will be received by taking a bond from the concerned teacher before being retained in the service.
6) A total of 50 teachers, including 29 assistant teachers (primary) and 21 assistant teachers (secondary), should be given permanent appointment as per the rules after checking the attendance default record, inquiry, service attendance, caste validity verification certificate, medical certificate, etc. The concerned teachers will be required to submit the caste verification, character verification report within a period of 6 months from the date of this order.
7) No one other than teachers A and B can be appointed to these posts.
8) The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation shall ensure that the pay scale of the said teachers shall not be more than the pay scale prescribed for that post.
9) The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation shall take action to adjust the educational qualification and professional qualification prescribed for such primary teachers. Primary teachers who have not passed the TET examination, the professional qualification mentioned in the background of the resolution, are required to pass the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) within a period of five years from the date of joining the said post, otherwise their services will be terminated.
10) Since the establishment cost of the Municipal Corporation is more than 35% while including the said teachers, the condition of 35% establishment cost is being relaxed in this case.
