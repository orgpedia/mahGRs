As per the decision of the Government dated 11.04.2018, the Government has approved the establishment of the" Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority Unit" by relocating the office of the Special Unit of Development Plan functioning under the Deputy Director, Town Planning at Aurangabad along with its staff. As per the decision of the Government dated 21.06.2018, the Town Planner (Gazetted Group-A), Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navnagar Development Authority have been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers. It was under consideration of the Government to declare the office as" Head of Office and Controlling Officer."
Government's decision:
The Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Unit MNPA and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navanagar Development Authority Unit have been declared as" Office Head and Controller" for the office of Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navanagar Development Authority Unit.
This Government Order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Rule No. 2 of Schedule 3 to the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division I.
03. While submitting the payments of Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation and Pimpri-Chinchwad Navanagar Development Authority Unit, Pimpri, Pune to the treasury, read the above. The Town Planner (Gazetted Group-A) shall sign as Drawing and Disbursing Officer as declared under the Government Decision dated 21.06.2018. Also the deputy director, town planning will sign as the controlling officer. The Director, Town Planning, Pune shall submit a certified copy of the signatures in this regard to the District Treasury Officer, Pune.
