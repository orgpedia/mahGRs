The Proposal:
Programme Management Unit of RCH-2 in Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation As per the order dated 17.07.2010, Mr. Sandeep Raut was appointed as' City Programme Manager' on contractual basis on a purely temporary basis on consolidated remuneration per month. In view of the requirement of the post of' City Programme Manager' for the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Medical Health Department, the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has decided to create 1 post of' City Programme Manager' on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation for the Medical Health Department. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 25.05.2022. The proposal and resolution of the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation regarding the inclusion of Mr. Sandeep Raut in the post of' City Program Manager' created under this government decision, as well as the eligibility and method of appointment for the post of' City Program Manager' in the Service Entry Rules, has been received by the government.
The appointment of Mr. Sandeep Raut, as' City Programme Manager' on contractual basis for the Programme Management Unit of RCH-2, Programme, is made in the prescribed manner i.e. advertisement, written test, interview, professional test where required, followed by selection committee of experts and officers. Therefore, the government was considering relaxing the condition of 35% establishment cost as a one-time matter while adjusting Mr. Sandeep Raut to the vacant post in the Municipal Corporation service, as well as making a provision in the Service Entry Rules regarding the eligibility and method of appointment for the post of' City Program Manager'.
Government Decision:- On the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, Dy. The post of' City Programme Manager' has been sanctioned under the Government decision dated 25.05.2022. Accordingly, Mr. Sandeep Raut, having been appointed to the post of' City Program Manager' in the year 2010 by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview), his' City Program Manager' is a sanctioned vacancy in the Municipal Corporation's figure.
