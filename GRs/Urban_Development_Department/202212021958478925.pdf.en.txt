GOVERNMENT ORDER: Commissioner, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation under reservation No. Regarding the project of houses for economically weaker sections (EWS) under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in the 124A plot, the following orders are being issued: 1. Considering the experience of Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority (MHADA) with regard to Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, MHADA should take up the construction of 6,348 houses taken up under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana by Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation.
MHADA will provide 30 per cent of the construction cost of the LIG component and 10 per cent of the commercial construction cost to the Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation.
3) The project should be completed within the stipulated time.
4) In pursuance of this order, the Commissioner, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi and the Vice-Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Authority, Mumbai shall take necessary action immediately.
