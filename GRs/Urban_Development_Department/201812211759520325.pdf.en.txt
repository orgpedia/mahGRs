Preamble: Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is being implemented in the State since 2015-16 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Under AMRUT Mission, the Water Supply Project of Chandrapur City, which was included in the plan for the financial year 2016-17, was accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 27th October, 2016 and 19th April, 2017 respectively. Subsequently, the administrative approval of the Chandrapur City Water Supply Project was under the consideration of the Government in view of the change in the components of the project.
The administrative approval granted under the Government Decision No. 4 and No. 5 shall, subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Decision No. 1 to 3, be subject to the following paragraphs: 2 is being amended as mentioned in the prospectus.
