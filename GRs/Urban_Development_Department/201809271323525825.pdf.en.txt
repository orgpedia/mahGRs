Preamble: With a view to bring dynamism, transparency and people-orientedness in the functioning of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, Reference No. Maharashtra Bill No. 1. The Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965 has been amended vide Act No. 25/2018.
Section 58 of the Act, under clause (1A), empowers the directly elected Mayor to approve proposals for development works to be proposed by the State Government and to approve the development works proposed to be taken up from the grants received from the State Government, when there are no specific suggestions and the Council has the power to select the works.
Further, by the proviso to section 93 clause 2, the Committee of the Mayor and the Principal has been empowered to approve such categories of contracts as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time.
In pursuance of the above amendments to the Act, Reference No. The Maharashtra Municipal Councils and Town Panchayats (Financial Powers to Directly Elected Town Chairpersons) Rules, 2018 have been notified under this notification, laying down the quantum and nature of powers of the directly elected Town Chairpersons for development works and sanction of contracts.
Government Decision: The issue of issuing explanatory Government Decision was under consideration of the Government for necessary action at the field level regarding the amendments to the Act and the provisions laid down by the Rules thereunder.
