Government Resolution No: MIBHAM-1122/ No. 253/ P.No. 70/ Navi-28
In order to maintain the uniformity in the format and service rules of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation and Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation as well as other Municipal Corporations and Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation, and in view of the increasing scope of legal work in Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation, the Government was considering to sanction a total of 03 new posts for the Law Department on the establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, such as' Chief Law Officer' (Class-1), 1 post and' Assistant Law Officer' (Class-3), 2 posts.
Government Decision: Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued the relevant orders. 04.02.2022 and D. In pursuance of the proposal for sanction of new posts submitted vide letter dated 04.07.2022, the Government is approving creation of 03 new posts of Chief Law Officer (Class-I) 1 post and Assistant Law Officer (Class-III) 2 posts in Annexure-B attached with the said Government Resolution, as per Section 51 (4) (a) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The total number of 2564 posts sanctioned as per Annexure' A' on the establishment of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation and 03 posts sanctioned as per Form' B' of this Government Decision will be the figure of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation. Similarly, for Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation, there will be a legal department as per Annexure-' C'.
