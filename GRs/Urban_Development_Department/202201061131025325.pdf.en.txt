The Proposal:
The state government has decided to build a memorial for Balasaheb Thackeray at Shivaji Park in Mumbai. The cost of the project will be borne by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). A proposal to open a new accounting head to reimburse the expenditure incurred by the Authority was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
The Government hereby approve the opening of new accounting records for reimbursement by the State Government of the expenditure incurred by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority for the construction of the Balasaheb Thackeray National Memorial Project on the site of the Mayor's Residence at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai as follows:
