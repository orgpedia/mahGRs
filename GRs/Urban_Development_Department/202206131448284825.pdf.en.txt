Introduction: Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Research, Training and Human Development (SARATHI), Pune The Company has been registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 25.6.2018. It was established by the Government of Maharashtra for the educational, economic and social upliftment of the target groups of Marathas, Kunbis, Kunbi-Marathas and Maratha-Kunbis. It has come into effect from 11.2.2019.
Sarathi Sanstha will work for the educational, economic and social upliftment of the target groups of Marathas, Kunbis, Kunbi-Marathas and Maratha-Kunbis by maintaining social commitments like hostels, training and skill development centers, women empowerment and etc. at the divisional level.
