The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission is being implemented in the State from the year 2021-22 as per the decision of the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Under this mission, infrastructure such as water supply, rejuvenation of lakes and green area development will be created in all the urban local bodies of the state and sanitation facilities will be provided in 44 cities of the state which were covered under the previous AMRUT Mission. Accordingly, Rs. The Central Government has approved the State Water Resources Plan (CAPEX cost only) with a project cost of Rs. 18236.39 crore. This includes the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation's Sewerage Project. As per the technical approval given by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Thane under Section 5, it has been approved by the State Level Technical Committee in its meeting dated November 22, 2022 and the State Level High Powered Steering Committee in its meeting dated December 08, 2022.

4.9 While raising self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on functional outcomes. Also, the project will not be funded if it does not meet the functional outcome. The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation will be fully responsible for fulfilling these requirements.
4.11 The first instalment of funds from the Central and State Governments for the project will be disbursed to the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation after the issuance of the work order.
