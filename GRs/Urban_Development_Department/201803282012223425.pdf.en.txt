Reference: Government Resolution Urban Development Department No. SRINAKHI- 2008/1338/ PR No. 171/08/ NO. 20 dated 02.03.2008 Preamble Urban amenities are provided through various Urban Local Bodies in the State.
For this, various development works are being carried out by the urban local bodies to create infrastructure. There are complaints that the quality of work is not up to the standard. The instructions for technical audit of the quality of such development works by a third party have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 02.03.2008. However, considering the increasing urbanization, the amount of urban taxes and the thousands of crores of rupees being distributed for development works in the urban areas, it is necessary to ensure that the work done through them is done in a quality manner. For this, the government was considering a fundamental reform in the procedures for technical audit of such works by third parties to make it more effective and efficient.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to ensure that the development works carried out by the Urban Local Bodies in the State are carried out in a dignified manner and generate sustainable value, the Government has decided to implement the following revised procedures for more effective and effective technical audit of the quality of these works by the tertiary system.
Stages of Tertiary Technical Audit: The Tertiary Technical Audit of development works carried out by the Urban Local Bodies in the State should be carried out in two stages.
(a) Phase-I (Urban Local Bodies Level): The third phase of Technical Audit shall be carried out for all the works being carried out with the funds of Urban Development Department.
For this, the Government Engineering Colleges/ Nominated Engineering Colleges in Form-A and Form-B are being taken on the empanelment list as per their demand.
