The Proposal:
The projects are funded by the National Highways Authority of India and other central agencies under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagrothan Mahabhiyan. The Government vide Government Resolution dated 04.05.2018 has laid down the policy for sanctioning of grants for acquisition of ancillary land to the respective Urban Local Bodies in respect of such projects. Accordingly, proposals are being received from various municipal corporations of the state. It was under the consideration of the Government to lay down a procedure for fixing responsibility in respect of the remuneration to be received in pursuance of these proposals.
Government's decision:
Ministry of Road Transport Highways NHAI provides funds to Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan (MSJNA) for land acquisition of land in areas where NHAI is not a statutory authority and is the responsibility of the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) for time bound completion of the projects being implemented by NHAI for construction of flyovers, ROBs, RUBs in urban areas on National Highways. Such land acquisition should be done in accordance with the land acquisition policy of the State Government and the consideration to be given for land acquisition as per the proposal should be at the rate prescribed in the land acquisition policy of the Government. Further, clear instructions have been issued vide Government Order dated 04.05.2018 that necessary scrutiny regarding eligibility and ineligibility of funds should be done by the concerned District Collector. The responsibility of determining the remuneration for this purpose should be done by the following committee under the chairmanship of the Collector.
