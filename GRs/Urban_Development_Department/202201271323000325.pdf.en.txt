Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government services for persons with disabilities. The central government has given. List of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, may be seen in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in and in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification, the list of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on the website. Published on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In pursuance of the directions in the said Government Resolution, the Government was considering the matter of ensuring posts in the cadre of Law Officers (Group-A) on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation for the disabled category.
Government's decision:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, has issued an order to the Government of India to provide for the following: Notification dated 04.01.2021 and issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. Town Structure and Valuation taking into consideration the Government Decision dated 02.02.2021
