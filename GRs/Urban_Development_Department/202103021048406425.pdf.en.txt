Preamble: The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and to improve their quality.
Under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission, a proposal to improve the riverfront development project of Roha City has been sent by the Directorate of Municipal Administration under reference No. The letter No. 6 was submitted to the State Level Accreditation Committee for approval. The project has been given technical approval by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Konkan, Thane.
Accordingly, as per the recommendation of the State Level Project Approval Committee in its meeting held on 24.12.2020, the matter of granting administrative approval to the said project was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Riverfront Development Project of Roha City, submitted under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission, was accorded administrative approval under the Government Resolution dated 12th January, 2018 at A.No. Now, the terms of reference of the Government and

Government Resolution No: Nagro-2020/ No. 258/ Navi-33, subject to the technical approval of Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran, the work of Rs. 2.9543 crore for the additional sub-station of the project is being approved by this Government Resolution. In this, the participation of the urban local body will be Rs. 0.7523 crore, which will be mandatory for the municipal council to set up.
All other terms and conditions of the project will remain unchanged as of January 12, 2018.
