The Proposal: -
Considering the digital policy of the state government, a policy is being made to make maximum use of computer in government work. Further, considering the inconvenience caused to the officers / staff, in order to avoid delays in the transfer process and to ensure transparency and consistency in the transfer process and to save precious time of the Government, the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation has decided to conduct regular / general transfers of Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadre officers / staff online. The policy decision has been taken under the Government Decision on 14.10.2024. The following instructions are being issued regarding the online Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for regular / general as well as request / premature transfers.
The Government's Decisions:
Under the above reference, No. Orders issued from time to time with respect to the various relevant definitions, eligibility criteria, transfer preferences, transfer / extension provisions, etc., in the Acts, Notifications, Government Decisions and Circulars mentioned herein and in force from 1 to 13 shall continue to apply in the transfer process. The request for changes made through the regulation of the said matters along with the general / regular as well as general replacement of officers / employees of Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadre of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation will now be done online.
The portal has been developed by the Urban Development Department in collaboration with the National Informatics Centre (NIC) for online implementation of online regular VB request transfers of Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadre officers / staff at the establishment of the Town Planning Department with the help of a computer and its access URL is: https://mahaehrms.nic.in.
Detailed information about the online system developed by the Department of Urban Development in collaboration with the National Informatics Centre (NIC) is being released through the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for online request for general transfers.

The information required to carry out the online transfer process will be taken on the basis of the information entered in the online service book of the MSHA officer / employee which is filled on the eHRMS system of the government.
Every government officer / employee working on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation must be filled in the information on the government's cHRMS system (in the online service book) and that information will be standardized and updated at the level of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation and the subordinate regional office of the Directorate.
It shall be the responsibility of the concerned officer / staff to ensure that the information filled on the cHRMS system is verified by the Directorate and if any error is found in the information filled on the cHRMS system, the matter shall be brought to the notice of the office head of the officer / staff concerned. The details of Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadre officers / staff at the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation in the service book on the cHRMS system should be complete, up-to-date and authenticated.
To be eligible for online transfer of government officers / employees of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation and other service matters, every government officer / employee of a Town Planning Establishment must have an official e-mail ID (@mah. gov.in, @ nic.in, @ gov.in, @ eauth.in) and telephone number registered with the CHRMS system.
The responsibility of registering any government e-mail ID (@mah. gov.in, @ nic.in, @ gov.in, @ eauth.in) and telephone number on the ecHRMS system shall lie with the concerned government officer / employee. Without this information, the officer / employee concerned will not be considered for any operation of the online transfer service.

10. Discharge and stay orders: -
The orders of dismissal shall be the orders of transfer and posting issued. Also, on the issue of transfer orders, the officer / staff concerned shall join the place of posting within the prescribed time. After the date of termination, they should not be paid their salary or any payments from the place where they were working before the transfer. The officers concerned will be responsible if the salaries of such employees are withdrawn. If the officer / employee concerned does not join the place of transfer after the transfer within the permitted period, disciplinary action should be taken against such officer / employee by the competent authority as per rules. Also, the report of the officer / employee who has been transferred should be immediately submitted by the concerned office to the competent authority.
Il. Retired officers / employees are required to join the place of posting keeping in view the prescribed period of posting.
111. All concerned establishments shall update the information of officers / employees who have been relieved / joined after transfer on the online system by 5th June. The head of the concerned office will be fully responsible for this.
119. Prevention of pressure by employee in transfer process:
Since transfers are to be made with the help of the system developed for general transfers of officers / staff at the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation, and as the transferable officers / staff are to be transferred through the system as per the rules prescribed for the transferable officers / staff, preference, availability of space, administrative urgency of the officers / staff and since transfers are purely administrative in nature, any employee exercising royal pressure will be liable for disciplinary action as he / she has violated Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
