The Proposal:
Mr. Appasaheb Shivamurthy Hattargi, City Planner (Group-A/ Gazetted) has expressed his desire to take voluntary retirement on medical grounds. 03 months advance notice has been given under the application dated 10.10.2018.
Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune. As per the letter dated 10.10.2018, Shri Appasaheb Shivamurthy Hattargi, Town Planner (Group-A/
gazetted), their prior notice of voluntary retirement was accepted and issued to them. The proposal for retirement from Government service with effect from 10.01.2019 (M.P.) has been submitted to the Government for approval.
The government has issued orders to this effect:
Government order:
As per the provisions of Rule 65 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, Shri Appasaheb Shivamurthy Hattargi, Municipal Councillor, Deputy Director, Municipal Corporation, Mumbai Region (Evaluation), Mumbai, has been appointed as the Deputy Commissioner of Municipal Corporation, Mumbai. Accepting the earlier notice of Voluntary Retirement dated 10.10.2018. The Government has approved the release from Government service with effect from 10.01.2019 (MP).
Shri Appasaheb Shivamurthy Hattargi, Town Planner (Group-A/ Gazetted), who is presently not facing any departmental enquiry/ judicial/ corruption/ disciplinary proceedings, pending/ proposed to be taken up, and has no pending official engagements, is being allowed to take voluntary retirement subject to the conditions.
The Director, Town and Country Planning, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take necessary action to issue necessary executive orders in this regard.
