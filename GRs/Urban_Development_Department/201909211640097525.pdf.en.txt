The process of distribution of funds:
3.1 As per the guidelines of the Centrally Sponsored Swachh Bharat Mission, the admissible share of funds of the Central and State Governments will be distributed in two tranches (50%+ 50%).
3.2 As per the instructions given at the time of disbursement of funds for the said project, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks allocated in accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government.
3.3 The funds allocated for the said project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, the matter will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
3.4 After the project is approved by the State Government for the Urban Local Bodies, any increase in the original project cost for any reason will be the sole responsibility of the concerned Urban Local Bodies/ Implementing Agencies. anything by the state government.
Additional grants will not be awarded.
3.5 For Solid Waste Management projects sanctioned under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), in addition to the grant admissible by the Central and State Governments, the remaining funds (as per Para 5 of the above table) shall be required to be raised by the respective Urban Local Self-Government Institutions and spent in proportion to the Central and State accounts.
3.6 It shall be mandatory for the Urban Local Bodies to set up self-accounting fund in proportion to the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of Swachh Maharashtra Mission and submit the utilization certificate of the total expenditure in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
3.7 The second instalment of funds will be disbursed after submission of utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the first instalment of funds provided for the project to the State Government.
4. terms and conditions:
4.1 The implementation mechanism for this project will be in the Ardhapur Nagar Panchayat.
4.2 It shall be mandatory for the implementing agency to implement the project within the prescribed time frame as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the Government Resolution No. 1 under the State Government and Solid Waste Management Rules, 2016.
4.3 Detailed Project Reports of Solid Waste Management Projects have been prepared as per the guidelines prescribed under the Solid Waste Management Rules, 2016. The report of the project
Success is based on segregation of solid waste at the point of generation. Therefore, it will be necessary to segregate 100% of the solid waste generated in the city at the construction site and collect it separately.

Accordingly, it will be mandatory for the Ardhapur Nagar Panchayat to collect at least 90% of the daily solid waste generated in the city by December 2019.
4.4 It will be mandatory to start the process on this segregated wet waste in a concentrated or decentralized manner.
4.5 The Implementing Agency shall be responsible for implementing the detailed project report on the transportation of segregated waste and the scientific procedures to be followed.
4.6 It will be mandatory to purchase the equipment/ machines/ machines etc. mentioned in the Detailed Project Report of the Solid Waste Management Project from the Government eMarketplace (GeM) portal only. Further, if the purchase is made by adopting other methods, the government will take further appropriate action by understanding the financial irregularities.
4.7 While preparing the Detailed Project Report, the requirements for the next 5 years have been taken into consideration. While starting the implementation of this project report, the necessary purchases and constructions should be done taking into account the current needs.
4.8 After the commencement of the project, it shall be the responsibility of the concerned local body/ executing agency to make payment of the work to the concerned contractor within 15 days within the limit of available funds after the competent authority certifies the quality of work and payment.
4.9 The local body concerned shall develop the necessary mechanism for meeting the maintenance and repair cost of the urban solid waste management project and thereby meet the maintenance and repair cost of the project.
While implementing the Detailed Project Report (DPR) being administratively approved for solid waste management of Ardhapur Nagar Panchayat under Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban), it will be mandatory for the implementing agency to meet the target set as follows. 100% segregation of solid waste generated in the city at source.
Transportation of 100% segregated waste in segregated manner.
Making compost manure from wet waste.
The organic manure generated from wet waste shall be tested as per FCO standards by a laboratory authorized by the State Government and shall be branded as' Harit Mahasiti Compost."
Also to sell this organic manure in the name of" Green City Compost."
Secondary segregation of dry waste should be done at the material recovery facility. The recyclable waste should be recycled or sold if possible.
