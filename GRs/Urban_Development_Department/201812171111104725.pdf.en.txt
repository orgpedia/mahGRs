The Proposal:
Various development works are being carried out by various civic bodies under the Urban Development Department. Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) provide services of private consultants for preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for these development works. The matter was under consideration of the Government to amend the orders issued under the Government Order dated 04.06.2018 regarding the fee to be charged for providing services of such private consultants.
Government's decision:
The rates prescribed under the Government Decision on retrospective application of Project Management Consultants rates as laid down by the Government Decision dated 04.06.2018 are being made applicable with retrospective effect from 04.06.2018.
The rates applicable under the Government decision dated 04.06.2018 shall be applicable to all Urban Local Bodies (Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats) in the State. Also, this
