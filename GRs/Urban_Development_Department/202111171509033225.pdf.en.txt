Government's decision:
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
The Government of India has approved the release of full and final instalment of funds to the State for capacity building and administrative and office expenses under Swachh Bharat Mission for the financial year 2021-22 amounting to Rs. 79,20,000/- (Rupees Eighty-nine Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) and State Government's admissible share of Rs. 52,80,000/- (Rupees Twenty-two Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) amounting to Rs. 1,32,00, 000/-crore (Rupees One Crore Thirty-two Lakh only). The expenditure shall be met from the provision made under that head during the financial year 2021-22 as shown in the table below.
