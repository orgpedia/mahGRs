Introduction: Establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. It was established on 01.01.1992 and is a C category Municipal Corporation. The total area of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation is 108 sq km and the population of the corporation as per Census 2011 is 11.50 lakh.
2. The Government has decided on the posts required by the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation in view of the increase in urbanization of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation area, the TTC industrial area located on a large area, the IT sector, the stress on infrastructure due to important initiatives/ institutions/ projects of the Central and State Governments, and the role of the Urban Local Body as a people's welfare and dynamic administration. A total of 3,935 vacancies have been approved as on 21.08.2017.
3. Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Health and Fire Services Access Rules. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 22.09.2017. As the service entry rules for administrative and technical posts were not approved, the corporation was facing difficulties in filling these posts through direct service/ promotion. Urban Development Department, Govt. As per procedure dated 02.07.2008, the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC) has framed the Service Access Rules of the corporation. It was passed by the general body meeting of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation vide Resolution No. 826, d. Approved on 03.01.2019. Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation As per the letter dated 08.11.2019, the said Service Access Rules have been submitted for Government approval. Similarly, the The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has issued a notification regarding amendment in the Service Entry Rules for certain posts in the Fire and Health Department approved on 22.09.2017. 03.03.2020 and D. The request has been made vide letter dated 06.03.2020.
Accordingly, the matter of amending the Service Entry Rules of the Health and Fire Department of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation for the year 2017 and giving combined approval to the Service Entry Rules for administrative and technical posts was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Resolution No.No.1219/ P.No.137/ NV-28
Government's decision:
The draft service access rules submitted by Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation for government approval as per section 457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act and the consolidated" Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Services (Classification of Services and Services) Rules, 2021" (with Annexure- 1 to 4) are being approved by the government under sections 454, 455, 456, 457 (3) and 465 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, as amended and added to the service access rules of the Health and Fire Department.
The said rules may be called the" Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2021."
(3) These rules shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
4) Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. In order to ensure uniformity in promotion of certain posts in the figure approved under the Government decision dated 21.08.2017, and for administrative convenience, details of adjustment of these posts to other posts, change in designation and change in pay scale/ grade pay are given in the accompanying Form-A and Form-B. It is being approved by the government as per Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The revised structure indicated in the" Form-C" is being approved as per Section-51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act by reorganizing the vacancies in the Engineering Department of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation.
