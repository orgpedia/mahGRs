Since a total of 5 such posts need to be created, for this, the Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued a notification to the concerned. The proposal for creation of new posts vide letter dated 17.04.2023 has been submitted to the Government for approval.
In view of the increasing scope of health work in Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, the government was considering to sanction a total of 5 new posts for the Public Health Department on the establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, such as' Chief Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class 2), 1 post and' Senior Pharmaceutical Officer' (Class-3), 4 posts.
Government Decision: Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued the relevant orders. In pursuance of the proposal for sanction of new posts submitted vide letter dated 17.04.2023, the Government is granting approval for creation of 05 new posts of Chief Pharmaceutical Officer (Class-II) 1 post and Senior Pharmaceutical Officer (Class-III) 4 posts in Annexure-B attached with the said decision, as per Section-51 (4) (a) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The establishment of Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation will have a total of 2569 posts sanctioned as per Annexure-' A' and 05 posts sanctioned as per Form-' B' of this Government Decision.
