02. Read about the deadline for submission of caste certificate by the government. The Maharashtra Ordinance No.2 of 2018. 20 have been announced. The Ordinance amends Section 5B of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act to make the period of furnishing caste certificate from the date of election to the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM) 12 months instead of six months. It has been implemented since April 7, 2015.
Similarly, the Ordinance also provides in Chapter V, Section 9, that" no person shall be deemed to be disqualified under the provisions of the said Municipal Acts if, before the date of commencement of this Ordinance, any person who has obtained a caste certificate and a certificate of validity but has not filed such certificate has submitted such certificate within a period of fifteen days from the date of commencement of this Ordinance."
