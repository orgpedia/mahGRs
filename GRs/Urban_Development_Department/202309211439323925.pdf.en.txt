The Proposal:
Mr. Chinmay Pradeep Deshpande, Junior Engineer (Electrical), Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation, Chandrapur has requested to transfer his services from Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation to Amravati Municipal Corporation. Accordingly, the Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation vide General Body Resolution No. 29, dated 25.10.2021 has approved and the Amravati Municipal Corporation vide reference No. As per resolution No. 3, it has been approved to transfer the services of Mr. Chinmay Pradeep Deshpande, Junior Engineer (Electrical).
Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Municipal Services (Recruitment and Classification of Services) Rules, 2008 provides for transfer from one Municipal Corporation to another by mutual consent on such terms and conditions as may be agreed to by the appointing authority. As the Chandrapur Municipal Corporation and the Amravati Municipal Corporation have mutually approved the transfer of Chinmaya Pradeep Deshpande, Junior Engineer (Electrical) on the establishment of Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation, the transfer of services to Amravati Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No. Champa-1423/ No. 50/ No.26 Government Resolution:
The Government is approving the transfer of the services of Mr. Chinmay Pradeep Deshpande, Junior Engineer (Electrical), Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation from Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation to Amravati Municipal Corporation on a permanent basis subject to the conditions of working in the pay scale equivalent to the post and the terms and conditions of Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Municipal Services (Recruitment and Classification of Services) Rules, 2008.
The Commissioner, Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation should relieve Mr. Deshpande, Junior Engineer (Electrical), Chandrapur City Municipal Corporation, immediately after fulfilling his service matters to join the Amravati Municipal Corporation.
The Commissioner, Amravati Municipal Corporation should appoint Mr. Deshpande to the equivalent post of Junior Engineer (Electrical) in the Municipal Corporation.
4.The expenditure of the salary of Mr. Chinmay Deshpande should be borne by the concerned Municipal Corporation.
