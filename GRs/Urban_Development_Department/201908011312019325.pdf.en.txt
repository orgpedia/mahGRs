Preamble: The designation of 6 male laborers working at the sanitation establishment of Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation should be changed to Sanitation Inspector and the pay scale of this post should be given. The standing committee meeting of the corporation unanimously approves that the said appointment should be made to the post of sanitary inspector lying vacant on the establishment of the corporation or the figure should remain subject to approval and this resolution is upheld in this meeting. The 85. 12.102018 has been passed by the Standing Committee of Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation.
This decision has not been taken on paper. As per the decision of the Speaker, No. 85, c. It was approved on 12.10.2018.
2. Municipal Administration under its purview. In the letter dated 29.11.2018, it has been mentioned that the said employees are working as male labourers in Municipal Sanitary Establishments. The posts of male/ female workers in Municipal Corporation Safai Establishments are in the category of Class 4 and the pay scale of this post is 4440-7440 Grade Pay 1300. The post of Sanitary Inspector is in the cadre of Class 3 and the pay scale of the post of Sanitary Inspector is 5200-20200 with Grade Pay of 2400.
There are a total of 14 posts of Sanitation Inspector sanctioned in the Municipal Corporation. Out of these, 12 sanitary inspectors are currently working and 2 posts of sanitary inspector are vacant. However, since the post of Sanitation Inspector does not fall in the chain promotion, as well as the concerned employee being from the cadre of Class 4 Male Labourer, the designation of all concerned cannot be changed or promoted. The said resolution passed by the Standing Committee of the Municipal Corporation was requested to be quashed under Section 451 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act by the Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation vide letter dated 29.11.2018.

3. In view of the above, the said Resolution No. 85 dated 12.10.2018 passed by the Government for dissolution under section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949. It was suspended on April 1, 2019.
The Deputy Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation said that no complaint has been received in this regard. vide letter dated 12.6.2019.
4. In view of the above, the Standing Committee of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 85, c. The matter of final dissolution of the resolution dated 12.10.2018 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Standing Committee of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed a resolution regarding the change of the designation of 6 male workers working at the sanitation establishment to the name of Sanitation Inspector and the pay scale of that post. The 85. 12.10.2018 has been approved. The posts of male labourer are in the category of Class 4 cadre and the post of sanitary inspector is in the category of Class 3 cadre. Also, the pay scale of both the posts is different. In view of all these facts, the Standing Committee of the Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 85, c. 12.10.2018 As it is not binding and binding, the said resolution is being finally quashed under Section 451 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
