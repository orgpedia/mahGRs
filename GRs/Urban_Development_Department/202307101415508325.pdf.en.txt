Mumbai Metro Line-7 (Andheri East) and Dahisar (East) and Mumbai Metro Line-2 Project Phase-2B (DN Nagar-Mandalay)
The government has decided to implement the projects through the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority. Approved by 1 to 3. The project will be funded by the state government, central and state governments and external funding agencies.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA), the implementing agency for the project, pays the first instalment and submits the reimbursement claims to the New Development Bank. After the New Development Bank approves the claim for reimbursement, the same amount is deposited by the New Development Bank into the Consolidated Fund of the State through the Central Government. The loan amount is to be disbursed as a loan from the state government to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority through the budget.
The State Government has sanctioned Rs.200 crore towards New Development Bank loan for Mumbai Metro 2A, 2B and 7 projects by the end of March, 2023. 643.8081 crore has been disbursed to the Authority.
