Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored" Suvarna Jayanti Shahari Rozgar Yojana" has been restructured and converted into" National Urban Livelihoods Mission" under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana and is being implemented in the State. The scheme is being implemented on a 60: 40 ratio between the Centre and the state. Under this campaign, the people of urban poor families will be provided with skill training and opportunities for employment or self-employment. Under this campaign, measures are taken to provide shelter facilities to the urban homeless, to solve the problems related to livelihood of street vendors in urban areas, to reduce urban poverty by improving the quality of life of urban poor families, etc. Reference No. 2 In a letter here, the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, has requested for the allocation of Centre-State share for this mission.

Government Resolution No: Deendayal Antyodaya- NULM-2020/ PR No. 159/ NV-20
Government Decision: Under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana, 50% of the allocation made available by the Finance Department for the programme under National Urban Livelihoods Mission has been allocated as Centre-State share of Rs. 3000.00 lakhs and Rs. 1900.00 lakh for the financial year 2020-2021 is being disbursed to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration and State Mission Director, National Urban Livelihood Mission.
The Assistant Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration and State Mission Director, National Urban Livelihood Mission are being declared as the Controlling Officers for the purpose of drawing the said funds.
They have to deposit the amount in the Payment and Accounts Office, Bandra (East), Mumbai and get the cheque.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be shared by the Urban Development Department under the head Demand No.F-3, 2230, Labour and Service Scheme, 02, Service Planning Service, 101 Service Planning Service, 03, Civil Services Subsidy Programme, (03) (01) Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana National Urban Subsidy Mission (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (Central Share 60% Share) (31) Subsidiary Grants (22306047) (Non-Payment) and State Share 40% (31) Subsidiary Grants (22306056) (Non-Payment) out of the provision sanctioned for the year 2020-2021.
