Preamble: The policy on legacy appointment of Safai Karamcharis has been prescribed by the Department of Social Justice vide Government Circular dated 21/10/2011 and is subject to change from time to time by the same Department. Prior to the Government Circular dated 21/10/2011 of that Department, Government Decisions/ Circulars/ Standing Orders have been issued from time to time by the Urban Development Department and the Directorate of Municipal Administration in this regard. The circulars/ standing orders issued by the Directorate of Municipal Administration are applicable only to the Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State. The Department of Social Justice while issuing the above circular dated 21/10/2011 has not violated the provisions of the earlier circular issued by the Urban Development Department/ Directorate. It is being asked from the government whether these circulars/ standing orders will be applicable to the municipal corporations or how.
The rules and policies regarding the inheritance rights of the safai karamcharis in the state are the same for all the departments of the state under which the safai karamcharis are working. Therefore, in order to maintain uniformity in the matter of appointment of inheritance rights of Safai Karamcharis in Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats and Safai Karamcharis in Municipal Corporations, the following directions are being issued:- GOVERNMENT Circular: In the matter of appointment of inheritance rights of Safai Karamcharis in Municipal Corporations in the State, the circulars/ standing orders issued from time to time including the circular dated 23/03/2006 issued by the Directorate of Municipal Administration will be applicable in the case of appointment of inheritance rights in Municipal Corporations.
