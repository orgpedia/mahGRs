The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Abhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and raising their standards.
The water supply project of Ratnagiri city under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission was granted administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 2 of the Context. the Directorate of Municipal Administration
The matter of approving the inclusion of the Ratnagiri City Water Supply Project in the Goods and Services Tax was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The water supply project of Ratnagiri city under the Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan has been granted administrative approval under the Government Resolution No. 2. The proposal submitted to the Government under Reference No. 3 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration regarding the inclusion of Goods and Services Tax in the water supply project is being approved by this Government Decision as mentioned in the following statement.
