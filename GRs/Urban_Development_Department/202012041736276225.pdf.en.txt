The provisions of sections 45, 53 and 54 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act are self-explanatory.
Further, the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 4th May, 2006 are clear. Meanwhile, the general body meeting of the Amravati Municipal Corporation passed a resolution regarding the administrative post of the Deputy Commissioner to be filled by promotion on the municipal corporation establishment, when no process of promotion has been implemented by the administration, temporary recruitment from the officer in the technical cadre on the municipal corporation establishment. 28 dated 18/09/2020 is wholly inconsistent with the provisions of Sections 53 and 54 of the Act and the Government Resolution dated 04.05.2006, pursuant to the dissolution under the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the Commissioner, Amravati Municipal Corporation, Amravati has submitted the proposal under the aforesaid Reference letter for the approval of the Government.
Accordingly, the proposal to take a decision on the proposal sent by the Commissioner, Amravati Municipal Corporation to the Government was under consideration at the Government level, accordingly, the Government has decided as follows:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The General Assembly of the Amravati Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 28 dated 18/09/2020 is deemed to be temporarily suspended under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
However, on this, as per Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, the Commissioner, Amravati Municipal Corporation and the Municipal Corporation are being given an opportunity to make a representation within one month from the date of the order. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further action will be taken as per Section 451 (3) of the Act.
