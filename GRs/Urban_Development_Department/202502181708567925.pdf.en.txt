Preamble: - Municipal Corporations / Municipal Councils / Municipalities in the State were given grants under the head "General Road Grants (3054 0022)" under the scheme "Special Road Grants / General Road Grants" for development of roads. The revised norms and guidelines of the scheme have been prescribed under the Government Decision No. (1) in this regard.
The Office of the Principal Accountant General has issued a notification regarding the change in the title of the accounts of the scheme vide Reference No. Since bananas are prescribed under Section 2, the issue of granting new SERN approval for the scheme was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following Government Decisions are being issued.
Government Decision: - The heading "General Road Subsidy (3054 0022)" of the Special Road Subsidy / General Road Subsidy (Compulsory) Scheme is now being changed to "Road Subsidy (Compulsory) (3604 1054) - the amount of motor vehicle tax payable by the State to the Urban Local Bodies."
