Preamble: Solapur Municipal Corporation is a D category Municipal Corporation. 01.05.1964. As per the 2011 census, Solapur Municipal Corporation has a population of 9,51,558 and an area of 178.57 km2.
2. Solapur Municipal Corporation has been sanctioned necessary posts from time to time due to the increase in urbanization of the Solapur Municipal Corporation area, the industrial sector, the stress on infrastructure due to important initiatives/ institutions/ projects of the Central and State Governments, as well as the role of the urban local body as a people's welfare and dynamic administration. A total of 4612 posts of Solapur Municipal Corporation have been approved as per the Government decision dated 28.12.2021.
Since the Solapur Municipal Corporation Service Admission Rules, 1964, are required to be amended from time to time, the General Body Meeting of Solapur Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 22, d. Committee constituted on 15.12.2020. In the last meeting held on 04.07.2021, the revised Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2022 were approved and a proposal was submitted to the Government for its approval.
Accordingly, the matter of approving the new Service Entry Rules by overriding the Service Entry Rules of 1964 of Solapur Municipal Corporation was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Solapur Municipal Corporation has submitted the" Solapur Municipal Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2022" submitted for the approval of the Government under Section 457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act (with Annexure- 1 to 4) under Sections 454, 455, 456, 457 (3) and 465 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
