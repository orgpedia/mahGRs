Letter of Intent dated 24.08, 2023: The proposal was submitted by the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration after preparing a list of 27 employees from the state-level municipal council cadre as per the merit of the limited departmental examination. As per the decision of the Government in the context of the Urban Development Department, the employees of 27 State Level Municipal Council Cadre have been appointed as On Field Training in the post of Trainee Principal, Group-B.
The training of the trainee headmaster under the relevant purview is required to be imparted to the said officers working in the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat in Group-B. Accordingly, in the year 2023-24, the Government was considering to disburse funds for basic training for 42 officers including 27 probationary trainee principals, Group-B and newly appointed 15 probationary trainee principals, Group-B.
Government Decision: A total of 42 trainees of the post of Trainee Principal, Group-B under probation as proposed by the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Navi Mumbai
