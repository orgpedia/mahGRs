Preamble: To provide a vehicle with a driver for the office of the Regional Deputy Director and 34 District Administration Officers working in 6 Divisional Offices under the Directorate of Municipal Administration and Rs. 30,000/- per month under the Municipal Assistance Grant of Urban Development Department. Under the Office Expenditure admissible as per the provisions of 31.08.2009 Reference No. 1 has been approved by the government.
However, considering the increasing responsibilities, administrative visits to Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the area of work for the control of the scheme and the need for field visits and increasing fuel prices, reference no. 1 The matter of revising the cost of vehicle rental permissible under the government decision here was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the increasing responsibilities of the officers of the posts of Joint Commissioners at the divisional level, the permissible vehicle rental cost for 6 Joint Commissioners at the divisional level for visits and field visits in Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats has been increased from Rs. 60,000/- is being approved.
