the prelude
The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was promulgated with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their growing expectations from the local administration. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28/04/2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority is required to notify under this Ordinance, within a period of 3 months from the date of promulgation of the Ordinance and from time to time, the public servants, designated officers, first and second appellate authorities and the time limits prescribed. Accordingly, the Urban Development Department, Govt. 2 Government Circulars have been issued from time to time in this regard. Referred to as No. The matter of determining the master list by combining the circulars of 2 was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Referred No. The circulars issued by the Urban Development Department under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 are being published as per the master list of public services provided under the Maharashtra Regional Planning and Town Planning Act, 1966. The details of the period of service of the said public service, the office to which the service is to be notified as well as the designated officer, the first appellate officer, the second appellate officer are mentioned in Annexure A and B.
