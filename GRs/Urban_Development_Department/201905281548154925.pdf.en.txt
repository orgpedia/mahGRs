Capacity of sewerage projects in urban areas.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No: Lawless-2017/ PR No. 533/ NV-33
Ministry, Mumbai- 400032.
The date: May 28, 2019.
The Proposal:
Sewerage projects are implemented in the cities of the state according to the schemes sponsored by the central and state governments as well as the local civic bodies with their own funds.
The capacity of sewerage projects depends on the present and future population of the city, the water supply to the city and the sewage generated. However, it is observed that the sewerage projects being constructed by the local civic bodies are of large capacity irrespective of the present and future population of the city, water supply to the city and the sewage generated. As a result, the city lacks proper planning and government funds are wasted. Proper planning regarding the capacity of the sewerage project.
The Public Accounts Committee has also made recommendations in its report. Therefore, the matter of providing suggestions for the implementation of sewerage projects as per the schemes sponsored by the Central and State Governments as well as the local civic bodies from their own funds was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The following instructions are being issued regarding the implementation of sewerage projects funded by the Central and State Government schemes as well as the local civic bodies
(1) The construction of sewerage projects should be done taking into account the present and future population of the city, the water supply to the city and the sewage generated. While taking action in this regard, it should be ensured that the government funds are not unnecessarily invested in the construction of sewage treatment plants of more capacity than the prescribed standards.
2) The DPR of sewerage projects should be prepared and the implementation should be planned according to the criteria set by the Central Public Health and Environmental Engineering Organization (CPHEEO) of the Central Government.
