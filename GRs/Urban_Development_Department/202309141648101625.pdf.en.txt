Ganeshotsav Mandals to be allowed only once in five years
The Government of Maharashtra
the Urban Development Department
Government Resolution No. 2023/ P.S. No.251/ NO.20
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 13th of September 2023
Read:- The Chief Minister presided over the meeting. Meeting held on 04.09.2023
The Proposal:
The Chief Minister presided over the public meeting. Dated 04.09.2023
As per the directions given in the meeting, the government was considering giving one-time permission for the next five years to encourage the best Ganeshotsav Mandals that have followed all the rules and regulations in the last 10 years and have no complaints.
Government's decision:
Mahesh Bedekar v. State of Maharashtra& Others PIL No. 173/2010. Ganeshotsav Mandals implementing the directions of the High Court should be encouraged.
The government has taken the following decisions:
1. d. All Municipal Corporations, all Municipalities and Nagar Panchayats should take action to allow excellent Ganeshotsav Mandals for the next five years during the Ganeshotsav starting from 19.09.2023.
2. The nominal rent/ fee of Rs 100/- (Rupees one hundred only) should be charged to Ganeshotsav Mandals while giving space to Ganeshotsav Mandals to celebrate Ganeshotsav on the land owned by Municipal Corporation, Municipality and Nagar Panchayat.
3. The Ganeshotsav Mandals complying with the conditions prescribed in the Government Decisions and Orders issued from time to time in this regard shall be allowed by the competent authorities of the concerned Urban Local Bodies through online mode after obtaining a no-objection certificate from the local police station.
