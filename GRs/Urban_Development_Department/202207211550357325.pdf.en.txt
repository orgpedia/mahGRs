The Proposal:
Inspired by the Hon'ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, the Ministry of Earth Sciences, in collaboration with other Ministries as well as NGOs and civil society, has for the first time launched a 75-day Clean Sea, Safe Sea/ Clean Coast Safe Sea Campaign to clean 75 beaches along the 7500 km long Indian coastline. This campaign International Coastal Cleanup Day is celebrated on 5th July 2022. It will run until 17 September 2022.
This campaign is the world's first and longest running coastal clean-up campaign in which maximum number of people have participated. Through this campaign, the aim is to bring about a massive human behavioural change among the masses by spreading awareness about how the untreated waste, especially plastic, being dumped on the beaches is destroying marine life.
Accordingly, this campaign/ drive will be carried out in various coastal towns of the State.
