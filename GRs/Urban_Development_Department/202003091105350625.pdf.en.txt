The Proposal:
In the Cabinet meeting held on 4th October, 2016 at Aurangabad, it has been decided to set up a memorial and memorial in the name of Late Balasaheb Thackeray in the 7 hectare park transferred from CIDCO Aurangabad to Aurangabad Municipal Corporation.
Accordingly, a committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Guardian Minister of Aurangabad District under the Government Decision dated 17/04/2017 to set up the memorial forest and memorial.
02. Ma. The Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Guardian Minister, Aurangabad District in its meeting held on 30/01/2020 has approved the project plan and budget of the planned memorial. The committee has also approved the resolution regarding the memorial and memorial to be built in the name of late Balasaheb Thackeray.
The concept/ design prepared by the architect appointed for the said Memorial Forest and Memorial has been given technical approval by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Aurangabad on 10/02/2020. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.

Government's decision:
In the name of late Balasaheb Thackeray, Rs. 25,49,15, 611/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores, Nineteen Lakhs, Fifteen Thousand, Six Hundred and Eleven only) is being accorded administrative approval under the Technical Sanction dated 10/02/2020 by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Board, Aurangabad, under this Government Resolution subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The Aurangabad Municipal Corporation is being declared as the" functional body" for the works to be carried out in connection with the memorial forest and memorial. Also, the Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" for the actual implementation.
2) The Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation will be responsible for completing the proposed works within the stipulated time. Further, a Committee constituted under Government Resolution No. 17/04/2017 will also review the construction of the memorial and memorial.
(3) The Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation shall be responsible as the" Controlling Officer" for ensuring that there is no duplication of works in the proposed works and that such works are not proposed or approved or in progress in any other Government scheme.
(4) All permissions/ approvals required under the relevant Acts/ Rules shall be obtained from the competent authority in time before undertaking the work of the memorial forest and memorial.
5) Ensure that no trees have to be cut while finalizing the plan of the memorial forest and memorial. The committee is headed by the Aurangabad district guardian minister.
