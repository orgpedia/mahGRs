The Proposal:
The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation's Revised Services (Access to Services and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 have been amended vide Reference No. 2, here. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 28.05.2019. In this service entry rule, there is a condition of 5 years of service in the lower cadre for promotion. In the state government, generally, 3 years of experience in a lower cadre post is required to be eligible for promotion. Accordingly, the revised Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2019 of Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation have been amended to provide for 3 years experience in lower cadre post for promotion. The decision has been taken in accordance with Government Order No. 5.
Commissioner, Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation has issued a circular regarding the revision of the ratio of nomination/ direct service to 50% and promotion to 50% instead of 100% in the post of Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Corporation approved in the rules of the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation. The proposal has been submitted to the Government for approval vide letter dated 07.09.2022.
In Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, there is 1 post of' Deputy Secretary' in the cadre, provision has been made in the Service Admission Rules to fill this post directly.
