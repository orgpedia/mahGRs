No.86- Shop Centre, Sheet No. 38/ c, Plot No. 31/1 Total area 16, 574.40 sq.m. Government approval is being given for construction of shop complex on O.T. basis and handing over the same to the developer on lease for a period of 30 years subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions 1. The Upfront Premium for the purpose has been announced by the Municipal Council at Rs. 83 crore. 83 crore, the Request for Proposal (RFP), Request for Qualification (RFQ) and Concession Agreement should be made before making public announcement of the change in the project cost and corresponding changes in technical and financial capacity. After making this change, the Municipal Council should submit the revised documents to the Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration and take further action by vetting them.
2. After receiving the upfront premium amount from the project, the remaining amount of land value from the said amount before the municipal council is Rs. 36,59,04, 226/- to be paid by the Municipal Council to the Revenue Department.
3. It shall be the responsibility of the Municipal Council to make available the land under the project free from encroachments and other encumbrances by the Revenue Department.
4. It is mandatory to construct on the land under reservation as per the approved map and it will be mandatory to obtain the certificate of completion of the construction and only the Municipal Council will have the right over the mat area index remaining on the land and the mat area index to be generated in future through other means.
The quality and management of construction shall be maintained in accordance with the quality of the Public Works Building Code and the construction shall be completed within a period of 2 years.
In the contract to be entered into the project, the tenant shall lease the shop premises for 30 years, payable within 2 years after the completion of the actual construction period of the proposed project. After this period, it will be mandatory for the developer to pay property tax and other taxes on the rent of the shop that has not been paid by the tenant. Shops not leased out will be deemed to have been leased out to the developer for a long term (30 years).
The 30 years period of lease will be reckoned from the date of completion certificate of the project.
Ownership of the parking lot will remain with the Municipal Council and the developer will construct the parking lot and transfer it to the Municipal Council free of cost.
The premium amount will be required to be paid to the Municipal Council before the developer can distribute the rent available to the tenant for 30 years.
