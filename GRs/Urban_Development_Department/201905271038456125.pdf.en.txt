The Proposal:
The Director, Town Planning, Pune, has issued a notification in this regard. 05.11.2018 and A.No. As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 03.05.2019, the work of 2 Regional Plan Units namely Deputy Director, Town Planning, Regional Planning, Nanded and Deputy Director, Town Planning, Regional Planning, Kolhapur has been completed and the matter of shifting of these offices was under consideration of the Government. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
To revise the development plan of Nagpur city for the second time and to revise the development plan of Kolhapur city for the third time, the government has prepared a base map and the existing land use map (ELU) as well as the proposed land use map (PLM). For the purpose of formation of LU, the existing Regional Planning Offices at Nanded and Kolhapur are hereby approved to be transferred and transferred with their respective designations to Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Unit, Nagpur Municipal Corporation, Nagpur and Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Unit, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur.
