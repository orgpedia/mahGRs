The Proposal:
Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation was formed. The Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking was formed on 16.11.1995. It was started on 23.05.1999. At present, the transport undertaking is providing passenger services on various routes including Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation, Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, Panvel City Municipal Corporation, Ambernath Municipal Council and Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council.
2. Considering the administrative functions of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, the changes taking place in the transport service day by day, modernization and increase in the transport service, Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Corporation has decided to create a new post for the transport undertaking. As per the Government decision dated 02.11.2022, a total of 619 posts have been sanctioned by creating 68 new posts.
For this, the Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, gave the relevant information. The Service Admission Rules for the sanctioned posts in the Transport Undertaking vide letter dated 04.08.2022 have been submitted for approval to the Government.
3. Under the consideration of the Government, the matter of approving the service entry rules for the posts of various departments of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking was under consideration.
Government Resolution: Commissioner, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation In respect of Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking vide letter dated 04.08.2022, the Government is giving approval to the" Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Transport Undertaking Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2022" introduced under Section-457 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, under Sections 347, 348 and 349 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The said Rules may be called the" Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking Services (Entry into Service and Classification of Services) Rules, 2022."
(3) These rules shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
