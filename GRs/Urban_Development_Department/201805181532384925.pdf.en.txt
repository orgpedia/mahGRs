The Proposal:
Narendra Madhukar Bornare, Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted), Shri. The matter of posting him to the post of Assistant Director, Town Planning was under consideration of the Government to implement the order of promotion made under the Government decision dated 30.12.2017. The government has issued orders to this effect:
Government's decision:
Mr. N.M. Bornare, Town Planner, Divisional Commissioner's Office, Konkan Division has been posted as Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted). The promotion was granted vide order dated 30.12.2017. According to the order, to implement his promotion, he is being posted as Assistant Director, Town Planning, Ulhasnagar Municipal Corporation, G. Thane (on deputation) instead of Assistant Director, Town Planning, Divisional Commissioner's Office, Konkan Division, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai.
The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the above officers to join the place of posting.
The officers being posted under the said order are required to join immediately at the place of their posting. If they fail to appear at the place of posting or try to change the post under political pressure, the matter should be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officer. """ Further, as the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officers under the said Rules,"" the order said."
