The Proposal:
The increasing population in the jurisdiction of Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, the rapid development of urbanization, the increasing scope of development works, the development works undertaken under various schemes have led to a large increase in the number of amenity works. Considering all these aspects, the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation had submitted proposals to the government from time to time regarding the increase in posts in various cadres and the creation of new posts/ cadres.
The Commissioner, Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, had submitted a proposal to the government to create new posts in various cadres of the Municipal Corporation.
Therefore, the matter of approving the creation of additional posts in the approved figure of the Municipal Corporation by creating the necessary posts on the establishment of the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
Considering the limit of establishment cost in various cadres of Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, at present, the figure of essential (fire and health department) posts of class-1, class-2 and class-3 is being demanded by the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation under the reference letters, so the government is giving approval to create 393 new posts as shown in the table in Annexure-A, B, C attached with this decision, and in the case of the posts being created, the Commissioner, Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, is determining the pay scale as proposed and according to Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
(2) The said incremental posts in the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation are being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions: (1) The posts available under the new figure may be filled up in the prescribed manner, if within the prescribed limits of establishment cost.
(2) If more officers/ employees are currently working in the corporation than the number of posts approved earlier while approving the creation of additional posts in the figure of Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation, such officers/ employees will not be accommodated in the newly created posts. However, in the posts in the direct service quota, if promoted, the promotion can be continued without any entitlement to promotion till the time direct service candidates are available. Also, if the posts in the promotion quota are available due to the creation of additional posts, those posts can be promoted or regularized by completing the prescribed procedure.
(3) The grade pay admissible in respect of the pay scale of the officers/ employees on the establishment of the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation shall not be more than the grade pay admissible to the Government posts equivalent to the said post.
(8) The posts indicated for the future hospitals on the establishment of Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation may be approved as per the need when the hospitals will be operational. The proposal for creation of posts required for such proposed hospitals should be sent to the government for approval before the expected date of opening of such hospitals.
(5) The services of the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation on which it is possible and necessary to fill the staff by external sources, such services should be filled by external sources.
(6) The officers/ employees of the Administrative Service under the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (Non-Technical) shall not be appointed to the posts of Technical and Accounts Service or the officers/ employees of the Technical and Accounts Service to the posts of Administrative Service.
