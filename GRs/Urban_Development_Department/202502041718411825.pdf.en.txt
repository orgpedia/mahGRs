03. Deputy Secretary / Joint Secretary (NV-10) is being appointed as the "Nodal Officer and State Scheme Manger" to coordinate the implementation of the Central Government's "Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme Part-V Promotion of Industrial Growth."
4. Government Orders of the Finance Department and Central Government. Taking into consideration the guidelines of the 23.03.2021, the "Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme Part-V Promoting Industrial Growth" scheme is being declared as a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) with Deputy Secretary / Joint Secretary (NV-10) for coordination and control among the State Governments and Implementing Agencies for implementation of the PFMS system.
A Single Nodal Bank Account of the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for funds received from the Central Government under the scheme and a Zero Balance Subsidiary Account for the implementing agency is being approved to be opened in Th of Maharashtra, Mantralaya Branch.
These accounts should be transferred only for the purpose of the said scheme.
UXSNA account and Zero Balance Subsidiary Account should be linked to the PFMS system.
The funds received for each scheme from the treasury should first be deposited in the bank account of the SNA attached to the PEMS system of the concerned scheme for disbursement of the funds of the scheme to the concerned agencies in a centralized manner and thereafter to the beneficiaries selected by the concerned agencies at their level.
09. Monthly / quarterly coordination and audit of transactions in the said account by the concerned agencies.
If the SNA account is closed in the future, the balance must be adjusted and deposited immediately under the appropriate government ledger.
