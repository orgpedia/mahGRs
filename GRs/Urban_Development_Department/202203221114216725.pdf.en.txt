2) Rs. 276.60 crore is being handed over to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the Urban Local Bodies as per Annexure. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration should immediately disburse their share of funds to the respective Urban Local Bodies through the PEMS system as per the annexure.
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai shall forthwith submit to the Government, the utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the 15th Finance Commission grants received from the Central Government for the financial year 2021-2022 for drinking water (including rainwater harvesting and recycling) and solid waste management.
