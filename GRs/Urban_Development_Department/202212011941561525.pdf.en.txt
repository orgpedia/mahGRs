The Proposal:
Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urban Mission is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and raising their standards.
The proposal of Jamkhed Municipal Council's Sewerage (Phase-I) Project under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission has been sent to the Directorate of Municipal Administration under reference No. It was submitted to the State Level Accreditation Committee for approval.
Accordingly, as per the recommendation of the State Level Project Approval Committee in its meeting held on 25.11.2021, the project was accorded administrative approval under Reference No.9. In the meantime, the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority has revised its tariff list and under reference number 11, the project has been given revised technical approval by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Jeevan Authority, Nashik Division.
