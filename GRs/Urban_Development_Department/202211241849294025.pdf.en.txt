Read: Letter No. 22030/ M.V.S./ A of Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat 01.11.2022 Preamble: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (1) (f) and Section 4 (7) of the Nagpur Reformatory Act, 1936, the Board of Trustees of the Nagpur Reformatory Trust shall be headed by Hon. There is a provision for the Speaker, Maharashtra Legislative Assembly to nominate one of the members of the Legislative Assembly as an honourable member.
The Speaker, Maharashtra Legislative Assembly has nominated Shri Mohan Mate, Member of Legislative Assembly as a member on the Board of Trustees of Nagpur Sudhar Pranyas, Nagpur under the above powers.
GOVERNMENT DECISION:" The Board of Trustees of Nagpur Reforms Trust is being constituted with Shri Mohan Mate, Member of Legislative Assembly as its member for a period till the expiry of his term of membership of the Legislative Assembly or until further orders of the Government.
