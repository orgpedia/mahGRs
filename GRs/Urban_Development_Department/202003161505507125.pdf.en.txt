Government's decision:
The Diamond Jubilee of the formation of the State of Maharashtra is being celebrated on May 1, 2020, and in order to realize the concept of clean, beautiful and green Maharashtra, the Diamond Jubilee Maharashtra- Urban Sanitation Campaign has been organized in all the urban local bodies of the state from March 1, 2020 to April 30, 2020.
In view of the increasing outbreak of Corona virus in the state, the government is currently suspending the city competition organized under the government decision at Reference No. 2 on the occasion of the Diamond Jubilee of the state, as preventive measures at the level of urban local bodies are urgently needed to prevent this outbreak.
3. Even though the competition has been suspended, separate instructions are being issued by all the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) and Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats regarding measures to be taken by the institutions regarding water, sanitation and personal hygiene to prevent the spread of Corona.
