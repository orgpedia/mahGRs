Use of satellite photography technology for protection of wetlands in urban areas of the State.

the Urban Development Department

Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
The date: November 16, 2018.
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. TPS-1815/ PR No. 359/15/ NV-13 dated 06.05.2016.
The Proposal:
The High Court of Bombay vide Petition No. As per the order passed in the case No. 87/2013 Vanashakti Public Trust v. Government of India and others, the matter of issuing instructions for the use of satellite photography technology for the protection of wetlands in urban areas of the State was under consideration of the Government.
Government circular:
(a) The procedures prescribed under the Government Decision dated 6th May, 2016 should be followed for use of satellite photography for area determination, monitoring, preventive action etc. for protection of wetlands under Urban Local Bodies/ Planning Authorities/ Special Planning Authorities.
(b) As soon as encroachment or threatening activity is noticed in the wetland area on the basis of satellite imagery, notice should be given to the concerned and necessary action should be taken in this regard immediately.
A report on the action taken should be submitted to the government.
