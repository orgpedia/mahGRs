Preamble: Subsidy is distributed to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance. For the year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 1491.00 crore has been budgeted by the Finance Department. Earlier, 60% of these funds were Rs. 894.60 crore has been disbursed. Now, the remaining 40% of the fund is Rs. 596.40 crore has been requested by the Finance Department for disbursement. The department has sanctioned an expenditure of Rs. 298.20 crore.
As per the above, the grant is to be disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Administration through BEAMS system.
Government's decision:
Subsidy is given to the Municipal Councils in lieu of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance of Municipal Councils in the State. For the year 2019-20, the remaining 40% of the funds received through the distribution system for Auxiliary Grants to the Municipal Councils in the State is Rs. 596.40 crore out of which at present 80% of the limit of Rs. 298.20 crore is being disbursed to the Directorate of Municipal Administration.
2. Subsidiary Grants received through BEAMS system for Municipal Councils. As per the Government decision dated 16.06.2017, the Municipal Council Administration Directorate should directly deposit the amount in the bank account of the Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat.

The Commissioner and Director are declared as Controlling Officers and Assistant Directors as Drawing and Disbursing Officers for disbursement of Subsidiary Grants to Municipalities by the Directorate of Municipal Administration directly into the bank accounts of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats.
4. Circular to the Government, Finance Department, while disbursing the said funds. The funds should be disbursed to the respective Municipal Councils only after fulfilling the conditions as on 02.04.2018 and on submission of 75% Utilization Certificate.
The said fund should be spent under the head Demand No.F-2, 2217, Urban Development, 192, Assistance to Municipal Councils, Schemes under Five Year Plans, (00) (60) Subsidiary Grants to Municipal Councils on account of abolition of VAT and Dearness Allowance, 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (2217 8066).
6. Withdrawal and disbursement wise settlement of pending utilization certificates as per the prospectus on the website of the Accountant General should be done within one month. These certificates should be submitted to the Accountant General's office instead of submitting them to the Adhiveshan office.
7. Settlement of pending short payments should be done within one month as per the prospectus on the website of the Finance Department.
8. The Directorate of Municipal Administration should recover the excess amount from the concerned Municipal Councils at the earliest. Also, the recovery should be made under the head 911, recovery of overpayment, (00) (02) recovery of overpayment (non-plan) (22179957) 70, minus recovery.
9. The said Government Decision is in accordance with the provisions of Circular No. Earths-2018/ P. No. 69/ Earth-3, D. being issued under the authority given on 02.04.2018.
