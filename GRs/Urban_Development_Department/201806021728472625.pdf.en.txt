To make all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to ensure clean and good health and environment to all the citizens and to keep all the constitutional cities of the country clean.
And with the objective of providing household toilet facilities to the citizens of these cities, the Central Government has started implementing the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)." On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state under the decision of the government at reference number 2.
2. Panchgani, Mahabaleshwar, Vita, Umred and Malkapur (Dist. Satara) Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) were released the first instalment of funds for Urban Solid Waste Management Project. Utilization Certificate Centre regarding the expenditure of this fund
After submitting to the government, the central government has released the second installment of Rs 1,38,25, 280/- to the state.
The Government of India has approved the release of second instalment of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,38,25, 280/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Eight Lakh Twenty Five Thousand Two Hundred Eight Only) for the year 2018-19 for the above mentioned Urban Solid Waste Management Projects of Urban Local Bodies (ULBs). The expenditure shall be met from the provision made under that head during the financial year 2018-19 as shown in the table below.
