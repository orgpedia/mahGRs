The Proposal:
An ambitious program of planting 50 crore trees has been taken up in the state for which the target has been fixed by the Forest Department of the State Government to all the Urban Local Bodies of the State as per the Government Circular, Urban Development Department No.Sanikya-2018/ PR No. 221/ NV-34, dated February 16, 2019. Accordingly, some of the Urban Local Bodies have also achieved their targets. However, some local bodies are yet to meet their tree plantation targets citing non-availability of funds. In this regard, it has also been observed that the expenditure from the 14th Finance Commission Fund has not been utilized despite the fact that it was already allowed under the Government Decision dated 03.08.2015.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the ambitious programme of planting 50 crore trees in the State, the Urban Local Bodies are being advised to work out the modalities for meeting the target of tree plantation and utilization of 14th Finance Commission funds for the purpose as under:
(a) The procedure for the appropriation of the 14th Finance Commission Fund has been prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 03.08.2015 at Reference No. 1. Forestry as per paragraph (II) (i) A. No. (3) of the said Government Decision- Ex. Planting of trees and green cover has been permitted.
Therefore, it is allowed to spend from this fund on the cultivation of trees as per the need.
