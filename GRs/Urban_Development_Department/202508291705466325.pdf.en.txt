The Proposal: -
Disciplinary action is planned in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, when an offence, misconduct or misconduct is committed by a government servant while discharging his / her duties. It is, then, imperative that such disciplinary action be directed by the competent authority. Otherwise, a departmental inquiry ordered without the consent of the competent authority as well as the punishment awarded is void and the punishment awarded to the offender concerned is not carried out despite conviction.
(2) No authority subordinate to the appointing authority concerned shall have the power to impose a sentence of dismissal or removal from service under clause (1) of article 319 of the Constitution in respect of disciplinary action. In the case of other punishments, powers may be delegated to an officer secondary to the appointing officer. Accordingly, the Department of General Administration, Government Circular, Govt. denoted by 07.04.2025.
3. The Government was under consideration to declare the competent authority for disciplinary action for the officers / staff on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation as notified by the General Administration Department, the Government has decided as follows.
The Government's Decisions:
The officers / staff working on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Evaluation under the Urban Development Department are being declared as the following competent authorities for taking disciplinary action.
