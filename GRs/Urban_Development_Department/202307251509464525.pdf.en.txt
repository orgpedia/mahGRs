(3) The promotions shall be for a period of 11 months from the date of the order of promotion, whichever is earlier, until the availability of the candidates by the Commission through direct recruitment and shall automatically terminate after the period specified in the promotion order.
(4) These ad-hoc promotions shall be continued after every 11 months.
