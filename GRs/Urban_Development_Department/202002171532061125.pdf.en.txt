In order to fill up the vacancies in the cadre of Assistant Town Planner, Grade-I (Group-B/ Gazetted) on the establishment of Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has invited applications in a prescribed format. In pursuance of the advertisement dated 09.03.2018, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has issued a notification. As per the letter dated 20.09.2019, a total of 172 candidates have been selected on the basis of merit. The Bombay High Court, vide its order No. The recommendation has been made to the Government for appointment subject to the final result of 8444/2019.
Accordingly, 165 candidates have been selected in the Government Service as Assistant Town Planner in the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Grade-I (Group-B) (Gazetted) (Pay Band Rs. 9300-34800, Grade Pay Rs. 4400/- (Pay Structure as per 7th Pay Commission S-15: 41800-132300). The appointments and postings have been made in accordance with the Government Decision dated 18.01.2020. Out of 172 candidates recommended, the matter of appointment and posting of 4 candidates who have not issued orders was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions: :
Government's decision:
On the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and considering the report received from the Director, Town Planning, Maharashtra State, Pune, the Assistant Town Planner, Grade-I (Group-B) (Gazetted), Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation (Pay Band Rs. 9300-34800, Grade Pay Rs. 4400/- (Pay Structure as per 7th Pay Commission S-15: 41800-132300) to the candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. The Bombay High Court, vide its order No. 8444/2019 and Writ Petition No. Subject to the final result of 27251/2019, direct appointment is being made as a Maharashtra Public Service Commission sponsored candidate through this order. The candidates will be appointed as Assistant Town Planner, Grade-I (Group-B) (Gazetted) in the post mentioned against their name in the table below.
