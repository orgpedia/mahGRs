Government's stand:
Paragraph 03. (a) of the Government Resolution in question shall be read as follows:
As per the decision of the Government No. 1 under reference, administrative approval is being given to the water supply project of Chimur Municipal Council subject to the following conditions: a) Project Implementation Mechanism:• The project should be implemented on full deposit basis by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
The municipal council should provide a fee equal to 3% of the project cost to the Maharashtra Life Authority.
• It will be mandatory for the implementing agency to fulfill all the conditions prescribed by the State Level Project Approval Committee while approving the project. "
