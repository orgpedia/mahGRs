There are a total of seven districts in Karnataka. The districts are under the control of the central government. The Cuttack Mandal is under the Central Government but is part of the State Government. Therefore, the local people's representatives have been repeatedly demanding that the Cuttack Mandal be provided funds for various development works. At present, there is no provision to provide funds to Cuttack Mandal under the provision of Urban Development Department. The government was considering to provide funds for various development works to the said Cuttack mandals from the sanctioned provision of the Urban Development Department.
