The Proposal:
Mumbai Metro Line-7 (Andheri East) and Dahisar (East) and Mumbai Metro Line-2 Project Phase-II (DN Nagar-Mandalay) are the elevated metro rail projects to be implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA). Approved by 1 to 3. The project will be funded by the state government, central and state governments.
Mumbai Metro Line 2A, 2B and 7 will be financed by two external financial institutions, the New Development Bank (NDB) and the Asian Development Bank (ADB). The New Development Bank has approved a loan of$ 260 million for these projects.
It was given. The Project Agreement between New Development Bank, Government of India and Government of Maharashtra and Loan Agreement between New Development Bank and Government of India have been signed on 26.12.2018. Similarly, the Asian Development Bank has approved a loan of$ 926 million for these projects. It was given. The Project Agreement between ADB Bank, Government of India and Government of Maharashtra and Loan Agreement between ADB Bank and Government of India have been signed on 01.03.2019. The projects will be financed by a loan of$ 260 million from the New Development Bank and$ 926 million from the Asian Development Bank.

The loan amount received from the New Development Bank for the Mumbai Metro 2-A, 2-B, and 7 projects should be submitted to the Escrow Account of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) shall maintain accounts for the loan amounts received from the New Development Bank for the Mumbai Metro 2-A, 2-B, and 7 projects in the format prescribed by the Accountant General.
The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority shall ensure that the loan amount received from the New Development Bank for the Mumbai Metro 2-A, 2-B, and 7 projects shall be utilized for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned as per the agreement with the New Development Bank and the utilization certificate of such expenditure shall be submitted by the Municipal Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
After disbursement of the amount, the details of expenditure out of this amount should be submitted to the State Government and Central Government and should be made available to the Governments/ Government appointed agencies for audit.
