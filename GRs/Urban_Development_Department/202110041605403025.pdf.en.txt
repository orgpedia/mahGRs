The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949
It is hereby amended to provide that under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958), the stamp duty to be levied on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property respectively, if any such encumbrance is in respect of immovable property situated in the city, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent on the value of such property situated in the case of sale or encumbrance and on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that encumbrance set up in the encumbrance. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 26 Municipal Corporations in the State will result in an accumulation of Rs. 210,00,00, 000/- to the Municipal Corporations is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The total amount released to the Municipal Corporations within the limit of subsidy available on Budget Distribution System for disbursement of 2nd installment of 1% Stamp Duty for the year 2020-21 is Rs. The Government is approving the disbursement of Rs. 210,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Ten Crores only) as under:

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations.
