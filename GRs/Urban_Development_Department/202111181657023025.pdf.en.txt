The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 of the General Administration Department, instructions have been issued to create more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes and transfer their services to the higher post. Accordingly, the Government was considering the creation of a supernumerary post for Mr. Sumit Uttam Jodge in the Town Planning and Valuation Department under the Urban Development Department. A decision in this regard has been taken.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, 1 supernumerary post is being created in the establishment of the concerned office for the officer of the structure assistant cadre who is appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category but who could not submit the caste validity certificate and his service is being transferred as per the return-1.
(2) The number of officers/ staff to be transferred to the post of Superintendents is being created in the existing posts and the appointments to be made by the concerned appointing authority shall be made on a temporary basis for a period of 11 months or till the date of retirement of the concerned officers/ staff or till the final decision of the Government on the transfer of the head of the study group, whichever is earlier.
