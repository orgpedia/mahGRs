The Government has given in-principle approval for development of Ramtek Pilgrim Centre at a cost of Rs.150 crore for development of the historical monuments and precincts of Ramtek in Nagpur district. Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 21/5/2018 for works to the tune of Rs. 49.28 crore to be taken up in the first phase of the project. The government has allocated funds for the purpose. A sum of Rs 5 crore has been made available through a supplementary demand in the winter session of the Maharashtra Legislature in 2021. The Planning Department and the Finance Department have approved the release of funds. Accordingly, the matter of handing over the fund of Rs 5 crore to the Collector, Nagpur was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following is approved by the Government:
