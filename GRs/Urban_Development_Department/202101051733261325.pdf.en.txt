Government Decision: The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context. Accordingly, the project of solid waste management of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation under the said scheme has been given administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3.
2. For the Detailed Project Report of Rs. 877.7600 crore sanctioned for the Solid Waste Management of Brihanmumbai City under Swachh Bharat Mission, the second instalment of Central share of Rs. 76,80,40, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Seven Crores Eighty Lakh Forty Thousand only) has been released to the Government of India vide GOI Reference No.
8 As per the communication received from the State Government, the funds are being released under the head (2217 9752) for the year 2020-21 for the sanctioned Solid Waste Management Project of the City of Greater Mumbai.
State Mission Director, Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban) is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" as well as" Controlling Officer" for drawing the said funds. They should prepare the payment of this amount and submit it to the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai immediately and draw the said fund and get the cheque. The State Mission Director should take action regarding the disbursement of this fund to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
