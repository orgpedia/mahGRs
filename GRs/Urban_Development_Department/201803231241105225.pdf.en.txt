Preamble: With effect from 01.10.2016, Panvel Municipal Corporation has been constituted comprising the erstwhile Panvel Municipality, parts of Cidco administered areas and 29 revenue villages. Infrastructure in Cidco administered areas viz. Parks, administrative buildings, dispensaries, etc. are required to be transferred to Panvel City Municipal Corporation through CIDCO.
The infrastructure in the erstwhile Cidco administered areas is still under Cidco and so are the civic services in the area. Solid waste management, water supply, sewage disposal, cleaning, street lighting etc are provided by CIDCO.
Being a local authority, all infrastructure facilities located in the area of Panvel Municipal Corporation must be under the jurisdiction of Panvel Municipal Corporation. Panvel Municipal Corporation is also expected to provide all civic services to the citizens.
The infrastructure in the then Cidco administered areas of Panvel Municipal Corporation area is required to be identified and transferred to Panvel Municipal Corporation through Cidco in a time-bound manner. Also, the civic services in the area need to be transferred from CIDCO to Panvel Municipal Corporation.
The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following three-member committee is being appointed for the transfer of infrastructure and civic services from Cidco to Panvel Municipal Corporation in the area of Panvel Municipal Corporation but in the area of the then Cidco administered area:
Joint Managing Director- 1, CIDCO 2) Municipal Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, Navi Mumbai 3) Municipal Commissioner, Panvel Municipal Corporation, Panvel
The committee should prepare a road map for the transfer of the infrastructure in the then Cidco-administered areas in the Panvel Municipal Corporation area and submit it to the government within three months.

Also, a definite and time-bound plan should be prepared and submitted to the government for the phased transfer of civic services provided by CIDCO to the municipal corporation.
In order to avoid disruption of civic amenities, Cidco should maintain and repair all infrastructure facilities in the areas under the then Cidco administration till the transfer of the said infrastructure and civic services from Cidco to Panvel Municipal Corporation is completed.
