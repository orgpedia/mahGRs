Preamble: The Central and State Governments have embarked on an ambitious mission to provide houses to all homeless families by 2022. For this, the Central Government has approved the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in 382 cities and adjoining planning areas of the state. Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, beneficiaries in urban areas are selected on the basis of their ownership and annual income and the approved action plan is implemented through an operational mechanism. However, if an eligible beneficiary who does not own land or is an encroacher on land owned by the local civic body, has to construct his own house, he has no other option.
Accordingly, in the relevant sections of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act and the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Towns Act, in the above context, No. 2 Amendments have been made to the Act. As per this amendment, No. As per the Notification dated 03.04.2018 (with copy), guidelines have been issued as to how land can be made available to individual beneficiaries eligible for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana from among the encroachers of the land belonging to the Local Bodies as per their encroachment holding area. Apart from these guidelines, the government was considering issuing some more guidelines under this government decision. Accordingly, the following guidelines have been issued:

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The encroachments of the encroachers who are eligible for benefits under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana and who have encroached on the lands under the local civic bodies for residential purposes should be dealt with in accordance with the rules and subject to the following additional terms and conditions:
Before granting permission for redevelopment/ construction on land leased out to eligible individual beneficiaries in accordance with the Encroachment Rules, the concerned Urban Local Body shall approve the construction plan within the limits of the permissible floor area index on the encroached area, subject to the approval of the concerned authority. 03 in the Notification No. 10 Care should be taken to ensure that such leaseholder keeps the area free as required under the provisions herein.
2. In respect of such land/ encroachment. The orders passed by the courts from time to time should be examined by the concerned Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Chief Officer, Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat and necessary action should be taken in this regard.
(3) The names of both the husband and wife concerned shall be included in the charter to be issued/ allotted in accordance with such encroachment rules.
Necessary action as per the Maharashtra Regional Planning and Town Planning Act, 1966, shall be taken by the concerned Urban Local Body and necessary approvals/ permissions as per the provisions of other relevant Acts applicable to this land shall be taken by the concerned Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Principal Officer, Municipal Council, Nagar Panchayat.
5. Encroachments on land belonging to Urban Local Bodies in the area of operation of Slum Rehabilitation Authority shall not be regularised under this Government Order.
However, if it is not possible to implement the slum rehabilitation scheme on a plot required for the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana in such a jurisdiction for any unavoidable reason, the policy should be implemented there if the Slum Rehabilitation Authority in respect of such plot has given no objection to the Urban Local Body in implementing the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana on the said plot.
The Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Chief Officer, Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat concerned should take care that there is no encroachment on the land of urban local bodies.
