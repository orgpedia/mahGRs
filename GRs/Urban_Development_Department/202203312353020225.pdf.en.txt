The Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the following amendments.
The following Plastic Waste Management Cells are being set up at State level and Urban Local Bodies for effective implementation of ban on single use plastic in the Urban Local Bodies in the State as per the Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the following amendment rules of the Central Government.
State Level Plastic Waste Management Cell: Plastic Waste Management Cell is being set up at State Mission Directorate at State level for effective implementation of single use plastic ban in Urban Local Bodies in the State. This cell will function under the control of a nominated officer under the control of the State Mission Director in the Mission Directorate.
Duties of the State Level Cell: To prepare a comprehensive action plan for implementation of the Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the following amendment rules and for implementation of single use plastic ban with coordinated efforts and resources of various departments at the State level.
To assess the plastic waste generated in the State and determine the programme with regard to its collection, recycling and final disposal (minimum use, recycling and recycling).
Effective implementation of Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and its subsequent amendments and phasing out of use of Single Use Plastic (SUP) in the State.
To identify and encourage the use of alternative materials to the banned Single Use Plastics (SUPs) in accordance with the implementation of Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the following amendment rules.
Establishing a proper control system in accordance with the implementation of the Plastic Waste Management Rules 2016 and the following amendment rules.
6. Creation of a special campaign plan for plastic waste management, minimum use, recycling and reuse of plastic and to create mass awareness about plastic pollution. This includes schools, colleges, universities, NCC, NSS, youth organizations, traders/ entrepreneurs, NGOs etc.

Plastic Waste Management Cell at Urban Local Bodies level: Plastic Waste Management Cell is being set up at the local body level at the city level for effective implementation of ban on single use plastic in Urban Local Bodies in the State. The Cell will function under the control of the Deputy Commissioner in case of Municipal Corporations in Local Bodies, Head of the Department dealing with Solid Waste Management or Solid Waste Management matters and Water Supply and Sanitation Engineer in case of Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats.
Duties of Plastic Waste Management Cell at Urban Local Bodies level: 1. Effective implementation of Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and its subsequent amendments and phasing out of single use plastic (SUP) in the State.
To identify and encourage the use of alternatives to banned Single Use Plastics (SUPs) in accordance with the implementation of Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 and the amendment rules thereunder.
