The Proposal:
The first Report of the 15th Finance Commission for the year 2020-21 and the clarificatory note of the Union Government have been received. Read on to No. 2 Under the 15th Finance Commission Order dated 21.12.2021, Rs. The first installment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2021-22 has been received and it was under consideration of the Government to disburse this grant to the Urban Local Bodies in the State.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission, Govt. The first instalment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2021-2022 released under the order is Rs. 184.40 crore is being approved for distribution to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats in Non-Million Plus Cities group and Cuttack Mandal on the basis of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
2) Rs. An amount of Rs. 184.40 crore is being handed over to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration for disbursement to the Urban Local Bodies as per Annexure. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration should immediately disburse their share of funds to the respective Urban Local Bodies through the PEMS system as per the annexure.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: FFC-2021/ PR. No.92/ NO.4, 3. The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Mumbai shall forthwith submit to the Government the utilization certificate in the prescribed format of the amount of 15th Finance Commission Untied/ Basic Grants received from the Central Government for the financial year 2021-2022. While spending from this subsidy, it will be mandatory to do as per the provisions of the government decision issued by the government as well as the instructions given from time to time. The grant is being distributed as" conditional."
As per the recommendations of the 15th Finance Commission, the first instalment of Untied/ Basic Grants for the year 2021-2022 is being disbursed to Municipal Corporations, Municipalities and Nagar Panchayats as mentioned above and to Cuttack Mandal as per the expenditure incurred under Demand No. F-2, 2217 Urban Development, 05, Other Urban Development Schemes, 191, Assistance to Urban Local Bodies, Urban Development Authorities, Urban Development Boards etc., (00) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Urban Local Bodies (Compulsory) as per the recommendations of the Fifteenth Finance Commission, (2217 A314) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried).
The Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration are being declared as Controlling Officers and the concerned Assistant Commissioner (Accounts), Directorate of Municipal Administration as Drawing and Disbursing Officers for the purpose of collection and disbursement of the said funds.
6. The said Government Decision is a non-official reference of Finance Department No. 53/2021/ F.C. Cell, dated 23/12/2021 is being issued with the concurrence received.
