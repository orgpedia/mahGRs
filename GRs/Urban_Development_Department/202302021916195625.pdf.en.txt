Preamble: On the establishment of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking by contract,
A total of 59 employees, including 48 conductors and 11 conductors, were recruited in 2003 and 2004 on a temporary basis on minimum wages and wholesale remuneration.
The Transport Manager, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has requested to take a decision at the government level regarding the inclusion of drivers/ conductors working on contractual basis in Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking, on the vacant posts available as per the figure, in the regular establishment of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking.
The Transport Manager, Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation has informed that the appointment of 59 employees, including 48 conductors and 11 conductors, who are working on minimum wages/ wholesale wages, on a temporary basis, has been made by following the prescribed route (Advertisement/ Written Test/ Interview) and that these employees are holding the educational and technical qualifications and qualifications prescribed for their post.

Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Project As per the Government Decision dated 02.11.2022, necessary vacancies are available for these employees. Therefore, the matter of inclusion of these employees in the service of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The government has approved the inclusion of 59 employees, including 48 conductors and 11 drivers, who are working on minimum wages/ wholesale wages on a temporary basis on a contractual basis, in the sanctioned and vacant posts in the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation.

Regular induction of these employees on the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking is being done subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) The said employees should be included in the service of the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation Transport Undertaking from the date of this government decision.
(2) The benefits of emoluments, salaries, pensions, etc. shall be admissible to the concerned employees beyond the date of their continuance in service.
(3) No benefit or arrears of service rendered earlier shall be admissible to such employees.
4) It should be clearly mentioned that no previous benefit will be received by taking a bond from the concerned employee before being retained in the service.
5) After the inclusion of these employees, no funds will be made available by the government for their salary.
6) The condition of 35% establishment cost is being relaxed as a one-time measure while making this inclusion.
