Subject to technical approval, the approval is being granted by this Government decision as mentioned in the following statement.
Implementation of Sewerage Project to focus on creation of Sewerage Treatment Capacity on priority basis, taking into account the fund constraints for implementation of Sewerage Project.
The state government has decided to implement the policy in two phases. 4 of the Government Order dated 30th November, 2017 as well as vide Reference No. The decision was taken on December 31, 2018. According to this policy, in the first phase, the sewage generated at present should be treated on priority by creating a sewage treatment system according to the future needs. The STP capacity required to treat the entire waste water should be sanctioned in the first phase.
These include a sewage treatment plant, a sewage treatment plant, a water supply station and a sewage treatment plant. Along with this, arrangements should also be made to collect waste water in some of the different wards of the city. For the rest of the ward, the sewage from the Trunk Main should be collected in a drain and the drain should be blocked near the Sewage Treatment Plant (STP) and the sewage should be taken to the Sewage Treatment Plant at that place and treated. In the second phase, a sewerage system should be constructed and connected to the sewage treatment system created in the first phase. The state government has adopted a policy that this phase will include second-level sewers. Accordingly, while implementing the phase-wise plan, the first phase of the sewerage project of Wadi Municipal Council is being given administrative approval through this Government Resolution as mentioned in the following prospectus.
