Government circular:
The year 2025 marks the seventy-seventh golden jubilee (750) of Sant Shrestha Shri Dnyaneshwar Maharaj. The upcoming "Gokul Ashtami" date 15.08.2025 marks the 750th birth anniversary of Sant Shrestha Sri Gyanadeva.
02. Ma. In pursuance of the direction issued by the Chief Minister dated 28.07.2025, Sant Shrestha
Dated 15.08.2025 to mark the seventy-seventh golden jubilee (750) of Shri Dnyaneshwar Maharaj.
Municipal Corporations / Municipalities / Municipal Councils in every town and city of the state from 5.00 pm
The festival should be celebrated by taking out a procession from the palanquin of the statue / idol of Sant Shrestha Shri Dnyaneshwar Maharaj at 7: 00.
All the above mentioned instructions should be effectively implemented by all the Urban Local Bodies and taken care of properly.
