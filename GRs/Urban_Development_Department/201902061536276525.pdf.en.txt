The Proposal:
Mr. Dnyaneshwar Uttam Rathod, Fireman, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation
Due to family reasons, I have requested to transfer my services from Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to Parbhani City Municipal Corporation. Accordingly, the Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation has approved the General Body Resolution No. 88 dated 14.08.2014 and the General Body Meeting of Parbhani City Municipal Corporation has also approved the Resolution No. Approval has been given for transfer of services of Shri Rathod with effect from 12.02.2016.
(2) As the rules for the entry into service of Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation and Parbhani City Municipal Corporation have not yet been finalized, the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Service (Service Recruitment and
Rule 6 of the Classification of Services Rules, 2008 specifies the following provisions for transfer from one Municipal Corporation to another.
" Notwithstanding anything contained elsewhere in this rule, if an employee of another corporation submits an application for transfer to a post in a corporation, the transfer may be made subject to the following conditions, in accordance with the provisions of the Act and on such terms and conditions as may be agreed upon by the appointing authorities of both the corporations with mutual consent:

(a) There shall be no transfer of posts. Appointments should be made only to the posts reserved for appointment by nomination.
(b) On such appointment, the seniority of the incumbent shall be zero seniority in the cadre of the post as on the date of appointment.
(c) The transfer of officers of Category-I and Category-II from one Municipal Corporation to another shall be decided by mutual consent of both the Municipal Corporations on definite terms and conditions with the approval of the Government. "
In view of the mutual correspondence and subsequent information regarding the transfer of Shri Rathod, both the Municipal Corporations are positive about the transfer/ transfer of Shri Dnyaneshwar Rathod, Fireman. The Parbhani City Municipal Corporation has mutually fulfilled the necessary provisions of the Government Resolution dated 3.6.2011 issued in accordance with the transfer policy of the Government. Also, Mr. Dnyaneshwar Rathod is a Class-3 employee of the Fireman cadre and the expenditure of his salary will be borne by the concerned Municipal Corporation.
In the above background, the matter of transferring the services of Mr. Dnyaneshwar Uttam Rathod, Fireman on the establishment of Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to Parbhani City Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Mr. Dnyaneshwar Uttam Rathod, Fireman, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation
Subject to the terms and conditions of the Classification of Services) Rules, 2008, the transfer of Nanded-Waghala City from the Municipal Corporation to the Parbhani City Municipal Corporation is being approved by the Government.
2. Commissioner, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to relieve Mr. Dnyaneshwar Uttam Rathod, Fireman, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation to join Parbhani City Municipal Corporation immediately after verifying their service matters. Also, the Commissioner, Parbhani City Municipal Corporation should appoint Mr. Dnyaneshwar Uttam Rathod as Fireman.
