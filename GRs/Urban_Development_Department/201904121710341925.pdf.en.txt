Government circular:
Section 107 (A) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 lays down the provision for audit by the Local Fund Audit. Section 102 to 104 of the Maharashtra Nagar Parishad Nagar Panchayat Industrial City Act, 1965, provides for the publication and audit of the accounts of the Municipal Council. Section 104 of the said Act makes the provisions of the Bombay Local Fund Audit Act, 1930 applicable to all Municipal Councils. As per the provisions of the said Act, it is mandatory to submit their accounts to the Local Fund Audit every year for audit. However, it has been observed that various Urban Local Bodies are not getting their accounts audited by the Local Fund Audit within the time limit.
2.In this regard, following instructions are being issued in accordance with the recommendation made by the Public Accounts Committee of the Legislature.
The provisions of the Bombay Local Fund Audit Act, 1930 have been made applicable to the audit of all Municipal Councils as per the provisions of Section 104 of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils Municipal Corporations Industrial Towns Act, 1965.
Further, as per the provisions of the Bombay Local Fund Audit Act, 1930, it is mandatory for all Urban Local Bodies in the State to submit their accounts every year to the Director, Local Fund Audit and get these accounts audited.
(3) All Urban Local Bodies in the State shall submit to the Local Fund Auditor the accounts of all the years in which the accounts have not been audited by the Local Fund Auditor after reviewing them at their level in this regard. Further, subject to the provisions of the aforesaid Act, it shall be ensured that the accounts for each year are submitted regularly to the Local Fund Audit for audit within the prescribed time limit.

The Commissioner, Municipal Corporation/ Principal, Municipal Council/ Nagar Panchayat concerned shall be fully responsible for submitting the accounts of the concerned Urban Local Bodies to the Local Fund Audit for audit on a regular basis within the stipulated time as per the provisions of the Act. They should ensure that the work is completed within the stipulated time.
Further, the Director Local Fund Audit shall take action for regular audit of the accounts of all Urban Local Bodies in the State as per the provisions of the Act.
