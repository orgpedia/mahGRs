It is necessary to promote and propagate the Swachh Bharat Mission through technology-based campaigns. Considering all these aspects, the government was considering to make it mandatory for all the urban local bodies in the state to use ICT based systems for effective management of solid waste management and urban sanitation works.
Government's decision
As mentioned in the Preamble, the Government has decided to make the use of ICT based systems mandatory for all the Urban Local Bodies in the State for effective management of solid waste management and urban sanitation works.

It has been observed that in some of the local bodies in the State, the collection and management of solid waste is done by a single contractor or agency. Therefore, a Government of India Undertaking, Indian Telephone Industries Limited, is being appointed by the Central Government to develop ICT-based technology for solar control through a tri-agency of solid waste management by the State Government and to implement this system in all urban local bodies in the State.
Accordingly, the ICT based technology based services provided by Indian Telephone Industries Limited (ITI Ltd- A Government of India Undertaking) and their rates have been made available and are given in the table below.
