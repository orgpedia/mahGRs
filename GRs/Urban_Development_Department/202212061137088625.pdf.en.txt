Considering their previous long period of daily service and their age at the time of retirement, as they hold the educational qualification required for the post of cadre, the resolution passed to accommodate the vacancies in the class-4 cadre sanctioned on the municipal establishment as a one-time special case in the regular service of the corporation. 115, p. 21.2.2022 in the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
As per the proposal of the Commissioner, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, it is being suspended in the first instance for disaffiliation under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
In case of suspension of this resolution, if the concerned have to make a representation under section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, it will be necessary to make a representation to the government within 30 days from the date of the suspension order. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further action will be taken as per Section 451 (3) of the Act.
