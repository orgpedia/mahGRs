The Proposal:
The Central Government has enacted the Equal Opportunities, Protection and Equal Participation of Persons with Disabilities Act, 1995. It came into effect from 01.01.1996. As per Section 40 of the said Act, all the Municipal Corporations and Municipalities of the State shall ensure that at least 3% of their budget is reserved for persons with disabilities under the poverty alleviation programme, as per the provisions of Sections 42 and 43 of the said Act, to provide assistive devices for persons with disabilities, to plan and implement schemes for housing for them, to set up business/ recreation centres/ special schools and research centres and to provide space for entrepreneurship. The instructions have been issued vide circular dated 30.10.2010
Similarly, 3% of the funds reserved for the disabled should be spent for what purpose. Detailed guidelines have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 28.10.2015. In order to ensure its strict implementation, instructions have also been given from time to time not to reallocate the funds, spend them in that year, etc.
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. It has come into effect from 27.12.2016. Section 37 of the Act provides as follows:
"The appropriate Government and the local authorities shall, by notification, make schemes in favour of persons with benchmark disabilities, to provide,

(a) five per cent. reservation in allotment of agricultural land and housing in all relevant schemes and development programmes, with appropriate priority to women with benchmark disabilities, (b) five per cent. reservation in all poverty alleviation and various developmental schemes with priority to women with benchmark disabilities, (c) five per cent. reservation in all allotment of land on concessional rate, where such land is to be used for the purpose of promoting housing, shelter, setting up of occupation, business, enterprise, recreation centers and production centers.
As provided in the said new Act, the matter of reserving 5% funds for the disabled under the poverty alleviation programme was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 37 (b) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, at least 5% of the funds under the poverty alleviation scheme are being reserved for persons with disabilities in the municipal corporations/ municipalities of the state.
2. All Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities shall take care to take action as per the instructions issued earlier under the relevant Government Decisions/ Circulars regarding the spending of funds reserved for the disabled.
