Introduction: The Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana, under the National Urban Livelihoods Mission, includes support to urban street vendors. In this sub-segment, emphasis has been laid on improving the quality of livelihood of urban street vendors by developing their skills based on market needs.
Accordingly, the objective of the sub-group is to conduct street vendor survey, issue identity cards to them, develop city street vendor blueprint, develop necessary infrastructure for street vendors sector, impart skill development training to them and their economic development.
2. Guidelines for the component on Assistance to Urban Street Vendors under Parashrut Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana, National Urban Livelihoods Mission, Government of India have suggested setting up of State Level Approval Committees to approve the proposals received from Urban Local Bodies. Accordingly, A State Level Accreditation Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (NV-2) is required to be constituted.
Government's decision:
In view of the facts stated in the Preamble, it is hereby approved to constitute a State Level Approval Committee to approve the proposals received from the Urban Local Bodies under the sub-section of Assistance to Urban Street Vendors under the National Urban Livelihood Mission as follows:
