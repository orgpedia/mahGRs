The Proposal:
Mumbai Metro 2A (Dahisar-D.N.Nagar), 2B (D.N.Nagar) and 3B (D.N.Nagar-Dahisar-Dahisar) are the metro stations in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR). The metro rail projects are N. Nagar to Mandalay and Metro 7 (Andheri East-Dahisar East). The Asian Development Bank and the New Development Bank have provided financial assistance for the project. A proposal was under consideration of the government to open a new account head for disbursing the loan amount to the state's Consolidated Fund through the state budget and depositing the loan amount and the interest amount from the MMRDA to the state's Consolidated Fund. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves opening of new head of accounts for the purpose of disbursement of loans taken from Asian Development Bank and New Development Bank to MMRDA for the Mumbai Metro 2A, 2B and 7 projects being implemented by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority and recovery of the loan amount and interest thereon from MMRDA as under:
