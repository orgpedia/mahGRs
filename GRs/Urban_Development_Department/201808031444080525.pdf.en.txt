Government's decision:
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state under the decision of the government at reference number 2. Under the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)," the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation has been given administrative approval for the Urban Solid Waste Management Project.
2. For solid waste management under Swachh Bharat Mission, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 As per the order dated 12.04.2018, out of the admissible share of Rs. 32.1282 crore, a sum of Rs. 16,06,41, 103/- (Rupees Sixteen Crores Six Lakhs Forty One Thousand One Hundred and Three only) has been released to the State as first instalment.
The Government of India has approved the release of first installment of Rs. 16,06,41, 103/- (Rupees Sixteen Crore Six Lakhs Forty One Thousand One Hundred and Three only) to Aurangabad Municipal Corporation for the year 2018-19 for its Urban Solid Waste Management Projects under Swachh Bharat Mission.
