Preamble: Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) is amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958), when any such encumbrance is in respect of immovable property situated in the city, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent on the value of the property so situated in respect of the sale or encumbrance of the encumbrance and on the amount secured by the encumbrance of the encumbrance of the encumbrance of the encumbrance. Accordingly, out of the 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to Nagpur Municipal Corporation for the financial year 2022-23, Rs. 50.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government to be disbursed to the concerned Municipal Corporation.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To disburse the 1% stamp duty to Nagpur Municipal Corporation for the year 2022-23, within the limit of subsidy available on the budget distribution system, the total amount to the Municipal Corporation will be Rs. Government approves disbursement of Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty Lakh only) as follows: Grants to be disbursed to Nagpur Municipal Corporation for the year 2022-23 at 1% Stamp Duty

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations. The Collector shall draw the said grant without any deduction from the amount due to the Municipalities (without intimation to the Government) and disburse the same to the respective Municipal Corporations within 10 days.
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge as well as the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be kept separately by the concerned District Collector and the accounts in this regard and all related accounts shall be maintained separately.
