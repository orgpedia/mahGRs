The Proposal:
As per the powers vested in the State Government under the provisions of Section 46-A of the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974, to determine the compensation for acquisition of land under the said Act, it is necessary to determine the policy of the Revenue and Forest Department, dated 12th May, 2015, regarding taking of land in the private sector by direct purchase through private negotiation with the consent of the land holder and the Government Order dated 29th May, 2018, regarding the sanction of the compensation for acquisition of land in accordance with Section 19B of the Maharashtra Highways Act. Accordingly, the matter of determining the policy and procedure for determining the compensation for land acquisition under the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 provides for the following policies and procedures for determining the compensation for land acquisition:
(1) In the proposals for acquisition of land under the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974, and in the proposals to be filed hereinafter, the State shall acquire land in accordance with sub-section (2) of section 35 for land in the municipal area and sub-section (2) of section 42 for land in the rural area.

Compensation will be determined by an agreement between the government and the person receiving the compensation.
(2) The amount of compensation to be determined in accordance with the agreement between the State Government and the person receiving the compensation under sub-section (2) of section 35 and sub-section (2) of section 42 of the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 shall be determined first after taking into account all the aspects relating to the said land, taking into account the compensation to be paid for the lands concerned under the provisions of sections 26 to 30 and Schedule I of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013. After this, 25% of the total amount of remuneration to be considered should be increased.
(3) Where an agreement has not been entered into in accordance with sub-section (2) of section 35 and sub-section (2) of section 42 of the Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974, the amount of compensation to be determined shall be arrived at after taking into account all the factors relating to the land in question and considering the compensation payable for the land in accordance with the provisions of sections 26 to 30 and Schedule I of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013.
(4) In order to determine the amount of compensation to be paid in accordance with the above sequence (2) and (3), the Government may, subject to the procedure prescribed, approve the decision of the" District Level Committee for Determination of Remuneration" established under the Chairmanship of the Collector under the Revenue and Forest Department, No. SRIKKHA- 03/2015/ PR No. 34/ A-2, dated 12.05.2015.
(5) An agreement for the acquisition of land made by the State Government under section 35 of the Bombay Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974, between the State Government and the person to be compensated shall be concluded by the competent authority appointed in accordance with section 40. Further, as per Section 42, the agreement for acquisition of land or settlement of acquisition to be entered into by the State Government between the State Government and the person receiving compensation shall be executed by an authorised officer not below the rank of Deputy Collector authorised by the Collector or the Collector.
