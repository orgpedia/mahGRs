Preamble: The model code of Latur City Municipal Corporation was approved on 28.5.2013. In the terms and conditions of approval of the said figure, A. No. 6 includes the condition that the posts which will be available to be filled up by the new figure may be filled up if the establishment expenses are within the prescribed limits. Commissioner, Latur City Municipal Corporation submitted a proposal under the letter dated 7.12.2021 and dated 20.12.2021 to relax the condition of establishment cost which is currently 59.83 percent, to fill a total of 81 posts of various designations directly and 3 posts by deputation, as it is necessary to provide essential services in the corporation.
Since it is necessary to provide essential services in Latur City Municipal Corporation, the matter of relaxing the condition of establishment expenses to fill a total of 81 posts of various designations with direct service and 3 posts with deputation was under the consideration of the government.
A total of 51 vacancies in essential services are required to be filled in order to streamline the functioning of the water supply department, health department and fire department in Latur Municipal Corporation. Also, to increase the income of the corporation by collecting tax, 10 vacancies in the cadre of Tax Superintendent, Assistant Tax Superintendent and Tax Inspector are required to be filled, excluding the post of Script Typist in the Tax Department.
