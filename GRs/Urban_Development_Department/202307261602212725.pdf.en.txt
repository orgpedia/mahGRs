Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, dated 28.04.2015 Preamble: In order to provide transparent, efficient and timely service to the eligible persons of the state and with a view to providing prompt and quality service to the people in view of the growing expectations from the local administration, the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was passed. It has been made applicable to the State of Maharashtra on 28.04.2015. As mentioned in Section 3 of the Ordinance, every public authority shall, within a period of three months from the date of promulgation of the Ordinance and from time to time thereafter, notify the public servants, designated officers, first and second Appellate Authorities and the time limits prescribed by it under the Ordinance. Accordingly, the government was considering to notify the time limit, designated officers, first and second appellate authorities for the services of Aurangabad Metropolitan Region Development Authority. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The Government hereby approve the notification of Schedule-A services in the Aurangabad Metropolitan Region Development Authority under the provisions of Section 3 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015. The details of the period of public service, office of service notifying, designated officer, first appellate officer, and second appellate officer for the services provided by the Aurangabad Metropolitan Region Development Authority under the Urban Development Department are mentioned in Schedule-A.
Accordingly, the Aurangabad Metropolitan Region Development Authority should take action to notify this service in the government gazette.
(2) The Designated Officer, First and Second Appellate Authorities responsible for providing services mentioned in Schedule-A shall take action to provide services to the citizens within the stipulated time.
