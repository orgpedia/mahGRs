Introduction: The State Government has sanctioned Rs.5 crore for Mumbai Metro Rail Line-5 (Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan). The construction of the fully elevated metro project worth Rs 8,417 crore has been approved by the MMRDA on December 21, 2017. As per the original approved DPR of the project, the elevated line is proposed from Kapurbawdi in Thane to Rajiv Gandhi Chowk in Bhiwandi city via Kalyan Road from Rajanoli to Durgadi and further from Kalyan city to Kalyan APMC via Kalyan-Shill Road.
As there are no major issues in Phase-I (Thane to Bhiwandi) of the project, the construction work on this stretch has been taken up and 79% physical progress has been achieved so far. However, due to large scale rehabilitation and resettlement related problems in Phase-II (Bhiwandi to Kalyan) corridor from Rajiv Gandhi Chowk to Saibaba Mandir, Temghar in Bhiwandi town (about 3 km length), the construction work of Phase-II is yet to be taken up.
Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority In the 152 nd meeting held on 22/02/2022, it has been decided to convert the originally planned elevated metro line between Dhamankar Naka to Temghar in Metro Line-5 project into an underground metro line. Accordingly, the Authority submitted the change for the in-principle approval of the State Government and the matter of granting the approval was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Mumbai Metro Line-5 (Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan) project was earlier approved by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 21st December, 2017 and the Authority has decided to construct an underground line between Dhamankar Naka to Temghar in Phase-II (Bhiwandi to Kalyan) after taking into consideration various technical and financial aspects. In view of the decision taken in the 152 nd meeting held on 22/02/2022, the Government is giving in-principle approval for the conversion of elevated line from Dhamankar Naka to Temghar in the Bhiwandi to Kalyan section of Mumbai Metro Line-5 (Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan) project into underground line on the following terms and conditions.

The State Government will not have any additional liability/ responsibility for the Mumbai Metro Line-5 (Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan) project beyond the State Government share sanctioned vide Government Resolution dated December 21, 2017.
The authority should prepare a detailed project report on the proposed underground line and submit it to the government for approval.
The approval of the Executive Committee of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority should be obtained before undertaking the work from Bhiwandi to Kalyan phase of the project.
The decision was taken by the Planning Department vide Informal Reference No. 92/1447, dt. 09/12/2022 and Finance Department Reference No. 389/ Expenditure-3, D. Feedback under 23/12/2022 is being issued accordingly.
