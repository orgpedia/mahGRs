the prelude
The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has approved the proposal for obtaining Government approval for the appointment of Mr. Dadasaheb Chabukaswar, Assistant Commissioner as" Deputy Commissioner" on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation under Section-45 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. submitted to the Government vide letter dated 16.03.2020.
2. Reference to the figure of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 21.08.2017. Accordingly, 10 posts of Deputy Commissioners have been sanctioned on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation.
3. In pursuance of the recommendation made by the Scheduled Castes Welfare Committee regarding the promotion of Shri Dadasaheb Chabukaswar to the post of" Deputy Commissioner," the Promotion Committee recommended his promotion. The general body meeting of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation passed a resolution in this regard. 1123, d. Approved on 8.02.2010. Accordingly, the matter of ratification of the appointment made by promotion of Shri Dadasaheb Chabukaswar to the post of" Deputy Commissioner" under the provisions of Section 45 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act was under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
The general body meeting of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation passed resolution No. 1123, d. In view of the approval given under 08.02.2010, the appointment of Mr. Dadasaheb Chabukaswar as" Deputy Commissioner" of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation by way of promotion is being approved by the Government under Section-45 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
