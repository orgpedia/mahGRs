The details of changes in the name, pay scale and grade pay of certain posts have been indicated in Form-B-1 and in Form-B-2, which is being approved by the Government.

6. In the establishment of Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, the Government has approved the figure of 6,678 posts as mentioned in Form-" A," excluding 494 posts to be lapsed out of the total 7172 posts, including 6,415 posts sanctioned by the Government and 757 newly created posts.
The pattern of posts of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) Care should be taken to fill up the posts available as per the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation diagram keeping in view the prescribed percentage of establishment cost (35%) of the Municipal Corporation as required.
(2) No funds/ grants of any kind shall be made available to the Municipal Corporation by the Government for the posts/ increments in the said figure.
(3) Officers/ employees of the Administrative Service under the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation shall not be appointed to the posts of Technical and Accounts Service or the officers/ employees of the Technical and Accounts Service to the posts of Administrative Service.
(4) The services which are possible and necessary to be done by the external sources of the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation should be done by external sources.
