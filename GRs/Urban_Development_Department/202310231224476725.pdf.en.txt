The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 and the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, provision has been made to increase the stamp duty chargeable under the Stamp Duty Act by one per cent on the sale, donation and mortgage of immovable property in the municipal area in connection with the ambitious urban transport projects being implemented in the state.
The Government was contemplating to release funds from the amount collected towards additional stamp duty surcharge of 1% to the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation for implementation of the ambitious urban transport project in the year 2023-24. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited (MMRCL) has been sanctioned additional stamp duty surcharge of 1% from the approved budgetary provision for the year 2023-24. (MRCL). By Government Decision dated 18th September, 2023, Rs. 181.05 crore has been disbursed. Now as shown in the following statement-A, Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. (MMRL) to Rs. The government has sanctioned Rs. 150 crore for the purpose.
