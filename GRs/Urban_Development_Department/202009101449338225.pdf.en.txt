The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 and the Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1988, a provision has been made to increase the stamp duty chargeable under the Stamp Duty Act on sale, donation and mortgage of immovable property in the area of the corporation where the ambitious urban transport project is being implemented, by levying a surcharge at the rate of one per cent and providing a supplementary grant equivalent to the amount collected against the surcharge to the agency/ project implementing agency undertaking such project. The date of February 8, 2019 has been specified as the date on or after which the additional stamp duty shall be levied under this provision.

Various metro rail projects are being implemented by Project Implementing Agencies such as MahaMetro, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA), which have been declared as Aspirational Urban Transport Projects for the purposes of the provisions of the Mumbai Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 and the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949. It was under the consideration of the Government to formulate a policy for disbursing the stamp duty surcharge amount of one per cent collected as per the provisions of the Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 and the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 to the ambitious urban transport projects of Mumbai, Nagpur and Pune Metro Rail being implemented under the Urban Development Department. GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MRD-3319/ PR No. 61/ NV-7 GOVERNMENT DECISION:
As per the provisions of the Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888 and the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, the modalities for disbursement of the amount collected as surcharge on stamp duty under the Stamp Duty Act on sale, donation and mortgage of immovable property in the Municipal Corporation area in respect of the ambitious urban transport projects of Mumbai, Nagpur and Pune Metro Rail being implemented under the Urban Development Department to the concerned Metro Project Implementing Agency shall be as follows:
1. Recovery of surcharge on stamp duty of 1% by Registrar General and Controller of Stamps. Starting from February 8, 2019. The amount so recovered shall be credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State under the heads of accounts to be taken for the purpose.
(2) One per cent of the amount to be deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State as surcharge.
