Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, under reference No. The Jalgaon City Water Supply Project has been approved under the Government Decision No. 2. However, considering the increase in the cost of the project due to the increase in the price of DI (K-7) pipe, the matter of granting revised administrative approval to the project was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In view of the increase in the price of DI (K-7) pipe included in the water supply project of Jalgaon city in the approved plan for the financial year 2015-16 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, in accordance with the approval received from the State Level High Power Committee as well as the Government, in pursuance of the project No. Article 2 of the Decision No. The following price revision is being done in the Jalgaon City Water Supply Project in 2 cities.
