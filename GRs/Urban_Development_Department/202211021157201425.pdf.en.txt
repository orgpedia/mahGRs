4.4 The State Government vide No. It shall be incumbent upon the concerned executing agency to complete the tendering process and execution of the project within the prescribed time frame as per the procedures prescribed under Sections 2 and 3.
4.5 The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will be required to publish the model tender booklet issued for the projects under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
4.6 The implementing agency shall ensure that the work orders for the said project are issued within 45 days from the date of order of administrative sanction.
4.7 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the mission city to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.
4.8. Mode of disbursement: As per the guidelines of Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission, the Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%). Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.
4.9 While setting up self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on the operational outcomes. Also, the
Funds will not be allocated to the project if the functional outcome is not met. The Chief Officer, Mahabaleshwar Municipal Council will be fully responsible for fulfilling these requirements.
4.11 The first instalment of funds for the project from the Central and State Governments will be disbursed to Mahabaleshwar Municipal Council after issuing the work order.

4.12 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.

4.14 The overall responsibility of completing the project within the stipulated time will be shared by the concerned implementing agency, Project Management Consultant and Project Management Development and Management Consultant.
4.15 Once the project work is started, the concerned executing agency will be responsible to make the payment of the work to the concerned contractor within 15 days within the limit of available funds after the project manager consultant certifies the quality of work and payment.
4.16 As per the guidelines under AMRUT 2.0 Mission, under reference No. A project manager should be appointed for the project. As mentioned in it, it will be mandatory for the concerned urban local body to make a decision on the payment of the project manager fee.
4.17 All No Objection Certificates required for the completion of the project and the process of making available the site should be done before giving the project work order.
4.18 The responsibility of ensuring that there is no duplication between the project plan and the plan plan plan approved by the Mahabaleshwar Municipal Council under any other scheme in the past will be of the concerned urban local body, the management consultant of the project and the Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
All the guidelines issued by the Central Government on the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Engineering Project will be applicable to the Lake Rejuvenation Project of Mahabaleshwar Municipal Council.
