The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations. The Collector should withdraw this grant from the treasury and immediately distribute it to the concerned Municipal Corporations.
The expenditure to be incurred under the head" Demand No.F-4,3604, Payment of Compensation and Stamped Amounts to Local Bodies and Panchayat Raj Institutions, 102, Stamp Duty (01) Stamp Duty due to abolition of Tax (01) Stamp Duty due to restoration of Tax in lieu of Local Body Tax (01) 1% Surcharge on Stamp Duty in the Municipal Corporation Area with a view to provide for Municipal Corporation (36040924) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary)" shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2018-2019.
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be separately accounted for by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
