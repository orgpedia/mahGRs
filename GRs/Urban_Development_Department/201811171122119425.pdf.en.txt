The Proposal:
The Government was considering filling up the vacant post of Assistant Director, Town Planning Friendship Cell, Maharashtra State Small Industries Development Corporation, Mumbai on deputation from the Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted) Cadre Officers in the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation. The Government have issued the following orders regarding the deputation of the Assistant Director of Town Planning to this post:
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005 and in accordance with the provisions of Annexure-III, Rule 40 (1) (2) (3) (4) and Annexure-II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment to Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, the officers of the cadre of Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted) are being transferred and posted on deputation as indicated in the following statement for administrative reasons.

Since the above officers are being appointed on deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of permissible posting and joint transfer grant should be sanctioned to them as per rules.
If the officers transferred under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers.
It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, State of Maharashtra, Pune, shall entrust the additional charge of the post falling vacant in pursuance of the said deputation to an officer of the same or an equivalent rank in the nearest office or to a senior officer in the same office in the cadre which is junior to the cadre of the officer to be transferred. However, it should be ensured that in no case the transferring officer is assigned additional charge of the post falling vacant due to his transfer.
