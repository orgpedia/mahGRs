The Proposal:
Smart City Mission is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme. According to the guidelines in No. 2 orders have been issued.
For the implementation of the Smart City Mission, the State Budget provides for provision of funds at the level of State Mission Director, Smart City Mission for implementation of the scheme, financial provisions for implementation of Smart City projects as well as administrative and office expenses and disbursement of grants on Central and State accounts, in addition to the existing head of accounts. 3 The new head of accounts is proposed to be opened in accordance with the revised instructions regarding Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement and utilization of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes issued vide Government Circular No.
The new head of accounts for each smart city is proposed to replace the existing head of accounts for administrative and office expenditure of the Centre and the States for the existing head of smart city projects.
