GOVERNMENT DECISION: The administrative approval granted to the sewerage project of Talegaon-Dabhade (Dist. Pune) under the Government Decision dated 28th March, 2018 is being cancelled in view of improvement in sewerage technology.
Accordingly, the revised project of Talegaon-Dabhade Municipal Council Sewerage Project (Phase-I) submitted under Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Mahabhiyan by superseding the Government Decision at Reference No.7 is being accorded administrative approval by this Government Decision subject to the conditions and provisions of the relevant Government Decision and the technical approval granted by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
The State Government has decided to implement the Sewerage Project in two phases, considering the limit of funds for the implementation of the Sewerage Project and to focus on the creation of sewage treatment capacity on priority. 4), as well as reference No. The decision was taken on December 31, 2018. According to this policy, in the first phase, the sewage generated at present should be treated on priority by creating a sewage treatment system according to the future needs. The STP capacity required to treat the entire waste water should be sanctioned in the first phase.
These include a sewage treatment plant, a sewage treatment plant, a water supply station and a sewage treatment plant. Along with this, arrangements should also be made to collect waste water in some of the different wards of the city. For the rest of the wards, the waste water from the Trunk Main should be collected in a drain and such a drain should be blocked near the Sewerage Treatment Plant (STP) and the waste water should be taken to the Sewerage Treatment Plant at that place. In the second phase, a sewerage system should be constructed and connected to the sewage treatment system created in the first phase. This phase will include the second phase. Also, the state government has adopted a policy that innovative technology should also be used. Accordingly, the entire sewerage project of Talegaon-Dabhade city is being given in-principle approval in a phase-wise manner. As per the above policy, the first phase of the scheme is being given administrative approval through this Government Resolution as mentioned in the Statement below.
