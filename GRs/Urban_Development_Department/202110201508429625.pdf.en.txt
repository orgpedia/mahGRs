Introduction: In order to make the administration of the state more dynamic and people-oriented, the e-governance policy of the state has been decided under the Government Decision No. 4 in the context. Computerization of Government departments/ offices. A comprehensive action plan has been prescribed vide Government Resolution dated 29.10.2001. Further, under the said Government decision, it has been directed to constitute a Project Implementation Committee under each administrative division to implement the computerization projects under the offices under the administrative division. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a Project Implementation Committee under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Navi-1) to implement the e-governance project under the Urban Development Department.
Government's decision:
The General Administration Department vide the above Reference No. Considering the guidelines/ directions given under the Government Decision mentioned herein 1-4, the following Project Implementation Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Navi-I) to implement the e-Governance project under the Urban Development Department.
