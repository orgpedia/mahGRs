Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) is being implemented in the state on the lines of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan of the Central Government as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 1. October 2, 2019 marks the 150th birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi. The Prime Minister in his address to the nation from the ramparts of the Red Fort on 15th August, 2019 and in his Mann Ki Baat address on 25th August, 2019 announced the ban on single use plastic (SUP) across the country from 2nd October, 2019. Accordingly, in order to achieve this desired goal with the participation of all sections of the society, the Government has notified the implementation of Swachhata Hi Seva campaign across the country from 11th September, 2019 to 27th October, 2019 as per the following schedule:
Accordingly, instructions have been given to effectively implement the" Swachhata Hi Seva" campaign in the state.
Also, as per the notification of the Department of Environment dated 30.06.2018 at Reference No. 2, plastic ban has been implemented in the State.
The main objective of the" Swachhata Hi Seva" campaign in phase 2 is to collect the plastic that all the citizens of the city have and to take a pledge that these citizens will not use plastic themselves and in phase 3, the plastic collected will be recycled/ disposed of scientifically, making Maharashtra plastic free.
Accordingly, Phase 2 will be implemented across the State from 1st October, 2019 to 3rd October, 2019. As a part of this, on Tuesday, 1st October, 2019 at 10.30 am, Ma. Chief Secretary of Maharashtra
