Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government services for persons with disabilities. The central government has given. List of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021, may be seen in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in and in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification, the list of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on the website. Published on 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In accordance with the directions of the said Government decision, it was under the consideration of the Government to ensure the posts in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D on the establishment of the Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation and to ensure the posts for the newly disabled category by reviewing the list of seats in the Government service ensured for the disabled after a period not exceeding 3 years.
