The Proposal:
The Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is being implemented in the State since 2015-16 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Under AMRUT Mission, Jalgaon City Sewerage Project, which was included in the plan for the financial year 2017-18, was given administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in the context. Subsequently, the matter of revising the administrative approval of Jalgaon City Sewerage Project was under consideration of the Government in view of the change in the cost of the components of the project.
Government's decision:
The administrative approval granted under the Government Resolution dated 26th March, 2018 to the Jalgaon City Sewerage Project included in the State Annual Action Plan approved by the Central Government during the financial year 2017-17 under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission is subject to the other terms and conditions of the relevant Government Resolution as per the following paragraph No. 2 and 3 are being revised as mentioned in the prospectus.
