Government's decision:
The Government has decided to include the following Engineering Colleges/ Technicians in the list of Engineering Colleges and Government Technicians taken on the Nominee List for technical audit by the Tertiary System of Development Works by Urban Local Bodies.
In pursuance of the Government decision dated 28th March, 2018, the name of Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), Nagpur is being included in the list of names prescribed in Form A accompanying the Government decision for technical audit of the project works under Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarothana Mahaabhiyan.
Further, the name of the institution VNIT is also included in Form-A of the said Government Resolution. Therefore, as per Para 5 (II) of the Government Decision dated 28th March, 2018, while ordering the second stage technical audit from the level of Government/ Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, where the first stage audit is done by VNIT, the second stage audit should be done by one of the remaining two agencies excluding the VNIT institution in Form-A of the Government Decision.
The following two Government Technical Institutions are being included in the list prescribed in Form B for the first stage of Third Stage Technical Audit as per Para 2 (a) of the Government Resolution dated 28th March, 2018.
