Sewerage Project of Panvel Municipal Corporation subject to the following conditions:
Administrative Sanction is being accorded: 4.1 The Panvel Municipal Corporation will be the implementing agency for the project.
4.2 The share of the local body for the project should be paid by the Panvel Municipal Corporation.
4.3 The implementing agency shall be bound to implement the said project as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the decision of the Government in the context of the State Government.
4.4 The State Government vide No. It shall be incumbent upon the concerned executing agency to complete the tendering process and execution of the project within the prescribed time frame as per the procedures prescribed under Sections 2 and 3. D.I. for this project.
All types of pipes, including pipes, should also be included in the tender.
4.5 The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority will be required to publish the model tender booklet issued for water supply and sewerage projects under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
4.6 The implementing agency shall ensure that the work orders for the said project are issued within 45 days from the date of order of administrative sanction.
4.7 Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT Mission, it will be mandatory for the mission city to complete the reforms prescribed by the Central Government within the prescribed time frame.
4.8 Methodology of Fund Distribution: Central Government sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission.
As per the guidelines, the Central share of funds will be disbursed in three tranches (20%+ 40%+ 40%). Funds are being provided by the central government

The State share of funds will be shared with the Central share.
4.9 While raising self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on functional outcomes. Also, the project will not be funded if the said functional achievements are not met. The Commissioner, Panvel Municipal Corporation will be fully responsible for fulfilling these things.
4.11 The first installment of funds for the project from the Central and State Government will be distributed to the Panvel Municipal Corporation after the issuance of the work order.
Accordingly, after submission of Utilization Certificate to the Central Government as prescribed by the Central Government and completion of functional achievements, the next instalment of Central share and State share will be disbursed accordingly.
4.12 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
4.13 As per the instructions given while disbursing the funds for the project, it will be mandatory for the Panvel Municipal Corporation to open a separate bank account and keep the funds in it in proportion to the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments for the project.
The funds allocated for the project are bound to be used for the same project and if it is used for any other purpose, it will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
4.14 After the project is approved by the State Government for the Urban Local Bodies, the original project cost will be included in the Government Decision No.
In case of extension of Grapadho-2021/ PR No. 122/ PAPU-06, dated 29th June, 2022, other than the stated reason, the entire responsibility will be of the concerned implementing agency. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government for this purpose.
