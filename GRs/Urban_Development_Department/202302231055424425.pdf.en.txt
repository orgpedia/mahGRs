Government Decision: All the Urban Local Bodies (Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats) and Planning Authorities in the State shall publish the commencement certificates and occupancy certificates issued for residential/ commercial projects in their area on their website (Website/ Webpage) for the general public. Also, the information on this website should be updated from time to time.
If the websites of the Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State are not updated or developed, the Directorate of Municipal Administration shall control, in coordination with them, the development and updating of such websites. These websites. To be developed/ updated by March 31, 2023.
In respect of construction in the area of Urban Local Bodies in the State, on the basis of fake Construction Commencement Certificates, it has been found that the construction of the project is being carried out unauthorisedly by obtaining a certificate from the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority (MahaRERA), so the Construction Commencement Certificates/ Occupancy Certificates issued by the said Urban Local Bodies/ Planning Authorities regarding the construction, as soon as they are issued, should be sent to the email of the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority. The district-wise list of these e-mails is given in the Annexure-A. However, after the websites of the Urban Local Bodies are developed/ updated, the said websites, as well as the websites of the Planning Authorities, shall be available on the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority (MAHARERA) website along with the website of the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority (MARERA). The Directorate of Municipal Administration should ensure that they are integrated before 31.03.2023. A report will also be submitted to the government in this regard.
The head of the Town Planning Department of the local bodies and the planning authority issuing the start certificates and the occupancy certificates issued by the urban local bodies as well as the planning authorities shall be responsible for publishing and updating the same on the website and coordinating with various authorities of the government in this regard.
The above orders should be strictly implemented by all the Urban Local Bodies (Municipal Corporations/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats) as well as the Planning Authorities and action should be taken immediately accordingly.
