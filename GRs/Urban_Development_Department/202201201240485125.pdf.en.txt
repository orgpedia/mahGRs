The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Govt. of India vide Reference No. In a letter dated 22 nd December 2021, it has been informed that the component of Swachh Technology Challenge has been included in Swachh Survekshan 2022. The challenge will be carried out in collaboration with the Agency Française de Développement.
As per the directions of the Central Government, the Swachh Survekshan to be conducted by the Central Government in the year 2022, it was under the consideration of the Government to determine its methodology for working on the Swachh Technology Challenge component and to form a committee at the state level to approve the proposals of the component and submit them to the Central Government.
As mentioned in the Preamble of the Government Resolution, the Government is approving the constitution of a State Level Committee to work on the Swachh Technology Challenge component of the Swachh Survekshan to be conducted by the Central Government in 2022 and to approve the proposals of the component and submit them to the Central Government.
