Introduction: In order to improve the development plan of Akola city, there is a demand to create a development plan, special unit in Akola Municipal Corporation. The Director, Town Planning, Pune has also issued a notification in this regard. As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 16.02.2019, the work of Regional Planning Unit of Deputy Director, Town Planning, Regional Planning, Latur has been completed and the matter was under consideration of the Government to shift the office.
Orders are being issued in this regard.
It is hereby approved to change the designation of the existing office of Deputy Director, Town Planning, Regional Planning Office, Latur to Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Cell, Akola Municipal Corporation, Akola and to relocate the said office with the said posts.
