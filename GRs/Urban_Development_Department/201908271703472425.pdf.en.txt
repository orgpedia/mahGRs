As per the provisions of Section 113 of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966, the Sindhudurg Urban Navanagar Development Authority has been constituted at the place of Sindhudurg district headquarters vide notification dated 02.05.1998. A 10-member committee has been formed for this authority under the chairmanship of Collector, Sindhudurg, which includes 8 government and 2 non-government members. There are 2 vacant posts of non-official members in this committee and the matter of appointing non-official members to the vacant posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: Considering the proposal received from the Chairman, Sindhudurg City Navanagar Development Authority and the Collector, Sindhudurg, the following persons are being appointed as" Non-official Members" in the vacant posts in the said Committee:
