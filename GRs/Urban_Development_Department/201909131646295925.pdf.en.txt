The Proposal:
The Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation is formed directly from the Gram Panchayat and property tax is levied under Section-127 and Section-129 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
In 2010, the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act was amended to provide for levy of penalty at the rate of 2% per annum under Rule-41 of Chapter VIII on unpaid property tax. Despite the fact that tax collectors are issued notices from time to time for tax collection, they are not paying tax due to financial difficulties and some other reasons, as well as property tax is not being paid on certain incomes for years. At present, the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation owes Rs. 2100 crore.
In the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation area, 1,45,887 outstanding property holders mainly include 68,633 Gaonthan, 15,801 Extended Gaonthan and 58,991 properties/ society plots etc. in Cidco node. As a result of many changes (e.g. CRZ Rules) There is a penal tax on property tax of many citizens. The scheme will help in effective recovery of tax dues from the central government, Maharashtra government, other government and semi-government properties, government-approved undertakings and other properties in the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation area.
The General Assembly of the Municipal Corporation passed a resolution regarding the implementation of the Abhay Scheme for the effective collection of property tax in the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation area. 1041, c. Approved on 27.02.2019. The resolution was passed under Section 152 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has requested vide letter dated 14.05.2019.

Government's decision:
The general body meeting of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC) has approved the implementation of the Abhay Scheme for effective recovery of property tax dues for the constituents of the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC).

Resolution No. 1041, c. 27.02.2019 is being approved by the Government under Section-152 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act subject to the following conditions: (1) No rebate in the amount of basic tax under the Abhay Yojana shall be applicable. Only interest/ penalty will be paid. The scheme will come into effect from the date fixed by the Commissioner after the issuance of the said Government Resolution.
The scheme will be applicable for a period of four months. 75% of the penalty should be waived off if the property tax amount due is paid within the first two months with a penalty of+ 25%.
If the property tax amount due in the next 2 months is paid with a penalty of+ 37.5 per cent, the 62.5 per cent penalty should be waived. The duration of the scheme will not be extended beyond 4 months under any circumstances.
(3) No exemption shall be available in respect of the amount paid in respect of penalty/ interest other than tax before the issue of this Government Order, nor shall they be refunded.
(4) The powers to implement the scheme are being delegated to the Commissioner of the Municipal Corporation or the concerned officer authorised by him. To avail the benefit of this scheme, the concerned units should apply to the Commissioner or the officer authorized by him.
(5) The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation shall issue necessary instructions/ guidelines with a view to the effective implementation of the said Abhay Yojana.
(6) It shall be further clarified that no such exemption in respect of property tax shall be granted to the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation and no further exemption in respect of property tax shall be admissible to the taxpayers concerned.
(7) The Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation shall deal with all constitutional matters in accordance with this decision.
(8) As soon as the period of the said scheme expires, action shall be taken in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act and no such proposals shall be submitted to the Government thereafter.
(9) The Government shall not compensate the corporation for any loss of income due to the implementation of the Abhay Yojana.
