Government's decision:
The Government of India has launched the" Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban)" with the objective of making all the cities of the country completely clean, healthy and livable and to provide clean and good health and environment to all the citizens and to provide sanitation and household toilet facilities to the citizens of all the constituent cities of the country. On the lines of this campaign, the" Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)" is being implemented in the state as per the decision of the Government No. 1 in the context.
The Central Government has released Rs. 12,11,50, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Eleven Lakh Fifty Thousand only) to the State for construction of Community/ Public Toilets under Swachh Bharat Mission during the financial year 2018-19 as per Order No.3 of the Government of India.
Accordingly, under Swachh Bharat Mission, the Central Government is required to release Rs. 12,11,50, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Eleven Lakh Fifty Thousand only) and the State Government is required to release Rs. 8,07,80, 194/- (Eight Crore Seven Lakh Eighty Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Four only). Accordingly, the Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 20,19,30, 194/- (Rupees Twenty Crores Twenty Nine Lakhs Thirty Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Four only) to the Urban Local Bodies of the State. The expenditure shall be met from the provision made under that head during the financial year 2018-19 as shown in the table below.
