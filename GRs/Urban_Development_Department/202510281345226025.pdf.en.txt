Introduction: - Simhastha Kumbh Mela is a very large public festival of great religious, cultural and social importance and is attended by lakhs of devotees, tourists, sadhus and employees of various departments from home and abroad. It is imperative that the fair is organised in a planned, GSMA and SRA manner. Works are undertaken by various departments to organize this fair. The government will make huge financial provisions for these works. Funds for the Kumbh Mela will be distributed by the state government for various development works and activities. As a result, accounting of such financial transactions, proper documentation of expenditure, coordination, and regular audits need to be strictly carried out.
The decision to set up the Nashik-Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority has been taken in the Cabinet meetings held on 06.05.2025. Accordingly, the Nashik-Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority Act, 2025, was enacted. Published in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette on 10.07.2025.
Accordingly, the Executive Committee has decided to open a new Personal Deposit / Personal Ledger Account in the name of Divisional Commissioner, Nashik and Chairman, Nashik-Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority, Nashik. Approval has been granted by Government Decision dated 25.09.2025.
4. Nashik - Section 15 (4) of the Trimbakeshwar Kumbh Mela Authority Act 2025 provides that the Kumbh Mela Fund shall be utilised by the Kumbh Mela Commissioner with the approval of the President.
