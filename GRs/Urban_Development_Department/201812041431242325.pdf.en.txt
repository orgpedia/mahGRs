While suspending the said resolution passed by the Standing Committee of the Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation, the Government Resolution dated 14/06/2018 in the context stated that as per the provision of Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, the concerned are required to submit a representation within 30 days. However, the Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation did not receive any representation in this regard. However, the Commissioner, Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation vide reference No. 3 The representations have been received by letter and referred to the Standing Committee Resolution No. 26/11/2013. 21, clear views on fragmentation are presented.
In view of the reasons given by the Commissioner, Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation in connection with the dissolution of the said resolution, the Standing Committee of the Municipal Corporation passed Resolution No. 21, dated 26/11/2013 was against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation and the matter was under the consideration of the Government to cancel the resolution.
Government's decision:
The Standing Committee of the Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation passed a resolution dated 26/11/2013 vide No. 21. As it is clear that this is against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation, the Commissioner, Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation, as proposed by him in his letters of reference, has passed the Standing Committee Resolution No. 21 dated 26/11/2013 is being finally dissolved in exercise of the powers vested in the Government under Section 451 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act, 1949.
