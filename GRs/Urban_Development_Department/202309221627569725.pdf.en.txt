Government's decision:
The approval given by the Central Government and the State Government to the Nagpur Metro Phase-II Project and the proposed Memorandum of Understanding to be signed for the project are as follows:
(a) The Central Government and the State Government have approved the Nagpur Metro Phase-II Project and the Central Government has approved the construction of the Metro Phase-II Project. As informed vide letter dated 16.01.2023, the Nagpur Metro Rail Phase-II Project is being implemented by Central Government, State Government and Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. A draft of the trilateral Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on Annexure-A is being approved.
(b) the likely financial burden on the State Government in the wake of the said agreement shall be borne by the Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. It is being approved to provide land (other than land shown in DPR) to Local Authorities, Government/ Semi-Government Organizations in accordance with the projects and develop such land from the financial resources generated through TOD, FSI and other ancillary sources.
(c) If the expenditure of Nagpur Metro Rail Phase-II Project is to be incurred in excess of the items indicated in the Detailed Project Report, it will require the approval of the State Government (Finance Department).
(d) In the wake of the said MoU, it is being approved to reimburse the financial burden on the State Government from the" Dedicated Urban Transport Fund" of MahaMetro.
The Government Finance Department vide reference No. 228/ Expenditure-3, dated 04/07/2023 is being issued based on the feedback received. The decision was taken by the Maharashtra government.
