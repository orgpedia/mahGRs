The Proposal:
Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation is a D category Municipal Corporation. on 26.03.1997. According to the 2011 census, the Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation has a population of over 5 lakh and a total area of 65 sq km.
2. Considering the huge increase in urbanization of the area of Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, the increasing population resulting in stress on infrastructure, various important projects/ initiatives by the state and central government, the implementation of various public welfare schemes by the municipal administration, the need for trained manpower in the municipal corporation for dynamic administrative work, it was necessary to create new posts for administrative and technical work in Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation.
3.Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation's general meeting held. Resolution No. 10.08.2016 62 and the Standing Committee. Resolution No. 04.06.2021 22 has approved the Municipal Corporation's model. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, has issued orders to the effect. As per the letter dated 01.12.2021, the proposal for the formation of the Municipal Corporation was submitted to the Government. In pursuance of these proposals, the Commissioner, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, has issued the following orders: The revised information sought under the Government letter dated 19.1.2022. received vide letter dated 25.11.2022. Accordingly, to approve the formation as per Section 51 (4) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949. Also, the matter of approving the figure of a total of 1903 posts in the Municipal Corporation along with the creation of new necessary posts on the establishment of the Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.
