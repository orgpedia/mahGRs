The Proposal:
As per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission, the Central Government has released Rs. 1102.35 crore towards 2nd instalment of Basic Grant-in-aid for the year 2018-2019 to the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) in the State under Order No. The grant was to be distributed to the urban local bodies in the state.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission, the Central Government has released the second instalment of basic grant for the financial year 2018-2019 amounting to Rs. 1102.35 crore is being approved to be distributed to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats based on the criteria of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
