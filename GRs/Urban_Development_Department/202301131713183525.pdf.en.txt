the prelude
The Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation's Service Access Rules, Reference No. The 1. Approval has been obtained by Government Resolution dated 30.03.2021. In this service entry rule, the service entry rules of all the posts under various departments of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation were approved. This includes the fire department.
Regarding the amendment in the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation's entry into service rules approved in the year 2021, the Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation has issued a notification regarding the appointment of the post of Fireman in the Fire Department. 2 and 3. 28.04.2021 and D. As per letter dated 14.10.2021, the proposal was submitted to the Government. According to the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation's service access rules, In the Government Resolution dated 30.03.2021, a proposal to amend the method of appointment for the post of Fireman was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. In Annexure-II to the Entry into Service Rules, approved by the Government Decision dated 30.03.2021, the powers conferred under Sections 454, 455 and 465 (1) (l) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act,
