Procedure for the distribution of funds:
7.1 Distribution of Central and State Governments will be done as per the guidelines of the Centrally Sponsored Swachh Bharat Mission.
7.2 As per the instructions given at the time of disbursement of funds for the said project, it will be mandatory to open a separate account in the banks allocated in accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government.
7.3 The funds allocated for the said project are bound to be used for the same project and if the same is used for any other purpose, the matter will be considered as serious financial irregularity.
7.4 After the project is approved by the State Government for the Urban Local Bodies, the entire responsibility of any increase in the original project cost for any reason shall lie with the concerned Urban Local Bodies/ Implementing Agencies. No additional funds will be provided by the State Government.

8.9 Solid Waste Management The local body concerned shall develop a mechanism for meeting the maintenance and repair cost of the project.
9. While implementing the Detailed Project Report (DPR) being administratively approved for solid waste management of Nagpur Municipal Corporation under Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban), it will be mandatory for the implementing agency to meet the target set as follows. 100% segregation of solid waste generated in the city at source.
Transportation of 100% segregated waste in segregated manner.
Preparation of compost manure from wet waste or processing it through biomethanisation.
If the compost is being produced, the organic manure produced from wet waste should be tested as per FCO standards by a laboratory authorized by the State Government and it should get the brand" Harit Mahasiti Compost." Also to sell this organic manure in the name of" Green City Compost."
Secondary segregation of dry waste should be done at the material recovery facility. The recyclable waste should be recycled or sold if possible.
After all the above processes, the remaining waste will be compulsorily disposed of in the landfill.
