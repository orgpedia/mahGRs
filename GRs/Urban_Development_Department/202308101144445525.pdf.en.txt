Under the Centrally Sponsored AMRUT 2.0 Mission, the Water Supply Project of Karanja Nagar Panchayat has been accorded administrative sanction as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Paragraph No. 4.1 of the said Government Decision stated that the project is being implemented on full deposit basis by the Maharashtra Jeevan Authority.
Under this Government decision, Karanja Nagar Panchayat is being changed as the operating mechanism of the water supply project under the context of Karanja Nagar Panchayat. Also, all other terms and conditions of the government decision in the context of the water supply project of Karanja Nagar Panchayat will remain unchanged.
