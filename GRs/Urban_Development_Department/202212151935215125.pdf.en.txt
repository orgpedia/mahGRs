The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Abhiyan is being implemented under the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 in the context of providing basic infrastructure in the urban areas of the state and raising their standards.
Under the Maharashtra Golden Jubilee Urbanization Mission, the water supply project of Satana city was granted administrative approval under the Government Decision No. 2. The matter of approving the inclusion of goods and services tax of the Satana City Water Supply Project, which was submitted to the government under Reference No. 3 through the Directorate of Municipal Administration, was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative sanction has been accorded to Satana City Water Supply Project under Maharashtra Suvarna Jayanti Nagarotthan Maha Abhiyan as per the Government Resolution No. 2. The proposal submitted to the Government under Reference No. 3 by the Directorate of Municipal Administration regarding the inclusion of Goods and Services Tax in the water supply project is being approved by this Government Decision as mentioned in the following statement.
