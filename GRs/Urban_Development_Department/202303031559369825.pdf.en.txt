GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In line with the Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) 2.0 of the Government of India, Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban) 2.0 has been implemented in the State as per the Government Decision dated 15 th July, 2022.
Under Swachh Maharashtra Mission (Urban) 2.0, State Level, District Level and Municipal Corporation Level Committees are being constituted for Solid Waste Management in the State for coordination and reduction of errors in Solid Waste and Sewerage Management.
