The government has decided to regularize the recruitment of 16 contractual engineers (deputy engineer, branch engineer, junior engineer and others) in Parbhani City Municipal Corporation. Government approval has been granted as on 30.9.2021. However, in the meantime, as the service entry rules of the posts of Parbhani City Municipal Corporation were fixed, the posts were divided into promotion and direct service cadre, so the Commissioner proposed to create more number of posts for regular induction of the concerned contractual engineers into the service of the Municipal Corporation. submitted to the Government vide letter dated 12.11.2021.
As mentioned in the proposal dated 12.11.2021 in the context of Parbhani City Municipal Corporation, the following posts are being created in the direct service cadre at the Parbhani City Municipal Corporation establishment. Further action should be taken as per the approval given under 30.9.2021.
