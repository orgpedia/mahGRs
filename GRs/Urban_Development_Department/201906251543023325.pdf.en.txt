Background
Read the above 1. As per the Government decision dated 27.05.2019, the Regional Planning Offices at Nanded and Kolhapur have been transferred with the posts of Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Cell, Nagpur Municipal Corporation, Nagpur and Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Cell, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur respectively. The Director, Town Planning, Pune has been given the responsibility to bear the salary and allowances and establishment expenses of the officers/ employees of these offices. The matter was under consideration of the Government to take a decision in pursuance of the proposal sent vide letter dated 11.06.2019. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Component, Nagpur Municipal Corporation and Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Component, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation.
After the end of the financial year, the Director of Town Planning, Pune, should receive the reimbursement of this expenditure from the concerned Municipal Corporation.
