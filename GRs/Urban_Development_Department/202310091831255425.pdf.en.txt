Accordingly, the State share of funds will be shared with the Central share in proportion to the funds released by the Central Government.
4.9 While raising self-financing funds for projects sanctioned under AMRUT Mission, Urban Local Bodies will be required to converge with the Finance Commission funds received by them. For this, they should mark the funds received from the Finance Commission.
4.10 As per the guidelines of the Mission, the funds allocated for the project will be based on functional outcomes. Also, the project will not be funded if the project does not meet the functional outcome. The Commissioner, Chandrapur Municipal Corporation will be fully responsible for fulfilling these requirements.
4.11 The first installment of funds for the project from the Central and State Governments
After issuing the work order, it will be distributed to Chandrapur Municipal Corporation.

4.12 It shall be mandatory for the concerned Urban Local Self-Government Institution to raise funds on the basis of the funds distributed by the Central and State Governments as per the financial figures of the Mission and submit the utilization certificate to the Government in the form prescribed by the Central Government.
As per the instructions given while allocating funds for the project, it will be mandatory for the Chandrapur Municipal Corporation to open a separate bank account and keep funds in it in proportion to the funds distributed by the central and state governments for the project.
