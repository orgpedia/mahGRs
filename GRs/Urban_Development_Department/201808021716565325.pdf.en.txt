Circular:
The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is being implemented across the country since 2nd October, 2014 with an aim to make the entire country clean by October, 2019. On the same lines, the Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban)
The implementation is going on in a" mission mode" across the state. Under this mission, two major aspects are to make cities" Open Defecation Free" and" Clean" under solid waste management.
2. In the first phase of implementation of Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), urban areas of the State became Open Defecation Free on 1st October, 2017. The President has been announced. In the second phase of the campaign, all the cities of the state are to be" cleaned" under solid waste management. Accordingly, a campaign" Garbage Lakh Mola" is being implemented from 1st May, 2017 for segregation of waste in urban areas of the State.
Under the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, the central government conducted a survey of all the cities of the country in January 2018.
In this" Swachh Survekshan-2018," some of the cities have performed well in segregation, collection, transportation and processing of waste, resulting in good ranking at the state and country level. However, some cities have been ranked poorly at the national level for their performance in segregation, collection and transportation of waste.
4. The core of urban solid waste management is segregation and scientific processing of waste. It has been the experience till now that without segregation of solid waste, solid waste does not get value. So far, the projects in this area have been

7. Daily action taken report under Swachh August Kranti campaign should be sent to the District Administration Officer of the concerned district. All the District Administrative Officers of the district
Daily reports should be collected. The district administration officials should visit the concerned urban local body once a week to ensure that action is taken on the basis of this report. The report should also be sent to the office of the Divisional Commissioner and the State Mission Directorate. The Regional Deputy Director/ Assistant Director, Divisional Commissioner's Office shall scrutinize the reports so received and implement the same in the Urban Local Bodies within the Division.
The cities which are unsatisfactory should be visited by themselves and suggested for effective implementation and they should do so and send a report to the State Mission Director.
The action taken by the Urban Local Bodies under the" Swachh August Kranti" campaign will be reviewed at the level of Principal Secretary (Navi-II) in the first week of September, 2018.
