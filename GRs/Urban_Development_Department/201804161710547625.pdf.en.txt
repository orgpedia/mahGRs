Preamble: Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan is being implemented in Urban Local Bodies in the State on the lines of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Urban) of the Central Government. Under this Mission, the scheme of Incentive Grant for Open Defecation Free Cities has been announced as per the Government decision dated 28th March, 2016 in Reference No. 1. The revised instructions have been issued in terms of the Government Decision dated 30th January, 2017 in Reference No. 2 regarding improvement in the implementation of the scheme.
The first instalment of grant in aid to Katol Municipal Council amounting to Rs. 30.00 lakh and second instalment amounting to Rs. 30.00 lakh have been disbursed as per the Government Decisions dated 31st March, 2016 at Reference No.4 and 23rd March, 2017 at Reference No.5 respectively. The Katol Municipal Council is a Class B Municipal Council and the Katol Municipal Council is entitled to Rs. 45.00 lakh as first installment and Rs. 45.00 lakh as second installment. Further, Rs. 45.00 lakh has been disbursed to Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Council as first instalment as per the Government decision dated 30.03.2017 in Reference No.6.
Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Council is an A category Municipal Council and the first installment of Rs. 60.00 lakh is admissible to Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Council.
In view of the above, the Government was considering to disburse a total amount of Rs. 45.00 lakh to Katol Municipal Council as difference between first and second instalment of Rs. 60.00 lakh and Rs. 90.00 lakh permissible and Rs. 45.00 lakh to Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Council as difference between first instalment of Rs. 45.00 lakh and Rs. 15.00 lakh permissible.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SWMA-2018/ P.S. 119/ NV-34 GOVERNMENT DECISION: After considering the points in the Preamble, it is hereby approved to disburse a total amount of Rs. 45.00 lakhs to Katol Municipal Council to the extent of difference in incentive grant amount of Rs. 30.00 lakhs and to Kulgaon-Badlapur Municipal Council to the extent of difference in incentive grant amount of Rs. 15.00 lakhs.
The aforesaid funds distributed as Incentive Grants may be used only for the purposes mentioned in paragraph 1 (a) of the Government Resolution dated 28th March, 2016 at Reference No. 1. Expenditure on the matters referred to in paragraph 1 (a) of the said Government Decision shall be made with the approval of the competent authority concerned as per the rules prevailing at present.
The said expenditure shall be met from the fund drawn from this head of account during the financial year 2016-17 (2217 1301) as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 3. 45.00 lakhs sanctioned under the said Government Decision to disburse the difference in the grant of incentives admissible to the Municipal Councils of Katol and Kulgaon-Badlapur should be handed over by the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai to the State Mission Director, Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban).
