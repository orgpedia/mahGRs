The government's decision:
Under Swachh Maharashtra Abhiyan (Urban), the detailed project of the price indicated in Rakana 4 of the Urban Solid Waste Management Statement of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation was approved as per the letter of the Government at Reference No. 2.
While the project was being implemented by the corporation, a request was made by the corporation to approve new items excluding some of the items included in the project. (The exclusions and costs of the approved projects are set out in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively of the Statement accompanying this Government Decision.) The items to be approved and their cost in place of the excluded items are indicated in Rakaana 7 and 8 respectively. )
The proposal submitted by the Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation was examined at the level of the State Mission Directorate and submitted for the approval of the High Power Committee held on 25.02.2021. The proposal was approved by the high-powered committee in the same meeting.
Accordingly, in accordance with the approval of the High Powered Committee to the said proposal, the Government hereby approve the deletion of the items of value mentioned in paragraphs 5 and 6 respectively and the deletion of the items of value mentioned in paragraphs 7 and 8 respectively in the accompanying statement.
With the above change, the revised cost of the Detailed Project Report of the Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation's solid waste will be as shown in the table in the statement accompanying this Government Decision.
