The Proposal:
The Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) was established under the Maharashtra Metropolitan Region Development Act, 2016. The Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) has initiated the process of preparing the draft Town Planning Plan as per the Maharashtra Regional Planning and Town Planning Act, 1966. The Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority (PMRDA) had requested for the appointment of an officer in the cadre of Assistant Director of Town Planning in the Authority for appointment as an arbitrator under the provisions of the said Act. In pursuance of the said request, the Government was considering filling up the post of Assistant Director Town Planning in Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority by transfer/ deputation from the post of Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group-A/ Gazetted) cadre officers in the establishment of Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation. The following Government Orders are being issued regarding the transfer/ deputation of Assistant Director Town Planning:
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees (Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties) Act, 2005, and as per the provisions of Annexure-III, Rule 40 (1) (2) (3) (4) and Annexure-II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions during Suspension, Removal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, the officers of the cadre of Assistant Director, Town Planning (Group A/ Gazetted) are being transferred and posted on deputation as indicated in the following statement for administrative reasons.

The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take immediate action to issue necessary executive orders for the above officers to join the place of posting by transfer/ deputation.
The officers transferred/ deputed under this order shall join the place of deputation immediately on receipt of the executive order of the Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, State of Maharashtra, Pune regarding deputation.
Since the above officers are being appointed by transfer/ deputation in the interest of public service and administrative convenience, the period of permissible posting and joint transfer grant should be sanctioned to them as per rules.
If the officers transferred/ posted under the said order do not appear at the place of appointment or try to cancel the transfer under political pressure, it will be recorded in the confidential report of the concerned officers. It may also be noted that the act of political pressure is violative of Rule 3 (3) and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
The Director, Directorate of Town Planning and Valuation, Maharashtra State, Pune, shall hold the additional charge of the post falling vacant in accordance with this proviso to the same or an officer of equivalent rank in the nearest office or in a cadre inferior to the cadre of the officer to be transferred.
It should be handed over to the senior officer in the office. However, it should be ensured that in no case the transferring officer is assigned additional charge of the post falling vacant due to his transfer.
