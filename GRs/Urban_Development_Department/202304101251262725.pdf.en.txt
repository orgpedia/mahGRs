Since the Navi Mumbai International Airport Affected Area (Naina) has been notified, the scope of the above posts will be limited to the jurisdiction of revenue villages in the Naina project area. Accordingly, the concerned officer/ employee shall have the powers conferred by the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 as well as the laws/ rules regarding revenue and land records while discharging their duties. As per the prevailing practice, the Controlling Officer and the Appellate Authority in respect of land record matters shall be the authorities prescribed by the said Act.
Raigad and Deputy Superintendent of Land Records, Panvel, G. Raigad shall regularly monitor the activities of other areas excluding the jurisdiction of revenue villages in the Naina project area till further orders.
