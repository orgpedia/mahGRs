Introduction: Health services are provided to the public through the Health Department of Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation.
To assist the Medical Health Officer, a proposal was placed before the General Body by the Hon'ble Members to appoint Mr. Balaprasad Mohanrao Kunturkar, Medical Officer as Assistant Medical Health Officer.
2. The Municipal Administration brought to the notice of the general meeting through administrative comments that Dr. Balaprasad Kunturkar could not be approved for the post as the post of Assistant Medical Health Officer did not exist on the establishment of Nanded Waghela City Municipal Corporation. However, the resolution was passed unanimously.
The above decision No. The 93. 14.10.2016 as it is contrary to the administrative note, the Commissioner, Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation has requested the Government vide letter dated 12.4.2019 to cancel the said resolution under section 451 of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
The post of Assistant Medical Health Officer does not exist on the establishment of Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation. Therefore, Dr. Balaprasad Kunturkar, Medical Officer, was allowed to work in the post of Assistant Medical Health Officer as per the resolution passed by the General Assembly of the Municipal Corporation. The 93. 14.10.2016 is not a rule.
5. In view of the above, the said Resolution No. 93 dated 14.10.2016 was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: NAMPA-2019/ No.121/ NV-24,
Government's decision:
As per the facts mentioned in the above introduction, the post of Assistant Medical Health Officer does not exist on the establishment of Nanded Waghela City Municipal Corporation. Therefore, Dr. Balaprasad Kunturkar, Medical Officer, was allowed to work in the post of Assistant Medical Health Officer as per the resolution passed by the General Assembly of the Municipal Corporation. The 93. 14.10.2016 is not in force, the said resolution is being suspended in the first instance for dissolution under Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
If the resolution is to be submitted to the concerned in the matter of suspension, they must submit it to the government within 30 days from the date of the suspension order.
