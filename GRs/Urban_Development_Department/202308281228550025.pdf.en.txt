the order
The Maharashtra Regional Planning and Town Planning Act, 1966, as a preamble to the Addendum to the Model Building Bye-Laws 2016 issued by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India, is to be incorporated in the approved Development Control Rules of all planning authorities in the State with immediate effect.
Since the matters in the Addendum to Model Building Bye-Laws 2016 are related to the internal protection of these buildings, it is necessary to take action through Navi-1, in the public interest and with a view to implement the guidelines of the Central Government, the government was considering to issue directions to all the planning authorities in the state under section 154 of the Maharashtra Regional Planning and Town Planning Act, 1966 in this regard.
Accordingly, in view of the public interest in the subject matter, the Government directs all the Planning Authorities of the State to incorporate the Addendum to Model Building Bye-Laws 2016 issued by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India, in the approved Development Control Rules of all the Planning Authorities of the State immediately.

Government directive
All the planning authorities of the state while approving the development proposals in their jurisdiction shall implement the Addendum to Model Building Bye-Laws 2016 issued by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India, as per the provisions of the Annexure attached. Also, it should be ensured that the provisions related to the development control rules in this Annexure are complied with.
(2) This Directive shall come into force with immediate effect and shall be enforced by all the planning authorities of the State.
