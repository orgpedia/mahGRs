The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document.

The District Collector of the concerned district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controlling Officer for the disbursement of this grant to the aforesaid Municipal Corporations. The Collector should withdraw this grant from the treasury and immediately distribute it to the concerned Municipal Corporations.
Expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2017-2018 by spending under the head" Demand No.F-4,3604, Payment of Compensation and Stamped Amounts to Local Self-Government Institutions and Panchayat Raj Institutions, 102, Stamp Duty (01) Stamp Duty on Repeal of Tax (01) Stamp Duty on Re-institution of Tax in lieu of Local Body Tax (36040924) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) to Municipal Corporations for payment of 1% surcharge on Stamp Duty in Municipal Corporations."
The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be separately accounted for by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
