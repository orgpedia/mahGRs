Government's decision:
As per the above mentioned facts, the said Resolution passed by the Special General Meeting of Latur Municipal Corporation No. 140 dated 20.2.2020, the following decisions A and B are against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. Therefore, the following decisions A and B are hereby set aside as final in terms of Section 451 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
