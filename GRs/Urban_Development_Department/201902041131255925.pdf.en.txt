The Proposal:
Section 149A of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporations Act (59 of 1949) has been amended to provide that the stamp duty to be levied under the Maharashtra Stamp Act (60 of 1958) on the sale, gift and encumbrance of immovable property, respectively, shall be surcharged at the rate of one per cent of the value of the property so situated, in the case of sale or encumbrance of any such document in relation to immovable property situated in the city, and in the case of encumbrance of the same, on the amount secured by the encumbrance of that document. Accordingly, 1% stamp duty surcharge payable to 26 Municipal Corporations of the State for the month of December, 2018 and January, 2019 is Rs. 231,60,96, 291.00 crore is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the month of December, 2018 and January, 2019 for the month of 2018-19, the total amount of Municipal Corporations towards 1% stamp duty is Rs. The Government approves the following disbursement of Rs. 231,60,96, 291.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Thirty One Crores Sixty Lakh Ninety Six Thousand Two Hundred and Ninety One only):

The amount credited towards the 1% surcharge and the expenditure on this subsidy to be disbursed in lieu thereof shall be separately accounted for by the concerned District Collector and the accounts and all related documents shall be made available to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as required.
