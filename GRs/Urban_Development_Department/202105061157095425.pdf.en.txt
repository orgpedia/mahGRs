The Proposal:
Under the National Urban Health Mission, the Municipal Corporation proposes to set up an Urban Community Health Centre to provide medical services to the people of slums and extended areas under the jurisdiction of Sangli-Miraj-Kupwad City Municipal Corporation and to strengthen the urban health system. The Sangli-Miraj Kupwad Municipal Corporation has decided to purchase private land in addition to the land owned by the Municipal Corporation reserved for the construction of the Urban Community Health Center. Commissioner, Sangli-Miraj-Kupwad City Municipal Corporation under reference No. In view of the facts found under letters 5 and 6, the Government was of the view that it was against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation to purchase private land (non-agricultural, no approach road, urban land with ceiling, construction not possible as per the approved size of the site as per the approved map of the permissible health care centre, etc.) at a time when the land owned by the Municipal Corporation was available and the design for construction of the building on the said site was decided at the Government level.
Government's decision:
Sangli-Miraj-Kupwad Municipal Corporation General Assembly resolution No. 19/09/2019 95, being against the financial interest of the Municipal Corporation, is finally being dissolved in exercise of the powers vested in the Government under Section-451 (3) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act.
