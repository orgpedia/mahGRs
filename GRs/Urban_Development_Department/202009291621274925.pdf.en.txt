The Proposal:
Under the National Urban Health Mission, an Urban Community Health Centre is proposed to be set up in the jurisdiction of Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation to provide medical services to the citizens of slums and extended areas and to strengthen the urban health system. The General Assembly of Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation passed a resolution dated 19/09/2019 regarding the purchase of private land other than the land owned by the Municipal Corporation reserved for the construction of the said Urban Community Health Centre. Vishnu Mane and Mr. Bhalchandra Mokashi in their Reference Letter No. 1 and 2 by the government.
2.According to No. In pursuance of the Reference letter at 1& 2, the Commissioner, Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation vide Reference No. 3 has been reported. According to the report, a land is available in the Warandi Kupwad area of the Municipal Corporation for the construction of an Urban Community Health Center. 334 is reserved. Accordingly, the design for the construction of the Urban Community Health Center building on the reserved land has been decided at the government level and the funds for this work have been approved. However, the General Assembly, on 19/09/2019, adopted Resolution No. 95 has been passed.
In view of the above facts, when the land owned by the Municipal Corporation is available and the design for construction of the building on the said land has been decided at the government level, it is necessary to purchase private land (non-agricultural, non-access road, urban land with a ceiling, etc., which is not possible to build according to the size of the site as per the approved map of the permissible health care center) by paying a monetary compensation.

The matter of suspending the resolution was under the consideration of the government before it was revoked as the government was of the opinion that it was against the financial interest of the municipal corporation.
Government's decision:
The General Assembly of Sangli Miraj Kupwad City Municipal Corporation passed on 19/09/2019 by General Assembly Resolution No. 95 is being suspended in the first instance under the provisions of Section 451 (1) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act, 1949.
However, on this, the opportunity is being given to the concerned to make a representation in this regard within one month from the date of the order as per Section 451 (2) of the Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Act. If the complaint is not received within the stipulated time, further action will be taken under Section 451 (3) of the Act.
