The Proposal:
The Central Government has released Rs. 952.9150 crore towards 2nd instalment of Basic Grant-in-Aid for the year 2017-2018 to be distributed to the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) in the State as per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission vide Order No. The grant was to be distributed to the urban local bodies in the state.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission, the Central Government has released the second instalment of basic grant of Rs. 952.9150 crore to all eligible D category Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils and Nagar Panchayats based on the criteria of i) Population ii) Area as indicated in the Annexure.
