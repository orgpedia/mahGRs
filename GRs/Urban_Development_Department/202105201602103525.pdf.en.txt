The Proposal:
The Director, Town Planning and Valuation, Pune, has issued the following orders: In pursuance of the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter dated 24.02.2021, the office of Deputy Director, Town Planning, Development Plan, Special Cell, Nanded Waghela Municipal Corporation has been completed and the office is going to be closed soon. Orders are being issued in this regard.
Government's decision:
Considering the scope and importance of the work of revising the development plan for the Bhiwandi-Nizampur City Municipal Corporation area and considering that the development plan, the office of the Nanded-Waghala Municipal Corporation has been completed and will soon cease to function, it is hereby approved to relocate the office on nomination basis as follows:
