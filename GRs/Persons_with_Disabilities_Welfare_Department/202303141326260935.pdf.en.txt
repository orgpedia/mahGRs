Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 1st December, 2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, a revised rule book on procedures for office procurement to be carried out by Government departments has been issued. Paragraph No. 2.9.3 of the said rule book provides for the composition of the" Purchase Committee" to be constituted for the administrative department (itself) of the Ministry. As per the Government notification dated 15.12.2022 of the General Administration Department, a" Department for Welfare of Persons with Disabilities" has been created and it was under consideration of the Government to constitute a" Procurement Committee" for procurement of goods/ services required for the said Department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provision made in para 2.9.3 of the rule book accompanying the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, the following" Purchase Committees" are being constituted for the purchase of goods/ services required for the Differently Abled Welfare Department (itself).

(2) The Procurement Committee while making necessary purchases of essential goods/ services for the Differently Abled Welfare Department (itself) shall act in accordance with the procedure laid down in the relevant Government Decision of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour. If the annual value of the procurement is less than Rs 10 lakh, the Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary, Department of Disabled Welfare as the head of the department will have the right to take a decision regarding the procurement without convening a meeting of the procurement committee.
