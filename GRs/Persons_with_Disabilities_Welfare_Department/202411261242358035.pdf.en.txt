The Proposal -
The state government provides transparent, efficient and time-bound services to eligible persons in the state. Also, keeping in view the growing expectations from the local administration of the people, the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 has been promulgated and implemented on 28.4.2015 with a view to provide prompt and quality services to the people within the prescribed time frame.
The various services being provided to persons with disabilities under the Department of Disabilities Welfare, pursuant to the provision of Section-3 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, have been made available from time to time by the Zonal Office, dated 2, 3 and 4 under reference. In its meetings held on Friday, dated 08.119.2004 under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, State Chief Services Commission, the Commission has suggested to notify more services for persons with disabilities under the Department of Disabilities Welfare and appoint a Study Group under the Chairmanship of senior officers of the Department for Business Process Re-Engineering to re-evaluate the services notified earlier. Accordingly, the government was considering appointing a study group headed by senior officers of the department to review the services being provided to persons with disabilities under the Department of Disabilities Welfare and notify more services as well as to make the services being provided offline available online.
