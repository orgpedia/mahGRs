Government circular:
Government to solve the problems of differently abled people at the district level
The officers of all the departments and all the government machinery will be present at one place and the grievances of the disabled people will be heard and the work can be solved at the same place. For this, the Department is implementing the campaign" Divyang Welfare Department at the doorstep of the Divyang" as per the Government Decision dated 23rd May 2023 and this campaign at the district level as per the Government Circular dated 28th July 2023 of the Department.
