The Proposal: -
The Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation is responsible for financing the self-employment of the disabled in the state in the form of loans. In addition to loan disbursement, the corporation also implements various schemes for the overall development of the differently abled. The Corporation is required to meet the management and administrative expenses for the financial year 2024-25 under Demand No. A budgetary provision of Rs. 95,64,000 - (literal Rs. Ninety-five lakh sixty-four thousand only) has been made under the head ZI-3, (2235 1) 364 (Compulsory) 36-Auxiliary Grants (Salary). Managing Director, Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation Limited. , Mumbai vide Reference No. A proposal has been submitted to the Government for devolution of funds from the budgetary provision approved under letter No. 5 to the Corporation in the matter of 36-Auxiliary Grants (Salaries). Accordingly, the issue of disbursement of funds was under consideration of the government.
