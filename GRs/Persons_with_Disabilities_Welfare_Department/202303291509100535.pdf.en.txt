The Proposal:
In order to make the administration of the state more dynamic and people-oriented, in order to ensure the proper use of information technology, the concerned department has been directed to form a project implementation committee to implement the project of computerization in line with the computerization of administrative activities. Further, as per the Government Notification dated 15.12.2022 of the General Administration Department, a separate" Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities" has been created at the Ministerial level by separating the functions of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities-I and Welfare of Persons with Disabilities-II in the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Therefore, the matter of forming a Project Implementation Committee for computerization of Divyang Welfare Department as well as the field offices under the control of the Department was under the consideration of the Department.
Government's decision
The following Project Implementation Committees are being constituted to implement the computerized e-governance projects of the Department of Disabled Welfare and the field offices under the control of the Department.
