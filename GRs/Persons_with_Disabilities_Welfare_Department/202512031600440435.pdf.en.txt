The functions of the Expert Committee under the Department of Disability Welfare are as follows: -
3.1 All the Ministries / Administrative Departments shall forward a consolidated proposal of all the posts in their subordinate office for finding suitable posts for the differently abled persons to the Department for decision in the format in the accompanying Statement "A" for approval of the aforesaid Expert Committee along with the feedback of the Committee of the Department concerned.
3.2 The provision in section 34 (1) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 empowers the government to exempt any government establishment from the provision of this section. The approval of the above expert committee of the Department of Disability Welfare is mandatory for granting such exemption.
Since fixing of posts is a dynamic process, the relaxation will be reviewed from time to time keeping in view the development of technology, availability of newer and more efficient assistive devices, capacity building of persons with disabilities, etc. So, any exemption will be valid for a maximum period of 3 years. Establishments which have already been exempted by the Department of Disability Welfare and have completed a period of 3 years from the date of notification of exemption, shall submit proposals within 3 months from the date of issue of the Government Decision to the Department of Disability Welfare with fresh proposal for review of exemption.
