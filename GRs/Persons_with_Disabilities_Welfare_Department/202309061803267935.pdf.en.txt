The Proposal:
As per the Government Order dated 8th May, 2014 of the Department of Finance, Clause 3 of the Maharashtra Contingency Expenditure Rules, 2014 provides that the administrative departments shall sanction permanent advances up to Rs.5000/- for the offices of the Head of the Department without the advice of the Accountant General. Accordingly, it has been approved to sanction a permanent advance of Rs.5000/-for urgent Government works in the newly created Department of Disabled Welfare as per the Memorandum dated 16th June, 2023 of the Department.
At present, the Department is functioning from leased premises at Mittal Tower, A Wing, Nariman Point, Mumbai and the Department has to spend money from time to time for refreshments/ tea for meetings being held on various subjects. For this, the department was considering reducing the amount of fixed advance to Rs. 10,000/- as the amount of Rs. 5000/- was falling short.
Government's statement:
A proposal was submitted to the Finance Department for approval to increase the amount of fixed advance to Rs. 10,000/- for the Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan). Accordingly, it has been approved to fix the amount of fixed advance in consultation with the Accountant General's Office under the Informal Reference Number: 250/ 2023/ Regulations, dated 6th July, 2023 of the Finance Department. Accordingly, the proposal was submitted to the Accountant General (Accounts& Licensure)-1, Maharashtra, Mumbai, to increase the amount of fixed advance from Rs.5000/- to Rs.10,000/- vide his office letter No. 5/ Persons with Disabilities Welfare Deptt/ UOR-81/ 617, dated 1st September, 2023.
