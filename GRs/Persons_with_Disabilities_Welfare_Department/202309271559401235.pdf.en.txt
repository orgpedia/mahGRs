Preamble: In order to make differently-abled persons self-reliant, it has been decided to provide a green-powered eco-friendly mobile shop on vehicles (Mobile Shop on Vehicle). 1 The above has been taken as per the Government decision dated 10.6.2019. Under this scheme, a maximum subsidy of Rs. 3.75 lakh will be provided to the disabled beneficiaries for shops on mobile vehicles. A Selection Committee has been constituted under Paragraph 4 to select the Divyang beneficiaries. The Committee consists of the Manager, Disabled Finance and Development

The matter of changing the composition of the committee and changing the terms and conditions for selecting the beneficiary was under the consideration of the government.
