Preamble: The Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation is registered under Section 25 of the Companies Act, 1956. At the time of incorporation, the authorized share capital of the Corporation was sanctioned at Rs. 50 crore. Loans are provided to the disabled persons as per the scheme implemented by the corporation from the share capital made available to the corporation from time to time. It also acts as the State Authorized Channel of the National Corporation for Finance and Development of Persons with Disabilities, New Delhi.
As per the decision of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, dated 08.06.2022, the authorized shareholding of Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation has been increased from Rs 50 crore to Rs 500 crore.
