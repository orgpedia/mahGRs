Government circular:
Government to solve the problems of differently abled people at the district level
All the government departments and agencies are present at one place.
The work can be completed at the same place. For this, the Department is implementing the campaign" Divyang Welfare Department at the doorstep of the Divyang" as per the Government Decision dated 23rd May 2023 and this campaign at the district level as per the Government Circular dated 28th July 2023 of the Department.
The procedure to be followed has been explained. The" Form B" attached with the said circular provides the schedule of the campaign to be conducted in all the districts.
