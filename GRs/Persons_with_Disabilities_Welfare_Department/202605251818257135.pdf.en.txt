Government Decision: Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities Scholarship Schemes for students with disabilities studying from Infancy to Class XII will be implemented from the academic year 2026-27 on the MahaDBT 2.0 portal through an integrated computer system called "Mahashivratri." Accordingly, the scheme implemented and to be implemented by the Department is being implemented through the "Mahashivratri * Integrated Computing System" on the MahaDBT 2.0 portal.
2. "The Herrera integrated computing system will make the scheme of scholarship for students with disabilities studying in RAPA to Class XII of this department accessible on a single digital portal, making the process of implementation of the scheme easy and consistent for the students.
3. Based on the information provided by the concerned institution to the students on the portal, they have been provided with the facility to view the information about the schemes for which they are potentially eligible, which will make it easier for the students to take informed and transparent decisions while applying for the schemes. This will help reduce the number of ineligible applications, making the entire implementation process more efficient, transparent, and student-centric.
More than 64 applications from the existing online manifestos have to be filled up plan-wise in the information fields (7181001 Fields). The system has reduced the number of these fields to 31 areas.
5. Auto-fetch of information and documents from Aadhaar, UDISE] e. Government databases is being implemented to avoid repetitive filing and submission of documents to students as the number of documents required to be submitted has been reduced.
6. Also, the existing three-tier screening mechanism is being simplified to an average of two screening levels, which will enable faster application screening and approval process.
The scholarship renewal process for differently abled students studying from Infancy to Class XII is also being facilitated through the pre-filled Renewal Application, students will only have to update the fee payment details and other changed information.
Also, for income-based schemes, the income certificate submitted in the first year will be valid for the entire course duration on the basis of self-declaration.
The applicant / beneficiary can view or check the status of his / her application online from time to time through the concerned institution.
The disbursement of this scholarship amount will be directly credited to the Aadhaar seeded bank account of the beneficiaries online.

Even though the integrated computerized system of "Mahashivratri" is being implemented on the MahaDBT 2.0 portal by this Government decision, the terms, conditions, eligibility criteria, financial limitations, nature of benefits and basic principles of implementation as laid down by the original Government decisions of scholarship schemes for students with disabilities studying from Class X to XII shall continue.
12. The present system is being implemented as a technical and administrative reform to simplify, transparent and time-bound the process of application filling, scrutiny, scholarship approval, transfer of scholarship amount and renewal of application etc.
