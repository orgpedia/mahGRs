2. The grants in the said Government Memorandum are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The guidelines mentioned in the circular dated 07.04.2025 of the Department of Finance and the guidelines in the Annexure with the circular should be strictly implemented before the funds available on the budget distribution system are withdrawn from the treasury.
2) The funds disbursed to the Corporation, plan / non-plan and subject-wise, should be spent only for permissible aspects of the relevant compulsory plan.
3) To streamline operations to limit the burden on management.
4) Immediately submit to the Government a detailed report of the amounts spent on various matters, the expenditure incurred thereon, as well as the half of the funds spent in the previous year.
5) The corporation should make its best efforts to effectively implement the amount of debt disbursed.
6) Don't spend just because there is a budgetary provision or because grants have been disbursed. Cut down on office expenses.
7) While spending the funds, the funds should be utilised in compliance with the provisions and terms / conditions of the State Government's Working Rules, Financial Rules and Budget Manual as well as CVC guidelines, CAG observations, etc.
