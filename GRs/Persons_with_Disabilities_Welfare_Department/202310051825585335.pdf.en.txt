The Proposal:
In addition to effective implementation of various schemes for the economic, social, educational and cultural development of Divyangjans, for their overall development, the General Administration Department under No. A separate Department for the Welfare of Persons with Disabilities has been created by a Government Notification No. 1. The reference No. 2 As per the Government decision dated 24.03.2023, 353 temporary posts of Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune have been approved to be continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. During the financial year 2023-24, 353 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Disabled Welfare, Pune were under the consideration of the Government for extension from 01.09.2023 to 28.02.2024.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The continuation of 353 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune from 01.09.2023 to 28.02.2024 is being approved subject to the following conditions
(1) Vacancies for a period exceeding six months shall be excluded except for the post of Head of the Office.
(2) The extension is subject to the assumption that the non-permanent posts, excluding the executive posts, are not vacant for more than six months.
3) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
