Instructions have been issued to scrutinize and submit proposals as per the scrutiny list done.
The Central Government has decided to implement the revised pay scale (7th Pay Commission) for Government employees with effect from 01.01.2016. Accordingly, the implementation of the pay structure for full-time teaching and non-teaching staff in government schools along with state government employees has been notified under the Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019. Further, the Government Circular dated 20.02.2019 of the Department of Finance prescribes detailed procedures regarding fixation of starting salary and payment of arrears. Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 23rd April, 2021 of the erstwhile Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, the revised pay structure of the Seventh Pay Commission has been approved with effect from 1st January, 2016 for full-time teaching and non-teaching staff in Government approved and aided Non-Residential Special Schools / Workshops and Maternity Homes for Persons with Disabilities run by NGOs in the State under the Department of Disabilities Welfare.
