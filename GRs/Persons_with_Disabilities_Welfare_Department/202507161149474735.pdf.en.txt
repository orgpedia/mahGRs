The Government decided to appoint administrators for the management of special schools / workshops for the disabled run by NGOs. As per the provision dated 09.03.2004 as well as the provision in Part III (84) (I) of the Special Schools for Disabled Activities Code-2018, the Government shall have the power to appoint administrators to the institution until such time as the management is satisfied that the management is competent to provide facilities to the students with disabilities and to take over the management of such institution and improve the functioning of the school or until the difference in management is resolved, but the tenure of the administrator shall not exceed three years, whichever is earlier. Accordingly, the matter of appointing an administrator on both these activities was under the consideration of the government.
