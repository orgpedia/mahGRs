In line with the Government of India's initiative of Developed India-2047, the concept of "Developed Maharashtra-2047" is being implemented in the state with the support of NITI Aayog for holistic and long-term development in the state. Under this, 5 NGOs have been included in the committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary, Disability Welfare vide Government Decision dated 2nd June, 2025 for preparation of Vision Document of the Department of Disability Welfare, in addition to the officers of the Department.
