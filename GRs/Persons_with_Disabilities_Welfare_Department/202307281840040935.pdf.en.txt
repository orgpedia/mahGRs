Government Circular: To solve the problems of different categories of disabled people, all the officials of the government at the district level, all the government systems can be present at one place and find out the grievances of the disabled and solve the work at the same place. For this, as per the Government decision dated 23rd May, 2023, the Department of Disabled Welfare is implementing the campaign" Divyang Ki Daroi." The campaign will be organized in all the districts of the state as per the" Form B" attached. According to this, if there is a standard operating procedure (SOP) for the effective implementation of this campaign at the district level, then it will be of maximum use. For this, the following general procedures should be followed while implementing the campaign" Divyang Welfare Department at the doorstep of the Divyang" at the district level.
