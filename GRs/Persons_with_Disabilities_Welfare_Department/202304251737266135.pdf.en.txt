Preamble: The Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. 2 In pursuance of the instructions contained in the Government Resolution here, the Government was considering the constitution of a" Women Grievance Redressal Committee" for the Department of Disabled Welfare to inquire into complaints of sexual harassment of women at the workplace.
Government Decision: In accordance with the provisions of the Central Government's Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 and the instructions contained in the Government Decision of the Department of Women and Child Development, the Department of Disabled Welfare is constituting a" Women Grievance Redressal Committee" as mentioned below.
