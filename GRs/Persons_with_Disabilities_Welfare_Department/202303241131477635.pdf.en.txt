The Proposal:
As per the Government Notification dated 15.12.2022 of the General Administration Department, separate" Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities" has been created at the Ministerial level by separating the functions of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities-I and Welfare of Persons with Disabilities-II in the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
As per the Government decision dated 4th January, 2023 of the Department, posts have been created for the said Department wherein the post of" Accounts Officer" has been created for the post of" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." Finance for providing the services of an officer of this rank
A request has been made to the department and it is likely to take some time to receive the services of the said officer. As per the decision of the said Government, the expenditure on salary of the officers/ employees of the said Department for the period till the proceedings for obtaining separate head of accounts for the newly created Department were being met from the head of accounts in the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. However, the process of obtaining the required head of accounts for the Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities has been completed and separate budgetary allocation under Demand No. ZI for the Department for the financial year 2023-24 has been received. Therefore, in order to start the functioning of the cash branch of the Department of Disabled Welfare at the ministerial level, the department was considering declaring" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" as well as" Controlling Officer."
Government's decision:
Commencement of the functioning of the Department of Disabled Welfare at the Ministerial level
In terms of the provisions of Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause I, Shri Vishnudas Pundlikrao Ghodke, Deputy Secretary, Department of Disabled Welfare is appointed as the Controlling Officer.
