The matter of transferring G. Dhule to this institution was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Late Ratibai Maganlal Jain Public Trust, Dhule is running Sanmati Matimand Vidyalaya, Dhule, a special school run by Shri Radhe Govind Multipurpose Institute, Dhule. The transfer of G. Dhule to this institution is being approved under this government decision.
