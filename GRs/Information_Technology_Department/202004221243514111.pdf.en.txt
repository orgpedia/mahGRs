The Proposal:
Government Resolution General Administration (IT) Department No. 1717/ PR No. 111/39 dated 09/04/2019, the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation has laid down the procedure for the supply of technical manpower. The rates of technical manpower (other than tax) for the period from 01/04/2019 to 31/03/2020 are set out in the Annexure annexed to this Government Decision. Since the revised rates of technical manpower for that financial year are to be announced by the Corporation before the commencement of every financial year, the matter of publishing the rates of technical manpower for the period from 01/04/2020 to 31/03/2021 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Read No. In accordance with the Government decision from 01/04/2020 to 31/03/2021, the rates of technical manpower (other than tax) for the period from 01/04/2020 to 31/03/2021 shall be as per the Annexure attached to this Government decision, for all the Ministries/ Departments, all the field offices under them, Public Sector Undertakings, Corporations, Companies and Institutions established under the Act passed by the Central Government or State Government to hire technical manpower for IT and IT enabled services from Maharashtra IT Corporation. However, as per the agreement entered into by the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation with the technical manpower supplying agencies, the Maharashtra Technology Corporation shall issue work orders as per the availability of technical manpower of the institutions mentioned in Annexure-I.
