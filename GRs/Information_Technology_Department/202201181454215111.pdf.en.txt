Regarding the formation of a committee to review and appeal the service of the officers/ employees of the Directorate of Information Technology beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Resolution No. Matans 2018/ Q.No.170/ Se-1/ Ka.39
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: January 18, 2022.
Reference:- General Administration Department, No. LPL-2017/ Q. No.21/ Q. 10th June, 2019.
Government Decision: Under the Government Decision in the context of the General Administration Department, the combined action regarding the premature retirement of government officers/ employees after reviewing their services beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completing 30 years of qualifying service is indicated in the prescribed head. Accordingly, 3 posts of Cell Officer (Technical) Group-A (Gazetted) and 5 posts of Assistant (Technical) Group-B (Non-Gazetted) on establishment of Directorate of Information Technology have been sanctioned. Two of these employees are working. A proposal to form a committee under the above provision was under the consideration of the Government.
The Committee for Review of Service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service of the employees of the Directorate of Information Technology under this Department and the Committee for Consideration of Grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service are being constituted as follows.
