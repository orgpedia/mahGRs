Empanelment of Consultants for IT Projects in Government of Maharashtra - Extension of empaneled Agencies
Government of Maharashtra
Directorate of Information Technology
Government Circular No: GAD-IT 080/4/2014-O/O DIRECTORATE OF IT-DIT (MH) Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date: 24th April, 2023
Reference: 1. Government Resolution - GAD-IT 080/4/2014-O/O DIRECTORATE OF IT-DIT (MH) dated 20th March, 2018 2. Government Resolution - GAD - IT 080/4/2014-O/O Directorate of IT-DIT (MH) dated 9th May, 2019 3. Government Circular - GAD - IT 080/4/2014-0/O Directorate of IT-DIT (MH) dated 19th March, 2021
Introduction: Government of Maharashtra vide above referenced Government Resolutions and Government Circulars had empanelled IT Consultancy firms for a period of 5 Years (Five Years) and these Government Resolutions & Government Circulars have detailed instructions regarding consultant empanelment. These consultancy firms are deployed by numerous Government Departments to assist them in implementation of their key e-Governance initiatives. Currently this empanelment expired on 19th March 2023.
The RFP for new empanelment of consultant is under consideration. Multiple Departments had requested for an extension of the current empanelment until the new empanelment is in place - so that the related projects are not hampered.
Government Circular: The empanelment of IT Consultancy firms vide GR dated 20.3.2018 is hereby extended for a period of 6 months - from 20th March, 2023 till 19th September, 2023 or till finalization of new empanelment whichever is earlier.
No change in Commercials and other Terms & Conditions including clauses in Annexures of the related Government Resolutions.
Existing work orders for IT Consultants can be extended for the time period mentioned above.
New work orders for IT Consultants may be issued in such a way that the project will be completed before the end of the extension period mentioned above.
