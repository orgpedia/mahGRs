The Proposal:
In accordance with the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Full Participation and Protection of Rights) Act, 1995, the above mentioned reference No. As per the Government decision dated 20th January, 2011, the posts in Group A and Group B cadres in the Directorate of Information Technology were earmarked for the physically challenged.
The Central Government has issued No. 4. The list of posts ensured for the disabled under the notification dated 4.1.2021 has been published. According to this list, the posts which are equal in the office under the department should be ensured for the disabled and such orders should be issued, according to the decision of the Government of Social Justice and Special Assistance Department at No. 5 in the context. Accordingly, a proposal was under the consideration of the government to identify suitable posts for the disabled in the posts of Group A to Group D cadre on the establishment of the Directorate of Information Technology.
Government's decision
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021.
As mentioned in it, the Central Government has ensured posts for the disabled from Group' A'.
