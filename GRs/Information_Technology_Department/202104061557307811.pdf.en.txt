The Proposal:
In accordance with the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Full Participation and Protection of Rights) Act, 1995, the aforesaid Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 20th January, 2011, the posts in Group A and Group B cadres in the Directorate of Information Technology were earmarked for the physically challenged.
The Central Government has issued No. 4. The list of posts ensured for the disabled under the notification dated 4.1.2021 has been published. According to this list, the posts which are equal in the office under the department should be ensured for the disabled and such orders should be issued, according to the decision of the Government of Social Justice and Special Assistance Department at No. 5 in the context. Accordingly, as informed under Reference No.7, a proposal to fix suitable posts for Divyangjan in Group A to Group D cadre posts on the establishment of Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, has notified the posts under the State Governments for the purpose of determination of posts under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
