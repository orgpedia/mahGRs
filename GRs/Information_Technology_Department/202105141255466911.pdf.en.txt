The Proposal:
Direct Benefit Transfer at the State level to regulate the process of disbursement of benefits of various welfare schemes implemented by the State/ Central Government through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to the beneficiaries directly.
The (DBT) Working Committee has been constituted as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Since 2018, our government has made the MahaDBT portal operational.
2. Since the term of the Working Committee has expired and the old members are not working and the members have to be re-inducted, the matter of reconstitution of the Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
Government letter-
GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF GENERAL ADMINISTRATION, MATS. 054/31/2016/ 39, Dt. Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) at the State level was introduced on 16th September, 2016.
