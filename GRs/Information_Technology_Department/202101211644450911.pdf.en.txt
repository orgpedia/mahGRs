The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the relevant Government Decision dated 19.9.2017 regarding direct recruitment of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C posts in the Government Offices of Maharashtra State, all the departments were mandated to conduct the examination through the MahaPariksha portal. Similarly, as per the Circular dated 14th March, 2018, it was made mandatory to use the' MahaPariksha Portal' while filling up the Group-C and Group-B (Non-Gazetted) posts in the office of the Ex-Service Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) directly. Accordingly, the examinations for Group C and Group B (Non-Gazetted) posts were being conducted online through the Mahapariksha portal. Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation, Mumbai (MahaIT) was given the task to select and empanel the services provider according to the revised examination system by making necessary changes in the examination system conducted under the MahaPariksha portal. Accordingly, MahaIT has published the RFP in this regard and selected the eligible OMR Vendor through a tender process and finalized the list.
