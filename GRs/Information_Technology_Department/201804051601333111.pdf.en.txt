Introduction:
The Project Implementation Committee (PIC) of DIT has granted approval to the Request for
Expression of Interest (EOI) For Empanelment of Agencies for Mobile Application Development in
Government of Maharashtra for Period of 5 years.
Government Resolution:
The Tender Evaluation Committee (TEC) to evaluate the responses of the bidders to the
Expression of Interest (EOI) For Empanelment of Agencies for Mobile Application Development in
Government of Maharashtra for Period of 5 years is constituted as follows.
a) Director (IT) Chairman
b) Representative of NIC Member
c) Under Secretary-2 (IT) Member-Secretary
d) Section Officer-Technical (IT) Member
e) Shri Komal Kant Maski, Consultant, SeMT Member
The Tender Evaluation Committee shall evaluate the response to the EOI and all supporting
documents/documentary evidence.
The decision of the Tender Evaluation Committee in the evaluation of bids shall be final. The Tender
Evaluation Committee may ask for meetings or presentation with the bidders to seek clarifications or
conformations on their bids. Each of the responses shall be evaluated as per the criteria and requirements
specified in the EOI and its corrigendum published.
