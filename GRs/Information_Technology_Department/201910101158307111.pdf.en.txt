Appointment of Grievance Redressal Officer as per the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Directorate of Information Technology
Govt. Decision No.M. 2019/ P.S.No.148/ S- 1/39
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: October 10, 2019
See also: Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016
Government Decision: As per the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in accordance with Section 20 of the said Act, it is necessary to appoint a Grievance Redressal Officer in every establishment of the Government to resolve service related issues arising out of the disability of the concerned officers/ employees while in government service.
Accordingly, the Joint Secretary, Information Technology is being declared as the" Grievance Redressal Officer" for the Directorate of Information Technology, Science Technology Cell and Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission.
