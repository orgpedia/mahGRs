By a circular dated 02.06.2016, the General Administration Department has prescribed the criteria for submission of proposals for foreign tours of officers and officials of All India Services, State Services, Boards of Public Enterprises, Corporations, etc. to the Recommendation Committee headed by the Chief Secretary.
National e-Governance Division, Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India As per the letter dated 24.07.2019, The e-Governance Leadership Training Programme has been conducted in New Zealand. Shri S. V. R. Srinivas, IAS, Principal Secretary (IT) has been selected for this training. Therefore, the matter of approving his foreign visit was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Shri S.V.R.Srinivas, Bh. Principal Secretary, General Administration Department (Information Technology). from November 9, 2019. The Government hereby accords approval to attend The e-Governance Leadership Training Programme being organized by the National e-Governance Division, Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India in New Zealand during 19th November, 2019.
2. The entire period of foreign tour should be treated as duty period.
Travel allowance/ dearness allowance and other incidental expenses will be admissible to them during the period of the visit as per the rules prescribed by the Government.
