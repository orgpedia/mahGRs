Introduction: The Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation has issued the procedure for the supply of technical manpower under the Government Decision General Administration (Information Technology) Department No. 1717/ PR No. 111/39 dated 24/03/2017. The rates of technical manpower (other than tax) for the period from 01/04/2017 to 31/03/2018 are set out in the Annexure annexed to this Government Decision.
Since the revised rates of technical manpower for the financial year are to be announced by the Corporation before the commencement of each financial year, the matter of release of rates of technical manpower for the period from 01/4/2018 to 31/3/2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Read No. In accordance with the Government decision from 01/04/2018 to 31/03/2019, the rates of technical manpower (other than tax) for the period from 01/04/2018 to 31/03/2019 shall be as per the Annexure attached to this Government decision, for all the Ministries/ Departments, all the field offices under them, Public Undertakings, Corporations, Companies and Institutions established under the Act passed by the Central Government or State Government to hire technical manpower for IT and IT enabled services from Maharashtra IT Corporation.
