Government Resolution: The Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission has been constituted under the Maharashtra Act No. 15 of 2004 with the objective of adopting, promoting and promoting science and technology for the benefit of society in various social issues of the state. As per Rule 14 of the said Act, the Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology (RGSCST) is funded by the Consolidated Fund of the Government in the form of Grants-in-Aid every financial year.
Regular officers/ staff have been appointed in Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission with effect from May, 2018 and they are being paid salaries and allowances. For the financial year 2020-21, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head' Science and Technology Cell' Head Accounts 2251 Secretariat Social Service' for the salary and allowances of the officers/ employees of the Commission have been released under the head' Detailed Accounts' for an amount of Rs. 2,80,00, 000/- has been sanctioned.
