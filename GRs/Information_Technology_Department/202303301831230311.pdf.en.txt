Introduction A robust network of telecom infrastructure for 5G services, which is essential for the overall development of the state, needs to be set up in the state. The Central Government has amended the Telecom Policy notified by the Central Government vide notification dated 22.10.2018 and the notification in this regard has been issued vide Notification dated 17.08.2022. In view of the ever-increasing role of 5G network connectivity in the economic, social and scientific spheres, it has become important for the state of Maharashtra to formulate guidelines and implement them expeditiously in order to make 5G services faster and smoother. Therefore, the government was considering the formation of a task force committee to harmonize the telecom policy of the state in line with the reforms made by the central government in the telecom policy, the infrastructure to be built for telecom services and the effective implementation of the telecom policy.
Government Decision: A Task Force Committee consisting of members of the concerned departments dealing with the issue of Optical Fibre Cable (OFC) Rights for setting up of telecom infrastructure and permission for setting up of mobile towers is being constituted as follows.

The senior-most officer of the Task Force Committee shall be the Chairman of the Task Force Committee.
The scope and functions of the Task Force Committee shall be as follows:
I. The Working Group (Task Force Committee) will issue guidelines and suggestions from time to time for creating infrastructure for 5G services in the state.
The Task Force Committee will review the infrastructure issues related to 5G services from time to time and try to resolve them and will also act as a monitoring authority on the implementation of 5G services.
The Task Force Committee will be exclusively responsible for formulating guidelines on the use of street furniture owned by State Governments/ ULBs/ MahaDiscoms for setting up of Small Cells to be set up for rolling out 5G services.
The Task Force Committee shall periodically review the applications for right of way and erection of Telecom Tower pending on the Mahasanchar Portal. The task force committee may call the concerned authority or district for long and long pending applications and ask for reasons.
V. The Working Group (Task Force Committee) shall submit a report to the State Broadband Committee from time to time.
The committee will be informed about the latest developments and progress of 5G implementation in the state.
