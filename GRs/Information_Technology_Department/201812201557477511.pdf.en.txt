Introduction: Under the Directorate of Information Technology, No. 2. Orders have been issued regarding the modalities of the Aadhaar project to be implemented in the State as per the Government decision dated 16.9.2017. The government has issued an order for Aadhaar enrolment.
MahaOnline has been appointed as the Enrolment Agency. A proposal to appoint the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MahaIT) as the enrolment agency for Aadhaar enrolment was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 16th September, 2017, dated 02.09.2017, the Directorate of Information Technology is appointing Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MahaIT) as the Enrolment Agency for Aadhaar enrolment in the State. In the above context, for the work of Aadhaar registration. The terms and conditions specified in the Government Resolution dated 16th September, 2017 shall be applicable to MahaIT. Also, MahaIT will do the work of Aadhaar registration as per the government decision.
2. MahaIT shall take over the work from MahaOnline as per the directions of the Directorate General of Information Technology from time to time.
Both MahaOnline and MahaIT will do the work of Aadhaar enrolment. The district-wise allocation of Aadhaar enrolment work will be done by the Directorate of Information and Technology.
The transfer of workload, Aadhaar sets, and related documents, etc. between MahaOnline and MahaIT should be done under the supervision of the District Collector.
