The Rajiv Gandhi Commission on Science and Technology has been established. As per Rule 4 of the said Act, the said Commission has been reconstituted vide Government Notification No. 2. As per the Notification issued herein, the Government had appointed Shri S.G. Markandeya as the Executive Head of the Commission in the capacity of Member Secretary on contract basis. The post had fallen vacant after his sudden death. Considering the recommendations of the search committee constituted to appoint a suitable person to the said post, the matter of appointment to the post of Member Secretary was under the consideration of the Government.
Governing Decision: Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology Act Rule 4 (Maharashtra Act of 2004, Rule No. In accordance with the procedure laid down in clause (b) of section 4 (1) of the Act, Shri Anil Shrikrishna Manekar is appointed as Member Secretary, Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology.
The terms and conditions of the appointment of Shri Anil Shrikrishna Manekar will be as mentioned below.
(1) The term of appointment to the said post shall be for a period of three years.
