Introduction:
The High Power Committee (HPC) has granted approval to the Request for Proposal (RFP) for
Empanelment of Consultants for IT Projects in Government of Maharashtra for period of 5 years in its
118th meeting held on 27th Dec. 2017.
Government Resolution:
The Tender Evaluation Committee (TEC) to evaluate the responses of the bidders to the Request
for Proposal (RFP) for Empanelment of Consultants for IT Projects in Government of Maharashtra for
period of 5 years is constituted as follows.
a) Director (IT) Chairman
b) Representative of NIC Member
c) Under Secretary-2 (IT) Member
d) Section Officer-Technical (IT) Member-Secretary
e) Shri Komal Kant Maski, Consultant, SeMT Member
The Tender Evaluation Committee shall evaluate the response to the RFP and all supporting
documents/documentary evidence.
The decision of the Tender Evaluation Committee in the evaluation of bids shall be final. The Tender
Evaluation Committee may ask for meetings or presentation with the bidders to seek clarifications or
conformations on their bids. Each of the responses shall be evaluated as per the criteria and requirements
specified in the RFP and its corrigendum published.
