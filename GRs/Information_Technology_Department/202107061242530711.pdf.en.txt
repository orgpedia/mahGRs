The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure a post in the administrative service for the disabled. Accordingly, in accordance with the notification dated 04.01.2021 of the Central Government, a notification has been issued regarding ensuring the posts of various cadres in the government service for the disabled. As per the notification, dated February 2, 2021, all the administrative departments have been instructed to ensure the posts for the disabled in the state government establishments as per the government decision. Accordingly, the government has issued a decision to ensure the posts on the establishment of this office for the disabled as of February 18, 2021. However, the post of" driver" in the Directorate of Information Technology was not excluded from the list of disabled posts. The matter was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Expert Committee constituted for the purpose of determination of posts of Divyangjan in Government Services in its meeting held on 21st June, 2021 has decided to exclude the post of' Driver' in Group-C from the determination of Divyangjan posts.
