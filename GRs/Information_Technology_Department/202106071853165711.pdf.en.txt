Introduction The institutions which are setting up telecom infrastructure, mobile towers in the state are facing various problems to set up these facilities. As per the guidelines dated 01.08.2013 of Department of Telecommunications, State Level and District Level Committees have been constituted to redress grievances arising out of setting up of telecom infrastructure. A district-level core committee was formed by the government in this regard.
Government's decision:
District Level Telecom Committees are being constituted for each district under the chairmanship of the Collector to resolve any issues arising during the construction of telecom towers and infrastructure.

The functions of the District Level Telecommunication Committee shall be as follows:
The District Level Telecom Committee will resolve the issues of pending clearances for installation of new/ existing towers as well as pending clearances on Optical Fibre Cable Right of Way (OFC ROW), taxation issues and grievances related to telecom infrastructure in the district.

The District Level Telecom Committee shall hold at least one meeting every three months.
The recommendations of the District Level Telecommunication Committee shall be followed by the concerned Municipal Corporations/ Gram Panchayats/ Local Authorities and Government offices.
