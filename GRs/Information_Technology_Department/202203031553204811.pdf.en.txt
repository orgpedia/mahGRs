Government's decision:
The Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission has been established under the Maharashtra Act No. 15 of 2004 with the objective of adopting, disseminating and promoting science and technology for the benefit of society in various social issues of the state. As per Rule 14 of the said Act, the Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology (RGSCST) is funded by the Consolidated Fund of the Government in the form of Grants-in-Aid every financial year.
