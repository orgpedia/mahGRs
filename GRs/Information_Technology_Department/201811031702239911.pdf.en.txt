Introduction: Various welfare schemes are implemented by the government departments for the citizens. Integrated, interactive and analytical information on the projects of these schemes. A proposal to implement the Hon'ble Chief Minister Dashboard (CM Dashboard) project to make it available to the Chief Minister's Office was under consideration of the government. With this, all the government departments and departments will be updated with the latest information. The main objective of the project is to get the Chief Minister's vision.
The High Level Committee for implementation of the project in its meeting held on 25th October, 2018 has approved a grant of Rs. A sum of Rs. 21.85 crore has been sanctioned.
The project is being implemented by the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MahaIT), a 100 per cent owned company of the state government. MahaIT will function as a Project Implementing Agency (SIA).
In line with the above proposal, the CM Dashboard project will be implemented.
Government's decision:
The CM Dashboard project has been approved by the High Level Committee in its meeting held on October 25, 2018. Also, Rs. A sum of Rs. 21.85 crore has been sanctioned.
