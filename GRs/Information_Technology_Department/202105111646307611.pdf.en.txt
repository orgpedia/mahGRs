The Proposal:
The decision to adopt e-tendering system for government procurement works, etc. was taken under the government decision in Article No. 1. Subsequently, the price ceiling in respect of the tender was decided in terms of the Government Decision in Articles 2 and 3. As per the Government decision under Articles 4 and 5, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour has prescribed a revised procedure for procurement. Accordingly, the following guidelines are being issued regarding the e-tendering process.
Government's decision
Ministry of Industry, Energy and Labour. Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 01/12/2016
Revised procedures for office purchases to be made by government departments have been prescribed. Further, as mentioned in paragraph No.5 of the Purchase Manual, now under reference. 3.2.3 is amended to provide for a monetary ceiling of Rs. 3 lakh (Rs. 3,00,000/-) Rs. 10 lakh (Rs. 10,00,000/-) inclusive of all taxes has been decided to be increased. Accordingly, an e-tender was issued by this Department in this regard
