The Proposal:
To encourage the best performing institutions in the field of Information Technology in the state of Maharashtra by giving them awards. July 7, 2021. The following decisions were taken in the meeting chaired by the Minister of State (ICT):
The use of science and technology for the social and economic development of India is central to modernization.
Therefore, on the occasion of the Memorial Day of Late Shri Rajiv Gandhi, the Government of India has decided to promote institutions that are innovating in the field of Information Technology and contributing to the overall development of the society. The award is named after Rajiv Gandhi.
As per the decision taken in the above meeting, the matter of encouraging the best performing institutions in the field of Information Technology in the state of Maharashtra in the name of former Prime Minister of India Late Shri Rajiv Gandhi was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation, Mumbai (MahaIT) is being selected to propose this award in the name of former Prime Minister of India, late Shri Rajiv Gandhi in the state of Maharashtra in a non-financial form through the General Administration Department, Information Technology Sub-Division.
Accordingly, the Maharashtra Information and Technology Corporation, Mumbai (MahaIT) has decided to provide Rs.
The following action should be taken in respect of awarding the best performing institutions in the field of Information Technology in the state of Maharashtra in the name of Rajiv Gandhi:
(a) This year. The award was announced on August 20, 2021. The award should be given by finalizing the criteria and selection of the institution through Maharashtra Information and Technology Corporation (MahaIT) before October 30, 2021.
2) Every year from next year. The award will be presented on August 20, the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi.
