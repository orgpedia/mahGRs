The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in the context, the Government has established the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation.
In this government decision, the organizational structure of the corporation has been fixed. Accordingly, on the institutional structure of the corporation, the matter of appointing an expert person in the field of information technology and an expert person related to the education sector as a nominated director was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The following are being appointed as Nominated Directors on Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation:
1) Mr. Hemant Dabke (Expert in the field of Information Technology) Nominated Director.
2) Shri R.K. Mathai, (Academician) Nominated Director.
