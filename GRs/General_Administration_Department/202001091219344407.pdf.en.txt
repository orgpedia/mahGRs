Allotment of the new administrative building building, second floor and ground floor of the office of the Directorate of Small Savings, Mumbai to the Directorate of Maharashtra State Lotteries.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department,
GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. SHAKAJA-1119/ PR. 139/2019/22,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: January 9, 2020
Preamble: Due to the permanent closure of the Directorate of Small Savings under the administrative control of the Finance Department, the office of the Directorate of Small Savings, Mumbai, has been shifted to a new administrative building at Chembur, 2nd Floor, 942 sq. ft. and 583 sq. The total area is 1525 square meters. The proposal to allot the plot to the Maharashtra State Lottery Directorate was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government have now decided as follows: GOVERNMENT DECISION: The new administrative building of the office of the Directorate of Small Savings, Mumbai, at Chembur, 2nd Floor, 942 S.O. ft. and 583 sq. The total area is 1525 square meters. The seat is being allotted to the Maharashtra State Lottery Directorate.
Executive Engineer, North Mumbai (WB) Division, Andheri (Pt. ), Mumbai should give the possession of the above-mentioned place to the concerned and submit a report to the government.
