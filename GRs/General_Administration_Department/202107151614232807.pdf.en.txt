Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department of the state government has issued a notification in this regard. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government Order dated 5.7.2021, the posts for the disabled category in the Group-A to Group-D category in the Office of the Secretary to the Governor and the Office of the Family Manager in the Raj Bhavan Secretariat under the General Administration Department have been ensured. Apart from this, the Principal Secretary (Social Justice) has been asked to exclude some posts.

The Expert Committee under the Chairmanship has approved in its meeting dated 7.7.2021. Accordingly, the government has taken the decision.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the meeting held on 7.7.2021, the Expert Committee constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Social Justice, regarding the reservation of posts for the disabled, has approved the exclusion of 02 posts in the Office of the Secretary to the Governor and the Office of the Family Manager in the Raj Bhavan Secretariat from the posts specified for the disabled.
