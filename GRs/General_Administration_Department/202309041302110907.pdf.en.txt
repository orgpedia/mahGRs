Government Circular: A comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. 1 has been prescribed by the government here.
Accordingly, the temporary seniority of Assistant Director (Information)/ Information Officer/ Superintendents, Books and Publications Group-B officers of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations up to 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022, and 01.01.2023 has been increased from the existing period of reference no. 2 was published here. The names of the officers who were promoted during the period mentioned above, including the names of officers who were appointed through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route and excluding the names of officers who retired voluntarily and those who retired on regular basis, were included in Annexure-A and the names of the officers who were promoted ad-hoc as they fulfilled the terms and conditions for ad-hoc promotion were included in Annexure-B and the provisional seniority as on 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022 and 01.01.2023 was published on the website.
According to clause 3 (3) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, there is no need to publish an ad-hoc seniority list.
