Government Circular No: Sep-2022/ PR No.21/ Q-17, dated 28th November, 2022 The schedule and important instructions for the year 2022 examination of Assistant Cell Officers and Assistant Cell Officers (Law) in the ministerial cadre and Maharashtra Public Service Commission have been published through a circular under the circular.
The details of the candidates who have appeared in the examination earlier but have failed in some subjects are attached with this circular. However, the candidates concerned should ensure that they appear for the papers of only those subjects in which they have failed.
