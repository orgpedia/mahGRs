Preamble: Emergency was declared in India from 25.6.1975 to 31.3.1977. A policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period by providing financial assistance to them. Sadar
The matter of sanctioning honorarium to eligible persons under the policy is under consideration.
Government's decision:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No. 1018/ PR No. 206/2018/ SWASAC-1
Disbursement of funds required to implement the policy of honouring/ giving due respect to the persons who were imprisoned for fighting for democracy during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977
As stated in the statement, the provision of the scheme has been approved by the Supplementary Demand for Demand No. A-05 Principal Accounts Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 102- Pension under Social Security, (00) (05) Financial assistance to persons fighting for democracy during the period of emergency (mandatory) 50 Other expenses (2235 C205). This amount is Rs. 42,00,0, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crores only) which is required to be disbursed to 5 districts of the State (Aurangabad, Nanded, Gondia, Ratnagiri and Jalna). As per the list of eligible applicants submitted by the respective District Collectors to the Government, an amount of Rs. 2,87,25, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Eighty Seven Lakh Twenty Five Thousand only) for the 15 months from January 2018 to March 2019 is being disbursed as per the amount of grant shown against the names of their districts.
2. All District Collectors shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grants released to them and is spent only for matters approved by the Government. Also, the subsidy should be spent according to the monthly fund distribution system.
(3) Expenditure for the current financial year. A-5,2235-Social Security and Welfare, 102-Pension under Social Security Scheme, (00) (05) Financial Assistance to Persons Fighting for Democracy during Emergency (Mandatory) 50 Other Expenditure (2235C205) to be met from the approved budgetary provision for the financial year 2018-19 under the head of this account and to be spent under the same head.
The honorarium should be sanctioned to the eligible applicant from 02.01.2018 as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 03.07.2018.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the approval given by the Department of Finance vide Non-official Reference No. 8/2019/ Expenditure-4 dated 03.01.2019.
