Government Refinement: -
Read above here Reference No. Ex-servicemen and soldier widows / spouses in the state of Maharashtra are being exempted from property tax under Government Decision No. 1. Paragraph 2A of the said Government Decision states, "In order to avail the benefits of this scheme, the ex-serviceman must be born in the State of Maharashtra or belong to the State of Maharashtra."
Must be a continuous resident for at least 15 years. "It said.
Instead of Reference No. 1 The Government decision herein shall save the condition in paragraph 2A as follows: -
The ex-serviceman must be a native of the state of Maharashtra by birth to avail the benefits of this scheme.
Or their parents / grandparents must be domiciled in the state of Maharashtra. Ex-servicemen who have migrated to the state of Maharashtra from other states for at least 15 consecutive years after retirement from military service in the state of Maharashtra
Should have resided and worked in any other State Soldier Welfare Office during this period.
The ex-serviceman must not have an ID card. "
