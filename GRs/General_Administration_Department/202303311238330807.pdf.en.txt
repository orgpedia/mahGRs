Regarding the release of the list of papers which are allowed to be carried along with the books, government decisions and notifications for the departmental examination for promotion to the post of Cell Officer.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No.: Narrow/ 2323/ Q. No.12/ Q. 17
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date: 31st of March, 2023.
Read- Government Resolution No. Vipani-2722/ No. 8/ 17, D. As of April 5, 2022
Government Circular- Syllabus of Departmental Examination for promotion to the post of Cell Officer under reference. The decision of the Government dated 5 th April, 2022 has been amended and the examination is conducted accordingly. Copies of books, government decisions and notifications have been allowed for some papers in this departmental examination.
However, it is necessary to publish the list as there is confusion among the candidates as to which books are required to be brought with them at the time of the relevant paper. Accordingly, a list of books, government decisions and notifications to be carried at the time of paper is being published as per the annexure attached to this government circular.
Candidates are advised to note that no other Government Decision/ Notification other than the Government Decision and Notification mentioned in the Annexure shall be allowed to be carried along.
However, it is requested that the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and the Department of Law and Justice should publish a list of documents permissible for the Assistant Cell Officer in the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and the Assistant Cell Officer (Law) in the Department of Law and Justice for a separate paper for the respective subject.
