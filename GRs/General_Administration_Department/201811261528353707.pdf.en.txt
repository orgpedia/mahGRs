The Right to Information Act, 2005 provides for making records available to citizens for perusal.

the General Administration Department

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 4000 032
The date: November 26, 2018
Preamble: In order to reduce the number of RTI applications received under the Right to Information Act, 2005 and to bring transparency in the work, the Pune Municipal Corporation has issued a circular No.
Under the order dated 31.7.2009, MA/ S/ 1062, an experiment was carried out to make the records available to the citizens for perusal. On the same lines, the government was considering making records available to citizens for review from the district level office to all lower level offices.
Government Circular: In order to bring more transparency in government affairs and to reduce the number of information applications, first and second appeals received under the Right to Information Act, all offices in the state, from district level offices to lower level offices as well as municipal corporations, municipalities, zilla parishads, etc. should make available the records for perusal of citizens on the following office days from 3.00 pm to 5.00 pm on every Monday or if the day is a public holiday, as per the procedure prescribed under the Right to Information Act, 2005, as per their demand.
(ii) Each Head of Office shall implement in his office the practice of making available to the public for perusal of records with necessary corrections in accordance with the local conditions.
