Preamble: The State Government 4th Class Employees Central Association, Mumbai District has given a memorandum to the Central Government regarding their various demands. The two-day strike was announced on September 21 and 22, 2017. The employees have been asked not to participate in the strike. The government was considering how to regularize the absence of government employees who participated in the strike.
GOVERNMENT Circular: The State Government 4th Class Employees Central Association, Mumbai District has given a memorandum regarding their various demands. Absence of Government officers/ employees who participated in the strike called for two days on 21st and 22nd September, 2017 be regularized as" Extraordinary Leave." However, the period of such extraordinary leave shall be subject to the decision of the Government in the Finance Department, No. Seniway 1001/29/ SEWA 4, Dy. With the exception of the order dated 14.01.2001, the service should be treated as qualifying service for pension, irrespective of the length of service.
