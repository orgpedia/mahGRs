GOVERNMENT DECISION, FINANCE DEPARTMENT NO.1013/ PR No.17/ SERVICE-3 dated 10.7.2013
Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 10.7.2013, a provision has been made for sanction of consolidated lump sum compensation to the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary and equivalent officers in the Department of Finance at a fixed rate of pay as per the grade. In the same manner. The matter of sanctioning bulk compensation to the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (V.V.M.) and Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Education) of the Governor was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary in the Governor's Office (viz. The Government has decided to pay monthly consolidated lump sum compensation to the VCs and Joint Secretaries/ Deputy Secretaries (Education) in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision, Finance Department dated 10.7.2013 and the terms and conditions thereof in respect of use of residential telephone, internet (residential mobile), cash allowance in lieu of residential peon, newspapers, magazines, magazines, residential cable, residential computer and its maintenance, laptop, iPad and its maintenance, data card, residential printer, toner for printer and other stationery and equipment.
If the concerned officer comes on deputation to the post of Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (V.V.M.) and Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Education) in the Raj Bhavan, the above amount of lump sum compensation will be admissible to the concerned officer for the period of deputation. After the officer concerned is transferred to his original post outside the Raj Bhavan, the allowances admissible to him in his original post shall continue to be admissible.
This Government Resolution is being issued pursuant to the concurrence received under the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. NO. 504/18/ Service-3, dated 19.11.2018.
