Government circular
The provisional additional provisional seniority of Selected Category Scribes in the Ministry Department as on 01.01.2019 has been annexed to this circular at Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to furnish the date of submission of the shortlisted short-listed candidates on 01.01.2019.
The selection of the additional provisional seniority in your department should be brought to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the shorthand cadre. In this regard, the objections/ errors received from the concerned selected category scribes should be examined as per the records of the department and sent to this department along with the department's opinion by 30.04.2019. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
