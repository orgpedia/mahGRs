The Provisional Common Seniority of Lower Grade Scribes in the Ministry Department as on 01.01.2021 was notified vide Government Circular dated 13.1.2021. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 31.1.2021.
However, no errors/ objections have been received from the Ministries/ Departments. Therefore, the said seniority is being made ad-interim and is annexed to this circular at Annexure-" A" and" B." The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
