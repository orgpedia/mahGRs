The above officers will be transferred to their place of posting with effect from 30th June, 2023 (M.No.). 3 has been removed from the post. These officers should immediately join the post mentioned in column 4 in front of their name and submit the necessary report to all the concerned offices and the government without any delay.
Further, in order to ensure that there is no administrative difficulty in the wake of the said transfer, due care should be taken to give additional charge as per the provisions of the General Administration Department, Government Circular No. SRV 2018/ PR No. 208/ Function-12 dated 5th September, 2018.
These orders will come into effect immediately. Any request for change in the postings of the transferred officers will not be entertained and any pressure in this regard will invite disciplinary action under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
