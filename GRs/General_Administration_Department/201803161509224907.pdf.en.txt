The Proposal:
The government order to the Deputy Secretary of the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench, Mr. A.M. Sable,
General Administration Department, No. S.P. 1117/ Q.No.124/ K. 14D. to the post of Joint Secretary vide order dated 1.1.2018
Considering Mr. Sable's experience in the Information Commission and the vacancies of officers/ employees in the Konkan Bench of the State Information Commission, the post of Deputy Secretary there has been given along with the post of Deputy Secretary.
The matter of elevation to the post of secretary was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision
1) The salary of the State Information Commissioner, Konkan Bench, Navi Mumbai is Rs. 15600-39100+ Grade Pay Rs. 7600/- for one post in the cadre of Deputy Secretary Rs. The pay scale of the post of Joint Secretary is being upgraded to 37400-67000+ Grade Pay 8700.
(2) The above arrangement is temporary in nature and shall continue to be in force till the time Mr. A.M. Sable, Joint Secretary is in office. Thereafter, the post will automatically be downgraded to the parent cadre of Deputy Secretary.
This Government decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 72/2018/ SERVICE-9 of the Department of Finance. being issued pursuant to the Consent received vide 6.3.2018.
