Government circular
In accordance with the Government Notification General Administration Department No. RTR 1013/ PR No. 50/13/14-A, dated 21.02.2018, the educational qualification for appointment by promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in the ministerial cadre from Group-D cadre in the ministerial department with effect from 21.02.2018 is Graduation. Thereafter, the General Administration Department, vide Government Notification No. RTR 1013/ PR No. 50/13/14-A, dated 23-01-2020, has notified the Clerk-Typist, Group-C (Entry into Service) (Amendment) Rules, 2020 in the Brihanmumbai Government Office, as per the proviso therein, the educational qualification for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist from Group-D to Group-C up to the date 20-02-2023.
It has also been amended so that employees who have passed the certificate examination in the school can also be considered for promotion.
