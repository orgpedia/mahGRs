General Administration Department, Govt. The Training Policy for all Government Officers/ Employees has been framed vide Notification dated 23.09.2011. The General Administration Department, Govt. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022 have been notified w.e.f. 02.02.2022. As per the rules, basic, revision and post-promotion training has been made mandatory for all government officials/ employees. The government has taken the decision. As per the State Training Policy dated 23.09.2011, the duration of post-promotional training for Group-B has been fixed at two weeks.
Accordingly, a 12-day post-promotion training has been organized by Marathwada Administrative and Development Training Prabodhini, Aurangabad between 31.10.2022 and 11.11.2022. A list of the officers nominated for training is attached.
The training is being imparted as per the State Training Policy and Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, and attendance is mandatory. Regarding the training, the government has issued a circular. The suggestions dated 13th May, 2015 should be brought to the notice of the concerned.

The Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, provide for withholding of increments for officers who have not completed their post-promotion training. This should be brought to the notice of all the concerned officials.
The officer nominated for training shall be appointed on the day before the commencement of the training. On 30.10.2022 upto 6.00 pm or on the first day of training upto 9.00 am on 31.10.2022 at Marathwada Administrative and Development Training Prabodhini, Nathnagar (North), Dist. They should be released on the condition that they reach Paithan, Aurangabad.
The concerned department should immediately inform the trainees about the training period keeping in mind the need for reservation for travel. It is mandatory for the nominated cell officers to be relieved by the respective department heads and a copy of the order of retirement should be sent to the Director, Marathwada Administrative and Development Training Prabodhini, Aurangabad, as well as to this office.
