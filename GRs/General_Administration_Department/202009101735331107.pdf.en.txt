General Administration Department at Reference No. 2, Govt. As on 27th September, 2011, a consolidated review of vehicles at the establishments of General Administration Department (GAD) and Subordinate Offices has been approved. The decision also includes review of vehicles under the office of Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta, among other offices.
At present, the number of posts of Deputy Lok Ayukta in the office has been increased from 1 to 2 and 1 (car) vehicle has been made permissible for the additional post of Deputy Lok Ayukta by the State Level Vehicle Review Committee. Therefore, it is necessary to issue a revised vehicle review order in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision at Reference 1. However, the revised vehicle review of the office of Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta, Mumbai is being approved as follows.
