Subject: Reference regarding reservation in promotion to Group A and Group B posts for differently abled employees: S.P.V., Government Resolution No. Divyang 2015/ PR No. 171/16 A, Dy. 05.07.2021
As per the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, an order has been issued to provide reservation in promotion to the employees with disabilities in Group A and Group B posts. Under this order, instructions have been given to provide 4 per cent reservation in promotion to the posts identified for appointment in Group-A and Group-B posts in the government service. Accordingly, a total of 4 per cent reservation has been provided for A, B and E categories of persons with disabilities. The order is applicable to all government and semi-government offices, boards, corporations, local bodies, district councils, municipal corporations, municipalities, government-aided institutions and all institutions and services to which the government has the power to issue orders.
As per the said order, the administrative departments and their subordinate offices are expected to take immediate action for promotion to the posts of the cadre in which the posts have been ensured for the disabled. Also during the process. High Court dated 29.06.2021 and Dt. Orders dated 08.07.2021 are to be considered. Therefore, the selection list should be prepared for 100 percent of the posts for promotion to the vacant posts. While preparing this selection list, action should be taken as per the provision prescribed in the relevant government decision to implement the Divyang reservation. However, at the time of promotion, non-disabled officers should be promoted to 50 per cent of the posts and in addition, eligible disabled officers in the selection list should be promoted to the remaining 50% of the total vacancies as per the 4 per cent reservation implemented for promotion for the disabled.
Candidates who are retiring at the end of July, 2021 as per the prescribed age limit, but are not eligible for promotion against 50% vacancies, may be promoted by excluding the last candidate in the 50% limit.
