Lok Sabha General Elections 2019: Allowance for non-gazetted work done by non-gazetted employees of the election branch at the divisional, district and tehsil levels appointed for the purpose of Lok Sabha General Elections
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. CEL-2019/ PR No. 579/19/33 (2)
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date is May 31, 2019.
The Lok Sabha elections in Maharashtra will be held in four phases on April 11, 18, 23 and 29. During the period from the announcement of the Lok Sabha General Election Schedule on 10th March, 2019 to the completion of the election process on 27th May, 2019, the working class in the Election Branches at the Ministerial, Divisional, District and Taluka level had to work extra days, including holidays, to complete the work related to the Lok Sabha General Election within the stipulated time. Accordingly, the General Administration Department, Divisional Commissioner's Offices, Election Offices of all District Collectors, Tehsil Offices, Non-Gazetted staff engaged for election work in General Administration Department, Chief Electoral Officer's Office and District Election Officer's Office, Additional Non-Gazetted staff drawn for election work from other departments/ District Establishments, are being sanctioned to pay ex-gratia allowance as per the following criteria, subject to the following conditions:
(1) The rate of overtime allowance shall be paid in proportion to the basic pay for every full hour of extra work done on any working day (if more than half an hour or half an hour of work is done in a full hour).
Note: For determining the rate of pay per hour, the month shall be treated as 30 days and for Group B (Non-Gazetted) and Group C employees, the days shall be treated as 7 hours while for Class IV employees, the daily working hours shall be treated as 8 hours in Mumbai and 9 hours elsewhere
(2) Terms and Conditions- (1) The overtime allowance shall be paid at the hourly rate in proportion to the basic pay pay payable as per the 7th Pay Commission for the month of April 2019. Also, while calculating the rate of daily wages, it does not include any other allowance (e.g. House Rent Allowance, Dearness Allowance, Local Supplementary Allowance etc.) will not be included.
(2) Overtime allowance shall be admissible for work done in excess of normal office hours and shall be payable only for the period from 10th March, 2019 to 27th May, 2019 (both days inclusive).
(3) Overtime allowance shall be paid for any election-related work done on holidays during the above period.

(4) Overtime allowance shall be paid in the above manner to the employees of other institutions appointed for election at the level of Ministry, Division, District and Tehsil.
(5) The maximum limit of overtime allowance shall be equal to the basic pay pay payable to them as per the 7th Pay Commission for the month of April 2019. Any other (e.g. House Rent Allowance, Dearness Allowance, Local Supplementary Allowance etc.) will not be included.
(6) Employees who travel outside the headquarters for election work shall not be paid overtime allowance in addition to the travel/ daily allowance as per rules. The employees will have the option to avail the benefit of both the allowances.
(7) The Gazetted Officers under whose supervision the election work is conducted shall certify the daily period of extra work done by the staff for overtime allowance on the basis of daily attendance.
(8) No overtime allowance shall be paid to the staff engaged on daily wages for election work.
Expenditure on these items may be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20 under the head Demand No.A-2, Principal Head 2015-Elections, Sub-head 105 Expenditure on Election to Parliament, (00) (01) Expenditure on Election to Parliament, (13) Office Expenditure (20150059). The Collector and the District Election Officer should separately demand the grant considering the honorarium/ overtime allowance on the basis of this government decision.
This Government Resolution is being issued in concurrence with the Department of Finance vide Non-official Reference No: Anusam- 117/19/ Service- 6/9 dated 27.05.2019 and Non-official Reference No: Anusam- 164/2019/ Expenditure-4 dated 31.05.2019.
