The Proposal: -
The office of the Chief Electoral Officer requires immediate expenditure on a number of election-related matters. Since such matters are not included in the Financial Rights Manual, it has to be approved by the Finance Department, which is not possible in the short term. The Department is, therefore, empowered under the Government Decision dated 19.06.2014 to approve expenditure on routine matters relating to elections.
The decision was taken by the government. The financial powers granted to the administrative division under the 19.06.2014 provision are limited as of 2014. It was now under the consideration of the Government to be revised / augmented with time.
The Government's Decisions:
The Department is empowered to approve expenditure on matters relating to elections under the Fiscal Rights Manual, Part-II vide Government Decision dated 19th June, 2014. It hereby approves the extension of the limitation of the power of 03 financial powers mentioned in Annexure A, No. 2, 5B8, dated 19th June, 2014, as indicated in the accompanying "Form-A." It is also approving amendments as indicated in the accompanying "Form-B" in the 03 Financial Strength Description in front of A.No. 9, 13B17 of the said Annexure.
If the expenditure is expected to exceed the financial limit prescribed in Column 4 of the accompanying Form A and Form B or if any of the prescribed conditions is not met, such proposal shall require the financial approval of the Finance Department (the concerned Expenditure Board).
