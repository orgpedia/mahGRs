the order
Shri Nitin Gaikwad, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of two years as Chief Administrative Officer in the State Police Complaints Authority under the Home Department subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Shri Nitin Gaikwad, Under Secretary, will commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date he enters upon his office of Deputy Commissioner and shall cease to hold office on the date he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Department of Industries, Energy and Labour and the Department of Home Affairs shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation of Shri Nitin Gaikwad after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Nitin Gaikwad, Under Secretary will be handled by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour.
4. Industries, Energy and Labour Department to relieve Mr. Nitin Gaikwad, Under Secretary to join the place of deputation. If Mr. Gaikwad's services have been requisitioned for the Lok Sabha Elections 2019, he will be required to perform the duties assigned to him for election work in the first place, only after which he will be relieved for deputation.
