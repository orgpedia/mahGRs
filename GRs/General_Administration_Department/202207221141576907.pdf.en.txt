Subject: To fill up the posts of 1 Deputy Secretary for Kolhapur Sub-Centre, 1 Under-Secretary for Latur Sub-Centre and 6 Under-Secretaries for 6 other Divisional Offices under Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune on deputation.
To call for the willingness of the Deputy Secretary and Under Secretary of the Ministry.
Mr. and Mrs.,
In pursuance of the above subject, it is hereby notified that the posts mentioned in the prospectus-A for the Kolhapur Sub-Centre of Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Research, Training and Human Development (SAARTHI), Pune under the Department of Planning, 1 Deputy Secretary (S-25: 78,800-2, 09,200) for Latur Sub-Centre and 6 Under-Secretaries (S- 23:67,700- 2,08,70) for the 6 Divisional Offices at Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Amravati, Aurangabad and Pune are to be filled up by deputation from the officers of the cadre of Deputy Secretary and Under-Secretary in the Department of Ministries.
Accordingly, all the administrative departments of the Ministry are requested to furnish the details of the names of the Deputy Secretary and Under Secretary of their department who are willing to go on deputation to the posts mentioned in the attached return-A in the 2 Sub-Centres and 6 Divisional Offices under Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune, along with the certified copies of their confidential reports for the last five years, bio-data, complete service details (duration, department, post), no-departmental inquiry certificate and no-objection certificate of the department, etc. It should be sent in a manner that it is received by 05.08.2022. Also, if any officer is not interested, he should inform this department.
