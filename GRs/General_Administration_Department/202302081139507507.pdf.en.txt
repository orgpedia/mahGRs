Preamble: As mentioned in the Government Circular at (1) above, for the financial year 2022-23, under the Head of Accounts 2059, Preservation and Maintenance of Martyr's Memorials (Mandatory), 2059 0015, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 20,60,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) has been approved. So far, 42% of the budgeted allocation of Rs. 8,65,200/- (Rupees Eight Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand Two Hundred only) has been received directly through the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS). Out of this, 35% of the funds have been disbursed to the Collector offices under the Government decision in the above reference numbers (3) and (4). Also, a proposal is under consideration of the Government to hand over the remaining 7% funds to the respective District Collectors for the financial year 2022-23.
Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, under the head of head accounts 2059, preservation and maintenance of martyr memorials (mandatory) 2059 0015, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 20,60,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 1,44,200/- (Rs. One lakh forty four thousand two hundred only) is being disbursed to the concerned District Collectors for the preservation and maintenance of the Martyrs Memorials in the proportion of the Martyrs Memorials shown parallel to their district in the statement A attached with the Government Decision.
(2) The said fund shall be spent for the maintenance of the memorial and for the maintenance of the buildings of the Martyrs' Memorial as well as the lighting and gardening facilities in their premises, in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision referred to in clause (2). Accordingly, all the district collectors should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the subsidy disbursed to them, as well as that the expenditure is made only for the matter approved by the government, and the utility certificate of the amount spent should be submitted to the government from time to time.
