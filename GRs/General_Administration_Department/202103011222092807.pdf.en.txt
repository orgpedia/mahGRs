DECISION- 2016/ PR No. 08/16/ APC, dated 15/02/2021 GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby approve the continuation of 77 (Seventy Seven) temporary posts mentioned in the Annexure in the offices of Sainik Welfare Department, Maharashtra State, Pune and in the offices of Zila Sainik Welfare Offices, Boys/ Girls Hostels, Sainik Rest Homes, Pre-Student Training Centre, Nashik under the control of this office for a period of 6 months from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021.
(a) The expenditure for the post mentioned in Annexure (a) to this Government Resolution shall be covered under the head Demand No. A-5, Head of Accounts 2235 Social Security and Welfare, (60) Other Social Security and Welfare Programmes, (200) Other Programmes, (00) (03) Department of Sainik Welfare (2235 0051).
(b) The expenditure for the posts mentioned in serial numbers 02 to 64, under the head Demand No.A-5, Accounts Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare (60) Other Social Security and Welfare Programmes, (200) Other Programmes (00) (02) Zila Sainik Welfare Offices (2235 0042), shall be covered under this head.
The expenditure for the posts mentioned in serial number 65 and 66 shall be covered under the head Demand No. A-5, Account Head 2235, Social Security and Welfare (60) Other Social Security and Welfare Programmes, (200) Other Programmes (00) (01) Pre-Student Training Scheme (2235 0033).
The expenditure on the said posts in all the above offices should be met from the sanctioned grants for the financial year 2021-2022 under the various headings indicated above.
