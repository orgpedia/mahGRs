GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government have decided to provide grant-in-aid to the Advertising Agencies of Category A, B and C listed under the Government Decision dated 16th March, 2020 of the General Administration Department. The deadline for the publication of works from January 1, 2023 to March 31, 2023 is being extended. The extension is being granted subject to the condition that the list is prepared afresh by adopting the prescribed procedure and guidelines given under various government decisions of the government for the functioning of class A, B and C institutions and in any case the list is approved by the government by the last week of February 2023.
The procedure regarding the applicability of the selection list shall be as prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 16th March, 2020 in the said Reference No. 5.
