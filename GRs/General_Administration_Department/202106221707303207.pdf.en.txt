Government's decision:
Medical Examination to be conducted by the officers of the Indian Administrative Service for the year 2020-21. Orders have been issued under the Government Decision dated 09.04.2021. The instructions for conducting these medical examinations up to 30.06.2021 have been issued as per the decision of the concerned Government.
In view of the outbreak of COVID pandemic, vide Reference No. 2, Department of Personnel& Training, Government of India, has issued a timeline for conducting medical examination for the year 2020-21 by the officers of All India Services. It is proposed to be extended up to 31.12.2021.
Accordingly, for the year 2020-21, the deadline for conducting medical examinations for IAS officers of Maharashtra cadre who are 40 years of age or above has been extended. It is being extended up to 31.12.2021.
