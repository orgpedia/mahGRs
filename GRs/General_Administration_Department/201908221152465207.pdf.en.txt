Government's decision
As per the decision of the government, the revised point list with SEBC and EWS reservation has been prescribed for direct recruitment to the posts in Group C and Group D at the district level in the tribal-dominated districts of Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Palghar, Yavatmal, Raigad, Chandrapur and Gadchiroli, where the population of Scheduled Tribes is mainly high.
However, considering the objections raised by the people's representatives in this regard, the decision is being postponed until further orders of the government.
