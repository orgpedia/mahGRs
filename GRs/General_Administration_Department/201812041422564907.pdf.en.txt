Government Circular, General Administration Department, No.-1196/ Q.No. 5/96/29, dated 18th September 1996.
Government Circular: As per Government Circular, General Administration Department No. Sarvasu-1196/ PR No. 5/96/29, dated 18th September, 1996, it has been agreed to declare' Anant Chaturdashi' as a local holiday every year in Government/ Semi-Government Offices in Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban Districts. In 2019, Anant Chaturdashi Thursday, September 12, 2019, is being declared a local holiday for all government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban districts.
These orders will be applicable to government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai city and Mumbai suburban districts.
This local holiday is being declared in accordance with the provisions of Government Decision No. P& S. No. P-13/ I/ B, dated November 5, 1958.
