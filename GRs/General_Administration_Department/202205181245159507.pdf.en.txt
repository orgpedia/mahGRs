Government circular
In accordance with the Government Notification General Administration Department No. RTR 1013/ PR No. 50/13/14-A, dated 21.02.2018, the educational qualification for appointment by promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in the ministerial cadre from Group-D cadre in the ministerial department with effect from 21.02.2018 is Graduation. Thereafter, the General Administration Department, vide Government Notification No. RTR 1013/ PR No. 50/13/14-A, dated 23-01-2020, has notified the" Clerk-Typist" Group-C (Entry into Service) (Amendment) Rules, 2020 in the Brihanmumbai Government Offices and as per the proviso therein, the educational qualification for promotion from Group-D to the post of Clerk Typist in Group-C has been revised as on 20-02-2023 in such a manner that the employees who have passed the Certificate Examination in Secondary School should also be considered for promotion.
(2) The number of employees of Group D cadre in the Ministry Department, who have qualified for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in Group C and have applied for promotion. Provisional seniority as on 01.01.2022 has been prepared.
3. Those who have qualified and applied for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in the list as per the said seniority, d. Group-D employees who have completed at least 3 years of regular service in Group-D as on 01.01.2022, have passed graduation or equivalent examination or certificate examination in secondary school and possess the required speed of typing (40 words per minute in English or 30 words in Marathi). All the employees who have qualified for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in Group-D cadre and have applied for promotion should be brought to the notice of the Chief Minister. The General Administration Department vide Government Circular No. Binduna 1018/ Q.No. 54/14-A, Dy. As per the instructions dated 05.12.2018, if the name of an employee is to be included in the list according to seniority, then the concerned employees should be instructed to submit a statement through the department. After carefully examining the facts/ objections in the representations of the employees as well as the information in column 3 to 15 of the list as per seniority, the errors or factual errors in the list as per seniority (if any employee's name remains to be included in the list as per seniority) should be brought to the notice of this Department before June 10, 2022, along with the feedback of the Department, by the concerned departments after checking the service chart and other available records of the employees so that it can be verified and necessary changes can be made in the list as per seniority.
Otherwise, further action will be taken in the sense that no changes are suggested to the concerned.
Objections received after June 10, 2022 will not be considered.
6. All the Ministries/ Departments may obtain the copy of the list in order of seniority from the website and inform all the employees who have qualified for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in the Group-D cadre of their department and have applied for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist that the copy of the list in order of seniority should be made available on the website.
