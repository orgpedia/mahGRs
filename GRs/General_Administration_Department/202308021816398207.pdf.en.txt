Government circular:
As per the All India Services (Performance Appraisal Report) Rules, 2007, the Additional Chief Secretary, Administrative Reforms and Structure is required to fix targets for the respective offices at the beginning of the year for the purpose of writing performance appraisal reports for the functions under the scheme.
The matter of fixing KRA for General Administration Department for the year 2023-24 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the KRAs for the year 2023-24 are being fixed as under:
