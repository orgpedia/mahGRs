the order
Mrs. Pratima Chaudhari, Cell Officer, Public Works Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially to the post of" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Civil Services, Brihanmumbai under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mrs. Pratima Chaudhry, Officer of the Cell shall commence the service of the Depute on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Depute, and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent Department, cease to hold the charge of the Office of the Depute after one year.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of their deputation, if the Government considers their services necessary in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Public Works Department and the General Administration Department should decide the terms and conditions regarding the deputation of Mrs. Pratima Chaudhary after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Pratima Chaudhary, Cell Officer will be handled by the Public Works Department.
4. Mrs. Pratima Chaudhary, Cell Officer to be relieved immediately by the Public Works Department for accepting the appointment on deputation.
