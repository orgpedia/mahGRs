Government circular:
The reference No. 1 As per Rule 2 (d) of the Government Notification at Reference No. 3 here. Interim Common Seniority Notice dated 01.01.2021 has been issued to eligible Assistant Cell Officers for promotion to the post of Cell Officer vide Government Circular dated 12.05.2022.
2. Refer No. 2 As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 01.01.2022, the eligible Assistant Cell Officers who have been continuously serving in the rank of Assistant Cell Officer for more than 5 years as per the ratio (from the revised date of seniority in respect of the Assistant Cell Officers who have lost their original seniority) and who have passed the departmental examination prescribed for promotion to the post of Cell Officer or who are exempted from passing the said examination, the provisional additional seniority as on 01.01.2022 has been prepared and attached to this circular.
3. As per the rules of admission to the service of the Assistant Cell Officer cadre, eligible clerks and typists are promoted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the promotion quota of 30% of the seats.
Accordingly, the process of regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is underway. Regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer

As soon as the process of giving is completed, the Assistant Cell Officer of the promoted quota will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.
The Assistant Cell Officers at Nos. 1& 24 will be included as per the seniority number in the Interim Common Seniority List as on 01.01.2021 and the Assistant Cell Officers at Nos. 25 to 82 will be included after the last seniority number in the seniority list as on 01.01.2021 (viz. 778).
5. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned and keep a copy of the same duly signed. If there are any genuine errors or omissions in the said seniority list, the concerned should submit a representation to your department by 18.10.2022, after which the objections received will not be considered. Accordingly, the facts of the representation received till 18.10.2022 should be examined by the concerned departments from the service books and other available records of the concerned and along with the feedback of the department, it should be informed to the General Administration Department by 20.10.2022, so that it can be verified and necessary changes can be made in the seniority notice. Please note that the seniority list will be finalized assuming that there is no objection from the concerned in the department if no information is received till 20.10.2022.
