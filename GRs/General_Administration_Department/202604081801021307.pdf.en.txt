Introduction: - To provide exposure to modern technology to government employees in the state. It was May 5. The event "TECH-WARI" was successfully organized during 09th May, 2024. The training programme TECH-WARI has been extended in the state. "TECH-Wari 2.0" is proposed to be held from 4th to 8th May, 2026. The main focus of the programme is to inculcate a culture of use of modern technology, continuous training and capacity building in the VET civil services. The government was considering administrative as well as financial sanction for the cost of organising the event.
The Government's Decisions:
The Ministry has sanctioned an estimated cost of Rs.2,000 crore for the conduct of the training programme "A'Thalla-Wari 2.0" from 4th to 8th May, 2026 at Mumbai and other ancillary activities. 200.00 Lakhs of funds are being given administrative and financial approval.
