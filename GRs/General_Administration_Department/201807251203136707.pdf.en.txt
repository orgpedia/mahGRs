Read:- (1) Government Order Department of Law and Justice, No. EST 2017/ P. No. 142/ K-2, D. January 9, 2018.
(2) The Deputy Secretary, Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai vide letter No. 52/ Law Officer/ Legal Assistant/ 2005/ V, Dy. April 17, 2018
Preamble: Mr. Sagar, working as Under Secretary (Law) in the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Bhongade, Law Officer under the Department of Law and Justice, No. 1 here. As per the Government Order dated 9th January, 2018, the pay band is Rs. 15,600-39, 100+ Grade Pay Rs. He has been posted in the Commission's office on temporary promotion to the post of Deputy Secretary (Law) in the pay scale of Rs.7,600/-.
Since the post of Deputy Secretary (Law) is not sanctioned in the office of the Commission, it is not possible to appoint Mr. Bhongade, Under Secretary (Law) to the post of promotion. The Government was contemplating to temporarily elevate Mr. Bhongade to the cadre of Deputy Secretary (Law) till the post of Under Secretary (Law) in the Commission's office was occupied by Mr. Bhongade.
Government Decision: The pay band for the cadre of Under Secretary (Law) on the establishment of Maharashtra Public Service Commission is Rs. 15,600-39, 100+ Grade Pay Rs. 6,600/- in the cadre of Deputy Secretary (Law) in the pay band of Rs. 15,600-39, 100+ Grade Pay Rs. 7,600/- per month. The government is giving approval to upgrade from January 9, 2018.
2. In this graded post Mr. Sagar Pr. Bhongade, Under Secretary (Law) is being promoted to the post of Deputy Secretary (Law) with effect from 9th January, 2018 (MP). This position was given to Shri.
Bhongade will continue to hold the post of Deputy Secretary (Law) in the Commission's office.
After this, the post will automatically be converted into the post of Under Secretary (Law) in the original post.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the head Budget Accounts approved by the Commission.
