The Proposal:
The reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision of 1.3.1997, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has been approved to get the paper printed from the private printing press on the lines of the Union Public Service Commission.
Finance Department No. 2 here. As per the Government Order dated 12.11.2021, all payments made by the Subordinate and Accounts Office, Mumbai to the various contractors/ suppliers/ grantee institutions of the Government should be directly credited to the bank account of the Subordinate Adalat through the e-Kuber system. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide No. 3 If the payment for printing of question papers under the letter dated 31.1.2022 is to be made to the private printing press through the e-Kuber system, the information of the printing press will be fed into the system and the identity of the printing press will be revealed and the confidentiality of the printing of question papers strictly observed by the Commission will be breached. Accordingly, in order to protect the confidentiality of the question papers by exposing the identity of the private press, the Commission has requested for relaxation of Maharashtra Public Service Commission from the e-Kuber system implemented by the Finance Department vide Government Order dated 12/11/2021.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has been exempted from the e-Kuber system while making payments for printing question papers by private printing presses. The decision also allows the grant to be made in the name of Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Maharashtra Public Service Commission and then disbursed to the concerned private printing press.
This Government Decision has been issued by the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 55/22/ Co. being issued with the consent given as on 17.3.2022.
