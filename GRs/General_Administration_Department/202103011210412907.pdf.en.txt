Government Decision: As per Section-33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. Instructions regarding ensuring the posts for the disabled in the state government establishments as per the notification of the Central Government dated 04.01.2021 have been issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021. The posts in Group A to D cadre in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad under the control of this Department as per the posts ensured under the notification dated 04.01.2021 of the Central Government have been ensured for Divyang by the Government decision dated 15.02.2021.
The duties and responsibilities of the post of Director of Physical Training in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad are different and are not included in the list of posts reserved for the disabled as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 of the Central Government. Therefore, the proposal for ensuring the posts for the disabled in these posts was submitted to the expert committee constituted by the Government vide letter dated 12.11.2020 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
