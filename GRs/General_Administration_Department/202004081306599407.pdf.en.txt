Government Circular: Various instructions have been issued under the Reference Order to implement various preventive measures to prevent the spread of Corona Virus (COVID-19) in the offices of the State and to prevent the officers/ employees of the State from getting infected.
Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR) and Pune Metropolitan Region (PMR). In view of the increasing outbreak of Corona virus, in order to prevent the spread of this virus in government offices, it is being made mandatory for all officers/ employees of all government, semi-government, municipal corporations, public enterprises and all other offices in this area to wear face masks during the entire period from entering the office to leaving the office.
