Government circular
The Departmental Examination is to be conducted for the promotion of Assistant Cell Officers in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the post of Cell Officer. It was held on August 30, 2018.
In addition to the candidates mentioned above, the Assistant Section Officers of the Administrative Department who have previously appeared in the departmental examination and have failed in the said examination or have been absent in the examination, if such candidates are to appear in the Cell Officer Departmental Examination 2018, their applications in the prescribed format (mentioning the full name of the candidate in the application form) should be sent to the Administrative Department by the Establishment Officers of the Administrative Department after verifying that all the details in the application form are correct. Applications received after this time will not be considered. The prescribed format of application is available on the Ministry Intranet under the heading" Application Form."
3. Qualified candidates who have given synchronized marks for the training and examination presented. As per the circular dated 04.07.2018, the applications of the candidates who have not been sent within the prescribed time limit, but who want to appear for the 2018 examination for the first time, the applications in the prescribed format should also be sent to this office along with the applications of the candidates appearing for the examination again by 05.10.2018.
All the administrative departments of the Ministry may kindly bring this circular in writing to the notice of the concerned Assistant Section Officer (including the Assistant Section Officer on deputation/ leave) immediately and take their signatures and preserve the documents in their records. The application of a qualified candidate was received within the prescribed time.
