As per Section 4 (1), 4 (2) and 4 (3) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Ministry Clerk has issued a notification regarding the transfer of typists. 1 dated 07-05-2018 and changes in the postings of certain Clerical Typists vide reference No. 1 dated 31-05-2018 and as per Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Transfer Act, 2005.
