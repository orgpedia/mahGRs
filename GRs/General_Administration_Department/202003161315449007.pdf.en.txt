the order
Shri A.N. More, Cell Officer, Industries, Energy and Labour Department, is being transferred and posted as Deputy Executive Officer, Sri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai under the Department of Law and Justice on deputation for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. More shall commence the service of a Deputy on the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original office after demitting the office of the Deputy Commissioner, expire one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The Department of Industry, Energy and Labour (Industry-10) and the Department of Law and Justice shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. More will be handled by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour (Industry-10) and the Department of Law and Justice.
4. Industries, Energy and Labour Department should immediately relieve Mr. More to join the place of deputation.
