Government circular
In accordance with the Government Notification General Administration Department No. RTR 1013/ PR No. 50/13/14-A, dated 21.02.2018, the educational qualification for appointment by promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in the ministerial cadre from Group-D cadre in the ministerial department with effect from 21.02.2018 is Graduation. Thereafter, the General Administration Department, vide Government Notification No. RTR 1013/ PR No. 50/13/14-A, dated 23-01-2020, has notified the" Clerk-Typist" Group-C (Entry into Service) (Amendment) Rules, 2020 in the Brihanmumbai Government Offices and as per the proviso therein, the educational qualification for promotion to the post of Clerk Typist in Group-C from Group-D has been revised to the extent that the employees who have passed the" Certificate Examination in Secondary School" as on 20-02-2023 shall also be considered for promotion.
(2) The number of employees who have qualified for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in Group-C and have applied for promotion from the Group-D cadre of the Ministry Department. Provisional seniority as on 01.01.2021 has been prepared.
3. The candidates who have qualified and applied for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist in the list as per the said seniority. The Group-D cadre includes employees who have completed at least 3 years of regular service as on January 1, 2021, have passed graduation or equivalent examination or certificate examination in secondary school and have the required speed of typing (40 words per minute in English or 30 words in Marathi), holding a certificate (Government Commercial Certificate) or computer typing certificate as prescribed by rules. The list in respect of such seniority is annexed to this circular at Annexure' A'.
