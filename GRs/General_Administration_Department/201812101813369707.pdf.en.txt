the order
Mr. Prafulla Thakur, Under Secretary, Department of Welfare of Women, Minorities and Insurance is being appointed on deputation to the post of Managing Director on Establishment of Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation for a period of two years initially subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. Prafulla Thakur, Under-Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Department of Excise, Excise and Insurance Welfare shall determine the terms and conditions of the deputation taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
3. All other administrative matters of Shri Prafulla Thakur, Under Secretary will be handled by the Department of Excise, Excise and Insurance Welfare.
