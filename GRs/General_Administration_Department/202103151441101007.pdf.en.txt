Preamble: Reference No. 1 The State Training Policy is implemented in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision. Similarly, the candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission through State Service Examination, Reference No. Two years training is provided under the Combined Probationary Training Programme as per the provisions of section 2.
2. In the recent past, due to the lockdown situation arising out of the pandemic COVID-19, as well as the physical distancing norms followed by the citizens across the country, it was not possible for the trainees to attend the training institutes physically. Similarly, it is not possible to release the concerned officers and employees to attend the training in training institutions in case of emergency in line with the government's work. The officers and employees should undergo basic training, post-promotion training, oral training in new subjects in accordance with their service progress and government activities within the prescribed time. Considering this, the government has decided to form a committee to study and submit recommendations to the government to make necessary improvements in the existing training system so that modern technology is used in the training system of the state government and online training can be increased.

The central government has provided a medium called IGOT (Integrated Governance Online Training) under Mission Karmayogi to provide online training through digital means. Keeping this in mind, the existing training system needs to be improved to provide training to the state government employees in online/ blended mode using technology and to create necessary systems and arrangements in the training institutes and government offices.
For this, a study committee is being formed to study the state training policy and the reforms to be made in the training system under the Combined Probationary Training Program and to submit necessary recommendations to the government.
