The Proposal:
To make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the government, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations publishes various schemes.
Government Decision: Special Publicity Campaign of Government Schemes On the occasion of Ashadhi Wari under the Scheme 2018-19, to disseminate information about various welfare schemes and development works of the Government of Maharashtra to the general public, through various activities (broadcast of government messages, grand exhibition at Pandharpur, hoardings, etc.) on the Dindi Yatra route during the Ashadhi Wari period, to disseminate information about various schemes of the Government to the right and deserving beneficiaries as well as to the citizens, an amount of Rs. 3,18,88, 600/- (Rupees Three Crore Eighteen Lakh Eighty Eighty Thousand Six Hundred Only) for the year 2018-19 is being provided administrative and financial approval under this Government Decision.
