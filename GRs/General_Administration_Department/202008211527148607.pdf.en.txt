the order
Suresh Khade, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department is being transferred and posted as Additional Commissioner, Nashik Municipal Corporation under Urban Development Department on deputation for a period of one year.
(a) Mr. Khade, Deputy Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original post, complete the period of his deputation by handing over the charge of the post of Deputy Secretary, one year thereafter.
If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the Urban Development Department (NV-14) and the General Administration Department after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Khade, Deputy Secretary will be handled by the Urban Development Department (NV-14) and the General Administration Department.
4. The Urban Development Department has notified the appointment of this deputation with a view to control the situation arising out of COVID-19 virus infection. Therefore, Mr. Khade, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department should be relieved immediately.
