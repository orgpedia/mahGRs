Preamble: The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2021-22, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the objective of 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) under the article Title A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Subsidiary Grants to Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Scrutiny (20700862). Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2021-22, the Institute of Accounts Heading A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. 75,91,000/- and 31-Additional Grants (Non-Payment) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,55,12, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) has been approved.
For the financial year 2021-22, under the head' Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)' for the financial year 2021-22, an amount of Rs.100 crore has been released to the Institute of Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur under the head' Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)'. 14,61,253/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Sixty Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Three only) has been disbursed to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute.
