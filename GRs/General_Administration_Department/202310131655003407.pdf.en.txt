Preamble: The selected institutions and their rates were approved by the Government decision dated 7th June, 2019 for digitally printing and affixing/ removing the information and messages of various public welfare schemes of the Government on hoardings and banners at various places in Maharashtra under the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. The term of this government was two years. After the expiry of the term of this government decision, the matter of approving the newly fixed institutions and their rates by following the prescribed procedure for the said work was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the institutions for printing the information and messages of the government's public welfare schemes digitally through flex and putting them on hoardings and banners in various places of the state and for determining the rates in the prescribed manner, the applications of 15 institutions received through the e-tendering process, fulfilling the entire criteria as per the terms, conditions and conditions of the tender process, the institutions mentioned in the following statement' A' and' B' and their rates are being approved for the revenue department mentioned in front of their names.
