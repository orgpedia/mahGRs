Preamble: General Administration Department vide Government Resolution dated 14/11/2014 at Chief Minister's Secretariat, Ma. For the establishment of the office of the Minister/ Minister of State, fixed posts were created for the duration of the Council of Ministers. The Hon'ble Chief Minister resigned from the post of Chief Minister on 08/11/2019. given to the Governor. Also, the period of Council of Ministers has come to an end with the imposition of President's Rule in the State with effect from 12/11/2019. Following that. With the abolition of the fixed posts created under 14/11/2014, Ma. Officers/ staff working in the office of the Chief Minister/ Minister/ Minister of State. They have been repatriated to their parent department/ office vide order dated 13/11/2019. However, a large number of donations have been received for the Chief Minister's Relief Fund in view of the flood situation in Kolhapur and Sangli districts. Reconciliation of the donation amount, accounting work and receipt of the donors is underway and the work will continue for some more time.
Government Decision: The Chief Minister's Relief Fund in the Chief Minister's Secretariat is a public trust and the government is approving the creation of 1 temporary post of Assistant Director, Fund and Accounts and 2 temporary posts of Assistant Accounts Officer for the Reconciliation of Donation Amounts, Accounting Affairs and Giving Receipts to Donors and to continue it during the President's Rule. Also, the term posts are being continued with effect from 13/11/2019.
