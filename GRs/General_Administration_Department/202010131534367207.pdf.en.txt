Reference No. Raut was given the post of Office Superintendent, Class-3 and Lutte was given the post of Residential and Premises Manager, Class-3. However, the General Administration Department/ Office of 12-A vide reference No. Raut, and Mr. Lutte were appointed on deputation to the post of Assistant (CPTP) for a period of 2 years. Mr. Raut has been on deputation as Assistant (CPTP) since 01-08-2018, for a period of 2 years as on 31-07-2020 and Mr. Lutte has been on deputation as Assistant (CPTP) since 10-08-2018.
There are a large number of vacancies in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officers in the Ministry Department. The services of Mr. Raut, Assistant Cell Officer, Water Resources Department and Mr. Yo D. Lutte, Assistant Cell Officer, Urban Development Department on deputation are being returned to their parent department from the date of order.
Mr. Raut and Mr. YoDi Lutte should immediately join the Water Resources Department and Urban Development Department respectively in their parent department and submit a report to this department.
The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, (Vanamati) Nagpur, should immediately relieve the above two employees and not pay any salary and allowances after 20-10-2020.
The fact that Mr. Raut, Assistant Cell Officer, and Mr. Yo D. Lutte Assistant Cell Officer have joined the administrative department of your ministry should be reported to this department.
