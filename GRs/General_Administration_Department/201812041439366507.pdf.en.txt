Proposal- Department of Women and Child Development, Govt. As of April 2, 2018, 1% parallel reservation in education and employment has been implemented from the open category to enable orphan children to avail various benefits of the state government. This reservation is applicable for direct recruitment to the posts of Group A to Group D. Orphaned children must be recruited according to the prescribed 1% reservation.
Government circular
All the appointing authorities should strictly implement the 1% reservation for orphans while filling up the direct service posts of Group-A to Group-D in the state government service. Appointment authorities shall fill up the posts in the open category only after calculating the number of posts in accordance with the prescribed reservation. 1% for orphans in the advertisement while filling up vacancies in all administrative departments and their subordinate offices
Be sure to make reservations. Department of Women and Child Development. Orphans should be given reservation according to the terms and conditions of the government decision of April 2, 2018.
The circular will be applicable for appointments in government/ semi-government services, government undertakings, corporations, boards, government-aided institutions and all other institutions and services for which the government has the power to issue guidelines. These instructions should be brought to the notice of all the heads of departments under them by the administrative departments.
