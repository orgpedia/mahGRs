Preamble As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the revised strength of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been decided. Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix is approved for 8 cadre posts.
Also, 8 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. Due to administrative exigencies, the matter of extension of 2 posts created under the Government decision dated 03.09.2019 in the said cadre on temporary basis was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, two posts created temporarily under the Government Decision dated 03.09.2019 in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix are being extended from 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2020.
