Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No. 2020/ Q. No. 35/18 (R. And why ), dated 20th March, 2020 Government Decision: Preventive Measures to prevent the spread of Corona Virus (COVID-19) in the offices of the State and to prevent the officers/ employees of the State from getting infected, the Government has now decided as follows in the background of the restriction imposed on all types of transport.
(a) 5% attendance of officers/ employees in all Government offices in the State.
The head of the department or the head of the office should take measures in this regard. All other matters shall remain as specified in the Government Resolution dated 18th March, 2020.
(b) In the case of Ministerial Departments, it shall be ensured that at least one Deputy Secretary/ Under-Secretary and one Cell Officer and one Assistant Cell Officer shall accompany the Secretary concerned in 5%.
(c) The provisions of paragraph 2 (a) of the Government Order dated 20th March, 2020 in respect of Private Establishment No. 3 are being made applicable throughout the State till further orders.
(2) This order shall remain in force until further orders.
