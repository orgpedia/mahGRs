Provisional promotion to the post of Deputy Secretary to the said officers is being made subject to the following conditions:
(a) No departmental inquiry has been initiated against the said officers till the date of the order.
(b) The said ad-hoc promotions are being made subject to the demotion of the junior most officers of the order, if there is no vacancy in the cadre of Deputy Secretary for the senior officers on deputation, after their repatriation.
(c) The said promotion has been granted in public interest as an administrative exigency in a very temporary manner.
(d) This temporary promotion will not entitle the officers concerned to any regular promotion i.e. seniority.
(a) The promotion. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 and Special Leave Petition No. 25181/2018 and I.A.
