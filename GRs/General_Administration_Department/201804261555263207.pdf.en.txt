Preamble: The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the Legislative Council after demand-wise discussion on the budget for the financial year 2018-19 as mentioned in the Government Circular referred above. The Bill has been published as Act No. 22 (Twenty-second) in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 27th March, 2018. Accordingly, the 9th Grants for the year 2018-19 (April, 2018 to December, 2018) have been handed over to the concerned Joint/ Deputy Secretaries of the Administrative Department for disbursement. A proposal is under consideration of the Government to hand over the Principal Accounts Title 2235, Pension to Freedom Fighters and their Dependents, etc., (22350015) for the year 2018-19 for the month of April to December, 2018 to the respective District Collectors.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In the statement appended to the Government Decision, under the column Budget Estimates for the financial year 2018-19, in the Head of the Head of Accounts 2235, Pension to Freedom Fighters and their Dependents, etc., (22350015), provision for this scheme is Rs. 72,77,15, 000/- (Rupees Seventy Two Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Fifteen Thousand only), out of which 70% i.e. 50,94,00, 500/- (Rupees Fifty Crores Ninety Four Lakhs Five Hundred only) is being disbursed to the respective District Collectors as shown before their districts.
2. All District Collectors shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grants released to them and is spent only for matters approved by the Government. Also, the subsidy should be spent according to the monthly fund distribution system.
The amount of grants spent from the funds distributed to the District Collectors should be reconciled with the Accountant General-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General-2, Nagpur within the prescribed time and submit a monthly report of the expenditure to the Government on a regular basis. In this regard, the Government Order No. Action to be taken as per Expenditure Compensation 2011/ S.No. 139/2011/ SWASAC-1, dated 16/8/2011.
