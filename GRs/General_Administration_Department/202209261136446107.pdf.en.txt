General Administration Department, Govt. The Training Policy for all Government Officers/ Employees has been framed vide Notification dated 23.09.2011. The General Administration Department, Govt. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022 have been notified vide Notification dated 02.02.2022
As per this rule, basic, revision and post-promotion training has been made mandatory for all government officials/ employees. The government has taken the decision. As per the State Training Policy dated 23.09.2011, the duration of post-promotional training for Group-B has been fixed at two weeks.
Accordingly, 12 days post-promotion training has been organized by Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI), Pune from 03.10.2022 to 14.10.2022 for the officers promoted to the rank of Cell Officers. A list of the officers nominated for training is attached.
3. Ramati, Pune. Instructions regarding training for trainees have been given vide letter dated 21.09.2022 and a copy of the same is enclosed. The instructions in this letter should be brought to the notice of the trainee concerned.

Government Letter No: Law 1122/ P. No. 70/14 B
The training is being imparted as per the State Training Policy and Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, and attendance is mandatory. Regarding the training, the government has issued a circular. The suggestions dated 13th May, 2015 should be brought to the notice of the concerned.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, provide for withholding of increments for officers who have not completed their post-promotion training. This should be brought to the notice of all the concerned officials.
The officer nominated for training shall be appointed on the day before the commencement of the training. They should be relieved on the condition that they should reach Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI), Pune by 6.00 pm on 02.10.2022 or by 9.00 am on 03.10.2022 on the first day of training. The concerned department should immediately inform the trainee about the duration of the training considering the need for reservation for travel. The nominated Cell Officers are required to be relieved by the respective Head of the Department and a copy of the order of dismissal should be sent to the Registrar and Assistant Professor, Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI), Pune as well as to this office.
The nominated Cell Officer should contact the Session Assistant, Shri Kumar Potbhare (Md. No.7798558582).
