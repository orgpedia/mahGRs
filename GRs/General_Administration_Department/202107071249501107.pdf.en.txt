The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, instructions have been issued under the Government Resolution No. 2 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of India to ensure posts for persons with disabilities in State Government establishments on the lines of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, New Delhi. Accordingly, posts in Group-A to Group-C of the Brihanmumbai Government offices in the ministerial department as well as in the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission have been ensured for physically disabled persons under the order at Reference No. 3. Now the matter of ensuring posts for Divyang for the cadre of Joint Secretary, Deputy Secretary and Under Secretary is under consideration.
Government's decision:
As per para (i) of the Government Resolution at Reference No. 2," a post which is ensured for the disabled at the lower (feeder cadre) level shall automatically be deemed to be ensured for the said category of disabled for the purpose of promotion. " This is the provision. The posts of Joint Secretary, Deputy Secretary and Under Secretary in the Department of the Ministry are filled by full promotion. He is also a cadre officer at the lower level of the cadre. For the cadre of Cell Officers, posts have been ensured for the disabled under the government decision at Reference No. 3.
