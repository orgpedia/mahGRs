Preamble: A one-man high level inquiry committee headed by Shri Kailash Uttamchand Chandiwal, retired Judge of the High Court, to look into the allegations made by Shri Param Bir Singh in his letter dated 20.3.2021. The appointment has been made in accordance with the Government Decision dated 30/03/2021. The Government was contemplating to provide honorarium to Justice Kailash Uttam Chand Chandiwal, retired Judge of the High Court, and to provide officers/ staff to the said High Level Inquiry Committee. The government has taken the following decisions:
Retired High Court Judge Justice Kailash Uttam Chand Chandiwal will be paid a salary and allowances equivalent to the salary and allowances admissible to a sitting High Court Judge.
The following officers/ staff are being appointed to perform the office functions of the High Level Inquiry Committee.
1. Shri. Behere (presently Deputy Collector, Land Acquisition, Manager (Secretary) of the Committee, M.N.P., Mumbai) with additional charge 2. Adv. Subhash Hanumant Shikhare (Retired Headmaster, Municipal Civil Court, Mumbai) Shri Harshvardhan Shriram Joshi (Retired Headmaster, High Court Headmaster Court, Mumbai).
Mr. Sanjay Karnik (Retired Office Superintendent, Office of the Metropolitan Committee of Judicial Magistrate)
3. Shri. Behere (presently Deputy Collector, Land Acquisition, M.N.P., Mumbai) is being given the additional charge of the post of Manager of the High-Level Inquiry Committee in addition to his original charge of Deputy Collector. The special pay orders will be issued after his basic pay is withdrawn from the post of Deputy Collector (Land Acquisition), Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and he takes additional charge of the post of Manager of the High-Level Inquiry Committee.
