The Maharashtra Airport Development Company (MADC) has sanctioned a project for setting up an Integrated Air Cargo Facility at Shirdi International Airport, Shirdi, under the PM GatiShakti Scheme of the Central Government. 55.00 crore was requested to be approved at the Government level.
The project has been approved by the central government. However, it was under the consideration of the government to give administrative and financial approval to the project at the state level. Accordingly, the proposal was sent to the Finance Department for approval. The proposal was approved by the Planning Department and the Finance Department directed to get the approval of the committee empowered by the department. Accordingly, the proposal was placed in the meeting dated 16/3/2023 of the Empowered Committee and it was approved. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision
The administrative approval is being accorded for setting up an Integrated Air Cargo Facility at Shirdi International Airport, Shirdi under Part-II (Gati Shakti) of the Scheme for Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment (SAPI) of the Central Government. Further, 50 years interest free loan of Rs. 55.00 crore is being approved for the financial implication of the loan. Details of the work and costs are as follows:
