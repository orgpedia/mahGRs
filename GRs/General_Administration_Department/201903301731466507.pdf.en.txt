General Administration Department Government Order No. ASC-1516/ PR No. 217/14-A dated 15th July, 2017, dated 28th August, 2017 and dated 12th April, 2018.
- Government order
The Clerk-Typist in the Ministry Department was given ad-hoc promotion to the rank of Assistant Cell Officer vide order dated 15.07.2017. Shri A.M. Gijaye, Clerk-Typist, being on deputation to the State Election Commission, was given on-line promotion to the rank of Assistant Cell Officer in the office of the State Election Commission vide order dated 28.08.2017 for a period of six months from the date of order. As the period of their promotion under the order dated April 12, 2018, had expired on 28-02-2018, the extension of their promotion was extended from 28-02-2018 for a period of one year or till they join the Department of School Education and Sports, whichever is earlier (till 28-02-2019). Now that the period of promotion of Mr. Gijeya, Assistant Cell Officer has expired on 28-02-2019, this order extends the period of promotion of Mr. Gijeya for one year from 28-02-2019 or till he joins the Department of School Education and Sports after promotion, whichever is earlier (up to 27-02-2020). After this, they will have to appear in the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the School Education and Sports Department after their promotion.
