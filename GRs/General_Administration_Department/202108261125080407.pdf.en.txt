The central government has given. The list of posts notified for Persons with Disabilities under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department of the state government has issued a notification in this regard. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision dated 23/02/2021, the posts in Group-A to Group-D cadre in the State Election Commission Office under the General Administration Department have been ensured for the disabled category. The Committee in its meeting dated 21.6.2021 has suggested that the post of" Driver" in Group-C cadre be excluded from the list of persons with disabilities.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published. Out of this, the decision to exclude the post of" Driver" from the list of persons with disabilities has been taken in the meeting of the Expert Committee dated 21.6.2021.
" Accordingly, the post of"" Driver"" in Group-C cadre in the State Election Commission Office under the General Administration Department is being excluded from the reservation of posts for the disabled."
