Allotment of Government Residence to Leader of Opposition, Maharashtra Legislative Assembly
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. SHANIVA-1522/ P.S. NO. 150/23-A
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date is August 2, 2022
Government's decision
The Government has decided to allot a Government bungalow at Devagiri, Malabar Hill, Mumbai to Shri Ajit Anantrao Pawar, Hon'ble Leader of Opposition, Maharashtra Legislative Assembly.
2. Devagiri is a bungalow which was earlier commonly known as Ma. It appears to have been allocated to the Deputy Chief Minister or the senior Minister. The government has decided to allot the bungalow to the leader of the opposition. This decision will not be reversed. And it won't be any longer. Based on this condition, the decision has been taken.
(3) It shall be mandatory for the Leader of the Opposition to vacate the bungalow allotted to him within a period of fifteen days after he demits office.
