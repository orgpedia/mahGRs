Strengthening of the headquarters of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations by establishing an updated system of publicity. Strengthening of establishment, creation of infrastructure, etc., as well as the responsibility of government publicity is being carried out by the departmental and district information functionaries under the Directorate General of Information through various publicity agencies, and most of the information offices are in rented premises, which indirectly affects the office functioning. Therefore, it is proposed to implement a new scheme for the construction of government buildings and information buildings by getting own space for all such subordinate information offices. The scheme has been approved in the Cabinet meeting held on 9 th February, 2022. The administrative approval has been granted in accordance with the government decision mentioned in section 2.
