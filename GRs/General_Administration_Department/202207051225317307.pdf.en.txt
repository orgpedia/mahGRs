Preamble: The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-23, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the head 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for the purpose of 36 months June 2022 and 7th Pay Commission difference under the head-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for Combined Undergraduate Training Programme (20700862) to the Institute of Accounts, Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2022-23, the Institute of Accounts Head A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, has been provided with an amount of Rs. 82,74,000/- and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,61,95, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Sixty-one Lakhs Fifty-nine Thousand only) has been approved.
