Preamble: The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-23, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute for the month of May, 2022 under the head 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for the purpose of Assistant Grants (20700862) to the Institute for Training Programme under the consolidated purview of Accounts Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2022-23, the Institute of Accounts Head A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, has been provided with an amount of Rs. 82,74,000/- plus 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-salaried) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,61,95, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Sixty-one Lakhs Fifty-nine Thousand only) has been approved.
As per the request of Vanamati Training Institute at Roll No. 4, hereunder, for the financial year 2022-23, under the head of Accounts Title A-4, 2070-Assistant Grants for Training Programme (20700862) to the Institute of Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, under the consolidated Schedules, 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries), a sum of Rs. 14,68,500/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand Five Hundred only) is being distributed by the Bureau of Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BIMS) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
