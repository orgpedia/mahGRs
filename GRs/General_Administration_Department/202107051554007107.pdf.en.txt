Under the State Training Policy, administrative and in-service training is being provided to the state government employees by the General Administration Department and technical training by the concerned departments. In view of the restrictions imposed due to the outbreak of the Covid-19 epidemic in the state and social distancing, there are currently restrictions on the physical training of government employees. At the same time, the attendance of government employees in the office is also being limited from time to time in accordance with the measures being taken to prevent the spread of coronavirus. However, there is a need for continuous training for dynamic governance by enhancing efficiency at all levels in the state government service. For this, under the State Training Policy, the apex training institute Yashoda, Pune and the 6 existing Divisional Administrative Training Institutes and District Administrative Training Institutes in Annexure' A' and' B' are currently providing administrative and in-service training on a large scale online in view of the Covid outbreak.
2. In view of the social distancing to be followed in the present situation, the following instructions are being issued to all the administrative departments to complete the pending training of employees at various stages and to maintain continuity in training. :
1) All departments should organize maximum online training for employees through their subordinate technical training institutes. All the heads of the departments/ offices under them should also be informed in this regard.
2) Necessary action should be taken by contacting the concerned training institute as per the training needs and requirements of the department.
