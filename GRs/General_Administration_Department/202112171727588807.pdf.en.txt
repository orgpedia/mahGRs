Introduction: In the wake of Corona, advertisements have not been published regarding direct recruitment in government service, some candidates have been deprived of the opportunity to appear in the examination as they have exceeded the maximum age limit during this period. The Cabinet The meeting was held on November 10, 2021. Accordingly, the government was considering providing such an opportunity.
Government Decision: In the backdrop of the foregoing, the following suggestions are being made through this Government Decision.
1) In the context of Corona, in the context of direct appointment in government service. Since the advertisement has not been published since March 1, 2020, the government has issued a notification through the service entry rules of the concerned post or under the relevant law. The maximum age limit prescribed for appointment vide Government Order dated 25.04.2016 is Rs. The candidates who have crossed the age limit from 01.03.2020 to the date of this Government decision, will be eligible for admission. Till 31st December 2022, the opportunity is being given to appear in the examination as a" one-time special case" for the advertisement which will be published regarding direct recruitment in the government service.
2) With the decrease in the prevalence of corona, in cases where advertisements have been published in the near future and the last date of application mentioned in the advertisements will be after the date of this government decision. During the period from 01.03.2020 to the date of this Government Decision, the entry into service of the concerned post shall be governed by the rules or regulations in force. Candidates who have exceeded the maximum age limit prescribed for appointment vide Government Order dated 25.04.2016 are also being given an opportunity to appear in the examination as a" one time special case."
3) With the decrease in the prevalence of corona, in cases where advertisements have been published in the recent past and the last date of application mentioned in the advertisements has passed before the date of this government decision, but in reality, the examinations have not been conducted.
