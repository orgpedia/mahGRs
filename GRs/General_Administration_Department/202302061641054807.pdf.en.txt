Preamble: The Office of the Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra had three servers for maintaining the website and the database of voters in the electoral rolls.
These servers have been in use for more than 10 years. The servers are currently down.
Therefore, the Director, Department of Information and Technology has provided a server to keep a database of voters in the electoral roll on a temporary basis and to keep the website of the Chief Electoral Officer's office running. Since this provision is temporary in nature, two new servers are being immediately purchased from GeM Portal to keep the database of voters in the electoral roll and to keep the website of the Chief Electoral Officer's office running. The tenders that will be received on the GeM Portal
The government has constituted a Tender Evaluation Committee.
Government's decision
The Tender Evaluation Committee is being constituted to evaluate the tenders received for the purchase of two servers through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal for the office of the Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra.
Additional Chief Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer-Chairman

The Committee will evaluate the bids received in this tender process and select the lowest level (L-1) tender from among them and proceed to approve the acceptance of the tender.
