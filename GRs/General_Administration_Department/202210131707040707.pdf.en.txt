Preamble: A proposal was submitted to the Finance Department to approve the purchase of 5 new vehicles instead of the 5 specified vehicles of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal Office. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has decided to replace 5 unregistered vehicles for the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal Office with the Government of Maharashtra, vide its decision dated 23.07.2021 of the Department of Environment and Climate Change, subject to certain conditions and conditions.
Accordingly, 4 electric vehicles and 1 petrol vehicle were approved for purchase. However, in view of the current shortage of charging stations for electric vehicles in Maharashtra, a meeting was held on 08.04.2022 at the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal. In the meeting with the Chief Secretary, the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal demanded the purchase of 2 electric vehicles and 3 petrol vehicles out of the 5 vehicles approved for the administrative tribunal office.
As per this demand, a proposal in this regard has been sent to the Ministry of Environment and Climate Change as a special case. It was submitted to the Prime Minister for approval. To that Ma. The Chief Minister has approved it. Accordingly, a proposal to approve the purchase of 2 electric vehicles and 3 petrol vehicles out of 5 specified vehicles for the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal was under consideration of the government.
