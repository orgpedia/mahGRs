As per the Government Circular dated 3rd March, 2018, instructions have been issued to hold meetings every six months or once in three months on the issues and demands of the backward class officers/ employees. On the same lines, the Maharashtra State Minority Officers Employees Association has also been requested to instruct all the offices to discuss the issues and grievances of the minority officers and employees in the state of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the following guidelines have been issued.
Circular:
In order to take decisions on sensitive matters like service conditions, recruitment, promotions, transfers, confidential reports of minority officers/ employees in a studied and serious manner and to resolve their issues by interacting with the representatives of registered Maharashtra State Minority Officers Employees Association working in the interest of such officers/ employees from time to time, the Head of the Administrative Department should take decisions at least once in every six months, the Head of the Office/ Head of the Department at least once in three months or, if necessary, at that time by discussing with the representatives of the organizations. Also, it should be ensured that the issues of minority officers/ employees are discussed and resolved at the local level.
The report of the action taken should be submitted to the head of the administrative department of the concerned ministry. All these actions will be reviewed once a year by the Principal Secretary (Services), General Administration Department.
