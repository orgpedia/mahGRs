GOVERNMENT DECISION, GENERAL ADMINISTRATION DEPARTMENT, NO. MALOA-2010/ Q. NO. 280/10/ VIII, D. 08.04.2011 Preamble: Subject to reference. In pursuance of the decision dated 08.04.2011 of the Government to fill the vacancies in the direct services under the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission in a timely manner, a schedule was issued regarding the action to be taken in respect of sending requisition for such posts to the Commission.
Since the suggestions given by the reference government decision need to be revised from time to time, the matter of publishing the Standard Operating Procedure regarding the demand letter to be sent to the Commission by authorizing the reference government decision was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: If the demand letter for the posts of direct service under the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission is not sent in the prescribed time every year, the posts remain vacant for a long time and it has an adverse effect on the administrative work.
Accordingly, the requisition for the vacancies in the direct service should be sent to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the month of September every year i.e. at the beginning of the selection year for promotion.
Also under reference. The Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) to be followed to facilitate the forwarding of requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC), as per the Government Decision dated 08.04.2011, is as follows:
