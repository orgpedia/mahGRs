Preamble: GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B who are directly recruited in the Government service as per the General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-23, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the head of 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) for the purpose of A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Scrutiny (20700862). Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2022-23, the Institute of Accountants A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been sanctioned a sum of Rs. 82,74,000/- and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,61,95, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) has been approved.
2. In pursuance of the letter at serial no. 3 and 4 under reference, Vanamati Training Institute has sanctioned grant-in-aid (non-salary) to the Institute of Accounts Head A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, for the purpose of providing grant-in-aid (non-salary) to the Institute under the head of 8 weeks training programme for Basic Training and Semester I Examination of University of Mumbai under Combined Probationary Training-7 and 8 (20700862). Out of the budgetary provision of Rs. 6,79,21, 000/-, a grant of Rs. 3,91,09, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Ninety One Lakh Nine Thousand only) has been sought.

Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-23, the total subsidy available on BEAMS system under the target of 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Payment) under the head of A4, 2070-Assistant Grants (20700862) for Combined Undertaking Training Programme to Vanamati, Nagpur under the head of Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), is Rs. 3,91,09, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Ninety One Lakh Nine Thousand only) is being disbursed by the Bureau of Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BIMS) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Manual and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing the funds available on the Budget Distribution System from the Treasury and while spending.
b) The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the disbursed subsidy and ensure that the amount is drawn from the treasury by depositing in the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(e) submit to the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred and after collecting the amount, it should be ascertained whether the amount collected has been recorded under the same head in the accounts of the Accountant General's Office or not and the certificate should be submitted to the Government within 15 days.
e) The entire responsibility of meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2021-22 will be of the concerned Controlling Officer as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
UTILIZATION CERTIFICATE of the grant being distributed by Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur, should be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra 1/ 2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and the Government without fail within the prescribed time.
The Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall maintain separate accounts and books of expenditure incurred from the above grant. The powers to examine the books and accounts shall be vested in the Accountant General, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and Vanamati shall provide full facilities to him.
The fund should be spent in compliance with the terms and conditions mentioned in the circular of the Finance Department dated April 6, 2021.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the approval of the Department of Finance vide its Informal Reference No.479/ Expenditure-4 dated 30/08/2022.
