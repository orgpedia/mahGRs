The Government of India has been conferring various awards such as Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, Padma Vibhushan, and Padma Shri to people for their contribution in the field of performing arts. In this regard, it has been observed in some cases that the award with which the respective awardees are honoured is mentioned before the names of the persons concerned.
Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, etc.)
Also, while starting the names of the awardees of many schemes of the government, the award received by them is mentioned at the beginning of their names.
In this case, the Supreme Court in Transfer Cases (Civil) No. 9 of 1994 and No. 1 of 1995 is ordered as follows:
The National Awards do not amount to titles within the meaning of Article 18 (1)
and they should not be used as suffixes or prefixes. If this is done, the defaulter
should forfeit the National Award conferred on him or her by following the
procedure laid in Regulation 90 of each of the four notifications creating these National Awards"
3. as above. In view of the Supreme Court's order, the concerned awardees, various government agencies/ non-government organizations, government departments, offices, etc. should ensure that there is no contempt of the Supreme Court. The award with which the respective awardees are honoured should not be mentioned at the beginning or end of the name of the persons concerned
These guidelines should be strictly implemented.
