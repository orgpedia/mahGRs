Government Circular: A comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. 1 has been prescribed by the government here.
Accordingly, the temporary seniority of Assistant Director (Administration) Group-B officers of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations up to 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022, and 01.01.2023 has been increased to the level of Additional Director General of Police (ADGP). 2 was published here.
The names of the officers who were promoted during the period mentioned above, including the names of officers who were appointed through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route and excluding the names of officers who retired voluntarily and those who retired on regular basis, were included in Annexure-A and the names of the officers who were promoted ad-hoc as they fulfilled the terms and conditions for ad-hoc promotion were included in Annexure-B and the provisional seniority as on 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022 and 01.01.2023 was published on the website.
According to clause 3 (3) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, there is no need to publish an ad-hoc seniority list. Therefore, the ad-hoc seniority list published earlier need not be considered at present. Further, as no objections, objections and errors have been received by the Government on the provisional seniority list, the provisional seniority list showing the status of Assistant Director (Administration) Group-B officers as on 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022, 01.01.2023 along with the seniority list for ad-hoc promotion is being finalised and annexed to this circular.
