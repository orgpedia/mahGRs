The Proposal:
Sainik Institute of Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad is functioning under the General Administration Department (Ex-Servicemen Welfare). The" Sainik Institute of Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad Regulating Board" has been established by the Government decision dated 13th August, 1976 to control the functioning of this institution. The Regulator Board was reconstituted vide Government Resolution No. SPI-2013/ (P. No. 87/13) 28 dated 31st August, 2015.
The appointments of 6 non-official members to the said Board were cancelled vide Government Resolution dated 08.07.2020. The matter of reorganization of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad Regulatory Board was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
To control the functioning of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad, to determine the procedure for the selection of children for admission to the institution and to carry out the tasks assigned by the Government to the Regulatory Board, the Government is reorganizing the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad Regulatory Board and the following persons are being appointed to this Board.

The term of the reconstituted Governing Body shall be for a period of three months from the date of issue of the said Government Resolution or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance of the non-official members of the Military Pre-Service Education Society, Aurangabad as per the provisions of clause 1 (1) (b) of Annexure 42-A of the Bombay Civil Services Rules, Vol. will be admissible as per the Government decision dated 03/03/2010. The Member Secretary of the Governing Body is being authorized to sign the copy of the Local Members Travel Allowance Bill for the meeting of the Governing Body.
