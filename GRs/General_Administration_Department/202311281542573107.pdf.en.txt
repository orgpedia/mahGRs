Introduction: DV vehicles have been placed under all the District Collector offices for the transportation of state guests/ dignitaries coming to the state. The government was considering the formation of a committee to examine the payment for the repair of DV vehicles beyond the prescribed limit. The government decision of August 23, 2023 is being superseded. The revised government decision is issued as follows.
Government's decision:
DV vehicles have been placed under all the District Collector offices for the transportation of state guests/ dignitaries coming to the state. Proposal for Revision of Vehicles As per the provisions of Part I, Sub-Division-2, No.19-B of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, the above petrol vehicles shall be charged at the rate of Rs. 50,000/- and Rs. Payments of more than Rs. 60,000/- are received by the Government for approval. However, just as there is a committee in the Public Health Department to examine the payment of medical expenses, there must be a mechanism at the ministerial level to ensure that the payments are reasonable.
The fee for repairing vehicles is Rs. 50,000/- and Rs. If the amount exceeds Rs. 60,000/-, the concerned payments shall be submitted by the Collector's Office to the Scrutiny Committee, with the approval of the Scrutiny Committee through the Collector's Office and only after the Scrutiny Committee certifies that the expenditure is fair and reasonable, the payments shall be sent for approval at the Government level.

Committee's mandate: Payments related to the repair of D.V. vehicles deployed for the transportation of famous visitors to the Collectorate in the state. 50,000/- for diesel vehicles and Rs. The Committee shall examine the payments before remitting the amount exceeding Rs.60,000/- to the Government for approval and certify whether the expenditure is reasonable and reasonable.
Action to be taken by the Collector's Office: (1) The Collector's Office shall submit the payments to the committee by the 5th of the following month.
(2) The Committee shall, within one month from the receipt of the payment, examine and certify whether the expenditure is reasonable and reasonable.
The work of the Committee shall commence on the date of the decision of the Government.
