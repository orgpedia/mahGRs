the order
Kisanrao Babanrao Palande, Under Secretary, School Education and Sports Department is being appointed as Deputy Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation under Urban Development Department on deputation for a period of three years subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence from the date on which Mr. Palande, Under Secretary joins the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall end on the date on which he resumes charge of his official office.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ Competent Authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Department of School Education and Sports and the Department of Urban Development shall, after considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981, decide among themselves the terms and conditions for the deputation of Mr. Palande.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Kisanrao Babanrao Palande, Under Secretary will be handled by the School Education and Sports Department.
