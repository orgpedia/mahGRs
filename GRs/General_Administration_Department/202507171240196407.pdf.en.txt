The Proposal -
The Sainik Welfare Department, Pune office has only one Xerox machine (black-and-white printing) multifunctional photocopier working and is overburdened with printing work. Therefore, the government was considering granting administrative approval for leasing a new Xerox machine for the Sainik Welfare Department, the Sainik Rehabilitation Branch of the Pune office, the Accounts Branch and the Rehabilitation Branch.
The Government's Decisions:
