The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Official Duties Act, 2006 was published in the Gazette on 12th May, 2006 and came into force on 1st July, 2006. Considering the provisions of the said Act, the matter of periodic transfers of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations is under consideration of the Government.
The officers who are eligible for transfer should report at least 3 places of their choice.
2. The officers who are not eligible for transfer but are willing to be transferred should submit the request form along with the documents stating the reasons for the transfer. And a copy of it was sent to the Secretary of State 34. Must be submitted by March 10, 2020.
