Petition No. Subject to the Judgment No. 28306/2017 and subject to the following conditions, the concerned Senior Grade Short Writer is being promoted to the vacant post in the Select Grade Short Writer Cadre on purely temporary basis:
The promotion will be a temporary one as an administrative exigency.
1) These promotions are being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Senior Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
2) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in this post.
3) The selection of eligible upper category shortlists for the year 2022-2023 is provisional and promotions to the post of Senior Shortlists are provisional. As a result of the selection

Senior Short Writers will not be entitled to the regular post of Select Short Writers and seniority in service.
In case of non-acceptance of provisional promotion to the post of Selection Grade Short Writer by the concerned Senior Grade Short Writer, his/ her name shall be struck off from the list for the year 2022-2023 as per General Administration Department, Government Resolution, No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ K. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility shall be checked for provisional promotion to the post of Selection Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the lists for the next two years.
(5) The temporary promotion of the said Senior Short Writer to the post of Senior Short Writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental enquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
6. The promotion of upper class book writers belonging to backward classes is being made subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the upper class book writers has been issued by the concerned caste verification committee. The concerned department should look into it.
(7) This order shall come into force with immediate effect. The higher grade short writers who have been promoted to the post of selected category short writers should be relieved immediately by the concerned department. Further, a Senior Grade Short Writer promoted under paragraph (d) (3) of the Government Notification dated 21.6.2021 should join the department in which he is posted within thirty days from the date of issue of this order. The concerned departments should send the copies of the orders issued by the concerned departments.
