Floral tributes were offered to the portraits of Jijau Maa Saheb and Swami Vivekananda as well as Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose and Balasaheb Thackeray on the occasion of their birth anniversaries.

Date: January 10, 2023.
Birth anniversaries and National Days of the great men and women of the nation are proposed to be celebrated in 2023. Accordingly, on the birth anniversaries of Jijau Maa Saheb and Swami Vivekananda on Thursday, January 12, 2023, and on Monday, January 23, 2023, on the birth anniversaries of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose and Balasaheb Thackeray, the programme of offering floral tributes to their statues should be celebrated in the Ministries and all Government/ Semi-Government offices.
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the said programme in all government/ semi-government offices in his department/ district and take appropriate action regarding their implementation.
