3. Thematic work as mentioned above Rs. The Government hereby grants administrative approval to the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 5,23,59, 778.00 (Rupees Five Crores Twenty Three Lakhs Sixty Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty Eight only) subject to the following conditions:
The Collector will be responsible for ensuring that the construction of these warehouses for permanent storage of EVMs and VVPATs is completed at a minimum cost and quality as per the guidelines of the Election Commission of India.
b) The Election Commission of India (ECI) has issued a notification for the storage of EVMs and VVPAT machines. 08.08.2017, Dy. The construction of these godowns should be done keeping in view the guidelines issued on 12.03.2019 and by the Election Commission of India from time to time.
(c) Use of e-tendering system for procurement related matters in the proposal or e-auction of e-auction from Department of Industries, Power and Labour. The GeM portal developed by the Central Government for procurement of goods and services under the Government decision dated 24.08.2017 should be used.
d. The work should be completed as soon as possible with care that no revised administrative approval is given for the thematic work.
