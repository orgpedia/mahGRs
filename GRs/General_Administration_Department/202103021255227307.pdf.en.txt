Government's decision:
A total of 24 temporary posts have been created in the office of the Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer under the Government decision dated 28th October, 2009 to conduct departmental inquiry of Group A and Group B gazetted officers in the State Service. These temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The extension granted under the order will expire on February 29, 2021. Therefore, the proposal to extend the tenure of these 24 temporary posts was under the consideration of the government.
2. Government Decision to Extend Temporary Posts Department of Finance, No. Post No. 2016
The powers have been delegated to the Administrative Divisions vide order No. 8/16/ APC, dated 15/02/2021.
Approval is being given for extension of 24 temporary posts in the accompanying prospectus for the period from March 01, 2021 to August 31, 2021.
