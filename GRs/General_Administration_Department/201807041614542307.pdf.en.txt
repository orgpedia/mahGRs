Government circular
Assistant Section Officer Post-Interview Training Examination-2018, the first time eligible candidates appearing for this examination are required to send their application in the prescribed format to the Office of the Assistant Section Officer, New Delhi. The request was made on April 5, 2018. Now, applications in the prescribed format are being invited from the candidates who have failed in the previous post-service training examination or have been absent in the examination, and the prescribed format of application has been made available on the intranet of the Ministry under the heading" Application Form."
However, all the administrative departments of the Ministry and the establishment officers of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission shall ensure that all the details of the application of the eligible candidate are correct and send the attested applications to this department in a manner that they reach by 24 July 2018. Please make sure that the candidate's full name is mentioned in the application.
Candidates who are eligible to appear for the post-service training examination 2018 for the first time. Training classes for the candidates whose applications have been sent within the prescribed time as per the circular dated 5th April, 2018 will be conducted at the time of registration. It was held on May 24, 2018. Apart from the candidates mentioned in the" Annexure A" of this circular, those who are eligible to appear for the first time in the Assistant Cell Officer Post-Entrance Training Examination 2018, but have not registered for the examination. Candidates who have not sent their applications within the prescribed time as per the circular dated April 5, 2018, can also submit their applications in the prescribed format. The establishment officers of the administrative department will certify the applications of such candidates in the prescribed format and hand them over to this department along with the application of the candidates appearing for the re-examination. Submitted by July 24, 2018.

All the administrative departments of the Ministry may kindly bring this circular in writing to the notice of the concerned Assistant Cell Officers (including the Assistant Cell Officers on deputation/ leave) and take their signatures and preserve the documents in their records. All the administrative departments should take note of the fact that only the candidates who are appointed regularly to the post of Assistant Cell Officer can appear in this examination and take appropriate action on time. If there is any error in this regard, the entire responsibility will be of the establishment officer of the concerned administrative department.
