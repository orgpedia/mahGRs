The Central Government has decided to open a new head of expenditure for availing interest free loans for Optical Fibre Cable (OFC) under Part-V to the Directorate General of Information Technology (DGIT) in the first phase under Part-I of the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Expenditure of the States.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is December 16, 2022.
Preamble: The Central Government has decided to provide 50 years of non-interest bearing loans to the States under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment under which the Directorate General of Information Technology has been included in the Incentive for Digitization project under Part-5.
Under the above scheme, the Directorate of Information Technology has been allotted Rs. 286 crore. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to open a new head of account for expenditure on works to be carried out from the loan received from the Central Government under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment to the States.
Government Decision: Under the Central Government's Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment to the States, the Directorate of Information Technology has been allocated Rs. 286 crore. A new head of account is being opened for expenditure on works to be done from that fund as follows.
