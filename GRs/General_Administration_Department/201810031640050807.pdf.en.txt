Sheikh, Senior Short Story Writer, Minority Development Department under reference No. 1 The above order dated 01.10.2015 had relieved him of the charge of accepting appointment on deputation to the post of" Private Secretary" in the office of Haj Committee of India, Mumbai for a period of 3 years.
Shri Shaikh's 3-year deputation period commencing from 01-10-2015 and ending on 30-09-2018. Haj Committee of India, Mumbai under reference No. 2 As per the letter dated 04-07-2018, Shri Shaikh is discharging a very important responsibility as Private Secretary by making necessary arrangements for the regular meetings of the Haj Committee. Considering the operational arrangements of the meeting as well as the critical situation arising out of the arrival of Hajj pilgrims in 2018, it is requested to extend their deputation till 30-09-2019. Accordingly, the Minority Development Department has submitted a proposal to the General Administration Department for extension of the deputation of Mr. Sheikh.
3. General Administration Department, Government Decision No: SRV 2011/ PR No.137/ Q. 12, dated 17.12.2016, the policy of deputation has been fixed and this Government Decision has been published in the Official Gazette. N.
SRV-2016/ PR No.510/ WORK-12 dated 16-02-2018. In the Government Resolution of 17-12-2016. 5 has been amended as under:- Paragraph 5 (a) (3):- The deputation shall be made for a period of 3 years in the first instance and the period may be extended for a further period of 4 years in accordance with the willingness of the officer concerned and with the concurrence of the Cadre Control Department. As per the provisions of this Government Resolution, the period of deputation of Shri Shaikh is being extended from 01-10-2018 for a period of one year upto 30-09-2019 subject to the following conditions:
(a) the extension of service of the deputation shall commence from the date on which the period of the first deputation ended and shall end on the date on which the employee concerned resumes charge of his official office.
