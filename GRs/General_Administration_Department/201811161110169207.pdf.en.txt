The Proposal:
The Resident Commissioner's Office is a leading office in the state government infrastructure in New Delhi, the capital of India, for maintaining coordination with the central and state governments and other state governments, fostering international relations, promoting investment in the state, and carrying out diplomatic activities. In order to fulfill this fundamental purpose, this major administrative system has been functioning since 1963 in the building first known as" Maharashtra House" and later as" Maharashtra Sadan" on the Sangli plot in New Delhi. With a view to facilitate the stay of the visitors in New Delhi who contribute to this noble fabric of the State, the State Government has constructed the second State Guest House (New Maharashtra Sadan) at Kasturba Gandhi Marg, New Delhi with state-of-the-art facilities and it has been opened for use from 15th August, 2013.
Reference No. The policy regarding reservation of rooms and rates in the new Maharashtra Sadan has been decided under the Government Decision dated 17th June 2014. Reference No. The Government Decisions 2 to 6 have been amended from time to time. It was under the consideration of the Government to issue a consolidated Government Resolution incorporating all the amendments and taking into account the representations received from the Government and making further amendments accordingly.
