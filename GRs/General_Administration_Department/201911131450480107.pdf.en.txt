The Proposal:
An amount of Rs. 15.00 lakh per annum is budgeted under Voluntary Grants to meet the expenses for making various gifts to the visiting foreign Ambassadors, dignitaries, guests, high ranking officials, visiting persons during their courtesy visits, for providing financial assistance to the needy persons, for meeting natural calamities, public, educational, charitable, exercise related institutions and organizations, for making gifts to the visitors during their visits to festivals or cultural events. The guidelines for utilization of the fund have been mentioned in the office order dated 11.9.2008 of the office of the Secretary to the Governor.
In the meanwhile, there should be uniformity in the amount of discretionary grants available for the use of the Governor of each State in the Conference of Governors held between 12.10.2017 and 13.10.2018. It has been recommended that the grant amount should be at least Rs 5 crore. Accordingly, the Union Ministry of Home Affairs vide its letter dated 18.4.2018 had informed the State Government to take an appropriate decision. According to The decision to increase the governor's voluntary grant was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the recommendation of the Governor. The annual grant of Rs. 5 crore (Rs. 5,00,00, 000/-) has been decided by the Government.
The amount should be spent only on the matters mentioned in the Office Order dated 11.09.2008 of the Secretary to the Governor.
