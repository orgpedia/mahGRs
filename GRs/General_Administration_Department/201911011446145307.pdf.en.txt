Government's decision
In view of the extensive and progressive changes in the people-oriented schemes, development projects and policies implemented by various departments of the government, the information of the schemes should be effectively disseminated to the public.
The list of Advertising Agencies was approved for a period of two years under Schedule III of the Act for the purpose of production of work and carrying out advertising activities.
2.It will take some time to start the process of e-tendering and prepare the final list for inviting applications in the prescribed format and in the prescribed manner from the qualified institutions for carrying out the promotional and publicity and advertising activities of various departments of the government. So the. List of Institutions approved under Government Resolution dated 12th October, 2017 It is being extended till 11.11.2019.
