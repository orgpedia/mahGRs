Government Resolution No. 1018/ PR No. 206/2018/ SWASAC-1
Preamble: Emergency was declared in India from 25.6.1975 to 31.3.1977. A policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period by providing financial assistance to them. The matter of sanctioning honorarium to eligible persons under this policy is under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The distribution of funds required to implement the policy of honouring/ suitably honouring persons who had to undergo imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977 is indicated in the statement attached to the Government Decision. This amount is Rs. 42,00,0, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crores only) which is required to be distributed to 4 districts (Raigad, Hingoli, Jalgaon, Thane) of the State. 72,75,000/- (Rupees Seventy Two Lakh Seventy Five Thousand only) for the period of 15 months from January 2018 to March 2019, as per the list of eligible applicants submitted by the respective District Collectors to the Government.
2. All District Collectors shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grants released to them and is spent only for matters approved by the Government. Also, the subsidy should be spent according to the monthly fund distribution system.
(3) Expenditure for the current financial year. A-5,2235-Social Security and Welfare, 102-Pension under Social Security Scheme, (00) (05) Financial assistance to persons fighting for democracy during the period of emergency (mandatory) 50 Other expenditure (2235C205) to be met from the approved budgetary provision for the financial year 2019-20 under this head and to be spent under the same head.
The honorarium should be sanctioned to the eligible applicant from 02.01.2018 as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 03.07.2018.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the approval given by the Department of Finance vide Non-official Reference No. 8/2019/ Expenditure-4 dated 03.01.2019.
