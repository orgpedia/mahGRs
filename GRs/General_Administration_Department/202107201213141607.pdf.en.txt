Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. Subject to the adjudication of the said petition, the concerned Short Writer is being given ad-hoc promotion in the lower category Short Writer cadre only on temporary basis to the vacant post in the open category: 1) The said promotion shall be ad-hoc promotion in the form of ad-hoc basis as an administrative exigency.
2) The said promotions are being given only on ad-hoc/ ad-hoc basis, considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Short Story Writer, and this seniority list has not been challenged.
(3) The said ad-hoc promotion is temporary in nature and the concerned shall not hold the post permanently.
4) The selection of eligible short story writers for the year 2020-2021 is ad-hoc in nature and promotions to the post of lower grade short story writer are ad-hoc in nature. As a result, shortlisted short writers will not be entitled to the regular post of lower grade short writer and seniority in service.

GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: ASC 1521/ PR No. 54/14-A, Dy. 20.07.2021 5) In case of non-acceptance of ad-hoc promotion to the post of Lower Grade Short Writer by the concerned Short Writer, the General Administration Department, vide Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ Q. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016, will remove his/ her name from the selection list for the year 2020-21 and will examine his/ her eligibility for ad-hoc promotion to the post of Lower Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The ad-hoc promotion of the said short story writer to the post of lower grade short story writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
The promotion of the short story writers belonging to the backward class is being done subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the short story writers has been issued by the concerned caste verification committee. The concerned department should look into it.
8) These orders shall come into force with immediate effect. Short story writers who have been promoted to the post of lower grade short story writer should be relieved immediately by the concerned department. Also, promoted lower grade scribes should join the posted department immediately. The concerned departments should send the copies of the orders issued by the concerned departments.
