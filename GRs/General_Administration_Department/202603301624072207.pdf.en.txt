The Government's Decisions:
It is proposed to approve the redeployment of Demand No. A-4 "2070, Other Administrative Services" and "2052, Secretariat - General Services" under the control of the General Administration Department and the mandatory provision in the year 2025-26 under this Principal Accounting Title.
Under the head of Principal Accounts "2070, Other Administrative Services" (00) (03) Arrangements to be made at the time of visits of Special Dignitaries (Compulsory / Deductible) (2070 0209), 50 - Saving in Other Expenditure Total Rs. 2,48,27, 978 / - (Rs. Two crore forty eight lakh twenty seven thousand nine hundred and eighty-eight only) along with the attached returns - 090 in "PRR, Secretariat - General Service," Secretariat, (00) (ov) Motorcars for Eminent Visitors (Compulsory / Dated) (2052 0043) -93, Office Expenses are being approved by the Government to be made available by way of refinance as indicated in the attached returns -2 for the scheme.
This Government decision has been taken by the Department of Finance in its informal Reference No. 225/2026 Expenditure-4, d. 23.03.2026 and Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Department of Finance. The Government Decision is being issued with the approval of the 25.03.2026 and also with the approval of the Secretary and V.S.A-2, General Administration Department.
