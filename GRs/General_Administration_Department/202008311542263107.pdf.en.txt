The said promotion shall be ad-hoc promotion in ad-hoc format as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary/ ad-hoc basis considering the fact that at present there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Lower Grade Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
(3) The said ad-hoc promotion is temporary in nature and the concerned shall not hold the post permanently.
4) The selection of eligible lower grade short writers for the selection year 2019-2020 is ad-hoc in nature and promotions to the post of upper grade short writers are ad-hoc in nature.
As a result, the lower category short-writers in the selection list will not get any rights to the regular post of upper category short-writer and seniority in service.
In case of non-acceptance of ad-hoc promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer by the concerned Lower Grade Short Writer, his/ her name will be deleted from the selection list for the year 2018-2019 as per General Administration Department, Government Resolution, No. SRV-2015/ Q.S. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for ad-hoc promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The ad-hoc promotion of the said lower grade short writer to the post of upper grade short writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion of lower category short writers in the backward class is being done subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the lower category short writers has been issued by the concerned caste verification committee. The concerned department should look into it.
