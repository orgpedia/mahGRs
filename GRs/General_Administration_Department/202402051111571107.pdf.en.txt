Preamble to the General Administration Department Government Decision. Under the 10/01/2023, the Military Pre-Service Education Institute for Girls has been established at Nashik to increase the representation of girls in the National Defence Academy (90 /) in the state. The government was considering a new sub-account title and an eight-digit computer code for the scheme.
Government Decisions General Administration Department Government Decisions. The Government is approving the introduction of a new head of account and eight-digit computer index for the Military Institute of Pre-Service Education, Nashik for girls in the state under the 10/01/2023 scheme, with the following headings:
