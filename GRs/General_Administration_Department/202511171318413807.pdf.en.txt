The Proposal:
The state of Maharashtra is the country's economically leading state, with Mumbai as the country's financial capital. Jhasana was also considering expanding the Royal Education Sub-Division under the General Administration Department to promote international trade, employment, cultural relations, Weary Marathi civic relations, and international tourism by attracting foreign investment and promoting new technologies, changes in the designation of Secretary and Chief Administrative Officer, as well as creation of three separate working groups - Foreign Direct Investment, Diaspora Affairs, and Outreach - with the required manpower.
The Government's Decisions:
Decisions are being taken regarding the expansion of the Administrative Sub-Division under the General Administration Department, change in the designation of Secretary and Chief Administrative Officer and creation of new posts for these posts as well as the subjects of these posts as follows: -
The designation of Secretary and Chief Administrative Officer in the Public Administration Sub-Division under the General Administration Department is being changed to Secretary (Public Administration, Foreign Direct Investment, Overseas Citizen Affairs and Liaison) / Secretary (Protocol, FDI, Diaspora Affairs and Outreach).
The Secretary (Diplomacy, Foreign Direct Investment, Various Citizen Matters and Liaison) will be the designated Chief Diplomat.
