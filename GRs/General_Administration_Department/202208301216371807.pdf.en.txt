Government Circular As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Government Secondary Ministry Service (Lower Level Clerks) Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, dated 6th June, 1977, applications in the prescribed format are being invited from the candidates who are eligible to appear for the first time in the post-entry training examination to be held in the year 2022 for Clerks and Clerks Typists in the Ministry Department as well as those who have failed or were absent in the previous examination.
2. The syllabus for the post-service training examination of the Clerk-Typist in the Ministry Department has been included in the basic training. Therefore, as in the previous year, the training of post-service training examination of clerks-typists in the ministerial department will be held during the basic training to be conducted under the State Training Policy.
The Maharashtra Government Secondary Ministry Service (Lower Level Clerks) Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977 contain a provision to withhold the increment in salary and to affect the seniority of the concerned clerk-typist if he does not pass the post-entry training examination on time and in time. Therefore, all the departments are being informed that instructions should be given to all the concerned clerks and typists to take this training seriously.
4. The prescribed format of application for post-entry training and examination of Clerk-Typist in the Ministry is available on the Ministry Intranet under the heading" Application Form." However, the applications in the prescribed format from the Clerk-Typists of the Ministry Department who are eligible to appear for the examination of the year 2022 for the first time as well as from the Clerk-Typists who have failed or are absent before the said examination, should be sent to this Department by the Establishment Officer after validating the same till 30th September, 2022. The establishment authorities should ensure that the application form contains correct information about the date of appointment of the candidate, details of the basic training of the candidate as well as the type of his appointment (direct service/ promotion/ compassionate), details of the opportunities of the examinations given earlier by the candidate, etc. Applicants must include their name, address, telephone number, and email address on the application form.
