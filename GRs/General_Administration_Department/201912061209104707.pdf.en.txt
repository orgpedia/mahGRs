The proposal: The Governor has accepted the recommendation of the 50th meeting of the Maharashtra State Tribal Advisory Council. 34 Notification regarding the filling up of direct service posts in 17 cadres notified in the Scheduled Areas. Issued on 29.8.2019. To recommend to the Government how to fill up the posts in the 17 cadres notified as per this notification.
The circular was issued after objections were raised to the revised point list regarding the increased reservation in the PESA sector along with the 13% reservation for the SEBC category. The suspension has been issued in pursuance of the Government decision dated 22.8.2019. The government had constituted a committee to look into the issue and submit a report.
Government's decision:
Hon'ble Governor, in the 50th meeting of the Maharashtra State Tribal Advisory Council, recommended No. 34 regarding the filling up of posts of direct service in 17 notified cadres in the Scheduled Areas. As per the notification issued on 29.8.2019, a committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector, Palghar to study and recommend to the government to fill the posts in the notified 17 cadres directly and to consider the objections to the increased reservation in the PESA sector with 13% reservation for the SEBC category.

The functions of the said Committee shall be as follows: 1. To study and recommend to the Government, the modalities for direct recruitment to the 17 notified cadres in the Scheduled Areas as per the notification dated 29.8.2019.
2) To recommend to the government to reconsider the increased reservation in the PESA sector in accordance with the 13% reservation of the SEBC category.
The committee has to submit its report to the government within 15 days.
