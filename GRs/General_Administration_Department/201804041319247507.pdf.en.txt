The Proposal:
No. 1 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education. Under the Government decision dated 06.06.2001, a company" Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited" was established. The cases related to this company were being handled by the Higher and Technical Education Department. Thereafter, in reference to the Department of Higher and Technical Education No. 2 2 mentioned herein d. As per the Government decision dated 05.01.2018, it was decided to transfer the subject" Matters/ Matters relating to the Company Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited" to the General Administration Department. The matter was under consideration to be transferred to the Information Technology Department of the General Administration Department.
2. To review the activities being carried out by the" Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited" and to use these activities in the functioning of the State Government. The Chief Minister presided over the meeting. The meeting was held on 05.03.2018. In this meeting, the coordination of all the matters related to Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited at the level of the state government should be done through the Information Technology Department. The Prime Minister has given.
Government's decision:
The coordination of all matters related to Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited at the level of the state government will be done through the Information Technology Office under the General Administration Department.
The government has taken the decision. Accordingly, all the cases/ matters related to the said company will henceforth be handled by the Department of Information Technology and coordination of all the matters at the level of the State Government.
