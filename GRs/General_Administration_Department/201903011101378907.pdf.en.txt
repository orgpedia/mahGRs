Government's decision:
A total of 24 temporary posts of Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer 6 posts, Short Writer (Lower Grade)- 6 posts, Typist- 6 posts and Constable- 6 posts have been created for conducting departmental inquiries of Group-A and Group-B gazetted officers in the State Service. Under the Government decision of October 28, 2009, the Regional Divisional Investigation Officer has been created in the office. These temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. 10 extended by the order. It will end on February 28, 2019. Therefore, the proposal to extend the tenure of these 24 temporary posts was under the consideration of the government.
Approval is being given for extension of 24 temporary posts in the accompanying prospectus for the period from 1st March, 2019 to 30th September, 2019.
