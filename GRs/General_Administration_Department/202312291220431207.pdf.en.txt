Publication of Government Decisions/ Circulars/ Notifications issued by the Administrative Department of the Ministry on Government Website
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: GAD- 32036/12/2023-GAD (DESK-18)
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: December 29, 2023
Reference: 1) Government Circular No: 2017/ Q. No. 117/18 (R.& K.) ), Dy. July 20, 2017
Government circular
All the Ministries/ Departments have been advised to ensure that the copies of the Government decisions/ circulars/ notifications issued by the administrative departments of the Ministry from time to time are not pasted on the Leader of the Opposition in both the Houses without fail and that the Government decisions/ circulars/ notifications are uploaded on the Government website. However, it has come to the notice of the government that the government decisions/ circulars/ notifications issued by many departments are not being published on the government website.
Therefore, all the Ministries/ Departments are again advised to ensure that the Government Decisions/ Circulars/ Notifications issued by their Departments are published on the Government Website without fail. Also, the government should take immediate action to publish the decisions/ circulars/ notifications on the website so that they can be easily accessible to the general public.
