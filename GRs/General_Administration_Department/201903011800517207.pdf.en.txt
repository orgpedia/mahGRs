Circular:
On the occasion of International Women's Day, the Brihanmumbai State Government Employees' Association, Mumbai is organizing a rally for the women employees of the Government of Mumbai on Friday, March 08, 2019 at Government Dental College and Hospital, Auditorium, St. George's Hospital, Near CST, Mumbai- 400 001 at 2.00 pm.
However, women employees of Government offices in Brihanmumbai are being allowed to leave the office at 1.30 pm on Friday, the 08th March, 2019 to attend the above function. All the Ministries/ Departments should make arrangements to communicate the same to their subordinate offices in Mumbai. However, due to this relaxation, the 4-hour relaxation given in office working hours should be replenished in the next week.
