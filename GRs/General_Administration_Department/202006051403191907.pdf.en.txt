Government Circular: In order to prevent the spread of the corona virus (COVID-19) in the state government offices, and to prevent the officers/ employees of the state from getting infected, various instructions have been issued under the government decisions at reference number 1, 2 and 5 of this department.
Attendance of employees in state government offices is being regulated during the lockdown period. Also, in view of such situations in the future, the government officers/ employees should take measures to dispose of the government work as per the office requirements and urgency, even while staying at their homes. For this, it will be acceptable to use government emails (such as NIC mail, etc.), other emails in their usual use, as well as WhatsApp for government business and to give instructions/ orders to the concerned.

Therefore, the following instructions are being issued to all the State Government officers/ employees:
(a) Every officer/ employee shall provide his/ her official e-mail ID/ other e-mail ID in his/ her usual use as well as mobile number with SMS/ WhatsApp facility to his/ her Head of Office.
For government work, the maximum disposal of their work should be done using the government email ID/ other email IDs of their usual use as well as WhatsApp.
c) Care should be taken to notify the concerned through SMS/ Whatsapp immediately after the proposal is forwarded through e-mail.
(d) A proposal submitted for final approval by email in accordance with the instructions received from superiors in the said manner shall be deemed to have been viewed, examined and approved by the officers at both the level of the submitter and the final approver.
The officers/ staff preparing the proposal should forward the proposal to the notice of all the concerned officers before finally forwarding it through e-mail and mark its copy (in C.C.) to all the concerned officers.
However, all Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries of the Ministries and all Heads of Departments and Offices under the Ministry are requested to bring these instructions to the notice of the officers/ staff under their control.
These instructions shall remain in force till further orders.
