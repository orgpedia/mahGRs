Introduction: DV vehicles have been placed under all the District Collector offices for the transportation of state guests/ dignitaries coming to the state. However, if several dignitaries come to visit the district at the same time and the D.V. vehicles are not enough, an agreement is made to provide vehicles on rent from private vehicle suppliers. As the previous tender agreement with the Collector, Nagpur office had ended, the e-tendering process was carried out through the Collector, Nagpur office for hiring vehicles on lease. After this tender process, the matter of approving the tenderer and the tender submitted by him in the e-tender process for Nagpur district was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is approving the Minimum Rate Sheet (Supplementary Return-A) received in the e-tendering process for providing vehicles on hire from private vehicle suppliers in the absence of availability of Government DV vehicles for transportation of the guests of honour and dignitaries visiting Nagpur district. Further, subject to the terms and conditions of the tender, the Government is also approving the availability of vehicles at this rate from the following suppliers who have quoted the Minimum Rate (L-1) in the e-tender.

(2) The agreement. 14/02/2023 to D. 13.02.2025 for a period of two years.
(3) The Government shall ultimately have the right to make any changes or amendments to this Agreement.
(4) If private vehicles are to be made available on hire, the Collector, Nagpur shall make the vehicles available only from the above Government approved suppliers.
(5) In case of inability of the above suppliers to provide vehicles in certain circumstances/ unavoidable reasons, the Collector, Nagpur is being authorized to make available vehicles from other suppliers at Government approved rates in such exceptional circumstances.
(6) The payment for the vehicle is to be made to the supplier within 3 months after it is delivered. After hiring the vehicles for this purpose, the Collector, Nagpur should immediately receive the payments regarding the supply of vehicles and submit them to the government without fail within 30 days along with his recommendation.
(7) The concerned suppliers shall immediately submit the payment to the District Collector, Nagpur after the supply of the vehicle.
(8) If other Government/ Semi-Government offices in Nagpur Division want to procure vehicles from private suppliers as per their requirement, they should make available vehicles from the respective suppliers as per the rates mentioned in the return. The expenditure thereon shall be borne by the concerned Government/ semi-government offices from their own provisions. The payment of these expenses need not be certified by the office of the Collector, Nagpur. For this, do not send unnecessary correspondence or demand letter to the office of the Collector, Nagpur.
