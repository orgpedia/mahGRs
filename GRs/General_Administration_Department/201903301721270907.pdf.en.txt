Memorandum: Based on the final results of the Saral Seva Clerk-Typist (Marathi/ English) Main Examination 2017 held for appointment to the post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi/ English) in Group-C, the candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the original list were allocated to the post of Clerk-Typist in various offices of the Ministry Department and Brihanmumbai under the Memorandum dated 04.07.2018. However, instead of the candidates who did not join, the reference No. 2 As per the provisions of the Government Circular here, the Commission was informed about the availability of candidates in the waiting list. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) had released the waiting list of 189 candidates on 23.01.2019. The 186 candidates in the list were allotted as per the memorandum dated 08.02.2019.
Out of the 189 candidates who were provided by the Commission from the waiting list, the candidates of the open category of Clerk-Typist (Marathi), who were recommended as ex-servicemen, in the recommendation number 145, 146 and 152, had informed through an affidavit that they would not accept the appointment of Clerk-Typist (Marathi). Accordingly, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission was informed vide letter dated 08.02.2019 to provide the following 03 Ex-Servicemen in lieu of the said candidates. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC), vide its letter dated 01.03.2019, has recommended 03 candidates for the post of Clerk-Typist.
These candidates are being allotted the post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi) in the Ministry Department indicated against their names in the accompanying prospectus' A'.
