As per the directions of the Election Commission of India, the work for the upcoming Lok Sabha General Elections, 2019 in the state has been started. Stating that it is very important to complete this important and time-bound work within the prescribed time frame, the Principal Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State had requested to provide the services of Assistant Cell Officer and Clerk Typist of the ministerial cadre to the General Administration Department/ Election Branch (Office Post-33) by transferring them.
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the following Clerk-Typist are being transferred inter-departmentally and posted in the General Administration Department/ Election Branch (Office Post-33) mentioned in Column-5 against their names:
