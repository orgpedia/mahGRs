Preamble Under the Finance Department, Government Resolution, dated 10.09.2001, a policy has been prescribed to review the posts in all government offices under the State Government and fix the revised figure. Accordingly, a proposal to prescribe a revised pattern of posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad, was under consideration of the government.
A total of 20 posts, including 8 regular posts in the cadre 1 to 8 and 12 supernumerary posts in the cadre 9 to 18, have been revised. The approval was given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary in its meeting dated 03.08.2019. Accordingly, the revised pattern of posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad, as indicated in Annexure A, is being approved by this Government Decision.
