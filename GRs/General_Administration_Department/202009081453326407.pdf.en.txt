Government Decision: A total of 24 temporary posts have been created in the office of the Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer under the Government Decision dated 28th October, 2009 for conducting departmental inquiries against the Gazetted officers of Group A and Group B in the State Service, namely, 6 posts of Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer, 6 posts of Short Writer (Lower Grade), 6 posts of Typist and 6 posts of Constable. These temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The extension granted vide order dated 15.08.2020 is expiring on 31.08.2020. Therefore, the proposal to extend the tenure of these 24 temporary posts was under the consideration of the government.
Approval for extension of 24 temporary posts in the accompanying prospectus for the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
