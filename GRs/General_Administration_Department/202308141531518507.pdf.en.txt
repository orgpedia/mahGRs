The date is August 14, 2022.
As per the Government Circular Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Cell Officers) Divisional Examination Rules, 1977 dated 6th June, 1977, the Cell Officers Divisional Examination is conducted once in a year for promotion to the post of Cell Officers of Assistant Cell Officers. However, it has been decided to conduct two Cell Officer Departmental Examinations in the year 2023 to regularize the gap of one year due to non-conduct of the examination in the year 2020 due to outbreak of COVID-19.
Accordingly, the first examination of the year 2023 has been organized in May, 2023 and now the second departmental examination will be organized. Applications are invited from the candidates who will be appearing in this examination for the first time.
Further, it may be noted that after the year 2023, the Departmental Examination for promotion to the post of Cell Officer will be conducted once every year as per the provisions of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Cell Officers) Departmental Examination Rules, 1977 dated 6th June, 1977.
Therefore, the candidates having first chance and possessing the following qualifications are required to submit their applications to this Department through the Establishment Branch of their Department by 31st August, 2023 in the attached form:
As per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Services Departmental Examination Rules, 1977, the Assistant Cell Officers (Law) in the Ministerial Cadre/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission and Assistant Cell Officers (Law) in the Department of Law and Justice have passed the post-entry training examination for the post of Assistant Cell Officer and have been appointed to the said post. Five years of uninterrupted service is being completed by October 31, 2023. "
Separate applications will be invited from all the candidates whose 2nd Zonal Examination in the year 2023 is not a first chance examination.
