Preamble: Department of General Administration A Public Trust namely" Chief Minister's Relief Fund" has been set up under the Government Decision dated 02/03/1967 with the objective of providing financial assistance to the victims of natural calamities in the State/ Country. Since the Trust has been receiving a large number of requests and recommendations from public representatives for financial assistance to persons affected by causes other than natural calamities, it has been decided to extend the scope of the mission of the Public Trust as per the provisions of the Bombay Public Trust System Act, 1950, vide Government Resolution General Administration Department, dated 15/11/2001.
2. m. A Public Interest Litigation has been filed in the Bombay High Court against the' Chief Minister's Relief Fund" trust. The case has been filed in Bombay High Court. Accordingly, a revised procedure should be followed and comprehensive rules should be framed for sanctioning financial assistance to charitable institutions from the Chief Minister's Relief Fund. The Government has issued a decision dated 15.2.2010.
3) Chief Minister's Relief Fund. Since a PIL has been filed in the Bombay High Court, the fund is not being used for any other purpose except medical assistance and natural calamities.
At present, the Chief Minister's Relief Fund is a separate trust under the Public Trusts Act, 1950. The Chief Minister is the chairman of the trust. The trust provides financial assistance to needy patients from economically weaker sections of the state for their treatment and surgery. The work of the Trust is carried out as per the orders of the Chief Minister. Due to the imposition of President's rule in the state, the work of this trust has been suspended as the officers/ employees currently working in the Chief Minister's Office have been transferred/ transferred to the service department. Therefore, it is necessary to formulate the procedure to be followed for providing assistance for medical treatment to the needy patients of economically weaker sections from the Chief Minister's Relief Fund, so the following government order is being issued regarding the activities of the Chief Minister's Relief Fund in the state.

Government Order: Chief Minister's Relief Fund With the approval of the Hon'ble Chief Minister, this fund was providing financial assistance to needy patients from economically weaker sections for their treatment and surgery. Due to the imposition of President's rule in the state, the services of the officers/ employees currently working in the Chief Minister's Office have been transferred/ transferred to the concerned department, due to which the process of providing financial assistance on medical grounds from the Chief Minister's Relief Fund has been temporarily stopped.
To ensure that the needy patients do not have to be deprived of financial assistance for medical assistance due to the imposition of President's rule in the state, temporary arrangements are being made for providing financial assistance to the needy patients of economically weaker sections for their treatment and surgery from the Chief Minister's Assistance Fund until the imposition of President's rule.
