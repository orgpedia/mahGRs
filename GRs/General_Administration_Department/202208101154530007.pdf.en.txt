The Proposal:
The Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 27 th May, 2022 to prepare a draft of the Manual of Good Governance for making governance accountable, accessible, clean, transparent and expeditious and submit the report of the Committee to the Government. The committee has been given six months to prepare a draft of the Manual of Good Governance and submit the report of the committee to the government along with the draft.
2. The proposal for payment of honorarium and travel allowance etc. to the Chairman and members of this Committee was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Chairman and Members of the Committee will be entitled to the following honorarium and transportation to attend the meetings of the Committee to prepare the draft of the Manual of Good Governance and submit the report of the Committee to the Government.
The Chairman of the Committee for the entire period of the Committee to submit the draft of the Manual of Good Governance and the report of the Committee, a lump sum of Rs. 6.00 lakh (Rupees Six Lakh only) and Rs. 4,80,000/- (Rs. Only four lakh rupees will be given.
The Chairman and Members of the Committee will be provided vehicles on lease basis at the fixed rates of vehicles hired from private suppliers through the office of the General Administration Department, dated 10th August, 2020 or Government Transport Service, Mumbai, as amended from time to time, to attend the meetings to be held for the work of the Committee. The expenses for this will be reimbursed by the General Administration Department.
The above expenses are covered by the Financial Powers Rules, 1978. 17th April, 2015 Part-I, Sub-Division-II, serial number 13, is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Rules 60 and 61.
