GOVERNMENT SURFACE: The direct service cell officers as well as the promoted cell officers are required to submit a report on the seniority of the ministerial cell officers in respect of the period from 1986 to 2000 as on 1st January, 2020. High Court, Bombay vide W.P.No. 2986/2000, W. NO. 2982/2000, W. P.No. 4958/2003 and W. P. No.
Petition No. 3374/2011 was filed. Pursuant to the order dated 06.08.2018, the Ministerial Cell Officers of the cadre of Dy. Seniority from 01.01.1986 onwards has been revised. Consequently, the seniority of the Under Secretary/ Deputy Secretary cadre has been reviewed. The circular was issued to invite objections/ suggestions to the revised provisional seniority list for the period 2005-2017 for the post of Deputy Secretary in the Ministry. Accordingly, the objections received are being resolved, communicated to the concerned and finalised subject to the following pending court cases of the said seniority:
1. m. The High Court of Bombay vide W.P.No. 2986/2000, W.P.No. 2982/2000, W.P.No. 4958/2003 and W.P. P. No. 3374/2011. against the order dated 06.08.2018. Special Leave Petition (SLP) No. 25181/2018 pending in the Supreme Court.
2. m. The Bombay High Court vide Petition No. 2797/2015. Against the order dated 04.08.2017 regarding reservation in promotions, the State Government has filed a Special Leave Petition (SLP) No. 28306/2017.
All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to obtain the copies of the final seniority certificates of the said Deputy Secretary Cadre from the website. Also, to bring this seniority to the notice of all concerned (including retired officers) in your department, inform them about making a copy of the seniority list available from the website.
