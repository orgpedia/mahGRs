The Proposal:
In order to ensure that the work of the common man is done within the stipulated time frame, the State Government has launched the" Apna Sarkar Seva Portal" in the year 2015. The work is expected to be completed within the stipulated time frame. However, on a review of the portal and the complaints received from the citizens at the Ministry level, it is observed that the applications/ grievances of the concerned citizens are not being disposed of by the competent authorities within the prescribed time frame.
