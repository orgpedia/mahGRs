As per the General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 20.01.2014, Maharashtra Public Service Commission is conducting a combined probationary training programme through Vanamati, Nagpur for the posts in Group-B to be recruited directly every year through the State Service Examination. Accordingly, the 15 candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for the post of Cell Officer on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2019 have been appointed as Trainee Cell Officers for the two-year Combined Competitive Training Programme-7 (CPTP-7) with effect from 17.01.2022 vide order dated 24.12.2021 and 13.01.2022 and they have been recruited with effect from 17.01.2022 (M.P.) vide office order dated 08.02.2022.
2. Integrated Peripheral Training Programme under reference No. It has been decided to implement the Government Decision 1 to 2 within a period of two years. The training in the Account-wise District Affiliation Training Phase of the Integrated Peripheral Training Programme is to be imparted by the concerned Administrative Department. Accordingly, the 14 probationary cell officers appointed in CPTP-7 will be trained for department-wise district affiliation training. The date under 7 will start from 15.11.2022.

A temporary posting is being made in the department shown in front of his name. The officers of this section should join the department immediately.
The following instructions are given for the implementation of this training program:
(1) For this purpose, the concerned administrative department shall appoint Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Establishment) as the coordinating officer and he shall submit the evaluation report to the General Administration Department.
(2) The administrative departments shall provide training to the probationary cell officers as prescribed in paragraph 3.
(3) Separate charge of the post of Clerk Typist and Assistant Cell Officer- During the period from 16.01.2023 to 19.03.2023, the trainee should be given separate charge of the post of Clerk Typist and Assistant Cell Officer in his department. Orders in this regard will be issued by the department.
Separate charge of the post of Cell Officer:- During the period from 20.03.2023 to 16.01.2024, the Subordinate Cell Officer is to be given separate charge of the post of Cell Officer. Accordingly, the concerned department should pass orders for appointment to the post of Cell Officer in the work force in which the vacancies/ trainees in their area can work with maximum efficiency.
(5) In case of any difficulty/ technical problem faced by the trainee in separate assignments, the concerned Coordinating Officer shall resolve it immediately.
(6) During the training period, the establishment branch of the concerned department shall keep a record of the attendance of the probation officers through biometric time system. The probationary officers should be given the identity card of the department concerned.
(7) During the training period, the training as well as the independent charge of the post of clerk, assistant cell officer, etc.
At the end of each stage of training, the concerned department should submit the attendance sheet and evaluation report of the probation officers to this department without fail. A sample report is attached.
(Statement B) (8) During the period of training, the salary of the above probationary cell officers will be paid by the General Administration Department.
(9) The training of the trainee shall be subject to surprise inspection by the General Administration Department.
Also, officers directed by Joint Secretary/ Deputy, S.P.V./ K. 14B of General Administration Department will be present during the presentation of the trainee.
(10) General Administration. On receipt of instructions from CPTPB, the probationary cell officers will be relieved by the concerned department to join Vanamati, Nagpur for review and revision training before completion of training.
Since the probationary cell officers will serve in various departments of the Ministry for about 30 years after the completion of their probation period, the concerned departments should provide necessary support to the training of their above independent assignments to make it fruitful.
