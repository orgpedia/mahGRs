The distribution of financial assistance payable in respect of the deceased soldier is divided as follows. (i) If the martyr is married and his parents are also alive, then a) his brave wife- 60%, b) the brave mother- 20%, the brave father- 20%, c) if the mother or father of one of his parents is alive, 40%, 2) the martyr soldier is married and if his parents are not alive, his brave wife should be given 100%.
