Preamble: GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the General Administration Department, dated 20.01.2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2020-21, the Government was considering to disburse funds to Vasantrao Naik Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur under the objective of 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) under the article heading A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Subsidiary Grant (20700862) to Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Schedules. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2020-21, the Institute of Accounts Heading A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. 70,94,000/- and 31-Additional Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,86,07, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,57,01, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Seven Lakhs One Thousand only) has been approved.
In the context of the pandemic situation due to Corona Virus, as per the circular of the Department of Finance at Annexure-II, the budgetary provisions for the mandatory and programme expenditure for the financial year 2020-21 have been distributed through the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS) for the first three months (end of June 2020) in the range of 15-25% of the total annual allocation.
Accordingly, for the financial year 2020-21, under the head of Accounts Title A-4, Auxiliary Grants for Training Programme under Integrated Schedules (20700862) to the Institute of 1970-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, an amount of Rs. 4,09,400/- for the month of May 2020 and Rs. 3,00,000/- in total. 7,09,400/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Nine Thousand Four Hundred only) has been disbursed by the Bureau of Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BIMS) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The Director, Vanamati, Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grant disbursed and shall ensure that the amount is drawn from the Treasury by depositing in the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
