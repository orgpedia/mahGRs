Government Circular: -
The department receives cases of disciplinary action of government officers / employees from other administrative departments for feedback / consent. On receipt of such proposals, feedback is provided by the General Administration Department as per rules after examining all the steps taken in line with the departmental inquiry or how the case has been handled as per the prescribed procedure.
If the proposals received do not include details of misconduct, service details of misconduct, chargesheets, vigilance reports, findings of the investigating officers and the department on the report, etc., and the relevant documents regarding certain details are not made available with the proposal, the general administration department has difficulty in examining and giving a clear feedback on the proposals due to lack of complete and clear details. It also takes time to follow up with departments for incomplete information. These factors cause delays in providing feedback on proposals received.
If the proposals submitted by the departments to the General Administration Department for concurrence / feedback include details of disciplinary cases in the prescribed format and details of malpractices, it will be possible for such TEMA General Administration Department to act expeditiously. Similarly, it will be convenient for departments to submit proposals. If there is a stage left in the handling of a departmental inquiry, it can be addressed at the departmental level itself by noticing it while submitting comments.
For this purpose, comments regarding the case of disciplinary action received by the General Administration Department have been drafted and are being communicated to all the Ministries / Administrative Departments that while submitting the draft of the letter to be sent to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the motion of impeachment, motion of punishment and final punishment of the offender received from the departments should be submitted in the comment forms attached to this circular.
