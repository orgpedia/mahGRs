Government circular
The provisional additional provisional seniority list of Selected Category Scribes in the Ministry Department as on 01.01.2020 is annexed herewith as Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the cadre of Selected Category Shorts in their department the present provisional additional provisional seniority as on 01.01.2020. In this regard, the information regarding the issues in the representations received from the concerned selected category scribes regarding objections/ errors should be examined as per the records of the department and sent to this department along with the opinion of the department by 30.09.2020. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the interim seniority will be published assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
