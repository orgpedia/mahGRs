the order
Ms. Shaila Jangam, Cell Officer, Water Resources Department is being appointed on deputation for the post of" Assistant Director" in the Pune Archives Office under the Directorate of Archives, Mumbai under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs for a period of two years with the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. Shaila Jangam, Cell Officer will start her deputation from the date she joins the post of Deputation, after which her deputation period will end on the date she joins her parent Department by handing over the charge of the post of Deputation after two years.
The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981, the Department of Water Resources and the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs have decided to appoint Shri. The terms and conditions regarding the deputation of Shaila Jangam should be decided.
Sir 3. All other administrative matters of Shaila Jangam, Cell Officer will be handled by Water Resources Department.
Sir 4. The Water Resources Department should immediately relieve Shaila Jangam, the Cell Officer, to accept the appointment on deputation.
