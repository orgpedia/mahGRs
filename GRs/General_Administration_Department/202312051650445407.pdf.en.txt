GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: MAVAJ 2023/ PR No.249/ MAVAJ 1
The following issues related to journalists will be studied by this study group: (1) Increasing the scope/ number of supernatural journals.
(2) Determining the criteria for digital media. To study the policy of the Central Government in this regard and make necessary amendments in the state policy accordingly.
(3) To establish Press Clubs at other important places in the State on the lines of the Press Club, Nagpur.
(4) To formulate a policy for journalists to get medical treatment in government hospitals, to formulate a cashless medical insurance scheme for them.
The study group should submit a report to the government through the Director General, Information and Public Relations.
4. Government Resolution of the Finance Department for the non-official members of the said Committee to attend the meeting, No. Travel-1010/ Q.No.2/ Service-5, dated 3.3.2010 in para 12
As per the provision, travel allowance and daily allowance will be admissible but they will not be allowed to travel first class in air travel and railway air conditioning.
