As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, under the order No. 1, the general transfers of officers of the cadre of Under Secretary in the Ministry Department have been made.
Some of the officers mentioned in the said order have not yet been relieved by the concerned departments, and some of the departments have requested this department to extend/ postpone/ cancel the transfer of officers, which is being denied.
