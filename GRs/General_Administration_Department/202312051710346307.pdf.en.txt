The Proposal:
As per the Maharashtra Government's Office Procedure and Rules Manual, 2023, the government was considering appointing and disbursing funds to Kaizen Institute, a tertiary institution, through QCI, on a pilot basis for three months to improve postal management, promote use of e-office, clear backlog of old bills, streamline mapping, digitalization, gap analysis process, identify countermeasures and improve workplace efficiency. For that, Ma. Office of the Prime Minister, Office of the Prime Minister, The Chief Secretary's office and the Central Post Office have been selected on a pilot basis. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to take a policy decision to fulfill the above mentioned points.
Government's decision:
As per the Maharashtra Government's Office Procedures and Rules Manual 2023, the Kaizen Institute through the QCI will be responsible for postal management, promoting the use of e-office, clearing the backlog of old bills, mapping, digitalization, streamlining the gap analysis process, identifying countermeasures and improving workplace efficiency. 67,20,000/- (Rupees Sixty Five Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
