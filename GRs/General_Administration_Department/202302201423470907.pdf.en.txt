Reference: Government Resolution, No.Shakaja-1122/ Q.No. 55/2022/22, dated 7.9.2022
Preamble: With a view to consolidate the Department of Ministerial Administration on a single floor, the Ministry has decided to shift the office of National Informatics Centre (NIC) from the 3rd floor of the main building to the 11th floor of the new Administration Building, 310 Sq. ft. of space was allocated. As this space is not sufficient for the purpose of functioning, the office of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) has been shifted to the 11th floor of the new Administration Building under the control of the Department of Information and Technology. The proposal to allot the land was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The office of the National Informatics Centre (NIC), located on the third floor of the Mantralaya Main Building, is located at 238 Sq. Space is being allocated. Similarly, the office of National Informatics Centre (NIC) has been shifted to the 11th floor of the new Administration Building, East Wing, with a total area of 548 sq. Space is being allocated.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
