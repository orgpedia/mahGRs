Preamble: A meeting was held on 17.2.2022 at 11.30 am under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services) regarding the renovation of the working halls in the General Administration Department. In the meeting, instructions have been given to form a core committee to inspect the space allotted to the working departments of the ministry and submit a report. Accordingly, the following committee has been formed:
Government Decision: In the light of the above proposal, the following committee is being constituted to inspect the location of administrative departments functioning in the Ministry. The composition of the Committee shall be as follows:
