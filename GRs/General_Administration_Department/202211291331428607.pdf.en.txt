Introduction: Refer No. 1 During the winter session of the Assembly in Nagpur. The Nagpur Accommodation Committee was constituted with the approval of the then Chief Minister to provide accommodation, office, vehicle and telephone facilities to the Ministers/ State Ministers and officers/ staff who went to Nagpur for the session. A new government has been formed in the state. In such a situation, it is necessary to reconstitute the committee constituted under the said Government Decision dated October 13, 2020. Besides constituting the committee, the government was considering issuing guidelines to bring clarity on the decisions to be taken by the committee and the action to be taken by all the concerned departments with the approval of the committee during the Nagpur winter session. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Government Resolution No.3214/ No. 75/ 7, D.C., regarding the formation of' Nagpur Accommodation Committee' to look after the arrangements during the session at Nagpur. 11th November, 2014 and Government Resolution No.3219/ No.86/ Office- 7, D. 9th December, 2019 and Insurance 3219/ S.No.86/ Office- 7, D. The Government has decided to reconstitute the" Nagpur Accommodation Committee" as per the order dated 13th October, 2020.

Meeting of the Nagpur Accommodation Committee: The meeting of the Committee shall be held one month before the proposed session at Nagpur. Before that, the Public Works Department should prepare a plan regarding the accommodation/ office arrangements. Since most of the work before the committee is related to the Public Works Department, the meeting will be organised by the Public Works Department. During the meeting, the details of the preparations for the session to be held during the session by the departments related to the Nagpur session arrangement will be presented to the approval of the committee. Further action on the same should be taken by the concerned departments/ offices of the General Administration Department as approved by the Committee in the meeting. After the approval of the committee, if any changes are required to be made as per the demand received from the ministerial departments regarding an issue, the concerned department should take further action with the approval of the committee.
The minutes of the meeting shall be issued by the Public Works Department in accordance with the decisions taken by the Committee at the meeting.
The decision taken by the said Committee on the arrangements for the Nagpur Session shall be final.
