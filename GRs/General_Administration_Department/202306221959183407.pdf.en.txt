2. m. The High Court of Bombay, in Writ Petition No. 2797/2015. As per the decision dated 4.8.2017, reservation in promotion has been declared invalid. Against this order. The Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. Subject to the Judgment No. 28306/2017 and subject to the following conditions, the concerned Selected Category Short Writers are being promoted to the vacant post in Senior Personal Assistant Cadre on purely temporary basis:
(1) The promotion shall be of temporary nature as an administrative exigency.
2) The said promotion is being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Selected Category Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
(3) The promotion is only temporary and there will be no permanent retention of the post.
The selection of eligible candidates for the post of Senior Personal Assistant for the year 2022-2023 is provisional and promotion to the post of Senior Personal Assistant is provisional.

As a result, the selected category of scribes will not be entitled to the post of Senior Personal Assistant Regular and no seniority in the service.
In case of non-acceptance of provisional promotion to the post of Senior Personal Assistant, their names will be deleted from the selection list for the year 2022-2023 as per General Administration Department, Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ Q. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and their eligibility will be checked for provisional promotion to the post of Senior Personal Assistant in the third year selection list without considering their names in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The temporary promotion of the said selected category of short writers to the post of Senior Personal Assistant is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental enquiry has been initiated or proposed against them till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion of Selected Category Short Writers of Backward Classes is being made subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the State Merit List with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the said officers has been issued by the concerned Caste Verification Committee. The concerned department should look into it.
(8) This order shall come into force with immediate effect. The selected category of short writers who have been promoted to the post of Senior Personal Assistant should be relieved immediately by the concerned department. Also, the selected Short-listed candidates promoted as per para (d) (3) of the Government Notification dated 21.6.2021 should join the department in which they are posted within thirty days from the date of issue of this order. The concerned departments should send the copies of the orders issued by the concerned departments.
