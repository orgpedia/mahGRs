The Proposal:
Ganesh Sridhar Ramdasi, Deputy Director (Information) Group-A, (Pay Scale S-23: 67700-208700/-), Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, has been appointed as Group-A Deputy Director (Information) in the Department vide Government Resolution No. Mavaj 2019/ PR No. 32/34-A dated 19.8.2019 for the post of Director (Information) Group-A (Pay Scale S-25: 78880-209200/-) on ad-hoc promotion to the post of Director (Information), Marathwada Division, Aurangabad for a period of 11 months or till the availability of candidates on nomination, whichever is earlier. The matter of ad-hoc promotion, continuation of the promotion quota post till it falls vacant and grant of only temporary promotion to such post as the promotion quota post falls vacant was under consideration of the government. In this regard, the Board of Directors. The Board in its meeting held on 18.2.2021 has recommended the promotion of Shri Ramadasi to the post of Director (Information) on temporary basis.
Government Decision: In view of the recommendation made by the Board of Establishment, the Government has taken the following decision regarding the promotion of Mr. Ganesh Sridhar Ramdasi, Deputy Director (Information) Group-A, to the post of Director.
(a) Mr. Ganesh Sridhar Ramdasi has been promoted to the post of Director (Information). on 20.7.2020. Further continuation during 31.8.2020 is being approved.
(b) Shri Ganesh Shridhar Ramdasi. The promotion to the post of Director (Information) with effect from 01.09.2020 is purely on temporary basis.
The above promotion is subject to the following conditions: 1. Hon. The promotion is being granted purely on temporary basis subject to the decision in the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in the Supreme Court.
Ad-hoc promotions for the period from 20.7.2020 to 31.8.2020 will not be eligible for any benefit of in-service promotion.
Ganesh Ramdasi, Director, is being transferred and posted as Director (Administration and Technical Cell) against the Government direction and Shri Ajay Ambekar, Director is being transferred and posted as Director (Information/ News and Public Relations).
