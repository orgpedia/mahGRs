The training for the Post-Interview Training Examination 2023 for the post of Assistant Section Officer in the Ministry Cadre and Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and Assistant Section Officer (Law) in the Department of Law and Justice will be held at the Conference Hall, 6th Floor, Mantralaya Extension Building, Mumbai. All the Ministries/ Departments and the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned Assistant Section Officers (including candidates on deputation/ leave) in writing and allow them to attend the above training classes.
