Preamble: Regarding the preparation and implementation of the media plan by the concerned departments for the promotion of government schemes through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. Orders have been issued here under the Government Order dated 21st July, 2017. Under this order, the procedure for preparing and implementing the media plan has been laid down. The government was contemplating to amend the act. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: Regarding the preparation and implementation of the Media Plan, the above reference No. 3 The Committee shall prepare the Media Plan prepared by the Committee referred to in Government Resolution No. 4 dated 21st July, 2017. This section includes the following points:
4.1) While preparing this plan, the Secretary of the concerned department or the officer authorized by him shall inform the Secretary of the concerned department or the Secretary of the concerned department. The Chief Minister's Officer on Special Duty (Media Advisor) should be consulted and the concerned Director (Information) should provide necessary assistance in this regard.
4.2) is the plan. Special Officer to Chief Minister It should be submitted to the Chief Minister.
4.3). After the approval of the Chief Minister, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations will implement the plan.
