Borge, Cell Officer, Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department, under the Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission under the aegis of the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation, State Mission Management Cell, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai is being handed over on deputation to the post of Cell Officer, Navi Mumbai office for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mrs. Borge shall commence her term of service as a Deputy on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, hand over the charge of the post of Deputy Commissioner, one year thereafter.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) Mrs. Borge, the Cell Officer shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ Competent Authority that she intends to return to the parent department.

(e) Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981, the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation (Scheme-III) and the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection shall decide the terms and conditions of Ms. Borge's deputation in consultation with each other.
All the administrative matters related to the deputation of Mrs. Borge will be handled by the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation (Scheme-3) as well as the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
