The memo:
The final result of the Clerk Typist Examination-2017 held for appointment to the post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi/ English), Group C. The Bombay High Court, vide its order No. 2053/2014 Subject to the final outcome in the case of Shri Sanjeev Shukla Vs. Government of Maharashtra and other connected petitions, the candidates in the waiting list No. 1 recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and included in the selection list, were allotted to the vacant post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi/ English) in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and various Government offices in Brihanmumbai as indicated against their names in the statement A and B attached with the memorandum dated 08.02.2019. Recommendation No. Re-allotment of candidates in 6, 32, 103, 108, 112 and 149 is being done on administrative grounds as shown in the accompanying return A. The candidates should join the revised allotted Department immediately (by 8th April, 2019) and submit the report to the General Administration Department (14-A). Also, the service excellence of the above mentioned candidates will be as per the candidates who have joined as per the due allotment list in the Government Notification dated 08.02.2019.
The other terms and conditions of appointment of the candidates re-allotted under the said order to the post of Clerk-Typist shall be as mentioned in the notification dated 08.02.2019.
