Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department of the state government has issued a notification in this regard. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In pursuance of the directions in the said Government Order, the Office of the Secretary to the Governor and the Office of the Family Manager at Raj Bhavan Secretariat under the General Administration Department, as per the Government Order dated 5.7.2021,

Posts have been ensured for the disabled category in Group-A to Group-D cadres. The exclusion of some of the posts has been approved by the Expert Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Social Justice) in its meeting dated 21.6.2021.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities. The Committee of Experts constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Social Justice in its meeting held on 21.6.2021 has approved the exclusion of the following posts in the office of Secretary to Governor and in the office of Family Manager in Raj Bhawan Secretariat under General Administration Department from the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities.
