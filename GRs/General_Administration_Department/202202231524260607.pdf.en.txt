Circular dated 17.12.2021 and dated 30.12.2021
Government circular:
In the circular dated 17th December 2021 and 30th December 2021, it has been instructed that the confidential reports for the years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 in respect of the officers involved in the Mahapara will be closed in the Mahapara system on 31st January 2022. Accordingly, action has been taken and arrangements have been made to ensure that the status of these assessment reports remains final.
However, there are still some cross-references pending at the level of the concerned officer/ reporting officer/ reviewing officer. Also, there are still requests from some officials to extend the deadline, however, it will not be technically possible to extend the deadline as it has now been closed in all cross systems. However, in view of the requests received for extension of time, the performance appraisal reports for the years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 are pending at the above (Reporting/ Reviewing Officer) stages of online mode. The process of mailing to this e-mail ID should be done by the concerned versioning officer by 28.2.2022.
All the concerned Reporting/ Reviewing Officers and Editing Officers should note that the crosses received after 28.2.2022 will not be uploaded in the system.
All pending performance appraisal reports up to and before 2020-21 in respect of officers/ staff not included in the Mahapar should be finalised by 28 th February, 2022.
