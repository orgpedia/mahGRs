The State Government Group-D (Class IV) Employees Central Federation, Maharashtra, has called for a strike to press for various demands of State Government Class IV employees. A notice for a State-wide symbolic strike on 29.1.2021 has been issued to the Government.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979. Under Section 6, government employees are prohibited from going on strike. Further, the State Government Group-D (Class IV) Employees Central Federation, Maharashtra has been recognized under Rule No. 29 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Many local Class IV employees' unions across the state are affiliated to the federation. Therefore, the possibility of the fourth-class employees of all state government offices in the state joining the state-wide strike cannot be ruled out.

In order to ensure smooth functioning of the Government during the strike period on 29th January, 2021, all the Ministries/ Departments and their subordinate offices are hereby directed to take the following measures: (a) Participation in the strike by Government/ Paramilitary employees, which will be treated as misconduct and disciplinary action will be taken against such employees, such orders shall be issued without delay and shall be brought to the notice of all the employees in person. A copy of the order should also be pasted on the notice board of every government department/ office.
b) Employees should try to persuade them to attend office as usual, without participating in the strike.
c) Proper arrangements should be made for opening and closing of the office at regular time during the strike period and the arrangements should be made by the responsible officer. If necessary, seek the help of the police.
(d) Heads of Departments/ Offices should not leave their headquarters during the strike period.
(e) The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office shall not grant any leave to any Government employee from the date of this order till the end of the strike and the employee who is on leave.
