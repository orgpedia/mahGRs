A large number of posts have been sanctioned for the office of the Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State. As the officers/ staff appointed to this post have to be provided with computers, printers and scanners, the matter of purchasing computer materials for this office was under the consideration of the government.
To make it possible for the office of the Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra to provide computers, printers and scanners to the officers/ staff appointed in the office of the Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra, and to make it possible to provide new computers, printers and scanners immediately in case of loss of working computers, printers and scanners, administrative approval is being given to purchase 20 computers, 20 printers and 10 scanners with the following specifications:
