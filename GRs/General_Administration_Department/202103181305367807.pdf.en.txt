GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 10th June, 2019 of the General Administration Department, a consolidated procedure has been prescribed for premature retirement of Government officers/ employees after reviewing their services beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service. The Government decision provides that the committee to be constituted to review the service of the officers/ employees shall consist of a senior officer of Group-A in addition to the Head of the Department as the Chairman and a senior officer of another Group-A of the Department as 1 member.
The Director, Class-A is the Head of Department and Head of Office in the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad, which is under the control of this Department. No other Group-A posts are allowed in this office. Therefore, as a special case, the Committee to be constituted to review the service of the officers/ staff of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service, is being constituted as per the approval received as follows:
1) Chairman Under-Secretary, General Administration Department (Works. 28, Mumbai.
(In the absence of senior officers of Group A other than the Head of the Department) 2) Member Deputy Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune (Senior most officer) (Group A officers in other field offices under the control of the Department)
3) Member District Sainik Welfare Officer, Aurangabad.
Member Secretary Superintendent-cum-Accountant, (Group-B Gazetted), Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad
