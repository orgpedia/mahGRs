Introduction: To carry out the operation and maintenance of LED screens owned by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations in the premises of the new Administration Building, Mumbai. India Post Ltd. The institute was appointed by the Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS), Mumbai for the period 2018-2020. Due to COVID-19, the operation and maintenance of LED screens were stopped from April 2020 to June 2020. The operation and maintenance of LED screens has been going on since July 2020, according to an oral order issued by the Chief Minister's Secretariat. Since the term of the Institute expired in March, 2020, the Government has decided to grant administrative approval for the fresh e-tendering process using the prescribed procedure for appointment of a new Institute and till the appointment of a new Institute through e-tendering process, the last date for submission of application is May, 2020. India Post Ltd. The matter of extending the term of the institution in Mumbai was under consideration. The following decisions are taken in this regard:
Government's decision:
The operation and maintenance of the LED screen owned by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, installed in the premises of the new Administration Building, Mumbai. India Post Ltd. Since the term of the institution, Mumbai has expired, the new e-tendering process will be implemented by adopting the prescribed procedure to select the new institution. India Post Ltd. Based on the terms and conditions of the original agreement entered into with the Institute, Mumbai, for the period from July, 2020 till the selection of the new institution through fresh e-tendering process or till 30.9.2022, whichever is earlier.
