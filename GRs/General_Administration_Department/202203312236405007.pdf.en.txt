The Institute has informed that since Amravati falls in the Vidarbha region, the temperature in the district ranges from 45C to 48C during the month of March to June and in order to provide maximum facilities to the trainees, air-conditioning is required in the residential rooms of the hostels. The building has 100 residential rooms, a dining hall, a library, a recreation room and a hall. According to this, there is a total of 120 AC, with 1 AC per room equal to 100 AC and 5 AC each for the hall. 1.5-tonne capacity is required. 108.59 lakh has been provided in the Budget. The matter of granting administrative and financial approval to the above activities of the Institute is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, has been allotted the cost of the programme for the financial year 2021-2022. In pursuance of the provision made in the Budget Estimates vide Circular No. 1, the entire grant has been transferred to the Controlling Authorities. 222.08 lakh has been made available on the Budget Distribution System for supporting subsidy expenditure.
