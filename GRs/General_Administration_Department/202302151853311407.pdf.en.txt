Circular: Circular dated 18.01.2023 at Serial No. 1 under reference has given directions regarding the celebration of birth anniversaries of national men/ great personalities of the State.
Every year on February 19, the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is celebrated in the government office. The birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is celebrated on 19th February at all the places where there are statues/ memorials of this great man.
On 18.02.2023 at 11.00 am, all the officers and the concerned department heads should inspect the statue premises themselves and ensure that it is done by the concerned agencies by painting, cleaning and beautification.
The State Anthem of Maharashtra has been announced by the Government Decision dated 01 February 2023 of the General Administration Department. In this decision, No. In view of the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, it has been decided to adopt this song as the state song of Maharashtra from February 19, 2023.
Further, on 19.02.2023 at 10.00 am, all concerned senior officers and heads of concerned offices shall offer floral tributes to the statue by singing the State Anthem of Maharashtra. A small cultural programme was held later.
Since the above mentioned activities are to be carried out successfully, it is essential to plan them properly and carefully. In view of this, the concerned agencies should strictly plan and implement all the aspects of the program of the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj on February 19, 2023.
