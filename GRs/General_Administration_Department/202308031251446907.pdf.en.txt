Government's decision:
The Central Government has directed the State Governments to recommend to the Government of India, eminent persons having distinguished themselves in various fields, for conferring the Padma Awards. The following Committees are being constituted to recommend to the Government of India the names of eminent persons for the Padma Awards to be announced on 26th January, 2024:
