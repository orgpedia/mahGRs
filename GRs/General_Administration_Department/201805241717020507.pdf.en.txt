Read 1) Government Circular No: Sep. 2018/ Q. No.4/ Q. 17, D. April 05, 2018.
Applications are invited from eligible candidates for the post of Assistant Section Officer (AO) in the Government of India Recruitment 2018. The application was filed on April 5, 2018. The Post-Interview Training Course for the year 2018 for the Assistant Section Officers in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission is being held from Monday, 28th May, 2018 at the Conference Hall, 6th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
The names of the trainees admitted to the said training classes on the basis of information received from the administrative departments are indicated in Annexure' A'.
The programme of training is given in Annexure B and the subjects of training are given in Annexure C.
All the Ministries/ Departments and the Maharashtra Public Service Commission Office are requested to bring this circular immediately to the notice of all the concerned trainees (including trainees on deputation/ leave) and take their signatures and preserve the documents in their archives. In accordance with Rule 14 of the Maharashtra Secondary and Higher Secondary Services (Assistant) Services Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977, the concerned candidates should be given necessary permission to attend the said training classes commencing from 28th May, 2018.
4. The prescribed books required for the study of the candidates admitted to the training should be provided by the concerned departments.
