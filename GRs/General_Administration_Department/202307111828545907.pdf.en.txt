Preamble: The Maharashtra State Newspaper and Media Licensing Rules, 2007 (Distribution and Control), regarding the licensing of persons associated with the media, have been made under the Government Decision dated 19th September, 2007 in the said Reference No. As per the said Rules, a provision has been made to constitute a State Authorization Committee and a Divisional Authorization Committee for issuing authorization letters to eligible persons related to the media in the State. Accordingly, the number of members, membership qualification, age and experience of the Committee have been prescribed and revised from time to time in accordance with the relevant Government decisions/ notifications. According to the Government Resolution No. 6 here. As per the Government decision dated 13th August, 2015, 27 members were appointed to the State Appropriation Committee and 45 members to the 9 Divisional Appropriation Committees.
As the term of the Appropriation Committees is 3 years as per the said Rules, the Appropriation Committee constituted vide Government Resolution dated 13th August, 2015 has been constituted. It ended on August 12, 2018. As a result, the new State Board of
