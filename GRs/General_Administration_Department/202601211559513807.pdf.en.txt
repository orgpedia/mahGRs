The Proposal: -
As per Section 26 (2) of the Right to Information Act, 2005, the appropriate government is required to publish within 18 months of the commencement of this Act, a guideline containing information required by it in the official language of the state concerned for exercising the powers conferred by this Act. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to print the guidelines in Marathi language required for the use of the Act and distribute them to the institute.
The Government's Decision-
According to Section 26 (2) T 26 (3) of the Right to Information Act, Look, the Government of Maharashtra has issued a guideline for printing and distributing 20000 copies of the Marathi language guideline of reasonable information required to use the Act at a cost of Rs. 12.79 This amounts to Rs. 2,55,800 - Administrative approval is being given to make such funds available to the organization successfully.
