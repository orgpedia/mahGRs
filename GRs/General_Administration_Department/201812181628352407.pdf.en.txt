Introduction: By disseminating the success stories of the beneficiaries of various schemes of the government through various media, other eligible citizens are motivated to take advantage of the scheme.
Other proposed beneficiaries get encouragement and good response to the schemes by showing how the beneficiaries have improved their standard of living by availing the benefits of the schemes. The government was considering to produce and broadcast TV reports based on the experiences of the beneficiaries who have directly benefited from the schemes from six private television channels in Marathi.
