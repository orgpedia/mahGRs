The ad-hoc promotion of the Backward Class employees in purely temporary manner is being made subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list by availing the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 25.05.2004. The concerned department should look into it.
4. The Department has informed that no departmental enquiry is being conducted against the above mentioned total employees who are to be promoted ad-hoc under this order. However, the departments should verify the departmental enquiry before giving posting to these employees in the post of clerk-typist.
In case of non-acceptance of purely temporary promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist, the General Administration Department, vide Government Resolution No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ Office-12, dated 12-09-2016, will remove their names from the provisional revised selection list for the year 2018-2019 and the eligibility for promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist will be checked in the third year selection list without considering their names in the subsequent 2 years selection lists.
6. These orders shall come into force with immediate effect and the employees who have been given ad-hoc promotion to the post of Clerk-Typist from Group-D only on temporary basis shall join the department assigned to them immediately.
