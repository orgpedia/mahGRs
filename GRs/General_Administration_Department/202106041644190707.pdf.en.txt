Mr. and Mrs.,
The procedure for the deputation of officers/ staff of the ministerial cadre has been laid down under the relevant Government decision. As per the said procedure, the posts of General Manager and Administrative Officer on the establishment of Mantralaya Restaurant under the General Administration Department are to be filled by deputation from the cadre of Under Secretary and Cell Officer in the Ministry. Accordingly, the General Manager and Administration Officer posts at the Mantralaya Restaurant Establishment are being filled on deputation from the Under Secretary and Cell Officers of the Mantralaya Division.
2. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this letter to the immediate notice of the Under Secretary and Cell Officers at the establishment of their department. Accordingly, the information in the accompanying prospectus of the Under Secretary and the Cell Officers who have expressed their willingness for deputation should be made available to this Department with the approval of the Secretary of the Department by 15.06.2021. The willingness once shown by the intending secretary and the chamber officers cannot be withdrawn.
