The Proposal:
It was under the consideration of the Government to grant administrative approval to the proposal for printing of various election-related formats (70111) out of the material required for the election process in the run-up to the forthcoming Assembly Elections-2024.
Establishment of Government:
As per the demand received from various districts for the forthcoming Assembly General Elections, Rs. 21,22,800 - A proposal for an estimated expenditure of (Rupees twenty-one lakhs only) one thousand eight hundred is being given administrative approval under this Government decision.
