Government Circular-
The Departmental Examination 2024 is the first opportunity for promotion to the rank of Circle Officer.
The training for such candidates is to be held in July 2024.
Applications for the exam are being invited through this circular.
For example, candidates who have a first chance and qualify as follows:
In the notification dated 7th June, 2024 attached to this Department by the Establishment Branch of the Department.
The application form should be submitted in the following format: -
"In Rule 3 of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministry Services Divisional Examination Rules, 1977.
As per the provision, the Auxiliary Cell of the Ministerial Cadre / Maharashtra Public Service Commission
Officer and J. Assistant Cell Officer (Law) of the Department of Law and Justice as Assistant
have passed the Post-In-Service Training Examination for the post of Cell Officer and whose
Five years of continuous service is being completed by 31st October, 2024. "
Also, those who have had their first chance and training for the exam even before the zonal exam in 2024.
Applications for the examination will be invited separately from all such candidates.
Submission of application by 07.06.2024, all Departments of Establishment Branch.
Officers All Assistant Cell Officers (on deputation / leave) who are eligible for the examination
with the Assistant Room Officer), please bring the document to their immediate notice and sign it.
Be sure to save it to your archive. Detailed schedule of training through separate circulars
will be notified.
