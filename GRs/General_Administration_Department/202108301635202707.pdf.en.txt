Read: 1) Home Department, Government Circular No. MIS-0919/ PR No. 226/ VISA-4, dated 16/03/2020.
Preamble: As part of the preventive measures to contain the spread of COVID-19 virus, the Department of Home Affairs, Govt. As per the circular dated 16/3/2020, the Ministry has closed the entry pass process till further orders to control the crowd of visitors coming to the Ministry. Therefore, in this situation, the Government is receiving representations from various organizations/ citizens to make necessary arrangements to receive the mail delivered to the concerned department in the Ministry near the entrance of the Ministry. In view of the above, it was under consideration of the Government to make arrangements for postal acknowledgement at the existing entry pass windows near the Garden Gate of the Ministry to receive the mail received by the citizens/ field office staff who come to the Ministry only to deliver the mail.
Government Circular- In view of the government policy to use e-office system in government work, it is intended to use e-mail more for office mail. In such a situation, it will not be in the interest of the health of the employees of the Ministry to enter the Ministry only to deliver the mail. In addition, instructions have been given that the most urgent mail should be sent through e-mail as per the circular of the Home Department, and for the convenience of the employees of the field office who come to the Ministry for daily mail and other important work, every department should make arrangements to receive mail at the entrance and for this, the existing entrance pass windows at the Garden Gate of the Ministry should be used.
Now, as per this Government Circular, arrangements are being made to accept mail received by citizens/ field office staff coming to the Ministry for delivery only at the entry pass windows near the Garden Gate of the Ministry as an accompanying letter.
The Public Works Department should make arrangements for fans, lighting, disinfection etc. immediately.

At present, the control of the entry pass windows is with the Home Department. During the COVID period, the staff of the Ministries/ Departments will be present at the entry pass windows of the Ministries to receive the mail received by the citizens/ field office staff as a temporary arrangement for acceptance of mail. Accordingly, the security of the entry pass windows and similar matters will be handled by the Home Department. Once the system of issuing entry passes to the visitors through the entry pass windows is operational again, the system of accepting the mail will automatically be terminated and all these entry pass windows will be used by the Home Department to issue entry passes to the visitors again.
5.The provisions of this Order shall come into force with immediate effect.
