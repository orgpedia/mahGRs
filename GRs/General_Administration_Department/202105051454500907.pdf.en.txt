Government circular
The Provisional Seniority List as on 01-01-2021 has been prepared for the employees of the cadre of Assistant Cell Officers in the Ministry Department only as per the date of their regular appointment and the same is enclosed with this Government Circular along with Annexure-A (Regularly appointed Assistant Cell Officers) and Annexure-B (Limited Departmental Competitive Examination for selection to the post of Assistant Cell Officers) from the year 2017 onwards. Employees appointed to the post of Assistant Cell Officers only on temporary basis) has been added here under the decision of Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court.
Clerk-typist are promoted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the 30% promotion quota as per the rules of entry into service of the Assistant Cell Officer cadre. However, due to various administrative reasons, the process of regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is underway. Hence, the seniority list of these officers has not been included. As per the rules for admission to the service of the Assistant Cell Officer cadre, the process of regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the 30% promotion quota will be carried out as per the procedure, only the Assistant Cell Officers of the promoted quota will be included in the common seniority list as on the date of regular appointment.
(3) As per the rules for admission to the service of the Assistant Cell Officer cadre, the appointment of the staff appointed to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the following examinations from the Limited Departmental Competition Examination 2017 for appointment by selection to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the 30% limited departmental competition examination quota shall be made only on a temporary basis. This is in pursuance of the decision of Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court. Therefore, these employees have been included in Appendix-B of the seniority list. The process of inclusion of these employees in the Annexure-A in the list of common seniority will be taken up after the court verdict/ government decision in this regard.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.

Further, the said Common Seniority List has been prepared only for the purpose of fixation of pay as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 09-02-2019 of the Department of Finance.
All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officers in their Department, the present date of 01.01.2021, which is only the date of regular appointment as per the provisional seniority. In this regard, the objections/ errors received from the concerned Assistant Cell Officers regarding the issues should be examined as per the records of the department and the representations along with the comments of the department should be sent to this department by 14-06-2021. In case of non-receipt of objections/ errors within the prescribed period, the final/ interim seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
