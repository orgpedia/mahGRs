Introduction: To make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the government, various schemes are published by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. In the financial year 2018-19, the Government was considering to produce and broadcast special programmes based on various schemes and initiatives of the Government on the Sahyadri channel of Doordarshan. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decisions: Government messages are broadcast through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the government. In the financial year 2018-19, the Government is giving approval to produce and broadcast special programmes based on various schemes and initiatives of the Government on the Sahyadri channel of Doordarshan. The expenses incurred for production, first broadcast and re-telecast of a particular episode are as follows:
