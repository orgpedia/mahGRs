Preamble: Office of the Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi. Under the scheme of providing medical facilities to the officers and staff working in the office of the Resident Commissioner with effect from 11th April, 1972, a sum of Rs. 250/- has been appointed as Medical Officer. After this, the remuneration of the said authorized medical officer was increased from time to time keeping in view the rising inflation. At present, the office orders of the Special Commissioner and Principal Secretary have been issued. As on 30.1.2006 Rs. 4000/- will be paid. Accordingly, in view of the rising inflation, the honorarium paid to the authorized medical officers posted in other state houses in Delhi and the increase in the number of officers and staff after the new Maharashtra Sadan was started from 2014, the government was considering to increase the honorarium of the three authorized medical officers currently posted in the office of the Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The three authorised medical officers presently posted in the office of the Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi, will be paid a monthly honorarium of Rs. 4,000/- to Rs. 10,000/- is being decided subject to the following conditions:
Authorized Medical Officers will provide medical facilities twice a week for two hours at the Resident Commissioner's Office, New Delhi.
(2) No charges shall be levied on the officers/ staff coming for medical treatment at the Hospital of the Resident Commissioner, New Delhi, by the Authorized Medical Officer.
