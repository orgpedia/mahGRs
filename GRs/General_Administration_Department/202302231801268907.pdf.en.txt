Special Leave Petition No. Subject to the final adjudication dated 28306/1017, the advertisement for filling up of 62 posts in the ministerial department under the" Assistant Cell Officer Limited Departmental Competitive Examination 2022" in accordance with the merit has been issued by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. It was published on 29-09-2022.
Maharashtra Public Service Commission conducted the said examination on 10-12-2022 and the final result of the said examination was published on the Commission's website on 01-02-2023. The Commission vide No. According to the letter, 62 candidates with disability reservation have been recommended for the post of Assistant Cell Officer. Out of these, 60 candidates have been recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. A Special Leave Petition No. Subject to the final Judgment dated 28306/2017, the allotment to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is being made purely on temporary basis subject to the following terms and conditions as per Annexure-A.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1) | This order is being made subject to the final adjudication of Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India for the post of Assistant Cell Officer only on temporary basis.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| 2) | If there are any court cases/ criminal cases/ departmental inquiries pending against the recommended candidates, these should be verified by the departments in which the candidates are presently working before relieving/ appointing them to the post of promotion.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| 3) | The candidates who have been allotted the post of Assistant Cell Officer should report to the department in which they have been promoted by 23-03-2023 (M.N.). The candidates who have been appointed in other departments from the existing working department should be relieved immediately by the concerned department. The concerned candidates should accept their posting to the post of Assistant Cell Officer immediately. If the promotion is not accepted within the above mentioned period, the General Administration Department will take action under the Government Decision No.SRV-2015/ PR.Addnt.R.303/Kr.A.-12, dated 12.09.2016. The Ministerial Departments are requested to issue orders for the appointment of these candidates |
