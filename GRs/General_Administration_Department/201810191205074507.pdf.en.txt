Circular:
Massive level of uncleanliness in many departments/ offices of the Ministry as well as the State Government
It has been decided in the meeting of the State Cabinet dated 3 rd October, 2018 to implement a special cleanliness drive.
Chapter XI of the Rules of Business prescribes the provisions relating to archiving, classification, destruction and destruction of records. All the departments/ offices of the Ministry and State Government have been informed from time to time to take action accordingly. However, due to non-compliance of the said provisions, it has been observed that the closed files are lying in large numbers in many departments/ offices and many offices are found to be unhygienic. The state government has taken the matter seriously.
Therefore, all the administrative departments of the Ministry as well as all the departments/ offices under their control are again advised to undertake a special drive for declassification, declassification and updation of records with a view to keep their departments/ offices clean.
For this, action should be taken as per the instructions in Annexure A of the Government Resolution dated 15th February, 2018.
