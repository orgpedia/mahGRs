the order
Mr. V. G. Koshti, Cell Officer, Public Health Department is being appointed on deputation for the first time as" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Right to Service Commission, Pune Revenue Division, Pune under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions for one year.
(a) Mr. V.G. Koshti, the Cell Officer shall commence his deputation on the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputation, and shall, after handing over the charge of the office of the Deputation one year thereafter, cease to hold office on the date on which he enters upon his parent Department.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
After the repatriation of the deputation, Mr. Y.G. Koshti, the Cell Officer will be required to join his parent department i.e. Public Health Department.
The Department of Public Health and the General Administration Department shall determine the terms and conditions for the deputation of Shri Y. G. Koshti, taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981.
4. All other administrative matters of Mr. V. G. Koshti, Cell Officer will be handled by the Public Health Department.
5. Mr. V. G. Koshti, Cell Officer to be relieved immediately by the Public Health Department to accept the appointment on deputation.
