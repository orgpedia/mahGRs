the General Administration Department,

Date of release: April 12, 2022
Reference:- Government Decision No. Shakaja-1115/ P.No.109/ Part- 1/2015/22, D. 3rd March, 2022.
Preamble: The Government decision dated 3 rd March, 2022 in the above regard is hereby rescinded.
The Office of the Director, Accounts and Treasuries, shown in the cottages (barracks) on the Free Pace Journal line in the route of the project plan prepared by the Metropolitan Development Authority for Mumbai Metro Phase-3 (Colaba-Bandra-Sipz), has been shifted to 7,200 square meters at Thackersey House, Mumbai Port Trust, Ballard Estate, Mumbai-01, as per the Government Decision, dated 9.11.2016. ft. of space was allocated. However, the proposal to allot new space to the office of the Director, Accounts and Treasuries at Thackersey House, Mumbai Port Trust, Ballard Estate, Mumbai-01 was under consideration of the Government due to its inconvenient location, and accordingly, the Government has decided as under: Government Decision: The office of the Director, Accounts and Treasuries, shown in the cottages (barracks) on Free Pace Journal Marg in the project plan for Mumbai Metro Phase-3 (Colaba-Bandra-Seepz) prepared by the Metropolitan Development Authority, will be shifted to Kasturi Building, Lady Jamsetji Tata Marg, Churchgate, Mumbai-01 in lieu of space at Thackersey House, Mumbai Port Trust, Ballard Estate, Mumbai-01. Seats are being allocated on a temporary basis.
The Director, Accounts and Treasuries will prepare a plan on how the office structure should be at the new shifted location and make the plan available to the MPV Authority and the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRC).
The authority and MMRC should take necessary action.
