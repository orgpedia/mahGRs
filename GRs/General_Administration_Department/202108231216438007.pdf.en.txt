As on 20/01/2014, a combined probationary training programme is being implemented for Group A and Group B cadre officers who are directly recruited in Government service. For the training programme under the Integrated Schedules, the training institute of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been provided with Reference No. A total of 12 posts have been approved under the government decision.
Out of which, only one post of Accounts Officer, Class-I has been filled up from 26/08/2020. During the financial year 2021-22, a total amount of Rs. 6,13,208/-for their salary and dearness allowance difference from March to June, 2021 and others has been provided in the Reference No. 4 has been distributed to Yashoda, Pune as per the decision of the government here. Now for the salary from July, 2021 to December, 2021, Rs. 7,40,094/- is under consideration of the Government.
