The Republic Day programme. January 26, 2026
For Brihanmumbai
At 5: 00 am, the Sanai Chaugada is played at important places.
At 8: 00 a.m., national flags should be raised in all parts of the city, on all buildings, schools, and colleges.
* 9: 15 a.m. Flag hoisting and ceremonial parade by the Chief Minister at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai.
* In the morning 10.00, parades of various sectoral elements should be held at maximum places in the city, institutions under the education department should organize events as given in the booklet "Republic Festival," and other institutions should organize events in memory of their diverse patriots and martyrs.
In the evening, cultural programmes in memory of patriots and martyrs should be organised as per convenience.
For Divisional and District Headquarters
At 8: 00 am, hoist the national flag on schools, colleges, and as many other buildings as possible in each area.
* In the morning, a joint parade of police, home guards, Rashtriya Chhatra Sena and Railway Protection Force covering a maximum area of 10.00 cities, institutions under the education department should organize events as given in the booklet "Republic Festival," and other institutions should organize events in memory of their diverse patriots and martyrs.
In the evening, cultural programmes in memory of patriots and martyrs should be organised as per convenience.
