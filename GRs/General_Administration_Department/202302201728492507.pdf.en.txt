The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in question, the vacant room for the office of the Rashtriya Samaj Paksha in the barracks behind the Yashodhan building at Churchgate, No. 5 (451.00 Ch. ft) has been allotted on a temporary basis. But the Executive Engineer, City Division of the department suggested to shift the office of the Rashtriya Samaj Party located in Barrack No. 4 for the construction of the government residence.
Therefore, for the office of the Rashtriya Samaj Party, Bhukar No. Construction No. O- 003/08 of Public Works Department Office No. 498.68 Square at 729, Insahtment Compound, Azad Maidan, Municipal Road. The proposal to allot the land on a temporary basis was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The office of the Rashtriya Samaj Paksha is located in the barrack behind the Yashodhan building at Room No. 5 (451.00 Ch. ft.) in the Fort Division of Mumbai City District subject to the condition of vacating Bhukar No. Construction No. A- 003/08 of 729, Insahtment Compound, Azad Maidan, Municipal Road, 498.68 Square of Public Works Department Office. Seats are being allocated on a temporary basis.
The Executive Engineer, Ilakha City (West) Division, Nirman Bhawan Building, 25 Marzban Road, Fort, Mumbai, shall hand over the possession of the said site to the President, Rashtriya Samaj Paksha and submit a report to the Government.
