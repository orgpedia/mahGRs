In order to ensure the credibility of the photo electoral rolls of all the assembly constituencies in the state of Maharashtra, as per the order of the Election Commission of India, Booth Level Officers have been appointed for each polling station. One BLO Supervisor has been appointed for each of the 10 polling station level officers.
The main functions of the BLO Supervisors are to assist the Electoral Registration Officers in improving the copy and accuracy of the Electoral Roll, to supervise the work of the Booth Level Officers and to evaluate their work.
The Election Commission of India (ECI) has also directed the Election Commission of India (ECI) to pay remuneration to the polling station supervisors as they will be performing the duties assigned to them by taking care of the work in their respective offices. vide letter dated 08.07.2015.
Accordingly, the proposal to pay honorarium to the polling station level officer supervisor was under the consideration of the government.
Under this Government decision, the Polling Station Level Officers Supervisors (BLO Supervisors) will be paid an annual honorarium of Rs. 12000/- (Rs. 12,000) is approved.
