The Proposal:
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 27.12.2016. As per the Act, 4% of the total number of vacancies for all categories of persons with disabilities in the direct services recruitment shall be reserved for persons with disabilities. The Government of India has notified the list on 04.01.2021 in terms of Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, specifying the posts for persons with disabilities in the establishments under their jurisdiction. As per the Government Order No. 3 issued in the context of Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, it has been notified that the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service, will be reserved for the disabled persons in the State Government Service, even if their designations are different. Accordingly, the concerned administrative department should include these posts in the government decision. In this regard, instructions have been given to ensure the posts for the disabled in the state government establishments as per the central government list.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to ensure the posts for the disabled in the Group-A to Group-C cadre in the Brihanmumbai Government Office in the function of the ministerial department and the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
