the order
The services of Smt.Dipali U. Jape, Cell Officer, Rural Development Department are being handed over on deputation for a period of one year as Programme Officer in the Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Pune Divisional Office under the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs.
(a) Mrs. Jape shall commence her service as a Deputy on the date on which she enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, hand over charge of the office of Deputy Commissioner one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the (Period of Appointment, Non-Residential Service, etc.) Rules, 1981, the Rural Development Department and the Tourism and Cultural Affairs Department shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Jape will be handled by the Department of Rural Development as well as the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs.
4. Mrs. Jape should be relieved immediately by the Rural Development Department.
