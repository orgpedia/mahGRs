Introduction: Mr. Milind Mhaiskar, Bh. Principal Secretary, Housing Department has been transferred and posted as Principal Secretary (Aviation and State Excise), General Administration Department. For the post of Principal Secretary (Aviation and State Excise), General Administration Department, there is no separate Secretary's chamber in the Ministry Main and Extension Building. Therefore, the proposal to allot the hall to the Principal Secretary (Aviation and State Excise), General Administration Department, was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
The third floor of the Mantralaya Extension Building, Hall No. 317-Extension, is being temporarily allotted for the office and office of the Principal Secretary (Aviation and State Excise), General Administration Department.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the Principal Secretary (Aviation and State Excise), General Administration Department and submit a report to the Government.
