Preamble: The policy on permanent induction from the establishment of one appointing authority to the establishment of another appointing authority under the cadre on the request of the Government servants has been prescribed by the relevant Government decision. Under this policy, it has been clarified that the policy of permanent inclusion at serial number 6 will not be permissible for any matter. Out of these matters, it has been prescribed by clause 6 (1) (a) that no permanent inclusion can be made from the post of Group-C in the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission to the post of Group-C outside the purview of the Commission and from the post of Group-C outside the purview of the Commission to the post of Group-C in the purview of the Commission. In this regard, Ma. The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, in its original application No. The matter of giving revised suggestions after considering the merits of the judgment passed in accordance with 73/2020 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In supersession of Rule 6 (1) (a) of the Government Resolution dated 15.05.2019, permanent inclusion of Group-C posts outside the purview of the Commission and Group-C posts outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the purview of the Commission shall be admissible subject to such terms and conditions as may be prescribed in the relevant Government Resolution. However, such permanent inclusion will require consultation with the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The decision was taken by the Law and Justice Department. Reference No. 263/ Civil/ 2022/ A Branch, Dy. Is being issued as agreed under 06/10/2022.
