Allotment of additional space in the new Administration Building for Maharashtra-Karnataka boundary question room.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department,
Government Resolution, No.Shakaja-1120/ PR No. 73/2020/ 22,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: December 22, 2020
The Maharashtra government has set up a special cell to deal with the Maharashtra-Karnataka border issue. The room has been allotted space in the new administrative building, 12th floor (west side). Due to the insufficient space allotted to the Border Cell, the new administrative building building under the control of the Food and Civil Supplies Department, 390 square meters on the 12th floor (west side). The proposal to allot the land for the Maharashtra-Karnataka boundary question cell was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government have taken the following decisions:- GOVERNMENT DECISION: The new administrative building under the control of the Food and Civil Supplies Department will be constructed at 390 Sq. ft. of space is being allocated for the Maharashtra-Karnataka boundary question cell.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
