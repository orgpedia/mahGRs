The Proposal:
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations publishes various schemes to make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the government.
In the financial year 2018-19, the government was considering the issue of broadcasting the government message through sound and LED screens at ST bus stations in the state. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decisions: Government messages are broadcast through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the government.
In the financial year 2018-19, the government is giving approval to broadcast the government message through audio and LED screens at ST bus stations in the state.
The scheme will be implemented by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations.
Administrative and Financial Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 1,64,06, 400/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Four Lakh Six Thousand Four Hundred) for the period October, 2018 to March, 2019.
1. Expenditure for the above purpose shall be borne by the General Administration Department, Demand No. A-6, 2220- Information and Publicity 60 Others, 101 Advertisement and Publicity (Under the Scheme 2220 1175) (Under the Scheme) to be covered under this head from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2018-2019.
The prevailing rules/ orders of the government should be followed while making such expenditure.
2. This Government decision is based on the informal reference No. 1 of the Planning Department. 167/18/1443, Dy. 08/10/2018 and the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 374/ Expenditure-4 dated 12.10.2018 is being issued with the concurrence received.
