A copy of the Office Memorandum dated 05.04.2022, No. 42/07/2022-P& PW (D), Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Department of Pension& Pensioners' Welfare, Government of India, New Delhi, forwarded for information and appropriate action in respect of pensioners/ family pensioners of All India Services in Maharashtra State Cadre.
As per the aforesaid Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India, with effect from 01.01.2022, the increase in Dearness Relief rate of 3% and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum shall be applicable to the All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre.
Accordingly, the All India Service Scholarship in Maharashtra State Cadre is now being given to the salaried/ family pensioners. Dearness Relief@ 34% will be admissible with effect from 01.01.2022.
