Preamble Financial assistance is provided to the next of kin of the soldiers/ disabled soldiers of the State who have been martyred in war, combat situations and security related operations in the country and to the members of Border Security Force and other similar paramilitary forces/ their families who have been martyred in security related operations in the country, as per the decision of the Government dated 04/10/2000. Accordingly, the Department of Sainik Welfare, Pune has issued a proposal to provide financial assistance to the dependents of the martyred jawans namely Havildar Dilip Dhondiba Gangarde and G. Ahmednagar. 2 submitted to the government.

In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Department of Sainik Welfare to the Government vide letter dated 04/11/2020, the Government is approving to provide financial assistance to the dependents of the deceased jawan, Havildar Dilip Dhondiba Gangarde of Maharashtra State as mentioned in the following table
