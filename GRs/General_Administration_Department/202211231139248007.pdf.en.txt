Preamble: In order to make governance accountable, accessible, clean, transparent and expeditious, a Good Governance Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 27 th May, 2022 to draft and submit to the Government the Manual of Good Governance. The committee has been given six months to prepare a draft of the Manual of Good Governance. May 27, 2022 to November 26, 2022). The committee has requested for an extension of time to prepare a draft of the good governance rules and submit it to the government. Following this request, the matter of extending the tenure of the Good Governance Committee was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 27 th May, 2022, the term of the Good Governance Committee constituted to prepare and submit to the Government a draft of the Manual of Good Governance to make governance accountable, accessible, clean, transparent and expeditious is expiring on 26.11.2022. In pursuance of the request made by the Committee, the Committee is being given an extension of 3 months from 27.11.2022 under this Government decision to prepare the draft Good Governance Manual and submit it to the Government.
