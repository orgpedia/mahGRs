GOVERNMENT Circular: As per the order dated 1st December, 2011 of the Central Government, guidelines have been prescribed for the treatment of Members of Parliament with courtesy and respect and these orders have been brought to the notice of all the Departments/ Offices vide circular dated 09th March, 2012 of this Department. However, it has come to the notice of the Committee of Privileges of Lok Sabha that the said principles are not being followed and the same have been communicated to all the departments/ offices vide Government of India Order dated 10th February, 2020.
Further, comprehensive instructions have been issued vide Circular dated 27.07.2015 of this Department regarding the treatment of Members of Parliament/ Members of Legislative Assemblies with courtesy and respect, processing of their representations, etc.
However, complaints of non-compliance with the said instructions are received from the members of the Legislative Assembly, as well as Government decisions issued by the Ministries/ Departments.
