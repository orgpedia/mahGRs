Circular: The Department of Parliamentary Affairs has brought to the notice of the Department that the following matters have come to the notice of the Legislative Council Assurance Committee during the testimony of the Secretaries held regarding the fulfillment of the promises made in the Legislative Assembly and Legislative Council of Maharashtra.
1. on behalf of the government. There are discrepancies between the assurances given by the Minister/ Minister of State and the information given by the department regarding the fulfillment.
2. As the concerned departments are not firm in the matter of departmental transfer of certain assurances, the action on fulfillment of those assurances remains pending for a long time.
The Secretary/ Officer concerned cannot provide complete and satisfactory information on the questions asked by the members at the time of testimony. Also, the information presented by the department is inconsistent and misleading to the committee.
4. In the absence of sufficient information, the Committee has to postpone the meetings.
In view of the above, all the Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries of all the Ministries are requested to take necessary action in accordance with the above issues raised by the Legislative Council Committee so that the promises of the Legislature are fulfilled within the stipulated time and in a proper manner. Also, the witness should be present before the assurance committee with full preparation so that the committee is satisfied.
