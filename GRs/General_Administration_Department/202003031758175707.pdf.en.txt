the order
Shri Satish Shivbal Bhartiya, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department is being appointed on deputation for the post of" General Manager (Administration)" of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation for a period of one year initially subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. Satish Shivbal Bhartiya, Under Secretary, will commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
In pursuance of the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Industries, Energy and Labour Department and Public Works Department Rules, 1981, Shri.
The terms and conditions of the deputation of Mr. Satish Shivbal Bhartiya, Under Secretary, shall be mutually decided.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Satish Shivbal Bhartiya, Under Secretary will be handled by the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour.
