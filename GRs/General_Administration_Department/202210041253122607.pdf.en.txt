Government's decision:
For the work of the Maratha community. The Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 10th February, 2020 is being reconstituted and a Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted as follows for all statutory proceedings to be taken on the recommendations of the report submitted by the State Backward Classes Commission on the social, educational and economic status of the Maratha community and other related matters.
