Regarding abolition of Group-D posts in Government Transport Service, Worli Office.

The Proposal:
Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai under the General Administration Department. As per the Government decision dated 27.08.2009, a total of 39 posts are sanctioned in Group D cadre. These include skilled assistants, unskilled workers, cleaners/ washers/ porters, assistants, tyremen, masons, peons, watchmen, cleaners, etc. In the above reference Government Decision of the Finance Department. As per clause 1 (5), instructions have been given to abolish 25% posts in Group-D cadre. Accordingly, the matter of abolishing the posts of Group-D cadre in the Government Transport Service Office was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 10 posts equivalent to 25% of the vacancies in Group-D cadre as shown in the Annexure attached to this Government Resolution in the office of Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai under the General Administration Department are being abolished under this Government Resolution.
