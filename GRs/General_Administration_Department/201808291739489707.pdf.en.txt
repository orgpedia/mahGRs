Circular:
In order to ensure the well-being of the senior citizens, instructions have been issued regarding the implementation of the State Comprehensive Senior Citizen Policy 2013 under the Government Decision dated 9th July, 2018 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Following are the guidelines issued in this regard:
(1) All the Ministries/ Administrative Departments and Government/ Semi-Government Offices shall ensure that the problems of senior citizens received in their offices are resolved on priority.
2) The Public Works Department should take appropriate action to make available the space in government/ semi-government institutions/ offices, community temples, recreation centers for senior citizens' programs within a certain time on a certain day.
(3) A Joint/ Deputy Secretary should be appointed as the nodal officer in each of the administrative departments of the Ministry to coordinate between the Department of Social Justice and all the government departments for the resolution of the problems of senior citizens and to control the schemes to be implemented by various departments. All the ministries and departments should provide information to the social justice department. Also, the social justice department should publish the information collected in this regard on the department's website.
