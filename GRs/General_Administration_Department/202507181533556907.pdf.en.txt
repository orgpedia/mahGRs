The order-
The Government has approved the appointment of Shri Vithal Ganapathy Inamdar, Additional Collector as Regional Divisional Investigation Officer, Amravati. Accordingly, Mr. Vitthal Inamdar, Additional Collector, is being posted as Regional Divisional Investigation Officer, Amravati Division, Amravati.
The term of the said appointment shall be for a period of 3 years from the date on which he assumes charge.
The Government shall have the power to recall and remit to the original Department the Regional Divisional Vigilance Officer, Amravati, for administrative reasons at any time before the expiry of the period of 3 years of his service.
The officer concerned shall be required to give at least 3 months' written notice if he wishes to return to the original department before the completion of the three-year period.
Mr. Fage: Inamdar should immediately join the post of Regional Divisional Investigation Officer, Amravati Division, Amravati.
