the order
Mr. Dattatraya Ghanwat, Cell Officer, Medical Education and Medicines Department is being transferred and posted as Assistant Commissioner, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation under Urban Development Department on deputation for a period of one year.
(a) Mr. Ghanwat shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his parent department, hand over the charge of the Deputy Commissioner after one year from the date on which he ceases to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) The terms and conditions of deputation shall be decided by the Urban Development Department and the Medical Education and Drugs Department after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Ghanwat will be handled by the Urban Development Department and the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department.
4. Mr. Ghanwat should be relieved from his duties by the Department of Medical Education and Medicines.
