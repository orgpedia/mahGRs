The Provisional Common Seniority of Senior Personal Assistants in the Ministry was issued vide Government Circular dated 23.01.2023. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 06.02.2023. Accordingly, the objections/ errors of some of the senior personal assistants have been corrected. The General Administration Department, Government Notification 21.06.2021 has adopted the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 while preparing the said seniority list.
