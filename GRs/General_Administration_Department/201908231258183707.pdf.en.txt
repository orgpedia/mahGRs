Smt. Ritika Narula, Indian Economic Service (IES) has been appointed as Director, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, on deputation by an order of reference. Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh has been appointed as the Director of Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati. It was under consideration of the Government to specify the terms and conditions of the deputation of Smt. Ritika Narula as Director, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati.
Government Decision: The terms and conditions of the appointment of Smt. Ritika Narula, Director (Indian Economic Service), Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati as a deputationist are as follows.
Period of Deputation: Smt. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service) with effect from 12.06.2019. The above appointees will be on deputation till 25.05.2020. Provided that, (a) if the Government considers it necessary in the interest of public service, the Government shall have the right to extend the period of service of the Government of India and the State Government in accordance with the rules made thereunder on or before the expiry of the period of service of the Government.
(b) If their services are not required by the Government, the State Government shall be competent to return them to their original service. However, this must be done at least three months in advance.

(2) Remuneration:- Smt. Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service) shall hold the office of Director during the period of such deputation. During this period, according to the Final Pay Certificate (LPC), the basic pay of Level-12 (6th Pay Commission- PB-03 Rs.
15600-39100+ Grade Pay Rs. 7600). Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati will take appropriate action regarding the sanction of annual increment.
(3) Allowance on deputation:- The deputation allowance shall not be admissible to Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service) during the period of such deputation.
(4) Transport Allowance:- The transport allowance shall be admissible as per the rules of the Central Government. However, if the vehicle is provided by Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, the transport allowance will not be admissible.
(4) Children Education Allowance:- As per the rules of the Central Government, Children Education Allowance will be admissible to Mrs. Ritika Narula (IAS). Also, during this period, special pay will not be admissible to them even if the same is mentioned in their final salary certificate.
Dearness Allowance:- Mrs. Ritika Narula (Indian Financial Service) will be entitled to Dearness Allowance at the rate at which Dearness Allowance is admissible to Indian Financial Service Officers from time to time during the period of her deputation.
Local Supplementary Allowance: Local Supplementary Allowance shall be admissible to Smt. Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service) at such rate as may be admissible to her from time to time in accordance with the rules/ orders of the State Government.
(8) House Rent Allowance in lieu of Residence:- Mrs. Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service) shall be entitled to House Rent Allowance as admissible to her from time to time as per the rules of the Central Government, if she does not have a government residence in her possession during the said period of deputation.
(9) Travel Allowance for travel on duty during the period of deputation.- Mrs. Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service) may be given the option to take Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance for travel on duty after joining the post-retirement service, as per the rules of the Central Government or as per the rules of Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, whichever is more beneficial.
Leave:- The Central Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1972 shall be applicable to Smt. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service) during the period of her deputation. The Divisional Commissioner will be competent to grant them all types of leave during the period of deputation. The entire expenditure on local supplementary allowance will be borne by Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati.
(11) Leave Salary and Pension Contributions:- The institution of Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati will take the action of depositing leave salary and contribution pension in respect of Smt. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service) under the respective head of accounts after considering the rates prescribed. In case of delay in payment of these amounts during the period of deputation, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati will be liable to pay the admissible penal interest during the period of delay.
(12) Temporary Leave:- Smt. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service) may, during the period of her deputation, enjoy temporary leave as per the rules of the State Government.
The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati will be competent to grant casual leave during the period of deputation.
Leave Travel Concession:- Smt. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service) will be entitled to get Leave Travel Concession as per the provisions of the Central Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1972, the cost of which will be borne by Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati. Divisional Commissioner, Amravati will be competent to sanction leave concession during the period of earned leave as the Divisional Commissioner is competent to sanction all leaves during the period of deputation to him.
Medical benefits: Mrs. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service) will be entitled to medical benefits as per the provisions of the Maharashtra State (Medical Care) Rules, 1961. The expenses will be borne by Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati. However, there will be separate rules for grant of medical exemption to Central Government employees and Smt. Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service), if she wishes to avail the same, will be at liberty to exercise the option of availing the same during the period of her deputation.
National Pension Scheme:- Contributions to the National Pension Scheme should be continued during the period of deputation of Smt. Ritika Narula, Indian Economic Service.
Departmental Enquiry:- The Central Civil Services (CCA) Rules, 1965, as applicable, in respect of Departmental Enquiry against Smt. (19) Appointment after return from leave or training while on deputation: If during the period of deputation Mrs. Ritika Narula, (Indian Economic Service), went on leave or went for training and other officers were appointed to the post held by her or if she was appointed to another post after returning from leave or training, all expenses in respect of salary, salary, tenure of office, compulsory waiting period, etc., shall be borne by Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, from the date of expiry of leave or training till the date on which she assumes charge of the other post.
Cash Allowance in lieu of Resident Peon:- Mrs. Ritika Narula, (Indian Financial Service), before joining the deputation, shall be entitled to residential cash allowance at the same rate as during the period of deputation, if cash allowance is admissible to her in lieu of Resident Peon, but shall be required to furnish a certificate that no Resident Peon has been paid in the month before taking such allowance.

Travel within the country/ abroad during the period of deputation:- While Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh is on deputation to Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, no prior permission of the Government will be required to travel anywhere in the country with the consent of the Divisional Commissioner in connection with the work of the Department.
However, if Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh wants to go abroad in connection with the work of Vidarbha Administrative and Development Training Academy, Amravati, or wants to go abroad for private work, then prior permission of the government will be required.
(22) Application of rules made by the Government from time to time:- During the period of deputation of Smt. Ritika Narula (Indian Economic Service), the rules made or executive orders issued by the State Government from time to time shall be applicable.
(23) In respect of terms/ conditions not included in the said terms and conditions:- The rules, regulations and executive orders which are applicable to the officers of the Central Civil Services Cadre in respect of specific terms/ conditions not included in the above mentioned terms/ conditions shall be applicable.
