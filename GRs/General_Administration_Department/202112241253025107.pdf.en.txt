the order
Shri Somnath Vasant Potre, Under Secretary, Public Health Department, is being appointed on deputation on deputation to the post of" Deputy Commissioner" on establishment of Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation under Urban Development Department for a period of one year initially subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mr. Somnath Vasant Potre, Under-Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary from the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes the charge of his office.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
In view of the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981, the Public Health Department and the Urban Development Department should mutually decide the terms and conditions of the deputation of Shri Somnath Vasant Potre.
3. All other administrative matters of Shri Somnath Vasant Potre, Under Secretary will be handled by the Public Health Department.
