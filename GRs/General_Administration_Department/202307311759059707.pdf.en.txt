The promotion will be a temporary one as an administrative exigency.
1) These promotions are being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Senior Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
2) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in this post.
The selection of eligible upper category short writers for the year 2022-2023 is provisional in nature and promotions to the post of selected category short writers are provisional in nature. As a result, the upper category shortlists in the selection list will not get any rights of the regular post and seniority of the service of the shortlists.
