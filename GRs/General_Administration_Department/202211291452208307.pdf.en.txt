The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 dated August 21, 2015.
Introduction: The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 has come into force in the state. However, it has been observed by the government that many of the services notified under the Act are offered in offline mode. """ Because of the offline services, citizens are not able to access the services in a transparent, timely and efficient manner and the purpose of the law is not served." Under the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP), all services are expected to be made available online as per the guidelines of the Central Government.
Circular: Following instructions are being issued to the Ministries/ Departments and their Subordinate Offices for effective implementation of the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015:
(a) The services notified under the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015, which are presently being offered in online format, shall be offered only in online format after 31st December, 2022.
b) To develop and implement an online system by 31st December, 2022 for providing services notified under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 but currently offered in offline format. After December 31, 2022, it will be available only online.
(c) The reasons for not being able to provide the services notified under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 in online format by 31st December, 2022 should be submitted to the Chief Secretary.
