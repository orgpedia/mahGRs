Government circular
A provisional Common Seniority Sheet as on 01.01.2023 for Selected Category Shorts in the Ministry Department has been prepared and annexed to this Circular.
The said seniority has been created as per the instructions of General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) of the Select Category Short Writers Cadre in their Department the tentative common seniority as on 01.01.2023.
In this regard, the information regarding the issues in the representations received from the concerned selected category scribes regarding objections/ errors should be examined as per the records of the department and sent to this department with the opinion of the department by 06.02.2023. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the interim seniority will be published assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
