The Proposal:
The comprehensive instructions on handling of issues of Legislature, issues of interest, etc. and how they can be dealt with at the level of the concerned Ministries/ Departments have been issued vide Government Circular dated 20th May, 2019.
Accordingly, the Department was contemplating to issue guidelines for transfer of Legislative Questions, etc. to other Departments and to fix timelines for acceptance/ rejection of transfer. In this regard, the following recommendations have been made:
Government circular:
(1) The Department to which any reference relating to the affairs of the Legislature has been received by the Legislature Secretariat shall, if the reference does not relate to its Department, in good faith propose to transfer the reference to the Department to which it relates.
(2) The Department to which a reference has been received to accept the transfer shall, if the transfer is acceptable, inform the Department proposing such transfer immediately accordingly.
3. In case the transfer is invalid, the opinion in support thereof shall be recorded and forwarded to the department proposing the transfer.
If a department sends a notice proposing transfer to another department, the said notice shall be dealt with as mentioned in the above Table 1 and 2 by taking a decision within 15 days whether it is admissible or not. However, without taking any such action, the department proposed to transfer the department within 15 days, keeping the decision pending in the department.
