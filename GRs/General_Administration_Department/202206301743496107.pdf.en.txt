the order
Smt.Sanika Mane, Cell Officer, Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially to the office of" Cell Officer" in the office of State Commission for Right to Service, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai under General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mrs. Sanika Mane, Cell Officer shall commence her deputation from the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner, and shall complete her period of deputation on the date on which she enters upon her parent Department after handing over the charge of the office of the Deputy Commissioner, one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of their deputation, if the Government considers their services necessary in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II& III) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981, the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection and the General Administration Department should decide the terms and conditions regarding the deputation of Mrs. Sanika Mane.
All other administrative matters of Smt. Sanika Mane, Cell Officer will be handled by the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection.
4. Smt. Sanika Mane, Cell Officer to be relieved immediately by the Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection for accepting appointment on deputation.
