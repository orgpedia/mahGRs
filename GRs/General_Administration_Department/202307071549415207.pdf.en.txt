The Proposal:
The 13th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Western Zonal Council was held in Gandhinagar, Gujarat on Saturday. Completed on 15.04.2023. In the meeting, for the first time, all the States and Union Territories which are members of the Western Zonal Council were informed about the submission of PPT on Good Practice. Accordingly, State of Maharashtra, State of Gujarat, State of Goa and Union Territories of Diu, Daman and Nagar Haveli submitted PPTs on Good Practices available with them. The PPT on Science City was one of the PPTs presented by the State of Gujarat in the PPT on Good Practice presented above. In this Science City, different materials have been kept for teaching about science and action is being taken to develop different equipment in other science related areas. It also includes reputed scientific institutions of India.
The government is planning to set up a science city in Maharashtra on the lines of the one in Gujarat.
Government's decision:
It will be beneficial to set up a Science City in Maharashtra to inculcate interest/ curiosity in science among the children of Maharashtra. For this, Ma. It is hereby approved to constitute a Study Group of the Committee of Secretaries under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary as follows:
