Introduction: In order to communicate directly with the people and to reach out to the people, various welfare schemes of the government are being implemented through audio-visual medium. Jai Maharashtra/ Dilkhulas with the participation of the Chief Minister and I am the Chief Minister speaking. It was launched on October 5, 2017. The government is implementing various schemes for the welfare of the people. The matter of re-launching the" I am the Chief Minister speaking" programme in the presence of the Chief Minister was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Various government schemes for the welfare of the people.
Approval is being given to re-launch the programme" I, the Chief Minister, am speaking" in a new format on TV channels and radio. Also, administrative approval is being given to implement the process of selecting the institution by publishing an e-tender for the creation of the program" I am the Chief Minister speaking."
2." I am the Chief Minister speaking" will be broadcast a maximum of two times in every month and a maximum of 24 times in a year.
