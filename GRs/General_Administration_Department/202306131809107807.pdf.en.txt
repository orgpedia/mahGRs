Post-Service Training Course for the post of Government Circular Assistant Cell Officer. The examination is scheduled as per the Rules of June 6, 1977. The service is allowed to carry copies of books, government decisions and notifications for some of the papers in the post-entry examination.
However, it is necessary to publish the list as there is confusion among the candidates about which books are required to be brought along at the time of the relevant paper. Accordingly, a list of books, government decisions and notifications is being published at the time of the paper as per the annexure attached with this government circular. Candidates are advised to note that no other Government Decision/ Notification other than the Government Decision and Notification mentioned in the Annexure shall be allowed to be carried along.
