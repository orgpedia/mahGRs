The Proposal:
Reference No. 1 has been issued for taking a policy decision to resolve the issue of graduate part-time candidates of the state pending for the last 15 years and to provide job opportunities on contractual basis to part-time candidates as a special case. 5 decision of the government. The committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Minister of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. In pursuance of the report submitted by the Committee to the Government on 6.9.2018, the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Govt. The proposal was placed before the Cabinet at its meeting held on 11.12.2018. It has been approved by the Cabinet. As per this decision, the Graduate/ Diploma holder part-time candidates will be eligible for direct appointment as Reference No. The upper age limit of 46 years has been relaxed to 55 years. Accordingly, the government has taken the decision.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to appoint part-time candidates holding Graduation/ Diploma in Government/ Paramilitary Services in direct service in Group C posts outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission in Reference No. The age limit of 46 years has been relaxed to 55 years.

The increase in the upper age limit for graduate part-time candidates cannot be construed as a precedent for the demand for upper age limit by any other parallel reservation entity.
It will be mandatory to follow the prescribed procedure for the recruitment. All the appointing authorities should fill up the posts by strictly implementing the parallel reservation accordingly.
Also, there will be no relaxation in the prescribed eligibility and criteria for admission to government services for part-time candidates holding a graduate/ diploma.
The order will be applicable to all government and semi-government offices, boards, corporations, municipalities, district councils, government-aided institutions and all other authorities, services and institutions.
The decision has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www. maharashtra. gov. in and its computer code is 201901021233502307. This decision is being taken digitally.
