Government's decision:
As per the guidelines issued by the Election Commission of India in Reference No. 1, a total of 450 Voter Helpline Centres have been set up in 359 Taluks and 91 Towns in the State for the period from 1st March, 2013 to 31st March, 2014 and further extended periodically for one year. Also, 450 Voter Helpline Centres have been extended till 31st March, 2021 as per Reference No.9.
As per the directions of the Election Commission of India, a special summary revision of electoral rolls with photograph based on the qualifying date" 01.01.2021" has been carried out and at present the voter registration is going on in the State under continuous revision programme. The Special Summary Revision of Electoral Rolls with photograph based on the qualifying date" 01.01.2022" is expected to be announced in 2021 by the Election Commission of India. Therefore, for the convenience of the voters, the extension of these 450 Voter Helpline Centres is being extended till 31st March, 2022 subject to the following conditions: (1) All the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government Resolution, Serial Number, dated 20th February, 2013 shall continue to be applicable.
(2) In particular, it is stated that the Collector and the District Election Officer shall make available the necessary manpower for the Voter Help Centre in their district through the Data Entry Operator, the agency on contract from the Directorate of Information and Technology at their level or the local body supplying the manpower and selected in the prescribed manner.

(a) Since the employees to be appointed are employees of the concerned manpower supplying organization, their remuneration should be made available through the concerned organization.
It should be ensured that these employees are not employed in any government or district collector's establishment or cannot establish their rights over this establishment.
The same should be given in writing to the concerned institution and employee. The written consent of the institution concerned and the staff concerned should be obtained.
