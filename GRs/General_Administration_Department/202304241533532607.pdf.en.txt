Clerk-typists in the Ministry Department have been given ad-hoc promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the ad-hoc selection list for the year 2022-2023. In this order, vide order No. 32, Smt. S. Rody, Clerk-typist, has declined the promotion offered to him.
In view of Mrs. Rody's rejection of her promotion, the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ K-12 dated 12-09-2016 is removing her name from the ad-hoc selection list for the year 2022-2023. Similarly, Ms. Rodi's name will not be considered in the next two years, i.e. 2023-2024 and 2024-2025, but her eligibility will be checked for promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the third year, i.e. 2025-2026.
